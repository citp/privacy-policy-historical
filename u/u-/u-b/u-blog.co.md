WHAT INFORMATION WE MAY COLLECT: We may keep your information that you provide by filling in any forms on our website or when you contact us, we may keep your record for further correspondence, details of any transactions that you carry out through our system, details of your visit to our website and the details of resources that you access using our website.

IP ADDRESSES AND COOKIES: Through our website we may collect information about your computer, including your IP address, browser type and version and operating system information for system administration and to report aggregate information to our advertisers. This statistically informative data about our users' browsing actions does not identify any individual.

Note that affiliates and advertisers who advertise on our site may use cookies, but we do not have access to them or control over them. If you do not want to allow access to the cookies on your system then you can set your browser to refuse cookies. However by doing so you will not be able to enjoy all the offers presented at the site. 

IF WE USE YOUR INFORMATION, HOW WE USE THAT? We may use your information in the following ways: Using your information we make sure that the content that you see from our website is presented to you and your computer in an effective manner. To provide you with products, information, services or special offers then we feel may interest you or that you have requested from our website, where you have shown consent to be contacted for such purposes; To confirm the accuracy of the information that we hold about you via third party providers of such services, to include phone, e-mail and postal verification methods, to inform you about the changes and upcoming services through our website. >

PASSING YOUR INFORMATION TO THIRD PARTIES: The data we collect using your browsing pattern may be provided to our clients and carefully selected third parties. However we do not disclose individual information to them, but only the aggregated and generalized information.U-blog.co is the sole owner of the information collected on http://www.u-blog.co. U-blog.co collects personally identifiable information from our users at several different points on our website. This data is used by u-blog.co to send you by, email, SMS, voice, post or other appropriate means, communications about our products or services and some carefully chosen third parties. If you do not want us to pass your details on to third parties for marketing purposes, please let us know by emailing us at thirdpartyunsubscribe@u-blog.co 

UNSUBSCRIBE: Drop an email to us at unsubscribe@u-blog.co if you do not wish to receive marketing emails from us. Drop an email to us at thirdpartyunsubscribe@u-blog.co if you do not want us to pass on your details to third parties. 

DISCLOSURE OF YOUR INFORMATION: In the following events we may wish to disclose your information to third parties: In the event that we sell or buy any business or assets, in which case we may disclose your personal data to the prospective seller or buyer of such business or assets. If u-blog.co or substantially all of its assets are acquired by a third party, in which case personal data held by it about its customers will be one of the transferred assets. If we are under a duty to disclose or share your personal data in order to comply with any legal obligation, or in order to enforce or apply any of our terms and conditions. 

THIRD PARTY WEBSITES: Our website may contain links to third party websites. Please note that if you follow a link to any such website, we do not accept any liability or responsibility, because these websites have their own Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policies. 

PERSONAL DATA STORAGE LOCATION: Any collection of your data that we may make (also mentioned above) while you use our website, may be transferred to a destination / storage location outside the European Economic Area (“EEA”). By submitting your personal data, you agree to this transfer, storage and processing. We strive to ensure security of your personal data, but we cannot guarantee the security of your data transmitted to our storage site and any transmission is at your own risk. 

YOU CAN REQUEST YOUR INFORMATION: The Act entitles you with the right to access information held about you. Your right of access can be exercised in accordance with the Act. Any such request may be charged by a fee of $10 to meet our costs in providing you with details of the information we hold about you. 

CHANGES TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY: Any changes we may make to our privacy policy in the future will be posted on this page and where appropriate, notified to you by e-mail. 

CONTACT US We welcome any of your concern or questions regarding our privacy policy and any services that we offer to you. Please drop an email to us at [support@u-blog.co](mailto:support@u-blog.co) if you have anything to discuss under above context.
