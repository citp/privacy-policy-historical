**Consent**   
Use of this website by you signifies that you (the Client[s]) consent to the Company collecting and using personal information that is provided to UKForex (the Company). 

The Company may amend this policy and continued use of the website by you will be taken as acceptance of any changes. We recommend that Clients regularly review the policy.

**Collection**   
Clients may visit the website without providing any personal data. The Company automatically collects certain information about website traffic to measure the usage of the website and improve the site. 

The Company also collects personal data from you during registration for the service. 

**Use**   
Personal information collected is used to provide the service you request from the Company. Occasionally we may make your information available to organisations whose products and services we think may be of interest to you.

**Disclosure**   
The Company will not disclose your information except in accordance with this policy unless we believe such action is necessary to conform with legal requirements, enforce the contract between us or to protect our rights.

**Security**   
The Company has strict security and confidentiality procedures covering the storage and disclosure of information to maintain its confidentiality.

**Cookies**   
The Company uses cookies for collecting user information from the website. 

A cookie is a small piece of information stored by your browser on your computer hard drive. Cookies are used to customise the website experience for you and to allow web pages to be personalised. Most browsers will automatically accept cookies. If you disable cookies from your browser you will not be able to use all features of this website.

**Phones recording**   
To ensure we carry out your transactions accurately, and to improve our service provision our telephone lines may be digitally recorded and the Company may monitor those calls for training or issue resolution.

**Links**   
This privacy policy applies to this website only and customers should refer to the privacy policy on any linked sites. The Company is not responsible for the privacy practices of linked websites.

**Accessing information**   
You are entitled to request the information the Company holds about you and have it corrected if it is inaccurate. Access will require a signed written request and payment of a fee. If you wish to access the information the company holds about you please contact us on [privacy@ukforex.co.uk](mailto:privacy@ukforex.co.uk)

**Export Data**   
If the Company needs to transfer your data outside the European Union to countries whose data protection laws are not as extensive, we will keep control of the information or require anyone we pass it to, to treat it to European Standards.

**Contact**   
If you have any questions about this Policy or the Company's information practices please email [privacy@ukforex.co.uk](mailto:privacy@ukforex.co.uk)

**Changes to policy**   
Any changes to the policy will be posted here so you will know what information we collect and how it is used. Continued use of the website is acknowledgment that you have read and accepted any changes to this website.
