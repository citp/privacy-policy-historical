# Privacy Policy | UKTODAYNEWS.COM

**UK Today News** can be reached via e-mail at

[](http://uktodaynews.com/contact-us)

**contact [at] uktodaynews [dot] com**

Our Web server does not automatically recognize any information regarding the domain or e-mail address of each visitor who comes to our web page.

We, at **UK Today News** , collect no information or details on visitors who browse our web pages.

This is the information we collect :

**Cookies** : We do not set any cookies.

**Ad Servers:** We, UK Today News, try as much as we can to get you relevant offers that may be of interest to you, the visitor. UK Today News has relationships with other companies and we allow these companies to place ads on our the web pages of www.uktodaynews.com. Due to your visit to our website,there is a likelihood that ad server companies can collect some information like your domain type, your IP address as well as your clickstream information.

For further information, we suggest that you check the the privacy policies of our adnetworks.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads and collect data when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site, other sites, and other forms of media about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please see: http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp

If, for any reason you feel that www.uktodaynews.com is not following this stated information policy, please contact us at the e-mail address provided below.

**contact [at] uktodaynews [dot] com**
