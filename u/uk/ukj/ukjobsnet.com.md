>

## 2\. What we collect and who we share it with

> In order to track online traffic flows we record anonymous statistical data.

>  We may use this data for marketing, security or any other purposes and Online Classifieds Limited may share this information with any 3rd party in the execution of these tasks. 
> 
> If you would like to have your personal data removed or updated from our records, please notify us using the "Contact Us" page or write to us at the address below. 
> 
> If you would like to have your personal data removed from our records, please notify us using the "Contact Us" page or write to us at the address below. 

## 3\. Our use of Cookies

> A cookie is a small amount of data sent to your browser from a website's computer and stored on your computer's hard drive. Each website can send its own cookie to your browser if your browser's preferences allow it. To protect your privacy, your browser only permits a web site to access the cookies it has already sent to you, not the cookies sent to you by other sites.
> 
> We may use temporary cookies to track and/or store anonymous data.
> 
> You may choose to reject cookies from this web site in your web browsing software. If you reject cookies from this site, some areas of this site that require the use of cookies may not be accessible to you.
