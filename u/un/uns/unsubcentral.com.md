# UnsubCentral | Privacy Policy

#### UnsubCentral Privacy Policy **  
**

UnsubCentral, Inc. (“UnsubCentral,” “us,” “we,” or “our”) respects and values your privacy. This privacy policy (the “Privacy Policy” or “Policy”) details UnsubCentral's treatment of personally identifiable information ('PII') and other information collected from our customers (“customers,” “clients,” “you,” “your,”) through this website at [www.unsubcentral.com](http://www.unsubcentral.com/) (the “Website”) and the Service (defined below), why we collect it and how it is used. Notwithstanding the foregoing, use of the UnsubCentral suppression service (the “Service”) shall be governed by the applicable services agreement between UnsubCentral and you (or the appropriate third party if other than you) and our information privacy practices for customer data as described below.

We may periodically make changes to this Privacy Policy and those changes become effective immediately upon posting them to the Site. It is your responsibility to review this Policy frequently and remain informed about any changes to it, so we encourage you to visit this page often. We are not required to provide notice of changes to this Privacy Policy.

#### Information Collected and Received

**Personally Identifiable Information**

Through the use of this Website or Service, UnsubCentral may collect or maintain the following PII about individuals from clients or prospective clients, which is typically business contact information:

  * First and Last Name
  * Street and/or mailing address, city, state and zip code
  * Email Address
  * Occupation/Title
  * Company
  * Phone number
  * Company URL
  * Business Type
  * Information volunteered by users, such as survey information
  * Any other PII the user submits during the course of normal correspondence with UnsubCentral or its third-party partners



We are the sole owner of information collected on the Website, except for contact lists and content that you provide to us in connection with your use of the Service (including but not limited to your logo or office locations). If you provide us PII or any other information, you are representing to us that the information is true, accurate, complete and current. You are also representing to us that you have the authorization to provide it to us.

UnsubCentral may keep a cached copy of the content you provide or disclose to us as a part of your use of the Service for an indeterminate or indefinite period of time, including media files. To assist in development, we may keep copies of HTTP requests and responses in your notification log. We may also keep call log, notification log, billing/ payment and other log data as we determine is appropriate.

**Non-Personally Identifiable Information**

Non-personally identifiable information is information collected by UnsubCentral in the normal course of operating its business that does not reveal the specific identity of the individual. We collect certain aggregate and non-personal information when you visit this Website and through the Service. This information does not relate to a single identifiable visitor. It tells us such things as how many users visited the website and the pages accessed. Collecting this information helps us tailor our Website to our visitors.

When you visit the Website to browse, read or download information, we automatically collect standard computer information that your browser sends to every website you visit, such as your IP address, browser type and language, cookie information, access times, referring website addresses and the page you request. We generally do not link standard computer information to anything that will allow us to identify users unless to prevent fraud and to protect our Service, Website, users, customers or others.

We also may collect aggregate and non-personal information through "cookies" as explained below.

#### Uses of Information

The information, including PII, UnsubCentral collects and receives is used to:

  * Provide, offer or deliver the Service
  * If you provide PII in order to obtain access to the Website or Service, to allow you to access and use the Website and Service and to monitor your use thereof
  * Improve the content and functionality of our Website and the Service
  * To notify consumers about updates to our Website, including but not limited to technical updates and changes in policy
  * To contact our corporate users for marketing purposes if they elect to continue to receive such communications
  * To provide you with product information and support services offered on the Website
  * And as otherwise may be necessary for UnsubCentral to provide requested services to users and visitors to the Website and to better understand our customers
  * Additionally, if you provide PII for a certain reason, we may use the PII in connection with the reason for which it was provided and for any other reason consistent with the terms of this Privacy Policy. For instance, if you contact us by e-mail, we will use the PII you provide to answer your question or resolve your problem



#### The Information Shared or Disclosed with Third Parties

UnsubCentral is not in the business of selling your information. We consider this information to be a vital part of our relationship with you. There are, however, certain circumstances in which we may share your PII with certain third parties without further notice to you, as set forth below:

  * Third Party Applications and Services: If you elect to use one or more third party applications that interoperate with the Service, those applications may upon your election be given access to data (which may include PII) on our systems generated in connection with your use of our Service. Please remember that UnsubCentral is not responsible for the policies and practices of third party application providers, and UnsubCentral shall have no liability arising from any action of any such provider as it concerns your data or information or otherwise. In connection with your use of any such third party applications, please review carefully the applicable privacy policies each such third party application provider to become familiar with their policies and practices as it concerns your data and information.
  * Business Transfers: As we develop our business, we might sell or buy businesses or assets. In the event of a corporate sale, merger, reorganization, dissolution or similar event, PII may be part of the transferred assets. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer or successor of UnsubCentral may continue to use your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy.
  * Agents, Consultants and Related Third Parties: UnsubCentral, like many businesses, sometimes uses other companies to perform certain business-related functions. Examples of such functions include mailing information, maintaining databases, developing new services, enhancing current services and processing payments. When we employ another company or individual to perform a function of this nature, we only provide them with the information that they need to perform their specific function.
  * Legal Requirements and Other Circumstances: UnsubCentral may also share PII in the following limited circumstances: (1) upon receipt of a properly authorized and authenticated governmental request for information; (2) in response to a subpoena or a court order; (3) to comply with relevant laws; (4) in response to an investigation of fraud regarding a specific individual; (5) in an effort to safeguard the person or property of UnsubCentral, an UnsubCentral employee or a third party; (6) when UnsubCentral believes it is necessary to protect our interests or we believe in good faith that the law requires the sharing of your personal information; (7) act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of the Website or Service or the public, or (8) protect against legal liability.



#### Cookies

Cookies are pieces of information that any website may transfer to your browser that are then stored in your computers system. Cookies are returned to the website that deposited them when a visitor returns to that site. Our Website uses cookies to keep track of your session, to save passwords and to make sure you are not subjected to repeated advertising. We may also use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests.

Most web browsers are set up to accept cookies. You can, however, reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. If you choose to decline cookies, some of the functionality of the Website may be impaired.

This Website may contain links to other sites that are not owned or controlled by us. Please be aware that UnsubCetnral is not responsible for the privacy policies of such other sites. We encourage you to be aware when you leave our Website and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information. This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected by this Website. Additionally, many other sites that are not associated or authorized by UnsubCentral may have links leading to our Website. We cannot control these links and are not responsible for any content appearing on these sites.

#### Security

UnsubCentral uses industry-standard technologies when transferring and receiving consumer data exchanged between us and other companies to help ensure its security. This Website has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Our servers are backed up regularly and protected by security systems. Please be advised that, although we take reasonable technological precautions to protect your data, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Therefore, UnsubCentral cannot warrant that your information will be absolutely secure. Any transmission of data at or through our Website is at your own risk.

UnsubCentral utilizes the third party cloud services of various companies to store, use and transmit your personal information. These third party cloud service providers are reputable companies, well known in our industry and they utilize current industry standard data encryption technologies and firewalls. We also use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to protect your personal information.

UnsubCentral has a security program intended to keep the personal information stored in our systems protected from unauthorized access and misuse. Our systems are configured with data encryption, or scrambling technologies and firewalls constructed to industry standards. We also use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology that protects the data you send over the Internet.

#### Children's Privacy

UnsubCentral places the highest value on the privacy of children. UnsubCentral does not knowingly collect PII from children under the age of 13. Any person who provides their information to UnsubCentral represents to us that they are 13 years of age or older. If you are the parent/legal guardian of a minor who has given us PII, please contact us so we can delete it.
