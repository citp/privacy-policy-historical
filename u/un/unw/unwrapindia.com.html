About Us

Conceived in early 2011, UnWrapIndia is an initiative to promote "100% Made in India" products. It was hurting to see some of the most fascinating Indian arts dying a slow death and we were wondering what we could do. With a strong marketing back bone like Eye and Buy, we knew we could reach the target market very easily and hence thought of creating a platform where NGOs, rural and urban entrepreneurs could showcase their products directly to the customers.
