# UXPin privacy policy

Any personal information provided or acquired by UXPin.com is controlled primarily by UXPin Sp. z o.o., registered under the registration number (KRS) 0000400136, with tax ID PL5862274376 and with business address at Abrahama Street 37/8, 81-366 Gdynia, Poland. 

This Privacy Policy is designed to assist you in understanding how UXPin Sp. z o.o. collects, discloses and safeguards the aggregate information UXPin receives when you visit, browse and use uxpin.com website and UXPin App Service. When you visit uxpin.com website, UXPin Sp. z o.o. receives and collects site use information and cookies. UXPin Sp. z o.o. collects personal information from Customers who visit uxpin.com website and register with UXPin Service.

Upon registration to the Service UXPin Sp. z o.o. requires such information as username, password, email address, first and last name, zip code, country and information necessary to fulfill the payment process. UXPin Sp. z o.o. uses the Customer's registration information to authenticate users and provide access to UXPin App Service.

UXPin Sp. z o.o. collects only personal information that you choose to provide voluntarily on registering for our UXPin App Service. UXPin Sp. z o.o. shall advise you which information is mandatory to provide and what is optional. 

UXPin Sp. z o.o. gives the Customer free access to personal information in an appropriate scope. The Customer may change or delete personally information at any time by contacting UXPin Sp. z o.o. UXPin Sp. z o.o. keeps all disclosed personal information secure. Information collected by UXPin Sp. z o.o. will not be shared or sold to third parties for any purpose without your express consent, except as provided in this Policy.

You agree and acknowledge that your information collected through this site and related Services or in connection with the UXPin App Service may be transferred across national boundaries and stored and processed in any of the countries around the world in which UXPin Sp. z o.o. conducts business activity, including the United States.

Your UXPin App Service account information including but not limited to username, password and customer profile are password-protected so that you have secure access to entering and editing personal data.

It is the Customer's responsibility to protect the security of their password. 

UXPin Sp. z o.o. servers automatically collect site use information, each time you visit our site. The site use information includes, but is not limited to, the following: the domain names, operating system in use (e.g. Windows), browser (e.g. Mozilla Firefox, Internet Explorer) and version, the website which referred you to us, and other similar information. This information may be aggregated to measure the number of visits, time and date of visits, average time spent on our website, pages viewed and other similar information. We may use and disclose site use information, for example, to measure the use of our website, improve the content, explain the utility of our website and services we provide, and to extend their functionality.

Similar to other commercial websites, the technology called "cookies" may be used to provide you with tailored information. A cookie is a piece of data that a website can send to your browser, which may then be stored on your hard drive so we can recognize you when you return. You may be able to set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie. Our cookies collect general information that enhances our ability to serve you and measure the utility of our website. 

Information UXPin Sp. z o.o. collects may be used to enhance your use of our website, and to provide you with the service that you ordered, arrange the website in the most customer-friendly way, communicate special offers and featured items, and/or respond to your questions and suggestions. From time to time, UXPin Sp. z o.o. may contact Customers via email to notify of changes to its Service and provide additional information about UXPin Sp. z o.o. and its Services.

All Customers information is restricted in our offices. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (e.g. our billing clerk or a customer service representative) are personally granted access to sensitive information. UXPin Sp. z o.o. stores its servers in a highly secure server environment, with surveillance and support to prevent unauthorized access and maintain data security. 

UXPin Sp. z o.o. cannot guarantee the security of your data while it is being transmitted over the Internet and through servers that are out of our control. We strive to protect your personal information but UXPin Sp. z o.o. cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to our website or services. Any data transmissions you make over the Internet are done so at your own risk. Once we receive the data transmission, we make our best efforts to ensure its security and privacy on our systems.
