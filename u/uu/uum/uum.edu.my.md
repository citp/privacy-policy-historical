# Universiti Utara Malaysia - Privacy & Security Policy

At UUM we believe strongly in protecting our users' privacy. We will not wilfully disclose information about our users to any third party without first receiving our users’ consent. 

This Privacy Policy sets out the privacy practices for the services offered on any UUM owned and/or operated websites ("the Website"). 

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. You should check this page from time to time to ensure that you are happy with any changes. 

  1. Your Personal Information 
    * All personal information you provide via the Website will be treated as private and confidential. Please see any pages where information is collected for further relevant information.
    * UUM may compile data in aggregate form so that UUM may better understand users that visit the Website. Aggregate data is anonymous and does not contain any personal information that identifies users. 
  2. Sharing of Information 
    * UUM will not sell, rent, trade or give away any user’s personal information or email lists to third parties without first obtaining the user’s consent.
    * The Website contains links to other websites. UUM is not responsible for the privacy practices and/or the content of such websites. You should exercise caution and look at the privacy statement applicable to the website in question. 
  3. Security 
    * UUM is committed to the security of our users’ personal information and we have security procedures in place to protect against loss, misuse or alteration of personal information under UUM’s control. 
  4. Cookies 
    * When you visit the Website, UUM may send your computer a "cookie", a small file that resides on your computer's hard drive. Cookies allow UUM to make the Website more useful by tailoring the services UUM offer from time to time. You can set your browser not to accept cookies, although you may not be able to access all of the features on the Website.
    * Overall cookies help UUM provide a better website be enabling UUM to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not. A cookie in no way gives UUM access to your computer or any information about you, other than the data you choose to share.



SECURITY POLICY

  * The latest technology including data confidentiality is utilized to protect the data submitted and the visitors have to abide to a strict protection standard that restricts them from visiting certain websites.
  * All electronic storage and personal information data will be protected and saved using the appropriate safety technique.
