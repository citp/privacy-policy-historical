Cookies

Cookies are simple text files stored on a visitor's computer. Cookies are just as safe as any other data stored on a computer. Cookies are created by web sites and used to store data for the convenience of the visitor. A cookie cannot be used by a web site other than the one that created it. A cookie cannot be used to read data from your computer other than the data stored in the cookie. The only data stored in our cookies is data we selectively choose to store in cookies from among the data you enter on our web site. Our site does not use cookies to store financial information, contact information, or other personal or sensitive data. Our site uses cookies only to remember our visitors' and their preferences and to deliver content specific to their needs.
