# UT Arlington Internet Privacy Policy

Internet Privacy Policy

You may be entitled to know what information UT Arlington (UTA) collects concerning you. You may review and have UTA correct this information according to procedures set forth in UT System BPM #32. The law is found in sections 552.021, 552.023 and 559.004 of the Texas Government Code.

The University of Texas at Arlington, 701 South Nedderman Drive, Arlington, TX, 76019, USA, makes the following statement in regards to information gathering and dissemination practices for its web site: [www.uta.edu](http://www.uta.edu/).

**Platform for Privacy Preferences - P3P**  
This information is also available in [computer-readable](https://web.archive.org/web/20021209101100id_/http%3A//www.uta.edu/uta/wwwteam/p3p.xml) format which complies with the specifications set forth by the [P3P](http://www.w3.org/TR/P3P/) group at the World Wide Web Consortium.

**Information Automatically Logged**  
Server logs - Server logs are studied for two reasons:

  * Improving server performance 
  * Improving site design. 



Logs are kept indefinitely. We do not provide access capabilities to server logs to people not associated with the University.

**Personal Information**  
Some pages may request and collect information that personally identifies you or allows you to be personally contacted. Some information submission will be encrypted, when required by State law. We do not share any personal information with any third parties nor do we use any personal information for any purposes other then specifed on the submission page.

**External Links**  
This site contains links to other sites. University of Texas at Arlington is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

**Contacting the Authors**  
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact **[webmaster@uta.edu](mailto:webmaster@uta.edu)**
