Privacy

UTAS Home ›  › Website Privacy Statement  › Privacy

The UTAS privacy statement is currently under review due to changes in legislation and the implementation of Google Analytics on some UTAS websites.

UTAS respects personal privacy and is bound by the Personal Information Protection Act 2004 (Tas). More information on how UTAS collects, holds, uses and discloses personal information can be found in the University’s Privacy Policy.

You have the right to access your personal information held by UTAS in accordance with the Right to Information Act 2009 (Tas).

You may contact UTAS in relation to any of your personal information collected when using UTAS websites by sending an email to service.desk@utas.edu.au or telephoning +61 3 6226 1818 or 1300 304 903 (local call charge from within Tasmania, mobiles excepted).
