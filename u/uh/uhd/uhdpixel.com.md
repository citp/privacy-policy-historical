# Privacy Policy | UHD PIXEL

## Log Files

UHDPixel.com servers have log files that collect information that includes Internet Protocol (IP) address, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, track users movement around the site and gather demographic information. This log file information are not linked to any personal information.

## Cookies

UHDPixel.com use cookies, this cookies are pieces of information that web servers store on your computer when you are browsing. These pieces of information allow servers to recognize your computer the next time you visit our site. Cookies can improve your browsing experience by allowing sites remember your preferences.

<https://www.wikihow.com/Disable-Cookies>

## Third Party Advertiser

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on UHDPixel.com send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.
