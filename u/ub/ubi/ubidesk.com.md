# Ubidesk - Online collaboration workspace, project management, document collaboration, file sharing

  * [](https://www.ubidesk.com/ "Online collaboration workspace, project management, document collaboration, file sharing")



  * Information We Collect
  * When you register with Ubidesk, we require a username, password, first and last name, and email address. We use the user registration information to authenticate users, and use the email address to communicate with the user. We may contact you via email to notify you of changes to Ubidesk services, maintenance notifications, information about Ubidesk and promotional materials from Ubidesk. 
  * We collect information on usage patterns including but not limited to accessed page, access time, user IP address, browser type, monitor resolution, etc. in order to improve our service and serve our users better. 
  * Disclosure
  * We may disclose aggregated user statistics in order to describe our services to prospective partners, advertisers, affiliates and other third parties for lawful purposes. We will never disclose such data on an individual or identifiable basis to third parties except when we must comply with laws that require such disclosure to law enforcement authorities or other government third party officials such as subpoenas. 
  * We will not share or sell your identifiable personal information to any third parties for commercial purposes without your express consent.
  * Cookies
  * A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. Ubidesk uses cookies to store and track information about your usage of our services. Cookies may be used to store your username and password so that you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit Ubidesk service. We may also use cookies to gather aggregated information about user habits on our website and services in order to measure traffic statistics and better understand how our users are using our services. We may share this data, in its aggregate form, with advertisers, affiliates and partners who may advertise on our website and services. 
  * Public Areas
  * Ubidesk service may have public areas such as message boards, user forums, etc. When you use these areas, you understand and agree that anyone may access, use, and disclose what you post to those areas.
  * Security
  * Although we try our best to keep your data safe and secure, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, we cannot guarantee the security of your data while it is being transmitted over the Internet and through servers that are out of our control. Any data transmissions you make over the internet are done so at your own risk.
