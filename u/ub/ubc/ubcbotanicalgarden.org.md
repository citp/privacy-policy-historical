# Privacy Policy | UBC Botanical Garden

By signing up for our email newsletter you consent to the secure storage of your personal information outside Canada (in the USA) in accordance with the permission and [privacy policy of Emma](http://myemma.com/privacy-policy) (our email provider).

Please note that only information that you choose to share is stored. The information that is stored on Emma’s server is accessible only to authorized UBC Botanical Garden staff and will not be used for any other purpose than to which you have consented (Emma does not have authorization to use this information for their own purposes nor do they share it with anyone else).
