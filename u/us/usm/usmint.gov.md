# The United States Mint Privacy Policy

The United States Mint appreciates your visit to our Web site. We support your right to know about information we collect and how it will be used. Our services are growing and our practices may change, so please check back for updates to our Privacy Statement. 

Click [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20090506152315id_/http://usmint.gov/policy/index.cfm?action=full) to see our full privacy statement. 

  * You do not have to give us any [personal information](https://web.archive.org/web/20090506152315id_/http://usmint.gov/policy/index.cfm?action=full#8) to visit our site. 
  * We do not attempt to collect personally identifiable information from [children under age 13](https://web.archive.org/web/20090506152315id_/http://usmint.gov/policy/index.cfm?action=full#4). 
  * The United States Mint does not [rent or sell your personal information](https://web.archive.org/web/20090506152315id_/http://usmint.gov/policy/index.cfm?action=full#9). 
  * We may use [personal information provided online](https://web.archive.org/web/20090506152315id_/http://usmint.gov/policy/index.cfm?action=full#8) to respond to electronic inquiries (job applications, product orders and inquiries, etc.) and distribute catalogs, job and other offerings, newsletters and educational materials, and we and our contractors store and use this information to process and verify requests and provide our products and services. 
  * We use software to maintain [security](https://web.archive.org/web/20090506152315id_/http://usmint.gov/policy/index.cfm?action=full#2), improve service and [collect data for statistical purposes](https://web.archive.org/web/20090506152315id_/http://usmint.gov/policy/index.cfm?action=full#5). 
  * We use [cookie](https://web.archive.org/web/20090506152315id_/http://usmint.gov/policy/index.cfm?action=full#3) technology to keep you connected to our server and operate our shopping cart. The United States Mint does not now use cookies to store individually identifiable information or track actions over time or across non-United States Mint websites. 



Click [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20090506152315id_/http://usmint.gov/policy/index.cfm?action=full) to view the complete text of the United States Mint's Privacy Statement.
