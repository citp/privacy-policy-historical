Privacy Policy

USBANKLOCATIONS.COM takes and respects your privacy seriously. USBANKLOCATIONS.COM collects small amount of user information when users register with USBANKLOCATIONS.COM or use any USBANKLOCATIONS.COM services. USBANKLOCATIONS.COM may collect information via cookie planted in your computer. The purpose of collecting user information is to customize services and enhance customer satisfaction.

The data we collect are:

  1. The information you fill and submit by the web forms on USBANKLOCATIONS.COM sites.
  2. Standard web log entries, including your IP address, page URL, and timestamp.

The credit card processing of USBANKLOCATIONS.COM will be handled by PayPal®. The credit card information won't be stored in USBANKLOCATIONS.COM database.

Please remember that your professional profiles, reviewss, and posts at USBANKLOCATIONS.COM become public and that you should exercise with caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. You are cautioned that any online communications may not be fully confidential. In addition, you should exercise caution when keying in information on public computers as the information could be stored in temporary files and could be retrieved.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.

  • Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site.
  • Google's use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

USBANKLOCATIONS.COM reserves the right to update this privacy policy with or without notice.
