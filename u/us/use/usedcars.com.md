# Privacy Policy – UsedCars.com

**Privacy Policy**

**SCOPE OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY**

At usedcars.com we take privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes our policies for handling the information about you that we collect and receive through this website. You can visit [usedcars.com](http://www.usedcars.com/) without revealing who you are or providing any personal information about yourself. By using this website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to practices of businesses that we do not own or control or people we do not employ. In addition, this website may contain links to other websites, such as automotive research, financing or insurance websites, and we are not responsible for the content, policies or privacy practices of such websites.

[Usedcars.com](http://www.usedcars.com/) is a Participant in the [TRUSTe Privacy Seal Program](http://www.truste.com/privacy-program-requirements/index.html). TRUSTe is an independent organization whose mission is to advance privacy and trust in the networked world. As this Web site wants to demonstrate its commitment to your privacy, it has agreed to disclose its information practices and have its privacy practices monitored for compliance by TRUSTe.

If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should first contact us at [usedcars@dealix.com](mailto:usedcars@dealix.com). If you do not receive acknowledgment of your inquiry, or if your inquiry has not been satisfactorily addressed, you should then contact [TRUSTe here](http://www.truste.org/consumers/watchdog_complaint.php).

TRUSTe will then serve as a liaison with the Web site to resolve your concerns.

**INFORMATION WE COLLECT**

**Information You Submit to Us**

At usedcars.com, we introduce shoppers like you to automobile retailers, retail networks or financing companies. We collect information that you submit to us through this website ("Submitted Information"), including personal information you may submit to us in connection with requesting an automobile price quote, financing or insurance, responding to surveys, contacting us by email or creating a usedcars.com user account. Submitted Information may include personal information about you such as your name, address, phone number, email address.  Submitted Information may also include information regarding potential trade-in vehicles, employer and employment status, social security number, residence information, bankruptcy status, date of birth, household income and availability of a cosigner.

**Passively Collected Information**

As you browse our website we collect certain information about your visit and your Internet activities through the use of cookies (if your browser permits it), web beacons and from your browser. This information does not consist of personally identifiable information, but includes your browsing history on this site or certain other sites, your IP address, browser type and other information.

We use this information, which does not identify individual users, to customize users’ experience on the site, to analyze and measure the effectiveness of the site, to administer the site, and to customize the content of advertisements users may see on this site as well as other websites.

We do not link this automatically-collected data to personally identifiable information.

We allow third-party companies to use cookies and web beacons on this site to collect non-personally identifiable information about user activity and to track users on this site and across the Internet.  In particular, we, or third-parties that we allow, use information from cookies to deliver relevant advertisements to users on this site and on other sites across the Internet.  If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by participating companies, click here: <http://www.networkadvertising.org/>.

You can also choose not to accept the cookies delivered by this site. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user’s computer for record-keeping purposes. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but browsers usually provide the option to disable this feature.  Consult your browser’s "Help" menu to learn more about disabling or deleting cookies.

**HOW WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION**

When you provide us with Submitted Information in connection with an automobile price quote or financing request, we will forward your Submitted Information in one of the following ways:

  * Directly to one of our participating automobile dealers who can respond to your request for information about a vehicle; 
  * To one of our business partners (generally an automobile manufacturer or another network of participating automobile dealers) who will then forward your Submitted Information on to the automobile dealer selling the vehicle you have requested information about; or 
  * To one of our business partners who can respond to your request for information about automobile financing or insurance (we do not provide financing or insurance directly, but instead transmit those requests to third-party vendors for consideration). 



Note that the use of your Submitted Information by our business partners or the automobile dealer, manufacturer, finance or insurance provider to which we forward your Submitted Information is not under our control and is subject to the privacy policies of those entities.

In addition to directly or indirectly forwarding your Submitted Information to an automobile dealer or provider of financing or insurance services, we may use your Submitted Information in the following ways:

  * We may use your information for our internal purposes unrelated to any transaction, such as to help improve our website, better understand our customers' needs or to develop new offerings in which you may be interested; and
  * We may aggregate your personal information with information from other visitors to prepare reports (that do not include personally identifying information) on site usage, purchase history or other factors for sale or distribution to others.



We do not share, sell, rent or trade your Submitted Information with third parties for promotional purposes except as described in this policy.

**COMMUNICATIONS FROM USEDCARS.COM AND OTHERS**

You will receive a thank you email following your submission of a request for information about a vehicle or related products and services through usedcars.com.  You may also receive a survey or marketing email from us by email after submitting your information. You will be able to opt-out of receiving future surveys or marketing emails from usedcars.com by clicking unsubscribe on the bottom of the email.

You should expect to receive communications from the automobile dealer, manufacturer, network of automobile dealers,  or finance or insurance provider to which we forward your Submitted Information.  Any request to opt-out of future communications from these parties must be made directly to that party.

**OTHER DISCLOSURE**

We reserve the right to disclose your personally identifiable information under the following circumstances:

  * As required by law and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process;
  * To investigate or take action against illegal activity, suspected abuse or unauthorized use of the site;
  * To protect the property or safety of usedcars.com users or others; 
  * To enforce our Terms of Service; and
  * In the event of a sale of usedcars.com to another entity, we will disclose the information to the buyer.



**TELL-A-FRIEND**

If you choose to use our referral service to tell a friend about our site, we will ask you for your friend’s name and email address. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email inviting him or her to visit the site. Usedcars.com does not store this information.

Your friend may contact us at [usedcars@dealix.com](mailto:usedcars@dealix.com) to request that we remove this information from our database.

**LINKS TO OTHER SITES**

This website contains links to other sites that are not owned or controlled by us. Please be aware that we, usedcars.com are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites.

We encourage you to be aware when you leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information.

This privacy statement applies only to information collected by this site.

**YOUR CONSENT**

Prior to providing us with any of your Submitted Information you can change your mind and cancel the submission by exiting the website. Once you submit your information to us through our website we may use it for any of the purposes mentioned above without first obtaining your consent. You do not have the choice of opting out, or requesting that we do not use your information, for any of the purposes mentioned above once you submit it to us. If you submit an application or request information about financing or insurance, you expressly authorize us and our third party partners to contact you in connection with each such request regardless of whether or not the number you provide us is listed on a "Do Not Call" list.

If you request to opt-out of receiving emails from us and subsequently submit a request for information regarding a vehicle or service through the site, we will assume that you wish to reinstate your relationship with us and we will use your information as described in this policy, unless you subsequently request to opt-out.

**ACCESS**

If you would like to access and update your personal information to correct or update inaccuracies, please log onto the site and re-populate the form to modify your information and resubmit your request. Should you want to modify information previously provided, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/about/feedback.aspx).

**INFORMATION SECURITY**

We maintain industry standard physical, electronic and other procedural safeguards to comply with applicable privacy laws to guard your nonpublic personal information. We also assess new technology for protecting sensitive customer information on an ongoing basis. When we forward your Submitted Information to an automobile dealer in our network, we ask that dealer to commit to use your Submitted Information only to respond to your request for information. However, please note that when your Submitted Information is forwarded to an automobile dealer or other third party provider, your information is no longer under our control will be handled according to the security and privacy policies of that dealer or third party provider which may differ from ours.

The security of your personal information is important to us. When you enter sensitive information (such as credit card number and/or social security number) on our registration forms, we encrypt that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). 

We generally retain your Submitted Information for two years following the deactivation of your account or, if you do not have an account, your submission of a request for information through usedcars.com. We may retain your information for longer in the event that it is needed to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.

**PRIVACY POLICY CHANGES**

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this privacy statement, the homepage, and other places we deem appropriate so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it.

We reserve the right to modify this privacy statement at any time, so please review it frequently. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you here, by email, or by means of a notice on our home page prior to the changes taking effect.

**OTHER INFORMATION**

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy or privacy policies, please feel free to [contact us](https://web.archive.org/about/feedback.aspx). You can also reach us by writing us at Dealix Corporation, 720 Bay Road, Suite 200, Redwood City, CA 94063.

**EFFECTIVE DATE**

The effective date of this Privacy Policy is October 26, 2011.
