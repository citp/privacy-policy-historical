# UseNeXT - Privacy policy

Following Privacy Policy applies to UseNeXT.

To better protect your personally identifiable information we provide this policy explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way information is collected and used.

If you have any questions regarding personal data, please don’t hesitate to contact us:

**1\. Collected Information**

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the website UseNeXT.

During your visit on UseNeXT, cookies are used to personalize UseNeXT according to your requirements and to provide UseNeXT with necessary status information for effective administration and further improvement. If you register, several or all following types of personal information are collected:

Name  
Address  
E-mail address  
Phone number  
Credit Card/PayPal/Debit Information  


**2\. Usage of Information**

Your given information is used to handle and process your registration and to provide and optimize services for you. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete your order, process payments, and, if necessary, to process debt collection.

You can register for newsletters on our website if you would like to receive further information on our products. Information you submit on our website will not be used for this purpose unless you fill out the registration form.  
We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website, or that Y number of men and Z number of women filled out our registration form, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals.

Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.

**3\. Data Security**

To prevent unauthorized access and data security we have put in place appropriate procedures to safeguard and secure your information.

This Privacy Policy applies to only to the website of UseNeXT. We do not have any influence on materials or information provided by the usenet. UseNeXT has no responsibility for third parties. Please consider that all commentaries, news, other contributions or information you provide within the usenet or the customer forum is publicly available. UseNeXT has no responsibility for anything you deliberately choose to circulate.

**4\. Correction**

You can access and change your personally identifiable information after login on UseNeXT. To better safeguard your information, if you want to change your provided payment data, please inform our support via support form or telephone.
