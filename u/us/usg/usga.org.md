# Privacy Policy Statement

This is the web site of The United States Golf Association.

Our postal address is   
P.O. Box 708  
Far Hills, NJ 07931

The USGA recognizes the importance its customers place on privacy issues. Please read on for more details about our privacy policy.

**WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT? HOW DO WE USE IT?**  
When you visit our web site, our web server automatically recognizes your Internet Protocol (IP) address. Your IP address is non-identifying and is used to compile statistics about traffic to our web site. In addition, the USGA collects the following identifying information:

  * When you sign up as a USGA member, we ask for your e-mail address, mailing address, daytime phone number, credit card number and expiration date. This allows us to process and fulfill your registration and to notify you of your registration status. 
  * When you order from our on-line catalogue at shop.usga.org, we ask for your e-mail address, mailing address, credit card number and expiration date. This allows us to process and fulfill your order and to notify you of your order status. 
  * When you post messages on our bulletin board ,we may ask for your name and e-mail address 
  * When you post messages on our guestbook, we may ask for your name and e-mail address 

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our web page and to customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual consumer. We may also use the information we collect to occasionally notify you via e-mail about USGA news and other announcements or changes to our web site. If you do not want to receive an e-mail from us, just e-mail us at usga@usga.org or call us at (908) 234-2300 Ext. 1312.

**HOW DOES THE USGA PROTECT CUSTOMER INFORMATION?**  
When you register as a member or order from our catalogue, we offer the use of a secure server. Our secure server software ("SSL") is the industry standard and among the best software available today for secure commerce transactions. The SSL encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us, including credit card number, name and address, so that it cannot be read as the information travels over the Internet. 

**WILL THE USGA DISCLOSE THE INFORMATION IT COLLECTS TO OUTSIDE PARTIES?**  
The USGA does not sell, trade or rent your personal information to other organizations. We may compile the information regarding what pages customers access or visit and non-personal information volunteered by the customer and use it for statistical purposes, but these statistics will not provide personal identifying information. 

**YOUR CONSENT**  
By using our web site you are consenting to the collection and use of this information by the USGA. If the USGA makes changes to its privacy policy, we will post such changes on this page so that you are always aware of what we collect.

**TELL US WHAT YOU THINK**  
The USGA welcomes your questions and comments about privacy. Please send us an e-mail at [usga@usga.org](mailto:usga@usga.org).
