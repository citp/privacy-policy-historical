These server logs may include information such as your Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.

Cookies are small files that contain information that Crawler uses to track trends and patterns of how people search. We send one or more cookies to your computer that uniquely identify your browser. We use cookies to improve the quality of our Service. We collect the full Uniform Resource Locator ("URL") of the Web page from which you came to Publisher, your IP address, which may include a domain name, the date and time for each page you view; the name of and information about any advertisement or other link that brought you to Publisher.
