PRIVACY POLICY

Effective July 11, 2006

Urban Outfitters Ireland Limited collects customer information to communicate with our customers about our products, services and promotions. We collect information including your name, e-mail and postal address that you provide to us when you register. We also monitor site use and traffic patterns to improve our web site design and the products and services we offer. Urban Outfitters Ireland Limited recognizes that it must maintain and use customer information responsibly.

Urban Outfitters Ireland Limited utilizes the latest in secure server software (SSL) to ensure that all personal information, including credit card details, remains private and secure. Urban Outfitters Ireland Limited will not rent or sell your e-mail address for any reason. We may disclose information about you to other members of the Urban Outfitters corporate family and as required by law.

By registering your information with us, you consent to Urban Outfitters Ireland Limited sending correspondence regarding the website and our stores to the e-mail address that you have registered. If you have registered to receive email newsletters from us, you can remove your email address from our list at any time by using the links in the emails we send you. Alternatively, you may e-mail us at UrbanEuropeCS@urbanout.com. Please include your e-mail address along with your full name and we will remove you from our mailing list.

Urban Outfitters complies with the standards, procedures and requirements detailed in the Data Protection Act.

Cookies

A cookie is a small data file that is temporarily stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive. The Urban Outfitters website uses a number of cookies to operate. The website uses cookies to help Urban Outfitters gather marketing information on website usage.
