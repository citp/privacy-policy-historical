It’s a Matter of Privacy

At University Federal Credit Union (UFCU), protecting your privacy and your financial assets is central to our mission. We understand it is important for you to know how we collect, retain, and use information about you. We recognize you have a right to expect your personal information remain private and secure and we commit to fully meet your expectations.

We are committed to providing you a broad array of competitive products and services to meet your financial needs. Sometimes that necessitates sharing pertinent information we collect about you with companies that (1) help us process transactions you initiate, and (2) help us offer you competitive financial products and services at UFCU and through UFCU Financial Services or UFCU Real Estate Services, LLC, DBA Members Home Advisor, our wholly-owned affiliates. We work only with companies which agree to maintain strict confidentiality and limit use of all information we share with them. Under no circumstances do we permit companies with which we conduct business to disclose or sell nonpublic information provided to them.

Information We Collect and Disclose About You

We may collect nonpublic personal information about you from the following sources:

  • UFCU membership and loan applications and other forms;
  • UFCU transactions of various types;
  • Consumer reporting agencies.

We may disclose information about you to process transactions on your behalf, follow your authorized instructions, or to comply wth applicable laws. We do not sell your nonpublic personal information to anyone for any reason.

If you terminate your membership with UFCU, we will not share information about you except as required by law.

How We Protect Your Information

UFCU restricts access to nonpublic personal information about you to employees who have a legitimate business purpose to utilize your data. We also train our employees in the importance of maintaining confidentiality and member privacy. Additionally, we maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards which comply with Federal regulations and industry best practices to protect your nonpublic personal information.

“Opt-Out” Options

If you want to limit disclosure of personal information as described in this notice, please complete UFCU’s Opt-Out Form.
