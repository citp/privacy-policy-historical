# UploadHero - Upload, Share and Download files!

### Privacy Policy

**Logging IP Addresses**  
For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server does not recognize any information regarding the domain or e-mail address. IP addresses are logged and used by UploadHero for measuring usage.

**Collecting e-mail Addresses**  
We collect the e-mail addresses of UploadHero Delivery recipients and senders for the purpose of logging and measuring usage. UploadHero does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with nonaffiliated companies.

**Certain Exceptional Disclosures**  
We may disclose your information if necessary to protect our legal rights or if the information relates to actual or threatened harmful conduct or potential threats to the physical safety of any person. Disclosure may be required by law or if we receive legal process.

**Use of Cookies**  
UploadHero uses cookies to store the preferences of users.

**File Transfer Security**  
We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site. Files stored for delivery are only accessible by UploadHero and through the clickable link generated for your recipient. All files stored for delivery are deleted when they expire.
