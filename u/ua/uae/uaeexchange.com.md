This privacy policy (statement) covers all websites that are operated by UAE Exchange including www.uaeexchange.com because UAE Exchange wishes to demonstrate its commitment to our users' privacy, we have agreed to disclose our information practices. 

**Information Collection**  
UAE Exchange is the sole owner of the information collected on any website that is owned an operated by UAE Exchange. UAE Exchange collects information from our users at several different points on our Web sites like when our users enter our website and their progress through the website. 

**Information Use**  
We store information that we collect through cookies, log files, and/or third parties to create a profile of our users. Consequently, collected information is used to improve the content of the site for the user. 

**Legal Disclaimer**  
Though we make every effort to preserve user privacy, we may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on our Web site.
