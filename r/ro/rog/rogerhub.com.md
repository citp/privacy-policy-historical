# Privacy Policy – RogerHub

If you require any more information or have any questions about this privacy policy, please feel free to contact me by email at postmaster at rogerhub.com. 

At RogerHub (rogerhub.com, subdomains, and websites officially owned under RogerHub), the privacy of visitors is of extreme importance. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by RogerHub and how it is used. 

**Log Files**  
Like many other Web sites, RogerHub makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, date/time, referring pages, and cookies to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, cookies, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. 

**Cookies and Web Beacons**  
RogerHub does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences (including the default comment author name), to link multiple requests made by a single user, and to limit the rate of requests made by a single user. 

**DoubleClick DART Cookie**  
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on RogerHub. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to RogerHub and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL - https://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html. Some of our advertising partners (Google Adsense) may use cookies and web beacons on our site. RogerHub has no access to or control over cookies that are used by third-party advertisers. You should consult the respective privacy policies of those third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. RogerHub’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites. 

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
