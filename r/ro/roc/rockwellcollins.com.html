Privacy Statement

PRIVACY

This privacy statement explains our policy regarding any personal information you may supply to us when visiting any Rockwell Collins Website.

PERSONAL INFORMATION

There are times when we may need information from you; additionally some areas of our Websites may require you to complete a registration form. Our goal in collecting this personal information is to offer you a more personalized browsing experience. When you let us know your preferences we will be able to deliver or allow you to access the most relevant information that meets your needs.

To extend this personalization experience Rockwell Collins Websites may save information on your system in the form of a cookie.

Rockwell Collins will not share e-Mail addresses with anyone outside Rockwell Collins nor disclose user information to any third party.

This site gives you the option to remove your information from our database if you no longer wish to have this information used, as a basis for further contact with you. Please click on the following link to send your e-mail request, collins@rockwellcollins.com .

Contact Us

In various areas throughout our Websites, we use e-mail links to allow you to contact us directly with any questions or comments that you may have. This information may be filed and used to help us prioritize our development needs and continuously improve our Websites to meet your needs.

BUSINESS RELATIONSHIPS

In working with our business associates, we require strict contractual obligations, which ensure that your information is safeguarded. Rockwell Collins Websites contain links to other Websites. Rockwell Collins is not responsible for the privacy practices, copyright or content of such Websites.

PUBLIC FORUMS

We also make available: forums, message boards and news groups to our customers. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.
