# Privacy Policy - Roches Pharmacy

At Roches Pharmacy, your privacy is very important to us. We have a policy of protecting the privacy of information contained or gathered via this website. We safeguard the use of information that registered members of its online directories have submitted and any other services offered that member may reasonably expect to be kept private.

Our Internet site is meant to be a service you. In order to provide you with the best products, excellent information and quality customer service, we record a variety of information from your visit to our site. We record your computer's domain name, information indicating which site pages you viewed, the address of the site you came from before visiting our site, and any search terms you used to find our site. We also record your email address if you chose to communicate with us via email. And, should we offer an online survey, we will record any information you provide us in that survey.

If you supply us with your postal address online you will never receive mailings from us unless requested by you.

If you supply us with your telephone number online you may receive telephone contact from us regarding your order. We will not contact you by telephone about any of our new products or offers. We use e-mails to let you know about our new products and specials. We never share or sell customer information with anyone. We assure all customers that any personal information disclosed to us during your time on our site will remain with us only. We will not share your personal information with any third parties. 

Roches Pharmacy does not sell or transfer the names of its online directories and maintains confidentiality of such information when undertaking projects with other companies, organisations and trade associations. Roches Pharmacy uses the information given to us to enable us to improve and enhance our service to you in the future From time to time, we may issue newsletters to our valued customers. Only customers that have registered for this service will receive this email.
