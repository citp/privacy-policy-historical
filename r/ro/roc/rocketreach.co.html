What information do we collect?

We collect personal information, such as your name and email address, when you register for an account on the Service.

If you are a user of our paid premium service, we will utilize a third party credit card payment processing company to collect payment information, including your credit card number, billing address and billing email. As you use our Services, we may use "cookies" and similar technologies to collect and store information that is generated automatically as you use the Services. This information is collected to help us understand your preferences, both individually and in aggregate, so that we can offer a better user experience. Most browsers allow you to manage cookies including blocking and deleting them. If you block or delete our cookies, the Services may not work at all for you. We may also use trusted third party services to track this information on our behalf, such as Google Analytics. Note, cookies do not contain personal data.

When do we collect information?

We collect personal information for the purpose it is voluntarily submitted and only at the time it is needed to fulfill that purpose.

We collect information that you voluntarily submit you when you register on our site, use service features, or pay for premium services. In addition, we collect anonymous usage data via cookies and pixels to help us remember your preferences and optimize user experience for you and other users of the site.

How do we use your information?

We will not use you information to contact you unless we are responding to a contact initiated by you or for one of the following reasons:

  • Managing your access to service
  • Sending you information on how to access features or updates
  • Fulfilling requests made by you
Your account information is completely segregated from the product parts of the Service and will not be used for any purpose other than to enable management and use of your account and the Services.

How do we protect your information?

The security of your information is important to us. We employ physical, technical, or managerial safeguards to protect your data. When you enter sensitive information (such as a credit card number) as part of our service, we encrypt the transmission of that information using industry-standard encryption. All transactions are processed through a gateway provider and are not stored or processed on our servers.

Although we do our best to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee the security of your personal information transmitted to our Website as the device use to access RocketReach, services you use to access the website, or 3rd parties may be compromised.
