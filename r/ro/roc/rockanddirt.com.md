# TAP Publishing Co.® Privacy Policy

TAP Publishing understands in today's digital world your privacy and the protection of your personal information are utmost concern to you. This Privacy Policy explains what information we gather and how this information is used. 

  * **1.** Tap Publishing uses cookies to gather information about visitors to its websites. This information tells us what browsers and operating system are being used. The data also tells us where our visitors are navigating on the sites. This information, in turn, helps us to better develop the sites so our visitors can more easily find the information they are looking for.
  * **2**. TAP Publishing will not sell or trade in whole or part the information we receive from our visitors or advertisers. The information is stored on servers that are not accessible to the general public. The information is used to notify users of news and information about TAP Publishing or its sites. If you do not wish to receive information from TAP Publishing, you can let us know and your information will be removed from this process.
  * **3.** TAP Publishing uses VeriSign® digital certificates to verify transactions that are secured by SSL encryption.
  * **4.** TAP Publishing uses the latest, most technologically advanced equipment, firewalls and software to protect your information from unauthorized access.
  * **5.** TAP Publishing has been in business for over 70 years and has built its company on the basis of trust and respect for its advertisers and customers. We take pride in our reputation for excellent customer service, and the security of our sites and your information.
