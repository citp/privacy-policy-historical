_Last updated June 2018_

This Privacy Policy discloses the privacy practices in effect for the Royal Draw (http://www.royaldraw.com) website (referred to as the "Site"). Consumer Smart Media Inc. (referred to as the "Site"). Consumer Smart Media Inc. (referred to as "us", "we", or "CSM") is the provider and operator of the Site and all its contents. Please read the following to learn what information we collect from you (referred to as "you" or "User") and how we use that information. We are committed to protecting your privacy online. If you have any questions concerning this privacy policy, please email us. 

**Please read this Privacy Policy carefully and in its entirety. By using the Site, you certify that you have read this Privacy Policy, understand it, and agree to its terms regarding the collection, storage and use of your information.   * **Types of Information collected** Depending on the activity you wish to engage in, some of the information we ask you to provide will be required, and some will be voluntary. If you do not provide required information for a particular activity that requires it, you will not be allowed to engage in that activity. If you use our Site to send information or offers, we may store any Personal Information attached to what you send. 

To provide the Site and all of its features, Royal Draw collects the following types of information about our Users: 

**Personal Information**   
Personal Information is information that is used to identify a specific User. Personal Information is collected when you engage in activities on the Site that require you to voluntarily enter in information, such as creating or updating an account with your real name, uploading an avatar image, redeeming points at the Points Store, publishing/posting content in discussion forums or comment areas, filling out a survey or form, entering a draw or sweepstakes, sending feedback to us or our Partners, requesting information about us or our partners, or signing up for special offers from third parties through the Site (collectively referred to as "Identifying Activities"). In such cases, you may be asked to supply certain Personal Information, which may include your first and last name, email address, telephone number, mailing address (including postal code), city of residence, date of birth, etc. When creating an account and posting to the Site, all posts will be related to your account and attributable to the name you supply during account creation. 

Some Personal Information, such as your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address and account information, will be collected automatically through your engagement with the site and will be used for account administration, such as to facilitate your subscription preferences, process your Points Store orders, and award you with points for completing Offers. Your Personal Information will be stored on our system for the purposes of our operations, and for User convenience. 

Your Personal Information is used to provide you with offers based on your subscription settings, improve Site operation, analyze Site use, conduct contests, improve marketing promotions, improve our goods and services offers, and to customize your experience with third parties as laid out in this Privacy Policy. If you send Royal Draw an email commenting on the draws, deals, goods, or services subject to our Site, we may use your comments and feedback either internally or externally. We may publish your comments and feedback as a testimonial, and/or to inform others about our services, or promote our Site or our offers. We may also post your comment in our internal marketing documents, and/or on our Site. Prior to posting your comments publicly on our Site, we will secure consent from you to use your full name along with your comment. In submitting a comment or reply to our social media, such as the Royal Draw Facebook page or Twitter feed, you are agreeing to have that comment shown publicly. 

We may also use Personal Information to resolve disputes, troubleshoot our Site, provide customer service, send promotional emails, accomplish administrative tasks, contact you, assert enforcement of the terms of our agreements with you (including our Terms of Use and this Privacy Policy) according to any and all applicable laws, and/or in cooperation with law enforcement actions. 

**Non-Personal Information**   
Non-Personal Information is information that does not represent or identify a specific User, and includes things like the Internet Protocol ("IP") address of the computer you use to access our Site, the Uniform Resource Locator ("URL") of the website you visited before coming to our Site, the URL of the website you visit after leaving our Site, the type of device you used to access the site, and the type of browser you use when accessing our Site. We and/or our authorized third-party service providers and advertisers may collect this information automatically through the use of electronic information collection tools like cookies, web beacons or pixel tags. In using the Site, you consent to the use of such electronic devices. 

We use Non-Personal Information to help us manage and troubleshoot the Site, analyze usage trends, gather User demographic information, comply with applicable laws, and cooperate with law enforcement activities. We may share Non-Personal Information with authorized third-party service providers and advertisers to measure the overall effectiveness of our online advertising, content, and programming.

Royal Draw shall not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your Personal Information with other parties except as described below: 

**Sharing information with authorized Third-Party Service Providers**   
Royal Draw provides services and products through Third-Party Service Providers (referred to as "Third-Party Service Providers") who perform functions on our behalf, such as sending and distributing Royal Draw's administrative and promotional emails. Royal Draw may share your Personal Information with Third-Party Service Providers in order to operate or troubleshoot the Site, fulfill customer orders, send mail or email, remove repetitive information on customer lists, provide search results and links, administer draws or sweepstakes, analyze data, provide marketing assistance, and/or provide customer service. Royal Draw may also collect Personal Information from individuals and companies with whom we have business relationships (referred to as "Partners") and may share your information with third parties to perform administrative functions. For example, when you complete an Offer, we release your user information to the company that provides the Offer on the Site in order to award you with points. 

We encourage Third-Party Service Providers to adopt and post their own privacy policies. The use of your Personal Information by Third-Party Service Providers is governed by their respective privacy policies, and is not under our control. 

**Sharing information collected through surveys**   
The surveys made available for Users on our Site are supplied by a third party (the "Survey Provider"). Any Personal and Non-Personal Information submitted through a survey our Site will be shared with the Survey Provider. If you participate in surveys through the Site, you may be asked to provide certain information including, but not limited to, your address, consumer and shopping habits, demographic information, and other information that could be used to qualify you for surveys (collectively "User Data"). User-submitted data collected through the surveys ("Survey Data") is anonymous, Non-Personal, and is owned by the Survey Provider and the company that sponsored the survey ("Survey Sponsor"). Survey Data collected through a Survey may be delivered to the Survey Sponsor, free of any restrictions or claims by any party. Neither Royal Draw, nor the Survey Provider, nor the Survey Sponsor shall send emails to any Users who have not opted in to receive those emails. Each party must also comply with all applicable laws, rules and regulations, including but not limited to CAN-SPAM and consumer privacy protection laws, subject to the collections, handling, storage and transmission of User Data and Survey Data. 

Neither Royal Draw nor the Survey Provider shall use User Data in any way other than to provide applicable surveys, and shall not disclose, disseminate or in any way publish or communicate User Data to any person, firm, corporation or other third party without the prior written consent of the User, except to employees, contractors, financial and legal advisors, subsidiaries who have a need to know, have been informed of the confidentiality terms of this Privacy Policy, and who agree in writing or are required by law to maintain the confidentiality of the User Data. Both parties are contractually obligated to use the same degree of care used in protecting its own confidential information from unauthorized disclosure to protect all User Data in its possession.

  * **Data tracking** **Cookies**   
To assist and customize your interplay with the Site, Royal Draw uses cookies. A cookie is a small text file containing information about a User that is stored on that User's computer as a record-keeper. We use cookies to save you time when using the Site, and to remind us of who you are. Cookies also facilitate our collection of your Non-Personal Information, such as which pages on our Site you visited and what links contained therein you clicked on, and whether or not you accessed our Site through a referral link. This information helps us create and maintain a user-friendly experience for all visitors. Cookies may be placed on your computer separately and in addition to ours by any Third-Party Advertising Companies we may use to present advertisements on our Site. Royal Draw has no access to or control over cookies used by any of its Third-Party Advertising Companies. This Privacy Policy is relevant to the use of cookies by our Site only, and does not account for the use of cookies by any Advertiser. Most browsers automatically accept cookies, however, you may be able to modify your browser settings to refuse cookies. If you refuse or delete our cookies, please be aware that some parts of the Site may not work properly for you. 

**Other data tracking devices**   
In addition to cookies, we may use other standard automations like pixel tags and web beacons to track your use of our Siteâ€™s pages and promotions, or we may allow our Third-Party Service Providers to use these automations on our behalf. Pixel tags and web beacons are tiny graphic images that may be placed on pages within our Site, or in our emails, that allow us to determine whether you have performed a specific action. When you access a page or open an email that contains pixel tags and web beacons, they create a Non-Personal record of that action. Pixel tags allow us to measure and understand visitor traffic and behaviour on our Site, measure responses to our promotions, and measure our Site's overall performance. Our Marketing Partners may supply us with pixel tags and web beacons for the same purposes.
