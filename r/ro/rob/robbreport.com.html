"Personal Information" means any information obtained through your use of our Website(s) and other digital products that might reasonably be used to specifically identify you, such as your name, title, company, address, email, and phone number, among other things. The Personal Information we collect from you is provided by you voluntarily and enables us to give you access to specific products, including our newsletters, all of which may include features, functions, promotions, products and services associated with our digital applications. COLLECTING NON-PERSONAL INFORMATION

When you use our Website(s), we often use third party operational service providers (see Disclosure to Third Parties below) and technology that can recognize, collect and transmit information that is associated with you or your computer and browser, but which does not personally identify you (Non-Personal Information").

Our Website(s) also may use cookies" to collect Non-Personal Information about you. The term "cookie" commonly is used to refer to a small text file stored on a computer that keeps track of your visits to web sites and/or can contain log-in information so you dont have to re-enter information each time you visit. We will use the term "cookie" to refer generically to any technology that can recognize, collect or transmit information about your visits to our Website(s).

"Cookie" information typically includes such Non-Personal Information or data as the Internet or web domain that referred you, the type of operating system and browser you are using, the date and time of your visit, information relating to your activities on our Website(s) (e.g. clickstream data), and other technical attributes of your visits to our Website(s). Cookies store information that allows our Website(s) to recognize a users computer or access device each time it is used to visit our Website(s). Cookies also can keep count of how often you return to our Website(s). Additionally, cookies can track how you use our Website(s), such as your preferences for features and functions, and can store information such as your log-in information, so you dont have to re-enter them each time you return to our Website(s). We use the information collected through cookies to analyze trends, administer our Website(s), track movements on our Website(s) and gather aggregate demographic information about all of our visitors so that we can continually improve our Website(s). We do not use cookies to retrieve information from your computer for purposes that are unrelated to our Website(s) or your interaction with our Website(s).

You may stop or restrict cookies on your computer or purge them from your browser by adjusting your web browser preferences. You should consult the operating instructions that apply to your browser to obtain instructions, should you decide to change the use of cookies on your computer or purge them from your browser, and, in general, to determine how best to configure your browser settings to meet your requirements.

Note that if you turn off, purge or disable cookies, you may not be able to use all of the features, functions or services available on our Website(s).
