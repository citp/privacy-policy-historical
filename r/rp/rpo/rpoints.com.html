Privacy policy

If you registered with Rpoints.com before the 20th of February 2011, please read our Fair Processing Notice here

General

The purpose of this Privacy Policy is the let you know what type of information we may gather about you, how we use it and the choices you have regarding use of this information. This Privacy Policy needs to be read in conjunction with our Terms of Use and the words and phrases in this page have the same meaning as in our Terms of Use.

Data Protection Act Compliance

We respect the privacy of all our members and take handling of information seriously. We are voluntarily registered under the UK Data Protection Act 1998 with the Information Commissioner's Office. Registration number: Z918442X.

What information do we collect?

We do not collect any more information from you than what is needed for providing our service. When registering on our site, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your name, username and e-mail address. To prevent misuse of our service, we also log your IP address. Any more information you provide (for example method of payments) to enable us to send your cashback payments will not be shared with any third party.

Where we collect sensitive information, we do take extreme care in doing so. Our members' account area of our site is security encrypted (256-bit Secure Sockets Layer encryption) to high standards.

We do not use any malicious software, adware or malware on our website.

What do we use this information for?

We only use the information we collect to improve our website, our customer service and provide you with our loyalty shopping service.

By providing us your email address, you agree to receive emails regarding your account with our website and payment related information.

Newsletters

We may also send you specially negotiated offers or discounts by email periodically as an email newsletter. You can unsubscribe to our newsletter by selecting the appropriate subscription options in your profile page or by clicking on the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of our newsletter.

Use of cookies

Our website and our partner merchants' links may use cookies. A cookie is a small piece of data or text that is sent from a web server to your browser and is stored on your computer. A cookie can't read data off your computer or read cookie files created by other sites. These cookies do not damage or harm your computer.

These cookies are essential for making sure you get the cash back for your purchases and for us to improve our website. It is necessary for our members to accept these cookies, as they are essential for the tracking to be able to earn your cashback.

If you want to learn more about cookies and how to manage them, you can read the BBC guide about cookies here

Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website and servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety.

Third party Websites

We have partnered with many retailers to enable our members to earn discounts and cashback. When you click on our merchant links, you are taken to the merchant website and make your purchases with the merchant. These third party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

Access to information

In accordance with that Act, we reserve the right to charge a small fee if you ask us to provide you with this information. If you have any questions and want us to provide this to you, please contact us using the contact details below.
