Brief summary of rijadeja.com

  • All competitive exam's updates including notification, syllabus, results etc...
  • All exams' details including UPSC, GPSC, SSC, TET/TAT, HTAT, Bank Exams, Railway Recruitment Board (RRB)'s Exams, Police Recruitment etc.
  • Full study materials for above exams including hand written notes.
  • Support for student's queries via Feedback/Queries form.
  • Teachers and School Students can also found resources for them e.g. Government circulars, notifications etc.... School Students can find complete books for NCERT, GCERT, Previous Years' questions papers (with answers), Short notes of GCERT and NCERT (std. 6 to 12)
  • Resources website where users can get various study materials on different topics. All materials are in pdf format and downloadable.
  • Monday Musings: An eMagazine for current affairs. You don't need to read daily newspaper for your exam preparation, just read MM. That's all.
  • Help Center: Complete help topics for technical help e.g. How to download study materials?, How to login?. This help center is available in both English and Gujarati language.
