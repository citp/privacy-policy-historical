# RiskIQ

## RiskIQ™ Privacy Policy

Any information we collect on this website, or that you provide: 

  * is used to provide information on our products and services,
  * may be used to notify you for marketing purposes,
  * will not be sold or rented to others,
  * and will not be shared with other organizations without your specific consent.



At RiskIQ™, we are committed to respecting and maintaining your online privacy. We recognize the need for appropriate protection and management of all personally identifiable information you share with us. 

If you want to correct any information, or wish to delete your information from our database, please let us know by [contacting us](mailto:contact@riskiq.net).
