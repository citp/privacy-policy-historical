ARTIST ARENA PRIVACY AND SECURITY AGREEMENT 
 
(Effective May 9, 2006; last modified November 2, 2010)
As used throughout this privacy policy, "you" will refer to the user of this website and "we" or "our" shall refer to Artist Arena. Protecting your privacy and keeping your information secure is a top priority for Artist Arena. We want you to understand what we do, so we ask that you read this Privacy and Security Statement carefully.
 
1. Collection and Use of Personal Data
Artist Arena collects names, e-mail addresses, mailing addresses, telephone numbers, credit card information and event preferences when you purchase fan club memberships our website, www.artistarena.com . Your e-mail address is stored so that we can send you special announcements and notifications of events that we believe may be of interest to you. Credit card information (including card type and issuer, credit card number, and expiration date) is not stored on-line. We do not now, and will not ever, provide this credit card information to others, except as required by law or in connection with criminal investigations.
 
Your mailing address is required so that we can mail your welcome kit to you and communicate with you about your order. Your mailing address is also needed to verify and authorize your credit card during your purchase. Additionally, your personal information may be added to our general Artist Arena customer list, which also includes information about individuals who purchase via phone or in person. From time to time in connection with certain events, we may use this customer list to send you special announcements and notifications of events.
 
Artist Arena provides you the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners each time we request specific information about you.
 
Additionally, Artist Arena may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our website. Artist Arena may use cookies to keep track of your shopping cart, and to save your password and other personal information so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site. We do not use cookies for any other purposes. Your use of this site is your consent to this process.
Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. We may use this data to tailor your experience at our site, showing you content that we think you might be interested in, and displaying the content according to your preferences. We may provide this information to others providing content to this site.
 
2. Children's Guidelines
Our Web site meets the guidelines of TRUSTe's Children's program. We do not target children in any way as users of this site, and do not promote this site to children. Persons under the age of 13 are expressly prohibited from providing any personally identifiable information to Artist Arena without the express consent of their parent or guardian.
 
3. Confidentiality of Information
Currently, Artist Arena does not sell its general customer list or any personally-identifiable information to third parties for marketing purposes. If we do decide to engage in such a practice in the future, you will have an opportunity to remove yourself from any such list, prior to its distribution, by e-mailing us at info@artistarena.com.
 
4. Our Security Goals
We safeguard information about you according to established and commercially reasonable security standards and procedures. We also assess new technology for protecting customer information on an ongoing basis. All financial transactions take place on a secure server and are transmitted immediately to an encrypted secure database.

7. Cookies
Non-personal information and data may be automatically collected by virtue of the standard operation of Artist Arena's computer servers or through the use of "cookies." Cookies are files which utilize minimal hard drive capacity and cannot damage your computer's files, but which allow a web site to recognize repeat users and track web usage behavior. Users should be aware that Artist Arena can not control the use of cookies (or the information collected thereby) by third parties. If you do not want information to be collected through the use of cookies, you should configure your browser to allow you to choose whether to deny or accept the use of cookies.

If you have any questions, comments, criticisms or complaints regarding this Privacy and Security Statement and/or the way it affects you, please feel free to e-mail us at info@artistarena.com or call us at 212-420-1700. If you've given us your e-mail address and do not want to receive via e-mail special announcements or notifications that might be of interest to you, send an email to info@artistarena.com. We will quickly remove your e-mail address from our mailing list.
