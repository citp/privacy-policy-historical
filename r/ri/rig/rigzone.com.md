# RIGZONE - Privacy Policy

**At Rigzone, your right to privacy is a primary concern**  
This Privacy Policy describes the guidelines we use to protect the information we collect about you during a visit to our Internet site. If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20090321070000id_/http%3A//www.rigzone.com/contactform.asp). 

**Will you share my email address with other parties?**   
**No.** Rigzone will not share your email address or other personal information with other companies or parties without your permission, unless we believe that we are required to do so by law or other special circumstances. 

**Can I be removed from your email lists?**   
You can remove yourself from our email lists at any time by simply logging in and [updating your profile](https://web.archive.org/member_acct.asp). Changes to your profile take place immediately, and you should not receive any further emails from Rigzone once you unsubscribe. 

**What information do you collect?**   
Rigzone.com requests personal information at various times during the use of our Web site. These requests are made to allow us to provide services you have requested, or to enhance your experience while visiting us. Information we collect may include your name, address, telephone number, email, and address. In addition, we periodically sponsor contests and other promotions that may require other personal information in order to participate. 

**What do you do with this information?**   
Only Rigzone.com has access to users' accounts. **We will not give or sell your private information to any outside company for its use in marketing or solicitation.** However, we may disclose your information in certain cases where we have your permission or under special circumstances, such as when we believe it is required by law. 

Periodically, we will use the information provided to us to notify you of important announcements regarding special offers or important functionality changes to our Web site. If you would rather not receive this information, you can request to be excluded from these announcements by checking the appropriate box in your user profile. 

Rigzone.com also uses information on an aggregate basis (without individual information) to better understand our users and to improve our site. Aggregate statistics may also be disclosed to potential advertisers and/or business partners. 

**Your consent**  
By using the Rigzone.com web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Rigzone.com. Periodically we may modify our privacy policy. We will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect and how we use it. 

**Give us your feedback**  
Rigzone welcomes your questions and comments about privacy. [Click here](https://web.archive.org/web/20090321070000id_/http%3A//www.rigzone.com/contactform.asp) to contact us.
