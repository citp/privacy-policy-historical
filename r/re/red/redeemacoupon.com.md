This privacy policy applies to the Site RedeemACoupon.com and all products as well as services offered by BuildNSee at this site.

## Personal Identification _\- (Improving Customer Service)_

We may collect personal identification information from Users in a variety of ways. This is inclusive of but not limited to users who visit our site, register on the site, subscribe to the newsletter and fill out a form. This coincides with other activities, services, features and resources we provide on our Site.

Users may be asked for:

* names
* email addresses
* phone numbers

Users are allowed to visit our Site anonymously. We will collect personal identification information from Users only if they voluntarily offer such information to us. Users can always refuse to provide personal identification information, except in the case that it may prevent them from interacting in certain Site related activities.

## Non-Personal Identification Information _\- (Personalizing User Experience)_

We may collect non-personal identification information about our Users whenever they interact with our Site. Non-personal identification information may include:

* browser name
* type of computer
* means of connection
* operating system
* Internet Service Provider
* other similar data

## Cookies

Our Site may use "cookies" to enhance the User experience. Cookies can be placed in the hard drive of the User's web browser for record-keeping purposes and to occasionally track information. User may set their web browser to refuse cookies or to alert when cookies are being sent. If they do, note that some sections of the Site may not function properly.

## Advertising

Ads appearing on our site may be delivered to Users by advertising partners who may set cookies.
