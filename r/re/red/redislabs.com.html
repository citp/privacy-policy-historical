about us

Redis Labs offers enterprise-class Redis and Memcached for developers. Our fully-managed cloud services - Redis Cloud and Memcached Cloud - deliver top performance in a highly-available, infinitely scalable, predictable and stable manner.

We power tens of thousands of customer apps in multiple cloud environments and enhance use cases such as real time analytics, social app functionality, job management, and geo-search.

Redis Labs has more than 2,900 paying customers and has raised $13M in venture funding from Bain Capital Ventures, Carmel Ventures, and others.
