Personally Identifiable Information is Provided by You

Much of www.redvector.com is accessible to you without your providing personally identifiable information. However, RedVector requires that you register and provide personally identifiable information to enable you to utilize certain features of www.redvector.com, including subscribing for RedVector's courses and being issued a username or password. In addition, you submit your personally identifiable information to RedVector in order to receive company updates, press releases, or other special offers from RedVector and select business sponsors. RedVector will obtain your consent for any use of your personally identifiable information not described in this Privacy Policy or any use beyond the original use for which you provided it.

The personally identifiable information that RedVector requests from you may include your name, address, email address, telephone number, driver's license number and professional license number. The personally identifiable information that you provide must be accurate, complete and current. RedVector will specifically ask you for any personally identifiable information requested, and your provision of such information will always be on a voluntary basis.

RedVector's Use of Your Personally Identifiable Information

RedVector uses your personally identifiable information within RedVector's internal organization to process any selections that you may make using www.redvector.com and to create, develop, operate, deliver and improve our products and services. RedVector may de-identify and aggregate your personally identifiable information and license, sell or otherwise disclose the aggregated information to third parties.

RedVector may share your personally identifiable information with select third parties for fulfillment of services and features. RedVector may share or disclose your personally identifiable information when it determines, in its sole discretion, that the disclosure of such personally identifiable information is necessary to identify, contact, or bring legal action against you if you may be: (i) causing intentional or unintentional injury to another; (ii) interfering with another's rights or property, including RedVector's rights or property; (iii) violating any applicable law, rule or regulation; or (iv) such disclosure is required by any applicable law, rule or regulation; or (v) in order to provide profession related product information through third party vendors.

To correct, update or change selections regarding your personally identifiable information after you have provided personally identifiable information to RedVector via www.redvector.com, you must inform RedVector of any changes to, or updates of, any personally identifiable information that you provide. You may at any time request a copy of the personally identifiable information that you provided, elect to revoke ("opt-out") the information provided, update that information, or change any selections you have made with regard to use of that information.

In order to access, opt-out, update or change selections for use of, personally identifiable information that you provided, please contact RedVector. RedVector will accordingly furnish you with a copy of your personally identifiable information, or change its records to remove, update or change selections regarding your personally identifiable information.

If you opt-out of providing your personally identifiable information, RedVector will no longer use your personally identifiable information or share it with third parties. RedVector's Use of Cookies

Like many Internet websites, RedVector utilizes "cookies" to track and store anonymous preferential information about you as you use www.redvector.com. "Cookies" are alphanumeric identifiers in the form of text files that are inserted and stored on your hard drive by your web browser. Cookies track your personal preferences and activities, but in no way contain or refer to any personally identifiable information.

RedVector's use of cookies allows RedVector to aggregate certain information about its users. The aggregated information helps RedVector to serve you better by compiling user preferences to improve the features of www.redvector.com. RedVector may also share, loan, rent or sell this aggregated information to third parties, but keep in mind that the aggregated information contains absolutely no personally identifiable information.

If you do not want www.redvector.com's cookies to be inserted into your hard drive, you can disable your hard drive's ability to accept and receive cookies through your web browser. However, keep in mind that if you disable your hard drive's ability to accept and receive cookies, you may not be able to fully utilize all of the convenience features of www.redvector.com. For instance, you may have to log in your username or password more than once per session.

Usernames and Passwords May Be Issued to You

You may be issued a username or password by RedVector to utilize certain features of www.redvector.com. In order to receive a username or password from RedVector for use on www.redvector.com, you will be required to submit personally identifiable information. You are responsible for keeping your usernames and passwords, if any, confidential. You are responsible for any access, use or activities on www.redvector.com under your username or password. For that reason, be sure to log off when you are finished using a shared computer.

RedVector utilizes its best efforts to protect the security of any information submitted or transmitted by you via www.redvector.com, including, but not limited to, personally identifiable information. RedVector uses a secure server and SSL encryption technology to help ensure that your submissions and transmissions are kept confidential.
