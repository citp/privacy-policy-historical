# Privacy Policy | Redshift

**At Redshift, we take your privacy seriously.**  
Our principles on privacy are simple:  


  * We only collect and store personal information for the purpose of providing you with a high level of service.
  * We only collect personal information with your consent.
  * We do not and will not sell or share your personal information with anyone.
  * We protect your personal information by reasonable security safeguards against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.



 **What kind of information do we collect?**

 _Website Use Information:_ Like many websites, we use tools (including Google analytics) to collect anonymous information from visitors in order to better understand how they use our website. We use session cookies to keep you logged in while you use our website. These disappear after you close your browser. We also use persistent cookies which stay in your browser and allow us to recognize you when you return to our website. Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can change the settings to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from websites you visit. If you turn off cookies, parts of redshift3d.com may not work for you.

 _Login Information:_ If you create an account on redshift3d.com, we ask you to provide your name, email address and a password. We collect and store this information so that we can identify you as a registered user for access to private areas of our website, provide you with product activation keys and personalized access to our forums.

 _Payment information:_ If you purchase a license of Redshift from our website, we collect your billing and payment information, and any other information necessary to complete the transaction. We do not store your payment information.

 **How do we use your information?**

 _To personalize your experience_. Your information helps us respond more effectively to your individual needs.

 _To improve our website_. Along with direct feedback from users, the information we collect provides us with valuable insight into our visitors' usage patterns.

 _To process transactions_. We never share your information with any outside parties except for the sole purpose of completing transactions for license purchases.

 _To keep you in the loop_. When you purchase a license of Redshift, the email address you provide will be used to send you information pertaining to your order, including information on how to download and install Redshift. In addition, you may _opt in_ to receive our newsletter and other company or product news via email.

 **If you have any questions pertaining to our privacy policy, or if you would like to update, correct or remove any personal information that we have about you in our records, please email us at[info@redshift3d.com](mailto:info@redshift3d.com).**
