# Privacy Policy - RedMancunian.com

Under the Data Protection Act 1998, we have a legal duty to protect any information that we collect from you.

Here at RedMancunian.com we do not pass on your details to any third party unless you give us permission or we are legally obliged to do so within all factors of the Data Protection Act.

**What information do we collect?**

When you enter information in other areas such as our forum, ticket information & other submitting forms we only use your email address to send you emails & monthly newsletters.

You can unsubscribe at the bottom of our newsletter which will be released soon.

Alternatively you can email us at info@redmancunian.com and we will remove you from any subscriptions that have previously been made with our website.

**How to contact RedMancunian?**

You can contact us direct by e-mail: [info@redmancunian.com](mailto:info@redmancunian.com)

If this privacy policy changes in any way, we will place an updated version on this page.

Regularly reviewing this page ensures you are always aware of what information we collect & how we use it.
