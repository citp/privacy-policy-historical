### 6\. Data Transfers

We store the Personal Data that we hold about You in the European Union (“EU”). Given the fact that we are an international company, we also transfer your Personal Data to non-European countries where data protection laws may be less protective than the legislation where you live. 

When such a transfer happens, we ensure that We get your consent and that it takes place in accordance with this Privacy Notice and is regulated by standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission as ensuring an adequate protection for data subjects. 

### 7\. Web Browser Cookies

We might use cookies and other similar technologies, such as web beacons, pixels, and mobile identifiers to understand the website usage and to improve the functioning of the service, in order to ensure a more efficient and seamless navigation. We may also allow our business partners to use these tracking technologies on our website or engage others to track your behavior on our behalf. 

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to help us analyze how You use our site. A cookie is a small data file able to track the online movements of an individual on a specific website. Cookies, by themselves, do not tell us your e-mail address or other personally identifiable information. 

We may additionally collect information using Web beacons, which are commonly referred to in the industry as web bugs, pixel tags or Clear GIFs. Web beacons are electronic images that may be used on the Site or in our emails to deliver cookies, count visits and determine if an email has been opened and acted upon.
