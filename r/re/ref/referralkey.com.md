# None

Your privacy is extremely important to us. The mission of Referral Key is to provide a business networking platform in order to help small business owners and service professionals generate more sales. Referral Key is home to millions of professional relationships, millions of profiles, and millions of pages of user-generated data. In order to fulfill our mission and provide members with outstanding service, we leverage the information our members share. Member information is collected and organized in order to, and not limited to; connecting members, locating members, and presenting new business opportunities to our members and their contacts. 

  1. All information you provide will be stored safely and is the property of Referral Key Inc. Members have controls on their individual “settings” page in order to customize which information is made public and which is private. Use your discretion when sharing information and when in doubt, whether personal or commercial, do not share information you deem too sensitive to be viewed by the public. 
  2. We will use all industry protocols and technology to secure any sensitive information you provide. 
  3. By becoming a member of Referral Key and or by agreeing to exchange referrals with members, we may email you announcements and promotional information. If you wish to opt-out of receiving promotional emails, simply click the opt-out message on any emails you receive from Referral Key and adjust your email preferences accordingly. 
  4. With our consent, all the personal and business information you enter onto the Referral Key website and choose to be displayed may be viewable by members, the general public, and third party organizations with commercial interests. 
  5. Like many websites, Referral Key uses cookies and similar software to collect data about users to track their movements around the website and in order to help users to automatically and seamlessly sign in on subsequent visits to the site. 
  6. You MUST be at least 18 years old to join Referral Key. 
  7. If at any time you have any questions or comments about this privacy policy, please notify us via email at [info@referralkey.com](mailto:info@referralkey.com) or contact us at:
