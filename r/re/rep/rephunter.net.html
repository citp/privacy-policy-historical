RepHunter.net Privacy Policy
What this Privacy Policy Covers
This Privacy Policy covers RepHunter.net treatment of personally identifiable information that RepHunter.net collects when you are on the RepHunter.net site, and when you use RepHunter.net services. This policy also covers RepHunter.net treatment of any personally identifiable information that business partners of RepHunter.net share with RepHunter.net or that RepHunter.net may collect on a partner's site. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that RepHunter.net does not own or control, or to people that RepHunter.net does not employ or manage.
Information Collection and Use
RepHunter.net collects personally identifiable information when you register as a RepHunter.net independent representative, when you register as a RepHunter.net manufacturer, when you request our pricing via email, and when you enter promotions or incentives. RepHunter.net may also receive personally identifiable information from our business partners. When you register with RepHunter.net, we ask for your name, email address, street address, city, state, zip code, phone, fax, cell phone, and sales related data. Once you register with RepHunter.net and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us. RepHunter.net also automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, RepHunter.net cookie information and the page you requested. RepHunter.net uses information for three general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, to fulfill your requests for certain products and services, and to contact you about specials and new products.
Information Sharing and Disclosure
RepHunter.net will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone. RepHunter.net may send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when:
  • We have your consent to share the information. You are deemed to have consented to sharing your information with our other members when you submit your profile;
  • We need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested;
  • We need to send the information to companies who work on behalf of RepHunter.net to provide a product or service to you. (Unless we tell you differently, these companies do not have any right to use the personally identifiable information we provide to them beyond what is necessary to assist us.);
  • We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process; or
  • We find that your actions on our web site violates the RepHunter.net Terms of Service.
Cookies
RepHunter.net may set and access RepHunter.net cookies on your computer. These cookies are deleted when you terminate your RepHunter.net session. In particular, in order to use the Contact button, cookies must be enabled. RepHunter.net does not allow other companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set or access cookies.
Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Profile
RepHunter.net gives you the ability to edit your RepHunter.net profile at any time. You may request deletion of your RepHunter.net profile by sending an email to webmaster@rephunter.net with your email address and password.
Changes to this Privacy Policy
RepHunter.net may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages.
Questions or Suggestions
If you have questions or suggestions send an email to webmaster@rephunter.net .
