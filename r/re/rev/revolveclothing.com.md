# REVOLVE CLOTHING

Your privacy is always guaranteed when you shop with REVOLVEclothing. We will NEVER release any of your personal information to outside parties and you will never receive unwanted emails. 

When you sign up with us, you will receive information on new styles and upcoming promotions, but you may request to stop receiving this information at any time. To stop receiving information, simply send an email request to [sales@revolveclothing.com](mailto:sales@revolveclothing.com)

All transactions submitted to us are encrypted with 128 bit SSL security and cannot be intercepted by outside parties, guaranteed.
