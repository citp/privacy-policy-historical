Privacy Policy

This privacy policy has been created by Karma Implementor in order to demonstrate the commitment of the company (Karma Implementor) to protecting the privacy of users of this website as well as the widget service. This paper explains how and for what reason information of users of this website/widgetservice is collected, published and utilized.

  1. Information collection in general:
    If someone requests a file from revolvermaps.com no more information is collected than is typically expected to be collected by a website (IP address, referer, browser information, OS information). This logged information will be used by Karma Implementor for debugging purposes, internal statistics, quality assurance but will not be published except of being hacked and/or stolen.

  2. Widget Service and public statistics:
    In order to utilize the widget service no personal user information will be required. The explanations under (1) apply to the use of a RevolverWidget on any website. In addition the widgetservice uses the Flash-Cookie (SharedObject) technology as a feature of the Flash Player technology in order to guarantee the proper function of the public statistics service. In general the usage of the widget is attached to the public statistics presentation at www.revolvermaps.com providing information about the URL (the complete link) of the website including the widget as well as estimated country and region information of the user opening the widget at this website.

  3. Cookies/Web Beacons:
    A cookie is a small amount of data stored on the clients hard drive containing information about the client but no personal information about the user. Cookies are used to keep user information during a session for instance. Most wellknown browsers support cookies and provide options to disable the cookie service.

    Revolvermaps.com uses HTTP-cookies in order to ensure an advanced usability of the website. Therefore cookies are just utilized at client side. No information from cookies is collected and stored at the server.

    Revolvermaps.com uses third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
