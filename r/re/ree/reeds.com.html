Who is REEDS Jewelers?

Founded in 1946, REEDS Jewelers is a leading full-service multi-channel jewelry retailer, with stores in multiple states, a national direct mail catalog, and an ecommerce web site, www.Reeds.com, serving customers across all 50 states and those stationed abroad at APO/FPO addresses. To view our current roster of REEDS Jewelers store locations, please visit our Store Locator.

For the purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation 2016/679 (the GDPR), the Data Controller is REEDS Jewelers, Inc. registered in North Carolina with a registered address at PO Box 2229, Wilmington, NC 28402.

PLEASE NOTE: REEDS Jewelers is not associated with any other jewelry chain or store using the name Reeds or Reeds Jewelers.

What Personal Information Does REEDS Jewelers Collect?

REEDS Jewelers collects Personal Information you provide to us to serve your needs.

Personal Information, is any information that enables us to identify you, directly or indirectly, by reference to an identifier such as your name (first and last), address (city, state, ZIP), email address, telephone number, identification number, location data, or online identifier.

Some examples of points of contact where your Personal Information may be collected include:

  • Registering for My Account, Wish List or Product Review account on our website;
  • Requesting that merchandise be sent to you or others, making purchases or returns with us, placing an order with us online, via phone, mail, email or fax, or requesting an e-receipt;
  • Fulfilling orders you place and delivering packages;
  • Requesting a catalog or other content and communications from us;
  • Subscribing to REEDS.com or other REEDS Jewelers owned or operated websites, email or other communications from us or managing your preferences with us;
  • Applying for an account with us at one of our brand locations or online;
  • Providing your Financial Account numbers when transacting with us;
  • Interacting with our websites or mobile sites, mobile applications and services;
  • Electing to participate in any of our programs such as contests, sweepstakes, surveys, loyalty programs, etc.;
  • Applying for employment which may include your social security number or social insurance number;
  • Working with our sales associates who assist you with your shopping experience and purchases;
  • Communications with REEDS Jewelers;
  • Submitting request for repair;
  • Submitting request for Insurance Replacement information.

REEDS Jewelers also receives information about you from other companies, including, but not limited to information on your creditworthiness for private label credit card offers and products you may be interested in purchasing. Additionally, we may also collect Personal Information about you from other third parties, such as demographic firms, to make our future service and marketing efforts more efficient and personalized for you. We generally do not receive information about EU citizens or residents from other companies; for these persons we will notify you when we receive Personal Information about you from them and the purposes for which we intend to use that information.

We may combine all information we collect and we may disclose this information within our corporate family of companies or our service providers.

When you visit our Sites we may also gather certain Personal Information automatically and store it in log files. This information may include Internet Protocol (IP) addresses or other device identifiers, browser information, Internet Service Provider (ISP), operating system, location, date/time stamp and clickstream data.

REEDS Jewelers is a highly ethical company and requires the highest standard of conduct from our employees and business partners. We are a member of the Better Business Bureau, Jewelers of America, Gemological Institute of America, Jewelers Vigilance Committee, and other distinguished industry organizations; we strive to adhere to the Direct Marketing Association's Guidelines for Ethical Business Practice, Shopping and Consumer Rights and the DMA's Privacy Promise.

REEDS Jewelers may use the information collected as follows:

  • to complete your purchase transactions and provide the services you request;
  • to send you catalogs and other offerings through the mail;

  • to submit or process your watch repair through the watch repair center;
  • to submit or process you insurance replacement claim; and
  • to improve our merchandise selections, customer service, and overall customer shopping experience.

REEDS Jewelers may supply the third party firm with just the Personal Information necessary for this partner to deliver an offer, product or service to you. For example, in some cases we may use service provider partners to help us perform some of these tasks, including, but not limited to, processing or fulfilling your orders or performing customer service, business analyses or quality control (e.g., we use carriers such as UPS and FedEx to deliver packages to our customers), and consequently we may share your contact information with these service providers

We may also disclose your Personal Information when you ask us to do so or when we believe it is required or permitted by law.

When you make a purchase through or register with the Sites, you provide us with an email address where we, or these service providers, may contact you for the purposes described in this section. For example, you may request to receive package tracking updates from one of our service providers.

Does REEDS Jewelers use Third-Party Advertising?

REEDS Jewelers uses third-party service providers to place Internet advertisements (banners or links) on our behalf. These advertising service providers may collect non-identifiable information about your visits to our Sites, and your interactions with our products and services. Such non-identifiable information does not include your name, address, e-mail address or other personal information. The information is collected through the use of cookies and pixel tags (also known as action tags), which are industry-standard technology used by most major websites.

In addition to the information about your visits to our website, our service providers may also use the information about your visits to other websites to target advertisements for products and services available from REEDS Jewelers.

Currently, we use third party-placed tracking pixels and cookies from the following advertising delivery partners: Google, Fetchback, SalesCycle, Criteo, Adnxs, LinkConnector, Channel Advisor, Bronto, and NetElixir. In the future we may partner with other companies to provide interest-based advertising, both on and off our Sites.

If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices for not having this information used by third-party service providers, please visit: http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp.

Legal Disclosures: In the unlikely event REEDS Jewelers is required to disclose Personal Information pursuant to lawful requests, such as subpoenas or court orders, or in compliance with applicable laws, REEDS Jewelers will make such disclosure. In the event the ownership of all or a part of a business within REEDS Jewelers were to change, Personal Information pertinent to that business's operations would likely be transferred to, or licensed for use by, the new owner.

Third Party Offers: If REEDS Jewelers chooses to offer you selected third party products and services that we think would be of benefit to you and you accept the offer, REEDS Jewelers would supply this third party firm with just the Personal Information necessary for this partner to deliver the offer, product or service to you.

REEDS Jewelers Credit Card Account: We may share your information with the bank that issues REEDS Jewelers credit card accounts, Synchrony Bank. (You can read their privacy policy at https://www.synchronybank.com/privacy/.)

Does REEDS Jewelers Use Cookies?

REEDS Jewelers uses HTML cookies and web beacon technology to enhance the browsing and shopping experience on the Sites. We use cookies and web beacons to identify your device, provide website user authentication, conduct fraud prevention and management services, distinguish you from other visitors, record your IP address and understand your use of our Sites, and identify the name of the website from which you linked to REEDS Jewelers. This information is used to help us serve you better by improving our website design, as well as our products, services, and promotions, and for obligations with our business partners. Information obtained through cookies and web beacons may be shared with or obtained by service providers on our behalf. More information is available under Third-Party Advertising Partners.

Please note: While navigating on the Rolex section of our website, some cookies are controlled by ROLEX SA which applies the following Cookies Policy.

Cookies are small files or records that are place on your computer's hard drive to collect information about your activities when you visit a website. A "session cookie" expires immediately when you end your session (i.e., close your browser). A "persistent cookie" stores information on the hard drive so when you end your session and return to the same website at a later date, the cookie information is still available. A web beacon is a small string of code that represents a clear graphic image and is used in conjunction with a cookie. When you visit our Sites, we may use both a session and a persistent cookie. This REEDS-placed cookie may contain information (such as a unique user ID) that is used to track your usage of our website and in some cases, your e-mail address. Your e-mail address is only saved in this REEDS-placed cookie if you register with us. The REEDS-placed cookie is also used for the auto-saved shopping cart so that when a user returns, the contents of their shopping cart are automatically retrieved. A web beacon allows us to capture certain additional types of information about a visitor's actions on a website, such as a visitor's cookie number, the time, date, duration and number of page views, a description of the page where the Web beacon is placed, and details about any items that were purchased. If you save your information with or order from us, we may also use cookies to monitor and maintain information about your use of our website. If you have not saved your information with or ordered from us, we may monitor and maintain information about your use of our website in a manner that does not identify you. You can disable cookies at any time by using your browser options; however, if you turn off cookies, we may not be able to track your order or enable you to make a purchase from our website. In addition, we will not be able to recognize you as a registered user to allow you access to your account information. These cookies transmit information back to the REEDS Jewelers web site computers; these computers are, generally speaking, the only computers which are authorized to read such information. The information captured makes it possible for us to, for example, (i) speed navigation, keep track of items in your shopping cart, and provide you with custom tailored experiences and content; (ii) remember information you gave to us so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit REEDS Jewelers sites; (iii) monitor the effectiveness of our marketing; and (iv) use analytics to monitor the performance of our websites and services.

You can choose to have your web browser notify you every time a cookie is being sent to you or you can turn off cookie acceptance altogether. Note that if you disable cookies, your overall shopping experience may be adversely affected as certain functionality will not work without cookies. You are welcome to contact us via phone, e-mail or postal mail for shopping assistance if you do not wish to use our website.

What Are My European Data Subject Rights?
