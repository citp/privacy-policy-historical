**Delete account Reetwo:**  
At any time you can easily delete your account on Reetwo, simply go to your Facebook profile, and then click Account Settings, Applications, select the application, click Edit, and   then Remove Application. Reetwo will be removed from your Facebook profile, your favorites and your page   Applications.  
Obs.: Removing the application in facebook, your account and all your data will be automatically deleted in Reetwo.

**The records:**  
Like other websites, we collect and use the data contained in log files. The information in the log files includes your ISP (Internet Service Provider), your IP (Internet Protocol), the browser you used to visit our site (eg Firefox), the time of your visit and which pages you visited throughout our website.

**Cookies and Web Beacons:**  
We use cookies to store information, such as your personal preferences when you visit our website. This may include a simple pop-up, or even a link to some of our features (eg forums). Google's use of the DART cookie to serve ads based on your interests and your visit to our site and other sites on the web. You may choose not to use the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad page and "policing program" company.  
In addition, our site also use third party advertisements. Some of these ads (sponsors) may use technology such as cookies and / or web beacons when they advertise on our site, which will also send these advertisers (such as Google through the Google AdSense program) also receive your personal information, such as your ISP, the IP address, your browser, and others.  
This is generally used for "geotargeting" (eg display advertising NEW YORK only to readers from New York) or showing certain ads based to a user type (eg display advertising to restaurants / bars to a user who regularly visits portals cuisine / drinks).  
You can turn off your cookies in your browser settings, or by changes in the tools of their Antivirus. I would like to inform you that such a procedure can change the way we interact with our site, or even with other portals. This may also affect or even not allow logins programs, websites or forums.
