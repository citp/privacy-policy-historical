Privacy Policy

Here at Receptional we value the privacy of our website users. Generally you can explore our site freely. However some aspects of the site do ask for personal information. We collect personal information, such as your name and email address, when you submit a request for information or when you submit your details for any other reason. Personal information is only collected with your consent which is given when you complete one of our forms.

If data has been collected then we take care to prevent any unauthorised access to the personal data and do not sell, or trade, information to any third parties.

Receptional uses your data only for the following:

  • To fulfil any requests for information on our services which users have placed
  • To keep you updated on news regarding our services and activities.

 

Cookie Policy:

Like many websites we use cookies in order to identify users and possibly prepare customized web pages for that user.

Almost all of the websites you visit will use cookies so that they can improve your user experience by allowing that website to ‘remember’ you, either for the duration of your visit (using a ‘session cookie’) or for repeat visits (using a ‘persistent cookie’).
