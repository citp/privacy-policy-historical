“Government Jobs” is hosted on Blogger and uses analytics tools like Google Analytics and Stat Counter. Though the owners of “recruitment4u.in” do not directly use any log files, but since the site is hosted on blogspot, there may be some log files being used by blogger. The information inside the log files may include internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
