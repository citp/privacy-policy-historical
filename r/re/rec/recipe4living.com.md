# Recipe4Living.com - Privacy Policy

[](http://www.ediets.com/)

**Privacy Policy**

We at Recipe4Living.com know that privacy is one of the most important issues for all of us on the Internet. 

**What We Collect:**

As part of our on-going efforts to ensure the best possible experience for all visitors to our web site, we collect data from browsers to aggregate statistical information. This includes the type of browser being used, its level of HTML support and what platform it is on. 

This Privacy Policy also applies to visitors to our Web page that have agreed to receive e-mail marketing from Recipe4Living.com. The personal profile information you submit to Recipe4Living.com remains your property, but by submitting that information to Recipe4Living.com, its partners including SilverNGold, ConsumerValueDirect.com, SmartBrands and BargainSpot.com or affiliates you grant Recipe4Living.com the right to use that information for marketing purposes. We may sell our e-mail lists, and we may join together with other businesses to bring selected retail opportunities to our members. In addition, Recipe4Living.com reserves the right to release current or past member information in the event Recipe4Living.com believes that the membership is being or has been used in violation of the Rules or to commit unlawful acts, if the information is subpoenaed, if Recipe4Living.com is sold or acquired, or when Recipe4Living.com deems it necessary or appropriate. By agreeing to these conditions, you hereby consent to disclosure of any record or communication to any third party when Recipe4Living.com, in its sole discretion, determines the disclosure to be appropriate. Recipe4Living.com may also use such information to fulfill orders, track shipments or for content improvement and feedback purposes. 

When visitors elect to become members of Recipe4Living.com, we request that they provide additional, personally-identifiable information. This includes your name, address, e-mail address, telephone number, age bracket, day and month of your birth. 

We may also provide new members the opportunity to receive e-mail from us regarding specific topics. Additionally, we may provide new members the opportunity to simultaneously join programs of our affiliates. When new members join affiliates, a link is provided to the affiliates' membership agreements; Joining the affiliates through Recipe4Living.com indicates agreement to abide by the affiliate's policies. 

All registrants may receive e-mail confirming their Registration, and subsequent correspondence. 

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by using the unsubscribe option on the bottom of every email. 

**How We Use Collected Data:**

From time to time, Recipe4Living.com may send promotional e-mail, postal mail, or engage in telemarketing based on the information members supply to us. By supplying information when you join Recipe4Living.com, you agree to receive these communications. 

**Children and Recipe4Living.com:**

We believe it is imperative that parents maintain an active relationship with their children interested in joining Recipe4Living.com do so through their parents only. We will not knowingly solicit information from minors, or permit minors to join our site. 

Recipe4Living.com does not endorse, nor is Recipe4Living.com responsible for the accuracy of, the Privacy Policies and / or Terms and Conditions of advertisers on this Website. The entities which advertise and place banner ads on the Recipe4Living.com website are independent third parties and are not affiliate with Recipe4Living.com. 

**Your Acceptance of These Terms:**

By using this site, you agree to the Recipe4Living.com Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use our sites. We reserve the right, at our discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time. All Privacy Policy changes will take effect immediately upon their posting on the site. Please check this page periodically for changes. Your continued use of the Recipe4Living.com sites following the posting of changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes.
