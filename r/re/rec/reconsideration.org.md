## Information Collection and Use by MEASURABLE SEO

MEASURABLE SEO collects and uses information voluntarily submitted by a user, such as a user’s name, email address, URLs, keywords, and credit card payment information, in connection with the services provided by MEASURABLE SEO on behalf of such user. MEASURABLE SEO may also share such information with its affiliated companies and business partners (such as agents or subcontractors) who are acting on MEASURABLE SEO’s behalf to support your customer relationship with MEASURABLE SEO, unless otherwise agreed by you or required by law.

MEASURABLE SEO may also log non-personally-identifiable information including IP address, profile information, aggregate user data, and browser type, from users and visitors to the MEASURABLE SEO Site. This data is used to manage the MEASURABLE SEO Site, track usage and improve the website services. This non-personally-identifiable information may be shared with third-parties to provide more relevant services and advertisements to visitors. User IP addresses may also be recorded for security and monitoring purposes.

MEASURABLE SEO may also use a user’s email address to send updates, such as a newsletter or news regarding MEASURABLE SEO’s products and services. Users may choose not to receive email of this type by clicking an “opt out” link located at the bottom of the update.

Unless otherwise agreed by you or required by law, information collected by MEASURABLE SEO may be (i) used by MEASURABLE SEO for marketing and business purposes, (ii) retained by MEASURABLE SEO indefinitely, and (iii) sold or otherwise transmitted to third parties.
