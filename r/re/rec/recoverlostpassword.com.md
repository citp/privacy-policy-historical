# Sitemap for SmartKey site - Find more password recovery solution faster

Privacy

General Statement

SmartKey fully respects your right to privacy, and will not collect any personal information about you without your clear permission. Any personal information which you volunteer to SmartKey will be treated with the appropriate standards of security and confidentiality, strictly in accordance with the Data Protection Acts, 1988 & 2003\. 

Collection and Use of Personal Information

SmartKey does not collect any personal data about you on this website or in connection with any other service, apart from information which you volunteer (for example by e-mailing us, by purchasing a product from us, by downloading a product from us, by registering your product with us or by using our online feedback form).  
Information which you provide is used by SmartKey only in line with the purpose for which you provided it and will not be made available to third parties, other than information provided to us during the purchase process which may be provided to our online billing provider and our banking institution to enable the purchase to be processed.  
If SmartKey has your email address, we may contact you by email to advise you of product updates, subscribe you to our newsletter or offer you products from SmartKey or third parties. You can opt out from receiving these emails at any time. SmartKey may also contact you in relation to customer support queries which you lodge.

Changes to This Policy

Please note that SmartKey may change this policy from time to time. Any amended policy terms will be posted to our website.
