# Privacy Policy RechargeTricks.iN

**Privacy Policy For www.rechargetricks.in**

Yes, Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:

1) To personalize your experience  
(your information helps us to better)

2) To improve our website  
(we continually improve our website offerings based on the information)

3) To administer a contest, promotion, survey or other site features.

 **Do we use cookies?**

We do not use cookies, but our parteners like paytm, flipkart, ebay, Google and others merchants uses cookies. If you want to dont access cookies to your browser so please goto your browser and disable cookies.

 **Do we disclose any information to outside parties?**

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising or other uses.

 **Third party links**

Occasionally, at our discretion, we may include or offer third party products or services on our website. These third party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. Nonetheless we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.

 **Online Privacy  Policy Only**

This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through our website and not to information collected offline.
