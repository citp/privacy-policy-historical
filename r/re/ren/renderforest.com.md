# Privacy Policy | Renderforest

Thank you for visiting Renderforest.com.

We highly recommend that you carefully review “The Renderforest Privacy Policy” before engaging in any activity this website has to offer.

The following Privacy Policy represents the limits that Rendeforest has set for the safety of its users regarding any privacy issues they might encounter. The policy stated below is implemented to maintain the safety of personal data that users voluntarily provide to us.

This privacy policy is part of our “Terms of Service” and applies to [www.renderforest.com](http://www.renderforest.com/) owned and operated by Wildek Entertainment.

Renderforest takes your privacy seriously. We will not sell, lease, or exchange your personal data with any third parties in ways other than described in our Privacy Policy. By using services provided by Renderforest, you are agreeing to be legally bounded by the terms of service and privacy policy.  

1. ** ** **Information we collect**

During the registration process, Renderforest will collect your first and last name, email address, and password. You must provide this information in order to obtain an account.  Renderforest agrees not to distribute this information to a third party in ways other than described in our Privacy Policy.

We may use the provided email address to send you service notifications, the release of a new product, or to ask you for feedback.

The personal information collected from Renderforest may be used to inform you about your purchase or if you want to purchase a certain product. We may also use this information to inform you about specific services that you may be interested in.

Your profile and edited videos will be available only to you as a sole user. 

2.  **Third Party Payment Processors**

If you choose to purchase any Renderforest services, we will assist in securely processing your personal identifiable payment information. Such third party processors who use your personal information are governed by their respective privacy policies which may or may not contain privacy protections in this privacy policy. 

3.  **Updates in Privacy Statement**

At any undisclosed time, our privacy policy can be updated. If so, these updates will be announced via website and all registered users may receive email updates if they opt to be notified regarding Renderforest privacy policy updates. You may choose to unsubscribe from Rederforest updates at any given time and can terminate usage of our site and services at your disclosure. 

4.  **Affiliate program, Contests and Promotions**

Rederforest allows all of its users to participate in any contest or take advantage of any promotion our website has to offer. If you agree to participate in our affiliate programs, contests, and promotions, you acknowledge that you may be obligated to share your personal information with us. Also, if you agree to use our affiliate program, any registered user must acknowledge that s/he voluntarily provided the email address of another person or party. This information can be used to contact and encourage this third party to use our services.   

5.  **Server Logs**

When accessing Renderforest web pages, we may log your IP address; access your URL, time of access, geographic location, browser type, and referral URL in order to identify users who are using the service for spam or other activity that is deemed in conflict with our Terms of Service. 

6.  **Social Media**

This website provides users the opportunity to interact and post content through various social media sites. Your interactions with such tools are publicly viewable outside of the website domain. Each social media site has its own privacy policies which may be more or less protected than our policy. If you do not wish for this information to be disclosed, then you should not use these online tools.

7.  **Security Information**

Renderforest will store all user information in secure databases protected in a variety of industry-standard access controls to assist us in protecting your data. However, no company can fully eliminate security risks associated with the transmission of personal information through online transactions, and you do so at your own risk.

If you have any questions about security measures on this website, please send us an email through our contact page.

8.  **Removing Information**

To remove your personal identifiable information from our system, it is recommended that you close your account. Your personal information will be removed immediately, although your actual login name will remain reserved on our system until re-collection.

9.  **Customer Support and Communications**

Renderforest will send you service-related announcements when it is necessary to do so. For example, we will send you an email if our service is temporarily suspended for maintenance.  Generally, you may not opt-out of these communications, which are not promotional in nature. If you do not wish to receive them, you have the option to deactivate your account. 

With your permission, we may periodically send you emails regarding new features or services that we are promoting to users. You also have the choice to opt-out of these communications at any time. 

Upon issuing a customer support request to Renderforest, we may use your information and email address to contact you regarding your inquiry.

10.  **Personally Identifiable Information**

Renderforest employs third-party service providers to perform such functions as: credit card processing, analyzing data and providing customer service. These service providers have access to personal information needed to perform such functions on our behalf and authorized to use your personal information to provide these services to us. 

We may release account and personal information when we believe it is appropriate to comply with the law or to enforce our Terms of Service, or protect the rights or safety of Renderforest and our users. This includes exchanging information with third parties for fraud protection and credit card risk reduction. 

Besides what was set out above, you will receive notice when personal information about you might be sent to third parties. In this case, you will have an opportunity to choose not to share your information. 

11.  **Cookies**

Renderforest may use cookies to improve our website and user experience. This will not, under any circumstances, affect your privacy or data security. Members who disable cookies will not be able to use the service, as they are required to maintain your login to our website within a browser session.

12.  **Disclosure**

Renderforest will not sell or lend your personal information to a third party.  We will only disclose personal information if we are required to do so by law in cooperation with a legitimate government investigation. 

13.  **Links to Third Party Sites**

Renderforest contains links or resources to other websites that are not owned or controlled by our website. These links are not endorsements to these external sites and those websites do not follow the same usage term or privacy policy as Renderforest.

We encourage you to be aware when you leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personal identifiable information. 

This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected by this Website. 

14.  **Legal Disclaimer**

Renderforest reserves the right to disclose your personally identifiable information as required by law and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on us, our subsidiaries, affiliates, employees or agents. 

15.  **Children under 13**

Renderforest is not affiliated with children less than 13 years of age and we do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from individuals under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information from our files.

16.  **Security and User updates.**

Renderforest may employ some safety procedures to help protect your information from loss, unauthorized access, alteration or destruction. As a registered user, you will be able to review, correct or delete your personal information from our website at any time.

17.  **Contact Us**

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our privacy policy, please send us a message through our Renderforest contact page. 

**This privacy policy is effective as of: April 7 2014**
