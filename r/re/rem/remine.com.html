2 Information We Collect

2.1 We collect information from a visitor when she or he accesses or interacts with the Website or Application. The manner in which this information is collected falls into two categories: (i) information that a visitor provides directly; and (ii) information that is automatically collected through a visitor’s use of the Website or Application.

2.2 Information you provide to us directly:

a. Registration Information: We collect the information that a visitor provides to us when activating an account. This includes identifying information about a visitor, such as name, password, [mailing address, phone number,] and email address. A visitor who has activated their account and who has used a password to access the Application is deemed an “Authorized User”. All provisions of this Privacy Policy that apply to a “visitor” also apply to an “Authorized User”. Some provisions apply only to Authorized Users.

b. Payment Information: The Website provides the ability to make online payments for access to certain Services. However, we do not directly access, collect, or store your credit card information. Our service provider processes this information in line with its own privacy policy and data handling practices. We are not responsible for the handling of this payment information by the service provider.

c. Electronic Communications: We may collect information that a visitor or Authorized User chooses to share with us via electronic communications, such as support or administrative requests. We use this information to understand and respond to a visitor’s questions and to improve our processes and customer experience.

d. Surveys and Questionnaires: We may invite visitors and Authorized Users to participate in surveys or to respond to a particular questionnaire. Participation in such surveys and questionnaires is voluntary, but may require that a visitor provide certain personal information, such as name, email address, or zip code. We will use this information for analytics purposes. 

2.3 Information automatically collected through your use of the Service:

We automatically collect information from visitors who use the Website or Application in order to understand how the Website or Application is used, and how to improve them. The information we automatically collect through the Website or Application includes:

a. In general: We record a visitor’s and Authorized Users IP address, the time of the visit, and information regarding the browser and operating system of the device used. We collect certain information about a the use of the Website, including actions taken on the Website, webpages visited, and time spent on specific webpages, and the like.

b. Mobile Device Users: If a mobile device is used to access the Website or Application, we may collect, in addition to the general information above, the type of mobile device used and other technical information about the mobile device (e.g., the manufacturer of the mobile device, its location, and etc.). 

2.4 Technology used to collect information through your use of the Service:

The technology we use to collect information automatically from visitors and Authorized Users includes the following:

a. Cookies: The information we collect using cookies allows us to monitor how many visitors are using our Website or Application and for what purposes. Cookies are also used to facilitate an Authorized User's log-in, as navigation aids, and as session timers. Cookies do not retain personal information about a visitor but do retain certain information about an Authorized User, such as to allow navigation from page to page in a restricted access area of the Website.

You may choose to block certain categories of cookies. Most browsers contain settings that allow you to control or delete cookies. These settings will typically be found in the “options” or “preferences” menu of your browser. Please note that blocking or disabling certain cookies may interfere with certain functionalities of our Service. Further, while disabling a cookie may prevent the future collection of information by that cookie, it does not prevent the use of the information collected prior to the cookie being disabled. For more general information about cookies you can visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org.

b. Embedded Scripts: Embedded scripts are used to collect information about a visitor’s and Authorized User’s interactions with the Website and Application, such as the links clicked. Embedded scripts are temporarily downloaded onto a visitor’s or Authorized User’s computer or mobile device and are deactivated or deleted when a visitor or Authorized User disconnects from the Website.

c. Third-Parties' Content and Widgets: Our Website may include third-party widgets and other content from third-parties. These third-parties may use their own cookies or web beacons to track the actions of visitors online over time and across different websites or platforms to deliver targeted electronic advertisements to a visitor. Please note that the Company does not have access to or control over the cookies and/or web beacons that third-parties may use. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-parties. For more information about how tracking works for online advertising purposes you can visit http://www.aboutads.info/choices. In addition, some third-party advertising companies may provide a mechanism to opt-out of their tracking technologies. For more information about this opt-out process, you can visit the Network Advertising Initiative website, available at http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/.
