##### Our Privacy Policy:

Remote Central recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of you as a user of this website. Remote Central does not collect Personal Information about you except for the information that you voluntarily provide (such as data submitted in contest entries, forum posts, emails or file submissions where you do not specifically indicate that the information contained within is to be considered confidential). All Personal Information is treated confidentially and is not released to any third-party entity. 

##### Third-Party Privacy Policy:

Remote Central uses third-party companies to serve advertisements. These companies may use non-personally identifiable information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice, or if you would like to opt-out of this type of advertising, please visit [networkadvertising.org](http://www.networkadvertising.org/).
