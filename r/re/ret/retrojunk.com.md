# RetroJunk.Com Privacy Policy

|  |  RETROJUNK.COM PRIVACY POLICY  
---  
Retro Junk respects the privacy concerns of the users of its Site. As a general policy, no personal information is automatically collected from users of our Site. However, certain non-personal information is recorded by the standard operation of the Internet servers. Information, such as the type of browser being used is gathered in order to customize your online experience.   
Personally-identifiable information that may be collected in connection with your voluntarily filling out forms, such as subscribing to our newsletter, or participating in other promotions is retained by Retro Junk and is not sold or otherwise transferred outside the company without your prior consent.
