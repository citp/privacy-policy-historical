Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to RetiringbyDesign.com. The following privacy policy applies to all users of RetiringbyDesign.com and discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices.

How is Information collected?
When you register your information with RetiringbyDesign.com, you will be asked for general contact information, such as your phone number and email address, as well as some demographic information. We use this data to send you more information about the communities that may be a good fit for you based on your specific interests and needs. We also provide this information to our registered communities, provided they are a good fit for you. By registering, you are granting us the right to provide this information to registered communities as well as granting us the right to receive communication from RetiringbyDesign.com or our registered communities.

We may automatically collect IP addresses and website usage information from you when you visit RetiringbyDesign.com. We use this non-identifying information to gather intelligence related to general market trends. This information helps us evaluate how our visitors and subscribers use and navigate our site.

RetiringbyDesign.com contains third party links. RetiringbyDesign.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these third party sites.

External Links
Please note that RetiringbyDesign.com does link to other websites that may collect personally identifiable information about you. This Privacy Policy only applies to RetiringbyDesign.com, and we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content for any linked websites. Please refer to their privacy policies for information regarding their policies.

Third Party Advocates
We recognize that our website is sometimes used by individuals on behalf of family members or other third parties. If you one of these individuals, you affirm that you are fully authorized to provide the information on behalf of someone else and that you have permission to forward the prospect’s information to a service provider.

Cookies
We use cookies to offer you a personalized experience of our site and to recognize your computer the next time you visit the site. Cookies are text files stored as plain text on a user's computer by a web browser. A cookie consists of bits of information, which may be encrypted for information privacy and data security purposes. Cookies are not viruses. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent to your computer.

Opting out
You have the ability to opt out from any and all communication from RetiringbyDesign.com. To opt out, visit Options > Account Information and follow the directions to opt out or unsubscribe. Or you can contact us at info@retiringbydesign.com.
