Please note that RGGB may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, and you should check this page periodically for changes. If changes are made, RGGB will notify users by stating the date the Privacy Policy change is effective. If significant changes to the Privacy Policy are made, it will be prominently indicated on the Site.

By accessing or using the Site or the Services you are accepting the terms of RGGB’s Privacy Policy.

•  communicating with you in general, including to provide information about the Site and the Services

•  enabling RGGB to publish content to the Site

•  developing new services

•  measuring interest in and improving the Site or the Service

•  otherwise customising your user experience with RGGB

•  soliciting information from you, including through surveys

•  resolving disputes or troubleshooting problems

•  enforcing our Terms of Use, and/or

We may collect information about your computer for system administration, including where available: your IP address, operating system and browser type. This is statistical data about our users’ browsing actions and patterns, and does not identify any individual.

RGGB may disclose and/or transfer your Personal Information to a buyer or transferee of all or a portion of its assets in conjunction with the sale or transfer of such assets; however, we will provide notice on our web site before Personal Information is transferred or becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

When you access the Site or the Services, RGGB’s servers may automatically track certain information about your use of the Site and the Services. This information may include your browser type and version, your IP address, any referring URL, any URLs you visit from the Site, and the time and date associated with, and the content of, any communications by, to or with the Site or the Services.

 **Cookies, Advertising and Links to other Web sites**

When you use the Site, a cookie may be sent to your browser. A cookie is a small text file sent by a website’s computer system that is stored on your computer’s hard drive. Cookies are a commonly used technology on the Web, and they are used in the Site’s functionality.

If you register with us or if you continue to use the Site, you agree to our use of cookies.

You are always free to set your browser to decline cookies, and you can delete cookies from your computer. However, disabling cookies may adversely affect your ability to use the Site or the Services. See your browser’s “Help” file for more details on cookies.

Cookies may be used to recognise you, as well as to track usage of the Site. These cookies are stored on your computer’s hard drive, and help to establish usage trends and to tailor website content and develop further enhancements. Advertisers and other third parties and/or affiliates’ use of cookies are subject to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to read those privacy policies.

The Site and the Services may contain windows or hyperlinks which provide access to other websites not affiliated with RGGB. Your use of an external website or any informational content found on an external website is subject to and governed by the terms and guidelines of that external website.

RGGB does not make any representations or warranties concerning, and will not in any way be liable for, any content, products, services, software, or other materials available on an external website, even if one or more pages of the external website are framed within a page of the Site.

RGGB is not responsible for the privacy practices of any external website. For your protection, please refer to the privacy policies of the respective external websites.
