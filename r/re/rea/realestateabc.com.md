| **RealEstate ABC Privacy Policy**  
---  
  
.  
  
.  
  
**Help-by-Email Forms**  
  
Any information you divulge on the help-by-email forms which include Ask the Experts, Ideal Home Search, and Listing Information Request, is not shared by RealEstate ABC, with the following single exception:  your email may be forwarded to one individual who is qualified to answer your question or provide the requested help.  
  
.  
  
**Add-Site Forms**  
  
No information divulged on any of these forms is provided to anyone outside of RealEstateABC.  We keep this information available in order to inform you of any major updates made to the web site and to provide you with any useful news or information.  
  
.  
  
**Advertising**  
  
There is very little advertising on our site because we don't like a bunch of banners all over the place.  However, IF there is an advertisement on a page, we cannot control whether they have inserted "hidden" cookies or anything that  may attach to a browser and monitor a web visitor's behavior.
