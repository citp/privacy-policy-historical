# Privacy Policy

**WHAT TYPE OF INFORMATION IS COLLECTED?**

**PERSONAL INFORMATION**  
We only collect personal information from visitors to our Web site when they voluntarily provide it to us.  
IE: We require members to provide us with information including, email address, username and password in order to login to the site.  
Please see our payment providers privacy policy with any other customer information concerns.

**USE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION AND DISCLAIMER**  
Users of the site agrees that the site may use any information that it gathers or collects about the member or visitor to include personal information  
that has been provided by the member or visitor for technical, administration, research and development, customer administration, age verification,  
marketing, promotional and advertising purposes.

**DISCLOSURE**  
We will not disclose or use any of your personal information except when we believe in good faith that the law requires it, or when we have reason to  
believe that disclosing this information is necessary to enforce any of our content or digital property.
