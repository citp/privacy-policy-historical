# RealTimeRPI.com Real Time Sport Rankings FAQs - A leading sports ratings and resources community on the Internet

_Information we collect_

We collect necessary information (email, college, etc...) for RealTimeRPI.com account members. These information will be only for security and internal usage. We'll never disclose these information to any third parites. 

Premium members are required to provide us their name, address, phone number, credit card information and e-mail address for billing purposes. Paypal, Google, or other selected credit card processors, also collect this information. To see their privacy statements, please go to their respective sites.
