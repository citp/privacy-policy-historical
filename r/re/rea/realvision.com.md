# Welcome to Real Mega - The Real Estate Megasource

Reference to [RealMega.com](http://www.realmega.com/index.php?domusr=) will also encompass any sub-domain sites owned and operated by RealMega.com, but not limited to [RealMega.com](http://www.realmega.com/index.php?domusr=), [NeighorhoodFind.com](http://www.neighorhoodfind.com/modules.php?domusr=),  [RealMegaMail.com](http://www.realmegamail.com/modules.php?domusr=),  [MegaFlashPage.com](http://www.megaflashpage.com/modules.php?domusr=), Free Community Flash Web Pages, [MegaBlogPage.com](http://www.megablogpage.com/) , Free Community Web Journals )  MNN [MegaNewsNetwork.com](http://www.meganewsnetwork.com/modules.php?domusr=), [MegaOrganizer.com](http://www.megaorganizer.com/) , and the [MegaFactor.com](http://www.megafactor.com/modules.php?domusr=) Newsletters. RealMega.com is committed to safeguarding your privacy online. Please read the following policy to understand how your personal information will be treated as you make full use of our many offerings. This policy may change from time to time so please check back periodically.

We do gather certain information that you provide to us - some passively and some actively - and we want to ensure that all users of the site are aware of exactly how and for what purpose that information is being used. RealMega.com believes that the information you provide to us is central to your enjoyment and successful use of the site. Our collection of information enables us to provide you with a personalized web experience, and assists our sales team and advertisers in efficiently reaching an appropriate audience. The information we request is the minimum we believe necessary to achieve this goal.

Some personal information is gathered when you registering a web page. During registration, RealMega.com asks for your name, email address,, zip code, occupation, industry and general information and interests. The more information you volunteer (and the more accurate it is), the better we are able to customize your experience. Once you register you are no longer anonymous to us - you are given a RealMega.com username and password, and you are able to take full advantage of Realmega.com's many offerings.

In addition to registration we may ask you for personal information at other times, including (but not limited to) when you enter a sweepstakes, contest or promotion sponsored by RealMega.com and/or our many partners; in the future, when you make a purchase using RealMega.com Shopping; when you setup a free webpage at RealMega.com; when you submit a listing for a webpage ; and when you report a problem with one of our sites or services. If you contact RealMega.com we may keep a record of that correspondence. RealMega.com also occasionally asks users to complete surveys that we use for research purposes. Wherever RealMega.com collects personal information, we make an effort to include a link to this Privacy Policy on that page.

**Information Provided and Its Use**

IP and e-mail Addresses \- Our site registers your IP and e-mail addresses. Your IP address is used to assist us in diagnosing problems with our server, to administer our Web site and to provide aggregate information to our advertisers. Your e-mail address, where voluntarily provided by you through:communicating with us via e-mail; registering , making a web page or subscribing to the newsletters may be used by us to contact you with information we believe you may find helpful or of interest. Users can always opt out of receiving future mailings from us (see below).

**Cookies** \- Our site uses cookies, which are pieces of information that most major Web sites transfer to your hard drive for record keeping purposes while at the site. Cookies, by themselves, do not contain any personally identifiable information. We use cookies to keep track of your selections on the site and to try to make sure that you don't see the same ad repeatedly. They are also used to deliver content specific to your interests, measure certain traffic patterns throughout RealMega.com and our network, and to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit the site.

**Contact Information** \- Certain areas of the site request contact information from users. We use the contact information you provide for marketing and promotional purposes. This information may also used to contact you under some circumstances. Again, you can always opt out of receiving future mailings from us (see below).

**Demographic Information** \- Demographic and profile data is also collected in some areas of the site. This data is used to tailor our users' experience at our site, directing them to content we think may be of interest to that user, and displaying content for which the user may have shown a preference.

**Financial Information** \- We may request financial information from our users, when purchasing from the RealMega.com store or from survey infromation.This information will not be shared with anyone outside of RealMega.com other than general information on an aggregate basis.

**Sales/ Advertising** \- Both our internal sales team and outside advertising firms may utilize the information you provide us to better tailor your experience at our site. Other companies that place advertising on our site may have the ability to assign you a cookie while you are accessing our site. We do not control these cookies, nor do have access to this information. Contact, demographic and financial information may be shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis.

**Opt-Out/Remove/Change/Modify** \- Our users have the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at any time. To remove, change or modify any information you have provided to us from our database, or to no longer receive our service, please make such a request by [e-mailing us](https://web.archive.org/web/20110601015350id_/http%3A//www.realmega.com/modules.php?name=Contact) or, to no longer receive communications from us, by replying to any e-mail you receive from us with the word "REMOVE" on the subject line. We will use all reasonable efforts to meet your request within seven (7) days.

Disclosure of Information: RealMega.com will not disclose any of the information you provide us, other than as set forth in this statement, except where we are required to do so by law or to protect the rights of RealMega.com. In addition to the disclosures set forth in this Statement, RealMega.com , may disclose certain information in the aggregate to provide more accurate information to our affiliates, partners, investors, advertisers and other third parties as may be necessary.

You should be aware that this site contains links to other sites. RealMega.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

RealMega.com , reserves the right to change or modify this policy at any time by posting such change or modification on this page.
