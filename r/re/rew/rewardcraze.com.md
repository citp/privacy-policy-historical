# Privacy Policy | RewardCraze

## Privacy Policy

### General

We are obliged to:

  * Access and disclose personal information to comply with applicable laws and lawful government requests.
  * Display advertising from third parties without any obligations on their privacy policies.



### Personal Information

  * Basic information upon registration
  * Preference information upon complete surveys and polls
  * Shopping information upon buying online through us
  * Additional information upon participating in special competitions
  * Misc information upon enhancing your profile



### Use of Information

We have the right to:

  * Communicate with you so long you have opted to receive emails from us with regards to RewardCraze updates, Newsletters, Third party Emails, and Surveys.
  * Capture data whether on RewardCraze or while visiting Third party websites through us.
  * Use data we captured for different purposes
  * Sell data only in aggregate form, without personal information



### Cookies

We will use cookies to facilitate:

  * Logging in to RewardCraze
  * Communication between RewardCraze and you
  * Administration of your account
  * Improve our services and Adapt to your needs
  * Collecting and Spending points



### Security

We offer the following:

  * A secure server when you access your account
  * A secure server when you complete surveys hosted by us



### Passwords

We assure you that:

  * Your account is password protected and never releases details as such to third parties, but only to the account holder.
  * However, we can’t be held responsible for any activity in your account in case you didn’t keep your account details secure.
