Information Collection and Use
RUE 21 is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. RUE 21 will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. RUE 21 collects information from our users at several different points on our Website, all of which information is voluntarily supplied by you, such as, for example, name, age, address, and e-mail address.

Registration
A user is given the opportunity to register on our site. During registration a user is required to give user contact information (such as name, age and email address). This preliminary information is used to determine if the user is a minor and whether parental notification is required. This information is also used to contact the user about the services on our site for which they have expressed interest. It is optional for the user to provide demographic information (such as income level and gender), and unique identifiers, but this information is encouraged so RUE 21 may provide a more personalized experience on our site.

Minor Web Users
Individuals Under 13 Years of Age
Rue 21 encourages parents and guardians to spend time online with their children and to participate in the activities offered on the site. No information should be submitted to or posted at Rue 21 by users under 13 years of age without consent of their parent or guardian.

Unless otherwise disclosed during collection, Rue 21 does not provide personally identifying information to any third party from our users under 13 years of age. When a user under 13 registers, he/she is required to provide the e-mail address of his/her parent or guardian and that parent or guardian receives an e-mail alerting them to that Registration. Registered users under 13 years of age may participate in activities upon registration, unless their parent or guardian asks that their registration be invalidated. No information collected from users under 13 years of age is used for marketing or promotional purpose outside Rue 21 and its affiliates, except as described in specific promotions.

In accordance with this Statement, RUE 21 may collect personal information from minors under age 13 in order to provide them with access to services and content on the RUE 21 Website.

Cookies
A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site. Once the user closes the browser, the cookie simply terminates. For instance, by setting a cookie on our site, the user would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our site. If a user rejects the cookie, the user may still use our site. The only drawback to this is that the user will be limited in some areas of our site. For example, the user will not be able to participate in any of our Sweepstakes, Contests or monthly Drawings. Cookies can also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our site.

Log Files
RUE 21 uses IP addresses to analyze trends, administer its site, track user's movement throughout our Website, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Links
RUE 21 Website contains links to other websites. Please be aware that RUE 21 is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other websites. RUE 21 encourages our users to be aware when they leave our Website and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by RUE 21 Website.

Contests, Promotions or Surveys
From time-to-time our Website may request information from users via contests, promotions, or surveys. Participation in these contests, promotions, or surveys is completely voluntary and the user therefore has a choice whether or not to disclose the requested information. Information requested may include, for example, and without limitation, contact information (such as name, email, and shipping address), and demographic information (such as the user's zip code and age). Any information provided may be used in accordance with this Privacy Statement.

In the event that RUE 21 offers contests, promotions, or surveys which may be entered by providing email and/or other information, whether on-line or off-line (e.g. via in-store entry forms), RUE 21 will provide entrants with the ability to chose not to receive future email from RUE 21.

Tell-A-Friend
The RUE 21 Website provides the user with a third party referral service for users over the age of 13. If a user elects to use our referral service for informing a third party about our site, RUE 21 asks the user for the third party's name and e-mail address. RUE 21 will automatically send the third party a one-time e-mail inviting them to visit the RUE 21 Website. RUE 21 stores the third party information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time e-mail. After receipt of the e-mail, the third party then has the option of visiting the RUE 21 Website, ignoring the invitation or requesting the removal of the third party's information for the RUE 21 database. We do not accept referrals for those who are under age 13.

Security
The RUE 21 Website takes several precautions to protect the privacy of our users' personal information. When users submit personal information via the Website, RUE 21 attempts to protect such information both online and off-line. To this end, RUE 21 has implemented certain security measures in an attempt to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of the user's personal information under our control. We may employ other companies and individuals to perform functions on our behalf. Examples include fulfilling orders, delivering packages, sending postal mail and e-mail, removing repetitive information from customers lists, analyzing data, providing marketing assistance, processing credit card payments, and providing customer service. Such third parties have access to personal information needed to perform their functions, but are not permitted to use it for other purposes.

If the user has any questions about the security at our Website, the user may send an e-mail to security@rue21.com.

Special Offers
RUE 21 sends all new users (with the exception of minors who do not have the parental notification described above) a welcoming e-mail to verify password and username. Established users will occasionally receive information on products, services, and special offers. We may send offers to selected groups of Rue 21 customers on behalf of other businesses. When we do this, we do not give the business your name and address. If you do not want to receive such offers, please e-mail us at security@rue.21.com. Out of respect for the privacy of our users, RUE 21 presents the option not to receive these types of communications. For further information, please see the Notification of Changes and Opt/Out Provisions section below.

Correction/Updating Personal Information:
If a user's personal information changes (such as your zip code), or if a user no longer desires to use our Website, RUE 21 will endeavor to provide a way to correct, update or remove that user's personal information provided to us. Such changes can usually be done through the Website or by e-mailing our Customer Support.

Choices:

  • As discussed above, you can always choose not to provide information, even though it might be needed to take advantage of some rue21.com features.
  • You can add or update certain information on pages. When you update information, we usually keep a copy of the prior version for our records.
  • If you do not want to receive e-mail or other mail from us, please e-mail us at security@rue21.com. (If you do not want to receive the User Agreement and other legal notices from us, such as this Privacy Notice, those notices will still govern your use of rue21.com, and it is your responsibility to review them for changes).
