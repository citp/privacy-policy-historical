Privacy

How Rubik’s protects your privacy

Rubik's takes your privacy very seriously and will not share your personal data with any other organisation. Rubiks.com is fully compliant with all statutory data protection rules and has taken every possible step to protect your data against unlawful access or misuse.

" All personal information collected by rubiks.com is securely stored and cannot be accessed externally."

Like all good websites, rubiks.com uses cookies to keep track of your preferences and choices and logs certain information about your visits to the site for statistical purposes. Your personal browsing habits will not be shown to or examined by anybody.

Any personal information that you provide to rubiks.com in order to become a member or purchase Rubik’s products will only be used for those purposes and will not shared be with anyone. All personal information collected by rubiks.com is securely stored and cannot be accessed externally. Any personal information that is no longer required by rubiks.com is securely destroyed.

To protect your personal information while processing payments this website uses a dedicated SSL (secure socket layer) connection to encrypt your data and ensure its authenticity.
