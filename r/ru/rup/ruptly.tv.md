# Ruptly

## Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Your personal data within the meaning of the Federal Data Protection Act (e.g., your name, your address, your date of birth) will therefore be handled with care and kept strictly confidential. All acts of data processing (collection, storage and sharing of personal data) on _ruptly.tv_ comply with German data protection regulations. This Privacy Policy relates to the use of your personal information on our internet platform _ruptly.tv_.

You may review this Privacy Policy at any time on [www.ruptly.tv](http://www.ruptly.tv/ "Ruptly") (the “ **Website** ”). You may revoke any declaration of consent for the use of your personal data at any time with future effect. For this purpose, or if you have further questions about our privacy policy in general, please contact us by e-mail: [info@ruptly.tv](mailto:info@ruptly.tv "Contact Ruptly").

  1. **1.          ****Controller**



The entity responsible for all acts of data processing in connection with the use of the Website (“ **Controller** ”) is _Ruptly GmbH, Lennestrasse 1, 10785 Berlin, Germany_.

  1. **2.          ****General** **Information**



During your visit to the Website, we collect and process your personal information by using two sources:

2.1.        the personal information voluntarily made available by you (e.g., as part of the registration and/or ordering process - see paragraph 3), and/or

2.2.        the personal information acquired by means of automated data processing (e.g. cookies or web analysis tools - see paragraph 4). The latter is used in particular for adapting and optimizing our services and content to your individual needs.

  1. **Registration** **and ordering information** **("Master Data")**



3.1.        In order to purchase any products on the Website, you need to register on our Website and establish a user account. Within the registration process, the following personal data is compulsory:

  * Username
  * Password
  * First and last name
  * Company name (if applicable)



3.2.        When ordering any specific Content you may be required to complete our order form asking for additional compulsory information:

  * E-mail address
  * Postal address
  * Payment details
  * VAT number (if applicable)



3.3.        We collect, store, modify and use the Master Data only as needed to establish, execute or terminate a contractual or quasi-contractual obligation between you and us. We will only transfer your data to other companies under the strict requirements of the Federal data protection law, such as in the event of a commissioned data processing.

3.4.        Establishing a user account requires your consent that all personal data made available within the registration process is saved to your account. You can access your account by logging in with your specified user name and personal password at any time. The additional data made available in connection with the ordering process will be saved to your user account, as necessary to create, execute or terminate the contract. We may also link the information automatically acquired (see paragraph 4) to your user account if deemed necessary to document the contract and the respective legal requirements (e.g. confirmation of the terms and conditions).

3.5.        We may use your data (e.g. your e-mail address) to inform you about our products similar to the products already purchased. Beyond that, we will use the data for our own promotional purposes and for newsletter distribution, only with your express, separate consent. You may revoke your consent at any time for the future.  We shall in no circumstances transfer your data to third parties for advertising purposes.

  1. **4.          ****Automated data processing**



**4.1.      ****Cookies:**

Our website uses so-called “cookies”, which are used to make our service more user-friendly, effective and safer. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer and saved by your browser. Data on your use of our Website (e.g., product search) will be stored in a cookie in order to retrieve it on a return visit to our website. In this way, we can identify you during a subsequent visit to our website and adjust our services to your exact needs. You can prevent or restrict the use of cookies in your browser. We should point out, however, that in this case you might not be able to use our website to its full extent. Some of the cookies we use are "session cookies" which are automatically deleted after your visit. Our cookies on your computer do no harm and do not contain viruses.

**4.2.      ****Google Analytics:**

4.2.1.    The Website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. (" **Google** "). Google Analytics uses "cookies" (see above) helping the website provider to analyze how users use the Website. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the Website is usually transmitted to a Google server in the USA and stored there. In the case of activation of the IP anonymization on the Website, your IP address will be truncated within any Member State of the European Union.

4.2.2.    Only in exceptional cases will the complete IP address be transmitted to a Google server in the USA and truncated there. Google will on our behalf use this information to evaluate your use of the Website, to compile reports on website activity and to provide other website and internet related services to Ruptly. Under no circumstances will your IP address be associated with any other data held by Google.

4.2.3.    You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser. We need to point out, however, that in this case you might not be able to use the Website to its full extent. You can also prevent the collection of your data (including your IP address) and the processing of such data by Google, by downloading and installing a browser plugin available on: <http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=de>.

**4.3.      ****Twitter** **Plugin** **:**

4.3.1.    On the Website, we offer the Twitter “re-tweet” function. This service is offered by Twitter Inc., Twitter, Inc., 1355 Market St, Suite 900, San Francisco, CA 94103, USA. You will recognize the “re-tweet” function by the Twitter logo and/or the “Follow-Button” on our Website. By using the "re-tweet" function, the page you are visiting will be linked to your Twitter account and made known to other users. This data is also transmitted to Twitter.

4.3.2.    Ruptly, as the provider of the Website, has no specific knowledge about which data is transferred to Twitter and what it is used for. For more information, please see the privacy statement of Twitter at <https://twitter.com/privacy>.

4.3.3.    You can change your privacy preferences in the account settings on: <http://twitter.com/account/settings>.

**4.4.      ****Other Social** **Plugins** **:**

4.4.1.    On our Website, we offer a number of other social media cookies that let you share/like/tweet etc. the content you are visiting.

4.4.2.    Ruptly, as the provider of the Website, has no specific knowledge about which data is transferred to the operators of these social plugins and what they are used for. If the respective social media service is operated from a country that is not a member of the European Union, your personal information might be transferred to such country. For more information, please see the privacy statement of the relevant social network before using such plugin.

**4.5.      ****Live Chat**

These cookies are set by our live chat client, supplied by Live Chat Inc., 405 North Street, Chapel Hill, NC 27514, USA. All cookies starting either __lc or lc_ are associated with the chat client and store information about any currently running chat sessions and previous chat sessions you have had. We use this to provide you with the best customer support possible.

  1. **5.          ****Newsletter**



If you have subscribed to our newsletter and have ordered future information about current offers around _ruptly.tv_ , you have expressly consented to our use of your name and e-mail address for advertising and information purposes in this context.

  1. **6.          ****Duration of storage**



We only store your personal data until the particular purpose for which it was acquired is satisfied or as long as we are legally obliged to do so. Once the particular purpose ceases to exist, your personal data will be completely deleted.

  1. **7.          ****Data Security**



We use appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against accidental or intentional manipulation, loss, destruction or access by unauthorized persons. Our security procedures are continuously revised based on the latest technological developments.

  1. **8.          ****Right to information and revocation**



8.1.        You have the right to information about all your personal data stored in relation to the use of our Website, including its source, recipients and the purpose of data processing.

8.2.        You are entitled at any time to revoke any declaration of consent given for the use of your personal data with future effect.

8.3.        To exercise these rights or if you have any further questions to our privacy policy, you may contact us at any time by e-mail at [info@ruptly.tv](mailto:info@ruptly.tv "Contact Ruptly").
