There are two ways we collect personally identifiable information: (1) information which you supply to us directly; and (2) information which we gather from your computer using cookies.

The cookies transmit this information back to the computers at our service; these computers are the only computers which are authorized to read such information. The information captured makes it possible for us (1) to speed your visit, keep track of items in your shopping cart, and provide you with recommendations for content; (2) to remember information you gave to us so you don’t have to reenter it each time you visit our website; and (3) to monitor the total number of visitors and pages viewed. Most people do not know that cookies are being placed on their computers when they visit our website or most websites because browsers are typically set to accept cookies. You can choose to have your browser warn you every time a cookie is being sent to you or you can turn off cookie placement. If your browser is not enabled to accept cookies you may not be able to use all of the features of our website.
