Information Collection and Use



Runaware enables users worldwide to evaluate different programs on the Internet. Our business concept is based on providing the service to our users free of charge. In exchange, the companies presenting their programs on the site have access to information about the traffic related to their own software.

The data collected on Runaware's site is forwarded to software companies that use Runaware to present their programs to the public. The data we collect can largely be divided into three categories - membership information, demographic information and detailed information taken from users who expressly request more information about certain software.

The demographic information comprises the number of visitors that have chosen to test particular software, when this evaluation took place and the length of time the user took to evaluate the software. No personal information or identifying details are issued.

If, on the other hand, a user requests more information about one or more software products, this information is forwarded to the party that can fulfill this request - usually the producer/ manufacturer or a local retailer. This voluntary information includes your name, country and e-mail. Membership information is primarily used by Runaware to help build the site around the needs of the user. Besides gaining unlimited testing times, those opting for membership also get a vote, which may possibly influence developments. By communicating with our members, we hope to develop the site's design and content.

All information submission is voluntary. If you have not expressly given your authorization, no information will be released. And, if you so wish, it is also fully possible to change any information you have submitted.

Cookies



Usage of cookies on the Runaware site are not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Should the user reject a cookie, that user will still have access to the site but will have limited access to certain areas and features.

Cookies are also used to track the interests of our users to enhance the experience on the Runaware site.

Some of our advertisers use cookies on our site. However, we have no access to or control over these cookies.

Log Files



IP addresses are used to learn more about how the site is being used and to gather general demographic information. IP addresses are not in any way linked to personally identifiable information.

Communication



Registered users of Runaware have the option of subscribing to a Runaware newsletter that will inform them of site updates. Users have the option of unsubscribing from this newsletter at any time.

Registered users of Runaware may also receive promotional and information via email from the Software vendors directly.

Runaware and Runaware partners will not contact users in any other way unless they expressly wish it.

Choice/Opt-out


Users may opt-out of having their information used by Runaware or Runaware partners when they register for the site.

At any time users may update their profile within Runaware to reflect changes in personal information or to deny usage of the information by Runaware or Runaware partners.

Runaware users will be notified when third parties are collecting their information. We do this to enable users to decide whether or not to opt-out of the services offered by the third party.

Users who no longer wish to receive our newsletter or promotional materials from our partners may opt-out of receiving these communications by changing their settings here “My Data”.

Updating Personal Information


At any time users may update their personal information by logging into their “My Runaware” page. They may change any and all information previously submitted, may add information and subtract information.

This will help ensure that user information is up to date and accurate.

External Links


This web site contains links to other web sites. Please note that when you click on one of these links, you are 'clicking' to another web site. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of these linked sites as their privacy policy may differ from ours.

Notification of Changes


If we are going to use your personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection we will notify you via email. You will have a choice as to whether or not we use your information in this different manner. In addition, if we make any material changes in our privacy practices that do not affect user information already stored in our database, we will post a prominent notice on our web site notifying users of the change. In some cases where we post the notice we will also email users, who have opted to receive communications from us, notifying them of the changes in our privacy practices.

We Protect our Customers


All information you submit to Runaware is protected with the highest security available. We adhere to the security regulations and legislation required to guarantee correct storage and issuing of information so that nothing happens concerning your personal details.
