# Runber.com: Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy has been created to demonstrate the high commitment to the privacy protection issue. The Privacy Policy applies to all the services and materials provided by Runber.com.

## Private Information Collection

Runber.com is limited to the services that do not require registering an account and provide any personal information. The creators of the Runber.com project provide this system as an open public service and encourage new audience to use the website. The administration of the website rejects collecting personal data in order to avoid abusive activities of any kind e.g. duplicate accounts, vandalism or other disruptive behavior.

## General Technical Information

Runber.com collects general log file information which may include visitor`s IP address. Servers log containing users IP addresses are not exposed for public information. The raw logs are stored for collecting general statistics and monitoring the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. This information may be provided to affiliated parties or other trusted companies for the same purposes on behalf of Runber.com.

Runber.com contains materials which are permitted for copying and distributing by GNU Free Documentation License. Changing of the content of the materials is prohibited.

## Links to Other Web Sites

Runber.com contains links to other web pages. Runber.com is not responsible for the collection, use or disclosure of information collected through third-party web sites and expressly disclaims any liability related to such collection, use or disclosure. The project creators are not responsible for the practices employed by web sites linked to or from the Runber.com nor the information or content contained therein. Links to other web sites are provided solely as a convenience. Accessing, browsing and interaction on any other web sites, including web sites which have a link to Runber.com, are subject to that web site's own rules and policies and the project creators recommend that you review the information collection practices of such sites before using them or providing them with information.

## Policy Amendment

By accessing or using the Runber.com user consent to Runber.com collection and use of the information as described in this Privacy Policy. Associated Content reserves the right, at any time and without notice, to add to, change, update or modify this Privacy Policy, simply by publicly posting such change, update or modification on the Runber.com. Any such change, update or modification will be effective immediately upon public posting on the project homepage. By continuing to access, browse, or use Runber.com or otherwise providing the information described above to the project, the user acknowledge that he or she have read, understand and agree to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy, as amended.
