  • General Questions

    Does Rudy’s cut kids’ hair?
    Rudy’s is for everybody! Rudy’s does not have special prices for youth haircuts. If you would like to book an appointment with a stylist who is great with youth, please call the shop you plan to visit for advice on selecting a stylist.

    How much is a haircut?
    Rudy’s service prices vary by market. Our service prices and descriptions are listed on each location’s page on this site.

    How much do you charge for color services?
    Color service prices depend on a number of factors, including hair texture and length, complexity of service, and the products and tools needed to complete a specific look. Your stylist will provide a cost for color services during consultation and prior to beginning the service.

    When is my stylist working?
    Stylist openings are available via our Book Now link. For more detailed information, please call the shop you wish to visit.

    I’m not happy with my haircut/color. Can I have it corrected?
    If you are unhappy with your hair service, you may return to the shop within seven days for a complimentary correction. Some restrictions may apply to color service requests. Please call the shop where you originally received the service to make an appointment.

    Whom do I contact about working for Rudy’s?
    Our current openings are listed on our Careers page. If you would like more information on a position, or you are having trouble with our application system, please use our contact form to send an email to our recruiting team.

    Can I purchase your products wholesale?
    Please send wholesale inquiries via our contact form to send an email to our product team.

  • Returns & Exchanges

    Online Purchases
    We are happy to accept returns for items within 30 days of order delivery. All return or exchanged items must be in their original condition. Refunds are credited to the original form of payment.

    To return merchandise, email webstore@rudysbarbershop.com for return authorization. Customer is responsible for all additional shipping costs for returns and exchanges. Rudy’s does not assume responsibility for reimbursement or compensation of returned packages lost in transit without proof of delivery to Rudy’s.

    Excluding the original shipping costs, refunds are processed within 5 business days after we receive the returned merchandise. You will receive a confirmation by email once your refund is processed.

    In-shop Purchases
    We want our clients to love their hair products, and we stand behind the product we sell. We will accept opened or unopened product returns with no time limit. Clients may receive a refund when they have the original receipt, or if we can find the purchase in our system. Clients without a receipt may receive shop credit for returned product at the current retail price.

    Gift Cards
    Gifts cards are only redeemable in our physical shop locations.  Not redeemable for cash except where required by state law
