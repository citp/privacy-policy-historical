Internet Privacy Statement for www.raymondjames.com

Raymond James Financial is committed to online privacy.

Visitors may browse the Raymond James Web site to learn about our many services, get delayed quotes and find career opportunities without giving any personal information. Any information collected on this site, i.e., the zip code entered for the Office Locator system, is used only to locate the closest Raymond James offices and is not kept in any format. Personal information is only made available when site visitors volunteer it through our Contact Us page, to request that a Raymond James Financial Advisor contact them. This information is sent to Financial Advisors through e-mail and is not collected in any other format, nor is any information including e-mail addresses or other data shared with anyone other than the intended recipient.

Web site visitor information is collected with the use of computer IP addresses on an aggregate, anonymous basis in which no personal information is associated with this data. This data is gathered through the use of Web server log files where site activity is stored. The information is used to help diagnose problems with our server, to measure the use of our Web site content and to administer our Web site. None of the information gathered by the Web server is associated with you as an individual. Although this contains no personal information other than the IP address, it may be used to track down an individual who is attacking or misusing our resources. Cookie files are used on the site to help visitors navigate the site more easily.

Our firm policy prohibits sending or accepting customer orders by e-mail. If you are a client, please do not send time sensitive information or instructions to execute transactions. These instructions need to be made in person or by telephone.

Raymond James offers online services via OnlinePlus to a limited segment of its client base. To protect our client information, users are required to log in using a unique user name and password. Raymond James uses encryption technology to secure client data as it is being transferred between computers and also has implemented a combination of computer hardware and software to keep unauthorized users from accessing information on Raymond James’ computer network.

This site contains links to other Web sites. www.raymondjames.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

The Raymond James Internet Privacy Statement applies to U.S. clients and Web site visitors only.

Raymond James Financial may change this privacy statement at any time. Any changes or updates will be effective immediately upon posting to this site.

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site or your dealings with this Web site, please contact me.
