# Privacy policy | Financial IT

Your privacy is very important to Financial IT, a Finnet Limited company.  This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Financial IT collects, and the ways in which it uses that personal information.

We gather and use certain information about users to provide products and services and enable certain functions on [www.financialit.net](http://www.financialit.net/). We also collect the data to better understand how visitors use this website and deliver them timely and relevant information.

Please note that we may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page regularly to ensure you are comfortable with any revisions. Your continued use of Financial IT website following the posting of changes to this Privacy Policy will be considered your consent to those changes.

Financial IT may collect and use the following kinds of personal information:

  * information about your use of this website  
  * information that you provide using for the purpose of registering with the website
  * information that you provide for the purpose of subscribing to the website services
  * information that you publish on our website
  * any other information that you send to Financial IT



Financial IT may use your personal information to personalize the website for you and enable your access to use our website services.

Where Financial IT discloses your personal information to its agents or sub-contractors for these purposes, the agent or sub-contractor in question will be obligated to use that personal information in accordance with the terms of this privacy statement.

In addition to the disclosures reasonably necessary for the purposes identified elsewhere above, Financial IT may disclose your personal information to the extent that it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defend its legal rights.

**Securing your data**

Financial IT will take reasonable technical and organisational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information. Financial IT will store all the personal information you provide on its secure servers.

**Other websites**

This website contains links to other websites. 

Financial IT is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third party.
