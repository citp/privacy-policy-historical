Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. We promise to protect your private information. This Privacy Policy describes the information we collect, how this information is utilized, with whom is is being shared, and your choices about disclosure.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully when using our website or services. Once you use our website, you are accepting the procedures as laid out in this Privacy Policy. This policy may change from time to time so you should check it frequently.

Collected tracking information

Rankpanel uses cookies when you visit our website or use any of our services. A cookie is a small file locally stored on your computer by your browser that contains an anonymous unique identifier. With cookies, we record session information, encrypt login information, facilitate access to the website, personalize the user experience, and optimize our marketing campaigns.

Also, our servers create logs for interactions with our website, which may include your IP address, referer, browser type, user agent, language, screen resolutions, time and date of your visit. This information allows us to trace usage within our website and improve our usability and product features.

Collected personal identifiable information

Rankpanel collects personal identifiable information depending on your level of use. Some features are possible to use without signing up. There, we do not collect personal identifiable information. When you do sign up for an account, you are required to enter personal identifiable data including your email, name, company, business address, and other information as may required by billing purposes. You can later on verify and edit the account information in the account management section of the website.

You can choose to log in via our site or use third party social networking site facebook.com. Should you choose to create your account using Facebook Connect, you will allow us to access and extract personal information you have provided on Facebook in order to create your account.

Collected usage information

Using our tools requires you to enter keyword, domain names, and other input data considered relevant in online marketing. We are storing and associating your input data with your account, so that we can provide personalization features, product improvements, customized support, fraud prevention, and detect terms violation.

Rankpanel uses the personal identifiable information and collected usage information to provide the service, to personalize the user experience, to facilitate billing, to prevent fraud, to improve the service offering, to provide support, research, and product development.

We do not share your personal identifiable information with any third party, with the exception of partners that are essential to provide the service (e.g. billing providers). When shared with such partners, they are not allowed to use such information for any other purpose. Under special conditions (e.g. complying with legal requirements, preventing fraud or abusive usage), we may disclose your personal information as required by law.

Information storage

We are using third party vendors to provide our services. This includes providers of hosting, hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to operate the service. We will make sure we are using appropriate and up to date security measures to keep your personal information safe from unauthorized access, tampering, or disclosure.

Web Analytics

Rankpanel uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies which are text files stored on your computer to help analyze your use of the website. The information generated by the cookie about your use of this website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to a Google server in the USA and stored there for analysis. Google and Rankpanel will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the website, compiling reports on website activities for website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage.

Google may also transfer this information to third parties where this is required by law or insofar as third parties process this data on behalf of Google. Google will never associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. You may refuse the installation of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser; however please note that if you do so you may not be able to use the full functionality of Rankpanel.

By using this website you consent to the processing by Google of data relating to you in the manner and for the purposes mentioned above. As a user of this website you hereby acknowledge that we accept no liability in connection with the obtaining, transmission, processing and analysis of the above-mentioned data for which reason no claims for compensation can be asserted against us.
