Site News

This Privacy Policy sets out how Rajah.com ("We" or "Us") uses and protects any information you provide when visiting the website. We are committed to ensuring your privacy is protected and any personally identifiable information will only be used in accordance with this policy. By using this site, you agree to our privacy policy below.

Access Logs
The Web Server stores logs of each request, in the form of an IP address, URL/file, User Agent and timestamp. This is necessary for diagnostic, management and site improvement purposes. These logs are purged from the server on a Daily basis.

Google Analytics
We use Google Analytics to track interactions - such as the number of visits, pages viewed, and geo location to help understand how the site engages with visitors, calculate and analyze traffic trends and to help maintain and improve the experience.

Google AdSense and other third party Ad Networks
Google AdSense and other network partners use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to rajah.com or other websites. The use of advertising cookies enables our ad partners to serve relevant advertisements to you, based on visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. For more information, click here.

Other
The rajah.com forums use cookies to store a user's login information on their browser so that the user does not have to log in each time they visit. This information is not shared with any external or third parties nor are usernames or passwords on the forums.

From time to time, rajah.com may hold contests or giveaways in which a user is required to enter an email address or other personal information for contact purposes. This information is used strictly for internal purposes to identify winners and to ship them prizes. The information is only stored for a maximum of one month and is not shared with any third parties. Please note that your name/nickname may be posted on the site if you are chosen as a winner and by participating in any contests, you agree to this.
