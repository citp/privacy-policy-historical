# About RadiologyInfo

## _RadiologyInfo_ Privacy Policy

A limited amount of information about your visit to _RadiologyInfo.org_ is collected by the [American College of Radiology (ACR)](http://www.acr.org/ "go to the ACR Web site") and the [Radiological Society of North America (RSNA)](http://www.rsna.org/ "go to the RSNA Web site") for use in measuring the site's activity and to gather broad demographic information for the purpose of evaluating the effectiveness of the site. ACR and RSNA will not disclose, give, sell, or transfer any personal information about your visit unless required to do so by law. This includes information sent via e-mail to the _RadiologyInfo_ Webmaster and information submitted via forms posted on the site.
