# Rap Basement - Rap Hip Hop Lifestyle Network - Content

**Rap Basement**   
is committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your   
privacy. Please read the following policy to understand how your personal   
information will be treated as you make use of Rap Basement's offerings   
and services. The following describes Rap Basement's information gathering   
and dissemination practices. To obtain information regarding the privacy   
policy of any of our affiliated web sites, please check directly with   
that web site. Rap Basementhas no control over the policies of its affiliated   
web sites. This policy may change from time to time so please check back   
periodically. In general, you can visit Rap Basement on the web without   
telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself.   
Rap Basement will never sell, rent, trade, loan or disclose your personally   
identifiable information to outside parties without your consent unless   
compelled by law or court order to do so. If you purchase a product or   
a service developed or owned by a third party through our website, we   
may provide to the product owner or the licensor the same information   
you provide to us. We may share non-personal, summary, or aggregate customer   
data with partners and other third parties. With your permission, Rap   
Basement may send you e-mail regarding our new products, upgrades available   
through our site, or select third party products. 

**Third Party Advertising**

We use DoubleClick and other third-party advertising companies to serve   
ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not   
including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about   
your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements   
on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest   
to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to   
know your choices about not having this information used by these companies,   
please click **[here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/optout_nonppii.asp)**.

  


**Third Party** **Cookies**

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party   
advertiser may place or recognize a unique “cookie” on your browser.

  


**What Information We Gather About  
You**

There are times that Rap Basement may need to gather information about   
you through our website. We gather such information to offer compelling   
and easily accessible online content in a manner that provides personal   
privacy protection as well. IP addresses are used by your computer every   
time you are connected to the Internet to identify your computer so that   
data (such as web pages you request) can be sent to you. We use your IP   
address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our   
Web site. We also use your IP address to help identify you and your shopping   
cart and to gather broad demographic information. A "Cookie" is an element   
of data that a website can send to your browser. Cookies are used to better   
personalize the content, banners and promotions that you and other visitors   
will see. Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart (if   
you purchase something through our site) and to make sure you don't see   
the same ad repeatedly. We also use cookies to deliver content specific   
to your interests, to save your password so you don't have to re-enter   
it each time you visit our site, and for other purposes. Furthermore,   
we may use outside ad companies to display ads on our site. These ads   
may also contain cookies in addition to those we use in other parts of   
our Web site. The cookies received with banner ads are collected by our   
ad companies. "Some customer data is shared with the advertising companies.   
There are a number of instances in which our website requires visitors   
to give us contact information voluntarily. Such voluntary disclosure   
of information is considered your consent to use the information in the   
manners we are describing in this privacy statement. We use customer contact   
information from the registration form to send you information about our   
company and promotional material from some of our partners. We also use   
this data to tailor our visitor's experience at our site, showing them   
content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the   
content according to their preferences. The visitor's contact information   
is also used to contact the visitor when necessary and shared with other   
companies who may want to contact our visitors. Our site uses an order   
form for customers to request information, products, and services. Through   
the order form we collect visitor's contact information (like their e-mail   
address) and financial information (like their account or credit card   
numbers). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders,   
information about our company, and promotional material from some of our   
partners to our customers. The customer's contact information is also   
used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary and shared with other   
companies who may want to contact our visitors. Financial information   
that is collected is used to bill the visitor for products and services.   
When you submit an order online, your credit card number is submitted   
to the credit institution for normal, routine processing. We do not share   
credit history or credit card information with other companies or third   
parties, unless compelled by law or court order to do so. This site contains   
links to other sites. Rap Basement is not responsible for the privacy   
practices or the content of such Web sites. From time to time, you may   
be offered the opportunity to receive materials or special offers from   
third parties. If you opt-in to receive information from these third parties,   
Rap Basement will, with your permission, share your name and email address   
with them. 

  


**Third Party Data & Data In The   
Aggregate**

Under confidentiality agreements, Rap Basement may match user information   
with third party data. Also, Rap Basement may disclose aggregated user   
statistics (for example, 40% of our users are female) in order to describe   
our services to prospective partners, advertisers, and other third parties,   
and for other lawful purposes.

  


**Supplemental Information**

This site supplements the information that you provide with information   
that is received from third parties. 

  


**Security**

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and   
alteration of the information under our control. Rap Basement is dedicated   
to network security, and has been a leader in the marketplace by housing   
all hardware and software at Exodus Communications, a nationally recognized   
co-location facility. For more information about our security measures,   
you may send an email to [**dave@rapbasement.com**](mailto:dave@rapbasement.com)

.   
However, due to the open communication nature of the Internet, we cannot   
guarantee that communications between you and us, and us and you, will   
be free from unauthorized access by third parties. 

**Choice/Opt-Out**

Rap Basement does not offer the option of opting-in for mailers, newsletters,   
etc. This site is intended as a factual gathering site. 

  


**Correct/Update**

You have the following options for changing, modifying or removing information   
previously provided to us through our website. email [**dave@rapbasement.com**](mailto:dave@rapbasement.com)

  
and in the body of your e-mail, describe to our administrator what you   
want to be done with your personal information.

**Your Acceptance of this Policy**

By using this site, you signify your assent to our Privacy Policy. If   
you do not agree to this policy, please do not access our site. We reserve   
the right, at our discretion, to change modify, add or remove portions   
of or the entire policy at any time. Your continued use of our site following   
the posting of any such changes to these terms means you accept these   
changes.
