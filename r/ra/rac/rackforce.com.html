Privacy Policy

RackForce Networks Inc. is committed to protecting and respecting the privacy of all our customers and visitors.

All information collected from customers when signing up is used for billing, administration, and customer relations purposes only and is kept confidential. We have taken security measures to ensure that our customers' information is protected against loss, alteration or misuse. All transmissions of private information over the Internet are encrypted using standard SSL and SSH protocols.

Any personal passwords or usernames given to a customer are their own responsibility. Customers must ensure that only authorized personnel access their accounts. If for any reason you are concerned about this, please contact us at abuse@rackforce.com.

When visitors use the website, information such as length of visit, the pages visited and the referrer are stored by us. This is for internal use only. Analyzing traffic to our Web site and Customer Service Center helps us improve our services and marketing strategies.

As you browse www.rackforce.com, advertising cookies will be placed on your computer so that we can understand what you are interested in. Our display advertising partner, AdRoll, then enables us to present you with retargeting advertising on other sites based on your previous interaction with www.rackforce.com. The techniques our partners employ do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address or telephone number. You can visit this page to opt out of AdRoll and their partners' targeted advertising.
