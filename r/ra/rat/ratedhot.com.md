# Rated Hot - The Best Adult Webmaster Resources

## Our Commitment To Privacy 

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested. 

**The Information We Collect:**

We collect no personable identifiable information beyond the basic information provided by the browser. 

However, other sites linking from our sites, may collect personal identifiable data, so you will want to ensure you read each site you visit's privacy policy. 

**How To Contact Us:**

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please [contact us](http://www.ratedhot.com/contact_us.php)
