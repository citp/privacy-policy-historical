Our Commitment To Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our every page of our site.

The Information We Collect

When you sign up with this site, your name, email address, street address, and billing information (credit card, ACH, or other) is stored securely in an encrypted database on our servers.

Securing Your Information

We utilize the most secure technologies to keep your confidential information secure, including but not limited to: HTTP (SSL channel encryption) between your browser and our servers, highly encrypted databases inside our datacenter (preventing unauthorized internal access at the datacenter level), physical security (requiring proof of ID, handscan, and access codes) to enter of cage within the datacenter, preventing physical removal of devices. Additionally, hackers are thwarted by the use of firewalls preventing remote access to this data (only available through VPN channels and secure database clients with proper digital certificates); and world-class intrustion prevention systems that block repetitive access attempts and log such attempts.

The Way We Use Information

We use the information to bill you and communicate with you. Additionally, we use your email address and password to give you access to this site.

How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information

You can access all your personally identifiable information that we collect online and maintain, either by logging into your account, or by contacting customer support and requesting that we send you a copy of all data in our records.

To protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections.

How You Can Control What Information Is Collected and Used (Opt-Out)

We do not share public or non-public (financial) personal information collected about you with third parties or non-affiliates, except for the purposes of processing your transactions with our payment gateway and merchant bank, or submitting an account to collections in the event of a disputed chargeback.

If you wish to opt-out of sharing personally identifiable financial information to third parties and non-affiliates per the Financial Services Modernization Act (GLB), or if you wish to opt-out of sharing creditworthiness information per the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA), please use the Contact Support form and choose the Privacy Category. We'll be happy to answer any questions you have about your collected personal information.

How To Contact Us

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please feel free to contact us via the contact form herein.
