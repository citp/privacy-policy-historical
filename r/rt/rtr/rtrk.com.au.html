Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

Our Directory utilizes cookies and other tracking technologies that may be provided by ReachLocal or its third party partners. A cookie is a small file that often includes an anonymous unique identifier and that is sent to your browser from a Web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. ReachLocal uses session cookies which expire upon the conclusion of your visit to the Directory. These cookies facilitate your interaction with our Advertisers sites.

In addition, ReachLocal and its third party partners may also place cookies that will do the following (Tracking Cookies):

  • Track the areas of the Directory that you are visiting for the purposes of potentially showing you targeted advertising when you visit other Web sites.
  • Track the Business Listings that you are viewing within the Directory for the purposes of showing you advertisements from that same business or similar businesses when you visit other Web sites.

You may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie or to block cookies. However, if ReachLocal or its third parties have deployed Tracking Cookies, simply because you have clicked the link to visit this Privacy Policy, ReachLocal has suppressed all of the functionality of the Tracking Cookies.If, however, you return to the Directory, Tracking Cookies may be dropped again, which may be suppressed again by clicking the link to visit this Privacy Policy. Please be aware that some features of the Directory may not function properly or may be slower if you refuse or block cookies.

You should also be aware that if you click on a link in one of the Business Listings that is for one of our advertisers or click on one of our advertisers sites, a cookie may be placed on your computer that enables ReachLocal to serve advertisements for such advertiser when you visit other Web sites and to track the effectiveness of those advertisements. You will be able to have such cookies removed by clicking on the link to the Notice of Marketing Practices on the advertisers site. These cookies will be in addition to any other cookies or other tracking technologies that may be used by the advertiser, if any, which will be governed by the advertisers own privacy policy.

Please note that we do not link the information in the Tracking Cookies or any other cookie to your Personal Information, to the extent you provide us with any Personal Information. Moreover, as previously stated, we do not obtain, store or maintain any Personal Information about you through the use of cookies (either the session cookies or the Tracking Cookies).
