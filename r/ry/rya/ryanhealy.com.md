# Privacy Policy

Gambit LLC respects your privacy. We will never rent, sell or trade your email address or physical address to any third parties. 

### You Acknowledge

By providing your name and email address and clicking a submit button you acknowledge that you have given permission to Gambit LLC to send you email communications, containing any content they deem appropriate and at any frequency until you so indicate that you do not wish to receive any further communications.

### CAN-SPAM Compliant 

ryanhealy.com is 100% compliant with the U.S. federal CAN-SPAM Act. Unless you are registering for a product purchase, you must confirm, via a confirmation email, that you (and not someone pretending to be you) have requested to be added to our mailing list.

We will protect your email address and promptly honor all unsubscribe requests. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking a convenient link provided at the end of ALL emails we send you. You can also contact us using the contact information made available at the end every single email or at the end of every single page of our website.

### **Our Commitment To Data Security**

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

You acknowledge, however, that in the event of theft or other unlawful activity by a third-party, wherein your personal information is obtained, you will not hold Gambit LLC liable in any way.

### Children

Gambit LLC does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 18. If children under the age of 18 have subscribed to his mailing list, provided a physical mailing address or otherwise used this site, they have done so in violation of the Terms of Use. If you are aware of children using this site, please contact us immediately so we can investigate the situation.

### General Data Collection

Gambit LLC gathers personal information about visitors to www.ryanhealy.com via several different sources including: (a) personal information that individual visitors provide when using or registering to use one or more of our services; (b) personal and contact information that visitors provide when voluntarily participating in contests, surveys, subscriptons and other promotions; and (c) through merchandise purchase transaction; (d) through aggregating tracking data derived by tallying and identifying users’ page views throughout our site; (e) by depositing tracking cookie; (f) by logging IP addresses; (g) by identifying browser type and through other technologies. Such information enables us to better tailor our content to readers’ needs and to better understand the demographics of our audience. 

### Changes to this Policy

Gambit LLC reserves the right, at any time, without notice, to modify this policy as indicated in the Terms of Use.

### Further Questions or Concerns

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies please [visit our contact page](https://web.archive.org/contact) or note contact information at the bottom of this page.
