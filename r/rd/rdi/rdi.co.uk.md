# Privacy Policy - Distance Learning | Online Study | MBA Degree Courses from UK University - RDI

**Security**  
Our strict security policy is followed in the processing of your personal data. When you submit payment information we ensure that your browser encrypts it.

**Information About Your Cookies**  
Cookies are frequently used on the Internet and will not affect your operating system. Cookies are used by websites to remember information about you at a later date. We do not hold any personal details that could be read or understood by others. It is possible to configure your browser so that your computer does not accept cookies. However, you may be unable to take full advantage of the site, if you do so.

**Applications**  
When you apply for an  course or service on-line, we will ask you for personal details required to process your application. This information will only be used for the intended purpose stated.
