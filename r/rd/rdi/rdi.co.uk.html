Privacy Policy

Privacy
RDI are committed to protecting your privacy whilst visiting RDI.co.uk. We will keep the information you provide confidential at all times.

RDI will not disclose your personal information outside the RDI Group and Partner organisations, and does not pass data to third parties for any other purpose. All personal information is handled in accordance with the United Kingdom's data protection legislation.

Security
Our strict security policy is followed in the processing of your personal data. When you submit payment information we ensure that your browser encrypts it.

Information About You Cookies
Cookies are frequently used on the Internet and will not affect your operating system. Cookies are used by websites to remember information about you at a later date. We do not hold any personal details that could be read or understood by others. It is possible to configure your browser so that your computer does not accept cookies. However, you may be unable to take full advantage of the site, if you do so.

Applications
When you apply for an RDI course or service on-line, we will ask you for personal details required to process your application. This information will only be used for the intended purpose stated.

Questions
If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy please contact us by emailing info@rdi.co.uk or telephone 024 76515 700.
