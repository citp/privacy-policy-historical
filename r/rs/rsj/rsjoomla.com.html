Privacy Policy

RSJoomla is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. This Statement of Privacy applies to the RSJoomla.com Web site and governs data collection and usage. By using the RSJoomla website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Collection of your Personal Information

RSJoomla collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. RSJoomla also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favorites.

There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by RSJoomla. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by RSJoomla for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the RSJoomla Web site.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through RSJoomla public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others. Note: RSJoomla does not read any of your private online communications.

RSJoomla encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from RSJoomla so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. RSJoomla is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of the RSJoomla and RSJoomla family of Web sites.

Use of your Personal Information

RSJoomla collects and uses your personal information to operate the RSJoomla Web site and deliver the services you have requested. RSJoomla also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from RSJoomla and its affiliates. RSJoomla may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

RSJoomla does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. RSJoomla may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, RSJoomla may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to RSJoomla, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

RSJoomla does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.

RSJoomla keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within RSJoomla, in order to determine what RSJoomla services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within RSJoomla to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

RSJoomla Web sites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on RSJoomla or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of RSJoomla; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of RSJoomla, or the public.

Changes to this Statement

RSJoomla will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. RSJoomla encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how RSJoomla is protecting your information.
