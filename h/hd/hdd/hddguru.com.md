# Privacy Policy

_Personal data_

We don't collect or store any personal data about our visitors. We don't store e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail. 

_Cookies_

We don't use cookies to gather any personal information. 

_Confidentiality_

Our website servers comply with strict industry security standards to prevent any unlawful access, change or manipulation of our website pages with the purpose of collecting user data. 

_Website ad services_

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html). 

_Rights of access_

Visitors can inquire about our server security as well as about advertising companies we work with. To do so, a written request should be submitted using our contact page. The information can be disclosed when legally required to do so, at the request of governmental authorities conducting an investigation, to verify or enforce compliance with the policies governing our website and applicable laws or to protect against misuse or unauthorized use of our website.
