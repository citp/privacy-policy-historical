Hy-Vee will take all appropriate steps to keep any personal information you share with us on our site confidential. Hy-Vee will not sell, rent, or give away personal information gathered from this site for use in selling other company’s products or services. 

Any information we do gather will be used to improve our relationships with customers. We may sometimes use e-mail to communicate with customers. If you get e-mail from us and would rather not be on our distribution list, let us know and we’ll take your name off. 

If you have questions about this privacy policy, just [send us a note](mailto:info@hy-vee.com).
