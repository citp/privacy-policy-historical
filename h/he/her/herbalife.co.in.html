Herbalife Privacy Policy

Last Revised: February 6, 2017

This Sites are hosted by Herbalife International of America, Inc. being Principal Company of Herbalife International India Pvt. Ltd. (“Herbalife”). It applies to all Personal Information (defined below) that is collected by Herbalife through its online properties and mobile applications (the “Sites”) where this Privacy Policy is posted. It also applies to Personal Information that Herbalife collects through offline methods, such as when an Associate submits an Associate-ship Application to us, or when an Associate or a Preferred Customer calls our Associate Services Department. Unless otherwise indicated, this Policy does not apply to an Associate’s collection or use of information obtained by the Associate directly from his/her customer.

Herbalife comply with the all relevant provisions including but not limited to the Information Technology Act, 2000, the Information Technology (Reasonable security practices and procedures and sensitive personal data or information) Rules, 2011 relating tof retention and handling of sensitive personal data and information etc..

The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to inform you of the kinds of information we may collect, how such information mafy be used, with whom such information may be shared, your choices regarding the collection, use and sharing of such information, your ability to access and correct such information and the security procedures we use to protect this information.

1. What kind of information does Herbalife collect?

Personal Information You Provide Us Directlyf

At different occasions, you may provide Herbalife directly with certain types of “Personal Information” (e.g., your first and last name, mailing address, city, state, pin code, telephone number, email address, credit card, and banking information). For example, you may provide us such information when using the Sites or when becoming a Herbalife Independent Associate (“Herbalife Associate” or “Associate”) or an Herbalife Preferred Customer (“Preferred Customer”). If you do not provide us with the requested information, in some cases we will not be able to provide you the service you have requested.

If you disclose to us any Personal Information of another individual, it shall be presumed that you have secured the written consent of that individual for disclosing such information to Herbalife.

Information About Your Device and Your Use of the Sites

We may also gather certain information automatically, such as Internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamps, and/or clickstream data to analyze trends in the aggregate, administer the Sites and improve the function and content of the Sites. We use standard internet technologies, such as cookies and web beacons, to collect this information, as explained below in the section on cookies.

We may also collect location information, such as your mobile device’s GPS signal or information about nearby WiFi access points and cell towers that may be transmitted to us when you use certain Sites. We will only collect this information with your affirmative express consent. You may opt out of the collection of this location information from your mobile device by adjusting the settings on your device.

Information from third party sources

We may receive additional information about you from public and commercially-available sources and other third parties. In some circumstances, Associates may share information about their customers with Herbalife with prior written consent from such customers. We may combine all of the information furnished to us about you and use or disclose it in the manner described in this Privacy Policy.

2. How does Herbalife use my Personal Information?

We use the information that is collected about you for a variety of purposes, including: providing you with the products, services and support you request; processing your transactions and shipping your orders; communicating with you about your account or transactions, changes to our policies and other afdministrative matters, or your questions and comments; to better understand your interests in order to provide you with promotional information, relevant content, surveys, questionnaires, and other materials, consistent with applicable law and choices and controls you may have; improving our products, services and operations; and ensuring compliance with our company policies and rules and the law, or as we believe is necessary to protect, enforce or defend the legal rights, privacy, safety or property of ourselves and others. We also use this information to validate sales made by Herbalife Associates and for Herbalife’s compliance purposes. Herbalife or its authorized third parties may contact you directly for these purposes.

Information about Preferred Customers may also be used to support, calculate and track your product discount and to provide you with information on special promotions. Information about Herbalife Associates may also be used to support, calculate and track discounts, earnings and bonuses, to issue payment to you and report income to taxing authorities, to provide training, to maintain the proper functioning of the Sales & Marketing Plan, and to ensure compliance with applicable law and company policies, plans and agreements, including the Associate-ship Application and Agreement.

3. With Whom does Herbalife Share my Information?

Affiliates and Partners

We may share your information if required with our relevant affiliates (companies that control, are controlled by, or are under common control with, Herbalife) as well as select partners on need to know basis. These entities may use your information to make predictions about your interests and may provide you with special offers, promotions, advertisements, and other materials.

Vendors & Service Providers

We may share your information as and when required with vendors that perform functions on ourf behalf. Examples of such functions include fulfilling orders, delivering packages, email administrative functions, processing credit card payments, and providing customer service.

Herbalife Independent Associates

If you are an Herbalife Associate or Preferred Customer, we may share your information with other Herbalife Associates, including as part of a Lineage Report which contains information on other Associates and Preferred Customers in an Herbalife Associates’s downline. (The downline organization consists of all Associates and Preferred Customers that were personally sponsored by a particular Associate, and in turn, all other persons sponsored by the subsequent Associate.) We have taken appropriate measures to protect the above mentioned data in accordance with applicable laws. Lineage Reports are provided to Associates in strict confidence and for the sole purpose of supporting the Associates in further developing their Herbalife businesses.

If you are a customer, we may share your information with other Associates to provide you with products and services you have requested. For example, if you use some of our Sites, such as GoHerbalife.com, your information may be shared with one or more Associates, such as an Associate who would be able to service your order. Similarly, if you participate in a Nutrition Club, we may share your information with any of the Associates operating the Nutrition Club. Herbalife Associates who receive your information are bound by the terms of their Associate-ship Agreement and the privacy and data protection covenants contained therein. Some of these Distributors Associates may be based in countries that do not offer the same or a similar level of privacy protection as is available in your own country. However, Herbalife will take reasonable measures to ensure that your information is kept in confidence.

Legal & Compliance Disclosures

We may also disclose your Personal Information:

  • as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, legal proceedings, or similar legal process, including disclosure to authorized third party auditors; or
  • when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate or prevent fraud, or respond to a government request.

Business Transitions

We may share your Personal Information if Herbalife is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of or transfer of assets, or in the unlikely event of bankruptcy.

Other Parties in Aggregated Form

We may share your information with third parties in aggregated or de-identified form.

Other Parties with Your Consent

In addition to the sharing described in this Privacy Policy, we may also share information about you with other third parties in other ways only when you consent to such sharing in writing.

If you are an Herbalife Associate, a Preferred Customer or a customer of an Herbalife Associate, Herbalife may communicate with you via email, postal mail, telephone or other means on a regular basis to provide requested products and services and/or in regards to issues relating to your Herbalife business (if you are an Associate).

9. Cookies and Tracking.

Herbalife, as well as certain third parties that provide content and other functionality on our Sites, use cookies, web beacons and other similar technologies for various purposes, including to analyze trends, administer the website, track users’ movements around the website and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole.

Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored on computer hard drives. A cookie enables the entity that put the cookie on your device to recognize it across different websites, services, devices, and browsing sessions. For example, we may use cookies to recognize you when you return to the Sites in order to provide you with a better user experience. We may allow third parties to use cookies on the Sites but we do not control the use or contents of third party cookies. Web browsers often allow you to configure the browser to accept all cookies, reject all cookies, or notify you when a cookie is sent. If you elect to reject cookies, please note that you may not be able to take full advantage of the features and functions of the Sites. To learn more about cookies and how to manage them, please click here

Web beacons and similar technologies are small bits of code, which are embedded in web pages, advertisements, and e-mail, that communicate with third parties. We use web beacons, for example, to count the number of users who have visited a particular web page, to deliver or communicate with cookies, and to understand usage patterns. We also may include web beacons in e-mails to understand whether messages have been opened, acted on, or forwarded.

There are other local storage and internet technologies, such as Local Shared Objects (also referred to as “Flash cookies”) and HTML5 local storage that operate similarly to the cookies discussed above. Please note that these technologies are distinct from cookies, and you may not be able to control them using standard browser tools and settings. For information about managing Flash cookies, please click here
