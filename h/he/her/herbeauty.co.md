# Privacy Policy | Her Beauty

Her Beauty does not sell or share personally identifiable data to any third parties whatsoever.

Information collected by Her Beauty about visits to the site in Her Beautyâ€™s generic web logs will not be shared with others, except for the use of non-identifying and aggregated information to better the design of Her Beauty or attract advertisers.

Her Beauty uses Google Analytics and Google Webmaster tools to learn about traffic to the site and improve the usability of the site.

Private information shared with Her Beauty through contact form or email or in any other method will remain private unless permission is provided to share the information, or the information is demanded through an order from the Courts or a specific legal authority of State or Federal Government with proper warrant or subpoena.

Her Beauty does not target nor purposefully or knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

Her Beauty contains links to other websites which have their own privacy practices, and this privacy policy only applies to the pages of Her Beauty, and not to any other site which may be linked to by this site.

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this privacy policy, please feel free to [Contact Us](http://herbeauty.co/contact-us/).
