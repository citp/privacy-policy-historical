# Herman Cain: T*H*E New Voice

  
Welcome To the Herman Cain Online Store. Please take a moment and review the following which directs your use and purchase of products from our site. 

**Site Content**  
All content, including images and written materials, presented on our site is controlled by the THE New Voice. Any reproduction including copying, publishing, modifications, and distributions of material from this site is strictly prohibited. This site is intended for personal use only. 

**Privacy Policy**  
This Privacy Policy covers any personal information acquired from you when items or services are purchased from our site. 

    **Personal Information:**  
Personal information is collected when users purchase merchandise from the THE New Voice. You have to supply a billing address, telephone number, email address, credit card information, shipping address, and shipping instructions (optional). 

Your personal information is collected and saved, and you will be notified of special events and services offered only to registered shoppers. These services include, but are not limited to, receiving electronic newsletters, answering surveys, purchasing items, and entering contests and other promotional opportunities provided. We may receive personal usage and demographic information from our third-party partners, and we also collect e-mail addresses from users who send us support mail or request technical or other help. This information enables us to deliver the services you have requested and improve our website. 

    **Credit Card and Personal Information Security**  
THE New Voice places a high priority on the security of your personal information. Our e-commerce host, Nova Network, uses Secure Server Software (SSL) which encrypts your personal information when transmitted over the internet. SSL renders your personal information useless by scrambling it. Your personal information is stored on a secured server, safe from unauthorized access. 

**Shipping**  
THE New Voice ships orders via UPS ground shipping (Allow 3-5 business days). 

**Standard Shipping Fees:**  
Shipping an item via UPS (Allow 3-5 business days) is $5.00. For each additional item up to 8, add $0.50. 

For overnight delivery orders, please place your order by sending an email to: karleen@hermancain.org 

**Return Policy**  
We are always more than willing to do anything to satisfy a customer. We also try to make your purchase as easy as possible for you. However, there are no returns allowed on our books, tapes and DVDs. 

For customer service inquiries, Email us at: [karleen@hermancain.org](mailto:karleen@hermancain.org)
