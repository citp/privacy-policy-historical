Tourism British Columbia respects the privacy of our customers. As a result, we pledge to never release your personal information (email, address, etc.) to any outside agency. We will only use this information to serve you better. We may from time to time, with your permission, send you information we feel will be of use to you. If you prefer not to receive this type of information, just let us know by updating your user profile. We will take you off our list and not contact you again.
