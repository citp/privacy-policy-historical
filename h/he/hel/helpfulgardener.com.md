# Helpful Gardener - Privacy Policy

The Helpful Gardener respects your privacy. We do not use your email for any marketing efforts, and all email addresses submitted to us are kept in strict confidence and never shared with any third parties.

Cookies  
We use cookies in our forum as part of the normal way of identifying you for the purpose of giving you private access to your Private Mail box, as well as allowing you to post under your chosen username. We do not use cookies to personally identify you.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, then please visit this URL http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

The Helpful Gardener respects your privacy. Should you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please email us: admin at helpfulgardener.com
