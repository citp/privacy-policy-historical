# About - Hellonetwork.com

Hellonetwork.com will treat our visitors with the utmost care and respect. We do not collect email addresses for advertising, nor do we buy email addresses or send SPAM. 

Anytime you purchase a product or service on our site, a high level of encryption is used to prevent fraud or theft. If services or products are purchased through links to an outside company, please refer to that company's policies. We will only use the most reputable vendors. Neither representatives from Hellonetwork.com nor our partners will ask for credit card or financial information through emails. 

Everything on our site will be suitable for the entire family. Sometimes we will link to places of interest in a particular city. We periodically review this site to make sure the content is appropriate for all ages. Occasionally, links to sites may lead to unsuitable content if that website has changed its content. If you observe this, please send us an email with the city site and link so we can remove it. 

Cookies: Hellonetwork.com may use cookies. Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to an individual's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. For more information about cookies, please visit www.allaboutcookies.org/cookies/ 

Third-Party Cookies: We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit Hellonetwork.com. These companies may use information about your visits to Hellonetwork.com (not including your name, address, or other personal information) to provide advertisements on Hellonetwork.com, other websites and other forms of media about goods and services that may be of interest to you. In providing advertisements, a unique third-party cookie may be placed on your browser. We do not control nor do we have access to these cookies.
