At hellopeter.com, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect about you to process reviews, comments and subscriptions and to provide a more personalized experience. Please read on for more details about our privacy policy.

The information we collect …

This site collects information about you, including your e-mail address. In addition, information you agree to provide us when registering for subscriptions, such as your name, title, company names, address, telephone number and fax number is collected and retained. We may verify or combine this information with data about you that we have gathered from other sources or may already have in our files.

What information do we collect? How do we use it?

  • When you subscribe, we need to know your name, e-mail address and mailing address. This allows us to process and fulfil your subscription.

  • When you submit a customer comment, we ask for your e-mail address and telephone number.

  • When you enter a competition or other promotional feature, we may ask for your name, address, and e-mail address so we can administer the contest and notify winners.

  • We personalise your web site experience by using your comments and reviews to shape our recommendations about the services and products that might be of interest to you. We also monitor customer traffic patterns and site usage to help us develop the design and layout of the web site.

  • We may also use the information we collect to notify you about important functionality changes to the Web site, new hellopeter.com services, and special offers we think you'll find valuable.

  • From time to time, we may be approached by third parties with an interest in identifying hellopeter.com visitors.

We may provide our mailing list to such third parties if we believe you would be interested in hearing from them.

Controlling third party access to your information …

  • At all times, you make the decision how much information you feel comfortable providing.

  • You always have the option to be excluded from third party use of your information. The personal information, which Peter Cheales collects, and records is clearly indicated at the time that you type the information into your browser or e-mail you send to us.

When you place comments, subscribe, or access your account information, we offer the use of a secure server. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us. Furthermore, all of the customer data we collect is protected against unauthorised access.

You may update or change information related to your Peter Cheales account by accessing your hellopeter.com subscriptions section of the Web site with your e-mail account and password. For other questions related to updating or changing your account information, please send e-mail to peter@hellopeter.com.

By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Peter Cheales.
