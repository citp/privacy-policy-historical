It is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy frequently and remain informed about any changes to it, so we encourage you to visit this page often. 

### What Information Is Collected?

"Personally Identifiable Information" means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, home or other physical address, an email address, phone number or other contact information, whether at work or at home. JN PROJECTS INC also automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookie information and the page you request. You can choose not to provide us with certain information, but then you might not be able to take advantage of many of our features 

### How Is Such Information Used?

If you choose to opt into HelloFax updates, we will email you technical updates or inform you about other services and features. 

### Is The Information Shared or Disclosed?

JN PROJECTS INC does not rent, sell or share personal information it collects about you to or with third parties. Information collected from you is only used to complete and support your purchases from and use of the JN PROJECTS INC Site and to comply with any requirements of law. JN PROJECTS INC may disclose personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (1) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on JN PROJECTS INC or this web site; (2) protect and defend the rights or property of JN PROJECTS INC; or (3) act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of JN PROJECTS INC, its web sites or the public. If JN PROJECTS INC should ever file for bankruptcy or merge with another JN PROJECTS INC, we may sell the information you provide to us on this Site to a third party or share your Personally Identifiable Information with any company with whom we merge. 

### Access.

If you would like to correct, amend or delete any information on the site, you can either change your personal preferences on the "My Account" page or email us at support@hellofax.com.

Personal Information collected on this web site may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country in which JN PROJECTS INC or its affiliates, subsidiaries or agents maintain facilities, and by using this web site, you consent to any such transfer of information outside of your country.

Any person who provides their information to JN PROJECTS INC through the Account Login page for new customers, Signup Page, or any other part of the JN PROJECTS INC Site represents to JN PROJECTS INC that they are 13 years of age or older. 

### Cookies.

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser in your computer when you visit our Site. We use cookies to improve our Site and make it easier to use. Cookies permit us to recognize users and avoid repetitive requests for the same information. We mostly use "session cookies" that are automatically deleted after each visit. Cookies from our Site can not be read by other Sites. Most browsers will accept cookies until you change your browser settings to refuse them.
