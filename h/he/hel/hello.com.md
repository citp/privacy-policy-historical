# hello.com - Help

**Overview**

At Hello.com, we respect your privacy. We have created this privacy policy and statement to demonstrate our commitment to protecting the privacy of our members and we are disclosing our policy to you so that you can be aware of our information and gathering practices. 

Although we collect information about you, we will not knowingly disclose personal identifying information about you to third parties without first obtaining your consent. In addition, we will not knowingly collect personal identifying information from children under the age of 13.

**1\. What general and personal information do we gather about you?**

> Hello.com collects a variety of general and personal information about you. We collect general information about you when you visit our web site. This information includes technical information related to your computer and the manner in which you are accessing our site and includes such information as the internet protocol (IP) address of your computer, what operating system your computer is using, what browser software (e.g., Explorer, Netscape or other) your computer is using, and who your internet service provider is, the Uniform Resource Locator ("URL") of the web site from which you just came and the URL to which you go next and certain operating metrics, such as the amount of time you use our service. 
> 
> In addition, during your registration process with Hello.com, we may ask you to provide us with personal information including, but not limited to, your name, address, city, state, zip code, birth date, phone number, credit card number and email address. Once you are a Member, from time to time, we may ask you to update your personal information so that we may offer more personalized services to you.

**2\. How is the information used?**

> Hello.com uses your information to monitor general use of our service and to provide personalized service to you and to better serve you, our advertisers and our sponsors. 
> 
> The general information that we gather about you is aggregated with general information of all users to help us better understand how our service is used. In addition, we aggregate the general information and the personal information. We compile and analyze this aggregated information so that we can provide more meaningful information to advertisers and sponsors and more relevant content and advertisements to you. 
> 
> Hello.com cooperates with law enforcement agencies to identify those who use our services for illegal activities. Hello.com reserves the right to release information about our Members or any user of our Service who we believe is in violation of our Terms of Service and End User Agreement, even without a subpoena, warrant or court order. We also reserve the right to report to law enforcement agencies any activities that we reasonably believe may be unlawful.

**3\. With whom do we share the information?**

> Hello.com will not use or disclose your personal information with any third party without your prior express consent through the opt-in process described below, except (i) as necessary to provide the Service, (ii) as required by law, governmental authority or court orders, (iii) in order to cooperate with law enforcement authorities or governmental regulatory bodies or (iv) as we deem necessary to enforce our rights under the Terms of Use Agreement or this Privacy Policy. 
> 
> **Please note that in order to offer you the Hello.com service for free, we must have your consent to disclose your personal information to third party advertisers and sponsors who may contact you directly regarding product or service offerings. If you do not opt-in to authorize this disclosure, we may not be able to offer you the Hello.com service for free. For more information on what may happen if we are unable to offer you the service for free, see Paragraph 4 below.**
> 
> We may contain links to other sites from our web site. Hello.com does not control the content or practices of any other site, including what information those sites collect about you. Accordingly, we do not endorse or hold ourselves responsible for the privacy policies of any site that you may link to from our site. In addition, you may choose to respond to advertisements that are posted on our web site or to direct marketing offers from third party sponsors or advertisers. We do not control the content or practices of our sponsors or advertisers and, accordingly, we do not endorse or hold ourselves responsible for the privacy policies of any third party sponsors or advertisers who may collect information about you. 
> 
> Hello.com provides an opportunity for a user to refer you to become a Member of our Service. In order for the person who refers you to get credit for referring you as a Member, you must properly register for the Service and download the Software. We may provide your first and last name to the person who referred you so that they may confirm whether they have received credit for referring you. However, we will not disclose additional personal information to the person who referred you other than your first and last name.

**4\. What choices do you have regarding the collection and use of information about you?**

> During the registration process, users have the choice of opting-in or electing to receive direct marketing materials from third parties. A user who becomes a Member and who opts-in to his service is expressly consenting to allowing Hello.com to share any of the Member's personal information with the third parties who provide such offers. Members may elect to change or remove their selections within the opt-in process or the opt-in choice by modifying their Member Account information; provided, however, that Hello.com shall be entitled to a commercially reasonable time after receipt of such election or modification to implement the changes. For more information on how to change or remove your selections or Member Account information, please refer to http://www.hello.com/signup/form_register.jsp. 
> 
> As mentioned above, in order to offer you the Hello.com service for free, we must have your consent to disclose your personal information to third party advertisers and sponsors who may contact you directly regarding product or service offerings. If you do not opt-in to authorize this disclosure, we may not be able to offer you the Hello.com service for free. If we are unable to offer you the Hello.com service for free because you have not opted-in to receive direct marketing materials from third parties, then we will attempt to contact you through your voice or email account. service. If we are successful in contacting you, then we will give you five business days to decide whether you would like to opt-in to receive such materials, agree to pay a subscription fee or agree to allow us to terminate your service. If, after three (3) attempts to contact you at the voice or email address specified for your Member Account, we are do not receive a response from you, then we reserve the right to terminate your service.

**5\. What are "Cookies" and how are they used?**

> A "Cookie" is a very small text file placed on your hard drive by a web server. 
> 
> We use cookies for a variety of reasons. For example, a cookie may be used to identify each Member who uses the Service and allow us to obtain session information for our Members. We may use this information to deliver a more personalized service to you In addition, cookies allow advertisers and merchants to track responsiveness to their marketing efforts and to process transactions at their online stores. Thus, we allow advertisers or partners to set cookies, but only if they agree to comply with our privacy policy. 
> 
> Please note that, in addition to allowing you to accept all cookies, your internet browser software may also allow you to be warned before accepting a cookie or to reject all cookies. Our Service is dependent on the acceptance of cookies by our Members. Therefore, if your browser is not set to accept cookies, you will be unable to properly use our Service.

**6\. How do we protect the privacy of children under the age of 13?**

> In an effort to comply with the Children's Online Privacy and Protection Act, and to protect the privacy of anyone under the age of 13 years, Hello.com does not knowingly allow children under the age of 13 to register for or use the Service, the Site or the Software.

**7\. How can you access and change or delete information about you?**

> You can access your personal information for the purpose of changing or deleting such information by selecting "My Account" on the navigation bar and then further selecting "Member Information" from the choices provided. 
> 
> You can terminate your account and delete your personal information from our online servers by sending an email to unsubscribe@hello.com and following the process set forth in the response to your email. 
> 
> You may choose to opt out of receiving promotional materials from Hello.com or any other third party that you have agreed to receive such materials from at any time by nopromos@hello.com. Please note that, as discussed in paragraph 4 above, if you opt out of receiving such materials, we may not be able to provide the Hello.com service to you free of charge. 

**8\. Amendments to our Privacy Policy.**

> We may, from time to time, amend our Privacy Policy. If we do, then we will post such amendments to this document so that you may always have access to our most recent policy. We reserve the right, at our discretion, to change, modify, add or remove portions of our Privacy Policy at any time and from time to time. 
> 
> This Privacy Policy has been modified once its original posting date of June 19, 2000.

**9\. Application of U.S. Law**

> Because we are committed to protecting your privacy, personal data relating to an identified or indentifiable individual which is submitted to this site will be collected, processed, stored, and disposed of in accordance with applicable U.S. Law. However, U.S. law may not provide the degree of protection to personal data which is available in other countries. The submission of personal data constitutes your acknowledgement that you read and understood this notice, and agrees to its terms.

As you know, your use of the Service, the Site and the Software is subject to the Agreement. When you registered as a Member, you affirmatively agree to all terms of the Agreement, including this Privacy Policy. Your continued use of the Service, the Site and the Software constitutes your continued agreement to the terms of the Agreement, including this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of our Privacy Policy, then you are not permitted to use the Service, the Site and the Software, and we ask that you terminate your account, as described in paragraph 7 above.
