Henley & Partners and its subsidiaries and affiliates are committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. Henley & Partners will not sell, rent, lease or otherwise make available to others any information about any person visiting our website.

We have structured our web site so that you can visit us on the Internet without identifying yourself or revealing any personal information. Once you choose to provide us with personal information, you can be assured that it will only be used in connection with your relationship with Henley & Partners.
