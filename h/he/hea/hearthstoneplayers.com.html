What this Privacy Policy Covers

This privacy policy covers Hearthstone Players’s treatment of personally identifiable information collected by Hearthstone Players through a website owned and operated by Hearthstone Players.

This privacy policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Hearthstone Players does not own or control, or of persons that Hearthstone Players does not employ or manage, including any third-party content contributors bound by contract and any third-party websites to which Hearthstone Players websites link.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

You can visit the websites of Hearthstone Players without revealing any personal information. Where required, this information may include your personal contact information and/or your company contact information. Hearthstone Players will use this information to reply to your inquiries, to provide you with requested products and services, to set up your member’s account, and to contact you regarding new products and services.

By accessing the services of Hearthstone Players and voluntarily providing us with the requested personal information, you consent to the collection and use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy.

Collection and Use of Non-Personal Information

Hearthstone Players automatically receives and records non-personal information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, cookie information and the page you requested. Hearthstone Players may use this information to customize the advertising and content you see and to fulfill your requests for certain products and services. However, Hearthstone Players does not connect this non-personal data to any personal information collected from you.

Hearthstone Players also allows third party companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. Again, these cookies are not connected to any personal information. Third party cookie usage is subject to their own privacy policies, and Hearthstone Players assumes no responsibility or liability for this usage.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Hearthstone Players may disclose your personal information to third parties who work on behalf of Hearthstone Players to provide products and services requested by you. We will share personal information for these purposes only with third parties whose privacy policies are consistent with ours or who agree to abide by our policies with respect to personal information
Hearthstone Players may otherwise disclose your personal information when:
We have your express consent to share the information for a specified purpose;
We need to respond to subpoenas, court orders or such other legal process;
We need to protect the personal safety of the users of our websites or defend the rights or property of Hearthstone Players;
We find that your actions on our websites violate the Hearthstone Players Terms of Use document or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services.

Display Advertising

Hearthstone Players has implemented Display Advertising and uses Remarketing with Google Analytics to advertise online. It means that third-party vendors, including Google, show our ads on sites across the Internet.

Hearthstone Players along with third-party vendors, including Google, use first-party cookies (such as the Google Analytics cookie) and third-party cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) together to inform, optimize, and serve ads based on your past visits to our website.

If you want to opt-out of Google Analytics for Display Advertising, just customize Google Display Network ads using the Ads Settings or using Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on that will instruct the Google Analytics JavaScript not to send any information about the website visit to Google Analytics.

Consent

If you do not consent to the collection, use or disclosure of your personal information as outlined in this policy, please do not provide any personal information to Hearthstone Players. If you have provided personal information to Hearthstone Players and no longer consent to its use or disclosure as outlined herein, please notify Hearthstone Players at hello@hearthstoneplayers.com.

Security

Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet can be considered 100% secure. However, your Hearthstone Players Information protected for your privacy and security. In certain areas of our websites, as identified on the site, Hearthstone Players uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.

We also safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, through access control procedures, network firewalls and physical security measures.
Further, Hearthstone Players retains your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes identified above or as required by law.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Hearthstone Players may at any time, without notice to you and in its sole discretion, amend this policy from time to time. Please review this policy periodically. Your continued use of Hearthstone Players websites after any such amendments signifies your acceptance thereof.

Questions or Suggestions
