In addition, if you are paying for your order by credit card, we will also need to collect your credit card number, expiration date, security code and billing address. All of the information we hold, you provide voluntarily in the normal course of using our services.

Please note that HBDirect.com does not sell products to children. If you are under 18 years old, you must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian to place orders on HBDirect.com.

Security

Credit card information you submit on HBDirect.com is always encrypted using secure socket layer technology (SSL) and is never stored on our server any longer than is required to process your order, after which time it is deleted. Other information that may remain on the server - your email address and password for example - is stored behind a firewall. The site is regularly tested for any security weaknesses and is constantly monitored for intrusion attempts.

Cookies

Cookies are employed on our website for the internal management of the site and are not used to collect any personal information. We use cookies to store visitor preferences and record session information, such as items customers add to their shopping cart or wish list and aggregate information on what pages users access or visit. You can restrict the placement of cookies on your computer by changing the settings in your web browser. Without cookies enabled, however, certain features of this site and many other sites will not function as intended.

What we might do with your information

First what we don't do with your information: we will not rent, sell or trade your personal information with others. We will not provide your information to any third party except if required by law or as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

If any of your personal information that we hold changes, you can edit your profile on the HBDirect.com website or inform us using any of the contact methods to be found at the bottom of this Privacy Policy Statement.
