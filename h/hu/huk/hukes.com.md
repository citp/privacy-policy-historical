# Hukes - Privacy policy

Hukes has established its privacy policy to explain how your information within the purview of Hukes is protected, collected and used. This policy may be updated by Hukes. Hukes will post notices of its policy changes on the Hukes site at: <http://hukes.com/privacy.html>

**PRIVACY PROTECTION**

Hukes does not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes, or engage in cross-marketing or link-referral programs with other similar sites. Hukes recommends its customers to keep their password safe and do not remember it on public (shared) computers (Internet-cafe, college, library, family computer etc). Hukes does not knowingly collect any information from persons under the age of 13. If Hukes learns that a posting is by a person under the age of 13, Hukes will remove that posting. Hukes, or persons who post on Hukes, may provide links to third party websites, with different privacy policies. Hukes is not responsible for, nor have any control over, those third party websites. 

**INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE**

Hukes collect traditional contact information such as your name, mailing and/or billing address, phone number and email address for few purposes: to send selfpublishing and confirmation emails, to authenticate user accounts, to provide subscription email services, to register for forums, to process any credit card charges, etc.

Hukes does not store full credit card information but Hukes may store the information partially, such as only last 4 digits of your credit card number for transactions authenticating, monitoring and fraud prevention purpose. All credit card transactions initiated by Hukes are transmitted through a financial gateway using encrypted Secure Socket Layer (SSL 128 Bit) protocol.

Your personal information may be collected by Hukes if you provide it in the feedback or comments, post it on our classifieds, forums, guestbook or other publishing areas of the website.

Should you expect your personal information to be private please do not post any personal information on Hukes site. Standard web log entries for each page served, including visitors IP address, page URL, and timestamp are collected by Hukes web logs in order to help diagnose problem with Hukes servers, to administer Hukes site, and to otherwise enhance visitors use of Hukes sites and services. 

**DATA STORAGE**

Hukes stores in its database all user generated content (classified ads, forums, guestbook postings etc.) even after they are deleted, as well as can archive such data elsewhere. Hukes indefinitely stores web logs and other records. Registered members can access and update personal account information through the account homepage. 

**DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION**

Hukes may disclose information about its users if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to respond to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal process. Hukes may also disclose information about its users to law enforcement officers or others, in the good faith belief that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to: enforce our Terms of Use; respond to claims that any posting or other content violates the rights of third-party; or protect the rights, property, or personal safety of Hukes, its users or the general public. Hukes complies with the provisions of C.R.S. § 6-1-716 pertinent to the breaches of security notifications effective September 1st, 2006. 

**GLOBAL USE**

By providing us with data and by visiting Hukes you acknowledge and agree that due to the global use of Hukes we may use the data collected in the course of your use of our site and/or services for the purposes identified in this policy or in our other communications to you, including the transmission of information outside your resident jurisdiction. Data provided to Hukes may be stored on servers throughout different jurisdictions. User agrees to consent to transfer of such data to different locations. 

**IF YOU DO NOT AGREE WITH Hukes PRIVACY POLICY PLEASE LEAVE THIS WEBSITE**
