INFORMATION WE COLLECT FROM YOU

Huge-IT servers automatically receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with us: such as your IP address, browser type and language, the date, time of your request. Huge-IT also uses “cookies” – small files containing a string of characters sent to your computer that can uniquely identifies your browser – in addition to Flash shared browser objects to administer its website to enhance your online experience and to learn about how you use Huge-IT services in order to improve the quality of our services.

COLLECTED DATA FROM PLUGINS

To help us better understand how you use our plugins, we ask users to allow us to collect some data in order to better installed plugins. We NEVER track users without their permission. This means, that after the installation of our plugins, users may be asked with a notification to allow or skip the option, approving us to collect some data.

Our opt-in policy requires a potential customer to self-select the services they wish to subscribe to. All the data we might collect is designed with the goal to ask the user/customer first. If you have noticed any option or code that is not user-friendly or by default would oupt-out any data, please report us such cases, using our contact us form.

What data is collected?

We restrict unauthorized collection of user's data. In interest of protecting the privacy of our users we don't share or keep the data with any third party services or users. All collected data is used to better the plugin.

DATA SECURITY

Huge-IT will take appropriate security measures designed to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. These include internal reviews of our data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures, as well as physical security measures to guard against unauthorized access to systems where we store personal data. Huge-IT processes personal information only for the purposes for which it was collected and in accordance with this Policy or any applicable service-specific privacy notice. Huge-IT takes reasonable steps to ensure that the personal information we process is accurate, complete, and current, but we depend on our users to update or correct their personal information whenever necessary. If Huge-IT learns of a security systems breach, then we may attempt to notify you electronically so that you can take appropriate protective steps. Huge-IT may post a notice on the website if a security breach occurs. Depending on where you live, you may have a legal right to receive notice of a security breach in writing. To receive a free written notice of a security breach you should notify us.

CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

Huge-IT complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act and does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are under 13, please do not send any information about yourself to us, including your name, or email address. No one under age 13 is allowed to provide any personal information to or on Huge-IT. In the event that we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13 we will delete that information as quickly as possible.
