# Hürriyet Daily News | PRIVACY POLICY

**HÜRRİYET DAILY NEWS PRIVACY POLICY**

Hürriyet Daily News Newspaper, which publishes by Hürriyet Gazetecilik ve Matbaacılık A.Ş. , value your right to privacy and respect your concerns about the privacy of your personal information and data security that you share with us. Therefore our Privacy Policy (the "Privacy Policy") is intended to protect your privacy and provide you with a safe and secure experience in obtaining information and daily news from all around the world. 

We may change or add to this privacy policy from time to time without notice. Accordingly, we encourage you to review it periodically so that you are aware of our updated policies. 

Personal information may include any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a name, an address and an email address or other contact information like phone number whether at work or at home, credit card information and any information about proof of identity. 

We use this information to notify you about important functionality changes and for statistical or survey purposes, to improve our website, services and the quality of the content that we provide to you on the site and to market products and services for each of our brands. We may also send you from time to time (by email or post) information about our services.
