# Privacy policy

The Policy Statement contained on Huion’s official online store (www.huiontablet.com, hereinafter referred to as “we” or the “online store”) is our commitment to protecting the privacy of registered members. Given the characteristics of the internet, we will unavoidably create, directly or indirectly, interactive relation with you, for which registered members of the online store are reminded of our policy on collecting, using and protecting of their personal information, and please carefully read the Privacy Policy Statement before visiting the store.

**I. Collecting and Using the Information**  
When registering membership on the online store, you’ll voluntarily disclose some personal information to us, and we’ll collect some of your personal information, like the details of your real name, position, address, telephone number, email address and so on, so that we’ll be able to identify your personal data as long as you register successfully on the online store and log on the server. Your information collected by us is intended to provide you with better service, enhance your user experience, while ensure internet security and improve the content contained herein on condition that any applicable national laws and regulations are complied with; to realize this overall objective, we’ll only use your personal information for the following purposes:

1.To provide you with the services and realize relevant functions set forth herein, and maintain and improve such services and functions.  
2.To improve our service quality, collect your opinions and suggestions for the online store and the services, and provide you with feedback or solution to specific issue on a timely manner.  
3.To maintain system security and help you to solve related problems you might encounter in using the services contained herein.  
4.To use your personal information as needed for business analysis and market research.  
5.We might transfer your personal information across border and use the same for investigation and analysis of the affiliates, or share them with third party service provider(s) and/or business partner(s) for making available service, investigation and analysis for you which we think fit. In this case, we’ll request relevant company/companies to follow these rules without failure.

**II. Disclosing and Sharing the Information**  
We fully protect the security of user information contained herein. We’ll also take technical measures as appropriate and other measures as necessary, in order to ensure information security, and prevent leakage and loss of such personal information. If you disapprove of collecting and using your personal information under the rules of the Privacy Policy Statement, please do not use the online store or disclose to us such personal information of you. The online store might share the user information with third party when meeting the requirements of applicable laws and regulations or satisfying relevant terms of service and/or provisions of software licensing agreement, if any:

1.To safeguard and defend our legal rights or properties;  
2.Follow the requirements of applicable laws and regulations as well as the rules of national authorities.  
3.To disclose such information to third party upon your approval.  
4.If you’re qualified complainant of intellectual property right and you’ve instituted a complaint for such purpose, we’ll disclose such information to the complained party upon the request of you as the complainant, to allow both parties in dispute to settle potential dispute over such right.  
5.To provide the products and services you require, it is necessary for us to share your personal information with third parties.  
6.Other disclosure which we think appropriate under applicable laws or according to relevant policy contained herein.

**III. Rules for Usage**  
The registration and use of your user name and nickname shall be in line with the ethics of the Internet and comply with the relevant laws and regulations of the People's Republic of China.  
Your user name and nickname shall not contain such controversial character(s) in the nature of menace, obscenity, obloquy, unlawfulness, infringement of other’s interests and etc.  
After successful registration, the members must safely protect their own user account and password. Any user who suffered any loss from information leakage as a result of his/her carelessness shall be solely responsible for the same.  
Any user who uses stolen account number of another user shall be solely responsible for such consequence thereof.

**IV. Usage of Cookies**  
We’ll use Cookies to allow us to provide with you more thoughtful personalized service. While you logging on the online store, we’ll store related information of yours with Cookies, in order for us to follow up your personal preference and habit of usage, to recognize your identity in case of your revisit, so as to provide your interested information, data or storage password, and enable you to visit the online store without unnecessarily entering your password for each repeated visit. Or you may close this function, and we’ll disable the service of Cookies to you.

**V. IP Address**  
Your IP address refers to your computer number automatically allocated when you’re using network server. When visiting the online store, such number and access time and viewed pages will be automatically identified and recorded in the log file in our server. It’s an internet practice to collect user IP address, and many online stores will do this by default.

**VI. Linking Third Party Online Store and Security**  
We’ll link the online store to other online stores, and we may add the linkage to those online stores of our business partners or common brands, if necessary, but only comprehensive information is shared with them, and in no way will we disclose your identity.

We’ll strictly manage and protect your entered information, and we’ll utilize corresponding technical and security measures to prevent your personal information from loss, stealing or tampering. However, you should notice that no “perfect security measures” exist on the internet, by which we make no commitment to absolute security of the said information.

**VII. Disclaimer**  
In any of the following cases, we’ll not assume any legal responsibility for disclosure of your privacy:

1.Leakage of any of your personal information caused by your disclosure of user password to others or sharing your registered account with others, or leakage of any of your personal information for any other causes for which we’re not accountable  
2.Disclosure of your personal information under applicable laws and regulations or as required by relevant government policy;  
3.Any dispute arising from the use of your personal information by any third party in such case as specified in the terms and statement of services herein;  
4.Any temporary shutdown of the website caused by any hacker attack, invasion of computer virus or government regulation;  
5.If you find any case of infringing your civil interests by the online store, you shall notify the same to us as soon as possible, so that we can take such measures as deletion, shielding, delinking etc as necessary; any loss in connection with such infringement due to your failure of timely notice shall be your sole responsibility;  
6.Any consequence as a result of force majeure;  
7.The usage or disclaimer of service terms and statements herein specified in the online store.

**VIII. Protection of Minors**  
Our services are only available for those persons who are 18 years old or older. If you’re a minor, please do not disclose your personal information, and you’re not advised to use our services.

**IX. Revision of Privacy Right and Feedback**  
You can log on your user account, to visit your account information and revise, restrict or delete your account data.

Moreover, you’re entitled to request for the information on your personal data stored with us at any time. Or you may request us to correct, update or delete the information on your own at any time. If you have any such request, you may contact us by emailing privacy@huion.com. If your information is provided by your employer, you should raise the request directly to your employer.

You’re not required to pay any fee to visit your personal information, but if your request for this is evidently groundless, repetitive or excessive, we may charge you with reasonable fee, or in such case, we may reject such request of yours.

With extending the scope of services on the online store, we may from time to time update the content of the Privacy Policy Statement without separate notice to the users. The Privacy Policy Statement, once is issued on the webpage after such updating, shall supersede the predecessor with full force and effect.

1.The contents of network services available on the online store may include characters, software, voice, images, videos, and graphs etc, all of which are protected by the laws governing copyright, trademark and other property ownership.  
2.The user may only use such contents after authorized by the online store, and may not duplicate and/or regenerate such contents or create any derivatives related to such contents without prior consent.  
3.We reserve the right of final interpretation of the Privacy Policy Statement.
