# Hirebridge :: Online Recruiting Staffing & Hiring Management Solutions

**Privacy Statement**

Questions regarding this statement should be directed to our company via email to: privacy@hirebridge.com or via telephone at 1-954.974.2435. We take our obligations to guard your privacy seriously. The purpose of this Privacy Statement is to inform you about (1) the information that is collected about you; (2) how Hirebridge, LLC may use the information that is collected; and (3) how information can be corrected or changed.

**Information Collection and Use**

Through the use of "cookies" (as described below) and online data entry, survey and registration forms, we may collect and monitor, in both the aggregate and individual form, personal identifying facts or data regarding you on, among other things, the following:

Technical information: home server, domain name, email address, site traffic, Reference information: name, company mailing address, telephone number and password.

Hirebridge, LLC gathers most of the personal information on its users during an initial registration process for certain services or demonstration of services. You may decline to provide this information, but may thereby be unable to participate in certain activities or opportunities offered on our Web Site.

Hirebridge also automatically gathers information about you and your usage of the Web Site, such as the number and frequency of your visits to the Web Site, and the nature of the use you make of the Web Site. In addition to the initial registration process, Hirebridge occasionally asks users for personal information at other times, including when they complete optional survey forms or fill out optional contact forms for additional information on particular products and services. Hirebridge uses this information to improve its content and service and product offerings. 

It is our continuing goal to make your visit to the Web Site more efficient, enjoyable and productive. By gathering this information, we can customize the Web Site and tailor it to meet your particular needs. Hirebridge will not share with third parties information about you in which you are identified, except in the following circumstances: (i) where Hirebridge secures your express prior consent; (ii) as necessary to provide you with goods or services you request; and/or (iii) to law enforcement or other government officials or as otherwise required by law as Hirebridge deems in our discretion to be necessary or appropriate.

In addition, from time to time, Our company may also use information you provide to inform you of features and services that we believe would be of particular interest to you. You can choose not to receive such information by letting us know as provided below. Our company may share, sell, lease, rent and otherwise disclose to business partners, advertisers, and other affiliates and third parties information about you, in which you may or may not be identified (such as in the form of statistical, demographic, or aggregated information about visitors generally to the web Site). 

**"Cookies"**

"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored by your web browser software on your computer's hard drive or temporarily in your computer's memory. The use of cookies is now an industry standard, and users will find them on most major web sites.  
We use cookies to personalize the viewing experience of a user on the Hirebridge Web Site. When the user revisits the Hirebridge Web Site, we can recognize the user by the cookie and customize the users experience at our Web Site accordingly. 

Although most web browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, if you prefer, you, as the user, may decline the placement of a cookie on your hard drive by using the appropriate feature(s) of your web browser software (if available) to delete the cookie. You, as the user, should understand, however, that certain areas within the Hirebridge Web Site may not function properly if the web browser will not accept cookies.

**Children**

Children are not eligible to use the services of the Web Site and Hirebridge asks that minors (under the age of 18) do not submit any personal information to it. 

**Links to Other Sites**

The Hirebridge Web Site may provide hypertext links to other web sites. Please be aware that once you leave the Web Site and enter another web site, you may be subject to different privacy practices and policies over which Hirebridge has no control and for which Hirebridge is not responsible. Our company encourages its visitors to be aware when they leave the Hirebridge Web Site and to read the privacy statements of other web sites that collect personally identifiable information. 

**Changes to Privacy Policy**

Hirebridge may change this privacy policy at its discretion provided that any such change shall be posted on this Web Site. If due to such changes you decide that you do not want your personal information to be used further by Our company (other than in a manner in which you are not identified), please notify Hirebridge as provided below. 

**Changes and Corrections to Your Information**

You can request details of information about you by contacting Hirebridge at: privacy@hirebridge.com or call customer service at 1-877-890-HIRE. You may change or correct any such information, request that Hirebridge not contact you with goods, services, opportunities, or information that may interest you, or request that information by which you may be identified not be used further in any manner, by contacting us as set forth above.
