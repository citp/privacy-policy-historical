# Privacy Policy

Thank you for accessing www.hiddensecretsofmoney.com, a sponsored goldsilver.com’s website (“site”) operated by Silver & Gold, Inc. it is our objective to respect your privacy and protect your personal information. Please read this private policy carefully. By accessing this website you accept the practices described below. GoldSilver.com™ is not responsible for the content or private practices of any website not operated by Silver & Gold, Inc., to which the site links or which links to the site.

**1.  Safeguarding your privacy.**

The security and confidentiality of your information is important to us. The Site has incorporated certain physical, administrative and technical procedures to safeguard your personal information online. Personal information may be printed or otherwise stored in a non-electronic form. Please be aware that despite our efforts to provide the best security measures to protect your personal information, no security is impenetrable or foolproof.

**2\. Use of cookies.**

We currently use  **Cookies**  to track our Site traffic in order to enhance our information and your experience on the Site. Although Cookies do not give us any personally identifiable information about you, once you choose to give us your personal information on an online form this information may be linked to Cookies.

**3\. Information we collect and use.**

We may collect information from you on this Site in several different ways. Our primary purpose in doing so, however, is for the purpose of registration and ordering, forms and applications, e-mail addresses and other technology, as determined. Secondarily, the information we collect is used to keep you apprised of any product promotions and/or special offerings. At any time you wish to opt out of being on our mailing list or receiving future direct mailings from us, simply send an e-mail to us at  support@hiddensecretsofmoney.com with your e-mail address in the subject line.

**4\. Modifying your information.**

Upon your request, we will (a) update or correct your personal information and/or (b) stop sending e-mails to your e-mail address. If you need to update any information you've previously submitted to us, please e-mail the changes to  support@hiddensecretsofmoney.com

**5\. How to contact us.**

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@hiddensecretsofmoney.com.
