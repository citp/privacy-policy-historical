# Privacy Policy - Offshore IT Company

**Personal information we collect:**

Personal information is information that is associated with your name or personal identity. Hi-Tech OS may use personal information to better understand your needs & interests and to provide you with better service. 

The types of personal information you provide to us from this website may include name, e-mail address, phone numbers & project details. 

Hi-Tech OS does not sell, rent or lease any individual's personal information or lists of email addresses to third parties for commercial & marketing purposes. 

**Notification of Changes:**

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on our website so you will always be aware of what information we collect and how we use it. 

**Webservers:**

Hi-Tech OS automatically receives and records information from our servers and from your browser, including your IP address, the time, and information about the page you requested. 

**Copyrights & Trademarks:**

Hi-Tech OS ensures that material on this website is accurate & current. 

Hi-Tech OS has independently developed all the content presented on its website. The copyright for content provided by Hi-Tech OS will remain the property of Hi-Tech OS and may not allow to re-print on any medium without prior a written permission.
