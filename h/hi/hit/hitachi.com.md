# HITACHI : Hitachi Ltd. - Concerning Protection of Personal Information (Summary)

  
  


  


In relation to this website (hereafter, "this site"), operated by Hitachi Ltd. (hereafter, the "Company"), ["Applicable scope"](https://web.archive.org/web/20060615004945id_/http://www.hitachi.com/privacy-e/index2.html#scope) prescribes the handling of personal information received by the Company from the customer.

On this site, the Company protects and manages customers' personal information with the utmost attention and effort, based on the documents ["Hitachi policy on personal information protection"](https://web.archive.org/web/20060615004945id_/http://www.hitachi.com/privacy-e/index2.html#approach) and ["Personal information protection policy"](https://web.archive.org/web/20060615004945id_/http://www.hitachi.com/privacy-e/index2.html#policy) as prescribed by the Company.   
In this summary, ["personal information"](https://web.archive.org/web/20060615004945id_/http://www.hitachi.com/privacy-e/index2.html#definition) refers to the following items of information that can identify individual customers: 

  1. Information entered and provided to the Company by the customer via the "Inquiry Input Form" or other windows on this site; and 
  2. Information provided by the customer to the Company in relation to this site. 


  1. The Company shall only collect and use personal information within the scope that is necessary for achievement of the stated objectives in its business activities, which primarily comprise the manufacture of electrical instruments and appliances, information communication machines and devices, and electronic components and the provision of information services. Moreover, when receiving personal information from the customer, the purpose of that information utilization shall be clearly specified in advance and the customer's consent shall be received.   

  2. Except under special conditions, the Company shall not disclose the customer's personal information to third parties without first obtaining the prior consent of the customer.   

  3. The Company may provide to its group companies the customer's personal information, including name, workplace and home address, telephone number, facsimile number, and email address, where necessary when conducting joint business activities with group companies. 



When making requests or complaints concerning the disclosure, correction, addition, deletion, suspension of use, suspension of provision to third parties, or notification of the purpose of use (hereafter, "disclosure, etc.") of the customer's personal information provided to the Company, the customer is requested to do so according to the designated procedure. 

  1. The cooperation of customers is requested [concerning the protection of personal information, in regards to its provision, and the acquisition of prior consent. ](https://web.archive.org/web/20060615004945id_/http://www.hitachi.com/privacy-e/index2.html#request)  

  2. There are extra considerations [concerning the information in the document "Concerning protection of personal information"](https://web.archive.org/web/20060615004945id_/http://www.hitachi.com/privacy-e/index2.html#note).  

  3. There are extra considerations [concerning the use of cookies and Web beacons ](https://web.archive.org/web/20060615004945id_/http://www.hitachi.com/privacy-e/index2.html#cookie)on this site.  

  4. In cases where the customer telephones the Company, the contents of the conversation may be subject to recording in order to allow an accurate response to be given.   




Except for requests or complaints regarding the disclosure, etc. of personal information, send questions and inquiries concerning this summary of handling by the following method.
