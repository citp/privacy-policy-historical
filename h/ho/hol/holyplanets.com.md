# Help Topic

We have created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: Holyplanets.Com _**E-Mails**_   
Holyplanets does not collect personally identifiable information about individuals at all except when individuals provide such information on a voluntary basis for the forum registration. Also e-mail addresses are maintained in the database of registered users. 

This information from the e-mails is used for registration purpose on our site to get in touch with the e-mail sender when necessary to notify them for updates to the site. The e-mail address is used to provide the user with the website information. Contact information is never shared with anyone or disclosed to third parties.
