HOTLD.COM Privacy Statement

The HOTLD.COM Privacy Policy
HOTLD.COM has developed this Privacy Policy Statement because of our firm commitment to securing your privacy on the internet. Our use of security technology and an adherence to privacy policies allows HOTLD.COM to maintain the privacy of this information.

What information does HOTLD.COM collect and how is it used?
HOTLD.COM needs to know your name, mailing address, email address, telephone number and some general information in order to process your request for long distance service.

How does HOTLD.COM protect your information?
HOTLD.COM uses a secure server that encrypts all of your personal information before it is sent to us in order to prevent unauthorized access to your information.

Changes to our Privacy Policies
As technologies, regulations or other situations warrant, HOTLD.COM may make changes to this Privacy Policy Statement.
