## 3\. Use and Disclosure of Information.

  * hotIMG collects, stores and processes your personal information on servers located in Tel Aviv, Israel. hotIMG shall use your personal information in order to (i) to make payments you may be entitled to receive from hotIMG pursuant to the Publishing Agreement; (ii) for such purposes stated above in Section 2(f); (iii) to provide user services.
  * If you are a registered PayPal user, your personal information, may be provided to PayPal in order enable hotIMG to make payments which you may be entitled to receive from hotIMG pursuant to the Publishing Agreement.
  * Unless otherwise expressly provided herein hotIMG will not sell, lease or otherwise share or provide your personal information to any third parties, without your express permission. Notwithstanding the above, there may be certain circumstances in which some of your personal information will be shared with third parties, under strict restrictions, as further provided in this Privacy Policy.

hotIMG may disclose personal information collected as provided herein:

    1. hotIMG shall disclose information that it, in good faith, believes is appropriate to cooperate in investigations of suspected fraud or other illegal activity, situations involving potential threat to the physical safety of any person, or to conduct investigations of violation of its Publishing Agreement; or any such similar information.
    2. hotIMG shall disclose information in response to a subpoena, warrant, court order, levy, attachment, or any comparable or applicable process; or otherwise to establish or exercise hotIMG's legal rights or to defend against legal claims.
    3. hotIMG may share information with or disclose information to its employees, officers, directors, consultants, advisers, subsidiaries, affiliates, business partners, joint ventures, provided such disclosure is on a need-to-know basis, and subject to confidentiality provisions.
    4. hotIMG may share aggregated statistic data provided such information is not connected with personally identifiable data.
    5. In the event that hotIMG is merged with or acquired by another entity, the successor entity will have access to the information maintained by hotIMG. In such case the successor entity shall continue to be bound by the provisions of this Privacy Policy, unless and until amended in accordance with the provisions in this Privacy Policy.
    6. hotIMG may disclose personal information to your agent, guardian or legal representative (upon presentation of such documentation hotIMG shall reasonably deem sufficient to establish such right).
