## 7\. How long do We retain your Personal Information for?

We retain your Personal Information for as long as is necessary for the purpose for which we obtained them, notably as long as you continue to use our Services and for a 3 (three) year period after our last contact with you or the last use of such Services in case you would have created an account on our Platform. 

At the end of the above mentioned period, We will be able to contact you to find out whether you wish to continue using our Services. In the absence of a positive and explicit response from You, your Personal Data will be deleted or archived in accordance with the provisions in force. 

Please note that even if your account and Personal Information are deleted: 

  * Your contributions, topics, deals and comments will be kept and anonymized, 
  * Your private messages will always remain available to their recipients.



In the event that your account is blocked, we will keep part of your Personal Information for a period of between 1 and 3 years in order to prevent you from circumventing the rules applying to our Platform. 

We retain cookies data for a 30 days period from when they were collected, in the event where you’re not logged in on our Platform, and for up to 13 months in other instances.

We prefer as much as possible to store your Personal Information within the European Union. For example, we host our servers in the European Union. 

We only decide to transfer some of your Personal Information where our recipients (for instance our IT providers) have provided adequate safeguards in accordance with GDPR. 

Such adequate safeguards may be notably provided for by:

By sending a request to privacy@hotukdeals.com we can provide you with the details regarding such recipients and their appropriate safeguards. 

## 11\. How do We use cookies?

A cookie is a small piece of data (text file) that a website – when visited by a user – asks your browser to store on your device in order to remember information about you, such as your language preference or login information. 

We use cookies for different purposes, find out more and manage your preferences in our [Cookies policy](https://www.hotukdeals.com/cookies-policy).

## 12\. What are your rights?

You have several rights to your Personal Information under GDPR: 

**Right of access**|  You have the right to access your Personal Information by making a request in writing to Us. It means you have the right to obtain a copy of your Personal Information.   
---|---  
**Right to rectification**|  You have the right to rectify or to complete your Personal Information if it is inaccurate or incomplete.   
**Right to erasure**|  You have the right to request the erasure of your Personal Information. Please note that this right is not absolute and only applies in certain circumstances (for instance only if the Personal Information is no longer necessary for the purpose which we originally collected or processed for it, etc.).   
**Right to restrict processing**|  You have the right to request the restriction of your Personal Information where you have a particular reason for wanting the restriction.   
**Right to data portability**|  You have the right to ask the portability of your Personal Information. It means you have the right to receive Personal Information you have provided to Us, in a structured, commonly used and machine readable format.   
**Right to object**|  You have the right to object to the processing of your Personal Information. This allows you to ask us to stop processing your Personal Information.   
**Right related to automated decision making including profile**|  You have the right not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated means, if the decision produces legal effects concerning you or significantly affects you in a similar way.   
**Right to lodge a complaint**|  You have the right to lodge a complaint before the data protection supervisory authority : Information Commissioner’s Office - ICO Wycliffe House, Water Lane, Wilmslow SK9 5AF, Cheshire. Helpline number: 0303 123 1113. Website: [ https://ico.org.uk/ ](https://ico.org.uk/)  
  


## 13\. How to exercise your rights?

  
 **Right to rectification**|  You can exercise this right by going into the settings of your account where you’re able to update your personal information.   
---|---  
**Right to erasure**|  You can exercise this right by going into the settings of your account, in the “Profile” section, and by clicking on “Delete Account”. We will retain your account and information for 30 days and then it will be permanently deleted. If you change your mind you can reactivate your account by logging back in (within 30 days).   
In the event where your account would be suspended or permanently banned, you remain able to access a restricted mode that will allow you to delete your account. Please note that some of your Personal Information may be kept, in accordance with the 7th Section of this Privacy Policy.   
**Right of access / to data portability**|  You can exercise this right by going into the settings of your account, in the “Profile” section, and by clicking on “Generate data”. You will then receive an email once the data will be ready, and you will be able to download it from the settings of your account, in the “Profile” section. You are allowed to generate the data related to your account every two weeks.   
In the event where your account would be suspended or permanently banned, you remain able to access a restricted mode that will allow you to download your Personal Information.   
  
To exercise your other rights, please contact Us by using the contact information at the beginning of this Privacy Policy. We may need to ask you for a proof of identity to be certain that it is you. 

We will do our best to answer you within a month. 

Sometimes we will not be able to accept your request because your Data Protection Rights may only apply in certain circumstances or depends of the legal basis of processing: we will then inform you.
