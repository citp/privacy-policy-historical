**Introduction To Our Privacy/Security Policy**

Hot G Vibe wants to help you make the most of your visit to our websites, and that incorporates helping safeguarding your individual privacy. All of the features on our websites are presented without gathering any personal information from you. You can browse the Hot G Vibe websites and view all of our content anonymously. Only when you purchase a product from Hot G Vibe do we require your personal information to process accurately your transcation.

By using this website, you are consenting to the terms of this Hot G Vibe Privacy Policy, as presented in this area of the website. If you do not a gree with any terms or practices described in this Privacy Policy, please exit our websites.

As used in this Hot G Vibe Privacy Policy, "Personal Information" means personally identifiable information, such as your full name, home address, telephone number, date of birth, and e-mail address. When personal information is collected, you will know because you will have to fill out an electronic form on the website. When you make a purchase we will also collect your credit card information as additional personal information. We will use credit card information only to complete the transactions you have requested.

**Privacy Information**

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. Hot G Vibe will never trade, lease, or sell your personel information that is obtained on this website. We will contact you only if your order/shipping information is incomplete or incorrect.

Hot G Vibe collects Personal Information from visitors to our websites in the following ways:

  * Purchases of merchandise on our website.
  * Participation in contests or events.
  * E-mail transmitted via various links on our websites.



Using state-of-the-art encryption security technology, your online order is secure. When you place your order, you will be taken to our secure server prior to being asked for your credit card number. We use an address verification system to compare your billing address to your credit card on file with your bank.

If you send us an e-mail, you should know that e-mail is not necessarily 100% secure against internet fraud. If your communication needs to include sensitive information like your bank account, credit card, or social security number, please contact us by postal mail or telephone (see our contact information below) rather than e-mail.

**Cookies**

In order to track your use of the site, "cookies" may be employed. Cookies are pieces of digital information that are sent by websites and stored in your computer's hard drive, which provide you a service by allowing us to recognize your computer to you from having to re-enter the same data every time you visit our websitese. They also allow us to gain knowledge of how visitors use our websites. This user data is amassed for internet tracking purposes mainly by adding all the pages viewed throughout our websiteds and loggeing Internet Protocol ("IP") addresses and browser types.

If you do not want to receive cookies, you may set your internet browser to reject them.
