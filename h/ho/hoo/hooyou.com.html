Privacy Policy

Purpose and Scope

This Privacy policy discloses what information we gather about you when you visit hooyou.com or buy product or service from us. It describes how we use that information.

Information We Gathered

hooyou.com collects user information in the following two ways: 1) data we collect when users contact us by email, contacting form or online Support links; 2) data that users volunteer by signing up to our membership services and by inquerying our product and service information.

Personal Information

We use the personal information you provide voluntarily to send information you've requested and to fulfill orders. The specific use of your personal information varies, depending on how you contact us:

  When you sign up to membership services or inquery product and service information, you provide email address. We never sell or release your email address you provide to third party.
  When you contact us, we may ask for your name, address, phone number and email address so we can reply to you.

We release personal information when we believe that release is appropriate to comply with the law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of hooyou Information Technology, our users, or others. This may include exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction.

Tracking Information

We analyze our site usage by tracking information such as traffic flow, pageviews, search terms, and click through. We use this information to improve our site only and do not share any information with advertisers that can identify an individual user.

Services

This site contains links to other sites. hooyou Information Technology is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other Web Sites.

Notification of Changes

If we may change our Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on our website. If we decide to use personal information in a way different from that stated at the time it was collected, we will notify users via email.

Contact the Web Site

If you have any questions or comments about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web Site, you can always contact us.
