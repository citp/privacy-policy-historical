# The Hoover Company

|   
You can visit the Hoover Web Site without revealing your name or any other information about yourself. Hoover collects the user names of visitors, monitors the number of visits and time spent on this web site so as to provide an indication of the helpfulness of this web site. 

The Hoover Company also have fields for collecting the name, address, phone number and e-mail address of visitors. This information may be used to respond to requests, complaints or process orders. In addition, this information may also be provided to Hoover affiliated companies to enable them to provide you with information regarding their fine products. If you wish your name to be removed from any listing, please write The Hoover Company, 101 East Maple Street, North Canton, Ohio 44720, ATTENTION: Business Planning Manager. 

The Hoover web site may contain links to other web sites. However, Hoover is not responsible for the privacy, practices or contents of these other sites.
