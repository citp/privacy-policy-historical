# Hook.tv: Flyfishing

## Hook.tv Privacy Policy

Hook.tv is committed to protecting your privacy. By using the Hook.tv website (www.hook.tv) or downloading the Hook.tv Media Center software and registering as a user, you are consenting to the collection and use of information by Hook.tv in conformance with the terms and conditions of the Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the Hook.tv Privacy Policy, please do not register with any Hook.tv product. This document was last updated February 12, 2007. Information Collection and Use We will only use the information we collect for the purposes of providing you with a more personalized experience. Hook.tv is the sole owner of the information collected and we do not share the information unless specifically stated otherwise within this policy. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to other parties in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. We collect information from our users during registration and when you make updates to your personal information in the Tools section of the Hook.tv Media Center. In order to ensure the integrity of your personal information, all of your data is stored in secure Hook.tv server databases.

### Hook.tv Registration

When you choose to view, purchase, or download media files from Hook.tv, you will be required to register as a Hook.tv user. Registering allows you to take advantage of our unique, personalized features and services. Upon registering, you will first be asked to provide your e-mail address, a unique username, a password, name, gender, and date of birth. For billing information we will collect the type of credit card, your name as it appears on your credit card, credit card number, CVN (if applicable), expiration date, billing address, city, state, and zip code. You may also elect to provide us with your areas of interest.

This information is collected for personal identification, billing authorization, and to further personalize your Hook.tv experience. Information is stored on secure Hook.tv servers. All information collected allows you access to your account information and various Hook.tv features.

This information also allows us to comply to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) ensuring that registered users of Hook.tv are 13 years of age or older. If a prospective user attempts to register and indicates that they are under the age of 13, we will not allow such registration to continue and will delete all information relating to such prospective user other than the name and email address for purposes of cross referencing any future attempts to register as a user with information relating to the age of the prospective user that may be different from the age set forth in the original registration.

### Cookies

"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. A cookie file can contain information such as a user ID the site uses to recognize you. A cookie can't read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. The only personal information a cookie contains is the information you provide yourself. The Hook.tv Media Center does not use "Cookies", but "Cookies" may be used when you use Hook.tv Website or connect to an advertisers Website. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but if you prefer, you can set your browser to block them in the future. This action, however, may result in a less personalized experience with Hook.tv and other websites. When you enter personal information on Hook.tv software, its website, or with a third party by means of advertising and you have elected to accept cookies, your personally identifiable information will be linked to a cookie on your computer. Hook.tv does not control the policies and practices of its advertisers. You are therefore advised to contact such advertisers directly with any questions you may have concerning their privacy or other policies or practices.

This information is stored in Hook.tv’s secure database and is used for purposes of more readily issuing licenses for media files that you have already downloaded and thereby improving the speed with which you may use such media files in the future. This information is also used to analyze trends, administer the applications and website, diagnose problems, fulfill royalty reporting requirements for acquired media, to gather broad demographic information for aggregate use, and deliver a personalized user experience within the Media Guide. No personally identifiable information concerning such usage is shared with any other party. Also note certain media players, such as the Microsoft Windows Media Player, may create log files identifying the media files that you have played. Hook.tv does not control the policies and practices of companies offering media players. You are therefore advised to contact such companies directly with any questions you may have concerning their privacy or other policies or practices.

### Security

Hook.tv takes every precaution to protect our users’ information. When users submit sensitive information, your information is protected both on- and off-line. However, you should keep in mind that any information that you disclose while on-line might be intercepted and misused by unintended third party recipients.

When Hook.tv uses sensitive information such as a credit card number during an e-commerce transaction, that information is encrypted and protected with industry-standard encryption software Secure Socket Layers (SSL). We have also obtained a digital certificate from GeoTrust Inc., a leading provider of Internet trust services. When you enter a secure portion of the (such as the shopping cart) which contains any of your personal information, the GEOTrust Logo will appear on the page. If you click on this image, a small popup window displaying site-security information will appear. This certificate guarantees that your personal information is being transmitted in secure (encrypted) form to a Hook.tv web server, not to some unknown or unauthorized server.

While we use SSL encryption to protect sensitive information online, we also do everything in our power to protect user-information off-line. All information you provide to Hook.tv online is stored on a secure Hook.tv’s server databases. The servers on which we store personally identifiable information are kept in a highly secure data center behind a series of limited-access locked doors. Access to all of our users’ information is restricted in our offices. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, our billing clerk or a customer service representative) are granted access to personally identifiable information. Furthermore, ALL employees are kept up-to-date on our security and privacy practices. Any time new policies are added or new employees are hired, our employees are notified and/or reminded about the importance we place on privacy, and what they can do to ensure our customers' information is protected.

Ultimately, your personal information is protected by the password you created when you registered an account with the (or another password you chose after changing a previous password). Please keep this password confidential. No Customer Service Associate or any other representative of Hook.tv will ever ask you for your password. So if you receive any correspondence purportedly coming from us, please do not supply any password in response to any such request. The confidentiality of your password is yours to protect. You may change it anytime by going to the My Account section of Tools that is part of the application.

If you have any questions about security, please send an e-mail to support@hook.tv
