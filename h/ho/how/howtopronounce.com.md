# Privacy Policy: HowToPronounce.com

Howtopronounce.com A pronunciation dictionary, with audio and text pronunciations with meaning, for everyone to learn the way in which a word or name is pronounced and you can share with others, so that people can say it correctly.

### What Howtopronounce.com is all about?

Howtopronounce.com is not only an online pronunciation dictionary but also helps with tools to learn how to pronounce online instantly by yourself. Our dictionary also helps users from all over the world to share their knowledge with others, with the help of Contribute System. Every visitor can suggest new words, voice pronunciation, text pronunciation, phonetic pronunciation, meaning, translation, sentence, antonyms, synonyms and can also edit, correct, confirm other users' added content.

### Terms of Use

All services offered by Howtopronounce.com can be used for free, both for private and business purposes (Give proper credit to Howtopronounce.com). 

### Disclaimer

As this site being an user contributed system we cannot guarantee the 100% accuracy or completeness of correct voice pronunciation, text pronunciation, phonetic pronunciation, meaning, translation, sentence, antonyms, synonyms. If you find any error you may contact us or you can edit and suggest the correct one. 

### Privacy Policy

We never share or sell personal information, including names and email addresses, with any third party. We allow third-party advertising companies to serve ads, when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. Howtopronounce.com has no control over, and is not responsible for, the ways these other websites collect or use your information. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](https://www.howtopronounce.com/cookies-policy/). For your information, we may change our Privacy Policy from time to time. Users can view our latest Privacy Policy at any time by visiting this page.
