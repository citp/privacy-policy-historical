**__HockeyGiant Privacy Policy__** _ _

HockeyGiant collects customer information to improve your overall shopping experience with us.  **Your privacy is very important to us.**   We know that you care how information about you is used and shared.  We appreciate the trust that you have placed in us in shopping at HockeyGiant, we respect your privacy and are committed to maintaining and using your information responsibly.  By visiting **HockeyGiant.com** you are accepting the practices described in our Privacy Policies and those of _Yahoo!_  

**HockeyGiant does not rent, sell or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies, unless we have your permission.**  

HockeyGiant may update our Privacy Policy from time-to-time and use of the information we collect now is subject to the Privacy Policy at the time of use.  Whenever we make a change, we will post the updated policy at this site and we encourage you to check our policy periodically.  By using our web site following the posting of a change in the Privacy Policy, you are consenting to the updated policy.  If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy, please [contact us](http://www.hockeygiant.com/info.html#CONTACT) at the [Contact](http://www.hockeygiant.com/info.html#CONTACT) information provided on this web site. 

Our Privacy Policy covers how HockeyGiant treats personal information that we collect and receive, including information relating to your past use of HockeyGiant products and services.  Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, e-mail address, phone number, street address, and that is not otherwise publicly available.  When you order from HockeyGiant, we ask for this information, which may often include credit card information.  This information is collected and used for the purposes of order fulfillment, communicating with you by e-mail, mail or telephone with respect to your orders, sending promotional information, for statistical purposes and to administer our systems.  

**More About Yahoo Privacy**

Both _Yahoo!_ and _Yahoo!_ Shopping are **Trust e** certified licensees of the **Trust e** Privacy Seal Program, which means that to demonstrate their commitment to your privacy, they have agreed to notify you of their privacy practices regarding your information.  _Yahoo!_ Privacy Policies are comprehensive and cover such matters as information collection and use, children, information sharing and disclosure, cookies, account information and preferences, and more.
