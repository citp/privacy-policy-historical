**With respect to email addresses:**  
Some of our blogs allow reader comments. If you leave a comment, you will be asked to provide your email address for verification purposes only. Aside from this, we do not collect any email addresses or personal information from our readers. Nor do we engage in spam.

**With respect to cookies:**  
By way of definition, a "cookie" is a bit of data created by a Web server and stored on a user's computer / web browser. Most computers / web browsers automatically accept cookies because they are often used to enhance the web user's experience (as is the case with any third-party cookies on our website).

We use third-party advertising services to display text ads and banner ads across our website. These ads are typically very relevant to the content of any given page, which increases the value and usefulness of our website for visitors such as yourself.

The ads generated by third parties may contain cookies that may be sent to your Internet browser through the aforementioned ads. We do not have control over cookies placed by our third-party advertisers, nor do we have access to any information their cookies may collect. However, if you prefer, you can adjust your web browser's settings so that it does not accept cookies from this or any other website.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this website. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our site visitors based on their visit to out sites and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content [network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above addresses or phone number.

You can learn more about Google's [ad personalization here](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites), and view their general [privacy policy here](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
