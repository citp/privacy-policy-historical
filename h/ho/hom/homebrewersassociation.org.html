    Privacy Policy

    The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for www.HomebrewersAssociation.org.

    Information Gathering

    This web server logs the IP addresses of the systems which access the site and the files visited.

    These logs are retained for our internal use only. In certain instances, we may ask visitors to voluntarily provide other information about themselves.

    Cookies may and will be used to maintain a user’s identity between web sessions.

    Use of Information

    We do not provide any personally identifiable information we gather or develop about our visitors to any third parties for any purpose.

    Visitor IP addresses and accessed files are used to identify server problems and to provide site usage statistics. This information is used to provide future content suitable to our audience and to generate statistical reports. Individual site usage information will not be disclosed.

    When commenting on HomebrewersAssociation.org using Facebook, your Facebook username and profile picture will be displayed when a comment or reply is made.

    Registered forum users, newsletter recipients and American Homebrewers Association members will receive occasional correspondence via email from the American Homebrewers Association and other Brewers Association properties, including CraftBeer.com, Support Your Local Brewery, Brewers Publications and the Brewers Association Professional Division. If you do not want to receive communications, you can request to have your registration deleted or unsubscribed through the American Homebrewers Association’s email service at the bottom of any communication.

    Security

    This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

    Contacting this Website

    For all privacy and policy questions in regards to this website, please contact: webmaster@brewersassociation.org.

    Though this site can and may contain links to non-HomebrewersAssociation.org sites, the American Homebrewers Association is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these websites.

    Policy effective date: October 20, 2014.
