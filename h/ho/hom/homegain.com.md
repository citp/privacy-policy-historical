# HomeGain -- Find a real estate agent - Realtor - Real Estate - Homes for sale - Home Buying - Home Selling - Home

We're committed to providing an online environment that is secure and private for our customers. To help ensure the security of all data transmissions -- including account viewing, submitting profiles and proposals -- we employ leading encryption technology and passwords selected by our customers. A high level of security and privacy protection is in place when you visit the HomeGain Control Centers. 

**Online Privacy**  
HomeGain.com was founded by Brad Inman, a leader in consumer real estate information and news, with a reputation built on a foundation of trust and integrity. The HomeGain service adheres to a philosophy based on trust and respect. 

We understand that the privacy and security of your profile information and information that personally identifies you are important to you. Our employees are responsible for adhering to confidentiality standards that are designed to protect our customers' personal information. 

Below is important information related to the protection and confidentiality of your personal and account information. If you have any questions, please feel free to email us at [info@homegain.com](mailto:info@homegain.com). 

**HomeGain.com Web Site**  
You can browse public areas of HomeGain without revealing any personal or account information. Should you choose to use non-public areas of HomeGain.com, designed for account holders, a username and password is required. Your username and password should never be shared with anyone and should be changed frequently. 

In certain cases, we may use your personal or account information to service your account to improve our services to you, to allow you direct access to non-public areas of your account, to provide new products and information that you have requested, and to inform you of additional products or services that may be of interest to you. 

We use proven encryption methods to transmit account information across the Internet to non-public areas of HomeGain.com. We restrict access to non-public areas of HomeGain.com by using a login process rather than cookies or less secure means. 

Any related account and personal information maintained by HomeGain is done so to fulfill our legal and regulatory requirements and to provide you with the products and services you have requested. 

**E-mail at HomeGain.com**  
E-mail is an important communication channel for our online customers. E-mails sent automatically to agents or sellers through the function of the Control Center are kept anonymous. On occasion, we may use your e-mail address to send you communications with information about service and product information that we believe may be of interest to you. If you do not want to receive such unsolicited communications from HomeGain.com, simply contact us at [info@homegain.com](mailto:info@homegain.com). 

The ads appearing on this Web site are delivered to you by DoubleClick, our Web advertising partner. This company may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services of interest to you. Please click here for more information on [DoubleClick and its privacy policy](http://www.doubleclick.net/us/corporate/privacy/privacy/default.asp?asp_object_1=&), information collected in the process of delivering an ad to you and your choices regarding delivery of targeted ad campaigns. 

**Third Party Cookies**   
In the course of serving advertisements to this site or in connection with certain e-mails or other forms of advertisements, our third-party advertisers may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser. These companies may use information about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. Unless otherwise notified, they do not use names, addresses, email addresses or telephone numbers, and do not link online actions to an identifiable person.
