Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Privacy Act 1993.

General

If you decide to purchase any travel products from us you will need to provide personal information necessary for us to provide you with the service you have asked for. In turn we may, and you agree, that this information may be disclosed to our service partners (who may be third parties) for the purpose requested.  The information requested will typically include your name, address, telephone number, and email address and the names of any other passengers traveling with you.  To pay for the services you will also need to provide your credit card details.

House of Travel does not retain your credit card details for security reasons so they will need to be re-entered each time you purchase a service from us.

Cookies
Should you choose to create a profile on our website we may on some occasions store a cookie on your computer. The information stored in the cookie allows us to personalize your visit and can help you obtain information you have requested.

You can always choose to disable cookies from being stored on your computer by changing your browser settings, however this may result in a limited experience of our functionality and services

Accessing your information and retention of information
In accordance with the Privacy Act 1993 you may at any time access and change your personal information or subscribed services. We will take all reasonable steps to keep your information secure and we request that you keep your information as current as possible so that we may continue to improve our service to you.
