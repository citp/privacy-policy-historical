INTERNET PRIVACY POLICY

At AHFC, your privacy is important to us. We respect the confidentiality of our customer information and are dedicated to consumer trust. Our goal is to protect your information both online and within our company. This statement explains our privacy policies and procedures and applies to everyone who visits this Web Site.

Information Sharing.

You can visit this Site without sharing personally identifiable information.

You can visit this Site without telling us who you are and without revealing any personally identifiable information to learn about AHFC, the company, our product information, and special offers and promotions. You can also access financial planning tools.

"Personally identifiable information" refers to any information that identifies or can be used to contact or locate you such as name, address, phone number, email address, social security number, and account number. Use of certain features and tools on this Site requires that you voluntarily provide us with personally identifiable information.

We may request personally identifiable information in order to enhance your experience on this Site.

Email-to-a-friend: If you wish to share information that you view on a page of this Site with a friend, we will email the web page to your friend for you. In order to do so, we will request that you provide us with your name, email address, and your friend's email address. This information is collected and used only in a manner appropriate to fulfill your request.

Apply online for Pre-Approved Credit: If you choose to submit an application to AHFC for pre-approved credit, together we will request that you provide personally identifiable information such as name, address, telephone number, social security number, date of birth, employment information, and gross monthly income. This information is collected and is used only in a manner appropriate to process your request for pre-approved credit. For example, we may use this information to investigate your credit, employment, and income to assist in our credit decision-making process.

AHFC EasyPay Enrollment: If you are already an AHFC customer, you may decide to enroll in our convenient AHFC EasyPay program and authorize AHFC to automatically withdraw scheduled monthly payments from your bank checking or savings account. In order to enroll, we will request that you provide personally identifiable information such as your AHFC account number, the last four digits of your social security number, your bank's name and routing number, and your bank account type (checking or savings) and account number. This information is collected and used in a manner appropriate to process your AHFC EasyPay enrollment request.

Owner Link: Owner Link "My Finance" is operated by our affiliate, American Honda Motor Co., Inc. If you are an AHFC customer and you choose the convenience of online account management by registering for Owner Link, together we will request personally identifiable information such as your email address, your AHFC account number, and your social security number. This information is collected and used to provide you with "My Profile" which is a summary of your account with us. In addition, we will be able to provide you with "My Finance" which contains your personal account information including current monthly statement, account activity, payoff quotes, and transaction history. Providing this information will also enable you to access "My Vehicle" which will provide you with important information concerning your vehicle such as maintenance schedules, roadside tips, FAQs, and dealer contact information. You are encouraged to see the Owner Link Privacy Policy for further privacy information. In addition, if you choose to utilize our online bill payment option, we will request that you provide information about your bank, your bank account type (checking or savings) and account number in order for us to activate your ability to pay bills online.

We collect and use anonymous information.

We will gather and store certain anonymous information about your visit, including the name of your Internet Service Provider (ISP), the type of browser and operating systems used to access our site, the date and time you access our site, and the pages your visit (click-through). This information does not identify you personally. This information is collected and used to generate statistics, measure site activity, and improve the usefulness of this site.

We use cookies.

A "cookie" is a small text file or text identifier placed on your computer. We use cookies to provide you with a convenience feature to save you time and provide a more personalized experience on our site. For example, you may log on once during a visit and will be able to access multiple servers without having to log on again and again. We use cookies provided by ClearTrust and WebTrends ecommerce software. You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Some portions of Owner Link, however, require the use of cookies for proper function. These cookies do not collect personally identifiable or confidential information. Click here for more information about cookies.

Information Use.

The information you share with AHFC is used to improve the services we provide you and to provide services to you that you request. To provide these services (such as OwnerLink "My Vehicle"), we may share some information with our affiliate, American Honda Motor Co., Inc. In addition, if you enroll in AHFC EasyPay or Pay Bills Online, we will be required to validate your bank account information with your bank. Your information is not shared with or sold to any other third party company for that company's independent use. In situations where required by law, AHFC may share this information with law enforcement officials such as in response to a court order or subpoena. AHFC customers are encouraged to see our Financial Privacy Policy below to learn more about our protections and use of your nonpublic personal information.

Security.

Together, AHFC and American Honda Motor Co., Inc. have has reasonable security measures in place to protect your personally identifiable information. This information is protected while being transmitted thru a Secured Sockets Layer (SSL) connection. All web pages that display personally identifiable information will be encrypted using 128-bit SSL which is an industry standard. In addition, we have internal security procedures and policies in place to safeguard against loss, theft, unauthorized access, destruction, misuse, and modification of this information after we receive it. If you are an OwnerLink registrant, you can help protect your personally identifiable information by selecting the "Log Out" icon before you close your browser.

You may update your information.

If you choose to enroll in our AHFC EasyPay Program, you can change or update your information by clicking on "Change" button off the AHFC EasyPay home page. In addition, if you choose to register for OwnerLink, you can update your information by clicking "Update" button off the OwnerLink "My Profile" page.

Links.

This Web site contains links to other sites. For example, you can directly access Kelley Blue Book and state property tax information from convenient links on this Site. However, please be aware that if you access these links you will be leaving our site. As a result, we encourage users to read the privacy policies of those sites as we are not responsible for their content, links, or privacy procedures.

Policy revisions. If we decide to change our privacy policies and procedures, we will post those changes to this privacy policy so our users are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it. We will use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected. We suggest periodically visiting this page to review our policies and procedures.

FINANCIAL PRIVACY POLICY

Our Financial Privacy Policy applies to the sections of the site where consumer credit pre-approval information is collected and to all Honda Financial Services customers. Our financial privacy policy explains how we handle your Nonpublic Personal Information. This Privacy Statement is given on behalf of the AHFC Family of Companies (affiliates):

  • American Honda Financial Services Corporation
  • American Honda Service Contract Corporation
  • Honda North America, Inc.
  • American Honda Receivables Corp.
  • American Honda Receivables Corp. II
  • Honda Titling Inc.
  • Honda Funding, Inc.
  • Honda Titling A-D LLCs
  • Honda Titling A-D LPs
  • Honda Lease Trust
Protecting your privacy is important to us. At the AHFC Family of Companies, we only permit access to Nonpublic Personal Information about you to our employees who need to know that information to provide products or services to you and to service your account.

We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to guard your Nonpublic Personal Information.

We collect NonPublic Personal Information about you from the following sources:
  1. Information we receive from you on credit application, or other forms, such as your name, address, social security number, income and obligations;
  2. Information we receive from consumer reporting agencies, such as your creditworthiness and credit history; and
  3. Information about your transactions with us, our affiliates, or others, such as the products you have purchased, financed or leased with the Honda Financial Services Family of Companies, your retail or lease account balance and payment history.
We may disclose information that we collect to the Honda Financial Services Family of Companies (affiliates), to companies that perform marketing services on our behalf or with whom we have joint marketing agreements and to nonaffiliated third parties, as described below.
  1. AHFC Family of Companies. We may disclose among the Honda Financial Services Family of Companies, including American Honda Motor Co., Inc., information about your direct transactions such as account balance and payment history, as a present or former customer of AHFC, or of the affiliate to which your account may be assigned and for which AHFC provides servicing.

  2. Companies that perform marketing services on our behalf or with which we have joint marketing agreements. We may disclose all of the information (1-3 above) that we collect about you as a present or former customer to nonaffiliated financial institutions, including automobile dealers, with which we have joint marketing agreements.

  3. Nonaffiliated third parties. We may disclose all of the information (1-3 above) that we collect about you as a present or former customer to financial service providers, such as banks, vendors or service companies that provide statement printing and mailing services or other types of servicing for your account(s) with AHFC or with the affiliate to which your account may be assigned and for which AHFC provides servicing.

We may also disclose Nonpublic Personal Information about you to nonaffiliated third parties as permitted or required by law, such as information provided to credit reporting agencies or in response to a subpoena. If we decide to change our financial privacy policies and procedures, we will post those changes to this privacy policy. We suggest periodically visiting this page to review our policies and procedures.
