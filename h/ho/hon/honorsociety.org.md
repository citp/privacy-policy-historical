# Privacy Policy | Honor Society

Effective on January 17, 2014

We at HonorSociety.org know that your privacy is important. This privacy policy is designed to assist you in understanding how we will collect and use the information you provide when you use our Website. Please read this privacy policy to learn more about the ways in which we collect and use your information. If we make any material changes to this privacy policy, we will notify you in accordance with the process described in the Changes section of this privacy policy.  By using our Website, you consent to our collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this privacy policy. 

This privacy policy applies only to information collected through our website and not to information collected offline.

## Information We Collect

### Information We Automatically Collect.

When you access our Website whether by computer, mobile phone or other device, we automatically collect certain information about your use of our Website (this information may include without limitation: geographical location and IDs of your computer, mobile or other device; bandwidth used;  system and connection performance; browser type and version, operating system; referral source; length of visit;  page views;  referral source; IP address or other unique identifier for your computer, mobile phone or other device; your mobile carrier) using technologies, such as Cookies and web beacons. This information is not linked to personally identifiable information.

### Information You Provide To Us.

In some situations you may provide us with your personal identifying information (called “PII”) PII could include your name, street address, telephone number, email address, answer to security questions, graduation date, profile photograph and, if necessary, social security number, driver’s license number, date of birth and credit card and/or other payment/ordering information and any other information we collect about you that by itself is not PII but if combined with PII could be used to personally identify you.

You may provide us with PII when you:   
enter a sweepstakes or contest,  
complete a survey,  
make a purchase,  
subscribe to our Website services, email notifications and/or newsletters,  
register for certain services, or register and/or set up an account/profile to access, visit and/or use certain portions of our Website  
provide comments, reviews, feedback, or testimonials about our Website or products or services  
any other transactions between you and us on or in relation to our Website

### Refer a Friend

If you choose to use our referral service to tell a friend about our Website we will ask you for your friend’s name and email address.  
We store this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email, tracking the success of our referral program, and following up with additional information if your friend opts in to receiving information from us.

Your friend may contact us at [[email protected]](https://web.archive.org/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#dab7bfb7b8bfa8a9bfa8acb3b9bfa99ab2b5b4b5a8a9b5b9b3bfaea3f4b5a8bd) to request that we remove this information from our database.  
Treatment of your friend’s information will be subject to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

### Cookies

A “Cookie” is a small file stored on your hard drive that contains information about your computer. We may use Cookies to enable us to track and target your interests for the purpose of enhancing your experience on our Website. By showing how and when visitors use the Site, cookies help us deliver advertisements, identify how many unique users visit us, save user preferences and track user trends and patterns. We use:

Session Cookies which are Cookies that are deleted when you leave our Website.  
Persistent Cookies which are Cookies that remain after you leave our Website so that you are recognized when you return.

The use of Cookies is industry standard so your browser may be set to accept cookies. If you would prefer not to accept cookies, you can alter the configuration of your browser to reject all Cookies or some Cookies. Note, if you reject certain Cookies, you may not be able to access all of our Website features.

Our Website may also contain third-party cookies from advertising partners. To opt-out of these type of cookies, visit the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) website by visiting [http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/](https://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/). Even if you opt-out through the NAI website, you can still receive communications you have signed up to receive from us unless you opt out of those by using the information in the Opting-Out and Updating Your Information sections of this privacy policy.

### Web Beacons

We may use web beacons (also known as clear GIFs.) to access Cookies and count users who visit our Website or open our HTML-formatted e-mail messages. Web beacons are single-pixel graphic files with a unique identifier, similar to Cookies. Web beacons are embedded invisibly on Web pages and are stored on a user's hard drive. You can modify your browser settings to control web beacons.

### Sharing PII with Third Parties

We may use third parties to perform certain services on our behalf. We may share your PII including payment card information, with these third parties solely to enable them to perform the services for us.

Such third parties include those:

hosting our Website  
managing the functionality of our Website

delivering products you have ordered

enabling us to send you e-mail

## Security

HonorSociety.org has implemented and follows commercially reasonable measures to protect against unauthorized access to and unlawful interception or processing of PII. Unfortunately, data transmission over the Internet is never 100% secure so we cannot guarantee the security of any information you transmit to us or from our Website therefore you use our Website at your own risk.

If you register on our Website you are responsible for protecting the security of your user name and password. You are responsible for any transaction made using your user name and password.

We use industry standard security measures. All credit card information you submit via our Website is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption.

All payments submitted through our Website are processed by our payment processor Authorize.net. You can review the Authorize.net privacy policy at: [http://www.authorize.net/company/privacy](https://www.authorize.net/company/privacy). We will share information with Authorize.net to the extent necessary for the purposes of processing payments you make, refunding such payments and dealing with complaints and queries relating to such payments and refunds.

Others will be able to view and access it and they may be able to use it and share it with third parties. The protections of this privacy policy will not cover your disclosure of information or content in Public Spaces. Carefully consider anything you intend to post before you post and use caution.

## Opting Out and Changing Or Updating Your Information

You may opt-out of having your PII shared with third parties for reasons other than for the express purpose of delivering the purchased product or service requested and except as required by law and you may also request updates or changes to your information or your preferences regarding receiving future promotional messages from us. To do this you may contact our Privacy Officer using the information in the Contact Us section of this privacy policy.

Please note that if you opt-out of promotional/marketing emails, you may continue to receive certain communications from us, such as administrative and services announcements and/or messages about your account/profile.

### Changing or Updating Your Information

You can review and change or update personal information you have provided and your subscriptions or preferences regarding information you receive from us by logging in to your account and making or requesting changes.  We will implement your requested changes as soon as we reasonably can. When you edit your information or change your preferences, information that you remove may remain in our databases or backup media because it is not always possible to completely remove or delete information from those locations. If you have made any postings in a Public Space of our Website, these cannot generally be removed.

Without your express consent, your information will not be sold, exchanged, transferred, or given to any other entity, other than companies acting as our agents to perform task(s) on our behalf and under our instructions, for any reason that is not compatible with the reason that you originally shared the information (except as described above).

Except as provided in this privacy policy, we will not provide your information to third parties.

## Child Privacy

This Website is not directed at, or intended for use by, children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly allow anyone under 18 to provide any personal information on our Website. Children should always get permission from a parent or guardian before sending personal information over the Internet. If you believe your child may have provided us with personal information, you can contact our Privacy Officer using the information in Contact Us section of this privacy policy and we will delete the information.
