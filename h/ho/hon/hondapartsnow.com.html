Privacy Policy

Hondapartsnow.com, our most priority is ensuring that your personal and payment information is secure, and your privacy is respected. Below is a description of how we maintain online security and respect your privacy.

Online Security
When ever you provide any order information, we use SSL technology to make sure that it is "encrypted," or, scrambled, so that no one else can read it while it is being transmitted over the internet. If you become a member of hondapartsnow.com, all information (such as your name, address, and e-mail address) is safely stored behind a firewall. We store this type of information so that you do not have to reenter it each time you make a purchase or log in to use your account.

Online Privacy

For purposes of this Privacy Policy Statement, "Personal Information" means any information by which you can be personally identified, and includes your name, your address, your telephone number, your e-mail address.

Our site collects Personal Information only when you voluntarily submit such Personal Information to us, such as when placing a product order through the Site, when becoming a member of hondapartsnow.com, and so on.

Hondapartsnow.com does not collect personally identifiable information from any person under the age of thirteen, and if you are under thirteen, please give us the parental contact information or exit this site.

For each visitor to the Site, our web servers automatically recognize information regarding the visitor's domain name (or that of the visitor's access provider) and e-mail address. Regardless of the purpose for which it is submitted, it is our (hondapartsnow.com) policy to maintain all Personal Information as confidential, and to use such Personal Information for the following purposes:

  • For the specific purpose for which it was volunteered (Purchase products, enter contests)

Our consumer list may also be shared with carefully selected firms whose products may be of interest to you.
