PRIVACY POLICY

We are committed to protecting all data that is collected with regard to our clients. When you use our services you agree to the use of that data in accordance with our privacy policy. We in no way collect any personally identifiable information about a visitor when coming to our website unless you choose to provide us that information or as specifically outlined in the privacy policy.

All information collected when you sign up for our services will be securely stored in our billing system. This may include, but is not limited to, first and last name, address, phone number, email address, dates and types of services purchased, payment method, credit card information, etc. Your financial information will only be used to charge for any products or services that are purchased.
