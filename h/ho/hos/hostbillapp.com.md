# HostBill | Privacy Policy | Complete Client Management, Support and Billing Software for WebHosts

This privacy policy document is to demostrate our committment to customer privacy. Informations below explains what information we collect and how it will be used 

**Personal Information**   
HostBill LLC collects only personally identifiable information about customers who supplied it volontarirly during order process on our website. This information is being kept in our database and it will not be shared with and sold to any third parties with the exceptions of financial institutions in cases where identity theft or fraud is suspected. Personally identifiable information are being collected by HostBill only for normal business purposes, to deliver products and services, administer customer accounts, and provide customer support.

**Mailing List**  
All of our customers emails are automatically subscribed to our mailing list. Mentioned mailing list is for sending updates and latest annoucements regarding HostBill, new products, security updates and other informations that may be interesting to hosting companies. As well as personal informations email adresses will not be sold or shared with third parties. 

**Confidentiality and Security Safeguards**  
HostBill LLC takes reasonable precautions to protect personally identifiable information against lost, missuse or unauthorized access. Such informations are only accessible to authorized personel who need it in order to perform their jobs.

**External Links**  
HostBillapp.com website, blog, wiki and most importantly forum (where users can post links freely) may contain various links to external websites. We are not responsible under any circumstances for the content and/or privacy practices of mentioned websites.
