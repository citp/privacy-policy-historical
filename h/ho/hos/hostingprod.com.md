# Yahoo! Privacy Policy

[Yahoo! GeoCities](http://geocities.yahoo.com/) is a free web page publishing service that allows you to create your own home page. In order to create a home page, you must be a registered Yahoo! user. If you create a home page, your home page address will include your Yahoo! ID in the form of: http://www.geocities.com/Yahoo!ID/.

Information Collection and Use Practices

  * When you create your page, Yahoo! asks you for some information describing your page.



Information Sharing and Disclosure Practices

  * Members who subscribe to certain GeoCities premium services will have access to the raw logs for their web site. These logs include information such as the referrer, IP address, and type of browser.
  * If you post personally identifiable information online that is accessible to the public, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return. 
  * Any information collected by a Yahoo! GeoCities member through their home page is not collected or used by Yahoo! and is not covered under the Yahoo! Privacy Policy. 



Other

  * When you use Yahoo! GeoCities, you are subject to the [Yahoo! GeoCities Terms of Service](http://docs.yahoo.com/info/terms/geoterms.html) and [Yahoo! Community Guidelines](http://docs.yahoo.com/info/guidelines/community.html). 
  * Please see [Yahoo! GeoCities Help](http://help.yahoo.com/help/us/geo/) if you have questions about this service.



This page describes current Yahoo! practices with respect to this particular service. This information may change as Yahoo! revises this service by adding or removing features or using different service providers. To find out how Yahoo! treats your personal information, please visit our [Privacy Policy](http://privacy.yahoo.com/privacy/us/).
