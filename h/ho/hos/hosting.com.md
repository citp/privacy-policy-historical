# Hosting.com's Privacy Policy

The creation of the Hosting.com privacy statement demonstrates our firm commitment to protecting the privacy of those with whom we interact. This statement sets forth our information gathering and dissemination practices for the Hosting.com website and newsletter.

  


## Information Collected

Our newsletter and website uses several different forms that customers and site visitors can use to request publications, information, products, and services. From these forms, we may collect contact information (email addresses) or customer identification pieces (customer ID).

  


## What is the level of security?

Hosting.com makes every effort to follow industry standard security measures to prevent the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All customer information is stored in our company databases and is accessible only to internal employees.

  


## What is the Purpose for Collecting this Information?

  


### Contact Information

The purpose for obtaining your contact information is to send you our monthly newsletter, information about our company and to get in touch with you when necessary. Our newsletter publication and website allows you to opt out of receiving communications from us. If you do not wish to receive future publications, please select unsubscribe from the bottom of the Hosting.com newsletter publication or website. For other ways to contact us, please see our Contact Us page. 

### Linked Sites

Our website and newsletter contains links to external Web Sites. Hosting.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these Web sites.
