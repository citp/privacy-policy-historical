# Hong Kong Standard Chartered Marathon

Privacy Policy

  * HKAAA collects personal information when you register as runners of Standard Chartered Hong Kong Marathon 2003 . HKAAA may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies. 
  * When you register we ask for your personal information such as your name, HKID / Passport, nationality, email address, birth date, gender, phone numbers, fax number, address and etc. 
  * HKAAA uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the sports or runners information, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and for general promotions of HKAAA sponsors, authorized related parties, companies and organizations connected with any HKAAA events promotions 

Practices Regarding Cookies

  * Standard Chartered Hong Kong Marathon Online Registration uses cookies to facilitate the checkout process. If you choose to change your browser preferences to disable cookies, you will NOT be able to register. 

Security Practices

  * HKAAA use secure servers to protect all of your personal information including your name, address and credit card information. Please remember that no data transmission over the Internet, or by other means, can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. 

Disclaimer

  * HKAAA expressly disclaims any responsibility for any loss arising out of the online entry in Standard Chartered Hong Kong Marathon 2003 

Enquiries and Change of Registration Data

  * After registration, you can edit your personal information by calling our hotline at 852-2577-0800 or email to hkmarathon@hkaaa.com.
