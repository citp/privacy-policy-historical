Privacy Policy

Hawaiian Airlines is aware of your right to privacy. When you visit the Hawaiian Airlines web site, you can be confident that any information shared with us will always be treated confidentially and will not be disclosed to anyone outside of Hawaiian Airlines or its Preferred Partners.

E-mail Addresses:
The e-mail addresses provided to Hawaiian Airlines or HawaiianMiles will not be shared with any third parties, except as described below. If you wish to subscribe or unsubscribe from any of Hawaiian Airlines mailing lists or need more information about the mailing list, please call us toll-free at 877-HA-MILES (426-4537).

Why Our Responses to Your E-Mail May Be Slow:
We want to provide you with the best possible Customer Service by responding to your concerns and questions in a timely manner. At the moment, due to the high volume of e-mail requests for information, our ability to respond to e-mail in a manner consistent with our service goals is not yet up to speed. If you feel you have not received a response to your inquiry on a timely basis please feel free to write us at the address below.

Your Hawaiian Airlines PIN (Personal Identification Number):
Your Hawaiian Airlines Membership number is unique to your account and used to identify you and your transactions for record purposes. In addition, you have a personal PIN number that serves as your personal password to your account and is designed to protect your account from unauthorized use. Hawaiian Airlines will not be responsible for any lost, stolen, or otherwise disclosed PIN numbers. Additionally, Hawaiian Airlines will not replace or reinstate any flight credits or HawaiianMiles, partner miles, or program awards that are generated or redeemed by unauthorized PIN use. Accordingly, your PIN number should be guarded and not provided to anyone.

Billing Information for Ticketless Travel (E-Tickets) Online:
Any information provided to purchase Tickets or E-Tickets Online (including billing name, billing address, telephone number, fax number, and travel details of the reservation) will not be shared with anyone outside of Hawaiian Airlines nor to any third party, except as described below. Payment information (credit card numbers and expiration dates) is also treated confidentially and will not be shared with anyone outside of the financial institutions used to process payments.

Hawaiian Airlines and HawaiianMiles Special Offers:
Hawaiian Airlines may use the name and address information or the e-mail address you provide to send you special offers that we feel may be interesting and useful for you. In some instances, we may provide Customer lists to our Preferred Partners so you can receive special offers or ways to earn HawaiianMiles awards more quickly. Hawaiian Airlines requires our Preferred Partners to ensure safeguards are always in place to protect the privacy of any information we share. We try to be careful in our selection of these Partners to ensure your privacy and the value of their offers. If you wish to be excluded from any such special offers, or have your e-mail address unsubscribed from any electronic offers please contact us toll-free at 877-HA-MILES (426-4537).

You should be aware that we require the same safeguards for the information received over the Internet as we do for customer information received by telephone and mail. Only Hawaiian Airlines or our approved Preferred Partners will send you special offers.
