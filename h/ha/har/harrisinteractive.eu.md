# Terms & Conditions - United Kingdom

Harris Interactive recognizes the privacy concerns of visitors to its site on the Internet. Specifically, Harris Interactive recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of personally identifiable information collected about the visitors to its corporate website.

As a general policy, no personally identifiable information, such as your name, address, or email address, is automatically collected when you visit the site. Harris Interactive may collect certain non-personal information, which is used only to optimize web pages for your computer (e.g., the identity of your Internet browser, the type of operating system you use, and the domain name of your Internet service provider). This non-personal information may also be used to determine the total number of visitors to its website. Personally identifiable information about visitors to the site is collected only when knowingly and voluntarily submitted. In those instances when Harris Interactive does collect such information, personally identifiable information that is submitted knowingly and voluntarily by visitors to the Harris Interactive site is retained by Harris Interactive and is not sold or otherwise transferred to an outside company, unless otherwise stated at the time of collection.

#### Use of Cookies

Harris Interactive does not use cookies to obtain any personal information about users.

#### Notice

Harris Interactive will notify you when it is collecting personally identifiable information about you, as well as the use or uses we intend to make of such information. In all cases, information is used in aggregate. No personally identifiable information is reported or used.

#### Explanation of Policy

By using this site, you agree to the Privacy Policy of Harris Interactive. If you do not agree to this Policy, please do not use this site. Harris Interactive reviews its Privacy Policy periodically and reserves the right, at its discretion, to modify or remove portions of this Policy at any time. This page should be reviewed from time to time so that you are updated on any changes.
