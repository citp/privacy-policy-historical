# Crapville - Privacy Policy

| **Information Automatically Logged:**  
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is also used to help identify you for on-line voting.  
---  
**Cookies:**  
We use an affiliate ad serving company that places a cookie on your machine to ensure you do not receive any pop-up advertisements or related content more than once in a 24 hour period.  
**External Links:**  
This site contains links to other sites. Crapville.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites.   
**Public Forums:**  
Crapville.com extends the ability to our visitors to sign up and post content on our free message boards. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in the forums becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.  
**Crapville Citizens & Videos:**  
All pictures and videos of the crapville citizens are submitted with the permission and forehand knowledge of the sender that they will have their picture and/or video placed on the website for the public to view.  
**Newsletter:**  
If you choose to become part of the periodic newsletter you can always unsubscribe by following the instructions at the bottom of the e-mail. If you are unable to do this, please contact us and we will manually remove you from list. All people on the newsletter are opt-in subscribers and are never tricked into receiving the newsletter.  
**Ad Servers:**  
With respect to Ad Servers: To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information. For more information, consult the privacy policies of:

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this web site, you can [contact the webmaster](https://web.archive.org/web/20060615214052id_/http%3A//www.crapville.com/contact_info.asp).
