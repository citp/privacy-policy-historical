Return / Cancellation Policy

Damaged item/s must be reported to us via email; info@hallohallomall.com (item photo must be attached) and the shop owner thru the Message Board immediately within 24 hours upon your receipt.

*Note: Exchange of items applies only for DAMAGED items. We do not allow return or exchange on merchandise that does not meet your expectations in fitting / liking. That's why it is very essential to check first the SIZE CHART OF EACH PRODUCT before submitting any orders.
