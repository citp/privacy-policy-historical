# afterglow privacy policy | mineral cosmetics | natural cosmetics | organic makeup

Privacy Policy

Privacy Statement  
Your online privacy is of the utmost importance to us. To ensure your privacy we secure your transaction with 128 bit encryption. In addition, we never sell or rent your personal information to any third parties under any circumstances. Your trust in our company and the security of your personal information are our primary concern. Our Afterglow Cosmetics servers only collect the IP address, domain name and user specific information on the pages you access or visit. We also collect any e-mail or correspondence and shipping information when creating an account or e-mailing us directly.

Transaction Security  
When you place an order online, your personal information and credit card information are encrypted using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption technology before being sent over the Internet. This technology makes it impossible for your information to be stolen or intercepted while being transferred and processed by PayPal or our Merchant Account for processing. The entire credit card transaction is done by the highly respected, trusted third-party, [PayPal Corp](https://www.paypal.com/) and our merchant account using bank level encryption. All PayPal and credit card transaction from our web site are protected by high levels of encryption with the use  
of firewalls, as well as other protective measures to ensure that your private information is completely secure during the transaction. All information held by Afterglow Cosmetics, PayPal and our merchant account is held as strictly confidential.

E-mail Privacy  
We will only use this contact information to improve your experience when visiting our site and notify you of Afterglow products, sales, and events.  
If you do not wish to receive any promotional/sales simply click on the 'safe unsubscribe' button on the e-mail. This will permanently remove you from  
our e-mail list. Alternatively, e-mail us requesting to “unsubscribe” to our mailings. Please know that we would never share, rent or sell your e-mail address  
or contact information with other organizations. If you have any other comments or concerns about e-mail you receive from us please e-mail us at [abuse at afterglowcosmetics dot com](mailto:abuse@afterglowcosmetics.com).

Phone Number Privacy  
We will only contact you by phone if we have a question or concern about your order. We will not call you for any other purpose or share your phone number with other companies.

Snail Mail Privacy  
We receive your mailing address online when you set up an account or order a product. You may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products, sales, and events. If you prefer not receive our mailings, please e-mail us at [support at afterglowcosmetics dot com](mailto:support@afterglowcosmetics.com). Please include your name as it appears on your account and your complete mailing address requesting removal.

Notification of Changes to the Customer Service and Privacy Policies  
We reserve the right to revise our Customer Service Policy, Privacy Policy and Store Policies as needed. Any changes or revisions to the Afterglow Cosmetic policies will posted here. We reserve the right to make changes at any time without notifying you, the customer. We suggest that you periodically consult this Privacy Statement & Store Policies. Please note that our rights to use your personally identifiable information will be based on the privacy policy in effect at the time the information is used.
