# Hallpass - Privacy Policy

In order to join [Hallpass.com](http://www.hallpass.com/), users are required to provide a unique username and password. To participate in the forums, a unique username, password, and e-mail address are required as well as the agreement to conform to the rules of the forums. Forum members must be over the age of 13 to register. Neither the Administrators nor the Moderators participating in the forums and game server are responsible for the privacy practices of any user. 

Remember that all information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to share any of your personal information. Any user who finds material posted by another user objectionable is encouraged to contact us via [email](http://www.hallpass.com/contactUs.html).

We are authorized to remove or modify any data submitted by you to the website and forums for any reason we feel constitutes a violation of our policies, whether stated, implied or not. This site may contain links to other web sites and files. We have no control over the content and can not ensure it will not be offensive or objectionable. We will, however, remove links to material that we feel is inappropriate as we become aware of them. 

Cookies must be turned on in your browser to participate as a user in the game server and forums. Cookies are used here to hold your username and password and viewing options, allowing you to login. 

You agree that you will not post any copyrighted material that is not owned by yourself or the owners of this website or forums. In your use of this website and forums, you agree that you will not post any information which is vulgar, harassing, hateful, threatening, invading of others privacy, sexually oriented, or violates any laws. 

During registration of an account, a user is required to create a unique username and password. Since new accounts are free, it is the user's responsibility to remember their username and password. 

Spam? You think you hate spam, well we hate it just as much if not more. We do not sell out email addresses to anyone. Not our advertisers, marketing companies, people with fetishes for email addresses. Not anyone. We will not give out your email address to anyone. Period. 

**Third Party Advertising**

We use third-party advertising companies to serve advertisements to you on our website. These companies (1) use information obtained from your visits to this website and other sites to serve ads to you and (2) place unique third-party cookies on your computer through your browser. Also, we use Web beacons provided by third-party advertising companies to help optimize our advertising. Web beacons enable us to recognize your browser when a cookie has been placed on your computer. For more information on the privacy policy and data collection of our third party advertisers, please visit their website and privacy policy here [Betawave Privacy Policy](http://www.betawave.com/privacy.html). Some but not all third party advertising companies provide a mechanism to opt-out of their technology. For more information and to opt out of advertisers that provide an opt-out mechanism, [please click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/consumer/opt_out.asp).
