# Havertys Furniture - Privacy Statements

**Havertys Website Privacy Statement**  
This site is controlled and operated by the Haverty Furniture Companies, Incorporated and Subsidiaries (Havertys). This statement discloses the privacy practices for the Havertys website. Included are: 

  * What personally identifiable information of yours and/or third party personal identification is collected from you through the website.  

  * How the information is used.   

  * With whom the information may be shared.   

  * The site's policy on removing personal information from our database.



Protecting your personal information is of the utmost importance to Havertys. Therefore, we have prepared this privacy statement to inform you as to how we will use the information you entrust to us.

**Information Collection and Use** Havertys is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information in ways different from what is disclosed in   
this statement.
