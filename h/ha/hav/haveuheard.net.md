# Privacy PolicyHaveUHeard

Privacy Policy

* * *

Have U Heard is an entertainment news and celebrity gossip website.  The Site is strictly for entertainment purposes.  Have U Heard does not verify the reliability of the content published.   Have U Heard recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of personally identifiable information collected about visitors to the Site. Except with respect to certain designated elements of the Site that require registration, you can use HaveUHeard.net  without divulging any personally identifiable information, including your email address. The Site does not collect personally identifiable information about you unless you expressly provide such information to us on a voluntary basis.

Cookies are not utilized internally by Have U Heard for data gathering. The publishers do not attempt to gather any personal information about you during the course of visiting our website.
