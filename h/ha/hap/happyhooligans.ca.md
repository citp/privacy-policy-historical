# privacy policy - Happy Hooligans

Happy Hooligans highly values your privacy.  I do not share personal information with third parties nor do I store any information about your visit to this blog other than to analyze and optimize your overall  experience through the use of cookies.

**What is a cookie?**

Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computers hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the sites or service providers systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information.

Cookies are used as a part of our commenting system so you don’t have to enter your details every time, and to collect information for Google Analytics, so I can better understand the viewing habits of my followers.  This helps me to know the best times for me to publish my posts, and which posts are most popular with folks like you!

We also use cookies to keep track of advertisements and compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future.

_You_ _can turn off the use of cookies at anytime by changing your specific browser settings._

**_DoubleClick DART Cookie_**

Google, a third party vendor, uses the DART cookie to serve ads on Happy Hooligans. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting [Google’s Content Network Privacy Policy](http://www.google.com/privacy/ads/).

**_Amazon Affiliates Cookie_**

Happy Hooligans is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com and Amazon.ca.

Amazon Affiliates, a third party system, uses cookies to serve advertisements and links to products on Amazon.com.  You may read more about Amazon Affiliates and their [Operating Agreement here](https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/gp/associates/agreement?ie=UTF8&pf_rd_t=501&ref_=amb_link_84018271_6&pf_rd_m=ATVPDKIKX0DER&pf_rd_p=&pf_rd_s=assoc-right-1&pf_rd_r=&pf_rd_i=assoc_join_menu).

 We are not responsible for republished content from this blog on other blogs or websites without our permission.

This privacy policy is subject to change without notice and was last updated on April 21, 2013. If you have any questions feel free to contact me at jackie@happyhooligans.ca.
