At Happy Place we believe information privacy is an important issue. Therefore, we have developed the following policy. This policy may be updated, revised and re-posted from time to time. This policy applies to all visitors to happyplace.com. By visiting Happy Place, and any other linked pages, features, content, or application services offered from time to time by Happy Place in connection therewith (collectively, the "Site"), or using any of our services, you acknowledge that you accept the practices and policies outlined in this Privacy Policy.

WE COLLECT INFORMATION, BUT WE RESPECT INDIVIDUAL PRIVACY
We collect certain personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") to facilitate card sends, card pickups, and the personalization of our Site. You can choose not to provide us with certain information, but then you may not be able to take advantage of many of our features. The Personal Information you provide is used for such purposes as answering questions, sending product updates, fulfilling orders for Happy Place merchandise, targeting advertisements to you, and communicating with you about our products and services, including specials and new features. The types of Personal Information we may collect include:

E-mail Addresses:
We do not use the e-mail address contained in an E-Card for direct marketing purposes nor do we compile e-mail addresses to build mailing lists to sell to or share with marketers. We do use these addresses to facilitate card delivery and to assist in card pickups. E-mail addresses are also used help protect a recipient's privacy right to know the identity of a card sender. We also have an e-mail newsletter and periodically send e-mails to our subscribers. When you receive e-mail or our newsletter from Happy Place, you can opt out of receiving further communications by following the included instructions to unsubscribe.

IP addresses and Information Shared with Card Recipients:
Happy Place is not intended as to be used as an anonymous mail service. Card senders share with card recipients certain information beyond the notes they include with their cards, including e-mail addresses and IP addresses. IP ADDRESSES OF CARD SENDERS ARE INCLUDED IN THE E-MAIL MESSAGE HEADER OF ALL CARDS TO PROTECT THE PRIVACY OF CARD RECIPIENTS AND TO PROTECT CARD RECIPIENTS FROM ANONYMOUS ABUSE OR HARASSMENT. Under certain circumstances, recipients, their legal representatives, and/ or law enforcement authorities may be able to combine this with information disclosed by ISPs to determine the source of a particular communication. By using the Site, you knowingly and voluntarily assume any risks associated with such disclosure. This is necessary to facilitate card delivery and to protect the privacy of card recipients.

Summary Demographics:
Demographic and profile data is also collected at our Site. We use this data to tailor a visitor's experience at our Site -- displaying content that might interest visitors, and displaying content according to visitor preferences. Generally, our Site automatically collects usage information, such as the numbers and frequency of visitors to our Site and its components, similar to TV ratings that indicate how many people watched a particular show. Happy Place only uses this data in aggregate form, that is, as a statistical measure, and not in a manner that would identify you personally. This type of aggregate data enables us to figure out how often customers use parts of the Site so that we can make the Site appealing to as many customers as possible, and improve our services. As part of this use of information, we may provide aggregate information to our partners about how our customers, collectively, use our Site or services. We share this type of statistical data so that our partners also understand how often people use the Site, so that they, too, may provide you with an optimal online experience. Again, Happy Place never discloses aggregate information to a partner in a manner that would identify you personally.

Cookies:

  • Cookies may be set upon users' browsers during Site visits, both by happyplace.com and its advertisers. Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your browser to enable our systems to recognize your browser and tell us how and when pages in our Site are visited and by how many people. Our Site may use cookies to track user sessions and to gather Site usage statistics. We also may use cookies to save your Site customization preferences and to save user passwords so they don't have to be re-entered upon each site visit.
  • Most browsers have an option for turning off the cookie feature, which will prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, as well as (depending on the sophistication of your browser software) allowing you to decide on acceptance of each new cookie in a variety of ways. We strongly recommend that you leave the cookies activated, however, because cookies may enable you to take advantage of some of our Site's most attractive features.
  • Ads appearing on the Site may be delivered to users by our advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Happy Place and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

Subscriptions:
Our Site may permit you to register as a subscriber. If so, the subscription registration form requires users to give us their email address. Users may opt-out of receiving mailings. If you register with Happy Place, your user profile information including your user name, email address, and other information you enter may be displayed to other users to facilitate user interaction within the Site. We may ask for other demographic information when you register, but you can refrain from providing it. Registered users' e-mail addresses will not be directly revealed to other users by Happy Place, except, when a user has chosen to include their email address in their User Profile. If you register with Happy Place, your Happy Place account Personal Information is protected by a password for your privacy and security. You need to ensure that there is no unauthorized access to your account and Personal Information by selecting and protecting your password appropriately and limiting access to your computer and browser by signing off after you have finished accessing your account. If you do not wish to receive email or other mail from us, please indicate this preference during the registration process.

Billing and Shipping Information:
We or our partners may also collect billing and shipping information, such as billing name and address and credit card number, if you make purchases on the Site.

Correspondence:
If you send Happy Place correspondence, via e-mail, fax, or regular mail, or if other users or third parties such as businesses send us correspondence about your activities in connection with your use of the Site, Happy Place may retain that information.

Happy Place does not collect Personal Information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided Happy Place with Personal Information without the consent of his or her parent or guardian, please contact us at feedback@happyplace.com. If Happy Place becomes aware that a child under age 13 has provided us with Personal Information, we will delete such information.You should also be aware that if you submit information to "chat rooms," "forums" or "message boards" such information becomes public information, meaning that you lose any privacy rights you might have with regards to that information. Such disclosures may also increase your chances of receiving unwanted communications.

SITES TO WHICH WE LINK
Happy Place does contain advertising and content links to other sites. We do not police the privacy practices of these linked sites nor are we responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. Individuals visiting these links should be attentive to the policies and practices of these sites and act with discretion.

UNDER WHAT CONDITIONS IS YOUR HAPPY PLACE PRIVACY RELINQUISHED?
Happy Place may disclose your Personal Information to our agents, as well as some third party suppliers and service providers to directly or indirectly provide you with services or information, and we may need to share your Personal Information with them in order to permit them to provide such services or information. If you make purchases on the Site, you or we may disclose your credit card information to financial institutions involved with the processing of your credit card transactions solely to facilitate your payments and/or credits related to your purchases, and we may disclose your billing and shipping information to agents who assist with the shipping and delivery of merchandise. We require all such third parties to whom you or we disclose your Personal Information to adhere to the restrictions in this Privacy Policy, to keep your Personal Information confidential, and to use your Personal Information only to provide you with the information and/or services you request.

Happy Place may release Personal Information to legal, corporate or internet authorities if there is reasonable suspicion that an individual has not complied with our Terms of Service. Examples of such non-compliance include the use of our card system for harassment, SPAM, profanity, pornographic dissemination or illegal activity. We may release your Personal Information to comply with legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena or court order; to protect the company's rights and property; to investigate reports of users sending material using a false e-mail address or users sending harassing, threatening, or abusive messages; to protect against misuse or unauthorized use of our website and/ or the happyplace.com service; or during emergencies, such as when we believe someone's physical safety is at risk.

Additionally, we employ other companies and people to perform tasks on our behalf and need to share your information with them to provide products or services to you. Unless we tell you differently, Happy Place���s agents do not have any right to use Personal Information we share with them beyond what is necessary to assist us. You hereby consent to our sharing of Personal Information for the above purposes. Additionally, we may send offers to you on behalf of other businesses. However, when we do so, we do not give the other business your name and address. If you do not wish to receive these offers, please send an email with your request to feedback@happyplace.com. In some cases, we may choose to buy or sell assets. In these types of transactions, customer information is typically one of the business assets that are transferred. Moreover, if Happy Place, or substantially all of its assets were acquired, or in the unlikely event that Happy Place goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, customer information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of Happy Place may continue to use your Personal Information as set forth in this policy.

WHAT PERSONAL INFORMATION CAN I ACCESS?
Happy Place allows you to access the following information about you for the purpose of viewing, and in certain situations, updating that information. This list will change as the Site changes.

  • Real name
  • Account and user profile information
  • User e-mail address
  • Username and password
  • User preferences

WHAT CHOICES DO I HAVE?
As stated previously, you can always opt not to disclose information, even though it may be needed to take advantage of certain features of the Site and services. You are able to add or update certain information on pages, such as those listed in the "What Personal Information Can I Access?" section above. When you update information, however, we often maintain a copy of the unrevised information in our records. You may request deletion of your Happy Place account by sending an e-mail to feedback@happyplace.com. Please note that some information may remain in our records after deletion of your account. Please note that if you do not want to receive legal notices from us, such as this Privacy Policy, those legal notices will still govern your use of the Website, and you are responsible for reviewing such legal notices for changes.

SECURITY

Information collected by Happy Place is stored in secure operating environments that are not made generally available to the public. Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, Happy Place cannot ensure the security of any information you provide, and you do so at your own risk. Once Happy Place receives your transmission, it will make reasonable efforts to ensure its security on its systems, and will restrict access to your personally identifying information to employees who require that information, at all time complying with this Policy.
