# Hach Privacy Statement

**Hach Privacy Statement**

Hach Company recognizes the importance of the privacy issue in use of the Internet and World Wide Web. Information which you provide to us either directly (through on-line forms or via e-mail) or indirectly (data normally collected on traffic to this site) is covered by our Privacy Policy:

_**Hach Privacy Policy**_

Hach Company holds in confidence all information (such as name, address, and phone number) given us by customers and visitors to this site. We will not sell or release this information to any organization outside of Hach Company without your knowledge and consent. Information you provide is used to support your customer relationship with Hach, and may be shared on a restricted basis with agents or contractors directly performing services for us.

Hach Company may from time to time contact you with news about products or special offers which we feel could be of interest you. You may at any time ask for more information about our privacy policy, or request that we remove your name from our mailing list, by using our [on-line forms](https://web.archive.org/web/20010410023311id_/http%3A//www.hach.com/forms.htm). We reserve the right to retain certain customer information which directly relates to billing, account status, and product warranties.
