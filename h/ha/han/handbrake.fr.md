# HandBrake: Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to the HandBrake Team. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that this website collects, and the ways in which we use that personal information.

**Information We Collect**

  * Information you enter into this website. For example, a username, password, email address, any information that you fill out on your account profile, any content that you post on our community forums, or any content you post on our code review site.
  * When you visit this website we maintain logs of each page that you visit. This includes your internet address and possibly the operating system, browser version, and other information that may be in your browser's http headers.



**What do we use your information for?**

  * To maintain and improve this website.
  * To allow us to offer features such as the community forums and code review tooling for developers.



**Use of Cookies**

Our website uses cookies.

A cookie is a file containing an identifier (a string of letters and numbers) that is sent by a web server to a web browser and is stored by the browser. The identifier is then sent back to the server each time the browser requests a page from the server.

This website publishes Google Adsense interest-based advertisements on this website. These are tailored by Google to reflect your interests. To determine your interests, Google will track your behavior across the web using cookies.

  * You can view, delete or add interest categories associated with your browser using Google's Ads Preference Manager, available at: <http://www.google.com/ads/preferences/>
  * You can opt-out of the Adsense partner network cookie at: <http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html> However, this opt-out mechanism uses a cookie, and if you clear the cookies from your browser your opt-out will not be maintained. To ensure that an opt-out is maintained in respect of a particular browser, you should use the Google browser plug-in available at: <http://www.google.com/ads/preferences/plugin>



Please be aware that our Community Forum and Review Board both use session cookies and these are required for their operation. By using these services you consent to their use.

**Storage, Usage, and Disclosure of your data**

This website and any data you provide is hosted on servers located in France. We reserve the right to change the location and country where these servers are located at any time.

Please be aware that any information that you enter into this site should be considered public. It will be accessible by any other users and third parties. Only your account login information (username and password) can be considered private.

We will take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse, or alteration of your account login information. Any other information you enter is at your own risk.

We may disclose your personal information to the extent that we are required to do so by law or in connection with any ongoing or prospective legal proceedings.

**Third Party and User Content**

  * Our website includes hyperlinks to, and details of, third party websites.
  * Our website also includes user created content.



We have no control over, and are not responsible for, the privacy policies and practices of third parties.

**Amendments to this Policy**

We may update this policy from time to time by publishing a new version on our website. The last time this document was updated is printed at the bottom of this policy.

**Contacting Us**

If you wish to contact us regarding this policy, please use the [Report Problem](https://handbrake.fr/report.php) page and select the Contact Us tab.
