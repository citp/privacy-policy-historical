# Hammacher Schlemmer

  
  
Thank you for shopping at Hammacher Schlemmer. We fully recognize our responsibility to protect your customer information and its confidential nature. We have done our best to ensure the privacy and security of your transactions with us. Your credit card number and other personal data will be treated with the highest standards of safety, security features, and confidentiality. 

**Secure Shopping**  
Protecting your order information is a priority. We utilize SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) to ensure safe, secure transactions. With SSL, information sent via the Internet is encrypted, and can be trusted to arrive privately and unaltered. We believe this element provides the most effective way to ensure that your order information is protected while offering a very secure shopping environment and the most effective way to ensure that your order information is protected. 

Please note that email is not encrypted and is not considered a secure means of transmitting credit card numbers. If you still have any concerns about ordering electronically, please call 1-800-321-1484 to place your order over the phone. 

**Your Privacy Online**  
Hammacher Schlemmer is committed to ensuring your privacy as a customer. We collect information in several areas on our web site from our visitors. All of the information collected on our web site is solely owned by Hammacher Schlemmer and is not sold or rented unless otherwise stated in this document. 

Hammacher Schlemmer contracts with Coremetrics, a service partner, to provide us with data collection, storage, and reporting services in order to help us provide our products and services to you in the best possible way for the most enjoyable shopping experience. When you visit our web site, Coremetrics collects, on our behalf, first name and email address (if you provide them) as well as your navigation path through hammacher.com. Coremetrics is contractually restricted from using your information in any manner, other than in helping us to make our site more useful to our visitors. For more information, including how to opt out, please click here: <http://www.coremetrics.com/info_eluminate2.html>.

“Cookies” are small bits of information that your browser stores on your computer hard drive. In order to purchase products or to retain items you place in your shopping cart through multiple visits to the Hammacher Schlemmer web site you must have your cookies enabled. We also utilize cookies to give credit to those web sites that direct traffic to hammacher.com for marketing purposes. Hammacher Schlemmer does not use cookies to store or collect personal information such as names, addresses, or payment information.

Hammacher Schlemmer requests personal information from you including your name, billing address, shipping address, telephone number, email address, and credit card number. The customer information you provide is strictly used to process your order and improve your shopping experience. Once you have placed an order with us, we transfer the necessary information to your credit card company in order to acquire confirmation to complete the purchase. We request your email so that we can send email messages regarding the status of your order. If you specifically requested email updates, we will periodically contact you with news about products and services that may be of interest to you. We do not share or sell our customer email list. Your information will not be shared with any other company, other than what was disclosed in this statement regarding our relationship with Coremetrics an our Catalog Mailing List practices.

If you decide that you no longer wish to receive these emails or wish to update your information, please notify us by one of the following three methods: (Please include your full name and address with any correspondence.)

  *  Email us at [customerservice@hammacher.com](mailto:customerservice@hammacher.com)
  

  *  Contact our Customer Service Department directly by calling 1-800-321-1484
  

  *  Write us at 9180 Le Saint Drive; Fairfield, Ohio 45014-5475



**Gift Cards and Gift Messages**  
These options allow you to send a personal greeting with your gift order. We make no use of the information contained in these greetings except to complete your order as you have requested.

**Tell A Friend**  
When you email a product to a friend, you and your friend's email addresses are required. This ensures that your friend will know you have requested that Hammacher Schlemmer send them an email. Any email obtained for the Tell a Friend service will only be used for this purpose. 

**Catalog Requests**  
We ask for a name and address to complete your request. We may share your name and address with other companies whose products we think may be interested to you. We also ask for your email address if you specifically request to receive emails about products and services that may be of interest to you. We do not share or sell our customer email list.

**Catalog Mailing List**  
Hammacher Schlemmer occasionally makes our customer's postal addresses, excluding email, available to screened merchants whose products may be of interest to you. If you do not wish to receive these mailings or wish to update your information, please notify us by one of the following three methods: (Please include your full name and address with any correspondence.)

  *  Contact our Customer Service Department directly by calling 1-800-321-1484
  *  Write us at 9180 Le Saint Drive; Fairfield, Ohio 45014-5475
