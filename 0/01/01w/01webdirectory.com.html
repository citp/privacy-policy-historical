01webdirectory.com and Cookies
   
Upon your first visit to 01webdirectory.com, 01webdirectory.com may send a "cookie" to your computer. Cookies are used by many sites, an internet search on 'cookies' may provide you with more information that you will ever need. The intent of cookies is to identify your computer preferences and searching patterns. 01webdirectory.com will not disclose its cookies to third parties except as required by a valid legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena, statute, or court order.
   
Most internet browser are set up to accept cookie. Although it is possible to reset your computer settings to inform you of cookie or to reject cookies, such actions may interfere with your searches at 01webdirectory.com
