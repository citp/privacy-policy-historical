# Song Lyrics and Soundtrack Lyrics, Music Lyrics, Lyrics Search.

  
The following policy is intended to explain how your personal information which we may obtain when you are using our site will be treated. Personal information includes your name, e-mail addresses, click-through activity and any other personal information you may provide here. 

**Traffic data**   
Each time a visitor comes to a LyricsKeeper.com Web site, our server logs some basic technical information such as browser and platform, visitor's domain name and referral data. We are also processing the visitor's activity through our site. We may present the results of this processing to outside companies, but no one will be able to identify you or contact you because of this data.

**Cookies**   
Companies which place advertising on our site have the ability to assign cookies to you. We may use cookies in some parts of our site (in forms to remember nickname and e-mail you used last time), but we cannot control that cookies coming with banner ads.

**"Send to friend" feature**   
LyricsKeeper.com offers an "e-mail a friend" feature, allowing site visitors to send song lyrics via e-mail to friends. For this feature to function, we need to ask once for e-mail addresses of recipient and sender. We do not store these addresses and you will not receive e-mail or other communications from LyricsKeeper.com as a result of having used this feature.

**"Submit Lyrics" features**   
Visitors of this site have an opportunity to submit new lyrics. LyricsKeeper.com reserves the right not to add submitted lyric.

**Other sites**   
LyricsKeeper.com may contain many links to other sites on the Web. We cannot control and are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these other Web sites.

**Contacting the Web Site**   
If you have any questions or comments about this privacy statement, please, complete our [Contact us](https://web.archive.org/php/contactUs.php) form.
