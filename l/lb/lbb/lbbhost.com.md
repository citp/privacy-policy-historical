# WebTV - MSNTV Hosting, LOWEST PRICES, NO LOST FILES & RELIABLE WebTV Hosting

**Webbywarehouse Privacy Policy.**

**Introduction.**

Webbywarehouse is committed to respecting your online privacy and recognizes your need for appropriate protection and management of any personally identifiable information, "Personal Information", you share with us.

We have established this Online Privacy Policy so that you can understand the care with which we use to protect your privacy, identity, and information.

Personal Information means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, a home or other physical address and an email address or other contact information, whether at work or at home. Webbywarehouse only requires and obtains that information which is essential for us to reasonabily verify and validate those individuals who seek to use the services we provide.  We make reasonable attempts to verify the information of each member to ensure the best service possible to all members.

Webbywarehouse will make every reasonable attempt to comply with all State and Federal laws that protect your privacy.  We do not share any information with any other company(s), individual(s), group(s), entity(s), organization(s), or agency(s).  We will comply with any and all legal court orders that would require us to provide information, to the extent that such an order (i.e. subpenoa) is defined.  Short of a legal court order however, we do not share any member information under any circumstances.

**Cookies and Other Tracking Methods**

Webbywarehouse uses Cookies and Sessions to authenticate members who login to their Webbywarehouse accounts, email accounts, and other Web Features offered by Webbywarehouse.

In some cases Cookies are required to be enabled and properly functioning to gain access to our services.  In those cases if there is a problem with Cookies, it is possible that access to our services may be denied.  The purpose of using Cookies in combination with Session controls, is to provide superior security.  It is through a combination of md5 encrypted Sessions and encrypted Session Cookies, that we can provide the most secure authentication and access for our members.

Webbywarehouse does not gather or track members web activities within Webbywarehouse, or otherwise, via Cookies.  We use Cookies soley for security and privacy purposes.

**Children's Privacy.**

Webbywarehouse is not structured to attract children. Accordingly, we do not intend to collect Personal Information from anyone we know to be under 13 years of age.   In the cases where children have an account with Webbywarehouse, we require the consent of a parent or legal guardian.  Webbywarehouse does not permit any adult or illegal content.  Should any such content, or any other content that is not acceptable as defined in our Terms of Service Agreement be discovered, we will take all appropriate actions to remove such material.  Webbywarehouse is a "Family Friendly" and moral host.
