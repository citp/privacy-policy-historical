Definitions
Affiliates                                      Companies related by common ownership or control. They can be
                                                financial and nonfinancial companies.
                                                     Lake Michigan Financial Group (including Lake Michigan
                                                         Insurance Agency)
Nonaffiliates                                   Companies not related by common ownership or control. They can be
                                                financial and nonfinancial companies.
                                                     LMCU does not share with nonaffiliates.
Joint Marketing                                 A formal agreement between nonaffiliated financial companies that
                                                together market financial products or services to you.
                                                     Our joint marketing partners include insurance and student loan
                                                         companies.
Other Important information
What members can do to help: Protect your account numbers, card numbers, and passwords. Use caution in revealing
account numbers, social security numbers, etc., to other persons.
