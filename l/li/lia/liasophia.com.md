> **lia sophia** is dedicated to protecting your privacy. We do not require any personal information to enter our website. All information gathered on this website is on a voluntary basis. 
> 
> If you choose to process orders or request information from us, you will be required to provide personal data. **lia sophia** will use this for our business purposes only and will not share, sell or barter it. We may provide collective data and statistics about sales, customers, trends and related site information to third party vendors, but this will not include personal information unless you have requested us to do so. If deemed necessary, **lia sophia** will release your information to protect our rights and comply with state and federal laws. 
> 
> **  
> Consent**  
>  By entering this website, you agree to **lia sophia's** collection and use of this information in accordance of this policy. 
> 
> **Collected Information and Its Uses**  
>  When placing an order online we need your name, mailing address, phone number, email address and credit card information with expiration date for processing. 
> 
> When you ask us to locate an Advisor in our "Advisor Locator" we need your name, mailing address and email in order to create your profile. 
> 
> This information will be used for our business purposes only. 
> 
> **  
> Security**  
>  All personal data collected on this website is secure. **lia sophia** has state of the art technology in place to guarantee any personal information you provide is secure and protected from transfer, storage and distribution by using secure sockets layer (SSL) technology. We use digital secure site service from Verisign to provide you with the best protection technology on the Internet.
