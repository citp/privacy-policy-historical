# Little About - Privacy Policy

This policy covers how littleabout.com treats personal information that littleabout.com collects and receives, including information related to your past use of littleabout.com. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that littleabout.com does not own or control, or to people that littleabout.com does not employ or manage. 

Littleabout.com automatically receives and records information from your computer and browser, including your IP address, cookie information, hardware, software, and the page you request. We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

Littleabout.com uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, improve our services, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

[DoubleClick DART cookie](http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/faq.aspx) is used by Google in the ads served on littleabout.com websites.
