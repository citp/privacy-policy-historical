  1. Sign up to receive newsletters, e-mails or other communications,

  3. Request help or other assistance,
  4. Participate in surveys, contests, sweepstakes or promotions offered via the Site,
  5. Otherwise communicate with us via the Site such as commenting on the articles,
  6. Submit credit card or other payment-related information if necessary in connection with a purchase or other transaction.



### **Non-Personal Information**

We may collect some information that does not identify you personally when you use the Site, such as:

  1. Information about your computer, mobile device, or another device that you use to access the Site, which may include IP your address, geolocation information, unique device identifiers, browser type, browser language and other transactional information;
  2. Usage information about your use of the Site, such as a reading history of the pages you view,

  4. Demographic usage data such as age, gender and zip code via analytics applications,
  5. Additional traffic data such as date of access, time of access, session identification number, access times, software crash reports,  and referring website URL.



### **Source IP Address/Location Information**

As seen above, we may collect and store your device’s source IP address. It discloses the location of your device at the time you access the Site. Also, we may provide features on the Site that can deliver content based on your current location. Your current location will be stored on your device or web browser and will be used to serve you to view accurate content tied to your location. If you select to have your location-based searches saved to your sessions, we will store the information on our servers.

### **Use of Cookies**

Like many other websites, our Site may use “cookies”, which are small text files that are stored on your browser or equipment when you visit certain pages of the site and that record your preferences and actions. We use cookies to track the use of our Site and online services. We may also use cookies to monitor user traffic, improve the Site and make it easier or relevant for user experience.

You have the ability to accept or discard cookies. Most modern web browsers automatically accept cookies, but, if you prefer, you can usually modify your browser settings to reject cookies. If you delete the cookies or if you set your web browser to decline cookies, some features of the Site may not work as designed. For more information about cookies and how to disable them, you can see the information provided by the Interactive Advertising Bureau at www.allaboutcookies.org.

We may also work with third-party advertising companies who may utilize cookies to provide better advertisements about goods and services that may be of your interest. Those companies do not have access to visitor name, address, e-mail address, phone number or other Personal Information from us. They may, however, anonymously track your usage of internet across other websites in their networks. Please visit the Network Advertising Initiative to learn more about the use of this information or to opt-out not to have this information used by our third-party advertising partners by clicking http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp.

We may store information about you that we collect from third-party sources to enable us to update and correct the information contained in our database and to customize the better experience with us and the Site.
