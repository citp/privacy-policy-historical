# Privacy Policy | Linqia

## Your Privacy

Linqia is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. Linqia does not share users’ personal data with third parties or non-affiliated companies without users’ prior consent. Only under extraordinary circumstances, Linqia may be required to disclose personally identifiable information, such as to comply with legal requirements or to exercise its legal rights.

By using the website [www.Linqia.com](http://www.linqia.com/) (the Website), owned and managed by Linqia, Inc., you (‘User’) accept the following privacy policy (the ‘Privacy Policy’), which governs the data processing undertaken through the Website and concerning the services found thereon (the ‘Services’).

Users are also aware of Linqia’s Terms and Conditions governing the Services offered on the Website. In the event of inconsistency between this Privacy Policy and the Terms and Conditions, the Terms and Conditions shall prevail.

Linqia is committed to protecting User’s privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how Linqia collects, receives, uses, processes, and shares information concerning the User, including but not limited to User’s name, address, email address, or phone number (‘Personal Data’). When User registers with Linqia, User’s Personal Data and other data will be stored and processed in the United States, and this information will be subject to the laws of the United States. In order to use the Services and provide User’s Personal Data, User must be of legal age or have User’s parent’s permission in accordance with applicable law.

## How We Collect And Use User’s Personal Data

### Registration

Linqia collects Personal Data when User signs up with Linqia to use the Services and each time User uses the Services.

When User signs up, Linqia asks for public and private information. Private User details contain User’s phone number, address and payment details. This information is used only be Linqia, and is always kept private.

Public information will form the User’s public profile and contain first name, last name, photo, bio, gender and country. The details a User provides here will be visible to registered Advertisers to the Linqia Platform.

The Personal Data collected by Linqia by any method will be incorporated in a database called [ldb.Linqia.com](http://ldb.linqia.com/), located in the United States. Data requests are mandatory wherever so indicated in the Website. The purpose of the data processing is to allow Linqia to properly perform the Services and to be able to provide User with best and most complete experience possible. During the performance of the Services, Linqia may require the international exchange or transfer of Personal Data solely for the provision of the Services. By entering Personal Data in the Services or by otherwise disclosing Personal Data, User gives explicit consent to data processing described herein and in particular that User’s Personal Data may be transferred across international borders to a location outside the country of User’s location, always in compliance with applicable law.

Additionally, Linqia may use the data collected for sending commercial communications from Linqia and third party companies that may be of User’s interest and which will be clearly identified in the relevant commercial communications. Linqia will process all the data and will send the communications to the User, not transferring the data to any third party.

Linqia guarantees that it will apply the legally required security measures in order to keep User data strictly confidential and will only use the data for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

Linqia automatically receives and records information on its server logs from User’s browser, including User’s IP address, cookie, and the page User requests

### Use Of Cookies

If User does not wish to have cookies placed on User’s computer or wishes to be notified when they are placed, User may set User’s browser to reject cookies or notify User of their use. If User rejects all cookies, User will not be able to use those parts of the Services that require User to sign in.

Linqia uses its own cookies for a number of purposes, including but not limited to, accessing User’s information when User signs in and keeping track of preferences User specifies while User is using the Services.

### How We Share User’s Personal Data

When an Advertiser creates or sends a message, the Advertiser’s first name, last name and company will be visible to selected  Community Leaders who respond to the campaign.

When a Community Leader receives a message from an Advertiser and accepts the campaign, their community name, URL, first name and last name will be visible to Advertiser.

Linqia does not share User’s Personal Data with third parties or non-affiliated companies without User’s prior consent except to the Services or in the following circumstances:

  * Linqia may be required to disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with legal requirements or to exercise its legal rights;
  * It may be necessary to share Personal Data in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding suspected or actual illegal activities on or through the Services, including but not limited to violations of the Terms and Conditions or infringement of third party intellectual property rights, or as otherwise permitted or required by applicable laws;
  * Linqia may share aggregated demographic information with its business partners. This is not linked to any Personal Data that can identify any individual person without the prior consent of that person; and
  * In the event Linqia enters into a joint venture or is acquired by or merged with another business entity, we may disclose User’s Personal Data to our business partners or new owners.



### Community Leaders

Within the Linqia platform, Linqia offers Community Leaders the opportunity to present information about their community to prospective Advertisers.

Linqia shows the profile of the community that was provided by the Community Leader within the Linqia Platform. The community profile shows the URL address, Community Name, Community Description, number of members, member gender, member age, member location, categories and policies. These are visible to any Advertiser registered to the Linqia Platform.

### Log Files

Each time User accesses the Website, Linqia will save the following data in a log file:

  * User’s IP address (by which User’s computer can be clearly identified),
  * The name and IP address of the computer requesting the respective page from the Website,
  * The time, status, and amount of the transferred data, as well as the Internet website from which User has reached the requested page of the Website,
  * The product and release information from User’s Internet browser.



This log file will be stored by Linqia in the standardized combined log file format for the web server. Linqia uses the data from this log file in anonymous form without reference to User’s persona for statistical purposes. In doing so, Linqia may analyse on which days and at which time the Services are particularly frequented and how much data volume is created on the Website. Furthermore, by using the log file, Linqia can identify defects on the Website, such as defective hyperlinks or program defects or security issues, and thus continually improve the quality of the Website.

Linqia does not attribute the website demands stored in the log file to specific individual Users. However, Linqia reserves the right to review the log files from the last known IP address of User where Linqia has reasonable cause to believe that User is using Linqia Services in breach of contractual agreements or the applicable legislation. In doing this, Linqia is protecting other Linqia Users, the safety of Linqia Users’ data, as well as the Website and the Services.

### Choices For Personal Information

When you sign up for a particular service that requires registration, we ask you to provide personal information. If we use this information in a manner different than the purpose for which it was collected, then we will ask for your consent prior to such use.

If we propose to use personal information for any purposes other than those described in this Privacy Policy and/or in the specific service privacy notices, we will offer you an effective way to opt out of the use of personal information for those other purposes. We will not collect or use sensitive information for purposes other than those described in this Privacy Policy and/or in the supplementary service privacy notices, unless we have obtained your prior consent.

### Editing Or Deleting User’s Account Information / Exercising User’s Rights

With regard to any of User’s Personal Data provided to Linqia, User shall have the rights of access, correction, deletion and objection regarding its personal data. Such rights may be exercised by notice in writing sent to Linqia’s registered address (as shown at the end of this Privacy Policy) or by e-mail sent to privacy [at] [Linqia.com](http://linqia.com/). Moreover, the User may unsubscribe to commercial communications received from Linqia by sending a request to community [at] [Linqia.com](http://linqia.com/).

User can edit User’s Linqia account information at any time and User can request their User account to be deleted at any time by contacting community [at] L[inqia.com](http://linqia.com/); however, please note that User’s identification, billing and contact information may remain in Linqia’s records for some period after the deletion.
