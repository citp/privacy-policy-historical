# 

  
**LinksValley.com** understands and respects your concerns about privacy. But we request you to provide such information about yourself which would help us in understanding your needs better and hence would help us to serve you better. 

Our **Privacy Policy** gives a clear picture to the users about what information might be collected about them and how it would be put to use or shared with third parties to benefit the user in the end. This **Privacy Policy** along with other policies are incorporated into and a part of the “Terms and Conditions of Use” of the site. Any changes made to this policy would be reflected in the Terms and Conditions of Use and it is up to the user to keep abreast of such changes. Any submission to the directory made after a change in the Privacy Policy would be deemed as complete knowledge and acceptance of the change.

** Confidentiality **   
The Information provided to **LinksValley.com** is kept confidential and is used to contact you if you have requested any of our services or products. We do not disclose any information and your Email Address will not be given or sold to any other source. 

This site contains links to other sites. **LinksValley.com** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites
