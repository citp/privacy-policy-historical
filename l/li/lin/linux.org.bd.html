Privacy Policy


We know that you are concerned about your privacy, so are we. You personal information, such as your name, postal address, e-mail address, or other data will be added to our records, only to be able to serve the specific request you have, such as joining a mailing list, registering you for an event, etc.

None of the information collected here will ever be passed on to third parties, nor will this information be used for any purpose other than contacting you with reference to your specific interaction with Bangladesh Linux Users Alliance.

Please note, however, that your participation in mailing lists (by posting to them) will reveal your email address to others. This cannot be constituted as a violation of this privacy policy.
