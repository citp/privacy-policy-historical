Privacy Policy

LineaFashion.com's commitment to privacy LineaFashion.com is committed to protecting the privacy of those using our site and the confidentiality of the personal information which our customers provide us with. We use the information we collect to ensure that orders are processed smoothly. We are keen to make the experience of shopping at LineaFashion.com as engaging as possible, but at no time will be pass on your information without your prior consent.

Your personal information is safe with LineaFashion.com. LineaFashion.com will never sell personal information or share personal information with third parties. At LineaFashion.com we use the information we collect to serve our customers in the following ways:

When you place an order, we need to know your name, e-mail address, delivery address and payment details. This allows us to process and fulfil your orders and to keep you up to date on their progress.

How customers use LineaFashion.com and the feedback they give us helps us develop and enhance the site.

We may also use your contact information to let you know about enhancements to the site, special offers and promotions. If you would rather not receive this information please inform us by e-mail.
