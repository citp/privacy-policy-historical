# Linkury Privacy

We at LINKURY, Inc. and its wholly owned subsidiaries ("LINKURY") are committed to providing web sites and software products that respect the privacy of its visitors and users respectively.  This page summarizes the privacy policy and practices for all LINKURY Web Sites, as well as for the LINKURY software products.

The LINKURY privacy policy is designed to allow the users of its web sites and software applications (the "LINKURY Products") with information allowing them to make informed choices with respect to their use of the LINKURY Products and their provision of personal information.

Your use of LINKURY, and any disputes arising from it, is subject to this Privacy Policy as well as the Terms of Use of particular LINKURY Products and all of its dispute resolution provisions including arbitration, limitation on damages and choice of law. LINKURY reserves the right to change our Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use at any time. Non-material changes and clarifications will take effect immediately, and material changes will take effect within 30 days of their posting on this site. We encourage you to regularly review the LINKURY Product web sites to familiarize yourself with changes which may occur from time-to-time.  If we make changes, we will post them and will indicate at the top of this page the policy's new effective date. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you here, by email, or through notice on our home page. We encourage you to refer to this policy on an ongoing basis so that you understand our current privacy policy. Unless stated otherwise, our current privacy policy applies to all information that we have about you, your account and information we obtain by your use of LINKURY Products or services.

When you use the LINKURY Products, you will be providing two types of information:  information you explicitly provide through interaction with the LINKURY Product, and information which is obtained by virtue of your visiting the LINKURY web sites.In

Information you explicitly provide

When you first register LINKURY Products, you will be asked to provide us with personal information such as your name, an Email address for us to contact you and certain information you wish to share with other users.  Certain aspects of this information will be displayed for others users of LINKURY Products, depending on your selection of privacy settings.  As well, we may occasionally use your name and Email address in order to send you information on products or services, provided by LINKURY or others, which we feel may be of interest to you.  Further, LINKURY may send you service related announcements, such as warning you of upcoming updates, service outages, or other information which we feel you may be interested in.  You will have the option to opt out of some of these announcements, but LINKURY reserves the right to provide certain of these announcements regardless of your opting out of receipt of these announcements, should you choose use the LINKURY Products. 

LINKURY may use information you have provided in registering for, or use in, LINKURY Products without directly or indirectly identifying you, to third parties.  This may be done, for example, in order to identify the number of people visiting a specific web site, or commenting on a certain product, person, or idea.  This may be used to provide advertisements to you on products or services that will potentially be more interesting or relevant to you.  Under no circumstances will we provide information identifying you to a third party, rather we will pass on an advertising announcement to you, but we will not tell the third party who you are.

Information obtained by virtue of your visiting LINKURY web sites

When you use LINKURY Products, we will collect the following information: 

1. The Internet domain and IP address from which you access the LINKURY Products; 

2. Screen resolution of your monitor; 

3. The date and time you access the LINKURY Products; 

4. The page you are visiting with the LINKURY Products; 

5. If you linked to a LINKURY web site from another referring web site, the address of that web site.

By using the LINKURY Products, you are consenting to have your personal data transferred to and processed both within and without the United States of America 

By using the LINKURY website, you agree to the preceding uses of your information in this way by LINKURY.
