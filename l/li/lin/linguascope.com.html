PRIVACY POLICY

Linguascope adheres to the highest standards of privacy and does not share any information about our users to third parties.

Linguascope does NOT:

  • Collect any information from students using its websites.
  • Post anywhere on its websites any email that is sent to or from our users (unless permission is granted – ie. testimonials section).
  • Send any mass emails, updates, or advertisements to students or teachers (unless they opt to subscribe to our newsletter).

The Information That We Collect:

Linguascope collects a minimal amount of information from subscribing schools in order to monitor their subcription. Our database holds the following information:

  • School name
  • Contact name
  • School address
  • School fax number
  • Username and password
  • Subscription expiry date

Linguascope uses the above information to update schools on the status of their subscription and occasionally send them information about our other products (currently once a year). If you'd rather not receive such information, let us know by contacting us.
