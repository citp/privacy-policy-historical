Privacy Statement

Librato, Inc. is committed to respecting and protecting your privacy.

This means that Librato, Inc. does not collect your personal information, such as name, company name, e-mail address, mailing address or phone numbers without your knowledge. Our employees and associated companies are obliged by us to respect this confidentiality.

At times we may request that you voluntarily supply us with personal information, such as name, your e-mail address, and/or phone numbers for purposes such as corresponding with us, participating in an online survey, or entering an online contest. This information will only be used for the express purposes for which it was submitted.

To maintain and improve site quality and integrity, IP addresses are logged. This log is a standard method of analyzing which sections of our web site are visited and how long visitors spend there. We also log the type of browser and operating system you are using and the URL from which you came to our site. We do not link IP addresses to personally identifiable information.
