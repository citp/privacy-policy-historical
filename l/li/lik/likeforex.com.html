Privacy Policy

LIKEFOREX.COM takes and respects your privacy seriously. LIKEFOREX.COM collects small amount of user information when users register with LIKEFOREX.COM or use any LIKEFOREX.COM services. The data we collect are:

  1. The information you fill and submit by the web forms on LIKEFOREX.COM sites.
  2. Standard web log entries, including your IP address, page URL, and timestamp.

The credit card processing of LIKEFOREX.COM will be handled by PayPal®. The credit card information won't be stored in LIKEFOREX.COM database.
