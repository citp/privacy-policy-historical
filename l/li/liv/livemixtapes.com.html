Cookies
Livemixtapes.com uses a feature on your Internet Web browser called a "cookie." A cookie is a small piece of information stored on your hard drive. It assigns a unique identification number to your computer and helps us maintain your preferences between visits to provide a personalized experience for you. Livemixtapes.com uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart and to tell us whether or not you have visited our Website before.
