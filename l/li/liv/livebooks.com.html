liveBooks, Inc. Privacy Policy


This Privacy Policy was last updated: October 22, 2009

This Privacy Policy is effective as of: November 6, 2006

Thank you for visiting the Internet web sites located at the following universal resource locators: www.livebooks.com, www.livebooksedu.com and pj.livebooks.com (collectively, the "Sites"). This Privacy Policy details certain policies implemented throughout liveBooks, Inc. ("liveBooks") governing liveBooks' use of personally identifiable information provided to liveBooks via the Sites, as well as through use of our software and services on or in connection with the Sites.

Privacy Policy Updates

Due to the Internet's rapidly evolving nature, liveBooks may need to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If so, liveBooks will post its updated Privacy Policy on this page and post notice of the change so it is visible when users log-on for the first time after the change is posted so that you are always aware of what personally identifiable information we may collect and how we may use this information. liveBooks encourages you to review this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes. Your continued use of the Sites will be subject to the then-current Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

You can generally visit our Sites without revealing any personally identifiable information about yourself. However, in certain sections of the Sites, to use certain services or purchase products, we may require you to register for an account or we may invite you to submit questions or comments or request information. Due to the nature of some of these activities, we may ask that you complete an online form and/or ordering process and provide personally identifiable information such as your name and/or company name, email address, address, phone number, password, username, credit card information and occupation. We also may collect information about you and your use of the Sites, services and/or software through your use of the Sites, services and/or software.

We may use your personally identifiable information to deliver certain services, products or information you have requested, provide you with notices regarding the products or services you have purchased, provide you with notices regarding products or services you may wish to purchase in the future, verify your authority to enter the Sites and improve the content and general administration of the Sites.

We also may collect your personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide to us when you submit your comments and/or feedback to the Sites. Other users will be able to view your comments and your name you have entered when posting your comments to the Sites.

Cookies

We may use small text files called cookies to improve overall experience on the Sites. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Cookies generally do not permit us to personally identify you other than as referred to above.

Aggregate Information

The Sites may track the total number of visitors to our Sites, the number of visitors to each page of our Sites and IP addresses, and we may analyze these data for trends and statistics in the aggregate, but such information will be in aggregate form only and it will not contain personally identifiable information. Such aggregate information is not linked to any personally identifiable information that can identify any individual person. We may use such aggregate information to analyze trends, administer the Sites, track users' movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.

Disclosure

We may provide the information we collect, including your personally identifiable information and the data generated by cookies, to the vendors and service agencies that we may engage to assist us. For example, we may provide your personally identifiable information to an organization in order to complete a service (e.g., data storage companies, or to process your payment). Such third party entities may be obligated to not further disclose your personally identifiable information. We will also disclose your personally identifiable information (a) if we are required to do so by law, regulation or other government authority, (b) to enforce the liveBook's Terms of Use or Hosting and License Agreement or to protect our rights, or (c) to protect the safety of users of the Sites and our services and software. We will not sell your personally identifiable information to any other company or organization except we may transfer your personally identifiable information to a successor entity upon a merger, consolidation or other corporate reorganization in which liveBooks participates or to a purchaser of all or substantially all of liveBooks's assets to which the Sites relate.

Children's Privacy

liveBooks recognizes the privacy interests of children, and we encourage parents and guardians to take an active role in their children's online activities and interests. The Sites are not intended for children under the age of 13. liveBooks targets its services and the Sites to adults and not to children under 13. liveBooks does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

Links to Third Party Sites

The Sites may provide links to other Web sites or resources over which liveBooks does not have control ("External Web sites"). Such links do not constitute an endorsement by liveBooks of those External Web sites. You acknowledge that liveBooks is providing these links to you only as a convenience, and further agree that liveBooks is not responsible for the content of such External Web sites. Your use of External Web sites is subject to the terms of use and privacy policies located on the linked External Web sites.

Security

We may employ procedural and technological measures that are reasonably designed to protect your personally identifiable information from loss, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration or destruction such as password protection and other internal restrictions and other security measures.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact liveBooks by emailing privacy@livebooks.com.
