# Watch Live Sports Streams Online | NHL, NBA, NBL, NHL, Football

## Personal Data Collected

Live-sport-streams.com collects personal information during the Account Registration, Contact Us and other feedback forms. Live-sport-streams.com may use cookies and tracking links on its site and in e-mails it sends. These are to track the effectiveness of pages and messages. Live-sport-streams.com never shares your personal information with anyone in any way unless you have given us specific permission to do so.

## Use of Data Collected

The data we collect from users of the live-sport-streams.com site will be used to allow us to inform you about live-sport-streams.com news and updates, as well as to notify you of issues, events, or special offers we believe may be of interest to you. By registering, you are giving live-sport-streams.com permission to contact you by e-mail.

## Third Parties and Unaffiliated Sites

Live-sport-streams.com uses takes appropriate precautions to keep the personal information disclosed to us secure and to NEVER disclose such information to third parties. However, we are not responsible for any breach of security or for any actions of any third parties which receive the information. Live-sport-streams.com may also link to a wide variety of other sites. We are not responsible for their privacy policies or how they treat information about their users.
