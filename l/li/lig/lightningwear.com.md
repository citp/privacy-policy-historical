# 

Our Commitment to Privacy Lightningwear.com respects the privacy of its customers and strives to maintain customer confidence. We collect customer information in an effort to improve your shopping experience and to communicate with you about our products, services, contests and promotions. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. 

This policy describes the personal information we collect about you, why we collect it, how we use it and when we share it with third parties. Our policy also describes the choices you can make about how we collect and use your information. Any information changes in the future will be posted immediately on our website. Revision dates will always be placed at the end of the policy. You may exercise your choices about how we collect and use your information at any time. 

**Privacy Policy FAQs**

What kind of information does lightningwear.com collect? 

We collect information, such as name, e-mail and postal addresses, phone and credit card numbers from you when you visit our website or one of our stores, place an order online or by phone, save your info with us online, or participate in a contest, promotion or survey. 

How does lightningwear.com use my information? 

When you place an order or make a purchase from lightningwear.com, we use your personal information to process your order, send you e- mails to confirm your order or to contact you via phone, postal mail or e-mail if we have other questions regarding your order or purchase. 

From time to time, we may use this information to contact you via e-mail, postal mail, or telephone to learn more about your shopping preferences. You always have the choice not to receive marketing information or calls. 

Does lightningwear.com share my information with third parties? 

Lightningwear.com does not sell any of your personal information provided on our site to any third partiers. LW reserves the right to disclose information about you as required by law, in response to legal process and law enforcement requests, and as necessary to protect the property, interests and rights of Lightningwear.com, its affiliates, and others.
