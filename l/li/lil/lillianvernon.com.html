The Lillian Vernon Online Privacy Pledge
 Lillian Vernon Online values their customers and respects their need to feel secure while shopping on our site. Since 1951, we have offered our customers a 100% customer satisfaction guarantee on all the products we sell. We now offer our guarantee online to protect your privacy and keep your confidential information...confidential!
We have gone to great lengths to provide a user-friendly and safe online shopping environment that features unique products at exceptional values. Please review the information below to better understand the safeguards and policies at Lillian Vernon Online. You can access this Privacy Policy at any time.
If you still have privacy concerns that you feel are not addressed in this information, please contact a Lillian Vernon Online Customer Service Representative at custserv@lillianvernon.com or 800-545-5426.
THE KIND OF INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND HOW WE USE IT
When you purchase from Lillian Vernon Online, order a catalog or register to be a preferred customer, we only collect the information that you provide us: name, address, email, credit card and any other data we need to complete your order or request and ensure that any marketing communications are tailored to your particular interests and lifestyle. Registration is optional, so you choose whether or not we store your information to make future shopping experiences more convenient.
When you order from the catalog or our online store, your name becomes part of our customer database. From time-to-time we may share our mailing lists with carefully screened companies. If you would not like to have your name and address shared with these companies, please go to page 2 of Update My Profile/Edit Preferences and UNCHECK the box that gives us permission to share your mailing address.
When You Visit the Site...
Every time you visit us, our web server automatically recognizes only your domain name and only collects basic information to help us best serve your current and future shopping needs. This data includes the number of pages you visit, types of products purchased and any feedback on site improvement or merchandise offerings. You must login as a "Registered User" to take advantage of the convenient features offered to registered users.
When You Register on the Site...
Although optional, registration for Lillian Vernon Online preferred customer status allows us to personalize your shopping experience. It also offers a number of convenience features such as stored billing and shipping and credit card information, and allows access to member-only areas like "Request a Catalog." Information requested includes name, billing and shipping data, telephone number, email address, marketing interests and privacy preferences. Although we never share your email address, your name becomes part of our customer database. From time-to-time we may share our catalog mailing lists with carefully screened companies. If you would not like to have your name and address shared with these companies, please go to page 2 of Update My Profile/Edit Preferences and UNCHECK the box that gives us permission to share your mailing address. When purchasing from www.lillianvernon.com you have the option to login as a "Registered User" or shop as a "Guest." Registration automatically places you on the list to receive marketing emails unless you UNCHECK the appropriate box granting permission.
When You Make A Purchase...
Your name is automatically added to our marketing email distribution list to receive information on hot products, specials and online promotions. Each email you receive provides a link to unsubscribe to future mailings; however, the next time you make a purchase, you will again be added to the list. If you NEVER want to receive marketing emails, when you visit the site simply login and go to the second page of "Update My Profile/Edit Preferences". There you can UNCHECK the box that gives us permission to continue emailing to you. You can also unsubscribe from these promotional emails at any time by following the unsubscribe directions that appear at the bottom of each email. Please note that you may still hear from us regarding your order status and any other service issues and order status updates that will allow us to provide you with the optimal shopping experience.
Your name will also become part of the Lillian Vernon direct mail/catalog list to receive future catalog mailings. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please send an email to custserv@lillianvernon.com, or call 800-545-5426 or write to us at: Customer Service Department, Lillian Vernon Corporation, 100 Lillian Vernon Drive, Virginia Beach, VA 23479-0002. Although we never share email lists, from time to time we may share our catalog mailing lists with carefully screened companies. If you do not wish to have your name shared, go to "Update My Profile/Edit Preferences" and UNCHECK the box that gives us permission to share your mailing address.
When You Ship to Another Recipient...
We only collect the name(s) and postal delivery information necessary fulfill your requests to ship to alternate addresses. We never share this information nor do we contact these individuals unless a service issue relating to the order makes it necessary. If you are a "Registered User" you have the option to either maintain or delete contacts from your "Address Book" at any time.
When You Use the "Tell -A-Friend" Option...
This unique feature allows you to share information about a particular item you think may be interesting to another individual. We do not retain your friend's name or email address, share their information with outside parties, nor does Lillian Vernon contact them.
When You Request We Email You When a Product is in Stock...
We also collect your email address so that we can email you when a back-ordered item on your "Wish List" becomes available.
When You Contact Us Via Email...
When you send an email to any Lillian Vernon department using our "Contact Us" feature your information is only kept until that area has satisfactorily replied to your inquiry. Your name and email address from that correspondence are not retained for any future marketing initiatives or contact.
WHAT ARE "COOKIES" AND HOW ARE THEY USED
"Cookies" are small pieces of data that your browser holds in memory or stores in a special file on your hard drive. These small pieces of data are used to help our server remember you while you browse our site from one link to the next and allows us to improve the quality of your experience when you visit our site. Cookies also allow you to place items in your shopping cart, continue browsing, and come back to check out at a later time.
LILLIAN VERNON® MASTERCARD® PROGRAM
If you have applied for the Lillian Vernon® MasterCard® through a link on our website, the application information provided to the credit card company is confidential.
SALE OF OUR COMPANY
As in any business venture, the sale of the Company is always an open question. Should Lillian Vernon ever be acquired or merged into another organization, all customer information we have collected will be transferred to the new company.
OUR COMMITMENT TO DATA SECURITY
Lillian Vernon Online is dedicated to preventing unauthorized access to your private information and ensuring a safe and secure online shopping experience. To that end we have installed security and encryption features (Secure Socket Layer Technology) and have a strict internal operating procedure guiding this ongoing commitment.
CHANGES TO THE LILLIAN VERNON ONLINE PRIVACY PLEDGE
Your use of our website constitutes your agreement to the policies and procedures set forth in this Privacy Policy. In the event Lillian Vernon Online amends its Privacy Policy in the future, these changes will be clearly posted on our site so you can be kept informed of how, where, when and why we collect information and how we use it. If you have any additional questions, please contact a Lillian Vernon Online Customer Service Representative at custserv@lillianvernon.com or 800-545-5426.
