Welcome

Thank you for visiting our website. Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used at our site.

The Information We Collect

You provide specific personal information during the purchase of goods or services from our site. The information includes: your name, address, e-mail address, telephone number, payment information. Your payment details (credit card numbers) are not stored on our server.

In our newsletter sign up we collect only your name and e-mail address.

We also collect certain non-personal information including the type of browser you are using (e.g. Netscape, Internet Explorer), the type of operating system you are using (e.g. Windows XP, Mac OS) and the domain name of your Internet service provider (e.g. America Online, Earthlink). This is collected to help us understand the technical aspects of our business.

How We Use the Information

We use the information you provide about yourself to fulfill your order for our goods and/or services, to respond to your inquiries about your order and to notify you about the status of your order.

We invite you to join our newsletter list so that we may send you educational information, news about our products and/or services, and special promotional mailings. If you do not wish to receive our newsletter, you can unsubscribe at any time.

The non-personal information gathered may be used to improve the design and content of our site and in the aggregate to analyze site usage. We may have to disclose personal information in response to legal process (e.g. in response to a court order or subpoena). We also may have to disclose such information in response to a law enforcement agency’s requests.

We may disclose personal information in response to a charge-back inquiry from our merchant bank about your payment.

We will not sell or transfer personally identifiable information provided to us to parties outside of our company except as described above.

Cookies

To enhance your experience with our site, many of our web pages use “cookies” and shopper ID’s. Cookies are text files we place in your computer’s browser to store your preferences. Cookies, by themselves, do not tell us your e-mail address or other personal information unless you choose to provide this information to us. However, once you choose to furnish our site with personally identifiable information, this information may be linked to the data stored in the cookie.

Products

We represent several independent producers of health enhancing products. We concentrate on products that support the immune system in general, allowing the body's innate abilities to heal and restore itself.

Shipping

We aim to please you and meet your expectations. All shipping errors will be corrected, and all products that you return in resalable condition will be credited. Opened consumable products can not be returned.

We use UPS Ground for Regular, and UPS 2nd Day for Express shipping. Orders to other countries are shipped by Air Mail.

Canadian orders are shipped by Canada Post.

Security - Credit Card Safety

Are You Safe? Yes you are! When you place an order with us, we use a secure server. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us. This means that no prying eyes will be able to see your personal information while it is in transit to us, because it will be encrypted. Our Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption software is the industry standard - it is the best encryption method available today for confident and secure online commerce transactions. Furthermore, all of the customer data we collect is protected against unauthorized access. All payment information is kept on a stand-alone computer behind a firewall.

That's alright! You can also provide us with your order and payment information by telephone, toll-free, at 1-866-543-3388. Or download our order form and fax it to us at 403-398-0489.

Do We Respect Your Privacy?

Yes. We are committed to protecting your privacy. We recognize the amazing leap of faith that you may feel you are taking in placing an order with us, and we will not do anything that may betray your trust in us. We only use the information we collect about you to process your order and to serve you better. Your information plays an important role in how we can serve you. We are not in the business of selling your personal information to anyone else.

In summary

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. If you never want to receive any announcements or special offers from us, just unsubscribe from our newsletter.

Exchanges and Refunds

If you have received the correct item, however, no longer wish to keep this item, it may be returned to us providing that it is unopened and unused. A full refund will be issued for the product returned, minus the cost of shipping and handling.

The following restrictions may apply:

Opened products may only be returned if they have a manufacturer’s defect.  Once a consumable product has been used it cannot be returned!

Returns

Please make it easy on us and find the order number on which you purchased the goods. Please ship in a way that can be tracked so that we may resolve any dispute conclusively. We expect you to pay for return shipping.

Where is my Order? When will my order arrive?

Typically, all orders are shipped by the next business day. All orders are shipped either by Priority Mail or by UPS Ground, and usually arrive at North American destinations in 3 - 5 business days. Please note that deliveries outside of North America can take 10 to 30 days.

Has my order been shipped?

If you have received a confirmation e-mail, it has been shipped.
