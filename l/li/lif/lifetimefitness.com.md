# Life Time Fitness - The Healthy Way of Life Company

Life Time Fitness Web Privacy Policy

Updated April 10, 2013

Information about Life Time Fitness and Our Web Sites  
Life Time Fitness, Inc. and its subsidiaries ("we" or "Life Time") have established this Web Privacy Policy to explain how we treat information we collect from you through www.lifetimefitness.com, www.mylt.com, www.myhealthcheckonline.com www.myhealthcheckonline.com and www.lifelabonline.com, including any sub-domains ("Sites"). This Policy does not apply to personal information collected from you in other ways, including in Life Time centers, or through websites that we do not own or control. We reserve the right to change this Web Privacy Policy at any time, so please review it frequently. If we change this Policy, we will post a revised Policy on our Sites. If you continue to use the Sites thereafter, you hereby provide your consent to the revised Policy.

1\. Collection of Information  
We may collect information that identifies you as an individual ("Personal Information") on our Sites. This Personal Information as discussed herein does not include your personal health information collected through the myHealthCheck Site, myPlan Site, or the LifeLab or myHealthScore processes ("Personal Health Information"). The participant terms and conditions and use and disclosure of your information ("Participant Consent Form") for those applicable services provide the terms relating to the collection and use of your Personal Health Information.

Account Creation  
In order to use myLT.com, the myHealthCheck Site and certain other Life Time Sites, you will be required to register, create an account and, if applicable, agree to certain additional terms and conditions relating to the services available through these websites (for example, myHealthCheck services). To register and create an account for these websites, you will be required to provide certain contact information, including your first and last name, date of birth, member or other identification number, and a username and password chosen by you. We will use the email address you provide to send a confirmation email, service-related notifications, scheduling confirmations and to send you other relevant emails based on your preferences.

Member Interest Groups  
You may join member interest groups focused on your health or fitness interests through myLT.com. If you join an interest group, we will send emails to you with announcements, updates and information related to your interest areas.

Memberships  
If you purchase a Life Time membership through our Life Time Sites, you will be required to provide certain information, including your name, address, birth date, gender, email address, and an emergency contact. You will also be required to provide similar information for other members on your membership. You will be required to provide financial account information. We use this information to establish and manage your membership. We will send you an email confirming your membership.

myLIFETIME Facebook Application at http://apps.facebook.com/lifetimefitness   
If you choose to download the myLIFETIME Facebook application (the "App") you will be asked whether or not you wish to share information about the classes, programs and activities that you have participated in or registered for at Life Time, whether in club or on-line. If you agree, and only after authentication through myLT.com, Life Time will share information to your friends on Facebook. You may change your privacy settings within Facebook at any time and you may also deactivate the application itself.

Other Products and Services  
If you purchase other products or services through our Life Time Sites, you will be required to provide certain billing, shipping and financial account information, however, no Personal Health Information will be requested unless needed for the purpose of providing the service and you voluntarily provide the information. We will use this information for customer services issues, payment purposes, and to process or fulfill your purchase through a third-party service provider. We may also provide the opportunity to obtain complimentary products or services through the Sites, such as a guest pass. In that event, you will be required to provide certain information, including your name, address, email address, phone number, and age. We will use this information to send you the guest pass or other product or service.

Surveys, Giveaways and Contests  
We may provide you the opportunity to voluntarily participate in surveys, giveaways or contests on our Sites. If you choose to participate, we may request certain Personal Information from you, including your name, gender, age, email address, membership status or other information. We will use the information to notify winners as applicable.

Inquiries  
We provide you the opportunity to voluntarily contact us with inquiries. If you choose to do so, you will be required or requested to provide certain Personal Information, including your name, phone number, email, and membership status. We will use this information to respond to your inquiry.

Log Files, Cookies, and Clear Gifs  
We use log files, cookies and clear gifs on our Sites and in our HTML-based emails to track the interests of our users. Log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring and exit pages, operating system, date/time stamps, and clickstream data. Cookies are small text files that are stored on a user's computer for record-keeping purposes, including session ID cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your hard drive for an extended period of time). Clear gifs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier which are embedded invisibly on web pages. If you remove persistent cookies, you may still use our Sites.

Strategic Partners  
We sometimes permit other companies to advertise on our Life Time Sites, such as our strategic business partners who offer discounts to Life Time members. Our strategic partners may use one or more of the technologies above to measure the effectiveness or personalize the content of their advertisements by tracking user interests. We do not control the use of these technologies, and they are not covered by this Web Privacy Policy. Life Time does not allow third party advertisements on the myHealthCheck Site and will only allow third party advertising if agreed to by the employer organization sponsoring the myHealthCheck program.

Children  
We do not knowingly collect Personal Information from or solicit persons under 13 years of age.

2\. Use of Information  
In addition to the uses of information noted above, we will use your Personal Information for member or customer service purposes, including to respond to your inquiries, to provide the services or products you request, and to manage your relationship with us, including but not limited to communications about changes in monthly dues, fees or center access privileges or collection of past due amounts related to your membership.

We may also use your Personal Information to contact you for marketing or informational purposes about Life Time, including communications to promote products or services offered by Life Time or our strategic partners or to provide information about activities, opportunities, programs, or other items which may be of interest to you as member of Life Time or a particular member interest area. If your information was obtained through the myHealthCheck Site, and also if subsequently presented on the myPlan Site, then your information will only be used in accordance with the applicable myHealthCheck Participant Consent Form. We will not sell or otherwise provide your Personal Health Information collected through the myHealthCheck Site or myPlan Site to any strategic partner for the purposes of advertising or marketing.

Discussion Boards and Forums  
Any Personal Information or other user content (e.g. , messages, text, files, images, video, photos, sounds or other materials) which you post, submit, transmit, email, message, upload, share, store or otherwise make available on a Discussion Board or Forum may be read, collected or used for any purpose by us and other users of the Discussion Board or Forum. We are not responsible for the Personal Information or other user content you choose to post in the Discussion Boards or Forums. We reserve the right to restrict, disable, suspend or terminate your access to or use of all or any parts of the Sites without prior notice or liability to you. This unrestricted collection and use does not apply to any information you post privately to your myHealthAdvisor on the myHealthCheck Site or the myPlan Site.

Third-Party Service Providers/Agents  
We use other companies to perform certain functions on our behalf and to provide you certain web-based applications, services and resources, including processing payments or fulfilling orders for products or services; processing athletic event registrations; servicing and maintaining our Sites; managing your relationship with us, including sending communications; and assisting you in managing your health and fitness experience. We may also use other companies to conduct research, surveys, giveaways or contests. In no event will we share your Personal Health Information obtained through the myHealthCheck Site or myPlan Site with other companies, unless specifically agreed to by you in the Participant Consent Form for the purposes of providing you the myHealthCheck services. In no event will we sell or otherwise provide your Personal Health Information to third parties for marketing purposes. Unless you consent to it, we do not sell or otherwise provide your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Legal/Law Enforcement  
We reserve the right to disclose Personal Information about you as required by law and when we reasonably believe that disclosure is necessary to comply with a court order, a judicial proceeding, public health requirements or legal process. We also reserve the right to disclose Personal Information to law enforcement officials engaged in legitimate investigations; to prosecute or defend against claims related to Life Time, its Site users, members, guests or employees, including but not limited to enforcement of the website terms of use or to respond to claims that User Content has been posted in violation of the rights of third parties; or to otherwise reasonably protect the interests, rights, property or safety of Life Time, its Site users, members, guests, employees or the general public.

3\. Your Information Choices  
Accessing and Changing Your Personal Information  
You may review, correct and update Personal Information related to your membership with Life Time on myLT.com. You may submit a written request in accordance with the Contact Us section below to ask us whether we hold any of your Personal Information, what that information is, how it is being used, and to whom, if anyone, it has been disclosed by us. We will make reasonable efforts to assist you and respond as accurately and completely as possible. Requests may be subject to any fees and disbursements the law permits us to charge. You may submit a written request to us to ask us to correct errors or omissions in your Personal Information we have in our custody or control. We will correct the Personal Information, and where appropriate and if reasonable to do so, send correction notices to third parties, if any, to whom we have disclosed the incorrect information. Alternatively, we may decide not to correct the Personal Information, but make an appropriate annotation.

Opting Out of Marketing Communications  
You may opt out of Life Time's marketing communications via direct mail, email and/or telephone by changing your Communications Preferences on our Site. No marketing communications will be sent as a result of using the myHealthCheck Site except for those communications relating to your employer sponsored myHealthCheck program and any related services, programs and educational materials approved by your employer sponsor.

4\. Security  
We follow generally-accepted industry standards to protect your Personal Information.

Security Measures  
When you create an account, we enable you to choose a username, password and security question answer in order to help protect access to the account. You are responsible to maintain the security and integrity of your username, password and security question answers. We encrypt sensitive Personal Information, such as your financial account information and Personal Health Information, using secure socket layer technology (SSL) for data transmissions and industry standard encryption methods for data at rest. While we use commercially reasonable security measures, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage of Personal Information is 100% secure and therefore we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Links to Other Sites  
The Sites contain links to other websites that we do not own or control. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of other sites. When you leave our Sites, we encourage you to review the privacy practices of such sites.

5\. Contact Us  
If you have any inquiry or concern about the Sites' use of your Personal Information as provided in this Web Privacy Policy, please send your request: via email to privacy@lifetimefitness.com or via mail to Life Time Fitness, 2902 Corporate Place, Chanhassen, MN, 55317, USA, Attn: Chief Privacy Officer.
