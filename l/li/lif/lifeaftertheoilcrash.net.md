# Life After the Oil Crash

**Privacy Policy**  


 **What Information I collect:**  


I use [Homestead](http://www.homestead.com/) as my webhosting and website building service. As part of my webhosting package, I have access to a site statistic tracker. The site tracker gives me access to general demographic information about who visits this site, including the IP addresses of the last 100 visitors.This information does not contain anything which would allow me to identify users personally.  


If you make a purchase or donation through this page, I keep the information you provide with your order as part of my business records.    


I occasionally send out emails to people who have made purchases through this website. If you make a purchase through this website and wish to not receive occasional emails from me, please notify me via email at the following address:

I will not reveal any personal information you provide to me to to any commerical third party.  Put another way: I won't sell your information to any entity.
