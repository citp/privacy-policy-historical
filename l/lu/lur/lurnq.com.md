# LurnQ : "Personal Learning Assistant" - PRIVACY POLICY

Technium Labs Private Limited (“Technium Labs”) provides this Privacy Policy to inform you of our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of the information that we collect and receive from users of our services at [ www.LurnQ.com ](http://www.lurnq.com/) (the "Services"). This Privacy Policy applies only to information that you provide to us for the purposes of using the Services. Unless otherwise defined in this Privacy Policy, terms used in this Privacy Policy have the same meanings as in our Terms of Service (the "Terms"). As used in this policy, the terms 'using' and 'processing' information include using cookies on a computer, subjecting the information to statistical or other analysis and using or handling information in any way, including, but not limited to collecting, storing, evaluating, modifying, deleting, using, combining, disclosing and transferring information within our organization or among our affiliates within India or internationally. 

To utilize the Services available, our registered users identify themselves to the site and we may display personal information to service providers as needed for the provision of the Services. Technium Labs cares deeply about online privacy. If you have any questions concerning this privacy policy, please email us at [team@lurnq.com](mailto:team@lurnq.com). 

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT

The types of information that [ www.LurnQ.com ](http://www.lurnq.com/) collects fall under two general categories: personally-identifiable-information (PII) and non-personally-identifiable information (non-PII). PII consists of any information which can be used to specifically identify you as an individual. Non-PII consists of aggregate information or any information that does not reveal your identity. 

[ www.LurnQ.com ](http://www.lurnq.com/) collects user submitted information such as name, email address, age to authenticate users and to send notifications to those users relating to the [ www.LurnQ.com ](http://www.lurnq.com/) Service. [ www.LurnQ.com ](http://www.lurnq.com/) also collects other profile data including but not limited to: personal interests, gender, age, education etc., in order to provide the optimum Services. 

When you visit our web sites, you might be providing statistics collected as you browse and personal information you knowingly give us. Be assured that safeguarding the contents of information related to you is our priority. 

We use your personal information to: 

  1. help us provide personalized features 
  2. tailor our sites to your interest 
  3. to get in touch with you when necessary 
  4. to provide the services requested by you 
  5. to preserve social history as governed by existing law or policy 



[ www.LurnQ.com ](http://www.lurnq.com/) also logs non-PII including IP address, profile information, aggregate user data, and information your browser sends, from users and visitors to the site. This data is used to manage the website, track usage and improve the website Services. User IP addresses and/or mobile location may be recorded for security and monitoring purposes.

The following sections describe how your PII and non-PII are collected by us, and how we use such information. 

HOW WE COLLECT AND USE INFORMATION 

Log Files

Any time you visit the site, our servers automatically gather information from your browser (such as your IP addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks). This information may be used to analyze trends, administer the site, prevent fraud, track visitor movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information. We do not share this information externally. 

Cookies

We use "cookies" to keep track of some types of information while you are visiting the Site or using the Service. Cookies are very small files placed on your computer, and they allow us to count the number of visitors to our website and distinguish repeated users from new users. They also allow us to save user preferences and track user trends.  We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information (PII) you submit while on our site, and any cookies used by us would not enable us to view, store or disseminate any PII relating to the user.  You may be able to configure your browser to accept or reject all or some cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. Each browser is different, so check the "Help" menu of your browser to learn how to change your cookie preferences -- however, you must enable cookies from [ www.LurnQ.com ](http://www.lurnq.com/) in order to use most functions on the site. We rely on cookies for the proper operation of our website; therefore if your browser is set to reject all cookies, the site will not function properly. Users who refuse cookies assume all responsibility for any resulting loss of functionality, and shall not at any later point in time, hold us responsible for any such resultant loss in functionality. 

Web Beacons

"Web beacons" (also known as "clear gifs" and "pixel tags") are small transparent graphic images that are often used in conjunction with cookies in order to further personalize our website for our users and to collect a limited set of information about our users. We may also use web beacons in email communications in order to understand the behavior of our users. 

Information About You

When you register for the Service, we collect a variety of information about you to complete your online profile. Except as otherwise stated in this privacy policy, we do not disclose your PII to other users of the Service. 

E-mail

We require an email address from you when you register for the Services. The email messages we send you may contain code that enables our database to track your usage of the emails, including whether the email was opened and what links (if any) were clicked. If you would rather not receive promotional emails from us, please see the section below labeled "Choice/Opt-Out". We reserve the right to send you certain communications, such as service announcements and administrative messages, without offering you the opportunity to opt out of receiving them. We may also contact you by text message (including to any wireless number you may provide to us) solely in connection with the Services.  

Logging onto the website

Log in to the website would be undertaken by the User using his user name and password. 

Demographic Data

Demographic data is also collected at the Site. We use this data to tailor our users’ experience, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences. Some of this information may be shared with advertisers on a non-personally identifiable basis. 

Blogs, Bulletin Boards, Buzz and Public Forums

We make discussion areas of the website available to our users. Any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and that can be used to send you unsolicited communications. You should exercise caution when deciding to disclose any PII in these areas. Please consult the Terms of Use and other guidelines available on our website when disclosing any such PII.

 ADVERTISING 

Ads appearing on our website may be delivered to users by one or more of our web advertising partners. Our web advertising partners may employ cookies and web beacons to allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement. This information allows an ad network to deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. 

This privacy statement covers the use of cookies and web beacons by Technium Labs  and does not cover the use of cookies or web beacons by any advertisers. We shall not be liable in any manner whatsoever for any use (whether authorized or otherwise) of any PII that may be collected by such advertisers.

SHARING AND DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION COLLECTED BY TECHNIUM LABS

(1) to conform to legal requirements or to respond to a subpoena, search warrant or other legal process received by Technium Labs, whether or not a response is required by applicable law; Technium Labs cooperates with government and law enforcement officials or private parties to enforce and comply with the law. Further, any such information may be required to be disclosed pursuant to an administrative or regulatory order, and in such cases, we reserve the right to freely disclose any user information that may be mandated under such regulatory or administrative order.

(2) to enforce the  Terms of Use or to protect our rights in any manner; or 

(3) to protect the safety of members of the public and users of the service. Technium Labs reserves the right to transfer personal information to a successor in interest that acquires rights to that information as a result of the sale of Technium Labs or substantially all of its assets to that successor in interest.  For more information see the "Changes in Our Privacy Policy" section below. 

Disclosure to Trusted Third Parties

We may engage certain trusted third parties to perform functions and provide services to us, including, without limitation, hosting and maintenance, customer relationship, database storage and management, and direct marketing campaigns. 

We may share your non-PII with third parties, but not in a manner that would reveal your identity. For example, outsourced customer support agents or technology infrastructure providers may need access to your information to perform services for you. Your information will be treated as private and confidential by such service providers and not used for any other purpose than we authorize.. If you do not wish to receive offers from our trusted partners, you can change your email preferences at any time by following the steps outlined in the "Choice/Opt-Out" section below.

As we develop our business, we may buy or sell assets and, depending on the transaction, your PII may be one of the transferred assets.
