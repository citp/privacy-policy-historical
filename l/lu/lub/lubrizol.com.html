Lubrizol's Privacy Statement

Last Updated 01-03-06

The Lubrizol Corporation and its affiliates (collectively "Lubrizol") respect your privacy. We understand your desire for privacy and we want you to understand how we intend to handle Personal Information, such as your name, address or telephone number, which you may provide to us via the Internet.

In general, you can visit Lubrizol's home page (www.lubrizol.com) without giving us any Personal Information. However, there are circumstances when we may need Personal Information from you, such as when authorized users access a Lubrizol Extranet site. We endeavor to let you know before we collect Personal Information via the Internet.

If you choose to provide Personal Information via the Internet, we want you know how we may use such information. We may use Personal Information to provide you with product information, to allow you to purchase products, to market products to you, or to communicate with you for other purposes. We may gather information that allows the site to communicate with your computer during the visit. We may also track the number of visits to the site and which parts of the site you select.

We intend to protect Personal Information that you provide to us. We want to maintain only information that is relevant, accurate and complete. Lubrizol does not intend to sell or otherwise disseminate your Personal Information to third parties without your permission. Upon written request, sent to Contact us or to 29400 Lakeland Boulevard Wickliffe, OHio 44092, Lubrizol will allow you to review, update and/or remove from our databases Personal Information that you provided to us. In such request, please include your name and e-mail address. Removal of Personal Information will prevent you from accessing the Lubrizol Extranet site unless and until you re-enter the Personal Information at a later date.

This site may contain links to third party sites that Lubrizol does not own or maintain. Lubrizol is not responsible for the privacy practices or other aspects of those sites.

Please see Lubrizol's Terms of Use for additional information about this site.
