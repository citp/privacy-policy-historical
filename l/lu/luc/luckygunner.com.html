Our Commitment To Your Privacy

We value our customers and their privacy. For this reason, the personal information you provide is used to ensure efficient processing of your orders and to otherwise assist our staff in making sure that you have a pleasant experience with LuckyGunner.com. We will not give, sell, rent, or loan any personally identifiable information to any third party, unless we are legally required to do so or you authorize us to do so.

Our Commitment To Data Security

We handle your information with utmost care. Our entire site is PCI compliant and our checkout process is SSL encryption secured and is tied directly into the secure Authorize.net payment gateway. Not even the owners of our company can access your credit card information within our system.

The Information We Collect

LuckyGunner.com's checkout system requires you to provide basic contact information like your name and email address as well as payment information relating to the payment methods you choose. Please be sure to input your information accurately so that our office can better serve you.

How We Use Information

We use the information you provide about yourself to complete your orders and to better serve you. For example, if we find that the shipping address you provided is inaccurate or incomplete, we will use the information you provided to contact you immediately so that our friendly staff can correct the address information and get your package on the road. We may also use the information you provided to occasionally inform you of product specials or simply to send you a follow up e-mail thanking you for your business.

How To Contact Us

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please call us at (800) 317-9506 or send a friendly email to customerservice@luckygunner.com. We look forward to assisting you.
