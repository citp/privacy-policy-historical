Privacy Policy

LucasKazan.com is committed to maintaining your privacy and confidentiality at all times. This Privacy Policy discloses how we gather, track, use and safeguard the information you provide to us through LucasKazan.com.

We collect certain non-personally identifying information through the use of 'cookies'. Some cookies are deleted when you end your online session ('session cookies'), while others may be stored on your computer indefinitely ('persistent cookies'). We use session cookies for your convenience, in order to store information such as your login status. These cookies expire automatically when you log out. We use the information stored in cookies to personalize your Web site experience and to manage your online session. The information in cookies is both set and read by our server and is not logged in the process. We do not share data that we collect from cookies with third parties.

This site contains links to other Web sites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or the content of such Web sites.

We use click-stream data to analyze trends and statistics and to help us understand how subscribers use our site. This allows us to identify areas and technologies that need improvement, measure the number of visitors to the different areas of LucasKazan.com and help us make our site more useful. None of this information is reviewed at an individual level.

Visitors to the public areas of LucasKazan.com who do not purchase a subscription do not provide us with any personally identifiable information.

The only personally-identifying information we collect and store about you is the information you voluntarily choose to provide to us when you join LucasKazan.com or sign up to our mailing list. This information enables us to process the requested transaction and/or to communicate any updates pertinent to LucasKazan.com. and it includes personally identifiable information such as name, address, birthday, e-mail address, and telephone number. Members also select, and thereby provide, a username and password combination. We never sell, share, or trade any of this information. Any unsubscribe requests are processed immediately.
