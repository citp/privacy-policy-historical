# Privacy Policy | LuckStock

##  WE RESPECT YOUR PRIVACY **· We don't share your private data with third-parties.

· We don't spam our users.

· We keep your private data secure.

**

The Policy governs how Luckstock collects and uses the personal information you provide on the site.

We collect the following personal information from you:  
\- Contact information  
\- Billing information  
\- Unique identifiers  
\- Preferences information  
We automatically gather information about your computer.

You are under no obligation to provide us with this information and can access many aspects of the site without providing us any personal information.

The security of your personal information is important to us.  
**We do not sell/give your personal information to third parties.**

Persons under the age of 18 years are not eligible to use our services unsupervised and we ask that children not submit any information to us. It is the responsibility of parents to monitor their children's use of the site (www.luckstock.com).

For further information about privacy policy, please [contact support](https://web.archive.org/contacts/).
