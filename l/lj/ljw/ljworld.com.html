Privacy policy

The World Company is sensitive to issues of individual privacy on the Internet. To that end, we provide the following policy on the use of information gathered on LJWorld.com:

Categories of information collected

¢ LJWorld.com gathers identifying information about individuals only when individuals provide that information on a voluntary basis such as in reader comments and blogs, for our SMS and email alerts and if using social networking tools.

Third parties

It is not the policy of The World Company to sell or make available such information to unrelated third parties; occasionally such information may be shared with other divisions of the World Company. We may inform advertisers of the results of such analyses. And under any circumstance, LJWorld.com will refer only to general, aggregate usage data from these analyses - never specific identifying information. If you choose to participate in our social networking tools, your favorites may be shared with other LJWorld.com users who are also taking advantage of those tools.

¢LJWorld.com may allow users to utilize site-wide social networking tools, which can include befriending other users, updating profile information, posting comments on other users profiles and identifying pieces of content as something of interest to a user. When a user takes advantage of the social networking tools, the user is agreeing to share personal information with other users. LJWorld.com provides privacy tools within its social networking framework to enable users to control which types of personal information is shared with other users.

¢ LJWorld.com performs statistical analyses of its server logs to study the use and popularity of various areas of our site. We also look at IP addresses to analyze where we are getting our audience.

¢ Users should keep in mind that, when posting their user name or e-mail address on bulletin boards or in other user-posted content areas, that information, along with any information in their postings, can be read by any third parties and may result in unsolicited messages from other posters or third parties. LJWorld.com has no control over that.

¢ LJWorld.com may make use of or refer to user-posted content for the purposes of promotion of LJWorld.com. This will mainly involve taking screen shots of a particular area of the site and using that screen shot as a graphic in an advertisement. LJWorld.com makes no claim of ownership to such user-posted content.

¢ LJWorld.com employs 'cookies' in circumstances that require session data or require user verification.

Process to remove your personal information

Most of our services include information on how to remove your information and cancel the service. You may alternatively send us an email at webmaster@ljworld.com requesting the services and information you would like removed. Please provide explicit reasons why we should remove the content. We reserve the right to decline requests for removal of information based on maintaining the historical and archival nature of our Web site.

Changes to this policy & effective dates

¢ LJWorld.com reserves the right to change this policy at any time. The effective date of this policy shall be reflected by the updated date at the top of this page.

Policy last updated November 2008
