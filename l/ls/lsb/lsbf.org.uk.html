Introduction

 

Welcome to the London School of Business & Finance (LSBF) privacy notice. LSBF takes the protection of your personal data very seriously and are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy.

 

The EU General Data Protection regulation (GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, gives people the right to know what information is held about them, and requires LSBF to ensure that personal information relating to living individuals is handled properly, held in confidence and is protected from inappropriate disclosure to third parties.

 

As part of our legal obligations we have published Staff, Student and General Privacy notices. Where required local privacy notices will be issued to inform individuals about what personal data is gathered, how it is used, stored and retained.
