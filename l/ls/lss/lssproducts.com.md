# Low Vision Aids | Hard of Hearing | Magnifiers | Assistive Technology - LS&S Products

Privacy Policy and Security Policy

This site is owned and operated by LS&S, LLC. LS&S is committed to protecting your privacy while using our site. This privacy statement discloses what information we gather and how we use that information.

LS&S is committed to protecting your privacy. The information we collect is gathered to ensure secure ordering and to provide a more personalized shopping experience.

Your Personal Information for Orders 

We require only the personal information that is needed for safely processing your online order and for contacting you about your transactions. It is used exclusively for that purpose. Your personal information is not shared with any other party, except those third-parties required to approve your credit card transaction.

Your Personal Information for Catalog Requests and Inquiries 

We gather your personal information such as name, address, and email to process your catalog request or online inquiries. You have the option to “opt-out” of future emails newsletters on our request forms. If you wish to “opt-out” of our print catalog mailings, please contact the company directly at (800) 468-4789 and speak to a customer service representative. Emailed order information such as order or shipping confirmations is generally exempt.

Sharing of Information 

LS&S does not sell, rent, or provide your information to any third party, except those previously stated. LS&S does not sell or rent our email lists or customer information. We do not share information about individual users with any third party, except to comply with applicable law or valid legal process or to protect the personal safety of our users or the public

Security 

To guard against credit card fraud, we offer secure online ordering using the latest SSL encryption technology. The connection between your browser and our servers is encrypted with industry standard Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology, which scrambles ordering information so that only your browser and our servers can decipher it. To use this option you must have a browser that supports SSL (e.g. Microsoft Internet Explorer or Netscape Communicator).

LS&S reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. Thus, it is recommended that you check this Policy periodically to see if any such changes have been made. By visiting and using this website, you agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy. By providing any information on our site, you expressly consent to our use and disclosure of your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms set forth herein, please do not use our Site.
