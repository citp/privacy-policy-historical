Third Party Sites:

In order to optimize your visit,  **LOLSOFUNNY.TUMBLR**  supports ads from third party sites. These sites may collect information such as your domain type or your IP address. For further information on these sites, please consult the privacy policies of the following: google, valueclickmedia, Nabbr , adbrite, burst media, and underdogmedia. We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, contact us.
