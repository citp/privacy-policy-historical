General Statement

LotLinx has two separate marketing/advertising functions. Firstly, we market our customers’ (auto dealers) inventory to shoppers currently searching the Internet with the intent to purchase, and secondly, we acquire clients that are interested in using the LotLinx Business Services (sometimes just referred to as “LotLinx AI Services”).[1] We’ve divided up our two functions, below into their own sections to highlight the separate methods LotLinx uses to achieve their goals and communicate to you how we collect and use the information gathered under these two functions.

In addition to our primary functions, above, LotLinx may collect information on its primary website (www.lotlinx.com) and affiliated sites related to information inquiries, technical support, events and promotions, employment inquiries, and job applications. This Privacy Statement applies to personal information – information that identifies you or reasonably can be linked to information that identifies you – that LotLinx collects through your use or interaction with our websites, applications, social media, software, and other services provided by or on behalf of LotLinx (collectively “LotLinx General Services” or “General Services”).

Special Notice to citizens of the EU visiting any LotLinx or LotLinx-Affiliated Sites: LotLinx does not offer products or services to citizens of the EU nor do we collect personal information, as that is defined under the European Union’s General Data Privacy Regulation (GDPR). LotLinx is not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties, and we encourage you to visit their sites and read their privacy policies related GDPR policies.
