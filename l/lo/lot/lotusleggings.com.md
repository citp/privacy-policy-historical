# Privacy Policy – Lotus Leggings

Your information’s security is taken very seriously and we have implemented procedures designed to safeguard it in our policies.  In order to protect your personal data from unauthorized access and use, we use security measures such as computer safeguards (firewalls, SSL and passwords) in addition to our secure server. 

What data is collected?

We collect data you give us through our website, email/contact form, phone contact and any other means of communication that you might use.  By viewing our site you agree to the collection and use of your personal information. If we believe it to be necessary to release your information to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect others or ours rights, property, or safety, we reserve the right to do that. However, information that doesn’t personally identify our visitors may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

  We will not use your personally identifiable information for sell, trade, or transfer. This doesn’t include third parties that we trust to keep your information confidential who in some way assist us in operating our site, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to comply with our policies. As you browse Lotus Leggings, advertising cookies may be placed on your computer so that we can understand what you are interested in.

Children:

Our website and all products are directed to people who are at least 18 years old or older.  We don't collect any information from minors under the age of 18 years of age. 

How Can I Update My Information?

If you wish to update your information, email support@lotusleggings.com.

Email Newsletters and Promotions

If you choose to opt in to our newsletter, Lotus Leggings will send you promotions or newsletters via email.  If you change your mind and decide you do not wish to receive e-mail from Lotus Leggings, you can easily unsubscribe from our newsletter or ask directly by contacting us support@lotusleggings.com. We will gladly unsubscribe you from our list if you would like us to do that.

What Happens When We Change Our Privacy Policy?

By using this site, you are agreeing to and accepting the terms laid out in this Privacy Policy.  Lotus Leggings reserves the right to update or amend this Privacy Policy at any time if our practices change or are updated. If Lotus Leggings decides to make such changes to this Privacy Policy, an easily identifiable notice will be posted on the web site and/or sent to you via email, so that you will know the information that Lotus Leggings collects, how it uses that information, and the parties with whom it may share that information.
