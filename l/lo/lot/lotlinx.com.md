# Privacy Statement - LotLinx

**General Statement**

LotLinx has two separate marketing/advertising functions. Firstly, we market our customers’ (auto dealers) inventory to shoppers currently searching the Internet with the intent to purchase, and secondly, we acquire clients that are interested in using the LotLinx Business Services (sometimes just referred to as “LotLinx AI Services”).[ **[1]**](https://www.lotlinx.com/privacy-statement/#_ftn1)  We’ve divided up our two functions, below into their own sections to highlight the separate methods LotLinx uses to achieve their goals and communicate to you how we collect and use the information gathered under these two functions.

In addition to our primary functions, above, LotLinx may collect information on its primary website (www.lotlinx.com) and affiliated sites related to information inquiries, technical support, events and promotions, employment inquiries, and job applications. This Privacy Statement applies to personal information – information that identifies you or reasonably can be linked to information that identifies you – that LotLinx collects through your use or interaction with our websites, applications, social media, software, and other services provided by or on behalf of LotLinx (collectively “LotLinx General Services” or “General Services”).

 _Special Notice to citizens of the EU visiting any LotLinx or LotLinx-Affiliated Sites:_ LotLinx does not offer products or services to citizens of the EU nor do we collect personal information, as that is defined under the European Union’s General Data Privacy Regulation (GDPR). Independent third-parties may advertise on the LotLinx Site(s). LotLinx is not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties, and we encourage you to visit their sites and read their privacy policies related GDPR policies.

 **Summary**

 **INFORMATION WE COLLECT**

The information we collect through our LotLinx AI and General Services _may_ include all or some of the following:

  * your contact information (name, postal or email address, phone number, etc.) ( ** _only if you give that to us to sign up for services, request information, or make an inquiry)_** ;
  * information about the device you use to connect or interact with our General Services, including geo-location;
  * details about your online activity, use and interaction with our General Services;
  * your vehicle information;
  * your shopping and other preferences; and
  * social media details and any other information you choose to share.



 **HOW WE COLLECT INFORMATION**

We collect information about you from a variety of sources, including:

  * details you choose to provide via our General Services;
  * from our affiliates;
  * from our business customers;
  * from outside, third-party sources; and
  * automatically, from devices that you use to connect or interact with to our AI or General Services.



 **HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION**

We may use the information we collect to:

  * provide the AI Services;
  * communicate with you;
  * provide and promote our products and Services;
  * market products and services of interest to you;
  * operate our business;
  * analyze the use of our products and services;
  * facilitate employee recruitment; and
  * comply with applicable laws, rules and our policies.



 **INFORMATION SHARING**

We may share the information we collect:

  * within our enterprise;
  * with our suppliers;
  * with our affiliates, Dealers, Dealer Associations, and business customers;
  * with our marketing partners;
  * as we otherwise may notify you; and
  * as LotLinx or its assets are acquired.



 **LotLinx Website and LotLinx Services**

LotLinx values the privacy of its dealer customers and users who respond to LotLinx’ information on the Internet. This section of the privacy statement explains our policies and practices regarding the different ways LotLinx may collect and use dealer customer information and an online user’s information when someone comes to our website or uses our Services. It is through this disclosure that we intend to provide you with a level of comfort and confidence in how we collect, use, and safeguard personal and other information we collect or that you provide through our website and those of our Affiliates, and how you can contact us if you have any questions or concerns. By using this or any of our branded or the LotLinx-specific sections of our affiliated sites, or our Services, you agree to the terms of this privacy statement. For information regarding the enrollment in the LotLinx Services, see Terms and Conditions and Terms of Use Document.

 **LotLinx Adserving**

This privacy statement also explains our policies and practices regarding the different ways LotLinx may collect and use shopper intent information and an online user’s information gathered through our adserving. By interacting with our adserver, you agree to the terms of this privacy statement.

 **Methods to Collect Information and Interest-Based/Online Behavioral Advertising**

 **LotLinx Website and LotLinx Services**

We may affiliate with sites that use third-party click tracking analytics tool (such as Adobe, DoubleClick, Efficient Frontier, Keynote, Google Analytics and ClickTale) to capture click through statistics.

This site may use spotlight ads/web beacons/GIF/pixel tags which are site instrumentation tools that help us to determine, for instance, whether a page has been viewed or not and, if so, how many times. Emails or electronic newsletters we send may use tools (e.g., pixel tags or web beacons) to gather email metrics and information to improve the reader’s experience such as how many of the emails are opened, if they were printed or forwarded, the type of device (e.g., mobile or PC) from which they were opened, and the city, state, and county associated with the applicable IP address. In general, any electronic image viewed as part of a webpage, including an ad banner, can act as a web beacon.

This website may generate a “session log” when you visit our site. We use session logs to help us determine how people travel through our site. In this way, we can structure our pages so that the information most frequently visited is easier to find. By tracking page visits, we can also determine if the information we’re providing is being used. The only data gathered is the Internet Protocol (IP) address from which you came (which contains no personal information), the website that referred you, the pages you visited and the date and time of those visits.

 **LotLinx Adserving**

Autos.lotlinx.com generates a “session log” when you pass through our tracking service. We use session logs to help us determine how frequently people pass through our service. In this way, we can structure our ads so that the information most frequently visited is easier to find. By tracking you, we can also determine if the information we’re providing is relevant to you. The only data gathered is the Internet Protocol (IP) address from which you came (which contains no personal information), the website that referred you, the pages you visited the date and time of those visits, and what VIN you were interested in.

 **Interest-Based/Online Behavioral Advertising**

 **LotLinx Website and LotLinx Services**

Interest-Based/Online Behavioral Advertising. On some pages of our site we may allow third-party advertising partners to set web tracking tools (e.g., cookies and web beacons) to collect anonymous, non-personally identifiable information regarding your activities on those pages (e.g., your IP address, page(s) visited, time of day). We may also share such information we have collected with third-party advertising partners. These advertising partners may use this information (and similar information collected from other websites) for purposes of delivering future targeted advertisements to you when you visit other sites within their networks. This practice is commonly referred to as “interest-based advertising” or “online behavioral advertising.”

Pages of our website that collect information that may be used by such advertising partners for interest-based advertising purposes are identified by a link to AdChoices on the page.

Opting out (site pages). If you do not want your browsing while on such pages to be used for interest-based advertising purposes, you may click on the AdChoices link to opt-out of such uses by the listed advertising partner(s). Even if you opt-out through this service, we may still collect non-personally identifiable information regarding your site activities and use it for non-interest-based advertising purposes as described in this privacy statement.

Opting out (advertisements). Advertisements on third-party sites that contain the AdChoices link and that link to this privacy statement may have been directed to you based on anonymous, non-personally identifiable information collected by advertising partners over time and across websites. These advertisements provide a mechanism to opt-out of the advertising partners’ use of this information for interest-based advertising purposes. Even if you opt-out through this service, we may still collect and use information from the advertisements for non-interest based advertising purposes, such as to determine the effectiveness of the advertisements.

 **LotLinx Adserving**

 **Sharing Your Information**

 **LotLinx Website and LotLinx Services**

Personally-identifiable information that you provide to us in the course of requesting information about a product or service through this website may be gathered and stored in one or more of our corporate databases. For site visitors in the U.S. personally identifiable information may be used for purposes of contacting you for things like promotional offers, marketing programs, or other communications from this website or other LotLinx programs and services.

Our “family of companies” is the group of companies related to us by common control or ownership. We share information within this “family” as a normal part of conducting business and offering products and services to our customers.

Vendors, contractors or partners of this site who have access to your personally identifiable information in connection with providing services for this site are required to keep the information confidential and are not permitted to use this information for any other purpose than to carry out the services they are performing for this site.

This site may share personally identifiable information with subcontractors. Subcontractors are restricted from using this data in any other way other than to provide these services to LotLinx and they may not share or resell this data.

This site will disclose your personally identifiable information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to a legitimate court or other regulatory order or other legal; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of LotLinx; or, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of LotLinx.
