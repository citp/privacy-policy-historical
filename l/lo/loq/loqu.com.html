Privacy Policy

Privacy Statement

Internet user privacy and confidentiality is of the utmost importance to Loqu. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.

Description of Information Collected and Usage

Unless the user provides it voluntarily, Loqu does not collect any personally identifiable information about visitors to Loqu.com.

Some Loqu pages permit visitors to input additional information. The information collected on these pages includes name, company name, and e-mail address. In addition, visitors are able to e-mail messages to Loqu that will contain the user's screen name and e-mail address, as well as any additional information the user may wish to include in the message.

Loqu uses the information visitors provide about themselves to fulfill those visitors' requests. Return e-mail addresses are used to answer messages received.

We keep your name, email address, mailing address, and phone number in our database to send registration information to you.

Loqu may use customer information to provide you with notices of new information that may be of use or interest to you. This may include newsletters, tutorials, website update notices, or maintenance releases.

Occasionally a customer may be asked to provide a statement, provide a reference, authorize a quote or testimonial, or participate in a press release or case study.

Policies Regarding Third Parties

Loqu does not currently share, trade, rent, or sell the information collected from this site - or information provided to us by our customers through offline means - to any third parties. Loqu does not have any plans to begin sharing, trading, renting, or selling any information about our customers or web site visitors to third parties.

Copyright

All editorial content and graphics on Loqu.com are protected and may not be copied without the permission of Loqu.com, which reserves all rights. If you see a violation of copyright on our site, please do not hesitate to let us know by email and we will deal with the rest.

Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Use of software author names, addresses and any other personal information is used only in our announcements and is not released to third parties nor do we send any mail to the return addresses offering or soliciting any services.

Loqu Privacy Policy Changes

If Loqu decides to change its privacy policy, Loqu will post these changes here. Please feel free to contact us if you have further questions regarding our privacy practices.
