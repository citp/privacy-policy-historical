This policy covers how we use your personal information. We take your privacy seriously and will take all measures to protect your personal information. We truly care about protecting your privacy. When you use LogotypeMaker, you agree to the collection and use of some personal information as described below. Any future changes to our policy will be noted on this page.

If you make a purchase, we collect your email and billing details. For your security, we do not store credit card data. You do not need to provide a credit card number to use the site. LogotypeMaker.com links to several other websites and products. Any data you provide to other sites is not covered by this privacy policy.

About LogotypeMaker

Logotype Maker generates itself millions of logos randomly, so all you'll have to do is press "generate logo" button to view them.
Free logos are located on in the right column on generate logo page and have a "Free" label in the left top corner.
With Logotype Maker editor you have the possibility to personalize your logo by changing color, font, shape, size, add Clip-ARTs and play with positioning and effects.
