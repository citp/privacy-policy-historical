# Local Jobs | every local job

LocalJobsAvailable.net is strongly committed to protecting the privacy of consumers ("Consumers") of its interactive products and services. The purpose of this privacy policy is to inform you, as a visitor to LocalJobsAvailable.net, what kinds of information we may gather about you when you visit _http://www.localjobsavailable.net_ ("Web Site"), how we may use that information, and whether we disclose it to anyone. This privacy policy applies only to information collected from you online, not offline. This privacy policy applies only to LocalJobsAvailable.net, its subsidiaries and affiliates, the Web Site, and other web sites owned by LocalJobsAvailable.net, but not to other companies' or organizations' web sites to which we link. 

If LocalJobsAvailable.net decides to change any of its privacy policies, the changes will be posted here in this area so that you will always know the information we gather, how we might use that information and to whom we will disclose it. We reserve the right, at our discretion to change, modify, add or remove portions of this policy at any time. Please make sure to check this page periodically for any changes. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not visit the Web Site. 

LocalJobsAvailable.net collects certain information from and about its users in four ways directly from you, from third parties, from our Web server logs, and with cookies. 

_User-Supplied Information_ : There are several areas in the Web Site where we collect personal information from you on a voluntary basis. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable that is not otherwise publicly available, such as your name, address, e-mail address, or phone numbers. We may collect this information at the following times: (i) when you register to receive communications from this Web Site and other LocalJobsAvailable.net owned websites; (ii) when you seek information or apply for products or services offered through this Web Site and other LocalJobsAvailable.net owned websites; (iii) when you enter promotions or sweepstakes offered by this Web Site or other LocalJobsAvailable.net owned websites; and (iv) when you provide personal information to us through feedback, survey, or other forms hosted on this Website or other LocalJobsAvailable.net owned websites. 

_Third Parties_ : We may receive information about you from third parties, such as service providers, for tracking purposes. We may add the information we receive about you to your existing account. 

_Server Log Information_ : When you visit an Internet Web site, you disclose certain information about yourself, such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address, the time of your visit, and the referring location (e.g., the site or page that offered a link to a LocalJobsAvailable.net page). Like many other sites, LocalJobsAvailable.netrecords this basic information about visits to its Site. 

_Cookies and IP Addresses_ : In order to offer and provide customized, personal service, LocalJobsAvailable.net may use cookies to store and help track your information. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive that contains information about the user. These cookies are relatively standard and do not collect personally identifiable information. Nor do we combine information collected through cookies with other personally identifiable information to tell us who you are or even what your username or e-mail address is. If for any reason you wish not to take advantage of cookies, you may have your browser not accept them, although this may disable or render unsuable some of the features of the LocalJobsAvailable.net website. 

You should remember that whenever you voluntarily disclose personal information online, such as on message boards, through e-mail, discussion groups, or in chat areas, your information can be collected and used by others. Although LocalJobsAvailable.net tries to protect your personal information, LocalJobsAvailable.net cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us, and you do so at your own risk. You are solely responsibility of maintaining the secrecy of any passwords you set up and/or any account information. 

We use information in different ways, depending on the type of information: 

_User-Supplied Information_ : This information is used for purposes such as personalization and verification. We also use this information to improve our internal operations, as well as to ensure that we: (i) bill you properly; (ii) administer your account in accordance with your agreements with us, including processing and fulfilling your requests for products and services; (iii) conduct research; (iv) customize the advertising and content that your see; (v) properly perform the services you have requested; and (vi) provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients. 

_Web Server Logs_ : This information helps us identify which areas of our site are of interest to our visitors. LocalJobsAvailable.net may also collect the Internet [Protocol] (IP) addresses of its visitors for the purposes of system administration and to report aggregate information to advertisers. 

_Cookies_ : We use to let you use our services more easily and to let us keep track of certain statistical information that helps us improve our Site. Cookies also allow us to save preferences for you so that you will not have to re-enter them the next time you visit. 

LocalJobsAvailable.net may share Consumer personal identifiable information with its partners, sponsors, advertisers, service providers and markets, as well as any other entities that LocalJobsAvailable.netbelieves are able to provide Consumers with special offers and opportunities. LocalJobsAvailable.net advises everyone who receives Consumer PII to support this privacy Policy. LocalJobsAvailable.net has no control over such party's information handling practices and is not responsible for any failure of such party to adhere to this Privacy Policy. 

LocalJobsAvailable.net may also share Consumer PII in aggregate form with select companies that wish to reach a particular Consumer demographic (e.g., male consumers between the ages of 35 and 55 with an average household income over $100,000). This information allows these entities to provide relevant offers to specific Consumers. 

**Legal Compliance**

LocalJobsAvailable.net may disclose Consumer PII in order to: (i) comply with applicable laws; (ii) respond to government inquiries; (iii) comply with valid legal process; and (iv) protect the rights or property of LocalJobsAvailable.net, the Web Site, or Consumers of the Web Site. 

**Minors**

THIS SITE IS NOT INTENDED FOR USE BY INDIVIDUALS UNDER 13 YEARS OF AGE. ACCORDINGLY, LocalJobsAvailable.net DOES NOT KNOWINGLY COLLECT, USE, OR DISCLOSE PERSONAL INFORMATION ABOUT INDIVIDUALS UNDER 13 YEARS OF AGE. 

**Opting-Out**

You may register to receive communicates from us directly on the Web Site or through a third party. After you register, we will periodically send you newsletters and other e-mails featuring special offers, promotions and other benefits. If you no longer wish to receive these communications, please let us know by clicking on the unsubscribe link in our e-mails. Our e-mail database is continually updated. However, you may continue to receive e-mail communications from us for up to ten business days from the time we receive your unsubscribe request. Please note that LocalJobsAvailable.net can only control its own mailing list and policies. Third party advertisers that maintain their own mailing lists may send communications that advertise our services, and you may need to contact those parties directly in order to stop receiving their e-mail communications. 

**Advertisements & Pop Up Windows**

Occasionally, you may receive advertisement for LocalJobsAvailable.net from third parties. These advertisements are served by the third party web site that you have visited or direct from this Web Site or other websites owned by LocalJobsAvailable.net. Additionally, such advertisements may be placed by third party servers or ad networks. We maintain relationships with a number of third party ad networks. You may find more information at the web site of either the individual ad network or the Network Advertising Initiative. 

**Third Party Links**

When you visit a website to which LocalJobsAvailable.netprovides a link, please be aware that you will be interacting with a third party that operates under its own privacy policy. If you choose to obtain services through a third party site, the data collected by the third party is governed by that third party's privacy policy. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any other service provider form whom you request services. 

**Framing**

In some cases, we may use third party vendors to operate portion of the Web Site. Although the particular section of the web site may have a similar appearance as the Web Site, information submitted through these outside third parties are not subject to this privacy policy unless otherwise stated. 

**California Privacy Rights**

This paragraph applies only to California residents. LocalJobsAvailable.net may, from time to time, share your personally identifiable information with unaffiliated third parties for their direct marketing purposes. As a California resident, you have a legal right to request and receive in writing or by email the names and addresses of all such third parties who received your personally identifiable information during the previous calendar year and a list of the types of personally identifiable information disclosed to such third parties. To request such information, please send an e-mail to [support@localjobsonline.net](mailto:support@localjobsonline.net) or write to: 402 Main Street Suite 100-224, Metuchen, NJ 08840. We will not honor such a request more than once per calendar year.
