# Privacy Policy - LocalSearch.ae

This Privacy and Security Policy addresses the privacy of visitors to the **LocalSearch.ae** website. It identifies the type of data and information **Local Pages Advertising & Publishing LLC** or its reseller collects from visitors, how we use and disclose it, and the steps we take to secure your privacy.

 **LocalSearch.ae** collects personal information when you become a Registered User with LocalSearch.ae when you use LocalSearch.ae products & services, when you visit LocalSearch.ae & when you enter promotions or contests.

We may also collect non-personal information about your visit, such as the domain name and the name of the web page from which you entered our site, how much time you spend on each page, and the IP address associated with your computer. This information is used to improve the performance and operation of the website.

When you become a Registered User, we ask for information such as your name, birth date, gender, country, city, contact numbers & email address.

LocalSearch.ae automatically receives and records information on our server from your browser, including your IP address, LocalSearch.ae cookie information & any page you request.

LocalSearch.ae uses the information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising & content you see, complete your requests for products & services, improve our services, contact you, and / or provide statistical reporting for internal & external customers. 

**Your Account Information**

You can edit your **LocalSearch.ae** Account Information at any time. By becoming a Registered User, you agree to receive select information from **Local Pages Advertising & Publishing LLC ** & its partners. 

**Internet Cookies and Related Technologies**

We may use cookies to improve the content delivered to you while you are on our website. Cookies do not contain or capture any personal information but it may capture computer identification information. Your decision to refuse cookies from LocalSearch.ae will not prevent you from using our site, but may limit your ability to take advantage of some of our advanced features.

Some pages of the website may use computer code called "one-pixel gifs," "clear gifs," or "Web beacons" which are embedded in web pages to monitor the activity on our website. This technology does not capture or contain any of your personal information. 

**Third Party Advertisers and External Links**

The LocalSearch.ae website contains advertisements from third-party advertisers or provides links to external websites. **Local Pages Advertising & Publishing LLC** is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of other websites. Our partners or advertisers may use third parties to serve or present the ads that appear on our site. These third parties may place cookies on your browser when you visit our site. Any third party cookies are subject to the privacy policy of the company placing them. 

**Information Disclosed To Third Parties**

**Local Pages Advertising & Publishing LLC** may use aggregated information about how visitors use our web site & disclose this information with our partners and related third parties in order to improve our website and attract advertisers and listings. For example, we may share how many individuals visit the website and on average how long they stay on each page. Such use and sharing of aggregated information does not disclose any of your personal information to third parties.

 **Local Pages Advertising & Publishing LLC** does not rent, sell, disclose or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies except to provide products, services, or information you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

•    We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.   
•    We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of **Local Pages Advertising & Publishing LLC**’s Terms and Conditions, or as otherwise required by law. 

**Local Pages Advertising & Publishing LLC** does not provide any personal information to the advertiser when you interact with or view an ad. 

**Security**

 **Local Pages Advertising & Publishing LLC** takes your security seriously & takes reasonable steps to protect your information. No data transmissions over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. We cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us and you do so at your own risk. Once we receive your transmission, we make reasonable efforts to ensure security on our systems. However, please note that this is not a guarantee that such information may not be accessed, disclosed, altered or destroyed by breach of any firewalls or secure server software we may employ. **Local Pages Advertising & Publishing LLC ** continues to evaluate and implement enhancements in security technology & practices; however we can only take steps to help reduce the risks of unauthorized access. 

By using this website or providing personal information to us, you agree that we can communicate with you electronically regarding security, privacy & administrative issues relating to your use of this site. 

**Secure Storage**

 **Local Pages Advertising & Publishing LLC** maintains reasonable physical, electronic & procedural safeguards to protect personal information about you. 

**Privacy and Security Policy Updates**

This Privacy and Security Policy supersedes and replaces all previously posted Privacy and Security Policies. **Local Pages Advertising & Publishing LLC ** reserves the right to update this Privacy and Security Policy to reflect any changes at any time.
