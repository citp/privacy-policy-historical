# localendar.com - Free web calendar hosting and calendar searching

_**Privacy Policy**_

localendar.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices.

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to help identify you and to gather broad demographic information. We use cookies to save a "session id" which is required for you to interact with our site properly. When you close your web browser, this cookie is automatically erased.

Cookies may also be used by any outside companies that display ads on our site. Cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information. 

Our registration form requires you to supply the following information: name, email address, zip-code, and home time-zone. This information is used as follows: 

  * Name: This information is used to help customize the site. We will also reference you by this name when sending e-mail. This information is not shared with anyone.


  * E-mail: This information is used to send you information about our company and to keep you informed of any changes to the service or availability problems. We do not share this information with others nor do we send solicitations for goods or services.


  * Zip Code: When you add a Public Event, a zip code is required information. We use the zip code you supply during registration as the default for this field. You can change this default value at any time.

We may also share your zip code with outside companies who send us ads. Your zip code may be used to send you information or advertisements specific to your geographic area.



  * Time-Zone: Your time zone is used to ensure that any Event Reminders you have requested are sent at the appropriate local time. This information is not shared with anyone.



Any information you enter in a Public Event on your calendars can potentially be viewed by anyone. Any information which you wish to keep confidential should only ever be entered as a Private Event. Private Event information is never disclosed or shared in any manner.

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All information is stored in secure operating environment that is not available to the public. All employees of localendar.com are dedicated to maintaining and upholding your privacy and security and are aware of our privacy and security policies.

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with localendar.com, please contact : 

[privacy_policy@localendar.com](mailto:privacy_policy@localendar.com)
