Using cookies

A cookie is a small text file placed onto your device that enables some LoansMonitor features and functionality to enhance your user experience. For example, cookies help us to identify your device and offer you the latest article you haven’t seen yet or to figure out how many new visitors we get each month. Cookies also make it simple for you to share interesting content on our website and help us serve relevant ads to you. In a nutshell, they contain information about the use of your device but don’t include personal information about you (for example, they don’t store your name).
