## Information We Obtain

The types of personal information we obtain through our websites and online services include:

  * Personal and business contact information (such as name, postal and email address, and telephone number);
  * log-in name and password;
  * demographic information such as date of birth, gender and marital status;
  * Social Security number, national identifier or other government-issued identification number;
  * information related to your transactions with us, our clients or our affiliates, such as your loan number, subject property, loan amount, payment information and history, financial account information, mortgage statements, tax information and documents, event notifications, credit information and history, escrow documentation and other documents and information related to your transactions; and
  * other personal information you submit to us, such as information you provide to us through emails, surveys, registrations, comments, the "Contact Us" feature on our websites, and other forms and documents you send us.



In connection with a job inquiry or application on our careers pages, you may provide login credentials; information regarding your work authorization status and citizenship, education history, employment history and professional designations; and other information about your qualifications and work-related skills. We also may obtain information regarding your political contributions, results of drug tests and criminal background checks, and government-issued identification numbers. In addition, where legally permissible, you may provide information regarding your gender, race and ethnicity, and your veteran and disability status.

We may obtain information that you may have provided to us through other methods, which we make available to you online as a part of providing the online services you have selected. In addition, we also may collect other information through our websites and online services in ways for which we provide specific notice or otherwise with your consent.

_Automated Collection of Data_

When you visit our websites, interact with our online services or open our emails, we may obtain certain information by automated means, such as cookies, web server logs, web beacons, and other technologies. A "cookie" is a text file that websites send to a visitor's computer or other Internet-connected device to uniquely identify the visitor's browser or to store information or settings in the browser. A "web beacon," also known as an Internet tag, pixel tag or clear GIF, links web pages to web servers and their cookies and may be used to transmit information collected through cookies back to a web server.

The information we obtain in this manner may include your device IP address, identifiers associated with your devices, types of devices, web browser characteristics, device characteristics, language preferences, clickstream data, dates and times of website visits, the site you visited just before coming to our websites, and other information about your use of our websites. These technologies help us (1) remember your information so you will not have to re-enter it; (2) track and understand how you use and interact with our websites and online services; (3) tailor our websites and online services around your preferences; (4) measure the usability of our websites and online services; (5) understand the effectiveness of our communications; (6) identify, diagnose and resolve technical issues; and (7) otherwise manage and enhance our websites, products and services.

Your browser may tell you how to be notified when you receive certain types of cookies or how to restrict or disable certain types of cookies. Please note that without cookies or the automated collection of certain information, you may not be able to use all the features of our websites. Our websites are not designed to respond to "do not track" signals received from browsers. For more information on deleting or controlling cookies, visit www.aboutcookies.org.

## How We Use The Information We Obtain

We may use the information we obtain about you to:

  * Provide and manage our websites, products and services;
  * process, evaluate and respond to requests, inquiries and claims we receive in connection with our websites, products and services;
  * create, manage and administer accounts, including identifying and authenticating you so you may access your account or use certain of our features of our websites and online services;
  * provide customer and technical support;
  * provide you with content, notices, offers, brochures, advertising and marketing materials on our websites and online services, by email and text, and in other ways;
  * personalize your experience with our websites, products and services;
  * maintain the security of our websites, products and services, and prevent fraud or misuse;
  * communicate with you about, and administer your participation in, surveys, special events, and other offers or promotions;
  * perform data analytics, market research and other processing;
  * operate, evaluate and improve our business (including developing new products and services; enhancing and improving our websites, products and services; managing our communications; measuring the effectiveness of our sales, advertising, communications and marketing; analyzing our customer base, websites, products and services; and performing accounting, auditing and other internal functions);
  * protect against, identify and prevent fraud and other criminal activity, claims and other liabilities; and
  * comply with and enforce applicable legal requirements, relevant industry standards and our policies.



We also may use the information we obtain about you in other ways for which we provide specific notice and obtain your consent if required by applicable law.

_Third-Party Online Analytics Services_

We use third-party online analytics services on our websites and online services, such as those of Google Analytics. The service providers that administer these analytics services use automated technologies to collect data (such as email, IP addresses, cookies and other device identifiers) to evaluate, for example, use of our websites, products and services and to diagnose technical issues. To learn more about Google Analytics, please visit [www.google.com/analytics/learn/privacy.html](https://service.loanadministration.com/euf/generated/optimized/1571924903/themes/chat/#).

## Information Sharing

We do not sell or otherwise share personal information about you except as described in this Online Privacy Notice. We may share your personal information with (1) our affiliates, and (2) service providers who perform services for us or on our behalf, such as related to payment processing, database and web hosting, advertising and marketing, data analytics and administrative or management functions. We do not authorize our service providers to use or disclose the information except as necessary to perform services for us or on our behalf or to comply with legal requirements. In addition, we may share personal information about you to our clients for whom Cenlar provides you with loan servicing or other services on their behalf.

We also may disclose information about you (1) if we are required to do so by law or legal process (such as a court order or subpoena); (2) in response to requests by government agencies, such as law enforcement authorities; (3) to establish, exercise or defend our legal rights; (4) when we believe disclosure is necessary or appropriate to prevent physical or other harm or financial loss; (5) in connection with an investigation of suspected or actual illegal activity; or (6) otherwise with your consent.

We reserve the right to transfer any information we have about you in the event we sell or transfer all or a portion of our business or assets (including in the event of a merger, acquisition, joint venture, reorganization, divestiture, dissolution or liquidation).

## Other Online Services and Third-Party Features

Our websites and online services may transfer you to or provide links to third-party websites and online services for your convenience and to provide selected services or functions. The privacy practices of the relevant third parties, including details on the information they may collect about you, is subject to the privacy statements of these parties, which we strongly suggest you review.

We maintain administrative, technical and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information against accidental, unlawful or unauthorized destruction, loss, alteration, access, disclosure or use.
