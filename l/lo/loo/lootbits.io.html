Privacy Policy
  1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy determines how your Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is being used online. PII is described by the US privacy law and information security as information that can be used on its own or with other information to identify, contact, or locate a single person, or to identify an individual in context. The following Privacy Policy informs how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your Personally Identifiable Information in accordance with our Website.

  1. Personal Information Collection

When visiting or registering on the Website, as appropriate, the Website asks you to enter and stores your email address, your created username and your created password. This information is needed to facilitate the login process and enable the features of the Website.

The Website determines and stores your IP address in order to facilitate invite system and fairly credit for referring users.

The Website does not require any real names, physical addresses or person verification.

The Website does not require any payment information and does not process payments.

  1. Personal Information Usage

We use the information we collect from you in order to enable the features of the website and personalize your experience.

Your created user name may be visible to other users.

The Website does not display your email address, full IP address or password to any of the Website users at any part of the Website.

  1. Personal Information Protection

We store as little data as possible to ensure delivery of all our Website features. We do not collect any sensitive personal data which could be used for making unauthorized payments or logins or to identify a person. We employ strict stored data protection measures.

At any time you are free to change your email address or password.

You can irreversibly erase the stored email address or password at any time by changing them to random values. The previous values will be erased permanently as soon as the new values are stored. Changing the email address to a non-working one will permanently eliminate the ability to edit it again or to use the password reminder function. This is the fastest way to erase your information.

You can contact us [email protected] if you need further assistance with account erasure proccess.

The user name is non changeable in order to ensure consistent working of all Website functions.

  1. Cookies

We use small text files (Cookies) that the Website or its service provider or a third party transfers to your browser's memory. Cookies allow the Website to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information. We use cookies to identify if you are logged in. We also use third party (Google Analytics) cookies to compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction.

You can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent in your browser settings. Turning off cookies completely will make some of the Website features (including signing up and logging in to restricted areas) not working.
