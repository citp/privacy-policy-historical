# Privacy Policy - LOOK-4IT.COM Meta Search Engine

  


## Internet User Privacy

 **LOOK-4IT.COM** is committed to protecting your privacy online. The following describes our commitment to the privacy of your information, the information we gather, and how that information is used.

 **LOOK-4IT.COM** provides links to many other Web sites that are beyond our control. **LOOK-4IT.COM** does not assume any responsibility for or make any warranties about the privacy practices or the Content of such Web sites.

## Information Collected on the Web Site

 **LOOK-4IT.COM** does record and track **IP addresses** , **referral website** , and **pages viewed** for statistical purposes. This data does not contain users personal information and is used to analyze our website logs (such as site hits, page views and referral websites). Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.

## Opt-Out of Promotional Communications

If you no longer wish to receive our occasional newsletters or other marketing communications from **LOOK-4IT.COM** you may **opt-out** of receiving these by sending an E-Mail message to us at [unsubscribe@look-4it.com](mailto:unsubscribe@look-4it.com).
