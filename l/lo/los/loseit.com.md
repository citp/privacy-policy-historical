# Herbalife Weight Loss Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy  
  
At Loseit.com we value every customer and want your online experience to be the best. To insure integrity, Loseit.com will only use any customer information collected by Loseit.com only for identification and contact purposes and/or for demographic information to be used by our staff. 

This information will be used to help Loseit.com ensure that its services are best designed to meet its customers' changing needs. Regarding e-mail, Loseit.com does not send unsolicited or "spam" e-mail. Loseit.com uses e-mail addresses collected for a number of e-mail alerts and newsletters which are used to communicate new information, special offers and new products to our customers. All customers are given the opportunity to unsubscribe at any time. Loseit.com does not sell or rent its e-mail lists to third parties. It is against Loseit.com's policy to provide any customer information to any third party without the customer's prior permission. 

If you need any help or have a question please call customer service toll free 1-888-475-5673. We are here to help you. We will help you find the person you are working with or direct you to a distributor.
