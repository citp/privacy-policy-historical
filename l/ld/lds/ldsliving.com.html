Our Privacy Policy


LDSLiving.com is committed to providing you with a secure and private online browsing experience. This privacy policy is our promise to you that the data we collect through this Web site will be used in a manner consistent with that commitment.

How does LDSLiving.com use personal information?


We use the personal information you give us to fulfill your magazine subscriptions or to send you our email newsletters if you sign up for them. We may also let you know about periodic product releases and offerings. Customers can unsubscribe from our email list at any time by visiting http://www.ldsliving.com/unsub.asp

Will personal information be shared with outside entities?


LDS Living is a Division of Deseret Book. We will use the information you provide us for internal purposes only. We will not sell, rent or trade the information to any outside entity.

How is my personal information protected from theft?


LDSLiving.com links to several outside websites that advertise on our site, such as DeseretBook.com. DeseretBook.com uses SSL (Secure Socket Layers) encryption technology, the standard for ensuring secure data transmission across the Internet. SSL ensures that the data you provide us can only be read by DeseretBook.com servers. When you purchase an item from us, your credit card number is stored in a location that is not accessible to the Internet.
