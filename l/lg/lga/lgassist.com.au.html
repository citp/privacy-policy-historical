Privacy policy

This site does not automatically capture or store personal information, other than logging the user’s IP address and session information such as the duration of the visit and the type of browser used. This is recognised by the webserver and is only used for system administration and to provide statistics which we use to evaluate use of the site.

We use cookies to tell whether job-seekers and advertisers are logged into the site, and to store session information.


You may be asked for personal information if you want to take advantage of specific services we offer such as email alerts. In each case we will only use the personal information you provide to deliver the services you have told us you wish to take part in. The information you provide will not be sold or distributed to third parties.
