# Privacy Policy

### Privacy Statement for Consumer Transactions

This is the Website of the Compulink Management Center, Inc., Laserfiche division.

Our postal address is:  
**3545 Long Beach Blvd.  
Long Beach, California 90807  
USA**  


We can be reached via e-mail at info@laserfiche.com.

You can reach us by telephone at **800-985-8533**.

When you visit our Website, [www.laserfiche.com](http://www.laserfiche.com/), our Web server automatically recognizes your domain name and e-mail address (where possible).

We collect the domain name and e-mail address (where possible) of visitors to our Website, the e-mail addresses of those who post messages to our bulletin board, the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, and the e-mail addresses of those who make postings to our chat areas. We also collect aggregate information on what pages visitors access or view, information volunteered by the visitor, such as survey information and/or site registrations, name and address, telephone number, and payment information (e.g., credit card number and billing address).

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Website, to customize the content and/or layout of the site for each individual visitor, and to contact visitors to reply to inquiries, and for marketing and other purposes.

We never make the e-mail addresses of those who access our Website available to other organizations. If you do not want to receive e-mail from us, please let us know by or sending us an e-mail at the following address: info@laserfiche.com.

With respect to cookies: We use cookies to store visitors’ preferences and to record session information, such as items that consumers add to their shopping cart.

If you supply us with your postal address online, you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or regarding upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending us an e-mail at the following address: info@laserfiche.com. In the e-mail message, please provide us with your exact name and address. We will then remove your name from our mailing list.

If you supply us with your telephone number online, you will only receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders you have placed online. If you do not want us to contact you by telephone with information regarding an order you’ve placed, please let us know by sending us an e-mail at the following address: info@laserfiche.com. In the e-mail message, please provide us with your exact name and telephone number. We will then remove your name from our list.

With respect to Ad Servers: We do not partner with or have special relationships with any Ad Server companies.

From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated purposes. If our privacy policy changes, we will post the updated policy on our Website to notify you of the change. Also, we will not use any customer information we collected prior to the change for these new purposes. If you are concerned about how your information is used, please check our Website periodically.

Upon request, we offer visitors the ability to have inaccuracies corrected regarding contact or financial information. You can have your information corrected by sending an e-mail to info@laserfiche.com.

With respect to security: When we transfer and receive certain types of sensitive information, such as financial information, we redirect visitors to a secure server and will notify visitors through a pop-up screen on our Website. We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you on our Website.

If you feel that this Website is not following the above-stated information policy, please contact us immediately at the above addresses or phone number.
