# LawCrossing - Privacy

| 

This privacy statement covers the site http://www.lawcrossing.com. If users have questions or concerns regarding this statement, they should first contact our User Privacy Department by emailing [privacy@lawcrossing.com](mailto:privacy@lawcrossing.com). LawCrossing is deeply committed to maintaining your privacy. 

**Why is your privacy so important to us?**  
Confidentiality and discretion are the cornerstones of every job search. We consistently challenge ourselves to rise above every competitor in our honesty and discretion, and we believe we have met this challenge day after day. **What personally identifiable information is collected on this site?**  
In order to register as a LawCrossing member, users are required to give us contact information and demographic information. Visitors may also give up personally identifiable information for other purposes, including submitting a resume or asking a question about LawCrossing. LawCrossing does not collect personal information from you unless you provide it to us. This means that you can visit our site without telling us who you are or revealing any personally identifiable information about yourself. **How is the information used?**  
When you supply personal information about yourself for a specific purpose, we use the information for only that purpose (such as to provide the service or information you have requested). Your contact information may also used to contact you when necessary to respond in accordance with the service or follow up with your expressed interest. Every communication with Law Crossing is held in strictest confidence, and none of your personal information will be disclosed to a potential employer without you taking affirmative action to disclose this information on your own. Your personal information will not be disclosed to other third parties under any circumstances. Any general demographic information that we receive will never be used to identify you, and may be used in our research to further develop our understanding of the job market. The general information may be disseminated or published if pertinent. 

Should any material changes be made to the ways in which we use personally identifiable information, LawCrossing will post the changes to our use of personally identifiable information on our site at least 30 days prior to a change, to give users time to request removal of their information from our database. 

**Security**  
Personally identifiable information is stored on our database and is not publicly accessible. All LawCrossing employees must sign non-disclosure contracts that ensure the confidentiality of all personally identifiable information before gaining access to such information. This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control, and meets or exceeds all industry standards for the protection and confidentiality of personally identifiable information. **Opt-out**  
If you have registered to receive our newsletter. job updates and other information and later change your mind, you may contact us to have your name removed from our distribution list. You have the following options to do this: 

You can send an e-mail to [privacy@lawcrossing.com](mailto:privacy@lawcrossing.com)

**Correct/Update**  
If you would like to verify the data we have received from you or to make corrections to it, or to deactivate the use of your personal information you may contact us directly at the addresses provided above. **What anonymous information is collected on this site?**  
IP addresses are briefly logged but discarded within a short period of time. No anonymous information that may be received from visitors is used in any of our work, promotions, or research. **How does this site use cookies?**  
LawCrossing uses cookies to identify your browser as you visit pages on the LawCrossing site. The cookies are used to present dynamic content so that you are not presented with identical content on every visit. No information from the cookies are used in any of our work, promotions or research. **Links to external pages**  
This site may contain links to other Web sites. Please note that when you click on one of these links, you are entering another site. LawCrossing is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. **Legal Disclaimer**  
We may disclose personal information when required by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary in order to conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on LawCrossing. **For more information**  
If you have any questions, concerns or comments regarding this statement, please e-mail [privacy@lawcrossing.com](mailto:privacy@lawcrossing.com).  |
