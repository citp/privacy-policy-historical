# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what Online Legal Media ("OLM") does with the personal information that is collected from the users of our website currently located at http://LawyersandSettlements.com (the “Site”). OLM may change its policy from time to time, so it is suggested that you check the Site from time to time to ensure that you know about current policy. If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, you should contact us via our [Contact Page](http://www.lawyersandsettlements.com/contact.html).

OLM may collect IP addresses to help understand how our audience uses the Site and to gather broader demographic information, so that OLM can make the Site better and improve users' overall experience when they visit the Site. For these same reasons, if your browser is set up to accept them, OLM uses a feature known as a "cookie." Cookies contain bits of information that websites transfer to your computer's hard drive for record keeping purposes. Cookies can make the Web more useful by storing information about your preferences on a particular site such as the Site. A cookie doesn't tell OLM who you are. Only you can do that.

**Children**  
If you are under 18, you may not use the Site.

**How OLM Discloses Your Personal Information**  
OLM will only disclose any personally identifiable information about individual users, in accordance with this Privacy Policy and the Terms of Service, or to comply with applicable laws or valid legal process, or to protect the rights or property of OLM.

OLM discloses personally identifiable individual information and the other information you provide us to lawyers, law firms, legal financial service providers, or other legal service providers sponsoring the submission forms for the topics you respond to. OLM cannot guarantee that the information you submit to us will not end up in the hands of third parties, including, without limitation, the company or person against whom you may have a complaint.

OLM may also disclose your contact information and the other information that you provide us in the following circumstances:

  1. OLM may employ other companies and individuals to perform functions on our behalf. They may have access to personal information needed to perform their functions, but will not be able to legally use it for other purposes.
  2. OLM might change, sell, reorganize or liquidate via bankruptcy this business. If so, your information will of course be one of the transferred assets.
  3. OLM may release account and other personal information when OLM believes release is appropriate to comply with law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of OLM, our users, or others. 



From time to time OLM may also provide aggregate (statistical) information to third parties to describe our services to prospective partners, advertisers, and other third parties, and for other lawful purposes. If OLM partners with another party to provide a specific service to you, and if you sign up for the service, then and only then will OLM provide your other information to such partner.

**Our other Services**  
OLM may send you occasional notices or newsletters about OLM' services, unless you tell us not to. If you do not want to receive such offers and mailings, you can click the unsubscribe link, which you will find on our email messages or send an email at any time via our [Contact](http://www.lawyersandsettlements.com/contact.html) page.

When you submit information to the Site, you will be sent a complimentary issue of our Class Action and Large Recoveries Newsletter, in order to keep you up to date with new class actions and settlements. If you do not wish to receive further issues, please use the unsubscribe link provided at the bottom of the newsletter.

**How to Edit the Information OLM Has About You**

If you want to correct or change any information we have about you, please [Contact Us](http://www.lawyersandsettlements.com/contact.html)

**Security**  
OLM takes reasonable precautions to protect our users' information. If you have any questions about the security at the Site, you can [send an email to us](http://www.lawyersandsettlements.com/contact.html).

**Conditions of Use**  
Any dispute over privacy is subject to our [Terms of Service](http://www.lawyersandsettlements.com/terms.html), including limitations on damages, venue of disputes, and the application of the laws of California. 

**Contacting Us**  
If you have any questions or comments about the practices of this site, or your dealings with us, you may [Contact Us](http://www.lawyersandsettlements.com/contact.html).
