How does Lightningwear.com use my information?

 

When you place an order or make a purchase from Lightningwear.com, we use your personal information to process your order, send you e- mails to confirm your order or to contact you via phone, postal mail or e-mail if we have other questions regarding your order or purchase.

 

From time to time, we may use this information to contact you via e-mail, postal mail, or telephone to learn more about your shopping preferences. You always have the choice not to receive marketing information or calls.

 

Does lightningwear.com share my information with third parties?

 

Lightningwear.com does not sell any of your personal information provided on our site to any third partiers.
