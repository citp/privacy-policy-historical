# Privacy policy - LaterThis

By using laterthis.com site you are agreeing to the following policies in terms of information collection.

The information we collect is used to notify you about updates and improvements, to contact you about your account and used for identification and authentication. **We will not sell or share your information with other companies or organizations for commercial purposes.**

## Privacy Settings

**Links saved to laterthis.com are set to be public by default.** This means that all links saved to the service can be seen by anyone visitng laterthis.com.

## About Cookies

We use cookies to store information about your visit. Cookies are small files or similar stored on your hard drive. They help us keep track of your current session and make the navigation and use of our web sites as easy as possible. **Having cookies enabled in your browser is required to use LaterThis.**

## Changes

From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our Web site periodically. The latest version of this Privacy Policy is always located at: <http://laterthis.com/static/privacy>.

Any questions about this Privacy Policy should be addressed to support at laterthis dot com
