​Collection of Information 

This page describes the privacy practices regarding what data is collected and how it is used from visitors to websites owned and operated by departments under the Lee County Board of County Commissioners, including www.leegov.com and its subsites. 

These practices may be updated periodically without notice. 

Security of Financial Transactions 

Lee County websites use industry standard data encryption for credit card transactions through third-party vendors to collect payment.

Written Communications & Public Records

Most written communication to or from County officials regarding County business, including information submitted in website forms, is not secure and is subject to public records requests by the public and media, unless specifically exempted by Florida law. If you do not want your email address released in response to a public records request, do not send electronic mail to this entity. Emails sent to staff will in no way be considered binding correspondence. For critical or legal correspondence, please contact staff via telephone or U.S. Postal Service.

Why Do We Collect Website Use Information?

Your privacy is important to us. We will not collect personal information about you when you visit Lee County websites, unless you explicitly choose to make such information available to us. When you visit our websites, our web server collects the referring domain name (E.g.: www.google.com) URL of pages viewed, IP address from which you accessed the site (E.g.: 52.167.215.137), user agent string from your browser, and operating system version. Our website uses standard technology called "cookies,” unique identifiers sent to computers by our web server when you visit our website that identify the computer but not the user. Cookies and web server logs collect information about how our website is used in order to improve content. Information gathered may include date and time of visits, pages viewed, time spent at our website, and referring websites. Our website analytics tool, SiteImprove, also collects non-personal information associated with the IP address and cookie session. Information collected includes URL visited, page title, user’s monitor resolution, referring address, page load times, session identifier, breadcrumb trail on leegov.com, search words used, scrolls and clicks on pages, and user agent string. Cookies from leegov.com expire 24 hours after you leave our site.

How Do We Use Shared Personal Information?

If you choose to share personal information, such as subscribing to an e-newsletter, sending a message, or filling out an electronic form, the information will only be used for authorized County business and not disclosed to third parties unless required by Florida or federal law. Once you register with us and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us, and the information will be saved for a designated period of time in order to comply with the state of Florida's archiving policies.

Opting Out

If you have registered with us, or subscribed to alerts or newsletters, you have the opportunity to unsubscribe/opt out of receiving future communications. 

External Websites

We are not responsible for the practices of websites that are linked to or from our website, nor the information or content contained therein. Contact InformationIf you have other questions about our privacy policies or suggestions for improvement, call 239-533-2306.
