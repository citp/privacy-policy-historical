# Lexsynergy - Domain name management

Lexsynergy Limited (Company Number 05992211) operates the website www.lexsynergy.com (“the Site”).

Lexsynergy has prepared this Privacy Policy to affirm its commitment to privacy, by disclosing the way it acquires and processes information obtained through the Site.

Lexsynergy Limited is registered with the Data Protection Registrar (No. Z9846105 ). If you wish to obtain more information about your rights under UK data protection laws, we encourage you to visit [www.dataprotection.gov.uk](http://www.dataprotection.gov.uk/).

As mentioned in our [Terms and Conditions](https://web.archive.org/index.php/terms-and-conditions/) this Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to make provision for new and/or changed services offered by Lexsynergy Limited. If you have any queries, please send an email to [info@lexsynergy.com](mailto:info@lexsynergy.com).

Lexsynergy Limited may collect the following information:

  * your name, company name, physical address, postal address, VAT number, telephone (including mobile) and fax numbers, email addresses and other contact information to enable Lexsynergy Limited to administer payment and technical functions relating to its services.
  * additional private information that may be provided during the course of your use of the Site.



Lexsynergy Limited shall use your information for:

  * the performance of our services as listed on the Site from time to time.
  * creation, maintenance and administration of your account.
  * Submission to various domain name registries / registrars in order to register, transfer, update and renew domain names.



Your information may be disclosed on the whois databases of various registry / registrars, which is required for the registration and other services related to domain name administration.

Lexsynergy is not responsible for the privacy of your information on external links not under the control of Lexsynergy Limited.
