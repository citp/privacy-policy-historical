We take our customer privacy very seriously and are committed to our customers' privacy. We have undertaken to disclose our information practices and have them reviewed and certified to the best in class privacy standards for our customers.

LendUp's Best-In-Class Privacy Policy

LendUp's Privacy Policy describes the types of user information LendUp collects and the ways we collect, store, use and protect it. We may amend this policy at any time by posting a revised version on our website, and you can find the last update date on the bottom of this document. The revised version will be effective at the time we post it. By using or accessing LendUp, you are accepting the policies and practices described in this Privacy Policy.

LendUp uses a state-of-the-art system to protect and safeguard our customer's information. When you enter personally identifiable information on our registration page we use a 128-bit secure socket layer (SSL) and our data is stored in a physically protected environment. Physical access is strictly controlled by professional security staff utilizing video surveillance, intrusion detection systems, and other electronic means. Our data is encrypted before, during and after it is submitted to have the best protection of your information. Only authorized employees can access the data and they must submit multiple forms of identity verification before accessing any information.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100 percent secure. While we strive to use best possible commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

LendUp Safely Protects ALL Your Information

LendUp collects two types of information to create the best customer experience: (1) Personal information that users like you submit to LendUp via our website and‚ (2) Website usage information that is automatically generated by users as they interact with the site or information that we access via application programming interfaces.

  1. Personal Information

    LendUp uses personal information as required by state and federal regulators to assure we are promoting safe and responsible lending.

    When users sign up for LendUp, they are asked to provide profile information, such as name, address, phone number, email address, preferences, and other similar information. LendUp may also collect certain financial information, including bank account numbers, routing numbers, debit card numbers or similar information for funding and repayment purposes. We also collect tokens from online services and payment systems.

    LendUp may also require users to provide additional information to help verify identity or address or manage risk, such as date of birth, social security number, or other information. LendUp may also obtain more information about applicants from third parties to help us validate this information.

  2. Website Usage Information

    LendUp uses "website usage" information to help create a better user experience.

    LendUp collects certain information from users' browsers using small text files called "cookies." LendUp uses session ID cookies to confirm that users are logged in. This type of cookie helps LendUp recognize a user if he or she visits multiple pages on our site during the same session, so that we do not need to require a password to access each page. These cookies terminate once the user closes the browser. We also use a persistent cookie that stores your login ID (but not your password) to make it easier for you to login when you come back to LendUp on future visits. We encode our cookies so that only we can interpret the information stored in them, but they do not contain personally identifiable information. Users can remove or block the persistent cookie using the settings in your browser if you want to disable the feature.

    LendUp also stores transaction history and user relationships/interactions. We collect this information so that we can provide users with a meaningful service.

    LendUp may collect additional information in other ways not specifically described here. For example, we may collect information related to interactions with customer services, collections, surveys or other feedback tools from third parties using technologies such as application programming interfaces (APIs).

Security

LendUp Protects Your Information With The Latest Internet Technology.

LendUp collects and stores certain information in order to provide the best solutions for our customers, today and tomorrow. We go beyond industry standards to make sure your information is safe and secure, and we restrict access to protect you against fraud.

LendUp stores and processes user information using secure servers located only in the United States. This information is protected by physical, electronic and procedural safeguards in compliance with applicable US federal and state regulations. LendUp uses computer safeguards such as firewalls and data encryption, and authorizes access to personal information only for those employees who require it to fulfill their job responsibilities.

LendUp does not store payment or related personal data at its office. Instead of storing payment information on company systems, LendUp stores this information in PCI-compliant and PA-DSS certified data centers. LendUp encrypts and securely transmits user payment information to the banking network at the time of initial payment acceptance. LendUp receives the response along with a "payment token" that it uses to reference that transaction for any future billing action. LendUp stores only this secure token.

LendUp Does Not Share Your Information Without Your Permission.

LendUp does not share a user's personal or financial information with other customers.

LendUp's primary purpose in collecting personal information during website usage is to provide users with a safe, smooth, efficient, and customized experience and allow them to take full advantage of our products and services.

LendUp uses this information to provide services and support; process transactions and send notifications regarding general operation of the service, such as messages or reminders, resolve disputes, collect fees, and troubleshoot problems; prevent potentially prohibited or illegal activities, and enforce our user agreement; customize, measure, and improve our services and the content and layout of our website; compare information for accuracy and verify it with third parties.

LendUp Does Not Sell Your Info To Third Parties.

LendUp takes your privacy seriously and does not sell your data to third parties.

LendUp may share personal information with partners under contract who help with certain parts of our business operations including application evaluation, customer services, payment, fraud prevention, secure data storage and other similar services.

LendUp will only use personal information in connection with the services LendUp provides. LendUp may share personal information in the event of a merger or acquisition. Should such an event occur, LendUp will seek to require that the new combined entity follow our privacy policy with respect to personal information you have provided.

LendUp may be compelled to share personal information with law enforcement, government officials, or other third parties in the event of a subpoena, court order or similar legal procedure, or when LendUp believes in good faith that the disclosure of personal information is necessary to prevent physical harm or financial loss, to report suspected illegal activity, or to investigate violations of our user agreement.

LendUp will not sell or rent any of your personal information to third parties in the normal course of doing business and only shares your personal information with third parties as described in this policy, unless we implement a policy change and inform you of such change.

LendUp Allows You to Control Your Account.

LendUp allows you to manage your account and information on www.lendup.com. Once you login you may update your personal information, assuming your account is in good standing. In that case, we will retain only the personal information required by law or that we deem necessary.

LendUp Publishes Our Privacy Policy For Everyone To See.

You can always see the most updated changes to the LendUp Privacy Policy on www.lendup.com or by contacting LendUp at privacy@lendup.com. In the event we modify this privacy policy, we may contact you via email and provide you with notice of the change and a link to review the new privacy policy.
