Amid different things, Cookies avoid you having to log in every time you come back to the website. Cookies also allow tailoring a website or advertisement to better match your interest and preferences.

Not to mention a session cookie that is stored only in your system and last for your browsing session. At the time of closing all browsers’ windows or at the time of shutting down your computer, the session cookie disappears forever.

You will also see persistent cookie remains in your system after you close browser. It remains to get used by your browser on subsequent visits to the services. Cookies may also stay in system until they expire or overwritten with newer cookies or you manually remove them.
