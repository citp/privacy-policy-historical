Privacy Policy

We collect no personal information about you when you visit our site unless you choose to provide it to us. However, we do collect and store certain information automatically.

What We Collect Automatically
  • The name of the domain and IP address from which you access the Internet.
  • The date and time you access our site.
  • The type of browser and the operating system you use to access our site.
  • The Internet address of the Website (if any) from which you linked directly to our site.

We use this information to determine the number and type of visitors to different pages on our site which helps us make our site more useful.

What We Collect If You Send Us Feedback

You may choose to provide us with personal information through our Feedback page. We use this information to improve our service or to respond to your request.
