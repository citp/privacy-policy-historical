# Privacy Policy

This website respect your privacy and is committed to safeguarding your privacy while online at [http://learnfinancialeducation.com](http://learnfinancialeducation.com/). The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site.

Like most standard Web site servers, I use log files. This includes Internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.

E-mail Subscription  
If a user want to subscribe to my blog via e-mail, I require for contact information such as name and email address. Out of respect for my users’ privacy, a way to opt-out of these feature is provided.

Cookies  
A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user’s computer tied to information about the user. This site use cookies for tracking visitors’ activity. Some of my business partners use cookies on our site such as our advertisers. However, I have no access to or control over these cookies.

Links  
This Web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that I am not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. I encourage my users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every Web site that collects personal information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site.

Advertisers  
I use advertisement companies to display ads on my site. These ads may contain cookies and are collected by the ad companies, and I do not have access to these information. I work with the following ad companies: Google Adsense and Infolinks. Please check their websites for respective privacy policies.

Contact Information  
If users have any questions or suggestions regarding my privacy policy, please contact me by sending email to: kumitakaonline at gmail dot com
