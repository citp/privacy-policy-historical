Privacy & Security

We're Committed to Protecting Your Privacy

Leatherman Tool Group, Inc. [Leatherman] is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. Leatherman securely protects the privacy of our members through stringent security policies to safeguard your personal data.

Data Management Policies

Our goal is to serve your needs and interests while upholding high levels of responsible data management. Leatherman never discloses your highly sensitive personal information to any third party without your express permission.

Contributions to Leatherman

Any information that you submit via our web site or email becomes the exclusive property of Leatherman — however, we will protect all of your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Leatherman's Privacy Policy Coverage

Leatherman's Privacy Policy covers the handling of personally identifiable information collected by Leatherman when you are on the Leatherman site or using Leatherman services. This Privacy Policy also covers the Leatherman use of any personally identifiable information that our business partners share with Leatherman or that Leatherman may collect on a partner's site.
