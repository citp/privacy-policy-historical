Our Privacy Policy

When you provide us with your email address, please know that in addition to your weekly league update emails, we also send out a bi-monthly newsletter.

We hate spam as much as anyone else. Therefore we will NEVER sell or rent your email to any 3rd party. There are times when a 3rd party would like to contact our users. In this case, we will send their email for them through our servers and will never provide them with your info.

Therefore, your email address is safe with us.
