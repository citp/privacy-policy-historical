# Privacy Policy | Learn PeopleString

LearnPeopleString.com is owned and operated by Secret Fortune Society, LLC.

 **Information Gathered From Visitors**

We may record and store information in the log files on our server and through third party analytics programs such as Google Analytics, Quantcast, GetClicky and ChartBeat. The records may include basic statistics such as the visitor’s IP address, browser type, referring page, time of visit, and other non-identifying and possibly identifying information (if carefully reconstructed AFTER you submit voluntary information to the site).

We may use cookies to remember your visits to this site. The analytics programs may use cookies as well, depending on the companies. You can read their policies at their respective sites.

The information submitted in the form on the front page or when purchasing a product is submitted to a secondary site, Aweber, a company that helps us manage all of our requests for services. Their privacy and anti-spam policies can be read at aweber.com.

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.

 **How we use the information**

We use the information indirectly collected, analytics, to optimize the site for the best possible experience for our visitors. We use the directly collected information, emails, in order to stay in contact and deliver the best communication experience for our members and other interested visitors.

E-mail addresses we collect are NEVER sold to third parties. We will be the only people contacting you if you submit a name and email address on our site.

We may send you emails in order to alert you of new [products](http://learnpeoplestring.com/products-2/ "products") or services we offer or endorse as well as to maintain an ongoing relationship.

Unless otherwise stated in this Privacy Policy data relating to you will not be disclosed to any third party unless you have specifically given your consent. We will not rent or sell your personal information other than as part of the sale of the assets of the website.

Secret Forutne Society may disclose your personal data acting in good faith if it believes such action is necessary: to conform with a legal requirement or comply with the legal process, protect and defend the rights or property of Secret Fortune Society, enforce this online agreement, act to protect the interests of its users.

 **Visitor Options**

At any point you may unsubscribe from our email list by clicking on the unsubscribe links provided at the bottom of every email.

Blocking cookies may or may not affect the functioning of the site. Doing so is entirely up to you.

 **Cookies**

Cookies are a very standard method of tracking visitors by placing a small piece of code on your computer in order to monitor your patterns of accessing our site, using our site, and leaving our site.

3rd party advertising companies may also use cookies for tracking purposes.

 **Information Security**

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to the best of our abilities to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
