# Learning Ace

Posted on December 5, 2011  
LearningAce (owned and operated Ace Learning Company, Inc. (hereinafter "LearningAce")) values the privacy of the users, subscribers, publishers, members, and others who visit and use the LearningAce Platform (collectively or individually, "You" or "Users"). By using the LearningAce Platform, you expressly consent to the information handling practices described in this notice. 

This Privacy Policy Notice is incorporated into and is subject to the LearningAce Platform Terms of Use. Your use of the LearningAce Platform and any personal information you provide through the LearningAce Platform are subject at all time to this Privacy Notice and the Terms of Use. Terms for "LearningAce" and the "LearningAce Platform" apply to any site or mobile application owned and operated by Ace Learning Company, Inc., including LearningAce.com. 

**The Information LearningAce Collects**

  * **User-provided Information** : You may provide to LearningAce what is generally called "personally identifiable" information (such as your name, email address, postal mailing address, home/mobile telephone number) if you log in to the LearningAce Platform, or otherwise use the features and functionality of the LearningAce Platform. 
  * **"Cookies" Information** : When you access the LearningAce Platform, we may send one or more cookies - small text files that contain a string of alphanumeric characters - to your computer. LearningAce may use both session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie disappears after you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser and may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the LearningAce Platform. One type of persistent cookie that may be installed on your computer when you use LearningAce is called a "Flash cookie" or an "LSO." Persistent cookies can be removed. Please review your web browser's "Help" file to learn the proper way to modify your cookie settings. For more information on how to manage Flash cookies, you can go here. For more information about cookies and how they are used, you can go here. 
  * **"Automatically Collected" Information** : When you access the LearningAce Platform or open one of our HTML emails, we may automatically record certain information from your system by using different types of tracking technology. This "automatically collected" information may include Internet Protocol address ("IP Address"), a unique device or user ID, version of software installed, system type, the content and pages that you access on the LearningAce Platform, the dates and times that you visit the LearningAce Platform, and information from your Facebook account that you make publicly available, which is provided to us by Facebook via the Facebook APIs. 



**The Way LearningAce Uses Information**

  * LearningAce uses the information that you provide or that we collect to operate, maintain, enhance, and provide all of the features and services found on the LearningAce Platform as well as to track user-generated content and Users to the extent necessary to comply as a service provider with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act. 
  * We will use your email address, without further consent, for administrative communications such as notifying you of major LearningAce Platform updates, for customer service purposes, to address copyright infringement or defamation issues, or to contact you regarding any content that you have posted to or downloaded from the LearningAce Platform. 
  * LearningAce uses all of the information that we collect to understand the usage trends and preferences of our Users, to improve the way the LearningAce Platform works and looks, and to create new features and functionality. 
  * LearningAce may use "Automatically Collected" information and "Cookies" information to: (a) automatically update the LearningAce application on your system; (b) remember your information so that you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or the next time you access the LearningAce Platform; (c) monitor aggregate site usage metrics such as total number of visitors and pages accessed; (d) provide such metrics to Users as they pertain to such User"™s uploaded content; and (e) track your entries, submissions, and status in any promotions or other activities. 



**When LearningAce Discloses Information**

  * LearningAce does not share your personally identifiable information with other organizations for their marketing or promotional uses without your prior express consent. Please be aware, however, that any personally identifiable information that you voluntarily choose to display on the LearningAce Platform - such as when you publish any works or profile information - becomes publicly available and may be collected and used by others without restriction. 
  * We may disclose User information to affiliated companies or other businesses or persons to: provide web site hosting, maintenance, and security services; fulfill orders; conduct data analysis and create reports; offer certain functionality; and assist LearningAce in improving the LearningAce Platform and creating new services features. We require that these parties process such information in compliance with this Privacy Notice, we authorize only a limited use of such information, and we require these parties to use reasonable confidentiality measures. 
  * LearningAce may disclose User information if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with state and federal laws (such as U.S. Copyright law) or respond to a court order, judicial or other government subpoena, or warrant in the manner required by the requesting entity. 
  * LearningAce also reserves the right to disclose User information that we believe, in good faith, is appropriate or necessary: to take precautions against liability; to protect LearningAce from fraudulent, abusive, or unlawful uses; to investigate and defend ourselves against third-party claims or allegations; to assist government enforcement agencies; to protect the security or integrity of the LearningAce Platform; or to protect the rights, property, or personal safety of LearningAce, our Users, or others. 
  * LearningAce shows aggregate geographical information about visitors on LearningAce document pages in our analytics section. This information includes the cities and countries of people viewing documents, the document that was viewed, and the time the document was viewed. This information is publicly available to all LearningAce users. 



**Your Choices**  
You may, of course, decline to share your personally identifiable information with LearningAce, in which case LearningAce will not be able to provide to you some of the features and functionality found on the LearningAce Platform. You may update, correct, or delete your user information and preferences at any time here.  
To protect your privacy and security, we take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting you account access or making corrections to your information. **YOU ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR MAINTAINING THE SECRECY OF YOUR UNIQUE PASSWORD AND ACCOUNT INFORMATION AT ALL TIMES.**

**Our Commitment to Data Security**  
LEARNINGACE USES COMMERCIALLY REASONABLE PHYSICAL, MANAGERIAL, AND TECHNICAL SAFEGUARDS TO PRESERVE THE INTEGRITY AND SECURITY OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION. WE CANNOT, HOWEVER, ENSURE OR WARRANT THE SECURITY OF ANY INFORMATION YOU TRANSMIT TO LEARNINGACE, AND YOU DO SO AT YOUR OWN RISK. ONCE WE RECEIVE YOUR TRANSMISSION OF INFORMATION, LEARNINGACE MAKES COMMERCIALLY REASONABLE EFFORTS TO ENSURE THE SECURITY OF OUR SYSTEMS.HOWEVER, PLEASE NOTE THAT THIS IS NOT A GUARANTEE THAT SUCH INFORMATION MAY NOT BE ACCESSED, DISCLOSED, ALTERED, OR DESTROYED BY BREACH OF ANY OF OUR PHYSICAL, TECHNICAL, OR MANAGERIAL SAFEGUARDS.   
IF LEARNINGACE LEARNS OF A SECURITY SYSTEMS BREACH, THEN WE MAY ATTEMPT TO NOTIFY YOU ELECTRONICALLY SO THAT YOU CAN TAKE APPROPRIATE PROTECTIVE STEPS. LEARNINGACE MAY POST A NOTICE ON THE LEARNINGACE PLATFORM IF A SECURITY BREACH OCCURS. DEPENDING ON WHERE YOU LIVE, YOU MAY HAVE A LEGAL RIGHT TO RECEIVE NOTICE OF A SECURITY BREACH IN WRITING. TO RECEIVE A FREE WRITTEN NOTICE OF A SECURITY BREACH YOU SHOULD NOTIFY US AT PRIVACY@LEARNINGACE.COM. 

**Our Commitment to Children'ss Privacy**  
IF YOU ARE UNDER 13 YEARS OF AGE, THEN PLEASE DO NOT USE OR ACCESS THE LEARNINGACE PLATFORM AT ANY TIME OR IN ANY MANNER. PROTECTING THE PRIVACY OF YOUNG CHILDREN IS ESPECIALLY IMPORTANT. FOR THAT REASON, LEARNINGACE DOES NOT KNOWINGLY COLLECT OR MAINTAIN PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION FROM PERSONS UNDER 13 YEARS-OF-AGE. IF LEARNINGACE LEARNS THAT PERSONALLY-IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION OF PERSONS LESS THAN 13-YEARS-OF-AGE HAS BEEN COLLECTED ON OR THROUGH THE LEARNINGACE PLATFORM, THEN LEARNINGACE WILL TAKE THE APPROPRIATE STEPS TO DELETE THIS INFORMATION. IF YOU ARE THE PARENT OR LEGAL GUARDIAN OF A CHILD UNDER 13 WHO HAS BECOME LEARNINGACE PLATFORM MEMBER, THEN PLEASE CONTACT LEARNINGACE AT PRIVACY@LEARNINGACE.COM TO HAVE THAT CHILD"™S ACCOUNT TERMINATED AND INFORMATION DELETED.
