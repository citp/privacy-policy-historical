# 


    Privacy Policy
    
    
    
    
    
    Our Commitment To Privacy
    
    
    
    Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice   
    
    explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about   
    
    the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find,   
    
    we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable   
    
    information may be requested.
    
    
    
    
    
    
    
    
    
    This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on Leadclub.net website.   
    
    On some pages, you can order products/subscriptions, make requests, and register to receive materials.   
    
    The types of personal information collected at these pages are:
    
    
    
    
    
    Name:  
    
    
    Address:  
    
    
    Email address:  
    
    
    Phone number:  
    
    
    SS if US resident and if you join the affiliate program.
    
    
    
    
    
    
    
    The Way We Use Information:
    
    
    
    If you sign up on our site for the above reasons  
    
    we use the information simply in for contact purposes and in relation   
    
    to your membership to our site. It is not sold to any third parties.
    
    
    If you place a free ad on our free links system you agree to receive a one time confirmation email   
    
    from our members in exchange for the free advertising. This is clearly marked on the free advertising registration form.  
    
    Members WILL have access to your email address and the information  
    
    that you posted in your ad on our system. You are agreeing to allow members to send you 1 email advertisement  
    
    per ad that you put on our system. If members send you more than one confirmation email please contact   
    
    us with a copy of the email at [info@leadclub.net](mailto:info@leadclub.net) and the member's account will be suspended.

  
With the affiliate program we do not require your social security number. You simply can fill in 000-000-0000.  
If you earn more than $600. and you are a US citizen we will contact you later for any needed information.   
Your SSN is NEVER given to anyone under any circumstances.Actually, we will most likely be taking that field off of our   
forms in the future.
    
    
    
    
    Our Commitment To Data Security
    
    
    
    
    
    To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct   
    
    use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic,   
    
    and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
    
    
    
    
    
    How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information:
    
    
    
    
    
    You can access all your personally identifiable information that we collect online and maintain by sending  
     
    an email to info@leadclub.net with a specific request.. We use this procedure to better safeguard your information.
    
    
    
    
    
    You can correct factual errors in your personally identifiable information by sending us a request that credibly shows error.
    
    
    
    
    
    To protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections.
