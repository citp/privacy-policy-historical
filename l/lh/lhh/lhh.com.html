WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect personal information (“Personal Information”) and other information (“Other Information”) about you when you visit this Website. Personal Information is information that can be used to identify you or information that relates to you. Other Information is information we collect but is not an identifier of you.

Personal Information

Types of Personal Information we might collect include any part of or your full name and your personal and/or work contact information which could include any or all of the following: physical address, email address, phone numbers, and fax numbers. We might also collect your job title, your position, role, and your company. We might collect your username and other login credentials for the Website. We might collect the results of your assessments, surveys and other content you provide, if any, to the online tools or other sections of the Website. We might collect a list of items or materials you download from the Website. And we might collect any other Personal Information you provide to us through the Website or offline of the Website through user-forms, contact requests or conversations with our personnel or consultants.

Other Information

We might collect your IP address, your browser version and the device type you are using to access the Website. We might collect general demographic information and your usage statistics on the Website including but not limited to the number of clicks, the number of times the Website is accessed, the pages you visited, the length of time you spent on each page and other information all in an aggregated form that does not identify you.

HOW DO WE COLLECT PERSONAL AND OTHER INFORMATION?

We collect your Personal and Other Information, both actively and passively, largely from your direct use of this Website but also in some cases from you in other ways, from your employer/ex-employer, and from third parties.

Active

We might collect information from your active use of the Website when you register to use or use the Website or any assessment or other tool on the Website. We might collect information when you submit a user form or any other request to us. We might collect information when you complete a survey, assessment or upload a resume or any other document. We might collect information from any other active event where you provide such information through the Website.

We might collect information from you that you provide to our personnel or consultants related to the services we provide either inside or outside of this Website, including any verbal communications.

In some circumstances your employer or ex-employer may provide information about you directly to us which we collect.

Passive

We might collect information about you passively from your use of the Website. For instance, we might collect your IP address and we may track the clicks, navigation, browser used and other information related to your use of the Website. In some cases we might collect information from third party social media profiles or plugins you provide or access on the Website. We might collect your geographical location such as when you are using a tool on our Website to find directions to one of our offices.

  • Marketing Communications: We use contact details that you provide us with to send you news and information about products, services and events offered by LHH. We may also send you information about products/services from third parties that we think might be of interest to you.
  • Legal: We use the information we collect to meet any required legal obligations or to protect LHH or other individuals from fraud or illegal activity.
  • Statistics: We use it to collect statistics and information on use of our Website.
