Information We Collect and How We Use It

THG strives to provide consumers with an informative and pleasant experience while using our Websites. Consumer's personal information and comments allow us to customize our services to make your online experience more efficient and enjoyable. This information also provides insight to new product development and services that are relevant to our consumers needs.

Personal Information: THG does not collect any personally identifiable information from you (i.e., name, address, telephone number, e-mail address or credit card number if you place an order with us) unless you voluntarily choose to provide it. We will not share, sell, trade or rent your personally identifiable information to third parties without your prior consent. Your information may be used for research on site usage patterns, new and existing product development, and online site customization. We may use your personal information to contact you regarding your comments or requests for information, to help process an order you have placed through our Website and to deliver information that is targeted to your interests.

Partner Companies: Some THG services are offered in conjunction with a partner company. In order to provide these services to you, it is necessary for us to share information with our partner company. If data is being collected and/or maintained by any company other than our partner companies, you will be notified prior to the time of the data collection or transfer. If you do not want your data to be shared, you can choose not to allow the transfer by not using that particular service.

Aggregate and Statistical Information: We collect non-personal information (such as your web browser, operating system, sites visited, etc.) about our customers, and aggregate this statistical information in order to identify well-liked attributes and tailor the design of our Website to meet the consumers needs. When we say that we may share such information in "aggregate" form, we mean that we will not disclose individual names, addresses, telephone numbers and e-mail addresses unless we have your permission. External companies may be provided aggregate information about our customers. For example, we may let one of our partners know that approximately 10,000 people visited a Webpage regarding a specific joint promotion.

IP Addresses: An Internet Protocol ("IP") address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you are surfing the Web. Web servers automatically identify your computer by its IP address. When visitors request pages from the Website, our servers log the guests' IP addresses. We do not normally link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable, which means that a visitor's session will be logged, but the visitor remains anonymous to us. THG collects IP addresses for the purposes of system administration and to audit the use of this Website. We also use IP addresses to collect statistical information to send to our advertisers and for other marketing and promotional purposes.

Children's Privacy: THG believes that it is especially important to protect children's privacy online and does not direct its Website to children under thirteen (13) years of age. Individuals under eighteen (18) should consult with their parent or guardian before using this Website for their benefit.

Monitoring, Reviewing and Disclosing Information: THG reserves the right at all times to monitor, review, retain, and/or disclose any information as necessary to respond to duly authorized information requests of governmental authorities or where required by applicable law, regulation or legal process.

Advertisements and Other Links: The Websites may provide advertisements to its users. By clicking on an ad, you may be directed to a third party Website that is not controlled by THG, and may have different information gathering practices than THG. Except as otherwise expressly discussed in this Privacy Policy, this policy only addresses THG's use and disclosure of information we collect from you. Some THG Websites may contain links to other Websites to the extent that you disclose personal information to other sites; you are subject to the privacy customs and policies of these other sites. We encourage and recommend that you read the privacy policy of each site you visit and ask questions before you disclose any personal information.

Our Use of Advertising.com Services:  THG sometimes uses the services of Advertising.com which sends advertising in the form of an advertising banner to web surfers that have accessed one of our websites. In order to provide this service, they may use “cookies,” beacons, and other technologies to collect anonymous and non-personally identifiable information which may include your web surfer's IP address, web pages which have been viewed by a web surfer, date and time, domain type, and responses by a web surfer to an advertisement delivered by us.  We use different types of technologies to collect this type of information, including cookies and web beacons. Advertising.com may use the information it obtains to make statistical reports to its customers on matters such as the number of ads delivered by it to computers; to determine how to effectively deliver advertising to web surfers for its customers;  and as required by law, or court or other governmental order.
 
By clicking on this link – http://www.advertising.com/privacy/privacy html – you will be able (a) to view Advertising.com’s Privacy Policy and a detailed explanation of its data collection practices and (b) to opt out of Advertising.com’s data collection practices.

Media & Tracking: THG uses pixels, or transparent GIF files, to help manage online advertising. These GIF files are provided by our ad management partner, DoubleClick. These files enable DoubleClick to recognize a unique cookie on your Web browser, which in turn enables us to learn which advertisements bring users to our website. The cookie was placed by us, or by another advertiser who works with DoubleClick. With both cookies and Spotlight technology, the information that we collect and share is anonymous and not personally identifiable. It does not contain your name, address, telephone number, or email address. For more information about DoubleClick, including information about how to opt out of these technologies, go to [http://www.doubleclick.net/us/corporate/privacy].

Change of Ownership or Control: In the event that THG changes ownership, the information we have collected may be transferred to such party or parties and such individuals or entities will continue to follow this Privacy Policy in the place of THG's current owners.
