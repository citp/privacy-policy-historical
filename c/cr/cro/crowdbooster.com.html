Privacy Policy

When accessing our Website (Crowdbooster.com), Conversely Inc. will learn certain information about you during your visit. How we will handle information we learn about you depends upon what you do when visiting our site.

If you visit our site to read or download information on our pages, we collect and store only the following information about you:

  2. The date and time you access our site
