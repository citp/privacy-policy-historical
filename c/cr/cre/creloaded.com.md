# Ecommerce Software by CRE Loaded - Shopping Cart Products - Pages

We respect your right to privacy. Any information you provide to us at this site (i.e. name, address, e-mail address and telephone number), is maintained in private files on our secure Web server and our internal systems. This information is used to enable Chain Reaction Ecommerce, Inc. to deliver services to you. To ensure compliance with federal law, Chain Reaction Ecommerce, Inc. does not maintain information provided by children under the age of 16. 

Chain Reaction Ecommerce, Inc. will only collect personally identifiable information (such as name, title, company name, address, telephone number, or e-mail address) that you voluntarily provide through our website or e-mail correspondence. We gather general navigational information about where you go on our Web site to help us improve the quality of your online shopping experience. Additional non-personally identifiable information such as domain type, browser version, service provider, and IP address may be collected to provide information regarding your use of our Web site.

Chain Reaction Ecommerce, Inc. believes in providing a safe and secure shopping experience for all of our clients. We provide stringent and effective security measures on our Web site.

 **Cookies**

Chain Reaction Ecommerce, Inc. uses a browser feature known as a cookie, which assigns a unique identification to your computer. Cookies also allow Chain Reaction Ecommerce, Inc. to make our sites more responsive to your needs, by delivering a better and more personalized experience to you. The cookies are typically stored on your computer's hard drive and are used by Chain Reaction Ecommerce, Inc. to track your navigation through the pages within our web site. In addition, Chain Reaction Ecommerce, Inc. uses cookies to help keep track of support requests and to tell us whether you have previously visited a Chain Reaction Ecommerce, Inc. website. This allows registered users to avoid reentering information upon every new visit to our site.
