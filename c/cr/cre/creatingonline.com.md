# PRIVACY POLICY - CreatingOnline.com

| **REASON FOR COLLECTION OF AND USE OF PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION  **  
---  
CreatingOnline may collect personally identifiable information from you only for purposes of email reply, support services, newsletter subscription, registration, feedback, hyperlink inclusion, or product ordering, which information will be freely given to us by you via the use of our site forms.  Some information may also be obtained via cookies only for the purpose of tracking site statistics.  
   
**DISCLOSURE OF PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION  **  
CreatingOnline shall not knowingly disclose to a third party any personally identifiable information pertaining to you obtained through the methods outlined above for any purpose whatsoever without your prior informed written consent.  
   
**ACCESS TO YOUR PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION**  
At your request, CreatingOnline will provide to you the personally identifiable information we currently have on file for you.  You need only  [**_ E-MAIL_**](mailto:webmaster@creatingonline.com) us to request same and provide adequate proof of identity.  CreatingOnline will permit you to verify such information maintained by us, correct any error in such information, or have the information deleted from our records in its entirety.  
   
  
**CREATINGONLINE RESPECTS OUR VISITORS' PRIVACY!**
