COOKIES

"Cookies" are small pieces of information sent by a web server to a web browser, which enables the server to collect information from the browser. creaTTor uses cookies for a number of purposes, for instance to enable us to simplify the logging on process for registered users, to help ensure the authenticity of registered users and to enable traffic monitoring.
Non-registered visitors of the site are sent anonymous cookies to keep track of their browsing patterns and build up a demographic profile.
Whilst you do not need to allow your browser to accept cookies in order to browse much of our web site or to access many of our services, you must have cookies enabled if you wish to access any areas reserved for registered users.
Most browsers allow you to turn off the cookie function. If you want to know how to do this please look at the help menu on your browser. As described above this will restrict the creaTTor services you can use.
