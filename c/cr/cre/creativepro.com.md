# Privacy Policy | CreativePro.com

Privacy Policy for CreativePro.com

Updated as of November, 2007

CreativePro.com is committed to protecting your privacy. We created this Privacy Policy to demonstrate that commitment and to ensure the confidence of visitors to the [www.creativepro.com](http://www.creativepro.com/ "www.creativepro.com") Web site.

All information that you voluntarily submit to us will be electronically delivered to our Web servers located in the State of Montana in the United States. By using creativepro.com you consent to the transfer of this information and to our use of such data as outlined in this policy.

**EMAIL NEWSLETTER SUBSCRIPTION INFORMATION**

If you would like to unsubscribe from a particular CreativePro.com mailing list, simply click on the opt-out link provided in the message you receive from us.

**TYPES OF INFORMATION COLLECTED**

Traffic Data. CreativePro.com automatically recognizes the following non-personally identifiable information when you visit our site: (i) IP address; (ii) domain server; (iii) type of computer; and (iv) type of Web browser (collectively, "Traffic Data"). Traffic Data is anonymous information that does not personally identify you, but is helpful for marketing purposes and for improving your experience on CreativePro.com. We use this information to examine our traffic in aggregate and to ensure the security of the data and our infrastructure. 

We use a third-party advertising server to present or serve the advertisements that you may see on CreativePro.com. These third-party ad servers may use cookies, Web beacons, clear .gifs or similar technologies to help present such advertisements, and to help measure and research the advertisements' effectiveness. The use of these technologies by this third-party ad server is subject to its own privacy policies and is not covered by our privacy statement.

We partner with third-party vendors, including, but not limited to, Amazon.com, MediaBakery, and Portfolios.com. These vendors are subject to their own privacy policies and are not covered by our privacy statement.

Personal Information Collected. To fully serve you and fully access certain services that we offer via CreativePro.com, we require you to provide us with information that personally identifies you ("Personal Information"). For example, we require accurate Personal Information to, among other things, sign up for our email newsletter(s), create a profile, submit a forum posting, or send us your feedback. Personal Information may include (i) Contact Data (such as your name, job title, occupation, e-mail addresses); (ii) Demographic Data (such as your zip code, age and interests). If you communicate with us by e-mail, post messages to any of our forum areas, or otherwise complete online forms or surveys, any information provided in such communication may be collected as Personal Information. Our use of this Personal Information is set forth below. SUBMITTING PERSONAL INFORMATION IS YOUR CHOICE. You may choose not to provide us with any Personal Information. In such an event, you can still access and use much of CreativePro.com; however. you will not be able to access and use those portions of CreativePro.com that require your Personal Information.

**USE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION COLLECTED**

The Personal Information you enter on CreativePro.com is used to respond or contact you when necessary and to provide our visitors with a better Internet experience. When you register with CreativePro.com, or subscribe to our newsletters, or participate in our community forums, we collect certain Personal Information from you, including your name and email address. From time to time, we aggregate Personal Information entered into CreativePro.com to identify our audience's demographics (collective characteristics/behavior) and to measure overall interest in specific areas of CreativePro.com. By aggregating the Personal Information into a form whereby we can no longer personally identify you, we are allowed to analyze how and where to best use our resources without impinging on your privacy. CreativePro.com may share such aggregated data with third parties and sales prospects, and we may use this information for promotional purposes. Also, CreativePro.com occasionally makes contact information available to select outside companies whose products or services we feel may be of interest to you. Except as otherwise provided in this Privacy Policy, we will keep your Personal Information private unless such disclosure is necessary for one or more of the following:

a. Comply with the law, a court order or legal process;  
b. Protect our rights or property; or  
c. Enforce our Terms of Use.

**SECURITY OF PERSONAL INFORMATION**

Your Personal Information is stored on secure Web servers that are not accessible by third parties. However, while we take efforts to ensure the integrity and security of our network and systems, we cannot absolutely guarantee that our security measures will prevent third-party "hackers" or other malfeasants from illegally obtaining this information.

**OTHER LIMITS TO YOUR PRIVACY**

CreativePro.com contains links to other Web sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. We also [word missing] forums, polls and RSS services available to you. Please understand that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information. We have no control over its use and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your Personal Information.

**RIGHT TO CHANGE PRIVACY POLICY**

We reserve the right, at any time and without notice, to add to, change, update or modify this Privacy Policy, simply by posting such change, update or modification on CreativePro.com. Any such change, update or modification will be effective immediately upon posting on CreativePro.com.

**CHILDREN'S PRIVACY**

CreativePro.com is not intended for children under the age of 18. If you are under 18, you must ask a parent for permission prior to submitting any information to CreativePro.com.

**CONTACT FOR PRIVACY INQUIRIES**

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us _privacypolicy@creativepro.com_.
