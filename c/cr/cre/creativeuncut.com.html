Privacy Policy

Visitor privacy is taken seriously here. This notice is provided to you from the home page and all other major navigational pages, to help you understand exactly how your privacy is treated when visiting the CreativeUncut.com domain and all of its subdomains.

Personal Information
 This website uses third-party advertising companies to serve ads to visitors. These companies may use information from visitors to this and other websites to provide advertisements about related goods and services. This information does not include your name, address, email address, or telephone number.

Message Boards
 Visitors wishing to use the Creative Uncut forums will be asked to provide their email to sign up. This is used to confirm your registration and will never be shared with a third party. Visitors will have the option of filling in additional personal details in the submission form. Please be aware that these details are for your public forum profile and as such will be viewable by the public. Caution should be taken when releasing any other personal details via the message boards.

Fan Art Submissions
 Creative Uncut hosts fan artwork submitted by visitors. These artists provide the username of their choice along with their fan art to be posted on Creative Uncut. By default, an artist's email address and real name will not be listed. However, if you express desire to have this information publicly listed, we will do so at your request. Should you request to have your email or real name listed, but later change your mind, we will remove it at your request. When submitting a fan art, your email and name will not be shared with a third party.

Cookies & Advertisers
 A cookie is a data file stored on a user's computer that holds information about the user. In the course of serving ads to this website, some of our third party business partners, such as advertisers, may be placing and reading cookies on users' browsers, or using web beacons to collect information. We have no control over these cookies, but we do actively strive to affiliate with trusted advertisers. Your browser should have user options for managing cookies, such as blocking all cookies, blocking cookies from specific sites, or controlling how long they are saved on your computer. Generally, these cookie options can be found under a privacy tab in your browser options.

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this website. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to visitors based on their visit to this website and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

We have contracted with ValueClick to monitor certain pages of this website for the purpose of reporting website traffic, statistics, advertisement 'click-throughs', and/or other activities on our website. Where authorized by us, ValueClick may use cookies, web beacons, and/or other monitoring technologies to compile anonymous statistics about our website visitors. No personally identifiable information is collected by or transferred to any party other than the advertiser. For more information about how the information is collected and used by ValueClick, please see ValueClick's privacy policy at: http://www.valueclickmedia.com/member_privacy.shtml

Links
 Creative Uncut contains links to other websites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy terms of such linked websites.

Changes to This Policy
 At times, there may be changes made to this privacy policy to enhance the accuracy of it or to reflect updates to the terms. Whenever this occurs, the changes will be made directly to this page, and will always be available from the home page.

Questions or Concerns
 If you have any further questions or related inquiries regarding this privacy policy, feel free to present them via the contact information on the info page.
