# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy 

This policy covers how we use your personal information.

We take your privacy seriously and will take all measures to protect your personal information.

Any personal information received will only be used to fill your order and for record keeping on products purchased. We do not store any credit card information.

If you opted to sign on for the newsletter, you will only receive news relevant to books and products sold on this website.

We will not sell or redistribute your information to anyone.
