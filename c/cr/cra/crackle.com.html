    What information we collect and why

    • You may use many of Crackle’s features anonymously, but we may use cookies and other technologies to keep track of your interactions with the Service and to highlight content that may be of particular interest to you based on past visits to the Service.
    • This information may be combined with information obtained from Crackle’s affiliates and other companies.

    • We use the information collected to provide you the services and products you request and to provide you more relevant content and ads, including information about Crackle sweepstakes and promotions.

    Information We Collect and How We use it

    We collect information in two ways: (i) when you provide information about yourself and (ii) automatically when you use the Service, for various purposes related to providing you the Services and products as described below.

    Information You Choose To Submit

    (a) Registering for a Crackle Account. When you register to become a member of the Service, we collect your user name, email address, gender, and date of birth (collectively "Personal Information"). You may also create a profile that includes a photo and a brief message about yourself.

    We use your Personal Information to notify you of new product releases, to send service notifications, to customize the content and advertising you see, to fulfill your requests for products and services, to improve our services, to conduct research, and to solicit your feedback and input about the Service. In addition, if you have opted into receiving newsletters or other communications from us, we use your Personal Information for that purpose.

    (b) Entering Contests and Sweepstakes. For contests, sweepstakes and other promotions conducted on or through the Service, we use the Personal Information and other personal information you choose to provide to communicate with you regarding such contest, sweepstakes or other promotion and you are deemed to consent to us sharing such Personal Information with our contest, sweepstakes or promotional partners for the purpose of administering awards.

    (c) Sharing with Third Parties. Other than as set forth in this Privacy Policy, we will not share your Personal Information with third parties unless you have given us permission to do so.

    Information We Automatically Collect

    (a) Usage Data. We automatically collect certain information to help us understand how our users use the Service (which we will refer to in this Privacy Policy collectively as "Usage Data"). For example, each time you use the Service we may collect your IP address or other unique identifier for the computer, mobile device, technology or other device, in part because some content is restricted to certain locations.

    (b) Cookies. In addition, we use a technology called "cookies." A cookie is a piece of data that identifies you as a unique user. We use cookies to improve the quality of our Service and make the Service easier to use. We do this by storing information (for example, settings preferences) about a particular user session in cookies in order to provide you with a seamless experience during a particular session. Certain of our third-party Providers (as defined in the next paragraph) may use cookies in connection with the services they perform on our behalf. If you choose to do so, you can configure your browser to delete existing cookies or disable cookies altogether. However, certain features of the Service may not work if you delete or disable cookies.

    (c) Using this Automatically Collected Information for Service Performance and Advertising. Advertising allows us to provide you the rich content available on Crackle for free. Some of our ads are based on the content of pages you are viewing elsewhere on the internet (i.e. online behavioral advertising). We believe that such tailored ads are more relevant to you and, therefore, more desirable.

    We also use automatically collected information for Service performance, data management and to display the appropriate content available in your country.

    We may contract with companies or persons to provide certain services including data management, promotional and advertising services ("Providers"). We give our Providers the information needed for them to perform these services. We also ask our Providers to abide by this Privacy Policy with respect to Usage Data or other data we share with them.

    Certain Providers of advertising services may use Usage Data (together with any information they collect directly from you via cookies and any information that you submit directly to them) to (a) improve, enhance and implement the sale, display and targeting of advertising on the Service and (b) combine such Usage Data with user data from their other customers to compile aggregate statistics, metrics and general trend data.

    You can opt out of the collection of data by certain Providers for online behavioral advertising at the Network Advertising Initiative’s opt-out page.. For more information and choices about interest-based ads, you may also visit the website for the Self-Regulatory Principles for Online Behavioral Advertising.. Crackle adheres to the Self Regulatory principles posted on this Service.

For more information about the measurement software and to learn about your choices with regard to Nielsen’s measurement, you may visit Nielsen’s Digital Measurement Privacy Policy.
