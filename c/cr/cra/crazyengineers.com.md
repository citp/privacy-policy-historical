# CrazyEngineers Forum - Privacy Policy

[CrazyEngineers.com](http://www.crazyengineers.com/) is committed to securing your privacy online. This privacy policy sets out how we use and protect information that you give to us while using this website. We may ask you certain information that will only be used as mentioned in this policy. We may change this policy and you are requested to check this page timely to ensure that you are aware of the changes made to this policy. 

This Site contains links to other websites. These include links to websites that we think may be of interest to you. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.   
**Using this website:**

If you browse through this website without entering any personal data or creating an account, we will gather and store certain data about your visit. This data cannot identify you personally and cannot be linked back to you unless you choose to identify yourself. We have deployed Google Analytics software on this site to monitor site traffic. The site complies with the policies set by Google Analytics: http://www.google.com/intl/en/privacy/ 

**Third Party Advertising**

We use third party advertisement programs (Such as Google Adsense, OpenX) on CrazyEngineers.com to support our site. These advertisements may use technology such as cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site. For example Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. 

Users may opt out of the use of DART cookie by visiting [advertising opt-out page](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).

These advertisement programs may collect information like your IP address, your ISP, Web browser and Operating System you used during your visit to site and whether you have flash installed. This information is generally used for ad-targeting purpose. That is, to display content, interest or geography relevant advertisements to you while you are visiting the site. 

**We collect:-**

· Your Contact Email   
· Engineering discipline of your interest  
· Your IP address  
· Your interests and short biography

**How we use it:-**

· To keep record of registrations on CE  
· To improve our website and offerings to you  
· We occasionally send informative bulletin to those who have opted to receive emails from administrator/site   
· To analyse traffic trends 

**Security of your information:-**

We are committed to keeping your information secure. We have employed software, electronic and managerial procedures to help protect against loss, misuse and alteration of information under our control. 

**How We Use Cookies:**

We may place “cookies” on your computer. “Cookies” are small identifiers sent from a web server and stored on your computer’s hard disk. We also use cookies to find out how you found this site. Cookies are not designed to collect data from your hard disk, your email or any other personal data about you. To protect your privacy, we do not use cookies to store or transmit any personal data about you over the Internet. You can disable cookies by changing your browser settings. However, please be informed that if you disable cookies the site may not serve pages properly. It may also result in you having to enter your personal information more than once. 

You may contact us for any questions related to practises of the site, privacy policy and your dealings with the site via following email address: contact [at] crazyengineers [dot] com.
