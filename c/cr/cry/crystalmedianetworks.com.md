# PRIVACY POLICY

	  
                                                                PRIVACY	  POLICY	  
Your	  privacy	  is	  important	  to	  us.	  	  Our	  privacy	  policy	  (“Privacy	  Policy”)	  covers	  how	  we	  collect,	  use,	  
disclose,	  transfer,	  and	  store	  any	  your	  specific	  information	  that	  we	  may	  collect.	  	  If	  you	  have	  any	  
questions,	  please	  let	  us	  know	  at	  support@crystalmedianetworks.com.	  
	  
Collection	  and	  Use	  of	  Personal	  Information	  
	  
Personal	  information	  is	  data	  that	  may	  be	  used	  to	  uniquely	  identify	  or	  contact	  a	  single	  person.	  
You	  may	  be	  asked	  to	  provide	  your	  personal	  information	  to	  us	  and	  we	  may	  use	  this	  information	  
to	  improve	  our	  products,	  services,	  content,	  and	  advertising.	  
	  
Here	  are	  some	  examples	  of	  the	  types	  of	  personal	  information	  We	  may	  collect	  and	  how	  we	  may	  
use	  it.	  
	  
How	  we	  may	  use	  your	  personal	  information	  
	  
▪ The	  personal	  information	  we	  collect	  may	  allow	  us	  to	  keep	  you	  posted	  on	  our	  company’s	  
       news,	  certain	  product	  announcements,	  software	  updates,	  and	  upcoming	  events.	  It	  also	  helps	  
       us	  to	  improve	  our	  services,	  content,	  and	  advertising.	  If	  we	  do	  send	  you	  information,	  you	  will	  
       be	  able	  to	  opt	  out	  of	  this	  information	  at	  anytime.	  	  
       	  
▪ The	  personal	  information	  we	  collect	  may	  allow	  us	  to	  send	  important	  notices,	  such	  as	  
       communications	  about	  product	  changes,	  new	  or	  modified	  terms,	  conditions,	  and	  policies.	  	  
       	  
▪ We	  may	  also	  use	  personal	  information	  for	  internal	  purposes	  such	  as	  data	  analysis	  and	  
       research	  to	  help	  us	  improve	  our	  products,	  services,	  and	  customer	  communications.	  
       	  
▪ If	  you	  enter	  into	  a	  sweepstake,	  contest,	  or	  similar	  promotion	  –	  whether	  through	  our	  websites	  
       or	  applications,	  through	  our	  partners	  websites	  or	  applications,	  	  or	  through	  any	  mobile	  
       campaign,	  we	  or	  our	  partners	  may	  use	  the	  information	  you	  provide	  in	  order	  to	  administer	  
       those	  programs.	  
       	  
	  
Collection	  and	  Use	  of	  Non-­‐Personal	  Information	  
	  
We	  also	  collect	  information	  that	  is	  not	  personal	  or	  data	  that	  cannot	  be	  associated	  with	  a	  specific	  
individual.	  	  We	  may	  collect,	  use,	  transfer,	  and	  disclose	  non-­‐personal	  information	  for	  any	  
purpose.	  	  
	  
The	  following	  are	  some	  examples	  of	  non-­‐personal	  information	  that	  we	  collect	  and	  how	  we	  may	  
use	  it:	  
	  
▪ We	  may	  collect	  certain	  non-­‐personal	  information	  such	  as	  zip	  code,	  area	  code,	  unique	  device	  
     identifier,	  location,	  and	  the	  time	  zone	  where	  any	  of	  our	  products	  are	  being	  used	  so	  that	  we	  


      may	  better	  understand	  customer	  behavior	  and	  improve	  our	  products,	  services,	  and	  
      advertising.	  
      	  
▪ We	  also	  may	  collect	  information	  regarding	  activities	  on	  our	  website	  and	  mobile	  application	  
      and	  mobile	  browser	  services.	  	  Typically,	  this	  information	  is	  aggregated	  and	  used	  to	  help	  us	  
      provide	  more	  useful	  information	  to	  our	  customers	  and	  to	  understand	  which	  parts	  of	  our	  
      website,	  products,	  and	  services	  are	  of	  most	  interest.	  	  Aggregated	  data	  is	  considered	  non-­‐
      personal	  information	  for	  the	  purposes	  of	  this	  Privacy	  Policy.	  
	  
Please	  note:	  If	  we	  do	  combine	  non-­‐personal	  information	  with	  personal	  information	  the	  
combined	  information	  will	  be	  treated	  as	  personal	  information	  for	  as	  long	  as	  it	  remains	  
combined.	  
	  
	  
Cookies	  and	  Other	  Technologies	  
	  
Our	  website,	  services,	  applications,	  email	  messages,	  and	  advertisements	  may	  use	  “cookies”	  or	  
other	  technologies	  such	  as	  pixel	  tags	  and	  web	  beacons.	  These	  technologies	  help	  us	  better	  
understand	  user	  behavior,	  tell	  us	  which	  parts	  of	  our	  website	  people	  have	  visited,	  and	  facilitate	  
and	  measure	  the	  effectiveness	  of	  advertisements	  and	  web	  searches.	  We	  treat	  information	  
collected	  by	  cookies	  and	  other	  technologies	  as	  non-­‐personal	  information.	  However,	  to	  the	  
extent	  that	  Internet	  Protocol	  (IP)	  addresses	  or	  similar	  identifiers	  are	  considered	  personal	  
information	  by	  local	  law,	  we	  also	  treat	  these	  identifiers	  as	  personal	  information.	  Similarly,	  to	  the	  
extent	  that	  non-­‐personal	  information	  is	  combined	  with	  personal	  information,	  we	  treat	  the	  
combined	  information	  as	  personal	  information	  for	  the	  purposes	  of	  this	  Privacy	  Policy.	  
	  
We	  may	  gather	  non-­‐personal	  information	  automatically	  and	  store	  it	  in	  log	  files.	  	  This	  information	  
includes	  Internet	  Protocol	  (IP)	  addresses,	  browser	  type	  and	  language,	  Internet	  service	  provider	  
(ISP),	  referring	  and	  exit	  pages,	  operating	  system,	  date/time	  stamp,	  and	  other	  data	  that	  allows	  to	  
understand	  what	  you	  may	  like	  on	  our	  websites,	  applications,	  or	  devices.	  
	  
We	  also	  use	  email	  messages	  that	  contain	  “click-­‐through”	  links	  that	  may	  take	  you	  to	  certain	  
content	  on	  our	  websites.	  	  We	  may	  track	  this	  click-­‐through	  action	  to	  help	  us	  determine	  interest	  
in	  particular	  topics	  and	  measure	  the	  effectiveness	  of	  our	  email	  messages.	  	  If	  you	  prefer	  not	  to	  be	  
tracked	  in	  this	  way,	  you	  should	  not	  click	  text	  or	  graphic	  links	  in	  the	  email	  messages.	  	  	  
	  
We	  or	  our	  partners	  may	  use	  cookies	  and	  other	  technologies	  in	  advertising	  services	  to	  control	  the	  
number	  of	  times	  you	  see	  a	  given	  ad,	  deliver	  ads	  that	  relate	  to	  your	  interests,	  and	  measure	  the	  
effectiveness	  of	  ad	  campaigns.	  	  
	  
We	  and	  our	  partners	  may	  also	  use	  cookies	  and	  other	  technologies	  to	  remember	  personal	  
information	  when	  you	  use	  our	  website,	  online	  services,	  and	  applications.	  Our	  goal	  in	  these	  cases	  
is	  to	  make	  your	  experience	  more	  convenient	  and	  personal.	  	  
	  
If	  you	  would	  like	  to	  disable	  cookies,	  please	  refer	  to	  the	  specific	  web	  or	  mobile-­‐browser	  your	  are	  
using	  to	  find	  out	  how	  to	  disable	  cookies.	  
	  
	  


Disclosure	  to	  Third	  Parties	  
	  
We	  may	  make	  certain	  personal	  information	  available	  to	  strategic	  partners	  that	  work	  with	  us.	  
Personal	  information	  may	  be	  shared	  in	  order	  to	  provide	  or	  improve	  our	  products,	  services	  and	  
advertising	  or	  our	  partners	  products,	  services,	  and	  advertising.	  	  For	  example,	  if	  you	  enter	  a	  
contest	  or	  opt-­‐in	  content	  delivery	  with	  one	  of	  our	  partners	  –	  via	  mobile	  devices,	  website,	  or	  
application	  –	  we	  and	  our	  partner	  may	  use	  and	  maintain	  the	  personal	  information	  you	  provide	  in	  
order	  to	  deliver	  the	  content	  or	  execute	  upon	  the	  contest.	  	  Please	  note:	  this	  information	  may	  be	  
kept	  by	  us	  and	  our	  partners	  after	  the	  contest	  or	  opt-­‐in	  content	  and	  after	  you	  opt-­‐out	  of	  such	  
contest	  or	  content	  delivery	  but	  this	  information	  will	  not	  be	  used	  by	  us	  other	  than	  for	  the	  
analysis	  of	  data	  or	  other	  requirements	  imposed	  upon	  us.

information.	  
	  
	  
Others

governmental	  authorities	  within	  or	  outside	  your	  country	  of	  residence	  −	  for	  us	  to	  disclose	  your

is	  necessary	  or	  appropriate.

information	  we	  collect	  to	  the	  relevant	  third	  party.	  
	  
	  
Protection	  of	  Personal	  Information	  
	  
We	  take	  certain	  precautions	  to	  safeguard	  your	  personal	  information	  against	  loss,	  theft,	  and	  
misuse,	  as	  well	  as	  against	  unauthorized	  access,	  disclosure,	  alteration,	  and	  destruction.	  
	  
Please	  note:	  You	  are	  responsible	  for	  the	  personal	  information	  you	  choose	  to	  submit	  in	  any	  
public	  forum.	  	  For	  example,	  if	  you	  list	  your	  name	  and	  email	  address	  in	  a	  forum	  posting,	  that	  
information	  is	  public.	  	  
	  
	  
	  
	  


Accuracy	  of	  Personal	  Information	  
	  
As	  a	  user	  of	  any	  of	  our	  products,	  you	  may	  be	  responsible	  for	  certain	  personal	  information	  that	  
you	  provide	  to	  us.	  	  In	  these	  cases,	  we	  will	  make	  it	  easy	  for	  you	  to	  ensure	  that	  your	  personal	  
information	  is	  accurate	  and	  up	  to	  date.	  	  We	  will	  retain	  your	  personal	  information	  for	  the	  period	  
necessary	  to	  fulfill	  the	  purposes	  outlined	  in	  this	  Privacy	  Policy	  unless	  a	  longer	  retention	  period	  is	  
required	  or	  permitted	  by	  law.	  
	  
	  
Children

have	  collected	  the	  personal	  information	  of	  a	  child	  under	  13	  we	  will	  take	  steps	  to	  delete	  the	  
information	  as	  soon	  as	  possible.

products,	  and	  services.	  	  For	  example,	  a	  user	  may	  be	  taken	  to	  an	  advertisers	  website	  or

advertiser	  or	  other	  website	  or	  application.	  	  We	  encourage	  you	  to	  learn	  about	  the	  privacy

Department	  of	  Commerce	  regarding	  the	  collection,	  use,	  and	  retention	  of	  personal	  information
