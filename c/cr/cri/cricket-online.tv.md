# Privacy | Cricket Online TV

Keeping in mind the importance of privacy of personal information of visitors to this site, the following details the kinds of information that is collected as part of the process of serving Advertisements on this site, and how this information is safeguarded. By visiting Cricket Online TV, you are accepting the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy covers Cricket Online TV’s treatment of personal information that Cricket Online TV gathers when you are on the Cricket Online TV website and when you use Cricket Online TV services. This policy does not apply to the practices of third parties that Cricket Online TV does not own or control, or to individuals that Cricket Online TV does not employ or manage. 

Information collected by Cricket Online TV:  
Cricket Online TV only collects personal information that is relevant to the purpose of the website. This information allows Cricket Online TV to provide you with a customized and efficient experience. Cricket Online TV does not process this information in a way that is incompatible with this objective. Cricket Online TV collects the following types of information from its users: 

1\. Information You provide through the comments section: The information provided is entirely up to you and you may choose not to provide certain information. 

2\. Log Files:  
As is the norm with most websites, data located in log files is collected and used by Cricket Online TV and its authorized agents. The information in the log files includes a visitor’s IP address, ISP, browser used at the time of visit to the site, the time you visited and the distribution of pages navigated on this site. Cricket Online TV uses this traffic data to help diagnose problems with its servers, analyze trends and administer the website.  
Many companies offer programs that help you to visit websites anonymously. While Cricket Online TV will not be able to provide you with a personalized experience if you cannot be recognized, Cricket Online TV wants you to be aware that these programs are available.

3\. E-mail Communications  
Cricket Online TV is very concerned about your privacy and will never provide your email address to a third party without your explicit permission, as detailed in the “Sharing Your Information” section below. You will receive an email from Cricket Online TV only if you have specifically subscribed to email reminders about new postings on the site.

3\. Cookies:  
Third party advertisements are made use of on this site to support maintenance of the site. The advertisers use technology including cookies as part of providing advertisements on this site, which send the third-party vendor anonymous non-personally identifiable information including your IP address, ISP, browser used at the time of visit to this site, and in some cases, info on whether you have Flash installed. Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that are transferred to your computer’s hard drive through your Web browser that provide information which is used for geo-targeting purposes in order to display advertisements appropriate to your location and your browsing-patterns while visiting this site. Cricket Online TV cookies do not collect personal information, and Cricket Online TV does not combine information collected through cookies with other personal information to find out who you are or what your screen name or e-mail address is.  
You may choose to disable or selectively turn off cookies or third-party cookies through the settings provided in your browser, or by controlling the preferences in programs like Norton Internet Security. However, this may affect some of your interaction with this site as well as other websites.  
Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet. You can specifically opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting **[this page](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)**.  
You can also visit Platform-A’s privacy policy and opt-out page.

**Please Note** : Neither do these ad-serving technologies in any way hinder or cause you any problems while using the internet to visit this or other sites nor do they spy on you in any way to collect information other than those mentioned. Their sole purpose is to display ads most appropriate to you based on your location.

Sharing Your Information  
Rest assured that Cricket Online TV neither rents nor sells your personal information to anyone and that Cricket Online TV will share your personal information only as described below.  
Cricket Online TV Personnel: Cricket Online TV personnel and authorized consultants and/or contractors may have access to user information if necessary in the normal course of Cricket Online TV business.  
Business Transfers: In some cases, Cricket Online TV may choose to buy or sell assets. In these types of transactions, user information is typically one of the business assets that is transferred. Moreover, if Cricket Online TV, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred.  
Protection of Cricket Online TV and Others: We may release personal information when we believe in good faith that release is necessary to comply with a law; to enforce or apply our Terms of Use and other policies; or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Cricket Online TV, its employees, users, or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction.  
Syndication: Cricket Online TV allows for the RSS syndication of all of its public content within the Cricket Online TV website.  
With Your Consent: Except as noted above, Cricket Online TV will contact you when your personal information is shared with third parties or used for a purpose incompatible with the purpose(s) for which it was originally collected, and you will be able to opt out to prevent the sharing of this information.

Children Under 18 Years of Age  
Cricket Online TV does not specifically collect information about children. If it is learned that Cricket Online TV has collected information from a child under the age of 13, this information will be deleted as quickly as possible. It is recommend that minors between the ages of 13 and 18 ask and receive their parents’ permission before using Cricket Online TV or sending information about themselves or anyone else over the Internet.

Changes to this Privacy Policy  
Cricket Online TV may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time, at its sole discretion. Use of information Cricket Online TV collects now is subject to the Privacy Policy in effect at the time such information is used. If Cricket Online TV makes changes to the Privacy Policy, you will be notified through an announcement on the website so you are always aware of what information is collected, how it is used, and under what circumstances if any, it is disclosed.

Conditions of Use  
If you decide to visit the Cricket Online TV website, your visit and any possible dispute over privacy is subject to this Privacy Policy and Cricket Online TV’s Terms of Use, including limitations on damages, arbitration of disputes, and application of California state law.

Effective Date of this Privacy Policy  
This Privacy Policy is effective as of December 10, 2008 and last updated August 11, 2010.
