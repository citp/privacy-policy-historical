Privacy Policy

Cricfeeds Team recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of information we may collect from our visitors. This privacy policy discloses how we collect, protect, use and share information gathered about you on our Web Site. We hope that this disclosure will help increase your confidence in this web site. Privacy issues are a high priority for us at Cricfeeds and we follow standard industry practices to maintain your personal information. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.
Cricfeeds does not spam or sell/lease its user information to third parties.

 
 
 
Information Collection and Use

We respect each visitor’s right to personal privacy. To that end, we collect and use information throughout our web site only as disclosed in this Privacy Policy. This statement applies solely to the information collected on Cricfeeds.com. Cricfeeds.com collects personal information when you register with the web site. This information consists of just your name and email address. The information we collect is:

  • Used to notify visitors about events, changes and other information about this web site.
  • Never shared with other organizations for commercial or other purposes.

 
 
 
Disclosure of Information to Third Parties

Cricfeeds.com will NOT disclose any personal information except in the following cases,

  • Protect and defend the rights of property of Cricfeeds.com.

 
 
 
Link to Other Sites :

As a means of providing useful resources to our users, Cricfeeds.com provides links to other web sites. We try to carefully choose web sites that we believe are useful and meet our high standards. However, because web sites can change so quickly, we do not guarantee the standards of any website links we provide nor do we imply endorsement. Cricfeeds.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. Cricfeeds.com does not share any of the individual personal information that you provide with the sites to which Cricfeeds.com links, although we may share traffic data with such web sites such as how many people visit our site.

 
 
 
Notification of Changes

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this Privacy Policy.
