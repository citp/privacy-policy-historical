# Crimereports.com Privacy Policy

  
The following FAQ for Crimereports.com should answer most of your important questions. However, you are welcome to send email to [feedback@crimereports.com](mailto:feedback@crimereports.com) if you have additional questions.

To facilitate delivery of reports, Crimereports.com has developed a variety of systems to collect and store customer information. 

In addition to improving your online experience, knowing a bit more about you allows _us_ to display advertising to you in a more intelligent fashion.  Targeted advertisements are probably mo re interesting for you, and they are definitely more productive for our advertisers.

Still, we understand how important your privacy is to you, and we hope that the following answers will reassure you that the information you have given us will not be misused.

**Does Crimereports.com ask for personal information?**   
No. We only ask for your e-mail address.

 **What other information does Crimereports.com collect?**   
In order to determine what the most popular areas of our site are, Crimereports.com also keeps track of on-site traffic, i.e. page views and ad impressions.

**How does Crimereports.com use this information?**   
Crimereports.com uses customer information in non-intrusive, common sense ways, sich as providing you with local links to advertisers and other references that may be of interest to you. We also collect customer information in aggregate to better und erstand our audience. Finally, tracking customer information allows us to sell targeted advertising. While we may not always get it right, we think that you will be more interested in banner ads that have something to do with your news interests and/or home Zip Code. Targeted ads are also far more efficient for advertisers. This is especiall y good because advertising dollars pay the bills and allow us to provide crime reports to you for free and without spending tax dollars.

**How does Crimereports.com target advertising?**   
We generate advertising sales based on geographic region and for companies that are interested in crime and safety products.

 **Will Crimereports.com disclose any of my personal information?**   
Crimereports.com will not disclose personal information under any circumstances. If Crimereports.com plans to disclose information, the purpose will be explained to you beforehand and we will request your permission.

However, Crimereports.com does disclose aggregated user statistics to describe our services to prospective partners, advertisers, and other third parties, and for other lawful purposes. However, aggregate data is not traceable to a specific user.

It is important to note that although Crimereports.com will use all reasonable efforts to safeguard the privacy of your personal information, transmissions made by means of the Internet cannot be made absolutely secure. Crimereports.com will have no liabi lity for disclosure of data due to errors in transmission or unauthorized acts of third parties.  
Finally, Crimereports.com will cooperate fully should a situation arise where we are required by law or legal process to provide information about a customer.

**Will Crimereports.com use my information for direct mailings?**
