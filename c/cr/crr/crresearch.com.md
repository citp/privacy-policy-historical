C + R RESEARCH PRIVACY POLICY - YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS  
**Who we are** | This Policy describes **C+R Research, Inc.** ’s information collection practices relating to the websites at which this policy is located, including www.crresearch.com, www.netcrimson.com, www.KidzEyes.com, www.teeneyes.com, www.latinoeyes.com, www.parentspeak.com, www.simplyfreshinsights.com (collectively, the "Sites"). C+R Research is located at 500 N. Michigan Ave, Chicago IL 60611. This policy does not cover information collected offline or elsewhere by C+R Research.  
**What we collect** | Please read this Privacy Policy carefully, it describes how we use personally identifiable information about you (information such as email addresses, names, physical addresses, and phone numbers that could be used to uniquely identify you as an individual). Information we collect also may include background information about you or members of your household, demographic information, information about your opinions and preferences, and other non-identifiable information we might collect.

To learn more about us and our panels and programs, please visit www.crresearch.com

By providing us with information and by using the Sites, you consent to have your information used as described in this Privacy Policy. Your use of the Sites indicates to us that you have read and accept our privacy practices, as outlined in this Privacy Policy.  
  
**How is my information collected?** |  **We collect information directly from you** \- for example, when you register for a panel, participate in a survey, fill out a form requesting information from us, or give us access to information you post to a social networking site, such as Facebook. 

**We collect information from you passively** \- we use common tracking techniques like traditional cookies, Flash cookies, and web beacons. This policy describes how and when we use these technologies. 

**Third parties we work with may also give us information about you** \- for example, our clients, on whose behalf we conduct research projects of various kinds.  
  
**What do we do with information?** | We use the information we collect: 

  1. To understand the use of the Sites and make improvements, including system administration or site usage auditing;
  2. To protect the security or integrity of the Sites if necessary;
  3. To administer our surveys and provide research services to our clients;
  4. To design new products or services, to improve existing products, and to developing advertising programs;
  5. To determine technology used by your browser and for survey administration, including through use of the tracking tools as described below;
  6. As otherwise disclosed, including as may be disclosed at the time we collect your information;
  7. To contact you regarding any problems or questions we have relating to your use of the Sites, or, in our discretion, notify you of changes to our Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, or other policy or terms that affect you and your use of the Sites.
  8. To respond to your requests;
  9. To register visitors for online activities such as online ordering, webinars, sweepstakes, contests, surveys, employment applications, comment forms, or any other online or mobile interactive activities and for related purposes, such as survey incentive fulfillment;
  10. To send information that we believe you may be interested in, such as information about upcoming panels, surveys or other of our services, including by email, postal mail, phone, or text message; and
  11. To send your friends messages at your request using refer-a-friend features.

**Our tracking activities and your choices** | We - or third parties - use browser cookies, web beacons, and Flash cookies. We use these methods to limit multiple responses and registrations, for market research analysis, to facilitate your ability to navigate the Sites, and as part of a verification or screening process. Information collected may be linked to your personal information, and may be shared with third parties for the reasons described in this policy. 

You can control browser cookies by modifying your settings in your browser. You can control Flash cookies (these are similar to traditional cookies, but reside in Adobe Flash Player rather than your browser and thus will not be affected by any browser setting changes you make) by going to http://phtshp.us/edMmSQ and following the instructions on that page. 

Some parts of the Sites may not operate correctly if you disable cookies, and you may not be able to participate in projects or join panels, or take advantage of some of the Sites’ features.  
  
**Security** | To help prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and help ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place what we believe to be appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect. 

We cannot guarantee, however, that the information we collect will remain secure. The Internet by its nature is a public medium, and we encourage you to use caution when disclosing information online.  
  
**Your Rights for California Residents** | If you have an established business relationship with us you may request from us a list of the categories of personal information we have disclosed to third parties for those third parties’ marketing purposes during the previous calendar year, and a list of those third parties. We will include in that list the names and addresses of the third parties who received the information and used it (or who we believe may have used it) for their own marketing purposes in the last calendar year. 

To exercise your rights, you may make one request each year by emailing us at [privacy@crresearch.com](mailto:privacy@crresearch.com?subject=%27Shine%20the%20Light%27%20inquiry) or writing us at the address listed in the "Contact Us" section below. Indicate in your letter that you are a California resident making a "Shine the Light" inquiry. Responses will be provided within 30 days.  
  
**Non-US Users** | The Sites are operated in the United States. If you are located outside of the United States, please be aware that any information you provide to us will be transferred to the United States and that by using the Sites/giving your information, you consent to such transfer, and understand that the U.S. may not provide the same level of protections as the laws of your country.   
**Links to Third Party Sites** | Please be aware that when you are on this Web Site or viewing an e-mail from us, you could be directed to other web pages or online content through links that take you outside our service and are beyond our control. These other web sites may set their own cookies, collect data, and/or have their own privacy policies. This Privacy Policy only covers information collected by us on the Sites, and we encourage you to review the privacy policy of any other web site you visit.  
**Modifications to this policy** | We reserve the right to modify, alter, or otherwise update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will post any changes here, so be sure to check back from time to time.
