Cirkled inTM Privacy Policy


At CirkeldIn, your privacy is very high on our priority. Please review the below privacy policy and reach out to us at help@cirkledin.com with any questions.

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Cirkled inTM (Application) on any platform: website, mobile, tablet or any other device or applications. Cirkled inTM provides a digital platform for you to capture information that you upload about your or your kids' life achievements while connecting and collaborating with like-mined parents and teachers.


What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information:

The Application obtains the information you provide when you download, register or use the Application. This includes an email address, your name as well as the name of your children for whom profiles are being created. In addition, all the data that you enter in a profile is obtained by the application.

Automatically Collected Information:

In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, relating to your devices, including your device model, operating system, browser type, unique device identifier, IP address, device internet browser, mobile phone number, mobile network carrier, location and information about the way you use the Application. We will also use cookies and web beacons (pixel tags, and/or other similar technologies) to collect visitor information.


Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

At this time, this Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.
