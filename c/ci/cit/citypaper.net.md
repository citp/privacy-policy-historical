City Paper's goal is to provide readers with the information they want. The minimal information gathered is used for that purpose only. Other personal information given voluntarily — in filling out surveys, entering contests, etc. — will not be used for advertising purposes. This information is used only for City Paper's own marketing, web site improvement, special incentives and report statistics. E-mails from City Paper may contain small advertisements, but we will not share our e-mail lists with any other organizations. Access to this information is limited to City Paper employees responsible for web site marketing and production.

For updating, deleting or opting out of any lists generated on our web site, please contact:
