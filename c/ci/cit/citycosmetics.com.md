# CITYcosmetics - Privacy Policy

CITY Lips Privacy Policy

CITY Lips believes that privacy and security are a major concern to our customers. This Privacy Policy discloses the privacy and security practices for our Website. 

Information You Provide  
When you purchase on our website, we request and collect personal information including your name, address, phone, email address. It is up to you to decide whether you wish to participate in these activities and provide the required information. 

  * We will NEVER ask for your Social Security number
  * Credit card information is collected and used for purchase transactions ONLY
  * You may receive periodic emails about news, promotions, and additional products of ours
  * You may at any time cancel your membership with CITY Lips



Use of Cookies  
When you visit CITY Lips, we place a small text file called a "cookie" on your computer that allows us to identify your web browser. We use cookies to track if you clicked through one of our affiliate websites or links and to give credit to that affiliate if you place an order. A cookie DOES NOT obtain any personally-identifiable information such as your name and address. The use of cookies is an industry-wide practice and you'll find them at most major Websites. Most Web browsers are usually set to initially accept cookies. If you prefer, you can set your browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being set.

Collecting IP Addresses  
CITY Lips records your IP address when you place an order to validate purchases and to help prevent online credit card fraud. An IP address is a number assigned to your computer by your Internet Service Provider. Web servers automatically identify your computer by its IP address.

Sharing of Information   
We do not sell, rent or trade any personally identifiable information you provide when using this website. We do not collect any personally identifiable information about you (such as your name or email address) when you use our website unless you place an order, subscribe to our Newsletter, or enter one of our contests.
