CityWaboo.com collects and stores personal information through different parts of this Site including online order forms. This information may include your legal name, email address, age, gender, address, telephone number(s), financial information, User ID, User Password and other self-identifying information. CityWaboo.com collects this information for the purpose of providing you with our customized services that fit your preferences. We may make message boards and discussion forums available to you. If you choose to voluntarily disclose certain information on any such public area of the Site, this Privacy Policy will not cover such disclosure of information. We urge exercising caution when disclosing information on public message boards or discussion forums. CityWaboo.com may also monitor, on an anonymous aggregate basis, patterns of use in connection with emails that it sends out, in order to enhance the content of our news letters that we send out to our users. We may also automatically collect your Internet Protocol address for technical support. We may also use your information to contact you, if necessary.

CityWaboo.com will not sell, rent or barter your personal information other than to its business partners as set forth herein. We cooperate with law enforcement agencies in identifying those who may be using our servers or services for illegal activities. We also reserve the right to report any suspected illegal activity to law enforcement for investigation or prosecution.

You may occasionally receive updates about the products or services. In the event you are requested to register, you may be given an opportunity to "opt in" to receive mailings from CityWaboo.com and/or its partners. You may subsequently contact us to request to be removed from this list. CityWaboo.com reserves the right to limit any registrations on our Site to those who will accept our information and to those who will provide the requested information.

4. Use of cookies.
CityWaboo.com uses cookies to store and sometimes track information about you to help provide customized services to you. A cookie is a small amount of data that is transferred to your computer's hard drive. All portions of the Site that prompt you to log in or that are customizable require that you accept cookies. Cookies will not be used by us to retrieve any information about you, or from your computer, that you have not voluntarily given to us. Cookies may also be placed on your computer when you link from the Site to our partners' web sites or when you click banner ads provided on our Site. CityWaboo.com is not responsible for Cookies placed on your computer by third parties by or through the Site. You can usually edit your browser not to accept cookies, but the features of our Site may not function properly if you do not accept cookies.

We cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information that you transmit to us, or that we transmit to you, or guarantee that it will be free from unauthorized access by third parties.
