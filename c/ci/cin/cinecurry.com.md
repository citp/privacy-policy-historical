# Privacy Policy | CineCurry

Cinecurry respects the privacy of its users and is committed to protect it in all respects. The following lists our practices with regard to the same.

**_Use of Personal Data_**

When you register or interact with us through the Site, we may collect ‘Personal Data’ and other information. While such information is never shared with anyone or disclosed to third parties, we use this information to customize better tailor your experience at our site showing content that we think you might be interested in, and displaying content according to your preferences. This data is also used to serve banner ads.

**_Protecting Personal Data_**

Your personal data is stored securely in our database and we take all reasonable measures protect it from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. 

**_Cookies_**

Our site use cookies to store visitor’s preferences, customize web pages and deliver specific content and avoid repeating the same ad. Information collected using cookies is never shared with anyone or disclosed to third parties.

**_IP Address_**

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically gathers your IP address. This is used to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. While the IP address may also be used assimilate broad demographic information, it is never shared with anyone or disclosed to third parties. 

**_Advertisers_**

Sometimes we use third-party advertising companies to serve ads. These ads may contain cookies which are collected by the advertising network. We do not have access to this information.

**_Changes to Our Privacy Policy_**

The Site may change from time to time. As a result, at times it may be necessary for us to make changes to this Privacy Policy. Accordingly, we reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time and from time to time without prior notice. Please review this policy periodically, and especially before you provide any Personal Data. 

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please contact us at [webmaster@cinecurry.com](mailto:webmaster@cinecurry.com)
