PRIVACY POLICY
        STATEMENT


        

          
This is the web site of CooksRecipes.com.

          

            
Mailing Address:
          

          
CooksRecipes.com

We can be reached via e-mail at:

          
CooksRecipes.com@cooksrecipes.com

          
For each visitor to our Web page, our Web
          server automatically collects information on your domain type,
          browser used and your IP address. 

          The Web server recognizes no information regarding the e-mail
          address.

          
As an option, we may collect the email
          addresses and names of those who submit recipes or tips (to have
          it posted at CooksRecipes.com) for purposes of notifying the
          submitter that his/her recipe has been chosen as a featured recipe.
          No email addresses, the names or other personal information of
          the submitter shall be sold, shared or passed on to others.

          
The information we collect is used to improve
          the content of our Web page, not shared with other organizations
          for commercial purposes.

          
With respect to cookies: We do not set
          any cookies.

          
If you do not want to receive e-mail from
          us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail at
          the above address.

          
If you supply us with your postal address
          on-line you will only receive the information for which you provided
          us your address.

          
Persons who supply us with their telephone
          numbers on-line will only receive telephone contact from us regarding
          the information for which you provided us your telephone number.

          
With respect to Ad Servers: To try and
          bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships
          with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages.
          As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may
          collect information such as your domain type, your IP address
          and clickstream information. For further information, consult
          the privacy policies of:

          

            
Advertising.com (Miscellaneous banner ads)
            
Amazon.com
            
ElectricFoods.com (Gourmet
            Emporium)
            
Gifts.com
            (A Reader's Digest company)

            
          

          
From time to time, we may use customer
          information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed
          in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at
          some time in the future we will use for these new purposes only
          data collected from the time of the policy change forward.

          
Customers may prevent their information
          from being used for purposes other than those for which it was
          originally collected by calling us at the number provided above.

          
Upon request we provide site visitors with
          access to all information [including proprietary information]
          that we maintain about them. Consumers can access this information
          by e-mailing us at the above address.

          
Consumers can have information corrected
          by sending us e-mail at the above address.

          
With respect to security: If and when we
          transfer and receive certain types of sensitive information such
          as financial or health information, we will redirect visitors
          to a secure server and will notify visitors through a pop-up
          screen on our site.

          
If you feel that this site is not following
          its stated information policy, you may contact The DMA's Committee
          on Ethical Business Practices at mgoldberger@the-dma.org, The
          Federal Trade Commission by phone at 202.FTC-HELP (202.382.4357)
          or electronically at http://www.ftc.gov/ftc/complaint.htm.
