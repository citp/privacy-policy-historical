# CoolFetch.com

**Privacy**

> Privacy Statement
> 
> The Search Engine has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices. 
> 
> IP Address Usage
> 
> IP addresses are a series of numbers like 111.111.111.111 which tell the Internet how to route information to and from your computer. We use your IP address to transmit to and receive data from your computer. We also use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. In addition, we analyze IP addresses to determine broad demographic information, such as what part of the world you are from. IP addresses are not used to identify you personally, and they are never linked to name, address, or any other personal information about you. 
> 
> Cookies
> 
> The Search Engine does not use cookies to collect information about online behavior. In select portions of our site we use "Session Cookies" to store login information throughout the course of your visit. This information is not stored on your hard drive and is deleted as soon as your web browser software is closed. In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser. 
> 
> Registration Forms
> 
> The Search Engine utilizes registration forms for many of its services. These forms require users to give us contact information such as their name, e-mail address, website, mailing address, and phone number.The customer's contact information is used to get in touch with the customer when necessary. Contact information is also used to send information to the customer about The Search Engine, primarily via e-mail. Users may opt-out of receiving future informational mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. 
> 
> Order Forms
> 
> The Search Engine uses order forms so that customers can request information, products, and services. We collect contact information (such as e-mail addresses), and financial information (such as account or credit card numbers). The customer's contact information is used to communicate with the customer when necessary. Contact information is also used to send information to the customer about The Search Engine, primarily via e-mail. Users may opt-out of receiving future informational mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for The Search Engine services. Under no circumstances will the NSBC ever sell/rent your personal information and/or e-mail address to any third party. 
> 
> External Links
> 
> The Search Engine contains links to other sites. The Search Engine is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 
> 
> Public Forums
> 
> The Search Engine makes forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. 
> 
> Choice/Opt-Out
> 
> The Search Engine provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving promotional/marketing information from us. If you would like to do so, you will be provided a link in the e-mail that will allow you to do so. 
> 
> Please note: The Search Engine reserves the right to contact any user with an active account at the Search Engine. You may choose not to receive promotional/marketing materials, but you must consent to other forms of communication if you choose to have a the Search Engine account. 
> 
> Data Quality/Access
> 
> The Search Engine provides a method for users to change information about themselves in our database. If you have an active account with The Search Engine, simply log in and change your information.
