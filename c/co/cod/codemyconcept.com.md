### Non-disclosure agreement (NDA) for CODEMYCONCEPT.com clients

Please be aware that every single project you hire CODEMYCONCEPT to work on will be worked under this Non-Disclosure Agreement unless you request for us to sign a different document.

CODEMYCONCEPT fully respects your professional artwork and will only use it to assist you with the successful completion of your PSD to HTML project and/or platform implementations. Your copyrights will remain yours at all times.

CODEMYCONCEPT will not take your artwork, files or images to use them in any other website that is not yours; we will not resell it, or give it to anyone under any circumstance.

CODEMYCONCEPT.com guarantees that all information shared with us during the entire working process, including artwork, files or images, communication and contact information, will remain confidential at all times.

CODEMYCONCEPT.com will not contact your clients or the clients of your clients under any circumstance.

### Data Security

We are able to prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information at all times. We currently count with appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online at all times.

### Information Collected

CODEMYCONCEPT will require you to provide some information in order to provide our services to you. This is an example of the type of information we will require from you to be collected or submitted to CODEMYCONCEPT through our order form, product request, and other pages. You will most likely be asked to share the following information:

  * Email address
  * Client information requests
  * Order-specific information, including source files and project notes
  * Some specific details of your project in general



### How we use your information

The personal information you share with CODEMYCONCEPT is only used to complete your order. We do not and will not share this information with outside parties.

Your email address is used only to for notifications about the status of your order. It is not used for any other purpose, and it is not shared it with outside parties.

Finally, we never use or share your project or any information related to your project with any other parties. All the information provided to CODEMYCONCEPT online will remain safe and secure at all times.

### How you can access or correct your information

You can certainly correct factual errors in your personal information by sending us an email and providing us with proof that shows an error.

Please understand that, to protect your privacy and security, CODEMYCONCEPT takes reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections to your project.
