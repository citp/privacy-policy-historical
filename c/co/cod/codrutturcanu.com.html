(your information helps us to better respond to your individual needs)

To improve our website
(we continually strive to improve our website offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you)

(your information helps us to more effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs)

In addition, you might receive occasional company news, updates, related product or service information, etc.

Note: If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, we include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email.

How do we protect your information?

We use AWeber’s PermissionPledge ™ to protect your privacy and inbox; we also use Google’s FeedBurner mailing service for blog updates.

Yes. In addition to e-mail address, our analytics trackers may also collect information such as the operating system you are working on, the Internet browser you are using, and the screen resolution of your computer.

It’s your option to enable or disable cookies in your browser.

We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.
