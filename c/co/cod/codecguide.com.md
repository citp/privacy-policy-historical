# Privay Policy

**What information do we collect?**  


This website does not collect or store any personally identifiable information.

 **Disclosure of personal information**

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

 **Cookies**

Cookies are small data files that a website you visit may save on your computer or handheld device that usually includes an anonymous unique identifier. This website may use cookies for user authentication, keeping track of your preferences, and in certain other cases.

This website uses third party services for collecting traffic statistics, regarding website visits and website interaction. Those services may create and access cookies, which will be subject to their privacy policies.

Third-party advertisers may also create and access cookies, which will be subject to their privacy policies.

We accept no responsibility or liability for the use of third parties’ cookies. If you do not wish cookies to be placed on your PC or handheld device, then they can be disabled in your web browser. The option to do so is normally found in your browser’s “security settings” section. Please note that permanently disabling cookies in your browser may hinder your use of our website as well as other websites and interactive services.
