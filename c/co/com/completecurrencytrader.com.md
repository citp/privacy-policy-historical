# Privacy Policy

## 1\. INTRODUCTION AND GENERAL TERMS:

This privacy statement is made on behalf of Complete Currency Trader Limited (Company No. 8097604). Complete Currency Trader (“CCT”) is a trading education company and offers a range of content, products and services related to various types of trading including but not limited to Spot Forex. We are committed to respecting your privacy and keeping your information safe. We want to give you clear information about what information we collect and how we use it. This privacy and cookies policy tells you what to expect when Complete Currency Trader collects personal information from you. Complete Currency Trader Ltd may occasionally change or update this policy, so you should check this page from time to time to be sure you are comfortable with this policy. The current policy is effective from 25 May 2018.

## 2\. WHO ARE WE:

Complete Currency Trader is a trading education company and market analysis software provider. Headquartered in London, England, we provide professional training, education, and tools to our customers.

## 3\. SCOPE OF THIS POLICY:

This Privacy Policy applies to how we collect, use, and disclose information from the following individuals:

  * Customers – individuals who register, on their own or on behalf, to use Complete Currency Trader Services, products and tools with the creation of a Complete Currency Trader Customer account.
  * Event/Marketing Participants – individuals who provide their information to Complete Currency Trader when they register for Complete Currency Trader webinars, subscribe to marketing material, participate in surveys or contests, or attend other Complete Currency Trader events.
  * Website Visitors – individuals who visit Complete Currency Trader’s Website and opt to provide contact information including an email address to receive communications from Complete Currency Trader.



## 4\. INFORMATION WE COLLECT:

With your cooperation we may collect the following details:

Complete Currency Trader asks for your information which may include your name, business name, email address, physical address, phone number, credit card and bank account information (stored directly with a third party processor). ## Event/Marketing participants

Complete Currency Trader may ask for your name, email address, physical address, and phone number. Participation in events, surveys, contests, or subscribing to marketing material is voluntary and you may choose whether or not to participate and therefore disclose this personal data.

## Website visitors

At your option, Complete Currency Trader asks and may collect your name, email address, physical address, and phone number. In addition, we also collect information such as web server logs, internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, or other information as part of aggregated data.

We will only collect personal information with your explicit permission and active consent whereby you submit your details to us in a web form. Before we proceed to contact you with additional details of our service and products in response to any request you make, we will ask you to manually confirm we have your permission and consent to send you any relevant email communications.

## 5\. COOKIES:

We use cookies, beacons, tags, and other tracking technologies to gather demographic information about you, identify your visits to our Website, other interactions with our Website, and personalize your search experience on our Website. A cookie is a very small text document, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier. When you visit our web site, the cookie asks your computer for permission to store this file in a part of your hard drive specifically designated for cookies. Cookies help us analyse web traffic and we use traffic log cookies to identify which pages are being used most often and for how long. This helps us analyse data about web page traffic and improve our web site in order to tailor it to visitor needs. We only use this information for statistical analysis purposes. A cookie cannot retrieve any other data from your hard drive or pass on computer viruses. The cookie’s we utilize on this site are completely anonymous and merely identify you as a “site visitor” with no ability to retrieve any personal or sensitive information about you. In some countries, including those in the European Economic Area ("EEA"), the information in this paragraph may be considered personal information under applicable data protection laws. The information our tracking cookie may collect is as follows:

  * Your Internet protocol address
  * The kind of browser you use
  * Number of links you click within the site
  * State or country from which you accessed the site
  * Date and time of your visit
  * Pages you viewed on the site and for how long
  * Web page you linked to our site from



Browsers are usually set to accept cookies by default because they are an integral part of virtually every website. However, if you would prefer not to receive our cookie, you can easily alter the configuration of your browser to refuse/block ours specifically, or to block all cookies from all sites. If you choose to have your browser refuse cookies, it is possible that some areas of our site will not function as effectively when viewed, and your visiting experience on our site may not be as pleasant as we hope.

## 8\. YOUR CHOICES:
