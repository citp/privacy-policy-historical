What is Personally Identifiable Information?
"Personally identifiable information" is information that can be used to identify you as an individual. Personally identifiable information includes your name, address, telephone number and any other information that is connected with you personally.

No Personally Identifiable Information Collected
King Features does not collect any personally identifiable information on kingfeatures.com.

Cookies
A cookie is a small amount of data (often including a unique identifier), that is sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive. King Features does not use any cookies on kingfeatures.com.

IP Address
Your Internet Protocol ("IP") address is usually associated with the place from which you enter the Internet, like your Internet Service Provider, your company or your university. King Features may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our servers, gather broad demographic information, and administer our website.
