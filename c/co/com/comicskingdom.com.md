# King Features Syndicate - Privacy Policies

**Privacy Policy**

**Purpose of this Privacy Policy**  
This privacy policy describes the policies King Features Syndicate division, Hearst Holdings, Inc. ("King Features") with respect to the collection of personally identifiable information from the web site to which this policy is linked. The web site to which this policy applies operates under the URL "kingfeatures.com."

**Information This Privacy Policy Does Not Apply To**  
This privacy policy only applies to the web site operating under the URL "kingfeatures.com" (and the underlying pages that operate under that primary URL) to which this policy is linked. King Features is a division of Hearst Holdings, Inc., a subsidiary of The Hearst Corporation; however, this policy does not apply to any other websites operated by or on behalf of The Hearst Corporation or its affiliates and subsidiaries. Any information you provide when you a visit web site other than that covered by this policy (including other web sites affiliated with Hearst) is subject to the privacy policies posted on those websites.

**What is Personally Identifiable Information?**  
"Personally identifiable information" is information that can be used to identify you as an individual. Personally identifiable information includes your name, address, telephone number and any other information that is connected with you personally.

**No Personally Identifiable Information Collected**  
King Features does not collect any personally identifiable information on kingfeatures.com.

**Cookies**  
A cookie is a small amount of data (often including a unique identifier), that is sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive. King Features does not use any cookies on kingfeatures.com.

**IP Address**  
Your Internet Protocol ("IP") address is usually associated with the place from which you enter the Internet, like your Internet Service Provider, your company or your university. King Features may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our servers, gather broad demographic information, and administer our website.

**Changes to Privacy Policy and Notice**  
King Features may change this policy from time to time in its discretion. Your continued use of this site indicates your consent to this privacy policy and any amended versions of it.

This policy was last updated on July 1, 2002.
