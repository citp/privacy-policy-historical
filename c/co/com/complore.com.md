# Privacy Policy

Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy. By visiting the Complore website, you are accepting the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.  
 **INFORMATION GATHERING AND USAGE**  
The contents of your Complore page is stored and maintained on Complore servers in order to provide the service.  
When you sign in to access your personal page from anywhere we ask for information such as your user name, password, email address, etc.  
Complore will never sell, rent or share your personal information, especially your e-mail addresses, with any third parties for marketing purposes without your express permission.  
Social sharing of data: Complore allows other users to browse content that you have, submitted, or commented on. This information is made available via search or within the permalink URL of the content in which you performed the action on. Complore may choose to post this data on other site features in the future.  
**COOKIES**  
A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive.  
Cookies are required to use the Complore service.  
We use permanent cookies to record your session information

Google 

  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on Complore.com.
  * Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to Complore.com users based on their visit to Complore.com and other sites on the Internet.
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).



**CHANGES**  
Complore may periodically update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified when you signed in or by placing a prominent notice on our site. **QUESTIONS**  
Any questions about this Privacy Policy should be addressed to privacy at complore.com
