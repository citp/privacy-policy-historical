Information we collect about getlippy.com users
Registration Information
You can access getlippy.com and browse through certain parts of getlippy.com without disclosing personal information. However, if you wish to use certain services offered on getlippy.com or obtain certain information made available by handbag.com, you may be asked for information such as your name, email address, residential address and postcode. If you choose not to provide the information that is requested, you may not be able to use the service which is being offered or obtain the information you seek. Handbag may also collect information that you provide as part of registrations, orders, surveys, contests and promotions and other communications with us. Handbag will collect your personal information only with your knowledge and your consent.

Cookies
Handbag.com Limited uses cookies in order to track your visit to getlippy.com. Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to your computer's hard disk for record-keeping purposes. The use of cookies is an industry standard, and many websites use them to provide useful features for their customers. handbag.com may associate the information stored in a user's cookie with other personal data and information that the user has provided to Handbag.

In addition getlippy.com also embeds cookies from a number of third parties in the site to monitor usage and to help improve the service provided to its customers.

Nedstat, an independent measurement company, gathers anonymous data regarding the visitors to the site on handbag.com's behalf to provide non-personal statistical information about visits to pages on the site, data on visitors' browser type and other general information.

Doubleclick, an independent advertisement delivery company gathers anonymous data regarding the visitors to the site on handbag.com's behalf to provide non-personal statistical information about the number and type of adverts served on the site.

Survey.com use cookies on the site to track the number of surveys running on the site and to limit the number of times a user will be presented with a customer research questionnaire.

Advertisers and business partners that you access through getlippy.com may also send you cookies. Handbag does not control these cookies.

Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you'd prefer, you can set your browser to refuse cookies (details can be found at www.aboutcookies.org). You should be aware that areas of certain websites (including getlippy.com) do not operate properly if you refuse to accept cookies.

How your information is used
The personal information that you disclose to Handbag may be used to provide you with goods, services and features and to communicate with you. Such information may also be used for verification purposes, fulfilling orders, prizes, tracking compliance with the terms and conditions of use for getlippy.com, http://www.getlippy.com/aboutus/termsandconditions/ compliance with applicable laws and the performance of legal obligations.

Handbag uses the information obtained from cookies for several purposes. The cookies enable us to understand your own personal preferences and tailor your experience on handbag.com by using those preferences. Handbag also uses cookies to track user trends and patterns. The information which Handbag receives from cookies enables us to modify and improve handbag.com to meet the needs of our users, including yourself.

When users provide getlippy.com with registration information such as name, email address and postal address, this data can be matched with cookie data, if cookies have been set to be received. This means Handbag is able to match an identifiable user with specific usage of getlippy.com. This information would be used by Handbag and its parent companies only.

Handbag logs IP addresses for purposes of site administration and collecting aggregate information about the visitors to getlippy.com.
