    We may collect personal data when you use our Service. By personal data we mean any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person. Records of all Content synchronized to your account with us may be used and shared as described in this Policy, including Content initially purchased through a service of ours with a separate privacy policy (for example, Content initially purchased through one of our mobile applications and then synchronized to your account).

If you receive our marketing emails, we may send you an annual “happy birthday” message that may also contain product offers.
