Privacy Policy

COMARCH respects your privacy and recognizes your need for protection of personal information you share with COMARCH, such as your name, telephone number, address etc.

You may browse www.comarch.com website without providing personal information to us. However, if you want to receive detailed information about our solutions, or participate in our partner programme, we may request that you provide us with information about yourself and your company. Providing this information is optional and not a requirement to browse our website.

COMARCH has introduced the following principles to protect your privacy:

Security

COMARCH takes all appropriate and reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us from disclosure. We employ security safeguards to protect personally identifiable information from unauthorized access and misuse. However, due to the open communication nature of the Internet, we cannot guarantee that communications between you and COMARCH, and COMARCH and you, will be free from unauthorized access by third parties

Choice

There are times when COMARCH may collect personal information from you. COMARCH will inform you before it does so and clearly explain what it intends to do with the information. You have the right not to provide the information and, you are still welcome to visit most of our web site.

Delete

COMARCH provides our web site visitors with the following option for managing or removing their information from our database - send an e-mail to webmaster@comarch.com. Be sure to include your name and clear instructions about what you would like us to do.

Should you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at webmaster@comarch.com.
