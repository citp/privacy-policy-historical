# Privacy Policy | CogniFit

## CogniFit.com Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This policy explains the accepted privacy practices for our services at [www.CogniFit.com](http://www.cognifit.com/ "www.CogniFit.com"), including the purchase and use, online and offline, of the software CogniFit Personal Coach, CogniFit Senior Driver and any future software or product (the **"Websites"** and **"Products"** respectively, or collectively: the **"Services"** ). It describes the ways we collect and use your personal information and your rights with respect to our privacy practices. You can access our Privacy Policy here.

This policy is incorporated by reference to the Websites' Terms of Use ( **"Terms of Use"** ) and the Products' License Agreement ( **"License Agreement"** ).

##### Personally identifiable Information that we may collect:

We collect your personally identifiable information only if you voluntarily submit such information to us.

When you register or purchase the Product/s, we may ask you to provide us with details such as your age, name, your physical address, e-mail address, and telephone number. By registering or purchasing Products, you represent and warrant that you are 18 years of age or older. When you purchase a Product, or take an on-line assessment test, we may ask you to provide us with additional details, such as credit card information, as well as characteristic information such as gender, language, country of birth, birth date, computer experience, health condition, whether you are left or right-handed, whether you are color blind, and other questions related to your everyday life and personal habits (e.g. occupation, sleep hours, medications, alcohol consumption, smoking habits, etc.). We will specifically designate the fields for compulsory completion.

Further personally identifiable information may be collected while we monitor your performance and progress and when we exchange communications with you, for example, if you submit an inquiry to our customer support.

We may automatically collect information through the use of log files and other means. If you register with the Services, then the information gathered will be partially personally identifiable. If you are not a registered user, we will not knowingly make use of information personally identifying you, except as specifically indicated in this policy, or on our Websites.

##### What do we do with your personally identifiable information?

We may use personally identifiable information for the following purposes:

  * to provide you with the Products and Services and to enable you to use them;
  * to provide you with an evaluation and assessment based upon the results of tests you take and the personal details you provided on purchase date;
  * to improve and customize your experience with the Services;
  * to enable the development and improvement of the Products;
  * to better learn about and understand cognitive patterns of users;
  * to provide you with support, assistance and updates;
  * to handle inquiries and complaints and to collect fees;
  * to send you updates and notices, to provide you with information relating to the Services and to provide you with further marketing and advertising material, subject to your prior indication of consent [prior consent is a must under EU law, but not under US law]. At any time you may opt out of our mailing lists, by contacting us at the '[Contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20100228202156id_/http%3A//www.cognifit.com/about/contact_us)' page on the Websites. In any case, we will not share your personally identifiable information with any advertisers, without your explicit consent;
  * to facilitate communication between you and us, and between you and others;
  * to enforce the Terms of Use and the License Agreement;
  * to contact you when we believe it to be necessary;
  * to comply with any applicable law and assist law enforcement agencies as required;
  * to collect fees and debts, and to prevent fraud, misappropriation, infringements, identity thefts and any other misuse of the Services;
  * to take any action in any case of dispute, or legal proceeding of any kind between you and us, or between you and other users or third parties with respect to, or in relation with the Services;
  * to transmit or transfer your personal information to locations other than your place of residence;
  * For any other purpose stipulated in this policy, or in the Terms of Use and the License Agreement.



##### Additional Personal Information we may collect

While you are using the Products on-line, the software processes the data you entered and produces an output of your results and assessment. We may use your results without recognizing you personally to rank and/or group your results with those of other users and for other statistical and analytical purposes. CogniFit will use your username and password to automatically save the full evaluation results of any evaluation you take. Your personal information will be kept with us in compliance with this policy.

##### The information we share with others

We do not sell, rent or lease your personally identifiable information to third parties for any of their marketing purposes.

We may share personally identifiable information with others in any of the following instances, or subject to your explicit consent:

  * When you use the Services. In these cases, we will share your information only to the extent required to facilitate your use of the Services;
  * When you perform an on-line assessment test or an assessment in any other form. In these cases, we may share your personal details and our evaluation of your performance with a third party (such as your insurance company, or your employer's car fleet manager), if the test was ordered by the third party, or otherwise conducted in collaboration with the third party;
  * If we reasonably believe that you have breached the Terms of Use or the License Agreement, or abused your rights to use any of the Services, or performed any act or omission that we reasonably believe to be violating any applicable law, rules, or regulations. We may share your information in these cases with law enforcement and other competent authorities and with any third party, as may be required to handle any result of your wrongdoing;
  * If we are required, or reasonably believe that we are required by law to share or disclose your information;
  * In any case of dispute, or legal proceeding of any kind between you and us, or between you and other users or third parties with respect to, or in relation with the Services;
  * In any case where we may reasonably believe that sharing information is necessary to prevent imminent physical harm or damage to property;
  * If we organize the operation of the Services within a different framework, or through another legal structure or entity, or if we are acquired by, or merged with another entity, provided however, that those entities agree to be bound by the provisions of this policy, with respective changes taken into consideration;
  * We may also share personally identifiable information with companies or organizations connected, or affiliated with us, such as subsidiaries, sister-companies and parent companies, with the express provision that their use of such information must comply with this policy.



##### Log files and aggregated information

When you use the Websites or purchase a Product, we automatically collect further information and store it in web-server log files. For example, we may record the frequency and scope of your use of the Websites, the duration of your sessions, the web pages that you visit, the information that you read, advertisements that you view or click on, and the geographic location of the computer system that you are using to sign-in or use the Websites.

We may use anonymous, statistical or aggregated information, including by posting, disseminating, transmitting or otherwise communicating or making available such information through the Services, and providing the information to users, suppliers, business partners, affiliates and any other third party, at our sole discretion, provided however, that the information will not identify you personally and that we will not knowingly, or intentionally use such information to reveal your identity without your consent, under the terms of this policy.

We may also use anonymous, statistical or aggregated information to properly operate and administer the Services, to improve the quality of the Services, to enhance your experience and customize the Services, to create new services, to change or cancel existing content or services and to facilitate other internal, functional and statistical purposes.

##### Accessing your personal account

At any time, you may access your personal account and edit, or amend your personal information. If you find that the information, such as the billing or shipping information is not accurate, complete or updated, than you must make all necessary changes to correct it. Please keep in mind that providing false, incorrect, incomplete, or out dated information may prevent you from registering with or using the Services, and/or may impair our ability to provide you with the Services, handle shipping arrangements or provide you with support and contact you when required.

##### Data retention

At any time, we may review, retain and disclose any information as we deem necessary to satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process or governmental request, as well as our internal needs. However, by doing so we do not either expressly or impliedly endorse, affirm or in any manner assume any responsibility for such information.

##### Deletion of personally identifiable information

If you wish to delete your personally identifiable information that is stored in our databases, you may notify us of such request at the following email address: [support@cognifit.com](mailto:support@cognifit.com). CogniFit will inform you whether your request has been accepted and may refuse to comply with your request, for example, if CogniFit believes that deleting certain information might prevent maintaining necessary contact with you, or jeopardize certain activities on the Websites, or harm other users. In any case, CogniFit may keep any aggregated or anonymized information for statistical, marketing and other purposes.

##### Information security

We implement information-security systems and procedures to secure the information stored on our servers. Such systems and procedures reduce the risk of security breaches, but they do not provide absolute security. Therefore, we cannot guarantee that our services are immune to unauthorized access to the information stored therein and to other information-security risks.

##### Cookies

We may use cookies on the Websites. Cookies are packets of information sent by our servers to your web browser and then sent back by the browser each time it accesses our servers. We may use cookies on your web browser whether you are a registered user, or not.

We may use cookies for various purposes, such as to save you the need to re-enter your user name and password each time you log in, to facilitate the use of the Services, to collect statistical information with respect to the use of the Services, to verify information, to customize the Services to your personal preferences and for information security purposes.

Some of the cookies may expire when the session ends and you exit your browser. Other cookies are saved on your computerâ€™s hard drive. If you wish to block our cookies, then please use the help button in your browser and follow the necessary instructions. However, bear in mind that disabling cookies may complicate or even prevent your use of the Services, or certain features thereof.

##### Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may change and update the terms of this Policy from time to time. All substantial changes that we make to this policy will be notified on the websites and take effect 30 days following their initial posting. Other changes will take effect 7 days after their initial posting on the Websites, unless we amend the policy to comply with legal requirements. In the latter cases the amendments will become effective as required.

Your continued use of the Services after the changes to this Policy have taken effect constitutes your acceptance of the amended terms. If you do not agree with any of the amended Policy, then you must avoid any further use of the Services.
