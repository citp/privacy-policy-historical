### WEBSITE PRIVACY STATEMENT— MARCH, 2019

Cognizant Technology Solutions Corporation and its affiliated companies (“ **Cognizant” “we”  **or  **“us”** ) are firmly committed to protecting your privacy.  You should understand what we do with personal information we collect when you visit this website (cognizant.com), our country-specific sites or any other websites to which this Cognizant Website Privacy Statement (“ **Privacy Statement** ”) applies (collectively, the “ **Sites** ”). As a global company, Cognizant has a number of legal entities in different jurisdictions which are responsible for the personal information which they collect independently and which may be processed on their behalf by Cognizant Technology US Corporation and its affiliates. The data controller for personal information collected from a visitor to the Sites is Cognizant Technology Solutions US Corporation, 211 Quality Circle, College Station, Texas, United States of America or the affiliate specified on the Site that references this Privacy Statement.

We may supplement this Privacy Statement to address specific situations. All supplemental notices should be read together with this Privacy Statement.

#### Annex: Additional information for individuals based in the European Economic Area (“EEA Annex”)

This EEA Annex complements and supplements the main body of the Privacy Statement and covers the processing of personal information relating to individuals located in the European Economic Area (“ **EEA** ”).  In case of any conflicts between the main body of the Privacy Statement and the EEA Annex, the EEA Annex will have precedence.

The main purpose of the EEA Annex is to provide information about our data processing activities in compliance with Regulation 2016/679, also known as the General Data Protection Regulation (“ **GDPR** ”). 

We will notify you of any material changes or additions to this Privacy Statement and will provide you with a copy of any amended Privacy Statement(s) by use of your e-mail address provided to us, or by any other suitable means.

If you have any questions relating to this EEA Annex or our processing of your personal information, please contact our Data Protection Officer at: [dataprotectionofficer@cognizant.com](mailto:dataprotectionofficer@cognizant.com).
