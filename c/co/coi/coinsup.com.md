This document describes how We collect, use, disclose, transfer, and safeguard Your information.

#### 1\. Acceptance of the Terms of the Confidentiality Policy

By using the Website, You are indicating Your agreement to the terms and conditions of this Confidentiality Policy. Your continued use of the Website indicates Your agreement to the terms and conditions of this Confidentiality Policy and all amendments made thereto.

#### 2\. The Personal Information We Collect

**2.1** Personal information is understood to mean data that can be used to identify or contact a given person.

**2.2** In the course of creating Your Account Record on the Website, We collect information from the Account Records of social networks that You have indicated is “Public Information”: that is, information that is accessible for viewing by an unlimited number of persons, even if that information is not accurate. We do not collect other information or information to which access is limited.

**2.3** The following is information which may be collected by Us from the Account Records of social networks:

> **2.3.1.** ID (the “User Identification Number” on a social network: VKontakte, Facebook, Odnoklassniki, and so on)
> 
> **2.3.2.** The User’s first and last names (if indicated)
> 
> **2.3.3.** The User’s profile photo (if provided)
> 
> **2.3.4.** The User’s gender (if indicated)
> 
> **2.3.5.** The User’s year of birth (if indicated)
> 
> **2.3.6.** The User’s date and month of birth (if indicated)

#### 3\. How We Use Your Personal Information

The User’s personal information is used by Us as part of assigning the User an identification number in the process of creating a User Account Record on the Website. The User Identification Number allows Us to identify You on the Website, and is used by Us for technical and statistical purposes, allowing Us to assure optimal operation of the Website.

#### 4\. Disclosure of Your Information

**4.1.** We may be required to disclose Your personal information in accordance with the law, a legal process, or court proceedings, and/or on the basis of public inquiries or inquiries by government agencies inside or outside the country in which You are located.

**4.2.** We may also disclose information about You if We determine that such disclosure is necessary or appropriate for the purposes of national security, law enforcement, or other instances of public importance.

**4.3.** We may also disclose information about You if We determine that such disclosure is necessary in order to execute Our regulations and conditions, or for the purposes of protecting Our activity and Our users. Additionally, in the event of the reorganization, merger, or sale of the company, We may transfer any or all of the personal information We have collected to the relevant third party.

**4.4.** We do not sell or in any way provide personal information on Our users to third parties. This does not include trusted third parties which are providing Us with services concerned with the operation of the Website or management of Our business, as well as any other services. Information about You may be processed by Us and other members of Our corporate group and Our partners. Such parties are required to rigorously monitor and accept the regulations of this Confidentiality Policy. Information about You may be processed both within and outside the jurisdictions of the British Virgin Islands and the EEC.

**4.5.** In addition to the disclosure of information in accordance with Clauses 4.1–4.3 of this Confidentiality Policy, We may also disclose information about You to Our employees and the employees of other companies in Our corporate group. All parties receiving information about You are required to comply with current data protection legislation and ensure the confidentiality of Your information to the same extent to which We are bound by those obligations.

#### 5\. Collection of Non-Personal Information

**5.1.** We also collect information that is not personal: data that does not enable a direct association with any specific person. We may collect, use, transfer, and disclose non-personal information for any purposes. For example, We may collect information such as data on type of employment, language, zip code, area code, unique device identifier, and the location and time zone in which the Website is being used in order to better understand consumer behavior and improve Our products, services, and advertising. For the purposes of this Confidentiality Policy, the sum total of those data is regarded as non-personal information.

**5.2.** If We combine non-personal information with personal information, such combined information will be regarded as personal information for as long as that information is combined.

#### 6\. Information Requested during Withdrawal of Funds

Funds that are earned can be withdrawn using any of the methods listed on the Website. In order to withdraw funds, We request that You indicate and confirm Your cellphone number in Your Account Record.

#### 7\. Information Collected Automatically: Cookies

**7.1.** The Website may make use of cookies and other technology that allows personalization of the viewing of Our Website. A cookie is text information located in a temporary file that We send via the web server to the User’s computer in order to save the session in the browser, and to identify You the next time You visit the Website. Cookies do not contain personal User data. These files contain exclusively technical information that is non-specific and necessary for the identification of the User on the Website. Some of Our partners may also use cookies: for example, when placing advertisements. Please note that We do not have access to such cookies. Our partners’ cookies are regulated by their own Confidentiality Policies.

**7.2.** You are not required to accept cookies from Us, and You may accept or refuse them. If You want to turn off cookies in Your web browser, follow the instructions for Your browser:

You may view and change all of Your personal information, including:

**11.1.** We reserve the right to amend this Confidentiality Policy at any time. Amendments to the Confidentiality Policy shall come into force immediately after they are posted on the Website, and will be accessible on the Website’s home page or in the “News” section.
