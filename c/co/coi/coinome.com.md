## Collection of Information

  1. For the purpose of this Privacy Policy, the term ‘User Information’ includes the user’s personal information that can be used to uniquely identify or contact a single person collected by the Company to provide specific services to the user through the Website. The User Information collected by the company inter alia include the following:

    * Traffic Data. The Company tracks certain information about the user when he/she visits the Website. The information that is tracked includes information such as: (a) IP addresses; (b) domain servers; (c) types of computers system accessing the Website; (d) types of web browsers used to access the Website; (e) referring source which may have sent you to the Website’s URL; and (e) other information associated with the interaction of your browser and the Website.

    * Personal Information. In order for you to use certain features of the Website, such as creating an account on the Website for placing orders, you would be required to register with us, which requires you to provide certain personal information such as your e-mail address, phone number, name, date of birth etc., and certain sensitive personal data or information such as passwords, credit card details, debit card details, bank account information etc.

    * Transactional Data. All transactional data, such as intra Website searches, additions to wish list, orders placed, orders cancelled, payment modes chosen, number of visits, etc., is collected and used to ensure a better service.

    * Cookies Data. Cookies are small files that the Website transfers to your computers’ hard drive through your web browser (if you allow it) that enables the Website to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information. We use cookies to help us understand and save your preferences for future visits and compile statistical data about the Website traffic and Website interaction so that we can offer better Website experiences and tools in the future. If you prefer, you can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies via your browser settings. Like most websites, if you turn your cookies off, some of our services may not function properly.

    * Web Analytics. The Company may use third party web analytics services to track and analyze your interaction with the Website, such as mouse clicks, mouse movements, scrolling activity as well as any text that you choose to type into the Website. The Company uses this information to enhance your experience.

  2. The Company may also obtain information, including personal information, from third parties and sources other than the Website, such as advertisers or other users who import your email address to their address book. We may also, at your discretion, receive information from third party services that provide a mechanism to expose information you have provided to such third party through use of an application program interface (API), such as Facebook, Twitter, Google etc. If we combine or associate information from other sources with User Information that we collect through the Website, we will treat the combined information as User Information for the purposes of this Privacy Policy.

  3. The Company may have access to certain financial information which you may have provided at the time of making payments on the Website. The information includes your bank account details, payment method (valid credit card number, type, expiration date or other financial information). The said information is collected and stored by our third party payment processing company (the “Payment Processor”), which may in turn provide us with a secured portion of your financial information in order to complete transactions made through the Website. You acknowledge that the use and storage of your financial information is governed by the Payment Processor’s applicable terms of service and privacy policy.

  4. The user has an option not to provide the aforesaid User Information if he/she wishes not to avail the specific service on the Website, for which the User Information is required.

  5. Further, the Company will collect the User Information from the user at the time of creation of a user account or when the user is browsing the Website or while transacting an online purchase of the Company’s service through the Website or while posting comments (in form of texts, images, videos, audios etc.), uploading contents or participating in any offers or contests or availing special gifts or communicating with customer support by phone, email or otherwise.

  1.     * respond to claims that any posting or other content violates the rights of third parties.

  2. The Company does not have, and will not accept, any obligations of confidentiality with respect to any communications other than those expressly stated in this Privacy Policy and Terms of Use of the Website.
