# Privacy Policy | Consulting Success

Consulting Success (CS) is committed to protecting your privacy. CS keeps any information you provide us strictly confidential. We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to other companies. No personal information will be collected except when you specifically and knowingly provide such information.

By using our Web site, you agree to the collection and use of this information by CS. This privacy policy may be updated. If such an update is made we will post the updated information on this page so you can always see what information we collect and how we may use it. CS may provide our site traffic data, and such information to third-party vendors, but this data will not include any personal identifying information.
