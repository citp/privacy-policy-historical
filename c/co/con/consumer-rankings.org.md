# Consumer Rankings - Privacy Policy

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, and information volunteered by the consumer (such as survey information and/or site registrations). The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web pages and the quality of our service, and is not shared with or sold to any other organizations for commercial purposes, except to provide the services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

\- It is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats, violations of our Terms of Service, or as otherwise required by law.  
\- We transfer information about you in an event of an acquisition or a merger with another company. In this event, we'll notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

**Information Gathering and Usage**    
When you register for our service, we ask for information such as your name, email address, billing address, and credit card information. We reserve the right to use this collected information to provide you with the service you signed up for, identification and authentication, service improvements, contact and research.

**Cookies**  
A cookie is a small piece of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. We use cookies to record your session information. 

**Data Storage**

We use use third party vendors and hosting partners to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to run our service. We store your past activity with our service to help our employees serve you better.

**Disclosure**  
We may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances. This includes complying wiht subpoenas and violations of our Terms of Service.

**Changes**  
We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you about significant changes by displaying a prominent notice on our homepage.

**Questions?**

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, feel free to [ **contact us**](http://consumer-rankings.org/contact).
