# Connections Academy - Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 20, 2005_

Connections Academy has established this privacy policy to let you know how we collect, use, share, and protect information that we may gather as a result of your visit to www.connectionsacademy.com (the "website"), including those portions of the site relating to schools that use the Connections Academy program. There is a separate privacy policy for users of the Connections Academy Learning Management System.

## Information Collected About all Visitors

Each time you visit our website, it automatically keeps track of your IP address, domain name, the name of the page from which you entered the site, which pages are viewed and for how long. This information is collected in order to improve the website and for trend analysis. As with most websites, we may use "cookies" to track usage and to improve your experience. The cookies do not collect any personally identifiable information and you can also decline cookies. Further information about cookies is available in the help function of your browser. No personally identifying information is collected as a result of your use of the website, unless you provide it voluntarily.

## Voluntary Personal Information

The site may collect personal information you provide voluntarily, for example by requesting catalogs or seeking additional information. This information is used to fulfill requests for information or stay in contact regarding special promotions, new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not want to receive information from Connections Academy, you can email us at [unsubscribe@connectionsacademy.com](mailto:%75%6E%73%75%62%73%63%72%69%62%65%40%63%6F%6E%6E%65%63%74%69%6F%6E%73%61%63%61%64%65%6D%79%2E%63%6F%6D) and ask to be removed from our contact list. 

## Enrolling a Student Using the Website

If you register for a Connections Academy school using the website, information will be collected in order to process your application. This information will not be maintained on the website, but will be included in the Learning Management System. Enrollment information is also provided to school districts or other bodies as required by state public school regulations or operating procedures. 

## Children Under 13

Except as provided by parents for the purpose of enrolling in a Connections Academy school, the website does not knowingly collect information about children under 13. We attempt to be in full compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

## Links

The website contains links to other websites, message boards or other resources that we provide for your convenience. These sites are not under our control and we do not endorse them nor are we responsible for their content or their privacy policies.

## Changes

This policy may be changed periodically. Any changes will be effective as soon as they are posted.
