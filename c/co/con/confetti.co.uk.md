# confetti - privacy policy

|    


****Because your privacy is important to us, confetti.co.uk and Confetti Network Ltd operates by these principles:  
| • |  We explicitly ask when we need information that personally identifies you ("Personal Information"). For example, if you wish to register as a member of confetti.co.uk, we ask you for your name and email address. Once you've registered, we will use your Personal Information to personalise your member area "my confetti" and for signing in to confetti.co.uk. We will also inform you of new features, services and products from Confetti Network Ltd if you have opted in to receive information from confetti when registering.   
  
---|---  
• | On some orders, we are required to pass your information on to our suppliers in order for them to fulfil your order. This information will not be used for marketing purposes.   
  
• | Confetti Network Ltd will not pass on your information to other 3rd party companies unless you have opted in to receive such information on the member registration page.  
  
• | To make sure that your Personal Information for your member registration is correct and current, you may review and update your customer information at any time.  
  
  
If at any time you believe that Confetti.co.uk has not adhered to these principles, please notify us by email to help@confetti.co.uk, and we will use all commercially reasonable efforts to determine and correct the problem promptly. Confetti Network Ltd is registered under the Data Protection Act.
