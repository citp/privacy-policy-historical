Information Collection and Use
We respect each site visitor's right to personal privacy. To that end, we collect and use information throughout our Web site only as disclosed in this Privacy Policy. This statement applies solely to information collected on this Web site.

For each visitor to our Web site, our web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address.

The information we collect is:
 

  • used to improve the content of our Web page.
  • used to notify visitors about updates to our Web site or other related information.
  • never shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

This site collects cookies only when you visit our member area or when you subscribe to one of our publications.

Information collected is only gathered to contact the user about services on our site for which he or she has expressed interest. It is optional for the user to provide demographic information (income level, gender, etc.), and unique identifiers (username, password, etc.). Giving additional information helps provide a more personalized experience on our site, but is not required.)

Subscription Forms
We require information from the user on our subscription form. A user must provide contact information (such as name, email, and shipping address) and possibly financial information (such as credit card number, expiration date). If we have trouble processing an order, we use the information to contact the user.

This information is used:

  • For mailing and billing purposes.
  • To fill customer's requests.

When we transfer and receive certain types of sensitive information such as financial information, we redirect visitors to a secure server and will notify visitors of this feature through a pop-up screen on our site.

We publish an e-magazine (Better Design), and we never sell or share our subscribers' e-mail addresses. We may also operate online surveys.

Notification of Changes
 If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this privacy statement.

Conflict Resolution
We are committed to resolving disputes within 24-48 hours. If problems arise, users may contact us by email or telephone.
