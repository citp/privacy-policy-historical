This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that ControlCenterMarketing.com does not own or control, or to people that ControlCenterMarketing.com does not employ or manage.
 
Information Collection and Use
ControlCenterMarketing.com collects personally identifiable information when you register for a ControlCenterMarketing.com newsletter or free report, submit feedback or sign on as a member of the ControlCenterMarketing.com community. ControlCenterMarketing.com may also receive personally identifiable information from our business partners.

ControlCenterMarketing.com uses information for three general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see on our pages, to fulfill your requests for certain products and services, and to contact you about specials and new products.
 
Information Sharing and Disclosure
ControlCenterMarketing.com will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.
ControlCenterMarketing.com will only send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when:
  • We have your consent to share the information;
  • We need to share your information in order to provide the product or service you have requested;

(Unless we tell you differently, these companies do not have any right to use the personally identifiable information we provide to them beyond what is necessary to assist us.);

Cookies
ControlCenterMarketing.com may set and access ControlCenterMarketing.com cookies on your computer. A cookie is a small amount of data - which often includes an anonymous unique identifier - that is sent to your browser from a web site's computer and stored on your computer's hard drive.
ControlCenterMarketing.com allows other companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. The way other companies use their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to ControlCenterMarketing.com 's cookies.

Web pages may contain electronic images (called a "single-pixel GIF" or "web beacon") that allow a web site to count users who have visited that page or to access certain cookies.

Security
Every effort is made to keep your information private and away from hackers. However, it is not possible for us to guarantee your privacy. You should weigh up the risk that your details will be misused by hackers with the benefit of receiving the great information that this site has to offer.
