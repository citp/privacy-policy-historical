This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Conduit only and does not cover the use of cookies by any other third party.

If you reject cookies, you may still use the Website, but your ability to use some areas of the Website may be limited. By visiting the Website you acknowledge, understand and hereby agree that you are giving us your consent to gather information about your use of the Website through these Information Gathering Technologies.

Server logs capture certain non–PII, such as the time and date of a web visit, the browser and operating system being used, the IP address or domain name of the user's ISP, and the web URL a user linked from. This information is generally but not exclusively used to help plan system enhancements when necessary and to provide content that is compatible with our visitors' web browsing systems.
