Con Edison Privacy Statement

Thank you for visiting the Con Edison Website and reviewing our privacy statement.

Con Edison has created this privacy statement to help you understand how we collect, use, and disclose information collected by Con Edison as a result of our interactions with you. These interactions could be through: (a) www.conEd.com and the other websites operated by us from which you are accessing this Privacy Statement (the "Websites"); (b) the software applications made available by us for use on or through mobile devices (the "Apps"); (c) our social media pages that we control from which you are accessing this Privacy Statement (collectively, our "Social Media Pages"); (d) HTML-formatted email messages that we send to you that link to this Privacy Statement (the "Emails"); and (e) discussions with you, our customers, either in person or over the phone or through written correspondence (the "Customer Interactions"). Collectively, the Websites, the Apps, our Social Media Pages, the Emails and the Customer Interactions are considered the "Services."

At the end of this Privacy Statement, we provide you with the opportunity to make your own choice regarding our use and disclosure of your Customer Information to third parties for marketing purposes. Customer Information includes your name, account number, home or business address, email address, telephone number, your energy usage, or other information that is specific to you as our customer.

How We May Collect and Use Customer Information

Con Edison gathers and uses Customer Information for billing purposes, to inform you of products, and to address questions or concerns that you may have. We will use Customer Information to transact business with you, including responding to and fulfilling your requests.

We may use Customer Information for our business purposes, such as data analysis, audits, fraud monitoring and prevention, developing new products, enhancing, improving or modifying our Services, identifying usage trends, determining the effectiveness of our promotional campaigns and operating and expanding our business activities and as we believe to be necessary or appropriate to: (a) comply with applicable legal process or orders from our governing regulatory agencies including, among others, the New York State Public Service Commission, or the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission; (b) respond to requests from government or judicial authorities; (c) enforce our terms and conditions of service and applicable tariffs; (d) protect our operations or those of any of our affiliates; (e) protect our rights, privacy, safety or property, and/or that of our affiliates, you or others; and (f) allow us to pursue available remedies or limit the damages that we may sustain. We also may use Customer Information to send you marketing communications, offers and promotions that may be of interest to you.

Periodically, we administer online surveys to gather information about your use of energy and related products and services, or to obtain your views on the usefulness of our Website. Participation in our online surveys is voluntary. If you choose to participate, the information you provide will be used to improve our site and the products and services we offer to you.

How We May Share Customer Information

We may disclose your Customer Information as follows:

  • To our service providers or contractors in order to help us transact business with you, including processing your payment, answering your inquiry, promoting programs and associated product and service offerings, fulfilling your request for a product or service, or to otherwise conduct our business with you, including the payment of bills by you.
  • To our affiliates for the purposes described in this Privacy Statement.
  • To other third parties to permit them to send information on services that may be of interest to you, or marketing communications, consistent with your choice regarding our sharing of your customer information with such third parties.
  • We may need to share information, including Customer Information as we believe necessary and appropriate to: (a) respond to requests from governmental authorities and other partners to help detect, deter, and prosecute suspected criminal activity; (b) comply with applicable law; (c) comply with a judicial proceeding, a subpoena, a court order, regulatory directives or orders, or other legal processes or requests; (d) protect our operations or those of any of our affiliates; (e) protect our rights, privacy, safety or property and/or that of our affiliates, you or others; and (f) allow us to pursue available remedies or limit the damages that we may sustain.
  • We may disclose Customer Information to a third party in the event of any reorganization, merger, sale, joint venture, assignment, transfer or other disposition of all or any portion of our business, assets or stock (including in connection with any bankruptcy or similar proceedings).

Other Information We May Collect, Use, and Disclose

Con Edison uses software to automatically collect information that does not directly identify you ("Other Information") primarily for statistical purposes. This information, such as the name of the domain or IP address from which you access the Internet, the date and time when you access our Website, and the information that you view while using our Services, is typically used to monitor and compile statistics for use in evaluating and/or modifying our services, to improve content, and to diagnose and correct technical problems.

Please note that we may use and disclose Other Information for any purpose, except where we are required to do otherwise under applicable law. If we are required to treat Other Information as Customer Information under applicable law, then we may use it for all the purposes for which we use and disclose Customer Information.

Security

Con Edison has implemented policy guidelines and reasonable organizational, technical and administrative measures to help safeguard Customer Information from unauthorized access or improper use. Unfortunately, no data transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. If you have reason to believe that your interaction with us is no longer secure, please immediately notify us of the problem by contacting us in accordance with the "Contacting Us" section below.

Con Edison's energy delivery and customer service systems are part of the critical infrastructure of our nation, and we take our responsibility to protect our systems seriously. We work with our governmental and other partners to help protect our property and our nation. Any correspondence that you send to Con Edison in electronic form, including email or any forms that you complete and transmit through this website, may be monitored for suspected illegal activity.

Your Choice Regarding our Use and Disclosure of your Customer Information to Third Parties

We give you choices regarding our use and disclosure of your Customer Information to other third parties for their direct marketing purposes (except in circumstances required by law, regulation or order). If you would prefer that we do not share your Customer Information on a going-forward basis with other third parties for their direct marketing purposes, you may opt-out of this sharing by:

  • unsubscribing from the list of customer information we would share with other third parties
  • unsubscribing directly from the emails sent to you by other third parties, or
