**General Knowledge: What are cookies?**

| Cookies are data that a web server transfers to an individual's computer for recordkeeping purposes. Cookies are an industry standard used by most websites and help facilitate users ongoing access to and use of a particular website, for example by providing information used to deliver content specific to your interests and for other purposes, such as security and other account administrative functions, and which may track personal identifying information.  
---  
Cookies do not cause damage to your computer systems or files, and only the website that transferred a particular cookie to you can read, modify or delete such cookie. If you do not want information collected through the use of cookies, there are simple procedures in most browsers that allow you to delete existing cookies, to automatically decline cookies or to be given the choice of declining or accepting the transfer of particular cookies to your computer. Please refer to your browser instructions or help screen to learn more about these functions. You should note, however, that declining cookies may make it difficult or impossible for you to use portions of the visiting site.  
More detailed information & video demonstration can be found @ **[http://cookies.insites.com](http://cookies.insites.com/)**. We do not operate or control this site and are not responsible for the opt-out choices available there!   
  
**CoverTarget** does not use, create or store cookies. We do not have access to any information about you from cookies you might receive while visiting **CoverTarget**.  
---  
**CoverTarget** however makes use of relationships with third party service providers known as Ad Networks to serve ads on **CoverTarget**. These Ad Networks may use cookies, which appear to be coming from **CoverTarget** , but in reality they are coming from the Ad Networks. This process does not involve the collection of any personally identifiable information. The Ad Networks do not know your name, phone number, address, email address or any personally identifiable information We do not share any personally identifiable information about you with these advertising networks.
