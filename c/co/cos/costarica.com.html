Corporate > Privacy

1. Personal Information

COSTARICA.COM collects personal information when you register with the website and use its services.

When you register and create a profile on any COSTARICA.COM service we ask for information such as your name, address, e-mail, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. Partial contents of your profile (your complete name, state, city, and country) are public.

COSTARICA.COM automatically receives and records information on its server logs from your browser, including your IP address, and the page you request.

COSTARICA.COM automatically records messages sent via its server from Forum and Commercial Directory area to prevent spamming.

COSTARICA.COM uses information to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products or services, improve our services, contact you, and conduct research.

2. Information Sharing & Disclosure

COSTARICA.COM does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other companies or people.

COSTARICA.COM will disclose information to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise its legal rights or defend against legal claims; COSTARICA.COM deems necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of COSTARICA.COM's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.

COSTARICA.COM transfers information about you if COSTARICA.COM is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, COSTARICA.COM will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

COSTARICA.COM does not provide any personal information to the advertiser when you interact with or view an ad. However, advertisers may make assumptions that you meet certain criteria when you view an ad.

3. Confidentiality & Security

COSTARICA.COM limits access to your personal information to employees who may need to access it due to site administration of the service or to provide you with products and or services to perform their jobs.

Your personal information is password-protected. Any sensitive personal information is password-protected. COSTARICA.COM uses industry-standard SSL encryption to protect data transmissions.

4. Changes to this Privacy Policy

COSTARICA.COM may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your COSTARICA.COM account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.

5. Questions & Suggestions

Please address your concerns, questions, and suggestions to privacy@costarica.com.
