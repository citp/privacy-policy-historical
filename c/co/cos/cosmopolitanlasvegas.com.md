# Las Vegas Luxury Hotel | The Cosmopolitan

_(Last updated: May 25, 2018)_

This Privacy Policy (the “Policy”) tells you what information Nevada Property 1 LLC, doing business as The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas (“The Cosmopolitan,” “we,” or “us”), collects as part of your interaction with The Cosmopolitan and its website located at http://www.cosmopolitanlasvegas.com (the “Website”), including your participation, if any, in the Identity Membership & Rewards program, and how that information is used, shared, and protected, along with the choices that you have regarding your information.

PLEASE READ THIS POLICY CAREFULLY TO UNDERSTAND WHAT WE DO WITH THE INFORMATION THAT WE COLLECT AS PART OF THE INTERACTIONS.

This Policy is incorporated into and made a part of our [Terms of Use](https://web.archive.org/terms-conditions). Please note that we reserve the right to modify this Policy at any time. Material changes will be conspicuously posted on the Website or notice will otherwise be provided to you. All changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the Website unless otherwise stated.

To the extent you have any questions about or complaints concerning this Policy please contact us at [Privacy@cosmopolitanlasvegas.com](mailto:Privacy@cosmopolitanlasvegas.com).

This Policy is intended to provide adequate and consistent safeguards for the handling of personal information in accordance with the Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC (“the General Data Protection Regulation”), the Privacy Shield, and any other privacy laws, regulations and principles concerning the collection, storage, use, transfer and other processing of personal data transferred from the European Economic Area or Switzerland to the United States.

### A. THE TYPES OF INFORMATION WE COLLECT

_Personally Identifiable Information._ You are not required to provide personally identifiable information to visit the Website. However, you must provide certain personally identifiable information to request information or purchase products or services from The Cosmopolitan. We may collect any content, record, or electronic communication of any kind that you elect to send to us, including without limitation your name, contact information, financial information, demographic information, and other personal information, so we may provide you with such products and services and communicate with you and send you information and materials for marketing and other purposes, subject to applicable laws and the terms of this Policy.

Specifically, we collect your personally identifiable information as part of: (i) receiving and fulfilling reservation and information requests, (ii) your initial sign on to access the internet, (iii) purchasing products and services, (iv) registering for the Identity Membership & Rewards program, (v) submitting a job application, (vi) responding to communications from us (e.g., surveys, promotional offers, or reservation confirmations), and (vii) processing a casino credit application. If you provide your mobile or other telephone number to us and provided express your express consent,, we may send you text (SMS and MMS) messages and call you (both in-person and from automated dialing systems) from us at that telephone number. If you provide your email address, you consent to receive emails from us.

We may request the following types of personal information:

  * name;
  * email address;
  * mailing address;
  * social security or national identification number;
  * date of birth;
  * gender;
  * IP address;
  * payment card information; and
  * phone number.



_Payment Information._ Purchases of products or services must be made by credit or debit card. All credit and debit card information is provided directly to our third-party booking engines, which vary based on the products and services requested. These may include TravelClick, Expedia, TripleSeat, SevenRooms, and OpenTable. If you elect to make a purchase, you will need to submit your card number, expiration data, security code, and billing address as well as other information as may be required by the applicable booking engine. The Cosmopolitan does not directly access or handle your credit card information. Payment by credit or debit card is subject to the approval of the card issuer and The Cosmopolitan will not be liable in any way if a card issuer refuses to accept a credit or debit card for any reason.

_Cookies._ The Website may use cookies to track the use of the Website and to facilitate and enhance the user experience on the Website. “Cookies” are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive. This Website uses both cookies that we implement and third-party cookies implemented by our vendors. For example, we use third parties to help display certain information to you on our Website and to track how you view our Website. The Website's cookies enable us to facilitate access to different areas of the Website. For example, by showing when and how you and others visit the Website, what search terms you use to find the Website, and which third-party websites directed you to the Website. Cookies also tell us which pages of the Website are popular and which are not. Cookies can also help us improve your enjoyment of the Website, for example, by remembering your contact information and other personal information when you access or use the Website. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can disable this function so that your browser will not accept cookies. Please be aware that disabling the cookie function may impact your use and enjoyment of the Website.

_Embedded Content._ The Website may incorporate content, including feeds scripts embedded in the Website’s code and visible content (for example, videos), provided by third parties including YouTube, Twitter, Instagram, and Vimeo. In some cases, those third parties collect data about how you interact with their content. For example, YouTube may collect or otherwise have access to usage data on videos embedded on the Website as described in [YouTube’s Privacy Policy](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy).

_Social Media._ Our Website may allow you to connect and share information with various social media platforms, such as Facebook, Google+, YouTube, FourSquare, Instagram, Twitter and Pinterest. These features may require us to implement cookies, plug-ins, and/or APIs provided by such social media platforms to facilitate communications and features. We may share information that you provide us or that we may collect about your use of the Website with these platforms, and such information becomes subject to their privacy and data policies. We encourage you to visit the privacy policies of these platforms for further information:

  * Facebook's Data Policy at <https://www.facebook.com/full_data_use_policy>
  * Google+’s Privacy Policy at <https://policies.google.com/privacy>
  * YouTube’s Privacy Policy at <https://policies.google.com/privacy>
  * FourSquare’s Privacy Policy at <https://foursquare.com/legal/privacy>
  * Instagram’s Privacy Policy at <https://www.instagram.com/about/legal/privacy/>
  * Twitter’s Privacy Policy at <https://twitter.com/en/privacy>
  * Pinterest’s Privacy Policy at <https://about.pinterest.com/en/privacy-policy>



In addition, by choosing to use any third-party social media platform or choosing to share content or communications with any social media platform, you allow us to share information with the designated social media platform. We cannot control any policies or terms of such third-party platform. As a result, we cannot be responsible for any use of your information or content by a third-party platform, which you use at your own risk.

We may also use the advertising services and networks offered by these social media platforms, such as Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest and Twitter to deliver ad content. Use of these services requires the social media platform to implement cookies and/or pixels to deliver ads to you while online with such platforms.

_Ad Networks; Behavioral and Targeted Advertising._ We use third-party ad networks (called third-party ad servers or network advertisers, including Google, Facebook, Sojern, VisualIQ, IgnitionOne, and Dstillery to deliver ads to you on our behalf. These third-party ad networks may place their cookies, web beacons or other technology on the Website or in your browser to collect non-personally identifiable information about you when you visit the Website (such as browser type, time and date, browsing or transaction activity, subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over, or a platform-provided identifier during your visits to this and other websites) to enable those third-party ad networks to deliver advertisements to you on third-party websites and mobile applications for our products and services or those of third parties that are likely to be of interest to you. For example, Google uses the Doubleclick cookie to enable it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our Website and/or other websites on the internet.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of advertisers and ad services to learn more about their cookies and other technologies.

If you do not want to receive those kinds of advertisements, look for the "Ad Choices" icon in the advertisements, which will contain a link to the privacy policy of the ad network that will provide you with information on how to opt out of receiving such targeted advertising.

**You can also visit the websites[http://www.networkadvertising.org](http://www.networkadvertising.org/) or [http://www.aboutads.info](http://www.aboutads.info/) to change your consumer preferences with respect to certain online ads and to obtain further information about third-party ad networks and online behavioral advertising.**

_Analytics._ Like many websites, our Website uses Google Analytics to collect information about your use of our Website. Your web browser automatically sends this information to Google, such as the web address of the page that you visit and your IP address. Google may also set cookies or read preexisting cookies. This information is stored on Google´s servers. Google uses this information to provide us with reports about traffic to our Website and your visit to our Website (such as the domain from which you access the internet, the web address of the website from which you linked to our Website, the time and date of your visit to the web pages that you view and click through). We will use this data to improve our Website structure and content. Google may also use this data in accordance with their Privacy Policy, which can be found at <http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/>. **For more detailed information on how Google uses your data through our Website, please visit<http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/>.**

You may opt out of our use of Google Analytics by visiting the Google Analytics opt-out page and installing the Google Analytics browser add-on, currently made available by Google at <https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout>.

_Google Analytics Advertising Features._ We have also enabled the Google AdWords feature along with the following Google Analytics Advertising Features: Google Display Network Impression Reporting, Remarketing with Google Analytics, and Google Analytics Demographics and Interest Reporting. The Google Display Network Impression Reporting uses the Google Analytics cookie to track our ad impressions and your interactions with those impressions, including the effectiveness of those ads, which generates reports for us to help us analyze our advertisements. Remarketing with Google Analytics uses the Google Analytics cookie to serve advertisements to you across the internet based on your visit to our Website. Google Analytics Demographics and Interest Reporting uses a third-party cookie to collect information about our Website traffic by tracking users across websites and across time, which generates a report for us to better understand our Website users.

For more information on how Google uses this data, visit [Google’s Privacy Policy](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy) and Google’s page on [How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.](http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners)

_Other Information._ We also collect information that, in and of itself, cannot be used to specifically identify you, such as the characteristics of the device and software that you use to access the Website. This information includes without limitation internet browser type, other characteristics of your device and software, domain names of your Internet Service Provider (ISP), your approximate geographic location, a record of your usage of the Website, the time of your usage and aggregated personal data that cannot be used to specifically identify you. IP addresses are not used to track a user’s session and we do not link IP addresses to any personally identifiable information. IP addresses help us determine your geographic location and how often different areas of the Website are visited. We will use this information to monitor and prevent fraud, diagnose problems, and anonymously estimate demographic information. Such information, which is collected passively using various technologies, cannot in and of itself be used to specifically identify you. We also collect this type of data (including without limitation information of the types set forth above) from third parties. The information we collect from third parties may be combined with the information we collect from you. Our Website provider may collect information about your visits to the Website and other websites, such as your user settings.

### B. HOW WE USE AND SHARE INFORMATION

We may use any personally identifiable information or other information that you voluntarily provide to us to provide you with information, products, or services that you may request from us. We do not sell, rent or loan any of your personally identifiable information to any third party. We may share information with those persons and third parties specifically involved in processing your purchase of our products or services or other activities involving the Website. We will not sell, share or rent the information that is collected to others in ways that differ from what is disclosed in this Policy.

We reserve the right to use and share your information if we believe doing so is necessary to operate the Website as further set forth below. We use your information to customize your browsing experience, display custom content to you and communicate with you, and otherwise respond to your questions and suggestions regarding the Website as may be permitted by applicable law. The Cosmopolitan may also use your personal information to send you promotional communications and information about The Cosmopolitan that we think may be of interest to you.

Your permission to use personal information we collect from you via Interactions in other ways will be implied from the nature of the service requested or transaction undertaken. For example, your permission will be deemed given for communications from us that are necessary to fulfill transactions and services that you request.

You may unsubscribe from marketing emails from The Cosmopolitan by clicking on the “unsubscribe” links in those emails. Please note that if The Cosmopolitan has shared your information with third parties as permitted by this Policy, opting out or unsubscribing from marketing messages from The Cosmopolitan will apply to marketing messages coming from The Cosmopolitan only, and not to marketing messages coming from such third parties. To opt out of messages from our strategic marketing partners and other third parties, please see above and refer to such third parties' privacy policies and terms of use.

We reserve the right to share your information collected from the Website with third parties to the extent permitted by applicable law, or if in our sole discretion, it is reasonable to do so, including in the event of a subpoena or law enforcement request or other security issue or to protect our rights or the rights of a third party. We may also disclose information that is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action in the event of a violation of our contracts, policies or procedures.

_Sharing Information with Our Suppliers._ We may share information with our Website suppliers, marketing suppliers, reservation system provider, advertising agencies, email suppliers, booking engines, and other suppliers as necessary to provide the Website (including all of the Website’s features) and our products and services to you. We require those suppliers to comply with all applicable data privacy laws and regulations. These companies are authorized to use your personal information only as necessary to provide the applicable services to us.

_Aggregate Data._ We may aggregate your personally identifiable information, either alone or with other data, to create anonymous “aggregate data” regarding the users of our Website. Aggregate data is information that describes the habits, usage patterns, or demographics of users as a group but does not reveal the identity of particular users. This data will not identify you but will be used by The Cosmopolitan internally as statistical information to determine such things as user demographics and usage patterns of our Website. We may use aggregate data to understand the needs of our community of users, to determine what kinds of programs and services we can help provide, and to improve and enrich our products, services, marketing and Website content.

_Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies._ Our Website suppliers may use cookies and other tracking technologies to track Website visitors across the internet to understand how you get to the Website.

### C. SECURITY MEASURES WE TAKE TO PROTECT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

We have implemented reasonable technical, administrative, and physical security measures in an effort to safeguard the privacy of your personal information from unauthorized access or improper use. We limit access to personally identifiable information to our employees and third parties whom we believe need to have access to such information for The Cosmopolitan to respond to your requests and provide you with the information, products, or services you request from us.

From time to time, The Cosmopolitan will review our security procedures in order to consider appropriate new technology and methods to enhance our security procedures as new technology becomes available. However, you acknowledge and agree that despite our best efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and your personal information may still be accessed and used by others without authorization in the event of a security breach.

### D. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to be provided with information as to the nature of the personal information stored or processed about you by The Cosmopolitan and may request deletion or amendments.

You may email [Privacy@cosmopolitanlasvegas.com](mailto:Privacy@cosmopolitanlasvegas.com) to review, update, and revise your personal information.

If access is denied, you have the right to be informed about the reasons for denial. You may resort to the dispute resolution described in this Policy as well as in any competent regulatory body or authority. The Cosmopolitan shall handle in a transparent and timely manner any type of internal dispute resolution procedure about personal information.

If any information is inaccurate or incomplete, you may request that the data be amended. It is your responsibility to provide use with accurate personal information about yourself and to inform us of any changes. (e.g. new home address or change of name).

If you demonstrate that the purpose for which the data is being processed in no longer legal or appropriate, the data will be deleted, unless the applicable law requires otherwise. You also have the right to request the deletion of your personal data for any reason by emailing [Privacy@cosmopolitanlasvegas.com](mailto:Privacy@cosmopolitanlasvegas.com).

Occasionally, information that you request to be removed will be retained in certain files for a period of time in order to properly resolve disputes, to troubleshoot problems, or as may be required by applicable law. In addition, some types of information may be stored indefinitely on “back up” systems or within log files due to technical constraints, or financial or legal requirements. Therefore, you should not always expect that all of your personal information will be completely removed from our databases in response to your request.

The categories of information we disclosed to third parties for their direct marketing purposes during the preceding calendar year;
