Privacy Policy

All communications or material passed by you to us through this Website or via the internet will be treated as non-confidential unless such communications or material contains personal information which shall be subject to our Privacy Policy.

We collect personal information from visitors to this Website through the "Contact Us" section of this Website. We will use your personal details and information we obtain from other sources to enable us to respond to complaints and queries and to ensure that the content, services and advertising that we offer are tailored to your needs and interests. We may keep your information for a reasonable period for these purposes. We may need to share your information with our service providers and agents for these purposes. We may disclose your personal information in order to comply with a legal or regulatory obligation.

Any personal information relating to you will be used by us in accordance with current data legislation and this Privacy Policy. You should read this Privacy Policy in conjunction with the above Terms of which this Privacy Policy forms a part.

We may also share your information with our business partners in the food manufacture business for marketing purposes or we may send you information about other organisations' goods and services. We or our business partners may contact you by mail, telephone, fax, e-mail or other electronic messaging service with offers of goods and services or information that may be of interest to you about our or our partners' products. By providing us with your fax number, telephone numbers or email address you consent to being contacted by these methods for these purposes.

We reserve the right to disclose your personal information to government bodies and law enforcement agencies (provided we are reasonably satisfied as to the circumstances surrounding the request), to third party companies to help us operate our systems properly and to protect our users and ourselves.

By providing us with your personal information, you consent to our processing your sensitive personal data, such as data relating to health, for the above purposes. You also consent to our transferring your information to countries or jurisdictions which do not provide the same level of data protection as the UK , if necessary for the above purposes. If we do make such a transfer, we will, if appropriate, put a contract in place to ensure that your information is protected.

If you provide us with information about another person, you confirm that they have appointed you to act for them, to consent to the processing of their personal data including sensitive personal data and that you have informed them of our identity and the purposes (as set out above) for which their personal data will be processed.

In addition, we may collect personal information in the form of CVs and job applications. This information will only be used for recruitment purposes and will not otherwise be used in the ways described above. All of your rights in relation to this information are as described elsewhere in this Privacy Policy.

You are entitled to ask for a copy of the information we hold about you (for which we may charge a small fee) and to have any inaccuracies in your information corrected.

For quality control and training purposes, we may monitor or record your communications with us.

If you object to the use of your personal information in any of the ways described above or have any queries with regard to this Privacy Policy please write to us at Premier Foods PLC, 28 The Green, Kings Norton, Birmingham B38 8SD.

This Privacy Policy may change and therefore you should review it regularly. We will of course notify you of any changes where we are required to do so.

Information about the data protection regime in the UK is available on the Information Commissioner's website at http://www.dataprotection.gov.uk/.
