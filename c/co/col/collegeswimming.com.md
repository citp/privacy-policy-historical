# Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

 ****Collegeswimming.com is committed to your privacy as a visitor to the collegeswimming.com website (“Site”). Whenever the terms “we,” “us,” or “our” are used in this privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”), they refer to collegeswimming.com. We believe it is important for you to know how we handle the information we receive from you via the Internet. You can visit our Site without revealing any personal information. However, you are required to provide personal information if you use the Site to sign up for an account or otherwise communicate with us through your use of the Site.

**Information Collection and Use**  
It is collegeswimming.com's policy to comply with all applicable laws and regulations relating to its use of the information collected on this Site to the extent such laws apply to it and its operations. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this Privacy Policy. We may sell, share, or rent this information to vendors and agents in support of collegeswimming.com’s activities. collegeswimming.com may share such information as is necessary in order to provide you with the information and services you request. We may also disclose such information to the extent we believe it is necessary in order to (a) comply with any law, rule, court order, or subpoena, (b) protect and defend the rights or property of collegeswimming.com, the Site, or Site users, or (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the safety of users of the Site, collegeswimming.com, or the public. In addition, we may keep anonymous statistical data about you and other users of our Site, and we may sell, share, or rent this information to third parties. Our use of the information collected on this Site may include, without limitation, using such information to communicate with you regarding the use of the Site, to inform you of changes to the Site or products and services that we think you may wish to know about, to maintain the Site, analyze statistical use problems, or to evaluate from where users link to the Site and where users then go. Using such information in this way may enable us to better serve you, and to present a website that best suits your needs. 

**Registration and Forms that Request Personal Information**  
On certain pages of the Site, we may give you the option of providing us with your name, address, phone number, email address, demographic information, preferences and other personal information. For instance, you may sign up for an account relating to your swimming activities and we may offer specialized registration for certain programs, events and classes that may require some or all of the above information. We only gather this personal information with your knowledge, permission and participation. If you are not comfortable with this, you may choose not to use this part of the Site. We may use such information to send you an email confirming your registration, to respond to questions from you, or to notify you of other related information. We may also, from time to time, send emails to you on special promotions or newsletters that may be of interest to you. 

**E-mail Communication, Newsletters and Related Services**  
The Site may offer features that provide you with the opportunity to receive communications from us or third parties that are delivered via email. 

Email communication that you send to us via the email links on our Site may be shared with a customer service representative, employee or agent that is most able to address your inquiry. We make reasonable efforts to respond in a timely fashion once communications are received. Once we have responded to your communication, it is discarded or archived, depending on the nature of the inquiry. 

The email functionality on our Site does not provide a completely secure and confidential means of communication. It’s possible that your email communication may be accessed or viewed by another Internet user without your knowledge or permission while in transit to us. If you wish to keep your communications private, do not use our email. 

If you sign up for a free email newsletter, you can unsubscribe to this newsletter at any time by following the opt-out procedure set forth in this Privacy Policy. 

**Children’s Privacy**  
collegeswimming.com encourages parents and guardians to monitor and participate in their children's online activities. This Site is designed for access by college-age swimmers and we do not request, seek or intentionally collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The Site is not intended for and does not direct content to children under the age of 13. 

**Use by Minors**  
By continuing to use the Site, you warrant that you are 18 or older OR have a parent’s or legal guardian’s consent to use the Site. Parents or legal guardians are responsible to determine the appropriate content for their children and to monitor use and access to the Site and any information it contains. 

**Security of our Site and E-mail**  
The security of any data you submit via this Site is very important to us. However, no system can perfectly guard against risk of intentional intrusion or inadvertent disclosure of information sent to us. Moreover, when you transmit information via the Internet or via email, your information will be transmitted over a medium that is beyond our control, and therefore the security of the transmission may be compromised before it reaches us. Accordingly, collegeswimming.com makes no guarantee as to confidentiality or security. IN ADDITION, YOU HEREBY EXPRESSLY AND SOLELY ASSUME THE RISK OF ANY UNAUTHORIZED DISCLOSURE OR INTENTIONAL INTRUSION, OR OF ANY DELAY, FAILURE, INTERRUPTION OR CORRUPTION OF DATA OR OTHER INFORMATION TRANSMITTED IN CONNECTION WITH YOUR USE OF THE SITE. 

**IP addresses**  
We collect and log the IP address of all visitors to our Site. An IP address is a number automatically assigned to your computer whenever you access the Internet. IP addresses allow computers and servers to recognize and communicate with one another. We collect IP address information so that we can properly administer our systems and gather aggregate information about how our Site is being used, including the pages visitors are viewing, general traffic, site usage and length-of-stay information. We may sell, share, or rent this aggregate information to third parties. This sort of information is shared only in aggregate and to maintain your anonymity, we do not associate IP addresses with records containing personal information. We may use IP address information, however, to personally identify you in order to enforce our legal rights or when required to do so by law enforcement authorities. 

**Cookies**  
"Cookies" are small bits of information that are stored by your browser onto your hard drive as you visit Web pages. Like may other websites, we may use these tools for purposes such as monitoring the traffic flow of visitors from other sites. Collecting this information, however, does not allow us to personally identify you.

Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you may be able to change your browser settings to prevent this. Contact your Internet service provider for more information. If you reject cookies from our Site, some parts of the Site may not work properly for you. 

Our sponsors and advertisers and their advertising servers (“Ad Servers”) may use cookies when you view pages on our Site. They use those cookies to collect non-personal information as a way of measuring the effectiveness of their advertising. We do not control these third parties use of cookies or how they manage the non-personal information they gather through them. If you click on an advertisement on the Site and visit a site maintained by one of our sponsors or advertisers, please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of that site. You should read the privacy policies of each site you visit to determine what information that site may be collecting about you. 

Some Ad Servers allow you to opt out of anonymous data collection through the use of cookies. To do so, you must opt out of such data collection with each individual site. You may be able to opt out of cookies for several Ad Servers. Contact your Internet service provider for more information. 

**Your Acceptance of This Privacy Policy**  
This Privacy Policy is incorporated into the Terms and Conditions posted on the Site. By accessing or using the Site, you signify your acceptance of this Privacy Policy and the Terms and Conditions posted on the Site. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY OR TO THE TERMS AND CONDITIONS POSTED ON THE SITE, DO NOT ACCESS OR USE OUR SITE.

We may revise and update this Privacy Policy at any time, and such modifications shall be effective immediately upon posting the modified Privacy Policy on the Site. You agree to review this Privacy Policy periodically to be aware of such modifications, and your accessing or using of the Site constitutes your acceptance of this Privacy Policy as it appears at the time of your access or use. 

**Opt-Out Procedures**  
You always may opt-out of receiving future contacts or mailings from us. collegeswimming.com provides you with the opportunity to opt-out of receiving electronic communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about you. You may at some point no longer wish to receive electronic communications from us or our partners. To stop receiving such communications, click on the Unsubscribe link at the bottom of the newsletter or email communication.
