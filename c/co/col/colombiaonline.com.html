1) DATA COLLECTION ON COLOMBIA NETWORK

Colombia collects both PII & non-PII data. We collect PII data through enquires or through Colombia registration page etc. We may collect name, email address, phone no. etc. We keep PII data in hashed form.

Colombia collects non-PIIs such as browser, device, device type, OS, IP, network provider, platform, geo-location (derived from IP), mobile advertising IDs such as Apple IDFA or Google AAID.

We collect events related to Colombia ad serving activity such as the number of ads or type of ads shown to a user or ads clicked by a user on different scenarios or post-click activity in relation to the ad. This allows us to personalize and develop user analytics in a secure & non-identifying way for users.

We also collect technical user identifiers from our advertising partners for the purpose of linking the different browsers and mobile apps you use and serve you relevant ads based on your behaviour across environments (ID syncing).

We collect information in order to improve the relevancy of ads being shown to you. The data we collect is used for reporting and analytics purposes and to give our clients & partners more information about the performance of their advertising campaigns and to improve performance over a long time.

We don't use or store full IP address for targeting purposes. We do not collect hardware-based identifiers such as Mac Address.

We do not process any information which could be used by Colombia, directly or indirectly, to determine the personal identity of the user.

Colombia doesn't knowingly show any interest based advertisement to & collect and maintain data from children less than 13 years of age. If we realize we have unknowingly collected data from children less than 13 years of age, we make considerable efforts to remove such information from our system.

WHAT DOES OPT-OUT MEAN

 

Not Opted Out: Colombia will serve you interest based sponsored content based on your history.

Opted Out: You have opted out of interest based advertising; Colombia will no longer serve you with personalized sponsored content recommendations based on your internet usage history.

Opting out will NOT prevent you from seeing our sponsored advertisements, but ads you see will likely be less relevant because they wont be tailored to your interests.

You may still continue to see sponsored content relevant to the current website/webpage you are viewing (i.e. context-based ads).

Note that if your browsers are configured to reject cookies, your opt-out may not be effective, since we identify your choice to opt-out based on a cookie we deliver to you. So you will need to confirm that your browser is configured properly in order to make the opt-out option  effective.

 

3) OPTING OUT OF DIFFERENT BROWSERS AND DEVICES

By clicking on above opt-out button user wont be shown interest-based advertising on current browser. If a user wants to opt-out from all the browsers, he or she has to exercise his or her choice in all the browsers and devices. In case we have matched a user in multiple browsers & devices, opting out on one browser he or she will be opted out from all the matched browsers & devices.

a) Online web & mweb environment:

To honour a users opt-out choice, Colombia drops an opt-out cookie. Colombia stores user profile for a maximum of 30 days between two subsequent visits i.e. if a user doesnt revisit any CAN publisher at least once within 30 days, the user profile along with opt-out preferences is deleted and then he/she will have to again opt out from interest-based advertising. The lifespan of Colombia cookie is 10 years. If the user regularly visits any of CAN publishers(at least once in every 30 days) then the opt-out choices are stored for a maximum of 10 years with the cookie. In case a user deletes opt-out cookie from a browser then he/she will have to again opt out from the interest-based advertising.

b) In-app environment:

Web browser environment and in-app environment are separate. If you don't want to receive interest-based sponsored content irrespective of the platform type, a user must disable or opt out from both the online web and mobile-app environment.

To opt out from interest-based advertising on an android app, the user should refer to Ads tab under google setting in his/her android device. Refer link for detailed steps for doing the same.
https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/3405269

To opt out from interest-based advertising on an iOS app, the user has to use the Limit Ad Tracking option. Follow the instructions provided by Apple. Refer link http://support.apple.com/kb/HT4228
