Privacy Policy




We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to CollegeRecruiter.com will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways to which you have not consented. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described below. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to let us know by clicking on the Contact Us button on the left.

Resale or Disclosure of Information to Third Parties:

CollegeRecruiter.com does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or email lists.

Browser Information Collected on Our Website:

We analyze our website logs to constantly increase the traffic to and revenues of our website and to improve the value of the materials available on website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.

Use of Cookies:

CollegeRecruiter.com uses cookies on this site to more effectively serve banner advertising and to track the website from which our visitors were referred. Our cookies are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.

Privacy of Our Email Lists:

We maintain several email lists to keep our users informed about new employment ads and articles running on our website, changes to our affiliate program, and to provide a meeting place for our users. Individuals must affirmatively request to join our mailing lists. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone. In addition, we have configured our list server software to refuse to divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone but authorized staff and contractors.
