Overview

Corp-Corp.com has created this Privacy Policy in order to demonstrate our commitment to your privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: corp-corp.com

Information Collected

While visiting any of the corp-corp.com Network web pages or sites or partner links (collectively, the "Site"), we may ask you to register and/or provide information that personally identifies you ("Personal Information") for purposes of interacting with the Site and provide customer support. Such Personal Information may include, but is not limited to, your name, company details, phone number, fax number, street address, and e-mail address. On any page that collects Personal Information, we will specifically describe what information is required in order to provide you with the product or service or enter you in the promotion you have requested, as well as respond to your inquiry or feedback.

General Information Disclosure

Except as described in this privacy policy, we do not disclose information about your individual visits to our site, or personal information that you provide, such as your name, address, e-mail address, telephone number, credit card number, personal references, etc., to any outside parties, except when we believe, in good faith, that such release is reasonably necessary to

(i) Comply with law

(ii) Enforce or apply the terms of any of our user agreements or

(iii) Protect the rights, property or safety of corp-corp, our users, or others.

We may record and share aggregated information with our partners, member companies and job seekers.

We employ other companies and individuals to perform functions on our behalf. Examples may include hosting our web servers, analyzing data, providing marketing assistance, market research analysis, processing credit card payments, and providing customer service. These other companies may have access to your personal information as necessary to perform their functions, but they may not share that information with any third party.

Aggregated Information

In general, we gather information about all of our users collectively, such as what areas users visit most frequently and what services users access the most. We only use such data anonymously and in the aggregate. This information helps us determine what is most beneficial for our users, and how we can continually create a better overall user experience. We may share this information with our partners, to create a better overall experience for you, as well.

Personally Identifiable Information

Corp-Corp may collect personally identifiable information from users when:

  • Registering for a corp-corp account (either as a recruiter or a job seeker)
  • Using certain products or services; or when they enter promotions or contests.
  • We may also ask you for other information, such as information on your interests, your likes and dislikes regarding the site, the types of jobs, job seekers, vendors, recruiters you are interested in, etc., in an effort to deliver to you the best possible user experience. Requesting it from you collects this information.

All users should be aware that when they voluntarily display or distribute personal information (such as their e-mail address), that information can be collected from our site and may be used by others. This may result in unsolicited messages form third parties, for which corp-corp.com may not be responsible.

Corp-Corp.com does not collect personally identifying information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals. The site may collect personally identifying information in several ways including:

Registering on our site to use services or to access areas of the site offered only to registered users such as:

  • Submitting a resume
  • Posting a Job
  • Submitting information for editorial purposes, such as news items, letters to the editor, press releases, etc.
  • Using a "Referral" or an "Fwd this to a Friend" features

Submitting information to conduct transactions or use services offered through the website

Uses of Personally Identifying information

When registering as a corp-corp.com user, we may request your email address and other contact information. We may use your email address or fax number to send account statements, invoices, news letters, announcements of service enhancements and new features on the web site, surveys to monitor or improve the use and satisfaction of the site, or to send you marketing or promotional materials from corp-corp.com or our affiliated partners.

Submitting a resume:

Since corp-corp.com is a career site, we give job seekers the option of placing a resume in our online databases. All recruiters who pay for access to the resume database services may access your resume. We use our best efforts to grant access to these database services only to recruiters, headhunters, staffing firms, and employment search consultants. However, we cannot guarantee that other parties will not, without our consent, gain access to these services.

You may remove your resume from our searchable database at any time. However, recruiters who have paid for access to the database services, and other parties who have otherwise gained access to the database services, may have retained a copy of your resume in their own files or databases. We are not responsible for the retention, use, or privacy of resumes in these instances, or for the use or privacy of resumes by any of such parties while resumes are in their database.

Corporate Profiles are provided as a service of corp-corp for the benefit of our registered users. These profiles may be listed and indexed in our site and other search engine directories, which are searchable, by job seekers and corporations. However, we cannot guarantee that other parties will not, without our consent, gain access to these services.



You may remove your profile from our searchable directories at any time. However, job seekers or corporations who have accessed to the directory services, and other parties who have otherwise gained access to the directory services, may have retained a copy of your profile in their own files or databases. We are not responsible for the retention, use, or privacy of resumes in these instances, or for the use or privacy of resumes by any of such parties while resumes are in their database.

When Posting a Job:

Job posting is provided as a service of corp-corp for the benefit of our registered users. These jobs will be listed and indexed in our site as well as search engines, which may be searchable either directly or indirectly by job seekers, other registered users, and corporations. However, we cannot guarantee that other parties will not, without our consent, gain access to these services.

You may remove your jobs from our searchable database at any time. However, job seekers, other registered users, and corporations who have accessed to the directory services, and other parties who have otherwise gained access to the directory services, may have retained a copy of your profile in their own files or databases. We are not responsible for the retention, use, or privacy of resumes in these instances, or for the use or privacy of resumes by any of such parties while resumes are in their database.

When submitted via "Forward this to a Friend" feature:

When a user of corp-corp.com uses this feature, they are asked to supply their friend's name and email address to automatically send the friend a one-time email version of the job. We receive the friend's personally identifying information solely for the purpose of sending the information, we consider the referred person is our prospective user and we may store the information for statistics purpose and may send promotional information about services.

Corp-Corp.com may match specific and/or aggregated Personal Information with third party data to provide you with product and content offerings that interest you and improve our advertising and marketing efforts. Corp-Corp.com also may disclose aggregated user statistics in order to describe our services to potential advertisers, partners, other third parties, our affiliate companies, and for other lawful purposes. Our Sites may operate as part of a larger group of sites, and may carry co-branded pages. In addition, our affiliate sites may use the Site's or corp-corp.com name.

Cookies

What are Cookies?

To enhance your experience at corp-corp.com, we may use a feature on your Internet browser called "cookie". Cookies are small files that your Web browser places on your computer's hard drive. Cookies cannot damage user files, nor can they read information from a user's hard drive. At corp-corp.com, we use cookies only for the protection and convenience of our users. Uses for cookies may include: remembering user names, passwords, and preferences.

Log Files

Corp-Corp.com uses Internet Protocol ("IP") addresses to help diagnose problems with our servers and to administer our web site, for example by blocking certain addresses that are inappropriately accessing or obsessively requesting files from our site. IP addresses are used to gather broad demographics information, such as a visitor's country, operating system, browser types, frequency of visit, etc.

Banner Advertising

Corp-Corp.com may also share web site usage information about visitors to our web site with a third party for the purpose of targeting Internet banner advertisement on this site and other sites. To do this, we may use clear GIFs, or pixel tags, on this site, which allow us to recognize a browser's cookie when a browser visits this site. The information we collect and share through this technology is not personally identifiable.

Opt In / Opt Out Policy

We respect your privacy and your ability to choose whether to share or not share information with Corp-Corp.com or any third party. When jobs seekers or companies opt to register at Corp-Corp.com, a business relationship is created between Corp-Corp.com and that job seeker or company.
