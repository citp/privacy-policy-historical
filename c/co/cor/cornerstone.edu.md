# PRIVACY POLICY STATEMENT - Cornerstone University

Cornerstone University is committed to honoring the privacy of visitors to its websites. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the Cornerstone University websites: 

## Forms 

All personal information filled in and sent to Cornerstone University via forms on its websites will be held strictly confidential. This information may be shared between departments within Cornerstone University, as needed or required.

## Security 

Cornerstone University strictly protects the security of your personal information and honors its intended use. We take appropriate reasonable measures to protect from loss, misuse, unauthorized access or disclosure, alteration, or destruction the information under our control. 

## Information gathering

We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our websites by identifying 

  1. which parts of our site are most heavily used,and 
  2. which portion of our audience comes from within the Cornerstone University network. We do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable. This means that user sessions may be tracked, but the users will remain anonymous. 



## Use of information

Cornerstone University may use the information gathered above to tailor site content to user needs and to generate aggregate statistical reports. 

At no time do we disclose site usage by individual IP addresses. Web server logs are retained on a temporary basis and then deleted from our systems. 

## Contacting us

If you have a concern regarding Cornerstone University Website privacy issues, please contact us and we will do our best to address the matter promptly. Be certain the words Website Privacy Policy are in the Subject line. 

Revised 17 January 2005
