**Collection and Use of Information.**

When you register with Corkd to become an authorized User, you will be required to submit certain personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, gender and city of residence. We may also collect other Information from you from time to time including your username and password. Furthermore, after you have become an authorized User you may elect to voluntarily provide additional information to Corkd which may enable you to better identify yourself to other Corkd Users. You should note that your Information may be embodied or incorporated in your User Content (as defined in the Terms) and that this Privacy Policy shall not limit the rights granted by you in the Terms with respect to any User Content. You may always refuse to supply any Information with it being understood that such refusal may not allow you to access certain portions of the Site.    


We collect such Information to provide our users with a personalized interface. Additionally, Corkd or its affiliates and business partners (including certain wineries) may use your Information to market to you, and provide you with information about their products and services. We adhere to an opt-in/opt-out policy in communicating with our Users and thus, if you no longer desire to receive any such information or communications from us, please follow the unsubscribed instructions provided in any of the communications. Please note, however, that if you register as a User on the Site, you may not opt-out of receiving certain administrative and service-related messages.   


When Users visit and use the Site, the web-servers serving Corkd may automatically collect and record certain information about such User's use of the Site. Such information may include a User's IP address, the domain from which the User is using the Site, the web pages visited by the User and other information relating to the User's use of the Site. Such Information shall be used for administration purposes in connection with an internal review of User's use of the Site. [We also provide this Information (as well as information from third-party market researchers) about our Users on an aggregated, anonymous basis to our advertisers. **Cookies.**

Cookies are small bits of information that may be placed on your computer. Corkd uses cookies to identify your internet browser, store your preferences, and determine whether you have installed the enabling software needed to access certain material on the Site. Data in cookies may be read to authenticate user sessions or provide services. Cookies alone tell us nothing about who you are unless you specifically give us personally identifiable information. By adjusting settings on your browser, you may elect not to allow cookies to be collected. Third party websites linked to on the Site may also contain cookies. Corkd does not control these third party websites or cookies and Users of the Site should check the privacy policy of the third party website to see whether and how it uses cookies.

These companies are only allowed to use the Information in order to help us fulfill our services to you. We do not provide your Information to these companies for their own, permanent use.
