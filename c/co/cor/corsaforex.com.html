Examples also include background information about you we receive from public records or from other entities not affiliated with Corsa Capital. The Anti-Money Laundering Policy requires us to collect information and take actions necessary to verify your identity.
  • Information about your subscriptions, such as signal, alert, news category and price subscriptions.
