Information We Collect

We only collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide to us. If you do not want this information to be collected by us, please do not submit it.

We collect this information so that we can provide you the service and offer personalized features. In most cases, we retain it so that, for instance, you can return to view prior messages you have sent or easily see your contacts list. When you update information, we usually keep a backup copy of the prior version for a reasonable period of time to enable reversion to the prior version of that information.

Additionally, should a user contact CouponPaste.com on their own initiative or respond to a communication from CouponPaste.com, we may collect the information submitted.

Any improper collection or misuse of information provided on CouponPaste.com is a violation of the CouponPaste.com Terms of Use and should be reported to contact@CouponPaste.com.

Cookies, Log Files and Web Beacons
Like most websites, CouponPaste.com uses cookies and web log files to track site usage. A cookie is a tiny data file which resides on your computer which allows CouponPaste.com to recognize you as a user when you return to our site using the same computer and web browser. Like the information you enter at registration or in your profile, cookie and log file data is used to customize your experience on the website. One type of cookie, known as a “persistent” cookie, is set once you’ve logged in to your CouponPaste.com account. The next time you visit the CouponPaste.com website, the persistent cookie will allow us to recognize you as an existing member so that you will not need to log in before using the service. If you log out of the CouponPaste.com website you will need to re-enter your password the next time you visit in order to log in to your account. Another type of cookie, called a “session” cookie, is used to identify a particular visit to the CouponPaste.com website. Session cookies expire after a short time or when you close your web browser.

The cookies we use do not store any personally identifiable information about you. Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser setting to decline cookies. Please be aware, though, that if you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to sign in or use some of the interactive features offered on our websites.

Like most websites as part of the communication standards on the Internet, we use log files on our servers. These log files store various non-personally identifiable data regarding your visit with us, such as the internet protocol (IP) addresses, information regarding your browser, internet service provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, platform type, and number and identification of clicks. This information is used to analyze overall trends to help us improve the CouponPaste.com services. Additionally, this information may also be used to help diagnose problems with our server, to administer the CouponPaste.com services, or to display the content according to your preferences. This and other anonymous information collected may also be shared with business partners and advertisers on an aggregate and anonymous basis. The linkage between your IP address and your personally identifiable information is never shared with third-parties without your permission or except when required by law.

We also use standard Internet technology such as web beacons to track site usage. Web beacons (also known as clear gifs) are tiny graphics with a unique identifier that function similar to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of web users or to access cookies. Unlike cookies which are stored on the user’s computer hard drive, web beacons are embedded invisibly on the web pages (or in e-mail) and are about the size of the period at the end of this sentence. Web beacons are used to track user navigation and usage patterns on our website, and to inform us as to the effectiveness of our content and/or communications. CouponPaste.com does not and cannot use web beacons to obtain personally identifiable information from your computer. If a user follows a link on CouponPaste.com (or in an email or newsletter from CouponPaste.com) to a third-party website, we may use a cookie or web beacon to assign an anonymous, unique identifier to the user’s browser to track the user’s non-personally identifiable activities on that site, including whether the user clicks on a link, or makes a purchase. If the user re-visits the third-party site directly, we are able to continue to track the user’s non-personally identifiable activities on that site.

These general targets may also be used by ad networks to display targeted ads on other websites as a substitute for random, irrelevant ads. CouponPaste.com NEVER provides personally identifiable information to any advertiser nor any third-party without your express permission.
