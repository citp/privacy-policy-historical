    Privacy Policy

    Information We Collect 

    When you shop with CoutureCandy you must submit the minimal required information necessary to complete your transaction. This information may include your name, address, telephone number, and email address.

    When you use the website in any capacity, such as registering as a customer, participating in one of our promotions or sweepstakes, or purchasing products, we may ask for additional information, including your age, interests, hobbies, product preferences, and favorite designers. Giving us this information is optional and serves the purpose of us being able to provide the most desirable services and products to our customers.

    To improve our website, we collect non-personal information about how users navigate through the site. We may share this information with our affiliates or other outside companies.

    How We Use the Information We Collect

    We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

    • to complete your purchase
    • to provide the services you request
    • to send you offerings by email
    • to keep you informed about the status of your orders
    • to identify your product and service preferences, so we can notify you of new or additional products, services, and promotions that might be of interest to you
    • to notify you of product recalls or provide other information concerning products you have purchased
    • to improve our merchandise selection, customer service, and overall shopping experience

    Others with Whom We Share Your Information

    When a product you have purchased is fulfilled through a vendor, we will send the information necessary to box and ship your product. Personal credit card information is never released to any company outside CoutureCandy.

    • Newsletter Emails: We do not share our newletter email list with anyone.
    • We do not re-sell personal information with any company or person.
    • We do not share personal information unless necessary to fulfill your order (ie. shipping information).

    Secure Ordering

    Your online credit card transactions are secure with CoutureCandy. We use Secure Socket Layers (SSL), the industry-standard encryption technology that ensures safe Internet transmission of your personal information. When the letters "http" in the URL change to "https," the "s" indicates you are in a secure area employing SSL; also, your browser may give you a pop-up message that you are about to enter a secure area.

    Making a credit card purchase is just as safe on our website as it is by telephone, but if you prefer the latter, you may purchase online merchandise by calling toll-free 1-866-399-2263. Our Customer Care Department will gladly assist you.

    Privacy Policy

    To achieve our goal of providing the highest quality products and services, we may use information from our interactions with you and other parties interacting with CoutureCandy. Because we respect your privacy, we have procedures to ensure that your personal information is handled in a safe, secure, and responsible manner.

    As we continue to develop our website and take advantage of advances in technology, our privacy policy may change. Please refer to this page frequently for the most updated information.

    This Privacy Policy has been in effect since 6/14/2005.

    Your Choices Regarding Use of the Information We Collect

    Email and mail is an 'opt-in' offering of CoutureCandy. If you have previously opted into a feature that you no longer wish receive, you can change your preferences in the My Account section of CoutureCandy. Alternatively, you can contact customer care and they will be happy to assist you with 'opting-out'.

    Please note that some promotions and mailings may be completed in advance of your request to stop sharing information, so you may continue to receive some offers for a short time afterward. We appreciate your patience in giving us time to carry out your request.

    Cookies and How We Use Them

    A "cookie" is a small data file stored by your Web browser on your computer's hard drive. It allows us to recognize your computer (but not specifically who is using it) upon entering our site by associating the identification numbers in the cookie with other customer information you have provided us. That customer information is stored on our secured server and database.

    We use cookies to identify the pages you click on during your visit to our site and the name of the Web site you visited immediately before clicking to CoutureCandy. We use this information to improve our site design, product assortments, customer service, and special promotions.

    We may contract with third parties who use cookies to collect information on our behalf or who provide services such as credit card processing, shipping, promotional services or data management. These third parties are prohibited by our contract from sharing that information with anyone other than CoutureCandy or our vendors.

    Children's Privacy

    The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act took effect on April 21, 2000. It imposes certain requirements on websites directed at children under 13 that collect information on those children, and on websites that knowingly collect information on children under 13.

    We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 on our website. Our online sweepstakes are restricted to entrants who are at least 15 years of age.

    No one under the age of 15 should submit any personal information to CoutureCandy without the consent of a parent or guardian.

    Links to Other Sites

    While visiting CoutureCandy, you may follow links to websites operated by our business partners or other third-parties. A link from CoutureCandy to a third-party site does not mean that we endorse this website. We do not make any representations or warranties about any website you may access from our website; Third party websites are independent from us, and we have no control over, or responsibility for, their information or activities. You need to make your own independent decisions regarding your interactions or communications with any other website.

    If you provide personal information at a third-party website, you are subject to the privacy policy of the operator of that website and not the CoutureCandy privacy policy. We encourage you to read that policy before submitting any personal information.

    Online Account Registration

    To make online shopping faster and easier, you must register with CoutureCandy. As a registered customer, you only have to enter your shipping addresses and billing information and they will be securely stored with us for your future use. Using your name and a password of your choice, you may access your account online to add, delete, or change information.

    If you are using a public computer, we strongly encourage you to log out at the conclusion of your session to maintain the integrity of your account. If you log out, your information will still be stored with us but it will not be accessible to anyone else from that computer.

    Emails

    Each time you receive an email from CoutureCandy, you will be given the opportunity to "opt-out" to stop receiving future emails.

    If you choose to "opt-out," we will stop sending you emails. Please note, however, that some email promotions are prepared well in advance, so you may continue to receive offers after you have chosen to "opt-out." We appreciate your patience in giving us time to carry out your request.

    Please also note, that if you have chosen to "opt-out", you still may receive emails from us, our affiliates, or shipping services, solely related to your online purchases with CoutureCandy.

    Any Questions?

    We'll be happy to provide additional information or answer any questions. Click here for information on how to contact CoutureCandy.

    SHIPPING, RETURNS, & EXCHANGES POLICIES:

    Free Ground Shipping on all orders over $149 within the Continental US.

    Every attempt will be made to process and ship your order the day it's received. All US orders are shipped ground via UPS or USPS and should be received within 1-4 business days after processing. Delivery time does not include Saturdays, Sundays or holidays.

    Please note that there is a different return policy for Evening Dresses.

     

    PROCESSING GUIDELINES

    All orders are processed pending availability and credit card verification. Most orders placed Monday through Friday at 9:00 am PST will begin processing on the same day. Orders placed on Friday after 9:00 am PST and through the weekend will begin processing on the following Monday.

    CoutureCandy will contact you if it is unable to process and ship your order for any reason, and if additional identification is needed for credit card verification. 

    Most orders are processed within 24 hours. Processing times may be longer for certain items, as referenced on an item's product page.

     

    Domestic Shipping Costs and Methods:

     

    • Free Shipping on US orders over $149
    • Shipping fee of $7.95 for order under $149
    • Delivery by Ground Shipping in 1-4 days
    • Within the Continental United States
    • Evening Dresses take 2-5 business days

     

    Standard Ground Shipping - to Alaska, Hawaii & Canada 

    • Delivery within in 2-5 business days
    • Hawaii $7.95
    • Alaska $7.95
    • Canada $19.95
    • Allow additional time for order processing (see Processing Guidelines)

     

    Expedited 2-Day Shipping

    • Delivery within 2 business days
    • Shipping fee of $24.95 applies
    • Weekend Delivery is not available
    • Allow additional time for order processing (see Processing Guidelines)
    • Shipping to P.O. Box/APO/FPO addresses is based upon confirmation by CoutureCandy Fraud Protection Review.

     

    International Shipping 

    • Delivery within in 5-8 business days
    • Generally an International Shipping fee of $39.95 applies to all orders. Some exceptions apply*
    • International shipping charges do not include taxes or duties. Please contact your local customs office to request details of these charges.

     

     

    International Shipping Policies 

    International shipping fees range from $39.95 - $99.95 USD, depending on the country you live in, the shipping service you select, and the weight of your package. If you exchange an item, you will be responsible for shipping charges that apply to the reshipment of the item.

    International orders are subject to verification upon request. First time international customers may need to submit a copy of their passport to by emailing a scanned copy to cs@couturecandy.com. We apologize for any inconvenience however this is necessary for protection against fraudulent orders. 

    CoutureCandy accepts no risk of merchandise loss due to international shipment. All risk passes to you upon our delivery to the carrier. You may be subject to import duties and taxes once a shipment reaches your country. Additional customs clearance charges must be paid by you; CoutureCandy has no control over these charges and cannot predict what they may be. International orders may be subject to customs clearance procedures which may cause delays beyond original delivery estimates.

    Customs policies vary widely from country to country; contact your local customs office for more information. When ordering from CoutureCandy, you are considered the "importer of record" and must comply with all laws and regulations of the country in which you are receiving the goods.

     

    Refused Shipments

    If you choose to refuse your shipments from CoutureCandy, you will be responsible for any duties, taxes and/or customs charges that are incurred on the package, and the cost of returning the package to CoutureCandy. This amount will be deducted from your merchandise refund.

     

    Privacy 

    CoutureCandy takes the utmost of care to ensure your privacy. We would like our international customers to understand that international shipments are subject to opening and inspection by customs authorities. Also, we are required to provide customs authorities with basic shipment and product information.

     

    Hassle Free Return Policy:

    We at the CoutureCandy.com want you to be fully satisfied with every item that you purchase. If you are not fully satisfied with an item that you have purchased, you may return the item(s) within 14 days for a full refund of the purchase price, minus the shipping, handling or other charges to the card used to make the purchase. Some items may have a shorter return period and re-stocking fees, please make sure to check item (s) description first prior to ordering.

    To return an item (s) please email: cs@couturecandy.com

    A customer service representative will provide you with a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA), as well as email a return shipping label to enjoy a hassle free return process. Please note if you chose to use the provided return shipping label the return shipping fees will be deducted from the credit issued to the card used to make your original purchase.

    If you received a damaged or defective item please contact us immediately by emailing: cs@couturecandy.com

    Please do not refuse the package or attempt to return it yourself prior to contacting us. We will remedy the situation for you!

    All item/s must be unworn and unaltered, and all original tags must be attached. Laundered, smoked in, used and worn items will not be accepted.

    After your request has been submitted and both the RMA and return label electronically received by email, please place the label on the outside of the package in insure safe delivery. If you chose to use your own shipping carrier make sure to send back via a traceable method having first obtained a return tracking number and RMA from CoutureCandy.com customer service.

    Returns will not be accepted if you do not first obtain a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) from cs@couturecandy.com.

     

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------

    General Sale Return Policy:

    CoutureCandy accepts returns for Sale merchandise unless otherwise noted on the specific product page as FINAL SALE. All item/s must be unworn and unaltered, and all original tags must be attached. You have 14 days (unless otherwise noted on product page) to return your item (s) for a full refund to the credit card used to make your purchase.

    Any returns which are received after 14 days will be accessed a 10% restocking fee, returns received after 30 days are assessed a 20% restocking fee, and all products received after 60 days will be returned to sender.

    Sale Returns for products with a 30% off discount or more:

    CoutureCandy.com accepts returns which are sold with a discount of 30% or more, however we apply a 10% restocking fee. This fee will be deducted from the refunded amount and applied to the original form of payment. Shipping fees, if any, which were applied at purchase are not refunded.

    All Sale Products 70% off or more are Final Sale and will not be refunded:

    Special Occasion / Evening Dress Return Policy

    This policy applies to the following brands:

    Allure Bridals, Alfred Sung, After Six, Atria, Brit Cameron, BG Haute, Baccio Couture, Cassandra Stone, Dessy, Emma Street, Janique, Jasz Couture, Johnathan Kayne, Jolene & Josh + Jazz, Lara, Lela Rose, Mac Duggal brands, Madison James, MNM Couture, MIGNON, Nicole Bakti, Nina Canacci, 57 Grand, SCALA, Sue Wong, Savee Couture, Sydney's Closet, Tony Bowls.

    110% PRICE MATCHING GUARANTEE & FREE SHIPPING

    Return your evening dress hassle free to us within 3 business days of receiving your package - no questions asked. Upon receipt of your purchase, we will provide the choice of either a Store Credit for the original cost of the item less the freight charges if shipped for free or a Refund to the credit card used to make the purchase less a 10% restocking fee, and any original freight charges. Store credit is valid for one year if chosen. All products must be in original condition, unworn, and with tags attached.

    Requesting a Return Authorization (RMA)

    To return a Evening Dress please contact cs@couturecandy.com for a return authorization number (RMA) and request either Store Credit or Refund. Write the RMA number on the outside of your return package. Our support team will issue you a Return Shipping Label for your convenience usually within one business day of receiving your request and deduct the return shipping amount from your store credit or refund if applicable.

    Packages returned without first obtaining an RMA number will be accepted, but a penalty of 25% percent of the purchase amount will apply so please email our Customer Service team to receive an RA first before returning your package!

    An additional 10% fee will be applied to your Refund or Store Credit if your package is returned after 3 business days of receiving your RMA from cs@couturecandy.com.

    Return Shipping Label (RSL) if used is deducted from your Refund or Store Credit.

     

    Store Credit

    At CoutureCandy.com we want you to be 100% satisfied. Your store credit can be used on any product sitewide without restrictions. Store credits once issued may be redeemed only one time at which point merchandise purchased with a store credit becomes a Final Sale.

    To use your store credit, simply sign into your account, and checkout as you normally would. At the payment step of checking out, your store credit will automatically be applied.

     

    110% Price Match Guarantee!

    We offer a price match of 110% on special occasion dresses. If you find the same dress from an authorized retailer we will deduct 10% from your purchase price.

    To be eligible for a price match, the retailer must be authorized by the designer to sell the item at that price, and they must have the item in stock in that same color and size. This policy excludes sale items.

     

    Return Shipping Labels

    Return Shipping Labels are issued to US customers only. All international customers are responsible to pay any and all fees when shipping an item back. (Duties, taxes, etc) - we recommend UPS and select Free Domicile when completing the shipping label.

     

    Order Cancellations

    We understand that you may change your mind about an item selected. We will gladly cancel any order as long as it has not been processed and shipped. If your selection has been processed and shipped, the order will follow our return policy listed above.

     

    Final Sale

    Because of the nature of Special Order products, Clearance Dresses, and Bridal Dresses we cannot accommodate any requests for returns or exchanges.

    Also, all items purchased with an issued store credit become final sale.

     

    Damaged Merchandise

    If your dress is damaged when you receive it, please contact cs@couturecandy.com the day you receive it for a return label and we will replace it.

    Your satisfaction is our top priority. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we will take every measure possible to deliver on our customer promise. Please use our instant chat, email, or toll free number and our staff will do everything we can to accommodate you.  

     

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------

    Swimwear Return Policy

    Items must have all original tags and hygienic liners still attached. Please see our fit guides before purchasing from any of our swimwear brands. A complete refund of the purchase price will be applied to your credit card if returned within 14 days of delivery. Any products returned after this period will be accessed a 10% restocking fee. No refunds to the credit card can be given after 30 days from the date of purchase for swimwear purchases.

     

    Price Match Guarantee

    CoutureCandy.com agrees to price match up to 110% of the price of a US authorized brand retailers price for all products with the same size, style, and color listed on the CoutureCandy.com site with the "110% Price Match" text ad within the price field of the product. Not all product are eligible for price matching so please refer to the product page or call Customer Service if you are unsure for qualification.

     

    Exchanges:

    If you want to exchange your item for another size just email cs@couturecandy.com. One of our staff will be happy to place a new order for you and provide a RMA for your previous purchase following our standard return policy. Please note re-stocking fees may apply, and the shipping cost of returning your original purchase will be deducted from your refunded amount.
