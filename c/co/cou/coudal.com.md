# Coudal Partners

#### CP Privacy Policy 

This Privacy Policy explains the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information by Coudal Partners, an Illinois Corporation. 400 North May Street, Chicago 60622. 

We collect personal information when you provide it to us by sending an email to us or signing up to receive a newsletter or to participate in forum discussions or when you buy one of our products. 

Site usage information is occasionally collected indirectly and automatically about your activities while visiting our sites, including what pages you view and when you view them and information about the browser you are using. We never link browsing information to the personal information you submit to us. Site usage information is used to help us understand how our sites are being used and for systems administration purposes. 

It is not Coudal Partners' practice to disclose personal information to third parties at any time. The only exception is if we are required by law or required by court order to do so. 

Coudal Partners has implemented reasonable measures to protect against unauthorized access to and unlawful interception or processing of personal information that we control. Questions? contact info at coudal dot com. We may revise these policies at any time.
