# Privacy Policy – The Blog Builders™

The information you give to me will not be given to a third party, but will be used to contact you. I may use this information to tell you about recommended products and services and I may send you promotional material.

**Use Of Personal Information**

I may use your personal information to and/or for:

  * Analyze the effectiveness of this website
  * Administer the site
  * Make changes and/or personalize the website for you
  * Enable or disable your access to the website
  * Send you products that you have purchased
  * Deliver services that you may have purchased
  * Send invoices
  * Setting up remarketing campaigns
  * Creating lists of categories you’re interested in
  * Grouping you with similar audiences
  * other types of interest-based advertising



**Securing Your Information**

The Blog Builders™ will take reasonable and technical precautions to prevent the loss, misuse, and/or alteration of your information.

**Updating This Policy**

The Blog Builders™ may make changes to this policy at any time by posting a new version on this website. Check this page occasionally if you want to remain aware of what is contained in this policy.
