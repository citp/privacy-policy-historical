"Personally Identifiable Information" refers to any information that identifies or can be used to identify, contact, or locate the person to whom such information pertains including, but not limited to, name, address, phone number, fax number, email address, financial profiles, social security number, and credit card information. Personally Identifiable Information does not include information that is collected anonymously (i.e., without identification of the individual user) or demographic information not connected to an identified individual.

1.

What Personally Identifiable Information is collected?

The Site collects email addresses and names from Visitors who use certain features. The Site collects the following information from its Visitors who apply to become Members: the email address(es) of the Member. In addition, the following optional information may be collected: name, gender, age, industry, job, and other information related to the work history of the Member. This information is collected in order for the Site to offer its services effectively.

2.

What organization is collecting the information?

COWORKERS.COM collects and securely stores the information submitted by Members in their Member Profile. COWORKERS.COM is owned and operated by Coworkers.com, Inc., a Delaware (USA) corporation. Our third party service vendors who may provide such services as online payment may collect other information from our Members of the Site. We do not control how these third parties use such information.

3.

How is the information used?

We use personal information to customize the Site and to make appropriate service offerings to Users.  We also use personal information to contact you in response to specific inquiries you have made to us, or to provide you with specific information and services you have requested or agreed to be notified about.

4.

With whom may the information be shared?

We may share aggregated information about our Members, including the demographics of our Members with our directors, agencies and third party vendors. We may only share personal information about you with third parties if you have permitted us to do so. We also offer you the opportunity to "opt out" of receiving information or being contacted by us or by any agency acting on our behalf.

By default, any optional, personal information that you have entered in your account profile (such as name and sex) may be viewed by other Users.  However, you have the option of "opting out" of this feature, which makes your profile invisible to other Users.

5.

Are Cookies used on the Site?

Other than temporary "session cookies", we use cookies only to "remember" the user name you last used to log in, if you enable this option.  Persistent cookies are not mandatory for the Site.

6.

How is logging information used?

We use logging information (including IP addresses, ISPs, and browser type) derived from a User's visits to the Site may and link that information to personally identifiable information. We use this logging information to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement and use, and gather broad demographic information.

7.

Is submission of personal information optional?

Visitors are not required to submit personal information to use the Site.  However, there are portions of the Site limited to Members only. Members are required to provide some Personal Information. At a minimum, an email address must be provided.

8.

What partners or service providers have access to personal information from users and/or members on the Site?

COWORKERS.COM may engage in special partnerships with certain vendors.  Such vendors may have access to certain personal information on a need to know basis for such purposes as to evaluate Member for service eligibility. Our privacy statement and policy covers their collection of this information.

9.

Disclosure of personal information to comply with law

We will disclose personal information in order to comply with a Court Order or subpoena, or a request from a law enforcement agency to release information. We will also disclose personal information where reasonably necessary to protect the safety of our Users and Members.

10.

How does the Site keep personal information secure?

Our employees are made aware of our security policy and practices.  The personal information of our Users and Members is only accessible to a limited number of qualified employees who are given a password in order to gain access to that information. We audit our security systems and processes on a regular basis.  Sensitive information, such as passwords, is protected by encryption protocols, in place to protect information sent over the Internet.

11.

How can Members correct any inaccuracies in the information?

Members may update their personal information to correct any inaccuracies by navigating to the Account page at http://www.coworkers.com/account.

12.

Can a Member delete or deactivate information collected by the Site?

We provide Members with a mechanism to delete/deactivate personal information from the site's database upon request by contacting us at http://www.coworkers.com/contact.  However, because of backups, and records of deletions, it may be impossible to delete a user's entry without some residual information. An individual who requests to have Personally Identifiable Information deactivated will be functionally deleted, and we will not sell, transfer, or use Personally Identifiable Information relating to that individual in any way moving forward.

13.

What happens if the Privacy Policy changes?

We will let our Users know about changes to our privacy practices by posting such changes on the Site. However, if we are changing the policy in a manner that might cause disclosure of your personal information, which you have previously asked not to be disclosed, we will contact you to notify you of the change and allow you to prevent such disclosure.
