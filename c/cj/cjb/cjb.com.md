# Privacy Policy

CJB Management, Inc., producer of this web site and its related content and service sites, believes that electronic privacy is crucial for the ongoing success of the Internet as a service, commercial, and entertainment medium.

#### Personal Information

On our web site, and through our software, CJB Management, Inc. will not collect any personally-identifiable information about you unless you provide it to us voluntarily. If you do not want your personal information collected, please do not submit it.

#### Cookies

The use of advertising cookies sent by third-party servers is standard in the Internet industry. You may occasionally get cookies from our advertisers.

#### Advertising

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, e-mail address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click [here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/).

#### Traffic Data

Each time a visitor comes to a CJB Management, Inc. site, information is collected to improve the overall quality of CJB Management, Inc. sites. CJB Management, Inc. collects the visitor's domain name/IP Address (e.g., whether the user is logged on from netscape.com or yale.edu or 255.255.255.0), referral data (e.g., we record the address of the last URL a user visited prior to clicking through to a CJB Management, Inc. site), and browser/platform type (e.g., a Netscape browser on a Macintosh platform).

In addition to collecting these types of information, CJB Management, Inc.'s advertisement delivery system collects aggregate search terms for internal reporting and targeted advertising. CJB Management, Inc.'s advertisement delivery system collects visitor information for purposes of product monitoring, product improvement, and targeted advertising.

CJB Management, Inc. also counts, tracks, and aggregates the visitor's activity into our analysis of general traffic flows at CJB Management, Inc. sites (e.g., tracking where traffic comes from and how traffic flows within the CJB Management, Inc. sites). CJB Management, Inc. may merge this information into group data, which may then be shared on an aggregated basis with potential advertisers, research firms, and business partners. CJB Management, Inc. will not disclose your individual identity or personal data. When CJB Management, Inc. does present aggregated information to potential advertisers, research firms and business partners, no one will be able to identify you or contact you.

#### Resale

CJB Management, Inc. does not resell any personally-identifiable information collected at our site to third parties.
