# CFSD Online Privacy Notice 2/15/2010

CFSD Online Privacy Notice                                                                  2/15/2010
Our Commitment To Privacy
Your privacy is important to us. Catalina Foothills School District respects the privacy of all visitors to
the district web site at http://www.cfsd16.org and protects your privacy to the extent permitted by law.
You may read and download information at the CFSD web site without identifying yourself or
providing any personal information.
This privacy statement discloses what information this web site collects and how district employees
use that information. The CFSD web site collects three types of information:
    o Information you voluntarily provide through email messages, web forms, and surveys.
    o Information automatically gathered through web visit logs.
    o Information collected by network traffic logs.
Online Privacy Policy Limitations
This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through our website and not to
information collected offline.
Voluntary Information
We do not collect any personal information about you unless you voluntarily choose to provide it to us
by sending email, filling out online surveys, or completing online forms. You are not required to
participate in any of these activities to view or download any information from our web site. Your
responses to an online survey will be used to fulfill the purpose of that survey. Any personal
information that you voluntarily provide to us through email messages, surveys, or forms will only be
used to respond properly to your request. CFSD does not share, disclose, or sell email addresses or
phone numbers to other organizations.
Web Visit Logs
The CFSD web site automatically collects and stores the following standard information when you
visit our web site:
        o The web pages you visited at our site.
        o The date and time you visited the site.
        o The web browser and operating system you used.
        o The Internet protocol address and domain name used to connect to our services.
        o The web site you visited prior to coming to this site.
This information is stored on our servers for the time necessary to perform site analysis and
maintenance. We analyze this information to personalize your experience, to improve our website
and to improve customer service. This information is not personally identifiable, and we make no
attempt to use it to identify individual web site visitors.
Network Traffic Logs
To secure the district network and provide reliable service, CFSD uses software programs to monitor
network traffic, scan for computer viruses, and identify attempts to access the network without
authorization. Any information gathered through these activities, such as network-packet addresses
or email-headers, is used solely for the purpose of maintaining network security. Personally
identifiable information gathered through these activities is not released to other parties unless
required by law.


Cookies
The CFSD web site does not use cookies to track user information.
Links to Other Sites
This web site provides links to other web sites. CFSD is not responsible for the privacy policies of
other web sites, and CFSD's privacy statement does not apply when you visit these other sites.
These third party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. Nonetheless, we seek to
protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites. We recommend that you
be aware when you leave our web site and encourage you to read the privacy statements of other
web sites you visit.
Changes to our Privacy Policy
If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page.
