CyberAlert's Privacy Policy 

Privacy Commitment

Your privacy is important to us. This notice explains our online information practices to better protect your privacy. and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. We make this notice available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.

The Information We Collect

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the CyberAlert Web site. On some pages, you can order products, make requests, and register to receive materials. The types of personal information collected at these pages are: Name, Title, Company Name, Address, Company Type, Email Address, Phone Number, Fax and media monitoring specifications.

The Way We Use Information

We use the information you provide about yourself only to facilitate your business dealings with CyberAlert. We do not share this information with outside parties except to complete an order you place. IF you provide information about someone else, that information is used only to deliver the services or materials you ask to be delivered. We do not share the information you provide with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete your request or order. We use return e-mail addresses to answer the e-mail we receive and for further contact with you. Such addresses are not shared with outside parties.

Commitment To Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have implemented appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
