# CS:GO Stats - Privacy Policy

#### Information We Collect and How We Use Them

Only non-personal identification information may be collected when users interact with our site. Such information includes browser names, operating systems, countries, internet service providers, and other technical details. These data are completely anonymous, and their sole purpose is to help us improve the experience of the site for the users. We do not sell, trade, or rent any information.

  


#### Use of Cookies

We integrate Steam OpenID on our website. This allows us to provide users who signed in a personalized experience.

In addition, third parties such as advertisers might also store cookies in the web browser. These cookies allow advertisers to recognize your computer with non-personal identification information. You may choose to opt-out of this feature by disabling cookies in your browser. Disabling cookies has zero impact on your experience with this website.

  


#### Google Adsense

We use Google Adsense to serve advertisements on this website. Google Adsense use cookies to recognize your non-personal identification information here and on other sites. Your personal information such as name, address, are never tracked. You may choose to opt-out of this feature on Google's official website.

  


#### Google Analytics 

We use Google Analytics to analyze users interactions with this website. Only non-personal identification information are collected, and we only use these information to improve this website.

  


#### Acceptance and Changes to This Page

By using this site, you agree to all the terms on this page. Any changes to this page will be notified to the users here.

  


#### Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/contact)
