**PRIVACY POLICY**

CSN is committed to protecting your privacy when you visit our web site and we are providing this document to inform you about information we may collect when you visit one of our sites, how we may use that information, and how you can update it should we use your information. CSN reserves the right to append or otherwise modify this privacy policy at any time, so please visit this page to check for updates.

**INFORMATION WE MAY COLLECT  
**While we do not collect information by default, we may collect various types of information from our visitors only through feedback/contact forms that may be available for completion and submission solely at each visitor's discretion. We do not use cookies or any other type of automatic information collection. Your Internet use outside of [www.csn.edu](http://www.csn.edu/) is not recorded or monitored by us in any way.

**  
HOW WE USE INFORMATION**  
CSN does not sell, trade or otherwise transfer any personal data submitted by visitors to our site to any third parties. We respect the privacy of users visiting our sites and will abide by all applicable laws concerning the release of personal information. On occasion we may use certain contact information that we have collected to send you information about programs and services, or updates and other information we think may be of interest to you. Please write to CSN using the information below if you would like to request that your name be removed from our lists. We will not share your personal data with anyone else, except as may be required by law.
