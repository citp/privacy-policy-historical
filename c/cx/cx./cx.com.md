## CX Privacy Policy

CX provides this Privacy Policy (the “Policy”) to inform you of our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personal information we receive from users of www.CX.com (the “Site”).  This Policy applies only to information that the user (“You”) provides to CX via the Site.  Our Policy may be updated from time to time; any material changes shall me made available on the Site at: [https://www.cx.com/privacy-policy](http://www.cx.com/privacy-policy "CX Privacy Policy").

## 1\. Information Collected

Through use of the Site, You may provide us with personally identifiable information, i.e., information about You that may be used to contact or identify you and information on Your use of our Site that may be connected with You (“Information”).  Information that we collect may include, but is not limited to, your name, phone number, billing information, email address and home and business addresses. Personal Information may also include information You supply to us concerning your preferences with respect to the use of our Site.

When You visit the Site, our servers record data that your browser sends when you visit a website.  This information may include items such as your computer’s internet protocol address, browser type and information you search for on our Site.  We may also use “cookies” to collect information.  CX may use “persistent cookies” to save your registration ID and login password for future logins to the Site; and CX uses “session ID cookies” to enable certain features of the Site, to better understand how You use the Site and to monitor web traffic routing on the Site. You can instruct your browser to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites You visit.  If You do not accept cookies, however, You may not be able to use all portions of the Site.

## 2\. How We Use Information

Information may be used for the following purposes: (i) to improve our Site, services, features and content, (ii) to administer your use of our Site, (iii) to better understand your needs and interests, (iv) to fulfill requests You may make, (v) to personalize your experience, (vi) to offer software updates and/or product announcements, and (vii) to provide You with further information and offers from us or third parties that You may find interesting, including marketing or promotional materials.  If You decide at any time that you no longer wish to receive any such communications, please follow the “unsubscribe” instructions provided in any of the communications sent to you, or update your “account settings” information.

We use Information we obtain by technical means (through the use of cookies, e.g.) for the above purposes and, including but not limited to the following additional purposes: (i) to monitor and analyze use of the Site and our services, (ii) for the Site’s technical administration, (iii) to improve our Site’s functionality and user-friendliness, (iv) to better customize the Site to your needs, to generate and derive useful data and information concerning the interests and characteristics of our users, and (v) to verify that visitors to the Site meet the criteria required to process their requests.

## 3\. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Users. CX will display your Information on your profile page and elsewhere on the Site according to the preferences You set.  You can review and revise your profile information at any time.

Service Providers, Business Partners and Others. CX may employ third party entities to assist in facilitation of our service, to provide the service on our behalf, to perform Site-related services (including but not limited to data storage, maintenance, database management, web analytics and payment processing) or to assist us in analyzing how our Site and service are used. These third parties have access to your Information strictly for purposes of performing these tasks on our behalf.

Compliance with Laws and Protection of CX’s Rights. CX may disclose to third parties files stored with CX and information about You that we collect when we have a good faith belief that disclosure is reasonably necessary to (a) comply with any law, regulation or compulsory legal request; (b) protect the safety of any person from death or serious bodily injury; (c) prevent fraud or abuse of CX or its users; or (d) to protect CX’s property rights.  If we provide your files to a law enforcement agency as set forth above, we will remove any CX encryption from the files prior providing them to law enforcement.  However, CX will not be able to decrypt any files that You encrypted prior to storing them on CX.

Business Transfers.  CX may sell, transfer or otherwise share some or all of its assets, including your Information, in connection with a merger, acquisition, reorganization or sale of assets or in the event of bankruptcy.

## 4\. Changing or Deleting Your Information

You may review, update, correct or delete the Information provided in your registration or account profile by changing your account settings.

## 5\. Security

CX considers safeguarding your information paramount. CX employs reasonable measures designed to protect your Information from unauthorized access.

## 6\. Our Policy Toward Children

This Site is not intended for use by children under 18 years of age.  CX does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.  If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with Information without their consent, they should contact us at support@cx.com.  If CX becomes aware that a child under 13 has provided us with Information, CX will delete such information from our files.

## 7\. Contacting Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@cx.com.
