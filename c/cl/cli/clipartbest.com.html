Privacy Policy

The owners and operators of ClipartBest take your online privacy seriously. This document outlines the types of information collected by our servers and provides links to the privacy policies of our third-party advertising partners.

Personally Identified Data

We strongly discourage users from entering their full name, phone number, physical address, or other sensitive information in user profiles, comments, or other areas of the site. Furthermore, we request that minors and any individuals with limited decision making ability not create an account on this site without the approval and supervision of a guardian.

We make a reasonable attempt to monitor for cases where users post personally identifiable information about other individuals to public portions of the web site and remove the offending material.

Server Logs

Like most web sites, ClipartBest logs web, database, and other server-software usage and access information. This information may include your internet protocol (IP) address, which in many cases can be translated to an affiliation (such as your work, school, or internet service provider), or a geographical location. We only use this information for debugging purposes and for aggregating into anonymous usage and traffic statistics.

Cookies

While browsing ClipartBest, a small number of text files referred to as "cookies" are created on your local file system by your web browser at the request of our servers. These files allow us to personalize the browsing experience. You are free to delete them at any time, or configure your browser not to create them. However, we do not guarantee that every feature of ClipartBest will function as expected without cookies enabled. Aggregate cookie and tracking information may be shared with third parties.

Why do we use cookies?

ClipartBest uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Statistical Analysis

    We perform statistical, demographic and marketing analyses of users of ClipartBest for product development purposes and to generally inform advertisers about the nature of our user base. We use this information for analysis purposes, including analysis to improve user experience.

  • Relevant Advertising

    We may use demographic and preference information to allow advertising on ClipartBest to be targeted to the users for whom they are most pertinent. This means users see advertising that is most likely to interest them, and advertisers send their messages to people who are most likely to be receptive, improving both the viewer's experience and the effectiveness of the ads. We disclose information to third parties only in aggregate or de-identified form.
