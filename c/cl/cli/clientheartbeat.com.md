# Privacy Policy - Client Heartbeat

Client Heartbeat Pty Ltd is committed to protecting your privacy.

This privacy policy describes the privacy practices for the Client Heartbeat (the "Site"), located at http://www.clientheartbeat.com). Client Heartbeat Pty Ltd owns and operates this Site.

## Information we collect

We may collect the following information when you register on or fill out a form on our site:

  1. First name, Last Name and/or Business Names 
  2. E-mail address
  3. Company Details
  4. Email addresses of your Customers
  5. First name, Last Name and/or Business Names of your Customers
  6. Other General Pieces of Information on your Business or your Customers



## Collected information uses

Any information provided by you is used to understand your needs and provide a useful service. We may use your information for the following reasons:

  1. To deliver you a useful product with valuable insights
  2. To send periodic e-mails and to contact you for market research purposes
  3. To personalize your website experience according to your interests
  4. To improve our products or services based on your needs



## Collected information security

In order to prevent any unauthorised access or disclosure of the personal information that we gather, we have in place electronic and managerial procedures to secure and guard these information.

## Links to other websites

The Site may contain links to other websites of interest. We cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which you provide while visiting such sites; such sites are not goverened by this privacy statement.
