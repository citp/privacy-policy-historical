PRIVACY POLICY QUESTIONS

1\. USERNAME

1.1. We may display your username within our site. However, to ensure your privacy, we will not needlessly expose it to the other people.

1.2. Your username will be displayed in our forum whenever you make a new post.

1.3. Your username can be displayed on different forums and social medias of ours if you have participated in one of our contest and have won one of the prizes.

2\. USER PASSWORDS

2.1. Your password will be stored in an irreversible format.

2.2. Your password will never be shown, sold or shared. Its sole purpose is to validate your ownership of your account.

3\. USER E-MAILS

3.1. Your e-mail address will never be shown, sold or shared!

3.2. Your personal e-mail address will only be used by ClickForBTC for communicating with you in regards to your support tickets, lost passwords, PIN recovery or some important official announcements.

3.3. Your Payment Processor e-mail address / account ID will only be used by ClickForBTC for receiving payments from you and sending them to you.

4\. COOKIES

4.1. Your browser must accept cookies.

4.2. Cookies will be used for storing your preferences.

4.3. Cookies can also be used for security purposes.

4.3. Cookies are used for storing "referral information" within the context of our affiliate program.

5\. ADVERTISEMENTS

5.1. ClickForBTC is not responsible for the content of the advertisements placed on our site. This includes, but is not limited to: user posts, advertisements and banner ads.

5.2. Clicking on an advertisement is your own responsibility. ClickForBTC is not liable for any problem/issue/damage caused by you visiting an advertisement or browsing the content of an advertiser.

5.3. All advertisements are the responsibility of the corresponding advertisers, and you must comply with their own Terms of Service and Privacy Policies.

5\. PERSONAL

6.1. ClickForBTC may collect some personal information from the users such as Full Name, Post Code, Year of Birth and Gender This information is only used to provide surveys trough our survey section

6.2. We might provide this information for the 3rd parties providing the surveys to our users.

6.3. Should you wish to delete your information we ask that you contact our support and we will honor your request. Do note however surveys will not be available then.
