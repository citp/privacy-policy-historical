# ClickThru Network

The following Privacy statement details the practices and policies of ClickThru, Inc., owner and operator of ClickThru.net: 

ClickThru may collect any or all of the following information: 

  * IP address / browser type - used to help diagnose problems, to log user visits, and to administer our Web site.
  * Email Address - Used to provide a least one form of contact. Users may opt-out of receiving any regular mailings
  * Street Address - Provided OPTIONALLY, and used for targeting purposes or mailing checks
  * Demographics/Interest profile - Provided OPTIONALLY, and used for targeting advertisements and searches

ClickThru, Inc. will never give or sell any of the above information to a third party without prior consent. We use this data to tailor the visitor's experience at our site to content that most closely matches individual interests. This information is shared with others only in the form of aggregate statistics. ClickThru.net requires the use of cookies to store login session information. Optional interest profiles may also be included in cookies. 

ClickThru.net serves as a directory of other web sites and is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

**Security / Financial Informtion**  
And Financial information that is collected is used solely to bill the user for products and services. Credit Card and Bank Account information is collected only over secure SSL Encrypted internet connections. No personal financial information is stored on ClickThru.net web servers. 

**Opting-Out**  
Any ClickThru.net users may opt-out of receiving regular e-mails by one of the following methods: 

  * Adjust online settings at http://www.clickthru.net/surfopts
  * Contact support at: http://www.clickthru.net/support

This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided. 
  * Adjust OPTIONAL personal interest and demographic profile at http://www.clickthru.net/surfopts
  * Contact support at: http://www.clickthru.net/support
