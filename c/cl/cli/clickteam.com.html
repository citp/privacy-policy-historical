Clickteam is committed to protecting your privacy.

We do not collect any personal data from general visitors to the site.

Only the following 2 areas of the website have any impact on your privacy:

The Online Store
To serve our customers purchasing our software we will need to collect and store some of your personal information. This would include: your name, address, email address, and phone number. This information is stored in the secure Clickteam commerce system for use in delivering your purchase. We do not and will not share this information with any outside companies at anytime. All of your banking details are directly transmitted to the banks secure server by secure channels. The customer service employees of Clickteam cannot access that data at any time.

The Forums at Clickteam
If you wish to participate on the interactive message forums at www.clickteam.com we will ask you to provide the following information in the forum registration.
Username – A name you will be known as on the forums
Email – A valid email address the forum can use to send a secure password for you to access the forum.
Over 13 years old – Yes or No (To comply with COPPA we do not allow children under 13 to register on the forum)
All other information in the forum profile is up to your own discretion on providing.
Your registration email address will not be disclosed to any other companies or people unless you make it public in your profile. By default all your data is private and hidden from public view.


You should be aware that when you visit any site on the Internet you leave a small trail of information. A website operator can not tell who you are but, some of the things they can learn about you are:
-- The link you came to Clickteam from
-- The time you arrived
-- Which pages you viewed
-- How long you stayed on the site

No personally identifiable information is ever disclosed to any third party people.
If you have questions about our policy, please email webmaster@clickteam.com
