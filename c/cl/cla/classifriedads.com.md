p>Privacy Policy 

[classifriedads.com] respects your privacy and recognizes the importance of protecting information collected from you through [classifriedads.com]'s Web site, related data, and/or related services (collectively, the "Services"). We have adopted this privacy policy. [classifriedads.com] will comply with this policy, and applicable privacy-related law, when we gather and use information from users of the Services.

This policy is current as of its last revision date below. However, please note that this policy may be amended from time to time to reflect changes and additions to the privacy policy. You should check back for the most current version before relying on any of the provisions in this privacy policy.

Collection of Information

[classifriedads.com] collects a variety of information relating to usage of the Services.

* Information you provide. You may provide information in connection with your use of the Services, such as information about Interests, Personal Identity, Geographic information including physical address information including telephone numbers. You may also provide us information about yourself, such as your e-mail address for contact purposes with [classifriedads.com], or for other Users or Members of[classifriedads.com] to be able to contact you. We may combine this information with other information we collect from the Services or from other companies.  
* Cookies. Cookies are bits of electronic information that can be transferred to a user's computer to uniquely identify the user's browser. When you visit the Services, we place one or more cookies on your computer. The use of cookies helps us improve the quality of the Services in various ways, such as identifying which Services are most interesting to you, or storing information you may want to retrieve on a regular basis or tracking user trends. You may adjust settings on your browser to refuse cookies. Also, we work with one or more service providers to track and manage cookie information; Some Services and features may not function properly if you disable cookies.   
* Usage logs. When you access the Services, your computer communicates a variety of information to computer servers operating the Services, including the existence of cookies, your Internet Protocol address, and information about your browser program. Your usage also creates a record of what information you are requesting from the Services.

Usage and Disclosure

In general, [classifriedads.com] uses the information collected to provide the Services and to communicate with you. The information that you choose to provide in a public area of the Services will not be protected by this privacy policy.

[classifriedads.com]sometimes collect your email address, for purposes such as sending posting and confirmation emails, authenticating /verification of user accounts, providing subscription email services, registering for forums, etc.   
For job postings, we may collect contact information, such as name(s), phone/fax number(s), and address for billing purposes.   
We sometimes collect your phone number for account authentication purposes.  
[classifriedads.com] does not store credit card information. Credit card details are transmitted from the User/Member to PAYPAL, and we work at the site level to protect the security of your payment information.  
We may collect personal information if you provide it in feedback or comments, post it on our classifieds or forums, or if you contact us directly. Please do not post any personal information on [classifriedads.com] forums or classifieds that should not be posted in a public place or forum.  
*Our and analytics collect basic web log entries for each page served, including your IP address, URL, and timestamp. Analytics help us to diagnose and fix issues with our server, and to otherwise provide top notch[classifriedads.com] service to all of our users/members.

* All classified and forum postings are stored in our database even after "deletion," and may be archived elsewhere. 

We will only share personal information that you provide outside the public areas of the Services in the following circumstances:

* Consent. When you consent or direct [classifriedads.com] to share the information.   
* Affiliates and service providers. When [classifriedads.com] provides personal information to our affiliated companies or service providers to manage and use on our behalf, consistent with the terms of this privacy policy.   
* Legal Obligation or Protection from Harm. When [classifriedads.com] has a good faith belief that access, use, preservation or disclosure of the personal information is reasonably necessary to (a) satisfy any requirement of law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request, (b) enforce or investigate a potential violation of the Terms of Use, (c) detect, prevent, or otherwise respond to fraud, security or technical concerns, or (d) protect the rights, property, or safety of [classifriedads.com], its users, or the public against harm.   
* Merger or sale. When and if [classifriedads.com] is involved in a merger, acquisition, or any form of sale of some or all of its business, personal information may be transferred along with the business.

We may share with third parties aggregate information or information that does not personally identify individuals.

Third Party Web Sites

The Services may include links to the Web sites of other companies and/or individuals. These third party Web sites may collect personal information about users on those Web sites, and [classifriedads.com]'s privacy policy does not extend to these external Web sites and third parties. Please refer directly to these third parties and Web sites regarding their privacy policies.

Under the Age of 13

[classifriedads.com] will not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from any person under the age of 13. The Services are not designed to attract the attention of persons under the age of 13.Accounts posted and verified from those age 13 years or younger will be deleted if detected.

Personal information will be processed and stored by [classifriedads.com] in databases hosted in the United States and or Canada[classifriedads.com] has taken reasonable steps to protect the information users share with us, including, but not limited to, setup of processes, equipment and software to avoid unauthorized access or disclosure of this information.

[classifriedads.com], or people who post on [classifriedads.com], may provide links to third party websites, which may have different privacy practices. [classifriedads.com] is not responsible for, nor has any control over, the privacy policies of those third party websites, and [classifriedads.com]encourages all users/members to read the privacy policies/Terms of each and every website visited.
