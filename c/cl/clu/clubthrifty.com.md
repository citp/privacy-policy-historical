# Privacy Policy & Terms of Use | Club Thrifty

Club Thrifty Media, LLC is very concerned about the privacy of your information. Below is our “Privacy Policy.”

## Privacy Policy

**Personal Information Collected on this Website**  
No personally-identifiable information is collected on this website from persons that browse our website unless it is provided by the user. The only personally-identifiable information collected on this website is gathered in our comment area, where fields are provided so that you can provide your name and email address to leave a comment. We collect this information so that we can respond to the comments or questions that you post. We also collect information about users who subscribe to one of our mailing lists. However that information is provided by the user through a double opt-in system and is kept in strict confidence by our service provider (Mailchimp). We do not (and will not) release or disclose any specific subscriber information under any circumstances, unless required by law. General aggregate information (e.g., total number of subscribers, etc.) may be disclosed.

**Anonymous Information Collected on this Website**  
Most of the information that we collect from this website is anonymous information. We collect anonymous data using a tool called Google Analytics. The program collects anonymous data that includes, the pages you visit, the name of your internet service provider, the website that you used to link to our site, the websites that you visit from our site, your operating system, your IP address, etc. This data is used for statistical purposes, but you, as the individual user, remain anonymous. We use this anonymous information to help improve the content of this site and to compile aggregate statistics about individuals using our site for internal market research purposes. We do not combine Google Analytics info with any personally identifiable information. Google Analytics does place a permanent cookie on your web browser which identifies you as a unique user the next time you visit this site. This cookie cannot be used by anyone but Google. Google is restricted in its ability to use and share information collected by Google Analytics by the [Google Analytics Terms of Use](http://www.google.com/analytics/tos.html) and the [Google Privacy Policy](http://www.google.com/privacypolicy.html). You may disable cookies on your browser if you wish to prevent Google Analytics from recognizing you on return visits to this site.

Additionally, we collect data about how people access our RSS feeds, but that data does not include any personally identifiable information about our subscribers.

**Cookies**  
We use cookies on this website. In addition to the cookies used by Google Analytics, we also use Google as a third-party advertising company to serve ads when you visit our website. Google uses a [DoubleClick DART cookie ](http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/faq.aspx)to serve ads to you based on visit to this sites and other sites on the Internet. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these Google, click [here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).

Some of the links on this website are affiliate links which do use cookies to track sales. The cookie is stored on your computer, generally (but not necessarily) for a period of 30 days. If you click on our affiliate links and complete a sale, we may receive a commission. To learn more about our affiliate sales, please see [our disclosure](http://clubthrifty.com/disclosure/).

## Terms of Use

**Use of Email**  
Any emails sent to Club Thrifty are considered to be property of Club Thrifty Media, LLC. Please be aware that your tips or questions may be published on the website, though we will refrain from posting personally identifiable information.

**Use of Comments**  
This site permits readers to leave comments expressing their views about the articles published on Club Thrifty. However, you are solely responsible for the materials you post here. By using Club Thrifty, you agree to indemnify and hold harmless this website and its agents with respect to any claims based upon the transmission of your messages.

We are not responsible for the the accuracy or usefulness of any information posted, nor do we warrant or vouch for the contents of any message. Comments express the views of the author of the message only. Comments do not reflect the opinions of this website’s administrators or bloggers.

If you chose to leave a comment, you agree to keep your comments:

  * relevant to the topic
  * respectful of others, and
  * suitable for a family forum



You shall refrain from posting any material that:

  * is abusive
  * contains knowingly false statements, libel, or slander
  * infringes upon the copyright, trademark, or other intellectual property of others
  * contains excessive use of foul language (note excessive)
  * contains references to adult content
  * contains spam or unwanted commercial solicitations
  * violates any laws or legal rights of other persons or entities



Comments appear in real-time making it is impossible for us to review every message immediately. We do not actively monitor the contents of this website and are not responsible for any messages posted. However, if we are made aware of a violation of our terms of use, we will remove the offending comments immediately without warning. We reserve the right to ban repeat offenders from the website, permanently.

Additionally, the following policy applies to all comments on Club Thrifty:

    * By posting a comment, you grant us the right to use, display, republish, sell, alter, or delete your comment in any way and for any reason.
    * You, the author, may not edit or remove your comments once they are posted. Furthermore, it is not our responsibility or duty to delete or edit your comment in any way at your request; however, we do reserve the right to edit your comment at our complete discretion.
    * This policy is subject to change at any time without notice.
    * Unsolicited commercial comments are not allowed.
