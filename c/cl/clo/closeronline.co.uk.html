Privacy Policy

Wednesday 29 November 2006
Confidentiality is Guaranteed
Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. Signing up for membership of Closerdiets.com requires us to ask you for certain confidential information, including your name and email address. This is entirely to enable us to create a personalised programme on your behalf and we guarantee your details will not, under any circumstances, be passed on to third parties.

Please be aware that if you submit information to the members' message board your anonymity cannot always be maintained.

You can include your email address if you wish but are not obliged to do so. While this area is moderated by Closerdiets.com technicians to prevent inappropriate messages from being posted, we cannot safeguard against unsolicited third party emails if an address has been supplied. So do be careful - and take care in turn not to violate the rights of others.


Online payments
All online payments to Closerdiets.com via debit or credit card are fully secured and firewall protected, using the most advanced technology and encryption methods available.


Closerdiets.com Privacy Policy
In order to provide you with our Services, we require certain personal information about you, such as your contact details. Except as disclosed in our terms and conditions we will not give your personal information to any third parties unless you specifically consent to us doing so.

For the purposes of service development we reserve the right to monitor the contents of the message board and secret diary facilities from time to time. However, we are not responsible for the privacy of any information which you reveal or post on the message board or secret diary facilities. The only instance in which we may disclose your personal data without your express consent is where we believe in good faith that you are breaching the terms and conditions or committing an illegal act and where we are required to make that disclosure to comply with any legal process including any regulatory requirement or court order.

The following is the specific information we will require in order to provide you with the Services:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Email address
  • Date of birth
  • Height
  • Sex
  • Weight
  • Exercise type and fitness level
  • Target weight
  • Medical issues
  • Lifestyle issues
  • Credit card details

We will use this data to adapt a programme best suited to your needs and we may email you with information related to the Services from time to time. We do not disclose your data to any other person without your express consent.

Closerdiets.com cookies
When you visit Closerdiets.com, we use "cookies" to enable you to sign back in to Closerdiets.com's services quickly and easily and to help personalise your online experience. A cookie is a small text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server. Cookies contain information that we can read when you revisit us. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser to decline cookies. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to take advantage of some Closerdiets.com features that may depend on cookies.

Our privacy policy may be amended from time to time and you should refer to our privacy policy on a regular basis.

If you are a member and wish to update your data at any time you can do so by using the change preferences section in your private members homepage. If you have questions regarding our privacy policy and how your data is stored please email us at customerservices@closerdiets.com
