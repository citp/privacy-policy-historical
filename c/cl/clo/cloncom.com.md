("FNBox") collect, use and share personal information of users of the website www.cloncom.com (the "Site"), which is owned by FNBox, and any products or services available from, or accessible through the Site. FNBox is committed to protecting the confidentiality of your personal information. We use state of the art hardware and encryption procedures to safeguard the privacy of your information. 
  * This document constitutes an integral part of the Terms and Conditions. By accepting the Terms and Conditions at the time of registration, you agree to the policies contained herein.
  * Please read the following to learn more about what information we may collect form our users and how such information is used and shared. By visiting our Site you are accepting the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy and the Terms and Conditions of our Site. If you do not agree with this terms, please do not visit or use this Site.
  * **We are committed to protecting the confidentiality of your personal information.**
  * ### 01\. What information we collect form our users and how we use it. 

  * When you register at the Site, we ask for personal information such as name,‎ postal address,‎ email address,‎ phone number and password. We request the same information to our users and affiliates. We may use your email address to send you special offers or promotions. You may unsubscribe from these at any time, in accordance with section 3. We may contact you by phone, but only in case there is a problem with your order.
  * FNBox primary goal in collecting personal information is to provide you with a safe, customized, and efficient experience. We use the information we collect from our users as follows:
  * **a) To provide our services.** FNBox uses the information we collect from users to help them process their orders or send them their calling cards, issue the inovoices, offer new products or services offered by our partners or advertisers, provide customer support, answer users comments and questions, send technical notifications or update the services. 
  * **b) To send communication from the Site.** FNBox will occasionally send users information or messages on new products or services, promotions and other service-related announcements we consider relevant for the users. 
  * **c) Dispute resolution.** FNBox may make disclosures of information from users in order to comply with the law and when we believe in good faith that the release of that information is necessary to investigate fraud, an intellectual property infringement, or other unlawful activity that may result in liability for FNBox or its users.
  * ### 02\. Cancellation, update and correct the information.

  * Users have the right to access, edit their information and cancel their account by sending an email to cloncom+legal@fnbox.com or a letter to Ayacucho 1246, 2nd Floor, (zipcode: 1111), Buenos Aires, Argentina. When you contact us, please indicate your name, address and email address and the information you would like to update.
  * Users guarantee the accuracy and authenticity of the information provided by them and undertake to keep it updated. FNBox urges users to promptly update their information if there has been any change.
  * If users decide to cancel their account or become inactive customers, all information will be placed in a deactivated status in our database. We will retain this information, only to be used to resolve dispute, solve problems and comply with our Terms and Conditions. Also, since FNBox tracks past transactions, the information associated with past transactions on the Site can’t be eliminated. In any case, the information from the users will not be immediately withdrawn from FNBox files due to legal and technical reasons. Therefore, the information from the users will not be definitely deleted from FNBox database.
  * If FNBox makes material changes in the way the information is managed, FNBox will notify users by email so they can make an informed decision whether to accept or not to have their information used in this way. If you do not accept these terms, then the contractual relationship will be dissolved and the information will be used in accordance with the privacy valid at the time the information was collected.

  * ### 07\. Information from other sources

  * **a) Cookies**
  * The user acknowledges that FNBox uses cookies in order to improve and customize the experience of the users in the Site. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user's computer for record-keeping purposes. We use cookies on this Site. We do not collect personally identifiable information with the cookies, we use them to show the websites the users visited before ours and the time spent in them. We use cookies to customize our Site, upgrade our services and offers. Furthermore, the cookies allow us track the number of register users in the Site and the activity of the user. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our Site.
  * You can remove cookies by following directions provided in your Internet browser's "help" file. If you reject cookies, you may still use our Site, but your ability to use some areas of our Site, such as our shopping procedure, will be limited. Third parties use cookies on this site, which allow them to recognize a user's cookie when a user visits this site. The information that they collect and share through this technology is not personally identifiable. FNBox does not control the use of cookies by third parties. 
  * **b) Log Files**
  * As is true of most Sites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, operating system, date/time stamp, the uniform resource locator (URL) of the sites you visited before visiting our Site and time spent in them, and the use you made of our Site.
  * We use this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze trends, to administer the site, to track users' movements around the Site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. We do not link this automatically-collected data to personally identifiable information. IP addresses will be used to customize the content of our homepage according to your country.
