3. Use of Cookies

Clozette uses "cookies", where a small data file is sent to your browser to store and track information about you when you enter the Site and App. The cookie is used to track information such as the number of users and their frequency of use, profiles of users and their preferred sites.

Like many web site operators, Clozette may use an independent company (the "Third Party Market Research Company") to measure and analyse the Internet usage across the Site and App. Clozette uses the Third Party Market Research Company's services to collect the following core information on the usage of the Site and App, including:

  • The number of page views (or page impressions) that occur on the Site and App;
  • The number of unique visitors to the Site and App;
  • How long these unique visitors (on average) spend on the Site and App when they do visit; and
  • Common entry and exit points into the Site and App.

This information is collated by the Third Party Market Research Company and provided to Clozette to assist in analyzing the usage of the Site and App.

Pages on the Site and App may be coded with software which enables the Third Party Market Research Company and Clozette to track visitors to the Site and App.


4. How Do We Protect Your Information

We are committed to protecting the information we receive from you. We follow reasonable technical and management practices to help protect the confidentiality, security and integrity of data stored on our system. While no computer system is completely secure, we believe the measures we have implemented reduce the likelihood of security problems to a level appropriate to the type of data involved.

Where applicable, the Site and App encrypts your credit card number and other personal information using secure socket layer (SSL) technology to provide for the secure transmission of the information from your PC to our servers. In addition, only those employees and third parties who need access to your information in order to perform their duties are allowed such access.


5. Newsletters

Clozette offers several newsletters to which you may voluntarily subscribe and unsubscribe at any time. When subscribing to our newsletters, a user needs to provide the mandatory email address, first name and last name.


6. Unsubscribe

Users who wish to no longer receive our newsletters may click on the "UNSUBSCRIBE" link which is found in every newsletter sent by us. This will prevent users from receiving future messages.

We may, from time to time, change our Privacy Policy for the Site and App. We will post the revised Privacy Policy here so that you will always know what information we gather, how we might use that information and whether we may disclose it to anyone. Your continued use of the Site and App indicates your assent to the Privacy Policy as posted.
