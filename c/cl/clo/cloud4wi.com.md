# Privacy

Cloud4Wi is committed to the privacy of its web site visitors.

In order to respond to your questions or fulfill your requests, it may be necessary to ask for personal information such as your name, address, email address and telephone number. We may use this information to respond to your requests, or to contact you via mail, email or telephone to inform you of new products, applications, or services we may offer. We will not provide this information to a third party without your permission.

Cloud4Wi has taken all appropriate measures to prevent unauthorized access to improper use and the inaccuracy of your personal information.

Cloud4Wi will on its own initiative, or at your request, replenish, rectify or erase any incomplete, inaccurate or outdated personal data. You can update your contact information by e-mailing [privacy@cloud4wi.com](mailto:privacy@cloud4wi.com) with the subject line heading of "Privacy Policy Request," or by updating the information via a web page. We may keep all information in an off-line form, for archival purposes or as otherwise required by law. Please note that if your information is removed from our databases, you may no longer be able to access certain areas of our web site requiring registration or a subscription.
