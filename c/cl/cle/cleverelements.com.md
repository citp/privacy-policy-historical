## Privacy Policy

We never share your information with third parties, but only use them in order to offer you our services. We commit ourselves explicitly as part of our membership in the [Deutschen Dialogmarketing Verband DDV](http://www.ddv.de/), by signing the [Ehrenkodex eMail-Marketing](http://www.ddv.de/) and by our certification by the [Certified Senders Alliance](http://www.certified-senders.eu/). So you can rest assured that your information is safe with us.

### 2\. What data is stored?

When registering, ordering or contacting Clever Elements you will be asked to provide us with personal information such as name, e-mail or phone number. Of course you are free not to provide this information. In this case, the Clever Elements GmbH is not always able to provide you with the requested products, services or information available.

### 3\. Technically needed data

When you visit any website to be - for technical reasons - stored information about an IP address, referring URL, date / time, browser version and operating system in the server's log files. In addition, partially so-called cookies are stored locally on your computer. Cookies are small text files that are temporarily stored on your hard drive and contain information about your visit to our website. They facilitate navigation and provide a higher degree of user friendliness. This data will be technically and in no way associated with any individual person. An evaluation is only in the form of anonymous statistics (see usage statistics).

### 4\. Usage Statistics

To further improve the offer of our site, we collect statistical data on its use. We use the services of Google Analytics. Cookies can and / or JavaScript are used. From this data is saved using an anonymous user profiles. The data will be collected and stored in anonymous form. The data are not used to personally identify a visitor personally and are not combined with information about the bearer of the pseudonym. It is thereby ensured that no link is made to the above protocol or user profile data. 

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. (“Google”). Google Analytics uses “cookies”, which are text files placed on your computer, to help the website analyze how users use the site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States . Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the website, compiling reports on website activity for website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however please note that if you do this you may not be able to use the full functionality of this website. By using this website, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner and for the purposes set out above. 

The data collection and storage can be revoked at any time with future effect. [On this website](http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en), the company Google offers a free opt-out add-on. Given the debate about the use of analytical tools with full IP addresses, please be aware that this website uses Google Analytics with the "_anonymizeIp ()" is used and therefore IP addresses will be processed only shortened to exclude a direct Personenbeziehbarkeit.
