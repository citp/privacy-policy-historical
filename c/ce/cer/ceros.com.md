# Privacy | Ceros digital magazines and interactive publications

## Collecting Information

To use our support area you will be asked to submit your name and email address, we usually capture your IP address at this time also. When you visit our website we log certain information about an individual's usage of the site. This may include your IP address, the pages you visit and the website that you just came from, we do this by the use of cookies. Your information is stored securely in the United Kingdom, in accordance with this privacy statement.

## Using information

Applecart Solutions will never sell or share personal information with third parties. We use this information to serve those who joined our mailing list on issues we feel they would be interested in, including website and company news. Cookies are used for internal research and to give a better understanding of our audience.

At some point we may provide prospective advertisers and partners with non identifiable statistical information. We may also employ the services of a third party to complete certain tasks, if they are provided with user's information they will be subject to confidentiality agreements. To protect Applecart Solution's rights and property, or to comply with a legal process, we may disclose your personal information.

You should also be aware that if you link out to a third party website these have there own privacy policies and Applecart Solutions accepts no responsibility other websites privacy statements. Please be sure to check other sites before use. Applecart Solutions cannot ensure the security of information that you transmit to us, please keep your details secret.

## Cookies

If you have your browser set up to accept them, are website also uses a feature known as a cookies. Cookies are text files that identify your computer to our server. Cookies in themselves do not identify the individual user, just the computer used. You can configure your browser to refuse them or notify you when you have received a cookie.

## Unsubscribing

At anytime you can unsubscribe from Applecart's mailing list. Just send an email to [unsubscribe@applecartsolutions.com](mailto:unsubscribe@applecartsolutions.com).

## General

We take privacy very seriously and are therefore committed to protecting the privacy of our users. If you have any queries regarding these issues please contact us at [privacy@applcartsolutions.com](mailto:privacy@applcartsolutions.com). Information on the Data Protection Act 1998 is also on the Data Protection Commissioner's website at [www.dataprotection.gov.uk](http://www.dataprotection.gov.uk/).

Applecart Solutions may amend this information from time to time, but changes will be posted here. Use of this site is subject to our terms of use.
