      Privacy Statement 

 
Centrelink collects a range of information about visits to the Centrelink site. This information is stored for 12 months and includes:
  • Entry and exit pages
  • How often the site was used
  • The time of day the site was accessed
  • The length of time spent on the site
  • How much information was downloaded
  • If the visit is from a company or an individual
  • If you are browsing from Australia or Overseas
  • What browser types are being used

This information is collected for statistical analysis use only and no attempt is made by Centrelink to identify users or their browsing activitiesThe only situation that such information may be sought is in the event of, an investigation, where a law enforcement agency may excercise a warrant to inspect internet provider logs.

Personally identifiable information such as your name, physical address and email address can be supplied to Centrelink when sending personal emails. Personal information collected by Centrelink is protected by the Privacy Act 1988 and or the confidentiality provisions of other legislation administered by Centrelink.

Currently the transmission of this information is not electronically secured or encrypted. A secure service will be available from Centrelink shortly.

The information you provide will only be used for the purpose for which you have provided it. Your details will not be added to a mailing list and we will not use your email address or other personal information for any other purpose. Unless required by law, we will not disclose this information without your consent.

Sites linked to the Centrelink website are not subject to this privacy statement and you are encouraged to review their privacy statements.
