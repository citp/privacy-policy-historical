Privacy Policy

PLEASE READ THE FOLLOWING. THE FOLLOWING POLICIES CONCERN YOUR PRIVACY WHEN YOU ACCESS OR OTHERWISE USE THIS WEB SITE AND ITS SERVICES.

Non-Personal Information We Collect

When you enter this Web site, information is gathered from your Internet browser. This information may include your IP address (which allows us to determine the identity of your Internet Service Provider), your operating system and the type of browser software that you are using to access this Web site. This information allows us to upgrade this Web site as necessary to optimize its efficiency. All information obtained from your browser is anonymous, and no personal information is ever transmitted to us in this manner.

While we do not at this time use cookies to collect information about how you use this Web site, we may do so in the future. A "cookie" is a small text file that a Web site can place on your computer's hard drive in order, for example, to retain your preferences and/or registration information for your next visit to that Web site. The cookie transmits this information back to that Web site's operator. You can choose not to receive a cookie file by enabling your Web browser to refuse cookies or prompt you before accepting a cookie. Note that refusing to accept cookies may impact the performance of a Web site.
