CEIVA PRIVACY POLICY

Ceiva, manufacturer of the world's first Internet picture frame, cares about your privacy. Ceiva's website logs your IP address (the Internet address of your computer) and browser type, however we do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable about you. Your session will be tracked, but your identity remains anonymous.

As a registered user, we ask that you provide us with your name, email address, preferences and other types of demographic information. Demographic information helps us learn more about your product needs and concerns. Other personal information may be gathered on your order form for our product, which would include billing and mailing addresses, credit card or other financial data to make an electronic funds transfer to purchase our product.

Occasionally, we will use your registration information to send you special offers. We might also share information with our business partners who are listed in the "Partner" section of this website. If you prefer, you can choose that your information not be shared with our partners or for any marketing promotions by indicating your preference on the Ceiva Member Setup form.

If you do not want us to use the information for marketing purposes, the information we ask for on our order form will only used to process your order quickly and accurately. Your contact information will only be used to send you the product that you purchase and your billing information will only be used to bill you for the product.

When you place your order online, your personal information and credit card information are encrypted or encoded. This makes it very difficult for your information to be stolen or intercepted while being transferred over the Internet. When we receive your order, it is kept encrypted until we are ready to process it. Your credit card information is erased from our website database so it in not connected to the Internet and is therefore more protected from "hackers."

Cookies
Our site may use cookies to keep track of your sign-in name, items in your shopping cart and to provide our visitors with custom shopping experiences. A cookie is a small file that your Web browser places on your computer's hard drive. When you log on and ask the computer to remember your sign-in name, you are asking that a cookie be created on your computer to remember that information. Once the cookie is in place, we can recognize future visits by accessing this piece of information.

You always have the option of setting your browser to reject cookies without any consequence other than the order form must always be manually filled out when placing an order, and you will always have to log on and sign in to the site.

If at anytime after you register, you change your mind about receiving information from us or allowing us to share your information with some of our marketing partners, send your requests to info@ceiva.com. If you need to send updates or correct information or contact us for any other reason, you may do so at this same address.

Links
This website may contain links to other websites. Please keep in mind that when clicking on one of these links, you are being transferred to another website. You are encouraged to read the privacy statements of these linked sites, as their privacy policies may differ from ours.

 

Ceiva Logic respects your privacy. All information is strictly confidential.
