# Privacy | CNM News Network

[ Privacy ](http://cnmnewsnetwork.com/privacy/)

CNM News Network considers the privacy of our readers very important.  For that reason, we only collect data in order to improve our readers’ satisfaction.  We will never sell, rent, lease, or otherwise disclose any personal information collected about our readers for purposes other than indicated in this privacy policy.

From time to time, we may link to third-party sites.  We are not responsible for the content of the sites, and link to them only for the convenience of our readers. We cannot be held accountable for the actions of any site that is not affiliated with CNM News Network.

Sometimes third parties, such as our advertising partners, may place or read cookies in readers’ browsers.  This is to enhance the readers’ experience and service ads to the site.  The information will not be used for any other purpose than to service advertisements.

We won’t send you unsolicited emails. If you believe you have received an unsolicited email from us, please contact us immediately and forward the email in question to [c.news@ymail.com](mailto:c.news@ymail.com) .

When commenting on a post, you may be asked for an email address.  This is not published on the site and will not be disclosed to anyone other than CNM News Network staff.

If you have any other privacy concerns, please feel free to contact us at the email address above.

All information on CNM News Network is copyrighted.  If you believe that one of our writers have violated your own copyright, please contact us immediately.

Effective Date: January 21, 2010
