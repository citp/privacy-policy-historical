Privacy Policy

1. Chennai Petroleum Corporation Limited is absolutely committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of visitors to our web site. We will not sell, rent or trade your personal information under any circumstances.

2. In cases where we do keep information about visitors to our site and the E-mail addresses of people who write to us, we store it in our off-line secure computing environment, not on the web. We store this information, but we do not sell it or release it to third parties.

3. We may track domain names, IP addresses, and browser types from people who visit our site. We use this information to track aggregate traffic patterns throughout Chennai Petroleum Corporation Limited site. Such information is not correlated with any personal information.

4. We do not use cookies to track information about a user's movements throughout our site.
