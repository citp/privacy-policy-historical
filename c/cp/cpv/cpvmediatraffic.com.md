# Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

In CPVMediaTraffic.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us (see this article to learn more about the Privacy Policy.). This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by CpaMediaPublisher.com and how it is used.

**Log Files**

Like many other Web sites, CPVMediaTraffic.com makes use of log files. The information contained in these files includes Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), date and time, pages referring / exit, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses and other information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

**Cookies and Web Beacons**

****CPVMediaTraffic.com uses cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on the type of browser visitors or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

**DoubleClick DART cookie**

Google, as vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on CPVMediaTraffic.com. Of Google’s use of the DART cookie allows you to serve ads to users based on their visit to CPVMediaTraffic.com and other websites.

Users can opt for the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and privacy of the network in terms of content at the following URL – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html.

These servers of third party advertising or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on CpaMediaPublisher.com sent directly to your browser. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by ad networks, third party to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content you see.

CPVMediaTraffic.com has no access or control over these cookies that are used by advertisers.

CPVMediaTraffic.com ‘s privacy policy does not apply to, and can not control the activities of other advertisers or web sites.
