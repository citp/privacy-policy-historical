    Privacy Statement

    • On some parts of our website you have the option to send us information about yourself that we may need for correspondence or for use in a survey. If you choose to provide us with this information, you consent to its collection and use by the University. Capilano University will not intentionally distribute this information outside of the University. You understand that information transmitted over public networks may be accessed by third parties, and Capilano University will not be liable for any such unauthorized disclosure.

      The CapU webpages may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the content and the privacy practices of other websites and encourage you to examine each site's privacy policy and disclaimers and make your own decisions regarding the accuracy, reliability and correctness of material and information found.
