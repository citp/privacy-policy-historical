# Cafe.com Privacy Policy

Cafe.com Privacy Policy:

Personal information you provide in connection with the account registration process, information contained in any communications made by you through the System, your frequency of using the System, and information about your use of the System will be provided to the Location from which you access the System. **Your use of the system indicates your consent to this disclosure.** The information you provide will otherwise not be provided to any third parties in a manner that would identify you unless Cafe.com or NetNearU is required to disclose such information in order to comply with applicable laws or unless you consent.
