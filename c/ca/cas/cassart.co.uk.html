Privacy Policy

Cass Art complies with all relevant Data Protection and consumer legislation and treats all personal information as confidential. We collect your personal information when you subscribe to our mailing list, opt for an electronic receipt, or sign up for a Cass Art Rewards card or Cobalt Blue student card. We collect your purchase data with your personal details through our technical partners to ensure we send you relevant content and offers. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of the newsletter. Cass Art will not share your details with third parties unless you give us permission to do so. Read our Rewards Card Terms & Conditions for more information.

By subscribing to the Cass Art newsletter, you will receive regular emails with news, opportunities and offers. You can unsubscribe at any time, by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any newsletter. Cass Art will not share your details with third parties unless you give us permission to do so. However, to perform credit-card transactions and fulfil orders, Cass Art uses reputable third-party banking and distribution institutions. These carefully selected companies receive only the information needed to verify and authorise payment cards and to process orders and are under strict legal obligations to keep all personal information private. In the interests of monitoring and improving our service, customers will receive an email from Ekomi asking you to review your experience shopping with us. Should you wish to not receive emails from Ekomi to review your product, contact us on 0207 619 2601.
