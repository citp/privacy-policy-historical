Privacy Policy

CASTLE Worldwide recognizes the importance of protecting your privacy. We have paid a great deal of attention to the issues of security, full disclosure, and a thorough privacy policy. Please note, however, that this Privacy Policy co-exists with our Terms and Conditions of Use and any independent contracts or documents signed by you. Together these policies govern the general practices of this site. CASTLE Worldwide encourages customers and visitors to familiarize themselves with these policies and documents. It is our policy to collect and store only the personal information knowingly provided by our members and visitors. We do not use "cookies" to collect information.

CASTLE Worldwide collects and maintains the following personal information provided by members and visitors: 1) certain personal information, including your name, address and e-mail address, when you register for our services or purchase our products; and 2) information on which tests and training courses you have taken and whether you passed or failed them. We do not maintain the diagnostic information generated by our tests. CASTLE Worldwide produces such information only to help examinees identify their personal strengths and weaknesses so that they may pursue focused training or courses of study. By using this site, you are giving your consent to the gathering and use of information as set forth herein.

Information Disclosure and Use

CASTLE Worldwide may use the personal information you provide in the following ways, unless stated otherwise:

  1. to deliver the product or service you requested
  2. to develop new products and services and improve existing products and services
  3. to notify you regarding updates to our products and services
  4. to provide you with information about related products and services, based on the preferences you have indicated
  5. to administer and troubleshoot our systems more efficiently
  6. to enable billing.

To describe our products and services to prospective clients and partners, CASTLE Worldwide may disclose user information in an aggregate form that does not contain any individually identifiable information. CASTLE Worldwide will not share your information with third parties, other than the client organizations with which you are affiliated. CASTLE Worldwide does not collect credit card information; an independent vendor collects this information. 

Security of Information

CASTLE Worldwide maintains personal information gathered online in a secure environment protected from unauthorized third party access, alteration, or misuse.  

Accuracy of Information

CASTLE Worldwide is committed to maintaining complete and accurate information about our customers and visitors. Therefore, please notify us as soon as possible if you suspect that we have recorded inaccurate information about you or if you would like to review the information that we have collected about you. To notify us, contact us at info@castleworldwide.com. We will employ every effort to correct the information and notify anyone with whom we may have shared the inaccurate information.

Children's Guidelines

CASTLE Worldwide recognizes that children are especially vulnerable and may not understand the consequences of providing their personal information online. Therefore, CASTLE Worldwide does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you are not 13 years of age or older, please do not use this Web site. All children between the ages of 13 and 18 must obtain a parent or guardian's permission before registering for our services, purchasing our products, or downloading any files from our Web site.
