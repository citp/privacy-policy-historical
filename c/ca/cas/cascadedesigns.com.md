# Cascade Designs, Inc.

_Established September 15, 2014; last updated September 15, 2014_

## Our Pledge

Cascadedesigns.com is an electronic marketplace for products produced by Cascade Designs, Inc.’s family of brands. These brands consist of Therm-a-Rest, MSR, Platypus, Sealline, E-Case, PackTowl, and Hummingbird. Cascade Designs, Inc. sets high standards and has a strong commitment to protect customers' privacy on all of its web sites. We pledge that, as we gather and use information about how you use our web sites, we do so responsibly. 

## Information We Collect

We collect some non-individually-identifiable generic information about our visitors. Our web servers automatically recognize standard technical information, including a visitor's Internet service provider, the IP address, the domain name, the type of browser, and the operating system. In addition, we track which pages are viewed on our sites, the web page you are on when you link to our sites, how much time a visitor spends on each page and other information related to the operation and interaction of Cascade Designs, Inc.'s web sites. This information does not reveal a visitor's identity. 

If you choose to make a purchase on, or register with, our website, or if you request information about our company or products, you will be asked to provide some individually-identifiable information, including but not limited to: name, email address, home/billing address, phone number, and debit/credit card information. You will be able to make changes to your individually-identifiable information through your account at any time. 

## How do we use your information?

We aggregate non-individually-identifiable information and use it to evaluate and improve our web sites. We may use your individually-identifiable information to fulfill your orders, or to send you information that you request.

We do not sell the information provided to any outside company or third party and all information is stored on secure servers with limited access. 

## Opting In

Our web sites include an option to “opt-in.” This means that, if you opt to allow us to do so, we may use your individually -identifiable information to send you periodic emails about our products and promotions.

Our web sites include an option to “opt-in.” This means that, if you opt to allow us to do so, we may store your individually-identifiable information with a third-party database provider contracted to send you periodic emails about our products and promotions. You always have the option to opt-out and remove yourself from any of Cascade Designs’ contact lists at any time. 

You always have the option to opt-out and remove yourself from any of Cascade Designs’ contact lists at any time.

## How do we use cookies?

"Cookies" are small text files stored on your computer that a web site can use to recognize repeat visitors, and to allow a web site to provide a personalized experience to each visitor. If you do not want information collected through the use of cookies, there is a simple procedure in most browsers that allows you to disable the cookie feature. The Cascade Designs, Inc. web site uses cookies for various reasons. The main use of cookies is to measure advertising effectiveness so that we can better tailor our advertising efforts. Cookies are in no way used to collect any personal information about you the user. Any personal information collected is given voluntarily by you the user in a catalog request or a request for more information.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

Cascade Designs, Inc. may periodically make changes to this Privacy Policy. When this happens, we will notify users by posting the updated policy on this Site. Please periodically review this Site to ensure you are aware of any updates to our Privacy Policy.

## Use by Minors Prohibited

This Site is not intended for users under the age of 18. We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act and will not knowingly collect PII from anyone under the age of 13.

## Consent to Transfer

For users outside the United States, we must advise you that your personal information could potentially be accessible by individuals located in countries that do not offer the same level of data protection as your country. Upon providing us your personal information, you consent to our potential use of your personal information across international boundaries. We will continue to adhere to the guidelines in this policy, and will endeavor to keep your personal information secure.
