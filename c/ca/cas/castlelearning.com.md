 Castle Software, Inc.
 Data Privacy & Security Policy
Castle Software, Inc. ("Castle") is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. Because
Castle gathers certain types of information about the users of Castle's web sites ("Service"), we
believe you should fully understand the terms and conditions surrounding the use of the
information we collect. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination
practices for the ​
                  CastleLearning.com​     and ​PrepPath.com​    web sites.
Please review the following statement to understand how the information you provide to Castle
through our web sites are treated.
Information Castle collects
You can visit the Castle's websites without identifying yourself. You can sign­up for the
company newsletter/blog where you need to supply an email address plus other optional
information (name, organization, title). You can opt­out of the newsletter/blog at any time and
modify or delete any personal information that you previously provided. We will not share any of
your personal information with any outside organization.
The Castle services require that you have a subscription. Each subscription is associated with a
Login ID and Password which are used for accessing the web site's information and activities .
Other information, including names and email addresses may be supplied to us.
For school subscriptions, a representative from the school specifies some or all of the following
information about the subscription­holders:
    ● First and Last Name ­ this information is used to identify students and teachers in
        various student, teacher, and administrator reports.
    ● Email Address ­ this is optional and is used only to email forgotten login information to a
        student or teacher. The school can disable this feature.
    ● Grade ­ where applicable, this is the student's current grade level or year of graduation
        and is optional. It is used to help teachers identify their students in the database.
    ● Login IDs and Passwords ­ the school representative supplies this information or agree
        to a formula for generating them. Passwords not supplied to us are specified by each
        individual user the first time they sign in.
    ● Identification Number ­ where applicable, the Student Identification Number helps
        identify a user in the database.

What Castle does with the information collected
The Login ID and Password assigned to users are used to login and participate in the activities
of the Castle websites.
The personal information supplied to Castle will not be given, sold, rented, loaned, or otherwise
disclosed to any third parties outside of Castle, except when we have your permission or under
special circumstances, such as when we believe in good faith that disclosure is reasonably
necessary to (i) comply with legal process, (ii) enforce the Terms of Use, (iii) respond to claims
that any content on the Castle web sites violates the rights of third parties, or (iv) protect the
rights, property, or personal safety of Castle, its customers, or the public. In addition, when you
subscribe to any 3rd party service through Castle, such as Method Test Prep, you are giving
Castle implicit permission to give student information to that 3rd party in order to achieve a
single sign on between the services.

Data Destruction
For yearly subscription products, student data is destroyed for accounts that were created
approximately 16 months prior to the current school year.
All student data will be deleted at end of contract if requested by the customer. Otherwise, the
student data remains in the database for approximately 16 months after the end of the contract.
Security
Physical Location
All personal information is stored on servers at a location designed specifically to ensure that no
unauthorized individuals have access to the server or its data. Our web hosting provider is
Rackspace, one of the leading hosting providers.
Encryption­In­Motion
All transmitted data is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL protocols.
Encryption­At­Rest
All data in the Castle databases that can identify a student is encrypted.
Staff Training
Castle requires all internal personnel who would be in direct contact with user data information
be trained in the secure handling and privacy distribution of student data. This includes, but is
not limited to, access rights, communication with customers, along with the importing and
exporting of files. Customer service and training personnel are trained to only release student
information to authorized school/district personnel or parents through a series of validation
methods.
Subcontractors
Subcontractors' are required to sign Non­Disclosure Agreements and must return or destroy all
Castle property and data at the end of their contracts.
Data Breach Protocol
Castle maintains a contact list of the key administrators for each customer. Should a data
breach occur, these administrators will be notified via email as soon as it is known what breach,
exposure, or data loss may have occurred.

Cookies Policy
Castle's websites use cookies. A cookie is a file containing an identifier (a string of letters and
numbers) that is sent by a web server to a web browser and is stored by the browser. The
identifier is then sent back to the server each time the browser requests a page from the server.
By using Castle's websites and agreeing to this policy, you consent to Castle's use of cookies in
accordance with the terms of this policy.
We use cookies on our website to:
     ● improve the websites' usability
     ● administer the websites
     ● prevent fraud and improve the security of the website
     ● personalize the websites for each user
We use both session and persistent cookies on our websites. We do not use third party cookies.
Most browsers allow you to refuse to accept cookies. If you block cookies, you will not be able
to use all the features on our websites. You can delete cookies already stored on your
computer. Deleting cookies will have a negative impact on the usability of many websites.
