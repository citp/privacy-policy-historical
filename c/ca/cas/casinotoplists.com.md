# CasinoToplists Privacy Policy - Secure Online Gambling │CasinoToplists

All information collected at Casinotoplists will be kept strictly confidential. It will not be sold, reused, loaned, rented, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to Casinotoplists will be held with great care. No information will be used in ways that you as a Casinotoplists visitor have not consented to.

  * **Use of cookies:** Casinotoplists uses cookies, mostly in order to track traffic coming from other websites and leaving to other websites. We make no attempt to link these cookies with individuals to make any information personally identifiable.
  * **Browser information collected on the website:** Casinotoplists analyses website logs to improve the value of the materials available on the website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable and we make no attempt whatsoever to link them with the people who actually visit the site.
  * **Advertising:** Casinotoplists currently has advertisements served by an in-house program, these ads follow the privacy guidelines we have set.
  * **Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:** Casinotoplists does not sell, trade, rent, loan, or lease any personal information collected from the site. This includes membership forms or email lists.
  * **Updates and changes:** Casinotoplists reserves the right to replace this "privacy policy" at any time and due to this, we do suggest that you regularly check in with us at: https://www.casinotoplists.com/privacy-policy to make sure that you don't miss a beat. In order for our internet business and communications to be properly managed and optimized, we currently contract several online partners. We also use the services of a marketing company to help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising and how visitors use our site when they are on it. To fulfill these demands, we use web beacons and cookies provided by our marketing company on this site. The information we collect includes details entered by visitors in forms, the pages they have visited etc, and it is these records which are able to help us improve the site and make it more attractive and engaging for our customers. That said, although our marketing company logs the aforementioned information mentioned above to help us make your gaming experience better, we are in full control of how the data is used and we will never disclose it.



Please contact us at info@casinotoplists.com if you have any further questions or comments about our privacy policy.
