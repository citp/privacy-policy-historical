# wincake.com > Privacy Policy

At Win Cake, we know your privacy is of concern. Have a read below to get comfortable with Win Cake’s privacy policy concerning your Player Account, player data and any other pertinent information we may hold. Our team at Security@wincake.com is always available to answer any further questions.

  * Win Cake Player Information
  * Deposits and Withdrawals
  * Confidentiality of Information
  * Cookies



##  WIN CAKE PRIVACY POLICY

Win Cake and its owner and operator, Win Cake Gaming N.V. understand the importance of privacy protection on the internet. Win Cake remains committed to upholding the confidentiality of the personal information that we collect. The information that we collect is safeguarded with the appropriate software and hardware tools in place as well as the techniques, methods and procedures that we choose to employ.

The following information will help you to understand our policies and your rights regarding the collection, storage, use and the safeguarding of information relating to you.

##  WIN CAKE PLAYER INFORMATION

When you choose to open a Win Cake Account you will be asked to provide us with certain pertinent personal information. This information will include your full name, mailing address, e-mail address and a chosen password. You will also be asked to certify that you are at least 18 years old or older in jurisdictions where specifically required. We will use this personal information to confirm and complete any financial transactions as well as to contact you regarding news and events information and provide you with customer support services. If at any time you do not wish to receive our newsletters, you may instruct us to remove you from the distribution list by following the appropriate instructions included in each of the newsletters or by contacting the Support Team directly by E-mail. It is very important that you complete the required account details with valid information as this information will be used in the process of validating financial transactions. Players may change their account details by selecting an account option in the lobby screen. 

##  BANKING: DEPOSITS AND WITHDRAWALS

Players will need to provide deposit specific information when making a deposit to play for real money (example: credit card number or NETeller account number). This information may be applied along with your personal information to allow us to clear financial transactions executed by you with Win Cake. 

##  CONFIDENTIALITY OF INFORMATION

Win Cake undertakes substantial efforts to ensure the confidentiality of the information it has collected about individual players and will not knowingly allow access to this information to anyone outside of Win Cake, other than to the player himself or as described in this Privacy Policy. As a player, you should ensure that your Win Cake account password remains confidential and we recommend that you take appropriate steps to ensure others are not able to access your account information.

**LIMITS OF CONFIDENTIALITY**  
Legally Required Disclosures: Due to the existing legal, regulatory and security environment, Win Cake may be required, under certain circumstances, to disclose personally identifiable information about our Players. Win Cake will use its best reasonable efforts to limit such disclosure to the following: where Win Cake believes in good faith that it is required to do so in response to a subpoena, warrant or other legal process; or where reasonably necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against persons or entities to preserve and/or enforce Win Cake’s rights. Further, we can, and you authorize us to, disclose your Player identification, name, street address, city, state, zip code, country, phone number, e-mail, and company to third party agents of Win Cake as we, in our sole discretion, believe necessary or appropriate in connection with an investigation of fraud, intellectual property infringement, piracy or other unlawful activity or activity that may expose us to legal liability.

**WHO RECEIVES YOUR INFORMATION?**  
In addition to disclosures due to legal requirements as explained above, we may from time to time disclose your personal information to:  
Our Win Cake employees

  * employees of other companies that are related to or affiliated with Cake Gaming N.V.
  * third party service providers who use your personal information to provide services to us in respect of your being a Player with Win Cake.



All recipients of your personal information shall be bound by applicable data protection laws to keep your personal information private. These parties will adhere to policies that, at the very minimum, reflect those of Win Cake. 

##  COOKIES

We may send you a temporary cookie when you visit the Win Cake website. A cookie is a text-only string of information that we place in your computer's cookie file so that we can remember who you are when you revisit our website. We may use the data that is generated from cookies to compile statistical data on your use of our website. You are not obliged to accept the cookie from us and you have the ability to accept or decline cookies by modifying the settings in your browser. Please see contact our Support Team if you require any further information about the use or deletion of cookies. 

##  YOUR RIGHTS PERTAINING TO YOUR INFORMATION

You may write Win Cake to, where appropriate, have your personal information removed, rectified, amended or completed. In order to contact us regarding your information please e-mail our Security Team at security@wincake.com. Please note that Win Cake may levy a small charge to cover administration costs in regard to any such request. 

##  CONSENT TO PRIVACY POLICY

By opening an Account with Win Cake, you agree to the terms of this PRIVACY POLICY. We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy and encourage you to periodically review it. Your continued use of the Win Cake services constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Policy and any updates.  
In the case of any conflicting presentation between this Privacy Policy and the Win Cake End User Agreement captured under our Terms Of Service, the terms outlined in the Win Cake End User Agreement shall prevail.
