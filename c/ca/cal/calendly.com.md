# Calendly - Scheduling appointments and meetings is super easy with Calendly.

Calendly LLC (“Calendly”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This document describes the information we collect when you use Calendly. We never sell or rent your information.

To help you understand this privacy statement, we’d like to start by defining the following terms:

  * The terms "us" or "we" or "our" refers to Calendly, the owner of the Website.
  * A "Visitor" is someone who merely browses our Website, but has not registered as a Member.
  * A "Member" is an individual that has registered with us to use our Services.
  * Our "Service" represents the collective functionality and features as offered through our Website to our Members.
  * A "User" is a collective identifier that refers to either a Visitor or a Member.



#### Website Visitors

When you visit our website, we may collect non-personally-identifiable information such as your browser type, browser language, operating system and referring/exit pages. Occasionally, we may release this information in aggregate form only. For example, we might publish a report on general Calendly usage trends. Such releases will not include any personally-identifiable information about any individual users.

For those users who visit our site to schedule events with Calendly Members, we will require your name, your email address, phone number and whatever additional information the Calendly Member you’re scheduling with requires. This information will only be shared with the Calendly Member you’re scheduling with so they can contact you. It will never be sold or rented to any third parties by Calendly. Also, Calendly will never use the information for marketing purposes. However, Calendly has no control over the legal privacy practices of Calendly Members; as such, you provide information to Calendly Members at your own risk.

#### Calendly Members

If you sign up to use our products and services, we collect registration-related information such as name, address, email and phone number. This information is never sold or rented to any third parties.

We may occasionally disclose personally-identifiable information to those of our employees, contractors, and affiliated organizations that need to know that information to

  * fulfill your request for products and services
  * communicate with you and respond to your inquiries
  * provide customer support



Some of these employees, contractors, and affiliated organizations may be located outside your home country.

#### Cookies

We use cookies to customize your Calendly experience. If you do not wish to have cookies placed on your computer, set your browser to refuse cookies before using Calendly, with the drawback that certain features of the Calendly website may not function properly without the use of cookies.

#### Security

We use appropriate administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect information about Calendly users.

#### Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, we reserve the right to change this privacy statement by posting a revised version and/or by notifying our users by email.

#### Contacting Us

Questions regarding this privacy statement should be directed to [support@calendly.com](mailto:support@calendly.com)
