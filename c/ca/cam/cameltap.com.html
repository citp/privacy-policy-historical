Privacy

 

Personal Information

  • Browsing MyWall You can view or watch content on MyWall without having a MyWall Account. You also can contact us through the MyWall Help Center or by emailing us directly without having to register for an account.
  • Your MyWall Account. For some activities on MyWall, like uploading content, posting comments, or flagging content, you need a MyWall Account. We ask for some personal information when you create an account, including your email address and a password, which is used to protect your account from unauthorized access.
  • Usage Information. When you use MyWall, we may record information about your usage of the site, such as the channels, groups and favorites you subscribe to, which other users you communicate with, the content you view or watch, the frequency and size of data transfers, and information you display about yourself as well as information you click on in MyWall (including UI elements, settings). If you are logged in, we may associate that information with your MyWall Account. In order to ensure the quality of our service to you, we may place a tag (also called a "web beacon") in HTML-based customer support emails or other communications with you in order to confirm delivery.
  • Content Uploaded to Site. Any personal information or content that you voluntarily disclose online (e.g., content comments, your profile page) may be collected and used by others. If you download the MyWall Uploader, your copy includes a unique application number. This number, and information about your installation of the Uploader (version number, language) will be sent to MyWall when the Uploader automatically checks for updates and will be used to update your version of the Uploader.

Uses

  • If you submit personal information to MyWall, we may use that information to operate, maintain, and improve the features and functionality of MyWall, and to process any flagging activity or other communication you send to us.
  • We do not use your email address or other personal information to send commercial or marketing messages without your consent. We may use your email address without further consent for non-marketing or administrative purposes (such as notifying you of major MyWall changes or for customer service purposes). You also can choose how often MyWall sends you email updates in your MyWall Account settings page.
  • We use cookies, web beacons, and log file information to: (a) store information so that you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or the next time you visit MyWall; (b) provide custom, personalized content and information; (c) monitor the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns; (d) monitor aggregate metrics such as total number of visitors and pages viewed; and (e) track your entries, submissions, and status in promotions, sweepstakes, and contests.

     Information That is Publicly Available

  • When you create a MyWall Account, some information about your MyWall Account and your account activity will be provided to other users of MyWall. This may include the date you opened your MyWall Account, the date you last logged into your MyWall Account, your age (if you choose to make it public), the country and the number of content you have view or watched.
  • Your MyWall Account name, not your email address, is displayed to other users when you engage in certain activities on MyWall, such as when you upload content or send messages through MyWall. Other users can contact you by leaving a message or comment on the site.
  • Any content that you submit to MyWall may be redistributed through the internet and other media channels, and may be viewed by other MyWall users or the general public.
  • You may also choose to add personal information which may include your name, gender, profile picture or other details, that will be visible to other users on your MyWall Account channel page. If you choose to add certain features to your MyWall Account channel page, then these features and your activity associated with these features will be displayed to other users and may be aggregated and shared with your friends or other users. Such shared activity may include your favorite content, content you rated and content that you have uploaded.
    Your Choices
  • If you have a MyWall Account, you may update or correct your personal profile information, email preferences and privacy settings at any time by visiting your account profile page.
  • You may control the information that is available to other users and your confirmed friends at any time by editing your MyWall Account and the features that are included on your channel page. If you have enabled Active Sharing, other users may see that you, as identified by your account name, not your email address, are view or watching the same content.
  • You may, of course, decline to submit personal information through MyWall, in which case you can still view content and explore MyWall, but MyWall may not be able to provide certain services to you.

Advertising on MyWall

  • MyWall strives to provide you with relevant and useful advertising and we use the DoubleClick cookie, as well as other third party ad serving systems, to do so. We may use a range of information including cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, usage data and other non-personal information about your computer or device (such as browser type and operating system) to provide you with relevant advertising. If you are logged into your MyWall Account, we may also show you advertising based on the information you have provided to us in your MyWall Account.
  • We use cookies to help advertisers and publishers serve and manage ads on this site. Advertisers may serve ads based on interests associated with non-personally identifiable online activity, such as content viewed, frequency of uploading or activity on other AdSense partner sites. Advertisers may also serve ads to you based on previous activity on that advertiser's website. This non-personally identifiable information is held separately from your MyWall Account data, or other personally identifying information in our possession.
  • Other third-party ad serving companies may display ads on MyWall, provided they comply with our ad serving requirements. MyWall does not provide any personal information to these third-party ad servers or ad networks without your consent. You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers or ad networks for more information on their practices and for instructions on how to opt-out of certain practices. MyWall's privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites. Any data obtained by third-party ad servers subsequently shared with MyWall is maintained and dealt with by MyWall in accordance with this privacy policy.
