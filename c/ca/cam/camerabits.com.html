Information We Collect

We may collect your email address, for purposes such as sending confirmation emails, authenticating your purchases, Camera Bits Forum registration, creation, etc. We may collect personal information if you provide it to us, or if you contact us directly. When you access the Site, web logs used to help us diagnose problems with our server, to administer the Site, and to otherwise provide our Services to you, may automatically collect standard web log entries for each page served, including your IP address, page URL, browser type, and language, access times, referring website addresses, information you search on the Site, date and timestamps associated with Site access and usage, system configuration information, metadata concerning the files that you upload/download, and other interactions with the Site and/or Services.  All aggregate information collected, including without limitation aggregate data collected by our servers and web logs, is the sole and exclusive property of Camera Bits.

This Site also use “cookies” to collect information and improve our Site.  A cookie is a small data file that we transfer to your computer or electronic device.  We may use “persistent cookies” to save your user name and login password for future access to the Site.  We may use “session ID cookies” to enable certain features of the Site, helping us to gain a better understanding of how you interact with the Site, and to monitor aggregate usage and web traffic routing on the Site.  You can instruct your browser, by changing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit.  If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all aspects of the Site.

Many electronic devices track real-time location-based information.  Currently, we do not actively collect this information from any computer or device; though, we may do so in the future.  However, some files you share may are embedded with this location information.  We may use this information to optimize your experience.  If you do not wish to share this information, do not upload those embedded files and/or turn off that feature on your camera, or other device.

When you register for the Camera Bits Forums, including the creation of your profile, and at other times during your membership, when you make purchases on the Site, and at other times in your use of the Site, we make ask you to provide information which may personally identify you and/or allow us to contact you, such as your name and email address, user name and password, billing address, credit card number, applications purchased, date of purchase, and information about your computer and software.

The personal information you provide, in combination with other information collected, will be used to operate the Site, to improve the quality of our Site, and to provide you with a better experience.  Specifically, it may be used to: (i) provide you with important information about the product or service that you are using, including critical updates, notifications, and product announcements; (ii) diagnose problems with the Site; (iii) reduce fraud, software piracy, and protect our users and customers; (iv) provide and improve functionality and user-friendliness of our Site; (v) verify a user name and administer your use of the Site; (vi) better understand your needs and interests, and personalize and improve your experience; and (vii) monitor and analyze use of the Site.  In addition, we distribute a newsletter via the email address you provide to us.  If you no longer wish to receive this newsletter from us, you may “unsubscribe” by following the instructions in the email.
