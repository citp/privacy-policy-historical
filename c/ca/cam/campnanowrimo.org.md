# Camp NaNoWriMo

Camp NaNoWriMo collects personal information from the users of this site. We require, at minimum, that users choose a username and provide us with a valid, working email address when they register for this site.

Users may, if they wish, provide a variety of other personal data, including contact information, location, interests, personal preferences, and novel excerpts. We also keep track of users who have donated to NaNoWriMo and users who have won NaNoWriMo.

All of this information, with the exception of email address, are available to the public in the Camper Profile section of this website.

NaNoWriMo does not share ANY user information with ANY third parties EVER.

Requests for account deletion and questions about our Privacy Policy should be sent to [info@campnanowrimo.org](mailto:info@campnanowrimo.org).
