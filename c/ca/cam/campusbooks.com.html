Our Commitment to Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and how your information is collected and used.  

The Information We Collect:

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the CampusBooks.com website and via our mobile applications. As part of the registration process, we collect personal information from you that is used primarily to service your account at CampusBooks.com. The types of personal information collected on these pages include but are not limited to: Name, Address, Email Address, Phone Number, School/University.  

The Way We Use Information

We use the information you provide about yourself to provide you with the service you signed up for, including but not limited to the Bookbag/Book List, Price Alerts, and Price Tweetbacks. We do not share your personal information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to provide you with the service that you signed up for.
We may use non-identifying and aggregate information to improve our website.
We may use information we collect from you to improve customer service.
We may use your email and/or mailing address for periodic communication.  

Our Commitment To Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.  

Information Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect our rights, property, or safety and those of others. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

These third-party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.  

Online Privacy Policy Only

This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through our website and not to information collected offline.
