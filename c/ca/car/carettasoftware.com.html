Privacy Policy

  1. We will never rent, sell or otherwise disclose your personal information to anyone except our partners where absolutely necessary to carry out the business of supplying you with our software products or services.
  2. We are totally opposed to spam and will never knowingly send unsolicited emails to anyone.
  3. We may send email to you under any of the following circumstances:
    1. To acknowledge the purchase of our software products or services and to provide you with license keys where appropriate.
    2. If you have filled in a feedback form on our web site and provided your email address, you may receive an acknowledgement of receipt. In addition, we may need to contact you to obtain further details.
    3. If you have requested information or subscribed to our information service or newsletter then you will receive these by email at an appropriate frequency. All such emails will contain instructions for removing yourself from the associated email list.

We endeavour to only send email related to our products and services. If the content seems unrelated then it could be that third parties have fraudulently used our address to send spam. In any case, please accept our sincerest apologies for your inconvenience.

If you ever feel that you are the victim of spam originating from Caretta Software then please let us know by email to privacy@carettasoftware.com so that we can investigate the matter.
