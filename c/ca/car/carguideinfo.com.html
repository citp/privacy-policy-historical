CarGuideInfo may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. CarGuideInfo may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, CarGuideInfo does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns.
