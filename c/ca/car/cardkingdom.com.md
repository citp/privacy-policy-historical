# Card Kingdom - Magic: the Gathering, MTG, Magic Cards, Singles, EDH, Decks and Supplies

**Card Kingdom Privacy Policy**

Card Kingdom takes your privacy very seriously. We only use personal information collected on our checkout form for order completion and for contacting customers regarding issues with order completion. 

You may receive an email newsletter containing new products or site updates if you have subscribed to our newsletter via our homepage. You will also receive our newsletter if you have selected the email newsletter preference at checkout. If you have received unwanted email from Card Kingdom, please [click here](https://web.archive.org/web/20161204052223id_/http%3A//www.cardkingdom.com/static/index.php?sid=425942681&main=maillist&mode=unsubscribe) to unsubscribe from all Card Kingdom email lists. 

Card Kingdom will never under any circumstance sell or distribute the email address or personal information of any customer. 

All checkout transactions are conducted via secure sockets layer (SSL) encryption to protect your data. Card Kingdom does not store customer credit card information on its servers. 

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please [Contact Us](http://www.cardkingdom.com/static/support).
