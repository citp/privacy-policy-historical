# CARad - eBay Motors professional tool for selling a used car online.

This policy governs our use of the information you provide to us through the use of the CARad.com service. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use our service.

 **Information We Collect.**  
We collect contact, financial, and other information provided to us through electronic forms on our site, electronic data feeds and offline communications including but not limited to telephone conversations and paper forms. We may also collect browser and Internet related information through your access to our website and through cookies.

If you browse the website, we collect your browser information but we do not know who you are and you remain anonymous until you provide us with your contact information, if ever.

 **How We Use Your Information.**  
We use your information to provide you with access to our services and to bill you accordingly. We use your information to communicate with you and inform you of our services and offerings that may be targeted to your interests. We use your information to improve and personalize our services to make it quicker, safer and easier to use. We use your information to enforce our terms of service and resolve disputes.

 **How We Share Your Information.**  
We do not share your information with third parties for their marketing purposes without your express consent. If you instruct us to share your information in conjunction with or related to the performance of our service, we will share that information with those third parties. We may share anonymous aggregate data with third parties to help improve and customize our site. We share your information with third parties that act on our behalf to help provide our service and our offers to you. In these instances their use of your information is restricted by confidentiality agreements as well as by law. We will share your information with another business entity should we plan to, merge with or be acquired by that business entity. Should such a combination occur, we will require that the new combined entity follow this privacy policy. If your information will be used contrary to this policy, you will receive prior notice in accordance with this policy.

 **Your use of Information we provide you.**  
To the extent you receive information from us, you may only use it to facilitate the transaction or service related to. You may not spam or add people to marketing lists without their express consent.

 **Account Control.**  
You are responsible for all actions taken with your account and password, including fees. Therefore, we do not recommend that you disclose your password to any third party. If you feel your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately and change your password.

 **Reviewing and Changing Your Information.**  
You may review and change your information on your profile page or by contacting us through customer support.

 **Security.**  
We store your information on servers located in the United States of America. We use industry standard technical and administrative practices to safeguard the confidentiality of your information.

 **Notice.**  
We may amend this Privacy Policy at any time by posting the amended terms on our site and posting notice to you. All amended terms shall automatically be effective 10 days after they are initially posted on our site.
