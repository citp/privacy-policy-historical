Carne Golf Links Privacy Policy

This statement relates to our privacy practices in connection with this website, ie the official website for Carne Golf Links. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of any other websites listing information about Carne Golf Links, Belmullet or having hyper links to our web site. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of sites to whom we have hyper links from our website.

Collection and use of personal information

Turasoireacht Iorrais Teoranta, the owners of Carne Golf Links and the operators of the websites www.carnegolfinks.ie and www.carnegolflinks.com does not collect any personal data about you on this website. Any personal information which you volunteer to Carne Golf Links (for example by emailing us or using the online green fee booking system) will be treated with the highest standards of security and confidentiality, strictly in accordance with the Irish Data Protection Act of 1988. Any information you provide is not made available to any third parties for any purpose whatsoever and will only be used by us in line with the purpose for which you provided it. Your information will not be used to contact you with marketing information relating to Carne Golf Links (promotional offers etc) unless you have specifically "opted in" to receive such contacts on a regular basis. We may however contact you at the email address you have provided once only offering you a chance to opt in to our e-mail newsletter.

Collection and use of technical information.

Technical details in connection with your visit to our website are logged by our Internet hosting provider for statistical purposes only. This website does not use cookies. No information is collected which would allow us to identify individual website visitors. The technical details logged are the following items only :

The IP address of the visitors web client
The top level domain name used
The previous website address from which the visitor reached us
Any search terms used by the visitor on another site to reach us
Data which shows the traffic of visitors around our site (eg pages accessed etc)
The web browser used by the visitor.

This data is collected on our behalf by our web hosting provider. Apart from them (and they are bound by a confidentiality agreement) this technical data will not be disclosed to any third party whatsoever. We will never attempt to link technical details with individual visitors. The only exception to this will be if we are required to do so by a rule of law in the Republic of Ireland. The technical data will only be used by Carne Golf Links and only for statistical and other administrative purposes.
