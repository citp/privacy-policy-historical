User Provided Information: You provide us information about yourself and the vehicle(s) you drive, such as your name and email address, the year, make, model, sub-model, mileage, vehicle identification number (“VIN”) and maintenance history when you register with the Service. Any electronic correspondence with us may be retained by us. You may, however, visit our Website anonymously.


Website Activity: We collect information from you when you register on the Website, search the Website, engage in a transaction, enter a contest or sweepstakes, respond to a survey or communication such as e-mail, or participate in another Website feature. We follow some of the actions you take on carcarekiosk.com, such as (but not limited to) watching a video, uploading content or referring a friend.


Cookies and other Non-Edible, but Tasty Sounding Information Collection Processes: When you visit carcarekiosk.com, we may send one or more small non-executable text files to your computer that uniquely identifies your browser and lets us help you log in faster and enhance your navigation through the Website (aka: cookie). A cookie does not collect personal information about you and we use both session cookies and persistent cookies. We also use clear GIFs (aka: web beacons), JavaScript and Log File Information that may collect and store your IP address. All of this stuff is used to facilitate more accurate reporting, improve the carcarekiosk.com experience and help us diagnose and repair problems with our Service. CarCareKiosk may use Google Analytics or Mixpanel to enhance our understanding of use of the Service. Google Analytics and Mixpanel collect information sent by your browser as part of a web page request, including cookies and your IP address. Google Analytics and Mixpanel also receive this information and their use of it is governed by their privacy policies. Through these means, we automatically collect information about your internet connection, the equipment you use to access our Website and usage details that help us improve our Website to enhance your experience and gather information about visitors and visits to our Website.


Third Party Log In Credentials: By logging into the Website using a set of Third Party Credentials (such as Facebook), you will be giving us access to certain general personally identifying information stored in your profiles. We do not view this information without first obtaining your consent. In some instances we will provide you tools to share our Service with other people in your networks. We encourage, and may record, the frequency of this activity.
