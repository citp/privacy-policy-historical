# CareerBeacon.com

**OUR COMMITMENT**

CareerBeacontm, CareerBeacon.com, JobBeacon.com, MyBeacon, CareerBeaconXpress ("CBX") and Virtual Career Fair are owned by Brunswick News Inc., a division of J.D. Irving, Limited (collectively "CareerBeacon") and are or may be protected by trade-mark. Through CareerBeacon, we are pleased to provide a wide range of services to employers, professional recruiters and human resources professionals (collectively "Employers"), and prospective employees for which the collection, use and disclosure of varying degrees of personal information is necessary. At CareerBeacon, we understand the importance of preserving and safeguarding your personal information and privacy rights. CareerBeacon is committed to providing a safe and secure experience each time you visit and strives to meet the requirements of the **Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act** ("PIPEDA"), which regulates the manner in which CareerBeacon collects, uses, discloses and otherwise manages your personal information. By accessing CareerBeacon or electing to use one or more of our services, you have expressly consented to our collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this privacy policy (the "Privacy Policy") and have also agreed to be bound by the terms and conditions of use (the "Terms and Conditions") pertaining to CareerBeacon which are also posted on our site . 

**CHANGES TO PRIVACY POLICY**

This Privacy Policy may be modified by CareerBeacon at any time to reflect changes in our collection, use and disclosure of your personal information. At all times, your access to and use of CareerBeacon, and any services provided by CareerBeacon, indicates your continued acceptance of the terms of our Privacy Policy as may be amended from time to time. We encourage you to regularly review this Privacy Policy in order to ensure that your knowledge of CareerBeacon's collection, use and disclosure of your personal information remains current at all times. This Privacy Policy was last modified on May 2, 2007.

**INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE**

Depending upon which of our services you use, CareerBeacon will collect varying degrees of information from you. The services offered by CareerBeacon and the scope of information which must be disclosed in order to access these services are described below:

**      Anonymous Browsing**

You are able to use some of CareerBeacon's services anonymously without registering and providing personal information about yourself. With all visits to CareerBeacon, however, we do track your IP address to gather broad demographic information about you, including your location, the domain name you used to access CareerBeacon, the type and version of browser you are using, and your Internet Service provider. This information is used by CareerBeacon to assist us in administering CareerBeacon. We also collect aggregate information about how you are using CareerBeacon. This information is used by CareerBeacon to measure the number of visits, average time spent, page views and other general statistics about CareerBeacon in order to improve our services and does not include any information which could personally identify any of our users. Apart from the collection of this anonymous information, you will always know when CareerBeacon is collecting personal and other information from you and, consequently, you may decline to provide such information at any time.

**      Notifier**

The notifier service (the "Notifier Service") is made available to users who have logged on to MyBeacon. To subscribe to the Notifier Service, you must provide your e-mail address, establish a password and specify your language capabilities. You must also select the province(s) in which you are seeking employment and to specify the employment categories that apply to you. Once you have subscribed, we will advise you, via e-mail, of employment opportunities that fall within the criteria you have specified. You may unsubscribe to or deactivate our Notifer Service at any time by using the applicable online function. 

**      Send to a Friend**

All of CareerBeacon's employment postings have a "Send to a Friend" option. After you have logged on to MyBeacon, you can choose to send e-mails with links to a particular employment posting to people you think might be interested. In order to use this service, you must enter the e-mail address you want to send the posting to, your name and your e-mail address. We do not gather, track or record this information.

**      Newsletter**

The newsletter service offered by CareerBeacon (the "Newsletter Service") keeps you up to date regarding a range of employment-related topics that may be of interest to you. Should you wish to subscribe to our Newsletter Service, you must enter your e-mail address. You may unsubscribe to or deactivate our Newsletter Service at any time by using the applicable online function.

**      Survey**

CareerBeacon has an on-line survey feature. Your participation is completely voluntary. The information that is requested may include your name, your e-mail address and other demographic information. CareerBeacon reserves the right to disclose survey results to our clients once the information has been aggregated and to use survey results to improve our services and for general marketing purposes. 

**      Applying for Employment Opportunities Posted on CareerBeacontm \- Personal Profiles, Resumés and Cover Letters**

CareerBeacon offers a quick and convenient service to registered users who wish to electronically apply for employment opportunities posted on CareerBeacon. In order to use this service you must register and complete a personal profile. The personal profile includes your first name, last name, e-mail address, province or state and country. You may also choose to complete optional personal profile information fields. After completing your personal profile, you proceed to submit, finalize and post your resumé on CareerBeacon. You control the content of your resumé, including the amount of personal information contained therein. Typically, a resumé will include your full name, contact information, a summary of your work experience, education and relevant skills, and other information that may be of interest to Employers. Finally, you may also elect to post a cover letter on CareerBeacon, the content of which you also control. In addition to retaining control over the content of your resumé and cover letter, you also control which Employers are provided with your personal profile, resumé or cover letter. This information is not made available to any Employers unless you directly and voluntarily choose to make it available by applying for a specific employment posting on CareerBeacon. You may log on to CareerBeacon and modify your resumé or cover letter at any time. When you elect to electronically apply for posted employment opportunities via CareerBeacon and forward your personal profile, resumé and cover letter to an Employer through this service, you should expect that the entire content of your application materials, as provided, will be reviewed and that this information may be retained by those Employers who receive it for their own use. Before accessing the personal profile, resumé and cover letter service, you will have to specifically indicate your acceptance of the terms upon which CareerBeacon will collect, use and disclose the information you are submitting as described in this Privacy Policy by clicking "Accept" where indicated or using such other method to indicate your acceptance as may be presented. 

**      Use of Cookies**

At CareerBeacon, we use "cookies" to help measure the volume of visits to CareerBeacon, the average time spent using our services per visit, page views and other general statistics. Cookies are simple text files that Internet browsers are configured to automatically place on computer hard drives when websites are visited. Cookies cannot damage files, nor can they read information from your hard drive. By placing cookies on your hard drive, CareerBeacon is able to recall specific information from your earlier visits, thus enabling you to avoid the inconvenience of re-entering certain data each time you visit CareerBeacon. Adjustments can be made to most Internet browsers which enable you to decline all cookies or, alternatively, to accept or decline cookies on an individual basis. Please note that if you choose to decline cookies from CareerBeacon, it will result in a less efficient user experience and may also affect the functionality of certain service features.

**USE OF INFORMATION**

The information that we collect and that is posted by you on CareerBeacon is used to provide you with our services, to assist us with making improvements to such services and to communicate with you regarding new developments and changes to our services. CareerBeacon aggregates certain demographic information that we gather from you and others in order to facilitate our evaluation of the use and success of our services. This aggregated demographic information may be shared with our clients and used for marketing purposes. At no time does the use of such aggregated information compromise your personal information or privacy rights.

The mandate of CareerBeacon is to facilitate contact between Employers and prospective employees. By registering with CareerBeacon, posting your personal profile, resumé and cover letter on CareerBeacon and forwarding your application materials to Employers, you should expect that Employers will review and evaluate these materials in detail and use the personal and other information contained therein to make hiring decisions. You should also expect that Employers will use your personal information to contact you regarding interviews and offers of employment in relation to positions for which you have applied. 

Although CareerBeacon has implemented mechanisms to ensure that your personal profile, resumé and cover letter is only accessed by authorized Employers to which you have chosen to provide such personal and other information, we cannot guarantee that all such Employers will adhere to the limits that we impose on the use of such information through contractual and other means, or that unauthorized users will not gain access to CareerBeacon. Although we take all reasonable steps to safeguard your privacy, our control over the manner in which Employers retain and use your personal information is limited. We caution that you are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of the password you have chosen to access services offered by CareerBeacon. We encourage you to frequently change this password as a further means of protecting your personal information. 

**DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION**

The personal information you provide to us will be disclosed to authorized users of CareerBeacon as described in this Privacy Policy. CareerBeacon will not sell, share or rent the information that you provide to third parties in any manner except as set out in this Privacy Policy. CareerBeacon reserves the right to disclose your personal information to companies that we contract with and individuals that we employ to carry out functions on our behalf only to the extent required to carry out such functions. In addition, circumstances may arise in which CareerBeacon may have an obligation to disclose your personal information to comply with the law, prevent a crime or protect our own interests. CareerBeacon may also disclose your personal information to third parties who acquire all or a significant part of our business, including by merger, amalgamation or sale of assets.

**UPDATING/RETAINING/DELETING INFORMATION**

CareerBeacon takes all reasonable steps to ensure that the personal or other information you provide to us is accurately displayed as provided. You may review, correct, update, change or delete your personal or other information found on CareerBeacon at any time by logging into your account and following the instructions provided. 

You may terminate your CareerBeacon account at any time and erase your personal profile, resumé and cover letter by sending an e-mail request to us. However, certain personal information may continue to remain accessible to Employers if the information, such as your name and e-mail address, was collected and stored in individual Employer accounts prior the date of such termination. CareerBeacon also retains archived account information, such as your name and e-mail address, that is not made available to any users. 

We have developed and implemented guidelines for managing personal information that is no longer necessary or relevant for the purposes identified or that is no longer required or permitted by law to be retained. We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes identified in this Privacy Policy, subject to the retention of archived account information, as described above. 

We offer you the opportunity to opt-out or discontinue receiving communications from CareerBeacon, including our Newsletter Service and Notifier Service using the applicable online function.

**SECURITY**

CareerBeacon has taken reasonable precautions to protect your personal information and privacy rights. When you submit personal or sensitive information to us, your information is protected both on and off-line. We have established appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to secure and safeguard the information we collect.

Our employees and the third parties with which we contract to provide services to you do not access user information, except as a requirement to maintain CareerBeacon and the services we offer. All our employees and the third parties with which we contract to provide services to you have been educated regarding the sensitivity of your personal and other information and the importance we place on privacy. We are committed to the protection of your privacy.

When using CareerBeacon, Employers can rest assured that the confidentiality of any information provided to CareerBeacon that is not disclosed to prospective employees through employment postings will be protected.
