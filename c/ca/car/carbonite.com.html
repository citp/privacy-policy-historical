During these interactions we will collect Personal Information relevant to your transacting with Carbonite, such as your name, mailing address, phone number, e-mail address and credit card information along with information about the Carbonite products you own, such as the activation code, date of purchase and information relating to a support issue. Cookies and Other Technologies

Following common e-commerce practices, Carbonite uses "cookies" and other technologies to collect non-personal data that enables us to better understand and improve the usability, performance and effectiveness of our Website. Cookies are unique text files sent to your browser and stored on your computer. If you do not want Carbonite to send cookies to your browser, you can set your browser options to reject cookies or notify you when a Website tries to put a cookie in your browser software. Rejecting cookies may affect your ability to use some features of our Website. Additionally, Carbonite gathers general information about visits to our Website and stores this information in log files. This information includes the number of total visits to our Website, and which browser is used to view our Website (including the version number). No Personal Information is contained in this data. Carbonite uses this information to understand traffic and downloads on our Website, enabling us to improve the site, provide the best online experience possible and improve our ability to serve our customers.

Carbonite's Commitment to the Security of your Personal Information

Carbonite has security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of your Personal Information in our customer database. Personal Information provided to Carbonite is stored in secure facilities with access restricted to authorized personnel only. To provide secure credit card processing when ordering, orders placed on our Website are processed through a payment gateway which operates under its own privacy policy. Carbonite does not store credit card information unless you choose an automatic renewal subscription plan. If you choose to purchase the software online using a credit card, the credit card information is sent to Carbonite using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption, an industry-standard method for protecting data as it travels over the Internet. Although we make good faith efforts to store the Personal Information we collect in a secure operating environment that is not accessible to unauthorized users, we cannot guarantee complete security. We currently use credit card processing services from VeriSign, Inc., our "payment gateway." VeriSign, Inc. has its own privacy policy which may be viewed at http://www.verisign.com/Privacy/index.html

Carbonite's Commitment to the Privacy and Security of your Backed Up Data

Carbonite has a number of security measures in place to protect the privacy and security of your backed up data. Your data is encrypted before it ever leaves your PC and encrypted again using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) during transmission to our secure data centers. Your encrypted backup files are stored in secure facilities with access restricted to authorized personnel only. Carbonite will not share your encrypted files with any third party unless such action is necessary to comply with a government or court order legally compelling us to do so.
