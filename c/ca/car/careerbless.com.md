# Privacy Policy

### PRIVACY POLICY

**Overview**

At www.careerbless.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information which is received and collected by www.careerbless.com and how it is used. The personal information which is submitted while registering to the site, will not be distributed or shared with any third-parties. We only use this data for our information, for research, to improve our services and for contacting you. www.careerbless.com is an open portal, so registering into site is not mandatory and also we won't verify your email address. Registration into the site is purely of user's interest. www.careerbless.com reserves the right to change this policy at any time and all such changes will be published in this site. 

**Log Files**

Like many other websites, www.careerbless.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, text inputs, and clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. 

**Cookies and Web Beacons**

www.careerbless.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the users access or visit, customize web page content based on visitors browser types or other information that the visitors sends via their browsers.

Third party vendors, including [Google](https://www.google.com/), use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to www.careerbless.com . Users may visit those websites to opt out of the use of cookies for interest-based advertising.

Google's use of the DoubleClick Cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to the users based on their visit to www.careerbless.com and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting [Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/ads/preferences).

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. www.careerbless.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers. You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. www.careerbless.com's privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites. 

For all other policies, please read our terms and conditions.
