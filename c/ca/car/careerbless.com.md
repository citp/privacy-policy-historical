# Privacy Policy

### Privacy Policy

**Overview**

At www.careerbless.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information which is received and collected by www.careerbless.com and how it is used. The Personal information which is submitted while registering to the site, will not be distributed and shared with any third-parties. We only use this data for our information, for research, to improve our services and for contacting you. www.careerbless.com is an open portal, so registering into site is not mandatory and also we won't verify your email address. Registration into the site is purely of user's interest. www.careerbless.com reserves the right to change this policy at any time and all such changes will be published in this site. 

**Log Files**

Like many other websites, www.careerbless.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, number of clicks to analyze trends and demographic information.

**Cookies**

www.careerbless.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit and customize webpage content based on visitors browser type.

Third party vendors, including [Google](https://www.google.com/), use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to www.careerbless.com . Users may visit those websites to opt out of the use of cookies for interest-based advertising.

Google's use of the DoubleClick Cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to the users based on their visit to www.careerbless.com and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting [Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/ads/preferences).

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.
