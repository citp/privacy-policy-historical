# Carrotfry privacy

  


  1. This privacy statement describes how carrotfry collects and uses the personal information you provide on our website: [ www.carrotfry.com ](http://www.carrotfry.com/)

  2. It also describes the choices available to you regarding our use of your personal information and how you can access and update this information.

  3. If you have questions or concerns regarding this policy, you should contact us at Mail to: **support@carrotfry.com**



  1. Contact Information such as name, email address, mailing address, phone number.

  2. Unique Identifiers such as user name, account number, password.

  3. Preferences Information such as product wish lists, order history, marketing preferences.



  1. Demographic information such as age, education, gender, and zip code.

  2. As it is true of most websites, we automatically gather information about your computer such as your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and operating system.



  1. Assess the needs of your business to determine suitable products.

  2. Send you requested product or service information.

  3. Send product updates or warranty information.

  4. Respond to customer service requests.

  5. Administrate your account.

  6. Send you a newsletter.

  7. Send you marketing communications.

  8. Administer contests and sweepstakes you entered, and notify you if you won.

  9. Respond to your questions and concerns.

  10. Improve our Web site and marketing efforts.

  11. Conduct research and analysis.




You may choose to stop receiving our newsletter or marketing emails by following the unsubscribe instructions included in these emails or you can contact us at Mail to: **support@carrotfry.com**

  1. Our Web pages contain electronic images known as Web beacons (sometimes called single-pixel gifs) and are used along with cookies to compile aggregated statistics to analyze how our site is used and may be used in some of our emails to let us know which emails and links have been opened by recipients. This allows us to gauge the effectiveness of our customer communications and marketing campaigns.

  2. We gather information about how you and others use our Web site. For example, we will know how many users access a specific page and which links they clicked on. We use this aggregated information to understand and optimize how our site is used.



  1. Our Site includes links to other Web sites whose privacy practices may differ from those of Carrotfry. If you submit personal information to any of those sites, your information is governed by their privacy statements. We encourage you to carefully read the privacy statement of any website you visit.



  1. The security of your personal information is important to us. When you enter sensitive information we encrypt the transmission of that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL).

  2. We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

  3. If you have any questions about security on our Web site, you can contact us at Mail us: **support@carrotfry.com**



  1. Our Web site may include Widgets, which are interactive mini-programs that run on our site to provide specific services from another company (e.g. displaying the news, opinions, music, etc). Personal information, such as your email address, may be collected through the Widget. Cookies may also be set by the Widget to enable it to function properly. Information collected by this Widget is governed by the privacy policy of the company that created it.

  2. Our Web site offers publicly accessible blogs or community forums. You should be aware that any information you provide in these areas may be read, collected, and used by others who access them.



  1. To review and update your personal information to ensure it is accurate, contact us at Mail us: **support@carrotfry.com**



  1. We may update this privacy statement to reflect changes to our information practices. If we make any material changes we will notify you by email (sent to the e-mail address specified in your account) or by means of a notice on this site prior to the change becoming effective. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.




You can contact us about this privacy statement by email us at the address below:

  1. Email: **support@carrotfry.com**
