# Privacy Policy - Carter's

**Our Commitment  
**The Carter’s brand has an excellent reputation built on generations of trust. Maintaining your privacy and trust is important to us at Carter’s, Inc. We are committed to managing your personal information carefully and respecting your privacy. 

**What Matters**  
We make it easy for you to update your personal information and preferences. To update your information or make any changes, please contact Carter’s customer service by calling 1-888-782-9548, by emailing [contactus@carters.com](mailto:contactus@carters.com) or by sending a note to _Carter’s Consumer Affairs, 112 Otter Avenue, Oshkosh, WI 54901_. 

**Our Policy**  
Carter’s, Inc. may use personal information provided by you verbally or in writing (including via electronic media) to communicate products, services and promotions that may be beneficial to you and to enhance your overall brand experience. From time to time, we may share your personal information with third parties that have a product or service that we believe may be of interest to you. If for any reason you do not wish us to share your information, you can always contact Carter’s customer service by calling 1-888-782-9548, by emailing [contactus@carters.com](mailto:contactus@carters.com?subject=do%20not%20share) with “do not share” in the subject line or by sending a note to _Carter’s Consumer Affairs, 112 Otter Avenue, Oshkosh, WI 54901_. 

Carter’s may disclose your personal information as required by law or legal process, and as necessary to protect the property, interests and rights of Carter’s, its affiliates and others. In the event of a sale of some or all of our business, we may disclose, license or sell your personal information to the purchaser. 

If we amend this privacy policy, we will post any such change on our website at www.carters.com. If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact Carter’s customer service at 1-888-782-9548.
