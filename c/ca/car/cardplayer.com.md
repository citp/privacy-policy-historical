# Privacy Policy

|  | 

  


CardPlayer.com takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy. 

**What This Privacy Policy Covers**

  * This policy covers how CardPlayer.com treats personal information that CardPlayer.com collects and receives, including information related to your past use of CardPlayer.com products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable, like your name, address, e-mail address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available. 
  * This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that CardPlayer.com does not own or control, or to people that CardPlayer.com does not employ or manage. 



**Information Collection and Use**

**General**

  * CardPlayer.com collects personal information when you register with CardPlayer.com, when you use CardPlayer.com products or services, when you visit CardPlayer.com pages or the pages of certain CardPlayer.com partners, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. CardPlayer.com may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies. 
  * When you register, we ask for information such as your name, e-mail address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. Once you register with CardPlayer.com and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us. 
  * CardPlayer.com collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners. 
  * CardPlayer.com automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, CardPlayer.com cookie information, and the page you request. 
  * CardPlayer.com uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients. 



**Children**

When creating an account, we require the new member to certify that he or she is 18 years old or over by checking the appropriate box. If a child under age 18 attempts to register with CardPlayer.com, we ask that he or she have a parent or guardian create a CardPlayer.com Family Account to obtain parental permission. 

  * CardPlayer.com will not contact children under age 18 about special offers or for marketing purposes without a parent's permission. 
  * CardPlayer.com does not ask a child under age 18 for more personal information, as a condition of participation, than is reasonably necessary to participate in a given activity or promotion. 



**Information Sharing and Disclosure**

  * CardPlayer.com does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances: 
    * We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; 
    * We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of CardPlayer.com's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law. 
    * We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with CardPlayer.com under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help CardPlayer.com communicate with you about offers from CardPlayer.com and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information. 
  * CardPlayer.com displays targeted advertisements based on personal information. Advertisers (including ad serving companies) may assume that people who interact with, view, or click on targeted ads meet the targeting criteria - for example, women ages 18-24 from a particular geographic area. 
    * CardPlayer.com does not provide any personal information to the advertiser when you interact with or view a targeted ad. However, by interacting with or viewing an ad, you are consenting to the possibility that the advertiser will make the assumption that you meet the targeting criteria used to display the ad. 



**Cookies**

  * CardPlayer.com may set and access CardPlayer.com cookies on your computer. 
  * Cookies are anonymous .txt files that allow us to remember you and your preferences from previous visits. CardPlayer.com cookies collect no personally identifiable information. 



**Confidentiality and Security**

  * We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs. 



**Changes to this Privacy Policy**

  * CardPlayer.com may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary e-mail address specified in your CardPlayer.com account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
