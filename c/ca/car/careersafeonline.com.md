# CareerSafe Online || Privacy Policy

At CareerSafe your privacy is of utmost concern. We will protect the information you provide to us and will not share, sell, rent, or otherwise distribute that information to any entity outside of CareerSafe unless you grant us permission to do so.

In some parts of this site we may ask you for personal information (including, but not limited to, your name, address, phone, employer, credit card information, and email address) in order to register you with the site, to register you for a training course or program, to enable you to shop in our online stores or pay for training courses or programs, or to participate in online promotions. All online financial transactions are processed using SSL encryption technology in order to provide you with the most secure experience possible. 

CareerSafe may use the information mentioned above in order to inform you of new products or services or to provide you information about upcoming events in which CareerSafe is involved. If you do not want our representatives to contact you, please let us know in one of the following ways:

Please be sure to include your name, address, phone, and email address (where applicable) in order to ensure prompt and accurate attention to your request.

CareerSafe does use cookies in order to provide you with a customized user experience and to track user trends, preferences, and usage patterns. The information stored in cookies will be used for internal purposes only and will not be shared with third parties. Should you decide not to enable cookies in your browser, you will still be able to view our web pages, but you may not be able to take advantage of several of our online services. 

CareerSafe uses log file analysis tools to track general site usage patterns and frequencies. The information collected is non-user specific and is used by CareerSafe to monitor site traffic, performance, usage patterns, system design specifications, performance problems, hardware utilization, etc. This information is used for site management purposes only and will not be shared with third parties.

Should you have questions regarding the CareerSafe Privacy Policy, please contact us using one of the methods listed above. This policy is subject to change without notice.
