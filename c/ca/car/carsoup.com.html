What information do we collect? How do we use it?
When you place an ad with CarSoup, we ask for your name, e-mail address, mailing address, credit card number and expiration date. This allows us to process and post your ad as quickly as possible and to notify you of its status. This same information allows us to accurately process your order for CarSoup products and services.

When you participate in a contest or other promotional feature we may ask for your name, address and e-mail address so we can administer the contest or promotion.

We may also use the information we collect to notify you of important functionality changes to the CarSoup web site, new CarSoup services and special offers.

How does CarSoup protect customer information?
CarSoup's Secure Server Software (SSL) encrypts all account and personal information you input before it is sent to us. In addition, all customer data we collect is carefully protected from unauthorized access. CarSoup.com does not sell, trade or rent your personal information to others.

Will Carsoup.com disclose the information it collects to outside parties?
CarSoup.com does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.
