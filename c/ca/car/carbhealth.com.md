## PRIVACY POLICY

CarbHealth will never provide any of your personal information including name or email address to any third parties. Said parties may be able to derive such information themselves from postings in the address book or forums. CarbHealth and its creators are not responsible for third parties getting hold of your personal information. You are posting your information of your own free will and you are solely responsible if your information is obtained by any third parties.

We value your privacy and will never provide information to anyone. We apologize if they are able to obtain it on their own, but we are not responsible if they do so. Thanks.
