Privacy Policy

Customer Information
When reserving a vehicle, joining one of our programs, or enrolling in our emailing list, we need to collect certain information about you in order to provide the specific service that you have requested. We make every reasonable effort to protect this information on your behalf.


Email Policy
We will only send email to your address once you have made a reservation and supplied us with your email address. You may also receive emails about programs or promotions in our newsletter. If you wish to have your name removed from our e-newsletter mailing list, you can easily unsubscribe by clicking the "unsubscribe link" at the bottom of each newsletter, or by sending an email to newsletter@carrentals.com


Credit Card/SSL Policy
If you are asked for your credit card information, this information will first be passed through a Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) before being sent over the Internet. The SSL first scrambles the data that is to be sent and then sends it to the correct receiver. Once this receiver accepts the data, it then validates the sender and unscrambles the data. Therefore, you may feel secure when supplying your credit card information on this site.

 
Cookie Policy
As is the standard for many major websites, this website may employ the use of "cookies". These "cookies" are files that are sent to your computer to store data about your visit to our site. This data can help to speed your rental process, such as remembering you when you return to this site. You have the ability to disable cookies, or have the browser that you are using inform you of when a cookie is being sent to your computer. We use third-party advertising companies to serve our ads on the Internet. These third-party advertising companies employ cookie and Clear Gif technology (aka 1x1 Pixel .gifs, Web bugs) to measure and improve the effectiveness of ads for their clients. To do so, these companies may use anonymous information about your visits to our Web site and other Web sites. This information can include: date/time of banner ad shown, the banner ad that was shown, their cookie, the IP address. This information can also be used for online preference marketing purposes.You may also delete these cookies from your computer at any time. However, if you delete or disable cookies on your computer, you may reduce the functionality of our website.
