Catapult Privacy and Cookie Policy

INTRODUCTION

Catapult Systems respects your privacy and recognizes your need for appropriate protection and management of any personally identifiable information you choose to provide us. Catapult Systems has established this Privacy and Cookie Policy so that you may better understand the purpose for which we collect and use personal information.
Personal information means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, a physical address, an email address, or other contact information including, gender, occupation, personal interests, or other similar information.

 

WHY WE ASK YOU TO SHARE PERSONAL DATA

The purposes and reasons for processing your personal data are included, but not limited to:

  • Buying Catapult Systems solutions and services
  • Receiving information about Catapult Systems solutions, services, and events
  • Participating in Catapult Systems online communities, including our blog, social media channels/pages
  • Storing your preferences for future interactions and communications from Catapult Systems
  • Resolving customer service issues or other service/solution complaints
  • Helping us to improve solutions and services
  • Creating campaigns that are designed to benefit you, allowing us to optimize customer service
  • Receiving personalized messages, special offers and advertisements that are relevant to your personal interests, based on the information you have shared with us and on the information we have collected through cookies regarding your use of the Catapult Systems website/social media/blogs. For more information on how we use cookies, see our Cookie Clause below.

 

TYPES OF DATA WE MAY COLLECT

When you interact with Catapult Systems, for instance by signing up for our blog, purchasing a solution/service or interacting with Catapult Systems via social media, e.g. “like us on Facebook,” we may ask for or receive certain data, such as:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your telephone number
  • Your social media profile
  • Your gender
  • Your age
  • Information about the products and services you have purchased
  • Your usage of and activities on our social media pages
  • Your interests
  • Other information related to your online behavior

Also, depending on the choices you have made regarding your settings on various social media sites, certain personal data may be shared with Catapult Systems about your online activities and social media profiles, e.g. interests, marital status, gender, user name, photo, comments you have posted, etc.
In addition, we may collect personal data via cookies or similar techniques, including, but not limited to:

  • Your IP address
  • Your cookie ID
  • Your web browser
  • Your location
  • The web pages you visit on our websites
  • The advertisements you’ve viewed or clicked through
  • Etc.

When you interact with Catapult Systems on behalf of a business customer, supplier, or business partner of Catapult Systems, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your telephone number
  • Your IP address
  • Your business address
  • Name and address of the company you work for

 

HOW WE COLLECT YOUR PERSONAL DATA

  • Contacting us offline via courier, ground mail, telephone, email, or facsimile and providing any personal data
  • Requesting information about our solutions or services via one of our website form
  • Registering for an in-person event or webinar
  • Ordering a solution or service; signing a statement of work
  • Registering for an online account or a mobile application
  • Subscribing to a Catapult Systems blog
  • Cookies placed on your computer or mobile device when visiting our website
  • Social listening: Catapult Systems may search the internet for relevant and publicly available content and use it to improve its products and services, resolve consumer issues, and provide targeted marketing

 

USE OF COOKIES ON OUR WEBSITE

A ‘cookie’ is a small piece of data sent from a website and stored on the user’s computer by the user’s web browser while the user is browsing. When you visit a site that uses cookies for the first time, a cookie is downloaded onto your computer/mobile device so that the next time you visit that site, your device will remember useful information such as visited pages, information you that were interested in, or your login options.

Catapult Systems uses cookies to help you get the most out of Catapult Systems solutions and services. In order to manage your cookie preferences, you may alter the cookie settings in your browser settings at any time. If you do disable cookies in your browser settings, you will find that you will not be permitted to enter our website. This will occur across all affiliated Catapult Systems sites.

 

Can a website user block cookies?

Yes. As explained below, under Managing Cookies, you can disable our cookies on www.catapultsystems.com. However, please be aware that this will reduce the functionality of our website. Please remember that if you do choose to disable cookies, you may find that certain sections of our website do not work properly.

 

HOW TO MANAGE COOKIES

As we explain in our Cookie Policy, cookies help you to get the most out of our websites. If you do disable our cookies you may find that certain sections of our website will not work. For example, you may have difficulties logging in or viewing certain information about our products. Unless you disable our cookies, your continued use of our website will be considered to be your consent to our use of cookies on this device in accordance with our cookie policy. USE OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA

Catapult Systems takes privacy and security very seriously and strives to put our website users first in all aspects of our business. We will never disclose, share, or sell your data without your consent, unless required to do so by law.

Catapult Systems will only retain your data for as long as necessary and for the purposes specified in this notice. We have strict review and retention policies in place to meet these obligations. If you may be a customer, we may be required under United States Federal laws to keep your basic personal data (name, address, contact details) for a minimum of 6 years after which time it will be destroyed.

The Catapult Systems website is not generally targeted toward anyone under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under 18 years of age on our website. Our solutions, services, and events are all directed to people who are at least 18 years old or older.

We do not automatically enroll you, or “opt you into” any marketing or mailing lists. Rather, when you express an interest in our solutions or services, we will ask you to confirm your consent freely and expressly in order that we may then contact you.

Again, we will only contact you, after you have first expressed your consent to learn more about our solutions or services that you have expressed interest in.
Processing for any purpose other than those specified in this policy is strictly prohibited. All interactions with you occur following your consent, which you are free to withdraw at any time.
