# CatFight - CatFight

##  [ Privacy Policy ](http://www.catfight.com/component/content/article/11-legal/9-privacy-policy)

Privacy Policy   
We are committed to protecting your privacy. We will only use the information that we collect about you lawfully   
(in accordance with the Data Protection Act).   
We collect information about you for a variety of reasons:   
• so we can process your order,   
• so we can contact you in case we have a problem with your order,   
• so we can send you our regular newsletter (if you chose to subscribe) 

We do not sell your details to advertising companies. We will not e-mail you in the future unless you have given us your consent.   
You always have the option to easily unsubscribe from our mailing list.   
The type of information we will collect about you includes:   
• your name   
• your postal address   
• your phone number   
• your email address   
• your orders

We will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent.   
The information we hold will be accurate and up to date. You can check the information that we hold about you by emailing us.   
Or by visiting the Your Account.

If you find any inaccuracies we will delete or correct it promptly.   
The personal information which we hold will be held securely in accordance with our internal security policy and the law.

Any problems or queries? Please Contact Us.
