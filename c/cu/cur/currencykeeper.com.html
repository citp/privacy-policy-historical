HobbySoft Privacy Policy

HobbySoft respects your privacy.

What this Privacy Policy Covers
This Privacy Policy covers HobbySoft's treatment of personally identifiable information that we collect when you are on the HobbySoft site, and when you purchase HobbySoft services and products.

Information Collection and Use
HobbySoft collects personally identifiable information when you subscribe to receive catalogs, newsletters, and product notices, when you participate in certain promotions, when you download demo software, and when you order from us.

Information Sharing and Disclosure
HobbySoft will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.

Cookies
HobbySoft may set and access HobbySoft cookies on your computer. These are used to assist you in filling out forms, and to help you shop for our products on our site.

You Can Change How We Contact You
HobbySoft maintains customer preferences for what kind of contacts our customers would like from us. You can ask us at any time to begin or stop sending catalogs, e-newsletters, and customer notices, and to change contact information in your record. We appreciate when you tell us what your interests and needs are. We make these changes to our customer service systems whenever you request.

Security
Your Information is kept in password-protected systems for your privacy and security.
In areas where you are purchasing HobbySoft products online, HobbySoft uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
HobbySoft may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages.

Questions or Suggestions
If you have questions or suggestions, please contact us.
