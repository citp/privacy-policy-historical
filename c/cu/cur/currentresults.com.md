# Privacy and Cookies at Current Results

Current Results Publishing Ltd. respects privacy and does not automatically collect or use personal information for any purpose. We never sell, rent or share personal information in any way with another party.

Webpages at CurrentResults.com use browser cookies to store anonymous information, and in some cases IP addresses, that help to improve the website and provide advertising. The cookies used here come from Cookie Control, CloudFlare, Google Analytics, Google AdSense and AddThis.

Cookie Control cookies are set to anonymously remember your cookie preferences. These cookies only apply to site visitors from the European Union.

CloudFlare may use a cookie to help maintain this website's security. The cookie does not store any personally identifiable information.

Google Universal Analytics gives us feedback on how visitors use this website. Analytics uses cookies to collect basic anonymous information such as how people arrive here, their type of device and the pages they view. We use that information to improve CurrentResults.com. You can learn more about [Google Analytics](http://www.google.com/analytics/learn/privacy.html) and [opt out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) if you wish.

Google uses DoubleClick cookies in AdSense ads on this website. The cookies enable Google and its partner ad vendors to serve advertising based on your visit to this site and other sites on the internet. DoubleClick cookies contain no personally identifiable information of visitors from the European Union. The cookies allow for frequency capping, aggregated ad reporting, and combating fraud and abuse. For visitors from outside the EU, the cookies also use IP addresses to target advertising based on what's relevant to you and to avoid showing ads that you have already seen. You may opt out of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising at [Google's Ad Settings](http://www.google.com/ads/preferences/).

AddThis provides the social sharing tools offered on our webpages. In doing so, AddThis uses a cookie or a third party web beacon to collect visitors' anonymous information and IP addresses. It may use cookies during your visits to this and other websites to provide ads about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you. You can easily stop AddThis from collecting your information for online behavioral advertising by [opting out at AddThis](http://www.addthis.com/privacy/opt-out).

To learn more about interest-based advertising and your cookie choices visit [Digital Advertising Alliance Self-Regulatory Program](http://www.aboutads.info/) and [Network Advertising Initiative](http://www.networkadvertising.org/understanding-online-advertising/what-are-my-options). People in the European Union can also find information at [Your Online Choices](http://www.youronlinechoices.eu/).
