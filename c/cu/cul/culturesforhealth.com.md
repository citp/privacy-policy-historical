# Privacy Policy

**Your privacy is important to us we will not share your information with any third-party except as required by law or as necessary to complete an order placed by you with us. To respect your privacy, all information collected on this Site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, disclosed, or loaned unless you consent otherwise! Please feel free to call or[ email us](mailto:customersupport@culturesforhealth.com?subject=Customer%20Support "Privacy Policy Inquiry") with any questions regarding our privacy policy.**
