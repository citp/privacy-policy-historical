# CustomerServiceNumbers.com :: Privacy Policy

**CustomerServiceNumbers.com Privacy Policy**

July 31, 2008

CustomerServiceNumbers realizes that your privacy is important. This Policy applies to CSN's Website CustomerServiceNumbers.com. CustomerServiceNumbers.Com created this policy to demonstrate our commitment to your privacy. We will never share or sell your email address and or any other personal information.

If you have any questions about this Policy, please feel free to contact us through our website or email us at [info@customerservicenumbers.com](mailto:info@customerservicenumbers.com "mailto:info@customerservicenumbers.com")

**Information we ask for and how we use it**

CustomerServiceNumbers.com CSN may collect the following types of information:

**Information you provide** \- When you add a company or comment on a page, we ask you for personal information (such as your first name (nickname), email address). For services, such as our premium listings, we also request payment information which is secured through Paypal's Payment System. We will never ask for your paypal or other payment password. If you receive any such notice from any party other than paypal, please contact Paypal's fraud department. 

**Cookies** \- When you visit CustomerServiceNumbers.com, we send a google analytics cookie to your computer that identifies your computer. Google uses cookies to improve the quality of Their service by storing user preferences and tracking user trends, such as how people search. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some Google or CustomerServiceNumbers.com features and services may not work properly if your cookies are disabled.

**Log information** \- When you use customerservicenumbers.com, Google's or other search engine servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website. These cookies may include information such as your web request, IP Address, browser, browser language, the date and time of your request and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.

**Information sharing**

CustomerServiceNumbers.com does not share or sell your personal email address provided by you. Also, we do not publish such email addresses.

**Public Forums & Comments**

CustomerServiceNumbers.com  may make forums, message boards, comment sections and or news groups available to it's users. Please remember that information disclosed on those sections become public information. We do not ask for your full name and we do not publish your email address in any such section. However, if you type in any such private information in a form that is not specifically name or email address, such information could be published. We are not responsible for any such postings where you specifically make an effort to publish personal information. If personal information is found, our administrators have authorization to delete your information and deny your posting privileges.

**Form Submissions**

Customer Service Numbers makes use of forms on its sites for users to comment or suggest companies. The name and email address is used only to contact the person for any questions related to the post or listing. We DO NOT share emails with our Advertisers in any form.

**Advertisers**

We may use outside companies to display text ads, banner ads or other advertising on our site. These ads may contain cookies. Cookies received by an advertising is not controlled by 

CustomerServiceNumbers.com and we do not have access to or control this information.

**Enforcement**

CustomerServiceNumbers regularly reviews its compliance with this Policy. Please feel free to direct any questions or concerns regarding this Policy or CustomerServiceNumbers's handing of personal information by contacting us by email: [info@customerservicenumbers.com](mailto:info@customerservicenumbers.com "mailto:info@customerservicenumbers.com"). CSN will cooperate with the appropriate regulatory authorities, including local data protection authorities, to resolve any complaints regarding the unauthorized use of personal data.

**Changes to this policy**

Please note that this Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will note significant changes to this policy on this page or by email notification.
