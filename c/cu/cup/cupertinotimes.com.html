Privacy Policy: CupertinoTimes.com

The privacy of our visitors is important to us.

At Cupertino Times, we recognize that the privacy of your personal information is important. Whenever you visit our website, our web server will recognize who you are, your browser, operating system, IP address etc.

When you comment on any post you will need to provide the user name and email id which will be stored in the database. Your personal information will never be released, sold, or rented to any entities or individuals outside CupertinoTimes.

We use third party advertisements on CupertinoTimes.com to support our site. Some of these advertisers may use technology such as cookies and web beacons when they advertise on our site. Google, through their Adsense Program, uses cookies to serve ads on this site. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to this and other sites. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
