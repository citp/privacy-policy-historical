# Corning Museum of Glass | Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy 

To protect your privacy when visiting the The Corning Museum of Glass website, we have adopted the following policies to safeguard any personal information: 

The CMoG web server tracks visitors to the site and collects generalized information on the number of visits, most popular pages, domain names, countries, downloads, etc. Additionally, some pages throughout the site may request specific information submitted by a form or e-mail. Information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, e-mail addresses, etc. will only be used by CMoG to respond to inquiries, or for specific CMoG marketing activities, events or services. If you have submitted personal information to CMoG and would like it removed or modified, please contact us and we shall use reasonable efforts to comply with your request. CMoG reserves the right to retain electronic communications sent by visitors. Further, CMoG uses a SMTP gateway to forward e-mail and to log e-mail transactions. Logs of e-mail transactions include: 

  * Date and time of message 
  * Name and IP address of connecting host 
  * E-mail address of message originator 
  * CMoG address of recipient



The Corning Museum of Glass does not intend to collect information from children under the age of thirteen (13) without the consent of the child's parent or legal guardian. Children under the age of thirteen (13) should only submit information to CMoG with the prior consent of their parent or legal guardian. If your child has submitted personal information to the The Corning Museum of Glass and you would like to request that such information be removed please contact us and we shall use reasonable efforts to remove such information. 

Information supplied to the CMoG through the CMoG Web site, including but not limited to feedback, data, answers, questions, comments, suggestions, plans, ideas or the like, will not be treated as proprietary or as confidential and CMoG assumes no obligation to protect such information from disclosure. Further, the submission of such information to the CMoG shall not restrict the purchase, manufacture or use of similar products, services, plans and ideas by CMoG or any purpose whatever and CMoG shall be free to reproduce, use, disclose and distribute the information to others without restriction. 

The Corning Museum of Glass does not endorse, nor does it participate in the practice of "spamming" (sending of unsolicited promotional e-mail). If you believe you have received such a message contact [web@cmog.org](mailto:web@cmog.org) .
