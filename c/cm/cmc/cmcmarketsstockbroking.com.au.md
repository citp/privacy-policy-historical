     Access to Information by Customers
     Customers can gain access to any information CMC Markets holds
     about them by calling +1300 660 262 or emailing
     clientservices@cmcmarkets.com.au. We will process your request
     usually within 14 days. If your request is complex, more time may
     be needed in which case we will advise you of the extra time required.
     There is no fee for requesting access to your information, although
     CMC Markets may charge you the reasonable cost of processing your
     request.
     In some circumstances the law may allow CMC Markets to deny you
     access. In such a case we will explain to you the reason for refusing
     access.
     Correction of Information
     We ask that you promptly notify us of any changes to the personal
     information that CMC Markets holds about you. This ensures that
     your personal information is up to date and enables us to keep you
     informed of the continuing circumstances of your account, your
     margin obligations and your trading activities.
     You may ask us at any time to correct personal information held by
     CMC Markets about you, and which you believe is inaccurate. Should
     we disagree with you as to the accuracy of the information, you may
     request that we attach a statement to that information noting that you
     consider it inaccurate or incomplete.
