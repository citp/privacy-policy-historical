CMSWatch Privacy Policy

CMSWatch is dedicated to protecting the privacy of its online audience. We adhere to a strict privacy policy to secure trust and foster confidence among our community of visitors.

CMSWatch requires you to provide various types of personally identifiable information in order to use certain features on our site, such as the CMSWatch e-newsletter and the free sample chapter download of the CMSWatch Report. We will not share your personally identifiable information with any third party organization without first obtaining your explicit consent. You are not under any obligation to provide your personal information if you wish to forego access to registration-only areas or features on the site.

CMSWatch also collects a variety of anonymous data that is not associated with a specific individual. This information is captured and analyzed for statistical purposes and to improve the performance of the site. CMSWatch gathers data on the number of visitors to the site, users' IP addresses, referral data, and browser and platform type.
