Privacy Policy

The Real Story Group respects the privacy of visitors to its websites. The information gathered by the Real Story Group during use of this website, will be used by the Real Story Group for evaluation and further perfection and personalization of this web site. The Real Story Group attempts, through the gathered information, to optimize the accessibility and quality of this website, including 1-to-1 marketing.

The Real Story Group utilizes third-pary web analytics services from Hitslink and Google that involve user tracking via JavaScript and cookies. We comply with the rules and norms of those services.

We do not share your information with third parties, unless required by the law or the judicial authorities. Personal information submitted to the Real Story Group will be treated confidentially but could be collected and archived in a database or file owned by the Real Story Group. Processing of this personal information can take place for marketing purposes of the Real Story Group. Personal information will be discarded when it is no longer legal necessary for the purposes it was collected.

Links to Third Party Sites or Products
Our web site contains links to websites operated and maintained by third parties. We have no control whatsoever over such third parties or their websites or products or services. Privacy policies of such linked sites or products or services may be different from our privacy policy. You access such linked sites or products or services at your own risk. You should always read the privacy policy of a linked site or product or service before disclosing any personally identifiable information on such site.

This new name better reflects what we actually do: offering the real story on a wide variety of content technologies, debunking marketing hype and delivering detailed, objective analysis.

Some things didn't change, though. We still have the same dedicated analyst team and support staff covering a wide array of vendors and issues. Above all, we keep our unique, dedicated focus on you, the technology customer.
