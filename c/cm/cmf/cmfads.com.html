Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and we are committed to safeguarding your privacy while you use this website, www.cmfads.com. This page outlines the information gathering practices for this website.

Log files

Like most other websites, our servers use log files. These files record information including Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, Internet service provider (ISP), referral/exit pages, operating system, web browser, date/time, and number of clicks. This information is stored to administer the site, analyse trends, prevent unauthorised access and gather broad demographic information for statistics. The log files are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Registration

If you wish to register, we reqire a valid email address. This is not displayed in public, nor is it shared or sold to any other website including but not limited to any partner websites.

Cookies

A cookie is a file stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user. This site uses a cookie so you can stay logged in if you choose to register. No personal data is stored or tracked in this cookie. However, some of our business partners (e.g. advertisers) may use cookies on our site. We have no access to or control over these cookies.

Links

This website contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the content and privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage you to note when you leave this site, and read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this website.

Advertisers

We use outside advertising companies to display advertisements on our site. These advertisements may contain cookies collected by the advertising companies, and we do not have access to this information. Please check the advertiser's websites for their privacy policies.

Contact information

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us.
