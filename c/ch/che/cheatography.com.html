Your Privacy

What is this?

As a user or member of Cheatography, you may share information with us, and this document outlines what information we collect about you and how we use it.

Changes

If we make any changes to this policy, we will let you know the next time you log in.

Questions

If you have any questions, comments or concerns, please email support@cheatography.com.

In Brief ...

We will:

  • Do our best to protect any information you give us, including usage of best-practice password storage.
  • Delete all personally idenfitiable information we have about you, if you ask us to.

We will not:

  • Give your information to any third parties.
  • Contact you without your express permission.
  • Access your personal information, unless required to do so by law, or to help resolve an issue you personally are having with the site.

Contact Options

By default, notifications are sent to you when people leave comments on your content and cheat sheets, or when a cheat sheet you like is updated. You can opt not to have these notifications sent to you in your account settings.

Third Parties

We will not release any of your personal information to a third party, unless required to by law.

Advertising

We do not share personal information with advertising providers. Advertising supplied by Google is subject to Google's privacy policy.

Cookies

We use cookies so we know when you are logged in, and so the site can remember you. You can delete cookies from this site at any time (although you'll need to log in again if you do).

Tracking

We monitor site usage statistics, including browsers, operating systems, countries and activity of users. This information is not personally identifiable, and is used to help us ensure the site is secure and works well for everyone.
