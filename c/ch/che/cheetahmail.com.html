Effective Date: September 1, 2004
CheetahMail, an Experian Company, offers a diverse range of email-related technology services for our clients. As a member of the Experian corporate family, we are dedicated to a similar set of fair information values for our clients and user-recipients of our services. This privacy policy applies to our corporate website, CheetahMail.com, and serves as our privacy commitment to our corporate clients.

For user-recipients of CheetahMail services, including all email recipients, please refer to CheetahMail's User Policy Guide.

Corporate Email Client Anti-Spam Policy:
Clients of CheetahMail have agreed to not send unsolicited commercial email through our systems. Any client who violates this agreement is subject to privacy review, services termination and potential legal action.

If you believe you have received an unsolicited commercial email from CheetahMail on behalf of any of our clients, please send an email (preferably with the reported email violation included or attached with the 'full sender header') to: abuse@cheetahmail.com

Information Collection & Use
CheetahMail is the sole owner of information collected on this website. We collect information from both business users and user-recipients through a 'Contact Us' form. We do not sell, rent or share this information with third parties unless it is in cooperation with a law enforcement investigation. CheetahMail collects the information and uses it to send only appropriate information to users who request it. If information requested is directed to an Experian-related interest, we may share the information with an appropriate Experian party in order to fulfill the directed request.

Website Activity
CheetahMail collects anonymous information about visitors to our website. Information collected includes Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, which help us identify such information as the web pages visited, unique visitations, type of browser used, and other session-based activity. This information is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Cookies
CheetahMail does not use cookies with our website visitors.

Website Security
Information collected through CheetahMail.com is done via a secure web interface and maintained within a secure operating environment. Our team of security professionals continually monitors our web interface touchpoints and ensures technology updates are made to thwart any attempts for unwanted intrusion.

CheetahMail Client Communications
CheetahSpots: CheetahSpots is a monthly newsletter delivered exclusively to clients as an educational resource and product awareness service. Clients can refer subscriptions to any member of their staff to receive this publication. Each newsletter includes a clear option to opt-out of future editions within the footer of the message. In addition, clients can communicate with their account representative to request preference management with their subscriptions to CheetahSpots.

In addition, CheetahMail may occasionally send clients or previous business inquiries email messages with product updates, educational events, or other important business related information. Each email includes an opportunity to unsubscribe from future messaging, or recipients can contact us directly through any of our points of contact listed online.

Links
The CheetahMail website contains links to other sites. CheetahMail is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these sites. We recommend users read the privacy statements of each linked website.

Notification of Changes to our Privacy Policy
When a change to our privacy policy or use of personally identifiable information occurs, we will post a notification on a 'Recent Updates' web page.
