# Privacy Policy - COMC Card Marketplace

Your privacy is important, so we have created the following Privacy Policy to let you know what information we request when you order from us, and how it is used. It is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy and remain informed about it.

All information requested by COMC.com at the time of your order will be kept confidentially and will not be distributed to any company or individual. The information will be used to ensure that your order gets to you as soon as possible and to communicate with you concerning your order.
