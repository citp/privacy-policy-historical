# Privacy Policy - Cheap Ass Gamer

This Privacy Policy is a part of the TOU, and covers information collected on CheapAssGamer.com. This policy does not cover information collected offline or elsewhere. The Management may amend this policy at any time.

You may be asked to provide personal identification information in connection with your access to all or part of CheapAssGamer.com. This personal identification information will only be collected from you if you voluntarily submit such information. CheapAssGamer.com uses security measures to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control; however, all security is subject to possible circumvention, and we cannot and do not provide any guarantees regarding the effectiveness of the security we employ or our ability to prevent third parties, acting unlawfully, from obtaining information that you provide to us.

The information that you submit may be used in the following ways: your IP address may be used to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer CheapAssGamer.com. Your IP address may also be used to help identify you and to gather broad demographic information that is anonymous in nature and is not associated with particular individuals. Other anonymous information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to CheapAssGamer.com may be gathered for use in providing advertisements on CheapAssGamer.com. In the course of serving advertisements to Site users, our third-party advertisers may place or recognize a unique “cookie” on your browser.

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on CheapAssGamer.com and their use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads based on users visits to CheapAssGamer.com and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html). CheapAssGamer.com may also use other third party vendors such as Demdex that provide similar services. Users may opt out of Demdex [here](http://www.donottarget.com/).

We reserve the right to reveal your identity (or whatever information we know about you) in the event of a complaint or legal action arising from your activities on CheapAssGamer.com. If identity or account information is sought in connection with a civil legal matter, the party seeking such identity or account information must serve CheapAssGamer.com with a valid subpoena.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, the TOU or the Site, please [contact the administrator](http://www.cheapassgamer.com/forums/sendmessage.php).
