Cookies typically remain active on your computer for about 30 days. If you visit certain pages on our site during that period, CheapestTextbooks.com and some of our web advertising partners may be able to tell that you came to our site (but not user name, address, or other personal information) via an advertisment and/or left our site and went to one of our web advertising partner sites.

If you want to disable the use of cookies, you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. Be aware, however, that some websites may not function properly if you refuse to accept cookies.

Log files are not linked to personally identifiable information.
