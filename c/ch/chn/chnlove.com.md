Chnlove.com (the "Site") is committed to safeguarding your privacy online. We reserve the right to change it at any time, and all changes are effective upon posting.

  1. **About Chnlove and Information We Collect From You**

This Site and the Service is an online network/system that provides a powerful tool for the local dating and marriage agencies in different cites in China to submit and have their ladies profiles posted online. Also, the functions of this Site enable those agencies to help you and the ladies communicate in an easier way by removing obstacles such as language barrier and cultural differences, ladies' computer illiteracy or unfamiliarity in using the Internet etc. So, you can easily build up a relationship with your favorite lady, which leads to marriage.

When you register to become a member of this Site, you are required to fill in the registration form online with your information which contains three parts:

a) **Personal Data**  
It includes your first, middle and last names, date of birth, ethnicity, your primary language, your height, education level, your profession, current income, whether smoker or not, alcoholic or not, your marital status, whether you have children or not and your religion.

b) **Self-description and Criteria for Your Ideal Match**  
You may include inside your profile description of your interests, hobbies or a description of your ideal match, which would make it more appealing to ladies who view your profile and even write to you as your admirers.

Also in the same form, you may select the criteria for your ideal mate, such as her age range, whether she has children or not, her educational level, marital status and her city of origin. Based on your criteria, we will send you weekly updates up to the criterion you selected whether receive or not.

c) **Contact Information**  
It consists of your email address, your postal address such as street address, city, state/province, country as well as postal code. Besides, your telephone or fax number are required when filling in the registration form.

  2. **Cookies and Historical Information On This Site**

Each time you visit the Site, various information is gathered automatically, such as: domain, computer platform, browser type, IP address and country. We gather this information in order to administer and optimize the Site for you and to diagnose problems with our Site and enhance your experience on the Site.

  3. **How We Use Your Information**

Your email address is used to help us to efficiently operate the Site, to contact you in connection with your transactions, enquiry and other activities on the Site, including confirmation emails, or notification of mails from your admirers or from the ladies you correspond with, or weekly updates that meet your criteria of ideal match.

  4. **Our Disclosure of Your Information**

As above-mentioned, your profile information contains three parts - 'personal data', 'self-description' and 'contact information'. Under no circumstance can we sell or release any of your information to any third party without your permission.

You can choose whether your profile should be visible or hidden on our Site.

a) **"Visible"** , which means your personal data, self-description will be viewed by agencies with whom we cooperate (the "Agencies"), and by the ladies from those agencies. And none of your contact information will be revealed to them.

b) **"Hidden"** , which means none of the information contained in your profile will be viewed by those agencies and ladies posted on this Site. Only Chnlove staff can see your profile.

No matter which profile status you choose, your profile will not be seen outside Chnlove and the Agencies office.

  5. **Your Credit Card Details**

If you pay by your credit card online, to protect your privacy we use highly secured industry order processing provided by third-party billing companies (such as WorldPay, 2CheckOut, and PayPal). Those companies we cooperate with enjoy good reputation and reliability in the industry of its kind. With use of the third-party credit card processors, your credit card information is entered using a secure server, and transferred internally using PGP, the most powerful encryption tool available. Your customer details may not be sold or made available to anyone else and your credit card details are only known by our order processors.

  6. **EMF Mails with the ladies on this Site**

With the use of the phone-informing Email Forwarding (EMF) Service, the content of your mails are forwarded to the ladies on the "AS IS" basis and kept private, only staff in Chnlove and the staff in the agencies who deal with Chnlove-related service can see them. All agencies that Chnlove cooperate with should operate in accordance with our contractual agreement, and should never expose them to any unrelated persons.

  7. **Update/Delete the information in your profile  
**  
If you want to update or correct inaccuracies in your information, you may login to your account and modify it. In case you want to delete your profile from our database, you may contact us, and we can do it for you.
  8. **Security**

Chnlove.com have security measures in place to protect and prevent the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. We use industry standard efforts to safeguard the confidentiality of your personal identity information such as firewalls. While "perfect security" does not exist on the Internet, our technical experts work hard to ensure your secure use of our services. 

  9. **Your Agreement to This Privacy Policy**

By using this Site and the Service, you understand that this privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site, and you agree to the collection and use of your personal information by this Site as outlined in this Privacy Policy statement.




If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, the practices of the Site, or your dealings with the Site, you can contact us.
