# Privacy - Retro, Indie and Unique Fashion

At Chicwish, we take your online security very seriously, and we're committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. We've compiled this statement to explain how we collect, use, and secure your information. Please read on for more information.

**Collection and Use of Information**

  
**A. Information You Provide to Us**

**Facebook**  
We collect information when you register to join Chicwish.com by logging into your Facebook account via our application. For example, we allow you to create a Chicwish account using your Facebook account credentials through Facebook's API. If you are not currently registered as a user on Facebook and you click on "Sign in with Facebook," you will first be asked to enter your Facebook credentials and then be given the option to join Chicwish.  
We allow Users to link their Facebook account to our Service. We will not share and disclose on your Facebook service without your permisson. Only you click "Like" or "Share" will have information related to our website will be posted on your Facebook page.

 **Non-Facebook Account Access**

For non Facebook account access, we ask your full name, email and password for your account creation.

When you place orders on Chicwish.com, we ask you for your full name, shipping address, telephone number and credit card number so we can ship your order. We use your telephone number only for express delivery or for order-related issue, not for marketing purposes. Our server only stores some of this information so that placing future orders will be quicker and easier. You must sign in to access your account.

**B. Information Collected Automatically**

We use third-party website analytics tools (including Google Analytics) that automatically collect non-personally identifiable information from your browser to our server logs whenever you interact with our Site. Such information includes, but is not limited to, IP address, pages viewed, site navigation patterns, details about the devices used to access the Internet and our Site, and geo-location tracking information. It is obtained by automated means, such as cookies. "Cookies" are small pieces of data that are sent to your browser from a Web server and stored on your hard drive. We use cookies to help us show advertisements on other websites likely to be more interesting to you; to compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction; and to help us understand your preferences based on previous or current site activity, which enables us to provide you with improved services, site experiences, and tools in the future. You may be able to adjust the preferences on your browser or device to change your device's acceptance of cookies, but doing so may limit our ability to provide content tailored to you.

**C. Disclosure**

Except as described in this section, we will not sell, rent or disclose your personally identifying information to third parties.  
In order to ship and track merchandise, we make your name and address available to such carriers as UPS and USPS. In no instance are these carriers allowed to use your personal information for any other purpose.  
We provide analyses of our users in the aggregate to our vendors and prospective vendors We do this so that we and our vendors can understand you better, and so we can keep bringing you the latest and most current fashions and fashion accessories. At no time, however, will we disclose personal information about specific users. 

  
**D. Email & Other Communications**

We may contact you via email to send you necessary order-related messages, such as order confirmation and shipment confirmation notices.

We may also send you newsletters and additional email messages about our products, services, contests, and promotions that we feel may be of interest to you. If you no longer wish to receive these and other promotional communications from us, you may select the unsubscribe link which is present in each email. You may also customize your communication preferences by accessing your account.

**How We Protect Your Information**

Chicwish utilizes some of the most advanced technology available today for Internet security purposes. When you access our checkout using industry standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology, your information is protected with both server authentication and data encryption, ensuring that your data is safe, secure, and available only to Chicwish.

Our site is verified by VeriSign.

While we make every effort to ensure that your information is secure on our system, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, we cannot guarantee the security of any information you transmit to us, and you do so at your own risk.
