Privacy Policy

Contact Information

To contact The Pueblo Chieftain Online regarding this privacy policy, please use one of the following methods:

  • Email: webmaster@chieftain.com
  • Telephone: 719-544-3520
  • U.S. Mail: Attn: Internet Privacy Policy
    PO Box 4040
    Pueblo, CO 81003

Information automatically gathered
by our web server

For each visitor to our websites, our web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name, but not the email address (where possible). We collect aggregate and user-specific information on what pages consumers access or visit. This information is used to generate reports that help The Pueblo Chieftain Online assess the value of and interest in the various web sites.

Information gathered by voluntary
submissions of users to our web site

We collect the email addresses of those users who post messages to our bulletin board, communicate with us via email and make postings to our chat area. In addition, we collect information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and site registrations.

Use of information
by The Pueblo Chieftain Online

The information we collect is used by us to improve the content of our webpage; to customize the content and layout of our page for each individual consumer; and to notify consumers about updates to our website. In addition, this information is used by us contact consumers for marketing purposes.

If you do not want to receive email from us in the future, please let us know by sending email to us at the above-listed address, calling us at the above-listed telephone number or writing to us at the above-listed postal address and specify in your request that you do not want to receive email from our company.

If you supply us with your postal address online, you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending email to us at the above-listed, calling us at the above-listed telephone number or writing to us at the above-listed address. Specify in your request that you do not want to receive postal mailings from our company. Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers online may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed online. They also may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such telephone calls, please let us know by sending email to us at the above-listed address or calling us at the above-listed telephone number or writing to us at the above-listed address. In your request, please provide us with your correct phone number and address and specify that you do not wish to receive telephone calls from our company.

The Pueblo Chieftain does not knowingly allow anyone younger than 13 to register with our services, nor do we seek personal information from them. If you are under the age of 13, please do not provide us with any personal information such as your name, email or phone number, and get permission from your parents before registering with this or any online service.

Information shared
with other organizations

From time to time, we make our customer list available to other companies or organizations whose products or services we think you might find interesting. This list can include your email address and, if you have provided it to us online, your postal address and your telephone number. If you do not want us to share your email address, postal address and/or telephone number with other companies or organizations, please let us know by sending email to us at the above-listed address, calling us at the above-listed telephone number or writing to us at the above-listed address. In your request, please specify what information you do not want to be shared, your email address, postal address and/or telephone number, and provide us with your exact name, postal address and telephone number. We will be sure your name is removed from the list we share with other organizations.

Third-party services

The Pueblo Chieftain partners with many third parties to provide access to products and services they offer. Please note that these third-party services have their own privacy policies that are beyond our control. Any use of their services or transactions with them will be governed by the privacy policy of the appropriate organization. The Pueblo Chieftain assumes no responsibility or liabilities whatsoever for the content or privacy practices of such linked services or websites, and linking to them in any way should not be seen as endorsement of their practices or products. Accessing any of these third-party sites or services should come with the understanding by you that it will be at your own risk.

Third-party organizations or websites providing services on Chieftain.com may use cookies, web beacons or other technologies to offer services or advertising based on your browsing behavior. To learn more about behavioral targeting and opt out if you wish, please visit http://www.networkadvertising.org
