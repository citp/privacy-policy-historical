# Privacy Notice - China Electronics Wholesale

We are collecting information like invoice and delivery addresses and payment options. These information are stored electronically and partly in printed form. Your details are used for order handling and accountancy. Your privacy is protected and guaranteed. ChinaTronic.com will not pass any information to any third parties. 

Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart. We use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password so you don`t have to re-enter it each time you visit our site. 

ChinaTronic.com contains links to other sites in the internet. We are not responsible for any information and privacy practices of such sites. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected on ChinaTronic.com.
