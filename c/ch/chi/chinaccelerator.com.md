# Privacy Policy - Chinaccelerator

1\. **Introduction**

1.1 SOSV provides its website visitors with access to information, updates and certain products and services (the “Services”) through this website (the “Chinaccelerator Website”).

1.2 This Privacy Policy details the SOSV practices in connection with the collection and use of information and the circumstances when SOSV may disclose such collected information.

1.3 By visiting the Chinaccelerator Website, you acknowledge that you accept the practices and policies as detailed in this Privacy Policy and you consent to the collection, use and sharing of your information by SOSV as detailed in this Privacy Policy.

1.4 This Privacy Policy deals with the treatment of the personal information of website visitors to the Chinaccelerator Website. This Privacy Policy does not extend to the practices of companies or people that are not controlled or managed by SOSV.

2\. **Commitment**

2.1 SOSV is committed to creating a safe and secure online environment and safeguarding the privacy of its website visitors.

3\. **Collecting Information**

3.1 Website visitors may access the Chinaccelerator Website while remaining anonymous and without disclosing any personal information.

3.2 During a visit to the Chinaccelerator Website, SOSV may collect, store and use the following kinds of personal information from website visitors, including but not limited to:

(a) information about your computer and about your visits to and use of the Chinaccelerator Website (including your IP address, geographical location, browser type and version, operating system, referral source, length of visit, page views and website navigation paths);

(b) information that you provide to SOSV for the purpose of subscribing to our email notifications and/or newsletters (including your name and email address);

(c) information that you provide to SOSV when using the Services on the Chinaccelerator Website, or information that is generated in the course of the use of those Services;

(d) information contained in or relating to any communication that you send to SOSV or send through the Chinaccelerator Website;

(e) any other personal information that you choose to send to SOSV.

4\. **Using Personal Information**

4.1 Personal information submitted to SOSV through the Chinaccelerator Website will be used for the purposes specified in this Privacy Policy or on the relevant pages of the Chinaccelerator Website.

4.2 SOSV may use your personal information to:

(a) contact you;

(b) administer the Chinaccelerator Website and business;

(c) enable your use of the Services available on the Chinaccelerator Website;

(d) send you non-marketing commercial communications;

(e) send you email notifications that you have specifically requested;

(f) send you the SOSV email newsletter, if you have requested it (such circulation can be terminated at any time at your request and notification to SOSV);

(g) provide statistical information only to third parties about SOSV users;

(h) address any queries and/or complaints made by or about you relating to the Chinaccelerator Website;

(i) maintain Chinaccelerator Website security and prevent fraud.

4.3 SOSV will not, without your express consent, supply your personal information to any third party for the purpose of their or any other third party’s direct marketing.

5\. **Disclosing Personal Information**

5.1 SOSV may disclose your personal information to any of its employees, officers, insurers, professional advisers, agents, suppliers or subcontractors insofar as reasonably necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

5.2 SOSV may disclose your personal information to any SOSV Affiliate (which shall mean for the purposes of this Privacy Policy any individual or corporation, partnership, trust, incorporated or unincorporated association, joint venture, limited liability company, or joint stock company that, directly or indirectly through one or more intermediaries, controls or is controlled by or is under common control with such individual or corporation, partnership, trust, incorporated or unincorporated association, joint venture, limited liability company, or joint stock company) insofar as reasonably necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

5.3 SOSV may disclose your personal information:

(a) to the extent that SOSV is required to do so by law;

(b) in connection with any ongoing or prospective legal proceedings;

(c) in order to establish, exercise, protect or defend the legal rights (including providing information to others for the purposes of fraud prevention and reducing credit risk) of SOSV;

(d) to the purchaser (or prospective purchaser) of any business or asset that SOSV are (or are contemplating) selling.

5.4 Except as provided in this Privacy Policy, SOSV will not provide your personal information to third parties.

6\. **International Data Transfers**

6.1 Information that SOSV collects may be stored and processed in and transferred between any of the countries in which SOSV operates in order to enable SOSV to use the information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

6.2 You expressly agree to the transfers of personal information described in this Section 6 and as may be provided elsewhere in this Privacy Policy.

7\. **Retaining Personal Information**

7.1 This Section 7 sets out the SOSV data retention policies and procedure, which are designed to help ensure that SOSV is compliant with its legal obligations in relation to the retention and deletion of personal information.

7.2 Personal information that SOSV processes for any purpose or purposes shall not be kept for longer than is necessary for that purpose or those purposes.

7.3 Notwithstanding the other provisions of this Section 7, SOSV will retain documents (including electronic documents) containing personal data:

(a) to the extent that SOSV is required to do so by law;

(b) if SOSV believes that the documents may be relevant to any ongoing or prospective legal proceedings; and

(c) in order to establish, exercise or defend the legal rights (including providing information to others for the purposes of fraud prevention and reducing credit risk) of SOSV.

8\. **Security of Personal Information**

8.1 SOSV will take reasonable technical and organisational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information.

8.2 SOSV will store all the personal information you provide on its secure servers.

8.3 You are advised that SOSV uses third party vendors to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage and related technology required to run the Chinaccelerator Website. By using the Chinaccelerator Website you acknowledge that your data may be stored on a third party storage server and transmitted through third party networks.

8.4 You acknowledge that the transmission of information over the internet is inherently insecure, and SOSV cannot guarantee the security of data sent over the internet.

9\. **Amendments**

9.1 SOSV may at its sole discretion and without notice update this Privacy Policy from time to time by publishing a new version on the Chinaccelerator Website.

9.2 You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are happy with any changes to this Privacy Policy.

9.3 SOSV may notify you of changes to this Privacy Policy by email.

10\. **Your Rights**

10.1 You may instruct SOSV to provide you with any personal information SOSV may hold about you; provision of such information will be subject to the supply of appropriate evidence of your identity (for this purpose, SOSV may accept a photocopy of your passport certified by an attorney or bank plus an original copy of a utility bill showing your current address).

10.2 SOSV may withhold personal information that you request to the extent permitted by law.

10.3 You may instruct SOSV at any time not to process your personal information for marketing purposes.

10.4 In practice, you will usually expressly agree in advance to the use by SOSV of your personal information for marketing purposes.

10.5 Website visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using the Chinaccelerator Website. If you refuse cookies, you do so with the knowledge that such refusal will result in a drawback to the extent that certain features of the Chinaccelerator Website may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

11\. **Updating Information**

11.1 Please let SOSV know if the personal information that SOSV holds about you needs to be corrected or updated.

12\. **Cookies**

12.1 The Chinaccelerator Website uses cookies.

12.2 A cookie is a file containing an identifier that is sent by a web server to a web browser and is stored by the browser. The identifier is then sent back to the server each time the browser requests a page from the server.

12.3 Cookies do not typically contain any personal information that may personally identify a user, but personal information that SOSV stores about you may be linked to the information stored in and obtained from cookies.

12.4 Most browsers allow you to refuse to accept cookies.

13\. **Child Users**

13.1 Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important to SOSV. SOSV does not knowingly collect or maintain personal information from persons under 13 years for age (“Child Users”). The Chinaccelerator Website is not directed to Child Users. If SOSV becomes aware of personal information that has been collected from a Child User without verified parental consent, SOSV will delete such information.
