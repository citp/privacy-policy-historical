# Privacy Policy

## Information on this Website

Any personal information you give us while visiting Chordbook.Com, is used to develop our understanding of our visitors needs and ways they use the website.

We use return e-mail addresses to answer the e-mails we receive.

Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside partiesÂ except when required to do so by law.

We will not send your data to third parties and we will not send data to areas where an adequate data protection policy is not in place.

The website may collect some anonymous information about you automatically as you browse the site. This information is used to help us monitor how you and other visitors use the site. This information does not contain anything that can identify you personally unless you are a logged in member of the site.
