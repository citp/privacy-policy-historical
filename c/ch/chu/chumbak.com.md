# Privacy :: Chumbak

* * *

Privacy Policy

If you provide us with your email address to subscribe to Chumbak, we promise to protect it and to keep it out of the wrong hands. That means we won’t share your information with anyone else. We don’t like people messing with our personal information, and we’re not going to let that happen to you.

And don’t tell anybody, but Bruce Wayne (wink wink) is a huge Chumbak fan, if you know what we mean. 

Links

This Web site may contain links to other sites. Please be aware that we do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they exit our site and to understand the privacy policies of each and every Web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site. 

Newsletter

We shall send you newsletters once in a while. If you would like to unsubscribe, we’ll miss you loads. But if you still want to miss out on the Chumbak experience, you can click here.
