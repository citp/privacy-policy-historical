Privacy Statement

Champlain College respects the privacy of your personal information and does not, under any circumstances, rent or sell personal information submitted by visitors to our site to any outside third party.

Personal information that you submit to this Web site will be used only for the purpose for which it was asked (for example, information submitted on the Undergraduate Studies section of our Web site will be used for admission communications purposes). Aggregate, nonpersonally identifying information may be both used internally and shared externally (for example, the number of applicants from specific countries).

The College is committed to upholding our community members’ and visitors’ right to privacy. Should you have any questions or suspect a breach of these policies, we encourage you to contact us by e-mail at webmaster@champlain.edu.
