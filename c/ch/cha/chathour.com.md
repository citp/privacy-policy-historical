# Chat Hour - Privacy Policy

• Chat Hour (formerly WeirdTown Chat) does not rent, sell, or share any personal information about you with any third party unless you expressly tell us to.

• Chat Hour automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP addresses, Chat Hour cookie information, and the pages you request.

• Chat Hour may set and access Chat Hour cookies on your computer.

• Chat Hour may reveal your IP address when you send messages in our chat rooms without logging in. If you are logged in, your IP address is private.

• Chat Hour may display your profile information including your gender, age and other information such as your interests, your photos, etc. through our websites, software and services.

• We limit access to your personal data such as your email addresses to our employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs. We may reveal your personal data to internet service providers, law enforcement agencies or other government agencies if the information is requested for investigations regarding possible illegal activities or terms of use violations.

• Chat Hour may update this policy from time to time. You are responsible for checking this policy periodically for changes.
