Your Privacy

In general, you can visit the Mid-Hudson Regional Information Center on the Web without telling us who you are and without revealing any information about yourself. There are times, however, when we may need information from you, such as a class registration form or if you email a question to our FAQ.

We are committed to comply with applicable privacy laws and requirements, such as the United States� Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the New York State Best Practice Guideline #Go2-001 (Internet Privacy Policy).

We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to the Mid-Hudson Regional Information Center will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described below. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to let us know.

Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:

The Mid-Hudson Regional Information Center does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including registration forms or email lists.


Browser information collected on the website:

The Mid-Hudson Regional Information Center collects no personal information from your visits to our site without your knowledge or permission. We do not gather or track information such as your IP address, your browser history, etc.

Email information:

If during your visit to this website, you send an electronic mail message to the MHRIC, your electronic mail address and the contents of your message will be collected. The information collected is not limited to text characters and may include audio, video, and graphic information formats included in the message. Your electronic mail address and the information included in your message will be used to respond to you, to address issues you identify, to improve this website, or to forward your message to another agency for appropriate action. Your electronic mail address is not collected for commercial purposes and the MHRIC does not sell or otherwise distribute your electronic mail address for commercial purposes.

Registration information:

If during your visit to this website you complete a transaction such as a survey or registration, the information, including personal information, volunteered by you is used by the MHRIC to operate MHRIC programs and services, which include the provision of services, and information. This information is collected by the MHRIC and may be disclosed by the MHRIC for those purposes that may be reasonably ascertained from the nature and terms of the survey, registration, or order form in which the information was submitted.

Use of cookies:

In general, we do not use cookies on this site. Exceptions: web surveys.

A "cookie" is a small text file that a website can place on your computer's hard drive in order, for example, to collect information about your activities on the site or to make it possible for you to use an online "shopping cart" to keep track of items you wish to purchase. The cookie transmits this information back to the Web site's computer which, generally speaking, is the only computer that can read it. Most consumers do not know that "cookies" are being placed on their computers when they visit websites. If you want to know when this happens, or to prevent it from happening, you can set your browser to warn you when a website attempts to place a "cookie" on your computer.
