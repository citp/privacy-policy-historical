Privacy Policy

Information you provide to us, such as your name, email address, physical address, company affiliation, remain for our eyes only unless you give us permission to divulge it to someone else.

Please note that if you provide your contact information in our MSPmentor 100 survey or Resource Center, you are giving permission to make this contact information known to our members.

From time to time, we may disclose the overall number of visitors, their geographic location and other information, but this information will not be the kind that identifies who you are.
