# Privacy Policy - Mixed In Key

## Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to the Mixed in Key, LLC ("we" or "us") owned and operated websites and its subdomains (“Sites”) as well as to the services that we deliver such as music composition, organization and analysis software for DJ's and musicians ("Services").

## Who You Are

Visitor. You may be a visitor to our Sites and either provide us with information without purchasing our Services or you may may contact us through our Sites seeking information about our Services.

Customer. You may be a customer of our Services. You may have an End User License Agreement that allows you to use our Services ("Services Agreement"). A Services Agreement may refer to this Privacy Policy.

## Your Privacy Matters

We are responsible for determining the purpose and means of processing any Personal Data (see definition in the next paragraph) about you during your visit to our Site or through the use of our Services. We know that you care how data about you is used and shared. Please contact us at contact@mixedinkey.com if you have any questions. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand our privacy practices and the choices available to you when you use our Site or Services.

This Privacy Policy describes how we protect your Personal Data, which is defined by the European Union, the U.S. government and many U.S. states in similar yet unique ways. For the purposes of this Site and our Services, we define "Personal Data" to be information that can be used to identify a person directly or indirectly and can include a name, postal address (including billing and shipping addresses), telephone number, email address, payment card number, other financial account information, log in account number and associated password, date of birth, government-issued credentials (e.g., driver’s license number, national ID, passport, Social Security number and Taxpayer ID), an IP address, location data or one or more factors specific to the physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural or social identity of that person.

The European Union Global Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR") provides data subjects like you with certain rights that are enumerated in the Your Rights section below. We will provide these same rights to all of our customers regardless of where you live.

## When this Privacy Policy Applies

This Privacy Policy explains what Personal Data we collect when you access the Sites or use our Services, how the Personal Data will be used, and how you can control the collection, correction or deletion of that data. The use of the Sites is possible without providing Personal Data; however, if you want to use Services via our Sites, processing of Personal Data is necessary.

We will not use or share your data with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy does not apply to data we collect by other means (including offline) or from other sources. The use of data collected through our Sites is limited to the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy and our Services Agreement. The processing of Personal Data will be in accordance with GDPR Principles.

We will process and store your Personal Data only for the period necessary to achieve the purpose of collection or as long as required by applicable law. Our legal basis for processing your Personal Data is either your consent indicated on this Site, that the processing is necessary for the performance of a contract to which you are party such as the Services Agreement, or in order to take steps at your request prior to entering into a contract.

## Information You Share with Us

We may collect Personal Data when you visit our Site such as information related to your online device, including your IP address, referring website, what pages your device visited, and the time your device visited our Site. For more information on this, see the section below titled Cookies and Other Tracking Technology.

We will collect Personal Data when you register for an account to activate Services, when you provide your Personal Data to interact with other Site users, or send us customer service related requests. When you sign up for our Services, we will receive and store information you provide directly to us. For example, when setting up a new customer we collect Personal Data such as a name, email address, and password. If you choose to purchase Services from us, we or our third-party payment processors will collect your payment information.

We may send promotional or customer service related emails. If you no longer wish to receive these emails, you may opt out of them by clicking the unsubscribe link in the email communications we send.

## How We Use Your Personal Data

We use your Personal Data when you visit our Site for the following purposes:

  * To administer the Site and for internal operations, including troubleshooting, data analysis, testing, statistical and survey purposes. 
  * To improve our Site to ensure that content is presented in the most effective manner for you and your device. 
  * To analyze your use of the Site for trend monitoring, marketing and advertising purposes. 
  * For purposes made clear to you at the time you submit the information such as to fulfill your request for a demo or to provide you with information you requested about our Services. 
  * As part of our efforts to keep our Site safe and secure. 



In addition to the purposes set forth above as applied to delivery of the Services, we use your Personal Data when you purchase or subscribe to our Services for the following purposes:

  * To set up a user account. 
  * To provide, operate and maintain the Services. 
  * To process and complete transactions, and send related information, including transaction confirmations and invoices. 
  * To manage your use of the Services, respond to your inquiries, and to provide you with customer service and support. 
  * To send you technical alerts, updates, security notifications, and administrative communications. 
  * To investigate and prevent fraudulent activities, unauthorized access to the Services, and other illegal activities. 
  * To store your projects in the cloud so you can access them from multiple devices. 
  * To provide you with automatically emailed tutorials based on your usage of the Services. 
  * For any other purpose about which we notify you. 



We may also use your Personal Data to contact you via email, and possibly other means, regarding new services or events we think may be of interest to you if this is in accordance with your marketing preferences.

We do use automatic decision-making or profiling. Profiling in general means any form of automated processing of Personal Data to evaluate or predict aspects concerning a person’s performance at work, economic situation, health, personal preferences, interests, reliability, behavior, location or movements. However, we only profile previous Services customers in order to offer them special upgrade or discount pricing on future Services offerings by evaluating your interest in our Services and those of our competitors as well as your age, gender and geographic location.

## Cookies and Tracking Technology

**Cookies**. Cookies are small pieces of data sent by a website to a visitor's hard disk. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. We use cookies to remember your settings as well as for authentication purposes. We use cookies in analyzing trends, administering the Sites, tracking your movements around the Sites and to gather demographic data about our user base as a whole. We may also place cookies from third parties for functional and marketing purposes. We may receive reports based on the use of these technologies by such companies on an individual as well as aggregated basis. If you reject cookies, you may still use our Site, but your ability to use some features or areas may be limited.

The only way to completely “opt out” of the collection of any data through cookies or other tracking technology is to actively manage the settings on your browser or mobile device. Please refer to your browser’s or mobile device’s technical data for instructions on how to delete and disable cookies, and other tracking/recording tools (to learn more about cookies, clear gifs/web beacons and related technologies, you may wish to visit http://www.cookiecentral.com/faq/.)

You can also visit the U.S. government website to learn about disabling cookies at https://www.usa.gov/optout-instructions.

If you access the Services or Sites on your mobile device, you may not be able to control tracking technologies through the settings. For more information on third-party advertising- related cookies and interest-based advertising, and to learn how to opt-out of these practices with companies participating in industry self-regulation, please visit www.aboutads.info/consumers. 

**Analytics Tools**. The Sites use an analytics tools to track user activity, page content, click/touch, movement, scroll, and keystroke activity (in some cases). In particular, we use Google Analytics, a web analysis service provided by Google, to better understand your use of our Site and Services. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and analyze information about use of the Services and report on activities and trends. This service may also collect information regarding the use of other websites, apps and online resources. You can learn about Google’s practices by going to www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/, and opt out of them by downloading the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on, available at https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout. 

**Pixel tags and similar technologies** : Pixel tags (also known as web beacons and clear GIFs) may be used in connection with Services to, among other things, track your actions (including email opens and clicks), measure the success of our marketing campaigns and compile statistics about use of the Services and response rates.

**Log File Information**. Log file data is automatically reported by your browser or mobile application each time you access our Sites or the Services. For example, when you access our Sites, our servers automatically record certain data that your web browser sends whenever you visit any website. These server logs may include data such as your operating system, browser type, referring / exit pages and URLs, number of clicks, domain names, landing pages, pages viewed and other such information. We do not intentionally connect this automatically-collected data to Personal Data but some Personal Data attributes may be present in the URL when it is logged into our system. We do not use this data from visitors for any purpose.

Data Portability. We will provide you with a copy of your Personal Data in a structured, commonly used and machine readable format.

**Complaints**. We hope that you won't ever need to, but you can complain about our use of your Personal Data. We will investigate and respond to you. ## Do Not Track Disclosure

"Do Not Track" ("DNT") refers to an HTTP header used by Internet web browsers to request a web application disable its tracking or cross-site user tracking. When DNT is enabled, a user's web browser adds a header to content requests indicating that the user does not want to be tracked. Some U.S. state laws require us to disclose how we respond to web browser DNT signals. We do not respond to or take any action with respect to a DNT configuration set in your internet browser, and therefore, do not disable tracking. For data about Do Not Track, visit www.allaboutdnt.org.

In addition, we deploy cookies, tracking pixels, web beacons, and other technologies on our websites and those of our customers. Those tools may be used by us, our customers, and third parties to collect data about you and your internet activity, even if you have turned on the Do Not Track signal.
