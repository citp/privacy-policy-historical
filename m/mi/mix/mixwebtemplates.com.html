Privacy Policy

General

Privacy Policy of mixwebtemplates is used in order to protect its customers privacy and security of information stored and shared with us through our site. By registering and using our site you give consent automatically to use and disclose information if needed. Unless you agree with any terms below, you should not use products or services offered by mixwebtemplates.

It is essential to collect your personal information in order to be able to make any purchase at mixwebtemplates. It would be impossible to make purchase unless you share personal information with us. It is required to provide us with data in the form of user name and password to be able to enter our website. Additionally, your data has to be used while accessing technical support and it is required to make consent to this. Correcting-Updating Personal Information

You are free to update your information at any time directly through our site. If you want to update the email address in our site or change password,  log in at http://www.mixwebtemplates.com and click on "Your Details" link.

Protection of personal information

Organic Law 15/1999, of December 13th, for the Protection of Personal Data. Royal Decree 1720/2007, of December 21st, approving the implementing Regulation for Organic Law 15/1999, of December 13th, for the protection of personal data.

Information gathered and stored at mixwebtemplates.com is fully protected. We do our best to secure data collected and do not disclose to any unauthorized person. However, we do not take responsibility for data used during PayPal payment as your personal information used there is not registered by our system and has to be protected by PayPal security system.
