**Use of "cookies"**

Cookies are bits of electronic information that a Web site can transfer to a visitor's hard drive to help tailor and keep records of his or her visit to the site. Cookies allow Web site operators to better customize visits to the site to the visitor's individual preferences. The use of cookies is standard on the Internet and many major Web sites use them. If you've set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies, you will receive the warning message with each cookie. You can refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser. You do not need to have cookies turned on to use [www.micros.com](http://www.micros.com/). We use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it as you navigate through the member areas, however, we do not store personal identifying information in cookies. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site.

**Children's Privacy Protection**

MICROS abides by the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) requirements. [www.micros.com](http://www.micros.com/) does not target and is not intended for children under the age of 13, and does not knowingly collect data from children. When [www.micros.com](http://www.micros.com/) requests that a user enter personal data, MICROS does not inquire as to the age of the user. If MICROS discovers that it has personal data from a child under the age of 13 through its website, MICROS will eliminate that data from our systems.
