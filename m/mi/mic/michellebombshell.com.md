# Michelle Bombshell :: Model and Spokesperson – Available for Appearances, Television, Internet and Print » PRIVACY POLICY

## PRIVACY POLICY

PRIVACY

This privacy statement explains our policy regarding any personal information you may supply to us when visiting our Web site.

PERSONAL INFORMATION

There are times when we may need information from you; additionally some areas of our Web site may require you to complete a registration form. Our goal in collecting this personal information is to offer you a more personalized experience. When you let us know your preferences we will be able to deliver or allow you to access the most relevant information that meets your needs. To extend this personalization experience our Web site may save information on your system in the form of a cookie. We will not share e-Mail addresses with anyone nor disclose user information to any third party. This site gives you the option to remove your information from our database if you no longer wish to have this information used, as a basis for further contact with you. Please click on the following link to send your e-mail request, [email support](mailto:info@michellebombshell.com).

CONTACT US

In various areas throughout our Web site, we use e-mail links to allow you to contact us directly with any questions or comments that you may have. This information may be additionally filed and used to help us prioritize our development needs and continuously improve our Web site to meet your needs.

BUSINESS RELATIONSHIPS

In working with our business associates, we require strict contractual obligations, which ensure that your information is safeguarded. Our Web site contain links to other Web sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, copyright or content of such Web sites.

PUBLIC FORUMS

We also make available: forums, message boards and news groups to our customers. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

Cookie Policy

WHAT IS A COOKIE?

A cookie is either a small text file that is stored on your hard drive, or some information that is stored in memory until you close your Web browser.

WHY ARE THEY USED?

MichelleBombshell.com uses cookies to allow a secure personalized browsing experience and to help us monitor usage of our site. By using a cookie, our site can automatically read your preferences and customize pages. We recognize our user’s privacy concerns and will only use cookies as an enabling tool. You may choose to reject cookies either outright, or on a case-by-case basis using the instructions for your browser. However, in order to use our website you will need to accept cookies.
