# MisterArt.com About Us | Shop Art Supplies, Craft Supplies

MisterArt.com is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and using personal information responsibly. Please read below to see how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. 

By using our Web site or purchasing a product from us, you agree to the collection and use of information as set forth in this privacy policy. We may from time to time update this policy. We will post any changes to our policy on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect and how we use it. Terms and conditions are subject to change with or without notice. Refer to this page to review our most current privacy information. 

### What We Collect

We collect information you provide when you place an order, create an account, subscribe to a newsletter, send us an e-mail, call us, refer a friend, or enter a contest. This information may include name, address, e-mail address, phone number, credit card number, and other personal or preference information you provide to us. 

We use this information to respond to your requests, process and fulfill your orders, customize and improve your shopping experience, and communicate with you. 

In addition, we use technology that allows us to identify what type of browser you are using, the Web site from which you enter MisterArt.com, and the pages you view on our site. We aggregate this information and use it to improve our store and better serve you. 

### Cookies

Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer through your Web browser. They make it possible for us to recognize your browser when you visit. By doing this, you can save time during checkout and take full advantage of all of the features of the MisterArt.com site, including creating an account, building your favorite products list, writing product reviews, and subscribing to newsletters. In order to enjoy these benefits, your browser must be set to accept cookies. 

### How We Use Information

MisterArt.com does not sell, rent, barter, or exchange your personal information with third parties. 

You may, from time to time, receive information from us about new features, new services, and special offers we think you'll find valuable. MisterArt.com may also send special offers and information on behalf of our business partners. When we do this, we do not give that business your name or email address. If you do not wish to receive announcements or special offers from MisterArt.com, simply follow the unsubscribe instructions included at the end of any MisterArt.com e-mail message you receive. 

MisterArt.com employs reputable companies to perform functions on our behalf. Examples include fulfilling orders, delivering packages, and processing credit card payments. They have access to personal information needed to perform their functions, but may not use it for other purposes such as marketing. 

In the event that MisterArt.com or substantially all of its assets are acquired or merged with another company, all of the customer information that we have collected may be transferred to the new company. 

### Security

In order to keep your online transaction secure, MisterArt.com uses a secure server (SSL) to encrypt all of your personal information before it travels over the Internet. SSL technology is the industry standard for secure online transactions. 

In addition, consumer protection laws protect you against the unauthorized use of your credit card. Under federal law, if someone steals your credit card or your credit card number, your liability is limited to $50 no matter how much the thief charges to your account. 

### Contacting MisterArt.com

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/aboutus/contact-us.html "Contact Us").
