Privacy

Welcome to Mizoram Express! We appreciate your participation as citizen journalist and visitor. Our goal is to encourage writers in English from all Mizo tribes and concerned citizens to bring to light the good, the bad and the ugly from all aspects of governance to societal practices. However, in order to maintain a civil community, we require you to follow the following basic guidelines:

Right to Delete Posts

While we do not and cannot review every message posted by you you must agree that you are responsible for the content of your news item, comments and views. We reserve the right to delete, move or edit messages that we deem abusive, defamatory, obscene, in violation of copyright or trademark laws, or otherwise unacceptable. All posts in the ‘ME Citizen Journalist’ Section must be written in English. We reserve the right to remove the posting privileges of users who violate the standards of ME Citizen Journalist Section’s behavior at any time.

Uploading

You cannot upload to the Web site, or distribute or otherwise publish through it, anything that is libelous, defamatory, obscene, pornographic or abusive, or that otherwise violates any Indian law. We regret that MExpress cannot honor requests to remove posts except when such postings violate our editorial and policies.

Graphics

Graphics and small audio files are permitted but javascript files are not allowed.

Noncommercial

The ‘ME Citizen Journalist’ section can be used only in a noncommercial manner. You cannot, without the express approval of ME Team distribute or otherwise publish material containing any solicitation of funds, advertising or solicitation of goods or services.

Spamming

Repeatedly posting off-topic messages or “spamming” our site by posting the same message in multiple topics or otherwise deliberately disrupting our site is unacceptable behavior that may result in the user’s losing all ME Citizen Journalist’s privileges and access to our site. We reserve the right to prohibit users who in any way violate acceptable ME CJ behavior from participating. Users remain solely responsible for the content of their messages.
