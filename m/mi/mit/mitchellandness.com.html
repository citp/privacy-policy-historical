    mitchellandness.com Privacy Policy

    Mitchellandness.com is an electronic marketplace for products distributed by Mitchell & Ness, LLC (referred to as "we," "us" or "our") and its affiliates. We understand that your privacy is important to you and we established this Privacy Policy to explain what information is collected on mitchellandness.com (the "Site") and how it is used. By using this Site, you agree to the collection and use of information as set forth in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, please do not use this Site.

    Information Gathered by Us

    For visitors and registered users to the Site, we log standard technical information, such as your IP address and the kind of browser you are using. If you choose to register with us or make a purchase, you may be asked to provide personally-identifiable information, including, without limitation, name, address, email address, phone number, date of birth and financial account information, such as credit or debit card number ("PII"). We may also collect other PII when you provide feedback or comments online, such as information about our products you own.

    This Site uses first and third-party cookies and tracking technology analytical features for advertising, marketing, click impression reporting, demographic and interest reporting, and similar marketing analytics reporting features. Some of these features are provided by third-parties that support our Site services. Some of these features may allow our third-party providers to track your interests across other areas of the web to inform you of matters related to your interests and to help us optimize our business models.

    We use and allow third parties to use cookies and other web monitoring technologies. For example, we use web beacons to collect information about you and to provide a more tailored and user-friendly service to you. We may use cookies to record session information, such as items that you add to your shopping cart, to record past activity or to provide better service when you return. Authorized third parties may link your name, address and email address to other information which they collect, such as past online/offline purchase information and Web usage from other sites. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but without our cookies you will not be able to complete a purchase or take advantage of certain features of this Site.

    Pixel tags are tiny graphic files that are included in HTML-encoded email messages. We use pixel tags to gather information about emails sent to our users. When such a message is opened in an HTML-capable email program, the recipient's computer accesses our server to retrieve the pixel tag file, and allows us to record and store the date and time, the recipient's email address, and other standard logging information. These pixel tags may also read cookies.

    Our Use and Disclosure of Information

    The information we collect about you in the course of our relationship is used to operate our Site and offer products and services to you. For example, we may use your PII to fulfill your order, to send you information about our company and services, or to contact you when necessary. We may also use your PII to send you periodic emails about new products and services or upcoming events ("promotional email"). In addition, we look at technical information to analyze traffic to this Site, to enhance our offerings and to provide our services to you. For example, we may use traffic information to see which portions of this Site are more frequently accessed and to gather broad demographic information. In connection with these services, we collect non-personally identifiable information, but we will not merge personally-identifiable information with non-personally identifiable information unless you consent to that merger.

    We work with and may share information with third parties who provide services that may include, but are not necessarily limited to, website hosting, data processing and analysis, back-up and security services, and processing of credit card transactions. We may share PII with these service providers for the purpose of providing these services. Although we seek to require these service providers to protect such PII, and to use it only for the purpose for which it was provided, we cannot be responsible for third parties' use of PII.

    In order to provide you with a full range of products and services, we may share your PII with our affiliates for marketing or other purposes. An affiliate means any company that controls, is controlled by or is under common control with us. In addition, we have built relationships with selected partners that are not affiliates, but which offer products or services that may be of interest to our users. If you opt-in to receive information from us, we may also share your PII with our unaffiliated third-party partners for marketing or other business purposes. Please refer to the section of this Policy titled "Opt Out," below, if you would like to opt out of having your PII shared with affiliated or unaffiliated third parties for third-party direct marketing.

    We reserve our right to share PII about our users with other third parties, including: (1) with your consent or as otherwise stated in this Privacy Policy; (2) when we have a good faith belief it is necessary pursuant to a subpoena or other judicial or administrative order; (3) where required by law; or (4) at our sole discretion, where we deem it necessary to respond to claims, to protect the safety of any individual or the public, or to protect our rights or property or the rights or property of any third party. In addition, we may share aggregated demographic and statistical information with third parties.

    In the event of a change of ownership or corporate restructuring (i.e., a sale, merger, acquisition or transfer of our assets), we may share or transfer your PII to a third party.

    Surveys and Contests

    At various times we may offer surveys or contests to users. Surveys may ask for contact information or unique identifying information to increase the value of the results. Contests may require contact information or other demographic or personally identifiable information in order to determine eligibility. We use information collected from surveys and contests to improve our service and to notify and contact winners of various contests. If you request to have your PII removed from your "My Account" page, we will remove it from its active database, though it may remain stored indefinitely in our back-up media and archival records.
