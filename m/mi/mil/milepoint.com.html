Privacy Policy

Because we at MilePoint respect our members and understand that you are concerned about privacy, we have instituted policies and procedures intended to ensure that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. We also post this privacy policy so that our members know what kind of information we collect, how it is handled and with whom it may be shared.

The purpose MilePoint gathers member information is to verify each member's Sponsor account information, including Sponsor mile and/or point balances, to improve the content on our Web site, to enhance your online shopping experience, and for marketing and promotional purposes. At this point, such information includes member information gathered in the course of the registration of each MilePoint member. Some of the information gathered may be shared in the aggregate with corporate affiliates of MilePoint, as well as other responsible organizations that offer products or services that we believe may enhance the experience of our MilePoint members.

MilePoint does not sell individual member names or other private profile information to third parties and have no intention of doing so in the future. MilePoint may share general member information with our partners only for specific and pertinent promotional use but only if our members have opted to receive such information.

As we continue to develop our Web site and take advantage of technologies to improve the services we provide, this policy likely will change. We therefore encourage you to refer to this policy on an ongoing basis so that you understand our current privacy policy. For additional information about protecting your privacy, please see the Frequently Asked Questions section.

MilePoint is a Licensee of TRUSTe

We are pleased to inform you that MilePoint is a licensee of the TRUSTe Privacy Program. TRUSTe is an independent, non-profit organization whose mission is to build users’ trust and confidence in the Internet by promoting the use of fair information practices. Because MilePoint wants to demonstrate its continued commitment to your privacy, we have agreed to disclose our information practices and have our privacy practices reviewed for compliance by TRUSTe.

If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should first contact Customer Support by email at mail@milepoint.com. If you do not receive acknowledgment of your inquiry or your inquiry has not been satisfactorily addressed, you should then contact TRUSTe . TRUSTe will then serve as a liaison with the Web site to resolve your concerns.

MilePoint Data Security

MilePoint provides a secure environment for your data and transactions. All MilePoint data and business processes are firewall protected, and accessible only through the MilePoint Web server. Our secured Oracle database resides behind a second firewall, accessible only by our Cambio engine.

Access to Cambio is limited to our Web server only. In order to access any information through Cambio, a valid username and password, as well as a matching, valid session ID is required. Access to the MilePoint Web server, Cambio, and the MilePoint database is strictly controlled and administered by the appropriate MilePoint personnel. All MilePoint security measures are periodically reviewed and updated.

Retail transactions are processed within the secure retailers environment. Data that is exchanged between retailers and MilePoint is passed directly from the retail Web site to the MilePoint Web site, and 40-bit IIS encryption is available for all transactions.

Any incoming and outgoing sponsor batch data will use a secure FTP site.

Information Collection and Use

MilePoint is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. As we have stated we will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. MilePoint collects information from our members at the registration page of our Web site.

Registration

In order to use the benefits of this Web site, a member must first complete the on-line registration form. During registration a member is required to give their contact information (such as name, e-mail address and Sponsor account information). This information is used to allow the member to use the services on our site. Members may provide demographic information (such as income level and gender), and other unique identifiers (such as social security number), however this information will only be used to enhance the member's experience at our site.

Links

This Web site is designed to contain links to numerous other sites. Please be aware that we cannot be responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our members to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every Web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by MilePoint.

Correction/Updating Personal Information:

If a member's personally identifiable information changes (such as your zip code), or if a member no longer desires our service, we will endeavor to provide a way to correct, update or remove that member's personal data provided to us. This can be done at the member registration page or by e-mailing our Customer Support.

Opt-In/Opt-Out

As a Member of MilePoint you have the option to receive updates from MilePoint about special offers from our program Sponsors and Merchants as well as noteworthy news provided to you in our newsletter. To receive this information, you must register to become a MilePoint member and ensure that the appropriate box(es) at the MilePoint registration page have been clicked. If you decide you would rather not receive these emails, you can always unregister, update your Member information or simply email our Customer Support.

Refer-a-Friend Program

Since we know that no one likes to be deluged by unwanted e-mail, when you use the Refer-A-Friend form, only a single e-mail is sent to your friend, just like if you sent it to him or her. MilePoint will not use the e-mail addresses for any purpose other than to encourage your friend(s) to enroll into the MilePoint.com program.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

In the unlikely event that we would materially change the use of personally identifiable information, we would use our best efforts to notify you of these changes by email and/or providing notice of these changes on our web site. You will continue to have a choice as to whether or not we may use your information if our policy changes. In addition, if we make any material changes in our privacy practices that do not affect Member information already stored in our database, we will provide the appropriate notice on our web site notifying Members of the change. For those Members who have opted to receive communication from us, we may notify those Members by email of such changes to our privacy practices.
