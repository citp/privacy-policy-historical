# Privacy Policy | Milo.com

**We respect your privacy.** This is why we have taken the time to disclose our information collection practices and our privacy policy. Please take the time to review this document.

Of the information we do collect, we allow our visitors the following access to the information:

  * The website does not collect data that can identify its visitors.



Our organization processes all disputes through email. The address to send comments to is: privacy@milo.com. Please allow 24-48 hours to receive a reply.

Collecting information provides specific benefits to our website visitors:

Collecting your information may allow us to provide you with more relevant content to help you find what you are looking for faster or to suggest content that you may otherwise find interesting. For example, we may use your location to allow you to search local stores in your area or past products that you were interested in to recommend new products you may enjoy.

We collect information for the following purpose(s):

  * Completing or supporting an activity.
  * Research and development.
  * Tailoring the website for its visitors.
  * Building records of visitors without linking identifying information to the records for the purpose of research, analysis and reporting.
  * Building records of visitors for the purpose of research, analysis and reporting.
  * Building records of visitors for the purpose determining visitors habits, interests or characteristics in order to make decisions that my directly affect the visitor.



### Who has access to the information we collect?

Ourselves and/or any of our agents which utilize the information only for the purposes outlined above.

### How long is information kept?

Information is retained to meet the stated purposes listed above. Information is discarded at the earliest time possible.

If you believe that our website has collected incorrect information or if you would like to dispute any information, please contact us by sending an email to [privacy@milo.com](mailto:privacy@milo.com)
