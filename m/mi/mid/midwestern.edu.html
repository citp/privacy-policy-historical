Privacy Policy

This privacy policy covers Midwestern University's use and handling of personally identifiable information we collect when you use this website. This policy cannot and does not cover the practices of companies we do not own or control, or people we do not employ or manage.

We may collect personally identifiable information when you visit or use our website. For example, we automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookie information, and the page you requested, as well as the page you linked from. We also receive and record any information you submit via forms on our Web site and correlate and analyze that information with commercially available databases when appropriate. We may set and access cookies on your computer.

We may use this information for four general purposes: to monitor and improve performance of our website and generate aggregate statistical reports, to customize the content you see, to fulfill your requests for certain services and information, and to contact you.

We will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.
