CUSTOMER INFORMATION DISCLOSURE

This disclosure provides information about the collection, use and security of consumers’ nonpublic personal information by MidFirst Bank (“we”). This disclosure applies to you if we provide one or more products or services to you to be used primarily for personal, family or household purposes. The information practices described in this disclosure do not apply to (1) products or services used for business purposes, (2) credit serviced by our affiliates Midland Mortgage Co. or First Credit Solutions Inc.

We collect nonpublic personal information about you from the following sources:

  Information we receive from you on applications or other forms;
Information about your transactions with us, our affiliates, or others; and
Information we receive from a consumer reporting agency.

As permitted by law, we may disclose nonpublic personal information about you to our affiliates (businesses related to us by common ownership or control) and to other nonaffiliated third parties under certain circumstances. We may disclose certain information we collect to companies that perform activities on our behalf to service your account, including but not limited to companies that perform check printing or data processing services, credit bureaus, real estate appraisers, and title companies. Additionally, we may disclose information about you to nonaffiliated firms that assist us in marketing our products. We may also disclose information about you to financial institution partners to offer jointly endorsed financial products or services, such as credit cards. We reserve the right to disclose your information in these and other circumstances as allowed or required by law. Other than as described in this disclosure or as otherwise authorized or required by law, we do not disclose your nonpublic personal information to nonaffiliated third parties. We maintain procedures whereby companies that receive your nonpublic personal information from us are required to maintain it in a secure manner and use it only for the purpose for which it was provided.

Our employees are trained regarding the importance of customer financial privacy and proper handling of confidential information. We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to reasonably guard your nonpublic personal information. Additionally, we restrict access to nonpublic personal information about you to those employees who have a business reason for accessing that information.

If you close your account(s) or become an inactive customer, we will adhere to the information practices as described in this notice.
