Singing Search
You can find songs with midomi and your own voice. Forgot the name of a song? Heard a bit of one on the radio? All you need is your computer's microphone.
  1. Click the "Singing Search" to start recording your search.
  2. Sing or hum your search. For best results, keep the volume bar in the green, record 10 seconds or more of the tune, and avoid background noise.
  3. Click the green arrow to see the results.
For more information, visit our introduction.
If you're having problems, test your microphone.
Text Search
Our text search is a quick way to find songs and people. You can enter a band's or singer's name, all or part of a song title, and even find other midomi users. Simply type in the term and press return or click the green arrow.
