Questions?

  • What do you mean by permission?

    What constitutes "permission" can vary but a simply summary of it is that your recipients should have given you direct permissions to email them or be a recent customer of yours. Email us anytime if you're not sure if your list is permission based and we'll figure it out with you.

  • Can I send to old lists?

    Sending to old and non-responsive emails is a drag on your delivery and an old list can cause more trouble than good but there are some techniques to send safely. Send only to those who've been actively in contact with you and receptive to your emails in the last year or so.

  • How do I know if my list is legit?

    Let's discuss this! Email us anytime with some details about how you collected your contacts and we'll help out. If the answer to "Did they signup directly with you to receive emails?" is a resounding "Yes!" then you're good to go!

Permission Policy

Mad Mimi is permission based. That means that the people who receive your emails must have given you permission to email them somehow, usually by signing up directly or as active customers of yours. Our Terms of Use conform to Can Spam regulations and more importantly, adhere to the best practices laid out by ISP's.

To send mailings using Mad Mimi, you must follow the content, privacy, and permission guidelines set by the postmasters of the domains you are sending to. You may only send emails that are designed with care and are the type of emails your recipients have asked for.

In addition, you should regularly remove subscribers who do not read your emails or interact with you, or your business, for more than 18 months.

Safety of your personal information

Mad Mimi collects certain information about you in order to allow your account to function properly. At any time, you may log in and change this information, or remove it. If your personal information changes, or if you no longer desire our service, you may correct, update, amend, delete/remove, or deactivate it by making the change on your account page or plans page, or by emailing our customer support here. We will respond to your request to access within 2 days. We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Protecting your customer lists and data

Although we reserve the right to monitor your lists, content and response data from time to time to make sure they comply with our Terms of Use, we will never share, sell or rent your customer data to anyone for any reason.

Tracking Technologies

A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user’s computer for record-keeping purposes. We use cookies on this site. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our site. We use both session ID cookies and persistent cookies. A session ID cookie expires when you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains on your hard drive for longer, and are used for the porposes or remembering your settings, local storage, for example making sure you don't lose your text while you're composing an emaail campaign, and "remember me" option. You can remove persistent cookies by following directions provided in your Internet browser’s “help” directory. Here is more information about cookies. If you reject cookies, you may still use our site, but your ability to use some areas of our site will be limited.

The use of cookies by our tracking utility company, for example, Google Analytics and service providers like SnapEngage Live Chat, is not covered by our privacy policy. We do not have access or control over these cookies. These providers use both session ID and persistent cookies to make it easier for you to use our live chat and for us to log general website traffic. Our third party partners employ clear gifs (a.k.a. Web Beacons/Web Bugs), images, and scripts that help them better manage content on our site. We do not tie the information gathered to our customers’ or users’ personally identifiable information.
