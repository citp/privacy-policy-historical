Privacy Policy

Data Collection

While browsing or downloading from our website, we will log your IP address and browser user-agent information. This is for tracking your session and for statistical purposes only. This information will not be shared with any third-parties and can only be viewed by MingleVille administrators.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve advertisements when you visit our website. These companies may use information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. The advertisements are served via Google AdSense who use cookies to serve ads to you. Google AdSense utilize DART cookies which allow them to serve ads based on your visits to ours and other sites. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

Personal Information

For your account we require the following personal information from you:

- Your e-mail address so we can send you notifications regarding your account.

- Your date of birth so we can identify your real age.

- Your gender as it is an important element for your profile.

- Your country as it is an important element for your profile.

Your e-mail address will not be visible to other users. However, those who already know your e-mail address (e.g. friends) will be able to find your profile via the Invite & Find Friends feature.

Your date of birth will be visible to your friends and you will appear in our Birthday Members on your birthday. You may disable these features in your Privacy Settings.

If you disclose your name or location, these will be visible on your profile. Your gender and country will also be visible and cannot be hidden.

Invitation E-mails & Blocked Addresses

E-mail addresses entered via the Invite & Find Friends feature will be stored in our database in a one-way hashing format.

Published Information

You must exercise caution when deciding what personal information you wish to be publicly viewable on the Internet. Anybody may be able to download your pictures or videos from your profile. MingleVille will not be held responsible for any misuse of your personal information, pictures or videos by another person.

Cookies

MingleVille uses cookies to keep track of your session and log you in automatically (if enabled). Your browser settings should allow cookies to be set or you will not be able to login.

When logging in, you may tick the checkbox to automatically log you in every time you visit our site. This will create a cookie which will store some of your account data to allow you to login automatically. This feature should not be used on a shared computer. Simply Logout to clear this cookie and prevent being logged in automatically.

3rdparty Content

MingleVille is not responsible for the content or practices of any other websites which may link to advertisements or links members have posted.

Contact Us

You may contact us at any time. Please go to our Contact Us page to see what current contact methods we have available.
