–Web traffic statistics: Summary information such as IP address, operating system, and browser type are collected.

–Comments: Comments may be edited for errors or deleted if screened as spam. Email addresses for comments are used to screen for spam and for receiving a reply from me.

These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
