Your online privacy is important. This document describes how Mind Your Decisions collects and uses information on the web site and through the mailing list.

RSS and email: Email addresses are gathered only for the purpose of delivering posts and newsletters. Summarized statistics (number of subscribers) may be disclosed.

Web traffic statistics: Summary information such as IP address, operating system, and browser type are collected.

Comments: Comments may be edited for errors or deleted if screened as spam. Email addresses for comments are used to screen for spam and for receiving a reply from me.

Third party advertising: We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.
