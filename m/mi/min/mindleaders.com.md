## Information Collection and Storage of Personal Information

When you visit our websites, platform and applications, MindLeaders may collect information in two ways: through information automatically gathered from your computer or through information you voluntarily provide at a website, platform or application. As to automatically gathered information, MindLeaders collects navigational information about where visitors go on our website, how many visits are made to the site, when the site is visited and other non-personal information (for example, this can include domain type, browser version, service provider, IP address). 

Voluntarily provided information is collected online when you register for our services or purchase our products. Whenever you register for our services or purchase our products, we will collect personal information such as your name, e-mail address, mailing address, company and phone number, as well as any billing information or specific comments or questions that you provide. Additionally, your contact information may be entered into our platform by a third party on your behalf in order to provide the service for you. This personal information will only be used for the purpose for which it was intended. We also offer general “contact us” features on our website, in which you will be asked to provide your name and e-mail address to request information or be contacted by a sales agent. We may also collect additional information in connection with events, special offers, contests, specialized products, or other promotions. As used herein, “Data” or “Personally Identifiable Information” refers to this class of information. 

The following is a list of the Data that may be collected by automatic or voluntary means or in connection with events, special offers, contests, specialized products, or other promotions: 

  * (a) name;
  * (b) gender;
  * (c) job title;
  * (d) profession;
  * (e) contact information such as email addresses and telephone numbers;
  * (f) demographic information such as post code, preferences and interests;
  * (g) financial information such as credit / debit card numbers;
  * (h) IP address (automatically collected);
  * (i) web browser type and version (automatically collected);
  * (j) operating system (automatically collected);
  * (k) a list of URLs starting with a referring site, your activity on this website, and the site you exit to (automatically collected); 
  * (l) Cookie information (see “Cookies” section below);
  * (m) information about your interest in MindLeaders products or services;
  * (n) information about your interest in receiving newsletters or being contacted by a sales representative;
  * (o) information provided in connection with the registration of your organization for certain products and services provided by MindLeaders;
  * (p) information about other transactions that you or your organization may enter with MindLeaders;
  * (q) any other Personal Information you or your company may disclose to MindLeaders through an online form or email.



By voluntarily entering Data you enable MindLeaders to store, process and transmit this Data and provide you with the services you select. Please be aware that if you choose not to submit the information requested, you may be limited in your use of the website and its services. 

## Use of Collected Information:

The navigational information that is automatically gathered when you visit our website(s) is used on an aggregate basis to evaluate the effectiveness of the website(s), analyze trends and administer the site(s). This navigational information allows us to determine such things as which areas of our website are most visited and difficulties visitors may experience in accessing the site. MindLeaders does not associate any navigational information with individual users. 

Any personal Data you submit will be retained by MindLeaders for as long as you use the Services and Systems provided by the website. We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Data that you may submit through any communications System that we may provide may be retained for a longer period. If you wish to cancel your account, delete your data or request that we no longer use your information to provide you services, contact us as described herein. We will respond to your request within 30 business days. You should also be aware that we may disclose specific personal Data if necessary to do so by law, or based on our good faith belief that it is necessary to conform or comply with the law, or if necessary to protect the users of our website, the site, or the public. All personal Data is stored securely in accordance with applicable principles of EU data protection legislation. 

This information is used: 

  * to deliver the service or product you requested;
  * to enable billing and shipping; 
  * to provide you with information about related products and services; 
  * to improve our website; 
  * to better administer and troubleshoot our systems;
  * for internal record keeping;
  * to improve of our products / services;
  * to transmit by email or standard mail promotional materials that may be of interest to you;
  * for market research purposes which may be done using email, telephone, fax or mail (such information may be used to customize or update the website);
  * to process employment applications submitted via the website.



**MindLeaders does not sell, rent or trade the information you voluntarily provide to us on this website to unrelated third parties for promotional purposes**. We may use third parties to help us monitor site activity, and to respond to your requests for specific information or provide support for products. If personal information (for example, name, address, e-mail address, telephone number) is provided to any of these third parties, we will require that such information be maintained by them in confidence and according to the same standards set out in the EU Safe Harbor Program and US-Swiss Safe Harbor Framework. We will not share this information with any other person, company or organization, except to third parties providing products or services to you pursuant to your agreement with MindLeaders, and as may be required by law. Unless otherwise stated elsewhere, information concerning an individual’s usage of our sites will not be disclosed to third parties except in aggregate form such that your information will not be personally identifiable. For more details on processing and storage, see “Security” and “Location” sections below. 

If you receive a promotional communication from us, you will be able to unsubscribe by following the instructions in the email.

##  Cookies and Tracking Technologies: 

MindLeaders and third parties use a browser feature known as a cookie which assigns a unique identification to your computer. Cookies are typically stored on your computer's hard drive and are used by MindLeaders to help track clicks as you move through pages within the MindLeaders website. Cookies may also be used to determine browser plug-ins so that we may optimize your experience on our site. With most Internet browsers, you can erase Cookies from your computer hard drive, block all Cookies or receive a warning before a Cookie is stored. Please refer to your browser instructions or help screen to learn more about these functions. MindLeaders' cookies are linked to personally identifiable information. 

We use a third-party tracking service that uses other tracking technologies such as web beacons, scripts, and images to track non-personal information about visitors to our site in the aggregate.

##  Security: 

MindLeaders limits access to the personal information collected at this website to employees and service providers with a valid business need for the information. Our employees are advised about the importance we place on privacy, and we hold our employees responsible for upholding our privacy standards. We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. When purchasing an item from our storefront, we encrypt the transmission of that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). In some cases, when you log into our platform, you may elect to have the transmission of that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). In addition, certain types of data collected on our platform will always use secure socket layer technology (SSL).

We assume that it is generally our customers, the “data controllers,” responsibility to notify individuals about the purposes for which they collect and use information about them, how they can contact you with any inquiries or complaints, the types of third parties to which they disclose your information, and the choices and means they offer you for limiting your use and disclosure. As your data processor, we make available to you this privacy policy so that you can better understand our data practices. 

The U.S.-EU Safe Harbor and US-Swiss Safe Harbor requires that members offer end users a choice to opt out of uses and disclosures of their data that are incompatible with the purposes for which that data was originally collected or subsequently authorized. We operate under the assumption that it is the customer's obligation to obtain from their data subjects the appropriate consent to transfer their data to us and to process their data using our products for defined purposes. As your data processor, we will not share, sell, rent, or trade with third parties for their marketing purposes any data subject information shared with us. 

The U.S.-EU Safe Harbor and US-Swiss Safe Harbor requires that data subjects must have access to personal information about them that an organization holds, and that they be able to correct, amend, or delete that information where it is inaccurate. We operate under the assumption that it is generally the data controller's obligation to provide their data subjects a means of accessing their data. If you would like the personal information we hold about you, the End User, deleted, please contact us as described below. We will respond to requests within 30 days of receipt. Also, please contact the data controller from whom you received the e-mail. 

##  MindLeaders Facebook Application: Facebook Connect 

If you are accessing MindLeaders through Facebook request for permission, you are authorizing us to store, collect, and use in accordance with this Privacy Policy any and all information that you agreed Facebook could provide to us through this action. The agreement takes place when you “accept” the application on Facebook. 

The purpose of the application is to enable you to purchase and complete a training course, which has been developed in cooperation with Facebook. This training is embedded within an i-Frame on Facebook. 

Personal Information such as name, email address, and credit card number are collected within the application is collected directly by MindLeaders. We share this information with third party service providers to provide payment services. 

We do share information back with Facebook to perform this service such as: 

(a) We will conduct a satisfaction survey of all users and these results will be shared with Facebook. For example, our survey will ask, “how satisfied are you with the training?” “how likely are you to recommend the training?” and “what suggestions do you have to improve the course?” These survey results will be shared with FB aggregated on country basis. 

(b) We will provide basic user data for all users (e.g., email, attendance, completion status, price paid for training) to Facebook for internal use by Facebook. This data may be used by Facebook for its own internal purposes. 

(c) We will ask you if you would like to post to your wall the fact that you have just completed the Facebook Trainer for Business course. By consenting to this request, you authorize MindLeaders to post and display your training completion status on your Facebook wall. 

All of this information is shared to perform the function of taking the training course.
