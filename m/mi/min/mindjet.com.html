Effective Date and last updated: October 2007.

Mindjet, LLC ("Mindjet", "we", or "us") is a licensee of the TRUSTe Privacy Program. TRUSTe is an independent, non-profit organization whose mission is to build users trust and confidence in the Internet by promoting the use of fair information practices. This privacy statement covers the web site www.mindjet.com (the "Web Site"). Because Mindjet wants to demonstrate its commitment to your privacy, it has agreed to disclose its information practices and have its privacy practices reviewed for compliance by TRUSTe.

If you have questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy (the "Privacy Policy"), you should first contact Mindjets Privacy Officer by emailing privacy@mindjet.com. If you do not receive acknowledgement of your inquiry, or your inquiry has not been satisfactorily addressed, you should then contact TRUSTe at the following web site address: http://www.truste.org/consumers/watchdog_complaint.php TRUSTe will then serve as liaison with Mindjet to resolve your concerns.

The TRUSTe program covers only information that is collected through the Web Site, and does not cover information that may be collected through software downloaded from the Web Site.

The Privacy Policy governs information that is collected through the Web Site, through the use of the Mindjet software ("Software"), and other services offered on or through the Web Site (collectively, the "Services").

Please read the Privacy Policy carefully. It will serve to explain what Personal Information we will gather from you, and when and how this information is used.

"Personal Information" is information about a person that is personally identifiable to such person like the persons name, address, e-mail address, or phone number, as well as other non-public information that is associated with the foregoing.

"Anonymous Information" means information that is not associated with or linked to Personal Information; Anonymous Information does not permit the identification of individual persons.

The Web Site is not directed at anyone under the age of 18. Please check back periodically for changes in our Privacy Policy.

  7. Use of Cookies, GIF Files, and other Technology.

    Cookies. Cookies are pieces of information that some web sites transfer to the computer that is browsing that web site ("Cookies"), and are used for record-keeping purposes at many web sites. The use of Cookies makes web surfing easier by performing certain functions such as saving your passwords, background information, and your personal preferences regarding your use of the particular web site. Many consider the use of Cookies to be an industry standard. Your browser is probably set to accept Cookies; however, it may be set to block acceptance of cookies, erase Cookies from your computer hard drive, or receive a warning before a Cookie is stored. If you would prefer not to receive Cookies, you can alter the configuration of your browser to refuse, erase, or to warn you of the use of Cookies. If you choose to have your browser refuse Cookies, it is possible that some areas of web sites that use Cookies will not function properly when you view them and your online experience will be limited.

    Mindjet uses Cookies to administer the Web Site and not to track surfing habits. We collect personal identifying information (the "Information") under the following situations: (1) to the extent that you provide Information to us in an e-mail message or in your profile; (2) to the extent that your web browser provides Information to our web server, like the site you linked from, the identity of your internet service provider, or the type of browser you are using; and (3) to the extent that you provide Personal Information to us in a CGI form (for example, when you complete a search request).

    GIF Files. Mindjet uses pixels, or transparent GIF files, to help manage online advertising. These GIF files may be provided by our ad management partner and enable it to recognize a unique Cookie on your web browser, which in turn enables us to learn which advertisements bring users to our Web Site. The Cookie was placed by us, or by another advertiser who works with our ad management partner. With both Cookies and spotlight technology, the information that we collect and share is Anonymous Information, which may be shared in aggregate form, and it is not personally identifiable and therefore does not contain your name, address, telephone number, or email address.

    Monitoring Scripts. Mindjet uses certain third party web analytics software in order to monitor the use of its Web Site. Mindjet may also employ a monitoring script to enables it to proactively request chats with you. If a user accepts a chat, the monitoring script will capture pre- and post-chat surveys that are submitted by such users, as well as the users IP address and browser version; however, the third party web analytics software provider does not use any of this data.

    If you have concerns about cookies or GIF Files, or need assistance on how to enable the cookies on your system, please contact us at support@mindjet.com.
