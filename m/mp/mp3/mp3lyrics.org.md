# Privacy policy of Mp3Lyrics

Mp3 Lyrics takes your privacy seriously

Personally Identifying Information

This means information that individually identifies you, such as your name, physical address or email address. You are never required to submit any such information. You may choose to supply your email address when submitting or correcting lyrics, or when you want to [contact](https://web.archive.org/Contact/) us. Such information is only stored for the purpose of enabling us to reply your request 

Data Collection

[Mp3 Lyrics](https://web.archive.org/ "Mp3 Lyrics Makes it Fun to Find Songs :-\)") collects limited non-personally identifying information your browser makes available whenever you visit a website. This information usually includes your [browser](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#webrowser) type, browser language, the date and time of your [page](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#webpage) request (or [query](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#query)) and one or more [cookie](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#cookies)s that may uniquely identify your browser. We may use this information to operate, develop and improve out searching services. Such information is only used in aggregated form. An example of this is the number of users who searched for a specific term, to record what artists or songs that are popular at a given time. 

In addition to your [browser](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#webrowser) information, we log your [Internet Protocol Address](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#ipaddress) or [host name](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#hostname). Such information is used principally to track your location in the effort of displaying relevant advertisement, and in the effort of figuring out what [crawlers](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#robot) are visiting this site. Your IP address or host name is only stored for a limited time, depending on your [http](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#http "What is HTTP?") / [IP](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#ip "What is IP?") behavior, before it is deleted. 

Cookies

During your visit to [Mp3 Lyrics](https://web.archive.org/ "Mp3 Lyrics Makes it Fun to Find Songs :-\)") a [cookie](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#cookies) may be sent to your browser that uniquely identifies your browser. We use cookies to improve the quality of this service and to better understand how people interact with us. Most [browser](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#webrowser)s are initially set up to accept cookies. Many browsers have the option to be configured to refuse all cookies or to indicate when cookies are being set. If you configure your browser to refuse cookies, some functions on [Mp3 Lyrics](https://web.archive.org/ "Mp3 Lyrics Makes it Fun to Find Songs :-\)") may not be working correctly. However you will still be able to search for lyrics. To [submit](https://web.archive.org/Submit/) or correct lyrics or make [contact](https://web.archive.org/Contact/) your browser must support the use of cookies. 

JavaScript

[Mp3 Lyrics](https://web.archive.org/ "Mp3 Lyrics Makes it Fun to Find Songs :-\)") uses [JavaScript](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#javascript) for the following purposes: 

  * Comply with [copyright](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#copyright) & [license](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#license) requirements.
  * Improve services and user experience with functionality and enhancements that supersedes [HTML](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#html "What is HTML?")-only pages.
  * Decrease [page](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#webpage) load times to visitors.



Information Sharing

[Mp3 Lyrics](https://web.archive.org/ "Mp3 Lyrics Makes it Fun to Find Songs :-\)") does not rent or sell any information that is collected of the surfers visiting this web site. 

Information Security

[Mp3 Lyrics](https://web.archive.org/ "Mp3 Lyrics Makes it Fun to Find Songs :-\)") take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration of data. 

Third Party Advertising

[Mp3 Lyrics](https://web.archive.org/ "Mp3 Lyrics Makes it Fun to Find Songs :-\)") allow [third-party advertising companies](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#tpads) to serve ads and collect certain anonymous information during your stay on mp3lyrics.org These companies may use non-personally identifiable information (e.g. click stream information, [browser](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#webrowser) type, time and date, subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over) during your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you. These companies typically use a [cookie](https://web.archive.org/Faq/#cookies) or third party web beacon to collect this information. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI).
