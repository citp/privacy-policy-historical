Mp3 Lyrics takes your privacy seriously
This policy covers how we treat personal information that is collected and received.
What is Privacy and Why is it Important?
Personally Identifying Information
This means information that individually identifies you, such as your name, physical address or email address. You are never required to submit any such information. You may choose to supply your email address when submitting or correcting lyrics, or when you want to contact us. Such information is only stored for the purpose of enabling us to reply your request
Data Collection
Mp3 Lyrics collects limited non-personally identifying information your browser makes available whenever you visit a website. This information usually includes your browser type, browser language, the date and time of your page request (or query) and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser. We may use this information to operate, develop and improve out searching services. Such information is only used in aggregated form. An example of this is the number of users who searched for a specific term, to record what artists or songs that are popular at a given time.

In addition to your browser information, we log your Internet Protocol Address or host name. Such information is used principally to track your location in the effort of displaying relevant advertisement, and in the effort of figuring out what crawlers are visiting this site. Your IP address or host name is only stored for a limited time, depending on your http / IP behavior, before it is deleted.
Cookies
During your visit to Mp3 Lyrics a cookie may be sent to your browser that uniquely identifies your browser. We use cookies to improve the quality of this service and to better understand how people interact with us. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. Many browsers have the option to be configured to refuse all cookies or to indicate when cookies are being set. If you configure your browser to refuse cookies, some functions on Mp3 Lyrics may not be working correctly. However you will still be able to search for lyrics. To submit or correct lyrics or make contact your browser must support the use of cookies.
JavaScript
Mp3 Lyrics uses JavaScript for the following purposes:
  • Comply with copyright & license requirements.
  • Improve services and user experience with functionality and enhancements that supersedes HTML-only pages.
  • Decrease page load times to visitors.
Information Sharing
Mp3 Lyrics does not rent or sell any information that is collected of the surfers visiting this web site.
Information Security
Mp3 Lyrics take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration of data.
Third Party Advertising
Mp3 Lyrics allow third-party advertising companies to serve ads and collect certain anonymous information during your stay on mp3lyrics.org These companies may use non-personally identifiable information (e.g. click stream information, browser type, time and date, subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over) during your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you. These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI).
Third Party Cookies
In the course of serving advertisements, our third-party advertising companies may place or recognize cookies with your browser
