Privacy Policy http://www.mrscleanusa.com/

Your privacy is protected with us. We do not collect and we will not sell or rent your personal information.

We will never release, or otherwise disseminate, your personal information without your permission. You may visit mrscleanusa.com without revealing any personal information about yourself.

Our visitors are able to read all pages on mrscleanusa.com including all information on our cleaning services, postings in the cleaning forums, and our house cleaning tips and stain removal help pages.

Personal information (name, address, email, phone, home specs) is required if you request an online house cleaning estimate. Other than using this information to verify the service area and provide an accurate cleaning estimate to you, this online data is not used by us further in any way.

Personal information is required via registration (name, email) to post information in the cleaning forum sections of mrscleanusa.com. Registration is not required to read any post, or read the house cleaning tips or stain removal pages.
