Privacy Policy Statement

 To Our Visitors...

The expansion and penetration of the Internet into our daily lives has yielded dramatic benefits, and potential risks. The abuse of user tracking and customization can result in the invasion of surfers' privacy and confidentiality. Further, privacy rights in the information age is a personal concern of the MRQE publisher. This assurance notwithstanding, let the following statement serve as MRQE's privacy policy statement.
 User Information
All visitors to MRQE retain complete anonymity by default. As pages are delivered, MRQE does not acquire or collect any personal information about you. Your name, address, phone number, and email address all remain your private properties. MRQE does, however, collect information which is specific to the technical details of your arrival at the site. Our web servers may note non-personally identifying details such as, but not limited to, your computer's IP address and operating and windowing systems, the type of browser you use, and your language preferences. This information is collected solely for customizing the pages delivered to you (minor differences for Unix/X vs. MS Windows users, for instance) and for estimating site popularity.

There are instances where a visitor may provide specific personal information in response to a survey, or as part of a future optional user customization process. For surveys, MRQE (or a third party working in conjunction with MRQE) acquires this information to help with the marketing of the web site, or to assist in the building of a demographic profile with the purpose of attracting appropriate advertisers. In such cases, the privacy policy statement of this third party will be made available. Any potential user customization would be, of course, optional, and the information collected would serve to improve the quality of the visitor's experience.

The privacy policy statement of our primary advertising broker, BURST!, is available here.

 Collecting Statistics
MRQE collects statistics on what titles its visitors are looking up, and -- with the intent of gauging reviewer popularity -- plans to soon do the same for the off-site links that are followed. Any tracking of individual user's actions is intended for customization purposes only, and will never be shared with third parties. Aggregate (non-personal) statistics may be displayed on MRQE pages (e.g., the Most Popular Titles pages) or shared with third parties.
 Use of Cookies
MRQE may soon begin issuing cookies for two reasons: 1) to assist in the estimating the extent of MRQE's popularity, and 2) to allow for user customization of the MRQE experience. We understand that some people are not comfortable with cookies and have customized their browsers to never accept them. For that reason (and browser compatibility), we will never require that one accept cookies to enjoy (non-personalized) MRQE services.
 Your Opinions Are Valuable
If there is something about these policies that you are not comfortable with, or if you find something missing from this statement, please contact us at the address below.
