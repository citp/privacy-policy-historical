Privacy policy

General

  • MyanmarYP.com collects personal information about you and your business when you create your business listing. MyanmarYP.com will not rent, sell, or share information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies. Any other data collected on the site is purely for the purpose of running the site and will not be shared, rented or sold.
  • We reserve the right to send registered users certain communications relating to the MyanmarYP.com service, such as service announcements, administrative messages and the MyanmarYP.com. Newsletter (when subscribed to), that are considered part of your MyanmarYP.com website. Business Listing, without offering you the opportunity to opt-out of receiving them.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

  • MyanmarYP.com may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your Business Listing or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
