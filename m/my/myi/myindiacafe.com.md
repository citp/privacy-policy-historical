# MyIndiaCafe.com - Privacy Policy

Our mission at MyIndiaCafe.com is to enrich the user experience by offering fast, convenient access to the very best of the Web, including content, interaction and commerce. To achieve this mission, we need to collect certain information to understand how you differ from our other unique users. We basically collect this information through registration. 

Registration 

You may provide us with certain information such as your name, email address and postal addresses when registering for certain services such as Chat, Email, Bulletin Boards and promotions. This information is used for purposes such as personalization and verification. 

Use of Information 

Any information you provide to MyIndiaCafe.com is maintained and accessible only by MyIndiaCafe.com and the Network of sites. MyIndiaCafe.com may use this information to offer you notices regarding other MyIndiaCafe.com  services and to further enhance your MyIndiaCafe.com experience. Information submitted in connection with diaCMyIndiaCafe.com Email, Chat and Bulletin Boards is shared with the providers of those services. These providers are not authorized to use any shared information other than to send you notices regarding updates to the services. We may use only the anonymous click stream data to help our advertisers deliver better targeted advertisements. MyIndiaCafe.com will not disclose any information about any individual user except to comply with applicable law or valid legal process.
