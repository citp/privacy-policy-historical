# Efusjon

efusjon collects personal identifiable information when needed, like names, addresses, social security numbers, email addresses, etc., when voluntarily submitted. The information you provide is used to fulfill your specific request. Privacy protection is critical to our business and the protection of our Customers and Distributors. 

efusjon may use tracking technology depending on the features offered. Tracking technology/cookies may be used to help customize the site for visitors and help efusjon better understand how visitors use the site. Personal information cannot be collected via cookies and other tracking technology. We at efusjon are not responsible for information that is obtained independently by third parties as a result of your use of the Internet. 

We may share information with governmental agencies or other companies assisting us in fraud prevention or investigation. We may do so when: 1) permitted or required by law. 2) trying to prevent against or prevent actual or potential fraud or unauthorized transactions. The information is not provided to these companies or entities for marketing purposes. 

Your personally identifiable information is kept secure. Only authorized employees, agents and contractors (who have agreed to keep information secure and confidential) have access to this information
