# MyUS.com - Privacy Policy

## **Philosophy**

  
We hold the privacy and security of our site visitors and members in the highest regard. The following information explains what kind of information is gathered on our site and how that information is used.

  
Please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/en/contact/) to bring to our attention any instance where you feel these policies have been violated.

## **  
How is email information used?**

Email addresses are used by MyUS.com® to send email for the following purposes:

  1. To send an initial, one time greeting to each new member;
  2. To send notices to members with respect to receiving and forwarding their packages;
  3. To occasionally email you notifications of any announcements at MyUS.com®; and
  4. To send newsletters or special offers to members who have requested that service. We do not sell, rent, or loan our email lists to outside firms.



## **  
Uses of Registration Data**

  
All submitted information is treated as confidential by MyUS.com®'s staff, affiliates, and advisors. We never show the Registration Information gathered in the application process to anyone outside the MyUS.com® team, or our affiliated merchants from whom you purchase merchandise, without your permission except for law enforcement or postal operation purposes, in which case MyUS.com® intends to cooperate fully. We reserve the right, and you specifically grant the right, for us to refuse, delete, edit, and modify any submitted information.

## **Third Party Vendors**

  
Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to our website. You may opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting the [Google advertising opt-out page](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).  
 

## Updates

  
MyUS.com® reserves the right to change or update this Privacy Policy, or any other MyUS.com® policy or practice, at any time with reasonable notice to users of the Site. Any changes or updates will be effective immediately upon posting to the Site.
