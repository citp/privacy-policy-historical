    INTERNET & CONTEST DATA
    PRIVACY POLICY

    CONTESTS

    Tractor Supply Company Respects Your Privacy
    Tractor Supply Company (TSC) collects personal information about our users during sign up for our contests. The information we collect is used in the aggregate to give us demographic information about the entire Tractor Supply Company customer base to improve our service and help us sell the advertising that supports the site. Tractor Supply Company does not sell or give visitor information to third parties for the purpose of direct mail or spamming without visitor permission. However, the personal information you provide lets us inform you about updates to Tractor Supply Company and, if you have elected to receive such information, the products and services of Tractor Supply's vendors who we believe may be of interest to you.

    Contest Entries
    Tractor Supply Company (TSC) collects information from all contest entrants except those under the age of 13 years old. Information collected during this process is used to tell us about your system ownership and preferences for awarding contest prizes, as well as, contact information should you win. In addition, should you also choose to receive promotional mailings from Tractor Supply Company contest sponsors (vendors) your contact and system ownership information may be forwarded to them so that they may contact you with information related to their products and services.

    Tractor Supply Company does not release any e-mail addresses from users unless they have specifically and actively consented to this use during the contest entry process. Tractor Supply Company generally conducts mailings on behalf of third parties to ensure the proper use of such information. Tractor Supply Company permits a third party to use the information for limited purposes only after the agreement to comply with certain safeguards and other requirements.

    Tractor Supply Company respects your right to privacy. As part of the contest entry process, we give you the opportunity to decline to receive any mailings from other parties to whom Tractor Supply Company may provide such information.

    Surveys and User Research
    Tractor Supply Company also conducts periodic e-mail and Web-delivered surveys to gather information about our audience. We share the aggregate results of these surveys - not individual data - with our vendors to help them better understand our Internet audience and monitor the reach of their advertising on our Web site. Tractor Supply Company uses reasonable precautions to keep the personal information disclosed to us secure and to disclose such information only to third parties we believe to be responsible. However, we are not responsible for any breach of security or for any actions of any third parties that receive the information.

    If you have questions please send an e-mail to cseay@tractorsupply.com.

    INTERNET

    Tractor Supply Company does not rent, sell or exchange information about its customers. Access to customer information is limited to those employees who need it to carry out their business functions.

    When transmitting sensitive information online (such as credit card information), Tractor Supply Company uses the industry standard security measure called Secure Socket Layer, or SSL, encryption (indicated by the locked icon at the bottom of most browsers).

    Tractor Supply Company collects personally identifiable information (such as name, address, telephone number or e-mail address) from Web site visitors only when visitors voluntarily provide such information. This information may be stored and used by Tractor Supply Company to better understand its customers' needs, to improve Tractor Supply Company products and services, and to contact customers regarding offers that may be of interest to them. Tractor Supply Company also collects information from Web site visitors that is not personally identifiable such as the visitor's operating system, browser, domain name, and the specific Web pages on the Tractor Supply Company Web site that were visited. Tractor Supply Company may implore the use of a 'cookie' or similar file to gather information that is not personally identifiable.
