# My Free Article Central Privacy Policy

  1. If you are under 18 years of age, you are not permitted to use this web site. 
  2. We do not condone the use of unsolicited bulk email (or spam) and we take this issue very seriously. Our newsletter subscription system is a double-opt-in system which requires the subscribers to confirm their intent to subscribe to our newsletters by clicking a special link in our confirmation email. Please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20100621054835id_/http%3A//www.myfreearticlecentral.com/contactus.aspx) if you encounter any incidents of unsolicited email coming from My Free Article Central. 
  3. We do not share any private information about our users (such as email address, IP Address, etc) with any third parties. Your private information is safe with us. 
  4. Details of subscribers (such as subscriber name and email address) will be collected at the time of subscription. They will be used for the sole purpose of delivering newsletters to the subscribers only. 
  5. Our newsletter subscribers may opt out of receiving future articles by clicking on the link at the bottom of each newsletter. Your email address will be removed from our subscription list immediately. 
  6. Any action or dispute arising from the use of My Free Article Central will be deemed to have been undertaken and concluded in Missouri and will be interpreted, construed and enforced in all respects in accordance with the laws of Missouri and all parties thereto shall submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Missouri Courts. A visitor agrees that the applicable law to be applied shall, in all cases, be that of the city of the web site owner, namely, St Louis, Missouri. 
  7. My Free Article Central provides this web site and its contents in good faith but makes no representations or warranties of any kind with respect to this web site or its contents. My Free Article Central disclaims all such representations and warranties, whether express or implied, including, but not limited to warranties of merchantability and fitness for a particular purpose. In addition, My Free Article Central does not represent or warrant that the information accessible via this web site or the products or services sold or given free on it are accurate, complete or current. 
  8. We use [third-party advertising companies](https://www.google.com/adsense/support/bin/answer.py?answer=94149) to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](https://www.google.com/adsense/support/bin/answer.py?answer=94149). 
  9. Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.
  10. Google's use of the [DoubleClick DART cookie](http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/faq.aspx) enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.
  11. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy.](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)
  12. We reserve the right to modify our Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice.
