# Cartoon Yourself for FREE!

h1>Privacy Policy 

To Better protect your privacy and to show our firm commitment to privacy of mycartooned.com's visitor's and we have explained our online information practices. Below is information describes our continued support for the responsible management of consumer information.

## Information Collected & Use of the Information

While you are visiting our site. mycartooned.com does not collect any personally identifiable information about you like your Name, address, phone number, or email address. However, mycartooned.com does obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information such as the ISP you use to connect to the Internet, your browser type, or the type of computer operating system you use . The information collected by mycartooned.com is used for the purpose of targeting ads and measuring ad effectiveness on behalf of our advertisers.

Information is collected by Our advertiser is the sole property of the advertiser and is shared only with the advertiser. Each advertiser individually controls what is done with the information collected. We host ads and collect the information from consumers on behalf of our client. mycartooned.com does not collect, use, or have access to this information. This information are collected by the third party publisher or vendor. Users are advised to check our clients privacy policy to get clear idea about each of our clients Privacy policies.

If you voluntarily send emails about any problems or requesting information about the special offers mycartooned.com and it partners will use them to respond your queries or send information about special offers.

## Cookies

We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. Ads may contain cookies, and appear to be coming from our site, but in reality they are coming from our partners who are serving ads on our site. Our site does not use cookies. We do not have access to any information about you from cookies you might receive while visiting our site.
