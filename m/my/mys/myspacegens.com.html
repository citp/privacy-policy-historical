Visitors can have this information corrected, or removed by sending us e-mail at the above address, or by visiting us at http://www.myspacegens.com/handler.php?page=contact.

With respect to security: We have appropriate security measures in place on our server in order to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.
