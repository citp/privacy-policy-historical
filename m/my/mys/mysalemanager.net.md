# MySaleManager.NET - Software Solutions For Seasonal Kids Consignment Sales

  


When you sign up for a MySaleManager.NET (MSM) account, we will ask you for information. This information may include:   


  * first and last name
  * email address
  * billing address
  * phone number
  * name of your consignment event
  * estimated date of your first consignment event using MSM
  * your consignment event's website address
  * how you heard about MSM

In the course of using the MSM service to facilitate operating your consignment event, you will also be asked to provide various other details about your consignment event. What we do with your information:

For any information you provide to MySaleManager.NET, here is where we stand: 

  * We do not sell or give your information to any other entity or company.
  * We keep your information strictly to ourselves and only use it to provide you with the services you need to help you run your consignment event.
  * We do not exploit your information, nor will we share it with any other entity or consignment event in any way.

Your IP address, cookies and other info:

When you visit MySaleManager.NET, we are able to view your IP address. However, your IP address does not identify you as a user, but merely the general area where you live. MySaleManager.NET also uses "cookies" to help maintain your session with our servers as you navigate our website. These cookies store no personally identifiable information. 

MySaleManager.NET's E-mail Services:

As a client of MSM, we maintain strict privacy for all your data. Hence, if you upload or create an email list stored on one of our servers, and decide to purge or delete information from those lists, we do not keep that information on our servers, it is immediately and permanently removed. 

Any non-transactional e-mail sent from any MSM client via MSM e-mail services include automatic unsubscribe functionality. Simply clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of the email will remove you from the mailing list for the MSM client who sent you the e-mail message. 

Anti-Spam Policy:

At MySaleManager.NET, we oppose spam in all forms. We hold the privacy of all our users in the highest regard, as well as recipients of emails sent by our clients. We, nor our clients ever knowingly engage in sending SPAM emails. All e-mails sent from the MSM e-mail servers are only sent to recipients who have registered with one of our client consignment events and have indicated their desire to receive e-mail notifications from them. All non-transactional e-mails sent from MSM e-mail servers contain unsubscribe functionality to allow respondents to easily and promply be unsubscribed from any future mailings from an MSM client. 

Complaint Procedure: 

If you receive an unexpected e-mail from a consignment event which utilizes MSM's services, please let us know. You have the following options: 

  * Click on the unsubscribe link at the bottom of the unexpected email that you receive.
  * Contact us at support@mysalemanager.net and request that your email address be unsubscribed from future emails sent from the MySaleManager.NET service.
  * Forward the e-mail that you believe to be spam to support@mysalemanager.net. Please include in the e-mail the reason you believe the e-mail to be spam.

Full Disclosure:

MySaleManager.NET will only disclose your data, the content of your private communications, or the contact information of your recipients if required to do so by law, or as otherwise stated in this Statement, or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (1) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on MySaleManager.NET; (2) protect and defend the rights or property of MySaleManager.NET and others, including among other things to enforce our Terms of Service; (3) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of its members or the public; (4) to correct technical problems and malfunctions in how we provide our service to you and to technically process your information; (5) to take precautions against liability; (6) to respond to claims that information you have submitted to MySaleManager.NET has violated the rights or interests of a third party; or (7) as permitted by law or to provide information to law enforcement agencies. Also, certain technical processing of e-mail messages and their content that are required for: (1) sending and receiving messages; (2) conform to connecting networks' technical requirements; (3) conform to the limitations of the Service; or (4) other similar technical connecting requirements. 

The MySaleManager.NET website, or e-mail messages sent from MSM e-mail servers, may contain links to other websites. MySaleManager.NET is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. If you do not want these companies to contact you, please contact them directly. 

Policy Changes:

As with any business, we reserve the right to make changes to our privacy statement, policies and practices. Should this happen, we will place a notice of this on our homepage at least 30 days prior to these changes taking place.
