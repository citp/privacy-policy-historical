# MySpace Gens Privacy Policy

### MySpaceGens Privacy Policy

**Effective: 3-7-06**

We can be reached via e-mail at [myspacegens@gmail.com]()

When using the contact form, or signing up with a service, we collect the domain name and e-mail address of you, and store it in a database. We also collect information on what pages visitors access or visit, information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations. 

The information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded, used to improve the content of our Web page, and used to notify visitors about updates to our Web site. 

With respect to cookies: We use cookies to store visitors preferences, record session information, ensure that visitors are not repeatedly sent the same banner ads, customize Web page content based on visitors' browser type or other information that the visitor sends. 

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail at the above address, or by visiting us at <http://www.myspacegens.com/handler.php?page=contact>. 

With respect to Ad Servers: To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information. For further information, consult the privacy policies of: <http://www.google.com/adsense>, [http://www.valueclickmedia.com](http://www.valueclickmedia.com/)

Upon request we provide site visitors with access to a description of information that we maintain about them. Visitors can access this information by e-mail us at the above address. 

Visitors can have this information corrected, or removed by sending us e-mail at the above address, or by visiting us at <http://www.myspacegens.com/handler.php?page=contact>. 

With respect to security: We have appropriate security measures in place on our server in order to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site. 

If you feel that www.myspacegens.com is not following its stated information policy, you may contact or us via email at the address above.
