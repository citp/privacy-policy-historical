# My Quality Day: Privacy Policy

The privacy of blog readers is important to me. This policy explains what I do with your personal information if you visit.

I personally do not collect any information about people who read or comment on this blog, with the exception of name and email IF you request to receive the Books Leaving Footprints Newsletter.

This blog is hosted by Blogspot, a Google product. Google uses cookies.

Google ads are served on the blog. I have no control over what Google collects. Users in the EEA will be served only non-personalized ads. From time to time, there may be personal ads placed on the blog by third parties. 

The counter is hosted by StatCounter, which collects no user-identifiable information, only location to quantify the number of visitors from various countries.
