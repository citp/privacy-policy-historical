# Privacy - My Provider Guide

Your privacy is very important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and how the information is collected and used on myproviderguide.com. 

### The Information We Collect

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on myproviderguide.com. On some pages, make requests, and register to receive materials. The types of personal information collected at these pages are Name, emails, cookies. We collect cookies to improve your experience on myproviderguide.com. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or email lists. 

### Privacy of Our Email Lists

We may maintain several email lists at myproviderguide.com to keep our web site members informed about new features and events. In addition, we configure our list server software to refuse to divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone but authorized staff, including other list subscribers. 

### Our Commitment to Your Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, to maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate electronic, and managerial safeguards to secure the information we collect on myproviderguide.
