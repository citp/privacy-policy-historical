# Privacy Policy - My Pornstar Book

|  1\. Cookies  
---  
   
When you visit My Pornstar Book, we may send one or more cookies to your computer that uniquely identifies your browser session.   
PornTube.com uses both session cookies and persistent cookies. If you remove your persistent cookie, some of the site's features   
may not function properly.  
   
   
2\. Google Analytics  
   
Google Analytics is software that grabs data about our visitors (you). Its something like an advanced server log. The information   
Google Analytics collects allows us to better understand the kind of people who come here and what content theyre enjoying. This   
allows us to make better decisions about what kind of content to provide in the future.  
   
All of this activity falls within the bounds of the [ _Google Analytics Terms of Service_](https://www.google.com/analytics/terms/us.html).  
   
   
3\. Log information  
   
My Pornstar Book's servers may automatically collect and record information sent by visitors' web browsers. This information   
includes the visitor's web request and the date and time of the request, browser type and language, IP address, platform type,   
referring URL, and domain names.  
   
   
4\. Emails  
   
If you contact us, we may keep a record of that correspondence.  
   
   
5\. Your credit card and payment information  
   
My Pornstar Book itself does not process credit cards, and My Pornstar Book does not collect or store complete credit card   
information. We rely on trusted third-party payment processors, providers, and gateways to process transactions for our website. We   
do not have access to your complete credit card information.
