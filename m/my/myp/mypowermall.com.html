Privacy Policy

 

First and foremost, MY POWER MALL respects your right to privacy.
We will never sell, give or loan your name or email address
to any third party, ever, without your consent or court order.

 

Though we make every effort to preserve your privacy, we may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on our Web site.

We will take great care and caution to keep whatever information we gather safe. We realize your relationship with us is the most valuable thing we have and we will do nothing to harm it.

What security measures are taken to protect member information?

Non-authorized personnel do not have physical access to our computers or database. Employees review security guidelines on a quarterly basis, as well as any time new policies are added. The servers that we use to store personally identifiable information are kept in a locked room at a secure facility which requires fingerprint authorization for entry.

Are cookies used by MY POWER MALL?

We, and certain of our vendors, use ‘cookies' to make visiting our site and visiting MY POWER MALL merchants a better, easier experience. These cookies allow us or our vendors to recognize you when you visit our Mall. We do not share this information with others.

If you have chosen to receive a newsletter you may opt out of receiving this correspondence at any time by following the link provided in each newsletter.

How will I be notified of changes to these privacy statements?

It's simple - there will be no changes. Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. We will never compromise the trust you put in us by sharing your name & email address.

Who can I contact if I have additional questions?

If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should contact us at: admin@mypowermall.com
