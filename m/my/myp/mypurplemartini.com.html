An "Internet protocol address" or "IP Address" is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer when you use the Internet. "Cookies" are small pieces of information that a web site sends to your computer's hard drive while you are viewing a web site.

MyPurpleMartini.com may use both session Cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent Cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience on the Sites.
