# Privacy Policy

MyGourmetConnection.com is committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy for our users.

While visiting the site you could provide us with two different types of information: personal information you choose to disclose to us for contact and/or subscription purposes and anonymous website usage information collected as you browse our site.

When you contact us or subscribe to our e-mail newsletter, MyGourmet News, your personal information will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold or shared with any individual or other organization, including those who advertise with us.

The anonymous information we collect is statistical in nature and not identified or associated with you as an individual.

Should you have any questions about the policy stated above, please feel free to [contact us](http://www.mygourmetconnection.com/site-info/contact-us.php) at any time.
