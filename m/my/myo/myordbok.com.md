# Myanmar online dictionary

### Recorded information about MyOrdbok visitors

MyOrdbok gather information about all visitors to http://www.myordbok.com/ web site. This includes the time and date of the visit, the operating system and browser used, the domain from where you visited, where you were redirected from and the pages you browsed. This information is used only for the purpose of generating server usage statistics and development of MyOrdbok. We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, postcode or e-mail address, nor do we relate personal information to usage statistics however we have recorded emails which used to sent from MyOrdbok zola. Members information in MyOrdbok Database: Any Members who holding account at MyOrdbok may request their own information that might be deemed personal. Whenever information is required for a service we will explain why it is needed and how it will be used. Information will be stored for as long as you use the service, until the purpose of the information is met, or until you request to terminate your registration.

### Disclosure

MyOrdbok will not disclose any personal information to outside organisations or companies. Members information shall not display in publish unless you have shared by yourself. Furthermore, you as/if holding account the information will only be available to relevant staff members within http://www.myordbok.com/ and shall be ask direct to you if we upgrade/increase our services.

### Cookies and Temp PlayLists

MyOrdbok define your ip address by Cookies (visited or not) from your direct machine. The purpose of the information is to count how many visitor we have and how long you have been since. Our Cookies is not collected any of your personal information and your machine. http://www.myordbok.com/ sessions deleted when the browser window is closed. If remember me checkbox has checked then session cookies are remained for the track of logined or not session to enable members who are from their own machine.

### Security

MyOrdbok will continue to do its upmost to protect user privacy. Any attempted breach of security will be dealt with immediately. Hosts or users that attempt to use the music server for unwanted or harmful purposes will be permanently blocked. Unauthorised access to the internal area of MyOrdbok, or to the server itself is strictly forbidden. Please Contact me for any abuse using khensolomon@gmail.com.
