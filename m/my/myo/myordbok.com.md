# Privacy Statement | MyOrdbok

[ ](http://www.myordbok.com/)

MyOrdbok do not collect any personal information from a visitor, unless the user explicitly and intentionally provides it.

MyOrdbok do not send email accept a user access on TELL A FRIEND or FORGOT PASSWORD.

MyOrdbok logs statistic information about browsers, operating systems, IP-addresses and referring websites for security purposes and to further improve the website.

MyOrdbok does use cookies {MyOrdbokUser} for visited members, in order to make sure your SOL/SIL are available the next time you visit.

If you choose to be a part of developer, member or user, we will request certain information from you such as your full name, email address and feedbacks. None of this information is distributed to any third party, Advertising Programs or mailing list.

MyOrdbok contact form and information are available at contact.
