# Privacy Policy - Mydearvalentine.com

_[http://www.mydearvalentine.com](http://www.mydearvalentine.com/)_

(hereinafter referred to as “MDVC”) respects your privacy and committed to ensure that your privacy is protected by means of protecting your Personally Identifiable Information you share with this Website. Personally Identifiable Information is information by which a person can be identified such as name, address, telephone number, SSN, e – mail ID and date of birth.

Should MDVC ask you to provide Personally Identifiable Information when using this website, you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

MDVC may revise this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should check this page periodically to ensure that you are comfortable with any changes. This Privacy Policy was last updated on June 27th 2013.

**What MDVC Collects**

  1. MDVC may collect Personally Identifiable Information
  2. MDVC may use ‘cookies’ and ‘web beacons’ to manage your status and verify your identity
  3. MDVC may collect geophysical IP Address/browser information/other technical information for system and server administration.
  4. MDVC may collect other information relevant to surveys and offers.



**What does MDVC do with the information collected**

MDVC requires this information to understand your needs and provide you with a better service, and in particular for the following reasons:

  1. To fulfill the services you have asked MDVC to perform 
  2. For internal record keeping.
  3. To improve the products and services of MDVC .
  4. MDVC may periodically send promotional e - mail about new products, special offers or other information which MDVC think you may find interesting using the email address which you have provided. However, you may unsubscribe yourself to such promotional e – mails through the link provided in every such e – mail itself.
  5. From time to time, MDVC may also use your information to contact you for market research purposes. MDVC may contact you by email, phone, fax or mail. However, you may unsubscribe yourself from this too by following the link provided in the e – mail or replying the mail with the subject “unsubscribe”.
  6. MDVC may use the information to customize the website according to your interests



**With whom MDVC shares the information**

As a thumb rule, MDVC will not willfully disclose your Personally Identifiable Information with any third party except under following conditions –

  1. When Information is forwarded to Service Providers to fulfill the services you have asked MDVC to perform.
  2. When MDVC is legally compelled to provide the information to the Law Enforcement Authorities or government regulatory or administrative or similar agency as part of a judicial or administrative proceeding to disclose the Information.



**Security**

MDVC is committed to ensure that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure MDVC has put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information MDVC collects online. While MDVC strives to protect your personal information, it can not ensure or warrant total security of any information you transmit over the transmission medium.

**Limitation** **of Liability**

MDVC may contain links to other websites or advertisements which drive you to another website. However, once you have used these links to leave this Website, you should note that MDVC does not has any control over that other website. Therefore, MDVC cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which you provide whilst visiting such sites as such sites are not governed by this Privacy Policy. You should exercise caution and look at the Privacy Policy applicable to the website in question.
