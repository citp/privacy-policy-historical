Privacy Policy

Commerce Australia Internet Privacy Statement Commerce Australia is committed to protecting the privacy of those who access its Internet site and is committed to complying with the Privacy Act, 1988 and the associated National Privacy Principles ("the NPP's").

It is important that readers of this statement note that Commerce Australia may change its Internet privacy statement and privacy policy from time to time as part of the Commerce Australia commitment to ensuring that it complies with all of its obligations under the NPPs.

Persons who access the Commerce Australia Internet site are urged to check to see whether Commerce Australia has amended its Internet privacy statement or privacy policy by returning to this site from time to time. To assist in this process, the date of the last update to this Internet privacy statement is recorded at the top of this statement.

Information collected on the Commerce Australia Internet site and use of that information Commerce Australia will only record your email address if you send Commerce Australia a message. That address will only be used for the purpose for which you have provided it and it will not be added to a mailing list or used for any other purpose without your prior consent.

Commerce Australia collects information on its Internet site when Commerce Australia members or the public complete on-line forms. When information is provided to Commerce Australia in this way that information will only be used for a purpose made known to you; a purpose you would reasonably expect; a purpose required, authorised or permitted by any law; or a purpose otherwise authorised by you.

Persons who do not wish their personal information to be used for any particular purpose should not hesitate to advise Commerce Australia of that fact. Commerce Australia will respect those wishes. Links to the third party websites The Commerce Australia Internet site may contain links to other websites.

These websites are conducted by third parties and Commerce Australia does not control the content or the information collected on these websites. You should refer to those websites directly or to the authors of those sites to obtain details of the privacy policies relevant to those web pages.

Further information Should any person have any queries regarding the Commerce Australia use of personal information in relation to its Internet site or have any other queries regarding the Commerce Australia Privacy Policy they should not hesitate to contact Commerce Australia on info@ca.com.au
