Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

1.1 The owner of Moteefe.com (the “Platform”), Custom Materials Ltd (“We” or “Us”), are committed to our users’ privacy.

1.2 This policy (together with the Moteefe Terms and Conditions and any other documents referred to on it) sets out the basis on which any personal data we collect from you, or that you provide to us during your use of the Platform, will be processed by us. Please read the following carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it. By visiting the Platform you are accepting and consenting to the practices described in this policy. Capitalised terms used in this policy will have the meaning given in the Moteefe Terms and Conditions, unless defined otherwise.

1.3 We collect, store, use and protect your personal information in a transparent way in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998. For the purpose of the Data Protection Act 1988 (the “Act”), the data controller is Custom Materials Ltd of 36 Limburg Road, London, SW11 1HB, United Kingdom.

1.4 If you do not agree with our Privacy Policy, then you must not use the Platform.

1.5 This policy was most recently updated on 3rd November, 2016.

2. Collection and use of information

2.1 We may collect the following data about you:

2.1.1 information about you that you give us when you use the Platform including by filling in forms on the Platform or by corresponding with us by phone, e-mail or by using any messaging or weblog functionality provided through the Platform, or otherwise. It includes your username and contact details including your address, e-mail address and phone number that you provide to us. Our payment services provider may collect, process and store your payment details. The privacy policies of our payment services provider(s) (please see here, here, here and here) applies in relation to the collection and processing of such data. We will not receive your payment details if you are a Purchaser but we will receive and store payment details of Designers in order to process payments as required;

2.1.2 information collected by us automatically about your visit to the Platform including but not limited to: technical information, including the Internet protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer to the Internet, your login information, browser type and version, time zone setting, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform; the full Uniform Resource Locators (URL), clickstream to, through and from the Platform (including date and time), products you viewed or searched for, page response times, download errors, length of visits to certain pages, page interaction information (such as scrolling, clicks, and mouse-overs), methods used to browse away from the page, any phone number used to call us.

2.1.3 details of transactions you carry out through the Platform, in particular orders placed between a Purchaser and a Designer for the purchase of Products.

2.2 The data you give to us may be used:

2.2.1 to meet any request you make on the Platform and deliver the services you request;

2.2.2 for the purposes of carrying out our obligations and to enable users to carry out any obligations under contractual agreements entered into using the Platform. For the avoidance of doubt, this includes the use of data for the purposes of performing Contracts entered into between a Purchaser and a Designer. You acknowledge that data collected by us or provided by you as a Purchaser may be disclosed to the relevant Designer for use in accordance with this policy;

2.2.3 to contact you where it is necessary concerning the services you request;

2.2.4 to keep you informed of the Platform services and news and to notify you about changes to our services; and

2.2.5 to provide you, or permit Designers or selected third parties to provide you, with information about goods or services that may interest you. If you have previously made purchases from Designers using the Platform, we or the Designer in question will only contact you by electronic means (e-mail or SMS) with information about other products similar to those which you showed interest in or purchased. If you are new to the Platform, or where we permit selected third parties to use your data, we (or they) will contact you by electronic means only if you have consented to this. If you do not want us to use your data in this way, or to pass your details on to third parties for marketing purposes, please tick the relevant box situated on the form on which we collect your data (the order form).

2.3 The data collected by us may be used:

2.3.1 to administer the Platform and for internal operations, including troubleshooting, data analysis, testing, research, statistical and survey purposes;

2.3.2 to improve the Platform to ensure that content is presented in the most effective manner for you and for your computer;

2.3.3 to allow you to participate in interactive features of our service, when you choose to do so;

2.3.4 as part of our efforts to keep the Platform safe and secure;

2.3.5 to measure or understand the effectiveness of advertising we serve to you and others, and to deliver relevant advertising to you;

2.3.6 to make suggestions and recommendations to you and other users of the Platform about goods or services that may interest you or them.

6. Cookies

6.1 Cookies are text files stored, either on a temporary or persistent basis on the hard drive of your computer. Cookies are used for authenticating, session tracking and maintaining specific information about the use and users of the Platform.

6.2 The data collected by cookies is anonymous. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer's hard drive by searching for files with "cookie" in it and deleting them. In addition, if you want to stop cookies from being stored on your computer, you can edit your browser settings so that cookies are blocked. However if you block cookies you may not be able to use the full functionality of the Platform. For more information about cookies, please visit: www.allaboutcookies.org

6.3 Web beacons consist of a small string of software code that represents a graphic image request on a page or email. There may or may not be a visible graphic image associated with the web beacon and often the image is designed to blend into the background of a page or email. Web beacons can be used for many purposes - including site traffic reporting, unique visitor counts, advertising auditing and reporting, and personalization. Web beacons used by the Platform collect only anonymous data.

6.4 The Platform uses cookies to distinguish you from other users of the Platform. This helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse the Platform and also allows us to improve the Platform. By continuing to browse the Platform, you are agreeing to our use of cookies for the following purposes:

(a) to remember your login or to keep session information;

(b) to make the Platform work more effectively; and

(c) anonymous cookies are used to analyse how many visitors come to the Platform and to track how they use it.

6.6 On the Platform, we are using information about your visit to serve you relevant advertisements on other websites, including Google, across the Internet. This is achieved by placing a cookie on your browser which is then called upon by a third-party website which then serves you our ad. Never in this process is person data (i.e. data allowing you to be personally identified) passed between us and the website serving the ad.

6.7 You can opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting the Ads Preferences Manager. Websites may include links as well as banner advertisements of/to third parties which use information about your visits to this and other websites. The Platform does not control the dissemination of their cookies. You should check relevant third party websites for more information about this.
