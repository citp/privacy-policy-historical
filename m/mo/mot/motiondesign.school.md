# Privacy Policy

Updated: November 12, 2019

## General Information

Website https://motiondesign.school (“Website”) is a property of Motion Design School Company, a Delaware Corporation (“Company”, "we", "us", "our"). We created this privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) to explain how we treat, collect, use, maintain and disclose your personal information collected and received from your visiting our Website and from your use of our services as defined in our Terms of Use (“Terms”) provided through our Website (“Services”), or third parties websites, services, platforms and products we use such as Facebook, Behance, Vimeo, Youtube, Google, Shopify, Shopify Payments, Instagram, etc. Terms not defined herein have the meanings set forth in the Terms. Third parties websites`, services`, products`, platforms` collection, use, and disclosure of your personal data will be subject to their own privacy policies, and not this Privacy Policy.

Please read this Privacy Policy as well as our Terms and Cookies Policy carefully.

If you are a citizen of China and processing of your personal data must conform to China privacy requirements please read carefully and give your consent to this Privacy Statement.

In this Privacy Policy, your information is sometimes called “personal data” or “personal information”. We also sometimes collectively refer to handling, collecting, protecting or storing your personal information as “processing” such personal information.

This Privacy Policy applies to any personal data provided to us, and any personal data created in connection with our Services. You should provide personal data only of yourself.

If required by applicable law, we will seek your explicit consent to process you personal information collected by us or volunteered by you. Kindly note that any consent will be entirely voluntary. However, if you do not grant the requested consent to the processing of your personal data, the use of our Services may not be possible.

You may revoke your consent with this Policy or to process your personal data any time by email to our Data Protection Officer at privacy@motiondesign.school

Motion Design School Company, a Delaware Corporation, is the data controller of your personal data. Please see the “Contact Information” section below for additional details.

Some our Services may have their own privacy policies that differ from this one and/or contain additional information as required under local law. Please refer to the Privacy Statements relating to the relevant Services in order to understand how they process your data.

## Agreement With this Policy and Use of Services

Your agreement with this Privacy Policy means that you consent to use our Services including but not limited to the Website and/or submit any information to us, understanding that we may collect, use, disclose, record, analyse and retain such information in accordance with this Privacy Policy, and as permitted or required by law.

By using our Services and providing personal information to us, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy, and subject to your explicit consent which we may separately seek from you as may be required by applicable law, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy (including international transfers of your personal data as set out in this Privacy Policy to countries outside where you are located).

Please be acknowledged that If you do not agree with these Privacy Policy, then do not provide any information to us and do not use our Services.

## Purpose of Processing Your Personal Data

We use the collected data to customize our Services for users, to communicate with users, to customize content, to show ads on other websites to visitors, and to improve our Services by analyzing how visitors navigate.

We may also share such information with service vendors or contractors in order to provide a requested service or transaction or in order to analyze the visitor behavior while using our Services.

When you use our Services, we may collect personally identifiable information about you either directly from you, or from social medias or other third-party sites including through cookies and analytics tools.

We may gather the following information about you:

  * Name, address, telephone number, IP and email address, date of registration and last log-in to our courses and masterclasses;
  * Log-in credentials, if you log-in through registered user account on our Website. If you use third party sign-in service (e.g. Teachable) that authenticates your identity and connects your social media login information to access our Services then we do not process your log-in credentials;
  * Information about your purchases or other transactions with us, such as your billing address. Please note, however, we do not collect, store, or transmit financial information. Instead, we use a third-party payment processors Shopify Payments and Paymentwall to process your payments, so you provide your personal information directly to such third party processors according to their own privacy policies, please read them carefully before payment;
  * Information about your customer service and maintenance interactions with us;
  * If you are a formal student (as eligibility for discount you want to apply for);
  * Photo or a scan of your ID (student ID) including data shown on the ID and your photo to get a confirmation if you are eligible for a student discount you apply for or after confirmation of your personality as fraud protection measure. We do not retain this data and delete immediately after we make sure of it;
  * Information relating to your progress in a course or a masterclass mastering, for example your tasks, practical works, quiz/exam scores, grades, project evaluations, user-generated content, educational and work experience, job placement, your goals, your progress in and other information;
  * Your social-media profile public information that we may see when you use live chat to contact us (e.g. Intercom) or interact with us in social medias;
  * Information needed in order to comply with any procedures, laws, and regulations which apply to us where it is necessary for our legitimate interests or the legitimate interests of others;
  * Information from third parties about you in connection with any referral programs we may offer;
  * Any other information you voluntarily choose to directly provide us for instance, when you use any “Contact Us” features of our Services, as part of the registration process, register a user account, and/or create and update your user profile.

### Information We Collect Through Automated Means

Cookies and Analytics Information. Please read our Cookies Policy for detailed information. Briefly, when you use our Services, we and our service providers (which are third party companies that work on our behalf) may use cookies (text files placed on your devices), and similar technologies to track, collect and aggregate certain information including, among other things, which pages of our Website were visited, the order in which they were visited, when they were visited, the URLs from which you linked to our Website, and which hyperlinks were “clicked.” Collecting such information may involve logging the IP address, operating system and browser software used by each user of the Website. Through such information, we may be able to determine from an IP address a user’s Internet Service Provider and the general geographic location (city/state) of his or her point of connectivity.

We also use or may use these technologies to help us determine and identify repeat visitors, the type of content and sites to which a user of our Website links, the length of time each user spends at any particular area of our Website, and the specific functionalities that users choose to use.

We use third-party web analytics services (such as those of Google Analytics or Mixpanel) on our Website to collect and analyze usage information through cookies and similar tools; engage in auditing, research, or reporting; assist with fraud prevention; and provide certain features to you. We may also use third-party services, as a medium for communications, either through email, or through messages within our Services. The use of these any such medium may be subject to the terms and conditions and privacy policy for those service providers and you should carefully review those terms and conditions. If you would like to opt out of having this information collected by or submitted to such third parties or have questions about other third-party service provider’s collection of your information for this purpose, please contact us. For more information about our use of cookies and similar technologies, including how you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent Google from using your information for analytics, or otherwise control or disable cookies, please see our Cookies Policy.

Online Advertising. We may allow third party companies, including advertising companies (such as Facebook, Google), to place cookies on our website. These cookies enable such companies to track your activity across various sites where they display ads and record your activities so they can show ads that they consider relevant to you as you browse the Internet. These Cookies store information about the content you are browsing together with an identifier linked to your device or IP address.

These Cookies also allow us and third parties to know whether you've seen an ad or a type of ad, and how long it has been since you've last seen it. This information is used for frequency capping purposes, to help tailor the ads you see, and to measure the effectiveness of ads. For more information about online advertising and how you can control or disable these cookies, please see our Cookies Policy.

Information We Collect from Social Media and Other Content Platforms. If you log-in to, or connect to, a third-party network (e.g., through a social network), or log-in to our Services, through a social media site (e.g., Facebook) or third-parties platforms (e.g. Teachable), you may allow us to have access to and store certain information from your profile. This includes your name, profile picture, depending on your settings on such services. If you do not wish to have this information shared, do not use a relevant sign-in service to access the Services. For a description of how social networking or education platform sites handle your information, please refer to their privacy policies and terms of use, which may permit you to modify your privacy settings.

## Retention and Deletion of Your Information

We keep your personal information for no longer than necessary for the purposes for which it is processed. The length of time for which we retain information depends on the purposes for which we collected and use it and/or as required to comply with applicable laws.

For example, all your information that is being collected through third-party web analytics services (such as Google Analytics) is retained during the term stipulated by privacy policy of such third-party services.

We keep contact information (such as mailing list information) until a user unsubscribes or requests that we delete that information. If you choose to unsubscribe from a mailing list, we may keep certain limited information about you so that we may honor your request.

You can send us the request to delete your personal data any time to the privacy@motiondesign.school
