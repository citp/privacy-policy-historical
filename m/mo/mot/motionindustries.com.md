# The policies below are applicable to Motion Industries

Privacy Policy

The policies below are applicable to MotionMRO.com, produced and maintained by Motion Industries, Inc. ("MI"). 

We at Motion Industries believe very strongly in personal privacy. For that reason, it is our policy not to sell or share any of our visitors' personal information. Additionally, it is our policy to employ certain security measures protecting, to the extent we are able, the integrity of the data transmitting personal and financial information from our visitors to us.

MI collects personally identifying information only during electronic catalog registration. This information is specifically and knowingly provided by users for the express purpose of purchasing merchandise from MI. MI may use such information for its internal purposes, including but not limited to special promotions, incentives, and targeted shopping.

MI reserves the right to change this policy at any time by notifying users, either through a notice posted on MotionMRO.com or by email, of the existence of a new privacy policy or any updates.

**ATTENTION CHILDREN:** IF YOU ARE UNDER THE AGE OF 18, YOU MUST GET PERMISSION FROM YOUR PARENT OR GUARDIAN BEFORE YOU DO ANY OF THE FOLLOWING ON OR THROUGH THE CATALOG: 

  * SEND ANY INFORMATION ABOUT YOURSELF OR OTHERS
  * BUY ANYTHING



IF YOU GET PERMISSION FROM YOUR PARENT OR GUARDIAN TO SUBMIT YOUR NAME TO THE CATALOG, YOU SHOULD NEVER USE YOUR FULL NAME. JUST SUBMIT YOUR FIRST NAME OR INITIALS.
