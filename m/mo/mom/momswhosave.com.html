Privacy

Newsletter

If you wish to subscribe to our newsletter, we will ask for contact information such as name and email address. No personal information will be sold or rented to any third party, or used for any other purpose. You may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time, and unsubscribe information will be included with every newsletter.

 

Links
This website contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy policies of other sites. We encourage you to be aware of the privacy policies of the websites that you visit, especially if you give your personally identifiable information.

 

Children’s Privacy
We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. We will delete the information from our records if we learn that we have information from a child under 18.

Questions?

If you have any questions, please use the “contact us” link found on every page of this site.
