# Privacy Policy | MobileDekho.com

## MobileDekho Privacy & Safeguards Notice

At MobileDekho, we value and preserve the privacy of our elite customers and are seriously committed to safeguard the information we receive and maintain about you. We abide by law and thus, in any case, do not sell information about any customer. We righteously share it as per the law and thus we need no notification from your side, whatsoever, that not to share any information about you, because we have already chosen to limit this.This underlying notice will help you understand what kind of information we gather, how we put it to use and the ways we maintain your privacy and secure all your personal information we have.

### INFORMATION WE GATHER

We gather the information about you from varied sources. The amount and nature of information collected may differ from transaction to transaction. For instance, we get information about you from you only- Information you provide to us on applications, other forms, and by other means, such as your name, e-mail ID, and contact numbers.

### SHARING INFORMATION WITH AFFILIATED COMPANIES

We may decipher all of the information we collect about you to our affiliated companies. These affiliated companies can be promotional activities, financial service providers such as leasing companies, insurance companies and payment processing companies and non-financial ones also such as automotive repair facilities and data processing companies. This sharing permits us to give you better service, make available a range of products to you and also operate our business more effectively. We do not share information with non-affiliated third parties. Wherever applicable, we reserve limits and restrictions set by law as to sharing certain kinds of information with our affiliates.

Google acts as a third party vendor and uses cookies to serve ads on your site.  
Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet.  
Consequently, users, if they wish, may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the[ Google ad and content network privacy policy.](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html "Privacy Policy")
