# MOBANGO - Share your mobile media with friends: free mp3 ringtones, mobile video, photos, software for your cell phone

Dear Registered User,  
Your privacy is very important to us. That is why privacy protection will ALWAYS be a high priority at Mobango. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all members. We will collect personal data as provided to us during the registration process. The personal information you provide to us will be held on Mobango servers. The Information we collect: email, first name, last name, zip, country, date of birth, gender, Cell Phone number, wireless carrier, Cell Phone brand and model. Home Town, Town of Residence, marital Status, Sexual Orientation, ethnicity, education.

Mobango will never share, rent or sell your information to third parties without your permission. Although unlikely, Mobango may be required by law enforcement or judicial authorities to provide Personally Identifiable Information to the appropriate governmental authorities.   
Mobango may offer content (e.g., contests, sweepstakes, or promotions) sponsored by, or co-branded with, identified third parties. By virtue of these relationships, the third party may obtain personally identifiable information voluntarily submitted to participate in the sites. This Privacy Policy may be changed at any time.   
Please refer to the section [Terms Of Services](https://web.archive.org/web/20060620155614id_/http://www.mobango.com/view/termsofservices.php) (TOS) for more detailed information about how to use Mobango.
