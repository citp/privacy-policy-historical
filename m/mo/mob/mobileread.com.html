About Us

MobileRead Networks is an online community dedicated to the exchange of ideas and information relating to all aspects of the Mobile Internet boom. Our goal is to become the leading resource for mobile geeks seeking information and advice for keeping their gadgets happy. Because when they're happy, you're happy too.

Mainly we want you to have fun! Speak your mind in our community and read stories from mobile geeks just like you.

Membership is open to all, and it is completely free and without any obligations.

MobileRead Networks is privately owned and has no affiliation with any of the commercial products mentioned on this website.

If you have more specific questions about this website, you can always contact the webmaster.

Privacy Statement

MobileRead Networks has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this website.

Information Automatically Logged
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website. The IP address is not displayed anywhere on the website, and is not disclosed to any other parties. The IP address is also used if it becomes neccessary to ban a member of the forums and to stop them from re-registering with another alias.

Cookies
This site uses cookies to store the login information of registered forum members. The option to save your login information is entirely up to yourself. When you register, you will be given the option to 'Automatically login when you return to the site'. This will store your username and password in a cookie, stored on your computer. You may clear all your cookies by clicking here.

Registration Forms
Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their name, email, etc). This contact information is only used to get in touch with the member in the event of a forum rule violation. It may also be used to prevent banned users from re-registering under a different username. The email address will only be seen by moderators and adminstrators.

External Links
This site contains links to other sites. However, we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of any such websites. We do however have moderators for each forum, who will delete/edit any links, content, etc. that they feel should not be printed in any of the forums.

Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. If you have any questions regarding these measures, please feel free to contact the forum administrator.

Choice/Opt-Out
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving periodical information from us.
If you like, you can click here and untick the "Receive Email from Administrators" option.

Contacting Us
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this website, you can always contact the administrator.
