# Privacy Policy : MobileWhack.com

## Privacy Policy

By Paul B - 05/10/2008 2:39:05 PM

We take our reader’s privacy very seriously.

The only information we retain are the emails and IP addresses from those who post comments. We never give this information out to anyone. If you do post a comment on our site, you can rest assure, that your email address will not be publicly visible to anyone that’s visits our site.

If an advertiser is interested in advertising on MobileWhack.com, we provide them with information regarding traffic and demographics only, we will never disclose individual visitors information.
