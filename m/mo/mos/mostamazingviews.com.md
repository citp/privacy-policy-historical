# Privacy Policy - Most Amazing Views

All videos, shared on our website has been shared not uploaded.  
All blogging is done by us. If there’s in accurate information we posted about in our blog about a specific story, Please don’t hesitate to contact us to fix this issue or concern. We respect others privacy rights.

Privacy Policy for mostamazingviews.com  
If you have any questions or concerns about anything, Contact us by email contact@mostamazingviews.com

At mostamazingviews.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by mostamazingviews.com and how it is used.

**Log Files:**  
Mostamazingviews.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
