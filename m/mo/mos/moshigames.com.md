# Privacy Policy | Moshi Games | Kids Games Online

Protecting children is the first priority of all activities at MoshiGames.com.

## Introduction

Privacy policies aren't very interesting, but we know they are important because we want to have the confidence and support of parents, teachers and Moshi Game players. A clear privacy policy is an important part of the Moshi Game experience.

## Who we are

MoshiGames.com is owned by Mind Candy, a company based in the United Kingdom.

## What data we collect

At this time, MoshiGames.com collects absolutely no data whatsoever. That's pretty simple isn't it :) In the future, we may require registration to play on the MoshiGames.com site. When that time comes, this privacy policy will change and be MUCH longer but we'll be sure it's still clear and easy to understand.

## Cookies

We collect non-personal information from all players by placing and accessing cookies on the computer being used to access MoshiGames.com. Cookies are used to store player preferences, but DO NOT contain ANY personal information.

## Game Play Data

When a player uses MoshiGames.com, we collect aggregated anonymous data for the purpose of identifying trends, likes and dislikes. That's it!

## Legislation

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), is a program administered by the U.S. Federal Trade Commission. MoshiGames.com endeavours to comply with COPPA, and other relevant legislation. If you would like to find out more about how to protect your child online please visit the following sites: US - [Net Smartz](https://web.archive.org/linkwarning/http://www.netsmartz.org/index.aspx) or UK - [Think U Know](https://web.archive.org/linkwarning/http://www.thinkuknow.co.uk/) or AU/NZ - [Cyber Smart](https://web.archive.org/linkwarning/http://www.cybersmart.gov.au/)

## Security

If any message claiming or appearing to come from MoshiGames.com is sent to you asking for personal information such as your password or user name for MoshiGames.com, please ignore the request and report it to us at [help@moshigames.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20111231213456id_/http%3A//www.moshigames.com/pages/help@moshigames.com) immediately. MoshiGames.com is not a secure web site and MoshiGames.com does not and cannot warrant or ensure the security of the information collected by and transmitted over the internet. Players agree that any data transmission you make or receive regarding Moshi Games is at your own risk.

## Information Sharing

MoshiGames.com does not collect any personal information so we are not able to share any personal information. MoshiGames.com makes every effort to prevent and discourage players from sharing any sort of personally identifiable information with each other via email, feedback forms or in any other way. As there is no way for players to communicate with other players on the MoshiGames.com site, we are not responsible for any potential personal information sharing that occurs outside the MoshiGames.com site.
