# Movie Gallery - Privacy Policy

MovieGallery.com, Inc. ("MovieGallery.com") has created this policy to demonstrate our commitment to protecting our visitors' privacy. In accordance with this policy, we will take reasonable steps to protect the privacy of our visitors' personal information. Please read the following to more fully understand how we gather and utilize the personal information that you provide. 

### How we obtain information from you, our Visitor. 

We collect information from you to help distinguish you from our other visitors. Your IP address is used to identify you and your shopping cart. We also use the IP address and 'cookies' to gather broad demographic information. 'Cookies' are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. You may choose to disable cookies on your computer and you will still be able to shop at MovieGallery.com. We also collect personal information about you through our order form or other requests by you for products, services or information. Information about our company, or about upcoming products or services available on our web site, may be sent to you based on the information provided by you. However, a visitor may choose to opt-out of receiving any future communication from us by following the procedure given below.

We request that all visitors be 18 years of age or older before submitting personal information. 

### Security Measures. 

Moviegallery.com uses a variety of security measures to maintain the security of your personal information. We exercise great care in providing secure transmission of the visitor's information to our servers utilizing encryption software. Only those employees who need access to your information in order to process your order or request are allowed access to your information. In addition, MovieGallery.com adheres to the requirements of the Video Privacy Protection Act of 1988. Under no circumstances will Movie Gallery.com disclose title-specific information to anyone except as required by law. 

MovieGallery.com is not responsible for links to other web sites. 

This web site contains links to other web sites. MovieGallery.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. Furthermore, such web sites may collect personal information about you, and the information they collect is not covered by our Privacy Policy. 

### Your Acceptance of this Privacy Policy. 

By using this web site, you hereby acknowledge that you have read and understand MovieGallery.com's Privacy Policy and consent to the terms thereof. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our web site. We reserve the right to change this policy from time to time. Your use of our web site after the changes will mean that you have accepted those changes. 

### Contacting MovieGallery.com, Inc. 

Our visitors may contact the company by (a) [emailing us](https://web.archive.org/company/contact_us.aspx) (b) by United States Mail addressed to: MovieGallery.com, Inc., Attn: Visitor Service Department, 900 West Main Street, Dothan, Alabama 36301.
