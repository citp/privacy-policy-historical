## The Information MovieNuke Collects

MovieNuke does **not** knowingly collect or maintain personally identifiable information on the Website from any person.   
\- Cookies: MovieNuke may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance and personalize your experience of the Website, You can reset your web browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some functionality of our Website may be impaired if the ability to accept cookies is disabled.   
\- Log File: When you use the MovieNuke WebSite, our servers record certain information that your web browser sends such as your web request, IP address, browser type, browser language, referring, landing, exit pages and URLs, operating system type, number of clicks, domain names, pages viewed and the order of those pages, the amount of time spent on particular pages, the date and time of your request.
