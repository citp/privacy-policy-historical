# Privacy | Morbark

Like all organizations that use your personal information, Morbark, LLC, is required to provide information about the purposes for which we use your data. We are committed to conducting our business in a manner that actively supports and ensures your personal privacy. Our Privacy Policy is formulated to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Collecting personal information

We collect information you choose to submit during your interaction with us. This may include:  
Name  
Address  
Telephone numbers  
Email addresses  
Credit card information  
Any other information you provide when completing contract forms, or when you contact us via our website or by other means.  
We collect information about you when you place an order for products or services.  
We also collect information when you voluntarily complete customer surveys or provide feedback to us.  
Our web server logs may collect additional information, such as the domain names of visitors to our websites, in order to measure the use of our websites and to improve content.  
For your convenience and to improve your user experience, we use common Internet technologies such as cookies on our websites.  
We may obtain additional information about you, such as equipment sale transaction data from public record data compilation services.  
We do not intentionally collect information about minors.  
How will we use the information about you?

We use your personal information in order to:

Process orders you have made  
Carry out or exercise our rights and obligations arising from any orders you have placed  
Provide you a requested product or service or information about our products and services  
Provide after-sale support  
Additionally, we may:

Use ownership information to send you necessary information, such as warranty information, product improvement programs, safety bulletins, and recall notices  
Contact you about promotions or other products and services we think may be of interest to your business. You have a right to opt out of these promotions at any time (see also Marketing and Advertising below).  
Morbark, LLC, will not share your information for marketing purposes with any other company not working on behalf of Morbark. In processing your order, we may need to send your details to delivery companies, suppliers, and other third parties. A list of these persons and/or companies can be provided upon request.

We may release your personal information when we believe that such release is reasonably necessary to enforce or apply our Limited Warranty or to protect the rights, property, and safety of others and ourselves.

Keeping your personal information secure

Morbark, LLC, has appropriate security measures in place to prevent personal information from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. We limit access to your personal information to those who have a genuine business need to know it. Those processing your information will do so only in an authorized manner and are subject to a duty of confidentiality.

Access to your information and correction

You have a right to request a copy of the data we hold that is related to you and to request corrections be made to it.

How long your personal information will be kept?

We will only retain your personal information as long as necessary to adequately serve you or as long as we are required by law to do so. You have the right to request us to delete your data at any time.

Reasons we can collect and use your personal information

We need some data to process your orders and provide you with services. We are also required to process your data to fulfill our contractual obligations and meet legal obligations.

Transfers to third countries and international organizations

We transfer personal data to third countries or international organizations using safeguards to enable these organizations to provide consumer services in connection with a service or product you have purchased from us. A list of the third countries and international organizations is available on request. Your information will only be used in accordance with this Privacy Notice and only as far as necessary for providing products and services. We may also share information with third-party data processors acting on our behalf to provide you with services in connection with your account with us.

Marketing and Advertising

We would like to send you information about products and services of ours that may be of interest to you. If you have consented to receive marketing, you may opt out at any time. If you have not consented, but have not opted out of marketing, we may still contact you to provide you with information about products and services that may be of interest to you. We have a legitimate commercial interest in processing your data to provide you with customized marketing and advertising messages to inform you of products and services that are directly relevant to your needs. You have a right at any time to tell us to stop contacting you for marketing purposes. To exercise this right, please contact us at: customercare@morbark.com

Cookies

The first time you visit our website, you will be asked if you wish to accept the cookie file. Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information. This information is used to track visitor use of our website and compile statistical reports on website activity in aggregate. This information also may be used to offer you targeted ads tailored to your interests on digital platforms that read the cookie data (e.g., Google, Facebook). If you do not accept the cookie file, it will not be placed on your computer. As such, your computer will not be recognized as a returning computer, and you will see the message asking if you wish to accept the cookie file when you next return to the site.

Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, European Union residents have a number of important rights. In summary, those include rights to:

Fair processing of information and transparency over how we use your personal information  
Access to your personal information and certain other supplementary information that this Privacy Notice is already designed to address  
Require us to correct any mistakes in your information which we hold  
Require the erasure of personal information concerning you in certain situations  
Request the transfer of your personal data to you or to a third party  
Object at any time to processing personal information concerning you for direct marketing  
Object in certain other situations to our continued processing of your personal information  
Otherwise restrict our processing of your personal information in certain circumstances
