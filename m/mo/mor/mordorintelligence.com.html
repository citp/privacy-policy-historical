We respect the privacy of everyone who visits this website. As a result we would like to inform you regarding the way we would use your personal data. We recommend you to read this privacy policy so that you understand our approach towards the use of your personal data

What does this privacy policy cover?

This privacy policy is to inform you regarding the use of your personal information that is collected during your visit to our website.

What information do we collect?

  • Details of your visits to our website and the resources that you access, including, but not limited to, traffic data, location data, weblogs, IP address, operating system, access times, what browser you are using and other communication data.

  • Information provided to us when you communicate with us for any reason.

Purposes of collecting your data

One of the purposes of our website is to inform you of who we are and what we do. We would therefore use your personal information in order to:

Furthermore, we may use your data, or permit selected third parties to use your data, so that you can be provided with information about unrelated products and services that we consider may be of interest to you.
