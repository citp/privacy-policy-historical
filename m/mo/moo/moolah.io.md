# Moolah: Next generation payments, today.

#### Privacy Policy

* * *

##### Information Collection

We may collect personal and non-personal information for the purpose of providing you with a service. 

* * *

##### Cookies

We use cookies like most sites use cookies, in our case, it is for the purposes of logging you in. 

* * *

##### Information Use

We will only use your personally identifying information for the purpose of providing you with a service (such as support, merchant services, or our consumer wallet), or performing necessary KYC and AML checks on our customers. 

* * *

##### Data Protection

All data is transmitted over SSL. All stored identifying information is encrypted. We have a number of measures in place to protect your information. 

* * *

##### Information Sharing

Your information is your information, it's that simple. We will not share, sell or rent your personal information to any third party. Generic visitor information may be shared for traffic analysis purposes. We may use a third party to send our newsletters, so your information may be shared in a limited fashion to enable us to do that. 

* * *

##### Policy Changes

Policy changes will be announced on the front page of the site and referenced here. 

* * *

##### Acceptance

Your usage of our platform signifies your acceptance of this policy.
