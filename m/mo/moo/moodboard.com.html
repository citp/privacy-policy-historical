moodboard.com Privacy Policy Statement

What this Privacy Policy means

moodboard respects your privacy. We do not distribute or reveal our customers personal information.

We are committed to ensuring we only use the data you are happy to provide and will disclose all data usage within this policy.

Due to the evolving nature of the technology moodboard uses to communicate with you, we may occasionally update this policy. You have a right to information about you and a right to opt out of allowing the use of your personal data for our marketing purposes.

Any comments or questions regarding our privacy practices should be sent to privacy@moodboard.com

Website registration details

These include personal identification details and email information. This information is used to provide services for purchasing, searching, account history and moodboard marketing and is private to the account holder and moodboard administration teams.

Shopping Safely

We process your credit card details instantly using ePDQ services and only store an incomplete number with nothing other than the last 4 digits which allow you to recognise the cards you used.

Cookies

A cookie is a very small file that the web server stores in a file on your computer to help us remember more about how best to personalise the site for you. Cookies are required for the website to operate correctly. This information allows us to remember your last visit and log you on for example.

Does moodboard share my information with others?

We do not share, sell or distribute any information that we store about our customers to other companies or individuals. Should it be appropriate, we will release your information to law enforcement agencies when requested.
