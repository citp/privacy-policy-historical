Moo Free Privacy Policy

Moo Free is committed to protecting the privacy of the users of our website. This statement outlines our privacy and data protection policy.

Data Collection

On some of our web pages, you may be able to order products, enter competitions, vote in polls or otherwise express an opinion, or subscribe to one of our services such as our online newsletters, or participate in one of our online forums or communities. The types of personal information that may be collected from these pages may include but are not limited to, either your or other peoples' name, e-mail address, postal address, telephone/mobile number, gender and date of birth. We request these details in order to provide the service or product to you (e.g. post prizes, send information via email or post), to respond to any queries you may have, or for other similar reasons.

Use Of This Information

Unless legally required to do so we will not intentionally share any of the information that you may provide with any third parties in a manner which may in anyway identify you without your expressed permission. For example, if we were to carry out a survey that you were to take part in then we may publish the results of the survey which may include your responses, however, we would not identify which responses were yours or reveal that you had taken part in the survey without your permission.

We may disclose information that you have provided in response to a law enforcement agency's request or where we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the safety of any person, violations of our Terms Of Use or as otherwise legally required by law.
