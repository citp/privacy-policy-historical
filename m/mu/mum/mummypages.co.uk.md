# MummyPages® is Britain's chirpiest website for mums and mums-to-be!

We will keep your personal information secure and safe and undetake not to provide it to third parties unless it is necessary for us to do so to perform our service to you, e.g. if you enquire about a product and we need to send your details to the seller in order for them to respond. We use email addresses for functional purposes only, such as to send registration information, receipts, reminders, etc, for offers related to the nature of our website and for services which you have opted into. If we conduct market research for third parties, we only provide aggregate results to such parties and promise never to release any personal details about users or individual responses unless we are compelled to by law.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your options in regards to not having this information used by these companies, please see: <http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp>

**Cookies**

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer by websites that you visit. They are widely used in order to make websites work, or to help them work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site. We use cookies to gain a better understanding of how visitors use our websites. Cookies help us tailor the website content to your personal tastes. We keep all the information collected from cookies in a non–personally identifiable format. Cookies located on your computer do not contain your name but an IP address.

Please ensure that your computer settings reflect whether you are happy to accept cookies or not. It is possible to adjust your settings on some browsers so they warn you before accepting cookies, or you can simply set it to refuse them, although you may not have access to all the features of this website if you do so. See your browser 'help' button to see how you can do this. You do not need to have cookies on to use or navigate through many parts of our websites.

The MummyPages website currently includes the use of the following first party cookies:

**PHPSESSID –** this identifies a user's session and allows them to provide relevant content through browsing experience. Also, it is required for login. Disabling it will prevent some functions from working properly, and login will not work.

**remember_me –** this remembers if a user has requested automatic login next time they visit the website. Disabling it will make automatic login impossible.

**attention-closed, digest_closed and others** \- required to remember closed dialogs, like newsletter subscription, etc. Disabling them will make dialogs appear again.

In addition, the MummyPages website uses Google Analytics (_ga), Facebook (fb) and certain third party cookies that are set by third parties we use for different services (for example, website analytics or advertising). These cookies are required to distinguish you from other users of our website, and helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse our website, while also allowing us to improve our site.
