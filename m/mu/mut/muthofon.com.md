# Privacy policy

Privacy policy 

**Muthofon.com** is responsible to keep your privacy intact and providing you a safe and comfortable experience.  
In order to browse **muthofon.com** website, personal information is not required. we do not share any information with any one, We only use information of our users for statistical purpose to understand our visitors choice and to get potential advertisers.  
we do not dissclose our users e-mail address to any third party or bulk e-mailing list, except for **muthofon.com** 's own mailing list which provides latest news and informative cell phone related articles.  
Cookies are used for maintaining user information and behaviour on the Forum. These cookies are small files, which are stored on your computer and contain information like your username and your current preferences.  
You may also receive advertising cookies from our advertisers and affiliate partners. This is a standard practice in the Internet industry. We have no control over these cookies.  
If you prefer not to receive cookies while browsing our website, you can set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies and refuse the cookie when your browser alerts you to its presence. You can also refuse all cookies by turning them off in your browser.  
 **Muthofon.com **will amend this policy from time to time. It is your responsibility to check this page frequently if you wish to be made aware of such changes.  
If you have any question or concerns regarding our collection, use or disclosure of your personal information, we will appreciate your e-mail. Please email us at ** info@muthofon.com**
