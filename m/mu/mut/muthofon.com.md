# Privacy policy

**MUTHOFON.com** is responsible to keep your privacy intact and providing you a safe and comfortable experience.

  
In order to browse **MUTHOFON.com** website, personal information is not required. we do not share any information with any one, We only use information of our users for statistical purpose to understand our visitors choice and to get potential advertisers.  
we do not dissclose our users e-mail address to any third party or bulk e-mailing list, except for **MUTHOFON.com** 's own mailing list which provides latest news and informative cell phone related articles.

We are using Google Analytics code on our site for understanding our visitors even better like their age, location, demographics etc with which Google & its affiliates programs (e.g., Google Display Network, the DoubleClick, Google Analytics Demographics and Interest Reporting) collects data about our visitors through cookies.

Moreover, cookies are used for maintaining user information and behaviour on the visit on site. These cookies are small files, which are stored on your computer and contain information like your username and your current preferences (not including any of your personal information like name, home address or phone numbers).

  
You may also receive advertising cookies from our advertisers and affiliate partners. This is a standard practice in the Internet industry. We have no control over these cookies.

_If you prefer not to receive cookies while browsing our website, you can set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies and refuse the cookie when your browser alerts you to its presence.
