# MuziNet Privacy Policy

  
MuziNet is deeply committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users, and more specifically, its own members. Below is information outlining our continued support for the responsible management of user information. 

During the process of delivering content to you, MuziNet does not collect any personally identifiable information about you such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address. However, MuziNet does obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information such as the Internet Service Provider you use to connect to the Internet, your browser type, etc. The information collected by MuziNet is used for the purpose of analyzing traffic pattern and readership "hotspot", so that MuziNet can deliver better content that suits your need. 

MuziNet, as well as most sites on the Internet, use "cookies" to enhance your experience as a pre-joined member. What are cookies and why do we use them? A cookie is a unique number that is provided to you the first time you log into MuziNet as a member. This unique number, which is stored in a cookie file on your computer, helps MuziNet target content to you. This cookie can help MuziNet ensure that only authorized user can get to his/her content of interest. 

If you do not want the benefit of cookies, there is a simple procedure to manually delete your cookies. Please consult your web browser's Help documentation for more information. 

This site uses the services of Engage for the serving and/or targeting of ads, promotions and other marketing messages. To do this, Engage collects anonymous data typically through the use of cookies. To learn more about Engage, including your ability to opt out of the Engage system, go to http://www.engage.com/privacy. 

If you have any additional questions about MuziNet's dedication to consumer privacy, please contact us at [support@muzi.net](mailto:support@muzi.net)

MuziNet use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email or telephone number) about your visits to this and other sites in order to provide advertisements of interest to you. It is also through these advertisers that MuziNet can continue to serve you quality content and services. To know more about the information gathering practices and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/) or [contact us](mailto:support@muzi.net) for policies from these companies.
