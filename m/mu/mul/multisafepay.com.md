# Privacy Statement | MultiSafepay

MultiSafepay has produced this privacy statement to demonstrate how important your privacy is to us. The following notes explain how we handle personal information within the context of the website. 

All data collected by MultiSafepay will be kept strictly confidential and will not be lent, leased, sold, or made public in any form. The information provided to MultiSafepay will be handled with the utmost care. 

The purpose of registering personal information by MultiSafepay, is to be able to provide tailored editorial and commercial information to our visitors. 

Our website uses cookies. Cookies are a standard internet technology that enables us to store certain login information and directly connect with the system of the user. Cookies cannot be used to identify individuals, only machines. Each visitor can set up his/her computer to prevent cookies from being accepted. 

Our registration forms request users to provide us with personal and business information, such as name, address, email and profession. 

Our website contains links to other websites and websites of (business) partners that do not belong to MultiSafepay. We cannot be held responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these websites. 

In exceptional cases MultiSafepay shares information with carefully selected partners to help meet your requirements more closely. You will be informed at all times. 

Some parts of our website provide our visitors with communities and forums. Any information published on these sites will appear in the public domain. Think carefully about the type of information you decide to publish. 

MultiSafepay uses security techniques to prevent the loss, mutilation or any other degradation of information. 

On request, MultiSafepay will remove a registered visitor (and his or her personal information) from the database. Visitors to the website can also adjust and change their data online.
