Returns and Other Policies

All of our products have a MONEY BACK guarantee.  If you are not completely satisfied, we will issue a complete refund.

  • Return and Refund Policy
    • You may return any item for a complete refund - including shipping and handling.  Before you return an item, e-mail us at returns@multiplication.com.  We will issue a return form.  When you return an item, follow the instructions on the form, and most importantly, please indicate the reason for the return.  If you paid for your order by credit card, a credit will be issued and will appear on your billing statement.
