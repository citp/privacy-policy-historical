# Privacy Policy

musicdirect has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for our website:We firmly believe in respecting the privacy and security of our customers. We do not resell or otherwise provide your email address to anyone, unless you ask us to or where we are required by law (such as a court order or other statutory obligation).We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. This site uses cookies to help identify you and your shopping cart, and to identify your session so you can use the e-commerce features of this site.Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their name and email address), so that we may provide them with services or information that they have requested. At the customer's request, we may use customer contact information from the registration form to send the user information about our company. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Unique identifiers (such as usernames and/or customer support numbers) are collected to verify the user's identity and for use as account numbers in our record system.This site may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Our site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect visitor's contact information (like their address and billing information. Contact information from the order form is used to send orders to our customers. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below..

### Security.

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Customer information is not stored in a location available through our web site. This site provides visitors with the option to use secured, encrypted communications between the web site and their browser via a secure sockets layer (SSL) connection. Access to customer information internally is strictly limited to authorized personnel. We do not resell or redistribute registration information collected through our web site. Choice/Opt-Out.

This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service..
