In order to operate the Site and to provide you with information about products or services that may be of interest to you, we may collect and store your personal that would be used to contact you directly such as your contact name, email address, and phone number. Consequently, the operators of these other Websites may collect different kinds of information about you, and may use and disclose that information in different ways than we would if it were collected on the Site. We encourage you to review their privacy policies and remind you that we will not be responsible for their actions.

Cookies
Our website, like many websites, uses cookies to keep track of your purchases and other activity on our website and enhance your experience on our website. We also use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests, to save your password, and if you choose, to save other personal information so you do not have to re-enter it each time you visit our website. If you wish to disable these cookies, the "help" portion of the toolbar on most browsers will tell you how.
