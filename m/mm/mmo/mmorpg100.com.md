# MMORPG ONLINE 100 chart

  
By publishing this page MMORPG100 wants to give full insight in its privacy policy. MMORPG100 respects privacy, and will do all in its power to protect your online privacy. 

1\. mmorpg100.com is committed to protecting your privacy online. MMORPG 100 does not collect and store any personal information on its servers. 

2\. Clicks on incoming and outcoming hits are stored per game, for the normal operation of our portal. We need to log your IP with a click to to avoid tempering with the ranking. Your IP address is also stored to gather broad demographic information and to track your general visiting paterns (how many pages you view while at one website, downloads, etc). 

3\. We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit mmorpg100.com. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. 

4\. You can optout advertisement cookies by visiting [networkadvertising.org](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp) Note: this service is creating cookies itself too, its adviced to read their TOS first to fully understand the risks. 

5\. In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser.
