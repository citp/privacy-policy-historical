 Privacy Policy

By publishing this page MMORPG100 wants to give full insight in its privacy policy. MMORPG100 respects privacy, and will do all in its power to protect your online privacy.

1. Your privacy is safe
mmorpg100.com is committed to protecting your privacy online. MMORPG 100 does not collect and store any personal information on its servers. Normal log files are disabled inside our webserver configuration for speed & privacy reasons. The server might create a crash log file only in the event of a server crash (EMERG log level) Such a crash log usually only contain information about the server and does not contain specific user information. Mmorpg is not using cookies(*) to track or store the behaviour of our visitors.

2. Entry tracking
Clicks on incoming and outcoming hits are stored per game inside our database using 2 digital numbers per entry. This is done for the normal operation of the ranking inside our portal. We need to log your IP with a click to to avoid tempering with the ranking. Your IP address is not stored to gather broad demographic information and not used to track your general visiting paterns (how many pages you view while at one website, downloads, etc). We have no systems in place to collect or process such information.

3. Third Parties
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit mmorpg100.com. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. The scripts of third parties are not located on our server. Please visit cpmstar.com or google.com/adsense to learn more about their privacy policy.

* what are cookies?
Cookies are digital text files that can be created by any remote connection that you make in your browser. A cookie can only store text or (binairy) numbers or a combinations of both. Because cookies are just text files, created and controlled by your browser, it's a harmless system for your computer health. However you need to be aware that websites, mobile apps and advertisers are able to write and read certain 'invisible' information on the background while you think you are just browsing some online or mobile App content. Our website is NOT using performance cookies and is NOT tracking your behaviour. However we are using third-party advertisers that might use cookies - please read the third party section of our privacy policy for more information about our third parties.
