# Cookies and Privacy - Mitsubishi Motors Philippines Corporation

Mitsubishi Motors Philippines Corporation recognizes the importance of personal and private information which you provide via the MMPC website. MMPC makes certain that the information you provide are collected, transmitted and stored securely.

The only information MMPC collects during normal website usage is the name of your internet service provider, the website that referred you to us, the pages you request, and the date and time of those request. This information is used to generate statistics and to measure site activity to improve the usefulness for visitors. To track this information, you may be issued a “cookie”, which helps track your “session” while online.

During normal website usage, MMPC does not collect or store any personally identifiable information such as name, mailing address, email address, phone number, or social security number.
