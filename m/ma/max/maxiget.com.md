### Privacy Policy

Maxiget Download Manager / Maxiget Software Manager, Maxiget Ltd's products, (hereinafter referred to as "Maxiget Manager", both together and separately) faithfully treats your personal info and privacy. Please look through the following to learn more about Maxiget Manager's Privacy Policy ("Policy"). This Policy deals with the way how Maxiget Manager receives and uses information in connection with our website and software provided on the maxiget.com website (hereinafter referred to as the as "Site").

### Collection and Use of Information

You have to be aware about the fact that Maxiget Manager does not knowingly collect any personal data that can identify a user, such as e-mail, name, gender, age, login names, telephone numbers, address and passwords or any other self-identifying information (hereinafter referred to as the "Personal Information"). Maxiget Manager shall take all reasonable measures to protect users' personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. Maxiget Manager reserves the right to use cookies on the Site. Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to a hard drive for records management. Cookies simplify the web-surfing process by saving parameters and browsing settings while you are at Maxiget Manager's Site. Cookies may be used to: analyze the process of the Site usage, improve and customize the Site's content and layout as well as for other purposes of customer service. Maxiget Manager's use of cookies is by no means aimed at gathering any Personal Information.

Maxiget Manager may automatically receive and save some non-personally identifiable information at its server, such as logs from the user's browser, including IP-address, type of browser, internet service provider (ISP), cookie, and a webpage that is being visited. Maxiget Manager collects non-personally identifiable information only for general purposes, including but not limited to tracking user movements, administering the site, analyzing trends, conducting research and providing anonymous reporting to external and internal clients. 

### Sharing and Disclosure of Information

Maxiget Manager does not sell, rent, or share any personally identifiable information about the user to the third-parties. Maxiget Manager reserves the right to disclose your Personal Information if Maxiget Manager believes such action is necessary to:

\- Stay in compliance with the law or legal process served at Maxiget Manager

\- Protect the rights or property of Maxiget Manager (including the enforcement of our agreements)

\- Act in urgent situations to protect the personal safety of users of Maxiget Manager services.

### Spyware and Adware

We oppose spyware and adware. Spyware is software installed on your computer without your consent or control. It can record and transmit your Internet activity and other personal information or change your computer's security settings. Adware is spyware that sends you unwanted pop-ups or spam. Maxiget by no means uses these unwanted and harmful technologies.

### Children's Privacy

Keeping safe the privacy of very young people is especially important for us. We operate our website in accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We never collect or keep any information at our website from those who we know are under 13. Any part of our website is not aimed at attracting anyone under 13. Visitors younger than 13 years old are not allowed to use the Maxiget Manager website and software and must LEAVE the current web site immediately.

Notwithstanding that, you agree to use the Site or the Application at your sole risk. You also agree that Maxiget Manager shall have no liability to you for content that may seem offensive, indecent, or objectionable. Maxiget Manager shall have no liability to you for any materials that may be accessible in connection with the Site or the Application that you might find offensive.

### Security

Maxiget Manager takes reasonable precaution to protect Personal Information from excessive use, data dump and unauthorized access. Although Maxiget Manager cannot guarantee that Personal Information will not be subject to unauthorized access due to customer's fault. Maxiget Manager has procedural, electronic, and physical safeguards to protect the Personal Information. Personal Information is protected by secured networks and access is limited to a few authorized employees. 

### Third Party Links and Co-Brand Relationships

In an attempt to provide increased value to our visitors, we may choose various third party web sites or services to link to, and frame within, the Sites or Services. We also participate in co-branding and other relationships to offer e-commerce and other services and features to our users. However, even if the third party is affiliated with us, we have no control over these linked sites, each of which has separate privacy and data collection practices independent of us. Data collected by our co-brand partners or third party web sites (even if offered on or through our site) may not be received by us. As such, we have no responsibility or liability for these independent policies or actions, and are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of any such web sites. These linked sites are only for your convenience and you therefore access them at your own risk.
