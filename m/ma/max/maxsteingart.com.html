These browsers show a symbol in the status bar, such as a key or a lock, when you are viewing a web site that uses SSL. To learn more about SSL, you may visit their web site at: http://www.ssl.com.

While we use SSL encryption to protect users' personal information on-line, we also protect our users' information off-line. All of our users' information, including both general and personal information, is carefully stored in our offices and the facilities of our web hosting provider. We make efforts we believe are reasonable to prevent the alteration, theft, loss or misuse of our users' information by others. However, please keep in mind that there is always some risk in transmitting information over the Internet.
