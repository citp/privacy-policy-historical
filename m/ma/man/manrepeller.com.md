# Privacy Policy | Man Repeller

Thanks for visiting! ManRepeller.com (referred to below as “the Website”) does not sell or give out your personal information, email address, or any other information to 3rd parties. The Website does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from persons under thirteen years of age. If you are under 13, please do not attempt to register for Website or send any information about yourself to us, including your name, address, telephone number, or email address. No one under age 13 may provide any personal information to or on Website.

Any paid collaborations or sponsorships are disclosed within the respective editorial. We periodically monetize outbound product links via SkimLinks, ReStyleMe, and other such services.

You are responsible for all of your activity in connection with the Services and accessing the Website. The Website is not responsible for the content of linked sites.
