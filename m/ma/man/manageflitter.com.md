# Privacy Policy | ManageFlitter

This site is owned and operated by ManageFlitter Pty Ltd. Your privacy is of the most importance to us. At ManageFlitter, we want to make your experience online satisfying and safe.

Because we gather certain types of information about our users, we feel you should fully understand our policy and the terms and conditions surrounding the capture and use of that information, This privacy statement discloses what information we gather and how we use it.

  1. **Information ManageFlitter Gather and Tracks** _ManageFlitter gathers three types of information about users._ _Information that users provide through optional, voluntary submissions. These are voluntary submissions to receive our electronic newsletters, to participate feedback forums and from participation in competitions and surveys:_ _Information ManageFlitter gathers through aggregated tracking information derived mainly by tallying page views and actions performed throughout our sites. This information allows us to better tailor our features to usersâ€˜ needs and to help us better understand the demographics of our audience._ _Information collected from third party services for which you provide ManageFlitter with API access credentials, These services include Twitter, Facebook and Google+, information collected from these services will be used to provide you with functionality visible on ManageFlitter, We may also utilize this data in to produce aggregate reports such as infographics or to produce additional value added information such as influence ranking to the public._ _ManageFlitter Gathers User Information In The Following Processes:_
  2. **Electronic newsletters policy** _We will offer a free electronic newsletter to users. ManageFlitter gathers the email addresses of users who voluntarily subscribe. Users may remove themselves from this mailing list by following the link provided in every newsletter that points users to the subscription management page._
  3. **Feedback forums** _Users of the siteâ€˜s Feedback Forums must register separately for this service in order to post messages, although they needn't register to visit the site. During registration the user is required to supply a username, password, and email address._
  4. **Twitter** _User who connect their Twitter account with ManageFlitter should be aware that this provide us with full access via Twitter's API to the details contained in your Twitter account. This includes, but is not limited to; your entire tweet stream, all of your friends and followers, your account settings and your account details. This does not include your Direct Messages or password._ _ManageFlitter will use this API access to provide you with the functionality on our website. We will collect information from this API about your social graph to provide you with unfollow tools. We will collect information from this AM about your Tweet stream to provide Tweet related tools._ _ManageFlitter may republish public information you provide in another public forum, This includes information about your Twitter account to provide inï¬uence ranking tools. We will never republish private information publicly and will always abide by Twitter's rules regarding Third Party Developers._ _Many people use our service to answer the question who unfollowed me on Twitter? You can do this without a subscription to a paid account._ _You can, at any time, revoke ManageFlitter's access to your Twitter account from your settings page on Twitter. You can also contact us and request we remove any information we have cached about you._
  5. **Google+** _We offer a service which allows you to post your public Google+ messages to Twitter. If you provide ManageFlitter with API access to your Google+ account via ManageFlitter, we will only repost public Google+ messages to your Twitter stream. Only information that is already publicly available can be accessed by ManageFlitter when you connect your Google+ account with us. We will only read and store explicitly public posts made by your Google+ account._ _ManageFlitter may publish aggregate information collected from your Google+ account in reports such as info graphics where we look for trends or interesting aggregate information. No previously private information will be public such that it is personally identifiable._ _You can, at any time, revoke ManageFlitterâ€˜s access to your Google+ account from your account settings page on Google. You can also contact us and request we remove any information we have cached about you._
  6. **Competitions** _We may offer optional voluntary competitions where we will collect explicit information which you provide. If you are currently logged into ManageFlitter we may track your entry to the competition with its connection to the ManageFlitter account._
  7. **Surveys** _ManageFlitter may occasionally conduct user surveys to better target our content to our audience. We sometimes share the aggregated demographic information in these surveys with our sponsors, advertisers and partners. We never share any of this information about speciï¬c individuals with any third party._
  8. **Children** _Consistent with the United State Federal Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998 (COPPAl, we will never knowingly request personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13 without requesting parental consent. In order to use ManageFlitter, users must have a valid TWitter account._
  9. **Usage tracking** _ManageFlitter tracks user traffic patterns throughout all of our sites. However, we do not correlate this information with data about individual users. ManageFlitter does break down overall usage statistics according to a user's domain name, browser type, and MiME type by reading this information from the browser string (information contained in every user-s browser)._ _ManageFlitter sometimes tracks and catalogs the search terms that users enter in our Search function, We use tracking information to determine which areas of our sites users like and don't like based on traffic to those areas. This helps us continue to build a better service for you._
  10. **Cookies** _We may place a text file called a "cookie" in the browser files of your computer. The cookie itself does not contain Personal information although it will enable us to relate your use of this site to information that you have speciï¬cally and knowingly provided, But the only personal information a cookie can contain is information you supply yourself. A cookie can't read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. ManageFlitter uses cookies to track user traffic patterns (as described above. Our advertising system delivers a onetime cookie to better track ad impressions and click rates._ _You can refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser. if you've set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies, you will receive the warning message with each cookie. You do need cookies in order to use most of the functionality on this site._
  11. **USE OF INFORMATION** _ManageFlitter uses any information voluntarily given by our users to enhance their experience in our network of sites, whether to provide interactive or personalized elements on the sites or to better prepare future content based on the interests or our users._ _As stated above, we use information that users voluntarily provide in order to send out electronic newsletters and to enable users to participate in polls, surveys, message boards, and forums. We send out newsletters to subscribers on a regular schedule depending on the newsletter), and occasionally send out special editions when we think subscribers might be particularly interested in something we are doing. ManageFlitter never shares newsletter mailing lists with any third parties, including advertisers, sponsors or partners._ _When we use tracking information to determine which areas of our sites users like and don't like based on trafï¬c to those areas. We track search terms entered in Search function as one of many measures of what interests our users. ManageFlitter creates aggregate reports on user demographics and traffic patterns for advertisers, sponsors and partners, This allows our advertisers to advertise more effectively, and allows our users to receive advertisements that are pertinent to their needs. Because we don't track the usage patterns of individual users, an advertiser or sponsor will never know that a specific user clicked their ad. We may also disclose information about any individual user to comply with applicable law or valid legal process or to protect the personal safety of our users or the public._
  12. **SHARING OF THE INFORMATION** _ManageFlitter uses the above-described information to tailor our content to suit your needs and help our advertisers better understand our audience's demographics, This is essential to keeping our service free, We will not share any previously private information about individual users with any third party, except to comply with applicable law or valid legal process or to protect the personal safety of our users or the public._
  13. **SECURITY** _ManageFlitter operates secure data networks protected by industry standard firewall and password protection systems. Our security and privacy policies are periodically reviewed and enhanced as necessary and only authorized individuals have access to the information provided by our customers._
  14. **OPT-OUT POLICY** _We give users options wherever necessary and practical. Such choices include:_ _Opting not to register to receive our electronic newsletters._ _Opting not to participate in certain interactive areas, which completely alleviates the need to gather any personally identifiable information from our users._ _You may contact us to have information we have cached about your from third party services removed from our system._
  15. **YOUR CONSENT** _By using this site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by ManageFlitter. if we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it._
