Manheim Auctions, Inc. ("Manheim," "we" or "us") respects the privacy of visitors to Manheim.com. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully so that you understand what personal information Manheim collects online through the Manheim.com service (the "Service") and what it does with that information. This Privacy Policy does not apply to information you may provide to Manheim offline or through other means, for example, via email, via telephone, or at a live auction or event. This Privacy Policy also does not apply to information you may provide to Manheim about your business, but only applies to personally identifiable information. If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@manheim.com.

The Information We Collect
The information Manheim collects online through Manheim.com falls into two categories: (1) information voluntarily supplied by users of Manheim.com, and (2) tracking information gathered as visitors navigate through the Manheim.com site. Third-party providers or websites featured on or linked to by Manheim.com may be gathering information, as well, through processes we don't control.

Examples of the types of information Manheim.com users may submit include, without limitation, registration information, purchase requests or feedback, and other inquiries or submissions. For example, to make use of certain features on Manheim.com, visitors need to register with Manheim and provide certain information as part of the registration process. Manheim also may ask for information from those who buy products or services or conduct other transactions through Manheim.com. The information you provide allows us to make the site more useful to you by providing information tailored to your individual needs or specific features that you may find useful and also allows us and our vendors to process transactions. Supplying this information is voluntary; however visitors to Manheim.com who don't wish to supply the information necessary to register or to use particular features will not be able to take advantage of the majority of features and content available on Manheim.com.

In addition, Manheim may use a variety of methods to track visitors as they navigate the Manheim.com site. Manheim may keep track of the pages visited by its users by placing a small entry in text file, called a cookie, on a user's hard drive. The cookie contains an ID number that allows Manheim to track the pages visited. This data is aggregated with data on the pages visited by other users to track overall visitor traffic patterns. Cookies don't reveal personal information; the only personal information a cookie can contain is information you supply yourself. Cookies can't read unrelated data off your hard drive.

The help function on most web browsers contains information on how to set your browser to notify you before accepting a cookie or to disable cookies entirely. If, however, you don't accept cookies, you may not be able to take advantage of various features on Manheim.com that are available to other visitors.

In addition, Manheim.com's web server may automatically collect limited information about your computer configuration when you request pages from the Manheim.com server, including the type of browser software you use, the operating system you're running, the resolution of your computer monitor, the website that referred you, and your IP address. Your IP address is a number that is used by computers connected to the Internet to identify your computer so that data (such as the web pages you request) can be sent to you. This information does not identify you personally.

Such aggregated information does not include any information that would identify you personally. We may use such aggregated information and disclose it to third parties as we see fit.
