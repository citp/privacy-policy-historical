4. Security of your personal data and using cookies, pixel tags, Google analytics

 Privacy and security are key considerations in the creation and delivery of Site and Services. We have assigned specific responsibilities to address privacy and security related matters. We enforce our internal policies and guidelines through an appropriate selection of activities, including proactive and reactive risk management, security engineering, training and assessments. Also, we limit access to our databases containing personal data to authorized persons having a justified need to access such information.

We use cookies to provide benefits to you. Cookies are small text files that are sent to your computer by a web server. Session cookies remember the previous activity performed by you. Persistent cookies are used to save your preferences over multiple sessions, for example personalization of settings on our websites. Cookies may be used to display targeted ads and recommendations, for example to eliminate showing the same ads to you numerous times or showing ads that we think are of interest to you based on your past actions on our website. Cookies are also used to determine the effectiveness of our promotions, advertising campaigns and to improve our Site and Services. We may use pixel tags in connection with some of our Sites and Services. However, we do not use them to identify individual users personally. Pixel tags are typically graphic images that are placed on a website and they are used to count visitors to a website and/or to access certain cookies. This information is used to improve our Services. Some 24h Technologies AB websites use third party advertising technologies to serve ads. It may collect information about your visits to Site and third party websites. This information will be used to serve ads on 24h Technologies AB and third party websites. Most browsers allow you to disable or allow the use of cookies. You may also delete the cookies on your computer if your browser so permits. If you disable cookies, you may not be able to use certain parts of Services or Sites. In addition, we may use Google analytics to catch and log all incoming traffic for statistics. If you disagree with abovementioned activities, please do not use Site, Services or provide us with your personal data.
