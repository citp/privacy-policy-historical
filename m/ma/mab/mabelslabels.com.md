# Privacy and Security

## Privacy Policy

All information collected by Mabel's Labels Inc. will be kept confidential within our organization. We will never sell, trade or release your information to anyone. We will only send you our newsletter if you ask to receive it. Questions or concerns? Please [Contact Us](https://web.archive.org/info/contact%20us).

## Credit Card Security

Your credit card payment is processed in real-time by [Moneris Solution](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moneris) and their privacy policy is available [here](http://www.moneris.com/index.php?context=/aboutus/privacy). Your credit card number, expiry date and security code are not stored by Mabel's Labels. A portion of your credit card number e.g. 5443 1200 1234 **** is kept in our records.

## Website Content Policy

Mabel's Labels voluntarily labels its pages with ICRA tags. ICRA has created a content description system which allows web masters and digital content creators to self-label their content in categories such as nudity, sex, language, violence, other potentially harmful material and chat. Learn more about how to protect your children using ICRA at [Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Internet_Content_Rating_Association) and [ICRA](http://www.icra.org/parents/).
