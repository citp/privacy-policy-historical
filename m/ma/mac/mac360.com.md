# Privacy Policy | Mac360

Mac360 (hereinafter referred to as the ‘Site’) is committed to maintaining the personal privacy of visitors to the Site. This Privacy Policy describes how we treat personal information which may be received by us when you visit the Site.

### Links To Other Sites

We may provide content, services, or links to and from other sites, and make them available to you from links located on the Site’s pages. These other sites are not subject to this Privacy Policy. Please review the privacy policy on each site to determine how that site protects your privacy.

### Information We Collect

Other than email addresses from commenters to articles, and visitor IP addresses, we do not collect or maintain personal information from visitors to the Site.

 **General Information** : When you visit the Site we do not collect or maintain personally identifiable information about you without your knowledge. Your name and email address may be collected when you leave a comment on an article. The email address is not published.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18.

 **Site Usage Information** : We automatically collect IP addresses and webpage usage information from visitors, but such data is not personally identifiable. This data helps us to evaluate which pages are visited and how pages are navigated on the Site, on an aggregate basis, including the number and frequency of visitors to each page on the Site, when they visit, and the length of their visits. Such data is not personally identifiable.

 **Surveys** : We may conduct occasional surveys and other visitor research. These are entirely voluntary and you may decline to participate.

 **Registration** : We do not require that anyone who wants to comment about articles featured on the Site or about other comments posted on the Site to register. Reading articles and leaving comments on the Site is free.

 **Comments** : All communication between readers and the Site, including comments and email to the Site’s administrators and editors, become the property of the Site. We may collect and use such information from each commenter, or from email messages provided by you to the Site, but only within direct communication, or wherever legally required.

### How We Use Information We Collect

The Site may collected information this way:

1) For the purposes for which you specifically provided the information including, for example, to send a response to information you have requested, or to publish your responses to articles.

2) We provide the Site’s advertisers with aggregate information about total visitors and usage patterns, but no other information is collected or personally identifiable, and not shared with advertisers or third parties.

### Third Parties

We do not use third party advertising networks, such as Google AdSense, and do not maintain site analytics trackers, including Google Analytics or similar user tracking mechanisms.

### Disclosing Information

We may disclose and use personally identifiable information communicated by you to the Site to resolve disputes, troubleshoot problems, enforce our Terms of Service (for example, when necessary to protect ours or a third party’s intellectual property or proprietary rights). Such communication may be made by email, postal letters, or by telephone.

### Cookies And Tracking

The Site does not use tracking “cookies.” Cookies are small text files that can tell websites and advertising what you are doing online, even though they usually don’t record your name or other personally identifiable information. These cookies are used by such companies to try to match advertisements to a user’s interests.

### Security

All IP addresses and any additional personally identifiable information we may collect about you is stored on our limited access servers. We will maintain safeguards to protect the security of these servers to ensure all information remains safe.

### Policy Modifications

When we make any material change to this Privacy Policy and you are required to read the policy for updates each time you view the Site.
