# MacRabbit - Privacy

## Privacy Statement

Space Carrot guarantees that your personal information will never be shared with third parties for commercial purposes. Space Carrot makes sure your information is sufficiently secured and respects Belgian privacy laws. 

You have the right to view, modify or remove your personal information. Please contact us if you'd like to make modifications to your information. 

Space Carrot reserves the right to track usage of the MacRabbit site and its services, software and content through means such as cookies and access logs. No personally identifiable information will be recorded.
