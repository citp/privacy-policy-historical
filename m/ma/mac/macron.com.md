# Privacy and cookie policies - Macron

### **Privacy Policy  for the processing of your personal data on the website www.macron.com (the “Website”).**

Every site user (“User”) may browse and explore the Website without registering or disclosing his/her identity or supplying personal data.

It is also possible to register and supply one’s personal data to Macron S.p.a. (“Macron”) (for example, name, address and email address may be supplied) in order to learn about the products and services offered by Macron or its promotional and business initiatives.

Macron does not collect or use the personal data of minors below the age of 18.

Personal data supplied by Users may be collected or processed by Macron for marketing purposes, subject to the express consent of the User (to present the products and services offered by Macron or the promotional and business initiatives of the latter, for example, to send the catalogue of the Macron collection).

Provision of the data is optional. However, refusing to provide the personal data may result in Macron not being able to supply news and communications about its products and the services offered or its promotional and business initiatives.

Personal data is treated by computer and on paper, preserving its integrity and confidentiality. Macron protects, controls and manages the personal data with appropriate safety measures and processes, regarding, also, the know-how acquired based on technical progress, so as to reduce to a minimum any risk of destruction, loss, unauthorized access or processing or processing not compliant with the aims of the collection.

Macron S.p.a., with its registered office in Via Brodolini 5 – 40053 Crespellano (BO), P.IVA 01675921207, is the data controller.

The data may also be made known to employees, consultants, Macron service suppliers and companies in the same group, as duly appointed and responsible persons. The personal data may be transferred abroad, also to non-EU countries, but shall not be disseminated in any way.

A complete list of the responsible persons can be requested from the data controller, by writing to the address given above or to the following email address: shopcustomercare@macron.com

In compliance with the Privacy Code (Italian Legislative decree 196/03), a User has the right to obtain confirmation of the existence or not of his/her personal data and its disclosure in an intelligible format. He/she also has the right to ask for the origin of the personal data; the aims and methods of the processing; the logic applied to the processing; the identifying details of the data controller and the staff employed by the latter to process the personal data. The User also has the right to ask for the update, correction or supplementation of his/her personal data, the cancellation, transformation into an anonymous form or blocking of the personal data processed in violation of the Privacy Code, including personal data not required for achieving the purpose for which the data was collected or processed; declaration that the operations detailed in the preceding points in this paragraph (also with reference to the content) have been brought to the knowledge of the parties to whom the data was disclosed.

In addition, the User has the right to oppose, for legitimate reasons, all or part of the processing of his/her personal data, even if pertinent to the purpose of the collection, for the sending out of advertising material or commercial communications or for any type of direct sale or market research.

The rights mentioned above may be exercised by writing to: shopcustomercare@macron.com

### **What are cookies?**

Cookies are small text files that the sites visited by the user send and record on his/her PC or on any other device, even mobile, to be sent back to the original sites on the next visit.

Cookies are used for various purposes, for instance, to remember the user’s actions and preferences (such as, for instance, login data, selected language, font size, other display settings, etc.) so that they must not re-entered when the user returns to the same site or surfs from one of its pages to another; or to perform computer authentications, to monitor sessions and store information on the activities of user who access a site, and it can also contain a unique ID code that allows keeping track of the user’s surfing within the site for statistic or advertising purposes.

Cookies can be sent by www.macron.com or also by different sites (the so-called “third party” cookies). Some operations cannot be carried out without using cookies that in some cases are technically necessary for the site to work.
