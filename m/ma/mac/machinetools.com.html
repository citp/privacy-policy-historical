All information we collect (user names, e-mail addresses, etc.) is used to process your requests for quotes and other services. This information is also used to notify you about important changes to the site and other useful information.

HOW MachineTools.com PROTECTS YOUR INFORMATION:
When you access your account information, we offer the use of a secure server. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information you enter before it is sent to us. In addition, all collected customer data is protected against unauthorized access.

Our cookies do not contain any personally identifying information and allow us to customize the site to suit your needs. We use cookies to personalize your visit and prevent you from having to re-enter information on each visit.
