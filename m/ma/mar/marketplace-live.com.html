Personally--identifiable information and its use

Innovative Learning Solutions, Inc. will not collect any personally-identifiable information about you (such as your name, address, telephone number, social security number, billing and shipping information, credit card information or e-mail address) unless you voluntarily submit it through the web site, email, telephone, fax or mail.

There are occasions when we need to collect personally-identifiable information about you (such as your name, address, telephone number, social security number). If we do so, it is done so that we were able to provide information to your game instructor (professor) about your participation in the business simulation. We keep this personally-identifiable information on our servers only temporarily and delete it once your simulation game is finished and the results are evaluated by your instructor/professor. We do not use this personally-identifiable information for any other purpose.

When you provide information via the web site or any of these other means, and request information about our products, we will enter your contact information in our database of inquiries. We may use the information to contact you with news and special offers to follow up on your request.

All credit card payments that are done through "www.marketplace-live.com" or "www19.secure-website.net/~marketp" web sites are processed in a timely manner. Once the credit card information is processed, the transaction is deleted from our electronic database. The transaction data is stored afterwards in paper form only.

We will not seek to gather additional information from third parties or enhance or merge personally-identifiable information with data obtained from third parties. We will not give or sell your personally-identifiable information to any outside organization.

Security

We keep your information confidential. Staff of the Innovative Learning Solutions, Inc. who have access to any of your personal information in connection with the Innovative Learning Solutions, Inc. are required to keep the information confidential and are not permitted to use this information for any other purpose than to carry out services on behalf of Innovative Learning Solutions, Inc.

We may disclose all the personal information we collect as permitted or required by law.

Use of cookies

We use cookies on our www.marketplace-live.com web site to measure the effectiveness of the marketing campaigns that we run through Overture.com and Google Adwords or via their affiliated networks.

Cookies are identifiers that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser to enable our systems to mark your browser when you arrive to our web site via Overture.com and Google Adwords or via their affiliated networks. The cookies that we placed on the hard drive of your computer are used to recognize your browser when you contact us via email from our www.marketplace-live.com web site. These cookies are inactivated after 30 days they were placed on the hard drive of your computer. We only use cookies to measure the performance of our marketing campaigns, we do not track any other activities.
