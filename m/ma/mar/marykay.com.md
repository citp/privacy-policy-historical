If you have concerns or questions about this statement or about Mary Kay online privacy practices, you should contact Mary Kay Inc. at [privacy@marykayinc.com](mailto:privacy@marykayinc.com).   


**Applicable Web Sites  
**This online privacy statement applies to Mary Kay Inc. and covers the U.S. corporate-operated Mary Kay Web sites ([www.marykay.com](http://www.marykay.com/) and [www.mkvelocity.com](http://www.mkvelocity.com/)). Mary Kay Independent Beauty Consultants may also operate their own Web sites, called Personal Web Sites. Each Consultant is an independent contractor having the right to operate their own Mary Kay business. In this regard, Consultant online privacy practices may vary. 

This online privacy statement does not cover or include the collection and use of personal information by Mary Kay Independent Beauty Consultants. Please consult your Independent Beauty Consultant for details.

**Information Collected and How It Is Used**

_Personal Information_  
  
  |  | · | Unless you choose to provide it, Mary Kay Inc. will not collect any personal information such as your name, address, telephone number or e-mail address.  
---|---  
· | If you choose to participate in contests and special promotions, Mary Kay Inc. may collect personal information from you for the contest or promotion. In such event, unless otherwise specifically noted, this information will only be used for that specific contest or promotion.  
· | Your information will not be sold, bartered or shared with anyone at anytime by Mary Kay Inc. in any way that is not noted at the point of collection or without your permission, unless we are required to do so by law.

_Technical & Aggregate Information _  
Mary Kay Inc. collects certain technical and browsing information from visitors to the two corporate-operated Web sites including [www.marykay.com](http://www.marykay.com/) a nd [www.mkvelocity.com ](http://www.mkvelocity.com/) (the "Sites"), such as the domain named used, pages visited and the date and time you logged on. Such information helps us make the Site more useful to visitors. Mary Kay Inc. utilizes cookies and beacons provided by a third-party service provider to collect and process this information.  Although this service provider collects and processes information coming from our sites on our behalf, Mary Kay Inc. controls how that data may and may not be used.  No personally identifiable information is collected.  Technical and browsing information from visitors also may be combined with the information of other visitors on an aggregated basis. Combined technical and browsing information may be disclosed to third parties to assist in these purposes. Technical and browsing information for individual visitors is not disclosed to third parties. 

_Cookies_  
A cookie is a small text file that can be stored on your computer's hard drive. When you view the Site, some information in the form of a "cookie" may be automatically stored on your computer. This allows us to tailor the Site to your specific interests and preferences. If you want to disable these cookies, most Internet Browsers will allow you to delete cookies from your computer hard drive, prevent them from being stored or signal a warning before a cookie is stored. You should refer to your Browser instructions or help screen to learn more about these functions.  If you do not wish to participate in the third-party collection of technical data, you can "opt-out" of by clicking on the following link: <http://optout.mk.rpts.net/cgi-bin/rpts/optout.pl>

**Security**  
Data security is important to Mary Kay Inc. and we have industry-leading technology in place to ensure any personal information you voluntarily submit is safe including secure sockets layer (SSL) technology to protect the storage and transfer of your personal information. We also use digital secure server identification from Verisign to provide you with the best protection technology on the Internet. Visitor information is stored in secured databases and is protected from unauthorized access.  


**Children's Online Privacy**  
Mary Kay Web sites are not intended for use by children and we will not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 14.

**Notification of Changes**  
This policy may be revised over time as new features are added to the applicable site. Please check for information about revisions to our online privacy policy. If, however, we are going to use visitors' personal identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection we will notify those affected via e-mail (if applicable). 

**Consent**  
Mary Kay Inc. respects the online privacy of all online visitors. By using the Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Mary Kay Inc. in a manner consistent with the guidelines stated in this policy.

**Contact Us**  
We use e-mail links on this page to allow you to contact us directly with any questions or comments you may have and try to reply promptly. We use this information to respond to your questions and may record your comments to improve our customer service. If you have any questions about the Mary Kay Inc. Online Privacy Statement, please e-mail us at [privacy@marykayinc.com](mailto:privacy@marykayinc.com).
