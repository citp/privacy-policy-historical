    Information necessary to verify your identity, such as an identification card, passport or drivers license. This also includes background information we receive about you from public records or from other entities not affiliated with Safecap.

PROTECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

Any personal information you provide us will be treated as confidential and shared only within Safecap and its affiliates and will not be disclosed to any third party except under any regulatory or legal proceedings.

The personal information that you provide in connection with registering yourself as a user of Markets.com is protected in many ways. You can access your Registration Information through a password selected by you. This password is encrypted and known only to you and shall not be revealed to anyone else.

Registration Information is safely stored on secure servers that only authorised personnel have access to via password. Safecap encrypts all personal information as it is transferred to Safecap and thus makes all necessary effort to prevent unauthorised parties from viewing any such information.
