## Confidentiality Policy

Maserati considers your privacy to be of utmost importance and takes its responsibilities regarding the security of your personal information seriously. We would like you to enjoy your visit to our web pages with your mind at ease.

In general, you may navigate our website in relative anonymity. However, we may collect or update a limited amount of personal information such as your name, address or even your interests, etc. when you register to use the services provided on this site. Any such information, unless legally required, is securely stored in a database to which only authorized Maserati personnel and agents (marketing and advertising agencies), acting on its behalf have access.

Our aim is to constantly optimize the content of our website in order to improve our online service for you.

This can only be achieved if we are aware of your expressed interests and which areas of our Internet pages you enter the most and for how long. This type of information may be used for marketing research, internal data processing such as compiling statistical information about the use of our website, and other service-related purposes such as sending brochures you request and staying in contact via email.

You are required to have a unique PIN and a valid Last Name to access information this Web site and to receive the related newsletters. If you advise us of any changes in your personal information through relevant areas in which contact data can be modified, we will make every effort to effect the relevant corrections in our database at the earliest opportunity.

You can update any information you have given to us at your convenience in My Profile. You can also use My Profile and other links if you wish to be taken off our correspondence list.

## Site Tracking

Maserati utilizes personalization technology to observe the patterns of behavior of visitors to our website. In doing this, we can provide Maseratisti with a richer and more individualized experience during future visits.

This technology may include using a "cookie", a small text file placed on your computers hard disk that identifies your browser software to a website. Cookies allow us to track when you enter, leave and re-enter our site and what route you take through our site. Our cookies do not contain any personally identifying information beyond that necessary to gain entry to the site, and only last the duration of your visit to our website.

Their use is controlled and guided by our Confidentiality Policy.
