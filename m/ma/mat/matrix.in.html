Privacy Policy
At Matrix Cellular, your privacy is our privacy. Any information Matrix Cellular gathers is for the sole purpose of meeting the needs of our customers. At no time will Matrix Cellular, LLC use, sell, or publish any information without the permission of the users of our site. This agreement is extended from random site viewers to clients to shareholders.

If the occasion arises that you should want/need to send information about yourself or Matrix Cellular, Matrix Cellular will offer a simple way to submit such information and a place to store it securely. Matrix Cellular, LLC strictly protects the security of your personal/company information and honors your choices for its intended use. We carefully protect your data from loss, misuse, unauthorized access or disclosure, alteration, or destruction. If at anytime you wish to have your information deleted from our own records, feel free to contact us.

If for some reason you believe Matrix Cellular, LLC has not adhered to these principles, please notify us by e-mail, and we will do our best to determine and correct the problem promptly. Make sure you put the words "PRIVACY POLICY" or words to that effect in the Subject: line of the e-mail message.
