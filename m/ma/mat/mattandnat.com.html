Privacy Policy

General

By accessing and using the MATT & NAT web site, you are consenting to the collection and use of your information as stated in the privacy policy.

MATT & NAT recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of personal and financial information. We have instituted reasonable policies and security measures to protect the information you provide to us. When you register online with MATT & NAT, or use our online store, you give us your consent to process and use such submitted personal data in accordance with this privacy policy.

This privacy policy applies to all protected personal information collected, maintained or used by MATT & NAT.

What information does MATT & NAT collect?

MATT & NAT collects personal data from visitors to the website who register their information or who use the online store. MATT & NAT uses cookies and tracks IP addresses using third party software, to continuously help improve our services and enhance our customers' online experiences.

Cookies are select pieces of information that a website sends to your computer's browser. Once collected, these cookies are used to understand which web pages are visited and how often, to help increase the site's usability and functionality. Most web browsers are programmed to automatically accept cookies, however you can choose to have yours disabled by accessing the "help" tab on your browser. If you do not disable your cookies, you accept that MATT & NAT will collect and store the information that these cookies contain in order to help improve the website.

MATT & NAT also collects personal information (ex. name, address, telephone number(s), credit card information, email address, etc.) from customers who submit an order or sign up to receive news updates. To serve you better, we may combine information you give us with other information which is publicly available. All of this information is protected and secure.

How does MATT & NAT use your information?

We use your information predominantly to process orders. This information may be used to contact you regarding your online order, if necessary.

In some cases we will send announcements and/or updates by email, only if we have been given direct permission by the customer to do so. Customers can agree to receive emails through providing their information on the MATT & NAT web site, to a customer service representative or by completing a customer information card. If you would like to join the database to receive these updates, email news@mattandnat.com today.

MATT & NAT does not sell, rent or disclose our client's names, addresses, email addresses or any other personal information to any 3rd party.

Notice of Changes

At any time, MATT & NAT may change the privacy policy without any prior notification. If for any reason we decide to change the scope and use of personally identifiable information, all users will be contacted via email for permission beforehand. All other changes will be posted on the web site; it is therefore highly recommend that users check the privacy policy from time to time.

We understand that privacy is a concern for our online users. We invite you to share your comments or questions regarding our policy by emailing info@mattandnat.com.
