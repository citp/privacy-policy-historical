1. Your Personal Information we Collect.
The primary purpose in collecting your personal information is to provide you with an effective, customized, and safe experience on our trading and forum network. Once you give us your personal information, you are not anonymous to MATCHBIN. Should you choose to use our services, you may be required to provide contact information, mailing information, billing information and other information as indicated on the forms throughout the site. Where possible, we indicate which fields are required and which fields are optional.

If you desire to list items or services on our trading network we may require additional information, such as financial information. We use your financial information, including credit card number, credit card billing address, expiration date, checking account name, address, bank routing number and bank account number to verify the accuracy of your name, address, and other information, as well as to bill you for your use of our services.

We use data collection devices such as "cookies" on certain pages of the MATCHBIN.com site and affiliated partner sites to help analyze our web page flow, measure promotional effectiveness, and promote trust and safety. "Cookies" are small files placed on your hard drive that assist us in providing our services. We offer certain features that are only available through the use of a "cookie". We also use cookies to allow you to enter your password less frequently during a session. Cookies can also help us provide information that is targeted to your interests. Most cookies are "session cookies", meaning that they are automatically deleted from your hard drive at the end on a session. You are always free to decline our cookies if your browser permits, although in that case you may not be able to use certain features on the Site and you may be required to reenter your password more frequently.

You may also encounter "cookies" or other similar devices on certain pages of MATCHBIN.com site or our affiliated partner sites that are placed by third parties. MATCHBIN does not control the use of cookies by third parties.

If you choose to bid, buy, or sell on the MATCHBIN.com site or any of our affiliated partner sites, we DO NOT collect information about your transactions. As stated in our Member Agreement, MATCHBIN.com is a matching venue and "is not a participant in any type of transaction".

We also collect member comments concerning you in our feedback area. If you choose to post messages in our message area or leave feedback for other users, we will collect that information you provide us. We retain this information to provide customer support and empower users as they use the trade network.

If you send us personal correspondence, such as emails or letters, or if other users or third parties send us correspondence about your activities or listings on the MATCHBIN.com or our affiliated partner sites, we may collect such information into a file specific for you.

4. Our Disclosure of Your Personal Information.
At MATCHBIN we do not sell or rent any of your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent. The following describes some of the ways in which your personal information may be disclosed in the normal course of business as we provide our users with our services.

MATCHBIN Trade Network. After registration, every member has the ability to determine the extent to which they would like their personal information shared on the Trade Network. Members may choose to keep all of their personal information private (except mandatory-shared information such as user screen name, city, state, country) or share all of their personal information. Your mandatory-shared information is displayed throughout the Network and is therefore available to the public. Your personal information that is optional to share is: first name, last name, address, postal code, phone number, and email address. You do not have to share your optional personal information on the network. Also, your user name and password are never displayed to the public. However, you have the ability to change your password if necessary. The Privacy Options section of a member’s account is the place where changes to personal information can be made. It has been the policy of MATCHBIN for users to freely trade amongst themselves without interference from us. We encourage the free flow of information (including personal information) in order for trade negotiations to be completed.

When a member opens a trade negotiation with another user, the system sends an internal message to that user indicating that the initiating member has a trade offer. Both initiating member and potential trade user receive sufficient personal information to either proceed or terminate with the trade negotiation. Trade negotiations are never monitored by MATCHBIN or its affiliated trade partners and all personal information is strictly between the two (or in some cases three) negotiating parties.

MATCHBIN "Talk" and "Meet" Venues. As with the MATCHBIN Trade Network, every member has the ability to determine the extent to which they would like their personal information shared on the "Talk" Venue and/or the "Meet" Venue on the MATCHBIN.com site. The Privacy Options section of a member’s account is the place where changes to personal information can be made. MATCHBIN Inc has no control over personal information disseminated other than such personal information contained on the member’s "personal information" section of the members account page.

Internal Service Providers. We may use third parties to help facilitate certain aspects of our business that we provide for you and therefore we may provide some of your personal information directly to these internal service providers. These internal service providers are subject to confidentiality agreements with us and other legal restrictions that prohibit their use of the information we provide them for any other purpose or reason other than the specific service they are providing for us. If you provide additional information to an internal service provider directly, then their use of your personal information is regulated by their applicable privacy policy.
