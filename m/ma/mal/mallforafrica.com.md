Information collected by the MallForAfrica website is never sold to third parties and it is our policy not to disclose or share personal information except in certain instances:   
· Personal information may be shared, transferred or disclosed to third parties who are contracted by us to perform specific functions related to the operation of our site and activities on the site such as delivery of particular activities (contests, e-cards, newsletters), processing functions, backend programming, maintenance and product fulfillment. · Business Transfers - Please see Transfer of Information.

  **Cookies and Log Data**  
Log data collected from this website on web servers both within TechMindSoft, LLC’s control and under the control of our third party webmaster (who is required to maintain such data on a confidential basis), is retained by TechMindSoft, LLC. This data includes aggregate information about the number of visits to different pages on our site. TechMindSoft, LLC uses such aggregate information to improve access to our content based on our visitors' browsers and operating system types to make our content available to as many visitors as possible and offer relevant products to our customers.
