# Malaysia.com | Privacy Policy

When you browse this site, FrequentTraveller.com does not collect personal information. The only way we are able to collect personal information is if you give it to us. 

When you send an inquiry, we will need to know your name and your e-mail address in order to respond to your questions or queries. We will also require this information as well as additional information once a reservation is pending. In such a case your personal information will only be submitted to third parties for the sole purpose of completing your transaction with us, enabling us to provide you with the requested services or products. 

FrequentTraveller.com may use this information to contact you about your purchased services. Frequenttraveller.com may use this information to contact you with details of other products and services offered by FrequentTraveller.com. 

To prevent unauthorized access of your personal information, FrequentTraveller.com will store all data in a secure database. 

By disclosing your personal information to us by using this website or over the telephone, you consent to the collection, storage and processing of your personal information by FrequentTraveller.com as stated in this Privacy Policy.
