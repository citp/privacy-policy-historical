# Privacy & Security | Malleries LLC

We are committed to your privacy. We take appropriate physical electronic and administrative steps to maintain the security and accuracy of personally identifiable information we collect, including limiting the number of people who have physical access to our database servers, as well as employing electronic security systems and password protections that guard against unauthorized access. Our website uses encryption technology, such as Secure Sockets Layer (SSL), to protect your personal information during data transport. However, both seller and buyer acknoledge that no internet data transmission can be guaranteed to be secure from access by unintended parties, and, by registering with Malleries LLC and providing personal information, you agree that you will not hold Malleries LLC liable. Please note that if logging into your Malleries.com account from a shared computer, that you must sign off your account before leaving that computer. Also note that e-mail is not encrypted and is not considered to be a secure means of transmitting credit card information.

Malleries LLC will not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties, unless you have granted us permission to do so by purchasing from Malleries. Examples of authorized third parties that we may share your personal information with include, but may not be limited to: sellers, shipping companies. If you use a credit card to place an order with Malleries, to confirm and process your order you are submitting your information to be shared with the seller whom you are purchasing from and only receive your information to perform their function(s) of collecting payment for your order. Sellers are not authorized to use any your information for any other purpose(s).

It may be necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to any person, violations or as otherwise required by law. We transfer information about you if Malleries is acquired by or merged with another company. 

The Information We Collect:   
When you register to buy items on the site we ask for information such as your name, address, email address, phone number, user name, credit card information and notification preferences. 

Malleries uses cookies to provide continuity from page to page. Within a single visit, these cookies store information such as your shopping list to simplify checkout. They do not contain any personally identifiable information. To access any secure areas such as your account information, you must still enter your password. The cookie itself does not tell us who you are, your email address or anything else personal. We use cookies to provide our visitors with a better experience on our site.

We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information in order to do their jobs.

We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect personal information about you.  
Your Malleries Account Information is password-protected.  
In certain areas Malleries uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions. 

How We Use the Information We Collect:   
We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  * to complete your purchase transactions;
  * to provide the services you request;
  * to keep you informed about the status of your orders;
  * to send you our catalogs and other offerings by mail; 

Online Account Registration   
To make online shopping faster and easier, you may register with Malleries.com. As a registered customer, you only have to enter your shipping addresses and billing information once; they will be securely stored with us for your future use. Using your name and a password of your choice, you may access your account online at any time to add, delete, or change information. 

If you are using a public computer, we strongly encourage you to LOG OUT at the conclusion of your session. Your information will still be stored with us but it will not be accessible to anyone else from that computer. 

E-Mails:   
You will receive promotional e-mails from us only if you have asked to receive them. If you do not want to receive email from Malleries you can click on the unsubscribe link and hit send at the bottom of any email communication sent by us. Please allow us 3 business days from when the request was received to complete the removal, as some of our promotions may already have been in process before you submitted your request. 

Acceptance:   
By using this Web Site, you accept the policies set forth in this Privacy Policy.

Changes to this Privacy and Security Policy:   
Malleries may update this policy. All such updates shall become effective immediately. Therefore, you should review this policy from time to time when you log on to this website.
