# Privacy Policy | Mainstreethost: Digital Marketing Agency Since 1999

Keeping your personal information secure is very important to us. When entering sensitive information, it is encrypted using secure socket layer technology (SSL). We do not sell your information to outside parties. We follow the industry standards to protect all delicate information submitted to us.

### How we use your information:

  * To process credit or debit card transactions.
  * To provide better service when responding to requests or questions.
  * To administer promotions, new services, contests or events.
  * To keep you informed about Mainstreethost discounts, specials, and the latest SEO news.



If you do not wish to receive e-mails from us, you can unsubscribe.
