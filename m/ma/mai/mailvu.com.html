personal information. The user has both the capability, and the responsibility, to designate the
recipients of their mail, and the ability to set a deletion time period.

 

Collection of Personal Information.

For certain services you need a mailVU.com user account. When you register we request personal
information which may include your name, address, phone number, email address, username,

such as number of uploaded files and frequency and size of data transfers.

 

A2Stream may collect non-personally identifying information provided by your browser as you use

connection type, webcam availability. We may assign your computer one or more cookies which may

that A2Stream may store, process, and transfer your personal information to any country.

Your Choice in Selecting Privacy Settings

Every account holder is provided with a Profile section in their account. It is the responsibility of each

Access to and Accuracy of Your Information

The mailVU.com site is generally accessible 24x7 every day of the year unless down for system

Keeping Your Personal information Secure

A2Stream takes seriously the trust you place in us. We will keep your private data secure consistent
with current industry standards. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, to maintain data
accuracy, and to ensure the appropriate use of the information, A2Stream utilizes appropriate physical,
technical, and administrative procedures to safeguard the information we collect.

Personal information, once transmitted to us, will be stored on computers that only selected personnel
and contractors have access to via password. These computer systems are located in controlled
facilities which have limited access. Your mail messages are limited-access, where access is gained
through an obscured URL system. This obscured URL makes it difficult if not impossible for a third
party to guess the URL address of your content without an invitation from you including the relevant
URL. However, unless the videos and content are password protected, your invitees may forward the
URL link to other parties and a possibility exists that an unauthorized third party could discover your
videos and/or content.
