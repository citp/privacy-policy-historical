# Majon Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

Majon International is committed to protecting your privacy. We do **not** ever sell or rent your personal information to any third parties, including advertisers. Your information is used for our internal purposes only. These purposes include such things as contacting you about your account or notifying you about additional services that we offer. Furthermore, you will only receive information pertaining to the subject matter that you have joined, participated or requested at Majon International or on any of our affiliated web sites. Additionally, we will always provide you with the option to unsubscribe from any of our emailing lists by following the instructions at the bottom of any of our emails. Thank you for making Majon International an industry leader in the online marketing and advertising arena!
