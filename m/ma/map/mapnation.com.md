# Privacy Policy: MapNation.com©

> MapNation.com Privacy Policy
> 
> To protect your privacy, we have provided this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way we collect and use your personal information. You may access our privacy policy from our home page; it is also accessible any time we request your personal information.
> 
> Information We Collect  
>  This notice applies to all information you submit on the MapNation.com website. On some pages, you can register with MapNation.com, make requests and sign up to receive materials. The types of personal information we collect at these pages include:
> 
> § Name   
>  § Address   
>  § Email address   
>  § Phone number 
> 
> How We Use Your Information  
>  All information provided while utilizing MapNation.com’s services to obtain information, will be used solely for the purpose of furnishing you with information. At no time, will we supply your name or information relating to you to any third parties other than those selected to supply you with information. We never use or share your personal information in ways unrelated to the ones described above, and we prohibit such unrelated uses. 
> 
> Data Security  
>  To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
> 
> To bring our customers and visitors the utmost quality and security, our servers use multiple redundant programs to monitor our site usage 24 hours a day for any unusual activity.
> 
> Children's Privacy  
>  Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 18, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 18.
> 
> How to Contact Us  
>  Should you have other questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please email us at privacy@ internetmedia-inc.com.
>
