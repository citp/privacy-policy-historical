## **Collection and Use of Personal Information**

When purchasing a product, we collect the following personal information from you:

  * Contact information, such as name (required), email address (required), mailing address (optional), phone number (optional)
  * Unique identifiers, such as user name, account number, license number, password



As is the case  for most web sites, we automatically gather information about your computer/device  such as your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and operating system.

We use this information to

  * Fulfill your order
  * Send you an order confirmation
  * Respond to customer service requests
  * Respond to your questions and concerns

## **Plugin update checker data**

Maps Marker Pro uses a [plugin update checker library](https://github.com/YahnisElsts/plugin-update-checker), which checks every 24 hours if a new version is available. The following unpersonalized info is transmitted and gets logged on our server with those requests:

  *     * IP address and domain of the server (e.g. 1.2.3.4, www.domain.com)
    * timestamp of the request (e.g. 2017-05-31 17:10:33)
    * Maps Marker Pro version number (e.g. 3.1)
    * WordPress version number (e.g. 3.1)
    * PHP Version (e.g. 7.2.1)
    * locale (e.g. en_US)



This data is only used in the context of the plugin update mechanism and is not shared with third parties.

## **User generated location data**

All the location data (like markers, layers, longitude, latitude, pop-up text…) that you create with your plugin instance within your WordPress installation is stored in your database only – we do not collect this data, nor do we get notified about it.

## **Comments**

When visitors leave comments on the site we collect the data shown in the comments form, and also the visitor’s IP address and browser user agent string to help spam detection. An anonymized string created from your email address (also called a hash) may be provided to the Gravatar service to see if you are using it. The Gravatar service privacy policy is available here: <https://automattic.com/privacy/>. After approval of your comment, your profile picture is visible to the public in the context of your comment. ## **Affiliate program**

If you choose to register for our [affiliate program](https://affiliates.mapsmarker.com/), you can use link to get provisions for sales on your site. If one of your links embedded on your sites is clicked, a so called return cookie is saved on the visitors computer so that in case he or she buys a license, this sale is referred to your affiliate account. We can clear those traffice logs on demand in total for your affiliate user or on IP basis for individual visitors.

## **Security**

When we collect personal information directly from you, we follow generally accepted industry standards – like SSL encrypted transmission – to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission, and when we receive it. No method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about security on our website, you can[ contact us](https://www.mapsmarker.com/contact).

In order to make a purchase from us, you must use our shopping cart provider to finalize and pay for your order. Its privacy statement and security practices will also apply to your information. We encourage you to read that privacy statement before providing your information.
