2. What are cookies and how MapsOfIndia.com uses them?
As combined part of offering and providing customised and personalised services, MapsOfIndia.com may use cookies to store and track your information. A cookie is a miniscule amount of data that is sent to your browser from a Web server that may be stored on your computer's hard drive.

Generally, we use cookies to:
  • Cache your personalised settings for items such as route language and default country location.
  • Determine whether you are using a public computer so that any MapsOfIndia.com settings changed while using a public computer do not affect the next user's ability to use MapsOfIndia.com
  • Roughly Estimate our audience or browsers. Each fresh browser trying to access MapsOfIndia.com may be given a cookie. That maybe used to determine the extent of repeat usage by the user.
  • Assist us to track the number of visits to and business activities at the MapsOfIndia.com or MapsOfIndia.com store, and to track and process the items in your shopping basket.
  • Track your progress and number of entries or queries in some of our promotions, and contests. When a Compare Infobase-hosted promotion uses cookies, the information written to the cookie may be used to track the entries, submissions made, and status in prize drawings list.
  • Measure certain specific traffic patterns to the site, track the areas of MapsOfIndia.com network of Web sites visited, track the Web site or geographical location through which you came to MapsOfIndia.com and your visiting patterns in average. We use this research to understand our users' visit habits and browsing preferences of if they are similar or different from one another so that we can make each visit by you on MapsOfIndia.com better then last one. We may use this information to better the content, banners, and promotions that you and other users will be likely to see on our sites.
  • MapsOfIndia.com also collects geophysical IP addresses for system and server administration and to report visiting information to our advertisers.
  • Other advertising networks that may serve advertisings onto MapsOfIndia.com network may also use their own cookies.
  • MapsOfIndia.com and its advertisers may also use small pieces of code called ''web beacons'' or electronic images that are sometime called "single-pixel GIF" to collect anonymous and average advertising patterns, such as counting hits, browser type, time spent viewing a page, type of page views, promotion views, or advertising responses. These "web beacons" may be used to deliver cookies that conform to our anonymous cookie policy. MapsOfIndia.com does not allow access or sell other advertisers or their advertising networks, information collected from you.
