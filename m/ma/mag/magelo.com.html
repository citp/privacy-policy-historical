Privacy policy
The intent of this privacy policy is to detail:
 The information that Magelo may gather about individuals who visit our websites and create accounts with us
 How that information is used
 Magelo's disclosure practices


 Site usage information

Magelo gathers site usage information from all visitors to our websites. This data is used in the aggregate to help determine general usage patterns and to fine-tune layout so that our sites are as useful and enjoyable as possible. This site usage information does not contain any information which would identify an individual user. Generally it contains information such as (but not limited to):

 What pages have been visited
 What pages referred visitors to our sites
 What search engine was used to find our sites
 What browsers and screen resolutions are used to view our websites
 What country visitors to our site are from
 How long a visitor remains on our website

This site usage information and statistical data may be made public at Magelo's discretion.

 Individual user information

Individually identifiable information will not be released without that individual's prior consent. Visitors are specifically asked for individually identifiable information when signing up for certain Magelo services. All information which would be individually identifiable (including billing information) is only transmitted over a Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) framework which encrypts information for your protection. From time to time, Magelo may contact individual users who have provided information to us for reasons such as (but is not limited to):

 Receipts for transactions
 Password reminders
 Support


 Credit card security

We collect credit card information for the purpose of conducting financial transactions with account holders. These transactions are conducted using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software, which encrypts the information both sent and received. We do not store any credit card information on our servers.

 Credit card statement

Should you conduct a credit card transaction with us, "Magelo" will appear on your credit card statement.

Magelo does not sell, rent, lease or allow any third parties to view or use the information collected.
Magelo takes reasonable measures to keep the information disclosed secure.
However, Magelo assumes no responsibility for any breach of security to this data.
