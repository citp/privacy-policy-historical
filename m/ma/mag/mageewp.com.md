# Privacy - MAGEEWP

At MageeWp, your privacy will be protected. Your security and privacy is pretty important to us.  
We will only collect your personal information for defined purposes. We will not distribute or share your personal information unless it is strictly necessary to fulfill our obligations to you.  
MageeWp will definitely not sell your personal information in any manner whatsoever.

### What information do we collect? What do we use it for

When you register or place an order on our website, your information including full name, email address, phone number, mailing address, credit card number (if you use this to purchase our products), and credit card expiration date will be required. The information is only used to complete the transaction and help us provide better support and services to you.

### How do the 3rd party credit card processors protect customer’s information

MageeWp’s transaction is completed via Paypal. All the customer’s information has been encrypted before it is sent to us, including name, credit card number, credit card expiration date, address, etc.. Encryption is used to prevent any malicious use of your personal information. Your credit card information is only used to complete the online transition.

### About Paypal

PayPal is the faster, safer way to send money, make an online payment, and receive money online. We uses Paypal to receive payment for our themes.
