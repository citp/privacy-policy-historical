Privacy Policy

Respecting your privacy and the law

The privacy of your personal information has always been important to us at Metlink Victoria Pty Ltd ('Metlink'). We are committed to respecting your right to privacy and protecting your personal information. We are bound by the National Privacy Principles in the Privacy Act 1988 (Commonwealth) as well as other applicable laws and codes affecting your personal information. Our staff are trained to respect your privacy in accordance with our standards, policies and procedures.

About this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how we manage your personal information. It also describes generally the sorts of personal information held and for what purposes, and how that information is collected, held, used and disclosed.

Our Privacy Policy applies to all your dealings with us whether using the Website or the Call Centres. We encourage you to check our Websites regularly for any updates to our Privacy Policy.

Collecting your personal information

When you visit our Websites, we may collect general information that will not personally identify you, such as:

• Internet browser
• Operating system
• Date of visit
• Time of visit
• Path taken through the site

This information alone does not identify an individual and we will not combine it with information that does identify you as an individual. It provides statistics that we use to analyse and improve the Metlink Websites. Through this statistical data, we are able to gain insights and a greater understanding of how people use our sites.

This website will use "cookies" to gather statistical information that helps us understand what you find interesting and useful on our client Web sites. These cookies are known as your "Unique Visitor Cookie" and identify you only by random number (e.g. #12489). You can decline our cookies by adjusting the "accept cookies" setting on your browser, however this may affect the functionality of Metlink Websites.

This website contains embedded code into pages of our Websites. This gives us statistical site usage information about the pages visited, duration of page view, location previous to accessing the Metlink Website, the navigational habits of user, and browser and operating system. We do not use this code to collect any personally identifiable information.

Links to Other Sites

You will be able to access external Websites by clicking on links we have provided. Those other Websites are not subject to our privacy standards, policies and procedures. You will need to contact or review those Websites directly to ascertain their privacy standards, policies and procedures.

Your sensitive information

Without your consent we will not collect information about you that reveals your racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs or affiliations, membership of a professional or trade association, membership of a trade union, details of health, disability, sexual orientation, or criminal record.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

To fully protect your privacy, we reserve the right to amend this policy at any time, and for any reason. We will post the amended terms on this Web site as they occur and encourage you to frequently check for updates. This privacy policy was last amended on 5 July, 2004

No Contract or Liability

Nothing contained in this policy is intended to create a contract or agreement between Metlink Victoria Pty Ltd and any user providing identifiable information in any form, whether visiting this site or any other Metlink Website.

We will take all necessary steps to comply with this privacy policy, however, to the extent permitted by law, nothing in this policy is intended to hold Metlink liable for any failure to comply with this policy.

Further, because Metlink does not control the privacy practices of our clients, we advise that you read and fully understand their privacy policies.
