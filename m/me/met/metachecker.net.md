# Meta Tag Analysis Tool by MetaChecker.net

Privacy Policy

At MetaChecker.net we believe that you the consumer should own and control your personal information. If you decide to share it anonymously with advertisers, then you should be fairly compensated for it. We will never share your information with third parties.

**Information We Collect**

We do not collect any personal information on our visitors. We run visitor statistics where a user is referenced by IP Address, Geographical Location. We know which browser and operating system you are using. However this information is not databased beyond the logging for the visitor statistics purposes. Advertisers on our site gather similar information simply to target the adds based on its relevance for example. If you are from London then you will only see adds that are of London origin.

**Third Party Advertising**

The ads appearing on this Network of WebSites are delivered to you by advertising partners. Information about your visit to this site, such as number of times you have viewed an ad (but not your name, address, or other personal information), is used to serve ads to you on this site.
