# Men's Health I Privacy policy

  


The MensHealth.co.uk website (the "Site") is owned by Rodale Limited and is committed to safeguarding your privacy. This statement explains what happens to the information you supply on our website and the control you have over it. 

EMAIL NEWSLETTERS 

When signing up for our weekly emails we typically ask that you provide us with your first and surname, age, gender, postcode, country and email address. We will send you an email to confirm that we have received your details. 

The information you supply is used to send out news by email and to get in touch with you where necessary. You may opt out of these mailings at the sign up stage. 

Instructions to securely remove yourself from our mailing list can be found at the end of every email you receive from us. 

Occasionally, mailings prepared several months in advance may include a customer's name, even after a customer has asked to be removed from our mailing lists. 

As a result, the customer may receive mail for one or two months after the request. We apologize for this inconvenience, and we will make sure that mailing lists prepared after such a request will reflect a customer's preference not to receive our mail.

MESSAGE BOARDS 

We shall accept no responsibility for your use of our message board and discussion group facility. 

You are responsible for the content of your messages and the consequences of any such messages.

We cannot and do not review every submission a user may make. We neither endorse nor guarantee the accuracy or propriety of any submission. 

REQUESTS FOR ADVICE 

We are unable to, and will not, respond to any individual requests for advice or assistance relating to any health, sexual or personal matters and shall delete any such requests immediately upon receipt. 

EXTERNAL LINKS 

Our sites contain advertisements and links to other sites which allow users to leave our pages. 

We are not responsible for the privacy practices, policies or the content of such websites. 

You should be aware that cookies may be used by sites to which you link from our site and as such information relating to your browsing of our site may be accessible by third parties. 

IF WE ASK FOR MORE INFO... 

Sometimes we require more specific information from you, for example when you enter a competition or purchase a product. 

The information requested may include your name, address, email address, telephone number, credit card number and type of computer. 

We may also request more details about your interests, your likes and dislikes regarding the site and the types of jobs you are interested in (for example) in an effort to deliver to you the best possible site experience. 

The information we gather may be used to make you aware of additional products and services, which may be of interest to you, or to contact you regarding site changes. 

WHO CAN ACCESS MY DETAILS? 

We will limit access to your information on a 'need to know' basis and to only those employees responsible for conducting business with our customers. 

We recognize the responsibility that comes with handling information and will apply stringent security measures to safeguard information about our customers and shall at all times comply with the provisions of the Data Protection Act 1998. 

Our site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All user databases are password protected. 

WHO DO YOU SHARE MY INFORMATION WITH? 

From time to time we make our customer email addresses, postal addresses and telephone numbers available to other reputable business associates whose products or services might be of value to you. 

If you do not want us to share your information please let us know by contacting us at one of the addresses shown below. 

Occasionally we may need to disclose certain information in order to comply with a legal requirement. 

If there is a change in control of this site your information could be sold or transferred as part of that transaction and your information may potentially be used by the purchaser or other recipient. 

SEEING THE INFO WE HOLD 

If you do not want to receive emails or other information from us please contact us at one of the addresses shown below.

If you wish to see the information that we have collected about you please contact us at one of the addresses shown below but please note that a fee may be charged to cover our expenses. 

If you wish to alter any information that we have collected about you or have your details removed from our database please contact us via one of the addresses shown below.

AUTOMATICALLY LOGGED INFORMATION 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. 

COOKIES 

When you visit our site a small text file (known as a 'cookie') may be placed on your computer. 

This cookie will enable us to recognise you when you return to menshealth.co.uk and may be used to show you offers which match your interests and to store information about your website browsing history. 

Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies but, if you wish to disable cookies, there is a simple procedure in most Internet browsers that allow you to turn off cookies. 

Please remember however that cookies may be required to allows you to use certain features of our site.

CHILDREN 

We do not specifically collect information about children under 16.

We believe that children under 16 should get their parents' consent before giving out personal information (such as their names, email addresses and phone numbers). 

Before accessing sensitive areas of the site (such as the 'Sex' section of the site) children under 16 must get their parents consent. 

Children under 16 do not have our consent to use the message board and discussion room facility.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY 

Any changes to our privacy commitment will be posted here so that you will always know what information we gather, how we might use that information, and whether we will disclose it to anyone.
