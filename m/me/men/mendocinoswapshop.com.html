Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy:

Ofertas y Avisos,  operates http://www.mendocinoswapshop.com. It is Ofertas y Avisos  policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our website.

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, Ofertas y Avisos collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Ofertas y Avisos purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Mendocino Swap Shop visitors use its website. From time to time, Ofertas y Avisos may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Ofertas y Avisos also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on http://www.mendocinoswapshop.com website. Ofertas y Avisos only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the website where the comment was left.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to Mendocino Swap Shop website choose to interact with Ofertas y Avisos in ways that require Ofertas y Avisos to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Ofertas y Avisos gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at www.mendocinoswapshop.com to provide a username and email address. In each case, Ofertas y Avisos collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with Mendocino Swap Shop. Ofertas y Avisos does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Aggregated Statistics

Ofertas y Avisos may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its website. Ofertas y Avisos may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Ofertas y Avisos does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

 

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

Ofertas y Avisos discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Ofertas y Avisos behalf or to provide services available at Ofertas y Avisos websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. By using Ofertas y Avisos websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. Ofertas y Avisos will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, Ofertas y Avisos discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or when Ofertas y Avisos believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of Ofertas y Avisos, third parties or the public at large. If you are a registered user of an Ofertas y Avisos website and have supplied your email address, Ofertas y Avisos may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new features, solicit your feedback, or just keep you up to date with what’s going on with Ofertas y Avisos and our products. If you send us a request (for example via email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. Ofertas y Avisos takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Ofertas y Avisos uses cookies to help Mendocino Swap Shop identify and track visitors, their usage of Ofertas y Avisos websites, and their website access preferences. Mendocino Swap Shop visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Ofertas y Avisos websites, with the drawback that certain features of Ofertas y Avisos websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Ads

Ads appearing on our website may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Ofertas y Avisos websites and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

Although most changes are likely to be minor, Ofertas y Avisos may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in Ofertas y Avisos Service sole discretion.Ofertas y Avisos encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. If you have a www.mendocinoswapshop.com account, you might also receive an alert informing you of these changes. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
