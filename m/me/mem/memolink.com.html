Memolink.com Privacy Statement Highlights
Have you ever wondered what we do with the information you give us, but maybe you didn’t want to take the time to read through a long, legal document to find out?  Well, we’ve made a change that will make understanding how we collect, use, and protect your information easier and less time consuming.   We’re proud of how we adhere to best practices when it comes to your information and we want you to feel good about it too.  Below are the highlights of the full Memolink.com Privacy Statement.  Click into any section to read additional details. 


Personal Information More Details
  • When you register for Memolink.com we will ask you to provide various personal and other information.
  • The information we collect may be combined with information obtained from Memolink Surveys and other business partners.
  • We use cookies and other technologies to keep track of your point-earning actions so we can credit Memolink Points to your account and to offer a customized experience.
Uses of Information More Details
  • We use the information we collect to provide the Memolink.com rewards program service.  Our services may include the display of personalized content.
  • We use your information to inform you of other products or services offered by Memolink.com and its business partners, and to send you relevant survey invitations.
  • We do not sell, rent, or lease our member lists to third parties.  In order to help provide our services, we may occasionally provide information to other companies that work on our behalf.
Your Choices More Details
  • To stop receiving promotion email, also called Memomail, follow the instructions in any email. See the email communication section of the full privacy statement for additional details.
  • To view and edit your personal information, go to the access section of the full privacy statement.
How to Contact Us
    For more information about our privacy practices, go to the full Memolink.com Privacy Statement, or contact our Member Support team.
