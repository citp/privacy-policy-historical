# MegaDVDArchive | Bonus Sites

### What information is collected?

  * We collect personally identifiable information during the registration process to our website, including your name, location, email address, IP address and time of registration.
  * We may collect various information submitted by you while using our website including, but not limited to, responses to surveys, ratings, searches, posts, reviews, suggestions, sweepstakes, purchasing, profile data, customer service requests etc.
  * While using our site we will also save various usage and statistical data including, but not limited to, resolution, browser, operating system, pages viewed, preferred content etc.
  * Email you receive from us may incorporate technology which allows us to compile data indicating if our email has been read and if any links in that email were clicked.
  * We rely on certified 3rd party to process credit card, check and other payment methods; for security reasons billing information such as credit card number, credit card expiration date, or CVV2, are not stored on our servers. This information is stored with our billing processor for convenience purposes to our members when making repeat purchases.



### How your information used

We require this information to understand your needs and provide you with a better service, and in particular for the following reasons:

  * Improving our services, products and websites.
  * Internal record keeping and customer support.
  * To provide an experience customized to you according to your interests.
  * Send website email such as notifications, invoices, password resets or support ticket replies. We may also send promotional email about promotional offers, special events, new products or services, site updates, or other 3rd party products or services.
  * We may share your information with 3rd party partners which allow us to provide a specific service or feature, or for marketing and promotional purposes, or if required to do so by law.
  * We will never sell you information.



### Commitment to Data Security

Your personally identifiable information is kept secure with suitable physical, electronic and operations procedures. Only authorized employees, agents and contractors (who have agreed to keep information secure and confidential) have access to this information.  
All emails and newsletters from this site allow you to opt out of further mailings.
