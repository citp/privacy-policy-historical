### User Personal Data Protection Policy

This web page describes Megri Soft Limited privacy policies with concern to the collection of data, usage and any disclosure of any personally-identifiable information (referred to as ‘personal information’) you provide to any of our mobile applications.

While accessing our any mobile application, web sites or any other related services (collectively referred and named as ‘Services’) you hereby consent to the collection, manipulation, storage, transfer, disclosure and other uses of your information as described in this User Personal Data Protection Policy. If you do not agree to our this policy, please we request you not to use any of our Mobile Applications and websites.

### Gathering Information

#### Getting Personal Information

Personal information is the data which provides user identity that can be used to contact you whether offline or online. We get your personal information during:

  * The sign up, sign in or any modifications of an account on any of our applications
  * While creating a personal profile or account on our applications
  * Request for the service from third party service providers on our site or accessing to our services on social networks and other third party services
  * While requesting for any technical support services, subscription to our newsletters or any other marketing related services such as email campaigns



The collected information can also vary. Such user information can include but not limited only to the user name, phone number, email address, date of birth, home address, country, gender or mobile phone number. Additional and optional information you willingly include but are not limited to a short description of your biography or a personal photo or image (or avatar). Doing same will enhance your user experience at any of our mobile applications, adding a more personalized touch to your app profile. You can choose to go out of this at any time by deleting this optional or additional information.

### Consequences with not providing Personal Information

If app user will opt not to give his/ her Personal Information that is necessary or mandatory to give for processing your request, then we can not be able to provide the corresponding service on your device.

### Collection of Information Automatically

In addition to information given by user while accessing any of our application, the Application can collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the mobile device you use, unique device ID of your mobile devices, IP address of your mobile device, your operating system of mobile, the type of Internet browsers you use on mobile, and the information about the way you use the mobile application and the location of your mobile device.

### Opt-out Rights of User

You can stop collection of information by any of our application easily by uninstalling the Application. You can use the standard uninstall processes available in your mobile device or by mobile application marketplace. User can also send request to opt-out via email, at [Support@megrisoft.com](mailto:Support@megrisoft.com).

### Do Third Parties see or have any access to the information obtained from application?

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services for  helping us in improving our Application and services. We will only share user  information with the third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.

We may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information:

  * as required by the law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or any similar legal process;

  * when we believe in good faith that the disclosure is important to protect our user rights, protect your safety or the safety of any others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request, with our trusted services providers who work on our behalf for our clients;

  * will never have an independent use to the user information we disclose to them, and have agreed to adopt to the rules set forth in this privacy statement.




### Policy for Data Retention and Managing Your Information

We will keep data provided by User for as long as you use our any of the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter. We will retain User collected information for up to 24 months automatically and thereafter may store it in aggregate or our collection.

If you would like us to delete User Provided Data that you have provided via any of our mobile application, please get in touch with us at [support@megrisoft.com](mailto:support@megrisoft.com) and we will respond to your email within few time. Please note that some or all of the User Provided Data or information may be required in order for the mobile application to function properly and smoothly.

### Security

We are always concerned with the confidentiality and safeguarding of your information. We provide physical and procedural safeguards to protect user information we keep, process and maintain. Please be aware that, although we endeavor provide reasonable security to user information we use, process and maintain, but no security system can make its prevention from all potential security breaches.
