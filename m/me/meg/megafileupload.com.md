# 

**Collecting Email Addresses**

We collect the e-mail addresses of Mega File Upload Delivery recipients and senders for the purpose of logging and measuring usage. Mega File Upload does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with nonaffiliated companies.

**Certain Exceptional Disclosures**

We may disclose your information if necessary to protect our legal rights or if the information relates to actual or threatened harmful conduct or potential threats to the physical safety of any person. Disclosure may be required by law or if we receive legal process.

**File Transfer Security**

We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site. Files stored for delivery are only accessible by Mega File Upload and through the clickable link generated for your recipient. All files stored for delivery are deleted when they expire.
