2.  **What is the personal information collected by the Company?**  
2.1   _Information Collection_  
Users will be asked to provide Meratask certain information (“ **User Information** ”). User Information includes, among other things without limitation:

  1. Contact data (personally identifiable information, such as Your name, email address and phone number) of the End-Users and visitors,;
  2. Demographic data (such as gender, birthday, zip code, city, country);
  3. Data regarding your usage of the services;
  4. Other information that you voluntarily choose to provide to the Application.



2.2   _Nature of Information_  
The information collected from you by the Company may constitute ‘Personal Information’ or ‘Sensitive Personal Data or Information’ under the SPDI Rules.  
“ **Personal Information** ” is defined under the SPDI Rules to mean any information that relates to a natural person, which, either directly or indirectly, in combination with other information available or likely to be available with a body corporate, is capable of identifying such person.  
 “ **Sensitive Personal Data or Information** ” is defined under the SPDI Rules to mean Personal Information about that person relating to:

  1. passwords;
  2. financial information such as bank accounts, credit and debit card details or other payment instrument details;
  3. sexual orientation;
  4. medical records and history;
  5. biometric information;
  6. information received by body corporate under lawful contract or otherwise;
  7. visitor details as provided at the time of registration or thereafter; and
  8. call data records.



Information that is freely available in the public domain or accessible under the Right to Information Act, 2005 or any other law will not be regarded as Personal Information or sensitive personal data or information.  
Since Sensitive Personal Data or Information means a particular kind of Personal Information. Any references to Personal Information in this Privacy Policy should be construed to include a reference to Sensitive Personal Data or Information.

2.3   _Voluntary_  
All the information provided to the Application by a User, including Sensitive Personal Data or Information, is voluntary. Collection of information which has been designated as ‘Sensitive Personal Data or Information’ under the SPDI Rules requires your express consent. By affirming your assent to this Privacy Policy, you provide your consent to such collection as required under applicable law. Our Services may be unavailable to you in the event such consent is not given. All statements in this Privacy Policy apply to all Users and all Users are expected to read and understand the Privacy Policy.  
You hereby also give your consent to the Company to store the User Information on the Company’s servers even if the servers are located outside India.

3.4  _Cookies_                                         
A cookie is a small piece of data that is not Sensitive Personal Data or Information that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. A cookie cannot read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. Use of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on Meratask. Once you close your browser, the cookie simply terminates. For instance, by setting a cookie on your browser, you would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on the Meratask. You can choose whether to accept cookies by changing the settings of your browser. You can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or allow your browser to show you when a cookie is being sent. If you reject the cookies on websites, you may still be able to use Meratask, but it shall be limited to certain minimal functionality. The only drawback to this is that you may be limited only to some areas of Meratask or limited to certain functions of Meratask. Some of our business partners may use cookies on Meratask. Please note that we have no access to or control over these cookies. The Company and our business partners may use cookies to better personalize the content, banners, and promotions that you see on Meratask.
