Privacy Policy

Medindia4u.com Pvt. Ltd. ("Medindia", we, us) operates this website. medindia.net (Site), and may operate other websites. Medindia is totally committed to respecting your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.

Information We Collect

Medindia collects some non-personally-identifying information of the type that web browsers and servers typically capture, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Medindias purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Medindias visitors use its website. From time to time, Medindia may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Medindia also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on Medindia.net blogs/sites. Medindia only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information
