# MediaMint Website Privacy Policy - Media Mint

This Privacy Policy describes the collection and use of information on our website. Please read this policy before using, or submitting information to our sites. By using this site, you signify that you agree to this Privacy Policy. You may find links to other websites on our site, please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of any other sites.

**Personal Information Collected on this Website**

No personal information is collected on this website from consumers who browse our website.

The only personal information collected on this website is gathered on our “Contact Us” forms. In the Contact Us form, fields are provided so that users can submit query and provide contact information including name, email address, and phone number. We collect this personal information so that we can respond to these inquiries.

**Cookies**

We use cookies on this website. Cookies are small text files created by a web server, delivered through a web browser, and stored on your computer. The use of cookies on our website is to show our visitors with our promotional advertisements.

You have the ability to manage the use of cookies on your computer using controls in your browser.
