# Privacy Policy of MediFee.com

**What information we collect?**

Medifee.com collects the following visitor information: 

  * Information explicitly provided by the visitor by filling a form on the website 
  * Information automatically collected by our server (like IP address, browser, operating system etc.) 
  * As you browse medifee.com, online ad networks we work with may place anonymous cookies on your computer, and use similar technologies, in order to understand your interests based on your (anonymous) online activities, and thus to tailor more relevant ads to you. If you do not wish to receive such tailored advertising, you can visit [this page](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/) to opt out of most companies that engage in such advertising. (This will not prevent you from seeing ads; the ads simply will not be delivered through these targeting methods.) 



**How do we use that information?**

While filling the form for any medical offer or service, user understands that this information will be sent to the relevant service provider who provides the medical service. MediFee.com assures you that this information will NOT be used for any other other service provider or third party. Medifee.com can and will use the email address and/or phone number provided in the form to contact the user in reference to the service opted or any other relevent. 

**How to opt out?**

If you want to us to delete all of your information we have collected through any forms on the website, please [contact us](http://www.medifee.com/contact.php).
