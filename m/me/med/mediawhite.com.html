Consumer Information

Media White uses one or more third party ad serving technology to serve ads at this site and at our client’s sites. In the course of delivering an ad to you, the third party ad serving party may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser and use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services that may be of interest to you.

Efforts to Protect Consumer Privacy

Media White is dedicated in serving it users by protecting their privacy. We strictly follow the industry guidelines. The cookies used by Media White and by the third part are not meant to invade your privacy or damage your system or files in anyway; rather it is used to enhance your web experience. A cookie that is stored on file on your computer is a unique number that is assigned to you first time you are served with an ad. It ensures that you are not repeatedly served with the same ad again. Cookies help advertisers to keep measure of how you utilize a website that is displaying you an ad. This helps the advertisers serve you with an enhanced experience that is it uses the information and provides you with ads that are of your interest. It is not necessary for Media White or the third parties to place cookies to deliver the ad to you, however it serves the purpose of enhancing your web experience by not serving the same ads to you again and placing specific ads that may be of your interest.
