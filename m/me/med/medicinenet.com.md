## MedicineNet.com Privacy Policy

At MedicineNet.com your privacy is important to us. We will never willfully disclose personally identifiable information about our customers to any third-party without prior consent.

**Information Collection and Use**     Back to Top

MedicineNet, Inc. does not sell, share or rent any of the information collected on our sites to any third party in manners different from what is disclosed in this privacy policy. Below is a list of the various ways in which we collect information.

**Newsletter Sign-up  
**If you choose to subscribe to one or more of MedicineNet's Newsletters, we need your e-mail address for delivery. You can unsubscribe to any newsletter directly from the Newsletter or through the Newsletter sign-up page simply by entering your e-mail address and clicking unsubscribe.

**My Account Registration**  
We need your e-mail address and a password of your choosing, to set-up and login to your account. If you choose to register for a Newsletter, establishing an account is not required, however you can choose to establish an account to manage your newsletters with increased security. You may at any time opt-out of receiving special information through the Newsletter Registration areas of My Account. 

**Traffic Patterns**  
We collect traffic patterns and site usage information throughout our site. As part of this we collect the URL you came from in entering our site, what browser you are using, your IP address, and the URL you go to from our site if it is a link or advertiser on our site. In addition, we use Cookies to assign a unique, random number to identify your computer. Cookies, by themselves, cannot identify you personally or any other individual using your computer. Cookies allow us to keep track of shopping carts, login information (for increased security), and site traffic patterns and usage.

**E-mail a Friend  
**If you choose to use the E-mail a Friend feature, we ask for your friend's E-mail address to deliver the information. This will be a one-time correspondence from MedicineNet.

**Viewer Suggestions  
**If you choose to submit a Viewer Suggestion to MedicineNet, we require your first name while your e-mail address and last name are optional. You may receive a one-time communication from MedicineNet in response to your suggestion. You can indicate through your comment if you would rather not hear from us. 

**Surveys, Polls and Contests**  
Periodically, we may also ask for other demographic information as part of a survey, poll or contest. It is not mandatory for you to answer these questions, your participation is voluntary. If you decide to answer these questions, your individual answers will remain confidential. We will only use the survey information in the aggregate to study statistical results. Examples of such information are as follows; age, gender, occupation, marital status, family size, areas of interest, or medical related questions. Contact information such as name, e-mail address and shipping address will be used to send out free gifts and prizes. In these instances the above information is collected and stored solely by MedicineNet with no personally identifiable information to be shared with any third party.

**Sponsored Surveys  
**Periodically, we may collect demographic information (as exampled above), as part of a survey sponsored by one of our partners or advertisers. Participation in any survey is voluntary.  In this instance, the survey information will be shared by both parties, this may include personally identifiable information such as an e-mail address. Due to the sensitive nature of such information, we use encryption technology to transfer the files between parties to ensure your privacy. All users will be notified in advance that the survey is 'sponsored' and subject to these guidelines and will be required to agree to these guidelines prior to answering any questions. ([Review Sample Sponsored Survey Disclosure](http://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=18449))

**Discussion Boards**  
If you choose to participate in a discussion (message board or chat), we need your name, e-mail address and a password of your choosing, to establish an account to allow access to discussions. Any personally identifiable information you disclose through the use of discussions (message boards or chats) is voluntary and will be used in accordance with MedicineNet's [Discussion Rules](https://web.archive.org/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13353). MedicineNet is not liable for any personally identifiable information you choose to disclose as part of a discussion (message boards or chats). 

**Disclosure of your Information**     Back to Top

We do not sell or rent any personally identifiable information about you to any third party, without prior consent. We may share non-personal, aggregate information regarding customer demographics, traffic patterns, sales, and site usage with our partners, advertisers or sponsors. We will never willfully disclose personally identifiable information about our customers to any third party without prior consent. 

In special cases MedicineNet may believe it is necessary to disclose personally identifiable information in order to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be violating or causing injury to or interference with MedicineNet's rights or property, other users, or any other party that could be harmed by such activities. We may also disclose or access personally identifiable information when we believe in good faith that the law requires it.

**Sponsored Surveys  
**If you choose to participate in a Sponsored Survey, the survey information will be shared between MedicineNet and the sponsor, this may include personally identifiable information such as e-mail address. Due to the sensitive nature of such information, we use encryption technology to transfer the files between parties to ensure your privacy. All users will be notified in advance that the survey is 'sponsored' and subject to these guidelines and will be required to agree to these guidelines prior to answering any questions. ([Review Sample Sponsored Survey Disclosure](http://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=18449)) 

**Opt-out Features**

Back to Top

MedicineNet believes that you should control your healthcare decisions, including what information you receive from us. The choices are yours to make. You can tell us if you don't want to receive the Newsletters through My Account, unsubscribe from any newsletter you receive, or by e-mail, phone, or mail request.

**Changing Your Personal Information**     Back to Top

You may correct and update your personal information at any time. All you have to do is Login to My Account and edit the information. You may also notify us via e-mail, phone, or mail request to assist you in making these changes. If you participate in discussions (message boards or chats), you can update your personal information at any time by choosing edit profile once in the discussion environment.

**Your Consent**     Back to Top

By using the MedicineNet web site, those sites powered by MedicineNet, and the services available to you on our sites, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described above. If we change our privacy policy, we will immediately post the changes on this page so that you are always aware of our policies.

**Links on our Site**     Back to Top

MedicineNet has provided links to various sites that we believe provide valuable information to you, our viewer. However, we are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or actions of such web sites.

**Notification of Changes**

We will notify you directly if there is a major change in our privacy practices. We will take reasonable measures to obtain written or active e-mail consent from you if MedicineNet is going to be using the information collected from you in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection.
