# Privacy Statement

Mediate.com respects the privacy of its visitors. Mediate.com does not collect personal identifying information from visitors to its website without their consent. 

**Personal Information:**

Information that you provide about yourself in the instance of a purchase, a survey, or other website feature, is used only for the purposes there stated. Participation is voluntary. If you provide personal information as part of a purchase (e.g., name, address, email address, phone number, or credit card information), we will take full reasonable precautions to ensure the security of your information, including the use of encryption to protect credit card information. We will use your personal information to process orders, to contact you regarding technical updates, special offers and upcoming events and for no other purpose. We do not trade nor rent any lists nor personal information. 

**Cookies:**

At our client's option, we may utilize cookies (information sent by our web server and stored on your computers hard drive) to identify you and to save you time when you return to manage your record or order new services. Cookies do not contain personal information about you.
