Our Privacy Policy

Meineke Car Care Center Inc. ("Meineke") respects the privacy of every individual who visits our Web Site and Meineke uses our best efforts to protect the privacy of its on-line visitors. The Meineke Web server automatically recognizes and gathers the domain name, but not the e-mail address (where possible) of visitors to our Web site. Meineke also gathers any information volunteered by visitors to our Web site, such as survey information and/or site registrations. This information is used to improve the content of the Meineke Web site and is not shared with other organizations for commercial or any other purposes except from time to time with the Meineke dealer network. Meineke does not use "cookies." If you supply Meineke with your postal address on-line you will only receive the information for which you provided us your address. Persons who supply Meineke with their telephone numbers on-line will only receive telephone contact if they have requested it. If you do not want to receive e-mail from Meineke in the future, please let us know by mail or by replying to any email we send with "unsubscribe" in the subject line. We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies. From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy statement. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our Web site periodically. Visitors may prevent their information from being used for purposes other than those for which it was originally collected by calling us at the number provided below. Upon request we provide site visitors with access to all information [including proprietary information] that we maintain about them. Visitors can access this information or have this information corrected by sending us an e-mail at the address provided below. If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the addresses or phone number below.
