# Metropolitan Business Products

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we are providing this notice to explain our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. 

The only personally identifiable information maintained by the **mbpsupply.com** website is information required for processing your orders, contacting you, or for site usage statistics. 

**mbpsupply.com** is committed to ensuring the privacy of our customers. We will not give, sell, or in any way redistribute your personally identifiable information to any third party unless we are legally required to do so. 

All personal information you submit to us (when placing an order) is encrypted using industry-standard SSL encryption to protect data transmissions. 

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collected online.

To prevent unauthorized use of your credit card, we MAY request that you provide us with the name and phone number of your credit card issuing bank. This can help to protect you as an online shopper from having unauthorized persons use you credit card as well as prevents charge backs for us as an online merchant.
