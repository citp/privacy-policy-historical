# Privacy Policy | MBAUniverse.com

You expressly agree that use of the website is at your sole risk, you are accepting the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

**INFORMATION GATHERING AND USAGE**

The contents of your MBAuniverse.com page is stored and maintained on MBAuniverse.com servers in order to provide the service.

When you access your personal page from anywhere we ask for information such as your user name, password, email address, etc.

MBAUniverse.com will never sell or rent your personal information, especially your e-mail addresses, with any third parties (besides MBAUniverse.com) for marketing purposes without your express permission.

Social sharing of data: MBAUniverse.com allows other users to browse content and pictures that you have, submitted, or commented on. This information is made available via search or within the permalink URL of the content in which you performed the action on. MBAUniverse.com may choose to post this data on other site features in the future.

**COOKIES**

Cookies are required to use the MBAUniverse.com services. A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive.

We use persistent cookies to record your session information on the servers and your local machines.

MBAUniverse.com may periodically update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified when you signed in or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
