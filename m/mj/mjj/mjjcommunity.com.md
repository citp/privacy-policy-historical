# MJJCOMMUNITY.com - THE Michael Jackson Community

Monday, 29 October 2007   
---  
  
We have created this email privacy policy to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy and the protection of your information. 

**Did you recieve an e-mail from us?**

If you received a mailing from us, you have registered or otherwise have an existing relationship with us. We respect your time and attention by controlling the frequency of our mailings. 

**Your Privacy**  

We use security measures to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of data used by our system. 

**Sharing and Usage**

We will never share, sell, or rent individual personal information with anyone without your advance permission or unless ordered by a court of law. Information submitted to us is only available to staff managing this information for purposes of contacting you or sending you emails based on your request for information and for purposes of providing services relating to our communications with you. 

**How can you stop receiving email from us?**

If you have received unwanted, unsolicited email sent via this system or purporting to be sent via this system, please forward a copy of that email with your comments to [abuse@mjjcommunity.com](mailto:abuse@mjjcommunity.com) for review. 

Privacy Policy Changes 

This policy was created on October 29, 2007.
