Privacy Policy

Our privacy Commitment

This notice is to provide you information on our online privacy policy.

Information

Information collected or submitted on www.mcminone.com includes: Name, Address, Email address, Phone number, and the reasons for any inquiry. In addition, we do collect domain names and IP addresses of visitors to our Web Site, information on how many visits each page receives and user-specific information on what pages are visited.

The Way We Use Information

Occasionally, we do engage in marketing efforts with responsible companies. In the course of these marketing activities, we may share our customer's postal and/or email addresses. This sharing may result in communications to you by such parties. This sharing is done solely as a result of our belief that the information communicated to you by such parties will be of interest to you

Other information is collected for marketing purposes only. By looking at usage patterns, we know which areas need expansion and which are currently meeting the needs of the customers.

Mailings

Email

If you choose to opt-out of future email special offers, please click Remove and follow the instructions on the page.

Postal Mail

If you supply us with your postal address online you may receive mailing from us with information on new products.

If you do not wish to receive these mailings, please let us know either via email to talk@mcminone.com or by calling 1-877-MCM-ELEC (626-3532). Please provide your exact name and address.

Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy and ensure the correct use of information, we have established physical, electronics and managerial procedures to secure any information collected online.

We use 128-bit encryption in all areas of the site, which require you to provide personal or account information. This inhibits unauthorized use of your information as it is transmitted to our server. The Verisign logo is pictured on the "Payment Details" areas of the website.

To ensure you are using the most updated security features, be sure you have installed the latest version of Microsoft Internet Explorer or Netscape Communicator.

Additional Information

MCM will not be held accountable for damages of any kind which may have been incurred through the usage of www.mcminone.com
