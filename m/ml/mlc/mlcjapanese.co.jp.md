MLC Meguro Language Center takes the privacy of your personal information very seriously.

All information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, occupation, address, Email address, or phone number is kept strictly confidential.

We will not sell, rent or share this information about you with any other parties.

We will use your information only in accordance with the terms of this Privacy Policy.
