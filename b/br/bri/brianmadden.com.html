BrianMadden.com Privacy Policy

Information I Collect about Visitors
My web server automatically logs information about site visitors, which may include their IP address, time and date of visit, pages requested, any errors they received, and (if possible) any search keywords they used to find this site. I also keep a record of the search terms that visitors type into the "search" box in the upper right corner of some pages on this site.

What I do with this Information
All of the information and metrics that I collect about users is anonymous in nature, and I use it to help me understand how people use my site. I do not, have not, and will never sell, rent, or otherwise make this information available to anyone outside of BrianMadden.com.

Information that you Provide
There are several areas of this site where visitors can provide information to me via web forms. (i.e. signing up for seminars, information requests, etc.) In all cases, I use the information only for the purposes clearly outlined in the form. I do not ever sell, rent, or make any names available to anyone outside of BrianMadden.com.
