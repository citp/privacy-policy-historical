Privacy Policy

Brick Planet Inc., or "Brick Planet" is committed to keeping any and all personal information collected of those that visit our website and make use of our services accurate, confidential, secure and private. This privacy policy was created and written to show our users and members what type of information we use, collect, and share.

1. Our web site collects various types of information, such as:
A. Voluntarily provided information which may include your name, address, email address, billing and/or credit card information ect., which may be used when you purchase products and/or services and to deliver the services you have requested.
B. Information automatically collected when visiting our website, which may include cookies, geographical information, third party tracking technologies, and server logs.

Please rest assured that our web site shall only collect personal information that you knowingly and willingly provide by way of surveys, completed membership forms, and emails. It is in the intent of our web site to use personal information only for the purpose for which it was requested and any additional uses specifically provided on our web site.

Brick Planet, Inc. may have the occasion to collect anonymous demographic information, such as the age or gender of our users. We may also gather information about the type of browser you are using, IP address, or type of operating system for informational and logging purposes.

It is highly recommended and suggested that you review this privacy policy frequently as a means to better understand the way in which our web site makes use of and shares information collected.
USE OF COLLECTED INFORMATION
1. Brick Planet, Inc. may collect and make use of personal information to assist in the operation of our web site and to ensure delivery of the services you need and request. At times, we may find it necessary to use personally identifiable information as a means to keep you informed of other possible products and services available on our web site. Brick Planet, Inc. may also be in contact with you with regards to completing surveys and/or research questionnaires related to your opinion of current or potential future services that may be offered.
2. Brick Planet, Inc. does not now, or will it in the future, sell, rent or lease any of our customer lists, names, and/or information to any third parties.
3. Brick Planet, Inc. may disclose your personal information, without prior notice to you, only if required to do so in accordance with applicable laws and/or in good faith belief that such action is deemed necessary or is required in an effort to:
A. Remain in conformance with any decrees, laws and /or statutes or in an effort to comply with any process which may be served upon Brick Planet, Inc. and/or our web site;
B. Maintain, safeguard and/or preserve all the rights and/or property of Brick Planet, Inc.; and
C. Perform under demanding conditions in an effort to safeguard the personal safety of users of our web site and/or the general public.
OPT OUT AND UNSUBSCRIBE
1. All users and/or members to our web site have the option to discontinue receiving communication from us and/or reserve the right to discontinue receiving communications by way of email or newsletters. Brick Planet, Inc. shall endeavor and shall take every precaution to maintain adequate physical, procedural and technical security with respect to our offices and information storage facilities so as to prevent any loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure or modification of the user's personal information under our control.
