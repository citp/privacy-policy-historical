We may track your visit for trends and statistics, but you will remain anonymous unless you tell us who you are. Once you choose to give us your personal information, you are not anonymous to us.   • Purchase

  • Participate in a promotion, contest or other website feature

  • Participate in online discussions

  • Modify your account or profile

In order to better understand your preferences and better serve you, we may combine information you give us online with other information from BrightNest sources, transactions and communications. This may include data from BrightNest stores, direct mail, events, or online marketing.
