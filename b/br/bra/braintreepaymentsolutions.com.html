Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy:

Your privacy is important to us. Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed to any third party. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our home page and at every page where personally identifiable information may be requested. If you have any questions regarding Braintree’s online privacy policy, please do not hesitate to ask us by sending an e-mail to privacy [at] getbraintree.com

In a few areas of our website, we may ask you to provide information that will enable us to enhance your site visit, and to assist you with technical support issues, etc. In each of those instances, we may ask for your name, e-mail address, phone number, address, customer preference information, as well as other similar personal information that is needed to register or authenticate you as a user.

We use e-mail addresses when exporting information to you, and when replying to any e-mail we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose. Braintree does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses we obtain to anyone. Braintree will not use, share, or sell personal information provided to us online to any outside organization without your consent. Braintree wants to keep your personal information accurate. If at any time you notice an inconsistency or inaccuracy in your personal information, please contact us at support [at] getbraintree.com
