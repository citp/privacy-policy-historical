# Info | Privacy Policy

## **Your privacy is important.**

The information you provide when placing an order is used only to complete your order. Brain Sync will NEVER, EVER share, sell, loan, rent or give your information to outside parties.

## **Data Security**

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, Brain Sync has appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure all information collected.

## **Email Subscriptions**

Your email address will NEVER be distributed, sold or rented to outside third parties. Email subscriptions are used only to fulfill your request to receive updates or notifications from Brain Sync.
