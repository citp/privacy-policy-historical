We respect your privacy. This is why we have taken the time to disclose our information collection practices and our privacy policy. Please take the time to review this document. 

We collect information for the following purpose(s):

  * Processing and delivering orders 
  * Completing or supporting an activity
  * Web site and system administration
  * Research and development
  * Research, analysis, reporting
  * Build records of individual visitors
  * Contacting our visitors (except via telephone) for promotion of the site or products (Optional) 



Who has access to the information we collect?  


  * Ourselves - To assist in the fulfillment of customer orders. 
  * Third party organizations purely for the purpose of processing orders (eg. Couriers, Payment Processing) 



Of the information we do collect, we allow our visitors the following access to their information:

  * Contact, Address and other information (such as :Name, Address, Email etc)
  * Order History
  * Returns History
  * Email Subscriptions (If applied for) 



If you believe that our web site has collected incorrect information or if you would like to dispute any information, please contact us using the address at the top of this page.
