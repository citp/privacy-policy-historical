privacy @ bzoink!

All personal information that you submit to bzoink.com will strictly be used only for bzoink.com. bzoink.com will not sell any of your information to a third party. The following is a list of the information we gather from you and what each piece of information is used for.

I. E-mail Address

We use your e-mail address for sending you your password when you forget it. We also use your e-mail address for sending you notifications that you have selected to receive. Your e-mail address will not even show up on your user profile.

II. Bio

Optional. Allows other users to know more about you. If you fill it in, other viewers will be able to view it. Do not release information that you do not want other users to see.

III. Cookies

BZOINK will place a 'cookie' on your computer if you request that we never forget your username and password. Cookies are very common and they're used practically everywhere. They're nothing to be worried about.

IV. Advertising

Any companies used for advertising on bzoink should be considered separate entities and not directly linked to bzoink.com. Any advertising companies that bzoink.com decides to use are not covered by this privacy policy.

V. Age Limit

All users are assumed to be 13 years old or older. Users younger than 13 years of age are not allowed to use the Site.

Have I forgotten something? Let me know at report@bzoink.com. If you have any questions about any item just send an e-mail.
