Privacy Statement

Internet:
Absent the use of encryption, the Internet is not a secure medium and privacy cannot be ensured. Internet e-mail is vulnerable to interception and forging. BMO Harris Private Banking cannot ensure the privacy and authenticity of any information or instructions you send to us or that we send to you over the Internet. BMO Harris Private Banking will not be responsible for any damages you may incur if you communicate confidential information to us over the Internet or if we communicate such information to you at your request.

BMO Harris Private Banking Client Information:
BMO Harris Private Banking takes the confidentiality of client information very seriously. This is a short summary of the unique features of BMO Harris Private Banking.

The BMO Harris Private Banking organization consists of three legal entities: Bank of Montreal, delivering banking services; BMO Trust Company, trust and estate services; and BMO Harris Investment Management Inc., investment management services. In addition, BMO Nesbitt Burns Inc. provides some custodial services to these companies.

In order to provide integrated services to accounts, these entities share personal client information with each other.

For new clients, personal client information will be shared among the BMO Harris Private Banking entities and within the Bank of Montreal Group of Companies for the purpose of identifying and providing information on products or services offered by any of those entities, solely with the consent of the new client.

Wills clients require special mention. BMO Harris Private Banking takes the confidentiality of the detailed personal information provided by will and estate planning clients very seriously. This information is kept in strict confidence and is shared with the solicitor drafting estate plan documentation further to a client’s consent.
