Privacy Policy

Collection and Use of Personal Information

This site does not collect private or personal information. If you give us your email address, it will be used only to send you information about new features and projects. It it not associated with any information you enter (e.g. weight and height).

Collection and Use of Non-Personal Information

We also collect non-personal information − data in a form that does not permit direct association with any specific individual. We collect information that people enter but save it so that it cannot be associated with email addresses, IP addresses, etc. We use such data to improve the project, detect bugs in the website, and to analyze aggregate statistical information about how the site is being used.

We also collect standard 'web analytics' about how the site is used. Again this is not associated with your email address should you provide one.

Cookies and other Technologies

We use cookies to improve your experience as you use the site, but we do not use them for long term or cross site tracking.
