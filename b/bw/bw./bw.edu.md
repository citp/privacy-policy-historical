# B-W: Privacy Policy

#### Privacy Policy Statement

This is the Web site of Baldwin-Wallace College, 275 Eastland Rd., Berea, Ohio 44017.

We can be reached via e-mail at [info@bw.edu](mailto:info@bw.edu) or by telephone at (440) 826-2900.

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes IP address.

We collect information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations, but no information on consumers who just browse our Web page.  The information we collect is used to customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual visitor and their identified interests.

If you receive e-mail communications from us and do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by or clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of the e-mail.  We do not share e-mail addresses with outside organizations. 

If you supply us with your postal address online you will only receive the information for which you provided us your address.

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers online will only receive telephone contact from us with information they have requested.

We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies.

Upon request we offer visitors the ability to have inaccuracies corrected in contact information.  Alumni, donors and friends of the College can have information corrected by sending us an e-mail at the above address, or filling submitting the online form at [_http://www.bw.edu/alumni/update/_](http://www.bw.edu/alumni/update/).

With respect to security: We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site. 

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above addresses or phone number.

Baldwin-Wallace College assumes no liability for issues, privacy or otherwise, associated with the use of these College resources.
