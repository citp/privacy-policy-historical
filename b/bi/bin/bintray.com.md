# 

JFrog Ltd. and its affiliates (“ **Company** ” or “ **we** ”) respect the privacy of its users (“ **User** ” or “ **you** ”) and are committed to protect the User’s information. We believe that you have a right to know our practices regarding the information we may collect and use when you visit or use our proprietary online website available at [http://www.bintray.com](http://www.bintray.com/) (the “ **Site** ”), our proprietary Bintray service which provides you, among other things, with an online cloud repository allowing you to access and/or make available for others certain Contributions (as defined in the Bintray Terms of Service available at <http://www.bintray.com/termsofservice>) (the “ **Bintray Service** ”). The Site and the Bintray Services shall be referred to, collectively and severally as the “ **Bintray** ”).

  1. **Your consent (important, please read carefully!)**

    * BY ACCESSING AND/OR USING BINTRAY AND/OR ANY PART THEREOF, YOU AGREE TO THE TERMS AND CONDITIONS SET FORTH IN THIS PRIVACY POLICY, INCLUDING TO THE COLLECTION AND PROCESSING OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION (AS DEFINED BELOW). 

    * **PLEASE NOTE : IF YOU DISAGREE TO ANY TERM PROVIDED HEREIN, YOU MAY NOT ACCESS OR USE THE REPOSITORY SOLUTION.**

  2. **Which information we may collect on our Users?**

We collect two types of data and information:

2.1. Non-personal Information. The first type is non-identifiable and anonymous information. To put it simply, we have no idea what is the identity of the User from which we have collected the Non-personal Information. Non-personal Information which is being gathered consists of technical information and behavioral information, as detailed below:

2.1.1. Technical information collected from your use of the Site:

    * Type of operation system 

    * Type of Browser/user agent 

    * Screen resolution 

    * Browser and keyboard language 

    * IP address 

    * Flash version 

2.1.2. Technical information collected from your use of the Software and/or the Bintray Service:

    * Type of build technologies 

    * Type of build server 

    * The number of servers you are using 

2.2. Personal Information. The other type of information we may collect is individually identifiable information. To put it simply, this information may identify an individual or is of a private and/or sensitive nature such as name and address. Personal Information which is being gathered consists of any personal details (e.g., e-mail address, social platforms profiles, organization, location, website etc.) provided voluntarily by you during registration to the Bintray and/or interaction with certain third party websites of your choice through which you may sign on to Bintray (such as Twitter, Google, GitHub, etc.) (the “ **Publishers** ”). We may also collect information from publicly available sources (including your public Account to the extent that it is defined therein as publically available per the applicable privacy choices, if any ), such as background user information (e.g., short bio, company/organization name etc.). We also allow you to store your SSH and GPG pass keys as part of your Account with Bintray, at your choice and responsibility.

  3. **How do we collect information on our Users?**  
We collect information mainly while you access or use the Site. In other words, when you access or use Bintray, including by viewing or accessing any User Generated Content or Contributions, we are aware of your usage of Bintray, and may gather, collect and record the information relating to such usage. For example, when you connect to Bintray, your computer tells us your IP address.  
Further, you may connect to Bintray through account with third-party Publishers). If you wish to log in exclusively via your Publisher’s Account (meaning - not to create a personal Bintray account), you may do so. In that case, the authentication mechanism relies on your direct communication with the applicable Publisher’s API and we receive confirmation from your Publisher that the Account is yours.. However, you may elect to open your own Bintray account, including by automatically filling out our registration form with your Publisher Account details.  
Lastly, you may wish to share with us certain other information about yourself, such as your codes, names of repository projects etc., by introducing such material voluntarily onto the Platform or by automatically importing your repository projects from your Social Platform Account. 

  4. **What are the purposes of the collection of information?**

4.1. Non-personal Information is collected in order to:

    * Enhance the User’s experience on the Site andor Bintray (e.g. by determining the screen resolutions). 

    * Learn about the preferences of Users and general trends on Bintray (e.g. understand which features and packages are more popular than others). 

4.2. Personal Information is collected in order to:

    * Enable and personalize the overall operation of the Bintray on your systems. 

    * Identify Commentators with their respective Comments on a particular Article. 

    * Identify you when conducting customer service operations (such as providing feedback, etc.). 

    * Allow you to register for special offers, contests or premium services. 

    * Metadata (as defined in the Terms of Use) is collected to enable the overall operation of Bintray. We may provide registered Users Metadata of persons who reviewed, used and/or accesses said registered User's Contributions including information regarding IP addresses of persons who reviewed, used and/or accessed the User's Contribution (as defined in the Terms of use).

Please note: When educing a person to view and/or use in any way your Contribution (e.g. by sending this person a link to your Contribution) You must inform this person that We collect this person's Metadata (such as IP address) and obtain their consent that we provide You with such information (should You choose to receive it). You hereby acknowledge and agree that obtaining the appropriate consent from any relevant person in connection with the Metadata information which you request us to provide to you is and shall remain your sole and exclusive responsibility and that you have indeed obtained any required consent in connection with Metadata you request from us. Without derogating from any of the above COMPANY HEREBY DISCLAIMS ANY LIABILITY AND/OR RESPONSIBILITY FOR ANY CLAIM LOSS OF DANAGE INCURRED BY YOU OTR ANY OTHER PARTY IN CONNECTION WITH THE COLLECTION AND/OR THE TRANSFER TO YOU OF ANY METADATA. 

  5. **Sharing Personal Information with Third Parties**  
We share Personal Information only in the following cases:  
(a) to satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process, subpoena or governmental request; (b) to enforce these Privacy Policy or the Terms of Service, including investigation of potential violations thereof; (c) to detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues; (d) to respond to claims that any content published on the Bintray violates any right or endangers the safety of any third-party; (e) to protect the rights, property, or personal safety of the Company or its affiliates and agents; (f) when Company is undergoing any change in control, including by means of merger, acquisition or purchase of all or substantially all of the assets of Company; (g) subject to your approval, in order to supply certain services you have requested from Company, if and as made available by the Company; and/or (h) for storage though customary services such as cloud platforms. 

  6. **Modifications to or Deletion of Personal Information**  
If for any reason you wish to modify or delete your Personal Information, you may contact us via e-mail to the following address [info@bintray.com](mailto:info@bintray.com). We will remove Personal Information from our servers reasonably after you delete your Bintray account. We reserve the right to maintain your contact details (e.g., your name and e-mail address) in case you have uploaded Contributions to Bintray. We reserve the right to keep one copy of your Bintray Account, for archival purposes. 

  7. **Cookies & Local Storage**  
When you access or use Bintray, Company may use industry-wide technologies such as “cookies” and Flash (or similar technologies), which stores certain information on your computer, your session (“ **Local Storage** ”) and which will allow us to enable automatic activation of certain features, and make your Bintray experience much more convenient and effortless. It is easy to prohibit the Local Storage. Most browsers will allow you to erase cookies from your computer’s hard drive, block acceptance of cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. In order to erase or disable the Local Storage option in Flash you should use the settings option of Flash according to the specific instructions provided by the technology provider. However, if you block or erase cookies, or change the settings of Flash, your online experience may be limited. 

  8. **Security**  
We take a great care in maintaining the security of the Bintray and your information and in preventing unauthorized access to it through industry standard technologies and internal procedures, including through the use of tokens and encryption mechanisms. However, we do not guarantee that unauthorized access will never occur. 

  9. **Third Party Websites**  
While using Bintray, you may encounter links to third party websites. Please be advised that such third party websites are independent from Bintray, and we assume no responsibility or liability whatsoever with regard to privacy matters or any other legal matter with respect to such third party websites. We encourage you to carefully read the privacy policies and the terms of use or service of such websites. 

  10. **Changes to the Privacy Policy**  
The terms of this Privacy Policy will govern the use of Bintray and any information collected therein. Company reserves the right to change this policy at any time, so please re-visit this page frequently. In case of any material change, we will make reasonable efforts to post a clear notice on Bintray. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective as of the stated “Last Revised” and your continued use of Bintray on or the Last Revised date will constitute acceptance of, and agreement to be bound by, those changes. 

  11. **Got any Questions?**  
If you have any questions (or comments) concerning this Privacy Policy, you are more than welcome to send us an email to the following address: [info@bintray.com](mailto:info@bintray.com) and we will make an effort to reply within a reasonable timeframe.
