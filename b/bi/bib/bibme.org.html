Privacy Policy

BibMe is committed to providing the utmost in privacy security. We do not sell nor trade any personal information obtained from the site.

BibMe does not collect personally identifiable information from anyone without his or her request. All e-mail correspondence is kept in strict privacy and will not be disclosed to parties other than BibMe.

BibMe collects user registration upon sign up. BibMe does not disclose user information to third parties without the express consent of the user.

When advertisers request, BibMe will provide aggregate information on the general makeup of the site's viewers. BibMe will not disclose any information that can be traced to a specific individual.

These policies are subject to change, so please make sure to check back periodically.

Although BibMe has a strict privacy policy, all interactions with the Internet pose some inherent dangers. Please be aware of these and conduct all your transactions with caution. Please be careful and responsible when you are surfing online.

Third Party Cookies

BibMe serves advertising, and ad networks may serve third party cookies that do their own tracking. You can opt out of third-party cookies either by blocking them from your browser or by visiting Network Advertising Initiative and opting out from the networks directly.
