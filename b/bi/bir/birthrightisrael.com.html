Privacy Policy

Birthright Israel, the Birthright Israel Foundation,Birthright Israel Excel and NEXT: A Division of Birthright Israel Foundation, are each committed to respecting your online privacy and security. We have developed this joint privacy policy to assure our web visitors that information provided to the websites of Birthright Israel will not be shared with any third party.

How Information is Collected and How Information is Used

On some of our website pages, contact information may be provided by visitors for project-related purposes, to receive notification of activities, programs, special events, and/or to make contributions. Birthright Israel, the Birthright Israel Foundation and Birthright Israel Next (collectively referred to as “Birthright Israel”) will use your information to understand your needs and to provide you with better service.

Specifically, we will use your information to help you complete a transaction, communicate back to you, update you on events, and to personalize our website for you. Credit card numbers are used only for donation or payment processing and are not retained for other purposes or stored in our records. We use the comments submitted by visitors to provide information requested, and we take seriously every suggestion regarding ways to improve communication.

No Sharing of Personal Information

Birthright Israel is committed to protecting the privacy of our donors. We will not sell, rent, lease, trade or share a donor's personal information with anyone else, nor send donor mailings on behalf of other organizations. We assure you that the identity of all who contact us through our websites will be kept confidential. Use of personal information will be limited to the internal purposes of Birthright Israel.

Security

Birthright Israel is committed to ensuring the security of your personal information. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the proper use of information, we have established and implemented appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

We use Internet encryption software, Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol when collecting or transferring sensitive data such as credit card information. Once your credit card information is received, it is accessible only to a small number of trusted, authorized staff who have been specially trained in processing this information.

Unsubscribing from our Mailing Lists

Our website gives you the opportunity to remove your name from our mailing list so you do not receive any future communications from us. If you wish to unsubscribe, please e-mail us at information@birthrightisrael.com or alumni@birthrightisrael.com or click “unsubscribe” on the bottom of our communication to you.

Contacting Us

If you have comments or questions about our privacy policy, please contact us.
