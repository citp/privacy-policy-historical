# BizBen.com Privacy Policy

We have the highest regard for your privacy. BizBen.com does not sell, rent, loan or transfer any personal information regarding visitors to any of our sites to any unrelated third parties. 

An example of personal information that may be collected includes full name, e-mail address, mailing address and phone number. When you subscribe to our E-mail Reports, we only require a valid e-mail address, so that we may send you the information you requested. 

Personal information you give us for either advertising or E-mail Reports is held with the utmost care and security and will not be used in ways to which you have not consented.
