Privacy Policy
Your right to privacy is something we take very seriously here at BizTeamUp. As such, we offer this clarification of what we do with the information we collect from you.

When we, as BizTeamUp, ask for customer information, it is done with the goal of improving the relationship between BizTeamUp and you and for meeting legal requirements for contest/sweepstakes entry.

When you provide your personal information to BizTeamUp (such as your name, address, phone number, email address, company name), we will not give or sell your individual information to any outside company for use in marketing or solicitation efforts. We will maintain the confidentiality of your personal information and it will be used only to support your customer relationship with BizTeamUp.

From time to time, a need will arise for BizTeamUp to partner with a third-party vendor to provide a service to our customers. Before we enter into any such relationships, we make every effort to ensure that their privacy policy is in accordance with ours.

Our intent in any email correspondence is (1) to share information that will enhance your experience as a BizTeamUp member and (2) to include only those individuals who have chosen to receive such emails. At any time you have the right to 'opt out' of receiving future BizTeamUp communications.

The above-stated policy is built around practices considered "industry-standard".
