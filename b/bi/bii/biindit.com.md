# Biindit - Ad Market For Everyone in South East Asia

**Gema Teknologi Cahaya Gemilang .Inc, operator of the Biindit.com website, provides an online advertising sale and display service for publishers of Websites.**

**A. USER INFORMATION**  
If you are an Advertiser or a Publisher, during your dealings with Biindit.com, Biindit.com may request any information related to your website and/or website and your use of Biindit.com’s ads. If you provide such information to Biindit.com, Biindit.com may collect and store such personal information. Biindit.com may collect your email addresses, mailing and billing address, IP address, information about your web site, including but not limited to your site’s page impressions,   
Biindit.com prices and data about your readers both current and historical. Biindit.com will collect information regarding your activities through the Service.   
Biindit.com will collect any comments you may send to Biindit.com and any information you may post to the Biindit.com site or a Publisher site. 

Biindit.com collects personal information when you:   
(1) register with Biindit.com to become a Publisher or Advertiser,   
(2) provide comments or submit a problem to Biindit.com or a Biindit.com Publisher,   
(3) request information from Biindit.com and provide your name or return contact information, or   
(4) respond to special offers, contests or surveys. As part of the registration process, you may be asked to provide contact information and other personally identifying information.

**B. SURVEYS**  
Biindit.com collects information through reader surveys published on websites under the Biindit.com network. Biindit.com retains the right to analyze, process, use and share this information with advertisers in order to segment the audience of each website in the hope of feeding relevant ads to website readers.

**C. PERSONAL IDENTITY INFORMATION**  
1) If you are a Publisher, we may share information about your Site, including but not limited to page impressions, site traffic, prices, information on publisher site readers/users and readership with Advertisers, Publishers or Users.   
2) If you are an Advertiser, we may disclosure your contact information and e-mail address to Publishers on whose Sites you have ordered advertisements.  
3) If you are a User, we may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information about you with Publishers or Advertisers such as the number of users who visit a Publisher site or the number of users who clicked on a particular advertisement. Such information does not identify you individually.  
4) Other Disclosures. Biindit.com may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or if Biindit.com has a good faith belief that such disclosure is necessary to protect and/or defend the rights and interests of Biindit.com. Biindit.com may, as permitted by applicable law, disclose your personally identifiable information to Publishers or Advertisers, or third parties in connection with an investigation of fraud, infringement, piracy or other unlawful activity and you expressly authorize Biindit.com to make such disclosures.  
5) Merger or Acquisition. If PT Gema Teknologi Cahaya Gemilang is merged or acquired by another entity, personally identifiable information may be transferred to such entity as a part of the merger or acquisition.

**D. REVIEW AND MODIFICATIONS TO YOUR PERSONALLY IDENTIFYING INFORMATION.**  
If you are a Publisher or Advertiser, you may add, modify or update your email address, name, mailing address and other contact information by accessing your account. It is your responsibility to ensure that the information you have on Biindit.com is the most up-to-date information as possible.   
Biindit.com may retain transactional history in order to document a transaction you may have had with a Biindit.com Publisher or Advertiser. 

**E. SECURITY**  
Biindit.com stores all information including personally identifiable information obtained through registration securely and considers it part of the assets of its business. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the appropriate use of information, Biindit.com has put in place physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information it collects online. However, data security is a constantly evolving art and science.   
You should consider any communication that you transmit to Biindit.com (such as data, questions or answers, comments, or suggestions) as non-confidential.
