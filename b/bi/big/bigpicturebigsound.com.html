Privacy Matters: Big Picture Big Sound

Privacy Statement

Big Picture Big Sound is committed to safeguarding your privacy online. Please read the following policy to understand how your information will be treated as you make full use of our Web site. This policy may change from time to time so please check back periodically.

IP Addresses and Cookies

Big Picture Big Sound currently identifies IP addresses and cookies (from visitors who allow cookies) in order to collect demographic and statistical information about visitors to our web site. This method does not store any personally identifiable information about you or share information with any third parties. We simply use it to track the number of visitors to our Web site, how these visitors found us and which pages are most popular on our Web site. 

Forum and Newsletter

Those visitors who sign up for our newsletter(s) and/or discussion forums will need to provide a valid e-mail address in order to receive newsletters or post messages in our discussion forums.  This information is not shared with any third party. 

E-mails to "Ask The Expert"

Visitors submitting questions to our staff via e-mail agree to have their first name and first initial of last name used in the article (if accepted for publication).  Your e-mail address will not be posted or published in any form for these questions. 

Outside Links

This site contains links to other Web sites. Big Picture Big Sound is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those Web sites.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please contact us at info@bigpicturebigsound.com.
