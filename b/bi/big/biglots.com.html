Providers.  We use third parties to operate the Site, collect information, conduct marketing and research, and perform other services for us.  We refer to those third parties collectively in this Agreement as our “Providers.”  Where we discuss our collection and use of information, we may use our Providers to collect and/or use the information on our behalf.

 

Collection of Personal Information.  We only collect personal information from you when you provide it to us.  For example, we collect personal information from you when you:  (1) sign up to receive our emails; (2) join the Buzz ClubTM; (3) participate in the Site’s interactive features, including those that invite you to submit an idea to us; (4) participate in a sweepstakes or contest; (5) participate in a survey, chat room, message board, blog or other forum; (6) make a purchase on the Site; or (7) contact us for any reason.  The information we collect may include your name, email address, mailing address, telephone number, payment information (such as credit card number), as well as the contents of your communications with us.  We may combine the information we collect from you with information we receive from you offline, as well as with information we receive about you from other sources, such as address update services. 

 

If you use any of the Site’s tell-a-friend features, we ask for your friend’s email address.  We use this information only to send the communication you requested and to monitor the effectiveness of our tell-a-friend features.

 

Collection of Non-Personal Information.  Similar to other commercial websites, we utilize “cookies” and web server logs to collect information about how the Site is used.  Cookies are small pieces of data that are stored by your browser on your hard drive.  Information gathered through cookies and web server logs may include your browser type, operating system, IP address, the date and time of visits, the pages and products viewed, time spent at the Site, the websites visited just before and after the Site and other clickstream information.  None of this information is associated with you as an individual.   We use this information to:  (1) conduct research, in order to improve the Site, our products and services and to better serve our customers; (2) provide you with customized content and advertising; and (3) monitor Site usage.  

 

We may also use web beacons on the Site and in our emails.  Web beacons are tiny graphic images that are used to collect information about your Site visit, such as the pages you view and the features you use, as well as information about whether you open and/or act upon one of our emails.  Web beacons help us analyze our Site visitors’ behavior and measure the effectiveness of the Site and our email campaigns.

Please keep your email address, log-in information and password in a safe place. We have no liability to you for a stolen or misappropriated email address, log-in information or password.
