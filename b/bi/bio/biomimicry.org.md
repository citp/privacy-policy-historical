# Biomimicry Institute - Privacy Policy

**This is the Web site of The Biomimicry Institute.**

We collect the domain name and geographic location, but not the e-mail address, of visitors to our Web site. In addition, we collect aggregate usage information on numbers of individuals visiting pages and length of viewing pages. The information collected may be used to improve the content of our Web site. Visitor addresses will not be shared with other organizations or used for commercial purposes. 

We do not sell, rent, give or lend personal information about our site visitors and/or clients to any advertisers. We disclose personal information only to the extent necessary to respond to your information request. For instance, if you subscribe to our newsletter your email address will be used only for that purpose. 

Although we do our best to protect your personal information, The Biomimicry Institute cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us, and you transmit information at your own risk. 

This Web site is linked to many other sites. Please be aware that as you leave our Web site in following those links, the privacy policy and data collection practices of those other Web sites apply.
