Your Privacy Is Important To Us

The Biltmore Company (“Biltmore”) is committed to your privacy and the protection of your Personal Data (as defined below in “Personal Data We Collect About You”). This Privacy Policy describes our privacy practices as required by the General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”) (EU) (2016/679) and other applicable data protection laws (collectively “Privacy Laws”). This Privacy Policy applies where Biltmore is acting as a Data Controller with respect to the Personal Data of our customers, website users, partners and service providers, in other words, where we determine the purposes and means of the processing of Personal Data.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, share, transfer, retain and protect your Personal Data and the rights you may exercise, where applicable, regarding your information. If you provide us with your Personal Data, or we obtain Personal Data about you from other sources, we treat your Personal Data according to this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy applies to Biltmore websites including Biltmore.com, apps, mobile apps dedicated social media accounts, etc. (“Sites”). This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party applications, products, services, websites or social media features that may be accessed through links on our Sites that have their own privacy policies. Accessing those links will cause you to leave our Sites and may result in the collection and/or sharing your Personal Data by a third party. We do not control, endorse or make any representations about third-party websites or their privacy practices, which may differ from ours. We encourage you to review the privacy Policy of any site you interact with before allowing the collection and use of your Personal Data.

Personal Data We Collect About You

Personal Data is any information which is related to an identified or identifiable natural person, including but not limited to name, identification number, location data, online identifier, email address, etc. The Personal Data we collect from customers, users of our Sites, partners and service providers helps us deliver services, manage our business, and personalize aa well as continually improve your Biltmore experience. We may collect Personal Data during every interaction with you including when you visit our Sites, stay with us on-property, inquire about our offerings by phone, provide services to us, etc. The types of Personal Data we may collect, and the activities where data is collected, include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Postal address
  • Telephone number
  • Credit and debit card number or other payment data
  • Driver’s license or other government-issued identification data
  • Important dates, such as birthdays, anniversaries and special occasions
  • Employer details
  • Travel itinerary, tour group or activity data
  • Prior stay information, goods and services purchased, special service and amenity requests
  • Geolocation information
  • Customer preferences and personalized data (“Personal Preferences”), such as your interests, activities, hobbies, food and beverage choices, services and amenities of which you advise us or which we learn about during your visit such as spa preferences, equipment rentals and child care
  • Language preference
  • Data about family members and companions, such as names and ages of children
  • Images and video and audio data from security cameras located in public areas; and (b) body-worn cameras carried by our staff and security personnel
  • Personal Data about you from other sources and add it to your account information
  • We may conduct surveys and promotions that collect your Personal Data. Our surveys and promotions are voluntary and any information you provide may be collected, used, shared, disclosed, stored, transferred, and processed in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Your participation in our surveys and promotions, and your submission of information, serves as your consent to these practices
Biltmore, our service providers, and other third parties may collect the following information about you through automated means (such as Cookies):
  • IP address
  • Location Information
  • Browser type and language, access time, and referring website address
  • The pages you view within our Sites and other actions you take while visiting us
  • Information related to whether you've opened an e-mail or clicked on a link contained in an e-mail
  • If you are referred to our Sites from another source (an advertising site, a blog, a social media site, etc.), we may collect data about the sites which referred you.
  • Some features of our Sites allow users to make data public or engage in other sharing activities. For example, if you post content or otherwise participate in discussion forums or other social areas of our Sites, your participation (including the content you post, your name, and a link to your profile) may be shared across our Sites and in other public or private areas of the internet

Collecting information through automated means allows us to collect statistics about the usage and effectiveness of our Sites, personalize your experience on our Sites, and tailor our interactions with you. For details regarding the technologies we employ, see our Cookies Policy here, and the section below regarding Cookies, Web Beacons and Similar Technologies.

How We Use Your Personal Data and Legal Bases for Processing

Biltmore collects and processes the Personal Data of customers, website users, partners and service providers to provide on-property, online, reservation, customer support, product purchasing, membership program, etc. services (“Services”). In some instances, we request that you provide certain Personal Data, or other data (such as browser data, device information, data collected through Cookies, aggregated data, etc.), to us directly. If you do not provide the data that we request, or prohibit us from collecting such data, we may not be able to provide all the Services that we offer or you request.

We may process your Personal Data for any reason that is permitted under data protection laws in accordance with this Privacy Policy, which may include the purposes below. Please note that the legal bases outlined below are required to be included under European Union (EU) data protection laws, but are not required to be included if you do not reside in the EU.

We use Personal Data and other data for our legitimate business interests, including the following:

  • Provide the Services You Request. We use Personal Data and other data to provide the Services you request, including to facilitate reservations, payment, administrative communications, reservation confirmations and pre-arrival messages, meetings and events, reservation payment processing, customer service and support, to send electronic receipts, etc. If you provide an email address when making a reservation, we will use that email address to send you a confirmation. If you make a reservation for another person using your email address, that person's confirmation will be emailed to you, as well. The legal basis for this processing is to provide the Services or products you have requested and our legitimate interests in the proper administration of our Sites/business.
  • E-mail. We may ask you for your email address when you make a reservation with us or purchase Services or products from us. The primary purpose for collecting your email address is to send you a sales or reservation confirmation. Where permitted by applicable laws, when you transact with us we will automatically send you marketing email about offers and promotions that may be of interest to you. Our confirmation of your email address, and every subsequent marketing email that is sent to you, will allow you to unsubscribe from our marketing communications. Please note that even if you unsubscribe from marketing email messages, we may still email you regarding your account and your transactions or if there is an emergency or situation that requires us to alert you. The legal basis for this processing is to provide the Services or products you have requested (i.e., it is necessary for the performance of a contract with you) and our legitimate interests in the proper administration of our Sites/business or your consent where required.
  • Personalize Our Services. We use Personal Data and other data to personalize our Services and improve your Biltmore experience, including when you: (a) visit on-property; (b) use our Sites; (c) request concierge services; (d) receive promotions and offers from us, and; (e) when we send you marketing communications. The legal basis for this processing is to provide the Services or products you have requested (i.e., it is necessary for the performance of a contract with you) and our legitimate interests in the proper administration of our Sites/business or your consent where required.
  • Recruitment and Employment Purposes. We use Personal Data in our employment and recruitment processes, including in the context of applicant selection and recruitment. The legal basis for this processing is our legitimate interests in the proper administration of our business/Sites, including legal compliance, and to make the best recruitment decisions. In certain circumstances, we will ask for your consent when processing your Personal Data related to employment and recruitment. You have the right to withdraw this consent at any time.
  • Business Purposes. We use Personal Data and other data for security and fraud prevention and monitoring, data analysis, audits, card keys, and other security processes and systems. The legal basis for this processing is our legitimate interests, namely the proper protection of our business against risks.
  • Membership Programs. We use Personal Data and other data to enroll you in our membership program and manage your account. We also use Personal Data and other data to send you offers, promotions (either directly or through our business partners) and provide information regarding your account status and activities. The legal basis for this processing is to provide the Services or products you have requested (i.e., it is necessary for the performance of a contract with you) and our legitimate interests in the proper administration of our Sites/business or your consent where required.
  • Surveys, Contests and Promotions. We use Personal Data and other data to invite you to participate in surveys, contests and promotions and to manage these activities. These activities have additional rules and may contain additional information about how we use and disclose your Personal Data related to the specific activity. We suggest that you read the rules related to each activity carefully. The legal basis for this processing is to provide the services or products you have requested and our legitimate interests in the proper administration of our Sites/business or your consent where required.

Where we are rely on consent to process your Personal Data, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. However, this will not affect the lawfulness of any processing carried out before you withdraw your consent. If you withdraw your consent, we may not be able to provide the Services to you.

How We Share Your Personal Data

Information about our customers, website users, partners and service providers is an important part of our business. We do not sell your Personal Data. We may share your Personal Data with third parties in certain circumstances including:

  • On-property Services. We may share your Personal Data with third-party service providers of on-property services including spa, childcare, sports or dining experiences.
  • Promotional Offers. We may we send offers to selected groups of Biltmore customers on behalf of other businesses. When we do this, we do not give that business your Personal Data, instead we send you offers on their behalf.
  • Third Party Service Providers. We do not sell your Personal Data. We share your Personal Data with third party service providers who provide services or functions on our behalf. These third party vendors include credit card processing companies, transactional processing partners, booking agents, license partners, event and meeting organizers, business analytics platforms, customer service organizations, advertising providers (for the purposes of ad targeting, remarketing and customizing ad content), business partners, distributers of surveys or sweepstakes programs, and fraud prevention firms
  • Future Business Transactions. As we continue to develop our business, we may sell, buy, restructure or reorganize our business or assets, or cease certain activities. In the event of a sale, restructuring, merger, acquisition, divestiture, etc., of all or part of our business, Biltmore may transfer, sell or assign information, including, without limitation, Personal Data, to affiliated or unaffiliated third parties in connection with these business transactions. To the extent that local laws require it, we will provide notice of our intent to transfer Personal Data to a third party for this purpose.
  • Public Forums. Some features of our Sites allow users to make data public or engage in other sharing activities. For example, if you post content, reviews, or otherwise participate in discussion forums, blogs, product reviews, or other social areas of our Sites, your participation (including the content you post, your name, and a link to your profile) may be shared across our Sites and in other public or private areas of the internet.
  • Insurers and Advisors. We may disclose your Personal Data to our insurers and professional advisers as reasonably necessary for the purposes of obtaining or maintaining insurance coverage, managing risks, obtaining professional advice, or the establishment, exercise or defense of legal claims, whether in court proceedings or other legal proceedings.
  • Protection of Biltmore and Others. In addition to the specific disclosures of Personal Data set out in this section, we may disclose your personal data; (a) where such disclosure is necessary for compliance with a legal obligation to which we are subject, (b) to enforce our Terms of Service, this Privacy Policy, or other contracts with you, including investigation of potential violations thereof; (c) to respond to claims that any content violates the rights of third parties; (d) to respond to your requests for customer service; and/or (e) to protect the rights, property or personal safety of Biltmore, its agents and affiliates, its users and/or the public. This includes exchanging Personal Data with other companies and organizations for fraud protection, spam/malware prevention, or in order to protect your vital interests or the vital interests of another natural person.
Sensitive Data

Cookies, Web Beacons and Other Technologies

Our Sites use Cookies. Cookies are text files containing small amounts of information which are downloaded to your device when you visit a website. Cookies are useful because they allow our Sites to recognize your device. Cookies help with a variety of functions including allowing users to “log in” to websites, making your navigation of websites more efficient, (such as remembering your password), and generally improving your experience on our Sites. Cookies can also help ensure that the advertisements and content you see online are more relevant to you and your interests. We may also include web beacons in marketing e-mail messages in order to determine whether messages have been opened and links contained within clicked on.

Some of our business partners set web beacons and Cookies on our Sites or we set Cookies for them. In addition, third-party social media buttons may collect certain information such as your IP address, location information, browser type and language, access time, and referring website addresses. Further, if you are logged in to those social media sites, they may also link such collected information with your profile information on that site.

If you would like to opt-out of Cookies, visit www.aboutcookies.org or www.allaboutcookies.org for further information. These websites also tell you how to set your browser not to accept Cookies and how to remove Cookies from your browser. Please note, some of the features on our Sites may not function, and/or you may be blocked from all or parts of our Sites, as a result of disabling or rejecting Cookies. More information on our use of Cookies can be found in our Cookies Policy.
