Privacy Policy 

At BillMatrix, a subsidiary of Fiserv, we are committed to protecting your privacy online. Our goal is to safeguard any personal information you provide to us. Personal information includes data that reasonably can be used to identify or describe an individual, such as name, address, phone number, and e-mail address. 

Overview 

BillMatrix may collect basic information about site visitors through our Internet access logs in order to learn about which pages and information our Web site visitors access and how often. We also collect user information when you voluntarily submit it to us. Users may contact us directly by [sending a message](mailto:contact@billmatrix.com). When we collect this type of information, we intend to use the information to determine how we can meet your needs and best respond to your requests. It is completely up to you whether or not you want to provide this information. 

Cookies

The BillMatrix web site may minimally uses cookies to assign a "User I.D." Cookies are small files of information, which save specific information about your visit. The cookies may be used only to assist in producing overall site visitor statistics and do not serve to describe individual users. Cookies, by themselves, cannot be used to find out the identity of any user. While cookies are not required in other areas of BillMatrix.com, they are required in the Career Opportunities section since they allow an applicant's profile information to carry from screen to screen. If you are uncomfortable regarding the use of cookies, you can disable them on your computer by changing the settings in your browser but you may experience diminished or nonfunctioning navigation within the Careers section. 

BillMatrix will not sell or distribute user information to third- parties. If you have voluntarily submitted user information to us through an e-mail or contact form, BillMatrix maintains your security by ensuring the information is only distributed within Fiserv. This enables us to respond to your request about a product or service. If you subscribe to an e-mail list, you will be given instructions on how to remove yourself from that list. 

The BillMatrix web site provides links to other third party web sites. Even if the third party is affiliated with Fiserv through a business partnership or otherwise, Fiserv is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices or the content of such external links. These links are provided to you for convenience purposes only, and you access them at your own risk.

Questions   
If you have a question about our privacy policy, you may contact us at [privacy@BillMatrix.com](mailto:privacy@billmatrix.com)[](mailto:info@billmatrix.com).
