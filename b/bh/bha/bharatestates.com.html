Privacy and Security Policy

BharatEstates takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

What this Privacy Policy Covers

  • This Privacy Policy covers BharatEstates treatment of personally identifiable information that BharatEstates collects when you are on the BharatEstates site, and when you use BharatEstates services. This policy also covers BharatEstates treatment of any personally identifiable information that BharatEstates business partners share with BharatEstates.
  • This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that BharatEstates does not own or control or to people that BharatEstates does not employ or manage.

Information Collection and Use

  • BharatEstates collects personally identifiable information when you register for a BharatEstates account, when you use certain BharatEstates services, when you visit BharatEstates pages, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. BharatEstates may also receive personally identifiable information from our business partners.
  • When you register with BharatEstates, we ask for your name, email address, birth date, and personal interests. Once you register with BharatEstates and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.
  • BharatEstates also automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, BharatEstates cookie information and the page you requested.
  • In addition, when you use BharatEstates services, you may make some of your personal information public. If you post personal information online that is accessible to the public, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return and BharatEstates will not be responsible for the use or misuse of such information.
  • BharatEstates uses information for three general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, to fulfill your requests for certain products and services promoted by BharatEstates or other companies, and to contact you about specials and new products. BharatEstates is not responsible for the accuracy, completeness veracity and reliability of any advertising and content, products and services which may be accessed or advertised to you and BharatEstates will not be liable for any loss or damage arising out of the same.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • BharatEstates will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.
  • BharatEstates will send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when:
    • We have your consent to share the information.
    • We need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested.
    • We need to share the information with certain service partners in order to respond to your comments or to resolve service issues. (Unless we tell you differently, these partners have no independent right to use this information except to respond to your comments or to resolve the service issues.).
    • We respond to requests from any Indian or foreign government, security, defence, revenue, regulatory or other authority, agency or officer.
    • We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process.
    • We find that your actions on our web sites violate the BharatEstates Terms of Service or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services.

Cookies

  • BharatEstates may set and access BharatEstates cookies on your computer and you may not be able to disable these cookies. BharatEstates ensures that all information collected and accessed by BharatEstates through the access of these cookies will be maintained in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to BharatEstates' cookies.
  • BharatEstates uses web beacons to access our cookies within our network of web sites and in connection with BharatEstates products and services.

Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information and Preferences

  • BharatEstates gives you the ability to edit your BharatEstates Account Information and preferences at any time, including whether you want BharatEstates to contact you about specials and new products. BharatEstates is not responsible and will not be liable for the accuracy, completeness, veracity and reliability of any information including any customized information which may be accessed by or provided to you, which could vary depending on your BharatEstates Account Information and preferences including your browser preferences.

Security

  • In certain areas BharatEstates uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

  • BharatEstates may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages.
