# Privacy Policy of Bhanvad.com

Bhanvad.com is committed to protecting your privacy. We want your time with us spent searching website / information you need, save favorite sites with us and not worrying about your privacy. Therefore, we have an extremely simple policy.

Bhanvad.com since we are strongly committed to your right to privacy, we have drawn out a privacy statement with regard to the information we collect from you.

When your register / sign up for a Bhanvad.com membership to gain additional functionality, we may ask you to provide your email address, last name (surname) and other optional information such as country (location), profession (occupation), interests, and developer skills. We will NOT disclose, sell or exchange your personally identifiable information to or with anyone.
