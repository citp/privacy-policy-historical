Privacy Policy

The 'Privacy Policy' of Bharatonline.com is intended to provide the users of the website the terms associated with the usage and protection of all the personal information that has been collected from them. By 'personal information', we mean any kind of information that can be associated with a particular individual. This includes his/her name, address, telephone number, e-mail ID, credit card number, and other such details. We, at Bharatonline, do not indulge in selling or renting out of personal information, belonging to our users, to any third party.

We will share personal information about you with other companies/parties/people when:
  • We have your full consent to share the information;
  • We have to share your information for providing products or services, which you have requested.
  • There is need to share the information with service partners/agents in order to respond to your comments or to resolve service issues. (These partners/agents have no right to use the information except for responding to your comments or to resolve the service issues.);
  • We have to answer/respond to requests from any Indian or foreign government agency/authority or officer; or
  • We have to respond to orders of court or during legal process/matters.
We would also like to inform you that our Privacy Policy is not totally invariable. There may be changes in it from time to time. You are advised to check the same on a regular basis. However, when you do provide us with your personal information, the policy existing at that time will come into effect. Any changes made by us in the Privacy Policy after the exchange will be ineffective in that particular context. Bharatonline may have links connecting with the other websites also. When you click on these links, you enter another website and from that moment onwards, Bharatonline holds no responsibility whatsoever. You are requested to see the policy of that particular website and act accordingly.

We don't track what individual members read, but rather try to determine how well each page performs. This helps us continue to provide better services to you.
