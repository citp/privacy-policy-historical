**1\. WHAT KIND OF INFORMATION WE COLLECT**

We collect the following types of information below through your use of the Application, the Bump Services and/or the Site. 

**Personal Information.** "Personal Information" is information that identifies you or another person, which may be transmitted or received when you use the Application, the Bump Services and/or the Site. Personal Information includes names, physical addresses, email addresses, telephone and fax numbers, files, content, photographs, and other information you transmit or receive using the Application, the Bump Services and/or the Site which identifies you or another person. 

We collect Personal Information if you provide it directly to us via email, the Application, the Bump Services or the Site. We may collect Personal Information when you send us messages, comments, questions or suggestions or if you participate in blogs, forums, message boards, chat rooms, profile pages and other services to which you are able to post information, if any, on the Site (collectively, "Forums"). 

**Non-Personal Information.** "Non-Personal Information" is information that does not identify you or any other individual, and includes Session and Usage Data, Log Data and Aggregate Information, each as described below.

_Session and Usage Data._ "Session and Usage Data" is information about your use of the Application, the Bump Services and/or the Site andÂ includes, without limitation, connection and service-related data submitted to us through your use of the Application, the Bump Services or the Site. Session and Usage Data includes information relating to the connection request, server communication and data sharing, including networkÂ measurements, quality of service, and date, time and location of the bump. We also may aggregate Session and Usage data as described in Aggregate Information below. Please note that Session and Usage Data does not include any Personal Information nor does it include Content you may transmit or share through the use of the Application (such as photos, contacts, calendar appointments, etc.), the Bump Services, or the Site. 

_Log Data._ When you interact with us or use the Application, the Bump Services or our Site, our systems may automatically collect your unique User Device number, the IP address, type of browser or operating system you use, and the dates and times of your use ("Log Data"). 

_Aggregate Information._ "Aggregate Information" is data we collect about a group or category of services or Users from which individual User identities have been removed. In other words, information on how you use the Application, the Bump Services and the Site may be collected and combined with information about how others use the Application, the Bump Services and the Site, but no personally identifiable information will be included in the resulting Aggregate Information. Aggregate Information helps us understand trends in our Users' needs so that we can better consider new features or otherwise tailor the Application, the Bump Services and the Site.

**9\. OUR POLICY TOWARD CHILDREN UNDER 13 YEARS OF AGE**

Our Services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. If you become aware that your child under 13 has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us at [privacy@bu.mp](mailto:privacy@bu.mp). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we take steps to remove such information and terminate the child's account. By using the Application, the Services or the Site, you are representing to us that you are not under the age of 13. If you are under 13 years of age, please do not use the Application and immediately delete it from your User Devices. We recommend that minors, who are 13 years old but under 18 years old, ask their parents or legal guardians for permission before sending any information about themselves to us or anyone else, whether by using the Application, the Bump Services or the Site.
