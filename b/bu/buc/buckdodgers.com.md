# United Kingdom - BuckDodgers Free Classifieds - Post Anything

## Privacy Policy

The following data is collected and stored:   
**Personal Information**

We may collect and use email addresses, IP addresses or any other personal information for our own internal use. Your personal information will not be shared with any third party. However, we may disclose your email address, IP address, etc. if required by law or court order. 

**Cookies**

Session cookies are stored in temporary memory and usually are not retained after the browser is closed. Session cookies do not collect information about your browsing activity from your computer. They typically store information in a way that does not personally identify the user.
