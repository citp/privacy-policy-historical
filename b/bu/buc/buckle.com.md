# Security/Privacy Polcy

|  |      
We care about the privacy of our guests. When you provide us with information about yourself, The Buckle, Inc. may use that information for our own internal marketing purposes. We may request information from you about your fashion tastes, your hobbies or your music interests. This information will be collected from you directly. Using the information you give us, we may contact you with information or special offers. We will not require you to disclose more information that is reasonable necessary to participate in an activity as a condition or participation. The Buckle, Inc. will not sell or disclose personal information to outside third parties. If you do not wish to be contacted by us in the future, simply reply to the unwanted e-mail message and type "Remove" in the subject reference line.

We value our relationship with our guests of all ages. We welcome parents and their children to explore our site together and participate in our online contests, Buckle Talk e-mails and other promotional activities. In accordance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act, we are requesting that any guest who is under 13 years of age provide us with the name and e-mail address of a parent or guardian whom we may contact in order to obtain consent prior to collecting or using any personal information from such pre-teen guest. The Buckle, Inc. will contact the parent or guardian via e-mail and request his/her consent for the pre-teen’s registration and The Buckle, Inc.’s internal use of such information. A parent or guardian may view the pre-teen’s registration information, ask to have it deleted and/or refuse to allow any further collection or use of the pre-teen’s information. Any such requests should be directed to Webmaster@buckle.com.

If you have any questions regarding our Privacy Policy or any other aspect of our site, please contact us. The following is the name and contact information of our Webmaster who is responsible for collecting and maintaining our database of information:
