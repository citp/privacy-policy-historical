Privacy Policy




Collected Information and Use

We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. We use cookies in the forums and chat room to deliver pages customized to your preferences.

When contacting us via the contact forms on our website, an email address is required. This is used to respond to your message.

When signing up for our newsletter (mailing list), an email address is required. This is used to send the newsletter emails to you.

When registering for our forums/chat, a valid email address is required to register. This is used to send you your password when you register, to send you your password again if you should ever forget it, to notify you of replies or private messages if you enable these options, and for the administration to email you with important information if necessary.

BuffyGuide.com will not rent, sell, or give away any email addresses subscribed to our mailing list, registered at our forums, or provided when contacting us via any method.

BuffyGuide.com will not disclose your name, email address or any other personal details supplied by you except in the following circumstances:
    a) You agree that we may pass on your details or disclose your identifying data, other information or content to any law enforcement or regulatory authority in order to comply with their direction or request in regard to legal process,
    b) You further agree that we may disclose your identifying data, other information or content to a third party in response to any claim of aggrievement or claim of violation of a third party's rights.

Opting Out

If you are subscribed to our newsletter (mailing list), you can unsubscribe at any time, and your email address will not be saved or used in any way.

If you are registered for our forums/chat, you can request at any time to have your forum/chat registration deleted. This will cause all of your related personal information to be deleted from the database, including your contact email address, none of which will be saved or used in any way. Note that having your registration deleted does NOT delete any posts or messages you made while registered. If you wish to delete any contents of your posts, you should do so BEFORE requesting to have your registration deleted.

Protection of Data

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. While there is no such thing as "perfect security" on the Internet, we will take all reasonable steps to insure the safety of your personal information. All of the data regarding users, in relation to forum/chat registration, are stored on a server. That server is owned and hosted by Rackspace.com, and leased by BuffyGuide.com. This server is located in a locked and guarded space and only authorized Rackspace personnel have physical access to it. Only the BuffyGuide.com webmaster, and authorized Rackspace personnel, have remote access to the server and the data stored on it.
