## Collected Information

In the registration process, we collect registration information from our users, including name, user name, and password, and contact information such as email and mailing address. Each of these different types of information is used to provide hotels and guests with access to a rich collection of on-line resources, including without limitation business web page hosting services, digital marketing services, on- and off-line advertising services, promotional pricing services, and the ability to contact merchants from whom hotels and guests seek services (collectively, the "Service"). Registration information is used to authenticate users and recall their profile pages, and contact information is used to provide information on Service updates.

In the operation of the Service, we collect and store hotel and guest information in order to provide communications and other services. This data is gathered from a variety of sources and enables us to prepare hotel pages, provide services to hotels and guests with minimal data entry, etc. buuteeq will continue to make your data available to you on the buuteeq platform as long as you are a user according to the terms and conditions of the buuteeq Terms of Service (as amended form time to time) (the "Terms of Service") ? and in some cases, as set forth in the Terms of Service, up to one hundred eighty (180) days after. Please consult the Terms of Service as well as this Privacy Policy to understand your rights and responsibilities as a user of the buuteeq platform.

Information such as your hotel name, screen name, full name, profile photo, and any personally identifiable information you have included in your reviews may be shown by users to their friends or other users primarily in conjunction with reviews or other content you have contributed. In addition, we may sell your reviews to third parties, in which case your provided information (e.g., screen name, first name and last initial, profile photo, city/state/neighborhood, and any personally identifiable information you have included in your reviews) may be shown to users of such third party web sites.

Your full name and email address may also be used when you send an email notification to a friend (such as in an invitation, or when sharing a review). We may also use your email address to send you updates or news regarding the buuteeq Site, but you may choose to not receive email of this type by accessing the "Account Settings" tab of the buuteeq Site and updating your notification settings. Additionally, we use your email address to contact you on behalf of your friends (such as when someone sends you a personal message).

You should be aware that when you voluntarily disclose personally identifiable information (e.g., user name, e-mail address) via reviews, messages, forums, postings, or in other areas of the buuteeq Site, that information, along with any substantive information disclosed in your communication, can be collected and correlated and used by third parties and may result in unsolicited messages from other posters or third parties. Such activities are beyond the control of buuteeq. PLEASE DO NOT POST ON THE BUUTEEQ SITE OR ANY OTHER AREAS ANY PERSONAL INFORMATION THAT YOU EXPECT TO KEEP PRIVATE.

If you would like to remove your information from the buuteeq Site, you can do so by deleting your account under "Account Settings." If your information has been indexed by a search engine (e.g., Google.com), please note that it may take some time for such search engine to remove it from it index, and that such removal from the search engine is outside of buuteeq?s control.

buuteeq may also collect and analyze from visitors and users certain information regarding the use of the Service and the buuteeq Site activity. Information collected may include but is not limited to the buuteeq Site traffic volume, frequency of visits, type and time of transactions, type of browser and operating system, IP address, requested URL, referring URL, etc. This information is logged to help diagnose technical problems, administer the buuteeq Site, and improve the quality and types of Service delivered. In making a payment transaction on the buuteeq Site, you agree to abide by the applicable terms of service and policies.

## Cookies

Like most websites, buuteeq and the buuteeq Site require cookies to function properly. Cookies are small text files that reside on a user's computer and identify you as a unique user. We use cookies to refine the buuteeq Site and simplify the user experience. For example, the use of cookies allows registered users to enter the buuteeq Site without explicitly typing in their email address and password every time. You should be aware that buuteeq cannot control the use of cookies by advertisers or any information advertisers may gather from their use of cookies. If you do not want information collected through the use of cookies, there is a simple procedure in most browsers that allows you to deny or accept the cookie feature; however, you should note that cookies may be necessary to provide you certain features (e.g., customized delivery of information) available on the buuteeq Site. For example, Google, as a third party vendor to buuteeq, uses cookies to serve advertisements on the buuteeq Site. Google?s use of its DART cookie enables it to serve ads to you and other buuteeq users based on your and other buuteeq?s users? visits to the buuteeq Site and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the ad and content network privacy policy at http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/index.aspx

Firewalls and other security technologies are employed to prevent interference or access from outside intruders; however, buuteeq cannot guarantee the security of user account information. Unauthorized entry or use, hardware or software failure, and other factors may compromise the security of user information at any time.
