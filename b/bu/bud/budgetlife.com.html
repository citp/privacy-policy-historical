Information Collection and Use

We collect information at this website on our own behalf, and on behalf of our customers, so that they may respond to our users' requests for assistance.  We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement.  We collect personally identifiable information at several different locations on our website:
 

Insurance Request
When requesting assistance from a life or health insurance agent, a user is required to give their contact information (such as name, phone, and email address).  This information is used to contact the user to provide the requested assistance.  It is optional for the user to provide additional information (such as age, gender, and health information), but encouraged so the agent can more readily respond to the user's request for assistance. All Insurance Request information is shared with one and only one agent, a third party customer of Budget Life, so that the agent can provide the requested assistance. 

 
Life Insurance Agent Specials
When requesting information about our agent special offers, a user is required to give their contact information (such as name, phone, and email address).  This information is used to contact the user to provide the requested assistance.

 
Contact and Help Requests
When filling out our contact or help request forms, a user is required to give their contact information (such as name, phone, and email address).  This information is used to contact the user to provide the requested assistance.


Cookies
A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored an your computer's hard drive.  We do not use cookies, unless you arrived at our site from one of our affiliate sites, in which case we may use a cookie to record the web site from which you arrived. This cookie is only used to give the proper credit to the web site that referred you to our site. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site.


Log Files
We use Internet industry standard practices using IP addresses to track visits, analyze trends, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.  IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.


Links
This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites.  We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information.  This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site.
