Privacy Policy

At Budovideos.com, we respect your privacy on the Internet. Because we gather certain types of information about our customers, we feel that you should understand the terms and conditions of our retrieval and use of that information.

Security Measures

Budovideos.com is committed to protecting your private information. To make your shopping experience more convenient, we gather address and payment information from you. We use the most advanced technologies to prevent any unauthorized use of your personal information. Passwords remain encrypted during transmission. Any credit information supplied by our customers are transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology. Budovideos.com has taken every known measure to make your shopping with us safe and easy.

Cookies

Budovideos.com uses "cookies" to assign a unique identification to your computer. Cookies are files that your web browser places on your computer's hard drive. These files allow you to easily access your customer data on our site. They also save you time by tracking your order information so that you do not have to re-enter this information upon every visit. Budovideos.com does not and cannot use cookies to retrieve other unauthorized personal information about you from your computer.

Orders

Budovideos.com uses an Internet order form for all customers to purchase products on our website. Each credit card and payment information will only be used for such an order. Under no circumstances will Budovideos.com use your credit card and address information for any other use other than to process your order. BUDOVIDEOS.COM WILL NEVER ASK FOR YOUR CREDIT INFORMATION so please do not respond if you receive any such requests via e-mail, telephone, or any other methods.

Privacy Policy Changes

Budovideos.com may update this policy. If we do, we will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
