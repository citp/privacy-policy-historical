# BulletAds

BulletAds/We are committed to provide both clients and end-users with the utmost in privacy and security. We abide by all industry guidelines and consistently monitor our management and operating methods in order to maintain our valued reputation and integrity. This is our promise to our clients, and end-users. 

The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: [http://www.bulletads.com](http://www.bulletads.com/)

**Information Collected and Use**

BulletAds.com is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. We will ask for name, postal address, phone number, e-mail address, Web site title, Web site owner, company name, social security number or tax identification number. Providing personally identifiable information is strictly voluntary. 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. 

Our site uses cookies to make sure you don't see the same ad repeatedly, and to track leads. 

Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information, unique identifiers, The customer's contact information is used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Unique identifiers (such as social security numbers) are collected for use as account numbers in our record system. 

This site contains links to other sites. [http://www.bulletads.com](http://www.bulletads.com/) is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

**Security  
**This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Your account is protected by a unique user name, and password.
