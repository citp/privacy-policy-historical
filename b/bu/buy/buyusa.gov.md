# U.S. Commercial Service Your Global Business Partner: Privacy and Security

## Privacy

Thank you for visiting a U.S. Commercial Service (US&FCS), United States Department of Commerce web site and reviewing our privacy policy. The US&FCS promotes U.S. exports and protects U.S. business interests abroad. This requires collection of company data and, in some cases, personal information. Here is how the US&FCS will handle information we gather about you from your visit. US&FCS will protect the collected information pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974 and the Freedom of Information Act, as applicable. If you visit us, we will collect and store non-personal information (i.e.: the name of domain from which you access the Internet; the date and time you access our site; the Internet address of the web site from which you linked directly to our site; and the information you view while visiting our web site) about your visit for internal statistical purposes. We may also collect personal information, such as a home address, home phone number, or personal email address if you provide it to us in an E-mail or a request for information, on a registration form, or on a feedback or comment form on some of our sites. We do this to respond to your requests and to provide better services to you, our clients. We will not disclose, sell or transfer any personal information to any third party unless required by law or authorized to do so by you.

## Security

This is a U.S. Government web site. Unauthorized attempts to upload information or change information on this site are strictly prohibited and may be punishable under applicable Federal law.
