# Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

Buydebest values the privacy of information pertaining to its acquaintances. We do not disclose any

information given to us for any specific purpose such as name, email address, address, telephone

number etc to any outside source without your authorization.

Buydebest provides means for you to manage and control any information that you have provided

us. We do not disclose any personal information to the advertisers and for other marketing and

promotional purposes that could personally identify you, such as password, credit card number, debit

card number etc.

Data protection is our duty and we fully accept to keep it and secure as much it is in our power. We

only collect that information from you which are important for us in our dealings and is relevant for

the purpose for which it is collected.
