# Buzzoola //

## Our Commitment

Simply put, we will never abuse your trust — that means that we will never share, sell, rent or otherwise disclose any of your personal or company information without your explicit consent.

To ensure the integrity of our privacy policy and to prevent unauthorized access to account information we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure your account information. All account information is encrypted and stored on our servers.

## Passwords

Please don’t give out or share your account password. If you forget your password apply to [ask@buzzoola.com](mailto:ask@buzzoola.com) for password reminder. In the event that you suspect that your account password has been or will be misused, please immediately inform Buzzoola. In no event shall Buzzoola be responsible for any such misuse(s).

## Video Distribution and Achieving Statistics

Buzzoola offers a technology platform for uploading, storage, transcoding, distributing and tracking video content. The platform is designed to report about an individual video impressions, across major tubes, social networks and direct sites. We provide video delivery and reporting for clients.

Buzzoola may use information about you that we collect through the viewing of videos uploaded via Buzzoola or via our partner’s sites to deliver targeted video on the websites of third parties. We do not share this information with third parties.

We use cookies (described more fully below) to facilitate the use of this information while you are online. In order to appropriately safeguard the information in them, as described above, these cookies are encrypted.

## Cookies and Data Collection

«Cookies» are small files placed on the hard drive of your computer. We use cookies to provide you better service by allowing you to enter your password less frequently during a session and to help us understand and analyze our web page flow. We collect only anonymous, non-Personally Identifiable Information, such as browser type (e.g., Firefox or Internet Explorer), operating system (e.g., Macintosh or Windows), IP address, and whether you respond to an ad. We do not collect sensitive data. Cookies are employed to track stream and viewership data and to improve user experience by providing information such as buffering events per user back to our video site partners. We also use the data collected to report on effectiveness of campaigns for our clients and to optimize campaigns as they run to improve viewing time and brand awareness metrics.

## We Never Transfer or Sell Data

We NEVER transfer or sell data to third parties.

## Data Storage and Security

We retain the non-personally identifiable information collected via our technology for up to one year. After one year, we discard the information. All data is encrypted and stored on our servers.

## Email and Spam

You may not use our communication tools to send spam. If you send an email to an email address that is not registered in our community, we do not permanently store that email or use that email address for any marketing purposes. We do not rent or sell these email addresses.

## Marketing

Again, we will never sell, rent or disclose your personally identifiable information (i.e., name, email and mailing address) to any third parties without your explicit consent. We may, but are not obliged to, occasionally send you strictly service or feature-related enhancement announcements.

## Third Party Sites and Services

We provide links on this site as a convenience for our users. We may also inform you about third party services or applications, or facilitate distribution of your videos to third party platforms or publishers. This Privacy & Security Policy does not address, and we are not responsible for, the privacy, information or other practices of any third parties, or of other websites or services operated by third parties. Your activities, including your provision of personal information, with any such third parties or such third parties' websites or services will be governed solely by the privacy policies of such third parties. The inclusion of a link on our website does not imply endorsement of the linked website by us or by our affiliates. Buzzoola is not responsible for the use of any personal information you disclose to third party sites or services.

## Changes to this Privacy & Security Policy

Buzzoola may update or modify this Privacy & Security Policy from time to time. If there are material changes to this Privacy Policy or in how Buzzoola will use your personal information, we will post such changes here. You will be notified if any of the material changes that affect the use of your personal information and asked to opt-in to the new use of your personal information. If you do not agree with the changes to how your information will be used, please do not continue to use this website.

By using this site, you signify your assent to the Buzzoola Privacy & Security Policy. We reserve the right to make changes in this policy at any time. Please check the policy each time you use our website to ensure you are aware of any changes in our privacy practices. Our Privacy & Security Policy will indicate the date it was last updated. Your continued use of our site will signify your acceptance of the changes to our Privacy & Security Policy.

## Contact Us

Please contact us to report violations or if you have any questions regarding our Privacy & Security policy. You can reach us at [ask@buzzoola.com](mailto:ask@buzzoola.com)
