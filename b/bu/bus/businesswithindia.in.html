Privacy Policy
 
www.businesswithindia.in is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website. You can visit our website without telling us who you are, and without revealing any information about yourself.

Data statistics are gathered about online usage on a collective rather than individual basis. As a result, tracking systems do not gather or store data at a personal level. This information is used to gauge the capacity of our servers and to measure the use of our website to allow us to improve our content. We collect information that you send us voluntarily but do not share this information.

When you visit our website, we will not collect your personal information unless you choose to use and receive online services that require it. This includes requests for service that require online payments and online applications. Personal information provided under these circumstances is not released outside our organization.
