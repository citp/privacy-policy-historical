Duke Communications International Privacy Statement

Duke Communications International (DCI), doing business as Business Finance, strives to protect the privacy of all information provided by users at the Business Finance Web site (http://www.businessfinancemag.com) and our other related Web sites (hereinafter referred to as "the Web sites").

The Web sites are maintained by

Information Collected by DCI

DCI collects information from and about users through their registration at the Web sites and through their participation in certain activities on the Web sites, including contests and surveys.

Persistent Client State HTTP Cookies ("Cookies") let Web servers store information on the client so that this information is available from session to session. On the Web sites, Cookies are created and used by the outside ad company we use to display ads on the Web sites. These ads may contain Cookies. Cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not currently use the Cookie information. The ad company uses the information to provide us with general statistics on banner impressions and click-through rates and to make sure that users do not see the same ad repeatedly.

Use of Information

The aggregated information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • to improve the content and design of the Web sites
  • to help advertisers and potential advertisers assess the suitability of the Web sites for their ad campaigns

DCI reserves the right to rent user information to interested parties, pending our approval of the party renting the information and the planned use. DCI does not rent phone numbers, fax numbers, or e-mail addresses at any time. DCI reserves the right to provide phone numbers, fax numbers, and e-mail addresses as part of infrequent name exchanges with other vendors, companies, and/or user groups.

Although DCI will seek to require third parties with whom it has agreements to follow appropriate privacy policies, we do not bear any responsibility for any actions or policies of such third parties.

Users may specify that they wish to not be (a) part of any list rental or exchange, (b) faxed, or (c) solicited by DCI, or any combination thereof. In order to do so, contact Brenda Roode at DCI at (800) 621-1544 or (970) 663-4700 or e-mail broode@businessfinancemag.com.

Using e-mail addresses provided at registration or otherwise (including non-electronic forms), DCI periodically sends out e-mail newsletters and promotional e-mail to users of our services. DCI also uses e-mail addresses to send renewal notices to existing Business Finance subscribers. Users can indicate that they do not wish to receive e-mail information from DCI. Third parties will not contact our users through the use of our e-mail newsletters except through advertisements within the newsletters themselves.

The Web sites contain links to other Internet sites. DCI is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such sites. In addition, links to other Internet sites are not endorsements of any products or services in such sites, and no information in such sites has been endorsed or approved by this site.

Users can send e-mail to us at webmaster@businessfinancemag.com with any further questions.
