# BustNow

We have made this Privacy Policy to demonstrate our dedication to respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy sets out our practices with respect to personal information collected regarding visitors and subscribers (collectively referred as "you") of this Site. We will only use the information that we collect about you lawfully (in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998).

We may use information collected from visitors to our Site for any of the following purposes:  
**1.** to provide you with the best possible service,   
**2.** to email you if necessary,   
**3.** to further improve our site.

We will **not** send out targetted advertisements and offers by email. None of the collected information will *EVER* be sold to third parties.

The type of information we will collect about you includes: 

(in case you contact us, so we can provide you with service):  
your email address   
(in case of illegal behavious on our site and / or community  
your IP address 

We may use technology to track the patterns of behaviour of visitors to our site. This can include using a "cookie" which would be stored on your browser. In case you don't want this to happen, you can usually modify your browser. The information collected in this way can be used to identify you unless you modify your browser settings.

If you have any questions/comments about privacy, you should [contact us](http://www.bustnow.com/contact).
