# Business Traveller | Privacy Policy

**Data Protection**  
The Site will comply with the relevant English Data Protection laws, so normally any personal details to be provided to us will not be disclosed to third parties outside businesstraveller.com without the User's consent. (please see paragraph 4 of the Terms, Data Protection).

Users should be aware and accept that, if businesstraveller.com is requested by authorised third parties or regulatory or governmental agencies investigating illegal activities to provide information concerning Users activities whilst using the Site, businesstraveller.com shall do so.

Similarly, businesstraveller.com will disclose Users' personal data if businesstraveller.com is compelled to do so by law. businesstraveller.com reserves the right to notify or provide such information to the relevant authority if, in our sole opinion, businesstraveller.com believe an emergency, illegal activity or some other reasonable basis exists for us doing so.

businesstraveller.com reserves the right to store and use Users' personal data solely for its own internal purposes and businesstraveller.com further reserve the right to access, monitor and retrieve password-protected information in order to fulfil our obligations in these terms and conditions.

**Uses of Registration Information**  
Registration information we receive from Users is used by businesstraveller.com as follows: 

| When you register, you can choose not to be contacted by us in this way. Any of our Users can also contact us at any time to ask to be removed from our User list. If you wish to remove your details, you should contact us by email: [online@businesstraveller.com](mailto:online@businesstraveller.com?subject=terms%20and%20conditions)

**Cookies**  
Users should be aware that businesstraveller.com uses cookies to identify registered users who return to the Site. It enables us to deliver tailored content to each of our Users and lets you store your own selection of material for future use. Some company networks block cookies which means that not all Users can enjoy these personalised components of the Site.

**Security**  
businesstraveller.com operates secure data networks protected by industry standard security systems.
