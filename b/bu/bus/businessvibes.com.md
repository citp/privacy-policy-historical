# Private Policy | BusinessVibes

**Date of Last Revision: 6 Sep 2010**

BusinessVibes respects your right to privacy and has complied with relevant Data Protection registrations in the United Kingdom. We use the information you provide us for our various services. All precautions have been taken to store your data in a secure environment. BusinessVibes uses cookies and other technologies to recognize you, customize your experience, and serve advertisements. We collect non-personal data such as the version of your browser, of your operating system and your IP address. Users may make certain information or contents available on the website for which they are wholly responsible. By providing such data on BusinessVibes, the user concerned demonstrates their consent to the processing of this sensitive data by BusinessVibes. 

If you do not want BusinessVibes to reproduce or distribute this data, you may delete this information directly on BusinessVibes or you may terminate your BusinessVibes account according to the conditions set forth in our Terms of Use.

Advertisers Space/Lead Space - BusinessVibes shall have the sole authority to decide whether to allow or remove content if a campaign does not respect the above principles. BusinessVibes shall have no liability in respect to any such action or inaction.

The data stored on BusinessVibes is exclusively intended to help you enhance your business network. You have a right of access, rectification and deletion of your data under European Law. You can exercise this right by writing to the customer service department at the following address:
