# Privacy Policy Business Espana truly establised, truly informative

Privacy Policy Page

_Our policy is simple:_

Here at businessespana.com **we do not collect identifiable data** about you except for the following situations:

  1. **Web statistics programes** that report number of visitors etc \- **none of which is traceable**
  2. **You volunteer the information** \- eg you submit your website to the directory 



_Advertisers:_

The following is taken directly from the **Google website** regarding how they serve ads on this site:

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies please see the following  http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/faq.aspx
