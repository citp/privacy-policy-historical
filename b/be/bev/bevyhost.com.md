# Privacy Policy

BevyHost recognize that the privacy of your personal information is important.

Users can join our website and rest assured that their data is held both conservatively and confidentially on a secure server. We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

Here is information on what types of personal information we receive and collect when you visit BevyHost.com, and how we safeguard your information.

Log Files  
As with most websites, we collect and use data contained in log files. The information in the log files include your IP (internet protocol) address, your ISP (internet service provider), the browser you used to visit our site, the time you visited our site and which pages you visited throughout our site.

Cookies  
We do use cookies to store information, such as your personal preferences when you visit our site. This gives you the ability to login and use some of the features of our website, such as posting and commenting messages.

We use third-party advertising companies (such as Google) to support our site by serving ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

However, this can affect how you are able to interact with our site as well as other websites. This could include the inability to login to the forum account.
