What is a cookie?

A cookie is a short text file sent through your browser, registered on your device when visiting a website. Your web browser then sends these cookies back to the originating website on each subsequent visit, or to another website that recognises these cookies. We use cookies for navigation needs on our website, and to optimize and personalize our services, like allowing pages to load faster because certain content is stored on your browser or by helping us authenticate you to deliver personalized content.
