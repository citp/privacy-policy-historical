# Betaout - Content Collaboration Suite for Wordpress Publishers and Digital Agencies.

Privacy Policy

**Information Collection and Use:**

During your visit and registration to our website we collect your personal information like Name, e- mail address, IP address, contact number, date of birth, gender, postal code, occupation, industry, and personal interests etc.

Betaout uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients and to list your name on our website as our clients. Betaout is not responsible for the accuracy, completeness, veracity or reliability of any advertising and content, products or services which may be accessed or advertised to you and Betaout will not be liable for any loss or damage arising out of the same.

For any correction in data provided to us at the time of registration you may contact us at [support@betaout.com](mailto:support@betaout.com) .

**Information Sharing and Disclosure:**

Betaout does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

  * We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with BETAOUT under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help BETAOUT communicate with you about offers from BETAOUT and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information.
  * We respond to court orders, legal process or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.
  * We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of BETAOUT's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
  * We transfer information about you if BETAOUT is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, BETAOUT will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.



**Confidentiality and Security:**

We respect your privacy and affirm the confidentiality of information provided to us. No sensitive information will be exchanged with any third party. We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.
