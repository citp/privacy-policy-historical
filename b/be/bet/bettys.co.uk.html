Privacy Policy

We promise to look after your personal information and to treat your details with respect. We’ll never share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we’d like to contact you via email now and again to keep you up to date with our latest catalogues and promotions - provided you've given us permission to do so, of course.

Please note we do keep hold of the personal information you provide, and any other information relating to you, to use for our own purposes, such as improving how our website works, market research, or in the prevention of Credit Card fraud. Out of necessity we may occasionally share information with relevant and trusted organisations to do this.
