How we collect personal information.

When you register with BETFOOT we collect and store the information you provide in a secure database.  Like many websites, BETFOOT’ websites use "cookie" technology. When you first connect to one of our sites, the cookie identifies your browser with a unique, random number. Cookies help us understand which parts of our websites are the most popular, where our visitors are going, and how long they spend there.
