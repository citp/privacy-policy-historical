# Privacy Policy

This is the privacy policy document for Bestmobs.com, it outlines the types of personal informationthat is received and collected by www.bestmobs.com and how it is used.

**Logs  
**Bestmobs.com makes use of log files, just like most websites on the Internet. The information inside the log files includes visitors’ IP addresses, user agent (browser), date/time stamp, referring pages. We use this information to monitor the website’s growth. The information we collect will never be given out.

**Cookies  
**Bestmobs.com make use cookies to store information about visitors preferences and user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

**External Links  
**Bestmobs.com sometimes put links to other websites, we may be recomending the website for a specific reason or as the source of a resource we used. Whatsoever the case, Bestmobs.com is not responsible for the content or business practices of these websites.

**Third Party Ad Servers**  
We make use of third party ad servers to bring ads to your browser. These companies may use information about your visits to this website in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. In as much as we try to make sure only safe advertisements are displayed, we do not fully control the type of advertisments our third party ad servers may show.

**Questions  
**If you have any questions about Bestmobs.com privacy policy, please do not hesitate to contact us via our e-mail address info@bestmobs.com.
