# BestMaleBlogs: Privacy Policy

#### What This Privacy Policy Covers 

This policy covers how bestmaleblogs.com treats any personal information that bestmaleblogs.com might have collected and received, including information related to your past use of bestmaleblogs services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address and that is not otherwise publicly available.

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that bestmaleblogs does not own or control, or to people that bestmaleblogs does not employ or manage. 

#### Information Collection and Use

bestmaleblogs collects personal information if you register with bestmaleblogs as a user, when you use bestmaleblogs services, when you visit bestmaleblogs pages or the pages of certain bestmaleblogs partners.

bestmaleblogs automatically receives and records information on our server from your browser, including your IP address and the page you request. This information is anonymous and are is not linked to any personal details we might have stored.

bestmaleblogs uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, conntact you, and conduct research. 

bestmaleblogs will not under any circumstances give out any information that might be stored about you such as email address or any other details to anyone!

#### What this means..

bestmaleblogs respects your privacy, any information we might have will not be disclosed or given away to anyone else or any 3rd party.
