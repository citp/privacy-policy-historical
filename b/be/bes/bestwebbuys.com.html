Children's Privacy

Pursuant to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, which became effective on April 21, 2000, we cannot collect personal information from children under 13 years of age without the consent of their parents. Personal information includes any information which can uniquely identify a child and includes information such as name and e-mail address.

Since we do not have the resources to verify parental consent, we have decided not to accept any personal information from children under 13 years of age. This means that we will not accept contest entries, surveys, or user registration from children under 13 years of age.
