# Sperling's Best Places - Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

Sperling's BestPlaces (a division of Fast Forward, Inc.) takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our policy regarding your privacy when using our site. 

This statement discloses the privacy practices for the Sperling's BestPlaces web sites (www.bestplaces.net), and any co-branded features provided to our partners, hereafter referenced as Sperling's BestPlaces, or "us" or "we." 

PLEASE REVIEW THIS POLICY CAREFULLY, AS WELL AS THE TERMS AND CONDITIONS BEFORE USING THIS WEBSITE. By using Sperling's BestPlaces website or any feature, you agree to the terms of these policies. If you do not agree to such terms, you may not use the site or features. 

**What is Covered by this Privacy Policy**   
This policy covers how we treat personal information that we may collect and receive, including information related to your past use of our products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available. This policy does not apply to the practices of other companies which are not under our control, such as our partners. 

**Information Collection and Use**   
Sperling's BestPlaces does not require you to register or otherwise log in to use our features, although you may wish to become a member to gain access to premium content.  We may collect limited personal information from you, with your permission, to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients. 

Sperling's BestPlaces automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, and pages you request. We currently use cookies to make our site and features more efficient, and to customize our content upon your return to our site.

**Information Sharing and Disclosure**   
Sperling's BestPlaces does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies without your permission. 

**Use of Cookies**   
Sperling's BestPlaces uses cookies for the following purposes: to track user traffic patterns, for membership, and in conjunction with advertising and marketing offers. While using our website or features, you may receive cookies from our advertisers or sponsors. We do not control such cookies or use the associated data. 

**Changes to this Privacy Policy**   
Sperling's BestPlaces may change this Privacy Policy in the future. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the email address you gave at registration or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
