# Privacy Policy

BestPricedFurniture.com, Inc. ("BestPricedFurniture.com") has created this privacy policy in order to demonstrate its strong commitment to the privacy of our visitors and customers. This privacy policy explains how Best Priced Furniture.com collects, uses, shares and protects information obtained from www.bestpricedfurniture.com.

### Collection and Use of Information

BestPricedFurniture.com may collect and store personally identifiable information such as name, email, address, phone and credit card information about persons who visit this site in order for us to process your order. BestPricedFurniture.com may also collect and store aggregate or anonymous information about the user contact with and use of the site. We may use the information to contact you about our products and services or those of our clients from time to time. To opt out of receiving these communications, you can use the unsubscribe link in the email or you can contact us using the information provided in "Contacting the Web Site".We do not sell your personally identifiable information to third parties or mass marketers. For example, we will not sell another company your name or email address. Unless you consent to the disclosure of your personally identifiable information, only we and our affiliates, our technical consultants, third party auditors and other third parties who make our site available, enhance its functionality, or provide associated services have access to this information, and we require them to agree that they are covered by this policy. Please note that we do reserve the right to transfer your personally identifiable information and other information to our successors in business and purchasers of site assets.

We may provide your personal information to companies that provide services to help us with our business activities such as email communications, payment processing and live chat. These companies are authorized to use your personal information only as necessary to provide these services to us.

We will share your personal information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy policy.

We may also disclose your personal information: 

  * as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process
  * when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request,
  * to any other third party with your prior consent to do so. 
  * if BestPricedFurniture.com is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our Web site of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information, as well as any choices you may have regarding your personal information, 
  * to any other third party with your prior consent to do so



### Tracking Technologies

_Cookies_

A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user's computer for record-keeping purposes. We may send your Web browser a "cookie", a small data file that's stored in your hard drive, to help us provide you with customized services. We do not use cookies to retrieve personal information from your computer. 

_Third Party Cookies_

The use of cookies by third parties is not covered by our privacy policy. We do not have access or control over these cookies. 

_Clear Gifs (Web Beacons/Web Bugs)_

Our third party advertising partner employs] a software technology called clear gifs (a.k.a. Web Beacons/Web Bugs), that help us better manage content on our site by informing us what content is effective. Clear gifs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of Web users. In contrast to cookies, which are stored on a user's computer hard drive, clear gifs are embedded invisibly on Web pages and are about the size of the period at the end of this sentence. We do not tie the information gathered by clear gifs to our customers' personally identifiable information.

### Children's Privacy 

Protecting the privacy of the young (i.e. individuals under the age of 13) is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our Site from those we actually know who are under 13, and no part of our Site is structured to attract anyone under 13\. If a child has provided us with personal information, we ask that a parent or guardian of the child contact us, if they would like the information submitted by a child deleted from our database. ### Security 

BestPricedFurniture.com has taken all the necessary procedures to ensure a safe visit to our site.

Bestpricedfurniture.com has installed a VeriSign Server ID, which provides our site with complete security and helps make BestPricedFurniture.com a trustworthy Internet experience for our customers. BestPricedFurniture.com uses VeriSign's Payflow Pro to for the secure acceptance and processing of on-line payments. Payflow Pro creates a dedicated SSL TCP/IP level communication thread for each transaction between the client and the server, resulting in outstanding security.

BestPricedFurniture.com has installed industry-standard SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) technology to ensure that your information is encrypted. We do not further access any credit card information you provide. Your credit card information will not be shared with any company outside of BestPricedFurniture.com and will only be used for the sole purpose of processing your order. Your personal information is important to us, and we, do not sell, share, or otherwise disclose any personal information gathered from our customers for commercial purposes.

Access to computer data and files at BestPricedFurniture.com are secured by password-protected services. Our network is protected by a firewall designed to protect unauthorized access.
