**Privacy and Data Security**

At BestOfferBuy.com, your right to privacy and data security is a primary concern. That's why, when you visit BestOfferBuy.com, we help you maintain control over your personal data on the Internet. Below are the guidelines we use for protecting the information you provide us during a visit to our Internet sites ([www.BestOfferBuy.com](http://www.bestofferbuy.com/)) or when you use our online support offerings.

  
 **BestOfferBuy.com only asks for specific types of personal information**

In a few areas on our Web site and online customer support tools, we ask you to provide information that will enable us to enhance your site visit, to assist you with purchase and technical support issues or to follow up with you after your visit. It is completely optional for you to participate.

For example, we request information from you when you:

1\. Register on BestOfferBuy.com  
2\. Request a quote  
3\. Place an order  
4\. Provide feedback in an online survey  
5\. Subscribe to a newsletter or a mailing list  
6\. Contact us

Information we may request includes your name, e-mail address, phone number, address, type of business, credit card details, customer preference, experience, demographic and interest information, customer number and RMA number, as well as other similar personal information. If we ever ask for significantly different information we will inform you.

  
 **BestOfferBuy.com only uses your personal information for specific purposes**

The information you provide will be kept confidential and used to support your customer relationship with BestOfferBuy.com. Among other things, we want to help you quickly find information on BestOfferBuy.com and alert you to product upgrades, special offers, updated information and other new products and services from BestOfferBuy.com. BestOfferBuy.com may enhance or merge your information collected at its site with data from third parties for purposes of marketing products or services to you.

In addition, BestOfferBuy.com may be required to disclose personal information in connection with law enforcement, fraud prevention, regulation, and other legal action or if BestOfferBuy.com reasonably believes it is necessary to do so to protect BestOfferBuy.com, its customers, or the public.

 **You can opt-out of BestOfferBuy.com's advertising and marketing list at any time**

Periodically, we may send you information about our various products and services, or other products and services we feel may be of interest to you. Only BestOfferBuy.com will send you these direct mailings. If you do not want to be included on BestOfferBuy.com's advertising and marketing list, simply tell us when you give us your personal information. Or, at any time you can easily opt-out of, or update your information on, BestOfferBuy.com's advertising and marketing list by clicking here.

BestOfferBuy.com will not disclose your personal information to any outside organization for its use in marketing without your consent

Information regarding you (such as name, address and phone number) or your order and the products you purchase will not be given or sold to any outside organization for its use in marketing or solicitation without your consent.

New technologies are emerging on the Internet that help us deliver customized visitor experiences. Our use of this technology does not mean that we automatically know any information about you. BestOfferBuy.com has no desire or intent to infringe on your privacy while using the BestOfferBuy.com site.
