# BEST ROM SITES - Privacy Policies

  
Information Collected:  
"Best Rom Sites" does not sell, rent, or give out any information about our visitors. This includes email addresses, IP addresses, or any other type of information we might collect.

"Best Rom Sites" does not collect any unique information about you (such as your name, email address, etc.) except when you specifically and knowingly provide such information. "Best Rom Sites" currently collects information about browser types, operating systems, screen resolution, screen colors, and other information which we can use to help design our web sites. These are basic computer stats which can only be used to make a small demographic of the types of computers people have.
