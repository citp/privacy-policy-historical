# Privacy Policy

No information of any kind (E-mail, IP addresses, ...) is sold or released to anyone about visitors to BestOfVids without prior consent or a court order. In other words, no information is released to anyone unless you give permission first.

BestOfVids does not attempt to collect personal information from children. Note that BestOfVids cannot always determine that a user is a child. BestOfVids does not maintain databases of children or adults.

When visiting any Internet site, your unique address called an "IP address" is recorded. BestOfVids does not release any information about the collection of this address to any third party except under court order. BestOfVids archives the log files in order to create aggregate statistical reports, detect errors on the web site, and for security reasons. The log files are eventually deleted.

IP reports for advertisement clicks are shared with the advertiser. However, they usually have this information already since it is captured by their server when you view or click an ad. This is done to verify billing for the ads. No additional information associated with any specific user is provided to the advertisers.

**Overview**

Your privacy is important to us. BestOfVids 's service is free for all to use, and prior registration is not needed, except for those who want additionally receive a newsletter.

BestOfVids will explicitly ask when it needs information that personally identifies you. All personally identifiable information BestOfVids collects is only gathered with your voluntary act. BestOfVids keeps all information private and does not, and will not, share it with any third parties except under these circumstances:

\- When such action is necessary to conform to legal requirements or comply with legal process.

\- When such action is necessary to protect and defend the rights or property of BestOfVids.

BestOfVids uses cookies as part of tracking process for advertisement banners so you do not see the same ad repeatedly, but cookies will not be used to personally identify you without your prior consent.

Some links on BestOfVids will take visitors outside its web server. Although these links were established to provide access to useful information, BestOfVids is not responsible for any of these sites or their content.
