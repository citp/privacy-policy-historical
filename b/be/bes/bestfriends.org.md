# Best Friends Animal Society - Privacy Policy

**General Statement  
** Best Friends Animal Society is committed to protecting the privacy of personal information collected online and has security policies in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. 

This privacy statement applies to Best Friends' website only. Links to other websites and to services hosted by other companies can be accessed from our site. Please be advised that the privacy policies on these sites may not be consistent with our own privacy policy. Places where we link to sites hosted by other providers include member bulletin boards, No More Homeless Pets forum, Best Friends Shopping/Pet Club, Best Friends Visa credit card application, and Telecare program. 

**Information Collection  
** In several areas of this site we collect personally identifiable information. "Personally identifiable" refers to any information that could be used to contact a person, such as full name, home address, email address, and telephone number. Personally identifiable information is collected on this site in areas involving voluntary transactions such as making a donation, signing up to become a member of the Best Friends Network, and subscribing to the Best Friends magazine. Personally identifiable information collected online is used by Best Friends solely for internal purposes. For more information on the collection and use of personally identifiable information, please see the paragraphs below on "Security" and "Cookies".

 **Use of Information  
** We do not sell, rent, or exchange personal information collected on our website with other companies. We may use email addresses collected on our website to circulate news about Best Friends Animal Society and its programs; to promote events; and to acquire new members and subscribers. We may share aggregated demographic information with our funders for the purpose of demonstrating that we are meeting our organizational goals. A visitor who receives email from us may opt out of email at any time by following the opt-out instructions contained in the email, or by sending an email to [info@bestfriends.org](mailto:info@bestfriends.org). 

**Visitors under the age of 13  
** Best Friends operates a general audience website. We have a humane education section that provides material of interest to students and teachers. If we receive personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13, we may use it to seek consent from a parent or teacher in order to fulfill information requests. (We follow the COPPA guidelines established by the FTC.) Purchases in our online store must be made by valid credit card holders only. Our forums/bulletin boards are intended for adults.

 **Security  
** Best Friends uses Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology to protect and encrypt online donation and order information while it is being transferred across the Internet. SSL is an industry standard security technology. To further enhance security, stored donation and order information is also encrypted to prevent unauthorized access to the data. 

**Cookies  
** Cookies are used on the Best Friends website for the convenience of our online visitors. We also use cookies for the registration of readers who want to comment on  
news stories or blog postings on news.bestfriends.org.We don't collect personal information via cookies. We do use "per-session" cookies in the Best Friends Pet Club/Shopping section of the site. Visitors with "per-session" cookies disabled cannot shop online with us or post to the bulletin boards. 

**Links  
** Best Friends does not restrict linking to our site from other sites. This does not imply that we endorse products or services provided by other websites, unless specifically indicated. 

**Copyright Information  
** All materials published on this website are protected by copyright. Materials that are copyrighted (including text, videos and photographs) may not be reproduced, retransmitted or distributed without the written consent of Best Friends. If permission is granted to reproduce materials from this site, we ask that credit be given to the author and Best Friends magazine or website. If you would like permission to reproduce an article from this website, please contact [info@bestfriends.org](mailto:info@bestfriends.org). Please specify the URL and article or feature you would like permission to reproduce.

 **Citing Best Friends Online in Publications  
** The material on this site comes from a range of sources. If you are citing information without a specific attribution, please list your citation this way: "Best Friends Animal Society, [the location of the page you are citing], [the date you found the item being cited]." If another attribution is given, please cite the original author.

If you have any questions about our privacy policy or would like more information, please contact us by email at [info@bestfriends.org](mailto:info@bestfriends.org) or by phone at (435) 644-2001.
