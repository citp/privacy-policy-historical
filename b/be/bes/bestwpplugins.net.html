BWP may use this information to customize the content you see and to fulfill your requests for certain products and services. However, BWP does not connect this non-personal data to any personal information collected from you.

    INFORMATION SHARING & DISCLOSURE

    BWP may disclose your personal information to third parties who work on behalf of BWP to provide products and services requested by you.     (a) We have your express consent to share the information for a specified purpose.
