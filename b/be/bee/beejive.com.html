Clear gif, cookies, and log file information are non-personally identifiable information because such information is not collected or used in such a way that an individual User can be identified, for example, by name or email address (collectively, "Non-Personally Identifiable Information"). Personally Identifiable Information and Non-Personally Identifiable Information are collectively referred to in the Privacy Policy as "Collected Information."
