# Privacy Policy | BeerAdvocate.com

  
Version 04/01/2004 

**Data Collection**   
BeerAdvocate.com collects limited non-personally identifying information your browser makes available whenever you visit a website. This log information includes your Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your query and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser. We use this information to operate, develop and improve our services. 

Some of our services require you to register for an account. BeerAdvocate.com asks you for some personal information in order to create an account (typically your name, email address and a password for your account) and we will use that information to provide the service. 

**Information Sharing**   
We do not rent, sell or giveaway your personally identifying information to other companies or individuals. Unless subject to display, all of your information is strictly confidential and its sole usage will be to provide enhanced content on BeerAdvocate.com. 

**BeerAdvocate.com Beer eMails and Fighting Spam**   
If you opt to receive "Beer eMails" from us, expect 1-4 a month on average and feel free to opt-out at anytime; by using the included links or by modifying your personal profile page. 

To fight spam, your email address will never be shown and is used solely by BeerAdvocate.com. 

**Cookies**   
[Click here to review the BeerAdvocate.com Cookies Policy / Requirements](https://web.archive.org/cookies.php). 

**Changes to this Policy**   
Please note this Privacy Policy will change from time to time. We expect most such changes to be minor, but there may be changes that are more significant. Regardless, we will post those changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will also provide a more prominent notice. 

If you have any additional questions, please feel free to contact us.
