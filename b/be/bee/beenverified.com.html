Security

BeenVerified™ takes every precaution to protect our Registered Users’ and non-Registered Users’ information. When we ask Registered Users to enter sensitive information (such as a credit card number, Social Security Number, credit score, etc.), and when such information is stored and/or transmitted, that information is encrypted and is protected with SSL encryption software. While we use SSL encryption to protect sensitive information online, we also do everything in our power to protect sensitive and non-sensitive information off-line.
