Privacy

Users' privacy is very important to B2c Technology. We are committed to safeguarding the information Users entrust to B2c Technology.


The Information We Collect

B2c Technology collects personally identifiable information from Users through online forms for ordering products and services. How We Use Information

Personal information will not be sold or otherwise transferred to our business partners without your prior consent, except that we will disclose the information we collect to third parties when, in our good faith judgment, we are obligated to do so under applicable laws.
