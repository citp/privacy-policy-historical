BCO Privacy Policy

  • This policy covers how B ConnectedOnline treats personal information that B ConnectedOnline collects and receives, including information related to your past use of B ConnectedOnline products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available.
  • This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that B ConnectedOnline does not own or control, or to people that B ConnectedOnline does not employ or manage.

Information Collection and Use

General

  • B ConnectedOnline collects personal information when you register with B ConnectedOnline, when you use B ConnectedOnline products or services, when you visit B ConnectedOnline pages or the pages of certain B ConnectedOnline partners, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. B ConnectedOnline may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.
  • When you register we ask for information such as email address and zip code. Once you register with B ConnectedOnline and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.
  • B ConnectedOnline uses information for the following general purposes: to provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

 Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • B ConnectedOnline does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:
  • We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with B ConnectedOnline under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help B ConnectedOnline communicate with you about offers from B ConnectedOnline and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information.
  • We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims;
  • We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of B ConnectedOnline's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
  • We transfer information about you if B ConnectedOnline is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, B ConnectedOnline will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
  • B ConnectedOnline displays targeted advertisements based on personal information. Advertisers (including ad serving companies) may assume that people who interact with, view, or click on targeted ads meet the targeting criteria - for example, women ages 18-24 from a particular geographic area.
  • B ConnectedOnline does not provide any personal information to the advertiser when you interact with or view a targeted ad. However, by interacting with or viewing an ad you are consenting to the possibility that the advertiser will make the assumption that you meet the targeting criteria used to display the ad.
  • B ConnectedOnline advertisers include financial service providers (such as banks, insurance agents, stock brokers and mortgage lenders) and non-financial companies (such as stores, airlines, and software companies).

Cookies

  • B ConnectedOnline does not set cookies on your computer.
  • B ConnectedOnline lets other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one.
  • We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.
  • We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect personal information about you.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

  • B ConnectedOnline may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your B ConnectedOnline account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
