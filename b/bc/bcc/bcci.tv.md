# Board of Control for Cricket in India - Privacy Policy

Here at the Board of Control for Cricket in India Official Website referred to hereinafter as "[BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/)", we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes the information we gather on [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/), what we do with it, and the safeguards we have in place to protect it.

Before you submit any information to [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/), please read this Policy for an explanation of how we will treat your personal information. By using any part of the [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/), including without limitation any official site of the BCCI, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as outlined in this Policy and to the collection, processing and maintenance of this information in the United States & India, and where applicable, other countries. If you do not agree to this Policy, please do not use [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/). Your use of any part of [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) indicates your acceptance of this Policy and of the collection and use of your personal information in accordance with this Policy. Please note that this Policy applies only to information collected on [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/); it does not apply to any other information collected or obtained by any member of the BCCI Family (as defined below) through other means.

[BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) do not knowingly solicit any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. In addition, [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) do not knowingly obtain, retain or use information from anyone who indicates that they are under the age of 13. Accordingly, children under the age of 13 may be restricted from participating in certain features and transactions on [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/). If you would like more information about [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/)'s policy with respect to children, please read our Note to Parents below.

You should note that [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) includes certain "Third Party Sections" (such as those identified below), which are operated by third parties and may be subject to the additional privacy policies of those entities. Please review the privacy policies, which are posted on the Third Party Sections and govern the collection of information through those Third Party Sections. You also should note that [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) work with (and may provide links to) third party sites (i.e., sites that are not part of [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/)) that advertise on and/or offer features and transactions described on [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) (e.g., tickets, fantasy games, subscription services, chat rooms, message boards, registration for camps and clinics). The organizations controlling those third party sites are responsible for ensuring that they comply with applicable laws, regulations, and rules. If you visit a site that is not part of [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/), we encourage you to review that site's privacy policy before providing any personally identifiable information.

If we make any material changes to this Policy, those changes will be posted here so that you are always aware of what personal information is collected, how that information is used, and under what circumstances that information may be disclosed. Since this Policy may change from time to time, you should review it periodically and specifically before you provide any additional personally identifiable information. Your continued use of [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) following the posting of changes to this Policy will signal your acceptance of such changes.

If you have any questions, comments, or requests regarding this Policy or the information practices of [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/), please contact us by e-mail at :

**7. The kind of security procedures that are in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of information under our control**  
[BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) have security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under its control. The servers for [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) that contain personally identifiable information are stored in a physically secure and climate controlled off-site facility. Beyond the physical protection of the servers, we protect against data loss by backing up data, performing consistency checks, and restricting access to information on our servers. When receiving financial information (e.g., credit card number, credit card expiration date) for completing certain online transactions, including those in connection with [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) Store, the BCCI Photo Store, and League Pass, our site uses SSL or "Secure Sockets Layer," an industry standard security protocol. Other features, like email and contact us are not encrypted. When providing sensitive information on the Internet, you should also look at your browser to determine whether SSL technology is being employed (usually indicated by an image of a lock in the browser frame). In addition, only those trained employees needed to carry out our business functions will be authorized to have access to information on individual [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) customers.

It is important to remember that whatever you transmit or disclose online can be collected and used by others or unlawfully intercepted by third parties. No data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot warrant the security of any information you transmit to us.

Your site membership account is password protected. We recommend that you do not divulge your password to anyone. At no time will any [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) employee ask for your password via e-mail. If you ever receive such an inquiry, please notify [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) immediately at [FanRelations@BCCI.tv](mailto:FanRelations@BCCI.tv). Ultimately, you are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your member names, passwords and/or other account information.

Any information voluntarily disclosed by users and posted on our site (on message boards, web logs, and/or in chat areas) may be viewed and used by others. If users post personal information that is accessible to the public online, the user does so at his or her own risk. Users accept that they may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return.

**[BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) NOTE TO PARENTS**

It is [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/)'s policy not to knowingly solicit any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. A number of interactive features on [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) require registration to participate (e.g., e-mail newsletters, membership). All registrants will be required to enter their full name, date of birth, e-mail address and complete address. In some cases, users will be required to select a special member name to be used as an identifier for the feature and must also enter a password and a secret hint that will help us verify their identity if they forget their password. If [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) determines that a person is under the age of 13, based on the information provided during the enrolment process, that person will not be permitted to register for features requiring the submission of personal information. Instead, when a child under 13 years old tries to register for such features on [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/), he or she will be directed to content which does not involve the submission of personal information. Children who indicate that they are under 13 years old will not be able to make credit card purchases or participate in interactive features on [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) that involve the collection and retention of personal information (e.g., entering a sweepstakes) and they will not be contacted about special offers or promotions.

[BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/) works with (and may provide links to) third party sites that offer features described on [BCCI.tv](http://www.bcci.tv/). Registration for these features is pursuant to the privacy policies of the third parties operating the web sites on which registration occurs. Such third party sites may permit children under the age of 13 to register for these features on their sites in accordance with the privacy policies posted on those sites. The operators of those third party sites are responsible for ensuring that their sites comply with applicable laws, regulations, and rules. For more information, please review the privacy policies of each respective third party site.
