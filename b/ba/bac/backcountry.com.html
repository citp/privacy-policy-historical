Your Personal Information

Personal Information as used herein refers to data and facts about you which include, but are not limited to, your name, age, address, email address, financial information (account or credit card numbers), income level, phone number, etc. Personal Information is personal to you in that belongs to you and identifies who you are. Personal Information does NOT include your IP Address as defined below. Personal Information is generally kept for Backcountry business which is primarily used to assist you in your current or future purchases or in analyzing sales trends.

Personal Information is primarily gathered from an order form where customers request information, products and services. Personal Information that is collected by Backcountry is used to bill the user for products and services, and to ship those products. Personal Information is also used to get in touch with the customer when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings by following the directions in those mailings.

Backcountry periodically runs contests on our site in which we ask visitors for selected Personal Information such as contact information (typically the customer's email address) and demographic information (like their zip code, age, or income level). We use Personal Information gathered from our contests to send users promotional material from Backcountry and at times, to some of our partners. Personal Information is gathered by Backcountry for the sole benefit of Backcountry, with the exception of Bill Me Later. If you choose to enroll in Bill Me Later, then we will share your email address with Bill Me Later in order to facilitate the enrollment process. Cookies And IP Addresses

Backcountry also collects, uses, and monetizes IP Addresses. An IP Address is a convention of the Internet Protocol that identifies your "virtual" address on the internet. This IP Address is not personal to you and does not identify any of your Personal Information. All devices connected to a network have an IP Address permitting communication to and from the device. If you connect to the Internet from your computer, then you have an IP Address that looks something like this: 209.17.654.152. In the simplest possible terms, your IP Address contains no data that identifies you as a person. It is only a logical address allowing you to connect to others and others to locate your computer. We use an IP Address for a number of uses as set forth herein. We use an IP Address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. The IP Address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart and to gather broad demographic information. Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart. We use cookies for other purposes such as site personalization. You can reject cookies and still use the Backcountry site, however, it will disable the ability for the site to recognize your cart, your account and any personalization efforts.

Backcountry may monetize or otherwise provide anonymous IP Addresses to third parties. Backcountry sometimes employs other companies and individuals to perform functions on our behalf. Examples of such may include hosting our website, providing marketing assistance, providing search results and links, retargeted advertising, analyzing data, etc. These third parties have access only to IP Addresses, no Personal Information is provided to them.
