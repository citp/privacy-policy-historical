**Security**  
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Once the SkyDesk product is installed, all data is encrypted twice on your PC before it is transmitted to SkyDesk. SkyDesk employs the most sophisticated security measures available and uses the same encryption technology as banks and the US Government. Employees do not have access nor are they able to read customer files. All employees sign a security policy and practices + a confidentiality agreement. Access to data is limited only to e-mail addresses, contact and billing information. SkyDesk offices are located on the San Diego General Atomics complex. The complex is gated, with physical security and requires I.D. to enter the property. In addition to enter the SkyDesk office requires a smart card. To access personal information also requires an additional password. Web security is reviewed on a monthly basis.
