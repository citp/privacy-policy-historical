# Bargainmoose.ca Privacy Policy | BargainMoose Canada

Bargainmoose is a Canadian deals community and while you are making use of the site, we want to let you know about our privacy policy. This privacy policy applies to the all services offered by Bargainmoose, to you, the “Moosers.”

******Personal identific** **ation information**

We may collect personal identification information from Moosers in a variety of ways, including, but not limited to, when Moosers visit or register on Bargainmoose, subscribe to the newsletter, and in connection with other activities, services, features or resources we make available on Bargainmoose. Moosers may be asked for email addresses when signing up or can also visit Bargainmoose anonymously. We will collect personal identification information from Moosers only if they voluntarily submit such information to us. Moosers can always refuse to supply personally identification information, except that it may prevent them from engaging in certain activities on the site itself.

******Non-personal identification information**

We may collect non-personal identification information about Moosers whenever they interact with Bargainmoose. Non-personal identification information may include the browser name, the type of computer and technical information about Moosers’ means of connection to our site, such as the operating system and the Internet service providers utilized and other similar technical information.

**Browser cookies**

Bargainmoose may use “cookies” to enhance the Mooser experience. Mooser’s web browsers place cookies on their hard drive for record-keeping purposes and sometimes to track information about them. Mosoers may choose to set their web browser to refuse cookies, or to alert when cookies are being sent. If they do so, note that some parts of Bargainmoose may not function properly.

******How we use collected information**

Bargainmoose may collect and use Moosers personal information for the following purposes:

  * To improve customer service: information you provide helps us respond to your customer service requests and support needs more efficiently.
  * To personalize user experience: we may use information to understand how Moosers as a group use the services and resources provided on Bargainmoose.
  * To improve Bargainmoose: we may use feedback you provide to improve our services.
  * To run a promotion, contest, survey or other Bargainmoose feature: to send Moosers information they agreed to receive about topics we think will be of interest to them.
  * To send periodic emails: we may use the email address to respond to their inquiries, questions, and/or other requests. If Mosoers decide to opt-in to our mailing list, they will receive emails that may include company news, updates, related product or service information, etc. If at any time the Mooser would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, we include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email.



**How we protect your information**

We adopt appropriate data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction of Moosers’ personal information, username, password and any data stored on Bargainmoose.

**Sharing your personal information**

We do not sell, trade, or rent Moosers personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners, trusted affiliates and advertisers for the purposes outlined above. We may use third party service providers to help us operate our business and Bargainmoose or administer activities on our behalf, such as sending out newsletters or surveys. We may share your information with these third parties for those limited purposes provided that you have given us your permission.

**Third party websites**

Users may find advertising or other content on Bargainmoose that links to the sites and services of our partners, suppliers, advertisers, sponsors, licensors and other third parties. We do not control the content or links that appear on these sites and are not responsible for the practices employed by websites linked to or from Bargainmoose. In addition, these sites or services, including their content and links, may be constantly changing. These sites and services may have their own privacy policies and customer service policies. Browsing and interaction on any other website, including websites which have a link to Bargainmoose, is subject to that website’s own terms and policies.

**Changes to this privacy policy**

Bargainmoose has the discretion to update this privacy policy at any time. When we do, we will revise the updated date at the bottom of this page. We encourage Moosers to frequently check this page for any changes to stay informed about how we are helping to protect the personal information we collect. Moosers acknowledge and agree that it is your responsibility to review this privacy policy periodically and become aware of modifications.

**Your acceptance of these terms**

By using Bargainmoose, you signify your acceptance of this policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use Bargainmoose. Your continued use of Bargainmoose following the posting of changes to this policy will be deemed your acceptance of those changes.

**Contacting us**

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this site, please **[contact us](http://community.bargainmoose.ca/contact)**.
