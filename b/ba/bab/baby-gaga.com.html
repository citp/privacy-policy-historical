Facebook

When using Facebook to register a new account this site may access information from Facebook to personalize your experience. Other than publicly available information this site will request the users Birthday, Email address and text from the About Me section of their Facebook profile. Once registered this data will be retained byBabyGaga.com and may be edited by the user in their account. The forums and member pages of the site can display a users Facebook profile picture and will keep it current with profile picture changes on Facebook. Users are asked if they wish to use their profile picture in this way upon registration and can opt out at any time on the privacy page of their account. This site contains features that can publish users forum posts and show them to a users Facebook friends. Permission will always be requested from a user at the point of posting and information published in this way can be removed from within Facebook if published in error. Social Plugin buttons and boxes containing Facebook content may appear on this site to create more social experiences for users. BabyGaga.com receives none of the information in these boxes. The content in these social plugins comes directly from Facebook. When clicking Like or make a comment using a social plugin, a users activity may be published on Facebook and shown to their Facebook friends who see a plugin on the site. The things a user likes may also appear on their Facebook profile based on settings within Facebook.
