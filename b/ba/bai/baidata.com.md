# Privacy - BARE International

### Bare Associates International, Inc. Online Privacy Policy

We, Bare Associates International Incorporated (registered office: US-22030 Fairfax, Pender Drive 3702. #305.; company registration #: 314809) as data controller.

Hereinafter we are referred to as the “Company” or “We” and have evolved this privacy policy to meet all legal requirements and demonstrate our commitment to the privacy and protection of your data, and furthermore to inform you about the procedure of data processing carried out by the Company. The Company collects and stores your personal data in order to 1) create a program to measure the experience of customers, guests, patients, patrons, riders, fans or clients of our partners with whom we had contracted (hereinafter referred to as ‘partner’), interacting with their services and brand and to have people resources to provide our Company’s services and provide you with information to schedule you to mystery shops/audits and, lastly to pay you for performing said assignments (hereinafter referred to as “Company’s services”). 2) to reach out you and your company by providing tailor-made solutions in customer experience research, also to share with you trends and news in this field.

Thank you for visiting our website, [www.BAREInternational.com](http://www.bareinternational.com/)

This privacy policy tells you how we use personal information collected at this site. Please read this privacy policy before using the site or submitting any personal information.

By using the site, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy policy. These practices may be changed, but any changes will be posted and changes will only apply to activities and information on a going forward, not retroactive basis. You are encouraged to review the privacy policy whenever you visit the site to make sure that you understand how any personal information you provide will be used.

Note: the privacy practices set forth in this privacy policy are for all BARE online platforms.

**Collection of Information**  
**We collect personally identifiable information, like names, postal addresses, email addresses, etc., when voluntarily submitted by our visitors.** The information you provide is used to fulfil your specific request or to lead you through our MSP registration process, unless you give us permission to use it in another manner,

We capture personal data through the Company’s website, which is a secure operated by Automattic Inc.’s WordPress service, a company organized and existing under the laws of the United States of America, with its registered address at 60 29th Street #343

We take all reasonable steps to protect information we received from you from loss, misuse or unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and/or destruction. We have put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure such information. The Company only processes personal information in a way that is compatible with and relevant for the purpose for which it was collected or authorized.

**Cookie/Tracking Technology**  
The Site may use cookie and tracking technology depending on the features offered. Cookie and tracking technology are useful for gathering information such as browser type and operating system, tracking the number of visitors to the Site, and understanding how visitors use the Site. Cookies can also help customize the Site for visitors. Personal information cannot be collected via cookies and other tracking technology, however, if you previously provided personally identifiable information, cookies may be tied to such information. Aggregate cookie and tracking information may be shared with third parties.

**Distribution of Information**  
We may share information with governmental agencies or other companies assisting us in fraud prevention, investigation or marketing activities. We may do so when: (1) permitted or required by law; or, (2) trying to protect against or prevent actual or potential fraud or unauthorized transactions; or, (3) investigating fraud which has already taken place; or, (4) sending and following up on marketing campaigns in regards to service advertisement or registration follow up.

**Commitment to Data Security**  
Your personally identifiable information is kept secure. Only authorized employees, agents, and contractors (who have agreed to keep information secure and confidential) have access to this information. All emails and newsletters from this site allow you to opt out of further mailings.

**Data retaining**

In order to comply with legal requirements, we retain the personal data you provided for the period of one year.

  3. **General Rights**



**The right to access your personal data and correction**

You have the right to access, correct or update your personal data at any time. We understand the importance of this and should you want to exercise your rights, please contact us via our contact page _ _[https://www.bareinternational.eu](https://www.bareinternational.eu/) _ _  or by sending an email to  _[welcome@bareinternational.com](mailto:welcome@bareinternational.com)_  indicating the subject of the contact (e.g. ‘request for access to/correct/update my personal data’).

**The right to data portability**

Your personal data is portable. This means it can be moved, copied or transmitted electronically. However, this right only applies where:

  1. a) The processing is based on your consent;
  2. b) The processing takes place for the performance of a contract;
  3. c) The processing takes place by automated means;



With respect of the above you have the right to receive your personal data provided by you, in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format and have the right to transmit those data to another controller.

If you wish to exercise your right to data portability, please contact us via our contact page [https://www.bareinternational.eu](https://www.bareinternational.eu/)   or by sending an email to  _[dataprivacy@bareinternational.com](mailto:dataprivacy@bareinternational.com)_  indicating the subject of the contact (e.g.’ request to exercise data portability right’).

**The right to deletion of your personal data**

You have the right to request the deletion of your data if:

  1. a) your personal data is no longer necessary in relation to the purposes for which we collected it; or
  2. b) you withdraw the consent that you had previously given us to process your personal data, and there is no other legal ground to process that personal data; or
  3. c) any time if you wish to do so; or
  4. d) the personal data is not being processed lawfully; or
  5. e) your personal data needs to be deleted to comply with the law.



If you wish to delete the personal data we hold about you, please let us know via our contact page [https://www.bareinternational.eu](https://www.bareinternational.eu/) _ _ or by sending an email to  _[dataprivacy@bareinternational.com](mailto:dataprivacy@bareinternational.com)_  indicating the subject of the contact (e.g. ‘request for deletion of my personal data’) and we will respond to your request in 25 days.

If the personal data we collect is no longer needed for any purposes and we are not required by law to retain it, we will do what we can to delete it.

**The right to restriction of processing**

You have the right to restrict the processing of your personal data if

  1. a) you do not believe the personal data we have about you is accurate; or
  2. b) the personal data is not being processed lawfully, but instead of deletion of the personal data, you would prefer us to restrict processing thereof; or
  3. c) we no longer need your personal data for the purposes we collected it, but you require the data in order to establish, exercise or defend legal claims; or
  4. d) you have objected to the processing of your personal data and are awaiting verification on whether your interests related to that objection outweigh the legitimate grounds for processing your data.



If you wish to restrict our processing of your personal data, please let us know via our contact page [https://www.bareinternational.eu](https://www.bareinternational.eu/) _ _ or by sending an email to  _[dataprivacy@bareinternational.com](mailto:dataprivacy@bareinternational.com)_  indicating the subject of the contact (e.g. ‘request for restriction of the processing of my personal data’) and we will respond to your request in 25 days.

The right to object You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data at any time. Please contact us via our contact page [https://www.bareinternational.eu](https://www.bareinternational.eu/)   or by sending an email to  _[dataprivacy@bareinternational.com](mailto:dataprivacy@bareinternational.com)_  indicating the subject of the contact (e.g. ‘request for exercise right to object) and we will respond to your request in 25 days.
