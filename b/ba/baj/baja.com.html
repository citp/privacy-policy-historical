Cookies are not programs that come onto a user's system and damage files. Generally, cookies work by assigning a unique number to theuser that has no meaning outside the assigning site. Users should be aware that baja.com can not control the use of cookies or the resulting information by advertisers or third parties hosting data for baja.com.
