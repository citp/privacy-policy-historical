# Boloji.com - A Study in Diversity - News, Views, Analysis, Literature, Poetry, Features - Express Yourself

Boloji.com is a website dedicated to study India in particular and expressions on the diversity of life. 

We at Boloji follow standard industry practices to maintain your personal information. Boloji does not spam or sell/lease its contributors/writers/user information to third parties.

As a user you should be aware that the communication standards of the Internet industry transmit the URL of the site from which you visit Boloji, the IP address of your computer, the IP address of your ISP, various attributes of your computer such as operating system, resolution and browser software, and the date and time of your various requests for pages on Boloji. Boloji file logging system automatically logs all of this information in its log files for its analysis only.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
