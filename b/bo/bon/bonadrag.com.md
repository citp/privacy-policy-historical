# Privacy Policy | BONA DRAG

At Bona Drag, we keep your information in the strictest confidence. We would never give, sell, or trade your information. If you purchase a product from our  
store, you will have the option to be added to our mailing list. You will receive emails updating you about new store products, sales and events. If you'd rather  
not hear from us kindly email info@bonadrag.com with "unsubscribe" in the subject line. You will immediately be removed from our mailing list.  
When you visit bonadrag.com, cookies (or similar technologies) are used to help us improve your shopping experience. Any information we gather this way  
will be used to enhance and personalize your experience, provide custom, personalized content, diagnose problems and maintain security.  Unless you have  
adjusted your settings to refuse cookies, our system will use cookies when you are browsing and shopping on our website.  These techniques do not collect  
personal information such as your name, email address, postal address, or telephone numbers. You can visit this page to opt out of targeted advertising.  
By using the Bona Drag website, you are accepting the terms and conditions of our policies. If you have questions or concerns, please [contact us](http://bonadrag.com/contact-us) for more information.
