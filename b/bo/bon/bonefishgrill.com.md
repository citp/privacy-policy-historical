# Bonefish Grill - Privacy Policy

Bonefish Grill values your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This policy provides our customers with information about how we use information we gather on-line.

##### Types of Information We Collect

Bonefish Grill collects and maintains personally identifiable information about you in order to meet your needs. We collect your first and last name, postal and e-mail address, phone number, and billing information only when you voluntarily submit it. We use this information to process your orders, notify contest winners, send newsletters and get your valuable feedback on our website and products. Bonefish Grill does not share, sell or trade any of your personal information to third parties without your permission.

##### Aggregate Information

In addition to obtaining your personal information, Bonefish Grill also gathers tracking information such as your IP address, browser type, and most popular web pages visited. We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you in this manner. We use the aggregate information to better serve you by continually improving the content of our website.

##### Making Changes to Personal Information You Provide

You may review and request changes to any personal information you have provided to us on-line. Simply e-mail our customer service department and we will make the requested changes.

##### Changes to Our Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective as of August 18, 2004. Bonefish Grill reserves the right to change this policy at any time and will post all changes on this web page. All changes will apply from the effective date forward, and will not be retroactive. If you have any questions or concerns about our Internet Privacy Policy, please contact our customer service department.
