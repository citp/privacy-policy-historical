**Privacy & Security Policy**

BookinHotels Reservation Network understands and respects your concerns regarding the use and sharing of your personal information. Our Privacy & Security Policy is detailed below. This policy is subject to change without notice and we encourage you to review this policy whenever you use the BookinHotels Reservation Network Web site. 

By visiting the BookinHotels Reservation Network site you are accepting the practices described in the following Privacy and Security policy.

**Information We Collect and How We Use It**  
Personal information is requested and used for identification and booking purposes, managing online accounts and establishing commission payments. All of your information is stored on a secured server. 

Generic and non-identifying information may be gathered and utilized for marketing purposes by BookinHotels Reservation and, in some instances, by BookinHotels Reservation Network affiliates. Information about your activity on the site may be automatically collected when you log in and is used for the sole purpose of improving the BookinHotels Reservation Network Web site. 

**Information Disclosure**  
BookinHotels Reservation Network does not sell or trade personal information to any outside sources. This information may, however, be used internally to help us further improve our services to you.

We may provide your information as required by law or legal process; to protect and defend the rights of BookinHotels Reservation Network, Inc.,The Web site, or its users, and under circumstances we believe reasonably necessary to protect the rights, property or safety of BookinHotels Reservation Network, our users, or the public. 

**Information Security**  
BookinHotels Reservation utilizes the best encryption software in the industry to ensure the highest level of protection for all transmitted personal and financial data. In-house state-of-the-art servers ensure complete control over all systems.

**Links to External and/or Co-Branded Sites**  
Our site may contain links to other sites on the Internet that are not under our control. BookinHotels Reservation Network is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. See our Terms and Conditions of Use for more information.

**Changing/Updating Information**  
You can update your account information at any time by BookinHotels Reservation Network, Inc. 

**E-Mail**  
BookinHotels Reservation Network may periodically send you e-mail messages describing updates, new features, or promotional offers related to this Web site. You can choose to stop receiving this e-mail at any time. If you do not want to receive e-mail from us, please contact BookinHotels Reservation Network, Inc.
