Kidimedia recognizes and understands the importance of your privacy and wants to respect your desire to store and access personal information in a private and secure environment. This Privacy Policy applies to our Platform and describes how we manage, store and utilize Your Personal Data. If you are a minor, we encourage you to inform you parents that you want to make use of the Platform and to invite them to read this Privacy Policy before you use the Platform.

For any Personal Data related to you that is processed by us on behalf of your Teacher via our Platform and for which your Teacher determines the purposes and means, your Teacher is responsible for any compliance with the Regulation of 27 April 2016 on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data (the “ **Regulation** ”) in its capacity as Data Controller.

However, this is limited to the Personal Data specified in Section 2 of this Privacy Policy (“ **Your Personal Data** ”). Please note that the provisions of this Privacy Policy shall only apply to the processing of Your Personal Data by Kidimedia.

## 4\. Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of data that a website asks your browser to store on your computer or other device. This enables a site or service provider’s systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information. Most browsers support cookies, but users can set their browsers to decline them and can delete them whenever they like.

We use the following cookies:

  * **Necessary cookies** : These cookies are essential to visit and use our website, or to use certain parts or functionalities of it. For instance, these cookies allow you to log in securely, and to switch between different pages of the site without having to login again on every page. They are required to make sure your personal information on the Platform is only accessible by you.
  * **Functional cookies** : Functional cookies are cookies that make using our Website more user-friendly or personalized. For instance, these cookies remember your preferences, or keep track of whether you’ve already taken a survey, so we don’t keep asking you the same questions over and over.
  * **Improvement cookies** : We use these cookies to gather information about how our Website is being used, with the goal to improve its functionalities. For instance, there are cookies that track which blog posts are most popular, or how many unique visitors access a particular tutorial.



Most internet browsers are initially set up to automatically accept cookies. Please note that you can change the settings to block cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent to your computer or device. There are a number of ways to manage cookies. Please consult your browser instructions or help screen to learn more about how to adjust or modify your browser settings.

If you disable the cookies that we use, this may impact your experience of our Platform, for example you may not be able to visit certain areas of the Platform.

## 7\. Your rights

### Right of access

If you are concerned or have any questions about Your Personal Data, you have the right to request access to the Personal Data which we hold or process about you. 

We will then provide you with all information relating to Your Personal Data that are being processed and on the source of such data. We shall also provide you with a copy of Your Personal Data undergoing processing. For any further copies requested by you, we shall charge you a fee of 10 EUR for administrative costs.

### Right to rectification and erasure

You have the right to obtain the rectification of any inaccurate Personal Data relating to you or to have any incompleted Personal Data completed free of charge. Furthermore, you shall also have the right to obtain the erasure of Your Personal Data without undue delay if one of the following grounds apply:

  * Your Personal Data have been unlawfully processed;
  * Your Personal Data are no longer necessary in relation to the purpose for which they were collected or otherwise processed; or 
  * you have withdrawn your consent on which the processing of Your Personal Data is based.



Please note that if you are a subscriber to our mailing list, you can make changes at any time to certain Personal Data by clicking the hyperlink included in your sign-up confirmation email.

### Right to object to data processing

You may withdraw at any time your consent to the processing of Your Personal Data by Kidimedia free of charge and without reasoning (including but not limited to the processing of Your Personal Data for direct marketing purposes). 

### Right to data portability

You have the right to receive a copy of Your Personal Data which you have provided to us in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format and have the right to transmit those data to another Data Controller. Where technically feasible, you have the right to have Your Personal Data transmitted directly to another Data Controller.

To exercise any of the abovementioned rights, please contact us at [support@bookwidgets.com](mailto:support@bookwidgets.com) and provide us with any proof of your identity. We will process any requests as soon as possible and at the latest within one month of the receipt of your request.

## 8\. Data retention and deletion

However, we do store Your Personal Data as long as your Account is active or when the processing of Your Personal Data is necessary to allow you to make use of any of the functionalities of the Platform. Upon removal of your Account, we will delete Your Personal Data as soon as possible.
