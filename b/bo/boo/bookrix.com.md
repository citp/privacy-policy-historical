### 24\. Use of cookies

Our websites use cookies to access users, which contain certain personal data of the users, such as user name and books read by the user as well as the page of the relevant book on which the user last interrupted.

Cookies are small text files that are stored by your browser and can be used to retrieve certain information while navigating a website or during future visits. We use both so-called first-party cookies, which are created and used by the BookRix website itself, and third-party cookies that are created on our third-party web site. Cookies allow us, for example, to recognize the last language setting used by your browser on our website or to save and read the permissions assigned to your browser or user account in the current session. They also help us to analyze the behavior of visitors to our website in an anonymized way, thus optimizing the website. Cookies only store text sequences that are always anonymous and in most cases encrypted. We do not store personal data in any way in cookies.

In the field of web analysis and advertising we work together with Google. From the data stored in the cookie, Google learns which pages, products, and services have been called to display appropriate ads. Google uses persistent cookies and session cookies. These cookies do not store any personal information. Disabling or deleting these cookies does not in any way affect the functionality of our website.

You can restrict or disable the use of cookies for all Web browsers by configuring the settings or options of your browser accordingly. The necessary steps vary from browser to browser; You can find the corresponding instructions in the Help menu of your browser. If you disable cookies in your browser, you will not be able to log in with your user account on our website. Your navigation on the website may also be affected.

Our website allows you to subscribe to our newsletter. The personal data that is transmitted to the Controller for the purpose of ordering the newsletter results from the input mask used for this.

This confirmation e-mail is used to check whether the owner of the e-mail address has authorized the receiving of the newsletter as the person concerned.

When registering for the newsletter, we store the IP address of the Internet service provider (ISP) of the computer system used by the data subject at the time of registration and the date and time of registration. The collection of this data is necessary in order to be able to understand the (possible) misuse of the e-mail address of an affected person at a later date and therefore serves the legal protection.

The personal data collected as part of an application for the newsletter will only be used to send our newsletter. Furthermore, subscribers to the newsletter could be informed by e-mail if this is necessary for the operation of the newsletter service or a registration in this regard, as in the case of changes to the Newsletter offer or the change of the technical circumstances. The personal data collected within the scope of the newsletter service will not be passed on to third parties. The subscription to our newsletter can be terminated by the person concerned at any time. Consent to the storage of personal data, which the person concerned has granted us for the newsletter, can also be revoked at any time. For the purpose of revocation of consent, a corresponding link is found in each newsletter. It is also possible to unsubscribe at any time directly on the website of the controller of the newsletter or to communicate this to the controller in other ways.

### 26\. Newsletter-Tracking

The newsletters, contain so-called web beacons. A web beacons is a miniature graphic that is embedded in such e-mails, which are sent in HTML format in order to enable a log file recording and a log file analysis. This allows a statistical evaluation of the success or failure of marketing campaigns. Using the embedded counting pixel, we can see if and when an e-mail was opened.

Such personal data collected via the Web beacons contained in the mailings are stored and evaluated by us or our contractors in order to optimize the mailing delivery and to improve the content of future newsletters even better to the interests of the Person concerned. This personal data is only used for the described purpose and is never passed on to third parties.
