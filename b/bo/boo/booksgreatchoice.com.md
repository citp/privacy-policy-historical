# Privacy Policy - Books Great Choice

Your privacy and protection is very important for us, and we pay special attention at storing private information of our clients. Our company bindingly declare that this web site is absolutely secure and your private information will be kept safe no matter what happens, we are taking all the needed measures to ensure this.

Our site uses an encrypted order form to request information and conduct the product transaction. We collect the user email address and other contact information. This information is provided to Site by the customer, and is entered into our certified secure network, and all data is stored at our secure database, designed specially for this purpose. Any information provided by our users is never shared, sold, or released to any third party outside of our network. We do not collect credit card numbers or any other information that is considered protected by law.
