Born 2 Impress does not disclose, share, exchange, sell or provide your information to any third party.  We treat your information with strict privacy and security and use it solely for the purposes for which it was initially collected.
Information we collect

We store the information you enter on our website when you fill out a form, send an e-mail, call our customer care center or enroll to participate in one of our offers or services. This information may include
your name
your e-mail address
your phone number
your credit card number

We will never request your password, user name, credit card information or any other personal information via e-mail.
Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy on the Internet in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.
Security

Our website uses Secure Sockets Layering (SSL) to encrypt your personal credit information before it is transmitted over the Internet. SSL technology is the industry standard for secure online transactions. Placing an order online at our website is just as safe as giving your credit card number over the phone.
