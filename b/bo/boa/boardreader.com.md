Privacy Policy

Effyis, Inc. (BoardReader.com) is a licensee of the TRUSTe Privacy Program. TRUSTe is an independent, non-profit organization whose mission is to enable individuals and organizations to establish trusting relationships based on respect for personal identity and information by promoting the use of fair information practices. This privacy statement covers the site www.boardreader.com. Because this Web site wants to demonstrate its commitment to our users' privacy, it has agreed to disclose its information practices and have its privacy practices reviewed for compliance by TRUSTe.

If users have questions or concerns regarding this statement, they should first contact our legal department at [legal@boardreader.com](mailto:legal@boardreader.com). If they do not receive acknowledgement of their inquiry or their inquiry is not satisfactorily addressed, they should then contact TRUSTe through the [TRUSTe Watchdog Dispute Resolution Process.](http://www.truste.org/users/users_watchdog.php) TRUSTe will serve as a liaison with the Web site to resolve users concerns.

This Privacy Policy (the "Privacy Policy") is intended to serve as a guideline to describe the ways in which BoardReader.com utlizes information about you, the user. BoardReader.com's User Agreement governs the use of this site. BoardReader.com respects and protects the privacy of the individuals that use BoardReader.com's search services (the "Search Services").

**Cookies**    
BoardReader.com utilizes "cookies" to identify individual users. Cookies help us to tailor our services and improve the quality of those services by tracking and storing user preferences, trends and patterns. BoardReader.com will not disclose or sell cookies to third parties except as required by a valid legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena, statute or court order. You may customize your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent but doing so may impair the performance of certain functions of the Search Services.  

**Information Collected**    
BoardReader.com does not collect any unique information about you (e.g., your name, mailing address, email address or age) except when you specifically provide such information. If you send personal correspondence such as e-mails or letters or if other users or third parties send us correspondence about your activities or postings on our web site, BoardReader.com may collect such information in a file specific to you. In addition to the information you provide to us, BoardReader.com collects and compiles information relating to its users such as time, date, browser type, browser language and IP address (collectively, the "Collected Information") with each query. The Collected Information is used to provide better services to users. The Collected Information is considered property of BoardReader.com. In the event that BoardReader.com or substantially all of its assets are sold or otherwise transferred, the Collected Information may be included in the transferred assets. If your personal information is to be used in a new manner you will be given a choice as to the use of your personal information in this new manner. 

**Information Update**   
We recognize that changes can be made to bulletin board and websites regarding website url's and message board placement. If you have changed your site url, message board software provider, or would like to update your information in any way, please contact us at: [update@boardreader.com](mailto:update@boardreader.com)

**Links**     
The sites, advertisements, boards and messages displayed as search results or linked to by BoardReader.com are developed and published by people over whom BoardReader.com exercises no control. Other sites and boards may send cookies to users, collect data or solicit personal information. Certain information, like [archive threads](http://www.boardreader.com/scripts/texis.exe/viewthread?intable=7&postid=3b6a478ed), may be collected by BoardReader.com as a result of the links displayed as a result of the Search Services. BoardReader.com uses this information to understand and improve the quality of BoardReader.com's Search Services and related technology including the accuracy and efficiency of its Search Services. 

**Sharing Information**     
Aggregate and personal Information about you is disclosed and shared with advertisers, business partners, sponsors and other third parties under limited circumstances.  BoardReader.com may disclose the frequency of visits to our site by the average user. BoardReader.com will release specific personal information in connection with any valid legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena, statute or court order. BoardReader.com may exchange personal information to protect the rights, property or safety of BoardReader.com, its users or other third parties including the exchange of information with other companies and organizations relating to fraud protection and credit risk reduction. 

BoardReader.com does not sell or lease any personally identifiable information about you to any third party. However, BoardReader.com may aggregate (gather up data across all user accounts) at the demographic level and disclose such information to advertisers and other third parties for other marketing and promotional purposes. In these situations, BoardReader.com does not disclose to these entities any information that could be used to identify you personally. Certain information, such as you name, e-mail address, password, are not disclosed to marketing advertisers. Due to the nature of out business, BoardReader.com cannot ultimately ensure that private communications and personal information will not be intercepted by third parties or otherwise disclosed in ways not described in this Privacy Policy. Although BoardReader.com attempts to protect the privacy of out users, we cannot absolutely guaranty that such information will remain private.

**Your Consent and Changes to the Privacy Policy**    
By using BoardReader.com and the Search Services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as we have outlined in this policy and to our User Agreement.  BoardReader.com may change this Privacy Policy from time to time by updating this page to reflect changes in the ways we collect, use or disclose such information. If, however, we are going to use users' personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time of collections we will notify users via email. Users will have a choice as to whether or not we use their information in this different manner. However, if users have opted out of all communication with the site, or deleted/deactivated their account, then they will not be contacted, not will their personal information be used in this new manner.
