Security and Protection of Your Personally Identifiable Information
We are committed to maintaining the security of Personally Identifiable Information (defined below) provided by you through this site. We take significant precautions to protect your Personally Identifiable Information both online and offline. Maintaining a high degree of confidence from you regarding the protection and secure transmission of your Personally Identifiable Information is very important to us. We have implemented a security policy to keep Personally Identifiable Information that is stored in our systems protected from unauthorized access. Our systems are configured with data encryption technologies and firewalls. When you send Personally Identifiable Information to us over the Internet, or we send it to service providers over the Internet, your data is protected by 128-bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to ensure safe transmission. We also have a digital certificate from VeriSign, Inc., a maker of hardware and software used to protect and manage computer network access, certifying the secure nature of this site. All of our servers storing Personally Identifiable Information are kept in a secure environment and may be accessed only by a limited number of authorized persons.

Information Collected
We collect information about you that is provided by you to us through the site or by other means, which may include, but not be limited to, information that can identify you as a specific individual ("Personally Identifiable Information"). Personally Identifiable Information may include, without limitation, your name, address, telephone numbers, e-mail addresses, financial information, transaction and billing information, and/or other such information. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years old. If a your birth date is required to order a specific product or service, you will not be able to enter a birth date that indicates that you are less than 13 years old. You may request that we update information that you provide by contacting Customer Service as indicated in the Terms.

We may share information with service providers or other business affiliates with whom we conduct business in order to provide you with information about new products or services, special offers, discounts, or other information that may be of interest to you. We may disclose Personally Identifiable Information to law enforcement authorities, government agencies, or other parties when provided with a subpoena, court order, or legal documentation of similar effect, or if in our reasonable discretion or good faith belief that the disclosure of such information is necessary to prevent imminent physical harm or financial loss to any person, or is necessary to report suspected illegal activity.

Cookies
We may collect certain technical information about your computer each time you request a page while using this site. This information may include your IP address, operating system and web browser software (e.g., Netscape Navigator or Internet Explorer). If your computer is enabled, we may automatically assign you a "cookie" (i.e., a tiny packet of identifying information passed between your browser and a web site) each time you visit this site. This cookie does not contain any Personally Identifiable Information about you. You can choose at any time to not receive a cookie by enabling your web browser to refuse cookies or prompt you before accepting a cookie. Please be aware that by refusing to accept a cookie you will not be able to make optimal use of this site. We may use third-party advertising technology and cookies to serve ads when you visit web sites upon which we advertise. In addition, we may use web beacons, provided by an ad serving partner, to help manage our online advertising. The information we collect and share through this technology is not Personally Identifiable Information.

This data allows us to improve the quality of your visit by streamlining your ability to navigate this site. Information about you and your use of this site may be aggregated with other information collected on this site or other sites or otherwise used in ways that do not personally identify you or constitute Personally Identifiable Information.
