Security and Protection of Your Personally Identifiable Information
We are committed to maintaining the security of Personally Identifiable Information (defined below) provided by you through this site. Maintaining a high degree of confidence from you regarding the protection and secure transmission of your Personally Identifiable Information is very important to us. We have implemented a security policy to keep Personally Identifiable Information that is stored in our systems protected from unauthorized access. Our systems are configured with data encryption technologies and firewalls. When you send Personally Identifiable Information to us over the Internet, or we send it to service providers over the Internet, your data is protected by 128-bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to ensure safe transmission. All of our servers storing Personally Identifiable Information are kept in a secure environment and may be accessed only by a limited number of authorized persons.

If a your birth date is required to order a specific product or service, you will not be able to enter a birth date that indicates that you are less than 13 years old. Cookies
We may collect certain technical information about your computer each time you request a page while using this site. This information may include your IP address, operating system and web browser software (e.g., Netscape Navigator or Internet Explorer). If your computer is enabled, we may automatically assign you a "cookie" (i.e., a tiny packet of identifying information passed between your browser and a web site) each time you visit this site. This cookie does not contain any Personally Identifiable Information about you. You can choose at any time to not receive a cookie by enabling your web browser to refuse cookies or prompt you before accepting a cookie. Please be aware that by refusing to accept a cookie you will not be able to make optimal use of this site. The information we collect and share through this technology is not Personally Identifiable Information.
