# Boxinus.com

We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.

We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect personal information about you. 

Boxinus.com may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your Boxinus.com account or by placing a prominent notice on our site. Boxinus.com collects personal information when you register with Boxinus.com.

This information may include your name, address, e-mail address, zip code, occupation, birth date and personal interests. Boxinus.com does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission.
