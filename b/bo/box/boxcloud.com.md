# BoxCloud: Dead Simple File Sharing

WiredReach respects your privacy and strongly believes in storing as little data about you as possible. We collect the email addresses of those who communicate with us via email, information about the pages consumers access on the BoxCloud website, and information voluntarily submitted by our customers (for example, filling out a survey or service registration form). 

The information we collect is used to improve the quality of the BoxCloud website and Service, to communicate information about the BoxCloud Service, and to bill your account for services provided.

Your information will not be sold to or shared with other companies or organizations expept when it is necessary to share this information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Terms of Service, or as otherwise required by law.

## Use of Cookies

When you use the BoxCloud Service we use cookies to store current session information on your computer. Cookies are small pieces of information stored on your hard drive that help you more easily navigate the BoxCloud website. You must enable cookies on your web browser to use the BoxCloud Service.

## Security and Storage

We believe the best way to guarantee your privacy is by putting you in control and letting you store and manage your own data at all times. After all, it's your data. We never store any of your Content on our servers and use strong 256-bit encryption to protect all BoxCloud related communication to and from your machine. 

## Cancellation/Opt out 

You can cancel your account at any time from your Account Page. Cancellations are effective immediately and all userid, password and other account information is deleted and cannot be recovered. 

## Changes in this Privacy Policy

WiredReach may update this privacy statement at any time for any reason. If we significantly change how we handle personal information, we will notify the primary email address specified in your account. We will also place a notice on our blog. 

## Contacting WiredReach

If you have any questions about this privacy statement or the BoxCloud website, you can contact us at support at boxCloud dot com. You may always view the most recent copy of the Privacy Policy here: [http://www.boxcloud.com/privacy.html](https://web.archive.org/privacy.html)
