  1. **Collection of personal information**

The operator declares that the user is not obligated to disclose any personal data for the purposes of the game to the operator in terms of the legislation regarding the protection of personal data and that the operator will not demand this kind of data. The operator bears no liability for the user's disclosure of his/her personal data to a third party, particularly during the payment process, and if such disclosure occurs, no obligations arise for operator regarding this disclosure. The user's e-mail address is not considered as personal information for the purposes of the legislation regarding the protection of personal data. The user has the option to fill out his/her name, region and phone number in the account settings, but this is completely optional and voluntary. Personal information such as name or mailing address may be individually requested by the operator in case the user has won a prize in a competition run by the operator or a partner of the operator. In case such information is requested, it is only used for the purposes of delivering the prizes to the users. The user may decline to disclose this information in such case, but it will also mean that he/she is not entitled to receive the prize won in the competition. 

  

  2. **Cookies and other information**

The operator may automatically store such information as the user's IP address or browser type. The operator may also store cookies on the user's machine without which the user would be unable to sign into his/her account and use operator's services. 

Cookies are small amounts of information in the form of text files which the website stores on the user's device. Cookies allow the operator to monitor traffic on the website, simplify the user's visits to the website (for example, by remembering which language the user selected or the last time you logged on to the website). A temporary cookie is also used to keep track of the user's "session". Without that temporary cookie (which is not stored after the user quits the browser), the user would have had to log in again on each page. 

Except for the purpose of prosecuting unauthorized use of www.boxingduel.com and all offered services on this website, the operator evaluates such information only for statistical and performance-related purposes. 

The user gives the operator his/her consent to store cookies on the user's device by allowing his/her Internet browser to install and store cookies. If the user does not wish the website to install and store cookies on his/her device, he/she can change the settings on his/her Internet browser to reject cookies. For more information about how to reject cookies using the Internet browser settings please consult the "Help" section of your Internet browser (or alternatively visit www.aboutcookies.org). Please note that, if you do set your Internet browser to reject cookies, you may not be able to access many of the functions on the website. 

In addition, third party advertisers whose advertisements appear on the website or whose websites the user visits when he/she clicks certain links on the website may use and install cookies on the user's device. Please consult the relevant third party advertiser's terms and conditions and privacy policy to find out how they collect and use your personal data and to establish whether they use cookies and what they may use them for. 

  

  3. **Registration via Facebook**

When registering via Facebook, the user's e-mail address connected with the Facebook account is stored in the database of the operator. This e-mail address can be changed in the account settings at any time. E-mail address is a necessary part of all user accounts registered in the game. 

  

  4. **E-mail address verification**

The user may use the account without verifying his/her e-mail address, but only to a certain extent. In order to use all the advantages of the user account, the user must verify his/her registered e-mail address by clicking the verification link in the e-mail delivered by the operator. 

  

  5. **Newsletters**

The operator reserves the right to send information about the Boxing Duel project, other projects of the operator or operator's partners to the user's e-mail address. The user is entitled to opt out of these newsletters at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any of the newsletters. 

  

  6. **Transmission of personal information**

The user's personal information and e-mail address will not be sold to any third parties by the operator. The operator may transmit the user's data to payment providers (e.g. credit card companies, banks, PayPal, mobile services, etc.) in order to provide support for the users, but only to the extent necessary to provide sufficient support. In the case that POWERPLAY MANAGER, s.r.o. is sold, user's customer information will generally be passed on with the transmitted part of the company. In this case, user's data is also subject to this Privacy Policy and the relevant data protection statutes. 

  

  7. **Storage of data**

The operator is entitled to store the e-mail address and the IP addresses of the user even after removal of the user's account from the game for any reason only for the necessary period of time in accordance with the applicable laws. The operator declares that the e-mail addresses of removed accounts will not be used for the purposes of sending newsletters about the game, other projects of the operator or operator's partners and that they will not be disclosed to any third party for these purposes. 

  

  8. **Security of your personal information**

The operator processes the information collected from users according to the data protection law effective in the Slovak Republic. All persons working with personal data of users are obliged and have been advised to maintain data with confidentiality and privacy obligations.
