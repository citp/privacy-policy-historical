# Privacy Policy

**Boxwood Technology Website Privacy Policy**

Boxwood Technology is committed to protecting the privacy of our visitors' personal information. This Policy explains how we gather and use the information that we collect from our website, located at www.boxwoodtech.com. By using Boxwood's website you signify your acceptance of our privacy policy. This policy may change over time; amendments to this statement will be posted at this URL and will be effective when posted. Your continued use of this site following the posting of any amendment, modification or change shall constitute your acceptance of the then current policy. 

**Collection and Use of Information**

If you browse through Boxwood's website or download information, we will gather and store your IP address automatically on our web servers. This information does not identify you personally. Boxwood uses this information to learn about the number of visitors to our site, the types of technology our visitors use, and to help diagnose problems with our server and administer our website. 

When you view our website we also store information on your computer in the form of a "cookie." Cookies are small pieces of information stored on your hard drive, not on our site. Cookies do not spy on you or otherwise invade your privacy, and they cannot invade your hard drive and steal information. Rather, they help you navigate a website as easily as possible and allow us to learn which areas of the site are most frequently used to help us improve the site in the future.

If you choose to complete our online form to request additional information, Boxwood may use that information to offer you products or services, to send information to you, to respond to you and for other purposes related to the reason you provided the information to Boxwood. Your information will not be provided to any other organizations.

**Links**

Boxwood's website may contain links to sites or services created and maintained by other organizations. Please note that Boxwood does not control, and is not responsible for, the accuracy, timeliness, security or even the continued availability or existence of this outside information. Opinions expressed on other sites are not necessarily those of Boxwood, nor does Boxwood endorse, warrant, or guarantee products or services described or offered on other sites. Neither is Boxwood responsible for the contents of any websites that choose to link to Boxwood.

Other companies linked to Boxwood's website may collect information about you when you view or click on these sites. Boxwood cannot control this collection of information. You should contact these companies directly if you have any questions about their use of the information they collect.

**Opting Out**

You may opt-out of receiving communications from Boxwood Technology by sending an e-mail to optout@boxwoodtech.com.
