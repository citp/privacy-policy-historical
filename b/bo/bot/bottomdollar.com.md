# Bottom Dollar: Find stuff cheap and fast!

**Overview  
\----------**

WebCentric, Inc. has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website, bottomdollar.com.

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, to administer our Web site, and to gather broad demographic information. We also assign cookies to users to track aggregrate traffic patterns. 

This site contains links to other sites. WebCentric, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

We utilize online survey, feedback, recommend a friend, affiliate signup, and newsletter signup forms to interact with our users. Some of these forms gather email addresses, phone numbers, and other personal information. None of the information gathered by these forms is disclosed to third parties.

For those users that explicitly signup for commercial mailings, we use a third party (PostMaster Direct, http://www.postmasterdirect.com) to collect and market the collected information. Users can opt-out of the commercial mailings at any time after signing up.

We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. Cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information. Some customer data is shared with the advertising companies.

**Security  
\----------**  
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All information is password protected from external access.

**Choice/Opt-Out  
\----------**  
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor. Users can also elect to have us remove their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service by sending an appropriate email to [webmaster@bottomdollar.com.](mailto:webmaster@bottomdollar.com)

**Correct/Update  
\----------**  
This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying  
information previously provided by sending an appropriate email to  
[webmaster@bottomdollar.com.](mailto:webmaster@bottomdollar.com)
