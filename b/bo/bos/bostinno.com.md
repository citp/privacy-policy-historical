This Privacy Policy Statement ("Privacy Policy") is applicable to the Streetwise Media, Inc. ("Streetwise") Websites located at [http://streetwise-media.com](http://streetwise-media.com/), [http://bostinno.com](http://bostinno.com/), [http://inthecapital.com](http://inthecapital.com/), [http://intheempire.com](http://intheempire.com/) (the "Website" or “Websites”), and all services provided thereon ("Services"), including all Streetwise Channels, Comments, Community Features, Blogs, Forums ( as defined in the Terms of Service ). **Notice**

Streetwise respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information that you provide in connection with your use of the Websites. This Privacy Policy is intended to provide you notice of Streetwise’s information practices, including the types of information gathered, how the information is used and safeguarded, and how you may control the maintenance and sharing of your information.

**Collection of Personally Identifiable Information**

You can visit the Streetwise Websites any time without providing any information about yourself. However, in order to use certain portions of the Services (e.g., to post articles, comment(s), or use other Community Features as described in the Streetwise Media, Inc. [Terms of Service](http://streetwise-media.com/corporate/termsofservice.html) **** (located at [http://streetwise-media.com/corporate/termsofservice.html](http://streetwise-media.com/corporate/termsofservice.html)), you may be required to register and create an “ **Account** ” as defined in the above mentioned [Terms of Service](http://streetwise-media.com/corporate/termsofservice.html).

There is no cost to create an Account, however, there may be a cost to update your company, business, or organizations Account to access or use premium Community Features. In either case, you will be asked to choose a user name, password, and display name, and to provide certain personally identifiable information, such as your real name and email address, age, address, and telephone numbers. As part of your Streetwise public profile, which may include your real name, you will be asked to provide additional personally identifiable information (PII) which may including your company, occupation, and bio; as well as other public profile links or references or PII including Twitter handle or link, Google+, LinkedIn, Facebook, or similar social services, as well as a RSS Feed of your Streetwise Community Feature activities.  Streetwise may publicly display this information in your public profile as well as part of your Community Feature activities, which include but are not limited to posts, comments, message boards, or chat rooms. If you do provide Streetwise with personally identifiable information, we will take all reasonable and appropriate steps to protect this information. We also urge you to keep your password confidential and refrain from sharing it with others. Streetwise will not disclose, share, sell or rent personally identifiable information to any third party without your prior consent.

Other than registration information, you may be asked to provide certain personal information in order to use or participate in certain promotions such as giveaways, sweepstakes, contests, surveys, newsletters, etc. ("Special Promotions"). In these instances, the Website will contain information regarding the intended use of the requested information.

Streetwise may also use technology, such as cookies (as described below), transparent gifs, web beacons, frames, server log analysis and other technologies as they are developed, to enhance your experience on Streetwise. 

**Special Notice Regarding Children Under 13 Years of Age**

Streetwise respects the privacy of children of all ages. Streetwise particularly recognizes the sensitivity of personal information concerning children under the age of 13 and therefore provides this special notice to parents and guardians regarding the collection and use of information collected from children in this age group.

The Streetwise Websites are not directed towards young children. Streetwise is intent on complying with applicable laws and regulations regarding children, including the United States ' Children's Online Privacy Protection Act. Specifically, Streetwise will not knowingly collect and maintain any personally identifiable information from children under 13. _If you’re under 13, please do not provide us with any personal information about yourself (such as your name, your e-mail address or your phone number). _Parents are hereby put on notice that as a result of the foregoing, Streetwise does not provide a mechanism to obtain parental consent.

If you are a parent or guardian who has discovered that your child has submitted his or her personal information, Streetwise will make reasonable efforts to delete the information from its database, at your request. To request the deletion of your child's information, please send an email to [businessaffairs@streetwise-media.com](mailto:businessaffairs@streetwise-media.com) **** and be sure to include in your message the same user name and password that your child submitted.

**Non-Personally Identifiable Information**

For example, you may be asked to provide information concerning your television viewing habits, music or other entertainment preferences, purchasing habits, and the like. In general, you can browse Streetwise while remaining anonymous. However, in many cases, if you wish to interact on the Websites, Streetwise will collect certain non-personally identifiable information about your use of the Services. For example, Streetwise might collect information concerning the type of Internet browser or computer operating system you are using, the domain name of your Internet service provider, your "click path" through the Streetwise Websites, the Website or advertisement from which you linked to the Streetwise Website, and/or the Website that you go to when leaving the Streetwise Website. The cookie was placed by us, or by another advertiser or provider. With both cookies and transparent GIF files, the information that we collect and share is anonymous and not personally identifiable. It does not contain your name, address, telephone number, or email address. These companies may use non-personally identifiable information (e.g., click stream information, browser type, time and date, subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over) during your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of interest to you. These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information. To learn or more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising for some companies, you can visit the [NAI](http://www.networkadvertising.org/) at [http://www.networkadvertising.org](http://www.networkadvertising.org/) and [http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/) to opt out.

**Cookies, Pixels, and Other Similar Technologies**

Cookies are small pieces of data that are stored on your computer, mobile phone or other device.  Pixels, transparent gifs or clear gifts, and web beacons are small blocks of code on webpages that do things like allow another server to measure viewing of a webpage and often are used in connection with cookies. The Streetwise Websites use technologies like cookies, pixels, and local storage, like on your browser or device, which is similar to a cookie but holds more information, to provide and understand a range of products and services. Streetwise uses these technologies to do things like:

· Make the Streetwise Websites easier or faster to use; 

· Facilitate your signing in to use the Website and its Community Features or Services;

· Enable Website, Community Features, and Services and to store information about you (including on your device or in your browser cache) and your use of the Streetwise Websites;

· Match your interests and preferences;

· Deliver, understand and improve advertising;

· Monitor and understand the use of our products, services, and Community Features;

· And to protect you, others and Streetwise.

Most Internet browsers will allow you to erase cookies from your computer hard drive, block acceptance of cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. Please note, however, that if you block cookies, many portions of the Website will not function properly. You should refer to your browser instructions or help screen to learn more about how to manage cookies.

**Our Commitment to Data Security**

If you give Streetwise sensitive information (e.g., credit card numbers), Streetwise will take all commercially reasonable steps to protect such information by establishing a secure connection with your Web browser. Streetwise employs a security technology known as a secure-socket-layer ("SSL") to protect the transmission of payment information to the Website. Unless otherwise specified herein or on the Website, credit card numbers are used only for payment processing and are not retained for marketing or any other purposes.

While Streetwise takes precautions against possible breaches in its databases, no Website or internet transmission or connection is completely secure. Consequently, Streetwise cannot guarantee that unauthorized access, hacking, data loss, or other breaches will never occur.

Streetwise urges you to keep your personal information safe by memorizing your password or keeping it in a safe place, separate from your account information, as sharing your password with others may lead to unauthorized access to your information.
