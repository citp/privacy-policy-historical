### 2\. TYPE OF INFORMATION MEDIA PRIMA DIGITAL COLLECTS 

2.1 Personal Information

2.1.1 Media Prima Digital collects and uses your personal information (i.e. personal information which identifies you such as, your name and residential or postal address, date of birth, gender, telephone and fax numbers, email address, and credit/debit card information [if applicable]; lifestyle and interests) only with your knowledge and consent when you use the Website or the Digital Service, make use of our product and service enquiries, or when you respond to communications from Media Prima Digital (such as contests, questionnaires or surveys) and other information collected on registration or through surveys.

2.1.2 When you request pages from the Website server, it automatically collects some information about your preferences, including your internet protocol (IP) address. Media Prima Digital uses this for account management and to help diagnose problems with the Website server, to administer the Website and for the purposes set out in Section 3 below.

2.2 Non-personal Information 

2.2.1 Media Prima Digital may automatically collect non-personal information about you such as the type of internet browsers you use or the website from which you linked to the Website. Media Prima Digital may also aggregate details which you have submitted to the Website (for example, your age and the town where you live). You cannot be identified from this information and it is only used to assist Media Prima Digital in providing an effective service on the Website. No personally identifiable information is gathered during the use of the Website except for information given by you via e-mails, which is in a secure portion of the Website.

2.2.2 If at any time the Website offers any e-mail, feedback or forum facilities, Media Prima Digital may collect the information, which you disclose. Such information will be used in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2.2.3 When you access the Website, Media Prima Digital’s web servers will automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website. These server logs may include information such as your web request, internet protocol (IP) address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser (explained further in Section 6).

2.3 Third Party Information.

2.3.1 Media Prima Digital does not monitor or permanently store third party information save for the purpose of accounting, billing [if applicable] or record keeping, or unless required by law.

### 6\. COOKIES 

6.1 Media Prima Digital may store some information (commonly known as a "cookie") on your computer when you log in to the Website. The Website is able to read these cookies for information purposes when you revisit the Website. The type of information Media Prima Digital collects as a result of a cookie being accepted by you is specific to your personal computer and includes the internet protocol (IP) address, the date and time the personal computer visited the Website, what parts of the Website were looked at and whether the web pages requested were delivered successfully. This information is anonymous; it represents a computer rather than a person.

6.2 Media Prima Digital uses the cookie information to improve its knowledge of the use of the Website and to enable Media Prima Digital to be able to ascertain whether the Website is operating at an optimal level. This allows Media Prima Digital to enhance its web offerings to you and to provide an enjoyable and an innovative online experience.

6.3 These cookies do not permanently record data and they are not stored on your computer's hard drive. Once you close your browser, the cookie is deleted. You can erase or block this information by changing the settings on your computer (please refer to your help screens or manuals). If you do erase or block this information you may not be able to utilise some features of the Website.

6.4 Some of Media Prima Digital’s business partners may use cookies on the Website (for example, links to business partners). Media Prima Digital does not want its business partners to use cookies to track the Website’s users' activities once they leave the Website. However, Media Prima Digital may not have total control over how its business partners may use cookies on the Website. If you become aware that Media Prima Digital’s business partner is placing an unwanted cookie on your hard drive, please contact Media Prima Digital to assist Media Prima Digital in resolving the problem.

### 8\. DATA RETENTION

8.1 Your Personal Data can be retained until three (3) months from the date you notify Media Prima Digital of your wish to deactivate the Digital Service’s membership / account, and may be retained in either a physical or electronic format. Upon the expiry of the aforementioned period, your personal data shall be securely and permanently deleted from Media Prima Digital’s database.

  


### 9\. SECURITY

9.1 Security measures, including Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption technology, are in place in order to protect personal information that Media Prima Digital collects from unauthorised access, improper use or disclosure, unauthorised modification, unlawful destruction or accidental loss. The said personal information will be stored and backed up securely in an environmentally-controlled location. Only authorized people with user ID and password from Media Prima Digital have access to the information. All Media Prima Digital’s employees and data processors, who have access to and are associated with the processing of personal information, are obliged to respect the confidentiality of your Digital Service’s membership / account information.

9.2 You are advised to log off from your Digital Service’s membership / account, clear your cache and close the browser window after use so as to minimise any risk on the user ID and password being compromised. You are further advised not to reveal your user ID and password to others. If you become aware of any loss, theft or unauthorized use of your user ID and password, please send an email to [Media Prima Digital](mailto:contactus@altmedia.com.my).
