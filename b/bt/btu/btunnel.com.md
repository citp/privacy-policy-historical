# BTunnel.com will protect your anonymity on the internet, helping you evade url and ip filters!

Btunnel does not intentionally collect or maintain any personally identifyable user data. In the event that personally identifyable or otherwise sensitive data is logged along with standard server performance data, it will not be used for any purpose whatsoever. Upon learning of any such logging, reasonable efforts will be taken to delete the logs and stop the logging process.  
Btunnel does use 3rd party advertisers which may track users via cookies to acertain the marketing performance of their ads.

Third Party Advertising  
We use BUDS INC and other third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please click here 

Third Party Cookies  
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique “cookie” on your browser.
