BTunnel.com Privacy Policy




Btunnel does not intentionally collect or maintain any personally identifyable user data. In the event that personally identifyable or otherwise sensitive data is logged along with standard server performance data, it will not be used for any purpose whatsoever. Upon learning of any such logging, reasonable efforts will be taken to delete the logs and stop the logging process.
Btunnel does use 3rd party advertisers which may track users via cookies to acertain the marketing performance of their ads.
