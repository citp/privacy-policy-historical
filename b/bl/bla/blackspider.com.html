BlackSpider Privacy Policy Statement

What do BlackSpider Technologies do with personal information?

Your personal information is only collected by this site when voluntarily submitted by you. BlackSpider Technologies may use the information collected to provide you with news and other information which you may have requested. Occasionally BlackSpider Technologies may notify you about important changes to the site and new information or services that BlackSpider Technologies think you may find valuable.

If you do not wish to receive this information, please send an email to the Data Controller at the address mentioned below.

Email: support@blackspider.com

What about "cookies"?

A "cookie" is a small file stored on your computer by a web site that assigns a numerical user ID and stores certain information about your online browsing. BlackSpider Technologies may use cookies to help us recognise you as a prior user of this site and to personalise your browsing. BlackSpider Technologies do not store any personal information about you using cookies technology.

Will BlackSpider Technologies disclose the information you collect to outside parties?

Any personal information received from you will be retained by us and will not be sold, transferred or otherwise disclosed to any third party, unless such disclosure is required by law or court order.

BlackSpider Technologies may provide aggregate statistics about our users and traffic patterns and related site information to reputable third parties, but these statistics will include no information personally identifying you.

Access to personal information
