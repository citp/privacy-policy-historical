# Privacy Policy for BlackFriday.com

Effective Date: November 7, 2012

## GENERAL

By accessing this site, you agree that you have read and understand the Reinvent International, Inc. (BlackFriday.com) privacy policy which describes how we use your personal information, and what options you have to update or request the deletion or correction of information submitted to us via www.blackfriday.com. If you do not agree with the terms and conditions of this policy, please do not use the Site.

## INFORMATION WE COLLECT & WHY

Personal Information Submitted By You. The Personal Information we collect from you includes but is not limited to your name, email address, zip code and phone number. We collect Personal Information you provide to us for purposes of (a) signing up for our Black Friday notification service; (b) filling out and submitting one of our online surveys; (c) participating in one of our contests; and (d) your general use of the Site such as your participation in one of our online comments.

## HOW WE USE THE INFORMATION COLLECTED

Personal Information is used by BlackFriday.com for the purpose for which it was submitted. For example, if you submit your email address and other information required to subscribe to our Black Friday notification, we will use that Personal Information to send you our Black Friday notification. Please see below for your Choice/Opt Out options. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer the Site.

## DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION TO THIRD PARTIES

We will share your Personal Information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy policy. We do not sell your Personal Information to third parties.

Demographic and aggregate Information may be shared by us with third party advertisers. Aggregate Information contains no personal identifiers. BlackFriday.com makes every reasonable effort to preserve user privacy. However, we may also disclose your personal information as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process, when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request, to any other third party with your prior consent to do so.

## TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

We use cookies on this site. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user’s computer for record-keeping purposes. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our site.

We use both session ID cookies and persistent cookies. A session ID cookie expires when you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains on your hard drive for an extended period of time. Persistent cookies are used to automatically log you in or remember you when you come to our site. You can remove persistent cookies by following directions provided in your Internet browser’s “help” file.

If you reject cookies, you may still use our site, but your ability to use some areas of our site will be limited.

Our third party partners employ clear gifs (a.k.a. web tracking images), images, and scripts that help them better manage content on our site. We do not tie the information gathered to our Customers’ or Users’ personally identifiable information.

## SALE OR TRANSFER OF INFORMATION

In the event of a sale, merger, liquidation, dissolution, or transfer of part of the business, trade or assets of BlackFriday.com, all Information collected about you via this Site may be sold, assigned, or transferred to the party acquiring all or substantially all of the equity or assets or business of BlackFriday.com. Subject to your election to opt out as described below. You will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our site of any change in ownership or uses of your Personal Information, as well as any choices you may have regarding your Personal Information.

## SECURITY

The security of your Personal Information is important to us. We follow generally accepted standards to protect the Personal Information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## THIRD PARTIES; LINKS

Linking. This Policy applies only to www.blackfriday.com. This Site may include links to the web sites of both our affiliated and non-affiliated entities (Other Sites), including access to content, products and services of such affiliated and non-affiliated entities. Whenever you choose to access Other Sites via this Site, a separate browser window will launch and you will leave this Site. We urge you to familiarize yourself with the individual privacy and other terms for each linked site prior to submitting your Personal Information.

## BLOG/FORUM

Our site offers publicly accessible blogs or community forums. You should be aware that any information you provide in these areas may be read, collected, and used by others who access them. To request removal of your Personal Information from our blog or community forum, please contact us. In some cases, we may not be able to remove your Personal Information, in which case we will let you know if we are unable to do so and why.

## SOCIAL MEDIA WIDGETS

Our site includes social media features, such as the Facebook Like button and widgets, such as the ShareThis button or interactive mini-programs that run on our site. These features may collect your IP address, which page you are visiting on our site, and may set a cookie to enable the feature to function properly. Social media features and widgets are either hosted by a third party or hosted directly on our site. Your interactions with these features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing it.

## SINGLE SIGN ON

You can log in to our site using sign-in services such as Facebook Connect or an Open ID provider. These services will authenticate your identity and provide you the option to share certain personal information with us such as your name and email address to pre-populate our sign up form. Services like Facebook Connect give you the option to post information about your activities on this Web site to your profile page to share with others within your network.

## NOTIFICATION OF CHANGES

Users will be notified of changes to this Policy by a notice posted on the Site within ten business days prior to the changes taking effect. Any material change in how Personal Information is collected or used by BlackFriday.com, will cause a notice to be posted on the site prior to the change. An email will also be sent to registered newsletter readers when a change is made, giving them the opportunity to opt-out.

## CONSENT

By using this Site, you consent to the use, storage and disclosure of your Information by us in the manner described in this Policy. We reserve the right to make changes to this Policy from time to time and will alert you to such changes via the Notification procedure described in the preceding paragraph.

## CORRECT/UPDATE/DELETE

Users may submit requests to BlackFriday.com for changing, deleting, and modifying Personal Information previously submitted, by sending an email.

We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.
