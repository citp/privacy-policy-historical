# Privacy Policy

We do not collect any customer information.Please see our payment processor's Privacy Policy concerning collected information. IBill and PSW are our credit card processors.Hardcorecash, does not store or maintain any credit or personal information. All information is stored at iBill's or PSW's highly secure sites.iBill's Privacy Policy can be viewed by [clicking here](http://www.ibill.com/sampleprivacypolicy.cfm), and PSW's can be viewed by [clicking here](http://pswbilling.com/privacy_policy.html).
