## Our Privacy Policy

Any information that you, the user of our site, disclose to 1-Source or any of its representatives remains between you and us. We will not disclose it to any third party. We do not sell, lease, exchange, or in any other way allow access to your information. This applies to any information from you, including contact information, and offers made.

The only information that we receive from or about you is what you decide to send us. We do not gather information automatically or without your knowledge using internet technology.

This site contains links to other Web sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such sites.
