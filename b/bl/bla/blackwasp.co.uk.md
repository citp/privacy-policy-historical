# Privacy Policy :: BlackWasp Software Development

##  Personal Information Collection

Your personal information will only be collected if you submit it to BlackWasp using the contact form.  Your name, email address and any comments you make will be retained.  If you elect not to have your details recorded by indicating that you do not want to be contacted then your details will be erased after your query is answered.

##  Web Site Logs

Information is gathered automatically in the form of web site logs and using Google Analytics.  This includes anonymous information such as your web service provider and IP address.  The pages that you visit are recorded in these logs.  This information is used for analysis purposes only to allow improvement to the site.  No personal information is gathered in this manner.

##  Use of Information

Your personal information will be used to contact you when new developments are made to the web site or when new articles or other items are published to the web site.  Your information will not be distributed to third parties unless this is required by law.

##  Linked Web Sites

The BlackWasp web site contains links to other web sites.  The privacy policy of these web sites may differ from that of the BlackWasp web site.

##  Policy Changes

This privacy policy is subject to change without notice. Changes to the policy will be made available via this web page.
