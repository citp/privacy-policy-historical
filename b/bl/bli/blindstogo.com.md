# Blinds To Go - Custom blinds and shades - Privacy Policy

##### Policy

Blinds To Go is committed to maintaining the accuracy, confidentiality, security and privacy of customer personal information.

##### Security

Information is retained in controlled facilities, protected from unauthorized access and kept only as long as is reasonably required.

##### Confidentiality

Your personal information will never be provided to third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent. Even where we allow other companies to provide services on our behalf, we only provide them with the personal information they need to provide the service. They are required to keep the information confidential and may not use it for any other purpose.

##### Control and Access

You may, at any time, choose to no longer receive marketing material from Blinds To Go by email. Upon request, you may view the personal information about you that Blinds To Go holds.
