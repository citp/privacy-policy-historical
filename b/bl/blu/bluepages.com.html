We use your personal information for three primary purposes. First, to help you quickly order copies of our publications or receive information and other new products, special offers, updated information and other new services from the Christian Blue Pages; third, to inform you of upcoming Christian or community events in your area. The Christian Blue Pages does not provide your e-mail address or phone number to third parties. If you do not want CHRISTIANBLUEPAGES.com to send you e-mail or postal mail regarding Christian Blue Pages' products or services, or if you do not wish to be updated on upcoming events, you may "opt out" when you give us your personal information, by selecting the "opt-out" button stating that you do not wish to receive such information from the Christian Blue Pages.
