# Bluefirepoker

When you open an account with BlueFirePoker.com, we will ask you to provide us with certain personal information. We will use your personal information to complete various transactions, to communicate with you, ensure compliance with our Terms and Services and to provide you with customer support services. We limit access to your personal information to authorized employees of the Company, its agents and, as described above, to service providers who adhere to stringent standards of privacy.Â 

We use security measures to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of data used by our system. We will never share, sell, or rent individual personal information with anyone without your advance permission or unless ordered by a court of law. Information submitted to us is only available to employees managing this information for purposes of contacting you or sending you emails based on your request for services relating to BlueFirePoker.
