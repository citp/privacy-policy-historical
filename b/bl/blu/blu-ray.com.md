# Blu-ray.com - Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy

### Cookies

Blu-ray.com uses cookies to store various site settings to enhance site usability and experience. Some 3rd party advertisers also use cookies to try to display relevant ads and to not display the same ads several times. If you wish to block cookies you can do so in your web browser settings. 

### IP address

Blu-ray.com automatically logs all accesses on the site (including access from the apps) with IP, date and referer. The purpose is to gather- and analyze general statistics to improve the site and find possible server problems. Those IP addresses are **not** shared with any third parties. 

### Registered members

* To benefit from all advanced site features (including features in the apps), visitors can optionally register on the site with a username and password. The password is encrypted and stored in the database.
* Members are also required to provide a valid e-mail address (also stored in the database) to prove that they are a human being and not an automated spam bot. The e-mails are **not** shared with any third parties.
* All data about/related to a member account that is stored on the server is property of Blu-ray.com. This data is **not** shared with any third parties.

### Third party websites

Links from Blu-ray.com to external web sites is for your convenience and does not mean Blu-ray.com endorse those web sites. Blu-ray.com has no control over external web sites and takes no responsibility for external links. 

### Third party advertising

This site allows third-party companies to serve ads and/or collect certain anonymous information when you and other Internet users visit. These companies may use non-personally identifiable information (e.g., click stream information, browser type, time and date, subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over) during your visits to this site and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you. These companies typically use a cookie or third-party web beacon to collect this information. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit <http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp>. 

### Contact

If you have any questions regarding Blu-ray.com's Privacy Policy, please contact us in our [Feedback forum](http://forum.blu-ray.com/feedback-forum/) or use our [Contact form](http://www.blu-ray.com/contact/).
