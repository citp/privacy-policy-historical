# The Christian Blue Pages

**BLUEPAGES.com Statement of Privacy**

The Internet is an amazing tool. It has the power to change the way we live, and we're starting to see that potential today. With only a few mouse-clicks, you can follow the news, look up facts, buy goods and services and communicate with others from around the world - even people you might never have met. It's important to the BLUEPAGES to help its customers retain their privacy when they take advantage of all the Internet has to offer. The BLUEPAGES is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful, safe, online experience that you can get anywhere. 

The BLUEPAGES has a mission to unite the body of Christ world wide and build users' trust and confidence in the Internet by promoting Christian principles in the secular business market and of fair information practices. Questions regarding this statement should be directed to info@bluepage.com. If the Web site has not responded to your inquiry within seven days or your inquiry has not been satisfactorily addressed, contact the Publisher, David Kelchner toll free @ 888-777-2583 ext. 120

**BLUEPAGES.com and Your Privacy**

BLUEPAGES.com may ask you to register and provide information that personally identifies you ("Personal Information") in certain circumstances, including for example when you enter a contest, join our online listing or advertisement program or request advertising information. We use your Personal Information for three primary purposes: To help you quickly order services or information on BLUEPAGES.com. To help us create Web site content most relevant to you. To alert you to new products, special offers, updated information and other new services from the BLUEPAGES. If you choose not to register and provide Personal Information, you can still use most of the the BLUEPAGES.com site. However, you will not be able to order our products and services online. Although the BLUEPAGES does not provide your e-mail address or phone number to third parties, we may carefully select other companies to send you information through postal mail about their products or services. If you do not want us to do this, you may opt out when you give us your Personal Information (by selecting the option stating that you do not wish to receive such marketing materials from third parties). Additionally, if you do not want BLUEPAGES.com to send you e-mail or postal mail regarding the BLUEPAGES' products or services, you may opt out when you give us your Personal Information (by selecting the option stating that you do not wish to receive such marketing materials from the BLUEPAGES).
