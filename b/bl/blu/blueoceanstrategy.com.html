BOS Does Not Sell, Share or Distribute Your Contact Information

While using this Web Site, you may choose to submit personal information through on-line forms. BOS and its affiliated entities (“Affiliates”) will use this information to process your requests, but we will not intentionally sell, share, or distribute your personal information to third parties, except as required by applicable law. Statistical Logs Kept

In addition to any information you may affirmatively provide to BOS through on-line forms or otherwise, the Web Site is programmed to collect data about visitor traffic to the Web Site. BOS does not attempt to personally identify visitors to the Web Site. Instead we collect data to analyze in the aggregate to determine trends and improve our site. The following are some examples of the types of data we routinely collect:

• Browser type
• Referring web page
• Date, time & duration of visit
