3 BECOMING A CUSTOMER
3.1 In order to use the Website for the first time, you will be required to provide us with a user name, password, and a current email address. Based on this information, we will send you a welcoming email to verify your username and password.
3.2 If, after we contact you about the services on our Website, you wish to purchase a product or service from us, you must provide additional personal information to us by electronically submitting an order form. This additional information will include, without limitation:
  1. your full name, email address, residential or business address and a contact telephone number; and
  2. financial information such as your credit card number and its expiration date, and your billing address as per your credit card statement.
3.3 We will only collect your personal information by lawful and fair means, and not in an unreasonably intrusive way.
3.4 When you submit an order form, you will be deemed to have consented to our collection, use and disclosure of your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

4.1 Subject always to paragraph 12 of this Privacy Policy, once you have become a Customer we will only use and disclose personal information about you:

4.2 Unless you have specifically requested otherwise, we will only communicate with you by email.
