# Privacy Policy | Blue Backlinks Blog

**Blue Backlinks**

This privacy policy sets out how we use and protect any information that you give us when you use our [backlink checker](http://www.bluebacklinks.com/ "Backlink Checker"). We are committed to ensure that your privacy is protected. Should we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this website, then you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.

We may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should check this page from time to time to ensure that you are happy with any changes. This policy is effective from October 2010.

**Collection of information  
****  
** We only store information that you give to us. For example name and email if you submit it to our newsletter subscription. We might use that information to send out newsletters.

**Security**

Your information is secure. We are not using it to spam and will never sell it to any third party.

**External links**

Blue Backlinks may contain links to other websites of interest. However, once you have used these links to leave our site, you should note that we do not have any control over that other website. Therefore, we cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which you provide whilst visiting such sites and such sites are not governed by this privacy statement. You should exercise caution and look at the privacy statement applicable to the website in question.

We reserve the right to make changes to this policy.
