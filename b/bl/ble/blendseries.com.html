Cookies, Web Beacons and Similar Technologies

You can control cookies through your browser's settings. If you reject all cookies, however, you will not be able to take advantage of certain Blend Series offerings.

We may use cookies, web beacons or similar technologies in combination with your Blend Series information to enhance and personalize your experience on Blend Series, including to operate and improve offerings through Blend Series; to help authenticate you when you use Blend Series; to remember your preferences; to present and help measure and research the effectiveness of Blend Series offerings and advertisements; and, to customize the content and advertisements provided to you through Blend Series.

These advertising networks may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies on your computer in order to provide targeted advertisements about goods and services that may be of interest to you while visiting other Web sites.
