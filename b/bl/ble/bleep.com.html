Privacy Policy

Our Policy

Bleep takes the privacy of its customers very seriously, and will only collect information in order to fulfil customer orders and (if requested by the customer) to provide information via our email mail-outs. We never send unsolicited emails to customers, and we do not sell mailing lists or customer information to any other companies or individuals.

Information Collected

To process your order, we require the following data: your name, email address, delivery address (if mail order), and if you store your details with us, your credit card number and expiry date. You are solely responsible for the way you use this data on our site. To receive our new release information emails, we only require your email address.

Security

The store checkout is secure. Your order details are encrypted in your browser before they are sent to us. The order is then processed by a PCI compliant 3rd party payment gateway.
