Privacy Policy

What information is collected?

When you enter you email on our site, we collect certain information from you.

To provide you with the best possible experience, collect information about our visitors and track the visits our website gets, we employ the use of cookies and web beacons, like most websites. For more information on the use of cookies, please refer to the “do we use cookies?” section below.

How is this information used?

Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:

  • Create a more personalized experience
    (With your information, we are better able to respond to individual needs.)
  • Make customer service improvements
    (Using your information, we will be able to respond to any requests for customer service and support as quickly and efficiently as possible.)
  • Create and administer site features, such as promotions, contests and surveys
  • Reach out to you periodically via email

Note: You will find detailed instructions on unsubscribing from our emails at the bottom of each email we send, if at any time you would like to unsubscribe from receiving future notification.

How is your information protected?

There are a variety of security measures implemented on our site that keep your personal information safe. Information Blend collects from you such as, activity information, including specific questions, answers and comments; is collected so that Blend can better respond to customer support needs and service requests. When you provide an email address during order processing, it will only be used to send information regarding your order.

Unsubscribe

If you wish to opt out of Blend emails or any future notifications please visit: http://www.Blend.com/unsubscribe.

Do we use cookies?

Our site does use cookies. (Cookies are small files transferred to your computer from a site or service provider through your web browser (if allowed). These enable each site or service provider to recognize which browser you use and capture and retain certain information.)

Cookies are used to assist us in understanding and retaining your preferences for future visits. We also use them to compile aggregate data about interaction with our site and site traffic, so we can improve site tools and offer better experiences in the future.

Is information disclosed to outside parties?

Information is not transferred to outside parties through any means, including sale or trade. However, third parties that assist us with website operation are not included in this, as long as they are in agreement that they will keep this information confidential. Your information may also be released when it is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce the policies of our site or protect our rights as well as the rights of others, property or safety. Information that is not personally identifiable may be provided for advertising, marketing or other uses.

Third party links

At our discretion, third-party products or services may occasionally be included on our website. The privacy policies for these sites are separate and independent from ours, therefore we retain no liability or responsibility for content or activity of these sites. In the interest of protecting the integrity of our own site, we welcome any feedback regarding these third-party sites.

Compliance with the California Online Privacy Protection Act

We have done everything necessary to comply with the California Online Privacy Protection Act in order to protect your privacy. Therefore, your information will not be distributed to outside parties without consent.

Compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Act

We have taken the necessary measures to comply with the COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act). Our site, services and products are all directed for use by people over the age of 13, and we do not collect information from anyone under the age of 13 in any circumstance.

Your Consent

You consent to our privacy policy by using our site.

Privacy Policy Changes

Any changes we make to the privacy policy will be posted on this page.

Contacting Us
