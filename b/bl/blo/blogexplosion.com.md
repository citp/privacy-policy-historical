# Privacy Statement of BlogExplosion.com - BlogExplosion.com

Privacy Statement of BlogExplosion.com

Why have a privacy statement?

**BlogExplosion** has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: 

Cookies

Our site use cookies (session ID) to track your login so you don't have to re-enter it each time you load a site requiring login. We use a session ID that is sent with every request to the site. This ID expires after approx 120 minutes of inactivity. 

Use of personal information and email address

We will not sell your info to others. Your personal information will not be disclosed to any party without a subpoena or legal claim to the information. We only use the personal info to get in touch with you when needed. We send out our site newsletter with updates and information about the site to keep you up with current developments. The newsletter is sent 1-4 times a month depending on how the site develops. When we release new features an email will be sent out. 

Registration Forms

Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their name, email, and postal address). 

The customer's contact information is used to get in touch with the customer when necessary. 

External Links

This site contains links to other sites. **BlogExplosion** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

Data Quality/Access

This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided: 

  1. You can visit the following URL: [ http://www.blogexplosion.com ](http://www.blogexplosion.com/)
  2. You can send email to [ webmaster@blogexplosion.com ](mailto:webmaster@blogexplosion.com)



Contacting the Web Site
