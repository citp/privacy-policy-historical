Highlights of Bloomingdale’s Notice of Privacy Practices

Updated June 19, 2015

Scope

This statement applies to information collected about you by Bloomingdale's and its affiliated companies.

To read our full Notice of Privacy Practices please click here.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Information We Collect

We collect information about you from a variety of sources, including:

  • Information from you directly, such as when you complete a form on our website or in our stores
  • Information about you when you visit our website, make a purchase, use our services, or view our online ads
  • Information about you from other sources, such as from our vendors

All the information we collect about you may be combined to help us tailor our communications to you, improve the content of our website, and optimize your shopping experience.

Please click here for more information

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Uses

We use the information we collect to provide the products and services you request, to tell you about other products and services we offer, and to manage our website and services.

Please click here for more information

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Information Sharing

We may share your information, including:

  • With our third-party service providers and business partners
  • To protect and defend the rights and property of Bloomingdale's

Your Contact Preferences and Opt-Out Choices

E-Mail

  • Use the unsubscribe link in the footer on any bloomingdales.com promotional or marketing e-mails
  • By mail at:
    Bloomingdale's Credit and Customer Services
    ATTN: Privacy Office
    P.O. Box 8215, Mason, OH 45040
  • Sign into Your Account on bloomingdales.com, click on My Profile and indicate your opt-out preferences

Mobile Marketing

  • Please text STOP to 25666 on your mobile phone/wireless device. You will receive a confirmation text that you have been opted out

Direct Mail or Telemarketing

  • Write to us by mail at:
    Bloomingdale's Credit and Customer Services
    ATTN: Privacy Office
    P.O. Box 8215, Mason, OH 45040
  • Call Customer Service at 1-888-922-7026

Third Party Sharing

  • Write to us by mail at:
    Bloomingdale's Credit and Customer Services

  • Call Customer Service at 1-888-922-7026
