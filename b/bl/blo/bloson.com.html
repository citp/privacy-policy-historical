Privacy Policy

Bloson respects the privacy of its users and non-users. This privacy policy discloses any information we obtain and how we use it. Bloson also goes to great lengths to ensure that the information that users add to our system is secure and only accessible to intended users. Bloson may modify this policy from time to time, and the changes will be posted here. If you decide that you no longer wish to use the Bloson app, you may uninstall it at any time via the standard uninstall process for your operating system and browser. If you would like to change any personally identifiable information you have provided to us, or have your personally identifiable information removed from our database, please contact us at info@bloson.com.

What information do we collect?

Upon installing the Bloson app, you will be prompted to register an account and provide some basic information about yourself. If you choose not to register an account at such time, you will still be able to use the app, but you will not be able to collect points and make donation. We may contact you from time to time to remind you of the benefits of registration, and you will have the opportunity to register at a later time.

What do we use your information for?

The personally identifiable information collected by Bloson is generally used as follows:

  • To conduct market research, project planning, troubleshooting problems, detecting and protecting against error, fraud or other criminal activity, to enforce our terms of use and for other purposes, as otherwise set forth in this Policy and as otherwise authorized by you.

  • To notify you about special offers or other products and services that may be of interest to you. We may also contact you and deliver information to you that may be targeted to your interests, such as targeted banner advertisements, and product offering communications.

  • To improve our product and user experience; We continually strive to improve our app offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you

  • To conduct a exceptional customer service; You information helps us to more effectively respond to your customer service request and support needs.

How do we protect your information?
