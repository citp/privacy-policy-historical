COOKIES
Most areas of BlogSkins use cookies to help personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server (such as a file the keeps track of a user's logged in status). Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

Google may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you.
