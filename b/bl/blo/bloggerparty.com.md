# Privacy Policy | Blogger Party

Submitted by Admin on December 29, 2005 - 9:57pm

**BloggerParty.com's Privacy Policy**

BloggerParty.com is an online community where people are able to view your blog entries, as well as any comments you write, and personal information you provide in your profile. Your email address will never be displayed to the public by our website. 

We will never share, sell, or otherwise compromise your email address or any other information that has been deemed by our website or our staff as private. 

Any links from BloggerParty.com to websites outside of BloggerParty.com are not our responsibility, and we are not responsible for the policies of those websites. 

Our site uses cookies to track preferences and record session information, for the purpose of creating a better experience for our visitors. This information will be used to customize your visit to our website. 

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, go to http://www.networkadvertising.org/ 

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html 

If you have any questions, please contact us by private messaging.
