**BloggerParty.com's Privacy Policy**

BloggerParty.com is an online community where people are able to view your blog entries, as well as any comments you write, and personal information you provide in your profile. Your email address will never be displayed to the public by our website. 

We will never share, sell, or otherwise compromise your email address or any other information that has been deemed by our website or our staff as private. 

Any links from BloggerParty.com to websites outside of BloggerParty.com are not our responsibility, and we are not responsible for the policies of those websites. 

Our site uses cookies to track preferences and record session information, for the sole purpose of creating a better experience for our visitors. This information will only be used to customize your visit to our website. 

If you have any questions, please contact us by private messaging.
