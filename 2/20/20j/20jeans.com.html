Privacy Policy

Our privacy is one of our top priorities. At www.DSTLDjeans.com, your personal information is only collected in order for us to process orders.

We will not rent, sell, or share any of your information with any other companies or third parties.

We process all credit card payment transactions through Braintree Website Payments and PayPal, highly secured payment processors. Braintree and PayPal host and handle all the payment information; we do not handle any sensitive financial data on our server. Your financial information will never be saved in our system. All Braintree and PayPal transactions are encrypted to ensure privacy of your credit card information. Please visit Braintree.com and PayPal.com for more details.

After registration, information such as name, address, and contact information can be edited at any time, and your account can be closed permanently by contacting our support team – cs@dstld.la
