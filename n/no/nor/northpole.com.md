# Visit with Santa Claus at Christmas at the North Pole

Northpole Productions, LLC has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this site. 

  * Any personally identifiable information collected by this site is not shared with any third party for any reason. 
  * All personally identifiable information collected by this site is used strictly for the purpose of making your use of this site more enjoyable. 
  * Cookies are used on this site strictly for the purpose of delivering content specific to your interests and to assist in the creation of postcards. None of the information stored in the cookie is shared with any third party. 
  * Certain information, such as the domain name of the Internet access provider used by visitors when they access our site and the country of origin of such domain (which is anonymous as to individuals and is aggregate only) is used for our internal purposes in order to better develop our site to address our visitors’ preferences. 


  * We do NOT use online information for any purpose other than responding directly to the child’s request. 
  * We do NOT distribute to third parties any personally identifiable information. 
  * We do NOT give the ability to publicly post or otherwise distribute personally identifiable contact information. 


  * If you are a sponsor, you are making your information available for displaying on the Sponsor Page. The only information that will be displayed is company logo, link to company web site, family members’ names, and/or family’s hometown, depending on sponsorship type and level. 
  * Offline contact information is collected solely for the purpose of mailing _northpole.com_ merchandise to the sponsors. 
  * Offline contact information is not shared with any third party for any reason. 



This site contains links to other sites; www.northpole.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites.
