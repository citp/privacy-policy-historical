  PRIVACY POLICY

Data Protection
Any information you provide us with will only be disclosed in accordance with the Data Protection Act of 1998.

Required Information
To open a NOCHEX account you must tell us your name, email address, date of birth and postal address. For security, you must also submit a password and answer a security question. In order to load money into the system and make withdrawals you need to register your debit card details. We only collect the information that is required for us to process your transactions securely and protect you against fraud.

Transaction Information
In order to process a transaction, we need to know the amount of money sent or requested and the email address of the parties involved.

Disclosure of Information
We will NEVER disclose any of your personal information without your consent. Your debit card details or NOCHEX account balance will NEVER be revealed to anyone you have paid or who has paid you. This makes NOCHEX transactions more private and secure than sending a Cheque or using your debit card.

Aggregate Statistical Information
We may collect aggregated statistical information for purposes of improving our service and your experience with NOCHEX. This data does not contain personally identifying information, such as your name or email address.

Cookies
We use a cookie to display your email address, so that you don't have to retype it each time you log in to your account. No personal information is held within this cookie.

Changing Information
You can update your account information in the personal details section of the NOCHEX web site.
