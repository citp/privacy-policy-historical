# nowplaying3d | Privacy Statement

_NowPlaying3D.com_

NowPlaying3D.com is concerned about your privacy and wants you to be familiar with how NowPlaying3D collects, shares and uses your information. When registering on our site, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your name, e-mail address, mailing address, phone number, etc. You may however also visit our site anonymously.

_Children_

NowPlaying3D is not designed or intended for use by children. If you are under 18, you should use NowPlaying3D only with the involvement of a parent or guardian.

_How Does NowPlaying3D Automatically Collect Cookies?_

Like most websites, we use cookies. A cookie is a message that is sent to your computer from a Web server and stored on your computer’s hard drive. NowPlaying3D’s cookies contain personally identifiable information, such as your login ID and your email address. NowPlaying3D uses cookies to allow you to log in automatically and to ensure that you have agreed to the terms and conditions and policies that govern your use of the site or other service. If you prefer, you can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies via your browser settings.

_Anti-Spam Policy_

NowPlaying3D is 100% opposed to unsolicited commercial email (spam). We do not have any desire to send unsolicited marketing emails to anyone without permission and we do not sell or provide email addresses to any unauthorized third party in violation of this Privacy Policy. If you receive any unsolicited emails from spammer, please be assured that NowPlaying3D did not provide your email to such parties. If you believe you have received unsolicited emails from us, please contact us and we will investigate. Please be assured that we do not contact anyone for marketing purposes without permission. At the current time NowPlaying3D in an information site.

There may be links that direct you the user to an outside website that we are not affiliated with, please understand that NowPlaying 3D is not responsible for any website/web link outside of NowPlaying3D and that each third party website has its own separate and independent privacy policies. There may be a third party product link listed on NowPlaying3D, if you choose to go to the third party site to make a purchase please read through all terms and conditions of that site, again please note that all third party sites have their own independent privacy policies and terms of use agreements, please investigate fully before you make a purchase through a site outside of NowPlaying3D. NowPlaying3D cannot be held responsible for any loss on a purchase made on third party website.
