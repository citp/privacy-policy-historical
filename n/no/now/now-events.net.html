Personal information you provide to us: We receive and store any information you enter using our service or provide us with in any other way. Facebook events

We only extract public events from Facebook users who use our application and the majority of these events are created by the Facebook community. To keep an event from appearing on Now Events, simply set it as private on Facebook. Comments with the icon have been included by Facebook, and we cannot moderate their content.
