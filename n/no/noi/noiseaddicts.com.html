NoiseAddicts Privacy Policy

NoiseAddicts respects the privacy of its users. Here is the current privacy policy:

This site collects no personally identifying information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals (e.g. email address). All personally identifying information such as email, phone numbers, and addresses are used only for internal purposes. Personal user information is not for sale or trade and is strictly confidential. Contact information may be used to contact you related to the site.
If you have any questions or comments about our privacy policy, please contact us.

Information Collected

Server Logs

The website servers automatically collect statistical information when you visit the website. The information includes IP (internet protocol) address,  ISP (internet service provider, such as AOL or Shaw Cable), the browser (such as Internet Explorer or Firefox), the time, and pages visited. The primary purpose of this information is to make the website more efficient, correct any webpage errors, and assist in making the site better.

Cookies

The website uses cookies to store information such as wordpress login and user preferences.

Google DART cookies

This site displays ads from Google, a third party vendor, who uses cookies to serve ads. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads based on visits to this site and other sites on the internet. You may opt-out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

You can also choose to disable or turn off cookies to this (or any other) website using your browser settings, or by managing preferences in internet security program. Note that turning off or disabling certain features can affect your browser experience.
