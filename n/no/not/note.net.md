# Clayton Homes, Inc.

Privacy Policy:

Clayton Homes respects the privacy of every individual who visits our web site. Occasionally, our servers may collect personal and non-personal information from our visitors. The information may be used to design and offer new products, to complete purchasing or financing transactions, or for other purposes. It is not our policy to sell, rent or distribute any personal information with any outside parties. Our intent is to maintain any data provided as confidential; however, information transmitted on the Internet cannot be guaranteed to remain confidential and Clayton Homes assumes no responsibility for maintaining such information as confidential.
