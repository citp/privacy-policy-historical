Privacy Policy

No More Calls want to protect your privacy, so we do not collect any personally identifiable information on this website. Information Collected

The information you submit will be displayed to the public on the Website. Avoid posting your real name, email address, address or phone number within the post message.

We use cookies to personalize and enhance your experience on our sites. No personal information is gathered via the cookies. If you want to use our website without the use of cookies, there is a simple procedure in most browsers that allows you to change your cookie settings.
