**INTRODUCTION**

NoBullying.com is an online website that allows users learn more and contribute to fighting bullying in all its shapes and forms. Please be aware that any comment, review, profile, video, picture or any other content added to NoBullying.com website is provided by you on a non-proprietary and non-confidential basis. When providing personal information to NoBullying.com you are voluntarily and expressly agreeing to accept the Terms and Conditions of this Privacy Policy along with the standard website Terms and Conditions.

NoBullying.com uses search facilities across the website. Information you have provided in your profile, including but not limited to any content you have added to the site will be used to populate this search engine.

**AUTOMATIC INFORMATION**

We receive automatic information through NoBullying.com.com and its authorised agents when you use the website. We automatically receive and record certain ‘traffic data’, including your IP address, cookie information and page views. We use this information to identify problems with the website and offer you a better service. The IP address shall only be analysed in case of attack on NoBullying.com’s system and for website statistics.

We use all information we gather to offer you a personalized website experience, to create personalized web pages and offer a better service to users.

We use Hyperlinks to link from one page on the website to another relevant page on the website. We may use technology to track how often these links are used and which pages are viewed. This technology does not identify you personally but provides statistics for the website, so we can improve our service.

We apply appropriate technical and organisational security measures in order to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, part or full loss or manipulation.

**USE OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA**

We will use your personal information to provide you with a personalized experience each visit to the website.

NoBullying.com employees and third party agents may have some access to personal information on NoBullying.com that is necessary during the normal course of business.

In some cases, we may choose to buy or sell assets. In these types of transfers, user information is usually the type of information that is transferred. In the case of a merger, acquisition, reorganisation, sale of assets or bankruptcy assets such as user data may be transferred to a third party successor. We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties without your consent.

We will release personal information if necessary to comply with a legal request.

NoBullying.com allows for RSS syndication of its public content from NoBullying.com.

From time to time, NoBullying.com may take part in surveys or polls. You may be asked to provide personal information or permit the transfer of such information as part of the terms of the survey or poll. It will be disclosed at the point of collecting if it is NoBullying.com collecting this information or a third party.

We will send you services or promotional communications through email and notices on the website – which you can opt out of.

We will use information gathered from the website when working with our partners to offer you on site advertising that is relevant to you.

We will use your personal information if a dispute arises and we need to contact you in an attempt to resolve this dispute.

Except in the cases above – we will contact you when your personal information may be shared with a third party, where you will have the option to opt out of such sharing.

**COOKIES AND THEIR USE**

We use Cookies to gather non personal information about your visits to our website. However, you may turn off these cookies as most of the browsers allow you to do so. If you want to know how to do this, go to your help menu on your browser. However, if you choose not to accept cookies, your user experience on our site may be affected. We use cookies to:

estimate our audience size and usage pattern.

store information about your preferences so we can customise our site according to your individual interests.

speed up your searches.

**WHAT ARE COOKIES?**

Cookies are alphanumeric information files which your browser automatically saves to the hard drive of your computer when you visit a website. Having active cookies allows for quick login every time you visit the website. Using cookies does not mean that we receive new personal data about you as a visitor to our website. Data collected using cookies is statistical in nature and indicates a user’s browsing actions and patterns. It does not identify any individual. To remove the cookies at any point from your computer, you can delete them using your browser.

NoBullying.com may also use from time to time a technology that is embedded into web pages or in email that allows the website to track page views. Unlike cookies these are not stored on your hard drive. This technology is often called Clear GIF’s or Web Beacons this technology does not personally identify you.

**YOUR ACTIONS ON NOBULLYING.COM**

When you complete your profile, you may choose whether to provide or not to provide your contact details or other personal information on this public website. Our advice is that you do not provide this information on your public profile to avoid it being used by any third parties that may result in unsolicited emails or communications. DO NOT POST INFORMATION ON NOBULLYING.COM THAT YOU WANT TO KEEP PRIVATE AS NOBULLYING.COM IS AN OPEN WEBSITE.

NoBullying.com operates an invite service throughout its website, where you have the option to use this invite system or not, to invite or tell your friends/connections about the website or content on the website. While doing so some part of your information may be available for such person to see and identify you.

When you write a comment, post and/or review, you will be identified by your full name and picture, information which is available from your profile. Your public profile, once confirmed by you will be shown and available to all users of NoBullying.com to view.

You may use a link on NoBullying.com to visit another website, as per standard Terms and Conditions, however, NoBullying.com does not endorse any other website with or without links to its own website. If you visit another website you should review their standard Terms and Conditions and Privacy policy as it may differ from NoBullying.com. We do not control the policies of other websites.
