**NECN.com Privacy Policy**

NECN.com places the highest priority on your privacy. We are committed to all the conventional practices regarding privacy. We will not use any of the information you give us for any mailings without your express permission. We will not sell your information to anyone. 

Our software may place a cookie on your computer. It is to help us serve video to you as efficiently as possible. 

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, or wish to make a suggestion on how we can improve it, please send an email to [help@necn.com](mailto:help@necn.com) and we will respond promptly.

THIRD PARTY ADVERTISING

The ads appearing on this Web site are delivered to you by DoubleClick, our Web advertising partner. Information about your visit to the site, such as number of times you have viewed an ad (but not your name, address, or other personal information), is used to serve ads to you on this site. For more information about DoubleClick, cookies, and how to "opt-out", please click here: <http://www.doubleclick.net/us/corporate/privacy>

THIRD PARTY COOKIES

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser.
