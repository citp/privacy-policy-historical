## **PRIVACY POLICY**

Newspress operates a strict privacy policy which provides you with the information on the personal details we collect from you, how we use it and to whom we may disclose such personal information.

We are notified under the UK Data Protection Act with the Information Commissioner.

### **Definitions Used**

a) ‘DPA 1998’ means the Data Protection Act 1998, as amended.

b) ‘personal information’ means any information that we have obtained from you or third parties in connection with a service or product provided to you that is held now or at any time in the future by Newspress Ltd. 

c) ‘Website’ means [www.newspress.co.uk](http://www.newspress.co.uk/).

d) ‘We/us/our’ means Newspress Ltd, company registration number 01106594




### **What personal information do we obtain and process and why?**

a) In order for you to be able to access and use the updating, archive and daily release services provided by the Website, you need to register with us. During the registration process, we shall obtain certain log-in information from you. This information includes your name, contact details and your categories of interest.

b) We use the personal information we obtain from you:

i) to administer your registration to the Website;

ii) for administration and statistical analysis;

iii) to supply you with the press information and services requested by you; and 

iv) to monitor, maintain, administer, enhance or develop the website, which may include disclosing your information to trusted and carefully selected third parties who in turn may contact you to provide press information that they think may be of interest to you (where we do this, your information will be disclosed only for this limited purpose).

c) We would like to contact you from time to time to provide you with information about:

i) Newspress;

ii) Changes to our services that we think may be of interest; and 

iii) The products and services of carefully selected organisations which we think may be of interest (primarily press information).

d) Newspress uses cookies to store and retrieve your login information so you are not required to enter your details every time you visit the site. Information stored on these cookies is not used for any other purpose. If you do not have cookies activated you will need to login each time you visit.

e) Site usage statistics and individual press release usage statistics are provided to our clients in the form of reports. This information does not contain your personal details, simply the number of click throughs and the media type you represent.

f) We may contact you by phone, post or email to supply you with press information. You may opt out of these services and if you wish to stop receiving emails at any time, simply send an e-mail to [newspress@newspress.co.uk](mailto:newspress@newspress.co.uk) and state ‘unsubscribe’.




### Your consent

b) you expressly agree to the collection and use of your personal information for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.
