NewsTonight.net Privacy Policy

The NewsTonight website stores only email address of members and basic profile data.

The web server logs record the data of your ISP and your IP address.

The website visitor records are measured using the solutions provided by a third-party, which includes statistics and visitor origin information. We store this information for 30 days on our web server.

The email address of members and their basic information is stored on our secure server. We do not share this information with anyone.

The emails sent by NewsTonight website only include the welcome emails for members. We do not offer any subscriptions or news alert service.

At www.newstonight.net, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by www.newstonight.net and how it is used.

Log Files
Like many other Web sites, www.newstonight.net makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons
www.newstonight.net does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
