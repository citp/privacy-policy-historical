    Privacy Policy

    The following statement explains our policy regarding any personal information we collect about you. We want you to feel confident about the privacy and security of your personal information. This policy is in force throughout the Club Free Spirit network. Including The Boot Camp Ltd known as New You Escapes. If you have any queries about this policy, please do not hesitate to contact our GDPR Officer at Club Free Spirit Floor 3, 42 South Molton Street, London W1K 5RR or email team@clubfreespirit.com

    The information we might hold about you

    Depending on the reason that you have contacted us or we have contacted you we will hold the following information about you:

    Information you supply to us directly

    This is information about you that you give us when you wish to make use of our services by filling in forms on our website, www.newyouescapes.com, or by corresponding with us by phone, e-mail or otherwise.

    It includes information you provide when you register to use our site, subscribe to receive information from us, enter a promotion or survey, and when you report a problem with our site.

    We retain all member and enquiry information on our databases for a reasonable amount of time to fulfil our service to each client and to then contact for future like events on offer. Each client can specify that you no longer wish New You Escapes to hold your personal information. You have the right to request that this is removed by contacting - GDPR Officer at Club Free Spirit Floor 3, 42 South Molton Street, London W1K 5RR or email team@clubfreespirit.com

    Our Lawful Basis for Data Processing is: -

    (a) Consent: the individual has given clear consent for you to process their personal data for a specific purpose.
    (b) Contract: the processing is necessary for a contract you have with the individual, or because they have asked you to take specific steps before entering into a contract.
    (c) Legitimate Interests: the processing is necessary for your legitimate interests or the legitimate interests of a third party unless there is a good reason to protect the individual's personal data which overrides those legitimate interests. (This cannot apply if you are a public authority processing data to perform your official tasks.)

    Cookies

    Where you consent www.newyouescapes.com site makes use of cookies. Cookies are messages given to a web browser by a web site, which are then stored on your computer. A cookie's main objective is to identify users and personalise their visit by customising web pages. Web sites can only read cookies that have been deposited by that website. They cannot read cookies left by other websites nor can they read other personal information stored on your computer.

    You can choose to disable cookies globally at any time through your browser, but this may prevent you from logging in to the members' area.
