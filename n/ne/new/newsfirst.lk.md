# Privacy

**How we may use your information**

We thank you for visiting our web site located at [www.newsfirst.lk](http://www.newsfirst.lk/)  ("Site"), We may use any User Information/data  entered by You or gathered for the following purposes:

1\. market research and to improve the quality of sevice to You by Us; and

 2\. in order to send You periodic communications (this may include e-mail), about features, products and services, events and special offers.

 If you wish that you do not require such communications You may click no communications at all, I would like communications from any party,  I would like communications only from newsfirst. When You enter your User Information, We presume that You accept that your User Information may be retained by Us and that it may be held by Us or any third party company which processes it on behalf of Us. By continuing to use this website and by providing any personal data to Us via this website or e-mail addresses provided on this website, You are consenting to our use of your data for the limited purposes as set in this Privacy Policy. This Policy is devised to help you feel more confident about the privacy and security of your personal details. We process personal data only in strict compliance with accepted privacy norms.

**Data Collection**

When using the Site You may encounter areas such as games, competitions, chat forums, shopping or video services where You may be asked to enter User Information. Such User Information will only be used for the purposes for which it was collected, for any other purposes specified at the collection point and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

 **Cookies**

You should be aware that information and data may be automatically collected through the use of Cookies. "Cookies" are small text files that store basic information that a web site can use to recognise repeat site visits and as an example, recall your name if this has been previously supplied. We may use this to observe behaviour and compile aggregate data in order to improve the Site, target the advertising and assess general effectiveness of such advertising.
