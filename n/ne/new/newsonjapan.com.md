# News On Japan - Privacy and Security Statement

Last Revised February 10/2003

At **_NewsOnJapan.com_** , we are committed to protecting your privacy. All of the personal data we receive is always kept private and confidential and is protected against unauthorized access. We do not sell or rent your personal information to others. Information that you provide to us is maintained in private files on our secure Web server and our internal systems. We will not sell or release your e-mail address to any other parties. Data-collected is only used to improve the **_NewsOnJapan.com_** web site and improve the quality of our services.

**Monthly News Report:**

Only your e-mail address is required to subscribe to our Monthly News Report which is stored on our secure e-mail server and used for the sole purpose of sending you the e-mail report once a month. From time to time we may send you a supplementary report.
