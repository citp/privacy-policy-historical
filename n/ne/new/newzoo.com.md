# Privacy policy - Newzoo

1\. PURPOSE OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY  
The following describes the information gathering and dissemination practices for the websites operated by Newzoo BV (“Newzoo”), including (without limitation) [www.newzoo.com](http://www.newzoo.com/),  a “Website”, and together being the “Websites”.

2\. USE OF YOUR INFORMATION  
Your personal and company data (meaning any information about You from which You personally or your company can be identified, such as name, address, telephone number or email address, and which is referred to in this Privacy Policy as “Your Information”), will be processed by Newzoo (which include its agents and employees), and (where appropriate) our partners and subcontractors. Your Information is collected when You register with us; or when You submit a written query to us.

While the Websites may contain links to websites operated by parties other than Newzoo, Newzoo is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of any such websites.

By registering with any of the Websites or contacting us in writing, You acknowledge that You have read, and agree to, the terms of this Privacy Policy and that, where You provide us with Your Information (including, for the avoidance of doubt, by submitting a query to us through one of the Websites, or where You only partially complete an account registration process), You consent to the use by Newzoo (and our partners and subcontractors) of Your Information for the purposes set out below.

Newzoo collects Your Information in order to enable us to set up Your account; enable us to receive questions and comments from You, and for us to be able to respond to them; and to keep You informed of news from Newzoo. Newzoo will not send You unsolicited information regarding any third party commercial offers or advertisements. We may transfer Your Information in the event of that we transfer or sell our business or any part of it; including in the event of insolvency.

If at any time You wish us to stop processing Your Information for the above purposes, then You must contact us and we will take the appropriate steps to stop doing so. Please note that this may mean that Your Account will have to be closed, entirely at the discretion of Newzoo.

3\. Contacting us  
If at any time You believe that Newzoo has not adhered to the principles contained within this Privacy Policy, please notify us by contacting Customer Services and we will use all commercially reasonable efforts to promptly determine and correct the problem.

This Privacy Policy is effective from 1st July 2012
