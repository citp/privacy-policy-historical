# Privacy Policy | Newz On Fire

**Newz On Fire** is concerned about the privacy of all of you visitors and we know that privacy of each visitor is important. Whenever each visitor visits our website, our web server will recognize who you are, your browser, operating system, IP address etc.

When you comment on any post you will need to give the user name and email id which will be stored in the database.

We are/will be using advertisements on Newz On Fire so advertising companies might store cookies in your computer to recognize you when you visit them next time.This cookie information will be stored by advertisers and we do not have any access on it.

The information we get from you will be used to improve the website and nothing else. If you subscribe to the RSS feed with email address, we will have access to those email addresses. We will use those email addresses to send RSS feed to you and not for any other purpose. We will never sell, rent or disclose them to any other third party element.

**DoubleClick DART Cookie**

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL -[Advertising and Privacy – Google Privacy Center](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)

We reserve rights to change or replace this Privacy Policy at any time and from time to time without any prior notice to or the consent of any person. The information on this website is only for informational purpose.

If you have any issues regarding privacy policy of this website then please feel free to [contact us](http://newzonfire.com/contact/).
