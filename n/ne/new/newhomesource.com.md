## Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Builders Digital Experience LLC ("BDX," "us," or "we") collects and uses personal information that we receive when you access and/or use our websites and online services, including [newhomesource.com](https://www.newhomesource.com/), [casasnuevasaqui.com](https://www.casasnuevasaqui.com/), [www.customnewhomes.com](http://www.customnewhomes.com/), [homluv.com](http://www.homluv.com/), and [www.newretirementcommunities.com](http://www.newretirementcommunities.com/) (collectively, the "Service").

By accessing and/or using the Service in any manner, you acknowledge that you have read and understand this Privacy Policy, and you consent to having your information collected and processed as described below.

Our privacy policy covers the following:

  1. What types of information are collected from you when you access and/or use the Service?
  2. How is this information used?

  4. What choices do you have about this information?
  5. What kind of security procedures are in place to protect the loss, misuse, or alteration of information under BDX's control?
  6. What other provisions apply to your use of the Service?

  1. ### COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

We may collect certain non-personal and personally identifiable information from you when visit or access the Service, including information you provide to us and information we collect automatically during your visit.

 _Registration_ \-- You may be required to register on the Service to use some of the resources available through the Service. We collect your personal information that you provide when you register, including, for instance, your name, address, e-mail address, a password, and phone number.

 _Optional Information_ \-- You may choose to provide information voluntarily at various other points during your use of the Service, for example, if you decide to include "favorite" images, communities, homes, or other content in your profile, if you decide to participate in a survey or a contest available on the Service, or if you decide to submit reviews, ratings, comments, feedback, or other user-submitted information that is submitted for publication on the Service or for distribution to our other websites and online services or to websites and online services of our affiliates or business partners. You agree that these types of user-submitted information may be publicly displayed and may be freely shared and otherwise transferred by us. Use of these types of features of the Service that require you to provide additional information is completely voluntary on your part, and you may choose whether to disclose the information necessary to use these features of the Service.

 _Cookies and Automatic Collection of Information_ \-- BDX may also collect non-personal information automatically through the use of cookies, web beacons, pixels, or similar technologies. Some of these technologies may track your online behavior over time and across third party websites or other online services ("Behavioral Tracking"). The information collected may include, without limitation, your IP address, unique device identifier, browser type, actions taken on the Service, referring URLs, metadata, and other similar information. This non-personal information does not identify you to BDX by name, and BDX does not link your IP address to your personally identifiable information. We use this information for purposes such as testing and improving user experience, delivering content tailored to you, and for other marketing and business purposes.

  2. ### USE OF INFORMATION

BDX may use the information we collect from and about you for a variety of purposes, including to:

    * provide services to you, such as the new home search, recommendations, matching services, home brochures, location of key resources, and other services;
    * develop and improve new and existing services;
    * make recommendations and customize the Service to your interests and enhance your overall experience;
    * communicate with you regarding the Service, such as updates, new features, additional listings, and new BDX opportunities, which may be of interest to you;
    * send emails and other communications to you and from you to others you have specified you want to collaborate with regarding various features of the Service;
    * send you free informational newsletters or notices relating to topics which may be of special interest to you, including builders, product manufacturers, related advertisers, and general home shopping advice;
    * perform internal business processes, such as analyzing data, conducting market research, and identifying trends;
    * combine it with aggregated behavioral or other information from third parties for internal and third party marketing purposes;
    * deliver relevant and customized advertising to you (see Targeted Advertising, below for more information);
    * comply with applicable law; and/or
    * combat fraud.

 _Targeted Advertising_

BDX advertises online, such as through our Service and on other sites not affiliated with us, and offline, such as through call centers and direct marketing. So that we understand how advertising performs, we may collect certain information on our Service and other sites through our advertising service providers using cookies, IP addresses, and other technologies. The collected information may include the number of page visits, pages viewed on our Service, search engine referrals, browsing activities over time and across other sites following your visit to one of our Service or apps, and responses to advertisements and promotions on the Service and on sites and apps where we advertise.

We use the information collected, as described here, to help advertise our products and services in a variety of ways, such as:

    * to present tailored ads;
    * to analyze the performance of our Service and effectiveness of our ads; and
    * to determine whether you might be interested in new products or services.

This means that we may use information about your relationship with us to help determine which advertisements or offers to present to you.

 _Online Behavioral Advertising_

We or our advertising service providers may use certain information about your activities on our Service, such as pages visited and search key words entered to help determine which of our advertisements or offers may be of interest to you. We limit access and collection of information by advertising service providers for specific purposes. We may use this online information for online and offline advertising. Much of this information is aggregated and deidentified.

Information provided by you to these third party sites may help select which of our advertisements or offers may appeal to you. Third party sites are not subject to this privacy policy. Please visit the individual sites for additional information on their data and privacy practices and opt out policies.

Some of our tailored ads are online behavioral advertising, and may be served using data collected by third party providers. Ads served on our behalf by these companies do not contain unencrypted personal information and we limit the use of information by companies that serve our ads.

  3. ### SHARING OF INFORMATION

BDX will not knowingly share, sell, rent, or otherwise transfer your information other than in accordance with the terms set forth in this Privacy Policy. We may share and otherwise transfer without restriction information that does not personally identify you.

During your use of the Service, such as during the registration process or in connection with specific offers, BDX asks you whether your personally identifiable information may be sent to third parties who have products, services, and opportunities which may be useful to you. If you give your permission to allow third parties to contact you, personal information about you (such as your contact information and other information collected during your visit to the Service) may be shared with individual users and other third parties that you have identified, as well as builders, product manufacturers, data aggregators, marketers (possibly in the form of list rental), and other organizations.

We may also share your personal information that we collect:

    * with our affiliates;
    * with your consent;
    * with our third party service providers to the extent necessary to provide specific services (such as sending newsletters to our users on our behalf) requested by BDX;
    * to fulfill the purposes for which you provided it;
    * in the event that BDX sells, assigns or transfers some or all of its business to a successor or acquirer, BDX may sell, assign or transfer all of your information, regardless of your opt status, to such successor or acquirer; and/or
    * as required by law or based on our good faith belief it is necessary or appropriate.

Out of respect for the privacy of our users, we present the option to opt-out of receiving newsletters and/or commercial e-mails whenever such messages are sent by BDX, as described below. E-mail messages from third parties are not governed by the opt-out provisions of this Privacy Policy, but all such third parties are strongly encouraged to include opt-out procedures in their messages to you.

  4. ### YOUR INFORMATION CHOICES

 _Updating Your Information_ \-- You can update your personal information by clicking on the "Update Profile" or "My Account" link on the Service, or by following the instructions you receive from BDX.

 _Choice/Opt-out_ \-- BDX users are given the opportunity to "opt-out" from receiving specified types of communications from BDX by following the unsubscribe link provided at the bottom of such communication.

 _Cookie Opt-Outs_ \-- If you want to disable cookies, you may block them by activating the setting on your browser that allows you to refuse all or some cookies. You may also delete them after they have been placed on your hard drive. Please note, the functionality of the Service may be impaired or disabled if you delete or choose not to accept our cookies. For more information about cookies, please visit: [http://www.allaboutcookies.org](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/).

 _California Do Not Track Disclosures_ \-- Most browsers can be set to send "Do Not Track" signals to third-party websites requesting them not to track your online activities, which may require you to set your browser to permit cookies. We do not respond to "Do Not Track" signals at this time. For a description of "Do Not Track" information for browsers and mobile devices, and information about opt-out programs that third party advertising companies participate in, please visit: [https://allaboutdnt.com/](hhttps://allaboutdnt.com/) or <http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/>.

 _Adverstising on Third Party Sites_ \-- We participate in the Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) self-regulatory program and adhere to the DAA self-regulatory Principles for Online Behavioral Advertising.

  5. ### SECURITY

BDX has security measures in place to help protect against the loss, misuse and/or unauthorized alteration of the information under our control. You are the only user authorized to update your profile, unless you provide your password information to another party.

BDX helps protect personally identifiable information by utilizing both online and offline security methods, including firewalls, passwords and restricted physical access to the places where your information is stored. Our staff is trained to comply with our security procedures, and our security procedures are regularly reviewed and revised, if necessary, by the Privacy Team at BDX.
