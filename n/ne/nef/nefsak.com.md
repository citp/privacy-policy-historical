# Privacy Statement - nefsak.com

We respect your privacy!  
Any and all the information collected on this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold or rented!  
Any information you provide will be held with the utmost care and will not be used in ways that you do not consent to. If you have any questions, please feel free to call or e-mail us.
