Privacy Policy

Data Protection of Netbiscuits GmbH for Netbiscuits.com

Netbiscuits GmbH (hereinafter referred to as „Netbiscuits“) take the protection of personal data very seriously. We would like you to know when we store which data and how long we use them. As a company under private law we are subject to the stipulations of the Federal Data Protection Law (BDSG) and the German Media Services Treaty (MST). We have taken technical and organizational measures that will ensure that the data protection regulations are observed both by us and by external service providers commissioned by us.

Registration of Personal Information

Personal data will be used according to your business relations with Netbiscuits. The manner of data processing depends on the detailed services you receive from Netbiscuits. Personal data is all information that can be used to establish your identity. This includes information such as your name, address or phone number. This does not apply to information that cannot be connected to your true identity (for example favorite Websites or number of users of a site).

Non-personal Identifiable Information

In connection with your access, data is stored on our servers for security purposes that could possibly allow identification (e.g. IP address, date, time and pages viewed). No personally identifiable utilization is carried out. We reserve the right to statistically evaluate anonymised data sets.

We store IP addresses for a period of 28 days. These are stored for reasons of data security to ensure the stability and operational reliability of our system.

Circulation of Personal Information to Third Parties

We will not pass on your personal information to third parties without your consent except if it is for reasons of services to be executed by Netbiscuits. If data should be passed on to service providers in connection with commissioned data processing, these providers are bound by the FDPL, by other statutory regulations and contractually by Netbiscuits' own data protection policy. If we are obliged to do so by the law or by a court ruling, we will pass your data on to authorities entitled to receive the information.

Right to Information and Cancellation

You can ask for stored information at any time and you also have the right to ask for personal data to be deleted in the future. In both cases we ask you to please contact us by per E-Mail (info@netbiscuits.com) or by phone (Tel.: +49 631 68036-100).

Use of Cookies

We place “cookies” (small files with configuration information) to collect information relating to customer trends and patterns and we use cookies to measure site usages and related information. No personal data is collected by the use of cookies. You can set your web browser to refuse cookies or to be informed as soon as cookies are sent.

Changes in Our Privacy Policy

Due to the fast development of the Internet it may become necessary from time to time to make changes to our privacy policy. If we make changes, we will post them on our site to make users aware of what the changes are so users will always be aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and when we may disclose it. A User is bound by any minor changes to the policy when she or he uses the site or software from Netbiscuits after those changes have been posted. If, however, we are going to use users' personally identifiable information in a manner materially different from that stated at the time of collection, we will notify by posting a notice on our Web site for 30 days.

Questions, Suggestions and Comments

Please send any questions, suggestions or comments on the subject of data protection by E-Mail to Netbiscuits (info@netbiscuits.com).

Kaiserslautern, 12/06/2008

 

Privacy Policy of Netbiscuits GmbH for users residing in the United States of America

Netbiscuits GmbH and its affiliates (hereinafter referred to as „Netbiscuits“) take the protection of personal data very seriously. Netbiscuits has created this privacy policy (the “Policy”) to explain our privacy practices so you will understand when and how personal information is collected, used, disclosed and protected with respect to our services (“Services”) and Web site (“Site”) located at http://www.netbiscuits.com. By using the Services or our Site, you consent to the privacy practices described in this Policy.

Registration of Personal Information

Personal data will be used according to your business relations with Netbiscuits. The manner of data processing depends on how you elect to use our Site and the detailed Services you receive from Netbiscuits. Personal data is all information that can be used to establish your identity. This includes information such as your name, address or phone number. This does not apply to information that cannot be connected to your true identity (for example favorite Web sites or number of users of a site).

Our Site and Services are intended for the general audience. However, we do not knowingly, directly or passively, collect information from children under the age of 13. If you are under the age of 13, please do not use our Services or our Site or provide personally identifiable information of any kind whatsoever.

Non-personal Identifiable Information

In connection with your use of our Site or Services, data and personally identifiable information about you is stored on our servers and may be archived elsewhere. Our records are stored indefinitely, as are our archival backup copies. We reserve the right to statistically evaluate anonymised data sets.

Circulation of Personal Information to Third Parties

Access to your personal information will be limited to specific Netbiscuits employees. In general, we use personal information internally to serve our users and enable them to take maximum advantage of our Services. For example, personal information may be used to conduct analysis, provide services or complete transactions you have requested, to anticipate and resolve problems with our Services, to respond to customer support inquiries, and to create and inform you of new products and services from Netbiscuits that better meet your needs. By registering for our Services, you give consent for us to communicate with you at any of the contact points you provide to us for any of the purposes permitted under this Policy.

You should review the following section to understand when Netbiscuits discloses personal information to non-affiliated third parties.

Disclosure of Personal Information.

We do not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies except (a) as may be necessary to perform internal analyses and business functions, (b) as part of a business transfer, or (c) when required by law or permitted to protect our rights, property, or safety. The following section describes these disclosure circumstances more fully.

  • Consent. Unless you choose to do so, we will not disclose your personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. With your consent, we may disclose your personal information to third parties so that they may provide you with products and services that you have requested. Please note that third parties with whom aggregate information is shared are required to comply with our privacy practices.
  • Business Functions. We may share personal information with third party vendors who assist us in performing internal functions necessary to operate our business. In the event that we use third party vendors to assist us in performing these functions, the third parties may not use or disclose your personal information for any other purpose.
  • Internal Analysis. We may use information about you for our own internal purposes, such as to estimate our audience size, measure service use and demands as well as to understand demographic, customer interest, and other trends among our users. We may outsource these tasks and disclose personal information about you to third parties in aggregate, provided the use and disclosure of your personal information by these third parties is restricted to performance of such tasks.
  • Business Transfers.Information about our users and the Services is one of our most important business assets. Therefore, information about our users, including personal information, will be disclosed as part of any merger or acquisition, creation of a separate business to provide the Services or fulfill products, sale or pledge of company assets as well as in the event of an insolvency, bankruptcy or receivership in which personal information would be transferred as one of the business assets of the company.

Protection of Netbiscuits and Others.

We may release personal information in the good faith belief that release is appropriate and necessary to comply with legal process or authority (e.g., a lawful subpoena, warrant or court order); to enforce or apply our policies; to initiate, render, bill, and collect for amounts owed to us; to protect our rights, property, or personal safety of Netbiscuits, our users, our employees, or the public; or if we reasonably believe that an emergency involving immediate danger of death or serious physical injury to any person requires disclosure of communications or justifies disclosure of records without delay.

Right to Information and Cancellation

If you are a resident of the European Union or any other jurisdiction where required by applicable law, you may ask for stored information about yourself at any time and you also have the right to ask for personal data to be deleted in the future. In both cases we ask you to please contact us by per E-Mail (info@netbiscuits.com) or by phone (Tel.: +49 (0) 631 30314-00).

California Civil Code section 1798.83 permits customers who are California, USA residents to request certain information regarding disclosure of personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. We do not provide your personal information to any third party for their direct marketing purposes without your consent. If you would like more information with respect to your privacy rights under California law, you may contact us as provided for above.

Use of Cookies

We place cookies, Web beacons, Internet tags, pixel tags web bugs or similar very small graphic files (collectively, "cookies") with configuration information, to collect information relating to customer trends and patterns and to measure site usages and related information. No personal data is collected by the use of cookies. You can set your web browser to refuse cookies or to be informed as soon as cookies are sent; however, if you decide not to accept our cookies, you may not be able to use some or all aspects of the Services.

Changes in Our Privacy Policy

Due to the fast development of the Internet it may become necessary from time to time to make changes to our Policy. If we make changes, we will post them on our Site to make users aware of what the changes are so users will always be aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and when we may disclose it. A user is bound by any minor changes to the policy when she or he uses the Site or software from Netbiscuits after those changes have been posted. If, however, we are going to use users' personally identifiable information in a manner materially different from that stated in our Policy at the time of collection, we will notify by posting a notice on our Site for 30 days.

Questions, Suggestions and Comments

Please send any questions, suggestions or comments on the subject of this Policy by E-Mail to Netbiscuits (info@netbiscuits.com).
