# Privacy

### Privacy Policy at NetDania

COOKIES

The login and download sections requires that you enable cookies in your browser. On this website we use only temporary cookies. This means that the cookies automatically are removed again, as soon as you end your browser session at our site. Neither do we save any kind of personal information in cookies. The temporary cookies are used to gather statistic information and to analyze the downloads from the site. 

PERSONAL INFORMATION 

We do not share any personal/contact information collected on this site with any third parties. Any information you give us is not transferred, sold, loaned or rented to third parties without your prior consent. The contact information collected on this site may be used in informing you about latest developments on the site.

EXTERNAL LINKS

This site contains links to other sites. NetDania is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

CONTACTING THE WEB SITE

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please do not hesitate to contact us at: [info@netdania.net](mailto:info@netdania.net?subject=Website%20Privacy)
