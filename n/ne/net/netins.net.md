# Privacy Policy | netINS

IOWA NETWORK SERVICES, INC., Inc. has provided this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. The following policy discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: www.iowanetworkservices.com.

## Information

IOWA NETWORK SERVICES, INC., Inc. is the sole owner of the information collected on this website.

## Viewer Privacy

IOWA NETWORK SERVICES, INC., Inc. does not collect personal information such as names, e-mail addresses, postal addresses, or telephone numbers. Since we do not collect any personal information on this Web site, we do not share any personal information with any third parties nor do we use any personal information for any purposes.

## IP Addresses

As noted above, we do not collect any personal information on this Web site. We do, however, collect and store information about your Internet connection when you visit our Web site to read or download information, such as reports, news, etc. We use this information to track Web site use, measure site traffic, and improve site navigation and information. We collect information that identifies: 路 The name of the domain you access the Internet with. 路 The type of web browsing software you use to view our site. 路 The date and time of your access. 路 The platform you are using (i.e. Windows, Macintosh or Unix). This information is never sold, given or disclosed to third parties. This information is used internally to administer our web site, provide better services to the public, and provide aggregate traffic statistics to IOWA NETWORK SERVICES, INC. staff. No personal information is collected
