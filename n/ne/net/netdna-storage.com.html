When you provide personally-identifiable information to netDNA Storage through one of our websites, it will be used to fulfill your specific request. netDNA Storage will not, however, sell or trade your personally-identifiable information unless we are authorized or legally required to do so, or in the case of imminent physical harm to the visitor or others. In general, netDNA Storage gathers some generic information automatically. Generic information does not reveal the identity of the visitor.
