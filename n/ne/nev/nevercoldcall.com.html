PRIVACY POLICY
WE ARE COMMITTED TO PROTECTING THE PRIVACY OF OUR CUSTOMERS AND ONLINE VISITORS.  WE COLLECT INFORMATION, LIMITED TO NAME AND E-MAIL ADDRESS, OF VISITORS WHO OPT-IN TO OUR E-MAIL NEWSLETTER LIST.  EVERYONE WHO WISHES CAN CHOOSE TO BE REMOVED FROM OUR E-MAIL LIST AT ANY TIME BY USING THE UNSUBSCRIBE LINK PROVIDED AT THE VERY END OF EVERY NEWSLETTER E-MAIL AND PERIODIC E-MAIL ANNOUNCEMENT.

ONLINE VISITORS WHO WISH TO PURCHASE PRODUCTS FROM US MUST PROVIDE THEIR NAME, E-MAIL ADDRESS, MAILING ADDRESS, TELEPHONE NUMBER(S), AND CREDIT CARD INFORMATION.  ALL CREDIT CARD TRANSACTIONS OCCUR OVER A SECURE CONNECTION.  WE DO NOT STORE CREDIT CARD INFORMATION.

ALL ONLINE VISITOR INFORMATION COLLECTED BY FJR ADVISORS, LLC, INCLUDING BUT NOT LIMITED TO E-MAIL NEWSLETTER SUBSCRIBER INFORMATION AND CUSTOMER INFORMATION, IS PROTECTED AGAINST UNAUTHORIZED ACCESS.  WE WILL NOT SELL, TRADE, RENT, OR GIVE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION AWAY TO ANY COMPANIES, PERSONS, OR ANY OTHER ENTITIES.
