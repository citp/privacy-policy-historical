# Northwest Territorial Mint Privacy Policy

##### Your Privacy is Important to Us

Any information collected from visitors to this website will only be used in serving the needs of customers, which includes completing business transactions and responding to requests for information pertaining to our products and/or services. Per company policy, this information is not shared, for the purpose of subsequent dissemination, with any third parties.
