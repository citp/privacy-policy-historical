Privacy Policy

No nonsense summary:

Your information is kept private and handled securely.
Your email address will not be shared, sold or rented.
If you subscribe to our periodic newsletter, you can safely unsubscribe at any time.

The following is the full version of our policies:

Your privacy is very important to us. Accordingly, we have developed this Policy in order for you to understand how we collect, use, communicate and disclose and make use of personal information. The following outlines our privacy policy.

  • Before or at the time of collecting personal information, we will identify the purposes for which information is being collected.
  • We will collect and use of personal information solely with the objective of fulfilling those purposes specified by us and for other compatible purposes, unless we obtain the consent of the individual concerned or as required by law.
  • We will only retain personal information as long as necessary for the fulfillment of those purposes.
  • Credit card information will only be retained for the duration required to process (approve/decline) an online order. Credit card information is Not stored in our systems for future use.
  • We will collect personal information by lawful and fair means and, where appropriate, with the knowledge or consent of the individual concerned.
  • Personal data should be relevant to the purposes for which it is to be used, and, to the extent necessary for those purposes, should be accurate, complete, and up-to-date.
  • Collection of Email addresses will only be used for the normal processing of orders, and if chosen by the visitor, for the purpose of sending occasional information in the form of an email newsletter.
  • We will protect personal information by reasonable security safeguards against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.
  • We will make readily available to customers information about our policies and practices relating to the management of personal information.

We are committed to conducting our business in accordance with these principles in order to ensure that the confidentiality of personal information is protected and maintained.

Nutiva® is the world’s leading brand of all-organic hemp foods, coconut oil, red palm oil and chia seeds. We’re a values-driven brand, dedicated to “Nourishing people and planet.”   Our virgin organic coconut oil is cold-pressed, great for sautéing and baking, enhancing your favorite recipes, and body care.   Coconut Manna is our delicious puréed coconut butter, a tropical melt-in-your-mouth treat containing fiber, protein and nourishing fats.  Our dry coconut products include coconut flour, certified gluten-free with a lower glycemic index than most traditional flours, and unrefined, unprocessed coconut sugar, similar to brown sugar with a hint of caramel.   Our delicious organic hemp oil is cold-pressed and unrefined, containing the ideal 3:1 omega-6 to omega-3 ratio.   Rich in protein, with omega-3 and minerals, organic shelled hempseed is the world’s most nutritious seed.    Our organic  hemp protein contains high-quality plant protein with the branched-chain amino acids that are vital for good health, and it’s super easy to digest!  Also try our high fiber hemp protein and our chocolate and vanilla hempshakes.   Mighty, gluten-free chia seeds, packed with omega-3, protein, rare antioxidants and fiber, are amazing in yogurt, oatmeal, baked goods or smoothies.  Grown on small organic family farms in Ecuador, our red palm oil is certified organic, non-GMO and Fair Trade. For medium heat cooking, we have a sunflower and red palm oil blend, and additional cooking options with vegan shortening and buttery spread.  Since 1999 Nutiva has donated $1 million dollars by contributing 1 percent of sales to sustainable-agriculture groups for programs that enrich the soil and support a healthy world.
