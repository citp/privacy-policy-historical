# Nuts & Bolts Speed Training | Privacy Policy

NUTS & BOLTS SPEED TRAINING PRIVACY POLICY IS IN LINE WITH OUR ETHICAL AND CORPORATE VALUES. WE WILL NEVER SELL YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION TO ANY THIRD-PARTIES AND ANY PERSONAL INFORMATION COLLECTED THROUGH THIS SITE IS SOLELY FOR USE WITHIN NUTS & BOLTS SPEED TRAINING.

WE COLLECT INFORMATION TO HELP US PROVIDE YOU WITH A BETTER ONLINE EXPERIENCE. ANY PERSONAL INFORMATION THAT YOU ENTRUST TO US IS PROTECTED USING SSL TECHNOLOGY.

WE DO USE COOKIES TO COLLECT NON-PERSONALLY IDENTIFYING INFORMATION ABOUT YOUR LOCATION AND BROWSING HABITS. THIS INFORMATION HELPS US DETERMINE HOW OUR WEB SITE IS SERVING YOU. THIS DATA IS COMPLETELY ANONYMOUS AND DOES NOT IDENTIFY YOU PERSONALLY.

IN OUR EFFORTS TO PROVIDE YOU WITH THE BEST POSSIBLE ONLINE EXPERIENCE, WE MAY HIRE THIRD-PARTIES TO HELP US DEVELOP OUR WEB SITE. IF THEY HAVE ACCESS TO ANY OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION, IT WILL BE KEPT COMPLETELY CONFIDENTIAL WITHIN OUR AGREEMENT WITH THEIR COMPANY.

EXCEPT AS NOTED ABOVE, WE WILL NEVER SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION WITH ANY OTHER PARTIES.

IMPORTANT NOTE  
WE WILL DISCLOSE PERSONAL INFORMATION IF REQUIRED BY LAW, OR IF THE EFFORT IS BEING MADE IN GOOD FAITH TO COMPLY WITH LEGAL ACTIONS.

BY VISITING THIS WEB SITE, YOU ARE AGREEING TO OUR TERMS AND PRIVACY POLICY.
