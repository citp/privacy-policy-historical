# Privacy Policy - Nimbus Themes

[](https://web.archive.org/)

## PRIVACY POLICY – WWW.NIMBUSTHEMES.COM

If you would like more information about the privacy practices of Nimbus Themes you can contact us at: [sales@nimbusthemes.com](mailto:sales@nimbusthemes.com)

We take the privacy of our customers and visitor very seriously. The following sections provide details on the kinds of personal information that we may collect and how that information is used.

### Website Visitors – Log Files

The Nimbus Themes website makes use of server logging technology to produce log files which document very basic information about each visit. This information includes IP addresses, time of visit, and the browser in use.

### Google Analytics

Google Analytics is a web analysis service built and provided to the public by Google. This service uses javascript and cookies to track the way each visitor interacts with the nimbusthemes.com website.

### Google Adwords

Google Adwords is a marketing and advertising platform built and offered to the public by Google. Nimbus Themes may Adwords to promote products or articles. This service operates with the Adwords advertising network and the Doubleclick Cookie. You can learn about opting out here: <https://support.google.com/ads/answer/2662922?hl=en>.

### Google Adsense

Google AdSense is an advertising service provided by Google. www.nimbusthemes.com uses the “Doubleclick” Cookie, which tracks use of www.nimbusthemes.com and user behavior concerning ads, products and the services we offer. You can learn about opting out here: <https://support.google.com/ads/answer/2662922?hl=en>.

### Cookies

Nimbus Themes does use cookies to store information about visitors and to improve the usability of the Nimbus Themes website. If you wish to disable cookies you can do so through the setting in your browser.

### Changes

We reserve the right to change this policy at any time.
