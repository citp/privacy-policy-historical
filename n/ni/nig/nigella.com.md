**PABULUM PRODUCTIONS LTD. PRIVACY POLICY**

**Who we are**  
In this privacy policy references to “we”, “us”, “our” and “Pabulum” are to Pabulum Productions Ltd. References to “our Website” or “the Website” are to www.Nigella.com 

**What information we collect and how**

The information we collect via the Website may include.

1\. Any personal details you knowingly provide us with through forms and our email, such as name, address, emails, location etc.

2\. Your preferences and use of email updates, recorded by emails we send you (if you select to receive email updates on products and offers).

3\. Your IP Address, this is a string of numbers unique to your computer that is recorded by our web server when you request any page or component on the Website. This information is used to monitor your usage of the Website.

4\. Data recorded by the Website which allows us to recognise you and your preferred settings, this saves you from re-entering information on return visits to the site. Such data is recorded locally on your computer through the use of cookies. Most browsers can be programmed to reject, or warn you before downloading cookies, information regarding this may be found in your browsers ‘help’ facility.

**What we do with your information**

Any personal information we collect from this website will be used in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998 and other applicable laws. 

In certain cases we may use your email address to send you information on our other products and services. In such a case you will be offered the option to opt in/out of receiving this information in future.

We may need to pass the information we collect to other companies for administrative purposes. We may use third parties to carry out certain activities, such as processing and sorting data, monitoring how customers use the Website and issuing our e-mails for us. Third parties will not be allowed to use your personal information for their own purposes or pass on or sell your details.
