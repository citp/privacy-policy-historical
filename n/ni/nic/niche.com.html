Information collected from users of premium content: If you choose to subscribe to certain Niche Services you may be asked to supply certain personally identifying information such as name, postal address, email address or telephone number. Any information from selected outside companies will be transmitted by Niche so that such companies will not have access either to your E-mail address or to your other personally identifying information.

The choice is entirely optional and has no impact on the applicant's submission to the scholarship contest. If a scholarship applicant indicates an interest in receiving offers from Niche and its trusted business partners, Niche reserves the right to use your personal information and may sell or share it with selected outside companies or schools in accordance with the school contact preference you set in your user profile. If you express interest in particular schools or affiliates and your school contact preference is set to “Allow any schools to contact me” then Niche may sell or transfer your personal information to those schools or affiliates, or similar schools. If your school contact preference is set to “Only allow the specific colleges that I have said ‘Yes’ to contact me”, then we will only sell or share your personal information with your ‘Yes’ schools.

All information entered is voluntary and it is solely up to the user whether he or she furnishes such information. If a user does provide such information, he or she is consenting to the use of that information in accordance with the policies and practices described in this Privacy Policy. If your school contact preference is set to “Only allow the specific colleges that I have said ‘Yes’ to contact me”, then we will only sell or share your personal information with your ‘Yes’ schools. Personal contact information includes a user’s full name, email address, mailing address, and phone number. Only the account user is able to edit and update the information provided in his or her account. We may, on occasion, email our users to send notifications related to features on the site. What are "cookies" and "pixel tags" and how do you use them?

Cookies are pieces of information that are placed by a Web site in a storage place on your computer. Cookies do not contain information that is, in itself, personally identifying. Cookies are placed on your computer in a limited way to help deliver advertisements, to calculate unique site visitors, and to track usage throughout our site. Other uses include placement of cookies to make the login process more convenient for users of the Niche Services.

We participate in cookie based advertising networks. These networks may use cookies to collect anonymous information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. We do not permit these companies to collect any personally identifying information (name, address, email address or telephone number) about you. Click these links to learn more about business practices and privacy policies of these companies, including how to decline participation in these programs: http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/, http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html and http://www.tynt.com/opt_out.php. We may share anonymous user browsing information on niche.com with our partners, who may in turn communicate with users via advertisements on display ad networks.

Pixel tags - tiny graphic images – are used to help us analyze your online behavior. Pixel tags also allow us to send you email in a format you can read and let us know when you have opened an email message from us. We may use pixel tags to collect information about your visit, including the pages you view, the links you click and other actions taken in connection with our sites and services. We also collect certain standard information that your browser sends to every website you visit, such as your IP address, browser type and language, access times and referring web site addresses.

No one under the age of 13 should provide any personal information and Niche does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the event that we learn that we have collected information from a child under the age of 13, we will terminate the account and eliminate this information from our database. If you believe Niche Services has any information about anyone under 13, please contact us at support@niche.com.
