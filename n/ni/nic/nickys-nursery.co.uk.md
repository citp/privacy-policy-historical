# New Page 1

PRIVACY

Nicky's Nursery Ltd is committed to protecting your privacy. Nicky's Nursery Ltd will only use the information that we collect about you fairly and lawfully (in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998) and according to the Which? Web Trader Code of Conduct.  
Nicky's Nursery Ltd collect information about you for 3 reasons only: firstly, to process your order, second, to provide you with the best possible service so as to enable us, if there is a query with your order, to contact you and thirdly to enable us to post comment from you on our site  
Nicky's Nursery Ltd may e-mail you in the future to advise you about offers of products and services or information regarding our business unless you have informed us that you do not wish us to do so. We will take you off our list of e-mail addresses if you ask us to do so. Since the information we may wish to give you could come about at any time in the future, then, unless and until you have asked us to remove your data from our database, we will retain the data for an unlimited period of time.  
Nicky's Nursery Ltd will not pass your information to other parties save and only as may be required to process your order such as with suppliers of items sold, delivery services and card processing services.  
The type of information we will collect about you includes:  
· your name  
· address  
· phone number  
· email address  
· orders  
· comments made by you on onsite forms or by email  
**.** Internet referrer information connected with your visit in web logs

Nicky's Nursery Ltd will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent.

The information we hold will be only as accurate and up to date as you give it to us. You can check the information that we hold about you by emailing us. If you find any inaccuracies we will delete or correct it promptly. You agree to regularly check the data and inform us if there are any errors or changes required.

The personal information which we hold will be held securely in accordance with our internal security policy and the law and the Which? Web Trader Code.  
Nicky's Nursery Ltd will not transfer your information outside the EEA (European Economic Area) without first obtaining your specific consent.  
Please note that all comments made in a Visitor Book may be displayed on this site, in whole or in part, at our complete discretion.

Nicky's Nursery Ltd may use technology to track the patterns of behaviour of visitors to our site. This can include using a "cookie" which would be stored on your browser, at the present time the cookie only stores your shopping information and name, address, phone number, referrer and email address. You can usually modify your browser to prevent this happening. The information collected in this way can be used to identify you unless you modify your browser settings.
