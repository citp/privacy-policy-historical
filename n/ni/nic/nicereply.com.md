### WHAT KIND OF INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

We collect the following information (the **Data** ​) about you, depending on you use of our Site and/or Services:

For the purpose of registration on our Site and further access to our Site and personalized services on our Site we process the following information: (i) your name, (ii) surname and (iii) e-mail. 

The processing of the above information is necessary for the performance of the contract entered into between you and us. The legal basis of processing these information is Article 6(1)(b) of the GDPR regulation. 

#### B. Information necessary for the provision of Services

For the purpose of providing our Services (i.e. the provision of your paid services) we process the following information: (i) your name, (ii) surname, (iii) address, (iv) e-mail, (v) information about you payment card (card number, expiry date and CVV code). 

The processing of the above information is necessary for the performance of the contract entered into between you and us. The legal basis of processing these information is Article 6(1)(b) of the GDPR regulation. 

#### C. Information collected for marketing purposes

For the purpose of receiving our news and/or newsletters, information of sales, actualizations, updates and offers and other marketing materials in the form of e-mails or letters and/or notifications we process (i) your name, (ii) surname, (iii) e-mail and (iv) address.

If you decide that you do not wish to receive such messages, you have the right to withdraw your consent by clicking on the following hyperlink directly in our e-mail communication or available directly in your account in section **personal settings​**. For the purpose of continuous improvement of our Services, developing your user experience when using our Site we process the following information: (i) about your computer, (ii) about your visit and usage of our Sites and/or our Services (including your IP address and geographical location, browser type and version, operating system, referral source, length of visit, page views and website navigation paths or any similar information that may be obtained by the Company directly or indirectly from third party providers.), (iii) generated in the course of the use of those Services (including the timing, frequency and pattern of Service use). 

The processing of the above information is necessary for the performance of the contract entered into between you and us. The legal basis of processing these information is Article 6(1)(b) of the GDPR regulation. 

#### E. Information published on your profile

Fur the purpose of creating a profile on or Site we process your following personal data: (i) name, (ii) surname and (iii) e-mail. 

The processing of the above information is necessary for the performance of the contract entered into between you and us. The legal basis of processing these information is Article 6(1)(b) of the GDPR regulation. 

#### F. Information we receive when you communicate with us

For the purpose of communicating with us for any reason (for example, support when using our Site/ Services, resolving complaints and any other problems etc.) we shall process your following personal data: (i) your name, (ii) surname, (iii) address, (iv) e-mail, (v) phone number, (vi) communication content, (vii) metadata associated with the communication and (viii) any other necessary data, which you shall decide to provide in your communication. 

The processing of the above information is necessary for the performance of the contract entered into between you and us. The legal basis of processing these information is Article 6(1)(b) of the GDPR regulation.

#### WHAT KIND OF COOKIES DO WE USE?

A cookie is a file containing an identifier (a string of letters and numbers) that is sent by a web server to a web browser and is stored by the browser on your mobile device or computer to identify you (usually based on an anonymous identifier). The identifier is then sent back to the server each time the browser requests a page from the server in order to identify your visit to a particular page, the list of ads you have responded to, the type of browser you are using, and also to remember information you have filled it on our website. 

We use cookies to personalise content and ads to provide social media features and to analyse our traffic. We also share information about use of our Site with our social media, advertising and analytics partners who may combine it with other information that you’ve provided to them or that they’ve collected from your use of their services. 

The purpose of using Cookies is to be able to adapt to your needs, to distinguish you from other users, to prevent the display of irrelevant ads in your web browser and to prevent the need for your repeated login to our Interface. 

Cookies may be either "persistent" cookies or "session" cookies: a persistent cookie will be stored by a web browser and will remain valid until its set expiry date, unless deleted by the user before the expiry date; a session cookie, on the other hand, will expire at the end of the user session, when the web browser is closed. 

There are also third-party Cookies: ​Third-party cookies are cookies that are set by a website other than our Site. For example, a website might have a social media like button on their site. That like button will set a cookie that can be read by social media provider. The aim of third-party cookies is often to collect certain information to carry out various research into behaviour, demographics and not least for targeted marketing etc. 

Cookies do not typically contain any information that personally identifies a user, but personal information that we store about you may be linked to the information stored in and obtained from cookies.
