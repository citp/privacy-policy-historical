# Privacy Policy | Nice HD Wallpaper

## Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

The owners and operators of nicehdwallpaper.com take your online privacy seriously. This document outlines the types of information collected by our servers and provides links to the privacy policies of our third-party advertising partners.

**Third-Party Advertisers**

Third Party are involved in advertising on Nice hd wallpaper. These third parties may use information (not including your name, email id etc.) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to your website. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites.

You can visit Google ad and content privacy policy at. <http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html>
