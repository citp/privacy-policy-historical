SAFE ON-LINE SHOPPING @ NCIX.com -- YOUR PRIVACY

Netlink Computers / NCIX.com respects your privacy and your concerns about how the information you provide might be used. Any information we receive from you will be kept private and we do not sell or share this information with anyone outside of NCIX.com.

On occasion, we e-mail updates and product specials that may be of interest to our customers. If you do not wish to receive these mailings, you may unsubscribe at any time by following the unsubscribe procedure described in the e-mail.

We guarantee that every transaction you make at NCIX.com will be 100% safe. We will guarantee up to the full amount of your purchase with us if any unauthorized charges are made to your credit as a result of shopping at NCIX.com.

NCIX.com uses all the latest security methods and fraud detection techniques to protect all of the information you entrust to us.

All private information is secured using SSL encryption to insure your privacy and safety while buying from us. SSL is the industry standard and is the safest software available today for secure commerce transactions. It scrambles all of your personal information including credit card number, name, and address so even if information is intercepted while the information travels over the Internet, the resulting information captured is entirely useless. For details about SSL encryption, please visit http://www.verisign.com/.

All the customer information is stored behind the extended security firewall. We will ensure that our customer information is stored in the most secured manner and never available to anyone but trusted NCIX.com staff.

We guarantee your absolute satisfaction and safety when shopping at NCIX.com!

SHIPPING & DELIVERY

NCIX.com delivers anywhere in North America by UPS, FEDEX, courier or postal services. While our goal is to ship as soon as we can -- often within 24 hours of receiving an order if an item is in stock -- shipment and delivery is subject to inventory and stock availability from our vendors and distributors. On some orders, we will ship partial shipments in order to get you your order as soon as possible.

Order fulfillment, shipments, customer support and customer inquiries occur between 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM weekdays, and 10 AM to 5:30 PM Saturday, Pacific Standard Time. We are closed Sundays and statutory holidays.

Please note that for security reasons, first time buyers may experience a 48 hour delay to verify a new customer's credit card.
