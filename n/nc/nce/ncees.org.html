Privacy of users

Our Web site hosting company collects general information such as the visitor's domain name, referral data, browser, and platform. Internet Protocol addresses are tracked only to determine the level of activity on our Web site. They are not tracked to determine identity. This information may be shared with a third party. However, unless otherwise provided for in this policy, NCEES will not disclose any personal identifiable data without first obtaining permission.

Our Web site contains many opportunities for users to provide information in response to surveys, on-line registration forms, on-line order forms, etc. These forms require personally identifiable information from the Web site user. NCEES uses firewalls and secure socket layer software to protect the integrity and security of this information while it is in our control.


Links

NCEES's Web site contains links to other sites. NCEES is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these other Web sites nor does NCEES officially endorse or recommend these sites and/or services.


Changes in terms of privacy

NCEES reserves the right to modify these privacy policies. Any modifications will be posted on this page. Please familiarize yourself with these policies and periodically check back here for changes.
