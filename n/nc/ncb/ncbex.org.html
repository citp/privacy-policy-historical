Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy at National Conference of Bar Examiners (NCBE). The information that you furnish is the property of NCBE and the admission authority of the jurisdiction to which you are applying. We do not sell, share, or rent this information to others in any way that we have not mentioned in this statement.

Security

We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards in conformance to industry standards to protect personal information about you. We limit access to personal information about you to NCBE employees and agents whom we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to do their jobs. Your account information is password-protected. We use industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.

The Information We Collect

NCBE's web server monitors traffic by automatically recording every visitor's host, domain name, pages visited, length of user sessions, browser type and/or IP address. This information includes the number of times the site is visited, how many visitors come to the site, which parts of the site they frequent, and the length of time of each visit. This information is limited to pages you visit within our website and does not contain personally identifiable information and is used to evaluate areas of interest and to improve the NCBE web site. NCBE does not share this information with any other organizations.

When using our online application we collect personal information such as name, address, residences, employers, etc. This information is used solely for the purposes of preparing a character report for the jurisdiction to which you are applying. All information solicited and received for character reports is treated confidentially by NCBE and restricted to official use by the proper admitting authorities. NCBE is an affiliated organization of the American Bar Association.

Cookies

Cookies are used by NCBE to keep track of your login information while you are currently using our website. These cookies are temporary and should not be saved by your web browser after you log off of NCBE's website.
