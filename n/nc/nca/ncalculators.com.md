## **Gathering User Information & Sharing **

Under any circumstances, ncalculators.com do not share any personal information it collects from the users with third parties without visitor consent or as otherwise required by law. 

  
Ncalculators.com may gather personal or general information about users activity within & around the website. Please read the below information to know how & what information we collect through our website.   


**For Personal Information**

Users or Visitors may required to provide personal information, such as names & email addresses when they initiate any communication through emails or through the Contact Us form available at http://ncalculators.com/feedback.php, otherwise, ncalculators.com never ask your personal information. Also we keep our users personal information **very confidential**. We never sell, lease or share personal information, including names and email addresses, with any third party unless required by law.

**Cookies**

Like many other websites, ncalculators.com using HTTP Cookies, a small piece of data sent from our website & stored in the user's web browser while browsing our website to collect the general information to measure our website visitors to ensure better user experience for using this wewbsite. This general information of user activity includes but is not limited to, IP (internet protocol) addresses, browser types, ISPs (Internet Service Providers), entry pages, pages visited, exit pages, access times, demographic locations, referring web sites and click activity. This information that is collected through the cookies is **completely anonymous** and does not identify any visitor by personally. We use cookies to personalise content and ads, to provide social media features and to analyse our traffic. We also share information about your use of our site with our social media, advertising and analytics partners who may combine it with other information you've provided to them or they've collected from your use of their services.

For analytics purpose, we use Google Analytics, a third party service to measure our visitors or user's activity within & around our website. Google Analytics may collect such general information which includes IP (internet protocol) addresses, browser types, ISPs (Internet Service Providers), exit pages, access times, demographic locations, referring web sites and click activity. Please visit [Google Analytics Privacy & Security](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/6004245) to get more information about Google Analytics user's data security and privacy. To opt out Google Analytics, please visit [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

Like most of other websites, We have embedded social media buttons & icons for Google+, Facebook, Twitter & Pinterest and others to include social media features to Like, Tweet & Share contents over social media networks. If you have logged into any social media site like not limited to Google+, Facebook, Twitter & Pinterest, such social media sites may identify that you are accessing our websites to enable you to avail the social media features. However, we don't have any control ourselves. Even we don't know how the social media features are functioning. For such social media networks or sites they have their own privacy policy, so please enable private browsing or cookie free browsing if you wish you don't want them to know when you are accessing our website, if you are using social media services.
