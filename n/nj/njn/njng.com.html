General Statement

This statement was created in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site, www.njng.com. New Jersey Natural Gas reserves the right to change this policy at any time. New Jersey Natural Gas does not disclose personal information to third parties.

Information Collected

Our Web servers automatically collect limited information about your computer's connection to the Internet, including your Internet provider (IP) address, when you visit our sites. Your IP address does not identify you personally. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. We use this information to deliver our Web pages to you upon request, to tailor our site to the interests of our visitors, and to measure traffic within our site.

Our online surveys and polls ask visitors for contact information (like their e-mail address). We may use contact data from our surveys and polls to send visitors information about our Company. The contact information is also used to contact visitors when necessary. Visitors may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

Links to Other Sites

This site contains a link to our parent company's Web site, njresources.com, and to njliving.com, sponsored by New Jersey Resources Corp. Please check the privacy policy statement on those sites to see how personal information is utilized.

This site also contains links to third-party sites. You should be aware that these sites are not controlled by New Jersey Natural Gas and therefore are not subject to this policy. You should check the privacy policies of these individual sites to see how proprietors of those sites will utilize personal information.

Public Forums

This site makes chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its visitors. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose personal information.

Security

No security system is impenetrable. However, we use state-of-the-art security measures to protect information that we have collected from you at our site.

My Account Services

My Account Services uses secure areas protected by firewall technology and data encryption. To access these areas, customers or their authorized agent, must provide their account number and account name exactly as they appear on their bill. Additionally, to view account information, a password is required. This information is used to protect against unauthorized access to a customer's account information. Please refer to our Help section for information on passwords.

Choice/Opt-Out

Our site provides visitors the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at the point where we request information about the visitor. A visitor has the option to remove their information from our database to discontinue receiving communications. This can be done by sending an e-mail to sitesupport@njng.com and telling us that you do not want to receive such communications from our Company.

Correct/Update

A visitor has the option to change and modify information previously provided by sending an e-mail to sitesupport@njng.com and requesting the changes.
