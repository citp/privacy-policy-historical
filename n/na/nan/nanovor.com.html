INFORMATION SMITH & TINKER COLLECTS FROM YOU

Smith & Tinker may collect information through your active submission of information or passively through use of automated technologies.

Active Information Collection

Much of the personally identifiable information we collect from our visitors is submitted voluntarily in response to our specific requests for such information. For example, visitors may provide us with personal information to sign up for our newsletter or in response to polls or other interactivity incorporated into the Sites. We do not require users to provide any more information than necessary to participate in the related activity.

The information we actively collect from you may include some or all of the following: name, date of birth, address, email address, username, password and/or gender.

For some of our online activities - such as polls or surveys - we may ask users to provide information that is not personally identifiable, such as country of residence, hobbies or feedback or suggestions for the Sites.

Passive Information Collection

As you navigate through the Sites, other information may be collected through use of technologies, which are common among websites and online environments. For example, we may track Internet protocol ("IP") addresses, which is a unique string of numbers assigned to your computer by your Internet service provider.

We also use "cookies," which are small pieces of information or files that a website puts on your computer when you explore the Sites. The cookies we use allow us to collect, store and sometimes track information about your use of the Sites or to save your settings (such as your username and password) or provide a customized experience.

In addition to our active information collection efforts, we may also use technology to gather operational information about the technology you use, such as your browser, type of computer, operating systems and Internet service providers. This information cannot be used to identify you personally.

HOW SMITH & TINKER USES AND DISCLOSES YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

Smith & Tinker uses your personal information to enable you to receive the particular items/services for which you are submitting that information. We may use your e-mail address to send you e-mails about the Sites or other Smith & Tinker products. We may use information about your location to provide information to you about Smith & Tinker or our products events in your area.

Although we do not generally give your personal information to third parties, we may disclose your information to Smith & Tinker partners only if you expressly consent to such disclosure.

We reserve the right to disclose information if required to do so by law or by a regulatory authority. We may also disclose your information as part of an investigation or enforcement action relating to improper or illegal conduct in connection with the Sites.

If a third party acquires the assets of Smith & Tinker related to Sites and services (whether by sale, merger, change of control, bankruptcy or otherwise), your information may be transferred to the new owner(s). In such case, your information would remain subject to the provisions of the Privacy Notice that was applicable to your information immediately prior to the transfer.

Smith & Tinker may maintain aggregate data or other non-personally identifiable information about our users, such as usage statistics, online traffic patterns and user feedback. In addition to using this information to support operation of the Sites, Smith & Tinker may disclose this anonymous information to third parties without restriction. This information cannot be used to identify you.

HOW SMITH & TINKER PROTECTS YOUR INFORMATION

We work hard to protect your personal information. Smith & Tinker and our third party partners store our email lists on secure servers. We use both technical and procedural methods to maintain the integrity and security of our lists and have restricted access to data. The Sites may contain links to other websites operated by third parties that may be of interest to you. When you follow a link and leave the Sites, you do so at your own risk because we do not control such third party websites. We strongly encourage you to review the privacy policies of such third party websites before providing them with any personal information.

You have the following choices regarding the personally identifiable information you provide for Smith & Tinker to use:

You may send us an e-mail message at privacy@smithandtinker.com to request access to review, correct or delete any personally identifiable information maintained on our systems that you or your child has provided to us.
