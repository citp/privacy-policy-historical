Information Collection and Use

We don’t collect data that people commonly understand to be personal, such as names, addresses, etc. However the concept of “personal data” under GDPR is a broad one.

If you are a Nanigans customer, Nanigans’ tracking solutions may gather first-party data on individuals. In the case of website pixels, a combination of unique user IDs, cookies, and IP addresses are used for attribution purposes. In situations where the Nanigans SDK is in place for mobile app integrations, unique user IDs and device / advertising IDs (IDFA, GDFA, Android ID) are collected for attribution purposes. Additionally, device specific user-agent and IP address data is carried by all network requests and collected along with the data listed above. The information that we do receive is generated from ‘clicks’ and/or impressions on our ads and enables us to best provide our customers with effective targeting and feedback.

Customer accounts with Nanigans are only available to advertisers. For these accounts, we hold user submitted information such as username and password in order to authenticate users and to contact users with relevant information having to do with their Nanigans accounts.

If you visit our website or receive our email newsletter, we may collect your contact information such as name, email address, mailing address, and phone number. We may use this information for internal purposes to send you a requested product, respond to your service requests, questions or concerns, administer your account, send you newsletters or catalogues in accordance with your marketing and communication preferences, and/or to improve our web site and marketing efforts.

Analytics/Log Files

As is true of most web sites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes information that may be regarded as personal information in certain countries, such as internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), search terms, referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data.

We use this information, to analyze trends, to administer the site, to track users’ movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole.

Cookies, Web Beacons, and Tracking

Nanigans and our partners use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies on our websites and mobile applications as well as those of our advertisers. Cookies are small files that are placed on your browser or device by the website or app you are using or ad you have viewed. Some cookies contain personal information, such as an identifier that can be used to connect the actions of an individual visitor on a website, while others contain anonymous information used in site operations.

There are two types of cookies: session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie expires when you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains on your hard drive for an extended period of time. Simply exiting your browser will remove all session cookies. You can remove persistent cookies by following directions provided in your Internet browser’s “help” directory.

We employ a software technology called Web Beacons (a.k.a. clear gifs) along with cookies. Web beacons are tiny graphics or scripts embedded in web pages, which are used to track information about the behavior of individual visitors, such as which products they browse over time. Web beacons cannot generally be disabled, but they rely on cookies to identify visitors.

Cookies on our properties

On the nanigans.com website and within our advertising platform, we use cookies to provide you with a better, faster or safer experience. Cookies are used to understand, improve, and research products and services. For example, cookies may be used on our website to understand how you are using social plugins in order to improve them, and in our platform to track usage and performance of individual features. They may also be used to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. We partner with other companies in these efforts, and may receive reports based on the use of these technologies by these companies on an individual as well as aggregated basis.

We use session cookies for functions such as server load balancing and denial-of-service attack protection, and persistent cookies for login, analytics, and marketing. If you reject cookies, you may still use our site, but your ability to use some areas of our site may be limited. Our platform requires an authorized login and cannot operate without cookies.

Cookies used in advertising

Advertisers use our platform to reach prospective customers across a variety of web and social properties. Our platform utilizes cookies and beacons placed in their sites and apps to help them advertise more effectively. It places ‘analytical’ and ‘operational’ persistent cookies. Analytical cookies allow us to identify visitors and measure how they interact with our advertisers’ sites and advertisements. This helps us to improve the effectiveness of advertising by delivering personalized ads to the visitors who find them most relevant. Operational cookies contain no personal information and are support operations of the advertising system such as segment management and pixel forwarding. Our platform also facilitates the insertion of web beacons from publishers, who may also place cookies for advertising purposes.

As an example of the use of cookies and tracking in advertising, a you may be shown an ad placed by the Nanigans platform as you browse websites, use mobile apps, or interact on social networks. A click or tap on the ad will take you to our advertiser’s site or app. At that point, a Nanigans beacon would place a cookie on your browser and our platform would analyze your usage of the site or app. If you show interest in certain products, the platform can tune future advertisements to be more relevant to you based on that information. If you show little interest, the platform can learn that the ad it showed you was irrelevant or poorly placed, and avoid showing it to others in the future.

3rd party tracking

The use of cookies and clear gifs by our technology partners and service providers is not covered by our privacy policy. Our technology partners and service providers use trackers to optimize your experience on our site and help them better manage content on our site.
