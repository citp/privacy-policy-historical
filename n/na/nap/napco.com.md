# North American Publishing Company Privacy Policy

**  
Privacy Policy Statement**

This is the Web site of North American Publishing Company.

Our postal address is:  
401 North Broad Street  
Philadelphia, PA 19108 USA

We can be reached via e-mail at [customerservice@napco.com](mailto:customerservice@napco.com). Or you can reach us by telephone at (215) 238-5482 or toll free at (800) 777-8074.

For each non-subscribing visitor to our Web site, our Web server automatically recognizes the visitor's domain name, but not the e-mail address.

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who order from us or voluntarily register. The e-mail information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web site, used to customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual visitor, used to notify visitors about updates to our Web site, used by us to contact visitors about new products and services or upcoming events, and is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes unless specifically stated as such.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending e-mail to us at the above address or writing to us at the above address and telling us that you do not want to receive e-mail from our company. You may also e-mail [webmaster@napco.com](mailto:webmaster@napco.com).

If you supply us with your postal address online you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending e-mail to us at the above address or writing to us at the above address. Please provide us with your exact name, company name and address. We will be sure your name is removed from the list we use internally which may on a limited basis be shared with other organizations.

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such telephone calls, please let us know by sending email to us at the above address or writing to us at the above address. Please provide us with your exact name, company name, address and phone number. We will be sure your name is removed from the list we use internally which may on a limited basis be shared with other organizations.

Questions & comments about this Web site? E-mail: [webmaster@napco.com](mailto:webmaster@napco.com)
