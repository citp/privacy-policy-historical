News & Events

Patients Needed for Eczema Studies
National Jewish is seeking patients with eczema, also known as atopic dermatitis, for research studies on skin infections and sleep disturbances associated with the disease.

Denver Nuggets visit National Jewish
The Denver Nuggets basketball team, coaches, cheerleaders, and mascot all spread holiday cheer to children at the Kunsberg School on the National Jewish campus.

National Jewish Offers Free Spirometry Testing
This simple test measures air flow into and out of the lungs.  Spirometry test results are useful in making the diagnosis of a specific lung disorder.  Yearly spirometry measurements help to detect lung disease at an early stage when lifestyle changes and treatment may help forestall future problems.

Helping Children Control Asthma
A simple, new questionnaire can identify children with poorly controlled asthma who suffer symptoms that limit their daily activities.
