What Information Natural Area Rugs Collects

Natural Area Rugs gathers personal data from visitors who register their information on our website or avail of our online store services. We also use cookies for tracking web browsers and IP addresses in order to continuously improve our online services.

How Natural Area Rugs Uses Information You Provide

All personal information collected will be utilized to process website related services such as inquires, quotes and online orders. We may from time to time, communicate via email, certain announcements concerning our website, but agree to omit from our mailing list, individuals who do not wish to receive such messages. Otherwise, we only use customer information to analyze our performance, or communicate matters such as order confirmation, quotes, or tracking information.

Does Natural Area Rugs Share Your Information With Third Parties?

We at Natural Area Rugs do not disclose our clients or website visitors personal information with entities outside of our organization. Whenever the need arises, we hire selected third parties to enhance our website. As such, there may be situations, which necessitate third parties to process customer information (under very secure procedures), solely for assisting our company in such undertakings. Unless, required by law, we do not share your information with any other entity.

Security

Natural Area Rugs adopts strict security measures to protect your personal information. However, since internet data transmissions cannot be guaranteed to be secure from access by unintended recipients, we shall not be liable for breach of security unless this is due to our negligence.
