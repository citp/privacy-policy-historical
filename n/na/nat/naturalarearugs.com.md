# Natural Area Rugs - Buy Contemporary Area Rugs Online At Discount Prices

** Privacy Policy**NaturalAreaRugs.com respects your privacy.  We will not provide your information to any third party, aside from Yahoo!, in the course of order processing, as outlined below.  We may send occasional newsletters to your email, if you request.  Please notify us if you would like your information deleted from our client service mail list. 

The ordering portion of this site is hosted by Yahoo! Store. Yahoo! hosts some store pages, ordering system, and order data. Yahoo! automatically collects order information but may only use this information in the aggregate. If you accessed this store through Yahoo! Shopping, Yahoo! also automatically collects information about your shopping experience. Please see the [Yahoo! Privacy Policy](http://privacy.yahoo.com/privacy/us/) for more information about how Yahoo! uses this information.
