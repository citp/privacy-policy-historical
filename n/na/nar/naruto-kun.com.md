# Privacy policy - Naruto-Kun

### Privacy Policy

The following webpage outlines Naruto-Kun's collection and use of personal [information](https://web.archive.org/information/ "information") from it's users. Naruto-Kun values the privacy of our members and users, we will never share any personal [information](https://web.archive.org/information/ "information") of anybody who logs on to Naruto-Kun with anyone. This includes your e-mail address, name, and location. Upon logging on to Naruto-Kun such things as your IP address and hostname are logged for statistical and security reasons. 

### Cookies

A cookie is a very small text file placed on your system upon logging on to Naruto-Kun (and most other websites). This file serves as your identification card and is uniquely yours, and can only be read by the server that gave it to you. Cookies tell us that you have returned to a specific web page on Naruto-Kun and [help us](https://web.archive.org/help-us/ "help us") track your preferences and transactional habits. The basic function of cookies is to [help](https://web.archive.org/help-us/ "help") our server remember who you are. 

### Children's Privacy

Naruto-Kun does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. We make reasonable efforts to prevent someone who is underage from joining as a member of Naruto-Kun, and will not collect [information](https://web.archive.org/information/ "information") from them. If Naruto-Kun learns that a child under the age of 13 has become a member, we will close that child's account and delete any [information](https://web.archive.org/information/ "information") collected about the child. 

Not withstanding the foregoing, Naruto-Kun may choose to retain some personal [information](https://web.archive.org/information/ "information") such as the child's e-mail address as a means to prevent the child from re-registering at our website. The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) went into effect in April 2000, and as a result websites all over the world wide web had to change their standards to not collect any [information](https://web.archive.org/information/ "information") from a child. 

### Disclosing Information

Naruto-Kun may store and disclose personal [information](https://web.archive.org/information/ "information") allowed or required by applicable law or when deemed advisable by us. This means that we may make disclosures that are necessary to conform to [legal](https://web.archive.org/legal-disclaimer/ "legal") and regulatory requirements or processes and to protect the rights, safety, and property of Naruto-Kun, users of the Naruto-Kun website, and the public. 

### Security

At Naruto-Kun we make reasonable efforts to protect personal [information](https://web.archive.org/information/ "information") such as passwords and use technology such as encryption, access control procedures, firewalls, and physical security. We urge you to use a unique password with both letters and numbers to protect your account on Naruto-Kun and it's affiliated websites. If others, including family, friends or other household members access and use the [message board](https://web.archive.org/forum/ "message board") through your [login](https://web.archive.org/login/ "login") credentials, you are responsible for the actions of that individual. Only in extreme cases will your account be fully terminated. 

### Third Parties

Third Party websites may collect [information](https://web.archive.org/information/ "information") from users of Naruto-Kun, this [information](https://web.archive.org/information/ "information") will include your IP address, hostname, and [information](https://web.archive.org/information/ "information") about your system to [help us](https://web.archive.org/help-us/ "help us") serve you better. These are purely used for statistical reasons, and will not be used in any way other then that.

Additionally, we use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use [information](https://web.archive.org/information/ "information") (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more [information](https://web.archive.org/information/ "information") about this practice and to know your choices about not having this [information](https://web.archive.org/information/ "information") used by these companies, click [here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/).
