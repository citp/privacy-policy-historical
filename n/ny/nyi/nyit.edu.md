# Privacy Policy | About | NYIT

**Privacy:**  
New York Institute of Technology is committed to respecting your privacy. You can visit the official NYIT website without revealing any personal information. If you choose to provide any personal information during your visit, it will only be used for the purpose specifically disclosed at the time of your visit and only to conduct official NYIT business. Personal information will not be disclosed to third parties without your explicit consent.

**Information Gathering/Use of Information:**  
The NYIT website staff uses the information gathered from its visitors to tailor site content to visitor needs and to generate aggregate statistical reports.

**IP Address:**  
The NYIT website collects and records its visitors' IP address (the unique identifier of a computer on the Internet) to help diagnose problems with NYIT's server and to administer NYIT's website by identifying which parts of the site are most heavily visited. NYIT does not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable. This means that user sessions will be tracked, but visitors will remain anonymous.

**Cookies:**  
NYIT uses cookies on nyit.edu to store visitor preferences and to record information on what pages users access or visit. The information that we collect (e.g., IP address of your computer and pages viewed) does not contain user names, addresses, telephone numbers, or email addresses. The information collected helps us refine and improve NYIT's website content and presentation, customize web page content based on visitors' browser type or other information that the visitor sends, deliver relevant advertising regarding NYIT on other websites users visit, and optimize our placement in search engine results.

To delete or manage cookies settings, please follow the instructions for your web browser:

  * [Internet Explorer](http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/internet-explorer/delete-manage-cookies#ie=ie-11)
  * [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647?hl=en)
  * [Firefox](http://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/delete-cookies-remove-info-websites-stored)
  * [Safari](http://support.apple.com/kb/index?page=search&q=cookies%20safari)



**Public Forums:**  
This site may make use of discussion forums, the contents of which are accessible to all users of the Internet. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and users should exercise caution when deciding to disclose any personal information. From time to time, NYIT may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in this privacy notice. If NYIT's information practices change at some time in the future the policy changes will be posted to the website; only data collected from the time of the policy change forward will be used for new purposes. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at the NYIT website periodically.
