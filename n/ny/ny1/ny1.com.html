Privacy Policy

No e-mail address sent to NY1 News as the result of correspondence and the sharing of information will be used or collected for the purposes of reselling to a third party.

Cookies

The NY1 News Website collects no personally identifying information about users except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals via e-mail or web form. This site may place a "cookie" in the browser files of a user's computer. The cookie itself contains no personal information and the information tracked is intended only to enhance the online experience of the user.

Cookies on NY1.com record the user's browser type to aid in the delivery of content, and remember the geographical preference set by the user when they view news content by borough, in order to organize news stories to their liking.
