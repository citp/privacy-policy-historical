# NYCtourist.com - legal stuff, contacts, misc...

**_Privacy Policy  
_**We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to NYCtourist.com will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to.

NYCtourist.com analyzes our website logs to constantly improve the value of the materials available on website. Our website logs **are not personally identifiable** , and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.

NYCtourist.com does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or E-mail lists.

NYCtourist.com maintains several E-mail lists to keep our members informed about New York City. Individuals must affirmatively ask to join our mailing lists by filling out various forms on this site in order to join the list.Â  We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone. In addition, we configure our list server software to refuse to divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone including other list subscribers.
