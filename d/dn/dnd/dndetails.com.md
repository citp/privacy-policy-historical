# Domain Name Details - Privacy Policy

**Privacy**

We never collect any personally identifiable information.  
We collect the following for aggregate usage statistics.  
This data will not be disclosed to any third parties.

Your IP address, Browser User Agent, Time of Access

**Domain Whois Searches**

We recognize the problem of unscrupulous WHOIS sites gathering search information.  
Search queries are stored only when neccessary to return requested data.  
**We never save search data for unregistered domain names!**

#### Cookies and Web Beacons

www.dndetails.com does not use cookies. 

#### DoubleClick DART Cookie

  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on www.dndetails.com. 
  * Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to www.dndetails.com and other sites on the Internet. 
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL - http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html 



Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include Google Adsense. 

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on www.dndetails.com send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see. 

www.dndetails.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers. 

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. www.dndetails.com's privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites. 

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your browser options.
