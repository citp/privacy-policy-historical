**Information We Collect**

  * _Personal Information You Provide_. We collect information that you provide to us through our Site that is used to identify you personally (“personal information”) when we provide customer service or you place an order, register with us, send us an email, sign up for our newsletter, call us, complete a survey or enter a sweepstakes or contest. Personal information includes your name, mailing address, billing address, email address, telephone number and credit card information. This information may also include any preference information you provide to us, including a record of your online product purchases or information you submit about other people, for example the name and address of a gift recipient.
  *  _Other Information that is Automatically Collected_. Our web servers automatically collect certain types of information about your computer hardware and software when you visit our Site. This information may include your domain name, IP address(es), type of browser you use, dates and times of your visits, page visits, the website from which your visit originated, time spent on web pages and other similar traffic data. No personal information is automatically collected. This information may be used by us to operate the Site and to provide general statistics regarding the use of the Site.
  *  _Cookies._ Cookies are bits of information that are sent to your browser and stored on your computer’s hard drive. We use cookies to customize your visits to our Site by customizing your shopping experience and keeping track of your shopping cart. Cookies allow us to more easily communicate and interact with you. You may disallow cookies through your browser and still browse and shop our Site. However, if you set your browser so that it does not accept cookies, you may not be able to access certain features of our Site.
  *  _Information from Other Sources_. We may obtain information about you from outside sources and combine it with your account information. For example, we may use commercially available demographics and other marketing information from third parties to help us better serve you or inform you about products or services that we think will be of interest to you. We sometimes receive updated delivery and address information from our shippers or other sources so that we can correct our records and deliver your next purchase or communication more easily. Finally, we may also purchase lists containing your personal information (i.e. postal address, phone number, email address) for advertising and marketing purposes.
