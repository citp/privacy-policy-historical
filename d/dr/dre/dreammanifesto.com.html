Our Commitment To Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.

The Information We Collect

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the Dream Manifesto website at www.dreammanifesto.com. On some pages, you can order products, make requests, and register to receive materials. The types of personal information collected at these pages are:

Name
E-mail address
Products you wish to order

Your mailing address and/or telephone number is never taken directly, nor are your credit card details. These items are handled by the relevant payments processor, such as SWREG or PayPal. We may maintain a copy of this information for our own records.

The Way We Use Information

We use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order to complete that order. If you opt-in to our newsletter, we will also use your name and e-mail address when sending newsletters to you.

We may also use your name, e-mail address, mailing address and/or telephone number to send you further updates or marketing material, either from ourselves or carefully selected partners. You will always be able to opt-out of this, should you wish to do so. Instructions for doing this are sent with each communication.

If you purchased through an affiliate link, we may also share your name, e-mail address and purchase details with that affiliate. We do not share any other information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete your order.

We use the information you provide about someone else when placing an order only to ship the product and to confirm delivery. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order.

We use names and return email addresses in order to respond to support email. We may also follow-up with potential customers using these details. Such details are only used for such purposes and are not shared with outside parties.

You can register on our website to receive certain complimentary ebook publications, and/or our regular newsletter. Newsletters are typically sent out once per week, and we may also mail special promotional offers to our subscribers. You will only receive this information if you fill out a registration form and confirm your email address by verified double opt-in. You can opt-out at any time, by clicking the link at the bottom of every message.

We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website, or that Y number of men and Z number of women filled out our registration form, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals.

Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.

Our Commitment To Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Our Commitment To Children's Privacy

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.

How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information

You can access all your personally identifiable information that we collect online and maintain by writing to us and making an official request. We use this procedure to better safeguard your information.

You can correct factual errors in your personally identifiable information by sending us a request that credibly shows error.

To protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections.

If you wish to unsubscribe from a newsletter you are receiving, or a mailing list you have joined, you can do so by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of the message.

How To Unsubscribe from Newsletters

If you are receiving a newsletter from us, you can unsubscribe by clicking on the link at the bottom of every single message we send out.

How To Contact Us

Should you have other questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@dreammanifesto.com.

You can also mail us at: Inspire3 Ltd, c/o Dream Manifesto, 2nd Floor, 145-157 St John Street, London, EC1V 4PY, United Kingdom. You can also phone our offices on UK (+44) (0) 7092 171819.
