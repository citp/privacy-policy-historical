# Drivesoul.com: The Home of Auto News

For[ Drivesoul.com](https://web.archive.org/index.php) and its owners, all the visitors to the Home of Automobile are valued guests and will be treated so. Drivesoul.com and its owners are committed to protecting interests and information of the visitors. Any information collected from the visitors will be kept secure and safe. Such information will never be shared to a third party without explicit consent from the visitors. 

Collection of Information  
Innovation is the forte of Drivesoul.com. To innovate constantly and invent new means for improvement, Drivesoul.com may obtain from information from the users. Such information will be used for extending better services to our valued visitors.Cookies  
Advertising banners and pop-up windows appearing on this Website are delivered to the Users by our advertising partners. These advertising companies may place or may not place a very small cookie file on the User computer in the course of delivering advertisements to this site, so that User does not have to view the same advertisements numerous times. Additionally, information about User visits to this Website and other websites (but not your name, address, or other personal information) is used to place ads on this site and other sites about goods and services that are of interest to the Users. This is now the norm when dealing with any website on the internet that has any form of advertising on it.
