Privacy Policy

Driscoll's Strawberry Associates takes the privacy of your personal information seriously. That is why we collect only the minimum personal or customer specific information necessary to support any value-added information or content. This is always on an "opt-in" sign-up basis.

Use of 'Cookies'

Cookies are text information files that your browser places on your computer when you visit a web site. To improve your visit to this site, we may use cookies to obtain non-personal information. We may aggregate this data to analyze the viewing habits of our site visitors to improve the experience and relevance, but it will not be associated with any personally identifiable data.

You need not have cookies turned on to visit this site. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but allow you to modify security settings so you can approve or reject cookies on a case-by-case basis.

Use of Email Addresses

Driscoll's periodically pushes out information relevant to berry lovers. To receive this content via email, you can "opt-in" by providing only your email address.

Sharing Your Information

Driscoll's Strawberry Associates will never share your personal information with any third party, unless compelled to do so by legal order.

Children's Privacy

This site is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We operate our sites in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Driscoll's Strawberry Associates will never ask for, collect, or use any personal information from anyone identified as being under the age of 13.
