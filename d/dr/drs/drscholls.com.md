# Dr. Scholls

  


Privacy Statement

Schering-Plough HealthCare Products, the manufacturer of DR. SCHOLL'SÂ® products respects the privacy of anyone who visits our web sites or responds to our interactive advertisements or promotions. This statement tells you what information Schering-Plough HealthCare Products will collect and how we will use that information. This statement also provides direction on what to do if you do not want your personal information collected or shared when you visit www.drscholls.com or respond to our on-line advertisements or promotions.

What information will Schering-Plough HealthCare Products collect?

Schering-Plough HealthCare Products will not collect any personally-identifiable information about you (your name, address, telephone number or email address) unless you voluntarily provide it to us.

In some cases, non-personally identifiable information may be collected automatically. Examples of this non-personally identifiable information include the type of internet browser you may be using, the type of computer operating system you may be using and the domain name of the web site from which you linked to our site, advertisement or promotion. 

How will Schering-Plough HealthCare Products use the personally identifiable information we collect?

We collect this information to better understand your needs and ways in which we can improve our products. We recognize that by providing us with personally-identifiable information you are demonstrating your trust in our company and our brands and we take that trust very seriously. We maintain this information in a secure database that we use to analyze consumer needs. To further understand key issues, we may contact you for additional information. At times, we may elect to share selected information with trusted business partners in support of joint marketing and promotion activities.

What do I do if I do not want my personally-identifiable information collected?

If you do not want personally-identifiable information collected, please do not submit it to us. If you have submitted the information and want it removed from our records, contact us at info@spcorp.com or 1-901-320-2998 and ask us to remove it. We will make a reasonable effort to remove your information from our files.

What about "cookies"?

When you view one of our websites, advertisements or promotions, we may store some information on your computer. These small pieces of information stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive do not contain any personally-identifiable information, but enable us to communicate more meaningfully with you. 

What about children?

Schering-Plough HealthCare Products does not intend this site for child audiences, and has no intention of collecting personally-identifiable information (such as name, address, telephone number) from children under 13 years old. Should a child under 13 attempt to provide us with personally-identifiable information, we will decline to collect that information, and set a session cookie [or use other means for tracking the child while on the site] to make sure the child does not attempt to change his/her age and provide us with his/her personally-identifiable information. If you discover that your child has provided us with personally-identifiable information, we ask that a parent or guardian of the child contact us at [info@spcorp.com](mailto:info@spcorp.com) or 1-901-320-2998 to have this information deleted from our records. We will take all feasible measures to delete the child's information from our existing files.

Your consent

By using our websites or clicking on our advertisements or promotions, you consent to the collection and use of non-personally identifiable information by Schering-Plough HealthCare Products. Any change to our privacy policy will be posted on this page so that you are always aware of the information collected and its intended use.
