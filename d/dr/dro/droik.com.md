# India Classifieds

Privacy 

1\. Droik.com will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to third parties.

Droik.com may disclose personally identifiable information about you to third parties when: We have your consent to share the information;  
We need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested and given your consent; We have to cooperate with the  
investigations of purported unlawful activities; When we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to protect and defend the rights and  
related property of Droik.com and that of its visitors; We respond to subpoenas, court orders in compliance with the legal process; 

2\. Protecting your privacy We offer email anonymization & relay, to reduce 3rd party harvesting & spam. Account information is password-protected.  
Keep your password safe.

3\. We are not responsible for, nor have any control over, the privacy policies of third party websites (if any links found on Droik.com in postings  
or other commercial or non-commercial content), and encourage all users to read the privacy policies of each and every website visited. 

4\. We sometimes collect your email address, for purposes such as sending self-publishing and confirmation emails, authenticating user accounts,  
providing subscription email services, registering for forums, etc.
