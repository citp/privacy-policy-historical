# Privacy Policy

**PRIVACY POLICY**

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices of [www.dragonpay.ph](http://www.dragonpay.ph/).  This privacy policy applies solely to information collected by this website.

**Information Collection, Use and Sharing**

​Dragonpay Corporation is the sole owner of the information collected on this site.  We only have access to collect information that you voluntarily give us via email or other direct contact from you.  We will not sell or rent this information to anyone.  We will use your information to respond to you, regarding the reason you contacted us.  We will not share your information with any third party outside our organization, other than as necessary to​ fulfill​ your request, e.g. to check payment validation.  Unless you ask not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell about promotions, new products or services or changes to this privacy policy.

**Security**

We take precautions to protect your information. When you submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and offline.  Whenever we collect sensitive information (such as bank information or payment  details) that information is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secure way. While we use encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted online, we also protect your information offline. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job ( ex. Customer support or billing) are granted access to personally identifiable information. The computers, servers in which we store personally identifiable information are kept in a secure environment.

**Links**

This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we (Dragonpay Corporation) are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this web site.

**Changes to Privacy Policy**

Dragonpay may amend this policy from time to time and updates will be posted on this page. If you feel that we are not abiding by this privacy policy, you should contact us immediately via email [admin@dragonpay.ph](mailto:admin@dragonpay.ph)

**Disclaimer**

Dragonpay ([www.dragonpay.ph](http://www.dragonpay.ph/)) reserves the right to amend its policies, and any content on its website without prior notice.
