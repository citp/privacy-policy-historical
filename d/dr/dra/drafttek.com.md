# Privacy Policy

**Draft Tek Sports Information LLC Privacy Policy**  
** **  
("Draft Tek Sports Information LLC" will herein be referred to as simply "Draft Tek".  Draft Tek Sports Information is a web site operated exclusively by Draft Tek Sports Information LLC)

  [Back to Home Page ](http://www.drafttek.com/)

**General Guidelines:**  
** **  
By accessing this website, you acknowledge your agreement and consent to the **Draft Tek** Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use the **Draft Tek**. In addition, shall you continue to use Draft Tek following changes to these terms signifies that you accept those changes.   
** **  
**Draft Tek** is strongly committed to protecting the privacy of its readership. Internet user privacy and confidentiality is of the utmost importance to us. There are instances where a user may provide specific personal information in response to a survey, purchase agreement, registration questionnaire or contest. This information is used by **Draft Tek** so the reader can receive the goods or services requested.  **Draft Tek** regards all personal information collected from its readers as completely confidential. This includes personal data provided during registration processes and financial information.   
** **  
**Draft Tek** will not sell any personal information gathered to third parties. **Draft Tek** will also not release any personal information gathered to third parties except under the following conditions: 

     -  **Draft Tek** reserves the right to disclose user information in special cases when we have reason to believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact, or bring legal action against someone who may be causing injury to or interference with (either intentionally or unintentionally) **Draft Tek** , other **Draft Tek** users, or anyone else that could be harmed by such activities, without prior notice.  
   -  **Draft Tek** reserves the right to disclose user information in special cases to Law Enforcement or Government agencies in such instances where the information is required for the express purpose of criminal investigation, without prior notice. **Draft Tek** may disclose user information when we believe in good faith that the law requires it, without prior notice.  
   -  **Draft Tek** reserves the right to use any general information gathered for its own statistical, marketing, promotional, and other such purposes without prior notice. This includes information collected in surveys. **Draft Tek** will provide only aggregated data from these analyses to third parties such as advertisers.

  **Draft Tek** will remove any personal information gathered from its database(s) upon written request from any registered users within a 30 day time period following the removal request. Removal from **Draft Tek** database(s) will no longer afford the registered user the ability to participate in activities conducted on the site at **Draft Tek's** discretion, and without prior notice.

  **Links:**  
** **  
This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that **Draft Tek** is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage you to read the privacy practices of each and every web site that collects your personally identifiable information. 

  **Draft Tek** may make interactive forums available to users at some point in the future, although no such forums currently exist or are currently planned for development. Users are advised that whenever posting personal information online, through bulletin boards or e-mail that this information can be collected and used by others. We will take all reasonable precautions to protect your privacy, but always keep in mind that when you are in a public forum, you control the information you disclose. Always use caution when giving out any personally identifying information about yourself or your family. **Draft Tek** will not control or endorse the content, messages or information found in interactive sites on this site. 

  It is always assumed that users who e-mail the [editor](mailto:warren@drafttek.com) of **Draft Tek** and who do not clearly label their e-mail as a PRIVATE MESSAGE, may have their e-mail posted as a letter to the editor. **Draft Tek** specifically disclaims any liability with regard to e-mail not clearly labeled as unsuitable for a Letters to the Editor column by the user or e-mail intended for Letters to the Editor and any actions resulting from your participation in Letters to the Editor. 

  In addition, users should be aware that when they voluntarily disclose personally identifiable information (i.e., user name, e-mail address) in Letters to the Editor that information, along with any substantive information disclosed in the user's communication, can be collected, correlated, and used by third parties and may result in unsolicited messages from other posters or third parties. Such activities are beyond the control of **Draft Tek**. 

  **Cookies:**  
** **  
A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site. Once the user closes their browser, the cookie simply terminates. For instance, by setting a cookie on our site, the user would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our site. If a user rejects the cookie, they may still use our site. The only drawback to this is that the user will be limited in some areas of our site. Cookies can also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our site.   
Users should be aware that **Draft Tek** cannot and does not control the use of cookies or the resulting information by advertisers or third parties hosting data for **Draft Tek**. 

  **Log Files:**  
** **  
We use IP (Internet Protocol) addresses to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.
