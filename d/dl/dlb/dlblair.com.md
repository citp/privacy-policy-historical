# Privacy Policy - D.L. Blair, Inc.

We at D.L. Blair, Inc. respect your privacy. We do not share personal information with anyone.

We collect IP address to help improve the site, but we do not link IP addresses to individually identify anyone.

Information collected on this site through the site will not be shared with a third party. This information is strictly used to understand your needs and help serve you better.
