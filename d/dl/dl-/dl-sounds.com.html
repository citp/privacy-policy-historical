Privacy Notice

DL-Sounds values your privacy. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our visitors at all times. Once you choose to become a member, you can be assured that the collected personal information about you is used to process your orders and that it will only be used to support your customer relationship with DL-Sounds.
DL-Sounds does not share your personal information with any outside parties.

Security

DL-Sounds uses industry-standard encryption technologies when transferring and receiving customer information. We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from users of our Web site.

Cookies

DL-Sounds uses cookies (information that is stored by your browser on the hard drive of your computer). Cookies allow us to recognize you each time you visit DL-Sounds. Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information. We use cookies to store information, such as your personal preferences when you visit our website.
If you delete our cookie from your browser, you will no longer be logged into our website.

IP address

The IP addresses (a unique number identifying all devices connected to the Internet) of our users are logged and used to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website. Your IP address is not linked to your personally identifiable information.

Credit Card Information

At no time is DL-Sounds ever in possession of your complete credit card information. We also cannot gain access to your complete credit card information.

Links

DL-Sounds.com contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personally identifiable information.
Changes to this privacy notice

We reserve the right to modify this privacy notice in the future. Please check our privacy notice periodically.
