##  Collection, processing and passing of personal data

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Website and App for you.

####  Data Collected by Softland

While using our Website, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Below is a detailed list of the information we collect and how we use it.

**Contacting us**

When you contact us using the contact form via the Website or emailing us directly, any personally identifiable information you send to us is only used to identify you as a client and help with your support issue. We use Google Apps to manage email correspondence. We also archive all the emails. We do not share your email correspondence details with other third party providers. We do not use your email address collected via the online form to send you promotional emails.

When you contact us via the telephone, any personally identifiable information you send to us is only used to identify you as a client and help with your support issue. No information is recorded or archived. We do not share your phone number with other third party providers. We do not use your phone number to contact you with promotions.

**Using the Forum**

We have a user forum powered by phpBB (an open source forum software). When you register to become a member of the forum or login and start posting we collect the information you submit to us to allow you access to the forum: a uniquely identifiable user name, your personal password and email address. Any other information is optional and you can decide what information will be displayed publicly. We do not use your email address collected via the forum to send you promotional emails.

**Newsletter**

When you subscribe to our mailing list you provide us a valid email address. This is stored on our hosting company's servers. We use your email address to notify you occasionally of newly released versions or other updates. You can unsubscribe at any time from our mailing database to stop receiving these notifications.

**App usage statistics**

In order to help development and improve our App's features, the installer has a setting for collection of usage data. This function collects anonymous App usage data and sends it to Google Analytics. The data is used in development to get reliable statistics on which features are used (or not used). This data is anonymous and does not personally identify you. You can opt out of sending anonymous usage statistics during the setup or later from the App options.

During the installation process, anonymous statistics are sent and stored on Softland's servers. These stats help us discover setup errors and improve the installation user experience. This data is anonymous and does not personally identify you.

For bug tracking purposes, you can send us crash/exception logs that help us improve the app. These logs have an optional setting to include your email address, which we only use to get back to you when we fix the bug.

**Tracking & Cookies**

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Website and App and hold certain information. Cookies are files with small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device. Tracking technologies also used are beacons, tags, and scripts to collect and track information and to improve and analyze our Website and App. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Website.
