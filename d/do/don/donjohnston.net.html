Don Johnston Incorporated Privacy Statement

Don Johnston Incorporated understands how important it is to keep information about our customers confidential and we thank you for placing your trust in us. Information that we gather on our customers is only used internally so that we are able to provide products and services that meet your needs and the needs of your students. We do not sell or offer customer information to third parties nor do we have any plans to do so in the future. Don Johnston Incorporated is not responsible for the privacy of your information in relation to sites which link to our site or are listed on our site. 

Collection of your Personal Information

The types of personally identifiable information that may be collected at these pages include name, address, e-mail address, telephone number, fax number, and information about your interests in and use of various products. We will only collect data needed to perform our services. Information collected is being held on a server located in the United States.

Use of your Personal Information

We may use the information you provide about yourself to fulfill your requests for our products and services, to respond to your inquiries about our offerings, and to offer you other products, programs or services that we believe may be of interest to you. We may use this information to communicate with you, such as to fulfill a request by you for an online newsletter or to contact you about your account with us.

We occasionally hire other companies to provide limited services on our behalf, such as handling the processing and delivery of mailings. We will only provide those companies the personal information they need to deliver the service. They are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information and are prohibited from using that information for any other purpose.

 We log IP addresses in order to analyze trends, track statistics within our site and gather broad demographic information. IP addresses are not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Security of your Personal Information

We have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and help prevent unauthorized access, maintain data security, and correctly use the information we collect online. All licensing and contact information is currently held in Illinois, United States.

How You can Access or Correct Information

To access the personally identifiable information that this Don Johnston Incorporated site has collected about you online, you may email your request to info@donjohnston.com

How to Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about the Don Johnston online policy for this site or its implementation you may email us at info@donjohnston.com

Changes to this Statement

Don Johnston Incorporated will occasionally update this Privacy Statement to reflect company and customer feedback. When Don Johnston Incorporated posts changes to this Statement, you will see the last updated date on the top page of this Privacy Statement. If there are material changes to this Statement or in how Don Johnston Incorporated will use your personal information, Don Johnston Incorporated will prominently post such changes prior to implementing the change.
