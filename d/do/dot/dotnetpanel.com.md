# Privacy Policy : Control Panel for Windows Hosting, IIS 7, VPS, Exchange, SharePoint, Dynamics CRM and Dedicated servers

The web-site dotnetpanel.com is administered by SMB SAAS Systems Inc. Our goal is to maintain privacy for our web-site visitors and registered users so that you can feel secure when browsing our web pages of submitting your personal information on our web-site.

The information gathered on dotnetpanel.com is used to analyze what is most effective about our web-site which helps us identify the ways how it can be improved. In cases we collect data for other purposes, the user will get description of the purpose at the point the information is collected.

We gather the information on dotnetpanel.com using several sources, including Internet access logs and cookies. When you access dotnetpanel.com, your Internet address is automatically collected and is placed in our Internet access logs. Cookies are small files of information which save and retrieve information about your visit to our web-site. Use of cookies helps us to know how you entered the site and which information was of interest to you. If you are uncomfortable regarding cookies use, please keep in mind that you can disable cookies on your computer by changing the settings in preferences or options menu in your browser.

When you register on dotnetpanel.com we collect the information you voluntarily submit to us. All information received from the customer is used exclusively for the purposes of supporting and administrating their account/license(s).

You will not get any unsolicited e-mails and will be contacted only with your consent. Every communication will contain details how to unsubscribe in the future.

The information collected on dotnetpanel.com will not be sold, rented or exchanged with any third party, except for sharing your e-mail address with SmarterTools Inc. when you order SmarterTools products from our web-site.
