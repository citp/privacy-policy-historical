# Legal Information

## Privacy Notice

**"Privacy Notice"**

Dotmed.com, Inc. ("DOTmed") values and respects your privacy. This Privacy Notice explains DOTmed's online/website information practices and the choices you can make with respect to how your personally identifiable information as defined under applicable law and your other personal information (“Personal Information”) is collected and utilized. 

_How Personal Information is Collected_

DOTmed may obtain Personal Information about you from the following sources: 

Personal Information you may submit to this site (please do not submit any Personal Information to us if you are less than 21 years of age). 

Personal Information DOTmed may receive from third parties. 

Personal Information DOTmed or other parties which maintain a presence or interact with this site may collect through a standard technology such as "cookies" when you interact with this site. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie and give you a chance to accept or reject it. Personal Information DOTmed may customize based upon your visit to this site or your interaction with this site to improve your experience on this site. 

_How Personal Information is Used_

DOTmed may share your Personal Information with its associates, affiliates and third parties with whom it has a business relationship, for purposes of products/services provision, marketing or advertising. DOTmed may share certain of your Personal Information with other users of the site as part of your use of the site However, DOTmed shall not share your user name, password with any party, and shall not share your credit card information with any party other than the card carrier or its authorized service processing agents. 

DOTmed makes every commercially reasonable effort to maintain appropriate electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure your user name, password and credit card information, prevent it from unauthorized access, and ensure its correct use and transmission. 

We may disclose any Personal Information about you as permitted or mandated by law, or as necessary based on our good faith belief to conform or comply with the law or to protect DOTmed, this site, its users, or the public. 

_Your Right to Opt-Out or Unsubscribe_

You may opt-out of all or some of DOTmed's services by clicking on Unsubscribe at the bottom of the DOTmed homepage and unsubscribing as prompted on the site. However, you must contact DOTmed in person in reference to deleting any of your Personal Information from our site. DOTmed in any event may retain and use such Personal Information as permitted or mandated by applicable law to be retained or used by DOTmed. 

_Disclaimer_

While DOTmed strives to protect your privacy, please be aware that we are unable to guarantee complete security. In addition, the following circumstances in connection with your Personal Information are not subject to this Privacy Notice: 

Clicking on any linked site/advertising banners appearing on DOTmed's site will take you to third party web sites, which are subject to other privacy practices that may be different from this Privacy Notice. 

Any Personal Information, disclosed in any chat rooms, forums, message boards, social networking sites, and/or news groups that are available to visitors may become part of the public domain and is not subject to privacy protection. 

Your account may be accessed and used by unauthorized third parties due to 

disclosure of your user name and/or password by you. 

Web beacons or bots may be used by other users or third parties through this site, without DOTmed’s knowledge, to monitor and collect data about your activities on the internet, which data may or may not include Personal Information and DOTmed has no obligation or liability to preclude or limit such from this site. 

A message once posted on this site, will be stored and cached permanently and may be accessed by third parties even if DOTmed were to remove such. 

DOTmed may revise this Privacy Notice from time to time. Any material amendments hereof will be posted on DOTmed's site for your awareness. 

Non-Personal Information, such as business or commercial information, is not subject to any protection by DOTmed except, where DOTmed has executed and delivered a separate confidentiality agreement. 

DOTmed and third-party vendors, including Google, use first-party cookies (such as the Google Analytics cookie) and third-party cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) together to inform, optimize, and serve ads based on someone’s past visits to your website. 

To opt out of cookie tracking on this website (and others using Google Analytics), please visit: <https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/>

Should you have any questions, concerns or requests about our privacy policy, please contact us via e-mail at [info@dotmed.com](mailto:info@dotmed.com) with the term "Privacy Policy" in the subject line.
