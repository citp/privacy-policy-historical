# Doclix: Privacy Policy

[](https://web.archive.org/web/20080913082121id_/http%3A//www.doclix.com/index.html)

Doclix respects the privacy of online users. This policy explains the accepted privacy practices for the services available at www.doclix.com (" **Doclix.com** ") and the use of the Doclix _ContextGain_ Program Service (" ** _ContextGain_** ", collectively " **the Services** "). 

ContexGain provides you with access to third party websites and third party content, including Google AdSense generated advertisements. Doclix does not assume any responsibility or control over any of these websites and content, and this policy does not apply to such websites and content. If you wish to learn how a third party handles your personal information, then you should carefully review its privacy policy. 

## 1\. Personal Information that Doclix collects

When you register to _ContextGain_ , you will be asked to provide personal details such as your full name, e-mail address, the name of your employer, your title, phone number, country of residence, your website's URL and your Google AdSense Client ID (" **Client ID** "). The fields for compulsory completion will be specifically designated. 

**_ContextGain_ is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18. If you are not 18 years of age or older, then you may not register with _ContextGain_ and must not provide Doclix with any of your personal details.**

When you use _ContextGain_ , Doclix may collect further information, such as session durations, page impressions, advertisements viewed or clicked on, the Internet protocol (IP) address and the domain name that served you to access Doclix _ContextGain_ , and the geographic location of the computer system that you are using to log-in to, or you use when using _ContextGain_. Doclix may also track further information pertaining to you, when you activate and access _ContextGain_ Panels, such as the frequency and the extent of your use of _ContextGain_.

Doclix may automatically collect information through the use of log files and other means. If you register with _ContextGain_ , then the information gathered will be partially personally identifiable. If you are not a registered user or you are an end user, than we will not knowingly make use of any information which personally identifies you, except as specifically indicated in the policy, or on doclix.com. 

Further information may be collected when you exchange communications with Doclix, for example, when you submit an inquiry to Doclix. 

## 2\. What does Doclix do with your personally identifiable information?

Doclix may use personally identifiable information for the following purposes:

  * to provide _ContextGain_ ;
  * to improve and customize your experience with _ContextGain_ ; 
  * to provide you with support and handle inquires;
  * to send you updates and notices, to provide you with information pertaining to _ContextGain_ and to provide you with further marketing and advertising material. At any time you may opt out of the mailing lists, by changing your preferences in your personal account. In any case, Doclix will not share your personal identifiable details with any advertisers, without your explicit consent; 
  * to facilitate communications with you, and between you and others;
  * to enforce Doclix's Terms of Use; 
  * to contact you as and when Doclix believes it to be necessary;
  * to comply with any applicable law and assist law enforcement agencies as required;
  * to collect fees and debts, and to prevent fraud, misappropriation, infringements, identity thefts and any other misuse of _ContextGain_ ; 
  * to take any action in any case of dispute, or legal proceeding of any kind between you and Doclix, or between you and other users or third parties with respect to, or in relation with _ContextGain_ ; 



## 3\. How does Doclix use aggregated information?

Doclix may use anonymous, statistical or aggregated information about _ContextGain_ use, including any aggregated information related to your personal account, and share, publish, post, disseminate, transmit or otherwise communicate or make available such information, to suppliers, business partners, sponsors, affiliates and any other third party, at Doclix's sole discretion, provided however, that such information will not identify you personally and that Doclix will not knowingly, or intentionally use such information to reveal your identity without your consent, under the terms of this policy. 

Doclix may also use anonymous, statistical or aggregated information to properly operate _ContextGain_ , to improve the quality of the services, to enhance your experience, to create new services, including customized services, to change or cancel existing content or services and for other internal and statistical purposes. 

## 4\. How does Doclix Share information with others?

Doclix does not sell, rent or lease your personally identifiable information to third parties for any of their marketing purposes. Doclix may share personally identifiable information with others in any of the following instances, or subject to your explicit consent: 

  * When you use _ContextGain_. In this case, Doclix will share your information only to the extent required to facilitate your use of _ContextGain_ ;
  * If Doclix believes that you have breached the Terms of Use, or abused your rights to use _ContextGain_ , or performed any act or omission that Doclix believes to be violating any applicable law, rules, or regulations. Doclix may share your information in these cases, with law enforcement and other competent authorities and with any third party, as may be required to handle any result of your wrongdoing;
  * If Doclix is required, or believes that it is required by law to share or disclose your information; 
  * In any case of dispute, or legal proceeding of any kind between you and Doclix, or between you and other users or third parties with respect to, or in relation with _ContextGain_ ; 
  * In any case where Doclix believes that sharing information is necessary to prevent imminent physical harm or damage to property; 
  * If Doclix organizes the operation of _ContextGain_ within a different framework, or through another legal structure or entity, or if Doclix is acquired by, or merged with another entity, provided however, that those entities agree to be bound by the provisions of this policy, with respective changes taken into consideration;
  * Doclix may also share personally identifiable information with companies or organizations connected, or affiliated with it, such as subsidiaries, sister-companies and parent companies.



## 5\. How can you access your personal information? 

If you are a subscriber with _ContextGain_ , then you may access your personal account and edit, or amend your personal information at any time. If you find that the information on your personal account is not accurate, complete or updated, than you must make all necessary changes to correct it. Please keep in mind that false, incorrect, or out dated information may prevent you from registering with _ContextGain_ , impair Doclix's ability to provide you with _ContextGain_ and the ability to contact you.

## 6\. Data retention

Doclix reserves the right at all times to review, retain and disclose any information as it deems necessary to satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process or governmental request, as well as Doclix's internal needs. However, by doing so Doclix does not either expressly or impliedly endorse, affirm or in any manner assume any responsibility for such information. 

## 7\. Information security

Doclix implements information security systems and procedures to secure the information stored on its servers. Such systems and procedures reduce the risk of security breaches, but they do not provide absolute security. Therefore, Doclix cannot guarantee that _ContextGain_ is immune to unauthorized access to the information stored therein and to other information security risks.

## 8\. Cookies 

Doclix may use cookies when operating _ContextGain_. Cookies are packets of information sent by Doclix's servers to your web browser and then sent back by the browser. Doclix may use cookies for various purposes, such as to save you the need to re-enter your password each time you log-in to _ContextGain_ , to facilitate the use of _ContextGain_ , to collect information for statistical and analytical purposes, to verify information, to customize _ContextGain_ to your personal preferences and for information security purposes. 

Some of the cookies may expire when the session ends and you exit your browser. Other cookies may be stored on your computer's hard drive. If you wish to block the cookies, then please use the help button in your browser and follow the necessary instructions. However, bear in mind that disabling cookies may complicate or even prevent your use of _ContextGain_ , or certain features thereof. 

## 9\. Advertising on _ContextGain_

_ContextGain_ Panels display advertisements, alongside the related content snippets and links. To use _ContextGain_ you must maintain a valid account with Google AdSense. In the course of setting up your personal account, you will be asked to provide Doclix with your Google AdSense Client ID, to facilitate the display of Google AdSense generated advertisements on the _ContextGain_ Panels. Doclix shall only use your AdSense Client ID under the terms of this policy and in accordance with the Terms of Use. 

Advertisers may use cookies on your computer and receive information on your advertisements use habits, including the number of page-views and click-through rates. Such information is gathered to customize advertisements to your preferences, to improve the quality of the advertisers' services and for other business related purposes. For example, Google can use this information to determine how many times an advertisement is clicked on, in order to calculate the website's revenues.

**Google's use of cookies is subject to its own privacy practices and not to this policy.** If you wish to review the Google AdSense privacy policy, then you should visit their website at: [www.google.com/adsense](http://www.google.com/adsense) or contact them directly. 

## 10\. Changes to this Privacy Policy

Doclix may change and update the terms of this policy from time to time. Therefore, it is recommended that you read it periodically. All substantial changes made to this policy will be notified on doclix.com, or in your personal account, at Doclix's discretion, and take effect 30 days following their initial posting.
