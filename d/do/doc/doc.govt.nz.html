  • Privacy and security

The Department of Conservation is committed to protecting the privacy and security of visitors to the Department of Conservation website.

You may browse and access information contained within this website without providing any personal information.

Where you voluntarily provide personal information (e.g. through email feedback), we will only use that information to communicate with you. We will keep any such information secure and will not disclose it to any third parties.

Online booking

Our online booking functionality requires us to use an in-memory cookie, which is set to last no longer than 30 minutes.  It doesn't collect personal information about the user (it is used to hold basic authentication-related information) and the content is encrypted.

Further information

If you have any further queries please do not hesitate to contact our Departmental Privacy Officer.

 
 

Find out more

 

DOC's online booking system allows you to check the availability of, and book, huts and other facilities on the Milford, Kepler, Routeburn, Heaphy and Abel Tasman Coast Tracks.
