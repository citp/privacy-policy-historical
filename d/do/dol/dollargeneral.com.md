# Privacy Policy

  |  **Dollar General Corporation

PRIVACY POLICY

Last Modified: May 31, 2007

**

We respect your privacy and value the trust you place in us. We want to ensure that you understand the type of information that we gather about you when you visit [www.dollargeneral.com](http://www.dollargeneral.com/) and how we use it. This Privacy Policy explains how we use, collect and protect this information and the choices you have regarding our use of that information. 

By using our Website (as defined below), you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy, you may not use our Website.

1\. **What does this Privacy Policy cover?**

This Privacy Policy applies to your use of the Dollar General website, the homepage of which is at www.dollargeneral.com (our “Website”). Dollar General owns the information we collect on our Website. This Privacy Policy applies only to the information about you collected by us on our Website. It does not apply to information, including Personally Identifiable Information (as defined below), you reveal or post in any public forum on our Website, your use of websites linked to our Website, or any other websites.

Our Website does contain links to other websites. Please be aware that we do not control and are not responsible for the content and privacy practices of such websites.

2\. **What personal information is collected from visitors to our Website?**

“Personally Identifiable Information” is information we collect on our Website that can be used to identify or contact you, including, but not limited to, your name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address. We only collect Personally Identifiable Information you voluntarily choose to provide to us. For example, we will ask you to provide Personally Identifiable Information when you contact our Customer Service Department so that we may respond to your question or comment when appropriate. We may also run certain promotions or sweepstakes that require your Personally Identifiable Information so that we may contact you. From time to time, we may supplement Personally Identifiable Information collected on our Website with information collected by third parties. We may also request information from you about your shopping and product preferences, though you are not required to give that information. 

You do not have to provide us with any Personally Identifiable Information to visit our Website. In fact, you may browse most of the pages on our Website without giving us any Personally Identifiable Information. If you choose to withhold requested information, however, you may not be able to use some features of our Website.

We use the Personally Identifiable Information we collect on our Website for the explicit purpose for which it is requested. If you identify yourself by sending us an e-mail with questions or comments, we may use your Personally Identifiable Information to respond to your questions or comments. We also may use your Personally Identifiable Information to communicate with you about your use of our Website.

3\. **Does Dollar General collect Personally Identifiable Information on children?**

Dollar General is committed to protecting the privacy of children. Our Website is not designed for or directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information about children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under the age of 13 has provided personal information to us, we will delete the information from our records.

4\. **How do you use my Personally Identifiable Information?**

We will use your Personally Identifiable Information only for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, on our Website or as otherwise disclosed at the time we request such information from you. We will not use your Personally Identifiable Information for any other purposes without your permission.

We use the Personally Identifiable Information we collect on our Website for the explicit purpose for which it is requested. If you identify yourself by sending us an e-mail with questions or comments, we may use your Personally Identifiable Information to respond to your questions or comments. We also may use your Personally Identifiable Information to communicate with you about your use of our Website.

We may also use your Personally Identifiable Information to advise you of promotions, sales or other special opportunities.

5\. **Does Dollar General disclose my Personally Identifiable Information to third parties?**

Dollar General uses your Personally Identifiable Information only to serve you better. Except as set forth in this Privacy Policy, on our Website or as otherwise disclosed at the time we request your Personally Identifiable Information, we will not share any of your Personally Identifiable Information with any third party without your permission. We also will not rent or sell your Personally Identifiable Information to any third party.

We limit access to the Personally Identifiable Information that we collect to those within our organization with a business need to know. We require all of our employees to maintain the confidentiality of confidential information entrusted to them unless disclosure is duly authorized or is required by law.

We may disclose your Personally Identifiable Information to our affiliates, selected product vendors, and other trusted parties with whom we have a business relationship in order to address a product concern you may have or to notify you of special offers and promotions relating to such companies’ products that are available through Dollar General. We also may provide your Personally Identifiable Information to companies that provide services directly to us, such as mailing and printing services, provided that such parties will be obligated to maintain the privacy of such information and not to disclose such information to other entities.

We reserve the right to transfer your Personally Identifiable Information to a third party in the event of a sale, merger or other transfer of all or substantially all of the assets of Dollar General, provided that the third party agrees to protect the confidentiality of your Personally Identifiable Information consistent with this Privacy Policy.

Otherwise, we will not disclose any of your Personally Identifiable Information unless we have your express permission, we are required by legal subpoena to do so, we believe in good faith that the disclosure is otherwise required by law, or to enforce our Terms of Use.

6\. **Does Dollar General use “cookies”?**

Our Website does not use “cookies,” which are small pieces of information that are stored by the browser on your computer’s hard drive that allow you to be recognized when you return to a website.

Our Website may collect IP addresses from you which will be used only to compile statistical data relating to the use of our Website. We use such non-personally identifiable information to help us understand who uses our Website and to improve and market our Website 

7\. **What security does Dollar General use for information collected from me?**

We have taken numerous steps to secure your Personally Identifiable Information. Our servers are located in controlled, secured environments, protected from unauthorized access, and our systems contain technical security measures, such as firewalls, as well. Although we exercise great care to protect your Personally Identifiable Information, you should be aware that no technology is perfect and that there is always some risk involved in transferring data over the Internet, particularly if you send information to us through an unsecured means, such as e-mail.

8\. **Can I review the information Dollar General has collected?**

If you would like to review or correct any Personally Identifiable Information we have retained about you in our active databases, please send an e-mail request to privacypolicy@dollargeneral.com. All such e-mail requests must be written in the English language. If you wish to update Personally Identifiable Information you have provided in connection with a promotion or sweepstakes, please consult the Official Rules of that promotion or sweepstakes.

If you receive an e-mail from Dollar General relating to any offer or promotion and wish to be removed from future e-mail contacts, you will have the option to do so by selecting a link located within that email. If you select not to receive future e-mail solicitations, the only future e-mail communication you will receive from us will be in response to questions from you or to resolve any customer service issues you may have reported. 

9\. **Can Dollar General change this Privacy Policy?**

We first posted this policy on our website on January 30, 2003, and updated this policy on May 31, 2007. We reserve the right to change, modify or amend this policy at any time, so we encourage you to review it periodically. We will post any changes to this Privacy Policy here so that you will always be aware of our privacy practices. Please refer back to this Privacy Policy regularly. Your continued use of our Website following our posting of any such changes will mean that you accept such changes.
