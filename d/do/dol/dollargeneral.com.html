PRIVACY POLICY

We respect your privacy and value the trust you place in us. We want to ensure that you understand the type of information that we gather about you and how we use it. This Privacy Policy explains how we use, collect and protect your personal information and the choices you have regarding our use of that information.

By using our Website (as defined below), you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use our Website.

1. What is the scope of this Privacy Policy?

This Privacy Policy applies to your use of the Dollar General website at www.dollargeneral.com (our “Website”). Dollar General owns the information we collect on our Website. This Privacy Policy applies only to the information collected by us on our Website. It does not apply to information, including Personally Identifiable information (as defined below), you reveal or post in any public forum on our Website, your use of websites linked to our Website, or any other websites.

2. What personal information is collected from visitors to our Website?

“Personally Identifiable Information” is information we collect on our Website that can be used to identify or contact you, including, but not limited to, your name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address. We only collect Personally Identifiable Information you voluntarily choose to provide to us. For example, we will ask you to provide Personally Identifiable Information when you contact our Customer Service so that we may respond to your question or comment when appropriate. From time to time, we may supplement Personally Identifiable Information collected on our Website with information collected by third parties.

You do not have to provide us with any Personally Identifiable Information to visit our Website. If you choose to withhold requested information, however, you may not be able to use some features of our Website.

We will use your Personally Identifiable Information only for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as otherwise disclosed at the time we request such information from you. We will not use your Personally Identifiable Information for any other purposes without your permission.

We use the Personally Identifiable Information we collect on our Website for the explicit purpose for which it is requested. If you identify yourself by sending us an e-mail with questions or comments, we may use your Personally Identifiable Information to respond to your questions or comments. We also may use your Personally Identifiable Information to communicate with you about your use of our Website.

3. Does Dollar General use “cookies”?

Our Website does not use “cookies,” which are small pieces of information that are stored by the browser on your computer’s hard drive that allow you to be recognized when you return to a website.

Our Website may collect IP addresses from you which will be used only to compile statistical data relating to the use of our Website. We use such non-personally identifiable information to help us understand who uses our Website and to improve and market our Website

4. Does Dollar General collect personal information on children?

Dollar General is committed to protecting the privacy of children. Our Website is not designed for or directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information about children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under the age of 13 has provided personal information to us, we will delete the information from our records immediately.

5. Does Dollar General disclose my personal information?

Dollar General uses your Personally Identifiable Information only to serve you better. We do not use that information to contact you for advertising or promotional purposes. We also do not rent or sell any personal information collected through our Website to any third parties.

We will not otherwise disclose any of your Personally Identifiable Information unless we have your express permission, we are required by legal subpoena to do so, we believe in good faith that the disclosure is otherwise required by law, or to enforce our Terms of Use.

In addition, we limit access to the Personally Identifiable Information that we collect to those within our organization with a business need to know. We require all of our employees to maintain the confidentiality of confidential information entrusted to them unless disclosure is duly authorized or is required by law.

We may disclose your Personally Identifiable Information to our affiliates, provided that such affiliates will be obligated to maintain the privacy of such information and not to disclose such information to other entities.

Our Website does contain links to other websites. We attempt to make it obvious to you when you leave our Website by opening a new browser. Please be aware that we do not control and are not responsible for the content and privacy practices of such websites.

We reserve the right to transfer your Personally Identifiable Information to a third party in the event of a sale, merger or other transfer of all or substantially all of the assets of Dollar General, provided that the third party agrees to protect the confidentiality of your Personally Identifiable Information consistent with this Privacy Policy.

Except as set forth in this Privacy Policy or as otherwise disclosed at the time we request your Personally Identifiable Information, we will not share, rent, sell or trade any of your Personally Identifiable Information with any third party without your permission.

6. What security does Dollar General use for information collected from me?

We secure your Personally Identifiable Information on servers located in controlled, secured environments, protected from unauthorized access. Although we exercise great care to protect your Personally Identifiable Information, you should be aware that no technology is perfect and that there is always some risk involved in transferring data over the Internet, particularly if you send information to us through an unsecured means, such as e-mail.

7. Can I review the information Dollar General has collected?

If you would like to review or correct any Personally Identifiable Information we have retained about you in our active databases, please send an e-mail request to privacypolicy@dollargeneral.com. All such e-mail requests must be written in the English language.

8. Can Dollar General change this Privacy Policy?

We posted this policy on our website on January 30, 2003. We reserve the right to change, modify or amend this policy at any time, so we encourage you to review it periodically. We will post any changes to this Privacy Policy here so that you will always be aware of our privacy practices. Please refer back to this Privacy Policy regularly. Your continued use of our Website following our posting of any such changes will mean that you accept such changes.
