# DoitBest.com: Privacy Policy

Because doitbest.com, "World's Largest Hardware Store", understands that our customers are concerned about privacy online, we have developed policies that will ensure personal information is handled in a safe, professional and confidential manner. Through the online posting of this policy, you will know what kind of information we collect, how it is handled and with whom it could be shared.

This policy might change from time to time as doitbest.com improves the services we can provide to our online shoppers. We urge you to review this policy regularly so that you understand our current privacy policy.

To improve your shopping experience, doitbest.com currently gathers marketing information from our customers online. The customer information retrieved through the sales transaction process will only be shared with corporate affiliates and the Do it Best Corp. member with whom you place your orders. Information will otherwise be shared only with other third parties that will assist doitbest.com in improving your shopping experience. We will not sell names or addresses to anyone.

"Cookie" technology is available on this web site. Cookies may be used to recognize you as a user and tailor our web site to your interests based on your previous online shopping preferences within the doitbest.com site. The cookie is sent by our web site to your browser and then it may be stored on your computer or computer system. For example, if you visit the plumbing section of doitbest.com, the web site would "remember" the visit and let you know of specials, new products, or related plumbing projects upon a future web site visit. You also have the ability to set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie. This enables to choose to accept or reject the cookie.

Please let us know if you have any questions about your online shopping with doitbest.com. You may e-mail us at [helpline@doitbest.com](mailto:helpline@doitbest.com).
