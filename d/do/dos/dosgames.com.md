dosgames.com Privacy Policy

  
**User Information** Your privacy is important to us. That is why no personal information is collected about you by dosgames.com. Our hit tracking service (currently Hitbox, run by WebSideStory) tracks general information about all visitors to the site, such as type webbrowser, operating system, screen resolution, etc. No information about specific visitors is ever tracked or stored. This information is used to ensure that the site is compatible with the majority of visitors. You may view WebSideStory's privacy policy [here](http://www.websidestory.com/privacy/). **Cookies** This site, DOS Games, uses the services of ad services, which may include Burstmedia ([www.burstmedia.com](http://www.burstmedia.com/)), ValueAd ([www.valuead.com](http://www.valuead.com/)) and/or others for the serving and/or targeting of ads, promotions and other marketing messages. To do this, the advertiser(s) may collect anonymous data through the use of cookies. To learn more about Burstmedia, go to <http://www.burstmedia.com/release/about.asp>. To read ValueAd's privacy policy, go to <http://www.valuead.com/privacy.html>. **DOS Games Email service** The dosgames.com free email service is hosted by a third party, bigmailbox.com. Information collected during signup for the dosgames.com email service is handled by this third party and is subject to their privacy policy, located [here](http://mail.bigmailbox.com/users/dosgamescom/signup.cgi?sw=1&tt=full). **Disclosure** dosgames.com openly volunteers its practices of collecting information, and reserves the right to change this privacy policy at any time. Any questions regarding our privacy policy can be directed to [webmaster@dosgames.com](mailto:webmaster@dosgames.com).
