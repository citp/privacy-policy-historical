Privacy Policy


This privacy document was last revised on December 19, 2013. At Team Android, your privacy is our number one concern. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by DevFiles.co (The Site) and how it is used.

Personal Information

DevFiles.co does not require any information which can be traced back to, and identify a unique user. Certain features of the site however (like comments for example) , do require a valid email address to verify legitimate users. We guarantee that the information provided to The Site would never be used for any other purpose.

Log Files

Like many other web sites, Team Android makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. All of the data recorded is not linked to any information that could personally identify a unique individual.

Cookies and Web Beacons:-

This site itself, does not use any cookies to store information. Third party service providers however may store information on your computer. Refer to the following sections to understand what information is collected and how to opt out of the process.

- DISQUS Comments

Team Android uses Disqus Comments system to collect and manage comments on the blog. Disqus, as a third party service provider uses cookies to maintain your Disqus profile. You may easily opt out of the process by not posting a comment on the site.

- DoubleClick DART Cookie

  • Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on DevFiles.co.
  • Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to DevFiles.co and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Advertising Partners:-

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include:

1. Google AdSense

DevFiles.co has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers. You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. DevFiles.co’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites. If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.

Note: DevFiles.co also uses Kontera, TribalFusion as other alternative ad networks. In the past, The Site has used Technorati Media, BuySellAds and several other publisher ad networks.

External Sites

Team Android may often link to content on external websites. The Site has no control over the nature of content on these external websites or their policies regarding information and privacy. For further information, please review the individual policies of these websites.

Policy Changes

Team Android reserves the right to make any (major or minor) change to this privacy document without any prior warning. The site may or may not notify the users regarding any change made to the policy, however we will always reflect the latest policies and their effective dates in this document.
