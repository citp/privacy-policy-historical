# John Deere - deerecom

**DEERE & COMPANY PRIVACY POLICY**

Our site uses order forms and feedback forms or surveys for visitors to express their interests or request information, products, and services. Personal identity information from order forms is used to send orders, information about our company, and promotions to our visitors. This information may be transmitted to our dealers or other business partners. We will not give or sell personal identity information to any other outside company for its use in marketing or solicitation. We will maintain the confidentiality of your personal identity information. It will be used only to support your customer relationship with Deere, its subsidiaries and business partners.
