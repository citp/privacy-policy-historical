# Search for College Programs by Zipcode

This privacy policy's purpose is to disclose our data collection methods and illustrate our continuous guarantee to your ongoing privacy.

Activity on DegreeinSuccess.net will be automatically recorded by our server to track site activity and analyze potential problems. Personal information cannot be extrapolated from this data.

Requesting information from one of our schools or universities requires the applicant submit a form that includes specific information such as name, phone number, address, and email address that are unique to the applicant. The information given on our site will only be distributed to the school you are requesting information from and/or its authorized agent.

DegreeinSuccess.net links to other sites and some of these sites may contain their own forms and have their own information gathering and distribution practices. While we make every effort to only link to responsible sites, please be aware that we do not take responsibility for any of their practices or actions.

DegreeinSuccess.net does not collect personal information using cookies.

For questions regarding this privacy statement or other company policies, please feel free to contact us.
