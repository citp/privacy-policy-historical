Privacy & Security Information


PRIVACY POLICY Information Collection and Use Delphi Creativity Group is the sole owner of information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to any outside parties. The information collected is used to process orders and better serve you with pertinent information, such as order confirmations and tracking numbers. Information collected includes your name, shipping address, billing address, telephone numbers, e-mail address, and payment information such as your credit card number. You may also submit a username and password of your choice for your future access to your account information. To safeguard that your user name and password remain confidential, DO NOT share this information with anyone. If you elect to receive our newsletter or special promotions, your contact information will be used for the delivery of these items. We reserve the right to disclose your personally identifiable information as required by law and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on our Web site. Registration In order to process your orders placed on this website, you must first complete the registration form. During registration, you will be required to provide your contact information, which includes your name, e-mail address, telephone number and street address. This information is used to provide you with important DelphiGlass.com services such as automated order status updates via e-mail. Order Most of the information collected in the registration process will be used to process orders. During the order process, you will have to provide payment information such as your credit card number, expiration date, money order, wire transfer or check information. This information is used for billing purposes and to fulfill your order. If we have trouble processing an order, we will use your contact information to get in touch with you. To properly process your credit card information, we must share your credit card information with the merchant bank for authorization and approval. This process is protected by an enhanced security system. See the Security section below for more details on security measures and procedures. We do not share any personal or payment information with any other third parties. Cookies We customize certain Web page content based upon your browser type and other information provided by our cookies. If you choose to reject the cookie, you can still browse our store but will be unable to use the shopping cart to buy merchandise. The DelphiGlass.com shopping cart cannot function without cookies enabled so that the necessary information to process your order is retained. If you disable cookies, DelphiGlass.com will be unable to accept your online order. We will not share any personally identifiable information provided by this cookie with any third party. We will, however, link data stored in cookies to the personally identifiable information you submitted while on our site. This allows us to personalize your shopping experience. Security
Delphi Creativity Group takes precautions to protect its customers' information. When you submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and offline.

When our registration/order form asks you to enter sensitive information (such as your credit card number), that information is encrypted and is protected with the latest encryption software currently available in the industry - SSL. DelphiGlass.com uses the most advanced form of SSL software available: 128-bit encryption by GoDaddy.com. To learn more about SSL, follow this link www.godaddy.com.

Access to all of our users' information is restricted. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job are granted access to personally identifiable information. All Delphi Creativity Group servers, including web servers and database servers, are housed and maintained in secure locations. Access to the database is strictly monitored and protected from outside access. Internet access is restricted and protected by multiple Cisco firewalls and password protection. If you have any questions or concerns about Delphi Creativity Group's security practices, you can send us an email at service@delphiglass.com. Correction/Updating/Reviewing Personal Information Customers may change or review their stored account information such as street address or e-mail address through our website by visiting our Customer Service section. You must have your username and password in order to access your account. In the event you forget both your username and password, please contact our customer service department for assistance. Choice/Opt-out Users who no longer wish to receive DelphiGlass.com newsletter or promotional materials may opt-out of receiving these communications by visiting our Customer Service section and clicking the name of the newsletter you wish to opt-out of. This will change the text next to it to "Unsubscribed". You must have your username and password in order to access your account. Another option is to visit http://clerk.delphiglass.com and follow the on-screen instructions. All promotional emails and newsletters also provide a link to automatically unsubscribe. We reserve the right to modify this privacy statement at any time, so please review it frequently. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you here, by email, or by means of a notice on our homepage.

If you have opted out of receiving communications from DelphiGlass.com, you will still be able to view news of policy changes at www.delphiglass.com.
