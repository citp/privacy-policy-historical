To enable javascript on IE 5.5+:
1. Go to Tools > Internet Options... menu
2. Click the "Security" tab
3. Click the "Custom Level..." button
4. Scroll to near the bottom of the list and click "Enable" under "Active scripting"
5. Hit "OK" button twice to apply settings

 To enable javascript on Firefox:
1. Go to Tools > Options... menu
2. Click the "Content" tab
3. Check the "Enable JavaScript" checkbox
4. Hit "OK" button to apply settings
