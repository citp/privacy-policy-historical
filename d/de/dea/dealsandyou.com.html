Deals and You is a group buying portal that features a daily deal on the best stuff to do, see, eat, and buy in some of India's leading cities. Headquartered in Gurgaon, we are a part of the Group Buying Global AG which runs leading collaborative buying websites across four continents. Fashion&You, our sister concern, is now the leading fashion, luxury & style site in India as well as among the top 10 online multi-brand retailers in the country. DealsandYou acquired www.wanamo.com in June this year to enter the Indian marketplace.

Our main insight behind this venture is Collective Bargaining Power. Across the globe, this is one of the strongest trends impacting buying behavior & nowhere is it stronger than the internet. As a collective, we can impact how we buy and drastically improve our negotiation power. What does this mean for you? All the cool places, things to do that you have wanted at a huge discount. Now that means your money gets stretched further while allowing you to enjoy the coolest deals in town. By focusing on one deal each day, we leverage the group size & make it possible to bring bigger & better deals. Remember, the larger the group, the better for everybody! So inviting your friends becomes twice as attractive, you share, shop & save together.

Our company philosophy is pretty simple: We put ourselves in the shoes of our customers. So, we bring to you deals we would love to indulge in ourselves!

We don't believe in compromises
A great price is only half the battle won. It's also got to be a great product or service. That is what makes these deals special! Between our top-rated business partners and unbeatable prices, with Deals and You deals, we want to make you comfortable about venturing out and trying something new. We want Deals and You to be an addiction you would love to have!

Exceptional customer service
Like we don't compromise in our business, we don't believe in compromising in our customers comfort as well. We want you to trust our help services just like we want you to trust our fabulous deals. Harish Bahl, Chairman & Founder

Harish Bahl is a serial entrepreneur. In 1999, he laid the foundation of Smile Group and since then led several successful ventures in association with the global players ie WPP, Yahoo!, BrandAlliance, ScanGroup, GBG in the online media, e-commerce, healthcare & retail infrastructure sectors.

Tarun Sobhani, Director & Co-Founder
