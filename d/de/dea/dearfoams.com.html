Information We Collect Online
Non-Personal Information
dearfoams.com does not collect passive information such as your IP address, which type of browser you are using, where you go or how long you stay on any part of our site.

Personal Information
dearfoams.com may ask you to provide personal information when you establish an account, purchase Dearfoams products online, check the status of an order and/or contact our customer service with questions or comments. Categories of information that we may collect include name, billing and mailing address, e-mail address, telephone number, fax number and credit card information. In addition, if you elect to email a friend about our products, we will collect the email address of your friend.

To comply with federal law, dearfoams.com does not knowingly collect or maintain information provided by children under the age of 13.

Information Use and Disclosure
We use your personal information to complete purchases you make at our site, deliver products that you have ordered, contact you regarding order status and in the event of order or delivery difficulties, respond to your questions or comments and to deliver services and information about our products or Web site. If you elect to email a friend about our products, we will use the email address to deliver your request and disclose that the email is from you.

We use third parties to help us provide services to you such as taking orders, fulfilling orders, processing payments, monitoring site activity, and administering e-mail. These third parties are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

We do not sell or rent any information you may voluntarily provide to us. We will however, disclose specific information about you if necessary to do so by law or our good faith belief that it is necessary to conform to or comply with the law or as necessary to protect the users of our web site or the public.

As Dearfoams and its Web site continue to develop, we may buy, sell, or combine with other businesses. If so, personal and non-personal information that we have collected online will likely be transferred as part of the transaction.

Cookies
dearfoams.com uses a browser feature known as a cookie to enhance your online experience. A cookie is a small data file that is stored by your Web browser on your computer. We use cookies to keep track of the items you put into your shopping cart. We do not collect or maintain personal information about you through the use of cookies.

Links to Other Web Sites
For your convenience and enjoyment, our Web site may contain links to other sites. Links that appear on dearfoams.com should not be deemed to imply that we endorse or have any affiliation with the linked sites. dearfoams.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such sites. We encourage you to review the separate privacy policies of each site that you visit.

Changes to Privacy Policy
As we continue to offer our visitors new and different types of content and services, dearfoams.com may modify our collection and use practices. Should there be a material change to our information practices, it will be applied only to information collected on a going forward basis, and we will update this privacy policy statement.
