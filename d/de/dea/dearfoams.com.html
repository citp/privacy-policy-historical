Information We Collect

Personal Information
We may collect and store personally identifiable information on certain areas of the Dearfoams.com website when users register, purchase products or services, request information or a response, sign up for sweepstakes or contests, or participate in user posting areas, such as bulletin boards, discussion forums, reviews and surveys.

Such personally identifiable information may include but is not limited to your name, address, age, date of birth, sex, e-mail address, telephone numbers, information about your interests and activities and your reviews of our website or products that we sell. We may combine the personally identifiable information that we receive from you through the Dearfoams.com website with information from other sources. >

If you transact business with us, such personally identifiable information may also include financial information such as your payment method (valid credit card number, type, expiration date or other financial information). You may elect not to provide certain types of information, but that may result in you being unable to use certain features of the Dearfoams.com website.

We may also collect and store personally identifiable about other people that you provide to us. If you use the Dearfoams.com website to send others (friends, relatives, colleagues, etc.) a product as a gift, information that may interest them or messages (such as invitations) through our system, we may store your personal information, and the personal information of each such recipient.

Other Information
We may also automatically collect and store certain types of anonymous information about your shopping experiences when you visit the Dearfoams.com website by setting and accessing cookies on your computer. “Cookies” are small files placed on the hard drive of your computer. These cookies track information such as how often you visit our web site, what pages you view, where you go after you leave the site and what you may have placed in your shopping bag. The cookies track your computer, not you. They are used to facilitate access to the Dearfoams.com website and to personalize your on-line experience.

We may also use internet technology like Web beacons on our site and in e-mail messages to determine whether pages and messages have been opened and acted upon. A “web beacon” is an electronic image placed in the code of a web page. The information we obtain in this manner enables us to customize and improve the services we offer visitors to the Dearfoams.com website to deliver targeted advertisements and to measure the overall effectiveness of our on-line advertising and content or other activities.

We may in the future allow third-party advertising companies to use cookies when you visit Dearfoams.com. These selected companies may use information (not including your name, address, credit card information, email address, or telephone number) about your choice to visit our website and your visits to other websites to provide advertisements about our products on this and other websites. These selected companies also provide this information to us which we use to gauge the effectiveness of the advertisements they place about our products and for marketing purposes. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about no longer having information from these third-party cookies used by these companies or provided to us, click here.

Children's Privacy Protection
The Dearfoams.com website is not intentionally designed for or directed at children under 13 years of age and we will not intentionally collect or maintain information about anyone under the age of 13.

Personal and Other information that we collect from customers and users of the Dearfoams.com website is used to improve our relevance to you and to improve your shopping experience with us. We use the information that you provide so that we may provide the products and services that you have ordered or requested, to fulfill your requests for information, and to manage our business. If we do decide to share information with our affiliates, we will post a notice in this Privacy Policy and on our home page, but we will only share information with our affiliates that is collected after we post the notice.

We may use other information -- i.e. non-personally identifiable information, summary or aggregate information that we collect to improve the design and content of the Dearfoams.com website -- to enable us to further enhance your experience with the Dearfoams brand and for other business purposes. We may also share such aggregate information with third parties.

We may also share your personally identifiable information that you provide to us as may be required by law or based on our good faith belief that it is necessary to conform or comply with the law or is necessary to protect the users of our website, the site or the public or to enforce our Terms of Use.

In addition, such postings may appear on other websites or when searches are executed on the subject of your posting. Also, whenever you voluntarily disclose personal information on publicly-viewable Web pages, that information will be publicly available and can be collected and used by others. For example, if you post your e-mail address, you may receive unsolicited messages. We cannot control who reads your posting or what other users may do with the information you voluntarily post, so we encourage you to exercise discretion and caution with respect to your personal information. Once you have posted information, you will not be able to edit or delete such information.

Correction of Personal Information
If you create an Account, you may revise the information you have provided for your Account by following the instructions on the My Account page of the Dearfoams.com website.

California Privacy Rights
Under California law, if you are a resident of California, you have the right to control how your personal information is shared with third parties for their direct marketing purposes. Following receipt of a request from you, we will provide you with a cost-free means of informing us that you do not want your personal information to be shared with third parties for the direct marketing purposes of such third parties. If you would like to exercise your right under California law to tell us that you do not wish your personal information to be shared with third parties for their direct marketing purposes, you should send your written request to privacy@dearfoams.com or the mailing address at the end of this page. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Security
Dearfoams.com has imposed confidentiality obligations on its service providers who will have access to the personal information collected on this site. In addition, we have implemented administrative, technical and physical measures to address the confidentiality of personal information collected on this website. We use secure-socket-layer (SSL) certificate authentication to protect order information during transmission by encrypting all sensitive information transmitted during your shopping experience. SSL is encryption technology that provides security for information that's transmitted over the Internet. This site accepts orders only from web browsers that permit communication through SSL technology. We also reveal only the last four digits of your credit card numbers when confirming an order.

While we implement the above security measures for this website, you should be aware that 100% security is not always possible.
