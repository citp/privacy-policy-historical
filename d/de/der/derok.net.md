# DeROK.Net Privacy Policy

DeROK.Net Privacy Policy  |  [ ](http://www.derok.net/index2.php?option=com_content&task=emailform&id=789&itemid=310 "E-mail")  
---|---  
  
**DeROK.Net** is committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. Your personal information provided voluntarily will be used only to fulfill your specific requests and will not be given, traded or sold to any third parties. 

This website uses cookies and does employ tracking technology to monitor visits, which may result in the collection of personally identifiable information. This information is for business development purposes only and will not be shared with anyone. 

Additional information regarding Google Adsense: 

  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site. 
  * Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet. 
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html). 



We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. 

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact ** ** [derok21@yahoo.com](mailto:derok21@yahoo.com)
