Privacy Policy


We are fully committed to the privacy of our online patrons.  We do not share or sell information or use data collected for any purposes other than those serving The Denver Center's mission.

Cookies are used on our site to track site data and statistics and to complete orders or current activities only.  No personal information is identifiable and is, therefore, not trackable.

Information is collected for transactional purposes and patrons have an opportunity to opt-out of any e-mail contact, other than specific, online transactional communications.

For questions about this policy, e-mail privacy@dcpa.org.
