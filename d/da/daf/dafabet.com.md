We use cookies to keep track of your browsing patterns and to build up a demographic profile. By monitoring such patterns and collating the captured data we are able to improve our service to you. The Affiliate system uses cookies as part of the tracking process back to the referring affiliates. 

There is a simple procedure in most browsers that allows you to deny or accept the Cookie feature if you do not want information collected through the use of Cookies. Note, however, that "personalised" services may be affected if the cookie option is disabled.
