This Privacy Statement verifies that DAWN - the Internet Edition is a publication of The Pakistan Herald Publications (Pvt) Limited. This statement discloses the privacy practices for DAWN - the Internet Edition. For further information you can contact us at: [webmaster@dawn.com. ](mailto:webmaster@dawn.com)

DAWN - the Internet Edition has adopted a set of information management guidelines which serve as the basis for our customer and advertiser relationships. Accordingly, these guidelines are subject to change. Any such changes will be posted on this page. 

DAWN - the Internet Edition is not responsible for the content or the privacy policies of Web sites to which it may link. 

**Feedback Form Information:** While filling a feedback form <http://www.dawn.com/fixed/feed/feed.htm> , DAWN - the Internet Edition requests that you provide your personal information, e-mail address, and demographic information (country, zip code, age and gender are required; household income is optional). DAWN - the Internet Edition may collect additional personal information and views from subscribers by contests, polling booths or surveys. By providing this information you agree that DAWN - the Internet Edition may store information you provide as part of its data-collection for future demographic study. 

**E-Mails:** DAWN - the Internet Edition maintains two mailing lists to keep its subscribers about the events and happenings in Pakistan and South Asian region. Individuals may click on the following links to subscribe to our mailing lists. 

**DAWN Internet NewsAlert** \- this mailing lists contains important headline news and breaking events in Pakistan. One issue is sent daily and Breaking News are sent for major news coverage. To subscribe: <http://www.dawn.com/fixed/subs/dinasub.htm>

**DAWN Wire Service** \- this is a weekly mailing lists that gathers information on all major happenings. It includes National News, Business Updates, Sports and Opinions of famous columnists. To subscribe: <http://www.dawn.com/fixed/subs/dwssub.htm>


**Data Security:** To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the appropriate use of information, we have put in place physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to protect the information we collect online. We never sell, rent, loan, trade or lease the addresses on our lists to anyone. We have configured our list-severs in such a manner that no list subscriber can subscribe/unsubscribe others by just putting their addresses in the online forms. The user has to confirm his/her subscription/un-subscription request by email before it is applied. 

**Banner Advertising:** DAWN - the Internet Edition may perform statistical analyses of user behavior in order to measure interest in the various sections of our site and to inform advertisers as to how many consumers have seen or "clicked" their advertising banners. Personal information about you as an individual subscriber will not be provided to any third party. 

We have ads served on our site by Advertising.com. To visit their privacy statement, please click here <http://www.advertising.com/privacy/index.html>
