# Dave & Busters Eat, Drink, Play Privacy Policy

This is the Web site of **Dave & Buster's, Inc.**

### Our postal address is

2481 Manana  
Dallas, Texas 75220 

For each visitor to our Web page, our web server identifies non-personal information. The information we collect is used for internal review. It is used to improve the content of our Web page and is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes. 

Visitors who do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, should please let us know by sending mail to us at the above address and telling us that they do not want to receive e-mail from our company. We value the relationship we have with our visitors to our Web site. 

From time to time, we may share our user's contact information with our partners, affiliates or advertisers. We may use outside companies to provide goods and services. These companies do not retain, share, store or use personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes. We partner with other parties to provide specific services. When the user signs up for these services, we will share names or other contact information that is necessary for the third parties to provide these services. These parties are not allowed to use personally identifiable information except for the purpose of providing these services. It is standard operating procedure for Dave & Buster's, Inc., to safeguard the privacy of our visitors to our Web site. 

We hope visitors understand and appreciate our insistence on a strong Privacy Policy Statement as a matter of standard corporate policy and procedure. 

We can be reached via telephone at (214) 357-9588 or via fax at (214) 350-0941 to receive visitors' comments regarding our Privacy Policy Statement.
