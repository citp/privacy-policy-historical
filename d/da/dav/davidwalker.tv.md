# Privacy Policy | DavidWalker.tv

**This statement discloses the privacy practices of DavidWalker.tv.**

If you have any questions regarding this statement, please open a support ticket at AskDavidWalker.com.

**Customer Lead Information:** Any leads generated from DavidWalker.tv, i.e. email address and any other associated information are held in strict confidence. Each lead’s information is kept so that the subscriber’s follow up messages may be sent to that lead. I will not sell or share your information with any other party.

I use AutomateYourList.com/AWeber Systems, Inc. to store lead information and send email communication.  AutomateYourList.com/AWeber Systems, Inc. does not contact subscriber leads, nor does it sell or share your lead information with any other party.
