We at The Daily Reckoning take your privacy very seriously.

e-Mail Address
When you subscribe to The Daily Reckoning via e-mail, your e-mail address is placed in a database and used to send your issues of The Daily Reckoning and occasional Daily Reckoning Investment Alerts. We will not sell, rent, or otherwise share your e-mail address with anyone.

Any e-mail you send to The Daily Reckoning is considered confidential unless you tell us otherwise. We will not add your name to our e-mail list without your permission. Cookies
Cookies are small files placed on your computer by a web page when you visit it. They can be used to store all sorts of useful information to make your next visit easier and more convenient, but some people are uncomfortable not knowing what information is in a cookie.
