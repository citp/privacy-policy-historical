# Privacy Policy

It is important to us that your privacy concerns are addressed so please read this privacy policy carefully.  This page will explain how we handle information on our site and give you information on how your information is used once it is collected.  A link to this Privacy Policy can be found on every page of our site for you convenience should you ever need to find it again.

COLLECTED INFORMATION

The information collected by this site and users includes email address and name and may include other information such as IP address.

USES OF INFORMATION

Information that is collected by this site helps us to understand the needs of our audience.  It is not shared with other sites or companies unless specifically stated.  When information is collected, it is done in aggregate so that specific identities are not revealed.  It may help us to find better advertisers or to improve the site in other ways.  This means that we will not say user XYZ visited a certain area of the site, but rather, that a certain number of users visit a specific page or post.  Any information used will not have identifying characteristics.

If the case arises that we do need to provide identifying information you will have the chance to refuse by opting out.

DATA SECURITY COMMITMENT

Appropriate electronic, managerial, and physical procedures have been put in place to secure and keep your information safe and prevent unauthorized access, keep data accurate, and make sure that information is used appropriately.

ACCESS TO YOUR INFORMATION

To access your personal information or to correct information, log in and go to your dashboard and go to Users and then My Profile.  It can only be accessed by logging into your account.  You can correct mistakes as needed.

Special Note About Google Advertising

Any advertisements served by Google, Inc., and affiliated companies may be controlled using cookies. These cookies allow Google to display ads based on your visits to this site and other sites that use Google advertising services. Learn how to opt out of Google’s cookie usage. As mentioned above, any tracking done by Google through cookies and other mechanisms is subject to Google’s own privacy policies.

CONTACT US

Please use the contact form to contact us about any privacy concerns you may have.
