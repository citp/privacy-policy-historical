Do you have a privacy policy?

We will NEVER sell your information or bombard you with promotional emails. When you sign up to make a purchase, we only use your information to tell you when your product has shipped, or to give you other important information related to your purchase. Of course, we'd love you to sign up for our daily email so you can keep track of our newest deals, but that's entirely up to you -- if you choose not to sign up, we won't take it personally.

Is my credit card information safe?

Safer than Fort Knox. Your credit card information is never stored on our servers. Your payment information is transmitted directly to a secure third-party server you can tell you're on a safe page by the padlock icon that appears on your browser. When you make an order, our system scans your information for potential fraud. If there is a reason to suspect fraud, we will contact you to verify your order. If you do not verify with us within 24 hours, the order will be cancelled.

We accept all major credit cards as well as PayPal. We do NOT accept Monopoly money under ANY circumstances.
