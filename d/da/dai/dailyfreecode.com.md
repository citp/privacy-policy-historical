# Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is important to us, However DailyFreeCode.Com reserves the right at all times to disclose any information necessary to satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process or governmental request. 

We do not share or sell your personal information to any third parties . But all personal information you submit as part of your profile will be publicly accessible in our site, except the email address. We try not to publish your email address to avoid spam, but it is possible that in certain cases your email address may be exposed to public. However we publish all/part of information you submit as part of contribution (including, but not limited to article, etc) to our site. If you prefer not to publish any information, you may not submit any article, post, or any other kind of content to this site. Our site also provides social networking service, wherein all your Friends and Activity on this website would be accessible to everyone using this website. If you do not wish to expose your social details, please don’t share it. 

**General visitor information**  
For statistical purposes, we gather and store general visitor information like IP Address, browser version, visting time, duration etc. We may share such information with third parties or publish in our site for marketing purposes. 

**Cookies**  
Cookies are small pieces of information stored in your computer. We store small cookies in your computer, to provide a better browsing experience for you. We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. 

  * Third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on DailyFreeCode.Com. 
  * Third party vendor make use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to DailyFreeCode.Com users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet. 
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy. 
  * Remember to always click the Log-out/sign-out link when you’re done using DailyFreeCode.Com, especially if you’re on a shared computer or using a friend’s computer. If you simply quit the browser but don’t log-out/sign-out, anyone can reopen DailyFreeCode.Com on that browser later and access your details. 
  * We also store email of last user login. By Registering with DailyFreeCode.Com you agree's that your email would be shared on computer you used to access our website.

 **Advertisements**  
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here. We try to avoid any kind of offensive and harmfull advertisements in our site, but in many cases we do not have complete control of which advertisements appear in our site, due to the fact that many advertisements are automatically served by third party advertisers. We will not be responsible for any harms caused by such third party advertisements served in our site. However, if such issues are pointed to our notice, we will make all attempts to stop publishing such advertisements in our site. 

**Activities**  
We keep a track of all activities performed by user, while using this site. This information is than shared in different form on other third-party websites, such as Twitter, In forms of Feeds, etc. If you prefer not to publish any information, you may not contribute on this site. 

**Social Networking**  
When you interact with any feature on this site, we may collect and maintain information about your activities linked to that feature. When you send messages through this site, your name and email address will be identified in the message. We also use your name and email address to notify you of new members, messages, or other information, such as invitations to join friends' networks, etc. Being a social networking nature of this site, activities performed by you on this site would be shared on this site and other third-party sites. We recommend not to use any features of this site, if you wish not to share those information with others. 

**Business Transfers**  
As we continue to develop and grow our web site, we may sell DailyFreeCode.Com or part of it. In such transactions, member information generally is one of the transferred business assets
