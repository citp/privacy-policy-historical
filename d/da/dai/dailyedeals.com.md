# DailyeDeals.com: Privacy Policy

**Personal Information  
**Your privacy is of the utmost importance to Daily eDeals. You are free to fully use DailyeDeals.com anonymously. 

When [contacting](https://web.archive.org/web/20010611210122id_/http%3A//www.dailyedeals.com/contact.htm) us or submitting [feedback](https://web.archive.org/web/20010611210122id_/http%3A//www.dailyedeals.com/feedback.htm), you are welcome to give your personal information or to remain anonymous. We intend to protect the quality and integrity of any of your personally identifiable information that you do provide us. The feedback form automatically captures your IP address and browser version information. This information is used to aid us in fixing problems and to serve you better.

**Business Relationships  
**This site contains links to other Web sites. DailyeDeals.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

**Cookies  
**This site does not use cookies.

**Mailing List  
**If you join our mailing list, we will keep your email address and any demographics information you volunteer strictly confidential. We will never give or sell your email address or any information about you to anyone else.
