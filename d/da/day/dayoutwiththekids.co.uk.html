Privacy & Cookie Policy

At Day Out With The Kids, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This policy outlines how we treat your personal data after it has been collected by any of our channels which include our website, app, social media pages or any future digital services or channels as they evolve.

Data Control

Any personal information provided to us or gathered by www.dayoutwiththekids.co.uk is controlled by Day Out With The Kids Limited, 3&4 Regal Court, 6 Sovereign Road, Kings Norton Business Centre, Birmingham, B30 3HN.

Collection Methods

  • When you purchase a product or service from us
  • When you subscribe to our newsletter
  • When you create a profile
  • When you complete a survey or enter a competition
  • When you download the app
  • When you leave a product review
  • When you follow us on our social media channels
  • When we use cookies to see which website pages are the most popular

We operate in accordance with data protection legislation.  If you have any questions about the information we hold on you or this privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at help@dayoutwiththekids.co.uk.

 Your Personal Information

All personal information you provide us with such as name, address, email etc will be held confidentially and will not sell, rent or trade your personal information to third parties unless you give permission to do so. Once a payment is processed we do not retain your credit card number, and any information we do retain is held in our secure database.

We also use your personal information to prevent or detect fraud or abuses of our website.

When you subscribe to our newsletter you consent to being sent news, information on products and special offers that we think may interest you. If you register with us you hereby grant permission for us to contact you in this way.

When you give us your personal information you consent to the collection, storage and processing of this information by www.dayoutwiththekids.co.uk in accordance with this privacy policy. If you send us information, when appropriate we may use this as testimonials, but we will not display any personal information unless you have given prior consent. 

Opt Out Policy

Subscribers can opt out of receiving further information from us at any time by logging into their account and opting out.

If you would like to unsubscribe to our newsletter you can also email us at help@dayoutwiththekids.co.uk.

Change of Details

To update any of your details such as email or address please log into your account and update as necessary.

Third-Party Advertising

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. 

If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having information used by these companies, please see http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/. 

Log Files 

Like many other websites, www.dayoutwiththekids.co.uk makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. 

Cookies

A cookie is a small file that is automatically issued to your computer when you enter a website, and is stored by the browser on your computer's hard drive. Cookies identify your computer. Cookies store some basic information that helps us to identify if a user of your computer has visited the Site before.

www.dayoutwiththekids.co.uk uses cookies which include session, permanent and third party cookies. Visit your browsers help menu to find out more on how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie and how to disable cookies.

Doubleclick DART Cookie

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on www.dayoutwiththekids.co.uk. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to www.dayoutwiththekids.co.uk and other sites on the internet. Users may opt out the use of the DART cookie by visiting https://www.google.com/policies/technologies/ads/. 

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include Google Adsense. These third-party ad serves or ad networks use technology to show advertisements and links that appear on www.dayoutwiththekids.co.uk sent directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. 

Other technologies (such as cookies, Javascript or web beacons) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure effectiveness of their advertisements and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see. www.dayoutwiththekids.co.uk has no access to or control over these cookies. You should consult the respective privacy policies of such third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices, as well as instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. 

The www.dayoutwiththekids.co.uk privacy policy does to not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or websites. If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
