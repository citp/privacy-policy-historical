Privacy Policy

Datafeedr.com is committed to protecting the privacy of its online visitors.

We will NEVER use your email address or any other personal details we collect for anything other than to send you the data that you opted in to receive.

We will NEVER sell or give your personal information to any third party whatsoever.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have personal information on a child under the age of 13, we will delete that information from our systems immediately. Datafeedr.com encourages parents to go online with their kids. Here are a few tips to help make a child’s online experience safer:

  • Teach kids never to give personal information, unless supervised by a parent or responsible adult. This includes name, address, phone, school, etc.
  • Know the sites your kids are visiting and which sites are appropriate. – Look for Web site privacy policies. Know how your child’s information is treated.
  • Check out the FTC’s site for more tips on protecting children’s privacy online.
  • Along with a subscription to any of our mailing lists, you agree to receive periodic advertising emails from us. We never send more than one email per day. An easy-to-use unsubscribe link will be at the bottom of every email that you receive, and you may unsubscribe at any time.

We detest “spam”. We do not participate in any such marketing practices and do not condone it in any way.

If you have any further questions or concerns at any time, please do not hesitate to contact us via the Contact Us page. We’ll do our best to get back to you as soon as possible.
