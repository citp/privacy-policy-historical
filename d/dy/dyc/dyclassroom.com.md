# DYclassroom | Have fun learning :-)

Last modified: 09 October 2014

At [www.dyclassroom.com](http://www.dyclassroom.com/), the privacy of our visitor is of extreme importance to us. This document outlines the type of information collected by our website and how it is stored and used. 

## Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics on our website to better understand the traffic and analyse user interaction with our website. 

## Cookie

We uses cookie in order to save information and your preferences and understand site traffic and offer better user experience. 

## DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our website. 

Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet. 

Users may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting [Ads Settings](http://www.google.com/ads/preferences/). 

## Information we collect

You may be asked to enter your name, email address and/or your website link when you subscribe to our newsletter or comment or register on our website. However, you can use our site anonymously. 

## How is the collected information used?

  * We continually try to improve our site based on the information collected and feedback that we receive.
  * To send newsletters. You can unsubscribe from the newsletter service at any time you like.
  * To improve services.



## Third Party Products and Links

We occasionally, at our discretion, may use third part products or services on our website. And our site may contain links referring to webpages that belongs to other websites. These third party products, services and websites have their own privacy policy. Therefore, we are not responsible and liable for their product service and any content on their website. Though, you are encouraged to send feedback regarding these third party products, services and websites. 

## Your Consent

By using our site you agree to our privacy policy. 

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes here on this page. If you have any question regarding the Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us.
