# Privacy Policy

We are strongly committed to protecting the privacy of the users. This Privacy Policy sets out how we collect and process your personal data that you may provide through this website.

### Collection and Use of Personal Information

The website collects personal information that you voluntarily provide when subscribing to the website and may include your name, mobile no, e-mail address, etc. This information is used to provide you access to the website and communicate with you the information on updates and modifications to the website via various means including e-mail.

### Use of Cookies and Other Electronic Tools

Dynamiclevels.com may place, view, and/or use “cookies”, web server logs, web beacons, or other electronic tools to collect statistical and other information about your use of this Web site and other websites. This information may include information about the IP address of your computer, browser type, language, operating system, your mobile device, geo-location data, the state or country from which you accessed this web site, the web pages visited and the date and the time of a visit.  
Such information may be collected for various reasons and/or for own purposes including research, analysis and to better serve visitors to this website. However, if you have deleted and disabled cookies, these uses will not be possible to the extent they are based on cookie information.

### Disclosure of Personal Information and Other Information

If you participate in certain features of this website (e.g., sharing of portfolio and other information), please note that any information you voluntarily disclose through use of these features, it becomes available to us. If you choose to provide access to designated individuals, please be aware that any information you post will be visible to such individuals for their view.  
We may also disclose your personal and other information if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary: (a) to comply with the law or in response to a court order, government request, or other legal process; (b) to produce relevant documents or information in connection with litigation, arbitration, mediation, adjudication, government or internal investigations, or other legal or administrative proceedings; (c) to protect the interests, rights, safety, or property of the Website or others; (d) to enforce any terms of service on this Website ; or (e) to operate the website ’s systems properly. 

### Accuracy and Security of Personal Information

Any personal information you provide to the website, it is kept on secure servers. The website uses reasonable administrative, technical, personnel, and physical measures to safeguard personal information against loss, theft, unauthorized use, disclosure, or modification. To help us protect your privacy, you should maintain the secrecy of the login ID and password you may have set up in connection with your participation in this website’s service.

By accessing this Website location or otherwise using the Services, you consent to collection, storage, and use of the personal information you provide (including any changes thereto as provided by you) for any of the services that we offer. The Company respects the privacy of the users of the Services and is committed to reasonably protect it in all respects.  
When you chose any subscription or paid service, we or our payment gateway provider may collect your purchase, address or billing information, including your credit card number and expiration date. 

### Information from other Sources

We may receive information about you from other sources, add it to our account information and treat it in accordance with this policy. If you provide information to the platform provider or other partner, whom we provide services, your account information and order information may be passed on to us. We may obtain updated contact information from third parties in order to correct our records and fulfil the Services or to communicate with you. 

### Links to 3rd party sites/ad servers

The Website may include links to other websites. Such websites are governed by their respective privacy policies, which are beyond our control. Once you leave our servers (you can tell where you are by checking the URL in the location bar on your browser), use of any information you provide is governed by the privacy policy of the operator of the website, you are visiting. That policy may differ from ours.  
When we present information to our advertisers — to help them understand our audience and confirm the value of advertising on our websites or Website — it is usually in the form of aggregated statistics on traffic to various pages / content within our websites or Website. We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our websites or Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number or other personally identifiable information) about your visits to this and other websites or website , in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. We do not provide any personally identifiable information to third party websites / advertisers / ad-servers without your consent. 

### Other Websites

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.
