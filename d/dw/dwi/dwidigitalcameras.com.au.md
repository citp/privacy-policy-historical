#### **Opt-In /Opt-Out**

Registering as a user and opening a user account is optional. To close an account, please email support@dwinternational.net. Your user account will be closed and all information will be removed from our server within 28 days. To opt-out of our newsletter subscription, please follow the instructions found at the bottom of the newsletter.

#### **Dispute Resolution**

If visitors or customer have any issues regarding the privacy practice of our website, please email support@dwinternational.net addressing our Privacy Policy Supervisor at the address above. Our Privacy Policy Supervisor will respond within 10 working days.

#### **Data Collection**

When visiting websites, certain information about you and your computer may sometimes be collected. This section explains what information our Privacy Policy Supervisor will be collecting, and how it is used.  
We may collect the following information about our visitors:

  * username data
  * user's home-info data
  * dynamic.clickstream data
  * dynamic.http data
  * dynamic.interactionrecord data
  * dynamic.miscdata data
  * dynamic.cookies data



We collect this information for the following reasons:

  * share remarketing insights with Google Analytics tool to provide the customize promotion benefits
  * Completion and Support of Current Activity
  * Website and System Administration
  * Research and Development
  * Affirmative Customisation
  * One-time Tailoring
  * Contacting Visitors for Marketing of Services or products (newsletter subscribers only)



User Account Registration Data: The user account registration is optional. The data is stored on our server and is used for the sole purpose of speeding up your order process.  
Data is collected when customers place an order: We collect customer's Physical Contact Address and Internet Contact Address, as well as Credit Card information (in the case of Credit Card payments) for the sole purpose of processing the order and delivering the goods ordered.

As we offer high value products, we may occasionally ask for additional personal information during the order stage like your IDs, utility bills, etc for the order verification for security reasons . All the information collected for this purpose is solely used for DWI’s internal business only.

Any such information collected from customers is handled diligently by our trained staff and gets deleted from our email servers within 60 days.

Newsletter Service: Customers may opt-in and opt-out of our newsletter service. Our newsletter subscription service application may collect customer's demographic data in order to provide suitable content to subscribers. Customers may opt-out of the newsletter at any time. Instructions on how to opt-out is provided in the bottom part of every newsletter sent.

We DO NOT SELL collected data to 3rd parties: All data collected is used strictly for the purposes defined in the above clauses. We will never sell collected data to any 3rd party individual or organization.

#### **Cookies**

Cookies are a technology which can be used to provide you with tailored information from a website. A cookie is an element of data that a website can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it or not.  
Our website uses cookies for the purpose of 'remembering' customers' choice of local site only.
