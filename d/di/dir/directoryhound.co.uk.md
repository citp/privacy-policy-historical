# Directory Hound - Privacy Policy

We respect and honour your privacy and we do not tolerate SPAM in any form. Please read the following that will outline our commitment to you to protect your privacy and any information you place into our care. 

### Your Data

We will never give, sell, lease or in any way make available the personal information you supply us with to any other organisation or individual. 

All information supplied by you to us will be used only by us to provide you with more security, better service and features. 

We will only store the minimum data that is applicable to be able to perform the service that you have asked us for. Any other surplus information you may have provided will be destroyed and will be unrecoverable by anyone. 

### Contacting You

When you make a submission to u-Hits, we need to contact you with regard to your listing and in order ascertain your submission is genuine and not made by automated means. As such, you will receive contact ONLY under the following circumstances: 

  * When or if you register an account.
  * When you submit a listing. 
  * When we approve your listing.
  * When you amend a listing.
  * When we reject your listing.
  * When we modify your listing.
  * When your listing expires.



We may, from time to time, contact you using the details you supplied upon registration, with regard to site updates, upgrades and maintenance, or if there is a problem with your listing. 

At no time will we contact you for the sole purpose of marketing, or with any third-party correspondence or promotional material. 

In addition to the above and depending on options you choose at the time of submission, our users may be able to contact you through our site. See the section below for more information on this. 

### Public Data

When you submit a listing and depending on your submission type, you may be given the option to publicly display or withhold certain data. These options are clearly marked and should be used with care. By supplying your address details or by ticking the appropriate boxes on the form, you give permission for this data to be displayed publicly. In short : - 

  * If you supply a business address AND tick box 'DISPLAY ADDRESS', it will be displayed.
  * If you supply a business address AND tick box 'SHOW MAP', a map of your physical location will be displayed. 
  * If you supply a business email address AND tick box 'SHOW EMAIL', it will be displayed.
  * If you supply a business email address AND tick box 'ALLOW CONTACT', it will allow users to contact you through our site.



All default settings are 'off', so if no action is taken by you, this information will be held and not displayed. 

### Cookies and Session Data

Like most websites, we use cookies and session data for correct functionality of the site. You should therefore understand that disabling cookies and overriding of session data may lead to certain functions of the site not working as expected. 

We do not use cookies that store any personal identifiable or financial information. 

Cookies / Session data are used for the following aspects of this website : 

  * Remembering account data when logged in as a user.
  * Remember certain user defined preferences.
  * In addition, we use third-party advertising services, who may place their own tracking cookies onto your computer. These are used to serve advertisements to better suit you and their placement is outside of our control. 



Please adjust your browser settings if you prefer not to have cookies downloaded to your computer when viewing websites. 

### Third-party Advertising

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/).
