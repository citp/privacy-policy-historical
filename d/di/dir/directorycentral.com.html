General. When SocialTerrain collects PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION from you it is because you are voluntarily submitting Registration PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION to us in order to register as a Member of SocialTerrain. Link to Privacy Settings.

Cookies. Cookies are small bits of information that SocialTerrain places on your computer. SocialTerrain uses cookies to identify your Internet browser, store Users' preferences, and determine whether you have installed the enabling software needed to access certain material on the SocialTerrain Services. Data in cookies may be read to authenticate user sessions or provide services.

Third party advertisements displayed on SocialTerrain Services may also contain cookies set by Internet advertising companies or advertisers (known as "third party cookies"). SocialTerrain does not control these third party cookies and Users of the SocialTerrain Services should check the privacy policy of the Internet advertising company or advertiser to see whether and how it uses cookies. See the "Notice" section below for more information on customized advertising on SocialTerrain. A pixel tag is a tiny image inserted in a webpage and used to record the number and types of views for that page. SocialTerrain may allow third party pixel tags to be present on SocialTerrain Services for purposes of advertising, providing services or data and statistics collection.

You can program your computer to warn you each time a cookie is being sent, block third party cookies or block all cookies. However, by blocking all cookies you may not have access to certain features on the SocialTerrain Services
