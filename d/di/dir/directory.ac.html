Once you provide your personal information, you are not anonymous to Directory.ac.

Directory.ac may combine this information with the personally identifiable information it collects.

Registration & Order

In order to submit a site for review, a user must first complete the Submit Site form. During submission or user registration a user is required to give contact information including name and email address. We use this information to contact the user about services on our site for which he has expressed interest.

During the submission or user registration, we require information from the user on our submission form or user registration form. A user must provide contact information (such as name, email, and address). This information is used for to contact the user.

In addition to the methods described above, we may also collect information using cookies, web web analytic services or similar technologies. These methods permit us to collect various types of information, including which pages you visit, which of our email messages you read, and other information.

Directory.ac is not directed toward children under 13 years of age nor does Directory.ac knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not submit any personally identifiable information to Directory.ac

Consequently, collected information is tied to the users personally identifiable information to provide offers and improve the user experience. We DO NOT share your profile with other third parties. Directory.ac may use your information:

  • To provide you with personalized content.
  • To process and respond to inquiries.
  • For the purposes for which you provided the information.
  • To improve the content and navigability of the Website.
  • To alert you to new Website features, special events, products and services, and to deliver newsletters from which you may opt out by clicking the unsubscribe link within the email. (On rare occasions it is necessary to send out a strictly service related announcement. For instance, if our service is temporarily suspended for any reason we might send users an email. Generally, users may not opt-out of these communications - these communications are not promotional in nature.)

  • In such transactions, customer information generally is one of the transferred business assets. In the event of a sale of our assets including our database, customer information will be transferred.
  • To communicate with users on a regular basis to provide requested services and in regards to issues relating to their account we reply via email or phone, in accordance with the user’s wishes.

Directory.ac attempts to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of your personal information. Our server are protected by reasonable physical and electronic security measures.

We will require buyers to honor our privacy policy.
