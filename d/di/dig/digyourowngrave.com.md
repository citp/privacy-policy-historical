# DIGYOUROWNGRAVE.COM - Privacy Policy

The following Privacy Policy covers all of the Littlegray Media Websites, including this site, _Dig Your Own Grave_ (also known as _DIGYOUROWNGRAVE.COM_ ). Please read this statement carefully. If you do not agree to these terms and conditions, do not use this, or any other Littlegray Media Website.

## Ownership of Information

Littlegray Media is the sole owner of the information collected on the Littlegray Media Websites.

## User Contributions

In order to enhance the use of Littlegray Media Websites, you will be given opportunities to leave input for other users to publicly view (for instance, commenting on particular articles of interest). If you chose to contribute input to this or any other Littlegray Media Website, you will be asked to leave personal information to accompany your contribution (for example, name/nickname, email, URL, etc). Leaving this personal information is always optional, and in any circumstances where the information will be posted publicly, you will be clearly informed beforehand. If any of your personal information is publicly posted in error, [contact Littlegray Media](mailto:littlegray@gmail.com) to have the information removed. Once submitted, your contribution (be it text, image, audio, video, etc) becomes the sole property of Littlegray Media. Although input provided by other users is moderated, it does not necessarily reflect the opinions or views of this, or any other Littlegray Media Website.

## Third Party Advertising and Cookies

The ads appearing on Littlegray Media Websites are delivered to users by third party advertisers. These companies may use information about your visits to this and other Websites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. These advertisers may use cookies on our site to accomplish this. However, Littlegray Media has no access to, or control over, these cookies once we have given permission for them to set cookies for advertising. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Littlegray Media only, and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

## Log Files

Like most standard Website servers we use log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, number of clicks, etc. to analyze trends, administer the site, track user movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. None of this data is linked to personally identifiable information.

## Sharing Information

Except as expressly set forth herein, Littlegray Media will never loan, give away, sell, distribute or rent your personal information to any third party. Furthermore, Littlegray Media does not divulge any information regarding the users who use email for correspondence with the webmaster or any representative of Littlegray Media.

## Legal Disclaimer

Though we make every effort to preserve user privacy, Littlegray Media may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order, or legal process served on any of the Littlegray Media Websites.

## Business Transitions

In the event that Littlegray Media goes through a business transition, such as a merger, acquisition by another company, or sale of a portion of its assets, users' personal information will, in most instances, be part of the assets transferred.

## Links

The Littlegray Media Websites contain links to other sites. Please be aware that Littlegray Media is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage you to be aware when you leave our site and to read the Privacy Policies of each and every Website that collects personally identifiable information. This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected by Littlegray Media.

## Notification of Changes

We reserve the right to change or modify our privacy practices that are described herein simply by posting a revised Privacy Policy on this Website. Any such change shall be effective immediately upon posting of the revised Privacy Policy on this Website. We reserve the right to make the revised or changed Privacy Policy effective for information we already have about you, as well as any information we receive in the future. We encourage you to refer to this Privacy Policy on an ongoing basis so that you understand our current privacy practices.

## Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, the practices of this Website, or your dealings with this Website, please [contact Littlegray Media](mailto:littlegray@gmail.com).
