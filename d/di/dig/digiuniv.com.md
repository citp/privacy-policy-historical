### Cookies

A "cookie" is a small piece of information stored by a web server on a web browser so it can be later read back from that browser. Digiuniv uses cookie and tracking technology depending on the features offered. No personal information will be collected via cookies and other tracking technology; however, if you previously provided personally identifiable information, cookies may be tied to such information. Aggregate cookie and tracking information may be shared with third parties.
