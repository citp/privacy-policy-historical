Digital Insight Privacy Statement

Digital Insight, the leading provider of outsourced eFinance solutions to visionary financial institutions, is committed to protecting your privacy when you use our corporate website.

Information We Collect
When you visit this site, we will collect information on two different levels about your visit:
  • anonymous statistics collected by our web servers through “log files” (Internet Provider address of requestor; name of web pages and images requested from the site; referring URL, if it is available)
  • personal information you knowingly give us (e.g., name; address; social security number; date of birth; etc.)
Why We Gather Information
When you visit this site, We gather anonymous statistics in order to monitor our products and equipment and to improve our services. We also gather your personal information in order to properly identify you and complete requests initiated by you.

How We Gather Information
Anonymous Statistics: We collect and are the sole owner of the following information which is collected from all of our website users. We do not use “cookies” to collect this information. We use log files to collect:

  • Internet Provider address of requestor
  • Name of web pages and images requested from the site
  • Referring URL, if it is available
Personal Information: In certain areas of our website (as detailed below) we also gather your personal information on registration or application forms, in order to complete requests initiated and authorized by you. In these particular areas, if you request information, then to complete your request we collect your name, mailing address, email address, phone and fax numbers, social security number, date of birth, and other optional information. You always have the option not to complete your request, and to provide information through another communication method such as telephone.

User Forms
Career Center: In this section of our website, you can submit your resume for a posted job, through the Resume Submission Form. This form is a “framed page” supported by OpenHire.com, a third party service provider that securely processes your personal information on our behalf. OpenHire.com only uses your personal information to process your resume, and is obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your information. The Resume Submission Form will ask you for personal information such as your name, address, email address, telephone number, salary, and citizenship status. Once you have provided your personal information via this form, you can update it or delete it at any time by emailing employmentwest@digitalinsight.com. You always have the option of using other methods to apply for posted job positions, such as fax.

Contact Us: This section of our website is NOT intended for individual consumer use. It is intended for financial institutions which are interested in our products and services, where they can request a follow up phone call or email from us by providing contact information on the form such as name, title, daytime phone number, and so on. Once the financial institution has provided this information on the form, it can be updated or deleted it at any time by emailing customersupport@digitalinsight.com. Financial institutions always have the option of using other methods to contact us, such as telephone.

Investor Relations: In this section of our website, you can request more information about the company, through a request for mailed printed material or through email updates. The forms in this section are “framed pages” supported by Shareholder.com, a third party service provider that securely processes your personal information on our behalf. Shareholder.com only uses your personal information to process your request and is obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your information. Under the “Printed Materials” tab of this section, you can request printed material by mail or express delivery. The form asks for your title, organization, name, address, and account number for express delivery. At your option, you may provide your phone number, email address and investor type (e.g., shareholder, analyst, employee, etc.). This information is used only to fulfill your one-time request for printed material. There is no mechanism for changing or updating your personal information, as there are no repeated transactions. Under the “Email Notification” tab of this section, you can request automatic emails any time we release news or earnings, by entering your email address. You will also be asked for your name and investor classification (e.g., shareholder, analyst, employee, etc.). Additionally, you will be asked to voluntarily provide other information such as title, organization, address, and home and work phone numbers. You can change or delete your email address at any time by resubmitting the form, or by following the link supplied by confirmation email at the time you request Email Notification. You always have the option of requesting investment information through other methods such as telephone and mail.

Customer Services: This section of our website is NOT intended for individual consumer use. It is designed for our financial institution clients only, who will be asked to provide their user name and password for identification and security purposes. This section also records failed log-on attempts for security purposes.

Information We Will Disclose
Within Digital Insight - We will share your personal information internally for the purpose of performing a transaction requested by you and for administering business.

Outside of Digital Insight - We are required to or we will provide information about you to third parties without your consent, as required by law; for example, to respond to a subpoena or court order, or judicial or regulatory process.

In addition, we will provide your personal information to third parties who are under agreement with us to help perform your requested transactions. These companies are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your personal information that is provided to them through our website. Your personal information will not be used for any purpose other than to help fulfill your request. For further details, please review above “How We Gather Information.”

Beyond Our Control
Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of the linked websites and encourage you to fully review those third parties' privacy and security statements, because they may differ from ours.

Our Security Practices
We also take steps to safeguard your information. We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to guard this information.

We restrict access to your personal information to those employees who need to know that information in the course of their job responsibilities. We educate our employees and contractors about the importance of confidentiality and privacy. We take appropriate disciplinary measures to enforce employee and contractor responsibilities regarding your information.
