# Privacy Policy | DigsDigs

The web site is protected to ensure that your information is not lost, misused, or abused.

Small text files stored on your computer hard drive are known as Cookies, these files store a variety of information used regarding your interaction with this website. The information collected is intended to enhance your web experience while visiting the site.

The site also use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit it. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. This includes Google as a third-party vendor, which uses cookies (including the DART cookie) to serve ads on the site, based on previous visits to this site and other sites. If you would like more information about this practice and would like to know your options in relation to not having this information used, please [click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
