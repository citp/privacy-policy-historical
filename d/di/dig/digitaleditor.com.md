# Digital Editor: Statement of Privacy

Simply put: We promise never to share your information with anyone unless we have to. 

DigitalEditor.com is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. This DigitalEditor.com Statement of Privacy applies to [companynamepossessive] family of Web sites and governs data collection and usage at all DigitalEditor.com sites and services. Please read the DigitalEditor.com Statement of Privacy below and also any supplementary information at the end of this statement for additional details about particular DigitalEditor.com sites and services that you may use. 

DigitalEditor.com is a general audience web site, intended for users of all ages. Personal information of all users is collected, used and disclosed as described in this Statement of Privacy. 

## Collection of your Personal Information

At some sites, DigitalEditor.com collects personal information, such as your email address and name. At other sites, DigitalEditor.com collects anonymous demographic information, that is not unique to you,such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favorites. Some sites collect a combination of the two types of information. In some cases,information collected by DigitalEditor.com may be combined with information obtained from other DigitalEditor.com services and other companies. 

There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by DigitalEditor.com and may be shared with other Web sites within the DigitalEditor.com family of web sites. This information may include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by DigitalEditor.com for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of DigitalEditor.com. 

DigitalEditor.com uses encryption technology to provide registration and sign-in services. All of the registration information you provide will be stored by DigitalEditor.com on our servers. 

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personal information through DigitalEditor.com Discussion forums, this information may be collected and used by others. Note: DigitalEditor.com may read all email sent through our web site such as your DigitalEditor.com Tell a friend or Contact the author emails. 

DigitalEditor.com encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from DigitalEditor.com so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. DigitalEditor.com is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of the DigitalEditor.com family of Web sites. 

## Use of your Personal Information

DigitalEditor.com and its operational service partners collect and use your personal information to operate DigitalEditor.com and deliver the services you have requested. DigitalEditor.com also uses your personal information to inform you of other products or services available from DigitalEditor.com and its affiliates. DigitalEditor.com may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered. 

DigitalEditor.com does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. 

We occasionally hire other companies to provide limited services on our behalf, such as handling the processing and delivery of mailings, providing customer support, processing transactions, or performing statistical analysis of our services. We will only provide those companies the personal information they need to deliver the service. They are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information and are prohibited from using that information for any other purpose. 

DigitalEditor.com keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within DigitalEditor.com, in order to determine what DigitalEditor.com sites and services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within DigitalEditor.com to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area. 

DigitalEditor.com may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on DigitalEditor.com or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of DigitalEditor.com, including its DigitalEditor.com family of Web sites; or (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of DigitalEditor.com, its web sites, or the public. 

Personal information collected on this site may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country in which DigitalEditor.com or its affiliates, subsidiaries or agents maintain facilities, and by using this site, you consent to any such transfer of information outside of your country. DigitalEditor.com abides by the safe harbor framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of data from the European Union. 

## Control your Personal Information

DigitalEditor.com allows you to modify the information we hold about you by visiting your membership profile page. Please [contact us](http://digitaleditor.com/typo3/contact) if you wish to delete your membership account. 

You may stop the delivery of future email from DigitalEditor.com by following the specific instructions in the email you receive. The instructions explain how to stop receiving such emails. 

There are occasions when you will receive emails informing you of technical service issues, product surveys, new feature announcements and news about DigitalEditor.com products and services. You may not be able to unsubscribe to these mailings. 

## Security of your Personal Information

DigitalEditor.com strictly protects the security of the personal information you provide. DigitalEditor.com secures the personal information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When highly confidential information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted over the Internet, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol. 

## Use of Cookies

DigitalEditor.com uses “cookies” to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. 

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. For example, if you personalize [sitenamepossessive] pages, or register with DigitalEditor.com sites or services, a cookie helps DigitalEditor.com to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as member ID, email address, and so on. When you return to DigitalEditor.com, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the DigitalEditor.com features that you customized. 

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the DigitalEditor.com services or Web sites you visit. for more information. 

## Use of Third Party Ad Networks

DigitalEditor.com does not use any third-party Advertising Networks. 

## Enforcement of this Statement of Privacy

If you have questions regarding this statement, you should contact DigitalEditor.com. 

## DigitalEditor.com Discussion Forums

While participating in chats, communities or other public forums, DigitalEditor.com cautions you against giving out personal information (such as names and addresses, phone numbers, social security numbers, credit card numbers, and driver’s license numbers) to strangers online, which they may use for illegal or harmful purposes. 

## DigitalEditor.com Newsletters and Watches

When you subscribe to an DigitalEditor.com Newsletter or Watch a page, DigitalEditor.com requests your email address. DigitalEditor.com uses your email address to send you the newsletter(s) or watch(es) you have requested. DigitalEditor.com does not share your email address with third parties except as required to deliver the service. Each DigitalEditor.com newsletter or Watch you receive will have instructions on how to unsubscribe prominently mentioned in it. 

## Changes to this Statement

DigitalEditor.com will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. When DigitalEditor.com posts changes to this Statement, you will see the word “updated” next to the Privacy Statement link on the front page of DigitalEditor.com. If there are material changes to this Statement or in how DigitalEditor.com will use your personal information, DigitalEditor.com will prominently post such changes prior to implementing the change. DigitalEditor.com encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how DigitalEditor.com is protecting your information. 

## Contact Information

DigitalEditor.com welcomes your comments regarding this Statement of Privacy. If you believe that DigitalEditor.com has not adhered to this Statement, please contact DigitalEditor.com by [email](http://digitaleditor.com/typo3/contact).
