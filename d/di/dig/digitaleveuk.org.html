DigitalEve respects your online privacy. We will never sell the information collected as a result of your interaction with our website, either as a member or visitor. We collect demographic data to better serve you.

We may, on occasion, allow sponsors to send emails concerning special offers and jobs to members who have indicated their preferences by checking the appropriate check-boxes during sign-up, but under no circumstances will we release your information (including email address) to our sponsors without your express prior permission.

DigitalEve uses cookies to track session information to improve the content of our site, but we will not associate cookies with individual information.

We want you to feel safe here. And to that end, we will maintain the highest degrees of security and privacy with your personal information.
