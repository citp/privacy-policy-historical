Diet Blog is the sole owner of information collected on the Diet Blog site. This information neither sold nor passed on elsewhere.

Commenting

Users may leave comments on articles. Comment submission includes an email address. This is not passed on to any other organization.

User Registration

Commenters may register and create a profile that includes an email address and possibly other demographic information. Again this is only stored on Diet Blog servers and is never passed on.

Cookies

“Cookies” are small bits of data stored in the user’s computer. This allows a user to remain sign on with continually having to supply their user name and password. If you reject cookies you will not be able to sign in properly.

Mailing Lists

Users may subscribe to mailing lists where content is sent via email. Mailing lists are handled by third-parties Feedblitz and Feedburner and Diet Blog is not responsible for their privacy policies.

Third Party Advertising

Advertising is served by Google and Blogads.
