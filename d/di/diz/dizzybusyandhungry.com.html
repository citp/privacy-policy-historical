About Me

Hi, I’m Kristin, and I am a really busy working wife and mom of two teenage boys. My life feels like such a whirlwind, it makes me dizzy sometimes! But even though things are always moving a mile a minute, I still strive to serve my family healthy, delicious food. I am not a kitchen whiz (really just started cooking about two years ago!) and I don’t have a lot of time and energy for planning, shopping, and preparing, so I am always on the hunt for recipes that are quick and easy and nutritious!
