# Privacy Policy - Mobile Phones UK - DialToSave

## Privacy Policy 

The names, email addresses and other details collected about visitors and customers of DialToSave are used only for those services requested or to provide support to website visitors.

No information will ever be sold on to third parties or used for any purpose that is not explicitly authorised by the website visitor.

DialToSave uses cookies to create statistics about visitors such as the operating system and browser, the referral page that brought them to DialToSave and their actions on the website. Cookies are not used to collect personally identifiable information.
