# Diamond Legal Information

**Privacy Statement**

Diamond Comic Distributors has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information-gathering and dissemination practices for this site: www.diamondcomics.com.

Currently, Diamond does not conduct surveys or in any other fashion collect information about visitors to its site. This web site does not use cookies or other means of tracking browsers. If this practice changes, this privacy statement will be updated to reflect that change.

Secured areas of our site occasionally use an order form for retail accounts and potential accounts to request information, products and services.
