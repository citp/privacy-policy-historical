# Privacy Policy | Disney Dreaming

DisneyDreaming.com is committed to respecting the privacy of our visitors. 

DisneyDreaming.com collects Personal Information only on a voluntary basis when visitors fill out contest entry forms, comment forms or subscribes to our newsletter. DisneyDreaming.com will never sell this personal information to or share this personal information with any other party.

DisneyDreaming.com would like to remind you to use caution when disclosing your Personal Information in any comments box, forum or chat room. We do our best to screen these comments to keep your Personal Information safe, but it is also up to you to help out by using good judgment when filling out your information.

We also use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please see:[ Consumer Opt Out](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/) and [Google Advertising Privacy](http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/ads/)
