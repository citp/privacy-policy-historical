# Magazine Subscriptions > Privacy Policy

**Our Privacy Policy**  
Our top priority is your privacy. Please read our privacy policy carefully.

**Information We Collect**  
We use the information we collect from you on this website to fulfill the services we advertise on discountmags.com. The information we collect or may collect from you:

Name/Company  
Address  
E-mail Address  
Phone/Fax Number  
Credit/Debit Card Information  
Demographic Information

We may also collect other information relevant to fulfilling any gift orders.
