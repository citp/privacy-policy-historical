# Privacy Policy | Discord Me: Discord Server Discovery

We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, do not hesitate to contact us immediately. 

This Privacy Policy governs the privacy policies and practices of our Website, located at Discord.me. Please read our Privacy Policy carefully and thoroughly as it will guide you into making decisions in sharing your personal information with us. 

* ##### INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Initially, we collect our users’ email address once they sign in to our site. Most of the information that we collect is controlled by the user. The user controls the information that he/she will be providing upon interaction with our site. The information we collect may include any or all of the following: 

  * Discord Username
  * Email Address



The following information will or may be collected automatically by our database and our site:

  * The number of times our users have logged on to the site, your IP address, browser name, internet provider, operating system and computer. 
  * Computer information collected from your interaction with our site and the pages you have visited. 
  * Cookies for us to know which areas of the site you have visited. Cookies can usually be turned on or off at the user’s discretion. Personal information is not usually stored in our cookies. 
  * Tracking tools that measure the performance and features of our site for us to improve and develop our site for our users. The tools are created by other parties outside our jurisdiction. With this, we are not liable nor responsible for the information that has been collected, processed, and protected by them. 


* ##### PROTECTION AND PROCESSING OF INFORMATION

The information that we receive, gather and collect will be used for this Privacy Policy, for our users’ website experience and for internal data analytics. 

Your personal information is protected and maintained in computers that are not situated in the jurisdiction of your location which could contain different privacy laws and policies. 

In compliance with this Privacy Policy we will be responsible in keeping and maintaining the personal information of your users and no transfer shall be made thereof without any security measures and protection of the information. 

We may only transfer your personal information under certain and specific situations that are required by law or in response a public authority like courts, the police and the government. Your information may be transferred should we be involved in a business merger, acquisition or sale. However, we will be posting notices upon the transfer of your information. 

Your personal information is only stored for the period of time that it is needed for the purpose of this policy. The information will only be retained to the extent necessary for our legal obligations, resolved disputes and enforce our legal agreements and policies. 

  

* ##### USERS’ DATA PROTECTION RIGHTS UNDER GENERAL DATA PROTECTION AND REGULATION

In this policy, our site is a Data Controller of our users’ personal information. If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) our legal basis in collecting and using your personal information depends on the extent and scope of the information that we gather and collect. 

You have the right to file a complaint with the Data Protection Authority about our collection and use of your personal information. For more information, please contact your local data protection authority in the European Economic Area (EEA). 

We may process data for the following reasons: 

  * We need to perform a contract with you, such as when you create a Policy with us
  * Consent was freely given
  * The processing is in our legitimate interests and it is not overridden by your rights
  * In compliance with the law



If you are a resident of the (EEA) you have the following data protection rights: 

  * The right to access, update or to delete the personal information we have on you
  * The right of rectification
  * The right of object
  * The right of restriction
  * The right to data portability
  * The right to withdraw consent



We may ask for a verification of your identity should you make requests of any of the following above mentioned.

Our site is not for the use of users below age 13. Our site does not do age verification. We do not hold any liability nor responsibility once a minor has entered our site. But we encourage that they seek parental guidance upon entering the site.
