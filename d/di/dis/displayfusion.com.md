## What information do we collect?

We only collect information from you when you register for the site. If you choose to register, we collect your name and email address. If you choose to remain anonymous, no personally identifiable information is collected about you.

## What do we use your information for?

Information that we collect from you is used to customize the site for you. You can opt-out of receiving any emails from us, and we'll never share your information with anyone.

## How do we protect your information?

We implement a variety of security measures (like database encryption and SSL) to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your personal information.

## Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information.

## Your Consent

By using our site, you consent to our privacy policy.
