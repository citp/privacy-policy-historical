Privacy Statement

Modified on: Tue, May 22, 2018 at 2:53 PM

At Discount Dance Supply, we treat your privacy with utmost respect. This document should answer any questions you may have regarding user privacy and our web site. By visiting our web site you accept and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Statement


Information Collection and Use


Discount Dance Supply is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, rent or give this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. Discount Dance Supply collects information from our users at several different points on our web site.



Information Sharing


We may share aggregated demographic information with our partners and/or advertisers. This is not linked to any personal information that can identify any individual person. Should Discount Dance Supply merge with, acquire or be acquired by another business entity, some or all of your information could be transferred to the other entity. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of Discount Dance Supply may continue to use your information as set forth in this policy.


We use a credit card processing company to bill users for purchases. This company does not retain, share, store or use personally identifiable information for any secondary purpose.


Occasionally we may provide our mailing list to partners that we feel our users would like to receive information from. The information provided will only include your name and mailing address, it will NOT include your telephone number, credit card information, e-mail address, ordering information/habits or any other personally identifiable information. If you do not wish to have your information included in this list we provide an opt-out choice for our customers as detailed below.


Should Discount Dance Supply merge with, acquire or be acquired by another business entity, some or all of your information could be transferred to the other entity. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of Discount Dance Supply may continue to use your information as set forth in this policy.


Link Issues


This web site may contain links to other sites. Please be aware that we, Discount Dance Supply, are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this web site.


Security


This website takes every precaution to protect our users' information. When users submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and off-line. All of your interactions on our site are protected by state of the SSL encryption, you may verify this through options in your browser.


While we use SSL encryption to protect sensitive information online, we also do everything in our power to protect user-information off-line. All of our users' information, not just the sensitive information mentioned above, is restricted in our offices. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, customer service representatives and order processing employees) are granted access to personally identifiable information. All employees are kept up-to-date on our security and privacy practices.


Finally, the servers that store personally identifiable information are kept in a securely locked environment.


If you have any questions about security at our web site, you can write to cs@discountdance.com.


Additional Uses & Choice/Opt-out


Telephone Solicitations Users will never be solicited by telephone for any product offer. They may be contacted by phone if there is a problem or question about an order that they have placed. Also, from time to time, we may phone a small percentage of our customers in order to conduct quality assurance surveys. These surveys are optional and take place only on rare occasions.


Email Solicitations


From time to time we may offer additional email communications (such as a newsletter, special offers, site update notifications, etc); customers may choose not to be included in these e-mail messages by using the customer service section as well as following the instructions included in the e-mail itself.


Choice/Opt-out


Customers will always be provided with an opportunity to opt out of any mailings or telephone calls not directly related to order fulfillment. If you are an existing customer and wish to opt out of any mailings please see our customer service section.


Notification of Changes


If we decide to change our privacy policy, notification will be linked from our homepage to the new policy so that our users are always up-to-date on what information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances (if any) we disclose it. Users will be notified via email and presented with an opportunity to opt-out should we decide to use information in a manner that is inconsistent with the way in which it was collected.
