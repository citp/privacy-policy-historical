Protecting your information is our highest priority task, and we appreciate your trust that we will handle all your information carefully and sensibly. This notice describes our privacy policy. **By visiting uFashionShop.com, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Notice.**

#### **What Personal Information uFashionShop.com Gather?**

The information we learn from customers helps us personalize and continually improve your shopping experience at uFashionShop.com. Here are the types of information we gather. 

  * **Information You Give Us:** We keep any information you enter on our Web site or give us in any other way. We use the information that you provide for such purposes as responding to your requests, customizing future shopping for you, improving our stores, and communicating with you. 
  * **Automatic Information:** We receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with us. For example, like many Web sites, we use "cookies," and we obtain certain types of information when your Web browser accesses uFashionShop.com 



#### **What Is Cookies?**

  
Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser. Cookeis are enable our systems to recognize your browser and to provide features and storage of items in your Shopping Cart. Because cookies allow you to take advantage of some of uFashionShop.com's features, we recommend that you leave them turned on. 

#### Do We Share the Information It Receives?

  
Information about our customers is an important part of our business, and we are not in the business of selling or sharing it to others. 

#### **How Secure Is Information About Me?**

  


  * We secure your information during transmission by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software, which encrypts information you input. 
  * We reveal only the last four digits of your credit card numbers when confirming an order. 
  * Your order is processed by Paypal. 
  * It is important for you to protect against unauthorized access to your password and to your computer. Be sure to sign off when finished using a shared computer. 



#### **Which Information Can I Access?**

  
You can access to a range of information about your account and your interactions with uFashionShop.com Examples of information you can access include recent orders, name, e-mail, password, communications and address book etc..
