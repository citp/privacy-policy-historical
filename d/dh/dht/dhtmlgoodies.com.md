# Privacy Policy

  


## DHTMLgoodies privacy notice

#### Does DHTMLgoodies share information about you?

No, Email addresses, ip addresses and other information you may give us is NOT shared with others. We are not in the business of selling this kind of information. It's very important for us that you are safe when browsing our site and communicating with us.

#### Cookies

DHTMLgoodies.com does not use cookies for standard browsing. Cookies are only used in some of the DHTML scripts in order to make the script work better.

#### IP addresses

IP addresses* are stored in our database for logging(number of visitors, page views etc.) and maintenance purpose only. This is what we store:

  * Your ip address
  * The date your ip address requests a page
  * What page on our site your ip address requests
  * URL of refering page if you were redirected to DHTMLgoodies.com from an external site.



_* All computers connected to internet have an ip address. It's your unique address when browsing the net. It makes it possible for you to send requests and it also makes sure that the information you requests is sent back to you._

#### Google - DoubleClick DART

DHTMLGoodies show ads from Google.

  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on dhtmlgoodies.com
  * Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet. 
  * You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)
