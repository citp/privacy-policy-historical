# Duarte

**Our Commitment To Privacy**

At Duarte Design, Inc., your privacy is extremely important to us.  This notice explains our information practices and privacy policy.

**The Information We Collect And How We Use Information**

We collect personal information as follows:

Personal information which may include your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address, work history and portfolio are collected when you voluntarily submit a resume through this site seeking employment at Duarte Design, Inc.  This information is used solely to facilitate our review of your application for employment.

Personal information which may include your name, address, telephone/fax number, or e-mail address may be collected from you when you voluntarily submit an inquiry or request through this site, or when you sign up for a newsletter and/or workshop.  This information is used solely to respond to any such inquiry or request and to periodically contact you about Duarte Design, Inc. services, workshops, events or other professional items of interest.

**Cookies**

We use cookies on our site in connection with client areas and in connection with contests such as our annual Pumpkin Carving Contest.  Cookies are used solely to improve the contents of our site, and to keep track of users.  For example, cookies are used with our contests to ensure that users only vote once.  We track only the network address of the computer visiting the site.  We do not collect any other personal information by way of cookies.

**Linked Web Sites**

This site may contain links to third party web sites.  Duarte Design, Inc. has no control and assumes no responsibility over the privacy policies of any third party web sites.  You should review the privacy policies of any third party web sites you visit.  The privacy policy stated herein applies solely to the Duarte Design, Inc. web site.

**Our Commitment to Data Security**

To prevent any unauthorized access or disclosure, maintain data accuracy, and to ensure the correct use of personal information, we have instituted policies and procedures to safeguard and secure any information we may collect from you.  We do not sell, share or otherwise disclose your personal information with any third parties.  We will not disclose your personal information to any State institutions or authorities except as required by law.

**How To Access or Correct Your Information**

Visitors to this site may access personal or contact information that we may maintain about them.  In order to obtain this personal or contact information or find out if we hold any information about you, please e-mail us at: [info@duarte.com](mailto:info@duarte.com).  Visitors to this site may correct any personal or contact information that we may maintain about them that is inaccurate.  To request a correction, please e-mail us at: [info@duarte.com](mailto:info@duarte.com).

**How To Contact Us**

You may contact us with any questions regarding our privacy policy by sending an e-mail to: [info@duarte.com](mailto:info@duarte.com).
