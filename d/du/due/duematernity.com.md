Privacy Policy

## Due Privacy Policy & Secure Ordering

We at www.duematernity.com are especially sensitive to the privacy and security of our customers' information. 

We realize that you don't like spam and we don't like it either. From time to time we may send you special offers and freebies from DueMaternity.com or our parent company BlueHighway Inc., however you are welcome to opt out and will be removed permanently if you choose to do so. 

We use the latest SSL encryption technology to secure your order and personal information. We are hosted by Yahoo Stores and therefore offer the most advanced security precautions for ordering online. Credit card information is only transmitted securely to our secure servers and is not stored on our servers.

Welcome to Due Maternity, since 2002 we’ve been fashioning new moms in affordable designer maternity clothes. Due Maternity is an online maternity clothing shop that knows just because your shape has changed doesn't mean your style has. Since 2003 Due Maternity has helped expecting moms buy designer maternity clothes that are both stylish and comfortable. In 2005 Due began to design their own line of affordable fun, flirty pieces to accentuate the feminine figure while giving comfort that you need. Once you see their maternity clothing you'll think twice about raiding your partner's closet for oversized sweatpants and t-shirts. Due Maternity is all about making pregnant women even more beautiful. The full line of designer maternity clothes and accessories includes everything you'll need for the next nine months and beyond. From professional attire to formal wear and swimsuits, Due Maternity boutiques are where fashion-forward women can express themselves. A personal shopper will help you through the buying process, making your shopping experience easier and hassle-free. Shopping for maternity clothes online has never been easier, but if you have any sizing questions, or just want to place a phone order please call 1-866-SHOP-DUE for personal shopping advice
