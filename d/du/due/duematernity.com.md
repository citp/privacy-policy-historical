# Privacy Policy

[](http://smallbusiness.yahoo.com/ecommerce/) [](https://www.scanalert.com/RatingVerify?ref=duematernity.com) This site is hosted by Yahoo! Store. Yahoo! hosts the store pages, ordering system, and order data. Yahoo! automatically collects order information but may only use this information in the aggregate. If you accessed this store through Yahoo! Shopping, Yahoo! also automatically collects information about your shopping experience. Please see the  [ Yahoo! Privacy Policy](http://privacy.yahoo.com/privacy/us/) for more information about how Yahoo! uses this information.

Unlike many maternity websites Due Maternity, inc. does not share, rent or sell your information to any third party companies. Make sure to review the Privacy Policy of any site you choose to do business with, as many choose to sell your personal information to data collection companies. 

* * *

** Privacy Policy/Secure Ordering: ** We at www.duematernity.com are especially sensitive to the privacy and security of our customers' information. We realize that you don't like spam and we don't like it either. From time to time we may send you speical offers and freebies from DueMaternity.com, however you are welcome to opt out and will be removed permanently if you choose to do so. 

We use the latest SSL encryption technology to secure your order and personal information. We are hosted by Yahoo Stores and therefore offer the most advanced security precautions for ordering online. Credit card information is only transmitted securely to our secure servers and is not stored on our servers. 5-1-06
