1. PRIVACY OF YOUR INFORMATION

Ted&Muffy is committed to protecting any personal information that you may provide to us and we believe it is important for you to know how we treat information about you that we may receive from this website. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully as it explains what information Ted&Muffy collects from you and how we use this information, including setting out the circumstances where we could provide it to third parties. You should also read the separate Terms and Conditions of Sale here.

What information do we collect?

• Should you choose to take advantage of some of our services and or to buy any of our products, we will collect personal information about you during this process, such as your email address, name and address, and credit card details.

• Should you choose to register to receive Ted&Muffy marketing emails, we will collect your email address.

• Should you choose to take part in Ted&Muffy competitions, prize draws or questionnaires/surveys we will collect your name, email address and mailing address, and we will also collect the answers to all questions asked

• Should you provide a friend’s email address via our ‘Tell a Friend’ feature, we will collect your name and email address, and the addresses and message that you input, solely to deliver this information to the intended recipients. This information will not be used for any other purpose.

• Even if you do not register or buy with us, we may also collect, and our third party providers of advertisements and content may collect, information about where you are on the internet (e.g. the website address you came from, IP address, domain types) and technical information such as your browser type and the pages of the Ted&Muffy website you viewed during your visit.

• Like most websites, our servers automatically record the page requests made when you visit our sites. These “server logs” typically include your web request, IP address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.

What do we do with the information we collect?


• All of the information we collect will be stored on secure servers, and treated as completely confidential.

• We will use the information which we collect to help us to administer our website, improve the usefulness and effectiveness of our website, to personalise your experience on our website, to help you log on in future and to continue to use our website. We may also use the information we collect to deliver appropriate content or advertising to you on the Ted&Muffy website or third party websites. By knowing your preferences we can deliver relevant information that meets your needs and ensures that your visit to our site is quick, easy and productive. We use a technology known as cookies to manage this process. For more information on how we use cookies, click here.

• We will use your personal details to process your order, respond to your enquiries, and inform you of the status of your order.

• Only if you have given express consent to allow us to do so when registering or buying with us, we may use your information to communicate with you through post, sms or email to notify you of new products, competitions, special offers and relevant service information. If you have subscribed to our emails in the past and would prefer not to receive emails from tedandmuffy.com in the future, simply click on the unsubscribe link that can be found at the bottom of all weekly emails to opt out. (UK Ted&Muffy mail order: +44 (0)345 070 5588).

• We may pass your personal information to third parties who help us to maintain, administer or develop our website, or help us to provide the products available on our website. If so, that information is only given to those third parties for that limited purpose.

• If you provide express permission for us to do so when signing up for your account on the Ted&Muffy website, we may use your data, or permit selected third parties to use your data, to provide you with information about products and services which may be of interest to you and we or they may contact you about these by post, phone or email. If you do not provide this permission at the point of account creation or purchase, or in your account settings at a later date, we will never use your data in this way. If we do disclose personal information to third parties, we will only do so to third parties whom we are satisfied have measures to protect your personal information in a manner consistent with this Privacy Policy.

• Reputable third-party banking and distribution institutions handle our payment transactions and order delivery. They receive the information needed to verify and authorise your payment for purchases made on this website, and to process and deliver your order. All such organisations are under strict obligation to keep your personal information private, and will only ever use your information for the above purposes.

• You should be aware that if we are requested by the police or any other regulatory or government authority investigating suspected illegal activities to provide your Personal Information and /or User Information, we are entitled do so.

• When entering any of our competitions or prize draws, you provide your name, email address, mailing address and answers to 10 ‘profiling questions’ which are designed to provide you with a relevant and personalised experience with the Ted&Muffy website and emails. If you win the competition or prize draw, we will send the prize to the address entered and notify you by email. When you enter the competition or prize draw, you are also included in our marketing email list to receive promotions, new products, and new additions to the website. You may unsubscribe from this subscription list by following the instructions in any email received.

• When providing a friend’s email address via our ‘Tell a Friend’ feature, we will collect your name and email address, and the addresses and message that you input, solely to deliver this information to the intended recipients. This information will not be used for any other purpose.

• As Ted&Muffy is an international company, there may be occasions where information may be transferred from one country to another.

2. TRANSACTION SECURITY

Ted&Muffy respects the privacy of its customers and is committed to complying with all laws relating to the protection of personal data.

To prevent unauthorised access, we follow appropriate security procedures in the storage and disclosure of personal information which you have given to us.

Certain areas of our website are password protected. You can help to preserve your privacy by never sharing your password with anyone else, or by using a secure password which contains a mixture of lower and upper case letters, along with numbers.

Our order processing environment features several widely accepted and endorsed security methods to ensure that transactions, ordering and fulfilment are entirely safe and that all exchange of information remain confidential. Credit cards details are processed through separate merchant services vendors. We encrypt your credit card information to ensure your orders remain private and protected as they travel across the Internet.

We accept orders only from web browsers that permit communication through Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology. This means you cannot inadvertently place an order through an unsecured connection.

You can tell whether a page is secure as 'https' will replace the 'http' at the front of www.tedandmuffy.com in your browser address window.

To view our security certificate click here (opens a new window).

Your payment details are held in an encrypted format until we process your order. If you have any questions regarding our credit card security policies please contact us at enquiries@tedandmuffy.com.

 

3. COOKIES


How Ted&Muffy uses cookies

Our website, as with many others, attempts to place a small data file (known as a “cookie”) onto your computer for several purposes, including in order to expedite your navigation throughout the website or to enable you to return to a purchase if disconnected, to record user preferences, to record your usage of our website in order to improve the site, or to serve content tailored specifically for you. We may use cookies to store your personal information for promotional or marketing purposes. We use cookies to improve the quality of our service.

Ted&Muffy will never disclose any information gathered from cookies to any third parties. All data passed by cookies is anonymous and will never contain your name, address, telephone number, email address, IP address or any payment details.

If you prefer not to accept cookies, you can set your browser to reject them or to alert you before one is placed. The Help menu on the menu bar of most browsers will tell you how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie and how to disable cookies altogether. You can also get useful information on what cookies are and how to control your browser’s cookie settings at the following website: http://www.aboutcookies.org ( Please note that we're not responsible for the content of external websites).

Third party cookies

Ted&Muffy uses cookies to help third party advertisers and publishers serve and manage Ted&Muffy -related adverts across the web. In this way, we use cookies to display a particular advert to a particular visitor based on that visitor’s past browsing activity on the Ted&Muffy website.

Ted&Muffy works with third party ad servers and ad networks who serve Ted&Muffy advertisements on our behalf. Ted&Muffy will not disclose your personal details to any third parties. All data collected is anonymous and will not contain your name, address, telephone number, email address or IP address.

These third parties may place anonymous cookies in your browser so that they can collect browsing data (such as the products you have viewed). These cookies will allow the third party to serve advertisements containing products that you have looked at and similar products.

Advertisements will always include a link to the third party privacy policy page that will include information for users on how to opt-out of being cookie retargeted. If you wish to opt out of receiving such advertisements, or if you would like to receive more information on why you are being retargeted, simply click the 'i' which appears in the top left hand corner of every advertisement.

Another way that you can opt out of behavioural advertising cookies is to go to the Network Advertising Initiative website and opt out of any active cookies that you can see are stored on your computer - http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp ( Please note that we're not responsible for the content of external websites).

For more detail on the third party ad networks with whom we currently work, please see our full cookie policy here.

Ted&Muffy ’s cookie policy

For more detail about what cookies are, Ted&Muffy ’s own cookie policy, what cookies Ted&Muffy uses, what third party cookies Ted&Muffy employs, information about how to manage cookies on your computer, and information on how Ted&Muffy uses cookies to deliver banner advertising on other websites, please see our full cookie policy here.
