Your right to privacy and how we protect it

At Duke University Press, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect about you to send you information about new releases and to learn from you about what you want us to put on our site. Read on for more details about our privacy policy.

What do we collect? How do we use it?

  • When you sign up for our e-mail updates, we need to know your e-mail address. This lets us notify you about changes and upgrades to our site.
  • When you sign up for e-mail updates, we may ask for your name, although you can choose not to give it to us. We will use your name in order to personalize any communication we have with you.
  • When you sign up, we may ask for your zip or country code, although you can choose not to provide it to us. We will use your zip or country code in order to better understand the demographics of our readers.
  • We may also use the information we collect to occasionally notify you about important changes to the Web site, new online services, and special offers we think you'll find valuable. If you would rather not receive this information, please send an e-mail to notify@dukeupress.edu. Make sure to list each e-mail address you have registered with us.

Will Duke University Press disclose the e-mail information it collects to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal e-mail information to others. However, we may provide aggregate statistics about our customers, traffic patterns, and related site information to reputable third-party vendors, but these statistics will include no personally identifying information.

Duke University Press uses WebTrends Live to analyze traffic to this Web site.

WebTrends Live does not create individual profiles for visitors. Unlike some tracking services, WebTrends Live does not have a database of individual profiles for each visitor. WebTrends Live only collects aggregate data. For more information about WebTrends Live's privacy policy, please click here.

In summary

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal e-mail information to others. If you never want to receive any announcements or special offers from us, send us an e-mail at notify@dukeupress.edu. Remember to list each of the e-mail accounts you have given us.

Your consent

By using our Web site, you consent to our collection and use of this information. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
