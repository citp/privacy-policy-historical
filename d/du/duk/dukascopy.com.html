We automatically log personal data, but we do not link information automatically logged by other means with personal data about specific individuals.

A cookie is a small data file that web-sites write to your hard disk for record keeping purposes. We use cookies to store data, but we do not link non-personal information stored in cookies with personal data about specific individuals. The use of cookies does not restrict user’s right to privacy in any way.
