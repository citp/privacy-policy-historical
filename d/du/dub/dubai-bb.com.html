Dubai Bulletin Board Privacy Policy


At Dubai-bb.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines what types of personal information is received and collected while you browse our website.

Advertising
Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to Dubai-bb.com or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to Dubai-bb.com users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

Log Files
Like many other Web sites, Dubai-bb.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons
On some pages, Dubai-bb.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

Links
Dubai-bb.com may contain links to other web sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these other web sites. We suggest our users to be aware of this when they leave Dubai-bb.com and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by our website.
