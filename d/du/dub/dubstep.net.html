Analysis Tools and our use of Cookies:

We may use cookies and other similar technologies to optimise your experience on the Website. Cookies can be used to recognise users when they return and may help us to customise the Website for you based on your preferences. Cookies may also be used to identify the user’s geographic location and enable us to restrict particular services to users in certain countries. Although they do identify a user’s Device, cookies do not personally identify users, and passwords and credit card information are not stored in cookies.

To ensure you get the best from the Website and to help us improve it and our products, offers and marketing strategy, your Device will need to accept cookies. If it doesn’t, you may not be able to use all the interactive features of the Website including certain personalised features.
