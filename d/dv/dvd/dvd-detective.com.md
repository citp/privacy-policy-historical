# Digital Drake - Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us here at [**DigitalDrake.com**](http://www.digitaldrake.com/). We don't collect a lot of personally identifiable data about you, and what we do collect is used only for our internal purposes. We will not share any of your personal information with third parties unless we explicitly ask your permission to share it at the time you give us the information. All of your information is kept strictly confidential.  


Please read the following summary of how we use your information. We may change this policy from time to time without advance notification, so please check back from time to time.

**Our Web Site**

We only collect information from you if you sign up for the **Digital Drake News newsletter**.Â  If you sign up for our newsletter, we will use your email address to send you periodic mailings. When you sign up for the newsletter, you also have the option to opt-in for special offers from our partners. If you do, your email will also be used to send you those offers as they are made available. You may [opt-out](https://web.archive.org/web/20021017124456id_/http%3A//www.digitaldrake.com/unsubscribe.html) of receiving future newsletter mailings at any time.

Our web servers also record standard HTTP information like your IP address and your referrer. This information is not personally identified to you, and is only used to optimize our web site performance and plan our future web site features.

**Our Products**

We are software developers and create various kinds of useful utilities. None of our products are used to gather information about you for the resale to email brokers. 

We are affiliated with DivX.com and when you purchase the **DivX Pro** software, DivX.com collects your personal information but does not store your credit card information to complete the purchase. The personal information you give DivX.com during the purchase transaction is used only for our records.

Thanks for visiting. We at **DigitalDrake.com** value our own privacy, so we work hard to protect yours as well. If you ever have any questions, please feel free to write us at [privacy@digitaldrake.com](mailto:privacy@digitaldrake.com).

Sincerely,  
**The Digital Drake Team**
