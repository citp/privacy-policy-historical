# Privicy Policy

As a general rule, this website does not collect Personal Information about you when you visit the site. You can generally visit the site without revealing the Personal Information, unless you choose to provide such information. Any personal information collected shall be used only for the stated purpose and shall NOT be shared with any other department/organization (public/private).

This site may contain links to non-governmental sites whose data protection and privacy practices may differ from ours. We are not responsible for the content and privacy practices of these other websites and encourage you to consult the privacy notices of those sites.
