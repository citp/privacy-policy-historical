Certain non-personal information about Site visits is recorded by the standard operation of the Site. iAmplify users are always free to decline cookies, but in doing so they may not be able to use certain features of the Site. The "help" segment of the toolbar on most browsers explains how to configure a browser to not accept new cookies, how to have the browser inform a user when they receive a new cookie, and how to erase cookies from their hard drives.
