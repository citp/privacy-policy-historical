## Privacy Statement for www.ironmaiden.com

**Iron Maiden.com Limited** has created this **Privacy Statement** in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: www.ironmaiden.com. 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information.

This site contains links to other sites. **Iron Maiden.com Limited** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites, including those linked through Maiden Central..

**Megastore:** this site uses an order form for customers to purchase products. We collect visitor's contact information (e.g. e-mail address). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders and information about Iron Maiden to our customers. The customer's contact information is also used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

**Our online surveys** ask visitors for contact information (e.g. email address) and demographic information (e.g.zip code, age group or sex). We use contact data from our surveys to send the user information about Iron Maiden. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. We use this data to tailor our visitor's experience at our site, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences. 

We run **contests** on our site in which we ask visitors for contact information (e.g. email address) and demographic information (e.g. zip code or age). We use contact data from our contests to send users information about Iron Maiden and promotional material from some of our partners. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. 

We use a 3rd party advertising agency (TVMV) to display banner ads on our site. Banner ads may contain cookies, and appear to be coming from our site, but in reality they are coming from our advertising agency partners, who are serving ads on our site. Our site does not use cookies. We do not have access to any information about you from cookies you might receive while visiting our site.

**Public Forums:** This site makes chat rooms and message boards available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. 

**Security** : This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

' **JointheList' e-mail service:** e-mail addresses submitted to our 'mailing list' are collected and managed by Probe Media Limited, a fully owned subsidiary of Sanctuary Facilities Ltd. We use contact data from our mailing list ('Join the List') to send users information about Iron Maiden and promotional material from some of our partners.(e.g. other artists managed by Sanctuary Music Management).

**Probe Media,** on our behalf, will include on all e-mails the option for customers to be removed from the database. Probe Media holds customer data on a secure server with no remote connections. Internet and e-mail connections are via a physically separate system. Back-ups of our database are kept in a fireproof safe. Registered in England. Registered No. 3050388. Probe Media is a member of the Direct Marketing Association and is registered with the Data Protection Registrar. Registered No. R09600066.

**Children's Guidelines:** This site collects online contact information from people under 18 years of age without prior parental consent or parental notification and collects personally identifiable offline contact information without prior parental consent. The bulletin board and chat room of this site gives persons under the age of 18 the ability to publicly post or distribute personally identifiable contact information without prior parental consent. 

**Choice/Opt-Out** : This site gives customers the opportunity to **opt-out** of receiving communications from us and our partners via our e-mailing list. 

We also give customers the following options for removing their information from our database and to no longer receive our service.
