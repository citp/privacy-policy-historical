# Ranks.com - Privacy Policy

**Privacy Statement for Ranks.com, LLC.  **  

Our goal is to make the Ranks.com directory the most effective it can be. One way we accomplish this is by sending to users additional information about our service. Submission of contact information is completely voluntary and not required to use any of the Ranks.com directory. The following discloses further details about our information gathering and dissemination practices.

**Information Collection and Use:**

Ranks.com’s registration forms and online surveys require users to give us contact information such as name and email address. We use customer contact information for marketing purposes such as sending out newsletters, new website features, and special features. The customer's contact information is also used to send the user details about our company and to contact the visitor when necessary. Additionally, we may use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests.

**Links to Outside Websites:**

Ranks.com is an internet search directory that contains links to thousands of websites containing various types of content and services. Ranks.com is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such websites. Users assume responsibility for reviewing and understanding the privacy and security policies of sites listed with Ranks.com.

**Children’s Guidelines:**

Prior to submitting any information online, children (users under the age of 13) should get the consent of a parent. Ranks.com abides by the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act in that we do not require children to disclose more information than is reasonably necessary to participate in any activity.

**Removal and Corrections:**

Users may remove their information from our database and cancel all future communications by sending an email to [remove@ranks.com](mailto:remove@ranks.com). All corrections, updates, or modifications of previously submitted contact information should be directed to [admin@ranks.com](mailto:admin@ranks.com). 

**Contact Us:**

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this website, please contact us:

By Email: [admin@ranks.com](mailto:admin@ranks.com)
