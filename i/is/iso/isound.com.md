### Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is taken seriously at dreamGEAR.net.

The data we collect is used to give us demographic information about the entire dreamGEAR.net audience to improve the dreamGEAR.net service and help us sell the advertising that supports the site. The personal information you provide lets us inform you about updates to the service and notify you of the products and services of dreamGEAR.net.

dreamGEAR.net will not give out your personal information to any third parties unless authorized. If at any time you would no longer like to take part in the e-mail program, simply e-mail us via the address that is provided at the bottom of any e-mail that we send to you with the UN-SUBSCRIBE command and you will be taken off of the list within 24-48 hours.
