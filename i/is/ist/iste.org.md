# ISTE | Privacy Policy

### ISTE Privacy Policy Notice

ISTE is sincerely committed to protecting the privacy of our membership, as well as all others who come to our Web site in search of Educational Technology resources and information. In order to better serve our constituency, we may collect certain types of data from those who visit the Web site:

>   * **IP Addresses and Server Information**. We collect this to help monitor the performance of our systems, so that we can continue to improve our visitors’ experience.
>   * **Personal Information**. We may request contact and other personal information in order to fulfill transactions, such as ISTE membership, the purchase of goods or services, registration for events, or to respond to requests for information. We may also use your contact information to market ISTE products and services. We reserve the right to share certain membership information with qualified third-party vendors for offerings that we determine to be of interest to our members. If you'd rather not receive those offers, you may ask to be removed from any or all lists not directly related to our membership services by contacting our list manager:
