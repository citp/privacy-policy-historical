Our Commitment To Privacy

Your privacy is very important to us. In order for us to display our firm commitment to your privacy, we are providing this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and across the entire site with a link.
The Internet is constantly developing and we reserve the right to modify this privacy policy in the future. By continuing to use the web site after the posting of such changes, you are deemed to accept the privacy policy with all of its modifications.
On some pages, you can subscribe to receive materials.
The types of personal information collected at these pages are:
Name
Address
Email address
Phone number

Surveys and Contests

Occasionally, our site will request information from users via surveys or contests. Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary and the user can choose whether or not to send in their information. Information requested may include contact information (such as name and shipping address), and demographic information (such as zip code, age level). Survey information will be used for purposes of monitoring or improving the use and satisfaction of this site.

How We Use Information

We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website, or that Y number of men and Z number of women filled out our registration form, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals.
Using the e-mail addresses provided on registrations, surveys, and contests, IsraelNationalNews.com will occasionally send promotional e-mail to its subscribers about services offered by our selected advertisers. All advertisers are carefully screened for suitability before we send any of their advertisements to our readership.
Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online to send unwanted e-mail. For any list that you subscribe to we also provide you an opportunity to opt-out with every e-mail that is sent to you.

IP Address

We use your IP address solely to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. For example, your IP address will be used for us to gather broad demographic information of our readership.

Links to Other Sites

This site contains links to other sites. IsraelNationalNews.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of such Web sites.

Third Party Advertising

IsraelNationalNews.com works with third-party advertising companies in order to serve ads when you visit our Web site and also to deliver email advertisements that may be of interest to you. These companies may use information about your visits in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites or via email about goods and services that may be of interest to you.

Third Party Cookies

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.

Our Commitment To Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. However, since data transmission over the Internet cannot be guaranteed to be 100% secure, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or from our online products or services, and you do so at your own risk. Once we receive your transmission, we make our best effort to ensure its security on our systems.

Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13.

Questions or Concerns

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, send us an email at info@IsraelNationalNews.com.
