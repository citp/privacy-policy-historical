**Itz Cash Card Privacy Principles:**

Our core businesses include technology enabled services i.e. “ItzCash Card” a unique payment instrument available for Indian consumer, which is safe and convenient to transact with. It is a smart option for all, and the safest online and on–mobile transaction instrument that allows you to be in control of your spending. ItzCash card is a full usage product which empowers you to do online transactions at any time and from anywhere. Through our portfolio of varied online services, spanning from Shopping to Donations, from Travel booking to Insurance, from Utility Bill Payments to Mobile Top-ups, we plan to add new dimensions to the business of retailing. These are confidential matters and Itz Cash Card and its affiliates recognize your expectations regarding privacy policy of Itz Cash Card Limited. Itz Cash Card Limited is committed to keep your information confidential and secure and that is why we have designed procedures to ensure your privacy in all facets of our relationship. No matter if you are our Merchant, business partner, franchise, backend user, a consumer, and a customer you are protected, not only by the applicable privacy laws, but, more importantly, commitment of Itz Cash Card Limited to safeguard your privacy and your right to decide how your personal information will be used.

**Scope of Privacy Policy:**

This Privacy Policy covers treatment of personally identifiable information collected by Itz Cash Card Limited from our Merchants and end users who use the ItzCash Card payment gateway services, as well as Merchants’ information that we have tie-up and acquire in the course of our business. This Policy also covers the treatment of personally identifiable information that our back end technology service providers / partners may share with us.

This Policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Itz Cash Card Limited does not own or control or to people that Itz Cash Card Limited does not employ or manage. This Policy is specific to Itz Cash Card Limited and does not apply to other Web sites, links or Internet services that are part of or to be part of the Network of Itz Cash Card Limited.

**What Personally Identifiable Information is Collected by ItzCash Card?**

ITZ CASH CARD collects and stores information collected from our customers, franchises and end users who use the ItzCash Card payment gateway services, as well as Merchants, business partners,  that we have tied-up and acquired in the course of our business and some visitor information regarding usage of this website, such as date and time of day you access our website, browser type, browser language, the Internet Protocol (IP) address of the computer you are using, number of hits, the pages visited, previous/subsequent sites visited and length of user session. Itz Cash Card Limited does not collect any unique information about you (such as your name, e-mail address, etc.) except when you specifically and know Itz Cash Card Limited only provide such information.

**Objective:**

  1. Provide consumers notice of our information practices.
  2. Offer consumers choices as to how their information is used beyond the purpose for which the information was provided.
  3. Offer consumers reasonable access to their information and an opportunity to correct inaccuracies.
  4. Take reasonable steps to protect the security and integrity of personal information.



Itz Cash Card Limited respects your privacy and wants to promote trust and confidence on the internet. As such, Itz Cash Card Limited feels it is necessary to disclose to you what information this site collects and tracks and what we do with the information that we collect.

Information security is critical to our business. We work to protect the security of your information during transmission by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software, which encrypts information you input. We store information gathered on secure computers. It is important for you to protect against unauthorized access to your Login ID/password and to your computer. Be sure to sign off when finished using a shared computer and otherwise protect the password used to access the services of Itz Cash Card Limited.

Currently, Itz Cash Card Limited collects information of our customers, franchises and end users who use the ItzCash Card payment gateway services, as well as Merchants, business partners, that we have tied-up and acquired in the course of our business. Itz Cash Card Limited asks merchants and customers to supply various personal data when placing an order, such as name and address, telephone number, and E-mail address. Itz Cash Card Limited uses this data only for the purpose of filling an order, or communicating with the customer or Merchants about the status of an order. The data so collected is used in the aggregate to give us demographic information about the entire services of Itz Cash Card Limited in order to improve the said service to you, to perform statistical analysis of the collective characteristics and behavior of our Merchants, customers/users and visitors, to measure overall patron demographics and interests regarding specific areas of the itzcash.com domain and to analyze how and where to use our resources. We use cookies to help us determine which sites users’ link to, and which services merchants and visitors use.

We also track information like the domain name from which you contact us, the pages you request, the products you buy, the referring site, and the time that you spend on the site. For everyone who visits our website, Sub-Merchant purchase page, we log IP addresses, the type of operating system that your computer uses, and the type of browser software used by you. From the IP address, we can determine the Internet Service Provider and the geographic location of your point of connectivity.

If you are our Merchant then the personal information you provide – name, address, etc.-allows us to inform you about updates, or changes to the service and to notify you of products and services of Itz Cash Card Limited, its backend service providers and affiliates that we believe may be of interest to you.
