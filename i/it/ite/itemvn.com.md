## Personal Information

|  | 

**Browsing Itemvn** You can listen to music on Itemvn without having an Itemvn Account. You also can contact us by emailing us directly without having to register for an account.

**Your Itemvn Account.** For some activities on Itemvn, like uploading music, posting comments, you need an Itemvn Account. We ask for some personal information when you create an account, including your email address and a password, which is used to protect your account from unauthorized access.

**Usage Information.** When you use Itemvn, we may record information about your usage of the site, such as the music you listen, the frequency and size of data transfers, and information you display about yourself as well as information you click on in Itemvn (including UI elements, settings). If you are logged in, we may associate that information with your Itemvn Account. In order to ensure the quality of our service to you, we may place a tag (also called a "web beacon") in HTML-based customer support emails or other communications with you in order to confirm delivery.

**Content Uploaded to Site.** Any personal information or music content that you voluntarily disclose online (e.g., music comments, your profile page) may be collected and used by others.

## Uses

| 

If you submit personal information to Itemvn, we may use that information to operate, maintain, and improve the features and functionality of Itemvn, and to process any flagging activity or other communication you send to us.

We do not use your email address or other personal information to send commercial or marketing messages without your consent. We may use your email address without further consent for non-marketing or administrative purposes (such as notifying you of major Itemvn changes or for customer service purposes).

We use cookies, web beacons, and log file information to: (a) store information so that you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or the next time you visit Itemvn; (b) provide custom, personalized content and information; (c) monitor the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns; (d) monitor aggregate metrics such as total number of visitors and pages viewed; and (e) track your entries, submissions, and status in promotions, sweepstakes, and contests.

## Information That is Publicly Available

| 

When you create an Itemvn Account, some information about your Itemvn Account and your account activity will be provided to other users of Itemvn. This may include the date you opened your Itemvn Account, the date you last logged into your Itemvn Account, your age, the country and the number of songs you have listened.

Your Itemvn Account name, not your email address, is displayed to other users when you engage in certain activities on Itemvn, such as when you upload music.

Any music that you submit to Itemvn may be redistributed through the internet and other media channels, and may be listened by other Itemvn users or the general public.

You may also choose to add personal information which may include your name, gender, profile picture or other details, that will be visible to other users on your Itemvn Account page. If you choose to add certain features to your Itemvn Account page, then these features and your activity associated with these features will be displayed to other users and may be aggregated and shared with your friends or other users. Such shared activity may include your favorite music, songs you rated and songs that you have uploaded.

## Your Choices

| 

If you have an Itemvn Account, you may update or correct your personal profile information, email preferences and privacy settings at any time by visiting your account profile page.

You may, of course, decline to submit personal information through Itemvn, in which case you can still listen to music and explore Itemvn, but Itemvn may not be able to provide certain services to you.
