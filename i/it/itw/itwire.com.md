The ITWire website and online newsletter, ITWire Update, are wholly owned by ITWire Pty.Ltd.. ITWire carries information, news, views and opinions about people, companies and organisations in the Australian IT & T business sector and also carries advertisements for companies their products and services.

This policy applies only to The ITWire site (www.iTWire.com.au) and the ITWireUpdate newsletter. ITWire publishes this privacy statement as a demonstration of its commitment to ensuring the confidentiality and privacy of personal information supplied to us by subscribers. 

What Type of Information ITWire collects

When you register with ITWire you provide us with your name, email address, occupation and position. If you do register to receive ITWire emails or other information we might choose to send you from time to time, information about your interest may be collected and stored. This information is recorded on the servers of our webmaster and used by ITWire for purposes of targeting advertising, learning more about your interests and to allow us to keep you updated with the latest news and information. Readers of ITWire and users of the website have the option to opt out or deregister themselves and to discontinue any services we provide, including weekly update emails sent to the IP address provided by you.

How ITWire uses the information we collect and/or which is supplied to us

ITWire may use information it collects from you, as a registered visitor to the site, or as a registered reader of the ITWire Update newsletter, for the primary purpose for which it is collected and for other secondary purposes that are related to the primary purpose of collection. ITWire uses personal information to:  
∑ Help us gain an understanding of what type of readers are visiting our site, the work they do, their position within the company or organisation   
∑ Assist us in understanding what sections of the site or newsletter users are attracted to, the frequency of their visits and their usage of the site or newsletter   
∑ Allows us to respond directly to user interests and helps our advertisers understand the audience they are reaching  
We may also make use of registered users IP addresses to help analyse problems with our server, to administer our website and to gather broad demographic information. When visiting our website, a record of the user's visit is logged. Information supplied by the user's browser may include the server address, operating system, the date and time of the visit, pages accessed and the type of browser used. We do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable.

How ITWire deals with sensitive information

ITWire may use and disclose sensitive information only for the purpose for which it is provided or a directly related secondary purpose or as allowed by law unless you have agreed otherwise

Accessing & updating your information

You can use our website to correct/update your email address at any time. You can update the information provided by you to ITWire at any time when online by sending the new information to [editor@iTWire.com.au](mailto:editor@iTWire.com.au)

Management and security of personal information

ITWire does not share any of your personally identifiable information with any other person or entity

Disclosure

We may disclose your personal information to external service providers that provide services for the purposes of our business, on a confidential basis, for example, including but not limited to representatives of any agency or company we use to solicit advertising for the site, those companies or organisations that may choose to advertise with us, and our IT support providers. However, no party is ever allowed direct access to the registered users' personal information held by ITWire.
