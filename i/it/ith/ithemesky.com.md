# Privacy Policy - iThemeSky.com

Your privacy is important to iThemeSky, as is your trust in iThemeSky services. iThemeSky does not divulge or sell your information to any 3rd party unless under legal order to do so. This privacy policy outlines how your information is processed and used here.

#### Information Collection

During the contact process, iThemeSky requires customers to supply necessary information such as their names, emails, project information, etc. We collect only necessary information so customers can be contacted for business or informational purposes and payments verified. We may send you email notifications or otherwise contact you about our new or existing products, services and special offers.

Every visitor to our website(s) is tracked for statistic purposes and data such as IP address, browser version, screen resolution and ISP hostname is collected. This information is used for analysis of webstats and is never made available to anyone outside the team in any way, shape or form. IP logging also enables iThemeSky to handle abuse such as spamming or service rule violations. All comments posted on the site are IP logged for abuse protection.

There might be some chance for you to publish more personal information while you intend to interact with other users on iThemeSky website(s) (e.g. writing a comment), by choosing to publish these personal information, you understand and agree that anyone may access and forward any information about you that is contained in or that you posted to those areas, and iThemeSky is not responsible for that.

#### Cookies

When you use some online services of iThemeSky, a cookie text file may be stored on your local computer for sessions only. This enables you to remain logged in while using the services. iThemeSky does not track your browsing habits with its cookies.

Cookies are small files which many websites transfer to your hard disk. They can inform the website what pages you visit, and your preferences, which enable websites to provide you with a more personalized service and experience. You may set your browser to refuse cookies, or to warn you before accepting them.

#### Outside Links

Our website(s) may contain links to third-party websites not controlled by iThemeSky or covered by this Privacy Policy. It is recommended that you check the privacy statement of other websites you visit before providing any personal information, and we are not responsible for their privacy policies or how they treat information about their users.

#### Disclaimers

This website contains general information that is intended, but not guaranteed, to be correct, complete, up-to-date or error free. iThemeSky uses reasonable precautions to keep all personal information provided secure. However, we are not responsible for any breach of security which is out of our control or for any actions of any third parties that receive the information.

#### Payments

iThemeSky does not store credit card details in any way, shape or form. All customers are advised to check the website of our payment processors and read their privacy policy and terms and conditions.

#### Changes To Privacy Policy

iThemeSky reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time we see fit and without notice.

#### Privacy Inquiries

Please feel free to [contact us](http://blog.ithemesky.com/contact-us) for any further information or questions about this page.
