# Privacy Policy — IG Hoot

Your credentials are stored on our databases until it expires, these credentials are used for liking, commenting and following purposes. We do not store any of your information except your credentials, and we maintain the security of the website to prevent disclosures of information. Your credentials will never be used for other purposes aside from liking, following and commenting. We guarantee the privacy and security of every users of this autoliker.

We store cookies on your browser to save the authentication information for this website to use the autoliker.

We DO NOT and WILL NOT share, sell, or give your information to other sites.
