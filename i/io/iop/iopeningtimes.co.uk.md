# Opening Times - Privacy Policy

iOpeningTimes may collect registration information, contact and customer requirement information, and other information (including e-mail addresses) on this Web Service. This is done to help us provide our customers with the best customer service and with valuable information regarding relevant products and services from iOpeningTimes and appropriate third parties.

iOpeningTimes is dedicated to protecting personal information and will make every reasonable effort to handle collected information appropriately. All information collected, as well as related requests, will be handled as carefully and efficiently as possible in accordance with iOpeningTimes standards for integrity and objectivity.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
