                                            Privacy Policy
Thank you for visiting illinoisproperty.com, the authorized website of RE/MAX Northern
Illinois (the “Website”). RE/MAX Northern Illinois has established a network of independently
owned and operated offices providing real estate services in the northern Illinois region. The
Website contains websites of these independently owned and operated offices and the websites
of their sales associates as well as links to 3rd party sites. Your privacy is important to us and we
know you care about how your information is handled so this document is provided as an
explanation. This Website Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy”) describes the privacy practices
with respect to information collected by or submitted to us on our Website. This Privacy Policy
is made a part of the RE/MAX Website Terms of Use (“Terms"). This Privacy Policy explains
our online information practices and the choices you can make about the collection and use of
the personally identifiable information you submit at our Website.
When you register or provide personal information anywhere on the illinoisproperty.com website
(or any of the other domains or sub domains) this information will be distributed to one or more
of the RE/MAX independently owned offices or their sales associates so they can better provide
real estate service and the information and data you requested both through the Website and in
person if you visit a RE/MAX office. Each office or sales associate may or may not have
specific privacy policies that they adhere to, and this document does not cover the practices of
those independently owned and operated RE/MAX offices or their sales associates. If you have
questions about how a specific office or sales associate may handle your personal information,
we suggest that you contact them using the information provided on the Website and request a
copy of their privacy policy.
By visiting our Website, you acknowledge and agree that you have read this Policy and agree to
the collection, use and other processing of your information in accordance with the practices
described in this Policy. If you do not agree to all of the terms, conditions and practices set forth
herein, do not access the Website or any of its pages.
Information Collection
To fulfill your requests for RE/MAX products, services or information, enable you to participate
in our Website offerings, and to provide you with opportunities to learn about other products and
services in which we think you might be interested, we ask that you provide us with certain
personally identifiable information. The types of personally identifiable information that may be
collected at our Website include but are not limited to: name, address, e-mail address, telephone
and fax number and information about your interests in and use of various products, programs,
and services.

Non-personally identifiable information
We also may collect certain non-personally identifiable information when you visit many of our
Website pages, such as the type of browser you are using (e.g., Internet Explorer, Mozilla,
Netscape), the type of operating system you are using, (e.g., Windows XP, Windows 2000 or
Mac OS), and the domain name of your Internet service provider (e.g., America Online,
Earthlink).
Information Use
We use information collected at our Website to communicate with users to provide requested
products, information, or services, to contact them (for example, by e-mail or phone) regarding
their account, use of our Website or changes to our Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, or other
policies or terms that affect them and their use of our Website.
We may also use personally identifiable information to provide users with additional
opportunities to learn about products or service we believe may be of interest to them.
As noted above, to provide you with your requested services your information is shared with
independently owned and operated RE/MAX® offices and their sales associates. This Privacy
Policy does not cover the practices of these independently owned and operated offices or their
sales associates, including the sharing and handling of data in these offices or by these sales
associates. For more information on the practices of a particular office or sales associate, please
consult with the particular office or sales associate with which or whom you are dealing. We
may also share information collected at the site with service providers to assist us in providing
our services. These service providers are required to protect this information in a manner that is
consistent with this Privacy Policy and may not use the information for any purpose other than to
carry out the services they are performing for us.
We also may disclose personally identifiable information collected at this site in the following
instances:
        Legal Action: We may disclose information about our users, including personally
        identifiable information, when legally required to do so, at the request of governmental
        authorities conducting an investigation, to verify or enforce compliance with the policies
        governing our Website and applicable laws, or to protect against misuse or unauthorized
        use of our Website. We also may disclose user information whenever we believe
        disclosure is necessary to limit our legal liability; to protect or defend our rights or
        property; or protect the safety, rights, or property of our users.
        Corporate Change: We may sell, transfer, or otherwise disclose user information,
        including personally identifiable information, to a successor entity in connection with a
        corporate merger, consolidation, the sale of substantially all of our assets, or other
        corporate change respecting the Website.

Your Choices
As noted above, we may use the personally identifiable information we collect from you online
to provide you with opportunities to offer you products and services that may be of interest to
you. If you would like to opt out of future promotional materials, there are opt out option
attached to emails sent from the website. If you wish to opt out please follow the instructions
and the process will provide more details on your options.
Cookies
To enhance your experience with our Website, many of our Web pages use “cookies.” Cookies
are text files we place in your computer’s browser to store your preferences. Cookies, by
themselves, do not tell us your e-mail address or other personally identifiable information unless
you choose to provide this information to us by, for example, registering at our Website.
However, once you choose to furnish our Website with personally identifiable information, this
information may be linked to the data stored in the cookie.
We use cookies to understand site usage and to improve the content and offerings on our
Website. For example, we may use cookies to personalize your experience at our Website pages
(e.g., to recognize you when you return to our Website). You may adjust your browser to reject
cookies from our Website. Please consult the “Help” section of your browser for more
information. However, certain areas of our Website can be accessed only in conjunction with
cookies or similar devices and you should be aware that disabling cookies or similar devices
might prevent you from accessing some of our content.
Data Security
We have taken reasonable and appropriate security measures through our Website service
provider to attempt to protect against the loss, misuses, and alteration of your personally
identifiable information under our control. In all cases, we have taken reasonable steps to
confirm the information is retained in secured facilities and that reasonable measures have been
implemented for protection from unauthorized access. However, no security system is
impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the security of our Website or databases, nor can we
guarantee that information you supply will not be intercepted while being transmitted to us over
the Internet. We ask that you do your part by maintaining any computer passwords you use to
access the Internet or this Website strictly confidential.
Access To, Amendment of Information
If you wish to review, correct, or update, the information that we maintain about you, including
your preferences, you may do so at any time by logging into “My Property Finder” and accessing
the My Profile section. Your information on our system may be retained for a period in our
backup systems as a precaution against systems failures. Some information may be retained for
longer periods as required by law, contract or auditing requirements.

Links
The Website contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the
terms of use or privacy practices of such other sites unless we specifically state that we are the
contracting party with respect to such sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they
leave our Website and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects
personally identifiable information. This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected
on the Website.
Changes to Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy became effective on 12/28 2005. We reserve the right to modify or amend
this Privacy Policy at any time with or without notice, and for any reason. Changes to this Policy
shall be effective when posted. You are responsible for reviewing this Privacy Policy each time
you use or access the Website. Your continued use of this Website is an acknowledgment and
consent to any terms contained in this Privacy Policy, as it may be amended from time to time.
