# Privacy Policy | welcome to ilikenewgames.com

Information of Client`s Data  
ilikenewgames.com always collects and maintains our consumers information when you visit our site for the first time.These data can be tracked automatically from these following paths.

1.Cookies  
Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies.  
When you visit helphint we send one or more cookies – a small file containing a string of characters – to your computer or other device that uniquely identifies your browser.  
Cookies make using our Website easier by, among other things, saving your passwords and preferences for you. These cookies are restricted for use only on our Website, and do not transfer any personal information to any other party. Data collected from this path are automatically and indirectly recorded into our database.

2.Google Analytics  
Google Analytics is a free web analytics tool offered by Google that generates detailed statistics about the visitors. The tool can be used to track all the usual site activities: visits, page views, pages per visit, bounce rates and average time on site etc.  
In order to get specific and reliable client`s data google analytics has been attached within this site to track every single information of the consumer including ip adress,country where they come from to access this site,browser which they use and etc.Thus when you visit our post your data indirectly and automatically have been recorded.

3.Comment  
Comment is a section in blog posts (typically immediately following the post entry) where readers can provide their reactions to the content of the post.  
When you comment on our post you have been asked for filling email and name. Anyway we automatically track your ip address,email address or url and etc. Thus your data you mentioned has been recorded in our data base automatically.

All of information directly or indirectly you revealed have become our confidential data that will never disclose to any third party whatsoever.
