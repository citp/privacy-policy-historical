# inMotionNow Privacy Policy | InMotionNow

## This privacy policy applies to the submission of personal information.

By using the site, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy policy. These practices may be changed, but any changes will be posted and changes will only apply to activities and information on a going forward, not retroactive basis. You are encouraged to review the privacy policy whenever you visit the site to make sure that you understand how any personal information you provide will be used.

We (inMotionNow) collect personally identifiable information, such as name, email address, phone number, company, and job title, when voluntarily submitted by our visitors. This information is used to contact you regarding questions or concerns submitted in the form. This information is only used to fulfill your specific request, unless you give us permission to use it in another manner, for example to add you to one of our mailing lists. We do not share your personal information with anyone outside of the company.

To opt-out of further communication from us, email [marketing@inmotionnow.com](mailto:marketing@inmotionnow.com?subject=Please%20remove%20me%20from%20contact%20list) to be taken off our contact list.
