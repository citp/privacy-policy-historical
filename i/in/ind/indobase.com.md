# Privacy statement

_PRIVACY POLICY statement_

User privacy and confidentiality is very important to us at indobase.com. We strive to protect the privacy of Internet users, and maintain the web as a highly credible medium. 

There are times where a user may provide specific personal information in response to containing a survey, requesting a webpage, purchase agreement, or registration questionnaire, or requesting advice. For such programs, we acquire this information. This information is used by the client so the user can receive the services requested. 

Often, advertisers on the on the site will be serving ads and managing and tracking the performance of an ad program.In these instances, we have no control over the information collected by the third-parties, and may not be held responsible for the actions of the ad services where collected information is concerned. 

We regard all personal information collected from its visitor as completely confidential, except when visitor fill up requests for posting their webpages on the website. This includes personal data provided during registration processes. We do offer to advertisers any statistical data which may facilitate the sale of advertising. This includes traffic data, click-thru rates, estimated figure of unique visitors, and any demographic data for the purpose of facilitating an advertising sale. 

This web site contains links to other sites. This statement is for the sole use and purpose of Indobase.com. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage you to read the privacy practices of each and every web site that collects your personally identifiable information. 

This site makes a few public forums available to you. Whenever you post personal information online, through chat rooms, message boards, or email that this information can be collected and used by others. We will take all reasonable precautions to protect your privacy, but always be aware that when you are in a public forum, you control the information you reveal. 

We reserve the right to change our privacy policy from time to time.
