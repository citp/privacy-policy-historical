 **INVITATIONS AND OTHER COMMUNICATIONS TO NON-MEMBERS**

India-forums members can invite friends to join the service by sending invitation emails via our automated invitation system. The India-forums Site stores the email addresses that members provide so that the respondents may be added to the friend's list of the member sending the invitations, and also to send reminders of the invitations. The India-forums Site does not sell these email addresses or use them to send any other communication besides invitations, invitation reminders (up to three (3) per email address). Recipients of invitations from India-forums may contact the India-forums Site to request the removal of their information from our database.

India-forums members may also store email addresses of people they know in their internal India-forums address book and may also choose to send invitations and other communications to those addresses.

You may prevent email invitations from us by sending a single email with the subject "BLOCK" to [Unsubscribe](https://web.archive.org/contact_us.asp "Unsubscribe"). Please note that the email must come from the account you wish to block.
