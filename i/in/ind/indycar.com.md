## Information Collection and Use

In order to take part in promotional activities for IRL's products and services, obtain information from IRL, or have questions answered by IRL, IRL may request and/or collect you User Information, which may include, without limitation, your first and last name, home address, shipping address, phone number, email address, credit or debit card number, and feedback on IRL's products and services. 

By entering User Information on the Website, you consent to our use of the User Information for purposes discussed herein, as well as for purposes related to this IRL website and the services offered and/or provided on or in connection with the Website. Without limiting the foregoing, by giving us User Information you are giving us permission to contact you and/or send you promotional and/or other marketing information about products, services and offerings. If you elect to receive such promotional and/or marketing information, you may opt-out of receiving such materials by following the opt-out instructions contained in the materials or by emailing IRL at online@indycar.com. 

We will not sell, share, or rent User Information to others in ways different from those disclosed in this Privacy Statement. We do not intend to collect information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that the Website has inadvertently collected information from a child under 13 years of age, we will promptly take all reasonable measures to delete that data from our systems. 

## Information Sharing

IRL may share any User Information collected on the Website with its affiliated companies and with non-affiliated third parties that provide services on behalf of IRL. By entering User Information on the Website, you consent to the disclosure of your User Information to IRL affiliates and non-affiliated third parties selected by IRL. IRL makes no representation or warranty as to the security or privacy of User Information after it has been disclosed to a non-affiliated third party. For more information regarding third parties and their privacy policies, please refer to their respective websites and consult any privacy policies posted there. We may share aggregated demographic information with third party sponsors and advertisers. This aggregated demographic information is not linked to any personal information that can identify any individual person. In addition, we may disclose any information collected from the Website (i) if required or permitted pursuant to any applicable law, regulation, or court or administrative order; (ii) in connection with any user's violation of posted terms of use for the Website or other agreements with IRL; (iii) to protect the property of IRL or its affiliates; or (iv) to protect the personal safety of employees of IRL or its affiliates, other users of the Website, or a member of the public. IRL also may disclose and/or transfer User Information to a purchaser or transferee of all or a portion of its assets in conjunction with the sale or transfer of such assets. 

## Security

This Website uses industry-standard security measures to protect certain User Information. When our Site asks users to enter sensitive information, that information is protected with encryption software such as Secure Sockets Layer - SSL. 

Unfortunately, no data transmitted over or accessible through the public Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we attempt to protect a user's personally identifiable information, IRL cannot ensure or warrant that any User Information will be completely secure from misappropriation by hackers or from other nefarious or criminal activities, or in the event of a failure of computer hardware, software, or a telecommunications network.

## Cookies

A cookie is a small computer file that is stored on a user's computer and contains information that this Website uses. We use cookies to store information that helps us analyze what is happening on our site so we can improve the functionality. Our cookies do not contain financial, tax-related, or personally identifiable information (such as social security number, log-in id, or user name). IRL does not correlate the cookie information with data about individual users, nor does it share this information or sell it to any third party. If you prefer not to receive cookies from this Website, you can set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies and refuse the cookie when your browser alerts you to its presence. You can also refuse all cookies by turning them off in your browser. Please be aware, however, that certain features of this Website may be unavailable if your browser is not set to support or enable cookies.
