# Indianfoodforever - Privacy Policy

Indianfoodforever.com respects your privacy and personal information that you give us. It is held with the utmost care and security. We will not intentionally monitor or disclose any private message or address information unless required by law, regulations or court orders.   
Indianfoodforever.com will not collect any personal information about you except when you specifically and knowingly provide such information. For example, while registering for the newsletter, you will be required to give your e- mail address.   
If in future we change our privacy policy for IndianFoodForever.com, we will let you know. If you have questions or concerns about our privacy policy please feel free to email us using our contact us form. Thank you for using IndianFoodForever.com. We'll keep working towards making your experience on IndianFoodForever.com a pleasure..
