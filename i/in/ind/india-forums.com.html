    About India-forums.com

    India-Forums.com is the # 1 Discussion Forums with more than 330,000 members currently (June, 2008) and was started in December 2003.  From the days of Jassi Jaissi Koi Nahi - India-Forums has succeeded in bringing together television viewers all across the globe. It has merged the virtual world and reality in a beautiful combination where virtual friends are now part of an extended family with many special bonds and relationships. India-Forums started as television discussion forum has branched out to several other sections like Bollywood, Tellybuzz, Celebrity Interview, Internet Radio, Fan Clubs, Debate Mansion, Sports, Books Talk, Fashion News update and Gizmo Talks.

    India-Forums is the leading Indian Website and is currently in the top 15 High Traffic Indian Website according to Big-Boards.com It is also positioned in top 5 websites in terms of daily pageviews. In terms of Alexa ranking (Amazon based company) India-Forum is at top 2,000 website in the world. With the new features adding in continuously and the support and active participation of its members India-Forum is progressing at a breakneck speed with surely sky is a limit.
