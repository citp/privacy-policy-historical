personal information to the extent that it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal

proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defend its legal rights.

Securing your data

Internet Billboards will take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information. Please see our terms of  use.

You agree to such cross-border transfers of personal information.
