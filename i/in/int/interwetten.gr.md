# Interwetten.com

  


##  PRIVACY POLICY

​  
Protecting our players’/customers’ privacy is important to Interwetten. All gaming companies, i.e. Interwetten Malta Ltd, Interwetten Gaming Ltd and Interwetten Entertainment Ltd, as well as Eurotrade International Ltd, the operator of [www.iwfriends.com](http://www.iwfriends.com/), have been notified under the Data Protection Act (Chapter 440 of the Laws of Malta) to the Office of the Commissioner for Data Protection in Malta. We strive to keep your personal information protected. Please take a moment to read the following policy to learn how we handle your personal information, as your use of our services will indicate your acceptance of its content. 

In general, any customer may open our website and move around on our webpage without transmitting information or data.

 Each player/customer, however wishing to use our services must open an account with Interwetten Malta Ltd. in order to be able to utilise any product offered by Interwetten (sports- betting, casino, skill games).  Whenever a player/customer strikes a bet or takes part in a game, s/he accepts the validity and applicability of this Privacy Policy, the [General Terms and Conditions](https://www.interwetten.com/Common/CMS/CMS.aspx?type=5&tid=Terms%20and%20conditions%20of%20business&ln=TermsAndConditionsCategory|TermsAndConditions), the relevant rules (sportsbook, casino or skill games) as well as the applicable terms for promotions, special offers and bonuses, as amended from time to time.

That is why Interwetten requests that players/customers confirm that they are of legal age and give their date of birth upon registration. Interwetten reserves the right to verify any player’s/customer’s statement of age and to exclude players/customers from its services, if there are any doubts regarding the attainment of the minimum age required. 

 Any customer using our services, who is identified as underage, shall have all his winnings forfeited and his account shall be blocked immediately. In principal, minors are not allowed to use any of our products, may it be the sportsbook or any other games of chance.

  **Profile**

We store information that we collect through cookies, log files, clear gifs, and/or third parties to create a profile of our customers (players). A profile is stored information that we keep on individual customers (players) users that detail their viewing preferences. Consequently, collected information is tied to the customers´ personally identifiable information to provide offers and improve the content of the site for the customer (player). This profile is used to tailor a user's visit to our Web site and to direct pertinent marketing promotions to them. We do not share your profile with any other third parties outside the Interwetten Group. 

   **Cookies**

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the customer´s computer tied to information about the customer. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site. We use both session ID cookies and persistent cookies. For the session ID cookie, once users close the browser, the cookie simply terminates. A persistent cookie is a small text file stored on the customer´s hard drive for an extended period of time. 

   **Log Files**

Interwetten uses log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses, etc. are not linked to personally identifiable information. IP addresses are tied to personally identifiable information to enable our Web-based service. 

   **Clear Gifs (Web Beacons/Web Bugs)**

Some of our third party advertising companies use a software technology called clear gifs (a.k.a. Web Beacons/Web Bugs), that helps us better manage content on our site by informing us what content is effective. Clear gifs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of Web users. The main difference between a cookie and a clear gifs is that clear gifs are invisible on the page. Clear gifs are not tied to users' personally identifiable information. Clear Gifs together with cookies can be used to identify the previous online behavior of the user of the respective computer. Such information would then be made available to an advertising company.

  Technical dimension within the Privacy Policy – Security  
We trace and record on our technical systems the IP address of our customers and use it in order to provide you with a better technical service and to prevent fraudulent activities. All data will be processed in an automated manner.

While we use SSL encryption to protect sensitive information online, we also undertake anything reasonable to protect customer-information off-line. 

  All of our users' information, not just the sensitive information mentioned above, is restricted in our offices. Only authorized Interwetten employees, or employees of subcontracted service companies with identical data protection obligations, are granted access to personally identifiable information. We perform checks on all our employees to ensure that none of them has a criminal record. Our employees must use password-protected screen-savers when they leave their desk. When they return, they must re-enter their password to re-gain access to user information. Furthermore, ALL employees are kept up-to-date on our security and privacy practices. Every year and whenever a , new policy is added, our employees are notified and/or reminded about the importance we place on privacy, and what they can do to ensure our users' information is protected. Finally, the servers that store personally identifiable information are in a secure environment.
