# Policies

** Privacy & Policies**

**Privacy**

In today's technological age, we know you care about how information about you is used and shared and ensures you that any information you provide is used only as necessary to conduct  business.

** Returns & Refunds**

Refunds on Package 1 will be granted in full, provided the product is sent back in its original condition within 365 days of purchase. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the purchaser.

**IP Addresses & Cookies**

The InternetMarketingSweetie.com website statistics program does keep track of IP addresses that visit this website. It also uses cookies to determine unique visits from a particular IP. This information is used to collect general statistics about website visitors and does not to track individual behavior. Our message board uses cookies to keep track of registered members preferences and to provide you with information on posts since your last visit. We do not use this information to track your individual behavior.
