We may collect information about the browser, computer, mobile phone, smartphone or other device you use to access the Services to measure the effectiveness of those Services.

We respect your privacy :

The information you, and/or your browser submit to our website will never be sold or rent to anyone.

The web server automatically collects website logs on whoever visits the site. Those logs are not personally identifiable and we will not sell or rent them to anyone.

We use cookies to improve the quality of our service. Cookies are also used for storing user preferences. Cookies and cookie information are not personally identifiable and we will not sell or rent them to anyone.
