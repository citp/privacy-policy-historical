# Inter Rail

| **Collection of information  
**Any information that you provide us voluntarily (e.g., user name, e-mail address, phone number) will be retained by us. If you send us personal correspondence, such as e-mails or letters, we may collect such information into a file specific to you. All such information collected by us (collectively, "Personally Identifiable Information") shall become our property throughout the world. In accordance with Data Protection regulations, you have the right to access, modify, correct or remove the information held about you from our database. This may be done by writing to : Grandes Lignes International, Tour Mattei, 207 rue de Bercy, F - 75012 Paris.  
We do not use cookies.

**Use of Information**  
We do not sell, share or license Personally Identifiable Information. We may aggregate statistics, traffic patterns, and related site information and disclose such aggregate data to third parties for marketing, advertising, or other promotional purposes, but such aggregate data will not include any Personally Identifiable Information. By using the Official Inter Rail website, you consent to such collection and use by us. **

Links to Other Websites

**  
The Official Inter Rail website contains links to partners, advertisers and third-party Internet websites and services that may collect data in connection with sweepstakes, promotions, e-commerce, etc. They each have separate privacy and data collection practices, independent of the Official Inter Rail website. The Official Inter Rail website has no responsibility or liability for these independent policies or actions, and is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. 

**Changes to our Privacy Policy**  
We may amend our privacy policy from time to time. If we make any changes to our privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page so that you are always aware of our policies. The Official Inter Rail website reserves the right to modify, alter or update these policies at any time, and you agree to be bound by such modifications, alterations or updates.
