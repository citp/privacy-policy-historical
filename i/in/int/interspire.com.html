Our Privacy Policy

This is the web site of Interspire.com. We can be reached via email at help@interspire.com. For each visitor to our web site, our web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address.

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who subscribe to our newsletter and order our products. Information collected from customers is used for order notification and to send product information only. It is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

All emails contain an unsubscribe link which can be used to opt-out. Alternatively, you can email help@interspire.com to be removed from any mailing list. With respect to cookies: We do use cookies on the Interspire web site to store certain login details to make future visits quicker and easier for you.

With respect to Ad Servers: We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies.

With respect to security: We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us via email at help@interspire.com or by mail at Level 9, 162-166 Goulburn St Sydney 2000 NSW Australia.
