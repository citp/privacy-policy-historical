# Inspiration: visual thinking and learning software

  


## PRIVACY POLICY

Inspiration Software Inc. respects the privacy of users who visit our site. We will not sell or lease your personal information. All information provided to us will be used only by a representative of Inspiration Software, Inc. When we request personal information, we provide explicit statements describing the potential use of that information. 

We log IP addresses in order to analyze trends, track statistics within our site and gather broad demographic information. IP addresses are not linked to any personally identifiable information. 

This web site contains links to other sites. Note that when you click on one of these links, you are leaving our web site. Please be aware that Inspiration Software, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites. We encourage you to read their privacy statements, as they may differ from ours.
