# INF Photo - Privacy Policy

|  |  Privacy   
---  
INSIGHT NEWS & FEATURES is committed to protecting your privacy. As a user of the INSIGHT NEWS & FEATURES web site (the "Site"), you acknowledge that we will collect certain information about you, and that you may provide information regarding your tastes and preferences. This statement sets forth our Privacy Policy and describes the INSIGHT NEWS & FEATURES follows with respect to information provided by visitors to this Site. Please be aware that this Site contains links to other sites that are covered by their own privacy policies.   
Personal Information   
To purchase items via our Site and/or to become a INSIGHT NEWS & FEATURES web site user, you must provide some personal information that includes your name, email address and some other contact information. We use this information for internal purposes, namely, tracking your downloads, processing your orders, notifying you of your order status, updating your account, and sending you notices of new services, and noteworthy news and events. We do not sell or rent information about our customers or email lists to third parties.   
Security   
We are committed to protecting the confidentiality of your personal information.
