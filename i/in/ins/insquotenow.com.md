**_Privacy Statement_**

_Insquotenow.com_ is owned and operated by Remark International B.V. ("we" "us" "our").

In this Privacy Statement we set out our personal information practices which we use in connection with the personal information we obtain from each user ("you") on the insquotenow.com website (this "Site").

Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. 

**_Purposes for the collection, use and disclosure of personal information_**

When you visit this Site, we collect personal information about you. We may collect, use and disclose this information for several purposes, (the "purposes"), including:

  * To process your request for a quote for products or services;
  * To provide your name and contact information, as provided by you to us through this Site, to one or more insurance agents, other intermediaries or insurers (who may in turn provide your information to another insurance agent, other intermediary or insurer).
  * To match you with one or more insurance agents, other intermediaries or insurers ("third parties") and to share the necessary information with such third parties in order to allow them to assist you in researching and obtaining an insurance quote, product or service;
  * For research and statistical analysis purposes;
  * To better understand your needs including your financial needs;
  * To respond to any question or request for assistance communicated though this Site;
  * For promotional purposes and to provide you with information about other products and services offered by us, or those organizations with which we have relationships, which may be of interest to you; and
  * In instances when we are legally obligated to collect, use or disclose the personal information.



**_Consent to the collection, use and disclosure of personal information_**

You have the option to refuse or withdraw your consent to the collection, use or disclosure of your personal information at any time. Please understand that this may limit our ability to serve you.

When you use this Site and provide information to us, you consent to our collection, use and disclosure of your personal information for the purposes described above.

Unless you tell us otherwise, we may contact you by any means for which you have provided contact information. For example, if you provide us with your email address, we may contact you via email from time to time, for any of the purposes described in this Privacy Statement.

By filling out the forms and providing information about yourself on this Site or submitting a quote request:

  * You agree that we may provide your information to one or more insurance agents, other intermediaries or insurers who may in turn call, email or otherwise contact you;
  * You agree that we may provide your information to one or more such third parties who may then provide the information to one or more insurance agents, other intermediaries or insurers who may in turn call, email or otherwise contact you;
  * You agree that we may collect, use and disclose your personal information and such third parties may in turn collect, use and disclose your personal information for any purpose and in any manner disclosed on such forms or quote request;
  * You represent that you are an actual person, you have the right to provide the information you submit, and the information you submit is accurate, complete and truthful; and
  * You acknowledge and agree that we are not responsible for the compliance of any third party with any applicable law including laws relating to communication by telephone or electronic means.



Some of our businesses and services have in past and may in future be transferred or sold in whole or part. As a result, your personal information may be disclosed to an acquiring organization, either at the investigation stage or on completion of any such transaction. 

**_Accessing your personal information_**

Upon written request, we will provide you with reasonable access to personal information about you that is in our possession. We will also update and/or correct your personal information as required. If we are unable to provide you with access to your personal information, we will provide reasons for our inability to do so, unless we are prohibited from doing so by law.

**_Provider and Agent Privacy Policies_**

By submitting a quote request with insquotenow.com, a third party may contact you using the contact information you provide. In order stop these communications, you must contact these persons or entities directly and request that they stop contacting you. If you choose to interact with any insurance agent, other intermediary or insurer, any personal information that they collect from you shall be subject to their privacy practices and not ours.

**_Cookies and Other Information_**

We collect and track certain technical information to track usage of the Site and how the Site is being accessed. We do this to monitor and improve the user experience on our Site. We may also use technical information to store your preferences and make future visits to our site quicker for you. 

**_Changes to this Privacy Statement_**

We may, at our sole discretion, revise this Privacy Statement at any time in a manner consistent with applicable laws and regulations. When we revise this Privacy Statement, including changes to how we collect, use and disclose personal information, we will incorporate the revisions into this Privacy Statement and will revise the "last revised" date at the bottom of this web page. We recommend that you regularly visit this Privacy Statement in order to remain informed about any changes that we may implement.

**_How to Contact Us_**

If you have any questions about our personal information practices you may contact us at: contactus@insquotenow.com
