# Insticator | Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting Insticator.com, owned and operated by Insticator, Inc(“Insticator ”). Our privacy commitment is described in this Privacy Policy. As our services evolve and we perceive the need or desirability of using information collected in other ways, we may from time to time amend this Privacy Policy. We post the effective date of our Privacy Policy on our website ("Site"). We encourage you to check our Site frequently to see the current Privacy Policy and Terms of Use in effect and any changes that may have been made to them. If we make material changes to this Policy we will post the revised Policy and the revised effective date on this Site. Please check back here periodically or contact us at the address listed at the end of this Policy.

This Policy is part of our Terms of Use Agreement. Please click  [here](https://www.insticator.com/terms)  to see that Agreement.

## General Information

We believe that you have the right to understand how your information is stored and used. We require that sites linked to our Site contain a notice identifying their privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third parties or the content of linked sites although we do encourage you to read the applicable privacy policies and terms and conditions of such parties or web sites.

## Information You Provide Us

In general, you can visit  [Insticator.com](https://insticator.com/insticator)  without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself. If you wish to obtain information about our products or services you can contact us. In order to participate in the functions of the Site, you will need to register. You may register through your Twitter or Facebook accounts or using your email address. When you register we may collect your name, email address, street or other address information, and information relating to your social media account, payment information (such as credit or debit card number and billing information) and any other information you may choose to submit ("Personally Identifiable Information" or "PII"). By providing your PII to us, you explicitly agree to our collection and use of such information as described in this Privacy Policy.

After you have registered we may collect information about how you use the Site, your predictions, your success rate, the information or data you post on the Site, and any other activity on your account using your log-in information.We may obtain information about you from other sources including, for instance, from your Facebook or Twitter account or from others who provide information about you. We may associate any or all of this information with your personal information.

## Protecting Children's Privacy

This Site is not designed nor intended to be attractive to use by children under the age of 13 ("Minors"). We do not knowingly collect PII from minors.If you are under the age of 13, please do not submit any information to us. If you are between the ages of 13 and 18 you represent that you have the consent of your parent or legal guardian to use the Site.  


## Cookies

We may use cookies or similar technologies such as web beacons to personalize our Site for you and to collect aggregate information about site usage by all of our users. A cookie is a text file that our Site transfers to your computer's hard drive for record-keeping purposes and to enhance the quality of your visit to our Site. The cookie assigns a random, unique number to your computer.We may use a third party to monitor activity on the Site who may also place cookies or similar technologies on your computer.Some cookies may expire as soon as you leave the Site, other cookies or other technology may remain on your device. We may use persistent cookies for our commercial purposes including, for instance, to recognize you when you return to the Site or to provide advertising that is relevant to you.You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but doing so may limit Site performance and may cause certain features of the Site to malfunction or not work at all.We may use persistent cookies as well as session cookies.  


## Use and Disclosure of Information

The use of your information is subject to the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use in effect at the time of use. The provisions contained herein supersede all previous notices or statements regarding our privacy practices with respect to this Site.

We may use aggregated data (independent of any personal identifiers) for research and commercial purposes. This information includes what areas our users visit most frequently and what services they access most often. This information helps us develop a better site that is more useful and accessible to you.

We may use your PII internally for our general commercial purposes including, among other things, to offer our own, our affiliates’ or third-party products and services that we think you may find of interest. We may share your information with our affiliated companies for their general commercial use, including for their own marketing purposes.

We may use your email address to contact you to respond to your inquiries or to provide information on products or services to you. You can elect not to receive emails from us either by "unsubscribing" to an email you receive or by contacting us as indicated below.

We may reveal Personally Identifiable Information about you to unaffiliated third parties: (1) if you request or authorize it; (2) if the information is provided to help complete a transaction for you; (3) if the information is provided to comply with the law, applicable regulations, governmental and quasi-governmental requests, court orders or subpoenas, to enforce our Terms of Use or other agreements, or to protect our rights, property or safety or the rights, property or safety of our users or others (e.g., to a consumer reporting agency for fraud protection etc.); (4) if the disclosure is done as part of a purchase, transfer or sale of services or assets (e.g., in the event that substantially all of our assets are acquired by another party, customer information may be one of the transferred assets); (5) if the information is provided to our agents, outside vendors or service providers to perform functions on our behalf (e.g., analyzing data, providing marketing assistance, providing customer service, processing orders, etc.); (6) to allow them to market to you,or (7) as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy.

## Opt-Out, Access, and Security

If you do not wish to receive email offers or other notices from us in the future, you can "opt out" by contacting us at the address indicated at the end of this policy. In addition, if you provide us with PII, you may review your PII, make any changes or corrections or to request that we remove your information please contact us at the address listed below or by logging into your account and modifying your Profile. We will try to complete your request as promptly as possible. Any changes will affect only future uses of your PII.

We have reasonable administrative, physical and technological procedures in place in our physical facilities, computer systems, databases, and communications networks that are designed reasonably to protect information contained within our systems from loss, misuse or alteration. If you use a payment feature on the Site we will provide reasonable security to protect your information during transmission to the Site.

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password and account, and are fully responsible for all activities that occur under your password or account. We are not responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of any non-public information that you provide us. If there is information that you wish to protect from disclosure to others, do not provide it to us.
