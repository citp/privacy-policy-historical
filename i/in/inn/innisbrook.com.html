Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

When you use our website, we will automatically collect certain information about your use of our site. This information may be used to analyze our site usage and to improve our marketing efforts and services.

When you use our website to place an order, we need to collect certain information from you. We ask you to provide your name, address, e-mail, phone number and your credit card info.

We will use this information to notify you of special promotions and new product introductions.

This information, as well as all other information collected, will not be shared with any third parties except under the circumstances outlined below.

Disclosure of Information

WE will NOT disclose your personal information except under the following circumstances:

  • If required by law.
  • If deemed necessary by us in order to protect our property and personal safety, visitors to our site and third parties.
  • To third parties assisting us in processing services requested by you, and for billing and collection purposes.

By using our website and providing us with information, you are consenting to our collection and use of your information described above. You may request to have all of your information collected and removed from our database by contacting our customer service department at 1-800-334-8461. We will remove your information in a timely manner, unless we are prohibited by law from doing so.

All personal customer information and payment information is collected using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption. All customer information is encrypted before being transmitted to us over the Internet. Additionally, all customer information on our server is secured against unauthorized access. Your credit card information will be used only to process your payment.

Feedback

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, or if you believe this policy has been violated, please notify our customer service department at 1-800-334-8461. We value your input and will do everything possible to protect you and your privacy.
