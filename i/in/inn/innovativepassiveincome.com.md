# Privacy Policy — Passive Income Ideas: The Innovative Passive Income Blog

This site collects general information about its users through Google Analytics and some specific information through the comments you leave.  To reduce spam, leaving a comment requires an email address.  However, this site will never give, sell, or release your email address information for any reason to anyone. We will not send you messages unless you either request information specifically or subscribe to the site’s RSS feed.
