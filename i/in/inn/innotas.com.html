Innotas Privacy Policy

Share your information with confidence

The Innotas Privacy Policy demonstrates our commitment to the privacy of our valued customers and site visitors. Privacy on our Web site is of great importance to us. This statement addresses our information-gathering and dissemination practices.

This Policy covers the Innotas treatment of personally identifiable information that we may collect when you are on our site, and also applies to the Innotas treatment of any personally identifiable information that our business partners share with us. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that we do not own or control, or to people whom we do not employ or manage.

Information Collection and Use

Innotas may collect personally identifiable information when you visit or use our site and may also receive personally identifiable information from our business partners. In addition, information is also automatically received and recorded on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookie information and the page you requested.

This information may be used for three general purposes: to customize the content you see, to fulfill your requests for certain services and information, and to contact you.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Your personally identifiable information is not sold or rented to anyone. Personally identifiable information about you is sent to other companies or people only when:

  1. Innotas has your consent to share the information;
  2. Innotas needs to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested;
  3. Innotas needs to send the information to companies who work on behalf of Innotas to provide a product or service to you (unless we tell you differently, these companies do not have any right to use the personally identifiable information we provide to them beyond what is necessary to assist us);
  4. Innotas responds to subpoenas, court orders or legal process;
  5. Or, Innotas finds that your actions on our Web site violate the Innotas Subscription Agreement, or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services.
