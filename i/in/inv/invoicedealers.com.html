PRIVACY POLICY

At InvoiceDealers.com we take privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes our policies for handling the information about you that we collect and receive through this website. You can visit InvoiceDealers.com without revealing who you are or providing any personal information about yourself. By using this website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to practices of businesses that we do not own or control or people we do not employ. In addition, this website may contain links to other websites, such as automotive research, financing or insurance websites, and we are not responsible for the content, policies or privacy practices of such websites.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Personal Information You Submit to Us

We collect personally identifiable information that you submit to us through this website (“Personal Information”), including personal information you may submit to us in connection with requesting an automobile price quote, financing or insurance, responding to surveys or contacting us by email. Personal Information typically includes name, address, and phone numbers, email address, and may also include social security number, residence information, date of birth, or other information that would allow you to be personally identified.

Passively Collected Information

As you browse our website we may collect certain information about your visit and your Internet activities through the use of cookies, web beacons and from your browser. This information does not consist of personally identifiable information, but may include your browsing history on this site or certain other sites, your IP address, browser type and other information.

Third-Party Cookies and Web Beacons

We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. HOW WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

We use the Personal Information that you submit to us through this site for the purpose of delivering our services to you. Specifically, we may use your Personal Information in the following ways:

  • We will forward your Personal Information directly to one or more or our participating automobile dealers or,
    if we do not have a direct relationship with a participating dealer, we will forward your Personal Information to
    an automobile manufacturer or to a trusted business partner that will forward your personal information to one or more dealers;
  • We may communicate with you to confirm the information you submitted, or to solicit your feedback about your experience using our services;
  • We may forward it to a trusted data service provider for the purpose of verifying or supplementing the Personal Information;
  • We may use your information for internal purposes unrelated to any transaction, such as to help improve our website or better understand our customers' needs;
  • We may aggregate your Personal Information with information from other visitors to prepare reports (that do not include personally identifying information) on site usage, purchase history or other factors; and
  • We may use or share your personal information to comply with any law, regulation, audit or court order.
  • Personal information provided on our Website may be linked together or merged with other personal information we obtain about you so that we may better understand your needs and inform you about our products and services and those third parties with whom we have business relationships to help meet your needs or provide additional information.
  • You have the right to decline to receive these third party communications. See Your Right To Decline Communications from us and others

YOUR RIGHT TO DECLINE COMMUNICATIONS FROM US AND OTHERS

We provide our users with the ability to choose whether to receive certain communications from us and whether to permit their information to be available to third parties for marketing or related purposes.

In addition, we provide you the opportunity to indicate your preferences as to what types of offerings of products or services you wish to receive.  Every email will provide you with the opportunity to indicate your preference to the types of emails you wish to receive or to opt out.  To opt out of a category of email from us, click on the remove link at the bottom of the email.  Alternatively, you may email Customer Service at Feedback@invoicedealers.com and we will remove you from all further email marketing communications within seven (7) business days.


YOUR CONSENT

Prior to providing us with any of your Personal Information you can change your mind and cancel the submission by exiting the website. Once you submit your Personal Information to us through our website we may use it for any of the purposes mentioned above without first obtaining your consent. You do not have the choice of opting out, or requesting that we do not use your information, for any of the purposes mentioned above once you submit it to us.

INFORMATION SECURITY

We maintain physical, electronic and other procedural safeguards to comply with applicable privacy laws to guard your nonpublic personal information. We also assess new technology for protecting sensitive customer information on an ongoing basis. However, if your Personal Information is forwarded to an automobile dealer or other third party provider, your information will be handled according to the security and privacy policies of that dealer or third party provider and those privacy practices may differ from ours.

PRIVACY POLICY CHANGES

We may change our Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update this Privacy Policy at our website.

OTHER INFORMATION

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy or privacy policies, please feel free to e-mail your questions to us at feedback@invoicedealers.com.
