Privacy Statement

 

The following comprises details of our privacy policy and how we use any personal data (as defined in the Data Protection Act 1998) that you supply to us through this website.
Please read this complete Invest NI.com privacy statement. By using this site, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.


Security of your Personal Information
We are registered under the Data Protection Act 1998 and will comply with this legislation, so any personal data which you supply to us will be used solely for the purpose indicated on the relevant section of the website. Please do not give us information if you do not want it stored. The nature of the data that we keep on record may include your name, email address, mailing address, telephone and fax number.

The information we hold about you is confidential and will only be disclosed outside Invest Northern Ireland at your request or with your consent, to investigate or prevent fraud or if the law permits it or it is in the public interest. Your information will not be sold to any other company.

We carefully protect your data from loss, misuse, unauthorized access or disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Inside the company, data is stored in password-controlled servers with restricted access.


Links to Other Web Sites
In some areas of this site we include links to other web sites. Note that we do not review the privacy practices of those sites, or guarantee the privacy of information you enter outside of www.InvestNI.com.

Your Responsibility
We would ask that you keep us informed (by email, telephone, or in writing) of any changes in your personal data so that we may have our records up to date at all times.

Changes to this Statement
We will from time to time amend this Data Protection and Privacy Policy Statement and such amendments will be posted on the website.

Contact Information
Invest Northern Ireland welcomes your comments regarding this Statement of Privacy. Please contact us by e-mail if you have any comments.
