InvestorLoft.com's Privacy Statement
InvestorLoft.com provides a variety of online services to real estate investors, professionals and industry service providers. InvestorLoft.com places a very high value on its Customers privacy, feedback and candor, as these attributes are the cornerstones around InvestorLoft.com's Mission Statement. Any Customer information that InvestorLoft.com collects is considered private and is not used or shared except as permitted herein, without the Customer's permission. InvestorLoft.com uses this personal information as a means to provide the best quality customer service possible. The following policy statement summarizes InvestorLoft.com's protection and use of any personal information provided by its Customers.

Information Collected by InvestorLoft.com:
InvestorLoft.com collects information volunteered by the consumer from multiple areas of the its site including the 'Member Signup' area; real estate services; the creation and editing of property listings; the adding and editing of Professional Profiles; billing fees for services rendered such as property listing and success fees, email forms; and any survey questionnaire or contests that InvestorLoft.com may sponsor or co-market with a third-party. InvestorLoft.com collects individual and business user search activity for statistical, archival, and business purposes. It also collects financial information (such as billing address, credit card number, expiration date) for billing payment and credit card authorization purposes. Investorloft.com may record an IP address of a user's computer for security purposes only.
InvestorLoft.com uses cookies for the purpose of enhancing the user's experience and enabling the features and tools used on the site, and additionally to provide improved service when visitors return to our site. Any information collected by InvestorLoft.com will not be shared with other organizations, subject to the case where there is a court ordered or otherwise legal demand to release this type of information.

Use of Information Collected:
Collected information helps InvestorLoft.com better analyze and understand its Customers' needs to improve the scope and quality of the products and services it offers. Email addresses are used to communicate with members through InvestorLoft.com for effective bid offer and seller interaction, notifications of new properties on the site, market promotions, Customer and Partner emails and for site administration purposes. Customers and Partners may be deleted from InvestorLoft.com, Promotional email and Partner email lists at their request at any time. Customer information may be used to contact Customers for the purpose of research, product development, marketing, or customer service, if the Customer has opted to receive such communication. Customer search activity is collected and analyzed and may be utilized by InvestorLoft.com in the aggregate or in the abstract for a variety of business purposes. Financial information is used to bill Customers for products and services purchased. Access to credit card account information is limited to certain management personnel and is not shared with anyone outside of InvestorLoft.com. IP address and cookie log data is analyzed to better understand Customer website interaction and preferences to identify areas of improvement in InvestorLoft.com site functionality and overall customer service.

Use of Correspondence Tools:
Customers may not use any of the correspondence services that are offered on InvestorLoft.com to send spam (i.e. unsolicited advertisement messages) or otherwise send content that would violate InvestorLoft.com's Terms of Use. InvestorLoft.com may monitor, track or archive the use or misuse of these features, but will be under no obligation to monitor, track or archive the messages sent from InvestorLoft.com by its Customers.

Shared information:
Though InvestorLoft.com will make every reasonable effort to preserve user privacy, it may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein it has good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on our Web site.

InvestorLoft.com shares non-personally identifiable member information in the following areas on the site: Create Listing Service, Buyer Search and Bid Service, Email Alerts and the Resource Center. When a Seller accepts a bid offer on a listing, the Buyer's contact information is made available to the Seller to contact the Buyer for the final negotiation and closing of the property. Likewise, the Buyer will receive the Seller's contact information upon offer acceptance. In addition, with InvestorLoft.com's Email Alerts functionality, users can save their property search criteria to have information about properties that match the specified criteria emailed to them. Unless required by law as detailed in this section, or a Customer specifically takes action as discussed in this paragraph, InvestorLoft.com does not share individual Customer search activity information and such activity information remains private to the individual Customer and to InvestorLoft.com.

Service Providers Professional Profile users agree to share their contact information and allow all InvestorLoft.com Customers to view their contact information via its Resource Center functionality. InvestorLoft.com shares information with various partners in its Strategic Alliance Partner Program only for the purpose of providing related services in which our Customers have requested a specific service or more information regarding that service via site question responses, questionnaires or inquiries generated from the use of educational content available on the site. The use that InvestorLoft.com's partners make of any Customer information will not exceed InvestorLoft.com's use of the data.

Key billing information (such as credit card number, expiration date and dollar amount) gathered from Customers making online purchases of products or services is shared with Cardservice International, our credit card processing partner, in order to authorize charges to the credit card. InvestorLoft.com also uses third party providers to perform email and postal mailings on behalf of InvestorLoft.com, but this information (such as name, address and email) is confidential and is not used by the provider for any other purpose.

InvestorLoft.com does not sell, rent or lease user information to any third party, except as disclosed here. From time to time, it may obtain information (such as electronic mail addresses) from third party providers. However, InvestorLoft.com intends to only contact individuals and businesses who it believes have a legitimate business interest in InvestorLoft.com, based on their memberships, affiliations or participation in other organizations or web sites, services that they offer, and who have indicated a willingness to be contacted for this purpose. Although InvestorLoft.com will work with other sites and companies with a similar respect for Customer privacy, it cannot be responsible for the privacy policies and practices of other sites. Please be aware that in the event InvestorLoft.com merges with a third party, or in the event all or substantially all of InvestorLoft.com's assets are acquired by a third party, customer information, including personally identifiable information, will be shared and/or transferred to such third party. The information may only be used or shared by such third parties for the primary purpose for which the information was originally collected. If such third parties, or if InvestorLoft.com for its own business needs, desire to use the personally identifiable information for another purpose, you will be notified prior to the information being used for a new purpose, and you will have the opportunity to opt out of such new use of your personally identifiable information.

Security of personal information:
InvestorLoft.com's private customer information is considered to be one of InvestorLoft.com's most important assets and thus its Customer's privacy is treated with the utmost regard and respect. InvestorLoft.com takes every reasonable precaution to secure personal information within its computers, and the facilities housing those computers at ViaWest's Englewood, Colorado facility and Castles Access's facility in San Diego, California. Both facilities are equipped with state of the art security including the following:
  • 24 X 7 card key access, hand-scan systems, thumb print scanners
  • 24 hour on-site staffed NOC and/or security staff
  • Intercoms, security cameras, pin code with picture ID
  • Closed-circuit television and/or video monitoring & recording
