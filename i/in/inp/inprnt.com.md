# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

INPRNT values your privacy. The following privacy policy applies to the use and disclosure of your information on our website:

Certain anonymous information, such as your IP address, may be logged by our server at any time. You may provide us with personal information when you sign up for an account, make a purchase, or otherwise provide information through your use of the website. This information will never be sold or shared with any third party.

We do share personal information with certain vendors who are performing services in connection with your use of the site, such as the servers for our email communications or the shipping companies who will deliver your order. These vendors use your personal information only at our direction and in accordance with our privacy policy.

We reserve the right to disclose your personal information when required by law and upon our good faith belief that disclosure is necessary to protect our legal rights. You agree to indemnify and defend the website against any claims brought upon it in connection with your use of its services.

We do not knowingly or intentionally collect personal information online from children under the age of 13. If you are an international user, please be advised that this website is governed by United States law regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Your security is very important to us. We encrypt personal information information using secure socket layer technology and other commercially acceptable means of encryption. However, these measures cannot guarantee absolute security. By using the website, you acknowledge that you understand and agree to assume these risks.

INPRNT may update this policy at any time in its sole discretion and without prior notice. We notify you of significant changes to the privacy policy by sending a notice to the primary email address connected with your account and posting it in our website.

If you have any questions or comments about this privacy policy, please email us at support@inprnt.com.
