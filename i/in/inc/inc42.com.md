# Privacy Policy - Inc42 Media

As used in this Privacy Policy, “Inc42” refers to all websites (including Inc42.com)

By visiting Inc42, viewing our content or research, attending or viewing any of our events or programs and/or using any of our services, you acknowledge that you accept the practices and policies outlined in this Privacy Policy.

 **What Types of User Information Does Inc42 Obtain?**

Inc42 may obtain the following types of information from you or concerning your computer or device (“Information”), which may include information that can be used to identify you as specified below (“Personally Identifiable Information”):

  * Name
  * Email Address
  * Mailing Address
  * Telephone Number(s)
  * Blog or Website URL
  * Photo
  * Demographic Information (e.g., age, gender, interests, and preferences)
  * Professional Title
  * Company
  * Industry
  * Activities or Content Viewed on Inc42
  * Information Collected Automatically



In certain cases, we may request that you provide some of the Information identified above in order to obtain specific products or services (such as registering for an Inc42 event or logging in to download a research report from Inc42). If you choose not to provide the Information we request, you may still use Inc42, but you may be unable to access certain features or services.

When you register to receive any products or services from Inc42 or provide information to Inc42 in any other manner, you agree to provide only true, accurate, current and complete information.

 **Information Collected Automatically**

Inc42 may automatically receive and log certain types of information when you visit or interact with our websites, services, mobile applications or other products, including:

  * Browser Information
  * Operating System Information
  * Mobile Device Information (e.g., device identifier, mobile operating system, etc.)
  * IP Address
  * Internet Service Provider
  * Content Viewed
  * Geographic Location
  * Connection Speed
  * Time of Visit
  * Referring Site, Application, or Service
  * Registration Time



 **How Do We Use This Information?**

We use the Information we obtain from or concerning you or your computer or device to provide Inc42 products and services, fulfill your requests, improve our products and services, personalize and tailor your experience on Inc42, operate our business, and understand how users are engaging with Inc42.

In addition, we may use Information from or concerning you or your computer or device to facilitate the delivery of content or advertisements that we believe may be of interest to you, or to communicate with you about new or existing products and services or special offers. For example, we may periodically send promotional materials or notifications related to our products and services to the email address associated with your account.

 **Inc42’s Use of Cookies**

Inc42 may place “cookies” on your computer or device that may contain anonymous Information that is associated with you. Cookies are small text files a website uses to recognize users, and are typically placed on your hard disk by a web server. Cookies contain information that can later be read by a web server in the domain (here, Inc42) that issued the cookie to you or that, in some cases, can be read by a third party domain.

Our cookies may record Information about your use of Inc42 so that we can provide a continuous and more personalized experience for you, including for one or more of the following purposes: to help identify visitors; assess usage patterns; identify preferences; control the display of ads; diagnose problems with our servers; gather demographic information; analyze trends and usage patterns; conduct research; deliver editorial content; record registration and personalized information; and otherwise administer Inc42 products and services.

If you do not wish to have Inc42 place and use cookies on your computer, you should set your browser preferences to refuse cookies before accessing Inc42. Inc42 is not responsible for any failure by you or your browser to accurately implement or communicate your browser preferences or settings. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to sign in or use other interactive features of Inc42 that depend on cookies.

 **Inc42 does not control and is not responsible for the use of cookies by its third party partners or advertisers**.

 **Disclosure of Information to Third Parties**

Inc42 may disclose Information (including Personally Identifying Information) from or concerning you or your computer or device to third parties in the circumstances described below. By visiting, accessing, attending or using Inc42’s products, services or events, you consent to the disclosure of your Information to certain categories of third parties in the manner described below. This Privacy Policy does not apply to the policies or practices of any of these third parties.

 **Service Providers**

Inc42 relies on third party vendors and service providers to provide support and assistance for its products, services and events (“Service Providers”), and we may disclose your Information (including Personally Identifying Information) to these Service Providers to the extent necessary to support our products, services or events, or as otherwise set forth below.

 **Event Registration**

Inc42 uses third parties, such as Meraevents and Explara, to process registration for Inc42 events and programs. Inc42 may provide your Information (including Personally Identifying Information) to such registration services when you register for a Inc42 event or program. In addition, as discussed below, Inc42 (or its third party registration service) may provide your Information (including Personally Identifying Information) to companies that sponsor or underwrite portions of an event that you register for, attend, or view.

 **Web Hosts**

Inc42’s website services are hosted by WpEngine, an enterprise web hosting platform. All Information obtained and stored by Inc42’s websites processed and stored on servers that are owned and operated by WordPress.

 **Third Party Websites**

Inc42 offers the ability to share Inc42 content on Third Party Websites, including social networking and blogging services such as LinkedIn or Facebook, through “plugins” or “widgets” (such as LinkedIn’s “in” button or Facebook’s “Like” button). When you elect to share content and information with others on Third Party Websites, Inc42 may transmit your Information (including Personally Identifying Information) to such Third Party Websites. This Information may be visible on the Third Party Website to anyone who has access to that content or Information. Your decision to share information in this manner is completely voluntary. You should ensure that you are comfortable with how Third Party Websites may share your Information by reviewing their privacy policies and/or modifying your privacy settings directly on those Third Party Websites.

 **URL/Referrer Header Information**

When you visit Inc42 websites, certain information about the content or activities you have engaged in on Inc42 may be reflected in the Uniform Resource Locator (“URL”) for the page you are viewing on Inc42 (i.e., the Internet “address,” commonly displayed in the browser window). For example, the date or title of an article you are viewing may appear in the URL for that page. In addition, if you are logged into a Inc42 account, certain account information (may be visible in the URL when you visit your account on Inc42. If you leave Inc42 to visit a third party website, including when you click on an advertisement or link or when you share information through a social plug-in (like the Linked In “in” button), the information contained in the URL may be transmitted to the third party website by your browser.

 **Advertisers and Analytics Services**

Inc42 uses third party advertisers, including ad networks, to provide advertising on its websites. In addition, as discussed in (“Analytics Services”), Inc42 uses third party analytics products to obtain information about how users are using and interacting with Inc42. These third parties may receive Information (including Personally Identifying Information) from Inc42 as discussed in greater detail in (“Security and Protection of Personally Identifying Information”).

 **Law Enforcement/Legal Process**

We may disclose your Information (including Personally Identifying Information) when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to comply with any law, regulation or legal request; enforce, apply, or investigate breaches of our terms of use and other agreements; respond to public requests; or protect the rights, property, user experience or safety of Inc42, our employees, our users, or others. This can include exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction.

 **Comments and Public Posts; Sharing on Third-Party Websites**

Inc42 encourages active participation by users, including through comments on Inc42. However, please be aware that if you voluntarily disclose Information, personal or otherwise, on Inc42, that Information can be viewed, collected and used by others, including other users, search engines, advertisers, third party applications developers, and anyone with access to Inc42. Inc42 has no control over the use, collection or disclosure of any Information that you post publicly on Inc42.

 **Change of Control**

If Inc42, or substantially all of its assets, is acquired by another company or party, user Information will be one of the assets transferred or acquired by the purchaser. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any purchaser of Inc42 or its assets may continue to collect, use and disclose your Information as set forth in this Policy.

 **Security and Protection of Personally Identifying Information**

We have implemented reasonable security measures and practices in an effort to protect the confidentiality of your Personally Identifying Information (other than Personally Identifying Information that you have elected to disclose to others). However, no security system is impenetrable. It may be possible for third parties to intercept or access information you provide to Inc42. Inc42 cannot guarantee the security of your Personally Identifying Information, nor can we guarantee that information you supply will not be intercepted while being transmitted to Inc42. It is important for you to protect against unauthorized access to your password(s) and computer or device, and to log off when using any shared computer.

 **Email or Text Message Communications**

We may contact you by e-mail, text message or other equivalent electronic communications if you access Inc42 products or services or register for events, or other content or services provided by Inc42. By visiting or using Inc42 content, products or services, or registering for or attending Inc42 events, you specifically consent to the receipt of these e-mail or text message communications. If you do not want to receive emails, text messages or other communications from us, please notify us by email at [support@inc42.com](mailto:support@inc42.com) and include sufficient information for us to identify your account, including your Name, Email Address (or Cellphone Number) and the specifics of your request. If you unsubscribe, Inc42 may still contact you via email or text message for administrative purposes or concerning any products, services or events for which you have registered or are using.

 **Changes to the Privacy Policy**

Inc42 retains the discretion to amend or modify this Privacy Policy from time to time. Use and disclosure of Information we obtain is subject to the later of: (1) the Privacy Policy in effect at the time such Information is collected or (2) any subsequent Privacy Policy of which you have notice. If we make material changes to the way we collect, use or disclose Personally Identifying Information, we will notify you by posting a clear and prominent announcement on Inc42 or through a direct communication. Your continued access to or use of Inc42 following notice of material changes to this Privacy Policy shall constitute consent to any amendments and/or modifications. Your use of Inc42 is entirely voluntary, and you may discontinue use of Inc42 at any time.
