# InfoEdge: Privacy Statement and Practices

You visit the InfoEdge Web Site without revealing your name or any other information about yourself. InfoEdge does not electronically collect any personal data from visitors or their browsers without their express and proactive consent. 

The site has forms such as the opt-in E-Mail Research List and the Shopping Cart to be used by visitors for sending their name, address, phone number and/or e-mail address to InfoEdge. This information may be used by InfoEdge to respond to customer inquiries, process sales orders, and send newsletters. This information is held in strictest confidence and is not resold for any reason whatsover.
