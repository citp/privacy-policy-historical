1.Customer support by third-party account.

The creation of an account is currently not supported in our products and services, so you need to log in through a third-party account (Facebook/Google/QQ, etc.)when you use our products and services.

In order to maintain our database updates and provide you with better experience, we might collect the information you provide and the content from the third-party account, we will manage and use it legitimately.

This includes: account and password, account information, login record, email address, IP address, and the other customer support by third-party account.

2.Your apps, browsers & devices.

We collect information about the apps, browsers, and devices you use to access our services and products, which helps us provide features like automatic product updates, optimization, etc.

This includes: browser type and settings, device type and settings, operating system, mobile network information, and products version number.

3.The activity data generated during the use of our products and services.

By collecting and analyzing the activity data, we could know the usage better, in order to make the corresponding improvement measures.

This includes: Custom settings, such as wallpapers, icons, etc. The textual content which is stored in local computer or the cloud, such as to-do list, notebook, etc. The interactive information during the use of our products and services, such as IP address, crash reports, system activity, the frequency of the use of websites and functions, your feedback and comments, etc.

Collecting the contens of this entry is just for product optimization, we will not disclose it to any others unless your agreement.

4.Web browser cookies

Your web browser places cookies on their hard drive for record-keeping purposes and sometimes to track information about them. we may use "cookies" to enhance User Experience.

These includes: session cookies, permanent cookies, third-party cookies, Flash cookies, etc.

They help our website remember your preferences and settings when you visit again in the future. Therefore, they could enhance User Experience. 

In order to make these cookies come into effect, they are stored on your device, and they won't be deleted when the browser is closed.

Or you can choose to set your web browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent.
