   **Privacy Practices**  
  
We may use your IP address to aid in service to your website as contracted or requested, to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our website. Otherwise, your IP address may be used only to gather broad demographic information. We may occasionally ask visitors for contact information (generally name and e-mail address) and demographic information (zip code, age, income level). The visitor's contact information is used to contact the visitor when necessary. It is not distributed to third parties. Visitors may cancel future mailings; please write to [webmaster@infocreek.com](mailto:webmaster@infocreek.com). Demographic and profile data may also be collected at our site. This data may be used to tailor the content viewed by the visitor according to our understanding of their preferences. It is not distributed to third parties. This site contains links to other sites. Infocreek is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. We encourage users of our site to update information previously provided: send e-mail to [info@infocreek.com](mailto:info@infocreek.com) with any corrections.
