# InfoAtoms | Privacy Policy

### Last Updated: November 11, 2012

## INTRODUCTION

InfoAtoms, Inc. ("Company, "we" or "us") is the distributor of the InfoAtoms plugin ("Plugin" or "InfoAtoms") which is available at www.infoatoms.com (the "Site"). InfoAtoms allows users to view definitions, translations and search results without leaving the page by just highlighting keywords or phrases. The Plugin, Site, and any related features, products, services, functionalities or content offered by the Company shall be referred to herein as "Services". Use of the Services is governed by the [InfoAtoms Terms of Service](https://www.infoatoms.com/terms-of-service), and this Privacy Policy (together the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy for the "Agreement"). Company and Services are based in the U.S. You must be 18 or over to use the Services. 

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of Services, including InfoAtoms and the Site. This Privacy Policy explains the type of information the Company collects, how the information is used, with who the information is shared, and extent to which the information can be associated with a particular user. Types of information may include Personally Identifiable Information ("PII") - such as email or name when submitting a customer service request - and Non- Personally Identifiable Information - such as IP Address and Browser. In some cases, information collected by the Plugin may be used or shared with third parties for the purposes of displaying targeted advertisements to you. InfoAtoms does not attempt to analyze web usage data the individual identity of any plugin user. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE TO THE TERMS OF THIS POLICY OR THE AGREEMENT IN ITS ENTIRETY, YOU SHOULD NOT USE THE INFOATOMS SERVICES OR SITE. IF YOU ARE CURRENTLY A USER AND DO NOT AGREE WITH THE TERMS OF THIS POLICY OR THE AGREEMNT IN ITS ENTIRETY, YOU SHOULD UNINSTALL IMMEDIATELY (See the [Uninstall Instructions](https://web.archive.org/support#uninstall)). 

InfoAtoms is ad-supported. During general internet usage on sites where InfoAtoms operates, users may see additional banner, search, pop-up, pop-under, and in-text link advertisements. These advertisements will be identified with the InfoAtoms Name or Logo. InfoAtoms and all related advertisements are not affiliated with or endorsed by any underlying websites. 

## COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

### WHAT INFORMATION IS COLLECTED?

#### USER SUBMITTED INFORMATION

When contacting InfoAtoms for issues related to the Service, Users have the option but not the requirement to submit personal information. The personal information (such as email address, name, and other identifying information) is collected to enable InfoAtoms to respond to issues and communicate effectively. However, the submission of personal information is not required but response cannot be guaranteed if a mechanism of communication is not provided.

#### COLLECTED INFORMATION

Non-personally identifiable information, such as IP Address, unique identifier number, operating system, browser, search queries, and browsing history is collected during normal use of the Service. The non-personally identifiable information is stored and used to further the performance of InfoAtoms such as maintaining and improving the Services and displaying more relevant advertisements. The information may be combined with information from third parties. InfoAtoms shares non-personally identifiable information (including search and URL history) to advertising partners to improve product performance and ad relevance.

### HOW IS THE COLLECTED INFORMATION COLLECTED?

#### TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

The company uses browser cookies, storage data, JavaScript, web beacons, pixels, flash cookies, and other tracking technologies to collect non-personally identifiable information. The tracking technologies collect and pass the collected information with an anonymous unique identifier. The collected information may include,but without limitation, search queries, browsing history, browser type and other performance data in order to improve the performance of the Services and improve marketing including, but without limitation, more relevant ads and to conduct market research. The company may also use cookies and similar tools to relate your use of the Services to personal information obtained from you or a reputable third party.

#### THIRD PARTY COLLECTION

Third parties may also use cookies and other tracking technologies described, but not limited to, above to collect similar usage information. The Company does not control these third parties and their use of these technologies is governed by the privacy policies of the third parties using such technologies. For more information about third part ad networks that use these technologies, see [www.aboutads.info](http://www.aboutads.info/). Most internet browsers are by default set to accept cookies. The settings for these cookies can be updated or set to block cookies altogether. To change your cookie settings please see the "help" guide of your particular internet browser for more information. To change the settings of Flash technologies, such as cookies, see the Flash Management Tool on Adobe's website. By changing certain cookie and other tracking technology setting the Services may not perform as intended. 

#### CHILDREN UNDER 18

The Company, and Services are neither intended for, nor directed to, persons under the age of 18. If InfoAtoms learns that a User or someone with access to InfoAtoms is under the age of 18, InfoAtoms will terminate the use of the Service for the User and delete any related information.

The information collected is used to provide you with Services.
