# Privacy Policy - ihelpyou, Inc.

ihelpyou, Inc. has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: ihelpyou, Inc.

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site.

This website contains links to other sites. ihelpyou, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. ihelpyou, Inc. works incredibly hard to ensure that the information contained upon our web pages is accurate, relevant, and of the high quality that our visitors have come to expect.

Further:

We are aware of software that exists which will create external links from our content to apparently related or unrelated material, which other companies have paid to have shown. We are not responsible for the quality of this software and nor are we responsible for the quality or the nature of the material that it links to. We have NO affiliation with any of these scumware companies. We have zero paid advertisers or links on the site.

We emphasize that we are strictly against this form of un-moderated and unregulated link advertising and strictly prohibit its use within our site content.

We will pursue any infringement of this notice under the maximum extent of United States Law.

For further information, please email: doug at ihelpyou.com

Our website uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect visitor's contact information (like their email address) and financial information (like their account or credit card numbers). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders and information about our company to our customers. The customer's contact information is also used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services. Absolutely no financial information or credit card information is stored on the ihelpyou, Inc. server. ihelpyou, Inc. does not know your information. We use www.linkpoint.com as our online processor. LinkPoint is owned by 1st Data Corporation. It's one of the largest online processors in the world, and has the highest quality security available.

## Security

This website has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.  
Anytime a visitor purchases a product or service from us at our website, they go directly to a secure server. If paying by credit card, a visitor is taken to linkpoint.com. This company uses a very secure server and are highly respected throughout the world in regards to secure transactions.

## Private Search Engine Forums

If your donation is enough to give you access to our Private Forums, your subscription runs out in 6 months or in one year. We do "Not" automatically renew your subscription. You have to register again to renew either for 6 months or one year.
