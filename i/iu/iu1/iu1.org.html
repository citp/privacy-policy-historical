Privacy Policy

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us as you use the IU1 website.

What information do we collect?

The IU1 website collects anonymous, non-personally identifiable information on all visitors to our website. Examples of the data that we collect include:

  • Browser information, like the type and version that a visitor uses
  • Operating system information, also like the type and version that a visitor uses
  • The website that referred a visitor to our site
  • The date and time a visitor came to our site
  • Whether the visitor had viewed our site previously

More personal information may be collected at times, but only when that information is provided by the visitor. Examples of such collections include when a site user fills out a form on our site (ie. a contact form, email newsletter sign-up form, etc.).

How do we collect visitor information?

IU1.org incorporates the use of browser cookies, a small file or collection of files stored on your browsing device that helps to identify and track users of a website. The cookie helps to identify a user when they leave and return to a site, or they can help in determining a user’s path through a website.

How do we use the information that’s collected?

Information we obtain from users’ cookies or responses collected on web forms are used to help improve the IU1 website. Analysis of user behavior can be used to improve the design and functionality of our site in order to make it more useful to those who rely on it.

If you provide us with personal contact information, we may use it to further communicate with you. You will always have the option of opting out of this.

With the exception of Google Analytics (a service we use to analyze visitor information and behavior) and instances where a court or legal order demands it, we will never share any of the information you provide us with third parties without your express permission.

Your consent

By using our website, you consent to this privacy policy.

Updates to this policy

We reserve the right to update this policy in the future as we see fit.

Questions

If you should have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, we invite you to contact us using our online contact form or by using the contact information found at the bottom of this page.
