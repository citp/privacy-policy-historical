# Privacy Policy - IKEA Hackers

Welcome to IKEAHackers.

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to [contact us](http://ikeahackers.net/contact "contact form").

At www.ikeahackers.net, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by www.ikeahackers.net and how it is used.

**Log Files**  
Like many other Web sites, www.ikeahackers.net makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

**Cookies and Web Beacons**  
www.ikeahackers.net does not use cookies.

**DoubleClick DART Cookie**  
.:: Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on www.ikeahackers.net.

.:: Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to www.ikeahackers.net and other sites on the Internet.

.:: Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

www.ikeahackers.net has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. www.ikeahackers.net’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.
