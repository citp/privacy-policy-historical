Privacy Policy

Ltd ("Travenues"), with registered office at 58/10, Manekshaw Marg, Delhi Cantt., New Delhi - 110 010. iXiGO helps you search, compare and buy travel products (e.g. flight tickets) from third party suppliers. iXiGO takes your privacy extremely seriously and will use your personal information only in conformance with the terms stated herein. iXiGO will neither sell nor rent your personal information to third parties; however, your personal information may be shared with third parties in some circumstances under certain restrictions described later. This Privacy Policy is incorporated into iXiGO Terms of Use. By making use of iXiGO, you agree to the Terms of Use as well as the use and disclosure of information provided to iXiGO as per the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information Collection and Use by iXiGO:

You can visit iXiGO without revealing any personally identifiable information. We collect personally identifiable information if you voluntarily submit them to us while registering for the service or while making use of the Site. You also have the option of not providing such information. However, in such a case you may not be able to make full use of certain portions of our site (such as customization of our search results based on your preferences). We may make use of your personally identifiable information to contact you to provide legitimate service information about iXiGO or our travel partners which we think may be of interest to you.

3. Cookies:

Cookies are small text files stored on user's hard drive containing data and information about the user. iXiGO may place cookies on your computer to enhance your overall site experience. These cookies generally do not allow us to personally identify you. However, external websites including our search partners may place their own cookies on your computer during your visit to their external website through links provided on our site. Use of the information contained in these cookies may be subject to their own privacy policies. Additionally, we may allow advertisers to place their own cookies in order to be able to serve more relevant adverts on this or other sites by tracking some of the searches and clicks on iXiGO as well as your response to each ad. In general, none of these third parties can make use of this information to identify you personally.
Additionally, some third-party advertising companies we partner with to serve ads when you visit our website may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.

4. Aggregate Information:

During the normal course of operating and administering the iXiGO website, we may track certain information such as our user's IP address, domain name of our user's Internet Service Provider, number of visitors to each page and overall number of visitors to our site. Information gleaned from this data will be used only in an aggregate form to analyze trends, online user behavior, gather broad demographic data and administer the website. None of this aggregate information is linked to any personally identifiable information which can be used to identify any individual.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure:

iXiGO is committed to maintaining your privacy and will not sell or rent any of your personally identifiable information. Any organization to which we provide access to your personally identifiable information would also be required to adhere to this Privacy Policy. We may disclose your personally identifiable information if we have reasonable reasons to believe that we are required to do so by law, regulation or government authority or need to do so to protect iXiGO's rights or property. We may transfer your information to a purchaser of all or substantially all of iXiGO's assets or to a successor entity following a merger, acquisition or corporate reorganization in which iXiGO participates. Such successor entity shall be required to adhere to the terms and conditions stated herein until such time it was amended as per the process of modification set forth herein.

6. Security of Personal Information:

iXiGO stores your information in secure locations accessible only by designated staff. We also deploys industry standard tools and processes to secure our servers and make all reasonable efforts to prevent loss, theft, alteration, unauthorized disclosure or other misuse of your personal information. However, no system is fool-proof and a security breach may take place. In the event of such a breach, we may try to notify you electronically by email or by placing a notice on our website. If you have provided your information to iXiGO, then you should keep us notified of any change in your email so that we may contact you in case such a breach occurs.

7. Access to Personal Information:

iXiGO is quite serious when it comes to privacy and is committed to providing you reasonable access and assistance in managing the personal information you share with us. If you would like to access, modify or delete the information provided, please write to us at privacy@ixigo.com . We however shall not be able to delete aggregate information collected during your visits to our site as well as the data that has already been archived as part of our normal business operations.

iXiGO website contains links to other third party websites on the internet. By making use of iXiGO, you acknowledge and agree that iXiGO does not endorse or control any of the content, advertising, products or information available from such external websites and hence shall not be responsible or liable for any damage or loss arising out of use of or reliance on the content or information provided on these external resources. 9. Modifications to Privacy Policy:

Due to the evolving nature of Internet, iXiGO may have to update this privacy policy from time to time. In such an event, iXiGO shall post its updated Privacy Policy on its website to ensure that our users are kept informed about the nature of personal information collected and the ways it is used. iXiGO encourages you to review these policies from time to time. Your continued use of the service implies your acceptance of the policies then in force.
