# Privacy Policy

**General.** Ixigo, LLC ("Ixigo") is committed to full compliance with federal, state, and local laws concerning online privacy. The company recognizes the need for appropriate protection and management of any personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") provided by persons who access the Ixigo web site. The web site includes, but is not limited to, the Home page, pages dealing with products and services, and any user groups or forums. Personal Information means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, a home or other physical address, an email address or other contact information, whether at work or at home. In general, users can access the web site without revealing any Personal Information. If a user provides Personal Information, Ixigo may transfer that information across borders and from the user's country or jurisdiction to other countries or jurisdictions around the world. Ixigo is committed to comply with all applicable laws that are designed to regulate the transmission and distribution of this information. 

**Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies.** Some of the Ixigo web pages utilize "cookies" and other tracking technologies. A "cookie" is a small text file that may be used, for example, to collect information about web site activity. Some cookies and other technologies may serve to recall Personal Information previously indicated by a web user. Most browsers allow a user to control cookies, including whether or not to accept them and how to remove them. A user can set most browsers to notify the user if it has received a cookie, or a user may choose to block cookies with the browser. Note that if a user chooses to erase or block cookies, the user will need to re-enter the original user ID and password to gain access to certain parts of the web site. Tracking technologies may record information such as Internet domain and host names; Internet protocol (IP) addresses; browser software and operating system types; clickstream patterns; and dates and times that the web site is accessed. Ixigo' use of cookies and other tracking technologies provides information that may be used to improve the web site and the user's web experience. The company may also analyze information for trends and statistics. 

**Choice.** A user may choose whether or not to provide Personal Information. If a user chooses not to provide Personal Information, the user can still visit most of the company's web site, but the user may be unable to access certain options, offers, and services that involve web interaction. If a user submits information to the Ixigo web site, or elects to enter into a contractual or other business relationship with the company, Ixigo may continue to contact the user. 

**Security.** If Personal Information is disclosed to Ixigo on its web site, the company intends to take reasonable and appropriate steps, as may be required by applicable laws, to protect the Personal Information shared with the company from unauthorized access or disclosure. 

**Accuracy.** If Personal Information is disclosed, Ixigo may elect to update or correct that information. Ixigo will make reasonable efforts to incorporate updates and corrections in Personal Information provided by the user, as may be required by law. 

**Third Parties.** Information obtained by Ixigo may be used to help deliver programs, products, information and services, maintain its web site and mailing lists, develop and market new products and services, or for other lawful purposes. Ixigo is committed to compliance with applicable laws concerning distribution of information to third parties.

**Consent.** Ixigo is committed to compliance with applicable laws dealing with online privacy. By using this web site, the user consents to the terms of the Online Privacy Policy and collection and processing by Ixigo of Personal Information. User acknowledges that Ixigo cannot guarantee or warrant the complete privacy and protection of Personal Information provided. 

Price, terms, specifications, and availability are subject to change without notice. Ixigo trademarks are indicated. Other marks are the property of their respective owners.
