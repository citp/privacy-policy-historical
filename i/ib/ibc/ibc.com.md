# Privacy Statement

  
**V** isitors to the International Bank of Commerce (IBOC) public Web site remain anonymous. IBOC does not collect identifying information about visitors to our site.

IBOC uses standard software to collect non-identifying information about our visitors, such as:

- Date and time our site was accessed  
- IP address (A numeric address given to servers     
  connected to the Internet)  
- Web browser used

IBOC uses this information to create summary statistics and to determine the level of interest in information available on our site.

Visitors may elect to provide us with personal information via e-mail or online registration forms. This information is used internally, as appropriate, to handle the sender's request. It is not disseminated or sold to other organizations.

For more information contact us at [Webmaster](mailto:gplink@laredo.globalpc.net)
