Definitions
Affiliates                            Companies related by common ownership or control. They can be financial
                                      and nonfinancial companies.
                                           Our affiliates include our banks, mortgage companies, insurance
                                            companies, and securities brokerages.
Non-affiliates                        Companies not related by common ownership or control. They can be financial
                                      and nonfinancial companies.
                                           Non-affiliates we share with can include mortgage companies, insurance
                                            companies, direct marketing companies, and nonprofit organizations.
Joint marketing                       A formal agreement between non-affiliated financial companies that together
                                      market financial products or services to you.
                                           Our joint marketing partners include reputable companies such as credit
                                            card issuers and insurance companies for the purpose of informing you
                                            about products and/or services they offer.
Other important information
State laws and individual companies may give you additional rights to limit sharing. Please consult your state laws
