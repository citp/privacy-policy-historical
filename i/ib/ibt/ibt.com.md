# Bull: Products & Services

| 

Privacy

You may browse www.bull.com without providing personal information to us. However, if you want to request information or download software, we may ask you to provide us with information about yourself and your company. Providing this information is optional and not a requirement to visit our site.

We at Bull value the relationship we have with our customers and web site visitors. We want you to feel comfortable whenever you visit www.bull.com, receive our online services, or download our online offerings.

We take your privacy very seriously and we are committed to:

  * Offer you choices regarding the personally identifiable information you provide us:   
When we collect information from you, we let you know what is being collected, for what purpose it is collected, and to whom it may be disclosed. We also identify the core information that is necessary to fulfill your request. Whenever we request additional or optional information, we mark those as voluntary so that you know what is and what is not required to provide the requested service.   

  * Let you modify or correct personally identifiable information:   
We provide you the opportunity to update or modify the contact information that you have provided us through [srv.webmestre-bfr@bull.net](mailto:srv.webmestre-bfr@bull.net?subject=Privacy)  

  * Protect your personally identifiable information:   
We employ security safeguards to protect personally identifiable information from unauthorized access and misuse. Furthermore, we take measures to ensure that the information used to process your request is current and accurate.   

  * Provide you with means to contact us about our information practices and other privacy aspects, through [srv.webmestre-bfr@bull.net](mailto:srv.webmestre-bfr@bull.net?subject=Privacy)  
Should you have any questions about this privacy policy and the practices of our web sites, please contact us at [srv.webmestre-bfr@bull.net](mailto:srv.webmestre-bfr@bull.net?subject=Privacy)
