# iBuildApp :: Create Free iPhone Application Using Online Interface Builder| Android, iPad

#### We Respect Your Privacy.

iBuildApp.com is a service provided by [SolovatSoft, Inc](http://www.solovatsoft.com/).

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our web pages and the quality of our service, and is not shared with or sold to other organizations for commercial purposes, except to provide products or services you’ve requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

  1. It is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of our Terms of Service, or as otherwise required by law.
  2. We transfer information about you if SolovatSoft is acquired by or merged with another company.



#### Information Gathering and Usage:

When you register for iBuildApp.com we may ask for information such as your name, company, email address, billing address, and credit card information. SolovatSoft uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provision, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research.

iBuildApp.com is a web service that facilitates the creation of mobile applications. **SolovatSoft does not take responsibility for content created by Users of the Service.** SolovatSoft makes clear in **Terms of Service** that Users are responsible for the content utilized by them in the Service. If you feel your privacy has been violated by content from a mobile application created through the iBuildApp service, please contact that User directly.

#### Cookies:

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier that is sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive.

Cookies are required to use the iBuildApp.com service.

We use cookies to record current session information. You may be required to log back in to your iBuildApp account after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents.

#### Data Storage:

SolovatSoft uses third party vendors and hosting partners to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to run iBuildApp. Although SolovatSoft owns the code, databases, and all rights to the iBuildApp application, you retain all rights to your data.

#### Disclosure:

SolovatSoft may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with subpoenas or when your actions violate the Terms of Service.

#### Changes:

SolovatSoft may periodically update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your iBuildApp primary account holder account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
