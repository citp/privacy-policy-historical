For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the visitor's e-mail address. 

We do collect information on visitors' domains (.com, edu, .org) and on which of our pages consumers access or visit. Periodically we assess this information in the aggregate to make improvements to the content of our site. 

Information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations, is utilized only for the purpose expressed on the given registration page. We do not share or sell any visitor information with other companies. 

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending e-mail to us at the above address or calling us at the above telephone number or writing to us at the above address and telling us that you do not want to receive e-mail from our company. 

If you supply us with your postal address on-line you will only receive the information for which you provided us your address. If you wish to be removed from our mailing list, please let us know by sending e-mail to us at the above address or calling us at the above telephone number or writing to us at the above address and telling us that you do not want to receive mail from our company. 

Penguin Group (USA) web sites do employ cookies. One cookie allows visitors to our global homepage (www.penguin.com) to select their preferred Penguin Group web site and set that site as their permanent preference. This cookie does not collect or recognize any personally identifiable information.The second cookie is employed in our shopping cart to ensure that items a visitor places in his or her shopping cart remain in the cart even if a visitor leaves the site and returns again. Items are stored in the cart for 90 days. This cookie does not collect or recognize any personally identifiable information.
