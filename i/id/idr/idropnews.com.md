# Privacy Policy -

iDrop appreciates our readers and values your privacy. We will never rent, share, or sell our subscriber list. The policy below describes how we treat the information we collect when you visit our site and/or register for iDrop. Please review this carefully, and let us know if you have any questions.

**Your Information**

We will not disclose your personal information, specifically your email address, to any third party without your consent. Our goal is to consistently provide you with quality iDrop material.

**Other Information**

In order to make improvements to our site and tailor it to our audience, we may also request other information from you from time to time. These requests could include, but are not limited to, information on income level or age. We may also share this information with third parties in aggregate, anonymous form (For example, we might inform an advertiser that, “Our average subscriber earns $55,000/year, loves Apple products, and spends 1/2 of their income on Apple gadgets”). No third party will receive information from us that personally identifies you or anyone else in any way.

**Usage Information**

It is important for us to gather information on how our site is used: How much time do users spend on our site, where did they come from, and where do the go once they leave? All of this information is collected in aggregate form. None of it is personally identifiable. This information is collected only to help us to, among other things, handle traffic on iDrop, and to make sure that our service reaches you in the most efficient manner possible.

**How to Unsubscribe**

You can unsubscribe from receiving our emails at any time. You can unsubscribe by clicking on the “Unsubscribe” link in the footer of our email and following the instructions. You can also unsubscribe by clicking on the “Unsubscribe” link on this site and following the instructions.

**Third Party Advertising**

In order to support our operations, we do attempt to deliver the most relevant content in forms of display/banner ads. If these ads are not fitting to you and you are not interested, please unsubscribe from our list or contact us at contact@idropnews.com.

**Cookies**

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer, in order to help websites like ours retrieve information vital to providing the best possible service. Our cookies help us recognize you and simplify your user experience. iDrop will never use cookies to obtain any information from your computer not related to our site.

**Third Party Cookies**

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser.

**Links to Other Sites**

Aside from our unique and original content that we deliver, we sometimes reference relevant information from other websites and provide links to these sites. We are not responsible for any content that appears on these websites. For questions about these sites you may visit from iDrop, please consult their individual privacy policies.

**Security  **  
We take data security seriously and that’s why we manage all our data internally.

**Modifications  **  
We may make changes to this policy in the future. If any changes occur, we will notify you in one of our newsletters and update the site.

**Contact Us**

If you have questions about this policy, or about the site, please write to us at contact@idropnews.com .
