Ideas.com,Inc. has created this privacy statement (the “ _Privacy Policy_ ”) in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. Ideas.com, Inc. is the company that created and operates the “ideas.com” site. As used in this Policy, the words "Site" refer to this Internet Web site ( _http://www.ideas.com_ ) and its related services, "You" or "User" refers to a **user** of this Site, and "ideas.com" or "We" refers to Ideas.com, Inc.

** ** **Our Commitment to Privacy**

This Privacy Policy is incorporated in our Terms and Conditions of Use by reference and by using our service You accept the terms of this Privacy Policy. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our services change and expand. If We update this Privacy Policy, We will post the updated Policy on this page and We will post notice that this Privacy Policy has changed in the “My Home” area of the Site of each registered User and in this Policy. Unless otherwise provided below, the revised terms will take effect when they are posted. Once any changes that We make to this Privacy Policy become effective, Your continued use of the Site will be subject to such revised terms. If We substantially change how We use personally identifiable information from the uses stated in this Policy, We will notify You by email and provide You with an opportunity to opt out (i.e., request that such changes not affect previously collected Personally Identifiable Information) at that time. However, because We may not be able to accommodate having users continuing to use the Site under an older version of the Privacy Policy, in the event that You choose to opt out, We may terminate Your account. 

**This Site contains links to other sites.**  
Ideas.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of any other Web site to which We link or which links to ideas.com. If You visit linked Web sites, We encourage You to read the posted privacy statements applicable to such other Web sites.

** ** **Information We Collect**

  * **Registration**   
If You register to buy or sell ideas using ideas.com, We collect and store information that specifically identifies You or Your account ("Personally Identifiable Information"). Personally Identifiable Information includes information that You provide when registering for the Site, which currently includes Your User name, password, first name, last name, corporation name (if applicable), state and country of residence, email address, and other information as may be requested from You and that You choose to provide when You register. We also collect and store ideas, submissions or other content that You choose to provide to the Site or to other parties through the Site, including information You place on the Site and Your responses to contests, pulls, and promotions. We may also collect and track information related to Your interaction with our customer service, emails and letters that You send us, complaints, reports of abusive conduct, and information related to Your interaction other parties, including other members’ or users’ comments about You. We may, in the future, include other optional requests for information from registered users, to help us tailor the Site for and deliver personalized information to these users.We may also store IP addresses, login times, pages visited and referrer logs as part of Your account information to help us identify You and to protect our Site against abuse. 

  * **The Use of Cookies**   
The "help" portion of the toolbar on most browsers will tell You how to prevent Your browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify You when You receive a new cookie, or how to disable cookies altogether. However, You will not be able to participate in our Site if Your browser does not accept cookies. Because Your information is required to ensure proper credit to any ideas You submit, ideas cannot be submitted with cookies disabled. If You are not a registered User, You may view some portions of the Site without having cookies turned on, but You will not have access to other portions of the Site (for example, portions that allow You to post information and communicate with other parties) without accepting cookies.

** ** **The Way We Use Information**

  * **Registration Information**   
The following describes how We intend to use Your Personally Identifiable Information. Due to uncertainties in the laws protecting online personal information and limitations of technology, We cannot guarantee that Your Personally Identifiable Information will never be disclosed except as expressly stated in this Privacy Policy. Without limiting the foregoing, We may be required by a court or other governmental authority to disclose or allow access to Personally Identifiable Information, or third parties may attempt unlawfully to access or intercept Personally Identifiable Information. We reserve the right to disclose any information, including Personally Identifiable Information, if required by law or by an order of a court, or to law enforcement or governmental officials, if We believe, in our sole judgment, such disclosure is necessary or appropriate to assist in criminal investigations or to avoid liability or damage to Ideas.com, Inc. or our Buyers and/or users. You authorize Ideas.com, Inc. to disclose Your registration information to Buyers and/or users if we believe, in our sole judgment, such disclosure is necessary or appropriate to assist in the investigation of fraud, illegal activity, intellectual property infringement or other violation of a third party’s rights. While we will use commercially reasonable efforts to protect Your Personally Identifiable Information, we cannot promise and You should not expect that Your information will remain at all times private.

We use Personally Identifiable Information for our own internal purposes including, for example, to communicate with You, to inform You about updates to our services or opportunities on ideas.com that may be of interest to You, and to provide You with information relating to transactions that You conduct on the Site.

In general, We do not rent or sell our users' Personally Identifiable Information. If You register to submit ideas, information or content to the Site, We may make certain Personally Identifiable Information available through a “user profile” or a similar description to our Buyers or other third parties who access and view Your submission to assist them in evaluating Your submission and contacting You about Your submission. Buyers or other users of the Site who may view Your submissions may also be able to see Your User name, Your name, email address, submitted ideas, comments and ratings about You by other Buyers.

  * **Advertising**   
We may send You email communications including information that We think may be of interest to You. You may opt-out of receiving these promotional email communications, either when You register, or at any time thereafter by sending a message to [_Unsubscribe@ideas.com_](mailto:unsubscibe@ideas.com). We use non-identifying and aggregated information to better design our Web site and to share with advertisers. For example, We may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our Web site, but We would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals. If We use or share with third parties the Personally Identifiable Information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above, We will provide You an opportunity to opt-out of such uses.

** ** **Our Commitment To Data Security**

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, We will use commercially reasonable methods to keep Personally Identifiable Information securely in our files and systems. Please note that with current technology, We cannot guarantee the security of Your data as it travels over the Internet. We work to protect the security of Your information during transmission by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software, which encrypts information You input. It is important for You to protect against unauthorized access to Your password and to Your computer. Be sure to sign off when finished using a shared computer. If You give access to Your User Name and password to another party, You may be held responsible for such party’s actions using Your account and You may lose protection for Your Personally Identifiable Information. If You believe that Your password is no longer secure, You may change Your password in the “My Profile” section of the Web site after you login.

** ** **Our Commitment To Children's Privacy**

Protecting the privacy of children is especially important. For that reason, We do not collect or maintain information at our Web site from those We actually know are under 13, and no part of our Web site is structured to attract anyone under 13. To register and/or submit an idea to ideas.com a user must be at least 18 years of age.

** ** **Terminating Your Membership**

To terminate Your membership, send email to [_Service@ideas.com_](mailto:Service@ideas.com). There may be a delay in processing Your termination request and in updating our records, and this may result in a delay in terminating any active use of Your Personally Identifiable Information that is permitted under this Privacy Policy. In the event that Your membership terminates, We may continue to store Your Personally Identifiable Information for our own internal business records. We may at our discretion delete Your Personally Identifiable Information at any time after termination, in which event, Your Personally Identifiable Information cannot be recovered. You may continue to receive email communications until You have requested to be removed from our email lists by following the procedure herein.
