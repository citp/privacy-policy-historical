# Ideal808 - Magic the Gathering MTG Singles, Yugioh Single Cards, Cardfight Vanguard, Boxes and Starter Decks! Premier Store Privacy Policy

To assist us with our goal to achieve the highest quality of products and services, we use data from our interactions with you and other customers. Your privacy is a priority to us, and to ensure that your personal information is handled in a safe, secure, and responsible manner company policies are constantly reinforced by management. We have outlined this privacy policy below to inform you what kind of information we collect, how it is processed, with whom it may be shared, and how you may access some of the information you provide us. As we continue to improve and develop Ideal808.com, our privacy policy may be altered at any given time. Please use this page as a reference for the most current details regarding our online policies.

### Website and Information Security

All of your online credit card transactions are completely secured with Ideal808.com. This website uses Secure Socket Layering (SSL), the industry-standard encryption technology that ensures safe internet transmissions of your personal information. If you prefer to purchase by phone, please call 808.847.6000, Monday - Saturday, 3:00 pm - 11:00 pm EST (Eastern Standard Time). One of our customer care associates will be more than glad to assist you.

### Usage of Information

We will collect information for the usage of:

  * Completing your purchase transactions.
  * Keeping you informed about the status order.
  * Providing the services you requested.
  * Sending you our catalogs and other promotions via e-mail.
  * Improving our merchandise selections, customer service, and overall shopping experiences.



### Sharing of Your Information

All customer information is kept strictly confidential and shall not be shared or bartered with in any way with another company other than, Ideal808.com.

### Your Account and Membership

To maximize your shopping experience we ask that all shoppers register with Ideal808.com. by storing your information on our secure server, you will only have to enter your e-mail address and password to have access and ability to add, delete, or change information under your account. By using Ideal808.com services, you are to take responsibility for the safety of your password and using it on our website. You agree to take responsibility for any activity that takes place under your account or password. - Children (people under the age of 18): Ideal808.com does not sell to children, unless accompanied by the parent or guardian who will buy on behalf of the person under 18 years of age. We are under the impression that any order receive are by an adult or accompanying parent or guardian.

### Promotional Emails...“Spam”

\- Members benefits such as promotional emails are sent only if you have requested to receive them. Should you decide at a latter date that you no longer want to receive such promotional emails, simply click on the link at the bottom of the e-mail received to complete your request.

### Cookies and Browser Settings

In order to be able to use our online shopping cart system, you will need to have cookies enabled in your web browser. This will allow you view and access your member information and other secured pages. To set you browser cookie settings, find your browser’s internet options or privacy section settings, and set your privacy setting to Medium.
