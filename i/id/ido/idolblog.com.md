# NZ Idol Blog - for the fans of New Zealand Idol. Congratulations Ben Lummis!

Your privacy is important to us. The information we collect in connection with our online forums and NZ Idol fan community is used to provide an interactive experience. We use this information to facilitate participation in these online forums and communities and to email you updates about the site. Contact information, including email addresses will not be disclosed or sold to third parties. 

We may disclose personally identifiable information in response to legal process, for example, in response to a court order or a law enforcement agency's request.

Any information automatically retrieved, such as website browsing patterns, will be used only in aggregate form (so that no individual visitors are identified).

Although we take appropriate measures to safeguard against unauthorised disclosures of information, we cannot assure you that personally identifiable information that we collect will never be disclosed in a manner that is inconsistent with this Privacy Policy.
