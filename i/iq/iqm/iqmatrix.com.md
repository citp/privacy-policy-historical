# IQ Matrix | Privacy Policy

**1\. INTRODUCTION**  


IQ Matrix is committed to the protection of your personal information (as defined in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)).

IQ Matrix policies and procedures include this Privacy Policy which has been designed to ensure that your personal information is protected and handled in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). This Privacy Policy has been modelled but is not bound by the National Privacy Principles.

This Privacy Policy contains the following important information for customers and users of the IQ Matrix website ([ _www.iqmatrix.com_](http://www.studymatrixart.com/))

This Privacy Policy deals with the following areas:

\- Collection of your personal information; 
  * \- How IQ Matrix uses your information; 
  * \- Disclosure of your information; 
  * \- Security of your information; 
  * \- How to access, correct or update your information; and 
  * \- How to contact IQ Matrix. 
  * \- Acceptance of our policies. 



**1\. COLLECTION OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION**

**1.1** IQ Matrix' primary reason for collecting Personal Information is to supply customers or users with the products and services it offers. IQ Matrix only collects Personal Information where the information is necessary for IQ Matrix to perform of its functions or activities.

**1.2** IQ Matrix will only collect Personal Information from individuals with consent.

**1.3** IQ Matrix collects information in the following way:

**i)** When you buy goods and services from IQ Matrix website.

**ii)** When you setup an account or register withIQ Matrix through its website.

**iii)** When financial transactions are conducted with IQ Matrix International or through its website.

**2\. How IQ Matrix uses your information**

**2.1** Â  IQ Matrix use the information collected for the primary reason of providing its goods and services to you. The use of the information includes administration and billing, account management, communication to you about goods and services, account keeping and other related functions.

**2.2** IQ Matrix may use your information to communicate offers of goods and services to you or of its related entities.

**3\. Disclosure of your information**

**3.1** The information that IQ Matrix collects is disclosed to third parties when you have:

**i)** consented to the use or disclosure of the information;

**ii)** when third party financial transactions are required to be processed to provide IQ Matrix goods or services.

**iii)** where we reasonably believe that the use or disclosure is necessary to lessen or prevent a serious, immediate threat to someone's health or safety or the public's health or safety;

**iv)** where we reasonably suspect that unlawful activity has been, is being or may be engaged in and the use or disclosure is a necessary part of our investigation or in reporting the matter to the relevant authorities;

**v)** where such use or disclosure is required under or authorised by law.

**vi)** where we reasonably believe that the use or disclosure is necessary for prevention, investigation, prosecution and punishment of crimes or wrongdoings or the preparation for, conduct of, proceedings before any court or tribunal or the implementation of the orders of a court or tribunal by or on behalf of an enforcement body.

**4\. Security of your information**

**4.1** IQ Matrix places a great importance on the security of all information associated with our customers. We have security measures in place to attempt to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of personal information under our control.

**4.2** IQ Matrix retains the information you provide to us to enable us to verify transactions and customer details and to retain adequate records for legal and accounting purposes.

**4.3** Whilst IQ Matrix strives to protect customer information, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100 per cent secure. As a result IQ Matrix cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information transmitted to it or from its online products or services, and users do so at their own risk.

**5\. How to access, correct or update your information**

**5.1** IQ Matrix is committed to maintaining accurate, timely, relevant and appropriate information about its customers and website users.

**5.2.** As long as your request for your personal information is reasonable and you provide us with a proper reason as to why you want to access the information, we will give you access to that information by contacting us via our contacts page on this website.

**5.3** We will attempt to correct inaccurate information upon receiving advice from you. To ensure confidentiality, details of your personal information will be passed on to you only if we are satisfied that the information relates to you.

**5.4** If we refuse to provide you with access or correct the personal information held about you by us, then we will provide reasons for such refusal.

**6\. How to contact IQ Matrix**

**6.1** If you have any information about our Privacy Policy please contact us by visiting the contacts page on this website.

**7\. ACCEPTING OUR POLICIES**

**7.1** By using our site and dealing with us you agree to our Terms of Use and our Privacy Policy.
