# Privacy Policy :: iQlik Movies

* In cases where certain joint offerings with businesses affiliated with Dream Axiz are made as a part of the Services, Dream Axiz may share user information related to those offerings with that affiliated business.

* We may employ other companies and individuals to perform certain functions including, but not limited to, sending e-mail, removing repetitive information from user lists, analyzing data, and providing marketing assistance. In such cases, user information shall be shared with such companies and individuals employed by Dream Axiz but such information as is shared may not be used for any other purpose.

* With the future development of Our business, We may sell or buy other additional services or business units. As a part of such transactions, information collected by Dream Axiz shall be shared with the newly formed entities. Moreover, in the event that Dream Axiz or substantially all of the assets of Dream Axiz are acquired, user information will also be included in the transaction.

* We release account and other personal information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with law, enforce or apply Our Terms and other agreements, or protect the rights, property, or safety of Dream Axiz, Our users, or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction. However, at no point does this include selling, renting, sharing, or otherwise disclosing personally identifiable information from customers for commercial purposes in violation of the commitments set forth in this Privacy Notice.
