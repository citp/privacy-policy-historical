# IMS HEALTH - Home

Data Protection Notification

IMS HEALTH will collect personal data about each permitted user which visits the website. The information will be used for billing purposes and to compile management/information statistics. By accepting these Terms and Conditions, each permitted user agrees that IMS HEALTH may transfer these data to companies within the IMS Health group and with selected third party partners, including companies outside the European Union. In addition, the IMS HEALTH group may automatically store information about the way each permitted user uses this website which will assist in any future visits to the site. IMS HEALTH may also compile statistical usage information relating to a particular client and provide this information to that client as part of the IMS HEALTH chargeable services agreement. Statistical usage information of this type will be anonymised and will not be passed on to any other third party. 

Cookies

IMS HEALTH uses standard technology called cookies on this site. Cookies are small data files that are downloaded and stored on your computer when you visit a particular website. By assigning a unique data element to each visitor, the website is then able to recognize repeat users and track usage patterns. This information is used to enable us to better serve you and your organization when you return to this site, by improving the contents of our web site and determining which features and services are most important to our users. The cookie does not extract personally identifiable information about you.
