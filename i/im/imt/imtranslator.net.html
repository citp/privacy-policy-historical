Privacy Policy

Smart Link Corporation is committed to respecting and protecting your personal privacy. Our privacy policy is succinctly explained below.
Questions regarding our privacy policy should be directed to:

Smart Link Corporation
2081 Business Center Drive, Suite 130
Irvine, CA 92612

Collected Information
Whenever you visit our web sites, Smart Link Corporation's web servers log automatically to receive and record anonymous information from your web browser including your IP Address, Smart Link web sites cookie information, Smart Link web sites page(s) you request and the time spent on each web page.

Use of Information
Smart Link Corporation uses this anonymous information to customize your experience on our site, to show you more relevant advertising messages, and to improve our services in general. For example, when you use Smart Link Translator, Smart Link can collect aggregate information such as the language pairs used and number of translations done on each language pair to provide more relevant advertisements. We do not attempt to link this information to a specific person.

Logging IP Addresses
For each visitor to our web page, our web server does not recognize any information regarding the domain or email address.

Collecting Email Addresses
We will only use this information to administer our mailings to you. Smart Link Corporation does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with a third party.

Certain Exceptional Disclosures
We may disclose your information if necessary to protect our legal rights or if the information relates to actual or threatened harmful conduct or potential threats to the physical safety of any person. Disclosure may be required by law or if we receive legal processes.

Text Processing Security
We use appropriate security measures to protect information that you provide to us for translation purposes. We only store the provided text during system operations such as Decoding, Spell Checking, Dictionary queries, Back Translation and Translation. We purge our systems of your text and no logs or cookies are kept after you leave our web sites.

Use of Cookies
No personal information is stored in the cookie.

Security Measures
We operate secure data networks protected by industry standard firewall and password protection. We have security measures in place to attempt to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of your user data.

Third-Party Advertising
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you.

Third-Party Cookies
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertisers may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.

This privacy policy applies to web pages ending in paralink.com, imtranslator.com or imtranslator.net that contain a link to this privacy policy at the bottom of the web page.

If you have any questions about this, contact us at privacy-support.
