Impact Media Privacy Policy

Introduction

Impact Media provide this website for information purposes regarding our company and services offered. Additional features are offered in the partner’s login facility which enables registered users to access further information.
We are fully committed to protecting your privacy. The following information describes how information collected via our website is secured, stored and transferred.  Please read this document carefully as use of this site is legally bound accordingly.

What information do we collect?

When visiting our website there are several services requiring personal information to be supplied. In order to contact us or utilise our call back service certain personal contact details must be given, including name, email address and contact telephone number. Registering with us to receive our monthly newsletter requires providing personal data including name and email address.

Access to the information collected

Impact Media retain all information collected from the website and in no circumstances is data divulged to third parties without prior consent from the persons concerned.

Securing and storing your data

We follow strict policies when dealing with your personal information to ensure the highest standard of security is adhered to. Our computer systems are installed with various firewall applications in order to protect your details and guarantee that no documents are damaged, destroyed or disclosed to third parties without necessary permission. All information received via the website is stored in our restricted office with no access to unauthorised persons.

Variations to the policy

Impact Media reserve the right to alter and change information provided in this document at our own discretion and therefore this document should be regularly referred to for updates and variations.
