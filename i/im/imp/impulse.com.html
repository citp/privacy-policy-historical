Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting the Impulse Point web site. Your privacy is very important to us and we are committed to protecting both the privacy and security of your personal information. Simply stated – our policy is to collect no personal information about you unless you choose to make such information available to us.

If you do choose to share personal information with us, we will not sell, rent, trade, or share that information; however, the information will be saved for a designated period of time to comply with the state of Florida’s archiving policies. Information will not be disclosed to third parties or other government agencies unless required by state or federal law.

children’s online privacy protection

Impulse Point is committed to protecting children’s privacy online. Our web site is not intentionally designed for or directed at children. In accordance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998 (COPPA), Impulse Point will not intentionally collect or maintain information about anyone under 13 years of age.

If you have questions about our privacy policy, or have ideas about improving the policy, please feel free to contact us at info@impulse.com. You can also reach us by telephone at 863-802-3738.
