# Welcome to Imgrr.com, a free image upload solution. Simply browse, select, and upload!

Imgrr.com policy is to respect and protect the privacy of our users. Imgrr.com will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent.

Imgrr.com will not sell or give your email address to anyone and you will never be placed on any lists. However, Imgrr.com may disclose your information when Imgrr.com believes in good faith that the law requires it.

Imgrr.com may use your email address to inform you about Imgrr.com major service changes, important updates, or special promotions.

Imgrr.com website record your IP address but uses it only for internal purposes. It will never be given to any other person/company/website for any reason.

Imgrr.com website uses cookies only to maintain your information as you move throughout this site. A cookie is a small piece of information used to identify an individual when the person is accessing a site. At no time is any personal information stored in a cookie, nor is any tracking information made available to other sites for any reason.

Imgrr.com reserve the right to change this Policy anytime without prior notification. In this case the new Policy will be published at http://imgrr.com/info.php?act=privacy_policy

If you have any question, please do not hesitate to [Contact us](http://imgrr.com/contact.php?act=contact_us).
