# Imglobal Online Marketing System

Our Privacy Policy

IMGlobal.me is committed to protecting the privacy of our online visitors and community. We collect information from online visitors who ask to be on our newsletter mailing list and/or receive periodic email announcements. Anyone who wishes can choose to be removed from our mailing list at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email.

In addition, online visitors who wish to purchase a membership through our Web site must provide their name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and credit card number and expiration date. All credit card transactions occur over a secure connection and third party processor and no credit card details are kept or stored by IMGlobal.me.

All online visitor data collected by IMGlobal.me is protected against unauthorized access. We will not sell, trade, or give your personal information to other companies or organisations.

Database Integrity

IMglobal and employee will never solicit any of our community members leads, members or personal database for any reason. Our community is only built around growing your brand and Business online. Our databases are secured by Bank standard 256 SSL encryption and our servers are fully managed and monitored real time 24/7/365 for any security threats.
