Privacy Policy


Last Updated January 2008

The following statement applies to data collected by Imagination Technologies through its website and with respect to products available for download, as well as its related product support services.

Personal Information Collected by Imagination Technologies

In order to access some areas and information on this website you will be asked to provide personal information, such as your e-mail address, name, work address or telephone number. We may also collect demographic information, such as your postcode, age, gender, preferences, interests and favourites. Imagination Technologies uses the personal information collected to operate and improve its sites and deliver the information or to communicate with you. When you use the Imagination Technologies website, Imagination Technologies may also collect certain information about your computer to track and evaluate your use of the website and products. For example, we may log environmental variables, such as browser type, operating system, and the Internet Protocol (IP) address of your computer. Personal information collected on Imagination Technologies sites may be stored and processed in the United Kingdom or any other country, and by using a Imagination Technologies site or product available online, you consent to any such transfer of information outside of your country.

Third Party Access to Your Personal Information

If you give us any personal information, we will use that only within Imagination Technologies. We will not share it with anyone else, except in the following circumstances: (a) in order to fulfil any contractual rights or obligations established by any online agreements accepted by you; (b) to protect and defend the rights or property of Imagination Technologies (including the enforcement of our agreements); or (c) to comply with the law or legal process served on Imagination Technologies.

Security of Your Personal Information

Imagination Technologies is committed to protecting the security of your personal information. We use a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorised access, use, or disclosure.

Changes to This Privacy Statement

We will occasionally update this privacy statement. When we post changes to this statement, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of this statement.
