# ImagesHentai - Tu Hosting de Imagenes Hentai

When you sign up for an account with ImagesHentai.Com, we collect personal information from you including your email address and a password. 

We may also collect the URL from which you linked into our site, your IP address, and your browser type and version. All of which are collected in an anonymous manner without being linked to any of your personal information. 

User privacy is something we take very seriously at ImagesHentai.Com. We do not, and have never, sold or disseminated (except in cases where required to by law enforcement agencies) the personal information of our users to any parties outside of ImagesHentai.Com. We use account information for the purposes of creating and maintaining individual user accounts, contacting a user in case of a problem with their account, for statistical purposes, to administer our systems, and to provide technical support. 

If you have any question, please do not hesitate to [contact us](http://imageshentai.com/contact.php?act=contact_us).
