# Immigration.com - Privacy Policy

### Privacy Policy

    We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to the immigration site will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented. 

    Certain information is required for registration, but other requested information is optional and may be given at your discretion. 

    The INS tracking Information is password-protected for your privacy and security. 

    The site provides Message boards for its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in this area becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. 

    This site contains links to other sites. Immigration.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

    [Immigration.com](http://www.immigration.com/) may amend this policy from time to time. In case of any concern about your privacy, please write to us at Info@Immigration.com we will respond to you immediately.
