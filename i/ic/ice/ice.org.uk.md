# Institution of Civil Engineers :: Privacy Policy

Users should be aware that if they disclose personal information (eg. E-mail addresses) on any public access Web site then this information may be collected and used by third parties and advertisers to send unsolicited messages. We strongly advise users not to disclose private contact information, particularly home address for public view on the Internet if they do not wish to receive such unsolicited correspondence.
