This is how information learned about you from your visit to our website is handled. The information received depends on what you do when you visit IPWatchdog.com.

    If you visit IPWatchdog.com to browse, or to read or download information:

      My hosting company collects and stores various information, but to the best of my knowledge and understanding none of the information collected is personally identifying information. The information that is collected, and to which I have access, includes: (1) the name of the domain and host from which you access the Internet (for example, aol.com or princeton.edu); (2) the browser software you use and your operating system; (3) the date and time you access the site; and (4) the Internet address of the website from which you linked directly to IPWatchdog.com.

      Information collected by my hosting company is used to measure the number of visitors, where and how visitors find out about IPWatchdog.com, and daily traffic. I do not consider these and other general Web site statistics to be confidential.

    If you respond to a survey question:

      To the best of my knowledge and understanding the company I use to conduct survey questions (Surveymonkey.com) does not collect any personally identifiable information about any visitors. Notwithstanding, I have set up the surveys such that only the first response will be counted. This will prevent one or more individuals from skewing the data set by voting multiple times. In order to accomplish this a cookie is placed on the users computer that will identify that a particular computer has already responded to a particular survey question.

    If you choose to send an email or submit an online form:

      You have the freedom to select what if any personally identifiable information is sent to me when you send an e-mail or through an online form. Those who submit an online form should know that the company I use to power the forms (I-Depth.com) forwards the form to me. To the best of my knowledge and understanding they do not collect or keep any personally identifiable information, but rather merely act as a conduit.

    If you subscribe to the IPWatchdog.com Week in Review:

      If you subscribe to the IPWatchdog.com Week in Review your e-mail address and name will be saved and used to provide periodic e-mail alerts, including the weekly newsletter titled the IPWatchdog.com Week in Review. If you wish to unsubscribe just sent an e-mail to unsubscribe@ipwatchdog.com.

    If you post a question or comment to the Message Board:

      Any information contained in a post to my Message Board is available for everyone to see. You have the freedom to select what if any personally identifiable information is posted. You should not post sensitive information or personally identifiable information on the Message Board. You may post anonymously and need not provide your name or e-mail address. If you do wish to provide an e-mail address you may wish to consider setting up a web based e-mail account first and then providing that address. This will allow others who read your post to contact you directly and privately, but you will not be providing your personal e-mail address.

    Please be aware that there are many links on IPWatchdog.com to sites that are owned, operated and maintained by others. I do not have any control over these other sites. When visiting a new site you should refer to any applicable terms and conditions, legal disclaimers and/or any privacy policy that governs that particular site. I make no representations regarding the policies and practices of sites that you link to from IPWatchdog.com. I merely provide these links to facilitate the acquisition of hopefully useful information.
