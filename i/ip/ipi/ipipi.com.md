# Text Message - Send SMS - International Texting - ipipi.com

ipipi.com ("The Service") is a service provided by Upside Wireless Inc. ("Upside"). We take your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy. 

**Coverage**

  * This Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") covers how our service treats personal information that our service collects and receives, including information related to your past use of this service and your communications with our service and Upside. 
  * This Privacy Policy applies to activities of Upside and its affiliates and subsidiaries (collectively "Upside", "we" or "us"). 



**Definition**

  * Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number. It also includes information you store in your account about your contacts (for example their names and contact information) and transactional information that is generated in the process of your using our service (messages you send and receive including their content, phone numbers and/or email addresses).



**Information Collection and Use**

  * We collect personal information when you register with our service and when you use our service. 
  * When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, zip code and personal interests. Once you register with our service and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us. 
  * We collect information about your login and messaging transactions with us. 
  * We store information about your contacts - people you interracted with using our service. 
  * You can view the entire content of what we store about you from a GDRP report available in your account. To view it, please login to your account and click Account->GDRP Report. 
  * We automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP Address, cookie information, and the page you request. 
  * We use information to fulfill your requests for our services, to improve our services, contact you, to conduct internal research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal purposes. 



**Information Sharing and Disclosure**

**We do not rent, sell, or share personal information about you, nor contacts you enter in your address book,** with other people or non-affiliated companies except: 

* when we have your explicit permission,
* when we are requested to respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.
* to prevent misuse. We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of our terms of use, or as otherwise required by law. 

**Cookies**

  * We may set and access cookies on your computer for the purpose of allowing you easier access to your account. 



**Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information and Preferences**

  * You can edit your personal account information, including your preferences at any time. 
  * We reserve the right to send you certain communications relating to our services, such as service announcements and administrative messages. 
  * You can delete your account on Account->GDRP page in your account. 



**Confidentiality and Security**

  * We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs. 
  * We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that protect your personal information.
  * Your Account Information is password-protected. 
  * Your account password is encrypted. 



**Changes to this Privacy Policy**

  * If we change our privacy policies and procedures, we will post those changes on this page. If we make any changes to this Privacy Policy that materially change how we treat your personal information, we will endeavor to provide you with reasonable notice of such changes, such as via prominent notice in the Quora Platform or to your email address of record, and where required by law, we will obtain your consent or give you the opportunity to opt out of such changes. 



**Questions and Suggestions**

  * If you have questions or suggestions, please [ contact us ](https://web.archive.org/web/20181027021346id_/https%3A//www.ipipi.com/ContactUs.do)
