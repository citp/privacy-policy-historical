# Privacy policy

Learn about 7Sudoku.com's privacy policy.

## Google analytics

We use Google Analytics for gathering statistical data about the usage of the website by our visitors. This is done exclusively with the intent of improving the website based on our visitors' location and preferred operating system and browser.

Google Analytics is a software that collects information such as what browser you are using, what pages of the site you visited and how long you spent on each page, whether you are a new visitor or you visited the website before, the link you followed to reach the website and more.

This website does not have interest and does not collect any data that can personally identifies you.

Google provides a tool for you to opt-out of analytics tracking. Click [here](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) for more information.

## Third party advertising companies

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this website. Google's use of the [DART cookie](http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/faq.aspx) enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
