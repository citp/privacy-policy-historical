Privacy
Credit Card Information

Customer credit card information is never shared with any third party. If you have completed credit card transactions for ZoomBucks products on ZoomBucks, your credit card information will not be shared.

Anonymous & Personal Information:

ZoomBucks collects anonymous information for statistical purposes. The information we collect is as follows:

  • IP Address
  • Browser
  • User Agent
  • Referrer
  • Platform Type

We may also collect other computer or geographical information (geographical information is determined by the IP Address). ZoomBucks does not require your email address and personal information unless you sign up for a free account. Searches and clicks are tracked along with the IP for statistical and user account purposes. The information is used to track trends, user behavior and other statistical purposes. Personal information is only collected if you sign up an account. You can change or correct the personal information at anytime. If you wish to cancel your account, please use our help desk.

Contact Us

When users contact us, their information is stored in a support system database.

Links to other sites

ZoomBucks contains link to external sites. Zoombucks is not responsible for the privacy or terms of other sites. Once you leave our site, please read the privacy statements of every website.

Cookies

ZoomBucks stores cookies for the following purposes:

  • Statistical - Cookies are stored when an anonymous user visits our site so that we can track browsing patterns. Please refer to "Anonymous User Information" to understand what's tracked.
  • User sessions - User sessions are created when a user logs into their account. The user information is kept in a cookie to track the user is logged in.

Toolbar

You may be retargeted based on previous website visits. ZoomBucks does not store web browsing history. Browsed pages are read by the ZoomBucks Online toolbar for the purpose of ad targeting.

Transfer, Merger, or Sale

If ZoomBucks is involved in a merger, or acquired by another company, the users personal information may also be part transferred.

Sharing Information with 3rd Parties

You will receive opportunities to sign up with additional offers during the join process. The information entered on the ZoomBucks sign up form will be automatically passed to third parties and if you confirm your information with the third parties (sign up with the third parties), the third parties will be entitled to your information and can use your information to market offers. To get more information on the privacy policy and terms of the third parties, please visit the third parties websites.

Changes to Privacy Statement

In the event that our privacy statement is to change, we will send an email to all members and will post a notice on our web site.
