Team Zofff considers personal privacy to be of paramount importance. It governs all its transactions with strict rules thereby ensuring customers with secure transactions that do not violate any privacy policy. As such Team Zofff uses only customers name and personal information as directed by the Privacy Policy. We concentrate on collecting information that is relevant to our dealings with you. The collected information is retained with Team Zofff for as long as required legally and as long as it is relevant for the purposes for which the data was collected. However, it has to be noted that the website allows free surfing and browsing of its contents without you having to provide your personal details.

Contact Details
Team Zofff collects customer information to process the order of a product on the site. We use the collected data for further purchase and later claims on the website. The information that we collect includes your name, title, your gender, postal and delivery address, contact number, fax and payment details. Payment details include payment card and bank account details.

Uses
We can only accept the placing of your order, process payment for the same and deliver the order to you after you furnish us with your details. It also helps us to keep you updated of the status of your order if we have these details.

We also use your details to manage the website and allow you to use parts of the website which otherwise may be restricted to you. Your personal details also help us to detect fraud or site abuses and credit risk reduction and also to provide you with marketing or customer service assistance. In cases, where we sell the whole or part of the business we may require to transfer our databases that has your personal information.

More Uses
Zofff would like to walk you through the other uses that we may use your information for. First is we may use it for opinion and market research. When we use your information for such purposes we will keep your details anonymous and use it for statistical purposes.

Zofff updates its customers by sending information regarding Zofff, its site, other websites, products, sales promotions, newsletters, and matters relating to Zofffs business partners. However you can also unsubscribe from receiving them if you are not eager to receive these updates. Zofff will process your request within 7 working days.

Zofff uses personal data to ascertain location and usage of certain aspects of the site. The collected data may be passed on to third parties. The data will be anonymized and as such you will not be identified personally.

Third Party
However, it has to be noted that there are cases where we require sending across your personal details to the franchisor. It is after your approval that the franchisor can use the information to sell products to you either directly or indirectly to carry out transactions with you. Secondly, we may also send across your details to a third party, for example, courier or delivery agency so they can deliver the product to your address.

It is however to be noted that we do not rent or sell your personal details to anyone nor do we allow anyone to use your details for their marketing interests. We use your personal information in a way that is described by the privacy policy.

Apart from the above mentioned third parties we may also share your details to other companies which are in the same group, agents and sub-contractors primarily to help team Zofff with the various uses of your details in our data set. We do not encourage the exchange of personal details that do not adhere to the laws of the privacy policy. If we are required to share your personal details to third parties we carry forward with it only after your prior consent. Note that the website may contain links related to third parties or other sites. Zofff however, is not responsible for any of the privacy policy of these other sites.

We consider the protection of your personal details to be of paramount importance. We therefore take measures to keep your details safe and secure. Your personal details are stored in computers that are protected by physical and technological security devices. We also have third parties that verify our privacy policy. We do not as stated above, sell or rent your personal information to third parties. If we do we communicate your details to only a third party that offers a legally-compliant request for its disclosure.

Expectations from You
Team Zofff assures the protection of your private details. However, it is expected that as a customer, you should furnish details only that are accurate and keep Team Zofff updated of any changes that are made.

Tracking
You can track the details of your transactions by logging into the website with your details. The details which you can view include the orders that are complete, open and those that are to be dispatched. You are expected to keep your details unavailable to unauthorized third parties. We accept liability of passwords if it is our fault.
Please furnish your personal details only if you accept the terms and conditions made available here.

Fraud Prevention
Team Zofff takes the required measures to curb any fraudulent activities. Zofff, therefore has teamed up with standard and authorized agencies that are well endowed with Credit Check and Fraud prevention Policies.

Cookies
Cookies as you may be aware are minuscule text files which help Zofff server to identify your computer as a unique server when you visit pages on our website. Cookies help recognize your internet protocol address.
You may restrict the use of your cookies. However, this would only affect your usability of your website. You may be banned from visiting certain pages if you do not enable cookies. Zofff uses cookies only to enhance your experience. Zofff cookies do not contain personal details and they are free of viruses.
Cookies are used to recognize the internet browser. The data that are collected are not used without separate authorization of the user to identify visitors to Zofff website; neither is it collated with personal data on the created pseudonym holder.

Security
Zofff transactions are secured by technologies like firewall and Secure Socket Layer Coding (SSL). All the transactions are held by secure servers. To keep your transactions secure you should be careful about sending your details in unencrypted electronic communications with Zofff. We may as a measure to secure your financial details and transactions require you to provide proof of identity before disclosing information to you. You are completely responsible to keep your password secure and safe from unrequired access.
