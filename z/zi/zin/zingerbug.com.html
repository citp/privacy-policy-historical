What Information Does ZingerBug collect?

ZingerBug.com does not collect any personal information from any user. Our web servers automatically record the IP address and referring URL from each visitor.

ZingerBug.com does not employ the use of cookies however, we use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. We have contracted with several Ad networks including ValueClick and Google AdSense to monitor certain pages of our website for the purpose of reporting website traffic, statistics, advertisement 'click-throughs', and/or other activities on our website. Where authorized by us, ad networks may use cookies, web beacons, and/or other monitoring technologies to compile anonymous statistics about our website visitors. No personally identifiable information is collected by or transferred to any party other than the Advertising Network.

 

For more information about how the information is collected and used by ValueClick, please see ValueClick's privacy policy at http://www.valueclickmedia.com/member_privacy.shtml.

 

For More information on the information collected and used by Google AdSense including how to opt out of cookies, please see Google's Privacy Policy at: http://www.google.com/privacypolicy.html

 

How will you use, or potentially use, this information?

ZingerBug.com uses information collected by our servers for the sole purpose of performing anonymous statistical analysis of our web visitors.

 

What options do users have to easily limit the use of their personal information?

ZingerBug.com does not collect any personal information from any user, so we do not have an Unsubscribe function on our website.

 

Who should I contact if I have any questions about your Privacy Policy or anything else related to your website?
