zipRealty.com's Privacy Policy

At zipRealty.com, we adhere to a philosophy of integrity, trust, and mutual respect among all parties involved with the real estate transaction. We understand that the privacy and security of information that personally identifies you are important to you. Therefore, zipRealty.com has created this privacy policy in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy.

Please read the following policy to understand how your personal information will be used while you take advantage of the services we offer. This policy may change from time to time so please check back periodically. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us.

Our Privacy Policy discloses the following information:

1. What personally identifiable information is collected and how it is used

2. What general information is collected and how it is used

3. With whom personally identifiable information is shared

4. User choices regarding collection and use of information

5. User access and ability to update information

6. Security measures used to protect personally identifiable information.

What personally identifiable information is collected and how it is used

zipRealty.com collects personally identifiable information from you in order to respond to your requests for our products and services. Depending on the services you request, this personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to, your name, address, zip code, e-mail address, telephone number, and information about the properties in which you are interested. If you are seeking to buy or sell a property with another person, we will collect much of the same information about them as well.

You will be asked to provide personally identifiable information at various places in our web site, including, but not limited to, the following:

  • Tips & Strategies Newsletter - When you request this service, we collect an e-mail address in order to send you a newsletter offering tips and suggestions on selling or buying a new home.
  • Getting Started - When you register to use our web site, we collect your first and last name, e-mail address, preferred phone number, and information on whether you are buying or selling. You will also select a Login ID and Password. Your Login ID and Password help ensure that the personally identifiable information you provide to us remains secure. Your Password should never be shared with anyone.
  • Mandatory Real Estate Agency Disclosure - We collect the date and time you read the disclosure statement that we, as licensed Real Estate Agents, are required by state law to provide. This protects you by insuring that we are complying with statutory requirements.
  • Find Your Home - In order to help narrow your search for a new home, we collect information from you regarding the type of home you want, including, but not limited to: price range, number of rooms, square footage, and property type.
  • House Features - In order to complete the listing agreement, we collect information from you including the address, size, features, and condition of the home you are selling. We will also collect information that you will need to disclose to potential buyers regarding material facts as well as any reports that have been prepared for your property. These disclosures are required by law. The information is collected to facilitate your compliance with the law.
  • Purchase Negotiations - If you choose to proceed to this step, you will be engaged in a complete negotiation for the purchase of a new home. You will need to provide detailed information regarding the terms you would like included in your purchase contract. This information will include such items as the title and escrow company you would like to use, what inspections you will require or waive, what warranties you will require or waive, the amount of any deposits you will make, and your ability to make such deposits. The information you provide at this stage will depend in large part on your decisions after extensive consultation with your Online Agent and will be incorporated into a Purchase Sale Contract as required by law.
  • Preparing Your Property - We collect information from you regarding your initial intentions to maintain or improve the current condition of your property.
  • Determining the Price - We collect information regarding the terms under which you wish to offer your property for sale, including but not limited to whether the property will be sold in its "As Is" condition, length of escrow, allocation of costs, amount, if any, of credits to buyer, the requirement for a seller rentback, the possibility of a sale contingent on the buyer selling their current home, any personal property included in the sale, and the seller's minimum price requested.
  • Sale Negotiations - If you choose to proceed to this step, you will be engaged in a complete negotiation for the sale of your home. You will need to provide detailed information regarding the terms you would like included in your sale contract. This information will include such items as what inspections or warranties you will provide, and the amount of any deposits you will require. The information you provide at this stage will depend in large part on your decisions after extensive consultation with your Online Agent and will be incorporated into a Purchase Sale Contract as required by law.
  • E-mail - Throughout your use of the zipRealty.com web site, you will communicate extensively with one of our Online Agents. Your Online Agent will use your e-mail address and all of the information you submit in order to provide you with guidance, suggestions and service in your efforts to sell your existing home or in your search for a new home,
  • Contact Us - We collect an e-mail address in order to respond to your questions or suggestions.

What general information is collected and how it is used.

When you visit zipRealty.com, our computer system, like most, will automatically log some basic information about you and your computer, including, but not limited to, your IP address, operating system and browser software. We use this information for routine maintenance and to gather broad demographic information to help us decide how better to implement our web site.

We use cookies. Cookies are used to store a session ID for tracking users as they move through the web site after logging on. The cookies are not linked to personally identifiable information. If a user does not accept cookies, they will not be able to register on our web site or re-log in as a registered user.

With whom personally identifiable information is shared.

We recognize that the information we collect from you may, in itself, or when tied together with other information, identify you as a specific individual. Your account information and personal data is maintained by zipRealty.com in order to fulfill our legal and regulatory requirements and to provide you with the information, products and services you have requested. As a general rule, zipRealty.com will not disclose your personal information to any third party except with your permission or under special circumstances, as discussed below.

zipRealty.com will disclose to interested parties information pertaining to the sale of a house listed in our database. This information will be posted on our web site and in the local Multiple Listing Service (MLS) for the purpose of marketing the home to the greatest number of potential buyers.

By requesting information or services from zipRealty.com, you give zipRealty.com permission to transfer your information, as necessary, to the appropriate parties, such as the title insurer, loan underwriter, appraiser, or inspector you select, so that they can fulfill your request and provide you with the services you require. All Online Agents are employees of zipRealty.com. All personally identifiable information you provide to zipRealty.com will be made available to your Online Agent. Generally, your Online Agent will coordinate the disclosure of your information to third parties. This will be done in much the same way as a traditional Real Estate Agent shares information with third parties. For example, your Online Agent may disclose your name and the address of the property you want to purchase to a building inspector when you request our assistance in arranging an inspection of the property. Your Online Agent will also share certain information with zipRealty.com Field Service Agents who will assist you in viewing properties.

The various lending institutions, title companies and other service providers involved in your real estate transaction may each have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your personally identifiable information. We endeavor to review these other policies in order to ensure that the philosophies and values of our affiliates are in alignment with our own. However, zipRealty.com accepts no responsibility or liability for third party privacy policies or the actions of any third party with respect to your personally identifiable information.

zipRealty.com does conduct research on our users' demographics, interests and behavior based on the information provided by the user and from our server log files. This research is compiled and analyzed on an aggregated basis only. For example, we may review how many users are women, or the average price range of our users. zipRealty.com may share this aggregated data with our business affiliates or, if required by law, any government agency. However, we will not sell or lease personally identifying information to any third party.

If you came to our website through a link on eBay's website ("eBay Referred Users"), zipRealty.com may provide eBay reasonably requested aggregated and personally identifiable data. zipRealty.com has also agreed to provide eBay reasonable access to its database of eBay Referred Users in order to support communication with and surveys of the eBay Referred Users.

zipRealty.com may disclose personally identifiable information in certain circumstances when we have reason to believe that disclosing such information is necessary to contact, identify or bring legal action against someone who may be intentionally or unintentionally causing injury to or interference with our rights or property, our users, or anyone else who could be harmed by such activities. We may disclose personally identifiable information when we believe in good faith that the law requires it and for administrative or other purposes that we deem necessary to maintain and improve our products and services.

Our web site provides links to other web sites. Please be aware that when you click on one of these links, you are going to another web site even though the zipRealty.com logo, banner, or tool bar may still be visible. Information you enter on these other web sites is being collected by that site, not zipRealty.com. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of these linked sites as their privacy policy may differ from ours.

User choices regarding collection and use of information.

On occasion, we may use your e-mail address to send you communications with information about your transaction, valuable tips, or services and product information that we believe may be of interest to you. If you do not want to receive such communications from zipRealty.com, simply contact us at team@zipRealty.com.

You are free to not provide any of the information requested on the zipRealty.com web site However, certain information may be necessary or required by law in order for you to obtain some zipRealty.com services. Therefore, if you choose not to provide the requested information you may not be able to access certain zipRealty.com services.

Your zipRealty.com account can be deleted or de-activated at any time by contacting team@zipRealty.com. However, you will not be able to access any of the products and services offered by zipRealty.com if you choose to delete your account. Please note that we may be required by law to retain certain personally identifiable or demographic information about you even if you choose to delete your account. For example, some loan programs are governed by state and federal law and require the disclosure and retention of certain information regarding loan applicants. However, we will only retain or use such information as is required by law. Please note that zipRealty.com will never share or sell your information.

User access and ability to update information.

You may edit your preferences and the information you have provided at any time by sending e-mail to team@zipRealty.com or mail to zipRealty.com, 1401 Marina Way South, Richmond, CA 94804. You may also visit www.zipRealty.com. You will need your Login ID and Password to access your account information online. If you have lost your password, you may request a new one by sending an e-mail to team@zipRealty.com. You may also contact your Online Agent to edit the information you provide to zipRealty.com.

Security measures used to protect personally identifiable information.

We restrict access to the zipRealty.com server and the personal information we store there by using a log-in process. When you enter the zipHomeManager area of the web site, which is designed for account holders only, you are required to provide your selected Login ID and Password. Other than our System Administrator and your Online Agent, only you have access to your account. Your password should never be shared with anyone. zipRealty.com will never ask you for your password in an unsolicited telephone call or e-mail.

E-mail is an important communication channel for our online customers. E-mail sent to Online Agents, Buyers or Sellers are carefully routed to the intended party. Unfortunately, no e-mail transmission over the Internet is guaranteed secure. While we strive to protect your information, zipRealty.com cannot warrant the security of any information you transmit to us from our online products and services, and you do so at your own risk. Once we receive your transmission, we make our best effort to ensure its security on our system.

Finally, we store all data on our system in a password protected database system, which is protected by our corporate firewall.

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, or privacy issues in general, please feel free to contact us.
