# Privacy Statement | ZIPCodeWorld.com ZIP Code and Postal Code Solutions

Welcome to **ZIPCodeWorld.com** , a division of **Hexasoft Development Sdn. Bhd. ("HDSB")**. We appreciate your every visit. 

**Collecting and Using Personal Information**

When you visit and navigate our sites and when you communicate with us via our sites, we will not collect personal information about you unless you provide us that information voluntarily. Any non-public personal information that you may provide via our sites will be used solely for the purpose stated on the page where it is collected. HDSB will not sell, license, transmit or disclose this information outside of HDSB and its affiliated companies unless  
(a) expressly authorized by you, or  
(b) necessary to enable contractors or agents to perform certain functions as requested by you, or  
(c) required or permitted by law. 

**Your E-Mail**

We welcome your comments or questions about our websites and have provided feedback box for that purpose. We will share your comments and questions with our customer service representatives and those employees most capable of addressing your questions and concerns. Again, HDSB will not sell, license, transmit or disclose your email address to any 3rd parties.

ZIPCodeWorld.com website may provide links to other websites, not owned or controlled by HDSB, that we think might be useful or of interest to you. We cannot, however, be responsible for the privacy practices used by other website owners or the content or accuracy of those other websites. Links to various non-HDSB websites do not constitute or imply endorsement by HDSB of these websites, any products or services described on these sites, or of any other material contained in them.
