##### **ZipNadaZilch Privacy Policy**

Registering for and using this site acknowledges that you have read, understood, and accepted these terms as well as understood and accepted the [Terms & Conditions](http://www.zipnadazilch.com/terms-and-conditions/).

##### **Information Collection and Use**

\- ZipNadaZilch collects personally identifiable information, such as name, mailing address, email address, telephone number, and birthday when you register.  
\- ZipNadaZilch also receives and records information from your browser including your IP Address, Cookie Information and the page(s) you request.  
\- ZipNadaZilch will also request an IRS Form W-9/W-8 from users who receive $600 or more in prizes in any one year, which includes their Social Security Number, before receiving any more prizes.  
\- ZipNadaZilch uses the collected information for the following purposes: contacting users, shipping orders/product, as well as detecting fraud. The information on the IRS Form W-9/W-8 is used so that we can send out a IRS Form 1099 as required by law.  
\- ZipNadaZilch may share personally identifiable information with third parties. The reasons for doing so may include verifying account information and identifying fraud.  
\- ZipNadaZilch will never sell or give away any personally identifiable information for marketing purposes.

##### **Credit Card Details**

\- ZipNadaZilch will never ask for any Credit Card details and request that you do not enter it on any of the forms when communicating with us. When completing an advertiser offer, you may be prompted to enter such information, but we do not have access to any of that information. All information provided to register for an advertiser(s) offer is communicated directly between the consumer and the advertiser.

##### **3rd Party Sites**

\- ZipNadaZilch is not responsible for the content of external internet sites. You are advised to read the privacy policy of external sites before disclosing any personal information.  
\- While we do our best to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee the security of any information that you transmit outside of our site and you are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of any passwords or other account information.

##### **Changes to this Privacy Policy**

\- ZipNadaZilch may amend this policy from time to time. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our Web site periodically.
