**The Information We Collect**  
We collect personal information from you on zagat.com  when  you provide it and in some cases, from third party sources.

**User Registration**. Registration on zagat.com is strictly voluntary. To register on zagat.com, you must provide your name, address, e-mail address and year of birth. This information is required in the registration process at zagat.com, but we recognize that this is your personal information and, except as we explain below, we do not give it out to third parties without your approval **.** Becoming a registered user on zagat.com allows you to vote on restaurants in your favorite city and purchase items in our Shop. Additional demographic information, such as your occupation, level of education, household income and special interests, are also collected in an "optional information" area. In this section, you are not required to provide additional information about yourself that you may find personal. We do this to learn more about you in tailoring our Web site for your needs and preferences. We also will collect your phone number for shipping and billing information if you purchase an item at the zagat.com store. Also, at some point we may ask you for optional information with regard to your personal preferences on dining, travel and leisure activities to personalize your experiences on zagat.com, but this information will be optionally provided by you as well. At any time you may later review or update the personal information we have collected online from you by logging on with your user name and password and then going to the "update profile" icon. 

**Subscriptions to Zagat.com**. To subscribe to zagat.com, you must provide the personal information requested in the zagat.com subscription process, including, without limitation, your name, address, e-mail address, other information required in order to become a registered user of zagat.com, and your credit card information for subscription fee billing. This information is required in the subscription process, but we recognize that this is your personal information and, except as we explain below, we do not give it out to third parties without your approval. Becoming a subscriber of zagat.com provides you with the benefits described in the subscriber section of zagat.com, including access to certain content that is not available to persons who are not subscribers and a discount for purchases in our Shop. The Subscriber Agreement applies to your subscription to zagat.com. In addition, as a subscriber to zagat.com, you will also automatically become a registered user of zagat.com, thereby subjecting you to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy applicable to registered users, and you will be entitled to all of the benefits and privileges available to registered users of zagat.com. 

**Subscriptions to the ZAGAT TO GO SM Service**. To subscribe to the ZAGAT TO GOSM service, you must provide the personal information requested in our subscription process, including, without limitation, your name, address, e-mail address, other information required in order to become a registered user of the service, and your credit card information for subscription fee billing. This information is required in the subscription process, but we recognize that this is your personal information and, except as we explain below, we do not give it out to third parties without your approval. You must become a subscriber of the ZAGAT TO GOSM service in order to take advantage of the service, unless you are subject to a free trial. In order to be eligible for a free trial you may be required to provide your name and email address. 

**Credit Card Policy**. To simplify ordering and product fulfillment in our Shop, and also to make online reservations on our site and/or for your subscription to zagat.com or the ZAGAT TO GOSM service, if applicable, we request your credit card number, its expiration date and your billing and shipping address. We transmit this information safely over the Internet in encrypted form and store it on a secure server. This information is used only to bill you for and ship to you the zagat.com products that you order or to bill you for your subscription to zagat.com or other services available on zagat.com. 

**Cookies**. zagat.com employs cookies (small pieces of text stored on your computer) to recognize you and your access privileges. For example, we use cookies to allow you to log on to zagat.com automatically without entering your ID and password each visit. Cookies also identify you and your preferences on our site. You may refuse to accept our cookies, but we will not be able to recognize you as a registered user or subscriber, which will limit your access to the site. 

**E-mail a Review**. If you choose to e-mail one of our reviews to a friend, we collect the friend's name and e-mail address. This information is used only to deliver the review to your friend and is not stored on our servers. If "a friend" decides that he/she will register with zagat.com, he or she will be given the same rights of access as registered users of zagat.com. The prospect information that you submit for "a friend" will be used only for the use for which it is submitted. Additionally, for "a friend" to register, he or she will have to follow zagat.com's registration process (see above) and will be given the same option in regard to receiving marketing materials that zagat.com offers to all registered users. 

**IP Addresses**. zagat.com logs IP addresses, or the location of your computer on the Internet, for systems administration and troubleshooting purposes. Your IP address is not linked to any personally identifiable information. 

**Non-Personally Identifiable Information**. This site uses Open AdStream NETWORK to serve and/or target ads, promotions and other marketing messages. This technology collects non-personally identifiable information about your visit to this and other sites, typically through the use of cookies. This information is used in aggregate format only to optimize the zagat.com web site. 

**How We Use Your Information**  
We use your e-mail address and home address to send you updates and newsletters periodically about new features, special offers and targeted promotional items available on zagat.com. You may choose to not receive certain of these communications by so indicating during the registration process or in response to one of our e-mails or postal mailings. Each communication will clearly explain how to opt out of future communications. Any demographic information that you voluntarily share with us is used to tailor zagat.com to your interests. We also may share this demographic information with other companies with which we do business. 

**With Whom We Share Information**  
We share your personally identifiable information with intermediaries. These intermediaries use your information in connection with processing payments for online purchases and with delivery of e-mail and postal mailings to our registered users . We do not permit these intermediaries to use, store or retain this information for secondary purposes, unless they indicate otherwise in communications to you. From time to time we may allow some of our select partners to send you special promotional offers that we believe may be of special interest to you. You may choose not to receive these communications from our partners by so indicating during the registration process or in response to any of these communications, each of which will clearly explain how to opt out of future communications. You may also opt out of having your personal information shared with these third parties by sending a request to have your zagat.com user account deactivated to feedback@zagat.com.  If you do so, however, you will  not be able to take advantage of many of our features, including the right to vote online, purchase items from our store and even continue as a subscriber to zagat **.** com. This site makes use of a technology designed to enhance your browsing experience and provide analytics. The technology, provided as a hosted service application by Fireclick, provides aggregate reporting information to the site owner only. No personal or personally identifiable information is gathered for this process. If for any reason you do not wish to have this technology enabled while browsing this site, please click here . A cookie will be set on your browser that instructs the service not to start. If you have disabled the service and wish to reactivate it, please click here . Additional information on Fireclick's technology may be found at [www.fireclick.com](http://www.fireclick.com/). 

**Legal Disclaimer**  
We may disclose personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to conform with the law or legal process or to protect or defend our rights or property.

**Security**  
zagat.com has security measures in place to help protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All information is stored in a secure operating environment. When sending sensitive information across the Internet - including your login ID, password and purchasing information such as credit cards - we use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption provided by VeriSign. Internally, all customer information is protected with multiple security checks. Employee access to the system is limited to the functionality necessary for each person's job. Relevant employees are informed of our security policies and their access to applications and data on our network. 

If you still do not feel comfortable about leaving your credit card information on our secured Web site, please contact our customer service department at (800) 333-3421 and we'll be happy to help you with your order or subscription. 

**Special Policy With Regard to Information From Children Under Thirteen**  
During the zagat.com registration process, users are asked to enter their "year of birth." This information is mandatory for registration on zagat.com because we do not allow children under the age of thirteen to go through the registration process at zagat.com. 

**Links**  
We may provide links to third party Web sites as a service to our users. Please be aware that we cannot control and are not responsible for the information collection practices of such Web sites, which may differ from those of zagat.com. We encourage you to review and understand the privacy policies on these Web sites before providing any information to them. 

**How You Can Access or Correct Your Information**  
If you are a registered zagat.com user you can make changes to your personal information at any time by clicking on "My Profile" on zagat.com or by e-mailing us at feedback@zagat.com. At any time you can also review your personal information by clicking on "My Profile" as well. 

**Questions**  
Protecting your privacy is one of our highest priorities. If you have any questions about our privacy policy, you may e-mail us at feedback@zagat.com, or write to us at Zagat Survey, LLC, 4 Columbus Circle, New York, NY, 10019, Attn: Privacy.

**Consent and Opting-Out**  
Upon request, zagat.com will: 

  1. Remove you and your personal information from our database, thereby canceling your registration 
  2. Correct personal information that you state is erroneous 
  3. Permit you to "opt out" of further e-mail contact (excepting notification from zagat.com of major changes to the service) while still allowing access to the site 



To request a correction or cancellation, send e-mail to feedback@zagat.com . 

**Notification of Changes**  
If we decide to materially change our privacy policy, we will post changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we disclose it. Regardless of later updates, we will never use the information you submit under this current policy in a new way without first providing you an opportunity to opt out or otherwise prevent that use (we will post a notification of such changes 30 days prior to their implementation). You may determine when this privacy policy was updated last by noting the "Last Updated" date directly beneath the title of this page. 

Thank you for using zagat.com.
