**What information we collect and why we collect it:**

We are a community. The information you provide us during your registration process will help us better know you and so we can better plan and create services that will best benefit you.

**How we use the personal and contact information we collect:**

We use the information we collect from our members to provide quality programs, communicate online and offline with the whole community and to update you on events and opportunities that might be relevant to you. Accessing and updating your personal information: Whenever you are logged in the community, you have full access to your personal information. You can, at any time, update any or all of the details you provide in your profile.

**Information we share:**

We, absolutely, will not share to any party (companies, organizations and individuals) outside of Zarfund.com
