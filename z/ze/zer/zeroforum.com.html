RelyNet Web Site Privacy Statement

NOTE: Please do not submit Personal Information via this site if you are 18 years of age or under without permission from your parent or legal guardian.

Your Personal Information

RelyNet may ask you to give us information that personally identifies
you ("Personal Information"). For example, we may request Personal
Information when you register for our forums, email us, or ask us to forward
information to you.

We use your Personal Information primarily to:
Provide you with information about RelyNet products, services, news and events
Allow you to purchase products, and Gather broad demographic information about user trends.

Online Payment

When you submit payment on-line, your credit card number is submitted
to the credit institution for normal, routine processing. RelyNet does
not collect your personal credit history information and does not share
credit or credit card information with other third parties unless compelled
by law or a court order to do so.

Linked Web Sites

The RelyNet web site may include links to the web sites of others,
however, RelyNet is not responsible for the privacy practices or the
content of these linked sites.

Use of Cookies
Some of our web pages use "cookies". A cookie is a small file
that a web site may send to your browser. This file is then stored on
your hard drive. A cookie does not contain any Personal Information, but
it does enable RelyNet to build a better web site by recording how and
when guests use our site and to better serve you when you return to our
site. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but if you do, you might
not be able to take full advantage of our site.
