Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to me. If you choose to provide your personal information to me (through contact form, email or other channels), you personal information will not be published without your permission.

When you visit Zemalf.com, we send one or more cookies - a small file containing a string of characters - to your computer or other device that uniquely identifies your browser. Cookies are used to improved the quality of this blog, including your preferences and credentials you have chosen to give, for example on comments.

When you come and visit my site, the web server automatically records information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website. These server logs may include information such as your web request, Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.

Your email is only visible to the administrators of this blog, the newsletters and the mailing list and you can unsubscribe at any point and stop receiving any emails from me, the services I use or the WordPress plugin(s) might send you.

I fight spam as hard as I can, you can count on that.

All-in-all, any information that is stored in your computer or the servers this site runs on, will only be used to improve this blog and your experience here.
