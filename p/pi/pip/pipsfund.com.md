# PipsFund

### Privacy Policy

PipsFund treats its members' personal information with the highest security measures to make sure your personal data is protected. PipsFund collects information about your I.P address and geographic location when you login to your PipsFund account and this information is kept in our databases for at least 6 months.

Pips Fund will often send you communication about our performance, status and future plans. These e-mails are sent to our entire membership and you agree to receive them. Pips Fund won't, under any circumstances, send its members any type of SPAM advertisement. 

Your personal information shall be used for statistical work and legal affairs if necessary but they won't ever be sold, traded or given away.
