# Welcome to , a free image upload solution. Simply browse, select, and upload!

policy is to respect and protect the privacy of our users. will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent.

will not sell or give your email address to anyone and you will never be placed on any lists. However, may disclose your information when believes in good faith that the law requires it.

may use your email address to inform you about major service changes, important updates, or special promotions.

website record your IP address but uses it only for internal purposes. It will never be given to any other person/company/website for any reason.

website uses cookies only to maintain your information as you move throughout this site. A cookie is a small piece of information used to identify an individual when the person is accessing a site. At no time is any personal information stored in a cookie, nor is any tracking information made available to other sites for any reason.

reserve the right to change this Policy anytime without prior notification. In this case the new Policy will be published at http://www.picbaran.com/info.php?act=privacy_policy
