FAQS

  • I am worried about identity thefts. Do you take every possible measure to ensure that it doesn't happen?

    • Yes. We use a 256 bit encrypted connection to make sure that the information that you give us remains secure. We are committed to your privacy and we do not disclose information about you to anybody. Please see our Privacy Policy and Security Policy.

  • Do I need to login in order to send gifts from your site?

    • You don't need to login. You can select the checkout without login option after clicking on Buy Now. However, we would suggest you to create an account with us as we, at present offer 5% discount on all orders for the first 30 days and also there are occasional special offers for our Registered Users.

  • Do you sell products at a discount?

    • Occasionally we sell some of our products at a discount. Such offers are clearly shown on the top right of our webpages.

  • Can I look for specific flowers like roses or lilies?

    • You can shop by product for flowers such as roses, daisies, carnations, and lilies. They are listed at the top left of every page.

  • How do I select flowers for various kinds of recipients?

    • Use the scroll-down boxes on the top left for selecting the country and the occasions on which you can give gifts for different types of recipients.

  • How long does it take for the flowers to be delivered after I place the order?

    • The flowers will be delivered on the same day if the order is placed before the cut-off time for that destination country. The cut off time for any country is usually 1PM Local Time.
