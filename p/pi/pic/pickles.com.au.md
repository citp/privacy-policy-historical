# Privacy Policy - Pickles Auctions Australia

## Our Commitment to your Privacy

Pickles Auctions Pty Limited will not sell, trade or rent any personally identifiable information obtained from you to a third party unless you consent.

As a user we pledge to provide you with some important rights:

  1. Decide what personally identifiable information we collect. 
  2. Our total commitment to your privacy. 
  3. Not to receive e-mail from Pickles Auctions you have not consented to receive. 



We hope that, like many of our users, you will want to provide information about yourself to Pickles Auctions because it will help us to make our sites and services more valuable to you.

The choice of how much personally identifiable information about yourself you disclose to Pickles Auctions is left completely up to you, our valued user, you have our commitment to your privacy, as stated above.

## Collecting information about you

  1. Pickles Auctions will not collect or monitor any personal information about you without your consent unless we believe it is necessary: 
    * Because it is required by law 
    * To provide you with a service you have requested 
    * To implement our terms and conditions 
    * To protect our rights of property, any Pickles Auctions user, or any member of the public
  2. All the information we gather is stored by Pickles Auctions on a secure site protected by state of the art 'firewalls' and never shared with anyone. 
  3. We track traffic patterns throughout sites. 
  4. We may use a feature of your browser called a "cookie" to assign a "User ID." Cookies, by themselves, cannot be used to find out your identity or the identities' of any of our users, it only identifies your computer to our servers when you visit our site. 
  5. It is our policy only to send e-mail to customers who give us permission to do so. In every such e-mail, we will also have instructions on how you may unsubscribe from the e-mail list so that you will not receive any future e-mails if you do not wish to. 



## In Summary

  * Pickles Auctions is committed to protecting your privacy. 
  * We use the information we collect on our site to make our services the best they can be and to enhance your overall experience. 
  * We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to others. 



## Your Consent

By using our sites, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Pickles Auctions. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect and how we use it.

If you require any further information on the National Privacy Policies please [contact](https://web.archive.org/pickles/contact/) Pickles Auctions.
