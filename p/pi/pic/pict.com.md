## The information we collect

**Cookies** — Pict! uses cookies to track users and to store users preferences. A cookie is a tiny element of data that a website can send to your browser, which may then be stored on your hard drive so we can recognize you when you return. With most web browsers you can block all cookies or delete cookies from your hard drive. 

**E-mail addresses** — Pict! collects the e-mail addresses of its users for the purpose of logging and measuring usage. Pict! does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you.

**Logging IP addresses** — your IP address is used to help identify you and to help diagnose problems with our server.
