Privacy Statement

Pick 'n Save understands your concern for privacy and has adopted the following Privacy Statement.

Online Privacy
Pick 'n Save tracks the total number of visitors to our website allowing for proper administration and improvements. Use of cookies are used to track non-personal information including browser type, operating system and domain names. We don't extract personally identifiable information.

We do not sell, rent or release information gathered on our website to any third parties.

Email
When an email address is given by a visitor indicating inclusion in the subscription list, we will send notices to the visitor in the form of an email. Users may opt-out of this subscription at any time.

Order Forms
Our site uses an order form to pre-order store items. This personal information is used to tie the order to the individual. Although stored on the server, it is used only for order history.

Contests
Our contests may ask for personal information to include on the entry blank. This personal information is not saved and is removed upon printing out the entry blank.

Shopping List
Our Shopping List program asks for an email and password to allow users to save their lists for future use. We keep a count of total lists generated on the site.

Advantage Plus Savers Card
Pick 'n Save is commited to insuring the privacy of each and every individual's purchasing history. The collection of purchase information through our Advantage Plus Saver's Card is for the sole purpose of better serving our customers.

Emails, special offers and discounts will only be sent upon consent given on the Advantage Card Application, or through the email signup on the Pick 'n Save website and will stop upon notification to Pick 'n Save. Consumers may opt-out of this service at any time.

Although we are committed to your privacy, we reserve the right to change this policy at anytime. In this event, we will post the new policy on our website.
