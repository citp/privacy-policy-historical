The Information We Collect From You

Information we collect from you depends on how you use the Website, and the information you may choose to provide to us.

Personal Information: We may require Customers who visit or use the Website and/or purchase products on the Website to supply us with personally identifiable information (“Personal Information”). Personal Information is uniquely identifiable information about you that we collect online, including, but not limited to, your full name, a user name/password, email address, phone number, physical address, financial information, and your credit card information and other billing information. Information in a form that (a) is aggregated with other information, (b) anonymized, or (c) is otherwise is detached, combined, organized, segmented, modified or processed, in each case so as not to be reasonably capable of being associated with you, will not be considered Personal Information, and will not be restricted by this Privacy Policy as to use, sharing or otherwise. Some features of our Services, and certain requested information or products may not be accessible if you choose not to provide us with Personal Information.

If you create an account using your login credentials from a third party service, such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc..., we will collect the information that your privacy settings on that third party service allows us to access. We will use this information to create an account for you. This includes collecting the contact information of your friends, if you choose to connect your contacts and address book information with your account, so that you can connect with your friends when you use our Website. Automatic Collection of Information: Similar to many web properties on the Internet, we automatically track certain information about you as you visit and use our Website to help us create a better shopping experience for you. This tracked or automatically gathered information includes, among other things, your computer’s or device's IP address, browser type, the URLs on the Website you have visited, sections of or content on pages on the Website on which you click, the frequency you visit each page on the Website, what downloads and/or search requests you have made, and time spent on specific pages of the Website.

Cookies

When you visit our Website, we may drop a small file on your browser called a "cookie". This allows us to make your shopping experience unique to you and ultimately give you a better shopping experience. Depending on the browser you are using, you can turn off "cookies". Please be aware that if you turn off cookies, many functions of our Website will not be available to you, including creating a shopping cart, and making purchases. Moreover, some of our third-party service providers working on our behalf may also utilize their own cookies.

How We Use Your Information

The personal information you submit to the Website is used to process your order, confirm your order by email, and/or to contact you as necessary regarding your online purchases. We keep your personal information to improve your shopping experience when you return to shop with us. For example, we will save your personal information to maintain your customer account and/or membership to our website so you can log-in to the Website at a later time. Additionally, we keep and may use your personal information to keep track of your purchasing preferences and to help us better understand the types of products and services you are interested in. We will use your e-mail and mailing address to send information about upcoming sales, arrival of new products, contests, and promotions. If you decide to participate in one of our contests or promotions, we may use your personal information to send you e-mails about our products, contests and promotions. If you do not wish to receive our weekly email newsletter, you may unsubscribe by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of the email.

How Your Personal Information Is Safe

Whenever Personal Information is stored on our computers, time in which depends on factors, that information is protected from unauthorized access or use by way of passwords or other industry-acknowledged means. Additionally, our servers are firewall protected. We take extra caution in keeping your personal information safe and secure. Any transmission of sensitive material, such as credit card information, will only take place under secure connections. We appreciate your help in safeguarding the integrity of your own and others' privacy. We hope you will use common rationale whenever you disclose Personal Information over the Internet. Just as importantly, we encourage you to let us know immediately if you suspect that Personal Information you share with us is being used in any way contrary to this Privacy Policy. Lastly, 100% secure data transmission or storage does not exist, so we cannot guarantee complete security of your Personal Information. Please contact us if you have knowledge of any illegal activity.
