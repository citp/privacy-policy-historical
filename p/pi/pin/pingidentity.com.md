# Privacy Policy - Ping Identity Corporation

##  Privacy Policy 

Ping Identity Corporation values privacy, and we want you to have the information you need to make your own decisions about your privacy. When you visit or supply information to any web site, you should understand what that site's privacy policies are. The Ping Identity Corporation site privacy policies are explained here. 

* * *

###  Collection and Notice 

As a visitor to this site, you can undertake many activities without providing any personal information. In connection with activities that require sign-in or subscription, you may be asked to provide certain information about yourself such as name, and e-mail address, etc. It is completely optional for you to provide this information, but you may not be able to complete the activity without providing some information. 

When you request information, or other services, we will use your email address and any other information you give us to provide you with the information or service you have requested, as well as contact you, until you ask us to cease such activity, or until the information or service is no longer available. 

This site will automatically capture some information about your session, other technical and routing information, information about your computer, and the paths taken as you move through the site. We may use this information to optimize your use of this web site and the design of the site. 

While Ping Identity Corporation will never sell or rent information about you or your use of this site to other companies, we may at times disclose parts of your information to companies with which we have a strategic partnership, or to our investors. Your information may be transferred to another company in connection with a merger or the sale of a substantial amount or all of the assets of Ping Identity Corporation. 

Ping Identity Corporation may use or disclose any information available to it, if required to do so by law, or if necessary to conform with the requirements of the law or to comply with legal process served upon us, to protect or defend our legal rights, or to investigate, prevent, or take legal action regarding suspected fraud, threats to physical safety, or other illegal activity. 

* * *

###  Linking to Other Sites 

This site contains links to other sites. Unless specifically stated otherwise, the collection and use of your personal information at other sites, linked to pingidentity.com, are not governed by this privacy policy. 

* * *

###  Choice 

Ping Identity Corporation may periodically send email or other communications related to this site, if you have chosen to receive them. You can choose to stop receiving such communications at any time. If you do not explicitly choose to receive further information from us, we will not include you on distributions of information related to this site. 

* * *

###  Access 

To modify or delete your personal information, follow the instructions included in an email you receive from us, or send a reply stating your request, and we will make the requested changes. 

* * *

###  Customer Service and Recourse 

If you have concerns about this policy, its implementation, or other related inquiries or concerns, please contact [info@pingidentity.com](mailto:%69%6e%66%6f@%70%69%6e%67%69%64%65%6e%74%69%74%79.%63%6f%6d).
