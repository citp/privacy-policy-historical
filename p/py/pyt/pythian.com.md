# Privacy | Pythian®

Pythian is committed to maintaining the privacy of your personal information. This privacy policy explains Pythian’s practices with respect to the processing of your personal information, which includes the collection, use, processing, transfer, storage, or disclosure of your personal information.

 **Pythian and Your Privacy**  
We collect information to provide a better experience when you visit our website or in connection with your request for more information, or in connection with a job application. Please be assured that Pythian does not sell, rent, or otherwise distribute to third parties personal information about its website visitors.

We use your personal information for three primary purposes:

  * To help us create website content most relevant to you.
  * To alert you to service offerings, event participation, updated information, and other new services from Pythian.
  * To send you service offerings, educational information, and special offers.



By filling out a form on Pythian’s website and providing your contact information, you consent to receiving communication from Pythian. We retain all personal information in a secure manner and only for the period of time necessary for the identified purpose. We destroy any copies of personal information upon the withdrawal of consent by the discloser.

If you choose not to provide any personal Information, you can still use the majority of the Pythian site. Additionally, you can contact our Marketing Department at [info@pythian.com](mailto:info@pythian.com) and request that we not send you Pythian marketing content.

 **Use of Cookies**  
Certain portions of the Pythian website may use certain technologies, including cookies, to enhance your user experience. Your browser is probably set to accept cookies. However, if you would prefer not to receive cookies, you can alter the configuration of your browser to refuse cookies. If you choose to have your browser refuse cookies, it is possible that some areas of the Pythian website will not function properly when you view them.

 **Links to Other Websites**  
Pythian’s website may contain links to one or more third-party websites. Pythian has no control over these third-party websites and the links do not constitute any endorsement or representation by Pythian as to the content or products or service set forth in such websites. Pythian is not responsible for the privacy or customer information use by third-party websites that link to Pythian’s website.

 **Internet Protocol (IP) Addresses**  
We collect an IP address from all visitors to the Pythian website. Your IP address is used to help diagnose problems with our server, to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement, and gather broad demographic information about aggregate site use.

 **Questions**  
If you have any questions about this privacy statement or the practices of this website, please email us at [privacy@pythian.com](mailto:privacy@pythian.com)
