Our online surveys ask visitors for contact information (like email address).

We never sell, rent, or give out customers personal or financial information to third parties without customers prior consent.

Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. All Online transactions are protected by secure server software.

CHOICE/OPT-OUT
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving promotional/marketing information from us.

ADVERTISERS
