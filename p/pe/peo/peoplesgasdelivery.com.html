What personally identifiable information is collected from me?
On some of the Peoples Gas Web pages you can make requests and/or receive materials. What are cookies and how are they used by Peoples Gas?
Peoples Gas may place Internet cookies on your hard drive. Internet cookies are small files that may be placed on your hard disk for record-keeping purposes. Cookies may be used to (a) remind us of who you are in order to deliver you better service; (b) estimate our audience size by determining repeat usage of the Web Site to help target advertisements based on user interests and behavior; and (c) measure certain traffic patterns for use as a research tool to understand how our users' habits are similar or different from one another. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it. By not accepting cookies, some Web pages may not display properly or you may not be permitted to access certain information.
 
Peoples Gas may also collect IP addresses (the Internet address of a computer) to track a user's session while the user remains anonymous. Peoples Gas analyzes this data for certain trends in statistics, such as which parts of our site users are visiting and how long they spend there. In general, Peoples Gas does not link your IP addresses to anything personally identifiable to you.

How is my information used?
Peoples Gas will not sell, rent or lease your personally identifiable information to others without your consent. However, by accessing this Web Site, you acknowledge that your information may be shared among our affiliated companies and with our agents, contractors or business partners. When you submit personal information to Peoples Gas, you are agreeing to permit Peoples Gas to access, store and use the information wherever in the world Peoples Gas does business. In particular, Peoples Gas, its agents, contractors and business partners may use your personal information for various business purposes, such as statistical analysis, doing market research, improving services, supporting your customer or other relationship with Peoples Gas and notifying you about services and changes that may affect you, among other business purposes, in an effort to better serve you.
 
Accordingly, Peoples Gas may send you direct mailings or contact you by email or telephone about your use of the Web Site, our various products and services or other products and services we feel may be of interest to you.
 
From time to time, Peoples Gas may conduct research on our users' demographics, interests and behavior based on the information provided to us during your use of the Peoples Gas Web Site. This research is compiled and analyzed on an aggregated basis. Aggregated data does not include personally identifiable information. Peoples Gas may share this aggregated data with others.
