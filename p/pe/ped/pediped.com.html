privacy policy

Thank you for visiting www.pediped.com.  Your privacy is very important to us.

When you provide us with information about yourself or others , it is our duty to act responsibly.  Please read the following to better understand how we collect information from you and how it is used.

Does pediped™  Disclose Information It Collects To Third Parties?
No.  We do not sell , trade or otherwise transfer any information to third parties about our customers or visitors to our site.

What If I No Longer Wish To Receive Email?
If a visitor wishes at any time to opt-out of future pediped™  mailings , they should send an email to customerservice@pediped.com and include their name , email address and write UNSUBCRIBE in the title of their email.  We will immediately remove them from our mailing list.

How Does  pediped™  Use The Information?
We may use the information you provide to us to respond to your inquiries about our products , store locations and offerings.  We may also offer you other products , programs or services we think you may find valuable.

Does pediped™  Use "Cookies"?
Yes.  Cookies are bits of electronic information that a website can transfer to a visitor's hard drive. Cookies , by themselves , do not tell us your email address or other personally identifiable information. We use cookies to help make our site better.  pediped™  does not use information transferred through cookies for any promotional or marketing purposes , nor is the information shared with any third parties.  Most web browsers automatically accept cookies , but you can change your browser to prevent them if you would rather not store the information for future use.

Security Statement
Maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information is important to pediped™.  All payment information including credit card related data is treated as confidential information.  We utilize Secure Server Software (SSL) to encrypt all payment information , including names and addresses.

Should you have any questions regarding our privacy policy , email us at customerservice@pediped.com
