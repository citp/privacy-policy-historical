# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 26, 2018

## Our Commitment To Privacy

Pedigree Online, Inc. ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this Privacy Policy. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online data collection, processing and usage practices, and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. This Privacy Policy applies to our pedigreeonline.com, allbreedpedigree.com, and pedigreequery.com websites ("Websites"). To make this notice easy to find, we make it available as a link in the footer of our Websites. We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time, so refer back often to read the most current version.

Please read this policy carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your information and how we will treat it, as you agree to this Privacy Policy by using any of the Websites. _If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use the Websites_. 

## The Information We Collect and How We Collect It

We may collect the following types of data about you when you use the Websites: 1. Personal Information; 2. Communication Information; 3. User Contribution Information; 4. Navigational Information; and 5. Payment Information.

_Personal Information_ is information that you voluntarily submit to us and identifies you personally when you fill out forms on our Websites, and may also be included in the other types of data described below. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, address, phone number, email address, business name, and other information about you or your business when creating an account on the Websites, setting your Display Name for the Websites, at the time of subscribing to any of our services, or when using our online support system. We may also ask you for information if we receive a complaint about you. Personal Information can also include information about any transactions, both free and paid, that you enter into on the Websites, and information about you that is available on the Internet, such as from Facebook, Twitter and Google, or publicly available information that we may acquire.

_Communication Information_ is collected if you contact us using the online support system, or by direct email. We may keep a record of that correspondence. The information we collect will include the details of the messages you send and receive using our internal support service including, without limitation, the date/time you sent or received a message and the contents of that message.

_User Contribution Information_ is collected when you add, edit, delete, or upload information to the Websites. Information we collect may include, but is not limited to, IP address, browser information, computer hardware and operating system information, text or images you might add, edit or delete, and account identifier information which can be used to link to your Personal Information.

You are free to explore the Websites without entering any personal information about yourself. When you visit the Websites in this manner, we collect _Navigational Information_.

_Navigational Information_ includes details of your visits to the Websites including, but not limited to, browser information, computer hardware and operating system information, geographical location data, IP address, length of visit, pages viewed, referral source, and other communication data. In general, we do not correlate tracking information to individuals, except that when you make a public post or change on the Websites, your IP address will be unique. If you are logged into one of the Websites when you make a public post or change on the Websites, your chosen Display Name will be listed with the post; if you are not logged in, your IP address will be listed with the post. We may also collect account identifier information which can be used to link to your Personal Information.

The _Payment Information_ we collect and process when you subscribe to any of our paid services, includes credit card numbers and billing information. Credit cards are processed using third-party PCI-compliant service providers.

## Children Under the Age of 13

The Websites are not intended for or targeted at children under 13, and we do not knowingly or intentionally collect information about children under 13. If you believe that we have collected information about a child under 13, please contact us at [info@pedigreeonline.com](mailto:info@pedigreeonline.com), so that we may delete the information.

## The Way We Use Your Information

In addition to uses explained elsewhere in this Privacy Policy, we use the _Personal Information_ you provide to us to fulfill a subscription request that requires a payment, identify you for login purposes, identify you for support purposes, and to assist us in administering your user account. We also may send you information about our services or communicate with you about your account with us. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete a subscription payment or for complying with court orders or other law enforcement requests.

_Communication Information_ is used for support services for the Websites, and to contact you about your account or our services from time to time. We may (but are not obligated to) view the contents of any email or message sent to us by you or others, whether or not you have viewed its contents. Messages or emails sent to us are not private and are not confidential. Email addresses are not shared with outside parties.

_User Contribution Information_ is used for support services, tracking and logging user contributions to the Websites, quality control purposes, and to allow for other administrative purposes that might arise from time to time.

_Navigational Information_ is used to operate the Websites, improve navigation, track user tendencies and contributions, and allow for other administrative purposes that might arise from time to time.

If you give us 

_Payment Information_

such as credit card information, we use it solely to collect payment from you. We use a third-party service provider to manage credit card processing. This service provider is not permitted to store, retain, or use information you provide except for the sole purpose of credit card processing on our behalf. For subscription management purposes, we do store the last 4 digits of your credit card number, as well as the expiration date.

## Cookies

The Websites use cookies. By using the Websites, you consent to our use of cookies. Cookies are small text files that are sent to web browsers by web servers, saved on a user's computer, and which may then be sent back to the web server when the browser requests a web page. They are widely used in order to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the web server to identify and track users as they navigate to different web pages on a web site, as well as identify users returning to a web site. Cookies may either be persistent or session cookies. Persistent cookies are stored by the browser and will remain until they expire or are deleted by a user. A session cookie will expire at the end of a user session, when the browser is closed. We use both persistent and session cookies. None of our cookies store any credit/debit card information nor password details.

Cookies do not contain information that personally identifies you. However, Personal Information that you provide and we store about you may be linked, by us, to information stored in and obtained from cookies. Cookies we use on the Websites are those which are necessary for access and navigation, track usage, remember your preferences, and provide you with targeted content or advertising. We may use the information we obtain from your use of the Websites and cookies to: 1. Recognize your computer when you visit our Websites; 2. Track your use as you navigate through the Websites; 3. Enhance the Websites's usability; 4. Administration of the Websites; and 5. Personalize the Websites for you, including targeting advertisements.

At this time sites the Websites do not recognize automated browser signals regarding tracking mechanisms, which may include ‘do not track' instructions. However, you can change your privacy preferences regarding the use of cookies and similar technologies through your browser. You may set your browser to accept all cookies, block certain cookies, require your consent before a cookie is placed in your browser, or block all cookies. Blocking all cookies will affect your online experience and may prevent you from enjoying the full features offered on our Websites. Please consult the ‘Help' section of your browser for more information

## Log Files

Our web servers record certain requests and transactions in log files. This log data is used for operation of the Websites, to maintain the quality of the Websites, maintain subscriptions, troubleshooting, statistics, analytics, and to monitor system security and can be analyzed to that end. There is also information about your computer hardware, operating system, and software that is automatically collected by us in log files. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, internet service provider (ISP), the files viewed on our site (e.g., HTML pages, graphics, etc.), operating system, clickstream data, access times, and referring website addresses.

We also log User Contribution Information. This log data can include your IP address, Display Name, browser type, computer hardware and operating system information, details of the user contributions, and your account identifier. Your account identifier is linked to your Personal Information.

## Our Commitment to Data Security

We secure the Personal Information you provide on computer servers within an infrastructure that puts strong safeguards in place to help protect customer privacy. All data is stored in data centers with a highly secure, controlled environment. When sensitive Personal Information (such as a credit card number and/or geo-location data) is collected on our Websites and/or transmitted to other websites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol. In addition, some areas of the Websites are inaccessible unless you login using a username or email address, and your password. Also, we do not store your complete credit card number or card code on our servers. We do store the last 4 digits of your card number and the card expiration date for subscription renewal and transaction history purposes.

## How You Can Access, Correct or Remove Your Information

You can access your Personal Information that we store by logging into your account through our Websites. Please be certain that you are logging in through our Websites and not through a "phishing" website masquerading as our Websites. Doing so will allow the phishing website to know your login credentials and then gain access to your account on our Websites. If you have forgotten your password, there is a password reset link on the login page. Once you are logged in, you will be able to correct any information that we store on your account dashboard page. 

If you would like to have your account and Personal Information deleted, please contact us at [info@pedigreeonline.com](mailto:info@pedigreeonline.com), or by mail at Pedigree Online, Inc., 345 S. Coast Highway Ste M2, Encinitas, CA 92024.
