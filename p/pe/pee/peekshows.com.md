# PeekShows.com takes your Privacy Seriously! Secure and Encrypted Website and Servers with Green Bar Protection through Geotrust Extended Validation.

The peekshows Network respects and safeguards the privacy of every individual who visits our website. This Privacy Policy is a formal document that outlines the privacy practices of the Peekshows Network. In this policy, the information we collect about you and how that information may be used will be explained.

**I. Your Personal and Identifiable Information**

Peekshows gives you the option of using a secure transmission method (Green Bar 256Bit HTTPS - Verified by GeoTrust) for sending your personal information. When you provide us with personal and identifiable information, such information will be gathered solely by the Peekshows Network and is for use solely by the Peekshows Network. We may use this information in the following ways, unless stated otherwise: we may store and process your information to contact you and to better understand your needs in order to help improve our services. Financial information that is collected is used to check the users' qualifications for registration and to bill the user for services which they personally purchase. Any purchase made will appear on your statements as Broadcasting Concepts, Inc. to further protect your privacy.

By creating a membership with the Peekshows Network, you enter into an agreement that: all information you provide is both accurate and current, otherwise your account may be put on hold or terminated.

**II. E-mail Correspondence**

The Peekshows Network may utilize electronic newsletters to reach all or a subset of all registered users, using only the verified e-mail addresses provided to us by each registered user. Users may remove themselves from such mailing lists at any time by clicking on the unsubscribe link at the end of any electronic newsletter, or by following this link: [PeekShows.com Email Opt-Out](http://www.peekshows.com/logon/unsubscribe.phtml). If you choose to un-subscribe, The PeekShows Network may still be required to contact you via your supplied email address with important information about your account or purchases you have made. Providing and maintaining a working email address for these reasons is basic requirement of maintaining your account in an 'open' status on The PeekShows Network.

**III. Information Sharing**

Under no circumstances, unless required to do so by subpoena or court order by an authorized law enforcement agent or appointed Officer of the Court, will the Peekshows Network release any personally-identifiable information, in whole or in part, to any third party, without first obtaining the express consent of the individual user.

Broadcasting Concepts, Inc. and PeekShows.com is the sole owner of the information collected on its sites. Broadcasting Concepts, Inc. and Peekshows.com has never sold, shared nor rented this information to any third parties.

**IV. Public Forums**

Peekshows features live video chat room environments available to all its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose any of your personal information.

**V. Minors**

Because Peekshows is an e-commerce driven, web-conferencing platform; the company's services are directed to adults who possess validated credit-card information. Peekshows does not market to individuals under the age of 18.

The Peekshows Network specifically instructs minors not to view our content or attempt to submit their information on our websites. If a minor has provided us with personally identifiable information, a parent or guardian of that minor should contact our customer service department to close the account. The customer service department can be contacted either via e-mail, support@peekshows.com or by phone at 1-877-262-1219.

**VI. Corrections/Updating Personal Information**

You may access information about your account for the purpose of viewing and updating by first logging into the members area and clicking on your account info link. If you discover any information that is inaccurate or out-dated please contact our customer service department via e-mail or phone.

**VII. Security**

Peekshows has implemented security policies, rules and technical measures to protect the personal data that we have under out control from unauthorized access; improper use or disclosure; unauthorized modification; unlawful destruction or accidental loss. Peekshows utilizes Secure Socket Layer (SSL) software when receiving and transmitting electronic payments. Peekshows also encrypts the information that you provided for extra protection.

All employees and data processors, having access to, and associated with the processing of personal data, are obliged to respect the confidentiality of our customers' personal data. Peekshows conducts background and credit checks on all of its employees prior to employment. Peekshows uses a Username and password scheme in order to safeguard your information and limit access to our computer network by our employees and third parties.
