# PEI Burger Love - Prince Edward Island Beef - April 1st to 30th

## Information Collection, Use & Security 

Fresh Media is the sole owner of the information collected on this web site. We take every precaution to protect and secure our databases and user's information, and we will not sell, share or rent this information to others.

## IP Addresses

We collect and analyze traffic on our web sites by keeping track of the IP addresses of our visitors. We log this information so that we can monitor things like the number of visitors visiting the web sites, analyze trends, administer the site, and gather broad demographic information. We do not link your IP address to any personally identifying information.

## Links

This web site contains links to other web sites. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of these linked sites as their privacy policies may differ from ours.

## Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Policy, you should email Fresh Media at [info@freshmedia.ca](mailto:info@freshmedia.ca).

## Changes to the Privacy Policy

Fresh Media retains the right to occasionally update this Privacy Policy in response to changing business circumstances and legal developments.
