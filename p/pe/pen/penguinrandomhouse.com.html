COOKIES

Cookies are small data files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you first visit the Site. We use cookies to anonymously identify new visitors to the Site, recognize those visitors when they re-visit the Site, and track how visitors use the Site (solely for internal analysis). The cookies we use fall into the following categories:

  1. Session Cookies: These cookies remember what you’ve done during the course of your browsing session on the Site. These cookies are erased when you close your browser.
  2. Persistent Cookies: These cookies are used to identify unique visitors to the Site, and help us recognize you when you subsequently visit the Site. These cookies stay on your computer or device, but can be manually deleted.

The information we collect through cookies and similar tools includes information about your computer or device, hardware, software, connection information, Internet Service Provider (“ISP”), browser type and language, clickstream data, operating system, Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, and standard server log information. In no event does the cookie we place on your computer or device tell us anything about your visits to other websites, or your use of the Internet in any way.

Most Web browsers can be set to erase or block the acceptance of cookies or to inform you when a cookie has been sent so that you can refuse the cookie. Please visit www.aboutcookies.org for more information on how to do this on your browser and how to delete cookies from your computer or device. However, please note that refusing a cookie may limit your access to certain areas and/or functions of the Site. By using the Site, you consent to our use of cookies in connection with this Site.
