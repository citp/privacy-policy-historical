# Pew Forum: Privacy Policy

The Pew Forum on Religion and Public Life collects personal information that is voluntarily submitted by visitors to this site in order to respond to requests for publications and other information, including requests for email addresses to be added to our weekly listserv of announcements and events. The Forum also collects aggregate data on visitors to see which parts of the site are being used most frequently, and which information is of the most interest to them.
