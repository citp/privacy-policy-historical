Privacy

PEGI may collect personal information directly from users of this website. Collected information may contain:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Location information (e.g. country, etc.)
  • Technical data (e.g. IP address, etc.)

PEGI may use the information we collect from users in the following ways:

  • Analytics
  • Internal operations

Users are encouraged to keep any personal information and passwords required to access that information safely guarded to prevent breaches of data security.

PEGI will never sell your personal information to third parties under any circumstances. We may distribute your information to third parties from time to time in the course of business but only in the circumstances provided for within this Privacy Policy e.g. where PEGI has contracted with a third party to render a service for PEGI. In any event the level of data protection required from these third parties contracted by PEGI shall be no less than the protection provided by PEGI to you at first instance.

Parental Control Tools

Parental control tools allow parents to protect their children's privacy and online safety according to various parameters. They can select which games children are allowed to play (based on the PEGI age ratings), limit and monitor their online spending, control access to internet browsing and online interaction (chat), and set the amount of time children can spend playing games.
