Privacy & Security

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to Peapod. Peapod does not sell personal information about individual members (such as name, address, e-mail address) to third parties. We do not sell or rent lists of members or use information about individual members outside the scope of the Peapod service. Peapod reserves the right to disclose aggregated information regarding its members and usage of the Peapod Service (such as member demographics and traffic patterns) to advertisers, partners and others for various purposes.

Site Security

At Peapod, we want you to feel safe while shopping with us. That's why we use VeriSign, the only company focused entirely on digital authentication. VeriSign encrypts all of your personal information, including name, address and credit card number, as it travels over the Internet. So you can feel safe entering your credit card information with Peapod. Once received, your personal information is stored in our secure data center.
