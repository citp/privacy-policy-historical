# Privacy Policy - Petra's Planet

> This privacy statement describes how Dramaforum Oy (“Dramaforum”) collects and uses the personal information you provide pursuant to your subscription of Petra’s Planet Magazine. It also describes the choices available to you regarding our use of your personal information and how you can access and update this information.
> 
> If you have questions or complaints regarding our privacy policy or practices, please contact us through support@dramaforum.fi.
>

> **2\. Name of the Data File**
> 
> Petra’s Planet Magazine (the “Service”) Subscriber Database
> 
> **3\. Collected Information**
> 
> Petra’s Planet Magazine collects the following information: Subscriber’s country, title, first name, last name, email, mobile and telephone number.
> 
> **4\. Purpose of Collection**
> 
> The personal information is collected for purposes of maintaining the client relationship, providing the Service, and for developing the Service. The personal information you provide may also be used for communication with you, and for sending you newsletters and marketing communications. Out of respect for your privacy, you may choose to stop receiving our newsletter or marketing emails by following the unsubscribe instructions included in these emails, accessing the email preferences in your account settings page or you can contact us through email support@dramaforum.fi.
> 
> Payment information is not stored.
> 
> **5\. Source of the Information**
> 
> The personal information is obtained directly from you in the following situations:
> 
> when you register as a subscriber to the Service;  
>  when you or someone on your behalf accesses and uses the Service; and  
>  when you or someone on your behalf makes purchases in or via the Service.  
>  Certain information is also collected automatically by means of cookies. Please see Section 9 for more information.
> 
> **6\. Erroneous, Incomplete or Obsolete Information; Right of Inspection**
> 
> Dramaforum does not verify the correctness of the personal information. A user who has logged into the Service may view, correct or delete his user information and purchase history stored in the data file by emailing our Customer Support through support@dramaforum.fi
> 
> If you would like to request an update or deletion of your information or refuse further collection, use or contact by the site please contact us through http://petrasplanet.com/contact-us
> 
> In addition, in accordance with Section 26 of the Personal Data Act, you have the right to inspect all information regarding you stored in the Subscriber Database. The right of inspection may be used by contacting Dramaforum.
> 
> **7\. Disclosures**
> 
> Generally Dramaforum does not disclose personal information to any third parties. However, Dramaforum may disclose personal information to such a third party who Dramaforum uses to provide the Service, where such disclosure is necessary for the provision of the Service. These companies are authorized to use your personal information only as necessary to provide these services to us.
> 
> In addition, Dramaforum may disclose information to the police in connection with criminal investigation and to officials where required by law such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request. The information shall not be disclosed to countries outside the EEA.  
>  If Dramaforum is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our Web site of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information, as well as any choices you may have regarding your personal information.  
>  To any other third party with your prior consent to do so.  
>  ·       Your personal information is not generally transferred outside the European Union. In the event that we transfer your personal information outside the European Union, adequate protection of your personal information is guaranteed by the contracts entered into by us, and for example by relying on the model clauses approved by the European Commission.)
> 
> **8\. Deletion of Information**
> 
> We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you wish to cancel your account or request that we no longer use your information to provide you services contact us through email support@dramaforum.fi. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Notwithstanding the foregoing, payment data is retained for such period of time as is required for accounting purposes or as may be required for the fulfillment of other statutorily obligations.
> 
> **9\. Cookies**
> 
> In order to monitor and enable the use of the Service, cookies may be stored from time to time to on the user’s computer. The cookies are used to collect information specified in this Privacy Policy for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. In addition, cookies are used by Dramaforum and/or its partners to monitor the number of visitors at the Service. When used for these purposes, the cookies are not used to collect personal information or other such information that could be connected to a single user.
> 
> If you do not wish to permit the storage of cookies on your computer, you may disable cookies from the settings of your Internet browser. Due to the nature of the Service it is impossible to use the Service without enabling cookies and thus if you do not wish to enable cookies, you cannot use the Service.
> 
> The use of cookies by our partners, affiliates, tracking utility company, and service providers is not covered by our privacy policy.  We do not have access or control over these cookies.  Our partners, affiliates, tracking utility company, service providers use session ID cookies to make it easier for you to navigate our site, in order for you to use the shopping cart, etc.
> 
> Our web pages contain electronic images known as Web beacons (sometimes called single-pixel gifs) and are used along with cookies to compile aggregated statistics to analyze how our site is used and may be used in some of our emails to let us know which emails and links have been opened by recipients. This allows us to gauge the effectiveness of our customer communications and marketing campaigns.
> 
> **10\. Data Security**
> 
> Access to your personal information is limited to such persons who need to have such access for the purposes of their work obligations. Such persons consist of user care and technical maintenance personnel.
> 
> The security of your personal information is important to us.  We follow generally accepted standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it.  No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however.  Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
> 
> **11\. Links to 3rd  Party Sites**
> 
> Our Site includes links to other Web sites whose privacy practices may differ from those of Dramaforum Oy. If you submit personal information to any of those sites, your information is governed by their privacy policies. We encourage you to carefully read the privacy policy of any Web site you visit.
> 
> **12\. Third Parties**
> 
> We work with Google Analytics to better understand your use of our site and improve your experience. This third party is prohibited from sharing your data. For more information, please visit: www.google.com/analytics
> 
> Petra’s Planet works with third party PocketMags to process your payments. We do not store payment information. PocketMags is prohibited from sharing your data. For more information, please visit: www.pocketmags.com
> 
> **13\. Changes to this Policy**
> 
> Dramaforum may make changes to this Privacy Policy from time to time for any reason. Use of information we collect now is subject to the Privacy Policy in effect at the time such information is used. If we make changes in the way we use your personal information, we will notify you via email or by posting an announcement on our website prior to the change becoming effective. Users are bound by any changes to the Privacy Policy when he or she uses the website or Service after such changes have been first posted.
