# Privacy Statement: PetAdorn

Your privacy is our priority!

Any and all the information collected on this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, disclosed, or loaned!  
Any information you provide will be held with the utmost care and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. If you have any questions, please feel free to call or e-mail us.

**1\. How PetAdorn use your information**

PetAdorn may use your information to:

  * assist you in completing a transaction or order;
  * prevent and detect security threats, fraud or other malicious activity;
  * communicate with you about products and services;
  * provide service and support;
  * update you on new services and benefits;
  * provide personalized promotional offers;
  * select content to be communicated to you;
  * allow you to participate in contests and surveys; and
  * contact you regarding PetAdorn products or services.



We will give you the opportunity to choose your privacy preferences regarding the communications we send.

The reviews posted on our Web site are only posted with the expressed permission of the individual or company who provided that reviews.

PetAdorn may send you email communications, survey invitations, or event invitations if we have your permission. And, it means you can have the confidence that when you subscribe our newsletter, you may safely unsubscribe at any time.

**2\. How PetAdorn Share Your Information**

Credit card information is used only for payment processing and fraud prevention. Credit card information and other sensitive personal information required to process a credit decision is not used for any other purpose by our financial services providers or PetAdorn and will not be kept in our system, unless you ask us to retain your credit card information for future purchases.

PetAdorn will not sell, rent or lease your personal information to others except as described in this Statement. PetAdorn shares personal information in the following ways:

PetAdorn retains service providers and suppliers to deliver complete products, services and customer solutions and to assist PetAdorn with marketing and communication initiatives. These providers and suppliers include, for example, credit card processors, providers of customer support and email service providers, live-help, automated data processors, and shipping agents. Suppliers and service providers are required by contract to keep confidential the information received on behalf of PetAdorn and may not use it for any purpose other than to carry out the services they are performing for PetAdorn.

If you have any questions, please feel free to call or [e-mail](https://www.petadorn.com/help.php?section=contactus) us.
