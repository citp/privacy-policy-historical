# Privacy policy - xxxgamergirls.com

* ##### User Supplied Information:

###### We receive and store any information you enter on our Site or give us in any other way. Information that we collect can be: Account details, name and address, e-mail address and any information you submit and accompanying information, and financial information, including Social Security numbers and Driver's License numbers (as necessary). This list is not exclusive, and may be altered without notice based on information that you supply voluntarily.

* ##### Right to refrain from giving information:

###### You have right to refrain from giving us certain information, but then you might not be able to take advantage of all of our features. We use the information that you provide for to enhance the service, such as responding to your requests. However, you may not mask your identity by providing false or misleading information. For example, you may not use IP masking to mask your Internet access location, including without limitation, proxy servers, masking software or the like.

* ##### Collection:

###### XXXGAMERGIRLS.COM collects personal information when you register with XXXGAMERGIRLS.COM, when you use XXXGAMERGIRLS.COM products or services, when you visit XXXGAMERGIRLS.COM pages, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. XXXGAMERGIRLS.COM may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.

* ##### Information Sought:

###### When you register we ask for information such as your full legal name, e-mail address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. For some financial products and services, and as necessary, we may also ask for your Social Security number, driver's license number and information about your assets. Once you register with XXXGAMERGIRLS.COM and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.

* ##### IP Address and Cookies:

###### XXXGAMERGIRLS.COM automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, XXXGAMERGIRLS.COM cookie information, and the page you request.

* ##### Supplemental Software:

###### A number of companies offer utilities designed to help you visit websites anonymously. Although we will not be able to provide you with a personalized experience at XXXGAMERGIRLS.COM if we cannot recognize you, we want you to be aware that these tools exist.

* ##### E-mail Communications:

###### We communicate and make special offers available via email. To help us make e-mails more useful and interesting, we often receive a confirmation when you open e-mail from XXXGAMERGIRLS.COM if your computer supports such capabilities. We often receive email lists from other companies who offer similar services to ours. To avoid sending duplicate emails to our customers, we compare our customer list to these lists received from other companies. If you do not want to receive e-mail or other mail from us, please adjust your e-mail contact preferences. Without exception, notifications relating to active Skill Challenges cannot be avoided and must be sent to Your supplied email address.

* ##### Information from Other Sources:

###### From time to time, we receive information about you from other sources and add it to our account information.
