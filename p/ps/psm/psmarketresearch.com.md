# Privacy Policy | Prescient & Strategic (P&S) Intelligence

### Last updated: May 25, 2018

#### Our Commitment to Privacy

This privacy statement describes how Prescient & Strategic Intelligence Private Limited collects and uses the personal information you provide on our **website,**[ psmarketresearch.com](https://www.psmarketresearch.com/) It also describes the choices available to you regarding our use of your personal information and how you can access and update this information.

#### European Union General Data Protection Regulation

Prescient & Strategic Intelligence Private Limited follows the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), as laid down by the European law, to protect your rights regarding personal information.

#### Cookie Policy

Cookies are files with small amount of data that is commonly used an anonymous unique identifier. These are sent to your browser from the website that you visit and are stored on your computer’s hard drive.

Our website uses these "cookies" to collect information and to improve our Service. You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies and know when a cookie is being sent to your computer. By continuing to use of our site you consent to our use of cookies.

The legal basis for the processing of these information is that it is necessary for the purposes of our legitimate interests in maintaining the safe operation of our website and improving our website, as well as in learning from how users interact with our website.

#### Protection of Children's Information

We are a general audience site and do not direct any of our content specifically at children under 13 years of age. We understand and are committed to respecting the sensitive nature of children’s privacy online. If we learn or have a reason to suspect that a site user is under 13 years of age, we will promptly delete that user’s account.

#### Security

We are strongly committed to protecting your information and, therefore, employ several industry standard methods for securing user information and data. However, because of the inherent data risks associated with the use of internet and cellular networks, we cannot guarantee 100% security to the users. Nonetheless, in the unlikely case of a security breach on our database, we assure to take a prompt action to address the issue.

We will retain your information for as long as it is required by us to provide you services. If you wish we no longer use your information to provide you services, contact us at support@psmarketresearch.com. We will retain and use your information, as necessary, to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements duly.

#### Additional Policy Information

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. When we do, we will also revise the "last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy from time to time to stay informed about how we are protecting the information we collect.
