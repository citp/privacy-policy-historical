# PhilippineCompanies.com - Privacy Policy

Last Modified: July 7, 2015

We at [PhilippineCompanies.com](http://www.philippinecompanies.com/ "Business Directory Philippines") respect the privacy of our site visitors and are committed in protecting any and all information we may require of our visitors as they peruse our website. Below are some of our commitments in the use and distribution of any private information that our visitors might divulge during his/her visit to our site. 

## Private User/Account Information

Information given by users to this website is strictly voluntary. We do require certain private information from users who wish to utilize certain services of this website such as in submitting reviews or when registering or editing business listings. These contact information will be kept strictly confidential and accessible only to qualified personnel. We will not divulge user information to any third party without the prior consent of the users other than as requested or required by the Government of the Republic of the Philippines of this website for any legal proceedings or other purpose.

## Public Content

Information contributed, posted or shared by users of this website such as reviews, photos, business information, etc. are understood to be for public consumption. PhilippineCompanies.com may display these information in its website, share it with other users and provide it to third parties without prior consent from the original poster.

## Logs and Analytics

This site utilizes logs and analytics software that track user behaviour across our website. This can include pages you have visited as well as time and duration of your visits.

## Advertising

PhilippineCompanies.com uses third-party advertising networks to support our website so we can offer its services to the public for free. These advertising networks uses cookies and web beacons that enables them and their partners to serve ads to visitors of this website based on your visit to our site and/or other sites you have visited in the Internet.

## Social Media Websites

If you signed up with our site using your Facebook account, we may have receive information about you have posted in that website. With your permission, we may also post your activities in our website to your social media account(s). Note that sharing, liking or tweeting about content of our website using the Share Buttons provided will allow the specified Social Media service to gather information about your visit to our website.

## Security

PhilippineCompanies.com strives to provide its users a secure experience by using industry standards in maintaining data transmission and storage security. However, no method of transmission via Internet or method of data storage is 100% secure and as such we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information stored with us.
