Privacy & Security Policy

This Privacy and Security Policy addresses the privacy of visitors to the Phonebook.com.pk website. It identifies the type of data and information J&S Enterprises and Jang Group of Newspapers collects from visitors, how we use and disclose it, and the steps we take to secure your privacy.

Information Collection: J&S Enterprises and Jang Group of Newspapers collects personal information when you become a Registered User with Phonebook.com.pk, when you use Phonebook.com.pk products and services, when you visit Phonebook.com.pk pages, and when you enter promotions or contests.

We may also collect non-personal information about your visit, such as the domain name and the name of the web page from which you entered our site, how much time you spend on each page, and the IP address associated with your computer. This information is used to improve the performance and operation of the website.

When you become a Registered User, we ask for information such as your name, birth date, gender, country, city, phone numbers and email address. For the delivery of Phonebook products we may also ask for your address and area.

Phonebook.com.pk automatically receives and records information on our server from your browser, including your IP address, Phonebook.com.pk cookie information, and any page you request.

Phonebook.com.pk uses the information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, complete your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, and / or provide statistical reporting for internal and external customers.

Your Account Information: You can edit your Phonebook.com.pk Account Information at any time. By becoming a Registered User, you agree to receive select information from J&S Enterprises / Jang Group of Newspapers and its partners.

Internet Cookies and Related Technologies: We may use cookies to improve the content delivered to you while you are on our website. Cookies do not contain or capture any personal information but it may capture computer identification information. Your decision to refuse cookies from Phonebook.com.pk will not prevent you from using our site, but may limit your ability to take advantage of some of our advanced features.

Some pages of the website may use computer code called "one-pixel gifs", "clear gifs" or "Web beacons" which are embedded in web pages to monitor the activity on our website. This technology does not capture or contain any of your personal information.

J&S Enterprises / Jang Group of Newspapers is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of other websites. Any third party cookies are subject to the privacy policy of the company placing them.

Information Disclosed To Third Parties: J&S Enterprises / Jang Group of Newspapers may use aggregated information about how visitors use our web site and disclose this information with our partners and related third parties in order to improve our website and attract advertisers and listings. For example, we may share how many individuals visit the website and on average how long they stay on each page. Such use and sharing of aggregated information does not disclose any of your personal information to third parties.

J&S Enterprises / Jang Group of Newspapers does not rent, sell, disclose or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products, services, or information you have requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:
