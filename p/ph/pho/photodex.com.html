privacy

Photodex protects the information our customers. We do not distribute or resell the personal information of any user.

Photodex uses any personal information entered on this or any of our sites only to process your requests and notify of important product updates. At no time will your personal information be made available to an outside party, or used to send unsolicited mail.

Collection of Personal Information

Certain personal information is required when you engage in some activities on this site. Personal information is collected when you enter information into any of several different places:

  • Online ordering / product registration
  • Requesting technical support on a product
  • Creating a member account on one of our sites

In the case of online ordering and product registration, your information is used to process your order, generate your product registration, and deliver products. This information is not shared with any other organization. For technical support requests, your information is used as part of our quality assurance program. We may use the information to contact you regarding your support issue.

Creating a member account uses your personal information to validate and protect your account.

Updating Your Personal Information

To continue receiving product upgrade notifications after your information changes, contact us and let us know what changed.

E-Mail Notifications

Photodex Corporation does not send unsolicited mail. Photodex may occasionally notify you of product updates and releases.

At no time will Photodex make your e-mail address available to a third party.

The personal information you provide to Photodex will never be used by an outside company, and will never result in unsolicited (spam) e-mail.

Unsubscribing Your E-Mail Address

You may unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time. If you choose to do so, Photodex will no longer send you e-mail notifications of product updates.
