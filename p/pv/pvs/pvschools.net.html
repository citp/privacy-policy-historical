Privacy Policy
We collect no personal information about you when you visit the Paradise Valley Unified School District website, unless you choose to provide this information to us. However, we may collect and store the following information automatically:

  • The Internet Protocol (IP) address and the name of the host from which you access the internet, whether yours individually or provided as a proxy by your Internet Service Provider (ISP).
  • The browser type and version you are using to access the site. 
  • The operating system and version you are running on your computer.
  • The date and time you access our site.
  • The pages you access.
  • The Internet address of the website from which you linked directly to our site.

We use the summary statistics to help us make our site more useful to visitors (such as accessing what information is of most and least interest to visitors) and for other purposes such as determining the site's technical design specifications and identifying system performance or problem areas.

This information is not shared with anyone beyond the PV Schools website staff except as required for site security purposes to ensure that the PV Schools websites remain available to all users and when required by law enforcement investigation. We use the information only as a source on anonymous statistical information, and no other attempts are made to identify individual users or their usage habits.
