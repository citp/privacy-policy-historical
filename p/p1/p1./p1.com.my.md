# P1 - Built For More: 4G, Fiber, Voice & More

Packet One Networks (Malaysia) Sdn. Bhd. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

**What This Privacy Policy Covers & its Benefits**

This policy covers how P1 and its affiliated companies treat personal information that P1 collects and receives, including information related to your past use of P1 products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available.

P1 is committed in protecting your privacy and is committed to ensuring that your personal information is not disclosed to non-permitted parties.

P1 reserves the right to change this privacy policy at any stage and you are advised to call our Hunting Line: 1300 03 1300

**Users below 18 years**

P1 websites are not directed at users under 18 years of age. If you are under 18 years, please get parental/guardian consent before posting your personal information on our website. If you do not have this consent, you are not allowed to provide us with your personal information. P1 is not obligated to verify your age.

**Information Collection and Use**

  1. P1 collects personal information when you register with P1, when you use P1 products or services and/or when you visit P1 web-pages. 
  2. When you register we ask for information such as and not limited to your name, identification number or company number, email address, birth date, gender, residential address and / or business address, occupation, industry, and personal interests and preferences. When you register with P1 and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us. 
  3. P1 uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, provide future services and support, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients. 
  4. P1 collects the personal information through the following methods or tools :-
    1. Communication protocol and cookies;
    2. Registration forms;
    3. Sales inquiries and financial transactions;
    4. Offline communications and user preferences;
    5. Third party sources of information; or
    6. Any other method deemed appropriate by P1.
  5. P1 uses remarketing to better target our ads to interested customers. When you visit our site, you may see additional P1 ads on sites with Google Display Network and on Facebook. You can opt-out of Google Analytics for Display Advertising and customize Google Display Network ads using the Ads Settings. 



**Information Sharing and Disclosure**

  1. P1 does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:
  2. We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or withP1 under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help P1 communicate with you about offers from P1 and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information unless No. 3 below applies.
  3. We shall exercise our legal rights if necessary or defend against legal claims.
  4. We shall share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of P1's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
