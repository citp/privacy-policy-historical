# XLNTads - Connecting Brands and Creators

Your privacy is important to you, the user ("You") and to XLNTads, LLC ("XLNTads"). XLNTads will operate its web site, www.xlntads.com ("Site") by the following principles. The principles apply to information that personally identifies You ("Personal Information"). BY CONSENTING TO THE TERMS OF USE FOR THE SITE, OR BY ACCESSING THE SITE, YOU CONSENT TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY (the "Policy"). 

* **Personal Information**

The portion of the Site accessible to the general public will not expressly ask for information that personally identifies You or that allows the Site to contact You. However, the Site will allow You to register and establish a profile containing information selected by You to showcase Yourself to Your peers and to the brand personnel that will be visiting the Site to see ads created for their brands ("Creator Profile"). You acknowledge and agree that any information that you place in a Creator Profile will be accessible by all users of the Site. Information contained in the Creator Profile is not considered Personal Information under this Agreement. 

Portions of the Site will expressly ask you to register and generate a user ID ("Your Site Unique ID") and password ("Site Password") in order to access and use such portions. Certain other portions may require additional codes. The information that You provide to XLNTads during the XLNTads member registration or renewal process, including without limitation the information listed below, together with any additional information You provide to XLNTads and information regarding the manner in which You use Your XLNTads membership is Personal Information, will not be processed or disclosed by XLNTads except as permitted by this Policy or as required or allowed by applicable law or legal process. 

The following information is requested and/or collected when You register to become a member or update Your membership with the Site:

* Required Information 

The Site is not designed to attract children under 13 years old, and members are required to be 16 years of age or older. Accordingly, XLNTads will not knowingly collect or maintain information at the Site from children. 

* **Accuracy of Personal Information**

When possible, the Site will provide You with the means to make sure that Your Member information is correct and current. You may view Your member information by entering the restricted portions of the Site using Your Site Unique ID and Site Password and certain other codes as necessary. To correct any factual inaccuracies, You must edit Your Member Information and hit submit. 

* **Protection of Personal Information**

XLNTads endeavors to protect the security of Personal Information collected on the Site from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. The Site uses an encrypted connection when transferring sensitive Personal Information. XLNTads also has put in place physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to help safeguard and secure the information XLNTads collects online in accordance with industry standards. When XLNTads contracts with third parties to perform any services supporting the Site, XLNTads will require that the third parties protect the security of the Personal Information collected with comparable security standards. 

Neither passwords nor security systems are foolproof or necessarily work as intended, including encryption, and people can commit intentional crimes, make mistakes or fail to follow policies. Although XLNTads takes what we believe are commercially reasonable security precautions, XLNTads does not guaranty security. You agree to do what You reasonably can to help promote security (e.g., never reveal your password or make it accessible to others). 

If You or any other person believes they are a victim of identity theft entitled by law to request information from XLNTads, write XLNTads at XLNTads' contact address in principle 4 below and also provide all required information. XLNTads will supply without charge the information legally required to be disclosed that XLNTads then has, subject to applicable law and reserving all of our rights and defenses. 

* **Use of Personal Information & Opt-Out Policy**

XLNTads uses Your Personal Information to operate the Site, to contact You (including without limitation regular e-mail alerts); to effect, enforce, investigate, collect on and account for transactions related to the Site; to adjust XLNTads' offerings of products and services; to provide information to sponsors; and to comply with applicable law. XLNTads may provide Your Personal Information, or You may provide Your information directly, to sponsors and third parties who are responsible for implementing some of XLNTads' services (e.g., third party hosting). Unique identifiers are used to verify Your identity. 

XLNTads uses Your Internet Protocol (IP) address to analyze trends, track a user's movement through the Site, help diagnose problems with XLNTads' server, and to administer the Site. IP addresses are not linked to Personal Information. 

XLNTads uses "cookies" to save Your Site Unique ID and Site Password so that You have the option of choosing not to have to re-enter them each time You visit the Site. A cookie is a small text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to Your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to You and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to You. 

The Site contains links to other sites, including to sponsor's sites. XLNTads is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites or of sponsors. 

XLNTads also uses outside companies to host portions of the Site and the ads. XLNTads will require that these third parties do not further use or disclose Your Personal Information for any purpose other than providing the services. 

Please be aware that this Policy and any choices You make on the Site will not necessarily apply to Personal Information you may have provided to XLNTads in the context of other, separately operated, XLNTads products or services.
