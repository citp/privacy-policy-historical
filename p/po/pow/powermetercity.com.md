# Privacy Policy - Power Meter City

At Power Meter City, we take your privacy very seriously. In order to protect your privacy, the following information explains what we collect, how it is used and how it is safeguarded.

## What Information Is Collected

When you place an order, we collect the following standard order information:

  * Contact information: Name, phone number and email address
  * Purchase information: Credit card number and billing and/or shipping information. (If you complete your purchase using PayPal, we do not require any credit card information.)



Please note – We have the ability to retain data that you provide on our website, even if you do not complete your transaction. Such contact information may be used to contact you to assist you in completing your transaction.

## How That Information Is Used

We use your information in order to process, ship and service your order as well as provide customer service.

Note that as a valued customer, we also use your email address in order to inform you of special offers. If you wish to remove yourself from these notifications, you can either “Unsubscribe” at the bottom of the form, or contact us at info@powermetercity.com and we would be happy to remove you.

We do not rent, sell or trade your information to third parties. Never.

## How We Keep Your Data Secure

When you complete a transaction with us, your data is securely processed through our SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) protected payment gateway using 128-bit encryption.

## Contact Us

We take your privacy very seriously. If you have any questions or comments regarding our privacy policy, please contact us [here](https://powermetercity.com/contact-us/).
