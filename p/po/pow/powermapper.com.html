Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us and we will not do anything to violate your trust.

We collect these sorts of information on this site:

  • Anonymous aggregate information about visits to this web site (this cannot be associated with a particular user)
  • Personal information collected from customers who purchase or evaluate products (name, company, address, email address, phone number, and fax number)
  • The optional e-mail address used by the SortSite online demo to send you the scan results.

We treat customer information as strictly confidential - it is never revealed to third parties. Online orders are processed by Plimus, please see the Plimus Privacy Policy for further details.

We use customer information for the following purposes:

  • Sending replacement copies to customers whose hard disks have failed
  • Determining upgrade/update eligibility
  • Informing existing customers of new versions
  • Conducting market research

Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. (“Google”). Google Analytics uses “cookies”, which are text files placed on your computer, to help the website analyze how users use the site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States . Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the website, compiling reports on website activity for website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage.
