# None

**Collection of Personal Information**  
Certain information such as your name and email are collected when you register or donate. This information will not be shared with any third party source, with the exception of law enforcement officials. Additionally, any communications exchanged between you and other players of Pokemon Planet may be stored. This includes in-game and forum public and private chat messages. 

**How This Information May be Used**  
Any personal information collected by Pokemon Planet may be used in the follow ways:

  * Development and debugging processes
  

  * To improve customer service
  

  * To verify possession of an account in the event of a lost or stolen password
  

  * To send occasional emails regarding donation statuses, game news, updates, service information, etc



**Cookies**  
Cookies are small files that are stored on your computer. Cookies enables websites or service providers to recognize your browser and remember certain information. Pokemon Planet uses cookies to enhance your web experience and save preferences for future visits, and to compile data about site traffic and interactions. 

**Third Party Links**  
Occasionally Pokemon Planet may offer third party products or services on the website. These third party sites have independent privacy policies and terms of use, therefore Pokemon Planet does not have any responsibility or liability for the content or activities of these websites. Other users may also link third party websites via the forums, and likewise Pokemon Planet is not affiliated nor has any responsibility with these websites. 

**Privacy Protections for Children**  
In order to protect the privacy of minors, we do not allow children under the age of 13 to participate in any service provided by Pokemon Planet without the consent of a parent or guardian. 

**Consent**  
By using the Pokemon Planet website you consent to this privacy policy.
