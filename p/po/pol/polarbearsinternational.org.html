Privacy Policy

Polar Bears International respects your privacy. We do not trade, share, nor sell your personal information with anyone, nor do we send donor mailings or other mailings on behalf of other organizations.

PBI Donations and Adoptions

PBI collects names, street addresses, contact email addresses, telephone numbers, and credit card information when you make a donation, adopt a polar bear, or order promotional materials through our online secure pages. We do not retain credit card information online. We keep a record of personal information derived from orders and donations to the extent required by our auditors to verify our accounting system.

If you adopt a polar bear, we may use your email or telephone number to request additional information about your order or preferred shipping method, or to notify you of your order status.

We may use your email address or mailing address to send you a free copy of our newsletter and/or information. If you don't want to receive communications from Polar Bears International, please let us know by marking that option on your personal contact information form, or by using the unsubscribe option on the information you receive.
We do not trade, share, nor sell your personal information with anyone.

Membership

PBI retains names and email and mailing addresses—and all optional information you provide us—in our secure membership database, which we use to keep you informed of PBI business and issues. This information remains private within PBI.

Log Files

PBI's website server maintains log files that contain IP addresses and/or domain names whenever you request a page. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information and we use information from the log files solely in the aggregate to analyze site traffic and evaluate public interest. The URL of a referring web page may also be logged for the same purpose.

Cookies

The PBI website uses cookies only for the purposes of authentication (member login) and sending data between pages (state). The cookie itself does not contain any personally identifiable information. The cookie is immediately deleted when you close your browser window.

Review of Anonymous Data from Google Analytics

We are committed to ensuring that our website contains content that is valuable and useful to our customers and potential customers who visit. To assist us in this, we have implemented the Google Analytics demographics and interest reporting features on our website. These tools allow us to review anonymous data regarding the gender, age, and interests of website visitors and adapt our website content to better reflect their needs. Although this information is collected through your Google Ad settings, it is not provided to us in a personally identifiable format. This means we cannot identify what information is about you, and we will not try to figure it out.
You can opt-out of Google Analytics collecting this data through the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Links

This website contains links to other websites. PBI is not responsible for the privacy practices of other websites.

Email

If you send PBI an email, your return address will be displayed in the header we receive. We will use this information only to answer your inquiry. We may on occasion keep your message as a record of the contact but at no time will we trade, share, or sell email information with others.

Social Networks, Blogs, and Email Threads

Any identifying information you post in a social networking site or an email will be public information. PBI will make no effort to collect personal information from these sources, but it's possible that other visitors may record your email address or other information. If this concerns you, please post under a pseudonym or with an altered email address.

Age

PBI may ask you to voluntarily provide your birth date for certain communications programs we invite you to participate in (such as receiving an electronic birthday greeting). You are not required to provide this information unless you choose to participate.

PBI Security

PBI provides our members and supporters with a secure experience when ordering memberships, making a donation, or adopting a polar bear. Any credit card information you enter on our site is strongly encrypted by industry-standard protocol, SSL (Secure Sockets Layer).

We take appropriate precautions to protect the security and privacy of the information stored on our computer networks. Our systems are secure, and access to your data by our employees is limited to a need to know basis, controlled by password-protected systems, and monitored for abuse.
