**Changes**

**Collection Of Personal Information**  
We do not collect personal information on the sly.  
We only collect personal information that is necessary for the functioning of our site, and only with your consent. We only use lawful and fair means to do so, and not in an unreasonably intrusive way.

**Disclosure**  
If our lawyers tell us to, we will hand over your personal details to the police.  
We will not use your personal information other than for the disclosed purpose unless we have reason to suspect that unlawful activity has been, is being or may be engaged in, and uses or discloses the personal information as a necessary part of its investigation of the matter or in reporting its concerns to relevant persons or authorities; or (a) the use or disclosure is required or authorised by or under law; or (b) we reasonably believe that the use or disclosure is reasonably necessary for one or more of the following by or on behalf of an enforcement body: (i) the prevention, detection, investigation, prosecution or punishment of criminal offences, breaches of a law imposing a penalty or sanction or breaches of a prescribed law; (ii) the enforcement of laws relating to the confiscation of the proceeds of crime; (iii) the protection of the public revenue; (iv) the prevention, detection, investigation or remedying of seriously improper conduct or prescribed conduct; (v) the preparation for, or conduct of, proceedings before any court or tribunal, or implementation of the orders of a court or tribunal.

**Data Security**  
Securing your personal information is very important to us.  
Pollenblog.com.au will take reasonable steps to protect the personal information we hold from misuse and loss and from unauthorised access, modification or disclosure. Pollenblog.com.au will take reasonable steps to destroy or permanently de-identify personal information if it is no longer needed for any purpose for which the information may be used or disclosed.
