# 

## Privacy Statement for Portlandtimbers.com

The following statements describes the privacy practices for portlandtimbers.com:

  * All personal information about our users is collected for internal purposes only
  * IP addresses are logged for aggregate statistics and diagnosis
  * We only share aggregated statistics with our partners
  * Security settings protects the misuse of sensitive information
  * Portlandtimbers.com does not use cookies to track any user information



* * *

## Personal User Information

Portlandtimbers.com collects personal information only from those users who complete and submit one of our contact forms. This information is used for the following purposed only:

  * To record the contact information of the user in order to fulfill his/her request
  * To identify demographic usage of portlandtimbers.com
  * To identify technilogical usage of Portlandtimbers.com



* * *

## Log Files

portlandtimbers.com uses IP addresses to diagnose server problems, analyze trends, administer the site, and gather demographic information for aggregate use.

IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Portlandtimbers.com only shares aggregated statistics with partners and advertisers. This information cannot identify any individual person.

Portlandtimbers.com has standard security measures in place to protect the loss and misuse of sensitive information.

* * *

## Cookies

Portlandtimbers.com does not use cookies to store any personally identifiable information. The sole use of cookies on this website is to aid navigation and site usage. Only information relevant to the site, not to or about the user, is stored in these cookies.

* * *

## Links

Portlandtimbers.com contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. This privacy document applies only to portlandtimbers.com.

* * *

## Contacting the Web Site

For any questions about this privacy statement, please contact [info@pgepark.com](mailto:info@pgepark.com)
