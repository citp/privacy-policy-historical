# Pornlib - Privacy Policy

### Privacy Policy 

The Pornlib.com site is committed to respecting the privacy of its online visitors. This statement discloses the site’s privacy practices- what information we collect and how we use it.

**Information Gathering and Use**  
Pornlib.com does not require visitors to submit personal information to access any pages of  the Pornlib.com site. Submission of personal data is always voluntary. Information that is received online is used for internal marketing analysis to enhance our Web site’s content.

Users who register to receive free e-mailings from Pornlib.com do so voluntarily. Any personal information provided by users who choose to sign up for these mailings is submitted at the user’s discretion. Information that is provided by the user will not be forwarded, traded or sold to outside organizations. Users who register for free e-mailings may request to be removed from our e-mail list at any time. Pornlib.com reserves the right to contact any registrant at any time to answer questions they submit or to notify them of any changes to our registration policy.

**Information Tracking and Cookies**  
Pornlib.com uses a software to track the number of visitors to the Pornlib.com site. The statistics show us the pages that are visited most often, and the domains from which people visit Pornlib.com. This data is analyzed internally for trends and helps us determine what pages need to be updated. No personal information is collected during the tracking process.

We may use "cookies" to improve our content and your next visit to the Pornlib.com site. "Cookies" are a form of technology that help us to track your interests based on the pages you visit. Cookies cannot be used to gather personal information about a visitor. Pornlib.com does not know the name, e-mail address, phone number, or address of anybody who visits the Pornlib.com site. Therefore, all visitors remain entirely anonymous.

**Information Security and Access**  
To ensure the security of personal information submitted by our users, any information we may collect is stored in a secure area.

Please direct any questions regarding our privacy policy, personal information you have submitted, or the use of the information collected to [**privacypolicy@Pornlib.com**](mailto:privacypolicy@pornlib.com)

Please note that the Pornlib.com site contains links to other Web sites. We are not responsible for either the content or privacy practices of those Web sites.

Pornlib.com reserves the right to update this policy at any time.
