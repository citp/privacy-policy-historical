Privacy Policy Points2Shop

1. Overview

The publication of this document rescinds all previous privacy policies, implied spoken or written and establishes

2.2 De Jong Webdevelopment is based in Netherlands at the following Postal Address:

3. Anonymous Access

3.1 Visitors can access the web site home pages and browse the sites without disclosing personal data.


4. Services and Links

4.1 De Jong Webdevelopment web sites allow communication with other visitors and posts information that can be accessed by other visitors. Communication via these posts is voluntary. All information communicated is the responsibility and discretion of the individual and may be collected by other visitors.

4.2 De Jong Webdevelopment sites may include links to third-party Web service providers. De Jong Webdevelopment is not responsible for the content, accuracy, or privacy policies of third-party web services.


5. Automatic Data Collection

5.1 De Jong Webdevelopment may automatically log personal information by means such as programming or link information automatically logged by such means with personal data about specific individuals for purposes of technical and customer administration.

5.2 De Jong Webdevelopment employs the use of cookies to store personal data or links information stored in cookies with personal data about specific individuals for purposes of technical and customer administration.


6. Data Collection and Purpose

6.1 De Jong Webdevelopment collects data volunteers while using our services.

6.2 De Jong Webdevelopment does not collect information about visitors from other sources, such as public records or bodies, or private organizations.

6.3 Access to collected personal data is available upon request.

6.4 Postal address information is only used to receive information for which the address is provided. This includes periodic contact from De Jong Webdevelopment to enquire about customer satisfaction and the status of customer accounts.

6.5 Telephone numbers collected by De Jong Webdevelopment are only used to promote and support affairs internal to De Jong Webdevelopment and are not shared or distributed to third parties for any reason.


7. Children's Privacy

7.1 De Jong Webdevelopment does not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 13. Minors 13 to 18 are permitted to access the services of the site only with parental permission. It is the responsibility of the individual and the parent to ensure such permissions are obtained.

7.2 De Jong Webdevelopment does not take specific steps to protect the privacy of children who disclose personal data.

7.3 De Jong Webdevelopment does not provide information about personal data practices on the home pages, or at those sites where personal data is collected.


8. Disclosure

8.1 De Jong Webdevelopment does not disclose personal data to subsidiaries or other organizations.


9. Confidentiality and Security

9.1 De Jong Webdevelopment does not employ the use of secure transmission methods to send personal data.

9.2 De Jong Webdevelopment has implemented security policies, rules and technical measures to protect the personal data from unauthorized access, improper use or disclosure, unauthorized modification and unlawful destruction or accidental loss.

9.3 De Jong Webdevelopment employees and data processors with access or association to the processing of personal data are obliged to respect the confidentiality of visitor personal data.

9.4 De Jong Webdevelopment does not disclose personal data to State institutions and authorities unless required by law.


10. Access to Personal Data

10.1 Visitors may request access to their own specific personal information collected by De Jong Webdevelopment by e-mailing a request to info@points2shop.com or visiting the member account information section of the web sites: http://www.points2shop.com/members/account/ or, http://www.cashle.com/members/account/

10.2 Access to personal information may require proof of identity

10.3 Access to personal data is provided without charge.

10.4 Personal data may be challenged or updated and ,where appropriate, data will be completed, erased, rectified or amended.

10.5 De Jong Webdevelopment reserves the right to refuse access to personal data. In such a case reasons will be given for refusal.


11. Privacy Compliance

11.1 The De Jong Webdevelopment privacy policy is compliant with the following international and national regulations:

11.1.1 Global: OECD Privacy Guidelines on the Protection of Privacy and Transborder Flows of Personal Data Guidelines

11.1.2 Global: United Nations Guidelines for the Regulation of Personal Data Files Adopted by General Assembly Resolution 45/95 of 14 December 1990

11.1.3 United States: Privacy Act of 1974

11.1.4 European Union: European Convention on Human Rights

11.1.5 Canada: Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act

11.2 Privacy compliance is ensured by periodic self-assessment.
