# Privacy Policy

|  | 

**Privacy Policy**

For visitors to PDR _health,_ our web server automatically recognizes the consumer's domain name, and e-mail address (where possible).

We collect the domain name and e-mail address (where possible) of visitors to our web site, the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, aggregated information on what pages users access or visit, and information volunteered by users, such as survey information.

The information we collect is used for internal review to improve the content of our web site. We will not use the information in any way that identifies users individually to any third party without the user's consent, but may use this information in aggregated form.
