We will continue to enhance our security procedures as new technology becomes available. We recommend that you do not divulge your password to anyone.

Use of Cookies
Some of our pages use a feature of your browser called a "cookie." Cookies, by themselves, are not able to extract any personal information from you. This cookie automatically identifies your computer - but not you - to our servers when you visit our site. Unless you specifically tell us, PDFConvertOnline.com will never know who you are, even though we may assign your computer a cookie. Also, PDFConvertOnline.com can only read cookies created by PDFConvertOnline.com. Cookies allow us to personalize our site for you and to provide you with information that fits your needs and desires.
