General Information   
CTdeveloping, LLC (hereafter "CTdeveloping") takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following information to learn more about our privacy practices. 

  * This policy covers what personally identifiable information CTdeveloping collects and how it is treated. Personally identifiable information is information that can be used to identify visitors to the http://www.ctdeveloping.com, http://www.pdfescape.com, & http://blog.pdfescape.com Web Sites. This information may be limited to an IP address / Session ID or extend to personally identifiable information such as your name, address, email address, phone number, or PDF form data.
  * This policy only applies to http://www.ctdeveloping.com, http://www.pdfescape.com, & http://blog.pdfescape.com and does not extend to the practices of other Web Sites that we may link to, companies that we do not own or control, or to people that we do not employ or manage.

Information Collection   
When visiting Web Sites certain information about you and your computer can sometimes be collected. This section explains what information CTdeveloping collects, and how it is used. 

Information Collected \- What information do we collect and why? 

We collect the following identifiable information about our visitors:

  * user.name
  * user.info.online.email   _(optional)_
  * user.info.postal   _(optional)_
  * user.info.telecom.telephone   _(optional)_
  * user.billing.account.information   _(optional, not saved to server)_
  * user.pdfescape.forms   _(optional, only applies to www.pdfescape.com)_
  * dynamic.browsertype
  * dynamic.clickstream
  * dynamic.cookies
  * dynamic.http
  * dynamic.interactionrecord
  * dynamic.ip
  * dynamic.operatingsystem
  * dynamic.sessionid

  
We collect identifiable information for the following reasons: 

  * Completion and Support of Current Activity
  * Web Site and System Administration
  * Research and Development
  * Private User Accounts
  * Public User Forum/Support/Knowledge Base/Blog Activity

  
Collection of name, email address, and billing information are used for process, verifying, and delivery of orders. They will also be used to set up your user account(s) and invoices. This information will be protected via password. Collection of name, email address, and phone number, may be used for responses to sales, billing, support issues, questions, comments, and concerns. Information collected in the forum/support/knowledge base/blog will be used as specified in preferences. Information collected in surveys will be used for research and evaluation.  
Information will not be shared with third parties except as necessary for delivery of orders, processing of payments, billing of orders, PDFescape form submission, and as required by law.

Information Recipients \- Who has access to the information collected?   


  * Ourselves and/or our entities acting as our agents or entities for which we are acting as an agent.
  * Delivery services possibly following different practices.
  * Forum/support/knowledge base/blog users whom look at your user profile have minimal access to your personal information as you provide and allow on the site. Your statements, comments, questions, issues, or other submitted data may be made public, not disclosing personal information such as name or email address associated with the statements, and/or published without credit, royalty, or consent. Submission of information, statements, comments, questions, issues, or other data to a CTdeveloping website implies that you allow and grant permanent right to CTdeveloping to publish, distribute, or make public all submitted information, statements, comments, questions, issues, or other data now or at later date.
  * PDFescape publishers to whom you submit PDF forms (only applies to www.pdfescape.com, only completed PDFescape form information, name, and email address will be provided to them).
