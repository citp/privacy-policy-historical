# PDF-Convert - Privacy Policy

## pdf-convert Privacy Policy 

pdf-convert,Inc does not gather any personally identifiable data such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address from users of this web site without previous notification.   
You may choose to join specific mailing lists for preferred information or just send emails when you have questions. 

Your email address will only be used by PDF-Convert to send replies or messages related to the topic of the mailing list you joined. It will not be sold or disclosed to others.

When purchasing our product, additional information is needed to fill your order. Any information provided by you will not be sold or given to anyone, and it will only be used by PDF-Convert for filling your order and notifying you by email of updates.
