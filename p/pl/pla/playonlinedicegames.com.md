# App Policy

## Website: PlayOnlineDiceGames.com

**Cookies**  
PlayOnlineDiceGames.com uses "cookies", which are small text files placed on your hard disk by a web server. Cookies contain text that can be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. That text often consists of a string of numbers and letters that uniquely identifies your computer, but may contain other information as well. 

PlayOnlineDiceGames.com uses the cookies to remember you on the computer once you log in to our site so that when you revisit the site you don't have to login again. The cookies expire automatically after certain interval. 

**Registration**  
Once you register on the site, your login credentials are stored in a database on the server. Other statistics are also only stored in the database server and not locally. You can play on the site without registering and there is no limitation on anything except that we won't be storing your game play statistics on our servers if you are not registered and logged in while playing. 

If you choose to register on the site, your email and username is saved in the database and is never shared with any other company, individual or a party. This information is only used to identify you on this site. 

**Advertisement**  
PlayOnlineDiceGames.com uses third-party vendors including Google for displaying ads. 

  * Third party vendors, including Google Adsense, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. 
  * Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet. 
  * Users may opt out of Google's personalized advertising by Google by visiting [Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads). You can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting [www.aboutads.info](https://web.archive.org/web/20190614090810id_/http%3A//www.playonlinedicegames.com/www.aboutads.info). 
  * Other third-party vendors used on the site for display of ads are Amazon Associates Ads and Propeller Ads.

In order to opt-out of personalized advertising from Amazon visit: <https://www.amazon.com/adprefs>  
In order to opt-out of personalized advertising of Propeller Ads visit: <https://propellerads.com/opt-out/>




Google's policy regarding their use of cookies can be read here: <http://www.google.com/policies/technologies/ads/>

**Google Analytics**  
PlayOnlineDiceGames.com uses Google Analytics which is an analysis service provided by Google Inc. This is mainly used to collect site usage data and prepare reports on user activities and other bahaviours. Google may use the data to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network. <https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy>
