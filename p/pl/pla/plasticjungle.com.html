Collected Information
Your specific information, gathered by PlasticJungle.com, will be kept confidential and will not be shared without your knowledge and permission as permitted by law.
 
  • You do not have to register to visit and view PlasticJungle.com. We may use this information to contact you about the services on our site in which you have expressed an interest and to validate credit card information for future transactions. Cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information about you and cannot be used to identify you personally.
