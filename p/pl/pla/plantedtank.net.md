# The Planted Tank - Advertising

Effective May 5, 2006 

**Our Commitment To Privacy**  
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested. 

**The Information We Collect:** This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the Planted Tank website. The types of personal information collected at these pages are:  
Name  
Email address  
Location  


**The Way We Use Information:** The information we collect from is in no way or shape sold to any other party. All information is kept private available only to yourself and The Planted Tank. On certain areas of the website cookies may be used in order for members to access "Members Only" areas of the website. This cookie in no way tracks your actions for the purpose of targetted advertising. 

**Our Commitment To Data Security** To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. 

**Our Commitment To Children's Privacy:** Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13. 

**How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information** Most submitted information can be edited by the user who submitted it. Any other information that you wish to be deleted or edit can be done so by contacting the webmaster of The Planted Tank. 

**How To Contact Us** Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please contact the webmaster at contact ("at") plantedtank ("dot") net.
