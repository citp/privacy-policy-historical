Policies

Delivery Policy

Usually an order will be dispatched within 2 working days of receipt. However, due to the handmade nature of the resources occasions may arise where it is not possible to immediately dispatch an order. Unless otherwise stated all goods will be supplied within 28 days of an order being placed with an appropriate substitute item included if appropriate. Where items are substituted these will be of equivalent value and quality to the original item. In the event of a substitution not being possible, the customer will be contacted and advised of the likely delivery date. If the goods cannot be provided within a mutually agreed timescale and an acceptable alternative is unavailable, a full refund will be offered. All product images supplied are illustrative and we reserve the right to substitute items if we have trouble or delays in obtaining items from the usual range. The overall balance and value for money will not be compromised.

Returns Policy

If your Play to Z resources do not meet your expectations you may return them undamaged (at your cost) within 7 days of receipt. Provided the goods are unused and undamaged, a full refund will be will be made excluding any postage or courier costs incurred by Play to Z.

Customers in the European Union have the right to withdraw from the purchase of an item within 7 working days of the day after the date the item is delivered. To cancel your purchase within the 7-working-day cooling-off period, please contact us at info@playtoz.co.uk  stating the reason for the return. As soon as we receive notice of your cancellation of this order, if the goods have not yet been dispatched the order will be cancelled and payment returned in full if applicable.

 

If the goods have already been dispatched or received, please package the relevant item(s) securely and return to us within seven working days of receipt. Please note that we cannot accept returns if you deliver them to us by hand unless prior agreement has been given. For your protection, we recommend that you use a recorded-delivery service. For more information on your right to withdraw from your purchase within the seven working day cooling-off period, visit the Department for Business, Enterprise and Regulatory Reform website at: http://www.berr.gov.uk/consumers/buying-selling/distance-selling/index.html 

In the case of any return, the customer will be responsible for the costs of returning the item(s) to us (and original delivery) unless we delivered the item in error or the item is damaged or defective.

Problems

Your goods should reach you in perfect order but if you have any problems Play to Z Ltd must be advised of these in writing within 7 days of receipt of the goods. Every effort will be made to satisfactorily resolve the problem. Where goods have been damaged in transit, problems should be reported in writing within 24 hours so that we can take this up with the courier. Failure to do so may affect our ability to resolve the problem.

How we use cookies

A cookie is a small file which asks permission to be placed on your computer's hard drive. Once you agree, the file is added and the cookie helps analyse web traffic or lets you know when you visit a particular site. Cookies allow web applications to respond to you as an individual. The web application can tailor its operations to your needs, likes and dislikes by gathering and remembering information about your preferences.

We use traffic log cookies to identify which pages are being used. This helps us analyse data about web page traffic and improve our website in order to tailor it to customer needs. We only use this information for statistical analysis purposes and then the data is removed from the system.

Overall, cookies help us provide you with a better website, by enabling us to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not. A cookie in no way gives us access to your computer or any information about you, other than the data you choose to share with us. You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. This may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.

Links to other websites

Our website may contain links to other websites of interest. However, once you have used these links to leave our site, you should note that we do not have any control over that other website. Therefore, we cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which you provide whilst visiting such sites and such sites are not governed by this privacy statement. You should exercise caution and look at the privacy statement applicable to the website in question.

Controlling your personal information
