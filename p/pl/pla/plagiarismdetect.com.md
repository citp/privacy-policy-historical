# Privacy Policy | plagiarismdetect.org

## What information do we collect?

We gather information from you when you sign up for an account at the website, fill out a contact form or apply for a discount. During the sign-up process, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your full name or e-mail address. However, you can visit our site anonymously. 

## How do we use your information?

Your information, collected by us, may be used in one of the following ways:

  1. Website improvement (the information we get from you helps us improve the service performance and ensure the best user experience) 
  2. Client support improvement (the information we get from you helps us to respond more effectively to any requests you may have) 
  3. Transaction processing. Your information, both public or private, will not be sold, exchanged, transferred, or given to any third party for any reason whatsoever, without your consent, other than for the express purpose of delivering the purchased product or service requested. 
  4. Service notifications. The email address you provide when registering may be used to send you information and updates pertaining to your order. Additionally, you may receive occasional company news, updates, related product or service information, etc. 



## What do we do to protect your information? 

We have a set of security measures in place to keep your personal information secured when you enter, submit, or access your personal information. 

All payment procedures are performed by using a secure server. We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to transmit all supplied information, that is then encrypted into our Payment gateway provider's database only to be accessible by those authorized with special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential. 

Our servers do not store your private information, such as credit card, billing info, financials etc., after a transaction.

## Does the website use cookies?

Yes (Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computers hard drive through your Web browser, given you allow this action). Cookies enable the sites or service providers systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information. 

We use cookies to compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future. We may contract with third-party service providers to assist us in better understanding our site visitors. These service providers are not permitted to use the information collected on our behalf except to help us conduct and improve our business. 

## Can any outside parties access your information?

Your personally identifiable information will not be sold, traded, or otherwise transferred to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other purposes. 

## Texts submission

The texts you submit for plagiarism scans are not saved anywhere except in your personal account. Only you can access them. Your texts can be used by the Company for quality assurance purposes only. 

## Can you change your personal information on our website?

Personal information can be changed by you at any time by logging into control panel and going to the 'My Profile' page. 

## Is this Privacy Policy applicable for offline session?

This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through our website and not to information collected offline.
