## Privacy Policy and Cookie Statement

<https://gabrielecirulli.github.io/2048> and [https://play2048.co](https://play2048.co/) are operated and maintained by Gabriele Cirulli, [@iirelu](https://github.com/iirelu) and [@sigod](https://github.com/sigod). This website is hosted on servers operated by GitHub Inc. This document outlines the data handling practices for this website. 

### What personal data do you collect?

For analytics purposes, anonymous and aggregated data may be collected, using Google Analytics. Google AdSense, used on this website for advertsing purposes, may also collect similar data. This data includes:

  * **Technical data:** Your IP address, browser type, operating system type, hardware model (if available), location and locale (language and region), screen dimensions, browser dimensions, etc.
  * **Navigational information:** What pages you access on this website, how long you spend on each page;
  * **Usage information:** Analytics tied to your use of website features, such as when you start, win or lose a game. 



AdSense is used to publish ads on this site. Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) are served non-personalized ads (NPA), which do not track users and are not based on the user's past behavior on this or other websites. The following data may be used in order to serve NPAs:

  * Coarse (such as city-level) geo-targeting based on current location;
  * Content on the current site;



For all other users, personalized ads may be displayed. Personalized ads may use the following information:

  * Sites or apps that have been visited in the past;
  * Past searches and location data;
  * Videos and ads that have been seen in the past;
  * Personal information provided in the past by the user, such as age range, gender, and topics of interest.



For more information on how Google Analytics and Google AdSense use this data see: 

  * <https://privacy.google.com/how-ads-work.html>
  * <https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites>
  * <https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/6004245>
  * <https://support.google.com/adsense/answer/7549925?hl=en>



### Are cookies stored on my device? How are they used?

We use your browser's local storage features to locally store information about the game's state. This lets us save your game at any point and allow you to pick it back up from where you left off. This data is kept in your browser and never sent to servers operated by us or a third-party. No personally-identifiable information is included.
