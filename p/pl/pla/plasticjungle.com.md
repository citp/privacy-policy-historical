# PlasticJungle.com : Privacy Policy

|  |  |   |  |  |   
---  
|  |  |   
---  
Plastic Jungle is strongly committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe online experience for our users. This privacy statement discloses what information we gather, how the information is used, and what steps are taken to safeguard personal information provided to us. We will occasionally update this Privacy Policy as necessary. We encourage you to read the complete Privacy Policy below and contact us if you have any questions.  
   
**Q: DEFINE PAYPAL?**  
When you register, PlasticJungle.com requires a valid Paypal account for transaction purposes. If you do not have an existing Paypal account, you will be required to register for one. It is fast and easy. This is also done for the protection of everyone on the site. We want to uphold an honest and open auction site.  
   
**Collected Information**  
Your specific information, gathered by PlasticJungle.com, will be kept confidential and will not be shared without your knowledge and permission as permitted by law.  
   
  
  * You do not have to register to visit and view PlasticJungle.com. To utilize the buy, sell and trade features on this Web site, you must complete the registration form and create a user name and password. We collect information such as name, mailing address and email address. We may use this information to contact you about the services on our site in which you have expressed an interest and to validate credit card information for future transactions. How can we add here that information is gathered to protect the integrity of business transacted on this site.  
 
  * At times, you may have the option to provide other information such as gender, income level and geographic location. We may use this information to make improvements to our site and enhance your business transaction experience.  
 
  * We also collect certain information automatically. This helps facilitate your online experience at our website. We use cookies, for example, to track usage patterns and to control the display of ads, help track items in your shopping basket and to “recognizing” you to speed up your log-in when you visit our site. Cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information about you and cannot be used to identify you personally.  
 
  * Sometimes we will give you the opportunity to opt in to receive offers from Plastic Jungle or third parties not affiliated with Plastic Jungle.

  
**Links**  
   
  
  * Plastic Jungle may offer links to and/or from other websites. We are not responsible for the content, practices or policies of sites operated by other parties and urge you to inquire about them before providing any personally identifiable information online.
