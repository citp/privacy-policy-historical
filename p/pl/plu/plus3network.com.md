# Plus 3 Network / Plus 3 Privacy Policy

Throughout this Policy, "Plus 3" and "We" refers to The Plus 3 Network, Inc. "You" or "Users" or "Members" refers to you, our Members and users and Guests - everyone who uses, subscribes to, publishes through, joins, or visits the Site. By using the Site, you expressly consent to the information handling practices described in this notice. This Privacy Policy is incorporated into and is subject to the Site's Terms of Use. Your use of the Site and any personal information you provide through the Site are subject at all times to this Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use. If you do NOT accept this Privacy Policy, then please discontinue your use of the Site.

We reserve the right, at our sole discretion, to change, modify, add, or delete portions of this Privacy Policy at any time without further notice. If we do this, we will indicate at the top of this page the date this Privacy Policy was last revised. Your continued use of the Site after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to abide by this Privacy Policy or any future Privacy Policy, do not use or access (or continue to use or access) the Site. It is your responsibility to regularly check the Site to determine if there have been changes to this Privacy Policy and to review such changes.

#### 1\. The Information Plus 3 Collects:

Information You Provide: You may provide to Plus 3 what is generally called "personally identifiable" information (such as your name, email address, credit card number, postal mailing address, and telephone numbers) and other personal information (such as your gender, age, weight, and places you exercise and sports that you play) when you set up your profile or if you upload, purchase, enter contests or challenges, view or download certain content or products from the Site or otherwise use the features and functionality of the Site.

**"Cookies" Information:** When you access the Site, we may send one or more cookies - small text files containing a string of alphanumeric characters - to your computer. Plus 3 may use both session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie disappears after you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser and may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the Site. Persistent cookies can be removed. Please review your web browser "Help" file to learn the best way to modify your cookie settings.

**Your Name:** Your name is displayed throughout the Site and is therefore available to the public. Other people can see your name when they visit your Profile Page, when you come up in the search results, when you post public content, when you post comments, on another member's site, or provide feedback, etc. Therefore, people will be able to personally identify your activities on the Site. 

#### 2\. The Way Plus 3 Uses Information:

Plus 3 uses the information that you provide or that we collect to operate, maintain, enhance, and provide all of the features and services found on the Site as well as to track user-generated content and Members to the extent necessary to comply as a service provider with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act and other applicable laws. If you provide email addresses to invite others to the Site and/or to participate in activities, Plus 3 may store and use the email address for the following purposes only: (a) to generate and send email invitations and additional reminder emails; (b) to allow the recipient of the invitation to sign up for an account if he or she so desires and (c) to track the effectiveness of our invitation program.

Member Profile and Activity Information including but not limited to your first and last name, city state and country, sports, and your Causes(s) and Sponsor(s) (defined below), and any activities you may have added, as well as your photo, are displayed on the Site and will be shared with people both inside and outside your network of friends (and co-workers for Wellbeing Members) based on how you select your Profile and Activity settings. We will use your email address, without further consent, for administrative communications such as notifying you of major Plus 3 Network Site updates, for customer service purposes, to address copyright infringement or defamation issues, or to contact you regarding any content that you have posted to or downloaded from the Site.

### Member Profile Privacy Options

Plus 3 will display the information you have provided on your profile on the Site (your "Profile"). You have the ability to set your profile as follows:

  * Public 
  * Friends
  * Co-Workers and Friends (available only to our Wellbeing Members - see #5 below)
  * Private



When the option is set to **Public** , your profile, as noted below, can be seen by all Plus 3 Members and guests, and can be found on Internet search engines, attached to your activities, in the People section, on leader boards, and in Goals and Results when you've signed up for Challenges and other contests or competitions. 

When the option is set to **Friends** , Plus 3 Members that you have invited and/or accepted as Friends will be able to see your profile. 

When the option is set to **Co-Workers and Friends** Plus 3 Members that are employed at the same company as you, and Plus 3 Members that you have invited or accepted as Friends will be able to see your Profile.

When the option is set to **Private** , Members and Guests cannot see your profile data details. Members can see your name attached to an activity, see your name in the People section, and see your name in the Goals and Rewards sections when you have signed up for Challenges and other contests or competitions.

### Activity Privacy Options

You also have the ability to set an Activity to:

  * Public
  * Friends
  * Co-Workers and Friends (available only to our Wellbeing Members - see #5 below)
  * Private



If the Activity is set to **Public** it can be viewed, with your name attached, by all Plus 3 Members and Guests and found by Internet search engines, and depending on your Profile setting, Members and Guests may be able to see your Profile.

If the Activity is set to **Friends** , Plus 3 Members that you have invited and/or accepted as Friends will be able to see your your activities. 

When the Activity is set to **Co-Workers and Friends** Plus 3 Members that are employed by the same company as you, and Plus 3 Members that you have invited or accepted as Friends will be able to see your Activities. 

If an Activity is set to **Private** , it is not displayed to other Members or Guests, nor on an Internet search engine, and only you can see it.

Activities - Geographic Data: Please be aware that when you post an activity, either one you have uploaded from a data collection device, such as a GPS, or one that you have entered by hand (collectively "Location Data"), Users may see the route you have covered, including your start and end points, which may include your residence or place of work. As a practical thought, consider starting and ending the record of your activities from a "neutral" location. YOU AGREE THAT PLUS 3 DOES NOT BEAR RESPONSIBILITY OF ANY KIND EITHER EXPRESS OR IMPLIED FOR YOUR SAFETY OR FOR THE POTENTIALLY HARMFUL ACTIONS OF OTHERS WHO MAY LEARN OF YOUR ROUTES VIA LOCATION DATA AND THAT PLUS 3 CANNOT CONTROL ACCESS TO PERSONAL INFORMATION THAT YOU HAVE ELECTED TO PUBLISH.

Activities - Physical Information: when you post an activity that has used a data collection device that includes information such as heart rate, cadence, watts, etc, realize that other people may see this information.

Plus 3 uses all of the information that we collect to allow you to earn rewards, raise funds for charitable causes, contribute activities to the Plus 3 Community, understand the usage trends and preferences of our Members and improve the way the Site works and looks, and to create new features and functionality.

Plus 3 may use "Automatically Collected" information and "Cookies" information to: (a) automatically update the Plus 3 application on your system; (b) remember your information so that you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or the next time you access the Site; (c) provide customized third-party advertisements, content and information; (d) monitor the effectiveness of third-party marketing campaigns; (e) monitor aggregate site usage metrics such as total number of visitors and pages accessed; and (d) track your entries, submissions, and status in any promotions or other activities.

Plus 3 may use the information that you provide to us, as well as information about your visits to the Site, to deliver advertisements or services that Plus 3 or its partners believe to be relevant to you.

#### 8\. Our Commitment to Children's Privacy:

IF YOU ARE UNDER 13 YEARS OF AGE, THEN PLEASE DO NOT USE OR ACCESS THE SITE AT ANY TIME OR IN ANY MANNER.

Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important. For that reason, Plus 3 does not knowingly collect or maintain personally identifiable information from persons under 13 years-of-age. If Plus 3 learns that personally-identifiable information of persons less than 13-years-of-age has been collected on or through the Site, then Plus 3 will take the appropriate steps to delete this information.

If you are the parent or legal guardian of a child under 13 who has become Plus 3 Network Site member, then please contact Plus 3 at support@Plus3Network.com to have that child's account terminated and information deleted.

We recommend that minors over the age of 13 ask their parents for permission before sending any information about themselves to anyone over the Internet.
