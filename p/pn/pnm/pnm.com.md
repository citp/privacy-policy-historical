# PNM | About PNM | Privacy Policy

|  | 

#### 

Except as required by law we will not, without your prior consent, disclose to any third party the personal information -- such as name, address, phone number, account information, and email address -- you provide to us or that we gather about you online. In no case will we sell or rent that information. We may, however, use that information internally to help us better tailor our website to your interests and needs.
