# Privacy Policy | Pulsar Media

**Last Updated July 2012**

Pulsar Media has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site:

**IP Address:**

In order to increase security we log your ip address during a login attempt to our project management system. IP address are removed from our database after 24 hours and are not used for any other purposes other than login attempts. We do not share your IP address with third parties, affiliates or any other organizations or corporations.

**Form Submissions:**

All inquiries made through our inquiry form, are kept strictly confidential and are not shared with any third parties or affiliates. Information collected from our inquiry form is used for internal purposes only.

To process your order, we require your name, email address and a verfied Paypal Transaction ID; this information is automatically provided to Pulsar Media by Paypal after a successful transaction has occurred. We use this information to process your order and, if any questions should arise, to contact you about your order. In general, we will try to contact you first by email and only if that fails, or if time is critical, will we try to contact you by phone. All information collected is held strictly confidential by Pulsar Media and is only shared with third parties such as Paypal and/or provincial authorities to assist in fraud investigation.
