Purlsoho.com can be visited without giving any personal information. If you wish to access the purlsoho service, you will need to enter personal information. You are solely responsible for any authorized or unauthorized access to your account by any person. You agree to bear all responsibility for the confidentially of your password and all use or charges incurred from accessing the purlsoho service with your password. You agree to keep current all account information, including address, payment information, telephone number and e-mail address. 

Corrections

If you need to make corrections to any information given to purlsoho.com, please e-mail the corrections to customerservice@purlsoho.com or you can update information by going to your account on the site.

Cookies

Like many web sites, ours uses "cookies" to make your shopping experience faster. A cookie is a piece of data stored on your computer's hard drive that allows purlsoho.com to identify you. Cookies cannot be used to access sensitive account information. You will always have to log in with a user name and password to place an order. Please note that if you disable cookies on your browser, certain features of the site may be disabled.

Every possible effort is made to ensure that your credit card information remains confidential. Purlsoho.com uses secure socket layer (ssl) encryption technology which encrypts your credit card number prior to transmission over the internet. Once we receive your order, it is only available to personnel who will provide you with the purlsoho service.
