Privacy Policy

Our companywide commitment to your privacy

Ola Beijing is very concerned about protecting the privacy of our valued clients. To make sure your personal information is secure, we communicate these guidelines to our employees and strictly enforce privacy safeguards within the company. Only senior members of our staff have access to client information, and at no time does specific client information shared with third parties.

What information we collect and how we may use it

You will be required to provide personal information when placing an order, contacting us for information or registering to become a member. The types of personal information we collect are: Name, Email Address, Phone Number, Mailing Address, etc. We use the information you provide about yourself to complete the order and contact you with the service you request. We never share this information with outside parties. The billing information like your credit card number or bank information is submitted to PayPal through the secured payment interface, they do not give the information to us.

How we protect your personal information

We take your privacy very seriously. To keep your personal information secure with Ola Beijing, we have put in place appropriate administrative, technical and physical precautions to safeguard your personal information against loss, theft, and misuse, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

How we ensure the integrity of your personal information

As stated above, we have safeguards in place to keep your personal information accurate, complete, and up to date for the purposes for which it is used. You have the right to access and correct the personal information you have provided by sending us a request or updating your personal profile.

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy please feel free to contact us.
