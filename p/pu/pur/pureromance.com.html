Privacy Statement

PureRomance.com collects client information to enhance your shopping experience. This privacy policy is intended to assist you in understanding what information we gather about you when you visit this Web site, how we use that information, and the safeguards we have in place for that information.

This online privacy statement applies to Pure Romance Parties, Inc. and covers the U.S. corporate-operated Pure Romance website (www.PureRomance.com). Pure Romance Consultants may also operate their own websites. Each Consultant is an independent contractor having the right to operate their own Pure Romance business. In this regard, Consultant online privacy may vary.

Information We Collect Online

General Browsing Information
PureRomance.com gathers navigational information during each visitor's use of our Web site. In the process of gathering navigational information, non-personally identifiable information (i.e. domain type, browser version, service provider and IP address) may be collected. The navigational information tells PureRomance.com about the use of our Web site by visitors, including the areas of our site that are most visited or the time of a last visit to a page on our site. It also tells us about the technical efficiencies of our Web site and services (i.e. the time it takes a visitor to connect to our Web site or download pages). The navigational information helps us better understand a visitor's experience at PureRomance.com and improve upon that experience by recognizing and delivering more of the features, services and products our visitors prefer.

Personal Information
PureRomance.com may ask you to provide personal information when you establish or update a PureRomance.com account, sign up for Pure Romance E-mail, enter a contest, participate in a survey, shop online, check the status of an order, request a catalogue, purchase and send an e-gift card and/or create or send a wish list or gift reminder.

Categories of information collected include name, address, e-mail address, recipient's name and e-mail address, order information, telephone number, credit card information and messages or reminders you create.

Categories of information collected when you sign up include name, mailing address, and e-mail address.

Using Personal Information We Collect Online

PureRomance.com uses the personal information we collect from you to deliver products that you have ordered, to fulfill requests that you have created, to contact you or the product recipient(s) in the event of order or delivery difficulties, for verification purposes, to respond to your inquiries, and to deliver services and information about our products or Web site, including adding you to our print catalogue mailing list.

We will use the information you provide when you visit us to sign up for Pure Romance E-mail to send you updates about Pure Romance products, promotions and events. In addition, we will use your name and mailing address provided during such sign up, in all the ways described in this privacy statement and we will use your e-mail address as described in this privacy statement for e-mail addresses collected after August 25, 2006.

Your information will not be sold, bartered or shared with anyone at any time by Pure Romance in any way that has not been specified above or without your permission, unless we are required to do so by law.

Cookies

PureRomance.com uses a browser feature known as a cookie. A cookie is a small data file that is stored by your Web browser on your computer. Cookies are used to help track your clicks and pass information as you go through the pages within PureRomance.com. This allows PureRomance.com to make our site more responsive to your needs by delivering a better and more personalized experience to you. PureRomance.com also uses cookies to keep track of how many items you put into your shopping bag and to remember you when you return to PureRomance.com.

Privacy Of Children On Our Website

To ensure compliance with federal law, PureRomance.com does not knowingly collect or maintain information provided by children under the age of 18.

Links To Other Web Sites And Services

For your convenience, our Web site may contain links to other sites, especially sites operated by independent Pure Romance Consulatants. Pure Romance is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. We encourage you to review the separate privacy policies of each site you visit.

Updates To Privacy Statement

Please review the Privacy Statement for any material changes made within the last thirty days with respect to categories of information collected at PureRomance.com or material changes in how we use information collected at PureRomance.com (whether a deletion or addition). If you are concerned about how your personal information is used, please visit our site often for this and other important announcements about PureRomance.com.

Consent

Pure Romance respects the online privacy of all visitors to our website. By using the website, you consent to the collector and user of this information by Pure Romance in a manner consistent with the guidelines stated in this policy.

Questions Or Concerns

If you have any questions or concerns with respece to our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us.

Security Statement

PureRomance.com takes the following types of security measures: use of technologies and policies such as limited access data-centers, firewall technology, secure socket layer certificate authentication and limitations of administrative access to our systems. In addition, it is our policy not to send your credit card number via e-mail. In fact, this is a practice we encourage you to adopt in all of your Internet activities.

We designed PureRomance.com to accept orders only from Web browsers that permit communication through Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology; for example, 4.0 versions or higher of Netscape Navigator, versions 5.01 or higher of Internet Explorer, versions 0.8 or higher of Firefox and versions 1.2 or higher of Safari.

While we implement the above security measures on this site, you should be aware that 100% security is not always possible.
