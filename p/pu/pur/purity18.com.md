# Purity18.com Privacy Statement

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. 

**The Information We Collect** : Name, Address and Email Address

**The Way We Use Information** : 

In order to combat fraud, your name, address, and credit card information is collected to be verified with your credit card billing company.

We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, lease or otherwise transfer personal information to any third party for any purpose at any time unless you provide you prior permission, with exception if we are forced to disclose such information by law.

**Our Commitment To Data Security** : To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, or send us an email at support@purity18.com .
