Privacy Policy for services offered by Public Technology Limited


Introduction.
 We take your right to privacy under the Data Protection Act 1998 seriously and want you to feel comfortable using our web site. This Privacy Policy deals with personally identifiable information (referred to as "Data" below) that may be collected by services offered by Public Technology Limited (“PT”) including the following web sites (and contributor and news alerts) from such sites: PublicTechnology.net, PublicTechnology.co.uk, PublicPolitics.net, PublicPages.net, Nesdsconsultation.org, EducationPages.net, e-governmentawards.org.uk (the“Sites”). This Policy does not apply to other entities that we do not own or control or persons that are not our employees, agents or within our control. Please take time to read our Terms of Use.

1. Collection of Data.
 From time to time PT may ask for and/or you may choose to submit personally identifiable information to any of the websites referred to above. By providing us with this information you are consenting to its use. Our contributor, email news alert or other registration process requires only a valid e-mail address and a unique user ID and password. Providing us with other information is at your option. Therefore depending on the information you submit the personally identifiable information we may collect could include your name, address, email address, phone number, other contact details and other submissions you make. Granting contributor log-in and email news subscription is at the discretion of PT. Please take note that your user name, e-mail address, or other submissions that you make on these Sites may contain your real name or other personally identifiable information and, as a result, may appear on these Sites. Like many web sites, we may also automatically receive general information that is contained in our server log files such as your IP address and cookie information. Information about how advertising may be served on this site (if at all) is set forth below.

2. Use of Data.
 We may use your Data to improve the operation, development and administration of the Sites and to customize and improve your user experience on this site. We will make efforts so that your Data will not be provided to third parties unless (i) provided for otherwise in this Privacy Policy; (ii) we obtain your consent, such as when you choose to opt-in or opt-out to the sharing of Data; (iii) a service provided on our site requires the interaction with or is provided by a third party, by way of example an Application Service Provider; (iv) pursuant to legal process or law enforcement; (v) we find that your use of this site violates our this Policy, Terms of Service, other usage guidelines or as deemed reasonably necessary by us to protect our legal rights and/or property; or (vi) PT (or its services or websites or other ventures) is purchased by/acquired by/merges with / becomes a subsidiary of a third party whereby that third party (whether based in the UK, EU or elsewhere) may be granted a licence to use and/or may become owner of the Data in the same manner as we can as set forth in this Policy. In the event you choose to use links that appear on our site to visit other web sites, you are advised to read the privacy policies that appear on those sites.

3. Marketing Communications
 If you have used the Sites before it is possible that PT have your personally identifiable information already and is using this to contact you about offers, events, items or related activities that you may find useful. If you are an existing customer PT will continue to keep you informed unless you tell us not to.
If you are a new user PT will give you the choice (for example once you have subscribed or signed up) whether or not you want us or other reputable companies to contact you about future offers, events and new services or related activities that you may find useful. PT will not bombard you with emails, but PT would like to keep you informed of the developments on the Sites.
PT will not send you any marketing emails or pass your information on to third parties except as provided above unless you give your consent or you have given it already.
Where requested please therefore check the relevant box marked:
> “I would like to receive information from Public Technology Limited in future” if you wish to receive future information from the Sites;
> “I would like to receive information from interesting third parties” if you want us to pass the information on to third parties.

 Like many web sites, we set and use cookies to enhance your user experience, such as retaining your personal settings which you may not be aware of. Advertisements may appear on our web site and, if so, may set and access cookies on your computer and is subject to the privacy policy of those parties providing the advertisement. However, the companies serving the advertising do not have access to our cookies. 5. Children.
 We do not allow persons who are ages thirteen or younger to become members of this site. For more information, please contact our webmaster.

6. Editing or Deleting Your Account Information.
 We provide you with the ability to edit the information that you provided to us when registering on the Sites by using your personal page configuration area. While we endeavour to ensure that the information we hold about you is accurate and, where necessary, kept up to date, we shall assume that in the absence of evidence to the contrary, that the information you provide us with is accurate. You may request deletion of your user account by contacting our webmaster. Content or other data that you may have provided to us and that is not contained within your user account, such as posts that may appear within our forums, may continue to remain on our site at our discretion even though your user account is deleted. Please see our Terms of Use for more information.
You are entitled to be informed of the personally identifiable information PT holds about you. PT is happy to comply with such a request once it has satisfied itself as to your identity. However, to protect the privacy of others PT cannot comply with such a request if it would result in the disclosure of information about another person unless it would be reasonable to do so or that person has consented to the disclosure. It may be necessary to charge and administrative fee to provide this information, but the fee shall not exceed £10 and the information shall be provided within 40 days of your request.
