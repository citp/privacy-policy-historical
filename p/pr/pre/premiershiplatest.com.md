# Privacy Policy | Premiership Latest Football News

Members and visitors to this website are protected by the terms set into this policy, which guarantees that their privacy will never be violated. PremiershipLatest.com fully complies with the UK Government's 1998 Data Protection Act and has a keen interest on protecting its members and visitors from prying eyes and malicious advertisers.

Premiershiplatest.com collects **non-personally identifiable information** , resulted from the member's usage of this website.

Certain personally identifiable information, like member IP addresses and name/surname are collected and used to secure our website from malicious users. We will always legally pursue those who try to gain access to our member's information through illegal means.

Our company will never send any of this information to any third party and under any circumstances.

Website usage is being tracked in order to improve the network and your experience of our site.
