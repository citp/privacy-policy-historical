    4. Information collected through cookies and third party pixels

      5. Besides the data which is collected by Intela via the Website, via surveys and via third parties, Intela uses cookies and so-called third-party pixels to collect data about you.

      A cookie is a small text file placed on your hard disk by the server of the Website. This allows the server to save your preferences and other data including passwords. Furthermore, Intela uses cookies to track the visits to the Website and to track and aggregate statistical data regarding site visits to gather information, for example for reporting purposes for its internal organization or for third parties. You can decide whether to accept or refuse cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies. However, if you use your browser settings to block all cookies (including essential cookies) you may not be able to access all or parts of the Website.

      Intela uses the following cookies:

      1. Strictly necessary cookies. These are cookies that are required for the operation of the Website. They include, for example, cookies that enable you to log into secure areas of our Website, use a shopping cart or make use of other services.
      2. Analytical/performance cookies. They allow Intela to recognizes and count the number of visitors and to see how visitors move around the Website when they are using it. This helps Intela to improve the way the Website works, for example, by ensuring that users are finding what they are looking for easily.
      3. Functionality cookies. These are used to recognise you when you return to the Website. This enables Intela to personalize content for you and remember your preferences (for example, your choice of language or region).
      4. Targeting cookies. These cookies record your visit to the Website, the pages you have visited and the links you have followed. Intela will use this information to make the Website and the advertising displayed on it more relevant to your interests. Intela may also share this information with third parties for this purpose.
