PrivateSchoolExam.com Privacy Policy

Introduction


PrivateSchoolExam.com is committed to protecting your privacy. We maintain the privacy of your information using security technologies and adhere to policies that prevent unauthorized use of your personal information.

In general, you can visit PrivateSchoolExam.com without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. However, you will not be able to access information that requires registration, and there are services that we will be unable to provide to you. In particular, many courses at PrivateSchoolExam.com are available only to students who have registered with us.


 
Registration Information


Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (e.g., names, email addresses, country, telephone number). Your contact information from the registration form may be used to confirm your registration, deliver e-mail notifications to you, and help us to contact you when necessary. Additional, optional information such as mailing address and company affiliation, may be provided by you. This additional information will allow to better server you.

In any this case, your personal information is available to you for updating using the user code and password that will be provided to you. PrivateSchoolExam.com allows users to correct, update, or remove the personal data they provide to us. Accessing this information requires the user to enter his personal user code and password.

Any information received will be used only for providing services to you at PrivateSchoolExam.com. We do not sell our customer names, addresses, or other information to third parties for any purpose.


 
IP address


We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address may also be used to customize the information that you receive to make it most relevant to you. In addition, we use the IP address to track the domains from which people visit us and analyze this data for trends and statistics, but the individual user remains anonymous. We may also use IP addresses to track fraudulent use of our services. 


 
Links to other sites


This site contains links to other sites. PrivateSchoolExam.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.


 
Cookies


PrivateSchoolExam.com uses a feature of your Internet browser called a "cookie" to assign a unique identification to your computer. Cookies are files that your Web browser places on your computer's hard drive. PrivateSchoolExam.com uses cookies to provide assistance during login processing. Using cookies, your browser can "remember" your user name and password, so that you will not be required to enter the information every time that you log in. If you check the "Remember this password" box when prompted by the browser, and you won't have to type in the information each time you come back. If you are concerned that other people might accidentally use your account, do not check the box. Also, if you use a shared computer (in a university, library, Internet cafe, or other common area) DO NOT check the checkbox.

You may set your browser so that it does not accept cookies. If you choose to do so, you may still browse our Site. However, you will not be able to login, register for courses or attend an online course with us.

 


Public sites


This site may make chat rooms, forums, and message boards available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and that you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. PrivateSchoolExam.com reserves the right to remove any posting that its editors consider inappropriate.


 
Secured Data


This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information stored therein. When you access you personal information, we use industry standard Secure Socket Layers (SSL) to encrypt all of your personal information. If you choose to purchase a course online, you will be asked to provide us with credit card information that is transmitted through Secure Socket Layer technology. Credit card information that you provide to us is sent to the credit card company for payment using an encrypted interface and is never stored on this site.
