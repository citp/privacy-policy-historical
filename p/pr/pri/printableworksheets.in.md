# Privacy Policy - Free Printable Worksheets

##  [ Privacy Policy](http://www.printableworksheets.in/index.php/2-uncategorised/43-privacy-policy)

## Personally-Identifiable Information

printableworksheets.in does not collect any personally-identifiable information about you — such as your name and email address — unless you provide it voluntarily, e.g. when you contact the author of printableworksheets.in. printableworksheets.in does not sell or disclose this information to third parties. 

## Information Collected Automatically

printableworksheets.in may automatically collect non-personally-identifiable information about your use of the site, such as the date and time you access this site, the Internet address of the website from which you linked directly to this site, or the IP address from which you accessed this site. This information is not linked to personally-identifiable information. printableworksheets.in may aggregate this information to analyse the usage of the site. 

## Third-Party Companies’ Advertising

printableworksheets.in website uses third-party companies’ advertising provided by Google Inc. to serve ads when you visit this website. These companies may be placing and reading cookies on your browser, which allows them to use information (not including your private information like your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. 

If you would like to find more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, visit [](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)[Atlanta web design](http://webdisart.com/index.html). 

You can find additional information in Appendix A of the [NAI Self-Regulatory principles for publishers (PDF)](http://www.networkadvertising.org/pdfs/NAI_principles.pdf). Please note that the NAI may change this sample language at any time. 

## Your Acceptance of these Terms and Changes

By using this site, you signify your assent to the printableworksheets.in Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this Policy, please do not use the site. 

This Privacy Policy may be changed at any time without notice. Your continued use of the site following the posting of changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes.
