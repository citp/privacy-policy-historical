**1\. Collection of Information**  
**1.1 Personal Data Collection**  
Only User's email address is collected when users make registration to www.prizeDB.com. In order to use certain portions of the services, however, you may be asked for, or you may choose to provide, certain personal information such as name, phone number, address and similar information. We will not collect your personal information while you use the services unless you provide us or authorize us to collect such information voluntarily. 

Referral emails that you initiate from the "Tell-A-Friend" page of your prizeDB account will be sent by TAF Master which is a 3rd party solution provider for tell-a-friend forms on your behalf and will display your email address and that of TAF Master's. By opting to use this form, you are consenting to TAF Masters terms. If a referral signs up for a prizeDB account, he/she will be subject to the same standards of our Privacy Policy and our prizeDB users. 

  


**1.2 Survey Information**  
prizeDB.com collects information form individuals by various methods, including, but not limited to, when an individual voluntarily completes a survey, order form, or a registration page either online or offline, or by means of online or offline surveys, order forms, or registration page by Third Parties (collectively, a "Survey"). (As used herein, &quality;online" means using the Internet, including the Websites, and related technologies, and "offline" means by methods other than online, including in person, in the postal mail, using telephones and cell phones, and other similar means.) In the surveys, prizeDB.com or a Third Party (a "Third Party") may ask an individual to provide various information to priceDB.com, which may include his/her name, email address, residential address, postal code, telephone numbers, (including cell phone numbers and carries), birth date, gender, salary range, education and marital status, occupation, employment information and personal and online interests and such other information as may be requested from time to time (together, "Survey Information"). prizeDB.com may also collect information concerning an individual form another source and uses that information in combination with information provided from this web site. Completing the Surveys is completely voluntary, and individuals are under no obligation to provide Survey Information to prizeDB.com or Third Party, but an individual may receive incentives from prizeDB.com or a Third Party in exchange for providing Survey Information to prizeDB.com. 

In order to enhance the service we provide, and to expedite the offer process, we may pre-populate data fields on a third party partner's survey or offer form with the information that you provided to the web site. We may also use certain third party partners to host the survey or offer form and transfer the completed survey or offer form to other third party partners. Third party partners are contractually prohibited from using any information in a pre-populated data field until they have posted a link to the privacy policy of the third party partners/advertiser listed in the applicable offer and you have consented to the transfer of such personal information by consenting to the offer or submitting to the survey. Likewise, third party partners that serve as the host of the survey or offer form are contractually prohibited from using or retaining your personal information for any purpose other than the compilation and transfer of such personal information to our third party partners/advertiser. Once your personal information is transferred to a third party partners your personal information is subject to that partner's privacy policy and prizeDB.com is no longer responsible for use of your personal information by such business partner.

**1.5 Outside Information**  
prizeDB.com may receive information about individuals from Third Parties or from other sources of information outside of prizeDB.com including information located in public databases ("Outside Information").

**1.6 Individual Information**  
As used herein, Individual Information means Survey Information, Third Party List Information, Other Information, Technology Information, and Outside Information, and any other information prizeDB.com gathers or receives about individuals.

**1.7 No Information Collected from Children**  
prizeDB.com will never knowingly collect any personal information about children under the age of 13. If prizeDB.com obtains actual knowledge that it has collected personal information about a child under the age of 13, that information will be immediately deleted from our database. Because it does not collect such information, prizeDB.com has no such information to use or to disclose to Third Parties. prizeDB.com has designed this policy in order to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act ("COPPA"). 

**1.8 Credit Card Information**  
prizeDB.com may in certain cases collect credit card numbers and related information, such as the expiration date of the card ("Credit Card Information") when an individual places an order from prizeDB.com. When the Credit Card Information is submitted to prizeDB.com, such information is encrypted and is protected with SSL encryption software. prizeDB.com will use the Credit Card Information for purposes of processing and completing the purchase transaction, and the Credit Card Information will be disclosed to Third Parties only as necessary to complete the purchase transaction. 

**1.9 Links to External Websites**  
prizeDB.com's emails and websites contain links to external websites. You will be taken to external websites if you click on these links. Your information may be passed from prizeDB.com's database to the external website, and/or external database, for your convenience (e.g. your information may be used to pre-fill a form at a destination website). prizeDB.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of any external website owner. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any website they visit. 

**2\. Use of Individual Information**

**2.1 Discretion to Use Information**  
THE COMPANY MAY USE INDIVIDUAL INFORMATION FOR ANY LEGALLY PERMISSIBLE PURPOSE IN COMPANY'S SOLE DISCRETION. 

The following paragraphs in Section 2 describe how prizeDB.com currently uses Individual Information, but prizeDB.com may change or broaden its use at any time. As noted below, prizeDB.com may update this policy from time to time. prizeDB.com may use Individual Information to provide promotional offers to individuals by means of email advertising, telephone marketing, direct mail marketing, online banner advertising, and package stuffers, among other possible uses. 

  


**2.2 Email and Unsubscribe Policy**  
prizeDB.com uses Individual Information to provide promotional offers by email to individuals. prizeDB.com may maintain separate email lists for different purposes. If email recipients wish to end their email subscription from a particular list, they need to follow the instructions at the end of each email message to unsubscribe from the particular list. 

prizeDB.com's unsubscribe process impacts only the future delivery of electronic mailings disseminated by prizeDB.com on its own behalf. You may still receive electronic mailings sent on behalf of Third Parties and your personal information may still be shared with Third Parties for use in offline marketing and data appends, including email appends. 

You should also note that unsubscribing from prizeDB.com's electronic mailings will not automatically unsubscribe the subscriber's information from any Third Party associates and licensees of the data. Since Third Party associates and licensee partners maintain separate databases from prizeDB.com, and you will need to unsubscribe from each source individually, if desired. This allows you the freedom to pick and choose which subscriptions to maintain and which to discontinue. 

**2.3 Content of Email Messages**  
In certain commercial email messages sent by prizeDB.com, an Advertiser's name will appear in the "From:" line but hitting the "Reply" button will cause a reply email to be sent to prizeDB.com. The "Subject:" line of prizeDB.com email messages will usually contain a line provided from the Advertiser to prizeDB.com. 

**2.4 Solicited Email**  
prizeDB.com only sends email to individuals who, by registration as a member to one of it's websites, have agreed to receive email and email solicitations from prizeDB.com, one of prizeDB.com's websites or who have agreed on Third Party websites to receive email from prizeDB.com, it's agents, affiliates, subsidiaries or from third parties. As such, prizeDB.com does not send unsolicited email messages and certain statutes requiring certain formatting for unsolicited email are not applicable to prizeDB.com' email messages. 

**2.5 Targeted Advertising**  
prizeDB.com uses Individual Information to target advertising to an individual. When an individual is using the Internet, prizeDB.com uses Technology Information (see also Section 2.7 below) to associate an individual with that person's Individual Information, and prizeDB.com attempts to show advertising for products and services in which the person has expressed an interest in the Surveys, indicated an interest by means of Technology Information, and otherwise. prizeDB.com may, at its discretion, target advertising by using email, direct mail, telephones, cell phones, and other means of communication to provide promotional offers. 

**2.6 Direct Mail and Telemarketing**  
prizeDB.com uses Individual Information to advertise, directly or indirectly, to individuals using direct mail marketing or telemarketing using telephones and cell phones. 

**2.7 Use of Technology Information**  
prizeDB.com uses Technology Information (1) to match a person's Survey Information and Third Party List Information to other categories of Individual Information to make and improve profiles of individuals, (2) to track a person's online browsing habits on the Internet, (3) to determine which areas of prizeDB.com's websites are most frequently visited. This information helps prizeDB.com to better understand the online habits of individuals so that prizeDB.com can target advertising and promotions to them. 

**2.8 Profiles of Individuals**  
prizeDB.com uses Individual Information to make a profile of an individual. A profile can be created by combining Survey Information and Third Party List Information with other sources of Individual Information such as information obtained from public databases. 

**2.9 Storage of Individual Information**  
prizeDB.com stores the Individual Information in a database on leased computers. Our computers have security measures (such as a firewall) in place to protect against the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under prizeDB.com's control. Not withstanding such measures, prizeDB.com cannot guarantee that its security measures will prevent leased computers from being illegally accessed, and the Individual Information on them stolen or altered.
