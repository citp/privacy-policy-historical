FMNA use such Personal Information collected through this Site for the purposes of:

FMNA may share such information, in the aggregate form only, with our affiliates, partners or advertisers, or other third parties.

We use "cookies" to identify you when you visit this Site and to build up a demographic profile. Find out more about the use of cookies on http://www.cookiecentral.com.

Any Personal Information you choose to provide to FMNA with your submissions posted to this Site may appear on the Site, but only to identify you as the author of the submission, unless you expressly authorise us to make further use of the information on the Site.  If you voluntarily disclose Personal Information on any bulletin boards or in chat areas that may be part of this Site, that information can also be collected and used by others and may result in unsolicited messages from other people.

 

WE STRONGLY ENCOURAGE YOU NOT TO DISCLOSE ANY PERSONAL INFORMATION ABOUT YOURSELF IN ANY SUBMISSIONS TO THE SITE.

 

Please review the Terms of Use to learn more about our policies regarding user submissions.

FMNA encourage parents and legal guardians to spend time with their age-appropriate children using the Site. We do not require a child to disclose more information than is reasonably necessary to participate in any activities the Site may offer.  At various places on the Site, we may ask users for their age.  By using or logging into the Site or related features or services, you agree to respond truthfully and accurately about your age.

Do not post your real name on the Site or in any other feature of the Site, and never tell anyone online anything private about yourself or your family, including your phone number and address.  Use your username at all times.  If you win a sweepstakes that has a prize, your parent will have to mail or fax us a signed note at the address or fax number listed in the rules for the specific contest that says it was OK for you to enter, and tells us they will accept the prize for you.  Some areas of the Site may have special rules for children under the age of 18.  When you are in those areas, you must follow those rules.

 

Parents and legal guardians may request to review and/or removal of their child's Personal Data at any time by emailing privacy.officer@fremantlemedia.com, which must include the same screen name, password, and e-mail address that their child submitted.  To protect the child's privacy and security, we will take reasonable steps to verify the parent's or legal guardian's identity before granting him or her access to the child's Personal Data.
