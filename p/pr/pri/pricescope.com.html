Privacy Policy

Pricescope Inc. ("the Company") maintains a strict privacy policy and uses state of the art technologies to safeguard personal information and communications from unauthorized intrusions. The Company recognizes that the growth of on-line services has created additional privacy concerns, particularly for internet users. On-line privacy concerns focus primarily on the protection of "user identifiable" information which an individual reasonably expects to be kept private. As the term suggests, "user identifiable" information is information that, when associated with an individual identifies that user, for example, a user's name, address, telephone number, and e-mail address. The Company has put in place the following Privacy Policy. This policy covers personal information collected using the Company's services available under the domain and subdomains of www.pricescope.com (the "Site") This policy was last updated on February 10, 2005.

Collection and Use

You may read most of the Site without giving the Company any personal information. Some pages of the Site, such as the discussion forums section, require you to register by submitting personal information using an online form. We may use this information to contact you from time -to-time. Unless required to by law, the Company does not share any of the personal information you submit to the Company without your consent.
 
If you use the Multi-Vendor Diamond Quotation System we will share, and you consent to the sharing of, the information that you submit, using the on-line form, with third-party vendors who might be interested in communicating a quotation by email to you. These third-party vendors may have different practices and policies than the Company with respect to their use of your personal information.
 
The Company receives anonymous usage data from the server that hosts the Site. This anonymous usage data includes, a record of the user's Internet Protocol address, the user's browser program used to access the Site, and the name of the user's internet service provider. It does not contain any personal information about the user.
 
When you submit your personal information using an online form you consent to the Company placing small files called a "cookies" on your computer. These files identify your browser and saves information such as passwords so that the Site can recognize you. You can set your browser to disable cookies, but some of the Site's pages may not work properly if you do this.
 
The Company uses its cookies for a number of purposes, including to:
  1. Access your information when you "sign in," so that we can provide you with customized content,
  2. Keep track of preferences you specify while you are using the Site's services — for example, when a visitor indicates a preference for a page on the Site to display in a certain manner;
  3. Estimate and report our total audience size and traffic;
  4. Conduct research to improve the Site's content and services;
  5. Track the pages that you visit within the Site;
  6. Require you to re-enter your password after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents.
The Company does not allow other company's or other websites to place cookies on your computer.
 

Improper Conduct

The Company may also use personal information you submit to investigate and help prevent potentially unlawful activity or activity that threatens the network or otherwise violates the Terms of Use. You consent to such use.
 

Account Information

The Company honours requests from users to review all personal information maintained in reasonably retrievable form, which currently consists of the information you provided to us on one of our on-line forms and will correct any such information which may be inaccurate. Customers may verify that appropriate corrections have been made.

User Concerns

If you are a user with concerns about the Company's on-line Privacy Policy or its implementation you may contact us at http://www.pricescope.com/contact.asp.
 

Changes in Practices

The Company will keep this Privacy Policy current. The Company will inform you of any changes that we make by posting those changes at : http://www.pricescope.com/privacy.asp
 

Additional Privacy Protections for Children Using the Internet

The Company joins the industry in recognizing that children, including young teens, may not be able to make informed choices about personal information requested on-line. Accordingly, Company does not target children or teenagers (younger than eighteen years of age) for collection of information on-line. The Company does not solicit or collect customer identifiable information targeted at children and teenagers under eighteen and does not allow anyone else to do so on the Site.
