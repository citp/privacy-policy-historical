# PriceSmart | Privacy Statement

PriceSmart believes it is very important to protect the privacy of its members. The following information concerning the gathering and use of data about our members is being provided to enable you to make knowledgeable choices about whether to provide information to us and to give you the opportunity to direct us to limit the manner in which your data is used. 

**_Security:_**  
If you use this web site to enter personal data to PriceSmart your transmission will be protected by Secure Socket Layer (SSL). SSL is a technology employed by PriceSmart that encrypts your information during transmission.

It is our policy to take reasonable steps to ensure that your personal data is securely maintained with access limited to those who need such access to serve you. However, as discussed below, we may share your personal identifiable information with unaffiliated companies and joint ventures where we are legally permitted to do so, subject to your rights described under Options to Limit Use of Data. It is a serious violation of PriceSmart policy for any employee to use your personal data for any reason not described in this Privacy Statement. 

**_Member Data Collection & Use: _**  
As a membership based discount retailer, PriceSmart gathers and maintains data about its customers including data supplied on its web site. Personal data may also be available from shopping point of sale (POS) transactional records. The types of personal information collected include names, addresses, employers, telephone numbers, fax numbers, email addresses, date of birth, PriceSmart membership number and your shopping history. 

As a general rule, PriceSmart will not disclose any of your personally identifiable information except when we have your permission or under special circumstances as described below. The following describes some of the ways that your information may be disclosed. 

**_Business Associates & Sponsors: _**  
PriceSmart may disclose your personal information to business partners or sponsors..Some or all data collected by PriceSmart may be shared with a sponsor. 

Many promotions require a member to request additional information from sponsors. By supplying more information, you give PriceSmart permission to transfer your personal information to the sponsor so they can fulfill your request. In many instances, only your email address will be shared. 

You also have choices with respect to cookies. By modifying your browser preferences, you have the choice to accept all cookies, to be notified when a cookie is set, or to reject all cookies. If you choose to reject all cookies you will be unable to use those PriceSmart services (e.g. PriceSmart-Delta Three web telephony servicer) that require registration in order to participate. 

**_Third Party Data & Data in the Aggregate:_**   
Under confidentiality agreements between PriceSmart and third parties, PriceSmart may match user information with third party data. Also, PriceSmart may disclose aggregated member statistics (e.g. percent female shoppers; percent online users) in order to describe our business or services to prospective partners, advertisers, and other third parties and for other lawful purposes. 

**_Online Shopping:_**  
If you make a purpose of a product or service via the PriceSmart.Com web site, the information you provide such as your credit card number and contact information will be provided to credit card processing company and/or bank for processing. This is to enable transactions to take place. Credit card information will, however, not be stored. 

**_Options to Limit Use of Data:_**  
Members of PriceSmart have the following options to limit the use of their personal data: 

As a member of PriceSmart you may give PriceSmart either or both of the forgoing directions by written notice to our membership services desk at anyone of our warehouse stores or by updating his or her personal profile on this website and checking the desired direction(s). 

**_How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information:_**  
You have the right of access to personally identifiable information held by PriceSmart about you. You may correct factual errors in such information by sending a request to us that credibly shows the error. Contact any of our membership services representatives at our warehouse stores about how to exercise your rights. To protect your privacy and security, we will take reasonable steps to verify your identify before granting access or making corrections. 

**_Consent:_**  
By using this web site to send us data about yourself, you consent to the computer storage and processing of your personal data by PriceSmart and its joint venture companies and licensees, and to the transmission of this data among such companies for the users described under Data Collection and Usage; you also consent to the transmission of such data by us to other companies where legally permitted. The forgoing is subject to your right, described under Options to Limit Use of Data, to give us directions limiting the use of your personal identifiable data. 

**_Web Trends:_**  
PriceSmart uses WebTrends Live to analyze traffic to this web site. WebTrends Live does not create individual profiles for visitors. Unlike some tracking services WebTrends Live does not have a database of individual profiles for each visitor. WebTrends Live only collects aggregate data. For more information about WebTrends Live privacy policy, please [click here](http://www.webtrendslive.com/privacy_policy.htm).
