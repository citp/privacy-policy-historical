# Price-Kicker.com

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacypolicy, please feel free to contact us by email.

At price-kicker.co.uk, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance tous. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information isreceived and collected by price-kicker.co.uk and how it is used.

**Log Files**

Like many other Web sites, price-kicker.co.uk makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol  (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

**Cookies and Web Beacons**

price-kicker.co.uk does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences and behaviour. See our [cookie policy](https://www.price-kicker.com/cookie_policy.html) for further details.
