# Privacy Policy - PRAGUE POST | The Voice of Prague

_Last modified: October 22, 2008._

Privacy Policy applies to all of the services provided at praguepost.com web sites. This Privacy Policy is subject to change.

1\. Collected information

When using praguepost.com web sites, we may collect the following types of information:

  1. Information in praguepost.com account – Upon registration to a praguepost.com account, the only required information is username, e-mail address, password and country of residence. You may also enter other personal information such as your name, surname, address, etc., which is not required. We may use this information in order to improve our services.
  2. Cookies are used on praguepost.com
  3. Affiliated sites – Services provided by our reservations section are provided by affiliated sites and the use and privacy at those sites is subject to their own terms of use and privacy policies.



Information we collect is used only to enhance our services, to perform research and to further develop praguepost.com

### 2\. Personal information and sharing

Personal information you provide is used only according to this Privacy Policy. You can decide not to provide the information, but we may not be able to provide our services. This information may be processed by a trusted third party, but we do not disclose this information unless it is required or enforced by law.
