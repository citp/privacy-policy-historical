PrajwalDesai.com does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

PrajwalDesai.com discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on PrajwalDesai.com behalf or to provide services available at PrajwalDesai.com websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. PrajwalDesai.com uses cookies to help PrajwalDesai.com identify and track visitors, their usage of PrajwalDesai.com website, and their website access preferences. PrajwalDesai.com visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using PrajwalDesai.com websites, with the drawback that certain features of PrajwalDesai.com websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
