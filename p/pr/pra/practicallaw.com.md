# practicallaw.com: Privacy policy

PLC Publications is committed to preserving its members' privacy. Please read the following privacy policy to understand how we use and protect the information that you provide us.

### Information that we collect from you

When you became a member of this site you provided us with information including your name, your company name and address, your e-mail address and telephone and fax numbers. This information allows us to process your membership and notify you of your username and password.

We will not disclose any of the information that you provide to us about yourself to any third parties without your express consent.

We may use the information we collect from you to notify you occasionally about important changes or developments to the site. If you would rather not receive this information, please send an e-mail to [info@practicallaw.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20040605202738id_/http%3A//www.practicallaw.com/jsp/info@practicallaw.com).

### Cookies

Cookies are small amounts of information stored by your computer. Our server issues cookies to your computer when you log on to the site in order to make it easier for you to log on, to allow you to personalise the content and to prevent unauthorised access to subscription material. These cookies only contain a unique ID so that we can identify you.

### Changes to our privacy policy

If we make any changes to our privacy policy in the future these will be posted to this page to keep you fully up to date with any developments.

### Comments

We welcome any questions or comments about privacy. Please send an e-mail to [info@practicallaw.com](mailto:info@practicallaw.com).
