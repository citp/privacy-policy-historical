# Privacy Policy | Practice Aptitude Tests

Please see below our terms and conditions of use if you have any questions please [Contact us.](mailto:practiceaptitudetests@gmail.com)

## Privacy Policy

### Who We Are

In the privacy policy below references to “we”, “us” or “our” refer to Practice Aptitude Tests Ltd (PracticeAptitudeTests) a registered company in England and Wales limited by shares. References to “our Web Site” or “the Web Site” refer to www.practiceaptitudetests.com or any of its sub domains.

### Information we collect

#### Information collected by us includes:

  1. Any personal details that you enter into the registration or sign up forms including but not limited to your email address, name, telephone number and personal address.
  2. Under no circumstances will we store or hold any confidential payment details such as your bank card number, account number, sort code, expiry date or security code. All payments will be processed through accredited payment service providers such as Sage Pay, Wirecard and PayPal.
  3. Information which allows us to identify your preferred settings, this saves you time re-entering your information when you return to the Web Site. This is, however, stored locally on your computer and is done through use of cookies. You can disable your cookies if you wish though your internet settings. You can also change your settings so you can be warned about cookies and block them if you wish. This can be done through your internet settings or through “help” in your internet browser or in internet settings accessed via control panel.



**Cookies**

We use Google Analytics to monitor site usage and which pages users visit. This is done to help us improve the service. The Google Analytics tool uses cookies to monitor pages visited through use of cookies and records information like your IP address. Your IP address is a number that is unique to your computer and is used by us to monitor your use of our website. Please refer to Google’s Privacy policy on this for more information. http://www.google.com/intl/en_uk/privacy.html

A cookie is a file which is stored on your hard drive.  If you agree or choose not to disable cookies in your browser the file is added added and it helps us analyse web traffic and allows website applications to respond to you as an individual user.  It remembers data and information about what preferences you have and allows you to log in to our site more easily.  At practiceaptitudetests.com we use this data and google analytics to find out which pages are most popular in order to improve user experience and develop the most popular areas of the site.  We use the data for statistical information and analysis.  Most web browsers automatically accept cookies but you can disable this in the settings section of your web browser.  This may, however, prevent you from getting full use of the website. By using practiceaptitudetests.com and not disabling cookies on your browser you are agreeing to the use of cookies.  A cookie does not give us access to your hard drive or access to your computer.  For further information on cookies please visit google’s page on how to manage cookies [https://support.google.com/chrome/bin/answer.py?hl=en-GB&answer=95647&p=cpn_cookies](https://support.google.com/chrome/bin/answer.py?hl=en-GB&answer=95647&p=cpn_cookies)

#### What we do with your information

We will use your personal information to process any order you make with us and provide after sales support. We may also use your email address to contact you from time to time regarding any other products, services or offers we have. Occasionally we may pass your name and email information to third parties for marketing services of related products or services this website offers. If you do not wish to be contacted with such information please email webmaster@practiceaptitudetests.com your personal information is treated in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998.

#### Your Rights

We link to other website and as highlighted in the terms and conditions do not control their content. The privacy policy detailed here only covers our website and does other websites are subject to their own privacy policy.

#### Changes to our privacy policy

We may make changes to our privacy policy at any time and reserve the right to do with without notice. Any changes made will appear on this web page. Please refer to this page regularly for updates.
