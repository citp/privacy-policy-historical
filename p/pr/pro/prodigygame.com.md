# Prodigy Math Game

SMARTeacher Inc. (“SMARTeacher”) is committed to protecting the privacy of our users and providing a safe environment for children to learn. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect and why, how this information is used, and what your options are regarding your personal information. 

## 1\. Personally Identifiable Information collected

SMARTeacher collects information in a number of different ways in order to provide our completely personalized education experience. During registration, SMARTeacher will ask for your name, email address, and how you heard of our service. When purchasing a paid subscription, we will ask for your billing address and credit card information. 

**Please note:** SMARTeacher does NOT store your credit card information. All transactions are processed using PayPal and Stripe.

SMARTeacher does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. When using our products, SMARTeacher will automatically collect data regarding usage patterns. This information may include questions answered, time spent in-game, and other information regarding the choices made in-game or through our website. We use this information to personalize each child’s experience and provide information to parents and teacher’s regarding their usage. 

## 2\. How information is used

The personal information that you provide allows us to provide our high-quality educational service. SMARTeacher may use data you’ve provided internally to refine our product or service offering. SMARTeacher may also share aggregated, anonymous data, such as overall performance improvements through the use of our products or services, with third-parties such as marketing agencies, advertisers, or potential investors. In the event that SMARTeacher sells some or all of its assets, customer information may be included in the sale. In such an event, the acquiring company would be required to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. 

## 3\. Your options regarding personally-identifiable information

While the information collected by SMARTeacher is required for the use of our products and services, you may contact us as outlined in Section 5 to request changes to your personal information, or to request that it be deleted. Please note, that by deleting this information, our products or services will no longer function correctly and may therefore lead to the termination of the affected SMARTeacher accounts and/or memberships. 

## 4\. Cookies

Cookies are small files that identify your browser when using our website. This allows us to save you time when returning to our site and anonymously track website usage to improve our services. These cookies may also be used to advertise to potential customers as they visit other websites. Cookies cannot store personally identifiable information about you from your computer. Should you choose to turn off cookies, some of our services may not function correctly. 

## 5\. Additional information.

If you have any questions or comments about SMARTeacher’s policies, please contact us directly by emailing support@prodigygame.com. This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time without notice.
