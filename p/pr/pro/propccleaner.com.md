# Privacy Policy - Pro PC Cleaner Software

Rainmaker Software Group, LLC provides this Privacy Policy to inform our users of our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personally identifiable information received from (i) users of our software Pro PC Cleaner, (ii) users of the website which supports our software found at [www.propccleaner.com](http://propccleaner.com/). This privacy policy applies to information collected from either the software or the aforementioned website.

We take our users’ privacy very seriously. The nature of our business requires the utmost trust from our valued customers. By using our software and/or our site, you are consenting to our handling of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us. “Handling” means using your personally identifiable information, using cookies or Tags (defined below) on a computer or using or touching information in any way, including, but not limited to, accumulating, storing, using, combining, disclosing, and deleting information. By using Pro PC Cleaner software or propccleaner.com or otherwise providing information to us, you consent to such treatment of your data. 

We wish to demonstrate our commitment to protecting user privacy and protecting user data, and will therefore disclose the following:

  1. What information is collected by Pro PC Cleaner software, and propccleaner.com website, how we use it, and with whom we may share it
  2. What the user’s choices are regarding the collection and use of this information
  3. How we protect the personal information that is under our control
  4. How the user can correct any inaccuracies in the information or opt-out of future correspondence



Collection and Use of Information

We collect information from our (i) software (Pro PC Cleaner) and (ii) website (propccleaner.com) users at several different points in accordance with the applicable data protection laws and strictly for the purposes necessary to our business and its users’ convenience. Personally identifiable information that we may collect through the use of Pro PC Cleaner or propccleaner.com may include email address, physical address and telephone number.

When you visit propccleaner.com, our servers may record and store data that your browser sends whenever you visit a web site. This data may include information such as your Internet protocol (“IP”) address, search term history (how you found our site and software), or the domain name from which you are visiting. We use this information it to monitor the use of the propccleaner.com. For users of the software Pro PC Cleaner, we may combine your personally identifiable information with non-identifying information and aggregate it with information collected from Site users to attempt to provide you with a better experience, to improve the quality and value of the services and understand the needs of our customers and how we may improve our software. We may use the contact information entered into our software, Pro PC Cleaner, or onto propccleaner.com to market to you, and provide you with information about, our products and services, including but not limited to promotions, recent news in computer and Internet related threats, and subscription information. 

If you become a paying customer for Pro PC Cleaner software we utilize a secure credit card processing system called Safecart. When our registration/order forms in Pro PC Cleaner or on propccleaner.com asks users to enter sensitive information (such as credit card number), that information is entered into Safecart and is encrypted, processed and protected through Safecart. While on a secure site or page, users should notice a small lock symbol either in the url for the site or somewhere else on the page. This indicates that the user is visiting a secure page or website. Customers may also visit SafeCart’s privacy policy for more information at https://www.safecart.com/privacy. SafeCart may collect and retain your name, email address, billing address, credit card number and other billing information. 

### Information Use

Subject to the legal exceptions listed below, we will not share our users’ email addresses or other confidential information to third parties. Unless otherwise requested via the manner described below, we may contact our users via email in the future to tell inform them of specials, new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy.

#### Legal Disclaimer

We may need to disclose personal information as required by law to comply with a current judicial proceeding, court order, or other legal process served on (i) Pro PC Cleaner software, (ii) propccleaner.com, or (iii) the parent company. We may disclose personal information to law enforcement, other government officials, or third parties in response to criminal or civil subpoenas.

### Business Transitions

Should (i) Pro PC Cleaner software or (ii) propccleaner.com website or (iii) the parent company Rainmaker Software Group, LLC go through a merger or acquisition to another company, or sell any portion of our assets, the personal information of our users may be part of the assets transferred. All users of (i) Pro PC Cleaner software (ii) propccleaner.com will be notified prior to a change of ownership or control of their personal information. If the users’ personally identifiable information will be used in a manner different than stated at the time of collection as a result of the business transition, all users will be given a choice not to have their information used in this different manner.

### Links/Advertisements

Propccleaner.com may contain links to other websites. Other websites may link to propccleaner.com at times without permission. Other websites may advertise Pro PC Cleaner software and provide a link to download the software. Neither (i) Pro PC Cleaner software (ii) propccleaner.com nor (iii) the parent company Rainmaker Software Group, LLC is responsible for the privacy practices and policies of any other website, including such other sites that may have a link to propccleaner.com or Pro PC Cleaner software on their website. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by (i) Pro PC Cleaner software or (ii) propccleaner.com. Pro PC Cleaner software, propccleaner.com, and the parent company have advertising and affiliate relationships with various third party sites, which drive interested parties to our product and our sites. Information that is collected on stated third party sites does not fall within the auspices of this policy.

### Surveys

We may periodically request information from our Pro PC Cleaner software and propccleaner.com users via a voluntary survey. Users have therefore the choice whether or not to disclose any information or participate in the survey. We typically request information such as name, address, email address, etc. Information collected in such surveys is used strictly for the purposes of improving or updating our software Pro PC Cleaner and our site, propccleaner.com. Users’ personally identifiable information is not shared with third parties. Though we may use an intermediary to conduct these surveys, they may not retain or use customers’ personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes.

### Cookies

We may use “cookies” on propccleaner.com. A cookie is a piece of data stored on a user’s hard drive. The cookies we may use help us improve the user’s access to propccleaner.com and enhance their experience on that site. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information on propccleaner.com.  
Some of our business partners may use cookies on propccleaner.com. However, we have no access to or control over these cookies, once we have given permission for them to set cookies for advertising.  
This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by propccleaner.com only and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

### Log Files

Our servers use log files to administer Pro PC Cleaner software and propccleaner.com website, analyze trends, track our users’ movements, and gather general demographic information for aggregate use. The information gathered in these log files is not shared with any third party.

### Customer Service

We regularly respond to customer service questions and issues via email or phone, whichever is the customer’s express preference.

### Security

We take every precaution to protect our customers’ confidential information. We are extremely concerned about protecting the confidentiality of our users’ personally identifiable information. We employ secure servers for the processing of any sensitive information. We will notify our users of any breach in security affecting their confidential information. When sensitive information is submitted into our software, Pro PC Cleaner, or our website, propccleaner.com, this information is protected both online and off-line.  
As previously stated, most sensitive information is entered into, encrypted and processed via Safecart.com. Customers may visit SafeCart’s privacy policy for more information at <https://www.safecart.com/privacy>

### Correcting/Updating/Deleting/Deactivating Personal Information

Customers may opt out of receiving any future contact from us at any time. Users may also contact us to correct, update, or delete their personally identifiable information. We encourage our customers to let us know if they no longer desire our services or correspondence.

### Notification of Changes

If we change our privacy policy we will post those changes in this privacy statement. We want our users to be informed of any changes to the information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it. Unless users are otherwise notified, with an opportunity to opt-out, we will use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected.
