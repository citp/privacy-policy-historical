# Prophecy News Watch - Biblical Prophecy in the News

  
 

 **Prophecy News Watch** **Privacy Policy**

* * *

Prophecy News Watch collects a users name, address and email for the purpose of sending items requested and receipting purposes. All email's are collected through our opt-in subscribe form on our homepage.  We do not email anyone unsolicited.

We do not sell our mailing list and any records are used for opt-in communication purposes only. We will will occasionally allow advertisers to include sponsored offers of value within our own email newsletter but we will never reveal your personal information to third parties. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link to be removed from our mailing list.  You may also send an email to [info@prophecynewswatch.com](mailto:info@prophecynewswatch.com) to request personal information modifications, deletion and removal from mailing list.
