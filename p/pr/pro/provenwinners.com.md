# Proven Winners - Press - Contact

### Privacy Policy

Proven Winners will only have personal information about you if you voluntarily decide to provide it to us. Personal information includes information such as your name, address, phone number, email address and other information that identifies you as the individual you are.

If you decide to identify yourself by providing personal information to us, our intent is to disclose it only to partners or affiliates of Proven Winners and other parties who may provide services related to Proven Winners.

You should know that personal information sent over the Internet whether by email or one of our forms is not necessarily secure against interception.

Also, we cannot absolutely assure you that your information will not be further disclosed without your permission or ours.
