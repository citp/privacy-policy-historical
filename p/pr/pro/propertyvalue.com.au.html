Privacy

Information for myrpdata is sourced from RP Data Pty Ltd, Australia's leading property information provider. RP Data Pty Ltd gathers information publicly available from sources such as the valuer general office in each state and newspaper advertising. The use of these details are in accordance to the rules set down by the relevant state department.

 

In designing this web site, we have incorporated security procedures and practices that we consider are consistent with Australian industry practice. We review our security procedures from time to time and update them when relevant. All orders placed online using your credit card are covered by SSL security technology to protect your details. Our site is a Verisign Secure Site so you can be assured that your security is never compromised.

 

Protecting your information is important. If you submit information to myrpdata either by using an electronic form or by sending an email we collect that information and use it for the purposes that you have provided it.

 

In addition, we may use your information to provide you with special offers, information about new products and special promotions that we think may interest you. If you do not want to receive this information, you can let us know by sending an email to privacy@rpdata.com, by mail Locked Bag 5, Eagle Farm, QLD 4009 or call 1300 734 318. Please provide us with your customer details including name, address and contact number. In addition, indicate if you do not wish to receive any more promotional material.

 

Personal information about you and your business with myrpdata is only accessible by you and by those who are authorised by us to access it. They also must use a password, and are bound to safeguard the information and access it only for approved business purposes.

 

If you have any questions or comments about myrpdata privacy policy please email us at privacy@rpdata.com
