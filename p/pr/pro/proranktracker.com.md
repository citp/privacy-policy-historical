# Privacy Policy - Pro Rank Tracker

## Privacy Policy

Pro Rank Tracker having a mailing address at P.O Box 22602, Tel Aviv 61226, Israel (hereinafter, referred to as “we”, “our, “us”, “Company”) collects from the individual user (hereinafter, referred to as “you” or “your”) certain information through the Website http://proranktracker.com/ (hereinafter, referred to as “Website”). This Privacy Policy contains our policies and procedures governing the collection and handling of information that identifies an individual user or that could be used to contact or locate him or her (hereinafter, referred to as “Personal Information”). This Privacy Policy applies only to the Website. It does not apply to any other websites. 

#### 1\. Personal Information collected by us

1.1 The Website may contain online forms that ask users to provide their: (1) first and second name; (2) company name; (3) email address; (4) Physical address; (5) phone number; and (6) PayPal account.

#### 2\. Non-personal information collected by us

2.1 When you visit the Website, the Company may automatically collect non-personal information, such as information related to keywords tracked through the Website, browser types, operating systems, and the URL addresses of websites clicked to and from the Website, pages views, and the average time spent on the Website.

2.2 The non-personal information collected by us will be aggregated and de-personalized. The non-personal information collected by us will not allow the identification of individuals. 

2.3 The Company may use cookies to automatically collect and use certain non-personal information.

#### 3\. The purpose of collection of Personal Information

3.1 We use your Personal Information to provide you with the services you request and to communicate with you. We also use that information to the extent necessary to enforce this Privacy Policy and to prevent imminent harm to persons or property. Moreover, the Company may use aggregated Personal Information for industry research and academic research. 

3.2 We may use your Personal Information to send you advertisements of our services or products which may be of interest to you. If you do not want to receive such advertisements, please contact us by using the contact details mentioned in Section 13 of this Privacy Policy. 

3.3 The Company will not use any personal or non-personal information collected by you through the Website to send offers of services or products to your clients.

#### 4\. The purpose of collection of non-personal information

4.1 The Company collects the non-personal information mentioned in Section 2 in order to analyze the keywords tracked through the Website and create statistics of such keywords. In addition, the Company collects the non-personal information to analyze what kind of users visit the Website, how they find it, how long they stay, from which other websites they come to the Website, what pages they look at, to which other websites they go from the Website. 

4.2 We may share the non-personal information collected by us with our partners. 

#### 5\. Merger, acquisition or sale of all or a portion of its assets

5.1 If the Company is involved in a merger, acquisition or sale of all or a portion of its assets, the Company reserves the right to transfer personal information or non-personal information to a party involved in the merger, acquisition or sale, to the extent such information is necessary to carry out the merger, acquisition or sale.

#### 6\. Protection of Personal Information

6.1 We employ information security tools to protect Personal Information collected by us. Such information security tools include Secure Sockets Layer (“SSL”), firewalls, and secure server facilities. SSL is a protocol for encrypting information over the Internet. 

6.2 However, even with the use of such tools, we cannot guarantee the security of Personal Information. By using the Website, you acknowledge and agree that we make no such guarantee, and that your use of the Website is at your own risk.

#### 7\. Third party access to Personal Information

7.1 We give certain independent contractors access to your Personal Information. Those contractors assist us with the maintenance of the Website. All those contractors are required to sign contracts in which they promise to protect Personal Information using procedures reasonably equivalent to ours.

7.2 We will disclose your Personal Information to courts, law enforcement agencies, and parties in legal disputes only if we are compelled by law or a court to do so. Unless we are compelled by law or by a court, we will not share any Personal Information to courts, law enforcement agencies, and parties in legal disputes. 

#### 8\. Accessing and correcting your Personal Information

8.1 If you would like to access or modify your Personal Information, please log into your account on the Website and click on the “Edit Account Details” page.

8.2 If you would like to delete your Personal Information stored on our servers, please contact us at the address mentioned in Section 13 of this Privacy Policy. We will respond within seven days from receiving your request for deleting your Personal Information.

#### 9\. Cookies

9.1 We use cookies to make use of the Website as convenient as possible. Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to a computing device for record keeping purposes. The majority of web browsers are set to accept cookies. The cookies do not themselves personally identify users. However, they identify users' browsers. The cookies allow the Company to remember your preferences and improve your experience of the Website.

9.2 It is possible to set your browser to refuse cookies. Please note that this may limit the services provided by us through the Website. If you would like to stop receiving cookies, check your browser's help information or visit the following links for further information:

  


  * [Aboutcookies.org](http://www.aboutcookies.org/)
  * [Internet Explorer](http://windows.microsoft.com/en-GB/windows7/How-to-manage-cookies-in-Internet-Explorer-9)
  * [Firefox](http://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/Cookies)
  * [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/bin/answer.py?hl=en-GB&answer=95647&p=cpn_cookies)
  * [Opera](http://www.opera.com/browser/tutorials/security/privacy/)



#### 10\. Amendment of this Privacy Policy

10.1 We may change this Privacy Policy at any time by posting a new version on the Website. The new version will become effective on the date it is posted.

#### 11\. Retention period

11.1 We will retain your Personal Information for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

#### 12\. Your consent

12.1 By using the Website, you are consenting to our processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us. The term “Processing” includes, but is not limited to, collecting, storing, deleting, using, combining and disclosing information.

P.O Box 22602, Tel Aviv 61226, Israel
