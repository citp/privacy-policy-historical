Our Commitment to Data Security

Progress Energy is concerned about the security of your information. As an added level of security, several online forms on this site utilize Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) when transmitting and receiving data. SSL helps prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the appropriate use of information. SSL works by using a private key to encrypt data before it is transmitted over an Internet connection. Both Netscape Navigator and Internet Explorer support SSL. However, we cannot guarantee the security of any information transmitted over the Internet.
