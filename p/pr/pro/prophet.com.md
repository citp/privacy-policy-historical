Prophet respects the personal privacy of our users. Prophet collects information provided by users in a database that is used to generate follow up messages and information that is tailored to the user's request. Prophet does not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone. If you have any additional questions or would like to be removed from our database, please contact [webmaster@prophet.com](mailto:webmaster@prophet.com).
