Cookies

Our website uses cookies. A cookie is a small data file sent to your browser and stored on your device. Most of the cookies we use are session cookies and will be deleted after the end of your browser session; some are persistent for a certain amount of time and allow to recognize your browser within the next session (for example if you choose the “remember me” sign in preference).

You can change your browser settings to restrict or block some or all cookies. Check the information provided in your browser’s help section for specific instructions on how to manage cookies. Opting out or blocking some cookies may affect the functionality of the website (such as the remember preferences).
