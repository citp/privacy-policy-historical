Privacy and Security

Your right to privacy and security is very important to PropertyGenie. PropertyGenie treats personal information obtained through the use of this website as private and confidential, subject to the provisions of this Privacy and Security Statement. We are committed to providing you with secure access to online services and PropertyGenie endeavours to take all reasonable steps to protect your personal information and to maintain confidentiality. However, the same cannot be guaranteed and any information you transmit to us online, or via the Website, is done so at your own risk.

PropertyGenie collects information about you to monitor and improve the Website. PropertyGenie is committed to keeping your information confidential, although it may be used to personalise your experience, and/or to directly communicate with you. Sometimes we may need to share aggregate information (for example, demographic data) with our stakeholders and business partners but we do not disclose to them information about our individual users. By submitting your information and/or using this Website, you agree that PropertyGenie may use your personal or other information to send you information on new and/or related services or products that may be of interest to you via mail, email or SMS. If you do not wish to continue receiving this information you may un-subscribe from these services which will remove you from the PropertyGenie mailing list.

PropertyGenie website may contain links to or from other sites. While PropertyGenie tries to link only to sites that share PropertyGenie high standards and respect for privacy, PropertyGenie is not responsible for the content or the security or privacy practices employed by other sites. PropertyGenie recommends that you always read the privacy and security statements on such sites.

PropertyGenie will not collect any information from you that is not related to the data functions contemplated by this Website and/or as otherwise contemplated by this Privacy and Security Statement. Information that has become obsolete will be destroyed from time to time.

PropertyGenie reserve the right to amend this privacy and security statement at any time. All amendments to this privacy and security statement will be posted on the website. Unless otherwise stated, the current version shall supersede and replace all previous versions of this privacy and security statement.
