# Proxy Sites And School Proxies At ProxB.Com

## Privacy Policy

At www.proxb.com and its network, the privacy of our guests is of extreme importance to us. That privacy policy document outlines the types of private information is obtained and acquired by www.proxb.com and how it is used.

DoubleClick DART Cookie 

Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website. " Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet. " Users may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting Ads Settings. (Alternatively, you can direct users to opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for interest based advertising by visiting aboutads.info.)

Record Documents 

Like many other Internet sites, www.proxb.com makes use of log files. The information within the log records includes net protocol ( Internet Protocol Address ) handles, type of visitor, Internet Company ( ISP ), date/time press, referring/exit pages, and number of ticks to analyze trends, provide the site, track user's activity across the site, and gather demographic information.

Should you need any additional information or have any questions about our privacy, please feel free to get hold of us by mail at admin@proxb.com

The service we provide allows indirect browsing of third-party websites. A third-party website may attempt to send cookies to the user. We store these cookies on our server and automatically forward them onto the target server. Your cookie data is treated strictly confidentially, stored privately and deleted soon after you leave our website. If you would prefer us not to store your cookies, please disable the "Allow Cookies" option.

The service we provide may attempt to forward third-party cookies from the websites you choose to browse. We have no control over indirect cookies and you are advised to consult the privacy policy of the applicable third-party website. If you do not wish to receive any such indirect cookies, please disable the "Allow Cookies" option.

* For more information on configuring your browser, see [AboutCookies.org](http://www.aboutcookies.org/Default.aspx?page=1) or the documentation for your browser. We are not responsible for the content of any third-party websites.
