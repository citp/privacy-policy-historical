# Privacy Policy | Progle

#### Privacy Policy

**General Information**

Progle offers services including price comparisons, product comparisons and coupons and offers. All the information displayed in progle is retrieved from open source resources and by using API interfaces provided by respective online shopping sites.

**What type of information we collect?**

We collect different type of information depending on type of user interaction and membership of progle.

  1. **For a regular unregistered visitors & Web Browser Extension users.**

We have collecting user visits and interaction information for all users who are visiting progle. this include user location information interaction on web page and time which users remain on website. We have use this information to improve user experience and will not transferred to any third parties.

  2. **Registred users.**

We have collect information regarding users including name, email address ,password and payment methods available to users. We use name to display on our web pages. Also use payment methods information to filter applicable coupons.




Apart from this we will collect and use information including products images and related information provided by user and use the same to display information on webpage.

**Usage of cookies**

  1. We are using cookies to save user information including location and cutsumisation informations. 
  2. Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser to enable our systems to recognize your browser and to provide features offers, Recommended for You, personalized advertisements on other Web sites and affiliation.
  3. The Help menu on the menu bar of most browsers will tell you how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie and how to disable cookies altogether. Additionally, you can disable or delete similar data used by browser add-ons, such as Flash cookies, by changing the add-on's settings or visiting the website of its manufacturer. However, because cookies allow you to take advantage of some of Servicesâ€™s essential features, we recommend that you leave them turned on. For instance, if you block or otherwise reject our cookies, you will not be able to get offers , best deals , recommendations etc.
  4. If you do leave cookies turned on, be sure to sign off when you finish using a shared computer.



**Regarding information displayed on progle.**

Progle web servers are collecting wide range of the information from different online stores by using API interfaces provide by different online stores. We have collecting and updating product information as faster rates as possible. Although we can’t guarantee the information is realtime. The information displayed will be depend on information provide by online stores and we can’t be responsible for an loss of money or information by using information displayed on progle.
