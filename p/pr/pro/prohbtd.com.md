# PRØHBTD - Privacy Policy

### PRØHBTD Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 14, 2019

Welcome to PRØHBTD.com (the “Site”, and together with the PRØHBTD mobile applications, online store, and related services the “Services”), provided by PRØHBTD Media, Inc. (together with its affiliates, collectively, “Us”, “We”, or “PRØHBTD”). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data (“Personal Information”). Personal Information may include, among other things, your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and/or any other personal identifier by which you may be identified or contacted. This PRØHBTD.com Privacy Policy (“Policy”) is designed to help you understand the type of information we may collect from our users (“user” or “you”) of the Site, how we use Personal Information and non-personally identifiable information that we collect, and how we protect your Personal Information.

Please read the following Policy carefully before using the Services. By accessing or using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read, understand, and agree to this Policy.

If you do not agree to this Policy, you may not use the Services.

**Children  **

****You may not use the Site or Services if you are under the age of 18, or the age of majority in your jurisdiction, if older than 18. By using the Site or the Services in any manner, you represent that you are at least the age of majority in the jurisdiction in which you are located.

In compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998, 15 U.S.C. §§ 6501-6506 and the regulations promulgated thereunder, the Company does not permit children under 13 years of age to register on the Site or Services and does not knowingly collect Personal Information from anyone under the age of 13. 

**Information We Collect**

When you use our Services, we collect Personal Information, such as:

  1. Information you provide us when you respond to a communication such as email, or participate in any other feature made available on the Services, ir purchase anything from our online store;
  2. Demographic information such as your gender and location;
  3. Any other information, such as reviews, content, and biographical information, that you provide us;
  4. Information you provide to third parties through the Services, including without limitation, information provided to our business partners.



We automatically collect non-personally-identifiable information such as: (i) Internet protocol addresses, (ii) domain server information; (iii) geo-location information, (iv) your browser type, language, browser settings and plug-ins, and other identifying information, (v) your operating system and application version, (vi) device types, (vii) device model and manufacturer, (viii) device identifiers, (ix) your device operating system type and version, and (x) pages viewed and other general tracking information, including without limitation the elements you have interacted with, metadata and other details about these elements, change states and other user actions.

When you purchase something from our online store, as part of the buying and selling process, we collect the personal information you give us, including but not limited to your name, address, email address, and order history.  

We also use cookies, Web beacons, and URL information to gather information regarding the date and time of your visit and the information for which you searched and which you viewed. Cookies are small pieces of information that a website sends to your computer’s hard drive while you are viewing a website. We may use both session cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent cookies (which usually stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience on our Site. Web beacons are digital images that are used to log information on the Services or in our emails. We use Web beacons to manage cookies, count visits, and to learn what marketing works and what does not. We also use Web beacons to tell if you open or act on our emails. We do not honor “Do Not Track” signals.

Others, including third party analytics service providers and advertising partners may also collect Personal Information and other information about your online activities over time and across different Web sites when you use our Services, including as described in this Policy. This Policy does not apply to and we are not responsible for those other parties. Third party analytics services may use cookies and web beacons through our Site (for example Google Analytics and Mixpanel.) We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third parties to learn about your choices about information they collect from you.

**Sharing of Your Information**

The Services may allow you to connect and share your actions, comments, content, profile, and other information, including but not limited to, personally identifiable information, publicly or with other people. Please be mindful of your own privacy needs as you choose who to connect with and what to share and make public. We cannot control the privacy or security of information you choose to make public or share with others.

The Services may also provide you with the option to share certain information from the Services with social networking services like Facebook, Google, Twitter, Instagram, etc. We are not responsible for the use or re-sharing by others of any of your information once it is made public. If you do not want your information to be made public, you should not use the PRØHBTD Services in this manner and/or you should adjust your privacy settings on the applicable social networking service accordingly. We are not responsible for and we do not control these social networking services privacy practices. Please review the applicable privacy policy for information about how they use your information.

**How PRØHBTD Uses Your Personal Information**

PRØHBTD generally uses your Personal Information to:

  1. Facilitate the creation of  security of your account on our Site;
  2. Identify you as a user in our system;
  3. Provide, personalize, and improve our products, Site, the Services, and how we operate our business;
  4. Communicate with you about your use of the Services;
  5. Respond to your comments or questions and for our support team to provide customer service to you;
  6. Develop new products and services;
  7. Customize the advertising you view and recommend content to you;
  8. Fulfill your requests;
  9. Send newsletters, surveys, offers and promotional materials related to the Services and for other marketing purposes of PRØHBTD using your contact information;
  10. Send you other information including confirmations, invoices, technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support and administrative messages;
  11. Protect, investigate, and prevent potentially fraudulent, unauthorized, or illegal activities; 
  12. Protect our rights and the rights of other users; and
  13. As otherwise described in this Privacy Policy or in notices we provided to you.



If you make any purchase from our online store or correspond with us or submit comments or suggestions, we may also store your Personal Information on our servers. We may also use your information to verify your geographic location. We may use your geographic location data to personalize our Service, to recommend content, determine whether the information you have requested is available in your location.

Email marketing: By providing us with your email address, you are giving us permission to send you emails about our store, new products and other updates. You have the opportunity to unsubscribe from these emails at any time by clicking the “Unsubscribe” button included at the bottom of each email.

**How PRØHBTD Discloses Your Personal Information**

The Company treats its users’ information with the utmost sensitivity and does not knowingly disclose your Personal Information to third parties except as follows:

  1. We may share your Personal Information to fulfill the purpose for which your provide the Personal Information, such as a sale or customer service inquiry.
  2. We may share your Personal Information with your consent or at your direction.
  3. We may also share your Personal Information with others who perform services on our behalf, provided that such third parties provide at least the same level of privacy protection as required by this Privacy Policy
  4. We may disclose your Personal Information if we believe we are required to do so by law, or to comply with a court order, judicial or other government subpoena, or warrant, or other legal process.
  5. We also may disclose your Personal Information if we believe doing so is appropriate or necessary to prevent any liability, or fraudulent, abusive, or unlawful uses or to protect PRØHBTD and our Services; or any rights, property, or personal safety of PRØHBTD or others.
  6. We may disclose your Personal Information to a buyer or potential buyer or other successor or potential successor to all or part of PRØHBTD’s business, whether by merger, divestiture, restructuring, reorganization, dissolution, or other sale or transfer of some or all of the Company’s assets, in which personal information about the Services’ users is among the assets transferred, or in the event of insolvency, bankruptcy, or receivership.



**How PRØHBTD Uses Your Non-Personally Identifiable Information**

We keep track of the pages visited by each user and may aggregate certain non-personally identifiable information with data on the pages visited by other users to track overall visitor traffic patterns. We may also use third-party automatic data collection technologies (for example, Google Analytics or HubSpot) to collect information about your online activities over time and across third-party websites or other online services (behavioral tracking). We use this information to, among other things, improve the Site and Services and our online store by making them more responsive to the needs and preferences of our users. We may also share this aggregated and anonymized data with our partners, advertisers, and other third parties. This information does not identify you personally.

**Payment Processing**

Our online store is built on an e-commerce software platform called Shopify that allows us to sell our products and services to you.

Payment:

If you provide us with your credit card information during the payment process, we send it to our PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway for processing using SSL encryption technology. Your credit card information is never stored for use beyond your current transaction.

All direct payment gateways comply with the standards set by PCI-DSS as managed by the PCI Security Standards Council, which is a joint effort of brands like Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Discover.

PCI-DSS requirements help ensure the secure handling of credit card information by our store and our payment processors and service providers.

Our Policy does not cover any third-party services. To learn about those third parties’ privacy practices, please read their privacy policies.

We do not have access to or control over cookies, Web beacons, platform device identifiers, software agents, or other technologies that they may use. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party advertisers. You should check the privacy policy of the third-party advertiser to determine how it handles information it separately collects from you. In addition, the [Network Advertising Initiative](https://www.networkadvertising.org/) offers information about some of the Internet advertising companies we may use.

**Information Choices**

When you visit the Site, we and others give you the following choices about use of mechanisms for tracking, including tracking of your online activities over time and across different websites and online services by third parties. Most Web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. If you prefer, you can usually choose to set your browser to remove cookies and to reject cookies from our Site or from third parties. If you choose to remove cookies or reject cookies, this could affect certain features or services of our Site. To opt-out of Google Analytics web tracking for certain browsers, you can download [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout). You may opt out of other cookies that may be present on the Site by following the directions on [Google’s opt-out page](https://www.google.com/settings/ads), [ScorecardResearch’s opt-out page](https://www.scorecardresearch.com/optout.aspx?returnurl=vfoptout&source=sroptout), and [Quantcast’s opt out page](https://www.quantcast.com/opt-out). To find out more and opt-out click [here](https://www.networkadvertising.org/choices). However, while we and others give you choices as described in this policy, there are many ways in which Web browser signals and other similar mechanisms can indicate your choice to disable tracking, and our Site may not be aware of or honor every mechanism.
