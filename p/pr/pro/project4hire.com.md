# Privacy | Project4Hire Freelance Marketplace

As revised on: September 29, 2014.

At Project4Hire, we recognize and respect the privacy needs of the visitors to our website. Safeguarding your personal information is of vital importance to us. Please read the following Privacy Policy to understand our policies and practices regarding the collection, use and disclosure of your personal information you submit to us through our website. This policy may change from time to time without notice so please check back periodically. We hope this will increase your awareness about our online privacy.

## Collected Information

If you register or purchase a service on our website, we may ask you to provide us with certain personal information, such as your name, physical and electronic mail ("e-mail") addresses, phone number, domain name and other information that by itself, or in combination with other information, may be identifiable to you as a specific individual. We may also ask for financial information, such as account or credit card numbers, and demographic information, such as your zip code.

Project4Hire does not store your credit card information on its servers. The credit card information that you provide is sent to third-party payment processors for processing. Credit card information is transmitted via SSL ("Secure Socket Layer") encryption for your protection.

Please be aware that Project4Hire advertisers, and web sites that have links on our sites, and provider/distributor partners may also collect personally identifiable information about you. The information practices of these Internet access partners, advertisers, and web sites linked to Project4Hire and provider/distributor partners, are not governed by this Privacy Policy. What these other sites do with that information is governed by the privacy policy for that site and not this policy. You are encouraged to review the privacy policy on any site you visit.

## Information Request

You may request information on our site regarding products and services, including Technical Support and Customer Service, by using online forms. We may ask that you provide certain contact information to allow us to fulfill your request and to send you information about Project4Hire's products and services.

## Security

Paying online for services at Project4Hire.com is secure and convenient. Credit card information is transmitted via SSL ("Secure Socket Layer") encryption for your protection to our payment processors.

## Disclosure

Project4Hire will not sell, rent, trade, or disclose individual customer information to any third parties or outside companies who will use the information to contact you or who will share the information with others without your permission. It may be necessary to disclose information in special cases when we have reason to believe that disclosing the information is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be violating Project4Hire's policies or may be causing injury to or interference with (either intentionally or unintentionally) the rights or property of Project4Hire, other Project4Hire users, or anyone else that could be harmed by such activities. Project4Hire may disclose or access account information when we believe in good faith that the law requires it or for administrative and other purposes that we deem necessary to maintain, service, and improve our products and services.

## Use of Information

The use of an individual's e-mail address is necessary to keep our Customers well informed of maintenance issues, escalated critical issues and problem resolutions that may arise with your use of Project4Hire services. We may also use e-mail addresses to provide our Customers with informative newsletters, which you may opt out of.

Project4Hire may use personal and demographic information to help us determine which Project4Hire products and services are most likely to be of interest to you or with our analysis of product usage, purchase, and target markets.

Please note that Project4Hire does provide information to outside companies (such as a credit card processing companies) who perform certain functions on our behalf for billing purposes. We will not permit those companies to use the gathered information for other than the stated purpose.

## Correct/Update

If you would like to change or modify information you have previously provided to us, please log on and go to our Account page.

## Cookies

Some of the websites we host place small bits of text or "cookies" onto your computer's hard drive. Cookies enable us to deliver content specific to your interests and keep track of your password so you do not have to re-enter it each time you visit our web sites. After you accept a cookie, it is passed from your browser to our servers each time you request a page. This cookie consists of a unique token that anonymously identifies to the server so that you can more easily access our features and services. Please note that you may configure your web browser to warn you before you accept a cookie.

## Customer Messages

Project4Hire will not read or disclose personal correspondance to others on the website unless required by law or deemed necessary to operate our services or resolve disputes.

## Contacting the Web Site

If you have any questions about Project4Hire's privacy policy or the privacy practices of our web sites, please [contact us](https://www.project4hire.com/contact-us/).

## Changes to the Privacy Policy

In the event our Privacy Policy needs to be modified or amended, we will post the updated policy on this page. Please check back often to review any revisions.
