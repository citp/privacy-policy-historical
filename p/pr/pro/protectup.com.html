What is Link Protection?

URLSAFETY.COM is an easy way to protect your links and shorten them. You can put additional settings to secure your link (password, referrer). Use URLSAFETY.COM Link Protector to protect the links of files put in a free file hoster website as Hotfile.com, Rapidshare.com, Megaupload.com . You can share your links safely and avoid to expose them to being reported or risk the deletion of the files. With URL-SAFETY you can edit your links after protecting them. You can add multiple links and all links will be protected in one. It's a safe way to moderate and share your links in an other web site.
