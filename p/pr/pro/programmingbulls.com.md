# Privacy Policy | Shoutability- World will Surely Listen

_**Important Note:** If you need any kind of information regarding this policy or have a question to ask, we would advice you to [contact us](http://www.shoutability.com/contact-us). Alternatively, you can also drop us a mail at  **admin[at]shoutability.com** and we will respond to it within 48 hours._

We at  **www.shoutability.com** , gives an extreme importance to the privacy of our visitors. This document contains all information about  the personal data collected by   **www.shoutability.com** and  also we are enlisting the ways in which it is used.

### **Log Files**

Just like other Websites and Blogs on Internet, we at **www.shoutability.com**  tracks our users by log files. Following is the information which is there in this log file:

  1. Internet protocol ( IP ) Addresses.
  2. Browser Name and Version.
  3. Internet Service Provider ( ISP ).
  4. Date an Time of Visit.
  5. Referring Page and Exit pages.
  6. Number of Clicks made (Just to analyze the latest trends and administrate the site in a better way.



### **Cookies and Web Beacons**

****Also   **www.shoutability.com**  makes use cookies to keep some information about the preference of our loyal visitors. We also record user-specific information such as the page accessed/visited. Also we customize our Webpage and its content on the basis of Visitor’s browser type. This is done just to increase the readability.

### **DoubleClick DART Cookie**

.:: Google , is used as a third party vendor by us,  they uses some cookies to display advertisements on **www.shoutability.com**.

.:: Google also uses a special cookie named **DART cookie.** This enables it to serve ads to readers on the basis of content they read on **www.shoutability.com**.

.:: Readers and Visitors can alternatively opt out of this DART cookie by visiting this link – [Google Ads Privacy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)

**Google Adsense** is the only Advertising Partner which we are using at present.

These Ads uses third-party technology to display the advertisements and links which appear on **www.shoutability.com**  and these are sent directly to reader’s browsers. They receive your IP address automatically, when this occurs.

**www.shoutability.com** has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. www.shoutability.com’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
