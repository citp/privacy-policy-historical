This Privacy Policy refers to Users who join Eve’s Network as “Members”. Project Eve’s Website enables Members to create a profile, interact with one another, promote their business or service, read content, sign-up for events, list their business in the directory, list their member events and make purchases.

2. COLLECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

In order to become a Member of Project Eve, Users must register for account credentials. When you register for account credentials, Project Eve collects certain Personal Information, including your name, email address, and a password that you select. Project Eve may use the Website to collect, use, and retain all information, including Personal Information and Network Data, that is disclosed and uploaded by Members in connection with their use of Project Eve. “Network Data” means all data collected from Project Eve or a Member and includes registration information, profile data, answers to profile questions, all Content and other information contributed by Members, and statistical information and analytics relating to usage and interactions within the Website When a User signs up to become a member of Project Eve, he or she is providing Personal Information to Project Eve, as such Project Eve and its designated administrators will have access to all Personal Information and Network Data. In addition, Project Eve will have the ability to reset Member passwords in the event that a Member loses or can’t remember it. Project Eve might also collect additional types of Personal Information.

In order to implement certain functionality on the Web site, Project Eve provides Members the opportunity to access and use third party applications (“Third Party Applications”) created by third party application developers (“Third Party Application Developers”). Where such Third Party Applications collect, store and use information provided by Members in some manner separate from the use made by Project Eve or Ning as disclosed in this Privacy Policy, you will be presented with an opportunity to review and accept or deny the privacy policy associated with any Third Party Software Application.  For each Third Party Application that you add to your profile, you should review the Third Party Applications Developer’s policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of Personal Information.

3. PASSIVE DATA COLLECTION

Like most web-based services, Project Eve automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser when you use the Project Eve Website. We may use a variety of methods, including clear GIFs (also known as “web beacons”), and “cookies” to collect this information. The information that we collect with these automated methods may include, for example, your IP address, Ning cookie information, a unique device or user ID, browser type, system type, the content and pages that you access on Project Eve, and the “referring URL” (i.e., the page from which you navigated to the Project Eve Website). We also use cookies on Project Eve to recognize your account credentials (e.g., for sign in purposes), and to store references to your account credentials and session validators on your hard drive. We may also include clear GIFs in some of our email messages in order to learn whether the messages are delivered and opened. We use passively-collected information to administer, operate, and improve Eve’s Website and our other services and systems, and to provide services and information that are tailored to you. If any information gathered through passive means by Project Eve is combined with Personal Information, we treat the combined information as Personal Information under this Privacy Policy. Otherwise, Project Eve uses information collected by passive means in aggregated or other non-personally identifiable forms. Project Eve and other third-party research companies also collect information. Also, please be aware that other third parties, including advertising servers, advertising networks, web analytic services (such as Google Analytics) or may set cookies or use other means of passively collecting information about your use of their services on Project Eve which as noted above is a part of the Ning Platform. We do not have access to, or control over, these cookies or other means of passive data collection. You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third parties. We cannot control the activities of such third parties.
