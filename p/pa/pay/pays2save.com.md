# Privacy Policy | Pays2Save

**Personal Information Collection**

Pays 2 Save may collect personal information such as Name, email address, and mailing address. This information will be needed if you enter any of the giveaways voluntarily. I will not sell or exchange your personal information.

**Use of Cookies**

Pays 2 Save has no control over cookies that are used by third-party advertisers. They automatically receive your IP address. The use of cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons may be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

My advertising partneres include, and are not limited too, MySavings Media, Logical Media, WeUseCoupons.com, Amazon.com, and Google Adsense.

You can consult the privacy policies of these third-party ad serves for more information. Pays 2 Save can not control the activities of these advertisers or web sites.

**Links to web sites**

Pays 2 Save is selective to which sites I provide links too, but I do not control these web sites, nor do I endorse or otherwise accept responsibility for the content or privacy policies of those sites. I strongly encourage you to review the privacy policies posted on third party sites. I also would like to make a suggestion that you make another email account for these third party sites. Because you will get a lot of emails and you do not want to these in your personal email account.
