PAYCHEX.COM PRIVACY STATEMENT
Paychex, Inc. is committed to providing cost-effective payroll, payroll tax preparation, and human resource solutions for your small- or medium-sized business. In an effort to meet that commitment, you will have to provide to Paychex business, financial, and/or personal information about yourself and your employees. Your privacy and the privacy of the information provided, is important to us. Paychex strictly protects the security of your business, financial, and personal information and honors your choices for its intended use. Paychex uses reasonable care to protect your data from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

At times, Paychex will provide you with links to other web sites. This notice applies only to information collected by Paychex for use in providing Paychex services. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This Privacy Statement may be revised from time to time without notice.

THE INFORMATION PAYCHEX COLLECTS
This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on Paychex' web sites.

You may sign up for Paychex' services either, in person, by telephone, or online. If you use our online forms, we require you to provide information regarding your business. We may use this information to contact you regarding Paychex' products and services. If you choose not to provide business information online, all of Paychex' services may be available to you through its branches. Click here for a list of Paychex branch locations.

THE WAY PAYCHEX USES INFORMATION COLLECTED
Paychex uses the information provided on its web sites to perform the services you request. We limit the collection of private customer information. We only collect information that we need to administer and improve the Paychex' Web sites, to provide superior service to our customers, and to fulfill any legal and regulatory requirements.

Paychex does not sell or disseminate customer information to any third parties under any circumstances, except to fulfill legal and regulatory requirements, and to facilitate customer requested transactions.

Cookies:
A "cookie" is a piece of data that our Web sites may provide to your browser while you are at our site. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site. Once the user closes their browser, the cookie simply terminates. If a user rejects the cookie, they will be able to browse our site but will be unable to use any online application. With or without cookies, our Web sites track usage information. This information is analyzed on an aggregated basis.

SECURITY
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information provided online, Paychex has put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information it collects online. Paychex uses industry recognized security safeguards to help protect your business, financial, and personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Whenever you provide business, financial, or personal information online, Paychex will provide you with access to a server that allows for the encryption of your data as it is transmitted to Paychex.
