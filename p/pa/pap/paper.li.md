# Privacy

## Privacy policy

Last revised March 02, 2010

Welcome to [www.paper.li](http://paper.li/) a service operated by SmallRivers SA ("SmallRivers").

When you use "www.paper.li" or register to "www.paper.li" to generate a new Paper Edition, you provide "www.paper.li" and SmallRivers with personally identifiable information (your "www.paper.li" information). This Policy explains the information practices that apply to your "www.paper.li" information. This Policy does not apply to information collected by third party Web sites linked to or otherwise accessible from offerings presented through "www.paper.li" pages and SmallRivers. The information collected or received by third party Web sites is subject to their own privacy policies.

What information do we collect and store ?

Globally, we collect and store the data we need to run pertinently the service. We may gather and process information about you, including (but not limited to) information in the following categories:

(a) For "www.paper.li" registered users ("www.paper.li" Members): Identification data you provided the thrid party service you are using to log in to "www.paper.li" (e.g. for Twitter: user name, location, user info, etc) ;

(b) For "www.paper.li" readers (unregistered): Electronic identification data (addresses IP);

(c) Survey result and usage information;

(d) Traffic data (data processed for the purpose of the conveyance of the SmallRivers Service or the billing thereof, including, but not limited to, the number of views, location of viewers, upload behavior, users' behavior linked to rating and commenting, etc.).

How do we use this information and for what purpose?

Our primary purpose in collecting information is to provide you with a safe, smooth, efficient, and customized experience. "www.paper.li" and SmallRivers collect and process, or have third party service providers acting on SmallRivers' behalf collecting and processing, personal data relating to you, as permitted or necessary to:

• provide rich media web services, in particular to convey the content you upload and organize by means of the "www.paper.li" Service;

• inform you about service updates and faults;

• verify your identity;

To whom do we transfer this information?

Except as provided below, "www.paper.li" and SmallRivers shall not sell, rent, trade or otherwise transfer any personal and/or traffic data or content to any third party without your explicit permission, unless it is obliged to do so under applicable laws or by order of the competent authorities.

In order to provide you with the "www.paper.li" service you have requested, SmallRivers may sometimes, if necessary, share your data with partner service providers and/or agents, and/or the third party banking organization or other providers of payment services.

Your information may be stored and processed in any country in which SmallRivers and selected partners maintain facilities, including outside of the EU. In this regard, or for purposes of sharing or disclosing data in accordance to the above, SmallRivers reserves the right to transfer information outside of your country. By using the SmallRivers Service you consent to any such transfer of information outside of your country.

How do we protect your personal information?

SmallRivers and its authorized partners shall take appropriate organizational and technical measures to protect the personal data and traffic data provided to us/them or collected by us/them, and shall not retain it any longer than permitted in order to perform its services or as required under relevant legislation. Your personal data can only be accessed by authorized employees of SmallRivers or the concerned SmallRivers authorized partners that need to have access to this data in order to be able to fulfill their given duties.

Your feedback and comments

Email notification: SmallRivers may contact its Members to send them messages about faults and service issues. Further, SmallRivers reserves the right to use email to notify its Members of any eventual claims related to their use of the Service, including without limitation claims of violation of third party rights.

SmallRivers may also use email to keep its Members up to date with news about the SmallRivers Service. If you, as Members, do not want to be kept informed in this way, you ask SmallRivers to exclude you from the mailing list by sending an email to support@smallrivers.com.

Can this privacy statement be modified?

SmallRivers reserves the right to modify the provisions of this Privacy Statement if deemed necessary. We will inform you of these changes by publishing the revised Privacy Statement at www.smallrivers.com. The revised Privacy Statement shall become immediately effective once published. You agree that your continued use of the SmallRivers Service, shall constitute your acceptance of the Privacy Statement.

Can you access to your personal information and how to contact us?

You may oppose to the processing by SmallRivers of your personal data. However, in this case, SmallRivers will not be in a position to provide the SmallRivers Service.

You can exercise your right to oppose to the processing detailed in the Privacy Statement by contacting SmallRivers at support@smallrivers.com.

How long is your data kept by SmallRivers?

SmallRivers will retain your information for as long as is necessary to: (1) fulfill any of the Purposes of "www.paper.li" service or (2) comply with applicable legislation, regulatory requests and relevant orders from competent courts.

Governing Law

By accessing the "www.paper.li", you and SmallRivers agree that all matters relating to your access to, or use of, "www.paper.li" shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Switzerland, without giving effect to any principles of conflicts of law.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Policy or on the "www.paper.li" service, please contact us at: support@smallrivers.com
