Privacy Policy  
 

This Privacy Policy discloses the privacy practices for www.PapaLouie.com. This policy may be updated from time to time, and the revised policy will appear on this website.

The Papa Louie website located at www.PapaLouie.com is run by Flipline IDS LLC under the trade name of Flipline Studios (www.flipline.com).

Personal Information
You can browse our website without providing any personal information. Should you decide to use the Contact form on our website, the information you provide, including your name and email address if you choose to provide them, will be sent to Flipline Studios. This information is used only for addressing your inquiries and concerns, and will not be sold to third parties.

You may also have the option of providing your email address or other personal information when subscribing to our newsletter. This information is used solely by Flipline Studios for sending our newsletter and is not sold or used for any other purpose.

High Scores
When playing games on the Papa Louie website, you have the option of submitting your nickname and score to the "Leader Board". The nickname you provide can be seen by other players who view the in-game Leader Board, as well as website visitors to www.PapaLouie.com. Please do not provide any personal identifiable information on the Leader Board. The Leader Board collects your provided nickname, your score, and the date submitted, and does not collect any other personal information.

Flash Shared Objects / Flash Cookies
When playing games on the Papa Louie website, your progress is saved on your local machine as a Flash Shared Object. This contains your score, inventory, level progress, and leader board nickname (if provided), and does not contain any other personal information. This data is stored on your machine and is not available to anyone else.

HTML Cookies
We use cookies on www.PapaLouie.com to store your preference regarding the "Style" theme you use on the site (jungle backdrops, snow backdrops, etc.). This information is stored on your local machine and is not available to anyone else.

External Links
Our website may contain links to external websites that are not covered by this Privacy Policy. In addition, pre-roll and in-game advertisements on www.PapaLouie.com may contain links to third-party websites that are not covered by this Privacy Policy. Please review the individual Privacy Policies for outside websites.
