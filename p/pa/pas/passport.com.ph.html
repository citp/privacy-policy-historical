Privacy Policy

This policy determines how the information we receive about you when you visit the main DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM website (www.passport.com.ph) or any of our pages (collectively "the DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM website(s)") is handled. Protection of your privacy and personal data is of utmost importance to us. Protecting your privacy and your personal information is an important aspect of the way we create, organize and implement our activities on-line and off-line.

Linkages Characteristics

The specific practices outlined in this privacy statement apply to the DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM website(s). You are encouraged to review the privacy statements of other bodies/entities when visiting their websites.

Purpose Specifications and Data Collection

Overall, you can browse the DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM website(s) without disclosing any information about yourself. If you visit the DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM website(s) to read information, we will collect and store only the following information that is automatically recognized: the date and time, the originating IP address, the domain name, the type of browser and operating system used (if provided by the browser), the URL of the referring page (if provided by the browser).

If you visit the DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM website(s) to get an appointment schedule, we collect and store as mandated by the DFA the following information: Application Type, Last Name, First Name, Middle Name, Birthday (mm/dd/yyyy),Birth Place, Birth Right, Gender, Civil Status, Father's Last Name, Father's First Name, Father's Middle Name, Father's Citizenship, Mother's Last Maiden Name, Mother's First Name, Mother's Middle Maiden Name, Mother's Citizenship, Purpose of Travel, Acquired Citizenship, Foreign Passport Holder?, Occupation, Office Number, Office Address, Phone Number, Mobile Number, Email, Philippine Address, City, Province, Landmarks, Deliver My Passport, Processing Type

Cookies and Other Technologies

As described above, we sometimes collect anonymous information from visits to our website(s) to help us provide better service. We use the information that we collect to measure the number of visitors to the different areas of our website(s), and to help us make our website(s) more useful to visitors. This includes analysing these logs periodically to measure the traffic through our servers, the number of pages visited and the level of demand for pages and topics of interest. The logs may be preserved indefinitely and used at any time and in any way to prevent security breaches and to ensure the integrity of the data on our servers.

We collect the anonymous information we mentioned above through the use of various technologies, one of which is called "cookies". A cookie is an element of data that a website can send to your browser, which may then be stored on your hard drive. The following information is stored in the cookies:
  • IP address
  • Unique ID
  • Session Token
We do not associate the information stored in a visitor's cookie with any other personal data about that visitor. All our employees and customer care officers who have access to personal data and are associated with the processing of that data are bound and obliged to respect the confidentiality of data.

Visitor's Choice

Registering to get an appointment in our website(s) is optional. If you choose not to register or provide personal information, you can still use the DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM website(s). However, you will not be able to schedule an appointment or receive e-mail alerts. The DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM views the applicant’s information that it collects as a valuable and confidential asset of which we take great care to ensure its safety in coordination with the Department of Foreign Affairs and the laws pertaining to passport application and processing.

Security

We intend to protect the quality and integrity of your personal information. The DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM has implemented technologies and security policies to protect the stored personal data of our users from unauthorized access, improper use, alteration, unlawful or accidental destruction and accidental loss. We will continue to enhance our security procedures, as new technology becomes available. DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM employees and processors who have access to personal data are bound and obliged to respect the privacy of our visitors and the confidentiality of their personal data.

Links

Within the DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM website(s), you may find links to third party websites. For example, our NSO Online website includes a link to a third party Web server for NSO requests by phone and online payments. Please note that the DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM is not responsible for the privacy policies or content on third party sites.

Consent Waiver

By using our website, you understand that you are signifying your unequivocal consent to the disclosure, collection and use of the personal information and data described above. Your consent effectively constitutes a waiver of any and all privacy rights pertaining to the disclosure, collection and use of the personal information and data under the specific terms and conditions of DFA Passport Appointment’s Internet Privacy Policy.

Changes in Privacy Policy

As the DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM creates new service upgrades that may generate a need to amend this privacy statement, the DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM will highlight such changes on the DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM website home page. This privacy policy is effective as of August 1, 2011. If changes in the policy are made, the revised version will be posted here. We suggest that you check here periodically to know the up-to-date version of our privacy policy. However, any changes will not be retroactively applied nor alter how we handle previously collected information.

Ang proteksyon ng inyong pribado at personal na datos ay mahalaga sa amin. Ang pagprotekta sa inyong pribado at personal na impormasyon ay isang mahalagang aspeto sa paraan ng aming pag-likha, pag-ayos at pagpapatupad ng aming mga gawain sa on-line at off-line.

Ugnayang Katangian

Ang mga particular na gawain na nakabalangkas sa loob ng pribadong patakaran na ito ay inaaplay sa DFA PASSPORT APOINTMENT SYSTEM website(s). Ikaw ay hinihikayat na suriin ang mga pribadong patakaran ng iba pang mga katawan / entity tuwing bumibisita sa kanilang mga website.

Hangaring Pagtutukoy at Koleksyon ng Datos

Sa pangkalahatan, maaari mong suriin ang website ng DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM nang hindi nagbibigay ng anumang impormasyon tungkol sa iyong sarili. Kung binisita mo ang DFA Passport Appointment System na website upang magbasa ng impormasyon, aming kinokolekta at kinukuha lamang ang sumusunod na impormasyon na awtomatikong kinikilala : ang petsa at oras , ang pinagmulang IP adres , pangalan ng domain , ang uri ng browser at operating system na ginagamit (kung ibinigay ng browser) , ang URL ng nagre-refer na pahina (kung ibinigay ng browser).

Cookies at Iba Pang Teknolohiya

Tulad ng sinasaad, kinokolekta minsan ang anonymous na impormasyon mula sa mga pagbisita sa aming (mga) website upang matulungan kaming magbigay ng mas mahusay na serbisyo. Ginagamit namin ang impormasyon na kinokolekta upang masukat ang bilang ng mga bisita sa iba't ibang lugar ng aming (mga) website, at upang matulungan kaming magawa ang aming (mga) website na higit na kapaki-pakinabang sa mga bisita. Kabilang dito ang pag-aaral sa mga tala ng pagbisita upang sukatin ang trapiko sa pamamagitan ng aming mga server, ang bilang ng mga pahina na binisita at antas ng pangangailangan para sa mga pahina at paksa ng interes. Ang mga tala ay maaaring hindi mapangalagaan walang katiyakan at ginagamit sa anumang oras at sa anumang paraan upang maiwasan ang paglabag ng seguridad at upang matiyak ang integridad ng datos sa aming mga server.

Kinokolekta namin ang anonymous na impormasyon na aming nabanggit sa itaas sa pamamagitan ng paggamit ng iba't ibang teknolohiya, kung saan ang isa ay tinatawag na "cookies". Ang cookie ay isang elemento ng datos ng isang website na maaaring ipadala sa iyong browser, maaaring pagkatapos ay naka-imbak sa iyong hard drive. Ang sumusunod na impormasyon ay naka-imbak sa mga cookies:
  • IP address
  • Unique ID
  • Session Token
Hindi namin inuugnay ang impormasyong naka-imbak sa cookie ng isang bisita sa anumang iba pang personal na data tungkol sa bisita. Ang lahat ng aming mga empleyado at mga customer care officer na may access sa personal na datos at ang naguugnay sa pagproseso ng datos na iyon ay nakatali at obligadong respetuhin ang pagiging kompidensiyal ng datos.

Kagustuhan ng bisita/aplikante

Ang pagrerehistro upang makakuha ng appointment sa aming (mga) website ay opsyonal. Kung pinili mong hindi magparehistro o magbigay ng personal na impormasyon, maaari mo pa ring gamitin ang DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM website. Gayunpaman, hindi mo magagawang mag-iskedyul ng appointment o makatanggap ng mga alerto sa e-mail. Kinikilala ng DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM ang nakolekatang impormasyon ng mga aplikante bilang isang mahalaga at kumpidensyal na asset na lubos naming pinangangalagaan upang matiyak ang kaligtasan nito sa koordinasyon ng Kagawaran ng Ugnayang Panglabas at ang mga batas na tumutukoy sa pag-aaply at pagproseso ng pasaporte..

Seguridad

Hangarin naming maprotektahan ang kalidad at integridad ng iyong personal na impormasyon Ang DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM ay nagpatupad ng teknolohiya at mga patakaran sa seguridad upang maprotektahan ang mga naka-imbak na personal na datos ng mga gumagamit mula sa hindi awtorisadong pag-access , hindi tamang paggamit , pag-iiba , hindi makatarungan o di-sinasadyang pagkasira at di-sinasadyang pagkawala . Patuloy naming paghusayin ang aming mga pamamaraan sa seguridad, sa pagpasok ng bagong teknolohiya. Ang mga empleyado ng DFA PASSPORT APPOINTMENT SYSTEM at prosesors na may access sa personal na datos ay nakatali at obligadong respetuhin ang privacy ng aming mga bisita at ang pagiging kumpidensyal ng kanilang personal na datos.

Ang pribadong patakaran na ito ay may bisa simula Agosto 1, 2011. Kung mayroong mga pagbabago na ginagawa sa mga patakaran, ito ay ipo-post. Gayunpaman, ang anumang mga pagbabago ay hindi retroactive at inilapat na baguhin kung paano namin pinangangasiwaan ang pagkolekta ng impormasyon bago ang pagbabago sa patakaran.
