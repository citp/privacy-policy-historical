# Paul Tan's Automotive News - Privacy Policy

As part of Paultan.org Automotive Network compliance to Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (“PDPA 2010”) and commitment towards the protection of our customer’s personal information, Paultan.org automotive Network has put in place a personal data protection and compliance principles and framework (“Framework”) in which Paultan.org Automotive Network stands guided by in the provision of our products and services to our members/customers/clients (“Products and Services”).  
The Paultan.org Automotive Network consists of Paultan.org, Carbase.my, oto.my, InfoKereta.my, any other Web site that we identify as a part of the Paultan.org Automotive Network, the mobile versions of our Web Sites, and any apps that we offer (for example, including but not limited to apps available through iTunes, Google Play Store and on Facebook).  
This Privacy Policy sets out how Paultan.org Automotive Network uses and protects any information that you give Paultan.org Automotive Network when you use this website.  
Paultan.org Automotive Network is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this website, you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.

### A. COLLECTION OF PERSONAL DATA

  1. The Personal Data about you which we may collect via print and digital channels (including but not limited to Website, Social Media and Mobile Apps) includes: 
    1. information collected when you register as member or update an online profile or when you fill in or update your information with us, which may include personal data such as your photograph, name, and contact details;
    2. the contents of all printed and electronic forms or documents submitted to us via printed collateral or forms, Paultan.org Automotive Network’s Website and any digital and mobile platforms (eg. Email, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Whatsapp, Viber and any social media and apps), including identity documents and proof of address;
    3. information input or submitted to online facilities such as search tools, and calculators;
    4. personalisation preferences you select as you use the Paultan.org Automotive Network’s Website, digital and mobile platforms;
    5. information submitted if you participate in survey or competition whether via online or otherwise;
    6. any messages or comments you submit to us via the Paultan.org Automotive Network’s Website or in whatever manner, which may include personal data such as name, email address and telephone number;
    7. information obtained independently by Paultan.org Automotive Network from other lawful sources;
    8. surveys conducted to better target our contents to our audience; and
    9. the contents of all forms and/or documents submitted and/or collected by Paultan.org Automotive Network.



### B. PURPOSES OF PROCESSING

  1. By providing any of your Personal Data to Paultan.org Automotive Network whether via online or otherwise, you hereby agree that Paultan.org Automotive Network shall collect, retain and use the Personal Data for the following purposes: 
    1. providing you with services or benefits under any of the Paultan.org Automotive Network’ s business or policies; and/or
    2. processing your application under any of the Paultan.org Automotive Network’s programme; and/or
    3. where relevant, marketing of goods and services and sending any updates, new products, special offers, advertisements about goods and services of interest to you, promotional material and/or commercial material to you (including emails, short message services or other means) and/or to be used in, to provide and/or improve the services of Paultan.org Automotive Network, processing invoices and payments (including card payments) and providing customized listings of Deal and Dealerships based on your preference and locations and other services to enhance and support the relationship of Paultan.org Automotive Network and you; and/or
    4. performing statistical analysis for various objectives in Paultan.org Automotive Network and providing this information within Paultan.org Automotive Network members; and/or
    5. purposes connected with the operation, administration, development, record-keeping or enhancement of Paultan.org Automotive Network’s business including for the purpose of which is relevant to support and/or assist in any of Paultan.org Automotive Network’s businesses or Paultan.org Automotive Network’s Website; and/or
    6. purposes connected with the administration, management and/or enforcement of the commercial transaction and for contact and communication with you; and/or
    7. purposes connected with user car reviews ownership verification; and or
    8. where required by law, where Paultan.org Automotive Network consider that such use or disclosure is necessary to respond to any claims or legal process, or where Paultan.org Automotive Network suspects that fraud or unlawful activity has been, is being or may be engaged in; and/or
    9. where a third party requires in order for such third party to perform functions or services as required by Paultan.org Automotive Network; and/or
    10. enabling the Paultan.org Automotive Network or where a third party requires in order for such third party to perform functions or obligations as required under the laws, rules, regulations, by laws and/or guidelines (whether or not having the force of law) or as required by any governmental and/or non-governmental authorities, agencies or departments or a third party requires in order to ensure compliance with agreement(s) or document(s); and/or
    11. making such disclosures as may be required for any of the above purposes or by law; and/or
    12. to access logs, search strings (if entered), comments and spam moderation; and/or
    13. customer loyalty programmes.
  2. If you do not consent to Paultan.org Automotive Network processing your Personal Data for the purposes listed in Section 2, please notify Paultan.org Automotive Network using the contact details stated in Section (P) below.

  1. When you access Paultan.org Automotive Network’s Website, Paultan.org Automotive Network may automatically (i.e., not by registration) collect non-personal data (e.g. type of Internet browser and operating system used, domain name of the website from which you came, number of visits, average time spent on the site, pages viewed).

### J. COOKIES

  1. When you view Paultan.org Automotive Network’s Website, Paultan.org Automotive Network may store some data on your computer in the form of a “cookie” to automatically recognize your personal computer next time you visit.
  2. Cookies can help Paultan.org Automotive Network in many ways, for example, by allowing Paultan.org Automotive Network to tailor a website to better match your interests or to store your password to save you having to re-enter it each time.
  3. If you do not wish to receive cookies, please configure your Internet browser to erase all cookies from your computer’s hard drive, block all cookies or to receive a warning before a cookie is stored.

  3. A username and password may be essential for you to use some sections of the Paultan.org Automotive Network’s Website. For your own protection, the Paultan.org Automotive Network’s requires you to keep these confidential and to change your password regularly (if required).
  4. Unfortunately the transmission of information via the internet is not completely secure. Although we will do our best to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee the security of your data transmitted to our site; any transmission is at your own risk.
