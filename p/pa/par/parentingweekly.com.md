# ParentingWeekly | Privacy Policy

ParentingWeekly, PreconceptionWeekly, PregnancyWeekly, and BabyWeekly ("ParentingWeekly") are committed to protecting your privacy and will not sell, share, or trade any personal information you provide with third parties. By using ParentingWeekly, you accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

**Information You Provide**  
Some personal information is gathered when you subscribe to our services. You will be asked for your name, e-mail, zip code, and other personally identifiable information to benefit from various site features such as a personalized start page, newsletter subscriptions, and community discussion boards. In addition, we may ask you for personal information at other times, including (but not limited to) when you answer a survey, enter a sweepstakes, contest or promotion, and when you report a problem with our site or services via email, feedback forums and community discussion boards.

**Information We Collect**  
We receive and collect information whenever you navigate through our Web site. As part of providing customizable and personalized services, ParentingWeekly uses cookies - a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a Web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. All areas where you are prompted to log-in or that are personalized require you to accept cookies. Cookies are used to help us provide relevant information and reduce data entry to create a personalized experience for you. We also use cookies to measure traffic patterns in aggregate so that we can make each new experience a better one.

**Use of Information**  
ParentingWeekly's primary goal in collecting personal information is to provide you, the user, with a valuable experience on our site. We may use the information you provide and may make full use of all anonymous information to optimize our site usability for our visitors. ParentingWeekly may combine data for such purposes as responding to your requests, personalizing content and services to your preferences, and improving your experience. Any user statistics that we may provide to prospective advertisers or partners regarding product or service usage on ParentingWeekly are aggregate only and do not include any personally identifiable information about any individual user. 

**Disclosure of Information**  
As a general rule, we will not disclose any personally identifiable information that you give to us via this site to any third parties except when we have you permission or under special circumstances, such as when we believe in good faith that the law requires it. We guarantee that your personal information will be kept strictly confidential, and we will never sell, share, or trade this information. The use of mailing information for prizes or gifts will be limited to such purpose.

**Intended Web Site Use**  
ParentingWeekly is an interactive, online resource for all your stages of parenting whether you are thinking of starting a family, actively trying to conceive, or expecting your first. ParentingWeekly is not intended for children under the age of 13.

**Public Areas**  
Our site has public areas that include our community forums, message boards, and blogs. Please remember that any information you voluntarily share in these areas is for public viewing. We encourage active participation in community discussions, but advise our visitors to use their discretion when posting to public areas. You are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your passwords and/or any account information.

**User Account Information & Security**  
You may update or correct information related to your account by updating your profile, accessible via the homepage after you are logged in. ParentingWeekly user accounts are secured by a unique email and member-created password. We keep your personal information strictly confidential and take reasonable steps to ensure your personal information is kept private. Unfortunately, no data transmissions over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100 percent secure.
