Parrot Privacy Policy

Scope: Parrot is committed to respecting your right to privacy. Our privacy policy is designed to explain how we collect, use and protect your personal information.

Personal information: means any information that may be used to directly or indirectly identify an individual, prospect, customer or business contact.

Collection of information: we may collect personally identifiable information, such as your surname, first name, age, gender, postal address, email address, telephone number, demographic information, preferences, interests and favourites, when you participate in online questionnaires on our websites, enter promotions, register for a newsletter, request information on our products and services, ask for demonstrations or request technical assistance. Your personal information may be kept in identifiable or aggregate form (such that individuals cannot be identified).

Use of information: Parrot may use your personal information when:
• Confirming your registration on the www.parrotpartners.com, www.certified.parrot.com, www.parrotoem.com, www.parrotcorp.com and www.parrot.com websites, and any other websites that Parrot might create
• Registering your purchased products
• Inviting you to take part in games, competitions and draws
• Asking you to take part in a survey on our products or services
• Inviting you to test our new products
• Sending our newsletters or information concerning our products and services
• Developing and testing new products
• Improving customer relations, especially in terms of after-sales service

Parrot will not use your personal information for commercial purposes, and in particular will not rent or share any of your personal information with non-affiliated companies.

Information sharing and disclosure: your personal information may be disclosed and used by affiliates of the Parrot Group worldwide, on condition that the necessary permission has been received if applicable. We will only disclose your personal information outside the group to any providers contracted to process your personal information on behalf of the Parrot Group. In such cases, we will ensure that the appropriate measures are taken to protect your personal information and guarantee the confidentiality thereof.

Your rights: you have the right to access, correct and delete your personal information or withdraw your permission at any time by writing to our Marketing Department at webmaster@parrot.com or contact@parrotpartners.com, depending on the site that you are visiting, or by disabling such technologies as cookies.


Compliance with legislation governing personal data protection: all personal information collected by Parrot on its websites is used and treated in accordance with European standards governing personal data protection and in pursuance of applicable local legislation and laws, such as may be amended from time to time.


Cookies: a cookie is a small text file, containing such information as your IP address and operating system, that is stored on your computer's hard drive and which helps us to recognise your browser and improve your online experience. Cookies do not contain any personal information. Browsers can be configured to inform you when a cookie is received. This allows you to accept or decline cookies.


Links to other websites: the Parrot website may contain links to other websites over which Parrot exercises no control and which are not covered by this privacy policy. If you use a link on our website to access other sites, the operators of such sites may collect personal information for their own needs and in accordance with their own privacy policy.

Security: Parrot ensures the security of its information systems to protect your personal information and takes the necessary precautions to safeguard against any unauthorised access or disclosure.

Changes to this privacy policy: Parrot reserves the right to update and revise this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in legislation or any other developments. Any changes made to our privacy policy will be published on this page, containing the "last updated" date.
