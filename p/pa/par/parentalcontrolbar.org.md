# 

We at Website Ratings and Advisory Council respect your concerns about privacy
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the
choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.
We do not collect any personal information. Your email address is not stored on our server for any purpose. Should you need your password
reset, you would contact us with a working email address for us to set a reset file to.
Our Commitment to Data Security
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate
physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information collected for the purpose of resetting passwords
only.
How to Contact Us
Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please call us at (305) 531-2979 or send us an email to
info@wraac.org.
