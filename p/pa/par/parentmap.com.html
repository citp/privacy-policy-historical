Our Online Privacy Policy
You know and trust ParentMap newsmagazine, and ParentMap.com, and we value your trust. We are committed to maintaining the privacy of all personal information that you provide to us.

Information we collect

We receive information in several ways:
  • You may have subscribed to our magazine or newsletter.
  • You may have filled out a “Contact Information” form when visiting our booth at an event.
  • You may have asked to receive information in the past.
  • You may be an advertiser.
  • You may be a present or past sponsor of a ParentMap event.

We may collect the following information:
  • Your name
  • Your address
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • How many children you have
  • The birth years of your children


How we use the information collected
The information we collect allows us to email you information on upcoming events that may be appropriate for you and your family. It allows us to send you notifications about anything new and exciting at ParentMap newsmagazine or ParentMap.com. We may occasionally conduct online surveys and want to offer you the opportunity to participate, if interested. It allows us to provide advertisers and potential advertisers with aggregate information about our user base and usage patterns.

Cookies
When you visit our Web site, we may pass cookies to your computer. A cookie is a string of information that is sent by a Web site and stored on your hard drive or temporarily in your computer's memory, so that the site can deliver content appropriate for you.

Security
Personal information, once collected, is never shown on our Web site. We do not sell or otherwise share information with other parties. Our email list is hosted and managed by MailerMailer. They maintain the Web site, and we are not privy to any passwords.

Privacy Policy Changes
We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes here. We will never change our policy of maintaining the privacy of your personal information.

Unsubscribe Here
If you are not interested in receiving any information from ParentMap.com in the future, you may unsubscribe here.
