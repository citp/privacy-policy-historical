# Help Center Privacy Policy - PandaHallStock.html

Date Updated: Jun 24, 2011 

**1\. Our Commitment to Privacy**

Your privacy is of great importance. To better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and your different choices of the way your information is collected and used.

**2\. The Information We Collect**

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the PandaHallStock.com

For the pages that you can order products, make requests, and register to receive materials, the types of business and personal information collected at these pages are as follows.  
**    > Business Name  
   > Contact Name  
   > Address  
   > Email address  
   > Phone number  
   > PayPal information**

**3\. The Way We Use Information**

The information you provided and we collected is only used to complete your order. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order.

We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties.

We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers.

We'll need your email address if you wish to receive our newsletters or use our service to contact a retailer. All other information is optional. Each newsletter will tell you how to unsubscribe if you want to stop getting our mailings or need to change your email address.

Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.

**4\. Our Commitment to Data Security**

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
