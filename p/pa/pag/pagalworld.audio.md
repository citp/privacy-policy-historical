Cookies & 3rd Party Advertisements

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on tamildada.info. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information.
