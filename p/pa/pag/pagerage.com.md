# Yontoo Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy

Yontoo Technology, Inc. ("Yontoo") respects the privacy of its users and non-users. This privacy policy discloses any information we obtain and how we use it. Yontoo also goes to great lengths to ensure that the information and content that users add to our system is secure and only accessible to intended users. Yontoo may modify this policy from time to time, and the changes will be posted here.

**1\. PUBLIC CONTENT**  
PUBLIC CONTENT FROM PUBLIC WEB SITES CAN BE RETRIEVED AND DISPLAYED ON THE YONTOO LAYER. The definition of public content is any information on the Web that is accessible by anyone without special user credentials.

Displaying collected information or content on Yontoo Layers is for the sole purpose of content syndication. You are not permitted to republish and modify any copyrighted material without owner’s permissions. In addition, you can only direct our machines to collect information from you, your friends, and/or others who have approved your access. Yontoo does not assume any responsibility if you choose to abuse the system.

Yontoo may collect and analyze your usage patterns that are not personally identifiable. These non-personally-identifiable data are used to improve our services, and may be shared with third parties in the future.

**2\. PRIVATE CONTENT**  
You are not required to provide any personally-identifiable information to use Yontoo. You can choose to provide third-party login credentials to enable certain Layer Apps that require access to private content from third-party Web sites. By entering in your third-party usernames passwords, you permit Yontoo access to all private content accessible on third-party Web sites, however, this content will NEVER BE TRANSFERRED OR SOLD TO ANYONE. Private content will only be used in ways that the user establishes within Layer App settings or has otherwise agreed to.

To protect and preserve the privacy of the original content, Yontoo authenticates all users’ access to private content with users’ personal credentials. Users can only view third-party content that they have access to on the third-party Web sites. All passwords and personal information are hashed and stored with the strictest security standards. Yontoo reserves the right to track and use aggregate information about our customers and website visitors for a variety of purposes. Such aggregate information does not identify individual customers. We may share such aggregate and related demographic information with third parties. 

**3\. BROWSER COOKIES**  
Yontoo may use cookies to maintain your login session, but does not store any personally-identifiable information. Cookies are small text files on your computer that store data about your visit. Yontoo does not share cookies with third parties.

**4\. EMAIL COMMUNICATION**  
Yontoo reserves the right to send you certain communications relating to our services, such as administrative messages, without your prior permission. We plan to do this sparingly.

**5\. LINKS TO OTHER WEB SITES**  
Yontoo.com contains links to other Web sites as will the Yontoo Layers App on other web sites. Yontoo does not control, operate, or endorse any information or services of such third-party Web sites. The use of any information provided to these third parties will be governed by their respective privacy policies.

**6\. INFORMATION DISCLOSURES**  
Yontoo reserves the rights to disclose personally-identifiable information in the following cases:  
a. To respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal processes. Or to establish and exercise our legal rights to defend against legal claims;  
b. If Yontoo acquired by or merged with another company, we might transfer your personal and non-personal information to that company. When this happens, Yontoo will notify you before any information is transferred.

We allow third-party companies to serve ads and/or collect certain anonymous information when you visit our web site. These companies may use non-personally identifiable information (e.g., click stream information, browser type, time and date, subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over) during your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you. These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit www.networkadvertising.org. 

**7\. NO ERROR-FREE PERFORMANCE**  
Yontoo does not guarantee error-free performance. Yontoo will always try its best to protect its user and data privacy; however, it may not catch all unintended privacy issue. Yontoo is not responsible for any damages, including incidental, consequential, or punitive damages, relating to the practices described in this policy.

Use of Yontoo Technology, Inc.’s services is subject to your compliance with those terms and conditions, which constitute a legal agreement between you and Yontoo Technology, Inc. If you do not agree with the terms and conditions of either document, please discontinue using any of Yontoo Technology, Inc.’s services.
