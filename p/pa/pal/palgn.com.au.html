PALGN Privacy Policy

Personal Information
PALGN will record any personal information that you will provide to the site. When you provide information to PALGN you will be told if this information is going to be on public display or otherwise. Please note that information entered for display on the forums system will be viewable by anyone who visits the site and we can not restrict access to this.

Storing Other Personal Information
PALGN may also record other information such as IP addresses and visiting time in order to trace illegal activities (e.g. stolen accounts). IP addresses will only be available to our staff (administrators, moderators, editors). Any other information stored will be obvious if it is for public display or otherwise.

Third Parties
Any information PALGN stores, which is not for public viewing (i.e. private email addresses) will NOT be sold or given to any third party companies.

Cookie Usage
PALGN will use cookies for storing information regard your usage of the site. This will allow you to easily view our site and log in for your personal areas. You may disable these from your browser, but it will cause problems if you continue to try to view the site.

Disclosure
PALGN openly volunteers the above information and any other such information pertaining to usage of the PALGN site. If you have any questions pertaining to this privacy policy and its contents, please direct your correspondence to admin@palgn.com.

Change to this policy
If this privacy policy is changed, we will post these changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. Regardless of later updates, we will never use the information you submit under this current policy in a new way without first providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prevent that use, via the email you provide to PALGN.
