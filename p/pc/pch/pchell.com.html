Privacy Policy for www.pchell.com

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at mark@webtrail.com.

At www.pchell.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by www.pchell.com and how it is used.

Log Files

Like many other Web sites, www.pchell.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Email Newsletter


PCHell.com does have an email newsletter that collects your name and email address for distribution. The mailing list is controlled by Aweber and every newsletter has an option at the bottom to unsubscribe from the newsletter. The subscriber information for the newsletter is never sold to outside contacts and is only used for the purpose of distributing the ezine.

Cookies and Web Beacons

www.pchell.com does not use cookies.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.

California Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

Because we value your privacy we have taken the necessary precautions to be in compliance with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. We therefore will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your consent.

Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older.
