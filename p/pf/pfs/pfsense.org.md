# pfSense Privacy Policy - pfsense.org

Privacy Policy

This is the web site of Electric Sheep Fencing, LLC (hereafter referred to as ESF).

We can be reached via:  
e-mail: [supportinfo@pfsense.org](mailto:supportinfo@pfsense.org)  
telephone: +1 512-900-2546  


## General Information

We are committed to ensuring the privacy of our customers, partners, employees and users. Our privacy statement has been established to discuss the collection and use of personal information that may be collected and utilized by ESF from time to time. Information about you may be collected when you visit our website, contact us by phone, email, fax or in person.

The most important things to know are:

  * **The information we collect is not shared with other unauthorized organizations.**
  * **We do not SPAM our customers.**



## Type of Information Collected

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name, but not the e-mail address (where possible). ESF will ask you to register and provide information when you sign up as a forum member, purchase products, services or request support. ESF will collect information necessary to use our products, forums, support and services. We may collect your name, address, phone number, fax number, email address, login id, password, contact preferences, IP address and referring URL.

## What We Do With Your Information

ESF uses the information you provide to deliver products and services to you. We provide forum access, answer your questions, communicate with you on support, customer service issues, account issues, billing issues and the like. Occasionally we may contact you with information we believe you will want to know, such as product upgrades, updates, bugs, product announcements, change in service terms, etc.

From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our web site to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our web site periodically.

Customers may prevent their information from being used for purposes other than those for which it was originally collected by calling us at the number provided above.

## Security of Your Information

ESF takes reasonable efforts to secure your personal data. We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site. We limit access to your data to those individual and agents having a need to know.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above addresses or phone number.

## External Links

The very open nature of our forum allows any registered user to post links to third-party websites, which are not associated in any way with ESF. If you click on those links you will leave our website. ESF does not control, endorse or make representations about third party websites.

## Changes to This Privacy Statement

ESF will review and periodically update this Privacy Statement. ESF reserves the right to change this privacy statement at any time. The date of the most recent change is noted here:

September 23, 2013.
