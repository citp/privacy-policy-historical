Privacy Policy
 

General

Visitors browsing the Kelley Blue Book Web site do so on a completely anonymous basis. Kelley Blue Book never collects any personal information (such as names, addresses, e-mail addresses, telephone numbers or credit card numbers) from visitors as vehicle pricing information is researched. Kelley Blue Book does, however, collect some non-specific visitor information, such as ZIP Codes and vehicle pricing requests, which are used to produce vehicle pricing reports, analyze the usage of the Kelley Blue Book Web site, and customize online advertising and promotional offers. Kelley Blue Book does aggregate non-specific visitor information for statistical purposes, such as the production of the Kelley Blue Book Top 50 Cars list. None of this information is personally identifiable to visitors.

Cookies

The Kelley Blue Book Web site makes limited use of "cookies" for the purpose of facilitating the gathering of non-specific visitor information to produce vehicle pricing reports. A cookie is a very small text file stored on a visitor's hard disk that allows a Web server to recall certain information that the visitor has already entered previously. The cookies issued from the Kelley Blue Book Web servers store a visitor's ZIP Code and contain no personal information. If a visitor wishes to not utilize cookie technology, this feature may be disabled within the visitor's Web browser

Third Party Information and Service Providers

The Kelley Blue Book privacy policy only applies to the Kelley Blue Book Web site. There are several links from the Kelley Blue Book Web site to third party information and service providers who may collect personal information from visitors in order to provide visitors with information or services. Kelley Blue Book has no control over the privacy policy of any third party company, so please examine their privacy policy prior to submitting any personal information.

Kelley Blue Book sometimes collects personally identifiable information from a visitor on behalf of a third party information and/or service provider in exchange for the information and/or services provided to the visitor by the third party. This information may include names, addresses, phone numbers, e-mail addresses and specific vehicle information. All personally identifiable information provided by visitors is used only as the visitor directs. This information becomes the property of Kelley Blue Book and/or the third party once it is received through the Kelley Blue Book Web site. The third party may use this information to process a visitor's request and provide valuable services. Any request to opt out of communications between the visitor and the third party must be made directly to the third party.

Kelley Blue Book Web site content is also featured on other Web sites. These Web sites may collect personal information from their visitors that may or may not be used in conjunction with Kelley Blue Book Web site content. Prior to supplying any personal information to any third party company linking to Kelley Blue Book, please read and understand their privacy policy.

Advertisements

Advertisements served by DoubleClick, a third party Internet advertising technology company, appear on pages throughout the Kelley Blue Book Web site. Some advertisements may request information directly from visitors or take visitors to Web sites that may request personal information. Kelley Blue Book has no control over its advertisers' privacy policies, so please examine the privacy policy of any company advertising on Kelley Blue Book prior to submitting any personal information. In addition, DoubleClick may place or recognize a unique cookie on a visitor's browser and may use information (not including any personal information such as names, addresses, e-mail addresses, telephone numbers or credit card numbers) about visits to the Kelley Blue Book Web site and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to visitors. Kelley Blue Book shares visitor Web site usage information with reputable third-party advertising companies for the purpose of targeting banner advertisements on the Kelley Blue Book Web site and other Web sites. For this purpose, Kelley Blue Book and third-party advertising companies note some of the pages visited on the Kelley Blue Book Web site through the use of pixel tags (also called "clear gifs"). The information collected by third-party advertising companies through the use of these pixel tags is not personally identifiable. Surveys

Occasionally, Kelley Blue Book, or Kelley Blue Book in conjunction with a third party, conducts surveys on the Kelley Blue Book Web site. Surveys are conducted at random and are completely voluntary to visitors. The survey may require a visitor to provide personally identifiable information in exchange for the information or services provided by the survey. Kelley Blue Book and/or the third party conducting the survey may use this information in an aggregate manner to better tailor the type of services, information, and advertising that are provided on the Kelley Blue Book Web site.

Products Sold by Kelley Blue Book

When a visitor on the Kelley Blue Book Web site purchases a product from Kelley Blue Book, the visitor is required to provide personally identifiable information along with a credit card number. This information is treated confidentially and will not be shared with any third party outside of Kelley Blue Book or the financial institutions used to process payments, except as may be required by law.
