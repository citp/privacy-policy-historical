Privacy Policy

In some cases, you may voluntarily provide personal information in response to a specific request or email conversation (example, your name as a signature on an email).  This is completely your choice and is not a requirement in corresponding with us.  If you do choose to correspond with us, we do not collect, sell or give any information to anyone for any purpose.
