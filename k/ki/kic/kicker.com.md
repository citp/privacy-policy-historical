**Stillwater Designs Privacy Policy**

Protecting your privacy is important to us at Stillwater Designs. We hope the following will help you understand how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide on our Web site. 

**Information Collection**

At times we may request that you voluntarily supply us with personal information, such as your email address and postal address, for purposes such as registering a new product, corresponding with us, registering at a site, making a purchase, entering a sweepstakes, or participating in an online survey. At no time will we share this information with anyone. 

**Information Use**

When you supply KICKER with your personal information, we may use it to register your product or to send you information/materials you request from us. We may use the information to tell you about new products, services, and offers that you may find interesting. We may also use information that you have given us to learn more who uses KICKER products so we can develop products and services better suited to our customers' needs. 

**Data Security**

KICKER is fully committed to data security. We have put in place physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information you provide to us online. However, while we make every effort to protect your personal information, we cannot ensure the security of the information you transmit to us. In this regard, we urge you to take every precaution to protect your personal data while you are on the Internet.
