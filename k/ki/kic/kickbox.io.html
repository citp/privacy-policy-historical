                                                                                  Overview.

                                                                                  Kickbox is committed to protecting the privacy of all its Web site visitors and users. Except as described in this policy, we do not share, sell, rent, trade or loan personal data, including verified email addresses, to third parties for any purpose. Additionally, except where described in this policy, Kickbox does not utilize or retain the email addresses you verify using this service. This Privacy Policy describes how Kickbox, Inc. ("Kickbox") treats information that it collects and receives related to your use of Kickbox's hosted email verification service (the "Service").

                                                                                  This Privacy Policy applies to Kickbox services only. Other sites may place their own cookie or other files on your computer, collect data or solicit personal information from you.

                                                                                  Please note that we may collect aggregate statistics for our own marketing research.

                                                                                  By using this Web site you accept that your visit and any disputes over privacy is subject to this Privacy Policy and to our License Agreement and Terms and Conditions of Use ("Terms of Use"), including the limitations on damages, arbitration, and selection of the law of the State of Texas.

                                                                                  Account Data.

                                                                                  To utilize the Service, you must create an account. Setting up an account requires you to provide your first and last name, a verifiable email address and a pass phrase ("Account Data"). Kickbox uses this information to identify you as an authorized user. This Account Data is stored on our secure servers.

                                                                                  You are entirely responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your pass phrase and user Account Data. You are responsible for all actions occurring under your account using your pass phrase.

                                                                                  Technical Support.

                                                                                  If you request technical support online, we will ask you for information necessary to complete the transaction such as your name, email address and information about your computer hardware, software, and the nature of the problem that you are experiencing. We rely on our support desk provider, Freshdesk, to store this information and use it to supply you with support and to improve the Service.

                                                                                  Posts to Support Forum and Blog.

                                                                                  Information and communications that you post to our support forum or to our blog are publicly available. If you use the support forum or post to the blog you should be aware that any personally identifiable information you submit there can be read, collected, or used by other users of these forums or by the public, and could be used to send you unsolicited messages. We are not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to submit in these forums. Kickbox reserves the right to terminate the accounts of visitors who use these services in a manner inconsistent with our Terms of Use.

                                                                                  Information that Kickbox Collects and Uses.

                                                                                  In addition to Account Data, Kickbox collects information about the nature of your usage of the Service which can include: IP addresses of users, date/time stamp, Verification Metadata and Verification Audit Data. "Verification Metadata" includes a one-way encrypted (hashed) version of email addresses sent for verification using the Service. "Verification Audit Data" includes the date/time stamp a verification has been performed and the results of the verification.

                                                                                  CSV Verification Data is stored for a period of up to 90-days on an encrypted file system. This data can include the original uploaded CSV data, including email addresses, and the Verification Audit Data.

                                                                                  This information is collected in order to render Services to you. Kickbox collects only the necessary and relevant data required to deliver, monitor, and secure Services for you.

                                                                                  Cookies.

                                                                                  Kickbox uses session cookies in the account establishment process. We also use persistent cookies to enable the "keep me logged in" feature. A session terminates when the user logs out. The session cookies are stored temporarily thereafter and self-destruct after a short period of time (normally within hours). We do not use cookies to store any personally identifiable information. If you would prefer not to accept session cookies, please turn them off in your browser. If you disable cookies, the features of the Service that require the user to be logged in will not function with cookies disabled. We recommend that you close your browser after logging out.

                                                                                  Log files.

                                                                                  As is true of most Web sites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data. We use this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze trends, to administer the site, to track users' movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user bases as a whole. We do not link this automatically collected data to personally identifiable information.

                                                                                  This Web site uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses "cookies", which are text files placed on your computer, to help the Web site analyze how users use the site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the Web site (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the Web site, compiling reports on Web site activity for Web site operators and providing other services relating to Web site activity and internet usage. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however please note that if you do this you may not be able to use the full functionality of this Web site. By using this Web site, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner and for the purposes set out above.

                                                                                  Security.

                                                                                  Kickbox limits access to Account Data, Verification Metadata, or log files to Kickbox personnel and service providers who need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs. We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. Information we collect is stored in a secure manner that is only accessible by authorized Kickbox personnel or service providers and is only disclosed under the provisions of this Privacy Policy. Our servers are located in the U.S.A.

                                                                                  Users log into the Service using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL). Passwords are encrypted before they are transmitted and are stored encrypted.

                                                                                  Email verification is done by automatic processes. Kickbox employees do not examine the contents of customer email verifications except when Kickbox in its discretion determines that it is required to address an issue with the Service, by law or government agency, or as permitted by the customer. When you upload a CSV to our Service the CSV data and verification data is transmitted to Amazon's S3 service for storage.
