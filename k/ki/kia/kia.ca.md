### How We Collect and Use Personal Information

If you are not already a customer of Kia, Kia may seek from you within this Site or otherwise certain personal information, and in particular your contact information, for the purpose of sending product brochures or promotional materials and facilitating the provision of certain services such as booking a test drive with one of our authorized Kia Dealers or applying for one of Kia’s finance/lease programs. Information such as name, mailing address, e-mail address, and any additional information you provide, is collected by Kia and is used to process your request. Kia will retain your personal information until such time as you unsubscribe from receiving such communications. By providing this information to Kia, you consent to the use and disclosure of the personal information as described in this policy.

If you are already a customer of Kia, then the personal information you provided at the time of your vehicle purchase or lease and any subsequent personal information you provide through this Site or otherwise is used by Kia to improve the services we provide to you as well as for the purpose of sending product brochures or promotional materials. Kia may use your personal contact information to notify you of any promotional offers or product information, such as Kia newsletters, and service reminders. Kia may also use your personal contact information to communicate and administer any safety and recall notices; warranty, maintenance, service and Roadside Assistance program; and generate statistical and aggregate data that may identify you personally. Similarly, to improve our delivery of products, services, and information, from time to time, Kia may ask you to reply to vehicle ownership satisfaction surveys directly or indirectly through marketing companies such as DMT Development Systems Group Inc., J.D. Power and Associates and Maritz Research. Kia will retain your personal information for the purpose of sending product brochures or promotional materials until such time as you unsubscribe from receiving such communications. For all other purposes described in this policy, Kia will retain your personal information for 25 years from the moment of your vehicle purchase or lease.

If you prefer that we do not use your personal contact information for promotional offers, product information or marketing surveys, please advise us by contacting Kia’s Privacy Officer at [privacy@kia.ca](mailto:privacy@kia.ca) or telephone (1.877.542.2886) and confirming your Vehicle Identification Number (VIN).

### Cookies and Automated Means of Collecting Information

A cookie is a small piece of information sent by a website that is saved on your device or computer’s internal storage by your browser in order to collect information about visitors to our Site. We may use cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, and related technologies to collect information such as your language of preference, viewed webpages, links clicked on our Site, browsing history and browser type and version. Our ad network providers, data providers, and other media-related or analytics services companies, including social media sites, may set and access their own cookies, pixel tags and similar technologies on your browser and they may otherwise collect or have access to information about you.

We use this information for the purpose of optimizing your experience on the Site, including through saving your Site preferences, and helping to identify products, features, and offers that may be of interest to you. Software on the Site also uses cookies to provide users an interactive session during their visit. Cookies are also used by our software for load management and database access.

For all persons that visit this Site, we collect data relating to your Site visit. This information may include your IP address, the date, time and duration of your visits as well as the web pages you consulted. We may use this information to serve advertising to you both on our Site and on third party websites, including without limitation, social media platforms. We and our service providers may use the information described above to help make our advertising more relevant to you based on your online behaviour, including your browsing and search activities on our Site. For example, if you view or search for a particular Kia vehicle, you may see advertising regarding that vehicle on our Site, or third party sites.

Most browsers allow you to disable the use of cookies, to learn how, consult your browser’s instructions. However, setting your browser to reject cookies generally hinders the browser's performance and will adversely affect your experience while using our Site.

Kia also uses certain Google Analytics features to collect information about use of this Site, but does not combine the information collected through the use of Google Analytics with personally identifiable information. You can prevent Google Analytics and other cookies from recognizing you on return visits by disabling the cookies on your browser, and you can opt out of Google Analytics and customize Google Display Network ads using the Ads Settings ([https://www.google.com/settings/ads).](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)

Any non-identifiable information gathered by Kia through your use of this Site may be used by Kia for its own marketing, promotional and product development purposes and more specifically may be stored in a database and used by Kia to identify, customize and personalize user access, and assess utilization of this Site. Such information may be shared among Kia and its affiliates, suppliers, licensors, partners and clients in furtherance of the forgoing purposes.
