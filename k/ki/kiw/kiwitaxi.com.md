# Privacy Policy Kiwitaxi

We protect your privacy very seriously and come to the question of data security of our clients. Please learn more about our privacy policy.

## What information do we collect and how we use it?

To make a reservation, we ask you to provide us with certain information. It includes the name of the person making the reservation, address, phone number, email address, credit card number and the period of its validity. We also need information about your flight, accommodation and vacation place.

All of this information is collected to process your booking and order fulfillment. We need information about your arrival and place of stay to apply to the appropriate flight car and know which hotel you need to deliver. Your phone number and email address are required in order to send you a booking voucher and to contact you in case of any unlikely changes until your departure. Your address, we need to handle the transaction on your credit card and proof of identity, so that any possible fraud.

## Special attention

Credit card details are processed in a secure way to ensure confidentiality. We use encryption protocol SSL (Secure Sockets Layer), which ensures protection of sensitive data.

## Who gets the information

LLC «KIWITAXI» collects information only to process your booking. If you make a reservation through KiwiTaxi, we guarantee that we will not share, do not sell or provide for temporary use to anyone your personal information.

## Security and data protection

We follow strict security procedures for the storage and disclosure of information provided to us in order to prevent any unauthorized access. In accordance with our security procedures, we may occasionally request proof of identity before you disclose confidential information.

## Cookies policy

Cookies — small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer hard drive. Our Cookies do not contain any personal identifying information. Cookies, if desired, can be removed from your hard drive. Most browsers automatically store Cookies, but, as a rule, you can change your browser settings to prevent this. Even without Cookies the most of the features of our site will work.

## Your Consent

By using site KiwiTaxi, you consent to the collection and use of this information by LLC «KIWITAXI» with methods described above. If we decide to change this Privacy Policy, we will post the changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances disclose it.
