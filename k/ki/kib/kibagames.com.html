Privacy Policy

1. Information on data protection

Protecting your data has highest priority. We compile user data in accordance with the currently applicable data protection laws and use this data among other things to improve our advertising campaigns. Especially in relation to our younger users, we take special care to ensure the responsible use of the data. We have set out our data protection policy to show you that you and your children can use our free website without risk.

Please never enter your personal data, such as your e-mail address, telephone number or passwords, etc., on public domains and never give this information to other users either. Our support team and other staff will also never ask you for personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains the type, scope and purposes of data collection and the use of personal data by the responsible provider KaiserGames GmbH, Schanzenstraße 6-20, D-51063 Cologne, info@kibagames.com, +49 221 7880610on this site (hereinafter called “website”).

This Privacy Policy applies to all services offered by the provider. Unless otherwise stated, this Privacy Policy exclusively governs how the provider handles your personal data. If you use third party services, only the data protection policies for these third parties apply. The provider does not check the data protection policies of third parties.

3. Data collection

Generally, you can access our website without disclosing any personal data. You are always free to decide whether you would like to communicate your data to us or not. We collect your data tofulfillthe user contract agreed with you. This includes monitoring adherence to the user conditions and rules of play.If you register on our website or would like to subscribe to our newsletter, you will need to provide personal data, such as your name, your address, your e-mail address and other personal data that we may request. In addition, individual data such as your IP address, browser type and access pages are automatically transmitted to us through your computer and stored on our server. Apart from the purpose of monitoring illegal access to our internet services, we only use this data for statistical analysis and performance-related purposes. Where possible, the data is anonymized.

Like many other companies, we use so-called "cookies" to make your visit to our website as pleasant as possible. These cookies are generally automatically deleted from your hard disk when they are no longer valid. You can set your internet browser to generally decline cookies. Please note that for technical reasons, you may no longer be able to use our website or your use may be restricted. We may also receive information from social network systems on which you use our website or if you have logged in through their interface (e.g. Facebook Connect). We only collect, process, and use personal data and data provided by you - unless already permitted by legal authorization -with your consent. If your consent is provided electronically within the scope of our website, we comply with statutory obligations to provide information.

Our website uses the Gemius Code (http://www.iabeurope.eu/corporate-members/gemius.aspx) from IAB Turkey (http://www.iabeurope.eu/national-iabs/turkey.aspx) to compile statistical parameters on the use of our website. The values are anonymized. The Gemius audience reach measurement method recognizes computer systems by either a cookie or a signature generated from various information automatically transmitted from your computer. This method does not store IP addresses; these are processed in an anonymized form only. The audience reach measurement method was developed in accordance with the data protection regulations and its objective is to statistically determine the intensity of use and the number of users of a particular website. Specific users are not identified at any stage. Your identity always remains protected. You will not receive any advertisements via the system.

You may also receive customer questionnaires by e-mail from us. The information we provide will point out how you can opt out of this. Finally, we use your data to prevent misuse of our website and to track unauthorized access.

We will, of course, not sell your personal data to third parties. These service providers only have access to your personal data to the extent which is necessary for the completion of their tasks.
