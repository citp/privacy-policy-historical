# Krkboxoffice

**"Krkboxoffice.com"**  has created a privacy policy as it respects your privacy. We would like you to feel secure and comfortable using the website.

 **Information Automatically Logged  **  
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our web site. It is used to collect demographic information and the amount of traffic we are receiving. 

 **Cookies  **  
Our site uses cookies to keep track of traffic for web site analysis. 

 **Advertisers**    
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

This site contains links to other sites. Krkboxoffice.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites.
