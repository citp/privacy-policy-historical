# Krystal Hosting - Krystal Clear

**Privacy Policy**

Krystal Solutions is commited to protecting the privacy of our customers and visitors. We follow the 8 guiding principles set down by the Data Protection Act (DPA) and as such any personal information submitted or data gathered at any Krystal Site[*](https://web.archive.org/web/20050309181132id_/http%3A//www.krystal.co.uk/network.php) is; - 

**1.** Processed fairly and lawfully;

**2.** Only collected for specified and lawful purpose(s) and is not processed in a manner that is incopatible with that purpose or purposes;

**3.** Adequate, releveant and not excessive to the purpose for which it is being held;

**4.** Accurate and up to date;

**5.** Kept no longer than it needs to be;

**6.** Proceesed in accordance to the rights of the person it applies to;

**7.** Secure;

**8.** Is not transferred outside of the European Econimic Area (EEA) unless an adequate level of security can be guaranteed there;

More information of the DPA, how it affect you, and your rights, can be found [here](http://www.dpr.gov.uk/).

**Clients**  
In accordance with the DPA, the information collected from customers when signing up is used for billing and administration purposes only and will be kept confidential. We NEVER pass on or sell your information to third parties. As a client, you have the right to view the data we have on record for you and ask that it is updated if it is innacurate. 

Clients ackowledge that the internet has a "world viewable" nature and accept sole responsible for the level of personal information displayed on their website(s)

**Visitors  
**As a visitor certain information will be recorded when you visit our sites. These include, but are not limited to; your ISP's domain, the time accessed and the pages visited. This is to analyse the web traffic and improve the delivery of content through our sites. Once again we never give away or sell this demographic information to third party companies or individuals.
