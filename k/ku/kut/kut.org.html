170 Million Americans for Public Broadcast


KUT has joined with hundreds of public media organizations across the country to educate the public on the importance of federal funding for public media. 170 Million Americans for Public Broadcasting is a national grassroots effort to rally the support of the 170 million Americans who listen to or use public media each month. We believe in the public-private partnership that has built public broadcasting—and KUT—into the essential service that it is today. To learn more check out our FAQ.

KUT Featured Program

O'Dark 30
Mondays 12am - 3am

O’Dark 30 is an exciting new addition to the KUT late night schedule. Produced by Mike Lee, David Alvarez, and Hawk Mendenhall, O’Dark 30 is an exploration of the world of independent audio production. Every week we present 3 hours of a little bit of everything from some of public radio’s best independent producers along with material from KUT’s audio archives. O’Dark 30 will operate with one rule… if it’s good, we’re gonna bring it to Austin ears.

Privacy Policy

It is KUT’s policy not to rent, sell, swap, exchange, or trade the names, email and mailing addresses, phone numbers, or any other submitted listener information with any other organization.

We consider the security of your information a top priority. That’s why all of the information you send to us from this site is secured and encrypted using 128 bit Secure Socket Layers (SSL).
