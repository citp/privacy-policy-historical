COMPLIANCE WITH THE CHILDREN'S ONLINE PRIVACY PROTECTION ACT OF 1998.

This web site complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998. Accordingly, CHILDREN UNDER 13 YEARS OF AGE MAY NOT ACCESS THIS WEB SITE WITHOUT KUDER RECEIVING EITHER: 1) WRITTEN PERMISSION FROM A PARENT OR GUARDIAN; OR 2) WRITTEN PERMISSION FROM THE SCHOOL WHERE THE CHILD IS ENROLLED AS A STUDENT, WHEREIN SUCH PERMISSION DESIGNATES KUDER TO ACT AS AN AGENT OF THE SCHOOL FOR THE COLLECTION OF THE DATA. Access to the data collection aspects of this web site is made only through a student's school. Such schools obtain access only through an agreement executed by both Kuder and the particular school. Accordingly, each school is specifically responsible for monitoring student usage of this web site. By utilizing the data collection aspects of this web site, you acknowledge that Kuder is acting as a direct agent of your school in the collection of data from students under the age of 13 and warrant that your collection and use of such data complies in all other respects with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998. As agent of your school, Kuder collects the following information from students: Name, address, school, age, gender, ethnicity, date of birth, e-mail addresses, career assessment data.

Privacy and Security

KUDER HAS TAKEN SEVERAL STEPS TO INSURE THE PRIVACY AND SECURITY OF ALL INFORMATION COLLECTED ON THIS WEB SITE. KUDER ONLY COLLECTS INFORMATION FROM INDIVIDUALS OVER THE AGE OF 12, OR FROM INDIVIDUALS UNDER THE AGE OF 13 IF THE INDIVIDUAL IS ACCESSING THE WEB SITE WITH THE CONSENT OF A PARENT OR WHEN KUDER IS ACTING AS AN AGENT OF THE CHILD’S SCHOOL. KUDER COLLECTS AND PROCESSES THE INFORMATION TO PROVIDE THE INFORMATION TO THE PARTICULAR SCHOOLS CONTRACTING FOR ITS COLLECTION. KUDER MAY ALSO PROVIDE THE INFORMATION TO FEDERAL STATE AND LOCAL GOVERNMENTAL AGENCIES PROVIDING CAREER PLACEMENT SERVICES. THE COLLECTED INFORMATION RELATES TO STUDENTS' CAREER PREFERENCES AND INTERESTS. THIS INFORMATION IS COLLECTED DIRECTLY FROM THE CHILD, PARENT, TEACHER OR SCHOOL ADMINISTRATOR. KUDER ALSO UTILIZES COOKIES TO ASSIST CHILDREN, PARENTS, TEACHERS AND SCHOOL ADMINISTRATORS IN ACCESSING AND UTILIZING THE SERVICES PROVIDED THROUGH THIS WEB SITE. KUDER WILL NOT PROVIDE INFORMATION RELATING TO ANY CHILD UNDER THE AGE OF 13 TO ANY PARTY OTHER THAN AUTHORIZED EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS. ALL OF THE THIRD PARTY EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS TO WHICH INFORMATION IS DISCLOSED HAVE AGREED TO MAINTAIN THE CONFIDENTIALITY AND SECURITY OF THE INFORMATION.

IF YOU ARE THE PARENT OF A CHILD UNDER THE AGE OF 13 WHO HAS PROVIDED INFORMATION THROUGH THIS WEB SITE AND WOULD LIKE TO PREVENT THE INFORMATION FROM BEING TRANSMITTED TO AUTHORIZED EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS, PLEASE CONTACT THE KUDER WEBMASTER AT THE ADDRESS LISTED BELOW UNDER CHANGING PERSONAL INFORMATION, OR DIRECTLY AT WEBMASTER@KUDER.COM. KUDER HAS TAKEN GREAT STRIDES TO COLLECT ONLY THAT INFORMATION NECESSARY TO FURTHER THE CHILDRENS' CAREER DEVELOPMENT STRATEGIES LISTED ABOVE.

If you do not opt out, Kuder is the sole owner of the information collected on this web site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. We collect information from our users at several different points on our web site.

Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data stored on your hard drive containing information about you. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our web site. Once you close your browser, the cookie simply terminates. For instance, by setting a cookie on our web site, you would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our web site. If you reject the cookie, you may still use our web site. The only drawback to this is that you will be limited in some areas of our web site. For example, you will not be able to participate in any of our Sweepstakes or Contests. Cookies can also enable us to track and target your interests to enhance your experience on our web site.

Some of our business partners use cookies on our web site (for example, advertisers). However, we have no access to or control over these cookies.

Connect 2 Business and Link 2 College

Certain Businesses and Colleges have contracted with Kuder for the opportunity to post information on our web site about the different careers or programs they offer. In addition, some of these Businesses and Colleges have asked Kuder to send messages to students/participants meeting certain criteria on their behalf. Kuder will send these messages to students meeting the criteria but will not provide any individual's information to the Business or College. You will also have an opportunity to view information posted on the Kuder.com web site about the Business or College.

If you are under the age of 18, you may receive these direct messages. If you choose to respond, certain personally identifying information will be made available to the Business. What the Business will do with that information and the uses it will make of it will be governed by that Business's Privacy Policy and not by the policy governing kuder.com.

Unless you are under the age of 13, you may request information from a College without first obtaining permission from your school. What the College will do with that information and the uses it will make of it will be governed by that College's Privacy Policy and not by the policy governing kuder.com.

If you are under 13 years old, you will not be eligible to respond to these targeted messages.

Limitations on Data Collection

In furtherance of Kuder's effort to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, and to insure the security of data collected from children under the age of 13, Kuder only collects data from such children pursuant to a specific agreement that Kuder is to act as an agent of the child’s school or educational institution. Kuder specifically contracts with such institutions for services which allow the school to input individual information for each child onto the web site. It is important that schools comport to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act and obtain parental permission, if required under the Act, before inputting a child's information onto the web site, or before allowing a child to input the information themselves. If Kuder receives a direct request from a child under 13 to access the system, and the child has not provided Kuder with written consent from a parent or guardian and the child is not affiliated with an authorized educational institution for whom Kuder acts as an agent, Kuder will utilize the information solely for a one-time response to the child.
