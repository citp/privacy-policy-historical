# Kubota Tractor USA - KTC Policy

Kubota Tractor Corporation respects the online privacy of all individuals who visit our website. The Kubota Tractor Corporation website has a guest registry, and may from time to time include surveys or other feedback forms which request certain personal identity information. The personal identity information collected may be used to provide information to site registrants regarding our products, company or promotional offers. This personal identity information may be transmitted to our dealers or other business partners. We will not give, rent or sell personal identity information to any other outside company for use in marketing or solicitation. We will exercise reasonable efforts to maintain the confidentiality of your personal identity information.

Customers of Kubota Credit Corporation can click [here](https://web.archive.org/finance/KCCPolicy.aspx) to see the Confidentiality Policy of KCC relating to that financial relationship.
