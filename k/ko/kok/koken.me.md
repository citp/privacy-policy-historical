# Privacy Policy / Koken

*Dominey Design Inc. is committed to respecting and protecting your online privacy. We are providing this Privacy Policy for Koken (the "Software") so you may understand our policies with regard to your personal information.

## Gathering and Use of Information

Dominey Design Inc. will only collect your personal information when the Software is installed or when you voluntarily sign-up for our mailing list. Information we collect from you will never be sold, rented or shared with third-parties.

## Information collected

Installations of the Software provide Dominey Design Inc. with location information including the target server's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, domain name, and email address should the administrator sign-up for our mailing list.

## Mailing Lists

If you sign up for our mailing list you may receive the occasional email newsletter from us. You may choose not to receive further emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of the email.

## Updates

Dominey Design Inc. reserves the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Updates to our proviacy policy will be sent to the email address you have provided us with.

Questions concerning this policy may be directed to [info@koken.me](mailto:info@koken.me).
