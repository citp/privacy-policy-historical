What information do we collect?

In order to provide you with our member services, we need to collect personal information about you purely for the administration and security of your membership. No information is used for any form of marketing by ourselves or anyone else. Your details would only be disclosed to a law enforcement agency if requested.

If you sign-up for a membership by credit or debit card via PayPal or Clickbank, your card information is not disclosed to us. This means we cannot debit any funds from your credit or debit card manually at any time. We do not know your debit or credit card number, expiry date etc. This information is never provided to us.

How do we use your information?

Your information is purely for the administration and security of your membership. We do not use your details for marketing nor do we provide them to anyone else. If you cease to become a member your details will be destroyed.

If you are identified as posting abuse or spam on our websites we may take action against you to try and prevent this. We will not hesitate to contact your Internet Service Provider (whoever is providing your internet connection) if you are detected as causing problems for ourselves or other members.

Disclosure of your information

Your information will never be disclosed to anyone unless specifically requested by a law enforcement agency.

Cookies

In common with most websites, we use standard technology called 'cookies' on our websites. Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent cookies being stored, however if you do turn cookies off this will limit the service that we are able to provide to you. If you block cookies you will be unable to log-in to our members area.

What to do if you do not want to receive marketing information

Nothing because we do not use your information for any form of marketing nor do we provide your information to any third parties.

Security

Sadly our members have previously been targeted by other LFC websites (spammed). To prevent this you should never post your email address on our website or any of your personal information. If you ever receive an email claiming to be from KOPTALK, when you hit reply you should check that the reply to email address is supportkoptalk.com - if in doubt, call us free on 0800 5300 192 or Mobile +44 (0) 7962 475020. Overseas members please call +44 7962 475020. We strongly advise that you use a different password on KOPTALK if you use other football websites.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to change our security and privacy policies at any time. Accordingly we recommend that you check this page periodically in order to review our current policies.

Updating and correcting information

You may correct your personal information by contacting us via our support website @ www.koptalk.info We would encourage you to promptly update your personal information if it changes.
