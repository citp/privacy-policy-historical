# KGB Report Privacy Statement

* * *

**_Privacy Policy_**

* * *

Any personal information collected at this web site is used solely by us to send you the KGB Report newsletter. We do not share, sell or otherwise make this information available to any individuals, agencies, or companies. 

No personal information of any kind is stored on our web server. Subscriber mailing lists are stored and maintained on systems not directly accessible through the Internet.

We don't use or condone the use of unsolicited bulk email, or "spam".

We offer free subscriptions to the KGB Report newsletter. You may request a subscription by sending an email to [kgbreport@kgb.com](mailto:kgbreport@kgb.com) with the word "subscribe" anywhere in the header or body of the message. You may also subscribe by completing the form that appears on various pages of the web site. If you subscribe by completing the web site form, we will send you an email requesting you confirm your subscription by replying to our message.

You may unsubscribe from our mailing list by sending an email to [kgbreport@kgb.com](mailto:kgbreport@kgb.com) with the word "unsubscribe" anywhere in the header or body of the message. When you unsubscribe, you are permanently removed from our mailing list and you will not be contacted by us again.

We have taken prudent and reasonable actions to control the integrity of the environment and content of this site.
