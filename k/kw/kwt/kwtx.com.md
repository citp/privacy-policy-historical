# KWTX-TV News 10 KWTX-DT Channel 53

**Privacy Statement for KWTX-TV**

 **Information Collection, Disclosure and Use:  
** This privacy statement demonstrates KWTX-TV’s commitment to your right to privacy and explains the privacy policies governing kwtx.com (the “Web Site”). It is our general policy to collect and store only personal information that our members and visitors knowingly provide.

Our site may require you to provide contact or other information, such as your name or e-mail address, which would only be used as described below. You may opt out of receiving any communications as provided in the choice/opt-out section below. None of this information will be provided to third parties to market their products or services.

Unless otherwise provided, we may use the personal information we collect from you to ensure that our community is protected against unauthorized access, to use e-mail to diagnose problems with the server or to administer the Web Site, and to contact you when necessary.

If our privacy policies change, we will notify all users by a special announcement on the Web Site.

 **Minors:  
** Minors may participate in our interactive community; however, a minor may not appreciate the consequences of providing personal information to third parties and must obtain a parent’s or legal guardian’s formal consent before registering to participate in our interactive community or downloading files from our Web Site. The applicable consent form is provided during the registration process.

 **Choice/Opt-Out:  
** If you do not want information provided by you during the Web Site’s registration process to be disclosed to third parties or used by us to communicate with you, you may opt out and elect not to have the information shared. AN OPT-OUT BOX WILL APPEAR WHEN YOU PROVIDE ANY REQUESTED INFORMATION. 

**Cookies:  
** Cookies are files that contain information created by a web server that can be stored on your hard disk for future use. Cookies are not used to disseminate significant information about you over the Internet or to analyze any information that you have knowingly or unknowingly provided. This Web Site uses cookies only to facilitate automated activity and to enable the server to recognize your computer. Your web browser will allow you to disable cookies, but you will be required to consent to our terms of service agreement each time you access the Web Site if you do so. 

**Links:  
** This Web Site contains links to other web sites. We have no control over and are not responsible for the privacy policies of such sites. 

**Public Forums:  
** We may make various public forums available to you. Any information you choose to share when using any of these forums becomes public information. It is incumbent upon you to exercise caution in deciding whether to disclose personal information. 

**Integrity/Security:  
** We use encryption and other technology to store personal information gathered online in an attempt to provide a secure environment that is protected from unauthorized third party access, alteration, theft, or misuse. 

**Corrections/Updates:  
** You may change or modify information that you have already provided via e-mail at [webmaster@kwtx.com](mailto:webmaster@kwtx.com).
