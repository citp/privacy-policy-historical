Our Privacy Statement
Simply put, Knowledge Matters, Inc. collects no personally identifiable information without your knowledge. When information is collected, its use is limited to the purpose expressed. We do not share it with others.

Personal information
At Knowledge Matters, Inc., we respect your right to privacy. You can visit Knowledge Matters, Inc. on the Web without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself. The only time we collect information about you is when it is needed as a part of or a consequence of responding to a request, a product or service order. You are fully aware of the extent of the information provided and its intent. Knowledge Matters, Inc. does not share or make your information available to others.

Control
If you tell us that you do not wish to be included in our periodic mailings, we will respect your wishes.

Traffic Analysis
Logs of all traffic through Knowledge Matters, Inc. are maintained and reviewed regularly. The data may include your URL. This data is used for analysis only.

3rd Parties
The Knowledge Matters, Inc. Web Site contains links to other Web Sites. Knowledge Matters, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web Sites.

Cookies
"Cookies" are technological features which can be used to provide you with tailored information from a Web Site. A cookie is a data element a Web Site can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system. Passwords you request to be "remembered" are examples. Currently Knowledge Matters, Inc. uses no cookies.
