K&N Privacy Policy

Personal information obtained by this web site from users that subscribe to product updates or other information will be used by K&N Engineering, Inc. to provide the data requested and for analyzing trends and product demands.  We send out email and direct mail to our online and store customers about products or events that we think may be of interest to them. We also may send you offers for discounts or free services (e.g., free gift wrap or shipping) on our site or in our stores. We will not share your personal data unless you have given us permission to do so.  If you give us permission to share your data we may share your information with some of our distributors and retailers.  Users can unsubscribe to any subscription service by selecting a link at the bottom of the sent e-mail and by following the instructions on the launched web page or by sending an e-mail request to tech@knfilters.com with the subscriber's e-mail address.  Changes to data are not permitted, however, you may resubmit the data and if the error is in a subscription service, cancel the old subscription as described above.  Changes to the K&N Engineering, Inc. privacy policy will be posted on this website.

Authorized Third Parties
We use third-party services to monitor pages of our website for the purpose of reporting website traffic and other statistics as well as to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. This website and third parties may use cookies and/or other monitoring technologies to provide anonymous statistics to us about our website visitors. No personally identifiable information is transferred to these third parties.

You may opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting the Google advertising opt-out page. You can also opt out of third party vendor's use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt out page.

Consent
By using this website, you consent to the collection and use of information by K&N Engineering, Inc. Changes to our privacy policy will be posted on this page. If you have any questions or comments regarding this policy, please contact us.
