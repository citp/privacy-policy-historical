# K&N Engineering, Inc. Privacy Policy

**  
** Personal information obtained by this web site from users that subscribe to product updates or other information will be used by K&N Engineering, Inc. to provide the data requested and for analyzing trends and product demands.  We will not share your personal data unless you have given us permission to do so.  If you give us permission to share your data we may share your information with some of our distributors and retailers.  Users can unsubscribe to any subscription service by selecting a link at the bottom of the sent e-mail and by following the instructions on the launched web page or by sending an e-mail request to  [tech@knfilters.com](mailto:tech@knfilters.com) with the subscriber's e-mail address.  Changes to data are not permitted, however, you may resubmit the data and if the error is in a subscription service, cancel the old subscription as described above.  Changes to the K&N Engineering, Inc. privacy policy will be posted on this website.

**Authorized Third Parties**   
We use a third-party service to monitor pages of our website for the purpose of reporting website traffic and other statistics. This website and the third party may use cookies and/or other monitoring technologies to provide anonymous statistics to us about our website visitors. No personally identifiable information is transferred to this third party. 

**Consent**  
By using this website, you consent to the collection and use of information by K&N Engineering, Inc. Changes to our privacy policy will be posted on this page. If you have any questions or comments regarding this policy, please [contact](https://web.archive.org/web/20101230223355id_/http%3A//www.knfilters.com/contact.htm) us.
