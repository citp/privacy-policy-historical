**Privacy Policy** informs how KAPDACLICK gathers personal and non-personal knowledge, classified as voluntary or obligatory and discloses, assists and uses such data through Website. KAPDACLICK gathers Personal Information that KAPDACLICK considers to be pertinent and is required to interpret you or your interests.

KAPDACLICK saves your Personal Information to list and expedite your participation in activities, record your interests, to give you a personalized Website experience, to inform you of any revised data and other relevant services offered by KAPDACLICK.

We keep you notified about the current content accessible on Website, offers on products and services of KAPDACLICK, to help you with customer service or technical issues, to follow up with you after your visit and guide your affiliation with KAPDACLICK or restrict fraud use.

To make our Website efficient, some data may be gathered every time you access the Website. Such details may be saved in server logs.

These encrypted statistics do not analyze you personally, but give us details about your visit to Website and the type of user who is visiting the Website and browsing processes by you.

This data may contain: IP address of your server from where the Website is being accessed, the type of browser (Internet Explorer, Firefox, Opera, Google Chrome etc.), the operating system of your system and the Website you last accessed before visiting our Website.

The period of time of your stay on the Website is saved in the session with the date and time of your visits. This data is used by KAPDACLICK to know the number of user visiting the Website.

Some of the data related to the Website and your visit may be shared by investors, developer, sponsors, strategic business partners, associates and advertisers of KAPDACLICK to enhance our business but we assure that these data will never contain any of your Personal Informations.

This anonymous data is gathered through a pixel tag, which is industry standard technology used by most major Websites. When you access the Website, KAPDACLICK's servers may gather data automatically and indirectly regarding your activities on Website by cookies.

A cookie is a minor amount of data that is sent to a user's browser from a web server and is stored on a user's system hard drive. This anonymous data is held discretely and is not attached to Personal Information submitted by you to KAPDACLICK. You can decide, If you want your web browser to accept cookies or not.

If you haven't varied your computer's settings, more chances are your browser already accepts cookies. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience all features of the Website. You can delete your browser cookies or disable them, but this may impact your experience with Website and may make parts of the Website inaccessible , “We recommend that you leave them turned on”.

We use third-party service providers to deliver ads on our behalf over the internet and on this Website. They may gather anonymous data about your access to the Website, and your interaction with our services. No personally identifiable data is used in this process. They do not know the name, address, email address, or any personally identifying data about you.

KAPDACLICK also uses the log file like other website servers which is the standard measures. They include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks. We use them to analyse trends, administer the site, track a user's movement and gather broad demographic data for aggregate use.

“IP addresses, etc. are not linked to personally identifiable data”
