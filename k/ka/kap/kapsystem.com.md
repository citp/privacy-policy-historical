We take care to protect the privacy of the customers and users of KAPS website. Set out below is an explanation of how we process information about visitors to the site:

  * The information which we collect and store during normal usage of the site is used to monitor and analyze how different parts of the website are used. The normal usage of the website does not result in the collection or storage of any personally identifiable data of the user.
  * The users have the option to submit personal information to KAPS in certain pages in order to enable us to send you further information or email alerts. These pages provide explanations as to how this information is to be used.
  * We will not disclose any personal information submitted by you on our website to any third party without your consent. In connection with any application, request or enquiry you make, your information will be passed directly to the relevant contact within the company. 
  * By submitting your personal information through this website, you shall be consenting to it being processed in the manner described above by KAPS.



If you have submitted personal information through this website and wish us to cease using it for the purposes submitted, please contact [info@kapsystem.com](mailto:info@kapsystem.com)
