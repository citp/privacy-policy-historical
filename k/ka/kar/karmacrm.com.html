KarmaCRM Privacy Policy

Online Privacy Policy

KarmaCRM agrees to keep all information submitted on the site private. Any information we gather at this web site is strictly for our use and is not shared with any other entity, public or private, for any reason - period. We will not sell or give away any lists or other data that we may retain and we do not purchase such information from other sources.

Statistical Data

Our servers track statistical data regarding referral address and keywords. This information is only used to analyze peak traffic points, and help direct our users to the right location. We track the keywords used to enter our site strictly to further aid and guide your inquiry. This information is not used for any other purpose.

Personal Information Collected

Any personal information collected will only be used for sales purposes. Your information will not be shared or sold to any entity. We keep an order history and a progress history in order to quickly provide you with the status of your order. If you do wish to have information removed from our site please click contact us. Your credit card and financial information is not stored online for security purposes.
