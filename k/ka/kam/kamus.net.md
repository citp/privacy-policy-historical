# kamus.net by kemangnet.com

| Privacy Policy  
---  
  
This privacy policy covers kamus.net's treatment of personally identifiable information that we collect when you are on our site, and when you use our services. This policy also applies to kamus.net's treatment of any personally identifiable information that our business partners share with us. This policy does not apply the practices of companies that we do not own or control, or to people that we do not employ or manage.  
  
Information Collection and Use  
  
We automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, and the page you requested. 

We automatically collect every missing word that you type to our database as our reference to complete database content.

We may use this information for three general purposes: to customize the content you see, to fulfill your requests for certain services and information.  
  
Cookies  
We are not using any cookies on your computer.
