# Privacy Policy :: kalsey

This site will look much better in a browser that supports [ web standards](http://www.webstandards.org/upgrade/ "Download a browser that complies with Web standards."), but it is accessible to any browser or Internet device.

[kalsey](https://web.archive.org/) > Privacy Policy

Your privacy is greatly repected here. If you would like more information about online privacy in general, please visit my [privacy pages](https://web.archive.org/web/privacy/).

#### Collection And Use of Information

I do not collect any personal information at all on this site. As with all web sites, information about which pages you view is logged by my server, and your [IP address](http://webopedia.internet.com/TERM/I/IP_address.html) is listed in the log. Other information commonly collected in a Web server log includes your browser version, your computer's operating system, and the date and time of your visit.

When you perform a search on this site, the words you searched for are recorded by Atomz, the company that provides the search service. Reports are available to me that list which words were searched for.

#### Cookies

This site does not use cookies at all.

#### Third Parties

[Atomz](https://web.archive.org/web/20010406143715id_/http%3A//www.kalsey.com/site/www.atomz.com) provides my search service. When you view search results, those results appear to come from my site but are hosted on their servers. Atomz is not bound by my privacy policy and may collect and use information in ways other than specified in this privacy policy. I would suggest that you read their privacy policy.
