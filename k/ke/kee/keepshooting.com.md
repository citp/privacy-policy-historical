**How We Protect Your Privacy**  
We never sell or rent your personal information to any third parties under any circumstances. We value your trust very highly, and will work to protect the security and privacy of any personal information you provide to us.  
**What Personal Information We Collect**  
First of all, you do not need to create an account to browse and purchase at Keepshooting.com. The only information we need from you when you make a purchase is the following:  

* Full Name
* Credit Card Number and Expiration Date
* Billing Address
* Shipping Address (if differ from Billing Address)
* Contact Phone Number 
* Email Address  
**How We Use Your Personal Information**  
Again, we do not share your Personal Information to any third-parties. We only use your Personal Information in the following situations:  

* Deliver merchandise that you purchase online or on the phone 
* Confirm your order
* Communicate great values and featured items to you
* Respond to your customer-service inquiries or requests  
**Changes to the Keepshooting.com Privacy Policy**  
We reserve the right to change, modify or amend this policy at any time. Please check our Privacy Policy periodically for changes.

If you use the Site, you are accepting the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy, and we will have the right to use your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to have your information used in any of the ways described in this Privacy Policy, you must discontinue use of the Site.
