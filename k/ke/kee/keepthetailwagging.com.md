# Keep the Tail Wagging Privacy Policy | Keep the Tail Wagging, An Online Magazine for Dog Lovers

PRIVACY STATEMENT

Your privacy is important to me. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Kimberly Gauthier and Keep the Tail Wagging collects and the ways in which Keep the Tail Wagging uses that personal information.

**Personal information collection**

Keep the Tail Wagging may collect and use the following kinds of personal information: _y_ _our first and last name and your email address_

**Using personal information**

Keep the Tail Wagging may use your personal information to _s_ _end you updates through the Keep the Tail Wagging newsletter_

**Disclosure of personal information**

Keep the Tail Wagging will not share (for free or for profit) your personal information (name and email address) with any outside parties.  Your personal information will not be share on the website or the internet unless it’s submitted within the body of a guest article that you’ve submitted to Keep the Tail Wagging.

**Securing your data**

MailChimp will store all the personal information you provide [on its secure servers].

**Guest article submissions**

Any personal information included within guest article submissions will be published on the website and the internet and may be available around the world.  Please take care in what information you share within your guest articles.  By submitted guest articles, you are agreeing that your content will be published on the website and the internet.

**Updating this statement**

Keep the Tail Wagging may update this privacy policy by posting a new version on this website.

You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.

**Other websites**

This website contains links to other websites.

Kimberly Gauthier and Keep the Tail Wagging are not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third party.
