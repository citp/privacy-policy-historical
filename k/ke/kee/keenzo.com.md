How we protect your privacy  
**We use security measures to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of data used by our system. Pages which require you to login or enter personal information are secured by Thawte.  Click the Thawte link below to verify our Security Certificate.

**  
Collected Information and Usage  
**Keenzo is the sole owner of all information collected on this website.  Keenzo will not share, sell, or rent any of this information with anyone unless ordered by a court of law.  Information submitted to us is only available to selected employees managing this information for purposes of order processing, contacting you,  or sending you information on your request.

Keenzo also logs your IP address and browsing patterns while on our website.  This information is used to analyze visitor trends and demographics as well as detecting and preventing fraudulent activity. We do not distribute or share IP address information with any other parties. 

**Security**  
Keenzo takes precautions to ensure our customer data is secure both on and off line. When entering sensitive information on Keenzo.com,  that information is protected by SSL (Secure Socket Layer), the best encryption in the industry.   Keenzo employs 128-bit encryption by Thawte, one of the most advanced forms of SSL available.  To learn more about Thawte, please visit their website at [http://www.thawte.com](http://www.thawte.com/).
