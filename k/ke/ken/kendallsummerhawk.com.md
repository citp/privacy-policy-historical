# KendallSummerHawk.com

### **PRIVACY POLICY**

This Privacy Policy (the "Privacy Statement") is made by and between Kendall SummerHawk, a DBA of _Heart of Success, Inc._ (the "Company"), and you, a user of the Company's website services ("You," "Your," "User").

This Privacy Statement governs Your use of this Company website, and any other Website owned, operated, licensed, or controlled by the Company including but not limited to [www.kendallsummerhawk.com](http://www.kendallsummerhawk.com/) (hereinafter and collectively known as the "Website"). Your use of the Website constitutes your affirmative acceptance of the Privacy Statement, which may be changed at the discretion of the Company, at any time, without notice to you. You are _strongly_ encouraged to read the Privacy Statement.

### **COLLECTION OF INFORMATION.**

  1. _Purpose._ Users can access limited features of this Website without disclosing personal information. In order to access the entire Website, users must register for an account, which will provide the registrant with full access to the Website, including access to any forums, discussion groups, or chat rooms that the Website may operate. 
  2. _Information Sought During Registration._ During the account registration process, You will be asked to submit personal information, including Your proper name and at least one active e-mail address. At this time, You will also have the opportunity to subscribe to any and all Website materials regarding Company's product news, services and special offers or events. Registrants can also unsubscribe to receiving these material at any time by updating registration account information online. 
  3. _Information Sought During Website Sweepstakes or Contests._ When the Website offers sweepstakes or contests, personal information is collected with the sole purpose of notifying You if You win the sweepstakes or contest. 
  4. _Automatically Collected Information._ Information concerning Your computer hardware and software is automatically collected and may be shared with any of the Websites. This information may include: Your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. Company uses this information to maintain the quality of its service and to provide general statistics about Website visitors. 
  5. _Storage and Transfer of Personally Identifiable Information._ Company may store and process, in the United States or any other country in which Company or its affiliates, subsidiaries, or agents operate and/or maintain facilities. By using Company Websites, You consent to any such transfer of information outside of Your country. 



### **USE OF AND SHARING OF INFORMATION.**

Company collects and uses your personal information in order to operate the Websites and to provide its online and electronic services. The collection of your e-mail address, name and address during account registration enables Company to send you any requested material(s) or to send you any requested mailings or offers. Information concerning your geographical location is used to determine if Company should provide regionalized information to its customers. When you voluntarily enter a sweepstakes or contest, we request your e-mail address and other contact information in order to be able to contact you in the event you are a winner of the sweepstakes or contest. Except as otherwise stated below, the personal information you provide on this site will not be shared with any third-parties, although Company reserves the right to share such information with its own affiliates and subsidiaries without your permission.

Company sometimes hires other companies to provide limited services on its behalf, such as sending materials, providing customer service, and performing statistical analysis of Company services. We will only provide those companies with the information that they need to provide us with such services, and we prohibit them from using that information for any other purpose.

Company Website reserves the right to disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Company or the Website; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Company or the Website; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Company, the Website, or the public.

Company provides hyper-links to send e-mail messages to this Website for Your convenience and Your e-mail address is used, so that this Website may reply to Your inquiry. Company tracks the pages on this Website that our customers visit in order to determine which areas of the Website is the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within the Website to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

### **INFORMATION FROM OTHER SOURCES.**

We sometimes supplement the information that we receive, which has been outlined in this Policy Statement with information from other sources and companies. Such outside information includes updated delivery and address information from carriers or third parties, which enables us to correct our records and deliver your next purchase from this Website or future communications more easily; account information, purchase or redemption information, and page-view information from some merchants with which we operate co-branded businesses or for which we provide technical, fulfillment, advertising, or other services; search term and search result information from some searches conducted through the Web search features; search results and links, including paid listings (such as Sponsored Links from Google); and credit history information from credit bureaus, which we use to help prevent and detect fraud and to offer certain credit or financial services to some customers.

### **INFORMATION SECURITY.**

Company Website secures the e-mail address You provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. In some cases, you may choose to create a profile on the Website or you may choose to register for an account, if so, you may be required to select a password. This password can be changed at any time after you first log into the service by using your current password. You must keep your password confidential and you must not share this information with anyone. If you share a computer with anyone, you should always log out of the Website before leaving it to prevent access to Your information from subsequent users of that computer.

### **USE OF COOKIES.**

The Website uses cookies to assist You with making Your online experience more individualized. A cookie is a text file that is placed on the hard disk of Your computer by a web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to You.

Cookies are used for convenience and to save You time when you next visit this Website. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the web server that you have returned to a specific page, so that when you visit the site again, the experience will be personalized. You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to set your computer to accept cookies from this Website, you may also choose to be logged into the site automatically. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Website, and You may experience technical problems receiving current information from this now cookie-disabled Website.

### **USE OF WEB BEACONS.**

Company Website pages may contain electronic images known as web beacons—also referred to as single-pixel gifs, which allow the Company to count users who have visited those pages and other related Website statistics e.g., recording the popularity of certain Website content and verifying system and server integrity). Web beacons are not used to access your personal information on Company Website; they are solely used to compile aggregated statistics concerning the use of the Company Website. Web beacons collect limited information, including, but not limited to cookie numbers, the time and date of a page view, and a description of the page on which the web beacon resides.

You may access some of the information that we collect and process about you. For example, you may access information regarding recent orders from the Website; personally identifiable information (including name, e-mail, password, communications preferences, mailing address); payment settings (including credit card information and gift certificates); e-mail notification settings; and prior order history.

With respect to your communication preferences, you can unsubscribe from materials by following the instructions included in the information you receive.
