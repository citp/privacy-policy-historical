# Privacy

As a member of KHMERFUN.COM, you enjoy the benefits of our strict privacy policy. We undertake to handle your private information responsibly, and you may actively control access to this information.

 **Privacy for Member Applicants**

  **Applicant Policy:**

    We do not collect your personal information, except when you voluntarily provide it to us. Other than information you choose to make available to our member companies, we will never sell or otherwise distribute your personal information to any third party.

       **General**

       KHMERFUN.COM reserves the right to analyze the activity and characteristics of its members and to distribute aggregated information to KHMERFUN.COM's members, advertisers, and other third parties. KHMERFUN.COM reserves the right to contact its members regarding alterations and additions to the substance of the site and data contained in the site, and other issues relevant to KHMERFUN.COM and/or information collected by it.

This privacy statement is for information only, and is not intended to and does not create any contractual or other legal rights in or on behalf of any party.
