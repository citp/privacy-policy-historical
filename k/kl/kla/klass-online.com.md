# Memory Cards, USB Memory Sticks, Micro SD Cards, SDHC - Free Delivery

**Privacy Policy**

 **GizzmoHeaven is firmly committed to respecting your privacy. We do not share your information with any third party and all of your details are protected by the relevant Data Protection Laws.**

GizzmoHeaven (Klass Online Ltd.) takes the protection of your on-line privacy seriously and this Privacy Policy sets out how we use and protect any personal information we obtain from dealings which arise from you using this website and orders placed on our eBay, Play or Amazon marketplaces. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any websites which may be linked from the GizzmoHeaven website, so it is the customers responsibility to check these separately.

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time so please this check this page again for any changes when you revisit our website. By using the GizzmoHeaven.com website you are consenting to this privacy policy.

 **What personal information do we collect?**

We may collect the following information when you register, make a purchase sign up for promotions, offers or competitions, or to receive news. 

Depending on the services you use, we may collect the following even if you do not complete your purchase:

• name and contact information including postal address;  
• e-mail address and telephone number;  
• details of any orders that you place through our website, eBay, Play or Amazon marketplaces, and any details we need for delivery;  
• card payment details if making a purchase through our website;  
• details you provide when you contact us or make enquiries either generally or about any purchases you make with us;  
• details of your visits to the website and the resources that you access (email, live chat) and information you provide if you report a problem with our website or merchant orders;

 **What we do with the information?**

We require this information to enable us to process your orders quickly and efficiently and to improve our service to our current and future customers.  
We may use the information for the following purposes:

• for internal accounting and regulatory purposes;  
• process your order, which allows you to track your order on-line;  
• collect payment;  
• ongoing customer support for order updates, cancellations, refunds, returns and replacements;  
• account updates and information which may include contacting you by telephone, email or SMS to keep you fully informed;  
• we may from time to time contact or update you about products, offers or services that may be relevant to you;  
• from time to time we may contact you about your shopping experience with us as your feedback is important to us;

 **Use of cookies**  
We only use one cookie on our site but the live chat and and ad tracking cookies are also present. as listed below.

• ASP.NET_SessionId - This cookie allows our web servers to respond to your actions on the website such as "Add to basket" or browsing the website. Without this cookie our website would not work as you wouldn't be able to add items to your basket.  
• _zlcid Stores the Zopim Live Chat ID used to identify the device across visits, and also across a single chat session.  
• __zprivacy Stores the user's preference to disable Live Chat, and cease using cookies. This is stored across visits.

 **Security**

We have put in place all of the required physical and managerial measures to ensure that there is no unauthorised access to your information and all of our servers are fully PCI compliant which is a legal requirement of the card processing authorities.

If you wish to unsubscribe from our newsletter, simply click on the Unsubscribe link, which can be found at the bottom of every newsletter we send. Alternatively update your profile by logging in here. Please allow 24-48 hours for your subscription to be cancelled.
