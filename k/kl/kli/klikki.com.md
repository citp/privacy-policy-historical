# Privacy | Nordic Morning

At Nordic Morning we respect your privacy and will protect your data in every way we can. This privacy statement will tell you how we collect, keep, maintain and upkeep your data and why we collect that data.

## INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### Information we Collect from Website Browsing

While browsing on our website we will collect information via cookies, tags, web beacons and other similar identifiers. This is done in order for us to get information about how the site is used and to optimise the user experience for all site visitors. Additionally we might use this information to provide you relevant advertising in channels you might visit. This processing is based on your consent. Learn more from our cookies section here and adjust your cookie settings from the footer link "Cookie Settings".

_Information we collect_ IP address, information provided via cookies, tags, web beacons and other similar identifiers.

_Why_ To better understand our site visitors and how we can improve the user experience of our website for all visitors. We might also use this information to provide you with relevant advertising. 

### Information you provide us via forms, during phone calls, meetings, and similar interactions

We might occasionally ask you to provide more information for us in exchange of content, information, or to learn more about our offering. This exchange of information is voluntary and you may opt-out of this any time you wish. We use this information to provide the best possible service to you, gather statistics of what content is useful to whom, and possibly to provide you relevant advertising in channels you might visit. We have a legitimate interest to improve our services and to contact you to provide information on our services. Given the relationship between us and the information provided to you in this Privacy Statement, you may reasonably expect such processing taking place. We can also ask you to give us permission for email, sms, or similar marketing. This type of marketing is voluntary and you can opt-out any time you wish. Learn more from our cookies here.

_Information we collect_ This information varies between sources and is explained at the point-of collection, but can include:

  * personal information such as your name, email, and phone number
  * information about your company such as name, your title and position, company business area, business id, yearly revenue, headcount, address, and similar information



_Why_ To provide you with more relevant content, relevant information about our offering, better user experience, news and information about our company and more relevant advertising. 

### Information we collect when you become our customer

When you become our customer, we will ask you to provide information for us to provide you with the service that has been agreed upon. This information will be collected purely in order for us to provide you with the best possible service and to enter into or execute a contract between us. We may also need to process this information in order to investigate a breach or defend a legal claim and to fulfil our statutory obligations. This processing is based on our legitimate interest or legal obligation. 

_Information we collect_ This information includes:

  * personal information such as your name, email, and phone number
  * information about your company such as name, your title and position, necessary information for invoicing, company business area, business ID, yearly revenue, headcount, address, and similar information
  * Information about our interactions such as your order history, NPS score, feedback you have provided, and other information you have provided us concerning our mutual contract



_Why_ To provide you with the services we have agreed upon in our contract, for developing our services, and to make sure you are satisfied with our services. In addition, we may process information about you to investigate breaches or for other required and legal obligation reasons.

## RETENTION OF PERSONAL DATA

In general, Nordic Morning will retain your personal data as long as it is necessary to provide you with the services, content, and other information you have requested from us. 

Personal information concerning customers are processed for as long as the customer relationship in force and thereafter as long as necessary to fulfil our legal obligations. We use your contact details for marketing for as long as your contact details remain accurate and up to date and you have not opted out from our marketing messages.

The retention time of data collected by third party cookies is governed by privacy policies of those third parties.

We make reasonable efforts to keep personal information accurate, complete, current and relevant, based on the most recent data available to us. We will delete all the data that is not necessary to be retained.

## COOKIES

### Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies like pixels, tags, web beacons, and other identifiers to help us personalise our website for you, remember your preferences, understand how users are using our website, and help customise our marketing offering and advertising. When first visiting our website we ask for your consent to our use of cookies and similar technologies for the purposes described in this Statement. Most of the information we collect is pseudonymised or anonymised as soon as it is collected, and for data used for advertising purposes you can opt out by following instructions on below or by changing your cookie settings. You can adjust your cookie settings from the footer link "Cookie Settings".

### Cookies

A "cookie" is a small data file containing a string of characters that is sent to your computer when you visit a website. When you visit the website again, the cookie allows that site to recognise your browser. The length of time a cookie will stay on your computer or mobile device depends on whether it is a "persistent" or "session" cookie. Session cookies will only stay on your device until you stop browsing. Persistent cookies stay on your computer or mobile device until they expire or are deleted. In general, our persistent cookies will last 18 months on your device.

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  * **Strictly necessary cookies**. These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you which amount to a request for services, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in or filling in forms. 

You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of the site will not then work. We cannot identify you personally from the information these cookies collect.

  * **Performance cookies**. These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us to know which pages are the most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site. All information these cookies collect is aggregated and therefore we can’t identify you personally. 

If you do not allow these cookies we will not know when you have visited our site, and will not be able to monitor its performance.

  * **Functional cookies**. These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalisation. They may be set by us or by third party providers whose services we have added to our pages. 

If you do not allow these cookies then some or all of these services may not function properly.

  * **Targeting Cookies**. These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant adverts on other sites. 

They do not store directly personal information, but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and internet device. If you do not allow these cookies, you will experience less targeted advertising.




_How to control cookie settings_ Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences, however if you limit the ability of websites to set cookies, you may worsen your overall user experience. Below you can learn about how to control cookie settings on popular web browsers:

  * [Google Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647?hl=en)
  * [Internet Explorer](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/17442/windows-internet-explorer-delete-manage-cookies)
  * [Safari](https://support.apple.com/kb/ph17191?locale=en_US)
  * [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences)



### Pixels

In addition to cookies, we sometimes utilise small graphic images known as “pixels” (also known as web beacons, clear GIFs, or pixel tags). We use pixels in our email communications sent to you (if you have selected to receive such communications) to help us to understand whether our email communication was viewed. Additionally, we can use third party pixels (such as those from Google, Facebook, and other advertising networks) to help us provide advertising that is relevant to your interests.
