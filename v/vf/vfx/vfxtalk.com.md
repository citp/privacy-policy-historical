# VFXTalk.com - About Us

  | 

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available right here for all to read.

**The Info We Collect**

We collect the IP addresses of those who visit our Web site. This information is used only for internal research. Additionally we also collect such information as name, e-mail addresses and other, as volunteered by the consumer for such services as site registration, subscriptions, surveys and more. 

Cookies are only collected for such functions as site voting and the forum and it's functions. Most of these functions are voluntary and may be avoided by our viewers. 

**The Way We Use Information**

No personal information is shared with outside parties other then non-identifying and aggregate information, and information which you allow to be made available. We use return e-mail addresses to answer the e-mail we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties. 

We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design and develop our websites and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website, or that Y number of men and Z number of women filled out our registration form, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals. 

Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses. Basically, we wil not disclose any of your private information, Period!

**Our Commitment To Data Security**

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. 

Any information that you think is personally identifiable and would like removed or edited, please contact us asap at [vfxtalk@vfxtalk.com](mailto:vfxtalk@vfxtalk.com). 

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please send us an e-mail at [vfxtalk@vfxtalk.com](mailto:vfxtalk@vfxtalk.com).
