  • User is always free to decline VFS Global’s cookies if their browser permits, although doing so may interfere with User’s use of the website.
  • User may encounter cookies from any links or third parties on certain pages of the website that VFS Global does not control. For example, if User views a VFS Global page created by another user, there may be a "cookie" placed by that VFS Global page.
