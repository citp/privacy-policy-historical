Privacy

Vancouver Film School gathers and maintains information used for the purpose of admission and registration in a private post-secondary institution in British Columbia.

We respect the privacy of every individual who visits our site.

We will not collect any personally identifiable information about you (e.g. your name, address, telephone number, or email address (personal data) through our site unless you have provided it to us voluntarily. We do not now and do not intend to sell, rent, or market personal data about you to third parties.

All information provided to Vancouver Film School by prospective and enrolled students, and any information placed into a student record is protected and used in compliance with the British Columbia's Personal Information Protection Act (2004).

All official permanent records are secured in the Vancouver Film School facility and are confidential; however, the file is always available for the student to review.

Information will not be given to any agency or person other than the student, unless the student has given written permission.

For further information please download Vancouver Film School's 'Personal Information Privacy Policy for Students' document.
