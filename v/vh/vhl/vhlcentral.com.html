  1. INFORMATION VISTA HIGHER LEARNING COLLECTS.

    VHLCENTRAL - MAESTROTM SupersiteTM & Web-SAM

    Vista Higher Learning gathers certain personal information about all users during the registration and technical support process for the VHL Central Sites. Information collected from instructors during this process will include full name, e-mail address, age, school affiliation, user name and one password. An instructor's school affiliation includes country (if non-US only), state/province (if within the US), time zone, school type, and full institutional name.


    Information collected during the student registration and technical support process will include full name and e-mail address, age, and a VHL Central Site user name and passcode. The student also has the option to provide a Student ID. A student's school information, which includes country (if non-US only), state/province (if within the US), time zone, school type, full institutional name, and course/class enrollment status may also be collected.


    Once the registration process is complete, the VHL Central Sites offer users the ability to correct or change some of the above personal information. Users may update or review such information at any time by selecting the "My Account" link within a registered VHL Central Site account.


    In addition, the VHL Central Sites offer instructors and students the ability to complete substantive course work on such sites, including the ability to upload documents and submit communications on such sites. The VHL Central Sites offer instructors the ability to store student grade information on such sites.


    Vista Higher Learning has engaged Hudson Software Corporation ("Hudson") to provide technical support regarding use of the VHL Central Sites. Hudson may request information from or about you in order to provide technical support that you request. Any information provided to Hudson is subject to Hudson's privacy and security practices and is not protected by this Privacy Policy. Vista Higher Learning urges you to contact Hudson regarding their practices prior to disclosing any information to Hudson, as Vista Higher Learning is not responsible for Hudson's privacy and security practices.


    General

    Certain identifying information is automatically collected when you visit the VHL Central Sites, including the IP address from which a VHL Central Site is accessed. We may collect identifying information that allows us to track the number of visitors we receive and the way they navigate through the VHL Central Sites. The types of identifying information collected include the date and time you accessed a VHL Central Site, the page or pages you visited and images you accessed, the website you arrived to the VHL Central Site from and the website that you depart to as well as the IP address of the computer being used to access the VHL Central Site. We may use IP addresses and anonymous demographic information in the aggregate to share with advertisers and market researchers, and to improve the design of the VHL Central Sites to better serve our customers and otherwise for Vista Higher Learning business purposes. While it may be possible to correlate this information to particular individuals, we do not generally do so. We may share this aggregated information with other third parties. We reserve the right to analyze anonymous demographic information or otherwise correlate it to particular individuals in order to investigate potentially unlawful conduct and/or breaches of this Privacy Policy. For example, we may report to appropriate school and law enforcement authorities any IP addresses and correlated email address that is associated with multiple unsuccessful code redemption attempts.

  4. COOKIES & INTERNET BROWSER SETTINGS

    We use cookies. "Cookies" are small pieces of data that can be stored by an Internet browser for visitor tracking purposes. Cookies cannot access your hard drive or send information about your computer system. Cookies are just simply a data storage system. We use them to study visitors' use of the VHL Central Sites and to improve our services for you. Most Web browsers accept cookies, but the "help" menu on your browser should give you options for preventing, accepting, or receiving notice of new cookies. You can configure your web-browser to disable cookies, but doing so will impair your use of the VHL Central Sites. Vista Higher Learning requires that its customers configure Internet browsers to enable session cookies from the VHL Central Sites in order to use the VHL Central Sites.
