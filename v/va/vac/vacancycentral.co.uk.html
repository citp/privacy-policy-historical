How to turn cookies on / off

Please note that we do not advise the blocking of all cookies as some websites require these to be at least accepted, so suggest (where available) the ‘Prompt’ option.


Microsoft Internet Explorer v5
1) Click on the 'Tools' menu.
2) Select 'Internet Options'.
3) Click on the 'Security' tab. and choose the 'Custom Level' button.
4) Find the 'Cookies' option and enable / disable both types, 'stored...' (Permanent cookies) and 'per session...' (Temporary cookies).

Microsoft Internet Explorer v6
1) Click on the 'Tools' menu.
2) Select 'Internet Options'.
3) Click on the 'Privacy' tab and choose the 'Advanced options' button , .
4) Check the 'Override automatic cookie handling' tick-box.
5) Enable / Disable the 'First party' cookies by choosing the 'Accept' or 'Prompt' option.
6) Enable / Disable the 'Third party' cookies by choosing the 'Accept' or 'Prompt' option.

Netscape Navigator v4 and above
 To change cookies using Netscape v4 and above:
1) Click on 'Edit' on the menu bar and select 'Preferences… ',
2) Choose the 'Advanced' category,
3) Choose the cookie option that meets your requirements and click OK to confirm

Firefox / Mozilla
 To change cookies using Mozilla / Firefox :
1) Click on ‘Tools’ in the menu bar,
2) Select ‘Options’
3) Scroll down to the ‘Cookie’ settings and check / un-check ‘Allow site to set cookies’
