[h] Browsing History: We collect and store your browsing and transactional history for targeted advertising purposes, and to analyze and/or improve user experience on our Site.

 

[i] Other Data You Provide: You may decide to provide us with other information regarding your travel such as data about your racial or ethnic origin, religious and political beliefs and sexual orientation. If you give us this information, we may store it as long as required for your travel and our business purpose.
