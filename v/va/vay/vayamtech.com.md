# Outsourced Offshore Software Development, Software Application Development India, Software Application Development, Offshore Software Development, Outsourced Software Application Development

No attempt will be made to identify users or their browsing activities. Vayam Technologies Ltd. does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies.

**Email Management-collection of personal information**

Vayam Technologies Ltd. will only record your email address if you send us a message. It will only be used for the purpose for which you have provided it and will be added to our private mailing list. We will not use your e-mail address for any other purpose, and will not disclose it, without your consent.

You may choose to give us personal information, such as your name and address or e-mail id that may be needed, for example, to correspond with you, to download our white papers or to provide you with a subscription. If you tell us that you do not want us to use this information as a basis for further contact with you, we will respect your wishes. We intend to protect the quality and integrity of your personally identifiable information.
