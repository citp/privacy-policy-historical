Privacy Policy - www.vesselfinder.com

The privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. We take every precaution to ensure that the information you supply is treated in the strictest confidence.
This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by www.vesselfinder.com and how it is used.

Login information
Your successful registration and membership depend on providing the following information:
- name
- email addres
- password
Without this minimal amount of information, you cannot create a VesselFinder account. Like other passwords, you should choose one that is known only by you.

Personal Information
During the Registration process or by creating an interactive resume/CV you may provide additional information to your VesselFinder profile describing your Seafarer status in respect to the maritime industry. Providing additional information about yourself beyond what is minimally required is optional, but providing additional information enables you to benefit more from VesselFinder. Any information you provide at registration or in the Seafarer information sections is valuable to recruiters in the maritime industry.

Log Files
The use of log files enables us to compile aggregate statistical information which is used to improve our services. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons
A cookie is a small text file that is written to your hard drive when you visit a website and contains information that enables a server to identify your visit to a website.
www.vesselfinder.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
