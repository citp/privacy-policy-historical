 Privacy Policy

While you avail of the products and services that the Ventura Group offers through this website, you may be required to disclose personal information through online application forms and questionnaires. Some of this information may even be confidential in nature. We would like you to know that we are strongly committed to protecting your privacy and towards this end, we have taken all necessary and reasonable measures to ensure that your personal information remains confidential and is not transmitted through the world wide web. Consequently, we will not be held liable if confidential information is disclosed in accordance with this Privacy Commitment or if it conforms to the terms of any agreement with you.

On our part, to safeguard and ensure the security of the information that you provide, we use 256-bit encryption while transmitting information. This is currently the permitted level of encryption in India. When the information that you provide is not transmitted through this encryption, your system (if configured accordingly) will display an appropriate message ensuring the best level of secrecy for your information.

We assure you that we will not disclose the information that you provide to anyone, unless we have to do so to:

  • conform to legal requirements or comply with legal processes;
  • protect and defend the Ventura Group’s rights, interests or property;
  • enforce the terms and conditions of the products or services.
  • protect the interests of Ventura Securities, other group companies, affiliates or the interests of other persons we deem to be relevant and necessary

We would like you to rest assured that we will restrict the information which we collection from you to absolute ‘need-to-know’ facts and those essentials which enable us to deliver better services to you. We may use and share the information provided by you within our group of companies and affiliates. We may also share some information with third parties such our agents, contractors and sub-contractors and other parties that may be involved in providing services and activities that are related to the products and services that you avail from us. However, we assure you that such third parties will be required to agree to use the information obtained from us only for specific purposes.

We request you to cooperate with us in order to ensure the security of your information by necessarily choosing passwords carefully. This will preempt any unauthorised third party assess to your information. We recommend that you use passwords that are complex and difficult for others to guess. Typically, we suggest that you use a combination of alphabets, numbers and special characters (like !, @, #, $ etc.). We also strongly advise that you do not disclose your passwords to anyone or keep them written or recorded in any form which can be accessed by unauthorized third parties.

Further, we oblige you not to disclose any information relating to the Ventura Group, which is confidential in nature and obtained in the course of availing the services through the website, to any other person, in any manner whatsoever. If you do not comply with this obligation, we will deem it a serious breach of the terms of this Privacy Policy. Such a breach will entitle us to terminate the services that we offer you, without prejudice to any damages, to which you may be entitled otherwise.

Lastly, you must authorize us to exchange, share or part with all information related to your details and transaction history with our affiliates/banks/financial institutions/credit bureaus and agencies and participate in any telecommunication or electronic clearing network as may be required by law, customary practice, credit reporting, statistical analysis and credit scoring, verification or risk management. In all such cases, you shall not hold us liable for use or disclosure of this information.
