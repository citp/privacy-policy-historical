# Privacy policy

This privacy policy main purpose is to express the idea how we handle the information we collect from our customers during their visit of our website and usage of our services.

Verify-Email.org will keep confidential the sensitive information of the Customer.

Verify-Email.org will not disclose the Customer’s confidential data to any person except as permitted by this policy.

Using reliable degree of protection, Verify-Email.org safeguards the Customer confidential information against non permitted disclosure.

Verify-Email.org uses personal data only on instructions and on behalf of the Customer.

Verify-Email.org takes both organizational and technical security measures against unauthorized and unlawful manipulation of personal data of the Customer.

Necessary security measures against corruption or loss of personal information are also taken on the behalf of the Customer.

This privacy policy sets out the obligations that shall not apply to the confidential information of the Customer that is required to be disclosed by a regulatory body, governmental authority, stock exchange or by law.

All data, including email addresses verified via our website or services, is not disclosed to any third-parties as persons or organizations. We handle your information responsibly and with respect. All customers’ data is protected and kept safe.{jcomments off}
