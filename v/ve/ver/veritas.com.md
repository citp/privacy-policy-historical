# VERITAS >> Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy 

VERITAS Software believes the protection of consumer privacy is critical to the long-term success of Internet Commerce and is committed to protecting the online privacy of its customers. This policy, developed to promote trust and confidence in our website, explains: 

* What information our site gathers/tracks about you 
* How We Use The Information 
* With Whom We Share The Information 
* Internet Commerce 
* Our Opt-Out Policy 
* How to Correct or Update Your Personal Information 

VERITAS Software Corporation is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of non-VERITAS web sites to which www.veritas.com may link. By using this site, you agree to the terms of the VERITAS Software Corporation Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not use this site. 

What information our site gathers/tracks about you  Top  
In general, you can visit www.veritas.com without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. Our site does collect information such as IP address, browser type and operating system to help us better understand our audience, but this information does not personally identify you. 

We also use cookies to improve your experience with our site. A cookie is a very small text file placed on your hard disc that uniquely identifies you, recording your passwords, purchases and preferences, however, it does not actually contain any personal information. When you return to our site, cookies simply help us to retrieve any personal information or preferences you may have already provided to us. A cookie can only be read by the server that gave it to you and cannot be executed as code or deliver viruses. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies, but you can set your browser to alert you every time a cookie is offered, letting you decide whether or not to accept it. For More Information on cookies, visit [www.cookiecentral.com](http://www.cookiecentral.com/). 

We may ask you to provide certain information about yourself such as name, address, email address, phone number and other personally identifying information when you make a purchase in the VERITAS Software Store, download a demo, sign up for a seminar, register a product or ask us to contact you. Participation is completely optional. If you elect to give us personal information over the Internet, we will take reasonable precautions to ensure the security of your information. In addition, our web site offers encryption technology to help protect the transmission of customer information over the Internet in the VERITAS Online Store. 

How We Use The Information  Top  
First and foremost, the personal information you provide to us will be used for the purpose for which it was provided - to contact you, to process an order, to register your product, etc. We will also use and transfer this information (excluding credit card information) to VERITAS sites worldwide to improve our web site and to contact you regarding new products, technical updates, special offers and upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such communication from VERITAS Software Corporation, please see out opt-out policy below. Information such as IP address, browser type and operating system is analyzed for site trends and statistics. 

With Whom We Share The Information  Top  
From time to time, VERITAS Software Corporation may make certain personal information (excluding credit card information) available to our business partners whose products or services we think may interest you. If you do not wish to receive such communications from our business partners, please see our opt-out policy below. 

Internet Commerce  Top  
The online store is designed to give you control over the privacy of your name, address, email, credit card information and other information you provide to us. We offer SSL encryption, available through your browser, which is the industry standard in online security. For More Information, read our store privacy policy. 

The information you provide will be kept confidential and will be used only to support your customer relationship with VERITAS. Information about you or your order and the products you have purchased will not be disclosed or sold to any outside organization. 

Our Opt-Out Policy  Top  
If at any time you do not want to receive electronic communications from VERITAS Software Corporation and/or our business partners, please contact the webmaster with one of the following as the subject of the message and your exact name, address, email address and phone number for unsubscribing in the body of the message: \- Unsubscribe partner lists \- Unsubscribe VERITAS Software Corporation lists \- Unsubscribe all lists 

VERITAS Software Corporation will record your opt-out request in our web database and will not contact you on any electronic communications campaigns that draw from this system. We will also make this information available to business groups within our company worldwide who are committed to our privacy policy and to respecting your choice to opt out. However, due to the distributed nature of our business and systems, we cannot guarantee that your web-based opt out request will be honored by all of VERITAS Software Corporation's communications programs. We apologize for the situation and are working on projects to eliminate this problem in the future. 

How to Correct or Update Your Personal Information  Top  
If you need to correct or update your personal information registered with us, contact the webmaster and use "personal profile update" as the subject of the message. Please make sure to include your previously registered name, address, email address and phone number along with any new personal information in the body of the message. 

VERITAS Software Corporation will record your personal information change in our web database and will make this change available to business groups throughout our company worldwide. These groups are committed to updating your personal information, however, due to the distributed nature of our business and systems, we cannot guarantee that your web-based change request will update your information in all VERITAS Software Corporation systems. We apologize for this inconvenience and are working on projects to eliminate this problem in the future.
