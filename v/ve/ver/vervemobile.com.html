DATA COLLECTION AND USAGE

Personal Information

 We only collect Personal Information when you knowingly and voluntarily provide it to us, such as when you provide us feedback or request information from us. You do not have to provide Personal Information to use our Services and we do not collect Personal Information through our Services. Verve Mobile does not collect the phone number or the unique device IDs for mobile devices. However, Verve Mobile may deliver advertising that is targeted based on location or other user-entered information. The advertisements delivered by Verve Mobile may include forms or other prompts that allow users to opt-in to share data. However, it is your choice whether or not to provide that data. For example, we may ask you to provide your precise location information (e.g. longitude and latitude determined by your device through GPS, wifi hotspots or cellular towers). If you elect to provide us this precise location information, we may deliver advertising to you based on this precise location information; if you elect not to provide the precise location information, we will not collect it. To be clear, in addition to precise location information, the prompts may ask you to provide other information, including Personal Information, which we will receive only if you provide it or consent to our receipt through the prompt. Verve Mobile tracks unique users through a unique but anonymous hashcode that cannot be tracked back to a device or any Personal Information. Verve Mobile does not collect nor store the unique device ID of a mobile device. Notwithstanding the foregoing, a Publisher’s Mobile Offering may include text messages, in which case the Publisher will provide Verve Mobile with the applicable phone numbers.

However, if you use a particular Publisher application or mobile website, that application or mobile website may independently collect information from you. Notably, this policy does not cover such information collected by a Publisher through its application or mobile website. When using a particular application or mobile website, you should familiarize yourself with the given Publisher’s terms and conditions and privacy practices. Verve Mobile is not responsible for, and cannot control, a given Publisher’s privacy practices. Publishers may use Verve Mobile technology to request Personal Information from you.

When you opt-in to provide Personal Information to Verve Mobile customers or partners, Verve Mobile restricts access to Personal Information to employees, contractors and agents who need to know the information in order to operate, develop or improve our services and to service our customers. We take all reasonable steps to ensure that these parties comply with confidentiality obligations and do not use such information for any purpose other than providing such services to a Publisher.

Anonymous Information 
From Our Site:
We collect Anonymous Information automatically when you visit our Site both through the use of log files and alpha-numeric identifiers known as “cookies” that are usually stored on a user’s computer hard drive cookies. This Anonymous Information we collect via the Site includes your Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, device type and version and the date and time of your request. We also use “cookie” technology. We generally use cookies to help with customer tracking and to reduce the time it takes for pages to load into your browser. Our cookies do not contain Personal Information. We may use both session cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and/or persistent cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience. Persistent cookies can be removed or blocked by following the help file directions in your Internet browser. We also use log files and cookies to improve the quality of our Site and pages you have visited on the Site.

From Our Services:
Verve Mobile utilizes technology to uniquely identify most mobile devices to deliver better targeted marketing messages to consumers. We believe that the presentation of more relevant services assist in providing a more meaningful mobile web experience. For example, this technology enables Verve Mobile to limit the number of times a specific message is presented to a consumer (frequency capping), to support product category preferential marketing and to deliver advertising based on your general location (but not precise location) as determined, for example, through your IP address.
