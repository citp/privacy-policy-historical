It is our goal to respect your on-line privacy. We use tracking technology (cookies) only to gather information about the number of visitors to our Web sites and to assist in tailoring our Web sites to our visitors. Personal information cannot be collected via cookies. 

We collect personal information, like names, addresses, e-mail addresses, etc., when voluntarily submitted by our on-line visitors. The personal information you provide us is used to fulfill your request, provide you with information, or to market Nestlé's products, events, and services which may interest you. We may, occasionally, co-promote with other reputable organizations and companies, and you may receive a joint offer. Otherwise, information is not sold or transferred to third parties. Information is kept secure and you may have it revised or deleted as directed at the point of collection or, if you have e-mailed us in the past, you may send us a new e-mail requesting that we delete your name from our mailing list. If you would like to add your name to our mailing list you may also e-mail us. We may also be contacted at 1-800-452-1971. 

We have no intention of collecting personal information from children. Any changes to this policy will be posted.
