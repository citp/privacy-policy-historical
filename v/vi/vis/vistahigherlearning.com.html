Privacy Policy

 

This Privacy Policy describes the nonpublic personal information that Vista Higher Learning collects and what we do with it.  Vista Higher Learning reserves the rights, in its sole discretion, to change, modify, add or remove any portion of Privacy Policy in whole or in part, at any time.  Changes will be effective when notice of such change is posted at  http://www.vistahigherlearning.com/privacypolicy/.  Your continued use of the MAESTRO Supersite, Web-SAM, the VHL Direct Online Catalog (www.vhldirect.com) or www.vistahigherlearning .com (collectively, the “VHL Sites”) or any other products or services offered by Vista Higher Learning after any changes are posted will be considered acceptance of those changes.  By using the VHL Sites and any other products or services offered by Vista Higher Learning, you agree that you have reviewed, understand and accept this Privacy Policy, and that Vista Higher Learning may use you information as set forth below.

 

 

1. Information Vista Higher Learning collects.

 

Maestro™ Supersite & Web-SAM

 

Vista Higher Learning gathers certain personal information about all users during the Maestro™ Supersite and Web-SAM registration and technical support process.  Information collected from instructors during this process will include full name, e-mail address, school affiliation, user name and one password.  An instructor’s school affiliation includes country (if non-US only), state/province (if reside within the US), school type, and full institutional name.

 

Information collected during the student registration and technical support process will include full name and e-mail address, and either one Maestro™ Supersite and/or one Web-SAM user name and passcode.  A student’s school information, which includes country (if non-US only), state/province (if reside within the US), school type, full institutional name, and course/class enrollment status may also be collected.

 

Once the registration process is complete, MAESTRO™ Supersite and Web-SAM offers users the ability to correct or change some of the above personal information.  Users may update or review such information at any time by selecting the "Preferences" link within a registered Maestro™ Supersite or Web-SAM account.

 

In addition, the MAESTRO™ Supersite and Web-SAM offer instructors and students the ability to complete substantive course work on such sites and offer instructors the ability to store grade information on such sites.

 

Vista Higher Learning has engaged Presidium Learning, Inc. to provide technical support regarding use of the Maestro™ Supersite or Web-SAM.  Presidium may request information from or about you in order to provide technical support that you request.  Any information provided to Presidium is subject to Presidium’s privacy and security practices and is not protected by this Privacy Policy.  Vista Higher Learning urges you to contact Presidium regarding their practices prior to disclosing any information to Presidium, as Vista Higher Learning is not responsible for Presidium’s privacy and security practices.   

 

VHL Direct Online Catalog

 

Vista Higher Learning is pleased to offer students the opportunity to order their required course materials directly from our VHL Direct Online Catalog, Vista Higher Learning’s online bookstore (www.vhldirect.com).  If you make a purchase from VHL Direct Online Catalog, Vista Higher Learning will share your credit card information and any other information required to process your transaction, such as name, email address, shipping address, billing address and school name with its third party payment processor, Authorize.net,.  Vista Higher Learning urges you to review Authorize.net’s privacy policy.  (See www.authorize.net/company/privacy).  Vista Higher Learning is not responsible for Authorize.net’s privacy and security practices.  

 

Vista Higher Learning does not knowingly sell products or services to anyone under the age of 18 years via the VHL Direct Online Catalog.  If you are under 18 years of age, you should use VHL Direct Online Catalog only with the consent or involvement of a parent or guardian.

 

General

 

Certain identifying information is automatically collected when you visit the VHL Sites.  We may collect identifying information that allows us to track the number of visitors we receive and the way they navigate through the VHL Sites.  The types of identifying information collected include the date and time you accessed a VHL Site, the page or pages you visited and images you accessed, the website you arrived to the VHL Site from and the website that you depart to as well as the IP address of the computer being used to access the VHL Site.  We may use IP addresses and anonymous demographic information in the aggregate to share with advertisers and market researchers, and to improve the design of the VHL Sites to better serve our customers.  While it may be possible to correlate this information to particular individuals, we do not generally do so.  We may share this aggregated information with other third parties.  We reserve the right to drill down into anonymous demographic information in order to investigate unlawful conduct and/or breaches of this Privacy Policy.

3. Security

 

Username and Passcodes

 

Instructors and students must keep their user names and passwords private and may not share them with any third party, even on a temporary basis.  Vista Higher Learning cannot be responsible for unauthorized access to accounts gained through use of user names and passwords obtained from the authorized user.  If you suspect that your password has been compromised, please contact Vista Higher Leaning immediately.

 

General

 

While Vista Higher Learning strives to protect nonpublic personal information and takes reasonable steps to ensure the security, integrity and confidentiality of such information disclosed to us, we cannot guarantee the security, integrity or confidentiality of any information you disclose and you do so at your own risk.  Vista Higher Learning does, however, use 128-bit SSL encryption for transmission of sensitive information on the VHL Direct Online Catalog site, which has been certified as a thawte trusted site.

 

 

4. Cookies & Internet Browser settings

 

We may use cookies.  “Cookies” are small pieces of data that can be stored by an Internet browser for visitor tracking purposes. Cookies cannot access your hard drive or send information about your computer system. Cookies are just simply a data storage system.  We use them to study visitors’ use of the VHL Sites and to improve your navigation of it.  Most Web browsers accept cookies, but the “help” menu on your browser should give you options for preventing, accepting, or receiving notice of new cookies. You can configure your web-browser to disable cookies, but doing so may impair your use of the VHL Sites.  Vista Higher Learning recommends that its customers configure Internet browsers to enable session cookies and occasionally clear temporary Internet files.

 

By enabling cookies, the Maestro™ Supersite and Web-SAM will allow customers to take advantage of some essential features found within the sites, from the ability to log in to the site(s), to utilizing some of the interactive features available within the site(s).
