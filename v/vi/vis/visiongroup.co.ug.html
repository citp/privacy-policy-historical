WEBSITE POLICY

Vision Group is committed to safeguarding the privacy of its online content users and visitors. This policy explains use of any personal data provided by or collected from users or visitors.

Information we collect

In operating the website, Vision Group may collect and process the following data:
  • Information about your computer and about your visits to and use of this website including your IP address, geographical location, browser type, referral source, details of visit such as time, length, number of pages, resources accessed;
  • Information provided in the course of filling in forms on our websites or information provided in the course of registering;
  • Personal data or information provided while visiting the websites; and
  • Information provided for the purpose of subscribing to the websites services such as newsletters, email notifications including email addresses but not limited to traffic data, location data, the weblogs and other communication data.

Use of Information

Vision Group collects and stores user information primarily to enable provision of quality service. In addition, we may use the information for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with information requested, relating to our products or services and on other products which we feel may be of interest to you, where you have consented to receive such information.
  • To meet our contractual commitments to you.
  • To notify you about any changes to our website such as improvements or service/product changes that may affect our service. Vision Group may contact existing customers with information about goods and services.


    • Further, Vision Group may use your data or permit selected third parties to use your data to provide you with information about unrelated goods or services considered to be of interest to you. Contact may be made through the methods consented to at the point of data collection. Vision Group will only contact or allow third parties to contact new customers with their consent and only by means consented to.

    Disclosures

    Vision Group may disclose, use, preserve or allow access to your information as is reasonably necessary to:
    • Enforce applicable Terms of service including investigating possible violations
    • Detect, establish, prevent or address fraud, security or other illegal concerns
    • Prospective purchaser of any business asset which we are selling or contemplating selling such as but not limited within mergers and acquisitions.


    Personal Information

    Personal data submitted on this website will be used for the purposes specified in this policy or on other parts of the website as displayed.

    All reasonable steps shall be taken to ensure protection and security of your data however by submitting your personal data, you agree to use, transfer, storage and process of the same. This includes use of personal information provided when submitting for publication on the website which may be published on the internet and may be available via internet around the world.

    We may use your personal information to:
    • Administer the website
    • Improve your browsing experience by personalizing the website
    • Enable your use of the services available on the website
    • Supply you services via the website
    • Send statements and invoices to and collect payments from you
    • Send you general (non marketing) commercial communications
    • Send you email notifications specifically requested
    • Send you newsletters and other marketing communications relating to our businesses or relating to third party businesses we consider to be relevant and which may be of interest to you provided you have consented to this. Users may also inform us if they are no longer interested in the communications
    • Provide or process on behalf of third parties such as our advertisers information about our users however users will not be individually identified
    • Deal with any enquiries and complaints made by or about you


    With your express permission your information may be used for publication or for such other purposes as you consent to. An opt out option will be provided where it is proposed to use your personal information for any purposes other than those described in this Policy or other notice.

    International data transfer
    Information collected may be stored, processed in and transferred between countries in which we operate and personal information may be processed on or from a server outside your country for use in accordance with this policy and the relevant laws. Accepting the terms of this policy is express agreement to this transfer, use, collection and storage of personal information

    Third Party Links
    This Privacy policy applies only to Vision Group services. Vision Group does not exercise control over other sites as displayed and is neither responsible for nor liable under the same. It is the duty of the user to check third party privacy policies.

    Cookies
    Vision Group does not use cookies however third party websites visited such as advertisers’ websites may or may not use them. Cookies uniquely identify your browser and may be downloaded when you click on the third party website. These may be used for research, service delivery improvement or for other reasons. Vision Group does not control third party websites nor the cookies placed thereon, therefore it is the duty of users to check for cookie use on visited websites. Third parties have no access to contact information stored on Vision Group website unless the user chooses to share it with them

In event of non compliance, Vision Group is not liable for any consequences, losses, damages suffered and stands indemnified against any such claims by agreement to the terms and conditions of this policy.

Unreasonable, repetitive requests may be rejected where the technical resources are unavailable, where the privacy of others if jeopardized, where impractical or unnecessary.
