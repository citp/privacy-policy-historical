# Privacy Policy

VisitingDC has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: www.visitingdc.com. 

This statement is subject to change without notice and users of our service are expected to review this page on a regular basis. 

**IP Address**

An IP (Internet Protocol) address is a unique identifier for a computer on a particular network; including the Internet. When you log on to the Internet, your computer is issued with an IP address. 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, for security purposes and to administer our Web site. 

**Cookies**

The main purpose of cookies is to identify Internet users and possibly prepare customized web content for them. They can be used for both benign and malicious means. 

VisitingDC.com does not use cookies. Some of our advertisers do issue cookies. 

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. 

**Links to Other Sites**

This site contains links to other sites. VisitingDC is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

**Security**

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Security of our site is controlled by our hosting service - NYI.net. 

**Legal Issues**

We may disclose personal information in response to a relevant legal process, for example, in response to a court order or in response to a law enforcement agency's formal request. 

**Childrens' Usage**

VisitingDC.com strongly urges parents to monitor children's usage of the Web, including this web site. 

**Mailing Lists**

We do not collect mailing addresses or maintain a mailing list of our users. 

**Abuse/Inappropriate Communications** b>

We reserve the right to forward abusive and inappropriate communications to the service providers of the originating communication and to relevant local, state, federal and international authorities. 

**Choice/Opt-Out**

We do not collect or maintain information about our users. We do not email or contact our users, except in response to user emails. Our advertisers may collect information about users if ads are clicked.
