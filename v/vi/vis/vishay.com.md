# Vishay Site Privacy Policy

| 

This is the web site of **Vishay Intertechnology, Inc**. Our postal address is 63 Lincoln Highway, Malvern, PA,19355-2120 USA. We can be reached via e-mail at [webmaster@vishay.com](mailto:webmaster@vishay.com), or you can reach us by telephone at 610-644-1300.  


We appreciate your interest in our products and your visit to our web site, www.vishay.com.  


There are times we may need information from you, for example, in order to send you catalogs, brochures, etc. If you give us personal information, we will collect and store that information. If you review or download information, we track the visit in order to provide you with tailored information and to give us information about which part of the site is frequented.

We do not sell any of the information collected or stored about you.  


The web site may contain links to non-Vishay Intertechnology, Inc. web sites. Vishay Intertechnology, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices of such web sites.
