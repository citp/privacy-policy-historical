What Information Do We Collect? 

When you register with us and/or visit the site, you generally provide us with two types of information: Firstly, the information you actively choose to disclose ("Active Information") and secondly, the information collected in an invisible way to you on an aggregate, anonymous basis as you browse the site ("Passive Information"). When we refer to the "personally identifiable information," we mean information that can be linked to a specific individual, such as a name, address, telephone number, or email address. Sharing personally identifiable information with us is entirely optional.

The Site is not intended to be used by anyone under 13, except with the explicit permission and under the supervision of a parent or guardian who accepts full legal responsibility for the minor's use. No individual under 13 cannot disclose information on this site. We don't collect or use personally identifiable information from children under 13 knowingly. You may also choose to provide us with your Zip code and city name or other location information to receive information about products and services in your desired area, and you may choose to provide your age, gender, and other information to receive information about products and services which may be of your interest.

Passive Information We Collect!

Our site utilizes a standard technology called "cookies" to collect information about how the site is being used. Passive Information gathered by use may include your Internet service provider, browser type, operating system, the date and time of visits, location of your searches, the site pages viewed, time spent at our Site, and the sites visited just before and just after the site. Passive Information is collected on an aggregate basis without any association to your personal information so that your identity remains anonymous. If you do not wish to transmit "cookie" information about yourself, you may turn off the cookie function in your web browser; please consult the "Help" section of your browser to correctly do so. If you choose to turn off the cookies, however, and/or if you elect not to share certain information with us, you may not be able to access certain features of the Site and your use of some portions of the site may be affected.

What is a Cookie?

Cookies are a web browser (Internet Explorer, Firefox, etc.) feature software that allows web servers to recognize the computer used to access a site. They are really small pieces of data that are stored by a user's web browser on one site to simplify subsequent interactions with that site by the same user or to use the information to streamline the user's transaction on related web pages. This makes it easier for a user to move from site to site and to complete transactions over the Internet. Cookies make your online experience easier and more personalized.

How Do We Use the Information Collected?

**Active Information**

The Active Information provided by you help us in delivering the content, features, advertising, services, or the information requested. If you provide us with your email address, we will send you the information or other messages related to your activities on the site or about our services. We may retain the Active Information you provide and a record of the service or information provided. Any Active Information retained would be used to assist the delivery of the content, features, advertising, services, or information and to optimize your experience and enhance the quality of the time you spend with us online. We may use the Active Information provided by users about the changes in the site, new services, features or products we offer, or other information we think you will find valuable. In case you wish to dis-continue receiving such information, please send us an email at privacy@viscoupons.com. When you send us a message, we retain the content of the message, your email address and any information you may provide to us, including your experience with the site, and your contact preferences as well as the response we send back to you. This helps us to manage any follow-up questions you may have, measure how effectively we address your concerns, and improve the Site. 

We may share or permit access to Active Information with persons we employ directly or as contractors or agents, partners, or affiliates at our direction, for purposes of administering our business activities, processing information, fulfilling coupon requests, providing marketing assistance, providing customer services such as support, and making available other product or service offers or deals we think may be of interest to you. If we are acquired by another company, your personally identifiable information will be shared with our new owner, but it will remain a subject to the terms of then-applicable privacy policy.

Passive Information

We use Passive Information to enhance and design our site to make it easier, faster and friendlier to use, and to optimize your experience and enhance the quality of the time you spend with us online. Passive Information is collected on an aggregate basis and we do not connect any of this information to your email address or other personally identifiable information. However, Passive Information may result in your viewing of particular content, features, advertising, services, or information based on your user habits or your viewing on the site.
