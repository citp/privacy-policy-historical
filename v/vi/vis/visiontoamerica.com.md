### Your Secrecy (Privacy)

Your privacy is very important to Vision to America. We do not collect personal information without your knowledge. The only information collected is that which you provide for fulfilling your purchases. Your information will not be sold, but kept privately with Vision to America and our publishers. If you have any questions about the confidentiality of your information, please [contact us.](http://www.dominiondownloads.com/contact-us.aspx)  
  


### Your Security

Your payment and personal information is always safe. Transactions are processed securely using our [Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Secure_Sockets_Layer), the industry standard and among the best software available today for secure ecommerce transactions. It encrypts all of your personal information, including credit card number, name, and address, so that it cannot be read over the Internet.

On top of that, you may also use [PayPal](http://www.paypal.com/us/)—the most respected, secure, easy way to pay—for all purchases.
