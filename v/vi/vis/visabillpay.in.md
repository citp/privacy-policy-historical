What are cookies and how does Visa Bill Pay use them?   
****  
Cookies are pieces of information that a Web site transfers to your computer's hard disk for record-keeping purposes. Cookies can make the Web more useful by storing information about your preferences on a particular site. The use of cookies is an industry standard, and many major Web sites use them to provide useful features for their customers. Cookies in and of themselves do not personally identify users, although they do identify a user's computer. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. If you'd prefer, you can set yours to refuse cookies. However, you may not be able to take full advantage of a Web site if you do so. 

Currently Visa Bill Pay does not use cookies but may do so in the future uses cookies to track user traffic patterns and to help us assist you with questions about site navigation, functionality or performance.

Sweepstakes, contests, games, and surveys  
****  
From time to time, Visa Bill Pay may conduct sweepstakes, contests, games or other promotions, and also conduct surveys through the Web site. If you choose to participate—unless otherwise noted in the applicable rules, terms and conditions, or disclaimers—we may use the information you provide in connection with the administration of the applicable promotion, including for selection and notification of sweepstakes or contest winners and for creation of new promotions, and/or to publish survey results in aggregate on the site. 

Promotions that run on Visa Bill Pay may be sponsored or cosponsored by entities / services other than Visa Bill Pay. Some or all data collected during a promotion may be shared with the sponsor. If data will be shared, you will be notified prior to the time of data collection or transfer. You can decide not to participate in the promotion if you don't want your data to be shared. 

Except where necessary to administer a sweepstakes, contest, game or other promotion, or survey, unless you have otherwise agreed or unless required by law, we will never permanently record or publish any response you give connected to your name or address. We will hold these responses only as anonymous statistics.

SECURITY  
****  
How we protect your personal information  
****  
The security of your personal information is very important to us, and we are committed to protecting the information we collect.

Visa Bill Pay will work with your biller institution to present your bills, account balances and history to you on your Visa Bill Pay member pages. In order to present your billing information to you, Visa Bill Pay receives that information from your financial institution and presents it to you over a secure-server. As with all personally identifiable information, we do not make this available to any third parties without your permission. Any user statistics that we may provide to prospective partners regarding service usage on Visa Bill Pay are provided in the aggregate only and do not include any personally identifiable information about any individual user.

****  
Security precautions in place to protect the loss, misuse, or alteration of your information  
****  
Your Visa Bill Pay account information is password-protected so that you and only you have access to this personal information. You may edit your Visa Bill Pay account information by using your Visa Bill Pay User ID and password. 

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the appropriate use of information, Visa Bill Pay has put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure all Personal Information that Visa Bill Pay collects online. To learn more about our security[ click here. ](https://web.archive.org/web/20071011024328id_/http%3A//www.visabillpay.in/jsp/security_vbp.htm)

We recommend that you do not divulge your password or your secret question and answer to anyone. Visa Bill Pay will never ask you for your password in an unsolicited phone call or in an unsolicited email. Also remember to sign out of your Visa Bill Pay account and close your browser window when you have finished your work. This is to ensure that others cannot access your personal information and correspondence if you share a computer with someone else or are using a computer in a public place like a library or Internet cafe. 

Visa Bill Pay uses firewalls and Secure Socket Layers for all personal data we collect from you. We also employ many different security techniques to protect personal data against loss, misuse, alteration, and unauthorized access. But we cannot guarantee perfect security of your personal information.
