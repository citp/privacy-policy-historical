# Sysprogs - Privacy Policy

Last revised: May 23, 2018

### Who we are

Sysprogs OÜ, controls the personal data you submit to us and is responsible for your personal data under applicable data protection law.  
We can be contacted via the [contact form](https://sysprogs.com/contact/) on our website.

### What we collect

We may collect information about the use of this website. We use this information to improve our website and services. This information is not personally linked to you.

We may also collect your contact data and payment data. This information is used to handle your orders and provide support.

We may collect the following personal information when you place an order with us:

  * Full name.
  * Company name and VAT ID if applicable.
  * Address.
  * Country.
  * Email address.
  * Phone number and fax number.
  * IP address.
  * Payment processing information.



This information is collected by [ 2Checkout](http://www.2checkout.com/) (our online payment processor) in order to process and fullfill your order and is further processed by us in order to validate the orders. We do not have access to credit card numbers, or any sensitive payment-related information, as it is handled directly by our payment processor.

For each Sysprogs license we store the following information:

  * Contact name.
  * Company name if applicable.
  * Contact email address.
  * Purchase date.
  * The purchased product, edition and the amount of seats purchased.
  * Unique IDs of seats on which the license has been activated, together with the activation dates.



This information is used to verify the status of your license, technical support, and issue automatic renewal/upgrade quotes. 

We collect the following information when you contact us:

  * Contact information such as name and email address
  * Support correspondence, including any attachments (e.g. proof of academic status)



This information is used to provide answers to your inquiries.

### How we handle your information

All information you provide to us is stored on our secure servers. We implement appropriate measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful access.

### Sharing information

We may disclose your personal information to appropriate third parties. Such third parties include the order and payment processing companies we use and to comply with legal requirements. We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your personal information may be processed, stored or transferred outside your country of residence and beyond the European Union or the European Economic Area. However, personal information will continue to be protected in accordance with this privacy policy and EU standard contractual clauses.

### How will the personal information be used

We may process personal information for the following purposes: for providing services to you, providing support, meeting our legal obligations (e.g. bookkeeping, accounting), managing our IT systems, ensuring the security of our systems and improving our services.

### How long will we store the personal information

We will retain your personal information for as long as it required to fulfill the purposes as outlined in this privacy policy. Personal information for order processing is retained in compliance with the bookkeeping rules in your country and the EU.

### What are your rights

You have the right to object to the processing of your personal information in certain situations e.g. for marketing purposes at any time.  
Depending on where you live, you may have additional privacy rights.  
For example under EU laws you have the following additional rights:

  * to access information we hold about you;
  * to request that we delete your personal information from our system unless legally required;
  * to ask us to rectify/correct your personal information if appropriate;
  * to ask us to restrict processing of your personal information;
  * to transmit your personal information to another entity;
  * to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.



You can exercise these rights by filing a GDPR request via our [support form](https://sysprogs.com/support/?topic=gdpr) or via the email addresses specified on our [ contact page](https://sysprogs.com/contact/).

### Security

We are commited to keeping your information secure. We have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

### Links to other websites

This website may contain links to other websites. We have no control over the content and privacy policies of other websites. You are responsible for checking the privacy policy on these websites.
