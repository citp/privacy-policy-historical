Viraless may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. Viraless may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Viraless does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Viraless uses cookies to help Viraless identify and track visitors, their usage of Viraless website, and their website access preferences. Viraless visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Viraless' websites, with the drawback that certain features of Viraless' websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
