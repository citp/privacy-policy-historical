# Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

ViralCoop.com makes use of browser "cookies" to enable us to track referrals. The small snippet of text stored in your browser is only used to track who referred you to our web site and contains no personally identifying information about you.

If you become a member of the Viral Coop or opt-in to one of our mailing lists, your contact information is never shared or sold. It is used only for sending you information relating to our services.

We use PayPal.com for payment processing, so if you make a purchase of our service, your payment information is never seen by ViralCoop.com. It is processed by PayPal, who is a leading and trusted 3rd party payment processor.

No payment information is ever stored on our web site.

If you have any questions about how information you provide us is used, don't hesitate to [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20110105135138id_/http%3A//www.viralcoop.com/contact.html).
