## 1\. Introduction

Here at VictoriaPlum.com, we are 100% committed to respecting the privacy rights of visitors to our website www.victoriaplum.com.

This policy, together with our terms and conditions, are designed to give a clear explanation of how we aggregate, cache and process the personal data we collect from you when you visit our website, or at any other time you provide us with your personal data. By visiting our website you are accepting and consenting to the practices set out in this policy.

By definition, personal information includes any recorded information that identifies you personally which includes things such as your name, address or email address.

Unless clearly stated in this privacy policy, VictoriaPlum.com will not disclose, share, lease or sell your personal information to any third party unrelated to us without your prior permission, other than as required by law.

For the purposes of the Data Protection Act 1998, the data controller is Victoria Plum Ltd, Bridgehead Business Park, Hessle, HU13 0DG.

## 2\. What legal basis do we rely on regarding your data?

The law on data protection contains several different reasons for which a company may collect and process your personal data, including:

**Contractual obligations**

In some scenarios, we will need your personal data to comply with contractual obligations. For example, if you place an order with us, your address details will be collected so we can deliver your purchase and may also be passed onto third party providers to complete your delivery.

**Consent**

In some instances, we collect personal data for processing with your consent. For example, when you “opt in” by tick box to our marketing or promotional newsletters.

When we collect this information, we will make clear which data is necessary regarding the specific service.

  * Legal compliance



This may be required in the event of fraud or criminal activity affecting VictoriaPlum.com, or any other legal reason, we may need to transfer details of individuals involved to law enforcement.

  * Legitimate interest



In some situations, we will use your personal data to pursue legitimate interests as expected as part of running a business, and will not materially impact your rights, freedom or interests.

## 3\. Which personal information we collect

We’ll ask for your personal information when you fill in any forms on our website or when you correspond with us via email, phone or otherwise. This includes information you provide when you:

  * Make an online purchase
  * Register with VictoriaPlum.com
  * Sign up for promotions, offers, competitions, or to receive news

  * Open a Trade Account
  * When you choose to complete any surveys, we send to you
  * When you comment on or review our services or products
  * When you enter into a prize draw with us
  * When you ask a VictoriaPlum.com team member to contact you about a service or product

## 6\. How we use cookies

**What are cookies?**

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your PC, phone or other web-enabled device when you visit our website. They’re not harmful and don’t contain any confidential information such as your home address, date of birth or credit card details.

Cookies are an essential part of how our website works. Some of these cookies are required by our site to enable you to transact whilst other cookies enable us to give you an enhanced, personalised web experience. By using our website, you also consent to the use of these cookies. Cookies are used on our site for the following reasons:

  * To allow you to securely sign in to your VictoriaPlum.com account, so that you can use "My Account" features such as order information, making payments and receiving onsite offers.
  * To store the content of your online shopping basket whilst you browse the site and to complete an order.
  * To record the areas of the website that you’ve visited, products you’ve viewed and time spent browsing, as well as the products you purchased. We use this information to help make the website more user-friendly, develop our site design and to continuously improve the quality of the service we provide. The information may be provided to other Walker Group companies and/or the third parties referenced in the "Disclosure of your information" section above.
  * To personalise our website content for you.
  * To distribute visitors to our site evenly across platforms to ensure the content is served at the fastest possible speed.



We also offer you the facility to share your experience on our website through social media sites such as Facebook and Twitter. By using these features, you are also consenting to allow cookies from these providers. More information about how these providers use cookies can be found on their websites.

**Are cookies safe?**

VictoriaPlum.com uses cookies to ensure that we provide the best possible standard of service to our online customers. However, as part of our privacy policy, we require your consent to use them.

To withdraw your consent, or if you want to be notified each time a cookie is about to be used, you should amend the settings provided in your web browser to prevent us from storing cookies on the hard drive of your computer. For information on how to disable cookies, please consult the "Help" tab of your browser via the menu bar.

If you choose not to consent to the use of cookies, your experience of our site will be severely impaired and many integral aspects of the site, including (but not limited to) adding items to your shopping basket and accessing your account, will not work at all.

**Our use of advertisement tracking**

We also allow selected third parties who participate with us in marketing programmes to place cookies when you visit our site. These cookies enable us to monitor which adverts you see and click on to get to our site. We currently use two companies for this and you can see their privacy policies at the following addresses: Adwords: http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/ads/

These cookies are as safe as the ones used whilst you browse our site and neither of these third parties collects any personal data from which they would be able to identify individual customers.

We’re not responsible for the content or privacy policies of third parties or other websites.

## 9\. What are your rights over your personal data?

You have the right to request:

  * Right to request access to any personal information we have on you.
  * The Correction of any personal data we hold on you, when this is inaccurate, out of date or incomplete.
  * When you withdraw consent, or object to processing, and we do not have an overriding legitimate interest to keep your data.
  * We will stop through either specific or all channels or direct marketing using your personal data if requested.
  * We stop any processing of your personal data which consent is based on after you withdraw consent.



You have the right to request a copy of any information about you that VictoriaPlum.com hold at any time and to have that information corrected if it is inaccurate. To ask for your information, please contact the Compliance Manager in one of the following ways:

**By post:**

Compliance Manager VictoriaPlum.com Bridgehead Business Park Hessle HU13 0DG

**By email:**

To ask for your information, please contact complaincemanager@victoriaplum.com

To request information to be changes, or updated, please contact our customer service team, or update your online account.

In the event that we choose not to action your request, we will explain why we have refused your request.

At any point you have the right to withdraw consent.

In the event you want to withdraw consent to use your personal data, you have the right to withdraw at any time. For business purposes we will rely on legitimate interest.

In order to operate as a business, there are times we will process your personal data through legitimate interests. You may request for seasons of your personal situation to stop processing. We will comply with this request, unless we believe we have an overriding legitimate interest.

**Direct marketing**

At any time you may request we stop using your personal data through all, or selected direct marketing channels. We will comply with your request.
