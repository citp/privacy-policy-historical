Privacy Policy
Last updated: August 12, 2009

Video Game Countdown knows that you care about how your information is used. We strive to collect only information that is necessary for you to enjoy this website. The following is our privacy policy.

What information does Video Game Countdown gather?

We gather information that you give us, such as username, password, and email address. We also use cookies to store information while you are visiting Video Game Countdown. Cookies are data stored on your hard drive, such as a cookie that stores what your username is.

Does Video Game Countdown Share the Information It Receives?

We are not in the business of selling or sharing customer information. Any information you provide will only be used at Countdown Network, LLC websites.
