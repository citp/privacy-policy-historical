Pro-G Media, operator of VideoGamer.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for all of our Internet-based services.

### Personal info

The following types of personal data, may be stored by us if you choose to sign-up to the website or connect via Facebook:

  * Full name
  * E-mail address
  * IP address
  * Browser software information
  * Date of Birth (Optional)
  * Your social/gaming network username or ID (Optional. May include Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus, PSN, Xbox Live or Steam)



### Tracking technologies

We use third party analytical tools to help us to understand how you and other users consume the information on our website. Most of these tools use JavaScript to log page views and other such statistical information about your visit to the site. This data is entirely anonymous, we do not store or use any personally identifiable data about you during this process.

We collect this data to enable us to improve the user experience across the site.

### How we use your info

If you create an account with us, your personal information is used to personalise your experience on the website and give you the ability to interact with us and other users. We use your IP address and browser information to help diagnose problems with our server and to gather broad demographic information.

### When we share your info

We **do not** generally share personally identifiable information (such as name, address, email or phone) with other companies except in these limited circumstances:

  * You provide your express consent,
  * when we have a good faith belief it is required by law,
  * when we have a good faith belief it is necessary to protect our rights or property, or
  * to any successor or purchaser in a merger, acquisition, liquidation, dissolution or sale of assets. Your consent will not be required for disclosure in these cases, but we will attempt to notify you, to the extent permitted by law to do so.



### How we store your info

Personal information is stored on a secure database server, sensitive information such as a password is encrypted and non-reversible.

### Cookies

Information gathered through the use of cookies is not related to any personally identifiable details. Many browsers are set to accept cookies. You may prefer to set your browser to refuse cookies. It is possible, however, that some areas of the site will not function properly if you do so. [Definition of a cookie](http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&lr=&ie=UTF-8&newwindow=1&oi=defmore&q=define:Cookie).

### Accessing or removing your data

At any point you may request a copy of the personal data we stored about you, you may also request for such data to be entirely removed from our database by contacting us.
