# Startupbank.com: Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is designed to assist you in understanding how this site, Startupbank.com collects, uses, and safeguards the personal information you provide to Startupbank.com and to assist you in making purchases.  If you have any questions about this privacy policy feel free to contact Startupbank.com. 

**What Information Do We Collect and How Do We Use it?  
  
**When you visit this site you may provide Startupbank.com with two types of information: personal information you actively choose to disclose ("Active Information") by way of making a purchase or contacting Startupbank.com.  Another type is ‘use’ information collected.  This information is collected in a method not visible to you as you read through Startupbank.com, which is information collected on an aggregate anonymous basis as you and others browse this site ("Passive Information").

**Active Information You Disclose For Purchase** If you make a purchase, Startupbank.com collects your name, billing address, billing phone number and email information. Startupbank.com uses such information only for processing your order. Your information is only used for processing your order. Your information will never be sold or used in any other or for any other use. 

We use  [ 2Checkout](http://www.2checkout.com/aboutus.htm) for payments made to us.  Neither Startupbank.com nor  2CheckOut   store any credit card numbers after your order is processed.  2CheckOut is a leader in the industry for processing secure transactions and uses the industry standard SSL encryption for data handling.  We have selected them based on their high standards for data security. 

We use [ PayPal](http://www.paypal.com/cgi-bin/webscr?cmd=p/gen/ua/policy_privacy-outside) as an accepted method of payment for items purchased through Startupbank.com.  Please refer to Paypal’s privacy policy for information on how your information in used by their company.  
  
**Passive Information Collected **The Startupbank.com site utilizes a standard technology referred to as ‘cookies’ to collect information about how this site is used. Passive Information gathered may include information such as: the date and time of visits, the site pages viewed, time spent at this site, the sites visited just before and just after this site. Passive Information is collected on an aggregate basis without any association to your personal information so that you remain anonymous. If you do not wish to transmit ‘cookie’ information about yourself, you may turn off the ‘cookie’ function in your web browser; please consult the ‘Help’ section of your browser do so correctly. 

We use Passive Information to gather information from users, to enhance and design this site to make it easier, faster and friendlier to use. Additionally, cookies help Startupbank.com know information about the number of people that visit this site, when they visit and how they use this site. No connection of this information is made to your personal information or email address ever.

**What is a Cookie?  
  
**Cookies are a feature of web browser (Internet Explorer, Netscape Navigator, etc.) software that allows web servers to recognize the computer used to access a site. They are small pieces of data that are stored by a user's web browser on one site to simplify subsequent interactions with that site by the same user or to use the information to streamline the user's transaction on related web pages. This makes it easier for a user to move from site to site and to complete transactions over the Internet. Cookies should make your online experience easier and more personalized. Cookies contain information such as if you have been to this site before, when you have been to this site, and what you have viewed on this site previously. 

**Sharing Information with the Government or As Otherwise Required by Law  
  
**We may be required by law or government agency to disclose both Active and Passive Information you have provided to Startupbank.com and will do so if required by law or subpoena, or for enforcement of or claims regarding our purchase agreement license.

**How Do We Secure Active Information and Passive Information?  
  
**We secure your personal information submitted by you using reasonable efforts to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, or accidental loss or destruction, anathema to us, of Active Information and Passive Information. Other communications you may have with Startupbank.com via email or snail mail may not be secure unless Startupbank.com advises you that security measures are in place prior communicating with you. Therefore, if you choose to communicate with Startupbank.com through these means, you are assuming the risk of doing so and Startupbank.com requests that you do not send or post sensitive information through these means of communication.

We reserve the right to revise this policy at our own discretion. Please check back to this Privacy Policy page for this site, as this policy may be revised from time to time without notice to you.
