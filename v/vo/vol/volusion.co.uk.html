Cookie Policy (May 23, 2012)

Volusion respects the privacy of our website visitors and never shares your individual personal information with third parties. We use cookies that track and analyse usage, as well as volume statistical visitor information to optimise visitor experience through understanding visitor behaviour whilst on our site.

Volusion also uses third party cookies to track the performance of our advertising so we can serve more relevant messages to our site visitors.

Due to their importance in enhancing and enabling usability or site processes, disabling cookies may prevent you from using certain parts of this website. If you don't wish to accept the cookies we serve, you may, at any time, adjust your cookie settings available within your individual browsers to reflect these preferences. For further information on disabling cookies in your browser, please click here.

Cookies We Set

The cookies we set when visitors sign up for a free trial allow us to provide visitors with an optimum user experience by serving more relevant advertising. These cookies may contain information about: log-in IDs, identifying which version of a webpage visitors prefer during testing, what pages audiences enter our site from, and which of our partners referred them to us whilst allowing us to analyse what users do on our site.

Third Party Cookies

Third party cookies are used to track the performance of our marketing and advertising. This information is shared with third parties anonymously and doesn't include personal information. However, this information may be associated with personal information from external sources after the company receives it.
