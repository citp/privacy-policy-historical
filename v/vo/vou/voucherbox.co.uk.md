# Privacy Policy » Voucherbox.co.uk

Voucherbox.co.uk takes your privacy seriously. Please read our full policy below to learn more. Voucherbox.co.uk contains links to third party stores and websites. If you follow a link to any of these websites, please note that these websites have their own privacy policies and are not affiliated with Voucherbox.co.uk. It is your responsibility to check these policies before you submit any personal data to these websites.

### 1\. Information collected on Voucherbox.co.uk

You are not required to provide any information in order to use Voucherbox.co.uk. However some services may not be available without providing your email address or equivalent information.

### 2\. Email address

If you register to recieve email updates or offers from Voucherbox.co.uk we will hold this email address on our systems. You can opt out of our emails at any point by using the un-subscribe option in the newsletter or contacting us directly. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise provide your email address to a third party.

### 3\. IP and general computer information

We may collect information about your IP address in order to optimise the site and user experience. We may analyse details of your visits to our site or your use of the app, including, but not limited to, traffic data, location data and other communication data and the resources that you access. This data is anonymised and is not linked to any personal information.

### 4\. Security

We ensure that our systems and processes are buit with your security in mind. The information collected will only be used for the purposes here.

### 5\. Cookies

We may obtain information about your usage of the website or the app by using a cookie file. Websites use cookies to make the browsing experience more efficient, and to help you use websites to their full potential. Cookies are a small text file that is transferred to your hard drive or the internal memory of your mobile device. Please read our Cookies page for more information.

### 6\. Changes to this policy

Voucherbox.co.uk reserves the right to change and amend this policy at any time. The policy in place when any information was submitted will apply.

### 7\. How to contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, security or any other aspect please don’t hesitate to contact us at [hello@voucherbox.co.uk](mailto:hello@voucherbox.co.uk).
