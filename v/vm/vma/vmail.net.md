# V-Mail™ PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: June 6, 2011
                                V-Mail™ PRIVACY POLICY
Protecting your privacy and the privacy of anyone receiving email is extremely important to V-
Mail™. Your personal information and your customer information will be kept strictly confidential.
We will never sell, barter, or rent your email addresses to anyone, unless required by law.
V-Mail™ strives to offer its visitors the many advantages of Internet technology and to provide an
interactive and personalized experience. In order to use this service your must complete the
registration form and create a user-name and password. During the registration process you are
required to provide contact information, such as name and email address. We use this
information to send you a confirmation link to activate your account.
Customer Lists
Your customer lists are stored on our secure V-Mail™ servers. We do not contact your lists, nor
do we market to, sell, or share your lists unless required by law. We do reserve the right to
monitor your customer lists to ensure that they comply with the terms of use. [Link to Terms of
Service]
Permission Policy
V-Mail™ is permission based. This means that you need permission to send your emails to the
people who will receive them. V-Mail’s™ permission policy is incorporated in the terms of use and
must be agreed to by every V-Mail™ subscriber.
To send an email to someone using the V-Mail™ service that recipient must be a customer,
member or subscriber of your business or organization or someone that has asked to receive
your emails by opting in or signing up to receive emails from your business or organization. For
more information on permission based emails and opting in and out see the V-Mail™ Spam
Policy. [Link to Anti-Spam Policy]
Children
V-Mail™ does not knowingly collect or solicit Personally Identifiable Information from or about
children under 13 except as permitted by law. If we discover we have received any information
from a child under 13 in violation of this policy, we will delete that information immediately. If you
believe V-Mail™ has any information from or about anyone under 13, please contact us at the
abuse@v-mail.com
V-Mail™ reserves the right to change this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically
for changes. Your continued use of our site following the posting of changes to these terms will
mean you accept those changes. Information collected prior to the time any change is posted will
be used according to the rules and laws that applied at the time the information was collected.
