# OBD-Codes.com Privacy Policy

NOTE: By using this site, you agree to the terms of the OBD-Codes.com privacy policy. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not use this site.

## Information Collection

  * OBD-Codes.com collects a minimum of personal information on visitors when they register for a free site membership or submit a support ticket. 
    * When visitors register for a free membership, all that is required is a valid e-mail address and a nickname. The nickname can be basically anything (i.e. not your real name), so in essense, the **minimum amount of personal information you need to provide to us is your e-mail address**. You have the option to include the following: location (city/state/province), occupation, and interests. **Remember these are optional items**.
  * We do not collect personal information such as home/work address, phone numbers, etc.
  * We do not collect any personal information pertaining to transactions you have with sponsors or advertisers. Once you leave this site, we do not track you (e.g. if you purchase a product from an advertiser, they do not share your personal information with us or vice versa).
  * For all visitors, we automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, and the page you request.

## Information Sharing And Disclosure

  * We do not under any circumstances share your e-mail address with sponsors or advertisers. Other profile information is freely obtained by viewing member's public profile pages (username, location, occupation, etc.)
  * We do not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or companies, period.
  * We respond to subpoenas, court orders, legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.
  * We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, or as otherwise required by law.



## Children Issues

  * For compliance with [COPPA](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Children%27s_Online_Privacy_Protection_Act), when a child under age 13 attempts to register, we ask that he or she obtain parental permission. There is a form that can be signed and faxed to us in order to activate an account.



## Cookies & Web Beacons

OBD-Codes.com will serve at most two cookies. One for direct advertising and one for your membership. If you have a free membership here, the cookie is used to log you in to the forums, photo galleries, classifieds, and product review areas. Our partners or external advertising partners may serve you cookies or web beacons in providing you advertising, web pages, or services.

If you're paranoid about Web cookies, [beacons](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/faqs/beacons.html), and similar temporary Internet files used by advertisers, you can disable them in your browser. (See your browser's help file to learn how.) Caution: Disabling cookies can have unintended consequences, such as forcing you to log in manually when you visit forums, social-networking sites, online editions of newspapers, and other membership-based sites.
