### UBM Medica OBGYN.net Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** October 28, 2010

As of October 8, 2010, MediSpecialty/OBGYN.net became part of UBM Medica LLC (“UBM Medica or “we”).

Your privacy continues to be important to us at UBM Medica. We will never willfully disclose personally identifiable information about our customers to any third-party without prior consent.

#### Information Collection and Use

  1. UBM Medica OBGYN.net (“OBGYN.net”) uses Personally Identifiable Information to customize and personalize content displayed on the OBGYN.net and Associated Websites and to send to its visitors newsletters, emails, features, and other material, or otherwise for the purposes for which it is submitted, including to complete transactions and fulfill services you have requested, as well as for reference in the event that we need to contact you when processing your requested transaction. Further, your Personally Identifiable Information, including any email submissions and their content, may be used in connection with customer-relations correspondence and may be retained for use in evaluating and improving our customer-relations and assistance programs. We may share Personally Identifiable Information you submit with our affiliates, as well as with unaffiliated third parties who perform services for OBGYN.net or who otherwise are involved in the fulfillment of your service request (e.g., information systems maintenance and development providers, data archiving, end user communication and similar services). 
  2. OBGYN.net also may use this information to identify and develop products and services that may be of interest to you and/or share this information with certain third parties for direct marketing purposes with respect to special offers, products, services, programs, promotions, surveys, publications, invitations or other opportunities that may be of interest to you. 
  3. Authorized parties with whom we may share your information are subject to terms similar in intent and purpose to those in this Privacy Policy. We do not sell individual customer/end user names or other Personally Identifiable Information to third parties and have no intention of doing so in the future. 
  4. Except as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy, OBGYN.net does not disclose or release your Personally Identifiable Information to third parties; provided that, to the extent permitted by law, OBGYN.net may use or disclose Personally Identifiable Information: 
    1. for compliance purposes, such as immigration and customs control, security, and legal support;
    2. in connection with legal requests from government authorities or third parties pursuant to a subpoena or other legal process;
    3. or to otherwise protect the rights or property of OBGYN.net, its employees, customers, or end users.
  5. You should be aware that if you voluntarily disclose Personally Identifiable Information (e.g., user name, email address) on a bulletin board, chat room, profile page or on any other community forum offered on the OBGYN.net And Associated Websites, such information may be collected and used by others and may result in unsolicited messages from other parties. OBGYN.net has no control over this use of your Personally Identifiable Information. We advise users to be especially careful in such public areas about disclosing Personally Identifiable Information, such as full name, home address, or phone number. 



Below are more details about the Personally Identifiable Information we, or third parties, may gather from you and how we may use it.

  1. **Email Newsletters.** OBGYN.net offers email newsletters to its users who sign up and Content Producers. We will use your email address to send you only the specific newsletter(s) that you signed up for. If you want to update your email address or stop receiving a newsletter, follow the procedures to unsubscribe at the bottom of any newsletter we send you, visit the Newsletter options page, or email or call OBGYN.net at any time at We may contact you regarding account status, changes to this Policy, our content agreements or other terms of use, to confirm your registration or for any other business reason related to your use of OBGYN.net and Associated Websites. 
  2. **Service Providers.** We offer our Members other services provided by third parties such as RSS and widget feeds, among others. When you register for membership on the OBGYN.net and Associated Websites or activate a related service by clicking on a particular service link (i.e. RSS feed) on MediSpecialty-operated websites, you may be automatically registered to receive the services we offer and some of your Personally Identifiable Information may be transferred from OBGYN.net to those third-party service providers. By becoming a Member, you authorize us and our third-party service providers to send you email updates and information about promotions and special offers. We take care to require that our third-party service providers only use our Member's data as necessary to provide the particular service and to give you updates on that service provider's products and services. 
  3. **Co-registration with Other Associated Websites.** As a Member, we may offer you the ability to co-register with select third-party partner sites. In some instances where OBGYN.net provides co-registration, the co-registration partners allow you to opt-out of their membership. If you choose to co-register with our partners and accept their terms of service, then you are automatically registered as a Member of their select web sites and your Personally Identifiable Information is transferred to those organizations. As a result, you may receive information from one of these web sites as any of their Members would. We advise you to review the privacy and data collection policies of the third-party partner sites for further information. In order to cancel your membership with any of these partners you will need to go directly to the partner's site. 
  4. **Advertisers.** OBGYN.net may send you emails on behalf of our advertisers. If you opt to receive information from our advertisers they may use your Personally Identifiable Information to send you advertisements or other notices, via email or postal mail, and it is possible that your information may be shared with others. 
  5. **Promotions.** We may ask you for Personally Identifiable Information at other times, including, but not limited to, when you enter sweepstakes, contests or promotions sponsored by OBGYN.net or a third party. If you choose to enter sweepstakes, contests or promotions sponsored by a third party, the Personally Identifiable Information you provide will be subject to the privacy policy of the third-party sponsor. We encourage you to check the applicable third-party policy before providing any Personally Identifiable Information. 
  6. **Surveys and User Research.** OBGYN.net conducts email and web-delivered surveys from time to time, as well as instant polls, to gather information about our users. Taking these surveys and polls is entirely optional. You have no obligation to respond to them. We share only the aggregate results of these surveys, not Personally Identifiable Information, with our advertisers and partners to help them better understand our services and monitor the reach of their advertising or other offerings on our service. We also use some of the Personally Identifiable Information that you provide in these surveys to help us understand your interests and to make the OBGYN.net and our associated websites more useful to you. 
  7. **Traffic Patterns.** We collect traffic patterns and site usage information throughout our site. As part of this we collect the URL you came from when entering our site, what browser you are using, your IP address, and the URL you go to from our site if it is a link or advertiser on our site. In addition, we use Cookies to assign a unique, random number to identify your computer. Cookies, by themselves, cannot identify you personally or any other individual using your computer. Cookies allow us to keep track of login information (for increased security), and site traffic patterns and usage. 
  8. **E-mail a Friend.** If you choose to use the E-mail a Friend feature, we ask for your friend's E-mail address to deliver the information. This will be a one-time correspondence from OBGYN.net. 



#### Your Consent

By using the OBGYN.net web site, those sites powered by OBGYN.net, and the services available to you on our sites, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described above. 

#### Links on our Site

OBGYN.net has provided links to various sites that we believe may provide valuable information to you, our viewer. OBGYN.net offers no warranty or guarantee of the privacy practices, content, or actions of such web sites. Links to third-party websites are provided as a convenience to you and should not imply endorsement or recommendation of the information contained in or actions of third-party websites. 

#### Advertising on our Site

The ads appearing on this Web site are delivered to you by various third parties as well as OBGYN.net. Our third-party advertisers also may separately place or recognize a cookie file on your browser in the course of delivering advertisements to this Site. Aggregate information about your visit to this site (but not your name, address, or other personally identifiable information) is used to serve ads to you on this site. This information is not collected for profiling. 

#### Children Twelve Years Old and Younger

OBGYN.net is directed to adults, including young adults, and to medical professionals, Our websites and services are not intended for children twelve years old or younger. 

#### Changes to This Policy

We may change the provisions of this policy from time to time and urge you to check back periodically. Your continued use of the website or pages within the scope of this policy will be your acceptance of the version of the policy in place at the time of your visit. 

If you have questions about the policy or its use, please contact us at [privacyrequests@ubm.com](mailto:privacyrequests@ubm.com).
