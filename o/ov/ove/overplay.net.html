OverPlay Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 24, 2018

Because we’re in the business of protecting your privacy, we want to be fully transparent about our data practices.

In Brief

  • We do not collect or log any traffic or use of our SmartDNS or Virtual Private Network services.
  • We do not sell or share personal information about our users to third parties, under any circumstance.
  • In order to provide you with our Services, we need to collect an email address and a valid payment method.

This Privacy Policy (the "Policy”) describes the information OverPlay collects, how we gather that information, and how we use that information. This Policy applies to: (i) visitors of the website www.OverPlay.net (the “Site”); (ii) members of the OverPlay SmartDNS and VPN services (the “Service(s)”).

Information OverPlay Collects

Site

OverPlay never tracks activity outside of our Site. Within our domains, we aggregate information from Site visitors through cookies, tracking pixels, and similar technologies. This is done to improve Site performance, and does not include personally identifiable information. Information collected using these methods includes but is not limited to:

  • Page requests
  • Browser type
  • Operating system
  • Average time spent on our Site

Services

While you are not required to submit personally identifiable information to visit our Site, in order to sign up for our Services, you must first create a member account. Member account creation requires:

  • A valid email address
  • A valid payment method

While this information must be provided to gain access to OverPlay Services, it is collected on the Site. Payment methods offered include credit card and PayPal. Users who sign up for our Service using credit card will also be asked to provide:

  • A first and last name
  • A valid billing address
  • A valid credit card number

This information is used strictly for payment processing, and can be modified at any time via the Member Area of the Site. All personal information is protected through Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption.

OverPlay does not log user traffic or any activity of its SmartDNS and Virtual Private Network Services, under any circumstances.

Cookies

Cookies are temporary identifiers sent from our Site to your device through your web browser. This helps us remember your previous actions to improve functionality when you revisit our Site. Cookies also improve efficiency by allowing you to save information, such as your OverPlay login credentials, to your browser.

In addition to Site cookies, we partner with third-party providers to gather and aggregate non-personally identifiable Site data. These third parties, such as Google Analytics, place cookies and tracking pixels on our Site to do so. The aggregated data is used to generate statistical reports for analytics. The collection and processing of this data from by third parties is done in accordance with this Policy. It cannot be traced to any individual user, and we never provide these third parties with personal information about any individual user.

You must consent to the use of any and all cookies before you access the Site. Note that some Site features require cookies to function properly, but if you prefer, you can alter your browser preferences and opt-out of cookies at any time.

How OverPlay Uses Your Information

OverPlay never sells your personal information to third parties.

Site Information

OverPlay uses Site information for the following purposes:

  • To create and process member accounts
  • To verify your member account when you log into the Site
  • To monitor and analyze site usage and trends
  • To manage memberships and contact customers with account issues
  • To share relevant content, news, offers, and product information with customers
  • To troubleshoot Service issues and respond to support requests

Service Information

OverPlay uses Service information solely for the purpose of processing payments. As previously outlined, we do not, under any circumstances, log member traffic or use of our Services.

Third-Party Information

We partner with third parties to provide essential services on our behalf, such as email messaging, payment processing, and support services. These third-party providers only receive the necessary information required to perform their intended functions, and are not permitted to disclose this information or use it for any other purposes. How To Modify Your Personal Information

You can make changes to your personal information any time in the Member Area of the Site. Additional data requests and inquiries should be sent to support@overplay.net.

Emails

Service subscribers may receive emails about Service updates, industry news, and relevant privacy content. Subscribers may also receive promotional emails about other products and services we think may be of interest to you. You can opt-out of future promotional emails at any time by clicking the ‘Unsubscribe' link contained in every email. Please note, however, that we will continue to send you emails for customer service purposes.

Changes to This Policy

From time to time, we may change and/or update this privacy policy. We recommend that you regularly review this page for updates.

GDPR

The European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is effective as of May 25, 2018. This regulation broadens the privacy rights that EU individuals have over how their personal data is collected, processed and maintained.

Privacy Settings and Requests

OverPlay provides information about our data privacy policies, including how we use cookies, in the Privacy section of this website. We also provide tools on this website that allow users to control privacy preferences for certain aspects of using this site or interacting with OverPlay, such as for cookies and marketing. If you have any data privacy or personal data questions or requests, please contact privacy@overplay.net.
