# Open Freely - Open Over 100 Different File Types For Free

This Privacy Policy explains the policy of Download Freely, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Download Freely," "we," "our"), for collecting and using any information that users (the "user," you," "your") submit to us.

This policy applies to our http://www.openfreely.com website (the "Website"). Download Freely owns and operates the Website. If you have privacy questions or concerns, send an email to privacy@openfreely.com. 

BY USING OR VISITING THE WEBSITE, YOU AGREE TO THE INFORMATION USE AND COLLECTION TERMS OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY DOCUMENT. DOWNLOAD FREELY HAS THE RIGHT TO REVISE THIS POLICY AT ANY TIME. WE WILL NOTIFY YOU ON THE WEBSITE IF WE CHANGE THIS PRIVACY POLICY. SUCH CHANGES WILL BE EFFECTIVE WHEN POSTED. ANY ACCESS OR USE OF THE WEBSITE AFTER HAVING RECEIVED NOTICE OF ANY CHANGES TO THIS POLICY YOU SHALL BE DEEMED TO HAVE ACCEPTED SUCH CHANGES.

**The Information We Collect and How We Use It**  
Download Freely does not collect any personally identifiable information via the Website, such as the names and addresses of users.

We collect non-personal information from users of the Website to evaluate how the Website is used. We use this data to make the Website work better for you in the following ways: to improve the design of the Website, to provide personalization on the Website, and to evaluate the performance of our marketing and advertising efforts. The technologies or mechanisms we use to gather and analyze this non-personal information may include IP addresses logs, browser cookies, browser detection, web log files, and web analytics software applications. 

You may be invited to participate in optional customer surveys or promotions, and Download Freely may request that you provide personal information in those surveys or promotions. We use information collected from surveys and promotions to learn more about our customers so that we can improve our services and develop new products and services.

IP addresses define the Internet location of computers and help us better understand the geographic distribution of our Website visitors and customers. Cookies are identification files placed onto the hard drive or other storage of your computer when you visit the Website, so we can immediately recognize your computer when you return to the Website and deliver content specific to you. You may modify your browser preferences to accept all cookies, be notified when a cookie is saved on your computer, or reject all cookies. Please consult your browser instructions for information on how to modify your choices about cookies. If you modify your browser preferences, certain features of the Website may not be available to you.

We may detect the type of web browser you are using to optimize the performance of the Website and to discover which browsers are used most by our visitors and customers. To learn about how people use our site, we examine web log files and use web analytics software applications. We analyze the order in which users access our web pages when viewing the Website and how long they spend in certain areas.

Download Freely may contract with unaffiliated third parties to provide services such as customer communications, website analytics, and other services. 

**Blogs and Product Reviews**  
The Website may include blogs, product reviews, or other message areas that allow users to post or send information to the Website. When you post information to the Website, other users of the Website will be able to view the information. We urge you to exercise caution when posting any personally identifiable information to the Website. 

**Use of the Website by Children**  
The Website is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. 

**Disclosure of Information**  
We reserve the right to disclose information that we collect from users as required by law and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or comply with a court order or legal process. Download Freely may elect to sell all or substantially all of its business operations and assets including the Website. In a transaction of this kind, user information may be among the assets that are transferred. 

**Privacy and Other Websites**  
The Website may contain links to other websites. In addition, if you chose to download a software application from the Website, you may be provided with an offer made by a third party contractor of Download Freely. Download Freely is not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites or third parties. We encourage you to be aware when you leave our site, and to read the privacy statements of each website that collects personally identifiable information. Some linked sites may be co-branded with our trademarks and those of our third party contractors. They may have the look and feel of our Website, but the uniform resource locator (URL) shown in your browser window will indicate that you are visiting a different website. This policy only applies to information collected by Download Freely at the Website.
