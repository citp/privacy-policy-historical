# Privacy Policy - Open Tech Info

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to [contact .](http://www.opentechinfo.com/contact)

At opentechinfo, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by opentechinfo and how it is used.

**Log Files** – Like many other Web sites, opentechinfo does not makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. So we do not use any kind of log files to store your data but we track them using another great Google Service called “Google Analytics” so it tracks everything down and keeps the record into their own data base.

**Cookies and Web Beacons** – opentechinfo does not use or store cookies. Yes off-course we do not store your cookies to provide you any useful information, in fact that is handled by our advertising partners
