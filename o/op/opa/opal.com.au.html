Opal Privacy Policy

Privacy

The personal information collected for the purposes of the Opal Ticketing System will be treated in accordance with the Privacy Personal Information Protection Act 1998 (NSW) (PPIPA). For information on how we handle such personal information we refer you to the Opal Privacy Policy published on opal.com.au.
