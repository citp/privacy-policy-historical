# OPSWAT Privacy Policy

|  |  |  | 

OPSWAT respects and protects the privacy of visitors to our Website. OPSWAT does not collect personal information from a visitor to our site unless the visitor intentionally provides it. When accessing certain documents or services on our Website, visitors will be asked to register, provide contact information, and depending on what they are accessing, more information about where they work and what they do. OPSWAT collects this information in an effort to better meet the needs and answer the questions of visitors to its Website. The information is used solely by OPSWAT or its authorized affiliates. We do not sell information to any third parties. 

### Information Collected

OPSWAT collects visitor information in the following areas: 

>   * Viewing product demonstrations
>   * Accessing white papers. Note: Once you have registered for a white paper or tool, you may visit at any time using your password and user ID.
>   * Career opportunities
>   * User surveys
> 


### Opting Out

OPSWAT provides visitors who elect to receive electronic or other communications the ability to opt-out of those communications by sending us a notice to unsubscribe. OPSWAT respects your wishes and will delete you from mailing or other types of lists. Please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20071215091528id_/http%3A//www.opswat.com/contactus.shtml) with any concerns.
