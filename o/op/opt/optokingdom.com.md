# Privacy Policy /LED Outdoor Screen Website

Thank you for accessing our website. We respect your privacy and take your online information security very seriously. In order to make convenient and efficient online shopping system and provide best product, efficient service to every user, we will collect and record a variety of information when you visit our website.

This Privacy Policy explains why your information will be collected and how we collect, use, and (under certain conditions) disclose your personal information. This Privacy Policy also explains the steps we have taken to secure your personal information. Finally, this Privacy Policy explains your options regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information. By visiting our Site, you accept the practices described in this Notice.

Optokingdom believe it is very important for you to know what personal information we collect. When you visit our website your computer's IP address might be collected. We will collect such your name, your name, your address, your telephone number, your email address, and your credit card number when your register your account on our website. This information related with you directly and some other information that you may not consider as your privacy, we might be collect. Such as your language preferences, how many times you've visited the site, and which page you've clicked on etc.
