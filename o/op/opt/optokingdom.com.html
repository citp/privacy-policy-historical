Privacy Policy
Thanks for visiting our website. In order to provide you with the best products and efficient service and timely updates, we record a variety of information from your visit to our website. We respect your privacy and protect your personal information. For more information, please read our privacy statement.

This privacy statement applies solely to this website.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and (under certain conditions) disclose your personal information. This Privacy Policy also explains the steps we have taken to secure your personal information. Finally, this Privacy Policy explains your options regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information. By visiting our Site, you accept the practices described in this Notice.

The privacy policy on optokingdom is the mutual trust between us, it needs to be protected by both of us. It contains the following three aspects:
A. what privacy information we will collect.
We will collect your computer’s IP address, your browser version and your language preferences when you visit our site, and we also record how many times you have visited our site.
When you register on our site, the system will create a shopping account for you. If you buy products, contact our customer service, we can collect your personal information such as your name, address, telephone number, email address, gender, date of birth and your credit card number through your account.

B. How we use and disclosure your information
1. Internal usage: your personal information is used to deal with your orders and offer service to you. We will internally use your personal information in order to improve the content and layout of our site, and in this way we will improve our promotional and marketing scheming (including offer better service and products to you).
2. Communication: We will use your personal information to communicate with you in case you release an order or when we deliver your goods. Besides, when you register on our site, we will send you’re a confirmation email or message via your information. When you release an order it necessary to collect your information, because when we have problems with your order, we can contact you via your provided information to quickly solve your problems, in this way you can get your goods asap.
3. External Usage: We want to offer qualified service and provide more selections to you. Our business is sell our products but not doing information service, so we won’t sale, rent or permit any form to disclose your specific personal information or financial information to public, except the following situations:
Sometimes we will ask help from internet service providers to improve the relative functions of our site, in this case we may disclose your information to the providers so that they can perform their service. And we have to share your information with some third parties. For example, in order to deliver your goods, we have to tell the logistics agency or express company to make sure we can deliver your goods. In this way we can also improve our service by the feedback information and analysis and improve the service quality of the third parties. When deliver your goods to the third parties, we also have to provide client’s personal information such as the name, shipping address, email and phone number, etc. Only in this way we can make sure the goods will be sent you directly.
Similarly, to help you buy products and provide customer service to you, we must provide your credit card number to financial-services corporations such as credit-card processors and issuers. When we submit your credit card number for authorization, we use the most reliable security methods to protect your information.

We may be required to disclose you information in response to requests from law-enforcement officials conducting investigations, subpoenas, a court order, or if we are otherwise required to disclose such information by law. 

We may share non-personal information (such as the number of daily visitors to a particular web page, or the size of an order placed on a certain date) with third parties such as advertising partners. This information does not directly personally identify you or any user.

We do everything possible to minimize the risk. We use existing security technology, to provide a safer environment and reduce the chance of unauthorized access. Personal information is stored in an environment which is protected by a combination of physical and technical measurements.  What’s more, we use the latest firewall protection to prevent unauthorized access into our information storage areas.
Through we use the industry-standard precautions to protect your personal information, we can’t guarantee it’s completely safe. 100% complete security does not exist either online or offline.
You can change and modify your personal information freely
You can change your information or delete your account on our website freely and conveniently.

Update policy

We work hard to make sure your visiting to our website is secure, so your privacy is always respected, if there are any changes or updates in our website, we will remind you to view our privacy policy time to time. If you have any questions about the Privacy Policy., please contact us.

隐私政策
非常感谢您访问我们的网站。我们尊重您的隐私权，并看重您的在线安全，为了向您提供最好的产品和高效的客户服务，以及及时更新，当您访问我们的网站时，我们会记录您的各种信息。我们尊重您的隐私和保护您的个人信息，要了解更多的信息，请阅读我们的隐私声明。 
本隐私声明仅适用于本网站。
本隐私声明说明了我们如何收集，使用，以及（在一定条件下）透露您的个人信息。该隐私声明也说明，我们已经采取措施来保护您的个人信息。最后，本隐私声明解释了你的选择，使用和披露您的个人信息。通过访问我们的网站，您已经接受本声明中所陈述的内容和做法。
Optokingdom.com 的隐私政策是我们相互之间的信任，需要我们共同去保护，主要包括以下三个方面：
一．我们收集您的哪些个人信息。
当您访问我们的网站时，我们会收集您的计算机IP地址，您的浏览器版本，您的语言首选项，您有多少次访问该网站等。
当你注册我们的网站时，会建立一个购物账户，购买商品，联系客服，我们收集一些您的个人信息，如您的姓名，地址，电话号码，电子邮箱地址，性别，生日，和您的信用卡的账号。
我们收集您的信息的目的是提供优质的服务，我们也会保护您的信息。
二．信息的使用和披露。
1.内部使用。我们使用您的个人信息处理您的订单并为您提供客户服务。我们可能会在公司内部使用您的个人信息来改进我们网站的内容和布局，以提高我们的推广和营销（包括提供服务和产品给你）策划。
2.与您沟通。我们将使用您的个人信息与您沟通，比如您的订单和交付。此外，当您注册我们的网站时，我们会向您发送确认邮件或短信验证。当您下订单时，我们收集您的信息是必要的，如果我们处理您的订单时遇到一些问题，我们可以用您提供给我们的信息与您联系,以便迅速解决您的问题，这样你就能最快的见到您购买的宝贝了。
3.外用。我们希望为您提供优质的服务，并为您提供更多的选择。我们主要是销售商品，而不是信息。我们不会出售，出租，许可或以其他方式披露您特定的个人信息或财务信息给任何人，除了以下几种情况：
网络运营商和网络服务商，我们有时会利用一些网络服务商来帮助我们完成一些网站相关的功能。我们会把信息提供给这些服务供应商，以帮助他们完成他们的服务。我们还会把您的信息提供给另外一些第三方合作伙伴。例如，为了把产品运送给您，我们必须分享一些信息给货运公司和快递公司。以保证交货，使我们能获得反馈，提高我们的服务质量，并测量和提高第三方服务质量。在交付货物给第三方时，我们为他们提供一些客户的个人身份信息，如姓名，送货地址，电子邮件和电话号码等，以保证产品顺利，准确地送到您的手中。
同样，为了实现您能购买到您想要的产品和提供客户服务，我们必须提供信用卡号码给金融服务企业，如信用卡处理器和发行商。当我们提交您的信用卡号进行授权，我们会使用最可靠的方法进行加密，以保护您的信息。
 我们也许要配合相关执法部门处理一些案件，当相关法律要求我们为相关执法部门提供客户这些信息的时候，我们可能需要在进行调查，传票，法庭命令，向执法人员的要求提供您的个人信息。
 我们可以与第三方如广告合作伙伴共享非个人信息（如每日游客人数到一个特定的网页，或放置在某个日期订单的大小）。此信息不会直接识别您的身份或任何用户。 
  我们将尽一切可能，尽量减少风险。我们利用现有的加密技术，为客户提供更安全的环境，并减少未授权访问的机会。个人信息存将储在通过物理和技术措施的组合保护的安全的环境。更重要的是，我们采用了最新的防火墙保护，以防止未经授权的访问到我们的信息存储区。
虽然我们使用行业标准预防措施来保护您的个人信息，我们不能保证完全安全，100％完全的安全是不存在的。
您可以自由使用和修改您的个人信息
在我们的网站上你可以很方便的更改您的信息，或者删除您的账户。
