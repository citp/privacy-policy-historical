# OldVersion.com

|   |  |   |  **Privacy Policy**

Our users are very important to us, and OldVersion.com respects your privacy. We have a firm commitment to keeping your personal information confidential. The following statement spells out our policy for keeping your information private. Rest assured, we will never intentionally give away any personally identifiable information about you to any party, unless you give us specific permission to do so. 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our web site. Your IP address is also used to gather broad demographic information. This demographic information is generally limited only to the country you are connecting from, and/or the ISP or company whose Internet service you are using to connect to our site. IP addresses are never associated with any other information, including an e-mail address. 

This site contains links to other sites. OldVersion.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. All the programs that can obtained here are provided by other companies. You should review the privacy policies of these companies, along with the terms of service for more information about the terms under which those programs are provided to you. 

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this web site, you can [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20030622123444id_/http%3A//oldversion.com/contact.php).
