# OldRide.com - Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent. Please read this privacy policy to learn more about the ways in which we use and protect your personal information. 

**1\. What type of information is collected?**  
Personal Information: We require personal contact information and vehicle and other ad listing item information from our members. The required information is used to effectively and accurately create, maintain, and display OldRide.com's classified services. Cookies: Some sites in the OldRide.com network utilize cookies in order to provide members and merchants with a more personalized experience by remembering who you are when you revisit the site. Cookies also track visitor activity and traffic patterns among the sites so that we can identify users' interests and behavior. With this information, we can improve existing user services and develop new products appropriate for our audience. 

**2\. How is the information collected?**  
Members are requested to submit personal and vehicle and other listing item information using the online Register, Place An Ad, and My OldRide services. Users unwilling to pass personal information online can choose to work with a sales representative or customer service representative to utilize member services. 

**3\. How information will be used?**  
We collect and record personal information in order to maintain email lists and to allow users full use of there OldRide.com account. We utilize the emails to keep members informed of the site services, site performance, promotions, and offer information regarding industry related services and products. At NO TIME will your personal information ever be sold to an outside vendor! 

**4\. Data Security**  
Personal Information To protect the member's account information, OldRide.com assigns each member with a unique username and password. These unique identifiers are disclosed to the user via email immediately upon registering with OldRide.com. Only members have the ability to modify their personal information and modify and/or delete their ads as needed. Credit Card Transactions OldRide.com uses thawte and secure servers to transmit all online credit card transactions in an encrypted format, no personal credit card information is stored on the OldRide Servers. 

**5\. Data Quality and Access**  
Members may access their information 24 hours a day using their designated user name and password. Members may modify or delete their information as needed.   


**This information is subject to change**
