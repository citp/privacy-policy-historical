# Exercise your brain with the Offerx Quiz

Offerx takes your privacy seriously and takes every reasonable precaution to safeguard the personal information you supply to it 

Offerx recognizes that your privacy and the protection of your personal information is important to you, this statement discloses how Offerx helps to protect your personal information while using the Offerx Web Site (http://www.offerx.co.uk). 

Offerx is registered under the Data Protection Act 1998. The information you provide is safeguarded under the terms of that Act. To find out more information about the data protection act please visit http://www.informationcommissioner.gov.uk 

Offerx Ltd collects data about you and answers to specific questions asked by sponsors. Offerx Ltd, its clients or sponsors will contact you with offers and information through mail, telephone or e-mail. 

Once you have accepted to complete the Registration your information will be transferred at the end of each page (i.e. when you click on the "continue" button) to Offerx Ltd. On clicking the 'play the game' button at the end of the registration you will have "completed" the registration. 

Offerx Limited ("Offerx ltd") may use other reputable partners to provide its services in connection with the Registration. However this will not affect your statutory rights under the Data Protection Act. No other information will be gathered other than that which you have completed on the Registration. 

The Internet is not a 100% secure medium for communication and, accordingly, Offerx cannot guarantee the security of any information you send to Offerx via the Internet. Offerx is not responsible for any loss or damage you or others may suffer as a result of the loss of confidentiality of such information.

Offerx is committed to protecting the security of your personal information. We use a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. For example, we store the personal information you provide on computer systems with limited access, which are located in controlled facilities.

Offerx collects demographic (information about you, such as name, age and address) and lifestyle (information about your interests and purchasing habits). Offerx collects data about you, including answers to specific questions asked by Offerx' sponsors. This information is used to assist Offerx and third parties in sending you by, email, sms, voice, post or other appropriate means, communications about their products and services.

When you visit Offerx.co.uk a small text file (known as a 'cookie') will be placed on your computer; the only thing this cookie is used for is to enable us to recognise you when you return to Offerx.co.uk and show you offers which match your interests based on the information you gave us when you registered. The cookie will not allow a website to learn any personally identifiable information (such as your name and address) that you have not otherwise given to us. The cookie will also not track where you go when you leave Offerx.co.uk or where you came to our website from. It's only purpose is to make your visit to Offerx.co.uk easier and more rewarding for you.

You may at any time deregister from Offerx Ltd by using the following link: unsubscribe. The consequences of deregistration are that your account details will be placed in a suppression file and you will not receive any further communications from Offerx Ltd

OfferX is NOT spam : should you ever decide that you don't want to receive any additional OfferX email, all you need to do in order to cancel your OfferX membership is to click on the unsubscribe link that will appear on every email that we will send you. 

Every email that Offerx sends contains the following information at the bottom of the email:

This email has been sent to the following email address: < _your email address_ >

  You are receiving this email because you have registered with OfferX. OfferX respects your privacy and only sends emails to registered members. Our emails are never sent unsolicited. 

  If you wish to unsubscribe from our offers list click here. 

  Please note that unsubscribe requests must be made from the email account used to register with Offerx: < _your email address_ >

  To find out more about us and our privacy policy: http://www.offerx.co.uk/Privacy.aspx 

Offerx will occasionally update this Privacy Statement. When we do, we will also revise the "last updated" date at the top and bottom of the Privacy Statement. Offerx will obtain your opt-in consent for any updates to this Privacy Statement that materially expand the sharing or use of your personal information in ways not disclosed in this Privacy Statement at the time of collection.
