If you are a car dealer or an exporter Off Lease Only would be happy to help you with your transaction! However we do have some different rules regarding this scenario. If you are exporting the car out of the country or a dealer purchasing the vehicle for resale there will be $1000 non-negotiable dealer fee. Payment for the vehicle must be in the form of wire transfer only. After we receive your wire transfer we will need you to sign the paperwork. Paperwork must be signed by the person purchasing the vehicle. If you are coming into the dealership, great, please just come in and sign your paperwork. If you are out of state/Country and not able to come into the dealership, we will gladly fed-ex you the paperwork at an additional charge which is $100 Domestic & $200 International Fed-Ex Fee. Once you sign & return and we have received your paperwork, then your vehicle will be ready for pick up. All export vehicles must be picked up by a carrier/transporter. Transportation receipt is needed to pick up vehicle. Dealers will just need to provide proof of insurance, copy of dealers license, sales tax resale certificate and you are good to go.
