Office Timeline is the first free timeline maker add-in for Microsoft Office PowerPoint 2007 and 2010 that directly integrates into PowerPoint and uses native PowerPoint objects to create timelines, gantt charts, and project schedules. This add-in gives you a lot of flexibility on the look and feel of your timeline compared to other timeline makers out there that do not fully integrate with PowerPoint.
