Privacy Policy

Virtual images notes:

We do not install any kind of package in any distribution that is available here or do not add any kind of file/script or do not perform update or do not configure anything in the Operating System, virtual images for VirtualBox and VMware are only available with default options. We believe that user should get a clean install image. From our site you get fresh image with default options (we do not modify anything within the image), just like you install fresh OS, after getting image you can make it your own. Our images are 100% free from malware/spyware/bloatware/adware and so. Feel free to audit any image you download from here, after that also send audit report to us(we may feature your report).
