# Privacy Policy | OMG

### OMG.com.au and OnlineMarketingGroup.com.au

When you disclose your personal information to us, we create a record about you and your purchase(s).

By using our website, you agree that we can collect information necessary to provide you with our products and services, and you agree that this information may be disclosed to third party product providers, such as airlines, hotels, car rental agencies and other third parties authorised by OMG.com.au , as required.

OMG.com.au may use “cookies”. Where applicable, you agree for us to use cookies on your browser.

If you have “opted-in” to receiving our promotional information, from time to time, we may e-mail you information about products and services which may be of interest to you. If you wish to stop receiving these communications, please email support@OMG.com.au from your primary email address stating “UNSUBSCRIBE” in the subject line.

What information is collected by OMG.com.au and how is it used? OMG.com.au will only collect personal information about you with your consent. The only personal information we collect is what you tell us about yourself and information about how you use our website.

Why does OMG.com.au collect personal information? We collect the personal information for the following reasons:

  1. To identify you when you use our site, and in order to customise your OMG.com.au experience
  2. For security purposes
  3. To enable us to process your subscription requests and purchases
  4. To send you confirmation e-mails, and advise you of any changes that might occur to your subscription and purchases
  5. For our staff to contact you, where necessary
  6. Where applicable, to send you updates and/or newsletters.
  7. To send you information about our products and services, that may be of interest to you.



Links may be present on the OMG.com.au site, which may result in you leaving the OMG.com.au site and being taken to third party web sites. You should note that any information that you provide to these third party web sites are not subject to the OMG.com.au privacy policy.

Accessing your information You may at any time access and change your personal information or subscribed services, either on our website, or by sending an email to our support team.

Minors As we cannot distinguish the age of persons who access our site, a blanket privacy policy is applied. OMG.com.au does not intend nor does it seek to receive any personal information from minors. Should a parent or guardian have reason to believe that a minor has provided OMG.com.au with personal information without their prior consent, please contact us to ensure that the information is removed from our database.

Security of your information OMG.com.au uses technology and security features to safeguard the privacy of your personal information. A secure server protects your personal information. In addition, you may complete bookings with OMG.com.au using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption. If you become aware of any security breach, please let us know as soon as possible.

Procedures and training ensure that OMG.com.au staff continuously respect the confidentiality of any personal information held by OMG.com.au.

OMG.com.au will make best endeavours to keep any information which we hold about you secure. As the Internet data transmission cannot be guaranteed to be 100%, we cannot be responsible for any event arising from unauthorised access to your personal information. OMG.com.au will not be held responsible for events arising from third parties gaining unauthorised access to your personal information.

Please note also that OMG.com.au may use overseas facilities to process or back up its information. As a result, we may transfer and store your personal information to our overseas facilities. However, this does not change any of our commitments to safeguard your privacy.

Contact us If you have any questions regarding our Privacy Policy, or should you have reason to believe that a breach of privacy has occurred, please submit your concerns to our support team.

Changes to the OMG.com.au Privacy Policy OMG.com.au may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in any way we use your personal information, we will notify you by way of e-mail.

Thank you for taking the time to review the OMG.com.au Privacy Policy.

OMG.com.au is operated by Online Marketing Group Pty Limited (”OMG.com.au”), a proprietary limited company based in Queensland, Australia. Our Privacy Policy covers OMG.com.au and OnlineMarketingGroup.com.au.
