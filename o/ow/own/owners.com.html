Owners.com, Inc. is dedicated to safeguarding your privacy online. The following explains how your personal information will be treated as you make use of our products and services.

In order to enhance your visit with personalized services, Owners.com uses cookies to store and sometimes track information about you. Cookies, for example, allow us to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site. Owners.com also uses IP addresses to administer our Web site and to report aggregate information to our advertisers.

Owners.com occasionally asks users to complete surveys that we use for research purposes. This information is only shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis. Individual user information is not released.

In order to take full advantage of Owners.com, or in response to contests or promotions, there are several forms that users may choose to complete. Some of these forms contain contact information (name, email address, etc.) that we use to update customers about their account, and our partners and sponsors regarding contest winners. Users may opt-out of receiving future updates, promotions or all correspondence; see the choice/opt-out section below.

In general, Owners.com will not disclose any of your personal information except when we have your permission or under special circumstances, such as when we believe in good faith that the law requires it. Owners.com does provide links to business partners and sponsors for additional products and services requested by our customers. Some of these products and services do require personal information from you in order to deliver the product. This information is specifically described prior to transferring the data. While Owners.com works closely with its partners to ensure your privacy, we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of our partner Web sites.

Choice/Opt-Out:
Owners.com provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor. Users may remove their information from our customer and email lists with any of the following options:

  1. You can send email to support@owners.com
  2. You can visit the following URL: http://www.owners.com/Accounts/EmailExclude.asp
  3. You can send mail to the following postal address:
    164 Townsend, Suite #9, San Francisco, CA 94107
  4. You can call the following telephone number (415) 427-1000

If you have questions regarding this statement, please contact privacy@owners.com.
