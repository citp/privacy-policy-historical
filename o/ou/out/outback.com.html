OSI Restaurant Partners, LLC (Outback) values your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This policy provides our customers with information about how we use information we gather on-line.

Types of Information We Collect
Outback collects and maintains personally identifiable information about you in order to meet your needs. We collect your first and last name, postal and e-mail address, phone number, and billing information only when you voluntarily submit it. We use this information to process your orders, notify contest winners, send newsletters and get your valuable feedback on our website and products. Outback does not share, sell or trade any of your personal information to third parties without your permission.

Aggregate Information
In addition to obtaining your personal information, Outback also gathers tracking information such as your IP address, browser type, and most popular web pages visited. We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you in this manner. We use the aggregate information to better serve you by continually improving the content of our website.

Making Changes to Personal Information You Provide
You may review and request changes to any personal information you have provided to us online. Simply email our customer service department and we will make the requested changes.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy is effective as of July 1, 2007. Outback reserves the right to change this policy at any time and will post all changes on this web page. All changes will apply from the effective date forward, and will not be retroactive.
