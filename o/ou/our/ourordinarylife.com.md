# Privacy Policy - Our Ordinary Life

This privacy policy tells you how we use personal information collected at this site. Please read this privacy policy before using the site or submitting any personal information. By using the site, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy policy. These practices may be changed, but any changes will be posted and changes will only apply to activities and information on a going forward, not retroactive basis. You are encouraged to review the privacy policy whenever you visit the site to make sure that you understand how any personal information you provide will be used.

**Note:** the privacy practices set forth in this privacy policy are for this web site only. If you link to other web sites, please review the privacy policies posted at those sites.

**Information** : We do not and will not collect your personal identifying information unless otherwise noted within.

**Distribution of Information:** If we do not collect, we are not sharing. We will not share your personal information unless otherwise noted.

**Commitment to Data Security** Your personally identifiable information is kept secure. . All emails and newsletters from this site allow you to opt out of further mailings.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
