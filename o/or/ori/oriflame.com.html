PRIVACY STATEMENT


Oriflame Cosmetics is the sole owner of any information collected through this website. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to other parties.

If you wish to subscribe to information from Oriflame Cosmetics, such as news or financial reports, you will be asked to provide an e-mail address. Oriflame will use this e-mail address only to send the information requested.

Users who no longer wish to receive our information may cancel their subscription at any time. If you wish to register as an Oriflame Consultant, you will be asked to provide certain personal information, which we will use only for that purpose.

“Cookies” is the name of a technology that allows our site to store tokens of information in your browser. This gives your browser an identifier only recognised by our site while you are on it, but does not identify you.

Cookies help us to track traffic patterns, which enable us to deliver a better service for our users. If you do not want to receive a cookie from the Oriflame website, or from any other site, you have the option of setting your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie thus allowing you to determine whether or not to accept it.

This website contains links to other websites. Please note that Oriflame Cosmetics is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. This privacy statement applies only to information gathered on this particular website.
