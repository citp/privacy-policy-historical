The O'Reilly Network's Privacy Policy

The O'Reilly Network brings you the best techology and developer information. To fulfill that mission, we try to understand our readers information needs, while at the same time pledging to protect your privacy.

O'Reilly Network collects customer information on our site, from registration and traffic measurement, to more effectively tailor our offerings. This FAQ should help you understand our methods and your choices about how you make information available to us.

What information does O'Reilly Network gather and track?

Each time you come to the O'Reilly Network, we collect some information that helps us improve our site. We look at how traffic flows throughout our site, what parts you visit, and what parts you don't.

We might compile this data to create profiles of O'Reilly Network users,and we share this with our advertisers in aggregate form. We do not share personally identifiable data about you with our advertisers.

What is registration at O'Reilly Network?

We value member registration at O'Reilly Network because by registering you can tell us what your interests are. It's useful information for our business.

By registering, you can receive any of our free email updates. We use this registration information only within the O'Reilly Network.

When you register, you can also opt to receive information from selected partners.

How does O'Reilly Network share information?

O'Reilly Network may share information with advertisers to help them present products our users might find interesting. We may share information with our partners so that we can study ways to be more productive and informative.

We want O'Reilly Network to be useful to you at all times, and don't provide information about you to other organizations without your consent.

How do I unsubscribe or have my registration removed from the list?

If you want to unsubscribe from an email subscription, go to your user account and choose the "None" value on the email list you would like to cancel.

For non-automated human help: send email to unsubscribe-help@oreillynet.com
