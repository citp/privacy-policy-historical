How Oregon Scientific uses cookies
A cookie is a piece of text to be placed on your computer's hard drive. If you are general user browsing our web site, no cookies will be placed on your hard drive. For users who log in to our web site , cookie will then be applied. However, when you quit your browser or log out from our web site, cookies will be erased completely from your hard drive. Cookies help us to deliver information specific to your interests, to eliminate the need to re-enter your password , or to keep track of your shopping cart. We do not follow your browsing path outside of the Oregon Scientific site.
