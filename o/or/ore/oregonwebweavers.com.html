Privacy Statement – last updated April 23, 2008

Oregon Web Weavers is committed to protecting and respecting the privacy of our clients as well as all visitors who visit our website.

For visitors, we've structured our web site so that, in general, you can visit us on the web without identifying yourself or revealing any personal information.

For our clients, when providing us personal information, you can be assured that it will only be used for purposes of supporting your client relationship with Oregon Web Weavers.


Visitors to Our Web Site

What information is collected and how it may be used:

When you visit our web site, our web server collects basic user information including your Internet service provider’s domain, the time you accessed our web site, and the pages from of our web site that you visited.  This information provides us statistics on popular content and website visits, helping us improve our website to enhance the end user’s experience.

What information do we not collect?

Our web server does not collect your name, e-mail address, phone number, personal information or any other information on other pages you have accessed or visited on the Internet.

Important note

Our web site provides links to affiliate programs, products, and services offered by other companies.  If you should choose to leave our website by clicking on one of these links, that company's web site will be able to determine that you clicked on a link from our web site. Oregon Web Weavers does not retain private information collected by affiliate websites nor can Oregon Web Weavers guarantee the security of your information outside of our website.

Oregon Web Weavers Clients

What information do we collect and how it may be used

When you sign up for Oregon Web Weaver’s services, you provide us with both business contact and billing information. This information typically includes your name, address, phone number and email address as well as the billing contact for your company.

While you are an Oregon Web Weavers client, we manage your contact information along with your choice of services and customer logs. In addition, we may store your email correspondence with us and any other information necessary to enable us to successfully complete the services you’ve purchased from us.

Oregon Web Weavers will not, except for reasons stated below, disclose to third parties the contents of any email or account information.  Oregon Web Weavers will only disclose client information:  

  1. When it is necessary to purchase services or products on the client’s behalf to carry out the stated service. 
  2. If it becomes necessary to provide information to a law enforcement agency and appears to pertain to the commission of a crime.
