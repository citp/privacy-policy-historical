> This is the web site of **The Orvis Company, Inc.**
> 
> Our postal address is **PO Box 798, Historic Route 7A, Manchester, VT 05254**  
>   
  
---  
  
> We can be reached via e-mail at [**Ask Orvis**](https://web.archive.org/intro.asp?subject=31),or you can reach us by telephone at 888-235-9763 
> 
> * * *  
  
> For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name(where possible), but not the email address. We also collect the referring domain name (where possible) of visitors to our Web page. The information we collect is used to improve our Web page's content and ease of navigation.  
  
> We ask for your email address in the course of doing business with us in order to send you a confirmation notice or to resolve a question about your transaction. In addition, we encourage you to subscribe to the **[Orvis Electronic Newsletter](https://web.archive.org/intro.asp?subject=57)** , which contains information about sales, special events, and enhancements to our web site sent by email. We will only send these promotional emails to you after you've specifically chosen to receive them and each email contains information on unsubscribing. **_Orvis does not share, sell or trade email addresses_**.  
  
> When you shop our commerce section we use a cookie to keep track of your selections. This cookie is like taking a number for service when walking in the door. Your selections are stored on our server, not on your computer. This is similar to asking a sales clerk to hold onto your merchandise while you continue shopping. When you are ready to check out, your cookie identifies which selections of merchandise belong to you. This cookie contains no personal information about you and no one but Orvis can retrieve the cookie.  
  
> If you supply us with your postal address on-line, you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending email to us at the above address, calling us at the above telephone number, or writing to us at the above address. You may also receive mailings from other reputable companies. You can, however, have your name put on our do-not-share list by sending email to us at [**customer_service@orvis.com**](mailto:customer_service@orvis.com?subject=Please%20do%20not%20share%20my%20address.), calling us at the above telephone number, or writing to us at the above address.   
  
> Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed on-line, **but not for any other reason**. Orvis does not share, sell or trade telephone numbers.
