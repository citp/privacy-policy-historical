# Oddpath - Privacy policy

The Oddpath Privacy Policy statement discloses the privacy practices employed by Oddpath. To learn more about Oddpath's privacy policy please read the information listed below. 

This policy covers how Oddpath treats personal information that we may collect and receive, including information related to your past use of our services and products. Identification like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that which is not otherwise publicly available is considered to be personal information. By no means does this policy apply to the practices of companies that Oddpath doesn't control or own or to individuals that Oddpath does not manage. 

Oddpath allows people to share their experiences and or insights about local services, businesses, and events with the community. In order to obtain this we gather information from and about users and distribute it with others. 

NOTICE: Click here ([onguardonline.gov](http://onguardonline.gov/)) for practical tips from the federal government and the technology industry to help you guard against Internet fraud, secure your computer and protect your personal information. 

## Collection and use of user information

Oddpath collects personal information (name and e-mail) from its users except as required by (a) federal, state, or international law, (b) subpoena, or court order, (c) the user's written consent, or (d) any other legitimate concerns, such as, ethical conflicts and genuine emergencies. Oddpath will not sell, share, or rent the user information to any third party. 

### General

When you register we ask for information such as your email address, name, and a password. We collect personal information when you register with Oddpath, when you use our services or products, when you visit our pages or the pages of certain associates, and when you enter certain sweepstakes or promotions. Oddpath may at times combine information about an individual that we have, with information we obtain, from business associates or other companies. 

### Children

Oddpath is not aimed at children, and doesn't knowingly solicit information from any child under the age of 13. In any case should a child of whom Oddpath knows to be under 13 should send personal information to Oddpath, we will use that information just to respond openly to that child (or guardian/parent) to inform him/her that we must have parental authority before receiving his/her personal data. 

If and when a child under age 13 attempts to register with Oddpath, we ask the child to have his/her parent or guardian create the account or to obtain parental permission. Oddpath is not responsible nor is it to be held accountable for children accessing or using their products or services. 

## Information Disclosure

Oddpath doesn't split, sell, share, or rent personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies apart from providing service's you may have requested, with your authorization, or under the following circumstances: 

Oddpath may, from time to time, displays targeted advertisements based on personal information. Advertisers may believe that people who network with, view, or click targeted ads meet the targeting criteria. 

Oddpath will not substitute personal information to the advertiser when you intermingle with or view a targeted ad. By viewing an ad you are accepting the possibility that the advertiser will make the hypothesis that you meet the certain targeted criteria used to display the ad. 

Oddpath works with vendors, associates, advertisers, and other service providers in different industries and categories of commerce. Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information and Preferences is your own choice. 

Oddpath reserves the right to transport information about you and your use of this website in the occurrence of a transfer of rights of Oddpath, such as a take over, acquisition, or merger with another company. 

### Information We Accumulate

Oddpath accumulates information that you present in the process of creating your account and user profile on this website, including your name, email address, and location information such as city, state and zip. Oddpath also collects information in the form of the substance that you submit in the course of your use of this website; this may include but is not limited to reviews, photos, and ratings of different service providers and businesses. Oddpath also collects information such as your IP address, web browser, URL, etc. 

### Use of Your Information

Oddpath stores information about individuals and their use of this website. We also exhibit your Content to other users of this website in order to distribute your understanding and insights about local services, events, and business with the community. By doing so your Profile may also be displayed in connection with your Content. Your name and email address will be shown when sending an email alert to a friend through this website. Oddpath uses your email address to send updates & news regarding this website. If you choose not to receive emails of this type you can update your settings. Additionally, we may use your email address to alert you on behalf of a personal message from a friend. We use Non-personal Information to help provide the highest possible level of service to you the end user. 

## Your Personal Information

Individuals should be attentive that when you willingly disclose information about yourself in the Content that you propose to this website (including your name or email address in a review or comment that you post to Oddpath), that information can be composed and used by third parties and may result in unsolicited communication from others. Such actions are beyond Oddpath's control. Individuals should not post any information on Oddpath that you expect to keep private. As a registered user of Oddpath you can change your Profile at any time by accessing the "Account" section of this website. 

### Personal Information of Others

You can provide Oddpath with someone's email address so that individual can be invited to open an account on this website or to become your friend. We will use this information to contact that individual about the request. If for some reason an individual wishes to not receive such invitations he/she must alert us to not receive further notices. We may divulge such information if required to do so in order to obey with legal processes, as previously described in the privacy policy. 

## Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of information stored on the user's computer. Oddpath may access and set cookies on your computer. The cookie allows the Oddpath system to identify a user as a registered member and save some preferences that have nothing to do with a user's personal information. A small piece of encrypted data is stored in the cookie when a user logs in and this helps Oddpath identify the user later and automatically signs him/her in without the need of any manual action from the user. This cookie, in no way exposes any sensitive information. Oddpath's business partners make use of cookies on the website for various purposes such as serving/tracking advertisements, logging and measuring the effectiveness of advertisements etc. Oddpath lets other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to Oddpath's cookies. Oddpath, has control over the use of these cookies and is not responsible for the data collected through these cookies and for how or what the data collected by these cookies are used. 

## Correspondences

Oddpath may contact its users from time to time to notify of important changes on the system, technical updates etc. Oddpath may also store correspondences from its users for tracking purposes and these won't be shared/sold or rented to any thirdparty except as requred by (a) federal, state or international law, (b) subpoena or court order, (c) the user's written consent or other legit,genuine and ethical emergencies. 

## Links

Oddpath may enclose links to third party websites to which we have no association. Except as set forth herein, we don't share information about your use of this website nor yourself with those third parties, and are not liable for their privacy practices. Oddpath suggests that its users read the privacy policies on all such third party websites. 

## Security

Oddpath employs the best methods to protect its user information and data. You hereby concede that Oddpath is not accountable for any intercepted information sent via the web, and you hereby wave Oddpath from any and all claims associated to the use of intercepted information in any unlawful manner. Sensitive user information is encrypted and securely stored in a database. None of this information is stored on shared servers, but dedicated servers in a secure environment. 

## Choice/Opt-out

Oddpath allows its users to opt-out of receiving communication (emails) or the service at any time. If the user decides to opt-out of the service, his/her personal information and files/data will be removed from Oddpath unless required by law or court order.

Your continual use of the site indicates your acquiesce to any changes to this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions or feedback regarding this policy, please contact support@Oddpath
