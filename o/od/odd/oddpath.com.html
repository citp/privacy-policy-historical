Oddpath Privacy Policy

The Oddpath Privacy Policy statement discloses the privacy practices employed by Oddpath. To learn more about Oddpath's privacy policy please read the information listed below.

By no means does this policy apply to the practices of companies that Oddpath doesn't control or own or to individuals that Oddpath does not manage.

Oddpath allows people to share their experiences and or insights about local services, businesses, and events with the community. In order to obtain this we gather information from and about users and distribute it with others.

When you register we ask for information such as your email address, name, and a password.
