# Privacy Policy | Online Tips Blog

At www.onlinetipsblog.com, the privacy of visitors is utmost important to us. This privacy policy document describes the types of personal information received & collected by www.onlinetipsblog.com and how its used.

Mention below are the information that we get from our visitors and how its been used or safeguard-

Log Files  
Like many other existing blogs, websites www.onlinetipsblog.com make use of log files. This information includes IP address, type of browser used, date and time stamp, referring pages, existing pages, number of clicks, administrator of the site, time on site, users movement around the site, demographic information. These statistics are kept for our record purposes only.

Cookie and Web Beacons  
www.onlinetipsblog.com uses cookie to store information about visitors record like visited pages, preferences & other valuable information send via their browser.

DoubleClick DART Cookies  
1\. To serve ads on .com, Google as a third party vendor uses cookies.  
2\. Google uses cookies to improve your experience on www.onlinetipsblog.com including the display of more relevant ads.  
3\. User may opt out of DART or Google interest-based advertising like Adsense by visiting http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Following are the list of our advertisers -  
Google Adsense

www.onlinetipsblog.com has no access to control over the cookies that are used by third party advertisers. You should contact the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practises and on instructions on how to opt out of certain cookies.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so on your browser.  
If you wish to further know about how information is collected & used by DoubleClick please fill the form at http://www.google.com/support/websearch/bin/request.py?form_type=user&stage=fm&user_type=user&contact_type=privacy&hl=en

Confidential and Security  
DoubleClick has implemented security measures in order to protect information being collected from loss, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure or alteration.

Changes to this Privacy Policy  
We amend the rights to change this privacy policy from time to time as we add new information.
