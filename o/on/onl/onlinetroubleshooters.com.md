Online Trouble Shooters respects the privacy of every individual who visits its Web sites and uses its services. This privacy statement provides notice of the standards and terms under which Online Trouble Shooters protects the privacy of information supplied by visitors to sites on the World Wide Web that are owned and operated by Online Trouble Shooters including [www.onlinetroubleshooters.com](http://www.onlinetroubleshooters.com/). This privacy statement provides notice of our information collection practices and of the ways in which your information may be used. This policy may change from time to time, so please check back periodically to review this information.

**3\. Non-Personally Identifiable (Generic) Information.  
**  
In general, Online Trouble Shooters gathers some generic information automatically. Generic information does not reveal the identity of the visitor. It usually includes information about the Internet address assigned to your computer, the number and frequency of visitors, and the Online Trouble Shooters sites visited. Online Trouble Shooters gathers this information for the limited purpose of determining customer service and Web site needs. We accomplish this by using certain technologies including "cookies" (a technology that can be used to provide the visitor with tailored information about Online Trouble Shooters services). Online Trouble Shooters does not combine information collected in this way with any personally identifiable information. You can set your Web browser to notify you when you receive a cookie and you can refuse it. However, refusing some cookies may make it impossible to use or navigate the Online Trouble Shooters Web site properly.

Online Trouble Shooters cannot safeguard any information you disclose in these locations. Additionally, Online Trouble Shooters Web sites may contain links to sites that belong to third parties unrelated to OTs. Online Trouble Shooters cannot protect any information you may disclose at these sites and recommends that you review the privacy policy statements of those sites you visit.

The Live Chat system that Online Trouble Shooters uses for customer support is a one-on-one chat. Online Trouble Shooters owns or licenses the Live Chat software and operates it on Online Trouble Shooters server and network. No third party collects any information that you provide through the Live Chat system. Online Trouble Shooters may retain and use logs of the Live Chat sessions for any of the following purposes:

* to analyze and resolve system problems;  
* to compile and update frequently asked questions (FAQ) and entries in the customer support Knowledge Base;  
* to report problems to and obtain technical support from Online Trouble Shooters vendors, suppliers, or service providers;  
* to train personnel providing support to customers via Live Chat;  
* to evaluate the job performance of personnel providing support to customers via Live Chat;  
* to measure the effectiveness of Live Chat in providing support to customers;  
* to improve the quality and effectiveness of Live Chat in providing support to customers; or  
* to perform any other internal task related to providing services to Online Trouble Shooters customers, provided that performance of the task does not require disclosure of any of the contents of a chat session without the customer's prior permission.
