Types of information Collected

The types of information that may be collected will vary depending upon your individual use of the OnLive Websites. The information that we may collect will likely include some personal information, such as your first and/or last name, gender, e-mail address, phone number, mobile number, mailing address, and birth date, and for those purchasing any particular product or service online, we may need to collect credit card information and the relevant billing and shipping address(es). We will also provide you a way to unsubscribe to emails from us if you have signed up to receive information from us.

Use of "cookies" and other technical means to collect information

We use cookies and other technologies to passively collect demographic information, personalize your experience on our site and monitor advertisements and other activities. For example, cookies can advise us whether you have visited one of the OnLive Websites previously, and/or may contain other information that will facilitate your next visit to one of the OnLive Websites.

Typically, cookies do not include individuals' names, e-mail addresses, or other information that is personally identifiable. However, we may combine information contained in cookies with your personal information in order to allow you to participate in particular activities you select that require the disclosure of any such personal information, and in an effort to make your subsequent visits to the OnLive Websites more enjoyable.

Please remember that with most modern browsers you can always disable cookies or set your browser to alert you when cookies are being sent to your computer. However, the decision to disable cookies may in fact prevent you from being able to use certain portions of the OnLive Websites.

Other technologies used by OnLive Websites include clear GIFs and IP address logging. Clear GIFs (a.k.a. web bugs, beacons or tags) are small graphic images placed on a web page, web-based document, or in an e-mail message. Clear GIFs are invisible to the user because they are typically very small (e.g., only 1-by-1 pixel) and the same color as the background of the web page, document or e-mail message. We do not use clear GIFs to collect personal information about you. However, we may use clear GIFs to capture statistical usage information for our web pages, features or other elements on a web page. We may correlate this information to a user to personalize user experience and for statistical analysis of user experiences on our web pages.

OnLive may also collect information in the form of logs (i.e., files that record website activity and garner statistics about users' web-browsing activities, such as how many users have visited the Websites, and how often, and how many "hits" a particular webpage has received). The entries comprising such log files are generated anonymously and are not associated with any particular user, computer, or browser. Log files are only used by us to operate and improve our OnLive Websites, for our internal marketing and demographic studies, and to enable us to assess overall website activity and users' web-browsing activities, preferences and habits. We also maintain log files which contain Internet Protocol ("IP") addresses. An "IP address" is a unique number that can be used to identify your computer whenever you log on to the Internet. In general, we only use such log files to monitor traffic on our Websites, to report aggregate information to our advertisers and to troubleshoot technical problems , and for security reasons and to foster the safety of the Websites.
