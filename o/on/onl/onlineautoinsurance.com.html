Information Collection and Use

TZ Insurance Solutions LLC collects information on OnlineAutoInsurance.com at several different points on our Web site. If you apply for auto insurance quotes or to purchase an insurance policy, we require that you provide certain personally identifiable information, including your name, address email address, date of birth, and phone number. When you enter another person’s driver information, we will also collect their name and date of birth. This information is necessary to process your application and to contact you if necessary. Once you have entered your name, address and email address your information is then passed onto a third party Insurance Company in order to supply you with a quote that best fits your needs. If you or the other driver does not wish us to share your personal information with these companies, please contact us at ben@onlineautoinsurance.com.

Accessing Your Personally Identifiable Information

If your personally identifiable information changes, or if you no longer desire our service, you may correct, update, amend, delete/remove or deactivate it by emailing our Customer Support at ben@onlineautoinsurance.com or by contacting us by postal mail at the contact information listed below.

If you choose to use our contact us form we require you to give us a valid email address. We do ask for Policyholder’s name, name of person inquiring, Insurance Company, Policy number and phone number only if you are inquiring about your or someone’s else’s coverage information. We collect this information for the purpose of responding to policy inquiries and to answer questions to the best of our ability.

We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.
