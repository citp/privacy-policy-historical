# Online Games Corp. Privacy Policy

#### Online Games Corp. Privacy Policy

Online Games Corp. is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. Currently no personally identifiable information about you is collected at all. The only data collected is used for statistical purposes and keep track of the traffic generated by the site.

This site, Online Games Corp., uses the services of Engage for the serving and/or targeting of ads, promotions and other marketing messages. To do this, Engage collects anonymous data through the use of cookies. To learn more about Engage, including your ability to opt out of the Engage system, go to <http://www.engage.com/privacy>.
