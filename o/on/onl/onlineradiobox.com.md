# Online Radio Box - listen to the radio online for free

## Collecting personal information

OnlineRadioBox.com use technologies like cookies (small files stored by your browser), web beacons, or unique device identifiers to anonymously identify your computer or device so we can deliver a better experience. Our systems also log information like your browser, operating system and IP address. 

OnlineRadioBox.com also may collect personally identifiable information that you provide to us, such as your name or email address. If authorized by you, we may also access profile and other information from services like Facebook, Google plus, Vk.com, OK.ru. 

Our systems are not designed to associate personal information with your activities (such as pages you view or things you click on or search for).

We do not knowingly contact or collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly obtain parental consent or remove the information.

## Social Sign-On

We may collect Personal Information from a social media website when you use your social media credentials to log into the OnlineRadioBox.com. For example, when you log in with your Facebook credentials, we may collect the Personal Information you have made publicly available on Facebook, such as your name and profile picture. We may also obtain other non-public information with your permission. If you subscribe to our newsletter, then we will collect certain Personal Information from you, such as your email address.

## Location

To customize our service for you, OnlineRadioBox.com may collect and store your precise geographic location (country and language) as part of your user profile. This data generally is not shared with others and uses only for better site usage.

## AD Tracking

Ad companies may use and collect anonymous data about your interests to customize content and advertising here and in other sites and applications. Interest and location data may be linked to your device, but is not linked to your identity.

This privacy policy was last updated on 10/2/2015. Our privacy policy may change from time to time. If we make any material changes to our policies, we will place a prominent notice on our website or application. If the change materially affects registered users, we will send a notice to you by email, push notification or text.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policies, please contact us: [http://onlineradiobox.com/feedback/](https://web.archive.org/feedback)
