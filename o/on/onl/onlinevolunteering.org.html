Privacy Policy


The United Nations Volunteers (UNV) program hosts and manages this Online Volunteering service, and respects the privacy of its users. The following statement details our practices related to the collection, use and disclosure of user's personal information. UNV values your trust and appreciate your participation in our online volunteering service.

    1. ACCOUNTABILITY: UNV is fully accountable for all practices related to the collection, use and disclosure of user's personal information. All personal information management practices are disclosed in this Privacy Policy.
    2. OPENNESS: If you have any questions, or you would like further information on our personal information management practices, please contact us.
    3. COMMENTS: If you would like to make a comment concerning our personal information management practices, please contact us.
    4. COMPLAINTS: If you wish to file a complaint as to our compliance with our Privacy Policy, please contact us. UNV takes your privacy complaints very seriously, so please be clear and detailed as to all facts related to your complaint.
    5. LIMITED COLLECTION: The personal information collected by our various forms is limited to that which is necessary to fulfill the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy. No additional information is collected, directly or indirectly.
    6. PURPOSE: The Feedback and Registration Form collects personal information (e.g., name, address, e-mail) which will be used to contact you for the following purposes:
      • Send you information from UNV. You may choose to withdraw from this service and no longer receive such information by changing the configuration on your "My OV" page or by contacting UNV.
      • Contact you in the event of a necessary communication exchange requested by you or initiated by UNV. No Opt-Out right is available given the explicit requirement for the UNV to contact you, for example, to provide you with confirmation regarding an activity you have engaged in via this web site.
    7. LIMITED USE, DISCLOSURE AND RETENTION: Your personal information will not be used for any other purpose other than stated in this Privacy Policy, except only with your explicit consent or as required by law. Your personal information will not be sold or transferred to any other third party under any circumstances. Your personal information will only be retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes defined, or as required by law.
    8. ACCESS: The personal information collected by the UNV is limited to what you have provided directly. No additional personal information is collected through any other source. You can view all of your personal information that you have submitted to UNV via our online volunteering service web site by logging in to our service and visiting you’re "My OV" page.
    9. ACCURACY: The accuracy of your personal information is governed by the accuracy of the information you provided at the time of collection. UNV does not verify the accuracy of personal information provided. At the time you entered your personal information you were requested to verify the information for accuracy. UNV is not responsible for any errors or problems that arise as a result of inaccurate information provided by you. If you still wish to verify the accuracy of your personal information, please see ACCESS above.
    10. SECURITY SAFEGUARDS: UNV will protect your personal information by appropriate security safeguards. Your personal information transmissions. Appropriate information security measure will be in place to safeguard your personal information during storage and use.
    11. INFORMED CONSENT: The statements made in this Privacy Policy constitute a complete and unambiguous disclosure of all personal information collection, use and disclosure practices to be conducted by UNV. Your access to our Online Volunteering service, and your request to make a donation constitute your informed consent and acceptance with these stated personal information practices.
    12. "EMAIL THIS PAGE": If a user elects to use our referral service for informing a friend about our site, we ask them for the friend's name and e-mail address. UNV will automatically send the friend a one-time email inviting them to visit the site. Email addresses collected will not be captured or used for any other purpose.
    13. LINKS: Our site links to other sites whose information practices may be different from ours. Visitors should consult the other sites' privacy notices they are not covered by our privacy policy.
    14. LOG FILES: Like most standard web site servers we use log files. This includes IP addresses, browser type, ISP (internet service provider), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses, etc. are not linked to personally identifiable information.
    15. COOKIES: This UNV web site makes use of "cookies." These expire as soon as the user closes all browser windows, unless the user selects the "Remember Me" option.
    16. OPT-OUT: Users who no longer wish to receive our email updates and newsletters must withdraw from our services entirely. Users are required to receive our email updates and newsletters. You may choose to withdraw from this service and no longer receive such information by changing the configuration on your "My OV" page or by contacting UNV.
    17. Users may change their personal information, or remove their information from our records, by selecting "Edit Profile" at the top of "My OV."
    18. If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, contact UNV.
