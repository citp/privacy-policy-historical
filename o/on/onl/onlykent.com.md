# Privacy

**Introduction**  
This privacy document describes the practices and policies Only Kent adopts in relation to personal data.

**Cookies**  
Only Kent may use cookies to store session information to help with some advertisement information.

**Advertisers**  
The advertisements on Only Kent are provided by outside advertisement companies and these could contain cookies. These cookies are not collected by Only Kent and are used by their respective companies and sites; they are not accessible to Only Kent.
