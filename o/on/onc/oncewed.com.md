# Privacy Policy | Once Wed

1) The Privacy Policy section explains how we(Once Wed) handle the use of information collected from our visitors on our website. Please read carefully this section since it may answer a lot of questions that you might have.

2) The Privacy Policy section does not apply to practices of third-party services that our website may use. For information on this matter, read the appropriate section below on this page. For more information, check the Privacy Policy of the third-party services on their websites.

Collection and Use of personal information  
You may visit Once Wed without revealing any personal information. However, we need some personal information when you decide to list a dress on our listing service or when you decide to comment on a blog post. For ex. when you want to comment on a blog post, we need your email address in order to inform to approve all comments.

By accessing the our services and voluntarily providing us with the requested personal information, you consent to the collection and use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy.

We may otherwise disclose your personal information when:

* We need to respond to subpoenas, court orders or such other legal process  
* We need to protect the personal safety of the users of our website or defend our rights or property  
* We find that your actions on our websites violate our Terms of Use document or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services

Consent  
If you do not consent to the collection, use or disclosure of your personal information , as this Privacy Policy outlines, please do not provide us any personal information. If you did, it means that you agree to our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

Changes to this Privacy Policy  
We may at any time, without notice to you and in its sole discretion, amend this policy from time to time. Please review this policy periodically. Your continued use of Once Wed websites after any such amendments signifies your acceptance thereof.

Questions or Suggestions  
If you have questions or suggestions about this privacy policy, or your own personal information, please contact us by clicking on the Ask Emily page that you will find on the navigation bar on every page.
