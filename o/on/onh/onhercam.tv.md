# OnHerCam.com - Privacy Policy

OnHerCam.com is committed to maintaining a privacy policy that benefits each individual customer's preferences.

NOTE: Our privacy policy is null and void in cases of fraud!

This policy explains the privacy practices for MASSIVE ONLINE LIMITED, a UK-based corporation, (hereinafter referred to as "Trifecta, Inc.", "Trifecta", "OnHerCam.com", "Massive Onlne", "OnHerCam", "site", "our", "we", or "us"). In order to provide interactive computer billing and fraud services, Trifecta collects and uses certain information about you. This policy explains what we collect, what we use and how you can interact with Trifecta in order to ensure the accuracy of the data we collect and use.

1) Information Collection:  
Visitors can access our Web site home page and browse our Web site without disclosing any personal data. However, in order to provide you with a particular product or service online, Trifecta may request that you voluntarily supply Trifecta with primary personal data (such as name and email address) and identifiers (such as credit card number and web site password). Trifecta gives you the option of using a secure transmission method of sending such information.

Trifecta receives and stores all information that you enter in our Web site and billing pages. In addition, Trifecta collects information about you during your visit, such as your IP Address, and Trifecta employs its software in order to ascertain your physical location when making a purchase. However, we do not collect information from other sources, such as public records or bodies, or private organizations. You are required to provide Trifecta with accurate and up-to-date information, and failure to provide Trifecta with such information could result in voiding your Agreement with Trifecta.

You can inquire about your personal data by sending an e-mail to admin.trifecta@gmail.com. Within a week, Trifecta shall provide you with a readable copy of the personal data whichTrifecta keeps about you. Trifecta charges a $25 processing fee for the request. Prior to completing your request, Trifecta may require that you provide Trifecta with proof of your identity. Trifecta reserves the right to refuse any request for information though Trifecta will communicate the reason for its decision.

2) Information Use:  
Trifecta is the sole owner of the information collected on its sites. Trifecta will not sell, share, or rent this information to third parties in ways different from what is disclosed it this policy. Trifecta shall release data when required by law, to enforce its Agreement or the contract rights of its clients. Trifecta shares the following data with the owner/operator of the web site from which you are purchasing goods and services utilizing Trifecta services: the URL you are purchasing goods or services from, your e-mail address, your name, your address, your phone number, your date of birth, and your agreement to be bound by Trifecta terms and conditions. Trifecta uses the data it collects for the technical administration of the Web site, research and development, and fraud deterrence.

3) Information sharing:  
We ensure that your personal data will not be disclosed to governmental institutions and authorities except if required by law or other regulation.

4) Email correspondence:  
Trifecta may request a confirmation when you receive and open and e-mail communication delivered by Trifecta. We archive and identify the origination IP address of all email sent into our network.

5) Third party information:  
Occasionally, Trifecta receives information from third parties to compare it to information that you had provided to Trifecta for fraud prevention purposes.

6) Cookies:  
A cookie is a piece of data stored on the visitor's hard drive containing information about the visitor. Trifecta employs "cookie" technology. We use Cookie to store personal data, to authenticate you as a Site user, for security purposes, and to track our affiliate click-throughs and sign-up rate.

7) Children:  
Trifecta does not request, or knowingly collect, personal data from anyone under the age of 18. Furthermore, Trifecta does not knowingly give children the ability to post messages or otherwise distribute information about themselves on our web site.

8) Security:  
We take every precaution to protect our visitors' information. We have implemented security policies, rules and technical measures to protect the personal data that we have under our control from unauthorized access, improper use or disclosure, unauthorized modification, unlawful destruction, or accidental loss. Trifecta utilizes Secure Socket Layer (SSL) software when receiving and transmitting electronic payments. Trifecta encrypts the information that you provided.

In addition, Trifecta does everything to protect your information off-line. All employees and data processors who have access to, and are associated with, the processing of personal data are obliged to respect the confidentiality of our visitors' personal data. Trifecta employs a username and password scheme in order to limit and achieve access to our computer network by our employees and third parties.
