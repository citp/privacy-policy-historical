OneLoad Privacy & Security Policy


Our Commitment

Simply put, we will never abuse your trust -- that means that we will never share, sell, rent or otherwise disclose any of your personal or company information without your explicit consent.

To ensure the integrity of our privacy policy and to prevent unauthorized access to account information we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure your account information. All account information is encrypted and stored on our servers in the United States.

Passwords

Please don't give out or share your account password. If you forget your password go to the following webpage to be emailed a password reminder, click here. In the event that you suspect that your account password has been or will be misused, please immediately inform TubeMogul. In no event shall TubeMogul be responsible for any such misuse(s).

Ad Targeting

TubeMogul offers a technology platform for multi-site video advertising. The platform is designed to value and place bids on individual ad impressions, across major inventory suppliers, exchanges, and direct sites. We provide ad delivery and reporting for clients.

TubeMogul may use information about you that we collect through the viewing of videos uploaded via TubeMogul or via our partner's sites to deliver targeted video ads on the websites of third parties. We do not share this information with third parties.

We use cookies (described more fully below) to facilitate the use of this information while you are online. In order to appropriately safeguard the information in them, as described above, these cookies are encrypted.

If you prefer not to receive targeted video ads as described above you can opt out by clicking the link below:

Opt Out

Note that opting out will not turn off advertising from other sources. Also, if you opt out and later delete your cookies, use a different browser or buy a new computer, you will need to renew your opt-out choice.

Cookies and Data Collection

"Cookies" are small files placed on the hard drive of your computer. We use cookies to provide you better service by allowing you to enter your password less frequently during a session and to help us understand and analyze our web page flow. We collect only anonymous, non-Personally Identifiable Information, such as browser type (e.g., Firefox or Internet Explorer), operating system (e.g., Macintosh or Windows), Internet provider (e.g., Verizon or Comcast), IP address, and whether you respond to an ad. We do not collect sensitive data. Cookies are employed to track stream and viewership data and to improve user experience by providing information such as buffering events per user back to our video site partners. We also use the data collected to report on effectiveness of campaigns for our clients and to optimize campaigns as they run to improve viewing time and brand awareness metrics.

We Never Transfer or Sell Data

We NEVER transfer or sell data to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

We retain the non-personally identifiable information collected via our technology for up to one year. After one year, we discard the information. All data is encrypted and stored on our servers in the United States.

Email and Spam

You may not use our communication tools to send spam. If you send an email to an email address that is not registered in our community (via Refer a Friend or other tool), we do not permanently store that email or use that email address for any marketing purposes. We do not rent or sell these email addresses.

Marketing

Again, we will never sell, rent or disclose your personally identifiable information (i.e., name, email and mailing address) to any third parties without your explicit consent. We may, but are not obliged to, occasionally send you strictly service or feature-related enhancement announcements.
