# Privacy Policy - ONE Championship

Thank you for visiting ONE Championship Website. Your privacy is important to us. We will not collect any information from you for use on this website or any other related website unless you choose to provide your information to us.

If you provide your personal/company information to us (via online enquiry and eNewsletter subscription form), please note that this information provided is through optional, voluntary submissions. Your contact information will be saved for record and follow-up communication but will not be distributed or sold to other third parties.

ONE Championship reserves the right to change the privacy policy and will notify users of any such changes via email or by posting a website notice, where appropriate.
