ON Networks Privacy Policy

ON Networks, Inc. ("ON Networks") recognizes that its customers, visitors, users, and others (collectively or individually "Users") who use www.onnetworks.com or other web sites owned or operated by ON Networks (collectively the "Website") value their privacy. This Privacy Policy details important information regarding the use and disclosure of User information collected on the Website.

  • Your use of the Website and any personal information you provide on the Website remains subject to the terms of this Privacy Policy and the ON Networks Terms of Use.
  • Please note that any comment or other content you post or send to the Website becomes published content and is not considered personally identifiable information subject to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

General
  • ON Networks does not require that you register in order to view its programming. Registration is required to use certain interactive features such as posting comments, participating in user forums, and in order to receive notices about shows.
  • ON Networks collects personal information when you register with ON Networks, when you visit Website, when you sign up to receive notices of upcoming shows, when you enter promotions or sweepstakes, or any time you provide information to ON Networks about yourself. ON Networks may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.
  • When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, and other personally-identifying information ("Personal Information"). When you register with ON Networks and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.
  • ON Networks automatically receives and records other non-personally-identifying information ("Non-Personal Information") on our server logs from your browser even if you do not register with ON Networks, for example, your IP address, ON Networks cookie information, and the page you visit.
  • ON Networks uses Non-Personal Information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.
Children
  • No part of the ON Networks website is directed at persons under 13. If you are under 13 years of age, then please do not use or access OnNetwork at any time or in any manner.
  • ON Networks does not knowingly collect or maintain personally identifiable information or non-personally-identifiable information from persons under 13 years of age.
  • If ON Networks learns that personally identifiable information of persons under 13 years of age has been collected on ON Networks without verified parental consent, then ON Networks will take the appropriate steps to delete this information.

2. Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • ON Networks does not rent, sell, or share Personal Information with other people or non-affiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:
  • We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with ON Networks under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your Personal Information to help ON Networks communicate with you about offers from ON Networks and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information.
  • We provide Non-Personal Information to interested third-parties for purposes of understanding the usage patterns for certain content, services, advertisements, promotions, and/or functionality on the Website.
  • We use both your Personal Information and certain Non-Personal Information to improve the quality and design of the Website and to create new features, promotions, functionality, and services by storing, tracking, and analyzing User preferences and trends.
  • We may share Personal Information in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other forms of legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.
  • We may share Personal Information if we believe it is necessary in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of ON Networks. terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
  • We may transmit Personal Information if ON Networks is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, ON Networks will notify active known users if there is a need to change this Privacy Policy based on an ON Networks acquisition or merger.
  • In the unlikely event of our bankruptcy, insolvency, reorganization, receivership, or assignment for the benefit of creditors, we may not be able to control how your personal information is treated, transferred, or used.
  • Keep in mind that any personal information, or comments that you voluntarily disclose on the Website, for example on video viewing pages, or on discussion boards, becomes publicly available. Either your account name or your real name (your choice) is displayed to other Users when you send messages through the Website.

  • ON Networks may utilize the third party advertisers or ad serving companies to display targeted advertisements to users, but ON Networks does not provide any Personal Information to any third party advertisers.
