# OkCupid! Privacy Policy

Humor Rainbow, Inc. is the owner and operator of the OKCupid.com website (the "OKCupid Website"). Humor Rainbow has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy and to establish certain guidelines for your use of the OKCupid Website. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the OKCupid Website. 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer the OKCupid Website. Your IP address is used to help identify you and to gather broad demographic information that is anonymous in nature and is not associated with particular individuals. 

The OKCupid Website contains links to other sites. Humor Rainbow, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. 

**Matching Service and Public Forums**  
The OKCupid Website offers a personality matching service (the "Matching Service") to users and makes chat rooms, message boards, news groups and other public forums available to all users without restriction. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in the course of using the Matching Service and participating in any of the public forums becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. 

**YOU UNDERSTAND, ACKNOWLEDGE AND AGREE THAT THE MATCHING SERVICE INVOLVES THE PREPARATION OF A PERSONALITY PROFILE THAT IS DERIVED FROM INFORMATION PROVIDED BY YOU, AS WELL AS ANALYSIS AND MATCHING TECHNOLOGIES PROPRIETARY TO HUMOR RAINBOW, INC. AND INCLUDES A LIBERAL MIX OF GUESSWORK, SARCASM, LIES, HYPERBOLE AND OTHER LITERARY DEVICES INTENDED TO MAKE THE PERSONALITY PROFILES AMUSING TO OTHER USERS. THE PERSONALITY PROFILE ALSO REVEALS, WITHIN A RADIUS OF APPROXIMATELY FIVE MILES, YOUR LOCATION. YOUR PERSONALITY PROFILE WILL BE MADE AVAILABLE TO OTHER USERS OF THE MATCHING SERVICE AT HUMOR RAINBOW, INC.'S SOLE DISCRETION. THE PERSONALITY PROFILES ARE NOT SCIENTIFIC, MAY BE FACTUALLY INCORRECT, MISLEADING AND SUBJECTIVE. HUMOR RAINBOW, INC. MAKES NO WARRANTIES OF ANY KIND WHATSOEVER IN RESPECT OF THE MATCHING SERVICE INCLUDING, WITHOUT LIMITATION, WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY OR FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE.**

**Security and Identity**  
The OKCupid Website has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control; however, all security is subject to possible circumvention, and we cannot and do not provide any guarantees regarding the effectiveness of the security we employ or our ability to prevent third parties, acting unlawfully, from obtaining information that you provide to us. 

**HUMOR RAINBOW, INC. ENCOURAGES ALL USERS TO MAINTAIN A PSUEDONYMOUS IDENTITY WHEN USING THE MATCHING SERVICE OR ANY OTHER PUBLIC ASPECTS OF THE OKCUPID WEBSITE. HUMOR RAINBOW, INC. CANNOT ENSURE THAT YOUR IDENTITY, TELEPHONE NUMBER, WORK OR HOME ADDRESS AND OTHER PERSONAL IDENTIFYING OR CONTACT INFORMATION WILL NOT BE DISCOVERED BY OTHER USERS OF THE OKCUPID WEBSITE. THIS IS PARTICULARLY TRUE BECAUSE YOUR LOCATION (WITHIN A RADIUS OF APPROXIMATELY FIVE MILES) WILL BE REVEALED. IT IS, OF COURSE, MORE LIKELY THAT YOUR TRUE IDENTITY WILL BE DISCOVERED IF YOU POST PHOTOGRAPHS OR SHARE OTHER UNIQUE IDENTIFYING INFORMATION ABOUT YOURSELF ON PUBLIC AREAS OF THE OKCUPID WEBSITE.**

**Third Party Advertising**  
We use various third-party advertising companies to display ads when you visit the OKCupid Website. These companies may use anonymous information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to the OKCupid Website (and web sites operated by others) in order to provide advertisements on the OKCupid Website about goods and services that may be of interest to you. 

**Third Party Cookies**  
In the course of serving advertisements to the OKCupid Website users, our third-party advertisers may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser. 

**No Children**  
The OKCupid website is for users 18 years of age and older. Children under the age of 18 are not permitted to use the OKCupid Website or to otherwise submit any personal information to the OKCupid Website. 

**Choice/Opt-Out**

We provide users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving certain types of advertising as well communications from our partners. If you wish to do so, [click here](https://web.archive.org/settings) for more information. 

**Archiving and Preservation of Information**  
We archive all information, including personal information, you provide in the course of using the Matching Service and any other aspects of the OKCupid Website. We store that information on servers that are controlled by us or by third parties who have agreed to comply with our commitments made under this Privacy Policy and with whom we have a contractual relationship. Archived information will not ordinarily be erased unless and until your entire account with OKCupid.com is deleted, and even then we may retain some or all of your information on back-up files. 

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of the OKCupid Website, or your dealings with the OKCupid Website, you can contact [privacy@okcupid.com](mailto:privacy@okcupid.com).
