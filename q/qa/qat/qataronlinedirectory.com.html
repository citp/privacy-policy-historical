Privacy Policy

 

Trans Media International W.L.L. is committed to protecting the privacy of personal information you might provide via the websites powered by us. This is to explain our privacy policy on how we manage the information received.



1.  Your Personal Information

You acknowledge and agree that we may collect information about you. The information may be obtained from:

 

  • Personal information provided voluntarily when registering online.

  • Website usage information through tracking your click pattern on our sites.
  • Cumulative data, measuring traffic to our websites but not relating to you personally.
 

You may decide whether to provide any information we seek from you. However, if you do not provide it, we may not be able to provide our services to you effectively.
 


2.  Access to, disclosure & correction of your information

You may seek information we hold about you from the website and ask for any details that are wrong to be corrected. We may require to use the information for related activities of Trans Media International WLL or its sister concerns, but we will not disclose it to third parties without your consent.

We may share or transfer the information in our databases if required by law to disclose the information, to protect the security or integrity of our databases or this website, to take precautions against legal liability, or in the event of a corporate sale, merger, closure or similar event.

 

 

 

3. Security of Database

We keep your personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or to comply with any applicable legal requirements.

In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. Our servers and our databases are protected by industry standard security technology, such as industry standard firewalls and password protection.

Our employees who have access to personal data have been trained to handle such data properly and in accordance with our security protocols and strict standards of confidentiality. Although we cannot guarantee against any loss, misuse, unauthorised disclosure, alteration or destruction of data, we will try to prevent such unfortunate occurrences.

The information collected by cookies enables us to decipher user preferences & patterns thus providing valuable insight into improving the quality of the services we provide to you. You can choose to disable all cookies by managing your browser preferences. For most browsers this can be done via "Options" or "Preferences" under one of your browser menus.
