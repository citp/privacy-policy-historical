    Privacy Policy

    Qango.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices:

    Information Automatically Logged

    We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, administer our website, produce statistical visitor data reports, and to protect you and us from security breaches of our servers, programs or files.

    Emails Automatically Sent

    We use several programs that automatically send you an email when we update, change, remove or classify/reclassify your information. These emails are generated by our software and relate to any listing you may have submitted for inclusion or actions you may have taken with regard to your mailing list subscription status, or forum profile or postings. Occasional emails are sent from other programs for information purposes only and these usually do not require a reply or response.

    Forms

    Some of our site's registration forms require users to give us contact information (like their name, email, and postal address, etc).

    Contact information from the registration forms is used for a variety of purposes, e.g.; to ship purchases, to send information about our company, etc.

    We do not distribute, sell, exchange or otherwise propagate your email address to any other party.

    External Links

    Qango.com contains links to other sites. Qango.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

    Security

    Qango.com has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. Should there be a breach in our security we will work to repair any damage done, speedily and immediately upon discovery or notification. We will also endeavour to identify and take action against any person or entity that may be held accountable for such incidents.

    Contact Details

    If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of Qango.com, or your dealings with us, you can contact us by using our online form.
