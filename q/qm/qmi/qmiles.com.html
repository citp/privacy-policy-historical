General

Your privacy is very important to Qatar Airways Privilege Club and we understand how important it is to you. Qatar Airways Privilege Club aims to be as clear and open as it can be about what it does with your information. Once you choose to provide us with any information by which you can be identified, then it will only be used in accordance with this privacy policy. This privacy policy applies to Qatar Airways Privilege Club. Please view this policy every time you use the website as its terms may change and by using this website you are deemed to have read and agreed to the most recent version of this policy.

What information does Qatar Airways Privilege Club collect?

Qatar Airways Privilege Club collects the following information:
Your name, contact details, information requested on the Qatar Airways Privilege Club application form and flight booking details;
Where you order products Qatar Airways will collect your billing information, transaction and credit information;
Some pages use cookies, a data analysis technique. Please see below for more information.

How does Qatar Airways use this information?

Qatar Airways uses the information collected to help us understand your needs and provide you with a better service, and in particular for the following reasons:
To ensure the efficient running of the Qatar Airways Privilege Club.
To assess what special flight offers and services we can offer you.
Internal record keeping.
To improve our products and services.
Qatar Airways may disclose your personal information to our Qatar Airways Privilege Club partners and other entities for purposes relating to your membership (including providing benefits and services to you) and travel.
Qatar Airways’ representatives may follow up, either by email, phone, SMS or mail customers who have used this website as a part of Qatar Airways’ customer care procedures
If you gave your consent to such marketing when you registered as a member or subsequently, Qatar Airways may periodically send promotional emails about new products, special flight offers or other information, which Qatar Airways Privilege Club thinks you may find interesting, using the email or postal address, which you have provided when you registered as a member of Qatar Airways Privilege Club.
If you gave your consent to such marketing when you registered as a member or subsequently, Qatar Airways may provide your personal information to selected Qatar Airways Privilege Club partners who wish to send promotional emails about new products, special flight offers or other information, which Qatar Airways Privilege Club thinks you may find interesting, using the email or postal address, which you have provided when you registered as a member of Privilege Club.
From time to time, Qatar Airways Privilege Club may also use your information to contact you for market research purposes. Qatar Airways may contact you by email, phone, fax, SMS or mail.

We will not disclose your personal information to third parties except as detailed above or where a government or regulatory body or the law requires us to do so.

Qatar Airways is committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, Qatar Airways Privilege Club has put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information it collects on-line.

How we use cookies

A cookie is a piece of text which if you agree to will be placed on your computer’s hard drive. It helps to analyse web traffic or lets you know when you visit a particular site. Cookies allow web applications to respond to you as an individual. The web application can tailor its operations to your needs, likes and dislikes by gathering and remembering information about your preferences.

Qatar Airways Privilege Club uses cookies to track which pages are being used by whom. This helps Qatar Airways Privilege Club to analyse data about web page traffic and improve this website in order to tailor it to customer needs. Qatar Airways Privilege Club only uses this information for statistical analysis purposes and then the data is removed from the system. Denial of a traffic log cookie should not prevent you from using this website.
