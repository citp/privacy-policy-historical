Privacy Policy

QBurst is committed to protect your privacy.

QBurst does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people.

You can visit our website without telling us who you are and without revealing any information about yourself. There are times, however, when we may request certain information from you. You are at liberty to respond or not to respond to these requests. We use this information to get valuable feedback from your experience using our website/products. In addition, QBurst may also use this information to help serve you better, be it by informing you of new services, products, alliances etc., that could help you in your business. You shall not claim that any information which is given by you on this web-site is confidential and/or proprietary.

QBurst collects domain information of its visitors as part of its analysis of the use of this site. This data enables us to better know which customers visit our site, how often they visit, and what parts of the site they visit most often. QBurst uses this information to improve its Web-based offerings. This information is collected automatically and requires no action on your part. By accessing our site you grant your unconditional consent to allow QBurst to collect your domain names.

Some pages on this site use "cookies," which are small files that the site places on your hard drive for identification purposes. These files are used for site registration and customization the next time you visit us. Your Web browser may allow you to be notified when you are receiving a cookie, giving you the choice to accept it or not. By not accepting cookies, some pages may not fully function and you may not be able to access certain information on this site.

This site contains links to other sites. QBurst is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those Web sites.

QBurst reserve the right to change, modify, or update this statement at any time without notice.
