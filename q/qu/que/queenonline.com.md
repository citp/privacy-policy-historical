# Queen + Paul Rodgers – Privacy and Terms & Conditions

I. General Policy.

Note: Please see SectionII below for a description of certain specific policies applicable tothe collection and use of information from children under the age of 13.

EMI Records Limited(“EMI”) respects the privacy concerns of the visitors to thisWeb site. As a general policy, no personal information is automaticallycollected from users of the site. In the event that we do collect anypersonally identifiable data about visitors to this site, that informationis only collected on a voluntary basis, and it is only known to EMI [and,with respect to certain information, the relevant artist and their management].

Certain non-personalinformation of visitors (for example, the type of browser being used bythe visitor, the type of operating system in use by the visitor and thedomain name of the visitor’s Internet service provider) is recorded bythe standard operation of EMI’s Internet servers. This information isprimarily used for internal purposes, and only in an aggregate and non-personally-identifyingform. However, EMI Records sometimes does provide this information, inaggregate and non-personally identifying form, to other parties for marketing,advertising or other use.

Personally identifiableinformation may be collected by EMI when Web Site visitors voluntarilyprovide this information (including when visitors fill out contest entryforms, subscribe to newsletters/artist information bulletins or participatein other types of promotions). All such personal information is retainedby EMI and is not sold or otherwise transferred outside the company unlesswe obtain the user’s express consent to do so or if we are required bylaw or by court/governmental order to disclose such information in a particularcircumstance. This information is used by EMI for internal marketing andpromotional purposes, in order to better understand the use of our sitesand to support promotions included on these sites, as well as to enhanceuser enjoyment during the on-line experience. This data, in aggregate,non-personally-identifying form, may be provided to other parties formarketing, advertising or other uses. We sometimes also use e-mail addressesand other personally identifying information to contact visitors who communicatewith us. For example, we direct e-mail to visitors who provide us withtheir e-mail addresses for specific purposes such as receiving our e-mailnewsletters/bulletins or being notified if they have won one of our contests.Each e-mail newsletter or bulletin contains instructions on how to discontinuereceipt of the newsletter or bulletin.

This site also uses“cookies,” which are pieces of information that are transferredto an individual user’s hard drive for record-keeping purposes. EMI neveruses cookies to retrieve information from your computer that was not originallysent in a cookie and the cookie itself does not contain any personallyidentifying information. Cookies function by saving your passwords andsite preferences – and as a result, it is possible to speed up your futureactivities at our site and allow us to provide you information specificallytailored to your interests. Most browsers are initially set to acceptcookies. If you would prefer, you can set your browser to refuse cookiesor to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, it may be possiblethat some parts of the site will not function optimally if you do so.

This site containslinks to other Web sites. EMI is not responsible for the privacy policiesand practices or the content of such Web sites.

II. Specific PoliciesRegarding Children Under the Age of 13

EMI encourages parentsand guardians to spend time online with their children and to participatein the interactive activities offered on this site. Parents should considerusing parental control tools available from online services and softwaremanufacturers to help supervise their children’s online activities. Thesetools can also prevent children from disclosing their name, address, andother personal information online without parental permission.

Activities on thissite are carried out in compliance with the Federal Children’s OnlinePrivacy Protection Act. In accordance with the requirements of that Act,no personal information (including name, mailing address, telephone numberand email address) will be collected from any visitor under the age of13 without first obtaining verifiable consent from the parent or legalguardian of such child. Accordingly, no information should be submittedto or posted at this site by visitors under the age of 13 without theconsent of their parent or legal guardian. Parents or legal guardiansmay provide EMI with consent to collect such information by e-mail, byfacsimile or by postal mail at the number/addresses set forth below under“Contact Us.”

Parents and legalguardians may request to review information collected from their childrenunder the age of 13, may ask to have that information deleted, and mayrefuse to allow further collection or use of any child’s information.To request such review or to give instructions regarding information wehave collected about a child under the age of 13, parents/legal guardiansshould contact EMI as specified below under “Contact Us.”
