# Privacy Policy | Quetext

Last Updated: June 23, 2016

This Privacy Policy describes Quetext’s practices regarding the collection, use and disclosure of the information we collect from you when you use Quetext's applications (the “Service”). We take our obligations regarding your privacy seriously and have made every effort to draft this Privacy Policy in a manner that is clear and easy for you to understand. By accessing or using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy, and our [Terms of Service](https://web.archive.org/web/20161119191544id_/http%3A//www.quetext.com/terms-of-service)

### You Content

Your use of the Service will involve you inputting data (the “Content”) that is processed and temporarily cached on Quetext's servers.

### Information We Collect

You are not required to provide us with any Personally Identifiable Information as a Visitor.

### Third-party Tracking

We use Google Analytics in order to improve the performance and features of our Website, as well as monitor general statistics. These third party tracking tools are designed to collect only Non-Personal Information about your use of our Website. However, you understand that such tools are created and managed by parties outside our control. As such, we are not responsible for what information is actually captured by such third parties or how such third parties use and protect that information.

### Data Aggregation

We retain the right to collect and use any Non Personal Information collected from your use of our Website and aggregate such data for internal analytics that improve our Website and Service as well as for use or resale to others. At no time is your Personally Identifiable Information included in such data aggregations.

### Privacy Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. It is the responsibility of the User to check for any updates to this Privacy Policy. Any changes we make to our Privacy Policy are effective as of this Last Updated date and replace any prior Privacy Policies.
