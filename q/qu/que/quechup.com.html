1. Voluntary Subscription and Use of service:

2.1 Like most websites we use log files located on our servers. These include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses, etc. are not linked to personally identifiable information.
2.2 We use data collection devices such as "cookies" on certain pages of our website. "Cookies" are small files placed on your hard drive that assist us in providing customized services. We also offer certain features that are only available through the use of a "cookie". Cookies can also help us provide information which is targeted to your interests. Most cookies are "session cookies", meaning that they are automatically deleted from your hard drive at the end of a session. You are always free to decline our cookies if your browser permits.
2.3 We use third party advertising companies to serve ads on our behalf. Third party advertisers may also collect information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you, principally through the use of action beacons and/or cookies.

Inviting Friends and Sharing

Quechup offers services to invite friends to Quechup using terms which include, but not limited to, 'Invite Friends', 'Tell Your Friends About Quechup', 'Search Webmail Contacts' and 'Check Address Book'.
If you choose to use these services to invite a friend to Quechup we will request and use your friend's name and email address. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email inviting him or her to Quechup. The invite will contain your Quechup username as well as your real name and your email address so that your friend may easily identify who has sent the invite. Quechup stores and uses this information for the purpose of sending this one-time email and tracking the success of referrals.

We also provide a 'share this' service, if you choose to use this we will ask for your friend's name and email address. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email with for the item you have shared and any additional message you included. Quechup stores and uses this information to ensure that a user can only share each item once with a particular email address, preventing the service being used to spam or harass others via Quechup.

Your friend may submit a request that we remove this information from our database; remove requests of this nature will be completed within 30 days.
