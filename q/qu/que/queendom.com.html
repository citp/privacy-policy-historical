At Queendom.com, your privacy and peace of mind is our top priority.


Collection of personally identifiable data

Queendom.com does not collect personally identifiable information from any of our visitors without prior consent.

For My Profile members, the results of each test they take are automatically saved in their My Profile account. This information is secure and protected by a password and login name. Users should keep passwords carefully guarded as they would any other personal information. My Profile takes all necessary precautions to protect this information from third parties, and does not provide any third party access to these results. My Profile does occasionally use test results for statistical purposes, but only in an aggregated and anonymous form.

When visitors submit stories to the Fine Kettle of Fish or questions to Chaise Longue, they may be sharing very personal information. They are, however, given the option of excluding their name, e-mail, age, and place of residence. All information that is voluntarily submitted may be posted in these sections.

In the SeX-Files Surveys, personal information is collected, but anonymously. We do not ask for e-mail addresses or names of our participants. Queendom.com does collect and analyze the data from our surveys, but on an aggregated (collective) basis. Each time that we collect individual data from our visitors that may be posted on the site (as in the comments for the SeX-Files Survey), we clearly announce this possibility. Of course, all comments posted are anonymous.

On the Discussion Boards, visitors have to register in order to be able to post messages. Registration is required because we occasionally have problems with spammers and individuals who choose to verbally harass other members of the community. We closely monitor the discussion boards because we want Queendom to be a safe place where people can share and be honest about their problems. We promptly remove any inappropriate messages and ban the offender. The e-mail addresses are not visible to other users, and are not used in any way by Queendom. We encourage users to get a web-based e-mail account that they use for communication with strangers. Such an account can be obtained free of charge from HotMail or Yahoo.


Collection of test data for validation studies

We often gather data for further validatation of our tests. Visitors have the choice of allowing or not the use of their information for validation purposes. When conducting validation studies, we always use aggregated (collective) data, never individual identifiable information (name, place of residence, etc.).

Unless otherwise specified, no records of scored tests and quizzes are kept.


Collection of e-mail addresses
Queendom.com never sends unsolicited e-mails nor spam. Our mailing lists are never sold nor shared.

Mailing lists and newsletters. Queendom.com sends regular newsletters to subscribers to Queendom's Newsletter and My Profile members. Both of these lists are opt-in.

    Queendom's Newsletter. We never ask for your name, place of residence, age, gender, etc. We only require your e-mail address. Anyone can remove their address from the Queendom's Newsletter mailing list at http://www.queendom.com/about/mailinglists.html.

    My Profile Newsletter. My Profile subscribers have the option to receive the newsletter and other related information by making the appropriate selection in their Account Information. To change the options, log into My Profile, click on the Account Information in the side navigation, and go to Edit Profile. To delete their profile, members can go to http://www.queendom.com/myprofile/delete.html.

A Fine Kettle of Fish and Chaise Longue. Visitors have the option of including an e-mail address when submitting a story or question to the Fine Kettle of Fish or Chaise Longue. These e-mail addresses are not sold or stored in mailing lists, but they are sometimes posted on Queendom.com by request only (and after an e-mail to the individual to verify).

QuizCards. We do not capture or collect the e-mail addresses of any senders or receivers.


Advertisers and other sites

Queendom.Com works with a third party that serves ads to this site. Queendom.com does not control the cookies that may be assigned by advertisers or networks when you click on a banner.

Queendom.com is not responsible for the content or the privacy policies of any of the sites that we link to.
