    Privacy Policy

     

    Information about the Website

    The Qatar University website is managed by the Websites Unit in the External Relations Department's Communications and Marketing Section, with support from the Information Technology Services Department

     

    Scope

    This Privacy Policy applies to all sites on the Qatar University web domain “qu.edu.qa”. Since the QU web community consists of several websites, other websites may adopt different privacy and security statements as their specific needs require. The QU website, as well as other sites across campus, contain links to various external websites. The University is not responsible for the privacy and security practices or the content of external websites linked to.

     

    Providing Visitors with Anonymous Access

    You can access our website homepage and browse our site without disclosing your personal data. Your personal data are requested only if you want to access online services (e.g. QU email, myQU, or other registration services). We do not sell or provide any third party with any of the data you disclosed. 
    Users surfing and reading information on the website can expect QU, just as any other website, to collect and store the following anonymous information about your visit:

    • The operating system and browser your computer uses.

    • Any search engine you were using before arriving to the website.

    • The date and time of your visit.

    • The URLs of the pages you visit on the website.

    The Websites Unit only uses this anonymous data for statistical purposes, and will not provide it to any third party. We may keep more extensive information if we are concerned, for example, about security issues (attacks against the Website). If necessary, or following a request of the relevant public authority, we may then keep and give additional information to the relevant authority, as necessarywithin the legal framework of the law of Qatar.

     

    The Services and links of the Website

    The QU Home Website does not enable visitors to communicate with other visitors, or to post information to be accessed by others.

     

    Automatic Collection of Information

    We do not automatically log personal data, nor do we link information automatically logged by other means with personal data about specific individuals.

     

    Data Collection and Purpose Specification

    The Websites Unit will collect personal data that you may volunteer while using our survey services. We do not collect information about our visitors from other sources, such as public records or bodies, or private organizations.

     

    Disclosure and Visitor Choice

    We do not disclose your personal data to any other individual or organization.

     

    Privacy Support

    If you have an enquiry or concern about the Websites Unit privacy policy, please contact us on: webadmin@qu.edu.qa

By reading this document, users of the website acknowledge that the terms of this policy may change, that only the latest version will be applicable, and that this latest version will be available on the QU website.
