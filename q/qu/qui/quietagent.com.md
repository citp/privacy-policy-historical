# QuietAgent - Anonymously evaluating every job, every day for you.

### "We will never provide your information to any other party."

QuietAgent gives you total control over who has access to your information and guarantees your privacy until such time as you choose to release your private information to one or more specific Employers/Recruitment Agents. 

  * We will never provide, share or sell your information to any other party
  * You can block your current employer
  * We ensure nobody can see who you are
  * Your information cannot be accessed or used outside the QuietAgent network
  * You will never receive solicited or unsolicited messages from any third party
  * As a job seeker, we will not ask you for any credit card information
  * We do not store credit card information
  * We will not knowingly ask, store nor make available to any party, any information that may lead to discriminatory recruitment practices
