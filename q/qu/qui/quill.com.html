Quill Respects Your Privacy

Quill Corporation holds the privacy and security of our customers in the highest regard. The following information explains what kind of information is gathered on the Quill website and how the information is used.

No information that we gather is shared with or sold to other organizations for any purposes.

For privacy and security reasons, Quill does not permanently retain information provided by customers utilizing the Quill Web site. Any information provided by a customer on the Web site is used solely to fulfill a product order or catalog request. When you place an on-line order or request a catalog, the information is transmitted to our processing center. The information that you provide is temporarily stored in house (separate from the Internet) and is used exclusively to ensure fulfillment of your order or request.

We do not and will not further access any credit card information you provide, nor do we or will we use your information for any purpose beyond fulfilling your order.

Does Quill offer secure on-line ordering?
Yes, we do. Information included in your purchase such as your name, address and credit card number is encrypted for your protection. Quill uses the industry-standard, SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) technology to ensure that your information is encrypted. You must use a security-enabled browser (such as Microsoft Internet Explorer version 2.1 or greater, or Netscape Navigator version 2.0 or greater) to utilize the SSL feature.

What kind of information does Quill temporarily retain?
Our Web server does not recognize or retain the e-mail addresses of our visitors. We do, however, temporarily retain:

  • e-mail addresses of visitors who communicate with us via e-mail
  • information that is volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or contest entries.
  • aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit.

How does Quill use this information?

We use this information to:

  • improve the content of our website in an effort to better serve our customers and enhance their on-line experience;
  • notify consumers about updates to our Web site;
  • contact consumers for marketing purposes

Why do I receive mailings from Quill?

  • You have communicated with us via e-mail
  • You have ordered from Quill
  • You have subscribed to a Quill newsletter

How do I remove my name from Quill mailing lists?
If you do not wish to receive e-mail from us, please let us know by contacting us at http://www.quill.com. Please provide us with your exact name and address and e-mail address and notify us that you want your name removed. We will be sure your name is removed from the list.

If you supply us with your postal address on-line you may receive periodic mailings with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please contact us. Be sure to include your exact name and address and e-mail address, and we will remove your name from the list.
