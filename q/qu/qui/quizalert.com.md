# Privacy

## Privacy policy

**Personal data collected by Quizalert.com**

We collect only personal data that our users will accept them voluntarily. So, our users will allow to our website to collect and use information and personal data to operate and improve our services, to enable us to provide relevant results and to generate a custom image.

By using our website www.quizalert.com applications and tests the users will agree that we will take over and process the follow data:

  * User Name
  * Profile picture
  * Facebook picures
  * Gender
  * Date of birth
  * Email Address
  * Facebook ID
  * User Posts



Quizalert.com uses only this information to generate or process your application when the users will use the apps.

If you are not agree with these terms, please do not use our services.

For any concerns or questions regarding these terms please contact us at **contact@quizalert.com**.

If you do not want that your data we have specified to appear on our website please contact us at our **contact@quizalert.com**.

Thank you,
