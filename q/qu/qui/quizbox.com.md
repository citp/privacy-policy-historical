# Privacy Policy

### QuizBox Privacy Policy

  * Your privacy is protected at QuizBox.
  * You control your personal information.
  * You can end your membership at any time.
  * We will never disclose your personal information to any third party.
  * We do not send members unsolicited emails.

QuizBox is committed to protecting your online privacy. We are also committed to providing you with the very best experience we can. In order to enhance your experience at our site we gather certain information about you that helps us customize our content to your tastes and preferences. Please read the following policy to understand how your personal information will be treated as you make full use of our website. This policy may change from time to time so please check back periodically. 

### What information do we collect?

  * **PERSONALITY TESTS, RIDDLES AND PUZZLES**  
We do not collect answers to any of the [personality tests](https://web.archive.org/personality/), riddles, and [puzzles](https://web.archive.org/puzzles/) on this site for any purpose. All of your answers are **completely anonymous and private**. 
  * **OPTIONAL REGISTRATION**  
We offer free, voluntary registration to users. By registering, users gain access to the [Quiz Builder](https://web.archive.org/builder) area, and they can take advantage of storing their own quizzes on this site. During registration the user **is required** to supply an email address, their name, password of choice, and they must answer mandatory survey questions about their age and gender. 
  * **USAGE TRACKING**  
We use tracking information to determine which areas of our sites users like and don't like based on traffic to those areas. QuizBox does not track what individual users read, but rather how well each page performs overall. This helps us continue to build a better service for you. We also collect IP addresses and referring URLs for system administration. 



### What do we do with the information we collect?

QuizBox's primary goal in collecting personal information is to provide you, the user, with a customized experience on our website. Such information also enables us to better tailor our content to readers' needs and to help our advertisers better understand the demographics of our audience. Because QuizBox derives its revenue mainly from advertising, providing such aggregated demographic data is essential to keep our service free to users. 

### With whom do we share your information and for what purpose?

QuizBox does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with others. We use the above-described information to tailor our content to suit your needs and help our advertisers better understand our audience's demographics. This is essential to keep our service free. We do not share information about individual users with any third party. 

### External links

This site contains links to other sites. QuizBox is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

### Changes to our privacy policy

From time to time, we may change our Privacy Policy. We will post those changes on this page so that our users will always be aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we share the information with others. 

### Who to contact

Please visit our [contact information](https://web.archive.org/contact) page. If you're interesed, you may want to learn more [about us](https://web.archive.org/web/20060622061231id_/http%3A//www.quizbox.com/contact/about.aspx).
