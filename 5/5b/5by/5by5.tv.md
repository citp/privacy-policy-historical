# Privacy Policy | 5 by 5

* * *

We respect your privacy. This document describes the collection and use of personal information that we may collect about you.

You can access and browse our site without disclosing your personal data. We collect the personal data that you may volunteer while using our services. We do not collect information about our visitors from other sources, such as public records or bodies, or private organizations.

Certain pages on the site invite you to give us your e-mail address to enable us to contact you in the future, typically to solicit your feedback or to give you news.

We do not automatically log personal data nor do we link information automatically logged by other means with personal data about specific individuals. We do not use cookies to store personal data nor do we link non-personal information stored in cookies with personal data about specific individuals.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. We do not take specific steps to protect the privacy of children who disclose their personal data to us.

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/contact).
