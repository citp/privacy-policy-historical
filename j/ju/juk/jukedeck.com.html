Jukedeck Privacy Policy

This is the privacy policy for the Jukedeck application ("Application") and website ("Site"). Both the Application and Site are owned and operated by Jukedeck Ltd. ("we", "us", "our").

We aim to protect and respect your privacy. This policy, which should be read in conjunction with our Terms of Use, sets out how we will process any personal data you provide to us or we collect from you.

By using the Application or Site, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this privacy policy.

We might change this privacy policy every now and then, so come back every so often to see if there have been any changes. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Information we might collect from you

We may collect and process the following information about you:

  • information (such as your name and email address) that you provide by completing forms on the Application or Site, including if you register as a user of the App, subscribe to any service, upload or submit any material via the Application or Site, or request any information;
  • your preferences, which helps us provide you with more tailored music;
  • communications you send to us, for example to report a problem or to submit queries, concerns or comments regarding the Application or Site or their content;
  • information from surveys that we may, from time to time, run on the Application or Site for research purposes, if you choose to respond to them;
  • details of your visits to the Application and Site, including but not limited to the resources you access, the screens or pages you visit, the type of music you listen to and any data you download; and
  • your IP address and/or unique identifier linked to your mobile device.

Uses made of your information

We may use the information you provide to:

  • help us build a profile of your preferences so that we can tailor the music the Application generates to your preferences;
  • contact you by post, phone or email when necessary;
  • audit the downloading of data from the Application and Site;
  • improve the layout and/or content of the pages of the Application and Site and customise them for users;
  • improve the music the Application and Site generate;
  • identify visitors to the Application and Site;
  • carry out research on our users' demographics and tracking of usage data; and
  • disclose to lawful authorities when required to do so by law.

We may also use information you provide to us for the purposes of contacting you with information regarding upcoming products, services, events or surveys. This shall only be done with your consent and our direct marketing policy which is explained in full below.

Additional information

When you visit the Application or Site, we may automatically collect additional information about you, such as the way you use the Application or Site, the type of phone or operating system you use and, if you came to the website, the type of internet browser you use and the website from which you have come to the site. Also, depending on whether you access Jukedeck on your computer or via your mobile device, we will collect your IP address (the unique address which identifies your home computer on the internet) or the unique identifier (which is linked to your mobile device). You cannot be identified solely from this information and it is only used to assist us in providing an effective service on the Application and Site and to collect broad demographic information for aggregate use.

Information sharing

We may disclose aggregate statistics about visitors to the Application and Site, customers and sales in order to describe our services to prospective partners, advertisers, potential merchants and other reputable third parties and for other lawful purposes, but these statistics will include no personally identifiable information.

We may disclose your personal information to any of our affiliates, or to our agents or contractors who assist us in providing the services we offer through the Application and Site, fulfilling requests for information, receiving and sending communications, updating marketing lists, analysing data, providing support services or in other tasks, from time to time. Our agents and contractors will only use your information to the extent necessary to perform their functions.

In the event that we undergo re-organisation or are sold to a third party, you agree that any personal information we hold about you may be transferred to that re-organised entity or third party.

We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or if we believe that such action is necessary to prevent fraud or cyber crime or to protect the Application or Site or enforce the terms under which you transact with us or enforce the rights, property or personal safety of any person.

Links

The Application and Site may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or the content of such sites.

Marketing policy

We may send you information we think you may find useful or which you have requested from us by push notification and/or (if you provide us with your e-mail address) by email, including information about the latest updates to the Application and Site, provided you have indicated that you do not object to being contacted for these purposes. We will always give you the option to opt out of receiving further emails or push notifications by following the unsubscribe instructions on any communications sent to you.

Storage

Information that you submit via the Application or Site is sent to and stored on secure servers located in the United Kingdom and other European Economic Area countries. This is necessary in order to process the information. Information submitted by you may be transferred by us to our other offices and/or to the third parties mentioned in the circumstances described above, which may be situated outside the European Economic Area and may be processed by staff operating outside the European Economic Area. By submitting information via the Application or Site, you agree to this storing, processing and/or transfer.

Access to information

The Data Protection Act 1998 (the "Act") gives you the right to access information held about you. Your right of access can be exercised in accordance with the Act. Any access request may be subject to a nominal fee to meet our costs in providing you with details of the information we hold about you.

For the purpose of the Act, the data controller is: Jukedeck Ltd of TechHub @ Campus, 4-5 Bonhill Street, London EC2A 4BX.
