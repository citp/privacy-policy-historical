## Privacy Policy

Paste Interactive is a small, and honest company. We will do our level best to protect the privacy of your information using modern standards in technology. We won't sell or give away your email address, and we won't share any of the information you put into Jumpchart. 

The only information we collect about you is the standard statistical information that Google analytics compiles, and the information you voluntarily input. 

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us at [info@pasteinteractive.com](mailto:info@pasteinteractive.com) \- A real person will respond to your questions.
