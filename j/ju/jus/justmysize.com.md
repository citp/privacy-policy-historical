This Privacy Policy Statement sets forth the privacy policy pertaining to this Site, www.justmysize.com which is owned by Net Apparel, LLC ("Net Apparel"). By using this Site, you are agreeing to the terms set forth in this Privacy Policy Statement. For other terms and conditions that apply to your use of the Site, please review our [Legal Statement](http://www.justmysize.com/cgi-bin/command/ExecMacro/info.d2w/report?info=legal). 

For purposes of this Privacy Policy Statement, "Personal Information" means any information by which you can be personally identified, and includes your name, your address, your telephone number, your e-mail address and the like. Some Site pages may request Personal Information in order to help Net Apparel serve you more effectively. Net Apparel collects Personal Information only when you voluntarily submit such Personal Information to us, such as when placing a product order through a Site. 

For each visitor to the Site, our web servers automatically recognize information regarding the visitor's domain name (or that of the visitor's access provider). Regardless of the purpose for which it is submitted, it is Net Apparel's policy to maintain all Personal Information as confidential, and to use such Personal Information only for the following purposes: 

  * for the specific purpose for which it was volunteered (for example, to complete a product purchase etc.); 
  * to improve the content, appearance and utility of the Site; 
  * to track our visitors' use of the Site for internal market research; and 
  * to notify our visitors of updates to the Site. 



At any time, you may obtain a summary of the Personal Information on record with Net Apparel which has been collected about you via this Site. Except for Personal Information we need to complete your purchase of products, you may request at any time that Net Apparel dispose of any or all Personal Information about you. In addition, at any time you may request: (i) that Net Apparel correct any such Personal Information; or (ii) [that such Personal Information not be provided to third parties; or (iii)] that such Personal Information be removed from Net Apparel's marketing databases. To make any request described above, send an e-mail to [jms.manager@justmysize.com](mailto:jms.manager@justmysize.com?subject=Privacy), write to PO Box 748, Rural Hall, NC 27098 or call 800-261-5902. When contacting us, please be sure to provide us with your exact e-mail address, name, address and/or telephone number(s) in order to be sure we handle your request correctly. 

Net Apparel reserves the right, at its discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this Privacy Policy Statement at any time. You should check this page periodically for changes. Your continued use of this Site following the posting of changes to this Privacy Policy Statement will mean that you accept those changes. 

### **E-MAIL POLICY**

Net Apparel collects and retains only those e-mail addresses that have been voluntarily submitted to us by consumers. We use these e-mail addresses to communicate with our customers about (a) orders they have placed; (b) events & promotions; and/or (c) changes to our websites. 

If you do not wish to receive e-mail messages from Net Apparel, you may easily unsubscribe from our list via one of the following options: (a) The next time you receive an e-mail message from us, press "Reply." Type "unsubscribe" in your subject line, then send your reply. (b) Compose a brief e-mail asking to unsubscribe from our list. Send it to [jms.manager@justmysize.com.](mailto:jms.manager@justmysize.com?subject=unsubscribe) (c) Write to PO Box 748, Rural Hall, NC 27098. Or (d) call 1-800-261-5902 toll free. 

For options b, c, and d, be sure to provide your exact e-mail address, name, street address, and/or telephone number(s), so that we can handle your request correctly. As soon as we process your request, we will unsubscribe you from our e-mail list.
