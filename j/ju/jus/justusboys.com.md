# JustUsBoys.com Privacy Policy"

**Your Personal Information is Safe**  
We support the rights of adults to explore entertainment and sexuality on the Internet in a private and secure environment. As a visitor of JustUsBoys.com, you will have peace-of-mind knowing that any information you share privately with us will be kept strictly confidential. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, e-mail address, address, phone number, or personal information regarding the use of this site. If information is collected from you, the information will only be used to provide the products and services you've requested, to provide customer service, or to share news about our service offerings.

Your e-mail address and personal information will never be sold to 3rd parties for marketing purposes, and will only be transferred to other entities should the JustUsBoys.com website change ownership. If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us via our on-site contact form located [here](http://www.jubsupport.com/).

Please note that any information you publicly reveal on your personal profile page, personal blog, personal gallery, in our community message board forum, or in public chat does not fall under the above policy. This policy applies only to personally identifiable information you share privately with the operators of JustUsBoys.com.  
**  
Cookie Policy**  
We use cookies to help remember you as you surf JustUsBoys.com. These cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information and help enhance your usage of the site. Without them, you may not be able to fully enjoy many aspects of the site such as staying logged in while using the message boards.
