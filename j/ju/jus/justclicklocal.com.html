Information you give us

The use of certain features and services on the JustClickLocal may require that you register and supply personally identifiable information (ex. business listings) such as your name, email address, telephone number and other information, which is stored by JustClickLocal.com. When you register as a member, we may also collect additional information from you in order to provide you with those specific services and to personalize your experience on the JustClickLocal. This information is strictly optional. JustClickLocal does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, with your permission.

Anti-spam policy

JustClickLocal.com is opposed to unsolicited bulk email aka spam. We do not send emails to anyone without permission and we do not sell or provide email addresses to any unauthorized third party. We hate spam as much as you do. While we cannot prevent spam, if you think you have received an unsolicited email from us, please contact our Privacy Department and we will investigate.

Cookies

JustClickLocal.com uses "cookies" to automatically recognize returning users. A cookie is a small file on your hard drive that allows us to display your site preferences and search criteria on JustClickLocal.Cookies used by JustClickLocal may contain your user name, email address, IP address or zip code. Your internet service provider (ISP) assigns an internet protocol address (IP Address) to your computer when you use the Internet. We collect each IP address that accesses JustClickLocal, computer and connection information such as browser type and version, operating system, and platform; your behavior on JustClickLocal and related services, including the products and merchants you view or searched for, as well as the URL you come from and go to next and cookie number. We use this information, as well as other data in our databases, to analyze site and user behavior, troubleshoot technical problems, address complaints prepare aggregated reports that we share with others and customize your experience on JustClickLocal.com. You can block cookies by changing the settings on your browser (contact your network administrator for help), but doing so may prevent us from delivering certain services to you (ex. wish lists).
