# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy tells you how We at JustDeals.com collect information when you visit our website, or if you purchase a product from us.

At JustDeals.com we know the importance of your personal information. We ensure that we comply with this obligation, to you our valued customers.

When you submit information to Justdeals.com by registering as a member, purchasing one of our products or by sending us an email, we collect that information and use it for the purposes of providing you with information or advice about our goods and/or services and for processing an order by you for our goods and/or services.

Specifically, we require your name, street address, email address, phone and fax number and method of payment details in order to complete an order for our goods and/or services.

At times Justdeals.com may email you special promotions, but you will always have the option to opt out of receiving these emails.

We shall only disclose your personal information to third parties where the disclosure is necessary and directly related to the purposes set out above - such as our financial institution to process your order. **We will not sell or give our customers name to any third-party.**
