# Privacy Policy | Jesus Loves You

## Privacy Policy

Here at Jesus Loves You, we value your privacy. We do not wish to send emails, make Facebook posts or share our content with anybody who does not wish to receive it. If you need help being removed from any communication channel, please email us at a href=”mailto:info@jesus-loves-you.org?Subject=Content%20removal”>info@Jesus-Loves-You.org. Please read below for more details.

www.Jesus-Loves-You.org is a blog-format website which posts inspirational articles. Some of the content (videos or images for example), may be embedded from a 3rd-party source like YouTube but our commentary on these articles is unique. We aim to give full credit for resources used and will always comply with any and all copyright laws. If you feel a piece of content has been used without permission, with incorrect credit, or we are using your open-source content, please contact us at and we will correct this matter.

## Email List

If you ever wish to unsubscribe from our email list, please click the “unsubscribe” link at the bottom of any of our emails. Follow that link and you will be given steps to remove your email address from our list. If, after following this action, you continue to receive emails from Jesus Loves You, please contact us at info@Jesus-Loves-You.org, we will gladly help you.

## Facebook Fan Page

Jesus Loves You maintains a Facebook Fan page @ [www.facebook.com/JesusLovesYou](http://www.facebook.com/JesusLovesYou). If you want to remove yourself from the page or wish to quit receiving our inspirational posts, simply visit the page, mouse over the button that reads “Liked” at the top of the page, and scroll down to “Unlike.”

For instructions written by Facebook about how to ‘Unlike’ a page, and how to stop receiving posts from a page on your Facebook wall, click [HERE](http://www.facebook.com/help/?faq=226926007324633).

## Advertising & Cookies

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on www.Jesus-Loves-You.org. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to www.Jesus-Loves-You.org and other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – <http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html>.

## More About Google Adsense

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on www.Jesus-Loves-You.org send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

Jesus Loves You has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

Users may opt out of the use of the Google cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – <http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html>.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
