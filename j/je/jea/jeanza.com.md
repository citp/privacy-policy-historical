# British Columbia, Canada - Jeanza Free Local Classifieds

**Jeanza Privacy Policy**

Jeanza has established this privacy policy to explain to you how your information is protected, collected and used, which may be updated from time to time. This policy is effective May 19, 2008. 

**Data Collection.** Information posted on Jeanza is publicly available. We collect your email address, for purposes such as sending self-publishing and confirmation emails, authenticating user accounts, etc. 

**Using Information from Jeanza.** We may collect and store personal or other information that you voluntarily supply to us online while using Jeanza. We never ask for your private information such as your billing information or credit card in an email or on the phone. If you receive an email or phone call requesting you provide personal information do not respond! Jeanza will NEVER solicit your account information in an email or on the phone. Jeanza does not offer any transaction or payment services. Avoid fraud by meeting all sellers in-person to pay for items. Keep in mind that whenever you voluntarily make your personal information available for viewing by third parties online - for example through email - that information can be seen, collected and used by others besides us. We cannot be responsible for any unauthorized third-party use of such information. 

**Your privacy.** We don't share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. We don't engage in cross-marketing or link-referral programs with other sites. 

**Credit Cards.** If you decide to add additional features to your ad such as Featured Ad or Featured Event you will be able to enter a credit card for billing convenience through our credit card partner PayPal. In order for the payment process to work propery we must confirm that sign-up information is valid. Entering your credit card information on Jeanza.com is safe; we do not store any credit card information in our system or database and paypal uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect sensitive data transmissions. 

If needed, you can contact Jeanza.com or our Security Officer at: via email: support -at- jeanza -dot- com
