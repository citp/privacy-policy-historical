**Information Collected from You**

The majority of this site is offered without collecting any personally identifiable information from our visitors. We collect personally identifiable information, such as name, address and e-mail, demographic information (age, gender, etc.), only when you voluntarily submit it to enter contests, to request information or to place an order through our online store. We may use your personal information to respond to a request for information or to award prizes or samples. 

We also maintain your account information when you create a personal account in order to buy through our online store. We maintain a record of any orders you place through our online store, including shipping addresses and other necessary information to process your transactions. We may collect the name and address of a gift recipient processed for an on-line purchase. Gift recipient names and addresses are only used to deliver the product and their names and addresses are not retained for any other purpose.

We may contact you with special offers from time to time, but only when you have given us permission to do so. You may decline to receive these offers by checking the appropriate box after entering any personal information. You may also change your mind and decline to receive special online offers by contacting us at our [Consumer Affairs Department](http://mycusthelp.com/JELLYBELLY/supportcontact.asp).

We also collect non-personal information (age and gender) to learn more about areas of the site that are of the most interest to visitors, or to learn who visits the site. We may on occasion post non-personally identified messages we receive from visitors to our sites, such as comments about our candy and company.

**A Note to Parents**

Jelly Belly is committed to protecting the on-line privacy of children. While this site is operated for the enjoyment of general audiences, we are mindful children may also visit this site. Children are not required to provide any personal information to use any part of this site. We also do not collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Personal information that a child under 13 years of age provides to us is only available to those who work with the site or to provide necessary technical or other fulfillment services for the site. We collect necessary data, which may include e-mail address, street address and telephone, in order to respond to requests for information for school projects, answer questions about the site or our candy. The child's information is immediately deleted from our system upon responding to the child's request. 

Children are not allowed to make a purchase from our on-line store. We require a credit card to make a purchase and no one under 18 years of age may buy online.

If you have questions or concerns about the Internet and privacy for your child, we encourage you to check out [http://www.ftc.gov/kidzprivacy](http://www.ftc.gov/kidzprivacy).

**About Our Gift Message Cards**

When you place an order with us online, you will notice that during the checkout process you may add a message, which we will print onto a gift message card for delivery with the product. We want to let you know that Gift Messages are strictly confidential and will be transmitted to the recipient without review or edit by Jelly Belly Candy Company. If you send a Gift Message with your shipment, we will not tinker with it in any way. If you receive a Gift Card from someone, we have not looked at it. Enjoy your gift giving!

**What About Security?**

Maintaining security of credit card transactions and other personally identifiable information has our highest priority. We follow strict security procedures for safeguarding information and preventing unauthorized access. Transactions sent between your server and Jelly Belly data center travels securely through the Internet using a highly secure technique based on Secure/Multipurpose Internet Mail Extensions (S/MIME). 

We protect information in our high-security data center and adhere to strict computer security standards. We have put in place privacy protection control systems designed to ensure that information about you remains safe and secure. Only authorized Jelly Belly Candy Company employees are permitted to have access to this information and such access is limited by need. All Jelly Belly Candy Company employees are bound by our privacy policy. 

We do not maintain any record of your credit card number, and your credit card number is deleted each time from our system after processing your order.

**The Use of Cookies**

This Web site uses cookies that send information to your computer while you are viewing this site. Cookies are unique to your computer, and allow the Web server to recall information that will make your use of the site easier and more convenient. Cookies add convenience that allow you to save time when you return to this site. They can only be read by a Web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer.

We use cookies to obtain non-personal information from on-line visitors. Cookies track the type of browser, operating system, and Internet service provider and allow us to tabulate the total number of visitors to our site. 

You may disable the cookie feature on your computer or set it to alert you when cookies are being sent by using the preferences on your browser. If you choose to disable cookies, however, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of Jellybelly.com or the other Web sites you visit.
