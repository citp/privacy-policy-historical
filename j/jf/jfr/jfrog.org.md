# Privacy Policy - Managing your binaries

**JFrog Ltd.**  
**Privacy Policy**

Last Revised: June 11, 2012  
JFrog Ltd. and its affiliates (“JFrog” or ““Company” or “we”) respect the privacy of its users (“User” or “you”) and are committed to protect the User’s information. We believe that you have a right to know our practices regarding the information we may collect and use when you visit or use our proprietary online website available at [www.jfrog.com] (the “Site”), our proprietary Artifactory Pro software (the “Software”) which provides you with a comprehensive open-source Repository Management Solution and related services (the “Services”). The Site, Software and the Services shall be referred to, collectively and severally as the “Repository Solution”).  
Your consent (important, please read carefully!)

By accessing and/or using the Repository Solution and/or any part thereof, you agree to the terms and conditions set forth in this Privacy Policy, including to the collection and processing of your Personal Information (as defined below).  
PLEASE NOTE: IF YOU DISAGREE TO ANY TERM PROVIDED HEREIN, YOU MAY NOT ACCESS OR USE JFROG’S REPOSITORY SOLUTION.  
What information we may collect from our Users?

We collect two types of data and information:  
Non-personal Information. The first type is non-identifiable and anonymous information. We do not record the identity of the User from which we have collected this Information. Non-personal Information which is being gathered consists of technical information and behavioral information, as in the examples below:

Technical information collected from your use of the Site:

Type of operating system (e.g. Windows, Linux, etc.)  
Type of Browser (e.g. Explorer, Firefox, Chrome, Safari, etc.)  
Screen resolution (e.g. 800×600, 1024×768, etc.)  
Browser and keyboard language (e.g. English)  
IP address.  
Flash version.  
Technical information collected from your use of the Software:

Type of build technologies  
Type of build server  
The number of servers you are using  
Background user information  
Public comments provided by you  
Personal Information. The other type of information we may collect is individually identifiable information. This information may be of private and/or sensitive nature such as name and address. Personal Information which is being gathered consists of any personal details provided voluntarily by the User during registration and download of the Software though the Site

How do we collect information on our Users?

We collect information while you access or use the Site, while completing the online forms for registering or downloading JFrog products or contacting JFrog team.  
What are the purposes of the collection of information?

Non-personal Information is collected in order to:

Enhance the User’s experience on the Site and/or with JFrog products and services (e.g. determining the screen resolution).  
Learn about the preferences of Users and general trends on JFrog products and community (e.g. understand which features are more popular than others).  
Personal Information is collected in order to:

Enable and personalize the overall operation of JFrog products on your system.  
Identify authors and their comments on a particular Article.  
Identify you when conducting customer service operations (such as providing support, feedback, etc.).  
Allow you to register for special offers, free evaluation and trial periods of JFrog products and professional services.  
Sharing Personal Information with Third Parties

We share Personal Information only in the following cases:  
(a) to satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process, subpoena or governmental request; (b) to enforce these Privacy Policy or the End User License Agreement, including investigation of potential violations thereof; (c) to detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues; (d) to respond to claims that any content published on the Repository Solution violates any right or endangers the safety of any third-party; (e) to protect the rights, property, or personal safety of the Company or its affiliates and agents; (f) when Company is undergoing any change in control, including by means of merger, acquisition or purchase of all or substantially all of the assets of Company; (g) subject to your approval, in order to supply certain services you have requested from JFrog, if and as made available by the Company; and/or (h) for storage though customary services.  
Modifications to or Deletion of Personal Information

If for any reason you wish to modify or delete your Personal Information, you may contact us via e-mail to the following address info@jfrog.com. We shall remove Personal Information from its servers reasonably after removing the Software from your servers and/or systems.  
Cookies & Local Storage

When you access or use the Site, JFrog may use industry-wide technologies such as “cookies” and Flash (or similar technologies), which stores certain information on your computer (“Local Storage”) and which will allow us to enable automatic activation of certain features, and to make your Repository Solution experience much more convenient and effortless. The cookies used are created per session, does not include any information about you, other than your session key and are removed as your session ends (usually after 24 hours). It is easy to prohibit the Local Storage. Most browsers will allow you to erase cookies from your computer’s hard drive, block acceptance of cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. In order to erase or disable the Local Storage option in Flash you should use the settings option of Flash according to the specific instructions provided by the technology provider. However, if you block or erase cookies, or change the settings of Flash, your online experience may be limited.  
Security

We take a great care in maintaining the security of the Repository Solution and your information and in preventing unauthorized access to it through industry standard technologies and internal procedures, including through the use of tokens and encryption mechanisms. However, we do not guarantee that unauthorized access will never occur.  
Third Party Websites

While using the JFrog products and services through the Site you may encounter links to third party websites. Please be advised that such third party websites are independent from JFrog, and we assume no responsibility or liability whatsoever with regard to privacy matters or any other legal matter with respect to such third party websites. We encourage you to carefully read the privacy policies and the terms of use or service of such websites.  
Changes to the Privacy Policy

The terms of this Privacy Policy will govern the use of the Repository Solution and any information collected therein. Company reserves the right to change this policy at any time, so please re-visit this page frequently. In case of any material change, we will make reasonable efforts to post a clear notice on the Repository Solution.  
Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective as of the stated “Last Revised” and your continued use of the Repository Solution on or the Last Revised date will constitute acceptance of, and agreement to be bound by, those changes.

Got any Questions?

If you have any questions (or comments) concerning this Privacy Policy, you are most welcomed to send us an email to the following address: info@jfrog.com and we will make an effort to reply within a reasonable time-frame.
