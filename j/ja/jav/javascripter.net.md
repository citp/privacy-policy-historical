# JavaScripter.net: Privacy

### JavaScripter.net Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This website does not collect personally identifying information that might result in compromising the privacy of visitors. Information collected by the Web server in its log files is used solely to determine the aggregate statistical characteristics of the traffic, such as the total or average numbers of requests, files served, and sessions accomplished, as well as the relative popularity of specific pages on our site. Such information is not disclosed to any third parties, except in the form of aggregate estimates of traffic provided to our advertisers. 

If you set your Web browser to accept _cookies_ , the following information applies to you. When you visit our website, we use _third-party advertising companies_ to serve ads, such as the ad displayed at the top of this page. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. For additional information about third-party advertising and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please visit: 

* [Google on Advertising and Privacy](http://www.google.com/privacy/ads/)
* [Google on Transparency and Choice](http://www.google.com/intl/en/privacy/)
