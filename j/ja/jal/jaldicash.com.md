Weizmann Impex Service Enterprise Privacy Principles:
Our core businesses include technology enabled services i.e. “Jaldi Cash” a unique payment
instrument available for Indian consumer, which is safe and convenient to transact. It is a smart
option for all, and the safest online and on–mobile transaction instrument. Jaldi Cash may be
used for transferring funds to Banks accounts, other Jaldi Cash Card (instrument holders),
making payment of utility bills & essential services. Jaldi Cash is a full usage product which
empowers you to do online transactions at any time and from anywhere or through any of our
authorized Franchisee / Retail Outlets. Through our portfolio of varied online services, spanning
from Travel booking to Insurance, from Utility Bill Payments to Mobile Top-ups, we plan to add
new dimensions to the business of retailing. These are confidential matters and Jaldi Cash and
its affiliates recognize your expectations regarding privacy policy of WISE. WISE is committed to
keep your information confidential and secure and that is why we have designed procedures to
ensure your privacy in all facets of our relationship. No matter if you are our Merchant,
business partner, franchisee / retailer, backend user, a consumer, and a customer you are
protected, not only by the applicable privacy laws, but, more importantly, commitment of WISE
to safeguard your privacy and your right to decide how your personal information will be used.

What Personally Identifiable Information is collected by Jaldi Cash?
Jaldi Cash collects and stores information collected from our customers, franchises and end
users who use the Jaldi Cash Services, its payment gateway services, as well as Merchants,
business partners, that we have tied-up and acquired in the course of our business and some
visitor information regarding usage of this website, such as date and time of day you access our
website, browser type, browser language, the Internet Protocol (IP) address of the computer
you are using, number of hits, the pages visited, previous/subsequent sites visited and length of
user session. WISE does not collect any unique information about you (such as your name, e-
mail address, etc.) except when you specifically and know WISE only provide such information.

used to access the services of WISE.

How WISE uses and share Personally-Identifiable Information:
At our website, we will use your personal details and information we obtain from other sources

link to a wide variety of other sites/links. We are not responsible for their privacy policies or
how they treat information about their users. We may disclose personal data in order to
comply with a legal or regulatory obligation.
If you (i.e. our Merchant, business partner, franchises, aggregators) would like to view the
information we have collected from you and your customers or you want to correct your
