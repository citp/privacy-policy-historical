Shree Bindu Sewa Sansthan (India) and Swami Balendu e.V. (Germany) are non-profit charitable organizations. All donations made are free of tax and thus fully support these charitable organizations.

The Jaisiyaram Trading Company is our partner company and all profits made are donated to Shree Bindu Sewa Sansthan for the support of their charity projects.

Our sites collect no personally identifying information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals. We do record information such as time of day, browser type, browser language, and IP address when you interact with the site. Jaisiyaram.com uses this information to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our web site. We also use this information to verify the number of discrete times you visit this web site each day.

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who subscribe to our newsletter, request any service and order our products. By filling a form on Jaisiyaram.com the subscriber agrees on receiving emails from Jaisiyaram.com. The subscriber's data is stored in our secured server and protected with up to 256-bit Secure Sockets Layer encryption. Jaisiyaram.com will contact the subscribers with mails regarding the requested service and order and promotional mails for programs and charity projects. The provided information is kept confidential and is not shared with or sold to any other company or organization. By filling any form on the JAISIYARAM.COM website in order to attend any of the programs, to invite for programs or to use any service, paid or free (for example distance healing), you give JAISIYARAM.COM the permission to display your picture, location and name which was provided on our website and across the internet. By filling a form you agree and confirm that you do not have an objection to this display. Following our privacy policy, your email address will not be shared or displayed in any case. If you do not want this, you are always free to unsubscribe or to ask us at [ info@jaisiyaram.com](mailto:info@jaisiyaram.com) to remove you from our list of using our services. All emails contain an [unsubscribe link ](https://web.archive.org/web/20110228025156id_/http%3A//www.jaisiyaram.com/unsubscribe.htm)which can be used to opt-out or you [click here to unsubscribe](https://web.archive.org/web/20110228025156id_/http%3A//www.jaisiyaram.com/unsubscribe.htm). Our web site contains links, including e-commerce opportunities with affiliates, partners and associates, to other sites that are not maintained by us. If you visit these sites, please look for their privacy policy.
