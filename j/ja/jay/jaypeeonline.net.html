Automatically-Recorded Information

Cookie/Tracking Technology
JaypeeOnline uses cookies to save an encrypted version of your account password so you won’t have to re-enter it as you surf through the Site. Your login cookie will expire immediately upon clicking the logout link.
Advertisers

We use various online advertising services to display ads on the Site. These ads may contain cookies. While cookies are used in other parts of the Site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by the ad company concerned, and we do not have access to this information.

Currently, we serve advertisements from the following:

* Google.com
* Amazon.com
* Commission Junction

Third parties such as our advertisers may also use cookies to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and other sites on the internet. If you want to opt out of our third-party advertiser (Google Adsense)’s use of cookies, please visit the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

Registration Forms
The Site’s registration forms require users to provide basic contact information including names and email addresses. General demographic information including zip codes, age, etc. may also be requested. This information is not verified and may be changed by site users at any time.

Email addresses provided with the Site’s registration forms are used to get in touch with registered users when necessary to remind them of their password, lapses in login or pending account termination. Your email address will never be used for any other purpose nor will it be divulged to any third party.

Demographic and profile data may also be collected at the Site. This information is shared with advertisers only on an aggregate basis and will never be used in any other fashion.

Online Applications

Public Forums
JaypeeOnline allows registered and anonymous users to participate in discussions within the Site, especially via the blog commenting system. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in this and other public areas, including but not limited to message forums, become public information. You should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. We may not be held liable for any harm resulting in your own disclosure of your personal information via the Site.

Security
The Site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of information under our control. While measures are in place, please be aware that nothing is 100% secure and we do not claim it to be so.

Data Quality/Access
To change or modify any personal account information provided during site registration, you may visit the following URL: http://jaypeeonline.net/wp-admin/profile.php.

Contacting Us
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, our practices, or regarding your activities within this Web site, you can contact us via the Contact page.
