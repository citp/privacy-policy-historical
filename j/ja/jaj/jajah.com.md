All capitalized terms and definitions in this Privacy Policy shall have the same meaning ascribed to them in the License Agreement, as applicable. 

As described below, some of your personal information is disclosed to third parties and used by JAJAH. By accepting the License Agreement, You consent to JAJAH’s use and disclosure of your personally identifiable information in accordance with the terms set forth herein. 

JAJAH participates in the Safe Harbor program developed by the U.S. Department of Commerce and the European Union. To view our certification, visit the U.S. Department of Commerce's Safe Harbor Web site http://www.export.gov/safeharbor. As part of its participation in the EU-U.S. Safe Harbor, JAJAH adheres to the Safe Harbor Privacy Principles of Notice, Choice, Onward Transfer, Security, Data Integrity, Access and Enforcement with respect to personal data transferred to the United States by JAJAH or its partners and service providers. 

**Information that JAJAH Collects**

Registration Data. JAJAH requires that You fill in the information marked as mandatory on the Registration Form at (the "Registration Data"). Such form may be updated by You from time to time. Automatic Tracking. By Using JAJAH’s site, JAJAH is able to and may track certain information about You and your correspondence on the JAJAH site in order to perform internal research on its Users' demographics, interests, and behavior, as well as to monitor and enforce the terms of the License Agreement. This information may include the URL from which You arrived, the next URL You may visit, what browser You are using and your IP address. Collecting Information. Information collected about You will be kept in a specific file for You. JAJAH may collect information about any activity being performed by You while using the Product, including any correspondence between you and other users of the JAJAH site, messages left by You on any board or any searches and requests performed by You. 

**Children's Information**

JAJAH's policy is that it does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from subscribers under thirteen years of age. The Product is not directed or intended for children under thirteen years of age. If you are under thirteen years of age, you should not register to become a User or provide personally identifiable information on the side. If you are the parent or guardian of a User under the age of thirteen whom you believe has disclosed personally identifiable information to JAJAH, please contact JAJAH at support@jajah.com so that we may delete and remove such User's information from our system. 

**Cookies at JAJAH**

You acknowledge and agree that JAJAH may use cookies on its site. A cookie is a piece of text that can be entered into the memory of your hard drive and can store information about your use of JAJAH’s Product (the "Cookies"). The Cookies will help JAJAH make its services more convenient, personalized and more secure. In addition, Cookies may allow JAJAH to keep track and retrieve information about any User who acts in contradiction to JAJAH’s Privacy Policy and its License Agreement. If You would rather not have Cookies on your computer, You can usually set your browser to deny some but not all Cookies. You can also delete Cookies from your computer at your sole discretion. 

**JAJAH’s Usage of the Registration Data**

Internal Use. JAJAH uses the Registration Data about You for internal purposes, to improve its site and to better tailor the site to meet Your needs. In addition, JAJAH uses the Registration Data to analyze site usage and to deliver to You information about certain promotions, services or items that might interest You. In addition, JAJAH may use your e-mail address and, in rare situations your mailing address or phone number, to contact You regarding site notices from time to time. Editing Registration Data. You may review and edit your Registration Data by using your e-mail address or User ID and Password. If You cancel your account, You may also have your Registration Data deleted from JAJAH’s database by sending your request to support@jajah.com. However, You should be aware that once posted, You cannot change or remove any public postings made in JAJAH’s web site. You should also be aware that even if you delete your Registration Data, there may remain residual information that will remain within JAJAH's databases, access logs and other records, which may or may not contain personally identifiable information. Resolving Problems. JAJAH may use the Registration Data and other data it obtains from your current and past activities, to resolve disputes and troubleshoot problems and to monitor and enforce the License Agreement. In the event that You breach any of the terms of the License Agreement, JAJAH shall be entitled to use the Registration Data to locate You and pursue any necessary actions at its sole and absolute discretion in accordance with its Privacy Policy and the License Agreement. 

**JAJAH Disclosure of Users’ Registration Data General Rule.**

JAJAH will not disclose, sell or transfer any of your Registration Data, to any third parties (e.g., advertisers or business partners), except in the following cases: (i) JAJAH receives your permission; (ii) if JAJAH believes in good faith that the law requires such disclosure or transfer or (iii) as set forth below. JAJAH Community. You should be aware that in order to enjoy the Product, You will be required to disclose your e-mail address. As a result, other users of the JAJAH site as well as third parties might send You unsolicited e-mail. Third Parties. JAJAH may at any time add business partners or co-branded sites to its site, but the sharing of information with them will be limited to only statistical information regarding Users in general, and JAJAH will not disclose any of your Registration Data, correspondence or any related information. JAJAH assures You that in the event that JAJAH shall wish to transfer any of such information, JAJAH shall notify You before transferring such information, so that You will always have a chance to object to such transfer. Special Cases. JAJAH may also disclose Registration Data or other information, in special cases when it has reason to believe that disclosing such information is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be violating JAJAH’s  License Agreement or this Privacy Policy or may be causing injury to or interference with (either intentionally or unintentionally), JAJAH’s rights or property, other users, or anyone else that could be harmed by such activities. In addition, JAJAH may disclose or access a User's account, in the event that JAJAH believes in good faith that the law requires such disclosure. You thus authorize JAJAH to disclose any information about You to law enforcement or other government officials if JAJAH, in its sole and absolute discretion, believes that such disclosure is necessary or appropriate. Although, JAJAH uses industry standard practices to protect your privacy, JAJAH does not ensure, and You should not expect, that your Registration Data or any private communications will always remain private.

JAJAH and its Users do not tolerate spam.
