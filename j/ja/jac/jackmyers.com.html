All changes to our privacy policy will be posted here so that you are aware of our current practices.

Information

MediaVillage collects personal information when you register with MediaVillage and use our products or services. When you register we ask for information such as your name, contact information, email address, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests.
