# Privacy Policy

Jackpot fully supports your right to privacy, and we help you maintain control over your personal data on the internet. To make your online shopping experience as efficient and enjoyable as possible, we collect the following information:

If you register for our casino newsletter, we collect your email address.

Our site uses cookies, which are small text strings used for identification. For more information, see below.

Any information we gather is kept confidential and will not be disclosed to any outside party without your consent. We use collected information to provide you with an optimised level of support, through analysis of web site usage to market research and promotions. Of course, with the Data Protection Act of 1998, you may request details of any personally identifiable information we store for a small fee.

## Cookies

A cookie is a small string of text stored in your web browser that allows a web site to respond to you as an individual and allows us to tailor content to your specific needs. Cookies do not give us access to your computer or enable us to gain any information about you, other than the data you have shared with us. Jackpot employs click-stream tracking as provided by Open Tracker.
