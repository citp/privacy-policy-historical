# Jack Wills | University Outfitters

## Privacy Policy

Jack Wills Limited collects customer information to communicate with our customers about our brand, products, services and promotions. We collect information including your name, e-mail and postal address that you provide to us when you register. We also monitor site use and traffic patterns to improve our web site design and the products and services we offer. Jack Wills Limited recognizes that it must maintain and use customer information responsibly.

Jack Wills Limited utilizes the latest in secure server software (SSL) to ensure that all personal information, including credit card details, remains private and secure. Jack Wills Limited will not rent or sell your e-mail address or personal details for any reason. We treat customer confidentiality with the highest priority.

## Feefo

As a service to our customers, Jack Wills employs Feefo Ltd. to ask for independent feedback on our and your behalf. For this purpose we send Feefo the minimum information required. Feefo maintains the same standards of data security as Jack Wills, and is bound to keep your data confidential, and only use it for the purpose we designate.

## Data Collection

By registering your information with us, you consent to Jack Wills Limited sending correspondence regarding the website and our stores to the e-mail address that you have registered. If you have registered to receive email newsletters from us, you can remove your email address from our list at any time by using the links in the emails we send you to log onto your account to change your preferences. Alternatively, you may e-mail us at [removeme@jackwills.com](mailto:removeme@jackwills.com). Please include your e-mail address along with your full name and we will remove you from our mailing list. You may also call us Toll Free on 1 866 280 9963.

Jack Wills complies with the standards, procedures and requirements detailed in the Data Protection Act.

## Cookies

A cookie is a small data file that is temporarily stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. The Jack Wills website uses a number of cookies to operate. The website uses cookies to help Jack Wills gather information on website usage so we can offer you a better service and an improved browsing and shopping experience.

We collect personal information from you every time you email or text us, use the chatroom or buy any of our products. We also use 'cookies' to identify you when you visit our website which helps us to build a profile of you and to offer you a better service.
