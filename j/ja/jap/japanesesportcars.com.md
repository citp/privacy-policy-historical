# Privacy | JapaneseSportCars.com

We are glad to welcome you to [JapaneseSportCars.com](http://www.japanesesportcars.com/). Please read this privacy statement to ensure that we are committed to keeping secure the privacy of our members’ details.

**What information do we collect?**  
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/).

Since Japanese Sport Cars is a Community that’s providing you a place to store your information and to connect with other members, we may require from you some information that may be considered as personally identifiable.

  * nickname
  * real name
  * password
  * e-mail address
