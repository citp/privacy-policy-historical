    When you make a purchase from janeiredale.com, we will collect the personal information needed to process and ship your order, including your name, phone number, e-mail address, shipping and billing addresses, and credit card number. We will use that information to process your order and to provide you with customer service.

(Please refer to Section 6 of our Terms and Conditions of Website Use for a fuller description of how we may use content that you submit to us.)

    In addition to the uses described above, we may use personal information to help us assess and improve our products and to notify you about products and services, sales and special events in which you might be interested. We may also internally use your personal information to improve the content and layout of our website and for our own marketing efforts.

In addition, if you have registered, you may log into your account at any time to opt in or opt out of our mailing list. You may opt out of our mailing list by using the supplied link in any of our e-mails.

    7. Cookies and Other Technology

    Our website uses cookies and web beacons (also known as clear GIF technology or action tags) to speed your navigation of the website, recognize you, and track your website usage. A cookie is a small data file that a site can send to your web browser, which may then be stored on your computers hard drive so that your computer is recognized when you return to the site. If you have registered with our website, these cookies may (a) let us know who you are, (b) be needed to enable you to access your account or other information, and (c) provide us with information that we may use to customize our website in accordance with your needs and preferences. Most Internet browsers are initially set to accept cookies. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, and you can set your browser to reject cookies. If you elect not to accept cookies from our website, it may limit the options that our website provides for you.

    Web beacons help to deliver cookies. They are used to help us determines whether and how many times a web page on our website has been viewed.

    Like most standard website servers, we use log files. These include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP) addresses, referring/exit pages, platform type records, date/time stamp, and number-of-click tallies. We and third party vendors we work with may use this information to analyze trends, administer the website, track users movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses, etc. are not linked to your personal information. Site visit information is used only in aggregate form to help enhance the overall user experience.

These companies may employ cookies and web beacons to collect data relating to your visit to our website in order to measure advertising effectiveness (such as which web pages are visited or what products are purchased and in what amount). The data these companies collect may be combined with data from other websites, mobile applications and sources. Any information that these third parties collect via cookies and web beacons is not linked to any personal information about you collected by us.
