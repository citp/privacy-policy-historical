Hanesbrands and it affiliates design, manufacture, source and sell a broad range of apparel essentials such as t-shirts, bras, panties, men’s underwear, kids’ underwear, socks, hosiery, casualwear and activewear. Hanesbrands markets directly to consumers through retail outlets, catalogs and websites, with multiple websites, catalog titles and retail stores. By using this Site, you expressly accept and agree to abide by the terms set forth in this Privacy Policy Statement. For other terms and conditions that apply to your use of the Site, please review our Terms of Use. For purposes of this Privacy Policy Statement, "Personal Information" means any information which you provide to us to a non-public area or section of the Site by which you can be personally identified, and may include your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address, and credit card information. Examples of non-public areas of our Site include private wish list registries, registration pages and email subscribes. Hanesbrands collects Personal Information about you only when you submit it to us, such as when subscribing to our e-mail program, submitting a testimonial or question, participating in special functions of the Site, such as e-cards, wish lists or games, responding to a survey, entering a sweepstakes, placing a product order, returning an item, creating an account, making suggestions or comments, or requesting a catalog.

Besides Personal Information, when you access the Site our web servers may automatically recognize and collect information regarding your referring domain, your type of internet browser, operating system software, screen resolution, cookie preferences and similar technical data. None of this information is personally identifiable and we use this information only in the aggregate, to measure overall Site usage patterns and trends.

Security

We are committed to keeping Personal Information secure, and we have reasonable technical, administrative and physical safeguards in place to help protect Personal Information from loss, misuse or alteration. However, we cannot be and are not responsible for unauthorized access to your Personal Information by hackers or others who have obtained such access through illegal measures.

Cookies and Web Beacons

We may use a technology called "cookies" to provide you with tailored information. A cookie is a small piece of data that the Site can send to your browser, which may then be stored on your hard drive so our Site can recognize you as you browse the Site and when you return. These cookies may collect the operating system and browser that you are using, and accumulate and report anonymous, statistical information on Site usage. The use of cookies shows how our customers navigate through our web site and thus allows us to make our Site more responsive to your needs, by delivering a better and more personalized experience to you. We do not use cookies to identify or track any Personal Information. If you do not wish to have a cookie sent to your browser, you can change the settings on your internet browser to stop it from automatically accepting cookies. While rejecting a cookie will not normally affect your browsing or your ability to access the features on our Site, you may be required to re-enter certain information when you browse or when you return to the Site. If you are experiencing trouble browsing our site, it is possible that you did not accept or have configured your browser to block cookies from JustMySize.

We also may use electronic image requests (also called "web beacons" or "single-pixel gifs") to count page views and to access cookies. The web beacons do not collect, gather, monitor or share any Personal Information; they are used only to compile anonymous information about Site usage

We provide these companies only with the information needed to perform their services (which may include Personal Information) and work closely with them to ensure that your Personal Information is maintained and used as set forth in this Privacy Policy Statement. Accessing and Changing Your Information

At any time, you may obtain a summary of your Personal Information on record with us. Except for Personal Information needed to complete your purchase of products, if any, you may request that: (i) we correct or update any such Personal Information; (ii) such Personal Information not be provided to third parties; or (iii) such Personal Information be removed from our active marketing databases. To make such a request, you may send an e-mail to jms.manager@justmysize.com; write to P.O. Box 748, Rural Hall, NC 27098-0748; or call 1-800-261-6098. When contacting us, please be sure to provide us with your exact e-mail address, name, postal address, and/or telephone number(s) in order to be sure we handle your request correctly. Please also note that each time we send you an e-mail message, we will give you the option to unsubscribe from our e-mail list by simply clicking the unsubscribe link.

These attempts may be sophisticated in nature and designed to appear that they are requests from companies with which you may have a legitimate relationship. Do not provide your personal information to a third party unless you are certain of that party’s identity. Hanesbrands does not request personal information via e-mail or telephone. If you are concerned that a third party is trying to obtain your information by impersonating Hanesbrands, please contact Hanesbrands toll free 1-800-261-6098.
