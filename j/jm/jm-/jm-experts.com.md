# Privacy policy

This is a privacy policy for the Joomla Experts and the www.jm-experts.com website.

What information do we collect?  
The Joomla Experts collects browser information and other non-user specific information to analyze visitor statistics and log data. The inquiry form found on this website is used for processing web development service inquiries.

What do we do with the information we collect?  
Information collected on this site is not sold or transferred to any person or party that is not directly involved in normal business operations with The joomla Experts. Information collected from this website is for internal use only and will not be shared with any partners, affiliates or third-party providers.

Cookies  
Cookies are a technology which can be used to provide you with tailored information from a website. A cookie is an element of data that a Web site can send to your browser, which may then be stored on your system. The Joomla Experts do utilize cookie technology.

Email policy  
The joomla Experts does not collect email address for onsolicitated communications. If you have inquired about our services, we will not use, distribute or sell your email address or contact information.
