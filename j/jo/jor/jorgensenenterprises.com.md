# Privacy Policy :: All the links in our directory are direct, so they will improve your link popularity.

We collect the e-mail addresses and contact information of those who order from us and no information on consumers who browse our Web pages.  This information is used exclusively for ease of future visits and customer security. 

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed on-line.  The WJ Enterprises does not sell or offer your email address, mailing address or phone number to any third parties for any reason.
