Privacy Policy

This is the website of JobsOnline™ LLC, one of the nation's premier job search and career information services on the Internet.

For each visitor to our web pages, our web server automatically recognizes the visitor's domain name and e-mail address (where possible). Data we collect about site usage and from registration information is used to improve or enhance the services we offer to our users, particularly Job Seekers. This information helps us to customize the content and layout of our page(s) for individual users and to notify users about new job opportunities and updates to our web site. Only with a user's permission is this information shared with employers or other reputable organizations with products or services that might be of interest to Job Seekers. All users are provided with the opportunity to choose whether or not they will allow JobsOnline to share their contact information, including telephone, fax, address and email address. In other words, JobsOnline takes customer privacy seriously and does not share information submitted by our customers without the their permission.
