Privacy Policy

This privacy policy covers all of the Share Select Media websites (“SSM Sites”) and related content. Please read this statement before using this or any of the SSM Sites. Your use of these sites indicates your agreement to abide by the terms stated herein.

Information Collection
Except where exceptionally indicated, Share Select Media (“SSM”) is the sole owner of the information collected on SSM Sites via the following mechanisms:

1) Comments
In order to enhance their use of the SSM Sites, users can leave comments on articles. When they do so, users must submit information including a valid email address. SSM uses this information to screen out users who leave comments prohibited by our terms and conditions of use, and will not pass this information on to any other organization.

2) Log Files
Activity on all SSM sites is recorded in standard web server log files. These files store information about each visit such as but not limited to the visitor’s IP address, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), pages viewed e.g. on entry/exit, platform type, and visit date/time. This information is used for SSM’s understanding of user behavior in order to improve the user experience. IP addresses, etc. are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Outbound Links
The SSM Sites will contain links to other sites. Please be aware that SSM is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites, and as such, users should be careful to search out and read the privacy policies of each and every linked Web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy policy only applies to information collected by Share Select Media.

Legal Disclaimer
Though we make every effort to preserve user privacy, Share Select Media may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on any of the SSM Sites.

Business Transitions
If Share Select Media undergoes a business transition, such as a merger, acquisition by another company, or sale of a portion of its assets, users’ personal information will likely be part of the assets transferred.

Notification of Changes
In the event of changes to this privacy policy, Share Select Media promises to inform our readers of such changes via blog posts, direct email or whatever communication method deemed best. It is important for us to keep our users abreast of the information we gather and how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we may be required to disclose it.

Contact Us
We at Share Select Media are always eager to hear from our users. To reach us if you have any questions or suggestions regarding our privacy policy, or for any other reason, users can submit information including a valid email address via our contact form. Following transmission, users will initially receive an automated reply, acknowledging the user’s message. Share Select Media uses the submitted email address solely for the purpose of corresponding with our users, and will not pass this information on to any other organization without our users’ express written consent.
