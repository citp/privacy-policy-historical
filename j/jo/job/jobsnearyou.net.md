The privacy of our visitors is of utmost importance. The following is our privacy policy, as it relates to your interactions with JobHuntLocal.com:

Uses of Personal Information JobHuntLocal.com uses your personal information to improve the content and services, to fulfill your requests of the Services on ordering products and services, and to allow us to contact you about special offers and new products if you register and come into a transaction with JobHuntLocal.com. Your personally identifiable information may also be aggregated and used by JobHuntLocal.com for market research, project planning, troubleshooting problems, detecting and protecting against error(s), fraud or other criminal activity, to executive administrative and internal purposes, to enforce JobHuntLocal.com' Terms of Service, and as otherwise notified to you at the time of collection. JobHuntLocal.com may also share your personal information with other third party businesses to bring selected retail opportunities via direct mail, email and telemarketing, or to cooperate with law enforcement or other legal processes. To fulfill your requests for products and services, we may share the personally identifiable information you provided with your telecommunication carrier and relevant venders and sub-contractors. This privacy policy does not cover those parties or entitles on the use of information collected. Please consult your telecommunication carrier about privacy policies. This Policy applies only to JobHuntLocal.com website and its affiliate sites on personal identifiable information collected by us and it is not applicable to the links to third party websites.

In case of corporate change(s), we may transfer, or disclose information collected, including personally identifiable information to a successor entity in connection with a corporate merger, consolidation and other corporate changes.

Promotional Materials

We will make use of personal identifiable information collected from you to contact you about the use and new opportunities to offer on products and services that maybe of interest of you electronically. You agree that JobHuntLocal.com may use the information to improve JobHuntLocal.com' marketing and promotional efforts, analyze site usage and product offerings. By accepting Terms of Service and this Privacy Policy, you expressively agree to receive this information. If you would like to stop the electronic promotional materials from us, you may read the unsubscribing instructions.

Links to Third Party Sites

This website may contain links to other sites where we cannot be held responsibility on their privacy policies. Visitors and users shall consult those sites about privacy notices that collect identifiable information.

Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data stored on your hard drive containing information about use of cookies on website. Cookie acts as alphanumeric identifiers transferring to your computer to enable the system to recognize your browser and to provide features. To enhance the interests and experience of using our website, we may use cookie on it. However, cookies, by themselves do not tell us your personally identifiable information only if you have chosen to provide such information to us when you register at the website. As a customer, your personally identifiable information may be linked to data stored in the cookie.

Cookies enable us to track and collect users' interests to improve the content and offerings on our site. We may use cookies when we conduct campaigns, including, but not limited to voting polls, surveys, contests and registration. We will never use cookie to retrieve information from your computer that it is originally sent by us to you in a cookie.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your security and have put in place certain physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to prevent unauthorized access and maintain data security. Unfortunately, there is always a potential risk involved in the Internet communication for visitors and customers of the website in sending information through any channels and we cannot guarantee that communications between you and JobHuntLocal.com will be free from unauthorized access by third parties.
