# Privacy Policy - Jobs24pk.com

We are committed to user information confidentiality. Should you require any information pertaining to our privacy policy, please contact us.  


  * Visitor privacy is of almost importance to us and our number one priority.
  * Should you provide us with any personal information, you can be rest assured we will treat it with confidentiality and will not redistribute it in any way.
  * The only user information retrieved is log files and we do not conduct any practice to obtain private information from our users.
  * It is important to note that we are in partnership with Google Adsense and we can’t be held liable for cookies collected from Google. We trust Google with their professional delivery of services rendered.
  * We strictly forbid distribution of immoral information in any form and therefore we pride in refraining from sharing or being associated with such information.
  * Our jobs are obtained from a variety of media sources like newspapers among other authentic sources which are free to be promoted.
  * To ensure the best service is offered to you, your co-operation would gladly be appreciated.
