# JobsOnline: Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is effective as of June 1, 2006. JobsOnline respects the privacy of consumers. JobsOnline will not knowingly disclose personally identifiable information about its consumers to any third party advertiser other than as set forth in this privacy policy. 

JobsOnline provides information on many topics. Some of this information is created by JobsOnline, and some of it is created by third parties. While our site strives to provide accurate expert advice, commentary and other information, JobsOnline does not make any warranty regarding the accuracy, reliability or use of any information provided on this site. 

JobsOnline and third party advertisers may use "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of JobsOnline. Our Web pages may contain electronic images known as Web beacons (sometimes called single-pixel gifs) that allow us to count users who have visited those pages and to deliver special services. 

This site contains links to other sites and advertisements by third party companies. Please be aware that JobsOnline does not exercise control over the information, products, services or policies of third party companies accessible through our site. We encourage our users to be aware and to read the privacy policies of each and every third party site and company. This privacy policy applies solely to how JobsOnline treats the information collected by this site. 

JobsOnline abides by federal law and will not knowingly collect any personal information from or allow the registration of young people under the age of 13. 

JobsOnline collects the following categories of personally identifiable information: first and last name; home and other physical address, including street name and name of a city and town; email address; telephone number; social security number; and other demographic and personal information such as age, gender, education, hobbies, background and interests. JobsOnline may share this personally identifiable information with the following categories of third party persons: any person or entity that the consumer has given permission; any third party affiliate, agent, service provider or business partner of such person or entity; any affiliate, agent, service provider or business partner of JobsOnline; any court, law enforcement agency or other investigator; any owner, successor or purchaser of JobsOnline or its assets. For example, your personally identifiable information may be shared with employers, job boards, job matching services, recruiters, colleges, universities, data aggregators, marketers (possibly in the form of list rental), third-party ad serving companies and other organizations and, in such event, you would be subject to such third-party's privacy policy. 

JobsOnline users can "opt-out" of having their information used for purposes not directly related to our site. You may opt-out from receiving specified types of communications from JobsOnline by clicking [here](https://web.archive.org/profile/opt.html). 

This privacy policy may be amended from time to time and any such privacy policy, as amended, shall appear on this page. Please visit this page from time to time and re-read the privacy policy then in effect to see if any material change to this privacy policy has been made. Your use of this site indicates an acceptance of the terms of this privacy policy. 

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/misc/contact.html?subject=privacy). 

Your privacy is important to us and we want you to have a safe and enjoyable experience when you visit our site. We hope you find our services helpful. The Privacy Team at JobsOnline wishes you the best of luck in your search for the perfect work experience.
