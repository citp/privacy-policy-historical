JOBNEWS PRIVACY STATEMENT

Overview
JobNews is a career site that provides a facilty for employers to get in contact with job seekers. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and want to provide a safe, secure environment so that you will have a reasonable expectation of privacy. We will use our best efforts to ensure that the personal information you submit to us remains private and that any other information you give us will only be used for the intended purposes. You should also read and agree to our Conditions of Use, which are incorporated into this Privacy Statement. We will work hard to earn your loyalty, therefore the following reflects our commitment to you:

Information About You
When you sign up for an account with JobNews, you choose a nickname, provide certain background information, and can post your resume information online, all of which become part of your public profile.We may also collect certain personal information about you, including your name, address, e-mail address, or telephone number which become part of your private personal profile. From time to time, we may also ask for supplemental information for your personal profile, such as interests, likes and dislikes regarding the site, types of jobs you are interested in, etc., in an effort to deliver to you the best possible service. Unless you tell us otherwise, we may use this information to make you aware of opportunities, job openings, services, products, site changes, etc. which we believe may be of interest to you.

General Information Disclosure
We do not disclose any personal information about you from your personal profile (your name, address, telephone number, or e-mail address) to any outside parties unless we believe the law requires it or are under order of a court with competent jurisdiction. However, your public profile (your nickname, employment history, company, job title, salary requirements, education level, and other background information you provide) will be accessible to all users of this site, including any employers, advertisers or recruiters who may use our site. Depending on what you put in your resume and public profile, it is possible that your current employer or other person may learn that you are looking for a job. We cannot be responsible for the disclosure of any information that you have made public. Also, we reserve the right to share with our partners any aggregated information about the general usage of this site.

Your Resume Information
Since JobNews is a career site, we give you the option of posting your resume to your public profile. What you put in your resume should be true and accurate to the best of your knowledge, but JobNews is under no obligation to verify any information you supply. Your public profile and resume will be accessible to all our customers who pay for access to our resume database, including employers, advertisers and recruiters. If you do not want to identify your current employer, do not put your company's real name but use a generic description, such as "large manufacturing company." We use our best efforts to grant access to this service only to legitimate employers, recruiters, hiring managers, advertisers, and human resource professionals.You may remove your resume from your profile at any time. However, customers and other users of our site may have retained a copy of your resume in their own files, therefore JobNews is not responsible for the retention, use, or privacy of any resumes that have been posted, or for their unauthorized use or disclosure by or to third parties under such circumstances.

Updating Account Information
JobNews allows you to change or correct your personal or public information at any time, including the removal or replacement of your resume. To do so, simply log into your account, go to your account profile, and you will find options for editing the information you have submitted. However, users who may have previously accessed the web site may have retained a copy of your resume or an earlier version of it, therefore we cannot be responsible for the retention, use, or privacy of any resumes that have been posted on our site, or for their unauthorized use or disclosure by or to third parties under these circumstances.

Online Order Security
If you place a credit card order online, your personal details and credit card information are scrambled using SSL encryption technology before being sent over the Internet. Although we cannot guarantee encryption or the privacy of your personal details and credit card information, SSL makes it very difficult for your information to be stolen or intercepted while being transferred. Our credit card transactions are maintained by a secure third party, and all credit transactions with this party are likewise encrypted to protect against unauthorized disclosure of your information to third parties.

Information About All Visitors
JobNews collects general usage information about all of the users of our site, such as what areas of the site they visit most frequently or what services they access the most. However, we only use such usage data anonymously and in the aggregate. This information helps us determine what is most beneficial for our users, and how we can continually create a better overall experience for you. We may share this information with our partners, but only in the aggregate so that they too may understand how JobNews visitors use our site and can help create a better overall experience as well.

Cookies
To enhance your experience at JobNews, we use a feature on your Internet browser called a "cookie". Cookies are small files that your Web browser places on your computer's hard drive. We use cookies for remembering user names and passwords and preferences, tracking click streams, and for load balancing. Because of our use of cookies, we can deliver faster service, consistent, updated results, and a more personalized site experience. You have the option of setting your browser to reject cookies. However, doing this will hinder performance and negatively impact your experience on our site.

Feedback
JobNews has an area on the site where you can submit feedback. Any feedback that is submitted through this area becomes the property of JobNews. By posting such information, you agree that JobNews may use your feedback, including success stories, for marketing purposes or to contact you for further feedback on the site.

Children
We specifically do not collect information about children. Any minor under the age of 18 must have his or her parent's consent before providing any personal information to JobNews. If you are concerned about your child's use of JobNews, we encourage you to use web filtering technology to supervise your child's access to the site. We also encourage you to participate in your child's experience with JobNews. With a little guidance, the site can prove very useful in finding a part time or summer job for your child, as well as providing them with excellent career counseling information.
