Use of Cookies
We may store some information such as cookies on your computer when you visit the Website. Cookies are small files that are stored on the hard drive of your computer ready for future access when you return to our Website. This information facilitates your use of the Website and ensures that you do not need to re-enter your details every time you visit it. You can erase or choose to block this information from your computer. The cookies do not contain or capture any personal information. You can configure your computer’s browser to alert you when the Website is attempting to send you a cookie and allow you to accept or refuse the cookie. Your decision to refuse cookies will not prevent you from using the Website, but may limit your ability to take advantage of some of the advanced features of the Website. We may use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests or for promotions or advertisements displayed on the Website. We do not have access to or control over information collected by outside advertisers on our Website. You may opt out of the use of the cookies by disabling cookies option in your web browser.
