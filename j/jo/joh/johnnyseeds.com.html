Privacy and Terms of Use

The "Web Account"

Personal information requested while creating your personalized "Web Account" is soley for Johnny's internal use, so that we may properly contact you if need be. The information you provide here is also used to quickly fill in order form fields upon repeat visits to the site (so long as you are logged in). The information you provide while setting up an account is not shared with anyone. Ever. Nor will it ever be.

Catalog Requests

The catalog request pages ask two (2) questions pertaining to information privacy: (1) Would you like to receive informational updates from Johnny's and (2) is it OK to share your name with other gardening-related businesses. These options are, by default, set to "Yes". This is done so that you may be privvy to information of interest. If you do not want to receive information from Johnny's or do not want your named shared with anyone, simply select "No" for these options. We will record your preferences in your account with us.

Online Ordering

The personal information that you provide while placing an order is primarily used by Johnny's for internal use so that we may contact you if need be. During the order process, you are asked if: (1) Would you like to receive informational updates from Johnny's and (2) is it OK to share your name with other gardening-related businesses? By default, these options are set to "Yes" in the interest of sharing valuable information with you. Your preferences for these options are recorded in your account with us. If you select "No" for these options, you will not receive information from Johnny's and your name will not be shared.
