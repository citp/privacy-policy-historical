Our Privacy Policy

If you do not agree to the revised Privacy Policy, do not access this website.

Your Privacy and Security at Johnsonville.com

Each visitor to Johnsonville.com is important to us. The security of your information is a top priority.

At Johnsonville, we always use industry-standard encryption technologies when transferring and storing consumer data. We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information.

Collecting Information

Johnsonville only collects personal information that is willingly provided, and then, only to provide our customers with the best possible product and experience. If you choose to sign up for newsletter or the Enthusiast Club, we will collect your name, email address, mailing address and your social media information if you choose to sign up using your social media account. If you choose to make a purchase on our online store, Swagville, your payment information will be collected by our third party credit card processor, Authorize.net, in order to complete your payment. We never share your personal information or use it for purposes other than what it was originally given to us and with those third party providers that are required to complete your purchase. We do believe you have a right to know about information that we collect, and how we use it.

Cookies

Like most web sites, Johnsonville.com uses cookies to improve your experience and provide a better service. 

A "cookie" is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive, containing information about the user, such as which pages of the site you visit or which products or recipes you click on for more information. We use cookies on our site, but none of them contain any information that could actually identify you as a person. Rather, cookies can help us provide you with information targeted to your interests. Cookies are stored on your hard drive, not on our site. Most cookies are "session cookies," meaning that they are automatically deleted at the end of a session.

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can disable this function so that your browser will not accept cookies. Please be aware that disabling this function may impact your use and enjoyment of this website. If you choose to disable cookies on your browser, you will not be able to interact with the Enthusiast Club.

IP Addresses

If you choose to submit an entry for a promotion we will collect your IP address for administrative purposes. IP addresses are not used to track a users session. This information only helps us determine how often different areas of our site are visited. We do not link IP address to any information that is personally identifiable.

Website Analytics

Like many websites, our website uses Google Analytics to collect information about your use of our website. This information consists of information that your web browser automatically sends to Google, such as the web address of the page that you visit and your IP address. Google may also set cookies or read preexisting cookies. This information is stored on Googles servers. Google uses this information to provide use with reports about traffic to our website and your visit to our website (such as the domain from which you access the internet, the web address of the website from which you linked to our site, the time and date of your visit the web pages that you view and click through). We will use this data to improve our website structure and content.

Google may also use this data in accordance with their Privacy Policy, which can be found at http://www.google.com/privacy.html. For more detailed information on how Google uses your data through our Site, please visit http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/.

You may elect to join our e-mail list when visiting our website, or we may automatically add you to that list when you use certain online features, such as signing up to participate in one of our online sweepstakes promotions through the Johnsonville.com website or through third party social media sites such as Facebook or Twitter. If you choose to receive e-mails from us, we will share your email information with our third party e-mail provider only for the purposes of sending those e-mails.
