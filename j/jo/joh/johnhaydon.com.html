Privacy

Like most website operators, I collect non-personally-identifying information, such as browser type, language preference, referring site, and the time of each visit. I use this info to better understand how you use this website.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors choose to interact with johnhaydon.com in ways that require personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that johnhaydon.com gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, email subscribers provide their email address. Those who buy products from me are asked to provide additional information. Either way, johnhaydon.com only collects the necessary information to fulfill the purpose of each visitor’s interaction with johnhaydon.com. I do not disclose personally-identifying information, and you can always refuse to share personally-identifying information.

Protection of Personally-Identifying Information

I will never sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone.

If you have joined my email list, I will occasionally email you about new products, solicit your feedback, or just share what’s going on with johnhaydon.com. If you send us a request (for example via email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. I take all reasonably necessary measures to protect against unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information, including secure SSL technology.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that your browser provides each time you visit. This website uses cookies to help identify and track visitors, their usage of johnhaydon.com, and their website access preferences. johnhaydon.com visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using johnhaydon.com’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of johnhaydon.com’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Privacy Policy Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, I may change this Privacy Policy from time to time, and encourage visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to the Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
