### Your Privacy and Security at Johnsonville.com

Each visitor to Johnsonville.com is important to us. The security of your information is a top priority.

At Johnsonville, we always use industry-standard encryption technologies when transferring and storing consumer data. We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information.

### Collecting Information

Johnsonville only collects information that is willingly provided, and then, only to provide our customers with the best possible product and experience. We never share that information or use it for purposes other than what it was originally given to us. We do believe you have a right to know about information that we collect, and how we use it.

### Cookies

Like most web sites, Johnsonville.com uses cookies to to improve your experience and provide a better service. 

A "cookie" is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive, containing information about the user, such as which pages of the site you visit or which products or recipes you click on for more information. We use cookies on our site, but none of them contain any information that could actually identify you as a person. Rather, cookies can help us provide you with information targeted to your interests. Cookies are stored on your hard drive, not on our site. Most cookies are "session cookies," meaning that they are automatically deleted at the end of a session.

If you don't want to receive cookies, you can adjust the settings of your web browser so it tells you each time you receive a cookie. Then you can reject them as they appear.

### E-mail Newsletters 

Johnsonville.com allows you to receive e-mails about our latest recipes, products, events and promotions. You may elect to join our e-mail list when visiting our Web site, or we may automatically add you to that list when you use certain online features, such as signing up to participate in one of our online sweepstakes promotions. We may also ask you to identify departments or areas of specific interest to you, so we may better tailor our e-mails to match your preferences.  
 

Each e-mail you receive from Johnsonville will offer you the opportunity to cancel or "opt-out" of the Johnsonville Enthusiast Club e-mail list. If you don't like the idea of hearing from us via Johnsonville Enthusiast Club e-mails, please let us know by contacting us at 1-888-556-2728, or go to Johnsonville.com and click on the “Contact Johnsonville” section of the home page. Please provide us with your exact name and address, and we'll be sure to remove your information as soon as possible.

### Children's Policy

Johnsonville recognizes the privacy interests of children and we encourage parents and guardians to take an active role in their children's online activities and interests. No personally identifiable information is collected by Johnsonville from children under the age of 13 and this site is not intended for children under the age of 13.

If someone under the age of 13 has provided us with personal information, Johnsonville asks that the parent or guardian of that person contact us at the e-mail address at the top of this Policy and request removal of the information from our records. Upon receiving your request, we will promptly remove such information from our records.
