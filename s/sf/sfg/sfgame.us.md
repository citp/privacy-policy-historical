# 

We at Playa Games GmbH take your personal information very seriously and strictly conform to privacy rules. This Privacy Policy applies to all products, online games, services and websites we offer (hereinafter referred to as "Services"). Personal data is only stored on our website to guarantee a fully functional gaming experience. Of course the collected data is not sold or shared with any third parties except for legal obligation.

The following describes how personal data is collected, processed and treated.

**Protocol data – non-personal data**

We automatically store information transferred by your browser on our servers. This data is non-personally identifiable and will only be traced back in case of an assumed violation of law.

**Registration – personal data**

When you sign up for our Services your e-mail address, password and chosen nickname are stored on our servers. If you do not longer agree to the collection of data at a later date, you can revoke your consent at any time by deleting your account in the game options.

**Cookies**

Our Services use so-called cookies. Cookies are small data files that are stored on your computer by websites. With the help of these cookies our products are more user-friendly, effective and secure.

**Personal communication**

E-mails or other messages you may send us will be stored to process your inquiry, answer your questions and improve our Services.

**Additional content**

Basically, our Services are free of charge. In addition, certain Services are offered for sale.

If you decide to use them, account-specific data will be submitted to other service providers where required (deduction, billing). We stcrictly adhere to the rules of the German Teleservices Act and do not share sensitive data with third parties unwarrantedly.

**Newsletter**

Your validated e-mail address is used for communication and our occasional newsletter we keep you informed about your subscribed services with. You are not going to receive third party-advertisements and we do not share your e-mail address with third parties.

**Other websites**

This Privacy Policy only applies to our Services. Clicking on links in our forum, in other members’ messages or in our chat room might forward you to third party-sites or offers we do not have a bearing on and that are excluded from our Privacy Policy.

**Right to information**

At any time, you have the legal right to obtain information on the content and extent of your stored personal data. You can receive this information by using the contact options listed here: Imprint

**Change of this policy**

This Privacy Policy is subject to change due to the internet’s fast development that can require an adaptation to current laws, technologies and our Services. In this case we will inform you at the right time.

**Further information**

Playing games together requires mutual trust. At any time, we would like to answer your questions on the processing of your personal data. If you have questions not covered by this Privacy Policy, if you have comments or are looking for detailed information, feel free to contact us whenever you want: Contact
