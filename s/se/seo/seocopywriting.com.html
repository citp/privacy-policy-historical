Ordering Online

Any data you submit to us through an online order form is transferred to us via a Secure Sockets Layer””an encryption protocol””and is kept in secure databases.

In other words, any data you send us is encrypted, so in the unlikely event a hacker intercepts your data, it cannot be easily read.

Email

If you subscribe to SEO Copywriting Buzz, or purchase any products from SuccessWorks Search Marketing, Inc., your name and email address will be placed in a database. We will also never rent or sell our email addresses to anyone. We may use your name and address to send you information on products that may suit your interests. Any email you send to SuccessWorks Search Marketing, Inc. is completely confidential. We will not add your name to our email list without your permission.
