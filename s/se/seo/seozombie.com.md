# Privacy Policy

The information you give to me will not be given to a third party, but will be used to contact you. I may use this information to tell you about recommended products and services.

I do not spam, scam, or sell with your information! I will only use your information to send you what you request and other information I think you may find useful and interesting.

You may need to provide an email for commenting and subscriptions of comments, but this information is kept private. I may use it to contact you.
