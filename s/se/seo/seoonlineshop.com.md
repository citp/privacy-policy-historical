# Privacy Policy - Seo Online Shop

[](https://web.archive.org/web/20100613234335id_/http%3A//www.seoonlineshop.com/contact-us.php) Privacy Policy 

At SEOonlineshopping.com, we respect your privacy. You are free to browse any of the web pages without having to reveal your personal details, and we do not collect any personal information without your consent. We also do not place any cookies to track our visitors' behavior. However, when it comes to utilizing any of our services or purchasing our products, we do require some level of identifiable information to provide our services and support. These include your communicable address, phone numbers, credit card details and the like. 

_Using Personal Information._

The personal information collected from you with your consent will be stored securely using latest data storage facilities protected by network firewalls and other access control procedures. We treat all your personal information with utmost confidentiality and use it only for business communications with you. 

_Information Sharing and Disclosure_

SEOonlineshop.com will not sell, share, or rent your personal information to any third parties under any circumstances. However, seoonlineshop.com cannot be held responsible for any accidental breach of security. 

_Changes in Privacy Policy_

SEOonlineshop.com has the right to make any changes to this privacy policy as and when required. Although from time to time we notify any changes made to our policies through newsletters to our clients, to keep yourself up to date with our policies, you are requested to visit this page often. 

_Questions and Suggestions_

If you have any doubts in our privacy policy or if you like to suggest anything valuable that will be beneficial to our clients, please use the contact us form provided in the website and we will respond promptly. However, we seldom encourage kibitzers at SEOonlineshop.com.
