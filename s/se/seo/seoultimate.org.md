# Privacy Policy | SEO Ultimate

_privacy poliicy with SEO Ultimate_

SEO Ultimate takes your privacy very seriously.This privacy policy section describes what information we collect from you and how we use it.

**Cookies** -  
Third-party vendors, including Google, also use cookies to serve ads on our website based on a user’s prior visits. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

**Email addresses**

Users may subscribe to our newsletter by providing their name and email address to get notified whenever there is a new articles. We assure you that we will never share your email addresses with any third party.Besides users may unsubscribe anytime, and every email delivered will contain a link which can be used for that purpose.

**Comments-**

Users are requires to providing their name and email address to comment.We actively monitor each and every comment. **Comment spamming  **is strictly prohibited.If some user tries to comment spam,his comment will be deleted.
