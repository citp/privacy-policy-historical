# :Free Classified Ads and Online Advertising

**Privacy Policy**

Revised: 01 Jan 2012

**Protecting your Privacy**  
We don't share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.We don't engage in cross-marketing or link-referral programs with other sites.We do use cookies to manage user sessions, user preferences, language preferences, region preferences and/or other parameters that help the site navigation. You have an option to turn off cookies from your browser. We use these cookies on any visits to the website whether logged in or not. We also use third party addons that might store cookies on your computer. We are not responsible for the use, storage and maintaince of the third party cookies. Selldude does not use cookies, beacons, scripts or any tracking device to monitor user activity for third party marketing purpose. Disabling the cookies might cause some issues logging in or browsing through the website. SellDude does not guarantee the site functionality without the use of the cookie. We never send you unsolicited communications for marketing purpose. We store a lot of personal information as provided by you but we only share the information that has has been marked for sharing on the listings.We never display your email . Actual email is hidden but users can communicate through the contact method provided by us thus maintaining the privacy of the individuals. We disclose the physical location of the seller in order to avoid spam and scams. By the use of this website, you consent to the use of ip address and all the information gathered from it to be used on the listings. Our private policy is restricted to our domain. If you navigate to a third party website through a link posted on our website, you should follow the private policy of the third party website. Selldude neither has control nor is responsible for the consequences from the usage of third party website. We do not knowing collect information from the users under the age of 13. If you are aware of any such usage of the website by person under the age of 13, please report us at abuse@selldude.com

Although rare, Selldude reserves the right to disclose information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to respond to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal process, to respond to claims related to the violations of the terms as forth in terms and conditions.Selldude reserves the right t disclose information if required to protect the rights, property, or personal safety of Selldude, its employees, its users or the general public.In the event that a part or whole of the Selldude is acquired by another business, the acquiring entity shall have access to all the data collected and stored without your consent. Selldude may inform you regarding the sale, acquisition, data access transfer or the transaction. 

**Data Collection & its usage: **  
We collect information from you such as email address, names, addresses, website address, phone numbers, third party authentication such as face book, Google, opened, web logs, server logs, database logs , your ip address, date and time stamps and browser information.We do not store credit card numbers or deal with any financial transactions with the website users. All financial transactions are transmitted and handled through financial gateways and Secured Socket Layer (SSL) Technology. We store this information even after the listings have expired in order to analyze our website performance and statistics. We do not share this data with a third party. We may also collect and store all the information from the user messageing and communications.We use this information to enforce terms and conditions and laws and regulations.We use this information to monitor website performance, reach and evaluate potential areas of growth.We use this information to diagnose issues with our website and take corrective actions.By the use of SellDude Websites, you voluntarily grant us rights to store and use this information as mentioned above.
