SEBULE does not  sell or rent users' personal data to third parties for their marketing purposes without users explicit consent. We may disclose personal information to respond to legal requirements, enforce our policies, respond to claims that a posting or other content violates other's rights, or protect anyone's rights, property, or safety.

SEBULE shares the information  with colaborators and service providers who help SEBULE  with business operations.

The use of personal data in  SEBULE website is meant only for the purpose of the posting and the content therein by users of SEBULE and not for collection or sending spam without the consent of the publisher.

SEBULE user can publish,  edit, extend or cancel a posting whenver  it is necessary by following the instructions given by SEBULE based on the posting made.
