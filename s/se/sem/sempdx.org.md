# SEMpdx: Privacy Policy

SEMpdx is commited to the privacy of our users. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices. If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, submit them online through our contact form.

All information that you submit on this site will be treated as confidential. We do not share, sell, trade or rent any personal information gathered from this Web site with third parties unless we are required to do so by law.

**Information Collected:  
Our contact form collects information to process the questions, comments and requests submitted by our site visitors.**

**Notification of Changes:**  
If at some point in the future, there is a change to our information usage practices that affect your Personally Identifiable Information, we will post those changes to this policy.

**E-mail Contact:**  
We may send promotional e-mail offers to customers who provide us with their e-mail addresses. We will stop sending promotional e-mail to anyone who requests it.

**Links To Other Web Sites:  
We are not responsible for the data collection practices, policies or contents of Web sites other than our own, including any web sites that are accessible from this web site.**
