# Sexy-Employee.com - Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. **We NEVER sell, barter, lend, give, or trade your email address, phone number, credit card information (if applicable) or birthday to any business, person, or entity for any reason, excepting where it is required by law or our partners.** The list of our business partners is provided below.

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted to Sexy-Employee.com.   
When joining the membership portion of our web site you are asked to provide your name, e-mail, username and password.   
When purchasing e-shop items you may be asked to put such information as name, email and postal address. 

**Our Business Partners:**  
We use www.CCBill.com and www.MyVirtualCard.com as third-party credit card processing services, who will obtain additional information such as address, phone number and credit card data. 

**The Way We Use Information:**  
The information collected by Sexy-Employee.com is used solely for the creation and maintenance of your membership account or for processing your purchase. We do not sell, loan, lease, swap, trade or otherwise disclose your personal data. That information collected by CCBill and/or by MyVirtualCard is used for the purpose of processing payment of your membership to Sexy-Employee.com or e-shop item purchase. Your credit card number is preserved by our business partners only. We do not have access to such information ourselves and will never ask you about that.

In addition to that, we reserve the right to use such personal data as name and email for our internal use and for providing our current and past customers with up-to-date information of Sexy-Employee.com events in which they can express their interest.

**Our Commitment to Data Security:**  
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
