# SeneGence Website Privacy Policies

**I. General**

SeneGence International respects the privacy of every individual who visits our Web sites and online areas or purchases SeneGence products online. This statement of our privacy policies outlines the information SeneGence will collect and how SeneGence will use that information. This policy statement will also instruct you on what to do if you want SeneGence to remove your personal information collected or stored during previous visits to SeneGence Web sites. 

**II. Use of Personal Information**

SeneGence will not collect or retain any information about you personally (that is your name, address, telephone number, or e-mail address) unless you provide it to us voluntarily. If you do not want any personal information collected, please do not submit it to us. If you have already submitted this information and would like us to remove it from our records, please contact us by clikcing the link at the bottom of this policy statement. We will use reasonable efforts to delete your information from our existing files. If you do provide us with personal information, we may retain and use that information for several purposes. First, we may store and process the information to better understand your needs and how we can improve our products and services. Second, we may retain information about your previous purchases of SeneGence products in order to customize our Web site services for your future visits to the site. Third, we may contact you to offer our products or services to you. Your e-mail address or other contact information will not be provided to any third party without your prior consent. 

**III. Security**

Data security is important to SeneGence. Therefore, SeneGence has technology in place to ensure any personal information you voluntarily submit is safe including secure sockets layer (SSL) technology to protect the storage and transfer of your personal information. We also use digital secure server identification from Go Daddy to provide you with state of the art protection technology on the Internet. Visitor information is stored in secured databases and is protected from unauthorized access. 

**IV. Children**

SeneGence does not intend to collect any personal information (such as name, address, telephone number, age or e-mail address) from persons younger than 13 years of age. If a parent or guardian of a child who has provided us with such information would like the information deleted from our records, he or she should contact us at the e-mail address or phone number listed at the bottom of this policy statement. We will make reasonable efforts to delete the child's information from our existing file. 

**V. Technical Information Collected Automatically**

In some cases, we may collect certain technical information, which does not allow us to identify you personally, pertaining to your use of our Web site. For example, on the Web such information may include the type of Internet browser or computer operating system that you are using and the domain name of the Web site which you linked to our site or advertisement. 

**VI. Information Placed Automatically on Your Computer's Hard Drive**

When you view one of our Web sites, we may store some information in the form of a cookie or similar file that is automatically downloaded to your computer. Cookies enable us, among other things, to tailor a Web site or advertisement to better match your interests and preferences. If you do not want cookies sent to or stored in your system, most Internet browsers will allow you to erase cookies from your computer's hard drive, prevent them from being stored, or trigger a warning before a cookie is stored. Please refer to your browser instructions or help screen to learn more about these functions. 

**VII. Whom to Contact**

If you have submitted information about yourself through a SeneGence Web site, and you would like us to delete that information from our records, please contact us by clicking here. We will make reasonable efforts to delete your personal information from our existing files.
