Updated 24 August 2016 

BY USING THIS WEB SITE AND PROVIDING PERSONAL INFORMATION TO SendaText.co, YOU CONSENT TO THE COLLECTION, USE AND DISCLOSURE OF THAT INFORMATION IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS PRIVACY POLICY.

## General

Please review the Terms of Use in conjunction with this Privacy Policy. All capitalized terms not defined in this Privacy Policy may have the meanings given to them in the Terms of Use. The information that users provide via this Web Site, together with information regarding the manner in which the user uses this Web site, will not be processed or disclosed by SendaText.co except as may be permitted by the Terms of Use or this Privacy Policy. By being a user of this Web site, the user agrees that SendaText.co may share with other parties aggregate, individual, communication content and locator information gathered by SendaText.co in the course of the user's continuing individual use of this Web site. 

  * "Communication Content" is information that contains the content of your communication including but not limited to the contents of the text messages.
  * "Aggregate information" is information that describes the habits, usage patterns and/or demographics of users as a group but does not describe or reveal the identity of any particular user.
  * "Individual information" is information about a user that is presented in a form distinguishable from of information relating to other users but not in a form that personally identifies any user or enables the recipient to.comunicate directly with any user. 
  * "Locator information" consists of a user's name, e-mail address, physical address and/or other data about the user that enables the recipient to personally identify the user. Any user who does not wish to receive any special offers or.comunications from SendaText.co directly or on behalf of suppliers, may so notify SendaText.co at the address listed below. (Note that a user's election not to receive such information will not affect the user's receipt of offers and.comunications that were processed prior to the users' election.) Locator information and individual information may be processed and stored by SendaText.co. Users may contact SendaText.co to determine whether such information has been accurately recorded and, if not, to request correction of any inaccuracies in the information recorded by SendaText.co.



The information collected is used to improve the content of our Web pages and the quality of our service, and is not shared with or sold to other organizations for.comercial purposes, except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

a) It is necessary to share or disclose information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding unlawful activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Terms of Use, or as otherwise required by law. 

b) SendaText.co transfers information about you if SendaText.co is acquired by or merged with another.copany. In this event, SendaText.co will notify you before information about you is transferred and b.coes subject to a different privacy policy. 

c) It is necessary to provide information to our independent contractors who host and maintain the Web Site and e-mail services, and other service providers and suppliers if the disclosure will enable that party to perform business, professional or technical support to SendaText.co. 

## 2\. Information Gathering and Usage 

When you register for SendaText.co or use SendaText.co, we may ask for information such as your name, email address,.copany name and address information. SendaText.co uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provision, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research. 

## 3\. Cookies 

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's.coputers and stored on your.coputer's hard drive. Cookies are required to use the SendaText.co service. SendaText.co uses cookies to record current session information and may use permanent cookies. You are required to log-in to your SendaText.co application site after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents. 

## 4\. Data Storage 

SendaText.co uses third party vendors and hosting partners to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to run the SendaText.co web site. Although SendaText.co owns the code, databases, and all rights to the SendaText.co application, you retain all rights to your data.

"SendaText.co may modify, alter, or update this Privacy Policy at any time. User's and account holders are responsible for reviewing this web page frequently to ensure they are aware of any changes."
