## 1\. Definitions and background

For ease of use of this document, certain terms have been associated with the specific meaning provided below. 

“ **Consumer** ” means an individual who owns or leases a home and who uses the Site or Service for identifying tradesmen. 

“ **Device** ” means a computer, laptop, desktop, smart phone, tablet, and any other similar equipment. 

“ **Personal Information** ” means information that identifies an individual or that, when combined with other information available to the data holder may allow identifying an individual with reasonable certainty. 

“ **Registered User** ” means a User who has provided Personal Information in order to use the Services. 

“ **Site** ” means and includes the Site and any affiliated microsite(s) that we operate alone or with a business partner. 

“ **Service** ” means the services and content provided from or through the Site, such as the ability to connect consumers seeking assistance for a home improvement, remodeling or construction project with tradesmen available to provide a quote for such projects, and perform the services requested by the consumer. 

“ **Tradesman** ” means an individual or company who has provided contact information in order to obtain information about home improvement projects, make proposals for these projects, and otherwise interact with consumers who are seeking tradesmen for a home improvement project, remodeling project or similar services. 

“ **User** ” is an individual who uses the Site or Service. A User can be a user of the Site (who merely explores and uses the Site without being a Registered User), a Registered User, or a Tradesman.

## 7\. Cookies and other technologies

We use cookies and other technologies to gather information about the Users of our Site and the recipients or readers of our communications, in order to know what information is or might be of interest to them. All Personal Information collected through cookies is processed in accordance with our Cookie Disclosure posted in the legal notices section of our Site. 

A cookie is a small file of information, letters and number, that our Site may transfer directly to your browser and then is stored on your browser, subject to the options resulting from the cookie setting of your browser. The cookie is then returned to our Site when you return on our Site. Cookies allow the Site to recognize the User’s Device. 

There are different types of Cookies: 

  * “Session cookies”, which allow websites to link the actions of a User during a browser session, and disappear when the User leaves the website;
  * “Permanent cookies”, which remember the User’s actions and preferences on a website, which remain in the User’s terminal;
  * "First-party cookies", which are set in the website domain name listed in the address bar, and
  * "Third-party cookies", which are set by other domain sources that have items on the page.



Cookies enable us, for example, to enhance your online experience, facilitate electronic communications and measure online audience and traffic. Each browser of each individual Device has its own cookie settings and configuration options that can be managed:

  * For Internet Explorer™: http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows7/how-to-manage-cookies-in-internet-explorer-9
  * For Chrome™: https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647?hl=en‎
  * For Firefox™: http://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer#w_cookie-settings



By using the Site and subscribing to the Services, you expressly consent to the use of Cookies in accordance with the United Kingdom’s Data Protection Act.

## 8\. User's rights

We want our Users to enjoy their visit to our Site, and we respect their privacy. To this end, we provide numerous privacy choices. 

  * Right not to provide informationYou can choose not to provide us with any Personal Information, or not to respond to certain questions. However, if you wish to obtain access to certain content, ask us a question, ask for a Tradesman quote, or to take advantage of certain features of the Site, you may be required to provide certain contact or other Personal Information, as indicated in the relevant form. 
  * Right to access, right to modify, right to correct and right to delete Personal InformationIf you are a Registered User of the Site (Registered Consumer or Registered Tradesman), you may update or correct the information you provided to us by requesting us to make such changes as indicated in the “How to contact us” section. 
  * Right to block cookiesYou can choose to block cookies. To do so, please review the cookies and other technology section. Please note that if you disable all cookies from this Site, you will not be able to access certain portions of the Site 
  * Right to object to new uses of your Personal InformationIf at any time we wish to disclose or use your Personal Information in a way or for a purpose other than as described in this Privacy Notice, you will be given the opportunity to opt-out. 
  * Right to know about third party marketingWe do not currently share your Personal Information with third parties for their direct marketing purposes. If at any time we wish to disclose, or use your personal data in a way, or for a purpose other than as described in this Privacy Notice, you will be given the opportunity to opt-out. 
  * Right to block marketing communicationsIf you no longer wish to receive marketing information or communications from us or from our affiliated entities, please send us an email as indicated in the “How to contact us” section. You can also unsubscribe from our distribution list by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in the communications that you receive from us. 
  * Right to block tailored advertisingYou can choose to block the use of tailored advertising about us on other websites, based on your visit to our Site by visiting the website of Network Advertising Initiative, that of the Digital Advertising Alliance, or similar websites that advertising networks offer for these purposes. 

Please note, however, that if you choose not to receive tailored advertisements, you will still see online advertisements, about us or other companies. In addition, any opt-out will apply only to the activities conducted with the specific browser that you used for opting out, and will not be limited to your use of the Site. 

  * Access RequestYou have the right to ask for a copy of the personal information we hold about you, which is called a “subject access request’, for which we charge a fixed statutory fee of £10.00.
