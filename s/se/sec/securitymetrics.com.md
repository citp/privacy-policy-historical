# Privacy Policy - SecurityMetrics

  


### Privacy Policy

SecurityMetrics, Inc. is aware of the privacy concerns of its customers. Our policy for collecting and using personal information is detailed below. 

**PRIVACY POLICY**  
SecurityMetrics, Inc.'s policy in relation to information collected through registration, testing, and/or any other means is to respect and protect the privacy and confidentiality of our users. SecurityMetrics, Inc. does not disclose, rent, or sell email addresses, security test results, or any other information that we may receive to any third party, unless specifically requested by the customer or requested or required by applicable credit card associations, credit card processors, or in connection with any legal proceedings where disclosure of such data has been requested or required. SecurityMetrics may use the information and data submitted by users and customers for any other purposes related to Company's business, including, but not limited to, conducting market research, improving its products and services, sending surveys, and notifying customers of product upgrades and updates, new products, special offers, seminars and conventions and any other changes within SecurityMetrics that may affect customers and users.

**CHANGES**  
We reserve the right to make changes to this policy at any time without notice.
