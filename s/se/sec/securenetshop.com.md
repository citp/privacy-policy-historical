# SecureNetShop

**SecureNetShop.com** is committed to respecting and protecting the privacy of visitors to our web sites as well as the privacy of our actual customers. 

For visitors, we've structured our web sites so that, in general, you can visit us on the web without identifying yourself or revealing any personal information.

For customers, once you choose to provide us personally identifiable information (any information by which you can be identified), you can be assured that it will only be used to support your customer relationship with SecureNetShop.com.

  
When you visit our web site, our web server collects basic user information about you that includes your ISP's domain, the time you accessed our web site, and the pages from within our web site that you visit. 

We may at times also use cookies, which are small pieces of information that a web site can store in a designated file on your computer. Cookies allow a web application to respond to you as an individual. By gathering and remembering information about your preferences, the web application can tailor its operation to your needs, likes, and dislikes.

Our web server does not collect your e-mail address or aggregate information on what other pages on the Web you have accessed or visited.

**How do we use this information?**  
The basic user information collected is used to help us analyze web traffic and to improve the content of our web site. Cookies may be used to facilitate your customer interaction with us.

We may from time to time disclose to third parties aggregate information on our web site's traffic profile. For example, Internet advertising companies may require this data to help us better target our advertisements on our web site and/or other web sites.

Note that our site provides links to products and services offered by other companies; the company's web site that you link to will be able to determine that you linked from our web site.

**SecureNetShop.com Customers**

  
When you sign up for SecureNetShop.com services, you provide us with both contact and payment information. Contact information typically includes your name, address, and phone number. Payment information typically includes your credit card number. 

**How do we use this information?  
**While you are a SecureNetShop.com customer, we manage information with regard to your account status, choice of services, and customer logs. In addition, we store information necessary to track orders processed through one or more of our services. 

We use the contact information you provide us to establish our service for you. We use payment information for payment processing purposes only. We create and maintain other information such as customer account status, choice of services, and customer logs in the normal course of providing service.

SecureNetShop.com will not disclose customer personal and account information unless we have reason to believe that disclosing such information is necessary to identify, make contact with, or bring legal action against someone who may be causing harm or interfering with the rights or property of SecureNetShop.com, our customers, or others, or where SecureNetShop.com has a good faith belief that the law requires such disclosure.

SecureNetShop.com also will not, except for reasons stated below, disclose to third parties information submitted by our customers' clients (those individuals, companies or organizations purchasing products, services or downloadable files from our customers via any SecureNetShop hosted service). The circumstances under which SecureNetShop.com will disclose such information are when: 

  1. It is necessary to protect the legitimate interests of SecureNetShop.com and its customers;
  2. It is required to cooperate with interception orders, warrants, or other legal process that SecureNetShop.com determines in its sole discretion to be valid and enforceable; and 
  3. It is necessary to provide to a law enforcement agency when the contents are inadvertently obtained by SecureNetShop.com and appear to pertain to the commission of a crime.

SecureNetShop.com disclaims any intention to censor, edit, or engage in ongoing review or surveillance of communications stored on or transmitted through its facilities by customers or others. SecureNetShop.com will, however, review, delete, or block access to communications that may harm SecureNetShop.com, its customers or third parties. The grounds on which SecureNetShop.com may take such action include, but are not limited to, actual or potential violations of SecureNetShop.com Terms of Service. 

SecureNetShop.com reserves the right to send Customers bulletins and other important information regarding our services at our discretion.
