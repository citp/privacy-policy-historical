SECURE-RES.COM privacy policy

Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All personal information is transmitted via SSL security encryption. Our site has been authenticated by VeriSign, Inc. which provides our certification and encryption keys.

You are responsible for safeguarding your unique SECURE-RES.COM User ID and password to prevent unauthorized use of or access to the online reservation system.


E-mails to guests

This e-mail contains a reservation reference number generated by SECURE-RES.COM and details of the reservation. It also may contain customized information from you. You are responsible for the contents of the customized portion of the automated e-mail to your guests.

If you subscribe to our automated Guest Welcome and Thank You E-mail Service, you are responsible for the content of those e-mails.

SECURE-RES.COM does not contact your guests via e-mail except in the instances stated above or if the guest contacts us for support.


Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of our site, or your dealings with our site, you can contact SECURE-RES.COM.
