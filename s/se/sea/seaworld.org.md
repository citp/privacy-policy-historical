# SeaWorld/Busch Gardens Animal Information Database - Legal Statement

**Terms of Use**

This is how we will handle information we learn about you from your visit to our web site.

_**KIDS, PLEASE READ THIS FIRST:**_ If you are 13 or younger, you will need your parent or guardian’s permission to give us information about you. Please ask them to read this Policy with you.

_**Parents or Guardians, Please Note:**_ We may ask you or your child for information such as name, age, address, city, state, zip code, comments , suggestions or other information. We may also ask you or your child for information regarding their hobbies and interests. **** In addition to asking for this information when you or your child log-in, we may also collect personal information through games, surveys, questionnaires or other clearly identifiable ways located throughout the web site. We will make it clear to children at all places on our site where personal information is being collected that they need your consent before providing the information.

Our purpose in gathering this personal information is to learn more about visitors to our site, and how we can improve our site. **** We may also use your personal information to contact you or your child about ideas we may have about improvements to our web site, for more comments, or to tell you about news, special events or special product promotions. If you do not want you or your child to be contacted for these purposes or if you would like to obtain a copy of you or your child’s personal information gathered by this web site or to correct or delete such information from our web site’s database, you can _always_ **** send us an e-mail to the following address: **[shamu@seaworld.org](mailto:shamu@seaworld.org)** or you can write to us at: 

> **Attention: Scott Gass  
>  SeaWorld/Busch Gardens Animal Information Database  
>  7007 SeaWorld Drive, Orlando, Florida 32821  
>  Attention: Scott Gass**

**_  
Parents and Guardians, Please Also Note:_** We will _not_ collect any personal information about your child _unless_ you have provided us with your consent. To give your consent, please see the brief form immediately following this privacy policy statement. You can also provide us with consent by sending us an email to the following address: **[shamu@seaworld.org](mailto:shamu@seaworld.org)** or by writing to us at **SeaWorld/Busch Gardens Animal Information Database, 7007 SeaWorld Drive, Orlando, Florida 32821. Attention: Scott Gass,** and specifying your name and your child’s name and your relationship to the child with the words: "I (we) hereby give my (our) consent to your collecting personal information about my child(ren) through your web site pursuant to your privacy policy." 

By using this site, you signify your assent to our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to our Privacy Policy, please do not use this site, and please do not allow your child to use this site. We reserve the right, at our complete discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time. Please check back periodically for any changes we may make to the policy. Your and/or your child’s continued use of the **SeaWorld/Busch Gardens Animal Information Database (www.seaworld.org/www.buschgardens.org/www.shamutv.com)** following the posting of changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes.

**

We want to be very clear: We do not obtain personal information about you or your child other than that which you or your child choose to provide, _and_ we will not sell, rent, trade or otherwise disclose personal information provided to anyone outside of the Anheuser-Busch family of companies or its advertising or promotional agencies or consultants.
