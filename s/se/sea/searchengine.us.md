# Pay Per Click Search Engine PPC Search Engine Cost Per Click

|  Privacy Policy

Your privacy is extremely important to us. Following is our policy regarding how we protect the privacy of the personal information you provide us. SearchEngine.us collects information when you register with SearchEngine.us. When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, zip code, your address. SearchEngine.us automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, SearchEngine.us cookie information and another accessible information.

SearchEngine.us uses information for the following general purposes: improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

SearchEngine.us does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except under the following circumstances: -We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with SearchEngine.us under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your information to help SearchEngine.us communicate with you about offers from SearchEngine.us and our marketing partners. -We respond to court orders or legal process.

SearchEngine.us does not provide any individually identifiable information to the advertiser.

Cookies

SearchEngine.us may set and access SearchEngine.us cookies on your computer. SearchEngine.us lets other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages and/or network to set and access their cookies on your computer.  
  
   
Surveys

From time to time, we or a company selected by us may offer you the opportunity to participate in surveys. You may be asked to provide membership information to participate. All surveys are optional.  
  
   
Contests

We may from time to time offer you the opportunity to participate in sweepstakes, product giveaways and similar services. You will be asked to provide membership information to participate in any sweepstakes or giveaways. We or a company selected by us administers the sweepstakes and contacts the winner. All sweepstakes are optional.

General

You can edit your SearchEngine.us Account Information at any time.
