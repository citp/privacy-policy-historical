Privacy Policy

Statement on Terms of Use

Southern New Hampshire University (SNHU) values your privacy.

This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that SNHU collects, and the ways in which SNHU uses that personal information.

SNHU will not share your information with any other party, except for third party vendors conducting business on behalf of SNHU.

The information we collect may include your name, address, phone numbers and email addresses where you may be reached. We may use this information to contact you through various means including phone calls, text messages, email and postal mail about products and services that we offer.

Please direct any questions to webfeedback@snhu.edu.
