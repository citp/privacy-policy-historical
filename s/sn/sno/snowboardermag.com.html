Privacy Policy

General

GrindMedia.com collects personal information when you register with or subscribe to GrindMedia.com, when you use GrindMedia.com services, when you visit GrindMedia.com pages or the pages of certain GrindMedia.com partners, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. GrindMedia.com may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.

When you register we may ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. Once you register with GrindMedia.com and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.

GrindMedia.com collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners.

GrindMedia.com automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, GrindMedia.com cookie information, and the pages of videos you request.

GrindMedia.com uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

GrindMedia.com does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you’ve requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with GrindMedia.com under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help GrindMedia.com communicate with you about offers from GrindMedia.com and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information.

We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims;

We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of GrindMedia.com’s terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.

We transfer information about you if GrindMedia.com is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, GrindMedia.com will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

GrindMedia.com displays targeted advertisements based on personal information. Advertisers (including ad serving companies) may assume that people who interact with, view, or click on targeted ads meet the targeting criteria – for example, men ages 18-24 from a particular geographic area.

GrindMedia.com does not provide any personal information to the advertiser when you interact with or view a targeted ad. However, by interacting with or viewing an ad you are consenting to the possibility that the advertiser will make the assumption that you meet the targeting criteria used to display the ad.

GrindMedia.com advertisers include financial service providers (such as banks, insurance agents, stock brokers and mortgage lenders) and non-financial companies (such as stores, airlines, and software companies).

GrindMedia.com works with vendors, partners, advertisers, and other service providers in different industries and categories of business.

Cookies

GrindMedia.com may set and access GrindMedia.com cookies on your computer.

GrindMedia.com lets other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies’ use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to GrindMedia.com’s cookies.

GrindMedia.com may use web beacons to access GrindMedia.com cookies inside and outside our network of web sites and in connection with GrindMedia.com products and services.

Confidentiality and Security

We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.

We have physical and procedural safeguards to protect personal information about you.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

GrindMedia.com may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your GrindMedia.com account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.

Questions and Suggestions

If you have questions or suggestions, please contact us at info@grindmedia.com
