# Privacy Statement

  |  **Privacy Policy**

We respect your privacy.  Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, loaned, or disclosed in any other manner.  Any information you give to Valley Weather Service/ snowforecast.com will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. More detailed information on how we safeguard your personal information is described below.  If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to [let us know](mailto:valleyweather@earthlink.net).

**Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:**

Valley Weather Service/ snowforecast.com does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including drawing entry forms or email lists. 
**Browser information collected on the website:**

Valley Weather Service/ snowforecast.com promises that our website logs will only be used to improve the value of the website to you.  Our website logs **are not personally identifiable** , and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site. 
**Use of cookies:**

We do not use cookies on this site and do not plan to, as we believe they give a false representation of the visitor demographic and most importantly they invade privacy. 
**Privacy of our email lists:**

Valley Weather Service/ snowforecast.com does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone.  If you have any further questions or comments,  
please [E-mail us](mailto:valleyweather@earthlink.net).
