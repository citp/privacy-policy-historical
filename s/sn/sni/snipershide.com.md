# Privacy | Sniper's Hide

**Privacy Policy**  
The information that is collected,  
For each visitor to Sniper’s Hide, our server automatically recognizes only the user’s domain name, but not the e-mail address. ( _This does not include registered user of the forum_ )

Sniper’s Hide collects the e-mail addresses of those who post messages to our forum, the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, aggregate information on what pages users access.

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web site and disclosed when legally required to do so, at the request of governmental authorities conducting an investigation, to verify or enforce compliance with the policies governing our site and applicable laws, or to protect against misuse or unauthorized use of snipershide.com.

With respect to cookies: Our Forum software does use cookies to log the user in and out, as well the software does track IP addresses for the purpose of registration and use. We also use cookies to ensure that visitors are not repeatedly sent the same banner ads though our Open X Server.

Sniper’s Hide does sent out bulk emails from time to time in the form or Newsletters, Updates and Questionnaires. If you do not wish to receive these emails you can unsubscribe from within the email or you can uncheck the option to receive the emails in your account under the area of “My Stuff”.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent. We use your information only as described in the Privacy Policy above. We view protection of users’ privacy as a very important community principle. We store and process your information on computers located in the United States that are protected by physical as well as technological security devices. You can access and modify the information you provide us and choose not to receive certain communications by signing-in to your account. We use third parties to verify and certify our privacy principles. For a complete description of how we use and protect your personal information. If you object to your Information being transferred or used in this way please do not use our site or services.
