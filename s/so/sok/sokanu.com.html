Introduction

The Privacy Policy describes Sokanu's policies and procedures regarding the personal information and other data collected through the Sokanu Site. By using the Sokanu Site, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

When you create a Sokanu account, you will be asked to provide some personal information, such as your name, username, password and email address. Some of this information you provide during the registration process (such as your name, gender and the careers that you follow) will be listed on the Sokanu Site and accessible by the public.

The Sokanu Site is designed to connect individuals to careers and help them find their passion in life. Except as otherwise specifically stated in this Privacy Policy, the information you provide to us via the Sokanu Site will be made publicly available on the Sokanu Site. Sokanu will store metadata associated with the use of the Sokanu Site and may share this information with various third parties, such as the career contributors you follow and the Content with which you interact.

To better improve our Content and the Sokanu Site, we track some of the actions you take on the Sokanu Site, such as "following" a career portal or "commenting on" a post.

We use "cookie" technology to collect additional website usage data and to improve the Sokanu Site. A cookie is a small data file that is transferred to your computer's hard disk. Sokanu may use cookies to better understand how you interact with our Content and improve the Sokanu Site.

Sokanu uses a variety of services hosted by third parties to help provide the Sokanu Site and to better understand the use of the Sokanu Site. These services may collect information sent to your browser as part of a web page requeust, such as cookies or your IP request.

Sokanu uses certain third party social media web sites and services such as Facebook, on the Sokanu Site. Sokanu may collect information you have made available on these sites. In accessing these services through Sokanu, you are sharing information with those sites, and the information you share will be governed by their privacy policies. You may be able to modify your privacy settings with these third party social media web sites.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We use the information we collect to make the Sokanu Site available to our users, to measure the use of the Sokanu Site, to improve the Sokanu Site, and to provide you with customer support. We may share or disclose information for these purposes without prior consent.

We may share or disclose your non-private, aggregated or otherwise non-personal information, such as your public posts or the number of users who clicked on a particular piece of Content.

We may share transaction information with the trusted third parties responsible for such transactions, including donations made through Network for Good. The beneficiaries of said donations will be notified of your donation transaction.

We may disclose your information if we believe that it is reasonable necessary to comply with a law, regulation or legal request; to protect the safety of any person; to address fraud, security or technical issues; or to protect Sokanu’s rights or property.

Registered users are encouraged to keep their information current and up to date. The Sokanu Site provides users with the tools to access or modify personal information provided to Sokanu via the Sokanu Site.

Third Party Sites and Services

The terms of this Privacy Policy apply only to information that is collected through the Sokanu Site. The Sokanu Site may contain links to third party sites and/or services, some of which may collect personal data and/or other information from users. Sokanu is not in control of or responsible for the privacy practices of those third party sites and/or services. Users are advised to contact the third parties directly for information about their privacy policies.

Children

The Sokanu Site is intended for users who are at least 13 years of age. Users who are under the age of 13 are not permitted to use the Sokanu Site or provide information to the Sokanu Site.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

The Sokanu Site may change from time to time. As a result, Sokanu may need to make changes to this Privacy Policy.

Sokanu reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy without prior notice. Your continued use of the Site after any changes or revisions to this Privacy Policy indicate your agreement with the terms. This Privacy Policy was last updated as of the date indicated above. Please check back and review this Privacy each time before you provide information to the Sokanu Site.

Contacting Us

If you have any questions or concerns about the online privacy statement for this site or its implementation you may contact us here.
