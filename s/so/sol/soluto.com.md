# Privacy Policy | Soluto – Anti Frustration Software

Thank you for visiting the Soluto site at soluto.com (the “ **Site** ”).

**Soluto's mission is to analyze what goes on under the hood of our users' PCs, let them know what slows them down, and recommend the necessary actions.**

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains our information practices in connection with your personal information. 

Please read the full terms of this Privacy Policy below, as they govern the privacy issues in connection with the Soluto service. For your convenience, here are some key points:

• You are not required to provide any personal information to use the Site.

• Soluto's software application collects anonymous technical information from your PC so that we may provide actionable recommendations to our users. The information collected is not related to your Personal Information. The information collected includes, for example, the behavior of software applications on your PC and your installation and uninstallation of applications on your PC. 

• The Site is for those over 13.

This Privacy Policy forms part of the Terms of Use and End User License Agreement; unless there are other agreements, they constitute the entire agreement between You and Soluto regarding Your use of the Site and Soluto’s Software. 

**Introduction**

We are committed to respecting your privacy and recognize your need for appropriate protection and management of any personally identifiable information you share with us. 

By accessing and using this Site and its services as may be offered from time to time, including downloading and use of Soluto's software (collectively, "Software") you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy and to Soluto's processing of Personal Information as described herein. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, please do not use our Site. We reserve the right, at our discretion, to change this Privacy Policy at any time, which change will be effective 10 days following the modification. Your continued use of our Site following the posting of changes to these terms or sending you an email means you accept these changes.

For the purpose of this Policy: 

• **“Personal Information”** means your email address and any information that may be used to identify an individual. 

• **"Anonymous Information"** means information which does not enable identification of an individual user, such as aggregated information about the use of our Site and Software. 

**The Way We Use and Collect Personal Information**

We do not require you to provide Personal Information in order to have access to information available on our Site. However, if you want to receive updates, a newsletter or news regarding our services, you must provide us with your email address. In the event that Soluto is over capacity and at that time the user cannot download the Software, Soluto will show a screen informing the respective user that the Software cannot be downloadable at the moment and offer such respective user to input an email address so Soluto will inform him by email once the Software can be downloadable. Under the email input box the user can also sign up for receiving updates, a newsletter or news regarding the Soluto's services, in which case such user will be included in the Soluto mailing list.

You may easily remove yourself from our mailing list. Each email you will receive from us will contain clear instructions on how to remove yourself.

Soluto collects Anonymous Information in the course of the use of the Software by users. This Anonymous Information may include information regarding products installed and/or being used on the PC, usage level of each installed product, performance data, ISP name, connection type, City, Country, other service providers you use, etc. Soluto may further collect Anonymous Information about the time, type and manner of use of the Software. This information may include, for example, information regarding the IP Address and Geo-IP parameters, time of usage, performance of your browser, various applications on your computer, certain web applications used by your computer, usage patterns of applications on your computer, socio-demographic information, all of which are created in the process of using the Software, and other information regarding the use of the Software. 

**Soluto's Wiki Service**

Users who participate in the Soluto wiki service may associate their email address with their submissions . In the future, you may, if you wish, also associate your actual name/user name with your submissions. Please note that if you do so, then all of your actions on the wiki service (future and past), such as voting or submitting comments, will be linked to your actual name/user name 

Please note that if you publish your contact information (by, for example posting your contact information in the wiki service), you may receive unsolicited messages from users and visitors of the wiki service. 

**The Way We Use Personal Information and Anonymous Information**

We will only send you additional information about the Software if you give us explicit permission to do so. 

Your privacy is important to us and we are committed to protecting your privacy. However, in the following extreme and unlikely events we may disclose your email and other Personal Information to the extent required if we have a good faith belief that disclosure of such information is required or reasonably necessary to: i) comply with any applicable law, regulation, legal process or governmental request, ii) enforce our Terms of Use including investigations of potential violations thereof, iii) detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, or security issues, or iv) protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of Soluto, its users, yourself or the public. 

Soluto may use Anonymous Information for the following uses, and other uses which it considers reasonable: 

• To improve our Software and enhance your experience with the Site, wiki service and Software.

• To perform analysis that will result in actionable recommendations for our users.

**Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies**

Some of our Site pages utilize "cookies" and other tracking technologies. A "cookie" is a small text file that may be used, for example, to collect information about Site activity. Most browsers allow you to control cookies, including whether or not to accept them and how to remove them. 

Soluto may use cookies to monitor potential fraud or use which is not in accordance with its Site's Terms of Use. You may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie, or you may choose to block cookies with your browser. 

**Collection of Information by Third-Party Sites**

Our Site may use a reputable third party to present or serve advertisements that you may see at our Site. These third party ad servers may use cookies, web beacons, clear gifs or similar technologies to help present such advertisements, and to help measure and research the advertisements' effectiveness. The use of these technologies by these third party ad servers is subject to their own privacy policies and is not covered by our Privacy Policy.

**Links to Other Sites**

Our Site may contain links to other sites (including to the Get Satisfaction website). Other sites may also reference or link to our Site. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such other sites.

**Children's Privacy**

The Site is not structured to attract children under the age of 13. Accordingly, we do not intend to collect Personal Information from anyone we know to be under 13 years of age. 

**In the Event of Merger or Sale**

In the event that Soluto is acquired by or merged with a third party entity, we may transfer or assign your email as part of such transaction. 

**Commitment**

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Protecting your privacy is an evolving area, and Soluto is constantly evolving to meet these demands. If you have any comments or questions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@soluto.com. **While we cannot guarantee privacy perfection, we will address any issue to the best of our abilities as soon as possible.**
