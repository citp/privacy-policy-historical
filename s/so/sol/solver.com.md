# Frontline Systems Privacy Policy

|   | 

  
We are strong proponents of privacy on the Internet, as well as effective use of the Internet to provide you with the information you want. We want you to understand what information about you does (and does not) become available to us when you communicate with us over the Internet; what we do (and don't do) with this information; and how you can control what happens to the personal information we may collect.

This Privacy Policy Statement was prepared in accordance with guidelines from the [Direct Marketing Association](http://www.the-dma.org/) \-- but we have gone further, seeking to explain exactly when and how information about you may be collected. If you have any questions about our policies, please contact us at the address, phone, fax, or email below, or you use our [Contact Page](https://web.archive.org/web/20080616072058id_/http%3A//www.solver.com/contact.htm).
