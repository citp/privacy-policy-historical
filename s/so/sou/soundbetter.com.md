# SoundBetter Privacy Policy | SoundBetter

### OUR PRIVACY COMMITMENT

Effective date: November 20th, 2018

Thank you for visiting https://soundbetter.com, a website created and operated by SoundBetter, Inc. (“SoundBetter,” “we,” “us” or “our”) as well as any subdomains operated by SoundBetter. SoundBetter is committed to treating the information of our users with respect and sensitivity. Our privacy commitment is described in this Privacy Policy. As our services evolve and we perceive the need or desirability of using information collected in other ways, we may from time to time amend this Privacy Policy. We post the effective date of our Privacy Policy on our website (“Site”) and within the services we provide through other connected devices (“Services”). The Site and Services shall be hereinafter referred to collectively as the “Platform.” We encourage you to check the Platform (but especially the Site) frequently to see the current Privacy Policy and Terms of Use in effect and any changes that may have been made to them.

If we make material changes to this Policy, we will post the revised Policy and the revised effective date on the Platform. Please check back here periodically or contact us at the address listed at the end of this Policy if you have questions about our Privacy policy. This Policy is part of our Terms of Use Agreement.

For purposes of data protection laws, SoundBetter, Inc., located 232 3rd St. Suite E302A, Brooklyn, NY, 11215 is the “data controller” of your information.

### General Information

This Privacy Policy applies to your use of the Platform, and any other websites, applications, or services provided, owned, or operated by SoundBetter. By providing information to us or by using our Platform, you explicitly agree to our collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. The provisions contained herein supersede all previous notices or statements regarding our privacy practices with respect to our Platform.

We believe that you have the right to understand how your information is stored and used. We require that sites linked to our Platform contain a notice identifying their privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third parties or the content of linked sites, although we do encourage you to read the applicable privacy policies and terms and conditions of such parties or web sites.

### Information You Provide Us

In general, you can visit portions of https://soundbetter.com without providing us any information about yourself directly. If you wish to obtain additional information about our products or services not on our Site or otherwise available on the Platform you can contact us as set forth below.

Portions of https://soundbetter.com are accessible only to signed-up users. In order to register on our Platform and become a user you will be required to provide information such as your name, address, telephone number, password, email address, device information, and payment information. You agree that you will only provide current, accurate information and that you will keep your information up to date. In the course of your use of the Platform we may collect information based on the pages you visit, the activities or transactions you engage in or perform, and statistics about your usage and performance.

Please note that in some cases, we need certain types of information so that we can provide you with the services you request. If you do not provide us with such information, or ask us to delete it, you may not be able to obtain these services.

### Use of Information

We may use aggregated data (independent of any personal identifiers) for research and commercial purposes. This information may include what areas our users visit most frequently and what services they access most often. This information helps us develop, improve and maintain our Platform so that it is more useful to and accessible by you.

We may use information you provide to us, as aggregated to protect your privacy, internally for our general commercial purposes including, among other things, to offer our own, our affiliates’ or third-party products and services that we think you may find of interest, but only SoundBetter, its subsidiaries and contractors involved in distributing the offers or providing the products or services will have access to your information. Our subsidiaries and contractors will be required to use any personally identifiable information we provide them only for that intended purpose. We may use your email address to contact you to respond to your inquiries or to provide information on products or services to you. You can elect not to receive emails from us either by “unsubscribing” to an email you receive or by contacting us as indicated below. If you unsubscribe we may still send you emails relating to your account or transactions with SoundBetter. We also use your information to operate, maintain, and improve the Platform and for our internal recordkeeping purposes.

We may reveal personally identifiable information about you to unaffiliated third parties: (1) if you request or authorize it; (2) if the information is provided to help complete a transaction or to use our Platform; (3) if the information is provided to comply with the law, applicable regulations, governmental and quasi-governmental requests, court orders or subpoenas, to enforce our Terms of Use or other agreements, or to protect our rights, property or safety or the rights, property or safety of our users or others (e.g., to a consumer reporting agency for fraud protection etc.); (4) if the disclosure is done as part of a purchase, transfer or sale of services or assets (e.g., in the event that substantially all of our assets are acquired by another party, user information may be one of the transferred assets); (5) if the information is provided to our agents, outside vendors or service providers to perform functions on our behalf (e.g., analyzing data, providing marketing assistance, providing customer service, processing orders, etc.); or (6) as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy.

### Cookies

We use cookies to personalize our Platform for you and to collect aggregate information about site and services usage by all of our users. A cookie is a text file that our Platform transfers to your computer’s hard drive for record-keeping purposes and to enhance the quality of your visit to our Platform. The cookie may assign a random, unique number to your computer or device. We use persistent cookies that remain on your browser after you leave the Platform as well as session cookies that expire when you exit or cease utilizing the Platform. Our cookies record “clickstream” information (data reporting the URLs or names of the pages on our Site that you have visited). We use persistent cookies, for instance, to remember you when you return to the Platform and to connect you and your user account activity. We also allow third parties to set cookies that help us maintain and operate our Platform or to help our Platform offer certain features that our unique to SoundBetter’s offering. We do not intentionally allow anyone else to access your cookies. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but doing so may limit system performance and may even cause certain features of the Platform to malfunction or not to work at all.

### Legal Bases for Use of Your Information

The laws in some jurisdictions require companies to tell you about the legal ground they rely on to use or disclose your “personal data” (as defined in such applicable laws). To the extent those laws apply, our legal grounds are as follows:

  * To honor our contractual commitments to you: Much of our processing of personal data is to meet our contractual obligations to our users, or to take steps at users’ requests in anticipation of entering into a contract with them. For example, we handle personal data on this basis to provide you access to or a an account to the SoundBetter Platform.
  * To enable and facilitate transactions and agreements between SoundBetter members
  * Consent: Where required by law, and in some other cases, we handle personal data on the basis of your implied or express consent.
  * Legitimate interests: In many cases, we handle personal data on the ground that it furthers our legitimate interests in commercial activities in ways that are not overridden by the interests or fundamental rights and freedoms of the affected individuals. This includes: operating our business and the Platform; providing security for the Platform, products, software, or applications; marketing; making and receiving payments; and preventing fraud.
  * Legal compliance: We need to use and disclose personal data in certain ways to comply with our legal obligations (such as our obligation to share data with tax authorities).

We will try to complete your request as promptly as possible. Any changes will affect only future uses of your information.

### Data Subject Rights

Residents of the EU can exercise certain data subject rights available to them under applicable data protection laws. Where such rights apply, we will comply with requests to exercise these rights in accordance with applicable law. Please note, however, that certain information may be exempt from such requests in some circumstances, which may include if we need to keep processing your information for our legitimate interests or to comply with a legal obligation. If these rights apply to you, they may permit you to request that we:

  * Obtain access to or a copy of certain personal data we hold about you
  * Prevent the processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes
  * Update personal data which is out of date or incorrect
  * Delete certain personal data which we are holding about you
  * Restrict the way that we process and disclose specific personal data about you
  * Transfer your personal data to a third-party provider of services
  * Revoke consent that you previously provided for the processing of your personal data



For more information on how to exercise these rights, contact us using the information in the “Contacting Us” section below. If applicable, you may make a complaint to the data protection supervisory authority in the country where you are based. Alternatively, you may seek a remedy through local courts if you believe your rights have been breached.

Where required, we will use appropriate safeguards when transferring your data outside of EU. For more information about these safeguards, please contact us as detailed in the “Contacting Us” section below.

### GDPR Compliance

If you are a user residing in the EU and covered by GDPR, we will provide a systematic approach to managing your personal information through our people, management processes and information systems by applying a risk management methodology that is consistent with and in furtherance of the fundamental principles of GDPR. For more information on our GDPR compliance program, feel free to contact us at privacy@soundbetter.com

### Protecting Children’s Privacy

The Platform is not designed nor intended for use by children under the age of 18 (“Minors”). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from Minors. If you are under the age of 18, please do not submit any information to us.

We do not knowingly collect data from European Union residents under the age of consent set by applicable data protection law. If we learn that we are processing data of such an individual, we will halt such processing and will take reasonable measures to promptly remove applicable data from our records.
