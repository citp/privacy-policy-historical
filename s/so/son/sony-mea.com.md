# Privacy Policy : Sony Middle East & Africa

Dear Customer,

This website (“Website”) belongs and operated by Sony Middle East and Africa FZE (referred herein as “Sony”, “we”, “us”, “our”). We at Sony value every person’s right to privacy and strive to protect your personal information. This website has certain features related to privacy and personal information that we want to share with you. Furthermore, in order to analyse how customers use our Website, and in order to provide more customised services and advertising to each individual customer, etc., we may use cookies and similar technology to collect certain information. Please read our Privacy Policy very carefully before using the Website.

Your access and use of the Website constitutes your consent to terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy.

In order to serve you better, we may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to periodically review this Web page so as to be informed of any changes to this Privacy Policy. 

## Personal Information

We don’t collect personal information on this Website, but should we decided to do so, we will do it only with your consent and knowledge. When handling personal information Sony shall comply with various laws and regulations relating to the protection of personal information. In accordance with Sony’s philosophy of being honest and fair, Sony is working to ensure the proper handling of personal information. 

## Cookies

Cookies and similar technology are the elements of data that can be sent to your browser. Your browser may then store them on your system based on the preferences you have set on your browser. Cookies, for example, gather information about your operating system including, but not limited to, browser type, and Internet Protocol address.

On our Website we occasionally use cookies and to enhance the information and services provided, and to make navigating the Website more convenient. The Website does not use cookies to obtain information that would allow it to identify you except in cases where you have agreed to provide such information beforehand.

You have the option to configure your internet browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it or delete it, if cookie has already been set. Further, you have the option to block all cookies. To learn more about how to reject cookies go to the help menu within your internet browser. If you experience any problems having deleted cookies, you should contact the supplier of your web browser.

Please note, however, that if you refuse or otherwise block cookies you may not be able to use all of the functionality available on the Web Site. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at [SonyCustomerCare.MEA@ap.sony.com](mailto:SonyCustomerCare.MEA@ap.sony.com)
