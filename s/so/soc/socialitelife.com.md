# Privacy Policy - Socialite Life

Protecting your private information is our priority. This Statement of Privacy applies to the www.socialitelife.com and Unimpressed Media, LLC and governs data collection and usage. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, unless otherwise noted, all references to Unimpressed Media, LLC include www.socialitelife.com. The Unimpressed Media, LLC website is a entertainment and lifestyle site. By using the Unimpressed Media, LLC website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

**Collection of your Personal Information**  
Unimpressed Media, LLC may collect personally identifiable information, such as your name. Unimpressed Media, LLC may also collect anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your age and gender. We may gather additional personal or non-personal information in the future.

Information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by Unimpressed Media, LLC. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. This information is used for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the Unimpressed Media, LLC website.

Unimpressed Media, LLC encourages you to review the privacy statements of websites you choose to link to from Unimpressed Media, LLC so that you can understand how those websites collect, use and share your information. Unimpressed Media, LLC is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on websites outside of the Unimpressed Media, LLC website.

**Use of your Personal Information**  
Unimpressed Media, LLC collects and uses your personal information to operate its website(s) and deliver the services you have requested.

Unimpressed Media, LLC may also use your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Unimpressed Media, LLC and its affiliates. Unimpressed Media, LLC may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

Unimpressed Media, LLC does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties.

Unimpressed Media, LLC may share data with trusted partners to help perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Unimpressed Media, LLC, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Unimpressed Media, LLC may keep track of the websites and pages our users visit within Unimpressed Media, LLC, in order to determine what Unimpressed Media, LLC services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within Unimpressed Media, LLC to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

Unimpressed Media, LLC will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Unimpressed Media, LLC or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Unimpressed Media, LLC; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Unimpressed Media, LLC, or the public.

**Security of your Personal Information**

Unimpressed Media, LLC secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure.

**Children Under Thirteen**  
Unimpressed Media, LLC does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of thirteen. If you are under the age of thirteen, you must ask your parent or guardian for permission to use this website.
