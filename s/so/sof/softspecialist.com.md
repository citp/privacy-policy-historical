|  |  Information collected through this website is strictly related to the purpose of providing the service to the visitors and is not subject of public disclosure.  
The website may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit it. These companies may use cookies to have non-personally identifying information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.   
Google, as a third party advertising company, uses cookies (DART cookie) to serve ads on the website.   
You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.   
Alternatively, you can adjust your Internet browser’s settings for handling cookies. At any time, you may, choose to disable cookies from your browser.  
Google uses the interest-based advertising system to show ads of interest to users, based on their previous interactions with advertisers, such as visits to advertiser websites. The user information collected through interest-based advertising is used in accordance with Google's privacy policies. It is not used to identify users personally and Google doesn't use personally identifiable information to show interest-based ads.   
In addition, users can see how ads are being customized to their interests via the Ads Preferences Manager, which they can find through the Google Privacy Center and most "Ads by Google" links seen in Google ads. Users can add or remove categories to improve the relevance of the ads they see or opt out of interest-based ads entirely.
