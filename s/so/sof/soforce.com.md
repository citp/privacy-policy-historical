# Privacy Policy | SoForce.com

## Privacy Policy

By using SoForce you automatically agree to our privacy policy.

The policy below sets out how we will use and collect your personal information.

**What information is collected?**

We reserve the right to collect, store and use information about your computer, visits to and use of our website, including your IP address, location, browser, referral, length of visit and number of page views;

**Using your personal data**

Any personal data submitted or collected on this website will only be used for the purposes specified and will never be given away, rented or sold.

We reserve the right to disclose your personal information, including the content of your emails, if required to do so in order to comply with any valid legal process or governmental request

We reserve the right to use your information for marketing and contact purposes.

**Policy amendments**

We reserve the right to update this policy whenever required without any prior notice.

**Third party websites**

SoForce links to many other websites which we have no control over and we are therefore unable to be held reponsible for any content or damage or loss of earnings caused by other sites.
