We may also send registered users of the Site email notifications notifying such users of any changes to the Privacy Policy. We may use such aggregate information to analyze trends, improve the services and Products we provide to our users, administer the Site and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.

Information Collection

Through the Site:
You can generally visit our Site without revealing any personally identifiable information about yourself. However, in certain sections of this Site, we may require you to complete a registration form. In the registration process, we will collect personally identifiable information such as your password, user identification name, and email address and any other information that you voluntarily transmit with your communication to us.

Through the Products:
Each Product contains a unique application number. The first time you use a Product, this unique application number and an activation time stamp is transparently collected by DeviceVM's servers. In addition, as part of the installation process when you first use the Product, you will have the option of helping DeviceVM improve the Product by authorizing DeviceVM to collect information about your use of the Product, including, without limitation, the number of times you use the Product or various applications included with the Product, the duration of use, how many times and the type of technical problems that occur during use of the Product and technical information about your computer and system software and the Product version you are using. Submitting such personally identifiable information to DeviceVM is voluntary and all information fields are optional, and we will only use such information as described in this Privacy Policy.
