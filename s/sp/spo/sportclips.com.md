# Sport Clips, Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains our use of any personal information you may supply us when visiting our stores or _[www.SportClips.com](http://www.sportclips.com/)_.

At Sport Clips, Inc. (“Sport Clips”) we recognize and respect the privacy of our clients and visitors. We do not collect personal information about visitors to this website unless it is provided voluntarily. Our goal in collecting personal information online is to provide you with relevant information that meets your specific needs and to ensure that your visit to our site is efficient and productive.

**Information you submit when requesting information about Sport Clips:**

We collect this information so that we may better tailor our response to your inquiry to meet your requirements.

**Automatic information:**

Sport Clips receives and stores certain types of information whenever you interact with us. We obtain this information when your browser accesses [www.SportClips.com](http://www.sportclips.com/ "www.SportClips.com"). This information is used to track the total number of visitors to the site and to specific areas of the site, and allow us to improve the effectiveness of the website.

**Sharing information:**

By providing information to Sport Clips, in salons or online, customers are providing their consent for Sport Clips to obtain and use their personal information as outlined in this privacy policy. Sport Clips will not release names, e-mail or mailing addresses, phone numbers, or any other client data to vendors, marketing groups, or any other third parties. If you send e-mail inquiries to Sport Clips, your personal information, including your e-mail address, will be used only to respond to your inquiry, and will not be shared with third parties. Such information may be retained for the purpose of tracking the types of questions received by Sport Clips. Please note that you are responsible for whatever material you submit and that you have full responsibility for the message, including its reliability, content, originality, and copyright.

**Agreement:**

By using this website, you signify your agreement with the forgoing Privacy Policy. We reserve the right at our discretion to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time. Your continued use of Sport Clips.com following changes to the foregoing terms signifies your acceptance of all such changes.
