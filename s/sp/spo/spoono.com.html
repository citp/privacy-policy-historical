COOKIES
Some areas of Spoono use cookies to help personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server (such as a file the keeps track of our poll). Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.
