SPORTSBET PRIVACY POLICY

This is the privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") of Sportsbet Pty Limited (ACN 088 326 612) ("Sportsbet"). Sportsbet is subject to the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

This Privacy Policy sets out how Sportsbet will deal with your personal information.


General

In using Sportsbet's website located at www.sportsbet.com.au ("Site") you agree to be bound by the terms of this Sportsbet Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") .

Sportsbet may review the Privacy Policy periodically and reserves the right to change the Privacy Policy at any time at its discretion. by posting such changes on the Site. Changes will be effected immediately upon notification on the Site. Your continued use of the Site following notification will constitute your acceptance of those changes.

Privacy Policy will not apply to websites which are linked to the Site and over which Sportsbet has no control.

In the Privacy Policy, a reference to “National Privacy Principles” means the National Privacy Principles contained in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).


Collection

Sportsbet only collects personal information ("Personal Information") from you:

(a)if you wish to subscribe to Sportsbet's weekly newsletter;
(b)to process your application to become a member of Sportsbet ("Member");
(c)to provide services to you as a Member;
(d)to identify you as a Member for security purposes;
(e)to maintain your Member betting account;
(f)to upgrade and enhance your experience within the Site or on the phone, or personalise information, services or products to tailor your needs;
(g)to create aggregate data about Members through demographic profiling, statistical analysis of the database to provide to potential stakeholders, and to allow for more efficient operation of Sportsbet business and;
(h)to determine Members' liability to pay goods and services tax and other taxes where applicable.

From time to time Sportsbet may use your Personal Information to inform you about Sportsbet's services or about, promotional activities which Sportsbet believes may be of interest or of benefit to you.

If you do not wish to receive marketing or promotional material from Sportsbet, you may contact Sportsbet at any time by sending an email to customerservice@sportsbet.com.au requesting that no further material be sent to you. Sportsbet will as soon as reasonably practicable after receiving your request remove your contact details from Sportsbet's marketing database.

If you do not provide Sportsbet with your Personal Information, it may be unable to provide you with its services.

Examples of the Personal Information that may be collected by Sportsbet include your name, address, email address, phone and fax numbers and date of birth.

By choosing to provide Sportsbet with the Personal Information, either by phone or via the Site, you consent to Sportsbet collecting and managing the Personal Information as contemplated by the Privacy Policy.Personal Information may also be collected by cookies when you use the Site. Cookies are pieces of data stored by your web browser on your hard drive which contain information relating to your use of the Site. When you access the Site, cookies may record details relating to your use of the Site, including what pages you view from the Site. Sportsbet does not use cookies to track your Personal Information and uses them for internal management purposes only.

Sportsbet will collect your Personal Information only by lawful and fair means and not in an unreasonably intrusive way.


Management of your Personal Information

Sportsbet will take all reasonable steps to ensure that the Personal Information which it collects, uses or discloses is accurately recorded and is stored in a secure environment which is accessed only by authorised persons.

If it is practicable to do so, and safe to the extent reasonably required to ensure compliance with any law, Sportsbet will destroy or permanently de-identify the Personal Information when this is no longer required.


Use and Disclosure

Sportsbet does not give, sell, rent, share or trade any Personal Information regarding its Members to third parties, except as set out in the Privacy Policy.

Sportsbet employees are subject to confidentiality agreements in respect of Members' Personal Information and trading behaviour.

By choosing to supply Personal Information to Sportsbet, you consent to Sportsbet using your Personal Information as set out in the Privacy Policy.

Personal Information collected by Sportsbet may be disclosed to a third party in the following circumstances:

(a)in order to provide you with services that have been requested by you or to meet some other purpose for which your Personal Information was collected;
(b)where, at the time of collection, Sports bet has informed you of the third parties or classes of third parties to whom your Personal Information will be disclosed;
(c)in order to enable Sportsbet to participate in activities with business partners and sponsors, who will be notified to you at the time your Personal Information is collected;
(e)where the disclosure is required or authorised by law or otherwise permitted under the Privacy Act;

Sportsbet will take all reasonable steps to ensure that any Personal Information which Sportsbet discloses to a third party is protected by that party in accordance with the Privacy Act.


Security of Personal Information

You acknowledge that no data transmission over the Internet is totally secure. Accordingly, Sportsbet does not warrant the security of any information which you transmit to it. Any information which you transmit to Sportsbet is transmitted at your own risk. However, once Sportsbet receives your transmission, Sportsbet will take reasonable steps to protect your Personal Information from misuse, loss and unauthorised access, modification and disclosure including password protected systems and databases and Secure Socket Layer ("SSL") technology.


Access to Personal Information

You may access the Personal Information collected by Sportsbet by following the “My Account” link on the Site.


Correction of your Personal Information

Personal Information can be updated by you in the "Account Details" section of the Site at any time after you log in. Personal Information can also be updated by calling Sportsbet's Customer Service hotline on 1800 990 907.

Details of your betting transactions can be also found in the "Bet History" section of the Member Services section of the Site.

If you believe that the Personal Information Sportsbet holds about you is inaccurate, incomplete or not current, please let Sportsbet know and it will correct it.


Transborder Data Flows

Sportsbet may transfer Personal Information to someone who is in a foreign country where:

(a)Sportsbet reasonably believes that the recipient of the information is subject to a law, binding scheme or contract which effectively upholds principles for fair handling of the information that are substantially similar to the National Privacy Principles; or
(b)you consent to the transfer; or
(c)the transfer is necessary for the performance of a contract between you and Sportsbet, or for the implementation of pre-contractual measures taken in response to a request by you; or
(d)the transfer is necessary for the conclusion or performance of a contract concluded in your interest between Sportsbet and a third party; or
(e) all of the following apply:
(i) the transfer is for your benefit;
(ii) it is impracticable to obtain your consent to that transfer; or
(iii) if it were practicable to obtain such consent, you would be likely to give it.
