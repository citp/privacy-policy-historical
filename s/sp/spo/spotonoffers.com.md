# Spotonoffers - Browse Our Range of Offers

We hold only a minimal amount of information on each registered client. In order to register, we only require name, email address phone number, address and password prior to sending any vouchers. 

We only hold information in order to allow us to send you vouchers and new offers. Registered users may update their account details online by following the relevant links from within their account page. 

Members basic related statistics may occasionally be exposed. 

• We may monitor traffic patterns in order to improve our services, to help   monitor purchases and to keep accounts up to date. 

• We do not send Junk Email or Spam. We may send emails with site updates   or offers. 

• We do not pass users details on to third parties, unless specifically requested   to do so by the users. 

• An exception to the above is that we may pass your details on to the   merchants or their agents for verification of transactions only and for fraud   prevention measures. These details passed on will not be used for marketing   or any other purposes. 

Users may request to see any information that we hold on them. This may include purchase information, account details, contact details and other information. Users may request this information by using our ‘[Contact Us](https://web.archive.org/web/20091231084802id_/http%3A//www.spotonoffers.com/contact_spotonoffers.html)’ form. There may be a small administration fee for using this service, which will be no more than £10.

It is possible that we will wish to use customer information in unanticipated ways, not stated in our Privacy Policy. In this event, we will contact individuals before using the data for new purposes, so that the individual users may opt-out if they wish. 

We aim to be very flexible and transparent in the use of users details. If users wish for their account to be deleted or amended in any way – or if they wish to prevent us from using their information for any purposes, either as initially stated or for any new purposes, then they may contact us using our ‘[Contact Us](https://web.archive.org/web/20091231084802id_/http%3A//www.spotonoffers.com/contact_spotonoffers.html)’ form and we will endeavor to comply with their wishes.

If any user feels this site is not following its stated information policy, then they may inform us through our ‘[Contact Us](https://web.archive.org/web/20091231084802id_/http%3A//www.spotonoffers.com/contact_spotonoffers.html)’ form. 

**Cookie Policy**

We recommend that all users enable cookies in their browsers in order to make full use of the site and in order to ensure that any purchases on third party sites are recorded. Cookies are small pieces of data or information that are stored by a users browser onto their hard-drive. The information cannot be seen by any other websites (so the information is secure) and is used by us to provide features to aid the use of our services. If users do not have cookies enabled, it is likely that third-party retailers will not record some of their purchases.
