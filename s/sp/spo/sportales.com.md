Any material changes will be effective only after Sportales will provide prior notice of the amended Privacy Policy at least 10 days in advance. Notice of the amended Privacy Policy may be given by general notice on the Web-Site ( [http://www.sportales.com ](http://www.sportales.com/)) (the “ **Site** ”) or by e-mail.
