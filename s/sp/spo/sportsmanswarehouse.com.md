# Privacy Policy | Sportsman's Warehouse

## **SPORTSMAN’S WAREHOUSE - PRIVACY POLICY**

### INTRODUCTION

Sportsman’s Warehouse (“We” or "Sportsman’s Warehouse”) respects your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit the website www.sportsmans.com (our “Website”) and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

**This policy applies to information we collect:**

  * On the Website;
  * In email, text, and other electronic messages between you and this Website;
  * When you interact with our advertising and applications on third-party websites and services.



It does not apply to information collected or processed by any other third party.

Please read this policy carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your information and how we will treat it. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, you are not authorized to use this Website. By accessing or using this Website, you consent to our practices as set forth in this privacy policy. This policy may change from time to time (see "Changes to Our Privacy Policy"). Your continued use of this Website after we make changes will be deemed to constitute your acceptance of those changes. Your use of the Sportsman’s Warehouse website indicates your acceptance of the provisions in this Privacy Policy.

### SUMMARY OF PRIVACY POLICY

  1. Sportsman’s Warehouse collects certain information about you either through your use of this website or through information you provide to us as described under " **Information We Collect and Use** " below.
  2. Sportsman’s Warehouse may share certain information you provide in connection with our website with carefully selected third parties as described under " **Information We Collect and Share with Others** " below.
  3. You can restrict our sharing of your information with others as described under " **How to Limit the Sharing of Your Information** " below.
  4. You can view and update certain information about you that Sportsman’s Warehouse maintains in its database as described under " **How to Update and Correct Personal and Account Information** " below.
  5. Sportsman’s Warehouse may send you email notices regarding products and services. If you wish, you can remove yourself from our email list at any time as described under " **Communications to Our Customers** " below.
  6. Sportsman’s Warehouse may change this Privacy Policy as our website evolves.
  7. Sportsman’s Warehouse is available to answer your questions about our privacy practices.



### PRIVACY POLICY

**1\. Information We Collect and Use

a. Personal Information

**

There are times when we collect information from you through our website. This occurs when you register to post comments, reviews, pictures, or other content on the Sportsman’s Warehouse website.

The information we collect on or through our Website about you (“ **personal information** ”) may include:

  * Information that you provide by filling in forms on our Website
  * Information provided when you register to use our Website or purchase products from us by which you may be personally identified, such as name, mailing address, e-mail address, payment information, or telephone number
  * Details of transactions you carry out through our Website and the fulfillment of your orders
  * Your responses to surveys that we might ask you to complete for research purposes



You also may provide information to be published or displayed on public areas of the Website, or transmitted to other users of the Website or third parties (collectively, “ **User Contributions** ”), such as (but not limited to) product reviews. By submitting any User Contribution, you grant us a license to use that content for our own internal purposes and to publish it on our Website AND RELEASE US FROM ANY AND ALL LIABILITY FOR THE CONDUCT OF ANY THIRD PARTY ARISING OUT OF THAT THIRD PARTY’S ACCESS TO YOUR USER CONTRIBUTION.

Personal information collected on our website is used in several ways:

  * to provide you with information or to ask you to participate in surveys about products, services, news, and events
  * to allow you to post comments, reviews, pictures, or other content on the Sportsman’s Warehouse website
  * to aggregate information in order to understand general user trends
  * to carry out our obligations and enforce our rights arising from any contracts entered into between you and us, such as (but not limited to) fulfillment, billing, or collection



**b. Usage Information**

In addition to personal information, Sportsman’s Warehouse also uses various methods to collect information about your device and behavior when you use the Website (“ **usage information** ”) including:

  * Referrers
  * Network information
  * Device information
  * Preferences (e.g. notification preferences)


  1. **Referrers -**  A "referrer" is any information passed along by a web browser that references a web URL that led you to the Website. Referrers are automatically collected by our web server and used by Sportsman's Warehouse to identify the websites referring traffic to our download pages, and to present tailored content to our potential customers. Referrer information is also tabulated by Sportsman's Warehouse on an aggregated basis to identify general trends and traffic patterns.  
From time to time, Sportsman's Warehouse may enter into agreements with content partners that require Sportsman's Warehouse to count the number of product downloads or product sales stemming from referrals from a content partner website. This information is collected, used, and aggregated by Sportsman's Warehouse to fulfill the reporting requirements of those agreements. This information may also be combined with referrer information and tabulated on an aggregated basis to identify general trends and traffic patterns.
  2. **Network Information -  **We collect certain information about the networks you use to connect with the Website. For example, IP addresses are used by your computer every time you are connected to the Internet. Your IP address is a number that is used by computers on the network to identify your computer so that data (such as the web pages you request) can be sent to you. We may also collect other information about your network, such as cellular network information, timing information, and other technical information, according to the settings on your device. These data are automatically collected by our web server.
  3. **System  Information -** System information we collect might include, among other things, MAC addresses, the type of web browser being used, operating system type and version, hardware platform make, model, or version, geolocation information, and CPU speed. This information is sent automatically by your web browser when you are connected to a website, according to your device’s settings, and is used by Sportsman's Warehouse only for broad demographic statistics. This information is used by Sportsman's Warehouse to identify broad trends and may be used to provide tailored content, or to format information appropriately for your computer system.
  4. **Cookies -**  We use cookies and other similar technologies to collect personal and usage information. A “ **cookie”** is a small text file that a website can send to your browser, which may then store it on your computer system according to the settings on your device. Websites, including this Website, may then read the information contained in that cookie when you access the Website in order to provide you with a tailored user experience, such as (but not limited to) by personalizing the content that you see. We may also use that personal and usage information to research and understand your preferences. Cookies can also store information from forms that you complete on our website to save you the trouble of re-entering this information.  
We treat any personal or usage information within or obtained from a cookie with the same confidentiality as the information we obtain when you purchase something through our website. Sportsman's Warehouse does not require that you accept cookies in order to use the Website. You can set your browser to accept, reject, or notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it. If you disable or refuse cookies, please note that some parts of this site may become inaccessible or not function as intended.
  5. **Do Not Track Signals -** Most web browsers can be configured to automatically transmit a "Do Not Track" signal to websites and online services that you visit. There is no consensus among industry participants as to what "Do Not Track" means in this context. Like many websites and online services, the Services do not alter their operations when they receive a "Do Not Track" signal from a visitor's browser. To find out more about "Do Not Track", you may visit https://www.allaboutdnt.com.




**2\. Information We Collect and Share with Others**

At times, we must disclose relevant information about our customers to our business partners. For example, when customers use a credit card, the customer's credit card number is submitted to the credit institution for normal, routine processing. We might also need to disclose certain information to our contractors, so that they may perform services for us. Sportsman's Warehouse requires such contractors and business partners to keep your information confidential. Sportsman's Warehouse may disclose personal information to such contractors and business partners without additional notice.

Advertisements displayed on the Sportsman's Warehouse website might be delivered by an advertising management service. When you view advertising on a page of our website, an advertising service company might place a cookie on your computer. Sportsman's Warehouse does not provide any of your personal information to the advertising service without providing you an opportunity to opt out. You may disable cookies in your browser if you do not wish to receive cookies from advertising servers. Sportsman’s Warehouse, these advertisers, and other third parties may collect personal information from users of the Website over time and across different websites or services.

We may also disclose to others the information collected by Sportsman's Warehouse regarding your access and use of the Sportsman's Warehouse website, provided that it is that is de-identified and aggregated with other data.

We may also disclose your personal information to comply with any court order, law, or legal process, including to respond to any government or regulatory request.

**3\. How to Update and Correct Personal and Account Information**

We strive to keep our records of the information collected in the registration process accurate and we will promptly update our records when you update your information at our website. It easy and simple to update your information:

  * On every page in the top right is a "Login Status" control ("My Account").
  * If not signed in to your account, click "My Account" to log in.
  * Once signed in, your "Login Status" will now show you as signed in, there will also be links for editing your account information ("Address Book", "Email Address", "Personal Details", etc.) and logging out ("Sign Out") of the system.



To edit your account, click the appropriate section that you'd like to update. Once in the section to be updated, you can update or correct the information you have previously submitted.
