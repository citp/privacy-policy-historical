# Information Security Statement | Privacy Policy | Sports Handicapping | Free Football Picks | NFL Predictions

**Privacy Overview**  
Any information submitted to SportsCapping.com is considered confidential and is used only for analytical purposes.  This information will not be shared, bartered, or sold to any third party.  Information we retain is used to improve the quality of our site.  It will only be used to communicate with those visitors who wish to receive additional information. An anonymous visitor to our site will maintain their anonymity in all cases. SportsCapping does not, and will not, attempt to identify visitors if they do not voluntarily register within the site. SportsCapping strives to only provide links to reputable sites, but we do not have control the privacy of third party sites.  You are encouraged to review the individual privacy policies of any websites in question.

**E-Mail**  
When you subscribe to our newsletters, SportsCapping.com uses the e-mail address you provide to deliver information.  We will never sell or trade your e-mail address to any third party under any circumstance.  If you no longer wish to receive e-mail communications from our site, please use the unsubscribe link provided on every e-mail we send out.

**Personal Information**  
All person information you provide to SportsCapping is confidential.  This includes your name, address, phone number, and any other details you may provide via our website forms.  This information is never sold, bartered, or used for any purpose other than to improve various aspects of our site.

**Payment Information**  
All payments are submitted through a third party processor.  We do not retain your credit/debit card number, or any other payment-related information.  While SportsCapping.com only uses the most respected payment processors, we cannot be held responsible for the privacy of their websites.  We encourage you to examine their privacy policy if you have any questions about the information they retain.
