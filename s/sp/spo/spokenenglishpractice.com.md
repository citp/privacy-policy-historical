# Practice Spoken English On Skype With Native Speakers

This policy covers how Spoken English Practice uses your personal information. Spoken English Practice takes your privacy seriously and will take all measures to protect your personal information.

Any personal information received will only be used to fill your order. Spoken English Practice will not sell or redistribute your information to anyone.

Please note that Spoken English Practice is not responsible for any data that is collected by or sent to Skype and/or Microsoft. According to the Skype Privacy Policy, data transferred on Skype may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country in which Microsoft or its affiliates, subsidiaries, or service providers maintain facilities. The Skype website further states that “in this regard, or for purposes of sharing or disclosing data in accordance with this article 4, Skype reserves the right to transfer information outside of your country”. By using Skype software, you consent to any such transfer of information outside of your country. To learn more about the Skype privacy policy please visit  http://www.skype.com/en/legal/privacy. Spoken English Practice will not be responsible for any changes that Skype makes to their Privacy Policy.
