# Privacy Policy - Sports Unlimited, Inc.

This site is hosted by Yahoo! Store. Yahoo! hosts the store pages, ordering system, and order data. Yahoo! automatically collects order information but may only use this information in the aggregate. If you accessed this store through Yahoo! Shopping, Yahoo! also automatically collects information about your shopping experience. Please see the [Yahoo! Privacy Policy](http://privacy.yahoo.com/privacy/us/) for more information about how Yahoo! uses this information.

#### Our Privacy Policy

Sports Unlimited, Inc. values and respects your privacy.  Sports Unlimited, Inc. will not sell or give your e-mail address and other personal information to any other source, no matter the circumstance.  Personal information includes, but is not limited to, your address, billing information, e-mail address, etc.

We may occasionally request survey information from you via e-mail, such as your date of birth, current location, etc.  This information is provided on a strictly voluntary basis, and will be used exclusively by Sports Unlimited, Inc. for promotional purposes.  Any and all personal information you provide us falls under the umbrella of this policy. 

#### Security

Our encryption software ensures that your shopping experience with us is completely protected and secure.
