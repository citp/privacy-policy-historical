Sportsman's Warehouse - Privacy Policy

 

Introduction

This statement discloses the privacy policies and practices of Sportsman's Warehouse. Your use of the Sportsman's Warehouse website indicates your acceptance of the provisions in this Privacy Policy.

Summary of Privacy Policy

  1. Sportsman's Warehouse collects certain information about you either through your use of this website or through information you provide to us.
  2. Sportsman's Warehouse may share certain information you provide in connection with our website with carefully selected third parties, however we will provide you an opportunity to restrict our sharing of your information with others.
  3. You can view and update certain information about you that Sportsman's Warehouse maintains in its database.
  4. Sportsman's Warehouse may send you email notices regarding products and services. If you wish, you can remove yourself from our email list at any time.
  5. You can restrict our sharing of certain information about you.
  6. Sportsman's Warehouse responds to your privacy questions.
  7. Sportsman's Warehouse may change this Privacy Policy as our website evolves.

  1. Information We Collect and Use

    2. Information You Provide to Us

    There are times when we collect information from you through our website. This occurs when you register to post comments, reviews, pictures or other content on the Sportsman's Warehouse website.

    Personal identity information collected on our website is used in several ways:

    • to provide you with information or to ask you to participate in surveys about products, services, news and events
    • to allow you to posts comments, reviews, pictures or other content on the Sportsman's Warehouse website
    • to aggregate information in order to understand general user trends

    Information We Collect From Your Use of Our Website

    In addition to the information we collect in registration process required for you to post comments, reviews, pictures or other content on the Sportsman's Warehouse website, Sportsman's Warehouse uses various methods to collect certain other kinds of information, including:

    Cookies
    Referrers
    IP addresses
    System information

    None of these methods can be specifically identified with you with the exception of cookies.

    Cookies

    A "cookie" is an element of data that a website like sportsmanswarehouse.com can send to your browser, which may then store it on your computer system. It can be used to provide you with a tailored user experience. Cookies are used to better personalize the content you see. Some Sportsman's Warehouse pages use cookies. We may use the information to research and understand your preferences. Cookies will also store information from forms that you complete on our website. Next time you visit, the cookies will save you the trouble of re—entering this information.

    We treat any personal information in a cookie with the same confidentiality as the information we obtain when you purchase something through our website. Sportsman's Warehouse does not require that you accept cookies, however, and you can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it.

    Referrers

    A "referrer" is the information passed along by a web browser that references the web URL you linked from. Referrers are automatically collected by our web server and used by Sportsman's Warehouse to identify the websites referring traffic to our download pages and to present appropriate content to our potential customers. Referrer information is tabulated by Sportsman's Warehouse on an aggregated basis to identify general trends and traffic patterns.

    From time to time, Sportsman's Warehouse may enter into agreements with content partners that require Sportsman's Warehouse to count the number of product downloads and/or product sales stemming from referrals from a content partner website. This information is collected, used and aggregated by Sportsman's Warehouse to fulfill the reporting requirements of those agreements. This information may also be combined with referrer information and tabulated on an aggregated basis to identify general trends and traffic patterns. Information you provide to Sportsman's Warehouse, including personal information and referring URLs, is collected in our web server logs and customer database.

    IP Addresses

    IP addresses are used by your computer every time you are connected to the Internet. Your IP address is a number that is used by computers on the network to identify your computer so that data (such as the web pages you request) can be sent to you. They are automatically collected by our web server.

    System Information

    System information we collect might include, among other things, the type of web browser being used, the operating system/platform, and CPU speed. This information is sent automatically by your web browser when you are connected to a website and is used by Sportsman's Warehouse only for broad demographic statistics. This information is used by Sportsman's Warehouse to identify broad trends and may be used to provide information appropriate for your computer system.

  2. Information We Collect and Share with Others

    3. At times, we must disclose relevant information about our customers to our business partners. For example, when customers use a credit card, the customer's credit card number is submitted to the credit institution for normal, routine processing. We might also need to disclose certain information to our contractors, so that they may perform services for us. Sportsman's Warehouse requires such contractors and business partners to keep your information confidential. Sportsman's Warehouse may disclose personal information to such contractors and business partners without additional notice.

    Advertisements displayed on the Sportsman's Warehouse website might be delivered by an advertising management service. When you view advertising on a page of our website, an advertising service company might place a cookie on your computer. Sportsman's Warehouse does not provide any of your personal information to the advertising service without providing you an opportunity to opt out. You may disable cookies in your browser if you do not wish to receive cookies from advertising servers.

    We may also disclose to others the information collected by Sportsman's Warehouse regarding your access and use of the Sportsman's Warehouse website, provided that it is that is de—identified and aggregated with other data.

  3. How to Update and Correct Personal and Account Information

    4. We strive to keep our records of the information collected in the registration process accurate and we will promptly update our records when you update your information at our website. It easy and simple to update your information:

    • On every page in the top right corner is a "Login Status" control.
    • If not signed in to your account, click "Login" to sign in.
    • Once signed in, your "Login Status" will now show you as signed in, there will also be a link for editing your account information ("Edit Profile") and signing out ("Logout") of the system.

    To edit your account click "Edit Profile". Once in the Edit Profile section you can update and/or correct the information you have previously submitted.

  4. Communications to Our Customers

    5. From time to time, Sportsman's Warehouse may provide information and special offers to you. We will communicate with you by email unless you tell us you do not wish to receive emails from us.

  5. How to Limit the Sharing of Your Information

    6. If you do not want us to share your personal information with third parties, you may elect to restrict us from sharing that information.

  6. Contact Us

    7. Please contact us directly at customerservice@sportsmanswarehouse.com with any Privacy Policy questions or comments you may have.

  7. Governing Law

    8. This Privacy Policy will be interpreted and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Utah. Any action arising out of, or relating to, this Agreement may be brought in courts situated in Utah, and you consent to the jurisdiction of such courts.

  8. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

    9. Our use of the information you provide to us, or we collect about you, is subject to the Privacy Policy in effect at the time the information is provided or collected. However, Sportsman's Warehouse may change this Privacy Policy. You should check our website frequently for changes to our Privacy Policy.

    Sportsman's Warehouse will use reasonable efforts to comply with this Privacy Policy and will take prompt corrective action when it learns of any failure to comply with this Privacy Policy. We may not always catch an unintended privacy issue despite our efforts to do so. We welcome user feedback on any privacy concerns. In light of the above and because of the complex and constantly changing nature of our technology, our business and the law, Sportsman's Warehouse does not guarantee error—free performance under this Privacy Policy.

    Sportsman's Warehouse will not be liable for any incidental, consequential or punitive damages relating to this privacy policy. This Privacy Policy shall be governed by the laws of the United States and the State of Utah.
