# Privacy Policy | Free Backlinks | Free One Way Links | Spiderizon.com

#### Privacy Policy

As a condition of opening an account at Spiderizon, we require that you disclose certain information about yourself and your website. We use this information to uniquely identify you within our system and to refine our own internal marketing efforts. We also collect information about the topic of your website by virtue of the categories with which you associate and the descriptive text in your backlink templates. We may use this information to target advertising to your specific interests both on our website and in our email correspondence. We will NEVER disclose ANY information about you or your account to ANY third party without your full knowledge and consent.
