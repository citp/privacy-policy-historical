Location:

Device specific data:

  • When accessing Spinrilla from a mobile device (such as Android or iOS) our analytics provider and our advertising networks may collect your device identifier. A device identifier is a unique set of characters to associate a device with yourself. Please note that a mobile device identifier cannot personally identify yourself.
  • The IP address used to access the service.
Analytics:
  • Our apps and the Spinrilla website use tracking software to monitor it's visitors to better understand how they use it. The software will save a cookie to your computers hard drive in order to track and monitor your engagement and usage of the website but will not store, save or collect personal information. You can read Google's privacy policy here for further information http://www.google.com/privacy.html.

2. HOW WE USE THE DATA

The Spinrilla mobile apps may display advertisements from third-party networks. The third-parties will do their best to serve a relevant ad while respecting your privacy. Under GDPR, you have the right to opt-out of providing data to third-party advertising networks before any data is collected by those services.

Device specific data (such as operation system and version) is used for analytics and advertising purposes. Our third party advertisers may serve ads targeted at a specific device type. In addition, knowing what type of devices are accessing the Spinrilla Services helps us build a better experience for a broader set of device types.

Collected data may be used to prevent fraud and abuse of the Spinrilla service.

Collected data is sent to billing providers, services that maintain and monitor our application infrastructure, and analytics software. Spinrilla collects the minimum amount of information required to provide the products and services you've requested. How we collect and use your data is covered by our legimate interest.

5. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER GDPR

Under GDPR, you have rights including the following:

  • Know whether we store your data.

  • Request the data we hold be provided to yourself or to another service.

  • Not be subject to decisions based purely on automated processing.

Spinrilla does not sell, trade, or rent Users' personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners and trusted affiliates for the purposes outlined above.

7. PROTECTING YOUR PERSONAL DATA

We adopt appropriate data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction of your personal information, username, password, transaction information and data stored on our Site and Mobile Apps.

8. CONSENT

When using any of the Spinrilla Services you consent to the collection, transfer, manipulation, storage, disclosure and other uses of the data outlined in this Privacy Policy.
