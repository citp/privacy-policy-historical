# SpinTop Games - Download Free Games

| 

SpinTop Media, Inc (SpinTop). is dedicated to providing you a safe and secure online environment for playing, downloading and purchasing games. We do not share your personal information with third parties. All of our newsletter emails have clear instructions on how to remove your email address from our mailing lists. Enjoy our games. 

### THIS POLICY MAY CHANGE FROM TIME TO TIME, SO PLEASE CHECK BACK FOR UPDATES OR CHANGES.

  * ### 1\. Collection of Personal Information.

    * | 1.1.| Customer Information: SpinTop does not sell or share your Personal Information with third-parties. SpinTop may sell or transfer Personal Information to third parties in connection with any merger, acquisition, reorganization, change in capital structure, sale of all or substantially all of its assets. When purchasing games from us, or from one of our ecommerce partners, your Personal Information is encrypted. We do not store your complete credit card information for your personal protection. We may transfer Personal Information to third parties, who are under contract, as necessary to provide a product or service that an individual purchased or ordered from SpinTop.  
---|---  
    * 1.2.| Newsletter: If a user wishes to end their email subscription, they need to follow the instructions at the end of each email message to unsubscribe, or users can unsubscribe from our "opt-out" newsletter at any time by emailing us at unsubscribe@spintop-games.com. Please allow up to 1 week to be removed from our email newsletter.  
---|---  
    * 1.3.| Internet Information: SpinTop's web sites and purchase processes may record your Internet Protocol (IP) address in connection with your general activity or game purchase. Our web site servers or third-party advertisers may use industry standard cookies to track general usage or to track general statistics about users and the pages they visit for the purpose of delivering the appropriate advertisements.  
---|---  
  * ### 2\. Encryption of Information/Security

    * 2.1.| Security: SpinTop stores Personal Information and uses industry standard encryption technologies to reasonably protect your Personal Information and to keep it safe and secure. Notwithstanding such safety measures, SpinTop cannot guarantee that its security measures will prevent our database records or computers from being illegally accessed, and the Personal Information on them stolen or altered.
