# Privacy Policy | Spirit Science and Metaphysics

At Spirit Science and Metaphysics, your privacy is very important to us.  The personal information we collect is safe, and we never sell this information to anyone.  We would like to tell you that we collect your personal information in the following ways -

1) We collect information about your Web Browser, IP, ISP, the time and date of your visit. This is standard procedure and all websites do the same.

2) We use Cookies to know about your personal preferences while visiting this blog to provide a better user experience when you visit again.

3) In compliance with Google Adsense Terms of Service Policy 8, we should also tell you we use a Third Party Advertising network called Google Adsense.  This party may place DART Cookies in your Browser to know about your personal preferences to target the ads more efficiently.  In viewing this site, you acknowledge that you give consent to the storing and accessing of cookies, device-specific information, location information, and other information which enables Google to give you the best experience to you and its advertisers. If you do not consent to this and feel that you want to opt out of this, then you can do so here -  <http://www.google.com/privacy/ads/>
