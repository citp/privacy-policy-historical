Privacy Policy

Spine-health.com is committed to maintaining the privacy of your online information.

Spine-health.com does not sell or disclose any personally identifiable information and does not use "cookies" or similar methods to collect personally identifiable information about you. We only collect personally identifiable information about you if you voluntarily provide it to us, for example, when you register on the site and provide your name and e-mail for us to forward the newsletter to you.

We do collect aggregate information about visitors to Spine-health to better understand how Spine-health visitors find the site and other statistical or background information, and this information may be shared with our advisors or others on an aggregate basis. No personally identifiable information is shared at any time.
