# SpellChecker.net - Privacy Policy

To enhance your Web experience, this site uses anonymous identifiers ("cookies") to collect anonymous preference and interest visitor information. This information is provided to Flycast Communications Corporation. ("Flycast") and is used by Flycast to create anonymous interest profiles. This information does not include and is never merged with personally identifiable information (such as name, address or email address) or any information that is considered sensitive or controversial or that would compromise your privacy. Flycast profiles are accessed by Flycast-enabled Web sites and marketers to tailor site content, promotions and advertising, and to perform marketing research. Consumers can opt-out from these profiles at any time. For more information on Flycast, including your option not to have this information used for this purpose, go to [www.flycast.com](http://www.flycast.com/).
