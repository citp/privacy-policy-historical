Last Updated: March 21, 2010


Spacetime Studios recognizes that you care about the personal information that is held about you and how it is used. We are committed to respecting and protecting the privacy our users. The Site also stores and reads cookies to and from your computer. By using the Site, you agree that both the webserver, and any trusted 3rd party we chose, may place a tracking cookie on your machine and access it at any time during your session. In general, cookies are safe and are not a security issue when being used from 'trusted' internet sites, such as Spacetimestudios.com. For information on how to manage cookies in your browser, please visit AboutCookies.org


The Site will not sell, transmit or distribute any data held without your prior permission or consent, unless required to do so under relevant United States legislation. This information is edited by you via your Control Panel in the forums.


You have a legal right for the personal information held on the Site to be updated if it is incorrect or deleted if you choose. Most of this can be done through your Control Panel in the forums. If you are having issues with this, please contact us if you need help.


The Site will take all reasonable effort to ensure that all personal information about you submitted is maintained secure on our server. However, we make no warranty to this.

According to the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (also known as COPPA), our site does NOT cater to children. "Operators of commercial websites or online services directed to children under 13 that collect personal information from children" require a COPPA agreement and privacy policy. Since we do not cater to this age group, no COPPA compliance exists on the Site.
