Privacy Policy


SPREETS PRIVACY POLICY

Spreets NZ Limited (“Spreets”) collects your personal information for the purpose of registering you for Deals and for processing payment if a Deal you have purchased is released.

Information Collection and Use

Spreets and its parent company Yahoo!7 Pty Ltd (“Yahoo!7”) use information for three general purposes:

1.      to customise the advertising and content you see on our pages,

2.      to fulfill your requests for certain products and services, and

3.      to contact you about specials and new products. 

Neither Spreets nor Yahoo!7 will sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone. 

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Spreets and Yahoo!7 will only send your personally identifiable information to other companies or people when:

(i)      we have your consent to share the information;

(ii)     we need to share your information in order to provide the product or service you have requested (for example to Spreets’ payment processing company PayFlow);

(iii)     we need to send the information to companies that work on behalf of Spreets or Yahoo!7 to provide you a product or service. (Unless we advise otherwise, these companies do not have any right to use the personally identifiable information we provide to them beyond what is necessary to assist us.); or

(iv)      we respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process. 

The web sites you visit work with online advertising companies to provide you with advertising that is as relevant and useful as possible. Some of the online ads you are served may be based on the content of the web page you’re visiting; some others may be based on registration information you provide; and other ads may be customised based on predictions about your interests generated from your visits to other web sites. To learn more about relevant advertising click here. 

Cookies

Yahoo!7 may set and access Yahoo!7 cookies on your computer. 

Spreets and Yahoo!7 allow other companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. The way other companies use their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to Spreets or Yahoo!7's cookies. 

Yahoo!7 uses web beacons to access our cookies within and outside our network of web sites and in connection with Yahoo!7 owned products and services. 

Other companies such as web analytics partners, application developers and ad networks may use cookies, web beacons and applications such as widgets on the Yahoo!7 network of websites as described here.

Questions

Will my credit card be charged once I complete my order?

No, we're good people over here at Spreets. We only charge your credit card if the deal is ON, no earlier than that.

I'm confused... How do I know the Deal is ON?

A Deal is ON if the minimum number of people buy the deal. In this case your credit card will be charged and we will send you a confirmation of payment as well as your voucher via email.

Is it safe?

Absolutely! We are using a secure SSL connection for the transmission of your credit card details.
