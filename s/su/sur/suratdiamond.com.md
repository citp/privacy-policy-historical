# Privacy Policy

Surat Diamond Jewelry respects your privacy. We do not share or sell personal information about visitors and subscribers with other companies.  
   
You may choose to share with us personal information, viz., your name, mailing address and e-mail id required to correspond with you and ensure shipment of products. If you do not want us to use this information for further contact, we shall respect your wishes.  
   
Surat Diamond Jewelry may, if you so choose, send e-mail newsletters and direct mailers to you at the address provided. You have the option to opt-out of this activity by way of links provided in each newsletter or e-mail us to exclude you from direct mailers.
