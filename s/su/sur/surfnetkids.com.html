Privacy Policy

"Surfing the Net with Kids" respects your privacy and does not distribute, sell or publish any private information. The Surfnetkids newsletter subscriber lists (both the semi-weekly Surfnetkids newsletter and the occasional Surfnetkids Affiliate newsletter) are never rented or sold, and are used only to receive requested information directly from "Surfing the Net with Kids."

In compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998), newsletter subscribers under thirteen years old are asked to supply a parent's email address. Parents are notified via email of their child's subscription and parents may cancel their child's subscription at any time.

Opt-in customized mailing lists are managed by industry leader PostMasterDirect.com, who pledges that your data (email address and other demographic data such as zip code and year born) will never be revealed to the marketers who rent the Surfnetkids opt-in lists. Opt-in customized mailing lists are ONLY for those thirteen and older. To learn more about how PostMasterDirect.com (and their parent company Net Creations) assures your privacy, read Net Creation's Privacy Policy.

Demographic information is used only in aggregate to provide advertisers with more information about the "Surfing the Net" audience. Any email addresses posted on the site (in user submitted posts on the bulletin board) are optional and are posted ONLY with the user's permission.

IP addresses and cookies are used to accurately serve banner ads, to assure that when you click on an ad, you are sent to the correct site -- and to insure that you are only served one pop-up window offering you a free newsletter subscription. With cookies turned off, you will get the pop-up offer EVERYTIME you visit the site.

"Surfing the Net" uses WebTrends Live to analyze traffic to this web site. WebTrends Live does not create individual profiles for visitors. Unlike some tracking services WebTrends Live does not have a database of individual profiles for each visitor. WebTrends Live only collects aggregate data. For more information about WebTrends Live privacy policy, please click here.

"Surfing the Net" is not responsible for the content of sites outside of its network.
