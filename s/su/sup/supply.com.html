Privacy Statement

This Web site has security measures in place to protect from the loss, misuse or alteration of the material under our control.

When you browse our Web site, our system automatically makes a record of your visit and collects the following for statistical purposes. We use the statistical information collected to administer our Web site and gather broad demographic information. The following types of information are collected:

  • Your server address
  • Top level domain name (e.g., .com, .net, .gov, etc.

  • The time spent on individual pages and the site overall

We will not use your e-mail address for any other purpose, and will not disclose it to other persons or organizations without your consent.
