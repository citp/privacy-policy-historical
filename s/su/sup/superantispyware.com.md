# SUPERAntiSpyware.com - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

SUPERAntiSpyware.com is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. The information below outlines the information we collect, and how it is used to help better serve our users.

**User Information**  
We do not collect any personally identifiable information about you while providing content via our website.

**Non-Identifiable Information**  
We do collect non-personally identifiable information for statistical reasons only. There is no way to identify you by name, phone number or any other way using this information. All of this information is available to any website you visit, and is standard information collected.

This information includes:

**Operating system:** Windows, DOS, Macintosh, Linux, etc.  
**Browser Identification:** Internet Explorer, Netscape, Opera, etc.

**Use of Internet Cookies**  
We only use cookies to facilitate the placing of orders via our secure website. The cookies are called session cookies, and are removed once you complete or abort your order and leave the site. This information is never used to track you as a user or shared with any 3rd party.

**Purchasing Products**  
In order to accept credit cards over the Internet, and reduce fraudulent charges, we collect standard information including your IP. This information is only used in the case of fraudulent charges and is designed as an added layer of security and protection for our valued customers.

If you have any questions regarding the above information, please do not hesitate to contact us.
