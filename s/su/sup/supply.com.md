# Supply Technology_Privacy Statement

**Privacy Statement**

* * *

Supply Technology, Inc. has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to protecting personal privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this web site: www.supply.com

Our Web site does not use cookies.

This Web site contains links to other sites. Supply Technology, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices of such Web sites.

This Web site has security measures in place to protect from the loss, misuse or alteration of the material under our control.

When you browse our Web site, our system automatically makes a record of your visit and collects the following for statistical purposes. We use the statistical information collected to administer our Web site and gather broad demographic information. The following types of information are collected:

  * Your server address 
  * Top level domain name (e.g., .com, .net, .gov, etc. 
  * Date and time of your visit 
  * Which pages are accessed 
  * The time spent on individual pages and the site overall 
  * Which files are downloaded 



We do not record your user identity or e-mail address unless you choose to send us a message. In that event, your e-mail address will only be used for the purpose for which you provided it, and you will not be added to any mailing list unless you ask for this to be done. We will not use your e-mail address for any other purpose, and will not disclose it to other persons or organizations without your consent.
