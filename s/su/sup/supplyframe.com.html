PRIVACY & SECURITY

SupplyFrame, Inc. is committed to the protection of personal and company information obtained in the process of using this site. Any information that can identify you personally or your company will never be sold, nor will it be distributed to any third party not directly associated with your business unless you provide your approval.

Our privacy policy is as follows:
  1. We disclose our privacy policy and procedures in this document.
  2. We do not sell your corporate identifiable information to any third party.
  3. We do not sell your personally identifiable information to any third party.
  4. We use industry standard data encryption and storage protection.

In order to utilize our application, we require that you provide various corporate data. This information is captured via a secure web page (the application page) and is encoded using 128-bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption provided by VeriSign before being transmitted over a secure, dedicated line to our processing center.

This data is shared with only three entities: a) SupplyFrame, Inc., for the purpose of providing customer service, b) your vendors which you specifically identify and c) potential vendors for which you have provided specific permission. Your corporate information may be aggregated and used to compile industry-wide data.

Additionally, we aggregate non-personally identifiable information, such as your computer's operating system, web browser and screen resolution, so that we can tailor the design and functionality of this site to suit the needs of our users. No cookies or any other site-to-terminal information collection mechanisms are used on this site.

All corporate identifiable information is stored on data servers at an off-site, high-security facility. These servers are key-locked and accessible only to specifically authorized personnel within the facility. All data transmitted between this facility and other users is encrypted. All data files are password protected and not accessible from any dial-up or external Internet connection.

We are confident of our system's ability to protect all transactions; however, this is not an invitation to attempt unauthorized access to the system. This is a private computing system which is restricted to use by our registered users only. Actual or attempted unauthorized use of this computer system may result in criminal and/or civil prosecution.
