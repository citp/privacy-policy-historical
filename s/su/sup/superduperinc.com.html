Super Duper Inc. utilizes temporary Cookie technology for web site traffic analysis such as the time/date of the visit, the time/date of last visit, the page viewed, the referrer, transaction information for eCommerce pages, and other data. Super Duper Inc. utilizes persistent Cookie technology for optional account log-in. Super Duper Inc. tracks click behavior in the emails it sends out.
