Pereless Systems is committed to honoring the privacy of our users. We strive to provide a safe, secure user experience. This Privacy Statement sets forth the online data collection and usage policies and practices that apply to our www.pereless.com Web site. By using the Pereless Systems Web site, you consent to the policies and practices described in this Statement.

Your data will be stored and processed in whole in the United States. If you access the Pereless Systems Website outside the United States, your usage of that site constitutes consent to the transfer of your data out of your country and to the United States.

Information that is gathered on the Pereless Systems site: Pereless Systems will not sell, share, or rent any customer information that is contained on the site.

Information that is gathered from Registration Forms:
Pereless Systems gathers contact information from certain registration and seminar forms. The contact information that is gathered is for the sole purpose of corresponding with the parties that have expressed interest In the Pereless products or seminars.

Email links on the Pereless Website :
The Pereless Systems Website contains links for our users to contact us directly. We use the information provided to respond directly to any issues or comments. In some cases we may store your comments in order to improve the system.

Security :
Pereless Systems has implemented reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to secure your personal information from accidental loss and from unauthorized access, use, alteration or disclosure. However, we cannot guarantee that unauthorized third parties will never be able to defeat those measures or use your personal information for improper purposes. This website takes every precaution to protect our users' information. When users submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected as stated above both online and off-line.
All of our users' information, not just the sensitive information mentioned above, is restricted in our offices. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, a customer service representative or sales representative) are granted access to personally identifiable information.

We welcome your comments concerning this Privacy Statement
