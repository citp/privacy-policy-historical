# Subeta - Privacy Policy

**Cookies**  
www.subeta.net uses Cookies to store encrypted user information. No personal information is gathered through these cookies

**Logging**  
IP Addresses are logged from each logged-in user, for security purposes. No information gathered through this method will be given to anyone outside www.subeta.net

**Personal Information**  
All information collected from members of www.subeta.net are completly confidental, and will never be provided to any third-party website. This includes information collecting during the registration process, as well as any changes made to your personal data throughout the duration of your account. This also includes any personal information you disclose to a staff member via mail, or any other online form of messenging. 

**Changes to Privacy Policy**  
If there are any change made to the privacy policy, you are always given the option to opt-out, before you begin browsing the site again. If for any reason you feel that your personal information is not safe, you can have your account disabled at any time.
