Privacy Policy

SugarCRM respects your online privacy. We understand and appreciate your need to control your personal data that you share with us.

Notice and Disclosure

SugarCRM has established this Online Privacy Policy to make you aware of how we intend to treat your personal information.

SugarCRM collects individual information including but not limited to a first name, last name, physical address, an e-mail address or other contact information. In general, you can visit SugarCRM's web pages without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself.

If you choose to provide us with your personal information on the web, we may transfer that information within SugarCRM or to business partners for marketing purposes.

Choice

You may choose whether or not to provide personal information to SugarCRM. You have the choice to opt-out of receiving information from SugarCRM. If you choose not to provide the personal information we request, you can still visit most of SugarCRM's web sites, but you may be unable to access certain options, offers and services that involve our interaction with you.

If you chose to have a relationship with SugarCRM, such as a contractual or other business relationship or partnership, we will naturally continue to contact you in connection with that business relationship.

Data Security

Wherever your personal information may be held within SugarCRM or on its behalf, we intend to take reasonable and appropriate steps to protect the personal information that you share with us from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Data Accuracy

It is important to SugarCRM to maintain accurate data. We are working on ways to make it easier for you to correct the information that we obtain about you. If ever you'd like to change your information, please visit us at https://www.sugarcrm.com/compref.

Use of Cookies

A cookie is a small data structure that allows a web site to store information on your web browser. Cookies help identify returning users so that we don't ask your contact information with every visit. A cookie will contain the information you supply yourself. A cookie cannot read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites.

You can reject or accept cookies when your browser alerts you of its presence.
