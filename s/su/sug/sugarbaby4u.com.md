# SugarBaby4u Privacy Policy

## SugarBaby4u.com Privacy Policy

SugarBaby4u.com is committed to protecting your privacy. We are providing this notice to better protect your privacy and to explain the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. 

Your email address is simply used as a way to verify your identity and to communicate to you outside the site when you have received messages on this website. We do not sell or pass on your information to any third party source. 

Our Commitment To Data Security: To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information: You can change your email address by contacting us at [support@sugarbaby4u.com](mailto:support@sugarbaby4u.com).

Use Of Cookies: A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user and are commonly used by websites to make it easier to navigate through web pages on the site. Sugarbaby4u.com uses cookies to manage sessions on our site. Users must enable cookies on your computer to use our site. We do not use cookies to collect personal information from any member which they did not intentionally submit to us. 

To protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections.

How To Contact Us: Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please send us an email at [support@sugarbabt4u.com](mailto:support@sugarbaby4u.com).
