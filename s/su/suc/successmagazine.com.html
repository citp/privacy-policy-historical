Privacy Policy

 

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

In certain situations you may be asked to provide us with personal information. For example, to subscribe to the Success newsletter, you will be required to give us your name and e-mail address. From time to time we carry out surveys to help us serve you better. In this case you may be asked to provide information on a voluntary basis such as your name, address, e-mail, age, occupation and other demographic information.  Again, this information is requested on a voluntary basis.

WHAT DO WE DO WITH THIS INFORMATION?

Third Parties – Success does not sell, rent or give away your personal information to any third parties.

Surveys – the voluntary information provided may be used by us to contact you for marketing purposes.

Site Analysis – Success uses aggregated information about our visitors to help monitor our website’s performance and to help us improve our content and website usability. Success uses WebTrends Live to monitor traffic on our website. WebTrends Live collects only aggregate data, and does not create individual visitor profiles. Please review the WebTrends privacy policy at http://www.webtrendslive.com/privacy_policy.htm

Cookies – A cookie is a small data file that is written to your hard drive. Success may use cookies for navigation purposes and to avoid displaying material you have already seen. Success also uses a third party ad server that may use cookies to track ad impressions and click-through rates and to avoid showing you information you have already seen. You can change your browser preferences or options to provide a warning before accepting a cookie or to disable cookies.

Legalese – We may update and modify our Privacy Policy from time to time. Please check back regularly to see if there have been any modifications. By using our website or continuing to use the website after a modification of the Policy, you accept the Privacy Policy and any modification thereto.

Questions – If you have questions about our Privacy Policy, please send your inquiries to Webmaster@successmagazine.com.
