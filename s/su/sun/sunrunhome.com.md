# Privacy Policy - SunRun

This privacy policy applies to information collected on SunRun's website. 

Certain features and pages on this website ask for you to provide personal information which may include name, email address, address, or telephone number. 

We use this information to determine whether solar and SunRun's service plans are right for you or to otherwise respond to your request. Your privacy is extremely important to us. We will never give or sell your information to third parties without your consent. We do not sell personal information about you to unrelated companies for their independent use. 

Personal information about our customers or former customers will only be disclosed as permitted or required by law. Information that has been collected is securely maintained in your file in our records. 

##  Future Correspondence 

From time to time we may send you marketing, promotional, or educational information. At any time you may request to correct your personal information or request that SunRun delete your personal information and no longer contact you by email, phone, regular mail, or any other means. Simply email [info@sunrunhome.com](mailto:info@sunrunhome.com), call us at 877-786-6656, or submit your request in writing to 717 Market Street, Suie 600, San Francisco, CA 94108. You may also use the unsubscribe link at the bottom of our marketing and promotional email correspondences. 

##  Data Security 

SunRun uses standard security measures to protect information submitted on this site. We protect your information with the same degree of security we protect our own proprietary information. Information on the customer log-in area of our website is encrypted through the use of SSL (AES-256bit encryption). Our SSL certificates are signed by VeriSign, Inc. 

##  Third Party Sites 

SunRun's website provides sine hyperlinks to third party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites and encourage you to read the site's privacy policy before submitting any personally identifiable information. 

##  Future Changes 

By using this site, you agree to the terms set forth in this privacy policy. We may change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time. If SunRun changes its privacy policy, we will post the updated version of that policy here. Please check this page periodically for changes. Your continued use of this site following the posting of changes to this policy will mean that you accept those changes. 

We would be happy to address any questions or concerns you might have about this privacy policy. Simply call us at 877-786-6656, email [info@sunrunhome.com](mailto:info@sunrunhome.com), or submit your concerns in writing and mail to 717 Market Street, Suite 600, San Francisco, CA 94108.
