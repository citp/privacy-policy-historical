# Privacy Policy | SunglassWarehouse.com

The Site ("sunglasswarehouse.com") is owned and operated by One Click Internet Ventures, LLC. We created this privacy policy to demonstrate our firm commitment to protecting your personal information. You have our promise that we will not electronically process your personal information in any way that is incompatible with this Policy. By visiting Sunglass Warehouse or providing us with information, you are agreeing to the practices described below.

  


### Collection of Data

We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we reserve the right to release information on Internet Users if compelled to do so by proper judicial or governmental authorities. Disclosure shall be limited to only those to whom we are compelled to disclose to. All information processing currently only occurs in the U.S. In terms of our collection of your personal information, we only use anonymous data to report browsing, purchasing, and/or lead form completion activites.

This Site uses cookies to keep track of session IDs. A cookie is a small piece of information stored by a Web server on your computer's hard drive. Any cookie in use by our server is temporary and will be removed when the user logs out or is timed-out by the server. Additionally, we record IP addresses to analyze trends and administer the site. An IP address (Internet Protocol address) is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer by your Internet Service Provider when you connect to the Internet. IP addresses are NOT linked to any personally identifiable information.

  


### Information Other Websites Collect From You

We do link to Websites operated by other parties. Some of these Websites contain our brand names, trademarks, and other intellectual property that we own. When you click on these links and visit these Websites (regardless of whether or not they contain our brand names, trademarks, and other intellectual property), you need to be aware that we do not control these other Websites' business practices. This privacy policy does not apply to other Websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any Website you visit to learn how your information is collected and disclosed. As a reminder, we are not responsible for the actions of other parties.

  


### Updating this Policy

We will update our privacy policy when appropriate (if our practices change, as we change existing or add new services, as we develop better ways to inform you of products we think will be of interest, or if the law requires us to do so). Refer back to this page for up-to-date information.

  


### Contact Us

We are committed to our privacy policy. If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, please call us at 1-800-559-4209 or email us at info@sunglasswarehouse.com.
