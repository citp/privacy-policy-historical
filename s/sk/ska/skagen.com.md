Skagen Designs, Ltd. respects your privacy. We use information provided by you for the specific purpose for which it is provided: generally, to complete your on-line purchase. This includes information such as your name and address, billing information, your email address, your phone number, the products you are purchasing and other information you voluntarily provide (Personal Information). In order to do this, your Personal Information may be shared with employees, agents, representatives and third parties who are under an obligation to use the information only for the purpose for which it was provided.

We distribute Skagen product in certain countries via our sister Skagen companies and independent distributors. Information obtained from individuals in such countries may be shared with the Skagen company and/or independent distributor representing Skagen in such country. Information shared with distributors becomes subject to the distributorâ€™s privacy policies.

We (Skagen and its sister companies and distributors) may send you promotional emails and also may share your email address with reputable business associates who sell Skagen products or complementary products so that they may send you promotional emails and regular mails. If you do not wish to receive promotional mailings, please click [here](http://www.skagen.com/myAccount/profile.fx).

We also may track data about your visitation to our website. This may include the time of day and times you access our website, and what pages you visit on our web site (Visitation Data). Visitation Data may be linked with or collected by reference to personal characteristics, such as whether or not a visitor makes a purchase, but is not linked to data that identifies any particular visitor. Visitation Data does not include data that allows a user of such data to identify or contact individual users. Generally, we use the Visitation Data to track usage of our web site, and may share such aggregate data with other reputable businesses. 

We may use Personal Information and Visitation Data to determine to whom we should send specific offers and advertisements (unless you have indicated you do not wish to receive such offers). 

We may use "cookies" so that our web site recognizes you when you are a repeat visitor. You can set your web browser to disable cookies; however, this may impair your ability to utilize all features of our web site.

This web site may contain links to other web sites. Skagen makes no representation or statement regarding the content or privacy policy of such other web sites. You must review the privacy statement of such web sites to determine their policies. 

Our website is not intended for visitation by children (those under age 13) and we do not seek any personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has furnished Personal Information, we will delete such information from our files.

Skagen Designs has policies and procedures to protect and prevent unauthorized access to Personal Information. We or service vendors engaged by us strive to maintain the privacy and confidentiality ofyour Personal Information. However, we cannot warranty that your Personal Information will be free from unauthorized access. 

We reserve the right to disclose any information we possess where required by law.

We may change our privacy policy at any time, but such change shall not apply to information communicated prior to the time such change is posted on this website. It is your responsibility to check this privacy policy each time you revisit this site to determine whether this policy has been revised.

This Privacy Policy is dated November 4, 2009, and has not been revised since such date.

 **Credit Card Security**  
It is Skagen.com's goal to offer customers a safe and convenient shopping experience. There are multiple safeguards in use specifically to protect the security and privacy of your personal information. When you enter your personal information at Skagen.com, you will notice that Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology is in place, to protect your information as it is transmitted to us. To confirm this service on our site or any other, note the URL or browser location line. If you are currently on a secure server, the first characters of the address in that line should read "https" instead of "http". If for any reason you are not able to access a secure server, please contact our Customer Service Team at 1-877-SKAGEN-1.
