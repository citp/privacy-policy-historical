By using our web site, you consent to the collection and use of certain information by @Last Software. Protecting the privacy of User information is important to @Last Software and to the way we conduct our business. The following policy outlines our information gathering and dissemination practices for our Service. @Last Software reserves the right, at our discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time.

If we change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. You, the User, should visit this page from time to time to review the then-current policy because it is binding on you.

**@Last Software's Collection of Personally Identifiable Information**

@Last Software web site collects personally identifiable information that is knowingly provided by its Users. @Last Software uses such information only for its own internal purposes. @Last Software reserves the right to share this information with its affiliates, successors or assigns; however, we will not sell, rent or release a User's personally identifiable information to any other third party except as outlined in this Policy or under special circumstances, including, without limitation, to comply with the law or legal process. 

Occasionally, @Last Software uses customer contact information to send the User information about our company and the services that we provide. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the user when necessary. If you choose to opt out of receiving non-essential communications from us, please email us at [sales@sketchup.com](mailto:sales@sketchup.com). If you would like to review the accuracy of your User information, you may e-mail your request to [sales@sketchup.com](mailto:sales@sketchup.com).

**Links**

The @Last Software service may contain links to other sites. Please be aware that when clicking on one of these links, you are being transferred to another web site. We encourage people to read the privacy statements of these linked sites, as their privacy policies may differ from ours.
