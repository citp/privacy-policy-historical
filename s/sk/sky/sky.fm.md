# Privacy Policy - RadioTunes | Enjoy amazing Free Internet Radio stations

### Overview

We are strongly committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. The policies below are applicable to the [RadioTunes.com ](https://www.radiotunes.com/)website ("Website") our mobile and desktop apps (the “Apps”) and all related sites, players, widgets, tools, apps, data, software, APIs and other services (the “Services”) produced and maintained by Digitally Imported, Inc. (“DI”) (collectively "Company" or "we", "us", or "our") The Website, Apps and Services shall be collectively referred to as the “Platform” within this Privacy Policy.  Please be advised that the terms "Company" or "we", "us", or "our" also includes Radio Now, LLC which is an owner of certain branded channels operated by DI but that DI is the sole controller of all data collected to operate the Platform. The policies below are applicable to the Platform (however accessed and/or used), whether via personal computers, mobile devices or otherwise, and other interactive features, applications or downloads that are operated by us that are made available by us. We have established this privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") to let you know the kinds of personal information we may gather during your use of the Platform, why we gather your information, what we use your personal information for, when we might disclose your personal information, and how you can manage your personal information. Please be advised that the practices described in this Privacy Policy apply only to information gathered online via our Platform. It does not apply to information that you may submit to us offline, or to websites maintained by other companies or organizations to which we may link or who may link to us.  The Company is not responsible for the privacy practices of these other websites, apps and services and we recommend that you review the privacy policies of each of these websites, apps or services before connecting your Radiotunes.com account or sharing any personal data. By using or interacting with our Platform, you are accepting the practices described in our Privacy Policy and you are providing specific, informed and unambiguous consent to the use of your information in the manner set out in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use any part of the Platform. We reserve the right to modify or amend the terms of our Privacy Policy from time to time without notice. Your continued use of our Platform and our affiliate sites following the posting of changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes. If we intend to apply the modifications or amendments to this Privacy Policy retroactively, we will provide you with notice of the modifications or amendments. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or don't see your concerns addressed here, you should contact us by email at privacy@radiotunes.com.

### Information Collected and Stored

Company adheres to the highest standards of ethical practices in all of our operations and is dedicated to protecting the privacy of all visitors to our Platform. Our privacy policy is simple and transparent: except as disclosed below, we don't sell, barter, give away, or rent your personal information to any company outside of Company.

### Personal Information

#### Information we collect about you (hereinafter “Personal Information”)

We collect information about you from various sources:

#### Information provided by you

You do not need to provide us with any personal data in order to visit the Platform for a certain period of time (currently set at 30 minutes). However, after that period of time we require that you register for a DI account and, by doing so, you will be required to provide us with certain information:

  * Essential information: When you register any kind of DI account, you will need to provide your email address and choose a password.  When using as social app/network to sign up for an account on the Platform, your first name, last name, UID and profile picture will also be collected as part of the process.  In addition, if you purchase a Premium Subscription, you will also need to provide your billing address and payment verification information.
  * Profile information: There are no public profiles of our listeners on DI.  However, you have the opportunity to provide the following information in connection with your personal profile on DI: 
    * your real name;
    * a profile picture
  * In the event that we institute user generated playlists, a curator profile will be publicly displayed which will include your chosen name, other profile information and a brief content description that will be provided by you along with links to your other playlists on the Platform.
  * Note: we do not utilize User Names for the user profiles, we simply use your email address.



None of this profile information is mandatory for simple use of the service under thirty (30) minutes in duration.  After using the Platform for thirty (30) minutes, you will be asked to register an account and the information set out above will be required for its formation.  If you do not wish to provide this information, please do not set up an account with us.  Any information you do provide may be deleted, edited, changed or amended by you at any time within the setting tab in your account. 

  * Information from correspondence: You will provide certain personal data if you contact us by email, use any of the webforms on the Platform, or contact us by mail, fax or other offline means.
  * Survey information: If you participate in any survey, you will provide certain personal data as part of your response, unless you respond anonymously.
  * Information that you post: You may provide personal data when you post comments, or contribute to community discussions.



#### Information we collect automatically

There is certain information that we collect automatically as the result of your use of the Platform, or through the use of web analytics services as described in our Cookies Policy forming part of this Privacy Policy.  This information includes but is not limited to:

  * the Internet Protocol (IP) address of the device from which you access the Platform (this can sometimes be used to derive the country or city from which you are accessing the Platform)
  * the site that you visited immediately prior to visiting the Website
  * the specific actions that you take on the Platform, including the pages that you visit, streaming, connecting your Facebook or Google+ account, sharing links related to the Platform with a third party, following or unfollowing another user, following of content and/or channels, posting a comment, or performing a search
  * any search terms that you may enter on the Platform
  * the time, frequency and duration of your visits to the Platform
  * your browser type and operating system
  * the nature of the device from which you are accessing the Platform, for example, whether you are accessing the Platform from a personal computer or from a mobile device and its device-ID
  * your location data (e.g. IP address) when using the Apps,
  * information collected through cookies and similar technology, as described in our Cookies Policy forming part of this Privacy Policy.
  * information regarding your interaction with email messages, for example, whether you opened, clicked on, or forwarded the email message.

### Information from other sources

#### Facebook Connect

We allow you to sign up and log in using your Facebook account. If you sign up using Facebook, Facebook will ask your permission to share certain information from your Facebook account with us. This will consist of  your first name, last name, email address, profile picture and UID in order for us to verify your identity.  This information is collected by Facebook and is provided to us under the terms of [Facebook’s privacy policy](https://www.facebook.com/policy.php). You can control the information that we receive from Facebook using the privacy settings in your Facebook account. If you sign up to the Platform using Facebook, your Company account will be connected automatically to your Facebook account but information regarding your activity on the Platform will only be shared with Facebook if you provide instructions to do so by clicking on the required boxes to confirm any sharing of your listening choices.  If you signed up using Facebook by mistake, you can delete your account from the Account page within your settings at any time or you can contact us by email at privacy@radiotunes.com.

#### Google+

You can sign up and log in to the Platform using your Google+ account. When you sign up using Google+, Google will ask your permission to share certain information from your Google+ account with us. This includes your first name, last name, email address, profile picture and UID in order for us to verify your identity.  This information is collected by Google and is provided to us under the terms of [Google's privacy policy](https://www.google.com/intl/en-GB/policies/privacy/). If you sign up to the Platform using Google+, information regarding your activity on the Platform will only be shared with Google+ if you provide instructions to do so by clicking on the required boxes to confirm any sharing of your listening choices If you signed up to the Platform using Google+ by mistake, you can delete your account from the account page within your settings at any time, or you can contact us by email at privacy@radiotunes.com. Unless you provide comments to the Platform, submit information to Company, or register for updates from Company, we do not collect any personally identifiable information from you. If you submit comments or information or register to receive updates or send us e-mail, we utilize identifiable information about you that you have already provided to us (for example: in order to process a support ticket).  Further if you contact us via a mobile device, we collect automatically the Member ID, App version, device type and operating system version to assist with any issue.  That information is not stored in our data bases but is stored with our trusted third party support ticket handler and is subject to the terms of their privacy policy and terms of service.   If you have any questions about this entity related to your data, please contact us by email at privacy@radiotunes.com. We also collect and store information that you enter into the Platform or that you provide to any of our representatives, or in conjunction with requests for more information.

In addition, when you interact with the Platform, our servers may keep an activity log that does not identify you individually ("Anonymous Information"). ### Use Of Cookies And Spyware  

We never use or install spyware on your computer, nor do we use spyware to retrieve information from your computer. Like many other third party websites, we use "cookies", which are small text files that are stored on your computer or equipment when you visit certain online pages that record your preferences. We use cookies to track use of our Platform and online services. We may also use cookies to monitor traffic, improve the Platform and make it easier and/or relevant for your use. You may occasionally get cookies from trusted third-party companies advertising on our behalf. We do not control these cookies, and these cookies are not subject to this cookie policy. If you do not consent to the use of cookies or other technologies, you have the ability to accept or decline them. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies or other technologies, but, if you prefer, you can usually modify your browser setting to decline them or you can use the tools we provide in your User Account settings to adjust your Ad Personalization under “Settings and Privacy”..   Your choice to use the Platform without blocking or disabling cookies or opting out of other technologies indicates your consent to our use of these cookies and other technologies and to our use, in accordance with this Privacy Policy, of any personal information that we collect using these technologies

### How We Use Your Information

We use the information that we collect about you for the following purposes: Use of and interaction with the Platform:

  * To operate and maintain your DI account, and to provide you with access to the Platform that you may request from time to time. Your email address and password are used to identify you when you sign into the Platform.   * To identify you as the creator of the comments that you post and/or the other contributions that you make to the Platform.
  * To seek your participation in surveys, and to conduct and analyze the results of those surveys if you choose to participate.
  * To provide you with technical support.
  * To respond to you about any comment or enquiry you have submitted.
  * To provide DI curators with data regarding people's listening behaviour of the Company radio shows, i.e. likes and dislikes but all anonymously.
  * To prevent or take action against activities that are, or may be, in breach of our Terms of Use or applicable law and to otherwise track your compliance with the Platform's rules and regulations.
  * To customize your account page: Any additional information that you provide as part of your Platform account profile, such as your real name, (but not your email address), will be published on your profile page. This information will NOT be publicly accessible.
  * To process payment for your subscription: If you purchase a Subscription, your name, address and payment verification information will be used to process your account subscription and renewals and to collect your payment. All payment verification information is transmitted using industry-standard SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption. 
  * To service your account: Your email address will be used to send you service updates and notifications regarding your account, and (if you have elected to receive them using your account setting) newsletters, marketing messages and certain email notifications. For information about how to change your settings, and to unsubscribe from newsletters, marketing messages and email notifications, please see the “Email Settings” tab in the “My Account” section of your account on the Platform. ** **
  * To help us personalize and continually improve your experience on the Platform.  We use the information from one website on other websites in our network of partnering websites which together constitute the "Digitally Imported Network" forming the Platform operated by Digitally Imported, Inc. and owned by the entities set out above.
  * To enable third parties to carry out technical, analytical, logistical, advertising or other functions on our behalf. 
  * Except as disclosed in this Privacy Policy, we do not use or disclose information about your individual visits to the Platform or your Personal Information collected online to any companies not affiliated with us.
  * In the event we plan to publicly post any of your Personal Information on a Website (as, for instance, a letter), we will request your permission to provide such posting.



Improving and customizing the Platform and advertising:

  * To analyze the use of the Platform, and the people visiting the Platform, in order to improve DI and customize it to your needs and interests.
  * To show you interesting DI ads, when you visit third party websites or use other apps with in-app advertising. When you see our ads on a third party website or apps, we or third party services we engage for this purpose, may collect information your browser or mobile app makes available when seeing the ad (e.g. ip-addresses, referral URL, browser details, timestamp of request). This allows us to show you ads that are more relevant and interesting to you. Please note that we do not share personal data with advertisers. If your profile matches these generic criteria, we may choose to display a suitable ad to you. We may also display ads based on the content that you have recently viewed or searched. Before we may use location data from the app for the first time, your mobile device will generally ask you for permission first. In case you gave permission to do so, location based services in your device will collect location data for a couple of seconds. These will then be shared with us or a respective service provider. Please see the privacy policy of such third party websites to understand what data may be collected and how to opt-out.
