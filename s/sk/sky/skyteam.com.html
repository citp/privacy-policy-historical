What information do you gather and how do you use it?
We collect information about your visits, such as service provider, browser type, operating system, pages accessed on our web site and the date and time of access to optimize your user experience and enhance your time spent with SkyTeam online. We also receive information telling SkyTeam what site you came from.

We may retain your name, and possibly other personal information, such as mailing address, e-mail address or credit card number when you provide this information to us. We may ask you to voluntarily provide SkyTeam with information regarding your personal or professional interests, demographics, experience with our products and contact preferences. This information may be combined with information about you that we gathered from our SkyTeam members. We use the information to design offers customized to your interests. For example, you may receive a special offer for your most frequently traveled destinations or be asked to participate in a survey.
Who do you share my personal information with?
We will not sell your name or other private profile information to third parties, and do not intend to do so in the future. We routinely share your information with our SkyTeam Member airlines and their subsidiaries. SkyTeam may also send you promotional offers in connection with its promotional partners if you indicate that you wish to receive offers from them. From time to time, we may engage third parties to process information on our behalf; however, SkyTeam requires that these third parties comply with SkyTeam's Privacy Policy and principles when processing this information. We may share anonymous, non-personal aggregated information about all our users with third parties.
How does skyteam.com use cookies and similar technologies?
SkyTeam uses cookies, tags and other similar technologies to improve your experience on our site. These technologies allow SkyTeam to enhance your visit to SkyTeam.com, provide you with information tailored to your needs and facilitate effective site administration.

You may elect to refuse cookies. Please refer to your browser Help instructions to learn more about cookies.
How does skyteam.com comply with applicable laws restricting marketing to children, e.g., Children's Online Privacy Act?
If a child under 13 in the United States has provided SkyTeam with personal information without parental or guardian consent, the parent or guardian may e-mail us, and we will remove the information and unsubscribe the child from any promotional contact opportunities. Likewise, SkyTeam takes appropriate measures to comply with similar local or national legislation in other countries.
Links
This website contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of each site that collects information from you. SkyTeam's Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected by SkyTeam through the skyteam.com site.
How is my information protected?
SkyTeam uses appropriate physical, electronic and managerial safeguards to protect the information you provide to us.
