# The Skin Cancer Foundation - Privacy Policy

### This is the web site of: 

The Skin Cancer Foundation  
149 Madison Avenue   
Suite 901  
New York, NY 10016 

We can be reached via e-mail at [info@skincancer.org.](mailto:info@skincancer.org)  
Or you can reach us by telephone at (212) 725-5756 

**The Skin Cancer Foundation does not sell or rent e-mail addresses to other organizations.**

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, information on what pages users access or visit and information volunteered by the visitor, such as survey information and/or site registrations. 

We share website usage information about visitors to our website with a reputable third party e-mail delivery company only for the purpose of targeting future Skin Cancer Foundation campaigns and upgrading visitor information used in reporting statistics. For this purpose we and our third-party e-mail delivery company note the e-mail you receive and some of the pages you visit on our website through the use of pixel tags (also called clear gifs). 

The information we collect is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes. It is used for internal review, to improve the content of our website, to notify visitors about updates to our website, and may be used by us to contact visitors for marketing purposes. 

If you would like to review the information we have on file for you or request corrections you may write to us at the above postal or e-mail address or call us at the above phone number. 

The Skin Cancer Foundation does not share your personal information with other organizations. 

The Skin Cancer Foundation takes steps to make all information received from our online visitors secure against unauthorized access and use. All information is protected by our security measures, which we periodically review and update. Sensitive information is protected using SSL encryption and is stored in a secure location on our servers. We employ physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to maintain the security of your information. 

**Opt-out options for e-mail newsletters**

If you would like to opt-out of Skin Cancer Foundation e-mail communications you may do so by following the instructions at the bottom of the newsletter you received from us.
