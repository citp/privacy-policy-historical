Privacy Policy

This is the website of

The Skin Cancer Foundation
149 Madison Avenue
Suite 901
New York, NY 10016

We can be reached via e-mail at: info@skincancer.org
Or you can reach us by telephone at (212) 725-5176

The Skin Cancer Foundation does not sell or rent e-mail addresses to other organizations.

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail and donate to or purchase from The Skin Cancer Foundation via our website. We also collect data anonymously concerning pages accessed or visited. Information volunteered by visitors via surveys and/or site registration is collected for internal use only by The Skin Cancer Foundation.

We share website usage information about website visitors with a reputable third party e-mail delivery company only for the purpose of targeting future Skin Cancer Foundation campaigns and upgrading visitor information used in reporting statistics. For this purpose we and our third-party e-mail delivery company note the e-mails we send and some of the pages you visit on our website.

The information we collect is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes. It is used for internal review, to improve the content of our website, to notify visitors about updates to our website, and may be used by us to contact visitors for marketing purposes.

If you would like to review the information we have on file for you or request corrections, you may write to us at the postal or e-mail address or call us at the phone number above.

The Skin Cancer Foundation does not share your personal information with other organizations.

The Skin Cancer Foundation takes steps to make all information received from our online visitors secure against unauthorized access and use. All information is protected by our security measures, which we periodically review and update. Sensitive information is protected using SSL encryption and is stored in a secure location on our servers. We employ physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to maintain the security of your information.

Opt-out options for e-mail newsletters
If you would like to opt-out of Skin Cancer Foundation e-mail communications you may do so by following the instructions at the bottom of any newsletters you received from us.

Donate Online:

Your donation makes it possible for the Foundation to continue our wide-ranging skin cancer prevention and public education programs, medical seminars and research.
