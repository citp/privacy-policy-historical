# Privacy | Skilledup

###  Scope of Privacy Policy 

This policy applies to website visitors and any other user of services offered through Apollo Lightspeed LLC, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Apollo Education Group, Inc. ('Lightspeeed' or 'we' or 'our'), including the SkilledUp education marketplace website ('SkilledUp') and any other websites or mobile applications operated by or on its behalf. 

Lightspeed works with employers, course providers and subject matter experts to offer technologies and informational services. SkilledUp collects information from users interested in particular careers and jobs, helps those users explore career and educational opportunities, and provides tools for users to find and quickly assess courses that may be a good match their career interests. Lightspeed is committed to protecting your privacy and we want you to know what Information we collect, how we use that Information, and how you can request that this Information be updated or corrected. 

We've summarized the key elements of our Privacy Policy below. To read our full Privacy Policy ("Policy"), click here. 

Our policy covers any visitor to Lightspeed Site(s) and any user of Lightspeed services. This policy applies to both online and offline collection, storage, processing, and transfer of Personal Information. However, certain Lighspeed Site(s) or services may be subject to additional privacy policies (known collectively as "Additional Policies"). 

Data you disclose to Lightspeed may be shared with employers, course providers and subject matter experts that pertain to career and educational opportunities that you explore when using SkilledUp and other Sites and services. 

###  Information Collected 

We collect various types of Information through our Sites. Some Information is collected automatically through various Web and Internet technologies, including Social Networking tools used by Lightspeed to foster communication and collaboration. Other Information is collected when you provide it in response to an advertisement, a survey, or other request for information; register for or participate in other activities on the Site; order educational or other products and services; set up a Social Network or other Site profile; or use one of our career resources, assessments or other interactive tools. We may also obtain information from other sources and combine that with Information we collect about you. 

###  How we Use Information 

We will not sell, rent, or lease your Personal Information to others except as provided in this policy. 

We may collect, use, and disclose Personal Information for the following purposes: 

  * to provide requested products and services
  * to respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  * to conduct promotions in which you have indicated an interest
  * for marketing purposes by Lightspeed and our Educational Partners, which includes, but is not limited to, sending you material about products, services, updates, etc. that we think may be of interest to you
  * to share with our Educational Partners working with Lightspeed, for the purposes of marketing their educational programs
  * for fostering communication and collaboration among users of the Sites through Social Networks
  * to analyze how Sites and services are being accessed and used
  * to improve Site and service performance and delivery
  * to prevent potentially illegal activities (including illegal downloading of copyrighted materials in accordance with our Copyright Infringement policy)
  * to analyze risk and business results
  * to maintain business records for reasonable periods
  * to enforce our Terms of Use
  * for sharing with our Educational Partners or Business Associates who are performing services on our behalf
  * to provide to Educational Partners, Business Associates or Unrelated Entities in connection with the contemplated or actual reorganization, merger, acquisition, financing, securitization, insuring, sale or other disposal of all or part of our business or assets, including for the purposes of determining whether to proceed with such transaction or fulfilling any records or other reporting requirements to such parties. In the event of any actual reorganization, merger, or acquisition such Information may be transferred as part of the transaction to the acquirer.
  * as may be required or permitted by legal, regulatory, industry self-regulatory, insurance, audit, or security requirements applicable to Lightspeed, our Educational Partners, or our Business Associates.

Do Not Track

Note that if you opt-out of certain uses of your Personal Information, we may no longer be able to provide certain products or services. For more information on your ability to opt-out, email [privacy@apollogrp.edu](mailto:privacy@apollogrp.edu). 

#####  Other Important Information 

We will take commercially reasonable measures to secure and store your Information to protect against the loss, misuse, and alteration of the Information under our control. 

Lightspeed may update this policy or revise it from time to time. If you are concerned about how your Personal Information is used or disclosed, you should contact us as described below or check back at this Site periodically to obtain a current copy of this Privacy Policy. 

###  How to Contact Us or Access Your Information

###  7\. Do Not Track
