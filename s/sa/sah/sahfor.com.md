# Sahfor - Privacy Policy

We understand that you may have questions about your data given while using Sahfor. We have therefore written this private policy to answer any questions you may have. This Private Policy applies to all of Sahfor's services. 

**Personal Information**

We store all of the information that we collate on submitting a site. This information is not given out or sold to any third parties. We may use your personal information to contact you about sahfor products or your submission(s).   
Any payments made to Sahfor are via paypal which is a secure online payment service, any questions on your personal information used for payments must be made to paypal.
