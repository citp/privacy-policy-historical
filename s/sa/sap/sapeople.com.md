# Connecting South Africans - find, reunite with old and new friends on South Africa's biggest social network

The Internet is a fun place for work and for play. However, in order for you to get the best possible use from our site it may be necessary, in some circumstances, for you to give us personal information about yourself. This policy tells you how we collect information from you, how we protect it and how we use it. We want to help make the Internet a safe and fun experience for you, your friends and your family.

If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should [contact SAReunited](http://www.sareunited.com/contact_us.php?ct=21).

**1\. NO Permanent Cookies Policy**  
SAReunited DO NOT USE ANY PERMANENT COOKIES on our website, therefore your private data does not lie around on your work, home or Internet café computer.

**2\. Password Protection**  
For additional protection we advise that you keep your password known only to you. On shared computers we advise that you do not accept the "remember password" setting when prompted by your browser's software.

**3\. Web Server Logs**  
We collect IP addresses and web browser information via Web Server Logs to analyse and predict trends on our web sites (as do virtually all other web servers) to gather generalised demographic information, and to administer the web site. The data collected is not linked to personally identifiable information.

**4\. Online Forms**  
Any information submitted via forms on our web sites is used exclusively by SAReunited for the purpose stated, unless otherwise indicated on the page.

**5\. Advertising**  
SAReunited sites take advertising from third parties. These companies may set cookies through their adverts. These cookies are not the responsibility of SAReunited but we accept that the organisations do act responsibly.

**6\. Tell-A-Friend and Refer-A-Friend Page Referrals**  
If anyone chooses to use this feature to refer a web page to some friends or colleagues, the visitor must enter both their name and email address (plus an optional message), plus those of their friends. SAReunited will automatically send the friend(s) an email containing a link to the referred web page. The email may also contain promotional information about SAReunited.

**7\. Email newsletters and Promotional Email Campaigns**  
Users of our web site have the option to subscribe to these services when they complete our online forms. All emails sent from SAReunited contain unsubscribe instructions. If members opt in for information from selected SAReunited partners in the form of promotions or offers, it us up to the partner to provide an opt out for members on this content.

**8\. Security**  
SAReunited takes every precaution to protect users information, when a user submits information via our web site for any purpose. Access to any information collected about a user is restricted to authorised SAReunited employees. We employ up to date industry standard procedures to keep all data secure against loss and unauthorised disclosure.

**9\. Competitions and Surveys**  
Participation in these events is entirely voluntary and the user can choose what information they wish to disclose about themselves. Survey analysis will result in generalised, non-personally identifiable results being published on our web site.

**10\. Data Protection and Opting Out**  
 All users are presented with the opportunity to "opt-out" of having their personal information used for any purpose not directly associated with our site at the point where we ask. Members can however amend their subscriptions options on their profile page or once logged in, at this link <http://www.sareunited.com/faq_subscribe_unsubscribe_site_newsletters.php>

**11\. Changes to this Policy**  
Any change to this policy will be highlighted on our web sites in the relevant sections to ensure that users are aware of the terms of use of the website. If we make any changes to the way we use personal information, we will attempt to advise everyone concerned in the form of an email to their registered email address , to check the site’s terms and conditions for revisions.
