Privacy

SAPDev.co.uk has a firm commitment to privacy and want to make every effort to address any concerns you may have about your privacy. The following policy explains our position on information-gathering and disclosure.

Information collected and how we use it
The only real time we will collect personal information is if you choose to join the SAPdev mailing list, at which point we only collect personal information such as Name and email address. We use this information to send you a periodic newsletter which keeps you up to date on the latest website updates as well relevant industry news. We may also occasionally inform you or allow others to inform you of relevant SAP related products or services which might be of interest to you. We use return email addresses to answer any emails received. These addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties.



Third-party advertising and cookies
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and would like to know your options in relation to not having this information used by these companies, please click here.

See here for a video explaning cookies from google or visit the about Cookies website.

Finally, we never use or share the information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise restrict such unrelated uses.

Third party websites
This Website contains numerous links that may take you to other third-party Web sites or services not under my control. These are provided for convenience to you. We are not responsible for the privacy policies on these websites.

Affiliate Links
This website uses selective affiliate links, this means that a commission is earned if you make a purchase via the link and helps keep this website free to use. The price you pay will be the same or sometimes cheaper whether you use the affiliate link or go directly to the vendor's website using a non-affiliate link.

Privacy Statement alterations
Please note that the content of this privacy statement may be altered at any time.
