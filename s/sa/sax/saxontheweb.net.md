# Sax on the Web > Privacy Policy

  


#### Copyright

We respect your privacy! As a rule no visitor information is collected by the _[Sax on the Web](http://www.saxontheweb.net/index.html)_ service. Any and all information you will volunteer at this site will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to the _Sax on the Web pages_ will be held with care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. Please see our [system provider's](http://www.asmallorange.com/) privacy policies. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to [contact us](http://www.saxontheweb.net/Resources/contact.html).

### Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:

Sax on the Web is a free services for saxophone players and devotees. several individuals contribute to this service knowing that we do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site. The [Guest Book](http://www.saxontheweb.net/addbook.html) entries may be used in the future for e-mailings or newsletters only related to this site (SOTW).
