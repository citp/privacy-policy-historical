#### Which information we may collect?

We may collect two types of data and information: The first type of information is non-identifiable and anonymous information ( **“Non-PII”** ). We are not aware of you identity as this type of information includes technical information, automated, anonymous and behavioral information. The second type of information is personal identifiable information ( **“PII”** ). This information may identify an individual or may be of a private or sensitive nature. The PII collected by us is provided by you voluntary and only when you register to the Program and create a Publisher/Advertiser Account (as defined below) or use the [Contact Us](http://www.songocpa.com/contactus.html) page. The only PII that we collect is the information you provide in order to open up an account after You register to our Program and become one of our Publishers or Advertisers. This information includes Your: email address, full name, contact number, website address, company name, country , state, physical address, zip code and VAT tax number and You financial information (e.g., banking information, PayPal account, etc.). Note that you can change such personal data through the profile section of your Account as detailed below. **To clarify, when browsing the Site we DO NOT collect PII.

We do not collect any PII from you without your approval, which is obtained, inter alia, through your active acceptance to the [Terms Of Use](http://www.songocpa.com/terms.html) and Privacy Policy.

The Non-PII is used for statistical purposes and enables us to further develop, customize and improve our Site and Program based on users common preferences and uses.

#### Cookies:

When you access or use the Site, the Company may use industry-wide technologies such as **"cookies"** (or similar technologies), which store certain information on your computer and which will allow Us to enable automatic activation of certain features (such as: remember your password when you log in for the next time), and make your service experience much more convenient and effortless. The cookies used by the Site are created per session, does not include any information about you, other than your session key and are removed as your session ends and password information. It is easy to prohibit the Local Storage. Most browsers will allow you to erase cookies from your computer's hard drive, block acceptance of cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. However, if you block or erase cookies your online experience may be limited. For the purpose of this section, "Cookies" shall mean a small text files, given ID tags that are stored on a users’ computer's browser directory or program data subfolders. Cookies are created when a user uses its browser to visit a website that uses cookies to keep track of the user’s movement within the website, remember a user’s registered login, preferences, and other customization functions.
