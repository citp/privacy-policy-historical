# Santa Cruz County Sentinel - Customer Service

Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties provide this Privacy Statement to inform you of our Privacy Policy and practices and of the choices you can make about the way your information is collected online and how that information is used. We've structured our website so that, in general, you can visit Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties on the Web without identifying yourself or revealing any personal information. We make this notice readily available at the bottom of every Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties web page.

## 1\. Sites covered by this Privacy Statement

This Privacy Statement applies to all Santa Cruz Setinel-owned websites and domains.[]()

### Links to non-Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties

The Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties may provide links to third-party websites for your convenience and information. If you access those links, you will leave the Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties. Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties do not control those sites or their privacy practices, which may differ from Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties. We do not endorse or make any representations about third-party websites. The personal data you choose to give to unrelated third parties is not covered by the Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties Privacy Statement. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any company before submitting your personal information. Some third-party companies may choose to share their personal data with Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties; that sharing is governed by that third-party company's privacy policy.   


## 2\. Types of information we collect

This Privacy Statement covers personal information, non-personal data collection and aggregate reporting. 

Personal information is information that is associated with your name or personal identity. Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties uses personal information to understand better your needs and interests and to provide you with better service. Once you choose to provide us with personal information, you can be assured it will be used only to support your customer relationship with Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties. We take seriously the trust you place in us. Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties will not sell, rent or lease your personal information to others.

The types of personal information you provide to us on these pages may include name, address, phone number, e-mail address, user IDs and passwords, billing and transaction information, credit card information, contact preferences, educational and employment background, and job interest data.

Non-personal data we collect may include the pages visited on the Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties websites, unique URLs1 visited within Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties, browser type and IP2 address. Most non-personal data is collected via cookies or other analysis technologies. Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties may use cookies for data analysis and personalization services. Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties also place ads on other websites that may use cookies. 

## 3\. Children's privacy

Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties are committed to protecting the privacy needs of children and we encourage parents and guardians to take an active role in their children's online activities and interests. Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 and Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties do not target its websites to children under 13.    


## 4\. How we use your information

Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties use your personal information to provide you with services and to help us better understand your needs and interests. Specifically, we use your information to help you complete a project, to communicate with you, to provide service and support, to update you on services and benefits, to personalize promotional offers and to personalize some Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties websites. Occasionally we may also use your information to contact you for market research regarding Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties services. Credit card information is used only for payment processing and fraud prevention. Credit card information and sensitive personal data are not used for any other purpose and will not be kept longer than necessary for providing the services, unless you ask us to retain your credit card information for future services. 

Personal data collected online may also be combined with information you provide Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties through other sources such as call centers or public events such as trade shows or seminars. 

Personal data given to Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties may be transferred across state and country borders for the purposes of data consolidation, storage and simplified customer information management. 

Non-personal data is aggregated for reporting about Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties website usability, performance and effectiveness. It is used to improve the customer experience, usability and site content.

## 5\. Who we share your information with

Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties will not sell, rent or lease your personal information to others. Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties will not share your personal information with third parties except in responding to your requests for products or services. Your permission will be requested when you submit your information. Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties shares customer information with Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties partners, but only as described above in "How we use your information." 

Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties contracts with third-party service providers and suppliers to deliver complete products, services and customer solutions described in "How we use your information." Suppliers and service providers are required to keep confidential the information received on behalf of Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties and may not use it for any purpose other than to carry out the services they are performing for Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties. These service providers may change or we may contract with additional service providers to better accommodate our customers. Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties will not share personal information with any other third parties without your permission, unless required by law enforcement action, subpoena, or local law. 

Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties or its related entities could merge with or be acquired by another business entity or some or all of their respective assets could be acquired. If such a combination or acquisition occurs, Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties will make every reasonable effort to notify you in the event we share with the merging or acquiring entity some or all of your personal information to continue serving you.   


## 6\. Your choices

Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties gives you the choice of receiving a variety of information that complements our services. You can subscribe to receive certain product- and service-specific information and Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties marketing communications. Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties communications may include new product information, special offers, or an invitation to participate in market research. We give you a choice regarding delivery of Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties communications by postal mail, e-mail and telephone. We will make every effort to honor your preferences.   


## 7\. Your privacy preferences and opting out 

### Non-subscription services

Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties will not use your personal information for a different purpose than the one stated when you provided your data. 

### Subscription services

To opt-out of receiving communications you have expressly requested (such as e-mail newsletters, software updates, etc.) [contact us](mailto:webmaster@santacruzsentinel.com) immediately. 

## 8\. Your information and third-party companies

Certain Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties services are linked with those from unrelated third-party companies, some which offer you the option to share with both Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties and the third party personal data you provide. Examples include requesting communications from Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties marketing or solution partners. We will not share your personal information with those third-party companies unless you make that choice. 

## 9\. Access to and accuracy of your information

Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties strives to keep your personal information accurate. To protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity, such as a password and user ID, before granting access to your data. Certain areas of Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties's websites may limit access to specific individuals through the use of passwords and other personal identifiers.

The most effective way to view and change your personal information is to [contact us](mailto:webmaster@santacruzsentinel.com).  

## 10\. Keeping your information secure

Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties is committed to protecting the information you provide us. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, to maintain data accuracy, and to ensure the appropriate use of the information, Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties has in place appropriate physical and managerial procedures to safeguard the information we collect. 

In the event that you may pay us on line, we use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption when collecting or transferring sensitive data such as credit card information. SSL encryption is designed to make the information unreadable by anyone but us. This security measure is working when you see either the symbol of an unbroken key or closed lock (depending on your browser) on the bottom of your browser window.

Credit card numbers are used only for processing payment and are not used for other purposes. As part of real-time payment processing, Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties subscribe to a fraud management service. This service gives you and Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties an extra level of security to guard against credit card fraud to protect your financial data. 

## 11\. Changes to this Statement

If there are updates to the terms of Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties Privacy Statement, we will post those changes and update the revision date in this document, so you will always know what information we collect online, how we use it, and what choices you have. For material changes to this Statement, Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties will provide notification to affected customers.

1A URL (Uniform Resource Locator) is the global address of documents and other resources on the World Wide Web. For example, http://www.barefootgypsy.com is the URL for Santa Cruz Sentinel Web Properties's home page.

2An IP address is an identifier for a computer or device on a Transmission Control Protocol/Internet Protocol (TCP/IP) network, such as the World Wide Web. Networks use the TCP/IP protocol to route information based on the IP address of the destination. In other words, an IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you are surfing the Web, allowing Web servers to locate and identify your computer. Computers require IP addresses in order for users to communicate on the Internet, browse and shop.
