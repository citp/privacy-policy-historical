Anonymous User Information - Cookies

In general, you can visit samys.com without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself. However, as you browse our Site, our web servers record data on your visit, using cookies and other tracking technology. A cookie is a small file that is stored on your computer that identifies it specifically with our Site, allowing us to simplify the login and checkout processes. This information also allows us to analyze what our visitors are looking for, whether or not they find it, and how they work with our Site. Some cookies are deleted once you leave our Site ("session cookies"), while other cookies remain after your leave our site ("persistent cookies") so that you are recognized when you return to the Site. None of the information collected by these methods in any way contains personal details about you or any of our other users - it is strictly statistical information about your computer and internet connection. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but doing so will limit system performance and may even cause certain features of the Site to malfunction or not to work at all.

Please be aware that emails are not secure, so do not send sensitive information to us in an email.

Opt-Out, Access, and Security

You may also direct us to change or delete your information by fax, mail or email at the addresses and numbers provided below. We will act on your instructions promptly, but making changes or deleting information may take two weeks or more to become effective. We will honor any requests for us to provide or change information when your log-on information and password are used.


Our Site uses SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) security technology to encrypt information you provide to us through the Site, but any information you provide us by email is unencrypted. SSL is an industry-standard protocol for encryption over the internet. Data transmission over the internet is never 100% secure.


While we take steps to protect your personal information and keep it secure, you also play a role in protecting your information. Samy's Camera cannot guaranty the security of any information you transmit to us or from our Site, and therefore you use our site at your own risk.
