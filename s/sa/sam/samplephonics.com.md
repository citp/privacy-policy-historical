# Samplephonics Privacy Policy | Info For Music Producers

* * *

To provide you with the services you have selected, it is essential for Samplephonics to store your personal information. This information will be used by Samplephonics and its service providers to allow for faster purchasing and the allocation of discount coupons, and will not be passed on to any third parties. No promotional information from Samplephonics or any third parties will be sent to the email address provided, except the Samplephonics newsletter, which you can unsubscribe to at any time using the link at the bottom of the email.  
When you enter the site you will be issued with a cookie, which is a small text file transferred from the website to your hard drive, and is used to validate your login details and allow the audio player to function smoothly when browsing the site, as well as record areas of the site that you visit. This helps us to provide the best browsing experience for our customers and we don't collect any other information than that outline above and that you enter on the website. This website complies with the Data Protection Act 1998 and Samplephonics takes all necessary measures to ensure your data is protected against loss, theft, misuse and alteration. Samplephonics is not responsible for any information contained in any 3rd party websites that are linked to from this website.

We do not store credit card details nor do we share customer details with any third parties
