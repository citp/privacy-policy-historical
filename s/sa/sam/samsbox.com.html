PRIVACY POLICY

Samsbox.COM

Last Updated: January 30th, 2013

This Privacy policy covers your rights, and our use of your Data and other information we collect and use. For more details on security policies or terms, please see the other pages on the Website.

Highlights

  • We use a highly secure form of Private Key Encryption, which means that your data is encrypted on your own computer before it is transmitted to us. This means that nobody, not even our employees or law enforcement, can read your data stored on our servers.
  • Your data is encrypted multiple times for added layers of security: When you enter your password (AES 128 or 256 bit), when it is transmitted to us (SSL connection), and when it is stored in our servers (Randomized Salting).
  • If you lose your Password, we cannot recover your data
  • We never sell or share your personal information with anyone, except to comply with the law or protect our rights.
  • We don't store your passwords or encryption keys on our servers to keep your data truly private
  • Login & Signup through our Linkedin application is required to fully use all the features of our Service; this requires you to have a valid Linkedin user account, which is subject to its own privacy policy and terms.
  • If you are under the age of 13, you may not use our Service.
