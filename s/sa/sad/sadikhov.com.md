WHAT THIS PRIVACY POLICY COVERS

INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE   
Sadikhov.com does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you’ve requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:  
• We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims;
