Privacy Policy

When you use an on-line shopping cart, make a purchase on-line, send an e-mail from a web site, fill out an on-line questionnaire, registration, or request for information, or make use of on-line interactive games or activities, you are sharing information with the proprietors of the web site. William H. Sadlier, Inc. (Sadlier) recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of its customers and visitors to our web sites. Sadlier's privacy policy is stated below.

Personal Information
The personal information collected on Sadlier's web sites includes such information as first and last name, address, phone number, email address, age, and so on. Sadlier's web sites do not collect any personal information about a user unless it is voluntarily provided by the user.

Use of Personal Information
Personal information may be used by Sadlier to help improve its web sites or for marketing or promotional purposes. This information will in most cases be entered into Sadlier's customer database. It is Sadlier's policy not to share any personally-identifying material obtained through Sadlier's web sites with any third party.

Use of Cookies
Some of the areas on Sadlier's web sites employ the use of "cookies." Cookies store pertinent information about the user during a session. Sadlier does not use cookies to gather any personal information about the user and generally discards information gathered in cookies after the user session. Cookies are used in some of Sadlier's on-line games to keep track of the user's scores and also in on-line catalogs to keep track of the user's status and contents of the user's shopping cart. Cookies may be used in other areas of Sadlier's web sites as well. Sadlier may retain information gathered in cookies to allow users access to password-protected areas of Sadlier's web sites.

Children's Online Privacy
Under the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, no Web site operator is permitted to require a child under the age of 13 to disclose more information than is reasonably necessary to participate in an activity as a condition of participation. William H. Sadlier, Inc. abides by this requirement. William H. Sadlier, Inc. does not gather information on the ages of our users and will never knowingly share personally identifying information about users who are under the age of 13 with third parties. When contacting our users, William H. Sadlier, Inc. requests that anyone under the age of 13 forward the e-mail to a parent or guardian.

NOTE TO CHILDREN
Please do not give your full name, email address, home address, telephone number or any other personal information that would enable someone to contact you either online or offline, without first asking your parent or guardian for permission.

NOTE TO PARENTS
Sadlier strongly encourages parents and guardians to spend time online with their children and to participate in the activities offered on its web sites. No personal information should be submitted to or posted on this web site by children without the consent of their parent or guardian. William H. Sadlier requires that parents need to give offline consent for kids 12 and under, either by phone, fax, or snail mail. Email consent is not sufficient. Sadlier encourages parents to monitor their children's online use, and to help protect their privacy by instructing them never to provide personal information on Sadlier's web sites or any other web site without permission.

You may wish to refer to The Core Rules of Netiquette
(http://www.albion.com/netiquette/corerules.html) by Virginia Shea for the "do's" and "don'ts" of communicating on the Internet.
