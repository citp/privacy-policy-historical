# Web Scripting Privacy Policies

**[Web Scripting](http://www.satya-weblog.com/) Privacy Policies **

  * I do not use or publish any personal information I receive from my visitors. 
  * I do not send any newsletters etc to any of my visitors. In future also, I do not have any plan of doing so. 
  * In future, if I think I should use the email for updating our visitors, then its frequency will be very less. May be one in 3 months. Still unsubscribe link will be there. I do not like spam. 
  * I use 3rd party advertisers, that may use cookies to track their performance. I will try using only reputed advertisers. 


  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site. 
  * Google's use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet. 
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html). 



We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
