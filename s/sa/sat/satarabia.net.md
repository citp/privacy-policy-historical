# Privacy Policy

Satarabia.Com is committed to protecting the privacy of those using our site and the confidentiality of the personal information which our customers provide us with. We use the information we collect to provide services and to ensure that your order is processed smoothly. Beyond that, we only use personal information if volunteered by you. Satarabia.Com will never sell personal information or share personal information with third parties unless these are involved to fulfill your order.

**Satarabia.Com will never sell personal information**

Satarabia.Com will never sell personal information or share personal information with third parties, unless these are involved to fulfill your order. Our employees and associated companies are obliged to respect confidentiality.
