We encourage you to review their privacy policies to learn more about what, why and how they collect and use Personal Information.

Non-Personally Identifiable Information.
When you access the Site or use the Service, (whether such use is performed on the Site or on a website of one of our partners or other third parties), we collect the following non-personally identifiable information (“Non-Personal Information”):
  • Information regarding your use of the Service;
  • Information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website or online service (“Log Data”). This Log Data may include, but is not limited to, your computer’s Internet Protocol address, language, browser type, location, time zone, unique device identifier, the web page you were visiting before you came to the Site and information you search for on the Site and through the Service
  • Like many websites, we use “Web cookies” and “Adobe Flash Local Shared Objects” to collect information. A cookie is a small data file that we transfer to your computer’s hard disk for record-keeping purposes. We use “session ID cookies” to enable certain features of the Services, to better understand how you interact with the Services and to monitor web traffic routing on the Site and aggregate usage of the Services. You can instruct your browser, by changing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the website you visit. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all portions of the Site or all functionality of the Services
  • Third party websites which may advertise on the Site and other third parties that you interact with through your use of the Service may also place or read cookies on your computer’s browser, which enables them to collect information about users who view or interact with their advertisements and content. The collection and use of such information and access of any third party websites are subject to such third party’s privacy policy. If Personal Information is integrated with Non-Personal Information, we will regard the integrated information as Personal Information for as long as such information remains integrated.
Links and Third Party Sites
The Site may contain links in a format that enables Us to keep track of whether you have followed them. Furthermore, the Services and Site may contain information in the form of, but not limited to, articles, advertisements, third-party opinions and links to third party websites – all which are not owned or controlled by Us. Such information is provided by Us as a convenience to our users and visitors and as such, We assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third party. While we do not transfer any information to such third parties (whether Personally Identifiable Information, Non-Personally Identifiable Information or otherwise), you should be aware that this Privacy Policy applies only to the Service. We do not exercise control over the sites that provide Content, ranking or other information, or over banners, other advertisements or links from within the Site or the Service. These other sites may place their own cookies or other files on your computer, collect data or solicit personal information from you. Other websites follow different rules regarding the use or disclosure of the Personal Information that you submit. We encourage you to read the privacy policies and other terms of the other websites as we do not have any control or liability over such practices. Whenever you connect with a third party application or website, we might receive information from them, including information about actions you take.

If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with Personal Information without their consent, he or she should contact us at support@savefront.com If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with Personal Information, we will delete such information from our files.
