Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

The use of cookies by other Third Party ad servers or ad networks may be used to track user activities in measuring advertisement effectiveness, etc. provided in their privacy policies. Saving Cents with Sense does not control these cookies by Third Party advertisers.
