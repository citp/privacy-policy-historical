# Terms & Conditions, Privacy & Legal:

## Terms and Conditions

Here at Savify.com, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users so we have outlined how we may use data collected on our site below. Savify.com will act in accordance with current legislation and will always aim to meet current internet best practices.

**Cookies**

When you visit our site a cookie will be placed on your computer. Cookies are text files that identify your computer to our web servers. Cookies will not identify the individual users, just the computer. We may use cookies to help personalise the browsing experience and also to help us analyse the path you take through the site and therefore help make improvements on the site in the future. Savify.com is best browsed with cookies enabled and should you disable cookies, the site may not work properly.

**Use and Storage of Personal Information**

There may be instances where personal information is transmitted to our webserver for example if you choose to buy a product or service or sign up to our newsletter. The information will always be collected fairly, that is we will explain how the data will be used and tell you if the information will be passed on to anyone else. Savify.com operates a strict no spam policy therefore your data will never be passed onto a third party without your explicit consent first, unless we are required to do so by law.

We will keep your personal information on our systems for as long as you use the services requested. You may request that we send you the information we hold on you and request that this information be removed at any time, however an administration charge for this may be levied.

We will hold all the information on our servers securely however the internet is a notoriously insecure medium therefore although we take as many precautions as possible to ensure the integrity and security of our data, we make no guarantees or warrantees.

**Links**   
Savify.com contains links leading to third-party websites. Whilst we try to ensure that all the sites we link to are appropriate, you are advised that we cannot be held responsible for the content of the sites we link to therefore you visit them at your own risk.
