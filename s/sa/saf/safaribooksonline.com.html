    We at Safari Books Online are committed to respecting your online privacy and recognize your need for appropriate protection and management of any personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") that you share with us.

    "Personal Information" means any information that may be used to identify you as an individual, including but not limited to your first and last name, a home or other physical address, your email address, or other contact information. In general, you can visit Safari Books Online Web pages without telling us who you are or providing us with any Personal Information.

    If you choose to provide us with your Personal Information via our site, we may use it within Safari Books Online and share it with Safari Books Online resellers. Such sharing may include the transfer of your Personal Information across borders, and from your country or jurisdiction to other countries or jurisdictions around the world.

    With respect to some specific instances in which we collect Personal Information, we may post a purpose statement that explains why Personal Information is being collected and whether we plan to share such Personal Information outside of Safari Books Online or those working on the behalf of Safari Books Online.

    Safari Books Online does not intend to transfer Personal Information without your consent to third parties who are not acting on behalf of Safari Books Online, and who do not treat such Personal Information with at least as much care as we do, unless such transfer is legally required.

  2. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGY
    A cookie is a small data file that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your web browser when you visit our site. Cookies enable our systems to recognize your computer, so that we can provide you with customized information and features. We also use cookies to track user traffic patterns. You can set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies or to block cookies.

    Cookies and other tracking technologies that we use may record information concerning your visits to our site, such as Internet domain and host names; Internet protocol (IP) addresses; browser software and operating system types; clickstream patterns; and dates and times that our site is accessed. We use this information to improve the performance of our site, and our products and services. We may also analyze information that does not contain Personal Information for trends and statistics.

Should our Privacy Policy change, we will post our revised Privacy Policy on our site. Your continued use of our site will be deemed to be your consent to the revised policy. You can always find the current version of our Privacy Policy at http://www.safaribooksonline.com/privacy.php.
