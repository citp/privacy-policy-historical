# Shabu Games - Privacy Policies

Shabu Games created this privacy policy to show our commitment to privacy issues. The content below describes our privacy practices.

**Anonymous information**

Anonymous information refers to data that cannot be referred back to a specific individual. We collect the visitor's IP address, referral stats, and browser and platform type. And you do not have to sign up with Shabu Games before we can collect this anonymous information. 

**Personal Information**

We respect customers' privacy. We do not collect personal information unless you give it to us when downloading or ordering a game or signing up for a free email newsletter. No personal information given to us is used for any other  purpose other than what is stated in this policy. We send emails to subscribers to inform about new games and useful information about games.

We do not sell, distribute, share, or give your information to any other company for any marketing or sale purposes. 

our email information is used solely by Shabu Games through our Newsletter and for adding users to ShabuGames's My Big Fish Games friends list and emails relating to the My Big Fish Games affiliate program.

If you don't want to receive any email from us, you can opt-out from the mailing list at any time. 

**Cookies**   
We do not use cookies on this site. But we use third-party e-commerce companies (RegNow, Big Fish Games, Reflexive Games) to track download/purchase on this site. All these e-commerce companies are well established in the market. Cookies are used on many game download links to track referrals from this site. All full version purchases made from these downloads are then credited to Shabu Games from game developer partners on a revenue sharing basis. 

**External Links**

This site links to other websites. Shabu Games is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other web sites that we are linking to.
