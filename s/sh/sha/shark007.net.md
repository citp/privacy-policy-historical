# Privacy Policy regarding this website: http://shark007.net

**_Privacy Statement regarding http://shark007.net_**

I use third-party advertising companies, Google and VibrantMedia, to serve ads to your browser when you visit my site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, read the following.

  * Google and VibrantMedia, as a third party vendors, uses cookies to serve ads to your browser. 
  * Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your browser based on visits to my site and other sites on the Internet. 
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)
  * User's of this site should also read the privacy policy of VibrantMedia which is posted here: <https://vibrantmedia.com/privacy.asp>
