Shana Logic's Privacy Policy



Shana Logic is a small independent business and we take your privacy VERY seriously!

 

1. Your Information and how we protect it

ShanaLogic.com collects your personal information if you provide it to us, when you place orders on our webiste. We DO NOT store any payment information on our server or in our cart software for security and privacy reasons. Our site uses industry standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology in addition to having a managed server so that we can keep your information as safe as possible!

Information we do store (if you provide it to us)

Name, Billing/Shipping Address, Email Address, Phone number (if you provide it), username and password (if you create an account).

We do not use your information for anything non-site related and we will NEVER sell your information to any third-party companies (that would be pretty rude :).  We care about our shoppers and keep their info private!

For any additional information, please contact us!
