Sharp Electronics Corporation Online Privacy Policy

Sharp is committed to respecting your privacy. We recognize your need to control personal information you share with us. Sharp uses your information to better understand your needs and provide you with better service.

What We Request
There are instances on our web site where we ask you to provide personal information such as name, address, phone number, email address, and business information. This type of information will improve your website experience or allow Sharp to follow-up with you after you visit our site.

What You Gain
The information you provide will not be sold, but may be shared within or among business partners to support your customer relationship with us. Sharp will use this information for:
1. Personal Acknowledgements
2. Personalized Messages
3. Pre-filled Forms
4. Sending targeted e-mails to alert you to product upgrades, special offers, updated information and other new services. In the event that you are not interested in email alerts, you will have the opportunity to decline this service.

Other Important Privacy Issues
Sharp will not sell your personally identifiable information to others, but we may share your information with Sharp entities and/or business partners to serve your needs.

This privacy policy references the contents of this site only and does not pertain to any other Sharp site.

Questions or Concerns
If you have any comments or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please Contact Us.
