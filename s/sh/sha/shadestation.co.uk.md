# Privacy Policy

Shade Station respects your privacy. Any information collected on the Website, excluding content submitted for the email bulletins, will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed in ways that you have not consented to.

Shade Station reserves the right to send you information, from time to time, from companies which Shade Station believes will be of interest or use to its registered customers.

From time to time Shade Station may compile general information you have provided us to draw conclusions about Shade Station customers, eg age range, location or profession. Shade Station reserves the right to publish and share such information, but undertakes that it will never link individual data with general data, and will ensure that information about an individual will not be attainable from the general information.

Shade Station constantly analyses its website logs to improve the value of the materials available on the Website. The Website logs do not allow individuals to be identified, and Shade Station makes no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.

Shade Station uses cookies to keep you logged into the site. These cookies are not harmful in any way to your computer.

Shade Station maintains an email list. Individuals must affirmatively ask to join the mailing lists by signing up via the Website. In addition, we do not divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone, including other list subscribers, with the exception of Shade Station.

You may request deletion of your Shade Station customer details at any time by notifying us at [info@shadestation.co.uk](mailto:info@shadestation.co.uk). Your details will then be removed from our database. You may also change these details at any time by logging in on the website and selecting "My Account" on the navigation menu. You will be able to unsubscribe from this section also.

Shade Station may amend this policy from time to time. If any substantial changes are made to in the way Shade Station use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on the Website or by email, as we see fit.

If you have any unanswered questions, please contact us at [info@shadestation.co.uk](mailto:info@shadestation.co.uk). 

Cookies are small text files which are stored on your computer by your browser when you visit web pages. We use cookies to allow us to store tracking information to make your user experience better. Cookie storage reasons include: Items in your shopping basket, usage statistics, and your favourite products. This information is not shared, and expires after a reasonable period of time. 

We collect and store data in cookies which is sent securely to remarketing companies, whose algorithms may determine if you will see advertising related to your browsing on other websites. You are able to opt out of these adverts at any time (more information below).

Cookies are necessary to purchase items at Shade Station. They are used to identify items in your shopping bag, shortlist, and also your browsing history so we can make better recommendations. The cookies themselves do not store any personal identifiable information. Instead, they store a 'key' on your computer which then identifies information stored temporarily on our own server. You can disable cookies in your browser to prevent this, however you will not be able to use the primary functionality of this website. You can disable cookies in your browser's settings. If you are unsure how to do this, follow this link: [Disable browser cookies](http://www.aboutcookies.org/Default.aspx?page=1).
