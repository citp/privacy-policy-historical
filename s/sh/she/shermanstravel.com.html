ShermansTravel's Privacy Practices

ShermansTravel does not collect personal information about individuals except when such individuals specifically provide such information on a voluntary basis, such as by subscribing to our electronic e-mail newsletters and products.

ShermansTravel will not share your email address or any other information you may submit on our Web site (www.shermanstravel.com) with any third party.

You can generally visit our Web site without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. However, our Web servers may automatically recognize your domain name when you visit our site, and this information is aggregated and used to measure statistics regarding the use of our site, such as the number of visitors, average time spent on the site, and pages viewed. This information is likewise not distributed to or shared with third parties.

ShermansTravel uses invisible GIFs in our HTML-based emails to tell us which emails have been opened by recipients. This practice allows us to gauge the effectiveness of certain communications and the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. ShermansTravel also uses invisible third-party GIFs on the Web site, which allow us to recognize a user when a user visits our Web site. The information collected and shared using this technology is not personally identifiable.

ShermansTravel may use the personal information it gathers for its own internal purposes. Our goal in collecting personal information is to continually improve the content of our Web site and offer consumers the travel products that best meet their preferences and interests. ShermansTravel may also use the personal data you provide to send you information about new products and services, or offers that we feel may be of interest to you.

If your personal information changes or you no longer wish to receive e-mail newsletters or products from us, you may correct, update, or deactivate your account at any time by accessing your personal profile.

ShermansTravel reserves the right to change this policy at any time. In the event we do decide to change our privacy policy, we will post said changes on our Web site. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you on this page, by email, or by means of a notice on our homepage. This statement and policies outlined herein are not intended to, and do not create any, contractual or other legal rights on behalf of any party. By using the ShermansTravel Web site, you signify your agreement to the terms of our privacy statement.
