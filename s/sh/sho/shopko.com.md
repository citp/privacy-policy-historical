# Privacy Policy: Shopko

Shopko respects your privacy. We maintain safeguards to protect any personal information you provide us, including your name, address, telephone numbers, gift registry event dates and selected products, email addresses, credit card information, and checking account information. We won't sell, rent or give information which identifies you personally to anyone if you've told us not to, as described below. This Privacy Policy sets out our policies and practices for collecting, maintaining, using, sharing and protecting your personal information. You may choose to share personal information with Shopko by visiting our website, by creating a gift registry, by making purchases using credit cards, debit cards or personal checks, by participating in Shopko’s loyalty program or in various promotions and sweepstakes, or by using the services of our pharmacy and optical centers, among other ways. Our updated Privacy Policy covers multiple areas of our business, including portions of our website, www.shopko.com, and, as applicable, our mobile sites, use of social media sites (together, the “Sites”) and any other personal information obtained when you call, email or otherwise communicate and interact with Shopko.  
By visiting the Sites, making a purchase from Shopko, or otherwise interacting with Shopko, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. We suggest you review this Privacy Policy periodically, as it may be updated form time-to-time. 

## Pharmacy and Optical Information

If we serve your pharmacy or optical needs, we follow government guidelines to protect your medical information, including email addresses collected by our pharmacies and/or optical centers as part of providing patient care. ## Personal Information Usage

The personally identifiable information you provide to us, and information about your purchases, may be combined with other personally identifiable information from our records and other sources. This information may be used to make our future marketing efforts more efficient. This information may also be shared with our third party service providers and other marketers whose products or services may be of interest to you. If you prefer that we do not share your personal information with other marketers, see Opt out Section below.

## Our Sites

Shopko's goal is to create an on-line environment where you can safely and easily shop our store, and learn more about our products and our Company.  
Portions of our Sites, are operated by Shopko, and are subject to the privacy statements below. Portions of our Sites are operated by our retail partners, and are subject to their privacy statements.  
**Web Information Usage:** We may use your personal information to improve our marketing and promotional efforts. to statistically analyze website usage, to improve our content and product offerings and to customize our Sites content, layout, and features. We may also use your information to protect against and prevent fraud, physical and financial loss, unauthorized transactions, claims, and other liabilities, and to comply with applicable legal requirements, industry standards, and our policies. We may also use your information to send you materials regarding our products, services and promotions. To "opt-out" from such communications, see Opt-out Section below.  
**Cookies:** When you visit our Sites, our web server may automatically recognize your domain name, your computer's operating system, browser type, the address of the web page you're coming from, and the addresses of the pages you visit on our Sites. We use this data in the aggregate, to determine how often our visitors use parts of our Sites so we can improve your shopping experience. During your visit to our Sites, we use "cookies" to store and sometimes track information. A "cookie" is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. Generally, we use cookies to remind us who you are, and enable us to access your information so you do not have to re-enter it when you visit our Sites more than once. We also use cookies to gather statistical information about usage by registered or unregistered visitors; research visiting patterns; help target advertisements based on visitor interests; assist our retail partners in the tracking of visits and the processing of orders; and track the progress and participation in our marketing promotions. In some instances, our retail partners and advertisers appearing on our Sites may use their own cookies. Preference and options configurations in your browser determine if and how a cookie will be accepted. You can change those configurations on your computer if you desire. If you do so and cookies are disabled, you may be required to re-enter your information more often, and certain features on our Sites may be unavailable.  
**On-line Tracking/Behavioral or Interest-based Advertising:** Shopko tracks how you browse or shop our online website. We gather click stream information, cookies and session ids, type of browser used, and other browsing / shopping data. We use this information to personalize product advertising on Shopko.com or our partner sites. We do not disclose your personal data with advertising partners when we place partner ads on our website.   
**Do Not Track signals:** At this time, www.shopko.com is unable to alter its behavior or change its services upon receiving Do Not Track signal requests from the browser you’re using to access our website. However, you can configure your browser so you will not send us the data we track.   
**On-line orders:** When you make a purchase using a credit card on-line, we and/or our contractors may collect some or all of the following information: your user ID, password, name, shipping and billing addresses, e-mail address, shipping and billing phone numbers, card type, card number, card expiration date, and optional personal information. This information is used to collect payment for your purchase, ship your order, or send e-mail notices. You can be assured that our Sites comply with the Payment Card Industry Data Security standards, your account data information (including credit card numbers and expiration date) and other information given during the order process is protected according to prevailing industry practices.  
**Important note:** E-mail is not encrypted and is not a secure means of sending credit card information. Please place all orders through the Sites order form or with our Customer Service department via the telephone. Do not attempt to e-mail Customer Service an order.  
**E-mail:** If you communicate with us via e-mail, we archive your correspondence. This information may be used to follow-up with you. If you supply us with your postal address or telephone number on-line or via e-mail, we will use that address to contact you regarding information you requested. 

## Guest Wi-Fi(wireless service)

When you use Shopko Guest Wi-Fi, we automatically collect information about your use of the wireless service, including: utilization data, session data, type of device (such as iPhone, Android or Blackberry), browser data, browser version, IP address, store address, device data (such as device ID number and MAC address), websites and pages visited. We maintain administrative, technical and physical safeguards to protect your information.

We use and retain the information collected:

  * For internal operations to enhance the effectiveness of the guest wireless service, analyzing how it is used and improving the experience; 
  * To filter or block inappropriate content, such as adult content or pornography;
  * To provide reasonable security measures such as logging and monitoring use of the wireless service to help prevent unauthorized access;
  * For legal compliance to assist law enforcement and respond to legal/regulatory inquiries as may be deemed necessary by Shopko; 
  * To ensure compliance with Shopko Guest Wi-fi Terms of Use; and/or
  * For Promotional communications including marketing with banner ads and mobile coupons.



We share the information we collect within Shopko, which includes all subsidiaries and affiliates. Shopko may use this information to offer you products and services that may be of interest to you. We may share information with companies that provide support services to us or that help us market our products and services. These companies may need information about your use of the guest wireless service in order to perform their functions. We may share information with vendors, business partners and other organizations that may use the information we share to provide special offers and opportunities to you. 

We may share aggregate information (information that does not directly identify an individual) with third parties for lawful purposes. 

We may disclose your information when we believe disclosure is appropriate to comply with the law; to ensure compliance with the Shopko wireless terms of use, apply applicable terms and conditions and other agreements; or to protect the rights, property or safety of our company, our guests or others.

## Children's Policy

We recognize the importance of protecting privacy where children are involved. We are committed to protecting children’s privacy. The Sites and Guest Wi-Fi are not directed at children under the age of 18 and Shopko does not knowingly solicit or collect personal information from children under 18 without prior verifiable parental consent. If Shopko learns that a child under the age of 18 has submitted personal information to us without parental consent, we will take all reasonable measures to delete such information from our databases and/or to not use such information for any purpose (except as necessary to protect the safety of the child or others as required or allowed by law). If you become aware of any personal information we have collected from a child under 18, please contact us

## Notice to California Residents:

Your California Privacy Rights. Alternatively, this same law permits Shopko to provide you with a cost-free means to choose not to have your information shared rather than providing the above described information. As stated in our Privacy policy, we may share your personal information with third parties for direct marketing purposes regarding our products, services or promotions unless you "opt out" by the means described within such policy. California residents may request further information about our compliance with this law by sending a written request to the address below.
