What Types of Information Does Shopit.com Collect From Me?
Personal Information:
Shopit.com provides Web-based service permitting users, among other things, to list, browse, buy, sell and trade products, and to communicate with other users via email, instant messages and chat. In almost all cases, we do not obtain any Personal Information about you unless you choose to reveal it. You are not required to disclose any personally identifiable information to us, although certain parts of the Website and certain services may not be available to you unless you do so.

We collect Personal Information, such as your name, address, phone number, email address, credit card number, and financial information that you submit to us in the user account registration process. You will also have the option to fill out an online profile with additional Personal Information about yourself. You may update your Personal Information at any time by logging into the Website and editing your online profile. You can view your updated profile to confirm your edits have been made.

Shopit.com also collects the information you submit or upload to your account to list items in your "store" or "widget" - the areas of your account where you advertise products you want to make available for sale to other users. Such information includes products descriptions, images, pricing and video commercials. As further provided in the Shopit.com Terms of Use, we are the sole owner of all information we collect relating to item listings.

When you use Website email, chat, instant messaging or other similar features of the Website to communicate with other users, we collect the content of those communications. We will also collect the content of any messages you send to us, including emails sent in response to Shopit.com surveys or announcements regarding special offers, promotions or sweepstakes. If you sign up to receive a newsletter, we will collect your email address.

Non-Personal Information:
Like the operators of most websites, we also collect and log non-personal information that your browser makes available whenever you visit the Website, such as your browser type, browser language, the URL of the previous website you visited, Internet domain and host names, your ISP, operating system, clickstream patterns, your Internet protocol (IP) Address, and the dates and times that the Website and the Shopit.com services are accessed ("Non-Personal Information"). We also track and compile aggregate statistics regarding use of the Website ("Aggregate Data"). In addition, we contract with several online partners who may track Non-Personal Information and Aggregate Data to help us manage, monitor and optimize our Website and the Shopit.com services and to help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising, communications and visitor use of the Website. To do this, we may use web beacons and cookies. See "What About Cookies and Web Beacons?" below.

Non-Personal Information and Aggregate Data cannot be easily used to identify you personally. Although new techniques exist to obtain personally identifiable information using IP addresses, Shopit.com does not use IP addresses for that purpose; provided, however, that we may log the IP address of a user's computer and compare the online information provided in the Shopit.com profile to try to prevent, eliminate, and investigate user fraud.

Personal Information:
We use Personal Information primarily for our own internal purposes, such as providing, maintaining, evaluating, and improving the Shopit.com services and the Website, and providing customer support. Shopit.com automatically displays a limited amount of personally identifiable user profile information, including users' pictures (if provided), age and location to other users and the public in order to facilitate interaction and foster a sense of community among users of the Shopit.com services. The recipient's Shopit.com email address is not available to the sender unless the recipient chooses to reply. Users' non-Shopit.com email addresses are not displayed or revealed to other users when they communicate through the service. To facilitate searching and finding friends and acquaintances on the service, Shopit.com allows users to search for other members based on Personal Information provided in numerous categories in the public profiles.

We may periodically view or access individual records for the purpose of resolving a problem or dispute, a support issue, a suspected violation of the Shopit Terms of Service or as may be required by law. Shopit.com uses age information to limit access to Shopit.com services to users 13 and older.  Non-Personal Information:
We use Non-Personal Information and Aggregate Data to track the use of the Website and the Shopit.com Services and for other internal purposes, such as improving Website security, diagnosing and resolving technical problems, providing, maintaining, evaluating, and improving Shopit.com services and the Website, and monitoring, preventing and investigating user fraud and abuse. What About Cookies and Web Beacons?
A cookie is a small file placed on the hard drive of your computer. Most websites use cookies. We use cookies to track your use of the Website and the Shopit.com services, provide you with a more personalized user experience, and to allow you to login and begin use of the Shopit.com services automatically when you visit the Website. Users who disable their web browsers' ability to accept cookies will be able to browse the Website but will not be able to use certain services and features.

A web beacon (also known as a clear GIF or pixel tag) is an often-transparent graphic image, usually no larger than a 1x1 pixel that is placed on a web page or in an e-mail that is used to monitor the behavior of the user visiting the Website or receiving the e-mail. In all such instances, the web beacon collects only anonymous information about your visit to a particular website or receipt of email and sends that information to servers for processing. Shopit.com and/or its third-party service providers use pixel tags to better understand the traffic patterns of visitors.

Cookies and web beacons used by Shopit.com and our online partners are not linked to Personal Information. Some of our users may use cookies or web beacons on our Website in connection with their stores. We have no access to or control over these cookies and web beacons. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies and web beacons by Shopit.com and our service providers only and does not cover the use of cookies or web beacons by any other third party, Shopit.com user, advertiser or marketer. For more information about how our third party advertising and marketing partners use cookies and web beacons, see "Disclosure to Third-Party Advertisers and Marketers" above.
