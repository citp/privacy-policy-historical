# Rich and Skinny Denim ~ Free People Clothing ~ Trina Turk Dresses ~ Mara Hoffman ~ Blue Life ~ Diane Von Furstenberg ~ Havaianas ~ Lauren Conrad Dress ~ Dolce Vita Shoes ~ Single Dress ~ Bohemian Dress

Planet Blue is completely committed to protecting your privacy. The information you provide us during checkout is used to process orders and to assist us in delivering administration type emails.  Planet Blue utilizes every available technology to ensure that your confidential information remains secure.

  
Planet Blue holds your email address in strictest confidence.  We do not sell, give away, or otherwise distribute your address to any third party.  Users cannot opt out of administrative type emails such as order confirmation messages.  When you place an order, we'll ask you for your name, email address, mailing address, billing address, and credit card number with expiration date.  This information allows us to process and fulfill your order and to notify you of your order status.

  
Planet Blue uses your purchasing information for the purpose of providing you a more efficient and enjoyable shopping experience.  We also monitor customer traffic patterns and site usage to help us develop the content, design, and layout of the web site.

Planet Blue considers the information you share with us to be personal and confidential.  We do not sell, trade, or rent any of your personal information to others.

  Planet Blue releases personal account information, in good faith, when such release is believed to be reasonably necessary.  This includes situations in complying with the law; enforcing or applying the terms of any of our user agreements; or protecting the rights, property and safety of Planet Blue, our customers, or affected third parties.

By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Planet Blue.  If we amend our Privacy Policy, we will immediately post the changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we gather, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.  We suggest that you check this page on occasion to keep up with the most current policies.

  
Planet Blue allows you to choose not to receive additional communications via email. If you only wish to receive basic communications regarding your order status and tracking, you can unsubscribe by sending an email to [info@shopplanetblue.com](mailto:info@shopplanetblue.com?subject=RE%3A%20Privacy%20Policy).
