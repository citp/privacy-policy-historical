## Cookie/Tracking Technology

The Site may use cookie and tracking technology depending on the features offered. Cookie and tracking technology are useful for gathering information such as browser type and operating system, tracking the number of visitors to the Site, and understanding how visitors use the Site. Cookies can also help customize the Site for visitors. Personal information cannot be collected via cookies and other tracking technology, however, if you previously provided personally identifiable information, cookies may be tied to such information.

## Collection of Information

Non-Personal Information May include the user's operating system (Windows or Mac), type of web browser (Internet Explorer, Firefox), IP address, monitor screen resolution and color depth, referring pages, and frequency of visits. This information is used to analyze how visitors browse the site in order to make future improvements.

Personal Information Personal information collected from users of Showitfast.com may include first and last name, phone number, physical address, email address, comments, and permission from the user to add him/her to our mailing list. All personal information remains private within Showit, Inc. and is not distributed or sold to any outside organizations.

At Showit, Inc. your credit card and private information is not sold or shared with any third party and is used exclusively for your purchase at this site. By using the site, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy policy. These practices may be changed, but any changes will be posted and changes will only apply to activities and information on a going forward, not retroactive basis. You are encouraged to review the privacy policy whenever you visit the site to make sure that you understand how any personal information you provide will be used.
