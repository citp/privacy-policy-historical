Non-PII may also be collected from you by means of Cookies and Pixel Tags. Cookies are small bits of information that this Site places on your computer. We and our 3rd party service providers use cookies and Pixel Tags to identify your IP address, browser type, domain name, and specific web pages through which you click. This data is collected automatically and utilized to help us look for trends to improve our Site and your interactive experience. We use this information to customize your experience on our Site and on other Sears Holdings family of business Sites and to provide you with the most relevant products, services and offers.
