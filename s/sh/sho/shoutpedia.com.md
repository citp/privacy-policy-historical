# Privacy Policy

**Shoutpedia.Com** takes your privacy very seriously. Just spare a few moments to read this policy and see how we are doing.

Every server and almost every website tracks some basic information like your browser, IP address, your location etc but this list is not limited. This information may be used for site administraton and maintenance purposes.

Your email address is stored while commenting, registering on this site or contacting with us. But **em** **ail address will never be sold, used, published** without your prior permission unless you personally have chosen to opt-in to such services.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html). After clicking on any advertisement, you may be directed to another website. After redirection from this site, this policy will not remain valid. So read their privacy policy before you furthure browser those sites.

A web cookie is used to store the visitor information, but you can disable all the cookies. To disable them, read the documentation of the browser which you are using. You can also selectively disable the cookies on specific sites. But keep in mind that these cookies are sometimes required for better browsing on the site. You can read more about the cookies and their functins [here.](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTTP_cookie)

  * Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s (your) prior visits to this site.
  * Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  * You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [advertising opt-out page](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html). (Alternatively, you can also opt out of a third-party vendor’s use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative [opt-out page](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp).)
