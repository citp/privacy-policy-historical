****This policy addresses our operations with respect to our website, but does not address operations of any other websites we link to. This policy is incorporated into, and subject to the terms of, our user agreement.

**How we use the information**

We use information which you had provided us to keep track of the shreeweb services you have signed up to receive. We may also use your personal identifiable information so that we could improve our marketing and promotional efforts, we could statistically analyse site usage for better performance, and we could improve our content and product offerings and if necessary could customise our site's content and layout. Our attempt in doing this is to improve our site and tailor it to meet the needs of our customers.

We may also use your personal identifiable information so that to deliver necessary information that is of your interests, such as special offers and promotions. We use your email address and mobile number to contact you so that we could send you administrative notices or latest news from time to time.

By default after an account registration you receive important notices from the administrative and various newsletters about promotions and special offers through emails. You may opt-in to unsubscribe from receiving such emails in future by clicking on “unsubscribe” link or by sending an email for unsubscribing.

We use your information for filing the documents we maintain about you, and any other information that we obtain from your current and past activities on the site, to resolve disputes, troubleshoot problems and enforce our Terms and Conditions.

**Protection of Database Information**

Information's sent for using any of our services will remain the property of the customers. Shreeweb will at no time collect or redistribute this information without your consent, except where legally required by law to do so.

**Disclosure of Your Information**

We do not sell or rent any of your personal identifiable information to any third party. We do marketing and promotions using your personal identifiable information in some cases but we never reveal your personal identification anywhere publicly.

**If you get spammed messages on your cell phone?**

Shreeweb provides services where visitors contact us and get test accounts that enables them to send text messages to friends, family, colleagues, using the Shreeweb SMS Gateway. Our SMS Gateway have several software filters in place to avoid such spams and block spammers. However, we cannot guarantee that no spamming will ever occur. You could possibly block your phone from receiving any message from Shreeweb SMS Gateway or Shreeweb members by contacting us by email. Contact Us here to stop receiving such spam messages.

**Changing Your Information**

If you would like to change any information any time within your account, You could login from the provided interface or could email us here to do the same.

**Limits on Our Abilities**

Although your privacy is very important to us and we strive daily to put efforts and maintain your privacy, due to the existing legal and technical environment, we cannot ensure you fully that your personal identifiable information will not be disclosed to third parties in ways not described in this policy. Additionally, we can (and you authorize us to) disclose any information about you to private entities, law enforcement or other government officials as we, in our sole discretion, believe necessary or appropriate to address or resolve inquiries or problems. This will not be done for commercial purposes or gain from Shreeweb as such.

If you have any further questions regarding our privacy policy, Contact Us.

You acknowledge that acceptance of this privacy policy is a condition to your use of our services and you agree to be bound by all of its terms and conditions.
