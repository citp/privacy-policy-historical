# Search The Salt Lake Tribune

**Privacy Policy Statement**

**Utah Online®, wants you to:**

* Feel comfortable using our web sites.  

* Feel secure submitting information to us.  

* Contact us with your questions or concerns about privacy on Utah Online®.  

* Know that, by using our web site, you are consenting to the collection of certain data to be used by Utah Online®.  


**Utah Online® will:**

* Collect and store only that information volunteered by you (in the registration process or in response to surveys or contests) for internal marketing or research efforts only.  

* Create standard usage logs through our Web server. 
* Require certain personal information from you to activate and maintain access to site features, such as "chat" and message boards or opinion polls.  

* Request certain personal information (such as names, address, telephone numbers, e-mail address or household demographics) from you to help us draw conclusions about site traffic and overall usage, build a better site, and help our clients make decisions about advertising Utah Online®.  

* Prominently disclose to you, the intended use of any information to be collected from you or about your use of Utah Online®

Be advised that Utah Online works with a third party, Flycast, that serves ads to its sites. To find out more about how Flycast manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please [**visit their site.**](http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html)

**Utah Online® will not:**

* Use the Web server to recognize information regarding the e-mail address or domain name of individual visitors to our Web pages.  

* Sell lists or user names, telephone numbers, mailing addresses, e-mail addresses or any other personal information to third parties.  

* Give user names, or any other personal information, to an advertiser without the user's permission.  

* Disclose user information to someone else unless required by court order or allowed by the user.
