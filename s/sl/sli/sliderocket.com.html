SlideRocket Privacy Policy

Welcome to SlideRocket’s website, application, products, services and software (collectively referred to as the “Services”), operated by SlideRocket, Inc., a subsidiary of ClearSlide, Inc. SlideRocket allows you to create, collaborate and share stunning, media-rich presentations. Any registered user can create a presentation that can include content like photos, videos, audio recordings and links to other websites.

Tip: SlideRocket presentations can be made publicly available around the world.

The privacy and protection of your personal information is very important to us, and we have created this Privacy Policy in order to help you understand how SlideRocket collects and uses your personal information and how your information may be made available to third parties.

Information We Collect:

When you interact with our Services, we may collect and store your personal and other information, as further described below:

When you register for an account, request information or request support services from SlideRocket, we may require that you provide some personal information, including your name, email address, password and birthdate. For some organizational, corporate or educational accounts, we may ask you to provide additional information, including your phone number, the name and size of your company, organization or school, your job title, billing information and address, postal code, contact information and your website. Additionally, if your account is part of a larger organizational account, such as a corporate account or an educational account, we may receive your email address from your organization, corporation or school’s account administrator. However, your account will not be active with SlideRocket until you have completed the registration process.

Information Collected when You Use SlideRocket’s Services:

We may also collect and store personal information about you that you provide to us through your use of SlideRocket’s Services, including personally identifiable information, photographs, video or audio recordings, and geolocational information through your IP address (as described below).

Additionally, SlideRocket may allow you to make your information available by publishing your presentation; commenting on users’ presentations; participating in surveys or polls in a users’ presentations; posting in support and community forums or blogs; and by collaborating with other users and their presentations. SlideRocket may, from time to time, respond to comments and questions posted on our forums and blogs. We may also send emails regarding our products and services to users who have posted to our discussion and support forums, blogs, user-editable documents, and commenting systems. Information Collected when You View a Presentation:

If you view a presentation as a registered SlideRocket user, by the invitation of a SlideRocket user or as a website guest, we may collect details about your viewing, including your name, contact information, IP address and geolocation information through your IP address (as described below). Additionally, we may track certain details about your viewing experience such as how long you spent on each slide, how much time you spent on a presentation, whether or not you shared another user’s presentation, and whether or not you choose to use some of the slides in your own presentation.

Information Collected About Others:

We may also collect and store personal information about other people that you provide to us through your use of SlideRocket’s Services, including any photos, images, audio and video recordings, information submitted through third party plug-ins, and/or confidential or sensitive personal information that you choose to upload or provide to our site. At your request, we may use guests’ contact information, including their name and email addresses, in order to invite them to view your presentations, to allow guests to respond to and comment on your presentations, and allow them to participate in interactive features that you choose to place into your presentations. By providing us with your guests’ information, you are telling us that you are entitled to use and provide us with your guests’ name and contact information.

For your convenience and ease-of-use, SlideRocket may allow you to log into your SlideRocket account through various third party applications and social networking sites, such as Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, or Google. Information Collected from Cookies and Web Technologies:

SlideRocket uses cookies on our Services.

Cookies are small amounts of data send to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer’s hard drive. Cookies can help a website recognize repeat users, and allow a website to track web usage behavior. Cookies work by assigning a number to the user that has no meaning outside of the assigning website. If a cookie is placed, thereafter when you visit that website, a message is sent back to the web server by the browser accessing the website. You can set your browser to notify you when you are sent a cookie, and you may also elect to not accept cookies by the designated settings on your web browser. However, not utilizing cookies may prevent you from using certain functions and features of websites.

SlideRocket uses some cookies that are strictly necessary for the delivery of services on our website and application. These cookies allow us to improve the safety and security of our Services, authenticate account users, assist with our shopping cart and marketplace, protect your privacy and sharing preferences, establish settings for your presentations, and verify that our users are eligible to use our Services.

SlideRocket uses cookies to improve our Service and personalize your web-browsing and SlideRocket experience, such as by gaining a better understanding of your interests and requirements regarding our site and application. These cookies may remember your settings and Services preferences, including prepopulating forms that you have previously filled.

SlideRocket also uses “performance” cookies that collect information about your use of our Services, including which pages you visit. These cookies use information to provide statistics on how our Services are used, help us optimize our Services by measure the traffic on our site, help us know how you came to our Services, help us to know how many devices you use to access our Services, and test different designs on our Services. SlideRocket’s performance cookies are only used to help us improve how our Services works, understand what interests our users, and measure how effective our advertising is.

Additionally, third party cookies may analyze your purchase selections in our online marketplace. Further, if you choose to import data from a third party site into your presentation, such as a flash video, then the third party media may contain a cookie or local shared object embedded in the media. SlideRocket does not have access to any data that is collected by any cookies or local shared objects that may be attached to media that you choose to put into your presentation, and the function and use of those cookies are outside of the use of this Privacy Policy. Third party cookies placed without the authority of SlideRocket may have the capabilities to override your browser settings and privacy choices, and SlideRocket is not responsible for the data collection or use by cookies or local shared objects located in any media you upload from third parties and their sites and applications.

Some SlideRocket pages use cookies that permit select third party partners, including Google, to serve you SlideRocket-related content, including SlideRocket advertisements, on their sites or elsewhere on the Internet. This is based on your prior visits to our site. You may opt out of Google’s use of cookies by visiting: http://www.google.com/privacy/ads/.

As is true of most websites, the SlideRocket Services collect and/or log Internet Protocol addresses (IP addresses), Internet domain names, your web browser and operating system, your location through your IP address (as described below), the files you visit, the time you spend in each file, the date of each visit, and how you arrived at our site, including which search engine you used, which search phrase you clicked on and/or which advertisement you clicked-through. SlideRocket collects these through the use of log files and web beacons.
