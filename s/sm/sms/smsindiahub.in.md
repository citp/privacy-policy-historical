# Smsindiahub Bulk SMS Privecy Policy | SMSIndiaHUB

  * Any personal information we collect from you will never be sold to any third party firm.
  * SMSINDIAHUB may send Users periodic emails and SMS containing information about your a/c status, festive offers, administrative notices about a User’s account (such as notices about pending activities), updates on new Service features, TRAI updates, usage tips relating to the Services and user satisfaction questions.
  * Once you visit our website, we monitor your activity for security reasons and to provide you with the information you’re looking for.
  * We actively monitor IP address of the connecting SMSINDIAHUB clients and store it in our database. If there is any kind of abuse of SMSINDIAHUB Services, We will work with relevant authorities to identify abusers and take legal action.



Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time without any prior update, so we request you to check the same regularly.
