# Smashrun - Stats for runners

## Privacy Policy

Smashrun believes that social accountability can be inherently motivating and can help runners maintain an active lifestyle. For this reason, you run data is public by default, but you can always change it within your Profile Settings. By creating an account on Smashrun, you are accepting the practices outlined in this policy. 

This Privacy Policy covers Smashrun's treatment of your personal information while you are on Smashrun's website and when you use Smashrun services. This policy does not apply to the practices of third parties that Smashrun does not own or control. 

**Introduction** This website and any other URLs and websites administered under the Smashrun brand and its affiliates (collectively, the "Website") are offered by New York on Tap, Inc. and its affiliates (collectively, "Smashrun" or "We" or "Us") to You, the User, subject to your acceptance of all the terms and conditions set forth below ("Terms of Service"). The terms "You" and "User" refers to all individuals and entities that access the Website. 

Smashrun is committed to maintaining the privacy, integrity, and security of any personal information about our Users. This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") explains how We protect personal information provided through our Website and how We use and, in some cases, disclose that information in connection with our service offered through the Website (the "Service"). "Personal Information" means information that identifies You, such as your name, age, address, phone number, or email address. 

This Privacy Policy is subject to the provisions of our current Terms of Service, which can be found at <http://smashrun.com/tos>. 

BY VISITING OR USING THE SMASHRUN WEBSITE, YOU ACCEPT AND CONSENT TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY AND THE SMASHRUN TERMS OF SERVICE. IF YOU DO NOT WANT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION TO BE USED IN THE MANNER DESCRIBED IN THIS PRIVACY POLICY, PLEASE DO NOT VISIT OR USE THE WEBSITE. 

If You have any security related concerns, please contact [ support@smashrun.com](mailto:support@smashrun.com), and we will work closely with You to ensure a rapid and personal response to your concerns. 

We take your privacy very seriously. If you ever have any concerns about security, please contact us at [support@smashrun.com](mailto:support@smashrun.com)

**We Limit the Collection and Disclosure of Personal Information** Although certain areas and features of Smashrun.com are available to You without registration or the need to provide to Us any personally identifiable information, your acceptance of Smashrun's Terms of Service are mandatory. Some features of the Website or the Service may require registration, which requires You to provide minimally your first and last name and an email address and to create an account password (collectively the "Registration Information"). You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login ID and password. In order to benefit from the full functionality of the Service, You also have the option to provide additional details about yourself, such as weight, gender, age, etc. WE DO NOT AND WILL NOT SELL OR RENT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION TO ANYONE, FOR ANY REASON, AT ANY TIME. 

Smashrun only uses our Users' personal information as follows: 

  * To analyze Website usage and improve Service; 
  * To communicate to You any relevant announcements or notices relevant to your use of the Service; 
  * For internal market research, development planning, troubleshooting problems, and detecting and protecting against error, fraud, or other criminal activity; 
  * To enforce the Smashrun Terms of Service; and 
  * As otherwise set forth in this Policy. 



In addition, access to your Registration Information, Account Credentials, Account Information and any other personal information that You provide is restricted strictly to Smashrun employees and contractors, as needed in order to operate, develop or improve the Service. This access is subject to Smashrun's strict internal standards of security and confidentiality. 

In some instances, We may request other personal information in order to provide You with access to additional features and benefits of the Service. In all such occasions, You will be given the option of whether or not to provide that information, and any information that You provide will be used only for the purpose(s) stated at the time You provide that information. 

Smashrun does not knowingly collect or store personal information from persons under 13 years of age. 

We collect registration and profile data and use it to improve Smashrun. We may also email you about new features that will make you happy. 

**Account Settings and User Generated Content** You should be aware that any information You submit to the Website can be read, collected, or used by other users or third parties visiting your user profile. WE DISCLAIM LIABILITY FOR ANY AND ALL PERSONAL INFORMATION YOU CHOOSE TO SUBMIT TO THE WEBSITE. 

Through our Service, Smashrun enables You, if You choose, to share information with others, as this is a crucial component of positive social reinforcement. SMASHRUN IS DEDICATED TO PROVIDING YOU WITH FULL CONTROL OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA. Smashrun's default settings for new users are set to PUBLIC, which allows anyone to see your “Run Overview” pages, “Ranks,” “Badges,” and your “Invite” page. However, You can always opt to maintain as PRIVATE your Smashrun user profile within your settings. We encourage You to consider this privacy setting before sharing information on the Website. 

Generally, Smashrun encourages search engine access to the Website and instructs search engines to index “public” user profile pages. SETTING YOUR PROFILE TO “PRIVATE” PREVENTS SEARCH ENGINES FROM INDEXING YOUR PAGE. 

You are always in control of your privacy settings. To hide your stats from everyone and to tell Google not to index your page, just visit your Profile Settings page. 

**Online Session Information (Cookies)** Smashrun uses standard web browser cookies. Most of the cookies that Smashrun sets are for the purpose of maintaining your logged-in state to the Website. An additional cookie helps Us determine whether You read the blog posts that We write, and another cookie helps us determine your timezone so that We can attempt to keep the leaderboards correct! It is highly likely that other future site functionality may utilize cookies as well. REST ASSURED, HOWEVER: NO PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION IS EVER STORED INSIDE THESE COOKIES, AND THEY CAN BE SAFELY DELETED AT ANY TIME. 

**Analytics and Conversion Tracking** Smashrun uses Google Analytics to help us evaluate how users and visitors interact with the site. 

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information in an anonymous form. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website, including your IP address, is transmitted to Google. This information is then used to analyze how users and visitors interact with the site and to compile reports on site activity. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. 

You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser. However, please note that if you do this, you may not be able to use the full functionality of this website. For more information or to opt-out, please see [Google’s Advertising and Privacy Policy](http://www.google.com/policies/technologies/ads/). 

Additionally, Smashrun has implemented Twitter website tags to monitor site visits via Twitter and to help us track the actions taken by a visitor after viewing or engaging with a tweet from Smashrun. This information will also be used to help us measure the results of our marketing efforts through promoted tweets. To opt-out, please see [Twitter’s privacy controls for tailored ads](https://support.twitter.com/groups/51-me/topics/267-security-privacy/articles/20170405-your-privacy-controls-for-tailored-ads). 

By using Smashrun, you consent to the processing of data about your interaction with this site by Google Analytics and Twitter in the manner and for the purposes set out above. 

A word on so-called “supercookies.” As technology progresses, so do the methods of tracking Users' online activities – specifically, their interactions with websites and advertisers. 

SMASHRUN DOES NOT CONDONE THE USE OF SUPERCOOKIES AND WILL NEVER ATTEMPT TO SURREPTITIOUSLY TRACK OR IDENTIFY YOU WITHOUT CONSENT ACROSS THIRD PARTY WEBSITES OR PLATFORMS. SMASHRUN ALSO WILL NOT DO BUSINESS WITH ADVERTISERS OR OTHER THIRD PARTIES THAT ATTEMPT TO OBFUSCATE THEIR USER TRACKING METHODS THROUGH SUPERCOOKIES OR ANY OTHER TECHNICAL METHODS. 

We will track you via industry-standard web browser cookies, so we can remember you and help load things a bit faster for your next visit. However, we will never attempt to use supercookies or any other surreptitious method of tracking you. 

**Promotions** From time to time, Smashrun may offer You the opportunity to participate in contests, giveaways and other promotions. Any information submitted in connection with such activities will be subject to this Policy, except as specifically set forth in the rules for those contests, giveaways or promotions. 

In the event that we're giving away cool stuff, any information you submit for the giveaway is subject to this same policy unless otherwise noted. 

**Disclosure of Your Information to Protect Our Rights or If Required by Law** Notwithstanding the foregoing, Smashrun reserves the right, and You authorize Smashrun, to share or disclose your personal information when Smashrun determines, in its sole discretion, that the disclosure of such information is necessary or appropriate: 

  * To enforce our rights against You or in connection with a breach by You of this Policy or the Smashrun Terms of Use; 
  * To prevent prohibited or illegal activities; or 
  * When required by any applicable law, rule, regulation, subpoena or other legal process; or
  * In connection with sale of our company or any of its assets, or disposition of its assets in bankruptcy. 



We may release your information to authorities where required by law. 

**Security of Your Data** We use a combination of firewall barriers and authentication procedures, among others, to maintain the security of your data and to protect Smashrun accounts and systems from unauthorized access. When You register for the Service, Smashrun requires that You set a password to ensure your privacy and security. Please keep this password safe! 

However, it is important to understand that these precautions apply only to our Website and systems. WE DISCLAIM LIABILITY FOR HOW YOUR INFORMATION IS STORED, MAINTAINED, OR DISPLAYED BY THIRD PARTIES OR ON THIRD-PARTY WEBSITES. 

Your Smashrun account is protected by encryption and we keep it secured but, you must also keep it safe. 

**Policy Changes** The Smashrun Privacy Policy is subject to change at any time, and We will notify all of our Users of any amendments made to our Policy. The Policy's most recently revised date will always appear at the bottom of the Policy. Changes take effect immediately upon posting. 

For any questions, comments, concerns, or feedback regarding this Policy or any other privacy or security concerns, please contact Smashrun at support@smashrun.com.
