  • Privacy Policy

    • SmartWebby.com Privacy Policy

      Jandus Technologies is committed to preserving your privacy online when you use our website SmartWebby.com (this site - also referred to as SmartWebby). Details of our information practices are as follows.

      The Information We Collect

      When you visit SmartWebby.com we collect two types of information from you:
      1) specific information you voluntarily submit, and
      2) general information our site gathers automatically.

      • Specific Information You Submit
        If you buy a SmartWebby.com product/service or email SmartWebby for any reason, the information in your email will only be used by us to contact you directly.
        Information Use: We will keep your information on file only for the purpose you signed up for or for providing pre and post sales service. We use a third party credit card processor so we do not store sensitive financial information. The credit card processor provides information to the site about whether the charge transaction was approved or not.

        We will never willfully disclose any individually identifiable information to any third parties without a user's specific permission to do so. We do not sell users' names or email addresses to other companies. We will only provide individually identifiable information to third parties only where it is required by law.

      • General Information We Gather Automatically
        As you browse our Web site, we collect standard tracking data.
        Information Use: This information is aggregated with tracking data from all site visitors and is used to evaluate site traffic and usage patterns. It is never associated with individually identifiable information that would reveal anything personal about a user. We use such aggregated information to help us evaluate our site and to improve our services to clients.

      Our Commitment To Children's Privacy

      We never collect or maintain information at our web site from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our web site is structured to attract anyone under 13.

      Security

      The web site takes every precaution to protect your information. When you submit sensitive information via the web site, your information is protected both online and off-line.

      When our order form asks you to enter sensitive information on our third party credit card processor, that information is encrypted and is protected with SSL.

      Supplementation of Information

      Information on SmartWebby.com is not supplemented with any outside sources, however we do use the following:

      • Third Party Plugins
        We use facebook social plugins and twitter code so you can stay connected with us.

      • Third Party Ads
        We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.

      • Third Party Links
        SmartWebby.come contains some external links to other web sites, such as (but not limited to) promotion, design, and webmaster resource sites. SmartWebby has no control over the content of third party sites and is not responsible for any information or content contained on these sites, nor is SmartWebby responsible for the privacy policies of those sites and the information they may gather expressly or automatically. User Discretion is advised when clicking on third party links. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every Web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site.

      Updating your Personal Information

      If your personally identifiable information changes (such as zip code, phone, email or postal address), or if you no longer desire our service, you can have this done by using the Contact information contained in this privacy statement.

      Contact Information

      If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us using our customer service input form.
