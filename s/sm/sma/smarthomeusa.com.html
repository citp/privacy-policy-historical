Cookies
Our Site uses cookies to save your password, ship-to and bill-to address (but not your credit card number), so you don't have to re-enter your information each time you visit our site. A cookie is a file which is placed on your machine's hard drive, and which contains information that may be accessed by our server the next time you visit our site.
