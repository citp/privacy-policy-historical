# Privacy Policy

Smart Profits Auto Trader respects the privacy of every individual who visits our website (www.smartprofits.com). This privacy statement provides notice of the standards and terms under which www.smartprofits.com protects the privacy of information supplied by visitors to sites on the World Wide Web that are owned and operated by www.smartprofits.com. This privacy statement provides notice of our information collection practices and of the ways in which your information may be used. This policy may change from time to time, so please check back periodically to review this information. 

By providing your email or clicking on "continue", you are giving us permission to send you emails about our offers. If you chose not to receive any more emails, you can always opt out at any time at the bottom of each email. Likewise, you can contact our support team at support@smartprofits.com and ask to be removed from the mailing list.

**A. Personally-Identifiable Information:**

www.smartprofits.com typically receives specific data about its website visitors only when such information is provided voluntarily, such as when our visitors request information, purchase or enroll for services, open a customer support inquiry ticket, provide resume information for employment opportunities, and/or send us e-mails. Some of these activities require that you give us information, such as when you make a purchase, use a credit card to pay for services, submit your resume, or request certain types of information. When you provide personally- identifiable information to www.smartprofits.com through one of our websites, it will be used to fulfill your specific request. In most cases, you will be given the opportunity to select whether or not you want www.smartprofits.com to use this information for additional purposes. You may also request that www.smartprofits.com not use your information by sending an e-mail to: support@www.smartprofits.com , however Smart Profits Auto Trader reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to send you newsletters and other important information about your www.smartprofits.com services. Smart Profits Auto Trader may use the information that you provide in order to inform you about additional services and products offered by us. www.smartprofits.com will not, however, sell or trade your personally-identifiable information unless we are authorized or legally required to do so, or in the case of imminent physical harm to the visitor or others. When you provide www.smartprofits.com with credit card or other ordering information via the web, www.smartprofits.com protects and secures this information by employing commercially customary web- based security and encryption protocols, examples of which include Secure Socket Layer (SSL) and Secure Electronic Transaction (SET). Voluntarily offered feedback, data, answers, questions, comments, suggestions, ideas, etc. will be treated as non-confidential and nonproprietary except as otherwise expressed in this privacy statement. Smart Profits Auto Trader assumes no obligation to protect such information from disclosure. 

Examples in these materials are not to be taken as a promise or guarantee of earnings. Earning potential is entirely dependent on the person using our product, ideas, techniques and the effort put forth. We do not purport this as a "get rich scheme" and nor should you view it as such. 

**B. Non Personally-Identifiable (Generic) Information:**

In general, www.smartprofits.com gathers some generic information automatically. Generic information does not reveal the identity of the visitor. It usually includes information about the Internet address assigned to your computer, the number and frequency of visitors, and the www.smartprofits.com sites visited. www.smartprofits.com gathers this information for the limited purpose of determining customer service and website needs. We accomplish this by using certain technologies, including "cookies" (a technology that can be used to provide the visitor with tailored information about www.smartprofits.com services). www.smartprofits.com does not combine information collected in this way with any personally-identifiable information. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie and you can refuse it. 

**C. Your www.smartprofits.com hosted website, server, bulletin boards, forum, Third-Party Sites:**

Information that you disclose in a public space, including on any bulletin board, chat room, social media channel, or website www.smartprofits.com may host for you as part of your www.smartprofits.com services is available to anyone else who visits that space. www.smartprofits.com cannot safeguard any information you disclose in these locations. Additionally, www.smartprofits.com websites contain links to sites that belong to third parties unrelated to www.smartprofits.com. www.smartprofits.com cannot protect any information you may disclose in these sites and recommends that you review the privacy policy statements of those sites you visit. 

**D. Exceptions and Limitations:**

Notwithstanding the foregoing and in compliance with applicable laws, www.smartprofits.com (i) cooperates fully with state, local, and federal officials in any investigation relating to any content (including personal or private electronic communications transmitted to www.smartprofits.com) or purported unlawful activities of any user of the Service, and (ii) takes reasonable measures to protect its proprietary rights. For the limited purposes of accomplishing such cooperation and measures and in compliance with applicable laws, www.smartprofits.com may be required to disclose personally identifiable information. In addition, www.smartprofits.com may elect to monitor the areas of communication of any kind (i) to satisfy any law, regulation, or government request; (ii) if such disclosure is necessary or appropriate to operate www.smartprofits.com; or (iii) to protect the rights or property of www.smartprofits.com or others. In connection with the potential sale or transfer of any of its interest in www.smartprofits.com and other sites owned by the company. smartprofits.com reserves the right to sell or transfer your information (including , but not limited to name, address information, and other information you provided to www.smartprofits.com) to a third party that (i) concentrates its business in communication products or services; (ii) agrees to be www.smartprofits.com's successor in interest with regard to the maintenance and protection of information collected and maintained by www.smartprofits.com; and (iii) agrees to the obligations of this policy statement.
