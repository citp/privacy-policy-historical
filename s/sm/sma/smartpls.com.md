# SmartPLS GmbH Privacy Policy Statement | SmartPLS

This Data Protection Policy applies to any processing of personal data with regard to Smart PLS GmbH, in particular the visit of the website https://www.smartpls.com/. Personal data means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, such as a name, the date of birth, an e-mail address or an IP-address. Processing means any operation or set of operations which is performed on personal data or on sets of personal data such as collection, recording, storage or any other use.

Data Protection is important for us. For this reasons we pay high attention to treat your personal data confidential and in accordance with data protection regulations, in particular with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) being into force from May 25, 2018 and this privacy policy.

## 3\. Collection, processing, use and storage of personal data

The way of collection, processing and use of personal data as well as the duration of storage depend on the way of you staying in contact with us. To understand the details of processing, we list the different categories of contact with us together with detailed information to purpose and legal basis.

### Visit of our website

When visiting our website the provider automatically collects, stores and transmits to us "SERVER-LOG-FILES” containing the following information: IP-address, browser type and browser version, operating system used, referrer URL, host name of the accessing computer, time of the server request. Such data processing provides an effective and comfortable use of website, avoidance of failure-free operation of our website and improvement of the administration of the website. The basis for data processing is Art. 6, paragraph 1 lit. f GDPR.

SmartPLS uses the services of the hosting company Domainfactory GmbH, Oskar-Messter-Str. 33, 85737 Ismaning, Germany, https://www.df.eu/int/impressum/ (“Domainfactory”) located within the European Union for any data processing in conjunction with the use of our website. This is governed by a data processing contract, as a result of our legitimate interests in a correct and professional presentation of our products on our website.

We use COOKIES on our website. Cookies help to make our website more user-friendly, efficient, and secure. Cookies do not harm your computer and do not contain any viruses. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer and saved by your browser. Most of the cookies are session cookies which are deleted automatically after your visit. Others remain in your device's memory until you delete them allowing us to recognize your browser when you next visit the site. As a visitor of our website you can control the use of cookies. You can configure your browser and decide, case by case, whether to accept or reject a cookie. Alternatively, your browser can be configured to automatically accept cookies under certain conditions or to always reject them, or to automatically delete cookies when closing your browser. Disabling cookies may limit the functionality of this website. Our use of cookies has the purpose to provide service free of technical errors, which is based on legitimate interests according to Art. 6, paragraph 1 lit. f GDPR.

In addition to the above we use cookies provided third parties such as Google analytics and Drip. Detailed information to this are given later in this privacy note.

## Google Analytics

Google Analytics is a web analysis service operated by Google. Google Analytics stores cookies on your computer for the purposes of evaluating your use of the website, compiling reports on website activity for website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage. Google uses the data collected to analyze the surfing patterns of visitors on our website. The information generated by the cookie about your use of this website (including your IP address) will be sent to a Google server in the United States and stored there. Google will use this information to evaluate your use of the website, to compile reports on website activity for website operators and to report other website activity and internet-related services. Detailed information about Google Analytics can be found here: https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/6004245?hl=en.

You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser. However, SmartPLS points out that this might lead to limited functionality of our website. Furthermore, you can prevent Google’s collection and use of data (cookies and IP address) by downloading and installing the browser plug-in available under https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en. 

Google will anonymize the last octet of the IP address for Member States of the European Union as well as for other parties to the Agreement on the European Economic Area. Only in exceptional cases, the full IP address is sent to Google servers in the USA but the IP address is anonymized before being stored.
