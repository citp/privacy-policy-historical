# Privacy Policy | Smart Publications

Smart Publications (SP) is committed to maintaining your confidence and trust, and accordingly maintains the following privacy policy to protect personal information you provide online.

##  What Information Do We Collect?

We automatically collect your Internet service provider's address, the web page from which you came, and a record of your activity on our site.

In addition, Smart Publications invites viewers to sign up for both/either our monthly printed newsletter or our email newsletters. Smart Publications Health & Wellness Updates are groundbreaking newsletters filled with articles, interviews and reviews of the best sources for hard-to-get health supplements. Each issue also shares recent scientific research and tips on ordering life-extending pharmaceuticals and smart drugs at tremendous savings and without a prescription. When you sign up for a Smart Publications Health & Wellness Update subscription, we collect information that you volunteer such as your name, email address, and postal address to process your order. Subscriptions can be canceled at any time.

##  Help Us Keep Your Personal Information Accurate

If your personal information changes or you would like to review the personal information we may have on file, please contact us with the new information or your review request.

##  Data Release

Smart Publications will abide by this privacy policy except as required by subpoena, search warrant or other legal process or in the case of imminent physical harm to a member, non-member or others.

##  Cookies

Smart Publications' Internet website uses "cookies". Cookies are messages that a Web server transmits to a Web browser so that the Web server can keep track of the user's activity on a specific Web site. The message that the Web server conveys to the browser is in the form of an HTTP header that consists of a text-only string. The text is entered into the memory of the browser. The browser in turn stores the cookie information on the hard drive so when the browser is closed and reopened at a later date the cookie information is still available. We use this information to collect demographic information such as frequency of user visits and the amount of time spent on the website.

##  Links to Outside Websites

Smart Publications' privacy policy applies only to its website. Our website may contain links to outside sites. Please be aware that other sites operate under their own privacy policies and that these sites may collect your personal information. Smart Publictions claims no responsibility for the data collection policies of outside websites.

##  Notification of Changes

In the event that Smart Publications should change its privacy practices or start using member information in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection, we would notify our customers of the changes, explaining the reasons and allowing them the opportunity to accept the changed policy, or demand exemption from it.
