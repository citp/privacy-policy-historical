# Smallpdf.com – Legal and Privacy

Zurich, 2014-01-24

Your privacy is important to us. To be transparent about your privacy, we explain in this notice the practices of Immaterial LLC regarding the way your information is collected and used.   
All content on smallpdf.com and its subdomains are created and owned by the Immaterial GmbH (LLC), Zurich (Switzerland).   
We reserve the right to change our Privacy Policy at any time. If changes occur, we will clearly indicate such alterations at the top of this page with the date of modification. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, please do not use any of our services and let us know about your concerns. 

### Logging IP Addresses

By visiting any domain or subdomain of smallpdf.com, your IP address will be logged along with the dates and times of access. All of this information is used only for administration purposes and broad demographic analysis. Most importantly, all of this data is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

In brief: We do log your IP but we are not able to link this information to who you are.

### Storage of User Files and generated data

All the files uploaded for conversion or compression on smallpdf.com are stored on our servers for conversion and the download afterwards. All user-uploaded files as well as the converted output files will be deleted after one hour after upload or conversion respectively. No backups are made of any uploaded files nor their converted output neither are the contents monitored without the explicit permission of user.

In brief: All files are deleted after one hour. No backups are made and no one has access to your files. 

### Security

We are committed to protect your information and your data. Our servers are protected and only employees of Immaterial LLC are able to access the data we temporarily store as we mentioned above.

In brief: We do a lot to make our service as secure as possible.

### How we use cookies

A cookie is a small piece of data placed on your computer’s hard drive that enables a service to monitor websites.  
We do not use any cookies on our site with the exception of cookies solely for functional purposes, without storing any user information.  
Third party applications that we use such as Google Analytics and Google Adsense do use cookies on our site to help analyse the web traffic or determine targeted advertisements based on your preferences, your visit on our sites and other sites on the internet.  
To opt out of Google’s use of cookies, please visit the Google ad and content network privacy policy.  
If you prefer not to use cookies at all, we recommend adjusting your browser settings accordingly. If you are unsure how to do that, use your favorite search engine to find out everything about it.

In brief: These cookies you can’t eat. We don’t use them but Google Analytics and Google Adsense do.

### Transfer and sharing of data

When you use our services, data is transferred to and from servers located in the United States. Uploaded files and generated files through our service are never stored longer than one hour. Also, user files as well as converted files are never shared with third parties.

In brief: Files are never shared with third parties.

If you do have any questions or concerns please email us at [info@smallpdf.com](mailto:info@smallpdf.com)

We are working hard to ensure that the services we offer are error free but we cannot guarantee that user files or converted files will never be deleted or lost. As a user of this service you agree that Immaterial LLC will not be held responsible for any failure of conversion or deletion of a user file as well as any loss or damage to the user for such failures. We recommend you to archive your files and backing up all user files and data.  
Immaterial LLC respects the copyright of others and we expect our users to do the same. We expressively prohibited to use our services for any unauthorized copying, modification, distribution or any other infringement of copyright holder’s rights of any copyrighted material.  
We do our best to make our services as great as possible and to keep them free. However, bear in mind that you use all our services on your own risk and we are not able to take any responsibility for any loss, damage or any other problems you might have by using our service.
