# Privacy Policy of Smallvideosoft.

**Privacy Policy**

SmallVideoSoft does not gather any personally identifiable data such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address from users of this web site without prior notification.

You may choose to join specific mailing lists according to your preferences or just email us with your query.  
SmallVideoSoft uses your email address ONLY to send replies or messages related to the topic of the mailing list you joined. It will NEVER BE SOLD or disclosed to third parties.

When paying for our products, additional information may be required to process your order. Any information you provide will not be sold or given to anyone. All such information is used ONLY by SmallVideoSoft for further processing of your order, and notifying you about the updates by email.
