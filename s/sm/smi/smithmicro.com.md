# Smith Micro Software

_Our Commitment To Privacy_

Your privacy is important to Smith Micro. We recognize that when you choose to provide us with information about yourself, you trust us to act in a responsible manner. To better protect this personal information we provide this notice explaining our information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used to personalize your experience with Smith Micro.

Smith Micro respects the rights users give us when opting to receive e-mail communications and enforces internal policies to preserve those rights. It is our objective to retain the long-term ability to continue to communicate with our users.

_Data Security_  
Smith Micro is committed to taking reasonable steps to protect your personal information that we collect. An example is when you provide us with a credit/debit card number (and other sensitive information). Smith Micro will assure that we protect this information through a secure (SSL - Secure Socket Layers) connection.

_Collecting Your Personal Information_  
This Privacy Statement is made available in order to make you aware of how Smith Micro collects and uses personal information of visitors to our website or through other means. We collect the following information while you are downloading trial versions of our products, placing an order, registering a product, requesting technical or customer support, answering a survey, corresponding to us or engaging in any other activities with Smith Micro. For example:

  * When placing an order we gather your name, company (if applicable), billing and/or shipping address, telephone number, email address, and credit/debit card number in order for us to complete the transaction;
  * When registering a Smith Micro product, we gather your name, company (if applicable), address, phone number, fax number, email address and product information;
  * While visiting technical or customer support, we may selectively gather information to better help us service your needs such as name, email address, computer hardware and software information, and the nature of your question;
  * On some website pages, you can make requests to receive materials where the types of personal information collected at these pages are typically name, address, phone number, email address, etc.; and
  * On some website pages, you can submit information about other people. For example, if you order a product online and want it sent directly to the recipient, you will need to submit the recipient's address. In this circumstance, the types of personal information collected are: name, address, phone number, etc. 



You make the decision whether to continue with any activity that requests personal information. Depending upon the activity, some of the information that we ask you to provide is identified as mandatory and some as voluntary. If you do not provide the mandatory data with respect to a particular activity, you will not be able to engage in that activity.

_Log Files_  
We use IP addresses in web server log files to analyze trends, administer the site, track a user's movements, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. Such information in log files does not enable us to access personally identifying information, such as email addresses.

_Using Your Personal Information_  
Smith Micro uses the personal information we gather for the following purposes:

  * To provide you with information about Smith Micro product updates, new service and product announcements, and any other product related information that may be of interest to you;
  * To help us create new products, enhance new services, and create content that supports your needs;
  * To help us inform you of new partnerships that we have entered into that may be of interest to you; and
  * To inform you of special offers that may be of interest to you.
  * If you desire to not receive information from Smith Micro, you can request to be removed by following the procedures described below.



_Sharing Your Personal Information_  
From time to time, Smith Micro may provide your information to third parties, including selected business partners, for marketing purposes to communicate to you about products or services that they believe are important to you or your business.

If you desire that we not provide your information to these third party and business partners, you can request to be removed by following the procedures described below.

Please be advised that in certain instances, it may be necessary for Smith Micro to disclose your personal information to government officials or otherwise as required by our legal obligations.

_Choice/Consent_  
We are always working on ways to better enable you to communicate with us and to let us know that your personal information should not be used for these or future purposes. Smith Micro currently provides users several ways to "opt out" of having personal information used for the purposes described above. 

_Online:_ Go to <http://www.smithmicro.com/list/unsubscribe.tpl> and enter the email address to be removed. 

_E-Mail:_ Send an email to [cs@smithmicro.com](mailto:cs@smithmicro.com?subject=Please%20Unsubscribe) and provide us with the email address you wish to be removed.

_Links To Other Sites_  
Our website may contain links to other Internet sites. Smith Micro is not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We strongly encourage you to become familiar with the privacy practices of those other sites. Also, be aware that this privacy statement applies solely to information collected at the Smith Micro. 

_Changes To The Privacy Policy_  
Smith Micro may occasionally update, amend or change this policy from time to time. A "last updated" date will be included on the bottom of the Privacy Policy. In order to remain up to date with Smith Micro's policy, please check this page periodically.
