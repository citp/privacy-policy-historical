# SillyJokes.co.uk's Privacy Policy

| 

Privacy Policy

|   
---|---|---  
  
SillyJokes.co.uk may, on occasion, collect personal information on this site. The information gathered is that given when users purchase items, submit articles or other information, or enter contests.  
SillyJokes.co.uk takes the privacy of people who come to this site very seriously.

Here is our privacy pledge: 

  * We will not send you unsolicited messages unless you request to be added to one of our mailing lists. 
  * SillyJokes.co.uk's partners will not send you unsolicited email messages unless you explicitly request that our partners may use your address. 
  * SillyJokes.co.uk will not provide any of your personal information to our partners, unless you request to make that information available to them. 

In other words, if you provide us with information about yourself, we'll only use that information for things that you ask for. When you provide us with an email address when you order something, we'll only use that email address for communicating information about your order to you. If you provide an email address for a contest, we will only enter you in the contest, unless you specifically request to be on our mailing lists. 

The SillyJokes.co.uk website occassionally uses Cookies to track visitors.

If you would like to opt-out of any of our mailing lists or you have any other privacy concerns please use our [Privacy Contact](mailto:privacy@sillyjokes.co.uk).
