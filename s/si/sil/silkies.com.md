# Pantyhose, Shapewear, Plus Size, Tights, Toeless Hosiery, Intimates | Silkies.com

**Privacy Policy Statement**

Sculptz and our brands, which include Silkies, Sculptz, Silkies Enriche, and Pain _Vanish_ (collectively Sculptz, "our company," "we"), and our websites,  [ www.Silkies.com](http://www.silkies.com/),  [ www.Sculptz.com](http://www.sculptz.com/),  [ www.Silkiesenriche.com](http://www.silkiesenriche.com/) and _[ www.Painvanish.com](http://www.painvanish.com/)_ regard our customers' privacy as one of our most important values. We operate the Silkies.com website and our other sites for our individual brands (collectively and individually the “Site” or “Sites”). This policy applies ONLY to information about you that we acquire through the use of the Sites. We are committed to protecting your privacy whenever you use the Sites. This privacy policy was last updated on October 1, 2012 and is effective as of that date.

**How do we protect your privacy?**

**1\. What Information We Collect**

Whenever you visit the Site, we will recognize you by your domain name. When you order a product from Our Company at the Site, you will be providing personal information to Our Company. Personal information includes your name, telephone number, address, email and other information by which you can be personally identified. This personal information allows us to process and fulfill your orders as soon as possible and to notify you of your order status whenever you request it.

If you use a credit card to make a purchase or join our program, the transaction and your credit card number and expiration date are stored on our server for archival purposes only. We will never disclose any of this information to any third party, except for the purpose of processing your purchase.

"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Our Company makes use of cookies to enhance your Web viewing experience, to target advertising and marketing offers to your particular interests, and to avoid showing you duplicate ads. Third parties advertising on the Site may also use cookies. Cookies cannot be used to get data from your hard drive, your email or any other personal data about you. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you a chance to decide if you want to accept it or not.

We contract with third parties to serve ads on our behalf across the Internet. They may collect information about your visits to our website, and your interaction with our products and services. They may also use information about your visits to this website to target advertisements for our goods and services. This information is collected through the use of a pixel tag, which is industry standard techonology used by most major websites. Such third parties are not permitted to sell or share your personally identifiable information as part of this process. To “opt-out” from retargeting  [ click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp).

Our company also collects usage information from visitors to the Site. We collect certain data on specific purchases. Usage information also includes how the Site is used, including the number and frequency of visitors to each page, the length of their stay, the domain names of browsers that visit the Site, time of day the Site was visited and other non-personally identifiable information.

**3\. State-of-the-Art Technology**

When you place an order or access your account information, we use a state-of-the-art secure server to keep all your personal information -- including your billing and purchase information -- as secure as possible. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us. Furthermore, all of the customer data we collect is protected against unauthorized access. We have put this privacy protection control system in place to help ensure that your personal data remains safe and private. However, we cannot guarantee the protection of your information from any actions taken by third parties that may unlawfully interrupt or access transmissions or private communications.

By using the Site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Our Company in accordance with our Privacy Policy. We reserve the right to make changes to our Privacy Policy. Please check this page regularly for changes.
