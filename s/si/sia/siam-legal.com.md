# Privacy Policy | Siam Legal International

Www.siam-legal.com is a website which deals with commercial services, legal disputes, visa applications, and other legal services in Thailand. This Privacy Policy is indicative of our commitment to secure the privacy of our visitors. It outlines how we protect the information given to us. It describes how we collect and use such information to improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Unless otherwise defined in this Privacy Policy, terms used in this Privacy Policy have the same meanings as in our [Terms and Conditions](https://www.siam-legal.com/terms_of_use.php)

### Definitions

**Service**  
Service is the https://www.siam-legal.com website operated by Siam Legal (Thailand) Co., Ltd.

**Personal Data**  
Personal Data means data about an individual who can be identified from those data (or from those and other information either in our possession or likely to come into our possession).

**Usage Data**  
Usage Data is data collected automatically either generated by the use of the Service or from the Service infrastructure itself (for example, the duration of a page visit).

**Cookies**  
Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device (computer, tablet or mobile device).

**Data Controller**  
Data Controller means the natural or legal person who (either alone or jointly or in common with other persons) determines the purposes for which and the manner in which any personal information are, or are to be, processed. For the purpose of this Privacy Policy, we are a Data Controller of your Personal Data.

**Data Processors (or Service Providers)**  
Data Processor (or Service Provider) means any natural or legal person who processes the data on behalf of the Data Controller. We may use the services of various Service Providers in order to process your data more effectively.

**Data Subject (or User)**  
Data Subject is any living individual who is using our Service and is the subject of Personal Data.

### Collection and Use of Information

Our Privacy Policy covers all online information consisting of personal identifiable or non-personal identifiable information shared with us while visiting our website. It does not cover offline information. We collect these information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

The personal information supplied to the website is used to provide services, carry out the transactions expected of us, process requests and notify clients of any material change to this Privacy Policy.

The non-personally identifiable information, on the other hand, shall be used to manage the website. It may be used to analyze data for easy navigation and access on the website.

Hence, the use of the website signifies your agreement with all the terms of the Privacy Policy.

Should you not want us to use your personal identification, do not submit any information to the website or to any of our lawyers.

**Tracking and Cookies Data**  
Cookies are files with small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device. Tracking technologies also used are beacons, tags, and scripts to collect and track information and to improve and analyze our Service.

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service and hold certain information.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of the Service.

Examples of Cookies we use:  


  * _Session Cookies._ We use Session Cookies to operate our Service.
  * _Preference Cookies._ We use Preference Cookies to remember your preferences and various settings.
