# Silicon Integrated Systems(SiS) -Privacy Policy

This privacy policy express the how the information collected from this web site will be used. If you have any questions or comments about this policy, please contact us at _pims@sis.com_

**Information Collected**

You do not have to provide any personal information to use this web site, submitting personal information is voluntary. However, when you submitting personal Information (such as your name, mailing address, email address and other contact information) it constitutes your consent to the use of such information for the purpose(s) stated below.

**Use of Information Collected**

The personal information collected is used to respond to your inquiries that you request. The information collected is also used to help us to upgrade the quality of our website and the services that we provide over the website. 

We reserve the right to provide such personal information to our officers, employees, contractors, agents, and designees to the extent necessary to enable them to execute certain web services (such as web hosting or maintenance services) for us. We also reserve the right to disclose such information to any third party if we believe that we are required to do so for any or all of the following: (i) by law; (ii) to comply with legal processes or governmental requests; (iii) to prevent, investigate, detect or prosecute criminal offences or attacks on the technical integrity of the web site or our network; and/or (iv) to protect the rights, property, or safety of our company, the users of the website, or the public.

**Update Your Personal Information**

Your personal information collected from this web site will be properly saved and reserved in the website database for six months. You may ask us to update or delete such information anytime during such six-month period. You may do so by sending email to the following address: _pims@sis.com_ .
