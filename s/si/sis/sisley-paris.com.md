The collected personal data is subject to technical processing necessary for the management of the database, including managing subscription to our newsletter and sending information about the Company and its activities. The recipient of this information is SISLEY and its service providers.

The personal data may be used by Sisley for the commercial prospection purposes via e-mail, post or SMS/MMS, subject to your explicit consent.

According to the Data Protection Act no 78-17 of 6 January 1978 modified in 2004, you have the right to access, rectify and delete your personal information by sending us a letter to the following address: SISLEY, Service Clients, 3 avenue de Friedland, 75008 Paris.
