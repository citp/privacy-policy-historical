SingleFeed Privacy Policy

 

Your privacy is very important to us. We use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order only to complete your order. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete your order.

We use your e-mail address to answer your e-mail correspondence, introduce you to SingleFeed upgrades/improvements, and provide you with information we think will help you achieve better results on the shopping comparison engines. Your email address is not used for any other purpose and is not shared with outside parties. To prevent unauthorized access and ensure the correct use of information, we have established appropriate procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Our Web site uses cookies to save your login name and password so you don't have to re-enter your information each time you visit our site. A cookie is a file which is placed on your machine's hard drive, and which contains information that may be accessed by our server the next time you visit our site.
