# Privacy Policy for Singles2meet.co.za

Privacy Policy

We at Singles2meet.co.za value every user and want your online experience to be as good as it can possibly be.

We will only use user information collected for identification and contact purposes. 

Information collected by us will not be shared with anybody. We will not give out or sell the information you provide us with. 

Singles2meet.co.za does not sell or rent its e-mail lists to third parties. It is against our policy to provide any user information to any third party without the users's prior permission.

Singles2meet.co.za does not send unsolicited or "spam" e-mail. We will only send email to communicate new information to you or to inform you of important information regarding your profile at Singles2meet.co.za. All users are given the opportunity to unsubscribe at any time. 

Our service can also be discontinued entirely if you don't want to participate anymore. Your information will be removed from our systems completely after you closed your account.
