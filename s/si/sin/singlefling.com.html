Privacy policy

Privacy is of utmost importance to Single Fling.com; your personal details will not be given to anyone except with your express consent to divulge your contact details to your matches after an event.

The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website.

Your name, address, and telephone number are confidential. We will maintain this information in our secure database. We may use it to facilitate your user experience with our website, as well as, when necessary, to contact you. Your e-mail address will also be stored in our secure database, and we may use this address to contact you with announcements and information regarding events. We will provide your contact information to individuals with whom there is a mutual match. We will not sell or provide your contact information to any other organisation who may be interested in making unsolicited contacts.

Credit Information

When signing up for an event using a credit card, you will be asked to provide additional financial information such as your credit card number, expiration date and billing address. We use encryption technology to protect your credit card information while in transit, via the Internet, to our servers.

Security

We may use ''cookies'' to simplify the user''s experience. If you set your browser to reject cookies, the Service may not function properly. The www.fastlife.com database resides behind a firewall in order to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. We have taken preventive measures to restrict access to sensitive data, as well as to detect and prohibit certain connections and operations by unauthorized users. No claim is made as to the impenetrability of the system under unusual circumstance or its resilience to future attacks.

Single Fling.com contains links to other web sites. Please note that when you click on one of these links, you are ''clicking'' to another web site. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of these linked sites as their privacy policy may differ from ours.

Single Fling.com sends email newsletters to members and periodically sends emails about new services, improvements, promotional events, etc. Our members can opt-out of receiving communications from us by unsubscribing from our mailing list at newsletter-unsubscribe@singlefling.com

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Website, please contact us on webmaster@singlefling.com
