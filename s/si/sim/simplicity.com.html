a) Information We Collect Automatically
Simplicity will automatically collect information about your use of the Site, such as the domain and Internet address of the website from which you accessed the Site, the date and time you access the Site, and your IP address (the series of numbers associated with your individual computer). d) Non-Personally-Identifiable Information

Simplicity may also collect information about you that is not personally-identifiable. Examples include your gender, interests, and preferences. Simplicity will not collect any such demographic information about you unless you provide it to us voluntarily, but if you do, we may share it with advertisers, business partners, and other third parties. For example, we may inform a sponsor that the membership of the Site is X percent female and Y percent male.

When you view the Site, we may store some information on your computer. This information will be in the form of a "cookie" or similar file. Also, advertisers and third parties that provide products or services may also assign their own cookies to your computer. Simplicity cannot control this process. Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can set your browser to erase cookies from your computer, block all cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. Please refer to your browser instructions or help screen to learn more about these functions.

b) Personal Information
When you provide Simplicity with your Personal Information, we may store and process it to better understand your needs and improve our products and services. We may also use it to send you for informational, marketing, and promotional information or administrative information about the Site. If you do not want to receive such notices, you may opt out of receiving such messages by emailing us at info@simplicity.com.

We may use and disclose your Personal Information to third parties to the extent necessary to provide specific products or services to us, including assistance with products or services you have ordered. We may also disclose Personal Information to third parties if we believe that disclosing it is necessary to protect the rights or property of Simplicity or anyone else or if we believe in good faith that the law requires it. Finally, we may transfer your Personal Information to successors of our business assets.

We may provide third parties information about you that does not allow you to be identified or contacted, and such information may be combined with similar information of other Site users. For example, we might inform third parties of the demographic breakdown of our registered users. These third parties may include potential or actual advertisers, providers of advertising products or services (including vendors and web site tracking services), commercial partners, sponsors, licensees, researchers, and other similar parties.

Except as stated in this Privacy Policy, or as otherwise stated at the time Personal Information is gathered, we will not provide it to third parties.
