PRIVACY POLICY

Here at Simmi UK Ltd (Simmi) we are committed to protecting and respecting the privacy of your personal data.

Simmi is a leading online fast fashion footwear retail company based in the UK. We design, source, market and sell shoes targeted at 16-35 year-old consumers in almost every country in the world. You can read more about us on our About Us page.

Simmi UK Ltd (company number 7954087), Unit 10 Prospect Business Park, Langston Road, Loughton, Essex, IG10 3TR (collectively referred to as Simmi, we, us and our in this privacy notice) is the registered data controller and responsible for your personal data collected through the ww.simmi.com website (the Site) . Simmi UK Ltd are the owners and operators of the Site and the relevant accounts on social media.

This policy explains how your data is collected, used, transferred, and disclosed by Simmi. We collect data when you use the Site https://www.simmi.com or interact with us through Simmis social media accounts, by email, by phone, when you participate in giveaways, and purchase Simmi products from the Site and/or via third party platforms. By using the Site, registering a Simmi Account, interacting with us on social media and purchasing products from us or third party platforms, you are accepting and consenting to the terms of this privacy policy.

We take the protection of your personal data seriously and will process your personal data fairly, lawfully and transparently. This privacy notice describes the personal data we are collecting about you and how it is used.

We reserve the right to amend this policy from time to time. This policy was most recently updated on 24 May 2018.

INFORMATION THAT WE COLLECT ABOUT YOU

During your use of our Site, whether youre just having a browse or making a purchase, its necessary for us to collect some personal data in order to provide the best service possible. We may also collect personal data about you when you register and use a Simmi Account, when you interact with us on our social media accounts, when you purchase products from us (or via third party platforms), and when you participate in any giveaways. We may collect and process the following information about you:

WHAT YOU GIVE US

a) Typically, we ask for your name, date of birth, email address, telephone number, and delivery address. This is necessary information for us to collect in order to send you what you purchase from us. We will also ask for these pieces of personal data when you register a Simmi Account, so we can tailor our promotions in a unique way to suit you and what is relevant. It also helps that you dont have to keep filling in your details every time you make a purchase, which can be very time consuming! For every order you make, there will be an order number that will be linked to your details when you make a purchase from us, so you can always see what you have purchased from us in the past.

b) When you fill in forms on our Site, you may also give us information about yourself. When you register to subscribe to our email newsletters, enter into our giveaways and other promotions, or when you participate in our social media activities or surveys, youll be giving us your information so we can contact you accordingly.

c) Where you are applying for a job, work placement or internship, you will be asked to provide certain additional information, for example about your education, employment history, right to work, and state of health.

When you give us your personal information, you must make sure that the data you give us is personal to you and no one else, unless you have the express permission to use their details. You must only provide an email address that is accessible to only yourself and not any others. You should not use another persons email address or a generic email address, like hello@simmi.com. You must also ensure that the information you give us is accurate and up to date. By giving us data about yourself, you verify that the information you gave is personal to you, is accurate and up to date, and that you will keep us informed of any changes.

WHAT WE COLLECT

When you use our Site and social media, we may collect the following data: Technical data. Information such as the Internet protocol (IP) address, your Simmi Account login information, device type, time zone setting, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and what platform you use; About your visit to our Site. We record the number of visits, the full Uniform Resource Locators (URL) clickstream (including date and time), products you looked at or searched for, download errors, page response times, how long you spend on certain pages, page interactions (like what you click on), and methods used to browse away from the page; How many times you have been on our Site and information about your order(s). This includes the products you have purchased and any details you entered when you placed your order(s). This will be linked to the email address you use when you place an order or your Simmi Account (if youve registered); More information on the ways that we collect some of the above can be found on our Cookies Policy page.

We may get the information set out in the points above when you use certain third party platforms. We work closely with these third parties, including the companies who deliver your order to you, card protection and payment processing companies that process your payments for orders, and business partners (like if we have a competition with another company), in order to fulfil our contract with you.

All processing of your personal data is justified by a legal "condition" for such processing. In the majority of cases, processing will be justified on the basis that:

a) you have given consent for your personal data to be processed for example, where you consent to the placing of cookies on our Site, or where you proactively sign-up to our email newsletters);

b) the processing is necessary for the performance of a contract (for example, to deliver goods that you have ordered);

c) the processing is necessary for us to comply with a relevant legal obligation (for example, compliance with a court order); or

d) the processing is in our legitimate interests, subject to your interests and fundamental rights (for example, we have a legitimate commercial interest in placing cookies and tracking tools on our website in order to understand how our site is used, and to make improvements to it).

These are downloaded to your device when you visit a website, if you agree to the Site doing so. Cookies are then sent back to the website on each visit, or to another website that recognises that cookie. These cookies help us to provide you with a good experience when youre browsing, and allows us to improve the Site and your shopping experience with us. For detailed information on the cookies we use and the purposes for which we use, please them see our Cookie Policy.

Generally we will only keep your personal data for as long as is necessary to satisfy the reason(s) for which it was collected. Where we have collected your personal data in order for marketing purposes, your personal data will be stored by us only as long as you do not change your mind to receive such materials from Simmi, and notify us of that fact.

You have several rights under the data privacy legislation, which are the following:

  • To request access to your personal data;
  • To request correction of your personal data;
  • To request erasure of your personal data;
  • To request restriction of processing of your personal data where:

  • To request the transfer of your personal data;

To exercise your rights above, you can simply log in to your Simmi Account and adjust where necessary, or contact our Customer Services team at hello@simmi.com or Customer Services, Simmi UK Ltd, Unit 10 Prospect Business Park, Langston Road, Loughton, Essex, IG10 3TR.
