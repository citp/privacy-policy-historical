II. INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND HOW WE USE IT
In addition to the specific use of personal information as outlined below, we use the information that we collect to (a) provide our products and services to users, including the presentation of customized content and advertising, (b) audit, research and analyze our services in order to improve the quality of our services, (c) ensure the technical functionality of our network, and (d) develop new products and services for our users.

In order to provide our services to you, we may collect the following types of P.I.I.:

Information you provide to us -

Using Simpli to conduct an Internet search does not require you to register for an account or provide any personal information to us.

When you sign up for an advertiser or publisher account on Simpli, registration is required. This requires that we ask you for, and you provide, certain personal information. For both advertisers and publishers, we require your name, postal address, email address, and telephone number. For advertisers, we collect credit card numbers, expiration dates and “cvv2” numbers to authorize payments for advertiser accounts. For publishers, we require a social security number or a federal tax identification number so that we can process payments under our agreements with you. We use this information for internal business purposes, such as contacting our advertisers and publishers, charging our advertiser’s credit cards as requested by our advertisers, billing our advertisers, providing assistance to advertisers and publishers in managing their accounts, paying publishers, and otherwise serving our clients in the normal course of our business. Also, from time to time, we may communicate with you by email. These email communications may be related to assisting you with your account or providing you with information about our services.

In order to provide our services to you, we may collect the following types of non-personally identifiable information:

Cookies - When you visit Simpli, we send one or more cookies to your computer. A cookie is a small file containing a string of characters that enables us to uniquely identify your browser. We use cookies to improve the quality of our service by storing user preferences and tracking user trends, such as previous searches. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to advise you of when a cookie is being sent. However, some Simpli features and services- such as our previous searches functionality- may not function properly if your cookies are disabled.

Log information - When you use Simpli services, our servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a Web site. These server logs may include information such as your web request, Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.

Information from sites that implemented Simpli’s search technology- We offer some of our services in connection with other Web sites. Personal information that you provide to those sites may be sent to Simpli in order to deliver the service. We process such information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. The affiliated sites may have different privacy practices and we encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Links - Simpli may present links in a format that enables us to keep track of whether these links have been followed and whether or not a transaction occurred. We use this information to improve the quality of our search technology and advertising platform.
