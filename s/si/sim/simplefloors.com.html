Privacy Policy

This is the web site of www.simplefloors.com.

Our postal and physical location address is:
272 Bayshore Blvd.
San Francisco, CA 94124

We can be reached via e-mail at info@simplefloors.com
or you can reach us by telephone at 1-800-220-7112

We adhere to a strict privacy policy for the surfing enjoyment of our customers. Internet shopping should be simple. Unless an action by a visitor requires personal information such as placing an order or requesting samples to be sent to a specific address, we intentionally do not require visitors to share information or register with us any way. For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name, but not the e-mail address.

We collect only the domain name, but not the e-mail address of visitors to our Web page.

We do not share your information with third parties.

With respect to cookies: We use cookies to record session information, such as items that consumers add to their shopping cart, customize Web page content based on visitors' browser type or other information that the visitor sends.

If you provide us with an email address but do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us a quick e-mail at the info@simplefloors.com address.

If you supply us with your postal address on-line you will only receive the information for which you provided us your address, such as our free sample packages.

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line will only receive telephone contact from us or delivery companies with information regarding orders they have placed on-line or by the phone. We do not sell your phone or any other contact

We do not share or sell your information with any other organizations. We do share your contact information with those companies who are responsible for delivering your order to you

With respect to Ad Servers: We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies. Customers may prevent their information from being used for purposes other than those for which it was originally collected by e-mailing us at the above address.

Upon request we offer visitors the ability to have inaccuracies corrected in contact information. Consumers can have this information corrected by calling us at 800-220-7112.

With respect to security: When we transfer and receive certain types of sensitive information such as financial information, we redirect visitors to a secure server and will notify visitors through a pop-up screen on our site.
