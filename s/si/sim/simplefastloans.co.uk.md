2\. When you enter a promotion or register on this site, you will be asked to provide certain information about yourself (such as your name, address and email address). Log Book Loans Ltd respects the privacy concerns of users of this web site and complies with all applicable UK Data Protection legislation currently in force in relation to its treatment of personal information. This privacy statement sets out Log Book Loans Ltd policy in relation to how it uses any personal information collected from you through your use of this site. 

3\. Log Book Loans Ltd collects information about you when your register on the site or register for a promotion, or which you provide through communications with Log Book Loans Ltd. Log Book Loans Ltd also collects information about your preferences in relation to this site by analysing site traffic, which includes the use of cookies. Cookies are pieces of computer code which are stored on your computer and which allow us to recognise the fact that you have visited our site previously. Most browsers can be altered to prevent your computer from accepting cookies, although this may mean that some of the features of this site will not work properly. 

4\. Log Book Loans Ltd may use personal information collected about you to contact you in relation to any promotion or transaction you have entered (e.g. to let you know if you have won a prize) and also to notify you if any goods, services, offers or promotions which we believe may be of interest to you. If you do not wish to receive any further communications from Log Book Loans Ltd please tick the relevant box when entering your registration details. Alternatively if you have subscribed and wish to unsubscribe please follow the unsubscribe procedure set out on the website. In addition, Log Book Loans Ltd may disclose and use personal information collected about you within its group for the purpose of internal business administration and analysis. Log Book Loans Ltd may also disclose personal information collected about you to third parties, but only where this is necessary for the purposes of completing any transaction you are undertaking with us, for providing any goods or services to you or for the purposes of associated administration or obtaining professional advice. 

5\. We may send your information internationally, including to countries outside the European Economic Association. However, this will only be undertaken if those countries outside the EEA can ensure an adequate level of protection of the rights and freedoms of data subjects in relation to the processing of personal data. 

6\. By disclosing your personal information to Log Book Loans Ltd, you consent to the collection, storage and processing of your personal information in the manner set out in this privacy policy. 

7\. If any of your personal information changes or becomes out of date you shall amend your registration details on the site.
