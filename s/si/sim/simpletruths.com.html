Use of Personal Information

Use of Cookies

In cyberspace, cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to an individuals computer for record keeping purposes. Cookies make web surfing easier by saving your passwords, preferences, and purchase information while youre online. The use of cookies is an industry standard practice and can be found on nearly all major websites.

We may use cookies, among other purposes, to record session and Site usage information, to help us see which areas are popular and which are not, to alert users to new areas and products that we think might be of interest to them when they return to the Site, and to record past activity to provide better service when users return to the Site, and to perform remarketing to users who have previously visited the Site. This helps us to target products and recommendations based on user interests, and also improve and update the Site based on such data as a total number of users and pages viewed. This information is most easily tracked with cookies.

Similarly, any third parties that may offer good or services on the Site may also use cookies to track which products or services youve chosen to purchase while shopping.

The use of cookies sent by third-party servers is standard in the Internet industry. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you choose to remove or block cookies, it is possible that some parts of the Site, such as the shopping and contest/sweepstakes areas, will not function properly if you do so.

Use of IP Addresses

An IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer by your Internet Service Provider (ISP) whenever you log on the the Internet. Web servers automatically identify your computer by its IP address, so when a user requests pages from the Site, our servers log the users IP address. The IP addresses of our users are collected only for the purposes of system administration and to report aggregate information to our advertisers, our corporate affiliates or consultants, or with third parties that perform a particular service on our behalf. We do not combine IP addresses with any personally identifying information, nor do we compile any session activity reports by IP address.
