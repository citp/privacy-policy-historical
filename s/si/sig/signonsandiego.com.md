# SignOnSanDiego.com > About us > Privacy Policy

[SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") and The Union-Tribune Publishing Company are committed to bringing you information tailored to your individual needs. We also recognize the importance of protecting the privacy of your personally identifiable information. In adopting this privacy policy, our intent is to balance our legitimate business interests in collecting and using personally identifiable information and your reasonable expectations of privacy. Please note: this policy applies only to information collected by [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") online, as specified below, and does not govern or apply to information collected or used by The Union-Tribune Publishing Company or its affiliates through other means. This policy does not cover practices of the San Diego Union-Tribune newspaper. 

**What personal information do I provide to[SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page")? **  
[SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") may ask you to provide various types of demographic information to enhance your experience on our site. The more information you provide, the better we are able to customize your experience. We may also ask you for other information at other times - such as when you [enter a contest](http://contests.signonsandiego.com/ "U-T Contests Portal") or participate in a promotion, when you post an online ad, when you [participate in our message boards](http://forums.signonsandiego.com/ "U-T Forums Page"). Whenever you provide personal information to us, we will make an effort to link to our privacy policy. See below about "cookies" and what other information is collected. 

**How does[SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") use my personal information?**   
Our primary goal in collecting personal information is to provide you, the visitor, with a customized experience on our site. [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") uses the personally identifiable information you provide to us in several ways. Some examples follow. In order to provide our service free of charge, we display advertisements. [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") delivers targeted [advertisements](http://www.signonsandiego.com/media/kit/index.html "U-T Online Media Kit") on behalf of advertisers. Advertisers give us an advertisement and tell us the type of audience they want to reach (for example, males over 35 years old). We take the advertisement and display it to visitors meeting the specified criteria. In this process, the advertiser never has access to individual visitor information. 

If you registered to [receive email](http://www.signonsandiego.com/newsletters/membership/membership.html "SignOnSanDiego.com Newsletter Registration") from [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page"), we may send you emails about [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") products, promotions, or services as well as on behalf of other companies. You may change your preferences for the future at any time by (i) using the unsubscribe links at the bottom of any of our email newsletters ii) emailing us at [newsletters@signonsandiego.com](mailto:newsletters@signonsandiego.com), or iii) updating your email preferences at <http://echo.bluehornet.com/clients/signon2/survey.htm>, or (iv) by contacting us by mail at 350 Camino de la Reina, San Diego, CA 92108. 

You will receive special offers via email from [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") consistent with your stated personal preferences. You can change your preferences at any time by visiting http://echo.bluehornet.com/clients/signon2/survey.htm. [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") also does research on our visitors' demographics, interests and behavior based on the information you provide to us including upon registration, from our server log files or from surveys. We do this to better understand and serve our visitors. This research is compiled and analyzed on an aggregated basis and [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") may share this aggregated data with advertisers or other businesses. 

This information is not shared with anyone outside of the Union-Tribune Publishing Co. or [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") except as provided for in this Privacy Policy. More specifics on the information we collect are outlined within this policy. The following products involving sharing your Personal Information with outside companies so that they can deliver features you have requested. 

**Coupons**  
If you requested to receive offers from the Coupon feature when you registered, your email address is shared with Boodle.com, the company that operates this system. 

**Singles**  
This system is operated by [Spring Street Networks](http://www.springstreetnetworks.com/ "Spring Street Networks Home Page"). Please review Spring Street's [privacy policy](http://www.springstreetnetworks.com/tos/privacy.asp "Spring Street Networks Privacy Policy") for details of the use of your personal information. When registering for Spring Street if you opt-in for any of their several options for email communications you could be contacted by them. The Spring Street registration asks for first and last name and email address. 

**Archives**  
To purchase access to articles in this system, visitors must input personal information into an ecommerce system operated by [ProQuest Archiver](http://www.pqarchiver.com/index.html "ProQuest Archiver Home Page"). This information is used only to complete the credit card transaction. To complete the ecommerce transaction ProQuest Archiver required requires full name, street address, email address and credit card information. 

**Home Delivery and eEdition Subscriptions**  
To [subscribe to home delivery](http://www.subscriber-services.com/sandiego/zipcheck.asp?pid=10 "Subscribe to U-T Home Delivery") or the [eEdition](http://eedition.uniontrib.com/ "eEdition Home Page") of The San Diego Union-Tribune, visitors must input personal information into an ecommerce system operated by [Chase Paymentech](http://www.chasepaymentech.com/ "Chase Paymentech Home Page"). This information is used only to complete the credit card transaction. To complete the ecommerce transaction, Chase Paymentech requires full name, street address, email address and credit card information. 

**eEdition Single Copy and eEdition Archives**  
To [purchase Union-Tribune eEdition single copies](http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/sandiego/offers.html "San Diego Union Tribune Document Purchase") or archives, visitors must input personal information into an ecommerce system operated by [PayPal](http://paypal.com/ "PayPal Home Page"). This information is used only to complete the credit card transaction. To complete the ecommerce transaction, PayPal requires full name, street address, email address and credit card information. 

**Razor Gator ticketing service**  
You must supply your email address to register for use of the ticketing service on our site, which is operated by [Razor Gator Inc](http://signonsandiego.razorgator.com/ "Razor Gator Inc Home Page"). [Razor Gator's privacy policy](http://signonsandiego.razorgator.com/policies.asp#5 "Razor Gator's privacy policy") is available at http://signonsandiego.razorgator.com/policies.asp#5. 

**Promotions:** Promotions that run on [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") may be sponsored by companies other than [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") or may be co-sponsored by [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") and another company. If you opt-in to receive information from a third party sponsor when entering a contests or participating in a promotion, some or all personally identifiable information provided by you may be shared with the sponsor. In certain circumstances, you may not be able to participate in a particular promotion if you chose not to share personally identifiable information. Currently, most [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") promotions are limited to California residents. 

**Advertisers and Links:** [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") advertisers, or Web sites that have links on our site, may also collect personally identifiable information directly from you. The information practices of companies collecting data on our site or Web sites linked to [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") are not covered by this privacy statement. 

**Shopping and Business Services:** If you make a purchase or request a service from a business in [Shopping](http://shopping.signonsandiego.com/business.php "Shopping Guide"), [Entertainment](http://entertainment.signonsandiego.com/ "Entertainment Guide"), [Singles](http://personals.signonsandiego.com/ "Singles Guide") or any of our business services sections, the information you provide for that transaction (as well as tracking information and cookies as described below) is provided directly to the business. Businesses listed in [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") [Shopping](http://shopping.signonsandiego.com/business.php "Shopping Guide"), [Singles](http://personals.signonsandiego.com/ "Singles Guide") and Services section have separate privacy and data collection practices. [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") has no responsibility or liability for these independent policies. For more information regarding the business and its privacy policy, go to that business' home page and click on the appropriate link. 

**Other:** If we run competitions or [contests](http://contests.signonsandiego.com/ "Contests Portal") on the site, you may be required to provide personally identifiable information such as your telephone number and address in order to participate. If you told us that you would be interested in receiving email from us, we may send you emails about [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") products, promotions, or services as well as on behalf of other companies. You can change your account preferences at any time by visiting <http://echo.bluehornet.com/clients/signon2/survey.htm>. In addition, in each advertising email you will be provided an ability to opt-out of receiving future emails from the specific advertiser. 

We do not control the privacy policies of our advertisers, sponsors or other sites or businesses to which we provide hyperlinks or access. Please visit the sites of these businesses to review their privacy policies. 

[SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") visitors should also be aware that, when you voluntarily disclose personal information in [chat areas](http://forums.signonsandiego.com/ "Forums") or [bulletin boards](http://forums.signonsandiego.com/ "Forums"), that information may be collected by others and may result in unsolicited messages from others. Except as stated in this privacy policy or at the time of collection, a visitor's personally identifiable information will not be transferred to a party outside The Union-Tribune Publishing Company, its divisions or affiliates, or its service vendors unless notice is given at the time of collection or prior to transfer. If you do not wish to receive any email or to have us share such information with any other party, you may change your preferences for the future at any time by (i) updating your personal preferences in your newsletter registration profile at <http://echo.bluehornet.com/clients/signon2/survey.htm> (ii) emailing us at [newsletters@signonsandiego.com](mailto:newsletters@signonsandiego.com)or (iii) contacting us by mail at 350 Camino de la Reina, San Diego, CA 92108. [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") may also disclose account information in special cases when we have reason to believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be violating our User Agreement or may be causing injury to or interference with (either intentionally or unintentionally) SignOnSanDiego.com's rights or property, other [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") visitors, or anyone else that could be harmed by such activities. Data Security: We have in place physical, electronic and managerial procedures to protect the information we collect online. However, as effective as these measures are, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the security of our database, nor can we guarantee that the information you supply will not be intercepted while being transmitted to us over the Internet. 

**What information do web servers collect?**  
Web servers serving [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") automatically collect certain non-personally identifiable information, such as which pages each visitor visits and the domain name (e.g., Cox.net) of visitors. This information is used for various purposes including internal review, to tailor information to individual visitors, and for traffic audits. We also provide this information (as well as information from third-party market researchers) about our visitors on an aggregated, anonymous basis to our advertisers. 

**What are cookies and how does[SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") use them? **  
[SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") places a "[cookie](http://cookiecentral.com/cm002.htm "CookieCentral.com Home Page")" on the browser of a visitor's computer to store and sometimes track information about you while you are on our Web site. A [cookie](http://cookiecentral.com/cm002.htm "CookieCentral.com Home Page") can be used to tell when your computer has contacted our Web site; we may also use the information for editorial purposes and for other purposes such as measuring certain traffic patterns. For example, [cookies](http://cookiecentral.com/cm002.htm "CookieCentral.com Home Page") are used to ensure that you don't see the same ad too many times in a single session and that you do not have to reenter registration information during your visit. We may also use [cookies](http://cookiecentral.com/cm002.htm "CookieCentral.com Home Page") to track your progress on our site. Advertising service vendors that serve ads into our site may also use their own [cookies](http://cookiecentral.com/cm002.htm "CookieCentral.com Home Page"). You may opt-out of the [cookies](http://cookiecentral.com/cm002.htm "CookieCentral.com Home Page") delivered by [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") by changing the setting on your browser. Please be aware that this will disable all [cookies](http://cookiecentral.com/cm002.htm "CookieCentral.com Home Page") delivered to your browser, not just the ones delivered by [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page")

**Children under 13:** Do not send any information about yourself to us - including information like your name, address or email address. In general, we do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If, in limited circumstances, we do knowingly collect personally identifiable information from a child under the age of 13, we will do so only with verified parental consent prior to collection. In the event that we learn that we have collected any personal information from a child under the age of 13 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information from our database as quickly as possible. 

**Changes to Privacy Policy**  
Any changes to our privacy policy will be communicated to you via email if you have registered for any of our services that require you to enter your email address. 

_**Registration Questions and Answers**_

**Why are you asking me to provide this information?**  
The registration process allows us to track the audience profile of our Web site. This enables us to provide greater demographic targeting for our advertisers and learn a little more about our visitors. So you may see advertisements that are more relevant to your interest. This information also helps to plan site improvements. 

**Will I be emailed if I provide personal information?**  
The information in the registration process is anonymous. It does not personally identify you. We will only send you email if you voluntarily opted in to receive information from us, if you signed up for one of our email newsletters, or if you are otherwise doing business with [SignOnSanDiego.com](http://sosd.com/ "SignOnSanDiego.com Home Page") or the Union-Tribune. 

**Comments?**  
Please send us any comments you have by email to [customersupport@signonsandiego.com](mailto:customersupport@signonsandiego.com)
