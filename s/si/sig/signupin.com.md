# Privacy Policy | How Sign Up

Your internet privacy is our most concerned subject. Our site protects your online privacy. We never ask your private personal information.

### Type of Information we collect

We collect only minimum information, you (users) provide at the time of commenting, asking questions, contacting us via contact methods.

### We protect your information

We never sell, rent and distribute our users’ information to any third parties. We only use users aggregate information such as daily visits, daily page views for further improvement of our site.

### Cookies

A cookie file can store information like your identification code, that  a website will use to know numbers of time you have visited. **SignUpIn.com** uses cookies to track your performance on this site. Third party vendor like Google also uses cookies to track your web search performance and use it to show you Google ad as per your interest. You can disable cookies doing some setting on browser. More information can be found on browser help section.

### The DoubleClick Cookie

  * The  **DoubleClick cookie**  is used by  **Google**  in the ads served on this website. DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.
  * You may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for internet based advertising by visiting [Ads Settings](http://www.google.com/ads/preferences/).



### Google Adsense

_**SignUpIn.com**_ uses **Adsense** for advertisement. Adsense is an advertisement product of **Google**. We have no excess to control types of ads displayed by **Google** Ads expect format of ads. You can see ads according to your search behavior and **Google Ads Policy**. Here: <http://www.google.com/policies/technologies/ads/> you can find more about Google Ads Policy and how to control it.

#### Other Advertisement

Till now this site use no other advertisement.

### Content Using Policy

You are free to read, participate on contents of this site. You use our content as references with right linking.

**Disclaimer:**  We are **not officially associated with Hotmail.com**.  Hotmail is a product of Microsoft. This site provides Hotmail login help and sign in tutorials.

If you have any queries and questions over our privacy policy contact us via contact methods.
