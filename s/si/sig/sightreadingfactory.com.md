When you register for any GraceNotes LLC service, we ask for information such as your name, email address, school/organization name, school/organization address, credit card information, and Paypal account details. We do not store credit card information as it is handled through Stripe.

GraceNotes LLC uses collected information for the following purposes: products and services provision, billing, identification and authentication, service improvement, contact, and general research.

## Cookies

Cookies are small text files containing a user's settings and other data used by websites, and are stored on the user's computer. They are transmitted as parcels of text sent by a server to a Web client (usually a browser) and then sent back unchanged by client each time it accesses that server.

Cookies are required to use the Sight Reading Factory service. We use cookies to record current session information, but we never use permanent cookies.

## Data Storage
