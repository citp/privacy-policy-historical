ScreenTek Privacy Policy

We care about cutomer privacy, and abide by all ethical standards. We pledge to protect our customers' personal information and only use this information for appropriate internal use.

Privacy - we recognize the importance of protecting customers' personal information. We follow legal requirements and take additional measures to protect necessary personal information that we may require from our customers. We have security measures in place for continuous protection of our customers' personal information. We will not sell or release customer's personal information to any third party.

Collection and use - we only collect the minimum information necessary using appropriate and legal means. We only store necessary customer information for our own internal purposes: customer communication, order processing, payment processing, and shipping.

Security - we have taken appropriate security measures to prevent any unauthorized access, disclosure, loss and destruction or falsification of personal information.

Opt out - in the event you want us to cease storing your personal information and stop future communications, you may remove your account from our online store.

If you have questions about the handling of personal information, please contact us at Sales@ScreenTekInc.com or by phone at +1 (713) 839-9640. If we make changes to our privacy policies, we will update the privacy-policies page of our website.
