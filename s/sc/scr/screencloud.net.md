# ScreenCloud | Home

###### Version 01

* * *

###### This document

This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by screencloud.net and how it is used. It also covers subdomains of screencloud.net like [blog.screencloud.net](https://web.archive.org/web/20140625133100id_/http%3A//screencloud.net/pages/blog.screencloud.net) and others.

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at [contact@screencloud.net](mailto:contact@screencloud.net). 

###### Log Files

Like many other Web sites, screencloud.net makes use of **log files**. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. 

###### Cookies and Web Beacons

screencloud.net does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser. Cookies are also used to **track if a user is logged in** and allow access to certain pages if they are.

###### Security of your information

We take steps and precautions to secure your data on our servers, but we **cannnot guarantee or warrant** the security of any information you provide to us. We do not accept liability for unintentional disclosure.

Please note that all screenshots are public by default. This means that they can be viewed by anyone with the screencloud link (e.g: <http://screencloud.net/v/cVAW>) and be indexed by search engines. It's therefore not recommended to upload screenshots containing any sensitive information.
