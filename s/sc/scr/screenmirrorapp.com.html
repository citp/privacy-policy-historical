Privacy Policy for Screen Mirror

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Screen Mirror (www.screenmirrorapp.com) for mobile devices that was created by Smart Dato vGmbH. The Application is able to stream your device's screen to a browser over WiFi.

 

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information 

The Application obtains the information you provide when you download and register the Application. No Registration is required.

When you send us a feedback email we can access your name and your email address.

We may also use the information you provided us to contact your from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices and marketing promotions.

Camera Information 

Screen Mirror accesses the camera of your device in order to scan QR codes. Camera images are only used for this purpose and are not shared or transmitted further.

Screen Information 

Screen Mirror accesses your devices screen to transmit it to a target brwoser. This data is only transmitted in your local network and is not shared or transmitted further.

Automatically Collected Information 

In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the local IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, information about the way you use the Application and the specific configuration information (local IP address, quality, settings, transmission time) needed to transmit to the target device, i.e. to the target browser. 

 

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device. 

 

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

Yes. We will share your information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.

We may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information:

  • when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;

  • with our trusted services providers who work on our behalf, do not have an independent use of the information we disclose to them, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement.

  • if Smart Dato vGmbH is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, a prominent notice on our Web site of any change in ownership or uses of this information.

  • to advertisers and third party advertising networks and analytics companies as described in the section below

We may work with analytics companies to help us understand how the Application is being used, such as the frequency and duration of usage. Contact us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using Screen Mirror, or have questions about our practices, please contact us via email at apps@smart-dato.com.


Screen Mirror stores no personal information, neither can your streaming screen be stored. All the screen mirroring transmission is held localy, only the network configuration is transmitted to our server in order to provide it to the target device via the website screenmirrorapp.com. We store on our server the length of the screen mirror sessions, the settings of the session (quality, local ip, port). It is impossible to infer from that information something about your identity.
We do not store your public ip address nor any other personal information.
