PRIVACY POLICY

We take the privacy of our visitors very serious at Screensavers Planet. We do not collect any personal information such as your name, address or e-mail. We do, however, collect anonymous data on the use of this website. This information, such as the pages on this website you visit, the screensavers you download, is used to improve the Screensavers Planet website. None of this information will be sold, rented or shared with any third party, ever. Third-party ad serving companies that we use, however, may also collect this information (still not including any personal information such as your name and e-mail address). In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser.

Please contact us if you have any questions regarding your privacy at Screensavers Planet.
