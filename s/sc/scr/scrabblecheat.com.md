# Scrabble Cheat privacy policy

* ScrabbleCheat.com will never SELL OR TRADE your contact information to anyone.  
* ScrabbleCheat.com will only GIVE AWAY your contact information in cases where you have requested to receive more information from one of the site sponsors.  
* ScrabbleCheat.com will only contact you directly if one of the following occurs:

o You’ve asked to be placed on my newsletter mailing list  
o You’ve contacted me directly and asked for a response  
o You’ve made a comment that attacks the site or the author  
o You have subscribed to the site and an announcement about the site is made.

* If you want to stop receiving information from our sponsors, you will need to contact them directly.  
* If you have additional questions about [Scrabble® Cheat](http://scrabblecheat.com/ "Scrabble® Cheat"), you can email [ us ](mailto:support@scrabblecheat.com) for more information.

**Privacy Policy for www.ScrabbleCheat.com**

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at steve@softwaretofit.com. 

At www.ScrabbleCheat.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by www.ScrabbleCheat.com and how it is used. 

**Log Files** Like many other Web sites, www.ScrabbleCheat.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. 

**Cookies and Web Beacons** www.ScrabbleCheat.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
