# Privacy Policy | SciQuest

This Privacy Policy describes the information SciQuest collects about you as a visitor to our website(s) and informs you how SciQuest uses this information and describes your choices regarding certain types of processing. This Privacy Policy does not cover any information or data collected by SciQuest when you are using SciQuest’s software applications and services. Please see our [Service Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/about/service-privacy-policy).

### Personal Information SciQuest Processes    __Return to Top

SciQuest collects personal information about a visitor to our website only if you voluntarily supply us with this information. For example, on certain pages you may choose to provide SciQuest with your name, e-mail address, telephone number or other information, to request details about SciQuest, including information about SciQuest’s applications and services. This information enables SciQuest to follow up with you after your visit. If you have voluntarily provided this information, you have consented to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. 

### Use and Disclosure of Personal Information    __Return to Top

SciQuest does not sell personal information collected through its websites. SciQuest may not share any personal information with third parties unless: (i) authorized by you in writing, (ii) with service providers authorized by SciQuest to perform services for the purposes set forth in this Privacy Policy, and only when confidentiality agreements are in place or (iii) compelled to do so by a governmental authority, regulatory body, or under subpoena or similar governmental request, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements, or to establish or defend a legal claim.

### Use of Website(s)    __Return to Top

SciQuest, or SciQuest-authorized service providers, may observe your activities, preferences and transactional data relating to your use of our website(s). While SciQuest may collect or log this information, SciQuest does not identify you or match this information with your other personal information (such as your IP address) unless you have chosen to identify yourself by completing a contact form on the website. SciQuest may use this collected or logged information to diagnose website technical problems. SciQuest or our authorized service provider may use this information to track aggregate traffic patterns throughout our website(s). If you have submitted contact information via forms on a site, SciQuest may use information from cookies and web beacons to provide you with information about SciQuest and our software applications and services.

### Cookies    __Return to Top

SciQuest uses technology that many other websites employ, including without limitation, “cookies” or small data files stored on your computer’s hard drive, to collect information. Cookies are small text files that our websites ask to place on your computer's hard drive. If your browser is set to accept cookies, then your browser adds the text in a small file. SciQuest does not use cookies to read any other information on your computer's hard drive. SciQuest uses cookie technology to authenticate your access to various areas of our websites, and tailor its content to your preferences. Cookies help us analyze web traffic in general and to track when site visitors complete surveys. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, or to warn you before a cookie is placed.

SciQuest also uses web beacons, or transparent GIF files, provided by our service provider to report activity on our site. The beacons help us manage our online advertising and measure its effectiveness. These files enable our service provider to recognize a cookie on your web browser, which in turn enables us to learn which advertisements are most effective. Either SciQuest or our services providers place the cookie. The information that SciQuest collects by means of these technologies is anonymous and is not personally identifiable unless you have chosen to identify yourself through a form on the website.

### Retargeted Advertising    __Return to Top

Retargeted advertising uses information collected on an individual's web browsing behavior such as the pages they have visited or the searches they have made. This information is used to display more relevant information to you on the SciQuest website. This information is also used to display SciQuest employment opportunities to you if you have visited our careers page. The information collected is only linked to an anonymous cookie ID (alphanumeric number); it does not include any information that could be linked back to a particular person, such as their name, address or credit card number. The information used for targeted advertising either comes from SciQuest or through third party website publishers.

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can change your browser to prevent cookies or to notify you each time a cookie is set. To opt out of receiving marketing related communications from SciQuest, please click on the “opt-out” link in the communication or please contact us at privacy@sciquest.com. Please note this does not opt you out of receiving generic ads.

### Social Media Features and Widgets    __Return to Top

Our websites include social media features, such as the Facebook Like button, and widgets, such as the Share this button or interactive mini-programs that run on our sites. These features may collect your IP address, which page you are visiting on our sites and may set a cookie to enable the feature to function properly. Social media features and widgets are either hosted by a third party or hosted directly on our sites. Your interactions with these features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing it.

### Opt-Out; Access    __Return to Top

If at any time after registering for information, you change your mind about receiving information from us, please click on the “opt-out” link in the communication or send us a request at privacy@SciQuest.com specifying your new choice. If you otherwise wish to contact SciQuest directly to access to the personal information that SciQuest holds about you, you may email your request to SciQuest at privacy@SciQuest.com. SciQuest will provide you with the information you have sought by email, or if possible, in another form that you have requested. SciQuest may need to verify your identity or to take other steps to respond to your request. If SciQuest decides to deny you access to some or all of your personal information, SciQuest will notify you in writing of our decision and explain why and how you can complain if you are not satisfied with our decision.

### SciQuest EU-US Privacy Shield Framework Privacy Statement    __Return to Top

SciQuest complies with the applicable provisions of the EU-US Privacy Shield Framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use and retention of personal information from the European Union. SciQuest adheres to the seven Privacy Shield Principles: Notice; Choice; Accountability for Onward Transfer; Security; Data Integrity and Purpose Limitation; Access; and Recourse, Enforcement and Liability. To learn more about the EU-US Privacy Shield Framework, and to view SciQuest’s certification, please visit [www.privacyshield.gov.](http://www.privacyshield.gov/)

### Swiss Safe Harbor Program Statement    __Return to Top

SciQuest complies with the applicable provisions of the U.S. – Swiss Safe Harbor framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use and retention of data from Switzerland. Information regarding the Swiss Safe Harbor Framework can be found at: [export.gov/safeharbor](http://export.gov/safeharbor).

### Complaint Handling; Dispute Resolution    __Return to Top

In compliance with the EU-US Privacy Shield Framework and the US-Swiss Safe Harbor Principles, SciQuest commits to resolve complaints about your privacy and our collection or use of your personal information. Individuals with inquiries or complaints regarding this Privacy Policy should contact SciQuest at: privacy@sciquest.com.

With respect to the EU-US Privacy Shield Framework, SciQuest has further committed to refer unresolved privacy complaints under the EU-US Privacy Shield Principles to BBB EU PRIVACY SHIELD, a non-profit alternative dispute resolution provider located in the United States and operated by the Council of Better Business Bureaus. If you do not receive timely acknowledgment of your complaint, or if your complaint is not satisfactorily addressed, please visit [www.bbb.org/EU-privacy-shield/for-eu-consumers](http://www.bbb.org/EU-privacy-shield/for-eu-consumers) for more information and to file a complaint. Additionally, in accordance with the EU-US Privacy Shield, an individual may under certain conditions, invoke binding arbitration.

With respect to the US-Swiss Safe Harbor Principles, SciQuest has further committed to refer unresolved privacy complaints under the US-Swiss Safe Harbor Principles to an independent dispute resolution mechanism, the BBB EU SAFE HARBOR, operated by the Council of Better Business Bureaus. If you do not receive timely acknowledgment of your complaint, or if your complaint is not satisfactorily addressed by SciQuest, please visit the BBB EU SAFE HARBOR website at [www.bbb.org/us/safe-harborcomplaints](http://www.bbb.org/us/safe-harborcomplaints) for more information and to file a complaint.

### Investigations; Enforcement; Liability   __Return to Top

In cases of onward transfer to third parties of data of EU individuals received pursuant to the EU-US Privacy Shield, SciQuest is potentially liable.

SciQuest maintains a comprehensive, written information security program that contains industry standard, administrative, technical and physical safeguards designed to prevent unauthorized access to personal information.
