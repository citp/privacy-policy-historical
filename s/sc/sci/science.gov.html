Privacy, Security, Accessibility, and Disclaimer Notices

Privacy

  • Information Collected

    This Web site collects no personal information about you pursuant to your use unless you choose to provide this information to us. Certain site-access information is automatically captured and stored, including:

    • The Internet Protocol (IP) address of the domain from which you access the Internet (i.e. 123.456.789.012) whether yours individually or provided as a proxy by your Internet Service Provider (ISP);

    • The type of browser and operating system used to access our site;

    • The date and time you access our site;

    • Pages accessed (recorded by the text and graphics files that compose that page); and

    • Referring Web site (if you linked to this site from another Web site).

    Site-access information is aggregated and used to assess the value of the Web site in accomplishing its stated information dissemination goals, to plan for enhancements to make this site more useful to visitors, to optimize the site's technical design specifications, and/or to identify system performance or problem areas. This information is used only as a source of anonymous statistical data, and is shared only when required by law enforcement investigation. We do not track or record information about individuals and their visits.

  • Personal Information

    You may choose to provide us with personal information by filling out a form or sending an e-mail message containing specific comments and/or questions. This information is used to respond to your request or to forward your request to the appropriate party who can best answer your question. Except for authorized law enforcement investigations, these e-mails and addresses are shared only to the extent necessary to assure that an accurate and timely response is provided. This Web site does not collect information for commercial marketing

  • Use of Computer Cookies

    In accordance with Office of Management and Budget (OMB) direction, this Web site does not use persistent cookies (bits of information placed on a user's computer to track customer use and related patterns) on any of its Web-based products. Session cookies may be used to provide salient session-related information and to allow product-specific functions to be performed, but in no case will cookies be used to capture information that might compromise or threaten personal privacy.

    For products that require a customer profile to accomplish business transactions, such as automated notifications or directed distribution of recurring information within a predefined set, this site may retain voluntarily-submitted customer data; however, persistent cookies will not be used for such purpose
