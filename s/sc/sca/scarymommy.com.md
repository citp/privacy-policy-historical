# Privacy Policy –

**Last Updated: October 24, 2018**

We are strongly committed to letting you know how we will use the personal information we collect from or about you. The policies below are applicable to data and information collected by Some Spider and its affiliated properties Scary Mommy, The Dad, and Cafe, their associated websites (including any versions optimized for viewing on a wireless or tablet device); their email newsletters; their apps; and all other interactive features, services, and communications provided by Some Spider (collectively, the “Service” or “Services”), however accessed and/or used, that are operated by us, made available by us, or produced and maintained by Some Spider. We have established this privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) to let you know the kinds of information we may gather when you interact with our Services, why we gather it, what we use it for, when we might disclose it, and how you can manage it.

By using our Service, you are accepting the practices described in our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Service. We may modify or amend the Privacy Policy from time to time without notice by posting the changes. Your continued use of our Service following the posting of changes is your acceptance of those changes. 

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or don’t see your concerns addressed here, you may contact us by email at legal@somespider.com.

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected when you use a website, mobile or tablet application or other online service (collectively, the “Services”) that links or refers to it. It does not apply to information collected or used by [Company] through other means.

  * Information you provide when you submit content or comment on content posted on our Services. The content you submit may be visible to other users of our Services. Please refrain from submitting content that you would not want to be revealed publicly.
  * Information you provide when you interact with us via social media (such as our Facebook, Twitter, Google+, Reddit, Instagram, or Pinterest pages), including any posts, pictures, videos and messages which you submit to us either via our social media sites (including by use of hashtags associated with us) or our site. Please note: we will comply with the privacy policies of the corresponding social media platform and we will only collect and store the information that we are permitted to collect by these social media platforms. Where you choose to interact with us through social media, your interaction with social media providers typically allows them to collect some information about you through cookies they place on your device and other tracking mechanisms. In some cases, they may recognize you using these cookies even when you do not interact with their application(s). Please visit those third parties’ respective privacy policies to better understand their data collection practices and controls they make available to you.
  * Information you provide when you participate in our surveys.



_Information We Collect Automatically_

  * Location information (via your IP address)
  * Details of your visits to our site and information generated in the course of the use our site (including the timing, frequency and pattern of service use) including, but not limited to, traffic data, location data, weblogs and other communication data, the resources that you access, and how you reached our site.

  * Information about how you interact with our newsletters, including whether you open or click links in any correspondence.



In addition to information that you give us or that we collect automatically about you, we also may collect and store information about you that we receive from other sources (such as our third party advertising partners) to, among other things, enable us to update and correct the information contained in our database and to better customize your experience on the Services.

**How Do We Use Your Information?**

We use information from or about you in the following ways:

  * _to provide you with our products and services_ , for example: to upload your content to our Service as you request; to communicate with you about your account (including service related announcements) and send you information about features and enhancements on our Service and third-party deals and offers, and to communicate with you about changes to our policies and about your content;
  * _to send you our newsletters_ , to make your Some Spider experience more efficient, and to provide you with information, offers, and deals that we think may be relevant. Please note: You may always opt-out of receiving future e-mail messages and newsletters from Some Spider. To opt-out, you can send us a message at legal@somespider.com. Please note, however, that you generally cannot opt-out of Service related announcements, e.g. if the Service is temporarily suspended or if delivery of a product or service is delayed;
  * _to enable additional features or interact with us outside of our website_ , such as via email or social media;
  * _to improve our Services and grow our business._
  * _to personalize content and experiences on our Service_ , including providing you reports, recommendations and feedback based on your preferences, for example: to tailor advertising to you, and to understand our customer base and trends by analyzing our user base;
  * _to detect, investigate, and prevent activities that may violate our policies or be fraudulent or illegal._

We use the information from one portion of the Services on other portions of the Services in our network and in reports and analysis, all of which are owned and operated by Some Spider, and we may combine the information we gather into a single customer record, analysis or report.

We take the security of your personal information very seriously. We exercise care in providing transmission of your information from your device to our servers. **Is Personal Information Collected From Children?**

Some Spider does not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 16 years of age. If you are under 16 years of age you should not provide personally identifiable information to Some Spider without providing us with consent from your parents. If Some Spider discovers that a child under the age of 16 has provided Some Spider with personally identifiable information and we do not have parental consent, Some Spider will immediately delete that child’s information from the Service.
