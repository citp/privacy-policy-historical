# Privacy Policy - Safe, Secure Online Shopping - From Professional Wholesale Distributor

**Privacy policy**  
Sw-box.com respects customers privacy, and has made strict policy to protect your personal information. Please note that this privacy policy only applies to this site. It does not necessarily apply to any offline collection of your personal information. Please see below for details.

**What information does sw-box.com collect?**  
Sw-box.com collects personal information from the people who registered or ordered online. Customers’ name, address, email address, telephone number, city, country, post code and credit card information would be collected. If you contact us, we may keep a record of that correspondence and information regarding your personal or professional interests, demographics, experiences with our products and contact preferences in order to provide you with further correspondence about our products and services.

**What is the purpose of the collection?**  
•help make the site more easier for you to use, no need to input the information many times.  
•help you find information, products more quickly and easier.  
•provide you an efficient, meaningful and customized experience.

**Will sw-box share your personal information?**  
Sw-box.com will never share the information with any non-affiliated entity. As necessary, solely for the purpose of helping sw-box, some third parties like the international carriers may process your personal information under the confidentiality agreement. We won’t sell, disclose or share your personal information with a third party.

**Changes of privacy policy**  
Sw-box.com reserves the right to change privacy policy at any time, customers will be informed promptly. In that case, you may choose to continue to use your account or terminate it.
