# Basic Privacy Principles | Swrve

## Basic Privacy Principles

Respect for privacy is fundamental to the culture of Swrve New Media Group which includes Swrve New Media, Inc. and its affiliates (“ **Swrve** ”) and helps maintain trust in our brand and in our products and services. Swrve is transparent about our privacy practices, including why we collect, use, keep, and share Personal Information. By visiting our website or providing us with your Personal Information, you consent to the collection, processing, and storage of your Personal Information as described in this Privacy Notice.  **Read our complete[Online Privacy Notice](https://swrve.com/company/privacy-policy) for more details.  See also our [EU-US Privacy Shield Notic](https://swrve.com/company/privacy-shield)**[e](https://swrve.com/company/privacy-shield).

#### The following are the basic principles we apply when collecting and using Personal Information:

## Transparent Collection and Choice

Swrve will always try to explain why we need to collect Personal Information when we ask you for it and give you a choice when appropriate.

## Limited Use

Swrve will only use your Personal Information for the purposes for which we collect it. Fr example, to deliver a product or a service that you requested, enable you to join online activities, or improve your experience with us. We will keep it only to fulfill compatible purposes, additional purposes to which you have consented, or as required by law.

## Limited Sharing

When Swrve shares your Personal Information with authorized third parties , we provide them with only the amount they need to complete the service requested. We do not allow third parties to use your Personal Information for something different. We do not sell Personal Information to third parties. 

## Security

The security of your personal information is very important to us. Swrve uses industry standard security methods to protect your personal information from loss and misuse.

## It's Your Information

You may update your Personal Information by emailing [sales@swrve.com](mailto:sales@swrve.com) and manage your communication preferences through unsubscribe options on our communications.

Our websites use cookies.  For more information about how we use cookies, please see our [Cookie Notice](https://www.swrve.com/cookies).
