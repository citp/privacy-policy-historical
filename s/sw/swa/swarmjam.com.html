If we learn that a SwarmJam user is under 18, we will take steps to remove that user's Personal Information from our databases.



Collection and Use of Information

In general our collection of personal information is described below in the following key sources of collection and site activities:


  • Information Voluntarily Submitted: Information voluntarily submitted for creating or editing your account and user profile on SwarmJam. When you personalize your profile and use the features of SwarmJam, we collect any information you voluntarily provide, and we may also request optional information to support your use of SwarmJam, such as your year of birth, gender and other demographic information.

  • Content Submissions: Information voluntarily submitted during your use of the SwarmJam site which can include content as photos, reviews, ratings and other information you choose to submit.

  • Personal Information from Other Sources: Personal information from other sources with which you have registered, companies with whom we have partnered with (collectively, "Partners") or other third parties. For example, we may collect your user ID or profile information that you have permitted to be displayed through SwarmJam in order to display you as a friend or in association with your profile and collections. We may associate this information with the other Personal Information we have collected about you. We may also collect information about you and your friends who use SwarmJam, from any social network you may have connected from, in order to provide you with a more personalized experience.

  • Payments: When you purchase the items offered on SwarmJam, you will need to submit your credit card or other payment information so that our service providers can process your payment for those products and services. When you sign up for, or win, any special auctions, sweepstakes, or other activities that we make available on SwarmJam, we will collect or utilize the information designated along with such activity, which may include your contact information such as your address and phone number.

  • Email Addresses: If you send an e-mail to us, or fill out our "Feedback" form through SwarmJam, we may file electronically your e-mail address and the full content of your e-mail, including attached files, and other information you provide. We may use and display your full name and email address when you send an email notification to a friend through SwarmJam or the social network from which you have connected to SwarmJam (such as in an invitation, or when sharing your content). We will use your e-mail address to contact you, for things such as notifications of our daily offers and other special offers or contests and other related information. You may indicate your preference to stop receiving further promotional communications as further detailed below.

  • Use of Technology: Information collected through the use of technology such as your Internet protocol address, browser type, and operating system collected to make SwarmJam and solutions more useful to you and to tailor the experience with SwarmJam to meet your special interests and needs. We also use Cookies and navigational data like Uniform Resource Locators (URL) to gather information regarding the date and time of your visit and the solutions and information for which you searched and viewed, or on which of the advertisements displayed on SwarmJam you clicked. In order to use our services offered through SwarmJam, your web browser must accept Cookies.

These advertisers and ad servers may use Cookies and/or "Web Beacons" (which are usually small, transparent graphic images) in order to monitor information related to served advertisements.
