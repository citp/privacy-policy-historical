    • Send an email to Customer support
    • Log in with your password and change/delete your profile

If you have questions or concerns, please use Customer support form on sweetandcasual.com web-site and our team will deal with your enquiry quickly, professionally and efficiently because we take privacy of our members seriously and are ready to address all the concerns they may have.
