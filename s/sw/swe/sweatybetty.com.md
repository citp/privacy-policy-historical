# sweatyBetty | Security and Privacy

**Privacy Policy**

At sweatyBetty we take your privacy seriously. We also want to use this great technology to treat you as real customers in the old fashioned sense of the word. We have drawn up these brief guidelines so that you know your information is secure. However, internet technologies are rapidly evolving so these guidelines are subject to change. 

**1) What We Do**  
We ask you to supply the following information during the ordering process:   
name   
mailing address   
email address   
telephone number   
credit card details   
we may ask for some demographic information for user profiling in aggregate form. 

We use this information for the following purposes:   
to send you the product that you have bought.   
to charge you for the product.   
to send email or conventional mail to you, after you have purchased a product.   
to let you know by email of new ranges and services that have come in.   
to perform statistical analyses measuring relative interest in the various areas of our site and customer profiles. 

In addition we may employ HTTP cookies:   
to recognize users.   
to track individual shopping carts through the ordering process. 

**2) What We Don't Do**  
We do not, under any circumstances:   
provide email addressses to any third party.   
share the information we track on this site, in any form, with advertisers or other parties.   
release personal information about any individual visitors. 

Upon request, we will remove you from the email update service. Simply write to **[info@sweatybetty.com](mailto:info@sweatybetty.com)** with 'unsubscribe' in the subject field. 

We are not responsible for the content or the privacy policies of Web sites to which this site may link.

For further details on the privacy of your information email [**info@sweatyBetty.com**](mailto:info@sweatyBetty.com) or else freephone 0800 1693889.
