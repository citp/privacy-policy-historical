# Swire Pacific Offshore - Privacy Notices

###  **Website Privacy Notice**

**PRIVACY NOTICE FOR WEBSITE USERS (“WEBSITE PRIVACY NOTICE”)**

  We respect the privacy of everyone who visits this website (“ **Site** ”). As such, we would like to inform you on the personal data we collect from you through your use of this Site and how we use such personal data.

  **Special Notice for Minors under 14 years old**

  We do not intentionally collect personal data of those under 14 years old and do not aim to market our services to those under 14 years old. If you are under 14 years old, please do not send us your personal data. If you are under 14 years old and wish to ask us a question or use our Site in a manner that requires you to submit your personal data, please get your parent or guardian to do so on your behalf.

  **_What does this Privacy Notice apply to?_**

  This Website Privacy Notice sets out the policy of the Swire Pacific Offshore Group (“ **SPO Group** ”) in relation to the personal data we collect through your use of our website (“ **Site** ”). In this policy, the SPO Group means Swire Pacific Offshore Operations Pte Ltd (“ **SPO** ”) and all its affiliates as set out in our Site.

  This Website Privacy Notice applies to the majority of the websites operated by the SPO Group, however there may be occasions where there is a need to have a slightly different privacy policy to account for local differences in privacy regulations. When you access another website belonging to our Affiliate, please check the privacy policy of each such website which you visit and do not assume that this privacy policy applies to all websites within the SPO Group.

  This Website Privacy Notice explains **what** personal data we collect from you, for **which purposes** we collect and use such personal data, **how long** we retain the personal data, how to **access and update** your personal data and how to contact us for **further information**.

  **_Who controls your personal information?_**

  Unless otherwise specified at the relevant part of the Site, your personal data is ultimately controlled by Swire Pacific Offshore Operations Pte Ltd , a company registered in Singapore with its business address at 300 Beach Road, #15-01 The Concourse, Singapore 199555.

  **_How long do we retain your personal data?_**

  For personal data submitted for job applications through our website, please refer to our privacy notice for job applicants on our retention policy for such information.

  For all other personal information submitted through our website by you, we will delete such personal data without undue delay once there is no longer a legitimate purpose to retain it, or where you object to or withdraw your consent for the processing of your personal data.

  Please note that in all cases, we may retain the personal data for a longer period of time where there is a legal or regulatory reason to do so.

  **_Changes to this Website Privacy Notice_**

  This Website Privacy Notice may be changed over time. You are advised to regularly review the Website Privacy Notice for any changes made. This Website Privacy Notice was last updated in May 2018.
