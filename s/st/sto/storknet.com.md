# StorkNet - The Pregnancy and Parenting Online Community

**Privacy Policy**

StorkNet has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the StorkNet Family of websites, which includes:  
     StorkNetFamily.com  
     StorkNet.com  
     PregnancyGuideOnline.com  
     Exploring Womanhood  
     Married Romance  
     EriChad.com  
     BooksforFamilies.com

Doerr Consulting is the legal owner of StorkNet and is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will never share, sell, or rent your personal information in ways that are not disclosed in this statement without your consent. Any breach of this policy by our staff will result in immediate dismissal. 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website, monitor site usage and help us collect broad demographic information. Such statistics help us identify popular areas so we can improve the visitor experience. 

**Advertisers**

We use outside advertising companies to display some of the ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies on our message boards, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad agencies, and we do not have access to this information. These companies may use information ( **not** including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [**click here**](http://networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp). 

**Surveys and Contests**

From time to time, our site requests information from users via surveys or contests. Participation is completely voluntary and the user therefore has a choice whether or not to disclose this information. This information is not shared with any third party. Survey information is used for purposes of monitoring or improving use and satisfaction of StorkNet and your name is not required.

**External Links**

This site contains links to other sites. StorkNet is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. When you click on a link that takes you off a StorkNet site, this Privacy Policy no longer applies including e-commerce sites listed in the StorkNet Mall.

**Newsletters**

If you subscribe to our Before Birth and Beyond newsletter, you will not receive separate email from other companies as a result of subscribing to our newsletter. Subscribed addresses are not shared or sold with other companies. If you decide to unsubscribe, you may do so via the link provided in the newsletter or going to **<http://www.storknet.com/newsletter>**.

**Send This Page to a Friend Feature**

If a user elects to use our referral service for informing a friend about our site, the user is asked for the friend's name and email address. StorkNet automatically sends the friend a one-time email inviting them to visit the site. This information is not stored and the friend will never receive another email from StorkNet as a result of this service.
