## Privacy Policy

StockPickTrading only contacts individuals who specifically request that we do so or in the event that they have signed up to receive our free newsletters. StockPickTrading collects personally identifying information from our users during online registration. Generally, this information includes name and e-mail address for registration to receive our free newsletters and name. All of this information is provided to us by you.

This information is used for internal marketing purposes only. StockPickTrading does not sell, exchange, or release your personal information (name, e-mail address) Your information used for the sole purpose of sending out Email newsletters on the information you have requested.
