# Privacy Policy | StockMarketsReview.com

No Release of Information to Third Parties: 

Your privacy is importance to us, which is why we will never share your personal information with third parties, except to further the purpose for which you provided the information, or if release of the information is required by law. Cases where your personal information may be required for judicial or governmental investigations and proceedings are exempt from this policy. 

There are no other circumstances under which will be provide or sell personal information to third parties. 

The information obtained through this site is to be used solely to provide you with efficient, professional service from StockMarketsReview.com
