STORE SUPPLY WAREHOUSE PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy describes what information Store Supply Warehouse, LLC (Store Supply Warehouse, we, us, or our) collects through our websites at www.storesupply.com, www.sswdealersupply.com, and www.sswgoodwill.com (individually and collectively, the Website), how we handle personal information, and the choices available to you regarding collection, use, access, and updating of your personal information.

By accessing or using any of our sites and/or our services or otherwise providing us with any information, you consent to the handling of your information as described in this Policy.

Collection of Your Personal Information

Information you provide to us

We (and our third party service providers) may collect data, including personal information, about you as you use our Website and interact with us, such as when you conduct transactions online, subscribe to receive information or email offers, participate in surveys, participate in a promotion, enter contests or sweepstakes, contact us, or interact with special event or program offers.  "Personal information" is any information that can be used to identify an individual, and may include name, address, email address, phone number, login information (account number, password), marketing preferences, social media account information, or payment card number.

We also collect personal information from third-party sources. 

Information we collect through these interactions may include, but is not limited to:

  • Contact information (such as name, address, city, state and ZIP code, email address and telephone number), which we may for example link to an IP address
  • Demographic information (such as age range, marital status and household composition)
  • Payment information (such as credit or debit card information)
  • Purchase information
  • User and activity data from our Website
  • Survey responses
  • Photographs (such as those that you may submit for contests or sweepstakes)

Information we collect automatically

We and our third party service providers may automatically collect information about your use of the Website through cookies, web beacons, log files and other technologies. This information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Domain name
  • Browser type and operating system
  • IP address
  • Web pages you view
  • Links you click
  • Length of time of your visit
  • Your activities on our Websites including your movement through our Websites
  • Referring URL or webpage that led you to our Websites

We and our third party service providers may combine this information with information that we (or they) already have collected about you.

Information we collect from third parties

We may collect information about you from third parties, including personal, demographic and aggregate information.  For example, we may also obtain information about you from our marketing service providers, advertisers and other third parties.

Information related to employment applications

Potential future employees of Store Supply Warehouse may choose to submit an employment application on the Website.  The pages listing employment opportunities and the Careers pages are operated by a third party vendor and information those third parties collect on their own website is subject to the terms and conditions and privacy policy of that site.

Your application information, once delivered to us, is used only to process your employment request, which includes corresponding with you to make arrangements for your association with us or to notify you of the results of our review of your application.

Privacy of Children

Our Website is not intended for individuals under the age of 13.  We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information from users of this site who are under the age of 13. 

If you believe that we may have collected personal information from someone under the applicable age of consent and without proper consent, please notify us using the methods described in the Contact Us section so we can investigate and address the matter. 

If we become aware that a user of our Website is under the age of 13 and has provided personally identifiable information to us via our Website, we will remove that childs personal information from our files. 

Uses of Your Information

We use your personal information and other information principally to provide our services to you and to fulfill your requests. We may combine, link, or aggregate your information with other information that we have about you, that is publicly available and/or that we have obtained from third parties (either individually or in the aggregate). We also may use your information for the following purposes: 

  • To improve your user experience, including by customizing information for you.
  • For our operational needs and to facilitate providing services.
  • For marketing and advertising, including by combining your information with information we get from third parties for email, direct mail, telephone, web or other electronic advertising and to communicate with you about offers from us or third parties that might be of interest to you. We may also use your information to determine appropriate advertising channels and venues and to place ads on such channels and venues, including placing advertising on social networking sites.
  • For surveys and promotions.
  • To improve and maintain our Websites and our services, including for other research and analytical purposes.
  • For security purposes, to protect our rights and interests and the rights and interests of other users of our services.

Third parties providing services to us or on our behalf may use your information for all of the purposes described above in order to provide such services.

Disclosing Your Information

Store Supply Warehouse may disclose your personal information to:

  • Third party service providers that assist us and perform functions for us (including technical support, website hosting, marketing support, research, analytics, service/order fulfillment, customer service and credit card processing). In some cases third-party service providers, such as information technology and data processing vendors, data storage services, etc., may be located outside the United States and your personal information may be processed or stored in other countries. We also use third parties to assist us in determining and placing relevant advertising.
  • Marketing partners with whom we are collaborating on joint promotions or marketing.
  • Our subsidiary and affiliated entities.
  • Law enforcement or other third parties to protect the rights or property of Store Supply Warehouse, our customers, our Website or Website users, or to comply with other laws or legal requirements (including subpoenas).
  • Third parties involved in business transactions involving us or our affiliates, such as a sale of any portion of our business, or obtaining financing, or as part of a bankruptcy proceeding.

Use of Cookies, Web Beacons and Other Similar Technologies

Store Supply Warehouse and our third party service providers collect information by using technologies and methodologies, such as "cookies", tags, web beacons or other similar technologies that enable tracking as you browse our Website. These technologies authenticate you as a user, remember your preferences for using the site, present offers that may be of interest, and facilitate transactions. We and our third party service providers also use these tools to analyze use of our Website and services, and may use this information in the aggregate or combine it with information that we have already collected about you.

Cookie. "Cookies" are small bits of information that are stored on your computer by your web browser. Cookies and other technologies described in this Privacy Policy allow us to recognize your preference information, to track your transactions and to facilitate effective website administration.

Cookies help establish a user session and allow our server to provide site users with tailored information, advertisements and services. We also use cookies to authenticate your authority to access the Website, to track the usage of our Website, and to improve the Website.

If you do not want your information to be collected through cookies, you may be able to change the preferences in your particular Internet browser. Some Website functionality may not work properly if your browser does not accept cookies.  Please consult your chosen Internet browser for instructions on how to change your cookie settings.

Local storage objects. We may use Flash Local Storage Objects ("Flash LSOs") to store your preferences and to personalize your visit. Flash LSOs are different from browser cookies because of the amount and type of data stored. Typically, you cannot control, delete or disable the acceptance of Flash LSOs through your web browser. For more information on Flash LSOs or to learn how to manage your settings for Flash LSOs, go to the Adobe Flash Player Help Page, choose Global Storage Settings Panel and follow the instructions. To see the Flash LSOs currently on your computer, choose Website Storage Settings Panel and follow the instructions to review and, if you want, to delete any specific Flash LSO.

Clear GIFs, pixel tags and other technologies. These technologies allow us to recognize your preference information, keep track of your transactions and facilitate effective website administration. Clear GIFs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies. In contrast to cookies, which are stored on your computers hard drive, clear GIFs are embedded invisibly on web pages. We and our third-party service providers may use clear GIFs (also known as web beacons, web bugs or pixel tags), in connection with our sites and services to, among other things, track the activities of visitors, help us manage content and compile statistics about usage. We and our third party service providers may also use clear GIFs in HTML emails sent to you to help us track email response rates, identify when our emails are viewed and track whether our emails are forwarded.

Other Monitoring Software.  This Website is being monitored by one or more third-party monitoring software(s), and may capture information about you and your visit that will help us contact you and/or improve the quality of our service and website. You may be able to control the data being collected by such software from your visit through a universal consumer options page located at https://smart-pixl.com/Unsub/unsub.html

Internet Based Advertising and Internet Protocol (IP) Address Targeting

We may work with data providers to serve advertising to you through display media, in printed form, or through other methods or media.  These providers may use personal information and other information that we have collected or that you have provided to locate you online, such as when you visit or log in to websites or mobile applications.  This information may be linked to online identifiers or IP addresses assigned to you, and to demographic or interest-based data, in order to deliver ads that are customized to what these companies believe are most likely to be relevant and of interest to you (including about our products or services).

To learn more about or opt out of this and other interest-based advertising, please visit the industry opt-out pages operated by the Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) at http://www.aboutads.info and the by the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) at http://networkadvertising.org.  Opting out of one or more NAI or DAI members means that only those particular members will no longer deliver targeted content or ads to you. It does not mean you will no longer receive any targeted content or ads on our websites, on other non-Store Supply Warehouse Websites or otherwise. In addition, opting out in this way (or through our opt-out cookie) is browser-specific, so if you change your browser, update it, use a different computer, or clear your cookie cache, you will need to opt out again.

In addition, some third party advertising technology Store Supply Warehouse uses to deliver advertisements may utilize separate cookies and other tracking technologies. This technology may be delivered through visits to our Website, email messages, apps or otherwise. Web beacons on some pages of our Website, in conjunction with these advertising cookies and our other cookies, enable the third-party advertising technology to better understand onsite activity in the aggregate, to recognize a browser's cookie when a browser visits this Website, other websites or apps, and to learn which advertisements bring users to our site or our apps or lead them to otherwise take action.

Third-Party Analytics

Store Supply Warehouse may implement Google Analytics as a tool to help us understand customer visits to our Website and to facilitate advertising features for the Website.  As a part of Google Analytics, Google, as a third-party vendor, makes use of certain cookies and anonymous identifiers to serve ads on the Website and to provide certain behavioral, demographic and interest data to third parties.  Googles use of these cookies enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to the Website and other sites on the Internet.

You may be able to stop the collection of information we do using Google Analytics by disabling Google Analytics in your browsers Add-on settings. You may be able to opt out of Google Analytics by performing changes to your browsers add-on settings by downloading and installing the Google Analytics opt-out add-on found on Googles Opt-out page (https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en).  If you opt out of Google Analytics this may also disable Google Analytics for other websites, but you may be able to re-enable Google Analytics again by resetting the add-on in the browser.
