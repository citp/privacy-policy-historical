# Website Policy | Privacy Center | StateFoodSafety.com

Privacy Policy

StateFoodSafety does not rent, sell or share personal information (name, address, email or any other information) about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide requested products or services.

Any information gathered online or over the telephone will be used solely to contact you regarding related AboveTraining Inc. products and services.

StateFoodSafety limits access to personal information about you to employees who reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.

StateFoodSafety has physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to protect personal information about you.

If you have any further questions regarding our privacy policy or practices contact our [customer service department](https://www.statefoodsafety.com/Support).
