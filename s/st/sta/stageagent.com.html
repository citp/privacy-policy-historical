Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 31st, 2018

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. The StageAgent Privacy Policy describes what data we collect from you, the purpose for collecting the data in conjunction with the StageAgent Sites and Services, how we share, process, and manage your Personal Data, and your rights associated with your data.

This Privacy Policy applies to www.stageagent.com, blog.stageagent.com and www.stageagent.org (together, "StageAgent"). It explains how we collect information from users of StageAgent, and how we use such information. Your use of StageAgent constitutes your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. Please also read our Terms of Use, which govern the basic operation of StageAgent. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy or the Terms of Use, you should immediately stop using or visiting StageAgent.

1. The Information We Collect and Use

  • StageAgent collects Personal Data that you voluntarily provide us, when you register for StageAgent, create a talent profile, or create a teacher account. We also collect Personal Data when you sign up for email newsletters or alerts on StageAgent. Personal Data may contain your name, email, contact information, your location, as well as other information you provide to StageAgent.

  • StageAgent collects billing information when processing payment on StageAgent.com and StageAgent.org in conjunction with subscribing for membership.

  • StageAgent logs your visits and use of StageAgent such as when you view or click on content, or ads or perform a search. We use log-ins, cookies, web beacons, device information and internet protocol (“IP”) addresses to identify you, your location, and log your use.

  • StageAgent collects and stores data about you when you use StageAgent’s messaging platform to communicate with other users.

  • StageAgent may place a "cookie" and on your computer when you visit a StageAgent website. StageAgent may use cookies to personalize your experience using StageAgent, and to gather usage data that will help to improve the quality of the websites. There is a simple procedure in most browsers that allows you to deny, accept or delete cookies. Users may also opt out of the NAI's interest-based advertising network by clicking here and following the on-screen instructions.

  • StageAgent may also obtain information about you through third party sources as permitted by applicable law, such as public databases, social media platforms, and marketing partners.

2. Examples of How StageAgent Uses Your Data

  • To create an account for you to access StageAgent content and services.

  • To personalize StageAgent to give you a better experience, such as sending you targeted job postings based on your location.

  • To respond to your requests or to manage user accounts.

  • To receive product feedback and understand our users better in order to improve StageAgent.

  • To monitor the safety and security of StageAgent's services.

  • To use data and content about users for communications promoting membership, job posting, and engagement with StageAgent, on StageAgent properties and across other platforms.

StageAgent reserves the right to investigate abuses on the Websites and enforce and apply this Privacy Policy, the Terms of Use and any other terms or conditions applicable to the Websites or the use thereof. 3. How StageAgent shares information

Talent Profile

You may choose to make your talent profile fully visible to all users on and Backstage, as well as publicly discoverable across the world wide web, or not publicly discoverable, where only logged in users can view it. You may modify the information you have provided to Backstage at any time through your StageAgent profile.

Messaging

The StageAgent service allows you to share information with other users by sending and receiving messages on the StageAgent messaging system. When you send email or message StageAgent customer support, StageAgent will collect and store your name, email address and the content of your message so we can provide customer support.

When a user posts an event such as a job, audition or performance announcement, the notice may contain personally identifiable information. Other Parties Designated by You

When using the StageAgent Service, you may share your data with other parties designated by you. For example, when inviting a StageAgent user to apply to your casting notice, you may also share personally identifiable information with that user.

Technology and Infrastructure: StageAgent distributes data to processors in order to host, enable and support the StageAgent platform and service.

Analytics and Reporting: StageAgent distributes data to analytics platforms to better understand user behavior and track key business metrics, in order to provide a better service to its users.

We will protect your Personal Data in accordance with this Privacy Notice wherever it is processed and take appropriate steps to protect the information in accordance with applicable laws.
