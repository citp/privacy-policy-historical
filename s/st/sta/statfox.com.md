# StatFox | Privacy Policy

We are committed to providing the utmost in privacy and security. We abide by all industry guidelines and consistently monitor our management and operating methods in order to maintain our valued reputation and integrity. This is our promise to our clients, and end-users.

**Security**  
In order to ensure security and protection of collected information, all employees are aware of our security policy and practices. Web security is constantly reviewed. Access to the information is limited and only obtained through codenames and passwords. Any loss, misuse, or alteration of information is prohibited followed by appropriate action and termination of employee. This is to uphold our promise to our clients, affiliate members, and property users.

**Member Contact**  
If the opt-in box is selected on the entry form, we have permission to contact the applicant via electronic mail regarding information, savings, or special offers that relate directly to the information, savings, or special offers that relate directly to the information they have provided. This is opt-in only, meaning that the applicant has complete control in allowing us to contact them. Applicant must opt-in to receive secondary communication from StatFox.com but the policies of our clients may differ.

**Notification Of Changes**  
In the event that there is a change in the StatFox.com privacy practices, all members of StatFox.com will be contacted via the e-mail address on record.

**Contact Information**  
StatFox.com abides by the laws and guidelines of the Internet. We welcome any inquiries as to the use of collected information and cookies, product capabilities, or company methods and practices. If you have any questions, comments or corrections regarding personally identifiable information obtained through us, please [contact us](http://www.statfox.com/company/contact/) at anytime.
