Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all Starfall Education Apps, the website www.starfall.com (founded in September 2002 as a free public service), the website More.Starfall.com (opened in May 2009 and supported by members who pay a modest fee to join), and the website order.starfall.org (where teachers and parents can purchase books and educational materials at low cost).

IMPORTANT! Starfall does not collect personal information from children. As a general precaution, warn children never to give out their name, address, or other personal information on any website.

Information Collection and Use: We at Starfall respect your privacy and are committed to protect it. We do not require users to provide personal information to use and access our sites. The only time personal information is required is during membership registration on More.Starfall.com, for purchases at the Starfall Store on order.starfall.org, or on our contact/feedback pages. This information is not shared and is used only for the purpose for which it was entered.

Games and Books: When children play games or read books we do not ask for any personal information.

Cookies: We use cookies only to determine the number of unique users, help you navigate or set preferences, verify sign-in information on More.Starfall.com and the More Starfall app, and keep track of your order on order.starfall.org. We do not collect personal information through cookies and do not share cookies with third parties.

Access Log and IP address: Our web site has an access and error log to record site activity and analyze problems. The log contains the user's IP address which is kept private.

Social Media: We do not offer social networking (commercial or otherwise) or "chat room" access of any kind, in accordance with children's internet safety concerns.

Advertising: We have no external advertisements on Starfall apps or websites.

Disclosure of Information: Starfall may disclose user information if required in response to judicial process, or to law enforcement agencies or in connection with an investigation on a matter related to public safety.

Updates: This privacy policy may be updated in the future and posted here. If you are concerned, please check back occasionally.
