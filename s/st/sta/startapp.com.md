# Privacy Policy - StartApp

#### Privacy Policy

StartApp is a mobile advertising and data company that provides mobile app developers and mobile advertisers with a platform to deliver personalized, targeted advertisements to mobile consumers that download or interact with mobile applications that use StartApp services.

This privacy policy, **written in full below** , describes how StartApp collects, uses, shares, and secures the data provided by or collected from visitors to or users of the following:

  * Our website available at startapp.com, and any service or feature associated with our website
  * The advertising platform for our business partners available at: https://portal.startapp.com/#/signin;
  * StartApp’s app developer partners who implement the StartApp Software Developer Kit (“SDK”) within their mobile app for the purpose of displaying advertisements. Our technology enables our partners to show users more relevant advertising that is based on user interests and behaviors. As such, we collect and use certain data about user activity and preferences. More details are provided in the below policy.



StartApp recognizes the importance of your privacy and we are committed to treating any data we collect with care and respect. To this end, we have designed this Policy in a format that is easy to read so that you can clearly understand which data is collected, how we process, use and protect your data, as well as your choice and controls.

The below policy is an integrated part of StartApp’s Publisher Agreement available at: <https://www.startapp.com/policy/publisher-terms/> as well as StartApp’s Advertiser Agreement available at: <https://www.startapp.com/policy/advertiser-terms/>.

If you have any questions about this below policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at [support@startapp.com](mailto:support@startapp.com). Our team will be happy to assist you.

**__Types of Data We Collect__**

Depending on your interaction with our Services (meaning, if you are just browsing our Site, or if you have registered to our Services, or interact with an app, etc.), we may collect two types of data, as follows:

  * The  ** _first type  _**of data is non-identifiable data which may be made available or gathered via your use of the app or the Services (“ **Non-Personal Data** ”). We are not aware of the identity of our Site visitors, User or Business Partner from which the Non-Personal Data was collected. The Non-Personal Data which is being collected may include aggregated usage information and technical information transmitted by a device, including but not limited to: (i) certain software and hardware information (e.g., type of device); (ii) the session ID, the time and date you access the app or our Site, connect or disconnect to the Services, etc.; and (iii) browsing data such as: browser type, visited URL, etc. (all as detailed below).
  * The  ** _second type  _**is individually identifiable data, namely data that identifies an individual or may with reasonable effort identify an individual (“ **Personal Data** ”). Personal Data includes either: (a) information you voluntarily provide us, such as your email address and private password submitted upon registration and contact information such as your name, telephone number, address upon a submitting voluntarily request to contact us; or (b) certain identifiers such Internet Protocol (“ **IP** ”) addresses or Android ID, Advertising ID, IDFA or Device ID (“ **IDs** ”).  Note that, while IDs are considered as personally identifiable data in many jurisdictions (such as the EEA), there are some jurisdictions in which such data is not considered as Personal Data. We treat all IDs from various jurisdictions as Personal Data.



Please note that we may collect different categories of Personal and Non-Personal Data from you depending on the nature of your interaction with the Services, as detailed below. You are not required by law to voluntarily provide us with information. You can always avoid providing us certain Personal Data; however, you acknowledge that it may prevent us from providing you certain Services. In addition, we will always treat Non-Personal Data as Personal Data if we combine it.

**__Children’s Privacy__**

StartApp complies with applicable legislation related to children’s privacy protection, including the US Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (“ **COPPA** ”). Thus, we do not knowingly collect or solicit Personal Data from children, as they are defined under applicable law, and specifically: under the age of 13 years old, if they are located in the US; (ii) under the age of 16 years old, if they are located in the EU; (collectively: “ **child** ” or “ **children** ”), except as permitted under applicable law. Notwithstanding the above, in any event, we will not knowingly collect Personal Data from children for the purpose of behavioural advertising. If we later obtain actual knowledge that a User is considered as a child under applicable law, and Personal Data was collected (other than as permitted under applicable law) we will take steps to immediately delete its Personal Data. In addition, we request that children will not voluntarily provide us with Personal Data through the Services. If you become aware or have any reason to believe that a child has shared any Personal Data with us, please [contact us](mailto:Support@startapp.com) and we will take steps to ensure that such Personal Data is immediately deleted from our files, to the extent required under applicable law.
