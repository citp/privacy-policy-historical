# Privacy Policy - StartApp

#### Privacy Policy

#### __[Last Revised: July 10, 2017]__

StartApp Inc. (“ **we** ,” “ **us** ,” “ **our** ” or “ **StartApp** ”) is a mobile advertising and data company that provides its mobile application developers, advertisers and publishers (“ **Business Partners** ”) with an advertising platform for the purpose of delivering targeted and personalized advertising to individuals who download, use or otherwise interact with an App Developer’s app or our Services, as defined below (“ **User(s)** ”)(hereinafter the “ **Business Partners”** ** ** and “ **User(s)** ” shall be collectively defined as “ **you** ” or “ **yours** ”).

Our technology enables our Business Partners to show Users more relevant advertising that is based on Users’ interests and behaviors. In order to provide these Services, we collect and use certain information about Users’ activity and preferences, all as detailed in this Privacy Policy (“ **Policy** ”).

StartApp recognizes the importance of your privacy and we take this issue very seriously. To this end, we have designed the StartApp Privacy Policy in a format that is easy to read so that you can clearly understand our privacy practices. We are committed to treating any information we collect with care and respect.

**1\. T** **YPES OF INFORMATION WE COLLECT**

The  _ **first type**_  of information is non-identifiable information which may be made available or gathered via your use of the App Developer’s app or the Services (“ **Non-Personal Information** ”). We are not aware of the identity of the User or Business Partner from which the Non-Personal Information was collected.

The Non-Personal Information which is being collected may include your aggregated usage information and technical information transmitted by your device, including: (i) certain software and hardware information (i.e., operating system, type of device, etc.); (ii) the session ID, the time and date you access the mobile app or Site, connect or disconnect to the Services, etc.; (iii) browsing data such as: browser type, visited URL, etc.; (iv) your IP addresses or Android ID or Advertising ID, IDFA or Device ID. Please note, we consider this data to be Non-Personal Information, however, in some countries and jurisdictions this data might be considered as identifying and “Personal Information”.

The  _ **second type**_  of information is individually identifiable information, namely information that identifies an individual or may with reasonable effort identify an individual (“ **Personal Information** ”). Personal Information includes your email address and private password submitted upon registration and contact information such as your name, telephone number, address upon a submitting voluntarily request to contact us.

Please note that we may collect different categories of Personal and Non-Personal information from you depending on the nature of your interaction with the Services, as detailed below. If we combine Personal Information with Non-Personal Information, the combined information will be treated as  _ **Personal Information**_  for as long as it remains combined.

We may also collect, from you, someone else’s Personal Information, such as an email address, if you choose to do so through our Refer a Friend option available here <https://portal.startapp.com/#/pub/referral/friend-referral>, we will only use this information for the specific reason for which it is provided.

The StartApp’s SDK, which is integrated into each App Developer’s mobile app, is software that allows our Business Partners to provide you with personalized content, including advertisements. By downloading and using the app, Users grant the App Developer with permission to collect such device information and share it with StartApp pursuant to the acceptance of the App Developer’s privacy policy. StartApp may track this information over time and across multiple apps (and may also combine the device information with Non-Personal Information from other sources) in order to deliver targeted advertisements through the Services. This information we collect and transfer includes non-sensitive device information (collectively referred to as “ **Device Information** ”), such as:

  * Non-real time GPS location, points of interest or other geolocation data;
  * Bluetooth devices and your device’s sensors such as the accelerometer, etc.;
  * Carrier, ISPs;
  * Device properties such as: operating system, operating system version, device type;
  * Name of the App Developer’s mobile application through which a User interacts with the Services;
  * Language settings;
  * Network connection details and WiFi data;
  * Internet browser used to access the Services;
  * A query of your installed applications that does not identify them; and
  * IP Address and Advertising ID or Apple IDFA (as applicable) or Android ID (solely when the Advertising ID is not available). Please note, we consider this data to be Non-Personal Information, however, in some countries and jurisdictions this data might be considered as identifying and “Personal Information”
