# StampWorld.com

**Privacy Policy**

Stampworld.com collects information about the user, which solely is used to provide users with better experience and offers on the page. These include: Internet Protocol address, what type of browser being used (Firefox, Google Chrome, Internet Explorer), type of operating system (Windows or Mac OS). Type of device that is used to log on the site. (Such as iPad, iPhone, Blackberry).

 **Cookies and other technical aids to improve the user experience.**  
We use "cookies" or similar technologies, to make your online experience better. Cookies are small text files placed on your computer `s browser to store information. Cookies do not collect personal information.

Stampworld.com uses cookies to improve content, offers and advertising on our site. We use cookies to recognize you by name when you return to the side, so you will be automatically logged in. Cookies can also be used to avoid showing you advertisements that are not relevant for you..

We may also use cookies to manage and measure the effectiveness of ads displayed on Stampworld.com.

You can change your personal preferences regarding use of cookies and similar technologies by your browser. You can set your browser to accept cookies, block cookies, or require your consent before a cookie is placed on your browser. Blocking cookies will affect your experience and may prevent certain features offered on Stampworld.com. You can find more information under "Help" in your browser.

 **Personal information - cancellation of service.**  
Stampworld.com have the right to disclose personal information to third parties whose behavior is not covered by this statement. The user may unsubscribe this at any time.

In addition, we may transfer personal information if we are acquired by, sold to or merges with another entity.

If you do not wish to receive our emails, please follow the "unsubscribe" instructions contained in any email you receive from Stampworld.com.

Stampworld.com has adopted appropriate measures to guard and help prevent unauthorized access. as well as to maintain data security and ensure information collected online is used correctly..

Although we do take appropriate measures, we can not guarantee that personal information we collect will never be disclosed in a manner inconsistent with this statement.

If you have questions about this privacy statement, please contact us at privacy@stampworld.com.

From time to time, Stampworld.com may update this Statement of Privacy. We will notify you about significant changes by sending an email to the email address you most recently provided us or by a notice on our website.

This Privacy Policy was last updated in May 2012
