Privacy Policy

Here is the bottom line. Your privacy is very important to us. We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

Privacy Policy Overview

In general, you can browse the Stat Trek site without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself.

If you choose to use certain services, we may ask you to provide contact information, identity information, and/or billing information. You always have the option to not provide information by choosing not to use a particular service or feature.

How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information that you provide for three basic purposes.

  • To facilitate the services you request.
  • To inform you about on-line offers, products, services, and updates.
  • To process payment for fee-based services.

To maximize security and protect Users, financial transactions are handled over a secure connection by PayPal Bank. Stat Trek does not retain User financial information.

We offer you the ability to review and change the information you submit to us. Licensed users can change their User ID, password, email address, contact information, security information, and User preferences on the My Profile page. Subscribers to the Stat Trek newsletter can "unsubscribe" and purge their email address from our subscriber list at the Newsletter page.

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us.
