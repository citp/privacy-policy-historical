# Starfall Website Privacy Policy

Starfall is committed to protecting your privacy. No private information is required or requested to view our educational content.

When you fill out a form, the personal information you enter is used only to process your order or other request. Rest assured, all your information is kept private, we will never give out your email address and we will not bug you.

Our web servers create normal logs of web page accesses and the IP locations of users,but this information is kept private and only used to measure website use and improve content.
