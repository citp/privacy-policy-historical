# Starfield Technologies, Inc. - STARFIELD TECHNOLOGIES, INC. PRIVACY POLICY

Starfield Technologies, Inc. respects your privacy. We have created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to you, our customer. This privacy statement discloses the privacy practices for this site, www.starfieldtech.com.

I. What information is collected?

**Personal Information:**  
In order to make suggestions or request product or partnership information, we require personal information from you, including your name, address, phone number, and e-mail address. If you are enrolling for a Starfield SSL Certificate, we will use your personal information for authenticating your identify in order to create and issue to you a Certificate, and to provide the accompanying Certificate Service. We do not provide your personal information from the Certificate to any third parties that are not part of the Certificate Service. For more information on our SSL Certificates, please visit [Starfieldâ€™s Certificate Repository](https://web.archive.org/repository/).

**Clear Gifs (Web beacons/Web bugs)**  
Our site does not use clear gifs.

**Cookies**  
Our site does not use cookies, unless you are enrolling for one of our Starfield SSL Certificates, and then only for the limited purpose of establishing that you have been authenticated. It is a temporary cookie and does not include any personally identifiable information. The information is erased when you close your browser window. If you refuse to accept the cookie, you will not be able to apply for an SSL Certificate.

**Non-personal information:**  
We use information gathered about you from our site statistics (for example, your IP address) to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. We also gather broad demographic information from this data to help us improve our site and make your browsing and purchasing experience more enjoyable. **This information is not linked to any personally identifiable information.**

II. How information will be used.

**Resale and Disclosure of Information:**  
**We maintain a strict "No-Spam" policy. We will never give any information about you to others without your express permission. We do not rent our mailing list to third parties.** Our site uses forms in which you give us contact information (like your name, address, and e-mail address) so you can place orders, request information and support, and make product suggestions. We use your customer information only when it will be important for us to contact you regarding necessary correspondence regarding your product or service, functionality changes to our products, our Web site, new Starfield Technologies services, renewals, and special offers we think you'll find valuable. If you would rather not receive this information, you may opt out of it by following the instructions in Section III, below. 

**Information from Starfield SSL Certificates:**  
If you are applying for a Starfield SSL Certificate, we may need to compare the information in your Certificate application with information from third-party sources in order to authenticate your identity as part of the Certificate Service. We also have partners that assist us in certificate authentication that we may share your information with. Your personal information is protected by these third parties through confidentiality agreements we have with them. If those third parties have subcontractors or affiliates assisting them, they are required to enter into confidentiality agreements with them, and their actions are strictly limited to authenticating your identity.

The information you provide for the content of a Certificate will be "published" in the Certificate where it can be seen by third parties. The content of a Certificate generally includes your e-mail address and name, and may also include other information such as your public key, the expiration date of the public key, the name of Starfield as the Certificate Authority, the serial number of the digital ID, and the digital signature of Starfield.

**Information Disclosure Required by Law:**  
The only exceptions to our disclosure policy occur when:  
We are required by law, in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary in order to conform to the edicts of the law. **Or**  
We must comply with a legal process served on our Web site.

**E-mail:**  
We use e-mail messages to communicate with you, to confirm your placed orders, and to send information that you have requested. We also provide email links, as on our "About Starfield Technologies" page, to allow you to contact us directly. We strive to promptly reply to your messages.

The e-mail and/or postal mail you elect to receive may include offerings from trusted Starfield Technologies advertising partners. We never sell or share your personal information for these offerings.

The information you send to us may be stored and used to improve this site and our products, or it may be reviewed and discarded. However, your information will only be shared with third parties with your explicit permission. 

III. User choices in receiving and modifying information

As a customer of Starfield Technologies, you may alter or delete your customer information or opt out of receiving communications from our partners and us at any time, unless it is information that has been provided as content for a Starfield SSL Certificate. 

You have the choice of opting out of receiving promotional emails and mail, while still receiving non-promotional e-mails that deal with your account. To modify your information that is not contained within a Starfield SSL Certificate:

**How to Revoke Your Starfield SSL Certificate:**  
If you subscribe to our SSL Certificate Service and would like to revoke/deactivate your Certificate, please [click here](http://certificates.starfieldtech.com/). If you are using a Certificate on your Web site which is hosted by us, you will need to contact our customer service in order to get it revoked. Please be aware that after your Certificate has been revoked, your Certificate information will still appear in our repository with a revoked status. This is necessary in order to allow third parties that may be relying on it to check its status.

IV. Changes in our Practices

If we change our information handling practices or other privacy aspects, we will post those changes on this privacy statement, at least 30 days prior to their implementation.

V. Your Consent to this Policy

By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of your data by Starfield Technologies as specified above. Your visit and any dispute over privacy are subject to this Policy.
