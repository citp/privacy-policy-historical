Members shall part with their personal information at their risk and cost, and accordingly STAR HEALTH shall not be held liable for any loss of data and the pilferage of information. of referring such dispute to the arbitration of the sole arbitrator to be appointed by the Chairman of the owners of STAR HEALTH. The law applicable shall be Indian Law and the venue of the Arbitration shall be at Chennai, Tamilnadu, India. The award to be passed by the Arbitrator shall be final and binding on the parties herein. Only Courts in Chennai alone shall have exclusive jurisdiction to try all consequential proceedings if any that might be arising from the award.
