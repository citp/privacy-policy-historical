Crimson Publishing takes the privacy of customer data very seriously and is committed to protecting the privacy of the users of Crimson Publishing websites. The primary reason for collecting data is to provide you with the best online experience we can, and that is tailored to your needs. 

When do we collect data  
1) When you subscribe to the startups newsletter  
2) Via explicit data capture - this will include surveys and competitions  
3) Via implicit data capture - Aggregate data is collected via our web server and will include which pages you visited during a session and which banner ads you clicked on. 

Crimson Publishing will not provide your personal information or individual user history to any third party if you have expressed your desire for us not to do so (this includes but is not limited to advertisers and direct marketing companies). 

Crimson Publishing will not contact you for marketing purposes if you have expressed your desire for us not to do so. 

For further information concerning the UK Data Protection laws visit [_www.dataprotection.gov.uk_](http://www.dataprotection.gov.uk/)
