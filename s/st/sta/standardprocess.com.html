Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2/1/2014

Standard Process Inc. ("Standard Process") shares your concerns about privacy on the Internet. In order to better protect your privacy, Standard Process has provided this notice explaining its online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used on the Site. Furthermore, this Privacy Policy, being part of the Terms, works in conjunction with the Terms and Conditions of Use, but does not modify or supercede it. This Privacy Policy applies solely to the information that Standard Process collects on our Site and does not apply to information that Standard Process may collect by any alternative means or media.

Standard Process' Collection and Use of Your Information

In the course of using the Site, Standard Process may ask you for your name, e-mail address, postal address, telephone number, fax number, or favorite supplements as well as other relevant information. Credit card information you provide will be collected by Standard Process and its employees in order to process your order(s). Our agents, such as credit card authorization and processing service providers, may receive and store your credit card information for this purpose. Standard Process may also collect account registration information, including user name and password. Standard Process also may collect certain non-personally identifiable information when you visit many of our web pages such as the type of browser you are using, the type of operating system you are using, the domain name of your Internet service provider, aggregate data about the number of visits to the Site, and/or aggregate data about the pages visited. Information gathered through this Site may be transferred, used, and stored in the United States and in foreign jurisdictions in which we, our agents, customers, and other users of the Site are located, and by accessing this Site, you consent to the transfer, use and storage of your information in those jurisdictions.

How Standard Process Uses the Information

Standard Process uses the non-personally identifiable information ("Aggregate Data") to improve the design and content of the Site and to enable Standard Process to personalize your online experience. Standard Process may use Aggregate Data related to favorite supplements to provide you and other users of this Site with helpful product information. Standard Process may use the Aggregate Data to analyze Site usage as well as create services and/or products to fit your needs. Also, Standard Process may share this Aggregate Data with Standard Process' related entities, advertisers, partners, and/or other third party vendors. Standard Process shall own such Aggregate Data and may maintain copies of such as part of its records.

Except as otherwise indicated, Standard Process uses the personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") you provide about yourself: (a) to process and fulfill your order(s) for products; (b) to respond to any of your inquiries or questions about our products and/or services; (c) for internal marketing purposes; (d) to provide you additional and updated information, materials, and other advertisements regarding Standard Process' products and/or services; (e) to offer you other materials and/or information that Standard Process believes may be of interest to you; (f) to cooperate with government officials or parties in litigation under process of law, to prevent a crime, or as otherwise required by law; (g) to enforce or apply the Terms or protect the security or integrity of the Site; (h) to protect against a threat of safety or destruction of property; and/or (i) to protect against legal liability. Standard Process may share your Personal Information and product order information, including the items and quantities ordered by you, with our independent sales representatives. In all cases, however, Standard Process' agents, employees, and contractors who have access to Personal Information are required to protect this information in a manner that is consistent with this Privacy Policy. Furthermore, Standard Process encourages all its business partners to adopt and adhere to standards of protection consistent with this Privacy Policy. Standard Process will not use the Personal Information, except as set forth in this Privacy Policy, without your permission; provided, however, Standard Process shall own all such Personal Information and may maintain copies of such as part of its records. If you do not wish to receive marketing materials or advertisements regarding Standard Process products, please contact Standard Process in the manner identified in the "Opt-Out" section below. The Aggregate Data and Personal Information may be disclosed, transferred, assigned, licensed, distributed, and/or sold as part of Standard Process or its assets. In addition, if Standard Process becomes the subject of a bankruptcy or insolvency proceeding, whether voluntary or involuntary, Standard Process or its trustee in bankruptcy may disclose, sell, assign, license or otherwise transfer such information in a transaction approved by the appropriate court.

Children

This Site is not intended for children under the age of 13.  Standard Process does not knowingly or specifically collect personal information from users under 13 years of age.  In the event that Standard Process becomes aware that it has collected personal information from a user under 13 years of age, it will take steps to delete such information from its database. 

Opt-Out

You may choose not to receive marketing materials or advertisements regarding Standard Process products by simply notifying Standard Process directly at info@standardprocess.com or at 800-848-5061. Upon receipt and processing of an "opt out" request, Standard Process will, within its commercially reasonable amount of time, remove your information from any future unsolicited marketing communications and advertisements. Your request not to receive marketing materials or advertisements will not apply to information that you request to receive.

Security; No Liability

Standard Process has implemented security measures to help protect against the risk of loss, misuse and alteration of any information under Standard Process' control. Nevertheless, such security measures may not prevent all loss, misuse, or alteration of information on the Site, and Standard Process is not responsible for any damages or liabilities relating to any such security failures. You understand that data and communications, including e-mail and other electronic communications, may be accessed by unauthorized third parties when communicated over the Internet. Furthermore, the Site contains links to other, non-Standard Process websites. Standard Process is not responsible for nor can Standard Process ensure the privacy practices or content of such other sites and this Privacy Policy does not apply to those other websites.

IP Addresses and Cookies

Like other web sites, Standard Process uses your IP address to help diagnose problems with Standard Process' servers, to administer the Site, and for security purposes. "Cookies" are pieces of data sent from a web server through a web browser, which writes a file on your local PC. This file enables a web server to identify users that visit a site hosted by that server. Standard Process may use Cookies in order to identify you to our Site so that Standard Process can provide more customized information and services to you. In addition, our Site may contain advertisements that may contain Cookies. In general, if you choose, you can disable the use of Cookies by reviewing your browser's preferences and options. If you disable the use of Cookies, it will make some portions of our Site inoperable.

Modification of Privacy Policy

Standard Process may change this Privacy Policy at any time, consistent with the terms set forth in the Terms and Conditions of Use, by posting the changed or amended Privacy Policy on the Site. Your continued viewing of or use of this Site following the posting of any changes to this Privacy Policy will signify your assent to any such changes or amendments.

How to Contact Standard Process for Any Reason

You may contact Standard Process with questions or comments regarding this Privacy Policy, updating any Personal Information or anything else related to Standard Process and/or this Site at info@standardprocess.com or at 800-848-5061.
