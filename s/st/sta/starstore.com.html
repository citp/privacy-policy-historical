The Information We Collect
We collect visitor information on an aggregate basis to use to monitor and improve this site. For example, we keep a record of the pages downloaded from our site (as all web servers do). We do not share this information with anyone else.
We store the personal details you enter when buying goods. We use this information to process and deliver your order. We do not share this information with anyone else except for delivery requirements when we employ third parties, couriers to make deliveries and Royal Mail to send post. We may provide your telephone number to a courier should they need to contact you regarding your delivery.

If you choose to pay by credit or debit card, you will be asked to provide your card details on a secure form using 128-bit encryption. We only store credit card information while we need it to process an order - and then delete it from our system. Credit card details are not shared with anyone other than the card holders issuing bank or the authorised secure processor to obtain credit card authorisation. Credit card details are not held on the web site, and the customer has to re-enter for every transaction.

For our News Letter, we collect and store the email address you enter when you sign-up. We do not share this information with anyone else. When placing an order, we will send the visitor e-mails directly relating to the registration or order. You may opt in to our mailing list by ticking the ‘remember me’ option on the Order form. We may additionally send one e-mail to the customer inviting them to opt in to the mailing list. Customers may opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time.

We may use your information to administer any prize draws or competitions you may enter.

We may use your personal information in the prevention and detection of fraud.
We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to third parties. If we choose to do so in the future, you will be notified by email of our intentions, and have the right to be removed prior to the disclosure.

By using our website you consent to the collection and use of this information by us.
