# StaffKit Privacy Statement

### Shopping 

Shopping at our store is 100% safe! It is our top priority to make sure you can shop safely in our store. When you place an order, StaffKit protects your credit card number and personal information by encrypting it. Encryption scrambles your credit card number and personal information. Your information remains encrypted while traveling over the internet. Your credit card number is not kept or recorded by StaffKit or our computers - it is simply passed through to the credit card company to finalize the purchase. For secure commerce transactions StaffKit uses the industry standard secure server software (SSL), among the best software available today.

### Personal Information 

StaffKit collects basic contact information when you fill in a order form. This information will be used for administrative and identification purposes only and is not shared with our partners or third parties.

### Software 

The software distributed on our site doesn't collect any personal information. We don't and never will distribute spyware. 

### Server Log Files 

StaffKit uses IP addresses to administer the site, analyze trends, analyze site traffic, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information. 

If you have any questions about the security of our web site or about this privacy policy, please contact us.
