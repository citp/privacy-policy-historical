Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy explains the policy of Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc., Inc. ("Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc.") regarding the privacy of information supplied by users or collected by Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. from users of the Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. web site. Because Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. wants to build users trust and confidence in the Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. web site and demonstrate our commitment to privacy, we want to disclose our privacy practices.

MODIFICATIONS

Since this policy may change over time as we modify or expand our services, we suggest that you check back from time to time in order understand how we treat your information.

WHAT PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION IS COLLECTED

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart and to gather broad demographic information.

Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (such as name, address, email address and military status). Certain other information, such as demographic information (like age, sex, marital status, children, Internet usage, etc.) may be collected by Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. during the registration process. Not all information is required to be provided (required items will be clearly noted), but if you should choose to withhold any requested information, you may not be eligible to participate in or receive full benefit from all services on the web site.

HOW INFORMATION IS USED AND WITH WHOM IT IS SHARED

We use your personally identifiable information to enhance the Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. web site and to personalize your experience. We use customer contact information from the registration form to send the user information about our company and promotional material from some of our partners. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary and shared with other companies who may want to contact our visitors. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

We also may run contests on our site in which we ask visitors for contact information (like their email address), financial information (like their account or credit card numbers), and demographic information (like their zip code, age, or income level). We use contact data from our contests to send users information about our company and promotional material from some of our partners. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary and shared with other companies who may want to contact our visitors. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

Financial information that is collected from our users may be used to check a users' qualifications for registration and bill the user for products and services. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. This web site also supplements the information that you provide with information that is received from third parties.

We may also collect and report to third parties (such as content and service providers and advertisers) aggregated information from the Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. web site. For example, we may aggregate and report to third parties that X people purchased a particular product during a month, or that Y% of visitors to the Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. web site are between the ages 25-34.

COOKIES

"Cookies" are an industry standard practice of placing a file or identifying "marker" on a user's computer for identification and personalization purposes. Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart and to make sure you don't see the same ad repeatedly. We also use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests, to save passwords so you don't have to re-enter them each time you visit our site, and for other purposes. Our cookies do not have the capability of "stealing" information from your hard drive. You are not required to accept cookies. However, certain functionality of the Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. web site may not work properly (if at all) if you reject cookies.

THIRD PARTIES

This site contains links to other sites. Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices or the contents of such web sites. Furthermore, we use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information. Third parties may collect information about you when you view and/or click on their advertising, and we do not control the collection of this information.

PUBLIC FORUMS

This web site may contain chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups publicly available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

SECURITY

Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. uses certain efforts to safeguard the confidentiality of your personal information such as firewalls and authentication protection. Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. will periodically review its security policies and implement changes from time to time. However, we cannot and do not guarantee complete security, as it does not exist on the Internet.

CHILDREN

Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. is committed to the safety of children and does not intend to collect personally identifiable information from children under age 18. If you are under age 18, please ask your parent for permission before sending any information to our site or anyone else online. Have your parent to contact us as indicated below in order to provide us with prior written consent. Stars and Stripes Omnimedia, Inc. will not intentionally use personal information from or send marketing communications to children under age 13 without prior parental consent.
