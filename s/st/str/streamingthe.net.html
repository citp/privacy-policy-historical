Privacy Policy

Streaming The Net (STN) knows that you care about how your personal information is used and shared, and we take your privacy very seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy. By visiting the STN website, you are accepting the practices as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy covers STN's treatment of personal information that STN gathers when you are on the STN website and when you use STN services. This policy does not apply to the practices of third parties that STN does not own or control.


Sharing Your Information

STN will not rent nor sell your personal information to any third party.

Information Collected by STN

STN only collects personal information that is relevant to the purpose of our website, which is to enable users to discover and share information with one another. We collect the following types of information from our STN users

Information You Provide to Us

STN receives and stores information you enter on our website.

Registration: In order for you to use some STN services, such as submitting new media links to the website, you must complete a registration form. As part of this registration form, we require personal information (including your full name, e-mail address, year of birth, and country of residence).
- User Profile: STN may enable you to provide additional information, such as a bio, favorite URLs, and instant messaging IDs. In addition, you may choose to include photos of yourself in your profile.

Automatic Information

- STN receives and stores certain information whenever you interact with us. STN and its authorized agents automatically receive and record certain "traffic data" on their server logs from your browser including your IP address, STN cookie information, and the page you requested. STN uses this traffic data to help diagnose problems with its servers, analyze trends and administer the website.
- STN may collect and, on any page, display the total counts that page has been viewed.

E-mail Communications

STN may send out e-mails with STN-related news, products, offers, surveys or promotions. You may also receive notification e-mails from STN, which inform you of actions (e.g. friend requests) that have been performed on the site. If you do not want to receive e-mail from us, you will have the ability to opt-out by clicking on an unsubscribe link at the bottom of an email message. However, please note that in all cases you will continue to receive all system e-mails (e.g. those regarding forgotten user passwords) and legal notices (e.g. updates to our policies) from us.

Cookies

Cookies enable our systems to recognize your browser and tell us how and when pages in our website are visited and by how many people. STN cookies do not collect personal information, and we do not combine information collected through cookies with other personal information to tell us who you are or what your screen name or e-mail address is.
- Some of our business partners, like our advertisers, may use cookies on our website. We have no access to, or control over, these cookies.
For example, Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of these cookies enables them to serve ads to our users. Users may opt out of these ads by visiting the Google Privacy Center.
- Protection of STN and Others: We may release personal information to a legal jurisdiction if necessary to comply with a legal complaint or investigation.
- RSS and XML Feeds: STN allows for the RSS and XML syndication of all of its public content within the STN website.

Keeping Information Secure

- Your STN account information is protected by an encrypted password for your privacy and security. STN does not have access to your password, and therefore, if you lose your password you will need to reset it by following the instructions on the "Forgot Password" page. - You may request deletion of your STN account by e-mailing us at contact@streamingthe.net

Children Under 16 Years of Age

You must be 16 years and older to register to use the STN website. STN does not specifically collect information about children. If we learn that STN has collected information from a child under the age of 16, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

Changes to this Privacy Policy


STN may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time, at its sole discretion. Use of information we collect now is subject to the Privacy Policy in effect at the time such information is used. It is your responsibility to review our Privacy Policy from time to time to be kept up to date. If the Privacy Policy is ammended, the last updated date will be changed at the bottom of this document to reflect the last changed date.

Conditions of Use

If you decide to visit STN's website, your visit and any possible dispute over privacy is subject to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use, including limitations on damages, arbitration of disputes, and application of the law.

Effective Date of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective as of December 15, 2008 and was last updated on December 1, 2012.
