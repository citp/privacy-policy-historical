Privacy Policy

StyleTread values your privacy and takes great care protecting the information you provide us.

 

Collection of Personal Information

When you create a StyleTread account, the personal information we collect may include your:

  • a) Name
  • b) Delivery Address
  • c) Email Address
  • d) Telephone Number
  • e) Mobile Number
  • f) Date of Birth
  • g) Gender

 

The personal information we collect from you will be used in some or all of the following ways:

  • a) To deliver the products you have purchased via the StyleTread website
  • b) To communicate with you during the delivery process of the product and for customer support purposes
  • c) To provide you with relevant product information
  • d) To aid Styletread in the development of new products and services
  • e) To optimize the site and your shopping experience

 

When you become a registered user of the Styletread website, we'll also use your personal information to send you marketing or promotional information from time to time. You can opt out of receiving marketing information from StyleTread at anytime by using the unsubscribe function within the electronic marketing material or by changing the settings under your My Account section.

 

Updating Your Personal Information

You can update your personal information anytime by accessing your account via the StyleTread website.  Use your unique identifier and password to amend your account details.

 

Security of Your Personal Information 

We assure you that all information we collect will be safely and securely stored.

We protect your personal information by:

  • a) Restricting access to personal information
  • b) Maintaining technology products to prevent unauthorised computer access
  • c) Securely destroying your personal information when it's no longer needed for our record retention purposes

When purchasing from StyleTread, your financial details are passed through a secure server using the latest 128-bit SSL (secure sockets layer) encryption technology. 128-bit SSL encryption is approximated to take at least one trillion years to break, and is the industry standard. If you have any questions regarding our security policy, please contact us at service@styletread.com.au.

Disclosure of Personal Information

We value our customers and will not share your information with any other organisations other than related companies and those third parties directly related to the delivery of the products you have purchased from the StyleTread website.

There may be some exceptional circumstances for disclosing your personal information, such as when there are grounds to believe that the disclosure is necessary to prevent a threat to life or health, or for law enforcement purposes.

Styletread is committed to complying with the Privacy Act and the National Privacy principles.

 

Complaints About Breaches of Privacy

If you believe that your privacy has been breached by StyleTread, you can contact us at service@styletread.com.au and all attempts will be made to resolve the matter.
