# Style, Decor & More: PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy for Style, Decor & More.

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact me by email at styledecor1@gmail.com.

At www.styledecordeals.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by www.stylendecordeals.com/and how it is used.

**Privacy Statement**  
If and to the extent that you submit any personal data (such as your name and email address) to Style, Décor & More through this Blog, Style, Décor & More confirms that it will only use any such personal data for the purposes for which you have provided such data. No private information will be given or sold to anyone, including sponsored events such as giveaways, etc. However, if you submit personal data to any sellers or resellers with which you discovered on the Blog, I disclaim any liability and responsibility with respect to the use and security of information by sellers or resellers.

**Log Files**

Like many other Web sites, www.styledecordeals.com/ makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.  
  


**Cookies and Web Beacons**

www.stylendecordeals.com/ does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
