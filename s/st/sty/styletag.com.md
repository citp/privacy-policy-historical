### PRIVACY POLICY

Styletag is committed to respecting the privacy rights of visitors to its website. This privacy policy explains how we collect, store and use personal data and information (personal data) about you when you browse our website (the portal), shop with us or otherwise provide your personal data to us. It provides you with details about the types of personal data that we collect from you, how we use your personal data and the rights you have to control our use of your personal data. The personal data will be stored and processed in whole or in part in India or in any other part of the World. You can review, correct or change your personal data and opt out of any subscribed services or options at any time.

**COOKIES:**

This portal uses cookies to collect information about you. Cookies are small data files which are placed on your computer or other device by this portal and which collect certain personal data about you. This enables us to tailor our service offerings (including the portal) to provide you with products and services which are more relevant to your individual tastes.

We use both session ID cookies and persistent cookies. We use session cookies to store your secure sessions and browsing preferences and make it easier for you to navigate this portal. A session ID cookie expires when you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains on your hard drive for an extended period of time. You can remove persistent cookies by following directions provided in your Internet browsers help file. We set a persistent cookie to store your username and interests so you don't have to enter it more than once. Persistent cookies also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance their experience on this portal. You may change your browser settings to reject cookies, although please note that if you do this, it may impair or reduce the performance and/or functionality of this portal.

**SECURITY:**

We use Internet standard encryption technology ("SSL" or "Secure Socket Layer" technology) to encode personal data that you send to us when placing an order through the portal. To check that you are in a secure area of the portal before sending personal data to us, please look at the bottom right of your internet browser and check that it displays an image of a closed padlock or an unbroken key or such other display which your Browser provides to indicate that you are in a secure area. 

Whilst we take commercially reasonable and appropriate physical, electronic, managerial, technical and organizational security measures and procedures to safeguard personal data that you provide to us, for the protection of information against loss, misuse and alteration of the information under Styletag control and access to Styletag portal is restricted by the use of a Username and password and/or use of certified digital signatures and Styletag also utilizes a firewall to block unauthorised access to the Styletag system, Styletag cannot guarantee that unauthorized third parties will never be able to defeat those measures or use your personal data for improper purposes. You may appreciate that no data transmission over the Internet can ever be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Consequently, please note that we cannot guarantee the security of any personal data that you transfer over the Internet to us. 

If you are using a computer or terminal in a public location, we recommend that you always log out and close the internet browser when you complete an online session for your security. In addition we recommend that you take the following security measures to enhance your online safety:

\- keep your account passwords private. Remember, anybody who knows your password may access your account.  
\- when creating a password, use at least 8 characters (recommended). A combination of letters and numbers is best. Do not use dictionary words, your name, email address, or other personal data that can be easily obtained. We also recommend that you frequently change your password. You can do this by going to "[My account](https://web.archive.org/account)" section.  
\- avoid using the same password for multiple online accounts.
