# Privacy Policy on StyleCaster

_October, 2008_

## Welcome to StyleCaster.

We hope to create a community of creative, stylish people who trust one another and the community. We know that you care about how your personal information is handled and protected. This Privacy Policy covers the Company's treatment of your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before submitting any personal or other information to the StyleCaster.com website (the " **Site** "). By accessing, using or posting information to the Site, you consent to this Privacy Policy and the terms and practices described in this policy.

In order to register with or use the Site, you must be 14 years of age or older. By registering, you represent that you are over 14 and that all information you provide in the registration form is complete and accurate. The Company does not solicit information from and will not knowingly collect personal information from anyone we actually know is under the age of 14. We recommend that minors between the ages of 14 and 18 ask and receive their parent's or guardian's permission before sending information about themselves or anyone else through the Site or over the Internet.

**We reserve the right to change the provisions of this Privacy Policy at any time. If we make changes to the Privacy Policy, we will notify you of any material changes by posting a notice on our home page or sending you an email at the address you provide in your profile. Your use of the Site following the posting of such changes or revised statement shall constitute your acceptance of any such changes.** We encourage you to review our Privacy Policy whenever you visit our Site to make sure that you understand how any personal information you provide will be used.

## What Personal Information Do We Collect? How Do We Use Information?

### Information You Give Us.

When you register for the Site, we will collect certain information from you. This information may include your name, your hometown (city/state), date of birth, gender, occupation or employer and your email address. From time to time, we may also gather information from you in response to survey questions, polls, contests and other interactive features through the Site. You can choose not to provide certain information, but, in such event you may not be able to use all of the functions of the Site or take advantage of the full capabilities and personalization of the Site.

To allow you to introduce yourself to the community and express yourself on the Site and to the Sociocast Network, we may enable you to provide additional information to your Stylecaster profile ("Profile"), such as a bio, favorite links, upload photos, preferences and other information and data about yourself and your opinions. Certain of your Profile information may be searched by third party search engines and available to all visitors of the Site. You may also be able to "privatize" certain User Content (as defined in the Terms and Conditions of Use) and Profile information and share such information only with your friends and community. However, please note that no security measures are failsafe and you post all User Content at your own risk.

The Company will store any information which you enter on the Site and will use your information in order to improve the Site and Sociocast Network and your experience on the Site and within the Sociocast Network and to customize the content and advertisements that you may encounter on the Site and within the Sociocast Network. If you purchase merchandise or services through the Site, such purchases may be updated to your Profile and shared with your community on the Site and within the Sociocast Network.

You may provide us information regarding a friend, such as an email address, so that you can tell your friend about the Site and invite your friend to join the Site and the Sociocast Network. If you provide such information to us, we will use this information only to invite your friend to visit the Site and track the success of our referral and friend programs.

### Information We Collect.

When you visit our Site, we will automatically collect and store certain information, such as your computer's Internet Protocol (IP) address, your computer's operating system, the browser type, the address of the referring Site if you linked to our Site and your activity on the Site. We use this information for purposes such as analyzing your use of the Site and your interests, discovering user trends, tracking user movement on the Site and within the Sociocast Network, administering the Site, improving customer service, diagnosing problems with our servers and gathering broad demographic information for aggregate use.

We may provide aggregate, nonpersonal and statistical data about our users and use of our Site to third parties from time to time for such purposes as we may deem appropriate.

We may also automatically collect certain information through the use of "cookies." Cookies are small data files that are stored on a user's hard drive at the request of a Web site to enable the site to recognize users who have previously visited them and retain certain information such as customer preferences and history. You can block, erase or be warned of cookies (check the instructions for your browser), but if you do not allow our cookies you may not be able to register, use or access certain Site features or Service. If you have registered for the Site, we may link certain automatically collected information with your personal information in order to provide you your preferred content and to customize your experience on the Site and within the Sociocast Network.

### Email

We will not provide your email address to any unaffiliated third parties without your permission, but we may use third party service providers who are engaged by or working with us in connection with operation of the Site ("Service Providers"). Such Service Providers may have access to your email address and other information to provide the services. For example, we may use a third party Service Provider to update and administer our mailing list(s) and to send you information regarding the Site. We require that such Service Providers use such information solely for the purposes of providing services to us.

We may send out e-mails with news, products, offers, surveys or promotions. You may also receive notification e-mails from us which inform you of actions (e.g. friend requests, community updates) that have been performed on the Site. If you do not want to receive e-mail from us, you will be able to opt-out of certain emails; however, please note that in all cases you will continue to receive all system e-mails (e.g. those regarding forgotten user passwords) and legal notices (e.g. updates to our policies) from us.

## How Do We Share Your Information?

As part of a new social network - the Sociocast Network - you agree that the sharing of information among people within the Sociocast Network is an important part of the community. Accordingly, you agree that we may share and disclose your information, including personal information provided by you and information gathered by us, to Sociocast Network, LLC and other operators of the sites within the Sociocast Network in order to provide to you a customized personal experience on the Site and within the Sociocast Network. However, we will not rent or sell your personal information to any unaffiliated third parties for marketing purposes and we will share your personal information only as described in this policy.

We may aggregate and provide nonpersonal information to third parties from time to time without your authorization. In addition, we may share personal information with our Service Providers who need access to such information to carry out their work for us as described above. We require that our Service Providers use any personal information we provide only to carry out their work for us.

We may customize the content and advertising you receive on the Site, or work with Service Providers to do so, and your personal and profile information as well as information we collect regarding your use of the Site, may be shared with such Service Providers for this purpose. We may also combine information collected from you or as a result of your use of the Site with information about you from other sites in the Sociocast Network in order to provide you with a more personalized experience.

We also may disclose personal information (i) when we are required to or we believe it is appropriate to comply with the law (e.g., a lawful subpoena, warrant or court order) or at the request of law enforcement or government representatives; (ii) to enforce or apply this Privacy Policy or our other policies or agreements; (iii) to protect the rights, property or safety of the Company, our employees, our users or any other person; or (iv) to investigate and protect against fraudulent, abusive, or unlawful use of our Site or our Service. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction and investigating or protecting against intellectual property infringement or misrepresentations of the identity of a user. Further, if the Company is acquired by a third party, your personal information will be transferred and disclosed as part of the acquisition.

## Third Party Sites.

Our Site may contain links to other sites, including sites in the Sociocast Network. Although sites within the Sociocast Network are required to comply with the Sociocast Network privacy policies permitting sharing of user information and data to all sites within the Sociocast Network, you should carefully review the privacy policies and other terms for all third party sites prior to using such sites. This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected by us and how we use and share such information. Our Privacy Policy does not apply to any personal information which you may provide on or through any third party sites, including Retailers. We are not responsible for and do not control, monitor or endorse the privacy policies or practices of such third party sites.

## How Do We Protect Information?

Your StyleCaster account information is protected by a password for your privacy and security. You should take steps to protect against unauthorized access to your password and to your computer including by logging off once you have finished using a shared computer.

We will use reasonable security measures to protect against any unauthorized access to and disclosure of your personally identifiable information. However, please note that we cannot guarantee its absolute security. You should also note that email is not secure, and you should not send any confidential or sensitive information to us via an unsecured email. If you have any questions regarding security on our website, please contact [info@stylecaster.com](mailto:info@stylecaster.com).

## How You Can Control Your Information.

You can update, correct, amend or delete information on the Site through the Your Profile pages. When you update or correct Your Profile, all changes will be automatically provided to the other sites in the Sociocast Network. We may maintain a copy of the unrevised information in our records.

You can terminate your account and request deletion of your personal information by emailing us at [legal@stylecaster.com](mailto:legal@stylecaster.com).

You will be able to specify your personal notification preferences and will be able to opt-out of certain email and text messages and notifications for announcements, reminders, bulletin board postings and comments in your Profile.

## Company Information

The Site is solely owned and operated by StyleCaster Media Group, LLC (the "Company" or "We" or similar language) and hosted on servers located in the United States. If you register on the Site, you are agreeing to have your personal information transferred to and processed in the United States.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [legal@stylecaster.com](mailto:legal@stylecaster.com).
