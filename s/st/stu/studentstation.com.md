Privacy Statement

**student** station **** / ******PRIVACY POLICY  
** \---------------------------------------------------------------- **  
 **

studentstation.com is committed to ensuring your privacy. 

studentstation agrees to notify you of: 

  1. What individually identifiable information of yours or third party individually identifiable information is collected from you through the web site
  2. The organization collecting information
  3. With whom information may be shared 
  4. How information is used
  5. What options are available to you regarding collection, distribution and use of the information
  6. The types of security procedures that are in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of information under studentstation.s control
  7. How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information. 



If you feel that this site is not abiding by its posted privacy policy, please contact our Department of Privacy by emailing  _[ privacy@studentstation.com](mailto:privacy@studentstation.com)_.
