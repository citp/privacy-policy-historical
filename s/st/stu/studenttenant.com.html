Jakuta Limited Privacy Policy

 

Jakuta Limited (“Jakuta”, “we”) is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. This privacy policy (“Policy”) explains how Jakuta uses the information we collect about you via this website (“Website”). In the event that you wish to limit the use of that information, the procedures below explain how this can be done.

 

Your privacy is important to us and therefore we make every effort to safeguard it through the implementation of this Policy. We will only use the information you provide to us for the following:

 

  • To provide you with the services offered by Jakuta;
  • To help us improve the services offered by Jakuta; and
  • To answer any enquiry you may have.

 

We will not share your information with any third party except to the extent necessary to answer your enquiry or in accordance with the terms and conditions, in the provision of our services. We have a policy whereby we retain e-mail addresses to answer any e-mail enquiries we receive. Any e-mail addresses that we receive shall not under any circumstances be used for any purpose other than to contact you in connection with the provision of the services by Jakuta and to inform you of any new services we may offer from time to time. You will be given the option to opt-in when subscribing to any services should you wish to be informed of new services offered by Jakuta. Subsequently, if you wish to stop receiving such information from us you will have the option to opt-out, by sending an e-mail to us at info@jakuta.co.uk  with ‘opt-out’ written in the subject box. In addition, Any emails sent from this Website will contain an unsubscribe link which is a one click unsubscribe process. Subject to our terms and conditions, your e-mail address will never be shared with third parties.

 

By submitting your information you consent to the use of that information as set out in this Policy.

 

Information collected by the Website may include:

 

  • Any personal details you provide to us through the registration process;
  • Your IP Address. This is a string of numbers unique to your computer that is recorded by our web server when you request any page or component on the Website. This information is used to monitor your usage of the Website.

 

Any personal information we collect from the Website will be used in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998 and other applicable laws. The details we collect will be used:

 

  • To process your request for the provision of services by Jakuta. We will make every effort to ensure that your details remain confidential.
  • In certain cases we may use your e-mail address to send you information on other products and services (such as internet shops or marketing campaigns). In such cases, you will be offered the option to opt-in or opt-out.

 

By accepting Jakuta’s Website terms and conditions, you have given your explicit consent to the processing of your personal data by Jakuta.

 

The form of cookie that will be used on this Website, as follows:

 

A session cookie and is only used during your visit to the Website. A Cookie will be stored on your computer as a small file and you would not physically be able to remove it from your computer when exiting the Website.   It is only used to maintain continuity during your interaction with the Website and as soon as the browser is closed the session cookie may be made to expire. You may limit the cookie to a single session and the cookie made to expire.


This Policy only covers this Website. Any other websites which may be linked to our Website are subject to their own policy, which may differ from Jakuta’s. You have the right to request a copy of any information that we currently hold about you. In order to receive such information, please e-mail us your contact details including address. There will be a charge of £10.00 to cover administration costs.

 

Various security procedures have been installed as set out in this Policy. To prevent unauthorised access to your information we have put in place appropriate electronic, physical, and managerial procedures to preserve and make safe the information we collect online. If any of the information that you have provided to Jakuta changes, (such as your e-mail address, your home address, or personal information) or you wish to be removed from our mailing list, please send an e-mail to info@jakuta.co.uk

 

You personal information will be held on our secure servers and at the address above.

 

Data Privacy Practice is constantly evolving and we therefore reserve the right to revise this Policy at any time. Your use of this Website constitutes an acceptance of the terms of this Policy as revised from time to time and appearing on this page of this Website.
