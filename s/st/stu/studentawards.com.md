# StudentAwards.com - Privacy Policy

|   
---  
|   
| Protecting our users’ privacy is of utmost importance to studentawards.com. The information collected is used primarily to match users with awards from our database for which they are eligible. User information is also used to customize our web page content for individual users, and to notify users, via email, about new awards which match their profile. Only with the user’s permission, contact information (name and mailing address only) is shared with established companies who provide products and services to post-secondary students.
