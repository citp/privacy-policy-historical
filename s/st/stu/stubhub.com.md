# Privacy Policy

Your privacy and security are a major concern for. Our service consists of a series of surefire methods to ensure you have the safest and most reliable experience possible. Please review this policy to see how we protect our fellow event enthusiasts.

We use Industry standard 128 bit SSL encryption technology to protect your credit card information so that it cannot be read as the information travels over the Internet. This makes it virtually impossible for your information to be stolen or intercepted while being transferred over the Internet. Once we receive your credit card information, it is stored securely in our firewall protected data center and will not be provided to anyone at anytime.

We will not provide your credit card information to anyone at anytime. Upon your initial registration you were provided with the choice to receive additional information and materials. If you opted to receive information, we reserve the right to mail and/or email the related information that you requested. We also reserve the right to provide professional sports teams with general demographic information regarding users who have purchased their tickets. The professional sports teams are not allowed to market to users who have not opted to receive additional information.
