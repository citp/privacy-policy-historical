# Privacy Policy | STUDYANDEXAM.COM

We understand the importance of our viewers' privacy. We ensure to protect the privacy of our viewers. This page delineates about the types of information collected on this website from the users; and that how the collected information is used. By using this website, you agree to our privacy policy and cookies policies.

**Cookies Policy:** A cookie is a small piece of data (text file) that a website - when visited by a user - asks your browser to store on your device in order to remember information about you, such as user's language preferences, and other such type of user-specific information on the pages the users access. The cookies are set by us and called first party cookies. We also use third party cookies - which are cookies from a domain different than the domain of the website you are visiting – for our advertising and marketing efforts.

More specifically, we use cookies for following purposes:

  * Assisting you in navigation;
  * Analyzing your use of our products or services;
  * Assisting with our promotional and marketing efforts;
  * to customize webpage content according to user's preference
  * and to improve your user's experience on the website.



These cookies are necessary for the website to function. You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of the site may not work then. The cookie data is safely and securely stored for improving our website according to the expectations of our viewers. Your privacy is protected by restricting access to these data collected in log files.

**Third-Party Cookies:** This site uses cookies from third-party services such as **Google Adsense** and **Google Analytics**. 

**Google Adsense and Double Click Cookie:**

  1. Google, a third party vender, uses cookies to serve ads on this website.
  2. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to viewers based on their visit to this website and other websites. 
  3. User may opt out the use of the DART cookie by visiting Google ad and content network privacy policy at the URL: _<http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html>_



Our advertising partner use cookies and web beacons on this site. These third-party ad-server or networks use technology to the advertisements and links appear on this website send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies such as cookies, JavaScript or Web Beacons may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness their advertisement and /or to personalize their advertising content. 

**Google Analytics:** Google analytic code is used in each page of the website to determine the statistics and demographics of the users to refine their experience on the website and adjust the contents according to user expectations depending on their statistical considerations. The data collected by Google Analytic include IP address, type of browser, time, date and number of clicks by viewers. This data helps us determine statistics, demographics and viewer’s movement around the website. The information stored in Log File, such as demographics, IP address or other such information, is not associated to any information that is personally identifiable. 

**Contact Us Form:** A 'contact form' on the website for viewers to contact the owner of the website, if they need some information or want to give a feedback regarding the website. The contact form asks the name and email address of the user to use the contact form. Any information given to us by using the contact form is kept strictly confidential and these information are destroyed completely when they are no longer needed for retention.

**Third-party cookies:** As mentioned above, the website uses Google analytics to understand statistics of the users and Google adsense to publish advertisement on the website, these are third-party cookies, you may consult the respective privacy policies of the third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instruction regarding how to opt-out of certain practices This website has no control over cookies used by the third-party network and this privacy policy does not apply to them. 

**Facebook page link:** A link to our 'Facebook Page' is given on some pages of the website. It is not a facebook plugin but a simple link which, when clicked, leads to external link of the facebook page of the website. 

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so by using the options given in your browser. 

If you need more information about the use of cookies on this website, feel free to contact us using our 'contact form'

**Thank You!**
