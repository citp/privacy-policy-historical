# Synergent: Privacy Policy

|  |  |  |   
---  
| 

|  |  |  The privacy of our website visitors is important to us, and, therefore, we have adopted the following privacy policy.

If you have any questions and/or 

comments about this policy, please contact [Synergent](http://www.synergentcorp.com/right.php/pid/24/sid/38).

1\. Credit Union Information Areas  
For staff use only, Synergent collects and stores information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, user ids and passwords of insitutions who register to access the "credit union-only" areas of the website. This information is used to improve the quality of website information and services to credit unions.

2\. General Information Areas  
To track website use, measure site traffic, and improve site navigation and information, Synergent collects domain names, dates and times of website visits.

3\. Online Registration  
This information is never sold, given or disclosed to third parties.

4\. Email  
Synergent may use feedback information from emails sent to the League to respond to inquiries, comments or suggestions. This information is never sold, given or disclosed to third parties.
