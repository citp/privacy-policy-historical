# Synopsys Privacy Policy

| 

### Synopsys Privacy Policy

**Respecting Your Privacy**
    While you are visiting [www.synopsys.com](http://www.synopsys.com/), we want you to know that we respect your privacy and recognize your need to protect your personal information. On occasion, we may request certain personal information from you, like your name, mailing address, job title and e-mail address. You may choose whether or not you wish to provide us with personal information. In some cases, failure to provide personal information will prevent you from accessing certain Synopsys information, services, options or promotional offers. Please do not provide Synopsys with any personal information if you are under 18 years of age.
**Use of Your Information**
    We will only use your personal information within Synopsys. In the future, you may be able to "opt out" of certain uses of your personal information as well as review or request changes to your personal information. Your personal information may be shared with Synopsys business locations throughout the world, but we will not share it with anyone else without your permission. We may also track the Internet addresses of our visitors and analyze this data for trends and statistics, however this information alone will not allow us to identify who you are.
**Use of Cookies**
    Some Synopsys web pages use "cookies," which are small files saved on your computer. Cookies help us recognize your user preferences and can improve your future web visits, but they do not allow us to identify who you are. You can set your web browser to notify you when you are receiving a cookie and choose to reject it. However, rejecting cookies may effect the performance of the Synopsys web site and prevent you from accessing certain Synopsys information.
**Conclusion**
    Synopsys is committed to privacy and shall update this policy from time to time to keep it current. If you have any questions or comments about Synopsys privacy practices, please contact Synopsys via electronic mail at [privacy@synopsys.com](mailto:privacy@synopsys.com).
