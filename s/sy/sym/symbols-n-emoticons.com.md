# Privacy Policy | Symbols & Emoticons

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services that may be of interest to you. The IP addresses we collect are not personally identifiable and do not contain personal details about you beyond basic demographic information.

 

Our advertising partners include Google Adsense which may employ cookies on our site. Google uses cookies to serve ads. You may wish to visit Google Adsense for additional information about their privacy policies as well.

 

Any user may opt out of Google's cookies by visiting: http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

 

Users can also disable cookies by navigating their individual browser options.
