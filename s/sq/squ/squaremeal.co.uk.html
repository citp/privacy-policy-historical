Square Meal's Privacy Policy

(menu)
  1. This Privacy Policy describes how and why we obtain, store and process data which can identify you. We are committed to safeguarding the privacy of our users whilst we provide the personalised and valuable services (the "Services") via the website located at www.squaremeal.co.uk (the "Site"). We may change this Privacy Policy at any time and such changes will take effect from the date and time the revised Privacy Policy is available on the Site and your continued use of the Site shall constitute acceptance. All personal information is processed by us in accordance with data protection legislation applicable in the UK.
  2. From time to time, we may ask you to provide some or all of the following: details of your name, company name, address, telephone/facsimile number(s), e-mail address and credit card details. This personal information will be stored together with any additional information you may provide to us from time to time, and will be used to provide you with the Services and services associated with them. We may also use your personal information:-
    • To analyse use of the Site; and
    • To inform you of other services, provided by SquareMeal, our subsidiaries and/or carefully-selected third parties, which we think may be of interest to you. If you do not want to receive this information, update your profile information.
  3. We will only keep your personal information for as long as is necessary for the purposes for which that personal information was given to us.
  4. We may use 'cookies' while you access the Site to avoid the need to re-enter details on different occasions. If you do not wish to receive cookies, you may change the settings on your computer or browser accordingly.
  5. Please note that, from time to time, we may transfer your personal data for internal business purposes to locations outside the European Economic Area, some of which may have different data protection laws to the UK, or no data protection laws. Note that in respect of information transmitted to the US, we shall only transmit such information to entities that comply with suitable policies.
  6. If you have any queries about this Privacy Policy or if you have registered with Squaremeal.co.uk and you wish to make changes to your personal information at any time, click on the "My Profile" link to the right of the Quick Search facility in the yellow bar or contact us at info@squaremeal.co.uk.
