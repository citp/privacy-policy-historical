# Sqip -

**Privacy Policy Statement**

This is the web site of SqipCom Ltd.

  
Our postal address is:  
95 Wilton Rd. Suite 3. London SW1V 1BZ. UK

We can be reached via e-mail: support@sqipcom.com

or you can reach our answering service telephone at +44 (845) 527 7481

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address.

We collect no information on consumers who browse our Web pages.

With respect to cookies: A cookie is placed on your computer when visiting the Sqip website. This cookie, which has a duration of 60 days, is used to track referral signups. 

**Privacy for consumers who have joined Sqip.**

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail at the above address or login and send a support ticket.

We never provide your contact information to other companies.

With respect to Ad Servers: We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies.  

We provide instant access for members to all information they have entered into our database. 

Members can have information corrected by sending us e-mail at the above address or self edit the information where applicable.

With respect to security: When we transfer and receive certain types of  information such as financial information, we redirect members to a secure server. We have appropriate security measures to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that the member has provided.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above addresses or phone number.
