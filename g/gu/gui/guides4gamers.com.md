# Privacy policy

**Thank you for visiting this website, a digital service presented to you by Guides for Gamers. Your privacy is important to us. As such, we provide this privacy policy explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used by guides4gamers.com website.**

In common with other websites, log files are stored on the web server saving anonymous information reported by your browser each time you access a web page such as the visitor's IP address, browser type, referring page, landing page, time of visit and other such information.

Cookies may be used to remember non-personal visitor preferences when interacting with the website.

Cookies are small digital signature files that are stored by your web browser that allow your preferences to be recorded when visiting the website. Also they may be used to track your return visits to the website.

To create an account for the Guides for Gamers features, we require you to provide your email address and a nickname (it is optional).

Third party advertising / tools companies may also use cookies for tracking purposes.

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads.

If you want to know how Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services or how ad personalization works or how you can control the information collected by Google then you should visit [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites) web page.

We use Google Analytics tools to help us measure traffic and usage trends for our website. These tools collect information sent by your browser as part of a web page request, including the web pages you visit, your browser add-ons, your browser's screen dimensions, and other information that assists us in improving our service.

We collect and use this analytics information in aggregate form such that it cannot reasonably be manipulated to identify any particular individual user.

This website uses Facebook Comments and Like / Share Buttons. This allows users to post comments or share pages and content from the site to their Facebook profiles. These Facebook services can also collect and store data.

We use Cloudflare solutions to help us improve website performance through their global CDN and web optimization features. Cloudflare services can also collect and store some data. The information that Cloudflare, Inc. gathers, how they use that information, and the options you have to access, correct, or delete such information can be found on the [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) web page.

The information is used to enhance the vistor's experience when using the website to display personalised content and advertising.

Most web browsers are set up to accept cookies. You may be able to set your browser to warn you before accepting certain cookies or to refuse certain cookies.

However, if you disable the use of cookies in your web browser, some features of the website may be difficult to use or inoperable.

For more information on how to disable cookies, please see the Help section in your browser (or the settings function on your mobile device) or visit www.aboutcookies.org. For further information on managing behavioral advertising, please visit www.youronlinechoices.com

You can also change your cookies settings at any time by clicking on the "Cookie Settings" link located in the page footer.
