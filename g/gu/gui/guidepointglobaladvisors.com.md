# GUIDEPOINT GLOBAL ADVISORS - Privacy Policy

    

This privacy policy explains how Guidepoint Global ("Guidepoint," "we" and "us") obtains, utilizes, shares and safeguards information collected from Advisors and visitors to its websites, including without limitation www.guidepointglobal.com and www.guidepointglobaladvisors.com (each, a "Website," collectively, the "Websites"). 

By visiting our Websites or by becoming a Guidepoint Advisor, you consent to the collection, use and transfer of your information pursuant to the terms of this privacy policy. 

Guidepoint collects information that you choose to provide to us on the Websites, through email, over the phone and otherwise, including your name, contact information, employment and biographical information and feedback concerning your experience as an Advisor. We may also obtain information from third parties about you. In addition, Guidepoint's Websites may automatically record information when you visit. This information can include your Internet Protocol address and data concerning webpage requests. 

Guidepoint may place small files called "cookies" on your computer when you visit the Websites. Cookies facilitate your utilization of the Websites and enable us to track activity and customize your website experience. If you do not wish to receive a cookie from Guidepoint Global, you may set your Internet browser settings to decline or disable the cookie. 

We utilize information collected from you to match you with consulting opportunities, notify you of Guidepoint Global developments, process your invoices and administer honoraria, and develop, support and improve our business. 

We do not or sell to or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing or solicitation purposes. Guidepoint and trusted third parties utilize personal information only for the purposes for which it was obtained. 

Personal information provided to Guidepoint will be held by us inside the United States and will be accessed by our staff and third parties working for us for the purposes set forth in this policy or for other purposes subject to your approval. Your personal information may be also held, processed and accessed inside the European Economic Area. 

Guidepoint may share your information with our clients, typically in connection with consulting opportunities. Guidepoint may disclose the fact that you are an Advisor and information collected about you in response to a valid order or request by a court or other governmental body, to enforce an agreement between you and Guidepoint or one of Guidepoint’s clients, to protect Guidepoint’s rights, or as otherwise required by law, legal process or for the prevention or detection of a crime.
