Privacy Policy

PERSONAL DATA & COOKIES

Last modified: October 2014

The guidelines below detail the data protection policy of the company GUERLAIN (hereinafter GUERLAIN) with regard to personal data provided on its website https://www.guerlain.com.

GUERLAIN respects your concerns regarding the confidentiality of personal information and data provided by you. By providing your personal information and data, you consent to the use, analysis and, where applicable, disclosure of such information by us for purposes associated in particular with your requests for our newsletters and information about the company, its activities as well marketing information. Consequently, we encourage you to read this policy carefully. This Data Protection policy applies to all personal data provided by you to GUERLAIN.


1. General information

GUERLAIN may collect personal data via its website from users subscribing to our newsletter, or creating an account or when ordering products on the website.

Any personal information you send GUERLAIN for the purposes of certain services is subject to the provisions of Data Protection Act no. 78-17 of 6 January 1978.


2. Right to access, correct and delete data and to object to data processing

You are entitled to access, correct and delete personal data which relates to you, and to object to the processing of such data. You may exercise this right at any time by writing to the following address:

3. Management of personal data by the company

3.1 Purpose of data collection

The Company collects your personal data via its website for the following purposes only:

- Managing subscriptions to our newsletter (subscribe/unsubscribe procedures),
- Sending information about the company and its activities as well as, subject to your consent, publicity or marketing information,
- Managing of your wish list.

Your personal data (mostly first name, surname, address, e-mail) that we collect to ensure these purposes and which are essential to respond to your requests are mentioned by an asterisk on the collect forms. If you dont fill in these fields, we will not be able to respond to your requests.

3.2 Retention of data

Data is retained for a period appropriate to the purposes for which it was collected or as long as required or permitted by law.

3.3 Data recipients

GUERLAIN is the only recipient of the data.

GUERLAIN will only send your personal data to a third party if:
- you have given us your prior approval to do so or to provide the service you have requested;
- GUERLAIN is required by a judicial authority or any other administrative authority to provide it with such information.

For the purposes of technical management of the storage of your personal data, your data will be stored on a centralized basis on servers hosted by our provider, based in France.

3.4 Data security and privacy

GUERLAIN has made every effort to put in place suitable precautions to safeguard the security and privacy of personal data, and to prevent it from being altered, corrupted, destroyed or accessed by unauthorized third parties.

The technical and organizational security measures implemented, in particular with regard to information systems, are compliant with the state of the art.

However, GUERLAIN does not control each and every risk related to the use of the Internet, and therefore warns visitors of the potential risks involved in the functioning and use of the Internet.

4. "Send to a friend" functionality

You can send your Wish List to your friends. You will be asked to complete the following fields: names and email address. We will also ask you to provide the name and email address of your friend, to enable us to send them the information. We only collect this data for the purpose of sending your email. Recipient data is not saved or recorded.

5. Cookies

1. What is a cookie?

A cookie is a text file which may be registered in a dedicated space on your terminals* hard disk when you consult an online service using your browsing software. A cookie file allows its issuer to identify the terminal on which it was registered, during the term of validity or registration of the aforementioned cookie.
* the terminal refers to the material equipment (computer, tablet, smartphone etc.) that you use to consult or view a website, application, marketing content etc.

2. The Cookies we produce on our website

When you connect to our website, we may, depending on your desires, install various cookies on your Terminal which allow us to recognise the browser on your Terminal during the validity of the cookie concerned.

The Cookies we produce allow us to:

- establish statistics, visit and use volumes for various components which comprise our website (sections and content which are visited and read), allowing us to improve the relevance and ergonomics of our services;

- to adapt the presentation of our website to your terminals display preferences (language used, display resolution, operating system used, etc.) during your visits to our website, depending on the viewing or reading materials and software your terminal uses;

- memorise information regarding a form you filled out on our website (registration or access to your account) or products, services or information you selected on our website (service selected, content of an order basket, etc.);

- allow you to access reserved and personal spaces on our website, such as your account, using identifiers or data which you may have given us previously;

- implement security measures, for example if you are asked to connect again to content or a service after a certain period of time.

3. Cookies produced on our website by third parties

The production and use of cookies by third parties is subject to these third parties privacy protection policies. We will inform you of the purpose of the cookies as soon as we are aware of the methods which are available to make choices regarding these cookies.

Cookies issued as a result of third party applications which are integrated on our website:
We are liable to include IT applications from third parties on our website which allow you to share content from our website with other people or to inform these other people of your use of the website or your opinion regarding the content of our website. This is the case with the Share and Like buttons on social networks (such as Facebook, Twitter etc.) Social networks which provide such an application may identify you through this button, even if you did not use this button during your visit to our website. In fact, this kind of application may allow the social network concerned to monitor your browsing on our website, solely as a result of the fact that your account with the social network concerned was activated on your Terminal (open session) during your browsing of our website. We have no control over the procedures used by social networks to collect information regarding your browsing on our website or information such as personal data which they have at their disposal. Please consult the privacy policies of these social networks in order to familiarise yourself with the purposes for using this browsing information (especially marketing purposes), which can be collected using these applications. The privacy policies must especially allow you to exercise your choices with these social networks, above all by configuring your user accounts on each of these networks.

3.2 Targeted advertising cookies issued on our website by third parties:
Our website may contain cookies issued by third parties (communications agency, audience measurement companies, targeted marketing supplier, etc.) which allow these third parties, throughout the duration of the validity of these cookies:
- to collect browsing information for the Terminals which consult our website
- to determine the advertising content for the purposes of promoting products/services offered exclusively on our website which may correspond to the interests of the aforementioned individuals who are browsing on the terminal concerned.

4. Your choices regarding cookies

Several options are open to you for managing cookies. Any configuration that you may undertake may amend your Internet browsing and your access conditions to some services which require the use of Cookies.

You may, at any time, choose to express and amend your wishes in terms of cookies, using the methods described below.

4.1 Choices offered to you by your browsing software:
You can configure your browsing software in such a way that the cookies are registered on your Terminal or, on the contrary, so that they are rejected, either automatically, or based on their issuer. You can also configure your browsing software in such a way that you are occasionally given the option to accept or refuse cookies, before a cookie is registered on your terminal. For more information, refer to the How to exercise your choices, based on the browser you use section.

(a) Cookie Agreement
The registration of a cookie on a terminal is essentially subject to the desires of the Terminal user. The Terminal user may at any time freely express or amend their decisions using the choices offered to them by their browsing software. In your browsing software, if you accept the registration of Cookies on your Terminal, the cookies integrated in pages and content you have consulted may be temporarily stored in a dedicated space on your Terminal. They can only be read by their issuer.

(b) Cookie Refusal
If you refuse the registration of cookies on your Terminal, or if you remove cookies which are registered on your terminal, you can no longer use a certain number of functions which are nonetheless necessary in order to browse some parts of our website. This would be the case if you attempted to access our content or services which require identification. This would also be the case if we or our suppliers could not, for technical accounting purposes, recognise the type of browser used by your terminal, its language and display settings or the country from which your Terminal appears to be connected to the Internet. If need be, we will reject any responsibility for the consequences associated with degraded operation of our services resulting from GUERLAIN inability to register or consult the cookies necessary for operation that you will have refused or deleted.

(c) How to exercise your choices, based on the browser you use
Configuration of your browser is different depending on the management of the cookies and your choices. The configuration is explained in the help menu on your browser and describes how to amend your wishes in terms of cookies.

4.2 Your choices are expressed online on interprofessional platforms
Regarding marketing cookies, you can also connect to the Youronlinechoices website, which is offered by digital marketing professionals at the European Digital Advertising Alliance (EDAA) and is managed in France by the Interactive Advertising Bureau France.
This means that you can find the companies recorded on this platform which give you the option of refusing or accepting the cookies used by these companies to adapt the adverts which may be displayed on your terminal to your browsing information: http://www.youronlinechoices.com/fr/controler-ses-cookies/.
This European platform is shared by hundreds of Internet marketing professionals and is a centralised interface which allows you to express your refusal or acceptance of cookies which may be used to adapt adverts which may be displayed on your terminal to your terminal browsing. Note that this procedure will not prevent adverts from being displayed on the websites you visit. It will only block technologies which make it possible to adapt adverts to your areas of interest.

5. If you share your terminal with other people

If your terminal is used by several people and if one same terminal has several browsing softwares, we cannot fully guarantee that the services and adverts intended for your Terminal really relate to your own use of this terminal and not the use of another Terminal user. Sharing the use of your Terminal and the cookie configuration of the parameters on your browser with other people is your own choice and responsibility. For more information regarding cookies and their use, you can consult the dossier of the French Data Protection Authority at the following address: https://www.cnil.fr.

6. Changes to Data Protection Policy

GUERLAIN may make changes to its Data Protection Policy. Due to these changes, we invite you to regularly visit the section Personal Data.
