# Privacy Policy

Gerard Fashions and Bolan Investments utilise the latest in Secure Server Technology to ensure that all personal and transactional information is protected to the highest standards.

**Data protection**

Gerard Fashions and Bolan Investments are registered data users under the Data Protection Act. We never make your personal details available to other companies for marketing purposes other than for the marketing of Gerard Fashions branded products and services where you have requested us to do so. We (and our representatives) may hold your personal details for research purposes.

**Cookies**

As part of our high security standards, when you enter this website we will use cookies. Cookies are small pieces of information which are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Gerard Fashions has relationships with carefully selected and monitored suppliers to assist in the delivery of a high quality website. Some of these suppliers may also set cookies during your visit in order to meet contractual obligations with Gerard Fashions. If you do not wish to receive cookies you will not be able to use this website. We also use JavaScript to speed up your online shopping experience. If you disable either cookies or JavaScript, you will not be able to use this website.

**Keeping In touch with you**

Where you have requested, Gerard Fashions and Bolan Investments may also use your details to send you further information on Gerard Fashions branded products and services, to keep you up-to-date on goods, services, new collections and promotional offers you may be interested in or for research purposes (this excludes emails about the website where you will receive a welcome email when you register online which enables you to manage the emails you receive).

If you prefer not to receive such information or be a part of such research at any time, please email us at [info@gerardfashions.com](mailto:info@gerardfashions.com) quoting your full name, address and postcode; or write to: Gerard Fashions, Glenwell House, 10-12 Glenwell Road, Glengormley, Belfast, BT36 7RF. Please include your name, address and postcode along with any Marks & Spencer account number or correspondence reference you may have.

Please note writing to this address (or the email address above) will not unsubscribe you from receiving email newsletters about the Gerard FashionsÂ website. If you have registered to receive email newsletters regarding the website, you can remove your email address from the list at any time by using the links in the emails that are sent to you.

LAST_PRODUCT | The most recent product you have viewed.  
NEWMESSAGE | Indicates whether a new message has been received.  
NO_CACHE | Indicates whether it is allowed to use cache.
