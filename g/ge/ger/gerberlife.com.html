Please take a moment to read the following Privacy Notice for some important information about our privacy policy and your rights.


Our Information Confidentiality and Security Policy

Our most important asset is our customers’ trust. We maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to protect your personal information. We permit only authorized persons who are trained in the proper handling of customer information to have access to that information. It is important to note that we at Gerber Life do not sell customer information to marketing, mailing list or telemarketing companies.


Information We May Collect

We may collect personal information about you from the following sources:

  1. From your insurance application, information such as your name, address and telephone number;
  2. From third parties, such as claims, credit, consumer or medical reports;
  3. From your transaction with us, information such as your premium payment and claim history.


Use and Disclosure

Within Gerber Life Insurance Company: We may use the information we collect about you for purposes permitted by law such as administering your insurance policy or claims, processing a transaction you request, or responding to your inquires.

Outside Gerber Life Insurance Company: We do not share personal information about you with nonaffiliated third parties except with your consent or as permitted by law.


How to Review and Correct Your Personal Information

You may contact us in writing regarding any personal information in your file. If you believe any of our information about you is incorrect, you may advise us of any corrections which you believe should be made. We will investigate only after you notify us in writing. If we agree, we will correct our records. If we do not agree there are any errors in our records, you may file a statement with us that disputes the information in your file. If you have any questions about the right of access to or correction of information in your file, please contact us at the address below. In order for us to safeguard your information, you must contact us in writing, and include your name, address, policy number, daytime phone number, the best time of day for us to call, and a copy of personal identification such as your driver’s license.
