# Privacy Policy

Information provided by users at **Geek Prison** is safe and are not used in any illegal form. We here at Geekprison strongly believe in keeping information we hold safe and maintain your privacy. We never ask for any sort of financial details such as your Credit Card or Debit Card details.

### Information We collect

We might collect some information such as your E-Mail Address, Name, IP Address, etc. This information’s are required to fight spam and keep the site clean. Some other plugins also may require the information. All of this information’s you provide to us remains safe and are not sold to any third organization or used in any other form. E-Mail Address provided while commenting are never displayed.

Like Most of the sites GeekPrison also display advertisement via Google Adsense. Google uses DART cookies on your computer or any device to display ads related to your search on our site or any other site on the Internet.
