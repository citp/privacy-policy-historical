# Privacy Policy

**The views and opinions expressed on this blog are purely the bloggers’ own. Contents of this blog-all texts and images may not be published, rewritten, broadcast or redistributed anywhere without permission.**

> **Disclaimer**

The views expressed in this blog(www.geekblogger.org) are solely mine(Piyush Dungrani) and do not reflect the views of my family, friends, employer or organization. All the thoughts expressed in this blog are true to the best of my knowledge. If you feel that anything is not correct, then do let us know about it.

> **Privacy Policy**

We give utmost priority to our visitors. Your privacy is very important to us. To better protect your privacy we are providing this notice explaining our online information practices so that you can make a better choice while providing your information.

> **Information we collect**

Our servers collect the log information related to your activity on this site. The log file will include your IP address, ISP provider, webbrowser information, OS used, time on the site, entry pages, exit pages etc. All this information is used to provide a better service. Your information is never shared with others. Your data is as safe as mine. On some pages we might collect your name and email addresses. We will be using this information to contact you incase you need any help from us. We will never sell your information or make it public.

> **Third party advertising – Cookies**

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads on our website(We do need some money to run the website right?). These companies may use cookies to get information (not including your name, address, email address etc) like IP address, browse patterns etc about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide relevant advertisements. You can however choose to disable or selectively turn off third-party cookies in your browser settings(But in such cases it might cause problems using this website to the fullest).

> **Commenting Policy**

All the comments made on this blog are the views of the respective authors. The author is not responsible for the comments that are made on this blog. While we welcome comments from anyone, we reserve the right to remove or edit any comment that is not suitable for publication. This could include but is not limited to objectionable and abusive comments, hate, racism etc.
