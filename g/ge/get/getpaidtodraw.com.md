# GetPaidToDraw.com Make money from home submitting artwork and photos through email!

The privacy and confidentiality of our members and prospects is upheld with the highest consideration. We will never disclose of any personal or identifying information to any third party in any form. A member's financial information is kept fully secured as to prevent any possibility of identity theft and the contact information is protected from any third parties seeking marketing information. 

Electronic newsletters policy (Dispatches)  
We will offer a free electronic newsletter to users. LearnPhotoshopVideos gathers the email addresses of users who voluntarily subscribe. Users may remove themselves from this mailing list by following the link provided in every newsletter that points users to the subscription management page. Users can also subscribe to the newsletters at the time of registration.

The only exceptions to the above statement are when action is necessary to: (1) conform to legal requirements or comply with the legal process, (2) protect and defend the rights or property of LearnPhotoshopVideos.com and its owners, (3) enforce our terms of use, (4) respect the transfer of opted-in prospect information if LearnPhotoshopVideos.com is ever transferred to a new owner, in which case the new owner must obey these same terms, or (5) act to protect the interests of our users or public welfare.

To better serve our users, we may reserve the right to collect aggregate data to improve the site's layout and performance. This will never be any individual data and will be used as a statistical study only. By accessing this page, a user has consented to our right for such aggregate information collection.  
By using this site and any of its derived work, you agree that you are over the age of 18. If you are under 18, then you must first obtain explicit permission from a parent/guardian to view and use the content or product herein.  


We uphold and comply fully with the Children's Online Protection Act (COPPA). For children and persons under the age of 13, UNDER NO CIRCUMSTANCE ARE YOU ALLOWED TO SUBMIT ANY TYPE OF DEMOGRAPHIC, PERSONAL, PSYCHOGRAPHIC, OR BILLING/FINANCIAL INFORMATION. Neither us nor our employees knowingly collect information from persons under the age of 13. We do not and will never collect nor distribute this type of information through third parties, with the exception of obeying the legal process.  
We reserve the full right to modify this privacy policy at any time.
