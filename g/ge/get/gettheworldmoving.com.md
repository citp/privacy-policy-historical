# Corporate Wellness Challenges: Health Care Activities for the Workplace

Gettheworldmoving Pty Ltd is bound by and adheres to the National Privacy Principles ( NPPs ) which form part of the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). Gettheworldmoving Pty Ltd has adopted the following policy in relation to any personal information which may be collected through this website. The purpose of this policy is to tell you what kind of information we may gather about you when you visit this website how we may use that information whether we disclose it to anyone and the choices you have regarding our use of and your ability to correct this information. Our policy allows you to choose what kind and how much information you provide to us and to control how we use whatever information you give us.

**Company Information**

**Collection of Information**

Information is collected from you primarily to make it easier and more rewarding for you to use our services. Depending on the service you are accessing you could be asked to provide information such as your name e-mail address or information about what you like and do not like. Gettheworldmoving Pty Ltd collects anonymous and personal information (i.e. information which might enable you to be identified such as your name e-mail address or postal address). Gettheworldmoving Pty Ltd will only collect personal information when you provide it to us. Sometimes we may specifically ask for personal information about you when you sign up to use a service through this website. Certain information may be required (such as your name age internet address or screen name billing address type of computer) in order to provide that service or product to you. This information may also be used to inform you of additional products and services which may interest you. You can choose not to receive such information when you are undertaking product or service registration on this website. We may also ask about your interests but it is your choice whether to respond.

**Use of Anonymous Information**

Gettheworldmoving Pty Ltd automatically gathers anonymous information to monitor the use of this website. We only use such data in the aggregate. Gettheworldmoving Pty Ltd may publish or provide this aggregate data to other people. Legal Disclaimer Gettheworldmoving Pty Ltd will only use the personal information you have chosen to provide us for the purpose for which you provided it. Although the GCC is a low-risk event encouraging participants to be more physically active in whichever way they feel appropriate the Employer on behalf of itself and its participating employees acknowledges that GCC is not in a position to determine the suitability or any other health risks that may be faced by an individual employee and hence each participating employee takes part in the Event solely at his or her risk.

**Cookies**

This website currently uses cookies. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer s hard drive. One of the reasons for using cookies is to identify the total number of users of a site and measure traffic patterns for research purposes. Cookies may also be used by sites that offer personalised or customised content so that a user s preferences can be remembered when they return to the site.

Gettheworldmoving Pty Ltd may collect information via cookies. If Gettheworldmoving Pty Ltd collects personal information via cookies then that personal information will be dealt with by Gettheworldmoving Pty Ltd in accordance with this privacy policy. In other words information collected via cookies will be treated in the same way as any other information.

Third party application allowing users to share content on the web may also use cookies. No personally identifiable information is collected by or transferred to this application. For more information and to opt-out please visit: <http://sharethis.com/privacy>
