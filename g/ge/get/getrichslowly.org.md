# Privacy Policy - UPDATED May 14, 2009 * Get Rich Slowly

##  [Privacy Policy - UPDATED May 14, 2009](http://www.getrichslowly.org/blog/privacy-policy/ "Permanent Link: Privacy Policy - UPDATED May 14, 2009")

Saturday, 16th February 2008 (by J.D.)   
This article is about [Administration](http://www.getrichslowly.org/blog/category/administration/ "View all posts in Administration") \- [0 comments](http://www.getrichslowly.org/blog/privacy-policy/#comments "Privacy Policy - UPDATED May 14, 2009")

Get Rich Slowly takes online privacy seriously. We actively protect the the privacy of the users of this web site and those who subscribe to the mailing list.

_Subscription Lists & Email Addresses:_ We only collect the names and email addresses of subscribers to the email newsletter and hold them confidentially. We will not release or disclose any specific subscriber information under any circumstances unless demanded by law. General aggregate information about the list itself (total number of subscribers, etc.) may be disclosed.

_Web Server Traffic:_ We record information about how people access the web site and this includes your IP address (or DNS), your browser, and your operating system.

_Email:_ Email sent to the site is considered property of this site. However, we will always attempt to obtain your consent if we decide to publish something you’ve written. 

_Comments:_ Comments left on the site are the responsibility of the commenter. We reserve the right to remove comments or edit them due to errors. We won’t censor comments, though we do attempt to screen for spam. As for email addresses included in the comments, please see the Subscription Lists & Email Addresses entry.

_External Links:_ The web site contains many links to other sites and due to the constantly changing nature of the Internet, we make no promises as to the reliability of those sites or their privacy policies.

_**Update** (April 2009):_ We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
