# F1, Formula 1: GPToday.com - Privacy Policy

GP Today, and its advertisers and affiliates are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users.

During the process of delivering our content to you, GP Today does not collect any personally identifiable information about you such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address without your knowledge.

Your email address is required to join GP Today's mailing list, 'PPP Competition', the Forums and the News Grab Service, but this information is not shared or sold to any other party.

GP Today does obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information about its visitors such as time of visit, browser type, the type of operating system, screen resolution and screen colours, etc but this information is not linked to individual visitors. This information is used to assist GP Today in producing web pages more suited to its average visitor.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp).
