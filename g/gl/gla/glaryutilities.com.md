# Glary Utilities - Privacy Statements

## Privacy Statements

It is recommended that you read this privacy policy. Below is a summary of Glarysoft's online privacy policy. This policy covers the Glarysoft community web sites. 

Glarysoft respects your privacy choices. Any personal information you give us will be treated according to our privacy policy. 

**Your personal information is used in the following ways:**

> **Customer Feedback and Support**  
>  Provides a channel for you to request information from Glarysoft. Also used to tell us how we can improve our products, services and site designs.
> 
> **Email Correspondence**  
>  Used to raise and respond to various issues and inquiries. Also used to distribute information about Glarysoft, our products, and services.

**Security measures:**  
We protect the personal information you share with us. When you make an online purchase with us, we encrypt your credit card number. Access to your personal information is limited to people who need it to do their job.

We do not share your personal information with third parties, unless you have granted us permission to do so.

**Cookies:**  
Most Glarysoft web sites use "cookies." Cookies help us provide a better user experience by measuring which web site areas are of greatest interest. Glarysoft can only read cookies from Glarysoft web sites. If you choose to disable cookies in your browser, you can still access most of our web sites.
