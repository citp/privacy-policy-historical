# GlobalizerNetwork :: Privacy

Your information is safe with us! We will never sell or use any personal information that we acquire. We will never betray your trust. Our policy about privacy is that it yours and we respect it! 

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We will only use the information that we collect about you lawfully (in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998). We do not, however, collect any information from you whatsoever. We neither collect nor sell any information.

Our adverstisers may set a cookie should you click on an ad or offer. These cookies are to track your general behaviour but contain no personal information. You can always refuse the acceptance of cookies in your browser settings.
