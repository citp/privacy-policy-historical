# Privacy

This Privacy Policy describes how Globalshopex collect and use the Personal Information customer provides on Globalshopex website and as part of Our Services, including Personally Identifiable Information, Personally Identifiable Information and Anonymous Information. It also describes the choices available to customers regarding Globalshopex use of customer Personal Information and how customer can access and update this information. By visiting Globalshopex, customer are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. 

All terms not otherwise defined herein shall have the definitions set forth in the Terms of Service.    
This Privacy Policy, which is part of our overall Terms of Service ("Terms of Service"), covers personal information shared through our website and Our Services, including Personally Identifiable Information and Anonymous Information (the "Personal Information"). "Personally Identifiable Information" or "PII" means information that can be used to identify Customer as an individual.

GlobalShopex will receive and store the basic information customer give us during checkout in order to process and deliver their products and provide them exceptional customer support.  Customer must provide contact information (such as name, email, telephone number and shipping address) and financial information (such as credit card number). The information customer provide us will be used for billing purposes and to fill their orders.  

We follow CAN SPAM laws and offer permission based email marketing.  If customer do not wish for us to contact customer about offers that may interest them, customer may uncheck the opt-in feature in the checkout, or opt-out from the emails themselves by following the unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of any e-mail message customer receive from GlobalShopex.

**II . Personal Data Security and Confidentiality   
**

We maintain adequate technical and organizational security measures to protect customer personal information from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. 

**1.-Third Parties**

If customer purchase, use or specifically express interest in a product or service of one of our partners, we may share Personal Information about customer with such partner. 

We use third party payment processors and fraud screening to assist us in processing and share with them customer payment information securely. Such third party processors’ use of customer Personal Information is governed by their respective privacy policies which may or may not contain privacy protections as protective as this privacy policy. 

We may use other third parties to help us provide services and marketing to customer, such as shipping or fulfilling orders, processing payments, monitoring site activity, identifying and preventing fraud, conducting quality assurance and testing, providing support, conducting surveys, and administering e-mails, and share with them customer Personal Information solely for those purposes. 

If we share any Personal Information with any of these third parties, we will require that such information be used only to provide the services requested by us and be maintained by them in strictest confidence. 

We do not control the use by third parties of customer Personal Information that we collect, and its use by them will be in accordance with their own privacy policies. However, we will ensure that they are bound by agreements with us that include non-disclosure and certain data protection provisions. If customer do not wish for customer Personal Information to be shared in this manner, customer may opt not to purchase or use these third party services or to specifically express interest in our partner products or services. 

We will always ask for customer consent before sharing customer Personal Information with Third Parties for purposes other than those described herein.    


  
**2.- Cookies and Opting Out**

Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to customer computer's hard drive through customer Web browser to enable our systems to recognize customer browser and to provide certain features and improve our business. 

The help portion of the toolbar on most browsers will tell customer how to prevent customer browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify customer when customer receive a new cookie, or how to disable cookies altogether. Additionally, customer can disable or delete similar data used by browser add-ons, such as Flash cookies, by changing the add-on's settings or visiting the website of its manufacturer. However, because cookies allow customer to take advantage of some of GlobalShopex's essential features, we recommend that customer leave them turned on. For instance, if customer block or otherwise reject our cookies, customer will not be able to add items to customer Shopping Cart, proceed to Checkout, or to Sign in to GlobalShopex's website.    


  
**3.-Third Party Tags and content**

We use and manage third party tags on Our Site. Third party tags may take the form of pixels or tracking snippets. This means that no cookies are used and no personal data is collected. We use Google Tag Manager to manage our third party tag usage. We use tracking snippets to capture data from customer browser, make requests to a third party partner or set cookies on customer device to store data. This may cause other tags to be activated which may, for their part, collect data and set cookies under certain circumstances. Google Tag Manager does not access or store this data.
