**_DoubleClick DART Cookie_**  
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on cuseeme.tv.

You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at - [www.google.com/privacy_ads.html](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)
