# Privacy Policy

  
At Global English TESOL courses we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy and personal data collected via this website, and to complying fully with all UK Data Protection legislation. This Privacy Policy (together with our [terms and conditions](https://web.archive.org/web/20190725130027id_/https://terms/)) sets out the basis on which any personal data we collect from you, or that you provide to us, will be processed by us. 

We confirm that we will keep the information on a secure server and that we will comply fully with all applicable UK Data Protection legislation and regulations.

\------------

**Global English TESOL courses cookie policy at a glance:**

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device as you view websites. We use cookies to help provide you with the best experience we can when you visit our website. The cookies on our site are:

1\. Necessary cookies – these help make a website usable by enabling basic functions, such as page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

2\. Statistic cookies – these collect and report information anonymously, so we can understand how visitors interact with our website.

3\. Marketing cookies – these track visitors as they journey through our website. It can mean later ads displayed to you can then be more relevant to each individual user.  

You can  **switch cookies off**  by adjusting your browser settings to stop your device from accepting cookies (see help section in your browser) although keep in mind that turning them off will limit your access and some functionality on our website. Otherwise, using our site means you agree to us placing these sorts of cookies on your device. Discover more about cookies here: [ _https://cookies.insites.com/about-cookies/_](https://cookies.insites.com/about-cookies/) __
