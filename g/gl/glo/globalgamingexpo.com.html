Privacy Policy

Reed Exhibition Companies and  Global Gaming Expo (G2E) respect the information provided by our customers.  Because we gather certain types of information when our customers communicate with us, we believe you should fully understand the terms and conditions surrounding the information we capture and the use of that information.  This privacy statement discloses the type of information we capture about our customers, how we use it to communicate with our customers, and how you can request to no longer receive communications from G2E.

 US Policy

G2E is produced and managed by Reed Exhibition Companies United States (REC-US), a division of Reed Elsevier, Inc.  This privacy policy applies to all REC-US offices and conforms to the spirit of the Reed Elsevier Corporate Privacy Policy, which can be found at http://reedexpo.cahners1.com/privacy.asp

Our Customers

This policy applies to all customers of G2E and REC-US, who include but are not limited to, our show exhibitors, show visitors, conference visitors, customer inquiries, speakers, press, and other individuals with whom REC-US maintains business relationships or communications.

 Customer Information and Use

Two primary types of information are gathered about our customers:

 Financial Information is payment information used for or gathered during the registration process and includes, but is not limited to, credit card numbers, financial institutions, and expiration dates.  This information is not used for any purpose other than that for which it is being provided to us, and is used only to the extent necessary for processing the transaction or to facilitate future transactions.

 Professional Information includes, but is not limited to, name, address, company name, company address, phone number, fax number, and email address, as well as questions answered on survey or registration forms.  Data captured during a customer’s visit to the G2E web site, as well as during a visit with a G2E customer is also considered professional information and may also be stored in our customer database for our internal use.  Professional information may be, upon the consent of G2E, shared with other organizations or G2E customers for the purpose of facilitating business relationships believed by REC-US to be in our customers’ best interests.

 Opting Out

REC-US will accept, implement and maintain any customer request to opt out of future communication from G2E, REC-US, or a third party to whom G2E provided the customer’s information.  If you would like to opt out of communications you are receiving from G2E, REC-US, or a third party contacting you on behalf of G2E, please click here www.recusprivacy.com and enter the information requested so we may quickly and thoroughly implement your request.

 Security

This site utilizes a secure server to protect your financial and professional information.  Secure server software is used to encrypt the information sent between your Internet browser and our Internet site.  This ensures the security of your professional and financial transaction details when using the site.  We follow strict security procedures in the storage and disclosure of information you have given to us and may, on occasion, request proof of identity before we disclose any professional information to you.

 Cookies

“Cookies” are small packages of information that are stored by your browser on your PC’s hard drive.  This site does not utilize cookies to manage your site experience.

 Policy Changes

REC-US reserves the right to review and change this Policy.  Policy changes may be made without prior notice to existing customers.  Policy revisions only apply to information gathered as of the effective date of the revised policy, unless we give you notice and choice as to the changes in use, in which case all information is subject to the new policy.
