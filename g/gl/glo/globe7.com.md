# Welcome to Globe7

Globe7 Inc. recognizes the significance of protecting your privacy and is committed to safeguard it. We will never reveal your name, e-mail Id, your contact, account and billing information to any third party. 

We will keep your Globe7 account information, billing information and call logs under strict confidentiality and all your information will be stored in your highly secure and password-protected account. We have numerous security measures in place to protect and secure your information. 

All your information is stored on our servers in encrypted form and is accessible only to the authorized members of the Globe7 staff. Our servers that store personally identifiable information are kept in a secure environment and are not reachable by unauthorized personnel.

We also use data firewalls to restrict unauthorized access from other computers. We assure you utmost security for your billing information from unauthorized access and tampering as your billing information will be encrypted using SSL encryption technology. We ensure that we are free from all sorts of viruses, spy ware, ad ware and spam.

We will contact you from time to time regarding your account expiry or low balance of your account or to inform you about our new services.

We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. When we change it, we will also revise the last updated date at the top of the Privacy Policy. Your continued use of the service constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Policy and any updates
