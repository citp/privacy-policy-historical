Sharing Information with Third Parties  
We will never share your email address, phone number or credit card information with any unrelated 3rd party except as necessary to fulfill transactions that you initiate or in connection with a business transfer (see "Business Transfer" below).   
Information We Collect  
When visiting the our web site, you may provide us with two types of information: personal information you voluntarily choose to disclose during registration that is collected on an individual basis, and web site use information gathered on a collective basis as you and others browse our web site.   
How Do We Use the Information That You Provide to Us?  
Generally, we use personal information for purposes of managing our business activities, providing customer service and making available other products and services to our customers and prospective customers. We will also use the information we collect to notify you about important changes to our web site, new services and special offers we think you will find valuable.  
Choice/Opt-Out  
If at any time you do not want to receive our e-mail announcements, simply opt-out by responding to the e-mail address on the bottom of the e-mail or contact us at [cancel@globalindustrial.com](mailto:cancel@globalindustrial.com).

If you do not want to receive our catalog, send your customer priority number to our email address, [deletes@globalindustrial.com](mailto:deletes@globalindustrial.com) or call our dedicated mail list removal phone line, #800-849-9167 or use our fax number 1-516-625-4329.   
  
Correct/Update  
Corrections or updates to your account information can be made on-line, once you have logged-on to the globalindustrial.com site. Simply click on the "Your Account" button, located in the top right-hand corner of the page. The "Your Account" page contains links to Add or Edit personal information, addresses, payment methods and shipping preferences. If you have any questions about your company profile, send an e-mail to [service@globalindustrial.com](mailto:service@globalindustrial.com).   
Use of Information/Cookies  
A cookie is a small text file, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier. When you visit a web site, that site's computer asks your computer for permission to store this file in a part of your hard drive specifically designated for cookies. Each website can only send and access its own cookie to your browser and only if your browser's preferences allow it.   
How Do We Use Information We Collect From Cookies?   
  
Our website uses cookies to differentiate you from other customers. In some cases, we also use cookies to prevent you from seeing unnecessary advertisements or to make it more convenient to login to our website. Cookies, in conjunction with IP addresses and our web server's log files, allow us to calculate the aggregate number of people visiting our web site, the click stream to and through our web site and which parts of the site are most popular. This helps us gather feedback in order to constantly improve our web site and better serve our customers.  
  
Business Transfer  
Global Industrial has been in business for over 60 years and is a wholly owned subsidiary of Systemax, Inc., a Fortune 1000 Company (NYSE symbol SYX). Our intent is to continue operating for many years to come. However, in the unlikely event that Global Industrial is sold, has its assets acquired or transferred, whether voluntarily or involuntarily, or merges or becomes affiliated with any other division, individual or entity, any customer information owned or under the control of Global Industrial may be one of the transferred assets.  
Web Sites Linked to globalindustrial.com   
We are not responsible for the practices employed by web sites linked to or from our web site nor the information or content contained therein. Your browsing and interaction on any other web site, including websites that have a link on our website, is subject to that web site's own rules and policies. Please read over those rules and policies before proceeding.   
Security  
The personal information you enter on our web site is transmitted securely via Secure Sockets Layer SSL, 128 bit encryption services. Pages utilizing this technology will have URLs that start with HTTPS instead of HTTP. Our web site, globalindustrial.com, is certified by VeriSign and your personal information and order history are securely stored on separate servers.
