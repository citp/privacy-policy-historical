Privacy Policy

Updated January 2013

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, and information volunteered by the consumer (such as survey information and/or site registrations). The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web pages and the quality of our service, and is not shared with or sold to other organizations for commercial purposes, except to provide products or services you’ve requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

It is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Terms of Service, or as otherwise required by law.

We transfer information about you if Global Apps Network is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, Global Apps Network will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Information Gathering and Usage

When you register for Global Apps Network we ask for information such as your name, email address, billing address, credit card information.

Global Apps Network uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provision, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research.

Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive.

Cookies are required to use the Global Apps Network service.

We use cookies to record current session information, but do not use permanent cookies. You are required to log-in to your Global Apps Network Project Site after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents.

Data Storage

Global Apps Network uses third party vendors and hosting partners to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to run Global Apps Network. Although Global Apps Network owns the code, databases, and all rights to the Global Apps Network application, you retain all rights to your data.

Global Apps Network may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with subpoenas or when your actions violate the Terms of Service.

Changes

Global Apps Network may periodically update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your Global Apps Network primary account holder account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.

Questions

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please click on the “support” link on the  Global Apps Network website to send a support ticket.
