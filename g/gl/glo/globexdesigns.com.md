## The Information We Collect

**Information you provide** When you sign up for a new Globex Designs account we ask you for some personal information such as your name, email address and password. We do not collect or store any billing information such as credit cards, bank account numbers, or billing addresses. All transactions are done via third party services such as [PayPal](http://www.paypal.com/ "PayPal"). Those transactions are protected by the policies of our partners.

**Cookies** When you visit this site, we send several cookies (small files containing system information) to your computer that uniquely identifies your browser. We use cookies to improve the quality of our service by storing user preferences and tracking user trends, such as which of our resources you found most useful. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some services may not work if your cookies are disabled.

**Log information** When you browse the site, our servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website. These server logs may include information such as your web request, IP address, browser, operating system, language and the date of your request. This information is strictly used for statistical use only in order to improve our website for all our users.

**Email communication** When you send in an inquery to our staff, we retain all email communication sent in order to respond to your requests in an efficient manner.

We never provide any of this gathered information to our affiliates, partners or any other third party website without clearly informing you of such actions beforehand.
