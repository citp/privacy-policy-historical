IV. Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

The Site utilizes "cookies" and other tracking technologies. A cookie is a small text file that a website transfers to an individual's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. For example, we may use cookies to collect information about website activity and, if applicable, to maintain your online shopping experience. We will never save credit card information in cookies. Most browsers allow you to control cookies, including whether or not to accept them and how to remove them. You may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie, or you may choose to block cookies with your browser. However, please be aware that some features of the Site may not function properly or may be slower if you choose to block the use of cookies.

You may occasionally receive cookies or other tracking technologies from our advertisers or other third parties with links on the Site (see, e.g., Section X and Section XI below). We are not the source of such tracking technologies, nor do we control the use or application of any such cookies or other tracking technologies. The use of advertising cookies sent by third-party ad servers is a common occurrence in Internet-based businesses and related online transactions.

Tracking technologies may record information such as Internet domain and host names; Internet protocol (IP) addresses; browser software and language, operating system type and resolution display capabilities; java settings; clickstream patterns; geographic location; and dates and times that our Site is accessed. An IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you are surfing the web. Web servers, the computers that "serve up" webpages, automatically identify your computer by its IP address. We may link the information we record using tracking technologies to Personal Information that we collect at the Site for our own marketing and advertising purposes, as well as those of our business partners and affiliated companies. In addition, we reserve the right to use IP addresses and other tracking technologies to identify a visitor when we feel it is necessary to enforce compliance with the Site's policies, to improve performance of the Services offered by the Site, to protect the Site, and our customers, subscribers and other visitors, and whenever we believe in good faith that the law requires it. We may use other tracking technologies in connection with the Site and related Services as well, including but not limited to:

  • "Tracer tags." These may also be referred to as "Clear GIFs" or "Web Beacons." This technology allows us to understand which pages you visit on the Site. Such tracking technologies are used to help us optimize and tailor the Site for you and other Site visitors.
  • "Local Shared Objects." Also called "flash cookies." This technology helps us store user preferences and improve how we serve our users. Disabling traditional cookies may not affect flash cookies. As of the date of this Privacy Policy, you can disable flash cookies by using the Global Storage Settings panel of the online Settings Manager at Adobe's website. However, as with traditional cookies, some features of the Site and the Services, and some features of other websites and related services, may not function properly if you disable flash cookies.
  • Device Specification Testing. When you are on a website or application that offers the Gaming Service or other of Gaikai's Services, our servers automatically detect and analyze your location and device settings (Java, internet bandwidth, display settings, etc.) to determine whether to offer you the Gaming Service or any of the other Services made available by or for Gaikai.

 

Pop-up blockers or stoppers prevent extra pop-up windows (which are oftentimes advertisements) from opening in your browser. Pop-up blocking software often has negative effects on other "good" software. If you disable JavaScript pop-up ad windows, you may also disable other website functionality that uses JavaScript. If you're using a pop-up blocker that allows you to manage a list of "pop-up friendly" sites, please refer to your "Help" files and add the URL of this Site to your approved list to ensure maximum functionality when you choose to use the Site.
