# Gadget Hacks: Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

GadgetHacks.com has established this privacy policy to explain to you how your information is collected, used, and protected. This privacy policy may be updated from time to time, so we recommend you check back often. If we make materially significant changes to this privacy policy, we will post a notice on the GadgetHacks.com site.

The only personally identifiable information about you that GadgetHacks.com collects is the information that you the user include in the submission form to submit a site, or Contact Us form. We use such personally identifiable information only in connection with the provision of GadgetHacks.com, and do NOT use it for marketing or financial purposes. We do not share, rent, or sell or in any other means give your personally identifiable information with any outside companies. In layman’s terms, we’ll NEVER give your name or email address out to anyone. We’re internet users too, and we hate spam and unsolicited emails too.

When you use or access GadgetHacks.com, our site automatically receives and stores certain information from your browser, including your IP address; your browser type; access date and time; the referring Web site address; country you are coming from; and the GadgetHacks.com page you requested. We use this information to learn more about our users, such as their usage patterns, what pages they find more interesting, and browser types. This information will help us improve the quality and usefulness of the site. We do NOT link any of this information with your personally identifiable information. For marketing purposes of our website, some of this information will be used when describing our site user demographics (such as country users are coming from, which browsers they use, what pages users come to our site from, etc.).

If you have any questions regarding your privacy, please contact us at [feedback@gadgethacks.com](mailto:feedback@gadgethacks.com)
