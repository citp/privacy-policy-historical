# Privacy Policy | GameBorder

GameBorder is aware of privacy concerns of visitors to this website and strives to respect the privacy of our users. 

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who post messages to our forum, the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or newsletter registrations. 

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web page, shared with other reputable organizations to help them contact consumers for marketing purposes, used by us to contact consumers for marketing purposes. 

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by [**sending us**](https://web.archive.org/contact-us) an e-mail 

Cookies: We use cookies to record session information, such as the user's country or the items that consumers add to their wish list. 

Advertisers: GameBorder advertisements are served by 3rd party advertiser servers. 

External Links: GameBorder contains links to other sites. GameBorder is not responsible for either the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. 

Security: We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site. 

Exceptions: We reserve the right to disclose personal information to necessary third parties if we are required to do so:  
* To comply with legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena or court order;  
* To protect the company's rights and property;  
* To investigate reports of users sending material using a false e-mail address or users sending harassing, threatening, or abusive messages;  
* To protect against misuse or unauthorized use of our site and/or any of our services; or  
* During emergencies, such as when we believe someone's physical safety is at risk.  


Policy Changes: GameBorder may, in the future, modify this privacy policy in order to provide additional clarity or to address additional issues, as needed. Any such changes will be posted here immediately.
