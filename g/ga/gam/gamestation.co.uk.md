# Gamestation :: Privacy Statement

We are committed to protecting your privacy.

  
**The type of information we will collect about you**  
your name  
address  
phone number  
email address  
credit/debit card details  
We do require a contact telephone number so that we may contact you in the event of a problem occurring with your order.  
Your credit card details are held on a secure server until your order has been processed. These credit card details are then permanently deleted from our system.  
*Please note: We would only obtain sensitive information about you once we have received your consent first.

**Use of the Information supplied by you**  
The information you supply to make an order is only required for 2 reasons:  
1\. to allow us to process your order and for online administration;  
2\. to make sure we can provide you with the best products, benefits and service possible. 

We may also use email addresses or other personally identifiable information when We are so authorised by you to contact you directly. For example, to notify you that you have won a competition, or to provide a requested newsletter.

In assessing your request for goods, We may use your information for the purposes of the prevention and detection of fraud. One of the purposes for which we may disclose your address and postcode details is to check against the IMRG Security Alert or any other Fraud Prevention Scheme. At all times where we disclose your information it will remain secure. 

**Unsubscribe**  
If you do not wish to receive any email correspondence from us please email us at [enquiries@gamestation.co.uk](mailto:enquiries@gamestation.co.uk) with the words "Unsubscribe" on the subject line of the email.

**Disclosure of your information**  
At Gamestation, We may keep our online customers up to date with details of the latest games and offers available with regular emails. If you would prefer not to receive this information, please de-select the "I would like to receive news about Gamestation checkbox  
Your personal information may be circulated within the Gamestation Group in both the UK and the USA, and disclosed only to our sub-contractors and persons acting as our agents who have agreed to keep your personal information strictly confidential.

Data Protection Act 1998 (the "Act")

Gamestation supports the requirements and intent of the Act and will require employee and business partner compliance with the Act. 

**Updating your details**

Please help Gamestation keep your records updated by notifying us of any changes to your personal information - either by sending an email to enquiries@gamestation.co.uk or by going to the 'change your details' section on the 'checkout' screen and inputting the amended information. If you email us, we will update our records as soon as reasonably practicable.

You can check the information that we hold about you by checking your Account details. If you find any inaccuracies you can delete or correct it. If you want it removed, please email us at [enquiries@gamestation.co.uk.](mailto:enquiries@gamestation.co.uk)

If you have any questions/comments about privacy, please email us.
