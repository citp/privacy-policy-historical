# GamesTracker - Disclaimer

* * *

This is the web site of GamesTracker.

We can be reached via e-mail at info@gamestracker.co.uk or you can reach us by telephone at 0871 288 4037.

For each visitor to our web page, our web server automatically recognizes your domain name, IP address and the pages that you visit.

We only collect the e-mail addresses of those who voluntarily register on our web site.

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our web site, used to customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual visitor, used to notify consumers about updates to our web site. Any personal identifiable information collected by us is never shared or transmitted to any other organization in any form without your permission.

With respect to cookies: We use cookies to store visitors preferences, record session information, record user-specific information on what pages users access or visit.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, you can change your preferences by visiting us at [www.gamestracker.co.uk/mydetails.htm](https://web.archive.org/mydetails.htm).

With respect to Ad Servers: To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information. Personally indentifiable information is never shared with these advertising companies.

We provide site visitors with access to all information that we maintain about them. Consumers can access this information by visiting us at [ www.gamestracker.co.uk/mydetails.htm](https://web.archive.org/mydetails.htm). Consumers can correct any of this information online at this page.

With respect to security: We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above addresses or phone number.
