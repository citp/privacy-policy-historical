# Privacy statement Â· Gambling Commission

This website is operated by the Gambling Commission whose principal place of business is at Berkshire House, 168-173 High Holborn, London WC1V 7AA.

We respect the privacy of every individual who visits our website. The Gambling Commission website does not automatically store or capture personal information, but merely logs the user's IP address that is automatically recognised by the web server.

We do not use cookies for collecting user information. We will not collect any information about you except your IP address, which is used to record the number of visitors to our site.

Personal data (ie information which could identify you) submitted to Commission's website will only be used to respond to your feedback or, if requested, to send you information updates on our work. We will not pass it on to any third party (including government departments or other agencies) without your consent.

We take reasonable precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of data that you give us. If you would like us to correct or update any information, or if you would like information deleted from our records, then please contact us on info@gamblingcommission.gov.uk, or write to:

Website Manager  
Gambling Commission  
Berkshire House  
168-173 High Holborn  
London WC1V 7AA

This privacy statement only covers the Gambling Commission website at www.gamblingcommission.gov.uk. This statement does not cover links within this site to other websites.
