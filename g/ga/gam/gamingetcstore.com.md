PRIVACY POLICY

When you shop at any of the SCS Collectibles, Inc sites, your privacy is our NUMBER ONE concern. We will NEVER give add you to any external mailing list. In addition, we will NEVER sell your name to any third party.

All our shopping carts are 100% encrypted on the ordering page, so that you will never have to worry about internet credit card or identity theft. Just look at any URL atop of the page when you enter sensitive information and you will notice HTTPS:// replacing the standard HTTP. This means that your information is being encrypted to protect you.

SCS Collectibles has been in business for more than 10 years and has more than 50,000 satisfied customers.

Our office is open M-F 9:00 am to 5:00 pm EST to answer any of your concerns or questions.

We thank you for your support.
