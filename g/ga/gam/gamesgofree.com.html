We take all necessary security arrangements to protect against unauthorized access to unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. These include domestic reviews of our data base, storage and processing practices and security measures, as well as physical security measures to guard against unauthorized access to systems where we store personal data. We strongly recommend that Parents take time to communicate and educate their children about Internet Safety.

4. Cookies

A "cookie" is a small file that contains a line of characters that is sent to your computer when you visit a website. When you visit the website again, the cookie allows that site to recognize your browser. However, we do not use cookies to store personal information such as your name. You can always disable cookies or set your browser to alert you when cookies are being sent to your computer.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. Thus, Google, as a third party supplier, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
