Services that we offer on our sites, such as message boards, may require registration as a condition of use.

Cookies
"Cookies" are pieces of information (a file) that a Web site transfers to a user's internet browser for record-keeping purposes. The use of cookies is common on the Internet. The process we use to serve advertisements and to track use of GameTours.com involves cookies. We use cookies to recognize users who have already registered on GameTours.com, allowing those users to speed up their future activities on the site. Cookies help GameTours.com track the frequency and placement of advertisements. Cookies also enable us to measure aggregate (total) usage and traffic to GameTours.com. This aids us in producing content that best meets our users' preferences. We may use that information to display advertisements that we think will be of particular interest to you. You may configure your browser to reject cookies, but this may interfere with some functionality of GameTours.com. Certain pages of GameTours.com may contain electronic images (called a "single-pixel GIF" or "web beacon") that allow a web site to count users who have visited that page. GameTours.com may occasionally use web beacons in order to count users and to recognize registered users by accessing the GameTours.com cookies.

- To send you information you agreed to receive about topics we think will be of interest to you.
