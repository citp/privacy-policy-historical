FAQ

Why Do you need publishing rights, how do you plan to use them to post on my behalf to my friends ?

We Post message on behalf only to those friends who recently played any game supported by us. So your friends of friends will be here too for exchanging items links which ultimately benefits you. For Example: You have 100 friends, and our system detected that 10 of your friends played game recently, then we post message on your behalf to those 10 only. After this 10 of your friends post to their 100's of friends who played game and so on.. which ultimately results in lots Unlimited Number of links.
---> 1 you + 10 your friends + 100's of their friends..... = Lots of Unlimited Links.


Are there any repeated posts?

We have Changed our posting system strictly to post ones no matter who posted. Message can be posted on your and your friend's wall for once only.


I don't need this posting feature. How can i disable it?. I just want to exchange Links and Posts?

We Strongly encourage you to don't disable it. because everyone should contribute to help this exchanging system running continously. But still if you want to do it then. Follow below Steps:

  • Click Top right corner""Arrow" in http://facebook.com
  • Click "Account Settings"
  • In Left Menu Click "Apps"
  • Find the Application name "Game Link Exchange"
  • Click Edit
  • Right Side of "Post on your Behalf" you see a cross click cross to disable posting on your behalf permanently.
