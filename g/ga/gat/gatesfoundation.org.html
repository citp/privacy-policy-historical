Privacy Policy and Copyright

The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation is committed to  protecting the privacy of its visitors. Information that our visitors  provide or that is derived from internal Web tracking is not sold, rented or shared with any individual or organization.
    

Personal Information
The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation collects personal information upon a visitor's request to subscribe to newsletters, announcements (press releases) and multimedia stories or as a part of applying for a job. The information provided is not sold, rented or shared with any individual or organization.

 
Cookies
The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation currently uses cookie technology to analyze site traffic and use. Information gathered is solely used to assist in improving Web site design and function.  This Web site is functional without the retention of cookies. You may elect to block cookies from this site through use of your browser settings. 
 
The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation reserves the right to change and update the way in which visitor information is collected.
 
If you have any further questions about our privacy policy, please send your feedback to
feedback@gatesfoundation.org.
