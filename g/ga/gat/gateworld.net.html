GateWorld is a fan site devoted to exploring and enhancing the science fiction fandom experience. At no time is personal information collected from users of this site.

The forums are currently hosted by Delphi Forums, and are subject to Delphi's privacy policy and terms of service.

GateWorld is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. The site does not use cookies, and does not track the browsing habits of individual users.

Current U.S. law requires that Web sites may not collect information of any kind -- including an e-mail address -- from children under 13 years old without their parent's consent. If you're under 13, be sure to get your parent's permission before e-mailing us, submitting a letter to the editor, signing up for the newsletter, or registering for the forums.
