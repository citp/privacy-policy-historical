Thank you for visiting our Privacy Policy. This policy applies to information collected on gandi.net and all affiliated websites that link to
this Policy (collectively referred to herein as the "Sites", “Gandi”, "we" or "us").
Gandi is strongly committed to protecting the privacy of those who entrust us with their personal information. Visitors to our Sites, our
customers and all those who do business with us trust and expect that we will protect their personal information. We want you to know
that we take the privacy, security and confidentiality of your information, including any personally identifiable information ("Personal
Data" or "PII") extremely seriously. Accordingly, we take all commercially practicable and available precautions to ensure the security
and confidentiality of Personal Data.
We also want you to know what information we collect, why and how we will treat it once collected. Finally, we want you to be able to
have control over your Personal Data so that if you need to update or to correct your information or want to opt-out of receiving certain
communications, you can do that easily. In short, this Privacy Policy is for you. Please read it carefully.
