# Privacy Policy

[](http://gaydia.in/ "Gaydia")

Gaydia provides superior privacy protection. As part of our commitment to maintaining your privacy, we have adopted this Privacy Policy.

**How Gaydia Protects Your Privacy**  
Unlike other services, Gaydia does not store personally identifiable information. In other words, you are 

_anonymous_

to us and third parties. On Gaydia, you are entirely in control of how much information you share with others. 

**Information Sharing and Disclosure**  
The information you provide us with will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which the information was collected or as required by law. We do not share your information with third parties except as necessary to carry out our business or as required by law or other legal processes. Our operations are governed by the laws of England and our servers are based in the United States. Homosexuality is not prosecuted under the laws of England or that of the United States. By submitting data through Gaydia electronically, you are providing explicit consent to trans-border transmission of data collected on Gaydia.
