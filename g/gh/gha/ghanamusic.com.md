# Ghana Music.com | Privacy Policy

Ghana Music.com® is a trademark of Ghana Music.com, an affiliate of MiPROMO Company Limited. This trademark together with any other owned by Ghana Music.com, may not be reproduced, translated, adapted, and used of distributed without prior written permission. Ghana Music.com is committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users.

**User Information**  
Ghana Music.com collects personally identifiable information about you, such as your name, address and telephone number. Ghana Music.com does obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information such as the Internet Service Provider you use to connect to the Internet, your browser type, or the type of computer operating system you use (Macintosh or Windows, for example). This information is used to improve the website by - for example - analyzing which pages are popular and which pages need to be improved. The collected information is not sold to third-parties.

**Cookies**  
Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive. Ghana Music.com uses cookies to recognize when you return to our website. Cookies also enable us to gain information about the use of our services and to enhance our site according to the preferences of our users. Some of our ad partners may use cookies to ensure that visitors are not repeatedly sent the same banner & video ads. For information on our ad partners privacy policies please read the Ad Server section of this policy. Of course, you can change your browser settings and use it without cookies.

**Ad Servers**  
With respect to Ad Servers: To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our site. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and click stream information.

We use Google to serve ads when you visit our website. Google may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by Google & Google certified ad networks please [click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).

We also use other third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. For further information, consult the privacy policies of: http://www.kontera.com, http://www.clickafrik.com, http://www.clickbooth.com, http://www.tremormedia.com, http://www.adbutler.com, http://www.caselmedia.com & http://www.linkshare.com. **** By using Ghana Music.com you have your consent for some data to be collected for processing.

This is the web site of Ghana Music.com. Ghana Music.com can be reached via e-mail at info@ghanamusic.com. If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above email address.
