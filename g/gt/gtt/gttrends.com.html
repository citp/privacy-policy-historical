Privacy Policy

Credit Card Information and Security

Our goal is your save and secure online booking experience. For this reason We DO NOT Store Your Credit Card Information. You will be asked to enter this information each time you use the various online booking engines and reservation systems. The individual booking engines and reservation systems have their own encrypted credit card data transmission procedures. For more information, read their security statements.

Online Privacy Statement

GT Trends, Inc. and the Sponsors of this website respect your privacy, therefore we do not sell, rent, give away, loan, modify, copy, distribute, transmit, license, or redistribute any of your personal profile information and have no intention of doing so in the future. We do not collect any personal information about you except when you provide us that information. Any information you give us is held with the utmost care and security, and will not be used in ways to which you have not consented. When you make a reservation online, you will be asked for your name, address, phone number, email address and credit card information. This information is shared with the suppliers that you have chosen. We will only share information with the suppliers that is necessary to process your reservation.

When you purchase products from GT Trends, Inc. and the Sponsors of this website or use our services, we place you on our in house list to receive email regarding updates about special offers, "hot deals", and new services. We hope you will find these updates interesting and informative. If you would rather not receive them, please "update your profile" by "Clicking No" on the "Send Me A Newsletter" button.

Cookies

We do not use cookies to gather or store information about you. We do set a cookie in order to aid you in logging in to the site on subsequent visits by detecting if you have signed up for our mailing list or are part of one of our membership programs.

Summary

GT Trends, Inc. and the Sponsors of this website take the issue of privacy very seriously. We never provide your personal information to other companies. This includes your e-mail address, name, home address or other personal information. Your trust is what we value most.

Thank you for the privilege of serving you.
