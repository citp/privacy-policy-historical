# Garena

  | 

Here at Garena and all its related services including Garena, Garena websites, forums and others, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes the information we gather from the users, what we do with it, and the safeguards we have in place to protect it.

Garena and its services collect information required for the user to access on the different services provided. The information asked may vary but will typically include the user’s preferred account name; the account’s password; e-mail address; and location. Other information is optional for the users to fill up. 

Garena may use your information to provide you with services and information you have requested (e.g. Announcements, e-mail newsletter, etc) and to resolve issues and troubleshoot problems. Garena also may use your information to respond to specific inquiries made and/or authorized by you. Also, we may use your information, particularly location, for analysis purposes on how to improve our services.

Garena will not disclose user's registration information and any other information given by the user when using any of its related services to the public or any third party groups, except on the following conditions:  
(a) Authorization from user;  
(b) Subject to correlative laws;  
(c) Subject to correlative government administration;  
(d) For maintenance of the society and public benefits;  
(e) For maintenance of Garena legal benefits.

Garena may share some information with third parties we have engaged to perform certain services in connection with the operation of certain aspects of Garena, including to customize, measure and improve our services, content and layout. Except with respect to the third party sections, these third party service providers who have access to your information are authorized to use the information only in a manner consistent with this policy.

Garena has security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under its control. The servers for Garena that contain personally identifiable information are stored in a physically secure and climate controlled off-site facility. Beyond the physical protection of the servers, we protect against data loss by backing up data, performing consistency checks, and restricting access to information on our servers.
