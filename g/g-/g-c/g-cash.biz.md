# Privacy Policy

Although we collect some basic information during the signup process, this information is used only on this site and is used strictly to provide our affiliates with the information they need to participate in our affiliate program. 

Email addresses are kept private and will only be used to communicate with those who do not opt out of receiving information regarding G-CASH program and it's sites. 

Email addresses will not be sold or given to any third party without the consent of the owners of the addresses. If you have further questions regarding your privacy, please contact us through our contact & support page.
