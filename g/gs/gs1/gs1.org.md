# | GS1 - The global language of business

GS1 gives paramount importance to adopting and implementing measures to ensure the privacy of users of this web site.

When you visit our website, we do not keep track of who you are. Your IP address (Internet address of your computer) will be used only to assist us in measuring traffic to our web site and to enable us to improve the quality of service we offer you via this website.

We will collect personal information about you only with your consent when you:

  * Reply to Job Opportunities announced on this site
  * Order our publications 
  * Download our publications 
  * Send a query to our help desk 
  * Register for an GS1 training event (members only) 
  * Update your address details (members only)



When you choose to provide personal information to GS1 for any of the above reasons, we will take all the necessary steps and use the best technology to protect your personal data. GS1 will not give away or sell your personal data.

This site contains links to other websites. GS1 is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.

If you have any questions regarding our Privacy Policy or if you would like to correct or update your personal information provided to GS1 on our website, please contact us at[ contactus@gs1.org](mailto:contactus@gs1.org).
