# Privacy Statement

This policy discloses the privacy guidelines of GS1 US for this GS1 US website.

1009 Lenox Drive, Suite 202, Lawrenceville NJ 08648 USA

We can be reached via email at info@gs1us.org or by telephone at +1 937.435.3870.

**Information Gathered**  
User information and site activity data is collected throughout the website, including domains, browsers, and pages accessed. This information is used for standard reporting purposes and to improve the site content and display. Information required for the purchase and delivery of GS1 US products and services, to allow GS1 US to contact customers when necessary, to tailor the user experience, and to protect GS1 US from fraud, may be collected throughout the site. Names, shipping addresses, billing addresses, credit card numbers and expiration dates, email addresses, and voice and fax telephone number are among the information collected.

Some GS1 US websites have restricted the use of certain content to registered users of the site. To become a registered user, send a request to GS1 US at the above contact information. A GS1 US representative will assist you in setting up an account to become a registered user.

**Use of Information**  
The information collected is used for internal review and consideration, to confirm that you are authorized to place orders on the site, to track any orders you place and to otherwise support your customer relationship with GS1 US. GS1 US may disclose information in situations where we reasonably believe that it is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal proceedings against someone who is misusing the site, using the site without proper authorization, is interfering with the rights of GS1 US or when GS1 US believes that the law requires such disclosure. In addition, the information will be used to verify your compliance with the GS1 US website Terms of Use.

The information we collect is used for internal review and consideration. It may be used to improve the content of our site,.

Information may at times be shared with GS1 US partners and other third parties. This information includes demographic information, domain names, and other site-traffic information but this does not include personally identifiable information.

**Use of Cookies**  
A cookie is a small data file that certain websites write to your hard drive when you visit them. A cookie file can contain information such as a user ID that the site uses to track the pages you've visited, but the only personal information a cookie can contain is information you supply yourself. This enables GS1 US to improve its website to serve you better. You may choose to not receive cookies from the GS1 US website by setting your browser to notify you before accepting cookies. You than have the option of rejecting the cookie. You may also set your browser to reject all cookies. If you choose to reject a cookie it may slow down your interaction with the website.

**Links to Third Party Sites  
**The GS1 US website may contain links that provide you with access to Internet sites and services that are maintained by third parties. Those sites may have privacy and security policies that are different from those followed by GS1 US.

GS1 US has no control over those sites and is not responsible or liable for the policies and practices followed by such third parties. It is always a good idea to check the privacy policy and terms of use of any site before offering any of your personal information.

**Security  
**While GS1 US tries to protect the information you provide us, we can not ensure the secrecy of any information that you transmit to us. You transmit information to GS1 US at your own risk.

Your purchase transactions with us are secure. They will always be encrypted even when you may think they are not. No one will be able to see your credit card number from our site. We can also assure you that we will not use or sell the information that you place on the order form.
