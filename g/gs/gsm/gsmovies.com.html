This privacy policy is intended to inform you about the types of information gathered by GS Movies when you visit GSMovies.com and/or purchase a GSMovies.com membership, how we use and safeguard that information, and the degree to which you may control the maintenance and sharing of your information.


Information Collection

When you visit GSMovies.com, we will not collect any personally identifiable information about you unless you choose to provide such information to us.


If you choose to purchase a GSMovies.com membership, we will ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information such as your name and email address, as well as credit card or other payment information and a billing address. We use this information as required to provide you with your GSMovies.com membership.


We do not directly collect or store any personal financial information provided in connection with your purchase of GSMovies.com membership. All personal financial information required to complete your purchase (such as your credit card number) will be provided to and/or collected by a third party vendor who will process payments and complete transactions.


Log Files

We may automatically and through third-party tracking services (e.g., Google Analytics) gather certain non-personally identifiable information about your use of GSMovies.com and store it in log files. This information may include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamps, and related data. We use this information, which does not identify individual users, solely to improve the quality of our services. Out of respect for your privacy, we do not link this automatically-collected data to personally identifiable information.



COOKIES


A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user's computer for record-keeping purposes. We use both session ID cookies and tracking cookies. We use session cookies to make it easier for you to use GSMovies.com. A session ID cookie expires when you close your browser. We use tracking cookies to better understand how you use GSMovies.com, and to enhance your user experience. A tracking cookie remains on your hard drive for an extended period of time. You are free to decline cookies, but by doing so, you may not be able to take full advantage of all of our offerings. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while using GSMovies.com.
