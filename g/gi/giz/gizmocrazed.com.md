# Privacy Policy | Gizmocrazed - Future Technology News

**Introduction**

GizmoCrazed is fully committed to ensure that your privacy is protected. We only gather reports regarding various happenings across the web. We in no way support any particular happening, research or comments regarding any issue. Our aim is only to bring the news to everyone. Posts that contain Racism, Religious discrimination, criticizing a certain country, school of thought, region are strictly forbidden here.

**Copyrights**

Supreme caution has been taken to make sure that there are no copyright infringements in terms of the multimedia content and the subject matter that is posted in this blog. In case we publish content, images or any other item that belongs to any other publisher, individual, we reference it properly. In case you feel this rule has been overlooked, let us know the case with proof and we will reference/remove it as per requirement.

**Cookies**

We also use third party advertisements on **GizmoCrazed.com** to support our site. Some of these advertisers may use technology such as cookies and web beacons when they advertise on our site. Google, through their Adsense Program, uses cookies to serve ads on this site. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to GizmoCrazed.com users based on their visit to this and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).

If you feel any of the above mentioned offenses are contained within the post, [contact us here](https://www.gizmocrazed.com/contact/).

**What information does your company collect from users or website visitors?**

We use Google Analytics (A third party) to collect information about our visitors which equips us with information on how our site is utilized. We also have a double-opt in newsletter that only shares blog posts from our site. We do not sell your information, nor do we send marketing materials to your email address aside from Gizmocrazed sponsored posts.

**Does the business collect personal or sensitive data?**

Aside from opting in with your email address to our newsletter, no.

**How will your data be used: currently or possibly in the future?**

If you have opted-in to our newsletter then we send you our current blog posts which might include sponsored posts. We don’t plan to change this in the future.

**Who is your data shared with?**

We don’t sell your information, period.
