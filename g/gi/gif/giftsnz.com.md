**Privacy Policy**

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We will only use the information that we collect about you lawfully (in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998). 

We collect information about you for 2 reasons: firstly, to process your order and second, to provide you with the best possible service. 

We will not e-mail you in the future unless you have given us your consent. 

We will not give you any marketing emails from us or from another traders in the future. 

The type of information we will collect about you includes: 

your name   
address   
phone number   
email address   
credit/debit card details 

We will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent. 

The information we hold will be accurate and up to date. You can check the information that we hold about you by emailing us. If you find any inaccuracies we will delete or correct it promptly. 

The personal information which we hold will be held securely in accordance with our internal security policy and the law. 

If we intend to transfer your information outside the EEA (European Economic Area) we will always obtain your consent first. 

If you have any questions/comments about privacy, you should contact us. 

**Our Returns Policy - Cancellation**

Cancelations can be made any time before delivery by email: [info@giftsnz.com](mailto:info@giftsnz.com)

Within 14 days of delivery of a shipment, you may return any item you purchased from the GiftsNZ.com store, for any reason, for a full refund of the items purchase price, provided:

> * The product is undamaged and in original condition.  
>  * The original receipt slip is included with the item.  
>  * The item includes all original packaging and associated parts or accessories.  
>  *21st keys and Carvings that have been engraved will not be refunded at all. 

We cannot accept returns of opened items, damaged or soiled items, or items returned (received by us) more than 14 days after delivery.

The shipping cost of returned items is the responsibility of the purchaser, unless the return is a result of our error. The original shipping cost of returned items will not be refunded, unless the return is again the result of our error.

Before sending any return please contact us!
