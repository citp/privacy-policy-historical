Cookies

A “Cookie” is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive.

We use session Cookies (which expire once you close your web browser), but not persistent Cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide our services to you, and to advertise to you on our Site or elsewhere, based on your prior visits to our Site. You can set your browser to deny Cookies, but this will not allow you to use the Site, or our products or services.

Web Beacons

A “Web Beacon” is an electronic image, called a single-pixel (1×1) or clear GIF that recognizes certain types of information on your computer, such as your cookie number, time and date of a page view and description of the visited page. We may use “Web Beacons” through the Site or html-formatted emails in order to compile aggregate statistics about Site usage. You may render some Web Beacons inactive by rejecting their associated Cookies.
