**~ ~ Privacy Policy ~** ~ **  
Your Privacy is Protected !!!**

**Personal Information**

Your privacy is critically important to GreenDIYenergy.com, and if you give us your name, email address, etc., your information will not be made public in any way.  You can unsubscribe from any communications from us at any time by clicking the “unsubscribe” link found at the bottom of every automated email we send.

Your privacy is critically important to GreenDIYenergy.com and if you choose to stay in touch with us by giving your name, email address, etc your information will not be made public in any way.  You can unsubscribe from any communications from us at anytime by clicking the “unsubscribe” link found at the bottom of every automated email we send.

GreenDIYenergy.com has put  security measures in place to protect your information.  Any personal information you give is securely held by iContact, one of the industry leaders in database security. 

Any information that you provide will never be sold, shared, exchanged, rented, transferred or given to any other 3rd party company or individuals for any reason now or in the future. We hate spam as much as you do, and we will protect your information to the fullest extent of the law. 

**Cookies**

Cookies are used to record shopping cart information. This is done through Clickbank, the World’s Largest Digital Retailer and is used for only for the ordering process. 

**Analytic Services**

This site may use ClickTale (or similar web analytic services). ClickTale may record mouse clicks, mouse movements, scrolling activity as well as any text you type in this website. ClickTale does not collect personally identifiable information that you do not voluntarily enter in this website. ClickTale does not track your browsing habits across web sites which do not use ClickTale services. For more information see [Privacy Policy for Information Collected by the ClickTale Web Analytics Service](http://www.clicktale.com/privacy_service.aspx). We are using the information collected by ClickTale service to study how visitors interact with the website and to better improve the users experience.  
You can choose to disable the Service at <http://www.clicktale.net/disable.html>. 

**Payment Information**

All payments are processed through Clickbank, the world’s largest digital retailer.  ClickBank shopping cart is used, GreenDIYenergy.com never sees or has access to credit card information; this is all handled through Clickbank which uses the most advanced SSL Encrypted Security technology available so your information is always secure. See the “Security” link for more details. ****

**Your Consent**

By viewing, visiting, using or interacting with this website, or any other pop-up or advertisement on this website, you are agreeing with and consent to our Privacy Policy. We reserve the right to change and update our Privacy Policy at any time, with or without notice. From time to time, this policy will be updated based upon new technologies, business practices, and our customers’ needs. However, if the Privacy Policy does change, we assure you that it will not change in any way that’s not consistent with our initial policy.
