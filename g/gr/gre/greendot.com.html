COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

  • When you request certain services using our web site, such as registering as a Member or activating a credit card for Membership, we will ask you to provide personal information about yourself. This information will include name, mailing address, e-mail address, telephone number, credit card number, credit card type, and expiration date. This information is necessary to your receiving and/or being billed for the services you request, and you will not be able to use such services without providing this information.

  • Information such as prior purchasing history, age, birth date, marital status, household information and any other personal information are optional. We will collect information such as this only when knowingly provided by you. We utilize this information in order to provide and improve our service to you.

  • When you visit our web site, we will automatically collect information regarding your use. Currently, this is done using "Cookies" - small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive and relayed back to our servers. We use both Session and Persistent Cookies. Session Cookies track your progression through our Web site in a single visit and are deleted as soon as you close your browser. Persistent Cookies enable such functions as storing and retrieving passwords, user name, geographic location and identifying you as a prior visitor, Member or customer of our Web site. Persistent Cookies remain on your computer until your erase them.

  • Cookies do not retrieve personal information unless you have previously provided it to us. Most browsers can be set to refuse Cookies, but refusing Cookies may affect certain functionality and prohibit your usage of services on this Web site.

USE AND DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION

  • We use the information we collect from you in order to process payment of your Membership, to manage and operate the Green.com™ membership program, including but not limited to identifying purchases made by you at participating merchants and to credit your activated credit card. This information may also be used by us to improve our marketing efforts and services, analyze site usage and customize our Web site's content and layout. We also may use this information to notify you of special offers, discounts, and new products and services via e-mail and other on and offline communications.

  • When you provide this information to us you are given the choice as to whether you would like to receive information regarding special offers, discounts and new products. If you receive an e-mail from Entertainment it will contain a link allowing you to opt out from receiving future e-mails from us. If you do not want to receive these communications from us, you may also change your e-mail preferences by logging on to the Green.com™ Web site and clicking on the "E-mail Preferences" link in the "My Account" section.

  • We may collect, create and disclose to third parties aggregate anonymous statistics regarding our customers, sales, traffic analyses and other information regarding or collected through our Web site. Other than in this anonymous form, we will not disclose your personal information without first obtaining your permission except as follows:

    • We may disclose personal information if required by law or legal process, and if we believe it is necessary to do so to protect the property and personal safety of us, visitors to our Web site or other third parties.

    • We may disclose your personal information to third parties providing, or assisting us in providing, services requested by you, or for billing and collection purposes (including but not limited to credit card issuers or credit card processors).

    • We may disclose your contact information, consisting of your name, mailing address and/or e-mail address as well as your computer's unique IP address (if any) to affiliates of Entertainment (such as Expedia, Ticketmaster, HSN, Hotels.com, Citysearch, Match.com).

    • By using our Web site, and providing us with information, you are consenting to our collection and the above uses of your information. You may request to have all of your personal information collected by us removed from our database by submitting a request to Customer Service, identifying the subject as "Privacy", and, unless we are prohibited by law, we will remove such information within a 3-month period.

    • We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads on our behalf. These companies may employ cookies and action tags (also known as single pixel gifs or web beacons) to measure advertising effectiveness. Any information that these third parties collect via cookies and action tags is completely anonymous.
