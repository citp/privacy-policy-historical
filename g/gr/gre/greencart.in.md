## **Collection of Personally Identifiable Information**

We collect personally identifiable information (email address, name, phone number, delivery address and birth date.) from you when you set up a free account with Greencart.in. While you can browse some sections of our site without being a registered member, certain activities (such as placing an order) do require registration.  
From time to time we may add or enhance services available on Greencart.in site. To the extent these services are provided, and used by you, we will use the information you provide to facilitate the service requested. For example, if you email us or call upon us with a question we will use your email address, name, nature of the question, etc. to respond to your question. We may also store such information to assist us in making the site the better and easier to use.

## **Safeguard to protect your personal information**

Our site has stringent security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. Whenever you change or access your account information, we offer the use of a secure server. Once your information is in our possession we adhere to strict security guidelines, protecting it against unauthorized access. ** ** All data on the Greencart.in site is Comodo positive SSL secured. Further all the payments on the Greencart.in site is Verisign SSL secured. You may notice that through the use of "https:" instead of "http:" url for [_https://www.Greencart.in_](https://web.archive.org/web/20141124172757id_/http%3A//www.greencart.in/shop/index.php) .

This means all personal information you provide to Greencart.in is transmitted using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption. SSL is a proven coding system that lets your browser automatically encrypt, or scramble, data before you send it to us. 

Of course, you are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your passwords, and your Greencart.in site membership account information. Please be very careful, responsible, and alert with this information, especially whenever you are online.

## **Optional Online Surveys**

Greencart.in values opinions and comments from members, so we frequently conduct online surveys. Participation in these surveys is entirely optional. Typically, the information is aggregated, and used to tailor your experience at our site, providing you with content that we think you might be interested in--and to display content according to your preferences. 

## **Automatic Logging of Session Data**

We automatically log generic information about your computer connection to the Internet, which we call "session data", that is anonymous and not linked to any personal information. Session data consists of things such as IP address, operating system and type of browser software being used and the activities conducted by the user while on Greencart.in site. An IP address is a number that lets computers attached to the internet, such as our web servers, know where to send data back to the user, such as the pages of the site the user wishes to view. 

We identify and use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our website. Your IP address is also used to help identify you and to gather broad demographic information.

## **Cookies**

A "cookie" is a small piece of information sent by a Web server to a Web browser, which enables to collect information from the browser. We also use cookies to allow you to enter your password less frequently during a session. Cookies can also help us provide information that is targeted to your interests. You are always free to decline our cookies if your browser permits, although in that case you may not be able to use certain features on the Site and you may be required to re-enter your password more frequently during a session.

Greencart.in places both permanent and temporary cookies in your computer hard drive. Greencart.in cookies do not contain any of your personally identifiable information.
