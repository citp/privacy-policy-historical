Great Scores Privacy Policy

Great Scores Ltd is committed to protecting the privacy of all visitors to our websites.

Great Scores will not use your personal data in such a way as to breach the Data Protection Act or any other relevant UK legislation.

We collect very little information about visitors to our websites.

In practice, every time you view a web page, we may store logs which show your IP address (the Internet number which your machine uses when it is connected to the Internet) or the IP address of any proxy you use. In addition we may store other information your Web browser passes to us including what you looked at, whether the page request was successful or not, and which browser you used to view the pages.

This data is used strictly for statistical purposes only. This tells us

  • the number of page hits and requests
  • pages of particular interest
  • which pages are requested that no longer exist.


This policy may change from time to time, without notice.
