# GreatSampleResume.com - Privacy Policy

[Home](https://web.archive.org/) Â» Privacy Policy 

  


Thanks for visiting greatsampleresume.com. We are very much concerned about your privacy protection while providing you with useful information. Our privacy policy describes everything about our privacy practices like how we treat the information you provided when using or interacting with our site. 

**Information We Collect** In our website we may collect and process the following data about you. 

  * Details of your visits to our website and the resources that you access, including, traffic data, location data, web logs and other communication data. 
  * Information that would allow a party to contact you. For example, your full name, address, telephone number or email address. 



We use your submitted information to send you e-mails about your requests, to meet our contractual commitments to you, for administration purpose and to notify you about any changes to our website. We use your personal information only for the specific submitted purpose and your information will be deleted when you remove/unsubscribe from our services. We do not share this information with outside parties. 

**Browsing Information** Our servers may collect information indirectly and automatically through, IP address about your activities while visiting the site. This information is used for internal purposes such as to help understand how the site is being used and to improve it. We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website, or that Y number of men and Z number of women filled out our registration form, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals. 

**Third Party Links** You may find links to third party websites on our website. These websites should have their own privacy policies and we do not accept any responsibility or liability for their policies whatsoever as we have no control over them. 

**Third Party Cookies** We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. 

**DoubleClick DART Cookie** We use Google, as a third party vendor, who uses cookies to serve ads on greatsampleresumes.com. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to greatsampleresumes.com and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL - <http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html>

**Security**

We implement and follow industry standard measures to protect against unauthorized access and unlawful interception or processing of Personal Information. However, no internet site can fully eliminate security risks.

We hope this policy clarifies our procedures regarding your Personal Information. We may amend this policy at any time by sending the amended policy to the email address you provided to us or by posting the amended policy on our site.
