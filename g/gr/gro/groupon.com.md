# Legal

_Please read our full[Terms of Service](http://www.groupon.com/doc/terms) (Which includes the [Terms of Use](http://www.groupon.com/doc/terms#tou) and [Terms of Sale](http://www.groupon.com/doc/terms#tos)) and [Privacy Policy](http://www.groupon.thepoint.com/doc/privacy). The most commonly requested information is summarized below._

## Your information

### What will you do with my email address?

Not too much. You'll receive our daily Groupon offering in your inbox, and occasional alerts relating to your purchases. We take your personal information very seriously – our focus is to use your e-mail to inform you of our great deals every day. Our privacy policy gives the full story – please take a look.

### What will you do with my credit card and billing information?

This info is recorded via a Secure Socket Layer connection (SSL) and is fully encrypted. This and your other personal information is safe and secure and will only be used for your intended purpose.

## Orders

### Can I return a Groupon?

Returns are handled on a case-by-case basis. Within the limits of reason, we'll do what it takes to make you happy. Unless there is something wrong with the purchase, we are generally unable to offer refunds after one week from the purchase date.

If you'd like to discuss returning a Groupon, [email](mailto:support@groupon.com) or call us: (877) 788-7858.

### What if the business for my Groupon closes down?

If anything happens that makes it impossible for you to redeem your Groupon, we'll make sure you get your money back.
