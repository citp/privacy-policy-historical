# Privacy Policy - Griffin's Honey Blog

Your privacy is extremely important to  **Griffin’s Honey Blog**  and we are committed to protecting your information.  It is our policy that we will never sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties.

Your email address is required when you comment on a post; however, you are guaranteed that it will never be sold or and we will not share your information.  If you are the winner of a giveaway your information will be sent out to our sponsor, so you are able to collect your prize.  When we ask for your address to receive a prize it will not be shared with anyone other than the sponsor of the giveaway. Please be assure that we take every precaution to secure this very sensitive information; however, we cannot be held responsible for any breach of security due to the actions of third parties.  

At our discretion, we may include or offer third party products or services on our website.  This site does contain Google Ads along with other third party vendors. There are links to third party websites and we are not responsible for the privacy policies they have in place.   **Griffin’s Honey Blog is** also an affiliate of many products, banners, or links seen on the blog.  We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of the companies that may be linked to our blog. 

Please note that by accessing [http://www.griffinshoney.com](http://www.griffinshoney.com/), you give us permission to collect and share the information as described in this document.  

Should you have any concerns please contact Griffin’s Honey Blog at  **griffinshoney@yahoo.com.**
