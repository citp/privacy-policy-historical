# Goshen College | Public Web Site Privacy and Copyright Policy

## Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy is to collect no personally identifiable information about visitors to our Web sites unless they affirmatively choose to make such information available to us. However, our web servers (like all web servers) do automatically recognize some non-personal information such as volume and timing of access as well as the Internet domain and IP address from which visitors accessed our sites. We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. 

If visitors choose to share personal information with us, we will not sell that information to commercial entities. Please go to [http://www.goshen.edu/registrar/privacy](https://web.archive.org/registrar/privacy) to read more about the Goshen College privacy policy.

### Policy Changes

Occasional changes may be made to these policies as content and new services are added to our site. Visitors are encouraged to check this document periodically to stay informed of current privacy and security guidelines.

### Acceptance of Terms

By using any of the Goshen College web site(s), visitors signify their acceptance of our privacy policy. If a visitor does not agree to this policy, we request that the visitor not use our sites. Continued use of any Goshen College site following the posting of changes to these terms will indicate acceptance of those changes. 

## Website Content and Copyright Information

### Website Content

The Goshen College Web pages are maintained by many departments and organizations across campus and are regularly updated and improved. As it is available, new content is added. Although we attempt to keep information in the Goshen College Web accurate, you assume the risk that the information and materials on this Web site could be incomplete, inaccurate, out of date, or may not meet your needs and requirements.

The personal employee, student, and student group home pages maintained on the Goshen College Web servers are the sole responsibility of the users who make information publicly available on the Web. The personal home pages and the opinions or information contained therein, as well as any pages linked from the Goshen College Web, are the creation of the person or persons maintaining the pages and do not necessarily represent the opinions of Goshen College.

With respect to any information available from this Goshen College Web site, neither Goshen College nor its employees assumes any legal liability or responsibility for the accuracy, completeness, or usefulness of any information, apparatus, product, or process disclosed, or represents that its use would not infringe privately owned rights.

### Copyright Information

Most graphics, images, and text, and all Goshen College-related seals, logos, and wordmarks are protected by trademark or copyright. They may not be reproduced in any electronic or written medium without the prior consent of Goshen College.

Please visit our "Technology and Copyright at Goshen College" ([http://www.goshen.edu/its/policy/copyright/](https://web.archive.org/its/policy/copyright/)) page for more information about Goshen College's copyright policy. Any questions or concerns regarding copyrighted materials on the Goshen College Web site should be directed to [msherer@goshen.edu](mailto:msherer@goshen.edu).

If you have questions about our privacy or copyright policies, or have ideas about improving our policies, please feel free to e-mail us at [arachnid@goshen.edu](mailto:arachnid@goshen.edu).
