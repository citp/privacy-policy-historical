# Goby Privacy Policy - goby.com

Effective January 11, 2010

At goby we respect your privacy. The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to let you know what information we are collecting while you are on goby, how we are using it and with whom it is being shared. By using the goby site, you are agreeing to the collection and use of certain information as outlined below.

#### Personal Information - on the [goby.com](http://goby.com/) site

Information we collect: Our servers automatically receive and store certain information whenever you visit our website including your IP address, referring URL, browser type and the date/time of your visit. We also utilize cookies to enhance the web site experience and to understand how users are interacting with the goby site. A cookie is a small text file sent to your computer in order to uniquely identify your browser. We may use both session cookies, which expire once you close your browser, as well as persistent cookies, which stay on your computer until deleted.

How we use the information: We use the information that we collect to administer the goby site, diagnose system issues and enhance the website experience. We may use cookies to identify whether you have visited the site before, to remember any information you may have previously provided and to track your site usage in order to target search results and advertisements based on your interests. We may also merge information collected about you with that collected from other users in order to identify and analyze trends with which to aqcontinuously improve the goby website.

Information shared with third parties: Goby may use third party companies to serve advertisements on the goby site and may share information collected about your searches queries with these companies so that the ad-serving company can present you with the most relevant, targeted advertisements based on your interests.

In the course of serving advertisements, these third party ad-serving companies may place or recognize a cookie, or another tracking technology such as a pixel, on your computer. Please note that the goby Privacy Policy does not cover the use of information collected by any third-party ad-serving company. To learn more, or for information on how to limit the use of information collected by ad-serving companies, visit <http://www.networkadvertising.org/>

goby may also share user trends information, on an aggregate basis, with certain third parties including its advertisers and distribution partners.

Links to third party websites: This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected by [goby.com](http://goby.com/). As a search engine, goby contains links to third party websites which are not owned or controlled by 

goby. We are not responsible for the actions or privacy practices of any sites accessed from the [goby.com](http://goby.com/) site. We recommend that you read the privacy statements of each website that you access from [goby.com](http://goby.com/) which collects personally identifiable information.

#### Personal Information - email addresses

[goby.com](http://goby.com/) only sends solicited email, in compliance with CANSPAM. Email addresses are collected via the [goby.com](http://goby.com/) website, via goby mobile applications, and via third party marketing promotions. [goby.com](http://goby.com/) only collects email addresses from users who have explicitly given their consent via opt-in to receive mail from goby. goby is transparent and named at the point of address collection. 

goby uses email addresses collected to send goby users information about the goby site, goby content and goby features. For users who opt-in a second time, goby may send special promotional emails from goby partners. If you no longer wish to receive email from goby, you may opt-out of receiving them by following the instructions included in each email or by e-mailing us at [mail@goby.com](mailto:mail@goby.com).

The security of your personal information, including your e-mail address and zip code, is important to us. We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during registration and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100 percent secure, however. Therefore, though we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

If we decide to change our e-mail practices, we will post those changes to this privacy statement and notify opted in users via email, and all users via a notice on our homepage.

We reserve the right to modify this privacy statement at any time, so please review it frequently. If we make material changes to our e-mail practices, we will notify you here, by e-mail, and on our homepage.

#### Changes to Privacy Policy:

This Privacy Policy was last updated on January 11, 2010 .

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time, so please review it periodically to remain familiar with it. If we make significant, material changes to this policy we will notify you here, and via a messaging on the goby.com home page.
