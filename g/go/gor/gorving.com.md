# Go RVing - Privacy Statement

  
| 

  
Go RVing is committed to protecting your privacy. Any personal information that you provide to Go RVing should be done on a completely voluntary basis. We only use the information that you voluntarily provide us to process your orders and requests for additional information about purchasing, renting, owning and enjoying recreation vehicles. To fulfill this processing, it may be necessary to provide your information to prescreened third party businesses affiliated with the RV industry. These include RV manufacturers, RV dealers, campgrounds and other outdoor recreation entities. In addition, Go RVing may provide aggregate statistics about our web site visitors, traffic patterns and related site information to RV industry members. Your detailed personal information will not be released for such purposes.

Any personal information you provide directly, will be collected and stored by Go RVing and its electronic business service provider contractees. If you later change your mind and decide that you no longer want your personal information made available to Go RVing, please contact Margie Spence at (703) 620-6003 ext. 357 or via email at [mspence@rvia.org](mailto:mspence@rvia.org). Also, please understand that once Go RVing has provided your information to third party business affiliates, it can no longer exercise control over subsequent users. In the event that you later decide that you do not want to receive any additional information from a particular business entity, it will be your responsibility to contact that entity and request your removal from its mailing list.

Please keep in mind that whenever you provide personal information online, there is a risk that it may be collected and used by people.
