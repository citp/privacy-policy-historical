# Welcome to Gone Raw - a place to share and discuss vegan, raw food recipes!

We respect your privacy. Your personally identifiable information will only be used in the context of your member account on goneraw.com. Any email addresses collected will only be used to send occasional news and updates about the Gone Raw service.

Information will only be shared if required by law or through an acquisition or merger with another company. In the case of the latter, you will be notified that your information is being transferred.

Although Needmore Designs, LLC owns the rights to the databases, data storage and Gone Raw application, your data is yours. We will not share your personal information with any third-parties, such as marketers.
