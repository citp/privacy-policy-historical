PRIVACY PROTECTION

We understand that the Internet presents an opportunity for us to reach you, the public, in new and exciting ways with information about our programming, content and other forms of interaction. Visitors to our Site can be confident that we respect user privacy. Specifically, we recognize the importance of protecting the privacy of personally identifiable information collected about our visitors. As a general policy, no personally identifiable information, such as name, address or e-mail address, is automatically collected from visitors to our site. It is voluntarily provided by the user upon registration. During the initial registration process, we collect the user’s email address, account username and password, and birthdate. Such information is only used internally within HealthTeacher and is not provided to any third parties. You should not use any personally identifiable information in your username, as it may be visible to others on the Site. If a user is under 18 years of age, we collect the email address of that user’s parent rather than the user’s email address. If the parent does not consent, or if the parent does not respond to the notice within ten (10) days from the date the notice was sent, HealthTeacher will delete the user’s contact information from its records.

When you delete your account, all of your information is permanently deleted. If you contact us by email, we use your email address only to respond to your request, then delete it.

Personally identifiable information about our visitors is collected only when knowingly and voluntarily submitted by those visitors.
