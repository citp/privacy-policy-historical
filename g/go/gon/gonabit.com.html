Privacy Policy

There's a lot of stuff to read on this page. The short version is that we'll always lean toward providing too much, not too little, protection of your info and your privacy. If you ever have any questions about what we do with your info and how we secure it, give us a shout.

 

GoNabit Customer Charter

Our users - Nabbers - are the backbone of our business. The strength of our business is built on the foundation of our commitment to protecting all user's personal data, and we take the responsibility of protecting this data very seriously. Therefore, we have formalized our commitment to our Nabbers in our Customer Charter.

 

1. The Right to Clear Data Usage Terms:

We will only collect and use your personal data as outlined on our website in our terms of use and privacy policy. In other words, we'll use your data to service your account and send you daily deal emails. We won't sell your data or use it inappropriately. Its our responsibility to protect against unauthorized access, destruction, use, or disclosure of your data.

2. The Right to Say 'Yes':

You decide if you want to receive our daily deal emails or not. You make this choice by subscribing to GoNabit. We don't make this choice for you. If you're receving our daily deal emails its because you subscribed, and not because we decided what was best.

3. The Right to Say 'No':

You may change your mind about receiving daily deal emails. If so, use the simple 'unsubscribe' link in every email we send or in your account on GoNabit. Otherwise, call us, tweet us, email us, visit us. We promise that one call, click, or tweet is all it takes.

4. The Right to Make Changes:

At any time you can access your GoNabit account and make changes to your personal information to correct it if necessary. Whatever changes you make is the information that we use, period.

5. The Right to Demand Proof:

If you think we're sending you daily deal emails without your permission, then call us on it. It's our responsibility to prove that you opted-in. This must be the case with every email subscriber list.
