# Goldenmine- Privacy Policy

  


  
At GoldenMine.com, our two-generation history in jewelry embodies the trust that has built our long-lasting relationship with customers. We take the utmost care to respect your privacy. We do not share or sell any of your private information to any third parties. We guarantee that any information you provide us will be kept confidential. Any user information we gather is used solely to improve your shopping experience. 

  
You do not have to register for an account to browse our shop. However, in order to shop, you must register for an account. All information you provide us is strictly confidential and used only to make shopping easier. 

  
You must register for an account to place an order. This information helps us streamline the ordering process and lets you track orders directly from our site. Some of your personal information like your name and address may be verified with the credit card bank to verify your order..

  
We use the internet standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption to protect your privacy and secure your transactions. SSL encrypts all your personal account information and secures your credit card information when ordering.
