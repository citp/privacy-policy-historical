Golden Eagle Coins may collect the following customer related information to facilitate the efficient processing of customer orders:

Name and address   
Telephone number   
Email address   
Credit/Debit card account number   
Credit/Debit card expiration date   
Name and address listed with card issuer   
Telephone number listed with card issuer   
Name of financial institution card issuer   
Telephone number of financial institution card issuer   
Opt-in or opt-out receipt of internal communications 

Customer Information: Information is collected at the time of a sale in order to complete the sale and deliver the products. Customer lists are not not sold or distributed to third parties.   
Credit Card Transactions: Credit card transactions are processed by Card Services International professional payment authorization service was chosen for added security and reliability. Only the purchase price and the credit card information provided by the customer is supplied to [Card Services International](http://www.cardservice.com/). Information collected during transactions is maintained in their database for accounting and billing purposes. Information from these transactions is not released to third parties.   
We are privileged to receive the information provided by our customers. We recognize the importance of personal privacy and we respect the trust and loyalty that our customers place in our business. In return, we guarantee that all customer related personal information will be used for internal business purposes only and we will not sell, lease, or exchange that information with parties, public or private, outside the company.
