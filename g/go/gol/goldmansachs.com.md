# Privacy Policy

  
Goldman Sachs is dedicated to the fundamental values of honesty, integrity and confidentiality in all of our relationships. Maintaining our clients' trust is a cornerstone of our success. We actively seek to protect the privacy and confidentiality of our client relationships. While obtaining accurate and timely information about our clients is critical to our ability to deliver the highest level of customer service, we also recognize the importance of protecting personal information. 

We will safeguard any personal information acquired by us in connection with your use of this Web site, by upholding the privacy principles and procedures set forth below. We will not use or distribute your personal information in any other way without notifying you in advance, via the provision of supplemental privacy policies and statements or otherwise, and giving you an adequate opportunity and means to respond. 

#### Use and Collection

> **We will only collect personal information from you in connection with your use of this site to facilitate your use of this site and the delivery of our services.**
> 
>   * The personal information we collect about you comes primarily from the information and materials you provide and your communications to us in connection with your use of this site. We may also collect information about your transactions and experiences as you utilize this site. We will use this personal information to confirm your identity, to process transaction and service requests, to assess the usage and performance of our on-line products and services, to service and administer any account you may have with us, to assist our sales and business representatives in understanding your needs and preferences, and to fulfill our legal and regulatory requirements.
>   * “Cookies” are small text files consisting of encrypted information assigned to a computer’s browser. Goldman Sachs may use cookies for administrative purposes, such as to maintain security on the site or to identify you so that you do not have to input your password multiple times as you navigate the site. The cookies do not collect or transmit your personal information.
> 


#### Security

> **Goldman Sachs is committed to being at the forefront of information security to protect the privacy and confidentiality of your personal information.**
> 
>   * Only authorized Goldman Sachs employees or agents will have access to your personal information. All of our employees, service providers and marketing partners are held to stringent standards of privacy and security and are bound by confidentiality agreements.
>   * Users of the non-public areas of this Web site will be required to identify and authenticate themselves prior to accessing our services. Generally, identification and authentication take place through the use of your user name and a password or with a SecurID Card® and Personal Identification Number (PIN).
>   * The non-public areas of this site are built upon a secure infrastructure with multiple layers of protection, including measures ranging from proper physical security of our machines to system intrusion detection capabilities. Within those non-public areas, Goldman Sachs makes use of industry-standard Secure Socket Layer and encryption technologies to protect your information from external compromise.
>   * Security is a cooperative effort between Goldman Sachs and the users of this site. Please remember that your password, SecurID Card and PIN are personal to you and should not be made available to any other person. Also, you should discontinue their use and advise us if you have any reason to suspect that someone else may be using them.
> 


#### Information Sharing

> **We will not sell your personal information.**
> 
> **In order to provide efficient and reliable services, and to improve product and service options available to our clients, Goldman Sachs may share your personal information with our affiliated companies or service providers around the world. In either instance, your personal information will remain subject to stringent confidentiality protections.**
> 
>   * We may share your information with our affiliated companies to facilitate the services provided through this site. The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc. and its subsidiaries adhere to applicable legal and industry standards regarding the protection of our clients' confidential information.
>   * To the extent that we may engage unaffiliated companies to assist in providing services on this site, those service providers will be subject to strict contractual requirements to maintain the confidentiality of any personal information that they may obtain in the performance of their services. Those service providers may use personal information only in the course of providing services to Goldman Sachs and only for the purposes that we dictate. In addition, we will make every effort to ensure that they receive only the minimum amount of personal information necessary and will be allowed to retain that information only for as long as required for the provision of these services.
> 


#### Disclosure

> **We may disclose information in order to cooperate with legal authorities or to protect our rights and interests and those of our business partners.**
> 
>   * We may, when and to the extent appropriate, disclose your personal information to law enforcement or regulatory authorities.
>   * We may also disclose your personal information as necessary to perform credit checks, enforce our legal rights, collect debts or otherwise protect our interests and property.
> 


#### Internet Links

> You should be aware that other Internet sites to which you link from this site might contain privacy provisions that differ from the provisions of this Privacy Policy. 

If your relationship with Goldman Sachs ends, Goldman Sachs will continue to treat your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. 

If this Privacy Policy changes in any way, you will be notified through this site or by other appropriate means. If you have any questions relating to this Privacy Policy, please contact your Goldman Sachs representative.
