# Privacy Policy | Gold Silver Reports

Our Privacy Policy

This website is administered by GoldSilverreports.com. We want you to feel secure when visiting our site and are committed to maintaining your privacy when doing so. The following provides an overview of how we protect your privacy during your visit to our site.

Information we collect

Information on goldsilverreports.com is gathered when you provide information across various pages on goldsilverreports.com.  
We collect information directly from you in a number of ways, some of which are described in this Privacy Statement. One way is through the use of cookies. Cookies are small files of information which save and retrieve information about your visit to goldsilverreports.com for example, how you entered our site, how you navigated through the site, and what information was of interest to you.

Is your Private Data Safe?  
As a commited organization, the data we collect will not be leaked outside our organization in any ways. In the event of a merger, consolidation, collaboration or reorganization involving www.goldsilverreports.com, your personal information will, in most instances, be transferred to the control of the third party that is part of that transaction. So, before you submit any information, we will notify you as to why we are asking for specific information and it is completely up to you whether or not you want to provide it.

Personal Data  
We are committed to protecting your personal information and do so with certain physical and technological procedures. However, we cannot ensure the security of the information as you transmit it to us, and so we urge you to take every precaution to protect your personal data when you are on the Internet. This includes using a secure browser like Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Sea Monkey, Netscape or Opera where applicable. We do not recommend Internet Explorer.
