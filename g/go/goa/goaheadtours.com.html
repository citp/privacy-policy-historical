Our Commitment to Privacy

Goaheadvacations.com is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. We would like to share our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. This notice is available at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested. This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the goaheadvacations.com website.

Go Ahead Vacations also participates in the Council of Better Business Bureaus' BBBOnLine® Reliability Program, and complies with all the BBBOnLine privacy standards. Further information about this program is available at http://www.bbbonline.org.

Collection of Personal Information

On our site, you can reserve and pay for your vacation with a credit card. Goaheadvacations.com maintains the confidentiality, security, and integrity of personal information that we obtain from our users. The personal information collected includes your name, address, email address, phone number, and credit/debit card information.

When you request a newsletter subscription we ask for your name, email address, and city. To send a digital postcard or tell a friend about a particular tour, we ask for the recipient's name and email address.

If you need to change any information you have given goaheadvacations.com, you can do so by calling our toll-free number 1-800-597-0350 or sending an email to goahead@ef.com.

The Way We Use Information

We use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order only to complete that order. We are required to collect this information in order to complete your travel documents. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete your reservation.

Personal information collected by goaheadvacations.com is stored in secure operating environments that are not available to the public. You should always be careful when distributing your personal information in this manner.

We use the information you provide about someone else only to confirm message delivery. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to confirm that message. Information you provide to us to create a message is only used for that purpose, and it is only disclosed to the recipient.

We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties.

You can request our catalog, as well as updates about our products and services. Information you submit via our website will not be used for these purposes unless you request them. You can subscribe to the Go Ahead Traveler, a bi -monthly e-Magazine designed especially for current or future Go Ahead customers. When you subscribe to the Go Ahead Traveler, you will also receive notice of special travel deals and promotions. You can unsubscribe at any time by visiting this page http://www.goaheadvacations.com/newsletter/unsubscribe.asp

Goaheadvacations.com may provide aggregate statistics about our customers, sales, traffic patterns, and related site information to reputable third-party vendors, but these statistics will include no personally identifying information.

Finally, we never use or share the personally identifying information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.

Our Commitment To Data Security

We exercise great care in providing secure transmission of our users' financial information from their computers to our servers by using encryption technology.

Who is collecting your information

When you request information on the Go Ahead site and are asked for personal information you are sharing that information with Go Ahead alone.

Please be aware that Go Ahead advertisers or Web sites to which our site has links may collect personally identifiable information about you. The information practices of those Web sites linked to Go Ahead are not covered by this privacy statement.

Use of "cookies"

"Cookies" are pieces of information that a website transfers to your computer's hard disk for record-keeping purposes. We use both session and persistent cookies. The use of cookies is an industry standard, used to provide useful features for customers. Cookies, by themselves, do not personally identify users, although they do identify a user's computer. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent that. However, you may not be able to take full advantage of our website if you do so. For example, the complete online reservation process can only be accessed if you accept cookies. Currently, we do not tie cookie usage to personally identifiable information.

Collection, use and distribution of your information

Go Ahead does not sell or rent user information to anyone.

If you opt in for the service, Go Ahead may send you email about products and services that we feel will be of interest to you. You may opt out of these mailings at any time by visiting this page: http://www.goaheadvacations.com/newsletter/unsubscribe.asp

You can also update or correct factual information about your account by emailing us at goahead@ef.com, or by calling 1-800-590-1170.

Your Consent
