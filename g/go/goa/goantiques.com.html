For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, "Personal Information" is any personally identifiable information about an individual person such as a person's name, physical address, email address and telephone number, and may include financial and other information provided by an individual who accesses our Site and/or uses our services.

For example, in order to register for a membership account through the Site (each a "Membership Account"), to sell items on the Site and to request information about products and services offered on our Site you must provide certain Personal Information.

Personal Information Provided Voluntarily by the User. When you register for a Membership Account you will be asked to provide Personal Information. You may also be asked to provide Personal Information when you participate in various services and offerings available through our Site, when you contact us via phone, mail, or otherwise, and when you participate in our sweepstakes, contests, surveys and related promotions. The Personal Information we may collect and store includes, but is not limited to, the following categories of information:

● your name, mailing address, email address, telephone number, year of birth, and gender, information concerning your hobbies, interests and occupation, and financial information (for billing purposes); and community discussions, chats and correspondence.

Anonymous Data. When you use our Site we may collect data that is not associated with or linked to your Personal Information ("Anonymous Data"). Anonymous Data does not, by itself, permit the identification of individual persons.

Information Collected from Other Sources. We may obtain information about you from other sources, such as companies that update and verify email and physical addresses, for the purpose of updating and enhancing our files.

User Communities on our Site. Our Site includes community areas (e.g., forums, bulletin boards and chat rooms) in which you may participate after establishing a Membership Account. Although your participation in these communities is governed by this privacy policy, there are certain important characteristics of these communities that you should always keep in mind. We use Membership Account registration information and other information users provide as they interact within the communities to:

● authenticate users; provide communications from us to users regarding the community; and administer the community (including, but not limited to, managing the technical requirements of the site and monitoring submissions and posts for offensive content after posting and removing such content in our sole discretion).

Please note that information (including your name, email address, address, or phone number) you provide in a posting on community or any bulletin board is available (even after you stop participating in the community) to any other individual who has access to that same community and may be used by another individual in a manner unintended by you, including as a target for unsolicited communications. For this reason, we urge you to act cautiously in providing any such information in your submissions or posts, as we are not liable for any use or misuse by an individual user of the information you provide in a submission or post. Upon termination of a community, Personal Information we collected through the community will continue to be treated in accordance with this privacy policy.

Personal Information. We use Personal Information you provide to us for our business purposes, such as:

● to allow you to access your Membership Account; (ii) to confirm your identity; (iii) for marketing our products and services to you and to customize this advertising and marketing information; (iv) to contact you regarding your Membership Account and to keep records regarding your Membership Account; (v) to carry-out promotions, including but not limited to sweepstakes, contests surveys and related promotions that you have entered; (vi) to provide the services and products you request and to respond to your other requests and inquiries', (vii) to enhance our service offerings; and (viii) to adjust our Site to your personal preferences.

Anonymous Data. Anonymous Data may be used to analyze request and usage patterns so that we may enhance our Site and to improve Site navigation. We reserve the right to use Anonymous Data for any purpose and disclose Anonymous Data to third parties at our sole and absolute discretion.

Marketing. We do not sell or rent your Personal Information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We may aggregate and use certain non-personally identifiable information for research or marketing purposes and may share, sell and rent or trade that information and statistical data derived from such information with our marketing partners, advertisers, or other third parties for research or marketing purposes.

COOKIES AND OTHER INFORMATION COLLECTED BY TECHNOLOGY

Cookies and Related Technology. We use Cookies (small files placed on your hard drive) to store information about your visits to our Site and to enhance your experience when visiting our Site. For example, we use Cookies to, among other things, identify previous visitors to our Site, identify a user as he/she moves through our Site, track user visits and account set-ups, determine what set-ups are most frequently requested, and save your password so you do not have to provide it each time you visit our Site. Storing information about your visits to our Site will allow us to, on occasion, customize account pages for you.

We do not use Cookies to collect, track, or store personally identifiable information. Our cookies will never be used to track your activity on any third party Site or to send spam. You can configure your browser to accept all Cookies, reject all Cookies, or notify you when a Cookie is received. (Each browser is different, so check the Help menu of your browser to learn how to change your Cookie preferences). You may reject, delete or otherwise disable Cookies at any time. However, by deleting, rejecting or otherwise disabling Cookies, you may interfere with your use of some of our Site or services.

URL Tracking. Some of our email communications to you may include a "click-through URL" linked to a particular page on one of our Sites. By clicking on one of these URLs, you will automatically enter our Site. Our systems track this information to gauge the effectiveness of our email communications and your level of interest in particular areas. You may avoid having this information collected by avoiding clicking on URL links in our email communications. Please note, that we will NEVER send you an email with an embedded link that asks for your password or any financial or account information; passwords are for your protection. If you EVER receive such an email, please contact us immediately using the information in the section below entitled "Contact Us".

We will use the information we automatically collected through your access and use of our Site to determine how our Site are accessed and used. For example, this information will allow us (i) to determine which pages are the most popular with users, (ii) determine if our Site are working properly, and (iii) to improve the user-experience on our Site. We do not use automatically collected information to identify a particular user.
