# Goood Ad Media ltd

This page describes our privacy practices with respect to [Goood Ad Media ltd ](http://www.gooodadmedia.com/).

**Information Collection and Use Practices**

  * Goood Ad Media ltd uses your location, in accordance with our [Privacy practices](http://www.gooodadmedia.com/privacy.php), to serve local weather information, photos and other information to you.
  * Mobile phone identifiers, model information, and operating systems may be collected for purposes of internal analytics, or to provide the service with the best possible experience.
  * Goood Ad Media ltd may offer you the opportunity to log in using Facebook. Goood Ad Media ltd uses this sign-in information to identify the user and may associate that information to an existing Goood Ad Media ltd profile to allow a single-sign on experience. Once signed in, Goood Ad Media ltd may configure your cities based on the location of your social graph. Goood Ad Media ltd may store your Facebook information on our servers.



**Other**




For additional information on how how we treats your personal information, please visit our [Privacy Policy](http://www.gooodadmedia.com/privacy.php) and [Goood Ad Media ltd Terms of Service](http://www.gooodadmedia.com/terms.php).
