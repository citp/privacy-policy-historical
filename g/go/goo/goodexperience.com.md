# goodexperience.com - customer experience, user experience

**Good Experience Privacy Policy**

1\. Policy. Good Experience, Inc. ("Good Experience") Privacy Policy covers Good Experience's treatment of personal or personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") that may be collected when you are on the Good Experience web site, or any other affiliated sites, and when you use the services of Good Experience. This policy applies to all information obtained through this website as well the Good Experience newsletter. 

The privacy of your nonpublic information is important to us. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address and your subscription to our newsletter, if applicable. We use safeguards to protect your privacy and confidentiality. This disclosure discusses information we collect and the manner in which it is used. 

2\. Information Collection. Good Experience may collect Personal Information when you use certain services on the Good Experience web site. If you do nothing during your visit but browse through the Website and read the information contained on the Web pages, we may gather certain information about your visit automatically. This information does not identify you personally. We may automatically gather the Internet domain, IP address from which you access our Website, and the date and time you access our website and the pages you visit. 

We may collect nonpublic personal information from you when you voluntarily provide personally identifiable information to us through our website. This information may include such information as name, address (email or postal service), telephone, financial information (personal or business), business information (including but not limited to corporate structure, employees names and contact information, corporate financial information), business addresses (email or postal service), business practices and preferences and any other information which we may gather from you in an effort to provide the best individualized service possible (which includes any information you may provide in response to any question we may ask you). If you subscribe to the Good Experience newsletter, we maintain your email address. 

3\. Sharing and Disclosure of Personal Information. Good Experience will not sell or rent your Personal Information to any individual, business, or government entity. Good Experience will share your Personal Information with other entities should you request Good Experience to share such information, or if Good Experience is required to respond to court orders, subpoenas or other legal process. 

4\. Employees. We restrict access to nonpublic information to those employees (if any) who need to know that information in order to provide services to you. 

5\. Amendments. Good Experience reserves the right to amend this policy at any time. Good Experience will contact registered users by e-mail, and shall also post a notice of changes on its web site, when and if the terms are amended. 

6\. Contact. If you have questions about our privacy policy, you may contact Mark Hurst at mark @(SPAMBLOCK) goodexperience.com.
