## Privacy Policy

#### Privacy

We love our readers and want you to love us too. In keeping with that, we respect your privacy and will protect your information as we would like our own information protected. Occasionally, we may make our subscriber list available to reputable organizations that offer products and services we believe would interest our readers. If you do not want to receive these offers, please advise us at [info@goodmagazine.com](mailto:info@goodmagazine.com).

The information submitted to our website during a transaction, including name, address, telephone number, email address, and credit card information is collected and used to process your subscription. We may contact you with questions about your subscription, or to notify you of important news, special promotions, contests, or other services that will improve your experience with us. To be removed from our contact list, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/about/contact/).
