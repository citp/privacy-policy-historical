good.net's privacy policy

Last Modified: November 6th, 2008

Your privacy is important to us. We have developed this privacy policy to help you understand how we use your Personally Identifiable Information (PII).

In brief:

  • We do not SPAM.
  • We do not give your PII, including your email address, to anyone.
  • Your PII is removed from our systems within 120 days of account termination, or upon your request.
  • Website/FTP log files that contain PII are kept for 90 days.

When you create an account, we gather your email address and the fact that you are an adult.

When you use our website, we collect the following: your IP address, your browser type, requested URL, referring URL and the time and date.

Our website uses session cookies that store authentication information. The use of cookies is not required to use our website.

We do not have any tracking beacons or other tracking technology on our website. We will not share the fact that you visited our website with anyone.

We will not track what URL's you visit after you leave our website.

If you need to correct any PII that we maintain, please visit the Members Dashboard or contact us at http://good.net/support.

If you want us to delete your PII (will require us to cancel your account), please contact us at http://good.net/support.

If you have any questions, concerns or complaints please contact us at http://good.net/support.
