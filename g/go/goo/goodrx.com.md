# Privacy Policy - GoodRx

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us through our website or write to us at:

GoodRx, Inc.  
2644 30th St., Ste 101  
Santa Monica, CA 90405  
USA  


When accessing our Website, GoodRx, Inc. will learn certain information about you during your visit. 

We may collect the following types of information: 

  * Log information - When you access GoodRx services, our servers automatically record certain information. These logs include information such as the date and time you access our site, the browser you are using, and other common browser information. 
  * Information you provide - When you register for notifications, we ask for personal information such as your email address. 
  * Communications with us - When you contact us by email or other communications, we may retain that information in order to respond to your request and improve our services. 
  * Location data - When you are using GoodRx with a browser or mobile device, you can enter a location or explicitly allow GoodRx access to your location. 
  * Personally-identifying information about you will not be sold or otherwise transferred to unaffiliated third parties without your approval, unless required by law. If GoodRx becomes involved in a merger, acquisition, or any form of sale, we will ensure the confidentiality of any personal information involved in such transaction and provide notice before any personal information is transferred that becomes subject to a different privacy policy. 



For site security purposes and to ensure that this service remains available to all users, GoodRx, Inc. uses software programs to monitor network traffic to identify unauthorized attempts to upload or change information, or otherwise cause damage.
