Privacy Statement

GoMapper is committed to protecting the privacy of the information provided to us by our customers. The following outlines the types of information we collect and how they will and will not be used by us. Please understand that GoMapper will NOT disclose, sell or trade any information collected to any third parties unless required by law, or if the information is necessary or appropriate to address an unlawful or harmful activity.

Information Collected During Site Access

GoMapper collects data which includes IP addresses and page URLs for session management and for purposes of compiling traffic and usage statistics. All access to the Site is logged and analysed periodically to determine user patterns and popular sections of the Site. GoMapper may use this information to further enhance the website according to these trends. To establish user sessions and identify unique/return visitors, GoMapper may utilise cookies stored in your browser for this purpose.

Cookies

Cookies are used to store a session identification string. The actual data related to a user session is not stored on the user's browser or local computer but in GoMapper's system temporarily while the session is active.
