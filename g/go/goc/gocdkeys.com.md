### Social logins

GOCDKEYS also allows websites users to connect social accounts to your profile. GOCDKEYS users that have a social login enabled on their profile will be able to see basic information related to the connected accounts. This includes but is not limited to your name, email address, social profile URL’s and associated profile picture.

GOCDKEYS will only ever use information that you have made public or specifically allowed us to access when you accept permissions. GOCDKEYS does not know or store any passwords associated with your social accounts.

GOCDKEYS does not track or provide any personal information to companies until you explicitly provide consent. This means that you do not expose any personal information to other companies.

### Cookies

If you leave a comment on our site you may opt-in to saving your name, email address and website in cookies. These are for your convenience so that you do not have to fill in your details again when you leave another comment. These cookies will last for one year.

If you have an account and you log in to this site, we will set a temporary cookie to determine if your browser accepts cookies. This cookie contains no personal data and is discarded when you close your browser.

When you log in, we will also set up several cookies to save your login information and your screen display choices. Login cookies last for two days, and screen options cookies last for a year. If you select “Remember Me”, your login will persist for two weeks. If you log out of your account, the login cookies will be removed.

If you edit or publish an article, an additional cookie will be saved in your browser. This cookie includes no personal data and simply indicates the post ID of the article you just edited. It expires after 1 day.

GOCDKEYS use Cookies to track information about users in order to serve them content. This includes but is not limited to:

  * Persist Customers sessions that are logged into GOCDKEYS Websites.
  * Tracking and awarding tickets for referring users via our reward program.
  * Tracked users referred by our referral program.
