# Privacy Policy | GoEuro

This is the Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy”) of GoEuro Corp. ("GoEuro" or "us" or "we"). GoEuro is committed to maintaining privacy protections for its users. Our Privacy Policy was developed as an extension of our commitment to combine our services (the “Service”) with integrity in dealing with our users. The Privacy Policy is designed to assist you in understanding how we collect, use and store the personal information you provide to us. Our Terms of Use, which incorporates by reference this Privacy Policy, governs your use of our Services, any content or information therein, the GoEuro websites at [www.goeuro.com](http://www.goeuro.com/) and at any corresponding country-specific GoEuro domains (including without limitation [www.goeuro.de](http://www.goeuro.de/), [www.goeuro.at](http://www.goeuro.at/) and [www.goeuro.co.uk](http://www.goeuro.co.uk/)) and any subdomains of the foregoing websites (the "Site"). By using the Service or the Site, you accept the Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use and you expressly consent to our collection, storage, use and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

## **I. Information we collect**

  1. **Information collected via Technology**

In an effort to improve the quality of the Site and the Service, we track information provided to us by your browser when you view the Site or use the Service, such as your Internet service provider, the website you came from (known as the “referring URL”), the type of browser you use, your operating system, the device from which you connected to the Service, the time and date of access, the Site pages viewed, time spent at our Site, the sites visited just before and just after our Site, and other information. We track this information using cookies, or small text files which include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are a feature of web browser (Internet Explorer, Firefox, etc.) software that allows web servers to recognize the computer used to access a site. They are small pieces of data that are stored by a user's web browser on visiting a site to simplify subsequent interactions with that site by the same user or to use the information to streamline the user's transaction on related web pages. This makes it easier for a user to move from site to site and to complete transactions over the Internet. Cookies should make your online experience easier and more personalized. You can find more information about cookies and how to manage them at <http://www.allaboutcookies.org/>.

Cookies are sent to a user’s browser from our servers and are stored on the user’s computer hard drive. Sending a cookie to a user’s browser enables us to collect information about that user and keep a record of the user’s preferences when utilizing our services, both on an individual and aggregate basis. If you click through to a third party website from the links provided on the Site or via the Service (to make a reservation or purchase a travel ticket, for example), these other websites may also place a cookie on your browser. We have no control over and are not responsible for cookies set by third party websites or applications, and we encourage you to read the cookies information provided on those other.

GoEuro may use both persistent and session cookies; persistent cookies remain on your computer after you close your session and until you delete them, while session cookies expire when you close your browser.

You may disable cookies by changing the settings on your browser. Check the help menu in your browser for further information. Remember that if you disable cookies it is likely to affect how our website works and you may not be able to access or use certain areas of our site or functionality (such as setting up your profile, or saving your preferred travel mode, for example). Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, our system will place cookies when you log on to our Site.

    * Strictly necessary cookies, which are essential in order to enable you to move around the Site and use its features and without which services you have asked for, search as travel searches and bookings history, cannot be provided.
    * Performance cookies, which collect information about your use the Site, for instance which pages you go to most often, how you use the travel search functions and which travel company websites you visit from the Site. These cookies don’t collect information that identifies you, and are only used to improve how the Site works.
    * Functionality cookies, which allow the Site to remember choices you make (such as your travel mode preferences (e.g. air, rail or bus), your home city and closest train station, bus station and airport, your preferred currency, your user name if you create a profile etc., and provide enhanced, more personal features.
  2. **Information you provide us by using the Service**

You can use all parts of the Service without creating a personal profile. However, if you do wish to create a personal profile, you will be asked to provide us with your email address, name, age and mailing address. You may also provide us with your frequent flier numbers and travel preferences (such as your travel mode preferences (e.g. air, rail or bus), your home city and closest train station, bus station and airport, and your preferred currency) so that we can provide you with a more personalized service. By registering and providing us with this information, you are authorizing us to collect, store and use this Personal Information, including your email address, in accordance with this Privacy Policy, in addition to the information provided automatically by your browser when you visit the Site.

  3. **Use of the Service or the Site**

You need to be of sufficient legal age to enter into binding contracts in order to register for and use the Service. GoEuro doesn’t knowingly collect or use personally identifiable information from children under 13. If a child under 13 has provided us with personally identifiable information without parental or guardian consent, the parent or guardian should contact us at the email address provided below ([contact@goeuro.com](mailto:contact@goeuro.com)) to remove the information and opt out of promotional opportunities. You may learn more about protecting children's privacy online by visiting [www.ftc.gov/kidzprivacy](http://www.ftc.gov/kidzprivacy).




## **II. How we use and share information**

  1. **Personal Information**

In general, the Personal Information you provide to us is used to enable us to provide you with the Service and to help us communicate with you. For example, we use Personal Information to contact users in connection with the provision of Service to users, in response to questions, to solicit feedback from users, to provide technical support, and to inform users about promotional offers. We also share Personal Information with vendors who are performing services for GoEuro, such as third party payment processors and service providers who send communications to you on behalf of GoEuro. Those vendors use your Personal Information only at our direction and in accordance with our Privacy Policy. We do not sell your name, email, contact, credit card or billing details (if collected) to anyone. If you have given us your consent, GoEuro may share your Personal Information with third parties for marketing purposes.

  2. **Non-Personal Information**

In general, we use Non-Personal Information to help us improve the Service and customize the user experience. We also may aggregate Non-Personal Information in order to track trends and analyze use patterns on the Site. Periodically, we might share or publish aggregated information on usage behavior such as booking patterns, preferences on modes of travel for market research or other purposes. Such shared or published aggregated information will not contain any personal identification information of any individual. This Privacy Policy does not limit in any way our use or disclosure of Non-Personal Information and we reserve the right to use and disclose such Non-Personal Information to our partners, advertisers, other third parties and/or the general public at our discretion.

  3. **Mergers, Acquisitions and Similar Business Transactions**

In the event we undergo a business transaction such as a merger, acquisition by another company, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your Personal Information as well as Non- Personal Information may be among the assets transferred. You acknowledge and consent that such transfers may occur and are permitted by this Privacy Policy, and that any acquirer of our assets may continue to process your Personal Information as set forth in this Privacy Policy. If our information practices materially change at any time in the future, we will post the policy changes to the Site so that you may opt out of the new information practices. We suggest that you check the Site periodically if you are concerned about how your information is used.

  4. **Sharing Information with the Government or As Otherwise Required by Law**

We may also use or disclose information collected on the Site to resolve disputes or investigate problems. We may disclose or access such information when we believe in good faith that the law requires it, or if we consider it necessary to maintain the Service and improve our products and services, including disclosure to countries outside your country of residence.

We implement security measures designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure or accidental loss or destruction of Non-Personal Information and Personal Information. We further protect your information from potential security breaches by implementing certain technological security measures including encryption, firewalls and/or secure socket layer technology. However, these measures do not guarantee that your information will not be accessed, disclosed, altered or destroyed by breach of such firewalls and secure server software. Although we take reasonable steps to safeguard and prevent unauthorized access to your personally identifiable information, we can't be responsible for the acts of those who gain unauthorized access, and we make no warranty, express, implied or otherwise, that we will prevent such access. By visiting the Site and using the Service, you acknowledge that you understand and agree to assume these risks. Communications you may have with us via email or Snail Mail may not be secure unless we advise you that security measures are in place prior to your sending information. Therefore, if you choose to communicate with us through these means, you are assuming the risk of doing so and we request that you do not send or post sensitive information through these **means.**

## **IV. Your rights regarding the use of your personal information**

You have the right at any time to prevent us from contacting you for marketing purposes. When we send a promotional communication to a user, the user can opt out of further promotional communications by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in each promotional e-mail. Please note that notwithstanding the promotional preferences you indicate by either unsubscribing or opting out in the Settings section of the Site, we may continue to send you administrative emails including, for example, in order to effectively provide you with the Service and periodic updates to our Privacy Policy. You also have the right to access personal information held about you, and to ask us to rectify any errors in that information. Please contact us via [contact@goeuro.com](mailto:contact@goeuro.com) or click the ‘Please help us’-button on the Site to get in touch for more information.

GoEuro may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. When we do update this Privacy Policy, we will also revise the "Last Updated" date at the top and bottom of the Privacy Policy.
