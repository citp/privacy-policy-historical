# Citrix Online Privacy Policy Overview - Citrix

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy Overview provides a short summary of the complete [Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/about/legal/citrix-online-privacy-policy-en.html) for GoToMeeting, GoToWebinar, GoToTraining, GoToMyPC, GoToAssist, VideoConferencing and Workshifting by Citrix (collectively, the “Covered Websites”). This statement and the complete privacy policy statement apply to the Covered Websites and products that link to this notice.

  * Citrix Systems, Inc. is committed to respecting your privacy.
  * Citrix is a licensee of the [TRUSTe](http://privacy.truste.com/privacy-seal/Citrix-Systems,-Inc-/validation?rid=1d5a0c5d-4797-4e1e-8972-9635479fc6dc&width=770&height=380) Privacy Program for the Covered Websites.
  * The Covered Websites also comply with the EU and Swiss Safe Harbor framework as set forth by the Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use and retention of data from the European Union and Switzerland.



## Information We Collect

  * When you register for a product or free trial, we will need to collect personal information such as billing information.
  * We collect information you voluntarily supply to us on our websites, through surveys, or as feedback.
  * Information is collected during the use of some of our products as needed to provide the service.
  * We use common Internet technologies, such as cookies and web beacons, to keep track of interactions with our sites and services.
  * To learn more about our information collection practices, please read our complete [Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/about/legal/citrix-online-privacy-policy-en.html).



## How We Use Your Information

  * We use the information collected to provide you with the services you request.
  * We may use your information to inform you about other products or services, partners, opportunities and events offered by Citrix.
  * We may use your information to improve our products or websites.
  * We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.
  * Citrix uses industry-standard efforts to safeguard the confidentiality of personally identifiable information, including encryption, firewalls and SSL (Secure Sockets Layer).
  * To learn more about our use of your information, please read our complete [Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/about/legal/citrix-online-privacy-policy-en.html).



## How We Share Your Information

  * In order to conduct business it is necessary that we share some of your information with third parties, such as for credit card processing.
  * Third parties must process your personal information in accordance with our privacy policy.
  * We may need to disclose your information as required by law to protect our rights or comply with judicial proceedings.
  * To learn more about our information sharing practices, please read our complete [Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/about/legal/citrix-online-privacy-policy-en.html).



## Communication Choices

  * To change your email subscription status or opt out of collection, use and distribution of your information, visit our [Unsubscribe page](http://learn.citrix.com/NA-Unsubscribe.html).
  * Every marketing email from Citrix contains instructions on how to opt out of receiving further marketing emails.
  * To learn more about the choices you have about the collection, use and distribution of your information, please read our complete [Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/about/legal/citrix-online-privacy-policy-en.html).
