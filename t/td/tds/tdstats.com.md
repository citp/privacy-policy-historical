# TDStats.com - Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

**IP Addresses  
**TDStats and sites using the TDStats service collects visitor information like browser domain names and IP addresses. This information used purely for statistical purposes and is not linked to any personally identifiable information. Visitors will be anonymous.

**Cookies  
**TDStats use cookies to track visitors on sites using the TDStats service. This cookie is used only to collect broad statistical information and is not used to identify individuals.

TDStats use cookies to remember your login information so you do not have to type it in everytime you want to access your statistics.

TDStats utilize third party companies for advertising. These ads may place cookies on visitors computer. Information gathered from cookies placed by third party advertising is not accessible to TDStats.

**Contact information  
**TDStats collects contact email addresses upon signing up for the service. This information is used purely to contact members when necessary, including, but not limited to support requests.

TDStats does not sell email addresses to third parties.

TDStats staff can be contacted [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20061221204500id_/http%3A//www.tdstats.com/contact.php).
