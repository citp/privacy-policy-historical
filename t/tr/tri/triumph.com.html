PRIVACY STATEMENT

Triumph of Europe, Inc. ("Triumph") are committed to protecting the privacy of all individuals who utilize this website (the "Site"). Please read the following policy to understand how we collect, use and safeguard the information you may provide to us via the Site. This policy may be revised from time to time, so please revisit this page often to remain fully informed of our policies.

APPLICABILITY OF THIS PRIVACY STATEMENT

This Privacy Statement applies only to information submitted and collected through the Site, and does not apply to information that may be collected by Triumph through any other means, including by phone, through postal mail and/or through other live and print media.

WHAT INFORMATION DOES TRIUMPH COLLECT FROM YOU?

Via the Site, Triumph collects from you: (i) personally identifiable information, and (ii) technical and other information.

  • Personally Identifiable Information.

    General Site Visitors. When you browse the non-password protected portions of the Site, you do so anonymously; no personally identifiable information is collected. However, if you click on social "plug-ins" on the Site (such as for Facebook or Twitter), those actions will be associated with, and we will have access to, your basic information on those social media sites. For example, if you click on the Facebook icon, we will have access to your name, email, gender, date of birth, current city and profile picture, notwithstanding that you have not registered on the Site. In addition, if you "Like" our Site or any item on the Site on Facebook, our Site will appear in the "Likes" section of your profile, and we will have the ability to publish updates to you.

    Registered Users. The Site allows you to become a registered user of the Site. If you choose to become a registered user, you will be providing to us your first name, last name, date of birth, email address, mailing address and password.

    Purchasers. If you choose to purchase any of our products via the Site, and you do not have a customer account, we require that you provide us at the time of such purchase your first name, last name, date of birth, billing address, shipping address, phone number and email address for such purposes as noted herein. We may also ask for certain product preferences and size information.

    Subscribers. The Site permits you to subscribe to our newsletter and to receive emails and other electronic or physical notifications and information regarding new collections, latest lingerie trends, exclusive offers, sweepstakes and specials. When you subscribe to our newsletter via the Site, you must provide us with your first name, last name, email address and date of birth.

    Employment. Further, the Site allows you to apply for employment positions with Triumph. If you choose to apply for employment via the Site, you will be providing us with your first name, last name, email address, phone number, the information contained in your resume, and any information you choose to provide us about your employment history and/or other relevant information.

    Contacting Us. The Site permits you to contact us via our online Contact Us form. Whenever you send an email via the online Contact Us form on the Site, you may be providing us with your first name, last name, email address, phone number and any information you choose to include in the text of your message. For example, you may choose to provide your mailing address or other information necessary or helpful for us to address your query or other concern.

    Feedback and Testimonials. We allow Site users to rate and/or review our products and, in the future, may allow users to post other information or content ("User Content"). Such User Content may include testimonials relating to users' personal experiences with Triumph, its products or the Site. As part of such User Content you may be providing us with personally identifiable information, such as your name, location, likeness, voice and other information you choose to make known.

    Sweepstakes, Contests and Offerings. From time to time, Triumph may offer you the opportunity to participate in sweepstakes, contests, offers or other activity on or through the Site. If you choose to opt into any such contest, sweepstakes, or other promotion or activity, you may be asked to provide us with certain personally identifiable information necessary to conduct such contest, sweepstakes, or other promotion or activity, such as your name, age, mailing address, email address and phone number.

  • Technical and Other Information.

    While you are browsing the Site, we automatically log your IP address (a number assigned to your computer when you use the Internet) and your activity while using the Site. Additionally, in order to personalize and enhance your experience on the Site, we may also collect information through "cookies." Cookies are small strings of text that are sent by the Site to your browser and then stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. We use cookies to help diagnose problems with our servers and to administer the Site. We also use cookies to gather broad demographic information and to help identify you in respect to visits to the Site so that we may improve your user experience, and to determine whether you visited the Site from a particular Internet link or advertisement. While a code in the cookie file enables the Site to label you as a particular user, including identifying you by name, address, or other personally identifiable information, such cookie files are encrypted in order to protect your information. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but it is possible to change your browser setup so that it does not accept cookies. However, rejecting cookies may prevent you from taking advantage of certain portions of the Site.

  • Google Analytics.

    The Site also may use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics also uses cookies to help analyze how users use the Site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the Site (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Please note that we use Google Analytics with the "_anonymizeIP()" extension, which deletes part of your IP address so it cannot be traced to an individual user. Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the Site, compiling reports on Site activity for website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and Internet usage. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. By using the Site, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner and for the purposes set forth above.

If you are a registered user, typically, information associated with your account will be kept until your account is deleted.
