# Privacy Policy | Triple Pundit: People, Planet, Profit

### Privacy Policy

3P’s privacy policy is straightforward. You don’t need to identify yourself or your affiliation to access the information on our website. It is our policy is to safeguard the personal information and protect the identity and confidentiality of all visitors and users.

If you choose to subscribe to our [free email newsletter](http://www.triplepundit.com/newsletter/) or discussion forums, you need only submit your e-mail address and basic contact information; no additional personal information is required. We promise never to sell, rent, or swap your address with others. We won’t try to sell you anything. (We may occasionally survey you to ask your opinion of how we’re doing — but no more than twice a year.)

By registering to comment on the site you also register for our [free email newsletter](http://www.triplepundit.com/newsletter/), however you may opt-out at any time and will not be re-subscribed.

This policy extends to the e-mail addresses of colleagues or friends you provide to us for purposes of sending a sample copy of the newsletter as well as to comments submitted to the site.

For more information, [please contact us](mailto:contact@triplepundit.com).
