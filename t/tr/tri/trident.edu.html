Trident University Privacy Policy

TUI Campus Crime Policy


Trident University (“TUI”) respects the privacy of every individual visiting our websites or responding to our promotions. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. Unless otherwise posted, this notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the TUI websites.


Security Measures

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.


Personally- Identifiable Information

TUI will not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, address, telephone number, or email address. You may provide your information voluntarily but are not required to do so. If you do submit personally-identifiable information we may use it to contact you; complete your application or enrollment requests; store and process the information to better understand your needs; and improve our products and services. TUI will NOT give or sell your individual information to any outside entity for marketing or solicitation purposes. We will maintain the confidentiality of your personal information and use it to support your relationship with Trident University. Any personal information collected online will remain confidential, unless, disclosure is required by law.


Research

We occasionally conduct online surveys. Your responses are confidential and will be used for statistical analysis only.


Nonpersonally Identifiable Information Collected Automatically

We collect IP addresses for the purpose of system administration and to report aggregate information. We monitor aggregate information such as the number of unique users who visit certain sections of our site. This information is not personally identifiable and is used to improve our site. We do not use ‘cookies’. At no time do we disclose site usage by individual IP addresses. In some cases, we may collect information about you that is not personally identifiable. Examples of this type of information include the Internet browser you are using, the type of operating system, and the domain name of the website that linked you to our site. Please refer to your browser instructions or Help screen to learn more about these functions.


Use Of Enrollment Information

When you enroll in a course or make an inquiry, we use your information to process your request. We may need to contact you if we have questions regarding your enrollment online. Occasionally we contact people who have enrolled or inquired about one of our programs online to ask them questions about their experience.


Third Parties Websites

Links to third party sites on the TUI website are provided solely for your convenience. If you use these links, you will leave the TUI site. TUI has not reviewed all of these third party sites and does not control and is not responsible for their content, policies or practices. Thus, TUI does not endorse or make any representations about the information, products or materials found on third party sites, or any results that may be obtained from using them. If you decide to access any of the third party sites linked to this site, you do so at your own risk.


Disclaimer

If you are not comfortable enrolling online or submitting a form containing personal information via the Internet, you have the option of communicating with us via the contact information below. You also have the right to view, modify of delete any personal information submitted online. Should you wish to do so please contact us by one of the following ways.


How You Can Correct Your Information

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or would like to have your personally identifiable information modified or deleted from our records, please email us.


How To Contact Us

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, send us e-mail at registration@trident.edu


Effective Date 1/31/2003

Accredited University

Trident University International is a 100% online university accredited by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC), which is one of six regional accrediting associations in the United States. Accredited online universities, such as TUI, can provide flexible distance learning and cutting edge military university programs, including: Online Military Degrees, Bachelor Degree, Masters Degree, MBA, PhD.

 

Gainful Employment Disclosure

For more about Trident University's programs, including the graduation rates for each program, median debt of students who completed each program, and other important information, please click here. In order to help students make informed decisions about academic choices, Trident University provides this information for all of its programs.

 

Degree Programs

At Trident University, online degrees are designed with a commitment to help you achieve your college, career, or military education goals. The online university faculty at TUI provides individual student support to help enhance your academic experience, and earn a quality online degree. Over 90% of our faculty have doctoral degrees.

 

Academic Colleges

Trident University offers distinguished online colleges and military education including PhD, Bachelors, MBA, Masters Degree. Online education tools are provided to help you succeed regardless of location, a hallmark of TUI military university programs. Online degrees are supported by our university online faculty and staff to help each step of the way toward earning online degrees. TUI provides a unique opportunity for accredited university degrees in Business Administration, Health Sciences, Information Systems, or Education.

 

Flexible Online Courses

TUI military university courses and online degree programs give you broad flexibility to complete the university courses online based on your schedule. Accredited online universities can provide an ideal opportunity for working members of the military and their families who need the flexibility of online military college degrees. Choosing a university degree online can save you both time and money.

 

Educational Programs Designed For Those in the Military

An online university degree at TUI is designed for active duty military personnel and their families seeking a degree from an accredited online university that offers undergraduate and graduate degrees online in a wide range of professional disciplines. Trident University International is a leader among online universities, providing education and career advancement tools for your military career or for a new professional career.

 

Publicity Statement

Trident University hosts and attends many events and gatherings throughout the year. As part of TUI’s ongoing communication about these activities, photos and/or videos from those events may be posted on TUI’s website or social media pages. It is possible that an individual may appear in these photos or videos. Please contact TUI if you would like to have your picture or video removed. Upon receipt of this notification, TUI will remove the images as soon as viable.
