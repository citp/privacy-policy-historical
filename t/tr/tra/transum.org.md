# Transum

Transum uses cookies for the 'Class Admin' application which stores email address and password on your local computer so that you don't need to log-in each time you visit a Class Admin page. This information is not used for any other purpose. This facility is only used if the 'remember me' box is ticked.

On some pages we use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
