Travisa Privacy Policy

At Travisa, Inc. we recognize that privacy is important. In addition to providing our customers with great service we are dedicated to protecting the privacy of our clients. Our privacy policy outlines the types of personal information we receive and collect when you use Travisa, Inc. services, as well as some of the steps we take to safeguard information and the benefits created for you as a result of this policy. We hope this will help you make an informed decision about sharing personal information with us.

Our current privacy policy has been in effect since December 1st, 2011. We may amend this Privacy Policy as determined by our discretion. In the event of an update, we will post the amended Privacy Policy on our site for your convenience. Personal information we collect:

  • Travisa, Inc. collects personal information from you when you voluntarily provide such information in the course of submitting visas, US passports, and other applications and services which we process for Embassies, Consulates, and the US Government. We will not use, reveal, or make available that information other than as necessary to complete the service you have requested and, if relevant, to secure payment for those services.
  • We may also use that personal information in evaluating your service needs and contacting you regarding additional services that we provide that you may find useful. In any such contact we will always give you the opportunity to opt out of such usage in the future. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time.
  • We may also use personal information for auditing, research and analysis to operate and improve our technologies and services.
  • Travisa, Inc. uses cookies and other technologies to enhance your online experience and allow you to quickly and easily complete our online application process. Cookies are identifiers conveyed to your computer's hard drive enabling our systems to recognize your browser. Cookies allow us to communicate certain information or content particular to your needs and interests. Our site also uses cookies to anonymously track movement within our site.
  • We may also share information with third parties in limited circumstances, including when complying with legal process, preventing fraud or imminent harm, and ensuring the security of our network and services. The data that we provide them is anonymous and does not reveal specific personal information related to our customers. This data is used for specific functions that require broad-based user statistics which help to improve the overall Travisa customer experience.
  • This site may contain links to other sites. Travisa is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.
  • While every effort is made to provide accurate information, travel visa and passport requirements are constantly changing and there may be a delay before we are advised of such changes. Therefore, we are unable to guarantee complete accuracy at all times. Processing times and charges given are solely for guidance purposes and cannot be guaranteed. Travisa recommends that travel visas be obtained before travel. Travel arrangements should not be confirmed prior to the visa or passport being issued, unless they are a specifically required for that high commission. In such cases, refundable arrangements should be made.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy contact Travisa by email at: support@travisa.com
