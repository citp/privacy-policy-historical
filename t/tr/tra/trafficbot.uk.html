Privacy Policy

TrafficBot Software (herein termed TrafficBot) respects the privacy of users who visit any of the sites within TrafficBots control, including but not limited to the TrafficBot site located at http://www.trafficbot.uk. This information is required as a necessary part of completing your order and will not be sold or rented to any third parties. If at any time you wish to be removed from any of the customer lists, you may request to be removed by e-mailing us here. Or, you may remove yourself from any mailing by clicking on the unsubscribe link at the bottom of every email I send out.

If at any time you wish to review, change or update any personal information provided to us, simply submit a support ticket! To better protect the security and integrity of your information, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections to your information.
