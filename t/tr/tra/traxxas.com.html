Privacy Policy

Your privacy is extremely important to us. To better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used at this site.

In summary, we are deeply concerned with your desire to protect your personal information on the internet. We are not in the business of, nor have any desire to be in the business of collecting, sharing, or selling personal information. Traxxas will never share or sell your personal information, including your e-mail address, to other companies for any reason unless required by law, such as in response to a court order or subpoena. We want you to use our site with the confidence that submitting your information to Traxxas does not expose you to receiving unwanted messages and solicitations from any third parties outside of Traxxas.

Our Commitment to Security

We have put in place all the appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and help prevent unauthorized access, maintain data security, and correctly use the information we collect online.

The Information We Collect

The types of personally identifiable information that may be collected from our site include: name, address, e-mail address, telephone number, fax number, and information about your interests in and use of various products, programs, and services. We may also collect certain non-personally identifiable information when you visit many of our web pages such as the type of browser you are using (e.g., Firefox, Internet Explorer), the type of operating system you are using (e.g., Windows or Mac OS), and the domain name of your internet service provider (e.g., America Online, Earthlink). Credit Card Information (where applicable) Traxxas does not keep or store credit card information on Traxxas servers. Traxxas uses a secure encrypted connection (SSL) to transfer your personal information (billing information, etc.) to a Bank of America credit card validation center. Once a transaction is complete, your credit card information is removed from our systems.

Fraud Protection

Traxxas.com requires that the shipping address be the same as the billing address on the credit card. We also require input of the 3 or 4-digit validation code printed on the back of the credit card. Finally, our shipping carrier protects against fraud by not allowing a package to be redirected to an address other than the cardholder's billing address after the package has been shipped.

How We Use the Information

The information we collect is used to provide an interactive experience. We use this information to facilitate participation in the interactive areas of our site. We may use the information you provide about yourself to fulfill your requests for our products, programs, and services to respond to your inquiries about our offerings. We sometimes use this information to communicate with you, such as to notify you when we make changes to our subscriber agreements, to fulfill a request by you for a catalog, or to contact you about your account with us.

From time to time, we may use your personal information to offer you other Traxxas products, programs, or services that we believe may be of interest to you. These would only be items created and sent directly from Traxxas and not by any outside party or vendor. Whenever there is a form requesting information, you will have the option to check a box in order to NOT receive any future marketing materials from Traxxas.

We sometimes use the non-personally identifiable information that we collect to improve the design and content of our site, and to enable us to personalize your internet experience. We may also use this information in the aggregate to analyze site usage.

If you choose to act on a third party offer, then your information may be transmitted outside of Traxxas to that third party in order to fulfill your request. Once submitted, your personal information would be subject to the rules of the third party's privacy and information policy.

We will disclose information we maintain only when required to do so by law, for example, in response to a court order or a subpoena. We may also disclose such information in response to a law enforcement agency's request.

Agents and contractors of Traxxas who have access to personally identifiable information are required to protect this information in a manner that is consistent with this Privacy Notice. Examples of this would include credit card processing and shipment tracking services. They are not allowed to capture and use your personal information for any purpose outside of official Traxxas business.

Although we take appropriate measures to safeguard against unauthorized disclosures of information, we cannot guarantee you that personally identifiable information that we collect will never be disclosed in a manner that is inconsistent with this Privacy Notice. Inadvertent disclosures may result, for example, as a result of unauthorized access (hack attempts) to the site. Finally, Traxxas will not use or transfer personally identifiable information provided to us in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you with an opportunity to opt out of these unrelated uses.

Cookies

To enhance your experience with our site, some of our web pages use "cookies." Cookies are text files we place in your computer's browser to store your preferences. Cookies (by themselves) do not tell us your e-mail address or other personally identifiable information unless you choose to provide this information to us by, for example, registering at our site; however, once you choose to furnish the site with personally identifiable information, this information may be linked to the data stored in the cookie. We use cookies to understand site usage and to improve the content and offerings on our sites; we may use cookies to personalize your experience at our web pages (e.g., to recognize you by name when you return to our site), or save your password in password-protected areas.

How To Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about the Privacy Notice for this site or its implementation please Contact Us.
