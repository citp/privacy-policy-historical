# Trade-Schools.net - Privacy Policy

  
Trade-schools.net is owned and operated by [Beeline Web Site Promotions, Inc. (Beelineweb.com)](http://www.beelineweb.com/).

Beelineweb.com does not sell or rent any personal data submitted by visitors to any unaffiliated third parties. Beelineweb.com. respects the privacy of users visiting our site and will abide by all applicable laws concerning the release of personal information.

The information submitted in the forms on our site is sent only to the school or college that you are requesting information about.

For further information, please [_contact us_](http://www.beelineweb.com/contact.htm).
