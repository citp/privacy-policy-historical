###  Tradable Bits Privacy Policy

Tradable Bits will be collecting information from user session to ensure that our applications (campaigns) are built as robust and with the best user experience. The information collected will be used to help improve our services including any web pages enhancements, increase quality and ensure strict security controls. Please note that this privacy policy applies only to information submitted and collected online at our website. It does not apply to information that you may submit to us offline, or to websites maintained by other companies or organizations to which we may link. We do not share or sell any information collected on our sites with the exception of the following we will provide: 

1) Statistics and data collected for the sites a customer administrates and manages where they have rights and commercial access for the Pages, domains and accounts. 

2) Share data and information when necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Terms of Service, or as otherwise required by law. 

3) Data and information will be shared within Tradable Bits Ltd and its partners to enhance services and application (campaign) development. 

What do we collect?

We will collect names, emails and/or phone numbers for those who registers and communicate with us. We will also collect statistics on Tradable Bits Apps (Campaigns) usage including app (campaign) page views and track time/clicks. We also may collect your IP address, browser type, domain name, access times and referring website address. 

When you register we will collect your Full Name, Email, Phone, Business/Corporation Name, Billing Address and Credit Card information for those who uses our Enhanced Services. This information is used to keep track of users accounts, billing information, identification, authorization, authentication, products/services activation, service enhancement, research and controls rights for Pages. 

Tradable Bits does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. This is not a website directed to children under the age of 13, and Tradable Bits does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. If you are under 13 years of age you should not use this website, and under no circumstances should you send information about yourself to Tradable Bits. If Tradable Bits discovers that a child under the age of 13 has provided Tradable Bits with personally identifiable information, Tradable Bits will immediately delete that child?s personally identifiable information from the website. 

Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. 

Cookies may be required to use the Tradable Bits service. We may use cookies to record current session information, but do not use permanent cookies. You are required to log-in to your Facebook User Account after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents. 

Data Storage

Tradable Bits uses vendor and 3rd party hosting partners to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to run Tradable Bits Apps (Campaigns) and services. Tradable Bits owns the code, databases, and all rights to the Tradable Bits application, however, you retain all rights to your own data. 

Disclosure

Tradable Bits may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with subpoenas or when your actions violate the Terms of Service. 

Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time. If we change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on our website so that you and other users are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it. You should also check back from time to time to ensure you are aware of any changes. If we are going to use personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection, we will notify you and others via email or by placing a prominent notice on our website. By using this website, you signify your assent to the Tradable Bits Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this privacy policy, please do not use this website or our applications (campaigns). Your continued use of this website or applications (campaigns) will signify your acceptance of these changes. 

Tradable Bits Privacy Policy was updated in content November 22, 2010.
