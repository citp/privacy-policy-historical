# TRAILFINDERS: Privacy Policy

**Trailfinders Ltd Privacy Policy**  
Trailfinders Ltd and, on our behalf, Nedstat are committed to protecting the privacy of trailfinders.co.uk/trailfinders.com users and the overall integrity of the internet.   
  
**Policy Guidelines** |    
  
These guidelines have been developed to comply with the Data Protection Act 1998.   
For information on the Data Protection Act 1998 please click this link.   
[www.dataprotection.gov.uk](http://www.informationcommissioner.gov.uk/)

Trailfinders Ltd and Nedstat take the following measures to protect our users' privacy as follows:  
a) We do NOT pass your details on to any third parties; you will only receive information directly from Trailfinders.   
B) All our email correspondence includes an unsubscribe option. 

Trailfinders Ltd and Nedstat collect information on our users through cookies (see below for further information). If you accept our cookies, please note that they will remain in your computer for five years, unless you delete them. Please note that turning off cookies, may limit your use of our website and services.  


We may periodically send you email updates on special offers from Trailfinders Ltd. If you wish to opt out of receiving such information from us, please use the unsubscribe option included in all our emails or click on this link <http://www.trailfinders.com/request.nsf/unsubscribe?OpenForm>   
to unsubscribe from such mailings.  
  
  
**What is a cookie?**  
  
A cookie is a small, numeric text file that is stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Any information you provide to us is held securely on our web servers and not on your machine.

Trailfinders and Nedstat use cookies to track users as they travel through the site -   
for instance, we might use cookies to count the total number of unique users who are accessing the site over a particular period of time, or to monitor the success of a particular campaign.  
The use of cookies in this way helps to make our system more efficient.

Firewalls set up by your company network may prohibit the use of browser cookies.

If you would like to know more about cookies, or would like to disable cookies in your browser settings (i.e. refuse cookies) please click this link [www.aboutcookies.org.](http://www.aboutcookies.org/)
