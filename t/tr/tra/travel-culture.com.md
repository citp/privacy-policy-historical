# Our Privacy Policy.

We care about you and your privacy, we have alrey been following the principals of GDPR since 1997, we further strengthened our policy to keep your privacy and your data secure. As of May 2018, we do not store your data ion our website so we fully keep your data in a secure file that only a computer on our prmises can read. 

We abide by the following principles of the GDPR 

**Lawfulness, fairness and transparency**. We ask for your name, phone and email only to contact you and to reply for your querry.  
**Purpose limitations:** Unless you send your data form for Pakistan visa, all your data to reach you for your request for service  
**Data minimisation:** We have reduced the data on our forms and now only take your name phone number & email  
**Integrity and confidentiality:** Your data is strictly in our system and will never be shared, rented or leased to any 3rd party including the government agencies.

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server does not automatically take/recognizes any information regarding the domain or e-mail address.

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who post messages to our bulletin board, the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations, no information on Customers who browse our Web page.

The information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded, not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

With respect to cookies: We use cookies to record session information, such as items that Customers add to their shopping cart.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail , writing to us at this <https://www.travel-culture.com/contact.shtml> page

If you supply us with your postal address on-line you will only receive the information for which you provided us your address.

With respect to Ad Servers: We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies. Customers may prevent their information from being used for purposes other than those for which it was originally collected by calling us at the number provided above.

Upon request we provide site visitors with access to transaction information (e.g., dates on which customers made purchases, amounts and types of purchases) that we maintain about them, contact information (e.g., name, address, phone number) that we maintain about them , a description of information that we maintain about them.

Customers can access this information by e-mail us at the above address, write to us at the above address. 

Upon request we offer visitors the ability to have inaccuracies corrected in contact information, communications that the consumer/visitor has directed to the site.

Customers can have this information corrected by sending us e-mail at the above address. 

With respect to security: We always use industry-standard encryption technologies when transferring and receiving consumer data exchanged with our site, When we transfer and receive certain types of sensitive information such as financial or health information, we redirect visitors to a secure server and will notify visitors through a pop-up screen on our site, We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.
