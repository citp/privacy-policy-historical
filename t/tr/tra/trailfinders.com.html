    Holiday Styles

    Beach Holidays

    Relaxing beach Holidays all over the world. Whether you are just looking for a week in the sun, a beach twin centre or to unwind after your tour, we've got the stretch of sand for you.

    Bespoke tours with your own private driver/guide where you choose the itinerary, sightseeing and accommodation with the help of our expert Bespoke Touring Team

    Coach Touring

    Escorted Coach Tours offer a hassle free, leisurely way to experience a destination. Travelling with a group of like minded people they are a sociable and carefree way to travel

    Cruise

    Tailormade ocean-going and expedition cruise holidays all over the world.

    Escorted Touring

    Browse all of our touring options in one place from bespoke private tours and safaris to coach tours and overland adventures!

    Family Holidays

    Inspirational Family Holiday ideas from Australia to Walt Disney World. Whether you are looking for adventure or relaxation we've got the family escape for you.

    Fly-Drives

    Car and Motorhome fly drives including inspirational itineraries and hand picked hotels.

    Great Rail Journeys

    Discover the world by rail Experience iconic rail journeys and tailormade holidays by train all over the globe.

    Luxury Holidays

    The finest luxury hotels, tours and experiences for when only the best will do.

    Multi Centre

    Trailfinders multi centre holidays bring the world together into some incredible combinations.

    Safari

    Discover Africa's incredible wildlife with our huge choice of safari tour styles, fly in lodges, fly drive safaris and even river safaris!

    Small Group Adventure

    Small Group adventures all over the globe travelling on local transport and staying in simple accommodation or even camping under the stars!

    Unmissable Experiences

    Don't miss out. Secure your place on the must do experiences and book your local tours with Trailfinders before you go.

PRIVACY POLICY

Trailfinders Ltd, and, on our behalf, comScore are committed to protecting the privacy of trailfinders.com users and the overall integrity of the internet.

These guidelines have been developed to comply with the Data Protection Act 1998.

Trailfinders Ltd and comScore take the following measures to protect our users' privacy as follows:

  1. We make a note of your title, first & last name, email address, house number and postcode but we do NOT pass your details on to any third parties; you will only receive information directly from Trailfinders.
  2. All our email correspondence includes an unsubscribe option.

Trailfinders Ltd and comScore collect information on our users through cookies (see below for further information). If you accept our cookies, please note that they will remain in your computer for five years unless you delete them. Please note that turning off cookies may limit your use of our website and services.

We may periodically send you email updates on special offers from Trailfinders Ltd. If you wish to opt out of receiving such information from us, please use the unsubscribe option included in all our emails to unsubscribe from such mailings.

What is a Cookie?

A cookie is a small, numeric text file that is stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Any information you provide to us is held securely on our web servers and not on your machine.

Trailfinders and comScore use cookies to track users as they travel through the site - for instance, we might use cookies to count the total number of unique users who are accessing the site over a particular period of time, or to monitor the success of a particular campaign. The use of cookies in this way helps to make our system more efficient.

Firewalls set up by your company network may prohibit the use of browser cookies.

If you would like to know more about cookies, or would like to disable cookies in your browser settings (i.e. refuse cookies) please visit www.aboutcookies.org.
