Privacy Policy

TRENDnet values our customers’ trust and is committed to the responsible management, use and protection of personal information. Should you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact us at webmaster@trendnet.com.

For example, we request information from you when you:

  1. Register for a Partner Program
  2. Register products for warranty
  3. Provide feedback
  4. Subscribe to a newsletter or a mailing list!
  5. Participate in promotional offers

You may make corrections and updates to your personal information by contacting webmaster@trendnet.com.

TRENDnet provides users with the ability to choose whether to receive such mailings, when you provide your personal information. You may also opt-out of receiving further marketing communications by sending an email to webmaster@trendnet.com.

Personal Information Disclosure
TRENDnet will not disclose your personal information to any outside organization without your consent. TRENDnet will take reasonable steps to protect user privacy consistent with the guidelines set forth in this policy and the applicable laws.

While we strive to protect your personal information, TRENDnet cannot guarantee protection of all information you transmit to us, and you do so at your own risk.
