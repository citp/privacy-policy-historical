# Website Usability Testing | User Testing | Web Usability Test by TryMyUI

Thank you for visiting TryMyUI. Your privacy is important to us. 

## Information we collect and how we use it

We do **NOT** share any information you give us with any other party. We use the information you give us only for the purposes of providing you with pricing quotes and other services customized for your particular needs. 

## Cookies

To enhance your experience, for example to avoid having to log in each time you visit the site, or to identify your session so we can provide service continuity, many of our web pages use "cookies." Cookies are text files we place in your computer's browser to store your preferences. Cookies, by themselves, do not tell us your e-mail address or other personally identifiable information. Our site will not function optimally if your browser is set to not accept cookies. 

From time to time, we may update this privacy statement. We encourage you to periodically check back and review this policy so that you always will know what information we collect, how we use it, and to whom we disclose it.

This Privacy Statement was updated on March 30, 2010.
