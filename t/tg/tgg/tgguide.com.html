Privacy Notice

We take your privacy very seriously! This is the web site of TGGuide.com. We will never share your personal information with anyone, except as legally required in connection with lawful investigations associated with allegations of member misconduct or safety concerns.

For each visitor to our web page, our web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address.

We collect aggregate information on what pages our website members and guests access or visit, along with information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations.

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our web page, to verify or enforce compliance with the policies governing our website and applicable laws or to protect against misuse or unauthorized use of our website.

With respect to cookies: We may set cookies to enhance the speed of your web browsing sessions, however, we do not use cookies for the purpose of tracking your activities on our site or any other sites.

With respect to ads: We are authorized affiliates of some dating sites and products or shopping services that appear on this site. All site content on TGGuide.com is free of charge and you are not required to purchase anything to continue using our services. However, if you decide to join one of our sponsor sites you may incur charges related to your membership or purchase at those sites. Please refer to the respective sites for information.

With respect to security: We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.

We can be reached via e-mail or you can reach us by telephone at 206-337-0299 if you have any questions or concerns regarding the privacy policy of TGGuide.com.
