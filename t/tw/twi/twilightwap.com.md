# Twilight Zone - Log In

### Privacy Policy

**Security**  
Twilight Zone's website takes every precaution to protect our users information. When users submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and off-line.

**Information Collection and Use**  
Twilight Zone is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to any outside parties. 

**Log Files**  
We use IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information. We do not distribute or share IP information with any third parties.

**Links**  
This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware Twilight Zone is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site.
