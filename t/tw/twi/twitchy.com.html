We use cookies to understand Internet usage and to improve our content, offerings and advertisements. For example, we may use cookies to personalize your experience at twitchy.com (e.g., to recognize you by name when you return to the site) and to save your password in password-protected areas. We are not responsible for the collection of these cookies and are not responsible for the actions of the ad service providers. We do not sponsor, endorse or guarantee any products or services advertised by third parties on twitchy.com.

We may also use small pieces of code such as “web beacons” or “clear gifs” to collect anonymous and aggregate advertising metrics, such as counting Site views, promotion views or advertising responses. These “web beacons” may be used to deliver cookies that conform to our cookie policy.
