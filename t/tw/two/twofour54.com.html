privacy policy

last updated on 28 October 2008

twofour54 FZ-LLC and its affiliates ("twofour54") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. Affiliate shall mean any person that controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with twofour54 FZ-LLC.
This Privacy Policy (together with the terms and conditions for use of the website found at the domain name www.twofour54.com and its relevant URL ("Website") located here ) sets out the basis on which any personal data twofour54 collects from you, or that you provide to it, will be processed. Please read the following carefully to understand twofour54's policies regarding information and data which twofour54 collects from you and how it will be treated.

1. INFORMATION COLLECTED FROM YOU

  1. twofour54 may collect and process the following data about you:
    1. Information that you provide by filling in forms available on the Website to be submitted to twofour54. twofour54 may also ask you for information when you report a problem with this Website to it;
    2. Records of correspondence that you send to twofour54 through the Website;
    3. Details of your visits to this Website including, but not limited to, traffic data, location data and other communication data, and the resources that you access; and
    4. Any other information, materials or data which you submit to twofour54 through the Website.
  2. If you are only browsing this Website, twofour54 does not capture data that allows it to identify you individually. Instead, twofour54 may collect information about your computer, including where available your internet protocol (IP) address, operating system and browser type, for system administration. Such information does not identify any individual.
  3. For the same reason, twofour54 may obtain information about your general internet usage by using a cookie file which is stored on the hard drive of your computer. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer's hard drive. They help twofour54 to improve this Website and enable it:
    1. to estimate its user size and usage pattern;
    2. to store information about your preferences, and so allow twofour54 to customise this Website according to your individual interests;
    3. to speed up your searches; and
    4. to recognise you when you return to this Website.
  4. You may refuse to accept cookies by activating the setting on your browser which allows you to refuse cookies. However, if you select this setting you may be unable to access certain parts of this Website or your experience of the Website may be impaired. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, the Website's system will issue cookies when you log on to this Website.
  5. Although twofour54 will do its best to protect your information which you have transmitted to it, twofour54 cannot guarantee the security of such information including but not limited to the information set out at clause 1.1 above.. Any transmission is at your own risk. Once your information is received by twofour54, twofour54 will use all reasonable procedures and security features to try to prevent unauthorised access.

2. DISCLOSURE OF YOUR INFORMATION

  1. twofour54 may disclose your personal information to third parties:
    1. if it is under a duty to disclose or share your personal data in order to comply with any legal obligation;
    2. in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Website; or
    3. to protect the rights, property, or safety of twofour54, its customers, its partners or other relevant third parties, including but not limited to exchanging information with other companies and organisations for the purposes of fraud protection and credit risk reduction.

3. YOUR RIGHTS

This Website may, from time to time, contain links to third party websites. If you follow a link to any of these websites, please note that these websites may have their own privacy policies and that twofour54 does not accept any responsibility or liability for these policies. Please check these policies before you submit any personal data to these websites.
