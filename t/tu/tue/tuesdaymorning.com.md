# Tuesday Morning

### Tuesday Morning Privacy Policy

Tuesday Morning values its customers and respects their privacy. Therefore, we do not sell or rent information you provide us online to third parties. We collect customer information in an effort to improve our customers' shopping experience and to communicate information about our products, services, and sale events. Tuesday Morning recognizes that it must maintain and use customer information responsibly. 

This policy describes the personal information we collect about you, why we collect it and how we use it. Our policy also describes the choices you can make about how we collect and use your information. If you have any questions about our privacy policy, contact us via email at [custserv@tuesdaymorning.com](http://mailto:custserv@tuesdaymorning.com/). 

### Collecting Information About You

You can visit most of our web pages without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. We may track the Internet domain address from which people visit us and analyze this data for trends and statistics, but individual users will remain anonymous unless you voluntarily tell us who you are when you make a purchase. 

There may be times, such as when you subscribe to a service or participate in a survey, at which we ask you to provide certain information about yourself. This may include your name, email, address, mailing address, phone number, birth date, gender, personal interests, etc. Whether or not to provide such information is your own personal choice. If you choose not to provide information that we request, you may be unable to access certain services, offers or content on our website. 

### How does Tuesday Morning use my information?

When you sign up for email at Tuesday Morning.com, we use your information to contact you regarding our monthly mailer, special sales, weekly ads, and store changes in your area. 

From time to time, we may also send you email surveys to help us learn about your shopping preferences so that we can make your Tuesday Morning shopping experience more enjoyable. 

### Security

We use sophisticated encryption and authentication tools to protect the security of your personal information that you share with us. Unfortunately, however, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee or warrant the security of any information you transmit to or from our web site, and you do so at your own risk. Once we receive your transmission, we do our best to protect its security on our systems. 

We urge you to keep any personal data, such as user names and passwords, in a safe place and not to divulge it to anyone. 

For the purpose of enabling better website design and a better user experience, we employ Google Analytics (a web service by Google, Inc.) to track site traffic and provide aggregate reports of how the public interacts with our website. Tier I session cookies, as detailed by OMB Memorandum M-10-22, are employed by Google Analytics in the form of client-side JavaScript code. In utilizing this analytics service,Tuesday Morning maintains its existing standards in regards to the sharing and disclosure of information, security and privacy safeguards for the data, and the data retention policy, each described below. Google Analytics analyzes website usage information automatically upon visits to Tuesday Morning websites. Users who do not wish to have their visit information collected by Google Analytics may opt-out by downloading a browser extension or by disabling cookies in their browser settings. Opting-out of web measurements will still permit users to access the full Tuesday Morning website, with all included information and functionality.
