# Tuberip - Browse Videos

* * *

Tuberip has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this web site.

Tuberip is commited to protecting your privacy. Tuberip does not sell, trade or rent your personal information to other companies. Tuberip will not collect any personal information about you except when you specifically and knowingly provide such information. By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Tuberip.

### Information Automatically Logged

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website. Your IP address is also used to help identify you for voting, comments, etc.

### Cookies 

We use an in-house ad system to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies to see how many times you have seen the advertisement. Cookies are also used throughout the site to remember your preferences.

### External Links 

This site contains links to other sites. Tuberip is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites.

### Public Forums 

This site may make chat rooms and forums/message boards available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

If we decide to make any changes to our privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page.
