TitanTV.com Privacy Policy

Broadcast Interactive Media, L.L.C. believes in your right to know what information is collected during your visit to our site and how the information is used and safeguarded.

Broadcast Interactive collects mailing address information for the specific purpose of identifying over-the-air broadcast signals that might be received at that address. The purpose for doing this is to more accurately present to the user what television programming is available and to provide the best program guide service.

Broadcast Interactive may aggregate this location information to provide visual displays of users of the site, such as maps. The specific information is not disclosed to third parties.

Broadcast Interactive places a cookie on your hard drive with encrypted information that is useful only to Broadcast Interactive’s server. This encrypted information allows the user to navigate through the site quicker, maintains personal settings and preferences, and allows for reduced data processing and transferring when a user returns to the site.

The Broadcast Interactive web server automatically recognizes only the domain name, but not the e-mail address, of anyone who visits our site. We collect aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit. This information is used to generate reports that help Broadcast Interactive assess the value and interest in its web site.

Broadcast Interactive collects the e-mail addresses for users who choose to receive information updates, who post messages to our bulletin board, and who communicate with us via e-mail, as well as information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and site registrations.

User e-mail addresses may be used by us to:

  • customize the content and layout of our page for each consumer
  • notify consumers about updates to our web site; or
  • contact consumers to notify them of new Broadcast Interactive products and services or upgrades to existing products

If you DO NOT want your e-mail address to be used for the above purposes, please let us know by contacting us via the e-mail address, telephone number or mailing address listed below and specifying that you do not want to receive e-mail from Broadcast Interactive.

If you supply us with your postal address online, you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending e-mail to us at the below address, calling us at the below telephone number or writing to us at the below address and specify in your request that you do not want to receive postal mailings from Broadcast Interactive.

The collected mailing address information is displayed on the site while you are using the site. If this information is incorrect or you would like this information deleted from our database, please let us know by sending e-mail to us at the below address, calling us at the below telephone number or writing to us at the below address and specify in your request to correct or delete information.

Third party advertisements may be displayed on the site, and may also contain cookies. Broadcast Interactive does not control these advertiser cookies and users of Broadcast Interactive’s site should check the privacy policy of the advertiser to see whether and how it uses cookies. Broadcast Interactive does not share any collected information with a third party site.
