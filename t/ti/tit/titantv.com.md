# TitanTV Privacy Policy - The Definitive Source for Analog, Digital (HDTV), Cable and Satellite TV Listings

Decisionmark Corp. believes in your right to know what information is collected during your visit to our site and how the information is used and safeguarded. 

Decisionmark collects mailing address information for the specific purpose of identifying over-the-air broadcast signals that might be received at that address. The purpose for doing this is to more accurately present to the user what television programming is available and to provide the best program guide service. 

Decisionmark may aggregate this location information to provide visual displays of users of the site, such as maps. The specific information is not disclosed to third parties. 

Decisionmark places a cookie on your hard drive with encrypted information that is useful only to Decisionmark’s server. This encrypted information allows the user to navigate through the site quicker, maintains personal settings and preferences, and allows for reduced data processing and transferring when a user returns to the site. 

The Decisionmark web server automatically recognizes only the domain name, but not the e-mail address, of anyone who visits our site. We collect aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit. This information is used to generate reports that help Decisionmark assess the value and interest in its web site. 

Decisionmark collects the e-mail addresses for users who choose to receive information updates, who post messages to our bulletin board, and who communicate with us via e-mail, as well as information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and site registrations. 

User e-mail addresses may be used by us to: 

  * customize the content and layout of our page for each consumer 
  * notify consumers about updates to our web site; or 
  * contact consumers to notify them of new Decisionmark products and services or upgrades to existing products 



If you DO NOT want your e-mail address to be used for the above purposes, please let us know by contacting us via our e-mail address, telephone number or mailing address listed below and specifying that you do not want to receive e-mail from Decisionmark.

If you supply us with your postal address online, you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending e-mail to us at the above address, calling us at the above telephone number or writing to us at the above address and specify in your request that you do not want to receive postal mailings from Decisionmark. 

The collected mailing address information is displayed on the site while you are using the site. If this is information is incorrect or you would like this information deleted from our database, please let us know by sending e-mail to us at the above address, calling us at the above telephone number or writing to us at the above address and specify in your request to correct or delete information. 

Third party advertisements may be displayed on the site, and may also contain cookies. Decisionmark does not control these advertiser cookies and users of Decisionmark’s site should check the privacy policy of the advertiser to see whether and how it uses cookies. Decisionmark does not share any collected information with a third party site.
