Ticketek Privacy Policy

We Respect Your Privacy

Ticketek respects the privacy of all our customers and business contacts, and is committed to safeguarding the personal information which you provide us. Also, Ticketek is subject to the requirements of the National Privacy Principles which are contained in the Privacy Act 1988 (the Privacy Act).

Our Privacy Policy outlines how Ticketek will handle personal information about you. As we collect personal information online through www.ticketek.com (our website), this Privacy Policy also outlines our approach to online privacy issues (at 8 below).

Our Privacy Policy is divided into the following sections:

  1. How is personal information collected?
  2. What personal information does Ticketek collect, and why?
  3. Will personal information be given to anyone else?
  4. Security of personal information
  5. Anonymous transactions
  6. Non-customer information
  7. Access and correction
  8. Online privacy issues
  9. Additional privacy information and how to contact Ticketek

Privacy Policy last updated on Tuesday 15 May 2007.
