Secure Ordering

Your information is secure with TicketCity.com. We use state-of-the-art secure server software (SSL) to prevent your valuable information from being visible to third parties.

Our secure server software encrypts the information you enter before it is sent over the Internet. This greatly decreases the ability of outsiders to gain access to, and use your personal data or credit card information. When you order from TicketCity.com, you will be joining one of the thousands of people who have placed orders or inquiries with us online without credit card fraud.

Privacy Policy

At TicketCity.com, we recognize and respect the importance of maintaining the privacy of our customers and visitors and have established this privacy policy as a demonstration of our firm commitment to privacy. The following describes why we gather customer and visitor information, what information we collect, how we collect it, and what we use the information for.

Information and data collected by TicketCity.com is used exclusively for order fulfillment, in-house research, and our own site development and marketing efforts. No data or personal information will ever be sold or traded to any third party for any reason.

When you order from our web site, we collect the following information: your name, telephone numbers, billing information (credit card number, expiration date, CVV2/CID and billing address), products purchased, and any relevant information.

We use the information we collect from you to process your order. Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services. And your contact information may be used to send news and offerings from TicketCity.com. But this is an optional feature, so users may opt-out, at any time, from receiving this by information by contacting our customer service department. Or by following the details included within the promotional material, email, etc.

A "cookie" is a small file stored on your computer by a web site which gives you a numeric user ID and stores certain information about your activity on the site. We use cookies to store information on your product selections and search queries as you shop on our site, but we do not store any personal information or financial information about you using cookies.

We also use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart and to gather broad demographic information. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but most allow you to instruct your browser to prevent the use of cookies. However, if you disable this feature, certain aspects of the TicketCity.com site, such as the shopping cart, may not function properly.

TicketCity.com reserves the right, at any time, to modify, alter or update these policies, and you agree to be bound by such modifications, alterations, or updates.
