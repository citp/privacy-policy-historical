# TicketCity.com - Sports Tickets

  
Secure Ordering

Your information is secure with TicketCity.com. We use state-of-the-art secure server software (SSL) to prevent your valuable information from being visible to third parties.  


Our secure server software encrypts the information you enter before it is sent over the Internet. This greatly decreases the ability of outsiders to gain access to, and use your personal data or credit card information. When you order from TicketCity.com, you will be joining one of the thousands of people who have placed orders or inquiries with us online without credit card fraud.You can know which pages are secure on our site and any site by looking at the URL in your browser's address bar. You will see that our shopping cart pages begin with "http **s** ://" instead of "http://," indicating that those pages are secure. Many browers also display a lock symbol  at the bottom of the page which you can view to check a page's security.

Privacy Statement

TicketCity.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our web site. Your privacy is assured because you have our promise that any personal information you provide will never be used by anyone not employed by TicketCity.com. It is not the policy of TicketCity.com to loan, sell, or share customer information to anyone.  


Our site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect visitor's contact information and financial information. Contact information from the order form is used to send orders and information about news and offerings from TicketCity.com. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings by contacting our [customer service department](mailto:customer_service@ticketcity.com). Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services..
