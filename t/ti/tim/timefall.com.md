**Timefall.com PRIVACY POLICY**

**1\. General**

**1.1.** This is the official Privacy and Policy for Timefall.com. Timefall.com can be used in this Privacy Policy as ”Timefall“, “site”, “we”, “us” or “our”.

**2\. Collection and Use of Personal Information**

**2.1.** Timefall.com uses any personal information available for us to respond to our visitors/costumers inquiry or comments. Whatever the activity may be, we will only collect information to the extent deemed reasonably necessary to fulfill your requests and our legitimate business objectives.

**2.2.** Timefall.com may request and/or collect information from you on a voluntary basis when you: register with us, or when you send questions or comments via e-mail to Timefall.com. The requested information may include personal information such as your name and e-mail address.

**2.3.** Timefall.com may also collect your IP address for safety and security purposes. An IP address is a number that is used by computers on the network to identify your computer every time you log on to the Internet.

**2.4.** Other non-personal information collected by Timefall.com is in the form of logs – files that record website activity, including how many “hits” a particular web page is getting. These entries enable Timefall.com to assess overall site activity, track interest in advertised sales, track interest in purchase behavior, and troubleshoot technical concerns.

**2.5.** Through the use of log files, we may also collect certain demographic information such as the type of browser you are using, the type of operating system you are using and the domain name of your Internet service provider.

Even if we have a relationship with the third party, we do not control those sites or their policies and practices regarding your Personal Information.

**4.3.** This Privacy Policy does not cover any use of Personal Information that a third party advertiser or ad server may collect from you. We encourage you to investigate and ask questions directly of them in this regard. Use of Advertisers’ tracking technology is subject to their own privacy policies.

**7\. Phishing**

**7.1.** Phishers use spoofed e-mails to lead visitor/consumers to counterfeit websites designed to trick recipients into divulging Personal Information such as account usernames and passwords. If somebody is asking these things form you or you receive such an email, please forward it to the site’s Privacy Policy e-mail support@timefall.com and delete the fraudulent e-mail.
