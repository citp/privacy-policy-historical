5 Cookies

We may obtain information about Your usage of Tipped by using "cookies", which are small pieces of information stored by Your browser on Your computer's hard drive. We use cookies to allow Us to understand who has seen which pages and advertisements, to determine how frequently particular pages are visited, to determine the most popular areas of Tipped and generally in order to monitor usage of Tipped. This information helps Us to improve Tipped and to deliver a better and more personalised service by allowing us to:

  • record statistical data about Our users' browsing actions so that We can estimate Our audience size and usage pattern:
  • store information about Your preferences, and so allow Us to customise Tipped according to Your individual interests;
  • speed up Your searches; and
  • recognise You when You return to Tipped.

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, though You do not have to. We do not control the use of cookies by third parties. If You wish to disable cookies then You can do so by readjusting Your browser settings although please note that by disabling cookies You may not be able to register with or use all features of Tipped. Please be aware that if You refuse cookies some of the features of Tipped may not work and You may be unable to access certain parts of Tipped.

For more information on cookies and how to disable them, You can consult the information provided by the Interactive Advertising Bureau at www.allaboutcookies.org.
