Whenever possible, we indicate which fields are required and which fields are optional.

We also allow users to pay by using PayPal. If a user chooses to pay by PayPal, the PayPal privacy policy will be accessible on PayPal’s website.

If you participate, we will request certain personally identifiable information from you. Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary and you therefore have a choice whether or not to disclose this information. The requested information typically includes contact information (such as name and email address) and demographic information (such as zip code). We use this information to notify contest winners and to award prizes. We may use a third-party service provider to conduct these surveys or contests.

Information from third parties. In order to provide certain services to you, we may on occasion supplement the personal information you submitted to us with information from third-party sources.

Automatic Information. As is true of most web sites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information does not identify individual users and includes, but it is not limited to, (internet protocol (IP) addresses,) browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data (“Non-Personally Identifiable Information”). 

Cookies. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user's computer for record-keeping purposes. We and our third-party advertising and analytics providers use cookies on the Site to record information about your activities on the Site and to "remember" you when you return to the Site. Some cookies remain on your computer until you delete them others, like session ID cookies, expire when you close your browser.    You may set your browser setting to attempt to reject cookies and you may still use the Site; however, your ability to use some functionality and areas of the Site, such as contests or surveys, will be limited. See the section “Do not track settings” below for more information on how the Site may respond to your browser settings.  We do not control the use of cookies by third parties. See the section on "Third-party advertisers and analytics providers” below and review the privacy policies of each of the third party advertising and analytics providers for more information on third party cookies.  

Clear GIFs (web beacons, pixels and bugs). Clear GIFs (aka web beacons, pixels, web bugs) are tiny graphics with a unique identifier that are used to track the online movements of web users. Unlike cookies, which are stored on a user's computer hard drive, clear GIFs are small graphics that are about the size of the period at the end of the sentence that are embedded invisibly on web pages or in HTML-based emails. We and our third party advertising and analytics providers may place Clear GIFs on our web site that track what other web sites you visit (both before and after visiting the Site).  We and our providers use information obtained from Clear GIFs to analyze the effectiveness of advertisements on our Site as well as on other web sites and to help us improve our business and the Site. We may also use clear GIFs in our HTML-based emails to let us know which emails have been opened by recipients. This allows us to gauge the effectiveness of certain communications and the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.  We do not control the use of Clear GIFs by third parties. See the section on "Third-party advertisers and analytics providers” below and review the privacy policies of each of the third party providers for more information on third party Clear GIFs.

Third-party advertising and analytics providers. Ads appearing on the Site may be delivered to users by third-party advertising providers, such as Google. We work with third party advertising providers for purposes such as targeting our advertisements on the Site, and, in doing so we may share with those advertising providers, Non-Personally Identifiable Information about users’ use of the Site.  We use third party advertising and analytics providers, such as Google Analytics, Quantcast, Adroll, Adobe Omniture, SaleCycle, TagMan, ChannelAdvisor, AdChoices, and Exact Target, to collect information about Site usage and users of our site, including demographic and interest-level information. 

Our advertising and analytics providers use cookies and Clear GIFs in order to collect demographic and interest-level information and usage information from users that visit the Site, including but not limited (to information about the pages where users enter and exit the Site, what pages and advertisements users view and visit on the Site, and what items users purchase. Cookies and Clear GIFs allow advertisers and analytics providers to recognize a user when a user visits the Site but also when a user visits other websites. Our advertising and analytics providers use the information they collect from the Site and other web sites to profile users in order to display relevant advertising and to share with us and other web site operators information about users including, but not limited to, age range, gender, geographic regions, general interest and details about devices used to visit web sites and purchase items. We do not link information we receive from our analytics partners with personally identifiable information. 

Specifically, with respect to Google, we use the following Google Analytics advertising features: Display Network Impression Reporting, Google Analytics Demographics and Interest and Remarketing with Google Analytics. For more information regarding Google’s use of cookies and collection and use of information see the Google Privacy Policy. If you would like to opt out of Google Analytics tracking, please visit one of the following links: Google Ads Preference Manager or the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
