###  Direct Advertisers 

In order to issue an official invoice, we ask everyone who purchases banner ads or any other advertising or sponsorship on our site, to provide the full corporate name, address and CIF. We charge 21% VAT on all invoices to companies located in Spain. The VAT is not charged to firms located outside Spain. The information collected in this manner is maintained by us and never made available to third parties.

###  Collection of Statistical Data 

This site does not collect or store personal data of its visitors. 

Only, in order to provide the best service through this page, and in order to facilitate its use, we analyze the number of pages viewed, the number of visits, and the activity of visitors to the web, and their frequency of use. 

For this purpose, we use the statistical data compiled by the Internet Service Provider of www.typicallyspanish.com and also Google.inc 

The Privacy Policy of Google is in the <http://www.google.es/privacypolicy.html>

With this information we can analyse the visitor numbers of www.typicallyspanish.com from the connection data and determine the most visited sections and pages.
