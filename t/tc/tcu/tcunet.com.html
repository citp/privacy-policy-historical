Privacy Statement

The following discloses our information collecting and dissemination practices for our web site.  Teachers Credit Union is committed to safeguarding the on-line privacy of our members.  We are obligated to make sure that your on-line banking is safe and secure.  We do not share any information collected from our members with any third parties and only utilize the information collected to improve our web site and the Home Banking Service.  Your account and personal information are password protected and only you have access.  You may change your password at any time through the User Options section in Home Banking.  We recommend that you do not tell your password to anyone.  It is also recommended that you sign off of Home Banking when you have finished a session.

If you have provided TCU with your e-mail address, we will periodically send you updates and special offers related to TCU products and services.  If  do not want to receive these notices, you may remove your e-mail address in the "User Options" section of our Home Banking Service.

If you have any questions about our Privacy Statement, please contact webmaster@tcunet.com.
