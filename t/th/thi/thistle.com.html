Privacy Policy

Date of issue 22nd April 2008

This site is operated by Guoman Hotel Management (UK) Limited for users in the United Kingdom. “Thistle” and “Thistle Hotels” our trading names used under license in the UK. This privacy statement has been prepared to demonstrate our commitment to the privacy of our customers’ and to assure you that your personal information, which will be treated appropriately by us at all times.

Use of personal information we collect.

We will use the personal data you provide via this website to:

  • Deal with your booking and other requests, Administer your booking and other services,
  • Tailor our services to your requirements and preferences,

With your agreement we also provide you with information about other products, services and special offers which we offer, and/or products and services made available by selected partners, including other companies within the corporate group of which we are a part.

Disclosures

We will not sell your personal information to third parties without your prior consent. We will only disclose your information to other Guoman Hotels Group companies and our selected business partners and service providers:

  • For the purpose of administrating your requests and any booking;
  • For providing other services which you have requested; and
  • Where you have agreed, to communicate offer of other goods and services.

Cookies

Our site uses “cookies” to keep track of your visits to the site. A “Cookie” is a small file that can be stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. No personal information is stored on the Cookie. Cookies may be used to compile anonymous statistics related to the use of services or patterns of browsing. When used in this manner you are not individually identified and data collected in this manner is only used in aggregate.

You can usually change your browser's settings so that it will not accept cookies; although this may restrict some website functionality (refer to the 'Help' facility)

Security of your information

We follow strict security procedures in the storage and disclosure of information which you have provided us. This is to prevent unauthorised access or unlawful processing of your personal information. For additional peace of mind, credit card details are not recorded on the website when booking a room.

Your consent

By using our website you consent to the collection, storage and processing of your personal information in the manner set out in this privacy policy. Should we change our privacy policy, we will post the changes on this website so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use and under what circumstances we disclose it.

Opt out / Data quality / Access

Our site provides you with the opportunity to opt out of receiving written promotional and marketing information or opt in to receiving electronic marketing information. You can do this by:

  • Sending an email to webmaster@thistle.co.uk with your name, address and (if you have made a booking with us) a confirmation number.
  • Selecting the appropriate opt in/out button each time you submit a form containing your personal details on this website.
