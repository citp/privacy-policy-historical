Privacy Policy
This privacy policy explains what we, P to M Limited, do with the personal information you give us. In general, we use the personal information that we collect from you to help you enjoy our Site, to identify your personal preferences and to match your needs with any relevant services or products. This policy only applies to the "theboatrace.org" website and not to the companies, individuals, organisations or other websites which may be linked to our Site.

Consent

Your use of the Site offered by or through our website signifies your consent to us collecting and using personal information about you in accordance with this policy. Should we choose to change this policy for any reason, the changes will be posted here, so that you are kept up to date about how we collect and use your personal information, and when we may disclose it.

How we collect information from you and how it is used

We collect information about you when you visit our website and when you use the Site. Some of the information we collect and uses to which we put the information includes:

1. When you register for a service you will need to give some or all of the following information: your name, e-mail address, postal delivery address, credit/debit card details so that the order can be processed and (where appropriate) delivered to you. Sometimes we may also need your telephone and/or mobile number.

2. We may collect information about your tastes and preferences both during the registration stage and by analysis of customer traffic, including by using cookies (see below).

3. Where we run our Site or any other services jointly with a third party ("Associate"), then we may share any information you give us in relation to that game, service or promotion with the Associate. The Associate will be named as such on our website.

4. We or our Associate(s) may use the personal information that we collect about you to personalise your visits to our website and to recommend our own or our affiliate's goods or services to you which you might find interesting. We also use the information to help us develop the design and layout of our website to ensure that our sites are as useful and enjoyable as possible.

5. We may use personal information collected about you to let you know about functionality changes to our website or changes to our terms and conditions and/or this policy.

6. We may carry out research to support our business and/or provide aggregate statistics about our services, sales, customers, traffic patterns and other site information to third parties such as advertisers, but these statistics will not include any information that could personally identify you.

Cookies

"Cookies" are small files of information which are stored on your computer. Adverts may contain cookies. We do use cookies in our Site. In addition, cookies received with banner ads may be collected by the ad agencies, and we do not have any control over this information.

External Links

Our website has advertisements and links to other websites and services. We are not responsible for the privacy policies, practices or content of these third parties.

When we may share your information with other people

We will not sell, share, or rent your information to others except as set out in this policy. We will only disclose personal information to other companies within our group of companies, our Associates, and to those suppliers we engage to process data on our behalf. These people may themselves gather information for their own purposes over which we have no control. When we supply your details to them we ask them to keep your details secure and not pass them on to third parties and unless you object to only use your details to market their own products to you. If our company and/or our assets are transferred to another person or company, then your personal information may need to be transferred.

We reserve the right to access and disclose individually identifiable information to comply with applicable laws and lawful government requests, to operate our systems properly or to protect ourself or our users.

How you may cancel your registration

You may at any time inform us that you do not want to receive information from us (for example, news services and bulletins), by sending an email to: editor@theboatrace.org
