# The Monitor - McAllen, Texas

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and appreciate the confidence you have placed in themonitor.com as your primary source of information about The Rio Grande Valley. We are committed to protecting your privacy and preserving your trust.

We will ensure that no one not affiliated with themonitor.com has access to information you provide during your time on our site.   
We will not distribute personal information to advertisers, sponsors or vendors without your explicit permission. We may include demographic information in reports to our partners or vendors without referencing names, phone numbers, email addresses or other identifying information. 

We will give you the opportunity to update information you provide to us to ensure that it is accurate and reflects your continued interest in the services provided by themonitor.com. This information assures that we provide up-to-date, appropriate, high-quality information to you. 

Our requests for information are intended to enhance your experience at themonitor.com. They are not intended to be intrusive or unwelcome. 

We may, with your permission, occasionally offer you an opportunity to participate in an event or purchase a product or service that we find worthwhile. These offers never will be made without advance notice and your permission. 

If you have concerns or questions about the specifics of our policy, please email us at [webmaster@themonitor.com ](mailto:webmaster@themonitor.com).
