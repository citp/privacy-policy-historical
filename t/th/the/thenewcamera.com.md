# Privacy Policy « NEW CAMERA

> **At thenewcamera.com, the privacy of our visitors and members is importance to us.**
> 
> **Cookies**  
>  thenewcamera.comuse cookies to store information about visitors preferences, type of internet browser they are using so we customize our webspage to make our website more user friendly.
> 
> Some of our advertising partners may use cookies on our site.
> 
> **Personal Information**  
>  We collect personal information such as First name, Last name and email.We do not collect this information unless users/visitors submit it to us. If you wish to remove your personal information then you can do so via the unsubscibe link at the bottom of each email that is sent to you.
> 
> **Communication**  
>  We send daily updates to our subscribed members via email, if anyone wish to un-subscribe he/she should click on unsubscibe link at the bottom of each email that is sent to you.
> 
> if you have any question or problem you can contact us at  contact@thenewcamera.com
