PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated February 15, 2010
ALL READERS:
Information that is gathered from all readers:
No individually identifying information is collected about readers of the comic.
Connection traffic, such as files requested, browser type, or other similar items, are stored in our website's traffic log.

Cookies may be used to remember visitor preferences when interacting with the website.

How this information is used:
Traffic is logged for the purpose of tracking general patterns such as overall site usage, internet browsers used, or similar items that will allow developers to create a better user experience. This will allow better planning for server load management, and may be used to count overall readership numbers. These readership numbers may be used for developing advertising strategies.

Cookies are used solely for the purpose of storing user preferences on the user's own machine, and are not tracked by The Zombie Hunters or The Zombie Hunters, USA.


NEWSPOST COMMENTING:
Information that is gathered:
A username and email address are required for anyone who wishes to post a comment on the newspost area.

How this information is used:
This information is required in an effort to reduce spam.
Email addresses are not sold, distributed, or in any way made public.


FORUM USERS:
Information that is gathered:
Registration is required to make posts on the forum. Email addresses and usernames are collected for the purpose of providing a reasonably secure forum environment. Names, ages, and other such information are not required for forum membership.

How this information is used:
Usernames are used to publicly identify the user to the rest of the forum. Email addresses are stored privately unless the user chooses otherwise. This information is not sold or otherwise distributed. The Zombie Hunters, and The Zombie Hunters, USA are not responsible for any content posted on the forum or the actions of any member of the forum.


CUSTOMERS:
Information that is gathered:
Customer information is not retained on this site. PayPal may collect customer information in accordance with their own privacy policy. The PayPal Privacy Policy can be found here: https://www.paypal.com/cgi-bin/webscr?cmd=p/gen/ua/policy_privacy-outside
