# Privacy Policy

**This Privacy Policy is issued to all our valued customers to the requirements of the Personal Data Protection.**

### What information we collect?

We collect and manage all user’s information that you provided to us while you register on our site or subscribe our newsletter. This is ThemeFreesia Privacy Policy, While using [ThemeFreesia](http://themefreesia.com/), you must agree the terms of this Privacy Policy. Theme Freesia is a web design/ development agency specializing in WordPress themes & plugins. We collect the information from every visitors to monitor traffic and fix the bugs.

### Why Do We Use Your Information?

The information which we have collected from you will be used for several purposes:-  
To send email  
To personalize your experience  
To process transactions  
To notify about the Bugs  
To notify you about the updates made changes to this website, new services and other special offers which we think you will find valuable.  
To improve customer service  
If you desire not to receive these offers then you may simply click the unsubscribe link contained in each email.  
To send periodic emails  
You are able to view, change and remove your data associated with your account. If you choose to delete your account, we will delete all data that is related to you.

### Do we use cookies?

Yes.A cookie is a file containing an identifier which is sent by a web server to a web browser and stored by the browser. The identifier sent back to the server each and every time the browser requests a page from the server.We use cookies to understand and save your preferences for future visits.

### Do we Share your Data/ Information?

We don’t share any of your personal information with third parties without your consent. Only gathered into mass data is periodically transmitted to external services to help us to improve the ThemeFreesia Website and service. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety.

### What about Third Party Links?

We may include or offer or recommended any third party products or services on our website. These third party sites have separate and independent privacy policy so we are not responsible for the practices employed by websites linked to or from our site nor the information. We also inquire to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.

### Your Permission

By using our site, you give an affirmative reply to our privacy policy.

### Change Privacy Policy

If we change our privacy policy then we will notify you by posting an announcement through emails or changes on this page. ThemeFreesia have right to amend the Privacy Policy if required.

### Third Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information.

### Contact Us

If you have any queries or concern questions regarding this Privacy Policy then you may [Contact Us](http://themefreesia.com/contact-us) using below this information.
