Privacy Policy

TheTravelEditor.com ("We", "us") recognise the importance of your privacy and keeping your details safe. However, at the same time we encourage you to share the responsibility for keeping your personal details safe by following Internet safety guidelines and not disclosing information that could be used fraudulently.

Information We Collect

All information you provide to TheTravelEditor.com will be used by TheTravelEditor.com for internal purposes only and will not be shared with 3rd parties. These purposes include but are not limited to the following: completing the transactions required to make a purchase, sending occasional solicitations for feedback, password reminders, sending newsletters and announcements of new products or services offered through TheTravelEditor.com, and sending occasional referrals to TheTravelEditor.com affiliates in which you have expressed interest.

Required Information

TheTravelEditor.com only requires a valid email address and password to register. We strongly recommend that you do not use a password that you use on other Internet sites that might hold more sensitive personal data. TheTravelEditor.com provides the option to change your password, which We recommend you do on a regular basis.

Optional Information

Customers may create a screen name on TheTravelEditor.com, however this is optional. If you decide to make use of a screen name, we suggest that you do not use your full name. Other optional profile information that you can provide include first and last names, address, country of residence, gender, and age. TheTravelEditor.com also allows users the option to upload photographs and blog entries, create travel itineraries, invite friends to join TheTravelEditor.com and make book recommendations. 

Questionnaires, Surveys and Profile Data

From time to time, we offer optional questionnaires and surveys to our users for such purposes as collecting demographic information or assessing users' interests and needs. The use of the information collected will be explained in detail in the survey itself. If we collect personally identifiable information from our users in these questionnaires and surveys, the users will be given notice of how the information will be used prior to their participation in the survey or questionnaire.

Customer Service Correspondence

If you send us correspondence, including emails, we retain such information in the records of your account. We will also retain customer service correspondence and other correspondence from TheTravelEditor.com to you. We retain these records in order to keep records of our relationship, to measure and improve our customer service, and to investigate potential violations of our Terms of Service.

Financial Information

TheTravelEditor.com does NOT store customer financial information, including credit/debit card numbers or PayPal account details.

Accessing and Changing Your Information

Customers may access and change their profile information at any time via TheTravelEditor.com website. Customers may request their account, and all associated data, be deleted by contacting us at hello@thetraveleditor.com.

Customers may communicate with TheTravelEditor.com by email at any time at hello@thetraveleditor.com.
