# TheThingsIWant.com : Privacy Statement.

Privacy Statement:

****TheThingsIWant.com is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. We will never share any personally identifiable information about you with any other organization that could use it to send you unsolicited mail or email without your explicit permission. As a measure of our commitment to your privacy we fully disclose what information we track, how we use it and your role in this process. Our privacy statement answers the following key questions:

1\. What personally identifiable information of yours or third party personally identification is collected from you through the web site   
2\. The organization collecting the information   
3\. How the information is used   
4\. With whom the information may be shared   
5\. What choices are available to you regarding collection, use and distribution of the information   
6\. The kind of security procedures that are in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of information under the control of **TheThingsIWant.com**   
7\. How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information.

Introduction to Privacy

**TheThingsIWant.com** is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. **TheThingsIWant.com** collects information from our users at several different points on our website.

Registration

In order to set up a personal list of **TheThingsIWant.com** , a user must first complete the short registration form. During registration a user is required to give their contact information (such as name and email address). The User is also asked to make up a password so that their information is protected. This information is used to contact the user about the services on our site for which they have expressed interest. We do not require or ask for information on age or income. We give users the option to give us their preferred shipping address, but only so that it may be shared with their potential gifters if they so choose.

Cookies

Cookies are tiny text files stored on the customer's computer by the Web server and can be read only by the Web server that placed the file. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it. Cookies are used for, among other things, remembering the user names of the people associated with that computer and it will simplify the log-in process of the site.

IP Address

Your IP address is a 12-digit number that is assigned to your computer by the Internet Service Provider each time you log on the Internet. The IP address enables sites that you visit to send data, such as the Web page, to your computer. IP addresses are automatically gathered by the Web server and help us diagnose problems with our server and help us administrate the security and service of the site.

"Referrer"

A "referrer" is the information sent by your Web browser that references the web URL you linked from, and is automatically gathered by our Web server. This information may be used to identify broad demographic trends of our users.

Sharing

**TheThingsIWant.com** does not share any personally identifiable information with anyone without the specific approval of the customer.  
**TheThingsIWant.com** does share aggregated demographic information with our partners and advertisers. This is not linked to any personal information that can identify any individual person.   
The user may invite their friends to visit **TheThingsIWant.com** to see their list of wants in which case the user initiates an email to their friend. **TheThingsIWant.com** keeps a record of that email address for the sole purpose of that one-time email. If the friend wishes their email address to be removed from our records altogether they may do so by contacting us [contact@thethingsiwant.com](mailto:contact@thethingsiwant.com)

Links

This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that **TheThingsIWant.com** is not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site. 

Surveys & Contests

From time-to-time our site requests information from users via surveys or contests. Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary and the user therefore has a choice whether or not to disclose this information. Information requested may include contact information (such as name and shipping address), and demographic information (such as zip code, age level). Contact information will be used to notify the winners and award prizes. Survey information will be used for purposes of monitoring or improving the use and satisfaction of this site. 

Security

This website takes many precautions to protect our users' information. Users are the only people authorized to alter their personal information. The information collected by this site is stored in a secure operating environment that is not available to the public. 

Site and Service Updates / Choice to Opt-out

We send all new members a welcoming email to verify password and username. Established members will occasionally receive product information updates about this service and site. Out of respect for the privacy of our users we present the option to not receive these types of communications other than the initial welcoming email. Users can opt-out at the moment of registration or by simply logging on and changing their personal information in "Manage Personal Info".

Special Offers from Partners and Affiliates / Choice to Opt-out

We also send the user information on products, services and special deals that are presented from time to time by our affiliates and partners. Out of respect for the privacy of our users we present the option to not receive these types of communications other than the initial welcoming email. Users can opt-out at the moment of registration or by simply logging on and changing their personal information in "Manage Personal Info".

Correction/Updating Personal Information:

If a user's personally identifiable information changes (such as your zip code), or if a user no longer desires our service, it can be updated easily at the "Manage Personal Info" page. At our website the user is in total control of all their personally identifiable information.

Legal Disclaimer

We may disclose personal information when required by law or in the good faith belief that such information is necessary in order to conform to the edicts of the law or comply with a legal process served on the site. 

Notification of Changes

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on our Homepage so our users are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it. If at any point we decide to use personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time it was collected, we will notify users by way of an email. Users will have a choice as to whether or not we use their information in this different manner. We will use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected.

Contacting the Website

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, this site's privacy practices, or any dealings with the site, you are welcome to contact: Jo Carraway [contact@thethingsiwant.com](mailto:contact@thethingsiwant.com)
