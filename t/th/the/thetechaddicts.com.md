# Privacy Notice : The Tech Addicts, Your source for console upgrades and repairs

**Privacy Policy**

We do not store any personal financial information. We do however store your name, address, contact information, etc. from your user registration. We do not disclose this information to anyone. It is only used for shipping, and if we need to contact you regarding your order.
