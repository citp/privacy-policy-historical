Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

We take your privacy seriously. TheDiamondGames.com does not collect or share personal information. 

Google Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics on any of our website properties.

Routine Information Collection

All web servers track basic information about their visitors. This information includes, but is not limited to, IP addresses, browser details, timestamps and referring pages. While we do not have access to this information, the web hosting company may retain some records. Generally, all web traffic goes through routers owned by various companies and some legal action such as subpoenas or routine monitoring may make your information available to other parties in either anonymized, aggregated, or non-aggregated forms. This is a function of the internet and is not specific or particular to our website.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Third parties may also use cookies, scripts and/or web beacons to track visitors. Such tracking is done directly by the third parties through their own servers and is subject to their own privacy policies. We add an affiliate code to some eCommerce site links that results in small commissions be paid back when you make purchases, but we do not work with any sites that share personally identifiable information, and this information is not identifiable to any particular user. Our webhost also uses a cookie to identify if youve viewed a media file before and to serve you a cached version, saving you time and bandwidth when loading the file.

Controlling Your Privacy

Note that you can change your browser settings to disable cookies if you have privacy concerns. Disabling cookies for all sites is not recommended as it may interfere with your use of some sites. The best option is to disable or enable cookies on a per-site basis. Consult your browser documentation for instructions on how to block cookies and other tracking mechanisms. Please note that disabling cookies does not completely protect your privacy, and third parties such as your employer or internet service provide will be unaffected.

Contact Information
