Privacy Policy

The Leela welcomes you to its website and looks forward to a meaningful interaction with you. The Leela respects your right to privacy. Any personal information that you share with us, like your name, date of birth, address, marital status, telephone number, credit card particulars and the like, shall be entitled to privacy and kept confidential.

The Leela assures you that your personal information shall not be used/disclosed by it, save for the purpose of doing the intended business with you, or if required to be disclosed under the due process of law.

The Leela assures you that in the event of your personal information being shared with its holding company, such sharing of information shall be for the purpose of doing the intended business with you.

The Leela reserves its rights to collect, analyse and disseminate aggregate site usage patterns of all its visitors with a view to enhancing services to its visitors. This includes sharing the information with its holding company as a general business practice.

In the course of its business The Leela may hold on-line contests and surveys as permitted by law and it reserves its right to use and disseminate the information so collected to enhance its services to the visitors. This shall also include sharing the information with its holding company as a general business practice.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy issues, please do not hesitate to contact The Leela at webmaster@theleela.com

While The Leela assures you that it will do its best to ensure the privacy and security of your personal information, it shall not be responsible in any manner whatsoever for any violation or misuse of your personal information by unauthorised persons consequent to misuse of the internet environment.

The Leela reserves its rights to revise this privacy policy from time to time at its discretion with a view to making the policy more user friendly.

In the design of our website, we have taken care to draw your attention to this privacy policy so that you are aware of the terms under which you may decide to share your personal information with us. Accordingly, should you choose to share your personal information with us, The Leela will assume that you have no objections to the terms of this privacy policy.

When you make a reservation on www.theleela.com , you will be asked to enter personal information in order to secure your reservation. This information is submitted to our booking partner and our Web Hosting provider. The Leela and these service providers WILL NOT sell or distribute any personal information about you or any other individual traveler. The name, address and phone number you provide may be used for marketing or quality assurance purposes for the benefit of The Leela. While you will receive an e-mail confirmation whenever you make a reservation on-line, you will not receive any unsolicited e-mail as a result of making a reservation on our site. On www.theleela.com , we strive to ensure that the most secure encryption technology is used, to allow you to book your travel reservations without the worry of someone misusing any. To make your reservation planning more convenient, we use what is known as a "cookie", which allows the system to temporarily remember your travel dates and preferences during each visit to the site. Cookies also allow us to track the more frequently visited pages of www.theleela.com , which helps us to improve the site for your benefit. Cookies are not programs that will corrupt your computer or damage your files. The cookies used do not reveal your personal identity, nor can they capture personal or private data. Our cookies automatically expire as soon as you leave our site.

If you do not want to accept cookies while on our site, please consult your Internet browser settings for more information. However, if you decide to not accept cookies while visiting www.theleela.com, you may not be able to complete certain transactions.

The www.theleela.com site does contain external links that will take you away from www.theleela.com to our travel partners. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these sites.
