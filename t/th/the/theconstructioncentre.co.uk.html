Privacy Policy

The Construction Centre Privacy Policy and Disclaimer

This Privacy Policy sets out the data processing practices carried out through the use of the Internet and the World Wide Web by theconstructioncentre.co.uk If you have any requests concerning your personal information or any queries with regard to these practices please contact us at info@theconstructioncentre.co.uk

Information collected

theconstructioncentre.co.uk collects personal information from visitors to this web site through the use of online forms and every time you e-mail us your details. We may also collect information automatically about your visit to our web site.

What information do we collect?

We collect the information necessary to provide the service you request. We will collect your name and address and your e-mail address if you e-mail to us or otherwise provide it. We will collect your URL if you provide it to us.

What do we use the information for?

We want to give our users the best possible service. We may therefore use the data gathered to contact you to check this is the case. We may use it to advise you by 'phone, fax or e-mail of new possibilities and changes to our service or new services that we or our associated companies offer.

You can opt out of receiving this information by emailing us at info@theconstructioncentre.co.uk, stating your full details and stating that you do not wish to receive any marketing details.

Use of personal information

theconstructioncentre.co.uk processes personal information collected via this web site for the purposes of:

  • Planning and managing the theconstructioncentre.co.uk business and workload
  • Identifying potential customers or subscribers
  • Carrying out market research
  • Maintaining information as a reference tool or general resource and
  • Providing you with information about products and services we offer

If you do not wish to receive information about theconstructioncentre.co.uk products and services please send an email to info@theconstructioncentre.co.uk, or call 01926 865825, fax to 01926 858050 or write to us at The Construction Centre, 2 Alpha House, Farmer Ward Road, Kenilworth, CV8 2ED

Automated information collection

We have a policy of continual improvement of our site. We therefore collect information automatically about your visit to it. This is used to help us see browsing preferences.
