# The Best Designs - [TheBestDesigns.com] - Recognizing The Best of Web Design

  
**TheBestDesigns.com Privacy Policy**

TheBestDesigns.com is committed to maintaining your confidence and trust. Your right to privacy and data security is a primary concern. 

**What types of information do we use?**   
When you email us to submit your site for review or to ask a question, we will put your email address, site name, and name in our own off-line database that we use for internal contact purposes only. We will NEVER sell or give out your information to 3rd parties. The only time we will use your email address will be to contact you about your submission, or to send you information about our site. If we ever decide to start sending out a monthly newsletter, we may use those email addresses in our database to send out our initial newsletter or invitation. If we ever do this, and you do not want to receive the newsletter, you will be able to "unsubscribe" at any time. Your email address, name, and any other information that you reveal to us in an email will be kept confidential and only be used internally by TheBestDesigns.com for future contact purposes. 

**Cookies**   
We do not use cookies. 

**Third-Party Sites or Persons**   
Please be aware that other web sites that may be accessed through our site may collect personally identifiable information about you. The information practices of those third-party web sites linked from TheBestDesigns.com are not covered by this privacy statement. If you post personal information online that is accessible to the public, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return. While we strive to protect your personal information, TheBestDesigns.com cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us, and you do so at your own risk. 

**Other Web Sites**   
TheBestDesigns.com contain links to other web sites that are not operated by TheBestDesigns.com. TheBestDesigns.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of the websites that it does not operate. 

**Changes/Updates to this Agreement**   
TheBestDesigns.com reserves the right to change or amend this agreement at any time.
