Information Collection and How it is Used

ThemeGarden will not sell or disclose your name or email address to third parties without your explicit consent, except as specified in this privacy policy.

At registration you are asked for your email address and certain other optional information. If you would like to obtain a Seller account, ThemeGarden will ask for your address and billing information. ThemeGarden automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookies, and the pages you request. This data is only used in the aggregate.

ThemeGarden uses information for products and services provisioning, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, research, and anonymous reporting. IP addresses are automatically reported by your browser each time you view a web page. Your IP address is stored in ThemeGarden’s user registration databases when you register with ThemeGarden. IP addresses may be used for various research purposes.

ThemeGarden will send you a welcome/confirmation email when you sign up for ThemeGarden’s services. Receipt of this email is required for using ThemeGarden’s services. If you do not wish to receive them, you have the option to deactivate your account by emailing support@themegarden.com from the email address you signed up with.

Administrative Messages

ThemeGarden may periodically email you with administrative messages. Your information may be combined with information ThemeGarden collects from other companies and used to improve and personalize services, content and advertising. Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive. You can configure your browser to accept all cookies, reject all cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. If you reject all cookies, you will not be able to use ThemeGarden products or services that require you to “sign in,” and you may not be able to take full advantage of the ThemeGarden service. ThemeGarden uses its own cookies for a number of purposes, including to: Access your information when you “sign in”; keep track of preferences you specify while you are using ThemeGarden’s services; estimate and report ThemeGarden’s total audience size and traffic; conduct research to improve ThemeGarden’s content and services. ThemeGarden cookies do not collect personal information, and ThemeGarden does not combine information collected through cookies with other personal information to tell us who you are or what your username or email address is.
