Privacy Policy

The LAM Foundation is committed to protecting your privacy when you visit our website. Information we collect is used to improve our site, provide you  with a better experience, and  to respond to your questions and requests.

Information Collection
We collect two types of information on our website:

1. Web server traffic information: This information is used to determine the number of visitors to our site, length of stay and sources of traffic. We collect aggregate data – or total of those visiting from a domain – not information identifying individual users. We use this information to help improve our site and our service to you.

2. Personal information provided by you: We collect email addresses and other personal information only when you provide it to us. If you fill-out a form or survey, request information or make a donation, we collect your name, address, city, state, zip code, telephone number and email address.  You have the option to opt out after you provide your information to us.

Use of Personal Information
The LAM Foundation collects information from different points on our website. We do NOT sell, trade or rent our website visitor’s personal information to others. If you willingly provide us with your personal information, The LAM Foundation may use it in the following ways:

Donations: When you make an online donation you are placed on our mailing list to receive publications and other mailings either via regular mail or email. As a donor, you may request to have your name removed from these mailing lists.

Email information requests and questions: When we respond to your email requests or questions, we collect your information and place you on our mailing list to receive publications and other mailings via regular mail or email. To opt-out and remove your name from future emails or mailings, just follow the procedure outlined below.

Your consent
By using The LAM Foundation’s website, you consent to the collection and use of the information described above, unless otherwise specified by you.

Your choice
We respect your privacy and do not require that you provide any information to us in order to view our site. We also recognize that you may wish to limit the ways in which we contact you. Simply send an email to info@thelamfoundation.org with the following information:

1. To remove your name from mailing lists, please provide your full name, mailing address and a sentence requesting removal.

2. To review or correct your personal information with The LAM Foundation, please provide us with your name and a sentence explaining what you wish to review and/or the correction.

Security
We take every precaution to protect your personal information. At The LAM Foundation, your personal information is kept confidential and stored in a secure environment, and access to your information is restricted only to authorized staff with a legitimate and specific business purpose. The LAM Foundation does not share contributor contact information or giving history with any person(s) or corporation(s) outside of LAM Foundation staff.

www.thelamfoundation.org is owned and maintained by The LAM Foundation. If you have any questions about your privacy, the use of information collected, or the security of our website, please send an email to our IT Department at IT@thelamfoundation.org
