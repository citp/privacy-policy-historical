# The Mobius Loop

Mobius Loop has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website:

[

### Mobius Loop

](http://www.mobius%20loop/)

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information. Log files collected by Mobius Loop are not shared with 3rd parties.

Our site uses cookies during your site visits to track the membership owners of each individual website, and for our members to manage the website navigation.

Where registration forms are provided, the site requires users to give us contact information (like their name and email address) and demographic information. The customer's contact information is used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. 

Forms may be provided on behalf of partners when special offers or discounts on services are made available to Mobius Loop visitors. Information collected on these forms is not used by Mobius Loop for any purposes other than to provide the service in question. Mobius Loop is not responsible for the privacy practices of our web site partners.

This site also contains links to other sites.  
Mobius Loop is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Our online surveys and contests ask visitors for contact information (like their email address) and demographic information (like their zip/postal code, business/occupation etc.). This contact information is used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. We use this data to tailor our visitor's experience at our site, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences. This information is shared with partners and sponsors of the site at a summary level only, no information likely to identify individual users is released.

### Choice/Opt-Out

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor.

This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service.

Auto-opt-out mechanisms detailed at the base of all mailing list items.
