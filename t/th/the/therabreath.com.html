What information do we collect and how do we use it?
When you order, we need to know your name, email address, mailing address, credit card number, and credit card expiration date.    This allows us to process and fulfill your order and to notify you of your order status.

When you sign up for services like the Questionnaires or download certain software such as the eBooks, we may ask for demographical information. We use the information to better understand our customers.

We may personalize your shopping experience by using your purchase and site behavior to shape our recommendations about our other products and merchandise that might be of interest to you. We also monitor customer traffic and patterns and site usage to help us develop the design and layout of the site.

We may also use the information we collect to occasionally notify you about important functionality changes to the web site, new Dr. Harold Katz, LLC/ TheraBreath products, and special offers we think you'll find valuable. If you would rather not receive this information, visit http://www.therabreath.com/emailoptions.asp and enter your email address. If you have more than one email address, please include the others in your email. Please note that you will be removed from our email distribution list and will no longer receive any promotional emails from us. If you place an order with us, you will still receive emails pertaining to any purchases you make from us.

We will never share your personal information with any third-party. On rare occasions we may notify you (with your permission) about a new product or service from a third-party that we endorse. However this contact will always initiate from us and your information will    never be passed to that third party.

What cookies are and do?
"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Our site uses cookies to speed navigation, keep track of items, and provide you with custom-tailored content. We use cookies to remember information you gave to us so you don't have to reenter it each time you visit our site. By showing us how and when shoppers use our site, cookies help us see which areas are popular and which are not.

Many improvements and updates to our site are based on the data derived from these cookies, such as total number of visitors, pages viewed, and the total number of ads served. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you would prefer, you can set yours to refuse cookies however you will not be able to take advantage of all the features and pages with Dr. Harold Katz, LLC/ TheraBreath's website.
