# Privacy policy

Privacy policy

_Last Updated November 2, 2016_

At FeedFeed, we are committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use and share information about you that we obtain through your use of our website at [http://www.FeedFeed.info](http://www.feedfeed.info/), [https://thefeedfeed.com/](https://thefeedfeed.com/), feedfeed.org, and ff.recipes (the “ **Site** ”) and the services we provide through the Site (the “ **Services** ”). This Privacy Policy does not apply to any third party websites or services that may be linked to by the Site or any relationships you may have with the businesses listed on the Site.

The term “ **Personal Information** ” means information that you provide to us which personally identifies you to be contacted or identified, such as your name, phone number, email address, and any other data that is tied to such information. Any capitalized terms used by but not defined in this Privacy Policy shall have the meaning set forth in the Terms of Service, which can be located at [https://feedfeed.info/terms](https://feedfeed.info/terms).

Please review this Privacy Policy to understand how we treat your information before providing us with any information about you. FeedFeed reserves the right, at any time and from time to time, to change, modify, add or remove portions from this Privacy Policy and will post the revised Privacy Policy at [https://feedfeed.info/privacy](https://feedfeed.info/privacy). The Privacy Policy is effective as of the date indicated at the beginning of the policy. You are responsible for periodically visiting the Privacy Policy to check for any changes. Your continued use of the Site following the posting of any changes to this policy means you accept such changes. 

BY USING OUR SERVICES, YOU ARE ACCEPTING AND CONSENTING TO THE PRACTICES DESCRIBED IN THIS PRIVACY POLICY (INCLUDING AS CHANGED FROM TIME TO TIME). IF, FOR ANY REASON, YOU DO NOT AGREE WITH THE TERMS OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY, PLEASE STOP USING OUR SERVICES IMMEDIATELY.

****1\. Who We Are****

As used in this Privacy Policy, The terms “FeedFeed”, “we”, and “us” refer to FeedFeed LLC and its affiliates, which include our vendors, contractors, and agents, unless otherwise stated in this Privacy Policy.

****2\. Information We Collect****

We use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that the collection of personal information is limited to that which is necessary to fulfill the purposes identified below. If we use your information in a manner different than the purpose for which it is collected, then we will ask you for your consent prior to such use.

A. Automatically through the Site

We collect certain information to help us operate and provide the Services. We automatically collect this information when you use the Services, including:

  * Information about the device(s) you use to access the Services, including MAC address, IP address, browser type and version, time zone setting, the searches you undertake, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform, device type, and device identifiers; 
  * Information about your visits to the Site, including the full URL clickstream to, through and from these websites (including date and time); 
  * Information we need and use to facilitate your use of the Services (including to provide access to third party websites and services), such as URL requests, destination IP addresses, or device configuration details;
  * Webpages you viewed or searched for, page response times, download errors, length of visits to certain pages, page interaction information (such as scrolling, clicks, and mouse-overs), and methods used to browse away from the page.  
B. Automatically through your mobile device



When you use our Services, such as access and use the Services from your mobile device, we may automatically collect certain information on your device. This information includes information about your mobile device and internet connection, including the device's unique device identifier, IP address, operating system, browser type, mobile network information and the device's telephone number. Some of this information is necessary for us to provide and maintain the security of our Services. If you do not want us to collect this information, do not use our Services.

When you access our Services through your mobile device, we may also automatically collect additional information on your mobile device. These information include: 

  * **Device Information**. We may collect information about your mobile device and internet connection, including the mobile device's unique device identifier, IP address, operating system, browser type, browser language, mobile network information and the device's telephone number (“ **Device Information** ”).
  * **Location Information**. Real-time location information about your mobile device when you are using the Service. These location information include, but are not limited to, any information recorded by the GPS from your device, and Wifi SSID, bluetooth ID and beacon identifiers from your environment (“ **Location Information** ”).
  * **Stored Information and Files**. Your metadata and other information associated with other files stored on your device. These may include, for example, photographs, audio and video clips, personal contacts and address book information.



If you do not want us to automatically collect these additional information, do not use our Services.

We may also use technologies to automatically collect tracking information on your mobile device or your browser with your consent. These technologies include: 

  * **Cookies and mobile cookies**. A cookie is a small file placed on your computer or mobile device. We may use cookies and similar technologies to support the functionality of our Services. This provides a better experience when you use our Services and allows us to improve our Services. We may use both session cookies (which expire once you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them). Persistent cookies can be removed by following your browser help file directions. It may be possible to refuse to accept cookies or mobile cookies by activating the appropriate setting on your browser or smartphone. However, if you select this setting you may be unable to access certain parts of our Services. 
  * **Web Beacons**. Certain parts of our Services, text messages and e-mails may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags and single-pixel gifs) that permit us, for example, to count users who have visited those pages or opened an e-mail and for other related statistics (for example, recording the popularity of certain content and verifying system and server integrity).
  * **Behavior tracking**. We may use technologies, which can collect information about your activities over time and across third-party websites, apps or other online services (behavioral tracking) with your consent.C. Information You Provide to Us


  * We collect information you provide directly to us through the Services, including when you make a Submission, subscribe to our mailing list, participate to a contest, participate in a twitter conversation, or otherwise provide us with your name, username, e-mail address, and other information through the Services. The term “ **Submission** ” refers to the information that you submit or post to the Site for public display, such as photographs, links, and comments. By utilizing the hashtag “#feedfeed” you consent to our collection, storing and sharing of such information and our publicly displaying and otherwise using of such information on our Site or in connection with providing our Services. In granting your consent, you acknowledge that when we share your Submissions with third parties, they may in turn publicly display or publish your submissions in connection with their own websites and media platforms. 


  * If you send us an email, provide us with a feedback, register for a complaint, ask a question, call us, or communicate with us in any other way, we will collect that information, including any personal information you provide in your communication so that we can effectively respond to your communication. We may also use and share information you provide to us as described in this Privacy Policy.
  * When you access our Site or interact with us through a third-party social media site, such as Instagram, Twitter, Tumblr, Pinterest, Facebook and Snapchat, or when you access a third-party social media site through the link provided by our Site, you authorize us to collect certain information from you or your social media account. These include your personally identifiable information such as, name, e-mail address, age, user name, your profile picture, contact list, the profile pictures of the contact list, education, work history, events, relationship status, and likes. In addition, you may also choose to provide us information about yourself, such as your gender, location, URL, a biography, and any additional image or information.
  * We may also collect records and copies of your correspondence (including e-mail addresses and phone numbers) with us. With your consent, we may also collect: 
    * Your responses to surveys that we might ask you to complete for research purposes.
    * Any photos, videos, locations and other information you tag.
    * Any other types of information you instruct us to collect. 



If you do not wish to provide us these information, please do not access our Site.

D. Personal Information

Some of the information we collect through your use of the Services or communications with us, such as your name, email address, mobile phone number, Instagram ID, Twitter ID, Tumblr ID, Pinterest ID, Facebook ID Snapchat ID or other social media ID, or your profile picture may personally identify you. We will treat this information as “ **Personal Information**.” We will also treat as Personal Information any non-Personal Information that is combined with Personal Information.

E. Aggregate or Anonymized Information

We may combine certain information about your use of the Services with similar information that we obtain from other users for use in an aggregate or anonymous manner.F. Other Collection of Information

We will not ask for or request sensitive information such as government identifiers, medical information or financial information. Please do not provide such information to us through emails, feedback forms or in any other way. ** **3\. How Your Information Is Used****

We use information that we collect about you or that you provide to us, including but not limited to any Personal Information and Location Information, to:

  * Provide you with our Services;
  * Perform any data analysis necessary to perform our Services, including administration, internal operations, troubleshooting, data analysis, testing, research, statistical and survey purposes;
  * Manage your access to the Services;
  * Send you information that allows you to use the Services;
  * Send you emails about updates, information or alerts regarding the Services
  * Give you notices about our Services you signed up for, such as subscriptions and contest;
  * Provide you with any other information, products and services from us or our partners including advertisers;
  * Carry out our obligations and enforce our rights arising from any contracts entered into between you and us, including for billing and collection;
  * To investigate, enforce, and apply our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
  * Notify you when an update is available, and of changes to any products or Service we offer or provide through the update;
  * Estimate our audience size and usage patterns;
  * Fulfill any other purpose for which you provide;
  * Store information about your preferences;
  * Allow us to customize our Services according to your individual interests;
  * Speed up our Services;
  * Recognize you when you use our Services;
  * Comply with applicable laws and regulations and other legal requirements; and
  * Maintain the security of the Services. ** **4\. How Your Information Is Shared****



We will not sell or share your information, including Personal Information, with third parties for the third party's own direct marketing purposes. 

We may share your information, including Personal Information, with our vendors, service providers and agents to the extent reasonably necessary to provide you with the Services. For example, we use a third-party platform for email and cannot communicate with you without disclosing your email address to our third-party email provider. These third parties use any information we share only to support us and our efforts to provide the Services.

We may share your information, including Personal Information:

  * With our subsidiaries, joint ventures, or other companies under a common control (collectively, “ **Affiliates** ”) that we may have now or in the future
  * With third parties, including service providers and contractors to the extent reasonably necessary to enable us to provide the Services, to conduct quality assurance testing, provide technical and customer support, or provide specific services;
  * With our actual or prospective partners, including third party advertising companies to, for example, serve relevant advertisements to you;
  * During an acquisition or transfer of a majority of the assets of FeedFeed;
  * To comply with any court order, law or legal process, including to respond to any government or regulatory request;
  * To enforce our rights arising from any contracts entered into between you and us, including the Terms of Service and for billing and collection;
  * To take action regarding a suspected illegal activity;
  * If we believe disclosure is necessary or appropriate to protect the rights, property, reputation or safety of FeedFeed, our users, affiliates or the public. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for the purposes of fraud protection and credit risk reduction;
  * In an event of emergency;
  * You, upon your request; and
  * To fulfill the purpose for which you provide it.



We may share non-Personal Information, aggregate or anonymous data with analytics, search engine or other service providers that help us improve our Services. ** **5\. Your Choices****

You may have the opportunity to receive certain communications from us related to our Services. If you provide us with your email address in order to receive communications, you can opt out at any time by using the unsubscribe links at the bottom of our emails. 

Please note that certain emails or text messages may be necessary to provide you with our Services. You will continue to receive these emails and text messages, if appropriate, even if you unsubscribe from our optional communications.

Our Services currently do not change based upon a detection of a Do Not Track or similar signal.

Please note that we cannot control how third party websites or online services collect, use or share your information. Check the privacy policies of those third parties for information on their privacy practices. ** **6\. Security****

We use industry standard measures to protect the personal information that is stored in our database. We limit the access to your personal information to those employees and contractors who need access to perform their job function, such as our customer service personnel. If you have any questions about the security on the Site, please contact us. Although we take appropriate measures to safeguard against unauthorized disclosures of information, we cannot assure you that your personal information will never be disclosed in a manner that is inconsistent with this Privacy Policy. 

FeedFeed cannot be responsible for information intercepted via the internet. ** **7\. Children’s Privacy****

The Service is not directed towards children under 13 years of age, and FeedFeed does not knowingly collect or market personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13 years of age, you are not permitted to submit any Personal Information to us. ** **8\. External Links****

Please check the privacy policies applicable to any other website you access via the Site before you submit any personal information to such other website. ** **9\. GEOGRAPHIC LOCATIONS**** A.
