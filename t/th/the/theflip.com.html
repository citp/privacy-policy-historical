What Does This Privacy Policy Cover?

This Privacy Policy covers Pure Digital's treatment of personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") that Pure Digital gathers when you are accessing Pure Digital's website and when you use Pure Digital products or services. The types of Personal Information collected may include your name, e-mail address, mailing address, and phone number. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Pure Digital does not own or control, or to individuals that Pure Digital does not employ or manage.

What Personal Information Does PURE DIGITAL COLLECT?

The information we gather from customers enables us to personalize and improve our products and services. We collect the following types of information from our customers.

Personal Information You Provide to Us:

We may receive and store any information you enter on our website, through our products and services, or provide to us in any other way. You can choose not to provide us with certain information, but then you may not be able to take advantage of many of our special features. The Personal Information you provide may be used for such purposes as responding to your requests for certain products and services, customizing the advertising and content you see, and communicating with you about specials and new products.

Personal Information Collected Automatically:

* We may receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with our website, products or services. Pure Digital may choose to automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, Pure Digital cookie information, and the page you requested.
* Generally, our service may automatically collect usage information, such as the users of our products and services, similar to TV ratings that indicate how many people watched a particular show. Pure Digital only uses this data in aggregate form, that is, as a statistical measure, and not in a manner that would identify you personally. This type of aggregate data enables us to figure out how often customers use parts of the products and services so that we can make the products and services appealing to as many customers as possible, and improve those those products and services. As part of this use of information, we may provide aggregate information to our partners about how our customers, collectively, use our products and services. We share this type of statistical data so that our partners also understand how often people use their products and services, so that they, too, may provide you with an optimal consumer experience.

E-mail Communications:

We may receive a confirmation when you open an e-mail from Pure Digital if your computer supports this type of program. Pure Digital may use this confirmation to help us make e-mails more interesting and helpful. If you do not want to receive e-mail or other mail from us, please notify us by e-mail at remove@puredigitalinc.com and include sufficient information for us to identify your account.

Cookies:

* Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we may transfer to your computer's hard drive through your browser to enable our systems to recognize your browser and tell us how and when pages in our site are visited and by how many people. Pure Digital cookies do not collect Personal Information, and we do not combine the general information collected through cookies with other Personal Information to personally identify you.
* Most browsers have an option for turning off the cookie feature, which will prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, as well as (depending on the sophistication of your browser software) allowing you to decide on acceptance of each new cookie in a variety of ways.

With Your Consent: Except as set forth above, you will be notified when your Personal Information may be shared with third parties, and will be able to prevent the sharing of this information.
