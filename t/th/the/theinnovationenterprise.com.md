# The Innovation Enterprise

This policy discloses what information we gather about you when you visit any of our Web sites or buy a product directly from us. It describes how we use that information and how you can control it. Our privacy policy:

**Information we gather and track**

IE. collect two kinds of information about users:

1) data that users volunteer by signing up to receive news and product information, entering contests, completing surveys, or buying directly from us; and

2) aggregated tracking data we collect when users interact with us.

**Personal information**

We use the personal information you provide voluntarily to send information you've requested and to fulfill orders. The specific use of your personal information varies, depending on how you contact us:

**Signing up for email newsletters or conference/expo updates:**

When you sign up online to receive newsletters, brochures etc you must provide your name and email address.

We will send you email announcements and updates about the event and other products and services. You will also receive relevant offers from our sponsors and partners. 

Sponsors and partners will then have the option to occasionally send you limited emails.

We will send you email announcements and updates about our products and services. We may send conference brochures to individuals who request information from us and provides a valid address.

**Registering for a Summit / Webinar:**

When you register for any Summit / webinar we will send you email announcements and updates about the event and other products and services. We may send conference brochures to past conference attendees.

When you register for any event, you will recieve relevant offers from our sponsors and partners. Sponsors and partners will then have the option to occasionally send you limited emails.

We also may collect information about your professional responsibilities (such as job title, purchasing responsibilities, company information and professional certification). We use such information to improve our service, for marketing purposes and/or for industry reporting purposes.

For our services that require payment (such as certain events, products and subscriptions), we also collect credit card information (such as account name, number and expiration date), which is used for our or our agent's billing purposes only, and is not otherwise shared.

With third parties we retain to perform functions on our behalf, such as fulfilling orders, processing credit card payments, managing mailing lists, and delivering packages. These parties are restricted from using your information for any other purpose. We do not rent or sell our email lists.

We release personal information when we believe that release is appropriate to comply with the law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users, or others. This may include exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction.

**Surveys and Contests**

From time to time we invite web site users and other customers to provide information via surveys or contests. Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary. In addition to the other uses set forth in this policy, contact information collected in connection with surveys and contests is used to notify the winners and award prizes and to monitor or improve the use of, and satisfaction with the event, website or other product or service.

**How to update, correct, or delete your information**

Email: To update or unsubscribe from our email newsletters or product announcements, change your address, delete your name from our mailing list or to opt-out of third-party mail please send an email with your request to info@theiegroup.com
