# The Open Press - Free Press Release News Wire

#### PRIVACY POLICY

This is The Open Press website.

Our postal address is  
The Open Press  
PO Box 2408  
Palatine, IL 60078

The following is the privacy policy for The Open Press at http://www.theopenpress.com

When you visit our site we do not log any information regarding your domain or e-mail address.

Information Sharing: We do not share user information with any third parties other than via press release distribution as described below.

The Open Press is a news wire service which distributes press releases on behalf of our users. If you decide to submit a press release for distribution through our system we will transmit your entire press release including any personal information therein contained to our media contacts and online distribution points including search engines. This is the only redistribution of your information that we engage in. Your submission of press releases through our system indicates consent with this policy.

The information we collect during your registration process is used to notify users about updates to our service and inform users of special events hosted by The Open Press. This information is not shared with other organizations for commercial or non-commercial purposes.

With respect to cookies: The cookies our site requires are ONLY for logging onto our web site to help you navigate and maintain your account logged in until you choose to log out. This is the ONLY purpose for our cookies. We do not use cookies to track anyone nor will we allow anyone else to make use of our cookies.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by following instructions included in our communication with you.

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such telephone calls, please edit your account and remove your phone number from your account profile.

With respect to Ad Servers: We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies. We run our own ad system on our web site and it does NOT track anyone at all.

From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our Web site periodically.

Upon request we provide site visitors with access to all information (including proprietary information) that we maintain about them. Consumers can access this information by logging into their account.

With respect to security: We always use industry-standard encryption technologies when transferring and receiving user data exchanged with our site where appropriate. We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site. We do not store credit card information in our systems.

We will never sell your e-mail address to anyone for any reason nor we will send you and spam.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above addresses.
