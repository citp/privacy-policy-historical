# The Garden Helper Privacy Policy

The Garden Helper will never share any information provided by you to any other source or entity. 

Advertisers, as well as our stat counter may use cookie files to collect _**anonymous**_ web surfing information to review the effects of their ad strategies, user relevance, and reader interests, but none of this information is personally identifiable. You can prevent the use of these cookies by adjusting the preferences on your internet browser. Most browsers permit you to remove cookies that have been previously stored, as well as prevent the future use of cookies.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here.](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html "Privacy policy") Thank you for visiting The Garden Helper, I hope that you will return soon!
