# Privacy Policy » Women, Fashion Advice, Beauty Tips, and More | The Budget Fashionista

### PRIVACY POLICY and DISCLAIMER

Simply Good Media, a division TBF Group, LLC, maintains internet websites such as thebudgetfashionista.com for your personal entertainment, information, education, and communication. Like most web businesses, Simply Good Media captures both personal and aggregated information about visitors to our websites (collectively the “Simply Good Media Sites”). We explain here why we need that information and what we do (and won’t do) with it.

**Overview** : We never disclose or sell personal information about you to third parties without your permission, unless compelled to do so by law. If you only read the articles, posts and comments on the Simply Good Media Sites, no more information is collected than is typically collected in server logs by websites in general. If you post comments or other material, you are publishing every word you post publicly. If you write something, assume that it will be retained forever.

**I. Why we collect and use information about our visitors:**

Simply Good Media collects information about visitors to the Simply Good Media Sites for the following reasons:

A. To allow us to respond to your questions and comments: Since the Simply Good Media Sites revolve a great deal around being interactive with our visitors, we need to know who our visitors are so as to better respond to the questions submitted by those individual visitors.

B. To know our audience: We use information about our visitors to understand our audience and to describe that audience to business partners, sponsors, and advertisers, which will enable us to provided an enhanced surfing experience for our visitors.

C. For user convenience and customization: To allow us to customize the use of the Simply Good Media Sites, to present the issues most important to our visitors in the fashion, style, and beauty worlds.

D. For product improvements: To help us understand how visitors to the Simply Good Media Sites use the sites and how we can improve them.

E. To provide a better browsing environment: By knowing about our visitors it is possible for us to be able to offer items and links we believe our visitors most want to have, thus reducing the amount of time spent searching online.

**II. Types of information we collect:**

Personal information is stored on secure web servers, and is available on the Simply Good Media Sites for you to review or modify at all times. Depending on how you use the Simply Good Media Sites, we may collect the following kinds of information:

A. Your name, e-mail address and zip-code, when you register for the Simply Good Media Sites or e-mail us a question.

B. Your user preferences, your interest in receiving our newsletters and product announcements, and similar information, which you can change at any time.

C. Your credit-card number and mailing address, if you purchase one of the products offered by us exclusively and/or enter one of our contests. Any purchases made through our affiliates are subject to their privacy policy.

D. Additional Personal Information: If you subscribe to our newsletter we may collect personal information that allows us to target visitors with information, products or services which fits the visitor’s specific needs or desires.

E. Demographic information that you choose to provide, such as your gender, age, martial status, occupation, income, number and ages of your children, hobbies, zip code, mailing address, and so on.

F. Aggregated usage data, such as the popularity of various pages on the Simply Good Media Sites, the times of day when most visits occur, and so on.

**III. Do we use cookies?**

Yes, like other web sites, Simply Good Media uses the technology called “cookies” to place small, anonymous data files on your computer. You can turn cookies off if you wish (see your browser’s Help instructions), but Simply Good Media features that rely on them will be disabled. Depending on how you use the Simply Good Media Sites, cookies may be used for the following purposes:

A. To help us track the number of visitors and the popularity of our pages.

B. To allow you to store personal preferences, such as your list of sites or list of purchases made.

C. To give you the convenience of having your username submitted automatically once you’ve registered.

IV. Simply Good Media uses information about you in the following ways:

A. To help us understand our audience as we grow our business.

B. To promote ourselves to potential sponsors, advertisers or business partners.

C. If you send us an email, to respond to your specific question, suggestion or concern. All reader letters are answered with an alias, or ghost phrase, and city rather than your real name.

D. To announce new Simply Good Media Sites products, enhancements, events and other announcements.

E. To meet legal demands on us by certain administrative or legal organizations, we may access and/or disclose your personal information if we believe such action is necessary to (i) comply with the law or legal process served on TBF Group, LLC; (ii) protect and defend the rights or property of TBF Group, LLC (including the enforcement of our agreements); or (iii) act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of TBF Group services or members of the public.

**V. Sale or transfer of personal information retained by us:**

Simply Good Media Sites will never disclose or sell personal information about you, including your e-mail address, to any third party without your permission, unless compelled to do so by law.

**VI. Conclusion:**

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Nevertheless, while we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us and you do so at your own risk. Finally, Simply Good Media Sites partners and third party sites have separate privacy and data collection practices, independent of us. Simply Good Media Sites has no responsibility or liability for these independent policies or actions. By using the Simply Good Media Sites, you consent to the collection and use of information by Simply Good Media Sites as outlined in this privacy policy. We have a right to change this policy at any time. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. If we decide for any reason to sell such information you will be given a right to opt out of such transfer. Any such procedure shall be outlined to you in our update.
