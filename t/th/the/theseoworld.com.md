# Privacy Policy | TSW - SEO Company Search Engine Optimization and Marketing

## [Privacy Policy](http://www.theseoworld.com/privacy-policy/ "Permanent Link to Privacy Policy")

As part of our privacy policy we protect your information and do not disclose any of your websites facts to 3rd party.

We need your websites information with contact details for submissions and off page seo purpose and we dont resale your personal information.All our employees have signed a confidentiality agreement which forbids them from disclosing this information to any 3rd party. We share information like visitors count, gross / approximate convertions but not details analysis information.

We reserve the right to change this privacy policy anytime and you can check out this section for the updated policies.
