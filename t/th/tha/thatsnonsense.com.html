Our Privacy Policy

We Collect

ThatsNonsense.com collects your name and email address when you contact us. These fields are required.

We collect this information so we know who you are and how to get in touch with you if we need to. We keep all personal information private, and will not give out your email address or full name to anybody outside of ThatsNonsense.com. You first name and any comment or feedback you give us may be submitted on the website without notification. However this does not apply to any given second names or your email address which will never be disclosed to anybody.

Links to other websites

Our website may contain links to other websites of interest. However, once you have used these links to leave our site, you should note that we do not have any control over that other website. Therefore, we cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which you provide whilst visiting such sites and such sites are not governed by this privacy statement. You should exercise caution and look at the privacy statement applicable to the website in question.

Cookies

thatsNonsense.com does not use cookies. However as we are a Google affiliated site, cookies may be used when viewing or clicking on Google ads on our site. The data gathered from these DART cookies will be used to help better serve and manage ads on our site and across the web. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
