Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us, so Thriftcat has created this Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") to explain how the information you provide through your use of the Thriftcat website is protected, collected and used. By accessing the www.thriftcat.org you agree to comply with and be bound by the Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. You can always review the most current version by clicking on "Privacy Policy" at the bottom of each page of www.thriftcat.org. By using www.thriftcat.org, you consent to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy and are aware that our policies may change in the future. Some information that you access via the www.thriftcat.org may be located on third party websites. If you click on that information, you will be taken to the third party website hosting such information and are subject to such third party's privacy policy and terms of use. Thriftcat is not responsible for such links or for information collected by such third parties.

I. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

A. E-mail Addresses

  1. When do we collect your email address?: We do not automatically collect your email address. We collect your email address only if you choose to provide it to us when you complete the optional registration process, or when you provide it to us via a contact form.
  2. How do we use your email address?: If you choose to provide us with your email address you will be opting in to receiving emails from us and we may send emails to you when you request for help or support.

B. Personally Identifying Information

  1. We do not automatically collect any personally identifying information from visitors to www.thriftcat.org. When you register for the www.thriftcat.org, you will be required to supply certain personally identifying information, including but not limited to your name. You will also be required to supply your email address. Your email will not be displayed or revealed to third parties. If you do not want your personally identifiable information collected by www.thriftcat.org, you may browse www.thriftcat.org without registering.

C. Tracking Information

  1. Cookies: Like many website operators, we use "cookies" to recognize your web browser. A "cookie" is a small piece of information that is stored in a designated file on your computer. Each cookie has a unique number with no meaning outside of the Website. Our cookies do not contain any personally identifying information about our visitors. We may use both session ID cookies and persistent cookies. Session ID cookies terminate when you close your browser. Persistent cookies may be stored on a hard drive for an extended period of time. Persistent cookies can be removed by following directions found under "help" in your internet browser. You can configure your browser not to accept cookies however, if you do so, you may not be able to use certain optional features or functions of www.thriftcat.org.
  2. Log Files: Like many website operators, we use tracking programs that collect information about log files, including Internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser types, internet service providers (ISPs), referring/exit pages, platform types, date/time stamps and number of clicks. The information that we collect regarding log files is not linked to personally identifying information.
  3. Web Beacons/Web Bugs (Clear GIFs/ Single-Pixel GIFs): Advertisements that appear on our website are sometimes delivered by third party advertisers. These third party advertisers may use web beacons or web bugs to recognize your computer each time they send you an advertisement in order to measure the effectiveness of their ads and to personalize advertising content. In this way, they may compile information about where individuals using your computer or browser saw their advertisements and determine which advertisements are clicked. No personally identifying information is gathered through web beacons or web bugs.

II. HOW DO WE USE THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT?

A. Purposes for Which Information Is Submitted

  1. Personally identifying information that you voluntarily submit may be used for the purpose(s) for which it was submitted. For example, if you voluntarily submit personally identifying information to receive help or support, we may use that information to respond back to you.

B. Facilitating Use of the Website and Customizing Content, Advertising and Offers

  1. We may use information collected from cookies and other tracking technology to recognize your browser when you visit www.thriftcat.org and to facilitate easy login to www.thriftcat.org, to attempt to personalize www.thriftcat.org for each user, to ensure that your browser does or does not see the same ad over and over again, to sequence ads and offers in a series and to track page usage and paths. We may also use collected information to allow editorial content and/or advertising on the Website to be targeted to groups of users for whom it is most pertinent. This means that users see editorial content and/or advertising most likely to interest them. In connection with these uses, we may disclose information to third parties, but only in aggregate form.

C. Administrative Use and Communications Related to Your Use of the Website

  1. We may use collected information for internal administrative purposes including site administration, troubleshooting, processing of e-commerce transactions or administration of the www.thriftcat.org network. We may also use collected information to send you communications related to your use of the www.thriftcat.org or content you post on www.thriftcat.org. This includes, when applicable, communications regarding account status, renewal/expiration notices and other transactional communications. We may also send you notifications about major changes to www.thriftcat.org and/or to this Privacy Policy.

D. Statistical Analysis

  1. We may use collected information to create aggregate statistics about activity on and visitors to www.thriftcat.org, including, the number of return visits to www.thriftcat.org or our advertisers' websites, the number of unique visitors that have viewed or "clicked" a particular ad or visited a particular web page and how much time is spent on each page of www.thriftcat.org. We may also create aggregate demographic statistics.
  2. We may use these aggregate statistics to measure interest in various areas of www.thriftcat.org, to better design www.thriftcat.org and to improve our marketing strategy and product offerings. We may share the aggregate statistical information with third parties.

III. THIRD PARTY COOKIES AND TRACKING

We use third-party companies to serve advertisements on www.thriftcat.org. These companies may use cookies and may collect other tracking information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to www.thriftcat.org and other websites. These companies may use the information that they collect to measure and research the effectiveness of advertisements, to track page usage and paths followed during visits, and to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. These companies may provide this data to us, but only in aggregate form. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here: http://networkadvertising.org/consumer/opt_out.asp

As effective as our security measures are, no security system is impenetrable. Moreover, we cannot guarantee that information that you supply will not be intercepted while being transmitted to us.
