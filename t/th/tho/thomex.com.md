# Thomex.com, Product Information Exchange

  


Thomas International Publishing Co India Pvt Ltd. (TIPCo India) is committed to safeguarding its users' privacy. All users are requested to read the following 'privacy policy' to understand how their personal & business information will be treated, as they make full use of our services to their benefit. This policy is applicable only to the entire network of marketplaces operated by TIPCo India and not by any other company.

For accessing specific services of Thomex.com, a user is required to register with the site by providing his/her name, address, E-mail Id, business information and other personal details. Once a user registers, he/she is no longer anonymous to Thomex and it is deemed that the user has given TIPCo India the right to publish the desired business & personal details throughout its marketplaces, including but not limited to, business directory, trade leads, catalogs etc.

Thomex.com carries out web usage studies such as pages viewed, number of unique visitors, browser software, screen resolution etc. for analysis that provides users improved experience and services. 

The primary goal of Thomex.com in collecting personal or public information is to provide the user with a customized experience on the site. This includes personalization services, interactive communications and many other types of services launched from time to time.

In order to provide services free of charge, TIPCo India displays advertisements based upon the site usage statistics and visitor trends. TIPCo India uses user's personal & business information to serve targeted advertisements through its portals, newsletters and customized mailers. In addition, once the user's personal and business information is displayed on our network, the user may start receiving business enquiries from various buyers and sellers that might or might not be of their interest. TIPCo India does not exercise any control over its users for sending business enquiries to the listed members. 

As a general rule, TIPCo India will not disclose or share any of the user's personal information, except when TIPCo India has the user's permission or under special circumstances, such as when TIPCo India believes in good faith that the law requires it or as permitted in terms of this policy.

TIPCo India may also disclose account information in special cases when TIPCo India has reasons to believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be violating TIPCo India's Terms of Services or may be causing injury to or interference with (either intentionally or unintentionally) TIPCo India's rights or property, other TIPCo India network website users, or if TIPCO INDIA deems it necessary to maintain, service, and improve its products and services. 

TIPCo India may, from time to time, send its users emails regarding its products and services. Only TIPCo India or its agents working under confidentiality agreements will send these direct mailings. 

A user may edit his/her account information and profile by logging onto our portal and/or by sending a request to Thomex.com. 

TIPCo India agrees not to sell user's personal or business information until and unless it is permitted by the user. Any user statistics that we may provide to prospective advertisers or partners regarding product or service usage on TIPCo India network is provided in an aggregate form only and does not include any personally identifiable information about any individual user.

TIPCo India strives to protect the user's personal & business information on Thomex but it cannot ensure the security of any information transmitted in the network. TIPCo India will make best efforts to ensure security of data during transmission.

Changes in Privacy Policy Users are requested to check the policy from time to time for any changes.
