# HIT Entertainment - Terms and Conditions

## HIT Entertainment Privacy Policy

### UPDATED SEPTEMBER 7, 2010

Welcome to HIT Entertainment Limited's ("Hit Entertainment") family of websites (each a "Website") and thank you for visiting. We are committed to providing a quality experience and have developed our Websites to provide an informative and entertaining experience for users. This Privacy Policy (the "Privacy Policy") describes HIT Entertainment's online information practices as they relate to information collected through the Websites; it does not apply to information collected offline or from other sources.

### PARENTS

We encourage parents/guardians to become involved in their children's online experience. Please discuss use of the Internet and disclosure of information on our Websites or any website with your children. Additionally, parents please discuss this Privacy Policy with your children so they better understand how to use our Websites and the kinds of information we may ask of them. With the exception of this section and the "CHILDREN'S PRIVACY" section below, this Privacy Policy governs information about visitors aged 13 and older.

By using any HIT Entertainment Website, you are acknowledging and accepting this Privacy Policy. Your continued use of any HIT Entertainment Website after changes have been posted to the Privacy Policy will constitute your understanding and acceptance of such changes.

### WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Scroll to top

If you are 13 or over, we may ask for and collect personal information from you at certain points throughout the Website. Depending on the information and services you request, you may be asked to provide your name, email address, mailing address, payment information (if applicable), birth date and other information. In addition to the information you knowingly provide, HIT Entertainment collects the domain names and IP addresses of its visitors, along with usage statistics (e.g., types of web browsers and operating systems used) and browsing history. This data is used to more efficiently operate HIT Entertainment's business, promote our products and services and administer the Website. HIT Entertainment may combine this information with the other information it collects.

### HOW HIT ENTERTAINMENT USES YOUR INFORMATION

Scroll to top

HIT Entertainment may use your information: (i) to personalize and enhance our Websites and your Internet experience; (ii) to process and respond to inquiries; (iii) for the purposes for which you provided the information; (iv) to provide you with additional product and service opportunities; (v) for internal marketing and demographic studies; (vi) to contact you for non-marketing purposes (such as for feedback or customer service); and (vii) to protect our legal rights or to enforce our Terms of Use http://www.hitentertainment.com/corporate/terms.html. In the future, we may sell some or all or our assets. In such transactions, customer information generally is one of the transferred business assets. In the event of a sale of our assets including our database, customer information will be transferred. We will contractually require buyers to honor our privacy policy.

### INFORMATION SHARING WITH THIRD PARTIES

Scroll to top

HIT Entertainment may disclose your personally identifiable and other information to another entity (i) for purposes of outsourcing one or more of the functions described in the previous section; (ii) to confirm or update information provided by you; (iii) to inform you of important information; (iv) as a part of a sale of assets as described in the previous section. We may also share your information in response to a subpoena, legal order or official request, when we believe you have acted in violation of the Terms of Use <http://www.hitentertainment.com/corporate/terms.html>, or when we believe that doing so may protect our legal rights, your safety or the safety of others. We may also share information collected through one of our Websites with any other of our Websites and with other HIT Entertainment companies.

HIT Entertainment may, from time to time, share your information with third parties so that they can provide you with additional product or service opportunities. Please email us at [privacyadmin@hitentertainment.com](mailto:privacyadmin@hitentertainment.com) if you prefer not to receive these third-party communications.
