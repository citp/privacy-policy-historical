# Privacy « Static HTML | Thunderpenny

December 15, 2011 

### Static HTML privacy policy

We use Google Analytics to track pageviews and events, but do not associate it with your personal information. We don’t pass your information on to third parties.

Your personal information is sent over SSL encrypted connections. Financial information, such as credit card information, is securely processed by our payment processor without ever touching our servers or database.

Our philosophy is to only ask from you information that we need in order to let you use our products to get done what you are trying to do.
