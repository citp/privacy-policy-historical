CloudEight Internet LLC: Privacy Policy

Cloudeight Mail Lists Privacy Policy:  CloudEight values your privacy. We never sell, rent, or give, your name or email address to anyone. We never use your email address for anything other than to send our periodic announcements of new stationery or other significant content. Mail list subscribers cannot see the addresses or names of any other subscribers.

Our Mail List host is a secure Lyris server. Your name and any information we ask you is never shared with anyone! Your subscription to our list is important to us. If you feel our privacy policy does not address your concerns please email us at: cloudeight@thundercloud.net/

We will not knowingly permit advertising for spyware products or products which may install spyware. We use limited 3rd party advertising and 3rd party networks to serve a very limited number of advertisements and make every attempt to block any ads via these networks we find to be offensive or that advertise spyware. The majority of our advertising banners are now being served by our own in-house banner server and we have complete control of these advertisements. Any Cloudeight Endorsed Software Product we advertise is guaranteed to meet our high standards for software and do not contain spyware.
