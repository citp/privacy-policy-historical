# Privacy Policy | Toktumi

| | 

We have teamed with Amazon Services to make buying quick and easy. Selections from our store and from Amazon.com are available through our site, which is powered by [Amazon Services](http://webstore.amazon.com/?ld=MerchantWebstore). 

When you order from our web site, you will need to use your existing Amazon.com account or create a new Amazon.com account. Your account information will be collected and used by Amazon.com and subject to the [Amazon.com Privacy Notice](http://www.amazon.com/privacy). 

In addition, Amazon.com will provide us with your account information, which will be subject to our privacy policy as set forth below. 

**Toktumi's Privacy Policy**  

Toktumi respects your privacy and will never provide your account information, contact information, calling data, voicemail messages, or any confidential information to third parties, nor will any employee or contractor of Toktumi have access to your confidential information except the minimum that is necessary to service inquiries made by you.   All of your information is stored in our secure data center, which is protected by armed security guards 24/7/365 and can only be entered by authorized personel with strict identity verification procedures.  Toktumi is committed to keeping your information safe and secure, and is dedicated to protecting your information under all circumstances.
