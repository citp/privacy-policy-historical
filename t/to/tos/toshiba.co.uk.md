# Privacy Policy Statement - Toshiba United Kingdom Portal

Toshiba is committed to respecting the privacy rights of all visitors to the Toshiba web site. This section describes how Toshiba seeks to protect the personal information it receives.

#### Information collected and its use

We do not capture personal information from this site without your knowledge or permission.

There are certain areas of the site where you are asked to provide us with personal information about yourself, such as when registering your details and products purchased, requesting a brochure or registering for a newsletter.

When personal information is requested, visitors are given a choice as to whether they would prefer to opt-out and not receive any mailings from Toshiba.

#### Protecting visitor information

Toshiba implements a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. User's personal information is contained within secure networks and is only accessible by a limited number of employees of Toshiba and its third party agencies, who have special access rights to such systems.

#### Disclosing information collected to outside parties

Toshiba does not and will not sell, trade or otherwise transfer outside the company personally identifiable information that visitors voluntarily provide in any registration or promotional activity submission.
