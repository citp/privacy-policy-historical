 Privacy Statement

At Toshiba of Canada Limited we take pride in providing our customers with the highest quality products, services and support.  To do so effectively, we require certain relevant information from our customers, which is treated with complete confidentiallity and respect.

In concert with the Federal Government’s legislation the PERSONAL INFORMATION PROTECTION AND ELECTRONICS DOCUMENTS ACT (PIPEDA), Toshiba has adopted Ten Privacy Principles.
All information received from individuals is handled in a manner which is consistent with these principles.

Toshiba has implemented policies and procedures which support the ten principles, and our Privacy Officer as well as our staff are committed to ensure that our obligations to you regarding your personal information are fulfilled.  

TOSHIBA’S PRIVACY PRINCIPLES

1. Accountability

Toshiba of Canada Limited is accountable for all personal information in its possession or control, including information disclosed to third parties for processing.

2. Identifying Purposes

 When collecting personal information from our customers, employment candidates or employees we will clearly identify in each case the specific purposes for doing so.

3. Consent

 Consent to the collection of personal information for the identified purposes will be obtained before or at the time the information is collected.  In some cases the party through the process of providing the information grants consent.

4. Limiting Collection

 The collection of personal information will be limited to information that is necessary for the purposes identified by Toshiba of Canada Limited.  Information will be collected only by fair and lawful means.

5. Limiting Use, Disclosure and Retention

 Personal information will not be used or disclosed for purposes other than those for which it was collected, except with the express consent of the individual or as required by law.  It will only be retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the identified purpose, after which time it will be destroyed erased or made anonymous.

6. Accuracy

 Toshiba will use reasonable efforts to keep personal information as accurate, complete and up-to-date as necessary for the purposes identified to the individual.

7. Safeguarding Personal Information

 Toshiba will protect personal information with physical, organizational and technological safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the information.

8. Openness

 Toshiba will make information about its personal information management policies and practices available on request.

9. Access to Personal Information

 Upon written request, Toshiba will provide, within a reasonable time, information about the personal information in its custody or control, including its use and the identity of any third parties to whom the information has been disclosed.  Individuals have the right to challenge the accuracy and completeness of their personal information and request that it be amended as appropriate.  Toshiba will advise third parties to whom the information has been disclosed of the amendment.

10. Challenging Compliance

You have the right to challenge our privacy compliance.  Before we are able to provide you with any information or correct any inaccuracies we may ask you to verify your identity and for other details to help us respond to your request.  If you wish to inquire about aspects of our Privacy Policy please direct your written request to our Privacy Officer, Human Resources Department, 191 McNabb Street, Markham, On., L3R 8H2 or E-Mail us at privacyofficer@toshiba.ca.
 


Personal information is collected from our customers, employment candidates and our employees at certain times and for various reasons.  It may be collected on-line on our Company website, on documents in person or on the phone. 

The following lists the various situations where personal information may be collected and discusses the granting of consent, the purposes for collection, length of retention and disclosure of this information in each situation.
 

EMPLOYMENT CANDIDATES

We encourage high quality individuals at all levels that are looking for challenge, growth and career development, to apply. By submitting your resume to us on-line through this web-site, by e-mail, fax, Canada Post or courier for a posted position or unsolicited, you are granting us consent. As well you may meet with one of our Human Resource Representatives and a hiring manager for an employment interview. During the interview you will be asked for employment related information through various questions. This personal information is used by Toshiba to determine whether you are a suitable candidate for current available or for future positions. The information is stored electronically and password protected or in hard copy in locked cabinets in a secured area. It is retained by us for a period of 6 months at which time it is deleted or confidentially shredded. Your information is not disclosed to any outside organization however may be shared with the appropriate hiring management outside of the Human Resources department but within Toshiba.

 

EMPLOYEES

Once you are an employee at Toshiba, we will require, as well as your resume, various pieces of personal information. By accepting employment at Toshiba you are granting us consent to collect and retain your personal information. This information is used for the express purposes of providing you with statutory and/or private benefits and pension, health, safety, emergency and for other reasons specifically pertaining to your continued working relationship with Toshiba. Your information is stored electronically and in hard copy and is protected by password and in locked cabinets in a secured area. The information is retained for a minimum of seven years and in most cases indefinitely. Your information will be disclosed to various provincial and federal government agencies for purposes of providing health and pension benefits, income tax remission or as required by law. In addition information is disclosed to a payroll services organization, and to our pension and benefits carriers. As an employee you may from time to time purchase our products. Should you do so please refer to the relevant privacy information listed below.

 

PRODUCT PURCHASES

To purchase a Toshiba product we will require your basic personal information such as address, phone number, etc. as well as your credit card information. By providing this information either on-line through this website or by any other method you are granting consent. The information is required in order to complete the purchase transaction, registration and provision of your product warranty, and to provide delivery services. Your credit card information will be retained for 30 days following the transaction after which it will be electronically deleted or confidentially shredded. Your address and phone number and product information will be retained for a period of 5 years following your purchase and will then be electronically deleted. Your name, address, phone number and product information will be disclosed to a third party organization for warranty provision. Your credit card number will be disclosed to a third party organization for verification.

 

WARRANTY REGISTRATION

When registering your new Toshiba product for warranty whether on-line, or in hard copy we will require your basic personal information such as address, phone number, product and date purchased, serial number, etc. By providing this information to us you are granting consent. The information is required in order to register and honour your warranty for the period specified for the product purchased. This information will be retained for a period of 5 years following the purchase date after which it will be electronically deleted or confidentially shredded. This information will be disclosed to a third party for warranty provision.

MARKETING/SALES SURVEY

During the warranty registration process you have the option of voluntarily completing a Sales/Marketing survey. Should you choose to complete it, you are granting us consent. This information will be shared with our Sales and Marketing departments for the purposes of future product promotions and market analysis. It may be retained indefinitely and will be disclosed to a market research firm for analysis. The information is stored electronically and therefore, if no longer retained, will be electronically deleted.

 

PRODUCT SUPPORT

Depending on the product you have purchased you may be able to access support. Should you do so, you will be asked various questions about your identity, address, phone number, product model and serial number and the specific issues you are asking for assistance with. The information that you provide in answer to these questions constitutes personal information and therefore grants Toshiba consent. It will be recorded electronically and represents your history and experience with the specific product in question and will be used to assist with additional issues and answer related questions that may occur in the future. It will be retained for a period of 5 years at which time it will be electronically deleted.

 

SERVICE

Should your product require service, you will be required to provide certain information which will be considered personal. By providing this information you are granting Toshiba consent. The information is required in order to diagnose problems and to effectively provide continued service of a related nature. This information is retained for a period of 5 years at which time it will be electronically deleted. The information is not disclosed to any organization outside of Toshiba.
