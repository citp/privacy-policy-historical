At toptests.co.uk, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by toptests.co.uk and how it is used.

Log Files
Like many other Web sites, toptests.co.uk makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

About TopTests.co.uk

TopTests.co.uk is designed for UK learner drivers to help you to pass your DVSA theory test (car and motorcycle). Extremely user friendly, the site provides free mock theory practice tests that imitate the official DVSA test in terms of question structure and grading standards. Additionally, these tests provide detailed question-level feedback and unique ‘hints’. This is a privately owned website that is not owned by or affiliated with DVLA/DVSA or any other government agency. We don’t provide driving test booking services, either.
