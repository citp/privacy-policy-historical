# Privacy Policies | Vyom Privacy Policies | Corporate Privacy policies

topmasala.com respects your privacy and strives to ensure that any personal information you provide is protected. The following describes our commitment to the privacy of your information, the information we gather, and how that information is used. This site has security measures in place to help protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. The information is stored in a secure operating environment.

All topmasala.com mailing lists are voluntary. We will never add you to an email listing unless you first request it or join a topmasala.com administered mailing list. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time. Additionally, any personal information you provide to topmasala.com is used to improve the quality of service we provide, and will not be intentionally misused. To remove yourself from the mailing list please click here.  
topmasala.com does record and track IP addresses, browser information (such as version, make and model), and referring links (i.e. how you got to the page). This information helps us analyze site traffic patterns and usage as well as improve our product offering and quality of service.  
This site may contain links to other sites. Although we are very selective in choosing these links, topmasala.com does not assume any responsibility for or make any warranties about the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Protecting your privacy is one of our highest priorities, but as with all Web sites, ours may be vulnerable to service interruptions, intentional attacks, or other events that may compromise the security of our site and the information contained therein (including that which you provide). Accordingly, by submitting such information, you acknowledge and agree that topmasala.com is not responsible for any direct or consequential damages that may result from such events.
