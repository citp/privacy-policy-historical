# Privacy Policy - Top Documentary Films

This page discloses the information about how we and our partners gather and disseminate your data when you visit topdocumentaryfilms.com.

1\. Analytics:

This site tracks and collects user's data via [Google Analytics](http://www.google.com/analytics/ "Google Analytics") in an aggregated (not personally identifiable) way. The data includes page-views, age, gender, interests, language, location, behavior and technology being used. We track this information in order to understand our audience and to improve the site.

2\. Newsletter management and distribution:

When you decide to subscribe to our newsletter we use services offered by [Aweber](https://www.aweber.com/landing.htm) and [Google's Feedburner](http://feedburner.google.com/). In this process, with your permission, we collect your email address and store it at the above mentioned partners' servers in order to be able to send our newsletters in an organized and timely fashion. While these companies are respectable and are well known to be reliable we can't be held responsible for any breaches on their end.

3\. Comments:

When you want to leave a comment on this site you're being asked for your name and email address. When you submit the comment we will also collect your IP address. This data (regarding comments) is stored on our servers but it will be never shared with anyone for any purpose.

4\. Third party advertising:

We use third-party advertising companies (e.g. Google Adsense) to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser. To learn more about how Google collects and uses your data you can visit [their Privacy Policy](http://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/partners/).

If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, visit [Network Advertising Initiative](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/), [Digital Advertising Alliance](http://www.aboutads.info/choices/), and/or [Google Ads Settings](http://www.google.com/ads/preferences/).

5\. Affiliate links:

Some of the links on this site are "Amazon affiliate links". This means when you click on one of those links, you will leave this site and land on an Amazon.com page. If you decide and purchase an item from Amazon.com, through our link, we will receive an affiliate commission. However, we only recommend products (documentaries) that we believe are worth purchasing and will add value to our visitors. If you want to know more about how Amazon.com uses your data visit [Amazon.com Privacy Notice](http://www.amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=468496).

6\. Contacting the website:

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can [contact us here](http://topdocumentaryfilms.com/contact/).
