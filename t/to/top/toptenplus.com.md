# Privacy Policy

Privacy of Users is really important to TopTenPlus and we endevour to ensure that the privacy of our users is protected. If we do request any personal information we assure that it will comply to our Privacy Policy listed below.

TopTenPlus may ask or store following data of its users :

  * Name


  * Email Address


  * Demographic Info


  * Interests



We may take this data for some of the following things

  * It will help us to know your interests and needs and we will keep on improving our website and content.


  * We may send you promotional offers and emails regarding your interests only .



**Security**

We at TopTenPlus ensure that all your information is safe and secure. To do this we use different kind of physical and electronics setups and procedures to make sure that whatever data we collect online is secure and protected. So that there is no disclosure to unauthorized organization or people.

**Use of Cookies**

A cookie is a small internet file which asks permission to be placed on your computer disk for temporary usage. A cookie is a small file which asks permission to be placed on your computer’s hard drive. If you agree to add cookies, it keeps storing the data of when and which website you visited on the internet. They allow web applications to respond to you.

We use traffic log cookies only to check and analyse which pages are being mostly used by our users. This helps up know and identify that what kind of information our users love to know from us so as to improve our services better. As soon as we get the required information , the data is instantly removed from our system.

Cookies help us provide you with a better website and run the blog better by enabling us to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not. A cookie in no way gives us access to your computer or any information about you, other than the data you choose to share with us only so you are safe from every side.

You can choose to accept or decline any kind of cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. This may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.

Now we also provide user searched based ads with Google adsense and we have no control of which cookies are used by our 3rd party.
