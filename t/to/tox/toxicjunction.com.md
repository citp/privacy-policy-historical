# About Toxic Junction

**How we collect information from you**

When you fill in our membership registration form or our newsletter subscription form, we collect personal details from you which we use to identify who you are. If you do not want us to find out who you are, then do not register accounts, post messages, or subscribe to our newsletter at ToxicJunction.com. 

**Cookies**

Many people use cookies. Cookies are small files of information which are stored on your computer. ToxicJunction.com uses cookies to remember your visits, so that we can provide a better experience on ToxicJunction.com. We may also use cookies to remember the type of advertisements being displayed on your browser. Please note that if you click on an advertisement, the third party site may set other different cookies on your browser. 

**How we look after your information**

ToxicJunction makes sure that all your information are stored as safely as possible. When you visit ToxicJunction on a public computer, you are responsible for the information entered and these information maybe be intercepted by other programs or parties.

 **In the Event of Merger, Sale, or Bankruptcy**

In the event that Toxicjunction is acquired by or merged with a third party entity, we reserve the right, in any of these circumstances, to transfer or assign the information we have collected from our Users as part of such merger, acquisition, sale, or other change of control. In the unlikely event of our bankruptcy, insolvency, reorganization, receivership, or assignment for the benefit of creditors, or the application of laws or equitable principles affecting creditors' rights generally, we may not be able to control how your personal information is treated, transferred, or used.

**When do we give information you provide us to other people?**

We never give your information directly to anyone outside of ToxicJunction.com.
