Cookies Policy

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. The Company uses cookies to identify and track visitors and their behaviors, their usage of the Site, and their access preferences.

Cookies may be classified under one or various of the following categories in accordance with their purposes:

  • Analysis cookies: These cookies, whether used by the Company or by third parties, allow us to quantify the number of visitors of the Site and to perform measurements and statistical analysis of the use made by visitors of the Site and the Services. We use them to analyze Your browsing of the Site in order to improve the Site and the Services.
  • Technical cookies: These cookies, whether used by the Company or by third parties, are used enable communication between Your equipment and the equipment of the Site, such as those used to enable the functionality of media players for displaying content or flash content, or such as those used to remember items placed by You on a virtual shopping cart should such functionality be offered in the Site.
  • Customization cookies: These cookies, whether used by the Company or by third parties, allow us to offer You an access to the Site which is tailored in accordance to certain predefined parameters based on the settings or characteristics of Your equipment such as for instance language settings or characteristics, type of browser used to access the Site, location, etc. Customization cookies may also be used to allow You to request that the Site remembers certain parameters on your behalf such as remembering your language preferences or Your user account so as to prevent you from having to resubmit your log in credentials in subsequent browsing sessions.
  • Advertising cookies: These cookies, whether used by the Company or by third parties, allow us to manage the advertising spaces in the Site in the most effective way possible based on several criteria such as the frequency at which the ads are displayed.
  • Behavioral Advertising cookies: These cookies, whether used by the Company or by third parties, allow us to manage the advertising spaces in the Site in the most effective way possible by means of tracking Your behavior through the continuous monitoring of your browsing habits and allowing us to develop a specific profile tailored to such browsing habits which is then used to serve You ads based on the said profile.

Based on how long they last before they are removed from Your computer, those cookies can also be session cookies, which are erased from Your computer when the browsing session expires or persistent cookies, are stored on Your computer during more than a single browsing session until their expiration date is reached.
