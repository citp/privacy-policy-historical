Privacy

At Todd Snyder adding to the modernity and versatility of your wardrobe is secondary only to our belief in protecting your privacy.

How we use your personal information
  1. When you make a purchase from our website, we’ll ask for and store your name, mailing address, phone number, email address and contact preferences. This information is all standard for fulfilling your order, providing you with newsletters, and helping solve any problems you run into using toddsnyder.com.
  2. If you enter any type of sweepstakes, promotion or contests we may use the information you provide us for administering and marketing purposes according to the terms and conditions of the specific promotion.
  3. We may use personal information towards research to improve Todd Snyder and our customer support.
  4. We may collect information regarding activities on our website, location and time zone. We use this information as a tool to improve Todd Snyder, our services and products and most importantly, your experience.
  5. As you browse toddsnyder.com, advertising cookies will be placed on your computer so that we can understand what you are interested in. Our display advertising partner, AdRoll, then enables us to present you with retargeting advertising on other sites based on your previous interaction with toddsnyder.com. The techniques our partners employ do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address or telephone number. 

    Protection of Personal information
    1. We will protect personal information by reasonable security safeguards against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification. All personal and non-personal information collected by us during purchases is protected by a Secure Sockets Layer encryption.
    2. When posting to Todd Snyder's product comments or social networking service, whatever you share is visible to other users and can be read, collected or used by other visitors to the website. Todd Snyder is not responsible for the misuse of any personal information that a person submits freely on our website through the use of product comments or social networking services. You are responsible for the personal information you choose to submit in these instances. For example, if you list your name and email address in a product comment, that information is public and Todd Snyder is not responsible for the misuse of that information in any circumstance.
    3. Any questions? If you have any questions or concerns about Todd Snyder's privacy policy please email us.
