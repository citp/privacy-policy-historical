| Privacy Policy  
---  
tolmolbol is an online application that helps you reach out to your locality and find information about your friends, businesses, events, classifieds and local favourites. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.  
Personal Information

tolmolbol asks for some personal information when you sign-up, including your email address and a password. By registering yourself, you will be able to access many services that require sign-up. As a tolmolbol member you can create an account based on your name, age, gender, hometown, interests, occupation, photos, gender and any other content. This information is voluntary and may be available to other users. 

Your name will be associated with your posts, reviews and messages. Additionally, your name and email address may be included in the messages, compliments, friendship requests or invitations you send to other users. When you invite new members or send messages through tolmolbol, we may collect and maintain information associated with those messages, including email addresses and content.

Further, we may also log your browser-type related information, IP address, requested URL, referring URL, and timestamp. This will help us diagnose problems and provide the best level of service to you. In addition, we will be able to perform statistical analyses of user behavior and characteristics in order to measure interest in and use of the various areas of the site.  
  
Links

Our website may contain links to third party websites. We encourage you to read the privacy polices on these websites as we are not responsible for their respective privacy practices.
