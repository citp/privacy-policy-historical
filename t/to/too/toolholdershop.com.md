# CNC VDI Tool Holders

IMTE Inc. is committed to maintaining the confidentiality of our customers. We collect personal information such as name, company name, address, phone number, email address, IP, and other applicable contact information. We also collect credit card information once it is provided to us. Credit card information is not stored in our electronic databases and only employees who are directly involved in the billing process have access to this information.   
We DO NOT share, sell or otherwise disclose information about our clients and/or their contact information to any other party except as required by law or to process and ship the orders.   
IMTE Inc. uses the contact information to communicate with its clients in order to understand their needs, to promote products or services that may be of interest to them, and to process and complete ongoing transactions.
