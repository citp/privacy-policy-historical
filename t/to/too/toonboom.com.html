1. What information do we collect?

Anonymous Browsing

You can visit this site without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself, including your email address. In this case, our web server may collect the IP address you used to access the Internet and the website from which you came. This information is used by us and our business partners to measure the number of visits, average time spent, page views, and other statistics about visitors to our site in general. We may also use this data to monitor site performance, for systems administration purposes, to make this site easier and more convenient to use, and to report information in aggregate form to our advisers and other third parties (e.g. how many visitors log on to this site).

You may set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie or to prevent cookies from being sent. Please note, however, that by not accepting cookies, you will limit the functionality we can provide you when you visit our site. Tracking user preferences also helps us serve you targeted advertising. Targeted advertisements are probably more interesting for you and are more productive for our advertisers. Please note that we do not share your personal information with advertisers.

Social Media (Features) and Widgets

Our Website includes Social Media Features, such as the Facebook Like button. These Features may collect your IP address, which page you are visiting on our site, and may set a cookie to enable the Feature to function properly. Social Media Features are hosted by a third party and your interactions with these Features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing it.

Personal Information Collection

There are times when we may ask you for, or you may choose to provide, information about yourself and your business to assist us in meeting your various needs. This information may be used for the purpose of providing you with products or services you have requested, servicing your account, improving our services, developing and/or informing you of additional products or services provided by TOON BOOM, our affiliates or our business partners that may be of interest, or answering questions you may have. To provide these products or services, we may need to ask you directly to provide, among other things, your name, mailing address, postal code, email address, phone number, and date of birth. Also, you may elect to apply online for one of our products or services, which may require us and our subcontractors to gather additional information from our affiliates and third parties (including credit information) to determine your eligibility or to fulfill your order. At times, we also conduct online surveys to better understand the needs, profiles, and experience of our visitors. You will be given the option of not participating in such surveys. Once you provide us such information about yourself, your browsing on this site will no longer be anonymous to us.

Analytics/Log Files

We gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes IP addresses and browser type.

We use this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze trends, administer the site, track user movements around the site, and gather demographic information about our user base as a whole.

We do link some of this automatically-collected data to personally identifiable information. This allows us to deliver a better experience by reducing the amount of data that needs to be entered when filling out forms, downloading gated content, completing surveys, etc.

Children

Access to this site is reserved to adults only, i.e. individuals who are considered major in the jurisdiction from which this site is accessed and no portion of this site is directed at children nor are they encouraged to visit this site.

Please note that we do not knowingly solicit information from children and we do not knowingly market our products and services to children.

4. How is your information secured and protected?

Where TOON BOOM considers it appropriate, TOON BOOM uses encryption and/or authentication tools, among other methods, to protect certain web-based personal information. Emails you send us are not necessarily secure when they are transmitted to us.

Certain sections of our Website allows you to order products and services for which you may be required to provide your credit card information. These sections use Transport Security Layer (TSL) protocol for browsers that support 256-bit encryption. TSL encrypts information as it travels between the customer and TOON BOOM so that your purchase information cannot be read as it travels over the Internet. You will know that your browser is secure by a change in the URL from "http://" to "https://". If the URL on a page asking you to provide information remains as "http://", beware as this may result in an unsecure transmission. If you are not using a browser which adheres to the TSL protocol and supports 256-bit encryption, the information you provide may not be secure and we recommend that you contact TOON BOOM by telephone to place your order.

When processing credit card purchases over the Internet, TOON BOOM or our third party service provider will verify your credit card information with the appropriate financial institution prior to processing your purchase. Neither we nor our third party service provider will supply your credit card information to any other third party.

We instruct our employees with access to your information that it is to be used only in adherence with the principles set forth in this Privacy Policy and the laws applicable to each specific business. Employees who misuse customer information may be subject to legal action by Toon Boom.
