# TotalVid - Skate, Surf, Street Racing, Snowboarding, and Action Sports Videos - Download, DVD, V

### PRIVACY POLICY

We will not provide any of your personal information to third-parties.

We respect your privacy and want you to know what information about you we collect and what we do with that information. The following Privacy Policy applies to all information that we collect or receive from you.

Information that you provide to us, and information that we collect from your activities on the TotalVid.com site, such as the pages you view and the videos you download and purchase, will be used solely by us to enhance your online experience by understanding you better and allowing us to make you aware of new services, programs, offers and products in which you may be interested.

If you authorize us to retain your credit card information in our database to facilitate your checkout process for future transactions, we will keep that credit card information confidential. 

Please note that TotalVid may contain links to other websites and occasionally other websites may be co-branded with the TotalVid name. Websites that are linked to TotalVid.com or carry the TotalVid brand may have privacy policies which differ from ours.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please send us an email at privacy@totalvid.com.
