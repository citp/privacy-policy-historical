By using this Service, you agree and consent that Tourbuzz may at its sole discretion and without your further consent and without notifying you (whether before or after disclosure) disclose your personal information to any person, at any time and for any purpose, including, without limiting the generality of the foregoing, in any one or more of the following instances:

## Log Files

Like many other Web sites, www.Tourbuzz makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. 

## Cookies And Web Beacons

Tourbuzz does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser. 

We may also use cookies for retargeting. Retargeting allows us to show you ads about Tourbuzz when you are visiting other sites on the internet. Our retargeting is managed by our advertising partners, including companies like Google and DoubleClick.
