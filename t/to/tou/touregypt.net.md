# Privacy Policy

  


##  Privacy Policy

Tour Egypt believes in protecting your privacy. We place high priority in securing your personal information from all sorts of invasive automated and manual forces. Additionally, our stored information has never been, nor never will be sold or rented to third party sources. Any information provided to us whether by request form or payment method, has never been, and will never be shared with a third party, and is only used to facilitate requests to us.
