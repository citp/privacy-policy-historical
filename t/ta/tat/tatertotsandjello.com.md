# Privacy -- Tatertots and Jello

We allow third-party companies to serve ads and/or collect certain anonymous information when you visit our web site. These companies may use non-personally identifiable information (e.g., click stream information, browser type, time and date, subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over) during your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you. These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit [www.networkadvertising.org](http://www.networkadvertising.org/).

**About Our Windows 8 App**

#### Network Capabilities

This application uses the network to get the data from the  **Tatertots and Jello** blog, and send your comment as the data you share.

#### Location Services

We do not request, collect, or access your location information.

#### Collection and Use

This application does not collect or publish any personal information.

#### Analytic Data

This application collects and sends  **anonymous  **information. This data will help to analyze the usage of the application and to fix reported crashes.

#### Sharing Services

We enable you to share information such as messages with others within your network. We cannot control the actions of others with whom you may choose to share your information.

#### Contact

If you would like to report any violations of this policy, please click on the following [link](mailto:admin@ideanotion.net?subject=Windows%208%20Privacy%20Policy%0d).
