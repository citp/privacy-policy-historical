# Texila American University Privacy Policy | TAU Privacy Policy

#### Scope

This policy is applicable for the current and former students of TAU, visitors of the website, and any other user who uses the services offered by the Texila American University. These may include websites or any mobile applications operated by and on their behalf. This policy relates to offline and online collection, storage, and transfer of Personal Information.

Some of the sites or services may come under the control of additional privacy policies or disclosures. These services are called "Additional Policies". If there is any conflict between any Additional Policies and policy, the Additional Policies will control.

#### Definitions

"Business Associates" means they are the third party service providers to which the Texila American University may contract with them to provide services on our behalf. This include the Social Networking, hosting, web analytics, Online Behavioural Advertising, lead generation, academic, business, career, or financial services.

"Educational Partners" of Texila American University have their subsidiaries and other affiliates including educational institutions or agents providing educational related services under a contract. 

"Financial Information" indicates particular Personal Information of financial characteristics such as your credit card number. This collected information may include: Personal Information, Financial Information, Web Analytical Information or any other Details gathered from you through our Websites. "Personal Information" means Details that may be used, alone to recognize a particular people and contains financial information. 

“Online Behavioural Advertising” means the collection of data from a particular computer or device regarding web viewing behaviours for the purpose of using such data to predict user interests to deliver advertising to that computer from such Web viewing behaviours.

"Site" or "Sites", means websites operated by or on behalf of Texila American University, regardless of domain name or IP address.

"Social Network", means various communication technologies used in the internet that facilitate conversation and interaction between people in online and includes, blogs, discussion forums, wikis, chat sessions, news groups, etc.

"Social Network Content" or "SNC" means user-generated content you may share via a Social Network. It includes Personal Information, videos, and pictures that you may upload to a Social Network and visible to every user of a Social Network pages.

"Your University" means University of Phoenix.

"We", "us", and "our" refer to the group of Texila American University.

"Unrelated Entities" means third parties who are not provides educational services as Educational Partners and as Business Associates.

"Web Analytical Information" means internet generated Information we collect when you visit our Sites. Then This Information may be linked with the Personal Information and the university may contact you for identical purpose. All the details we collected are kept safe

We collect various types of Information from you through our websites where you can express interest in our services, through mobile applications, over the phone, or printed forms, and in person. Some Information is collected automatically through various web technologies including Social Networking tools used by our University. 

Sometimes we get Information when you provide it to us voluntarily. We may also obtain information from Educational Partners and Unrelated Entities Information Collected Automatically on the Sites

Web Analytical Information automatically gathers all the information whenever you visit one of our sites. In general, the collected details do not recognize you personally. Examples of Web Analytical Information include, but are not limited to:

  * IP address

  * Connection Speed

  * Language settings
  * Country, State, Designated Market Area (DMA), City 
  * Domain (.com, .net, .mil, .org, .edu, etc.)



This Information may be linked with Personal Information to get the specifications described in this privacy policy

#### How We Use and Disclose This Information

We will not sell or misuse your Personal Information to third party agents except in accordance with this policy.

We may collect, use, and disclose your personal Information for the following purposes:

  * to figure out your admissibility and to signup you for the best academic applications
  * to provide requested goods and services 
  * to reply to your queries and provide client support
  * to provide information in which you have indicated as a interest
  * for our internal marketing purposes, which includes, but is not limited to, sending you details about products, services, updates, etc. that we think may be of interest to you 
  * for fostering communication and collaboration among members of our University community through Social Networks
  * for discussing with our Academic Associates who may get in touch with you with regard to their educational or other services 
  * for sharing with our Educational Partners or Business Associates who are performing services on our behalf 
  * to evaluate how Websites and services are being accessed and used
  * for Online Behavioural Advertising purposes 
  * to enhance Site performance and delivery
  * to prevent potentially illegal activities (including downloading of copyrighted materials according to our Infringement policy) 
  * to analyze academic outcomes and preferences of studying
  * to analyze risk and business results
  * to acquire transaction for services that we provide to you 
  * to offer you with details concerning preparations and other choices for the repayment of resources financed to you for your education
  * to maintain business records 
  * to enforce our Terms of Use
  * to provide to Educational Partners, Business Associates or Unrelated Entities in connection with the actual reorganization, merger, acquisition, financing, securitization, insuring, sale or other disposal of our business, including purposes of determining whether to proceed with such transaction or fulfilling any records. In the event of any actual reorganization, such Information may be transferred as part of the transaction to the acquirer

#### Safe senders list

To ensure you receive email notifications from Texila American University about your application status, program details, payment receipt confirmation etc., please add ['info@tauedu.org'](mailto:info@tauedu.org) to your safe sender's list. Below is the procedure to add ['info@tauedu.org'](mailto:info@tauedu.org) to your safe sender's list across mail clients.

**Outlook :** Under 'Tools' menu bar, select options. Under 'preferences', select 'junk email' and click on 'safe sender' tab. Click 'add' button. Type ['info@tauedu.org'](mailto:info@tauedu.org) and submit.

**MSN Hotmail :** In the options page, click 'mail' on the left hand menu. Then click on 'junk email protection' and click on 'add senders to safe list'. Type ['info@tauedu.org'](mailto:info@tauedu.org) and then click 'add’.

**Yahoo! Mail :** Under 'addresses' on the top menu, click 'add contact'. Type ['info@tauedu.org'](mailto:info@tauedu.org) in the email field and click 'save'. 

**Gmail :** Go to 'Mail settings' and select 'filters'. 'Create a new filter' and type ['info@tauedu.org'](mailto:info@tauedu.org) at the 'From' field. Click on 'next step' and check 'never send it to spam'. Click on 'create filter'.
