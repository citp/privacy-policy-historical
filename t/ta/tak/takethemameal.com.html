privacy policy . . .

What information does TakeThemAMeal.com collect regarding meal recipients, and how is it used?
We request a name, and provide a user password. This information is used to allow meal providers to find the meal schedule for the recipient to whom they are delivering a meal. We also allow for a meal coordinator to provide details on when and where to deliver the meals. This information is also displayed to meal deliverers.

What information does TakeThemAMeal.com collect regarding meal coordinators, and how is it used?
We request a name, email address and phone number. This information is then displayed to meal providers in case they need to contact the meal coordinator.

What information does TakeThemAMeal.com collect regarding meal providers, and how is it used?
We request a name, email address and phone number. This information is displayed to the meal coordinator and to the meal providers, in case either would need to contact a meal deliverer regarding changes or cancellations of meals.

Does TakeThemAMeal.com use the information collected for any other purposes not described above?
From time to time we may send an email to all past meal coordinators and meal providers if we have a particularly exciting TakeThemAMeal.com announcement. Aside from the (very) occasional update, we do not use the information for any other purposes. We do not give or sell the collected names, phone numbers or email addresses. Any exceptions to this policy of sharing your name, address or email address with a partner company will be done only with your permission. Third parties (including advertisers) may serve content and/or advertisements and collect information directly from visitors and may place or recognize cookies on visitors’ browsers.

In what ways can the general public access the information stored on TakeThemAMeal.com?
As is hopefully evident through use of TakeThemAMeal.com, information can only be accessed by knowing the last name and user password for a meal recipient.
