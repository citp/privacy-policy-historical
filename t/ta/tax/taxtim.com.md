# Privacy policy | TaxTim SA

## Protecting Your Privacy

**We strongly believe in protecting your privacy**. We've put together this privacy policy to explain how your information is protected, stored, and used. This privacy policy applies to our web site. By using our website, you consent to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy and are aware that our policies may evolve in the future. 

## Data Collection and Storage

We collect and store all information provided during the [sign up process](https://www.taxtim.com/za/get-started.php). This includes email / mobile number and first name. We also store the information you enter into the system during use. This information will not be disclosed to any third-party without your explicit consent, except as aggregate, statistical information that will not include any identifying information.

TaxTim uses third party vendors and hosting partners to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to run the website. However, you retain all rights to your data.

TaxTim uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provisioning, identification and authentication, services improvements, contact, and research.

Account cancellation results in a complete removal of your data from our live systems. Back-up and archival data will be retained, but not accessible except for the purposes of a complete restoration of our systems for emergency use.

## Disclosure

TaxTim may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with subpoenas.
