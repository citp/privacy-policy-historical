# Taxback.com Privacy Policy

Taxback Inc, trading as taxback.com, and is affiliate companies, fully respect your right to privacy in relation to your interactions with this website.

Any information which is provided by you will be treated with the highest standards of security and confidentiality in accordance with Data Protection Acts 1988 and 2003, Directive 95/46/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of 24 October 1995.

We will only use the personal information you provide to deliver the services you have requested, or for our lawful, disclosed purposes. We will not make your personal details available outside our organisation without your consent, unless obliged by law.

If at any time you decide you no longer wish us to hold or use the personal information you have provided us, or in the event that the information becomes out of date, please feel free to notify us and we will remove or correct the information as soon as possible.
