# Tapjoy :: Fueling Great Apps -

We at Tapjoy, Inc. ("Tapjoy") know that you care about how your personal information is used and shared, and we take your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our Privacy Policy. **By visiting or using Tapjoy's applications, website and services, and any other linked pages, features, content, or any other services we offer from time to time by in connection therewith (collectively, the "Tapjoy Services"), you acknowledge that you accept the practices and policies outlined in this Privacy Policy, and you hereby consent that we will collect, use, and share your information in the following ways.**

Tapjoy has been awarded TRUSTe's Privacy Seal signifying that this privacy policy and practices have been reviewed by TRUSTe for compliance with [TRUSTe's program requirements](http://www.truste.com/privacy_seals_and_services/consumer_privacy/privacy-programs-requirements.html) including transparency, accountability and choice regarding the collection and use of your personal information. TRUSTe's mission, as an independent third party, is to accelerate online trust among consumers and organizations globally through its leading privacy trustmark and innovative trust solutions. If you have questions or complaints regarding our privacy policy or practices, please contact us at [privacy@tapjoy.com](mailto:privacy@tapjoy.com). If you are not satisfied with our response you can contact [TRUSTe here](http://watchdog.truste.com/pvr.php?page=complaint). The TRUSTe program covers data collected on our website at [www.tapjoy.com](https://www.tapjoy.com/) and not the Tapjoy services. 

## I. What does this privacy policy cover?

This Privacy Policy covers our treatment of personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") that we gather when you are accessing or using the Tapjoy Services. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that we do not own or control, or to individuals that we do not employ or manage. 

## II. What personal information does Tapjoy collect?

The information we gather from customers enables us to personalize and improve our services and to allow our users to set up a user account and profile that can be used to interact with other users through the Tapjoy Services. We collect the following types of information from our customers: 

### A. Personal Information You Provide to Us

We receive and store any information you provide to us. The types of Personal Information collected may include your name, user name, email address, IP address, and phone number. You can choose not to provide us with certain information, but then you may not be able to take advantage of many of our special features that are optimized based upon some of your Personal Information. We use the Personal Information you provide to enhance your experience with the Tapjoy Services. 

### B. Personal Information Collected Automatically

  * Whenever you interact with the Tapjoy Services, we automatically receive and record information on our server logs. The type of information recorded is dependent upon the Tapjoy Services with which you are interacting. When interacting with Tapjoy's website, we record your IP address, "cookie" information and the page you requested. When you interact with Tapjoy's mobile applications or services we record your unique phone I.D. number (the "UDID") and logs of your specific interactions with Tapjoy-enabled applications or mobile websites. We may associate this usage information with your Personal Information in connection with providing the Tapjoy Services.
  * "Cookies" are identifiers we transfer to your computer or mobile device through your browser that allow us to recognize your browser and tell us how and when pages in our site are visited and by how many people. You may be able to change the preferences on your browser to prevent or limit your computer's or mobile device's acceptance of cookies, but this may prevent you from taking advantage of some features of the Tapjoy Services. Cookies are not linked to your personal information.
  * When we collect usage information (such as the numbers and frequency of visitors to the Tapjoy Services) through 3rd party tracking technologies such as cookies, web beacons, scripts and other tracking technologies., we never disclose this information to any partners other than in aggregate form and not in a manner that would identify you personally.



### C. E-mail Communications

We may receive a confirmation when you open an email from us. This confirmation helps us make emails more interesting and improve our service. If you do not want to receive email from us, please indicate your preference by following the "Unsubscribe" link on the bottom of any email from us. 

## III. Will Tapjoy share any of the personal information it receives?

We neither rent nor sell your Personal Information to anyone. We share your Personal Information in personally identifiable form only as described below. 

### A. Affiliated Businesses We Do Not Control

In certain situations, businesses we're affiliated with may sell items or provide services to you in conjunction with the Tapjoy Services (either alone or jointly with us). You can recognize when an affiliated business is associated with such a transaction, and we will share your Personal Information with that affiliated business only to the extent that it is related to and to fulfill such transaction. 

### B. Agents

We employ other companies and people to perform tasks on our behalf such as email service providers, payment processors and hosting agents and need to share your information with them to provide products or services to you. Unless we tell you differently, our agents do not have any right to use the Personal Information we share with them beyond what is necessary to assist us. 

### C. Your Actions

Any content (including without limitation images, captions, and comments) you voluntarily disclose for other users to view via the Tapjoy Services is not Personal Information; it becomes publicly available and can be collected and used by others, and may be redistributed through the internet and other media channels. To request removal of your personal information from our blog or community forum, contact us at [privacy@tapjoy.com](mailto:privacy@tapjoy.com). In some cases, we may not be able to remove your personal information, in which case we will let you know if we are unable to do so and why. 

### D. Communication About the Tapjoy Services

As part of the Tapjoy Services, you may receive communications from us, whether by email or other means. We may send you email, contact you, or engage you in other communication that is related to your use of the Tapjoy Services. 

### E. Promotional Offers

We may send offers to certain users on behalf of other businesses. However, when we do so, we do not give the other business your name and address. If you do not wish to receive these offers, please unsubscribe to these communications via the link at the bottom of such communications. 

### F. Business Transfers

We may choose to buy or sell assets. In these types of transactions, customer information is typically one of the business assets that are transferred. Also, if we (or substantially all of our assets) are acquired, or if we go out of business or enter bankruptcy, Personal Information would be one of the assets transferred to or acquired by a third party. You would be notified as explained below in "Changes to the Privacy Policy" 

### G. Protection of Tapjoy and Others

We may release Personal Information when we believe in good faith that release is necessary to comply with that law; enforce or apply our conditions of use and other agreements; or protect the rights, property, or safety of Tapjoy, our employees, our users, or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction. 

### H. With Your Consent

Except as set forth above, you will be notified when your Personal Information may be shared with third parties, and will be able to prevent the sharing of this information. 

##  IV. Is personal information about me secure? 

We follow generally accepted standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. We endeavor to the privacy of your Personal Information we hold in our records, but we cannot guarantee complete security. Unauthorized entry or use, hardware or software failure, and other factors, may compromise the security of user information at any time. When you enter sensitive information (such as login credentials) we encrypt the transmission of that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). The Tapjoy Services regularly interact with third party sites and applications. We are not responsible for the privacy policies and/or practices on other sites and applications. When linking to another site or application, you should read that party's privacy policy. 

## V. What personal information can I access?

If you have any questions about viewing or updating information we have on file about you, please contact us at [mobilesupport@tapjoy.com](mailto:mobilesupport@tapjoy.com). 

## VI. What choices to I have?

  * You can always opt not to disclose information to use, but keep in mind some information may be needed to take advantage of some of our special features. 
  * You may be able to add or update certain information as explained in Section V above. When you update information, however, we may maintain a copy of the unrevised information in our records. 
  * We will retain personal data we process on behalf of our client for as long as needed provide services to our client. We will retain and use this personal information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. 
  * You may request deletion of your certain of your Personal Information by sending an e-mail to You may request deletion of your certain of your Personal Information by sending an e-mail to [privacy@tapjoy.com](mailto:privacy@tapjoy.com). We will respond to your request within 30 business days. Please note that we may retain your UDID in our records to indicate that you requested that we delete your information and so that we do not send you advertisements or other products to you in the future. 
  * If you do not wish to receive email or other mail from us, please contact us at [privacy@tapjoy.com](mailto:privacy@tapjoy.com). Please note that if you do not want to receive legal notices from us, such as this Privacy Policy, those legal notices will still govern your use of the Website, and you are responsible for reviewing such legal notices for changes. 



## VII. Other Items

You can log in to the blog on our site using sign-in services such as Facebook Connect or an Open ID provider. These services will authenticate your identity and provide you the option to share certain personal information with us such as your name and email address to pre-populate our sign up form. Services like Facebook Connect give you the option to post information about your activities on this Web site to your profile page to share with others within your network. 

With your consent we may post your testimonial along with your name. Our Web site offers publicly accessible blogs or community forums. You should be aware that any information you provide in these areas may be read, collected, and used by others who access them. To request removal of your personal information from our blog, testimonial, or community forum, contact us at [privacy@tapjoy.com](mailto:privacy@tapjoy.com). In some cases, we may not be able to remove your personal information, in which case we will let you know if we are unable to do so and why. 

## VIII. Changes to this privacy policy

We may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time. Use of information we collect now is subject to the Privacy Policy in effect at the time such information is used. If we make changes in the way we use Personal Information, we will notify you by posting an announcement on our website or sending you an email. You are bound by any changes to the Privacy Policy when you use the Tapjoy Services after such changes have been first posted. 

## IX. Questions or concerns

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policies, please send us a detailed message to [privacy@tapjoy.com](mailto:privacy@tapjoy.com). or at 111 Sutter Street, 13th Floor, San Francisco, CA, 94104. We will make every effort to resolve your concerns. 

## X. Opt-out

To opt your mobile device out of Tapjoy services, please submit your device id below. 

_iOS Devices:_ Connect your device to iTunes and click on the Serial Number to reveal your device id. (Identifier - UDID) 

_Android Devices:_ From your device, go to Settings -> About Phone -> Status. Your device id will be listed either as IMEI or MEID depending on your carrier.
