.What This Privacy Policy Covers

Information Collection and Use

General

  • Tapesh.Com collects personal information when you register with our services that require registration, when you visit pages or the pages of certain Tapesh.Com partners, and when you join any programs.
  • When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. Once you register with Tapesh.Com services of any kind and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.
  • Tapesh.Com automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address , cookies information, and the page you request.
  • Tapesh.Com uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • Tapesh.Com does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:
    • We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims;
