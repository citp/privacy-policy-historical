As a Member of the Site, you may elect to use various Site features which provide us with additional information about you, such as a personal User statement and description, a personal photo or graphic, personal fight predictions and commentary, custom rankings and commentary, forum posts, and forum post ratings and replies.

We also use cookies, which are small text files containing a string of alphanumeric characters identifying your browser which are stored on your computer's hard drive. A cookie does not collect personal information about you. We use cookies to speed your Site login, validate your identity, customize Site options and features based on your preferences, and otherwise facilitate your use of the Site and improve Site quality. Cookies set by third parties are governed by the privacy policies of those third parties and not by this Privacy Policy. Generally, you can set your browser not to accept cookies or to notify you if you are sent a cookie, giving you the opportunity to choose whether or not to accept the cookie. Please note that if you set your browser not to accept cookies, certain areas of the Site may not function properly.

Information and content that you post on the Site becomes available to the public. In the event that we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.
