# Privacy Policy | Tapoutmusic

Privacy Statement

Tapoutmusic.com is committed to protecting your privacy online. We will not rent, sell or share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law.

What information do we collect?

Tapoutmusics.com doesn’t collect any personal information at the moment.

Cookies and Web Beacons 

Tapoutmusic does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.

Modification of Privacy Policy

Tapoutmusics.com may modify the Privacy Policy which will  
become effective immediately upon posting on the website. Your continued use of the website and any associated services indicates your acceptance of the Privacy Policy.
