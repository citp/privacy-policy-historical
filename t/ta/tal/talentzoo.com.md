# Advertising Jobs at Talent Zoo:

As a user of TalentZoo.com, you can be certain your privacy will be respected and your confidentiality maintained. Talent Zoo understands the need for privacy and employs a strict privacy policy. 

Talent Zoo does NOT collect information, except when you voluntarily provide information to us, and once information is provided to Talent Zoo, that information is NOT shared with anyone without your prior consent. The only information Talent Zoo permits you to share via our web site is your contact information and your resume. 

Talent Zoo reserves the right to analyze the activity and characteristics of its members and to distribute aggregated information to Talent Zoo's members, advertisers, and other third parties. Talent Zoo reserves the right to contact its members regarding alterations and additions to the substance of the site and data contained in the site, and other issues relevant to Talent Zoo and/or information collected by it 

This privacy statement is for information only, and is not intended to and does not create any contractual or other legal rights in or on behalf of any party.
