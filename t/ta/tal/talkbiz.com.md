# Privacy Policy Statement

Our postal address is:

**TalkBiz News  
Attn: Paul Myers  
49 Hunt Ave  
Buffalo NY 14207** We can be reached via e-mail at [webmaster@talkbiz.com](mailto:webmaster@talkbiz.com) or by telephone at 1-716-877-0956 

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes the visitor's domain name, but **not the e-mail address**.

We collect aggregate information on what pages visitors access, information volunteered by the visitor for specific purposes, (such as survey information and/or site registrations), and email addresses of those who subscribe to our publications.

The information we collect is used to notify visitors about updates to our Web site, and to send newsletters or other periodic emailings to those who request subscriptions. Contact information is not shared with other organizations for any purposes.

The bulletin board software used on this site offers visitors the option of being notified when responses to their posts are made, or of being notified when any new post is made on a board. These addresses, if entered by the visitor, are not accessed (except for the purpose of making backups of the entire site) for any purpose, except by the automatic functions of the bulletin board software.

People requesting information concerning products or services from TalkBiz via autoresponder or direct inquiry may receive additional followup materials and contact concerning their inquiries.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending email to us at the above address and telling us that you do not want to receive e-mail from our company. You may unsubscribe from the various lists we offer individually, while remaining subscribed to others, or you may request to be removed from all lists.

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us with regarding orders they have placed on-line.
