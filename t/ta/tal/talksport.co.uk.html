4. Cookies


A cookie is a piece of information, like a tag, that is stored on your computer's hard drive by your web browser when you visit a site. As with may other on line services, talkSPORT uses cookies to gather certain usage information such as how and when pages in our site are visited, and by how many people. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or show when a cookie is being set up without affecting your use of most of the features of the talkSPORT site.
