# TamPogo™ Privacy Policy

TamPogo™ Privacy Policy
Effective Date of this Policy: July 13, 2009
Welcome to the TamPogo website. We understand that privacy online is important to TamPogo visitors, Direct
Customers and iReps. TamPogo wants individuals to understand the policy, laws and regulations that govern
user privacy. This document details our privacy policies with respect to users of the site. Users include, but
are not limited to visitors, Direct Customers and iReps.
TamPogo protects user privacy by not collecting or transmitting any personally identifiable information from
users Personal Computer. Users have the right to complete privacy. TamPogo follows all industry standards
to ensure user privacy is protected. This privacy policy explains our information collection practices on the
TamPogo website and related services.
Personally Identifiable Information
When individuals become a TamPogo iRep, they will be entitled to earn commission in accordance with our
Terms & Conditions and Compensation Plan. Direct Customers have the right to shop the TamPogo site at
will. TamPogo will collect and store the information users provide during the registration process, including
personally identifiable information such as name, street address and e-mail address. TamPogo uses this
information for the purposes of sending applicable commission payout and communicating with members.
TamPogo does not collect personally identifiable information in any other manner, only information provided
during the registration process.
TamPogo does not provide personally identifiable information to third parties for direct marketing purposes.
TamPogo may engage third party contractors who provide services to TamPogo and who may have access to
such information in connection with performing their services. None of these third parties have permission to
use or disclose such information. Such information may be made available to affiliates of TamPogo (i.e. any
parent or subsidiary entities, or its agents, such as independent auditors, consultants or attorneys) for internal
business purposes. Personal information may be disclosed if, in TamPogo’s reasonable judgment, such
disclosure is necessary to comply with law or government agency requests.
Please note that Personally Identifiable Information does not include information that is collected
anonymously (without identification of the individual user) or demographic information not connected to an
identified individual.
Changing Personal Information/ Discontinuing Service
Members may change their personal information by going to the Profile pull down after logging into the
TamPogo site. Members may chose to discontinue use of the TamPogo site by contacting the customer
service department.
Changes to Policy
TamPogo reserves the right to make periodic changes in this privacy policy. The effective date of the current
version of our policy is indicated at the top of this page. Please check back from time to time to ensure that
you are aware of any updates or changes in this policy. If prior versions of this policy are made available, they
will be located at the bottom of this page. Your continued use of the site and the services indicates acceptance
of this privacy policy. In the event that TamPogo is acquired by another company by merger or acquisition,
use of your information is subject to the privacy policy in effect at the time of the transaction until the
acquiring company changes the policy.
Opting out
Members may opt out of receiving unsolicited information or being contacted by TamPogo, Inc. by responding
to e-mails as instructed.
Outside Security Concerns
While TamPogo takes commercially reasonable measures to maintain a secure site, electronic communications
and databases are subject to errors, tampering and break-ins and it cannot be guaranteed or warranted that
such events will not take place and TamPogo will not be liable to visitors, Direct Customers or iReps for any
such occurrences.
