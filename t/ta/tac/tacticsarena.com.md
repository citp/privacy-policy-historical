# Tactics Arena Online - Privacy

**Privacy Policy**  
Please carefully read and understand the following agreement.

In order to join the Tactics Arena Online game server, users are required to provide a unique username and password. To participate in the forums, a unique username, password, and e-mail address are required as well as the agreement to conform to the rules of the forums. Forum members must be over the age of 13 to register. Neither the Administrators nor the Moderators participating in the forums and game server are responsible for the privacy practices of any user.

Remember that all information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to share any of your personal information. Any user who finds material posted by another user objectionable is encouraged to contact us via email.

We are authorized to remove or modify any data submitted by you to the game server and forums for any reason we feel constitutes a violation of our policies, whether stated, implied or not. This site may contain links to other web sites and files. We have no control over the content and can not ensure it will not be offensive or objectionable. We will, however, remove links to material that we feel is inappropriate as we become aware of them.

Cookies must be turned on in your browser to participate as a user in the game server and forums. Cookies are used here to hold your username and password and viewing options, allowing you to login.

You agree that you will not post any copyrighted material that is not owned by yourself or the owners of these forums. In your use of these forums, you agree that you will not post any information which is vulgar, harassing, hateful, threatening, invading of others privacy, sexually oriented, or violates any laws.

During registration of an account, a user is required to create a unique username and password. Since new accounts are free, it is the user's responsibility to remember their username and password.

Gold Account Membership. All monetary transfers are to be completed using the PAYPAL merchant account system. For more information about this please visit www.paypal.com.

Spam? You think you hate spam, well we hate it just as much if not more. We do not sell out email addresses to anyone. Not our advertisers, marketing companies, people with fetishes for email addresses. Not anyone. We will not give out your email address to anyone. Period.

Choice/Opt-out Users who no longer wish to receive our newsletter or promotional materials from TAO Gaming, Inc may opt-out of receiving these communications by changing your own personal settings, located in your Personal Profile Section.
