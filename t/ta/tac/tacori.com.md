# Tacori® Privacy Policy

### Introduction

The websites located at the following URL [www.tacori.com](http://www.tacori.com/) and [blog.tacori.com](http://blog.tacori.com/) collectively the "Website") are operated by Tacori Enterprises (hereafter referred to as "TACORI" or "we" or "us"). Unless otherwise stated, this TACORI DATA USE & SECURITY POLICY (“Privacy Policy”) covers information collected and or submitted by the user through the Website. By using or accessing the Website, users of the Website (“you” or “user” or “TACORI Consumer”) accept the following terms set forth in this Privacy Policy.

### Privacy Policy

To help us achieve our goal of providing the highest quality products and services, we use information from our interactions with you and other customers, as well as from other parties. Because we respect your privacy, we have implemented procedures to ensure that your personal information is handled in a safe, secure, and responsible manner.

We have posted this Privacy Policy in order to explain our information collection practices and the choices you have about the way information is collected and used.

As we continue to develop our Website and take advantage of advances in technology to improve the services we offer, this Privacy Policy likely will change. We therefore encourage you to refer to this Privacy Policy on an ongoing basis so that you understand our current privacy policy.

### Information We May Receive From Users

Generally, you may browse the Website without providing any personally identifiable information. However, we may ask you to provide personally identifiable information at various times and places on the Website. This may include the following:

  * **Information you choose to share** , such as photo(s), stories, names, favorite pieces of jewelry, item(s) purchased, location, name, age, gender, friends etc. We may also receive your name, email and zip code if you choose to sign up for our newsletter. If you choose to register your Tacori purchase through our authenticity registration, located at http://www.tacori.com/authenticity-registration, in addition to the above information we may receive your phone number, significant others name, birthday(s) and anniversary date.
  * **Information other users share about you** , such as photo(s), stories, names, favorite pieces of jewelry, location, name, age, gender, friends etc.
  * We may also receive **other types of information from your use of the Website** , such as the date and time you visit, the city and state you are located in, and log data and IP addresses.
  * **Third parties may provide information to TACORI** , such as our advertising partners and affiliates that assist us in understanding online preferences and help develop the functionality, appeal and usefulness of the Website.



In some cases, depending on the functionality of the Website page you are interested in, you may not have the ability to fully access the contents of the Website or participate in all of its features.

### How We Use the Information We Receive

How we use the information we receive will vary depending on the information and/or how the information is received.

### Public Information

Information that you choose to make public through the Website will be viewable by any and all users of the Website. By choosing to post publicly available material like images, stories or videos the user understands and agrees that this information can be:

  * Associated with the user, such as your name, image and/or likeness.
  * May be accessible through search engines and/or websites affiliated or unaffiliated with TACORI.
  * Will be accessible to any and all other users of the Website.



TACORI cannot limit the audience for any material that is posted by users for public viewing. As a general rule you should assume that information you provide to enter into a contest or game sponsored by TACORI on the Website will be made available for public viewing. Please note that other users may choose to publicly share materials about you that will be subject to the same terms above. Other users control how information is shared about you.

If you do not like any information a user has shared about you through the Website please tell them or report the post to TACORI via email at info@tacori.com and we will use our best efforts to remove the information.

### Non-Public Information

We use the information we receive through the functionality of the Website from use by you and other users. For example, we may use the information we receive about you:

  * To protect the safety and security of the Website.
  * To protect TACORI and other users’ rights or property.
  * To tailor our products to the user’s desire.
  * To measure and understand the effectiveness of our ads, including delivering relevant ads to our users.
  * To send you our email newsletter and other offerings that may arise from time to time.
  * To identify your product preferences, so we can notify you of new or additional products, services, and promotions that might be of interest to you.
  * To improve our merchandise selections, customer service, and overall shopping experience.



### Cookies

We may use tracking pixels/web beacons, cookies and or other technologies to receive and store certain types of information. This information includes Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser information, Internet Service Provider (ISP), operating system, date/time stamp and clickstream data. This information helps us customize your website experience and make our marketing messages more relevant. This includes our content presented on other websites. In order to provide the best customer experience possible, we also use this information for reporting and analysis purposes, such as how you are shopping our website, performance of our marketing efforts, and your response to those marketing efforts.

### Third Parties

TACORI does not share information we receive from our users unless we have permission from the user, given you notice, such as this Privacy Policy, or after personally identifiable information is removed.

A few examples of how TACORI may share information about you the user to third parties, includes:

  * Providing the information to service providers that help us bring you the products and services we offer. For example, we may work with outside companies to manage a database of customer information, assist us in distributing e-mails, assist us with direct marketing, provide fraud prevention, and other services designed to assist us in maximizing our business potential. We require that these outside companies agree to keep confidential all information we share with them and to use the information only to perform their obligations in the agreements.
  * We may transfer or share a copy of personal information about you in the event that TACORI goes through a business transition, such as a merger, being acquired by another company, or selling a portion of its assets. You will be notified via email or prominent notice on the Website prior to a change control of your personal information, if your personal information will be used contrary to this policy.
  * We may provide access to information when legally required to do so, to cooperate with police investigations or other legal proceedings, to protect against misuse or unauthorized use of our Website, to limit our legal liability and protect our rights or to protect the rights, property or safety of visitors of the Website or the public.



### Third Party Cookies

We may allow third-party companies to collect anonymous information when you visit the Website and to use that information to serve ads for our products or services when you visit the Website. These companies may use anonymous information (e.g., navigational, non-personally identifiable information, click stream information, browser type, time and date, subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over, etc.) during your visits to the Website in order to provide advertisements about our goods and services likely to be of interest to you. These parties may use a cookie or a third party web beacon, or other technologies, to collect this information.

**No Sharing with Third Party Vendors** : We do not share you information with any third parties who may send you email solicitations.

### Security

We are committed to handling your personal information with high standards of information security. We take appropriate physical, electronic and administrative steps to maintain the security and accuracy of personally identifiable information we collect, including limiting the number of people who have physical access to our database servers, as well as employing electronic security systems and password protections that guard against unauthorized access. Please note that e-mail is not encrypted and is not considered to be a secure means of transmitting confidential information.

### Minor's Privacy

Protecting minor's privacy is important to us. Our Website is a general audience site. We do not collect or maintain information from those we actually know are under 18, and no part of our Website is targeted to attract anyone under 18. We also do not send email correspondence to anyone who tells us that they are under the age of 18 without their parent's consent.

People under the age of 18 should always ask their parents or guardians for permission before providing any personal information to anyone online. We urge parents and guardians to participate in their children's online activities and use parental control or other web filtering technology to supervise children's access to the web.

### Email(s)

We only send our TACORI email newsletter and promotional emails to those who have specifically requested receiving same. If you have decided that you no longer wish to receive email from TACORI, you can click on the unsubscribe link and hit send at the bottom of any email communication sent by us. Please allow us 3 business days from when the request was received to complete the removal, as some of our promotions may already have been in process before you submitted your request.
