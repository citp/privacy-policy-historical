    1. Acceptance
    Thank you for visiting and using Taboola.Com Ltd. ("Taboola") website, any of this website variations or successors and any of Taboola features, services, content, products and solutions (collectively, "Website"). Any information personal or otherwise provided to Taboola, will be treated in accordance with the terms of the following Privacy Policy, subject to Taboola Terms & Conditions and the Website. If you do not agree with the Privacy Policy, please do not use the Website. By using the Website, you give your expressed consent to the Privacy Policy.


    2. General
    Your privacy is important to us. We gather certain types of information about our users and we would like to explain you about our information practices and the choices you can make in that respect.


    3. The Information We Collect

    Information You Provide     - When you visit or use the Website, you may provide certain personally identifiable information, (such as, your name, I.D. number., email address, postal, home or business address, telephone numbers, profession, credit card numbers and billing information).

    Your Communications     - When you send email or other communication to us, we may retain those communications, inter alia, to process or respond to your requests and to improve our services and the Website.

    Automatically Collected Information     - When you visit the Website we may automatically record certain information from your web browser. Our server logs may include information such as, your web request and its date and time, Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, browser language, web pages you visit before or after your visit to the Website, URL's, platform type, clicks number, domain names, landing pages, viewed pages and the order of those pages and time spent on pages.
    Cookies - When you visit or use the Website, we may send one or more cookies - a small text file containing a string of alphanumeric characters - to your computer that, inter alia, uniquely identifies the browser you employ when visiting the Website. We may use both session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie disappears after you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser and may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the Website. Persistent cookies can be removed. Please review your web browser "Help" file directions, in order to learn the proper way to modify your settings. You may also set your web browser to refuse cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

    Web Beacons     - We may use a software technology called "clear gifs" (aka Web Beacons), that are small pieces of code placed on a web page to monitor the behavior and collect data about the visitors viewing a web page. In addition, we may also use "clear gifs" in HTML-based emails sent to our users to track which emails are opened by recipients.


    4. How We Use the Information
    We shall use your information, to operate, maintain, enhance, understand research, analyze, customize, improve, personalize and provide, in connection with features, content, advertisements, information, services and the Website.
    We may disclose personally identifiable information: in response to legal process; in response to a law enforcement agency's request; where we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, mitigate or take actions in connection with illegal, abusive or immoral activities; or in connection with third party claims or proceedings.
    We may transfer personally identifiable information and/or non-personally identifiable information to our connected entities (such as parent corporations, subsidiaries or affiliates), partners, agents, services providers and contractors.


    5. Our Commitment to Children's Privacy
    Protecting the privacy of children is especially important. For that reason, we do not knowingly collect or maintain personally identifiable information or non-personally identifiable information on the Website from persons under 13 years of age, and no part of the Website is directed to persons under 13. If you are under 13 years of age, then please do not use or access this Website in any manner. If we learn that personally identifiable information of persons under 13 years of age has been collected on the Website, without verified parental consent, then we will take the appropriate steps to delete this information.
    If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child under the age of 13 has obtained a Website account, then you may alert us at E-mail: privacy@taboola.com and requests that we shall delete that child's personal information from our system.


    6. Our Commitment to Information Security
    We have put reasonable physical, technical, and managerial procedures to safeguard and to prevent unauthorized access and to maintain the information security. However, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of your information, which might be accessed, disclosed, modified, or destroyed, and it should be emphasized that you do so at your own risk.


    7. Third Parties
    We may allow third parties, such as ad servers or ad networks, to serve and/or receive content and/or advertisements to and/or from the Website. These third parties may use technology to send, directly to your browser, the content and/or advertisements and/or links that appear the Website. These third parties automatically receive your IP address when this happens. They may also use other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) to measure the effectiveness and accuracy of their content and/or advertisements and to personalize the content and/or advertisments targeted to you.
    We do not provide any personally identifiable information to these third parties, without your consent or except as part of a specific program or feature for which you have the ability to opt-in or opt-out. However, please note that if a third party, asks us to show content and/or advertisement to a certain audience segment and you respond to that, that third partyr may conclude that you fit the characteristics of the audience they are attempting to reach. This third party may also use information regarding your use of the Website, such as the number of times you viewed specific content and/or an advertisment, to determine which content and/or advertisements to deliver to you. Our Privacy Policy does not apply to these third parties, and we cannot control their activities.


    8. Opt-Out
    We enable you to remove, at any time, your personally identifiable information from the Website, by notifying us, in advance, of your intention to opt-out at E-mail: privacy@taboola.com.


    9. Assignment
    We may transfer and/or assign your information, without any notice, and without a need to receive your consent, to any third party as a result of change of control, consolidation, merger, acquisition or reorganization.


    10. Updates
    We may revise, from time to time, this Privacy Policy, without notifying you, and said revisions shall be reflected by the "last modified" date below. Your use of the Website constitutes your expressed consent to the Privacy Policy and its future updates.


    11. Effective Date
    This Privacy Policy is effective as of March 24, 2008 and was updated on or about March 24, 2008.


    12. Our Contact Information.
    For any questions or comments about our Private Policy please contact us at E-mail: privacy@taboola.com.
