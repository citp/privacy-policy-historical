Privacy Policy

We are committed to preserving the privacy of all visitors to our website. If you are interested to understand how we use and protect the information that you provide to us, please read on.
p. By registering or placing an order on this website, you consent to the collection, use and transfer of your information under the terms of this policy. p. Information that we collect from you p. When you visit, register or order products or services on this website you may be asked to provide certain information about yourself including your name, contact details and credit or debit card information. p. We may also collect information about your usage of our website as well as information about you from messages you post to the website and e-mails or letters you send to us. p. To maintain customer service standards and to assist staff training, we may record and monitor incoming calls. h3. Use of your information p. Your information will enable us to provide you with access to all parts of our website and to supply the goods or services you have requested. It will also enable us to bill you and to contact you where necessary concerning your orders. We will also use and analyze the information we collect so that we can administer, support, improve and develop our business. p. In particular, we may use your information to contact you for your views on our services and to notify you occasionally about important changes or developments to the website or our services. Further, _where you have consented_, we might also use your information to let you know by email or post about other products and services which we offer which may be of interest to you. If you change your mind about being contacted in the future, please "let us know(contact us)":/info/contact/ and we'll make sure you're not contacted for these purposes again. h3. Disclosure of your information p. The information you provide to us may be accessed by or given to third parties some of whom may be located outside the European Economic Area who act for us for the purposes set out in this policy or for other purposes approved by you. Those parties process information, fulfill and deliver orders, process credit card payments and provide support services on our behalf. We may also pass aggregate information on the usage of our website to third parties but this will not include information that can be used to identify you. p. _Where you have consented_ when providing us with your details, we may also allow other companies in the Informa group, to contact you occasionally about products and services which may be of interest to you. They may contact you by emails. If you change your mind about being contacted by these companies in the future, please "let us know(contact us)":/info/contact/ and we'll make sure you're not contacted for these purposes again. p. If our business enters into a joint venture with or is sold to or merged with another business entity, your information may be disclosed to our new business partners or owners. p. *Unless required to do so by law, we will not otherwise share, sell or distribute any of the information you provide to us (including your email and postal address) to companies or persons outside the Informa Group without your consent.* h3. Cookies p. Cookies are a technology which can be used to provide you with tailored information from a website. A cookie is an element of data that a Web site can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it. p. We use these cookies to enable you to access certain parts of this website, to complete certain transactions (e.g. ordering goods), and to allow you to customize some areas of the website. p. For example, when you log into the site to place an order, a cookie is stored on your computer to keep you logged in as you navigate from page to page. Security and Data Retention p. We employ security measures to protect your information from access by unauthorized persons and against unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction and damage. We will retain your information for a reasonable period or as long as the law requires. h3. Changes to our Privacy Policy p. Any changes to our privacy policy in the future will be posted to the website and, where appropriate, through emails notification.
