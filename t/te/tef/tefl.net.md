# TEFL.net Privacy Policy Statement

TEFL.net is firmly committed to privacy on the Internet. That means that we consider any information provided to us by you to be provided in confidence and we are committed to maintaining that confidence.

 **Personal Information**

We collect only information that is voluntarily provided by our visitors, and we use it only for the purpose for which it was provided. We securely log this information for a reasonable period of time and then discard it. We send our visitors occasional updates regarding TEFL.net, and visitors may opt-out of future mailings (see the amend/unscubscribe section below).

This information is not shared with or sold to any third party, and would only be made available to certain law enforcement agencies in the event of a criminal offence and a resulting court order requiring us to comply. This information is not collected for advertising purposes, and is never shared with advertisers.

**Amend/Unsubscribe**

If at any time you wish to amend details that you have previously provided, please [**let us know**](mailto:support@tefl.net). If you simply need to remove your email address from our mailing list, [**send this blank email to unsubscribe yourself immediately**](mailto:unsubscribe@tefl.net?subject=Unsubscribe).

**Questions Or Concerns**

If you have any questions about TEFL.net's privacy policy or practices, or about your dealings with this web site, please do not hesitate to [**contact us**](mailto:support@tefl.net).
