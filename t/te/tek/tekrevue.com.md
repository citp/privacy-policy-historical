# TekRevue Privacy Policy - TekRevue

TekRevue LLC recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of you as a user of this Website. This Website does not collect Personal Information about you except for information that you voluntarily provide (for example, when you communicate with us via email, Twitter, or Facebook and do not specifically indicate that the information contained within that communication is confidential). On certain occasions, TekRevue may use cookies to measure website traffic or overall readership activity while visiting the Website. No Personally Identifiable information is collected via these cookies.

Any Personal Information that is obtained by TekRevue is treated confidentially. We consider the following to be Personal Information: your name, address, phone number, date of birth, title and place of employment, personal interests, credit card information, your e-mail address and data specific to you about the pages you visited on this Website.

## Third Party Advertising

TekRevue may use third party companies to serve the advertising displayed when you visit the Website. These companies may collect and use information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. This is accomplished through the use of cookies stored and read via your browser.
