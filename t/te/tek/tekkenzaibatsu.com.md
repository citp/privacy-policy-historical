# Privacy Policy - Tekken Zaibatsu

This site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We prohibit forum registration by and will not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under 13. No information should be submitted to or posted by guests under 13 years of age, this includes submissions of publically viewed content such as public forum posts or game FAQs.

Tekken Zaibatsu does not provide any personally identifying information, regardless of its source, to any third party for any purpose whatsoever from our guests under 13 years of age. No information collected from guests under 13 years of age is used for any marketing or promotional purposes whatsoever.

The only individually identifiable information we currently collect is aggregate data ,submitted content or email addresses, which are volunteered by visitors wishing to participate in public forums, submit site content or sign up for free email services.

Tekken Zaibatsu does not sell, trade, lease or distribute any personal information of its registered forum users. However, any users who wish to participate in the public forums are required to provide a valid email address as a condition of participation, which will be publicly viewable along with any and all of their postings.

Tekken Zaibatsu does not consider your IP as private personal information and therefore does not warrant its protection in this regard. We only collect IP addresses to facilitate system administration, to report aggregate information to our advertisers, and to audit the use of our site. We do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable, which means that a user’s session will be logged, but the user remains anonymous to us. We can and will use IP addresses to identify a user when we feel it is necessary to enforce compliance with our forum rules or terms of use or to protect our services and site.

Visitors should be aware that when you are on Tekken Zaibatsu, you could be directed to other sites that are beyond our control. For example, if you click on a banner advertisement or click a link that takes you off our site. These other sites may send their own cookies to users, collect data or solicit personal information. Please keep in mind that whenever you give out personal information online, that information can be collected and used by people you don’t know. While Tekken Zaibatsu strives to protect its users’ personal information and privacy, we cannot guarantee the security of any information you disclose online, and you do so at your own risk.

Tekken Zaibatsu is not responsible for communication and information exchange offered by its free e-mail service through Everyone.Net. This responsibility lies on the user.
