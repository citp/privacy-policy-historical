"Cookies" are small text files a web site can use to recognise repeat users, facilitate the user's ongoing access to and use of the site, track usage, follow site behaviour and compile aggregate data that will allow content improvements and targeted advertising. Cookies are not programs that come onto a user's system and damage files. Generally, cookies work by assigning a unique number to the user that has no meaning outside the assigning site. Users should be aware that TEAMtalk cannot control the use of cookies or the resulting information by advertisers or third parties hosting data for TEAMtalk.
