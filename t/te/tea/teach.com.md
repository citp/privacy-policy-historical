# Welcome to Intellinex

Intellinex intends for Intellinex.com to complement the services that we provide. We believe that online privacy is important to the success of the Internet and electronic commerce. This statement sets forth the Intellinex privacy policy for Intellinex.com and describes the practices Intellinex follows to respect the privacy of all visitors to this site.

Intellinex has a strong commitment to the issues of trust and privacy on the World Wide Web. At Intellinex, we have focused on implementing fair information practices that are designed to protect your privacy. We believe that everyone benefits from the free flow of information when that information is gathered and used responsibly. We welcome visitors to our site, and we want you to be informed about or privacy practices. So before you explore any further, we want you to read this statement so that you know what our practices regarding the privacy of your information are. In addition, you should be aware that Intellinex.com contains links to other sites which are not governed by this privacy policy.

If you have questions or you do not feel that your concerns have been addressed in our privacy statement, or you just want to talk with us, feel free to contact us by email.

**Collection, Use, Disclosure**  
If you are a visitor to Intellinex.com, Intellinex does not collect any personal information about you. If you decide you would like more information about Intellinex, you will be asked to register on Intellinex.com. During registration, Intellinex.com collects basic online contact information about you, such as your name and email address. Please note that at this time, registration is not required for you to use our site.

We also request during registration other demographic information such as country, company name and role. Some of this information is optional, and the submission form identifies which fields are optional. We use demographic information in the aggregate only for statistical analysis and internal research purposes to help us understand who is using our site and become better equipped to serve your needs.

**Data Security/Integrity**  
Intellinex has technological and operational security functions in place to protect personally identifiable information from loss, misuse, alteration or destruction. Only authorized employees have access to the data you provided and that access is limited by need.

**Children**  
Intellinex understands the importance of protecting children's privacy in the interactive, online world. Intellinex.com does not intentionally target or solicit to children 16 and younger.

**Accountability**  
Intellinex will comply with applicable privacy regulations. Intellinex supports consumer, government and industry efforts to identify and resolve privacy issues.

**Consumer Recourse**  
If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy while using Intellinex.com, please contact the Intellinex webmaster.
