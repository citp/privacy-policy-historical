# Privacy Policy - Tealium

## Tealium Inc. Web Site Privacy Statement

Privacy Statement Effective as of April 30, 2014

Tealium, Inc. (“Tealium”) is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who visit Tealium’s web site (http://www.tealium.com) (the “Tealium Web Site”) and individuals who use the Services (as such term is defined below). This Tealium Web Site Privacy Statement describes Tealium’s privacy practices in relation to the use of the Tealium Web Site and the related Services offered by Tealium, which Services are accessible via the Tealium Web Site (the “Services”). The Tealium Web Site may contain links to other websites. The privacy statements of such other websites govern the content and use of information of those websites. Tealium encourages you to review the privacy statements of such other websites to understand their information practices. Please read the following carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal information and how we will treat it. Tealium will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your information is treated securely and in accordance with this Web Site Privacy Statement.

## 1\. Information We May Collect From You

Tealium collects individually identifiable information (“Personal Information”) from: (i) individuals who visit the Tealium Web Site (“Site Visitors”); and (ii) individuals who use the Services (“Customers”).

**Site Visitors**  
Tealium may collect information from your computer or mobile device as you navigate the Tealium Web Site through the use of commonly used web site information-gathering tools, such as cookies and web beacons (“Web Collection Information”). The Web Collection Information includes information from your web browser (such as browser type and browser language), your Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, and the actions you take on the Tealium Web Site (such as the web pages viewed and the links clicked). Read more detailed information about how we use web beacons and cookies and the purpose for which we use them.

**Customers**  
Tealium may ask you to provide Tealium with personal contact information, such as name, company name, address, phone number, and email address (“Contact Information”) if you request additional information about the Services or register to use the Services. If you purchase the Services, Tealium may require you to provide financial and billing information, such as billing name and address, company bank account number, credit card number, and the number of employees within the organization that will be using the Services (“Financial Information”). Tealium may also ask you to provide additional information, such as number of website visitors or number of employees (“Other Customer Information”). Contact Information, Billing Information, and Other Customer Information about Customers are referred to collectively as “Customer Information”.

**User Generated Content**  
Some areas of our website allow you to provide content to us directly, for example, where comments are left on a bulletin board, blog or chat room on our website (a “Public Forum”). In using any Public Forum on our website you give your consent that any personal data or information you submit about yourself or any third party may be read, collected, or otherwise used by anyone who reads that public forum. We will not be responsible for any personal data or information that is posted on a publicly viewable public forum. To request removal of any personal data or information from our blog or community forum, contact us at [privacy@tealium.com](mailto:privacy@tealium.com). In some cases, we may not be able to remove your personal data or information, in which case we will let you know if we are unable to do so and why.

## 2\. How We Use Collected Information

Tealium uses Customer Information to perform the Services requested. For example, if you fill out the web form on our Contact Us page, Tealium will use the information provided to contact you about your interest in the Services. Tealium may also use Customer Information for marketing purposes. For example, Tealium may use information you provide to contact you to discuss your interest in the Services and to send you information about Tealium, its partners and other information such as current promotions or events. You can read more detailed information about how to manage how we use Customer Information for marketing purposes here.

Tealium uses Billing Information solely to check your financial qualifications and collect payment for the Services.

Tealium uses Web Collection Information to operate and improve the Tealium Web Sites. Tealium may also use Web Collection Information alone or in combination with Customer Information to provide personalized information about Tealium and the Services. To read more about Tealium’s use of Web Collection Information, please click here.

Tealium may use Customer Information and Web Collection Information to enforce Tealium’s web site policies, or to protect Tealium’s intellectual property and other rights or the rights of others using the Tealium Web Sites or the Services.

## 3\. Web Collection Information

This section describes the types of Web Collection Information used on the Tealium Web Site and in connection with the Services, and how this information may be used.

**Cookies**  
Tealium uses cookies to make interactions with the Tealium Web Site easy and meaningful. When you visit the Tealium Web Site, Tealium’s servers send a cookie to your computer. Client-side tracking objects (commonly called cookies or JavaScript files) are small text files located in your browser directory that may be placed on your device. Each cookie will typically contain the name of the domain from which the cookie has come, the “lifetime” of the cookie, and a value (usually a unique number). For a more thorough explanation of what cookies are and how they operate, please visit [aboutcookies.org](http://www.aboutcookies.org/) or [allaboutcookies.org](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/). On their own, cookies do not personally identify you; they recognize your Web browser. Unless you choose to identify yourself to Tealium, either by responding to a promotional offer, opening an account, or filling out a web form (such as a “Contact Me”), you remain anonymous to Tealium. You have the ability to manage the use of cookies on your computer using controls in your browser. To learn more about how to manage cookies, visit [allaboutcookies.org](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/).

Tealium uses cookies that are session-based and persistent-based. Session cookies exist only during one session, i.e., only while you are reading or navigating the Tealium Web Sites. They disappear from your computer when you close your browser software or turn off your computer. Persistent cookies remain on your computer after you close your browser or turn off your computer. Using your browser, you may disable the use of cookies, delete cookies, or individually reject cookies at any time. You may also disable JavaScript at any time. However some features of our website may not function properly unless cookies and JavaScript are enabled and some areas of our website may not function at all. In addition, you may not be able to successfully use the Services.

**Web Beacons**  
Tealium, or third parties with whom we contract, may use web beacons alone or in conjunction with cookies to compile information about Customers and Site Visitors’ usage of the Tealium Web Site and interaction with emails from Tealium. Web beacons are transparent pixel images that are used in collecting information about website usage, e-mail response and tracking. For example, Tealium may place web beacons in marketing emails that notify Tealium when you click on a link in the email that directs you to the Tealium Web Site. Tealium uses Web beacons to operate and improve the Tealium Web Site and email communications.

**IP Addresses and URLs**  
Like many other web sites, we collect information about our Site Visitors’ utilization and navigation of the Tealium Web Site. This information helps us to design the Tealium Web Site to better suit our Site Visitors’ needs. We may also use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer the Tealium Web Site, analyze trends, track visitor movements, and gather broad demographic information that assists us in identifying visitor preferences. For example, Tealium uses IP addresses to monitor the regions from which Customers and Site Visitors navigate the Tealium Web Sites.

Tealium may collect aggregated data regarding use of the Services and the Tealium Web Site, including, without limitation, number of Site Visitors, frequency and patterns of use, tag usage, feedback request trends, etc. (“Aggregated Data”). Tealium uses Aggregated Data as a statistical measure and not in a manner that would identify you personally. Aggregated Data enables us to determine how often certain parts of the Tealium Web Site or the Services are used so that we can improve them. Tealium may make use of, or make such Aggregated Data available to, third parties, in any manner in our sole discretion.

**Social Media Features**  
The Tealium Web Sites may use social media features, such as the Facebook ‘like’ button (facebook.com) and Twitter (twitter.com) (“Social Media Features”). These features may collect your IP address and which page you are visiting on the Tealium Web Site, and may set a cookie to enable the feature to function properly. You may be given the option by such Social Media Features to post information about your activities on the Tealium Web Site to a profile page of yours that is provided by a third party Social Media network in order to share with others within your network. Social Media Features are either hosted by a third party or hosted directly on the Tealium Web Site. Your interactions with these features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing the relevant Social Media Features.

## 4\. Public Forums and Customer Testimonials

Tealium may provide public forums on the Tealium Web Sites. Any personal information you choose to submit in a public forum may be read, collected, or used by others who visit these public forums, and may be used to send you unsolicited messages. Tealium is not responsible for the personal information you choose to submit in these public forums. Customers and Site Visitors may elect to use Tealium’s referral program to inform friends about the Tealium Web Site and the Services. When using the referral program, Tealium may request the friend’s name and email address. Tealium will automatically send the friend a one-time email inviting him or her to visit the Tealium Web Site, or will share with them via email the information you have requested to be shared. Tealium does not store this information with other parties.

Tealium posts a list of Customers and testimonials on the Tealium Web Sites that contain information such as Customer names, photos and testimonials. Tealium obtains the consent of each Customer prior to posting any information on such a list or posting testimonials.

## 5\. How We Share Information Collected

**Service Providers**  
Tealium may share Customer Information with Tealium’s contracted service providers, such as payment processors, so that these service providers can provide services on our behalf. Tealium may also share Customer Information with Tealium’s service providers to ensure the quality of information provided. Unless described in this Privacy Statement, Tealium does not share, sell, rent, or trade any information with third parties for their promotional purposes.

**Tealium Affiliates**  
Tealium may share Customer Information with other members and affiliates of the Tealium corporate group in order to work with them, including affiliates of the Tealium corporate group. For example, Tealium may need to share Customer Information for customer relationship management purposes.

**Partners and Affiliated Businesses Not Controlled by Tealium**  
From time to time, Tealium may partner with other companies to jointly offer products or services. If you purchase or specifically express interest in a jointly offered product or service from Tealium, Tealium may share Customer Information collected in connection with your purchase or expression of interest with our partners. Tealium does not control our partners’ use of the Customer Information, and their use of the information will be in accordance with their own privacy policies. If you do not wish for your information to be shared in this manner, you may opt not to purchase or specifically express interest in a jointly offered product or service.

**Billing**  
Tealium may use a third-party service provider to manage credit card processing. This service provider is not permitted to store, retain, or use Financial Information except for the sole purpose of credit card processing on Tealium’s behalf.

**Compelled Disclosure**  
It may be necessary for Tealium to disclose your Personal Information, either by law, legal process, litigation, and/or requests from public and governmental authorities within or outside your country of residence. Tealium may also disclose information about you if we determine that for purposes of national security, law enforcement, or other issues of public importance, disclosure is necessary or appropriate. Tealium may also disclose Personal Information about you if we determine that disclosure is reasonably necessary to enforce our terms and conditions or protect our operations or users.

## 6\. Global Data Transfers, Storage and Processing

To facilitate Tealium’s global operations, Tealium may transfer and access Customer Information from around the world, including the United States. This Privacy Statement shall apply even if Tealium transfers Customer Information to other countries.

**Safe Harbor**  
Tealium complies with the U.S.-EU Safe Harbor Framework and the U.S.-Swiss Safe Harbor Framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of personal information from European Union member countries and Switzerland. Tealium has certified that it adheres to the Safe Harbor Privacy Principles of notice, choice, onward transfer, security, data integrity, access, and enforcement. To learn more about the Safe Harbor program, and to view Tealium’s certification, please visit <http://www.export.gov/safeharbor/>. If you have any questions or complaints regarding our compliance with the U.S.-EU Safe Harbor Framework or the U.S.-Swiss Safe Harbor Framework, please contact us at [privacy@tealium.com](mailto:privacy@tealium.com).

## 7\. Use of the Tealium Web Site by Children

Tealium does not sell products for purchase by children. If we learn that we have collected the Personal Information of a child under 13 without first receiving verifiable parental consent we will take steps to delete the information as soon as possible.

## 8\. Your Control and Choices

You may correct, update and delete your Customer Information, change your choices for alerts and other messages, choose whether to receive offers and promotions from us or you may request access to the Personal Information we hold about you and ask us to amend or delete such Personal Information. You can send your request to [support@tealium.com](mailto:support@tealium.com). Tealium offers Site Visitors and Customers who provide contact information a means to choose how Tealium uses the information provided. You may manage your receipt of marketing and non-transactional communications by [clicking here](http://offers.tealium.com/unsubscribe.html) or by clicking on the “unsubscribe” link located on the bottom of Tealium’s marketing emails. Additionally, you may send a request specifying your communications preferences to [support@tealium.com](mailto:support@tealium.com). Customers cannot opt out of receiving transactional emails related to their account with Tealium or the Services.

To update Billing Information or have your registration information deleted, please email [support@tealium.com](mailto:support@tealium.com). To discontinue your account, please email [support@tealium.com](mailto:support@tealium.com). Requests to access, change, or delete your information will be handled within 30 days.

## 9\. Data Retention

Tealium retains the information we receive as described in this Privacy Statement for as long as needed to provide our Services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, establish legal defenses, conduct audits, pursue legitimate business purposes, and enforce our agreements.

## 10\. Customer Data

As part of using our Services, Customers may submit electronic data or information to the Services (“Customer Data”) that constitutes Personal Information of other individuals to us. Such data may include an individual’s name, email address, or phone number. Tealium has no direct relationship with the individuals to whom Customer Data may pertain, which we process on the behalf of our Customers. Any uses of Customer Data by Tealium are done so pursuant to Tealium’s subscription agreement in place between Tealium and the relevant Customer, which agreement exclusively governs Tealium’s treatment of Customer Data and supersede this Privacy Statement in case of conflict, or as required by law. If Personal Information pertaining to you as an individual has been submitted to us by a Customer as Customer Data and you wish to exercise any rights you may have to access, correct, amend, or delete such data, please inquire with the Customer directly.

## 11\. Security

Tealium takes precautions, including administrative, technical, and physical measures – to help safeguard Customer Information against loss, theft, and misuse as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. While we follow generally accepted standards to protect information submitted to us, no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure. Users are solely responsible for protecting their passwords, limiting access to their computers, and signing out of the Services after their sessions.

## 12\. Changes to this Privacy Statement

From time to time, we may change this Privacy Statement to accommodate new technologies, industry practices, regulatory requirements or for other purposes. When we change the privacy statement in a material way, a notice will be posted on the Tealium Web Site along with the updated privacy statement.
