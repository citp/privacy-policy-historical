# Privacy Policy - Manage your sports team schedule for free with Team Cowboy - Organize your sports team

Team Cowboy is committed to maintaining the privacy and security of the personal information of all visitors to this site to the fullest extent permitted by law. 

When you submit information or content on the Team Cowboy Web site or related services, interfaces, or applications, you are voluntarily releasing that information to us. Team Cowboy will not distribute your personally-identifiable information to third parties without prior consent from you, the user of this Web site. Non-personally-identifiable information (such as general demographic information or regional geographic information) may be provided to formal partners of Team Cowboy. Ownership and copyright of this information or content remains with you (or the original owner/author); ownership and copyright are not transferred to Team Cowboy in any way. Team Cowboy will not use information or content you provide for advertising or marketing purposes without your prior consent. 

When you visit the Team Cowboy Web site, some basic information may be passively collected (automatically sent to us by your Web browser). This information will be used solely to help us make this site more useful to visitors, and to learn about the number of visitors to our site and the types of technology they use. All of this helps us ensure that this site is as useful as possible. This basic information that may be collected is as follows: 

  * The Internet domain and IP address from which you accessed our site; 
  * The type of browser and operating system used to access our site; 
  * The date and time you accessed our site; 
  * The specific pages you visited; and 
  * If you linked to the Team Cowboy site from another Web site, the address and other information for that Web site. 



Different data collected on the Team Cowboy web site is retained by Team Cowboy for different periods of time. If you have specific questions about data retention, please contact us.
