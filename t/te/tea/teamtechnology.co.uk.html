This is the privacy policy that is in operation at the MetaRasa.com website and all other sites operated by Team Technology.  Please read this privacy policy carefully.  Team Technology (“we” or “us”) takes your privacy seriously.  This privacy policy sets out how we use your information.  If you have any questions about this privacy policy please contact us via the administration contact page.  By using the website and giving your information to us you are deemed to have accepted the terms of this privacy policy.

Information we Collect About You

The website automatically collects certain information about you including:

the site you visited previously;
the site you visit next;
the browser you are using;
any searches you have done; and
your IP address.

This information does not identify you.  We use it for internal purposes to monitor how the website is being used.

You can use the website to contact us.  We may use your contact details to send you information about the website and our services.  Please tell us if you do not want us to contact you any other way.  We will not send you information by e-mail unless you tell us to.

Members may include information on their public profile or on articles they post.  If you are a member you acknowledge that this information will be available to public view.

You may complete an online personality questionnaire on the website.  The results may be posted by us on public areas of the website but will remain anonymous.

Who we Share Your Information With

We do not share your information with anyone except:

we may pass your information to the police or another similar body if it is requested
if we sell Team Technology or merge with another business we may pass your information to the prospective purchaser or the other business.

Security

We do our best to keep your information secure.  The website has security features such as backups and firewalls etc..

We may use servers outside the European Economic Area to store your information.  By using the website you acknowledge and agree that your information may be transferred outside The European Economic Area.

Updating

Please make sure you keep your information up to date.  You may update your information at any time.  We may contact you to check whether the information we have is up to date.  We need to do this to comply with our legal obligations.

Links to Other Sites

There may be links to other website on the website.  These sites may have their own privacy policies.  We recommend that you view these policies.  We are not liable for any use made of your information by such websites.

Cookies

Cookies are pieces of software or information that a website transfers to your computer in order to record your visits.  The website sometimes uses cookies.  You may set your internet browser to reject cookies.  However, if you do so you may not be able to use all of the functions of the website.  For more information please visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org.

Google, as a third party vendor, also uses cookies so they can serve appropriate ads to you based on your visits to this site and other sites on the Internet.  You may opt out of Google’s cookie system at their website, for example see: http://www.google.co.uk/privacy_cookies.html

General

English Law applies to this privacy policy.  Any dispute will be heard by the English courts. You are deemed to have accepted this when you use the website.

This privacy policy applies to all websites that are operated by Team Technology, including (but not only) teamtechnology.co.uk, metarasa.com, metarasa.biz, metarasa.net.

For enquiries relating to this website, please contact MetaRasa Admin.  Members can also post questions in the private forums (available via your control panel).
