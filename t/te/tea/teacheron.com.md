# Privacy Policy | TeacherOn

The privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. 

We use cookies to maintain sessions throughout the website. This helps us to serve you relevant content.

We also store information about your IP to detect and prevent spammers and help keep your account secure. 

We do not sell or share your personal information with anybody. The only time such information is shared is when you agree to share details with student/tutor.

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to [contact us](/contact).
