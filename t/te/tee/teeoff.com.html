This Privacy Policy explains our information collection practices, such as the types of information we collect and how we may use that information. Please review this Privacy Policy carefully. By visiting and/or using the EZLink Properties, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

In connection with serving content and providing analytics and advertising services, third parties may collect certain information about your visits to this or other websites and we may receive information regarding the use of the EZLinks Properties and the effectiveness of our advertisements. These advertising networks, advertisers, ad agencies, analytics service providers and other vendors may set and access their own tracking devices (including cookies and web beacons) and they may otherwise collect or have access to information about you and your online browsing activities.
 
Lastly, our services are general audience services and are not intended for individuals younger than the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect Personal Information from individuals younger than the age of 16. If EZLinks discovers that it has inadvertently collected Personal Information (as defined below) from anyone younger than the age of 16, it will delete that information. In addition, you must be at least 18 years of age to make a purchase through the EZLinks Properties.
 
INFORMATION COLLECTION
1. Registration and Other Information You Provide
The EZLinks Properties may collect "Personal Information" (information that identifies you as a specific individual, including without limitation, your full name or email address), account information (password or other information that helps us confirm that it is you accessing your account) and demographic or other information (such as your zip code and information about your interests and preferences). We will also collect certain payment information if you choose to purchase products or services through the EZLinks Properties. We may also collect information, including Personal Information, about other members of your golfing party which you may submit to us when you use the EZLinks Properties. If we combine demographic or similar information with the Personal Information we collect, we will treat it as Personal Information under this Privacy Policy.
 
2. Information Third Parties Provide About You
We may receive information about you from third parties. For example, someone may provide information about you in order to reserve a golf tee time on your behalf or to send you information or a gift card through the EZLinks Properties. We may combine this information in our database and we may use it to contact you about a reserved tee time or other product purchased for you, or to offer you products or services, or for similar purposes. Third parties also may forward your information to us; for example, if you are using a service that uses the EZLinks Properties or an EZLinks application program interface (API) to present available golf tee times. If we combine the information we receive from others with information we collect through the EZLinks Properties, we will apply this Privacy Policy to the combined information.
 
3. Social Media/Integration with Third Party Properties
The EZLinks Properties may permit you to interact with a third party site or service. If you choose to interact with another site via any of the EZLInks Properties (e.g., sharing content or otherwise posting information from or via the EZLinks Properties to a third party site or service), that information may be publicly displayed, and the third party may have access to certain information about you and your use of the EZLinks Properties. Similarly, if you interact with us through a third party site, we may have access to information about you (for example, if you "like" or "follow" our page on a third party site).
 
When you choose to interact with a third party site or service, that third party may collect information about your visits to the EZLinks Properties and may publish that activity to your profile or similar feature on that third party site (such as if you choose to share one of your golf course reviews from the EZLinks Properties with your connections on the third party site). The information collected and stored by the third party remains subject to the third party's privacy practices, including whether the third party continues to share information with us, the types of information shared, and your choices with regard to what is visible to others on that third party site.
 
4. Public Forums and User Content
The EZLinks Properties may provide features through which you can publicly post content (comments, golf scores, messages, images, videos, text or other material). Note that anything you post publicly may be viewed by others. If you choose to voluntarily disclose Personal Information or other information publicly, that information will be considered public information and the protections of this Privacy Policy will not apply.
 
5. Information We Collect Automatically
EZLinks, and our third party service providers (including analytics providers and third party content providers), may automatically collect certain "Usage Information" whenever you visit or interact with the EZLinks Properties. "Usage Information" typically includes the browser and operating system you may be using, the URL or advertisement that referred you to the EZLinks Properties (if applicable), the search terms you entered into a search engine that lead you to the EZLinks Properties (if applicable), all of the areas within the EZLinks Properties that you visit, and the time of day you used the EZLinks Properties, among other information relating to your use of the EZLinks Properties. We may use Usage Information for a variety of purposes, including analyzing and improving the EZLinks Properties and products, to provide special offers, and to select appropriate content to display to you.
 
In addition, we automatically collect your IP address or other unique identifier ("Device Identifier") for any computer, mobile phone or tablet, or other device you may use to access the EZLinks Properties. A Device Identifier is a number that is automatically assigned or connected to the device you use to access a Service, and our servers identify your device by its Device Identifier. We may collect information about your mobile device, including, for example, the hardware model, operating system and version, software, preferred language, advertising identifiers, serial number, device motion information, and mobile network information.  Some mobile service providers may also provide us or our third party service providers with information regarding the physical location of the device used to access a Service (i.e., GPS information). Usage Information is generally non-identifying, but if we associate it with you as a specific and identifiable person, we treat it as Personal Information.
