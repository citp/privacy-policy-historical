Privacy Policy

You may access most of the Tegrity website without giving any personal information about yourself. In addition, Tegrity collects information from website visitors such as what Tegrity web pages you access, how often you access them, your domain name and from which website you entered the Tegrity website.

Some pages on the Tegrity site use "cookies", or small files that the site places on your hard drive, for identification purposes. Your web browser may be programmed not to accept cookies or it may allow you to be notified when you are receiving a cookie, thus giving you the option of accepting it or rejecting it.

Tegrity may use the information that it gathers to contact you or your company for marketing purposes, or may provide the information to Tegrity's business partners for marketing purposes. Tegrity may also use the information to improve its web-based offerings and to customize the website to provide content most relevant to you.

The personally identifiable information that we collect about you is stored in limited access servers. We will use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain safeguards to protect the security of those servers and your personal information.
