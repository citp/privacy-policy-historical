Android Privacy

Privacy Policy for Android Apps

Our apps do not disclose personal data like keystrokes, telephone-call logs, SMS logs, contacts, pictures, credit card numbers, credentials, etc. to TEC-IT or to anyone else. TEC-IT's apps are designed to respect the privacy of the user.

Points of Special Interest:

  • Key Logging

    Some of our apps (especially Barcode/NFC Scanner Keyboard, GetBlue and BluePiano) are intended (or can be configured to operate) as an alternative input method. An alternative input method replaces the standard soft-keyboard with another one and thus has the theoretical possibility to log all keystrokes. This is the reason why the Android system displays a warning when such an alternative input method is enabled by the user. This warning does not automatically imply that the app in question will log the keystrokes or exposes you to any risks and vulnerabilities.

    In short: TEC-IT apps do not log, store or disclose keystrokes.

  • Credentials

    Some TEC-IT apps can be configured to access online services (like Google Docs) to store or retrieve information. In order to access such online services the user needs to enter his credentials. The credentials are stored on the device and are used by the app exclusively for the purpose of accessing the specified online service.

    In short: TEC-IT apps do not disclose credentials to unauthorized parties.

    Important: If your device gets lost or stolen you should be aware that this exposes you to risks and vulnerabilities. In such a case, we recommend you to change your credentials for the involved online services as soon as possible.

  • Device Information

    For volume or bulk-licensing purposes, special versions of our apps can be licensed via TEC-IT's activation server. For each single activation, this activation server records date/time, IP address and the Android device ID of the device in question. This information is treated confidential and kept private by TEC-IT.

    In short: TEC-IT does not record personal or user information.
