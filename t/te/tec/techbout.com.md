# Privacy Policy - Techbout

Visitors who contact us, comment on posts or sign up to be notified about new posts are required to provide username and email address. In such cases the information collected will be used to fulfill the purpose of visitors interaction and to provide useful information relevant to the users interest. Under no circumstances will this information be sold to a third party, unless required by law or in case the ownership of this website is transferred to its new owner.

Non-personally-identifying information of the kind that web browsers and servers make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and date and time of each visit is generally available to us and used for the purpose of analytics and to provide better service to our users.

### External Links and Ads

Techbout.com links out to external websites as way of directing users to further information or to direct them to sellers websites in case of product reviews. Users may also land on external websites when they click on Google AdSense or other Ads appearing on this site.

These external websites may use cookies to track visitor behaviour, measure effectiveness of their advertising or other reasons. It is recommended that you consult the privacy policies of these external websites for more information about their privacy policies and practices.
