# Privacy Policy - Techbout

## Use of Cookies

In accordance with the Adsense Terms of Service, we are required to let you know that all advertising programs use cookies.

This is true for practically every website on the internet. According to Google, Cookies are used to make advertising more effective and relevant to the user. For more information, you can visit this [page](https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/technologies/ads/) to learn about how Google uses Cookies to serve you relevant ads.

If you are unhappy with this, you can [disable cookies](http://www.techbout.com/enable-disable-cookies-on-chrome-6773/) in your browser. However, you may not be able to fully access most websites on the internet with cookies disabled in your browser.

### Email Privacy

Visitors who contact us, comment on posts or sign up to be notified about new posts are required to provide username and email address. 

In such cases the information collected will be used to fulfill the purpose of visitors interaction and to provide useful information relevant to the users interest. Under no circumstances will this information be sold or divulged to a third party.

Non-personally-identifying information of the kind that web browsers and servers make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and date and time of each visit is generally available to us and used for the purpose of analytics and to provide better service to our users.

### External Links and Ads

This website uses advertising to sustain itself and links out to external websites as way of directing users to further information or to products and services that might be of interest to users.

Users may land on external websites when they click on Google AdSense or other Ads appearing on this site.

These external websites may use cookies to track visitor behaviour, measure effectiveness of their advertising or other reasons. It is recommended that you consult the privacy policies of these external websites for more information about their privacy policies and practices.
