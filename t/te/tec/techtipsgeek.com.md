# Privacy policy

**Cookies:** Whenever you visit our website or Comment on it you may be asked for your name, email address or Blog, Website address. Generally a website uses cookies to remember these information for visitor’s convenience  and it is purely optional. When you will visit this site again these cookies will ease your work and will save your time from typing those fields again.

**Comment:** All the email addresses submitted by you when commenting or contacting us through the contact page, will be used only for replying your comment or email. We assure you that we will not publicize you email address or private information elsewhere.

**Visitor Statistics:** We use Google Analytics to analyze our Blog visitor statistics which helps us in monitoring the number of visits and other Metrics so that we can improve our Blog’s content. We will not reveal any kind of information related to those statistics that may hurt you.

**Ad servers:** We use Google Adsense as a third party ad Vendor.Google serves ads to our visitors based on their visits to our site and the other sites on the Internet, using DART cookies. You may opt out of the use of DART cookies by visiting the [Google ad and content network **privacy** **policy**](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).

We also have relationships With other Companies or Websites that allows us placing Advertisements on our Web pages. As a Result of your Visit to our site these ad servers may collect information. Please note that these companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

If you think that we are deviating from our Information **Policy** , you may Contact Us. Also if you want to use our content to your website or Blog then read the instructions.
