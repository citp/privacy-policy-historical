# Privacy Policy | Latest Technology News and Updates, Mobile, Gadgets, Social Media, Software & Utilities | Techowiz

Techowiz.com utilizes various Internet tracking tools to keep track of website visitors in order to serve them better. Techowiz.com is currently using Google Analytics to track visitor information, which includes IP address, Operating System and Web browser etc. The purpose of obtaining such information is to enhance visitors web experience while they are on our site. We do not share this information with any third party.

We also collect your email address when you willingly provide us to subscribe to our RSS feeds.
