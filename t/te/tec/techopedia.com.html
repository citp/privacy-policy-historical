Cookie Disclosure Statement
"Cookies" are files written to the hard drive of your computer by your browser so that we may track activities on the web site. FreeTechMail.org uses cookies to help track activity on the site so that we can make the web site more interesting and useful to visitors. Cookies allow us to understand who has seen which pages and ads, to determine how often particular pages are visited, and to track the most popular areas of the site. Cookies also allow us to make our site more convenient to use by allowing us to save your password so that you do not have to reenter it each time you visit FreeTechMail or ensure that you do not receive duplicate information.

A cookie will NOT permit a web site operator to learn any personally identifying information about you, such as your real name or address, unless you have previously provided that information to the web site operator or you have set the preferences in your browser to provide this information. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the opportunity to decide whether to accept it. Our primary use of cookies is to facilitate the login process for our users and to track the login history. We may use cookies to correlate usage of our web site with our users' personal information. Information collected through cookies is for the exclusive use of FreeTechMail.org and is used for statistical purposes.

Subscription/Unsubscription Process
We require user contact information when subscribing to any of our newsletters or any of our Partner Publisher newsletters on our web site. All subscriptions use the opt-in method of subscribing. If a user wishes to subscribe to any newsletter(s), we ask for personal and demographic information such as name and e-mail address. If the user indicates they are under 13 then their personally identifiable information will automatically be removed from the site’s database and will not be retained by the site.

You can unsubscribe from our newsletters and for some of our Partner newsletters using the same interactive process as subscribing, however not all our Partner newsletters have this option. To unsubscribe from those that do not, please follow the instructions provided by each Publisher within the body of their newsletter. There can be a delay of up to 1 week before all subscribe or unsubscribe requests are completed.

Through our web site, users may also subscribe to promotional newsletters from PostMasterDirect. If users volunteer to subscribe to a PostMaster Direct newsletter through our web site, please be aware that the information provided during the subscription process is shared between us and PostMasterDirect for the sole purpose of providing promotional newsletters. When users sign up for promotional email from Postmaster Direct through our web site, they have the ability to opt out of receiving third-party promotional email prior to receiving such mail from Postmaster Direct.
