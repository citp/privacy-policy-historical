    Our Commitment To Privacy

    Technology Advice values the privacy of its users and customers. Technology Advice has established this Privacy Policy to explain how Technology Advice handles information collected from our website and our customers.

    Information Collected

    Technology Advice may collect the following information from users of our website:

    Referral data – such as websites users have visited before visiting our website;
    Pages viewed by users of our website;
    Time spent on our website by users;
    Users’ IP addresses; and
    Cookies – small packets of information that our website may store on your users’ computers.

    In the event that a user completes a Contact Us Form or a Submit a Free Consultation Form, Technology Advice may collect a:

    User’s name;
    User’s address;
    User’s email address; and
    User’s phone number.

    In the event that a user becomes a customer of Technology Advice, Technology Advice may collect the following information from a customer:

    Dollar volume of transaction;
    Customer’s credit card number; and
    Customer’s billing address

    Use of Information

    Protecting our users’ and customers’ information is important to Technology Advice. Therefore, Technology Advice shares and discloses information only as set forth below:

    With third party credit card processors for billing purposes;
    As required by law;
    As necessary to enforce the terms of any agreements; and
    With other divisions, subsidiaries, and affiliates of Technology Advice.

    Technology Advice does not sell, distribute, or rent users’ and customers’ private information for financial gain.

    Business Transfers

    As Technology Advice continues to develop its business, it might sell or buy subsidiaries, or business units. In such transactions, customer information generally is one of the transferred business assets but remains subject to the promises made in any pre-existing Privacy Policy (unless, of course, the customer consents otherwise). Also, in the unlikely event that Technology Advice or substantially all of their assets are acquired; user and customer information will of course be one of the transferred assets.

    Our Commitment to Data Security

    To prevent unauthorized access, to maintain data accuracy and to ensure the correct use of information, Technology Advice has in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information it collects. Technology Advice restricts access to information about users and customers to those employees who need to know that information.

    With Your Consent

    Other than as set out above, you will receive notice when information about you might go to third parties, and you will have an opportunity to choose not to share the information.

    How to Contact Us

    Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please contact Technology Advice hello@technologyadvice.com.
