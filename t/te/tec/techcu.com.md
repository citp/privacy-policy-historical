# Privacy of Member and Former Member Information - Tech CU

Financial companies choose how they share your personal information. Federal law gives consumers the right to limit some, but not all, sharing. Please read this notice carefully to understand what we do.

### What We Collect and Share

The type of personal information we collect and share depends on the product or service you have with us. This information may include:

  * Your social security number and income 
  * Account balances and payment history
  * Credit history and credit scores



### How We Collect and Share

All financial companies need to share customers’ personal information to run their everyday business. In the table below, we list the reasons financial companies can share their customers' personal information, what Tech CU chooses to share, and whether you can limit this sharing.

If you are the parent or guardian of a child under the age of 13 and that child has an account at Tech CU, you may have different rights with respect to the collection and sharing of certain information that your child may have provided online or through our mobile application. Click [here](https://web.archive.org/Resources/Privacy-and-Security/Childrens-Online-Privacy-Policy/ "here") for more information.

### Other Important Information

California law allows you to opt out of information sharing with joint marketing partners. You may opt out by using one of the methods described in this notice. You will also receive, as required by California law, a separate "Important Privacy Choices for Consumers" notice and election form, and can opt out of information-sharing with joint marketing partners using that form.

### IMPORTANT PRIVACY CHOICES FOR CONSUMERS

You have the right to control whether we share some of your personal information. Please read the following information carefully before you make your choices below.

#### Your Rights

You have the following rights to restrict the sharing of personal and financial information with outside companies that we do business with. Nothing in this form prohibits the sharing of information necessary for us to follow the law, as permitted by law, or to give you the best service on your accounts with us. This includes sending you information about some other products or services.

#### Your Choices

Restrict information sharing with other companies we do business with to provide financial products and services. Unless you say "No," we may share personal and financial information about you with outside companies we contract with to provide financial products and services to you (called "joint marketing partners"). 

#### Time Sensitive Reply

You may make your privacy choice(s) at any time. Your choice(s) marked here will remain, unless you state otherwise. However, if we do not hear from you, we may share some of your information with other companies with whom we have contracts to provide products and services (joint marketing partners). If we receive contradictory instructions, for example on the federal opt-out form, or for the same account number from a joint account holder, we will implement the instructions most protective of your privacy.

#### Exercise Your Choice

To exercise your choice regarding the collecting and sharing of your information, do one of the following:

  * [Click here](https://web.archive.org/WorkArea/DownloadAsset.aspx?id=19327353123 "Click  here") to download, fill out, sign and send back to us the appropriate form. You may also want to keep a copy for your records.
  * Call us at (800) 553-0880.
  * Reply electronically by completing our online form in Online Banking.
