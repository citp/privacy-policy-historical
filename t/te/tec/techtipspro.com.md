# Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is applicable to any personal information which is given by you to TellyOne via the TellyOne website located at www.techtipspro.com.

When using the TellyOne website you will encounter areas such as polls, newsletter sign-ups, chatbox or comment areas where you may be asked to enter user information.

In entering user information on the TellyOne  website you confirm that you understand and agree that the information you provide will be held on a database to allow TellyOne to contact you to keep you informed about the latest updates, articles, breaking news or any other information TellyOne wishes to communicate with you, using e-mail.

You also agree to allow TellyOne to pass your details onto selected third parties for their marketing purposes.

In all correspondence between you, the user, and TellyOne, you will have the opportunity to indicate whether you would like to "opt out" or “unsubscribe” from receiving promotional and/or marketing information about other services from TellyOne.

TellyOne reserves the right, at any time, to modify, alter, or update this privacy policy.
