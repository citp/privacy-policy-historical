# Technology News - Latest TechNews from around the world :: TechnewsNow.com

### Privacy Statement

TechNewsNow.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.   
The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this website and/or other sites that are connected with this site and owned by TechNewsNow.com We use outside ad companies to display ads on our site. These companies may collect your IP address and access time, for analyze purpose, to improve their services. This site contains links to other sites. TechNewsNow.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

We don't sell, transfer any personal or business data we collected. We do our best to keep our site child-safe. But since all data are inputed by users or automated, sometime we don't have enough time/control to monitor all the data collected. So we strongly suggest parents' supervise when a child visit our site.  
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact: admin [a t] TechNewsNow.com 

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
