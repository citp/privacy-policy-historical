    Privacy Policy


    We respect your privacy. We do not share or sell your information to any third parties.

Our website is protected with Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) Encryption to insure that none of your personal information is compromised.

    The registration information provides us with your name, your mailing address and your phone number which we use for customer service purpose only. Creating an account also allows for an easier shopping experience by letting you order much more quickly and efficiently without entering your personal information again. Your email may be used for our promotions and announcements. You may opt-out by calling us.

Your credit card information does not go anywhere besides our credit processor, and your financial institution. If we have difficulty processing the order, your contact information is used to get in touch with you.

We keep the aggregate visitor / usage information without any kind of personal data for statistics and traffic analyzation. This allows us to improve our site and tailor it to your needs.

    This website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for content and privacy practices of those sites, and cannot guarantee anything. We suggest you be aware of leaving our site and review the privacy policy of other sites.

    Security

    All of the transaction and personal information is encrypted with 128-bit Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption which safeguards the information sent to and from us. Please note the small lock icon on the bottom-right of the browser window when you’re placing an order. That icon shows that your browser is in a secure mode with our site. We do not keep your credit card information nor do the credit card processors. Our computers are protected against hackers, and our employees abide by the highest privacy standards. If you have any questions about security of our site, please contact us.

    Special Offers

    Our established members may receive special offers and discounts. If you wish to opt out from these mailings, please contact us.

    We strive to provide you with the best shopping experience possible. If you have any questions, feel that our privacy policy is lacking, or would like to remove your account from our database, please give us a call.
