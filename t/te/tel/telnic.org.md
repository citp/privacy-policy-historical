# Telnic - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Telnic understand that privacy is an important issue for visitors to this site and we respect normal protocol and practices and we make our best efforts to protect confidentiality.

As is common, Telnic may collect normal information at several different points on our website. Telnic is the sole owner of any information collected on this site. We do not sell, share, or rent this information.

Telnic may, from time to time, make changes to this policy.
