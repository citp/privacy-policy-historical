Privacy Policy

The Texas Association of REALTORS® collects the following types of information from users of its website located at www.TexasRealEstate.com in order to provide users with an experience that is tailored to their professional real estate needs:

  1. You may provide us with contact information in addition to the information contained in the National Association of REALTORS® NRDS database.
  2. You may provide us with information regarding any firm affiliations, professional titles, and professional designations.
  3. Our web server may gather information based on your usage of www.TexasRealEstate.com and the pages you visit (this includes information you may communicate directly to us via the website).
  4. From time to time, we may conduct surveys and you may complete applications from which we obtain information about you.

The information collected by the Texas Association of REALTORS® over www.TexasRealEstate.com is used in the following ways:

  1. To customize individual user experiences while using the website and to tailor information delivered to the user via the website and contact information provided by the user.
  2. To notify you of updates to our site, relevant products and services, and upcoming events and programs.
  3. Information provided by users will be used to populate consumer "REALTOR® searches" on the Texas Association of REALTORS® consumer site located at www.TexasRealEstate.com.
  4. To track usage of the site.
  5. To inform you of current issues affecting the real estate industry.

Information provided by and used by us as mentioned above may be shared with local associations for their similar use.

Except as otherwise stated in this privacy policy, the Texas Association of REALTORS® will not sell or otherwise provide your contact information except:

  1. To major sponsors at the Texas Association of REALTORS® Convention or Winter Meeting may be provided with contact information for attendees of the meeting for which they are a major sponsor or
  2. When required by law or valid legal process, or to protect the personal safety of our members or the public.

Information you provide over this site related to credit card payments or authorizations will be maintained and shared in accordance with the Texas Association of REALTORS® Privacy Policy which may be accessed here.

You may opt-out of receiving information through this website at any time by contacting the Information Technology staff at the Texas Association of REALTORS® at 800/873-9155 or P.O. Box 2246, Austin, TX 78768.

Please note that our site contains links that may take you to sites operated by a third party other than the Texas Association of REALTORS®. If you click on one of these links, you understand that you are no longer on the Texas Association of REALTORS® site located at www.TexasRealEstate.com and this policy no longer applies. The Texas Association of REALTORS® has no control over third-party sites or their operation.
