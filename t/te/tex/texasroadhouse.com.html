About Texas Roadhouse and this Privacy Policy

Texas Roadhouse is an award-winning, full-service restaurant chain featuring hearty, hand-cut steaks, fall-off-the-bone ribs and hot, fresh-baked bread. Our motto is Legendary Food, Legendary Service®. We hope that you enjoy our food and our Website and come back to visit us again soon.

At Texas Roadhouse, we take the privacy and security of your information, particularly any Personal Information (defined below), extremely seriously. We want you to know that we take reasonable precautions to protect the security and privacy of your information. We also want you to know what information we collect, and why and how we will use that information. Finally, we want you to be able to have control over your information so that if you no longer wish to provide us information or want to opt-out of receiving certain communications, you can do that.

This Privacy Policy is for you, and it governs your use of any Website, web page or mobile application operated by or on behalf of Texas Roadhouse (collectively, the “Website”), and applies to Personal Information provided to us. Please read it carefully. By accessing or using our Website and/or providing any Personal Information to us, you agree to the terms and conditions set forth in this Privacy Policy.

  • Personal Information We May Collect About You

    Personal Information

    We collect certain information that does or could identify you personally, such as your name, address, ZIP code, phone number, mobile phone number, email, credit card information, date of birth and other personally identifiable information that you choose to provide us with (collectively, “Personal Information”). If you sign up to receive mobile or email messages from us, we will collect and retain, in accordance with this policy, the information you submit.

    You may be asked to provide certain Personal Information when you use or access certain features on the Website, including:

    • Completing a “Contact Us” web-form;
    • Opting-in to receive emails via enrolling in the VIP club;
    • Providing mobile marketing messages when requested;
    • Purchasing gift cards and other merchandise;
    • Applying for employment;
    • Participating in sweepstakes, contests or other promotions that allow for entry and/or participation via the Website; and
    • Using the Website in other specified manners.

    The decision to provide any Personal Information is voluntary and completely optional; however, if you choose not to provide the requested information, you may not be able to use some or all of the features of our Website. We also may receive Personal Information through the use of various social media platforms, websites and services and/or from other sources.

    Usage Data & Site Activity

    We will collect your physical location by longitude and latitude if you select a service on a mobile device that requests this information, such as the Find Your Roadhouse function. We automatically collect information regarding the actions you take on our Website (“Usage Data”). For example, each time you use our Website, we automatically collect the type of web browser you use, your operating system, your Internet Service Provider, your Internet protocol (“IP”) address, the web pages you view and the time and duration of your visits to our Website. This anonymous information may be associated with Personal Information. We use this information to help us understand how people use our Website and to enhance the services we offer.

  • How We Use the Personal Information We Collect

    Texas Roadhouse uses your Personal Information for various internal purposes, such as to provide you with better access to our Website, to improve our Website, to notify you of new products or services, to complete your gift card and merchandise purchases, to respond to your inquires, to process and to evaluate your job application, as well as to otherwise maximize your access to our online services and communicate with you about Texas Roadhouse. We may disclose to third parties Usage Data regarding our Website. Your Usage Data may be aggregated with the Usage Data of others and not identify you individually or it may include associated data that does identify you.

    Except as expressly provided herein, we do not sell, trade, share or otherwise disclose (in any manner) to third parties any Personal Information obtained through our Website or from third parties without your consent..

    Service Providers

    So that we can focus on what we do best—providing quality, great tasting food—we, from time to time, may establish relationships with other businesses whom we believe, based on investigation and/or reasonable inquiry, to be trustworthy and who have confirmed that their privacy practices are consistent with our exacting requirements (collectively, “Service Providers”). For example, we may contract with Service Providers to provide certain services, such as order processing and fulfillment, payment processing, hosting and maintenance, data storage and management, as well as certain marketing and promotions functions. We only provide our Service Providers with the information necessary for them to perform their services on our behalf.

    Other Transfers

    In the event of a transfer of all or substantially all of Texas Roadhouse’s assets to a third party, we reserve the right to transfer your Personal Information and Usage Data to the third party. You will have the opportunity to opt-out of any such transfer if, in our discretion, the new entity plans to handle your Personal Information in a way that differs materially from this Privacy Policy.

    Texas Roadhouse may also disclose Personal Information: (1) to comply with a court order, subpoena, search warrant or other legal process; (2) to comply with legal, regulatory or administrative requirements of any governmental authorities; (3) to protect and to defend Texas Roadhouse, its subsidiaries and affiliates and all of their officers, directors, employees, attorneys, agents, contractors and partners, in connection with any legal action, claim or dispute; (4) to enforce the terms of use of our Website and (5) to prevent imminent physical harm.

    Occasionally, Texas Roadhouse posts on our Website stories, comments, letters and/or photographs provided to us by our guests. By submitting a story, comment, photograph, letter or other information to Texas Roadhouse, you are representing that you are authorized to submit such materials to us and that you are authorized to and are granting permission to us to use such materials on our Website. Texas Roadhouse retains the right to archive posts in the future at our discretion.

    Promotions

    If you choose to enter a contest, sweepstakes or promotion, your personal information may be disclosed to third parties in connection with such promotion, including, without limitation, for purposes of posting your entry with attribution or otherwise as permitted in the official rules for the promotion in question, fulfilling your prize or including your name on a winners list. Also, by entering a promotion, we may require you to consent to the use of your personal information or other information, such as name, voice or likeness, in advertising, promotional and marketing materials. In addition, we may offer certain promotional content, such as a sweepstakes, sponsored by or co-branded with a third party, who may obtain the personal information that you submit.

  • Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

    There is a technology called “cookies” which can be used to provide you with tailored information from a website. A cookie is an element of data that frequently contains an anonymous unique identifier; the data is sent from our Website to your web browser, which may then store it on your system for future use. Cookies are usually account identifiers and user preferences that allow you to speed through the log-in process by automatically providing the data so that you do not have to type it out and tailors your online experience in general, including content and/or advertising that is shown to you on our Website and other websites. Some Texas Roadhouse pages may use cookies so that we may better serve you when you return to our Website and to offer you certain services. Texas Roadhouse also tracks the domains from which our Website is visited and uses that data to analyze for trends and statistics.

    You can set your web browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the option to accept or reject it, or to refuse all cookies. You may not be able to conduct some transactions or use some services on our Website if you disable your web browser from accepting cookies. Our systems do not recognize browser “Do Not Track” signals, but some of our Service Providers who utilize cookies and other tracking technologies on our Website enable you to opt out of certain online tracking. For more information about third party cookies and tracking technologies, including information about how to opt out of certain of these technologies, visit one or all of the following:

    http://www.sharethis.com/legal/privacy/#sthash.1GCAknzT.dpbs
    https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
