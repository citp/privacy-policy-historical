# Privacy Policy :: Texas Roadhouse

**Posted:** October 1st, 2004

**Texas Roadhouse is an award-winning, full-service restaurant chain featuring hearty, hand-cut steaks, fall-off-the-bone ribs and hot, fresh-baked bread. Our motto is Legendary Food, Legendary ServiceÂ®. We hope that you enjoy our food and our Website and come back to visit us again. Because we value your patronage and respect your privacy rights, we want to provide you with this statement of our Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy governs your use of any Website or Webpage operated by Texas Roadhouse, including any site from which you access this agreement (collectively, our "Website"), and applies to Personal Information (as defined below) collected by us at our Website.**

#### Personal Information We Collect About You

"Personal Information" is information through which you can be identified. Texas Roadhouse only collects Personal Information that you voluntarily provide to or share with us. The Personal Information that we collect through our Website depends on the nature of your activities, including, but not limited to whether you send information to the Guest Comments page, join the Willie Nelson Fan Club, purchase merchandise, submit a personal story, etc. Personal Information that Texas Roadhouse may collect from you when you contact us, sign up to become a Texas Roadie, order merchandise from us, email or submit a post to us, or become a fan club member, may include your name, address, phone number (daytime, home, and/or cell), fax number, billing address, email address, age, gender, photograph, credit/debit card information (including credit card name, type, number, expiration date), purchase information, and other information that you voluntarily provide. 

In accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998 ("COPPA"), it is necessary for Texas Roadhouse to obtain parental permission for all children under the age of 13 to provide personal information through our Website. Persons under the age of thirteen (13) are not authorized to make purchases from our Website and we do not collect unauthorized information about children. Texas Roadhouse is concerned about the safety of children and their use of the Internet, and therefore, in accordance with COPPA, we will never knowingly request or solicit personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13 without prior verifiable parental consent. In the event that Texas Roadhouse receives actual knowledge that we have collected such personal information without the requisite and verifiable parental consent, we will delete that information from our database as quickly as is practical. 

#### How We Use The Personal Information We Collect

Texas Roadhouse uses your Personal Information to complete your merchandise orders and respond to your inquires, for marketing purposes (when you sign up to become a Texas Roadie), to identify your story as authentic when you submit a guest story or photograph, to enroll you for membership in the Willie Nelson Fan Club and identify you as the source of your posts, to help identify you in the event you lose your password, and to otherwise maximize your access to our online services and products. If you choose not to give us your personal information, you can still use most of our Website, however, you may not be able to send us email inquiries, post on a fan club, sign up to become a Texas Roadie, or order merchandise. In the event of a transfer of all or substantially all of Texas Roadhouse's assets to a third party, we reserve the right to transfer your Personal Information to the third party, provided that the third party agrees to adhere to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Texas Roadhouse may also disclose Personal Information: (1) to comply with a court order, subpoena, search warrant or other legal process; (2) to comply with legal, regulatory or administrative requirements of any governmental authorities; (3) to protect and defend Texas Roadhouse, its subsidiaries and affiliates, and all of their officers, directors, employees, attorneys, agents, contractors, and partners, in connection with any legal action, claim or dispute; (4) to enforce the terms of use of our Website; and (5) to prevent imminent physical harm. 

Except as provided in this Privacy Policy, Texas Roadhouse will not sell, trade, or disclose to third parties any Personal Information obtained through our Website or from third parties without your consent.

Occasionally, Texas Roadhouse posts on our Website stories, letters and/or photographs provided to us by our guests. By submitting a story, photograph, letter, or other information to Texas Roadhouse, you are representing that you are authorized to submit such materials to us and that you are authorized to and are granting permission to us to use such materials on our Website. Additionally, Texas Roadhouse's Willie Nelson Fan Club allows you to post information to that club. Texas Roadhouse retains the right to archive posts in the future at our discretion. In addition, posts may be lost or purged due to technical difficulties. Texas Roadhouse may have a website administrator review your information. In the event that you post information that violates the terms of our Website, our administrator may remove your post and/or may change, lock, or delete your profile. 

#### Cookies

There is a technology called "cookies" which can be used to provide you with tailored information from a website. A cookie is an element of data that frequently contains an anonymous unique identifier; the data is sent from our Website to your browser, which may then store it on your system for future use. Cookies are usually account identifiers and user preferences that allow you to speed through the log-in process by automatically providing the data so that you do not have to type it out. Some Texas Roadhouse pages may use cookies so that we may better serve you when you return to our Website. Texas Roadhouse also tracks the domains from which our Website is visited and uses that data to analyze for trends and statistics. We discard the data once the analysis is complete. We do not track individuals - only instances of entry onto our Website. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the option to accept or reject it. You may not be able to conduct some transactions or use some services on our Website if you disable your browser from accepting cookies. 

#### Security

We ensure the security of your Personal Information by: (1) keeping our databases and our web servers separate and protecting each with electronic and physical firewalls; and (2) using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption technology to transmit credit card information between your browser and our web servers. No security system is impenetrable. The secure servers generally are not accessible by unauthorized third parties, but could become accessible in the event of a security breach. Texas Roadhouse has no control over the security of the computers or other electronic devices and means you use to send emails and submit information to us over the internet. Therefore, we are not responsible for the disclosure or interception of your information before it is received by us.
