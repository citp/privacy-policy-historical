# TextFixer.com - Privacy Policy

This is the web site of TextFixer.com. We can be reached using the [contact form](https://web.archive.org/contact "contact form").

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes general statistical information.

We do not save or are interested in the type of data that you are modifying on this website and since the vast majority of that kind of activity occurs on the client side (the processing is done by your computer) - the specifics of that data are not known to us.

The only information we do collect is used to improve the content of our Web pages.

We do not at present use cookies but if we did we would use cookies to store visitors preferences, record past activity at a site in order to provide better service when visitors return to our site.

We do not at present collect email addresses so you should not receive any email from us unless you have initiated direct correspondence with us. If you receive an unsolicted email from this domain, know that the email headers have been faked and that the email is not valid and should be disregarded.

With respect to Advertising companies: we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information.

From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our web site periodically.

At present, we provide site visitors with no access to information that we have collected and that we maintain about them.

At present, we offer visitors no ability to have factual inaccuracies corrected in information that we maintain about them

**Google Privacy Conditions**

We do on occasion use Google for serving ads and because of this, you should be aware of the following:

  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site.
  * Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet.
  * For more information about the DART cookie, please visit the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html). 



If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact us using the [contact form](https://web.archive.org/contact "contact form").
