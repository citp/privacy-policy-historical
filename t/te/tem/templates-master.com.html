PRIVACY STATEMENT

This privacy statement discloses our practices for the gathering and use of information from visitors to our own Web sites, from people who register for services that we offer online, and from visitors to other sites where we collect information pursuant to our customer contracts.

By using this site, you agree to the Templates Master Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use or register at our site. We reserve the right, at our discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for changes. Your continued use of the Templates Master site following the posting of changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes.

The Information we collect

We collect the domain name, ip address of visitors to our web page, the e-mail addresses of those who post messages at our site, web adresses of stores our products used atm the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, the e-mail addresses of those who make postings to our chat areas, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, name and address, telephone number.

We use cookies to enrich your user experience and to allow you to visit multiple pages of our site without having to login at every page. We do not use cookies to identify, monitor, or track any personally identifiable information. Our cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information about you. By modifying your browser preferences, you have the choice to accept all cookies, to be notified when a cookie is set, or to reject all cookies.

Our site may contain links to other sites. Please note, however, that these other sites may have privacy policies that differ from those of Templates Master. We have no control over any personal information you choose to provide those sites.

To refer a friend, you will be asked to provide their email address so that we can send them a one-time invitation on your behalf to join our website. All referral email addresses are securely protected.

User registration

Our membership registration process requires that you provide us with basic contact information such as name, email and address. Additional information, including mailing address, are required in order to disburse payment. Without such details, we will not be able to minimize fraud and/or ensure transaction authenticity. We will never sell or otherwise share this information with any other organization.

Minors under 18 years of age are not allowed to participate in any of the services and/or programs offered by Templates Master. No information should be submitted to, or posted at, any Templates Master Web sites by visitors under 18 years of age. We encourage parents and guardians to spend time online with their children and to participate and monitor the interactive activities of their children.

The information we collect is used for
  1. To improve the content of our Web page
  2. To allow us to ship your free gifts
  3. To enable us to respond to you or to process, validate, and verify service requests and to perform the requested services
  4. To tailor the registrant's experience on our sites, showing them content we think might interest them or altering the content according to their preferences.
  5. We will use your e-mail address to send out occasional updates and offers.
  6. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by contacting us at our contact form. If you supply us with your postal address on-line you will only receive the information for which you provided us your address. Except for purposes stated expressly in this Privacy Policy, Templates Master will not use your personal information.
