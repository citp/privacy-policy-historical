TemplateToaster Privacy policy

 

TemplateToaster is committed to ensure you that the information we gather from you is completely anonymous and protected, we never share that information with any third party. Please note that TemplateToaster may make minor changes in this privacy policy anytime if needed.
 
What type of information we collect: We collect your email and name while registering with us we use this information for maintaining your account for future communication activation/deactivation of your license and so on. Our site may also save cookies on your browser for better performance.

 

Security: We ensure you that the information we collect from you is protected physically, digitally, electronically from unauthorised access and moreover we never share that personal information with any third party. We will not sell, redistribute, or lease your personal information unless required by law to do so.
 
If you believe the information we are holding on you is incorrect or incomplete, please contact us and we’ll update it in our records.
