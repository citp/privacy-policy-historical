####  Informational Collection: 

TerraServer, a division of Precision Hawk USA Inc., is the sole owner of the information collected on our website. TerraServer collects information at several different points on our website. Information is collected for the purpose of making online purchases of our products and include all of the necessary data needed for describing products and the customer information for delivering our products. We use this information to complete the e-commerce transactions, contact customers by email or regular mail and to maintain and secure our website. We retain this information for an indeterminate period of time. 

####  Cookies: 

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user. Session ID cookies simply terminate once you close your browser. A persistent cookie is a small text file stored on the user's hard drive for an extended period of time. Persistent cookies can be removed by following Internet browser help file directions. You can set your browser to notify you to either accept or deny the cookie according to the following information. 

TerraServer uses cookies to enhance the performance of the website, to track subscription sign in status and to store items added to your shopping cart.

If you have communicated to us about our products and services and you are located in a jurisdiction that permits us to send promotional communications, we may send you promotional communications via email or other electronic communications, including, but not limited to, newsletters, special offers, surveys and other news and information we think will be of interest to you. ####  Data Security: 

TerraServer uses physical security measures to protect against the loss, misuse, or alteration of information. Our company uses secure encryption technology (secure socket connection) for confidential and e-commerce data transmitted on our website. 

####  Changing Information: 

If a user wishes to correct/update personally identifiable information, such as zip code, phone, email or postal address, or no longer desires our service, they can notify us of what they desire by contacting the company. 

####  Links and Advertisements: 

Our website may contain links and/or advertisements to other websites. We do not partner with, endorse, or have special relationships with any ad server companies. TerraServer is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of any other websites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our website and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this website.
