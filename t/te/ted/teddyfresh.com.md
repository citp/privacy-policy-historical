# Privacy Policy – Teddy Fresh

## Privacy

Your information and privacy is extremely important to us. We will only use it in ways that would be expected during the course of normal business operations.

We may use your information to provide you products and services, notify you of product issues, to respond to your comments or questions, and to personalize your experience on the website. With your permission we may send you marketing communications and other information you have chosen to receive regarding our products or special events.

We will never sell or share your information with third parties.
