General, aggregated, and non-personal information may be collected by Xstreet SL. This type of anonymous, aggregated profiling data may include information that you have provided to us through surveys, polls, etc., but will not be tied to any personal information. It may also include aggregated anonymous information about site usage and the customer base.

How we use information we collect from you

The main purpose of collecting information from you is to provide you with convenient and customized services. In general this means information collected for a particular purpose will only be saved and used for that purpose, unless you have agreed to allow us to use it for some other purpose, as described in this Policy. This includes:

  • processing transactions and maintaining accurate business and financial records,
  • resolving disputes and troubleshooting problems,
  • prevent potentially illegal or prohibited activity and enforce the Xstreet SL Terms of Service, and
  • send you service update notices and targeted marketing and promotional materials based on your preferences.

We may disclose your personal information to law enforcement or other appropriate third parties in connection with criminal investigations, investigation of fraud, infringement of intellectual property rights, or other suspected illegal activities, or as otherwise may be required by applicable law, or in order to protect the legitimate legal and business interests of Xstreet SL.

Except as described in this policy, we will not give any of your personal information to any third party without your express approval. We sometimes share general or aggregated (not personal) information with third parties about our site activity, but that information does not include any personal information.

Security
