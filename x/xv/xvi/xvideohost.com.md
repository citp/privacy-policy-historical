# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. We want you to feel comfortable using XVideoHostservices. Because we gather certain types of information about the users of XVideoHost's network, we feel you should fully understand what information we gather and how we use that information. XVideoHostemployees are required to familiarize themselves with this privacy policy as a condition of their employment, and any employee violating the policy is subject to disciplinary action. 

When you register for a XVideoHostaccount, XVideoHostobtains contact information, such as your name, address, and phone number, as well as payment information (if neded). When you use XVideoHost's service, our systems records information about your session, such as the dates and times of use, and the specific Internet address assigned to you. Finally, XVideoHostkeeps records of your payment history and communications you have with XVideoHost's support team. 

XVideoHost uses the information it gathers in order to provide you with the best online experience possible. As you might expect, contact information is used to contact you concerning your account and our company. Payment information is used to validate your identity and to bill you for the services and products you order. Information on your online sessions is used to allocate system resources and to ensure compliance with XVideoHost's Terms of Service Agreement. 

Information on your communications with XVideoHost's support team is used to address the issues raised by your communications. XVideoHostregularly sends out mailings regarding the operation of its service. We DO NOT send out mailings containing sales or marketing material related to other goods and services. We keep our mailing to strictly informational purposes on the services to which you currently subscribe. WE DO NOT share your data with other companies. 

XVideoHostWILL NOT share or disclose information regarding its subscribers except   
(i) when we receive legal process such as a court order;   
(ii) when reasonably necessary to protect the company's rights or property; or   
(iii) in emergencies where someone's safety is at risk. 

You may correct all of the personal information that XVideoHosthas on file for you by visiting the XVideoHostControl Panel. Please keep in mind that under XVideoHost's Terms of Service Agreement, subscribers are required to provide XVideoHostwith current contact and billing information. 

If you ever leave XVideoHost, XVideoHostwill normally delete your personally identifiable account information six months after the account is cancelled, unless we are under a legal obligation to retain it. We preserve account information for this short period of time in the event you wish to reactivate your account and retain your previous e-mail address. However, in the event of any accounting irregularities, XVideoHostreserves the right to retain your contact and billing information as necessary to resolve the matter. 

XVideoHostreserves the right to make changes to this policy. Those changes will become effective 30 days after notice of them is posted here. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please send them via e-mail to our contact email or by contac form.
