Privacy Policy for xcweather.co.uk

The privacy of visitors to XCWeather is very important to me.

Here is information on what types of personal information I receive when you visit xcweather.co.uk, and how I safeguard your information. I never sell your personal information to third parties.

Log Files

As with most other websites, I collect the data contained in log files. The information in the log files include your IP address, your browser type, the time you visited and which pages you viewed.

Cookies and Web Beacons

I use cookies to store information, such as your preferences (mph, kmh, kt etc.) when you visit my site.

In order to pay for XCWeather I use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
