Pink Triangle Press (collectively we, our or us) is committed to protecting the privacy of the users (collectively you or your) of our websites (the Sites). This Privacy Policy is applicable to any of our Sites that make reference to this Privacy Policy.

While many aspects of our Sites do not require you to register before using them, other activities such as signing up for a newsletter or entering a contest require registration or membership. Personal information is collected through these registration and membership processes, which may be conducted through other platforms such as Google Forms, MailChimp, Gleam and others. Apart from using the information for the stated purposes, all data and findings collected through these means may be used for statistical purposes, presented as anonymous or aggregated information. We will never release personal information about individual users.

Under-Age People. Anybody under 18 years of age should ensure they have permission from their parent or guardian before providing any personal information.

Cookies. In order for our Sites to function properly, we occasionally place small data files called cookies on your computer or mobile device here is some additional information about cookies:

What are Cookies? A cookie is a small text file that is saved on your computer or mobile device when you visit one of our Sites. It enables the Site to remember some of your actions and preferences (such as login, display preferences, etc.), so you dont have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the Site or browse from one section to another.

How Do We Use Cookies? When you create an account with us, we use cookies for the management of the signup process and general administration. These cookies will usually be deleted when you log out; however, in some cases they may remain afterward to remember your preferences on the Site when you are logged out.

We also use cookies while you are logged in to prevent you from having to log in again every time you visit a new page; these cookies are typically cleared when you log out to ensure you can only access restricted features and areas of the Sites while you are logged in.

In order to provide you with a great experience on our Sites, we provide you with the ability to set your own preferences for how a Site functions when you use it. We need to set cookies on your device to remember your preferences, so this information can be retrieved whenever you use a function of a Site that is affected by your personal settings.

Third Party Cookies. In some cases, we also use cookies provided by trusted third parties. The following section details which third party cookies you might encounter through our Sites.

We use Google Analytics which is one of the most widespread and trusted analytics solution on the Internet, to help us understand how you use our Sites and to develop ways to improve your experience. We have also enabled the Demographics and Interest feature which provides us with data from Googles Interest-based advertising and 3rd-party audience data (such as age, gender, and interests). This feature allows us to better evaluate who our users are, how their behaviour varies by demographics and interests, and ultimately help us create more appropriate content for our users. You can choose to opt-out of this feature using Google Ads Settings or Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

How To Control Cookies. You can control and delete cookies as you wish for more information, please see aboutcookies.org. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust or set some preferences every time you visit one of our Sites and other websites and some services and functionalities may not work. Depending on your mobile device, you may not be able to control tracking technologies through settings.

It is the responsibility of users to review the privacy policies of such advertisers and linked websites before submitting any personal information to them.

We do not permit our service providers to sell, release or otherwise distribute any personal information they may collect on our behalf from our users and they will only retain your personal information as long as you are subscribed to the email list.

Any information collected by this means is only used for that specific membership, poll, survey or contest. These types of information may include things like income, age, location and gender. We reserve the right to internally share all marketing information, including Site usage and personal demographic information so gathered with our employees to develop marketing and sales strategies. All data and findings collected for these purposes are presented as anonymous or aggregated information. No identifiable personal information about individual users will be released to outside third parties.

When you interact with us through various social media, such as when you Like us on Facebook or Share through another social media site, we may receive information from the social network such as your profile information, profile picture, gender, user name, user ID associated with your social media account, age range, language, country, friends list, and any other information you permit the social network to share with third parties.
