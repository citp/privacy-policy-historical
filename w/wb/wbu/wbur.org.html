Privacy Policy

WBUR recognizes the importance of privacy to wbur.org visitors. WBUR does not collect personally identifying information from the WBUR website unless you choose to provide that information to us. All of the information collected by WBUR is used for internal purposes only and is not distributed to outside parties, except when a visitor orders a transcript or CD through the NPR website. In that case, relevant information is provided to NPR’s outside fulfillment service for the sole purpose of delivering the requested transcript and/or CD.

If you have any questions or comments concerning our privacy policy, please contact us by email at webmaster@wbur.org or by mail at:
