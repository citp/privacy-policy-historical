Privacy Policy


1. Introduction

1.1 Wyevale Garden Centres Limited (“We/Us/Our”) is committed to safeguarding your privacy. The information that you provide to Us will only be used in accordance with this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy is intended to help you understand what information may be gathered from you and how We will use it.
1.2 By using this website (”Website”) you consent to Us using your information in accordance with the terms of this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy does not apply to third party sites.
1.3 If We amend this Policy We will post the changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information We collect and how We use it.
1.4 We do not accept any liability for and this Privacy Policy does not apply to any content or information contained in or collected from or submitted to any third party site that you access via any link from the Website.

2. What Personal Information do We Collect?

2.1 If you sign up to any of our subscriber services, We will store your email address and/or mobile telephone number.
2.2 If you purchase anything from Our on-line shop or reserve any goods through Our on-line shop, We will require your name, geographical address including post code and contact details (email address and telephone numbers).

3. How is Your Personal Information Used

3.1 The personal information that you provide to Us will be used to:
3.1.1 contact you, if necessary, about your order for goods
3.1.2 deliver goods that you have purchased to you
3.1.3 inform you by email, SMS and/or MMS of other products which may interest you
3.1.4 inform you by email, SMS and/or MMS of other products or services from other companies within Our Group which may interest you.

3.2 If you
3.2.1 wish any or all of your personal information submitted to be removed from Our database at any time; or
3.2.2 do not wish to receive any marketing information from Us; or,
3.2.3 wish to update or correct any inaccuracies in the personal information We hold about you

please contact us by:

4. Passing of Information to Third Parties
4.1 Your information shall only be passed to third parties:
4.1.1 if it is necessary for the performance of the services you have requested; or
4.1.2 if such third parties are the Police or any other regulatory or Government authority where We are legally required to do so; or
4.1.3 if such disclosure is to any one to whom We transfer or may transfer Our business and/or Our rights and duties under Our contract with you; or
4.1.4 if you have given us your permission to share such information with carefully chosen, reputable and trustworthy third parties in order to keep you informed about goods or services that may interest you.
If we wish to pass your personal information to third parties in any other situation, we shall only do so with your permission.

5. Call Monitoring and Recording
5.1 We may monitor and/or record telephone calls in order to improve Our services.

6. Data Protection
6.1 We comply with the requirements of all data protection legislation in force in England and Wales from time to time. You have the right to receive a copy of the information We hold about you on written application. This will be information you have specifically provided to us during your visits to the Website or during the phone call. If you would like a copy of it please contact customer_services@wyevale.co.uk. We are entitled to charge the current statutory fee for providing you with such information.
