# Privacy Policy - WordPress Users Association - WPUA.org -

## [Privacy Policy](http://wpua.org/privacy-policy/ "Permalink to Privacy Policy")

WPUA.com is committed to protecting the privacy and security of the information provided by customers visiting our Web site or using our services. 

WPUA.com may request information from people visiting our Web sites or using our services. WPUA.com is the sole owner of the information collected. WPUA.com respects your privacy and will not knowingly distribute or cause to be distributed your personal information to any entity for any reason. We will not collect any personally identifiable information unless the visitor/customer provides it to us voluntarily. Personally identifiable information includes a visitors/customer’s name, address, telephone number and e-mail address. WPUA.com is dedicated to building a long-term relationship with all of our customers and visitors. We will not sell, share or rent this information to others in ways other than as disclosed in this Privacy Policy. 

**Prequalification and Service Quotes:**

In some cases, a visitor may be required to complete a prequalification or inquiry form to order a product or to receive certain information. During the registration process, the visitor is required to give specific information such as name and e-mail address, mailing address, location and phone number. This information is used to pre-qualify and contact the visitor regarding services offered by WPUA.com for which the visitor has expressed interest. 

**Cookies:**

A “Cookie” is a piece of information that is sent from a WPUA.com Web server to a visitor’s browser and stored on the visitor’s hard drive. WPUA.com uses Cookies when a visitor orders a product or registers for a specific program. The Cookie stores information that allows WPUA.com Web sites to remember a visitor the next time he or she visits a particular WPUA.com Web site. Cookies also allow us to tailor our Web sites to better match visitors’ interests and preferences. Visitors may choose to disable Cookies by changing their browser settings; however, they may not be able to access some of the information on our Web sites. 

**Links:**

WPUA.com Web sites contain links to other Web sites. WPUA.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of these Web sites. We encourage our visitors and customers to read the privacy policy of each Web site they visit. This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected on WPUA.com Web sites.
