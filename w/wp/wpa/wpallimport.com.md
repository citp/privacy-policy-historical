# Privacy - WP All Import

If you purchase WP All Import we collect your name and e-mail address.

This information will be used by us to contact you when our software is updated or we have other news we feel is relevant to you.

You can opt-out of our mailing list at any time. 

You can update your personal information in our customer portal.

As you browse WPAllImport.com, advertising cookies will be placed on your computer so that we can understand what you are interested in. Our display advertising partner, AdRoll, then enables us to present you with retargeting advertising on other sites based on your previous interaction with WPAllImport. The techniques our partners employ do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address or telephone number. We do not collect or store credit card information. Credit card transactions are processed off-site, by PayPal.
