This policy was last updated on November 2, 2005.

Dow Jones & Company, Inc., the parent company of Dow Jones Publishing Company (Asia), Inc., publisher of The Wall Street Journal Asia, and its affiliates (hereinafter referred to collectively as "Dow Jones") are committed to maintaining the privacy of personal information that you provide to The Wall Street Journal Asia (the "Journal Asia"). This Privacy Policy describes how we treat the personal information of our subscribers and those who visit the Journal Asia customer service web site at www.wsj-asia.com (the "Journal Asia Web Site").

Other Dow Jones Products, Services and Web Sites
Dow Jones has many other products, services and web sites with their own privacy policies, including the print and online editions of The Wall Street Journal , the print and online editions of The Far Eastern Economic Review and The Wall Street Journal Briefing at www.briefing.wsj.com (collectively, "Dow Jones Products"). If you subscribe to or visit any other Dow Jones Product or web site, even if you do so through the Journal Asia or the Journal Asia Web Site, the other Dow Jones Products' privacy policies apply.

Information We Collect
General: When you subscribe to the Journal Asia or use any of the tools on the Journal Asia Web Site, and at other times, we may collect personally identifiable information from you that may include your name, e-mail address, mailing address, telephone number, credit card account number and similar information. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of thirteen.

Surveys: We may conduct occasional surveys. These surveys are entirely voluntary and you may easily decline to participate.

How We Use Information Collected
We may use information in the following ways:
  • For the purposes for which you specifically provided the information, for example, to process and fulfill your subscriptions to the Journal Asia, or to process your changes of address or delivery suspension requests.
  • To send you notifications about your account with us or to otherwise contact you.
  • To provide you with promotional materials.
  • To permit select companies to send you promotional materials about their products and services.
  • To enhance our products and services or develop new products and services.
  • To provide advertisers and other third parties with aggregate information about our subscribers.
We may combine the information that we collect from you with information that you provide to us in connection with your use of other Dow Jones Products and with information provided to us by third parties.

We may hire other companies to perform services on our behalf, including without limitation sending e-mails, fulfilling subscription and other requests (for example, to provide your home delivery of the Journal Asia) and facilitating some aspects of the Journal Asia Web Site. These service providers may be supplied with or have access to your personally identifiable information for the purpose of providing services to us.

We may disclose and use personally identifiable information in special circumstances where it is necessary to enforce our rights (for example, when necessary to protect our intellectual property rights) or when we, in our sole discretion, believe it is necessary or appropriate, in connection with or as the result of a change in ownership of all or a portion of the Journal Asia as the result of a merger, acquisition or transfer to another company. We also may disclose personally identifiable information when we, in good faith, believe that the law requires us to do so.

Third-Party and Corporate Subscriptions. If your subscription to the Journal Asia is provided by a third party, such as a subscription agent or your employer, we may provide to that third party limited amounts of your personally identifiable information as appropriate. In some cases, the third party providing you with the Journal Asia subscription may provide us with limited amounts of personal information about you.

Your Ability to Choose
You can let us know that you do not want to receive promotional materials by indicating your preference when you subscribe. You also may contact us by e-mail at
service@wsj-asia.com, by telephone at +852 2831 2555, by fax at +852 2503 1549 or by mail at Circulation Dept., The Wall Street Journal Asia, 25/F Central Plaza, 18 Harbour Road, Hong Kong. You may opt-out of receiving promotional materials even if you are not a subscriber to our publications.

Access and Correction
If you would like to access or update your subscription or any other personally identifiable information that we may have about you, you can do so by contacting us by e-mail at service@wsj-asia.com, by telephone at +852 2831 2555, by fax at +852 2503 1549 or by mail at Circulation Dept., The Wall Street Journal Asia, 25/F Central Plaza, 18 Harbour Road, Hong Kong. You may access and correct the personally identifiable information that we may have about you even if you are not a subscriber to our publications.

Transfer to Other Countries
Your information may be stored and processed in Hong Kong, the United States or any other country where Dow Jones has facilities, affiliates or agents, and by subscribing to our products and services, you consent to the transfer of information outside of your country.

Security
We will maintain appropriate safeguards to protect the security of your personally identifiable information.

Policy Modifications
When we make any material change to this Privacy Policy, we will inform you by posting a notice on the home page of the Journal Asia Web Site (at www.wsj-asia.com) and by changing the date on this page noting when the policy was last updated.

Comments and Questions
If you have any questions, comments or concerns about our Privacy Policy, you may contact us by e-mail at service@wsj-asia.com, by telephone at +852 2831 2555, by fax at +852 2503 1549 or by mail at Circulation Dept., The Wall Street Journal Asia, 25/F Central Plaza, 18 Harbour Road, Hong Kong.

The Journal Asia Web Site
The following provisions apply specifically to the Journal Asia Web Site:

Links to Other Web Sites. The Journal Asia Web Site may feature content or services from third party web sites such as Newstand Inc.'s digital delivery services at www.newstand.com. Remember that any information that you provide on any other web site is not subject to this Privacy Policy. We recommend that you review the privacy policy at each other web site to determine how that site treats your personal information.

Web Site Usage Information. We may collect your IP address and Web Site usage information when you visit the Journal Asia Web Site. This information can help us to evaluate how our visitors use and navigate the Journal Asia Web Site, including the number and frequency of visitors to each web page, and the length of their visits.

Network Advertisers. We also may use third party network advertisers to deliver ads to you on our behalf. These service providers and network advertisers may be supplied with or have access to your personally identifiable information for the purpose of providing services to us.

Cookies and Web Beacons. We may use cookies, invisible images (often referred to as pixel tags, clear GIFs or web beacons) and other similar technology on the Journal Asia Web Site to make sure that you do not see the same pop-up offers or other advertisements over and over again, to assess traffic on the site and for similar purposes.

When you view an ad or link for the Journal Asia on other web sites, we may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser. We also may use invisible images (often referred to as pixel tags, clear GIFs or web beacons) to note which pages of the Journal Asia Web Site you visit after viewing our ads. The information that is collected through the use of this technology is used only to track responses to our advertising efforts.
