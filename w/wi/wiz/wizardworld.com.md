# Privacy Policy Wizard World Comic Con

**At  **[ **wizardworld.com**](http://wizardworld.com/) **  we have a “No sharing” policy. This means that our policy is to not share your information with others. You can feel comfortable knowing that all  of your personal information used in the ordering process and the opt-in form is for our use and only our us. The only exception is on those occasions, if any, when you specifically agree to allow us to share particular information with a third party.**
