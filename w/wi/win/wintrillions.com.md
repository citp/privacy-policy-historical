# Online Lottery - Powerball, Mega Millions, Super Lotto, California, New York, Florida, Power Ball, Megamillions - Online scanned lottery tickets

|  | 

Collection of information

When you subscribe to our service we ask for your name, address and other contact information, billing information, some preferences and certain demographic information. While some of the information you provide is essential to conduct our business, other information is optional, clearly marked as such, and may be given at your discretion.

The information you provide allows us to provide an efficient service and helps us improve our level of service to better meet your needs.  
  
---|---  
  
How we use the information

We use the information you provide about yourself to fulfill your requests for our products and services and to respond to any inquiries about our offerings.

We sometimes use this information to communicate with you, such as to notify you when you have won lotteries or contests, when we make changes to our subscriber agreements, to fulfill some of your requests, or to contact you about your account with us.

We also may use non-personally identifiable information that we collect in order to improve the design and content of our websites and to collect usage statistics.
