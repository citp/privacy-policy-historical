# Privacy Policy - A Taste of California Wine Clubs

Our Pledge to You, Our Member: 

We believe strongly in protecting the privacy of our members. Any information you give us, such as telephone number, credit card number or e-mail address, is held with the utmost care and security, and will not be used in ways to which you have not consented.

We will not collect any personal information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals. Our order form requires you to supply information specific to your particular order, including your billing, shipping and e-mail addresses as well as credit card information. This data is used to expedite, process and ship your order(s). We may use the contact information in case we need to alert you to a problem with your order. We may also use this information to notify you of updates to our wine inventory lists.

We may also use any contact information we collect to occasionally notify you about important functionality changes to the site, new A Taste of California services and special offers we think you'll find valuable.

If you would rather not receive any communications from us at info@ATasteofCalifornia.com. We will be happy to remove you from any of our contact lists.

A Taste of California welcomes your questions and comments about privacy. If you would like to email us, please [click here](https://web.archive.org/web/20071210082250id_/http%3A//www.wineinsiders.com/contact.cfm) or call us at 1-800-615-7304.
