Personal Information We Collect

We collect the following Personal Information that you submit to us voluntarily:

Personal Information Collected Via Technology

Willow Garage may use both session Cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent Cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them). While you will still be able to use the Services if you disable Cookies in your browser, certain parts of the Services may not function as effectively.
