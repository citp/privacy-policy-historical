Privacy Policy

At WiseStepp.com we know that you care how information about you is used and shared; Your privacy is important to us. The details below allow you to learn more about how your information is used and with whom it is shared and so on
WiseStepp.com is a network of Professionals connecting with each other or their friends, to share opportunities in business, career, knowledge and other professional or social areas. When you open an account with us, you provide personally identifiable information.

A. Information Collected

1. To become a WiseStepp.com member, you must open an account yourself or at the request of an existing member. To open an account you are required to provide the following mandatory information: name, email address, country, industry, Postal code and a password.

2. You can create your own profile page that has information on your education, work experience and share it with the other members. You can choose to display the information you want to, to other members of the site by modifying your profile settings. WiseStepp.com will use this information to customize your experience on the web site. For example, when you login to your account we will display the new users who have joined the network. Similarly job opportunities that are new on the website may be shown to you.

3. To Invite others to connect with you directly on WiseStepp.com, you can either invite them directly if you know their email addresses or if they are already existing members of WiseStepp.com you can get introduced to them through other members. This information will be used by WiseStepp.com to send your invitation including a message that you write. The names and email addresses of people that you invite will be used only to send your invitation and reminders. WiseStepp.com may choose to remind some of the users of any pending request that is initiated by other members on the website. You may also choose to Import contacts from your other email providers or networking communities. All information imported will only be viewable by you and will not be searchable by others in the WiseStepp.com network. None of your friends will be contacted or sent emails until you decide to invite them.

4. All information that you enter or import about your contacts will be covered by the same terms of this privacy policy as it covers your own personally identifiable information.

5. WiseStepp.com provides specific areas on the website where external weblinks may appear. WiseStepp.com does not endorse these websites but these websites may choose to customize the links shown on WiseStepp.com depending on their customization of weblinks based on the WiseStepp.com professional community.

B. Cookies & Web Logs

WiseStepp.com uses cookies and web log files to track site usage. A cookie is a tiny data file which resides on your computer which allows WiseStepp.com to recognize you as a user when you return to our site using the same computer and web browser. Unfortunately, if your browser settings do not allow cookies, you will not be able to use our website. Like the information you enter at registration or in your Profile, cookie and log file data is used to customize your experience on the web site.
One type of cookie, known as a "persistent" cookie, is set once you have logged in to your WiseStepp.com account. The next time you visit the WiseStepp.com web site, the persistent cookie will allow us to recognize you as an existing user so you will not need to log in before using the service. Additionally, if you log out of the WiseStepp.com site you will need to re-enter your password the next time you visit in order to log in to your account. Another type of cookie, called a "session" cookie, is used to identify a particular visit to the WiseStepp.com site. Session cookies expire after a short time or when you close your web browser.

Due to the communications standards on the Internet, when you visit the WiseStepp.com web site we automatically receive the URL of the site from which you came and the site to which you are going when you leave WiseStepp.com. We also receive the Internet protocol (IP) address of your computer (or the proxy server you use to access the World Wide Web), your computer operating system and type of web browser you are using, as well as the name of your ISP. This information is used to analyze overall trends to help us improve the WiseStepp.com service. The linkage between your IP address and your personally identifiable information is never shared with third-parties without your permission or except when required by law.

2. We will only share your personally identifiable information with third parties to carry out your instructions and to provide specific services.

4. Communications between Users:

Many communications or messages that you initiate through WiseStepp.com, will list your email address and name in the header of the message.
