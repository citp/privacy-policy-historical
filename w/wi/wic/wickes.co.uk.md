# Wickes - Privacy

| Print this page |

Wickes is committed to protecting your privacy. Please read the following privacy policy to understand how we use and protect the information that you provide to us. 

**By registering or placing an order on this website, you consent to the collection, use and transfer of your information under the terms of this policy.**

##  INFORMATION THAT WE COLLECT FROM YOU AND HOW WE USE IT 

When you visit, register or order with us, you may be asked to provide certain information about yourself including your name, contact details and credit or debit card details. This allows us to process and fulfil your orders and notify you of your order status. We also ask for your telephone number, which enables us to contact you urgently if there is a problem with your order. Should you enter a competition or promotional feature, request a brochure or other information from us or take part in a survey we may ask for your details so we can administer such competition or request. 

We may also collect information about your usage of our website as well as information about you from messages you post to the website and e-mails or letters you send to us. 

Your information will enable us to provide you with access to all parts of our website and to supply the goods or services you have requested. We will also use and analyse the information we collect so that we can administer, support, improve and develop our business. 

We may use other companies to provide services on our behalf, such as fulfilling orders, processing payments, packing and delivery as well as analysing data and carrying out market research. We will only disclose your information to them to the extent needed to deliver the service. 

We may use your information to contact you for your views on our services and to notify you occasionally about important changes or developments to the site or our services. Further, we may also use your information to let you know about other products and services which we offer which may be of interest to you and we may contact you by post, telephone or e-mail. If, at any stage, you decide that you do not want to be contacted in the future, please let us know by emailing custservices @wickes.co.uk

Where you have consented when providing us with your details, we may allow other companies in our group to contact you occasionally about products and services which may be of interest to you. They may contact you by post, telephone or by e-mail. If you change your mind about being contacted by these companies in the future, please let us know by emailing custservices @wickes.co.uk

Unless required to do so by law, we will not otherwise share, sell or distribute any of the information you provide to us without your consent. 

## COOKIES

Cookies are small amounts of information which we store on your computer. Unless you have indicated your objection when disclosing your details to us, our system will issue cookies to your computer when you log on to this website. Cookies make it easier for you to log on to and use this website during future visits. They also allow us to monitor website traffic and to personalise the content of this website for you. You may set up your computer to reject cookies although, in that case, you may not be able to use certain features on this website. 

## SECURITY 

Ordering online is protected by a secure server. We employ security measures to protect your information from access by unauthorised persons and against unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction and damage. 

## ACCESSING AND UPDATING

You are entitled to see the information held about you and you may ask us to make any necessary changes to ensure that it is accurate and kept up to date. Any changes to our privacy policy in the future will be posted to this website and will take effect immediately.

All comments, queries and requests relating to our use of your information are welcomed and should be addressed to emailing custservices @wickes.co.uk
