# Privacy

                                       Privacy Policy
WOW! assures you that we will never sell your personal information to anyone. We always
want you to enjoy your visits and any interaction with us. That mandates that you never worry
about or receive spam, telemarketing calls or pesky salespeople approaching you in person.
Please understand that WOW! contains links to other websites. We cannot accept responsibility
for their privacy practices or the content of their Web sites.
When you enter our Contests, you will be sharing your contact information. We will only use
the information when contacting you about the contests.
We will never hold anyone against her will and that means you may elect to cancel your
newsletter any time you wish.
Any personal information used to contact you, our visitor, will be for the sole purpose of
facilitating your request or keeping you updated with WOW! information that you have
indicated an interest in.
