Privacy Policy

We use commercially reasonable procedures and work diligently to protect the personal information that we collect in support of our clients' hiring processes. The following describes our Privacy Policy with respect to our recruitment and selection products and the collection, use, and disclosure of personally identifiable information. Your continued use of this site constitutes your agreement to all terms and conditions herein and your consent to the collection, use, disclosure and retention of personal information in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

Personal Information

When you apply for employment with a prospective employer or seek promotion with a current employer through our hosted web site, personal information about you will be collected for use in connection with the application or promotional process. This information may include but will not necessarily be limited to your name, e-mail address, address, social security number, prior work history and other background information. This information will be used by the specific employer for assessing your suitability for employment or promotion with that employer, record keeping, governmental reporting, and compliance with legal obligations or as reasonably necessary to defend the rights of the specific employer or Wonderlic. In the course of the application process, this information may also be disclosed to others such as companies hired by the specific employer to perform background checks, to government agencies and to third party vendors who assist the specific employer in performing its business functions. Information collected via our hosted website may also be disclosed in the following situations: (a) in response to a subpoena, court order or legal process, to the extent required by law; (b) to protect user security or the security of other persons, consistent with applicable laws; or; (c) in connection with a sale or transfer of relevant Wonderlic assets.

Information Transmitted Via the Internet

We use commercially reasonable means to protect user information against disclosure to third parties, however you acknowledge that no method of transmitting information over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. By using our hosted website or sending us information, you consent to any transborder transmission of the information supplied by you.

Links

Wonderlic's website may use content or provide links to third-party websites. Wonderlic has no control over third parties and assumes no responsibility in connection with your use of third party content or websites. We recommend that you check the terms of use and privacy policy of any website before providing any personally identifiable information.

Updating and Correcting Information

You may correct your personal information by contacting us by email (Attn:Customer Services), by mail at Wonderlic Customer Services, 1795 N. Butterfield Road,Suite 200, Libertyville, IL 60048 or by phone at 888.397.8519.
