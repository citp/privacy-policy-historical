# Privacy Policy - wooga

**Scope**

Welcome to the world of gaming, welcome to wooga. The aim of our privacy policy is to tell you how we will use any personal data you provide through this website. Please read it.

This website is operated by wooga GmbH (”wooga”, “we” or “our”). wooga has with its registered office in Saarbrückerstr. 38, 10405 Berlin, Germany, entered in the register of businesses Berlin-Charlottenburg under the number: HRB 117846 B.

Our service consists of any software that you have embedded on a website or are about to embed, any applications created by wooga, applications available through social networking sites and any software running on our servers, content, related documentation and information on the domain of wooga.

By using our service, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as outlined in this Privacy Policy. We recommend visiting our page periodically to review any changes to the privacy policy of wooga.

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other web sites. We encourage you to be aware when they leave our site to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected by wooga.

**Collection of personal information**

In case of using our service, we may collect and record the information you have provided. Other than names and email addresses provided at your discretion, we don’t generally collect any personally identifying information about you, exception being for billing and payments where we store your billing information such as name, street address, phone number. 

We may offer you the opportunity to submit other information about yourself or we may be able to collect that information from social networking systems on which you have used wooga games or applications which would then also be covered by this privacy policy.

If you invite new members into your network or send messages through our service, we collect and maintain on secure servers the information associated with those messages, including email addresses and content.

**How we use your information**

Our primary purpose in collecting personal information is to provide you with a safe, smooth, efficient, and customized experience. We use your personal information to manage your registration and to personalize your visits to our website, and the games that you access. In case you have asked to receive information from us we will use your personal information to send you newsletters or other information about us.

We don’t sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent. However, we may employ the services of a third party to help us provide our online services, such as processing any payments on our website. In some cases the third party may receive your information. We will control and be responsible for the use of your information at all the time.

We may also display targeted advertisements on our website based on your personal information. For example this could include adverts designed for your age group, where you live, or the types of games you play. We will not provide any personal information to the advertiser if you view a targeted advertisement.

We may use aggregated information for the purposes of tracking website usage of the web site and in order to help us improve our website and for research and analysis purposes. We may pass this information to third parties. The aggregate information and statistics will not, however, include details that can be used to identify you. 

We also use non-personally identifiable information and certain technical information about your computer and your access of the Service (including your internet protocol address) in order to operate, maintain and manage our service. 

Please be advised that if you send messages or post material containing personal information, that information may be collected and used by others.

You can terminate your account at any time which will remove your profile and other personal information from view. We may keep information about you that is on its system following your termination. Please contact wooga to terminate your account or for any other questions about your personal information.

**Cookies**

A cookie (small files placed on your computer’s hard drive) will typically contain the name of the domain from which the cookie has come, the lifetime of the cookie, and a value, usually a randomly generated unique number.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies by modifying the settings in your browser. However, you may not be able to use all the interactive features of our site if cookies are disabled.

**Security**

We use standard industry practices, including encryption, passwords and physical security, to protect your personal information against unauthorized access and disclosure. Your account information is located on a secured server behind a firewall. When you enter sensitive information (such as credit card number or your password), we encrypt that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). Because email and instant messaging are not recognized as secure communications, we request that you not send private information to us by email or instant messaging services. If you have any questions about the security of our website, please contact us.
