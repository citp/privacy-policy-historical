# Relationship Headquarters - Privacy Policy

As a Licensed Professional Counselor, Mr. Grant is bound by a code of ethics regarding clients confidentially. We will never sell your information to any third party. In addition, we do not schedule appointments via email for these reasons: 

  * The email might be sent to the wrong person, (with your name on it).
  * Something in the email might be misconstrued (either by you or us) and we don't want there to be any misunderstandings regarding appointment times.
  * There is a time gap between when you email and when we return your email that makes accurate scheduling almost impossible.



We appreciate your understanding in this matter.  
 

In addition, Relationshipheadquarters.com contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personally identifiable information.  

All information, excluding email address through the newsletter signup, are collect by a third party namely Clickbank.com which has it's own policies regarding how the information you volunteer to them will be stored, distributed and used. For a listing of Clickbank.com's privacy guidelines [please click here](https://www.clickbank.com/privacy.html). It is our policy to never share of sell your email address or personal information unless we are legally compelled to do so.
