W.I.N. Privacy Policy

This Women Inventorz Network privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) was developed to help you understand more about Women Inventorz Network’s privacy practices, and describes the collection, use, and disclosure of various information Women Inventorz Network may collect from you with respect to your use of the Women Inventorz Network Service and the Women Inventorz Network web properties (“Women Inventorz Network Websites”).  By using the Women Inventorz Network Service and/or Women Inventorz Network Websites, you agree to the following:

DEFINITIONS

The following defined terms shall have the meanings set forth below:

“Financial Information” means information relating to payments and billing (e.g. name, account information, billing address) provided in connection with your use of Women Inventorz Network Service.  All Financial Information collected is for billing and payment purposes only and may be protected and administered by Women Inventorz Network and Women Inventorz Network’s authorized third party partners.

“Non-Personal Information” means information that, by itself, does not identify a specific individual or entity (e.g. demographic information, usage data or downloads).

“Personal Information” means specific identifiable information about an individual or entity which may be used to identify, contact or locate that person or entity (e.g. name, mailing address, e-mail address or phone number).

What Information Is Collected

Information You Provide. Women Inventorz Network may receive and store any Personal Information, Financial Information and/or Non-Personal Information that you provide during the use of any Services. Sometimes, you will have a choice whether or not to provide information, but if you fail to provide certain information it could make some features and/or functionality of the Women Inventorz Network Service unavailable to you. Women Inventorz Network will use information you provide to respond to your requests, communicate with you, and to improve the Women Inventorz Network Service. Here are some examples:

a)  User Account Information The information you provide in setting up your User Account with Women Inventorz Network may include, without limitation, your name, login, e-mail address, physical address, payment information, phone number, password, computer and connection information such as browser type, version, and time zone setting, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system, and platform.

b)  E-mail Communications.  Women Inventorz Network may use your Personal Information to send e-mails periodically describing promotions, events or changes relating to the Women Inventorz Network Service or Women Inventorz Network Websites. You may opt-out of receiving promotional e-mails by following the instructions in the relevant correspondence.  Once Women Inventorz Network has processed your opt-out request, Women Inventorz Network will not send you further promotional e-mails unless you opt back in to receiving such communications.  Whether you opt out of receiving promotional e-mails or not, Women Inventorz Network reserves the right to communicate with you via e-mail with respect to important information, notices or changes arising out of or relating to the Women Inventorz Network Service and/or the Women Inventorz Network Websites.

c) Other Information. You may provide information whenever you interact with the Women Inventorz Network Service including, without limitation, when you search, upload, organize, create, buy, post, participate in a contest, survey or questionnaire, place an order, update information in your User Account, provide corporate, employer or other business information, communicate with Women Inventorz Network by phone, e-mail, or otherwise, use community features, and share information with other users. With any of these actions, you might supply Women Inventorz Network with information such as your name, address, and phone number, credit card information, third party information, alternate contact information, information about your use of the Women Inventorz Network Service and Women Inventorz Network Websites, personal descriptions, product descriptions, and in some cases, financial information including Social Security and driver's license numbers.

Information Collected Automatically or Through Technology. Women Inventorz Network may receive and store certain Personal Information and Non-Personal Information whenever you interact with Women Inventorz Network. While you are able to visit the Women Inventorz Network Websites and remain anonymous, Women Inventorz Network and/or its third party service providers may still collect Personal Information and Non-Personal Information through technological means. Such information may be gathered by the following methods:

a) Cookies. Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive which work by assigning to your computer a unique number that has no meaning outside of the Women Inventorz Network Websites. Cookies do not generally contain any Personal Information. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually configure your browser to prevent this. However, because cookies enable you to take advantage of some of the Women Inventorz Network Service features, Women Inventorz Network recommends that you leave them turned on or you may not have access or use of such features.

b) Metadata. Metadata is data collected and used by Women Inventorz Network to facilitate the understanding, characteristics, and management of data arising out of or relating to the Women Inventorz Network Service, to facilitate and monitor system usage and, in certain instances, for fraud prevention and other services. Some examples of information and metadata that Women Inventorz Network may collect and analyze include, without limitation, the Internet protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer to the Internet, computer and connection information (such as browser type, version, and time zone setting, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system, and platform), Women Inventorz Network Service usage history (which Women Inventorz Network may use to create new and improved features for the Women Inventorz Network Service), the full Uniform Resource Locator (URL) click sequence to, through, and from the Women Inventorz Network Websites, including date and time; cookie number; and any products, services or features you use or interact with arising out of or in connection with your use of the Women Inventorz Network Service.

c) Tools. Women Inventorz Network may use software tools to measure and collect information about your use of the Women Inventorz Network Service, including page response times, upload and file transfer errors, interaction information (such as scrolling, clicks, and mouse-overs), features use, and methods. Sometimes, you will have a choice whether or not to provide information, but if you fail to provide certain information it could make some features and/or functionality of the Women Inventorz Network Service unavailable to you. Women Inventorz Network will use information you provide to respond to your requests, communicate with you, and to improve the Women Inventorz Network Service.

Women Inventorz Network might receive information about you from Women Inventorz Network third party service providers, or other sources such as purchase or redemption information, Women Inventorz Network Service usage, payment information, reporting information page-view information, technical, fulfillment, advertising, search information and credit history information.
