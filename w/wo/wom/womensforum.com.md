# Privacy Policy - WomensForum.com

Privacy Policy

Womensforum.com (www.womensforum.com) is a premiere destination for Web entrepreneurs who want to join our network, and for women and girls on the Internet, looking for first-rate content sites to call their online home. Womensforum.com is a showcase for the content, personalities and utilities behind the womensforum.com Partner sites.

As we respect the privacy of individuals who visit our web site, we've designed this site with your privacy in mind. This policy describes how we obtain, use and disclose nonpublic personal information about you to others. You will be subject to the terms of this Policy if you register with us or use any of our services.

This Privacy Policy is subject to change from time to time. 

Customer Profile Protection

Unless otherwise noted in special promotions, womensforum.com generally gathers information about all of our users collectively and only uses these data in the aggregate. This information helps us determine how we can continually create a positive overall experience for our users. If this information is shared with our partners or sponsors, it is only in the aggregate, which enables them to better understand how womensforum.com users collectively use our site.

We may release subscriber data to responsible third parties for marketing purposes. Womensforum.com uses two third parties, 247Media and DoubleClick, to serve the advertisements that you see on the pages of our web site. If you would like to know more about the information gathering practices and "opt-out" procedures of our third party ad servers, please click on their respective names. 

Womensforum.com may research our users' demographics, interests, and behavior based on the information provided to us upon registration, during a promotion, from our server log files, or from surveys. We may do this to better understand and serve our users. This research is compiled and analyzed on an aggregated basis. Womensforum.com may share this aggregated data with advertisers or business partners.

In the "MyCenter" section of womensforum.com, you will be asked for some personal information, including your user name, password, first and last names, e-mail address, birth date, zip code and country. We may also request Optional Information including gender, marital status, and annual household income. This information is stored in a secure database. Once you register as a member, you will have a personal identification number and a password. 

In order to provide you with womensforum.com's exemplary services, products, and the benefits of our selected partnerships, womensforum.com often displays advertisements. By knowing our customer, womensforum.com is able to deliver focused content and advertising. 

Womensforum.com uses an opt-in system before sharing user information with anyone. You, of course, have the option of restricting the transfer. If you do not wish to allow your data to be shared, you can choose not to use a particular service or not to participate in certain promotions or contests.

We may seek information from you from time to time in connection with surveys, contests, promotions or special offer goods and services. Participation in these activities is completely voluntary on your part. The information requested may include contact information (such as name and address), demographic information (such as age and income level), preferences and interests. 

You may have the opportunity to receive materials or special offers from third parties. If you choose to opt-in to receive information from these third parties, Womensforum.com may share your name and e-mail address with them.

Partners and merchants listed in womensforum.com have separate privacy policies and data collection practices. We encourage you to read the privacy statement of every web site that collects personally identifiable information about you. When you click on a banner ad of a third party linked to our site, for example, you will leave our web site and will be interacting directly with the third party. That party may collect and use information about you in a way that is different from this Policy. Although the third party will not receive personally identifiable information about you from us (without your consent), it may know that you meet the criteria it sets for the distribution of its offers on our site.

Womensforum.com may disclose aggregated user statistics in order to describe our services to prospective partners, advertisers, and other third parties, and for other lawful purposes.

We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. We feel strongly about protecting the privacy of children. As such, we do not collect or use any personal information from children we know to be under the age of 13, and will remove personally identifying information about such minors from our system.

Our customers have the option of canceling their membership with womensforum.com at any time by sending an email to [unsubscribe@womensforum.com](mailto:unsubscribe@womensforum.com).

We may note your IP address when you visit our site. An IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer when you use the Internet. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information. We use IP addresses to analyze trends, administer our web site, track user movements, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. We collect aggregate (anonymous) information on persons who access our site so that we can keep it up-to-date, useful and relevant to visitors. 

"Cookies" are files with information that is stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Womensforum.com uses cookies only to keep track of user I.D. information. 

Womensforum.com may disclose information in special cases when we have reason to believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact, or bring legal action against someone who may be violating womensforum.com's terms of use or causing injury to or interfering with womensforum.com's rights, property, other users, or anyone else. 

Womensforum.com may disclose or access account information when we believe in good faith that the law requires it and for administrative and other purposes that we deem necessary to maintain, service, and improve our service to you. 

By using our web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by womensforum.com. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post the revised policy on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we will disclose it. If you do not want to receive opt-in e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail at [unsubscribe@womensforum.com](mailto:unsubscribe@womensforum.com).

Your womensforum.com ID is password protected. Only you have access to your personal information. Cautiously guard your password. 

Please be aware that no data transmitted over the Internet is guaranteed to be 100% secure. Thus, womensforum.com cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or information that is transmitted to you from our products, services or partners. Sometimes the information you disclose can be collected and used by others. If you post personal information that is accessible to the public, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return.
