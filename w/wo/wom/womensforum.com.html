Dear Viewers,
I was up the late the other night, trying to deal with a throbbing toothache and literally counting the minutes until my ibuprofen kicked in. While I was cursing myself for not calling my dentist earlier in the day and having to wait till morning to do so, a minor miracle occurred—I flipped on the TV and there was one of my all time favorite chick flicks: You’ve Got Mail.

I’ve seen this movie so many times that I can literally recite most of the dialogue (a habit that drives my husband crazy when I proceed to do so, the minute he sits down to watch with me). Of course, this isn’t really a problem, because after two seconds, he promptly asks for the remote (to find some testosterone-laden football or anything as far from Meg Ryan/Tom Hanks as possible). Since I was proclaiming life-ending pain (toothache, remember?) he gave up the fight for (remote) control and simply left me to watch the movie by myself.

Bliss!
