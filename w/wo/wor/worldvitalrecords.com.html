Privacy Policy

Last updated December 28, 2007

Thank you for placing your trust in us and in our continued work to bring you global information as we steadily acquire, process and post records to our site. We value this trust and your privacy as you grow with us. We, at WorldVitalRecords.com, won't rent, sell or trade your personal information or email address to any third party. We use your information only for your subscription and/or the newsletter services as described here in our Privacy Policy. We regard the protection of your privacy as an important part of our business.

You can unsubscribe for any reason at any time to the Web site or the newsletter. If you ever have a problem with your subscription to the Web site or the newsletter, feel free to contact our Customer Service personnel at feedback@worldvitalrecords.com. You can unsubscribe to newsletter at unsubscribe@worldvitalrecords.com. If you wish to cancel your subscription at World Vital Records, please contact us by phone at 1-888-377-0588 between the hours of 8-5 p.m. (Mountain Standard Time). If you live outside the United States, please dial the international country code for your country, and then dial 1-888-377-0588.

If you are a subscriber to our services, it is your responsibility to keep your password confidential. If you are sharing a computer or are using a computer in a public area, you should log out before leaving our Web site in order to protect your information from other users.

If at any time during your first 30 days of membership you do not get your money's worth simply call us on our toll free number at 1-888-377-0588 and request a refund. The sale of magazine subscriptions downloads, or other electronic, digital, or physical products are final. The amount refunded will be 50% of the purchase price to cover the cost of the World Vital Records membership.
