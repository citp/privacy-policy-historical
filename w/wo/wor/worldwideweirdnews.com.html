Privacy Policy for WWWN

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at support@worldwideweirdnews.com.

At WWWN, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by WWWN and how it is used.

Log Files
Like many other Web sites, WWWN makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons
WWWN does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

Cookies and Web Beacons
WWWN does not use cookies.

DoubleClick DART Cookie
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on WWWN.
Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to WWWN and other sites on the Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL - http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

WWWN has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.
