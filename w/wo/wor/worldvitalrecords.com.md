# Privacy Policy

Last updated November 2, 2007 

Thank you for placing your trust in us and in our continued work to bring you global information as we steadily acquire, process and post records to our site. We value this trust and your privacy as you grow with us. We, at WorldVitalRecords.com, won't rent, sell or trade your personal information or email address to any third party. We use your information only for your subscription and/or the newsletter services as described here in our Privacy Policy. We regard the protection of your privacy as an important part of our business. 

You can unsubscribe for any reason at any time to the Web site or the newsletter. If you ever have a problem with your subscription to the Web site or the newsletter, feel free to contact our Customer Service personnel at [feedback@worldvitalrecords.com](mailto:feedback@worldvitalrecords.com). You can unsubscribe to newsletter at [unsubscribe@worldvitalrecords.com](mailto:unsubscribe@worldvitalrecords.com). If you wish to cancel your subscription at World Vital Records, please contact us by phone at 1-888-377-0588 between the hours of 8-5 p.m. (Mountain Standard Time). If you live outside the United States, please dial the international country code for your country, and then dial 1-888-377-0588. 

If you are a subscriber to our services, it is your responsibility to keep your password confidential. If you are sharing a computer or are using a computer in a public area, you should log out before leaving our Web site in order to protect your information from other users. 

We are so sure that you will get valuable genealogy information and family history help with your World Vital Records subscription that we offer a 30 day money back guarantee. If at anytime during your first 30 days of membership you do not get your money's worth simply call us on our toll free number at 1-888-377-0588 and request a refund. We will gladly refund your total membership amount. Simple as that! This money back guarantee applies to all World Vital Records membership plans and does not apply to any shipped products.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit some pages of our Web site. **Changes to This Privacy Policy**

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our services. When we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. Please review this Privacy Policy from time to time. If you are concerned about how we are protecting your information, please contact us at World Vital Records, Inc., 1234 North 900 East, Provo, Utah, 84604, 888-377-0588.
