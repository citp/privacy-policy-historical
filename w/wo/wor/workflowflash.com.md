# Privacy Policy

We here at :psb_blogtitle: greatly respect and value your privacy. Here are the guidelines we follow to ensure your privacy and our integrity: 

### Site Usage Statistics

Anonymous site usage statistics are used to track a user’s activity while he or she moves throughout :psb_blogdomain:. These statistics are analyzed in order to understand and improve the information presented on :psb_blogdomain: and the user experience of :psb_blogdomain:. Site usage data is not _linked to any personally identifiable information_ and cannot be used to identify you.

### Links and External Content

This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that :psb_blogdomain: is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this website. 

### Conclusion

Additionally, :psb_blogdomain: will never sell or transmit e-mail addresses or any other personal identifiers to 3rd parties. Use of the registration system is optional; however it does maximize the user’s experience here at the site. 

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, please do not hesitate to contact us! Thank you for your cooperation.
