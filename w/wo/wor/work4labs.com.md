# Privacy | Work4 Labs

**1.             Privacy Policy**

1.1          Work4 Labs collect the email addresses of those who communicate with us via email, aggregate information on what web pages users access or visit (log data), and information volunteered by users (such as survey information, account registration information, and all user‑submitted and -posted material).

1.2          When you subscribe to a paid account, you will be required to provide additional payment-related information to us and/or our payment processing partners. This information may include your credit card number or other payment method information, your full name, your billing address and phone number, and other information necessary to process your subscription billing transactions.

1.3          The information Work4 Labs collects is used to provide the Services to its users and to improve the quality and user experience of the Services. Work4 Labs does not share or sell user information with others except for the purpose of providing the Services, when it has your express permission, or when it becomes necessary to share such information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of these Terms, or as otherwise required by law.

1.4          Information collected by our payment processing partners will be used according to their privacy policy or policies, which will be made available to you at an address provided to you at the time of subscription.

**2.             Changes to the Terms and Privacy Policy**

Work4 Labs reserves the right to update and change the Terms and the above Privacy Policy from time to time without notice. Continued use of the Services after any such changes shall constitute your consent to such changes. You can review the most current version of the Terms at any time at www.work4labs.com/privacy
