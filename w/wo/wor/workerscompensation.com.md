Information for the WorkersCompensation.com website is gathered in two methods:   
**First Method:** Information for the website is gathered through tracking of general online website statistics; for example, how many visitors to the site, which pages were viewed, what files accessed, etc. While certain features of the site may employ "cookies" to better facilitate services, at no time is any personal or specific information about the visitor collected without specific knowledge or awareness of the visitor knowingly providing it. 

**Second Method:** Information for the website is gathered through direct interaction with the specific visitor to the site; for example, completion and direct submission of an online form, email from the visitor to WorkersCompensation.com, etc.
