# Your Privacy - World Golf

**Your Privacy**

Worldgolf has a commitment to your privacy while visiting our site. 

Any information submitted to us by forms or email will only be for the use of Worldgolf. Worldgolf will not sell or distribute any personal information that you have included in correspondence or submissions. While you will may receive an e-mail confirmation from some of your submissions, you will not receive any unsolicited e-mail from others as a result. 

Worldgolf does provide external links that will take you away from our site to other golf related internet sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these sites. 

Should you have any questions regarding our personal privacy policy, please feel free to e-mail us at [privacy@worldgolf.com](mailto:privacy@worldgolf.com). 

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. Our third-party advertising company also separately places or recognizes a cookie file on your browser in the course of delivering advertisements to this Site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please click here. <http://www.sonar.com/content/privacy.htm>
