# World of Chat Privacy Statement

World of Chat has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: http://www.worldofchat.co.uk

## Advertisers

We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we do not use cookies ourselves, cookies received with banner ads may be collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information.

## Personal Information

We request information via 2 ways 1 via registration to the chat room and 2 via the forums, the information gathered via both these areas will not be passed onto anyone, it is stored securely. You will not be emailed with spam emails.

## External Links

This site contains links to other sites. World of Chat is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

## Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. It is hosted on professionally administered servers in a secure facility. We also have adequate physical security on the computers on which we create the site to protect our data there.
