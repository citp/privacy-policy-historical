# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used on this Website. Please see our Terms and Conditions of Site Use for additional information applicable to use of our website. 

1\. How is the personal information collected? 

[www.way2k.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20141216073627id_/http%3A//way2k.com/default.aspx) uses the personal information from you only if you provide it voluntarily. This includes your economic details, contact details, academic details, personal details, current qualification, future interest in the course, personalized alerts. You might also enter another person’s email address and name when you use our ‘Invite your friend’ feature. This information is passed to the institutes and organizations of your interest and benefit. The kind of information that is gathered automatically includes the type of browser you are using (e.g., Netscape, Internet Explorer), the type of operating system you are using, (e.g., Windows '95 or Mac OS) and the domain name of your Internet service provider (e.g., America Online, EarthLink). All such usage data shall be the exclusive property of this way2k.com used for their research, analysis and as statistical presentation. 

2\. How does way2k.com use the information? 

We store the information provided by you in our highly confidential database further used to provide personalized alert services like e-mails, newsletters, SMS alerts and telephone calls to you. 

3\. To whom is the personal information disclosed? 

way2k.com will provide your personal information to selected third-party entities such as Institutes or organizations only under special circumstances and only with the approval of the end user which will be beneficial for you and your area of interest. These institutes or organizations may be a tie-up or partner of way2k.com. 

4\. Security measures taken by way2k.com to protect the information? 

way2k.com understands that storing your information in a secure manner is important. way2k.com stores general and personally identifiable information using industry standard physical, technical and administrative safeguards to secure data against foreseeable risks, such as unauthorized use, access, disclosure, destruction or modification. 

5\. Disclosure to third parties assisting in our information? 

way2k.com may provide your personal information to its knowledge partner’s tie-ups with institutes or organization, working with way2k.com under confidential agreements. These respective bodies may use your personal information for education related services or communicate to you the offers or other information relevant to their area of interest and benefit with the consent of the end user.
