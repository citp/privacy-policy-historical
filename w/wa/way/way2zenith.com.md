# WordPress for Business - Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy 

We understand the importance of keeping your personal information private. We set out our privacy practices and policies in this Privacy Policy. You do not need to give us any information to browse our website, but when you email us you are providing us with various amounts of personal information including, but not limited to, your email address and name.

Your Personal Information may be passed to and used by our partners or contracted providers in connection with providing services to you over our website and delivering or making our services available to you.

We may also disclose your Personal Information in the following instances:

  * if we are required to do so by applicable law or under a court or similar order;
  * as deemed necessary, in our discretion, to protect our legal rights.



### Hyperlinks to other web sites 

This website contains hyperlinks to websites owned and operated by third parties which are beyond our control. These other sites have their own privacy policies and we urge you to review them. We do not accept any responsibility or liability for the privacy practices of such third party websites and your use of such websites is at your own risk.

Please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/contact.htm) if you have any questions about this privacy policy.
