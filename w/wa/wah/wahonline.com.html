Our Commitment to You

We at ListedinEurope.com  respect your privacy.
We do collect your information but only if you ask to be added to our newsletter or request to be added for updates to new products and services that we offer.  We never share your information in anyway it is only for our internal use.

When you request ListedinEurope.com services, the information you provide to us is used only to provide our services, to deliver email notifications to you (or to people you designate as your recipients), and from time to time, to validate your identity. Although a certain limited amount of information is required for us to be able to serve you, our data collection policies otherwise allow you to choose how much information you provide to us.

By visiting Listedineurope.com, you accept the policies set forth in this Privacy Policy.

The Information We Collect

The information we collect allows us to provide you with the services we offer. Information collected online is typically defined as being either Anonymous or Personally Identifiable:

Anonymous (or Technical) Information means information that we can't identify with a specific individual. For instance, we know that thousands of people have visited this privacy policy page, but we do not necessarily know their names, where they live, or their e-mail addresses. Also, some of our pages collect technical information about your session (such as browser type, ISP, referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, number of clicks, domain name and country/state). This information may be used to analyze trends, administer our website and track usage of our website. What we collect from interactions such as this constitutes "anonymous information." Visiting the ListedinEurope.com website will also cause a ListedinEurope.com cookie to be issued. This cookie allows ListedinEurope.com to gather such anonymous or technical information.
Personally Identifiable Information means information that tells us something specific about an individual, such as their name, email address or phone number. We need this type of information to provide the personalized services that you have requested. We hope that, like many of our customers, you will find it beneficial to provide Personally Identifiable Information to ListedinEurope.com so that we may provide our valuable services to you.

Third Party Advertisers

We allow outside companies to advertise on certain sections of our web site. We select product and service providers that are responsible and afford privacy protections to their customers. These third parties serve their ads from their own web sites, independently of ListedinEurope.com. As such, they may use cookies or request personal information from you, or they may share anonymous information about your visit with third parties. Please be aware that any such third parties would be separate entities from ListedinEurope.com, and that we would not be responsible for the practices employed by websites linked to or from our website, nor the information or content contained in such sites, and we may not be held liable in the event they violate their own or our privacy policy. In the course of serving ads to this site, a third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.

Information Distribution

If you are providing us Personally Identifiable Information, it will be used within ListedinEurope.com's system to deliver our services. ListedinEurope.com may also be required to provide Personally Identifiable Information to comply with valid legal requirements such as law, regulation, warrant, subpoena or court order.

ListedinEurope.com offers you the ability to control the email communications we make to you. It is our intention to give you as much control over our relationship as possible. ListedinEurope.com gives you the opportunity to unsubscribe from general email communication from us and our affiliates. Clear directions for doing so will appear in the email messages we send to you. At times, ListedinEurope.com may enter into co-branding arrangements with other companies, and if so, an opt-in or opt-out question may be provided to allow email contact from these companies as well. Their opt-in/out features are specific to each organization, but you should not receive email from these organizations unless you have indicated that it is acceptable. Before consenting to receive email from third parties, you should read their privacy policy and terms of use, if applicable, to make sure you are comfortable dealing with that company. ListedinEurope.com cannot make commitments on behalf of other companies. All email from ListedinEurope.com affiliates who gain your permission to send you commercial email will include an opportunity to opt out of future communications. ListedinEurope.com reserves the right to send a one-time registration confirmation email to its customers to inform them of service changes.

Security Procedures

ListedinEurope.com operates secure data networks protected by industry-standard firewall and password protection systems. Our security and privacy policies are periodically reviewed and enhanced as necessary, and only authorized individuals have access to the information provided by our customers.

Policy Change

If we alter our privacy policy, any changes will be posted on this page of our Site so that you are informed of the information we collect about you, how we use it, and the circumstances under which we may disclose it. Please check back from time to time to ensure you are aware of our current privacy policy.
