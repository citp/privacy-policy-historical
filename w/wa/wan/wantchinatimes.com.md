# WantChinaTimes.com

For site management, this computer system uses software programs to create summary statistics that are used for such purposes as assessing what information is of most or least interest, determining technical design specifications, and identifying system performance areas. The WantChinaTimes website collects and stores the following information: 

  * The name of the domain from which you access the Internet
  * The date and time you access our site
  * The pages you peruse and files you access
  * The Internet address of the Web site from which you linked directly to our site



If you choose to provide us with personal information, as in an e-mail message, the WantChinaTimes website will use this information to respond to your request. There are times when your e-mail is forwarded within the WantChinaTimes website to employees who are better able to assist you. The WantChinaTimes website may also forward requests for assistance to another appropriate institution for response. Except for the foregoing exceptions and for authorized law enforcement activities, the WantChinaTimes website does not share e-mail with outside organizations without obtaining your permission.

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on WantChinaTimes.com Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to WantChinaTimes.com users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
