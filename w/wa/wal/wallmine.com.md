# Privacy Policy | wallmine

If you have questions about deleting or correcting your personal data please contact our support at [hello@wallmine.com](mailto:hello@wallmine.com)

HODL ltd operates a website wallmine.com. 

It is wallmine.com’s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.

  


### Website visitors

Like most website operators, wallmine.com collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. wallmine.com may also collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. wallmine.com's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how wallmine.com’s visitors use its website. From time to time, wallmine.com may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website. 

### Gathering of personally identifying information

Certain visitors to wallmine.com’s websites choose to interact with wallmine.com in ways that require wallmine.com to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that wallmine.com gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up for an account at wallmine.com to provide an email address. Those who engage in transactions with wallmine.com – by purchasing access to the premium services, for example – are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, wallmine.com collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with wallmine.com. wallmine.com does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities. 

### Protection of certain personally identifying information

wallmine.com discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that 

(i) need to know that information in order to process it on wallmine.com’s behalf or to provide services available at wallmine.com’s websites, and 

(ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. 

Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using wallmine.com’s websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. wallmine.com will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, wallmine.com discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information wallmine.com in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or when wallmine.com believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of wallmine.com, third parties or the public at large. If you are a registered user of an wallmine.com website and have supplied your email address, wallmine.com may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new features, solicit your feedback, or just keep you up to date with what’s going on with wallmine.com and our products. We primarily use our various other channels to communicate this type of information, so we expect to keep this type of email to a minimum. If you send us a request (for example via a support email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. wallmine.com takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information. 

### Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. wallmine.com uses cookies to help wallmine.com identify and track visitors, their usage of wallmine.com website, and their website access preferences. wallmine.com visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using wallmine.com’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of wallmine.com’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies. 

wallmine.com uses Google Analytics features based on Display Advertising. Visitors can opt out from this at any time. 

### Business transfers

If wallmine.com, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, or in the unlikely event that wallmine.com goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of wallmine.com may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy. 

### Privacy policy changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, wallmine.com may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in wallmine.com’s sole discretion. wallmine.com encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
