# Privacy Policy | Wall Street Oasis

This site is owned and operated by IB Oasis Corp. ("Company"). Your privacy on the Internet is of the utmost importance to us. At WallStreetOasis.com, we want to make your experience online satisfying and safe.

Because we gather certain types of information about our users, we feel you should fully understand our policy and the terms and conditions surrounding the capture and use of that information. This privacy statement discloses what information we gather and how we use it.

INFORMATION IB Oasis Corp. GATHERS AND TRACKS

The Company gathers two types of information about users:

Information that users provide through optional, voluntary submissions. These are voluntary submissions to receive our electronic newsletters, to participate in our message boards or forums, to participate in our Company Reviews, Interview Insights or Compensation Data, to email a friend, and from participation in polls and surveys:

Information the Company gathers through aggregated tracking information derived mainly by tallying page views throughout our sites. This information allows us to better tailor our content to readers' needs and to help our advertisers and sponsors better understand the demographics of our audience. Because the Company derives its revenue mainly from sponsorships and advertising, providing such aggregated demographic data is essential to keeping our service free to users. Under no circumstances does the Company divulge any information about an individual user to a third party.

Additionally, IB Oasis Corp. uses Google to collect visitor data on our site and then serve targeted display ads to you on other sites.
