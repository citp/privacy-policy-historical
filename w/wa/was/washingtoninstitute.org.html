 Privacy/Security Policy

The information we gather and receive from our customers helps us personalize and continually improve your shopping experience at the Bookstore. We receive and store certain information you enter on our website or provide to us in other ways. We use this information for purposes such as processing and fulfilling your orders, communicating with you and improving the Bookstore.


COOKIES

Like many websites, we use “cookies” to keep track of the items you've added to your cart as you're shopping. Cookies are small text files written into the browser’s memory or hard drive for this specific purpose. Each server can only read the files it left behind in a particular browser. The information stored in the cookies does not reveal any detail about your store access, purchase, or any other customer information.

You can examine and delete the cookie files on your computer at any time. The Help portion of the toolbar on most browsers will tell you how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie, or how to disable cookies altogether.


PERSONAL INFORMATION AND PRIVACY

Financial Information. Your personal financial information and credit card details are never revealed to any third party except our bank and yours to the extent necessary to process and complete your transaction.

E-Mail Addresses. We collect email addresses when a customer purchases a book from us online only to facilitate orders. We don’t sell, trade, or share email addresses with third parties.

Other Personal Information. In connection with processing your order, we may collect other personal information about you, such as your address. For example, when a customer purchases a book from us, his or her name and street address are stored in our order processing system, and the customer may receive follow-up mailings from us with information on their order.

Children. We do not sell products to children. Accordingly, we do not collect information about children.

Legal Compliance and Fraud Prevention. We release account and other personal information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law or to enforce our terms of use. We may also exchange information with other organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction.


TYPES OF INFORMATION WE COLLECT

You provide personal information to us when you search, buy, or communicate with us through the Bookstore. For example, you provide information when you search for a product, place an order through the Bookstore, or provide information about yourself.

We also collect certain information automatically when you visit the Bookstore. Examples of the information we may automatically collect and analyze include your purchase history, the internet protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer to the internet, and information about your computer and connection such as browser type and version, operating system, and platform.

Finally, we may receive and store information about you that we receive from third parties. Examples of information we may receive from third parties include delivery information from our carriers and financial information from banks. We use this third party information only to maintain our records, process orders, and deliver your purchase.


SECURITY

Orders placed in this online bookstore are secured using the standard Web encryption technology called Secure Sockets Layer (SSL). The personal information you provide and your credit card information are transmitted in encrypted form, never in clear text. This ensures that any information traveling between your browser and the web server is protected from being read by those who might attempt to intercept the transmission. The identity of our Bookstore site is vouched for by digital certificates issued by Pair Networks.


TERMS OF USE, NOTICES, and REVISIONS

If you choose to visit the Bookstore, your visit and any dispute over privacy is subject to this Privacy/Security Policy and our terms of use, including limitations on damages and the resolution of disputes. If you have any concern about privacy at the Bookstore, please contact us with a thorough description, and we will try to resolve it. 


SAFEGUARDING YOUR PRIVACY

In spite of the precautions we take to protect your information, it is equally important for you to protect against unauthorized access to your personal information, financial information, passwords, and computer. Keep all information stored in a safe place, do not provide your personal information to anyone else, and be sure to sign off when finished using a shared computer.

The Federal Trade Commission provides useful information about online privacy on its own website, Site Seeing on the Internet.


CHANGES AND UPDATES

The Washington Institute for Near East Policy reserves the right to modify or amend this policy at any time by posting the revised policy on our website. If you have any questions regarding our Privacy/Security Policy, please contact us.
