# Privacy Policy - WholesaleSuppliesPlus

Privacy Policy

2\. Sharing of Information

Wholesale Supplies Plus believes that confidential information should remain confidential. We will never sell your Personally-Identifiable Information to third parties or other companies. However, we may share your Personally-Identifiable Information with our corporate affiliates, including without limitation Crafters Choice Brands, LLC and Handmade Studio, LLC, as well as with our trusted third party service providers, to allow for integration of their services with ours and to provide you with better services on our behalf. Such sharing of your Personally-Identifiable Information may occur for the following purposes, without limitation: (i) Processing of your credit card payments; (ii) Communicating with you via email; (iii) Managing customer relationships; (iv) Performing analysis of our services or those of our third party service providers; or (v) Performing analysis of customer demographics. Please note, while we do not store your credit card payment information, our third party credit card processor may do so. 

In addition, we may share Non-Personally Identifiable Information, such as aggregate information, with our trusted third party service providers, to allow for integration of their services with ours and to provide you with better services on our behalf, as well as with State and Federal legislators to the extent that such information is needed to advocate for small businesses in the soap and cosmetics industries. 

3\. Security of Information

The Website has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and/or alteration of the information under our control. The security of your Personally-Identifiable Information is important to us. We use suitable, industry-standard physical, electronic, technical, administrative, and managerial procedures and safeguards to protect against unauthorized use, disclosure, alteration, and/or destruction of the Personally-Identifiable Information we collect from users on our Website. You should keep in mind, however, that no data transmitted over the Internet is 100% secure and any information disclosed online can potentially be collected and used by parties other than the intended recipient. As a result, while we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee or warrant the security of any information you transmit to or from our Website. 

4\. Choice to Opt-Out

You may opt out of receiving communications from us, including without limitation marketing and promotional materials, on the online form that we use to request information from you. The Website also provides a form, located here, which gives you the option to not receive future communications from us (including without limitation marketing and promotional materials), or to no longer receive our services. Please note, however, that even if you opt out, we will retain Personally-Identifiable Information sufficient to contact you in the event of a product recall or a product safety issue, as permitted and required under applicable law, and we will also maintain your order confirmations and back order and shipment tracking records. 

5\. Disclosures of Information Required by Law

If at any time Wholesale Supplies Plus is required by law, regulation, subpoena, court order, or other valid legal process or requirement to release any information, including without limitation your Personally-Identifiable Information, we will fully cooperate with legal authorities in doing so. Likewise, we reserve the right to disclose your Personally-Identifiable Information to the extent necessary to protect our rights or safety, or the rights or safety of others. In addition, we may share your Personally-Identifiable Information with our credit card processors, banks, and shipping contractors if required to investigate fraud or credible allegations of fraud. 

6\. Links to Other Sites

The Website may contain links to other sites that are not owned or controlled by Wholesale Supplies Plus. Please be aware that Wholesale Supplies Plus is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such other sites, and we will not incur any liability therefor. We encourage you to be aware when you leave our Website, because when you do, this Policy is no longer applicable. We further encourage you to read the privacy policies of each and every website, as the privacy policy of such website may differ from ours. 

7\. United States Only

This Website is intended for use by residents of the United States of America only. All matters relating to this Website are governed by the laws of the State of Ohio and the United States of America, as applicable. If you are located outside of the United States of America and you contact us, please be advised that any information you provide to us will be transferred to the United States of America and that by submitting information to our Website, you explicitly authorize such transfer. Further, as of April 27, 2018, this Website is expressly no longer intended for use by customers or potential customers in EU member states, because, due to the high cost of complying with the EU GDPR, we have discontinued all marketing, services, and sales to customers and potential customers in EU member states. Accordingly, if you are in the EU, please do not use or access the Website, and do not submit any information (whether Personally-Identifiable or Non-Personally-Identifiable) to it. 

8\. Business Transition

In the event we go through a business transition, including without limitation a merger with or acquisition by another company, or a sale of all of or a portion of our assets, your Personally-Identifiable Information will likely be among the assets transferred to the acquiring entity. In addition, if any bankruptcy or reorganization proceeding is brought by or against us, your Personally-Identifiable Information may be considered an asset of ours and may be sold or transferred to third parties. If any of the foregoing occurs, this Policy may no longer be applicable to your Personally-Identifiable Information. 

9\. A Special Note to Parents

We respect the privacy of all of our online users, especially children. This Website is not directed or targeted to, or intended for use by, children under the age of 13. By proceeding to access and use the Website, you represent and warrant to us that you are over the age of 13. **_If you are 13 years old or younger, you are not authorized to access or use the Website, and in that case, please do not proceed._** Furthermore, we do not intend to collect Personally-Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. Our Policy is intended to adhere to the Children's Advertising Review Unit (CARU) privacy guidelines and the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We encourage parents to monitor, supervise, and join their children in online activities.

We reserve the right, in our sole discretion, to change, modify, update, or replace this Policy at any time and for any reason. Please check back from time to time to ensure that you are aware of any changes or updates to this Policy. We will indicate the Policy’s effective date on the first page hereof. Your continued access to or use of the Website after such changes or replacement indicates your acceptance of and agreement to such changes or replacement. If you disagree with any changes to or replacement of this Policy, please cease your access to and use of our Website immediately.
