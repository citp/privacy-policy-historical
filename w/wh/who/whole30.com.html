Cuban Grilled Zucchini

It’s Michelle from the the Whole Smiths with the PERFECT summer Whole30 recipe. Grilling and summer squash? I mean, helllllo does it GET any more summer than that. I didn't think so.

We already have a million zucchinis from our garden so I’m thinking this will be a staple all summer long. It’s crazy easy and delicious. Check out more awesome ideas on the Whole30 Recipes page!

Ingredients:
3 zucchinis cut into spears
1/2 C. olive oil or melted ghee
1 Tbsp. paprika
2 Tbsp. chili powder
1 tsp. salt
Lime
1/3 C. chopped cilantro

Directions:
In a small bowl combine oil/ghee with paprika, chili powder and salt and combine
Brush oil mix all ove zucchini
Grill until tender and slightly charred and remove from gill
Squeeze lime over top and add chopped cilantro prior to serving
