# Privacy Policy from China Wholesale Clothing Distributors

Welcome to WholesaleClothing4U and we appreciate your trust in us. By visiting [www.wholesaleclothing4u.com](http://www.wholesaleclothing4u.com/), you accept and consent to below privacy policy:

  * When you make order(s) on this website, we will ask you to input personal information such as your name, e-mail address, billing address, delivery address, telephone number, product selections and payment information, etc. Please ensure that the details you provide to us are correct and complete, some of the information can be updated by yourself after sign-in.

  * After creating an account on our website, you agree to receive information from us by e-mail about products, promotions or special offers which we feel may be of interest to you.

  * You are responsible to take all necessary steps to ensure that your account and password are kept confidential and secure.  You agree to accept responsibility for all activities that occur under your account or password.

  * To prevent online fraud case, we may ask you to unify your addresses if you place an order with a different billing and shipping addresses. Or we may ask you to prove validity of billing address by sending us copy of proofs of your billing address i.e. utility bills, driving license, etc.

  * To help us to improve the quality of our service, our system may collect some information about your visit (E.g. the URL you came from, IP address, your browser type, the pages of our website that were viewed during your visit, and any search terms that you entered on our website, etc.)

  * WholesaleClothing4U reserves the right to refuse your access to the website, terminate your account, remove or edit content or cancel your orders at our discretion.​
