Protecting your private information is our priority. This Statement of Privacy applies to the www.wholelifechallenge.com and Whole Life Challenge, Inc. and governs data collection and usage. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, unless otherwise noted, all references to Whole Life Challenge, Inc. include www.wholelifechallenge.com. The Whole Life Challenge, Inc. website is a Social Fitness site. By using the Whole Life Challenge, Inc. website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Collection of your Personal Information

Whole Life Challenge, Inc. may collect personally identifiable information, such as your name. If you purchase Whole Life Challenge, Inc.'s products and services, we collect billing and credit card information. This information is used to complete the purchase transaction. Whole Life Challenge, Inc. may also collect anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your age and Health statistics. We may gather additional personal or non-personal information in the future.

Information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by Whole Life Challenge, Inc.. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. This information is used for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the Whole Life Challenge, Inc. website.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through Whole Life Challenge, Inc.'s public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others.

Whole Life Challenge, Inc. encourages you to review the privacy statements of websites you choose to link to from Whole Life Challenge, Inc. so that you can understand how those websites collect, use and share your information. Whole Life Challenge, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on websites outside of the Whole Life Challenge, Inc. website.

Use of your Personal Information

Whole Life Challenge, Inc. collects and uses your personal information to operate its website(s) and deliver the services you have requested.

Whole Life Challenge, Inc. may also use your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Whole Life Challenge, Inc. and its affiliates. Whole Life Challenge, Inc. may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

Whole Life Challenge, Inc. does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties.

Whole Life Challenge, Inc. may share data with trusted partners to help perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third￼parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Whole Life Challenge, Inc., and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Whole Life Challenge, Inc. will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Whole Life Challenge, Inc. or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Whole Life Challenge, Inc.; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Whole Life Challenge, Inc., or the public.

Security of your Personal Information

Whole Life Challenge, Inc. secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other websites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) protocol.

Children Under Thirteen

Whole Life Challenge, Inc. does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of thirteen. If you are under the age of thirteen, you must ask your parent or guardian for permission to use this website.

Opt-Out & Unsubscribe

We respect your privacy and give you an opportunity to opt-out of receiving announcements of certain information. Users may opt-out of receiving any or all communications from Whole Life Challenge, Inc. by contacting us here: info@wholelifechallenge.com

Whole Life Challenge, Inc. will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. Whole Life Challenge, Inc. encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how Whole Life Challenge, Inc. is protecting your information.
