Whois.com is committed to principles of internet security and respect for your privacy. This privacy policy explains why and when your corporate/personal information is collected and why this information is needed by Whois.com or a third party.

Your corporate/personal information
In order to register a domain name at Whois.com you are required to provide us with corporate/personal information by way of an electronic online form. To register a domain name corporate/personal information is required by the relevant registry of the TLD (top level domain) for which you apply to register a domain name through Whois.com. This information may include but not be limited to company name, full name, mailing address, email address, contact information for the account holder, registrant, billing, administrative, technical contacts and your credit card details.

Security
In order to protect the security of corporate/personal information and credit card details provided by you through our Web site, our Web servers support the use of the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) Protocol where appropriate. Using this protocol your credit card details are encrypted when transmitted to the bank.

*Note: Whois.com does not track or store credit card details of its customers.

Use of your information by third parties or as required by the relevant TLD registries.
The Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers, the international governing body for TLDs (Top Level Domains) requires that registrars must make available the corporate/personal information collected from you when you apply to register a domain name to the public through the publicly accessible database known as the Whois Database. This is a public policy adopted by the domain name industry as a whole.

On occasion, Whois.com may also engage third party companies or individuals to perform services on our behalf. Our relationships with such third party companies are governed by our contracts with them. Those companies are required to hold your personal information strictly confidential.

Use of your information by Whois.com
We will use your personal information to advise you of any information relating to the services provided to you through whois.com. This includes but is not limited to notices for renewal of services, of system upgrades or any information relevant to you. Whois.com limits access to personal information about you to employees who Whois.com believes reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide services to you or in order to do their jobs. If you do not wish to receive notices or other communications from Whois.com regarding additional product and service information, simply send an email to unsubscribe@whois.com.

Corrections or changes to your corporate/personal information
You have the ability to correct or change certain information in our records, such as your address and contact information. The process for changing your information begins at www. whois.com. You may change this information at any time and as often as necessary. If you need assistance or have questions about correcting information, you can contact us via email at info@whois.com.

*Note: Whois.com may make changes to this privacy policy at anytime, so it is your responsibility to check our privacy policy from time to time on Whois.com website.

If you have any question or comment, please contact us at info@whois.com.
