# Web Design Privacy

Any information you provide us with either through email or forms on this site will only be used in the following ways: 

  * In order to contact you 
  * To deliver the services you require
  * To disclose information if required by law



Your information will not be passed onto any third parties without your permission with the exception of our affiliated partners where IT equipment, photography and printing services are required.
