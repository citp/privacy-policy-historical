PRIVACY POLICY


White Fox Boutique Pty Ltd strives to ensure security, integrity and privacy of personally identifiable information of its customers. White Fox Boutique Pty Ltd uses a variety of physical and electronic security measures to keep personal information secure from misuse, loss or unauthorised access, modifications or disclosure, including restricting physical access to its offices, firewalls and secure databases. Our website www.whitefoxboutique.com uses Secure Socket Layer technology (SSL) encryption to protect all your personal information and credit card details. We follow all PCI-DSS requirements & industry standard encryptions.  

White Fox Boutique Pty Ltd does not & will not sell any of your personal data to any third party. However data is shared with the following categories of companies as an essential part to being able to provide our services to you.

-       Professional companies responsible for dispatch & returns such as the delivery companies chosen by you at checkout.

-       Professional Payment Services approved by you such as AfterPay once chosen at checkout to use their payment service.

-       Professional service providers such as our advertising & marketing partners

-       Professional analytic tool providers

-       Law enforcement, NAB, AMEX & PayPal (fraud prevention & chargebacks), so we can help tackle fraud for any disputed purchases.

 


HOW WE COLLECT YOUR INFORMATION

There are many ways that White Fox Boutique Pty Ltd obtains information from you:

- via email signup on www.whitefoxboutique.com website either at any point on the website located at the email gate or footer or at checkout & creating an account

- through out of stock notification sign-up on a selected product.  Please note that email subscription sign-up can be de-selected at time whether creating an account or signing up for a restock update.

- navigational information about where visitors go on our website, analytics & tracking

- upon signing up at an event. 

 


INFORMATION PROVIDED DURING CHECKOUT

White Fox Boutique Pty Ltd ensures that any personal information that you provide during the registration and checkout process is kept confidential. Upon checkout & placing an order you may save your address & information to your account for the purposes of faster checkout. Information provided at checkout will be used for;

-       To deliver your order to you

-       Send order update notifications via e-mail

-       To send you shipment update SMS

-       Fraud prevention & detection to protect & detect fraud against you or White Fox Boutique Pty Ltd

-       Google Customer Reviews (if you opt-in at checkout)

Your cards security/CCV code is not stored at checkout & all payments are encrypted as per PCI-DSS requirements & industry standard encryptions. Your payment information is only used by White Fox Boutique Pty Ltd for payments, refunds & for fraud detection.

 


PURCHASE HISTORY, SAVED ITEMS & WISH-LIST ITEMS

Your purchase history, saved items & wish-list items are stored within your customer account. Purchase history is stored so you can easily look up past orders & re-order any items. Saved items are stored so you can add items to bag, checkout or save them for later. Wish-list items are stored for future purchase or can be shared with family or friends.

 


CONTACT HISTORY

Your contact history is anything you have said to us over phone, chat, email or social media. This information is used to provide customer service & support & to train staff members to make sure you receive the best possible customer service. 

 


WHAT DO WE DO WITH YOUR INFORMATION

In order to provide our customers with the most efficient and enhanced service and to better understand your preferences, we may request information from you. All personal information that we obtain about you will be recorded, used and protected by us in accordance with current data protection legislation and the White Fox Boutique Pty Ltd privacy policy. We will primarily use your personal information to provide products and services to you and, for example:
- to send you information via e-mail about products, offers and news that may interest you. White Fox Boutique Pty Ltd will only send you this information if you have opted-in to receive email or product notifications
- to analyse customers visiting the www.whitefoxboutique.com website and learn about the interests & actions of our visitors in order to understand your interests and needs. This will help White Fox Boutique Pty Ltd improve our products and services and deliver to you the type of content, offers and promotions that you are most interested in.
- to improve your web experience on www.whitefoxboutique.com, ensuring easy checkout & no errors while you are shopping & browsing.

 


NON-PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION

Navigational information collected allows White Fox Boutique Pty Ltd to see which areas of our website are most visited & helps us improve your experience online.  Other information such as browser version, device & IP address information may be collected. This information is used to improve our website such as default options of currency to give you the best possible shopping experience additionally fraud & website protection.

 

 

COOKIES

White Fox Boutique uses cookies to help you place an order, remember the items in your shopping cart, recognise when you return to the website & analyse your visiting behaviour.
You can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent to your browser or you can choose to turn off all cookies through your browser settings.
We also work with third party companies who use tracking technologies to serve ads on our behalf across the internet. These companies may collect non-personally identifiable information about your visit to www.whitefoxboutique.com.

 


HOW TO UNSUBSCRIBE

If you subscribe to the White Fox Boutique Pty Ltd email list your email address will be used to receive information about new products and special offer. If you do not wish to receive updates after subscribing, you may remove yourself from the mailing list by logging into your account and opting out of the mailing list or unsubscribing at the bottom of any email received.

 


 HOW TO RE-SET YOUR PASSWORD

In the event that you have forgotten your password to your White Fox Boutique Pty Ltd account, you can request a new password to be sent to your registered email address. Alternatively, you can email [email protected] for assistance and you will be sent a new temporary password to your email address. Upon receiving your new temporary password, you may change it in your 'My Account' tab for added security purposes.
