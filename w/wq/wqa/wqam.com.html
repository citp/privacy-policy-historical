Privacy Policy

Here at WQAM we take your electronic privacy seriously. Questions regarding this statement or our practices should be directed to the Beasley Broadcast Group Inc. Privacy Coordinator by email at privacy@bbgi.com. You may also send inquiries by mail to:

Beasley Broadcast Group Inc. Privacy Coordinator 3033 Riviera Drive, Suite 200 Naples, FL 34103

We reserve the right to change our policy from time to time as we deem appropriate in our sole discretion. Information:

Web sites can obtain two kinds of information about you:
1. Aggregate Information: Any anonymous data that a web site uses to diagnose problems on the sites' servers, administer the site, and to help improve the quality of the Web pages, is Aggregate Information. This may be your domain name (i.e. "XYZ.com") or your IP address.

2. Personally Identifiable Information: This can be your first and last name, your email address, your home address, or your telephone number. This personal information is sometimes needed when you order products online or subscribe to an email newsletter. Other times, web sites collect this information to help target advertising and marketing. We Collect Aggregate Information - such as your computers browser type, type (e.g., Netscape or Internet Explorer), domain (e.g., "XYZ.com", and IP address (a number that's automatically assigned to your computer whenever you're surfing the Internet). This is done in the background so it does not interrupt your browsing speed. This information is used to help diagnose problems with our servers, to administer our site, and to gather broad demographic information. Aggregate Information is shared with third parties for advertising and promotional purposes. None of this information is connected with Personally Identifiable Information. So, when you enter our site, you are anonymous and will remain that way unless you choose otherwise.

Cookies:
When we conduct polls, surveys, chat rooms and contests Cookies are sometimes used to limit multiple responses and registrations. Occasionally you may receive cookies from one of our advertisers/vendors. These cookies are not controlled by us and are not subject to our privacy policy. The use of advertising cookies sent by third-party servers is standard in the Internet industry. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you'd prefer, you can set yours to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, it is possible that some parts of the site, such as the polls, surveys, contest, and game areas, will not function properly if you do so.

Contests and Sweepstakes:
From time to time we run contests and sweepstakes in which we ask for Personally Identifiable Information such as your name, email address or telephone number. Sometimes we request additional Personally Identifiable Information such as your age, gender, and income level. And occasionally we require additional information in exchange for allowing you to enter the contest. Here are some of the ways we might use the information that we collect:
1. To notify you if you are a winner
2. To send you information about Beasley Broadcasting Group Inc. and our site
3. To give to content partners, who may send you promotional email
4. To give to advertisers, who may send you emails about their products and services


You do not have to answer surveys or register for newsletters or service. All required information will be marked with an asterisk (*), so any optional information can be skipped. And on that same page we will give you a way to "opt out" of being contacted by WQAM and any third parties. We may share Personally Identifiable Information and Aggregate Information with WQAM affiliates and other sites operated by Beasley Broadcast Group Inc and its affiliates. If you win one of our contests or sweepstakes, we will publish your name, and hometown on the Site.

Subpoenas and Law Enforcement Agencies:
We reserve the right to cooperate with government and law enforcement agencies engaged in investigations of activities that are, or are believed to be, illegal. Further, we may share your Personally Identifiable Information with such agencies if we believe that a crime or any act that endangers the health and safety of others may have been or may be committed. We may also share your Personally Identifiable Information in response to an appropriate subpoena.

Community and Public Area:
Information posted to message boards or chat areas is available to the general public. Caution should be used when posting such information. Users under the age of 18 should be particularly careful not to provide any personally identifying information while using chat rooms or message boards. We reserve the right to, but are not obligated to, refuse or delete any content that in our sole discretion is considered inappropriate. Please see our Terms of Service for additional information.

Links To Other Sites:
When you visit our site, you may be directed to other sites outside our service. For example, if you click on a banner advertisement, you will leave our site. This includes links to sites operated by advertisers, sponsors and partners that may use our logo as part of an agreement with us. These other sites may send their own cookies to users, collect data, or solicit personal information. These other sites are not subject to our privacy policy and we are not responsible for the privacy policies for these other sites, nor for the use of any information that these sites may collect.
