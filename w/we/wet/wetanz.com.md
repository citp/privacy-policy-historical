We respect your right to privacy and your right to view and update your personal information which we hold about you. We are committed to protecting your privacy when you visit our site or contact us in any way.

**Collecting your personal information**

We collect and use personal information about you in order to be able to complete your order

**Changing your personal information**

You have the right to view and change any personal information we hold about you at any time.

You can do this on the Site by entering your username and password and going to the My Weta section and logging in with your username and password.

Alternatively, you may contact us at info@wetaNZ.co.nz.

**Storing your personal information**

When you set up your profile, you can choose to store your personal information on the Site in a secure server. This is so you can view and update it as necessary. Your personal information is only able to be accessed by entering your username and password.

If you choose to store them, your credit card details are stored on the Site on our secure server.

You can also purchase without storing your details, in which case we hold your details until your order is completed and your payment is confirmed. After your order is complete, these details are deleted from the system.

**No disclosure**

We will not sell, rent, or otherwise disclose your personal information to any third party unless:

  * We need to do so to provide you with the content, service or features you have requested;
  * We are completing financial transactions with our bank;
  * We need to do so to protect the rights or property of Weta Limited;
  * We are required to do so by law.



**Collecting site usage information**

We monitor site usage by analysing aggregated user information. This helps us improve the Site to give you the information, services and features you want. This information is aggregated and does not identify you individually.

We do monitor the number of successful logins on each subscriber user name. This allows us to identify potential abuse or unauthorised use of the Site.

**Cookies**

Weta uses "cookies" to improve your use of Weta services.

We use temporary (per-session) cookies, for the following purposes:

  * To "remember" that a Weta account member has logged in. This means that you don't have to enter your username and password more than once per visit.
  * To help us keep track of items you have added to your shopping cart for purchase, so we can process your order.



Please note: Our temporary cookies must be accepted in order to login and purchase a product from us. If your browser is set to notify you when you receive a cookie, you will be prompted to accept the cookie (Yes/No). Please select "Yes".

Our cookies are temporary and disappear when you close your browser.

**Contacting you**

If you have an account, we may contact you to:

  * Offer you information regarding products and promotions on the site. This will be in the form of a newsletter from which you will be able to unsubscribe.
  * Clarify details about your subscription or purchase decisions;
  * Respond to any enquiries you make; or
  * Provide any information necessary for the operation and security of our Site.



**Links to other websites**

Weta provides links to other websites when we consider you may be interested in the content on those sites. We do not necessarily endorse those sites or their content and we have no control over the conduct of the companies or organisations operating those sites.

Before you disclose any personal information to another site, we advise you to check its terms and conditions, including its privacy and security policies.

**Privacy complaints**

Weta is committed to dealing quickly and appropriately with any complaint you make about your privacy.

If you are concerned that this Privacy Policy may have been breached or your privacy has been violated, please email us immediately at info@wetanz.co.nz. Please include your email address or a daytime telephone number where we can contact you.
