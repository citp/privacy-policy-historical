# Weegy

Privacy Statement

We do not sell your information to other parties. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices. As a visitor to our website you are not required to reveal any personal information. This website does collect readily available information about visitors usage of the website. We use this information to create a more relevant experience for the visitor and to measure the website's effectiveness. We sometimes use an Internet device called a 'cookie' to recognize your browser when you visit our site. A cookie is a small amount of information that a website transfers to your computer's hard drive. This website uses cookies only to better individualize and improve your experience with the site. You can choose whether to accept cookies by changing the settings of your browser.
