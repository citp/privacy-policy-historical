WEDDINGTON WAY, INC. PRIVACY POLICY

We at Weddington Way, Inc. (“Weddington Way,” “we,” “us,” “our”) know that

our users (“you,” “your”) care about how your personal information is used and shared,

and we take your privacy seriously.  Please read the following to learn more about our

Privacy Policy.  By visiting or using the WeddingtonWay.com website and domain

name, and any other linked pages, features, content, or any other services we offer

acknowledge that you accept the practices and policies outlined in this Privacy

Policy, and you hereby consent that we will collect, use, and share your information

in the following ways.

I. WHAT DOES THIS PRIVACY POLICY COVER?

This Privacy Policy covers our treatment of personally identifiable information

(“Personal Information”) that we gather when you are accessing or using our Website.

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that we do not own or control, or

to individuals that we do not employ or manage. 

We do not knowingly collect or solicit Personal Information from anyone under

the age of 13 or knowingly allow such persons to register. If you are under 13, please do

not attempt to register for the Website or send any information about yourself to us,

including your name, address, telephone number, or email address. No one under age 13

may provide any personal information to us or on the Website. In the event that we learn

that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without

verification of parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If

you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under 13, please

contact us at legalweddingtonwaycom.

We gather various types of Personal Information from our users, as explained

your requests for certain products and services, to analyze how users utilize the Website,

and as otherwise set forth in this Privacy Policy. We may share certain types of Personal

Information with third parties (as set forth in this Section and in Section III below).  We

collect the following types of information:

A. Information You Provide to Us:

We receive and store any information you knowingly provide to us.  For example,

during the registration process, we collect Personal Information such as your name, user

name, email address, IP address, browser information, credit card information, the date of

your wedding or the wedding you’re a part of (“Event Date”) and third-party account

credentials (for example, your log-in credentials for Facebook or other third party sites).

aggregate information to a partner in a manner that would identify you

personally.
