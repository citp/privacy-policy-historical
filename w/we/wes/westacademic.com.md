# Privacy Notice - West Academic

Your privacy is important to West Academic, and we go to great lengths to protect it. This Privacy Notice applies to the collection of information about you via this West Academic website (the “Site”), via email, over the phone, or from third parties.  Our goal is to provide you with a personalized experience that delivers the information, resources and services that are most relevant and helpful to you.  To achieve this goal, we collect information from you and about you. We will not disclose your information to any person or organization outside West Academic, except as described in this Privacy Notice.

To demonstrate our commitment to your privacy, we have prepared this notice disclosing our privacy practices.  Here, you will learn what personal identifying information of yours is collected, how and when we might use your information, how we protect your information, who has access to your information, and how to correct any inaccuracies in the information.

### Types of Information That We May Collect

To create an account, you will need to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including type of customer (e.g., student, faculty, etc.), and contact information.  You will need to give us accurate contact information, keep that contact information accurate, and set a unique password that you keep confidential.  When you access products and services through that account, we may collect information about your use of those products and services.

To make a purchase, you will need to provide us with contact information and payment card information.

When you interact with our customer service representatives or communicate with us via email, we may collect the information that you provide to us.

When you purchase some of our products that contain quizzing functionality you are authorizing us to communicate with representatives of your school about your use of those services.  In those instances, we may collect information about you through that process.

_Web server logs:  _When you visit the Site, we may track information to administer the site and analyze its usage, and we may use third party online analytics services. Examples of information we may track include:

  * your internet protocol address
  * browser used (user-agent)
  * links clicked
  * the state or country from which site was accessed
  * the date and time of your visit
  * the website you visited before you entered our Site
  * the pages viewed within our Site



_Cookies:  _Our websites use cookies (i.e., small bits of text that our servers place into a file on your computer’s hard drive). This is used to save your preferences user name, and provide personalized functionality. You can reject cookies by changing your browser settings, but be aware that this will disable some of the functionality on our websites.

### How we Use the information That We Collect

We will use the information we collect to provide products and services to you, and to allow you to access products and services using your account credentials. 

We may use the information we collect to provide you with promotional information about additional products and services we think you may be interested in.  If you don’t want to receive promotional materials from West Academic, you can contact us to opt out at any time.  You can also opt out when creating a user account, or later when managing your user account profile.  If you have questions about opting out, send an email message to [privacy@westacademic.com](mailto:privacy@westacademic.com).

We will not sell, rent or lease mailing lists or other customer data to others, and we will not make your personal information available to any unaffiliated parties, except as follows. 

  * To agents and/or contractors who may use it on our behalf or in connection with their relationship with us (for example, we may use third parties to fulfill your request for products or services or help us with promotional campaigns).These agents or contractors may have access to your information if needed to perform their functions, but may not use such information for their own purposes.
  * West Academic includes quizzing functionality that is available for students or educational facilities in conjunction with assessment of student progress as provided in conjunction with certain designated casebooks or questions associated with subject areas.When you purchase this functionality, you agree that representatives of the school may have access to the results of your quizzing to assess progress and or/scores.In certain situations, West Academic may have a written contract with your school.
  * In connection with our Site, we use third-party online analytics services such as those of Google Analytics. These analytics services use automated technologies to collect information (such as email address, IP address, and device identifiers) to evaluate use of our products and services and to diagnose technical issues. You can obtain more information about how Google Analytics collects, and processes and uses this data at <https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en> and <https://marketingplatform.google.com/about/analytics/>
  * To respond to your requests, or otherwise with your consent.
  * As required by law, in a matter of public safety or policy, as needed in connection with the transfer of our business assets (for example, if we are acquired by another company or if we are liquidated during bankruptcy proceedings) or if we believe in good faith that sharing the data is necessary to protect our rights or property.
  * We may share information that does not identify you personally for any reason at our sole discretion.



### How We Protect Your Information

West Academic uses reasonable physical, technical, and administrative measures to prevent the loss, misuse and alteration of information under our control.  We allow our employees and service providers to access your information only when the information is necessary for them to do their jobs. Our employees and service providers who have access to your information are required to protect it and keep it confidential.

When customers provide financial information (such as a credit card number), that data is encrypted using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology.  Although no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, we maintain PCI-DSS compliance. 

We will keep your information for as long as needed to provide you with our products and services and otherwise at our discretion or in accordance with applicable law.   


### Process to Review and Opt Out

You have the right to review and request changes to your personal information. Except as defined herein, West Academic does not share your data with third parties, but you have the right to opt out of any future exchange of covered personally identifiable information.  Please send an email message to [privacy@westacademic.com](mailto:privacy@westacademic.com) and let us know if you require changes to your information or are electing to opt out of making information available to a third party.  Once we verify the authenticity of the request, we will make the requested changes.

### Children's Information

We will not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information personally identifiable information about a person who is under the age of 13, we will remove that information from our files.

### Your California Privacy Rights/Do Not Track Disclosures

California Civil Code Section 1798.83 permits California residents to request certain information regarding our disclosures of your personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. To make such a request, please send an email to  [privacy@westacademic.com](mailto:privacy@westacademic.com) or a mail us at the address below.

Some web browsers may transmit “do-not-track” signals to the websites with which you are communicating. Because of differences in how web browsers incorporate and activate this feature, it is not always clear whether you intend for these signals to be transmitted, or whether you even are aware of them. Because there currently is no industry standard concerning what, if anything, websites should do when they receive such signals, we currently do not take action in response to these signals. 

### We are an American Company so We Follow US Law

Our products and services are directed towards users who live in the United States. By establishing an account, making a purchase, or using the Site, you consent to the collection, storage, processing, and transfer of your information in pursuant to US law. 

### Privacy Policy Changes

If our information practices change in a significant way, we will post the policy changes and/or email you at the address you have provided to us.
