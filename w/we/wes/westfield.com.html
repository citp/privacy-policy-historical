Privacy Notice

Welcome and thank you for visiting Westfield!  Westfield understands that our patrons are our lifeblood and we are in the business of creating the most enjoyable and unique shopping, dining, leisure and entertainment experience while protecting the privacy and security of our consumers personal information.  To help inform you, we provide this Privacy Notice explaining our privacy and information practices for our website, mobile applications (together, "Online Services"), and centers. This Notice also describes the types of information we collect, how that information is used and disclosed, and how you can access, modify, or delete your information.

This Privacy Notice ("Privacy Notice" or "Notice") was last updated on July 3, 2018.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Westfield may collect the following types of information from you when you use our Online Services or visit Westfield centers: 

Online Services.

  • Information you provide to us. When using our Online Services, you may provide information directly, such as by creating an account, filling out a form, signing up for SmartParking, completing a "contact us" query, applying for a career with Westfield, or signing up for newsletters or other communications. Additionally, you may choose to provide your location when using a Westfield Online Service.
  • Information we collect automatically from your device.  We may also collect information from you automatically when you use our Online Services. For instance, we may collect information about your use of our Online Services, including the time and duration of your visit, the referring URL, your Internet Protocol (IP) address, the type of device you use and its operating system, your advertising ID (e.g., AAID and IDFA), and other device identifiers.
  • Cookies and similar technologies. Westfield and its partners use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies when you use our Online Services. For more information, including how to manage your cookie preferences, see our Cookie Policy.
  • Competitions and Sweepstakes. When you enter a competition, sweepstakes, or register for an event you may provide contact information directly to us including name, address, email address, and/or phone number.

In Our Centers.

  • Location Information. We may use Bluetooth (BLE) Beacons, Wi-Fi, and similar technologies within some of our centers that may communicate with your (mobile) device and identify its location if the device has Bluetooth and/or Wi-Fi enabled and is properly configured. As a result, we may collect your Wi-Fi and/or Bluetooth MAC Address and send BLE signals to your device. To completely opt-out of BLE Beacons and/or the collection of your MAC address, turn off your device Bluetooth and Wi-Fi capabilities.  To learn more about mobile location analytics and to opt-out of mobile location tracking for companies that have agreed to the Mobile Location Analytics Code of Conduct please visit https://smart-places.org/.
  • Westfield Wi-Fi. If you use one of the Wi-Fi networks in our centers, we will collect personal information from you in order to log you on to the network, including your name and email address. We may also collect information about your use of the Wi-Fi, such as the web pages you visit, your MAC or IP address, and your location while using the Wi-Fi.
  • Concierge Services. If you use our concierge services in center or online, we may collect information from you to facilitate the products or services you request. Concierge services vary by location--check with the concierge in your local center to see what services are available and what personal information is required to use those services.
  • Competitions and Sweepstakes. When you enter a competition, sweepstakes, or register for an event at one of our centers you may provide contact information directly to us including name, address, email address, and/or phone number. 

Third-Party Sources. Westfield may obtain information about you from third parties and/or link the information we collect from you to additional records made available by third parties.

HOW INFORMATION IS USED

Westfield uses the information it collects for three primary purposes: 1) to operate our business, including providing and improving our products and services; 2) to send you communications; and 3) to tailor and show advertisements to you.

  • Providing Westfield products and services. We use the information we collect to perform essential business operations, for example to understand usage patterns (such as foot traffic) within our centers, and to develop, provide, improve, and personalize Westfield products and services and to provide customer support.
  • Communications. We use information we collect to deliver and personalize communications through various channels, including email, SMS messaging, push notifications, and other channels where you have consented to receiving communications from Westfield. See the "Choice" section below on how to change your communications preferences.
  • Advertising. Westfield uses the information you provide us to help us tailor and show advertisements to you. We may partner with third parties to help us display relevant advertising and to manage our advertising across multiple channels. Our third-party partners may use cookies and non-cookie based technologies in order to help us show you advertising based upon your browsing activities and interests.

You may opt out of some interest-based cookie advertising by clicking "Cookie Settings" and for more information about third-party advertisers and your ability to opt-out of targeted advertising from such third-parties please visit the Network Advertising Initiative (http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/) and the Digital Advertising Alliance (http://www.aboutads.info/choices/).

If applicable, this information would include a list of the categories of personal information that was shared and the names and addresses of all third parties with which we shared information in the immediately preceding calendar year. Westfield does not currently take actions to respond to Do Not Track signals because a uniform technological standard has not yet been developed. We continue to review new technologies and may adopt a standard once one is created.

YOUR PRIVACY CHOICES

We enable you to maintain control of your privacy by providing choices about what information you provide us and how we communicate with you, and by providing access to your personal information.

  • Choice: You have choices about what information you provide Westfield. Where information is required, it will be marked as such. If the information input fields are not marked as required, providing the information is optional. Note that failing to provide some of the requested information may limit your use of our products or services.

You may adjust your communication preferences or opt out of communications at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" at the bottom of the email, following instructions provided in the communication, or changing your preferences within your Westfield account. If you are having difficulty updating your communication preferences, contact us.

  • Access. You may access, correct, or delete your personal information by logging into your Westfield account and following the relevant prompts, or by contacting us.

We will endeavor to provide you with access to your personal information wherever possible. Note that in some cases we may not be able to provide you access, such as where providing access would be unreasonably difficult or would compromise the privacy of others.

CHILDREN

Westfield does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Children under the age of 13 are not permitted to use Wi-Fi networks in Westfield centers.

If you have reason to believe that a child under the age of 13 has provided personal information to Westfield, please contact us and we will delete that information from our databases.

HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?

Westfield retains the information we collect for as long as necessary to provide the products and services you have requested, or for other essential purposes such as complying with legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our agreements.  If you have any questions about the retention of your information, please contact us directly at privacy@westfield.com.

HOW WE PROTECT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

The security of your personal information is important to us. Therefore, we encourage you to take precautions to protect your information, including protecting your passwords, using secure networks to access the Internet, and logging out of online services when you are away from your device.
