#### **What Are Cookies:**

The Cookies – small text files that are placed on your Computer to help the site delivering better user experience. In general, cookies are used to preserve user preferences, store information for things like shopping carts, and provide anonymized tracking data to third party applications like Google Analytics. in general, cookies are there to make your browsing experience better. However, you are allowed to disable cookies on this site and others. The most comfortable way to do this is to disable cookies in your browser. We advise consulting the Help section of the browser or taking a look at [  **About Cookies website**](http://www.aboutcookies.org/)  which provides guidance for all modern browsers.

#### **Collection of The Personal Information & Opting-Out**

We never force users to disclose their personal information, But such information such as Browser info, and Location (through Log Files) is required by the website to track its performance and to implement and utilize better ways to enhance users experience and website’s quality. An example medium through which we monitor such information is “Google Analytics” which is extremely helpful for us to track site’s activity on the daily, weekly or monthly basis. GA helps us to collect the certain statistics which are required to ensure content quality and to improve the website’s quality accordingly. But your personal identification is assured to be safeguarded at WebForPC as far as its being disclosed to us only. Our visitors may opt out of Google Analytics by using the opt-out service provided by Google:  **[Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/)**.

For data collection through “Google Analytics,” we have set the new limit to for the analytics to retain the data and its deletion. When data touches the end of the retention limit, it is deleted automatically on a monthly basis. We have set the **data retention limit to “14 Months”** which is basically the period after which the data will be automatically deleted from the analytics account.

#### **Cookies & Advertisements**

We only display ads (solely) from **Google Adsense**  (no ads from any third party advertising networks) which utilizes cookies (Double-Click) to deliver its services, while being a third party service provider, Ads served by Google Adsense are no way in our control, so we are not responsible for that. But being a mighty network, we trust the services provided by Google; still one may go with opting out of the cookies by visiting detailed privacy policy laid down by a competent authority at  **<https://policies.google.com/>**.Again! We use Google AdSense, and they serve ads on our site based on a user’s prior visits to our website. Google’s use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its official partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet. **Users may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie** for interest-based advertising by visiting  **[Ads Settings](https://webforpc.com/cookie-policy/)**. Alternatively, users can opt-out of a third-party vendor’s use of cookies for interest-based advertising by visiting  **[aboutads.info](http://www.aboutads.info/)**.If WebForPC.com use other advertising networks (other than Google AdSense), their privacy policies will always be listed on their official (respective) sites. Most of these companies belong to the Network Advertising Initiative. Our visitors may opt-out of behavioral advertising methods by visiting [ **networkadvertising.org**](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/).
