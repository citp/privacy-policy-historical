Privacy Policy Document for Webloggerz

Hi there! Welcome to Webloggerz.

Webloggerz.com respects your privacy and at webloggerz, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by webloggerz.com and how it is used.

Any personal information you provide to us including and similar to your name, address, telephone number and e-mail address will not be released, sold, or rented to any entities or individuals outside of webloggerz.com.

We have a strict no spam policy and do not send you any emails, unless you subscribe to receive email updates.

Our main primary purpose in collecting personal information is to provide you with a safer browsing experience.

Tracking:

When you visit this website, we can record certain information in relation to your visit such as:

+ Your IP or proxy server IP
+ Basic domain information
+ Your Internet service provider is sometimes captured depending on the configuration of your ISP connection
+ The date and time of your visit to the website
+ The length of your session
+ The pages which you have accessed
+ The number of times you access our site within any month
+ The size of file you look at
+ The website which referred you to our website
+ The operating system which your computer uses.

This is done by making use of analytical softwares such as Google Analytics, Bing Webmasters primarily for the data anaysis and web developement process and we do not share this with any third party.

Cookies:

We do make use of session cookies, which are used only during a browsing session, and expire as soon you quit your browser.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options.
More detailed information about cookies can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.

webloggerzs.com favours high-quality, neutral, unbiased and only original work by each author.

We accept well-written, knowledge-powered, original articles from tech writers, designers and developers and intense care is been taken for not allowing any
copied content to be republished or copied images to be reposted on our website.

User Comments:

Webloggerz.com allows users to comment on the site. However, we hold all rights to accept or reject any comments based on our discretion.
While commenting on this site please refrain from using content that is not suitable.
