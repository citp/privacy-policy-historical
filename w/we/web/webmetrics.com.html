Webmetrics Privacy Statement
  


Webmetrics is committed to the privacy of our customers. The following privacy statement provides general information about the data collection, maintenance and dissemination practices for our website.

When subscribing to Webmetrics' services online, users are required to submit general contact information, such as name and email address. Webmetrics does not sell or share customer contact information with persons outside of our company. We reserve the right to utilize contact information for the distribution of promotional materials and special offers to our customers, sponsored by Webmetrics and/or our partners. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings (see the opt-out section below for more details). Customer information is also used by Webmetrics' personnel to contact users when necessary. Financial information that is collected by Webmetrics is used solely for the purpose of verifying users' qualifications, and to bill customers for services purchased.

Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Webmetrics does not store financial information. For more information about our Security Policies, go to http://www.webmetrics.com/security.html.

Opt-Out
Webmetrics provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from our company and partners when contact information is request from the user during service sign-up. Customers can opt-out at any time by emailing an opt-out request to support@webmetrics.com.
