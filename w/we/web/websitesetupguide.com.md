# Website Setup Guide: Privacy Policy

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us.

At websitesetupguide.com, the privacy of our visitors is of great importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information received and collected by websitesetupguide.com and how it is used.

Like many other Web sites, websitesetupguide.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyse trends, administer the site, track userâ€™s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

websitesetupguide.com does not use cookies. Some of our advertising partners may use cookies on this site or on their advertising pages. Our advertising partners include Google Adwords. Third party vendors, including Google, show our ads on sites on the internet. Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website.

You can opt out of behavioural advertising at the [NAI](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp).

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to generate the advertisements and links that appear on websitesetupguide.com and on advertising partners. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs

websitesetupguide.com has no access to or control over cookies that are used by third-party advertisers. You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. websitesetupguide.com's privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.

More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.
