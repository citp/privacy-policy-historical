Airzip does not automatically collect any Personal Information on or through the Site, except as provided by you in connection with the registration process. Cookies may be used to analyze Site usage, improve content, customize the Site's content and layout and for other customer service purposes. Airzip's use of cookies is in no way linked to any Personal Information.
