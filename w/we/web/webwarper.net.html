WebWarper: Privacy Policy

To contents of this section

This document is devoted to public WebWarper service, located at the address http://webwarper.net, and intended for accelerating access to Internet.

WebWarper service is developed by and belongs to SIAMS Company.

This document does completely describe collection, storage and distribution of personal information we collect from WebWarper service users. We do neither collect, store, nor distribute information about our users in the ways other from described in this document.

Information about page views through WebWarper service

In order to collect statistics, and also to analyze WebWarper service operation, during every Web page view we do register the following information about user in special log-files of WebWarper server:

  • user's IP-address;
  • precise date and time of addressing the page;
  • address of a viewed page;
  • address of a page, user surfed to the given page from (Referrer);
  • type of a user's browser (User-Agent);
  • time characteristics, describing the process of loading a page to webwarper.net server, and its further compression.

Some statistic information, describing the number of views for pages with different types in some intervals of time, but not containing any personal data on users, is published openly on our Web site at http://webwarper.net/wwlogs/.

Other information from log-files of WebWarper is also available on our site, but access to it is protected by standard Basic-scheme of HTTP-authorization. Besides that, 1-800-HOSTING hosting company, from which we are renting dedicated computer for placing our webwarper.net server, does also have access to this information. Logins and passwords required to gain access to this information are known to SIAMS Company, and 1-800-HOSTING hosting company only.

Information about addressings to webwarper.net server

Every addressing to our webwarper.net server through HTTP protocol is fixed in standard log files of Apache Web server. These log files, same as WebWarper service log files, do contain user information: IP-address, precise date and time of addressing to a page, page address, Referrer, and User-Agent.

Only SIAMS Company and 1-800-HOSTING, from which we are renting dedicated computer for placing our webwarper.net server, do have access to these log files. Logins and passwords required to gain access to this information are known to SIAMS Company, and 1-800-HOSTING hosting company only.

External system for statistics collection - SpyLOG

We are using the system for attendance analysis by SpyLOG Company, registering users' calls to the majority of pages viewed through WebWarper service. Please keep in mind that we are not responsible for privacy policy of SpyLOG Company!

Due to our information based upon an experience of working with SpyLOG system, though we do not guarantee their confidence, SpyLOG system registers the following information about a visitor:

  • visitor's IP-address;
  • user's unique identifier, created during the first addressing of this user to WebWarper service;
  • precise date and time of addressing the page;
  • address of the viewed page;
  • address of a page user surfed to the given page from (Referrer);
  • detailed characteristics of user's browser.

To trace calls to our server from a concrete visitors, and particularly to save the unique user identifier, cookies mechanism is used.

The specified information is stored on SpyLOG Company servers.

Some statistic information about attendance is published on SpyLOG Company site in an opened form. Access to the rest of information is protected by the standard Basic scheme of HTTP-authorization. Login and password required to gain access to this information are known to SIAMS and SpyLOG companies only.

For more detailed information about storage, use and distribution of data collected by SpyLOG system, visit official site of SpyLOG Company.

Banners on WebWarper panels

We are placing banners of different companies and private persons on WebWarper panels that are added in upper and lower parts of every page viewed through WebWarper service. Owners of some of these banners can register a fact that a user did click on banner or that a banner was loaded on user's computer. The banner owner can receive the following information about user:

  • user's IP-address;
  • precise date and time of addressing the page;
  • address of the page viewed;
  • type of user's browser (User-Agent).

No other information is transmitted to browser owners.

Please keep in mind that we are not responsible for privacy policy of companies and private persons whose banners are placed on WebWarper panels!
