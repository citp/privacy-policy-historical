Privacy Policy

We keep your data safe

Web-Stat respects the privacy of its visitors and customers. The following policies are in effect for all of the web pages owned and operated by Web-Stat and are in place to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy.

USER'S PRIVACY

Web-Stat does not sell or give away any information concerning its users or its user's data to any third party. Moreover Web-Stat does not collect any data about its users unbeknownst to them.

NO SPAM

If you sign up for a trial, or product purchase at the Web-Stat site, you will be asked for your email address. This information is only used to keep you informed of any changes in your account status and occasionally to pass on important information about the Web-Stat product that you are using (for instance, new features or new version availability).

Web-Stat maintains a strict 'no-spam' policy. Your e-mail address or mailing address will not be sold or rented to a third party.

If at anytime you wish to be removed from our trial product or customer list simply contact us. Removals are usually done within 24 hours.

OPT-OUT

If at anytime you wish to cancel an ongoing subscription to Web-Stat, simply contact us. We will confirm the cancellation within 24 hours of receiving your request. There is no need to justify your desire to unsubscribe in any way.

SECURITY

All data collected on our secure product order forms is encrypted for your security and privacy.

CHANGE/MODIFY

You can change or update any personal information provided to us from the Settings Panel, or contact us with the requested changes.

COOKIES

Web-Stat may send cookies to your computer. This information is collected to identify your account and to personalize your browsing experience.

If you have questions about our privacy policy, contact us. It is our goal to provide a private, risk-free experience with our company.
