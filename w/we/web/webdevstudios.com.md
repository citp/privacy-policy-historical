# Privacy Policy - WebDevStudios

Whether requesting a quote or subscribing to our blog, the information you give to WebDevStudios (WD3, LLC) will not be sold or otherwise transferred to unaffiliated third parties without the approval of the user at the time of collection.

How we handle the information we learn about you depends upon what you do when visiting our website. If you are requesting a quote or more information, we collect and store only the following information about you, should you voluntarily provide it:

  1. The name of the domain from which you access the Internet
  2. The date and time you access our site
  3. The Internet address of the website you used to link directly to our site.
  4. Your email address
  5. Your phone number
  6. Your name
  7. Project type
  8. Who referred you



If you are subscribing to our blog, only your email address will be requested and used to share our newest blog posts with you.

If you identify yourself by sending us an email containing personal information, then the information collected will be solely used to respond to your message.

The information collected is for statistical, communication, and customer service purposes only. WebDevStudios may use software programs to create summary statistics, which are used for such purposes as assessing the number of visitors to the different sections of our site, what information is of most and least interest, determining technical design specifications, and identifying system performance or problem areas.

For site security purposes and to ensure that this service remains available to all users, WebDevStudios uses software programs to monitor network traffic to identify unauthorized attempts to upload or change information, or otherwise cause damage.
