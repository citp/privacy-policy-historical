The Cookies are placed on Users hard drive for record-keeping purposes and sometimes to track information about Users' internet usage. Clear gifs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of Users. In contrast to cookies, which are stored on a User’s computer hard drive, clear gifs are embedded invisibly on web pages and are very small in size. InstalleRex does not gather PII by clear gifs.
