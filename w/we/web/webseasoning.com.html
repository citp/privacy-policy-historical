Privacy

Web Seasoning respects your privacy. Any personal information you provide to us including and similar to your name, address, telephone number and email address will not be released, sold, or rented to any entities or individuals outside of Web Seasoning.

We have a strict no spam policy and do not send you any emails, unless you subscribe to receive email updates. Web Seasoning will never ask for Credit Card details and request that you Do Not enter it on any of the forms on Web Seasoning.

Web Seasoning is not responsible for the content of external Internet sites. You are advised to read the privacy policy of external sites before disclosing any personal information.

Cookies : A “cookie” is a small data text file that is placed in your browser and allows Web Seasoning to recognize you each time you visit this site(customization etc). Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and Web Seasoning does not use cookies to collect personal information. Cookies may also be used by 3rd party content providers such as news feeds. Visitor Tracking : Web Seasoning makes use of analytical softwares such as Google Analytics for statistical purposes. For the purpose of stats your IP (Internet Protocol) Address, browser, screen resolution, operating system are captured. We at Web Seasoning use this information only for statistical purpose and do not share it with any third party.

While we do our best to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee the security of any information that you transmit to Web Seasoning and you are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of any passwords or other account information.

In addition other Internet sites or services that may be accessible through Web Seasoning have separate data and privacy practices independent of us, and therefore we disclaim any responsibility or liability for their policies or actions.  Please contact those vendors and others directly if you have any questions about their privacy policies.

These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
