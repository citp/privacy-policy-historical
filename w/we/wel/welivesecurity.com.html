Cookies allow us to serve You better and more efficiently, and to personalize Your experience at our site. Through the use of cookies, ESET is able to further maintain, develop and improve the Site. If You choose to decline some cookies, be aware that You may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Site, nor will You be able to download certain content and/or take part in certain activities on the Site. It is possible to block all cookies but this may result in diminished functionality or non-functionality of the Site. Cookies enable us to recognize Your computer when you revisit our Site. We do not use cookies to collect personal information about you. This Site may also facilitate third party cookies such as those from Google Analytics, etc. We do not hold responsibility for third party cookies or how they collect information about Your visit on this Site. Therefore, make sure to read their privacy policies. Available Cookie Opt-out – Google Analytics: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
