Information Collected Online

Most of the information provided by The Weather Channel is offered without our collecting any personally identifiable information from you. For instance, you can view weather conditions in your hometown or anywhere in the world without providing any personally identifiable information. In order to maximize your experience and to provide you with enhanced products and services, however, The Weather Channel will collect personally identifiable information (e.g., name, e-mail address, etc.) and demographic data (e.g., zip code, gender, birth date, interests, etc.) that you voluntarily supply to us. Although you are not required to disclose any personally identifiable information to us, portions of our Web site and other specialized services may be unavailable to you without such information.
When you make a purchase or sign up for one of our premium services, we will also collect your billing address, phone number, and credit card information. Some other examples of instances when we collect personally identifiable information include when you subscribe to our premium or enhanced services, for example The Weather Channel Alerts (weather information delivered to you via e-mail or text), The Weather Channel Desktop (a software application which you can download to your desktop to receive weather information), and The Weather Channel downloads (software applications you can download to certain wireless devices). Others are when you:
  • Complete a survey
  • Register for a promotion, contest or sweepstakes
  • Make a purchase from our online store
  • Send us an e-mail message or complete an inquiry form to receive additional information
  • Submit a photo or video to iWitness Weather
  • Participate in an online forum or community, or
  • Customize the weather.com site.

In the case of participation in a chat session you are not required to provide any personally identifiable information, but we require you to select a user name. Please be aware that chat sessions are monitored and transcripts are posted on the Web site for access by other users. Any personally identifiable information you submit during a chat session, in blog comments, in any other user comment field, or in connection with your content submissions, including photo and video submissions, can be read, collected, or used by other users of these forums, and could be used by other users to send you unsolicited messages. We are not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to submit in these forums.
