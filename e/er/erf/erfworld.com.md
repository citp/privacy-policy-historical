Erfworld's Privacy Policy:

By signing up to be a Tool, or to use many of the features of Erfworld.com (Wiki, Forum, Store, or this site) we need to collect certain information from you such as: email address, physical address, phone number, or other information that uniquely identifies you. It our policy to keep this information private to internal site usage and to only use this information to verify your identity or to provide a specific purchased service to you (i.e. Tool membership benefits, or other purchased items). We do not sell your information to 3rd parties. All email or snail mail contact is strictly in relation to site usage (i.e. password resets, sign up confirmations, etc.), or to inform users of our new policies, products, or services. You may request to be removed from any of these contact methods at any time by using our [contact form](http://www.erfworld.com/erfworld-customer-support-form/).
