eatz is an online portal that provides free online restaurant reservations for the diners apart from helping the restaurants in managing their guests. With more than 2000 restaurants on its site, eatz is a huge online table reservation site in Hyderabad. Its profound and intricate network helps the diners to reserve their restaurant tables at any given time in a more convenient way,

 

It provides operational benefits to the restaurants to fill in their seats through the online booking services of the company. It allows the restaurants to build a huge diner databases for targeted e-mail marketing and further business promotions.

 

For diners,eatz provides a convenient method of table booking without charging a penny from them. Its network professionals offer a fast, efficient way to find available tables that meet desired criteria for cuisine, price and location at a specified time. Diners can also book reservations using eatz mobile solutions, including apps for the most popular smart phone platforms. Reservations are free and can be made around the clock.
