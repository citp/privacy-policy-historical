EatonWeb Privacy Policy

Let's keep this as simple as possible:

Eatonweb will never redistribute your email address.

Your email address will not be used for any purpose other than official Eatonweb announcements. By registering at Eatonweb, you assent to receiving an occasional email from EatonWeb regarding important announcements having to do with the EatonWeb blog directory.

For your safety, we do not collect or store any personal information. Email addresses are collected for the sake of verification and announcements. All payments are made through PayPal, and we therefore do not store any credit card information.
