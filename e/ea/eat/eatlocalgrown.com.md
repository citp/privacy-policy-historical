# Privacy Policy

A privacy policy is your guarantee that any personal information given to EatLocalGrown.com Directory will remain private. Any personal information gathered will never be sold to any third party for any reason. EatLocalGrown.com Directory only collects personal information from visitors in order to process requests initiated by the filling out of forms on our site. EatLocalGrown.com Directory is committed to keeping your personal information private. Please review our privacy policy for more detailed information. At EatLocalGrown.com Directory, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We promise to keep all the information that you share with us confidential. We use your personal information only to process requests for information; we do not share your information with any third parties.

## Collection of Information

We have provided web forms on our site that allow you to:

## 1\. Contact EatLocalGrown.com Directory directly.

In this case we collect your name, email address and your message to us. We only use this information to respond directly to your request. Your information will not be stored in a database or shared with a third party.

## 2\. Send (Recommend) a page to a friend.

In this case we collect your name, your email address, your friends email address and your message to your friend. This information is processed by our Web server to provide you with a simple way of recommending a link to a friend. We do not store any of the information you submit from the form and it is not shared with any third party.

## 3\. Apply to get listed in EatLocalGrown.com Directory.

As part of the application to become listed in our directory we will need to capture information about your company. This includes your contact information as well as the answers to the questions that we ask as part of the application process. This information will be used as part of your application and will be stored in a secure location. We do not share any of your information with third parties. Please read our Terms and Conditions for information regarding the modification or removal of information from EatLocalGrown.com Directory.

## Cookies and information collected about your visit to our site

'Cookies' are small pieces of information that may be received and stored via your browser to your computers hard drive. We do not use cookies that write any type of information to your hard drive. We do not use cookies to store or collect any personal information such as names, addresses, e-mail addresses, telephone numbers or payment information. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, to administer and tune the operation of our site, and to log your browser type and referring URL information.

Your IP address may also be used in collecting statistical data such as number of 'hits' and 'page views' which is gathered for broad usage information only. None of this information can identify you personally. When you visit our site your IP address is recorded as a normal function of the following server logs.

Access logs show us which pages an IP address accesses on the visit. Error logs show any errors your browser encountered during a visit. Agent logs show us what type of browser you used to view the site. Referer logs show us the keywords used to find our site and which search engine or point of entry you used.

We withhold the right to modify this privacy policy at anytime. However, if we do this we will provide adequate notice on our web site before any change occurs.
