# The Earth Times Online Newspaper - Privacy Policy

**Privacy update :** Our visitors privacy is important to us here at Earth Times 

**Important Update w.ef 29/2/2008:**

Earthtimes.org respects your privacy; certain information are collected from our web server for example your IP address, the type of computer you are using, the web browser, the domain server from which you access this site. The information collected is used by Earthtimes to tailor, improve the site based on userâ€™s interest. Our efforts are to provide complete information in simple and easy to understand language. 

**Cookies:**

Earthtimes makes use of the cookies to save visitor preferences for example the " **Show news from section** " , the Earthtimes **themes**. Cookies may also be used by our Advertising Partners for example [Google.com](http://www.google.com/). It should be noted that our Advertising Partners may from time to time collect information such as the visitors IP address, Browser, Location, Screen resolution etc. The information collected are used to understand visitor behavior and thereby enhance the advertising experience, by offering products that might be of interest.

Consult the privacy policy of <http://www.google.com/privacy.html>, for further information.

**Member Section:**

Earthtimes.org provides Email alert facility free of charge to registered users. We collect User Name and the Email id. The information collected and stored is used only by the earthtimes.org, no information is sold or shared with any other party.

**User Comments/ Email this Article:**

When visitors either send an article via email and/or leaves a comment, the email id is collected, this information is not displayed anywhere on the web site earthtimes.org, and neither shared with any other party.

**We reserve the right to change or update this policy at any time by placing a prominent notice on our site. Such changes shall be effective immediately upon posting to this site.**
