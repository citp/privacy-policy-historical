Online Privacy & Security Policy

Policy Last Updated: August 6, 2006

Easy Fundraising Ideas (hereafter EFI) is concerned about privacy on the Web. As such, we have created this Privacy Policy to let you know how EFI assembles and safeguards your information related to this Site. This Privacy Policy works in conjunction with the Terms of Use, but does not modify or supersede it.

1. Preventive Security. EFI is HACKER SAFE® Certified. This means our website is audited and certified daily to pass the FBI/SANS securtiy test as well as the Payment Card Industry (PCI) Data Security Standard Requirements of Visa CISP and AIS, MasterCard SDP and American Express DSS programs.

2. EFI's Collection and Use of Your Information

A. Personal Information.

1) Logging On. Like most Internet sites, EFI uses your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer this Site. Your IP address is also used to gather broad, demographic information. This information is for internal use only and will not be disseminated to advertisers. However, name and specific registrant information is not linked to broad demographic data.

2) Submitted Information. In certain areas of the site, EFI asks you to provide it with personal information in order to submit contact or information requests. EFI may use the contact information to send you information about EFI and the products that it has to offer. Generally, EFI may collect the demographic and profile data for internal use only to tailor your experience at our Site, showing you material that EFI thinks you might be interested in, and displaying the material according to your preferences.

3) Purchases. When you make an online purchase, the information you provide allows EFI to process and fill your order, and to notify you of your order status. To complete the order, EFI will need to know your credit card brand, credit card number and expiration date. Once an online order has been confirmed, credit card information is not reused or stored. When you provide any part of the requested information, you certify that any information that you provide, including without limitation, any information related to identification, billing and marketing, is truthful, accurate, complete and updated. As more fully described in Paragraph 4, your browser communicates with a secure server and all information you submit is encrypted, keeping it private. This technology makes it safer to transmit your credit card information over the Internet. Only authorized EFI employees have access to stored information. EFI safeguards information according to established security standards and procedures, and EFI continually assesses new technology for protecting information. EFI employees are trained to understand and comply with these information safeguards. For more information regarding security, see Paragraph 4.

B. Cookies. "Cookies" are pieces of data sent from a Web server to a Web browser that enable a Web server to identify users that visit a site hosted by that server. EFI may use Cookies in order to identify you to this Site so that EFI can provide more customized information and services to you. In addition, this Site may contain advertisements that may contain Cookies. To disable the use of Cookies by your browser, review your browser's preferences and options.

C. Other Information. You may choose to send EFI a question via e-mail, register for a special service or otherwise communicate with EFI. EFI uses such information to communicate with you and to enhance this Site to better meet your needs.

2. Aggregate Data. EFI may: (a) extract information that does not identify you specifically, commonly referred to as Aggregate Data, from the personal information, Cookies or other information collected on this Site; and (b) use such Aggregate Data for internal and/or commercial purposes. You hereby grant EFI and its agents and third party licensors an unrestricted, exclusive, perpetual and no cost right to develop, relicense, sell, analyze, use and exploit such Aggregate Data in accordance with the guidelines set forth in this Privacy Policy.

3. Right to Opt Out. You may choose whether to receive direct marketing communications relating to EFI. If you do not want to receive such information, you should contact EFI, in accordance with Paragraph 6, and request to "opt out." Upon receipt and processing of an opt out request, EFI will not include you in any future marketing communications. You may also use the information contained in the Updating Email Subscription section of our Help Center for other opt out options.

4. Security. EFI and its third party licensors have implemented security measures to help protect against the risk of loss, misuse and alteration of any information under EFI's control. Specifically, EFI uses a Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol at high-grade encryption (RC4 128 bit) to safeguard the security of your transactions. When making a purchase or entering personal information, SSL technology encrypts your information in an effort to prevent others from obtaining it. Nevertheless, such security measures may not prevent all loss, misuse or alteration of information on this Site, and EFI is not responsible for any damages or liabilities relating to any such security failures. Furthermore, this Site may contain links to other sites. EFI is not responsible for nor can EFI ensure the privacy practices or content of such other sites.

5. Modification of Privacy Policy. EFI may change this Privacy Policy at any time by posting the changed or amended Privacy Policy on the Site. Your continued viewing of or use of this Site following the posting of any changes to this Privacy Policy will signify your assent to any such changes or amendments.
