# privacy

Privacy Policy

We at Easy Dial are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy applies to our app (Easy Dial) and our website (Easydialsearch.com).

Information gathering and usage

When installing for Easy Dial we collect anonymous usage data using an industry standard third party analytic service (Google Analytic) to better understand the performance of our service. The information we collect via third party services does not contain personal information. The information we collect is used to improve Easy Dial and the quality of our service, and is not shared with or sold to others.

Your data

Easy Dial uses third party vendors and hosting partners to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to run Easy Dial. Although Easy Dial owns the code, databases, and all rights to the Easy Dial application, you retain all rights to your data.

Security

When we transfer and receive certain types of sensitive information we redirect visitors to a SSL secure server.
