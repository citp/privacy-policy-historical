# Easypromos - Privacy policy - Create promotions and contests in your Facebook page.

## Privacy policy

1\. EASYPROMOS, SL, (hereinafter EASYPROMOS) meets the requirements established in Law 15/1999 of December 13 regarding the Protection of Personal Data and its implementing regulations, as well as the provisions of Law 34/2002 of July 11 for Information Society Services. Your personal details are treated in accordance with the provisions of that legislation. All necessary technical, organizational and security measures will be applied in order to ensure the confidentiality of data, and to prevent alteration, loss or unauthorized access or treatment thereof.

2\. Under no circumstances will your data be transferred to third parties or used for sending publicity, nor will it be used for any purposes other than those for which it was collected.

3\. Your data will be included in a file owned by EASYPROMOS and will be used for the sole purpose of providing the requested services and the performing of administrative tasks.

4\. You give your authorization and express consent to EASYPROMOS for the processing of personal data for the purposes outlined. Such data is that which you provide, or that which may be collected on the websites owned by EASYPROMOS from contact forms, through the signing-up process, through the processes of installation or activation of EASYPROMOS products and applications, and / or through any other means.

5\. You may, at any time, exercise your right to access, rectify, cancel and oppose the processing of personal data held by EASYPROMOS, by written request to sales@easypromosapp.com, with the subject "Data Protection" and proving your identity in accordance with the provisions of the law.

6\. Minors.  


  * The procurement of products, activation of promotions or use of EASYPROMOS applications is prohibited to those under the age of 14, except with the appropriate consent.
  * EASYPROMOS does not collect personal information from children under 14 unless with the express and written consent of parents or guardians. In this case, such consent shall be submitted to EASYPROMOS to sales@easypromosapp.com for its subsequent processing, verification and authorization.
  * If, at any time, EASYPROMOS finds evidence of having received data from a minor who does not meet the requirements of the regulations in force, it shall proceed to cancel and eliminate said data.



7\. Security.  
Any transfer of personal data is done using technology that meets the appropriate safety standards for data protection.

8\. Navigation with Cookies.  
Our website may use cookies - small data files that are generated on your computer to speed up navigation and ensure the operation of some of our applications. You have the chance to eliminate, and prevent the creation of, cookies by selecting the appropriate option in the browser. However, note that in some cases, the deactivation of these elements may hinder the correct operation of the website.
