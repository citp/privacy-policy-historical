# eAstrolog.com - Privacy Statement

We log access to our web site so that we can learn more about how it is being used. This information is only used in aggregate.

We do not collect any personal information, as all pages are freely accessible and do not require users to log in. 

eAstrolog displays ads from various ad networks. These third-party advertising companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
