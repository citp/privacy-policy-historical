Privacy Statement

The basic gist of this is that we won't sell or rent your private info. We care about your privacy. We hate spam, too.

This personal information is collected and used by EasyOutsource.com for the purpose of facilitating a relationship or business transaction. We will not sell, trade or rent your personally identifiable information to third parties.

HTTP Cookies

A HTTP Cookie is a piece of data that is stored by your browser in either your computer's memory or on your computer's hard drive. EasyOutsource.com uses cookies to keep track of user session identity on the website, such as tracking items in a shopping cart or maintaining access to restricted website services via EasyOutsource.com Service Account. Cookies are also used to track the effectiveness of various services provided on EasyOutsource.com website. Browsers generally allow you to disable cookies. Services on EasyOutsource.com website that require a login to an EasyOutsource.com Service Account require that cookies are enabled.
