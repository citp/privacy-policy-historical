Computer Log Files

We use log files. Log files are those files which records the information about the Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, Internet service provider (ISP), referral/exit pages, operating system, web browser, date/time, and number of clicks. This information is used to watch out the trends and analyst the statistic of blog. The log files are not linked to personally identifiable information.
