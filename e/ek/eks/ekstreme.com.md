# eKstreme.com's Privacy Policy

I have [joined Google](http://ekstreme.com/thingsofsorts/ekstremecom/joining-google) and none of the tools on eKstreme.com are official Google tools nor are they endorsed by Google. See [this](http://ekstreme.com/thingsofsorts/ekstremecom/joining-google).

Although eKstreme.com is a personal site, I do collect some information about the visitors. This page describes what exactly gets collected, why, and how it is stored. I am not a lawyer, so I will not give you a legal document, but will give you a simple explanation.

## Browsing Pattern Information

eKstreme.com uses Google Analytics. It does not store any personal information. It collects aggregate traffic data to help me understand which pages and tools are being used the most and how. This way I can improve them.

## Personal Information Submitted to eKstreme.com

The blog posts have comment forms and the information you submit can contain personally identifiable information such as your name and email address. These are never shared with anyone.

The contact page tells you how to get in touch and if you do, you are (by definition) disclosing some information about yourself such as your name, email address, and your website. This information is not disclosed to anyone and will be used to have the conversation you started when you got in touch.
