Privacy Policy
 

This website was designed to supply information about Eye On Education and its products and services. We value our relationship with you and recognize the importance of protecting the privacy of your personal information. The information we have about you is protected and secure, and we work diligently to honor your personal preferences regarding the use of your information.

If you have any questions regarding this policy or your personal information, you can contact us via e-mail: customer-service@eyeoneducation.com

Please include your full name and contact information.

Web Site Visits

When you visit our Web page, our server automatically recognizes and collects your domain name, but it will not collect your e-mail address unless you volunteer it. Eye On Education uses standard cookie technology to store visitor's preferences, record information such as the items you added to your shopping cart, and record past activity. You can disable this through your Web Browser Options. We do not use cookie technology to harvest e-mail address or other personal information. Additionally, when you view our site by linking from another web page, we may store the name of that web page. All the information collected through Cookies is for internal use only, to compile aggregate information to analyze site, page, and service usage and consumer purchasing behavior.

Security

Eye On Education uses industry-standard encryption technologies when transferring and receiving consumer data exchanged with our site. When we transfer and receive certain types of sensitive information such as financial information, we redirect visitors to a secure server and will notify visitors that they are on a secure server. We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you on our site.

Access to Special Content and Services

Access to some areas and content on the website requires that you register with Eye On Education. If you choose not to register and provide personal information, you may still use most of the Eye On Education site.

Users who submit a whitepaper download request are automatically signed up for Eye On Education's Insights eNewsletters. Subscribers can unsubscribe at any time.

E-mail

When you set up your account, make a purchase, request an professor’s examination copy, or register for free E-excerpts, Eye On Education may ask that you provide your e-mail address so we can contact you regarding your transaction. You can decide whether or not your e-mail address can be used for marketing purposes and we will respect your request. Please let us know via mail, e-mail, or phone as shown above.

Contact Information

As part of purchasing products from Eye On Education or accessing special areas on our site, you may be required to supply us with personal information. Eye On Education may use your contact information, demographics, request for information, and purchasing behaviors to periodically contact you to determine your interest in products or services or to inform you of new products and services or upcoming events.

If you do not wish to be contacted, please let us know via mail, e-mail, or phone, as shown above. If you discover any inaccuracies in your contact information corrected, please contact us as shown above, or by updating your account.

Disclosure of Personal Data

Eye On Education currently does not sell or rent any personally identifiable information to Third Parties. We do occasionally, upon careful consideration, exchange postal information with other publishers when we feel their products may be of specific interest to you. Eye On Education may also disclose customers' purchasing behaviors and customer data in aggregate form to Marketing/Industry Research Firms and Associations. If you do not wish to have your customer data exchanged or disclosed, please let us know via mail, e-mail, or phone as shown above.

Additionally, we may disclose information in special cases when we have reason to believe that disclosing this information is reasonably necessary to comply with the law, enforce or apply our terms of business, protect our rights and those of our users, or with your consent.

Links

Links may be provided from this website to other third party websites. In using such links, you should be aware that each third party website will vary in terms of its privacy and data protection policies, and Eye On Education does not take responsibility for the privacy policies of those websites.

The Eye On Education Privacy Policy is subject to change at any time. We encourage you to review the privacy policy regularly for any changes.
