E46Fanatics.com Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting E46Fanatics.com, a PerformanceIX Inc site. Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining our online information practices.

What personally identifiable information does E46Fanatics.com collect from me?

E46Fanatics.com does not collect any information without the knowledge of the user. Information may be collected in the event that a user submits content to the site via a web-based form or email. This information may then be used on the site in relation to the content submitted. Users have the option of displaying their personal information with their content or E46Fanatics.com will display the content anonymously. Any information that E46Fanatics.com attains through normal business practices will not be sold in any form to third-parties. The forum section of the site incorporates a separate privacy policy because of its nature and the information needed to register. Please be sure to read the privacy policy in the forum area before registering to be a user.

Does E46Fanatics.com use Cookies?

Yes. The E46Fanatics.com forums use cookies merely as an avenue to expedite the task of posting. Cookies are used simply so that once you enter your posting information (username, e-mail address, and password), the browser will conveniently store that information, precluding the tiresome task on the part of the user of repeatedly populating the aforementioned fields. The user has the option of turning this feature off if so inclined.

E46Fanatics.com's Commitment to Security

We have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and help prevent unauthorized access, maintain data security, and correctly use the information we collect online.

How to Contact E46Fanatics.com

If you have any questions or concerns about the E46Fanatics.com online policy for this site or its implementation you may contact us at info@E46Fanatics.com.
