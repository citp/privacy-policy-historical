# Privacy Policy - Everything USB

**Everything USB respects your privacy.**

You do not have to register or provide any personal information in order to access the content on Everything USB. However, in order to post on the [forum](http://www.everythingusb.com/forums/) or to contribute a user review, you will have to go through registration during which you must provide an valid email address only for reply notifications and admin purposes. Without your explicit permission, Unique Link Company Ltd. will not sell or share your email to anyone. 

During your session on Everything USB, data such as browser information, screen resolution, operating system and country origin are collected by third-party traffic analyzer (privacy policy) for aggregate visitor behavior analysis. Basically, the data just tells us more about how you use the Internet. The aggregated analysis only allows us to improve site functionality and usability. 

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please see: [**http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp**](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp)
