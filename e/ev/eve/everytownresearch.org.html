Everytown for Gun Safety Support Fund Privacy Statement

Updated: April, 2015

EverytownResearch.org is a website of Everytown for Gun Safety Support Fund, Inc. This Privacy Statement describes the information collection, use, and disclosure practices of EverytownResearch.org (“Site”).

Information Collection

When you visit our Site, we may collect certain personal information you knowingly choose to disclose to us, such as your name, email address, and financial information. You may provide this information when you register with the Site, send a message on the Site, or make a contribution through the Site.

When you sign up on the Site or send a message on the Site, your personally identifiable information, including your email will automatically be added to our mailing list. We will not collect email addresses of recipients of tell-a-friend emails.

The Site may also passively collect non-personal information that is automatically sent to us by your web browser or device including but not limited to your domain name and user name.

We may also collect aggregated information as you visit the Site.

Information Use & Sharing

We may use the information we collect from your browser or device to:

  • respond to your requests;
  • collect your contribution;
  • send information about like-minded organizations to you;
  • operate the Site and customize your experience;
  • identify or remedy potential threats to the Site’s security;
  • conduct research about your use of the Site and our services; and
  • for any other purpose stated at the time of collection.

Cookies

This Site is cookie-enabled to give you a more customized user experience. A cookie is a small piece of information that a website can store on a computer and later use to recognize a user. A purpose of cookies is to enhance your ability to receive the information you want. We use two types of cookies to enhance the experience of users: session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie expires when a user closes the browser in which this website was viewed. A persistent cookie expires on a pre-determined date. Users can personalize their browser settings to reject cookies, although certain features of the Site may not function as intended.

Sharing Information

From time to time, we may send you information from other like-minded organizations we think you might find interesting, including our 501(c)(4) affiliate, Everytown for Gun Safety Action Fund. We may also choose to share your personal information with such third party organizations for their own use, including sending information to you. You may unsubscribe from any of these messages at any time, by following the unsubscribe link located at the bottom of each email communication.

We may share personal information with third parties who provide services, such as service providers, affiliates, and subsidiaries in connection with the services we provide.

If we dissolve or some or all of our assets are acquired by another organization, that organization will possess the information collected by us, and it will assume the rights and obligations regarding your information as described in this Privacy Statement.

We may disclose personally identifiable information or other data to comply with law or legal requirements, enforce or apply our Terms of Service and other agreements, or protect our rights, property, or safety of our users, or others.

Third Party Links

For your convenience, this Site may contain certain hyperlinks to other websites. We make no promises or guarantees regarding data collection on the hyperlinked pages that are not operated by Everytown for Gun Safety Support Fund. We recommend that you read the privacy policy/statement for each site you visit.

Your Choices

You can unsubscribe from the Everytown for Gun Safety Support Fund mailing list at any time by following the instructions included within any email you receive from us. If you would no longer like to receive offers from other organizations, you can unsubscribe by following the unsubscribe link located at the bottom of each email communication.

Updates

From time to time, we may update this Privacy Statement. We will notify you of any material changes to our Privacy Statement by updating this page. We encourage you periodically to check this site to learn about the information we collect, use, and share. Your continued use of the Site affirms your agreement to such changes.

Contact Us

Please contact us if you have questions about our Privacy Statement at info@everytown.org.
