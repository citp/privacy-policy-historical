**Privacy/Security Statement**

Everything Furniture has taken every precaution to protect your security and privacy.

**Security**

In the course of using the EverythingFurniture.com web site we may ask you for your e-mail address, postal address, telephone number and your credit card information (if you place an order).

Information obtained at our site is encrypted to ensure your security. Our Yahoo store uses industry-standard, SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) technology to ensure that your information is encrypted. We do not and will not further access any credit card information you provide. Your credit information will not be shared with any company outside of Everything Furniture and will be used for the sole purpose of processing your order.

**Privacy**

Because we value you as a customer we will not rent, sell, or make available in any other way e-mail addresses outside of Everything Furniture. As a valued customer we will use e-mail from time to time to notify you of specials and closeouts, follow-up information on your order, etc.
