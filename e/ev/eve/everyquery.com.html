Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is designed to assist our clients and users in understanding how we collect and use personal information here at EveryQuery.com. We at EveryQuery.com are committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and would never do anything to shatter the trust of our clients in using our services.

EveryQuery.com may sometimes collect information online, primarily to make it easier and more rewarding for you to use our website. You may be asked to provide information such as your name, email address, or information about what you like and do not like about our website. We request this information so that we may provide the best experience in using our website.

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on EveryQuery.com. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to this site and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

EveryQuery.com does occasionally send email to our customers who send email to us that may require feedback. But we will never send unsolicited email, aka. SPAM. The information that EveryQuery.com obtains through your use of our site, will never be sold to third-parties.
