EverydaySource.com® has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: EverydaySource.com®

Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart. We use cookie to deliver content specific to your interests.
Choice / Opt-Out
Information about our customers is an important part of our business, and we are not in the business of selling it to others.
For Opt-in customers only, Contact information from the order form is used to send our company information, and promotional materials. For opt-out customers, contact information from the order form is used to send our customers orders and order related communication only.
