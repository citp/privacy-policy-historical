  13. Usage of cookies

    Cookies are used to offer certain services (e.g., saving session IDs, auto-login, item lists). After receiving a respective notice on the front page, you declare your acceptance to our use of cookies by visiting our website.

    Cookies are small amounts of data which are saved onto your computer by your Internet browser. Information on your visit to our Internet platform may be saved in cookies. The most important cookies regarding analyzing tool, social media plug-ins, trackers are outlined in more details under sec. 14 below.

    • Strictly necessary cookies: Cookies required for the operation of our website. They are used to provide content to you, as a user, to navigate our website (e.g. ASP.NET_SessionID) and to provide services that you have requested e.g. the usage of our customer care / RMA services or payment (e.g. PayPal). Such cookies collect personal data in the form of IP address, location data payment data and identify you as long as you are logged into our website. These cookies are deleted when you close your browser session.
    • Analytical / performance cookies: These cookies allow us to recognize how many users visit our Internet platform and how they navigate across our pages (e.g. Alexa Metrics, Pingdom, Google-Analytics). These statistics are used for internal purposes to help us to test and improve functionality. While these cookies do not identify you personally while you are not logged in, they will collect your browser information and anonymized data, for the purposes outlined above. Analytical /performance cookies are valid for various periods and can be deleted in the browser settings at any time.
    • Functional cookies: These cookies are used to recognize you when you return to our website and allow us to remember you for an improved convenience or more features (e.g. IID, IdentifyKey, viewType). These cookies remember personal settings and preferences and collect the data saved in such settings. Functional cookies expire after a maximum of 2 years or can be deleted from your browser history at any time before their expiry.
    • Targeting cookies: These cookies record which pages you visited and on which links you clicked while visiting our website and are used to link to social media or display advertising tailored to your interest. Targeting cookies are valid for various periods.

    The standard settings of most browsers are set to accept cookies. However, you can configure your browser so that it will reject some or all cookies or will ask for prior confirmation. However, if you reject all cookies, you may not be able to use all of our services without interruption.
