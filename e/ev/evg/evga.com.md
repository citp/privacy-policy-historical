# EVGA - Legal - Privacy Policy

The protection of your personal data is of highest importance to us. This Privacy Policy tells you about the information we collect from you in accordance with the regulations set forth in the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) when you use, purchase our products or services or benefit from other services (e.g. Forum /Community) from our website www.evga.com.

With checking the box "Yes, I accept EVGA's Privacy Policy" you agree to the processing of your personal data as set out below.

This privacy policy applies to the processing of personal data of data subjects in the context of using our website, purchasing goods and services via our online-shop and to all monitoring of data subject's behavior during the visit of our website.

  4. ###### Scope of collection and storage of personal data

We are processing personal data solely for the performance of a contract to which the data subject is party or in order to take steps at the request of the data subject prior to entering into a contract or for support reasons. Moreover we are processing personal data were processing is necessary for the purposes of the legitimate interests pursued by the controller or by a third party, except where such interests are overridden by the interests or fundamental rights and freedoms of the data subject which require protection of personal data.

Not providing personal data may mean that EVGA is unable to provide services to you. In the following sec 5. – 14. it is described how we process personal data and how such processing is justified.

  5. ###### Visiting our website

If you visit our websites we automatically collect and store the following information in so-called server log files which your browser automatically transmits and saves until automated deletion:

    * browser type/version
    * used operating system
    * referrer URL
    * IP address
    * host name of the accessing computer
    * time of query

The lawful processing of these data occurs for the purposes of enabling use of the website, for system security, for technical administration of the network infrastructure and for optimization of our website.

  6. ###### Usage of personal data

In accordance with the principle of data avoidance and data economy, we only collect personal data on our website when it is either required for your desired purpose, we are obligated to do so due to legal regulations or contracts, if we have a legitimate interest and/or if you voluntarily give us your consent.

We keep your personal data stored until the completion of the transaction or the enquiry, for the duration of a warranty and moreover to the extent and for the duration necessary to comply with legal obligations to which we are subject, for archiving purposes and for the establishment, exercise or defense of legal claims. In case you create an account we keep your order information until you delete your account, provided that all transactions under your account are completed and closed.

  13. ###### Usage of cookies

Cookies are used to offer certain services (e.g., saving session IDs, auto-login, item lists). After receiving a respective notice on the front page, you declare your acceptance to our use of cookies by visiting our website.

Cookies are small amounts of data which are saved onto your computer by your Internet browser. Information on your visit to our Internet platform may be saved in cookies. The most important cookies regarding analyzing tool, social media plug-ins, trackers are outlined in more details under sec. 14 below.

    * Strictly necessary cookies: Cookies required for the operation of our website. They are used to provide content to you, as a user, to navigate our website (e.g. ASP.NET_SessionID) and to provide services that you have requested e.g. the usage of our customer care / RMA services or payment (e.g. PayPal). Such cookies collect personal data in the form of IP address, location data payment data and identify you as long as you are logged into our website. These cookies are deleted when you close your browser session.
    * Analytical / performance cookies: These cookies allow us to recognize how many users visit our Internet platform and how they navigate across our pages (e.g. Alexa Metrics, Pingdom, Google-Analytics). These statistics are used for internal purposes to help us to test and improve functionality. While these cookies do not identify you personally while you are not logged in, they will collect your browser information and anonymized data, for the purposes outlined above. Analytical /performance cookies are valid for various periods and can be deleted in the browser settings at any time.
    * Functional cookies: These cookies are used to recognize you when you return to our website and allow us to remember you for an improved convenience or more features (e.g. IID, IdentifyKey, viewType). These cookies remember personal settings and preferences and collect the data saved in such settings. Functional cookies expire after a maximum of 2 years or can be deleted from your browser history at any time before their expiry.
    * Targeting cookies: These cookies record which pages you visited and on which links you clicked while visiting our website and are used to link to social media or display advertising tailored to your interest. Targeting cookies are valid for various periods.

The standard settings of most browsers are set to accept cookies. However, you can configure your browser so that it will reject some or all cookies or will ask for prior confirmation. However, if you reject all cookies, you may not be able to use all of our services without interruption.

  16. ###### Data subject's rights

You always have the followings right:

    * access to your personal data, their origin and recipients and the purpose of data processing, free of charge (Art. 15 GDPR),
    * rectification of your personal data (Art. 16 GDPR), 
    * erasure of your personal data (Art. 17 GDPR)
    * restriction of processing of your personal data (Art. 18 GDPR). 
    * right to data portability, which means the right to obtain the personal data relating to you that you have provided to us in a structured, common and machine-readable format (Art. 20 GDPR).

You may ask us at any time and at your sole discretion to object processing of your personal data (Art. 21 GDPR).

You may also revoke a consent granted by you for the processing and use of your data, which is not necessary owing to a superordinate legal regulation or directly for the processing of an existing contractual relationship, at all times.

Upon request, we will inform you if and which personal data we have stored about you in accordance with applicable law.

Any request for erasure of personal data may also be placed via our online form provided under [www.evga.com/erasure](https://www.evga.com/erasure/). You may submit any enquiry or request regarding the data subject's rights mentioned above via email [dataprotection@evga.com](mailto:dataprotection@evga.com) or by telephone 714-528-4500.
