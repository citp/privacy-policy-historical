Privacy Policy

We protect your privacy. Accordingly, we have developed this Policy in order for you to understand how we collect and use.

For Website:

  • Web log statistics, such as number of hits to our web site, downloads and updates.
  • Name and email address when you purchase.
  • Email address when you email us for support purposes.

For Software:

  • We DO NOT store your recorded files anywhere. We can not retrieve your deleted files for you. The recorded audio and video files stored on your personal computer only.
  • In order to activate , retrieve, renewal license, your license will be stored on our license server.

We track hits to our web site, number of downloads etc. through the use of standard web logs. Web log statistics do not include any personal identifying information, but they do include IP addresses. We use IP addresses to analyse web site usage, for example by country or region. We do not attempt to tie IP addresses back to individual users. We do not disclose information contained in web logs to anyone else. We will use the name and email address to send you license information when you purchase a license. We do not disclose these details to anyone else. If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via email: support@evaer.com.
