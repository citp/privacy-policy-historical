eGifter Privacy Policy

Collection of Information.

In connection with the Service, we may collect Information in the following ways:

  • Through forms filled out by a User on one or more of the Sites
  • Through the maintenance and analysis of Web server logs
  • Via e-mail and other communications between a user and a Site
  • Through the use of third-party databases from which user Information is extracted and combined with Information obtained through other means.
  • Through any surveys or other marketing questionnaires that we use for research purposes.
  • All Information provided including pictures, videos, biography and any other Information that you provide voluntarily when organizing or participating in a Group Gift.

We may also collect non-personally identifiable information from users via "cookies" (small text files placed on user computers), single-pixel GIF image files (also called "Web beacons"), Web server log analysis and other similar technological means.  We use cookies to save you time while using the Service, tell us who you are, track and target your interests in order to provide a better User experience.  Such information may be used to track site trends and enhance the user experience, and may be shared with third parties.    

Please note that cookies are widely used by many reputable companies in order to make Web sites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information about the Service as well as enhancing a personalized experience.  We believe they are critical in providing you the best user experience.

To the extent third parties may place advertising on one or more of the Sites, such third parties may utilize cookies or other technological means within the advertising to collect and utilize non-personally identifiable information.  We are not responsible for information collected by third parties in this manner, nor for the collection or use of Information by other sites to which a Site is linked.

Types of Information Collected.

The following types of Information about a user are among those that may be collected in connection with the Service:  Name, postal address, e-mail address, telephone number, cell phone number, IP address, date of birth, age, gender, payment information, social media account information, referring site, and other technical information collected by a Site's servers.

Collection of Information from Children.

The Sites are intended for users 18 years of age and over.  We do not knowingly collect or retain Information in connection with a Site from children under the age of 13 without prior parental consent.

Use of Information.

We may use Information collected in connection with the Service in the following ways:

  • To improve the user experience with the Sites
  • In connection with the operation of the Sites

  • For fraud prevention purposes.

In order to do the foregoing, we may provide the Information to trusted third parties, including but not limited to selected third party marketers as well as third party contractors providing services such as the operation of the Sites, communication services, and information verification.  We will use commercially reasonable efforts to limit use of the Information by such third parties to the specific uses set forth above. The entities with which eGifter shares personally identifying information are independent third parties and are not affiliated with eGifter.

We may also disclose Information when it determines it is necessary to comply with applicable laws or regulations, in connection with a merger, acquisition, asset purchase or sale of or by eGifter, or to protect our interests or safety, our customers, or other visitors to the Sites.

Opting Out from Offers.

At any time, a user may opt out from having the user's Information transferred by eGifter to third parties for use in such third parties' direct marketing efforts by sending a request to unsubscribe@egifter.com. Such opting out may not apply to any communications from third parties to whom we may have provided Information regarding the user.
