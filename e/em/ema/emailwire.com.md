# Emailwire.com: Press Release Distribution Service

**Emailwire.com Privacy Statement**   
The privacy of users of Emailwire.com is our priority. We use available resources to prevent personal information from falling on unwanted hands. Emailwire.com and the parent company, GroupWeb.com, do not sell, rent or share personal information collected on this site. We, however, send commercial messages from our advertisers to individuals and companies who have opted to receive news from us. 

To reach us, if you have any questions on our privacy policy, please send e-mail to [info@grouweb.com](mailto:info@groupweb.com)
