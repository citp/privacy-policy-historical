EmailDirect Privacy Statement

EmailDirect, Inc. or ("we") respect your and or (“clients” or “user”) preferences concerning the collection and use of your personal information ("data") and or (“information”). Our goal is to deliver content targeted to the industry you work in, the products you use, the role you play in your organization, and your specific preferences. In order to provide you with relevant information, fulfill your specific requests, and enhance your experience across our system, we will at times request data from you or your clients and or prospects. This policy lets you know how we collect data, why we collect it, and what choices you have concerning its use.

EmailDirect.com, Inc. takes great pride in assuring our clients their data in all forms is kept private and safe from all forms of third-parties both from outside the network and within the network, such as employees and associated partners. In addition, EmailDirect.com, Inc. participates in the EU Safe Harbor Privacy Framework as set forth by the United States Department of Commerce. To learn more please see the section below titled EU Safe Harbor. We will continue to strive to ensure that our network services and website, complies with the strict standards for online privacy, security, business practices, and transaction integrity. If you have any questions about the EmailDirect.com, Inc. Privacy Policy, please contact us via email at privacy@emaildirect.com.

The Collection and Use of Information
All information that EmailDirect, Inc. collects from its clients or from the visitors is solely owned and protected by EmailDirect, Inc. In no way do we sell, trade, or give out such information.

Collection Methods and Information Use

Log Files
 EmailDirect.com logs system information from visitors to address the ever changing requirements technically to provide our clients and visitors with the best possible experience. This information includes, IP Address, Browser Version, and other non-personal identifiable information.

Sharing
At times we will share non-personal information with our clients and or partners. All shared information is non-personal and will not link to any individual and or break any proprietary information clauses or break any privacy claims by either EmailDirect, Inc. or its clients or the individuals that the information was collected from.

Surveys
To insure we are always providing the best possible customer service or technology to our clients we may conduct surveys about our services or about industry specific topics. In no way will this information without the permission of the client or individual that the information was received from will it be traded, sold or given out to a third-party.

Cookies or Web Beacons
Client-side cookies are used to keep login-information for our clients within our services linked to our public site. Web Beacons may be used in emails sent from our clients through our service to track and measure the success of a particular marketing campaign. In no way do client-side cookies link to personal identifiable information.

Client Customer Data
Our system allows clients to keep their customer’s private information and buying habits on EmailDirect, Inc. servers. This data may or may-not be personal identifiable information. However each client’s data is kept securely separate from all EmailDirect, Inc. clients and can only be accessed by the client in which provided the information and the employees that need to deal with the clients accounts. All EmailDirect, Inc. employees sign a comprehensive nondisclosure agreement and only those employees can access the information on a need-to-know basis. All information is protected by leading edge technology and security processes are in place to insure the privacy of all clients customer’s data.


Other Information
Clients of EmailDirect, Inc. will place email address and personal data and or content on the EmailDirect, Inc. servers. EmailDirect, Inc. does not monitor content or review this information nor can other clients that did not add this information. EmailDirect, Inc. servers use the content to construct email messages and the data provided to deliver electronic messages. EmailDirect, Inc. will not use client-provided content or data for any purpose whatsoever other than to construct and send messages as intended by the client providing the data and or content. Email addresses and or data will never be used by EmailDirect, Inc. for solicitation nor will they ever be provided to any third party or be accessible through EmailDirect, Inc. by any user or client other than the client providing the data unless to the discretion of the policies laid out below under the title Disclosure of Information.

Disclosure of Information

Also, certain technical processing of email messages and their content that is required for: (1) sending and receiving messages; (2) conform to connecting networks' technical requirements; (3) conform to the limitations of the Service; or (4) other similar technical connecting requirements.

As described above throughout each section of this privacy policy third-party companies which means EmailDirect, Inc.’s clients may collect information on you for purposes pertaining to their business needs. If you feel that we need to get involved on our clients collecting information practices please contact us at privacy@emaildirect.com

Security
The importance of security for all personally identifiable information associated with our clients and their customers is of utmost concern to us. We exercise great care in providing secure transmission of information, and utilizing encryption software. We store the information in a database in a secure environment at a highly secure Data Center.

Only those employees who need access to your information in order to do their jobs are allowed access, each having signed comprehensive nondisclosure agreements, which provide explicit legal confidentiality protections. Any employee who violates our privacy and/or security policies is subject to disciplinary action, including possible termination and civil and/or criminal prosecution.

Notification of Changes
At any time we may change and or update our privacy policy to conform to the regulations given to us by the U.S. Department of Commerce EU Safe Harbor Framework.
