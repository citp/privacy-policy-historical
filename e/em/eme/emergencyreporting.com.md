# Privacy Policy | Emergency Reporting

Emergency Reporting respects your privacy and takes great care to safeguard information in our possession. The company does not share customer information without permission, unless we are legally required to do so.

We restrict access to your information only to those individuals within the company who need it to provide you with services. This may include your account activation process, account support, and service news updates. 

Any third-party companies contracted to provide services are required to keep your information secure and confidential.
