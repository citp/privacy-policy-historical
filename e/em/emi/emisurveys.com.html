Privacy Policy
At EMI Surveys we respect your privacy.  EMI Surveys will never share any of your personal information, corporate information or campaign performance, without your prior knowledge and consent. All information collected, via your permission, will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, leased, or otherwise disclosed. The information our partners gather from e-mails is expressly used for contacting our clients for advertising purposes only. It is not used or shared in any other way, and certainly not with any other third party. Each of our vendors have their own privacy policies that relate directly to the lists they create. It is our job to make sure that the lists we represent adhere to the strictest privacy guidelines.
