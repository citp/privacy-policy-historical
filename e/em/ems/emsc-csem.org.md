### Privacy

The EMSC does not use spy-ware, ad-ware, spam or other annoying or intrusive advertising. We do not sell, rent, or exchange your personal information to any third party for any commercial purposes. According to the law n° 78-17 "Informatique et Libertés/software and freedoms", you have the right to access the information as well as to rectify and delete data that concerns you personally on this site.

The information we collect is used for internal review and content improvement. It will be disclosed if the EMSC is legally required to do so. It might also be shared with other institutes/organizations for scientific purposes only.
