IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Employment News 24 does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
