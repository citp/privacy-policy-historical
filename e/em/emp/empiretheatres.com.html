Why Connect With Facebook?

Empire Theatres gives you the option of signing into your Empire Insider account using your Facebook login information through Facebook Connect.

Empire Insider members who log in through Facebook Connect can easily share information from the Empire website on their Facebook profiles, including our newly expanded movie reviews feature which allows you to share movie reviews you make at empiretheatres.com on your Facebook profile.

To sign in with Facebook Connect, simply click the "Connect with Facebook" button and enter your Facebook login information. On your first sign in, you'll be asked to sync your Facebook account with your current Empire Insider account, or create an Empire Insider account if you don't already have one.

On future visits, you'll automatically be logged in if you're already logged into Facebook. If you aren't, you can simply click the "Connect with Facebook" button to login with your Facebook account information.

Syncing Your Empire Theatres and Facebook Accounts

To ensure a site experience that is customized to your user preferences, your Facebook account information needs to be synced with the information and preferences you set within your Empire Insider profile. After your accounts are synced, you will be able to login to the Empire site with just your Facebook account information.

Once logged in, you can change your preferences at any time by clicking on the "Your Account" link at the top left side of any page on the website.

Creating a New Empire Insider Account

There are many benefits to becoming an Empire Insider member, including easy site access, automated theatre preferences, exclusive offers and rewards, relevant movie information based on your preferred genres, and the ability to participate in interactive features such as our movie reviews feature.

All users participating in interactive features on our website are required to be Empire Insider members in order to protect the authenticity and validity of the comments being posted on our site.

After you create your Empire Insider account, you will be able to login with just your Facebook account information in the future. Once logged in, you can change your preferences at any time by clicking on the "Your Account" link at the top left side of any page on the website.

No Passes Accepted: Studio Pass Restricted Films

Some films when released are marked as 'No Passes Accepted' – this means certain vouchers cannot be accepted, generally for the first 10 days of the movie's release. Below are some tips on how to know if your voucher will be accepted at the theatre. Always read the terms and conditions on your voucher for detailed information on use and other restrictions (Tuesdays, RealD 3D, Special Events - Opera, WWE, etc.):

Accepted while Pass Restricted

  • Empire Theatres Gift Cards
  • Corporate Tickets: Admit 1, Night Out Certificates – purchased from Empire Theatres, or another company (Restaurants, etc.)
  • Air Miles Certificates: Movie Package for Two, Single Admission, 2 for 1 Admission
  • 'Your Ticket to the Movies' Free General Admission: Issued by a theatre for Film Interruptions, Evacuations, etc.
  • BOGO Movie Admission Coupons & Discount Coupons ($9.99 Movie Packages, $2 Off, etc.) unless otherwise stated on the coupon

Please note surcharge for RealD 3D or Empire Extra may still apply when using vouchers listed above.

Not Accepted while Pass Restricted

  • 'Enjoy the Movies' Free General Admission
  • Any Voucher reading 'This coupon is not valid for studio pass restricted films', or 'Studio Pass Restricted'

Always remember to read the terms and conditions on your voucher so that you can enjoy your night out at the movies hassle free!
