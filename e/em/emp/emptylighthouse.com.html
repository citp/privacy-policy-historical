Privacy Policy

We will protect your privacy by not sharing your email address or any other contact information with anyone. By signing up for our magazine, you agree to newsletters from the site, but may opt out at any time.

By submitting information, you agree to Empty Lighthouse Magazine's use of such information for publishing consideration. Artwork/Articles published on the site may be removed at anytime by the artist or their representatives, if requested. Such a request will be subject to a formal review.

The public feed is moderated by Empty Lighthouse and our users. Any information posted on the public feed is solely the responsibility of the user. When you share information or content like photos, videos, and links via the public feed, you should think carefully about what you are making public.

Empty Lighthouse uses cookies. A cookie is nothing more than a small text file that resides on your hard drive. Empty Lighthouse Magazine uses cookies to maintain the login status for those users who have an account with our site. You will not be able to use this feature if your browser client is set to refuse cookies.

Our Services are not directed to persons under 13. If you become aware that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us at contact@emptylighthouse.com. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we take steps to remove such information and terminate the child's account.

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on this page so that you are always aware of the information that we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. If the privacy policy changes, our users will be notified.

If you have any concerns or questions about any aspect of this policy, you may contact: contact@emptylighthouse.com
