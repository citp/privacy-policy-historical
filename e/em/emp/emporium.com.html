We use this data to verify information about you and to administer and improve Emporium.com.

Cookies
We make use of a standard feature of browser software called a "cookie" to help us recognize you when you pay a repeat visit to Emporium.com. Cookies let us personalize your shopping experience based on your preferences and help Emporium.com to work better to serve you. Most browsers accept cookies. However, if you wish, you can usually set your browser to refuse cookies. If you set your browser to refuse cookies, you may not be able to submit an order online. Emporium does not use cookies to gather any personal information.
