# Privacy Notice - Empire

Empire offers a new generation of consumer-friendly products that put individuals back in control of their health. As such, we intend to give you as much control as possible over your personal web information utilized by our site. 

You can visit us on the web without sharing information about yourself or revealing any information about who you are. Sometimes however, we may need information from you, such as your name and address or other personal information, in order to communicate with you, or allow access to special secure sections of our site. 

If you are required to furnish personal information in order for us to provide you with the services you need or request, you will be fully informed about how we intend to use this information. You always retain the choice of declining to furnish us with your personal information. 

Your personal information will always be stored confidentially and is used in order to personalize your experience with us and offer you features and services that more closely meet your needs. 

Our site contains links to other websites not controlled by Empire. The information you provide us does not transfer to those websites except as expressly permitted by you to provide you with access to benefits, services and wellness opportunities. We do not assume responsibility for the privacy practices or the content of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of those sites.

About Cookies

We utilize "cookies" (a code generated and stored to identify your computer). The cookie does not extract personal information about you. 

We use cookies so you don't have to re-enter information you have already provided us each time you visit special sections of our site. You have the ability to turn "cookies" on or off at your computer, or to have your browser tell you when a "cookie" is being sent. You can choose to accept or deny the "cookie" if you have this feature enabled on your browser. For more information on "cookies" consult the help section of your browser. 

Empire utilizes a variety of encryption and other security technologies to ensure the security of customer data. 

At Empire our mission is to provide a choice of quality health care coverage and health services designed to meet the changing expectations of individuals, families and their sponsors throughout a lifelong relationship. We believe a relationship is built on trust, and thus we make a commitment to you that your personal information will be kept in confidence by us. 

The above web privacy notice is effective as of May 1, 2002. Empire reserves the right to change the web privacy notice from time to time. This privacy notice applies only to online activity on this website and is not intended to and does not imply any contractual or other legal rights in respect to Empire or any other party.
