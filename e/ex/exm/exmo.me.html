  • to ensure the most effective presentation of the content of our website to you and use it from your computer;
  • for administration of our website and for internal operations, including troubleshooting, data analysis, testing, statistical data collection and for conducting surveys;
  • to improve our website and to make sure that our content is most convenient for the operating system of your computer;
  • to give you access to the interactive features of the website in case you wish to use them;
  • to ensure the security of our website, our services, and your accounts;
  • to evaluate and understand the effectiveness of promotional materials that we provide to you and other users, as well as to provide you with the necessary advertising information;
  • to support, respond and resolve your complaints and issues related to use of our services and the capabilities of our website.

We may share your personal information with any member of our group, which means our subsidiaries, affiliates, our ultimate holding company and its subsidiaries.

We may share your information with selected third parties including:

  • business partners, suppliers and sub-contractors for the performance of any contract we enter into with them or you;

We may also use such aggregate information to help advertisers reach the kind of audience they want to target (for example, women in SW1). We may also disclose your Personal Data to third parties in the following cases:

  • in case it is necessary to protect own rights, own property, to guarantee the safety of EXMO, our users or others. This includes the exchange of information with companies and organizations to prevent fraud and reduce the likelihood of default.

We collect and process your Personal Data only when we have a legitimate reason for such processing. Legal grounds include consent (in cases where you provide it), a contract (in cases where processing is necessary to fulfill the terms and conditions of contracts between you and EXMO), compliance with a legal obligations (in cases where EXMO is required to request / receive and process as well as to store your Personal Data in order to comply with the requirements of applicable laws, for example the laws related to anti-money laundering and counter-terrorist financing and legitimate interest (for example, when processing is necessary to protect you or us from certain threats (fraud, security, etc.), compliance with the provisions of applicable laws, the proper level of conduct of our business (the quality of the services provided, identification of needs, preparation of reports), conclusion and implementation of corporate transactions (sale of participant interests / shares, mergers, acquisitions), etc.).

In cases where the basis for data processing is the consent, you have the right to withdraw it at any time.

  • the right to delete your data (all or part thereof);
  • the right to change or correct your data, in particular when it is incorrectly stated;
  • xthe right to object or restrict processing of your data;
