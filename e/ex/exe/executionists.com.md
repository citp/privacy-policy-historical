# Executionists | Web Design, Development and Marketing Agency Privacy Policy - Executionists | Web Design, Development and Marketing Agency

This Privacy Policy covers the privacy practices for Executionists.com. This privacy notice applies solely to information collected by this web site.

### Ownership of Information, Collection and Usage

We only collect information that you voluntarily give us via direct contact

We will only use your information to respond to you regarding the reason you contacted us. We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill your request, e.g. to learn more about our services or third party product offers. Except as expressly set forth herein, we will never loan, give away, sell, distribute or rent your personal information to any third party.

In order to acquire a free consultation on this website a user must first complete the consultation form. In this form, a user is required to give certain information (such as name and email address). This information is used to contact you about the services or products on our site in which you have expressed interest.

### Links

Where the Executionists website contains links to other sites, please be aware that Executionists is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage you to be aware when you leave any of our sites and to read the Privacy Policies of each and every Website that collects personally identifiable information. This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected by Executionists.

### Notification of Changes

We reserve the right to change or modify our privacy practices that are described herein simply by posting a revised Privacy Policy on this Website. Any such change shall be effective immediately upon posting of the revised Privacy Policy on this Website. We reserve the right to make the revised or changed Privacy Policy effective for information we already have about you, as well as any information we receive in the future. We encourage you to refer to this Privacy Policy on an ongoing basis so that you understand our current privacy practices.

### LEGAL DISCLAIMER

Though we make every effort to preserve user privacy, Executionists may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order, or legal process served on the Executionists website.

### Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, the practices of Executionists, Inc., or your dealings with the Executionists Website, please contact us at the following address: **[info@executionists.com](mailto:info@executionists.com)**
