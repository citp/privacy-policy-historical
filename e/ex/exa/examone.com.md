# Privacy Policy | ExamOne: A Quest Diagnostics Company

## Privacy Policy

Exam _One_ and its affiliates (referred to collectively in this policy as “Exam _One_ ”) are committed to respecting and protecting your privacy and personal information (i.e. information that can personally identify you as an individual).

Exam _One_  does not use any electronic means to automatically collect personal information from you or your computer. However, Exam _One_ ’s web servers will automatically collect IP addresses and we may view our web server IP log from time to time to prevent fraudulent acts and possible network attacks.

The only personal information Exam _One_ collects via the Website is the information voluntarily supplied by you if you contact a member of Exam _One_ via the Website.

It is Exam _One_ ’s policy to treat your personal information as private and confidential and to restrict the use and disclosure of such personal information to the specific purpose for which it was provided. Exam _One_ will not sell or in any manner distribute your personal information to any third party unless specifically authorized by you to do so or as required by law.

Although we strive to ensure that your personal information is protected at all times, we cannot guarantee that your communication over the Internet is fully secure or that the information you transmit to Exam _One_ will not be intercepted and/or misused by third parties.

At all times, you shall have the right to access, verify and amend your personal information provided to and maintained by Exam _One_. In addition, you also have the right to withdraw your consent to the collection and treatment of your personal information by Exam _One_. To access, edit or delete your personal information provided to Exam _One_ , please contact [privacy@labone.com](mailto:privacy@labone.com). Exam _One_ reserves the right to maintain your personal information off-line as may be required or permitted by applicable law.

In providing Exam _One_ with personal information through the Website, you consent to the collection and treatment of that information in accordance with this privacy policy.
