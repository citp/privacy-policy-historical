# Exide Life Insurance - Privacy Policy

Exide Life may be required to disclose information pursuant to applicable law, rule, regulation or such other requirements as may be in force from time to time.

Exide Life Insurance undertakes not to disclose, except as otherwise provided, the Personal Information provided by You to any person, unless such action is necessary to: 

  * - Conform to legal requirements or comply with legal process
  * - Protect and defend Exide Lifes or its Affiliates rights, interests or property
  * - Enforce the terms and conditions of the products or services or Terms and Conditions; or
  * - Act to protect the interests of Exide Life, its Affiliates, or its members, constituents or of other persons



Except as otherwise expressly permitted under copyright law, no copying, redistribution, re-transmission, publication or commercial exploitation of material will be permitted without our express permission or that of the copyright owner. In the event of any permitted copying, redistribution or publication of copyright material, no changes in or deletion of author attribution, trademark legend or copyright notice shall be made.

### Use Of Information

The User of this website specifically acknowledges and agrees that Exide Life will not be liable for any illegal and/or slanderous conduct of any person. Exide Life's website sometimes requires Users to give personal information in order to do things like access webcasts, download documents, participate in online surveys, or to provide feedback. In situations like these, where Users are accessing the website, Exide Life recognizes Users interest in the product or service and considers that to be "opting in"to allow Exide Life and appropriate business partners of Exide Life to contact Users with information, special offers, or promotional materials in the future. Exide Life may transfer Users personal information to or share it with the business partner only for future marketing, development of new products, customer service, delivery of products, aftercare service and related activities thereof. If the User does not wish to be contacted, the User is requested to intimate Exide Life accordingly.

Exide Life may also collect information in other ways, such as running quizzes, contests or any other program on Exide Life's site or otherwise, in which Exide Life will ask the User for contact information (like postal and email address), unique identifiers (like password), and demographic information (like occupation). If User does not wish to be contacted, Exide Life requests that the User does not participate in these promotions.

Exide Life may share the information provided by the User with any other person(s) including its affiliates, business partners, re-insurers, and other third parties such as service providers. In addition, should the law or an authority require to do so, Exide Life may disclose any information provided by the User in the website, as required. Further, Exide Life will release personal information to (i) enforce or apply Exide Life agreements, (ii) protect Exide Life rights and property; and (iii) protect integrity or safety of Exide Life and this site.
