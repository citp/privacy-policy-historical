# Free RSS Software Tools

 

We respect your right to privacy. We will never sell your name to a third party without your express consent.

Because we feel your privacy is so important, we are providing this page to describe when and how we collect and use your information.

#### **Order**

Our site uses an order form for customers to order our products. We collect contact information (email addresses, phone numbers), and financial information (account or credit card numbers). We will use contact information from the order form to ship orders. Telephone numbers are optional but can be useful for contacting you if there is a problem with your order.

We does not sell or use your private information (name, address, phone, email address, etc.) for any purpose other than to ship you your order or to contact you if there is a problem with your order.

You will receive a confirmation email immediately after you order. You will later receive a registration code by email, and possibly a follow up thank you email. Your email address is not used for any other purpose. To receive notifications of future updates, you need to subscribe to our newsletter.

#### **Our Software**

  * Virus-Free guaranteed. We always take full virus detection on the files before we release our software to web server. 100% Virus-Free guaranteed. 
  * Spyware-Free guaranteed. We guarantee that we do not make spywares! We highly respect our users' privacy. 
  * No ads. We will not install any ad-component on your computer. 



#### **Free Registration**

We provide free temporary key codes for a registration to allow the users to unreveal all software features for an evaluation period. Registration process is similar to ordering, except that you do not need to provide your credit card number. We do not use the information about registered users at all. We only collect statistics to compile general demographic information.

#### **Web site**

IP addresses of visitors to our Web site are used to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. We use IP addresses also to compile general demographic information. We will not attempt to identify individual users through their IP addresses.

We sometimes use an outside advertisement company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. Our ad company may collect cookies received with banner ads, and we do not have access to this information. We do not use cookies.

#### **Forum**

To post messages into our forum, you do not need to register or provide your contact information. However, you may place your contact information inside a message.

#### **Bug Report**

When you report us about a bug that you have found in our software, you may provide your email address. We will use it only to ask for details about this bug and to send a key code for fixed version of a product as reward for your report. 

#### **External Links**

This site contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
