# The Extra Money Blog – Privacy Policy

I am committed to making your experience on this blog safe and convenient.

I will never disclose or sell your e-mail address or any of your data from this site.

All of the information on this website is original and copyright protected EasyExtraMoneyOnline.com, ExtraMoneyBlog.com, Easy Extra Money Online and The Extra Money Blog – All rights reserved.  Duplication of any information is prohibited under the copyright laws.

## Log Files

Like most other websites, I collect and use the data contained in log files. The information in the log files include your IP (internet protocol) address, your ISP (internet service provider, such as AOL or Time Warner), the browser you used to visit our site (such as Internet Explorer or Firefox), the time you visited my site and which pages you visit.

## Cookies

I do use cookies to store information, such as your personal preferences when you visit this site. This is generally used for geotargeting purposes (showing Los Angeles restaurants to Los Angeles residents, for example) or showing certain ads based on specific sites visited (such as showing web development ads to someone who frequents web design sites).

You can choose to disable or selectively turn off our cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings, or by managing preferences in programs such as Norton Internet Security.

However, this can affect how you are able to interact with my site as well as other websites. This could include the inability to login to services or programs, such as logging into forums or accounts.

## Interest Based Ads

This website allows third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit the website. These companies may use browsing information (not including your email address or other personal information) about your visit to this and other websites in order to serve advertisements most relevant to you. See Cookies section above.

## Third-Parties & Sponsor Ads

Text and image ads that appear on this website are placements posted by third party and are sponsor ads. I may not necessarily personally endorse the products and services advertised in these ads as sometimes we have no control over what contextual ad vendors such as Chitika and Google decides to show. The companies displayed are not necessarily affiliated with us.

If you have questions about any affiliations on this website, please don’t hesitate to contact me. I would be more than happy to clarify or provide additional details if necessary.

Income generated from this website includes product sales, private ads, public ads and affiliate commissions.

## Google Adsense

The Google’s AdSense program uses the [DoubleClick DART cookie](http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/faq.aspx). Google’s use of the DART cookie enables this site to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).

## Your Email ID

If you contact me or subscribe to any of my information via email, I will never sell or give away your email address.

## Disclaimer

Because of my educational and practical background, some of the technical information on this website may entice you to take action based on my personal opinions or recommendations. I strongly recommend researching all ideas thoroughly and conducting your due diligence before implementation.

All tax, business related, legal and/or any other technical advice on this website must be consulted with a CPA, attorney, financial planner, business advisor or any other licensed technical professional prior to implementation.

Remember that this site is meant for you! I welcome you to continue to contribute your thoughts and opinions to the community. Your valuable feedback will continue to shape the direction of this blog.
