# ExtMatrix - The Premium Cloud Storage

We at ExtMatrix.com respect your privacy. All information collected at ExtMatrix.com is kept confidential and will not be sold, resued, or rented in any way. We do not share your infromation with any 3rd parties.

The information we collect:

 **IP Addresses**  
IP addresses are logged by ExtMatrix.com to ensure account safety features availability, measuring usage and statistics.

 **Email Addresses**  
We collect email addresses of users at the time of registration as means of contact and verification. ExtMatrix.com does not rent, sell, or share your emails with anyone.

 **Disclosures of Exceptional Kind**  
We at ExtMatrix.com may disclose your information if necessary to protect our legal rights or if the information suggests to actual act of violence or threatened harm or potential threats to the physical safety of any person or persons. Disclosure may also be required by law or if we receive a legal notice or similar request from authorities.

 **Cookies**  
ExtMatrix.com uses cookies to store the preferences of users, only if they so choose.

 **Third-Party Cookies**  
The third-party advertisers we might have may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.

Third-Party Advertising  
We use third-party advertising companies. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other web sites for statistical purposes.

 **Changes to Privacy Policy**  
We reserve the right, at any time and without notice, to add to, change, update, or modify this Privacy Policy. Any change, update, or modification will be effective immediately upon posting on the site.
