ExtremeFitness Privacy Policy

ExtremeFitness takes user privacy very seriously. Absolutely no information submitted to ExtremeFitness via membership, feedback form, or mailing list will be given out, sold, or distributed to any outside organization. Your contact information is used to contact the visitor (you) when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings by contacting ExtremeFitness. Children under the age of 14 are not permitted to register on our site, nor are they permitted to submit personal information via feedback forms or through our mailing list.

These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
