Cookies are not programs that come onto a user’s system and damage files. Generally, cookies work by assigning a unique number to the user that has no meaning outside the assigning site. Users should be aware that KCO cannot control the use of cookies or the resulting information by advertisers or third parties using WEBJOBZ, to display content.
