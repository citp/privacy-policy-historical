We here at ExpatExpress.com view your privacy with the utmost respect. We do not sell, rent, or loan any identifiable information regarding our customers to any third-party. We may use and share aggregated information when it enables us to improve and enrich our product and content offering. Any information you give us is held with the utmost care and security, and will not be used in ways to which you have not consented. 

**Information Collection and Use**  
In order to provide you with excellent products and services, ExpatExpress.com asks you for specific pieces of personal information when you register as a customer. ExpatExpress.com will not collect any personal information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals.
