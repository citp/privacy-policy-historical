## Privacy Policy

This is the web site of eFavormart.com at http://www.efavormart.com. We can be reached at [sales@efavormart.com](mailto:sales@efavormart.com).

This web site does collect personal information such as name, shipping address, billing address, telephone number, e-email address, and payment information when placing an order. The information that we collect on the web site is used solely in helping you fulfill your order. Any information provided throughout the ordering and/or registration process is kept strictly confidential. It will not be shared or sold to third parties. We respect your privacy and have taken every precautionary step to ensure that it is protected as you order from our web site. 

Shopping online at eFavormart.com is completely safe. We have a firm commitment to do our utmost best in ensuring that others may not intercept or have access to the private information that you provide to us This site has security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse, and alteration of your information. We use industry standard efforts to safeguard the confidentiality of your personal identifiable information, such as firewalls and Secure Socket Layers (SSL) where appropriate. All orders are encrypted and transmitted through a Secure Socket Layer (SSL). SSL is fully supported by Microsoft Internet Explorer and Netscape Navigator. All sensitive information will be encrypted before being sent over the Internet. This means that your private information will remain private.
