Privacy Policy


Escape Motions, s.r.o. ("Escape Motions" or "we", “our” or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

Escape Motions’ privacy policy covers the collection and use of personal information that may be collected by Escape Motions anytime you interact with us, such as when you visit our website, purchase and use an Escape Motions software product, contact a technical support, create an account in our Community center or sign up for an e-newsletter.

Personal information does not include information such as data, messages, text, graphics, images, photos, profiles, works of authorship and any other materials that have been posted on to the Escape Motions website. The use of this information is detailed in our Terms of Use.

We may modify this Privacy Policy from time to time without notice. When we do we will update the “Last Updated” date. It is therefore important that you regularly review this Privacy Policy to ensure you are updated as to any changes.

Who Controls the Personal Information


All Personal Information collected is in the control of Escape Motions, s.r.o. in the event that Escape Motions, trading names and brands are subject to a change of ownership, the control and permissions that you have granted us with regard to your Personal Information will be transferred to the new owner.

Information We Collect


Information You Provide to Us

- When you place an order with us for our software products, the information we collect may include your name, e-mail address, mailing address, billing address, shipping address and, if applicable, EU VAT number or other tax ID. We will also keep the details of your order and of its fulfillment. We never store your credit card information.

- When you create account on our Community website, we may collect your username and e-mail address for your account.

- When you sign up to receive our newsletters, we may collect your name and email address. By confirming the subscription you give us consent to send you occasional development updates and marketing communications.

- When you purchase or download a full version of an Escape Motions software we have a legitimate interest in sending you free updates, upgrade offers, new software releases and other news. If you change your mind, you can update your consent or email preferences at any time using the link provided at the bottom of each email you receive from us. If you do not want Escape Motions to contact you, you may “opt-out” at any time. You may “opt-out” by contacting Escape Motions Customer Service Department or by following the instructions included in the newsletter.

When we process your personal information for our legitimate interests, we make sure to consider and balance any potential impact on you, and your rights under data protection laws. Our legitimate interests do not automatically override your interests. We will not use your personal information for activities where our interests are overridden by the impact on you, unless we have your consent or those activities are otherwise required or permitted to by law.

We collect your personal information because it assists us in delivering a high level of customer service. For example, your personal information helps us to keep you informed and up-to-date on the latest product updates and announcements as well as special offers that may be extended from time to time.

Information We Collect from our Software

We collect information when you use the Escape Motions software. When you use our software while your computer is connected to the Internet, we collect your registration details of the software, IP address, home-dir and MAC address of the computer, the version of the software and operating system. This is needed to ensure that the software is used in accordance with the software license agreement, to ensure the correct content is shown in the welcome screen of the applications and to identify a possible update notification.

Escape Motions software also collect crash report data anonymously.

Cookies

Cookies are unique identifiers which are used to customize your website, mobile application or social media site experience. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent or notify you whenever you are sent a cookie. This gives you the chance to decide whether or not to accept the cookie. We may use cookies to collect your personal information and settings (language preferences, discounts, etc.). We use the information it provides to help us improve our website, mobile application, social media site and our services, and to provide our visitors with a better visit. Even without accepting a cookie you can still access all features on the site. There may, however, be limitations on your use of some site functions.

You can also choose not to accept the cookies by clicking “I disagree” button on the information window that pops up when you visit our website. Here you can also view our cookie declaration.

How We Use Your Personal Information


Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:
- To provide and improve our software products (your information helps us better respond to your individual problems and needs).
- To improve customer service (your information helps us effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs).
- To carry out our obligations and enforce our rights arising from any contracts entered into between you and us, including for billing and collection.
- To send periodic emails about our own products that may be of interest to you (the email address you provide for order processing may be used to send you information and updates pertaining your order, in addition to receiving occasional company news, updates, related product or service information, etc.) and to keep you informed about all the news from our world.

Note: If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from receiving newsletters, we include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email. - To administer a contest, promotion, survey or other feature.

We are not liable or responsible for the personally identifiable or other information you choose to submit in the community center of our website such as a forum, gallery or any other publicly accessible area of the site.


How Long We Store Your Personal Information


If you purchase an Escape Motions software, download a full version of an Escape Motions software or subscribe to our newsletter, we will store your data for up to three years from the date of the latest purchase or from the day you subscribed. If you do choose to opt into our marketing, we will continue to contact you via email until you opt out. All of our emails contain an unsubscribe link.

Where We Store Personal Information


Website Data

We store all personal data collected via our website and forum on secure servers administered by Exohosting, s.r.o., located in the Slovak republic (SK).

Payments

We use Paypal (www.paypal.com) and Braintree (www.braintreepayments.com) payment gate ("Payment Gate") to process payments made via our store. After purchase we will pass your order details to Payment Gate to complete the transaction. Payment Gate will store some of your details as a reference against your transaction in case we need to process a refund or provide support, and for fraud prevention purposes.

For more information, see Paypal’s and Braintree’s Privacy Policy.

Email Marketing

Some of our email marketing is administered through Mailerlite (www.mailerlite.com), and we may store some of your information - including your name, email address and contact preferences - on a Mailerlite server. If you contact Mailerlite directly regarding your subscription to our email communications, Mailerlite may contact you directly; otherwise, they will never contact you. For more information, see Mailerlite’s Privacy Policy.
