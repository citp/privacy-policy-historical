# Essential Wholesale | Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**  
Essential WholesaleTM is concerned about the privacy of its users and is committed to providing a secure environment for transactions. 

All areas of this site are open to all visitors to read, search, browse, and download, at no cost. To order wholesale supplies requires the submission of customer verification and contact information. Visitors need not register or give any personal identification to use the general areas. (No software or hardware techniques are employed to capture a visitor's e-mail address or browser identity. We do not use "cookies" to track how you use our site. 

We employ a simple "count" system that tells us how many visits the site receives in a given period, but no identities or individually-identifiable information is gathered by this process. Where visitors choose to provide the site with a name, e-mail address or other information in order to purchase products or receive e-mail notices of new products, Essential WholesaleTM keeps all such response lists confidential. From time to time we do send out promotional emails to our customers. 

If you have any questions about the above mentioned statement please contact us via email: [ Info@essentialwholesale.com](mailto:Dennis@essentialwholesale.com)
