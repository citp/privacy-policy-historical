# Essential Wholesale | Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

Essential Wholesale is concerned about the privacy of its users and is committed to providing a secure environment for transactions. Our website shopping cart is 128 bit encrypted for your protection.

All areas of this site are open to all visitors to read, search and browse at no cost. To order wholesale supplies requires the submission of customer verification and contact information when placing the order on our secure shopping cart. Visitors need not register or give any personal identification to use the general areas. (No software or hardware techniques are employed to capture a visitor's e-mail address or browser identity. We do not use "cookies" to track how you use our site. 

We employ a simple "count" system that tells us how many visits the site receives in a given period and which pages are the most used, but no identities or individually-identifiable information is gathered by this process. Where visitors choose to provide the site with a name, e-mail address or other information in order to purchase products or receive e-mail notices of new products, Essential Wholesale keeps all such response lists confidential. From time to time we do send out promotional emails to our customers. If you'd like to sign up for the Monthly E-Newsletter Mailing List, then go to the HOME page and enter your information in the box to the right. We _do not_ sell or give any information provided by our customers or site users to any third party company for any reason.

If you have any questions about the above mentioned statement please contact us via email.
