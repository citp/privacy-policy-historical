Privacy Policy

The statements below show details of our policy, and about what personal information we collect every time you visit the EssayShark.com website.

The information we collect is only used for improving the content of our webpages and for customizing it in accordance with our clients’ individual preferences. No personal information collected for these purposes is disclosed to any third parties.

Information we collect as you browse our website

The statistical data that our webserver collects as you browse the website includes information about your IP address, the type of browser you are using, your operating system and access times. Sometimes, cookies can be used to customize the content of our webpages, based on your browser type, IP address or other anonymous information.

About Cookies

Cookies are text files used to identify your computer to our server. Cookies identify the computer being used, not a specific individual user. This tool is used by many different websites to monitor visitor traffic.

Cookies gather information about areas of the website the user visits, and the time spent on a particular page. Users can modify browser settings to accept all cookies, to receive a notification every time a cookie is used, or not to receive cookies at all. In case of the last option, the user might not be able to use some personalized services.

Use of optional information

On some webpages, you may be asked to provide some personal information, including your name, e-mail address or some other contact details. Please note that such information is never distributed to third parties; it is only used to enable communication with the customer while the order is in progress. Demographic information you provide is used exclusively for internal market research purposes.

If you contact us by e-mail to report your feedback, express a request, or ask for additional information, we only use your personal contact information (including your e-mail address) to respond to your request. This information is not shared, sold, or disclosed to any other party.

Future developments
