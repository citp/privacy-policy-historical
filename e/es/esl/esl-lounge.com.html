Your Privacy Is Our Priority

esl-lounge.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: esl-lounge.com

Information Automatically Logged

There is information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, screen size, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by esl-lounge.com for the operation of the service, to maintain and improve quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the esl-lounge.com site.

Advertisers

We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information.

Registration Forms

Our site's registration forms sometimes require users to give us contact information (like their name, e-mail, and postal address), and demographic information (like their zip code, age, or geographical location).

Contact information from the registration forms is used to maintain and improve quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the esl-lounge.com site.

Contact information from the registration forms is also used to maintain a mailing list used by esl-lounge.com to inform site users of changes to the site and is not passed on to any third parties.

Information collected from registration forms regarding the general demographic make up of esl-lounge.com visitors (i.e. percentage of visitors from Canada) may be made available to third parties, but not that information which is associated only with individuals (i.e. e-mail addresses, names)

Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

Our online surveys ask visitors for contact information (like email address), and demographic information (like zip code, age).

Contact information from the surveys is used to maintain a mailing list used by esl-lounge.com to inform site users of changes to the site and is not passed on to any third parties.
