# Privacy Statement -

The ESL Chronicle Respects your privacy

The information you provide on our site is used to contact you if necessary, the improve the website and nothing else.If you are subscribed to the RSS feed with email address then it will be stored and used only to send you article updates and not for any other purpose. Any personal information you enter on The ESL Chronicle including your name, address, telephone number and email address will not be rented, sold, or released to any persons or entities outside of The ESL Chronicle.

Third Party Information  
There are links to other sites as a way to provide you with more information that may be found on other domains. These links are for informational purposes only and does not mean that we endorse those websites. The ESL Chronicle is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites and we encourage you to read such sites privacy policy before submitting any personal information.

The ESL Chronicle reserves the right to change or replace this Privacy Statement at any time and from time to time without any prior notice to or consent.The content on this website is for informational purposes only. If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy please see the contact us page.
