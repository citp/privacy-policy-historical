# ESRB privacy policy summary

## What Information May Be Collected

  * First and last name
  * Email and physical address
  * Phone number
  * Demographic (e.g. country, etc.)
  * Technical
  * Credit Card Payment Information



## Why It's Collected

  * Analytics
  * Internal operations
  * To allow a user to submit a complaint or otherwise contact us
  * To allow a publisher to create an account
  * To make payment or fulfill a transaction



## With Whom It's Shared

  * Internal operations' service providers
  * Merchant Services Provider
  * Third parties if necessary to address a user complaint
  * Third parties if compelled by a legal obligation



## Where Is It Stored and Processed

  * All collected information is stored and processed in the United States
