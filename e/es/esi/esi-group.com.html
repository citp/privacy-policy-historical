Privacy & Terms Of Use

Collection of personal data

Registering on this site will allow you to download documents, access data directly via this site or have it sent to your email address. Registration requires the communication of some personal data: first name, last name, email address, country.

Protection of children

We do not process any data concerning children and refuse registration for minors under the age of 18. By pressing the registration key, you automatically confirm that you are over 18.

Use of personal data

The data you provide will be used as follows:

  • analysis of sales statistics,
  • creation of personalized sales proposals.

This data will neither be sold nor communicated outside of our distribution network. If you do not wish to provide this information, you will not be able to access the documents, information and services available on this website.

Confidentiality and communication

The data will be kept on an ESI Group server, or a supplier’s server, protected by all the necessary precautionary measures to avoid fraudulent access. ESI Group will respect the confidential nature of such data, which will not be communicated to any third party outside of ESI companies, its distributors and agents. However, if illegal use is made of this web site, if criminal law is broken or if any acts of counterfeiting, defamation or unfair competition are committed and traced back to you, we reserve the right to transmit your data to the appropriate legal authority.

Rights of access, correction or removal of data

You may contact us at any time to check the way in which your data has been registered, to request a correction or the removal of your data should you decide to unsubscribe. To do this, please use the contact form.

Cookies

In some circumstances, cookies may be used. If you refuse this option, please make the necessary changes to your Internet navigator.

Terms of use of the website documents

The documents you can download here are protected by the Copyright laws of their authors and beneficiaries. Your use of these documents should be strictly personal. Copies may be made only in the case of information sharing with your colleagues. Apart from this, you agree not to send the documents to any third parties, to respect the authors’ rights, not to change the text, or to remove the Copyright mentions.
