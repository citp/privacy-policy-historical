# Euroleague TV - Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting WWW.EUROLEAGUE.TV ("the Website") powered by [WWW.NEULION.COM](http://www.neulion.com/)

This Privacy Policy explains how NeuLion, Inc. (as owner of NEULION.COM), and Euroleague Properties SA (as owner of EUROLEAGUE.TV), ("the Companies"), use information we collect about you and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. 

The Companies provide an online video service offering a selection of live sports events, edited highlights, extended highlights and other content (collectively, "Content") available on the Website. Our video service, including the Content and our video player, and any other features, tools, applications, materials or other services offered from time to time by the Companies in connection with its business are referred to collectively as the "Services".

By your use of the Website you consent to be governed by this Privacy Policy.

#### 1\. PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION

You can visit the Website without disclosing any information about yourself. However, to purchase any live events viewable on this Website, participate in any contest or message feature, download any content, take part in any other interactive feature of the Website, or receive information from the Companies, such as newsletters or email regarding upcoming the Services, you must register and provide accurate information about yourself to the Companies, as the Services may not work properly without such information.

Additionally, the Website contains links to third-party websites that are not operated by the Companies. These third parties may collect data from you and share some of that data with us. The Companies are careful when choosing its partners, sponsors, and affiliates, but the Companies are not responsible for any misuse of personal information by such third parties. We recommend that you view the privacy policy posted on each of the websites that you use to confirm that the companies' policies are acceptable to you. 

By filling out the Registration Form, you give authorization to Euroleague Properties S.A. (hereinafter "EP") to include your name, likeness, and/or contact information in a database owned and managed by EUROLEAGUE ENTERTAINMENT & SERVICES, SLU. These data will be processed in accordance with all applicable national and international data protection laws including those derived from Directive 95/46/EC. Through the Registration Form, you consent to receive from Euroleague Entertainment & Services, S.L.U., EP, EP's official sponsors and/or EP's service providers(*), newsletters, commercial information and/or promotions related to the competitions organised by EP. You can exercise your right of access to modify, cancel and object to the information provided and/or cancel your consent to receive newsletters, commercial information and/or promotions by directly contacting by email address: [marketing@euroleague.net](mailto:marketing@euroleague.net), clearly indicating your name, surname, ID number, email address.

(*) The list containing the EP's official sponsors and EP's official providers may be modified regarding the contractual commitments that EP may arise.

Please note: If you do decide to modify, cancel and object to the information provided and/or cancel their consent to receive newsletters, commercial information and/or promotions, , the Companies will be unable to keep you informed of any new products, services, or special offers that may interest you and will have an impact on our ability to inform you of any live events important information as part of any subscription service you may have.

#### 2\. AGGREGATED INFORMATION

To evaluate the Website and ensure that we are providing Content and Services of interest to you, we may collect and store statistical information regarding how you use the Website, the name of your Internet Service Provider, the Website that referred you to the Website, and the time and date you view the Website. We may also use Personal Information to develop demographic information regarding Website users. Such aggregated information will not identify you personally. We may share this aggregated, statistical information with our partners, sponsors, affiliates, and other third parties.

We may on occasion combine information we receive online with other records to enhance our ability to notify you of our products and services that we think will be of interest to you. If you do not want to receive these notifications please send an e-mail to letting us know.

#### 3\. COOKIES

From time to time the Website may place a cookie on your computer. A "cookie" is a small line of code that is stored in your browser when you visit a cookie-enabled website. A cookie allows us to track and keep records on user activity. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you set your browser to reject cookies, it is possible that some parts of the Website will not function. Moreover, in order to use the Services, your browser must accept cookies.

#### 4\. DISCLOSURE FOR LAW ENFORCEMENT PURPOSES

The Companies reserve the right to access and disclose Personal Information to comply with applicable laws and lawful government requests, to operate its systems properly, or to protect itself or others. The Companies may attempt to obtain the prior consent of the individual before disclosing the Personal Information, but have no obligation to do so.

#### 5\. CONTACT AND NOTICES

Although absolute security cannot be guaranteed on the Internet, please be assured that we have in place security measures to protect the confidentiality of your information. We will continue to maintain and improve these measures over time in line with legal and technological developments.

You can contact us at any time at [marketing@euroleague.net](mailto:marketing@euroleague.net) to see the personal information we have collected through the Website that relates to you. To protect your privacy and security we will take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access.

If we decide to change this Privacy Policy, the changes will be posted on this page so that you are always aware of the information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we disclose it. We will not use or disclose personal information provided to us in ways unrelated to the ones described above without informing you and providing you with an opportunity to opt-out of these unrelated uses.

If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy please feel free to contact us at [marketing@euroleague.net](mailto:marketing@euroleague.net)

#### 6\. PARENTS & GUARDIANS:

These Website are general audience Website. We do not knowingly collect and/or share Personal Information from children under the age of thirteen on the Website. However, your child cannot use some of the interactive features of the Website without providing Personal Information. We therefore recommend using the Website with your child if he or she is interested in participating in an interactive feature of the Website.

If, in the future, the Companies collect Personal Information from children, it will do so in compliance with the Directive 95/46/EC
