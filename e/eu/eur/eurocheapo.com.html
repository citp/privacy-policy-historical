WHAT IS THIS?
EuroCHEAPO is the free Internet guide to sleeping cheap in Europe.

EuroCHEAPO investigators show up unannounced at inexpensive hotels all over Europe, check out the rooms, and get a feel for the place.

If we like it, we snap a photo, write a review, and list the hotel on our site. Plus we give you full, up to date information, so that you can book a room immediately.

EuroCHEAPO also searches the Web for the best deals in online reservations. We research, add our two (or three) cents, and pass the hotels on to you.

Our goal is to help you find the best hotels for your budget in Europe.
