WHAT IS THIS? EuroCHEAPO is the free Internet guide to sleeping cheap in Europe.
 
The euroCHEAPO investigators show up unannounced at inexpensive hotels all over Europe, check out the rooms, and get the vibe of the place.
 
If we like it, we snap a photo, write a review, and list the hotel on our site. Plus we give you full up to date information, so that you can book a room immediately.
 
EuroCHEAPO is a free online guide. We're not an agency, we don't charge any fees or charge hotels to be included.
 
We're here to help you find the best budget accommodation in Europe.
