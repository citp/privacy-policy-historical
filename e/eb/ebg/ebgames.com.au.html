Privacy policy

Electronics Boutique Australia Pty Ltd. ABN 50 077 681 442 takes your concerns about the collection and use of your personal information seriously. We collect personal information from our customers in the regular course of doing business. This notice describes our Privacy Policy, as per the requirements contained in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), and the included Australian Privacy Principles. By visiting ebgames.com.au or ebgames.co.nz, signing up to the EB World program, or accessing the EB World smartphone app, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

In this document "EB Games" means Electronics Boutique Australia Pty Ltd, and covers EB Games and Zing Pop Culture retail stores, the ebgames.com.au and ebgames.co.nz websites, the EB World loyalty program, and the EB World smartphone app, as is relevant.

In this document, the word "computer" means any device used to connect to ebgames.com.au and ebgames.co.nz, including, but not limited to, PC’s, laptops, tablets and mobile phones.

Personal Information: What is it?

When used in this document, "personal information" has the meaning given to it in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). It is any information that can be used to personally identify you. This may include, but isn't limited to, your name, gender, date of birth, address and contact details (including phone numbers and email addresses). If the information we collect personally identifies you, or you are reasonably identifiable from it, the information will be considered personal information.

How We Collect Your Personal Information

Our Privacy Policy applies to personal information we collect during the normal operation of our business from individuals who:

  • visit and/or transact at an EB Games or Zing Pop Culture retail store or outlet;
  • visit and/or interact with our web presences at ebgames.com.au or ebgames.co.nz;
  • sign up to or access the EB World loyalty program;
  • access the EB World smartphone app;
  • respond to surveys;
  • enter competitions;
  • attend events hosted by EB Games, including but not limited to EB Expo;
  • interact with our social media presences, including but not limited to Facebook, Twitter and Instagram; and/or,
  • contact our Customer Service team.

We may also collect personal information for other reasons allowed by the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

If we ask you for personal information for a secondary reason, like the running of competitions or games of skill, we will either ask you directly for your consent or provide you with an opportunity to decline your consent.

We may ask you to give your consent, such as by checking a box to indicate your consent to receive marketing communications, or by providing your signature to indicate your acceptance of the use and disclosure terms presented on an application form. Other times, we may obtain your consent implicitly when we can reasonably conclude that you’ve given consent by an action you have taken or an action you have decided not to take. Generally, this occurs where the purpose for use of your personal information would be reasonably apparent to you.

How to Change or Withdraw Your Consent

When you choose to provide us with your personal information, you consent to the use and disclosure of your personal information as stated in this Privacy Policy, and as allowed by the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

You may notify us at any time that you wish to withdraw or change your consent to our use and disclosure of your information. We will accommodate your request following receipt of such notice, subject to legal and contractual restrictions related to your transactions.

If you have signed up to our EB World loyalty program you can adjust your preferences for all communications and other contact options at any time via your online EB World profile, or by contacting our Customer Service team. We also provide an ‘Unsubscribe’ link in all marketing mailings, and the option to reply ‘STOP’ to all SMS marketing material, as required by the Spam Act 2003 (Cth).

You may request to have your EB World account deactivated, and to receive no further communications in relation to your EB World account. Deactivation of an account may not result in all related personal information being deleted, as required by legal and/or operational requirements, and allowed by applicable laws.

Information That We Collect About Customers

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • name;
  • email address;
  • telephone number;
  • age or birth date;
  • mailing or street address;
  • details of the products and services you have purchased from us, together with any additional information necessary to deliver those products and services and to respond to your enquiries;
  • any additional information relating to you that you provide to us directly through our websites or indirectly through use of our website or online presence through our staff or otherwise; and
  • information you provide to us through contact with our Customer Service team or staff during store visits.

We receive and store any information you enter on our website or that you give us in any other way. You may need to provide us with personal information to take advantage of some of our features for website customers. You can choose not to provide certain information, but this may limit or prevent us from providing you with the product, service or information that you requested.

We also collect non-personal information. Non-personal information does not identify you as an individual. For example, when you visit our websites we may collect your Internet Protocol address, the type and version of browser you are using or the referring URL; or we may collect and summarise customer information in a non-personal, aggregate format for statistical and research purposes.

We ask for your age to determine what promotions we send. We limit some promotional offers and product availability to customers of a certain age or older, as required by Australian and New Zealand video game ratings regulations.

We occasionally run our own email surveys and we ask visitors for contact, demographic and technical information.

Automatic Collection of Information & Cookies

Through interaction with our website, we will receive and store information that you provide or that is generated by your actions. For example, like many websites, we use "cookies," which are placed on your computer (explained in more detail below). It lets us personalise your experience at ebgames.com.au and ebgames.co.nz, tailoring the content to your interests. A number of companies offer browsers or utilities designed to help you visit websites anonymously, but if you use these browsers or utilities, it may limit your ability to use the website features we offer.

The information we receive or can determine from your interaction with our website includes your Internet (IP) address; login and password information to our website and/or your EB World account; email address; web browser type and version; operating system and computer platform; purchase history, which we may aggregate with similar information from other customers; the full Uniform Resource Locator (URL) click stream to, through, and from our website, including date and time; cookie number; products you viewed or searched for; and/or areas of our website you visited. We also may log the length of time of your visit and the number of times you visit and purchase from us. We assign you a unique internal identifier to help keep track of your future visits.

Information EB Games Collects Directly from You

In-Store Purchases

When you purchase a product or service from EB Games, you may need to provide us with contact and payment information (such as credit or debit card information) so that we can process your request or complete your transaction. We also may need contact information to provide delivery services, product service, and warranty coverage. If we want to use your information to send you promotional information, we will ask for your express consent.

Trading

For any transaction that includes a Trade of software, hardware or accessories, we are required by law to witness a government-issued photo ID to prove your identity, and to record personal information such as your name, address, and your ID reference number.

Returns and Exchanges

In connection with any returns, refunds or exchanges, we may ask for personal information such as your name, address, and telephone number for the purposes of detecting and preventing fraud. You also may be asked to produce photo identification for verification purposes. This process does not affect your statutory rights.

Purchase Follow-Up and Surveys

Following a purchase, we may send you a follow-up communication either thanking you for your business or inquiring as to whether you are satisfied with your new product. We may use contact information collected in-store, online, or via our call centres to conduct occasional surveys and other customer research. These surveys are entirely voluntary.

Our Websites

You can browse our websites anonymously. However, to order products and services from our websites, or to use special features or functions of our websites, you may be asked to provide personal information, such as your name, email address, postal address, telephone number, or credit or debit card information. We use this information to create your account, contact and correspond with you about your order, respond to your inquiries, and monitor functions or features of our websites that you may use.

Social Media

Information that you have provided via social media pages, such as providing your name or contact details via the EB Games Facebook, Twitter or Instagram presences, may be used to contact you regarding issues or complaints registered via our social channels or other social media sites that we use.

Customer Reviews and Posted Content

We may provide you with the ability to rate or review products or services we sell, or otherwise post content on our websites. Any comments or reviews that you provide are accessible to all visitors to our website, so you should use discretion when posting information and you should not post personal information. EB Games has no responsibility if you do post personal information. Subject to the other provisions of this Privacy Policy relating to your personal information, you grant a perpetual worldwide non-exclusive license to EB Games to copy, use and/or display any content that you post publically on any of our websites or services.

Loyalty Program

EB Games may offer you the opportunity to sign up to the EB World customer loyalty program. If you decide to do so, we will collect your personal information, including but not limited to your name and email address. By signing up to the EB World loyalty program you are providing consent for us to communicate with you via email, SMS or in-app notifications. You may rescind your consent for these communications at any time by using the ‘Unsubscribe’ link provided in the footer of every marketing email sent, by replying ‘STOP’ to any marketing SMS we send you, by updating your Communications Preferences in your EB World Online Profile, or by contacting our Customer Service team.

Smartphone App

EB Games may make available the option to access offers and services provided via a smartphone app. To access such an app you may be required to sign up to the EB World loyalty program, and provide us with personal information including but not limited to your name and email address.

Your activity within the smartphone app and connected systems, including EB Games websites, may be stored for the purposes of software development and maintenance; marketing research; managing the provision of offers and services provided via the smartphone app; and other purposes allowed by the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). While parts of this data may be stored in anonymous form, personally identifiable information may also be stored.

In order to provide certain services to the EB World smartphone app, your device’s unique identifier, hardware model, operating system version, and other technical detail may be collected. In order to use certain functions on the smartphone app, you may choose to give the app access to your Photos on your device; your Photos are not collected by EB Games or transmitted from your device, they are used exclusively within the app on the device itself.

If you choose to nominate a Nickname in your EB World profile, your Nickname may be made visible to other users of the EB World program and/or the general public. We will never use your real name, only the Nickname you have provided for this purpose.

Contest and Other Promotions

From time to time, we may run contests or promotions. If you participate, you may be asked for contact information as well as additional optional survey information (for example, product preferences). Personal information from contest entries will be used only to administer the contest and contact you if you win. For research and analysis purposes, we may also aggregate contest survey information in a manner that no longer identifies individual participants. All contests and promotions are subject to this Privacy Policy, applicable legal requirements, and the rules provided for each particular contest or promotion. If a contest is by a third party, it will be subject to the third party’s contest rules and policies.

Subscription Services and Activations

EB Games may offer activation of certain products and services like online games, downloadable games or game components, or Internet services. These activations may require that certain contact and financial information be collected and provided to EB Games or to the respective service providers as part of the activation process. Downloadable games available through our website are offered by Real Networks in the United States, and our website page for downloadable games is hosted by Real Networks. All order and payment processing, delivery of downloadable games, and customer service for these downloadable games is provided by Real Networks. Any information collected from you in connection with purchase of downloadable games is subject to the Real Networks Privacy Policy, and all purchases will be subject to the Real Networks terms of use, which are stated on the Real Networks hosted website.

Call Centres

EB Games operates customer support call centres to respond to your questions, concerns, or complaints regarding our products or services. When you contact our call centres, we may ask for personal information to help us respond to your inquiry or to verify your identity. For example if you have a question about the status of a recent online order, we will ask for personal information to verify that you are the account holder. We may also monitor or record your telephone discussions with our customer support representatives for training purposes and to ensure service quality.

E-mail Communications

We provide email links on our "Contact Us" page that allow you to contact us directly with any questions or comments you may have. We may receive a confirmation when you open any email that we send to you, if your computer supports such capabilities. If you send us any email, we will retain such email and your email address for response. We also may compare our customer list to lists received from other companies, in an effort to avoid sending unnecessary or duplicate messages to you.

Information from Other Sources

If we receive any information about you from other sources, we may add it to our account information. For example, if we receive a list of subscribers to a gaming publication, and we note that you are a subscriber, we may include that information in the account information that we store about you. We also may receive page-view information from business partners who operate their own websites or for which we provide any services, or search term and search result information from searches conducted through the website.

How does EB Games Protect Your Information?

EB Games takes appropriate steps to protect your personal information. We maintain appropriate safeguards and current security standards to protect your personal information, whether recorded on paper or captured electronically, against unauthorised access, disclosure, or misuse. For example, electronic records are stored in secure, limited-access servers; electronic data is stored behind secured encryption access; we use technological tools like firewalls and strong passwords; and we ensure our employees are trained on the importance of maintaining the security and confidentiality of personal information. Although we cannot take responsibility for any theft, misuse, unauthorised disclosure, loss, alteration or destruction of data by a third party, we take reasonable security measures to safeguard against the loss, destruction, use, modification or disclosure of, or unauthorised access to, any personal information you have given us. EB Games also has taken precautions to prevent the fraudulent use of your information on our websites. EB Games has adopted industry standard authentication procedures, as discussed below, to increase your protection while shopping with us online. This includes, but is not limited to, contacting credit reporting agencies to ensure the authenticity of your credit card and to validate your credit card billing information. Our third party credit card processor also may take actions to determine the validity of your credit card.

SSL Technology

Our websites use encryption technology, such as Secure Sockets Layer ("SSL"), to protect your personal information during data transport. SSL encrypts ordering information such as your name, address, and credit card number.

Choosing a Password

When you create an online account on our websites, you will be prompted to select a personal password. To maximise your level of protection, you should choose a strong personal password, which means that you should use at least 6 characters including a combination of both letters and numbers. You are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your password and any account information. EB Games will never send an unsolicited communication asking you for your password or requesting that you update your account credentials.
