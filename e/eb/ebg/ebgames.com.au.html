Privacy Policy

Electronics Boutique Australia Pty Ltd. ABN 50 077 681 442 takes your concerns about the collection and use of your personal information seriously. We collect personal information from users of our Websites in the regular course of doing business.

This notice describes our Privacy Policy, as per the requirements contained in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Privacy Act 1993 (NZ) (the Acts), and the included Australian Privacy Principles and Information Privacy Principles respectively. By visiting ebgames.com.au or ebgames.co.nz, signing up to the EB World program, or accessing the EB World smartphone app, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy sets out the type of personal information we collect from you and how we collect, hold, disclose and use that information.

Our Privacy Policy may be revised at any time without notice to you. This Privacy Policy represents the latest version of the EB Games Privacy Policy.

In this document, "EB Games" means Electronics Boutique Australia Pty Ltd, and covers EB Games and Zing Pop Culture retail stores, the ebgames.com.au and ebgames.co.nz websites, the EB World loyalty program, and the EB World smartphone app, as is relevant.

In this document, the word "computer" means any device used to connect to ebgames.com.au and ebgames.co.nz, including, but not limited to, PCs, laptops, tablets and mobile phones.

In this document, Websites means the ebgames.com.au and ebgames.co.nz Websites and the EB World smartphone app, as is relevant.

In this document, devices means a phone, laptop, computer or any other device that may be used to interact with our Websites, products and services.

In this document, users includes customers of EB Games and any individual who interacts with our Websites.

Kinds of Information Collected

EB Games may collect, hold, disclose or use your personal information for the purpose of providing selected services to you and to ensure that you receive the best possible service. As an example, and without limiting the types of personal information we collect, we may collect your name, address, email, telephone number and accounting information such as your bank account details and credit card details including credit card number, credit card type and expiry date.

We also collect non-personal information. Non-personal information does not identify you as an individual. For example, when you visit our Websites we may collect your Internet Protocol address, the type and version of browser you are using or the referring URL; or we may collect and summarise user information in a non-personal, aggregate format for statistical and research purposes.

If the information we collect personally identifies you, or you are reasonably identifiable from it, the information will be considered personal information.

How We Collect Your Personal Information

Our Privacy Policy applies to personal information we collect during the normal operation of our business. We may collect your personal information from you when you:

  • visit and/or transact at an EB Games or Zing Pop Culture retail store or outlet;
  • visit and/or interact with our web presences at ebgames.com.au or ebgames.co.nz;
  • sign up to or access the EB World loyalty program;
  • access the EB World smartphone app;
  • respond to surveys;
  • enter competitions;
  • attend events hosted by EB Games, including but not limited to EB Expo;
  • interact with our social media presences, including but not limited to Facebook, Twitter and Instagram; and/or,
  • contact our Customer Service team.

We may also collect your personal information from any person authorised to provide your information or from external sources in circumstances your information is publicly and readily available.

If we ask you for personal information for a secondary reason, like the running of competitions or games of skill, we will either ask you directly for your consent or provide you with an opportunity to decline your consent.

We may ask you to give your consent, such as by checking a box to indicate your consent to receive marketing communications, or by providing your signature to indicate your acceptance of the use and disclosure terms presented on an application form. Other times, we may obtain your consent implicitly when we can reasonably conclude that youve given consent by an action you have taken or an action you have decided not to take. Generally, this occurs where the purpose for use of your personal information would be reasonably apparent to you.

We will not collect personal information by unlawful means or means that are unfair or intrude on you to an unreasonable extent.

Through use of our Websites, we may automatically record details including your internet address, domain name and the date and time of your visit to our Websites (including the web pages viewed), your browser and operating system and whether you are accessing our Websites across multiple devices or sessions. We may collect information about interactions with our Websites by you from different devices or via different sessions. We may also use cookies that allow us to track websites browsed by you. Please see heading Automatic Collection of Information & Cookies below for further information about the use of cookies.

EB Games may also track your usage of our Websites via Google Analytics. You will be assigned a unique user identification (User ID) that allows us to track your use of our Websites from different devices and over multiple sessions. We will connect this unique User ID with your EB World loyalty account information, though we may still obtain information about your use of Websites even if you are not logged in. We may also use the Google Analytics feature to connect together all of your visits to our Websites. This will assist us in determining how you interact with our content over an extended period of time and your product preferences.

The information collected via Google Analytics about you may include, but is not limited to, the pages you view on our Websites; the products you view on our Websites; the length of time you spend interacting with our Websites and the devices used to interact with our Websites.

By using our Websites, you give your consent for EB Games to track your usage of our Websites and to connect your visits to our Websites across multiple devices. We will not track your usage of any other websites. We will not upload any data that will allow Google to personally identify you or data that permanently identifies a particular device. If you would like to opt out of this feature, please contact us by email at customer.service@ebgames.com.au OR customer.service@ebgames.co.nz with the subject heading "Opt Out Google Analytics. We will try to respond within 14 working days from receipt of your request.

How does EB Games Protect Your Information?

EB Games takes appropriate steps to protect your personal information. However, we acknowledge that the Internet is not a secure environment, and any information you send to us via that method is sent at your own risk. We maintain appropriate safeguards and current security standards to protect your personal information, whether recorded on paper or captured electronically, against unauthorised access, disclosure, or misuse. For example, electronic records are stored in secure, limited-access servers; electronic data is stored behind secured encryption access; we use technological tools like firewalls and strong passwords; and we ensure our employees are trained on the importance of maintaining the security and confidentiality of personal information. Although we cannot take responsibility for any theft, misuse, unauthorised disclosure, loss, alteration or destruction of data by a third party, we take reasonable security measures to safeguard against the loss, destruction, use, modification or disclosure of, or unauthorised access to, any personal information you have given us. EB Games also has taken precautions to prevent the fraudulent use of your information on our Websites. EB Games has adopted industry standard authentication procedures, as discussed below, to increase your protection while shopping with us online. This includes, but is not limited to, contacting our third party providers for payment processing, fraud detection; card and merchant management and electronic bill presentment and payment. Third party providers located in Australia and New Zealand may take actions to determine the validity of your credit card and to detect any fraud issues prior to completion of an online purchase.

Our third party credit card processor also may take actions to determine the validity of your credit card.

SSL Technology

Our Websites use encryption technology, such as Secure Sockets Layer ("SSL"), to protect your personal information during data transport. SSL encrypts ordering information such as your name, address, and credit card number.

Choosing a Password

When you create an online account on our Websites, you will be prompted to select a personal password. To maximise your level of protection, you should choose a strong personal password, which means that you should use at least 6 characters including a combination of both letters and numbers. You are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your password and any account information. EB Games will never send an unsolicited communication asking you for your password or requesting that you update your account credentials.
