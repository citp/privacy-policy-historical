# eBillme - Corporate - Privacy Policy

**Your Privacy**  
eBillme is committed to keeping your personal information confidential, secure and private. We are not in the business of selling or renting an individual's personal data to other companies for marketing purposes. In this Privacy Policy we provide detailed information on when and why we collect personal information, how we use it, the limited conditions under which we may disclose it to others, and how we keep it secure. 

**What data does eBillme collect?**  
eBillme collects information to facilitate the billing and delivery of eBillme services.   
Personal information that eBillme collects from customers includes the customer's email address.  
Personal information that eBillme collects from merchant partners where you shop includes: 

  * customer's name, email, address and other contact information;
  * historical usage data of eBillme, and 
  * customer subscription records. 



eBillme deems it imperative to collect and use this information. Without it eBillme can not provide you with necessary support and service levels. eBillme does not collect, archive or use any personal financial information.

**How does eBillme use this data?**  
When an individual uses eBillme, the customer is made explicitly aware of the purposes for which eBillme is requesting the personal information. If eBillme identifies other purposes for which the personal information may be used, we will seek the individual's consent prior to such use. eBillme will advise that it is the individual's right to refuse permission for eBillme to use personal information for any new purposes. 

Additional purposes for collecting personal information may be identified to an individual before or at the time of collection. However, at a minimum eBillme will collect personal information for the following purposes: 

  * to establish and maintain effective communication and provide on-going services;
  * to deliver services as subscribed to by the merchants;
  * to deliver services as subscribed to by the consumer;
  * to meet legal, regulatory and settlement requirements; and 
  * to verify the identity of a consumer. 



**How does eBillme collect this information?**   
eBillme collects your information by lawful and fair means using physical or electronic methods. We collect information directly from you when you submit your email address at [www.eBillme.com](http://www.ebillme.com/).

**Supplementation of Information**  
In order to provide order processing services to you, we may on occasion supplement the personal information you submit to us with information from third party sources. 

**ID and Address Verification**  
We use third party software to verify your IP address, billing and shipping information in order to verify your identity.

**LiveChat**  
We provide a livechat feature on our website. This feature is powered by a third party provider; this feature collects your name and email address. This information is used solely to communicate with you regarding your inquiry

**Is my data shared?**  
We use third party web analytics software to track website visits and usage. We do not and will not share, sell, or rent your information to any third party to enable them to market their products and services to you. eBillme may only share your information with third parties with your consent. We maintain the right to release personal information to third parties when we believe it is appropriate to do so to comply with the law and in cases of emergencies. We will also release personal information to cooperate with law enforcement investigations, comply with court orders or subpoenas, to protect our legal rights and the rights of our users, or when we believe it is needed for identity verification and risk reduction. 

**How is my data protected?**  
eBillme takes all reasonable precautions to ensure that your personal information is kept safe from loss, unauthorized access, modification or disclosure. We implement the following safeguards: 

  * Premises security, including badge access to all facilities and iris scanners to the server farm. 
  * Authorized access to digital data, using the latest technological means, including and not limited to firewalls and security software. 



**What Are Cookies?**  
A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user's computer for record-keeping purposes. We use cookies on this site. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our site. 

Some of our business partners use cookies on our site. We have no access to or control these cookies.

MODASolutions Corporation ("MODASolutions") respects the privacy of every individual and organization that visits our Website(s) or responds to an online invitation. This Privacy Statement outlines our information gathering and dissemination practices for our website(s). Please feel free to visit our sites anonymously or as a registered guest.

**Information Collection  
**In order to personalize your Web experience and improve customer service, we may at times require information from you. Due to our strong commitment to privacy, we will inform you of our information requirements before we collect it from you. 

**Personal Information**  
MODASolutions may require personal information (i.e. user name, e-mail address) from you whether you visit our site(s) as an anonymous visitor or registered guest. We will not collect personally identifiable information unless you knowingly provide it to us. Please do not submit your personally identifiable information to us if you do not want it to be collected. MODASolutions will endeavor to not use personally identifiable information outside of the context in which it was provided unless otherwise approved by you at time of submission.

**Business Related Information**  
MODASolutions may require business related information (i.e. product plans, cost models, project schedules) from you whether you visit our site(s) as an anonymous visitor or registered guest. We will not gather business related information unless you knowingly provide it to us. This information will be used by MODASolutions to propose business solutions based on your requirements. Please do not submit your business information to us if you do not want it to be collected. MODASolutions will endeavor to not use this confidential information outside of the context in which it was provided unless otherwise approved by you at time of submission. 

**Anonymous Data**  
To help us understand the trends of a general user and user preferences, we may collect, during your session as either an anonymous visitor or registered guest, information that is not identifiable to you. Examples of this type of information include the: 

  * Internet browser in use 
  * Type of computer operating system 
  * Domain name of the website from which you linked to our site or online invitation 
  * Pages viewed 
  * Documents downloaded 



**Improved Services  
**MODASolutions uses confidential information to improve services to you in the following ways:

  * Better understand your needs and target information products or services to you 
  * Improve our products and services 
  * Establish communication with you 
  * Recognize and better serve you in the future 



**Cookies**  
Cookies are pieces of information placed on your hard drive after you visit websites. Your browser manages the cookie. You can make the choice of whether to accept a cookie or not with your browser settings. Please see your browser instructions for other guidelines on cookie use. 

Cookies may or may not contain personally identifiable information. MODASolutions currently uses cookie technology in the following ways: 

  * To identify you during future logins and registrations which saves you from re-registering for other services 
  * To ensure that we serve new information to you and not repeated information 



**Information Access and Security**  
MODASolutions only shares confidential information with its business partners and only in the context for which it was provided. You should be aware that when you disclose personal information (i.e. user name, e-mail address) in the discussion forum or chat areas, this information is accessible and can be collected and used by others who visit this area. 

If you have submitted confidential information to a MODASolutions website or online invitation, and would like that information deleted from our records, please contact the MODASolutions Webmaster. We will use reasonable efforts to delete this information from our files.
