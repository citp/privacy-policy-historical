# Privacy Policy

The purpose of this statement is to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website. 

###  Customer Contact Information 

Our website requests contact information in order to distribute additional information about our products and services. Some of the information requested includes name, telephone number, address, and e-mail address. This information may be provided to our sales, marketing, and customer support groups and is not disclosed to third parties.

A customer list may be requested as part of a formal Request for Proposal process but is only supplied after a non-disclosure agreement is secured with the requesting provider.

Customer testimonials may be posted on our site with the permission of the customer.

Customer-specific usage and transaction information is collected for billing purposes and is provided to that customer on a monthly basis. Aggregate transaction information (without disclosure of any specific customer's information) may also be used for sales and marketing opportunities in the future. 

###  Access Restrictions 

Because of the private nature of data available through our website, security measures are in place to prevent unauthorized access. Claims information is provided to and for a user's own facility and providers. Security measures are in place to prevent unauthorized access to the claims of other facilities. 

###  Cookies 

A cookie is a small file stored on a website user's computer. The Passport OneSource product website currently uses temporary or session cookies while the OneSource product is being used. These cookies are specific to each user session and are not reused after that session terminates. The Passport OneSource registration site uses persistent cookies in order to maintain information related to the user's registration progress. This cookie is not used in order to track activity outside the registration process. 

###  Non-Disclosure 

Information collected on individual patients to perform the services provided by Passport Health Communications is not disclosed to third parties. 

###  Other Links 

Our site contains links to other sites. 

Passport Health Communications, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. 

###  Security 

This website has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. An industry-standard firewall provides security services to protect and manage website access.
