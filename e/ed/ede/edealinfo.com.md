# eDealinfo.com Privacy Policy

[Dell System Deals page](https://web.archive.org/dell/) has deals with upto _$750 off_ Laptop coupons and _30% off_ Desktop coupons! (New Coupons released on 12/22/2005)

eDealinfo.com is committed to the security and privacy of our visitors.

Personal Information

Our Web Server recognizes only the visitors' IP address and the Browser Version. This information is used to aid us in fixing problems and to serve you better.

Mailing List

If you join our [mailing list](https://web.archive.org/list.shtml), we will keep your email address and any demographics information you volunteer strictly confidential. We will never give or sell your email address or any information about you to anyone else. Our [mailing list](https://web.archive.org/list.shtml) is strictly opt-in only and any subscriber may unsubscribe at any time.

Cookies

This site does not use cookies.

Business Relationships

This site contains links to other Web sites. We use third-party ad servers in order to bring you offers which are of interest to you.  As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information. eDealinfo.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Questions?

eDealinfo.com welcomes comments and questions on this policy. We are dedicated to protecting your personal information and will make every reasonable effort to keep that information secure. Any revisions to our policy will be posted to this site.
