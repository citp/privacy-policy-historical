# Privacy Policy | Edu360.in Education & Information Hub

## Privacy Policy

Log Files :

Like many other Web sites, [www.edu360.in](http://www.edu360.in/) makes use of log files.The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) address, type of  
browser, internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp,referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site , track user’s movement around the site , and gather demographic information . IP address , and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons :

[www.edu360.in](http://www.edu360.in/) does use cookies to store information about visitiors preferences record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit ,  
customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
