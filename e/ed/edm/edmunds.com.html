Date Last Revised: June 9, 2008

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Statement, you should contact us though our Contact Us form.

This personally identifiable information will be shared between us and those companies, and may also be disclosed to other entities who work with us or those companies to fulfill your request. Edmunds cannot control, and is not responsible for, the manner in which any of such companies and other entities use such information. Edmunds has been advised by certain manufacturers that they may contact individuals who use our Dealer Locator service (described below) to obtain price quotes for vehicles they produce.

If you give your consent, this information also may be used by us and shared by us with other entities to notify you about other products and services. That is, you may receive information from us or from other companies by e-mail, U.S. mail or other means. However, if we do not have your express consent, our policy is to not provide your personally identifiable information to anyone else, except as provided below. We may disclose whatever information we may have or know about you, including personally identifying information, without first notifying you or obtaining your consent, to third-party service providers who assist us to improve our data or our services or to better understand their value to our visitors or to the companies with which we do business; or to third parties, if we determine that disclosure of such information: (i) is or may be required by law or legal process; (ii) is or may be reasonably necessary to enforce the Edmunds Automotive Network Membership Agreement or Visitor Agreement , or to take appropriate action if a visitor is determined by us to have violated either of those agreements; or (iii) is or may be reasonably necessary to protect the rights, property or safety of our company, the Edmunds Automotive Network, our visitors or other individuals or entities. We may also disclose to third parties portions of the Edmunds Automotive Network's log files that show which "Internet service provider" a visitor has used or other visitor information. That information may enable third parties to discover, through that Internet service provider, the identity of a visitor.

In certain locations on the Edmunds Automotive Network in which we obtain personally identifiable information from you, we may include "check boxes" for the purpose of soliciting your consent to receive special offers and/or other information from us and other entities selected by us. By leaving these check boxes "checked," you are choosing to receive commercial communications from us and/or those other entities. In that event, you will have the opportunity at any time to "opt-out" of receiving future commercial communications from us (on behalf of itself and/or such third parties) either (i) by modifying your Membership account, (ii) by following the instructions located at the bottom of the e-mail we send you, (iii) by sending a request to us through our Contact Us form, or (iv) by sending us a request by mail to 1620 26th Street, Suite 400 South, Santa Monica, California 90404; attention: Customer Service. Access to Your Personally Identifiable Information

If your personally identifiable information changes, or if you would like to delete any of your personally identifiable information, you may correct, update or delete it by either (i) modifying your Membership account, or (ii) sending a request to us through our Contact Us form.

Your Zip Code

We request your Zip Code in a number of locations on this Web site, including when you first engage in vehicle research. Providing us your Zip Code enables us to display to you vehicle selling prices adjusted for your region, incentive and rebates that apply in your region, your vehicle's five-year True Cost to OwnSM for your region, and dealer listings specific to your region. We provide this information for Zip Codes that are within one of the 50 states or the District of Columbia, but not those in other U.S. territories or possessions or for the postal codes of Canada or other countries.

When you first provide your Zip Code to us, whether through the use of one of our applications (such as the Edmunds.com True Market Value® tools or Dealer Locator), or in response to a direct request by us that you provide your Zip Code, we serve a "cookie" to you that includes your Zip Code. ("Cookies" are described below.) That cookie permits us to recognize your Zip Code when you use other applications, or upon your return to the Edmunds Automotive Network, without our having to ask you again for your Zip Code.

You have the opportunity to change your Zip Code at appropriate times during your visit to the Edmunds Automotive Network if you want to get information for a different Zip Code; changing your Zip Code in any application automatically updates your Zip Code cookie.

Other Cookies

"Cookies" are small data files that many Web sites write to your hard drive when you visit them. A cookie file contains information such as a user ID that a site may use to track the pages you've visited. However, the only personal information a cookie can contain is information you supply yourself. Cookies served by us don't permit us to read data from your hard drive or to read cookie files created by other Web sites.

We use cookies in many ways, including the following:

First Visits: When you first visited the Edmunds Automotive Network, we served a cookie to you. This cookie resides permanently on your hard drive (unless you chose to delete it) and enables us to track visitor activity across our Web sites.

Applications: Whenever you use certain applications on the Edmunds Automotive Network, such as Dealer Locator, we may serve a "session cookie" to you, which is a cookie that is only used by us during your use of that application. In addition, for some applications we may serve a cookie to you that has a longer expiration date. These cookies help us manage our delivery to you of application information and related content.

Lead Forms: In certain applications, such as Dealer Locator, you may be presented with a form that requests, among other items, your name and contact information. When this form is submitted by you the information you provide is stored on our computers, so that we have a record of what we have sent to our partners. We also serve a cookie to you that encodes where on our computers that information is maintained, so that we can "pre-fill" your name and contact information for you when in the future you call up a similar form. You may opt to not have this cookie served to you by deselecting this feature within the application.

Membership Registration: When you register as a member of the Edmunds Automotive Network, we use a cookie to store your registration information on your computer if you click the "Remember me" box. We use this cookie to enable you to enter the Edmunds Automotive Network as a member without having to sign in each time.

Advertisements: Our advertising results in the serving of cookies that facilitate the delivery and administration of such advertising. See "Third-Party Ad Servers" below.

Advertisers: The current Advertising Terms and Conditions for advertisers on the Edmunds Automotive Network provide that advertisers may write cookies to our visitors' computers solely for advertising campaign tracking and management purposes, but not for advertisement "retargeting" (i.e., for the purpose of targeting advertisements to visitors to an Edmunds Web site when they subsequently view other (non-Edmunds) Web sites).

Please note that we do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our Web sites.

We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

If you have any questions about security on our Website, please use our Contact Us form.
