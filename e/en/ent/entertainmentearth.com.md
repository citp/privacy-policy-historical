**PRIVACY POLICY**  
Entertainment Earth, Inc. has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. By participating in the Council of Better Business Bureau's BBB _OnLine_ ¨ Privacy Program, we have made a commitment to meet the programs' strict requirements regarding how we treat your information and have it verified by BBB _OnLine_. Further information about this program is available at [http://www.bbbonline.org](http://www.bbbonline.org/). **Our Commitment To Privacy**  
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.  **The Information We Collect**  
This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the Entertainment Earth website. On some pages, you can order products, make requests, and register to receive materials. The types of personal information collected at these pages are: 

  * **ORDERING PRODUCTS** \- When ordering from Entertainment Earth, the following information is required: 
    * Name - We use this info to send you your order, and to address you with information or questions regarding your order. 
    * Billing Address - We use this info to send you your order if you have requested that your order be sent to your billing address. In addition, your billing address is used to verify your credit card information to prevent fraud. 
    * Phone Numbers (daytime/nighttime) - We use this to phone you if there is any problem or concern with your order. We do not give out phone numbers to any third party, they remain entirely confidential. 
    * E-mail Address - This is optional, and it is only used to send you information regarding your purchase order, and to send you news and information regarding products and offers available at Entertainment Earth. Your e-mail is NEVER shared, sold, or given to any third party. Anyone may remove themselves from any news list either by sending email to unsubscribe@entertainmentearth.com if so instructed, or by sending a request to cs@entertainmentearth.com. We also store your e-mail in conjunction with a password to allow you secure and private access to view on-line the status of your orders. 
    * Ship-to Name and Address - If you are sending products to an address different than the billing address, the information collected is ONLY used to direct shipments to the appropriate destination. 
    * Credit Card Information - This information is used only to charge your credit card for the orders you submit to us. 
    *   * **PRICE LIST REQUESTS** \- We ask for your e-mail address which we use to send you a price list of our entire stock. You may simultaneously join our news list when you submit your e-mail, but your are given the choice to OPT OUT of the newletter. 
  * **RECEIVING OUR NEWS** \- You must submit your e-mail address to receive our news, but we do not share your address with ANY third party. Your address is used only to send you news regarding our products and offers, and your subscription can be cancelled by sending email to unsubscribe@entertainmentearth.com. 
  * **AFFILIATE PROGRAM** \- The information we collect for our affiliate program is explained if you fill out an application to join our program. All the information you submit is used solely in conjunction with executing the affiliate program as described, and none of the information is shared in any manner with any third party. 
  * **EE GREETING CARDS** \- We offer free electronic greeting cards that allow you to send messages to friends. The e-mail addresses we collect are used only to send the cards, and notify the senders of the receipt of the card. The addresses are not shared with any outside party. 

**How We Use Information**  
An explanation of the information we collect, and how we use it is detailed here. We repeat our general use of information we collect here: We use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order only to complete that order. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order. We use the information you provide about someone else when placing an order only to ship the product and to confirm delivery. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order. We offer free electronic greeting cards that allow you to send messages to friends. The e-mail addresses we collect are used only to send the cards, and notify the senders of the receipt of the card. The addresses are not shared with any outside party. We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties. Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.  **Our Commitment To Data Security**  
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.  **Our Commitment To Children's Privacy**  
Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.  **How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information**  
You can access all your personally identifiable information that we collect online and maintain by contacting our customer service representatives by phone at 818-255-0090. We use this procedure to better safeguard your information. You can correct factual errors in your personally identifiable information by sending us a request that credibly shows error.
