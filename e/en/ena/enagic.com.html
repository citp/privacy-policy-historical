Section 28 Policies and Procedures:  Privacy Policy

The Company collects information from independent distributors during the application procedure and from time to time as required for internal purposes such as compliance with state and federal laws and regulations. The Company implements a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of the information collected.

The Company does not sell or trade the information collected. The Company only shares information as permitted or required by law and with third-party vendors (eg. merchant card processing company) for our everyday business purposes such as merchant card processing companies.
