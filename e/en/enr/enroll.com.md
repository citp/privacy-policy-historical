# Enroll: Privacy Policy

The Enroll website (“Site”) contains links to other sites, usually our partners. This privacy policy applies solely to information collected by Enroll, Inc. (“Enroll”) and we do not bear responsibility for the privacy practices of the other sites linked to ours.

### Personal Information

  * __At Enroll we respect your privacy, and take great care to safeguard information in our possession. Your preferences for use of your information are our highest priority.
  * __Any and all the information collected on Enroll will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, disclosed, or loaned. Enroll, Inc. collects personal information when you use our Site.
  * __Information You Give Enroll:  We receive and store any information you enter on our Site or give us in any other way. We use the information to respond to your requests, customizing your experience, improve our service, and to communicate with you. When you enter into a transaction through Enroll, you are automatically signed up on our email list. You may at any time request removal or remove yourself automatically by using the contact us page. You may also send a request to [unsubscribe@enroll.com](mailto:unsubscribe@enroll.com).
  * __Automatic Information:  We receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with us. For example, like many Web sites, we use “cookies,” and we obtain certain types of information when your Web browser accesses Enroll. This information includes Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser information, Internet Service Provider (ISP), operating system, date/time stamp and clickstream data. Please see the cookies section of our Privacy policy for more details.
  * __Email Communications:  We may receive confirmation when you open email from Enroll if your computer supports such capabilities. Please note that email is not encrypted, and therefore NOT a secure means of transmitting credit card information. Enroll will NEVER request sensitive information over e-mail.  If you do not want to receive email or other mail from us, please contact us at [unsubscribe@enroll.com](mailto:unsubscribe@enroll.com) and we will promptly remove you from our mailing lists.
  * __Enroll uses your information for the following general purposes: (i) make your tutoring sessions personal; (ii) store all of your past sessions, so you can review them at any time; (iii) complete your purchases; (iv) contact you about your account; (v) send you newsletters by email about our service; (vi) ask you for feedback to help make Enroll better; and (vii) Respond to your email requests for information and/or customer service.
  * __You and/or your parents control your personal information. You can visit your User Account section of the Enroll Site to see the personal information we have collected about you. If you are under the age of 13, the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Action (CAPPA) says that we must have a parent’s permission before we may collect or use your personal information. If you are under 13 you are prohibited from using the Enroll Site.
  * __Enroll will send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when: we have your consent to share the information; or we need to send the information to companies who work on behalf of Enroll to provide a product or service to you. Enroll will share your personal information with vendors that help us process transactions, such as charging your credit card. All vendors are required to keep your personal information confidential.
  * __If your parents subsidize any of your tutoring, Enroll may share your personal information with them at their request. This may include the Instructor’s contact information, as well as information regarding any of your tutoring sessions, including but not limited to your written comments left in the post-session surveys. These companies are required to keep your information secure and confidential.   Otherwise, we DO NOT RELEASE any information about you to other companies.
  * __Enroll may disclose your personal information if required by law, as part of an internal or external legal investigation or to reasonably protect someone from possible injury.



### Compliance

  * __Enroll is committed to adhering to and remaining informed of all applicable federal and state laws relating to maintaining a compliant internet presence.



### Cookies

  * __Enroll (or third party service providers on our behalf) may set and access cookies on your computer. A cookie is a small amount of data (often including a unique identifier), that is sent to your browser from a website's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. Enroll uses cookies to understand Site usage and to improve the content and offerings on our Site.
  * __Enroll may use cookies, IP address and browser data to control the display of ads, to track usage patterns on its website, to deliver editorial content, and to personalize information.
  * __Our cookies may contain personally identifiable information and such cookies may be shared with others to the same extent indicated above.
  * __Enroll (or third party service providers on our behalf) may also use cookies to collect aggregate information about website users on an anonymous basis. We may share aggregate demographic and usage information with our prospective and actual business partners, advertisers and other third parties for any business purpose.
  * __Some of our vendors may serve you cookies as well. We do not have control over cookies placed by advertisers.
  * __When you visit our Site, we may use both a session and a persistent cookie. This Enroll placed cookie may contain information (such as a unique user ID) that is used to track your usage of our Site and in some cases, your e-mail address. Your e-mail address is only saved in this Enroll placed cookie if you register with us. A web beacon allows us to capture certain additional types of information about a visitor's actions on a website, such as a visitor's cookie number, the time, date, duration and number of page views, a description of the page where the Web beacon is placed, and details about any items that were purchased.
  * __If you register with us, this Enroll placed cookie allows us to recognize you when you return to our Site and provides you with access to your account information. If you save your information with or order from us, we may also use cookies to monitor and maintain information about your use of our website. If you have not saved your information with or ordered from us, we may monitor and maintain information about your use of our Site in a manner that does not identify you.
  * __If you don't want cookies, most Web browsers include an option that allows you to not accept them. However, if you set your browser to refuse cookies, some portions of our sites may not function efficiently.
