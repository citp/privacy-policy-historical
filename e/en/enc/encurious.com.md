# Encurious

Encurious.com (“Encurious”, “we”, “the Site”) is hosted by the microblogging platform Tumblr. Our advertisements are served by Google AdSense and we use MailChimp to send messages and collect your email-adress when you subscribe to our newsletter. We also analyze information collected via Google Analytics. Some of this information includes the Internet Protocol Address (“IP Adress”), type of browser, Internet Service Provider (“ISP”) and are collected to improve the Site. We have also implemented Google Analytics Display Advertising Features which you can opt-out of anytime through Ads Settings, Ad Settings for mobile apps or through [this browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/). 

Third-party services (like [Tumblr](https://www.tumblr.com/policy/en/privacy), [Google AdSense and Google Analytics](http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/)) may use first-party cookies (such as the Google Analytics cookie) or other first-party identifiers, and third-party cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) or other third-party identifiers together. These cookies and identifiers are used to personalize your experience.

Encurious has no access to or control over cookies that are used by third-party services. We do, however, use cookies on our own which are also used to personalize your experience.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party services for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. This privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of other web sites. The same goes for any links to other websites that we may include.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
