EngineersGarage does not bear any responsibility in any form for the content posted by its users.

 **Personal Information**

EngineersGarage may collect personal information during registration or otherwise from time to time. This information is kept confidential and will not be shared by any third party. This information might be used for internal purposes and for advertising programs.

 **Agreeing to Terms**

Please do not use this site and its services in case you do not agree to the Privacy Policy of EngineersGarage. Acceptance to this privacy policy is considered in case you are using this site.
