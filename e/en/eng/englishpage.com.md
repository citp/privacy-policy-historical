# Privacy Statement

####  Information Automatically Logged

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. Englishpage.com logs IP addresses and browser types for systems administration purposes and these logs will be analyzed to constantly improve the value of the materials available on the website. We do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable. This means that a user's session will be tracked, but the user will be anonymous.

####  Advertisers

We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While Englishpage.com does not collect cookies, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information.

####  Registration Forms

Our site's _Dont' Have a Cow_ registration form requires users to give us their email address. Contact information from the registration forms is used for our weekly newsletter. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

####  Surveys

Our online surveys ask visitors for contact information (like email address).

####  External Links

This site contains links to other sites. **English Page** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
