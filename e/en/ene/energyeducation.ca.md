# Energy Education:Privacy policy - Energy Education

**Any information we collect on this website, or that you provide to us through this website:**

  * Will be used to provide information on our products and services,
  * May be used to notify you for marketing purposes,
  * Will not be sold or rented to others,
  * And will not be shared with other organizations without your specific consent. 



If you want to correct any information, or wish to delete your information from our database, please let us know by using our [contact page](https://web.archive.org/encyclopedia/Special:Contact "Special:Contact").
