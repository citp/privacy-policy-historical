# The E-ppraisal Technology

**Online Privacy Policy**

By accessing or using the E-ppraisal Inc's (E-ppraisal) web site, www.e-ppraisal.com, you agree to the terms of the E-ppraisal Online Privacy Policy, as outlined below. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not access or use this site.

**Collection of Personal Information**

When you engage in certain activities on this site, such as becoming a subscriber to the services provided by E-ppraisal, downloading software or utilizing our web site on an occasional basis, E-ppraisal may ask you to provide certain information about yourself or business activities by filling out and submitting an online form. It is completely optional for you to engage in these activities. If you elect to engage in these activities, however, E-ppraisal may require that you provide us personal or business information, such as your name, mailing  address, e-mail address, credit card information and other personal or business  identifying information. When ordering service from this site, you may be asked to provide a credit card number.

When you submit personal or business information to E-ppraisal, you understand and agree E-ppraisal and its trusted vendors may transfer, store and process your customer profile in its offices in the United States.

E-ppraisal collects this information in order to record and support your participation in the activities you select. If you subscribe to E-ppraisal's services, for example, the information is used to register your usage agreement, including the information provided therein, and rights, if any, to technical support, system upgrades or other benefits that may be made available to registered users. E-ppraisal may also use certificate and membership numbers provided to it to ensure such numbers are valid and active with the agencies to which they represent. If you do not provide the certificate or membership numbers and/or you do not approve of E-ppraisal validating such numbers, it may limit the data available to you as it would violate certain third party agreements. E-ppraisal also uses information that you provide as part of our effort to keep you informed about product upgrades, special offers and other E-ppraisal products and services.

E-ppraisal recognizes and appreciates the importance of responsible use of this information. If you do not want the information you provide to E-ppraisal to be used to inform you of other products and services and special offers from E-ppraisal, and you indicate this preference when you provide the information to E-ppraisal, E-ppraisal will honor your preference. In addition, E-ppraisal may selectively provide certain or organizations that have products or services we think may interest you. If you do not want the information you provide to E-ppraisal to be shared with other companies, and you indicate this preference when you provide the information to E-ppraisal, E-ppraisal will honor your preference.

Your personal and/or business  information is password- protected to prevent unauthorized access to it. This password is encrypted. We recommend that you do not divulge your password to anyone. If you are a business, you have the option to divulge the passwords to whom you feel has a need to access  E-ppraisal's web site: however, it is your responsibility to protect and change your password as often as necessary to protect from unauthorized access.
