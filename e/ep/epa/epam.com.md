# Privacy Policy

EPAM Systems, Inc. has created this Privacy Policy in line with our commitment to your privacy on EPAM websites. The following discloses our information gathering, dissemination and protection practices.  


## **Compliance**  


By accessing this site you agree to the terms of the EPAM Privacy Policy outlined below. If you do not agree to the terms of this policy, please do not access or use this site.  


## Collection of information  


EPAM does not collect any personally identifiable information about you other than details that you affirmatively choose to submit to us (such as your name, e-mail address, street address and company name) for the purpose of accessing various downloads, viewing specific content, subscribing to EPAM newsletters, etc. We do collect the IP address, browser type, ISP, referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, number of clicks, domain name and country/state of all visitors to the site.  


## Use of information  


Any information collected by EPAM Systems, Inc. will be done in accordance with this privacy policy for the purposes of making better business decisions, supporting the activities you choose to engage in, and providing higher quality solutions and services to our customers. We also may use any non-personal information we collect to enhance our web site, and your IP address for web site diagnostics and administration. We will not sell, share, or rent this information, for commercial purposes, to any person or organization.  


## Information security  


EPAM Systems, Inc. treats your personal information as Confidential Information. We protect this confidential Information by storing it on secure servers that only select EPAM Systems, Inc. personnel have access to, and by encrypting it to help prevent access by any unauthorized parties.  


## Links  


This website contains links to various third party web sites. EPAM has no control over, and is not responsible for the content, privacy policies or reliability of such sites.  


## Changes to the Policy  


EPAM Systems reserves the right to modify or amend this Privacy Policy at its own discretion. The changes can be viewed here or elsewhere at this site. Your continued usage of this site will mean you accept those changes.
