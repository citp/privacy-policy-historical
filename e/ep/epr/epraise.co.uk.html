Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains what personal data we collect and how we process it. If you have any questions, please use the contact form to get in touch.

This policy may be updated from time to time, and any changes will be reflected on this page. This policy was last updated on 17th August 2012.

What data do we collect?

  • Schools upload data into EPraise, such as student names, tutor groups, year groups, classes, teacher details, etc.
  • We collect data from visitors to the website, such as IP addresses, browser versions and operating systems.
  • We also keep a record of any data that visitors provide in forms, such as the contact or order forms.

What is the data used for?

  • The data we collect is used to provide and improve the service we offer to our customers and visitors.

Is the data ever disclosed?

  • We do not disclose personal data to any third parties, beyond the school that the student, teacher or user in question attends, unless we required by law to do so.

What if the data is wrong?

  • Data held about students or teachers is the responsibility of the schools to keep up to date. If you believe that any information we are holding on you is incorrect or incomplete, please notify the relevant school.
  • If you believe that any other information we are holding on you is incorrect or incomplete, such as your contact details, please use the contact form to tell us.

What cookies to we use?

  • We use cookies to allow us to track our visitors, allowing us to see statistics such as the browsers and operating systems our visitors use, or how they found our website. These statistics help us improve the design of the website and improve the marketing of our products.
  • Cookies are used to allow users to log in to the system, and stay logged in for a period of time.
  • Cookies many also be to set defaults, such as the visitor's school, to make using the website easier for them.

What if I don't want to use cookies?

  • Our EPraise service will not work without cookies enabled, as these are required in order to allow users to log in.
  • You are however free to browse our website without cookies - simply switch them off in your browser.
  • For more information about cookies, please visit allaboutcookies.org.

What about security?

  • We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Are links to other websites safe?

  • Our website may occasionally contain links to other websites. Please note that these websites are outside our control, and we cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which you provide whilst visiting such sites.
