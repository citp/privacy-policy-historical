Privacy Policy

EPage® has created this privacy statement in order to  demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.  The following discloses our  information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: ep.com.

Your IP address is transmitted to each web server every time you make a request. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site.  We also use your IP address to make sure you do not see the same ad repeatedly.

If you take out an ad on EPage® or become an affiliate we use cookies to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit a password-protected page on our site (such as your account management).

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.

For affiliate relationships, Social Security Numbers may be used to report income to the appropriate governemnt authorities.

This site contains links to other sites. EPage® is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

EPage® serves and receives ads from third party sites (our affiliates). Information not displayed in the ad (and thus not available to the general public via the Web) is not made available to these third party participants.

EPage® uses third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. Our third-party advertising companies also may separately place or recognize a cookie file on your browser in the course of delivering advertisements to this Site.  These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
