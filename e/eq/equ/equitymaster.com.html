When you browse through the pages of the website, read or download information, we may collect, note or analyze details about your visit such as:

  • Your server address

  • Whether you have visited the website previously
  • What pages of the website you accessed and what you downloaded
  • Your usage pattern and characteristics in order to measure interest in and use of various areas of the site.

This information we collect relates to your server or PC rather than you yourself.

The information we collect will be used within Equitymaster to help us improve our online services keeping in mind your needs, and to help us to provide you faster navigation and access to the various sections of the website. We will use your details:

  • To send you the documents you have ordered

  • To facilitate payment through Auto debit mode

We may also contact you to follow up on your order or request, or to provide you with further information about our products and services which may be of interest to you. We may share information we collect via the website to other members of the Equitymaster Group who may use the same to provide you with information on various products and services which may be of interest to you.
