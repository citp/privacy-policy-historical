General information

Etihad Guest LLC ("Etihad Guest", “we”, “us”, “our”) is a Limited Liability Company incorporated in the Emirate of Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Etihad Guest operates through various branch offices.

This policy describes how we may collect and use your personal information including when contacting us or using etihadguest.com and our related websites ("Etihad Guest Websites").

Use of the Etihad Guest Websites is subject to our terms of use and any products and services supplied are subject to our Terms and Conditions.

Privacy Policy

Information we collect

Membership
When you join Etihad Guest, we will collect details from you to set up your Etihad Guest account and allow you to start collecting and spending reward points. This will include your name, date of birth and contact details (such as your address, email and phone number) and details of any linked family accounts. We will also ask you to set up a user name and password.

Where you are asking us to link family members to a single account, it is your responsibility to ensure each family member understands how we will use their personal information and obtain their consent to using their details in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We will assume that you have done this.  

Personalized account
We may invite you to provide additional information about your travel preferences, next of kin, dietary requirements, personal interests and any related reward scheme memberships that you may have. You don't need to provide this information to open an account, but it helps us to provide a more personalized service and makes it quicker to process travel bookings and mileage redemptions.

Earning and Redeeming miles
If you make a qualifying purchase with Etihad Airways or one of our other carefully-chosen network of international and local travel, leisure and lifestyle partners ("Partners"), or otherwise quote your membership of the Etihad Guest programme, they will provide information to us about the purchase or card activity so we can log it to your account. We do this to ensure you are awarded appropriate miles and help the Partner to validate any benefits you are claiming.

Together with our Partners, we offer you the chance to redeem your miles through our Reward Shop. Where you choose to redeem miles through the Reward Shop, we may ask you for additional information to redeem the benefit. In many cases, we will transfer you to the Partner to complete the relevant transaction. For example, if you choose to redeem miles for a holiday offered by Etihad Holidays, once you have claimed the miles on the Reward Shop, we will put you in touch with Etihad Holidays who will then make arrangements to complete the holiday booking.

Any additional information that has been provided to complete a redemption will be governed by the terms and conditions and privacy policy of the relevant merchant who is responsible for providing the product or service which you are purchasing through the Reward Shop. Merchant details (including any terms and conditions) are clearly displayed at the point of redemption.

Events and promotions
From time to time we hold events and special promotions to introduce Etihad Guest to new members. If you come to one of these events, or take part in a promotion, we may ask for your personal details to help us run the promotion and keep in touch with you about Etihad Guest services that think may be of interest to you. Any information which you provide will be kept in accordance with the terms of this Privacy Policy and you can unsubscribe from further communications from us at any time.

Customer support and online services
If you contact us with a query or comment about any of our services, or would like us to keep you informed about latest news and developments at Etihad Guest, we will need to collect information about you (including your contact details) to help us respond to your query or request. We may do this when you can call our customer support centre, or interact with us through our online support tools.

We may also keep a record of any communications we have with you (including a recording of any calls you make). We do this to help monitor and improve the quality of our customer support services. Where you have asked to receive an information update from us, you are free to change your preferences.

How we use personal information

We will hold, store and use personal information in the following ways:

  • to fulfil the terms of any agreement we have with you, including to accrue mileage to your Etihad Guest account, validate your tier status and manage your account;
  • to allow you to register on the Etihad Guest Websites and administer and personalize any of the online services which you have asked us to provide to you;
  • to keep you notified of your account status, including to tell you about any important changes to the Etihad Guest programme;
  • to keep you informed about products and services which we and our Partners offer where we think you will find this valuable;
  • to allow you to take part in any contest or other promotional offers you have asked to participate in;
  • to analyse how our users access and navigate the Etihad Guest services and use of Etihad Guest Websites;
  • to get feedback on the quality of our services - we may invite you to participate in questionnaires or surveys;
  • to help detect and prevent criminal activity; and
  • for any other purpose notified on the Etihad Guest Website.

How we share information with others
We work closely with a number of trusted partners with whom we need to share personal information to help us provide our services. These include:

  • our group companies around the world, including other Etihad brands such as Etihad Airways and Etihad Holidays;
  • Partners, to validate your entitlement to miles and enable redemption of miles;
  • Partners, to tell you about special benefits and other offers which they provide for Etihad Guest members;
  • Partners, to deliver Etihad Guest benefits that you have asked them to provide to you, such as priority boarding, upgrades, lounge access;
  • banks and payment providers, to authorize and complete payments;
  • service providers who work with us to help provide our services, including local agents who help manage customer needs at airports or operate our limousine service, and contractors who help us run our office operations;
  • companies whom we transfer or may transfer our rights and duties under our agreement with you;
  • other agencies, law enforcement or governmental organisations where we are required to so by any applicable law; or
  • our contractors and agents to do any of the above on our behalf.

How we protect your information
We invest significant resources in protecting the security and confidentiality of personal information.

  • We offer the use of secure communication transmission software (known as "secure sockets layer" or "SSL") that encrypts all information you input to our website before it is sent to us.  SSL is an industry standard encryption protocol and this ensures that the information is reasonably protected against unauthorised interception;
  • We also follow strict security procedures in the storage and disclosure of information, which you have given us, to prevent unauthorised access to that information; and
We take all reasonable steps to protect your personal information but cannot guarantee the security of any data you disclose to us via email or online.

More about direct marketing

We are committed to keeping you informed about our products and services in line with your personal preferences. We will always ask your permission before sending (or allowing our Partners to send) marketing material about products and services that we or they think may be of interest to you. We will usually send this by email but we may choose to contact you in other ways for example by phone, post or SMS if that is more appropriate. You can change your preferences at any time (see below).

Updating your personal profile and preferences

At any time you can update your personal profile, or change your preferences for the way in which you would like us to communicate with you - including how you receive news updates from us, or details of our latest offers.

If you have an online account, the easiest way to do this is by logging into your account and changing your personal details, click here. You can also click the "unsubscribe" link that you will find on any online newsletters, which you receive, or contact our customer support team by sending email to guest@etihadguest.com.

Accessing your personal information

In some countries, you have the right to see the personal information we hold about you. We may charge a small fee for processing this request. To receive a copy, please write to Etihad Guest, PO Box 35566, Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates or email guest@etihadguest.com. If you are concerned that any of the information we hold about you is incorrect, please contact us so that we can amend our records.

Transfer of your personal information

We need to transfer personal information across the Etihad group of companies to help operate our business efficiently. We also allow third party service providers to process personal information where this is needed in connection with a service they provide to us. These arrangements may involve your personal information being located in various countries around the world, including in the UAE where we have our head offices.

You need to be aware that different privacy laws may apply in these countries from any laws that may apply in the country where you are located. We will always strive to adopt the highest standards of privacy protection, wherever your personal information is located.

Updates

We keep this Privacy Policy under regular review and place updates on the Etihad Guest Websites from time to time. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically for changes. Your continued use of our website or services once you have been notified of the revised Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of it.

Our policy on "cookies"

A cookie is an element of data that a website sends to your browser, which then stores it on your system. Cookies allow us to understand who has seen which pages and advertisements, to determine how frequently particular pages are visited and to determine the most popular areas of our website. Cookies also allow us to make Etihad Guest Websites more user-friendly by, for example, allowing us to take you to the language site of last use, so that we can give you a better experience when you return to our website.

A cookie will be deployed on your device when you visit Etihad Guest Websites unless you indicate that you do not wish this to happen or if you have turned this feature 'off' in your web browser settings. For more information about how our cookies work and information about how to manage your cookie settings please visit our Cookie Policy.

Other websites

Etihad Guest Websites may link you to other websites (including etihad.com, etihadairways.com, etihadholidays.com). We are not responsible for the way in which third party websites operate or the way in which they may process any personal information, which you provide to them. It is important that you understand this and check their respective privacy policies and terms of use.
