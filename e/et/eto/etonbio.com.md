# Privacy and Security Notice

We respect your privacy and confidentiality. Your use of our web site is subject to the following Privacy Policy. We reserve the right to change this policy from time to time.

#### Information we collect from you on this website

In order to provide services with convenience, efficiency and accuracy to our customers, we collect the following information from customers through this wetsite: personal name, company name, company address, telephone numbers, and email. We do not collect any financial information from customers through this website. We may also receive and retain information from you via email. Further, we post sequencing results on this website in your password protected account.

#### How we use information

Almost all of the above mentioned information is collected to serve the purpose of providing high quality services to customers: business correspondence and communication, sample pickup, account login, etc

#### Information disclosure

We do not provide information about you and your results to any third party, unless legally required to do so. Access to portions of your information will be limited to Eton employees assigned to work with your orders. Internal procedures are implemented to vigorously protect customer information.

However, please be advised that technical and legal circumstances may compromise such efforts. Under certain circumstances we may be required by law, government regulations or under court order to disclose information to relevant authorities. Further, unauthorized parties may unlawfully intercept electronic communications between Eton and Eton's customers, or break into the website to gain access to protected areas, despite the ongoing efforts within our capability in the establishment of security. Finally, corporate restructuring, merger, or ownership changes may require full information disclosure as needed by corporate law.

#### We respect to your rights

Feel free to [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20130421031625id_/http%3A//www.etonbio.com/contact.php) if you have any concerns. If you do not feel comfortable about the security of this website, please let us know immediately, and we will take all or portions of your information off this website.

#### Other web sites

This site contains links to other sites. Eton is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these sites. Unless explicitly stated, the linkage does not imply any relationship, sponsorship, or affiliation between Eton and these sites. Further, linkage to these sites does not imply any adoption or approval of their content either.
