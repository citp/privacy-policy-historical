When we use the phrase "Anonymous Information" in this Privacy Policy, we mean information that that is not used nor intended to be used to personally  identify an individual, and is not associated with or linked to Personal Information.

As a general rule there are four categories of users of the Site and Services: “Partners" who offer Toolbars to End Users, “Search Providers” who operate search engines that are accessed through the Toolbar, "End Users" who are individual users of a Toolbar or the Site and “Visitors” who are individual visitors who browse the Site.   Our privacy policy terms with respect to Partners and Search Providers are as set forth in our agreements with such parties, and this Privacy Policy does not describe how we collect, use, share or protect information from Partners and Search Providers.
