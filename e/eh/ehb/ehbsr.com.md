## PRIVACY POLICY

**Our Commitment**  
Information about you and your home buying needs and preferences will give HBM II the ability to personalize your search criteria and display selected properties on your _Home Buyers Scouting Report_ ® ( _HBSR_ ) website that match your needs, preferences and financial ability to purchase. When you provide us with such information, you consent to its collection by HBM II, and its use by HBM II, its affiliates, and trusted cooperating businesses as described in this Privacy Policy and Terms of Use. Also, if you request HBM II to arrange a home showing for you or provide other additional assistance in your home buying efforts, HBM II may recommend a real estate agent who is available to provide such assistance, and will share your information with that agent. Having this information will help the agent be more responsive to your specific needs and preferences.

Nevertheless, we fully understand that it is important to keep any information we collect from or about you safe and confidential. As described below, when we share information with other parties with your consent, we do so only with parties who have agreed to keep your information confidential. However, you may prohibit our sharing this information by clicking on the "I Decline" button below, although this will result in your not being able to use the features of the _HBSR_ website. You may also terminate our ability to share this information at any time by sending us an e-mail asking to have your account canceled. We will not share the information you provide to us or that we collect from monitoring your use of our password-protected website with any third party except as described in this Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

 **Security Practices Protect Your Information**  
We permit our employees and contractors to access your personal information only when the information is necessary for them to do their jobs. Our employees and contractors who have access to your information are required to protect it and keep it confidential. We use technology to keep your information and transactions private and secure. We maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to protect the confidentiality, availability and integrity of your personal information.

 **The Information We Will Collect**  
The only information we will collect from you is:  


  * Basic identification data such as your name, address, telephone number and e-mail address.
  * Your stated preferences as to the characteristics and location of properties in which you are interested.
  * Data about your use of the site that we will use to verify the level of your interest and your preferences.



 **Sharing of information with Third Parties**  
We will share the information we collect only with:  


  * Trusted cooperating businesses who work with HBM II under confidentiality agreements. Our trusted cooperating businesses are businesses that either referred you to us and/or provide a service we believe may be useful to you in the homebuying process. Cooperating businesses do not have any independent right to share your information. Cooperating businesses include the real estate agency and/or mortgage company, as well as their agents and/or loan officers, who may have referred you to us or who are working with you and HBM II. Cooperating businesses may also include providers of other services related to the homebuying process such as a title company or attorney, a home insurer, a home builder, or a moving company.
  * Our non-affiliated service providers such as technical systems consultants and programmers who agree to keep this customer information strictly confidential;
  * Other third parties only to comply with a subpoena or court order, to protect against potential or actual fraud, or to facilitate the work of our auditors.
