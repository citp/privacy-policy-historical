# 

ELTbase is firmly committed to privacy on the Internet. This privacy policy document outlines the types of cookies used on ELTbase.com, the kind of information they collect and how that information is used.

#### **What are cookies?**

A cookie is a small piece of data sent from a website containing information that is transferred to your computer’s hard drive. Cookies are used to store information such as the time that you visited a website, whether you had been to the site before, and what site referred you to the web page. There are two programmes on ELTbase.com that make use of cookies. 

#### **Cookies used by Google Analytics**

Google Analytics is a web analytics tool that helps website owners understand how visitors engage with their website. Google Analytics uses first-party cookies to track visitor interactions and collect information which is then used to compile reports that can help us improve ELTbase.com. 

Google Analytics collects information anonymously. It reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. 

You can opt out of Google Analytics without affecting your experience on ELTbase.com. For more information on opting out of being tracked by Google Analytics across all websites you use, visit [**this Google page**](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=). 

#### **Cookies used by Google Adsense**

Google AdSense is a program that allows publishers to serve automatic text, image, video, or interactive media advertisements that are targeted to site content and audience.

• Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve advertisements on ELTbase.com.

• Google's use of the [**DoubleClick**](https://support.google.com/adsense/answer/2839090?hl=en) cookie enables it and its partners to serve advertisements based on your visits to ELTbase.com. 

• You may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting [**Ads Settings**](https://www.google.com/settings/u/0/ads/authenticated?hl=en).
