Privacy Policy

Personal Information
 We want you to use and enjoy the website without having to reveal personal information. We do not collect any personal data from our visitors when they use our services. You can access, browse our web site without disclosing your personal data.

Log Files
As with most other websites, we collect and use the data contained in log files. For each visitor to our website, our web server automatically recognizes only the user's domain name, but not the e-mail address.We collect our users’ IP addresses and information regarding the use of our website and services to help diagnose problems with our servers, track movement within our website and, if necessary, deny service to abusing visitors. We may also collect information related to the referring URL, access times and browser type. Unless required by law, we will not share our users’ personal information, Internet Protocol (IP) addresses and usage information, except with their consent or in aggregate or anonymous form, with third parties.

Cookies
We do not use cookies in our site but our advertisers may use cookies when serving ads in our site. We use outside ad companies like Google Adsense, Kontera to display ads on this site. These third parties may be placing and reading cookies on your browser, or using web beacons to collect information, in the course of their ads being served on this website. We do not have access to these information. Currently we work with the ad company: Google Adsense. Please check their websites for respective privacy policies.
