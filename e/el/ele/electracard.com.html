Home     Privacy

Respecting and protecting customer privacy has been vital to the success of our business. Keeping your information secure is one of our most important responsibilities. We value your trust and handle your information with care.

We always limit our collection of information and do not share your personal information, including your e-mail address, with other parties. Neither do we send unsolicited e-mail. If you are receiving e-mail from us, it is because at some point you, or someone with access to your e-mail account, requested it.

Protecting Online Applications

When you apply online for services or products offered by our web site, your sensitive information is only accessible to authorized representatives.

We do not sell nonpublic information to anyone and do not disclose nonpublic information about our customers or former customers to anyone, except as permitted by law. In no circumstance, will we provide customer account information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Links to Other Sites

This site may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
