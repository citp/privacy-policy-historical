Hachette Filipacchi Media U.S., Inc Online Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the Web sites and online services on which it is posted. This includes our Web sites, Wireless sites designed for access from mobile devices, applications (including those accessed from third party online services such as social networks) and other electronic services (each referred to in this Privacy Policy as an "Online Service").

The Online Service is published and maintained by Hachette Filipacchi Media U.S., Inc. ("HFMUS"), with offices located at 1271 Avenue of the Americas, New York, NY 10020. This Privacy Policy governs HFMUS's collection of information through the Online Service and our use and disclosure of such information. Except as set forth herein, this Privacy Policy does not apply to information HFMUS collects in connection with your subscription to any of our print publications.

You may opt out of our sharing of your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes by following the directions in the ["Right to Opt Out"] section below.

Advertisements displayed to you through the Online Services and elsewhere online may be customized to your interests and preferences, based on your Personal Information and Usage Information derived from your use of our Online Service, as well as information about your use of other Web sites and online services. To opt-out of this, [click here].

By visiting or using this Online Service, you agree to the terms of this policy as they may be amended from time to time. As we update and expand our services, this policy may change, so check back to this page from time to time.

Purpose
Your privacy is a serious matter to HFMUS. In order to make your use of our Online Service as worthwhile as possible, we collect certain information from your computer or device each time you visit us and sometimes we ask you for Personal Information. This privacy statement explains what Personal Information and Usage Information is collected through our Online Service, how the information is used, and with whom we may share such information. Please take a few minutes to read our privacy policy so that you understand how we treat your information.

Right to Opt Out
You have the right to "opt-out" of certain uses of your Personal Information. At the time you are requested to provide Personal Information through this Online Service, you may have the opportunity to elect to, or not to, receive e-mail offers from HFMUS or have your Personal Information shared with other companies. You may also make this request by sending an e-mail to privacy@hfmus.com. In this e-mail please identify which Online Service you are referring to and say whether you would like to opt-out of (i) receiving e-mail offers from that Online Service (ii) having your Personal Information shared with other companies or (iii) both. You may also opt-out of HFMUS e-mails by clicking on a link within the email you receive. Please understand that if you opt-out of receiving e-mails from HFMUS, we may still contact you by e-mail in connection with your account with us or to provide you with important information about HFMUS or this Online Service. Also, a request to have us stop sharing your Personal Information with other companies will only apply going forward from the date of the request, and HFMUS will not be responsible for any communications that you may receive from companies that received your Personal Information prior to such request. In these cases, please contact that company directly.

Attention Kids
If you are under the age of 13 (12 or younger), you may not provide any information about yourself to this Online Service. While you may view content on the Online Service, you may not register with the Online Service, enter any of our sweepstakes or contests, or participate in any of our chat rooms, bulletin boards or other activities that would let you enter information about yourself. If we learn that you have provided information about yourself to this Online Service, we will delete such information.

Note to Parents.
HFMUS will not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from kids under 13. If you have any concerns about this Online Service, wish to find out if your child is a member of one of our Online Services, or wish to cancel your child's membership, please contact us at privacy@hfmus.com.

What Information is Collected through this Online Service?

HFMUS does not collect any Personal Information such as name, address or e-mail address on its Web sites without the knowledge and consent of the individual submitting such information. Such information is collected, for example, through registration forms, games, sweepstakes and contests and through voluntary online questionnaires that appear from time to time on some Online Services. "Personal Information," includes your name, home or other address (like a school), e-mail address, telephone number, and/or any other information that may identify you or allow online or offline contact with you. We occasionally update or enhance the Personal Information we have about you with Personal Information about you that we obtain from third parties such as data aggregators.

We may also ask you to provide other information about yourself such as your age, hobbies, likes, dislikes, etc.

You may be able to send information about our products and services to your friends and family members through our Online Service by clicking on an "E-mail to Friend" or similar feature on our Online Service or in a message that we have sent you. In some of these cases (unless you simply forward our message on your own), you may provide the name and e-mail address of your friend or family member to us. Such information will be treated in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable law. It is your responsibility to ensure that your friend or family member consents to this.

You may review the Personal Information that you have provided to HFMUS’s Online Service by e-mailing privacy@hfmus.com or writing to HFMUS at 1271 Avenue of the Americas, New York, NY 10020 ATTN: Legal Department. We will send you a copy of such Personal Information we have on file in your user account (if any). You may send us new or updated information at any time via e-mail or mail.

Usage Information

Any or all of the following activities with regard to Usage Information (i.e., cookies, beacons, IP addresses and clickstream data) may be performed by us or on our behalf by our services providers:

Cookies

We try to provide you with services, products and offers customized to your interests. In order to do that, we may use "cookies" and "local shared objects" to keep, and sometimes track, information about you. Cookies are small pieces of data that are sent to your computer or device from a server and stored on your computer's or device’s hard drive or memory. Local shared objects are also files that are stored on your computer or device using a media player that is installed on your computer or device (such as a Flash player) or using HTML5, which is the language that your computer or device uses to communicate with our servers.

We may use cookies and local shared objects to access your account information (stored on our computers) in order to deliver better and more personalized services and promotions to you; estimate our audience size for our advertisers; assist in processing items in your shopping basket; track the number of entries in our promotions, sweepstakes and contests; identify which areas of our Online Service and the Web you have visited; and optimize our ads on the Web and on search results pages. Cookies and local shared objects do not gather any Personal Information about you; they simply track where you travel on the Online Service and what you look at. (Cookies and local shared objects may, however, store your login name which allows you access to some of our Online Services.) In doing so, they may enable HFMUS to relate your use of the Online Service to Personal Information that has already been submitted. We may also match information obtained from cookies and local shared objects with data provided by you or collected by third parties. We analyze this data both in the aggregate and in connection with individual users. This information lets us give you the services you want that are connected with your interests. We also provide the analysis of our users (as a group, of course) to prospective partners, advertisers and other third parties. Many other sites and services use cookies and local shared objects for very similar purposes.

Most browsers can be set to inform you when a cookie has been sent to you and provide you with the opportunity to refuse that cookie. Similarly, most media players that enable local shared objects can be set to manage local shared objects. As an example, Adobe’s Flash player allows you to manage local shared objects (otherwise known as "Flash cookies") though its Flash Player Settings Manager. You may be able control the use of HTML5 local shared objects by reducing the amount of space you allow for them through your Web browser’s settings. Refusing a cookie or local shared object will generally not interfere with your use of an Online Service. However, refusal may, in some cases, preclude you from using features of the Online Service that require registration. You may occasionally get cookies from our advertisers. These cookies are sent from third-party computers and are subject to such third parties' policies.

By using our Web Site, you consent to our use of cookies and local shared objects as described above.

Beacons

We may also use beacon technology to help track usage of the Online Service. Beacons (also called single pixel gifs or action tags) allow HFMUS and our service providers and business partners to track page viewing counts and patterns, which we use, for example, for future service development and to measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. We may also use beacons within the context of certain emails to determine the effectiveness of the mailing.

Advertising Partners

To the extent that the Online Service carries advertising by third parties, HFMUS's digital advertising technology companies, which include, but are not limited to, AOL, Audience Science, BlueKai, Collective, eXhalate and Google also use cookies, beacons and other technologies. The non-personally identifiable information collected by these companies, for example, about your visits to this and other sites and services is used for the purpose of targeting ads and measuring ad effectiveness on behalf of advertisers and publishers who specifically request it. For additional information on the information that is collected by HFMUS's digitaladvertising technology vendors and to know your choices about not having this information used by them, please click on their names (above) or visit http://www.networkadvertising.org/consumer/opt_out.asp.

IP Address

Our server automatically collects data about your Internet Protocol address when you visit us. This information, known as an IP Address, is a number that’s automatically assigned to your computer or device by your Internet service provider whenever you’re online. When you request pages from HFMUS, our servers log your IP Address. HFMUS uses IP Addresses for internal system administration, to help diagnose problems with our server, to report aggregate information to our advertisers, and to administer our Online Service. Your IP address is also used to gather broad demographic information, such as your country of origin, geographical location, and Internet Service Provider (i.e. AOL, Earthlink, etc.). Our server cannot automatically record your name or address, but we may link this information with your Personal Information.

Clickstream Data

Our servers may also record the Web page from which you linked to our Online Service (e.g., another Web site or a search engine); the pages you visit on our Online Service; the Web site you visit after this Online Service; the ads you see and/or click on; other information about the type of Web browser, computer, device, platform, related software and settings you are using; any search terms you have entered on this Online Service or a referral site; geographical location; and other usage activity and data logged by our servers. We use this information for internal system administration, to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Online Service. We may also link this information with your Personal Information.
