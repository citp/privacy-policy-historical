Eliteweb Incorporated Privacy Policy

Eliteweb.cc is committed to providing you a first-class Internet experience and that includes taking your privacy very seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

What This Privacy Policy Addresses

  • This policy addresses how Eliteweb Incorporated (“Eliteweb”) treats personal information that Eliteweb collects and receives, including information related to your past use of Yahoo! products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available.
  • This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Eliteweb does not own or control, nor to people that Eliteweb does not employ or manage.
  • Eliteweb links to many external sites. This privacy policy by no means governs those sites. Each site should have their own privacy statements which should be reviewed to determine their practices.

Information Collection and Use

General

  • Eliteweb collects personal information when you register with Eliteweb for an Elite ID, when you use Eliteweb subscription products or services, when you visit certain Eliteweb pages and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes.
  • When you register for an Elite ID we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, zip code, time zone, occupation, industry, and personal interests. For some subscription products and services we may also ask for your mailing address and contact numbers. Once you register with Eliteweb and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us. We use that information to provide you with a more customized Elite experience.
  • Eliteweb automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, Eliteweb cookie information, and the page you request.
  • Eliteweb uses information for the following general purposes: to fulfill your requests for products and services, to customize the advertising and content you see, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous aggregate reporting for internal and external clients.

Children

  • We kindly request that a child under the age of 13 obtain parental permission prior to registering for an Elite ID.
  • Eliteweb will not contact children under age 13 about special offers or for marketing purposes.
  • Eliteweb treats a child’s privacy on the Internet with the utmost care. If you are a parent or guardian and have a concern about your child’s use of an Elite ID, please contact us.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • Eliteweb does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:
    • We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with Eliteweb under very strict confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help Eliteweb communicate with you about offers from Eliteweb and our marketing partners. These companies do not, under any circumstances, have any independent right to share this information.
    • We respond to court orders, subpoenas, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims;
    • We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Eliteweb’s terms of use <http://about.eliteweb.cc/Policies/TermsofUse.html>, or as otherwise required by law.
    • We transfer information about you if Eliteweb is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, Eliteweb will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
  • Eliteweb displays targeted advertisements based on personal information. Advertisers (including ad serving companies who work with Eliteweb) may assume that people who interact with, view, or click on targeted ads meet the targeting criteria. (Example: Male aged 18 – 34 within a particular geographic location.)
  • Eliteweb does not provide any personal information to the advertiser when you interact with or view a targeted ad. By interacting with or viewing an ad, however, you are consenting to the possibility that the advertiser will make the assumption that you meet the targeting criteria used to display the ad.
  • Eliteweb’s marketing partners and advertisers include financial service providers and non-financial companies.

Cookies

  • Eliteweb may set and access Eliteweb cookies on your computer.
  • Eliteweb will access Eliteweb cookies previously stored on your computer when on our network of web sites in order to provide you with the best most customized experience.

Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information and Preferences

General

  • You can edit your Eliteweb Account Information at any time by accessing “Elite ID: At Your Service”. <http://www.profiles.eliteweb.cc/?action=editinfo>
  • New interest categories may be added from time to time. Existing users are not added to new categories unless they explicitly update their account to reflect their new preferences.
  • Elite ID users can adjust their interest categories at any time.
  • We reserve the right to send you certain communications relating to the Eliteweb service, such as service announcements, administrative messages and the periodic Eliteweb Newsletter, that are considered part of your Elite ID account, without offering you the opportunity to opt-out of receiving them.
  • You can submit a request to permanently delete your Elite ID account by contacting us. Certain information will remain in our archives even after your account is deleted.

Confidentiality and Security

  • Eliteweb’s servers are monitored 24 hours a day by security guards and closed circuit video cameras. Only authorized users may enter the server area.
  • Eliteweb limits access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs at the highest caliber level.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

  • Eliteweb may periodically update this policy. Major updates will be complimented with prominent notice on our site noting that a change has occurred.
