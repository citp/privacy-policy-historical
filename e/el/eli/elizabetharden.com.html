INFORMATION ELIZABETH ARDEN COLLECTS AND HOW WE USE IT

Personally Identifiable Information

We may ask and you may volunteer to provide us with personal information ("PI") when you visit our Site. PI is any information that concerns you individually and would permit someone to contact you. PI can include your name, billing address, shipping address, e-mail address, telephone number or mobile telephone number, date of birth, credit card information or information about other Elizabeth Arden products you have purchased. For example, we may seek PI when you choose to open an account with us, purchase a product, participate in a game, electronic bulletin board, contest, sweepstakes, or other promotional event. Elizabeth Arden will not collect PI about you unless you submit it. If you e-mail Elizabeth Arden or contact the Consumer or Customer Service hotline, we may collect PI during these communications as well.

Collecting PI can assist us to determine how best to provide useful information to customers and to determine which of our products or services are of the most interest to them. We may also ask you to create a screen name and/or a password, where applicable, and provide us with demographic information (such as information about your age or gender), information regarding your interests, or similar information.

PI will only be collected when it is voluntarily provided to Elizabeth Arden. You can always choose not to provide certain information, but then you may not be able to take advantage of or participate in certain features of the Site.

Non-personally Identifiable Information

As is true with most websites, we may also collect from you non-personally identifiable or anonymous information, such as an IP host address, the browser type, the Site usage, and other data which do not identify any individual ("Aggregate Information").When your computer contacts our web servers (for example, when you visit the Site,view an HTML e-mail, or download an interactive desktop from the Site),Aggregate Information may be collected automatically. Said Aggregate Information may include the date and time you accessed the Site, the pages you viewed and the internet address of the website, if any, from which you linked directly to this Site. We also may determine the applicable technology available to serve you the most appropriate version of a web page, e-mail or similar service.

We use a third-party service provider to serve ads on our behalf across the Internet and sometimes on this site. They may collect anonymous information about your visits to our website and your interaction with our products and services. They may also use information about your visits to this and other websites associated with the network advertiser to target advertisements for goods and services. This anonymous information is collected through the use of a pixel tag, which is industry standard technology used by most major websites. No personally identifiable information is collected or used in this process. They do not know the name, phone number, address, email address, or any personally identifying information about the user.

The Site may use anonymous browsing information collected by its server mainly to help it administer and improve its website.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technology

The Site may use browser "cookies." Cookies are pieces of information that may be placed on your computer by a website for the purpose of facilitating and enhancing your communication and interaction with a particular website. In addition to cookies, the Site uses a variety of technical methods for tracking purposes,including web beacons. Web beacons are small pieces of data that are embedded in images on the pages of websites. Elizabeth Arden also uses these technical methods to analyze the traffic patterns on the www.elizabetharden.com website, such as the frequency with which our users visit various parts of the Site.
