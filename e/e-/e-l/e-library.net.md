# Privacy - eLibrary - Open Ebooks Directory

  
**Privacy Statement**

The eLibrary, collects information from ebook sellers/authors applying for ebook submission. 

The eLibrary will not divulge any personal information. The only information to be released will be information about submitted ebooks.

Notifications - This site abides by a No-Spam Policy. Emails are only sent to ebook sellers and to newsletter subscribers.

 **Our Commitment To Privacy**

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. 

**The Information We Collect:**

On our "Submit ebook" page, we collect the following information. 

Name   
Email  
URL & Description Of Ebook

We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties. We have a no spam policy.

 **Our Commitment To Children's Privacy:**

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.
