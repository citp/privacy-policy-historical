# Online Privacy Policy | e-academy Inc

**Your Email Address:** If you provide us with your email address for the purposes of receiving information from us, or if you communicate with us through email, we may retain the your email address for future communications. We may use this address to send you information about our products and services. If such information is sent to you, we will provide you with instructions on how to unsubscribe. We will not sell, trade, or otherwise distribute your email address to anyone.

**Other Personal Information:** If you provide us with your name, address, phone number, company name, position title, or other personal information we will not sell, trade or otherwise distribute your information. We will use it for the purpose of contacting you per your requests. We may use it internally to understand the needs of our customers or to improve our Web Site.

**Web Site Use Information:** Like other online sites, e-academy uses standard "cookie" technology and Web server logs to collect information about how our Web Site is used. Information gathered through cookies and Web server logs may include the date and time of visits, pages viewed, and time spent on our site. We may also use "Web beacons" or "clear gifs" to determine if any promotional offers have been seen and responded to.
