# Yaesu - Privacy Policy

| 

**Privacy Policy**

To protect your privacy, yaesuusa.com collects only the personal information you provide for the purpose of mailing information to you. This information is placed in a private database for future mailings of product information and/or services to you which may be of interest and will not be given or sold to other entities. This website does not collect any personally identifiable information about you other than information that you affirmatively choose to submit to us. It does automatically collect information about the use of our site, including which pages are most frequently visited, how many visitors we receive daily, and how long visitors stay on each page. This policy does not describe information collection policies on other sites, including those linked to or from yaesuusa.com. 

Yaesu.com may revise and update this Privacy Policy at any time by posting such revised Privacy Policy at [http://www.Yaesu.com](http://www.yaesu.com/). Your continued usage of Yaesu.com will mean you accept those changes
