# Year of the Affiliate

**Year of the Affiliate** respects your right to privacy. This policy summarizes what personally identifiable information we may collect, and how we might use this information. This policy also describes other important topics relating to your privacy.  **Year of the Affiliate** reserves the right to modify this privacy policy at any time.

**Year Of The Affiliate** is affiliated with Universal Wealth Creation Pty Ltd and Sean Rasmussen. Any emails or contact is kept relevant and is made on behalf of **Year Of The Affiliate** , Universal Wealth Creation or Sean Rasmussen to support you or any of the products or services you may have purchased or requested.

## Information Collection 

**Year of the Affiliate** will only collect personally identifiable information (such as name, title, company name, address, telephone number, or e-mail address) that you voluntarily provide through one of our online forms (such as Contact Us, Whitepaper download, Request for Free Consultation, or our Newsletter Subscription). We will collect general information (such as the type of browser you use, the files you request, and the domain name and country from which you request information) to improve our website and better meet your needs. However this information is aggregate and cannot personally identify you. 

E-mail addresses provided for the express purpose of subscribing to our newsletter or to receive information will only be used in order to transmit the requested information.  We will never trade, rent, sell or otherwise distribute your e-mail address.
