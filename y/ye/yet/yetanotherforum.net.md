# Privacy Policy

YetAnotherForum.NET collects only the information that we need to process your order and to allow you to use the website. We will not share, rent, lend, or sell your information to anyone without your express permission. Cookies are used to keep your session and shopping cart information during your visit to our site. If you have any questions, please contact us to discuss.
