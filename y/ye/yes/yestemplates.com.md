# Web design Templates, Logo, Template Customization, Complete Website Services

|   
---  
**YesTemplates** has created this privacy policy to demonstrate our commitment to the privacy of the users of our web sites. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy, and how we treat personally identifiable information collected from our visitors and users.  
   
**WebSite Registration**..   
  
Registration process requires users to submit contact information. This data is used to identify the customer when logging into the system as well as for invoicing purposes. Your contact information may be used in order to contact you during the authorization process.

Our company will not sell or provide client information to any other party.  
  
   
**Payment Transactions**..   
  
All payments on our website are processed through **paypal** or **2checkout** merchant services. Payment information collected is stored only at Paypal or 2checkout.  
  
  
   
**IP addresses and logging** **..**  
Our server’s record information regarding visitor on our website, such as IP addresses, date/time, and pages accessed. This information is used to generate reports for statistical purposes.  
   
Customer Information...  
Information, such as names and email addresses with phone number, testimonials and all information, are collected from visitors at the time of a sale in order to complete the purchase. This information is **NOT** sold or distributed to any third parties. We are firmly against spam and neither participates nor promotes any activities that assist spammers.  
   
Advertisers...  
Some of the advertisements on our site are provided by outside advertisement companies and may contain cookies. While we use cookies on our site, the cookies associated with these advertisements are collected by their respective companies and are therefore not accessible to us.  
   
External Site Links...  
This site contains links to other websites on the internet. While we provide links to them, we are not responsible for their content or privacy policies.  
   
**Member Profile update**..  
When any Member logged in, he can change his member information in his profile anytime.
