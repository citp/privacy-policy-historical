# Privacy Statement - Yuni Words of Wisdom

**Yuni Words of Wisdom** has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site.

**1\. INFORMATION AUTOMATICALLY LOGGED.** We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information.

**2\. COOKIES.** Some services on this site requires that you have cookies enabled in your web browser (the minimum or default settings). Cookies are only used to maintain your identity while you are connected. Nothing is written back to your system. 

**3\. ADVERTISERS.** We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information. 

**4\. REGISTRATION FORMS.** Our registration forms require users to give us contact information (like their name and email address), financial information (like their account or credit card numbers), and demographic information (like their zip/post code or age).

We use customer contact information from the registration form to send the user selective updates about our products and services. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. We use this data to tailor the visitor's experience at our site, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences.

Under no circumstances will Yuni Words of Wisdom ever resell, redistribute or allow third party access to email lists or personal information.

**5\. EXTERNAL LINKS.** This site contains links to other sites. **Yuni Words of Wisdom** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

**6\. PUBLIC INFORMATION.** Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

**7\. CHOICE/OPT-OUT.** Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at the point where we request information about the visitor.

This site gives users the options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service. The necessary instructions can be found accompanying each service.

**8\. CORRECT/UPDATE.** Yuni Words of Wisdom offers users the ability to correct or change the information collected during registration for any of the site services.

The relevant instructions for doing this can be found within each service. Users may change this information at any time and as often as necessary.

**9\. CONTACTING THE WEB SITE.** If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can [email us](https://web.archive.org/web/20110527145305id_/http%3A//www.yuni.com/contact/index.htm.html).
