# Pacific Yurts Privacy Policy

Pacific Yurts has created this privacy policy to demonstrate a firm commitment to the privacy of this site’s users. The following statement discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices. If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, Contact [Pacific Yurts](https://web.archive.org/contact-us/).

**All information that you submit on this site will be treated as confidential.** We do not sell, trade or rent to third parties any personal information gathered from this website unless we are required to do so by law. **Your privacy is our priority.**

**Information Collected**  
Our “Newsletters” form, “Get In Touch/Contact Us” form and “Yurt Builder 3D” form collect information so that we can better understand the needs of visitors to this site. The information also enables us to contact visitors who request more information about our products.

**Notification of Changes**  
Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page.

**Email Contact**  
We may send promotional e-mail offers to visitors who provide us with their e-mail addresses. We will stop sending promotional e-mail to anyone who requests it.

**Links to Other Websites**  
We are not responsible for the data collection practices, policies or content of websites other than www.yurts.com, including any websites that are accessible from this website.
