## Privacy Statement

**YOURLifeChoices** website has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this web site: [http://www.yourlifechoices.com.au](http://www.yourlifechoices.com.au/)

  * We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our web site. 
  * This site contains links to other sites. **YOURLifeChoices** is not responsible for the privacy practices nor the content of such web sites. Visitors to our site should be aware of the privacy and confidentiality policies of those sites to which we provide links.
  * We may collect email addresses from entrants to competitions, participants in surveys and users of the site's facilities. Also site visitors can select to opt-in email lists. The email lists are used to send you information, news, and details of offers, competitions and surveys on the **YOURLifeChoices** web site. The email lists are stored on a separate email server and are protected from unauthorised use. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the Opt-Out section below.
  * We do not collect names and postal addresses except where required to send a prize. This information is not stored in a database.
  * **YOURLifeChoices** does not profile users other than in surveys, and then we do not collect names and/or postal addresses. Any personal information which may be requested in a survey cannot be related to any individual. Sensitive information is not collected in any survey conducted by **YOURLifeChoices**.
  * We do not provide any email addresses to third parties.
  * Our site does not use cookies except that visitors may choose to have their name and email address saved in the Discussion Forum and we may use cookies where only one entry is allowed in a competition or survey.
