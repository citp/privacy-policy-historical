**Cookies:**

Cookies are small text files stored on the hard drive of your computer while the browser is running. Online Training does not use cookies to track, collect or store your personal information. However, we may use cookies to make it easier for older users to fill one of our insurance quote forms. If you do not accept cookies, you can easily change the settings on your browser to prevent cookies from being stored and still visit the online education.

**Tracking Pixels:**

Some pages on Education Online may contain electronic images known as tracking pixels or single-pixel GIFs. These points may collect non-personally identifiable information, such as cookie numbers of visitors, time and date of page view, visitor referring URL and a description of the pages they visit. The information collected using these pixels does not identify individual users. Online Education is committed to the security of your personal and financial information. Our servers and data processing are in a secure environment protecting your account information to access a third party.
