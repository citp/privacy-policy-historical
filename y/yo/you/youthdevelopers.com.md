# Privacy Policy | Youth Developers

**What information do we collect?**

We collect information from you when you subscribe to our blog or newsletter. When registering or subscribing to our website or blog, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your full name and your email id or mailing address. You may, however, visit our blog anonymously.

Google and other ad publisher, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve or publish their advertisements on our website or blog. Google and other ad publisher use of the DART cookie enables it to serve different kinds of advertisements to your users and it is based on the visit of users to your website and also other sites on the internet. Any user or visitor may opt out of the DART cookie by clicking or visiting the Google advertisement and may collect your information, if any user fill up the contact form or any kind of leads provided by them.

**What do we use your information for?**

We do not use your information for wrong purposes. The information is collected from any user on our website may be used in one of the following ways:-

1\. **To personalize your experience** – The information collected from you helps our team to better respond to your individual needs.

2\. **To improve our website** – We are regularly trying to improve our website according to our users’ point of view. We always appreciate your feedback and comments on our website.

3\. **To send periodic emails** – The email address provided by you may be used to send you the regular updates of our website and respond to your queries or questions.

**How do we protect your information?**

We never sell your information to anyone. The information we collect from you is always safe and secure. We understand the privacy of your personal information. That is why we never share your personal or any kind of information with others. However, advertisers may be sharing your information to others if you filled their form by clicking on the advertisement shows on our website.

**Your Consent**

By using our website, you consent to our privacy policy.

**Contacting us**

If anyone has any question or doubt regarding this privacy policy, then he or she feel free to **[Contact](http://youthdevelopers.com/contact-us/)** with us. You can mail us your query or doubt or question to our mail id contactyouthdevelopers@gmail.com
