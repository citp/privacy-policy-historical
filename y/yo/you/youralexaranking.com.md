# Privacy Policy

* General 

We respect your online privacy and recognize the need for protection and management of any personally identifiable information. Any information given to us will, of course, be treated in strict confidence. We absolutely fulfill our commitment. 

All information collected of the clients will be kept in strict confidence. Every process is assured by the person in charge. We have confidential agreement with employees as well as restriction and access control mechanism.

We do not share with or disclose to any third parties the information about current or former clients without permission.

We have independent electronic data protection measures and system. 

* Third Party Access to Personal Data 

YourAlexaRanking.com will forward your Personal Data to third parties only if and only to the extent necessary to process a payment transaction or other relevant business transaction pertaining to the YourAlexaRanking.com's services, where a third party service provider may be involved. Any such third party has agreed to strictly comply with the applicable privacy policy regulations. 

YourAlexaRanking.com will not disclose any Personal Data to third parties insofar as you have not expressly consented to such disclosure, it is possible that due to judicial orders, or legal regulations, YourAlexaRanking.com will be compelled to reveal information to authorities, or third parties under certain circumstances, or in the case that third parties could intercept or access certain information or data transfers, then in any such cases YourAlexaRanking.com will not be held responsible for the revealed information. 

Where YourAlexaRanking.com has knowledge that one of YourAlexaRanking.com's employees or agents is using or disclosing Personal Information in a manner contrary to this Policy, YourAlexaRanking.com will take reasonable steps to prevent or stop the use or disclosure. YourAlexaRanking.com holds its employees and agents accountable for maintaining the trust that our customers place in our company. 

Also, in the unlikely event that YourAlexaRanking.com or substantially all of YourAlexaRanking.com's assets are acquired, your Personal Information may be one of the transferred assets. It is YourAlexaRanking.com's practice to seek appropriate protection for information in these types of transactions. 

* Security 

To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, to maintain data accuracy, and to ensure the appropriate use of your Personal Information, YourAlexaRanking.com utilize physical, technical and administrative procedures to safeguard the information YourAlexaRanking.com collect.

Among other things, we use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption when collecting or transferring sensitive data, such as credit card information. SSL encryption is designed to make the information unreadable by anyone but us. This security measure is working when you see the symbol of either an unbroken key or a closed lock (depending on your browser) at the bottom of your browser window.

We also use firewalls and restricted access methods to ensure the security of the information we collect. We limit access to your Personal Information and data to those persons in YourAlexaRanking.com, or as agents of YourAlexaRanking.com, that have a specific business purpose for maintaining and processing such information. YourAlexaRanking.com's employees who have been granted physical access to your Personal Information will be made aware of their responsibilities to protect the security, confidentiality and integrity of that information and have been provided training and instruction on how to do so.

* Protection from Web Crawlers or Spam 

To the best of its ability, YourAlexaRanking.com will prevent your e-mail address from being recorded by "web crawlers" or "web spiders". If you believe that your e-mail address has been recorded in this way, please kindly inform us promptly. 

* Changes to Privacy Policy 

YourAlexaRanking.com may change its Privacy Policy from time to time. You should check the Privacy Policy frequently for changes.
