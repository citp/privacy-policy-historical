# IABUK : Privacy

### We are committed to upholding your privacy as a valued user of this website.

The personal data we collect, process or use is treated securely and in accordance with the IAB's privacy policy, described below. Whenever you give us personal data, you are consenting to its collection and use in accordance with this policy, including our use of cookies explained below.

### Your personal data

We log your Internet Protocol (IP) address and use cookies to recognise you when you return to our site.

### What is a cookie?

A cookie is a text-only string of data that is entered into the cookie file or “memory” of the browser on your computer. It will typically contain the name of the domain from which the cookie has come, the “lifetime” of the cookie, and a value, usually a randomly generated unique number. 

### How to manage the use of cookies at our website

All browser technologies enable you to manage the cookies in the cookie folder of your browser. This means that you can either delete cookies from your cookie folder once you have finished your visit at our site or you can set your preferences with regard to the use of cookies before you begin surfing our website. Please note that deleting or rejecting cookies may affect your user experience of our website for example you may be asked to re-enter some information e.g. your name and password to use some pages. 

### How we use cookies

Using cookies is useful for us in optimising our site and making sure we are offering the best content to our users. We use cookies in order to give us information on the number of visitors to different parts of our website and thus to monitor which areas are most useful. We do not use them to identify you.

### How we use your personal data

We collect your personal data in order to register you for IAB events, send you relevant email communications and maintain the relationship between the IAB and your company. All our email communications have a clear opt-out procedure. 

### Information from third parties

From time to time we may wish to contact you with further information and events of other companies we may think may interest you. Please email us on [info@iabuk.net](mailto:info@iabuk.net) if you do not wish this to happen. It is not our policy to pass on any personal data to third parties.

### Links

This site contains links to other websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy policies of any of these sites. 

### Access to your personal data

We endeavour to keep your personal data accurate and up to date. If you become aware of any errors or if you would like to see what personal data we have collected from you, please e-mail [info@iabuk.net](mailto:info@iabuk.net).

### Changes to this policy

The IAB privacy policy may change at any time, so you may wish to check it each time you visit our website.
