Privacy Policy

We at Yixe Media LLC. understand that you value your privacy and we strive to respect that privacy when you are using our services. This Privacy Policy describes what information we gather from you, how we use that information and what we do to protect it. By using the Yixe Media website, you are expressly consenting to the information handling practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect From You

When you sign up with Yixe Media, you establish a password-protected personal account for ordering ringtones, graphics, games and other items and services. During the account creating process and/or to place an order, we request contact information (such as name, a valid email address, cell phone number and address) and certain billing information (such as a credit card number and expiration date.)

In addition, we may occasionally request information from users via online membership profiles, surveys or contests. Participation in these profiles, surveys or contests is completely voluntary and you therefore have a choice whether or not to disclose this information. Information requested may include contact information (such as name and mailing address), and demographic information (such as zip code, age level).

Once you create an account, we keep track of your interactions with us, including but not limited to your site activity, order history, payment history and correspondence with us.

Our website may provide you with the opportunity to send emails to your friends, family members or relatives. For this feature of our site to work, we need to collect the email address(es) of those to whom you send messages. The email addresses we collect in this context are not used for any other purpose other than to send your request. We will only resend an email to these email addresses at your request.

Our websites’ servers collect and retain technical information regarding visits to our website such as the IP address assigned to the computer visiting the site, the type of browser used by a visitor and the operating system running the user’s connections to the service. This data is not linked to your personal account. It is used in the aggregate to analyze the ways in which our site is used, and how we can improve the user experience, and may be shared, in the aggregate, with third parties such as advertisers.

Yixe Media is in no way directed at children under 13 years of age. We will not knowingly collect personal information of children under 13 years of age, and if we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will promptly delete it.

Yixe Media uses your personal information in the following general ways: To provide you with the Yixe Media services, to allow for your identification and authentication onto the Yixe Media website, to contact you for billing and administrative functions, and for internal research and reporting purposes. We also use the information provided by you in surveys for purposes of monitoring or improving the use and satisfaction of the Yixe Media service.

Cookies

Cookies are pieces of information that your browser receives from Web sites and stores on your computer's hard drive. Information in cookies includes preferences you select while visiting a particular Web site. For example, by storing cookies on your hard drive, you'll return to a specific Web site with the same settings you used on a previous visit. Yixe Media never saves information such as your credit card number on cookies. We do require cookies in order to manage and individual visits the Yixe Media site. Certain cookies are also used to determine how you were referred to the Yixe Media site as well as your browsing and buying activities, in order that we can determine the success of any particular online marketing campaign. If you have cookies turned off, you will not be able to access the Yixe Media service. If you enter our pages from the site of one of Yixe Media’s partners, our system will recognize the path you have taken. Yixe Media advertises on such partners' sites or we've entered into a promotional partnership with them. We track the number of visitors that these partners send us to see the success of our online marketing or promotional campaign. These partners will not know your personal information, unless you have given it to them separately or consent to give it to them through us.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with non-affiliated third parties for direct marketing purposes or otherwise, without your consent. We do, however, share your information with trusted third party contractors who provide services for us. For example, we may provide your personal information to phone companies in order to ensure that we can properly bill for our services. These third party contractors are prohibited from using the information we provide for purposes other than performing services for us.
