# FHPL:::

##  Policy Features 

At Family Health Plan Insurance TPA Ltd, herewith called FHPL, your privacy is important to us. We ensure secure transactions and follow customer privacy policy of high standards. As part of our priorities, we maintain your trust and confidence.

At FHPL, our information gathering and dissemination practices state as under: 

**By using our website you agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of our privacy policy. Please do not access or use our website, if you do not accept to the terms and conditions of our Privacy Policy. This policy is subject to change at any time without prior notice. We therefore request you to go through our privacy policy frequently to be updated with changes incorporated from time to time.**

###  Collection of Information and Usage: 

We collect certain personal and non-personal information about you classified as mandatory and non-mandatory when you use our website. 

Personal Information shall include but not limited to your name, date of birth, gender, contact information like your telephone and mobile numbers, residential address, e-mail id etc. While using some of our services you would be required to give us your personal information for creating a User ID or registering on our website, communicate with our Customer Care by call, email or otherwise. You may also choose to go through our website without providing us your information in which case you will not be able to have access to few of the sections in the website.

Also you would be required to give us certain personal sensitive information about you such as your medical records including but not limited to your prescription, test reports, past medical history etc., We assure you that these information will be used only for providing required healthcare services as mutually agreed to be rendered by us. . Also you are given the privilege to upload your medical records with us and store the same to which only you shall have access to using User ID unique to you. This will help you have access to all your medical records from anywhere in the world at a click away. 

We would like to state that we collect information that is absolutely necessary and relevant for us to make your experience convenient, smooth and most importantly safe. This also will help us in providing you with customized services, assist you with all your queries and set right the issues faced by you, to follow up with you in order to safeguard you from any kind of fraudulent and unlawful usage. 

We use data collection devices such as "cookies" on certain pages of the Website to promote trust and safety. "Cookies" are small files placed on your hard drive that assist us in providing our services. We offer certain features that are only available through the use of a "cookie". 

We also use cookies to allow you to enter your password less frequently during a session. Most cookies are "session cookies," meaning that they are automatically deleted from your hard drive at the end of a session. You are free to decline our cookies if your browser permits you to do so although we recommend strongly that you allow them to ensure you have access to all of our services. 

  


###  Sharing of Information:

We respect your privacy and hereby declare we do not disclose your information except as under: To law enforcement agencies in connection with an investigation having the interest of general public at large, after having been approved by the due process of law. 

To our associates and affiliates to help detect and prevent fraud, identity theft and other illegal activities, correlate related or multiple accounts to prevent abuse of our services; and to facilitate joint or co-branded services that you request where such services are provided by more than one corporate entity 

###  Safety Precautions: 

We have stringent, strong security measures undertaken to ensure against the loss, misuse or alteration of your information with us. We shall take all necessary precautions to safeguard your information and protect it from any unauthorized use. We have SSL Certificate issued by Global Sign and other safety measures in place to guard your information with us against any theft or unlawful usage or modification thereof. 

A "Force Majeure Event" shall mean any event that is beyond the reasonable control of FHPL and shall include, without limitation, sabotage, fire, flood, explosion, acts of God, civil commotion, strikes or industrial action of any kind, riots, insurrection, war, acts of government, , unauthorized access to computer data and storage device, computer crashes, breach of security and encryption, etc.
