# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

This site has been provided by Femail.com.au/Female.com.au for the Australian Internet Community as a whole. We expect that whilst using this site that respect is given to any other users of the site. That is, we expect that no abusive, inappropriate or racial language is used in the chat or discussion areas of the site.

Femail.com.au, Female.com.au, Femail nor Trellian shall be held responsible for any loss of income, data, downtime or any direct or indirect damage claims caused by this Website.

 **Your Privacy**

Femail.com.au is committed to protecting your privacy. Information collected about you is simply for Femail.com.au's feedback purposes and is intended to improve our ezine and enhance your femail.com.au reading experience.

 **Contests**

When you enter a contest or other promotional feature, we will request your email address and postal address in order to notify you in the event of winning. Your email address may also be used to inform you of future competitions and events on affiliated websites.

 **What about "cookies"?**

"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computers hard drive. Our cookies are safe and do not contain any personally identifying information. Most web browsers except cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent this.

 **What happens to information collected by Femail.com.au**

Femail.com.au does not trade, sell, or rent your personal information to customers, sales, traffic patterns, and related site information to reputable third party vendors. Any information disclosed from statistics is general and does in no way disclose personal identification details. 

Occasionally the information collected could be used to notify you of important functionality changes to the femail.com.au website, email system and/or offer you genuine opportunities to enter fabulous competitions and the option to read important articles that concern all women.

 **In summary**

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect from you to enhance your femail.com.au experience. We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to others. 

**Your consent**

By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Femail.com.au. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will display those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we disclose it. 

**Tell us what you think**

Femail.com.au encourages feedback and any questions you may have about privacy. Please send email to the Editor [michelle@femail.com.au](mailto:michelle@femail.com.au).
