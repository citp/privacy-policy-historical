Privacy

The Felix Films website retains information SOLELY for your own use when checking out at our online store.  We do not place any tracking cookies on your computer, and will not monitor any activity within, or outwith, the Felix Films website.  If you choose not to register with us, the only information that will be stored on our server and your own PC is a unique Cart ID which allows the store software to recall any items that you placed within your cart on your last visit.  No other information is stored.

For customers who registered with Felix Films, your details are stored securely to aid your checkout process and your email address is added to our newsletter mailing list which allows you to be kept up to date with new releases and projects, as well as special offers.  Every newsletter that we send contains a prominent link to allow you to remove yourself from our list.  You can remove yourself at any time by clicking the link at the bottom of the newsletter and you will no longer receive any future mailings from us.

Felix Films will not display, distribute, sell or rent your information to any third parties or companies.  Any information you submit is entirely for use with Felix Films.
