FedSmith.com Privacy Policy

FedSmith.com is committed to our readers' privacy. We handle information submitted to us from our readers with care. By visiting and using FedSmith.com, you agree to the terms stated in this privacy policy.

Personal information we collect from our readers
FedSmith.com will collect personal information from our readers from time to time. Examples of personal information that FedSmith collects includes names, email addresses, agencies, and titles of our readers. This information is collected at different times through forms on our web site, such as when users sign up to customize the FedSmith home page, for our mailing list, or when responding to one of our articles.

Sensitive Information
Some parts of the FedSmith.com web site require readers to submit sensitive information to us, such as credit card numbers. FedSmith.com takes great care in collecting this information to ensure that it is kept safe. Any page on our site that requires a user to enter sensitive information is secure so that the information submitted is encrypted when it is processed and therefore cannot be read by a third party while in transit over the Internet. You can tell that these pages are secure because a small padlock icon will be displayed in the bottom corner of your web browser.

Does FedSmith.com ever give my personal information out to third parties?
No. FedSmith.com does not disclose the personal information of our readers to another party. We do not rent or sell our mailing list to other businesses or individuals. We securely store and use your personal information solely for correspondence between us and our readers.

FedSmith.com will post comments from our readers on our web site on occasion, such as comments on FedSmith Interactive. These comments are posted anonymously, unless a reader grants the company permission to display personal information with the comments.

Technical/Site usage information collected
FedSmith.com collects information about our site visitors whenever they view our site, such as IP addresses, browsers and operating systems used, the full uniform resource locator (URL) to, through, and from our site, and cookies. This information is used by FedSmith.com for statistical/tracking purposes only. Cookies are used to remember our visitors for offering custom features such as the customized home page. FedSmith.com only stores insensitive information in cookies to ensure security.

Additional information about features on the FedSmith.com site is located in our FAQ section.
