# Privacy Policy - FeedbackFive

Your privacy is very important to FeedbackFive.com. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that FeedbackFive.com collects, and the ways in which FeedbackFive.com uses that personal information.

FeedbackFive.com collects personal information during the secure order process, including such information as name, address, email address and credit card information. All of this information is kept strictly confidential. In addition, we may also collect information used to maintain the security and usability of the site, including information such as pages visited, your IP address, and browser type.

FeedbackFive.com uses SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption for the transfer of all sensitive data, and takes precautions to ensure that this data is protected at all times.

FeedbackFive.com will never use your information, including your email address, for any purpose not explicitly authorized by you. For example we will use your data to help provide the agreed-upon services and to maintain appropriate communications. We may disclose your information if necessary to comply with legal requirements or procedures, or if required to protect our legal rights.

We may update this policy from time to time. Please check back frequently to this page for revisions to our Privacy Policy.
