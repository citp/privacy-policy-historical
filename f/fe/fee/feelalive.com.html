Privacy Policy

Schwabe North America, Inc. - Website Privacy Policy
Date Last Modified: August 15th, 2012

Your privacy is important to us. Schwabe North America and affiliates ("Schwabe North America") are committed to maintaining the privacy and accuracy of your personal information and to protecting such information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction.

This Statement of Privacy describes how Schwabe North America treats information received about you when you become a Schwabe North America subscriber, when you provide information to us, and when you use the Schwabe North America web site. This statement addresses the following.

Please read this Statement of Privacy carefully. Questions regarding this statement should be directed to privacy@enzy.com.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

You do not need to provide any information that identifies you ("Personal Information") to use the Schwabe North America web site. In general, you can visit our websites without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information.

How Personal Information Is Protected

Schwabe North America takes reasonable steps to protect Personal Information. Schwabe North America uses technologies and processes such as encryption, access control procedures, network firewalls and physical security. These technologies and methods increase the security and privacy of information traveling to, from and within the Schwabe North America network.

Schwabe North America also safeguards Personal Information from unauthorized access. Only authorized Schwabe North America employees or agents carrying out permitted business functions are permitted to access Personal Information. Employees who violate Schwabe North America's privacy access policies are subject to disciplinary actions, including termination when appropriate.

How To Access and Modify Personal Information

If you submit Personal Information to Schwabe North America, you may modify your Personal Information by notifying Schwabe North America Customer Service at 825 Challenger Drive, Green Bay, WI 54311 or e-mail Customer Service.

What About Cookies?

We use cookies to store information between visits to our site. Cookies are a mechanism to remember information about a visitor from one page to another. We use cookies to remember your name and the contents of your shopping cart. This allows you to leave our site, come back later, and still have the contents of your shopping cart intact. Our website requires the use of cookies. Turning off cookies or rejecting the Schwabe North America, Inc. cookie will prevent you from accessing the website.

Changes To Schwabe North America's Statement of Privacy

Schwabe North America may make changes to this statement from time to time. We will post changes to our privacy statement here, so be sure to check back periodically.

Questions, Comments, Concerns

If you have any questions or comments about our use of Personal Information or about this Statement of Privacy, please contact us at privacy@enzy.com. Schwabe North America will use reasonable efforts to promptly investigate any complaint you may have regarding our use of Personal Information and to comply fully with the legal and regulatory supervisory authorities responsible.
