What are cookies and how does The FeedRoom use them?

COOKIES:
A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. Each web site can send its own cookie to your browser if your browser's preferences allow it, but (to protect your privacy) your browser only permits a web site to access the cookies it has already sent to you, not the cookies sent to you by other sites.

USER CHOICES ABOUT COOKIES:
You can configure your browser to accept all cookies, reject all cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. In order to use the FeedRoom and enter its sweepstakes, we recommend that our users surf, with cookies turned on.

THE FEEDROOM USES ITS OWN COOKIES FOR A NUMBER OF PURPOSES, INCLUDING TO:
1. Keep track of preferences you specify while you are using FeedRoom services.
2. Display the most appropriate advertising, based on your interests and activity on FeedRoom.
3. Estimate and report our total audience size and traffic.
4. Conduct research to improve The FeedRoom's content and services.
5. The FeedRoom uses cookies to track user entries in its sweepstakes.

OTHER COMPANIES' COOKIES ON THE FEEDROOM:
1. Please note that the FeedRoom allows other companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages, or serving email newsletters, to set and access their cookies on your computer.
2. Advertisers' use of cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not the FeedRoom Privacy Policy.

What is The FeedRoom's policy regarding children's privacy?
The FeedRoom is committed to the safety of children. Children under the age of 18 may not participate in The FeedRoom sweepstakes or promotions. If children under 18 continue to use The FeedRoom, they should ask a parent for permission before sending any information to the site, or sending personal information to anyone on line.
1. The FeedRoom will not request or knowingly collect from a child any information that can be traced to the child, such as an email address, name or information about the child's family.
2. The FeedRoom will not knowingly use personal information from children under the age of 13 for any marketing or promotional purposes.
3. The FeedRoom will not send any direct email offers to users who indicate that they are under the age of 13.
