# fashionUK >> privacy policy

We at **fuk.co.uk** are committed to protecting your privacy. We will only use the information that we collect about you lawfully (in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998). 

**fuk.co.uk** is owned by WideMedia Ltd, a UK registered company. Our address is PO Box 33052, London W9 2XE. Telephone: 020 7286 7660. Our office is open Monday-Friday 10am-6pm. 

We will not email you in the future unless you have given us your consent by registering for the **fuk.co.uk** email newsletter or by opting in when entering a competition. If you no longer wish to receive newsletters, just email us and you will be removed from our database. 

We collect information in three ways: 1) Email addresses of everyone who opts to receive our monthly newsletters. 2) Visitors who opt to fill-in our online survey. 3) Competitions. Occasionally we allow a company supplying competition prizes to also receive the entries - in such cases we clearly state this on the competition entry form and always include an opt out option. 

Any information about you is held securely in accordance with our internal security policy and United Kingdom law. We will not sell or divulge your information to any 3rd parties. 

All advertising on **fuk.co.uk** is organised by DoubleClick and conforms to the standards of the British Codes of Advertising and Sales Promotion and the rulings of the ASA. If you have any questions or comments about our advertising, please email us [here](mailto:advertise@widemedia.com). 

To contact DoubleClick UK telephone +44 (0) 207 399 4400 or email [info@uk.doubleclick.net](mailto:info@uk.doubleclick.net). To view their privacy policy please click [here](http://www.doubleclick.net/us/corporate/privacy/privacy/default.asp?asp_object_1=&).
