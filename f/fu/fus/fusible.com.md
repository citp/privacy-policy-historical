# Privacy - Fusible

Fusible.com collects and maintains certain information (including e-mail addresses, IP addresses, names, geographic information, etc.) on its website and via the make-an-offer and other forms. This is done to help us provide our customers with the best customer service and with valuable information regarding relevant products and services from Fusible and appropriate third parties.

Fusible.com is dedicated to protecting personal information and will make every reasonable effort to handle collected information appropriately. All information collected, as well as related requests, will be handled as carefully and efficiently as possible.

If you need assistance in updating the information you have provided to Fusible.com or you have questions or comments about these policies, contact Fusible.com at admin@fusible.com.
