We can be reached via e-mail at [webmaster@mc6.com](mailto:webmaster@mc6.com) **Introduction**  
MC6, INC respects each individual’s right to personal privacy. We will collect and use information through our Web site only in the ways disclosed in this statement. This statement applies solely to information collected at MC6, INC’s Web sites 

**Part I. Information Collection**  
For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes the consumer's IP address and the website that the consumer came from. 

**Part II. Information Usage.**  
The information collected by MC6, INC is used for internal review and to improve the content of our Web page. It is then discarded. 

**Part III. Access to Information**  
MC6, INC does not sell or share our data with any third parties. 

**Party IV. Links to Other Sites**  
Our sites contain links to sites other than our own. Those sites may not follow the same privacy policies as MC6, INC. Such links may be to advertisers, content providers or other third parties. These sites may send their own cookies to you, and may collect information and use it in a way inconsistent with MC6, INC's privacy policy. MC6, INC does not control the content that appears on these sites. The Links are provided only as a convenience, and the inclusion of a link does not in any way imply endorsement of the linked site by MC6, INC.
