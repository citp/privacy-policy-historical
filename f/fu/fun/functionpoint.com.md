# Function Point Productivity Software Inc - Privacy

Function Point Productivity Software Inc. (FP) respects your online time and privacy. FP never sells or rents e-mail addresses or customer information to third parties. Information regarding demonstration of the productivity and timesheet management software provided is only used by FP and its authorized dealer network. Survey information is used for FP's internal marketing usage only. Customer information will not be used for the purpose of broadcasting unsolicited e-mail bulletins. Only individuals that specifically request to subscribe will receive broadcast e-mail newsletters and bulletins.
