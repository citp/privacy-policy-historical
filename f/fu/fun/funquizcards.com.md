# FunQuizCards.com:: Privacy Policy

|  We respect the privacy of our visitors and surfers. We do not sell, barter or exchange email addresses you give us when sending a quiz card unless a visitor has given us explicit permission to do so. No information is saved regarding the number of quizzes, names or topics of quizzes or their results, which you took on this website. 

**Limitations on privacy:**  
We reserve the right to provide information if we think - correctly or incorrectly and reasonably or unreasonably - that our quizzes and ecards, are being used in violation of our terms of use, including, but not limited to, being used in any illegal, harmful, threatening or anti-social activity. 

We try to protect your privacy, but you must please understand that any use of the Internet or of email creates the possibility that someone could access the data transmitted. There is always some risk on the web that personal information might be accessed and privacy cannot be guaranteed. 

**Affiliates, Advertisers and Others:**   
Before providing information to any party other than funquizcards.com, you should check their privacy policy, as they are third parties. We may use third party TAF services for the function of sending pages and ecards. We are not responsible for the content or the privacy practices utilized by any other sites, linked by us. 

For any queries, [contact us](http://www.funquizcards.com/info/contactus.php).
