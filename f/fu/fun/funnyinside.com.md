# Policy

  
**Funnyinside.com** has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site. 

**Personal Information**  
This Web site does not request any personal information or collect any information that personally identifies you or allows you to be personally contacted.   
Since we do not collect any personal information on this Web site, we do not share any personal information with any third parties nor do we use any personal information for any purposes. 

**Cookies**  
Cookies, by themselves, cannot be used to find out the identity of any user. Site name will only store information provided voluntarily, but this information is not personally identifiable. If for some reason you would rather not take advantage of the benefits made possible with cookies (for example, to track what ads you've seen to avoid you seeing them again), most browsers allow you to reject cookies or to choose which cookies to accept. 

**Newsletter**  
We collect optin subscriptions to our humor newsletter, but we will never sell or disclose your e-mail address. If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, you can unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time. 

**Sitemeter**  
Sitemeter is our third party tracking system to track the usage of our site. For more info you can view their privacy policy.
