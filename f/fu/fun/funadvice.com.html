Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy - please read and understand this document before using, registering, or engaging in any interaction with this site if you are concerned about cookies, personal privacy and security, tracking systems, and the like. 

What information do you collect?
This site collects "personally identifiable information" on users such as name, email address, zip code, gender, date of birth, country and tracks statistics on interactivity with the site. 

None of the information collected is sold, bartered, traded, or given to any third parties by us, so you can trust that all of the information you give to us we keep confidential, and you know that no third parties obtain your data.

Why do you collect all this information?
We collect the information we do in order to provide the products & services to our members that we provide today, and to develop new products & services in the future for our visitors, members and advertisers.

New notice regarding our third party advertising providers
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here to contact us.
