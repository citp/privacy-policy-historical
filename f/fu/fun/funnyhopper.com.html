This privacy policy (hereafter referred to as the "Privacy Policy") is applicable to our FunnyHopper.com website ("Website").

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our home page.

The information we collect

The information our Website collects includes the following:
  • IP address, Cookies and Web Beacons

    When you visit our Website, our server automatically save your computer's IP address.

    We use cookies to store certain information gained from your browser. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer and is linked to information about the user. We may use cookies, web beacons or similar technologies to enhance and personalise your experience on the Website, for example to improve the Website, remember your preferences as applicable, present and help measure and research the effectiveness of our Services and advertisements, customise the content and advertisements provided to you through the Website. We may also use ad network providers to help present advertisements on the Website. These ad network providers use cookies, web beacons and similar technologies to help present, better target and measure the effectiveness of their advertisements using data gathered over a period of time and across their networks of web pages to determine or predict the characteristics and preferences of their users.

  • Email address and Name

    We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with any third parties.

  • Demographic information (Country) and browser information

    We may use certain anonymous demographic information about the user, i.e. the country for statistics and we may share such information with certain ad network providers to help them deliver more relevant advertisements through their ad networks. The use of cookies, web beacons and similar technologies by such third parties as ad network providers will be subject to their own privacy policies, and not this Privacy Policy.


We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our Website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that A number of individuals visited a certain area on our Website, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals.

We never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in any way unrelated to the described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.

Our Commitment To Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
