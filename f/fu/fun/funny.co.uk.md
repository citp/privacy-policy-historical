# Funny.co.uk - The UK's Premier Comedy Site - Privacy Policy

This is the website of Funny.co.uk. You can reach us at feedback@funny.co.uk 

On this website we collect information from those who become registered users. This is to help us improve the content of Funny.co.uk, notify users of updates and tailor the site better to them. You will not receive any unauthorised e-mail or postal mail from any third parties as a result of the information we hold. Other users will not be able to see your email address when you make any sort of posting to the site. 

**Cookies**   
On Funny.co.uk we use cookies to for logging in registered users and providing a user friendly interface for our visitors. We do not use cookies to store any personal information other than that you give us. To become a registered user you must have cookies enabled. Browsing the site with cookies disabled is possible but enabling them will provide a much better experience of the site. 

**Visitor Information**   
We do not make any of the information on our visitors (including your email addresses) available to any other organisations, other than broad demographic information (i.e. percentage of users who are from the UK) for marketing purposes. Some of the information given by registered users is used to create a personal profile page. This is to create a community atmosphere and no email addresses are revealed on these pages. 

Any information collected from our visitors including address, phone number etc will at no time be given or sold to any organisation unless otherwise stated before the information is collected. 

**Email From Funny.co.uk**   
If you receive email from Funny.co.uk it is because either you sent us an email which required a response or we thought that it required a response, or you became a Funny.co.uk Registered User. You have the option to leave any of these features at any time through notification to the proper channels. If you have questions about e-mail that you do receive from Funny.co.uk, contact us and address the specific problem so that we can take any necessary actions to rectify the situation. 

**Other Sites**   
Funny.co.uk contains links to other sites across the internet, however we are not reponsible for the privacy policies or content of these web sites. If you feel a web site is inappropriate then please contact us and we will review our link. 

**Our Advertising Agency**   
Scotti Advertising Media works with a third party that serves ads to this site. To find out more about how Engage Media manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please go to <http://www.engage.com/privacy>. If you still have concerns regarding your privacy after reading our policy please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/feedback.phtml) and we will try to answer your queries.
