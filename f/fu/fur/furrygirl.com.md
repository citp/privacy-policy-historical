# Furry Girl > Privacy

**Privacy Statement for FurryGirl.com**

Following is my policy regarding your privacy if you come to my site. I appreciate your interest and will never disclose your information to other parties.

* Use of Cookies 

I do not use cookies on my site and do not track you or your email address in any way.

* Sale of Member Information

I do NOT sell, share or give away any information about you. I do use a third party billing processor for processing your payment, but those companies do not share information with any other parties.

* Privacy Policy Changes

If I decide to change any of my privacy policies, I will post those changes here. By checking this page, you will always know how if I am using your information. 

If you have any questions, please [**contact me**](mailto:webmaster@furrygirl.com).
