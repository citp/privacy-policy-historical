FFXIV Info takes privacy very seriously to ensure the safety of your data. We will only store the data that you provide to us. 

## Log Files

Like many other Web sites, http://www.ffxivinfo.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. 

## Data

When you join the website you are asked for a username, a password, your e-mail address, your date of birth and your country of residence. 

All passwords are encrypted and we will not know what they are. We recommend that you use a unique password and definitely not your FFXIV game password for extra security. 

Your IP address will be saved upon login to ensure further security. Any further data you provide with us is optional. 

Some of the data you provide on signup and while editing your profile will be used on your own personal profile and be available for anyone to see. We advise you to be careful what information you decide to reveal as we can't be held responsible for anything that happens as a result. 

We will not sell or trade your information to a third party. However, anonymous traffic and usage details may be used to benefit the website by gaining more visitors. 

## Cookies and Web Beacons

http://www.ffxivinfo.com uses cookies as a way to remember preferences about our visitors such as keeping you logged in. Cookies are tiny pieces of information that your browser creates to help remember details of websites that you frequent.
