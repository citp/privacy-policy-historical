# Privacy Policy - FDRLab Data Recovery Centre

Fdrlab Data Recovery Centre is committed to protect the privacy of the users who visit our web site. All the information that we collect is used to facilitate our services. We value your privacy and will take all the necessary steps to protect your information.

All the information collected at FDRLab is kept strictly confidential and we will not sell, or otherwise disclosed the information to a third party.

The information we collect at FDRLab Data Recovery Centre is used for internal review and is then discarded or used to improve the content of our Web pages. We do not share information with other organizations for commercial purposes.

We may use customer information for new uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will contact you before we use your data for these new purposes to notify you of the policy change and to provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses.

Fdrlab Data Recovery Centre is aware that the information we collected from our customers are of much importance and hence we are very particular about the security of our web site. We carry out appropriate security measures to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you.
