Privacy Policy

User Profile Information
Our web site is designed, modified and maintained to best serve your needs. Although we do ask you for information at various areas of our site, we only use the information to provide you with better product news, service offerings, special promotions, and web site features.  The personal information we ask for includes your name, address, email, and phone number.  We do not sell or rent your information to third parties.  You may unsubscribe from information and personalized services at any time.

Once you register on our web site, you may update your profile at any time.  Your usage of our web site provides us with valuable information on exactly what you need when you visit us.

We use web site tracking technology to prevent you from having to enter redundant and duplicate data at various areas of our web site.  We analyze the user trends of our web site visitors to better understand their needs, and to make special offers and web site features available for frequent users.

We will contact you by email if there is a substantive change to our privacy policy.

Security
Keeping your information secure is one of our most important responsibilities.  We safeguard information based on established security standards and procedures.  Security measures are in place to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.
