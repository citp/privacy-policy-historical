# Privacy policy of fapgay

This document details the usage of data collected by fapgay.com

We do not collect any personal information from our users, registered or unregistered. We do not even require an email address in order to sign up.

## Data collected

Registered and non-registered users can browse videos and images without giving up any of their personal information. An IP address is recorded on registration and in the server logs for security reasons.

Registration is required in order to upload image galleries and favorite videos. An email address is only required for uploading image galleries and is only used in the event that illegal content has been posted (so we can contact you). Your email address and any other information will not be sold or used for any other purpose.

 **Content Uploaded** : Any images you upload to the site and any channels you make will be publicly viewable, as will your public profile.

 **Cookies** : Cookies are used only for the following purposes - keeping you logged into the site when you sign in with your fapgay account and preventing more than one popunder from displaying during your session.

 **Third Party Cookies** : Third party cookies are set by Google Analytics to track pageviews.

 **Log file information** : Server logs are rotated daily regarding visitor IP addresses, web useragents, referring urls, and the time of visit.

## Disclosure of information

Fapdu will not disclose or use any of your information unless required to do so by a law enforcement agency or in the event of a [Terms of Use](https://web.archive.org/terms) violation.

## Security

Fapdu takes the following steps to maintain a secure website:  
 _Google safebrowsing tools_  
 _Daily maintenance_

You use fapgay at your own risk, however we do everything in our power to make your experience a safe and secure one.
