GENERAL
Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy is intended to give you confidence in the privacy and security of the personal information we obtain from you. However, please note that we are not responsible for any use of your personal information you provide to third-party applications or websites that may be accessed via the Websites. We recommend that you review the privacy policy of any third-party applications or websites that you use.

COLLECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION
When you use our Products or Websites, you may be asked for personally identifiable information such as your name, address, email address, telephone number. By giving us such information, you consent to our using it in the manner described in this policy.

USE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION
We use personal information to provide access to premium areas of the Websites, send out e-mails, personalize your visit to our Websites, personalize your use of our Products, and enable us to improve the products and services we offer. We may occasionally carry out market research and send you details of services and offers that we think may be of interest to you. If you do not wish to receive such information, please check the bottom of our e-mails, which contain a provision for you to opt out of receiving any further information from us.

DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION
We will not disclose your personal information to any third party unless you have consented to such disclosure or where we are required to do so by law. 

COOKIES
Cookies are small data files that a website you visit may save on your computer or handheld device that usually includes an anonymous unique identifier. Our Websites may use cookies for user authentication, keeping track of your preferences, etc. 

Third-party advertisers may also create and access cookies, which will be subject to their privacy policies we accept no responsibility or liability for the use of such third parties cookies. If you do not wish cookies to be placed on your PC or handheld device, then they can be disabled in your web browser. The option to do so is normally found in your browsers security settings section. Please note that permanently disabling cookies in your browser may hinder your use of our Websites as well as other websites and interactive services and/or force you to re-login every time you access the Websites.

SECURITY
All security on our Websites is treated seriously. Where applicable, we undertake security steps, including use of SSL technology, on our back-end systems that store customer account information and to protect data transmissions. However, this is not a guarantee that such data transmissions cannot be accessed, altered or deleted due to firewall or other security software failures. Please note that we use a leading payment gateway service to process your online credit card orders, so we do not see nor do we store your credit card information.

POLICY CHANGES
Changes in this policy will be posted on our Websites. You are advised to check our Websites regularly to view our most recent privacy policy.

SYSTEM

This coming Sunday, 10/1, custom scoring systems on MyGuru will not be available. We want to be clear: MyGuru is not going away...it will just be temporarily closed on Sunday while we work correct the issues.

13 hrs ago
View Details

We have been working round the clock to track down the issues causing site stability on Sunday's and we have narrowed down the issues to MyGuru and the customization it provides. While we know this is an important feature and one of the primary reasons you have subscribed to FantasyGuru, it is causing an abnormal load to the site; and en masse, causing issues on Sundays when all are simultaneously accessing features, pages, etc. Therefore, this coming Sunday, custom scoring systems on MyGuru will not be available. We want to be clear: MyGuru is not going away...it will just be temporarily closed on Sunday while we work correct the issues.

That being said, we will have a number of scoring presets available for use, including PPR, Non-PPR, Yahoo, ESPN and more. We understand that not all of our presets match your individual systems so we will only be limiting the results to our presets only on Sunday. We encourage you to login and take a look at custom scoring projections between now and Sunday while it is available. We also recommend finding a comparable preset that you can use on Sunday, before Sunday, for any last minute projection changes. We do not anticipate this being a long term solution, but it is an effort to getting you the information available that you have come to expect from us.

NEW FEATURE

The Strength of Schedule Tool, found under the Projections & Statistics dropdown, has been repaired and is fully functional.

4 days ago
View Details

The Strength of Schedule Tool, found under the Projections & Statistics dropdown, has been repaired and is fully functional. Use "Basis Start" and "Basis End" to determine the desired range of historical games for FPG accuracy. Use the "Output Start" and "Output End" to determine how far ahead you want to look.

Example: Since it's Week 4 currently, we would set Week 1 as our Basis Start and Week 3 as our Basis End, so that the tool will calculate FPG over those three weeks. We would then set our Output Start to Week 4.
