In addition, we may also collect non-personally identifiable information, such as your computer's IP host address, pages viewed, browsing habits on the Website and other data which does not specifically identify you ("Non-Personal Information").

We will never sell your Personal Information to any third party not affiliated with Fantapper without your consent except in connection with the sale or merger, in whole or in part, of Fantapper, its assets or the division responsible for such services.

We may use Non-Personal Information to improve and enhance your experience on the Website and for our research activities.
