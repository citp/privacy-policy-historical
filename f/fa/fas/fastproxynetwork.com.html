What information do we collect?

Ultius, Inc. (the Company) collects information from you when you subscribe to our newsletter or fill out a form on our website or for example if you “like” us on Facebook or “follow” us on Twitter or Google+. We also collect analytics data from you, including your IP address, location, internet source, browser, device used, operating system and other analytics. When registering on our site or placing an order, you may be asked to enter your name, e-mail address, mailing address and/or phone number. If you are placing an order, you will be asked to enter your payment information, such as credit card number, billing information and other security questions. Since our payment Gateway is PayPal and Authorize.NET, we do not collect or store this information. You are not obligated to create an account with PayPal unless you choose to do so. In the case of your participation in Ultius Identity Verification, we would collect from you a form of government issued identification and the payment method that you used for processing your order. 

How do we use your information?

Any of the information we collect from you may be used in any or all of the following ways: 

  • To provide access to the services you may have registered for and/or ordered on our website;
  • To better understand the ways in which our website and services are being used in order for us to improve them;
  • To help diagnose problems on our website; 
  • Customizing our website and applications and the services we provide to meet your likely needs;
  • To continually improve our website based on the information and feedback we receive from you;
  • To improve the customer service and support experience we provide through our website and customer service department in order to help us to more effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs;
  • To administer any types of contests, promotions, surveys or other site features;
  • To verify that your payment method is authentic and belongs to you;
  • To speed up the login and registration process by using Facebook Connect.
  • To send periodic emails to provide you with information, marketing communications and offers related to the Company. These may include communications such as:
    • A welcome email that confirms your username and password;
    • Communications replying to your inquiries;
    • Communications providing the services you request;
    • Communications regarding management of your account;
    • Service-related communications and/or announcements (such as email or telephone contact regarding your account);
    • Promotional email and communications, newsletters and offers.

If at any time you wish to stop receiving emails, simply send an email to support@ultius.com with the subject line STOP or click unsubscribe at the bottom of the email message.

How do we protect your information?

We use fair and reasonable electronic, physical, legal and administrative protection, and secure servers in connection with all transactions and regarding all of your private data that is collected by us. All sensitive/credit information supplied to us is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into our Payment gateway provider’s database to be accessible only by those authorized persons and/or entities with special access rights to such systems, which persons and/or entities are required to keep such sensitive/credit information confidential. After you conduct a transaction, your private information (credit cards, social security numbers, financials, etc.) will not be stored on our servers. However, for administrative purposes, your name and address are stored in our system and subject to the same privacy protection referred to above. Notwithstanding these efforts to protect your private data, no protection or security system can be 100% effective, and the Company cannot guarantee the security of your private information aside from the stringent efforts mentioned herein. Consequently, the Company disclaims any and all liability for the interception, loss or theft of, or other unauthorized access or harm to your data. By your use of our Website, and/or any of our applications, facilities or services you specifically acknowledge and agree that you understand the foregoing and our Privacy Policy as stated herein, and you fully assume the associated risks and hold the Company harmless in connection therewith. 

In the event we request information from you as part of our Ultius Identity Verification (UIV), we store your personal data through an SSL encrypted file storage method and then destroy the data after a period of ninety (90) days. This process is also subject to potential security breaches despite our best efforts to protect all UIV requested data using the latest technology, and thus the Company disclaims any and all liability for the interception, loss or theft of, or other unauthorized access or harm to the UIV data you provide to us. By your participation in our UIV process, you specifically acknowledge and agree that you understand the foregoing and our Privacy and UIV Policies as stated herein, and you fully assume the associated risks and hold the Company harmless from any and all damages or injury for the interception, loss or theft of, or other unauthorized access or harm to your data in connection therewith. 

Email Restrictions: Certain email addresses reflect a higher level of risk of intrusion. For example, temporary email accounts registered through institutions such as governments, armed forces, or higher education are provided on an at-will basis and are therefore temporary in nature. Additionally, when you use these email accounts, you submit to unwarranted access at the discretion of your administrator. For that reason, you cannot register an Ultius account using the following email address extensions: (i) .GOV; (ii) .EDU and; (iii) .MIL. Instead, we recommend that you use private email accounts available from a number of consumer vendors: Gmail by Google, Outlook by Microsoft, or Yahoo! Mail.

Dial-in Pin: When you register an Ultius account, we will ask you to create a four (4) digit dial in pin. This is a security code that we will ask you in order to verify your identity and account ownership when speaking to our customer service reps via phone, text, or live chat. You can easily view and change your dial in pin code in your edit profile view.

Do we use cookies and other similar technologies?

Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enable the sites or service providers’ systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information. We use cookies to help us provide service to you and conduct our business in various ways, including:

  • Identify and authenticate you on our website, so that we can provide the services you order;
  • Keep track of information we receive from you in order to provide the services you order from the company;
  • Keep track of advertisements and compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future; 
  • Keep track of your preferences or where you left off in your use of our website or website tools;
  • Understand your potential or likely interests, and perform market research, so we can provide better service on our website;
  • Using Web beacons or “tags” to measure how you use our website and web pages, such as which links you click. We use this to improve our website and our online services, and customize our services to match our website users’ preferences and likely interests;
  • We may contract with third-party service providers to assist us in better understanding our site visitors. In some cases, these third-party service providers have access to some of your private data in order to provide services. These service providers are not permitted to use the information collected on our behalf for their own purposes, except to help us conduct and improve our business; 

If you prefer, you can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies via your browser settings. Like most websites, if you turn your cookies off, some of our services may not function properly. However, you can still place orders by contacting customer service.

Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses or purposes. 

Do we display any private information anywhere on our website? 

We do not post any of your private information on the public part of our website. Certain private information disclosed by you is posted with any order you submit and is only visible to our select staff and writers.  We urge care in posting information on our website regarding information you do not intend to be shared with anyone and the Company disclaims any and all liability for the interception, loss or theft of, or other unauthorized access or harm to data you submit to or post on our website.
