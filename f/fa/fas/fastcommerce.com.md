# Free ecommerce free online web store free shopping cart software

Fastcommerce.com is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. 

**Information We Collect:** Our primary goal in collecting personal information from you is to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and personalized experience while using our services. This allows us to provide services and features that most likely meet your needs, and to customize our service to make your experience easier and quicker. 

In some circumstances, Fastcommerce.com may request personal information from you, like your name, e-mail address, company name, or telephone number. Fastcommerce.com will never sell or share your personal information, unless permission is given to specifically share with our partners. 

**Disclosure to third parties**   
When purchasing our products or services, Fastcommerce.com secure server does collect your personal information and credit card information. HOWEVER! This information is NEVER shared, or sold. It is for use within our system to better serve our customers.
