Policy
FashionGo.net has created this privacy statement to demonstrate our firm commitment to protecting your privacy in all aspects of our business, from websites and print publications to trade shows and conferences.
This privacy statement discloses what information we collect and how we use it.
Information Collection and Dissemination
In general, you may visit this website without identifying yourself or revealing any personal information. As you browse FashionGo.net, the website collects the IP address of your computer and collects other data that is used to evaluate site traffic and usage patterns. Such information is aggregated with tracking data from all site visitors. We will use the information to personalize FashionGo.net according to your individual preferences based on the aggregated information. Some portions of this website may require you to give us personal information such as your name, job title, company, physical mailing address, e-mail address, telephone and fax numbers.

This information is primarily used to deliver the specific services that you have requested. If you submit financial information, we use that information primarily to verify your credit and collect payments for your purchases, orders, and registration.
Information on Minors
As a business-to-business company, FashionGo.net does not collect information from minors. Online registration and web site usage is restricted to adults who are professionally engaged in the businesses we serve.
Updating Visitor Information
You have the option of correcting, updating, and modifying your personal information, financial information, or visitor profile at any time, as often as desired. The only thing you cannot change is your username ID selected during registration.
Third Party and Affiliated Links
FashionGo.net contains external links to other websites including suppliers, advertisers, and affiliates. FashionGo.net has no responsibility concerning the content of or information gathered by these other websites. We do not claim to endorse any of these websites merely because they are linked on FashionGo.net.
Cookies
A "cookie" is a bit of data sent by a website through the browser to the computer of the user visiting the site that enables the website to return the results the browser expects. FashionGo.net uses temporary, session-specific cookies to ensure visits to its websites are smooth and customized for the visitor. Such cookies allow us to provide a visitor's browser with information tailored to the visitor's preferences and needs. FashionGo.net uses cookies that remain on a visitor's computer after the visitor leaves the FashionGo.net

website. Our cookies neither reveal nor contain any identifying or personal data. Cookies also cannot read any information on a visitor's computer or interact with other cookies on the visitor's hard drive.
If you do not want your browser to accept cookies, you can turn off the cookie acceptance option in the browser's settings. However, disabling the cookie support function of the browser will prevent our website from functioning properly and you may not be able to utilize fully all of the site's features and information.

FashionGo.net uses advertising companies to develop banner ads for our websites. These ads may contain cookies. We have no responsibility or control over cookies that may be utilized by these banner ads.
Opt-out Option
Visitors to our websites can decide whether or not they want to receive targeted promotional information. On the visitor registration pages, prospective visitors can choose to allow FashionGo.net to email them with advertising and promotional information. Should visitors accept promotional email from FashionGo.net, any email they receive will include instructions on how to be removed from FashionGo.net's promotional email list.
Notification of Changes
If we decide to change its privacy policy, those changes will be posted so that you are always aware of what information is collected, how it is used, and under what circumstances, if any, it is disclosed. If at any point we decide to use personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time it was collected, you will be notified of a change in this privacy policy.
Your Acceptance of these Terms
By using our website, products or services, you signify your acceptance of the terms of the FashionGo.net Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the terms of the FashionGo.net Privacy Policy, please do not use the website, products and/or services and exit the Site immediately.
