# FastStone Soft - Thank You

**Privacy Statement**

FastStone Soft is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell, trade, or rent any personal information to others.

**Web Site Visit** Our website is structured so that you can visit and download FastStone software without identifying yourself or revealing any personal information.  


**Online Order / Donation** When you place an order with us or donate online, we use third-party e-commerce partners (FastSpring and PayPal) for billing. We do not receive your credit card information, only the registration name/address that you provide and a transaction identifier. All payment details such as credit card number, expiration date etc stay with our billing partner and are never transmitted to us.

You can access our e-commerce partners' privacy statement online: 

[ https://fastspring.com/privacy/ ](https://fastspring.com/privacy/)

[https://www.paypal.com/webapps/mpp/ua/privacy-full](https://www.paypal.com/webapps/mpp/ua/privacy-full)

**FastStone software** FastStone programs contain neither adware nor spyware. FastStone programs do not collect or transmit any personal data or information.  

**Feedback and Support Email** If you choose to provide us personally identifiable information in your emails, you can be assured that this information will only be used to support your customer relationship with us.
