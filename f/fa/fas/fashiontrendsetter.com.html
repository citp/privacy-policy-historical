Privacy Policy

Fashion Trendsetter respects the privacy of our readers. Since we request personal information for some of our online features, this policy summarizes the types of information we will collect and how it will be used.


While you are free to visit our website, read articles, and use selected features without providing any personal information, we may collect information such as your name and e-mail address as you voluntarily sign up for our Newsletter.

Fashion Trendsetter may provide links or ads to other websites. Fashion Trendsetter allows other companies, called third-party ad servers or ad networks, such as Google AdSense, to serve advertisements on FashionTrendsetter.com. These third-party ad servers or ad networks may use technology to send, directly to your browser, the advertisements that appear within contents of FashionTrendsetter.com and they automatically receive your IP Address and other log file information when this happens. Such third-party ad servers may also use other technologies such as cookies, javascript, or web beacons to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and to personalize their advertising content. Certain third-party ad servers, such as Google, may also use these cookies to serve ads to you based on your visits on all sites on the Internet.

Fashion Trendsetter does not provide your Personal Information to these third-party ad servers or ad networks without your consent. However, please note that if an advertiser asks Fashion Trendsetter to show an advertisement to a certain audience or audience segment and you respond to that advertisement, the advertiser or ad-server may conclude that you fit the description of the audience that they were trying to reach. We will -never- sell your information to third parties without your permission.

We may provide general information and statistics, such as click-through rates and user trends to advertisers, but we will not share personal information.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers or ad networks. This Privacy Policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites. Fashion Trendsetter reserves the right to add or remove third-party ad networks or ad servers in its discretion and Fashion Trendsetter may not at all times list such updated ad network or ad server partners in this Privacy Policy. If you would like more information about these activities and to know your choices about not having this information used by these third parties, click the following link at: http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp. Additionally, if any Google AdSense advertisements are used by Fashion Trendsetter, you may opt out of Google's use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy located at http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html or to Opt -out Google's Ad Cookies please click the following link at: http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/index.aspx

To receive our e-mail Newsletters, you must voluntarily opt-in to our mailing list. We do not buy or sell lists. We maintain personal information and do not give that information out to those third parties. You may opt-out of our mailing list at any time by clicking here.

Please note that Fashion Trendsetter's privacy practices as described above do not apply to the privacy policies or practices of our affiliated programs.

Fashion Trendsetter reserves the right to modify this policy at any time. Thank you for your cooperation.
