# Privacy Policy - Nexus Mods Site Help

This Nexus Mods Privacy Policy applies to your use of [www.nexusmods.com](http://www.nexusmods.com/), and any other websites, applications, or services provided, owned, or operated by Black Tree Gaming Limited that link to this Privacy Policy. Nexus Mods values the privacy of users, subscribers, publishers, members, and others who visit and use the Nexus Mods Services (collectively or individually, “you” or “users”) and wants you to be familiar with how we collect, use, and disclose personal information from and about you.

For purposes of data protection laws, Black Tree Gaming Limited, located at 10 Southernhay West, Exeter, EX1 1JG, United Kingdom, is the “data controller” of your information; with respect to data collected by Black Tree Gaming Limited in connection with the Nexus Mods websites and/or services.

You may share personal information when using the Nexus Mods Services. One example is when you provide information about yourself as part of the Nexus Mods account creation process. Another is when you take certain actions on the Nexus Mods Services that are public or intended to be public in nature, such as when you upload mods, post on file comments, post profile information or endorse a mod. Given the social nature of some of the Nexus Mod Services, that information may be collected, used, or disclosed by others who are part of that social interaction. In addition, some features of the Nexus Mods Services are designed to provide others with information about user activity, such as identifying the user who uploaded a particular image or the whether a user has endorsed a specific mod. We encourage you to be mindful of this when considering your activity on the Nexus Mods Services.

By agreeing to this Privacy Policy in your Nexus Mods account setup, or by using the Nexus Mods Services, you expressly consent to the information handling practices described in this Policy.

##### Personal Information Nexus Mods Collects

We obtain information about you through the means discussed below when we provide the Nexus Mods Services. Please note that we need certain types of information so that we can provide the Nexus Mods Services to you. If you do not provide us with such information, or ask us to delete it, you may no longer be able to access or use the Nexus Mods Services.

  * User-provided Information: You may provide a variety of information about yourself to us, such as your name, gender, email address, postal mailing address, telephone number, credit card number, and billing information when you purchase Nexus Mods Premium Membership; upload, view, or download certain content or products from the Nexus Mods Services; enter contests or sweepstakes; or otherwise use the features and functionality of the Nexus Mods Services.
  * Automatically Collected Information: When you access the Nexus Mods Services we may automatically record and store certain information about your system by using cookies and other types of technologies. Cookies are small text files containing a string of alphanumeric characters that are sent to your browser. For further information visit [www.aboutcookies.org](http://www.aboutcookies.org/)  or [www.allaboutcookies.org](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/). You can set your browser not to accept cookies and the above websites tell you how to remove cookies from your browser. However in a few cases some of our website features may not function as a result. Examples of such information we automatically collect may include Internet Protocol address (“IP Address”), a unique user ID, device and browser types and identifiers, referring and exit page addresses, software and system type, and information about your usage of Nexus Mods Services. Examples of how Nexus Mods uses automatically collected information include to: (a) automatically update the Vortex application on your system, if you have it installed; (b) remember your information so that you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or the next time you access the Nexus Mods Services; (c) provide customized advertisements, content, and information; (d) monitor the effectiveness of marketing campaigns; (e) monitor and store aggregate site usage metrics such as total number of visitors and pages accessed; and (f) track your entries, submissions, and status in any promotions or other activities.
  * Information from Other Sources: Nexus Mods uses third parties in order to process your data and to help us in our statistical analysis on the site and help us make informed decisions about the number of users who make use of Nexus Mods Services. Nexus Mods does not buy in data from any third parties, or sell on your data to any third parties.

Nexus Mods uses such information to operate, maintain, enhance, provide, create, and develop all of the features, functionality, and services (new or existing) found on the Nexus Mods Services; improve your experience with the Nexus Mods Services by delivering content that we hope you will find relevant and interesting, including advertising and marketing messages; allow you to comment on content, and participate in contests, or rewards programs; and understand the usage trends of our users.

We use your email address to communicate with you, including to notify you of major Nexus Mods Services updates, for customer service purposes, or to contact you regarding any content that you have posted to or downloaded from the Nexus Mods Services.

Nexus Mods may periodically send promotional materials or notifications to you related to the Nexus Mods Services. If you want to stop receiving promotional materials, you can go to your account settings once you have logged in to the Nexus Mods Services or follow the unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of any email from us.

In certain cases, we have a legal obligation to collect and process your personal information (such as our obligation to share data with tax authorities).

Nexus Mods will rely on legal grounds to process your personal information to the extent permitted by applicable law, which may include, without limitation: to honour contractual commitments, to take steps in anticipation of entering into contract, to fulfil legal obligations, your consent, and Nexus Mods legitimate interests.

You may decline to share certain information with Nexus Mods, in which case Nexus Mods may not be able to provide to you some of the features and functionality found on the Nexus Mods Services. If you have created a Nexus Mods Services account, then, depending on the service, you may be able to update your profile information and preferences or disable your account by logging in and going to the settings page. For other requests to review, update, delete, or otherwise limit Nexus Mods’s use of information that you have provided directly to Nexus Mods, you may contact support@nexusmods.com. In your request, please include your email address, name, address and specify all relevant background. To protect your privacy and security, we may take steps to verify your identity before granting you access or making corrections to your information. You are responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your unique password and account information at all times.

Residents of the European Economic Area (“EEA”), can exercise certain data subject rights available to them under applicable data protection laws. We will comply with requests to exercise these rights in accordance with applicable law. Please note that in some circumstances, we may need to keep processing your information for certain legitimate interests or to comply with a legal obligation. If these rights apply to you, they may permit you to request that we:

  * Obtain access to or a copy of certain personal information we hold about you
  * Prevent the processing of your personal information for direct marketing purposes (including any direct marketing processing based on profiling)
  * Update personal information that is out of date or incorrect
  * Delete certain personal information we hold about you
  * Restrict the way that we process and disclose specific personal information about you
  * Transfer your personal information to a third-party provider of services
  * Revoke consent that you previously provided for the processing of your personal information



If applicable, you may make a complaint to the data protection supervisory authority in the country where you are based. Alternatively, you may seek a remedy through local courts if you believe your rights have been breached.

##### Children’s Privacy

IF YOU ARE UNDER 13 YEARS OF AGE, THEN PLEASE DO NOT USE OR ACCESS THE NEXUS MODS SERVICES AT ANY TIME OR IN ANY MANNER.

Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important. For that reason, Nexus Mods does not knowingly collect or maintain personal information from persons under 13 years-of-age. If Nexus Mods learns that personal information of persons under 13-years-of-age has been collected on or through the Nexus Mods Services, Nexus Mods will take appropriate steps to delete this information.

If you are the parent or legal guardian of a child under 13 who has become a Nexus Mods Services member, then please contact Nexus Mods at support@nexusmods.com to have that child’s account terminated and personal information deleted.

For residents of the EEA, where processing of personal information is based on consent, Nexus Mods will not knowingly engage in that processing for users under the age of consent established by applicable data protection law. If we learn that we are engaged in that processing with such users, we will halt such processing and will take reasonable measures to promptly remove applicable information from our records.
