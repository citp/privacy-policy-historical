Privacy Policy

FamilyFriendlySites.com is committed to safeguarding your privacy online because we recognize the importance of protecting information from our customers.

When registering for our products or services online, you create your own password, and we ask you for personal information. The personal information that we collect when our members register is for internal use only, and we maintain appropriate security measures to keep this information private. Your email address will never be shared with any outside organizations or individuals.

FamilyFriendlySites.com uses cookies to determine user preferences and to help track unique visitors. However, users can choose not to enable cookies by accessing their browser's cookie settings and then turning off the option to receive cookies.

Any changes in our privacy practices will be posted clearly on our site. We are confident that your experiences at FamilyFriendlySites.com will be a success.

If you have any concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us by email at: Privacy@Family FriendlySites.com
