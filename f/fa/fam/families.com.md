# Privacy Policy - Families.com

## Your personal information is safe

While we do require you to provide us with some basic information in order to enhance your experience on the site, we will never share your information with third parties. We won’t sell your email address to marketers or provide any personal information to anyone. We do track our visitor’s traffic patterns in aggregate in order to enhance our product, but we do not retain any records of activity from a specific user beyond what they contribute to the community.

We store a minimal amount of information on your computer in order to aid with statistical tracking across the site. This information is used to tell us how many people visit the site, and technical details such as what functionality their web browser is capable of. We must collect this data so that we can be sure that we’re providing the best possible user experience to the widest variety of visitors.

## Third party Advertising

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you.

## Third Party Cookies

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique “cookie” on your browser.
