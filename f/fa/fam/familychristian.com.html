Privacy Policy



Our commitment to You, Our Customer

FamilyChristian.com respects and wants to protect the privacy of our users. We do not sell, rent, or loan any identifiable information regarding our customers to any third party. Any personal information you give us will not be given to other third parties without your consent.


Use of IP Addresses

An IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you're surfing the Web. Web servers identify your computer by its IP address. FamilyChristian.com uses your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our website. Your IP address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart and to gather broad demographic information.


Use of Cookies

Cookies are files that your Web browser places on your computer's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. FamilyChristian.com creates and then uses these cookie files to track your clicks as you go through the FamilyChristian.com website. The use of cookies is an industry standard and you will find them at most major websites.

The information we retrieve from the cookie files helps us track user trends and patterns. We use the information from cookies to provide services that better fit our users' needs. FamilyChristian.com also uses cookies to deliver content specific to your interests.

You may occasionally get cookies from our advertisers. FamilyChristian.com does not control these cookies. The use of advertising cookies sent by third-party servers is standard in the Internet industry.


Use of Information

FamilyChristian.com will not collect any personal information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by the individual. Examples of personal information include, name, e-mail address, mailing address and phone number. FamilyChristian.com may share non-personal aggregate or summary information regarding its customers with partners or other third parties, but we do not sell, rent, or loan any customer level identifiable information to anyone, except that FamilyChristian.com may be required to disclose personal information by applicable law or in connection with the enforcement of FamilyChristian.com contract or property rights, and except that information collected by FamilyChristian.com may be transferred in connection with the sale or merger of the company or any division or business line of the company to which the information relates. If you make a purchase through FamilyChristian.com, your credit card information will be provided to our credit card processor.

Unique identifiers (such as passwords) are collected to verify the user's identity, for use as account numbers in our record system, and for other purposes. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site.
