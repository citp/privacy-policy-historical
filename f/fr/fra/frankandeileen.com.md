# Shop Frank & Eileen | Privacy Policy | Woven in Italy Made in Sunny California

Frank & Eileen is committed to your privacy. Frank & Eileen does not trade, rent, or sell personal information to any third parties. We only share specific information with third parties that provide services on our behalf or with whom we have partnered to offer a particular product or service. The personally identifiable information that you provide to us and information about your order may be combined with other personally identifiable information from our records and other sources. This information will be used to make our future marketing efforts more efficient.

_Last updated on 2/10/15_

Signing up for our mailing list enables you to receive promotional emails about Frank & Eileen new releases, promotions and events. We do not share your email address with any other third party. At anytime you may choose to stop receiving promotional emails.
