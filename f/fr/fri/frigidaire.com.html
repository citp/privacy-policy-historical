Your Privacy is Important to Us!
You do not have to provide personal information to gain access to or browse our web site. However, Frigidaire or one of our affiliate companies may use personal information collected for internal marketing purposes such as market research. It may also be used to contact you about the latest product information or special promotions.

If you have provided personal information, but do not wish to receive further information from Frigidaire, you can simply respond to our e-mail and tell us that you do not want to receive future communications.

If you receive solicitations for products or services of no interest to you, promptly notify the solicitor that you do not want to receive any further unwanted solicitations. Remove your name from many national mailing and telephone solicitation lists by contacting the Direct Marketing Association (DMA) and asking them to add your name to their "Do Not Solicit" files, or by writing to Direct Marketing Association, Mail Preference Service, P. O. Box 9008, Farmington, NY 11735-9008.
Cookies
Frigidaire uses "cookie" technology to obtain non-personal information from online visitors. A "cookie" is a small text file that is sent to your web browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. Frigidaire uses "cookies" as a means of compiling information detailing the usage of our site. These findings help us understand our customer's needs so that we may improve your browsing/shopping experience at Frigidaire. We may use "cookies" to:
  1. Estimate the number of users of our site
  2. Quantify general usage of our site
  3. Track aggregate traffic patterns
  4. Assist in the personalization and general improvement of your experience at Frigidaire.
Personal information cannot be extracted by simply placing a "cookie" on an individual's computer. If you have submitted personal information to Frigidaire, then the aggregated data may be combined with some personal information for internal marketing and reporting purposes.

Even though most Web browsers accept "cookies", most browsers also give you the option to reject "cookies". Please note that if you set your browser to reject "cookies" you will still be able to visit Frigidaire's web site but your shopping and browsing experience may be somewhat affected.
