**Your Privacy is Important to Us!**

For your peace of mind, we do not sell or rent our customer list to another entity. However, Frigidaire® or one of our affiliate companies may use personal information collected for internal marketing purposes such as market research. It may also be used to contact you about the latest product information or special promotions.

If you have provided personal information, but do not wish to receive further information from Frigidaire®, you can simply respond to our e-mail and tell us that you do not want to receive future communications.

If you receive solicitations for products or services of no interest to you, promptly notify the solicitor that you do not want to receive any further unwanted solicitations. Remove your name from many national mailing and telephone solicitation lists by contacting the Direct Marketing Association (DMA) and asking them to add your name to their "Do Not Solicit" files, or by writing to Direct Marketing Association, Mail Preference Service, P. O. Box 9008, Farmington, NY 11735-9008.

**Cookies**

Frigidaire® uses "cookie" technology to obtain non-personal information from online visitors. A "cookie" is a small text file that is sent to your web browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. Frigidaire® uses "cookies" as a means of compiling information detailing the usage of our site. These findings help us understand our customer's needs so that we may improve your browsing/shopping experience at Frigidaire®.

We may use cookies to provide you with more relevant advertising on our site and on third party sites.  To learn more about the use of cookies to deliver more relevant advertising and to know your choices about not having this information used by certain service providers, please [click here](http://www.aboutads.info/choices/ "click here").

Even though most Web browsers accept "cookies," most browsers also give you the option to reject "cookies." Please note that if you set your browser to reject "cookies" you will still be able to visit Frigidaire's website but your shopping and browsing experience may be somewhat affected.

**Security**

We have chosen our e-commerce partners carefully in an effort to make your credit card transactions safe. All transfers of credit card information via this site use Secure Socket Layers (SSL) encryption.
