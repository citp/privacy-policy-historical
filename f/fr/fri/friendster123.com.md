# Free High Quality Friendster Layouts - Friendster123.com » Privacy Policy

Friendster123.com makes every effort to protect your privacy and identity. Friendster123.com does not actively collect information about it’s users. We do not ask for your email address, name, or any other identifying information. Unless you are subscribing in our site, we need your e-mail addresses. We use FeedBurner as a service for the ones of our members for subscription on this site. But we will never distribute or we will still keep privacy on your e-mail address. We use multiples of advertising agents such as Google AdSense, AdBrite, BidVertiser and personal contacts with our site. And most of our advertising agents use cookies in your computer with your IPs logged. This is because to track all users for any illegal actions done with their program. Friendster123.com may contain links or advertisers’ banners to other sites, and we are NOT responsible for the privacy practices or the contents linked from these sites. 

  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site. 
  * Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet. 
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
