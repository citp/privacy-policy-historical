Privacy

Thomas Brunt's OutFront has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate OutFront's firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web sites: Thomas Brunt's OutFront, The OutFront Forums, and frontpagecommerce.com.

  1. Use of Cookies
    Our message board system relies on "perishable" cookies to help users to see what threads have new posts since the their last visit to the OutFront Forums.  These cookies are not permanent and will expire 1 year.  Although they are not permanent, your browser's security settings must be set to allow cookies for you to place orders at our site to its full potential.
     
  2. Statistical Data
    Our servers (as most) track IP addresses and referring pages to help with site maintenance and improvements.  This data is viewed only as anonymous statistics to show the busiest times of the day or week, pages with errors and how effective our advertising has been.  This information is not used for any other purpose.
     
  3. Personal Information collected - Mailing List
    You have two opportunities to be included on our free mailing list.  This list is used to notify of product updates, Web site changes or news of interest to FrontPage webmasters.  As with all information collected at OutFront, it is for our use only.  To be included on this list you can sign up directly on our home page.
     
  4. Personal Information Collected - Order Forms
    With the exception of credit card info, we store the information from your order form to allow us to verify sales to suppliers, to track consulting issues, or refer to a previous order to help provide some customer service.  You may elect to have your information completely removed from this system by e-mailing us at thomas@thomasbrunt.com with your request. 
     
  5. Outside Links
    This site may on occasion provide links to other sites for your convenience or additional information.  Thomas Brunt's OutFront.net is not responsible for any content or practices of these sites.
     
  6. Information Correction or Removal
    If you wish to correct, update or remove any information about you that may be in our records, please send us e-mail thomas@thomasbrunt.com with the details of your request at.  If you wish to contact us further, please find complete contact information on our contact page.
