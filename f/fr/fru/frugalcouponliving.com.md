# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to Frugal Coupon Living, LLC.

This privacy statement sets forth the privacy practices with regard to the information collected from your computer in interaction with Frugal Coupon Living.

Frugal Coupon Living uses cookies to gather information on one’s user activity to measure the best advertisement effectiveness. Frugal Coupon Living has no control or access to these cookies which are used by other third party advertisers. Third party advertisers on Frugal Coupon Living include but are not limited to Glam, [Savings.com](http://www.savings.com/), Google Adsense and [Coupons.com](http://www.frugalcouponliving.com/Coupons1). As Frugal Coupon Living does not have direct control over the policies of these companies, they are not responsible for the privacy practices or contents of those sites. Frugal Coupon Living selects third party affiliates based on integrity, reputation, and research. Frugal Coupon Living encourages and recommends you review the privacy policy of these merchants. You may read more about [Google’s Privacy Policy ](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)and option out if you desire.

Any information you voluntary provide (name, email address, mailing address, phone number) will be used for correspondence from Frugal coupon Living to you only. In the circumstance of a giveaway, the information will be shared with the company supplying you with the winning item. When visiting Frugal Coupon Living, an IP address is collected to gather use, times of access and dates. In addition, tracking of pages, page views, downloads, browser use, clicks, and regional information will be noted so that Frugal Coupon Living can better serve you. IP addresses do not share personal identifiable information. This information is used to for analytical purposes only.

Frugal Coupon Living website does contain affiliate links. In the case of directing you to other sites, my opinion will be shared. This is not to used or substituted as your own. Links are for your reference. Once you are on a third parties site, you are responsible for the privacy policies of this website. Web beacons are placed on Frugal Coupon Living as a service provider to traffic direction andpreferenceto help our third party companies determine advertisement placement and location.

This Privacy Policy discloses the online privacy practices for Frugal Coupon Living, LLC. and its affiliates including Frugal Coupon Living site (collectively, “Frugal Coupon Living”) and applies to the information we collect through www.frugalcouponliving.com (the “Site”) and incorporates the Site’s Terms of Use. By accessing and/or using the Site, you are consenting to the collection, storage, and disclosure of data as described in this Privacy Policy.

Frugal Coupon Living reserves the right, at any time, to modify, alter, update, or remove portions of this Privacy Policy. We will post any changes to this Policy on this page so you can readily review the most current version. We will alert you that changes have been made by indicating on the bottom of this Privacy Policy the date it was last updated.

**Your IP Address**. An Internet Protocol (IP) address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer every time you browse the Internet. When a user requests a page from the Site, our servers log the user’s current IP address. We use IP addresses to help diagnose problems with our servers and to administer the Site.

**Cookies**. The Site is developed using Active Server Pages (ASP), and we use per-session “cookies” (pieces of data your browser stores in your computer’s memory at [Passion for Savings](http://www.frugalcouponliving.com/PassionforSavings)’ request) to establish user sessions on our server. We use the cookies to carry information about usage from page to page through the Site. [Passion for Savings](http://www.frugalcouponliving.com/PassionforSavings) does not use cookies to retrieve personal information about you from your computer.

**Acceptance of Cookies**. Most browsers are set up to accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser settings to refuse them. If you disable cookies, your browser may experience some erratic behavior on some portions of the Site. Frugal Coupon Living does disclose your cookie to third parties including Affiliates, advertisers, or marketing firms.

**Disclosures to Third-Parties**. Frugal Coupon Living will not disclose your Submission Information to third parties, including, without limitation, Affiliates, advertisers, or marketing firms unless it is for the sole reason of disclosing Winner Email or physical address for shipment of a Prize.

**Links**. This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected by Frugal Coupon Living. Other Web sites (including those that we may link to) may have their own policies. Frugal Coupon Living has no responsibility for or control over the information-collection practices of such third-party Web sites.
