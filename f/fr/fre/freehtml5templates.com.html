Collection of personal information

This information is purely used to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement and gather broad demographic information for internal use. Changes to this privacy policy

The contents of this Privacy Policy may be altered at any time, at our discretion. If you have any questions regarding the privacy policy of Free HTML5 Templates, you may contact us.
