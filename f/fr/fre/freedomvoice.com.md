Any information collected on this website, including, but not limited to, names, addresses, phone numbers, and email addresses, are for the sole purpose of creating a business relationship between interested parties and FreedomVoice. No information collected on this website will be sold, shared, or otherwise transferred to 3rd parties. Information is stored on secure databases and is not publicly available.
