**Terms of Use/ Consent**

By using this Site and/or volunteering information you are giving your express consent to the Site's privacy policy as outlined in this document. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site 

**Personal Information**

Personal information may include such information as your name, email address, mailing addresses, financial information, birthdates and information collected electronically via cookies. 

**What we collect**

The Site collects personal information when you register for The Site, when you visit The Site's pages, when you contact the Site, or any of its employees, agents or representatives, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. 

When you register we ask you for personal information such as your name, address, email address, telephone number, mobile phone number, and credit card number. Once you register and log in to the member area, you are not anonymous to us. 

The Site automatically collects and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your [IP Address](http://www.webopedia.com/TERM/I/IP_address.html), [Cookie](http://www.webopedia.com/TERM/c/cookie.html) information, and the page(s) you request. 

Uses of Personal Information - Why We collect it? 

The Site uses personal information for the following general purposes: 

  1. to customize the advertising and content you see; 
  2. fulfill your requests for products and services; 
  3. improve our services; 
  4. contact you; 
  5. provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients. 

By providing your personal information to this Site, you are expressly consenting to the use or disclosure of your personal information by the Site for the purposes stated above and in the manner that it sees fit. 

This site may share your personal information including your IP Address, your e-mail address with its affiliates and third parties. 

From time to time we will present offers to you and if accepted, we will use the financial information we have on file to complete your request. 

The Site may also share information when we believe it is necessary in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of The Site's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law. 

The Site may transfer information about you if The Site is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, if this transfer subjects you to a different privacy policy, The Site will notify you before information about you is transferred.
