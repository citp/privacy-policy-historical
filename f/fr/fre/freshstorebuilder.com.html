Privacy Policy

Fresh Store Builder are committed to protecting your privacy, we use the information we collect for your account and personalised experience. We will only use the information we collect about you lawfully (in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998). Please read on if you wish to learn more about our privacy policy.

What information do we collect?

When you create an account we need to know your name, e-mail address and websites that will use Fresh Store Builder. This allows us to grant you access to private materials, forums and verify your license. Payment details are processed by Paypal and this information is governed securely by their own Privacy Policy.

How do we protect your information?

The personal information which we hold will be held securely in accordance with our internal security policy.

Do we use cookies?

Yes, we use cookies to enable a personalised experience and access to your private area. Cookies are small pieces of information stored locally on your computer by your browser. We use cookies to enable you to be recognised as a license holder. We do not store any personally identifying information in these cookies.

Will we sell your information?

Fresh Store Builder does not sell any information about their customers, it's as simple as that. We will not forward your details on to any third party at any time.
