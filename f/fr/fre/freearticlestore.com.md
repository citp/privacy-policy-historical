# Privacy-Policy - FreeArticleStore.com

### 1\. Information we collect

We collect two types of information:

  * a. Non-Personal information
  * b. Personal information



#### a. Non-Personal information

Non-Personal information is collected when you browse the site anonymously. It may include browser information, clicks on the site or Operating System.

#### b. Personal information.

we collect Personal information from you when you register on our site. When registering on our site, you may be asked to enter your name, e-mail address or password.

### 2\. Purpose of collecting the information

Any of the information we collect may be used for

  * a. To personalize your experience
  * b. To administer a contest, promotion, survey or other site feature
  * c. To send informative emails
  * d. To provide certain services to registered users



The email address you provide may be used to send you information or response to inquiries.

### 3\. Security of information

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit or access your personal information like your email address is not visible to any website user.

### 4\. Cookies

When you visit the site, we may use Cookies to store certain information on your computer

### 5\. Disclose personal information to third parties

We do not sell or transfer to third parties your personal information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may share your information when we believe it is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies or protect ours or others rights or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising or other uses.

### 6\. Third party site links

Sometimes, we may include links or offer third party products or services on our website. These third party sites may have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability whatsoever with regard to privacy matters or any other legal matter with respect to such sites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites. So, we advise you to carefully read the privacy policies of these sites.

### 7\. Online Privacy Policy

This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through our website and not to information collected offline.

### 8\. Terms of use

Please also read our Terms and conditions on [http://www.freearticlestore.com/terms](http://www.freearticlestore.com/terms.aspx).

### 9\. Change of policy

We reserve the right to change privacy policy at any time. So, Please read privacy policy before use of this site.

### 10\. Contact us

If you have any queries, you can contact us on [http://www.freearticlestore.com/contactus](http://www.freearticlestore.com/contactus.aspx).
