# Privacy Policy

1\. INTRODUCTION

By using the Freedocast our website, the User consents to the collection, use, and storage of User information by Freedocast in the manner described in this Privacy Policy and elsewhere on the Freedocast sites. This Privacy Policy applies only to information Freedocast and the Services collects about the User and does not apply to information about the User collected by Freedocast’s affiliated providers or third party websites and offerings linked to or otherwise accessible from the Freedocast website or the Services. The information collected or received by Freedocast’s affiliated providers and/ or third parties is subject to their own privacy policies. 

This Privacy Policy details important information regarding the use and disclosure of User information collected on the Freedocast sites to enable the User to make an informed decision about whether to use or continue using the Freedocast sites and the Services. It is clarified that any video, image, or other User Content posted at the direction of the User onto the Freedocast site and the Services becomes published content and shall not be considered personally identifiable information subject to this Privacy Policy. 

2\. COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

The information gathered by Freedocast may either be information that the User voluntarily supplies to Freedocast when the User registers or initiates transactions with the Services and/ or information gathered on usage patterns and preferences as the User navigates through Freedocast’s sites and/ or uses the Services. An agent or affiliate of Freedocast may, at times and on being so authorized, collect information on behalf of Freedocast. Third party providers featured on or linked to by the Freedocast sites, such as vendors of products and services offered through the sites, may be gathering the same kinds of information.

User provided Information. To make use of certain features on the Freedocast sites and to use the Services and when choosing to participate in various activities on the Freedocast sites such as uploading videos, posting messages, entering contests or sweepstakes, taking advantage of promotions, responding to surveys, or subscribing to newsletters or other mailing lists, the User may be required to register and/ or provide certain personally identifiable information as part of the participation/ registration process. Freedocast may also ask for information from Users who buy products or services or conduct other transactions on its sites. 

Any personal information or video content that the User voluntarily discloses online (on discussion boards, in messages and chat areas, etc.) becomes publicly available and can be collected and used by others. The User’s account name (not the email address) is displayed to other Users when any videos are uploaded or messages are sent through the Services and other Users can contact the User through messages and comments. Any videos that the User submits to the Freedocast sites may be redistributed through the internet and other media channels, and may be viewed by the general public. 

**Cookies:**

 To enable Freedocast to make its sites more responsive to the needs of the Users, Freedocast may use a standard feature of browser software called a "cookie," to assign each visitor a unique, random number, a type of user ID that may reside on the User’s computer. The cookie doesn't actually identify the User but identifies only the computer that is used to access the site. Advertisers and other third parties that provide products or services through the Freedocast site may also assign their own cookies to the User’s browser and such process is beyond Freedocast’s control. Freedocast may use both session cookies (which are temporary and disappear after the User closes the browser) and persistent cookies (which remain after the User closes the browser and which may be used by the User’s browser on subsequent visits to the site). The User may reset the web browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some features of the Freedocast sites may not function properly if the ability to accept cookies is disabled. 

**Other Methods.**

The Freedocast web pages may contain electronic.images (called "single-pixel GIFs" or "web beacons") that allow Freedocast and its affiliates to count users who have visited particular pages or to access certain cookies. These tools and other technologies to may be used to recognize which pages users visit, and which links they click on. These features may also be included in email newsletters so that Freedocast may learn which messages have been opened and acted upon. These tools may also enable Freedocast to verify online purchases by the User from the Freedocast sites or from third party sites that have agreed to share such information with Freedocast. Browser Level Information and IP Addresses. The Freedocast web servers automatically collect limited information about the User’s computer configuration, including browser software, the operating system, the website that referred the User, and the User’s Internet Protocol (“IP”) address browser language, platform type, number of clicks, domain names, landing pages, pages viewed, the order of those pages, and the amount of time spent on particular pages, the date and time of the User’s request, and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify the User’s browser. 

**Third Party.**

Freedocast may permit third-party ad servers or ad networks, to display advertisements within the Freedocast sites. Such third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to send, directly to the User’s your browser, the advertisements and links that appear on the Freedocast sites and automatically receive the User’s IP address when this happens. The third party may also use other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and to personalize the advertising content the User sees. The User should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers or ad networks for more information on their practices and for instructions on how to opt-out of certain practices. Freedocast’s privacy policy does not apply to, and Freedocast cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.

3\. STORAGE OF INFORMATION

Any information provided by the User to Freedocast shall be stored and held under generally accepted standards of technology and operational security in order to protect the User’s personally identifiable information from loss, misuse, alteration, or destruction. Only authorized personnel are permitted access to the User information and these personnel are required to treat this information as confidential. Despite these precautions, Freedocast cannot guarantee that any and all unauthorized access to the User’s information will be prevented. All classifieds, User Content, postings, etc. are stored in the Freedocast database, even after "deletion," and may be archived elsewhere and the web logs and other records are stored indefinitely. Search engines and other sites not affiliated with Freedocast - including archive.org, google.com, etc. – may archive or otherwise make available Freedocast postings.

4\. USAGE OF INFORMATION

Freedocast uses IP address information for systems administration and trouble shooting purposes. This information is also used to deliver web pages to the User upon request; to tailor the site to the interests of the users and to measure traffic within the Freedocast site. Any personally identifiable information provided by the User is used to operate, maintain, and provide the User with the features and functionality of the Freedocast sites. Freedocast does not use the User’s email address or other personally identifiable information to send commercial or marketing messages without the User’s consent except as part of a program or feature for which the User may exercise an option to not receive such messages. Freedocast may, however, use the User’s email address without further consent for non-marketing or administrative purposes. Freedocast may use both personally identifiable information and certain non-personally-identifiable information to improve the quality and design of the Services and to create new features, promotions, functionality, and services by storing, tracking, and analyzing User preferences and trends. Freedocast uses cookies, clear gifs, and log file information to: remember information so that the User is not required to re-enter it during subsequent visits; provide custom and personalized content and information; monitor the effectiveness of marketing campaigns; monitor logs such as total number of visitors, pages viewed, etc.; and to track entries, submissions, and status in promotions, sweepstakes, and contests of the User. 

5\. DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION

**Affiliates.** Freedocast may share certain types of information such as account information, diagnostic information, transaction information, and usage information with subsidiaries, affiliated companies, or other businesses or persons for the purpose of processing such information on its behalf. Freedocast requires such affiliates to process such information in compliance with this Privacy Policy and shall undertake all reasonable efforts to limit their use of such information and to use other appropriate confidentiality and security measures. 

**Third Parties**. Freedocast does not share the User’s personally identifiable information with third-party companies or organizations for their commercial or marketing use without the User’s express consent except as part of a specific program or feature for which the User has the ability to opt-in or opt-out. Freedocast may share non-personally-identifiable information with interested third-parties to assist them in understanding the usage patterns for certain content, services, advertisements, promotions, and/or functionality on the Freedocast sites and the Services. Freedocast may use the information to present offers to the User on behalf of business partners and advertisers. These business partners and advertisers may receive aggregate data about groups of Freedocast but do not receive information that personally identifies the User Transactions/Requests with Third Parties.

The User may choose to use the Services to purchase goods/services from third party providers. Freedocast may share necessary information with third parties to fulfill a transaction the User has requested. Freedocast may share information where the User has consented to the sharing of such information, or except as described in this Privacy Policy. Legal Obligations/ Fraud. Freedocast may be required to may disclose such records as it believes in good faith to be appropriate or necessary to protect its legitimate business interests, safeguard others, or respond to legal process like subpoenas, court orders, or other legal process, or to establish or exercise its legal rights or defend against legal claims. The information may also be required to be disclosed in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of TOU, or as otherwise required or permitted by law. 

**Statistical Information**. 

Much of the information Freedocast collects is in the form of aggregated statistics, such as the traffic that visits various pages within the Services, the habits and preferences of the audience, etc. Such aggregated information does not include any personally identifiable information and Freedocast may use such aggregated information and/ or disclose it to third parties as it deems fit. 

6\. FINANCIAL INFORMATION

Freedocast does not store credit card information and other financial data. Credit card transactions are transmitted to a financial gateway, and Freedocast endeavors to protect the security of the User’s payment information during transmission by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology. Freedocast will not disclose to third parties the User’s your credit card information or other financial data except to the extent necessary to complete the transaction initiated by the User.

For e-commerce transactions, Freedocast may transmit the User’s billing information using encryption which scrambles the credit card number and personal information. However, since no security system is impenetrable, Freedocast cannot guarantee the security of its databases, nor can it guarantee that information supplied by the User won't be intercepted while being transmitted to us over the Internet.

7\. SECURITY OF INFORMATION

Freedocast has established safeguards to help prevent unauthorized access to or misuse of User information, but cannot guarantee that such information will never be disclosed in a manner inconsistent with this Privacy Policy. To protect the User’s privacy and security, Freedocast may use passwords to help verify the User’s identity before granting access or making corrections to any User information. 

Freedocast uses commercially reasonable physical, managerial, and technical safeguards to preserve the integrity and security of User information. Freedocast, however, ensure or warrant the security of any information transmitted to Freedocast by the User and is transmitted at the User’s own risk. Once Freedocast receives the transmission of information, Freedocast makes commercially reasonable efforts to ensure the security of its systems. However, Freedocast cannot a guarantee that such information may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed by breach of any physical, technical, or managerial safeguards. 

If Freedocast becomes aware of a security systems breach, then Freedocast may attempt to notify the User electronically so that the User can take appropriate protective steps or may post a notice on the Freedocast sites. Depending on where the User resides, the User may have a legal right to receive notice of a security breach in writing. To receive a free written notice of a security breach (or to withdraw consent from receiving electronic notice) the User should notify Freedocast using the contact form. 

8\. USER CHOICES 

The User may decline to submit personally identifiable information through the Freedocast sites, in which case Freedocast may not be able to provide certain services. If the User opts to register with and provide information to Freedocast, the User will have the opportunity to unsubscribe, review, update, or delete the information provided at any time. If the User opts to remove registration information completely, the User may not be able to continue using certain services that require registration. To protect the User’s privacy and security, Freedocast takes reasonable steps (such as requesting a unique password) to verify the User’s identity before granting profile access or making corrections. The User is responsible for maintaining the secrecy of the unique password and account information at all times. 

It is clarified that media files uploaded by Users to the Freedocast sites and the Services cannot be removed and remain subject to the TOU. The User may contact Freedocast with any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, personal information, User consent, or opt-in or opt-out choices using the contact form.

9\. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

Freedocast’s websites are not intentionally designed for or directed at persons less than 13 years of age. Freedocast does not knowingly permit any person who is under 13 years of age to register with the Services or to provide any other personally identifying information. Any person under 13 years of age visiting the Freedocast sites is prohibited from accessing or using the Services in any manner and at any time. If Freedocast becomes aware that any personally identifiable information of persons less than 13 years of age has been collected on the Freedocast’s sites without verified parental consent, then Freedocast will take the appropriate steps to delete any such information.

10\. INTERNATIONAL USERS

The Freedocast websites may be viewed and hosted anywhere in the world, including countries that may not have specific laws regulating the collection, use and transfer of personally identifiable data. By using the Freedocast sites and the Services and submitting such information on it, the User voluntarily consents to such transborder transfers and hosting of such information. By visiting the Freedocast websites and providing information, the User acknowledges and agrees that due to the international dimension of Freedocast, it may use the information collected for the purposes identified in this policy or in our other communications with the User, including the transmission of information to outside the User’s resident jurisdiction. A User accessing the Freedocast sites from any location outside the United States with laws or regulations governing personal data collection, use and disclosure that differ from United States laws, such user is advised that through continued use of the Freedocast sites, which are governed by U.S. law, this Privacy Policy and the TOU, the User is consenting to the transfer of personal information to the United States.

In the event of bankruptcy, insolvency, reorganization, receivership, or assignment for the benefit of creditors, or the application of laws or equitable principles affecting creditors' rights generally, Freedocast may not be able to control how the your personal information is treated, transferred, or used.
