# freeshipping.com - Privacy Policy

  
**Privacy Policy**  


freeshipping.com is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers. Information we collect is used solely to process orders and enhance your personal shopping experience. Please read below for more detailed information about our privacy policy. 

**How we use information:**

How we use information freeshipping.com does not rent, trade or sell your personal information to any third party. 

Our site uses forms so that you can request information, products, and services. Contact information (e-mail address, telephone number, etc.) from the forms are used to send your orders and information about our company to you. Your contact information is also used to get in touch with you when necessary. 

You may, from time to time, receive information from us about new features, new services, and special offers we think you'll find valuable. You may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the section below. 

Demographic and profile data is collected at our site. We may use this data to tailor content to your needs and display the content according to your preferences. 

We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address can also be used to gather broad demographic information and to recognize customer traffic patterns and site usage. This information aids us in merchandising, and in developing the design and layout of the store. 

**Contests and Surveys:**

From time to time, we may run contests on our site in which we ask you for contact information and demographic information. We use contact data from our contests to send you information about our company. We use the demographic data in aggregate to better understand the needs of all of our customers. 

freeshipping.com sometimes administers surveys from the site in which we ask you for demographic information and opinions. We do not ask you for contact information in regards to survey data. We use the data to better understand the needs of all of our customers. We will never contact you regarding the answers or results provided in a survey. 

**Choice/Opt-Out:**

If you do not wish to receive announcements or special offers from us you can: 

Send an e-mail with "Remove" in the subject line to [support@freeshipping.com](mailto:support@freeshipping.com)

**Your Agreement:**

By using our Web site, you agree to the collection and use of this information. We will post any changes to our Privacy Policy on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect and how we use it.
