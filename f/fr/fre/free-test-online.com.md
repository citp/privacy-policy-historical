# Email Privacy Policy

This email privacy policy is intended to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy and the protection of your personal information. 

How we protect your privacy

We use security measures to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of data used by our system.Â  Our data is not accessible to any other party.

Sharing and usage

We will never share, sell, or rent individual personal information with anyone unless explicitly instructed by a court of law. Information submitted to us is only available to employees managing this information for purposes of contacting you or sending you emails based on your request for free math practice.  
We will not send more than 2 emails monthly with free math practice.

How can you stop receiving email from us?

Each email sent includes information about the mechanism for removal. Simply follow the instructions at the end of any email.
