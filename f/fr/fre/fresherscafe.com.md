By using Fresherscafe.com you agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions:

**The information collected by our services**

We may collect our website visitor's system information such as IP Address, Location, Operating System or Browser Type. This is a partial list, however all this collected information is used only to know about user preferences and to make improvements to our service. fresherscafe.com does not sell or share this information to any other websites, third-parties or agents either directly or indirectly. 

When you register with our website, we may collect the users personal information such as name, address, contact number, email address. However most of the details are optional. fresherscafe.com does not sell or share this information to any other websites, third- parties or agents either directly or indirectly.

**No Spam activities**  
As part of our services, Fresherscafe.com sends daily job emails and SMS to our users registered for our services. However the users can change their email preferences or unsubscribe at any time. Every mail sent by us includes the 'Un-Subscribe' link at the end of the job mails sent by us. Fresherscafe.com does not involve in transmission of any 'junk mails', 'chain emails' or 'spamming'. 

**Cookies and Web beacons**  
We may access and set cookies using Web beacons, also known as single-pixel GIFs, which are invisible graphical images. These Web beacons tell us useful information regarding our site such as which pages users access. However we use this information only for the purpose of improving our services.
