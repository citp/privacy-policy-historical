# FreeTrafficWorld.com

**FreeTrafficWorld.com** is deeply committed to your right to privacy and takes your privacy seriously. We have very strict privacy policies and we strive to keep your personal and financial information secure.

###  **Information Collected:**

FreeTrafficWorld.com collects personally identifiable information you provide in order for you to access and use our customized services. This information may include your name, contact information and billing information, behavior patterns, purchase history, Web site information (Name, URL, visitors per day/month, description, categorization),Ad Campaign information and user comments.

###  **Use of Information:**

We will never use your e-mail address to send you unwanted advertisements, & We will NEVER sell or rent your name, email address or personal information to any third party. 

### **Downline Emails**

When you signup as a member of this website you agree to recieve promotional emails from your upline. You can choose to open these and read them as you see fit or you can delete them. Each email will contain a credit link, that when clicked on, will allow you to earn email credits good for sending an email to your downline. Referral tiers are set up 3 levels deep so you will never recieve more than 6 emails a week from our system!

###  **Data Security:**

This site has security measures in place to prevent unauthorized access, to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Any member found trying to access this information will be prosecuted to the full extent of the law and immediately banned from our program.

###  **External Links:**

This site contains links to other sites not owned or controlled by us. FreeTrafficWorld.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites you may encounter.

###  **Data Access/Update:**

Members can access and update their personally identifiable information by logging in to their Password-Protected account and choosing different options to change their particular information.

###  **Contacting Us:**

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact us by using the link in the left hand menu.
