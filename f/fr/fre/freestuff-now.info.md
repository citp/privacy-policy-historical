# Free Games and gaming systems

Our e-mail address is:   
**E-mail: profitplusonline@gmail.com  
**

Your information will never be sold to any third party's. Your information will be used only to contact you via our products and services. 

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes the consumer's domain name and e-mail address (where possible). 

We collect the domain name and e-mail address (where possible) of visitors to our Web page. 

The information we collect is used to notify consumers about updates to our Web site, used by us to contact consumers for marketing purposes, . 

With respect to cookies: We do not set any cookies. 

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by or You may unsubscribe from our list at any time.. 

If you supply us with your postal address on-line you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by calling us at the number provided above. 

Please provide us with your exact name and address. We will be sure your name is removed from the list we share with other organizations 

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact or Contact us at profitplusonline@gmail.com
