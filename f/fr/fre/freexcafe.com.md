# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage.

## The Information We Collect

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted to freexcafe.com. On some pages, you can enter your name and email address.

## Cookies

When you visit the freexcafe.com website, we send one or more cookies to your computer or other browsing device. We use cookies to improve the quality of your experience, for storing your site preferences, improving search results and tracking user trends, such as how people surf the site. These cookies do not contain any private information and can not be traced back to you as a user in any way. Advertisers displaying banner ads on this site may be placing and reading cookies on users' browsers, or using web beacons to collect information, in the course of ads being served on this site. You may choose to not accept these cookies by adjusting settings in your web browser.

## The Way We Use Information

The information collected by freexcafe.com is used solely for improve the quality of your experience on our site. WE DO NOT sell, loan, lease, swap, trade or otherwise disclose your personal data, IP address or email address.

## Our Commitment To Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

## Links To Other Web Sites

This site contains links to other sites. freexcafe.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

## How To Contact Us

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please use contact form to send us your message.
