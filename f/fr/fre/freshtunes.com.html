Cookies

The Service uses cookies to distinguish you from other users of the Service and to improve your overall experience. When you use the Service, certain information may be stored locally on your device using 'cookies' or similar technologies such as objects stored in the device’s local storage. A cookie is a small text file used to store limited information about the user of the device. They help FreshTunes to identify how FreshTunes’ users use the Service, and so that FreshTunes can continue to develop and improve it.

Cookies do this by storing information about your preferences on your device, including the device model, manufacturer, screen resolution, device capabilities, service provider, country and city location data.

FreshTunes accesses this information when you use the Service, to provide you with content, in accordance with the rights FreshTunes have licensed, which is more relevant to your individual interests and for reporting and analytics purposes.

Specifically, the cookies FreshTunes use help FreshTunes to:

  • Estimate the number of visitors to FreshTunes’ website and usage patterns so that FreshTunes can understand how its website is used and how FreshTunes can improve visitors’ experiences;
  • Store information about your preferences and to therefore allow FreshTunes to customise its website according to individual interests; and
  • To recognise you when you return to the website.

FreshTunes use the following types of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary: These cookies enable FreshTunes’ Service to function correctly and deliver the services and products visitors have requested.
  • Performance & Functionality: These cookies are used to enhance the performance and functionality of FreshTunes’ Service but are non-essential to their use (for example, FreshTunes use cookies to remember your preferences on the Service). However, without these cookies, certain functionality (like videos and survey functionality) may become unavailable.
  • Analytical: These cookies collect information that is used either in aggregate form to help FreshTunes understand how the Service is being used or how effective FreshTunes’ marketing campaigns are, so FreshTunes can research the usability of its Service, or to help FreshTunes customise the Service for you. In particular FreshTunes use the Google analytics cookies. For more information about Google’s privacy policy, please visit http://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/. If you do not wish to allow the use of Google Analytics cookies at all, Google provides an opt-out plug-in for most common website browsers http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
  • User Authentication: These cookies are used to enable you to share pages and content that you find interesting on the Service through third party social networking websites.

By using the Service, you accept the use of cookies in accordance with this privacy policy.

For more information about what cookies are and your rights to control cookies, please visit the third party educational resource www.allaboutcookies.org.
