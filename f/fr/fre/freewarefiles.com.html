Freeware Files.com Privacy Policy

Freeware Files.com is aware of the importance in protecting the privacy of information provided by its users. Information is never collected from our visitors without their prior knowledge and consent. This Privacy Policy summarizes how we handle that information. 

Information Freeware Files.com Collects

Freeware Files.com collects information about its users during their registration for our newsletters, software submissions, contests, and polls. Information provided to us is used for internal purposes and for periodic opt-in newsletters to which users have subscribed. Information is never collected without the users knowledge. Freeware Files.com will not reveal information provided by its users to any third party without their permission. 

Users will not receive e-mail from other companies or individuals through the use of our newsletters unless users request to be placed on a list to receive information from our advertisers and/or partners on subjects of interest. Users have the option to "opt-out" of our newsletters and may request to have their provided information removed from our database. 

Freeware Files.com uses WebTrends Live to conduct anonymous web traffic analysis for our site. While WebTrends Live makes use of cookies, no personal information is ever collected or analyzed by this service.
