# Free-Codecs.com :: Privacy : Please read carefully this Privacy Policy before using Free-Codecs.com

This Privacy Policy only applies to Free-Codecs.com and its mirrors.

Free-Codecs.com may use cookies to enhance your surfing experience, but cookies will not be used under any circumstances to personally identify you without your prior consent. If the user decides to participate in the forum, with user's consent, a cookie will be issued to keep track of the username and password for the period of 1 year.

We will not sell, share, or rent this information. We may, from time to time, use this information to contact you - although this will happen infrequently - or to respond to questions or comments that you send us.

Third Party Advertising  
The ads appearing on this Web site are delivered to you by our Web advertising partner. Information about your visit to this site, such as number of times you have viewed an ad (but not your name, address, or other personal information), is used to serve ads to you on this site.

Third Party Cookies  
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser.

If you have any questions, suggestions or complains about our Privacy Policy please [contact us](http://www.free-codecs.com/contact_us.htm "Contact us") immediately!
