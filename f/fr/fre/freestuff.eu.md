# Win Your Prize - Free Stuff

  1. **Privacy Policy - Freestuff.eu**

Last updated: January 1st,2012

  2. **Who is FreeStuff.eu?**
  

    1. This Privacy Policy describes how FreeStuff.eu treats your personal data. FreeStuff.eu is a website operated by AllGroove Limited on behalf of Brandling AG. AllGroove Limited processes your personal data in the UK and is governed by English law concerning the protection of personal data.
  

    2. AllGroove Limited deals with so-called opt-in marketing. By participating in activities via FreeStuff.eu you can win prizes. The rules for participation and prize draw can be found in our conditions of [prize policy](https://web.archive.org/prize-policy.php).
  

  3. **What personal data do we collect?**
  

    1. Information provided by you through FreeStuff.eu  
When you participate in a contest through FreeStuff.eu, you are asked to provide information about yourself. This includes your name, address, date of birth, e-mail address and telephone number. We may also ask further questions regarding your marital status, education and for example your interests, preferences and your receipt of products or information. AllGroove Limited can also use your telephone number to send you a text message. These messages are only to confirm any order you have made. You will not receive SMS marketing from AllGroove Limited.
  

    2. Information provided by you via surveys  
Besides issuing competitions through FreeStuff.eu, AllGroove Limited or a client of AllGroove Limited can contact you through telephone, email or Internet for a survey. When you participate in such a survey, AllGroove Limited or the client which sends the survey shall process the personal data you provided in the survey. In such surveys you are asked to provide personal data such as your name, email address, address, postal code, telephone numbers, date of birth, gender, salary, education, and marital status, occupation, hobbies, interests, and (online) surfing habits.
  

    3. Information provided by you to third parties  
AllGroove Limited can obtain information about you through third parties or 'list rental'. AllGroove Limited acquires for example the following personal data through third parties: name, email address, address, postal code, telephone numbers, date of birth, gender, salary, education and marital status, occupation, the industry sector in which you workin addition to information about your hobbies, interests and online habits. AllGroove Limited ensures that you have agreed that your data can be transferred to third parties such AllGroove Limited. If you do not want your data to be further processed by AllGroove Limited, you can send an e-mail to info@freestuff.eu
  

    4. Information collected through cookies and related technology  
Besides the data which is collected by AllGroove Limited via FreeStuff.eu, via surveys and via third parties, AllGroove Limited uses cookies and other technology such as so-called third-party pixels to collect data about you.
  
A cookie is a small text file placed on your hard disk by the server of FreeStuff.eu. This allows the server to save your preferences and other data including passwords. Furthermore, AllGroove Limited uses cookies to remember that you have used the website before, to track your visits to the website and to track and aggregate statistical data regarding site visits , for example for reporting purposes for its internal organisation or for third parties.

You can decide whether to accept or refuse cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or to notify you each time a cookie is set. You can also learn about cookies by visiting AllAboutCookies.org which includes additional useful information on cookies and how to block cookies using different types of browser. Please note however, that by blocking or deleting cookies used on the website you may not be able to take full advantage of the website.

  4. **For what purposes do we use your personal data?**
  
AllGroove Limited uses the data you provide or which is otherwise obtained for the following purposes:
    1. Awarding prizes and communication with winners about their prizes, including carrying out promotional activities regarding winners and their prizes;
  

    2. Making offers on behalf of AllGroove Limited or on behalf of its clients and partners. AllgrooveLimited can contact you on behalf of AllgrooveLimited or on behalf of clients and partners via e-mail, telephone or regular mail.
  

    3. Providing your data to clients and partners so that they can approach you with targeted surveys, offers and information. Clients and partners of AllgrooveLimited who received your information can contact you via e-mail, telephone and regular mail;
  

    4. Creating interest profiles for marketing purposes.
  

  5. **Who does AllGroove Limited provide with your data?**
  

    1. Once you have given permission for the provision of your data to clients and partners of AllGroove Limited, AllGroove Limited sends your personal data to the concerned clients and partners.
  

    2. AllGroove Limited may hire other companies to manage its website or to provide you with products and services.
  

    3. Also, AllGroove Limited can give third parties access to your personal data where it is legally entitled to do so or where it is obliged to do so in order to comply with the law, (such as in response to a Court order) or for conducting legal proceedings.
  

    4. Furthermore, AllGroove Limited can use or disclose your personal data if we believe that it is necessary to share that information in order to investigate, to prevent, or to be able to take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, and situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of individuals.
  

    5. Finally, in the event that we undergo a reorganisation or are sold to a third party, you agree that any personal information AllGroove Limited hold about you may be transferred to that reorganised entity or third party.
  

  6. **How do we deal with personal data of minors?**
  

    1. AllGroove Limited does not knowingly collect data from persons under the age of sixteen. As soon as it becomes known to AllGroove Limited that personal data of a person under the age of sixteen are included in its database, it will remove this data.
  

  7. **How do we protect your data?**
  

    1. When you provide personal data via the website, or when third parties provide personal data to AllGroove Limited, AllGroove Limited ensures the adequate security of your personal data. All data is stored in databases protected by among other things firewalls, to protect data from loss, abuse or accidental changes. We will review and update these measures as new technology becomes available.
  

  8. **What is the relationship between AllGroove Limited and third party websites?**
  

    1. The website of AllGroove Limited and e-mail messages sent by AllGroove Limited contain links to other websites. AllGroove Limited accepts no responsibility for the content of these websites.
  

  9. **How can I unsubscribe from AllGroove Limited and its partners and clients?**
  

    1. Unsubscribe from AllGroove Limited  
If you no longer wish to be contacted by e-mail by AllGroove Limited, please send an e-mail to info@freestuff.eu . Also for e-mails sent by AllGroove Limited on behalf of its partners, you can unsubscribe through info@freestuff.eu
  

    2. Unsubscribe from clients and partners of AllGroove Limited  
Once you have agreed to provide your data to clients and partners of AllGroove Limited, these third parties can approach you with offers and information. To Opt out of receiving offers and information from these third parties, you should submit a direct request to the concerned third party.
  

  10. **International transfers**
  

    1. As part of the services offered to you through our website, the information you provide to us may be transferred to countries outside of the European Economic Area ("EEA"). By way of example, this may happen if any of our servers are, from time to time, located in a country outside of the EEA or one of our service providers is located in a country outside of the EEA. We also share information between our group companies, which are located in countries worldwide. These countries may not have similar data protection laws to the EEA. If we transfer your information outside of the EEA in this way, we will take steps to ensure that your privacy rights continue to be protected.
  

    2. In addition, if you use our services while you are outside the EEA, your information may be transferred outside the EEA in order to provide you with those services.
  

  11. **Your rights**
  

    1. You are entitled to ask for a copy of the information we store about you (for which we may charge a small fee not exceeding the prescribed maximum) or to update the details you have supplied us with. You can also prevent the continued use of your personal information for direct marketing purposes as mentioned above.
  

  12. **How Can I Get Contact?**
  

    1. If you want to change your personal data or have other questions about how AllGroove Limited processes your personal data, please contact us at info@freestuff.eu.
