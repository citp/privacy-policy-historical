# Privacy Statement

PRIVACY POLICY

Freedict.com does not collect or save any personal information about any visitor and will not send any unsolicited information (spam) to anyone.

Freedict.com logs statistic information about browsers, operating systems, IP-addresses and referring websites for security purposes and to further improve the website. None of this information is distributed to any third party.

Freedict.com does not use cookies.
