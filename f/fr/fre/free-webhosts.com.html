General
We do NOT collect personal information from you unless you provide it to us.
We do NOT provide or sell personal info such as the names, addresses, or email addresses of our visitors to anyone else.
We do NOT send unsolicited email to you (Spam).

Contact Forms
Any info submitted by contact forms is optional. If email addresses are given for us to reply to, these will not be shared with anyone else. If you wish to not be contacted, you may leave the name and email address fields blank to send an anonymous comment.

The IP addresses of abusers may be banned from further postings. Any info that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information. Therefore you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your email address or any personal information. We reserve the right to delete or edit the content of any message in order to remove offensive content or to improve readability. We are not responsible for the postings of other visitors. The forums may also make use of cookies for security purposes. If a valid email address is required to use the forums, these will not be given to anyone else, or used by us to send unsolicited email. See the forum for more info on its privacy policy.

Free-Webhosts.com displays ads for other companies' websites. Some of the advertisements on this website may contain cookies which collect information for the advertised company. We do not have access to this information. In the future, we might use cookies to rotate ads.

Cookies
Free-Webhosts.com and its advertisers may set and access cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the visitor's hard drive containing info about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on the Free-Webhosts.com website. You can set your browser to not accept cookies. See your browser's documentation for more information on accepting cookies. By setting a cookie on our site, the user would not have to log in a password more than once, thus saving time while on our site. If a user rejects the cookie, he or she may still use our site. The only drawback to this is that the user may be limited in some areas of the Free-Webhosts.com website, such as not being able to post messages in the forums. Cookies also may be used for statistical purposes, to remember user preferences, or to rotate ads.
