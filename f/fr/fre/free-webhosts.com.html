Cookies and Web Beacons
Free-Webhosts.com and its advertisers (such as Google) may set and access cookies on your computer. Google's use of their DART cookie enables it to serve ads to website users based on their visit to this website and other websites on the Internet. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the visitor's hard drive containing info about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on the Free-Webhosts.com website. You can set your browser to not accept cookies (or by managing preferences in programs such as Norton Internet Security). See your browser's documentation for more information on accepting cookies. By setting a cookie on our site, the user would not have to log in a password more than once to leave user reviews, thus saving time while on our site. If a user rejects the cookie, he or she may still use our site. The only drawback to this is that the user may be limited in some areas of the Free-Webhosts.com website, such as not being able to submit user reviews. Cookies also may be used for statistical purposes, to remember user preferences, or to rotate ads. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the "Google ad and content network privacy policy", which you can read at http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html
