# Free Stuff Hot Deals Privacy Policy

**Introduction:**

> FreeStuffHotDeals.com is committed to safeguarding your privacy online. Please read the following policy to understand how your personal information will be used. 

**General:**

> FreeStuffHotDeals.com does NOT share personal information you provide to us with other companies. We will NEVER release, sell or give a member's name or e-mail address to any other entity, party or organization, without the member's explicit prior permission. 

**Other sites:**

> This site contains links to other sites. FreeStuffHotDeals.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. Links found in the FreeStuffHotDeals.com forums are for informational purposes only, and do not necessarily imply an endorsement by FreeStuffHotDeals.com. 

**Email:**

> To participate in the [forums](https://web.archive.org/freebies/) you must provide a valid working email address. Others users cannot see this address. We will never give or sell your email address to any third party; nor will we reveal your address to other users. 
> 
> We may, from time to time, email our members about exciting new website features or special promotional offers. Upon receiving such an email, you will be given the opportunity to permanently and immediately opt-out of such mailings. 

**Information Gathered:**

> Your profile page in the [forums](https://web.archive.org/freebies/) allows you to provide various information for others to see. The fields _Username_ , _Location_ , _Website_ , _Occupation_ , _Interests_ , _ICQ Number_ , _AIM Address_ , _MSN Messenger_ , and _Yahoo Messenger_ are viewable by the public. You are free to leave these fields blank if you do not feel comfortable sharing this information. All other information on your profile page is kept strictly confidential. 
> 
> FreeStuffHotDeals.com collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners, including information about your use of products and services that we offer. 
> 
> FreeStuffHotDeals.com uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients. 

**Cookies:**

> FreeStuffHotDeals may set and access FreeStuffHotDeals cookies on your computer. These are used to automatically sign you into the forum, and to differentiate you from other users of our website. 
> 
> FreeStuffHotDeals.com lets other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to FreeStuffHotDeals's cookies. 

**Security & Confidentiality:**

> We will not release any information about you, including your email address, to any third party other than the exceptions noted above. We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect personal information about you. 

**Children:**

> When a child under age 13 registers with FreeStuffHotDeals.com, we suggest that a parent is present to prevent the child from inadvertantly volunteering private information. 

**Logfiles:**

> FreeStuffHotDeals.com uses standard web logfiles, which automatically record all requests made of the Freestuffhotdeals.com servers. Such information includes your I.P. addresses, the pages requested, the kind of browser used, etc. While we do not analyze these logfiles with regards to individual users, we study these data in aggregate in order to improve the site.
