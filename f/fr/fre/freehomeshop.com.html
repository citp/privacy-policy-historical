Free Home Shop Privacy Policy

At Free Home Shop, we recognize the importance of and are dedicated to protecting your privacy. We have developed methods of allowing potential buyers or renters to contact you without revelaing your email address to them. In addition, we follow these policies:
  • We will only use your email address to contact you regarding your account, or to send you other information to which you have chosen to subscribe. We will not automatically subscribe you to any information mailing lists.
     
  • We will only use your telephone number in urgent situations to contact you regarding your account.
     
  • We will never disclose your email address or telephone number to anyone unless you indicate that you want it displayed or if required to do so by law.
     
  • This site uses cookies to keep track of logins. The cookie contains only random numbers used to associate a web visitor with a member in our database. The cookie contains no personal information.
     

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Users must be at least 13 years of age to register on Free Home Shop. We do not collect or store information about children under 13 years of age.
