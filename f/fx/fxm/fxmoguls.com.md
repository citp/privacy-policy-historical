FX Moguls LTD is committed to your privacy and the protection of your personal information. If you receive email correspondence from FX Moguls LTD, (1) your email address is either listed with FX Moguls LTD as someone who has expressly shared the listed email address for the purpose of receiving information in the future ("opt-in"), or (2) you have registered or purchased or otherwise have an existing relationship with FX Moguls LTD. Therefore, we respect your time and attention my controlling the frequency and content of our mailings.

FX Moguls LTD implements various security measures and procedures to protect your personal information against the loss, misuse, abuse, and alteration. FX Moguls LTD will never share, sell, or rent individual personal information with anyone without your advance permission or unless ordered by a court of law. Information submitted to FX Moguls LTD is only available to employees managing this information for purposes of contacting you or sending you emails based on your request for information and to contracted service providers for purposes of providing services relating to our communications with you.

Each email sent by FX Moguls LTD contains an easy, automated way for you to cease receiving email from FX Moguls LTD. Should this be your desire, you will need to simply follow the instructions at the end of that email.

If you have received unwanted, unsolicited email sent from FX Moguls LTD or purporting to be sent from FX Moguls LTD, please forward a copy of that email with your comments to support@fxmoguls.com for review.
