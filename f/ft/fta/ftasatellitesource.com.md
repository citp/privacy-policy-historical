FTA Satellite Source Privacy Policy

FTA Satellite Source does not sell, transfer, or give away our customers names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses. We do not collect data without your knowledge when your visit [http://www.ftasatellitesource.com](http://www.ftasatellitesource.com/), and if you choose to provide your information, we make it non-accesable to any individual or other business. All payment information is entered through Paypals secured site during check out. This means additional security and less to worry about for our customers.
