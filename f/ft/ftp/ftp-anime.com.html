This includes your e-mail address, name, and location. Upon logging on to FTP-Anime.com such things as your IP address and hostname are logged for statistical and security reasons.

    Cookies:
    A cookie is a very small text file placed on your system upon logging on to FTP-Anime.com (and most other websites). This file serves as your identification card and is uniquely yours, and can only be read by the server that gave it to you. Cookies tell us that you have returned to a specific web page on FTP-Anime.com and help us track your preferences and transactional habits. The basic function of cookies is to help our server remember who you are, editing cookie given by FTP-Anime.com will only work against you.

If FTP-Anime.com learns that a child under the age of 13 has become a member, we will close that child's account and delete any information collected about the child.
