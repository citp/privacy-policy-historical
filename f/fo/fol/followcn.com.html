1.4 Details of your visits to the Website(s) including, but not limited to, traffic data, location data and other communication data, whether this is required for our own billing purposes or otherwise and the resources that you access.

IP addresses and cookies

2.1 We may collect information about your computer, including where available your IP address, operating system and browser type, for system 2 administration. This is statistical data about our users’ browsing actions and patterns, and does not identify any individual.

2.2 For the same reason, we may obtain information about your general internet usage by using cookies. Cookies are small software files, typically consisting of numbers and letters, which are placed by a website onto the computer, telephone or other device used to access that website.

2.3 We will place cookies onto any computer, telephone or other device you use to access the Website(s) (“Device”). These cookies will enable information to be;

2.3.1 stored on your Device; and 2.3.2 sent to and from the Website(s) and your Device. 2.3.3 Cookies help us to improve the Website(s) and to deliver a better and more personalized service. They enable us to: 2.3.4 estimate our audience size and usage pattern; 2.3.5 store information about your preferences, and so allow us to customize the Website(s) according to your individual interests; 2.3.6 speed up your searches; and 2.3.7 recognize you when you return to the Website(s).

2.4 We operate a self-service affiliate platform, which allows Affiliates to secure and drive traffic to offers and products chosen by Advertisers. The Affiliates must place special tracking links on their website when they choose which offers to promote. This enables people to click on the link and be directed to the website of the online retailer via one of our web servers. Affiliates earn commission on the basis of the sales generated by the advertisements they display. As part of our business, we calculate the commission that is earned by recording which affiliates have generated sales for which advertisers. To do this, our web server uses cookies to record when a visitor of a website publisher clicks on a link to the online retailer. However, these cookies are not placed on any of your Devices by any of our Websites.

2.5 You may refuse to accept cookies by refusing to accept the terms and conditions of some of the Websites; or by activating the setting on your internet browser which allows you to refuse the setting of cookies; however, if you do not accept cookies you may be unable to access certain parts of the Website(s).

2.6 If you wish to withdraw your consent, you must notify us immediately and stop accessing the Website(s).

2.7 If you want to delete the cookies on your Device, you should consult the Help function of your internet browser.

2.8 Please note that our advertisers and affiliates may also use cookies, over which we have no control.

By submitting your personal data, you agree to this transfer, storing or processing.
