# Football Champions - Sweet Nitro

We, Sweet Nitro SL and Sublinet SARL, take the protection of your personal data very seriously and rigorously comply with the rules of data protection laws. Person-related data is only collected on the **Football Champions** application and or website to the extent technically necessary for providing the service. Under no circumstances will the collected data be sold or unlawfully provided to third parties for any other reasons.

The following explanation provides you with an overview of how we guarantee this protection and what type of data is collected for what purpose.

## Data collection, processing and use on this internet site.

Sweet Nitro collects person-related data

  * that you provide during registration,
  * that you provide for the billing of goods and services requiring payment
  * to, upon your request, participate in advertising campaigns and surveys or to gather information about products and services (advertising).



Sweet Nitro automatically collects and stores information in their server log files, which your device (mobile, tablet, browser) transfers to us, within the scope of the use of their internet sites and their games. These are:

  * Unique advertising and/or device identifiers (IDFA, Google ADID etc.)
  * Browser type/version
  * Operating system used
  * Referrer URL (the previously visited site)
  * Host name of the accessing computer (IP address)
  * Cookies
  * Time of the server request



This data cannot be assigned to certain individuals by Sweet Nitro. It shall be used by Sweet Nitro in a pseudonymous and/or anonymous form to tailor and improve our offer according to the needs of the user. This data will not be combined with other data sources; the data will be deleted following a statistical evaluation.

Sweet Nitro shall process and use their person-related data for providing services and for billing our services as well as for advertising purposes. In individual cases, we shall commission related companies or other third parties to provide services. This particularly applies to the handling of payments via external service providers (PayPal, Global Collect, etc.) as well as to collection agencies in the case of late payments. These external companies are obligated to treat your data confidentially and safely to the extent required in fulfilling their duties. 

## Cookies

The internet sites use so-called cookies at multiple points. They serve to make our offer more user-friendly, effective and secure. Cookies are small text files placed on your computer and stored by your browser. Alone, cookies cannot be used to identify the user. Most of the cookies we use are so-called “session cookies”. They are automatically deleted after your visit is concluded. Cookies do not cause harm to your computer and do not contain viruses. Naturally, the websites and games operated by Sweet Nitro can in principle also be used without cookies if the web browser is set in such a manner that cookies are generally denied or you are notified if a cookie is received, thus allowing you to decide every time whether you would like to accept it or not. You can manually delete cookies that are already on your computer at any time from the hard drive. Sweet Nitro would like to inform you that using the website and games without cookies may only be possible on a limited basis. 

## Data sharing

We will share your information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement. We may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information: 

  * as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process;
  * when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;
  * with our trusted services providers who work on our behalf, do not have an independent use of the information we disclose to them, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement.
  * to advertisers and third party advertising networks and analytics companies as described in the section below



## E-Mail Advertising

Subject to your consent, your person-related data shall be stored, processed and used by Sweet Nitro and our marketing partners for sending e-mail advertising for mobile and/or browser games (products and services). You may revoke your consent for this at any time in writing (e.g. per e-mail) without costs being incurred, apart from the costs for sending the revocation (at the basic rates). In every piece of information and in every newsletter sent by Sweet Nitro or our marketing partners, you will be given the option of rejecting the receipt of further information and sending a respective cancellation. 

## Data security

We are concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. We provide physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect information we process and maintain. For example, we limit access to this information to authorized employees and contractors who need to know that information in order to operate, develop or improve our Application. Please be aware that, although we endeavor provide reasonable security for information we process and maintain, no security system can prevent all potential security breaches. 

## Consent

By using **Football Champions** , you are consenting to our processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us. "Processing,” means using cookies on a computer/hand held device or using or touching information in any way, including, but not limited to, collecting, storing, deleting, using, combining and disclosing information. 

## Right to Information

You have the right to information regarding your stored personal data, its origin and recipients as well as the purpose for storage. The data protection representative will provide information regarding the stored data (the supplier, complete as an e-mail link) upon request. 

## Further Information

Your trust is important to us. Therefore, we will answer any of your questions regarding the processing of your person-related data. If you have questions that were not addressed in this declaration of data protection, please contact our data protection representative at any time via our [contact form](https://web.archive.org/web/20180507073051id_/https%3A//www.sweetnitro.com/en/contact.php). 

## Children

We do not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20180507073051id_/https%3A//www.sweetnitro.com/en/contact.php). We will delete such information from our files within a reasonable time. 

## Updates to this policy

We update this Data Protection Statement regularly and reserve the right to make changes to it. Therefore please regularly check whether a new version is available. The date of the last change of this document is shown at the bottom of this page.
