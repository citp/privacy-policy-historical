# Foot Locker Europe: Privacy

**Privacy Statement**   
Foot Locker Europe B.V. believe in keeping your confidential information confidential. With this in mind, we have adopted the privacy policy described below, which addresses: 

**Use of Information**   
Information collected by Foot Locker Europe B.V. is used by us for three main purposes: 

We collect information to process customer orders, to contact our customers concerning the status of an order or to answer any of their questions.   
We collect information to contact those visitors that have opted-in to receive information about upcoming sales, contests, promotions, and product information, and to participate in contests and promotions.   
At times, we may share aggregate or summary information, such as statistics about our customers, sales traffic patterns and related site information, with reputable third-party vendors. However, this information will not include any personally identifiable information.   
We do not sell or share any information about our users on the individual customer level. Foot Locker Europe B.V. will not collect any personally identifying information, except when specifically provided by visitors to our Website. Such information includes name, address, e-mail address, and telephone number.
