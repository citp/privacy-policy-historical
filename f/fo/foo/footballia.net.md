# Privacy policy | Footballia

#### Privacy policy

The current Privacy Policy establishes the terms in which the information provided by the users of FOOTBALLIA is used and protected. When we ask you to fill in a personal information field that can identify you, we do so ensuring that it will only be used in agreement with the terms of this document. However, this Privacy Policy may change over time or be updated, for which reason we recommend you check this page frequently to ensure you agree with such changes. Our safety standard is the highest possible, as imposed by the EU General Data Protection Regulation.

 **Information gathered**

Our website may gather personal information, for example: name, contact information such as your e-mail address and demographics. Likewise, whenever necessary specific information may be requested to process an advantage or carry out a delivery or billing.

 **Use of information gathered**

Our website uses the information in order to provide the best possible service, more specifically to keep a user register to make the MASTER zone work and improve our study material. It is possible that e-mail messages are sent periodically through our website with special offers, new products and other advertising information we may consider relevant or beneficial to you. These e-mail messages will be sent to the address provided by you and may be cancelled at any time. FOOTBALLIA is highly committed to keeping your information safe. We use the latest systems and constantly update them to ensure no unauthorized access takes place.

 **Cookies**

A cookie refers to a file sent in order to request permission to be stored in your computer. When accepting this file, the cookie is created and it stores information related to web traffic, as well as it facilitates future visits to a recurring website. Cookies are also useful to recognize you individually, and therefore they can help provide you with the websiteâ€™s best customized service. Our website uses cookies to identify the pages visited and the frequency. This information is only used for statistical analysis and is then deleted permanently. You can delete the cookies from your computer at any given time. However, cookies help provide better website service. The ones used by FOOTBALLIA do not grant access to information about your computer or you, unless you request it and provide it directly through explicit consent. You can accept or refuse the use of cookies, however most browsers accept cookies automatically, as they help improve the service. You can also change the configuration of your computer to refuse cookies. If they are refused, it is possible you may not use shortcuts in the MASTER zone.

 **Links to Third-Party Websites**

This site may contain links to other websites that may be of interest to you. Once you click on those links and leave our site, we no longer have any control on the site you will be redirected to, and therefore we are not responsible of the terms, privacy or the protection of your data in those third-party websites. Such websites are subject to their own privacy policies, and it is therefore advisable to consult them to confirm you agree with them.

 **Control of your personal information**

At any given time, you may restrict the gathering or use of the personal information you provide to our website. Any time you are requested to fill in a form, such as the sign-up form, you may check or uncheck the choice to receive information via e-mail. In case you checked the choice to receive our bulletin or publicity, you may cancel it at any time. FOOTBALLIA will not sell, relinquish or distribute the personal information it gathers without your consent, unless required by a judge with a court order. We reserve the right to change the terms of the present Privacy Policy at any time.

 **Your rights**

Regarding the personal data we may have of you, you have the rights acknowledged by the current European Regulation: access, rectification, cancellation, opposition, to be forgotten, suspension of treatment and portability. For that reason we have made the following e-mail account available, where you can exercise those rights or ask for any clarification: rgpd@footballia.net
