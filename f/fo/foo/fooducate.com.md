# Fooducate - eat a bit better

## _Fooducateâ„¢ Privacy Policy_

Last Updated: **_January 1, 2010_**

Fooducate is committed to protecting your privacy. This Statement of Privacy applies to the Fooducate and Cereal Scan website (hereafter the "website") and governs data collection and usage. By using the website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

**Personal Information**

Fooducate collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address and name.

**Fooducate does not sell, rent or lease its email lists to third parties.**

There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by Fooducate. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. This information is used by Fooducate for the operation of the websites, to maintain quality of service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the websites.

In the event that you contact us through the website, we may also request that you provide additional User Information relevant to the support requested by you. Users are under no obligation to provide such information.

Fooducate collects and uses your personal information to operate the Fooducate website and deliver the services you have requested. Fooducate may also use your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Fooducate. Fooducate may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services.

Fooducate keeps track of the websites and pages you visit within Fooducate, in order to determine what are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content within Fooducate to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

Fooducate websites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Fooducate or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Fooducate (including enforcing this agreement); and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Fooducate, or the public.

**Changes To This Privacy Policy**

Fooducate is committed to protecting your privacy. As our services evolve, there may be changes to this policy. We'll let you know in advance of such changes.

For questions or concerns please email [privacy@fooducate.com](mailto:info@fooducate.com?subject=Privacy%20from%20webform)
