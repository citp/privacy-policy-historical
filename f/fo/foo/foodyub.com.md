# Privacy Policy

**Feb. 19, 2015**

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to FoodYub.com. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. Please read the following to learn more about how we treat personal information when you use our services including the privacy policy when you use FoodYub.com. 

**Personal Information**

  * FoodYub.com asks for personal information such as your email address and password which is used to send you notifications should you decide to receive them.
  * When you add messages to the FoodYub.com website, we collect and maintain the content of the message as well as the date and time.



**Uses and Information Sharing**

  * FoodYub.com maintains and processes your personal information and content for the purposes described in the FoodYub.com Privacy Policy. Your social profile information is displayed according to the preferences you set.
  * When you send messages through FoodYub.com, your name will be identified in the message. We also use your name and email address to notify you if you are one of our subscribers. You can unsubscribe from these notification emails at any time.
