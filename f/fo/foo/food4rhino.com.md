# Privacy | Food4Rhino | Rhino

**Food4Rhino Privacy Policy**

Your personal information is treated by **food4Rhino** ([McNeel Europe](http://www.en.emea.mcneel.com/ "McNeel Europe")) with the maximum confidentiality and with the sole purpose to improve our products and services according to your needs. It is just much easier to help if we know what is important to you and have a way to contact you.

**What we do with your information**

1\. Keep it confidential. We share your information only  with the developers of any Rhino plug-ins, Grasshopper add-ons or textures you might have downloaded from food4Rhino website, as agreed when accepting  the [terms and conditions of use](http://www.food4rhino.com/terms-and-conditions-of-use).  
2\. Improve this web site so that it better fits your needs.  
3\. Improve our products so that they better fit your needs.  
4. Build an electronic newsletter subscription list so that we can notify subscribers of update information on our products, services and support.

**What we do NOT do with your information**

1\. Sell or provide your information to anyone outside this organization, with the exception of the developers of any Rhino plug-ins, Grasshopper add-ons or textures you might have downloaded from food4Rhino website, as agreed when accepting  the [terms and conditions of use](http://www.food4rhino.com/terms-and-conditions-of-use).  
2. Provide your information to our resellers, distributors, or OEMs.

**Updating your information**

To change your e-mail address, just [edit your account](http://www.food4rhino.com/user/login).

**Deactivate account**

To deactivate your account, [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20130605224452id_/http://www.food4rhino.com/privacy/4FQ.RYY3Q4@F3GULnPFFO:FKRGS4@) with the subject “Deactivate account”.
