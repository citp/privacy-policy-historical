So-called "cookies" are used at the FOREXGHQ TRADING LTD.'s website to help you in personalizing your online presence. A cookie means a text file which a web-page server places on your hard disk. Only web-server in the domain, which issued it to you, can read it, because cookies are uniquely assigned.

One of the main purposes of cookies is saving your time, which is very convenient for you. Cookie is intended to signal the web-server that the user has returned to a particular page.
