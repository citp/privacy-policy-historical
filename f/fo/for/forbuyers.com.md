### 2\. Collection & Use of Your Information

Forbuyers.com is sole owner of the information collected on this website. The information we obtained from you is used to build the database which will help us review, develop, and improve our services. 

(1)What kind of information do we collect?

We collect all the information in your account on forbuyers.com, such as your name, address, company name, telephone number, fax number and email address, etc. In addition to your account data, we also collect records information when you browse and visit our website, including but not limited to cookies, IP address, etc.

We collect all the information in your account on forbuyers.com, such as your name, address, company name, telephone number, fax number and email address, etc. In addition to your account data, we also collect records information when you browse and visit our website, including but not limited to cookies, IP address, etc. 

### 3\. Security Measures

We have taken appropriate measures to safeguard the privacy of your personal information. And we have implemented relevant technology to encrypt your account avoiding being divulged or viciously modified. We recommend that you do not divulge your password to anyone. Our personnel will never ask you for your password in an unsolicited phone call or in an unsolicited e-mail. Remember to sign out of your account and close your browser window when you have finished your session. However, no method of transmission on the internet can guarantee 100% security, but we will strive to make your private information much safer. 

### 4\. To whom we Disclose Your Personal Information?

We will not disclose your information unless we have ample reasons to believe that such disclosure is necessary in the following cases: 

(1) Within your authorization;

(2) You are willing to share your information publicly;

(3) Required by law, legal procedures, or government enforcement;

(4) Aiming at academic research or public service;

(5) In order to safeguard rights of forbuyers.com, such as searching, preventing, and handling fraud or security issues.

### 5\. Modifications

Forbuyers.com will at its sole discretion identify and correct the inaccuracies which may occur in our terms. If any modification is made, we will make you aware of what information we collect, how to use it and under what circumstances. It depends on your own choice whether we are authorized to use your information we collected. No authorization is sure to affect our capabilities in providing you with our best service. 

### 6\. Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of information that a website can store in a designated file on your computer. When you browse a website, cookies could help you improve your web surfing experience. We use cookies to gather and record your surfing information, with which we can provide you with tailored information to your needs. And also cookies can save your time when you re-enter your account. Many browsers are designed to accept cookies, but you can set your browsers to refuse cookies. One point should be noted that if you elect to disable our cookies, you will not be able to take full advantage of a personalized experience on our website.

For the convenience of your surfing experience, we may post links of other web sites, please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of web sites not operated by us. Before you visit relevant websites, please read relating privacy statements of each. This privacy statement applies solely to the information collected by forbuyers.com.
