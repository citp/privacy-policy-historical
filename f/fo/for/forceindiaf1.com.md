# Home - Sahara Force India F1 Team

PRIVACY POLICY

Our site is operated by _Force India Formula One Team Limited_ (competing as _Sahara Force India Formula One Team_ ). We are registered in England and Wales under company number 2417588 and have our registered office and trading address at _Dadford Road, Silverstone, Northamptonshire, NN12 8TJ_. References to "we", "our" or "us" are to _Force India Formula One Team Limited_.

We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy and this policy (together with our [Website Terms of Use](https://web.archive.org/Terms) sets out the basis on which any data we collect from you (personal or otherwise), or that you provide to us, will be processed by us. Please read the following carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your data and how we will treat it.

Information we may collect from you 

The business purpose of our site _www.forceindiaf1.com_ ("our site") is to keep users updated on all news relating to the _Sahara Force India Formula One Team_.

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  * Information that you provide by filling in forms on our site. This includes information provided at the time of registering as a member to use our site or when subscribing to a particular service or requesting further services; 
  * Information that you provide if you enter a competition or promotion sponsored or organised by us;
  * Information that you provide when you report a problem with our site;
  * Information you provide when you complete surveys on our site;
  * Details of transactions you carry out through our site and of the fulfilment of your orders should you order any merchandise from our site; and
  * Details of your visits to our site including, but not limited to, traffic data, location data, weblogs and other communication data, whether this is required for our own billing purposes or otherwise and the resources that you access. 



If you contact us, we may keep a record of that correspondence.

Where we propose to use your personal data for any other purpose we will ensure that we first obtain your consent to such processing or collection.

IP addresses 

Through use of services such as Google Analytics, we may collect information about your computer (including where available your IP address, operating system and browser type) for system administration and to report aggregate information to our advertisers. The collection of such information also allows us to perform site metrics that enable us to improve the functionality of our site. The information collected is statistical data about our users' browsing actions and patterns, and does not identify any individual or collate any personal data.

Uses made of your data 

We use information held about you in the following ways:

  * To ensure that content from our site is presented in the most effective manner for you and for your computer;
  * For "service administration purposes", which means that we may contact you for reasons related to the service, activity or online content you have signed up for (e.g. to provide you with password reminders or to notify you that a particular service, activity or online content has been suspended for maintenance); 
  * To personalise the way the content of our site is presented to you. For example, to provide you with information relating to events that are happening in your area (to do this we make use of a Google "geocoding web service application"); 
  * To provide you with information, products or services that you request from us or which we feel may interest you (where you have consented to be contacted for such purposes);
  * To carry out our obligations arising from any contracts entered into between you and us;
  * To allow you to participate in interactive features of our service, when you choose to do so;
  * To notify you about changes to our service.



We will also use your data, or permit selected third parties to use your data, to provide you with information about goods and services which may be of interest to you (if you consent to such use of your data when you register with us). If you do not want us to use your data in this way, please untick the relevant box situated on the membership registration form or contact us directly (at the address below).

We do not disclose personal data to our advertisers, but we may provide them with aggregate information about our users (for example, we may inform them that 500 men aged under 30 have clicked on their advertisement on any given day). We may also use such aggregate information to help advertisers reach the kind of audience they want to target. We may make use of the personal data we have collected from you to enable us to comply with our advertisers' wishes by displaying their advertisement to that target audience.

Cookies 

Our site uses "cookies" to distinguish you from other users of our site. A cookie is a small text file that helps us to make your visit to our site more enjoyable and meaningful to you. For example, cookies avoid you having to log in every time you come back to our site. Cookies also help us to provide you with a good experience when you browse our site and allow us to improve our site. We use the following different types of cookies:

  * _a "session" cookie_ ; this is stored in your computer's working memory and only lasts for your browsing session. When you close all your browser windows, or when you shut down your computer, the session cookie disappears forever. 
  * _a "persistent" cookie_ ; this is stored on your computer's hard drive until a specified date, which could be tomorrow, next week, or 10 years from now. Persistent cookies stay on your computer until either: a) they expire; b) they are overwritten with newer cookies; or c) you remove them. 



We may allow other companies to display advertisements on our site. To do this, an advertiser will place a persistent cookie on your computer. This allows the advertiser to recognize your computer each time you view an online advertisement. In this way, advertisers may compile information about where you saw their advertisements and determine which advertisements have been clicked on. This information allows an advertiser to deliver advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. We do not have access to or control of the cookies that may be placed by an advertiser. These cookies do not allow the advertiser to collect personal data from the user.

Users may be directed to our site via banner advertisements or site links. Advertisements or site links that are placed by us on third party sites use cookies to deliver metrics on the effectiveness of the advertisement or link. They do not collect personal data from the user.

Your browser can be configured not to accept cookies, however, this may prevent you from having access to certain functions or features of our site.

Where we store your personal data 

The data that we collect from you may be transferred to, and stored at, a destination outside the European Economic Area ("EEA"). It may also be processed by staff operating outside the EEA who work for us or for one of our suppliers. Such staff maybe engaged in, among other things, the fulfilment of your order, the processing of your payment details and the provision of support services. By submitting your personal data, you agree to this transfer, storing or processing. We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.

All information you provide to us is stored securely by us and we have systems, policies and procedures in place to protect your data from loss, misuse or alteration. Where you have a password which enables you to access certain parts of our site, you are responsible for keeping this password confidential. We ask you not to share a password with anyone.

Unfortunately, the transmission of information via the internet is not completely secure. Although we will do our best to protect your data, we cannot guarantee the security of your data transmitted to our site; any transmission is at your own risk. Once we have received your information, we will use strict procedures and security features to try to prevent unauthorised access.

We may disclose your personal information to any member of our group, which means our subsidiaries, our ultimate holding company and its subsidiaries, as defined in section 1159 of the UK Companies Act 2006.

Further, we may disclose your personal information to third parties:

  * In the event that we consider selling our business or assets, in which case we may disclose your personal data to the prospective buyer of such business or assets;
  * If _Sahara Force India Formula One Team_ or substantially all of its assets are acquired by a third party, in which case personal data held by us about our customers will be one of the transferred assets; and 
  * If we are under a duty to disclose or share your personal data in order to comply with any legal obligation, or in order to enforce or apply [our Terms of Use](https://web.archive.org/Terms) or to protect the rights, property, or safety of _Sahara Force India Formula One Team_ , our customers, our employees or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organisations for the purposes of fraud protection and credit risk reduction.
