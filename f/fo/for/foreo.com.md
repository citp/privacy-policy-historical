# Privacy policy | FOREO

## Summary

FOREO is committed to and recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of any and all personal and credit card information you may provide. We operate under strict policies and security measures to protect our customers.  
When you sign up with FOREO, register your warranty or use our online shop, you give us your consent to process and use your personal data in accordance with the terms and conditions of this privacy policy.

## Information Collected Online

FOREO will collect, through online forms, personal information from website visitors who choose to sign up, register their warranties or use our online shop. If you are under the age of 18, please do not attempt to submit any personal details. FOREO will not collect any personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 18.  
To improve our services and enhance your FOREO experience, certain other information is collected for statistical purposes. This statistical information is not personally identifiable and includes measuring the number of visitors to the various sections of our website and identifying system performance or technical problem areas. The following information is automatically collected about your visit:  
If you have used another website to find us, the name of the website from which you have linked to foreo.com.  
The name of the domain you use to access the Internet.  
The country from which the visit originated.  
The browser and OS you use to view web pages. The date and time you visited foreo.com.  
This website uses the Google AdWords remarketing service to advertise on third party websites (including Google) to previous visitors to our site. It could mean that we advertise to previous visitors who haven’t completed a task on our site, for example using the contact form to make an enquiry. This could be in the form of an advertisement on the Google search results page, or a site in the Google Display Network. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on someone’s past visits to the foreo.comwebsite. Of course, any data collected will be used in accordance with our own privacy policy and Google’s privacy policy.

## Cookies

foreo.com utilizes cookies for statistical purposes, to ascertain which pages are visited and how often and to enhance your online experience upon your return.  
The majority of web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you do not have to accept them. To disable cookies, access ‘help’ on your web browser or use the user manual that came with your browser software. However, please bear in mind that your visit to foreo.com will be significantly enhanced if cookies are not disabled. For more information about cookies, please visit [www.allaboutcookies.org](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/).

## How FOREO uses the information you provide

If you choose to sign up or register your warranty, thus providing us with your personal information, we will enter this information into our customer database. The information will be used to administer, simplify and improve our online services to you, and to process any orders you place with our online shop.  
We will email you FOREO info, offers and news only if you give us authorization by ticking 'yes' when signing up or registering.

## Policy on Third-Party Information

To protect privacy, any and all third-party information you provide after clicking on 'Tell a friend' will be deleted from our system immediately after our email has been sent.

## Will my information be shared with others?

In order to fulfill online purchase deliveries and maintain our website, FOREO contracts with selected third parties. These third parties may process your personal information under strict contractual confidentiality agreements, for the purpose of assisting FOREO in providing a quality online service.  
Otherwise, FOREO will never disclose, sell or transfer any personal information (names, addresses, email addresses, etc.) provided by our clients to a third party, except as might be required by law.

## Site Security

The FOREO website uses 128-bit encryption to protect any and all personal and / or credit card information you may submit online. However, while we take all reasonable and available security measures to protect your information, you accept that no online data transmission can be deemed 100% secure from access by unauthorized recipients and will thus not hold us responsible for any breach of security unless this is due to our negligence.

## Acceptance of Privacy Policy

When you choose to access this website, to sign up, register your warranty or use our online shop, you consent to our collection and use of your information as described in this privacy policy.  
FOREO reserves the right to periodically make changes to and / or update these privacy policies or practices. To keep yourself updated, you should review our privacy policy from time to time, in particular every time you use the FOREO online shop.
