About Cookies: a cookie is a small text file that contains a unique identification number that is transferred from a website to the hard drive of your computer so that the website may identify you and track your activities on the website. This allows ForeignLadies computers to identify your computer whenever you visit our site. ForeignLadies uses cookies to identify misuse and abuse of the system and block those intrusions. A cookie does not identify any personal information about you, such as your real name and address. ForeignLadies does not collect information about a given person using cookies. Because cookies are only text files, they cannot run on your computer, search your computer for other information, or transmit any of your information to anyone else. ForeignLadies uses cookies to determine in what state your computer workstation is located and thus improve speed of service. The use of cookies is considered an industry standard, and their use is incorporated into most major websites. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. However, you can reset your browser to notify you when you have received a cookie, or to refuse to accept cookies. ForeignLadies does not require you to use cookies but you may have difficulty using this service if you set your browser to not accept cookies.
