# FOXTEL

|  |   
A. General  
| 1.  | By using this Web Site you agree to the privacy policy of FOXTEL Management Pty Limited ("FOXTEL") as set out below.  
---|---  
2.  | FOXTEL will review this Privacy Statement periodically and reserves the right, at its discretion, to modify or remove portions of this Statement at any time. You should review this page periodically so that you are updated on any changes.  
3.  | FOXTEL recognises that your privacy is very important to you. For this reason, if we can (and you want to), you do not have to identify yourself when you use or access most of the features of the Web Site (http://www.foxtel.com.au).  
4.  | For the purposes of this Privacy Statement, Personal Information means information or an opinion (including information or an opinion forming part of a database), whether true or not, and whether recorded in a material form or not, about an individual whose identity is apparent, or can reasonably be ascertained, from the information or opinion.  
5.  | This Privacy Statement will not apply to web sites which are linked to this Web Site and over which FOXTEL has no control. You should check the privacy policy of any other web site you enter.  
6.  | In this Privacy Statement, a reference to "National Privacy Principles" means the National Principles for the Fair Handling of Personal Information.  
  
B. Collection  
1.  | From time to time, we may invite you to provide Personal Information to us by filling in details on-line. For example, we may ask you to provide Personal Information when you send us comments or enter competitions.  
---|---  
2.  | Subject to section 3 below, you are welcome to use the Web Site without ever providing us with your Personal Information, and you will not be penalised if you do not.  
3.  | Wherever possible, we will make the provision of Personal Information optional. However, for certain parts of the Web Site, we may require your Personal Information before you can enjoy that feature. For example, you will not be able to enter competitions without providing your name and address. If you do not wish to provide your Personal Information, you should not use these parts of the Web Site.  
4.  | If you do decide to provide us with Personal Information, at any time on the Web Site, we will collect and retain that Personal Information.  
5.  | By choosing to provide Personal Information on our Web Site, you consent to our collecting and retaining that Personal Information.  
6.  | We will only collect Personal Information if this is necessary for one of our functions or activities.  
7.  | We will collect Personal Information in a lawful and fair way, and not in an unreasonably intrusive way.  
  
C. Use  
1.  | When we collect Personal Information on our Web Site, we may use this information to:  
• provide you with the service you have requested, or meet the purpose for which the information was submitted;  
• streamline and personalise your experience within the Web Site, or tailor our information, services or products for you;  
• if necessary, and for legitimate reasons, identify who we are communicating with;  
• enable you to participate in promotions and other activities described at the Web Site, when you have indicated to us that you wish to do so;  
• contact you for direct marketing or other promotional purposes;  
• create aggregate data about our visitors, such as average age, gender and other statistics to provide to potential and existing stakeholders, and to allow for more efficient operation of this Web Site.  
---|---  
1.  | By choosing to provide Personal Information on our Web Site, you consent to our using your Personal Information as set out in section C(1) above.  
  
D. Disclosure  
1.  | Personal Information that you submit to our Web Site may be disclosed to a third party:  
(a) to the extent necessary, to provide the service you have requested or to meet the purpose for which it was submitted;  
(b) if we have informed you of the particular third party to whom it will be disclosed, at the time of collection;  
(c) in order to participate in activities with business partners and sponsors (whose identities will be explained to you when the relevant Personal Information is collected);  
(d) to the extent necessary to carry out the uses which are set out in section (C)(1) above;  
(e) if the disclosure is:  
|      | • required by law;  
---|---  
  | • permitted by the National Privacy Principles;  
  | • necessary to co-operate with a judicial process or a law enforcement agency;  
  | • necessary to protect and defend the rights or property of FOXTEL, this site, or participating sites; or  
  | • necessary to lessen or prevent unlawful activity or serious threats to individuals, public health or safety.  
3.  | If you tell us that you do not wish to have your Personal Information used or disclosed for a particular purpose, we will respect your wishes, unless this is not legally or practically possible.  
4.  | If you wish to have your Personal Information deleted from our records, or you wish us to cease using or disclosing your Personal Information you should contact Nicole Crabb on 9200 1000. You will not be penalised for requesting us to do this, and you will still be able to use our Web Site, but if you then decide to contribute your Personal Information to our Web Site you consent to our collecting, using and disclosing it as set out in this Privacy Policy, until such time as you request as to stop using or disclosing that information.  
  
E. Sensitive Information  
1.  | We will not collect, use or disclose sensitive information (such as information about racial or ethnic origins or political or religious beliefs) except with your specific consent or in the circumstances permitted in the National Privacy Principles.  
---|---  
  
F. Non Personal Information  
1.  | We may decide to collect, use and disclose non-personal information about you. For example, we may decide to disclose aggregate average data about visitors to our Web Site to current or potential stakeholders in FOXTEL.  
---|---  
2.  | This Web Site may stoore cookies on your web client in order to improve your future visits to the Web Site. By using cookies, we can track information about your usage of the site, to provide customised content. You may be able to configure your browser to disable cookies.  
  
G. Accuracy and Security  
1.  | We will take reasonable steps to ensure that the Personal Information we collect is accurate, complete and up-to-date.  
---|---  
2.  | We will take reasonable steps to protect Personal Information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access, modification and disclosure.  
3.  | We will destroy or permanently de-identify Personal Information which we no longer use.  
4.  | No data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be totally secure. We cannot warrant the security, integrity or privacy of Personal Information submitted to this Web Site.  
5.  | To the extent permitted by law, we exclude any liability for loss or damage caused by breaches of the security, integrity or privacy of this Web Site.  
  
E. Enquiries, Access and Correction  
1.  | If at any time you wish to know more about our privacy practices regarding the Web Site, you believe the website has not adhered to the principles as set above, or you wish to review or make corrections to your Personal Information held by us, please contact us via e-mail at feedback@foxtel.com.au. FOXTEL will use all commercially reasonable efforts to promptly deal with your enquiry.  
---|---  
  
F. Your Consent  
FOXTEL may, and you consent to, the use or disclosure of your Personal Information in circumstances which are:  
(a) set out in this Privacy Statement;  
(b) clearly explained to you at the time when the information is collected from you; or  
(c) permitted under the National Privacy Principles
