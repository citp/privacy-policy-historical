One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize Software Development by Foursquare Solutions Inc pages, or register with Software Development by Foursquare Solutions Inc site or services, a cookie helps Software Development by Foursquare Solutions Inc to recall your specific information on subsequent visits.
