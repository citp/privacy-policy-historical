Privacy Policy

Introduction
Your privacy is important to us. Below are details about the information we collect, and what we do with it.

In Our Mobile Apps
Our apps may collect automatic, non-personally-identifying analytics such as the length of time the app has been used. No personally identifying information is ever collected for any purpose unless expressly initiated by you, the user.

Data stored in our apps remains within the app and is not sent to our server or any other device unless specific action is taken by you, the user, to do so.

On Our Website
On some pages, you can order products, initiate support requests, or post comments. The types of personal information collected on these pages are:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Credit/Debit Card Information

The Way We Use Information
We use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order only to complete that order. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order.

  • We use the information provided when placing an order only to ship the product and to confirm delivery. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order.
  • We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not shared with outside parties.

Our Commitment to Data Security
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Opting Out of Mailings
If you wish to stop receiving newsletter and announcement mailings you can click the 'unsubscribe' link found at the bottom of any of these mailings.
