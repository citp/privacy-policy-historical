# Firstphera Privacy Policy - Free Indian Wedding Site

FirstPhera.com values the trust its customers place in the Site. Accordingly, FirstPhera.com adheres to the highest ethical standards in gathering, using and safeguarding customer information that is entrusted to the Site. 

By accessing this Site, certain information about you, such as Internet protocol (IP) addresses, navigation through the Site, the systems and programs used and the time spent, along with other similar information, may be stored on our servers. Such stored information may not specifically identify you, but may be used by us for website traffic analysis or other similar purposes as we may deem appropriate. 

When you register with our Site and provide unique identifying information, such as name, email-address, location or any other content that is posted by you including but not limited to text, images, music, information and such on your sub-site or through other services, such information may be used by us for statistical analysis and optimization of our products, services, sales and marketing programs. 

FirstPhera.com does not rent, sell or exchange information about its customers. Access to information about FirstPhera.com member / user is restricted to FirstPhera.com and its associates, affiliates, group companies and vendors of goods and services that accept payments for goods sold and services rendered, from FirstPhera.com 

FirstPhera.com, on receiving any customer personal information might provide access of such information or any part thereof to its employees, its associates, affiliates, group companies and Vendors of goods and services or to any other person or entity that requires access to this information, judged in the sole discretion of FirstPhera.com, to administer its business responsibilities, provide superior service and communicate offers on merchandise and services that FirstPhera.com believes will be of interest to its customers. 

For cases where you configure your sub-site as private, we will only provide authentication to visitors you have invited through our website sharing functionality and other visitors that are invited through your invited guests. In other cases where your Site is set up as public, it may be accessible by anyone who wishes to visit your Site. 

While we strive to keep all personal information confidential, we may divulge such information if illegal in law. 

We want you to feel confident about using FirstPhera.com to plan your wedding, so we are committed to protecting the information we collect. While no company can guarantee absolute security, we have implemented appropriate administrative, technical, and physical security procedures to help protect the personal information you provide to us. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure correct use of information, we will employ reasonable and current Internet security methods and technologies. 

Please note that we might change this policy from time to time based on user feedback or owing to changes in our company policy.
