## Information Collection

Westbrook Technologies, Inc. does not collect personal information from the users of the Site. However, emailed requests and inquiries are passed to the appropriate Westbrook Technologies, Inc. personnel, who may retain records in the ordinary course of business.

We are committed to children’s privacy and do not intentionally collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you are younger than 13, please ask a parent or guardian for help in accessing and using the Site.

## Cookies

“ Cookies” are routinely used by entities engaged in E- commerce, including Westbrook Technologies, Inc.. A cookie is a piece of data stored on a user's hard drive through the Web browser program containing information about the user which enables us to track and personalize the Site to enhance your online experience. You can avoid cookies by configuring your browser to reject them or at least notify you when a cookie is being placed on your hard drive.

## Log Files

Like most other online entities, Westbrook Technologies, Inc. collects technical information from a user’s computer when that user requests a Web page while visiting the Site. The information tells Westbrook Technologies, Inc. the user’s Internet Protocol address, type of browser, the address of any linking Web site, and other technical information. Westbrook Technologies, Inc. occasionally uses a third party to collect and analyze this information. However, we do not sell or rent the information to any third party.
