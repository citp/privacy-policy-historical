# FileFormat.Info Privacy Policy

* * *

## Automatically Gathered Information

When you visit the FileFormat.Info, the following information is automatically gathered:

  * Your Internet location (IP address)
  * Which pages you visit on our site
  * From which page you came to our site
  * Which software you use to visit our site and its configuration



All this information is recorded and tracked.

## Information Voluntarily Provided by You

During the course of your visit to FileFormat.Info, you may provide additional information, including:

  * Search queries
  * Files
  * Email address and other contact information
  * Feedback, additions & requests



All this information is recorded and tracked.

## Information Sharing

The following information is available to the public:

  * [Searches](https://web.archive.org/search/recent.htm)
  * [Browsers](https://web.archive.org/about/browser.htm)
  * [Referrers](https://web.archive.org/about/trackback.htm)



All other information is for internal use only. It is not shared with any third parties.
