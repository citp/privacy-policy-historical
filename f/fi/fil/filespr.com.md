# Privacy Policy

Filespr.com is tended to user confidential and our principles described below in maintaining, protect the privacy information, trust and confidence of user.

**Information Gathering and Use**

Any message that you put in this site with the aim of subscribing to any of our services are used exactly for these aims, at any time you can cancel from our services in which case the email address is firmly removed from our database.

Filespr.com does not gather or use any personal information when you enter on our website. Filespr.com may, nevertheless, step by step, collect non-personal information accessible from your browser when you need a page from our website. This information consist of your IP address, your browser type, the URL needed for your browser and the date and time of your request. We use this material to update the use of our website and to raise the quality of our service.

As opportunity offers and in the future, non-personal information we collect may be divided with third-parties to give more relevant information and advertising to our user. For example, we may share the users that have looked for a specific files or clicked on a specific advertising posted on our site.

We adhere to existing legal processes and provide data as required by legislation to protect and defend the policy of our company, and in some cases, to protect the confidence of users and the public.
