# Filmaffinity Privacy Policy - FilmAffinity

| 

  * FILMAFFINITY takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

  * FILMAFFINITY is a website created by Pablo Kurt Verdu and Daniel Nicolas, both web design professionals, movie-goers and owners of the FILMAFFINTY copyright and the Registered Trademark Movie Soulmates, movieaffinity and filmaffinity.  
We are commited to maintaining the privacy of our users of our website and we strive to carry this out in the strictist form and abiding by current legal regulations.

  * FILMAFFINITY guarantees that all the information gathered through the webforms are stored in a database that is electronically protected. We have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and help prevent unauthorized access, maintain data security, and correctly use the information we collect online.

  * Under no circumstances will this information be shared with a third party.

  * The database is registered under and follows the regulations of the Data Protection Agency, ruled under European Union Laws.

  * To have access to the service of personal film-taste affinity that this website offers it is necessary to register with FILMAFFINITY. When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, country and personal movie ratings.

  * FILMAFFINITY uses information for the following purpose: to find who are your "Movie Soulmates" and helping people to find out what is the opinion about movies from this "Movie Soulmates". Your "Movie Soulmates" will only have access to knowing your country and movie ratings. If you do not wish to show your name you are welcome to register under a nickname. Age and personal email will not be known by your "Movie Soulmates", unless you authorized it.

  * To enhance your experience with our sites, many of our web pages use "cookies." Cookies are text files we place in your computer's browser to store your preferences. Cookies, by themselves, do not tell us your e-mail address or other personally identifiable information unless you choose to provide this information to us by, for example, registering at one of our sites. However, once you choose to furnish the site with personally identifiable information, this information may be linked to the data stored in the cookie. We use cookies to understand site usage and to improve the content and offerings on our sites. For example, we may use cookies to personalize your experience at our web pages (e.g. to recognize you by name when you return to our site), save your password in password-protected areas, and enable you to use shopping carts on our sites. We also may use cookies to offer you products, programs, or services.
