What kind of information does Filson collect?

We collect information, such as name, e-mail and postal addresses, phone and credit card numbers from you when you visit our website or our store, place an order online, by mail, or by phone, make an in-store purchase, save your information with us online, or participate in a contest, promotion or survey. We collect and store certain information automatically whenever you visit our website. For example, we collect your IP address, browser information and reference site domain. We maintain a record of your product interests and the purchases you make online, by mail, or by phone and in our store, and may also acquire information about you from our joint marketing partners and from unrelated third parties.

How does Filson use my information?

When you place an order or make a purchase from Filson, we use your personal information to process your order, send you e-mails to confirm your order or to contact you via phone, postal mail or e-mail if we have other questions regarding your order or purchase.

When you place an order, make a purchase or save your information online, we will use your e-mail and postal address to send you information about our products, services, and promotions and to help us learn more about your shopping preferences. If you participate in a promotion (online, by phone, by mail, or at our retail store), we may use your personal information to send you e-mails, postal mail, or telephone (including text messages) regarding our products, services, contests and promotions. From time to time, we may use this information to contact you via e-mail, postal mail, or telephone to learn more about your shopping preferences. You always have the choice not to receive marketing information or calls.

We may share your personally-identifiable information, including your name, address, email address, as well as a record of any transactions you conduct on our website or offline with us with a third-party advertising partner and its service providers in order to deliver to you banner advertisements and other advertising tailored to your interests when you visit certain websites. Our advertising partner will make the data we provide anonymous. To choose not to have this information used by our third party advertising partner, click here.

In addition, we may use information about your product interests and purchases from us to help us improve our catalog and site design and your catalog, online and in-store shopping experiences. We may contact you, subject to the choices you have made, via e-mail, postal mail, or telephone, to conduct market research and learn more about how we can improve our product and service offerings.

To serve you better, we may combine information you give us and information about your product interests and purchases with information from third parties, including demographic information and information that is publicly available. We use that combined information to enhance and personalize your shopping experience with us, and to communicate with you in accordance with this policy.

We do not authorize any of these service providers to make any other use of your information.

What are cookies? How does Filson use cookies on its site?

A cookie is a small data file that websites often store on your computer's hard drive when you visit their sites. A cookie may contain information, such as a unique user ID that websites use to track the pages of the sites you've visited. We use cookies but do not store personally identifiable information in your cookie.

We use cookies in order to improve your shopping experience. Cookies enable us to keep track of your order as you shop on our site.

Through our use of cookies, we also track and maintain the identity of the website you visited immediately prior to visiting our website. We do not otherwise track any information about your use of other websites.

We use the services of a third party to help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising and determine how customers use our website. For this activity, we use web beacons and cookies provided by this third party, which enable us to collect the following types of information: pages visited, products viewed and/or purchased, search term usage, and other common website metrics. This information helps us to learn things like what pages are most attractive to our customers, which of our products most interest our customers, and what kinds of offers our customers like to see. Although our third party service provider collects this information on our behalf, we and the service provider who operates our website on our behalf control how the information may and may not be used.

How do I know my order information is secure?

Protecting your order information is important to us. We use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology to protect your online order information as it is transmitted over the Internet.

Some versions of browsers and some firewalls don't permit communication through secure servers like the ones we use to process orders at Filson. To check the security of your connection, look at the lower left-hand corner of your browser window after accessing our server. If you see an unbroken key or a closed lock (depending on your browser), then SSL is active. You can also double-check by looking at the URL line of your browser. When accessing our secure server, the first characters of the site address will change from "http" to "https."

If your browser or firewall doesn't support SSL, you will not be able to connect to our server, which means you won't be able to mistakenly place an order through an unsecured connection. If you cannot access the secure server for any reason, please call us at 1-866-860-8906 and we would be happy to help you by telephone.
