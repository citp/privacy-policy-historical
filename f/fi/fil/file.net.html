Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. We don't collect email addresses. We don't send out spam.


Who Are We?

File.net, an open forum for sharing information about files, is one of the oldest sites on the Web dedicated to communicating detailed, carefully vetted information about the principal files used in software that runs on Microsoft Windows.

Ten years ago, we were among the first experts to analyze and classify Windows files according to their properties and behavior. Nowadays, our analysis tools are used all over the world by anti-virus software publishers, the military, government agencies, and law enforcement, and for special projects.

We have been members of the Association of Shareware Professionals since 1998. We also develop educational software for the blind and visually impaired, and take part in a variety of charitable activities.


Information That We Collect

We may collect non-identifying personal information from you through our website.

IP Address. As is common practice for most Internet sites, our server automatically records users' IP information in our logs. These log files may include information such as your request, your IP address, browser type, browser language, and the date and time of your request. However, we do not use this information to individually identify visitors to www.file.net.

Personal Information.  This website does not collect, save or store any personal information from visitors to www.file.net. We do not collect personal information without your knowledge or consent. You are free to use a pseudonym when posting a comment.

Cookies. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user's computer for record-keeping purposes. Cookies cannot damage user files, nor can they read information from a user's hard drive. Cookies allow websites to "remember" a user's past activity on a website. Like many other companies, we or certain embedded third-party services like Google Analytics may use cookies to collect non-personal information.

Embedded Advertisements. We use reputable third party vendors like Google to provide advertising on our site. These vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits. You may visit www.aboutads.info to opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for interest-based advertising.

User Communications.  If you contact us with inquiries or requests, we will retain copies of these communications in order to process and respond to them.


What Information Do We Collect From Children?
