Safe and secure payment gateway

findmyfare.com uses a technology called SSL (secure sockets layer) with 128-bit encryption key to keep your personal information safe and confidential as it travels from your computer to our servers. SSL is an encryption technology that scrambles/encodes a message so that only the recipient can unscramble the data. All of the ordering information you provide is encrypted by your SSL enabled browser BEFORE it gets sent.
All the payment related information such as Credit Card Details are collected and stored with the relevant banks while the rest of the information such as Name, Email, Phone number that had been collected here is for findmyfare.com use only.
