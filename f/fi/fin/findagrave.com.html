Privacy Statement

All users who receive an ad served at the Find A Grave website retain complete anonymity where Find A Grave is concerned. This information is obtained solely for the purpose of site statistics generation, targeting ads and measuring a given ad's effectiveness. Please note that this policy applies to all webpages served within the findagrave.com domain as well as the auxillary domains including findagrave.org, findagrave.net, cemeteryrecords.com, cemeteryrecordsonline.com and remembermyfamily.com..
 
This privacy policy does not extend to any site or sites that a user may navigate to should the user exit the Find A Grave website by clicking on an advertisement or other link. Intentional misuse of the Find A Grave website voids this privacy statement. Intentional misuse includes but is not limited to posting illicit images, posting information or images known to be inaccurate and posting offensive or inflammatory 'virtual flowers'.
