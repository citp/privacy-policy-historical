# Privacy Policy – FIFPlay

FIFPlay’s privacy policy covers all of FIFPlay (www.fifplay.com) sites and FIFPlay is committed to respecting the privacy rights and concerns of all users of the FIFPlay websites where this Privacy Policy is posted. FIFPlay kindly asks you to read this statement carefully before using or proceeding to access our websites (www.fifplay.com). 

This policy statement provides answers to the following questions: 

**Collection of Information**  
We collect personal information about you that can be used to identify and contact you. We do collect information from the forms we have on our websites (comments, survey, registration, newsletter, subscriptions forms and cookies). 

**How We Use Your Personal Information**  
The personal information we are collecting from you will help us to provide you better and more tailored products and services, alert you of new products, services, offers and promotions, surveys, opportunities or informations . We also use your e-mail address to send you messages and newsletter about our serices/products (FIFPlay), FIFA/PES/Football Referee related topics and other announcements. We may also use your personal information to contact you when necessary. 

**Sharing of Information**  
We may use your personal information to provide statistics and analyses of our users for our partners, advertisers and other third parties. 

* This Privacy Policy may change from time to time and we will post all changes on this page.
