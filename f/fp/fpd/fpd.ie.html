Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Do people actually win when they enter the competitions?

Yes they do! Free Prizedraws has free prize draws that close every week and month so we give away hundreds of free prizes to our members each year. Our winners page shows all the winners over the past few years who entered in all the various competitions that we have on the site. Free Prizedraws is a legitimate, regulated, fun and free to enter competition website.

Q. So how do we know if we have won?

Once a winner has been pulled we email the winner within 96 hours. The email will arrive from winners@fpd.ie. To make sure that you receive this email, enter this address into your contacts to ensure you won’t miss the chance to win the fantastic prizes.

Q. How do I claim my prize?

Once you have received an email from winners@fpd.ie you have to reply to it with the postal address that you sign up with. Once this is done and everything is correct your prize will be dispatched within the next 28 days.
