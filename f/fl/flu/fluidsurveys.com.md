# Survey Privacy Policy

## We will not share your data in an way, shape, or form.

Collected data
    

When you register on Chide.it we collect very basic information including but not limited to: email and name. For the paid accounts, we ask for more information including but not limited to your address. This personal information is private, we will not share it.

back to top

Collected data on your surveys
    

The data collected by your surveys is yours. We will not use it or share it in any way shape or form.

back to top

Cookies
    

Cookies are required in Chide.it, we use it to identify unique visitors, to provider per-user customization, and to make Chide.it easier to use. We don't share our cookies, nor we use cookies to track your behavior on other sites.

back to top

How we use the data collected
    

We reserve the right to contact you, regarding your account or any other matter regarding your use of Chide.it. With your authorization, we may use some information collected from you to help diagnose technical problems, and improve the quality and types of services delivered. We may use and share non-identifiable aggregated usage and statistical information. We may also share information with third parties in limited circumstances including when complying with legal processes, preventing fraud or imminent harm, ensuring security of network and services and due to violation of the terms of service.

back to top

What you can do
    

Your data is yours, you can delete it at anytime. If you'd like your account to be deleted, please do send us an email at info@chide.it.(we may for a time, maintain a residual copy of your data in our backups).

back to top

Security
    

Chide.it servers are protected with generally available security technologies, including firewalls and data encryption. These technologies are designed to prevent unauthorized access, but no guarantee can be made that your information and data will be secure from intrusions and unauthorized released to third parties.

back to top

Contact
    

Any questions regarding this policy should be addressed to info@chide.it. If a question comes up not covered by this policy, we will answer it remembering that your data belongs to you.

These policies are effective as of July 27, 2008. Chide.it Inc. reserves the right to change this policy at any time by notifying it's users of the existence of a new policy. The policies outlined in this document are not intended to and do not create any contractual or other legal rights in or on behalf of any party.
