Privacy Statement for Fluke Networks

Fluke Networks respects your privacy, we do not rent, sell or exchange customer information including email addresses. The following Privacy Policy discloses our current information gathering and dissemination practices for our web site and for our customer email subscription program. Please note that this statement is subject to change, so we suggest you refer to it any time you may have questions regarding Fluke Networks' privacy practices.

www.FlukeNetworks.com

Information this site gathers
The Fluke Networks web site logs IP addresses and browser types, but we do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable. Therefore, you can browse this site without entering any personal information, and remain anonymous during your visit. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is also used to gather broad demographic information, and to help identify you.

This site also includes a variety of registration and order forms that may ask for particular information about our visitors such as name, e-mail address and demographic information as well as product interest or usage information. Information gathered through these means is used to learn more about our visitors and in some instances, to provide them with requested information using their requested form of communications.

Please also note that certain countries may not allow information (such as e-mail) to be transmitted outside of that country or region. If you have any concerns about this practice, we suggest you do not use the forms or contact mechanisms included in this web site, and use an alternative form of contacting us.

Cookies
This site uses cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site. If a shopping cart feature is used, we would also use cookies to keep track of your shopping cart. If advertisements are used in the site, we would also use cookies to make sure you don't see the same ad repeatedly.

This site's registration serves multiple purposes including product registration, MyVision registration, and support ticket registration which may require information like name, e-mail address, and some demographic data. Fluke Networks' Email Subscription Program

Fluke Networks offers email delivery of timely and relevant information about our products, promotions, events and technical resources to those who request it. Users opt-in or subscribe to receive these communications by selecting the content categories and areas of interest you wish to receive regularly from Fluke Networks. We do not rent, sell or exchange email addresses.

Contact Frequency
Your positive experience with our email subscription program is our priority. Therefore, in addition to sending you only the types of communications you have opted-in to receive, we also regulate the number of times we will email you within these designated preferences. You will be emailed no more than THREE times per monthly calendar. This means that you may receive three email communications relevant to your subscription interests during a calendar month.

There are the following exceptions to this rule in order that Fluke Networks meets its legal, contractual, financial and safety obligations to our customers:

  • Gold SuperVision Support notices as required by the customer's Gold program agreement including product updates, upgrades and "members only" promotions
  • Product upgrades or bug fixes to registered product owners
  • Product safety notices to the product users
  • Email confirmations of contest entries/winners as noted in contest rules
  • Email communications from retail sales organizations who carry Fluke Networks equipment. Note: Fluke Networks does not distribute user contact information so any direct contact with third party organizations fall outside of our email subscription program and policies.
  • One-on-one correspondence with Fluke Networks' employees
How Fluke Networks' Email Subscription Program works
You can opt-in to receive email updates when you register on MyVision. Upon completion of registration and receipt of permission from the user to receive the specified types of email communications, Fluke Networks will communicate to you only those communications preferences positively indicated on the MyVision registration form.

Subscription Pre-selections
Existing Eye On Networks subscribers will continue to receive the e-newsletter in its new technical-only format unless you opt-out by deselecting the field provided. These subscribers are also pre-selected to receive separate email communications on the content previously delivered in Eye On Networks, namely New Product Announcements, Promotions and Events. New MyVision users are pre-selected to receive just our technical e-newsletter Eye On Networks until you change your preferences.

Opting out of Email Subscription Program
To opt-out of receiving all or part of the email communications that you have expressly requested do one of the following:

  • From an email you receive from Fluke Networks:
    • Click on the Unsubscribe link and you will be unsubscribed from all future email communications in our program.
    • Click Update My Account, and you will be able to immediately update your Subscription Preferences.
  • You can also modify your subscription preferences or unsubscribe by returning to the MyVision site where this information is maintained. Follow the instructions on the MyVision Email Subscription Preferences page to modify or unsubscribe to email communicatations.
  • Send an Email to: info@flukenetworks.com and indicate which categories you would like to unsubscribe from.
  • Call 800-283-5853 and indicated which categories you would like to unsubscribe to.

Email Fraud Security Notice
Fluke Networks does not permit:

  • Spamming: sending unsolicited advertising to those with whom Fluke does not have an existing business relationship; posting off-topic advertising in newsgroups.
  • Spoofing: using a return email address that is not the sender’s valid reply address; sending an email message that does not contain enough information to enable the recipient to identify the sender.
  • Passive Spamming: promoting a website hosted by Fluke by spamming from another source.
  • Trolling: posting controversial messages in newsgroups to generate responses.
  • Mail Bombing: Inundating a user with email without any serious intent to correspond; sending large or multiple files to a user.
If you suspect that you have been the victim of any of these types of conduct, please email info@flukenetworks.com.

Fluke Networks reserves the right to seek the imposition of criminal and civil penalties for any unacceptable online activities, including punitive damages, under any applicable law, including the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (18 U.S.C. § 1030), the Electronic Communications Privacy Act (18 U.S.C. § 2511), the Lanham Act (15 U.S.C. § 1125(a), 15 U.S.C. § 1114), the Uniform Trade Secrets Act (RCW 19.108.010), the Unsolicited Electronic E-mail Act, and the Washington Unfair Business-Consumer Protection Act.
