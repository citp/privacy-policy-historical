Send Events

Whenever readers use the "send event" function to forward emails to others, the recipients are NOT added to any distribution lists.

Phone Numbers

Flavorpill Productions collects telephone numbers through its sms text messaging functionality so that it can pass this information onto its technology partner IPSH.NET for delivery. These numbers will never be leased, rented, sold, or disclosed to third parties.

Flavorpill Productions uses IP addresses and email addresses to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movements, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.

Sharing

Flavorpill Productions shares aggregated demographic profiles with our partners and advertisers. This is not linked to any personal information that can identify any individual person.

Links

Both the Flavorpill Productions mailers and websites contain links to other sites. Flavorpill Productions is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. This privacy statement applies solely to information directly collected by Flavorpill Productions.

Security

Flavorpill Productions takes every precaution to protect users' information. All user-related information is restricted in our offices. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job are granted access to personally identifiable information. Furthermore, all employees are kept up-to-date on our security and privacy practices.
