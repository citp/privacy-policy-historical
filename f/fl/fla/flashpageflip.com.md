# FlashPageFlip | Create Digital Publications (HTML5 and FLASH)

The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to tell you what kind of information we may gather about you when you visit our site and how we may use that information.

When you place an order with us, we will collect certain information related to that transaction such as the location from which you are requesting the transaction, the product or service involved, the quantity or amount of the product and certain personally identifiable information such as your name and contact information.

Personally identifiable information will never knowingly appear in public areas of our website. We will not share any of your information with any third parties.

Credit Card information provided by the customer is used by 2Checkout or PayPal, which handles Credit Card transactions on behalf of dp Network LLC. for all FlashPageFlip.com products and services. We can't obtain the customer's Credit Card information from 2Checkout or PayPal. Credit Card numbers are confidential between the customer and 2Checkout or PayPal. For more information about 2Checkout or PayPal and their Privacy Policy please visit their website at [www.2Checkout.com](http://www.2checkout.com/) \- [www.PayPal.com](http://www.paypal.com/).
