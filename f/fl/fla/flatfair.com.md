# Privacy Policy

Owing customers's information, FlatFair.com protect personal information and will never sell or rent this information. We will not share it in any ways according to what issued below unless the customers authorize us to do it.

 **Cookies and IP Addresses**

FlatFair.com is firmly committed to our customers' privacy. Your IP address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart and gather general demographic information. We use cookies to keep track of your shopping cart and for other purposes like personalizing the site. Cookies are a small piece of information stored by your internet browser to your computer's hard drive. Your browser is usually set up to initially accept cookies. If you set your browser to reject cookies, the site will not be able to recognize your cart, your account, or any personalization. We do not and cannot use cookies to gather your confidential information that may be stored on your hard drive. Third Party advertisers also help us display tailored content to our customers and serve ads on our behalf. These advertisers may also utilize cookies and/or web beacons to measure effectiveness, efficiencies, etc. of the sites.

 **Personal Information**

Our site uses forms for you to request information, products and services. We collect your contact information, including addresses, email addresses, phone numbers, and financial information. Collected information is used for the benefit of FlatFair.com to send information to customers about orders, to fulfill orders, to process and collect payments, to send promotional marketing material about our company and partners, and to contact customers about orders when necessary. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our sites. We use this data to tailor your experiences at our sites, to show you content that we think you might be interested in, and to display the content according to your preferences.
