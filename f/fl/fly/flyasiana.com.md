# Asiana Airlines - Privacy Policy

At Asiana Airlines, we are committed to protecting your privacy and creating a safe secure online environment for anyone who visits and purchases on the Asiana Airlines website. Asiana Airlines will not disclose personal information to third parties without the user's consent, except as provided in this policy, and will take appropriate measures to safeguard the personal information. When Asiana Airlines requests sensitive information, commercially reasonable steps will be taken to establish a secure connection with the user's web browser to assure privacy. 

From time to time, Asiana Airlines request user's personal information to provide a specific product or service. This information may include a user's name, shipping address, billing address, telephone numbers, travel details, credit card information and e-mail address. 

Your e-mail address will be used to receive scheduled Asiana Club e-mail newsletters, special offers, and other similar e-mail promotional information. 

Asiana Airlines uses cookies to collect general usage information to customize website services for the users. Information collected by cookies may be disclosed to third parties in the form of aggregate data that does not describe or identify any individual user. 

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please send an e-mail to Asiana Airlines. ([customer_us@flyasiana.com](mailto:customer_us@flyasiana.com))
