We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by FAD 3.0.

Whenever FAD 3.0 changes its privacy policy, we will post those changes to this privacy statement, and other places we deem appropriate.
