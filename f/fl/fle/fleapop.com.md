# FleaPop – Buy and sell home decor, furniture and antiques

## Collection of Information

When you register and download the Site, FleaPop will collect the following information (" _Registration Information_ "): 

  * Name 
  * Email address 
  * Mailing address 
  * Username and password 
  * PayPal account username and address (if different from your email address) 
  * Login information for any social media accounts you wish to connect to your account 



In addition, when you use the Site, the following types of information may all be logged automatically (" _Usage Information_ "): 

  * Transaction related information about the types of Stores you visit and purchases you make 
  * Type of mobile device you use 
  * Operating system of your mobile device 
  * Wireless carrier 
  * Browser type 
  * Non-precise geographic location (e.g. zip code and city) 
  * Technical information about your device, system and Site software, and peripherals 



Finally, FleaPop may collect precise geographic location in order to provide you with information about local stores and pick up availability for items in which you are interested. Before precise location information is collected, you will be asked whether you allow precise location tracking. You may decline to allow this tracking, but if you do so, certain features of the Service may not be available to you. 

## Third Party Advertisements 

We may also use third parties to serve ads on the Site. Certain third parties may automatically collect information about your visits to our Site and other websites, your IP address, your ISP, the browser you use our Site (but not your name, address, email address, or telephone number). They do this using cookies, clear gifs, or other technologies. Information collected may be used, among other things, to deliver advertising targeted to your interests and to better understand the usage and visitation of our Site and the other sites tracked by these third parties. This policy does not apply to, and we are not responsible for, cookies or clear gifs in third party ads, and we encourage you to check the privacy policies of advertisers and/or ad services to learn about their use of cookies and other technology. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here: <http://www.aboutads.info/choices/>. 

## Use of Information

FleaPop may use your Registration Information to help facilitate transactions into which you enter and, in our sole discretion, to resolve disputes between you and other Users. We may also use your Registration Information as necessary to investigate fraud, protect our legal rights, and otherwise provide the Service. 

FleaPop may use Usage Information to facilitate the provision of software updates, product support and other services to you (if any), including by sending email or text messages to your wireless phone, tablet, or other mobile device (each a " _Device_ "). You also grant FleaPop an irrevocable, perpetual, royalty-free right and license to use your Usage Information to enhance our products and services and to develop new products and services. 

FleaPop may use your precise geographical location information in order to provide you with information about local stores and pick up availability for items in which you are interested. 

You also agree that FleaPop may share any information you provide, including your Registration Information and Usage Information, with affiliates and related companies of FleaPop and to third parties providing services on behalf of FleaPop and to transfer such information in the event of a sale, merger, dissolution, bankruptcy, corporate reformation or other corporate restructuring of FleaPop, or in the event that FleaPop sells, assigns, or transfers substantially all of its rights in the Site. 

## Disclosure of Information

FleaPop may disclose some of your Registration Information, such as your contact information, to other Users in order to complete a transaction initiated by you. We will not disclose your name or physical address. 

FleaPop does not disclose any of your information with third parties other than as follows: (i) to the extent necessary to comply with applicable law and our legal obligations, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process; (ii) when FleaPop believes in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect its rights, your safety or the safety of others; (iii) with third party vendors, consultants and other services providers who work for us and need access to your information to do that work; (iv) to investigate fraud, or respond to a government request; (v) in connection with or during negotiation of any merger, financing, acquisition, or dissolution, transaction or proceeding involving sale, transfer, divestiture, or disclosure of all or a portion of our business or assets to another company. FleaPop does not have any access to any of the personal information or credit card information that you have provided to PayPal in order to register for their services. You should be familiar with the privacy practices of PayPal before providing such information. 

FleaPop may also share aggregated or deidentified information. In addition, FleaPop may elect to create accounts with certain third party sites, such as Facebook, (" _Third Party Accounts_ ") and you expressly provide FleaPop with your consent and permission to share information and posts that you make via the Site available via our Third Party Accounts, for example, FleaPop may release and distribute information about your store and your products and listings via FleaPop’s Facebook pages. 

We allow third party ad networks (including ad servers, ad agencies, ad technology vendors and research firms) to place ads on our Site. These ads may be targeted to users based on anonymized information inferred from the user’s other online activity. We do not provide personally identifiable information to any third party ad network. 

## Children

The Site is not directed to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us at privacy@fleapop.com. We will delete such information from our files within a reasonable time. 

## Security

FleaPop takes commercially reasonable steps to help protect your information against loss, misuse and unauthorized access, or disclosure. **HOWEVER, FleaPop expressly disclaims any representation or warranty, whether express or implied, with respect to ensuring, guaranteeing or otherwise offering any definitive promise of security in connection with your Information.**

## Your Consent

By using the Site, you are consenting to the use and processing of your information as set forth in these Terms, all of which activities will take place in the United States. If you reside outside the U.S. your information will be transferred to the U.S., and processed and stored there under U.S. privacy standards. By using the Site and providing information to us, you consent to such transfer to, and processing in, the U.S. 

## Contact FleaPop

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Site, or have questions about FleaPop’s practices, please contact FleaPop via email at privacy@fleapop.com.
