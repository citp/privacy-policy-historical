#### Cookies

Our website uses cookies to ensure that it is functioning. Cookies are small data packages that the website exchanges with your web browser.

This section describes the cookies we use and the purposes of this usage.

We use session cookies for supporting the user session. This type of cookies helps you get authenticated and work under your account. Besides, cookies used by the partner company Cleverbridge are required for purchasing on the website.

Marketing cookies are data we receive from the Google Analytics system and use for collecting and recording website session statistics.

FlippingBook Limited also uses the Google Analytics remarketing feature, which can take users back to a website they have visited before using special banners placed on other websites. To use this feature, FlippingBook Limited uses a third-party cookie from DoubleClick that enables remarketing in Google Inc products such as AdWords.

If you prefer, it is possible to block some or all cookies, or even to delete cookies that have already been placed, but you need to be aware that you might lose access to some of the functions of that website.

You can restrict or block the web browser cookies that are placed on your device through your browser settings; the Help function within your browser should tell you how. Alternatively, you can visit www.aboutcookies.org, which contains comprehensive information on how to do this for a wide variety of desktop browsers.

We use this information for your convenience exclusively. We guarantee that we neither receive any personal data nor transfer any data we receive to any third party except for those mentioned under this clause. In addition, you can disable cookie exchange in your web browser’s settings, which will affect website experience and render your personal profile unusable.
