Cookies - What are they?

A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your computer through your web browser.

Cookies - How do we use them?

We use the following types of cookies throughout our site:

Analytical and Tracking cookies - These are used for information such as tracking your path through our site, and recording sites that referred you to our site. Along with our own tracking cookies, we use Google Analytics to log and analyse information about the usage of our site.

Social plugins - We use plugins from social networks such as Facebook, Twitter, and Google+. These plugins set cookies that allow you to like/follow/+1 certain pages on our site. It is likely that these cookies also store information about other sites you have visited that include the same social plugins. We have no control over what is stored in these cookies as they are set by third parties.

Functional cookies - We store cookies throughout the lifetime of your 'session' on our site. These are used to track your progress through our reservation system, and for remembering your search criteria, etc. These cookies have a short lifetime and are automatically deleted when your browsing session has ended.

Cookies do not contain any personal information, and Cheapholidays.com Ltd does not use cookies to collect personal information. If you wish, you can change your internet browser settings to refuse cookies, however this may affect the functionality of the websites you visit that rely on cookies to operate correctly.

Cookies - Refusing and removing cookies stored by websites

You can reject certain types of cookies (or all cookies) by modifying your browser settings. This can be done for specific sites or for all sites. For more information on controlling cookies in your browser, please check your browser's help pages (normally by pressing F1) or search the Internet.
Please note that rejecting or removing cookies can have adverse effects on your ability to browse certain websites.
