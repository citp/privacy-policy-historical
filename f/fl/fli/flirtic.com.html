Cookies

Cookies are required to use Flirtic's website properly. Flirtic uses both session ID cookies and persistent cookies. A cookie is a small data file that can include an anonymous unique identifier that is stored on the computer hard drive of the user. Session cookies are temporary cookies that remain in a file of the browser until logged out of the website. Session cookies do not store any personally identifying information, only a unique visitor ID that is used to improve the Flirtic user experience. Persistent cookies remain in the cookie file of the browser after leaving the Flirtic website. Persistent cookies also do not store any personally identifiable information, only preferences such as "Remember Me" so users do not have to log in to the site for every visit.

Please be aware that cookies may be required to complete certain functions on the Flirtic website. Flirtic makes no warranties of site performance if the user changes browser settings or employs other actions to disable cookies. Flirtic will not allow third parties to place or recognize cookies on your browser while you are browsing Flirtic website.
