FLStudioMusic has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

FLStudioMusic’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
