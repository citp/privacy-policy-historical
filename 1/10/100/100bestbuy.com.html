HOW TO MEASURE YOUR FEET LENGTH?
Measure in bare foot
Set your foot on a sheet of paper and draw the outline of your foot with a pencil held vertically
Draw a mark at the end of your heel and the tip of your longest toe, also mark the sides of the widest part of the foot
For the foot length, measure between heel and toe pencil marks
If you find one foot's measurement is larger than the other, always use the larger foot size
