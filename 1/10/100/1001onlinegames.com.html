We use this information to help diagnose problems, view statistics for our website and allows us to improve the overall user-experience.

Such information is used only for the purpose of assisting you with, and validating, your inquiry or submission.

Forms

By using our search form, you enter a "search term" which will be used to search through the content of our website. This search term will be stored internally, in combination with your IP address. We may use this information to find out what our visitors are searching for so we can determine if our content matches the visitor's interest. We delete this information typically every few weeks and it will only be used to help us improve our website.
