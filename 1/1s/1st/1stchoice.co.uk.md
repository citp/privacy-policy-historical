# Privacy policy - 1st Choice Spares

Your personal contact details are used to allow our members to contact you with no obligation quotes for parts you may request using 1st Choice Spares or to inform you of any new developments or services available from 1st Choice.  We may send you emails from time to time about 1st Choice and services which we feel may be of interest to you.

No credit card or debit card information is stored by 1st Choice.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads (e.g. Google Adsense) when you visit our website. These companies may use information via **[DoubleClick DART cookie](http://www.google.com/intl/en/privacy/)** (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and would like to know your options in relation to·not having this information used by these companies, [Click Here. ](http://www.networkadvertising.org/)

The **[DoubleClick DART](http://www.google.com/intl/en/privacy/) **cookie is used by Google in the ads served on publisher websites displaying AdSense for content ads. When users visit an AdSense publisher's website and either view or click an ad, a cookie may be dropped on that end user's browser. The data gathered from these cookies will be used to help AdSense publishers serve and manage the ads on their site(s) and across the web more effectively.
