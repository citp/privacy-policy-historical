Applications used to be simple and monolithic; now they’ve grown to be distributed and highly complex. IT operations and development teams struggle to quickly find root cause when performing Java performance monitoring and .NET monitoring. They find themselves hunting for root cause and facing lack of visibility.

AppDynamics is built from the ground up for production environments that are highly distributed. It reduces Mean-Time-to-Resolution by 90% and helps App Ops teams gain complete visibility. And it’s a fraction of the cost of traditional enterprise solutions. AppDynamics is the right application performance monitoring and management solution to enable these Ops and Dev teams to succeed in today’s challenging environments.

The right application monitoring solution can ensure that you’re able to experience complete uptime and availability, even when your organization has agile release cycles and a high-volume production environment. AppDynamics can help you:

  • Manage your production environment with low overhead. Whether you’re doing tomcat monitoring, JBoss monitoring, Glassfish monitoring, websphere monitoring, weblogic monitoring, or .NET monitoring, AppDynamics can ensure that your JVMs and Windows OS instances are monitoring with less than 2% overhead.
  • Give your APM strategy a “brain.” AppDynamics learns the behavior of your application, so it only triggers alerts when it senses true performance problems. This is how we’re able to perform in production, 24/7 while keeping overhead very low.
  • Give your team an easy to use, affordable APM solution. Log files and basic JMX monitoring are great, but they don’t scale. As your application becomes more complex, your team will require a simple, affordable application performance management solution. That’s what AppDynamics gives thousands of companies all across the world.

The success of our customers tells you everything you need to know. Thousands of companies all over the world have successfully deployed our application performance monitoring solution to troubleshoot faster and gain better visibility. This includes Blackboard companies, ATG companies, OnCore companies, and other companies that need to perform Java monitoring and .NET monitoring.

We also work with companies deploying their applications to the public cloud. In fact, one of our clients has the largest public cloud deployment in Amazon EC2 in the world. We are able to monitor this deployment and ensure that nodes remain under watch even when they spin up and spin down quickly. For cloud application performance monitoring, we have a truly unique offering.

You don’t need to worry about long, time-consuming Proof-of-Concepts where you can’t get pricing or any information about actually deploying the software in your production environment. We don’t work that way. Most of our customers are up and running in a few hours, and our pricing is clearly displayed on our web site.

Forget about Java profilers or swimming through log files. In almost no time at all, you can be engaging in application performance monitoring that’s easy, fast, and affordable. Download our free Java performance solution if you want to try it for yourself—or take our 30-Day trial. There’s no cost or obligation of any kind.
