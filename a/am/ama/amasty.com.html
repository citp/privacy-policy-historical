Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to Amasty.com.

This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Amasty.com collects, and the ways in which Amasty.com uses that personal information.

Using personal information

Amasty.com may use your personal information to:

You agree to such cross-border transfers of personal information.
