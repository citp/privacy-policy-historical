American Royal is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. American Royal collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by American Royal. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through American Royal public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others. Note: American Royal does not read any of your private online communications.

American Royal is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of the American Royal and American Royal family of Web sites.

Use of your Personal Information

American Royal collects and uses your personal information to operate the American Royal Web site and deliver the services you have requested. American Royal also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from American Royal and its affiliates. American Royal may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

American Royal does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, American Royal may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to American Royal, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

American Royal keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within American Royal, in order to determine what American Royal services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within American Royal to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

American Royal Web sites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on American Royal or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of American Royal; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of American Royal, or the public.

Use of Cookies

The American Royal Web site use "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize American Royal pages, or register with American Royal site or services, a cookie helps American Royal to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same American Royal Web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the American Royal features that you customized.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the American Royal services or Web sites you visit.

Security of your Personal Information

American Royal secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. American Royal secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.
