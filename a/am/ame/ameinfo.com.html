Last Updated on Tuesday, July 23th - 2005

Introduction

AME Info FZ LLC / Emap Limited is a licensee of the TRUSTe Privacy Program. TRUSTe is an independent, non-profit organization whose mission is to build user’s trust and confidence in the Internet by promoting the use of fair information practices. This privacy statement covers the site www.ameinfo.com

Because this Web site wants to demonstrate its commitment to your privacy, it has agreed to disclose its information practices and have its privacy practices reviewed for compliance by TRUSTe.

If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should first contact us. If you do not receive acknowledgement of your inquiry or your inquiry has not been satisfactorily address, you should contact TRUSTe. TRUSTe will then serve as a liaison with us to resolve your concerns.


AME Info keeps log files that include your IP address for statistic and logging of abuse purpose only. Log files are under no circumstances shared with any third party except if required by law or court order by a competent court.

By signing up to the AME Info newsletter, updating and adding your company details to the database or other subscriptions on the AME Info Site, you agree that:
  1. AME Info FZ LLC / Emap Limited stores your contact details and personal information if provided including: full name, e-mail, gender, company name and address, city, country, IP address, phone number, fax number, birth date, nationality, primary role, corporate website, number of employees and industry in a database;
  2. AME Info FZ LLC / Emap Limited may use your personal information to send you information about issues AME Info finds relevant, including third party information however the sender will always be AME Info for easy identification;
  3. we may contact you via email if our terms changes, if authorities demands it, when services are added, updated or modified;
  4. you can manually request to be deleted from our database and all information held about you by AME Info FZ LLC / Emap Limited will be removed;
  5. AME Info FZ LLC / Emap Limited may use, store, transfer and process your personal information; and
  6. if you choose to contact a company or organisation through AME Info or the Site, AME Info FZ LLC / Emap Limited is entitled to disclose any of your information or details that it holds (including your IP address) to that company or organization.
You can however, remove yourself from any of the AME Info mailing lists, commercial messages or info messages by following the instructions at the end of each email sent to you by AME Info FZ LLC / Emap Limited. Any mailing lists on the site will in no way be shared, sold or any other way be revealed to any third parties other than as specifically provided in these TOS or if required by law or court order by a competent court.

Cookies

To enhance your experience with AME Info, many of the AME Info web pages use "cookies." Cookies are text files placed in your computer's browser to store information about your preferences. Cookies, by themselves, do not reveal your e-mail address or other personally identifiable information unless you choose to provide this information, for example by registering on the Site. However, once you choose to furnish AME Info with personally identifiable information, this information may be linked to the data stored in the cookie.

AME Info uses cookies to understand Site usage and to improve the content and offerings on the Site. For example, AME Info uses cookies to personalize your experience (e.g., to recognize you by name when you return to the Site), save your password in password-protected area, and enable you to save articles and to build customized news feeds.

Email and Communication Services - Member account, password and security

If a particular service offered on the Site or any other AME Info owned web site requires you to open an account, you must complete the registration process by providing AME Info with current, complete and accurate information as prompted by the applicable registration form. You then will choose a password and a user name. You are entirely responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password and account. You must not disclose your password to any third party. Furthermore, you are entirely responsible for any and all activities that occur under your account.

You agree to notify AME Info immediately of any unauthorized use of your account or any other breach of security. AME Info will not be liable for any loss or damages that you may incur as a result of someone else using your password or account, either with or without your knowledge. However, you could be held liable for losses incurred by AME Info or another party due to someone else using your account or password. You may not use anyone else's account at any time, without the permission of the account holder.

Updated and changes

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this privacy statement, the homepage, and other places we deem appropriate so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it. We reserve the right to modify this privacy statement at any time, so please review it frequently. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you here, by email, or by means of a notice on our homepage.

If your personally identifiable information changes, or if you no longer desire our service, you may correct, or update by making the change on our member information page or by contacting us by telephone or postal mail.

Discussion Forum

If you use the discussion forum on this site, you should be aware that any personally identifiable information you submit there can be read, collected, or used by other users of these forums, and could be used to send you unsolicited messages. We are not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to submit in these forums.

Third party websites

This Web site contains links to other sites that are not owned or controlled by AME Info FZ LLC / Emap Limited. Please be aware that we, AME Info FZ LLC / Emap Limited, are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites.

We encourage you to be aware when you leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every Web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies only to information collected by this Web site

Third party advertisers

The ads appearing on this Web site are delivered to users by 24/7 Real Media, our advertising partner. We share Web site usage information about users with a reputable third party 24/7 Real Media for the purpose of targeting our Internet banner advertisements on this site.

24/7 Real Media uses cookies and clear GIFs on this site, which allow it to recognize a user's cookie when a user visits this site. The information that 24/7 Real Media collects and shares through this technology is not personally identifiable.

For more information about our third-party advertiser, or to learn more about the choices available to you regarding this anonymous information, please click here.

Security

We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Send content to a colleague

If you choose to use our referral service to tell a colleague about our site or a specific article, we will ask you for your friend’s name and email address. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email inviting him or her to visit the site. AME Info FZ LLC / Emap Limited does not store this information.
