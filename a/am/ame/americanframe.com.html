Privacy Statement

American Frame has created this privacy statement in order to set forth our limited use of information generated from our Web site.

We do not share information about individual visitors or customers with any third party.

We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information.

Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping in The Frame Shop. This cookie expires 12 hours after you visit the site. We may use other cookies in the future, to help deliver specific content to your interests.

Our site's "CONTACT US" form requires users to give us contact information (like your e-mail address). We use customer contact information to send information you request about our company. Your contact information is also used to contact you when necessary. If you sign up for our newsletter and other e-mails, you may opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time; appropriate information will be provided with each mailing. All information is held in strictest confidence, and shared with no other business entity. Any information you provide on our contact form will be used only to respond to your question, comment or request.

Our site's order form uses industry-standard SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption to protect the confidentiality of your personal information. The form requires users to give us contact and financial information. We use this information to fulfill your order. Any and all information submitted in these forms will be held in strictest confidence and not shared with any other business entity outside of your Credit Card Service and/or your Bank. We do reserve the right to contact you, using this information, to answer questions, offer suggestions, and make corrections to your order.

This site may contain links to other sites. American Frame Corporation is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, or your dealings with this Web site, you can send us e-mail via the "CONTACT US" form.
