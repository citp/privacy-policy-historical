# whos.amung.us - legal - privacy policy

(This statement is only available in our official website language of English.) 

Our Commitment to Your Privacy 

At whos.amung.us Inc. your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we have provided this notice explaining our online information practices. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where any personally identifiable information may be requested and/or collected. whos.amung.us Inc. reserves the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time without notice. This privacy policy only affects whos.amung.us and other sites/domains operated by whos.amung.us Inc. it does not pertain in any manner to any websites linking to whos.amung.us (or related domain, operated by whos.amung.us Inc.), or any websites linked to from a whos.amung.us Inc. site or domain. 

whos.amung.us Inc. does not collect any personally identifiable information - except where noted below, and does not sell trade, or give away any user information that may be personally identifiable. Data is collected in the following ways: 

  * **Free widget services:**  
You will not be required to provide any personally identifiable information, or any information at all, should you choose to install one of our free widgets on your website in order to utilize our statistics services. You, and any user who visits a website containing a whos.amung.us Inc. free widget, will be providing an IP address, page URL reference(s), and browser/operating system information in the request header. This is a standard HTTP request header and is available freely to the operator of any website you choose to visit. We log certain aspects of this header and/or data as part of our services. We may log certain aspects of the content where our widgets are installed, as well as non-personally identifiable actions users may take on these sites/pages (such as a copy action). 
  * **Subscription based Pro widget service:**  
In order to effectively provide our subscription based Pro service we require owners of websites who wish to use our services provide relevant information for contact and billing purposes only, this may include, but is not limited to: names, addresses, emails, zip/postal code information, phone numbers, billing information, etc. In addition to the information collected by the above free widget service, Pro widgets may collect information from you or any visitor to a site containing a Pro widget, such as user operating system and browser. Again, this information is derived from a standard HTTP request header and is available freely to the operator of any website you choose to visit. We log certain aspects of this header and other information collected via Javascript as part of our services, no personally identifiable information is collected, or released to any third party. We may log certain aspects of the content where our widgets are installed, as well as non-personally identifiable actions users may take on these sites/pages (such as a copy action). 
  * **Advertising and all other business relationships:**  
Any person, company, or entity choosing to enter into an advertising and/or other business relationship with whos.amung.us Inc. will be required to provide any necessary personally identifiable information in order to complete the business transaction/relationship. This information may include, but is not limited to: names, addresses, phone numbers, server information, email addresses, trademarks and copyrights used in and advertising capacity. 



whos.amung.us Inc. will not sell, trade, or give away any personally identifiable information we may have collected from any of our users, without the expressed written consent of the users in question. Any information collected by whos.amung.us Inc. will be held in a secure manner to the fullest of our abilities; however whos.amung.us Inc. does not take any responsibility for any 3rd party unauthorized access to our servers, offices, computers, or other information containing stores. Access to any information we collect is granted only to whos.amung.us Inc. employees, agents, partners, or authorized contractors. 

Cookies, Web Beacons & Partners 

whos.amung.us Inc. may place cookies on your system in order to provide a better and more informative service to our users. These cookies may include, but are not limited to, user preference information and unique user IDs for internal tracking purposes. No personally identifiable information will be contained in these cookies. 

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site, products, or services. Our advertising partners include, but are not limited to, Google. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about these practices, used by Google, or to opt out of having cookies set on your system, please visit [Google's Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html) page. 

These third-party parters, ad servers, or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on whos.amung.us Inc. send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see. 

whos.amung.us Inc. has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers or partners. 

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. The whos.amung.us Inc. privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites. 

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites. 

whos.amung.us may engage in business relationships with partners that would grant these parties access to non-personally identifiable data collected by whos.amung.us and/or provide these parties with product integration with whos.amung.us products. While the data whos.amung.us collects is non-personally identifiable, 3rd party partners may use this data to behaviourally target ads. whos.amung.us ensures all partners engaged respect opt out lists for ad targeting, you can opt out using the NAI's opt out mechanism at <http://www.networkadvertising.org/optout_nonppii.asp>. 

By using this website and/or our services you agree to this privacy policy and agree to indemnify whos.amung.us Inc. of any claims, demands, or actions resulting from a breach of this privacy policy. 

whos.amung.us Inc. may update this privacy policy at any time. The date of last change will be posted on this page alongside the modified privacy policy.
