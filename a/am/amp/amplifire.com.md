# Privacy Policy amplifire | amplifire

Knowledge Factor, Inc. (“KFI” or “we” or “us”) owns and operates this website [www.knowledgefactor.com](http://www.knowledgefactor.com/)  and [www.amplifire.com](http://www.amplifire.com/) (this “Site”). 

This Privacy Policy applies regardless of whatever user device you are using to access the Site (including desktop, laptop or mobile web browser, mobile phone, tablets, or via an app, and any other technology whether now known or developed in the future).

By accessing the Site, you agree to be bound by these terms and conditions whenever you access the Site on any device. This Privacy Policy forms part of and is incorporated into our [Website Terms and Conditions](http://www.knowledgefactor.com/amplifire-application-terms-use). 

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted here. We reserve the right to vary this Privacy Policy from time to time and such changes shall become effective as soon as they are posted. Your continued use of the Site constitutes your agreement to all such changes.

We are committed to protecting your personal information ("Information"). This Privacy Policy both describes the Information that we may collect on the Site or which may be supplied to us for processing and explains how we will use this Information.

**Safe Harbor, TRUSTe and Privacy Laws  **KFI complies with the U.S. — EU & U.S. — Swiss Safe Harbor Frameworks as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use and retention of personal information from the European Union member countries and Switzerland (“Safe Harbor”).

KFI has certified that it adheres to the Safe Harbor privacy principles of notice, choice, onward transfer, security, data integrity, access, and enforcement. To learn more about the Safe Harbor program, and to view KFI's certification, please visit <http://www.export.gov/safeharbor.>

KFI also complies with all applicable local data protection and privacy laws in the jurisdictions in which it operates.

Knowledge Factor, Inc., has been awarded TRUSTe's Privacy Seal signifying that KFI’s privacy policy and practices have been reviewed by TRUSTe for compliance with [TRUSTe's program requirements ](http://www.truste.com/privacy-program-requirements/)including transparency, accountability and choice regarding the collection and use of your personal information. The TRUSTe program covers only information that is collected through the Site ([www.knowledgefactor.com](http://www.knowledgefactor.com/) and [www.amplifire.com](http://www.amplifire.com/)), and does not cover information that may be collected behind the log in on the Site or client portal websites.

TRUSTe's mission, as an independent third party, is to accelerate online trust among consumers and organizations globally through its leading privacy Trustmark and innovative trust solutions. If you have questions or complaints regarding KFI’s privacy policy or practices, please contact us at [marketing@knowledgefactor.com](mailto:marketing@knowledgefactor.com). If you are not satisfied with the response you receive, you can contact TRUSTe [here](https://feedback-form.truste.com/watchdog/request).

If you have any complaints regarding our compliance with the Safe Harbor program or with any applicable local privacy or data protection law you should first contact us at [marketing@knowledgefactor.com](mailto:marketing@knowledgefactor.com).

If contacting us does not resolve your complaint, you may raise your complaint with TRUSTe by Internet [here](https://feedback-form.truste.com/watchdog/request), fax to 415-520-3420 or mail to TRUSTe Safe Harbor Compliance Department ([click here for address](http://www.truste.com/about-TRUSTe/contact-us)). If you are faxing or mailing TRUSTe to lodge a complaint, you must include the following information: the name of the company, the alleged privacy violation, your contact information, and whether you would like the particulars of your complaint shared with the company. For information about TRUSTe or the operation of TRUSTe's dispute resolution process, [click here](http://www.truste.com/products-and-services/dispute-resolution-services/) or request this information from TRUSTe at any of the addresses which can be accessed above.

**Third Party Sites  **You should note that when you leave the Site, whether via a link provided by us or otherwise, you may enter a site owned and controlled by a third party ("Third Party Sites"). Accordingly, this Privacy Policy does not apply to any Information that you provide to or share on any Third Party Site.

**Customer Testimonials/Comments/Reviews  **We may post customer testimonials/comments/reviews on the Site, which may contain personal information. We will obtain your consent via email prior to posting the testimonial. To request removal of the testimonial and/or your name, contact us at [marketing@knowledgefactor.com.](mailto:marketing@knowledgefactor.com)

**How KFI collects and obtains your Information  **We may obtain Information in four ways:

1\. Personal information you disclose to us.

You may, for example, provide us with your information when you post something on the Site (information might include first name, last name, company name, email, etc.), when you register for any features on the Site that require registration, complete surveys, when you contact us (online or offline) or when you order or access goods or services from us.

2\. Information provided to us by Third Party Clients

If you are using the Site pursuant to an agreement between us and a third party such as your employer, academic institution, government agency or otherwise (“Third Party Clients”), the Third Party Client may also give us Information about you for the purpose of fulfilling its obligations under that agreement and this Information will be processed in accordance with this Privacy Policy. It is the Third Party Client’s responsibility to make certain that he is entitled to provide us with your data; any of your queries should therefore be directed to the Third Party Client and not to us.

3\. Using Cookies

We collect information of the way you use the Site by using "cookies". For more information on “cookies”, and what cookies we use, please see our [Cookie Policy](http://www.knowledgefactor.com/cookie-policy) which forms part of this Privacy Policy.

4\. Via our Servers

Our web servers also collect your IP address and browser type and details of the website you came from before entering this Site.

**How KFI uses your Information**

1\. By using the Site, you agree that we may collect, hold, process and use your Information for the purpose of providing you with this Site and products and services available via the Site and developing our business which shall include (without limitation):

(a) Contacting you, including for the purpose of administering the Site, informing you about the latest changes to the Site, or products, services or promotional offers that you might find interesting, however, this communication does not serve promotional purposes but is destined to help you use the services;

(b) Personalizing your visits to the Site and developing the Site to improve the services provided to you;

(c) Processing any online purchases;

(d) Improving the quality of the products and services offered to you;

(e) Using it for market research, marketing, statistical or analytical purposes;

(f) Using it to help diagnose problems with our server, for determining the frequency with which our visitors look at various parts of this Site;

(g) Using it to gather broad demographic information such as geographic distribution of our visitors and to allow us to provide goods and services appropriate to your place of residence; and/or

(h) Using it for archival purposes, e.g., in order to retrieve your learning results.

Please note that we only use your personalized data where necessary to deliver our services.

2\. We collect your response to every question you answer, including confidence and correctness; the time it takes you to respond to each question; the time you spend reviewing the results of each question. Our adaptive learning algorithm uses this data to determine which questions to show you, and in which order. Our reporting system uses this data to display to instructors how each person is performing, including their levels of initial knowledge, and how long it took them to master the learning information. 

3\. Subject to obtaining your consent, we may send you direct marketing communications about us and our business partners’ products and services, events and special offers. By direct marketing activities, we mean the communication directly to particular individuals (by email or phone) of any advertising or marketing material. If having given your consent you subsequently decide you no longer wish to receive direct marketing or information from us or our associated companies or third parties or no longer want us to pass your information to third parties, please notify us at the following email address: [marketing@knowledgefactor.com](mailto:marketing@knowledgefactor.com).   You can also opt out from receiving further emails from us by clicking on the ‘unsubscribe’ link in the direct marketing/newsletter emails from us. 

**Information Sharing and Disclosure  **KFI may share your Information with third parties when:

1\. We have your consent to share your Information; or

2\. We need to share your Information to provide the product or service you have requested.

3\. We may also contract with companies or persons to provide certain services including credit card processing, shipping, data management, promotional services, and other services related to our business and to the products and services that you may purchase and we provide our service providers with the Information needed for them to perform these services, but these companies or persons are never allowed to use your Information for any purpose other than providing the services contracted for.

4\. Where you are using this Site pursuant to an agreement between us and a Third Party Client, that Third Party Client will have full access to all of your activity on this Site and to your Information. It is the Third Party Client’s responsibility to make certain that he is entitled to have access to your data; any of your queries should therefore be directed to the Third Party Client and not to us. You understand that the administrators designated by the Third Party Client will be able to monitor all of your behavior on this Site and your Information and we have no control over the use they may make of your Information. If you have questions about the Third Party Client’s use of your Information, you should consult them.

5\. We compile anonymous records of user trends, which we may make use of ourselves, or pass to our associated companies.

6\. We may disclose details about use of the Site to other businesses, e.g., to demonstrate patterns of use to advertisers and other business partners. The information we pass on will not include any personal information by which you may be identified.

7\. In certain instances we may disclose your Information when there is reason to believe that it is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against persons or entities that may be causing injury to you, to us or to others in circumstances where it is not practical for us to obtain your consent.

8\. We will comply with any court order requesting or requiring the disclosure of the identity or location of or any other information in relation to anyone who has accessed the Site and we may also disclose such information where we are advised by our lawyers that a court order would be granted requiring us to disclose it, even if no legal proceedings have been commenced in relation to the same.

9\. We may transfer, sell or assign any of the information described in this policy, including your Information, to third parties as a result of a sale, merger, consolidation, change of control, transfer of assets or reorganization of our business.

10\. We may share your Information with other companies within our group of companies and they may use and share, within that group, the Information you provide and other information held about you for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

11\. If we or our service providers transfer any information out of the European Economic Area (EEA), it will only be done with the relevant protection (stated under UK law) being in place and you consent to such transfer.

**Data Retention  **We will retain your Information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you wish to cancel your account or request that we no longer use your Information to provide you services, contact us at [marketing@knowledgefactor.com](mailto:marketing@knowledgefactor.com). We will retain and use your Information as necessary to comply with KFI’s legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce KFI’s agreements.

Personal information provided to Knowledge Factor will be stored and processed in the United States, where it will be subject to the laws of that jurisdiction.

**Security  **KFI has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of your Information under KFI’s control. Once we receive your Information, we will store it on secure servers behind a firewall. Our management team and its engineers regularly review and update the integrity of the Site and the security of your Information. KFI cannot, however, safeguard any data stored on your own personal computer or any public computer upon which you might elect to access the Site. In addition, the Internet is not a secure medium and we cannot guarantee the security of information transmitted via the Internet.

**Unsubscribing, Correcting and Updating Your Information  **You are not able to un-subscribe from service announcements, which contain important information about service notices and responsibilities.

If you do not wish to receive them, you have the option to deactivate your account.

Requesting the deletion of certain types of your Information may prevent you from accessing some features on this Site.

Please note that if you enter Information for KFI’s Sample a Course section, you cannot update or delete previously submitted information via this Site. If your Information (such as your email address) should change, we ask that you re-submit a Sample a Course form.

If you are a registered user of this Site, you may correct, delete or update your Information by contacting us through email our Customer Support at [marketing@knowledgefactor.com](mailto:marketing@knowledgefactory.com) or by contacting us by telephone or postal mail at the contact information listed below.  We will respond to your request to access within 30 days. If you are using this Site further to agreement between us and a Third Party, the administrator designated by such Third Party.

Your friend may contact us at [marketing@knowledgefactor.com](mailto:marketing@knowledgefactor.com) to request that we remove this information from our database.

**Changes To This Policy** We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes to our information practices.
