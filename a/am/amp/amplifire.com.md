# Privacy Policy | amplifire

Knowledge Factor, Inc. (“KFI” or “we” or “us” or “our”) owns and operates this website [www.amplifire.com](http://www.amplifire.com/) (this “Site”).

This Privacy Policy applies regardless of whatever user device you are using to access the Site (including desktop, laptop or mobile web browser, mobile phone, tablets, or via an app, and any other technology whether now known or developed in the future).

By accessing the Site, you agree to be bound by these terms and conditions whenever you access the Site on any device. This Privacy Policy forms part of and is incorporated into our [Website Terms and Conditions](https://amplifire.com/terms-conditions/).

Your continued use of the Site constitutes your agreement to all such changes.

We are committed to protecting your personal information (“Information”). This Privacy Policy both describes the Information that we may collect on the Site or which may be supplied to us for processing and explains how we will use this Information. It also describes your choices regarding use, access and correction of your personal information.

**Safe Harbor and Privacy Laws**  
KFI complies with the U.S. — Swiss Safe Harbor Frameworks as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use and retention of personal information from Switzerland (“Safe Harbor”).

KFI has certified that it adheres to the Safe Harbor privacy principles of notice, choice, onward transfer, security, data integrity, access, and enforcement. To learn more about the Safe Harbor program, and to view KFI’s certification, please visit <https://safeharbor.export.gov/swisslist.aspx>.

If you have any complaints regarding our compliance with the Safe Harbor program or with any applicable local privacy or data protection law you should first contact us at [marketing@knowledgefactor.com](mailto:marketing@knowledgefactor.com). KFI also complies with all applicable local data protection and privacy laws in the jurisdictions in which it operates.

**EU-U.S. Privacy Shield**  
Knowledge Factor, Inc. participates in and has certified its compliance with the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework.  Knowledge Factor, Inc. is committed to subjecting all personal data received from European Union (EU) member countries, in reliance on the Privacy Shield Framework, to the Framework’s applicable Principles.  To learn more about the Privacy Shield Framework, visit the U.S. Department of Commerce’s Privacy Shield List. <https://www.privacyshield.gov/list>

Knowledge Factor, Inc. is responsible for the processing of personal data it receives, under the Privacy Shield Framework, and subsequently transfers to a third party acting as an agent on its behalf.  Knowledge Factor, Inc. complies with the Privacy Shield Principles for all onward transfers of personal data from the EU, including the onward transfer liability provisions.

With respect to personal data received or transferred pursuant to the Privacy Shield Framework, Knowledge Factor, Inc. is subject to the regulatory enforcement powers of the U.S. Federal Trade Commission.  In certain situations, Knowledge Factor, Inc. may be required to disclose personal data in response to lawful requests by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements.

If you have an unresolved privacy or data use concern that we have not addressed satisfactorily, please contact our U.S.-based third party dispute resolution provider (free of charge) at <https://feedback-form.truste.com/watchdog/request>.

Under certain conditions, more fully described on the Privacy Shield website [[https://www.privacyshield.gov](https://www.privacyshield.gov/)], you may invoke binding arbitration when other dispute resolution procedures have been exhausted.

**Third Party Sites  **  
You should note that when you leave the Site, whether via a link provided by us or otherwise, you may enter a site owned and controlled by a third party (“Third Party Sites”). Accordingly, this Privacy Policy does not apply to any Information that you provide to or share on any Third Party Site.

**Customer Testimonials/Comments/Reviews**  
We may post customer testimonials/comments/reviews on the Site, which may contain personal information. We will obtain your consent via email prior to posting the testimonial. To request removal of the testimonial and/or your name, contact us at [marketing@knowledgefactor.com](mailto:marketing@knowledgefactor.com).

**How KFI collects and obtains your Information We may obtain Information in four ways:**  
_1\. Personal information you disclose to us._  
You may, for example, provide us with your information when you post something on the Site (information might include first name, last name, company name, email and/or phone number), when you register for any features on the Site that require registration, complete surveys, when you contact us (online or offline) or when you order or access goods or services from us.

_2\. Information provided to us by Third Party Clients_  
If you are using the Site pursuant to an agreement between us and a third party such as your employer, academic institution, government agency or otherwise (“Third Party Clients”), the Third Party Client may also give us Information about you for the purpose of fulfilling its obligations under that agreement and this Information will be processed in accordance with this Privacy Policy. It is the Third Party Client’s responsibility to make certain that he is entitled to provide us with your data; any of your queries should therefore be directed to the Third Party Client and not to us.

_3\. Using Cookies and Similar Technologies_  
We and our partners use cookies or similar technologies to analyze trends, administer the website, track users’ movements around the website, and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. Users can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level. We partner with a third party to either display advertising on our website or to manage our advertising on other sites. Our third party partner may use cookies or similar technologies in order to provide you advertising based upon your browsing activities and interests. If you wish to opt out of interest-based advertising [click here](http://preferences-mgr.truste.com/) (or if located in the European Union [click here](http://www.youronlinechoices.eu/)). For more information on “cookies”, and what cookies we use, please see our [Cookie Policy](https://amplifire.com/cookie-policy/) which forms part of this Privacy Policy.

_4\. Via our Servers_  
Our web servers also collect your IP address and browser type and details of the website you came from before entering this Site.

**How KFI uses your Information:**

1\. By using the Site, you agree that we may collect, hold, process and use your Information for the purpose of providing you with this Site and products and services available via the Site and developing our business which shall include (without limitation)  
(a) Contacting you, including for the purpose of administering the Site, informing you about the latest changes to the Site, or products, services or promotional offers that you might find interesting, however, this communication does not serve promotional purposes but is destined to help you use the services;  
(b) Personalizing your visits to the Site and developing the Site to improve the services provided to you;  
(c) Processing any online purchases;  
(d) Improving the quality of the products and services offered to you;  
(e) Using it for market research, marketing, statistical or analytical purposes;  
(f) Using it to help diagnose problems with our server, for determining the frequency with which our visitors look at various parts of this Site;  
(g) Using it to gather broad demographic information such as geographic distribution of our visitors and to allow us to provide goods and services appropriate to your place of residence; and/or  
(h) Using it for archival purposes, e.g., in order to retrieve your learning results.  
Please note that we only use your personalized data where necessary to deliver our services.

2\. We collect your response to every question you answer, including confidence and correctness; the time it takes you to respond to each question; the time you spend reviewing the results of each question. Our adaptive learning algorithm uses this data to determine which questions to show you, and in which order. Our reporting system uses this data to display to instructors how each person is performing, including their levels of initial knowledge, and how long it took them to master the learning information.

3\. Subject to obtaining your consent, we may send you direct marketing communications about us and our business partners’ products and services, events and special offers. By direct marketing activities, we mean the communication directly to particular individuals (by email or phone) of any advertising or marketing material. If having given your consent you subsequently decide you no longer wish to receive direct marketing or information from us or our associated companies or third parties or no longer want us to pass your information to third parties, please notify us at the following email address: [marketing@knowledgefactor.com](mailto:marketing@knowledgefactor.com). You can also opt out from receiving further emails from us by clicking on the ‘unsubscribe’ link in the direct marketing/newsletter emails from us.

2\. We need to share your Information to provide the product or service you have requested.

You understand that the administrators designated by the Third Party Client will be able to monitor all of your behavior on this Site and your Information and we have no control over the use they may make of your Information. If you have questions about the Third Party Client’s use of your Information, you should consult them.  
5\. We compile anonymous records of user trends, which we may make use of ourselves, or pass to our associated companies.  
6\. We may disclose details about use of the Site to other businesses, e.g., to demonstrate patterns of use to advertisers and other business partners. The information we pass on will not include any personal information by which you may be identified.  
7\. In certain instances we may disclose your Information when there is reason to believe that it is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action, against persons or entities that may be causing injury to you, to us or to others in circumstances where it is not practical for us to obtain your consent.  
8\. We will comply with any court order requesting or requiring the disclosure of the identity or location of or any other information in relation to anyone who has accessed the Site and we may also disclose such information where we are advised by our lawyers that a court order would be granted requiring us to disclose it, such as to comply with subpoena, bankruptcy proceedings, or similar legal process, even if no legal proceedings have been commenced in relation to the same.  
9\. We may transfer, sell or assign any of the information described in this policy, including your Information, to third parties as a result of a sale, merger, consolidation, change of control, transfer of assets or reorganization of our business in which case,you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our website, of any change in ownership, uses of your personal information, and choices you may have regarding your personal information.  
10\. We may share your Information with other companies within our group of companies and they may use and share, within that group, the Information you provide and other information held about you for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.  
11\. If we or our service providers transfer any information out of the European Economic Area (EEA), it will only be done with the relevant protection (stated under UK law) being in place and you consent to such transfer.

**Data Retention**  
We will retain your Information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you wish to cancel your account or request that we no longer use your Information to provide you services, contact us at [marketing@knowledgefactor.com](mailto:marketing@knowledgefactor.com). We will retain and use your Information as necessary to comply with KFI’s legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce KFI’s agreements.

Personal information provided to Knowledge Factor will be stored and processed in the United States, where it will be subject to the laws of that jurisdiction.

**Security**  
KFI has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of your Information under KFI’s control. Once we receive your Information, we will store it on secure servers behind a firewall. Our management team and its engineers regularly review and update the integrity of the Site and the security of your Information. KFI cannot, however, safeguard any data stored on your own personal computer or any public computer upon which you might elect to access the Site. In addition, the Internet is not a secure medium and we cannot guarantee the security of information transmitted via the Internet.

You are not able to un-subscribe from service announcements, which contain important information about service notices and responsibilities. If you do not wish to receive them, you have the option to deactivate your account. Requesting the deletion of certain types of your Information may prevent you from accessing some features on this Site.

Please note that if you enter Information for KFI’s Sample a Course section, you cannot update or delete previously submitted information via this Site. If your Information (such as your email address) should change, we ask that you re-submit a Sample a Course form. If you are a registered user of this Site, you may correct, delete or update your Information by contacting us through email our Customer Support at [marketing@knowledgefactor.com](mailto:marketing@knowledgefactor.com) or by contacting us by telephone or postal mail at the contact information listed below.  Upon request Knowledge Factor will provide you with information about whether we hold any of your personal information. You may access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information by  contacting us at [marketing@knowledgefactor.com](mailto:marketing@knowledgefactor.com). We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe. If you are using this Site further to agreement between us and a Third Party, the administrator designated by such Third Party.

Our website includes Social Media Features, such as the Facebook Like button, and Widgets, such as the Share This button or interactive mini-programs that run on our website. These Features may collect your Internet protocol address, which page you are visiting on our website, and may set a cookie to enable the Feature to function properly. Your interactions with these Features are governed by the privacy statement of the company providing it.

**Send to Friend  **  
If you choose to use our send to friend service to tell a friend about our careers, we will ask you for your friend’s email address.  We will automatically send your friend a one-time email about the position.  KFI does not store this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email. Your friend may contact us at marketing@knowledgefactor.com to request that we remove this information from our database.

**If you are under Legal Age**  
If you are under legal age in the jurisdiction in which you are accessing this Site, please be sure to read this Privacy Policy with your parent or guardian. You should ask them to explain anything you don’t understand about this Privacy Policy. Please get their permission before you provide us with any personal information.

**Abuse of this Privacy Policy  **  
We may immediately suspend or terminate your access to the Site without notice if we become aware that you are in breach of this Privacy Policy.

**Changes to This Policy  **  
We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes to our information practices. If we make any material changes we will notify you by email (sent to the e-mail address specified in your account) or by means of a notice on this Site prior to the change becoming effective. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
