Privacy Policy

amzreviews.co.uk ("AMZ Reviews") operates amzreviews.co.uk and may operate other websites. It is amzreviews.co.uk's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, AMZ Reviews collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. amzreviews.co.uk's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how amzreviews.co.uk's visitors use its website. From time to time, AMZ Reviews may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

AMZ Reviews also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on amzreviews.co.uk blogs/sites. AMZ Reviews only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to amzreviews.co.uk's websites choose to interact with AMZ Reviews in ways that require AMZ Reviews to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that AMZ Reviews gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at amzreviews.co.uk to provide a username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with AMZ Reviews are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, AMZ Reviews collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor's interaction with AMZ Reviews. AMZ Reviews does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Aggregated Statistics

AMZ Reviews may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. AMZ Reviews may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, AMZ Reviews does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

AMZ Reviews discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on amzreviews.co.uk's behalf or to provide services available at amzreviews.co.uk's websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using amzreviews.co.uk's websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. AMZ Reviews will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, AMZ Reviews discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or when AMZ Reviews believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of AMZ Reviews, third parties or the public at large. If you send us a request (for example via email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. AMZ Reviews takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information, and is not liable for any damages as a result of unauthorized access.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. AMZ Reviews uses cookies to help AMZ Reviews identify and track visitors, their usage of AMZ Reviews website, and their website access preferences. AMZ Reviews visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using amzreviews.co.uk's websites, with the drawback that certain features of amzreviews.co.uk's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

If AMZ Reviews, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, or in the unlikely event that AMZ Reviews goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of AMZ Reviews may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.

Ads

Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by AMZ Reviews and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

Why Wasn't I Selected?

You are essentially competing with a whole lot of other reviewers when requesting to review a product. Here are some ways to improve your Amazon profile and look more inviting to the sellers.


Fill in your profile details

Sellers want to know about you so they can send you appropriate products. Tell us a bit about yourself, your interests and hobbies.

If you are an exercise junkie, let us know and sellers of yoga mats, drink bottles and dumbbells will be happy to send you things to review.

If you tell us you are a Mom then baby products and childrens toys are more likely to come your way.


Have more reviews on your profile.

If you don't have many reviews on your profile sellers will think you won't review the products they send you.

Go and review products you have previously purchased on Amazon or used in the past. Think of all the household goods you use, movies you've seen and books you've read and write reviews on those.


Write good quality reviews.

Think about what you would like to see in a review when deciding on whether you should purchase a product.

The review heading should be a good summary of the review. DO NOT put the product name here. Buyers already know what the product is.

One sentence reviews do not help subsequent buyers out. DO NOT copy and paste the product information here. Reviews should be detailed, informative, well written and complement the product description already on Amazon.


Enhance your reviews with photos and videos.

Some people like reading reviews. Some people are more visual. Help out other buyers with unboxing videos and product close up photos.
