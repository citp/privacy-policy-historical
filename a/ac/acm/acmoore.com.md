# Privacy Policy | A.C. Moore

**Last Updated: August 15, 2016.**

**A.C. Moore Privacy Statement**

This Privacy Statement (“Privacy Statement” or “Statement”) explains information we collect from you and how we use it. We may collect this information online, such as on our website, www.acmoore.com, or via social media, or offline, such as when you visit A.C. Moore stores (collectively, “A.C. Moore Sites”). By using the A.C. Moore Sites or otherwise providing us with your information, you consent to and agree to be bound by this Privacy Statement.

**I.** **Types of Information We Collect**

The types of information we collect from you depends on the services and features you use on A.C. Moore Sites, but may include:

  * **Name and Contact Information:** We may collect your first and last name, email address, postal address, phone number, company, and other similar contact data.  
 
  * **Demographic Information:** We may collect data about you such as your age, gender, city, state, and country.  
 
  * **Personal Interests and Favorites:** We may collect data about goods or services that you are interested in.  
 
  * **Purchase and Payment Information:** We may collect data necessary to process your payment if you make purchases. This data may include your name, email address, phone number, billing information, shipping information, and payment information, including your credit card number and its expiration date, in order to process and track your order. We may use a third party to process your purchase and to handle your payment information. We may also maintain a record of your product purchases.  
 
  * **Usage Information:** We may collect data about how you and your device interact with A.C. Moore Sites and our related services. This could include features you use, the items you purchase, the web pages you visit, and the search terms you enter. This could also include data about your device and the network you use to connect to our services, including IP address, device identifiers, and data about the performance of the services you use on A.C. Moore Sites and any problems you experience with them.  
 
  * **Location Information:** We may collect data about your location. This information may help us provide recommendations to you based on your location, serve you with advertisements or direct you to a product or service that we believe may be of interest to you and that is marketed to people near your location.  
 
  * **Content You Provide to Us:** We collect additional content that you voluntarily provide us, such as content you submit when entering a sweepstakes or promotion, information you send us when you contact us via email, or questions and information you provide us for customer support.



You have choices about the data we collect. When you are asked to provide personal data, you may decline. But if you choose not to provide data that is necessary to provide a service, you may not be able to use some features or services.

**II.** **Information Collection Practices**

We collect personal information from a variety of sources, both online and offline. When you visit A.C. Moore Sites, for example, with the help of our third party authorized agents, we may collect your personal information as described below:

**A.** **Passive Collection**

We may collect information passively, while you are visiting or interacting with A.C. Moore Sites. We call this “passive” collection since you may not know that this data is being collected when you visit or interact with A.C. Moore Sites. This information may consist of the following items: website visitors’ IP address, IP address-related information, system Media Access Control address, network configuration information, network device information, browser plug-in type. and versions, and operating system.

  * **Cookies and Web Beacons:** One way that we collect information passively is through cookies or web beacons. Cookies or web beacons serve to improve your experience when visiting A.C. Moore Sites. Cookies are small text files placed on your device that uniquely identifies your device and which a website can transfer to a consumer’s hard drive to keep records of his or her visit at a website. Web beacons are small pieces of data that are embedded in images on the pages of websites. We may use cookies or web beacons to improve your experience on A.C. Moore Sites, such as remembering you when you return to A.C. Moore Sites and what you have saved in your Shopping Bag, anonymously track interest in and collect aggregate information on A.C. Moore Sites, and analyze traffic patterns on A.C. Moore Sites. You may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie, or you may choose to block cookies with your browser, but if you do, you may not be able to take advantage of the personalized features enjoyed by other users to our Sites. Some of the cookies we use may be more persistent in nature. These persistent cookies may not be deleted when your cookies are deleted. Please check your browser and browser settings to determine where these types of cookies are stored and whether and how they may be deleted. In any event, if you reject our cookies, you may still use A.C. Moore Sites, but you may be limited in some of the features. We may also work with service providers that use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your visits to A.C. Moore Sites and to other websites you may visit, and to serve advertisements to you across the Internet based on that information.  
 
  * **Location Data:** We may also collect your location if you choose to allow us to track your location through A.C. Moore Sites or if your personal or non-personal information indicates a certain geographic location. This information may help us provide recommendations to you based on your location, serve you with advertisements or direct you to a product or service that we believe may be of interest to you and that is marketed to people near your location. Finally, in order to help facilitate the delivery of relevant content, we may utilize third party data analytics tools to identify how users engage with A.C. Moore Sites and to provide us with information regarding our users.  
 
  * **Other Sources: __** We may collect your personal information from other sources, such as public databases, joint marketing partners, and social media platforms. If you elect to connect your social media account, we may also receive your personal information from social media platforms. The purpose of this data collection is to optimize your experience on A.C. Moore Sites, to ensure we are partnering with third parties that offer products and services that are of interest to you, to facilitate a transaction or fulfill the terms of an agreement, or to ensure that the advertisements offered to you are relevant.



**B.** **Direct Collection**

We also collect information from you directly, at the time that you supply data to us via A.C. Moore Sites. This information may be collected in the following instances:

  * **Purchases & Refunds: __** When you place an order through the A.C. Moore Sites, we require that you provide your personal information, such as your name, email address, shipping/billing address, phone number, credit card number and its expiration date in order to process and track your order. We may maintain a record of your product purchases. We may also require you to provide your personal information to process refunds.  
 
  * **Creating an Account:** When you create an account on any of A.C. Moore Sites, we may ask for your full name and email address. We also ask you to create a password to access your account. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account and password. You need an account to make an online purchase and to use some of the other features on A.C. Moore Sites, such as creating and saving to your crafts collection(s).  
 
  * **Sign-up for Emails, Offers, and Rewards Program:** You can sign up on A.C. Moore Sites to receive promotional emails from us that include the latest information about our new products, exclusive offers. If you sign up to receive offers from us, we will ask you for your email address. When you register to join the A.C. Moore Rewards program, we ask you for your name, A.C. Moore site account information (e-mail address and password), birthdate, address, and related interests.  
 
  * **Social Media Sites/Applications:** If you choose to link a social media account to the A.C. Moore Sites, we may request your permission to access certain personal information on these social media sites, such as your name, profile picture, gender, networks, user ID, list of friends, and other profile information. Depending on the social media site/application, you may have the opportunity to post reviews, questions, answers, and other content.  
 
  * **Sweepstakes, Contests, and Promotions:** If you enter one of our sweepstakes, contests, or other promotions, we may ask you for information such as your name, date of birth, and email address.  
 
  * **Feedback:** If you contact us to give us feedback or ask questions about A.C. Moore Sites or any of our services or products, we may store your communications, including any personal information you include in them, so we can effectively respond to you.  
 
  * **Job Application:** If you submit a job application on our careers site, we ask that you register and create an account and request that you provide us with extensive information for the purpose of assessing your employment application. This information includes name, contact information, job preferences, educational history, employment history, personal references, and other hiring-related information.



The above list provides examples of how and when we may collect personally information about you from A.C. Moore Sites. If you do not want us to collect your personally information, please do not provide it to us.

**III.** **Use of Information**

We may use the information we collect from you for the following purposes:

  * **To Provide & Improve Our Services:** We use the information we collect from you in order to, for example, process orders, refunds, and other related transactions; fulfill a request from you; contact you concerning the status of your order; respond to customer questions and comments; process A.C. Moore Rewards memberships; diagnose service problems; and to evaluate and resolve problems with A.C. Moore Sites.  
 
  * **Communications:** We also use your information to communicate with you about upcoming A.C. Moore sales, special events, product information, contests, and other A.C. Moore news, as well as to send you catalogs, promotional emails, surveys, newsletters, or notifications.  
 
  * **Advertising, Marketing, and Promotions:** We may use your data for generating web analytics to compile user data for marketing purposes, advertising, conducting marketing analysis, providing you with recommendations for products or services based on your profile, and promotional material planning and distribution.



**IV.** **Information Sharing and Disclosure**

 We may share or disclose your personal information in the following instances:

  * To fulfill purchases and other services. If you choose to make a purchase on the A.C. Moore Sites, we may need to provide your information to third parties as necessary to complete your purchase (for example, to process your credit card). If you choose to sign up for a class on A.C. Moore Sites, we may share your personal information in order to provide the class to you. In addition, if you email us a question, we may use your email address to process your request and respond to your question. Also, if you are entering a sweepstakes or contest, we may use your personal information in order to fulfill the terms of that promotion. This means that we may share the information for prize fulfillment purposes or mail carriers. We also may share your information with the co-sponsor, if any, of that promotion.  
 
  * To affiliates, strategic partners, agents, third party marketers, or other unaffiliated parties who are offering products or services that we believe may be of interest to you or who require your personal information for research, administrative, and/or internal business purposes. These parties may use your personal information to contact you with an offer or advertisement related to a product or service, or they may use such information for their own research, administration or business purposes. If you do not want us to share your personal information in this manner, please do not provide us with this information.  
 
  * To maintain A.C. Moore Sites and provide other administrative services. We may share personal information with our website hosting partners, and other third parties who assist us in operating and improving our website or conducting A.C. Moore business. We seek to ensure that these unaffiliated third parties will not use the personal information for any other purpose than to provide the administrative services for which they are responsible. Because such unaffiliated third-party service providers that help us administer A.C. Moore Sites will have access to users’ personal information, if you do not wish for our unaffiliated third-party service providers to have access to your information, please do not register or submit any personal information to us.
  *   
To comply with the law, to enforce our website policies, or to protect our or others’ rights, property, or safety. We may disclose your information to comply with the law, or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary. We may do this to conform to legal requirements or comply with legal process served on us, protect and defend our rights or property, including the rights and property of A.C. Moore, A.C. Moore Sites, or act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of our employees and customers.
  * To third parties as part of any corporate reorganization process including, but not limited to, mergers, acquisitions, and sales of all or substantially all of our assets. We might sell or buy online stores or other assets, which could include information about customers. In the event that our company or substantially all of our assets are acquired by a third party, such information may be one of the transferred assets.
  * To protect against potential fraud, we may verify with third parties the information collected from A.C. Moore Sites. In the course of such verification, we may receive personal information about you from such services. In particular, if you use a credit card or debit card to purchase services with us, we may use card authorization and fraud screening services to verify that your card information and address matches the information that you supplied to us, and that the card has not been reported as lost or stolen.



**V.** **Updating and Deleting Your Information**

You can update, change, or delete certain personal information (such as your email address, mailing address, and payment method) on A.C. Moore Sites by logging into your account and clicking “My Account” in the menu at the top of each page of the website.

**VI.** **Opt-Out**

We may send you communications or information regarding the A.C. Moore Sites and our products and services. For example, we may use your email address to confirm your request, to send you payment notices, to send you information about changes to our products and services, and to send notices and other disclosures as required by law. Generally, users cannot opt-out of these communications, but they will be primarily informational in nature rather than promotional.

Some communications may be more commercial in nature, as they may advertise a new product or service, or a new feature of the A.C. Moore Sites. We provide you the opportunity to opt-out of such communications by clicking the “unsubscribe” link in email communications or by contacting us. We will process your request as soon as possible, but please be aware that in some circumstances you may receive a few more messages until the unsubscribe is processed.

**VII.** **Information Security**

We have various security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse, or alteration of the information you provide us. For example, we utilize access controls in handling your personal information and we encrypt and tokenize your credit card information.

We store your data on servers located within the United States, but we may choose to utilize storage technology that take advantage of global servers. We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the services and fulfill the transactions you have requested, or for other legitimate business purposes, such as complying with our legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our agreements.

We may use third party security products and services to secure your personal information.

**VIII.** **Children’s Information**

A.C. Moore Sites are not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and none of the A.C. Moore Sites are designed to attract children. In the event that we learn that a person under the age of 13 has provided personal information to us, we will delete such personal information. We require that you be 13 years of age or older to register for shopping at our website, to download our mobile apps, or participate in the A.C. Moore Rewards program.

**IX.** **Third Party Websites and Advertisers**

A.C. Moore Sites may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such other sites. We may also use advertisers, third party ad networks, and other advertising companies to serve advertisements on A.C. Moore Sites. Please be advised that such advertising companies may gather information about your visit to A.C. Moore Sites or other sites (such as through cookies, web beacons, and other technologies) to enable such advertising companies to market products or services to you, to monitor which ads have been served to your browser and which webpages you were viewing when such ads were delivered. If you would like to learn more about this practice, and know your choices with respect to it, please [visit here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/).

PLEASE NOTE THAT THIS PRIVACY STATEMENT DOES NOT COVER THE COLLECTION AND USE OF INFORMATION BY SUCH THIRD PARTY SITES AND ADVERTISERS.

**XIII.** **Changes to Our Privacy Statement**

We may amend this Privacy Statement at any time. When we post changes to this statement, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of the statement. If we make any material changes to this Privacy Statement, we will notify you by prominently posting a notice of such changes before they take effect or by directly sending you a notification. We encourage you to periodically review this privacy statement for changes to our practices relating to personal information.

Please note that, at all times, you are responsible for updating your personal information to provide us your current e-mail address. In the event that the last e-mail address that you have provided us is not valid, or for any other reason is not capable of delivering to you the notice described above, our transmittal of the e-mail containing such notice will nevertheless constitute effective notice of the changes described in the notice.

In any event, changes to this Privacy Statement may affect our use of your personal information that you provide us. If you do not wish to permit changes in our use of your information, you must notify us prior to the effective date of the changes.

**XIV.** **How to Contact Us**

Should you have any further questions regarding this Privacy Statement or our personal information practices, in general, please contact our customer service department at customerservice@acmoore.com.
