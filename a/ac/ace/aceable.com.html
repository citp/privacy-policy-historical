Our Privacy Policy explains how Aceable collects, uses, and discloses personal information and data we receive through your use of our website and other online services we may offer. Use of our website or other online services is subject to, and constitutes consent to, our Privacy Policy.

I. WHAT INFORMATION DOES ACEABLE COLLECT?

Aceable is unique because much of the information we collect from users goes into state certificates, including names, birthdays, and gender. If you choose to login via Facebook, we will collect your credentials from them. For questions concerning Facebooks privacy policy, please visit this page.

We collect the following additional personal information you may provide to use the website and our services, such as a username and password, name, address, phone number, email address, and sometimes school (since we are often used by students). We collect payment information to process charges. We also collect content that you provide to us (such as user reviews) and communications between us (such as via email).

In order to better tailor our user experience, we may automatically collect other information including, but not limited to, the users location, type of mobile device, browser type, IP address, language, operating system, unique device identifier, the date and time of your visit, the pages you view, and the website that referred you to Aceable. The purpose of this practice is to identify which content our users find most useful and to tailor a better experience for you. When you visit our website, Aceable or other partners we work with may use cookies or other similar technologies like pixels, web beacons and local storage to collect information about how you use the website and provide features to you. You may be able to set your browser or device to prompt you before you accept a cookie, accept cookies automatically or reject all cookies. However, if you choose not to accept cookies from the website, you may not be able to access and use all or parts of the website or benefit from the information and services it offers.

II. WHAT DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION FOR?

As stated above, our collection of information is first and foremost for the purposes of filling out state certificates and verifying compliance with state requirements. We also use the information to: provide you with our services and customer support; remember information so you will not have to re-enter it during a subsequent visit; help you efficiently access your information after you sign in; provide, improve, test, update and monitor the website and other services for a better user experience; and diagnose or fix technology problems. We will not release your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes. However, we may use your information to provide personalized content and information to you, which can include ads or other forms of marketing on behalf of third parties.

III. DISCLOSURES

We may share your information with certain third parties selected by us to help support our operations. These include, for example, service providers that help us process payments, analyze web traffic, send emails, and track customer service requests. In addition, based on your use of our website and services, our display advertising partners may present you with personalized advertising on other sites. The techniques our partners employ do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address or telephone number. We may also disclose your information when we believe we have your consent to do so, such as when you contact customer service and ask us about your account, or when we have the consent of someone we believe is authorized to consent on behalf of you, such as the individual associated with the payment method for your account. We may disclose your information to Aceable partners if you give us consent to do so, such as requesting more information on an offer or service.

We may also disclose your information when we have a good faith belief (i) we are required to do so by law, or in response to a subpoena, court order, or other legal mechanism, or (ii) it is necessary to: detect, prevent and address fraud and other illegal activity; to protect ourselves, you and others, including as part of investigations; and to prevent death or imminent bodily harm. If you voluntarily share or submit any information for posting on the website, such as a user review, it may become available to the public.

Finally, we may share all or some of your Personal Information with any of our subsidiaries, joint ventures, or other companies under common control to be used in a manner consistent with this policy, and if Aceable or our assets gets acquired or transferred in part or whole to another organization, your information and any other information the Company has collected may be among the items transferred.

IV. MODIFICATION

You may modify much of your Personal Information within the Aceable website. We retain your Personal Information as long as you have an account with us or as long as needed to provide you services. Should you wish to delete your Aceable account or make other changes, please contact us at privacy@aceable.com. A member of the Aceable team will be happy to help you.

V. OPT-OUT

Should you wish to opt out of all communications from Aceable, you may unsubscribe from our emails at any time. It may take up to ten (10) business days for us to process your opt-out request. Well shed a tear, but respect your wishes. Please note that we may still send you important communications regarding your account, payment transactions, administrative announcements and security notices.

VI. CHILDRENS PRIVACY

Aceable does not knowingly collect or solicit any information from anyone under the age of 13 or knowingly allow such persons to register on our website or services, which are not directed at children under the age of 13. In the event that we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under 13, please contact us at privacy@aceable.com.

IF YOU ARE A CALIFORNIA RESIDENT UNDER THE AGE OF 18, DO NOT SHARE ANY PERSONAL INFORMATION ON ANY PUBLIC FORUM OR MESSAGE BOARD. If you do post any Personal Information, you can remove it by contacting us at privacy@aceable.com. This removal may not be complete given the nature of the Internet.
