# Privacy Policy Animeboards.com

**Animeboards.com** has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: **Animeboards.com**. 

**Animeboards.com** is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. **Animeboards.com** will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. 

**Animeboards.com** will not release or sell ANY of the information that our members provide us without the specific permission of a member who chooses to participate in a specific promotion or opportunity. 

**Registration Forms**  
Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their email address). 

Contact information from the registration forms is used to get in touch with the member when necessary. 

Users may opt-out of receiving future forum newsletters from within their User Control panel.

Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site through web traffic statistics. **Animeboards.com** uses this data to tailor the visitor's experience at this site showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences. 

**Cookies**

To help make sure that you receive information that is relevant to you, our site uses cookies. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the userâ€™s hard drive containing information about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site. Once the user closes their browser, the cookie simply terminates. For instance, by setting a cookie on our site, the user would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our site. Cookies can also enable the forum software to perform certain member features such as marking a particular forum or all forums read, or searching for new posts since they last visited. 

**Advertisers**

Some of our advertisers may use cookies on our site. However, we have no access to or control over these cookies.

**Network Partners**

**Animeboards.com** has several network partners which we link to on our website. Any data that you supply to these network partners to obtain information or buy products or services from is beyond our reach and will be the responsibility of each of the linked-to entities and not **Animeboards.com**. We do not have access to the information you give them for products purchased by you through linked-to entities. 

**Surveys**   
Our online surveys may ask visitors for contact information (like email address), unique identifiers and demographic information (like zip code, age, or income level). Contact information from the surveys is used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings. Unique identifiers are collected from Web site visitors to verify the user's identity. 

**Contests**  
We may run contests on our site in which we ask visitors for contact information (like email address), unique identifiers, and demographic information (like zip code, age, or income level). Contact information from the contests is used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings. Unique identifiers are collected from Web site visitors to verify the user's identity. 

Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. We use this data to tailor our visitor's experience at our site showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences. 

**Security**

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. For security reasons, all member passwords are MD5 encrypted in the forum database. **Animeboards.com** collects the following information from all visitors to our site: the name of the domain through which you access the Internet (for example, earthlink.net, if you use Earthlink); the date and time you access our site; the Web page you "clicked" from to get to our page; and the individual address of the computer you used to access our site. We use this information to administer our Web site, to help diagnose and troubleshoot potential server malfunctions, and to gather broad demographic information. 

**Public Forums**  
This site makes forums, message boards, and/or mailing lists newsletters available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. 

**External Links**   
This site contains links to other sites. **Animeboards.com** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
