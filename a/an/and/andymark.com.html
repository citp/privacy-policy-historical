Privacy Policy





AndyMark, Inc. is dedicated to preserving your privacy. We will not violate your personal sanctity in any way or form. What this means is:

We use the information you provide to let us ship your parts as quickly as possible and to send your bill to the right address. We understand that your personal and credit information are confidential, so we use a secure server to protect the information as it's sent over the Internet. The details you provide to us about your shipping and billing addresses remain securely protected within AndyMark as well.

We obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information such as the Internet Service Provider you use to connect to the Internet, your browser type or the type of computer operating system you use (Macintosh or Windows, for example). We don't see such information as an invasion of your privacy and use it solely for our own research and development.

Our ad provider may use "cookies" to enhance your experience on the web. They cannot use these cookies to personally identify you in any way. If you don't want the cookies, just turn the cookies off on your browser.

We may choose to log your IP address on certain parts of the site, this is nothing more than a security protocol.

Any information you choose to disclose to us will be treated with respect and discretion. We DO NOT sell on, trade or give out any personal details that we may gain during your usage of the site.

In extreme cases it may be required of us, by law, to provide personal detail about users (for example, user misuse of services).
