    When you access the Site or use the Service, we collect the following information:

    • Log Data: Information that your computer and your browser sends whenever you visit a website or online service ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include, but is not limited to, type and information about your computer's operating system, your computer's Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, the languages supported by your browser, the type of your flash software if any, web page you were visiting before you came to the Site and information you search for on the Site;

We use "persistent cookies" to save your username and login password for future logins to the Site. We use "session ID cookies" to enable certain features of the Service, to better understand how you interact with the Service and to monitor web traffic routing on the Site and aggregate usage of the Service. You can instruct your browser, by changing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the website you visit. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all portions of the Site or all functionality of the Service.

We will automatically send the friend a one-time email inviting him/her to visit the site. We store this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email.
    • Third Party Links: We may also present links in a format that enables us to keep track of whether these links have been followed.
    • Information collected by Third Parties: Third party websites which may advertise on the Service (by placing banners, links, chat messages and other means) may place or read cookies on your computer's browser, which enables them to collect information about users who view or interact with their advertisements and content. We do not control or monitor the activities of such third parties. The collection and use of such information and access of any third party websites are subject to such third party's privacy policy.
