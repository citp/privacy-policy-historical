Our Privacy Commitment

As such, we have established and implemented this Privacy Policy to inform visitors to the Site(s) and users of the Anedot Features how we use and protect the information we collect through the Site(s). By visiting the Site(s) or using any of the Anedot Features, you consent to Anedot’s collection, use, storage, deletion and disclosure of information relating to you as set forth in this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date set forth above and is only applicable to the Site(s) and not to any other website that you may be able to access from the Site(s) which may have its own data collection and use practices and policies.

This policy answers the following questions:

What information do we collect about you and how do we use it?

Types of Information We Collect

Personal information means information that can be used to identify and contact you. You do not need to submit personal information to access certain areas of the Site(s), but if you do not want to disclose certain personal information to us, you will not be able to access certain aspects of the Anedot Features.

We collect personal information when you create an account through the Anedot Features; make a payment, purchase a product or make a donation through the Anedot Features; or receive customer or technical support.

To create an account for the Anedot Features, we require you to provide your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, your employer and occupation. You will also need to create a screen name. In order to accept payments and donations through your account, you will need to provide your bank name, bank account number, bank account routing number and other bank account information as requested by Anedot. When you make a payment, purchase a product or make a donation through the Anedot Features you may have to submit personal information including your name, email address, billing address and additional billing information depending on your preferred method of payment, such as your credit card number and payment method expiration date. We also collect information about your company to the extent you provide such information through our account holder branded payment sites. You may also be given the ability to import information from third party sources for association with individuals or companies who you make payments to or receive payments from.

How We Use Your Personal Information

The personal information you provide to us will allow us to complete your transaction and provide the services offered through the Site(s); provide transaction data to you in order to manage your payments and donations through the Anedot Features; handle/route your customer service questions or issues; to create and display information through your account holder branded payment sites; to administer contests promotions and surveys; to provide you our newsletter; and/or notify you of promotions, special events and offers. We may also contact you via e-mail, text message, telephone, or postal mail to send you messages about the Anedot Features, to request your feedback and to provide other general announcements. We keep track of your interactions with us, including but not limited to your activity within the Anedot Features. We may use personal information that you provide to us to send messages to you in response to your questions, inquiries or requests. We may also use your personal information to contact you when necessary.

How We Share Your Personal Information With Other Users

To process your payments, we may share some of your personal information with the person or company that you are paying or that is paying you. Your contact information, including your name, email address, billing address, mailing address and other related contact information, and information regarding the payment made, including payment amount, payment date and payment status are provided to other users who you transact with through the Anedot Features. Third parties that receive your information may be able to associate other information with your information that such other users input or import into the Anedot Features. Unless you have agreed otherwise, other users who you transact with through the Anedot Features are not allowed to use this information for any purpose other than to make and process payments through the Anedot Features. Regardless, we will not disclose your credit card number or bank account number to anyone you have paid or who has paid you through the Anedot Features, except with your express permission or if we are required to do so to comply with credit card rules, subpoena or other legal process.

Cookies

“Cookies” are small bits of electronic information that a website sends to a visitor’s browser and are stored on the visitor’s hard drive. Like many websites, we employ cookies in certain areas of the Site(s) to allow us to provide information which is targeted to you and to make your online experience more convenient. For example, when you return to one of our websites after logging in, cookies provide information to the website so that the website will remember who you are. Should you disable cookies in your browser, you will have to provide your screen name and password each time you log in and some aspects of the Anedot Features will not work. Additionally, analytics tools that are managed by third parties which may appear on the Site(s) use cookies. These analytics tools use cookies to collect non-personal information while you utilize the Site(s). You may not realize this information is being collected. In some cases, we may have access to information from cookies used by these analytics tools on the Site(s) and this information may permit us to identify other sites that you have visited.

Log Information

Our servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit the Site(s). Additionally, some of the Anedot Features may collect information regarding your computer system. This information includes your Internet Protocol address, your browser type and version, domain names, physical location, which Anedot Features you use and when and how long you use them. We use this information to monitor and analyze how users use the Anedot Features, to provide customer service, to maintain and improve the Anedot Features, to help prevent fraud and misuse and for other security purposes.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. We take children’s privacy seriously, and encourage parents to play an active role in their children’s online experience at all times.

Social Network Features

The Anedot Features may contain certain linking and sharing features made available by third party social networking sites such as Facebook, Twitter, and others. You acknowledge that by using these linking and sharing features you are voluntarily making the information linked to or shared through such features publicly available through such third party social networking sites. The terms and conditions related to such third party social networking sites are governed by such sites’ separate terms and conditions and privacy policies. Once you share or link information from the Anedot Features, Anedot has no control over such information.

  • Asset transfers. Should such an event occur, we will use reasonable means to notify you, either through email and/or a prominent notice on the Site(s).

  • Service Providers. We may share your personal information with service providers that we engage for the purpose of processing information on our and your behalf, fraud prevention, bill collection, marketing and technology services. For example, we have partnered with other companies to process payments and to process, analyze, and/or store data. While providing services for us, these companies may access your personal information. To the extent applicable, we require these entities to only use your personal information in connection with the services they perform for us and not for their own benefit.

  • Third Party Offers. We may also share your personal information with third party partners when you have opted in to receiving third party offers, newsletters and/or other promotions on our Site(s). We will not disclose your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes when you have not opted in to receiving such third party offers and/or newsletters through the Site(s). You may request that we stop sharing your information with a third party or third parties for direct marketing purposes after you have opted in to such disclosure through the Site(s) by emailing us at privacy@anedot.com and informing us of your desire for us to stop providing your information to a specific third party or a list of specific third parties. Anedot’s disclosure to third party partners may be made for cross-promotional purposes, but Anedot will not receive compensation as the primary consideration for such disclosure.

  • Reporting. We may also share your information with credit bureaus to report account information as permitted by law and with banking partners as required by credit card association rules.

What choices do you have about the collection and use of your personal information?

Control of Your Information

You may update or delete your personal information or modify your account preferences for the Anedot Features by accessing the “My Account” area of the Anedot Features or sending us an email to privacy@anedot.com requesting us to update or delete your personal information.
