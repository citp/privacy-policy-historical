# Privacy Policy

_**Privacy Policy**_

This policy covers how AwesomePennyStocks treats personal information that AwesomePennyStocks collects and receives. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available. AwesomePennyStocks, Inc. collects personal information when you register anywhere on AwesomePennyStocks. We ask for information such as your full name, full address, email address and phone number. Once you register with AwesomePennyStocks and sign in to our website, you are not anonymous to us. By becoming a member of AwesomePennyStocks, you are giving us the permission to contact you via telephone (voice broadcast/live person), text message (SMS-standard messaging rates apply-contact your carrier for details), email, direct mail and by any means necessary. AwesomePennyStocks, Inc. automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookie information, and the page you request. AwesomePennyStocks, Inc. uses information for the following general purposes: to personalize the content you see, fulfill your requests, contact you, and to improve our services. We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs. We may place a cookie on your personal computer. A cookie is a small computer file that our computer sends to your computer and that your computer sends back to us each time you make a request from our site. Cookies help us recognize you when you visit our site again. We use cookies on our site to collect session data for several purposes to personalize and improve your individual viewing experiences on our site. Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies. However, you can instruct your browser, by editing its options, to stop accepting cookies or prompt you before accepting a cookie from the sites you visit.
