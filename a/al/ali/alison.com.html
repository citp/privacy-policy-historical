What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed onto your computer or device as you browse the Internet. Cookies can be used to collect, store and share bits of information about your activities across websites and services, including on Alison. They also allow us to remember things about your visit to Alison, such as your preferred language and other choices/settings and generally make the site easier for you to use.

Alison uses both session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie is used to identify a particular visit to Alison. These cookies expire after a short time, or when you close your web browser after using Alison. We use these cookies to identify you during a single browsing session, such as when you log into Alison. A persistent cookie will remain on your devices for a set period of time specified in the cookie. We use these cookies where we need to identify you over a longer period of time. For example, we would use a persistent cookie if you asked that we keep you signed in.

The Alison “Premium Monthly” (“Premium”) Terms and Conditions should be read in conjunction with the Alison Terms of Use. Where there is conflict between the “Premium” Terms and Conditions and the General Terms of Use, the specific “Premium” Terms and Conditions shall apply, but all other provisions with the General Terms of Use shall remain in full force and effect.

 

If you buy our paid Subscription (“Premium Monthly”), you agree to pay us the applicable Fee. Failure to pay this Fee will result in the termination of your paid Subscription. By signing up for Alison’s Premium membership, you agree to all of these terms, conditions, limitations and requirements.

 

  1. Alison will provide you with the Features of Premium immediately upon the successful validation of your payment information and payment of the associated Fee.
  2. When you sign up to Premium, Alison will automatically start to charge you on a recurring monthly basis, until such time as you cancel your subscription, which you can do at any time. You will be charged on the same date each month that you signed up to Premium. By providing your payment details, you agree to this charge using such payment details.
  3. If you do not want to continue to be charged on a recurring monthly basis, you must cancel your Subscription. Learn how to cancel your Subscription to Premium.
  4. Cancellation will cease auto-renewal of Subscription. If you cancel your Subscription, you will still have access to the Features for the period you have already paid. On cancellation, you will receive communication from Alison confirming your cancellation, and providing the date up until which you can enjoy the Features. ALISON WILL NOT REFUND ANY FEES THAT YOU HAVE ALREADY PAID.
  5. You are required to maintain accurate and up-to-date payment information. Alison assumes no responsibility or liability if your Premium membership fails to renew or otherwise expires because of outdated or incorrect payment information.
  6. Your purchase of Premium membership may be subject to foreign exchange fees or differences in prices based on location (e.g. exchange rates).
  7. Alison reserves the right to change the Fee for Premium from time to time. We will communicate any Fee changes 30 days in advance with currently active Premium members only. If you do not agree with the Fee changes, you have the right to reject the change by cancelling your Subscription.
  8. Alison reserves the right to remove or add any Features of Premium at any time. The current Features of Premium include: no advertisements (“Ads”) on courses or the Alison site; a monthly 10% discount (“Discount”), which can be used on any Alison Certificate, Diploma or Merchandising.
  9. The following terms apply specifically to the Discount received on Premium: On successful payment of the associated Fee, subscribers will receive a Discount. The current Discount is 10% off any one Certificate or Diploma (“Certification”) purchase. The Discount does not have an expiration date and can be applied to a Certification purchase even after a Subscription has been cancelled. If a User does not redeem their monthly Discount, it will roll over to the next month and multiple Discounts can be applied to any one product purchase up to and including 50% off (5 months’ Discounts accumulated). Users cannot apply more than 50% worth of Discounts to any one product purchase. If users are purchasing multiple Certification in one order, Discounts can be applied to individual products, not to the order as a whole.
