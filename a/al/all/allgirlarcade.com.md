### Children Under the Age of 13

We collect all user's age, User Name, Password and Parent's Email address. A child's participation or access to an activity is not conditioned on him/her providing more Personal Information than is reasonably necessary for that activity. We collect Parent's Email address to provide Direct Notice. The parent or guardian will receive an e-mail notifying them that the identified child wishes to register on _www.SparkCityWorld.com_ The parent or legal guardian will then be required to follow the directions in such notification in order to activate the kid's registration.

In the event that we change our policy with respect to the collection of personal information from kids, or if new features, contests or sweepstakes which a kid wishes to use or participate in require the provision of personal information, their parent or guardian will be notified and will be required to give their consent to the collection and use of such information and/or be given the option to opt out of participation.

We do allow Parent/Guardians to purchase a VIP membership that will allow their child unlimited access to all that Spark City has to offer, this includes: clothing, pets, private/VIP areas, furniture, special events and more. 

A Parent/Guardian is able to consent to the site's use of the child's Personal Information without having also to consent to the site's disclosure of the child's Personal Information to third parties. You may contact us at _support@SparkCityWorld.com_.

### What Information is Collected

As mentioned above, we do not collect personal information from children under the age of 13 ("kids") except for the purposes described above (to provide Direct Notice to Parents). ### How is Information Collected?

Direct Collection

Any personal information collected by us will be collected directly from the user (or in the case of a parent's or legal guardian's e-mail address, from a user). While we may use "cookies", no personal information is collected via cookies or other passive means. 

Cookies

Cookies are small data files containing information to specify unique preferences and to provide personalized content. Cookies collect information such as your browser type, time and length of your visit and any personal information you agree to provide.

We do not store any personal information in cookies nor do we link or combine information collected through cookies to any personal information. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies but visitors always have the option of disabling cookies and you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you refuse cookies, you may be limited in your ability to access and/or use some areas of www.SparkCityWorld.com. 

The use of cookies and Web beacons by our tracking utility company is not covered by our privacy policy. We do not have access or control over these tracking technologies.

IP Addresses and Other Data

Our site recognizes information about your computer, such as the browser, operating system, IP address, and the home server from which you access the Internet. Your IP address is used to help gather broad demographic data, identify trends and statistics, help diagnose problems with our server, and administer our website. We may use your IP address information when compiling and reporting aggregate information about our website such as the number of visits to different pages on our site. We use the aggregate information to improve access to our content. We do not link the data collected to any personally identifiable information. 

Third Party Websites

As this site may contain links to other Internet sites, we cannot be held responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these other sites. You should check the privacy policies of these other websites when providing personally identifiable information.

### Interactive Forums, E-mails and "Chats" with Friends

We provide users the opportunity to send games and other messages to family or friends. In order to send such messages, we ask users to submit the email address of the recipient.

We also provide the opportunity to send e-mail to us. We may ask for first names, but we request no personally identifiable information except an e-mail address, which we use solely for the purpose of responding to messages and/or requests.

All information collected for sending games and other messages and for responding to email messages is used only to send the requested game or email message and to respond to email questions and/or requests and is deleted from our system once the game, email message or email response is sent. 

Some Fuel Sites provide a messaging service or other interactive forums so that registered users participate in "chats" or other forms of dialogue with their friends. In some cases, chat messages can only include predetermined words and phrases, so that kids cannot send personal information. Users are prohibited from disclosing personal information including passwords, first and last names, phone numbers, e-mail addresses, instant message screen names or other contact information when use the interactive forums or chat rooms on Fuel Sites. IF A USER CHOOSES TO MAKE ANY OF HIS OR HER PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE OR OTHER INFORMATION PUBLICLY AVAILABLE IN AN INTERACTIVE FORUM OR CHAT ROOM, HE OR SHE DOES SO AT HIS OR HER OWN RISK. We strongly encourage parents to monitor their child's messages.

### Security

The security of your personal information is important to us. When you enter sensitive information (such as credit card number) on our order forms, we encrypt that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). 

We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personally identifiable information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about security on our Web site, you can email us at support@SparkCityWorld.com
