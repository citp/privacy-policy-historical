# Privacy Policy - ALOCATIONS.com

#### Privacy Policy

* * *

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies link to <http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html>.
