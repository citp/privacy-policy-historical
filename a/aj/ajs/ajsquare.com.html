What This Privacy Policy Covers
 
  • This policy covers how AJ Square Inc treats personal information that AJ Square Inc collects and receives, including information related to your past use of AJ Square Inc products and services. Information Collection and Use
 
General
  • AJ Square Inc collects personal information when you register with AJ Square Inc, when you use AJ Square Inc products or services, when you visit AJ Square Inc pages or the pages of certain AJ Square Inc partners, and when you enter promotions.
  • When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. For some financial products and services we may also ask for your address. Once you register with AJ Square Inc and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.
  • This site is intended for use by persons over the age of 13. No information should be submitted to or posted on this Web site by users under 13 years of age.
  • AJ Square Inc automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, AJ Square Inc cookie information, and the page you request.
  • AJ Square Inc uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.
  • Our Live Chat function is hosted by a service provider and the personal information you submit here is collected by AJ Square or by our service provider.

If you choose to use our referral service to tell a friend about our site, we will ask you for your friends name and email address. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email inviting him or her to visit the site. Your friend may contact us at feedbackfeedback@ajsquare.com to request that we remove this information from our database.

 
Information Sharing and Disclosure

AJ Square Inc does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non affiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

  • We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with AJ Square Inc under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help AJ Square Inc communicate with you about offers from AJ Square Inc . The Live Chat, Forum, and shopping cart functions are also hosted by our service providers. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information and they will only use this information to fulfill the specific service they provide.
  • We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims;
  • We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of AJ Square Inc's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
  • We transfer information about you if AJ Square Inc is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, AJ Square Inc will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
If you use a blog on this Web site, you should be aware that any personally identifiable information you submit there can be read, collected, or used by other users of these forums, and could be used to send you unsolicited messages. We are not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to submit in these forums.
 
Cookies

  • AJ Square Inc uses web beacons to access AJ Square Inc cookies inside and outside our network of web sites and in connection with AJ Square Inc products and services.
  • We do not tie your personal information to our cookies.

Confidentiality and Security
 

  • We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.
  • We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect personal information about you.
