# Privacy Policy | AKMG

Privacy Policy

Web User Privacy Statement 

This privacy statement discloses the privacy practices for http://www.akmg.com. 

Herein lies the privacy details for AKMG, Inc.

We have agreed to notify the consumer of:

* What personal information is collected from www.akmg.com  
* Information regarding the collecting party  
* How the information is used  
* Whom said information will be shared with.  
* What the consumer options are regarding this information and its respective submission  
* Data security processes and procedures in place  
* How information can be changed.

If you have any questions regarding this document, you should contact AKMG, Inc. by emailing your request to info@akmg.com. If you do not receive a response in a timely fashion, you can can contact AKMG,Inc. at the phone numbers listed at www.akmg.com.

Information Collection and Use  
AKMG, Inc. a is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. Information collected is in two categories: Information regarding members and various web user information. This portion of AKMG, Inc.’s privacy statement applies only to Members of AKMG, Inc. a. See the Web User Privacy statement regarding cookies and advertisements.

Affiliates and Clients of the AKMG, Inc. include publishers and advertisers involved in any advertising campaign transaction. Member information collected includes personally identifiable information that is necessary to conduct daily business, which could include items such as name, phone number, and email address. Payment from and to AKMG, Inc. a require said collection of personally identifiable information which could include credit card numbers and bank account information. Phone contact data may be used to contact Members for sales or inventory acquisition purposes. All Member information is collected, modified and stored in a secure, protected environment. Any information collected used for any service, media delivery, contact, billing and accounts payable purposes. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to any parties outside of AKMG, Inc. We always provide the ability to opt-out.

Cookies are used to simplify Member login and to facilitate secure navigation through the password protected areas of the website. Members who choose to reject all cookies may have difficulty navigating the password protected areas of the website depending on the nature of their connection (dynamic IP, proxy server, etc.).

Sharing of Information  
All collected information is not shared with any third party outside AKMG, Inc.or its affiliated companies within AKMG, Inc., except for that necessary to conduct daily business, such as refunding a credit card transaction for a campaign purchase or completing a wire to pay a Publisher, or as required by law. The security of collected information is crucial to AKMG, Inc. as a business. AKMG, Inc. a may, from time to time, use "display" pages to identify Publishers and Advertisers by business identity ONLY (e.g. Advertiser.com is a new Member of the AKMG, Inc. Network).

Server Log Files  
We record the IP address of new affiliates and advertisers at sign-up. This assists in deterring and detecting fraudulent activity. This information is part of the secure data structure behind the backbone of AKMG, Inc.

Links  
This website contains links to other websites. AKMG, Inc. a is not responsible for the privacy practices of any other websites. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this website.

Security  
This website employs industry standard security measures to ensure the security of all data. Any data that is stored within our systems is treated as exclusive and confidential and is not available to the public for any reason. We are always aware of security statuses internally and employ practices to ensure the strength thereof.  


As is public knowledge, any data transfered over the Internet is not guaranteed to be secure. It could be possible for outside parties not under the control of AKMG, Inc. to intercept or transmissions or private data unlawfully. While we strive to protect your data, AKMG, Inc. cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us. You acknowledge and assume this risk when communicating with a AKMG, Inc. company.  


Site and Service Updates  
We may employ company updates via email. Clients/Affiliates are not able to unsubscribe from such announcements, which contain important information about the service. We communicate with said parties in order to provide services or updates in regard to issues relating to any account via email or phone.

Updating or Correcting Personal Information  
If a client or affiliate should require personal information changes, we provide several ways to update this information. The following options are provided:

· Make the changes online by logging to your account  
· Email: info@akmg.com with updated information  
· Contact your account or affiliate manager

If a client or affiliate no longer wishes to be affiliated with AKMG, Inc., there are two ways to remove the respective data. All any requests to remove a client or affiliate from our system will be verified prior to removal to make sure the request is approved by the client or affiliate, respectively.

The following options are provided:

· Email: info@akmg.com  
· Contact your account or affiliate manager

Change Notifications  
If we decide to change our privacy policy,we'll post these changes on the first accessible page. If at any point we decide to use personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time it was collected, we will notify Members by way of an email. Members will have a choice as to whether or not we use their information in this different manner. We will use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected.
