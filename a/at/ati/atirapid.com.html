ATI Statement of Privacy

 

ATI understands that your privacy is important. We have implemented this Data Privacy Policy with respect to information that we collect from you over the web and while providing software or services to you. We want to inform you of our Privacy Policy and to ensure you that we are taking steps to make sure that the information you give us is secure.

ATI only collects information to provide you with products, services and related information.
ATI only collects information which you provide to us in the course of ordering or obtaining support for ATI services, at our web site atirapid.com or at ATI marketing and sales events. You are generally free to provide only such information as you determine, however, certain offerings will require that you provide specific data (e.g. for online support you must provide an email address). In order to improve the content of atirapid.com, ATI tracks the amount of time you spend browsing atirapid.com and the pages you visited. atirapid.com does recognize your domain name but does not automatically collect personal information about you. ATI also collects aggregate information on what pages of atirapid.com are visited. However, no personal information is collected unless volunteered through mechanisms such as surveys or registrations. Atirapid uses WebTrends Live to analyze traffic to this web site. WebTrends Live does not create individual profiles for visitors. Unlike some tracking services WebTrends Live does not have a database of individual profiles for each visitor. WebTrends Live only collects aggregate data. For more information about WebTrends Live privacy policy, please click here."

ATI utilizes "Cookies" in order to make atirapid.com more user friendly and to provide enhanced and customized features during your visits to atirapid.com. Cookies are small bits of data that are sent to your browser and stored on your computer's hard drive. Cookies permit atirapid.com to recognize your user name and email address but do not collect your personal information. ATI does not mandate Cookies for you to access atirapid.com and you may freely set your browser to reject all Cookies or prompt you to accept or reject them.

You can refuse to give us personal information
For certain areas of atirapid.com, ATI requests registration information from you and you are free to provide this information or deny the request. If you do not provide this information, you will still be able to access most of atirapid.com. However, you will not be able to access certain areas and/or services offered on atirapid.com

We restrict access to your personal information
It is ATI's policy that the information you provide to ATI is not distributed to third parties for their use or sale. In certain circumstances ATI may provide your information to third parties, such as upon your consent, when ATI reasonably believes that it is required to do so by law, or to certain business partners or service providers in the course of providing you with information, products or services. It is ATI's policy to never sell your information to list brokers or agents for commercial gain.

ATI will use the information that you provide in order to better service you with respect to ATI services that are available from time to time. ATI may also notify you of updates to atirapid.com and of special events or new product offerings that ATI or its business allies may introduce. These may be in the form of emails or mailings. ATI permits you to opt-out of certain uses of your information when you initially provide personal information and at a later date if you so request.

You can correct the information we collect
You may access the data that atirapid.com has recorded and update or correct the information at any time. You may send an email to webmaster@atirapid.com to determine what information has been recorded or to request opt-out information.

We protect the information you give us
ATI will take reasonable steps necessary to protect the information you provide to us. We continually review our security procedures and mechanisms to ensure that adequate protections are in place. atirapid.com may contain links to other web based sites. ATI is not responsible for the privacy practices, security or the content of these sites.
