# Privacy Policy for ArcadeBomb.com

## Statement

This statement is the official published ("Privacy Policy") for arcadebomb.com ("Site," "we," "us," or "our"), a gaming portal owned and operated by Explosive Games Networks, Inc. This Privacy Policy governs only the personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") collected by the Site. FPS is committed to protecting your privacy online. To further this commitment, we have adopted an Online Privacy Policy that guides how we collect, store, and use the information collected about you online. 

## Personal Information

arcadebomb.com is committed to protecting your privacy online. arcadebomb.com does not collect or store any personal information on its servers. 

## IP tracking

Your IP address is used to track broad demographic information and to track your general visiting paterns (how many pages you view while at one website, downloads, etc) Arcadebomb does not store personal, or individual data on its servers, we respect your privacy and strongly believe that maintaining privacy, as an independant portal, is one of our main objectectives. 

## Third Parties

We use third-party advertising (OBA) companies to serve ads when you visit arcadebomb.com. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. 

## Cookie Policy

\- In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser.  
\- Visit nai opt out to protect your privacy. Note that by using the nai opt out service you will create cookies on your machine, created by nai itself.  


## Opt Out Cookies

It is possible to protect yourself from unwanted cookies by using the opt out service. Please note that the opt out service is creating cookies itself too!  
[click here to opt out cookies](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp)

## Liability

It is arcadebomb.com's policy to respond quickly (under 24 hours) to claims of intellectual property violation. Upon receiving notification in accordance with any related copyright organization or owner, arcadebomb.com will immediately disable and remove games in question.
