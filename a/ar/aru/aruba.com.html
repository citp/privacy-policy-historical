Collectively, AHATA and ATA are referred at different times as “we” and“Aruba.com”.  “Personal Information” (as defined in the Agreement) includes all personally identifiable information described in this Privacy Policy.

    Social Security Numbers

    We will not and do not collect your Social Security Number on this Site. Do not provide your Social Security Number on this Site or to any party that says it is from this Site. 

    1.2 Your California Privacy Rights

    The following only applies to California residents.

    We may from time to time share your personally identifiable information with our subsidiaries, affiliates, sponsors, partners, advertisers or other third parties for their direct marketing purposes. 

    If you do not want us to share your personally identifiable information with such third parties for their direct marketing purposes, please let us know by email at email@aruba.com or by mail at Aruba Tourism Authority L.G. Smith Blvd 172, P.O. Box 1019, Subject Line: Aruba.com Unsubscribe. Your opt-out request will be processed within 30 days of the date on which we receive it.

    For more information on how your personally identifiable information is collected and used, please read the remainder of our Privacy Policy.

    1.3 Children Under 14

    This Site is not intended for nor directed to persons under the age of 14. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under 14 years of age, except for the limited circumstances described below. In the event a person under the age of 14 submits a question to us on this Site and identifies his or her age, we may use the person’s email address for the sole purpose of responding to the person’s question on a one-time-only basis. However, we will not knowingly use the person’s email address to re-contact the person and we will delete it from our records once we have responded. If we become aware that we have unknowingly collected personally identifiable information from a person under the age of 14, we will make reasonable efforts to delete such information from our records.

    1.4 Information Collection

    This Site collects two kinds of information regarding Users: (1) information that personally identifies you (such as your name, e-mail address, and mailing address); and (2) information that does not personally identify you (such as the number of users who log on to this Site on a daily basis). As a general policy, no personally identifiable information is automatically collected from your visit to this Site. However, in certain circumstances, such as when you register for the Site, you may be asked to provide us with some specific information about yourself in order to use certain features on this Site or to take advantage of certain opportunities that we may offer through this Site.

    1.5 Registration

    To simply view this Site, you are not required to register with the Site. However, your use is restricted to solely viewing limited portions of this Site. To use the full functionality of this Site, including making reservations and posting comments or reviews on the Site, you must first complete the registration form and create a user name and password. Your user name will be visible on all postings that you make on the Site. During registration, you are required to provide contact information (such as name and email address), as well as demographic information. We use this information to contact you about the services on our Site in which your demographic and interest information may suggest that you would find interesting or useful. Further, we also use the information you provide in developing user profiles and in targeting marketing that will reflect your expressed interests. (You may opt out of receiving such direct marketing by email at unsubscribe@aruba.com.  Please allow 30 days for implementation.)  We also use the personally identifiable information you provide in connection with any reservations, prospective reservations, or reservation-related activity you conduct on the Site.  Questions regarding the registration process may be emailed to Aruba.com at registration@aruba.com.

    Registration is available to individuals between the ages of 14 and 18 with prior consent from a parent or legal guardian.  Registration is not available to persons under 14 years of age.

    1.6 Orders

    If you purchase a product or service from the booking engine on the Site, we request certain personally identifiable information to process your order. You will need to provide contact information (such as name, email, and shipping address) and financial information (such as credit card number, expiration date). AHATA and/or its members may use this information for billing purposes and to process your orders

    1.7 Contests

    We may hold contests on our Site from time to time. If you decide to participate, we may request certain personally identifiable information from you. Participation in contests is completely voluntary and you have a choice whether or not to disclose this information; however, not providing such personally identifiable information may not make possible for you to participate. The information you provide may be added to your profile, and we also use the information to notify winners, and enhance our monitoring of the Site. A third party service provider may conduct these contests. The third party service provider’s contract will contain provisions governing the use and protection of any personally identifiable information that is collected.

    12.8 Surveys or Focus Groups

    From time to time, we may invite you to participate in a survey or a focus group. You are not required to participate, and may decide not to. If you decide to participate in a survey or focus group, you may be asked to provide certain demographic information (such as your gender, date of birth and zip code). We may use the information that you submit, including your responses to the survey questionnaires, for the purpose(s) identified to you prior to your participation in the survey. In some cases, we may publish the results of the survey, but only as aggregate information. As we may be conducting certain surveys with our advertisers, sponsors or other third parties, we may also share the results of such surveys with such third parties, but in no event will the shared information identify you.

    1.9 Send to-Friend

    If you choose to use our referral service to tell a friend about our Site, we will ask you for your friend’s name and email address. We will automatically send your friend emails about our Site. Your friend will be able to opt out of receiving additional emails, and instructions in the email will provide the steps that your friend should take to opt out of receiving further communications from the Site. Aruba.com will store your friend’s email address indefinitely, but will honor your friend’s request to stop receiving email from the Site. Further, your friend may contact us at unsubscribe@aruba.com to request that we remove this information from our database.

    We also may collect information at other points on our Site, including the Newsletter Sign-up page, and feedback forms. All information that you provide is optional from these areas, and you do not have to sign up if you do not want to receive the newsletter or provide feedback.

If you do not wish to receive them, you have the option to deactivate your account.

    5.2 Log Files

    We gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data. We use this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze trends, to administer the Site, to track users’ movements around the Site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. We link automatically-collected data to personally identifiable information for purposes of tracking Site usage and to allow us to tailor the Site to your preferences, to enhance Site offerings, to increase ease of navigation, and for session timeout.

    5.3 Cookies

    A Cookie is a small text file that our computer sends to your computer, and that your computer sends back to us each time you make a request from our Site. A cookie contains a unique identification number that identifies the visitor's browser, but not necessarily the visitor. We use cookies on this Site. We also link the information we store in cookies to personally identifiable information you may submit while on our Site. Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies. You can instruct your browser, by editing its options, to stop accepting cookies or prompt you before accepting a cookie from the Sites you visit. 

    We use session ID cookies and persistent cookies. A session ID cookie expires when you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains on your hard drive for an extended period of time. You can remove persistent cookies by following directions provided in your Internet browser’s “Help” file. 

    We use cookies to keep track of login names and passwords, to keep track of click-stream data, to provide tailored content, to enhance navigation, and to offer a personalized experience. Persistent cookies also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our Site. We also capture information regarding the next site that you may visit after you leave our Site. 

    If you decide not to accept our cookies, you will be able to access those parts of our Site that are available to the general public, but you will not be able to access many of the products and services offered at our Site.
