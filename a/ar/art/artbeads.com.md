# Customer Service

**Customer Service Schedule**  
You can reach Artbeads.com Customer Service from 7:30am to 5:15pm (Pacific Time) everyday except for these U.S. holidays: _New Year's Day, Easter Sunday, Memorial Day, Independence Day (Fourth of July), Thanksgiving Day and Christmas._

**Ordering Information**  
To find information on how to order, payment methods, policies and other helpful information, go to our [Ordering Information page](https://web.archive.org/web/20100819155931id_/http%3A//www.artbeads.com/ordering-info.html).

**Shipping**  
Artbeads.com is your online source for exceptional beads and jewelry making supplies at low prices. As part of our effort to help you save money, Artbeads.com offers FREE shipping in the U.S. on orders of $10 or more. For more information about our shipping options [visit our Shipping page.](https://web.archive.org/web/20100819155931id_/http%3A//www.artbeads.com/shipping.html)

**Returns and Exchanges**  
If for any reason you are not 100% satisfied with your order, you may return or exchange most items for a full refund of the original price paid, less shipping. Items returned to us MUST be in their original condition and packaging. [To view exclusions and return instructions please click here.](https://web.archive.org/web/20100819155931id_/http%3A//www.artbeads.com/returnorders.html)

**FAQ**  
Check out our [FAQ page](https://web.archive.org/web/20100819155931id_/http%3A//www.artbeads.com/faq.html) for frequently asked questions about ordering, our site, our products or jewelry making.

**Our Guarantee to You**  
Artbeads.com proudly stands behind the products that we sell. If for any reason you are not 100% satisfied with your order, you may return your items for a full refund if purchased during the last year (go to Returns in the Ordering Info section).

**Secure Ordering**  
All online transactions at Yahoo! Stores such as Artbeads.com are handled with industry-standard SSL encryption. When you enter your credit card number into the order form, it is transmitted across the Internet in an encrypted (scrambled) form, then decoded when it gets to us. According to CNN, "The prevalent opinion...is that online credit card use is actually no riskier for consumers than traditional 'low-tech' transactions."

**Top-Service Award**  
Artbeads.com has won Yahoo! Stores' Top Service Star, which is considered a sign of superlative quality. It means that our online bead store participates in Yahoo! Stores' Customer Rating Program, whereby customers who have ordered from us have given our bead shop the highest ratings based on factors such as service and overall satisfaction. According to Yahoo!, companies that have been awarded the Top Service Star are the best of the best, with at least 95% positive ratings.

**Privacy Policy**  
This site is hosted by Yahoo! Store. Yahoo! hosts the store pages, ordering system, and order data. Yahoo! automatically collects order information but may only use this information in the aggregate. If you accessed this store through Yahoo! Shopping, Yahoo! also automatically collects information about your shopping experience. Please see the Yahoo! Privacy Policy for more information about how Yahoo! uses this information at [Yahoo! Privacy Policy](http://privacy.yahoo.com/). While Artbeads.com will never rent, sell, or otherwise distribute any personal information to a third party, we may use your email address for communications about your order or to inform you of upcoming promotions and new products.

Third-Party Web Beacons: We use third-party web beacons from Yahoo! to help analyze where visitors go and what they do while visiting our website. Yahoo! may also use anonymous information about your visits to this and other websites in order to improve its products and services and provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by Yahoo!, [click here](https://reports.web.analytics.yahoo.com/optout,OptOut.vm?domain=a.analytics.yahoo.com).
