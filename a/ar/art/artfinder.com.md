# Privacy Policy - Artfinder Help

[ ](https://web.archive.org/)

Effective as of 30th October 2012.

Last updated: 30th October 2012.

The website  `artfinder.com` and any related domains (the “Site”) is owned and operated by Art Discovery Limited (“AF”, “we” or “us").

AF aims to respect and protect your privacy. This policy (“Privacy Policy”) together with our  [Terms of Use](http://help.artfinder.com/article/168-terms-for-users) and any other documents referred to in either sets out the basis on which any personal data we collect from you, or that you provide to us, will be processed by us. Please carefully read this Privacy Policy in order to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat your personal data.

## 1\. CHANGES TO THE PRIVACY POLICY

    1. _1.1_  We may at our sole discretion, amend, modify or make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time. If we do this we will post the amendments, modifications and changes on this page. We may also, at our sole discretion, send an email to registered users giving notification of any changes. We recommend that you regularly check this page to take notice of any changes. This Privacy Policy should be read in conjunction with the [Terms of Use](http://help.artfinder.com/article/168-terms-for-users).

## 2\. INFORMATION COLLECTION

We may collect and process the following information about you: 

    1. _2.1_  User Registration: Personal information that you provide when registering as a user on the Site including but not limited to, for example, your name, date of birth and email address.
    2. _2.2_  Usernames and/or Email Addresses: If we decide to offer users the opportunity or ability to participate in online discussion forums then you may be required to provide us with your username and/or email address so we are able to respond to any queries you or other users may have. AF recommends you take great care when choosing your username as even if you do not provide any personal information to us, your username may reveal personal information about yourself (e.g. age or name). This information may be available to registered users, us, and the general public.
    3. _2.3_  Site Visits: Details of any visits you make to the Site which may include, but is not limited to, traffic data, location data, weblogs, other communication data, and the resources that you access or use.
    4. _2.4_  IP Address and Browser Information: We may collect information about your computer, laptop or mobile media device which may include, but is not limited to, your IP address, operating system and browser type, for system administration, to develop the Site and to report aggregate information to our advertisers. This is statistical data about our users' browsing actions and patterns, and no individual is identified.
    5. _2.5_  Cookies: For the same reason, we may also obtain information about your general internet usage and browsing habits. We may do this by using a cookie file which is stored on the hard drive of your computer. Please read Section 6 which provides more information on cookies.
    6. _2.6_  Competitions: We may request additional information when you enter a competition or promotion. The information we may request you to provide may include, but shall not be limited to, your name, age, gender and other demographic information.
    7. _2.7_  Records: If you contact us to report any abuse or make a complaint in accordance with our [Terms of Use](http://help.artfinder.com/article/168-terms-for-users), report a problem with the Site or for any other reason, we may keep a record of that correspondence.
    8. _2.8_  Surveys: We may provide you with the opportunity to complete surveys that we may use for research purposes. You may be offered some form of incentive to complete the survey. These surveys are completely optional and you may decide not to respond to them.
    9. _2.9_  Third Party Products and Services: We do not collect any personal information (e.g. credit card numbers and addresses) related to any transaction where you choose to purchase any products or services by clicking on a third party link on the Site. However we may collect information relating to the number of generic users who click on such links, the number of purchases and/or the value of the purchases made by these generic users.

These entities may be located in countries outside the EEA. By registering with AF, you consent to AF and their direct and indirect consolidated subsidiaries’ or parents’ transmission to, and processing of your information in any jurisdiction in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

It may also be processed by staff operating outside the EEA who work for us or for one of our suppliers. Such staff may be engaged in, among other things, the provision of support services. By submitting your personal data, you consent to the transfer, storing or processing of such data outside the EEA.     4. _3.4_  All information you provide to us may be stored on our servers or the servers of a third party in which case you consent to the transfer, storing or processing of such data by third parties which may be situated outside the EEA.
    5. _3.5_  Where you have been required to choose a password which enables you to access certain parts of the Site, you are responsible for keeping this password secure and confidential at all time in accordance with the Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use.
    6. _3.6_  The transmission of information via the internet is never completely secure. Although AF will do our best to protect your personal data and use industry standard procedures and security features to try to prevent any unauthorised access by a third party, we cannot guarantee the security of your data transmitted to our Site. You accept that transmission of your personal data is done at your own risk.

You must be 13 (thirteen) or over to register for the Site or, if you are under the age of 13, you must ask your parent or guardian to register on your behalf especially as there may be content that is inappropriate for children. We do not knowingly collect any person information from anyone who is under the age of 13 or allow them to register as a user of the Site without their parent or guardian’s express permission. If you have reason to believe that this has occurred then please contact us at  [issues@artfinder.com](mailto:issues@artfinder.com) and we will delete all relevant personal information. 

## 8\. COOKIES

Cookies are small files that contain information that is transferred to your computer, laptop or mobile media device's hard drive. They are used by many sites to provide features to their users. They help us to improve our Site and to deliver a better and more personalised service. They enable us to log and quickly retrieve your login information and provide data to enable us to develop and improve the Site. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information provided by users. We also use persistent cookies which stay on your computer, laptop or mobile media device after you have gone offline. Persistent cookies can be found in your browser’s cookies folder and remain there after you have gone offline. 

You may refuse to accept cookies by activating the settings on your browser which allows you to refuse the setting of cookies. You can learn how to do this by accessing the ‘Help’ function on your browser. However, if you select this setting, you may be unable to access certain parts of the Site or discover that you have a reduced functionality whilst using the Site. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, our system will issue cookies when you log on to the Site. 

Please be aware that our advertisers may also use cookies. Advertisers’ use of cookies is not under our control and is therefore not subject to this Privacy Policy. 

This website uses Google AdWords's conversion tracking software to monitor and better understand its visitors, but will not store, save or collect personal information. This website uses remarketing to advertise online - third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on someone's past visits to this website. Visitors can opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting the Ads Preferences Manager.
