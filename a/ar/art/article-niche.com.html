The basics of our privacy statement

The Article Niche Project does not share, sell or rent any personal information such as your name and email addresses to outside third parties.

We will not send you unsolicited e-mail. We will only send e-mails to those who request them through our opt-in process.

We honour all removal requests--you may unsubscribe at any time.

We appreciate you subscribing to our list and using our services. We will not abuse that privilege. The Article Niche Project puts a high priority on list accuracy and quality content.

Who receives our emails
Only those who subscribe to our services receive our The Article Niche Project Newsletter and email programs. Those who subscribe are sent a confirmation email. To ‘opt-in’ means you have entered your first name and email address and clicked on the ‘submit’ or ‘go’ button.

What we e-mail
To those who specifically request them, we send the The Article Niche Project newsletter. For maximum control and security, we maintain our e-mail list and distribution system on our own dedicated server.

Unsubscribing & making changes
You may at any time choose to unsubscribe to the The Article Niche Project newsletter. To unsubscribe, click on the bottom link of your newsletter. Your request will be processed immediately. You will be removed from our list and you will not receive future messages from us.

All email list messages contain the information necessary for removal from their appropriate list.

Why were you sent an email you do not want?
There are a few possibilities:

  • You have forgotten that you signed up for a service.
  • We are following up on a service you once requested.
  • Someone signed you up without your permission.

We use your IP address, browser type, domain names, and access times, operating system, country of origin, page views, and users sessions (normally available from most computers with the email address) to help diagnose problems with our servers and to administer our Web site.

Cookies
A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer. We do not use cookies at all.

Security
The The Article Niche Project web site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Sensitive data, collected through The Article Niche Project's website is transferred via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) server using only the highest standards of 128 bit encryption.

We have our own dedicated servers with daily maintenance and backup. We do not share computing resources with any web sites outside of the The Article Niche Project family of sites.

The Article Niche Project will occasionally update our Privacy Policy based on users’ feedback. If there are any material changes to how we use your personal information, we will notify you via email and provide you the opportunity to opt-out prior to any substantial change to be taken. However, if you have opted out of all communication from The Article Niche Project, you will not be contacted, nor will your personal information be used in this new manner.
