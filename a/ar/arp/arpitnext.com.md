# TechRaga - Privacy Policy, Disclaimer And Terms Of Use

[TechRaga](http://blog.arpitnext.com/ "TechRaga") is a general news website, you can read more about us [here](http://blog.arpitnext.com/about-techraga).

**Privacy Policy**

Log, Cookies and Web Beacons: As with most other websites, we collect and use the data contained in log files. The information in the log files include general stats like your IP address, your ISP, the browser you used to visit our site etc. The privacy of our visitors is important to us. Your information is never sold, shared or distributed in any form. We do use cookies to store information, such as your personal preferences when you visit our site. We use third party advertisements on blog.arpitnext.com to support our site. Some of them may use technology such as cookies and web beacons to collect data about your visit. This is very normal, and generally all other similar websites use them. Visitors are free to turn off browser cookies, or opt-out of these third party vendors and ad networks serving ads, but this may cause the problems with the regular use of the website as few content may not be displayed correctly. 

We use third-party companies to serve ads and collect non-personally identifiable information (e.g., the pages you visit, and which links you click, which ads you see and click on, and the categories of search terms you enter) when you visit our web site. These companies may use the information outlined above (which does not include your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. These companies often use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp) (link to the NAI universal user opt-out).
