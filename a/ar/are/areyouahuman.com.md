# Are You a Human - Privacy

[](http://areyouahuman.com/)

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which [Are You a Human](https://web.archive.org/) collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the areyouahuman.com website (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy applies to Information collected on or through the Site.

## 1\. Information That We Receive or Collect on the Site

a. We may collect personal information from Users in a variety of ways, including, but not limited to, when Users visit our site, register on the site, subscribe to the newsletter, fill out a form, and in connection with other activities, services, features or resources we make available on our Site. Users may provide to us, for instance, contact information such as name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and, as applicable, credit card information and related billing information.

b. We may collect other Information passively or automatically, when Users interact with the Site. This Information may include: network or Internet protocol address and type of browser you are using (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Internet Explorer), the type of operating system you are using, (e.g., Microsoft Windows or Mac OS), the name of your Internet service provider, browser settings, how you may have been referred to our Site (such as, through a link or a referrer website), your IP address (from which we may infer your location), We also may assign or read a unique identifier based on your browser (or work with other companies that do that). Please see our Section titled Cookies and Similar Technologies for more information about how that works.

c. When you engage with the Site through social media services or other third party platforms, plug-ins, integrations or applications, you may allow us to have access to certain Information in your profile. This may include your name, e-mail address, photo, gender, birthday, location, an ID associated with the applicable third party platform or social media account user files, like photos and videos, your list of friends or connections, people you follow and/or who follow you, or your posts or “likes.”  For a description on how social media services and other third party platforms, plug-ins, integrations or applications handle your information, please refer to their respective privacy policies and terms of use, which may permit you to modify your privacy settings.

d. The Site may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance our User’s experience and to communicate with you, as described in the Section below titled “Cookies and Similar Technologies.”

## 2\. Cookies and Similar Technologies. We Use Cookies and Other Technologies

a. **Cookies:** We also collect and store certain information from and about you by automated means, using technologies such as cookies, Web server logs, Web beacons, JavaScript, mobile SDKs and similar technologies. 

b. **Disabling Cookies:** Most Web browsers are set up to accept cookies. You may be able to set your browser to warn you before accepting certain cookies or to refuse certain cookies. However, if you disable the use of cookies in your web browser, some features of the Services may be difficult to use or inoperable. 

c. **Web Beacons:** We may also use "web beacons" or clear GIFs, or similar technologies, which are small pieces of code placed on a website or in an email, to monitor the behavior and collect data about the visitors viewing a website or email. For example, web beacons may be used to count the users who visit a web page or to deliver a cookie to the browser of a visitor viewing that Service. Web beacons may also be used to provide information on the effectiveness of our email campaigns (e.g., open rates, clicks, forwards, etc.).

## 3\. How We Use Collected Information

We may use the Information to provide our users with services, operate the Site and fulfill the immediate purpose for which it was requested, as well as for other purposes. These purposes may include: 

  * Operating our services, including any tools and features associated with them, and making improvements to those services.
  * Providing, administering and communicating with users about, other and related products, services, and promotions (including by sending you newsletters and other marketing communications).
  * Performing diagnostics and analysis to evaluate how our Services operate, and how people are using them.
  * Processing, evaluating and responding to users’ requests, inquiries and applications.
  * Managing our customer information databases.
  * Administering contests, sweepstakes and surveys.
  * Sending users advertisements when they visit our Site or other websites across the Internet (or mobile applications), including through the use of cookies and similar technologies.
  * Customizing user experience.
  * Managing our communications; performing market research, data analytics and data appends; determining and managing the effectiveness of our services and our advertising and marketing; analyzing our products and services; and performing accounting, auditing, billing, reconciliation and collection activities.
  * Protecting against and preventing fraud, unauthorized transactions, security issues, claims and other liabilities, and managing risk exposure and quality.
  * Complying with and enforcing applicable legal requirements, industry standards and our policies and terms 
  * Providing customer support and diagnostic assistance

## 4\. Sharing of Information

We may share any Information we collect on the Site (including personal information) with certain service providers, such as service providers that provide customer or technical support, billing support, marketing and email services, data support and services, data enhancement and append services and other operational services that help us offer and improve our services.

We also may disclose Information (i) if we are required to do so by law, regulation or legal process; (ii) in response to requests by government agencies, such as law enforcement authorities or other parties, such as in response to a civil or criminal subpoena; (iii) when we believe disclosure is necessary or appropriate to prevent physical, financial or other harm, injury or loss; or (iv) in connection with an investigation of suspected or actual unlawful activity or violation of our Terms of Use or other policies. We also may disclose aggregated or non-identifiable information to third parties in a manner that does not identify particular users, for any legal purpose. 

If we sell or transfer all or a portion of our business or assets (including in the event of a reorganization, dissolution or liquidation), then the Information we have collected from or about our Users will likely be part of that transfer, as well.

## 5\. How We Protect the Information

Although we may take certain precautions that we believe may help protect against unauthorized access to Information we hold, no security is perfect. We make no promises regarding the security, integrity, availability or retention of the information collected or handled by us or related systems.

Browsing and interaction on any other website, including websites which have a link to our Site, is subject to that website's own terms and policies.
