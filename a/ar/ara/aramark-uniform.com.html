Your Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: September 28, 2017

This Privacy Policy covers information that Aramark Uniform Services ("Aramark", "we", "us" or "our") collects when you (i) are on Aramark's websites (the "Websites"), (ii) when you purchase products or services through the Websites and (iii) when we collect information through channels where this Privacy Policy is referenced, such as emails. By visiting the Websites, interacting with or accessing our communications or otherwise submitting information, you consent to the collection, processing, use, transfer and/or storage of your personal information as set forth in this Privacy Policy. You unconditionally agree that using the Websites, interacting with or accessing our communications or otherwise submitting information where this Privacy Policy is referenced signifies your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE TO BE BOUND BY THIS PRIVACY POLICY, DO NOT USE THE WEBSITES, COMMUNICATE WITH US OR RESPOND TO REQUESTS FOR INFORMATION WHERE THIS PRIVACY POLICY IS REFERENCED. Please take a moment to review our Privacy Policy in detail.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to the practices of companies that we do not own or control, or to people that we do not employ or manage. This policy only covers information collected on the Websites or information collected where this policy is specifically referenced, and does not cover any information collected at any other website other than the Websites, or offline by Aramark (unless this privacy policy is referenced).

What information does Aramark collect?

Personal information

"Personal information" is information that can be used to specifically identify an individual, such as your name, mailing address (including shipping and billing addresses), email address, telephone number (including a mobile device number) or credit/debit card number. Aramark collects personal information you give us when you visit and use the Websites, order our products or services, communicate with us by mail, phone or email, and/or fill out requests for information through our advertisements, surveys or promotions. We also collect personal information from you when you make a purchase on our Websites, including delivery and billing addresses and credit card number.

Non-personal information

"Non-personal information" is any information that does not reveal your specific identity, such as device and browser information, geographic and other information collected through server logs, cookies, pixel tags and other technologies, demographic information and other information provided by you and aggregated information. We and our service providers may collect non-personal information, such as demographic information (age, gender, etc.), employment and job preferences, taste or other preference information, and the like, when submitted to us, when you visit the Websites and directly or indirectly gathered from our communications with you. We may also keep a list of a visitor's past purchases. We may also obtain non-personal information from third parties; for example, we may obtain geographic or demographic information, or information regarding visitor's possible interests, from third parties, or we may receive non-personal information from a marketing partner in connection with a co-branded website or promotion. All of this information is considered non-personal information because, unless combined with personal information, it does not personally identify you or any other visitor of the Websites.

Additionally, when you visit the Websites or interact with communications where this policy is specifically referenced, Aramark's or Aramark's service providers' servers and other technologies automatically collect certain types of technical and computer and connection information from you, such as device, geo-location, browser type and operating system, Internet Protocol (IP) address (a unique number that identifies your Internet access), cookie information, the pages viewed and accessed, how long a visitor accessed our Websites in any given session and the date and time of access. Aramark or Aramark's service providers may also collect and record non-personal information regarding the types of products ordered on the Websites and in our marketing communications, the pages visited before coming to our Websites and the pages linked to when visitors leave.

We may also aggregate personal information in a manner such that the end-product does not personally identify you or any other visitor to the Websites, for example, by using personal information to calculate the percentage of our visitors who have a particular telephone area code.

What technologies does Aramark use on the Websites and other marketing communications?

Aramark uses a variety of technologies on the Websites and other communications, including cookies and web beacons. Cookies are small data files that are transferred to the computer (or other Internet-accessing device) that you are using when you visit our Websites. Cookies allow us to track statistical information about navigation to and throughout certain areas of our Websites, both during a session and over time. Cookies allow us to improve and enhance the Websites and provide visitors to the Websites with more appropriate content.

We also employ cookies to allow us to maintain products in shopping carts and to otherwise personalize your online experience. Through cookies, Aramark can customize our Websites and advertisements to your individual preferences in order to create a more personalized, convenient online experience.

If you do not want cookies, you can set your computer or other device to warn you each time a cookie is being set or turn off all cookies through your browser (e.g., Internet Explorer or Mozilla Firefox). Check your browser HELP menu to learn the way to change or update your cookies. However, it is possible that some parts of the Websites will not operate correctly if you disable cookies and you may not be able to take advantage of some of the features of the Websites by doing so.

Although cookies are not necessary to view Aramark's Websites or receive advertisements from us, cookies may be required to place an online order with us. Should you wish to not accept cookies in your web browser, you may place an order by calling the phone numbers as listed on the applicable Contact Us pages of the Websites.

Parts of the Websites and our emails to you may contain electronic images called web beacons, sometimes known as one pixel GIFs, clear GIFs or pixel tags. We may use web beacons or similar technologies for a number of purposes, such as to count visitors to our web pages or to monitor how visitors to our Websites navigate the Websites and to count how many emails (or particular articles, links, etc.) that we sent were actually opened or acted upon. Web beacons allow us to develop statistical information about the activities and features that most interest our consumers for the purpose of providing more personalized content on an aggregated and individual level.

We may also use non-personal information, such as technical data and information collected by or shared with third parties for internal business and marketing purposes, and to monitor security of the Websites.

How can I opt-out?

We give you many choices regarding our use and disclosure of your personal information for marketing purposes. If you prefer, you can send us a letter with the same information at the following address: Aramark Uniform & Career Apparel, LLC, 141 Longwater Drive, Norwell, Massachusetts 02061, or call us at (800) 388-3300. What security measures does Aramark have in place?

Aramark takes commercially reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide. Aramark maintains reasonable physical, electronic, and administrative safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized or inappropriate access, including through the use of Secure Socket Layer (SSL) security software. SSL is the industry standard security protocol, which makes it difficult for anyone else to intercept the personal information in the credit card / transaction and order information that you send us when you submit your online order. Otherwise, the transmission of information to and from us is not encrypted or secure. We seek to restrict access to information about you to those individuals who need to know the information to respond to your inquiry or request.

Although Aramark has implemented measures designed to secure personal information collected on our Websites, and otherwise collected where this Privacy Policy is referenced, from accidental loss and from unauthorized access, use, alteration or disclosure, we cannot guarantee that unauthorized third parties will never be able to defeat those measures or use your personal information for improper purposes, nor can we guarantee that the transmission of data over the Internet that includes your personal information, or information storage technology, will be 100% secure. If you have reason to believe that your interaction with us is no longer secure (for example, if you feel that the security of any account you might have with us has been compromised), please immediately notify us of the problem by contacting us in accordance with the "How can I contact Aramark?" section below.

This information may be requested once a year. If applicable, this information will include a list of the categories of personal information that was shared and the names and addresses of the third parties that received the information. If you are a California resident and want to make a request for this information, please submit your request in writing to: feedback@aramark-uniform.com or Aramark Uniform & Career Apparel, LLC, 141 Longwater Drive, Norwell, Massachusetts 02061 (please reference the "Shine The Light Law" in your email or letter).
