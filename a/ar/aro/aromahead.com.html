Data Protection

We only collect your data to provide you with the best and most relevant information regarding aromatherapy and aromatherapy education. You are free to provide or redact your information at will at any time.

We try to send you only information that is relevant to your interests and needs, and we do try to clear uninterested contacts from our lists on a regular basis. We occasionally send out (optional) surveys to get your feedback, and may reach out to you to suggest programs, trainings or special offers that we believe you may be interested in.

You are free to choose the type of information that you receive from us through email correspondence. At any time you can update your email preferences, unsubscribe completely or request to be removed from our email list.

If you sign up for one of our free trainings, your name and email address may be passed along to our webinar software platform so that you will be able to access the training that you’ve signed up for. You will receive reminders for the trainings, replay emails and some special offer emails that relate to the training that you can opt out of at any time.

If, and only if, you request a text reminder then your phone number will be passed along to our text provider to send the information that you’ve requested to your cell phone.

If you purchase an online course from Aromahead Institute we will only use your payment data in a secure fashion and will not share any of your payment details with third parties.

Students of Aromahead have forever access to their online courses, so Aromahead will keep their login details safe and secure unless they choose to delete their account and all information within. All other contacts will remain active in our database as long as you are receiving our email correspondence.

You have the right to request access, rectification, and/or erasure of any of your data from our database. You have the right to withdraw consent at any time. You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Cookie Policy

Information about our use of cookies:

Our website uses cookies to distinguish you from other users of our website. This helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse our website and also allows us to improve our site.

A cookie is a small file of letters and numbers that we store on your browser or the hard drive of your computer if you agree. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer's hard drive.

The cookies we use are "analytical" cookies. They allow us to recognize and count the number of visitors and to see how visitors move around the site when they are using it. This helps us to improve the way our website works, for example, by ensuring that users are finding what they are looking for easily.
