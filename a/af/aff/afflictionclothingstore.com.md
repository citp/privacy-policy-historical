# -Affliction Clothing

## Privacy Notice

Affliction Clothing Inc. is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. This Statement of Privacy applies to the Affliction Clothing Inc. / Affliction Clothing Store website and governs data collection and usage. By using the Affliction Clothing Inc. website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement. 

Affliction Clothing Inc. collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. Affliction Clothing Inc. also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests, and favorites. 

There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by Affliction Clothing Inc.. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. This information is used by Affliction Clothing Inc. for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the Affliction Clothing Store website. 

Affliction Clothing Inc. collects and uses your personal information to operate the Affliction Clothing Store website and deliver the services you have requested. Affliction Clothing Inc. also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Affliction Clothing Inc. and its affiliates. Affliction Clothing Inc. may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered. 

Affliction Clothing Inc. does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Affliction Clothing Inc. may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, Affliction Clothing Inc. may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Affliction Clothing Inc., and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information. 

Affliction Clothing Inc. does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent. 

Affliction Clothing Inc. keeps track of the websites and pages our customers visit within this website in order to determine what Affliction Clothing Inc. services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within Affliction Clothing Inc. to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area. 

Affliction Clothing Inc. websites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Affliction Clothing Inc. or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Affliction Clothing Inc.; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Affliction Clothing Inc., or the public. 

Affliction Clothing Inc. website may use "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. 

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize Affliction Clothing Store pages, or register with Affliction Clothing Inc. site or services, a cookie helps Affliction Clothing Inc. to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same Affliction Clothing Inc. website, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the Affliction Clothing Inc. features that you customized. 

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Affliction Clothing Inc. services or websites you visit. 

Affliction Clothing Inc. secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Affliction Clothing Inc. secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other websites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol. 

Affliction Clothing Inc. will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. Affliction Clothing Inc. encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how Affliction Clothing Inc. is protecting your information. 

Affliction Clothing Inc. welcomes your comments regarding this Statement of Privacy. If you believe that Affliction Clothing Inc. has not adhered to this Statement, please contact Affliction Clothing Inc. at help@afflictionclothingstore.com. We will use commercially reasonable efforts to promptly determine and remedy the problem.
