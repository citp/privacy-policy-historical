# CGBilling.com Support

**PRIVACY POLICY**

The information is used only to compare with bank data for payment approval/denial. We do not spam-mail you about 3rd party products. We do not give out any of your information to any 3rd party, ever. We'll use your email to contact you regarding your account with us and information on our websites if it's necessary. No personal information will be shared with any third parties without the customer's permission.

Credit card details will not be stored by Fibercore Services Ltd.
