**PRIVACY POLICY**

A Culinary Journey with Chef Dennis is committed to maintaining the privacy and protection of your personal information when you visit this website. This policy outlines what information may be collected when you visit the website, and how that information is used. When you continue to visit this website, you are consenting to the practices described in this policy.

**Information Collected When Leaving Comments**

When you leave comments on A Culinary Journey with Chef Dennis you have the option of providing your name, email address, blog or website URL, in addition to your comment. This information becomes a permanent part of the website when you provide it with a comment; the name you indicate can be seen by other visitors to the site and is linked to any website your choose to share (e.g. if your name is clicked it will lead to the website you note if applicable). Your email is required but is not displayed and is never used or sold to third party vendors.

**Information Collected Through Tracking Software**

All websites which collect traffic data have the ability to monitor visitor information such as IP addresses, browser type, operating system, location, date/time of visit, and information about which areas of the site are visited. On A Culinary Journey with Chef Dennis we monitor such information, but do not store it in permanent files or attempt to link it to individual site visitors.
