# Privacy Pledge

_This site is hosted by Yahoo! Store. Yahoo! hosts the store pages, ordering system, and order data. Yahoo! automatically collects order information but may only use this information in the aggregate. If you accessed this store through Yahoo! Shopping, Yahoo! also automatically collects information about your shopping experience. Please see the[Yahoo! Privacy Policy](http://privacy.yahoo.com/privacy/us/) for more information about how Yahoo! uses this information._

At **AsianIdeas,** we are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect from you to process orders only. We do not sell, trade, or rent our customers' personal information to others.

When you place an order, we need to know your name, e-mail address, mailing address, billing address, credit card number, and expiration date. This allows us to process and fulfill your order promptly, and to notify you of your order status.

If you sign up for our montly newsletter, your e-mail address will only be used to notify you about new services and special offers at **AsianIdeas** we think you'll find valuable.

The quality of **AsianIdeas** depends on you, too! So start shopping and be sure to tell us what you think! We need your feedback in order to provide you with the best possible shopping experience. Please send us your questions and comments at:

[Suggestions@asianideas.com](mailto:suggestions@asianideas.com)
