Focus on Astrology: Did You Know?

Crystal Oracles
Using stones or crystals for divination is called lithomancy. There are a number of methods that can be used. Some fortune tellers make use of the traditional 13 stones and some use only two stones for a simple "yes" or "no" answer. If using 13 stones, seven of them represent the planets from the Sun through Saturn and the other six represent home, life, love, health, luck, and news. In other systems 16 stones are used with the all of the planets being represented from the Sun through Pluto, with the six areas represented including place, love, luck, time, commitments, and life. In some readings the stone's physical characteristics are noted, in others the shadow cast by the stones or even the sound they make when they collide is significant in the reading. The results of a reading are based mainly on intuition rather than any hard or fast rules. Intrigued by alternative forms of fortune telling?
