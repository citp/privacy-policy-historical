Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

Google use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit their website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
