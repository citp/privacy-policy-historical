At AutoAnything you can save up to 40% off list price every day. More often than not, "List Price" is the suggested retail price of a product as set by the manufacturer or supplier. In the very few cases where the manufacturer or supplier has not provided a suggested retail price, the "List Price" represents either 1 - the manufacturer's suggested retail price for a comparable product; 2 - an approximate price formulated using standard wholesale/retail markups for this particular type of product; 3 - the "Comparison Price" for the same or comparable product as advertised by at least one other vendor.

The "List Price" is not necessarily the lowest price and often can be higher than the actual price at which the product is commonly being sold or offered.
